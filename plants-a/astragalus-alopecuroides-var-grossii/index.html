Astragalus Alopecuroides Var. Grossii

Astragalus alopecuroides var. grossii is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the rocky slopes of western North America. It belongs to the legume family and is known for its small, spiny leaves and pale yellow blooms. This variant is distinguished by its larger flowers and more robust growth compared to its parent species. Due to its limited habitat and threatened status, it is of great interest to botanists and conservationists. Protecting this unique plant is essential for preserving biodiversity in its native environment.
