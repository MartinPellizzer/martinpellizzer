Aspidium manilense

Aspidium manilense, commonly known as the Manila spleenwort, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is a small, evergreen fern that typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit, with fertile fronds that produce spores on the underside of the leaves. Its leaves are usually lanceolate or ovate in shape, with a serrated margin.

Aspidium manilense is native to Southeast Asia and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.
