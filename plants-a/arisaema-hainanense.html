Arisaema hainanense

Arisaema hainanense, commonly known as the Hainan arrowhead, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of southern China.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its distinctive arrow-shaped leaves and spadix inflorescence. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands. Its flowers are unisexual, with separate male and female flowers on the spadix, a common feature in the Araceae family.

Arisaema hainanense is also noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its unique foliage.
