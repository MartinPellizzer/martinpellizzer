Asterocephalus africanus

Asterocephalus africanus, commonly known as the African starhead, is a unique plant species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, star-shaped inflorescence, which gives it its common name. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun conditions. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, displaying a range of colors from white to pale yellow.

Asterocephalus africanus is often found in arid or semi-arid regions, where it plays a role in stabilizing soil and supporting local biodiversity.
