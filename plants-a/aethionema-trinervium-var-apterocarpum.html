Aethionema trinervium var. apterocarpum

Aethionema trinervium var. apterocarpum, commonly known as the rock cress, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial that thrives in rocky and sandy soils, often found in coastal areas. The plant features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and have a distinctive three-veined structure.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to tolerate harsh environmental conditions.
