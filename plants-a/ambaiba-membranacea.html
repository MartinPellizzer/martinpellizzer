Ambaiba membranacea

Ambaiba membranacea, commonly known as the paperbark tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its smooth, peeling bark that reveals layers of pale, papery skin. The plant has large, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green on top and lighter underneath. It produces small, fragrant flowers that are white or pale yellow, followed by small, round fruits.

Ambaiba membranacea is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
