Anthurium longipeltatum

Anthurium longipeltatum, commonly known as the longipeltatum arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its unique inflorescence consisting of a spadix and a spathe. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy green leaves that are often variegated with white or cream markings. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers surrounded by a colorful, showy spathe.

This plant thrives in humid, warm environments and is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant.
