Aloe praetermissa

Aloe praetermissa, commonly known as the Cape aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, sword-shaped leaves that are often covered in a white powdery coating. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellowish-green flowers during the summer months. Its leaves contain a gel-like substance that has been used for its healing properties.

Aloe praetermissa is adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water to thrive.
