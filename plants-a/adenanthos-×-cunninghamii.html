Adenanthos × cunninghamii

Adenanthos × cunninghamii, commonly known as the coastal adenanthos, is a hybrid shrub native to southern Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and small, tubular flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant typically grows in coastal heathlands and sandy soils, thriving in well-drained conditions. Its leaves are narrow and leathery, providing a textured appearance, while its flowering period occurs during the spring and summer months.

Adenanthos × cunninghamii is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience to coastal environments.
