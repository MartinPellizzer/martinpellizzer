Adonis annua var. castellana

Adonis annua var. castellana, commonly known as the Castellana larkspur, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe.

It features slender, upright stems that bear clusters of blue to violet flowers, which bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with serrated edges, and its root system consists of fibrous roots that help it anchor in rocky or disturbed soils. It is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive floral display.
