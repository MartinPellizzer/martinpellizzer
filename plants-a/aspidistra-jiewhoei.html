Aspidistra jiewhoei

Aspidistra jiewhoei, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen, is a member of the Asparagaceae family and is native to China.

It is a shade-tolerant evergreen perennial, characterized by its dark green, leathery, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are typically followed by bright red berries. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers indirect light, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its ornamental value lies in its elegant foliage rather than its flowers.
