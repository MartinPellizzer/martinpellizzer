Anthurium pazii

Anthurium pazii, commonly known as the Pazii arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are often a deep green with a reddish tinge. The plant produces vibrant red spadices, which are the modified leaves that surround the true flowers. It thrives in humid, warm environments with bright, indirect light.

Anthurium pazii is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
