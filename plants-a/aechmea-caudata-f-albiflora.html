Aechmea caudata f. albiflora

Aechmea caudata f. albiflora, commonly known as the white-flowered pineapple bromeliad, is a hybrid form of the pineapple bromeliad native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking silver-green foliage and large, showy white bracts that surround its small, inconspicuous flowers. The plant is a bromeliad, which means it belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and typically thrives in humid, tropical environments. It has a rosette-shaped structure that collects water in its central cup, which supports a microhabitat for small organisms.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its unique appearance and ease of care.
