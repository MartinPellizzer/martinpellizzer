Aeschynomene selloi

Aeschynomene selloi, commonly known as Sello's sesban, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellow flowers. The plant is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in arid environments due to its deep root system.
