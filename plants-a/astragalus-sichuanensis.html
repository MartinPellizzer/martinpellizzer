Astragalus sichuanensis

Astragalus sichuanensis, commonly known as Sichuan milkvetch, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Sichuan, China.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant is adapted to high-altitude environments and thrives in well-drained soils. It plays a role in soil stabilization and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.

Its ecological significance is notable in alpine ecosystems.
