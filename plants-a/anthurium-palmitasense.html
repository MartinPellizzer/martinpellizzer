Anthurium palmitasense

Anthurium palmitasense, commonly known as the Palmitasense arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to Central America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are often glossy and deep green. The plant produces spadices, which are specialized inflorescences that consist of a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Anthurium palmitasense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in greenhouse settings.
