Antidesma bunius var. wallichii

Antidesma bunius var. wallichii, commonly known as the Indian star gooseberry, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, star-shaped yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, orange-red berries that are edible and often used in traditional medicine. Its leaves are opposite, elliptical, and have a slightly hairy texture.

The species is valued for its ornamental appearance and medicinal properties.
