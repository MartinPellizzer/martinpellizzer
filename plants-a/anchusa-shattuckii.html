Anchusa shattuckii

Anchusa shattuckii, commonly known as Shattuck's Anchusa, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features basal leaves that are lance-shaped and hairy, with purple or blue flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens or borders.

Its ornamental value comes from its vibrant flower colors and compact growth habit.
