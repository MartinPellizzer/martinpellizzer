Amerimnon ebenus

Amerimnon ebenus, commonly known as the African ebony tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the rainforests of Africa.

It is characterized by its dark, dense wood, which is highly valued for its strength and durability. The tree has a straight trunk and broad, compound leaves that provide shade in its natural habitat. It produces small, yellowish flowers followed by black, spherical fruits containing seeds.

The plant thrives in humid, well-drained soils and plays an important role in the ecological balance of its native environment.
