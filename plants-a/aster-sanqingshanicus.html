Aster sanqingshanicus

Aster sanqingshanicus, commonly known as the Sanqingshan aster, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions and has lance-shaped leaves with a hairy texture. Its flowers bloom in shades of purple and white, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
