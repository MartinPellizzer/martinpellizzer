Arisaema balansae

Arisaema balansae, commonly known as the Balansae arrowhead, is a herbaceous plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its unique inflorescence consisting of a spadix surrounded by a spathe. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its leaves are arrow-shaped, with a rough texture and a prominent midrib.

The flowers are unisexual, with separate male and female flowers on the spadix, a feature common in many arum species.
