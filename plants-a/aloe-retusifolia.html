Aloe retusifolia

Aloe retusifolia, commonly known as the woolly aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are covered in white, woolly hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of yellow flowers during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil.

Aloe retusifolia is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional medicinal uses.
