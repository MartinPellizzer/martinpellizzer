Anotis lancifolia

Anotis lancifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved anotis, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features slender, lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged alternately along its stem. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grassland habitats.

Its botanical characteristics make it a distinctive member of the Asteraceae family.
