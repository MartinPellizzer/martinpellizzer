Anthemis cretica f. minor

Anthemis cretica f. minor, commonly known as the lesser cretic chamomile, is a low-growing perennial herb native to southern Europe.

It features slender, branched stems and small, daisy-like yellow flowers with white ray florets. The plant has aromatic, hairy leaves that are often used in traditional herbal remedies. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, commonly found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable species for ecological and horticultural purposes.
