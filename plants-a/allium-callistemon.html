Allium callistemon

Allium callistemon, commonly known as the red onion.

It is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of Asia. The plant features grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and mild onion scent.

Allium callistemon prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens and borders.
