Acaciella Painteri Var. Houghii

Acaciella painteri var. houghii is a rare and striking plant native to the arid regions of South Africa. Known for its vibrant yellow flowers and unique foliage, it captivates botanists and gardeners alike. This succulent thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a hardy addition to xeriscapes. Its compact growth habit and ornamental appeal make it a favorite among collectors. Despite its beauty, it remains a relatively obscure species in the world of horticulture.
