Ardisia thorelii var. latifolia

Ardisia thorelii var. latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf ardisia, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are broader than those of the typical species. The plant produces small, white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by red, fleshy berries that are attractive to birds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.
