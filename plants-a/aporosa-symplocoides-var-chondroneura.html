Aporosa symplocoides var. chondroneura

Aporosa symplocoides var. chondroneura, commonly known as the purple-leafed aporosa, is a deciduous shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive dark purple to almost black leaves, which provide striking visual contrast. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers in clusters during the spring. Its fruits are small, capsule-like structures that contain numerous tiny seeds.

This species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and adaptability to various soil types.
