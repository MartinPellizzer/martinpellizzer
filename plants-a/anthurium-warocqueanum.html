Anthurium warocqueanum

Anthurium warocqueanum, commonly known as the golden star anthurium, is a striking tropical plant native to Colombia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, spatulate-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a waxy texture. The plant produces colorful, spadix-like inflorescences surrounded by a broad, showy spathe, often in shades of yellow or orange. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Anthurium warocqueanum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
