Aloe × perfectior

Aloe × perfectior, commonly known as the hybrid aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is a hybrid between Aloe arborescens and Aloe ferox, resulting in a plant with unique characteristics. The leaves are fleshy, spiny, and often have a reddish hue, especially when exposed to full sun. It produces bright yellow flowers in clusters during the summer months.

This plant is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it suitable for xeriscaping and rock gardens.
