Amaryllis staminea

Amaryllis staminea, commonly known as the white amaryllis, is a striking ornamental plant native to South Africa.

It features large, showy white flowers with a distinctive greenish hue at the base, often arranged in a raceme. The plant has strap-like, dark green leaves that are broad and lance-shaped. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Amaryllis staminea is prized for its elegant appearance and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
