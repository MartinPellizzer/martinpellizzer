Aloe edulis

Aloe edulis, commonly known as the edible aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The gel inside the leaves is rich in mucilage and has been used for its soothing properties. It is also valued for its potential medicinal and culinary uses.

However, it is important to note that not all aloe species are safe for consumption, and proper identification is crucial.
