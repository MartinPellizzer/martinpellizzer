Anotis lanceolata

Anotis lanceolata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved anotis, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in grassy areas and along roadsides. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, pale yellow flowers arranged in spikes. It is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types and is often found in disturbed habitats.

The species is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphology and ecological adaptability.
