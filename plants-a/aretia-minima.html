Aretia minima

Aretia minima, commonly known as the dwarf aretia, is a small flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows to a height of less than 10 centimeters, with narrow, grass-like leaves and small yellow flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments.

Due to its compact size and ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
