Aeschynomene versicolor

Aeschynomene versicolor, commonly known as the variable legume, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is a member of the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and agricultural fields.
