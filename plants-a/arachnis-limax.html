Arachnis limax

Arachnis limax, commonly known as the velvet tamarind, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant produces woody pods that contain edible seeds, which are often used in traditional medicine. Its botanical features include a fibrous root system and a sprawling growth habit.

Arachnis limax is valued for its ornamental appeal and medicinal properties.
