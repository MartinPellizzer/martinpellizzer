Atriplex hastata var. salina

Atriplex hastata var. salina, commonly known as saltbush, is a halophytic shrub adapted to saline environments.

It possesses succulent leaves that help reduce water loss in arid conditions. The plant thrives in coastal areas and salt-affected soils due to its tolerance for high salt concentrations. Its small, greenish flowers are clustered in dense inflorescences.

Atriplex hastata var. salina plays a vital role in stabilizing soil and preventing erosion in saline habitats.
