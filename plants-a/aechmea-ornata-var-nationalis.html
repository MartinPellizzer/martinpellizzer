Aechmea ornata var. nationalis

Aechmea ornata var. nationalis, commonly known as the Christmas star, is a striking bromeliad native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its rosette of broad, fleshy leaves that are often brightly colored, ranging from green to red or orange. The plant produces a striking inflorescence with vibrant red flowers that bloom during the holiday season. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This species is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
