Asplenium adiantoides var. minus

Asplenium adiantoides var. minus, commonly known as the small maidenhair fern, is a delicate and compact fern species native to parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its finely divided, feathery fronds that resemble those of the common maidenhair fern. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its small size and elegant appearance make it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.
