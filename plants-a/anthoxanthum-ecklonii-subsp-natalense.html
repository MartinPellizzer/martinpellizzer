Anthoxanthum ecklonii subsp. natalense

Anthoxanthum ecklonii subsp.

natalense, commonly known as Natal grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa. It typically grows in grassland and coastal areas, thriving in well-drained soils.

The plant has slender, wiry stems and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. Its leaves are narrow and have a slightly hairy surface, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often used in landscaping and restoration projects.
