Asystasia stenosiphon

Asystasia stenosiphon, commonly known as narrow-lipped asystasia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, tubular flowers in clusters. Its flowers are usually pink or purple, with a distinctive narrow lip.

Asystasia stenosiphon is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.
