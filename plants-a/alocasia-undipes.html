Alocasia undipes

Alocasia undipes, commonly known as the velvet leaf alocasia, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are velvety to the touch, with a deep green color and intricate veins. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, rich soil to flourish. Its foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and as a decorative plant in tropical landscapes.

Alocasia undipes is also valued for its ability to purify indoor air, making it both aesthetically pleasing and beneficial.
