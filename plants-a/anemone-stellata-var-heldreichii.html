Anemone stellata var. heldreichii

Anemone stellata var. heldreichii, commonly known as the Heldreich's star anemone, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Balkan Peninsula.

It features showy, white to pale pink flowers with five to seven petal-like sepals that radiate from a central cluster. The plant typically grows in rocky or limestone soils, often in mountainous regions. Its foliage consists of palmately divided leaves with a coarse texture.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in partial shade.
