Athyriopsis angustifolia

Athyriopsis angustifolia, commonly known as narrow-leaf water fern, is a species of fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Athyriaceae and is characterized by its delicate, feather-like fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests or wetlands. Its rhizomes are often submerged or partially submerged in water, aiding in nutrient absorption.

The leaves are usually slender and have a distinctive, intricate structure that contributes to its ornamental value.
