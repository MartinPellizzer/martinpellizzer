Arbulocarpus somalensis

Arbulocarpus somalensis, commonly known as the Somali tamarind tree, is a flowering plant native to the Horn of Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, showcasing adaptations to drought conditions. Its flowers are typically small and clustered, while the seeds are enclosed in woody pods.
