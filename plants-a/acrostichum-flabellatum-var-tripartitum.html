Acrostichum flabellatum var. tripartitum

Acrostichum flabellatum var. tripartitum, commonly known as the tripartite palm fern, is a tropical fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, pinnately compound fronds that are divided into three distinct sections, giving it its name. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense colonies.

The fronds are typically bright green and can reach lengths of up to two meters, making it a striking addition to tropical gardens.
