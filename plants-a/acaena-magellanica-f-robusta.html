Acaena magellanica f. robusta

Acaena magellanica f. robusta, commonly known as the robust acaena, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Chile and Argentina.

It belongs to the family Rosaceae and is characterized by its dense, thorny growth habit. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are typically clustered in spherical inflorescences. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a serrated edge.
