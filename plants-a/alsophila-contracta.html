Alsophila contracta

Alsophila contracta, commonly known as the narrow-leaf tree fern, is a species native to Southeast Asia and parts of the Pacific.

It is characterized by its slender, upright trunk and a crown of pinnate, dark green fronds. The plant belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is known for its slow growth and long lifespan. Its fronds are typically narrow and have a leathery texture, contributing to its ornamental value.

Alsophila contracta prefers humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.
