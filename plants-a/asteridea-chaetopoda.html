Asteridea chaetopoda

Asteridea chaetopoda, commonly known as the hairy aster, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its distinctive hairy stems and leaves, which provide a unique texture. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically purple or blue in color. Native to temperate regions, it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun.

Asteridea chaetopoda is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
