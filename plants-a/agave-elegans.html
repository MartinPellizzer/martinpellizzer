Agave elegans

Agave elegans, commonly known as the silver leaf agave, is a succulent plant native to Mexico.

It features rosettes of narrow, silver-gray leaves that are covered in fine, white hairs. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellowish-green flowers during its flowering stage. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for xeriscaping and rock gardens.
