Anthemis carpatica f. alibotusensis

Anthemis carpatica f. alibotusensis, commonly known as the Alibotusensis anthemis, is a flowering plant native to specific regions of Eastern Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of both ray and disk florets. The plant typically features aromatic, yellow-orange flower heads that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are usually narrow and slightly hairy, with a strong, sometimes bitter scent.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its visual appeal and ecological benefits.
