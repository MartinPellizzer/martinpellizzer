Agave heteracantha var. univittata

Agave heteracantha var. univittata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved agave, is a succulent plant native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its narrow, spiny leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces a tall, branched inflorescence with yellowish-green flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for arid and semi-arid climates.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and resilience in harsh environments.
