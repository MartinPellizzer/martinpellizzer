Armeria elongata var. sibirica

Armeria elongata var. sibirica, commonly known as the Siberian thrift, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the colder regions of Asia.

It features dense clusters of small, pink or white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and a compact growth habit, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a border plant. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, preferring cooler climates.

Its hardy nature allows it to survive in harsh environments, including alpine and tundra regions.
