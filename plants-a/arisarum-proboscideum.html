Arisarum proboscideum

Arisarum proboscideum, commonly known as the giant arrowhead, is a striking plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its unique inflorescence composed of a spadix and a spathe. The plant features large, arrow-shaped leaves that can grow up to several feet in length, giving it its common name. Its flowers are typically green or white, with a central spadix that produces small, inconspicuous blooms.

Arisarum proboscideum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and can thrive in moist, shady environments.
