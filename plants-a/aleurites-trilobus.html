Aleurites trilobus

Aleurites trilobus, commonly known as the three-lobed nut tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized tree that produces distinctive fruits with three lobes, each containing a single seed. The leaves are alternate, simple, and ovate with a pointed tip. The tree is valued for its hard, durable wood and its seeds, which are used in traditional medicine.

It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in lowland forests.
