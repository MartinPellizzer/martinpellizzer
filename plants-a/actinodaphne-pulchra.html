Actinodaphne pulchra

Actinodaphne pulchra, commonly known as the fragrant laurel, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, including parts of India, Malaysia, and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruit is a small, smooth, yellowish drupe that matures in late summer.
