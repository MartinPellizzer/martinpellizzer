Amarantesia

Amarantesia, commonly known as red amaranth, is a flowering plant belonging to the Amaranthaceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant, feather-like leaves and colorful, cone-shaped flowers that range from red to orange. The plant is native to the Americas and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and nutritional content. It thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Amarantesia is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
