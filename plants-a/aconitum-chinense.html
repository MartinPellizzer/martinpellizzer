Aconitum chinense

Aconitum chinense, commonly known as Chinese aconite, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It features striking blue to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has dark green, palmately lobed leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Due to its toxic alkaloids, it is considered a dangerous plant and should be handled with care.
