Alternanthera pycnantha

Alternanthera pycnantha, commonly known as the red-stemmed Alternanthera, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking red stems and lance-shaped leaves that often display a vibrant red or purple hue. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow, clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and used in water features.
