Aleuritia subularia

Aleuritia subularia, commonly known as the Chinese snowberry, is a deciduous shrub native to East Asia.

It features small, white, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant produces bright red berries that are attractive to birds. It has opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges.

Aleuritia subularia is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
