Alvesia cylindricalyx

Alvesia cylindricalyx, commonly known as the cylindrical-leaved alvesia, is a unique species belonging to the Alvesiaceae family.

It is characterized by its distinctive cylindrical leaves that are arranged spirally along the stem. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences at the base of the leaves. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its botanical features make it a subject of interest for botanists studying plant morphology and adaptation.
