Aeschynanthus tenuis

Aeschynanthus tenuis, commonly known as the slender flame vine, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and clusters of small, tubular, brightly colored flowers that range from red to orange. The plant produces fleshy, elongated leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Aeschynanthus tenuis is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its vibrant floral display.
