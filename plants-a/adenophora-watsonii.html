Adenophora watsonii

Adenophora watsonii, commonly known as the Chinese bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to China.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped, pale blue to lavender flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream sides. Its leaves are lanceolate and covered with fine hairs, while its stems are upright and branching.
