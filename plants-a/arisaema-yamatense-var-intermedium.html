Arisaema yamatense var. intermedium

Arisaema yamatense var. intermedium, commonly known as the Yamato arrowroot, is a herbaceous plant native to Japan.

It features a short, rhizomatous stem and possesses distinctive, arrowhead-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a silvery underside. The plant produces a unique spadix surrounded by a spathe, characteristic of the Araceae family. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value and cultural significance make it a notable species in botanical gardens and horticultural collections.
