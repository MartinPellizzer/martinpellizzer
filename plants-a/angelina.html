Angelina

Angelina, commonly known as *Angelina* or *Angelina japonica*, is a species of flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is a deciduous shrub that produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant is characterized by its fine, needle-like leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it suitable for gardens and landscapes.

Angelina is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
