Arisaema omeiense

Arisaema omeiense, commonly known as the Omei Mountain arisaema, is a flowering plant native to the Sichuan province of China.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its distinctive spadix and spathe, which are typical of the genus Arisaema. The plant produces bright yellow spadices surrounded by a greenish-white spathe, making it visually striking. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has cultural significance in traditional Chinese medicine.
