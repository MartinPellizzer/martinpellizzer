Albizia rotundata

Albizia rotundata, commonly known as the African wisteria, is a leguminous shrub or small tree native to Africa.

It is characterized by its compound leaves, which are usually pinnate with rounded leaflets. The plant produces small, fragrant, pea-like flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Albizia rotundata has a deep root system that helps stabilize soil and prevent erosion.

It is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
