Abelia buchwaldii

Abelia buchwaldii, commonly known as the Chinese abelia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features showy, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink, often appearing in clusters. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained soil.

Abelia buchwaldii is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.

Abelia buchwaldii is primarily native to China, specifically the regions of Yunnan and Sichuan.

Abelia buchwaldii is known for its fragrant, tubular flowers that bloom in the summer months.
