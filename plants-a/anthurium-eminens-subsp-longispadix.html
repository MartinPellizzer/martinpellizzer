Anthurium eminens subsp. longispadix

Anthurium eminens subsp.

longispadix, commonly known as the long-spadix anthurium, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America. It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy surface.

The plant produces an unusual inflorescence consisting of a spadix surrounded by a showy spathe, which can be either white or reddish in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires consistent moisture and high humidity to flourish.

This species is popular among indoor gardeners due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
