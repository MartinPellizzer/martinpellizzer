Alantsilodendron

Alantsilodendron, commonly known as the golden flame tree, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, fiery orange to red flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. The plant has a tall, straight trunk with smooth, greyish bark and can grow up to 20 meters in height. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a glossy, dark green surface.

Alantsilodendron is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and public spaces due to its vibrant floral display.
