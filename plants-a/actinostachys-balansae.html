Actinostachys balansae

Actinostachys balansae, commonly known as the Balansae clubmoss, is a species of spore-bearing plant belonging to the family Lycopodiales.

It is characterized by its slender, creeping stems that produce small, scale-like leaves. The plant forms dense mats and is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors. Its reproductive structures, called strobili, contain microspores and megaspores, typical of lycophytes.

Actinostachys balansae plays a role in soil stabilization and provides habitat for various microorganisms and invertebrates.
