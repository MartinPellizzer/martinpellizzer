Arenaria tetraquetra var. pseudoquerioides

Arenaria tetraquetra var. pseudoquerioides, commonly known as the sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to arid and rocky environments.

It features small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink, blooming in early spring. The plant has succulent, fleshy leaves that help it retain moisture in dry conditions. It is often found in sandy or gravelly soils, thriving in full sun.

Its compact form and drought tolerance make it a hardy addition to rock gardens and xeriscapes.
