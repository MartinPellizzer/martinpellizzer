Arisaema atrorubens var. zebrinum

Arisaema atrorubens var. zebrinum, commonly known as the Zebra Arum, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and a distinctive, spadix-shaped inflorescence surrounded by a colorful spathe. The plant's unique morphology includes a reddish-brown stem with contrasting white and purple markings, giving it its zebra-like appearance. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The spathe and spadix are not only visually appealing but also play a key role in the plant's reproductive strategy.
