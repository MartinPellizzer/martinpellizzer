Agrostis collina

Agrostis collina, commonly known as slender bentgrass, is a perennial grass species native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It typically grows in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in meadows, pastures, and along stream banks. The plant has thin, wiry stems and small, spike-like inflorescences that produce numerous tiny flowers. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green coloration.

Agrostis collina is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping or as a turfgrass.
