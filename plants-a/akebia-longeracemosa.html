Akebia longeracemosa

Akebia longeracemosa, commonly known as the Japanese angel's trumpet, is a deciduous climbing vine native to East Asia.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, orange-red flowers in early summer, which are followed by long, narrow, yellowish fruits. Its stems are woody and can grow up to several meters in length, often climbing or trailing along supports.

Akebia longeracemosa is valued in horticulture for its ornamental flowers and adaptability to various soil types.
