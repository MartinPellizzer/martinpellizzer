Alyssum novum

Alyssum novum, commonly known as the New Zealand alyssum, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. The plant features small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale purple, arranged in dense clusters. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often covered in fine hairs.

Alyssum novum thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal or alpine regions.
