Asyneuma linifolium subsp. eximium

Asyneuma linifolium subsp.

eximium, commonly known as the slender leaf mistletoe, is a hemiparasitic plant that grows on the branches of various host trees. It has small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

The plant produces small, white or pale green flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its fruits are small, fleshy berries that contain seeds dispersed by birds.

This subspecies is native to parts of South America and is adapted to thrive in humid, tropical environments.
