Aster yunnanensis var. angustior

Aster yunnanensis var. angustior, commonly known as the narrowleaf yunnan aster, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its slender, lance-shaped leaves and showy blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions and thrives in cool, moist environments. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.

Its botanical features make it a unique addition to both horticultural collections and ecological studies.
