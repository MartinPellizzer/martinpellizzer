Asterostigma lineolatum

Asterostigma lineolatum, commonly known as the zigzag aster, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its distinctive, zigzag-shaped leaves and clusters of small, daisy-like yellow flowers. Native to South America, this plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full sunlight. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

The plant is also known for its medicinal properties in traditional medicine.
