Allionia glabra var. recedens

Allionia glabra var. recedens, commonly known as the smooth allionia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and clusters of small, white to pale pink flowers. The leaves are opposite, simple, and often have a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate blooms.
