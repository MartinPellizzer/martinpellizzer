Artemisia glomerata

Artemisia glomerata, commonly known as silver bluestem, is a perennial herb native to North America.

It is characterized by its silver-gray, woolly foliage and small, blue-green flowers that appear in dense clusters. The plant thrives in dry, open habitats such as prairies and rocky areas. Its aromatic leaves contain volatile oils, which contribute to its distinctive scent and medicinal properties.

Artemisia glomerata is often used in traditional herbal medicine and as a ornamental plant in gardens.
