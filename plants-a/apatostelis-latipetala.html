Apatostelis latipetala

Apatostelis latipetala, commonly known as the broad-petalled orchid, is a species of orchid native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its distinctive, broad, and often pale green or white petals. The plant typically grows in damp, shaded environments such as forest floors or along stream banks. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, with a unique structure that includes a prominent lip.

Apatostelis latipetala is a rare and endangered species, highlighting the importance of conservation efforts in its native habitat.
