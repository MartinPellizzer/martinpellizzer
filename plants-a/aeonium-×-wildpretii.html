Aeonium × wildpretii

Aeonium × wildpretii, commonly known as the "Crimson Fire" or "Red Aeonium," is a hybrid succulent plant native to the Canary Islands.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically red or pink in color, with a velvety texture. The plant produces tall, branched flowering stems that bear yellow flowers in the spring. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Aeonium × wildpretii is popular in rock gardens and as a container plant due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
