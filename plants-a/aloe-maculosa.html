Aloe maculosa

Aloe maculosa, commonly known as the spotted aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, spiky leaves that are typically green with distinctive white or yellow spots. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow flowers, which bloom in the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Aloe maculosa is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
