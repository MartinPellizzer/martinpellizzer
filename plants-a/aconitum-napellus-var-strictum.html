Aconitum napellus var. strictum

Aconitum napellus var. strictum, commonly known as the European monkshood, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It features striking blue to purple hooded flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has dark green, heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive veining pattern. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodlands and stream banks.

However, it is highly toxic and should be handled with caution.
