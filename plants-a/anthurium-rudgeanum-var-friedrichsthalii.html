Anthurium rudgeanum var. friedrichsthalii

Anthurium rudgeanum var. friedrichsthalii, commonly known as the Friedrichsthal's anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-like inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, often in shades of red, orange, or yellow. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.

Its unique foliage and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for indoor and greenhouse cultivation.
