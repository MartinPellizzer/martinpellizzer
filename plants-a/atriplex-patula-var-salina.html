Atriplex patula var. salina

Atriplex patula var. salina, commonly known as saltbush, is a shrub native to arid and saline environments.

It is characterized by its succulent, gray-green leaves that are adapted to reduce water loss. The plant thrives in soils with high salt content, making it a valuable species for reclaiming saline land. Its small, greenish flowers are typically clustered in dense inflorescences.

Atriplex patula var. salina plays an important ecological role in stabilizing soils and providing habitat for various wildlife.
