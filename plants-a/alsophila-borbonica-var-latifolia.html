Alsophila borbonica var. latifolia

Alsophila borbonica var. latifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaf tree fern, is a species native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feathery fronds that can reach several meters in length. The plant has a robust rhizome system that allows it to thrive in humid, shaded environments. Its fronds are typically bipinnate, with a distinctive, elegant appearance.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
