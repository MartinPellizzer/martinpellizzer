Anthurium kittredgeanum

Anthurium kittredgeanum, commonly known as the Kittredge's anthurium, is a tropical flowering plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped leaves and striking, spadix-like inflorescence. The plant thrives in high humidity and warm temperatures, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation. Its unique foliage and exotic flowers make it a sought-after ornamental plant.

Anthurium kittredgeanum requires bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to flourish.
