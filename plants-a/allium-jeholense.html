Allium jeholense

Allium jeholense, commonly known as the Jehol garlic, is a perennial bulbous plant native to China.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its tall, grass-like leaves and clusters of white or pale pink flowers. The plant produces small, bulbous structures that are often used in traditional Chinese medicine. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Allium jeholense is valued for its ornamental qualities and medicinal properties.
