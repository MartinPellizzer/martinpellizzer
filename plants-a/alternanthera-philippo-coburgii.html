Alternanthera philippo-coburgii

Alternanthera philippo-coburgii, commonly known as the red-leaf alternanthera, is a striking aquatic plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red or pink foliage, which becomes more intense under strong light conditions. This plant thrives in shallow, warm waters and prefers moderate to high water flow. Its leaves are opposite and lance-shaped, often with a waxy texture.

It is a popular choice for aquariums due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
