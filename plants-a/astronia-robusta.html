Astronia robusta

Astronia robusta, commonly known as the broadleaf hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa, Asia, and Australia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to red. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its flowers bloom throughout the year, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Astronia robusta is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant foliage and blooms.
