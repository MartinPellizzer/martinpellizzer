Aristea caerulea var. robusta

Aristea caerulea var. robusta, commonly known as the blue carpet grass, is a low-growing perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features dense, mat-forming foliage with narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically blue-gray in color. The plant produces small, clustered blue to purple flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a ground cover.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for drought-tolerant landscaping.
