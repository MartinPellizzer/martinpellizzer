Adenocarpus Aureus

Adenocarpus aureus is a small, flowering plant native to southern Africa. It is known for its bright yellow flowers and compact growth habit. This species belongs to the family Leguminosae, also known as the bean or pea family. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats. Its unique appearance and ecological significance make it an interesting subject for botanical study.
