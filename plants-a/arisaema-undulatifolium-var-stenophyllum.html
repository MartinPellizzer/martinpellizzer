Arisaema undulatifolium var. stenophyllum

Arisaema undulatifolium var. stenophyllum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf arrowhead, is a member of the Araceae family.

It is characterized by its distinctive, undulating, arrow-shaped leaves that are typically narrow and elongated. The plant produces a spadix surrounded by a spathe, which is a common feature in the arum family. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is native to parts of Southeast Asia.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for woodland gardens and shady landscapes.
