Abies jezoensis

Abies jezoensis, commonly known as the Korean fir, is a coniferous evergreen tree native to the Korean Peninsula and parts of northeastern China.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and dense, pyramidal crown. The leaves are needle-like, dark green, and arranged in spirals along the branches. It produces cones that are typically red-brown in color and contain seeds.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in horticultural settings.
