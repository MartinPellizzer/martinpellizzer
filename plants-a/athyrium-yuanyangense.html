Athyrium yuanyangense

Athyrium yuanyangense, commonly known as the Yuyang fern, is a species of fern native to China.

It belongs to the family Athyriaceae and is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its spores are produced on the underside of the fronds, and it reproduces through sexual reproduction.

The Yuyang fern is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
