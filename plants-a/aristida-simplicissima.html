Aristida simplicissima

Aristida simplicissima, commonly known as little bluestem, is a perennial grass native to North America.

It is characterized by its blue-green foliage and dense, tufted growth habit. The plant produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences that are typically green to brown in color. It thrives in a variety of soil types and is drought-tolerant, making it a common species in prairie ecosystems.

Its botanical features make it valuable for both ecological restoration and ornamental gardening.
