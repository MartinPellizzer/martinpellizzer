Aristolochia melastoma

Aristolochia melastoma, commonly known as the Chinese birthwort, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a member of the Aristolochiaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, tubular flowers that resemble a heart shape. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are often clustered in umbels. Its leaves are heart-shaped and have a rough texture, while the stems are hairy and branched.

The plant contains various bioactive compounds, some of which have been studied for their medicinal properties.
