Astrocasia neurocarpa

Astrocasia neurocarpa, commonly known as the star-leaf tree, is a tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, palmate leaves that resemble a star, with five to seven lobes. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release seeds.

Astrocasia neurocarpa is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in local medicine.
