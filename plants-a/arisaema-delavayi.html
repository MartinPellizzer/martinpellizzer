Arisaema delavayi

Arisaema delavayi, commonly known as the Delavay arrowhead, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of southern China.

It is characterized by its distinctive arrow-shaped leaves and a spadix surrounded by a broad, hood-like spathe. The plant produces small, white to greenish flowers that are typically borne on a long, narrow stalk. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forested areas at higher elevations.

Due to its unique morphology and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists alike.
