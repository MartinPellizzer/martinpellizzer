Aloe × commutata var. tricolor

Aloe × commutata var. tricolor, commonly known as the Tricolor Aloe, is a hybrid succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features striking, triangular leaves that are typically green with red and yellow variegation along the edges. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with orange to yellow flowers, which bloom in late spring or early summer. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
