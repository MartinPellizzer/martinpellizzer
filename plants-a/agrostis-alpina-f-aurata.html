Agrostis alpina f. aurata

Agrostis alpina f. aurata, commonly known as the alpine bentgrass variegated form, is a variant of the alpine bentgrass species.

It is a low-growing perennial grass that typically thrives in cool, moist environments. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves with a distinctive variegated coloration, often displaying shades of green and yellow. It is native to mountainous regions of Europe and Asia, where it grows in rocky or alpine habitats.

This ornamental grass is valued for its aesthetic appeal and ecological role in stabilizing soil in harsh environments.
