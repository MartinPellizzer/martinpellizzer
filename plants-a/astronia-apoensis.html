Astronia apoensis

Astronia apoensis, commonly known as the Philippine star orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to tree bark in tropical rainforests. Its flowers are usually pale yellow to white with a distinctive central dark spot.

Astronia apoensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for horticultural purposes.
