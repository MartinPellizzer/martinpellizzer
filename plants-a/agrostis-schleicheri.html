Agrostis schleicheri

Agrostis schleicheri, commonly known as Schleicher's bentgrass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and along riverbanks. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in landscaping.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species in many regions.
