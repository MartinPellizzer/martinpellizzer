Anthericum roseum

Anthericum roseum, commonly known as the pink lily or rose lily, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its striking, pinkish-white spatulate leaves and showy, spadix-like flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream sides. Its rhizomatous root system allows it to spread and form dense colonies.

Anthericum roseum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its elegant appearance.
