Acalypha communis var. brevipetiolata

Acalypha communis var. brevipetiolata, commonly known as the broad-leaved iron plant, is a shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features large, heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive reddish or purple underside, which adds to its ornamental value. The plant produces small, clustered greenish-white flowers that are followed by woolly, spiky seed heads. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires partial to full sunlight.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for garden landscapes and container planting.
