Agrostis alba var. minor

Agrostis alba var. minor, commonly known as slender meadow grass, is a perennial grass species native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

It typically grows in open habitats such as meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils. The plant has slender, hairless stems and narrow, grass-like leaves that are often bluish-green in color. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences with numerous tiny flowers.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping or as a component in native plant gardens.
