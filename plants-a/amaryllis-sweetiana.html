Amaryllis sweetiana

Amaryllis sweetiana, commonly known as the sweet-scented amaryllis, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that come in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant produces strap-shaped leaves that are broad and often variegated. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate to bright light.

Amaryllis sweetiana is popular as a ornamental plant due to its striking blooms and pleasant fragrance.
