Arachnorchis uliginosa

Arachnorchis uliginosa, commonly known as the bog orchid, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the wetlands of southeastern Australia.

The plant thrives in acidic, moist soils and is often found in swampy or heathland environments. Its unique morphology includes a single, narrow leaf and a cluster of tiny, spurred flowers that bloom in late summer.
