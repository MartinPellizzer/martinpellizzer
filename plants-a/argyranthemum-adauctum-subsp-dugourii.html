Argyranthemum adauctum subsp. dugourii

Argyranthemum adauctum subsp.

dugourii, commonly known as the African marguerite, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its dense, fluffy white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters.

The plant has a compact, bushy growth habit with narrow, gray-green leaves. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil.

This species is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive appearance and ability to attract pollinators.
