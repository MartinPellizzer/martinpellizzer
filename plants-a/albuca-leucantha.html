Albuca leucantha

Albuca leucantha, commonly known as the white-leaved albuca, is a perennial bulbous plant native to southern Africa.

It features slender, grass-like leaves that are typically light green to white in color, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Albuca leucantha is relatively low maintenance and drought-tolerant once established.
