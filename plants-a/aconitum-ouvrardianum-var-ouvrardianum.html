Aconitum ouvrardianum var. ouvrardianum

Aconitum ouvrardianum var. ouvrardianum, commonly known as the Chinese aconite, is a member of the Ranunculaceae family.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces striking blue to purple spires of flowers. The plant features large, palmately compound leaves with a rough texture. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Native to China, it is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine.
