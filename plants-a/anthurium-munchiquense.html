Anthurium munchiquense

Anthurium munchiquense, commonly known as the Munchique arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to Colombia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves with a glossy surface. The plant produces an inflorescence consisting of a spadix surrounded by a spathe, typical of the genus Anthurium. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and botanical collections.
