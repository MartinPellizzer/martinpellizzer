Acinos multiflorus

Acinos multiflorus, commonly known as the Sierra starflower, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a low-growing perennial with small, star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of white and yellow. The plant has hairy, lance-shaped leaves and is often found in rocky or alpine habitats. It is adapted to dry, sunny conditions and produces numerous small, showy blooms.

The Sierra starflower plays a role in supporting local pollinators and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
