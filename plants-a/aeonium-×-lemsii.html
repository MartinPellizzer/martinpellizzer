Aeonium × lemsii

Aeonium × lemsii, commonly known as the Ghost Plant, is a hybrid succulent native to the Canary Islands.

It features rosettes of fleshy, blue-green leaves that are often tinged with red or purple at the edges. The plant produces striking pink or red flowers on tall, branching stems during the spring. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with plenty of sunlight.

Aeonium × lemsii is popular in rock gardens and as a container plant due to its ornamental appearance and low maintenance requirements.
