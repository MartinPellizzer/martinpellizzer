Aristida jucunda

Aristida jucunda, commonly known as slender dropseed, is a grass species native to North America.

It is characterized by its fine, hair-like leaves and compact, cylindrical seed heads. This plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as prairies and disturbed soils. It is well adapted to arid conditions and can thrive with minimal water.

Its delicate appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.
