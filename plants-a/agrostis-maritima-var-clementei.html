Agrostis maritima var. clementei

Agrostis maritima var. clementei, commonly known as the Clement's coastal grass, is a variant of the coastal grass species native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing, perennial grass that thrives in sandy or gravelly soils along coastal areas. The plant typically has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It is well adapted to salt spray and wind exposure, making it a hardy species in maritime environments.

Its ecological importance includes stabilizing dunes and providing habitat for various coastal organisms.
