Armeria maritima subsp. azorica

Armeria maritima subsp.

azorica, commonly known as Azorean thrift, is a flowering plant native to the Azores and Madeira islands. It is a low-growing, evergreen perennial that produces dense clusters of white or pink flowers.

The plant thrives in coastal environments, tolerating saline and well-drained soils. Its foliage consists of linear, gray-green leaves that are often covered in a fine, powdery coating.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to stabilize coastal soils.
