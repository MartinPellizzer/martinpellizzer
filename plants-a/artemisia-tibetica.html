Artemisia tibetica

Artemisia tibetica, commonly known as Tibetan wormwood, is a perennial shrub native to the high altitudes of the Tibetan Plateau.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which consists of aromatic, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in summer, typical of the Asteraceae family. It thrives in cold, arid environments and is often found in alpine meadows and rocky slopes.

Artemisia tibetica is valued for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional Tibetan medicine for various ailments.
