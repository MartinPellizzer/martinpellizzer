Anthurium alegrense

Anthurium alegrense, commonly known as the golden anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a metallic sheen. The plant produces vibrant red spadices, which are the modified leaves that enclose the true flowers. Anthurium alegrense thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and botanical collections.
