Alsine tenuissima

Alsine tenuissima, commonly known as slender sandwort, is a small annual herbaceous plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of North America.

It typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and is adapted to thrive in drought conditions. The plant has small, linear leaves and produces clusters of tiny white or pink flowers. It is often found in disturbed areas such as roadsides and fields.

Its botanical features make it a characteristic species of xeric environments.
