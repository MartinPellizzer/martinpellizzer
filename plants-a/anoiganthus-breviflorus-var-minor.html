Anoiganthus breviflorus var. minor

Anoiganthus breviflorus var. minor, commonly known as the small-flowered anoiganthus, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the southeastern United States.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and swampy areas. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, white to pale pink flowers in clusters. Its botanical characteristics include a fibrous root system and a short, upright stem.

It is often found in wetland habitats and is valued for its ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
