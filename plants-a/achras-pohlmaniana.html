Achras pohlmaniana

Achras pohlmaniana, commonly known as the African milk tree, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height. The plant produces yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences, and its leaves are palmately lobed with a distinctive appearance. The bark is thick and corky, and the plant is known for its milky latex sap.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, rocky habitats.
