Anthurium chlorostachyum

Anthurium chlorostachyum, commonly known as the silver-stem anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a silvery or white midrib. The plant produces bright red spadices, which are the spiky, club-shaped structures that house the true flowers. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.

Anthurium chlorostachyum is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
