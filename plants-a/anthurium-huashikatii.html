Anthurium huashikatii

Anthurium huashikatii, commonly known as the Huashikatii arrowhead, is a striking tropical plant native to the humid lowland forests of South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive inflorescence consisting of a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe. The plant features glossy, heart-shaped leaves that are often deep green with prominent veins, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil to flourish.

Anthurium huashikatii is prized for its vibrant foliage and is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
