Arrojadoa rhodantha var. theunisseniana

Arrojadoa rhodantha var. theunisseniana, commonly known as the Theunissen's red hibiscus, is a striking flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that are typically red or pink in color, with five petals and a prominent central stigma. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green, providing a striking contrast to its vibrant blooms. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and floral arrangements.
