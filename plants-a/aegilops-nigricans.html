Aegilops nigricans

Aegilops nigricans, commonly known as black aegilops, is a perennial grass native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its dark green, narrow leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry soils and is adapted to arid environments. Its seeds are often used in traditional herbal medicine for their purported health benefits.

Aegilops nigricans is also valued in ecological restoration projects due to its resilience and ability to stabilize soil.
