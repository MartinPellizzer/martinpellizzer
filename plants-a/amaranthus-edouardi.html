Amaranthus edouardi

Amaranthus edouardi, commonly known as the red amaranth, is a species of flowering plant in the amaranth family.

It is characterized by its vibrant red bracts that surround the small, inconspicuous flowers. The plant is native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas. It typically grows as an annual herb with upright stems and lance-shaped leaves.

Amaranthus edouardi is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional cuisines in some regions.
