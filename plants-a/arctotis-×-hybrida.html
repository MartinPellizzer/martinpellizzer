Arctotis × hybrida

Arctotis × hybrida, commonly known as the African daisy, is a hybrid perennial plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, daisy-like flowers that come in a variety of colors such as red, yellow, and orange. The plant typically grows to a height of 30–60 cm and produces showy inflorescences with central yellow disk flowers surrounded by ray florets.

It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
