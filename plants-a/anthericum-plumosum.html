Anthericum plumosum

Anthericum plumosum, commonly known as the plume anthericum, is a striking perennial plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It features tall, slender stems that bear clusters of soft, white, plume-like flowers, which give the plant its common name. The leaves are lance-shaped and slightly hairy, often forming a basal rosette. This plant thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodlands or along stream banks.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking a unique and delicate addition to their landscape.
