Arenaria fasciculata subsp. funkii

Arenaria fasciculata subsp.

funkii, commonly known as the sandsteppe mouse-eared cress, is a low-growing perennial plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of North America. It typically thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is adapted to harsh environmental conditions.
