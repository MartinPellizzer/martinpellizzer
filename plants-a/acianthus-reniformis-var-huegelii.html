Acianthus reniformis var. huegelii

Acianthus reniformis var. huegelii, commonly known as the Hugel's heartleaf orchid, is a unique orchid species native to specific regions of South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves, which are often covered in a dense layer of fine, white pubescence. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pale green or yellow, with a slight reddish tinge. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, acidic soil.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and delicate species, making it a subject of interest for botanists and orchid enthusiasts.
