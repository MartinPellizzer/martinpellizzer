Anthurium alienigenum

Anthurium alienigenum, commonly known as the alien giant arrowhead, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length, with a deep green color and a prominent midrib. The plant produces spadices, which are spiky, finger-like structures that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in humid, warm environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.

Anthurium alienigenum is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental foliage.
