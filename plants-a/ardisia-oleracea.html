Ardisia oleracea

Ardisia oleracea, commonly known as the edible ardisia, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, dark green leaves and produces small, red berries that are edible when ripe. The plant is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, thriving in moist, shaded environments. Its berries are used in local cuisines and traditional medicine.

The plant has a compact growth habit and is sometimes cultivated as an ornamental species.
