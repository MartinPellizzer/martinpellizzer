Aechmea × compacta

Aechmea × compacta, commonly known as the compact aechmea, is a hybrid bromeliad native to South America.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that form a central cup, which collects water and nutrients. The plant produces striking inflorescences with colorful bracts that can range from red to orange. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil.

Aechmea × compacta is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental appearance and relatively low maintenance.
