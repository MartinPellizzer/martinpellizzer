Asplenium × karasense

Asplenium × karasense, commonly known as the Karasen holly fern, is a hybrid fern species native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Aspleniaceae and is characterized by its dense, dark green, leathery fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often found in rocky or forested areas. Its sporangia are clustered in sori on the underside of the fronds, a common feature among ferns.

Asplenium × karasense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage.
