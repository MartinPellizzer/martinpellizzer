Anthurium andraeanum var. salmoneus

Anthurium andraeanum var. salmoneus, commonly known as the salmon anthurium, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, elongated, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a metallic sheen. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-shaped inflorescences that range in color from red to orange, often with a contrasting white or yellow spathe. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

This variety is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
