Arisaema macrospathum

Arisaema macrospathum, commonly known as the giant arrowhead, is a striking member of the Araceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves and distinctive spadix surrounded by a showy spathe. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its unique morphology makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening, though it requires careful cultivation due to its specific environmental needs.
