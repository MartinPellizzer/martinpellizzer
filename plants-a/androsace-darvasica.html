Androsace darvasica

It belongs to the Primulaceae family and is characterized by its compact, mat-forming growth habit. The plant features small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink, blooming in early summer. Its leaves are succulent and fleshy, adapted to thrive in arid and rocky environments.
