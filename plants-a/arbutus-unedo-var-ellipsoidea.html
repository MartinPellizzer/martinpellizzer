Arbutus unedo var. ellipsoidea

Arbutus unedo var. ellipsoidea, commonly known as the elliptical strawberry tree, is a variant of the strawberry tree native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that typically grows in rocky or coastal areas. The plant features elliptical-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green, and it produces small, white flowers in the spring. Its fruit is a red, fleshy berry that resembles a strawberry, hence the common name.

This variant is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological benefits in its native habitat.
