Ardisia pulverulenta

Ardisia pulverulenta, commonly known as the powdered ardisia, is a flowering plant species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, dark green leaves and produces clusters of white or pale yellow flowers. The plant's fruit is typically red or orange, containing small, hard seeds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Ardisia pulverulenta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
