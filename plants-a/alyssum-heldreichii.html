Alyssum heldreichii

Alyssum heldreichii, commonly known as the Balkan alyssum, is a flowering plant native to the Balkan Peninsula.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically reaches heights of 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features small, fragrant flowers that are usually white or pale pink, arranged in dense clusters. Its leaves are linear to lanceolate, with a slightly hairy texture.

Alyssum heldreichii thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable ornamental plant in gardens.
