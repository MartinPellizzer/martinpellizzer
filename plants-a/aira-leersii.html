Aira leersii

Aira leersii, commonly known as the lesser panicgrass, is a perennial grass native to parts of Asia and Africa.

It typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands. The plant has slender, erect stems and produces small, spike-like inflorescences with numerous tiny flowers. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green color.

It is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil and support biodiversity.
