Aloe ericetorum

Aloe ericetorum, commonly known as the heath aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, sword-shaped leaves that are typically green with a waxy coating. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Aloe ericetorum is well adapted to arid and rocky environments, making it a hardy choice for xeriscaping.
