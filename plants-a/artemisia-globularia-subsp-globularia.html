Artemisia globularia subsp. globularia

Artemisia globularia subsp.

globularia, commonly known as globe artemisia, is a perennial shrub native to arid and semi-arid regions of North America. It is characterized by its compact, spherical growth habit and silver-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, aromatic hairs.

The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in compact clusters during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

This subspecies is also valued for its use in traditional medicine and as a source of essential oils.
