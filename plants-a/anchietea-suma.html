Anchietea suma

Anchietea suma, commonly known as the Brazilian orchid, is a species of orchid native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, and it produces clusters of small, white or pale yellow blooms.

Anchietea suma is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
