Agrostis schimperiana

Agrostis schimperiana, commonly known as the Schimper's bentgrass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. The plant has slender, tufted shoots with narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its inflorescence consists of a single spikelet, which is usually slender and cylindrical in shape.

Agrostis schimperiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
