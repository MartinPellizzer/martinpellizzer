Adonis turkestanica var. tianschanica

Adonis turkestanica var. tianschanica, commonly known as the Tianshan Adonis, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features showy, yellow to orange flowers that bloom in early summer, typically arranged in a dense, cup-shaped inflorescence. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, and it thrives in well-drained, rocky soils. It is adapted to cold, alpine environments and is often found at high elevations.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
