Aerangis albidorubra

Aerangis albidorubra, commonly known as the red-veined orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features striking, showy flowers with pale pink petals and deep red veins, making it a popular ornamental plant. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers bloom in clusters and are characterized by a strong, sweet fragrance.

Aerangis albidorubra is known for its resilience and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
