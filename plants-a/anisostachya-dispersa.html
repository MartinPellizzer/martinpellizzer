Anisostachya dispersa

Anisostachya dispersa, commonly known as the scattered spikegrass, is a perennial grass native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and slender, branched inflorescence. The plant typically grows in grasslands and open woodlands, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, with a bluish-green hue.

Anisostachya dispersa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
