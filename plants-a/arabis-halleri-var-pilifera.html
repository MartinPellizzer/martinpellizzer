Arabis halleri var. pilifera

Arabis halleri var. pilifera, commonly known as the hairy rockcress, is a flowering plant native to central and southern Europe.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, which includes many economically important crops and weeds. This variety is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves, which help reduce water loss in dry environments.
