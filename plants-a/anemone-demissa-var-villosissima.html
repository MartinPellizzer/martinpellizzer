Anemone demissa var. villosissima

Anemone demissa var. villosissima, commonly known as the downy anemone, is a herbaceous perennial native to the western United States.

It features showy, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has fuzzy, hairy leaves that give it a distinctive, soft appearance. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for woodland gardens and native plant landscapes.
