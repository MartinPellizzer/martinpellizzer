Agrostis olympica

Agrostis olympica, commonly known as Olympic bentgrass, is a perennial grass species native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It is characterized by its fine, tufted growth habit and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows, pastures, and along streambanks. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, with a bluish-green coloration.

Agrostis olympica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping and turfgrass applications.
