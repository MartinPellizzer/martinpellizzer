Aralia elata var. mandshurica

Aralia elata var. mandshurica, commonly known as the Manchurian aralia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to eastern Asia.

It features compound leaves with a distinctive, feathery appearance, typically arranged in a palmate pattern. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers in umbel-like clusters, which are followed by bright red berries. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in moist, well-drained soils.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in horticulture for its attractive foliage and berries.
