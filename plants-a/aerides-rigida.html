Aerides rigida

Aerides rigida, commonly known as the rigid orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its erect, often branched stems and linear, grass-like leaves. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers with prominent sepals and petals. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Aerides rigida is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to cultivate in humid conditions.
