Anathallis paranaensis

Anathallis paranaensis, commonly known as the Parana orchid, is a species of orchid native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Epidendroideae subfamily and is characterized by its small, delicate flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its flowers are usually greenish-yellow with a distinct lip, and it produces small, fleshy fruits.

Anathallis paranaensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
