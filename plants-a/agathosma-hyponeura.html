Agathosma hyponeura

Agathosma hyponeura, commonly known as Cape spearmint, is a small, aromatic shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant contains volatile oils, particularly cineole, which contribute to its distinctive mint-like fragrance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in fynbos vegetation.

Due to its aromatic properties, it is sometimes used in herbal teas and traditional medicine.
