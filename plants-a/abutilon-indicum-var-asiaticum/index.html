Abutilon Indicum Var. Asiaticum

Abutilon indicum var. asiaticum is a unique and ornamental plant known for its velvety foliage and showy flowers. Native to parts of Asia, it thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal. This variant of Abutilon indicum is distinguished by its heart-shaped leaves and clusters of lantern-like blooms. It is a popular choice for gardens and indoor spaces due to its adaptability and low maintenance requirements. Its striking appearance makes it a favorite among plant enthusiasts and horticulturists alike.
