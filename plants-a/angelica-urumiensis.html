Angelica urumiensis

Angelica urumiensis, commonly known as the Uromi angelica, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of northern India and Nepal.

It belongs to the family Apiaceae, which includes many aromatic herbs and vegetables. The plant features a tall, slender stem with large, compound leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. Its flowers are small and white to greenish in color, arranged in umbels, and bloom during the summer months.

The roots of Angelica urumiensis are valued for their medicinal properties, particularly in treating respiratory and digestive ailments.
