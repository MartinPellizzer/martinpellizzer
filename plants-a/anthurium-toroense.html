Anthurium toroense

Anthurium toroense, commonly known as the Toroense Arrowhead, is a striking tropical plant native to Colombia.

It features large, heart-shaped leaves with a deep green hue and a glossy texture. The plant produces vibrant red spathes that enclose its spadix, creating a dramatic floral display. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium toroense is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and ease of care.
