Anthurium rotundistigmatum

Anthurium rotundistigmatum, commonly known as the round-stigma anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves with a vibrant green color and a distinctive red or orange midrib. The plant produces spadices, which are inflorescences that consist of a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe. It thrives in humid, warm environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.

Anthurium rotundistigmatum is popular as a decorative plant due to its unique foliage and relatively low maintenance requirements.
