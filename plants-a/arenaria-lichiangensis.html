Arenaria lichiangensis

Arenaria lichiangensis, commonly known as the Yunnan sandwort, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Yunnan, China.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its low-growing habit and succulent leaves. The plant produces small, white or pink flowers that bloom in spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to the cool, humid climates of its native habitat.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological significance, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
