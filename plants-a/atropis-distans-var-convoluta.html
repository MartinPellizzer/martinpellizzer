Atropis distans var. convoluta

Atropis distans var. convoluta, commonly known as the spiral orchid, is a rare and striking member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its unique spiral arrangement of leaves, which gives it its common name. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers with a distinctive coloration pattern. It thrives in shaded, moist environments, typically found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens for conservation and study.
