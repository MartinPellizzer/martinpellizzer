Anthurium paloraense

Anthurium paloraense, commonly known as the Paloraense arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive spathe and spadix flower structure. The plant features large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a waxy texture. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Anthurium paloraense is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated as a houseplant.
