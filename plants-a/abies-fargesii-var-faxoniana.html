Abies fargesii var. faxoniana

Abies fargesii var. faxoniana, commonly known as the Farges fir, is a species of evergreen conifer native to the mountainous regions of western China.

It is characterized by its conical shape, dark green needles, and distinctive, rounded cones. The plant typically grows in high-altitude forests, where it thrives in cool, moist climates with well-drained soil. Its foliage is dense and provides habitat for various wildlife species.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and landscapes.
