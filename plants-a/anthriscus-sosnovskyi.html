Anthriscus sosnovskyi

Anthriscus sosnovskyi, commonly known as Siberian cow parsley, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is a member of the Apiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and vegetables. The plant features finely divided, fern-like leaves and small white flowers that bloom in umbels. It thrives in rocky and alpine environments, often growing at high altitudes.
