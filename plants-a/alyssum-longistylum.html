Alyssum longistylum

Alyssum longistylum, commonly known as the longstem alyssum, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial with dense clusters of small, fragrant white or pale yellow flowers. The plant features slender, branched stems and narrow, linear leaves that are covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Alyssum longistylum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract beneficial insects like ladybugs and lacewings.
