Apium ikenoi

Apium ikenoi, commonly known as Japanese celery, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to theApiaceae family, which includes other familiar plants like carrots and parsley. The plant features hollow, ridged stems and fern-like, pinnately compound leaves. Its flowers are small and clustered in umbels, typical of the family.

Apium ikenoi is valued for its aromatic leaves and is often used in traditional Asian cuisine.
