Aeonium × barbatum

Aeonium × barbatum, commonly known as the tree aeonium or curly aeonium, is a hybrid succulent plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped growth pattern, with thick, fleshy leaves that are often adorned with distinctive red or purple edges. The plant produces tall, branching inflorescences that bear small, yellow flowers in the spring. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its leaves and stems to survive in dry conditions.

Aeonium × barbatum is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its ornamental appearance and low maintenance requirements.
