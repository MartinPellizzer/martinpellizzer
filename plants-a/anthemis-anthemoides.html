Anthemis anthemoides

Anthemis anthemoides, commonly known as the golden marguerite, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

The plant produces small, yellow to orange flowers that are surrounded by white ray florets, creating a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Anthemis anthemoides is also known for its aromatic properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
