Agathosma dregeana

Agathosma dregeana, commonly known as Cape spearmint, is a herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its aromatic, finely divided leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family and is often found in rocky, well-drained soils. Its essential oils, rich in monoterpenes, are widely used in aromatherapy and natural medicine.

Agathosma dregeana is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in native habitats.
