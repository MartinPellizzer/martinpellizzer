Aeschynanthus tubulosus var. tubulosus

Aeschynanthus tubulosus var. tubulosus, commonly known as the tubular-flowered waxplant, is a species native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a trailing evergreen shrub characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and clusters of tubular, brightly colored flowers. The flowers, which are typically red or orange, are adapted for pollination by birds and insects. This plant thrives in humid, well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light.

It is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its ornamental value and ability to bloom throughout the year.
