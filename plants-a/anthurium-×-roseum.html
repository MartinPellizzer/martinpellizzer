Anthurium × roseum

Anthurium × roseum, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a hybrid plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped, brightly colored spathes that can range from red to pink. The plant produces small, green berries that mature to a dark red, containing seeds. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.

Anthurium × roseum is popular as a decorative plant due to its vibrant foliage and long-lasting blooms.
