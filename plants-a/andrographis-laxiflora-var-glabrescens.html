Andrographis laxiflora var. glabrescens

Andrographis laxiflora var. glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth-leaved andrographis, is a herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its slender, upright stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow-green in color. It thrives in moist, tropical environments and is often found in shaded, forested areas.

This variety is distinguished by its smooth, hairless foliage, which sets it apart from other varieties of Andrographis.
