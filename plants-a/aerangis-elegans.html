Aerangis elegans

Aerangis elegans, commonly known as the elegant orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its leaves are broad and glossy, while its roots are specialized for absorbing moisture from the air.

Aerangis elegans thrives in humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.
