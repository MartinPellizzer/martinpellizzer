Astragalus kurnet-es-saudae

Astragalus kurnet-es-saudae, commonly known as the Saudi desert milkvetch, is a perennial legume native to arid regions of Saudi Arabia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its small, yellow flowers and compound leaves. The plant is adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments with minimal water availability. Its root system is extensive, allowing it to access deep groundwater sources.

Astragalus kurnet-es-saudae plays a role in stabilizing desert soils and supporting local biodiversity.
