Anthurium platyglossum var. nanegalense

Anthurium platyglossum var. nanegalense, commonly known as the Nanegalense Philodendron, is a tropical plant native to parts of South America.

It features large, heart-shaped leaves with a deep green color and a glossy texture. The plant produces striking spadices, which are the characteristic inflorescences of the Araceae family. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in greenhouses.
