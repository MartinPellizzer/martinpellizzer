Avena pubescens subsp. laevigata

Avena pubescens subsp.

laevigata, commonly known as smooth oatgrass, is a perennial grass species native to North America. It typically grows in moist to wet habitats such as marshes, swales, and along stream banks.

The plant has slender, erect stems that are often hairless or only slightly hairy. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green color and a smooth texture.

The inflorescence consists of a single spikelet at the tip of each stem, bearing small, wind-pollinated flowers.
