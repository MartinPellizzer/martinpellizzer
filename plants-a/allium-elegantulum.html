Allium elegantulum

Allium elegantulum, commonly known as the slender leek, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features grass-like leaves that are narrow and bluish-green, growing up to 30 centimeters in height. The plant produces small, white to pale pink flowers arranged in a dense, umbel-like cluster. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Allium elegantulum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping for its elegant appearance.
