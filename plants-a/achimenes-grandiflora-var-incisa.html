Achimenes grandiflora var. incisa

Achimenes grandiflora var. incisa, commonly known as the serrated achimenes, is a tropical perennial shrub native to Central and South America.

It features striking, tubular flowers that range in color from red to pink, often with a contrasting throat. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with serrated edges, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This variety is often cultivated for its vibrant blooms and is popular in gardens and as a potted plant.
