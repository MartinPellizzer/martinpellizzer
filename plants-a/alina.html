Alina

Alina, commonly known as the silverleaf tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, silvery-green leaves that reflect sunlight, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies. Alina thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
