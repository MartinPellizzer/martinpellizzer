Aloe johannis

Aloe johannis, commonly known as the Cape aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, with a waxy coating to store water. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow flowers, which bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Aloe johannis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional medicinal uses.
