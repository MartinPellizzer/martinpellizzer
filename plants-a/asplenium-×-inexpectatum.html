Asplenium × inexpectatum

Asplenium × inexpectatum, commonly known as the hybrid shield fern, is a rare and naturally occurring hybrid between Asplenium trichomanes and Asplenium bulbiferum.

It is characterized by its distinctive shield-shaped fronds, which are typically dark green and have a leathery texture. The plant produces sporangia on the underside of its fronds, which are clustered in sori, a feature typical of many ferns. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories.

Due to its rarity and unique morphology, it is of particular interest to botanists and conservationists.
