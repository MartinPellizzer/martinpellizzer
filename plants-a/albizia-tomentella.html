Albizia tomentella

Albizia tomentella, commonly known as the silky albizia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to parts of Asia, including India and China.

It is characterized by its soft, silvery-gray foliage, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or white, arranged in racemes. Albizia tomentella is often grown for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.

It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.
