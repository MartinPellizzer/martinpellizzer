Armeria chilensis var. magellanica

Armeria chilensis var. magellanica, commonly known as the Magellan's thrift, is a flowering plant native to southern South America.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces dense clusters of white or pink flowers. The plant has linear, grass-like leaves and is often found in coastal and rocky habitats. It is well adapted to harsh climates and is drought-tolerant.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens in temperate regions.
