Argyranthemum webbii

Argyranthemum webbii, commonly known as the silverleaf geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-white leaves and showy, daisy-like flowers that range in color from pink to purple. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and containers. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.

Argyranthemum webbii is also valued for its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements.
