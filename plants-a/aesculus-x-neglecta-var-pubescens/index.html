Aesculus × Neglecta Var. Pubescens

Aesculus × neglecta var. pubescens is a unique hybrid plant known for its ornamental value and hardy nature. It belongs to the soapberry family and is prized for its showy, clustered flowers that bloom in the spring. This variety features a pubescent or hairy texture on its leaves, giving it a distinctive appearance. It is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions. Despite its beauty, it requires proper care to ensure healthy growth and flowering.
