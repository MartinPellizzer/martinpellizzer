Arisaema amurense var. purpureum

Arisaema amurense var. purpureum, commonly known as the purple-veined arrowhead, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to East Asia.

It features a distinctive arrowhead-shaped leaf with purple veins, giving it its common name. The plant produces a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe, typical of the Araceae family. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
