Anthurium galipanense

Anthurium galipanense, commonly known as the Galipanense Arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to Central America.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive inflorescence consisting of a spadix and a spathe. The plant typically features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are dark green in color. It thrives in humid, warm environments and requires indirect sunlight for optimal growth.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical landscapes.
