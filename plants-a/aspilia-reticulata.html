Aspilia reticulata

Aspilia reticulata, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, showy yellow or orange flower heads. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, tubular florets that form a composite flower head. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grassland and rocky habitats.

Aspilia reticulata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
