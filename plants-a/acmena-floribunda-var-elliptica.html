Acmena floribunda var. elliptica

Acmena floribunda var. elliptica, commonly known as the elliptic flame tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces striking clusters of bright red or orange flowers during the warm season, which are followed by small, round fruits. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular ornamental plant in tropical and subtropical gardens.

Its rapid growth and showy blooms make it a valuable addition to landscapes seeking both aesthetic and ecological benefits.
