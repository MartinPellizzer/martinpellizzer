Arisaema thunbergii var. corniculatum

Arisaema thunbergii var. corniculatum, commonly known as the horned arisaema, is a striking plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, helmet-shaped spadix surrounded by a broad, heart-shaped spathe. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream sides. Its foliage consists of large, arrowhead-shaped leaves that are often a deep green.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its distinctive appearance.
