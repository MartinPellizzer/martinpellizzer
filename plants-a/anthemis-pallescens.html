Anthemis pallescens

Anthemis pallescens, commonly known as the pale ragwort, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of western Asia.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble a single flower. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. It produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in the summer months.

The leaves are usually gray-green in color, with a fuzzy texture, and the plant is known for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal medicine.
