Aster ganlun

Aster ganlun, commonly known as the Ganlun aster, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically features showy blue to purple flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its botanical structure includes fibrous roots, lance-shaped leaves, and a robust stem that supports its vibrant blooms.
