Acleisanthes palmeri

Acleisanthes palmeri, commonly known as the desert morning glory, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a herbaceous annual with slender, hairy stems and trumpet-shaped, pale yellow to white flowers. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in sandy or rocky soils.

Its rapid growth cycle and ability to bloom quickly after rainfall make it a characteristic species of desert ecosystems.
