Afzelia laciniata

Afzelia laciniata, commonly known as the African ironwood, is a tropical tree native to Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, hard wood that is highly valued for its strength and durability. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are often deeply lobed, giving it a distinctive appearance. It produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or yellow in color.

The tree is also known for its ability to thrive in dry, sandy soils and its role in traditional medicine.
