Aglaonema schottianum var. brownii

Aglaonema schottianum var. brownii, commonly known as the Brown's Chinese evergreen, is a tropical evergreen plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves with striking red and white variegation. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers, though it is primarily grown for its ornamental foliage. It thrives in low-light conditions and requires moderate humidity and well-draining soil.

Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
