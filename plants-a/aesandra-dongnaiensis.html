Aesandra dongnaiensis

Aesandra dongnaiensis, commonly known as the Dong Nai sand orchid, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant blooms. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and thrives in tropical climates. Its flowers are usually yellow or greenish-yellow, with a unique, intricate structure.

Aesandra dongnaiensis is relatively rare and is often found in specific habitats within its native range.
