Acmispon distichus

Acmispon distichus, commonly known as the slender needlepod, is a perennial legume native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, pinnately compound leaves. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are typically pink to purple in color. Its seeds are enclosed in slender, two-lobed pods, which are adapted for wind dispersal.

Acmispon distichus is often found in dry, sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments.
