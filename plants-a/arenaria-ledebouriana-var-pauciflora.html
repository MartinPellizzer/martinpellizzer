Arenaria ledebouriana var. pauciflora

Arenaria ledebouriana var. pauciflora, commonly known as the sparse-flowered sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to arctic and alpine regions.

It typically forms dense mats of narrow, linear leaves and produces small, white or pink flowers in clusters. The plant is adapted to harsh environments, thriving in well-drained, sandy or rocky soils. It is often found in tundra and mountainous areas where temperatures are cold and conditions are dry.

Its botanical features make it a hardy and resilient species suitable for ecological restoration projects.
