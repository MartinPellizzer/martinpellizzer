Astroloba bicarinata

Astroloba bicarinata, commonly known as the star-shaped bulbous plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features a basal rosette of fleshy, narrow leaves that are typically green with a reddish tinge. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
