Adenandra speciosa

Adenandra speciosa, commonly known as the flame tree, is a striking ornamental plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy red or orange flowers that bloom in spring, creating a dramatic visual impact. The plant has compound leaves with elongated, pointed leaflets, and it produces small, woody fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for warm climates.

Adenandra speciosa is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and public spaces.
