Angraecum andersonii

Angraecum andersonii, commonly known as the Anderson's star orchid, is a rare and striking epiphytic orchid native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stem and large, showy white flowers with intricate purple markings. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its nocturnal blooming habit is a unique adaptation to its native habitat.

Angraecum andersonii is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts due to its beauty and rarity.
