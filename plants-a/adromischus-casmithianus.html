Adromischus casmithianus

Adromischus casmithianus, commonly known as the Cape snowball, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, rounded leaves that are often covered in a fine, white powdery coating, which helps reduce water loss. The plant produces clusters of small, white, star-shaped flowers during the winter months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light.

Due to its compact size and ornamental appeal, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
