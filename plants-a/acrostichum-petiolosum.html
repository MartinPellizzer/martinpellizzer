Acrostichum petiolosum

Acrostichum petiolosum, commonly known as the slender fern, is a species of fern native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its long, slender fronds that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant has a creeping rhizome that allows it to spread across the ground, forming dense mats. Its leaves are typically bipinnate, with a delicate, feathery appearance.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forests or along stream banks.
