Arisaema japonicum var. atropurpureum

Arisaema japonicum var. atropurpureum, commonly known as the purple-veined arisaema, is a herbaceous plant native to East Asia.

It features a distinctive, palmately lobed leaf with deep purple veins against a dark green background. The plant produces a spadix surrounded by a showy, hooded spathe that ranges in color from white to deep purple. It thrives in shady, moist environments and is often found in woodland areas.

The species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and unique floral structure.
