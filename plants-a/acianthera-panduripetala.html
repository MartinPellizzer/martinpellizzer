Acianthera panduripetala

Acianthera panduripetala, commonly known as the panduripetala orchid, is a tropical orchid species native to South America.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and clusters of small, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant produces pendulous, strap-like leaves and has a complex root system adapted for nutrient absorption in its natural habitat. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

This orchid is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
