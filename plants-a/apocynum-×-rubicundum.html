Apocynum × rubicundum

Apocynum × rubicundum, commonly known as the red-root dogbane, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Apocynaceae family.

It is characterized by its erect, branched stems and opposite, ovate leaves with a rough texture. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late summer. Its roots are thick and reddish, which is a distinguishing feature.

It is native to the eastern United States and is often found in moist, wooded areas.
