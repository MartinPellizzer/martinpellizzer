Astragalus shinanensis

Astragalus shinanensis, commonly known as the Shian Astragalus, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family Fabaceae.

It is native to China and is characterized by its small, pea-like flowers that typically range in color from white to pale pink. The plant produces pods that contain several seeds, which are often dispersed by wind. It thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare species with potential ecological significance.
