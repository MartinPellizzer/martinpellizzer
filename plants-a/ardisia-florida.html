Ardisia florida

Ardisia florida, commonly known as the Florida beautyberry, is a shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its dark purple, berry-like fruits that appear in late summer to early fall. The plant has glossy, ovate leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. It produces small, white flowers in clusters during the spring.

Ardisia florida is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to moist, acidic soils.
