Agathis Dammara Subsp. Flavescens

Agathis dammara subsp. flavescens is a rare and lesser-known member of the Araucariaceae family. Native to the rainforests of Indonesia, this plant thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its unique yellowish-brown bark and slender, conical shape distinguish it from other agathis species. Despite its ecological importance, it faces threats from habitat loss and overharvesting. Conservation efforts are crucial to ensure its survival in the wild.
