Areca insignis var. insignis

Areca insignis var. insignis, commonly known as the areca palm, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and feathery, compound leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by edible, coconut-like fruits. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Areca insignis var. insignis is often cultivated as an ornamental plant in gardens and landscapes.
