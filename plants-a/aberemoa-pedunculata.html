Aberemoa pedunculata

Aberemoa pedunculata, commonly known as the red-flowered evergreen, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, leathery foliage and clusters of vibrant red flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has small, oval-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green in color. It thrives in humid environments and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and landscape design.
