Alchemilla zamelisiana f. autumnalis

Alchemilla zamelisiana f. autumnalis, commonly known as the autumnal lady's mantle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central Asia.

It features basal rosettes of silver-green, velvety leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in early summer, which are clustered in dense, rounded inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained, moist soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Its ornamental value comes from its attractive foliage and long flowering period.
