Astragalus panguicensis

Astragalus panguicensis, commonly known as the Panguicene milkvetch, is a flowering plant species native to China.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its compound leaves and pea-like flowers. The plant is known for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional Chinese medicine. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, typically found in rocky or sandy soils.

Its unique genetic makeup and ecological adaptations make it an important subject in botanical research.
