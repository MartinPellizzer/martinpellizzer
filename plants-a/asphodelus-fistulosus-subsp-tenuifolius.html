Asphodelus fistulosus subsp. tenuifolius

Asphodelus fistulosus subsp.

tenuifolius, commonly known as the slender-leaved asphodel, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region. It features narrow, linear leaves that are typically blue-green in color, and it produces tall, spike-like inflorescences with pale yellow to orange flowers.

The plant thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or gravelly habitats. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.

The species is also noted for its ability to tolerate drought conditions once established.
