Aucuba filicauda var. filicauda

Aucuba filicauda var. filicauda, commonly known as the Japanese aucuba, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Japan.

It features glossy, dark green, elliptical leaves that are deeply veined and often have a waxy texture. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in the spring, which are followed by orange-red berries that persist into winter. It thrives in partial to full shade and is highly tolerant of urban conditions, making it a popular choice for landscaping.

Its dense growth habit and attractive foliage make it an excellent ornamental plant for gardens and landscapes.
