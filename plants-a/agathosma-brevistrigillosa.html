Agathosma brevistrigillosa

Agathosma brevistrigillosa, commonly known as the Cape star or star agathosma, is a succulent shrub native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, star-shaped leaves and yellow flowers. The plant is adapted to arid environments, with thick, fleshy leaves that store water. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires minimal irrigation.

Agathosma brevistrigillosa is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
