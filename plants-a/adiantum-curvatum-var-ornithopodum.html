Adiantum curvatum var. ornithopodum

Adiantum curvatum var. ornithopodum, commonly known as the feather-leaf maidenhair fern, is a delicate and ornamental fern species native to parts of Asia.

It features finely divided, arching fronds that give it a graceful, lace-like appearance. The plant's leaves are typically bright green, with a silvery underside that shimmers in sunlight. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and requires high humidity and well-draining soil.

This variety is prized for its aesthetic appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and indoor spaces.
