Anaphalis corymbifera

Anaphalis corymbifera, commonly known as the clustered leaf everlasting, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its hairy, silver-gray foliage and small, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest floors or rocky slopes. Its flowers are usually white or pale yellow and bloom in the summer months.
