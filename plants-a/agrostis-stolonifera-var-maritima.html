Agrostis stolonifera var. maritima

Agrostis stolonifera var. maritima, commonly known as beach grass, is a perennial grass species native to coastal regions.

It is characterized by its low-growing, spreading growth habit and silver-blue foliage. The plant produces dense, tufted inflorescences that are often tinged with pink or red. It thrives in sandy, well-drained soils and is highly tolerant of salt spray and wind.

This variety plays an important ecological role in stabilizing dunes and preventing erosion along coastlines.
