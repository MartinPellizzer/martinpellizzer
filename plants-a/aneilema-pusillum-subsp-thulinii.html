Aneilema pusillum subsp. thulinii

Aneilema pusillum subsp.

thulinii, commonly known as the dwarf aneilema, is a small evergreen shrub native to tropical Africa. It typically grows to a height of about 30 cm, featuring dense, dark green foliage with a slightly hairy texture.

The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers that bloom in the summer months. It is well-suited for shady, moist environments and is often cultivated as a decorative plant in gardens.

Its compact size and attractive foliage make it a popular choice for container gardening.
