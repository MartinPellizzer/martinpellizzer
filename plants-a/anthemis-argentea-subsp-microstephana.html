Anthemis argentea subsp. microstephana

Anthemis argentea subsp.

microstephana, commonly known as silver ragwort, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe and parts of western Asia. It features wiry stems covered in dense white or silver hairs, giving it a distinctive silvery appearance.

The plant produces small, yellow composite flowers arranged in flat-topped clusters, typical of the Asteraceae family. Its leaves are narrow, lance-shaped, and also covered in fine, silvery hairs.

It thrives in dry, sunny habitats such as rocky slopes and gravelly soils.
