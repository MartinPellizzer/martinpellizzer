Argyroderma amoenum

Argyroderma amoenum, commonly known as the silverleaf tree, is a small evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-white undersides on its narrowly elliptical leaves, which give it its common name. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are followed by round, fleshy fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Due to its unique foliage, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
