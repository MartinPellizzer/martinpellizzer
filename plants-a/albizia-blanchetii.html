Albizia blanchetii

Albizia blanchetii, commonly known as the African silk tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant is known for its fragrant, pale pink to white flowers that bloom in summer. Its bark is smooth and gray, and it can grow up to 5 meters in height.

Albizia blanchetii is often used in traditional medicine and for ornamental purposes due to its aesthetic appeal.
