Agave sobolifera f. spinidentata

Agave sobolifera f. spinidentata, commonly known as the spiny-leaf agave, is a variant of the agave plant native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that are covered in sharp spines, providing both protection and a distinctive appearance. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences that bear yellowish-green flowers, which are typically short-lived but attract various pollinators. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, requiring minimal water and well-draining soil.

This ornamental and potentially medicinal plant is valued for its resilience and unique aesthetic features.
