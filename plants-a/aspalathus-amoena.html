Aspalathus amoena

Aspalathus amoena, commonly known as the sweet pea bush, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its small, sweet-smelling flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is known for its leguminous properties. It typically grows in coastal areas and has a trailing habit, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its foliage is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
