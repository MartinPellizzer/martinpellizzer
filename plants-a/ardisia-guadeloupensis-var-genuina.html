Ardisia guadeloupensis var. genuina

Ardisia guadeloupensis var. genuina, commonly known as the Guadeloupe ardisia, is a shrub native to the Caribbean island of Guadeloupe.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
