Astracantha tokatensis

Astracantha tokatensis, commonly known as the Tokat thornveld, is a flowering plant native to Turkey.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features spiny, grass-like leaves and produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the summer. It is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a resilient species in its native habitat.

Due to its unique morphology and limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
