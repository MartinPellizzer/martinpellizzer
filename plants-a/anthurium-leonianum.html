Anthurium leonianum

Anthurium leonianum, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are dark green with a reddish tint. The plant produces vibrant red spadices, which are the showy inflorescences that attract pollinators. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium leonianum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in both indoor and outdoor settings.
