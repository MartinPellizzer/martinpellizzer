Ayenia latifolia

Ayenia latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf ayenia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments.

Ayenia latifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
