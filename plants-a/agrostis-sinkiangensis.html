Agrostis sinkiangensis

Agrostis sinkiangensis, commonly known as the Xinjiang bentgrass, is a species of grass native to the arid and semi-arid regions of Central Asia, particularly in Xinjiang, China.

It is a low-growing perennial plant with narrow, grass-like leaves that are adapted to withstand harsh environmental conditions. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. Its root system is extensive, allowing it to thrive in nutrient-poor soils and extreme climates.

Agrostis sinkiangensis plays a role in stabilizing soil and preventing erosion in its native habitat.
