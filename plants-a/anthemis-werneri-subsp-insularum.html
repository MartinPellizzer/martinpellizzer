Anthemis werneri subsp. insularum

Anthemis werneri subsp.

insularum, commonly known as the island anthemis, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, yellow to orange composite flowers.

The plant typically grows in rocky or coastal environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its foliage is usually gray-green, with narrow, linear leaves that help reduce water loss.

This subspecies is of interest to botanists due to its unique ecological niche and limited distribution.
