Arenaria modesta subsp. tenuis

Arenaria modesta subsp.

tenuis, commonly known as the slender sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of North America. It typically features small, rounded leaves and produces clusters of white or pink flowers in early spring.

The plant thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats. Its compact growth habit and drought tolerance make it well-suited for xeriscaping.

It plays a role in stabilizing soil in disturbed areas and supports local pollinator populations.
