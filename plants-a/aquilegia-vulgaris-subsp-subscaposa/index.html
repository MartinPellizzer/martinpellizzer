Aquilegia Vulgaris Subsp. Subscaposa

Aquilegia vulgaris subsp. subscaposa is a lesser-known variant of the common columbine plant. It features striking blue and purple flowers that bloom in early spring. This subspecies is often found in alpine and rocky habitats across Europe. Its leaves are deeply lobed and resemble those of its parent species. Despite its beauty, it remains underappreciated in horticulture compared to other columbines.
