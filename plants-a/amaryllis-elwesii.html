Amaryllis elwesii

Amaryllis elwesii, commonly known as the Elwes' amaryllis, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from red to orange, often with a contrasting white or yellow throat. The plant produces large, strap-like leaves that are typically dark green and broad at the base. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens in warm climates.

Amaryllis elwesii is known for its long blooming period, often extending from late winter through spring.
