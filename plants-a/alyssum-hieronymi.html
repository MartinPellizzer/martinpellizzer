Alyssum hieronymi

Alyssum hieronymi, commonly known as the Sierra foothill alyssum, is a flowering plant native to California.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces clusters of small, fragrant white or pale yellow flowers. The plant has fine, silvery foliage and is often found in rocky or sandy soils. It thrives in full sun and is drought-tolerant, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Alyssum hieronymi is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
