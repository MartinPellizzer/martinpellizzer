Aira caryophyllea var. hostii

Aira caryophyllea var. hostii, commonly known as the Hosta air plant, is a unique variant of the aira caryophyllea species.

It is characterized by its dense, grass-like foliage that forms a compact rosette. The plant produces striking purple or pink flower spikes that rise above the foliage during the growing season. It thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sun, making it a versatile choice for gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to bloom repeatedly throughout the summer months.
