Agiortia pleiosperma

Agiortia pleiosperma, commonly known as the Cape orchid, is a species of orchid native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking violet and white flowers that bloom during the summer months. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to retain moisture in arid conditions.

Agiortia pleiosperma is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
