Aeschynomene pimpinellifolia

Aeschynomene pimpinellifolia, commonly known as smallleaf aeschynomene, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its small, trifoliate leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are often clustered in racemes. It is a perennial herb that thrives in well-drained soils and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.

Its ability to fix nitrogen makes it beneficial in soil enrichment and agroforestry systems.
