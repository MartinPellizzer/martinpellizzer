Alsophila mertensiana

Alsophila mertensiana, commonly known as the Mertens' tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and a crown of feathery, compound fronds that can reach several meters in length. The plant has a slow growth rate and can live for many decades, making it a long-lived species. Its rhizome system supports the growth of the trunk and anchors the plant in moist, shaded environments.

The fronds are typically dark green and contribute to the plant's ornamental value in gardens and natural habitats.
