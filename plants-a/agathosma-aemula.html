Agathosma aemula

Agathosma aemula, commonly known as the Cape daisy, is a perennial shrub native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant has aromatic leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for rock gardens and as a border plant.
