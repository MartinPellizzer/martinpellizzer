Aphelandra diachyla

Aphelandra diachyla, commonly known as the golden flame plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the Caribbean and Central America.

It is characterized by its striking, elongated leaves that are often a vibrant green with a waxy texture. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant, white flowers that bloom in the summer months. Aphelandra diachyla thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture to maintain its lush appearance.

It is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
