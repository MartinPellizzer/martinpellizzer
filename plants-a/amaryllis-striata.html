Amaryllis striata

Amaryllis striata, commonly known as the Zebra Lily, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features strap-shaped leaves that are often striped with white and green, giving it its common name. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, typically during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

Amaryllis striata is relatively easy to care for and can be grown in containers or in the ground in suitable climates.
