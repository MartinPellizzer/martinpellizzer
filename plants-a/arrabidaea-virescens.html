Arrabidaea virescens

Arrabidaea virescens, commonly known as the green arrabidaea, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers and trifoliate leaves. The plant typically grows in open grasslands and is often found in sandy or well-drained soils. It produces pods that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.
