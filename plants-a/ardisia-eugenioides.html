Ardisia eugenioides

Ardisia eugenioides, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen or Indian flame tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

Ardisia eugenioides is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens and landscapes worldwide.
