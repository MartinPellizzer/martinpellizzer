Astragalus gongliuensis

Astragalus gongliuensis, commonly known as the Gongliu milkvetch, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to China.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant is known for its ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen through its root nodules, which helps improve soil fertility. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often growing in rocky or sandy soils.

Astragalus gongliuensis is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional Chinese medicine.
