Androsace salicifolia

Androsace salicifolia, commonly known as the willowleaf sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and western Asia.

Androsace salicifolia thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in alpine or subalpine environments.
