Adhatoda natalensis

Adhatoda natalensis, commonly known as Natal Adhatoda, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves that are often pubescent on the underside. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or violet in color, arranged in clusters. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or riverine habitats.

The species is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional medicine for its respiratory benefits.
