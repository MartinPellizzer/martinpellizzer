Aster yuanqunensis

Aster yuanqunensis, commonly known as the Yuanqun aster, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically features showy purple or blue flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated for landscaping and floral arrangements.
