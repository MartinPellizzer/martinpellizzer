Aneilema herbaceum var. divergens

Aneilema herbaceum var. divergens, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial with rosettes of velvety, dark green leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of purple, pink, and white. It thrives in humid, well-drained soil and requires indirect sunlight for optimal growth.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
