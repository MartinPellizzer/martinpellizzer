Arachnorchis osmera

Arachnorchis osmera, commonly known as the Osmera spider-orchid, is a rare and striking orchid native to the eastern regions of Australia.

It is characterized by its unique, spider-like appearance, with long, slender petals that resemble the legs of a spider. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as rainforests and understorey habitats. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish in color, with intricate patterns that aid in pollination by specific insects.

Due to habitat loss and environmental changes, it is considered a threatened species, highlighting the importance of conservation efforts for its survival.
