Arisaema wumengense

Arisaema wumengense, commonly known as the Wumeng arrowhead, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its unique, arrow-shaped leaves and striking, spadix-like inflorescence. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as mountain forests and stream banks. Its foliage and floral structure are adapted to its specific ecological niche, contributing to its ornamental and potential medicinal value.

Arisaema wumengense is of interest to botanists and horticulturists due to its distinct morphology and habitat requirements.
