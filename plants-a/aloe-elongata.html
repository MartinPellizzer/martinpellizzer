Aloe elongata

Aloe elongata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features long, narrow, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, often with a waxy coating. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow flowers that bloom in summer. Aloe elongata is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its leaves and stems.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
