Adenaria floribunda var. grisleoides

Adenaria floribunda var. grisleoides, commonly known as the slender spider-orchid, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, delicate flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in coastal or inland regions. It produces greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn, and its rhizomes are sometimes used in traditional medicine.

The species is listed as threatened due to habitat loss and is protected in several Australian states.
