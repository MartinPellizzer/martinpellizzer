Anthurium ameliae

Anthurium ameliae, commonly known as the Amelia's Arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are often deep green with a glossy texture. The plant produces vibrant spadices, which are the showy, modified leaves that enclose the small, true flowers. It thrives in humid, warm environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium ameliae is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
