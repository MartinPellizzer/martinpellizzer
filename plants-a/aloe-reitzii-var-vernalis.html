Aloe reitzii var. vernalis

Aloe reitzii var. vernalis, commonly known as the Vernal Aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a slight blue or grayish hue. The plant produces tall, branched stems with clusters of yellow flowers during the spring season. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
