Aphelandra nitens

Aphelandra nitens, commonly known as the silver leaf cycad, is a member of the Araceae family and is native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, dark green, glossy leaves that are often variegated with silver or white markings. The plant produces inflorescences that consist of a spadix surrounded by a spathe, typical of many aroid species. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in botanical gardens.
