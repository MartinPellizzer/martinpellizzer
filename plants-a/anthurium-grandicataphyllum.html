Anthurium grandicataphyllum

Anthurium grandicataphyllum, commonly known as the giant shield fern, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to several feet in length. The plant produces striking, spadix-shaped inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Anthurium grandicataphyllum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is popular in both indoor and outdoor gardens.
