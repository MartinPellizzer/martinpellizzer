Abarema cyclosperma

Abarema cyclosperma, commonly known as the yellow bell.

It is a shrub native to South America, particularly Brazil, and belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae. The plant is characterized by its yellow, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are trifoliate, consisting of three oval-shaped leaflets.

Abarema cyclosperma is often found in moist, shaded areas and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
