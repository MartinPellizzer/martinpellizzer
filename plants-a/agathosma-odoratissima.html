Agathosma odoratissima

Agathosma odoratissima, commonly known as sweet fennel, is a small shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its aromatic, finely divided leaves and small, yellowish flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces small, oval-shaped fruits that are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in well-drained soils and is drought-tolerant, making it suitable for Mediterranean climates.

Its essential oils, extracted from the leaves and stems, are valued for their medicinal and aromatic properties.
