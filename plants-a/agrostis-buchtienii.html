Agrostis buchtienii

Agrostis buchtienii, commonly known as the Buchtien's bentgrass, is a species of grass native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial plant characterized by its fine, needle-like leaves and dense, mat-forming growth habit. The plant typically thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in grassy areas or along stream banks. Its inflorescence consists of spikelets that are arranged in a panicle, and it produces small, wind-pollinated flowers.

Agrostis buchtienii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in horticultural settings.
