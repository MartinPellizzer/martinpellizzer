Alsophila dichromatolepis

Alsophila dichromatolepis, commonly known as the two-colored tree fern, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feathery fronds that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant has a fibrous root system that helps it anchor in moist, shaded environments. Its fronds are typically bi-colored, with a lighter underside and a darker upper surface.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value in tropical gardens and greenhouses.
