Anthurium hebetatum

Anthurium hebetatum, commonly known as the "Red Anthurium," is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves and vibrant red spathes. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically hidden within the spathe, which is the showy part often mistaken for the flower itself. Anthurium hebetatum thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Its unique foliage and ornamental value make it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical landscapes.
