Anemonoides × lipsiensis

Anemonoides × lipsiensis, commonly known as the Lipsian anemone, is a hybrid perennial plant native to the Balkan region.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its showy, white to pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows to a height of 30–60 cm, with pinnately divided, lance-shaped leaves. It prefers moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade, making it a suitable addition to woodland gardens.

Anemonoides × lipsiensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its delicate, early-blooming flowers.
