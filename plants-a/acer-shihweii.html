Acer shihweii

Acer shihweii, commonly known as the Shihwei maple, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It is characterized by its vibrant autumn foliage, which ranges from red to orange and yellow. The tree has palmately lobed leaves that are typically 5 to 9 inches long. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers in early spring, followed by winged seeds.

This species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental qualities and adaptability to various soil types.
