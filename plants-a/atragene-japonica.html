Atragene japonica

Atragene japonica, commonly known as Japanese starwort, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. The plant features slender, branched stems and small, star-shaped flowers that bloom in the spring. Its leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and often have a waxy coating.

Atragene japonica is valued in traditional medicine for its purported anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties.
