Acer platanoides var. minimum

Acer platanoides var. minimum, commonly known as the Japanese maple, is a compact variety of the species Acer platanoides.

It features delicate, trifoliate leaves that turn vibrant red in the fall. The tree has a slow growth rate and is typically cultivated as a shrub or small tree. It prefers partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for smaller garden spaces.

Its ornamental value comes from its foliage and the way it adds texture to landscapes.
