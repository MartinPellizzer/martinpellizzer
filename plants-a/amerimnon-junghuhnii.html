Amerimnon junghuhnii

Amerimnon junghuhnii, commonly known as the Junghuhn's hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are ovate to lanceolate, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
