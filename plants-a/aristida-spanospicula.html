Aristida spanospicula

Aristida spanospicula, commonly known as the slender dropseed, is a grass species native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its fine, narrow leaves and compact, upright growth habit. The plant produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences that are typically pale green to brown in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands and prairies.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological benefits, it is sometimes cultivated in native plant gardens.
