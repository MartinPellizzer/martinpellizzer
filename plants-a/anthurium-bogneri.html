Anthurium bogneri

Anthurium bogneri, commonly known as the Bogner's anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy surface. The plant produces vibrant spadices, which are the modified leaves that enclose the true flowers. Anthurium bogneri thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical landscapes.
