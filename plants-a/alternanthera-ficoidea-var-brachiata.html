Alternanthera ficoidea var. brachiata

Alternanthera ficoidea var. brachiata, commonly known as the Crimson Rose or Red Alternanthera, is a striking aquatic plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red to pink foliage, which intensifies under bright light conditions. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are often slightly curled, giving it a unique, ornamental appearance. It thrives in freshwater environments and is popular in aquariums for its aesthetic value.

Due to its rapid growth, it requires regular pruning to maintain its shape and prevent overcrowding.
