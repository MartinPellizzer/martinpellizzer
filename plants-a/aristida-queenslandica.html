Aristida queenslandica

Aristida queenslandica, commonly known as the Queensland threeawn, is a perennial grass native to Australia.

It typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is found in arid and semi-arid regions. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It is drought-tolerant and adapted to low-nutrient environments.

Its ecological role includes providing habitat and forage for various wildlife species.
