Anemone moorei

Anemone moorei, commonly known as the pink anemone, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, showy flowers that range in color from pink to white, resembling the petals of a traditional anemone. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in partial to full shade. Its foliage consists of heart-shaped, dark green leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem.

Anemone moorei is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to add a touch of elegance to shaded landscapes.
