Anthurium idmense

Anthurium idmense, commonly known as the red-tipped anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a reddish or purple tinge on the underside. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-shaped inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, which can range from white to red or orange. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium idmense is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and long-lasting blooms.
