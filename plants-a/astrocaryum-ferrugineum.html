Astrocaryum ferrugineum

Astrocaryum ferrugineum, commonly known as the yellow or red palm, is a species of palm native to the Amazon Basin.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate leaves that can reach several meters in length. The fruit is a large, yellow or red drupe, which contains a single seed surrounded by a fibrous mesocarp. The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional medicine.

Its growth habit and ecological role make it an important component of the rainforest ecosystem.
