Alstroemeria longistaminea

Alstroemeria longistaminea, commonly known as the long-stamen alstroemeria, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Alstroemeriaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant features strap-like leaves that are often arranged in a spiral pattern around the stem. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.

Its long stamens give it a unique appearance, contributing to its ornamental value.
