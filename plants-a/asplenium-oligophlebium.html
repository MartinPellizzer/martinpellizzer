Asplenium oligophlebium

Asplenium oligophlebium, commonly known as the narrow-veined spleenwort, is a species of fern belonging to the family Aspleniaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped leaves with narrow veins, typically growing in moist, shaded environments. This fern is often found in rocky crevices or on the bases of trees in temperate regions. Its rhizome is short and produces a dense cluster of small, upright fronds.

Asplenium oligophlebium is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in shaded gardens.
