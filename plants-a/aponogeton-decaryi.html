Aponogeton decaryi

Aponogeton decaryi, commonly known as the Decary's arrowhead, is a perennial aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its arrowhead-shaped leaves that are typically submerged or floating on the water surface. The plant produces striking white to pink flowers that bloom above the water, adding ornamental value to ponds and water gardens. It thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments with moderate to high light exposure.

Aponogeton decaryi is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal and ease of care in aquatic settings.
