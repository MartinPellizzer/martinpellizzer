Artemisia czekanowskiana

Artemisia czekanowskiana, commonly known as the Czekanowsky's wormwood, is a perennial shrub native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, silver-gray foliage. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are typically clustered in spherical heads. It thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its medicinal properties, it has been used in traditional medicine for various ailments.
