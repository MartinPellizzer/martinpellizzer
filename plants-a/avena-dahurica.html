Avena dahurica

Avena dahurica, commonly known as Siberian oat, is a flowering plant native to the cold regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like stems and long, narrow leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny, white to greenish flowers. Avena dahurica is adapted to grow in harsh, arctic or alpine environments.

It is often used in traditional medicine and has potential applications in modern herbal remedies.
