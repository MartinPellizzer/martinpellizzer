Alexa grandiflora

Alexa grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flower alexa, is a striking plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and deep red. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 6 meters. Its leaves are broad, lance-shaped, and have a glossy green surface.

The flowers bloom in clusters, attracting a variety of pollinators such as bees and hummingbirds.
