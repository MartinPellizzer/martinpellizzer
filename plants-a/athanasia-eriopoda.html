Athanasia eriopoda

Athanasia eriopoda, commonly known as the woolly everlasting, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly covering on its stems and leaves, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.

Athanasia eriopoda thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.
