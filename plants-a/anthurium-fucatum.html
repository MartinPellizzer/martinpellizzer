Anthurium fucatum

Anthurium fucatum, commonly known as the black anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its dark green, velvety foliage and striking, spadix-shaped inflorescence that is often surrounded by a deep red spathe. The plant thrives in high humidity and indirect light, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation. Its unique appearance makes it a sought-after ornamental plant.

Botanically, it belongs to the Araceae family, which includes other well-known plants like philodendrons and callas.
