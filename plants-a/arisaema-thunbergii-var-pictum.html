Arisaema thunbergii var. pictum

Arisaema thunbergii var. pictum, commonly known as the painted arisaema, is a member of the Araceae family.

It is native to eastern Asia, including Japan and China. The plant is characterized by its striking, mottled leaves and a distinctive, spadix-shaped inflorescence. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The species is also noted for its ability to produce a toxic sap, which can cause skin irritation.
