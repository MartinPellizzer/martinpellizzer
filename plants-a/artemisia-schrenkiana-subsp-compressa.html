Artemisia schrenkiana subsp. compressa

Artemisia schrenkiana subsp.

compressa, commonly known as the Eurasian wormwood, is a perennial shrub native to Central Asia. It is characterized by its aromatic, silver-gray foliage and small, yellowish-green flower heads.

The plant thrives in dry, rocky habitats and is often found in mountainous regions. Its leaves are narrow and densely packed, giving the plant a compact appearance.

It is valued for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally in herbal remedies.
