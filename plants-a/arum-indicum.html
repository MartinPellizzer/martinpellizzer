Arum indicum

Arum indicum, commonly known as the arrowhead plant, is a tropical herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. The plant produces a spadix, a type of inflorescence, surrounded by a spathe, which is a modified leaf. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found near water bodies.

The plant has been used for its medicinal properties, including treating inflammation and digestive issues.
