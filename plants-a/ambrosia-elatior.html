Ambrosia elatior

Ambrosia elatior, commonly known as giant ragweed, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and yellow-green flowers that appear in late summer. The plant is known for producing copious amounts of pollen, which can contribute to seasonal allergies. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found along roadsides and in agricultural fields.

Ambrosia elatior is an annual plant that can grow up to several meters tall.
