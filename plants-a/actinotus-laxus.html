Actinotus laxus

Actinotus laxus, commonly known as the silver fern, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its distinctive silver-white undersides on its leaves, which give it its common name. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.

Actinotus laxus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
