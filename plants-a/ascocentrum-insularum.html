Ascocentrum insularum

Ascocentrum insularum, commonly known as the Island Vanda, is a species of orchid native to the Philippines.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in humid, shaded forest environments. The plant has fleshy, dark green leaves that are often covered in a fine, white pubescence. Its flowers are showy, with a distinctive yellow and green coloration, and they bloom in clusters.

Ascocentrum insularum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
