Ambrosina reticulata

Ambrosina reticulata, commonly known as the net-veined ambrosia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

Ambrosina reticulata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
