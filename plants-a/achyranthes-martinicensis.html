Achyranthes martinicensis

Achyranthes martinicensis, commonly known as the Martinique knotweed, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean.

It is a member of the Polygonaceae family, characterized by its succulent stems and distinctive leaf morphology. The plant typically grows in moist, disturbed soils and can be found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its small, greenish-white flowers are clustered in dense inflorescences, and it produces small, round seeds that are dispersed by wind.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes considered an invasive species in certain areas.
