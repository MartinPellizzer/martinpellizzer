Aponogeton leptostachyus

Aponogeton leptostachyus, commonly known as the slender-stalked arrowhead, is a tropical aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its arrowhead-shaped leaves that are typically bright green and have a waxy texture. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in summer, followed by elongated, boat-shaped fruits. It thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments and is often cultivated in ponds and water gardens.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for aquatic landscaping.
