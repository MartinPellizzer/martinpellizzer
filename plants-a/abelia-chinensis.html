Abelia chinensis

Abelia chinensis, commonly known as Chinese abelia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features opposite, ovate leaves that are typically dark green and have a smooth texture. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink, blooming in late summer. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained soil.

Abelia chinensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various garden conditions.
