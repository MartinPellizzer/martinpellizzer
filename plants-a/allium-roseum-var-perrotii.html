Allium roseum var. perrotii

Allium roseum var. perrotii, commonly known as the Purple Stripe Onion, is a striking ornamental plant characterized by its tall, upright stems and clusters of purple-pink flowers.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is native to Central Asia. The plant produces small, round bulbs that are often used in culinary applications, though it is primarily grown for its ornamental value.

It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
