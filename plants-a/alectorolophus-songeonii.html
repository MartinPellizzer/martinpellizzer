Alectorolophus songeonii

Alectorolophus songeonii, commonly known as the Korean orchid, is a species of orchid native to South Korea.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid with a single, erect stem that bears a few flowers. The plant typically grows in forested areas with high humidity and partial shade. Its flowers are usually pale green or white with a distinctive yellow lip.

Alectorolophus songeonii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
