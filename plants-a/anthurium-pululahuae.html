Anthurium pululahuae

Anthurium pululahuae, commonly known as the Pululahua anthurium, is a striking tropical plant native to the Andean region of Ecuador.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a metallic sheen. The plant produces vibrant red spathes that enclose its yellow spadix, creating a dramatic floral display. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Anthurium pululahuae is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
