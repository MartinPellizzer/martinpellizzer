Abutilon multiflorum

Abutilon multiflorum, commonly known as the Chinese lantern plant, is a deciduous shrub native to China and Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are often hairy and have a velvety texture. The plant produces clusters of showy, lantern-shaped orange or red bracts that resemble small lanterns, which are not actually flowers but modified leaves. These bracts are often mistaken for flowers and are the main ornamental feature of the plant.

It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is commonly cultivated for its vibrant, long-lasting display.
