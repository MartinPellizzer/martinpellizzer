Aspidium apiifolium

Aspidium apiifolium, commonly known as the carrot fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Athyriaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds that are divided into narrow, linear segments. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and rocky slopes. Aspidium apiifolium is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and can thrive in a variety of soil types when provided with adequate moisture.
