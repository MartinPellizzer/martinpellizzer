Astracantha morganii

Astracantha morganii, commonly known as the Morgan's thornveld, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a compact, rounded shape with thick, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the summer months, adding a delicate touch to its appearance. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water to thrive.

Due to its unique appearance and drought tolerance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
