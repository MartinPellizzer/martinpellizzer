Anoectochilus longilobus

Anoectochilus longilobus, commonly known as the long-lobed toothed-orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and fleshy, dark green leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers with a unique toothed lip, often in shades of white and yellow. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is typically found in montane forests.

Due to its ornamental value and rarity, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation programs.
