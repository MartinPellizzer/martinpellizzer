Aloe harlana

Aloe harlana, commonly known as the Harlan's aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a bluish tinge. The plant produces tall, branched flower stalks that bear yellow-orange flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Aloe harlana is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its potential healing properties.
