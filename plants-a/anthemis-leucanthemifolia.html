Anthemis leucanthemifolia

Anthemis leucanthemifolia, commonly known as the white marguerite, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of western Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are hairy and grayish-green in color. Its flowers are typically white with a yellow center, forming a daisy-like appearance.

It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun, often growing in rocky or sandy habitats.
