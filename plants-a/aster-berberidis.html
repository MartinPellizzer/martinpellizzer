Aster berberidis

Aster berberidis, commonly known as the barberry aster, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which is characterized by its composite flower heads composed of numerous small florets. The plant typically grows in woodland areas and thrives in partial shade to full sun. Its bright yellow flowers bloom in the fall, adding vibrant color to the landscape.

Aster berberidis is also valued for its ornamental appeal and can be used in native plant gardens.
