Aloe nobilis

Aloe nobilis, commonly known as the noble aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically bluish-green in color, often with white or yellowish markings. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. Aloe nobilis is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its leaves to survive dry conditions.

It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes and has some traditional medicinal uses.
