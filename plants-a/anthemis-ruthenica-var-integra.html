Anthemis ruthenica var. integra

Anthemis ruthenica var. integra, commonly known as the Caucasian Anthemis, is a flowering plant native to the Caucasus region.

It is a perennial herb with yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has finely divided, gray-green leaves that are aromatic and covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or mountainous areas.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
