Angraecum spathulatum

Angraecum spathulatum, commonly known as the Madagascar star orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its unique, star-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale green with a dark red or purple lip. The plant produces a dense cluster of narrow, linear leaves and has a long, slender stem that bears the flowers. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Angraecum spathulatum is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
