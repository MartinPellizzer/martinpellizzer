Anemone Albana Subsp. Andina

White anemone, scientifically known as Anemone albana subsp. andina, is a delicate flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of South America. Its pale, almost translucent petals create a striking contrast against its lush green foliage. This species thrives in cool, moist environments, often found in mountainous areas with well-drained soil. With its elegant appearance, it is a popular choice for botanical gardens and conservation efforts. Despite its beauty, it remains a relatively obscure plant in the wider horticultural community.
