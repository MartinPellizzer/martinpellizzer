Acer × creticum var. microphyllum

Acer × creticum var. microphyllum, commonly known as the miniature maple, is a compact cultivar of the maple genus.

It is characterized by its small, ovate leaves that are typically a vibrant green in summer and turn yellow in autumn. The plant produces small, red flowers in early spring, followed by orange-red samaras. It is a slow-growing shrub that thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often used in small garden spaces or as a container plant.
