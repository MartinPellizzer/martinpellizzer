Anemone narcissiflora var. pekinensis

Anemone narcissiflora var. pekinensis, commonly known as the Peking anemone, is a herbaceous perennial native to East Asia.

It features showy, cup-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, often with a yellow center. The plant produces basal leaves that are heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate, early spring blooms.
