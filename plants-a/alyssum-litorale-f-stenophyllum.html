Alyssum litorale f. stenophyllum

Alyssum litorale f. stenophyllum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved coastal alyssum, is a compact, low-growing plant native to coastal regions of southern California.

It features small, fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, typically from spring to early summer. The leaves are narrow, linear, and densely arranged, giving the plant a distinctive, mat-like appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in sandy or rocky coastal habitats.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to stabilize coastal dunes.
