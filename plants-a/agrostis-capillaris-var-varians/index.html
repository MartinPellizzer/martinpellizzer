Agrostis Capillaris Var. Varians

Agrostis capillaris var. varians is a lesser-known grass species that thrives in moist, nutrient-rich soils. This variant of the common bentgrass is often found in wet meadows and along the edges of marshes. Its fine, silver-green blades make it a distinctive plant in its native habitat. Despite its beauty, it is not widely cultivated or recognized in horticulture. Understanding this plant offers insight into the ecological diversity of grassland ecosystems.
