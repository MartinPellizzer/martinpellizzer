Aconitum sanyoense var. piliferum

Aconitum sanyoense var. piliferum, commonly known as the Sanyoense Aconite, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant features basal leaves that are deeply lobed and hairy, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Due to its ornamental value and toxicity, it is often cultivated in gardens but requires careful handling.
