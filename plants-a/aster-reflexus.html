Aster reflexus

Aster reflexus, commonly known as the reflexed aster, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands. It produces showy blue to purple flowers that bloom in the summer months.

Its leaves are usually narrow and hairy, with a distinctive reflexed (bent backward) appearance at the tips.
