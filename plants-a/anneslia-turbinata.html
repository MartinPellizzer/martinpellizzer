Anneslia turbinata

Anneslia turbinata, commonly known as the spiral bellflower, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive spiral arrangement of petals, which gives it a unique and ornamental appearance. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Anneslia turbinata is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic value and easy care requirements.
