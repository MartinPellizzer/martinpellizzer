Adhatoda matammensis

Adhatoda matammensis, commonly known as the Madras neem or Indian banyan, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to India.

Adhatoda matammensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional medicinal uses in some cultures.
