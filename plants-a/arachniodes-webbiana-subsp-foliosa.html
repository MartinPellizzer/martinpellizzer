Arachniodes webbiana subsp. foliosa

Arachniodes webbiana subsp.

foliosa, commonly known as the broad-leafed rock fern, is a perennial herb native to Southeast Asia. It belongs to the family Davalliaceae and is characterized by its large, leathery, lance-shaped leaves.

The plant typically grows on rocky substrates in humid, shaded environments. Its fronds are often divided into segments with a distinctive, almost spider-like appearance.
