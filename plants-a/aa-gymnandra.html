Aa gymnandra

Aa gymnandra, commonly known as the mountain apple tree, is a tropical plant native to the Pacific Islands.

It is a small to medium-sized tree that can grow up to 10 meters in height, with a broad, rounded canopy. The leaves are large, simple, and alternate, typically measuring up to 30 centimeters in length. Its flowers are small and yellow, and it produces edible fruit that is similar in appearance to a small apple.

The plant is well adapted to volcanic soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.
