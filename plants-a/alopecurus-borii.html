Alopecurus borii

Alopecurus borii, commonly known as silver hair vetch, is a perennial grass species native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-haired leaves and small, spike-like inflorescences. This plant typically grows in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in meadows and pastures. Alopecurus borii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.

It is also a food source for various herbivorous insects and small mammals.
