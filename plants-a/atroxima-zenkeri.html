Atroxima zenkeri

Atroxima zenkeri, commonly known as the giant African snail, is a large terrestrial gastropod belonging to the family Achatinidae.

It is characterized by its heavy, spirally twisted shell that can reach up to 30 centimeters in length. Due to its size and ecological impact, it is considered an invasive species in many regions outside its native range.
