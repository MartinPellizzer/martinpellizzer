Anisochilus sericeus

Anisochilus sericeus, commonly known as silver fern, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the eastern United States.

It features delicate, fern-like leaves that are covered in fine, silvery hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in loose inflorescences. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as along stream banks and in woodland areas.

Its unique foliage and adaptability make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
