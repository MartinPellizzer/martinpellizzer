Anthurium buchtienii

Anthurium buchtienii, commonly known as the Buchtien's anthurium, is a striking tropical plant native to Colombia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped spathe and spadix inflorescence. The plant features broad, dark green, leathery leaves that are often veined with silver or white markings. Its flowers are typically greenish-white with a red or orange spadix, adding to its ornamental appeal.

Anthurium buchtienii thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.
