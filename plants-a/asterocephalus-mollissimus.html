Asterocephalus mollissimus

Asterocephalus mollissimus, commonly known as the velvet-headed aster.

It is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Brazil, and is characterized by its soft, velvety foliage. The plant produces showy, daisy-like flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists alike.
