Alternanthera laxa

Alternanthera laxa, commonly known as the red-veined sorrel, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves with prominent red veins. The plant typically grows as an annual herb, reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardeners and indoor plant enthusiasts.
