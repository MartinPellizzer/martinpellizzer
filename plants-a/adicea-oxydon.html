Adicea oxydon

Adicea oxydon, commonly known as the narrow-leafed daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies, sunflowers, and thistles. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, white to yellowish flowers arranged in a composite head. It typically grows in grasslands and rocky areas, preferring well-drained soils.
