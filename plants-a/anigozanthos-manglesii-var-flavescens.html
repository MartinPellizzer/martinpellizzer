Anigozanthos manglesii var. flavescens

Anigozanthos manglesii var. flavescens, commonly known as the yellow kangaroo paw, is a striking perennial plant native to Western Australia.

It features vivid, spurred flowers that resemble the foot of a kangaroo, with a distinctive yellow-orange hue. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, exhibiting drought tolerance once established. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves, and it produces showy inflorescences that bloom in spring.

This variety is highly valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to Mediterranean climates.
