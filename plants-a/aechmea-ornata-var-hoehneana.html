Aechmea ornata var. hoehneana

Aechmea ornata var. hoehneana, commonly known as the Hoehneana pineapple bromeliad, is a striking epiphytic plant native to the subtropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its rosette of long, narrow, grass-like leaves that form a central cup, which collects water and nutrients. The plant produces an inflorescence with vivid red bracts that enclose small, fragrant white flowers. Its foliage is often adorned with silver or gray markings, adding to its ornamental appeal.

It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate humidity to flourish.
