Athrixia angustissima

Athrixia angustissima, commonly known as the narrow-leaved thornveld grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky outcrops. The plant has slender, erect stems and narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its inflorescence consists of a compact panicle bearing small, spikelet-like flowers.

Athrixia angustissima is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
