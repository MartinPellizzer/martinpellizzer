Aloysia Virgata Var. Elliptica

Aloysia virgata var. elliptica is a unique variant of the native Australian plant Aloysia virgata. It is characterized by its elliptical leaves and is commonly found in coastal regions of southern Australia. This plant is known for its fragrant flowers and ability to thrive in sandy soils. It plays an important role in local ecosystems by providing habitat and nectar for pollinators. Due to its ornamental value, it is also cultivated in gardens across the region.
