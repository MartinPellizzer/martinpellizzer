Amsinckia media

Amsinckia media, commonly known as the desert smokeplant, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small, fluffy spheres. The plant typically grows in arid and semi-arid environments, showcasing its adaptation to dry conditions with its deep root system and reduced leaf surface. Its flowers are yellow to orange in color, and it produces small, wind-dispersed seeds.

Amsinckia media is often used in xeriscaping due to its low water requirements and ornamental value.
