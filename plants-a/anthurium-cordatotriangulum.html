Anthurium cordatotriangulum

Anthurium cordatotriangulum, commonly known as the heart-shaped arrowhead plant, is a tropical species native to the rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its glossy, heart-shaped leaves that are often a deep green with a reddish underside. The plant produces striking spadices, which are colorful, leaf-like structures that house the tiny, inconspicuous flowers. Anthurium cordatotriangulum thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and ornamental purposes.
