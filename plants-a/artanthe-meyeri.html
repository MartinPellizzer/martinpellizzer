Artanthe meyeri

Artanthe meyeri, commonly known as Meyer's artanthe, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the family Amaranthaceae and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, pink to red flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its foliage is often bluish-green, with lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs.

Artanthe meyeri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
