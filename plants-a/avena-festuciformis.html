Avena festuciformis

Avena festuciformis, commonly known as slender oat, is a perennial grass species native to the northern hemisphere.

It is characterized by its fine, grass-like leaves and compact, spike-like inflorescence. The plant typically grows in moist, temperate environments such as meadows and wetlands. Its botanical features include slender stems and small, wind-pollinated flowers.

Avena festuciformis is often used in ecological restoration due to its ability to stabilize soil and support biodiversity.
