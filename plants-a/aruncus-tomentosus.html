Aruncus tomentosus

Aruncus tomentosus, commonly known as the fuzzy goat's beard, is a herbaceous perennial native to eastern North America.

It features tall, branched stems that bear clusters of small, white to pale pink flowers. The plant has soft, hairy foliage that gives it a distinctive, fuzzy appearance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Its botanical characteristics make it a striking addition to gardens with the right growing conditions.
