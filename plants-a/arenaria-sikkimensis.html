Arenaria sikkimensis

Arenaria sikkimensis, commonly known as the Sikkim sandwort, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayan region.

It belongs to the family Caryophyllaceae and is characterized by its low-growing habit and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically thrives in rocky and alpine environments, often found at high elevations. Its leaves are usually linear and covered in fine hairs, while the flowers are pink or white.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in rock gardens.
