Adenia wightiana

Adenia wightiana, commonly known as the Wight's Adenia, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a woody shrub or small tree that typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant features elliptical, dark green leaves with a smooth surface and a slightly waxy texture. Its flowers are usually yellow or greenish-yellow, clustered in umbels, and are often fragrant.

Adenia wightiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
