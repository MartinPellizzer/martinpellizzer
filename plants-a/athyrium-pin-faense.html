Athyrium pin-faense

Athyrium pin-faense, commonly known as the Chinese maidenhair fern, is a species of fern native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the family Athyriaceae and is characterized by its delicate, lace-like fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine in some regions.

The plant's unique foliage makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
