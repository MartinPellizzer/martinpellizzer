Aegopogon cenchroides var. multisetus

Aegopogon cenchroides var. multisetus, commonly known as the hairy dock, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Polygonaceae.

It is characterized by its upright, hairy stems and broad, heart-shaped leaves with a distinct reddish underside. The plant produces small, greenish flowers arranged in dense clusters, which are typically borne on slender pedicels. It is often found in moist, disturbed soils along roadsides and in wetlands.

This variant is distinguished by its dense pubescence, particularly on the stems and leaves, giving it a distinctive appearance.
