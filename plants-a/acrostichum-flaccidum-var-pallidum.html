Acrostichum flaccidum var. pallidum

Acrostichum flaccidum var. pallidum, commonly known as the pale fern, is a variant of the flaccid fern.

It is a perennial plant native to tropical and subtropical regions, particularly in Southeast Asia. The plant features large, broad, and often pale green fronds that are typically divided into multiple pinnae. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found near water bodies.

Its botanical characteristics make it a distinctive and ornamental species in horticulture.
