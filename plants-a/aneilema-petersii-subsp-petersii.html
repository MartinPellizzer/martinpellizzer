Aneilema petersii subsp. petersii

Aneilema petersii subsp.

petersii, commonly known as the Peters' aneilema, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical Africa. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.

The plant features lance-shaped leaves with a glossy surface and produces clusters of small, white or pale green flowers. Its inflorescence is a spike-like structure that bears numerous tiny blooms.

The subspecies is notable for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage.
