Asplenium jiulianshanense

Asplenium jiulianshanense, commonly known as the Jiulianshan shield fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to China.

It belongs to the family Aspleniaceae and is characterized by its compact, shield-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its leaves are usually dark green and have a leathery texture, with a distinctive, narrow shape.

Asplenium jiulianshanense is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphology and limited distribution.
