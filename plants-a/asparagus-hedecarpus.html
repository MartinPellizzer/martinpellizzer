Asparagus hedecarpus

Asparagus hedecarpus, commonly known as the silver asparagus or asparagus fern, is a perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its feathery, arching foliage composed of delicate, needle-like leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous yellow flowers that are followed by green, berry-like fruits. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Its ornamental value comes from its elegant appearance and ability to thrive in a variety of garden settings.
