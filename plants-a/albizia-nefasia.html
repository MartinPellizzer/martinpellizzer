Albizia nefasia

Albizia nefasia, commonly known as the silky albizia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its compound leaves, which are often feather-like and arranged in pairs. The plant produces small, fragrant yellow or greenish-white flowers that bloom in clusters. Its seeds are enclosed in flat, pod-like fruits that split open when mature.

Albizia nefasia is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
