Agathosma filamentosa

Agathosma filamentosa, commonly known as the Cape daisy, is a small, evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It features narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine, hair-like filaments, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, pale yellow to white flowers that bloom in spring and summer. It is well adapted to arid conditions and thrives in full sun with well-drained soil.

Agathosma filamentosa is often used in ornamental gardening for its drought tolerance and attractive foliage.
