Aloe bulbillifera var. paulianae

Aloe bulbillifera var. paulianae, commonly known as the Paulian's Aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a waxy coating, which helps it store water. The plant produces clusters of small, red or orange flowers during the warmer months. Its growth habit is compact, forming a rosette shape that makes it suitable for rock gardens or container planting.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
