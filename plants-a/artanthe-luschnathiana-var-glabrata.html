Artanthe luschnathiana var. glabrata

Artanthe luschnathiana var. glabrata, commonly known as the smooth artanthe, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the eastern United States.

It features basal rosettes of ovate, glabrous (smooth) leaves that are typically bluish-green in color. The plant produces showy, pink to purple flowers in terminal clusters during the summer months. It thrives in moist to wet soils and is often found in swampy areas or along the edges of ponds.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable addition to wetland gardens and native plant collections.
