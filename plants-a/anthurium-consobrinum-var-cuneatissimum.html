Anthurium consobrinum var. cuneatissimum

Anthurium consobrinum var. cuneatissimum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its elongated, narrow leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces striking, spadix-shaped inflorescences surrounded by a vibrant, colorful spathe. It thrives in humid, warm environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Its unique foliage and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
