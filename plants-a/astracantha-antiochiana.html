Astracantha antiochiana

Astracantha antiochiana, commonly known as Antioch's milkweed, is a flowering plant native to California.

It is characterized by its erect stems and opposite, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It is a member of the milkweed family (Asclepiadaceae) and is known for its milky sap.

Astracantha antiochiana is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to thrive in arid conditions.
