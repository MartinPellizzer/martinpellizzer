Astronium

Astronium, commonly known as the starfruit or carambola, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small evergreen tree that produces distinctive five-lobed fruits with a star-shaped cross-section when cut. The leaves are large, simple, and alternate, often with a glossy surface. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Its fruit is widely cultivated for its sweet and tangy flavor, making it a popular fruit in many cuisines.
