Aira melicoides

Aira melicoides, commonly known as slender false oat, is a grass species native to North America.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny flowers. It thrives in a variety of habitats, including meadows and disturbed soils.

Aira melicoides is often used in ecological restoration due to its hardy nature and ability to stabilize soil.
