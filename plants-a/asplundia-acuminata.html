Asplundia acuminata

Asplundia acuminata, commonly known as the sharp-pointed asplundia, is a tropical plant belonging to the Araceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant features a short, stocky rhizome with broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often glossy and dark green. Its inflorescence consists of a spadix surrounded by a spathe, typical of the Araceae family.

Asplundia acuminata is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and unique flowering structure.
