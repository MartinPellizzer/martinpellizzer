Aerangis divitiflora

Aerangis divitiflora, commonly known as the golden orchid, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has long, strap-like leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.
