Alpinia sichuanensis

Alpinia sichuanensis, commonly known as Sichuan ginger, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes other ginger species. The plant is characterized by its tall, rhizomatous growth and narrow, linear leaves. Its inflorescence consists of showy, red or orange flowers that bloom in summer.
