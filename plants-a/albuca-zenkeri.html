Albuca zenkeri

Albuca zenkeri, commonly known as the Zambesi lily, is a perennial bulbous plant native to southern Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces striking, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and containers. It is drought-tolerant and requires minimal maintenance once established.

Albuca zenkeri is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in horticultural displays.
