Anthurium martianum

Anthurium martianum, commonly known as the Martian arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a velvety texture. The plant produces bright red spathes that enclose small, spadices, which are its actual flowers. It thrives in humid, warm environments and requires well-draining soil.

Anthurium martianum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is popular in indoor gardening.
