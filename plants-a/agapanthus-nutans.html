Agapanthus nutans

Agapanthus nutans, commonly known as the African lily, is a striking perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features large, showy blue or purple flower clusters that bloom in summer, creating a dramatic visual impact. The plant has strap-like, evergreen leaves that form a dense rosette at the base. Its rhizomatous root system allows it to thrive in well-drained soils and full sun.

Agapanthus nutans is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
