Aizoon Collinum

Aizoon collinum is a unique flowering plant native to the arid regions of the southwestern United States and northern Mexico. It is known for its striking blue-violet blooms that appear in the spring. This species is adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments with minimal water. Its ability to thrive in such conditions makes it an important part of its ecosystem. Despite its beauty, Aizoon collinum remains relatively obscure in popular horticulture.
