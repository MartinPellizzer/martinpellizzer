Aristida engleri var. engleri

Aristida engleri var. engleri, commonly known as the Engler's threeawn, is a grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences with bracts that are often shorter than the flowering stems. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and open woodlands.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in native plant gardens.
