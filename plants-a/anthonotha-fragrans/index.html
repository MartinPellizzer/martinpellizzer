Anthonotha Fragrans

Anthonotha fragrans is a rare and captivating flowering plant known for its delicate, fragrant blooms. Native to the mountainous regions of South America, this species thrives in cool, moist environments. Its small, white petals emit a sweet aroma that attracts pollinators like bees and butterflies. Due to habitat loss, it is now considered endangered and requires conservation efforts to survive. This plant is a symbol of both natural beauty and ecological fragility.
