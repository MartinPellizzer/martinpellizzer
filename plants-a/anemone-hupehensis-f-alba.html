Anemone hupehensis f. alba

Anemone hupehensis f. alba, commonly known as the white peony, is a cultivar of the Japanese anemone.

It is characterized by its large, white, single flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant features heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its elegant appearance and moderate growth habit.
