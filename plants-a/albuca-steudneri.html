Albuca steudneri

Albuca steudneri, commonly known as the Cape tulip, is a bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features striking, tubular flowers that range in color from white to yellow and orange, often with a distinctive red or purple base. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that form a rosette near the ground. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Albuca steudneri is known for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its vibrant, long-lasting blooms.
