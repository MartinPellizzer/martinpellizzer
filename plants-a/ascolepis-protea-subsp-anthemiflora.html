Ascolepis protea subsp. anthemiflora

Ascolepis protea subsp.

anthemiflora, commonly known as the Cape daisy, is a perennial shrub native to South Africa. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like yellow flowers.

The plant typically grows in coastal regions and has a compact, bushy habit. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, while its inflorescence consists of numerous small florets.

It is well adapted to dry, sunny environments and is often used in ornamental gardening.
