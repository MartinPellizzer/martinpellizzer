Asta schaffneri subsp. schaffneri

Asta schaffneri subsp.

schaffneri, commonly known as the Schaffner's asta, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit.

The plant produces showy, daisy-like flowers in shades of yellow to orange, which bloom in late summer. Its leaves are typically narrow and grass-like, with a slightly hairy texture.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
