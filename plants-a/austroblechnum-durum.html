Austroblechnum durum

Austroblechnum durum, commonly known as the Southern Shield Fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to southeastern Australia.

It belongs to the family Blechnaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, shield-shaped fronds that are typically dark green and leathery. Austroblechnum durum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with suitable conditions.
