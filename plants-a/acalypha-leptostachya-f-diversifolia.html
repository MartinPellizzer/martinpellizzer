Acalypha leptostachya f. diversifolia

Acalypha leptostachya f. diversifolia, commonly known as the Variegated Ironwood, is a striking ornamental plant characterized by its unique foliage.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a striking variegation of green, white, and sometimes pink or red, making it visually appealing. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that are clustered in dense spikes. It is native to tropical regions and thrives in warm, humid climates with well-drained soil.

This cultivar is often used in landscaping for its vibrant leaf color and adaptability to various growing conditions.
