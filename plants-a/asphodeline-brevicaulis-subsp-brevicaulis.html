Asphodeline brevicaulis subsp. brevicaulis

Asphodeline brevicaulis subsp.

brevicaulis, commonly known as the narrow-leaved asphodel, is a perennial plant native to southern Africa. It features succulent, grass-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color and grow in dense rosettes.

The plant produces tall, slender flowering stems that bear small, white to pale yellow flowers in early to mid-summer. It is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it drought-tolerant and suitable for xeriscaping.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
