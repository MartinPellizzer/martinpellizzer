Aechmea × fulgofascicata

Aechmea × fulgofascicata, commonly known as the fiery torch bromeliad, is a hybrid plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, spiky inflorescence that resembles a torch, often displaying vibrant red, orange, or yellow hues. The plant forms a rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves that collect water, creating a small pool that supports various microorganisms. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil with high humidity.

This bromeliad is popular as a decorative plant due to its unique appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.
