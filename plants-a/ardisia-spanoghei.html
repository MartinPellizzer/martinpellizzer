Ardisia spanoghei

Ardisia spanoghei, commonly known as the Greek star anise, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its small, dark purple berries that are clustered in star-like formations. The plant produces white or pinkish flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are opposite, oval-shaped, and have a slightly glossy texture.

Ardisia spanoghei is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
