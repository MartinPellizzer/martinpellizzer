Amelanchier sinensis

Amelanchier sinensis, commonly known as Chinese serviceberry, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its ornamental value, featuring showy white or pink flowers in spring. The plant produces small, edible berries that ripen in summer, which are often used in culinary applications. Its leaves turn vibrant shades of red and orange in autumn, adding to its aesthetic appeal.

Amelanchier sinensis is also valued for its ecological benefits, providing food for birds and other wildlife.
