Angelonia goyazensis

Angelonia goyazensis, commonly known as the Brazilian angelonia, is a flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the family Plantaginaceae and is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that come in shades of red, pink, and purple. Angelonia goyazensis is valued in horticulture for its long blooming period and adaptability to various growing conditions.
