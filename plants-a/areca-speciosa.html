Areca speciosa

Areca speciosa, commonly known as the areca palm, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnately compound leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.

Areca speciosa is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for landscaping in tropical and subtropical regions.
