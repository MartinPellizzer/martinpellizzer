Ayenia noblickii

Ayenia noblickii, commonly known as the noble ayenia, is a rare flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and latex-producing structures. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats, showcasing adaptations to arid environments. Its leaves are usually narrow and hairy, helping to reduce water loss.
