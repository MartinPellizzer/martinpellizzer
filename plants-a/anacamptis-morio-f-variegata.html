Anacamptis morio f. variegata

Anacamptis morio f. variegata, commonly known as the Variegated Green-winged Orchid, is a striking orchid species native to southern Europe.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem and showy, greenish-yellow flowers with distinctive purple veins. The leaves are lance-shaped and often have a silvery or variegated appearance, contributing to its ornamental value. This orchid thrives in sunny, well-drained habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils.

It is a perennial plant that blooms in late spring to early summer, adding vibrant color to its native environment.
