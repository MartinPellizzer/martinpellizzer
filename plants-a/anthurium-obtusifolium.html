Anthurium obtusifolium

Anthurium obtusifolium, commonly known as the blunt-leaf anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces striking spadices, which are modified leaves that enclose the actual flowers, often in vibrant red or orange hues. Anthurium obtusifolium thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical landscapes.
