Anthurium ingramii

Anthurium ingramii, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically deep green with a reddish or maroon tint. The plant produces vibrant, spatulate-shaped flowers that are usually bright red or orange, which are highly prized in horticulture. Anthurium ingramii thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.

It is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
