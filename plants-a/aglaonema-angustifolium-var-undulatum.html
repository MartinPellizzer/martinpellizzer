Aglaonema angustifolium var. undulatum

Aglaonema angustifolium var. undulatum, commonly known as the wavy-leaf aglaonema, is a tropical evergreen plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features dark green, wavy-edged leaves that are often adorned with striking white or cream veins, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant thrives in low-light conditions and requires moderate humidity to maintain its vibrant foliage. It is prized for its ornamental value and is often used as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.

Aglaonema angustifolium var. undulatum is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
