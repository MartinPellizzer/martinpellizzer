Anthericum robustulum

Anthericum robustulum, commonly known as the broad-leaved arum, is a herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It features large, arrow-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a prominent midrib. The plant produces a spadix surrounded by a showy, violet-colored spathe, which is typical of the Araceae family. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Anthericum robustulum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting pollinators.
