Asplundia pastazana

Asplundia pastazana, commonly known as the Pastaza arrowroot, is a herbaceous plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its arrow-shaped leaves and striking inflorescence. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in montane forests. Its rhizomes are used in traditional medicine and as a source of starch.
