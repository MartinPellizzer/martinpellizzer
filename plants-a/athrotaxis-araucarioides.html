Athrotaxis araucarioides

Athrotaxis araucarioides, commonly known as the mountain araucaria, is a unique coniferous tree native to Tasmania.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and dense, pyramidal crown. The tree has scale-like leaves that are arranged in spirals along the branches. It produces small, woody cones that contain edible seeds.

This species is adapted to cool, moist climates and is often found in mountainous regions with well-drained soils.
