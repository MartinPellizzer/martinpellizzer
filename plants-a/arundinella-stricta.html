Arundinella stricta

Arundinella stricta, commonly known as bamboo grass, is a perennial grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems that can grow up to several meters in height. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers and has narrow, linear leaves that are often arranged in dense clumps. It is often found in wetlands, marshes, and along riverbanks due to its preference for moist environments.

Arundinella stricta is known for its rapid growth and ability to form dense stands, which can have ecological impacts in certain areas.
