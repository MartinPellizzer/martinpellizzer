Anthotium humile f. typica

Anthotium humile f. typica, commonly known as the small anthotium, is a member of the Araceae family.

It is a compact, herbaceous plant native to South America, particularly Brazil. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and produces small, inconspicuous flowers in a spadix, typical of the Araceae family. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as a houseplant.

Its botanical characteristics make it an interesting subject for both horticultural and taxonomic study.
