Alternanthera acaulis

Alternanthera acaulis, commonly known as the upright Alternanthera, is a perennial herb native to South America.

It is characterized by its compact, upright growth habit and strikingly colored foliage, often featuring shades of red, pink, and purple. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow, arranged in dense spikes. Its leaves are opposite and ovate, with a waxy texture that helps reduce water loss.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant in water gardens and bog environments.
