Arenaria pharensis

Arenaria pharensis, commonly known as the coastal sandwort, is a low-growing flowering plant native to the coastal regions of the Mediterranean.

It thrives in sandy and saline soils, showcasing its adaptability to harsh coastal environments. The plant produces small, white or pink flowers during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are typically narrow and succulent, helping to reduce water loss in arid conditions.

Arenaria pharensis plays a role in stabilizing dunes and supporting local biodiversity in coastal ecosystems.
