Antidesma cubanum

Antidesma cubanum, commonly known as Cuban antidesma, is a small evergreen shrub native to the Caribbean region.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves that are usually elliptical in shape. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas and lowland forests.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation efforts.
