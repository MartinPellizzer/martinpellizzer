Aster pimeleoides

Aster pimeleoides, commonly known as the narrowleaf aster, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads composed of ray and disk flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. Its leaves are narrow and linear, and it produces small blue to purple flowers during the summer months.

Aster pimeleoides is often used in native plant gardens due to its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
