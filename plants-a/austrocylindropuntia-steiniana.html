Austrocylindropuntia steiniana

Austrocylindropuntia steiniana, commonly known as the Stein's cylindrical cactus, is a species native to Argentina.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems that are ribbed and covered in spines. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, requiring well-drained soil and full sun exposure.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
