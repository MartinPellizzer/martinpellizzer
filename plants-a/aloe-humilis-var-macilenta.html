Aloe humilis var. macilenta

Aloe humilis var. macilenta, commonly known as the dwarf aloe or narrow-leaved aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, rosette-forming growth with narrow, fleshy leaves that are typically green to bluish-green in color. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers on tall, branched inflorescences during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its slow growth rate and ornamental appearance make it a popular choice for collectors and gardeners in dry climates.
