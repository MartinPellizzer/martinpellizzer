Agrostis chilensis

Agrostis chilensis, commonly known as the Chilean bentgrass, is a perennial grass species native to South America.

It is characterized by its fine, tufted growth habit and slender, dark green leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and along riverbanks. It produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences that contain both male and female flowers.

Agrostis chilensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in landscaping and erosion control.
