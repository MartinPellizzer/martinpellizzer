Allium tokaliense

Allium tokaliense, commonly known as the Tokali onion, is a species of wild onion native to Turkey.

It is a hardy perennial plant that typically grows in dry, rocky soils. The plant features basal leaves that are narrow and linear, with a strong, pungent odor. Its flowers are small and clustered in umbels, producing white to pale purple blooms.

This plant is valued for its culinary and medicinal uses in traditional practices.
