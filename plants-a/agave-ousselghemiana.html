Agave ousselghemiana

Agave ousselghemiana, commonly known as the Ousselghem agave, is a striking succulent native to the arid regions of northern Mexico.

It features a rosette of thick, blue-green, sword-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine, white bloom of hair, which helps reduce water loss. The plant produces a tall, branched inflorescence with yellow flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it well-suited for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
