Adelbertia

Adelbertia, commonly known as the New Zealand tree fern, is a member of the Cyatheaceae family and is native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds that can reach several meters in length. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in coastal forests and along stream banks. Its unique appearance and ecological significance make it an important component of New Zealand's native flora.
