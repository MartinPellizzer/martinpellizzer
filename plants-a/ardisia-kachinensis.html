Ardisia kachinensis

Ardisia kachinensis, commonly known as Kachin evergreen or Kachin ardisia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in regions of Myanmar, Thailand, and Vietnam. The plant is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and produces small, red berries that are attractive to birds. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in montane forests.

Ardisia kachinensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and has limited use in traditional medicine.
