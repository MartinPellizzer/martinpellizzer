Anoectochilus × croesus

Anoectochilus × croesus, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a hybrid between Anoectochilus macranthus and Anoectochilus roxburghii, resulting in a plant with unique floral characteristics. The species is known for its vibrant, showy flowers that typically range in color from yellow to orange and red. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-draining, nutrient-rich soil.

This orchid is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
