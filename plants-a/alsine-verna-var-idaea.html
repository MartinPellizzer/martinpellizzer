Alsine verna var. idaea

Alsine verna var. idaea, commonly known as the spring sand-verbena, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a low-growing annual herb with hairy stems and small, clustered leaves. The plant produces delicate blue to violet flowers that bloom in early spring. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in open, disturbed areas.

Its botanical features make it well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.
