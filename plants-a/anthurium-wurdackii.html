Anthurium wurdackii

Anthurium wurdackii, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a metallic sheen. The plant produces vibrant red spathes that enclose its spadix, which is often covered in tiny, white to yellowish flowers. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium wurdackii is popular as a decorative plant due to its ornamental foliage and unique floral structure.
