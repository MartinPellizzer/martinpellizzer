Aceras × anthropophora-simia

Aceras × anthropophora-simia, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a cross between two species of orchids, *Anthropophora* and *Simia*.

It is a tropical plant characterized by its striking, showy flowers that often feature vibrant colors such as red, orange, and yellow. The plant has a complex inflorescence with multiple flowers arranged along a single stem. It requires a warm, humid environment with bright, indirect light to thrive.

Botanically, it is notable for its intricate floral structure and the genetic diversity it represents in hybrid orchid breeding.
