Ardisia sieboldii f. rubricarpa

Ardisia sieboldii f. rubricarpa, commonly known as the red-fruited ardisia, is a compact evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a leathery texture. The plant produces small, white flowers followed by bright red, fleshy berries that are highly ornamental. It thrives in partial to full shade and prefers moist, well-drained soil.

This variety is valued in horticulture for its attractive foliage and showy fruiting display.
