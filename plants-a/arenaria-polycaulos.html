Arenaria polycaulos

Arenaria polycaulos, commonly known as the many-stemmed sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to arctic and alpine regions.

It typically features a mat-forming growth habit with numerous slender, branching stems. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in early summer. Its leaves are usually narrow and covered in fine hairs, adapted to withstand cold and windy conditions.

Arenaria polycaulos thrives in well-drained, sandy or rocky soils and is often found in tundra and mountainous environments.
