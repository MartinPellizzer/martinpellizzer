Aponogeton involucratus

Aponogeton involucratus, commonly known as the African water lettuce, is a floating aquatic plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It has broad, waxy, heart-shaped leaves that are typically light green to dark green in color. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are often submerged or just at the water's surface. It thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments and is often found in ponds and lakes.

Aponogeton involucratus is valued for its ornamental appearance and ability to provide habitat for aquatic life.
