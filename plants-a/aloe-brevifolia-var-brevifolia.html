Aloe brevifolia var. brevifolia

Aloe brevifolia var. brevifolia, commonly known as the short-leaved aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features compact, rosette-shaped leaves that are thick and fleshy, adapted for storing water in arid environments. The leaves are typically gray-green with white teeth along the edges, giving it a distinctive appearance. It produces yellow flowers in clusters during the warmer months, which attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.

This plant is well-suited for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its low water requirements and hardy nature.
