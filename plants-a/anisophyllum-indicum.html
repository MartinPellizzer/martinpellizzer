Anisophyllum indicum

Anisophyllum indicum, commonly known as Indian feather fern, is a perennial fern native to the Indian subcontinent.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds that are typically light green and have a lacy appearance. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories. It has rhizomatous growth and produces sporangia on the undersides of its leaves.

Anisophyllum indicum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
