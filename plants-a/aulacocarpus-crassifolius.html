Aulacocarpus crassifolius

Aulacocarpus crassifolius, commonly known as the broadleaf aulacocarpus, is a tropical plant belonging to the family Aulacocarpaceae.

It is characterized by its large, thick, leathery leaves that are typically elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually greenish or yellowish in color. Its fruit is a woody capsule that contains several seeds.

This species is native to parts of Central and South America, where it thrives in humid, tropical environments.
