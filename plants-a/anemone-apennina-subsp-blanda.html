Anemone apennina subsp. blanda

Anemone apennina subsp.

blanda, commonly known as the soft anemone, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe. It features showy, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring.

The plant has palmately divided, lobed leaves that are typically hairy and have a distinctive bluish-green color. It prefers moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or streamside habitats.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable addition to native plant gardens and ecological restoration projects.
