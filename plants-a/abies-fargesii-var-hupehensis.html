Abies fargesii var. hupehensis

Abies fargesii var. hupehensis, commonly known as the Hupeh Fir, is a coniferous evergreen tree native to the mountainous regions of central and southern China.

It belongs to the family Pinaceae and is characterized by its pyramidal shape and needle-like leaves that are blue-green in color. The cones are typically pendulous and contain winged seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and parks for its aesthetic and ecological benefits.
