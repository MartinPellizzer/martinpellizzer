Abrodictyum cumingii

Abrodictyum cumingii, commonly known as the Cuming's spider orchid, is a rare and striking orchid species native to the tropical regions of South America.

The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories where it can receive filtered sunlight. Due to habitat loss and its limited distribution, Abrodictyum cumingii is considered a vulnerable species in its native range.
