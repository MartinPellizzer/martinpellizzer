Adromischus umbraticola

Adromischus umbraticola, commonly known as the shade-loving crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, rosette-forming leaves that are typically green with a bluish or purplish hue. The plant thrives in partial to full shade and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. It produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and shaded rock gardens.
