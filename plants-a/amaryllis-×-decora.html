Amaryllis × decora

Amaryllis × decora, commonly known as the decorative amaryllis, is a hybrid plant that combines traits from various amaryllis species.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that come in a range of colors, including red, pink, white, and yellow. The plant typically produces multiple flower spikes from a single bulb, making it a striking addition to gardens or indoor spaces. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, with a dormant period during the winter months.

Amaryllis × decora is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for both novice and experienced gardeners.
