Antirrhinum Majus Subsp. Cirrhigerum

Antirrhinum majus subsp. cirrhigerum is a unique variant of the common snapdragon known for its distinctive floral features. This subspecies thrives in specific ecological niches, often found in rocky or arid environments. Its flowers display a range of colors, from vibrant reds to soft pinks, attracting various pollinators. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively obscure compared to its more widespread relatives. Studying this plant offers insights into plant adaptation and biodiversity in challenging habitats.
