Aponogeton capuronii

Aponogeton capuronii, commonly known as the Capuron's arrowhead, is a striking aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features broad, heart-shaped leaves that float on the water's surface, often with a waxy texture. The plant produces attractive white or pale pink flowers that bloom above the water, adding visual interest to ponds and aquariums. It thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments with moderate lighting.

Aponogeton capuronii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of care in aquatic settings.
