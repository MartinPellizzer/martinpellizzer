Actinostemma lobatum f. subintegrum

Actinostemma lobatum f. subintegrum, commonly known as the smooth-leaved actinostemma, is a variant of the species Actinostemma lobatum, which belongs to the family Apiaceae.

This plant is characterized by its smooth, unbranched stems and compound leaves with a distinctive, lobed appearance. The flowers are small and white, arranged in umbels, typical of the Apiaceae family. It is native to certain regions of North America and is often found in moist, open habitats.

The subspecies subintegrum is distinguished by its more densely packed, smooth foliage compared to other forms of the species.
