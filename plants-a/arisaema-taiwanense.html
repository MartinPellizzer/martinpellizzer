Arisaema taiwanense

Arisaema taiwanense, commonly known as the Taiwan arrowhead, is a flowering plant native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive spatulate leaves and inflorescence. The plant produces a spadix surrounded by a modified leaf called a spathe, which is often brightly colored. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically found in mountainous regions.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has cultural significance in traditional medicine.
