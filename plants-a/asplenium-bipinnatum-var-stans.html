Asplenium bipinnatum var. stans

Asplenium bipinnatum var. stans, commonly known as the Stans' spleenwort, is a fern species belonging to the Aspleniaceae family.

It is characterized by its finely divided, bipinnate leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine hairs. This variety typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as rock crevices or on the trunks of trees. Its fronds are usually dark green and can reach lengths of up to 30 centimeters.

The plant is known for its slow growth and is often found in temperate regions with high humidity.
