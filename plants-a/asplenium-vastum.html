Asplenium vastum

Asplenium vastum, commonly known as the broad fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, leathery, lance-shaped leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length. The fronds are typically dark green and have a distinctive, broad, and somewhat wavy appearance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is commonly cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
