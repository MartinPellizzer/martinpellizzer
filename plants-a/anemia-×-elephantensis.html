Anemia × elephantensis

Anemia × elephantensis, commonly known as the African daisy, is a hybrid plant native to southern Africa.

It is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in open, sunny areas and thrives in well-drained soils. It produces yellow or orange flowers with a central disk, and its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like.

Anemia × elephantensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
