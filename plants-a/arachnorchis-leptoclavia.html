Arachnorchis leptoclavia

Arachnorchis leptoclavia, commonly known as the slender spider-orchid, is an epiphytic orchid native to Australia.

It is characterized by its delicate, spider-like appearance with narrow, hairy stems and small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. Its roots are specialized for absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air.

Arachnorchis leptoclavia is a rare and protected species, often found in the subtropical regions of Queensland.
