Alchemilla vulgaris var. crinita

Alchemilla vulgaris var. crinita, commonly known as the silky ladies' mantle, is a perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, fuzzy foliage that gives it a soft, silvery appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in early summer, which are clustered in spherical inflorescences. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas or along stream banks.

Its unique texture and ornamental qualities make it a popular choice for garden use.
