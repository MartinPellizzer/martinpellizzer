Adnaria retusa

Adnaria retusa, commonly known as the sea holly, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Apiaceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive blue or purple flowers and feathery leaves, which are often used in herbal medicine. Adnaria retusa is also valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens and its historical use in traditional remedies.
