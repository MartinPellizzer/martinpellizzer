Aster schaffneri

Aster schaffneri, commonly known as Schaffner's aster, is a perennial herb native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and showy blooms. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as woodlands and wet meadows. Its flowers are usually blue to purple in color, with ray florets surrounding a central disk.

Aster schaffneri is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, providing nectar for pollinators.
