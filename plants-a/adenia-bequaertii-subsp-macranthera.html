Adenia bequaertii subsp. macranthera

Adenia bequaertii subsp.

macranthera, commonly known as the large-flowered Adenia, is a shrub native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its woody stems and large, showy yellow flowers that bloom in summer.

The plant has pinnately compound leaves with serrated margins, and its roots are often tuberous. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or coastal areas.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in horticultural collections.
