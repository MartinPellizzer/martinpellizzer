Arabis Gemmifera F. Alpicola

Arabis gemmifera f. alpicola is a rare and delicate plant species native to high altitude environments. It belongs to the Brassicaceae family and is known for its small, white flowers that bloom in late spring. This alpine variant thrives in rocky soils and cool, moist climates. Its unique growth form and limited distribution make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists. Protecting this plant is crucial for maintaining biodiversity in mountain ecosystems.
