Aloe chrysostachys

Aloe chrysostachys, commonly known as the golden aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a yellowish or reddish tint. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with small, yellow flowers that bloom in summer. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Aloe chrysostachys is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional medicinal uses.
