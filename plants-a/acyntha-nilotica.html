Acyntha nilotica

Acyntha nilotica, commonly known as the Egyptian nightshade, is a flowering plant native to Africa and the Middle East.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually white or pale blue, which bloom in the evening. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

The fruit is a capsule that contains several small, black seeds.
