Abrotanum platylobum

Abrotanum platylobum, commonly known as narrow-leaved bushveld daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces small, yellow flowers arranged in a flat-topped cluster. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grassland and rocky habitats.

Abrotanum platylobum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
