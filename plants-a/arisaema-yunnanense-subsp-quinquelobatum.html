Arisaema yunnanense subsp. quinquelobatum

Arisaema yunnanense subsp.

quinquelobatum, commonly known as the Yunnan arrowhead, is a species native to southwestern China. It is characterized by its distinctive, arrow-shaped leaves and a spadix surrounded by a broad, showy spathe.

The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its reproductive structures are adapted for pollination by insects, and it produces berries that contain toxic seeds.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
