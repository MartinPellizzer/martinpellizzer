Astilbe thunbergii

Astilbe thunbergii, commonly known as Japanese feather reed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to East Asia.

It features delicate, plume-like inflorescences that range in color from white to pink and purple, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has compound, lance-shaped leaves that are often bronze or reddish in color, providing visual interest even when not in bloom. It thrives in moist, partially shaded environments and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.

Astilbe thunbergii is relatively low maintenance and resistant to common pests and diseases.
