Agrostis kuntzei

Agrostis kuntzei, commonly known as the slender meadow grass, is a perennial grass species native to temperate regions of Asia and Europe.

It typically grows in open habitats such as meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils. The plant has slender, erect stems that often reach heights of 30 to 60 centimeters. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue, and it produces small, spike-like inflorescences.

Agrostis kuntzei is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in landscaping and erosion control.
