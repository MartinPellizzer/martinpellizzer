Aneilema bracteatum

Aneilema bracteatum, commonly known as the velvetleaf or African violet, is a tropical plant native to Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, velvety leaves that are often dark green in color. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to purple. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Aneilema bracteatum is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and flowering habit.
