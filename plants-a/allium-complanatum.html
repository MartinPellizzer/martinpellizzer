Allium complanatum

Allium complanatum, commonly known as the flat-leaved garlic, is a flowering plant in the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is native to the eastern United States and thrives in moist, well-drained soils. The plant features grass-like basal leaves and produces clusters of small, white, star-shaped flowers. Its bulbous roots are used in traditional medicine and culinary applications.

Allium complanatum is also valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
