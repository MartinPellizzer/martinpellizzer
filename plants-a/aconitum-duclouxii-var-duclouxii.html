Aconitum duclouxii var. duclouxii

Aconitum duclouxii var. duclouxii, commonly known as the Ducloux aconite, is a member of the Ranunculaceae family.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. The plant features showy, blue to purple spurred flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are typically palmately compound, with a deeply lobed structure.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in alpine or subalpine habitats.
