Asarum nakaianum

Asarum nakaianum, commonly known as the New Zealand asarum, is a member of the Aristolochiaceae family and is native to New Zealand.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest undergrowth. The plant features heart-shaped leaves and produces small, irregularly shaped flowers that are usually greenish or brownish in color. Its rhizomatous root system is notable for its aromatic and medicinal properties.

Asarum nakaianum is also recognized for its ecological importance in supporting local biodiversity.
