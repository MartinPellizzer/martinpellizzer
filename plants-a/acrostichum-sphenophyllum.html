Acrostichum sphenophyllum

Acrostichum sphenophyllum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Blechnaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, linear fronds that are typically less than 30 centimeters in length. This fern is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
