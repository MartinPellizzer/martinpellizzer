Actinostemon estrellensis var. latifolius

Actinostemon estrellensis var. latifolius, commonly known as the broadleaf starstem, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the southeastern United States.

It features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are broad and often have a waxy texture. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in the spring. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or swampy areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a desirable plant for native gardens and restoration projects.
