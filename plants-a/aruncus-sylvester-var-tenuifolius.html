Aruncus sylvester var. tenuifolius

Aruncus sylvester var. tenuifolius, commonly known as the slenderleaf goat's beard, is a herbaceous perennial native to eastern North America.

It features striking, feathery plumes of white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has finely divided, fern-like leaves that are narrow and slightly hairy. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodlands and along stream banks.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable addition to native plant gardens and ecological restoration projects.
