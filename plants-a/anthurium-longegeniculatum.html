Anthurium longegeniculatum

Anthurium longegeniculatum, commonly known as the longegenericulate anthurium, is a member of the Araceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves with a glossy surface and striking, spadix-like inflorescences. It thrives in humid, warm environments with indirect light.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and botanical collections.
