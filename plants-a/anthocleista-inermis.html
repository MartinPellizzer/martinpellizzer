Anthocleista inermis

Anthocleista inermis, commonly known as the African moonseed, is a perennial shrub native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Loganiaceae and is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves and showy, yellowish-green flowers. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain small, smooth seeds. It is often found in moist, shaded habitats such as forest understories.

Due to its medicinal properties, it has been used in traditional African medicine for various ailments.
