Alternanthera rigida

Alternanthera rigida, commonly known as the red flame plant, is a species of flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that can range in color from deep red to orange and purple, depending on light exposure. It is a herbaceous perennial that thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full sun.

Alternanthera rigida is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its vibrant, showy leaves.
