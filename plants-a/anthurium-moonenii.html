Anthurium moonenii

Anthurium moonenii, commonly known as the Moonen's arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are often a deep green with a velvety texture. The plant produces clusters of spadices, which are typically green or yellow, surrounded by brightly colored bracts that resemble flowers. Anthurium moonenii thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and botanical collections.
