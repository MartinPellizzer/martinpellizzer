Anagallis monelli var. lilacina

Anagallis monelli var. lilacina, commonly known as the lilac morning glory, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

It is a herbaceous annual with slender, trailing stems that often grow along the ground. The plant produces small, delicate flowers in shades of lilac and pink, which bloom in the early morning. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and covered in fine hairs.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
