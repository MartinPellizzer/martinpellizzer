Ammocallis rosea

Ammocallis rosea, commonly known as the pink iris, is a perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features striking pink to lavender flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that form a dense clump. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Ammocallis rosea is valued for its ornamental beauty and relatively low maintenance requirements.
