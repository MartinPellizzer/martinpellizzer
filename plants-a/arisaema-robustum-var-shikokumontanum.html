Arisaema robustum var. shikokumontanum

Arisaema robustum var. shikokumontanum, commonly known as the Shikoku mountain arisaema, is a herbaceous plant native to Japan.

It features a distinctive, spadix-like inflorescence surrounded by a large, hooded spathe. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its leaves are broad and heart-shaped, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

The species is valued for its ornamental appearance and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage and floral structure.
