Aster spithamaeus

Aster spithamaeus, commonly known as the New Zealand aster, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in coastal and subalpine regions. The plant features showy blue to violet flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, giving them a velvety texture.

Aster spithamaeus plays an important role in the local ecosystem, providing nectar for pollinators and habitat for various insects.
