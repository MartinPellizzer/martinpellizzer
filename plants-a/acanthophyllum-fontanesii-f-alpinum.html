Acanthophyllum fontanesii f. alpinum

Acanthophyllum fontanesii f. alpinum, commonly known as the alpine acanthophyllum, is a flowering plant belonging to the Amaranthaceae family.

It is a low-growing shrub characterized by its dense, spiny foliage and small, clustered flowers. Native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, this plant thrives in arid and semi-arid environments. Its adaptations include reduced leaf size and thick cuticles to minimize water loss.

It plays a vital role in stabilizing soils and providing habitat for small desert animals.
