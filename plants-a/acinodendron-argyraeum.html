Acinodendron argyraeum

Acinodendron argyraeum, commonly known as the silverleaf acinodendron, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes in suitable climates.
