Aeschynanthus hians

Aeschynanthus hians, commonly known as the "Red-veined Orchid" or "Climbing Orchid," is a tropical shrub native to western Africa.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and striking, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant produces small, elliptical leaves with prominent veins, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Aeschynanthus hians is often cultivated as a houseplant or in hanging baskets due to its attractive foliage and vibrant blooms.
