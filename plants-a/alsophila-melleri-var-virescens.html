Alsophila melleri var. virescens

Alsophila melleri var. virescens, commonly known as the green-ferns tree fern, is a species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and a crown of feathery, evergreen fronds that give it a lush, tropical appearance. The plant belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is known for its ability to thrive in humid, shaded environments. Its fronds are typically bright green and can grow quite long, contributing to its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
