Adromischus caryophyllaceus

Adromischus caryophyllaceus, commonly known as the silver leaf kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, silver-green leaves that are covered in fine, white hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, pink to purple flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect sunlight.

Adromischus caryophyllaceus is drought-tolerant and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and low maintenance requirements.
