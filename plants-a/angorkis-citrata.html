Angorkis citrata

Angorkis citrata, commonly known as the citron tree, is a small evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, lemon-yellow flowers and oval, glossy leaves. The fruit is a large, citrus-like citrus fruit with a thick, peels and a sweet, aromatic pulp. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and for the essential oils extracted from its leaves and flowers.

Angorkis citrata is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
