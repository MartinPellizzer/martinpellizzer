Actinomeris helianthoides var. nuttallii

Actinomeris helianthoides var. nuttallii, commonly known as the Nuttall's sunflower, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. Its yellow flowers are arranged in a cluster, with ray florets surrounding a central disk.

The species is notable for its drought tolerance and ability to thrive in poor soils.
