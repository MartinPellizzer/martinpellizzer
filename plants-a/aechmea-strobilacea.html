Aechmea strobilacea

Aechmea strobilacea, commonly known as the pineapple bromeliad, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaf structure that collects water. The plant produces a tall, branched inflorescence with small, showy flowers. Its leaves are typically blue-green in color and have a waxy coating.
