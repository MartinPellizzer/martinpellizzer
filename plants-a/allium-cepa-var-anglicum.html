Allium cepa var. anglicum

Allium cepa var. anglicum, commonly known as the English onion, is a variety of the common onion.

It is characterized by its bulbous stem and long, narrow leaves. The plant produces white to pale pink flowers in umbels, which are typical of the Allium genus. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a hardy crop in temperate climates.

This variety is often used in culinary applications for its mild, sweet flavor compared to other onion types.
