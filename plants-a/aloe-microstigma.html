Aloe microstigma

Aloe microstigma, commonly known as the narrow-stigma aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, with a waxy coating to reduce water loss. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in a dense inflorescence, which bloom during the summer months. Aloe microstigma is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its leaves and stems.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and low maintenance requirements.
