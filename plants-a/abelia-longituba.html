Abelia longituba

Abelia longituba, commonly known as the Chinese abelia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features slender, arching stems and small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in late summer. The leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a smooth texture with a slightly hairy underside. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained soil.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various garden conditions.
