Allium haneltii

Allium haneltii, commonly known as the Hanelt's onion, is a species of flowering plant in the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, particularly in the Tian Shan and Pamir Mountains. The plant produces a cluster of narrow, grass-like leaves and features a bulb that is often used in traditional medicine. Allium haneltii is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
