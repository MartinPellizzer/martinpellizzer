Artemisia chitachensis

Artemisia chitachensis, commonly known as the Chitachen wormwood, is a perennial shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, silvery-gray foliage. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are typically clustered in dense heads. It thrives in dry, well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or alpine environments.

Due to its medicinal properties, it has been traditionally used in herbal remedies.
