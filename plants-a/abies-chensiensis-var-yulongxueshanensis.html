Abies chensiensis var. yulongxueshanensis

Abies chensiensis var. yulongxueshanensis, commonly known as the Yulong Snow Mountain fir, is a rare and endemic coniferous species native to the Yulong Xueshan mountain range in Yunnan, China.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and dense, pyramidal crown, which is adapted to the high-altitude, cold climate of its habitat. The needles are dark green, flat, and have a waxy coating, which helps reduce water loss in its arid environment. It produces cones that are typically pendulous and contain small, winged seeds.

This variety is of significant botanical interest due to its unique morphology and ecological role in alpine ecosystems.
