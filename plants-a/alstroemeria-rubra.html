Alstroemeria rubra

Alstroemeria rubra, commonly known as the Peruvian lily, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the family Alstroemeriaceae and is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant typically grows to a height of 30 to 60 centimeters, with long, strap-like leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Alstroemeria rubra is also valued for its ornamental qualities and is often used in floral arrangements.
