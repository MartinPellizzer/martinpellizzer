Acrostichum ornatum

Acrostichum ornatum, commonly known as the golden fern, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a member of the fern family and is characterized by its large, feathery fronds that can grow up to several feet in length. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires consistent moisture to flourish. Its leaves are typically a bright yellow-green, which gives it its common name.

Acrostichum ornatum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.
