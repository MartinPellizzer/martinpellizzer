Arenaria katoana var. katoana

Arenaria katoana var. katoana, commonly known as Katoan sandflower, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Japan.

It typically features dense clusters of small, white or pink flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has succulent, fleshy leaves that help it conserve water in its rocky, well-drained habitats. It thrives in full sun and is often found in alpine meadows and rocky slopes.

Its botanical characteristics make it a hardy and ornamental species suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
