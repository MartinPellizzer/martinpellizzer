Achillea × kolbiana

Achillea × kolbiana, commonly known as the hybrid yarrow, is a perennial plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is a natural hybrid between Achillea millefolium (common yarrow) and Achillea ptarmica (tall yarrow), resulting in a plant with intermediate characteristics. The plant features finely divided, fern-like leaves and produces clusters of small, flat-topped flower heads in shades of white, pink, or red. It is often found in disturbed soils and open habitats, thriving in full sun.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice in gardens and landscapes.
