Amaryllis petiolata

Amaryllis petiolata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved amaryllis, is a species native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, strap-like leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces striking, showy flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, often with a strong fragrance. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Amaryllis petiolata is a popular ornamental plant due to its elegant appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
