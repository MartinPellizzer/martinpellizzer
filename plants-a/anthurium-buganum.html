Anthurium buganum

Anthurium buganum, commonly known as the velvet leaf anthurium, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are velvety to the touch and come in shades of green, red, and purple. The plant produces showy, spadix-shaped inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, which adds to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in humid, warm environments with bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Anthurium buganum is popular as a houseplant due to its aesthetic value and relatively easy care requirements.
