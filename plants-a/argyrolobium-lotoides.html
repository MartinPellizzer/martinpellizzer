Argyrolobium lotoides

Argyrolobium lotoides, commonly known as the silver globular pea, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive silver-gray foliage and small, globular, green pods that contain edible seeds. The plant belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and it has a creeping growth habit with hairy stems. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

The flowers are typically pale yellow or white, adding ornamental value to gardens.
