Asplenium × huawuense

Asplenium × huawuense, commonly known as the Huawu shield fern, is a hybrid fern species native to China.

It belongs to the family Aspleniaceae and is characterized by its compact, evergreen foliage. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often found in rocky or forested areas. Its fronds are leathery and have a distinctive shield-like shape, with a dark green coloration.

Asplenium × huawuense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
