Aechmea blanchetii

Aechmea blanchetii, commonly known as the white-tipped pineapple plant, is a species of bromeliad native to South America.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that form a central cup, which collects water and nutrients. The plant produces vibrant orange or red flowers that bloom from the center of the rosette. Its leaves are thick and fleshy, adapted to store water in arid environments.

Aechmea blanchetii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and air-purifying properties.
