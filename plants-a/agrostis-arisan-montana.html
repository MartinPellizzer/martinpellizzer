Agrostis arisan-montana

Agrostis arisan-montana, commonly known as the Arisan mountain grass, is a perennial grass species native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its fine, tufted growth habit and narrow, blade-like leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers.

Agrostis arisan-montana is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in stabilizing soil in its native habitats.
