Arctotis decumbens

Arctotis decumbens, commonly known as the common arctotis, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a low-growing, mat-forming perennial that produces vibrant blue or purple daisy-like flowers. The plant features narrow, linear leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming period, which extends from spring to autumn.
