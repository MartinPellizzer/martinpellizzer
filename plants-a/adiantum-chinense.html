Adiantum chinense

Adiantum chinense, commonly known as the Chinese maidenhair fern, is a member of the Adiantaceae family.

It is a delicate, evergreen fern characterized by its finely divided, bioluminescent fronds that shimmer in the light. Native to Southeast Asia, it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant's fronds are typically arching and have a distinctive, lace-like appearance.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and ability to thrive in indoor conditions with proper care.
