Anemone flaccida var. hofengensis

Anemone flaccida var. hofengensis, commonly known as the Hofeng Mountain Anemone, is a perennial herb native to China.

It features delicate, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has basal leaves that are palmately lobed and covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in mountainous regions.

Its botanical characteristics make it a notable species within the Ranunculaceae family.
