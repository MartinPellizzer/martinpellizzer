Anthyllis polyphylla var. schiwereckii

Anthyllis polyphylla var. schiwereckii, commonly known as the Schiwereck's anthyllis, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit and produces small, yellow to orange flowers clustered in spherical heads. The plant has trifoliate leaves composed of three oval-shaped leaflets, and its root system is well adapted to nutrient-poor soils. It is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in rocky or sandy habitats.

This species is often found in open, sunny areas and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
