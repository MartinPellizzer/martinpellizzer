Asterosedum stellatum

Asterosedum stellatum, commonly known as the star sedum, is a succulent plant native to the United States.

It features small, star-shaped leaves that are typically blue-green in color, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of yellow flowers in the summer, adding visual interest to gardens. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice for landscaping in arid climates.
