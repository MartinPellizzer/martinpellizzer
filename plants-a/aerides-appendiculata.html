Aerides appendiculata

Aerides appendiculata, commonly known as the appendiculate orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a short, erect stem and several fleshy, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers with a distinctive lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Aerides appendiculata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
