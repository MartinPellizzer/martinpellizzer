Anthocleista macrophylla

Anthocleista macrophylla, commonly known as the large-leaved anthocleista, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can reach up to 30 cm in diameter. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release seeds covered in a sticky, resinous substance.

The plant is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional medicine to treat various ailments.
