Anthurium sulcatum

Anthurium sulcatum, commonly known as the zigzag anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive zigzag pattern along the veins. The plant produces bright red spadices surrounded by a colorful spathe, which is typical of the Araceae family. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium sulcatum is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and unique floral structures.
