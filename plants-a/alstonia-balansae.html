Alstonia balansae

Alstonia balansae, commonly known as the Balansae tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Apocynaceae and is characterized by its smooth, grey bark and opposite, ovate leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers followed by woody, spherical fruits containing numerous seeds. It is often found in lowland rainforests and is valued for its ornamental appearance and potential medicinal properties.

The tree also plays a role in local ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various wildlife species.
