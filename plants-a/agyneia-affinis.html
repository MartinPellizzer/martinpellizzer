Agyneia affinis

Agyneia affinis, commonly known as the narrow-leafed agyneia, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Acanthaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, tubular flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink or purple. Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
