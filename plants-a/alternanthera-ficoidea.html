Alternanthera ficoidea

Alternanthera ficoidea, commonly known as red veined sorrel, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking red veins on heart-shaped leaves, which contrast beautifully with its bright green foliage. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full sunlight.

Due to its vibrant appearance, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
