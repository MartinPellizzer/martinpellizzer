Aloe simii

Aloe simii, commonly known as the Natal aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a waxy texture. The plant produces vibrant orange to red flowers during the summer months. Aloe simii is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Its leaves contain a gel-like substance that is often used for its soothing properties.
