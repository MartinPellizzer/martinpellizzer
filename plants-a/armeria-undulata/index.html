Armeria Undulata

Armeria undulata is a delicate yet resilient flowering plant known for its charming, bell-shaped blooms. Native to the coastal regions of southern California and Baja California, it thrives in sandy and rocky soils. This evergreen shrub produces clusters of white or pink flowers that bloom in spring and early summer. Its unique undulating leaves and compact growth make it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively obscure compared to other members of the Plumbagaceae family.
