Atriplex hortensis var. purpurea

Atriplex hortensis var. purpurea, commonly known as red oriole spinach, is a hardy, frost-tolerant plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features succulent, lobed leaves that range from green to reddish-purple, depending on the variety and environmental conditions. The plant produces small, green flowers that are followed by triangular, edible seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant foliage, which adds visual interest throughout the growing season.
