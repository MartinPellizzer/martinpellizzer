It is native to Southeast Asia, including regions such as Indonesia, Malaysia, and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are usually ovate to elliptical in shape. Its flowers are small and greenish-white, often clustered in panicle-like inflorescences.

The fruit is a small, woody capsule that splits open to release its seeds.
