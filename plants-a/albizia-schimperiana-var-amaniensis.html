Albizia schimperiana var. amaniensis

Albizia schimperiana var. amaniensis, commonly known as the East African Albizia, is a leguminous shrub or small tree native to eastern Africa.

It features compound leaves with pairs of ovate to lanceolate leaflets, and its flowers are typically pale yellow to white, arranged in dense, spherical inflorescences. The plant is adapted to tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in open woodlands or along riverbanks.

It has a shallow root system and is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential use in soil stabilization.
