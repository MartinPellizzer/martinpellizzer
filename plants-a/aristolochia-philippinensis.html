Aristolochia philippinensis

Aristolochia philippinensis, commonly known as the Philippine pipevine, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its distinctive, pipe-shaped flowers that are typically greenish-yellow with a prominent, hooded structure. The plant is a climbing vine that produces heart-shaped leaves with a rough texture. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in moist, shaded forest environments.

The species is notable for its chemical compounds, some of which have been studied for potential medicinal properties.
