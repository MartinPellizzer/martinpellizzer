Agrostis taliensis

Agrostis taliensis, commonly known as the Taliensian bentgrass, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its fine, tufted growth habit and slender, grass-like leaves. This plant typically thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetland or marshy environments. Agrostis taliensis produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny flowers.

It plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various small organisms in its native ecosystems.
