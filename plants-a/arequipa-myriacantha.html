Arequipa myriacantha

Arequipa myriacantha, commonly known as the Arequipa thornapple, is a flowering plant native to the highlands of Peru.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes nightshades, and is characterized by its spiny stems and showy, bell-shaped flowers.
