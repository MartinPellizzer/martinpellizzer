Areca tenella

Areca tenella, commonly known as the slender areca palm, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and feather-like leaves that grow in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that develop into three-winged fruits. Areca tenella thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.
