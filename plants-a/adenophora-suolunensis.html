Adenophora suolunensis

Adenophora suolunensis, commonly known as the Chinese bellflower, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to purple. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered with fine hairs, while its stems are often hairy and branching.

Adenophora suolunensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and has been used in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.
