Anisotome imbricata var. prostrata

Anisotome imbricata var. prostrata, commonly known as the prostrate leaf anisotome, is a low-growing perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive texture. The plant produces small, pale blue to purple flowers that bloom in spring, adding a subtle color to its foliage. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a ground cover.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for xeriscaping and coastal gardens.
