Aster rutaeodorus

Aster rutaeodorus, commonly known as the rough aster, is a flowering plant native to the northern United States and southern Canada.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands. Its leaves are rough and hairy, while its flowers are blue to purple in color.

Aster rutaeodorus plays an important role in supporting pollinators like bees and butterflies.
