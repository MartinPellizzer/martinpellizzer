Aster retinervius

Aster retinervius, commonly known as the silver aster, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its striking silver-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, daisy-like flowers that range in color from white to pale purple. Aster retinervius thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight.

It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
