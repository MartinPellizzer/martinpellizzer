Aizoon teretifolium

Aizoon teretifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leafed aizoon, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb with slender, upright stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of tiny, pale yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or coastal habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and hardiness, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
