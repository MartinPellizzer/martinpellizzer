Acer huianum

Acer huianum, commonly known as the Huian maple, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It is characterized by its vibrant autumn foliage, which displays shades of red, orange, and yellow. The tree has palmately lobed leaves that are typically 5 to 9 inches long. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Acer huianum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and parks.
