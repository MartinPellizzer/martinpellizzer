Aloysia decorticans

Aloysia decorticans, commonly known as silverleaf sage, is a shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and fragrant, tubular flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is part of the verbena family (Verbenaceae) and is known for its aromatic properties. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for Mediterranean climates.

Its ornamental value and use in herbal medicine contribute to its significance in horticulture and traditional practices.
