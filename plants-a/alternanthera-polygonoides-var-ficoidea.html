Alternanthera polygonoides var. ficoidea

Alternanthera polygonoides var. ficoidea, commonly known as red arrowhead, is a striking aquatic plant native to South America.

It features vibrant red to purple leaves that are often variegated with white or cream markings. The plant thrives in shallow, slow-moving freshwater environments and prefers moderate to high light conditions. Its rhizomes spread along the substrate, forming dense mats that help stabilize sediment.

It is popular in aquariums for its aesthetic appeal and ability to provide shelter for aquatic life.
