Asystasia zambiana

Asystasia zambiana, commonly known as Zambian violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its showy, violet-colored flowers. The plant features heart-shaped, velvety leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is popular as a garden ornamental.

Its rapid growth and easy care make it a favored choice for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.
