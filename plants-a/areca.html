Areca

Areca, commonly known as the areca palm, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and pinnate leaves that can reach several meters in length. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by edible fruits known as areca nuts. These fruits are widely used in traditional medicine and as a source of fiber.

The areca palm is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
