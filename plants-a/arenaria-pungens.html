Arenaria pungens

Arenaria pungens, commonly known as the sharp sandwort, is a flowering plant native to arctic and alpine regions.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically forms dense mats on rocky or sandy soils. The plant produces small, white or pinkish flowers during the summer months. Its leaves are succulent and adapted to withstand cold and dry conditions.

Arenaria pungens is often found in tundra and mountainous environments where it plays a role in stabilizing soil and supporting local ecosystems.
