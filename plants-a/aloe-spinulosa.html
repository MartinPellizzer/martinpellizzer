Aloe spinulosa

Aloe spinulosa, commonly known as the spiny aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a thick, fleshy stem that stores water, allowing it to survive in arid conditions. The plant produces clusters of yellow flowers during the summer months, which are followed by small, red berries. Its leaves are narrow and covered in small, spiny tubercles, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Aloe spinulosa is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique look and low maintenance requirements.
