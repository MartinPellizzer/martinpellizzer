Antholyza splendens

Antholyza splendens, commonly known as the golden torch plant, is a striking member of the Asteraceae family.

It is native to South America, particularly Brazil, and is characterized by its bright yellow, torch-like inflorescence. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its vibrant color and unique appearance make it a standout in both garden beds and container plantings.
