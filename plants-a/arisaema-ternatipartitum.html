Arisaema ternatipartitum

Arisaema ternatipartitum, commonly known as the Three-parted Arisaema, is a member of the Araceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant features a stout rhizome and produces striking, arrow-shaped leaves with a distinctive three-lobed structure. Its inflorescence consists of a spadix surrounded by a modified leaf called a spathe.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its unique foliage and exotic appearance.
