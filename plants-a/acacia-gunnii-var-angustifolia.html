Acacia gunnii var. angustifolia

Acacia gunnii var. angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf golden wattle, is a shrub native to Australia.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is drought-tolerant, making it suitable for arid and semi-arid regions.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping and reforestation projects.
