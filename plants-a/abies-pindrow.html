Abies pindrow

Abies pindrow, commonly known as the Kashmiri fir, is a coniferous evergreen tree native to the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its conical shape and dense, dark green foliage. The tree produces cone-like structures that contain seeds, typical of the Pinaceae family. Its bark is thick and scaly, providing protection against harsh environmental conditions.

Abies pindrow is often found in high-altitude regions, where it plays a vital role in local ecosystems.
