Anthurium × wanbeckianum

Anthurium × wanbeckianum, commonly known as the velvet leaf anthurium, is a hybrid plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically deep green with a velvety texture. The plant produces striking spadices, which are often bright red or orange, surrounded by a showy spathe. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium × wanbeckianum is popular as a decorative plant due to its ornamental foliage and unique floral structures.
