Astracantha thracica subsp. cyllenea

Astracantha thracica subsp.

cyllenea, commonly known as the Balkan thornvetch, is a flowering plant native to southeastern Europe. It is a perennial herb characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, yellow flowers clustered in dense heads.

The plant produces small, oblong pods that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in open woodlands and scrublands.

Its botanical features make it an important component of the local flora in its native habitat.
