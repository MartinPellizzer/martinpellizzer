Allium rhizomatum

Allium rhizomatum, commonly known as the purple onion, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central Asia.

It features a bulbous stem with a fibrous root system and produces clusters of purple to pinkish flowers. The plant has strap-like leaves that are typically bluish-green in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Its ornamental value is complemented by its edible bulbs, which are used in various culinary applications.
