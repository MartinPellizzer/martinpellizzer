Aneilema pauciflorum

Aneilema pauciflorum, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of violet, pink, and white, often with a contrasting throat. Its leaves are thick, fleshy, and typically have a velvety texture.
