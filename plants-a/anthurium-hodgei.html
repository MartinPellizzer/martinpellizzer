Anthurium hodgei

Anthurium hodgei, commonly known as the Hodge's anthurium, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped spathes that come in a variety of colors, including red, orange, and yellow, often with intricate patterns. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers inside the spathe, which are typically hidden from view. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium hodgei is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
