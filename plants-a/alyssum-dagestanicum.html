Alyssum dagestanicum

Alyssum dagestanicum, commonly known as the Dagestan alissum, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Caucasus region.

It features small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pale pink, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has hairy, narrow leaves that are arranged in a basal rosette, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Alyssum dagestanicum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract beneficial insects.
