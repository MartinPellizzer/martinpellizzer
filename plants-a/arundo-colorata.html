Arundo colorata

Arundo colorata, commonly known as the colorful reed, is a striking ornamental grass native to China.

It features tall, slender stems that can reach up to 6 feet in height, often displaying vibrant hues of pink, red, and purple along the edges of the leaves. The plant thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for water gardens and pond edges. Its foliage turns a striking coppery color in the fall, adding seasonal interest to landscapes.

Arundo colorata is also valued for its ability to filter water and provide habitat for aquatic life.
