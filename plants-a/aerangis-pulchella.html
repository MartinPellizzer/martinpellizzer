Aerangis pulchella

Aerangis pulchella, commonly known as the Elegant Orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant has long, slender stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in humid environments with bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil.

Aerangis pulchella is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
