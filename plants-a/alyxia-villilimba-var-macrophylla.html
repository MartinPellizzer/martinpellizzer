Alyxia villilimba var. macrophylla

Alyxia villilimba var. macrophylla, commonly known as the large-leaved alyxia, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America.

It is characterized by its large, elliptical leaves that can reach up to 15 cm in length. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers, which bloom in the summer months. It typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters.

This species is often found in moist, well-drained soils in tropical and subtropical regions.
