Aniseia hastata

Aniseia hastata, commonly known as the pink anise tree, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, branching stems and small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, round fruits that are often used in traditional medicine. Its leaves are aromatic and have a mild licorice-like scent.

Aniseia hastata is often cultivated for its ornamental value and medicinal properties.
