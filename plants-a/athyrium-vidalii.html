Athyrium vidalii

Athyrium vidalii, commonly known as the Vidal's fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Dryopteridaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

The leaves are bi- or tri-pinnate, with a graceful, airy appearance that makes it a popular ornamental plant.
