Ammannia rautanenii

Ammannia rautanenii, commonly known as the red sand mat, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of Africa.

It belongs to the family Amaranthaceae and is characterized by its prostrate growth habit and fleshy, succulent leaves. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences with pink to red flowers, which bloom during the rainy season. It is well adapted to hot, dry environments and can thrive in poor soils.

Ammannia rautanenii is often used in xeriscaping due to its low water requirements and ornamental value.
