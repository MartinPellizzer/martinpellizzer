Aloe powysiorum

Aloe powysiorum, commonly known as the Powys aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, with a waxy coating to reduce water loss. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers in clusters during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Aloe powysiorum is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and hardy nature.
