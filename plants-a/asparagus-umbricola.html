Asparagus umbricola

Asparagus umbricola, commonly known as the shadow asparagus, is a rare and elusive plant species native to the shaded understories of temperate forests.

It thrives in low-light conditions, making it well-adapted to environments with limited sunlight. The plant features delicate, fern-like leaves and produces small, inconspicuous yellow flowers. Its growth is slow, and it often requires specific soil conditions rich in organic matter.

Due to its rarity and specialized habitat, Asparagus umbricola is of interest to botanists and conservationists alike.
