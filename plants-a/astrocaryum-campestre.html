Astrocaryum campestre

Astrocaryum campestre, commonly known as the Brazilian buriti, is a tall, palm-like plant native to the Amazon rainforest.

It produces large, yellowish fruits that are encased in a hard, fibrous outer layer. The plant has a robust trunk with a smooth, grayish-brown bark and large, feather-like leaves. Its botanical features include a sympodial growth habit and the ability to thrive in wet, tropical environments.

The fruit is often used in traditional medicine and as a source of edible seeds.
