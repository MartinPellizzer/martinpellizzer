Agave rutteniae

Agave rutteniae, commonly known as the Red Agave, is a succulent plant native to the arid regions of Mexico.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color with a reddish tinge. The plant produces a tall, branched inflorescence that bears small, white flowers. It is adapted to survive in hot, dry climates with minimal water.

Its unique appearance and resilience make it a popular choice for xeriscaping and ornamental gardening.
