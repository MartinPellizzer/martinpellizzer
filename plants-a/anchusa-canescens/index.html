Anchusa Canescens

Anchusa canescens is a hardy perennial plant known for its vibrant blue flowers and silver-green foliage. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders. Native to southern Europe, this plant has been cultivated for centuries for its ornamental value and medicinal properties. Its ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies adds to its ecological importance. With its low maintenance requirements, Anchusa canescens is a versatile addition to many garden landscapes.
