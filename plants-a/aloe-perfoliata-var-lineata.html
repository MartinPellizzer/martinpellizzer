Aloe perfoliata var. lineata

Aloe perfoliata var. lineata, commonly known as the lined aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, linear leaves that are often tinged with red or purple at the edges. The leaves are covered in a powdery coating and have a distinctive striped pattern along their length. It produces yellowish-green flowers on tall, branched stalks during the warmer months.

This plant is well-suited for arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
