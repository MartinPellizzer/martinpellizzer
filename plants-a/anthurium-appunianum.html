Anthurium appunianum

Anthurium appunianum, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a striking tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped spathe that ranges from red to orange in color. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers within a spadix, which is surrounded by the showy spathe. Its foliage consists of large, glossy, dark green leaves that are often deeply veined.

Anthurium appunianum thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.
