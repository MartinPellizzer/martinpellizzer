Aloe angiensis var. kitaliensis

Aloe angiensis var. kitaliensis, commonly known as the Kitali Aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically gray-green in color, with a waxy coating to reduce water loss. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing yellowish-orange flowers, which are attractive to pollinators. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

The variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in dry climates.
