Anthemis alpestris f. hemirradiata

Anthemis alpestris f. hemirradiata, commonly known as the alpine anthemis, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its compact, cushion-like growth habit and is typically found in alpine and subalpine regions. The plant produces small, yellow to orange composite flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are usually gray-green, hairy, and adapted to withstand cold and windy conditions.

This variety is notable for its ornamental value and ecological importance in high-altitude ecosystems.
