Achyrocline saturejoides var. vargasiana

Achyrocline saturejoides var. vargasiana, commonly known as the Vargasian chia, is a flowering plant native to the Andean regions of South America.

The plant is known for its aromatic leaves and small, tubular flowers that bloom in clusters. It typically grows in high-altitude environments, preferring well-drained soils and full sun exposure.
