Antidesma tulasneanum

Antidesma tulasneanum, commonly known as the African starfruit or African gooseberry, is a small evergreen shrub or tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its opposite, simple, ovate to elliptic leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by bright red, fleshy berries that are edible. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forest understories.
