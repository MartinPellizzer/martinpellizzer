Anthurium temponiae

Anthurium temponiae, commonly known as the Temponiae arrowhead, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its unique inflorescence consisting of a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy, dark green leaves that are often veined with white or silver patterns. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium temponiae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in greenhouse settings.
