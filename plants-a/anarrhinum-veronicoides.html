Anarrhinum veronicoides

Anarrhinum veronicoides, commonly known as the silverleaf veronicoid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, silvery hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or violet in color, arranged in dense clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Anarrhinum veronicoides is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive foliage and flowering display.
