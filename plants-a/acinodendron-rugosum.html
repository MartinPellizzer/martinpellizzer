Acinodendron rugosum

Acinodendron rugosum, commonly known as the rough acinodendron, is a member of the Rubiaceae family.

It is a shrub characterized by its rough, bark-like texture and compound leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by capsule fruits containing minute seeds. Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and taxonomic studies.
