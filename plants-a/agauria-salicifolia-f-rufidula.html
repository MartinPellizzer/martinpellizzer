Agauria salicifolia f. rufidula

It is characterized by its distinctive reddish-brown foliage, which sets it apart from the typical green leaves of the species. The plant thrives in high-altitude environments, often found in cloud forests and rocky terrains. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a smooth texture, while its small, inconspicuous flowers appear in clusters.
