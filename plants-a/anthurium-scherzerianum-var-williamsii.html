Anthurium scherzerianum var. williamsii

Anthurium scherzerianum var. williamsii, commonly known as the Williams' Anthurium, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-shaped inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, often in shades of red or orange. It thrives in humid, warm environments with bright, indirect light.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is commonly cultivated as a houseplant.
