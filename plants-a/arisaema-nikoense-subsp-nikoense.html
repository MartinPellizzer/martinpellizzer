Arisaema nikoense subsp. nikoense

Arisaema nikoense subsp.

nikoense, commonly known as the Niko's arisaema, is a member of the Araceae family. It is native to the mountainous regions of southern China, particularly in Yunnan Province.

The plant is characterized by its distinctive, hooded spathe that encloses a spadix, typical of the genus Arisaema. It produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered on the spadix.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine in some regions.
