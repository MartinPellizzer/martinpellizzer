Aloe claviflora

Aloe claviflora, commonly known as the sword aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features narrow, sword-shaped leaves that are typically blue-green in color, covered in a white powdery coating called farina. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow flowers that bloom in summer. Aloe claviflora is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice for rock gardens and arid landscapes.
