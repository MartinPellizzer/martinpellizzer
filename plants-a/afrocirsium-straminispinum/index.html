Afrocirsium Straminispinum

Afrocirsium straminispinum is a rare and striking plant native to the grasslands of southern Africa. It is known for its unique, spiny leaves and showy yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. This species belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers. Its distinctive appearance makes it a subject of interest for botanists and plant enthusiasts. Due to habitat loss, it is also considered threatened in its natural range.
