Ardisia proteifolia

Ardisia proteifolia, commonly known as the glossy evergreen ardisia, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It features dense, dark green foliage with a glossy texture, and its small, fragrant flowers are typically white or pale pink. The plant produces bright red berries that are an important food source for various bird species. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often used in ornamental gardening.

Ardisia proteifolia has a compact growth habit and is valued for its year-round foliage and seasonal floral display.
