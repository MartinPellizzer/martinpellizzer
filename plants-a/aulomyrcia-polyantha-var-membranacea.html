Aulomyrcia polyantha var. membranacea

Aulomyrcia polyantha var. membranacea, commonly known as the membranaceous aulomyrcia, is a species of flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is a small evergreen shrub characterized by its lance-shaped leaves and clusters of fragrant white flowers. The plant produces small, round berries that are typically red when mature. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical forests.

Due to its ornamental value and unique foliage, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
