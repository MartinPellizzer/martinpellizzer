Aloe albovestita

Aloe albovestita, commonly known as the white-flowered aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are pale green with a powdery white coating. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing yellow flowers. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Aloe albovestita is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional medicinal uses.
