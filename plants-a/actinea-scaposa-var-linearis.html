Actinea scaposa var. linearis

Actinea scaposa var. linearis, commonly known as the linear leathery fern, is a variant of the scaposa fern characterized by its slender, linear leaves.

It is a perennial plant native to certain regions of Asia, particularly China and Japan. The plant features a creeping rhizome with upright, lanceolate fronds that are leathery in texture. Its foliage is typically dark green and glossy, with a distinctive, almost metallic sheen.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in shaded gardens and rockeries.
