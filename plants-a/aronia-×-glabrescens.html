Aronia × glabrescens

Aronia × glabrescens, commonly known as the red chokeberry, is a hybrid shrub native to North America.

It is characterized by its dark red to black berries, which are rich in antioxidants and anthocyanins. The plant typically grows in moist, acidic soils and thrives in partial to full shade. It has small, ovate leaves with serrated edges and produces clusters of white flowers in early spring.

Aronia × glabrescens is valued for its ornamental appeal and edible fruits used in jams, juices, and health supplements.
