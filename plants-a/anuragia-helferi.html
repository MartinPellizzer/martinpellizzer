Anuragia helferi

Anuragia helferi, commonly known as the Helfer's orchid, is a species of flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Thailand and Malaysia. The plant features delicate, fragrant flowers with a distinctive coloration, often displaying shades of yellow and white. Its growth habit is epiphytic, meaning it grows on the surface of other plants without being parasitic.

Anuragia helferi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and greenhouses.
