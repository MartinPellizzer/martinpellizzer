Alchemilla acutiloba f. gracilior

Alchemilla acutiloba f. gracilior, commonly known as the slender lady's mantle, is a low-growing perennial plant native to Europe.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves that form a dense mat. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in early summer, which are clustered in narrow racemes. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas or along stream banks.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking a compact, shade-tolerant ground cover.
