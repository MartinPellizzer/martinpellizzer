Ananas fritzmuelleri

Ananas fritzmuelleri, commonly known as the Fritzmueller pineapple, is a rare and unusual member of the Bromeliaceae family.

It is native to the Andes mountains of South America, specifically Ecuador and Peru. The plant produces a striking, spiky inflorescence that resembles a pinecone, with vibrant red bracts. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often covered in a waxy coating.

This species is prized for its ornamental value and is cultivated in specialized botanical gardens due to its unique appearance and limited natural distribution.
