Anthurium subellipticum

Anthurium subellipticum, commonly known as the narrow-elliptic anthurium, is a tropical flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are often a deep green with a waxy texture. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-like inflorescences surrounded by colorful spatula-shaped bracts, typically in shades of red, orange, or yellow. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium subellipticum is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
