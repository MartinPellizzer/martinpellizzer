Aeschynanthus flammeus

Aeschynanthus flammeus, commonly known as the flame vine, is a flowering plant native to western Africa.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and vibrant red-orange tubular flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, oval-shaped leaves that are opposite on the stems. It thrives in warm, humid environments and prefers well-draining soil.

Aeschynanthus flammeus is often cultivated as a decorative plant for its striking floral display.
