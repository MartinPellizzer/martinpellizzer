Anthyllis plumosa

Anthyllis plumosa, commonly known as the silky anthyllis, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial shrub that typically reaches heights of 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features slender, wiry stems covered in soft, silky hairs, which give it its common name. Its small, pea-like flowers are usually pale pink to white and bloom in early spring.

Anthyllis plumosa thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
