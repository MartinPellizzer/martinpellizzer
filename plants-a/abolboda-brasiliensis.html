Abolboda brasiliensis

Abolboda brasiliensis, commonly known as the Brazilian starfruit tree, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree that can grow up to 10 meters in height. The plant produces small, star-shaped fruits that are typically yellow or orange in color. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a glossy surface.

The tree is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its unique fruit.
