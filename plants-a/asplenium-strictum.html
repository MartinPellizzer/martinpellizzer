Asplenium strictum

Asplenium strictum, commonly known as the narrow fern, is a species of fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves that are typically arranged in a compact, upright form. The plant belongs to the family Aspleniaceae and is often found in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rock crevices. Its fronds are usually dark green and may have a slightly serrated or toothed margin.

Asplenium strictum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant.
