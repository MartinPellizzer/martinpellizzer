Aconitum anthora var. grandiflorum

Aconitum anthora var. grandiflorum, commonly known as the Alpine Monkshood, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the alpine regions of Europe.

It features striking, hood-shaped blue or purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has dark green, heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its toxicity, it should be handled with care.
