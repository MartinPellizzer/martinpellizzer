Atraphaxis Teretifolia

Atraphaxis teretifolia is a rare and resilient plant native to the arid regions of the Arabian Peninsula. It thrives in harsh desert conditions, showcasing remarkable adaptations to survive with minimal water. Its slender, cylindrical stems and small, spiky leaves help reduce water loss and maximize sunlight absorption. This plant plays a vital role in stabilizing desert soils and supporting local wildlife. Studying Atraphaxis teretifolia offers valuable insights into plant survival strategies in extreme environments.
