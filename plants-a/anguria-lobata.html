Anguria lobata

Anguria lobata, commonly known as the lobed cucumber, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Cucurbitaceae family, which includes other cucurbits like pumpkins and melons. The plant produces elongated, lobed fruits that are typically green and have a mild, cucumber-like flavor. Its leaves are broad and lobed, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Anguria lobata is often cultivated for both ornamental and edible purposes.
