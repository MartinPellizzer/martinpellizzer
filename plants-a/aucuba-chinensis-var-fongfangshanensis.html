Aucuba chinensis var. fongfangshanensis

Aucuba chinensis var. fongfangshanensis, commonly known as the Fongfangshan aucuba, is a rare evergreen shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are often marked with striking yellow or white veins. The plant produces small, inconspicuous yellow flowers followed by red berries that are edible for birds but not for humans. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Its ornamental value makes it a sought-after plant in horticulture, though its cultivation is limited due to its specific habitat requirements.
