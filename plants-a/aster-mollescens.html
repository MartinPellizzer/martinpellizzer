Aster mollescens

Aster mollescens, commonly known as the soft aster, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its soft, hairy stems and clusters of small, pale blue to white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, woodland habitats and blooms in the early summer. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Aster mollescens is often used in native plant gardens for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
