Anthemis atlantica

Anthemis atlantica, commonly known as the Atlantic everlasting, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact, herbaceous growth habit and produces small, yellow-orange composite flowers. The plant features linear, gray-green leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rocky or coastal environments.

Anthemis atlantica is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
