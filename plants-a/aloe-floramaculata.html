Aloe floramaculata

Aloe floramaculata, commonly known as the spotted aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, upright leaves that are typically green with distinctive white or yellow spots. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with small, tubular flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Aloe floramaculata is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for.
