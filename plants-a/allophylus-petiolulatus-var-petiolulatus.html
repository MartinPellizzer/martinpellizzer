Allophylus petiolulatus var. petiolulatus

Allophylus petiolulatus var. petiolulatus, commonly known as the slender-leafed alderleaf, is a species of flowering plant in the family Euphorbiaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its compound leaves with elongated petioles. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers clustered in cyathia, typical of the Euphorbiaceae family. Its fruit is a capsule that contains several seeds.
