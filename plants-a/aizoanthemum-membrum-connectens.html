Aizoanthemum membrum-connectens

Aizoanthemum membrum-connectens, commonly known as the "Beaded Ice Plant," is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family, commonly referred to as the "ice plant" family, characterized by its succulent leaves and vibrant flowers. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments, showcasing its resilience to drought.

Its unique appearance and ability to attract pollinators make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
