Aralia junghuhniana

Aralia junghuhniana, commonly known as the giant aralia, is a striking plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, palmately compound leaves that can reach up to 1 meter in length. The plant produces clusters of small, greenish-white flowers that are followed by dark red berries. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
