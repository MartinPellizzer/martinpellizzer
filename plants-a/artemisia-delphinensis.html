Artemisia delphinensis

Artemisia delphinensis, commonly known as blue tansy, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its aromatic, silver-gray foliage and small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family and is known for its medicinal properties. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often growing in rocky or dry habitats.

Its essential oil is widely used in aromatherapy and natural remedies.
