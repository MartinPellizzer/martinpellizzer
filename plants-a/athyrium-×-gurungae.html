Athyrium × gurungae

Athyrium × gurungae, commonly known as the Himalayan shield fern, is a hybrid fern species native to the mountainous regions of Nepal and India.

It belongs to the Dryopteridaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, shield-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest floors and rocky crevices. Its fronds are bi- or tri-pinnate, with a delicate, lace-like appearance.

Athyrium × gurungae is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and adaptability to various garden conditions.
