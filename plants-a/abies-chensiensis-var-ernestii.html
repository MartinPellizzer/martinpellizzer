Abies chensiensis var. ernestii

Abies chensiensis var. ernestii, commonly known as the Ernestii fir, is a rare and ornamental conifer native to the mountainous regions of China.

It is characterized by its slender, pyramidal shape and dark green, needle-like leaves that are short and stiff. The cones are typically reddish-brown and mature to a dark brown, adding visual interest to the tree. This variety is valued for its unique form and is often cultivated in botanical gardens for its aesthetic appeal.

It prefers well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for specific horticultural settings.
