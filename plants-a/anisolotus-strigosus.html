Anisolotus strigosus

Anisolotus strigosus, commonly known as the hairy anisolotus, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features hairy stems and leaves, which help reduce water loss in its arid environment. It produces small, daisy-like flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color.

Anisolotus strigosus is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to thrive in harsh, dry conditions.
