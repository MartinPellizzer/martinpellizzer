Aspidium abyssinicum

Aspidium abyssinicum, commonly known as the Abyssinian water fern, is a species of fern native to Africa.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine in various regions.

The spores are dispersed through the wind, contributing to its propagation in suitable habitats.
