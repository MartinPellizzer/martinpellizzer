Aspidium filix-mas var. marginatum

Aspidium filix-mas var. marginatum, commonly known as the marginal shield fern, is a variant of the common shield fern.

It is a terrestrial fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae, characterized by its feathery, lanceolate fronds. The leaves are typically green with a distinctive marginal variegation, giving it a striking appearance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

This plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is relatively easy to cultivate in suitable conditions.
