Anemone hortensis proles grandiflora

Anemone hortensis proles grandiflora, commonly known as the garden anemone, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe.

It features showy, single-layered flowers in shades of white, pink, and red, which bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has basal leaves that are palmately lobed and often covered in a fine white down. It thrives in cool, moist environments and prefers partial to full shade.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking ornamental foliage and blooms.
