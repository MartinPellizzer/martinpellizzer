Anthurium acaule var. scolopendrium

Anthurium acaule var. scolopendrium, commonly known as the red-tipped anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with red or purple veins. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-shaped inflorescences that are often surrounded by a colorful spathe. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

This variety is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and ease of care.
