Acaena cylindristachya subvar. pusilla

Acaena cylindristachya subvar. pusilla, commonly known as the dwarf strawberry burr, is a low-growing perennial plant native to New Zealand.

It features dense, mat-forming growth with small, dark green leaves that are covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in spring, followed by bright red, berry-like fruits that are edible but have a gritty texture. Its unique morphology and habitat preferences make it an important component of its native ecosystems.

It is often found in moist, shaded areas such as forest floors and rocky habitats.
