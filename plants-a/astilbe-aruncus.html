Astilbe aruncus

Astilbe aruncus, commonly known as the red twig astilbe, is a herbaceous perennial native to the eastern United States.

It features feathery plumes of flowers that range from white to pink and red, depending on the variety. The plant has lance-shaped, dark green leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. It thrives in partial to full shade and moist, well-drained soil.

Astilbe aruncus is valued for its ornamental foliage and long-lasting blooms that attract pollinators.
