Arthrostemma ciliatum

Arthrostemma ciliatum, commonly known as the threadleaf arrowhead, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Acanthaceae and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves with finely toothed margins. The plant produces small, white or pale blue flowers that bloom in summer, typically in clusters at the ends of its stems. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for water gardens and bog landscapes.
