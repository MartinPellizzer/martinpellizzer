Astrophytum myriostigma var. coahuilense

Astrophytum myriostigma var. coahuilense, commonly known as the Coahuila star cactus, is a compact, globular cactus native to the Chihuahuan Desert.

It features a smooth, pale green to blue-gray stem with radial spines that are typically white to yellowish. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is adapted to arid environments and requires well-draining soil and minimal water.

This variety is prized for its unique appearance and drought tolerance in xeriscaping.
