Adesmia lotoides f. normalis

Adesmia lotoides f. normalis, commonly known as the common adesmia, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, clustered flowers that typically range in color from white to pale purple. The plant has trifoliate leaves composed of three oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid regions.

Its growth habit is generally shrub-like, with multiple stems arising from the base.
