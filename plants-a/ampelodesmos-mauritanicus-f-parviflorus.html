Ampelodesmos mauritanicus f. parviflorus

Ampelodesmos mauritanicus f. parviflorus, commonly known as the small-flowered ampelodesmos, is a flowering plant native to North Africa.

It is a shrub characterized by its compact growth habit and dense foliage. The plant produces small, white to pale pink flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, showing adaptability to harsh conditions.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for study in xerophytic flora.
