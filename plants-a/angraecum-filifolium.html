Angraecum filifolium

Angraecum filifolium, commonly known as the thread-leaf orchid, is a species native to Madagascar.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant features slender, thread-like leaves that give it its common name. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, with a distinctive, elongated shape.

Angraecum filifolium is known for its ability to thrive in poor soil conditions and requires careful cultivation due to its specific environmental needs.
