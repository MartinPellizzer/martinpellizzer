Astrophytum pulcherrimum

Astrophytum pulcherrimum, commonly known as the "Cactus Rose," is a species of cactus native to the Sonoran Desert.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems that are covered in prominent, radial spines. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It has a shallow root system and is drought-tolerant, making it well adapted to arid environments.

Due to its striking appearance and slow growth, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
