Aerides uniflora

Aerides uniflora, commonly known as the single-flower orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, pseudobulbous structure and produces a single, showy flower per stem. The plant typically blooms in late spring or early summer, with a delicate, fragrant flower that is often white or pale pink. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate humidity and well-draining soil.

Aerides uniflora is valued for its ornamental beauty and is relatively easy to care for in a home setting.
