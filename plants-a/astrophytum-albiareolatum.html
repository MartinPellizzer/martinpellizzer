Astrophytum albiareolatum

Astrophytum albiareolatum, commonly known as the white-rimmed star cactus, is a species of cactus native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its spherical or slightly elongated stem, covered in white, waxy tubercles that give it a star-like appearance. The plant produces yellow flowers during the summer months, which are borne on short, thick pedicels. It is a low-maintenance plant that thrives in well-draining soil and full sun, making it popular among cactus enthusiasts.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a striking addition to arid gardens and indoor collections.
