Astragalus naftolskii

Astragalus naftolskii, commonly known as the Naftolskii milkvetch, is a flowering plant species native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its small, yellow flowers and compound leaves. The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions.

It has been studied for its potential medicinal properties, though further research is needed to confirm its efficacy.
