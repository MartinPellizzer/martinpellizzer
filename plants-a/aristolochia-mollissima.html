Aristolochia mollissima

Aristolochia mollissima, commonly known as the velvetleaf arrowhead, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves that are covered in a dense, velvety pubescence. The plant produces striking, hooded flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in wetlands and along stream banks.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
