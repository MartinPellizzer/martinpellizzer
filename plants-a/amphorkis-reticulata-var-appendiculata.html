Amphorkis reticulata var. appendiculata

Amphorkis reticulata var. appendiculata, commonly known as the appendiculate reticulated orchid, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its intricate, reticulated pattern on its leaves and stems, which gives it its scientific name. The plant produces small, fragrant orchid flowers in shades of white and pale yellow, often arranged in clusters. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is typically found in tropical rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and by plant enthusiasts.
