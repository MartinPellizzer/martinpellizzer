Asplenium munchii

Asplenium munchii, commonly known as the Munch's spleenwort, is a species of fern belonging to the family Aspleniaceae.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that typically grows on the bark of trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant has finely divided, lance-shaped leaves that are usually dark green and have a leathery texture. It produces spores in small, clustered structures called sorus, which are located on the underside of the fronds.

Asplenium munchii is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, where it thrives in moist, well-drained conditions.
