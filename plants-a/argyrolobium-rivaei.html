Argyrolobium rivaei

Argyrolobium rivaei, commonly known as the River's Silver Globule.

It is a flowering plant native to South Africa, particularly found in the Western Cape region. The plant is characterized by its globular, silver-gray foliage and produces small, yellow flowers during the spring season. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to Mediterranean climates with mild, wet winters and hot, dry summers.

This species is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and drought tolerance.
