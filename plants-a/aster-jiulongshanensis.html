Aster jiulongshanensis

Aster jiulongshanensis, commonly known as the Jiulongshan aster, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers. The plant is characterized by its conical flower heads and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. It typically grows in mountainous regions with well-drained soil.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological importance in its native habitat.
