Acianthera fumioi

Acianthera fumioi, commonly known as the smoky orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to regions of South America.

It produces delicate, fragrant flowers that are typically pale green to brown in color, resembling smoky or ash-like hues. The plant has a short, compact stem with a single or few leaves, and its roots are adapted for attachment to trees. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires high air humidity and indirect light for optimal growth.

The species is noted for its unique floral structure and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
