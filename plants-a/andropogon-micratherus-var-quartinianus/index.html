Andropogon Micratherus Var. Quartinianus

Andropogon micratherus var. quartinianus is a lesser-known grass species native to the southeastern united states. it belongs to the poaceae family and is often found in wetland and swampy environments. this variant is distinguished by its slender blades and compact growth habit. it plays an important role in maintaining ecological balance within its habitat. despite its modest appearance, it holds significance for both wildlife and botanical study.
