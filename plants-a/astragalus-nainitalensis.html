Astragalus nainitalensis

Astragalus nainitalensis, commonly known as Nainital milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayas, specifically found in the state of Uttarakhand in India.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant is typically found in alpine meadows and rocky areas at high altitudes. It plays a role in soil stabilization and is of interest for its potential medicinal properties.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
