Alternanthera denticulata var. parvula

Alternanthera denticulata var. parvula, commonly known as the dwarf Alternanthera, is a compact, trailing plant native to South America.

It features small, ovate leaves with serrated edges, often displaying vibrant red or pink hues on the undersides. The plant thrives in moist, well-draining soil and prefers partial to full sunlight. It is popular in water gardens and as a foreground plant in aquatic setups.

Its rapid growth and ornamental foliage make it a favorite among aquatic plant enthusiasts.
