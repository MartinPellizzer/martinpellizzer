Aspilia pluriseta subsp. gondensis

Aspilia pluriseta subsp.

gondensis, commonly known as the Gondwe aspilia, is a flowering plant native to parts of southern Africa. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads.

The plant typically grows as a herbaceous perennial with hairy, lance-shaped leaves. Its inflorescence consists of yellow disc flowers surrounded by pale green to white ray florets.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open grasslands and rocky outcrops.
