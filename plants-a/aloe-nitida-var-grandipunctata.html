Aloe nitida var. grandipunctata

Aloe nitida var. grandipunctata, commonly known as the spiked aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features narrow, upright leaves that are covered in white to yellowish tubercles, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow flowers, which bloom in spring. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its compact growth habit makes it an attractive addition to rock gardens and xeriscapes.
