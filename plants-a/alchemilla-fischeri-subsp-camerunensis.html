Alchemilla fischeri subsp. camerunensis

Alchemilla fischeri subsp.

camerunensis, commonly known as the Cameroun lady's mantle, is a perennial herb native to Cameroon. It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its basal rosette of soft, hairy leaves.

The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in clusters during the spring and summer months. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
