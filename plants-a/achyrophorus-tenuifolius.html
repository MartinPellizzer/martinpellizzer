Achyrophorus tenuifolius

Achyrophorus tenuifolius, commonly known as the narrow-leafed achyrophorus, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in dry, rocky soils. The plant features slender, linear leaves and produces small, pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters. Its botanical characteristics include a fibrous root system and a branching stem.

It is often found in coastal areas and is adapted to withstand saline and arid conditions.
