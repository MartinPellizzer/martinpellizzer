Artemisia variabilis var. americana

Artemisia variabilis var. americana, commonly known as American tarragon, is a perennial herb native to North America.

It features silver-gray foliage with finely divided, aromatic leaves that emit a distinctive herbaceous scent. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Its essential oils, rich in thymol, contribute to its use in culinary and medicinal applications.
