Aster caffrorum

Aster caffrorum, commonly known as the Cape aster, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in grasslands and rocky areas, thriving in full sun. Its showy, daisy-like flowers bloom in shades of white, yellow, and orange, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Aster caffrorum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant display.
