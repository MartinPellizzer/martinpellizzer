Androcalva perlaria

Androcalva perlaria, commonly known as the pearl orchid, is a species of flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are usually broad and fleshy, adapted to store water in its native habitat.

The flowers are small but numerous, often arranged in clusters and displaying a range of colors from white to pale pink.
