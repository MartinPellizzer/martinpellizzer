Artabotrys luxurians

Artabotrys luxurians, commonly known as the velvet tamarind, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant, pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves that are opposite and ovate in shape. Its fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release its seeds.

Artabotrys luxurians is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
