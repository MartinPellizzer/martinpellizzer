Artemisia ussuriensis

Artemisia ussuriensis, commonly known as Siberian wormwood, is a perennial herb native to the cold regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its aromatic, gray-green foliage and slender, branching stems. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in alpine or subarctic environments.

Its essential oils contain compounds with potential medicinal properties.
