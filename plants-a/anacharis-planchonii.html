Anacharis planchonii

Anacharis planchonii, commonly known as the African sword plant, is a species of aquatic plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its long, narrow, sword-shaped leaves that are typically green and slightly wavy in appearance. This plant thrives in freshwater environments and is often found in slow-moving rivers and ponds. It is a popular choice for aquariums due to its hardiness and ability to oxygenate water.

The plant reproduces through both sexual means and vegetative propagation, making it easy to cultivate.
