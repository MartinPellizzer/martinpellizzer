Arctium abolinii

Arctium abolinii, commonly known as the lesser burdock, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Arctium abolinii is often found along roadsides and in meadows, where it contributes to local biodiversity.
