Aloe × delaetii

Aloe × delaetii, commonly known as the hybrid aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a waxy texture. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow or orange flowers. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil.

Aloe × delaetii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to store water efficiently.
