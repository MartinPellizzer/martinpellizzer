Astrocaryum murumuru

Astrocaryum murumuru, commonly known as babassu, is a palm tree native to the Amazon Basin.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and pinnate leaves. The plant produces large, yellowish-orange fruits that contain a high oil content. The oil extracted from its seeds is used in cosmetics and biofuels.

It plays an important ecological role in its native habitat and is also valued for its economic potential.
