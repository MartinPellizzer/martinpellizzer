Angraecum ramosum var. arachnites

Angraecum ramosum var. arachnites, commonly known as the spider orchid, is a rare and striking orchid native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its long, thin, and often hairy stems, which can resemble spider legs, giving it its common name. The plant produces delicate, pendulous flowers with white petals and a distinctive yellow lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
