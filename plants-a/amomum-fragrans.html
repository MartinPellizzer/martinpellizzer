Amomum fragrans

Amomum fragrans, commonly known as the fragrant amomum or Chinese cardamom, is a perennial herb native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Zingiberaceae, which includes ginger and turmeric. The plant is characterized by its aromatic, narrow leaves and clusters of pale yellow flowers. Its rhizomes are the primary part used in traditional medicine and as a spice.
