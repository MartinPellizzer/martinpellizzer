Abelia chinensis var. ionandra

Abelia chinensis var. ionandra, commonly known as the Chinese abelia or red abelia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features narrow, elliptical leaves that are glossy and dark green, with a slightly serrated edge. The plant produces clusters of showy, tubular flowers that range from white to pink, often with a red throat, blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in partial to full sun and well-drained soil, making it a versatile choice for gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its attractive foliage and long-lasting blooms.
