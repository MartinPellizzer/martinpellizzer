Anthurium validifolium

Anthurium validifolium, commonly known as the broadleaf anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces striking spadices, which are the modified leaves that enclose the small, inconspicuous flowers. Anthurium validifolium thrives in humid, warm environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical landscapes.
