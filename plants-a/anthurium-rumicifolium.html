Anthurium rumicifolium

Anthurium rumicifolium, commonly known as the spiny anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves with spiny margins, which give it its common name. The plant produces striking spadices, which are the modified leaves that house the small, inconspicuous flowers. Anthurium rumicifolium thrives in humid, warm environments and requires indirect sunlight for optimal growth.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and indoor plant collections.
