Aechmea aiuruocensis

Aechmea aiuruocensis, commonly known as the aiuruocensis bromeliad, is a species of flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that form a central cup to collect rainwater. The plant produces striking inflorescences with vibrant flowers, often in shades of red, orange, or yellow. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Its unique structure supports a variety of epiphytic and terrestrial growth habits.
