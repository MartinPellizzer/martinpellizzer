Acnistus breviflorus var. spinescens

Acnistus breviflorus var. spinescens, commonly known as the spiny acnistus, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub characterized by its dense, spiny stems and small, clustered leaves. The plant produces yellowish-white flowers that bloom in summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and unique appearance, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
