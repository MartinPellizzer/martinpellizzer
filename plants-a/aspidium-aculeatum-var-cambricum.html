Aspidium aculeatum var. cambricum

Aspidium aculeatum var. cambricum, commonly known as the Welsh shield fern, is a perennial fern native to the British Isles.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, shield-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and rocky habitats. Its sori are arranged in a single row along the margins of the pinnae.

The varietal form cambricum is distinguished by its slightly different frond structure compared to the typical variety.
