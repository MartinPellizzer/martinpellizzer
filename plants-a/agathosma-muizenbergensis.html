Agathosma muizenbergensis

Agathosma muizenbergensis, commonly known as Muizenberg sage, is a succulent shrub native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Rutaceae and is characterized by its aromatic, narrow leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant is well adapted to arid environments, with thick, fleshy stems that store water. It is often found in rocky outcrops and sandy soils, thriving in full sun.

Its essential oils are used in aromatherapy and traditional medicine.
