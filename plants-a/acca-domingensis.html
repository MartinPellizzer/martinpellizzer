Acca domingensis

Acca domingensis, commonly known as the tree tomato or yellow tomato, is a small evergreen shrub or tree native to South America.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its woody stems and alternate leaves. The plant produces small, yellow or red fruits that are edible and have a tangy, acidic flavor. The flowers are typically white or pink and are borne in clusters.

It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and fruit.
