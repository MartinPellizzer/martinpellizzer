Alsophila ulei

Alsophila ulei, commonly known as the Ulei tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds. The plant has a robust root system that helps stabilize soil and prevent erosion. Its fronds can grow up to several meters in length, contributing to its ornamental value in tropical landscapes.

Alsophila ulei is often found in humid, shaded environments such as forests and along riverbanks.
