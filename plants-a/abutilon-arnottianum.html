Abutilon arnottianum

Abutilon arnottianum, commonly known as the silky abutilon, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, featuring soft, hairy leaves that are often lobed or serrated. The plant produces showy, lantern-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, often with a red or purple throat. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to full sun, making it a popular choice for coastal gardens.

The plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in horticulture for its unique foliage and floral display.
