Arachnothryx albida

Arachnothryx albida, commonly known as the white arachnothryx, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its climbing habit and compound leaves. The plant produces small, white flowers that are often clustered in inflorescences. Its seeds are encased in pods, which are typically hairy and contain several seeds.

Arachnothryx albida is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
