Anthurium olfersianum var. kunthianum

Anthurium olfersianum var. kunthianum, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a striking tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped, red bracts that contrast with its deep green, velvety foliage. The plant belongs to the Araceae family, which is known for its unique inflorescence structure called a spadix, surrounded by a modified leaf called a spathe. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect sunlight to flourish.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical landscapes.
