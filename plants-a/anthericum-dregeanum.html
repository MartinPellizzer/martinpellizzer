Anthericum dregeanum

Anthericum dregeanum, commonly known as the Cape umbrella plant, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features a rosette of broad, waxy, blue-green leaves that form a dense, umbrella-like shape. The plant produces tall, slender flowering stems that bear small, white, star-shaped flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a distinctive addition to Mediterranean-style gardens.
