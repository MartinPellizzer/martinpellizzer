Alafia zambesiaca

Alafia zambesiaca, commonly known as Zambesi grass, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, cylindrical stems that can grow up to 2 meters in height. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in dense, spike-like inflorescences. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often with a slightly hairy texture.

Alafia zambesiaca thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found near water bodies.
