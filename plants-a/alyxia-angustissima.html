Alyxia angustissima

Alyxia angustissima, commonly known as the narrowleaf alyxia, is a flowering plant native to the southern United States and Mexico.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in dry, rocky habitats. The plant produces small, fragrant white or yellowish flowers that bloom in summer. Its leaves are narrow and often have a slight pubescence.

Alyxia angustissima is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance.
