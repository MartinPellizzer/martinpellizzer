Achras sapatilla

Achras sapatilla, commonly known as the "Sapatilla tree," is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is a member of the Arecaceae family, which includes palm trees, and is characterized by its large, fan-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that develop into edible, fleshy berries. Its trunk is often swollen at the base, giving it a distinctive appearance.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated for its fruit.
