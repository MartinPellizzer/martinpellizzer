Aloe wilmsii

Aloe wilmsii, commonly known as the Wilms' aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, spiky leaves that store water, typical of arid environment adaptations. The plant produces yellow flowers in summer, which are clustered in racemes. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their soothing properties.

Aloe wilmsii thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for xeriscaping.
