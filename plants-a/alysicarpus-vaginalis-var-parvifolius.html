Alysicarpus vaginalis var. parvifolius

Alysicarpus vaginalis var. parvifolius, commonly known as small-leaf velvet bean, is a tropical legume native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, ovate leaves and clusters of yellow flowers. The plant produces small, hairy pods containing kidney-shaped seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in disturbed soils.

Due to its rapid growth, it is sometimes used for soil stabilization and as a forage crop.
