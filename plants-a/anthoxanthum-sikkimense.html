Anthoxanthum sikkimense

Anthoxanthum sikkimense, commonly known as the Sikkim oxalis, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayan region of India.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, clustered inflorescences. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer, adding a vibrant touch to its native habitat. Its leaves are typically narrow and grass-like, with a slightly hairy texture.

Anthoxanthum sikkimense is often found in moist, well-drained soils in mountainous areas, where it thrives in cooler climates.
