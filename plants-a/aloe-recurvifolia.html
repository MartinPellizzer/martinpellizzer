Aloe recurvifolia

Aloe recurvifolia, commonly known as the recurved aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features tall, branching stems with narrow, fleshy leaves that are often recurved or rolled under. The leaves are typically gray-green in color and have a textured, wrinkled surface. It produces yellow-orange flowers in clusters during the warmer months.

This plant is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
