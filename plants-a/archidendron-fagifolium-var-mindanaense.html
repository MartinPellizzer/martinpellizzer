Archidendron fagifolium var. mindanaense

Archidendron fagifolium var. mindanaense, commonly known as Mindanao legume or Mindanao acacia, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant belongs to the Fabaceae family, known for its nitrogen-fixing capabilities. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in lowland areas with well-drained soils.

Its bark and leaves have been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
