Aloe ambigens

Aloe ambigens, commonly known as the narrow-leaf aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features tall, slender stems with fleshy, narrow leaves that are typically green with a slight bluish tint. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers in clusters during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Aloe ambigens is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional medicinal uses.
