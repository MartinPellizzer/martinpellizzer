Artanthe trichostachya

Artanthe trichostachya, commonly known as the silverleaf artanthe, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Amaranthaceae and is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and striking, spiky inflorescences. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Artanthe trichostachya is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
