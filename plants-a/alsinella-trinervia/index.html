Alsinella Trinervia

Alsinella trinervia is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to specific regions of southern Africa. It is known for its small, clustered blooms that appear in late spring and early summer. This species thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and requires minimal water once established. Its unique appearance and limited distribution make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists. Despite its beauty, Alsinella trinervia faces threats from habitat loss and climate change.
