Anthurium × leonardianum

Anthurium × leonardianum, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a hybrid plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped, crimson spathes that enclose the spadix, a feature typical of the Araceae family. The plant produces clusters of small, greenish-white flowers, which are often overlooked due to the showy bracts. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium × leonardianum is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
