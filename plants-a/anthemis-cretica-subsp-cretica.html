Anthemis cretica subsp. cretica

Anthemis cretica subsp.

cretica, commonly known as Cretan tansy, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region. It features aromatic, yellowish-green leaves with a distinctive, serrated edge.

The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in a flat-topped cluster, typical of the Asteraceae family. It thrives in dry, sunny environments and is often found in rocky soils.

Its essential oils contain compounds like thymol and carvacrol, which contribute to its medicinal and aromatic properties.
