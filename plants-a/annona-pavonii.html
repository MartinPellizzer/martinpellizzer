Annona pavonii

Annona pavonii, commonly known as the purple annona, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height. The plant produces large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. Its flowers are yellow with a distinctive purple or red calyx, and it bears edible, yellow, pear-shaped fruits.

The plant is valued for its ornamental beauty and is also used in traditional medicine.
