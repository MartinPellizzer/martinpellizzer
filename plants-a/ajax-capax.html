Ajax capax

Ajax capax, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to eastern Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments and thrives in well-draining soil. It is popular as a houseplant due to its ability to bloom year-round under proper care.

Its compact size and vibrant flowers make it a favorite among indoor gardeners.
