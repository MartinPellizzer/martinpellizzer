Aglaonema modestum

Aglaonema modestum, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated foliage with shades of green, white, and pink. The leaves are typically broad and oval, arranged in a rosette pattern along the stem. This plant is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and its resistance to common indoor pests.

It is also valued for its air-purifying properties and ornamental appeal.
