Anoda pristina

Anoda pristina, commonly known as the narrowleaf horsegram, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its upright growth habit and compound leaves. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It is often found in dry, open habitats and is valued for its nutritional properties.

Anoda pristina is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
