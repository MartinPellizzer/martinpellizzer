Acacia nebneb

Acacia nebneb, commonly known as the red acacia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, often multi-branched stems and bipinnate leaves that are covered in fine hairs. The plant produces yellow flowers in clusters, which are followed by long, flat pods containing edible seeds. Acacia nebneb thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, making it an important component of local ecosystems.

Its wood and seeds have been used by indigenous communities for various traditional purposes.
