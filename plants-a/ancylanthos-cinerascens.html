Ancylanthos cinerascens

Ancylanthos cinerascens, commonly known as the silverleaf daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its flowers are usually white to pale yellow, with a central disc that is darker in color.
