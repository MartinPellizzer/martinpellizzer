Adenophora coelestis var. handeliana

Adenophora coelestis var. handeliana, commonly known as the bluebell bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves and produces cylindrical seed capsules that contain numerous small seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and partial to full sunlight.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
