Achyrodes aureum var. variegatum

Achyrodes aureum var. variegatum, commonly known as the variegated golden achyrodes, is a striking succulent native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, ribbed stems that are typically green with distinctive yellow or white variegation. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the warmer months, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid or semi-arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
