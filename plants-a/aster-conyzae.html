Aster conyzae

Aster conyzae, commonly known as the common ragwort, is a flowering plant belonging to the sunflower family, Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its opposite, ovate to lanceolate leaves and clusters of small, yellowish to greenish flowers. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and is native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa. It is an annual or biennial herb that can reach heights of up to 1 meter.

Aster conyzae is often considered a weed but has been used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
