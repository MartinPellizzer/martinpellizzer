Aralia guatemalensis

Aralia guatemalensis, commonly known as the Guatemalan aralia, is a flowering plant native to Central America.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 6 meters tall. The plant has compound leaves with serrated edges and produces small, white flowers in umbel-like clusters. Its fruits are berries that turn red when mature, containing seeds.

Aralia guatemalensis is known for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and unique appearance.
