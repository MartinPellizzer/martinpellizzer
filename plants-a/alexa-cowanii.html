Alexa cowanii

Alexa cowanii, commonly known as the cowanii hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from pink to red, with a distinctive yellow center. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a soft, velvety texture. It thrives in humid, warm climates and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Alexa cowanii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant blooms and attractive foliage.
