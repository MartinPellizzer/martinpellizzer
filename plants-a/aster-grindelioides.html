Aster grindelioides

Aster grindelioides, commonly known as the woolly aster, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of western North America.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage and showy, violet-blue flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in full sun. It is a perennial species, often found in alpine and subalpine habitats.

Its unique appearance and ecological adaptations make it a notable species in its native range.
