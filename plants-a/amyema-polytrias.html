Amyema polytrias

Amyema polytrias, commonly known as the rock fig, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Moraceae family and is characterized by its small, rounded leaves and clustered, edible fruits. The plant thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid regions. Its fruit, which resembles a small fig, is a food source for various animals.

The plant's resilience to drought makes it an important component of local ecosystems.
