Aleuritopteris sichouensis

Aleuritopteris sichouensis, commonly known as the Sichuan water fern, is a species of fern native to China.

It is characterized by its large, compound fronds that are typically bipinnate, with a distinctive, delicate appearance. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in mountainous regions with high humidity. Its rhizomes are thick and creeping, allowing it to spread and form dense colonies.

Aleuritopteris sichouensis plays an important ecological role in its native habitat, contributing to soil stability and providing habitat for various microorganisms.
