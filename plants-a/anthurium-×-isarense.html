Anthurium × isarense

Anthurium × isarense, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a hybrid plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped spathe that ranges in color from red to orange, often with a contrasting green spadix. The plant thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect sunlight to maintain its foliage and floral display. Its leaves are typically broad and glossy, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Anthurium × isarense is popular as a houseplant due to its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
