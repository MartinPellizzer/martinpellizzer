Adenocarpus foliolosus var. gomaraeus

Adenocarpus foliolosus var. gomaraeus, commonly known as the Gomara's leafy legume, is a perennial shrub native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces distinctive pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grassland and savanna habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
