Amaryllis spectabilis

Amaryllis spectabilis, commonly known as the belladonna lily, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It produces large, showy flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, often with a white or pink throat. The plant features strap-like, evergreen leaves that are broad and pointed, growing from a bulbous base. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, blooming in late summer to early fall.

Amaryllis spectabilis is popular as a garden plant and for its dramatic floral display.
