Arthrochilus dockrillii

Arthrochilus dockrillii, commonly known as the Dockrill's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the coastal regions of southern Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces strikingly beautiful, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It blooms during the cooler months, usually between June and September.
