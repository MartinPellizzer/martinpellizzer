Aulomyrcia microcarpa

Aulomyrcia microcarpa, commonly known as the dwarf myrcia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its small, aromatic leaves and clusters of white or pale yellow flowers. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments within tropical rainforests.

Aulomyrcia microcarpa is valued for its ornamental appeal and has limited use in traditional medicine.
