Alsine rupestris var. usneoides

Alsine rupestris var. usneoides, commonly known as the rock sandwort, is a low-growing, mat-forming plant native to arid and rocky environments.

It features small, fleshy, greenish-blue leaves that are adapted to conserve water. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers during the spring and early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in crevices or on rock surfaces.

Its succulent nature makes it a resilient species suited to harsh, dry conditions.
