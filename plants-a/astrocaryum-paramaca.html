Astrocaryum paramaca

Astrocaryum paramaca, commonly known as the Paramaca palm, is a tropical plant native to South America, particularly found in the Amazon Basin.

It is a member of the Arecaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate leaves. The plant produces a unique fruit that is often used in traditional medicine and as a source of edible oil.

Its botanical features make it an important species in both ecological and economic contexts within its native habitat.
