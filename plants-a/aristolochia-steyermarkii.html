Aristolochia steyermarkii

Aristolochia steyermarkii, commonly known as the Steyermark's Dutchman's pipe, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

The plant typically grows as a vine, climbing or trailing along supports, and features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture. Its flowers are pollinated by specific insect species, and the plant produces small, berry-like fruits that contain seeds.
