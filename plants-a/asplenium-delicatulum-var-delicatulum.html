Asplenium delicatulum var. delicatulum

Asplenium delicatulum var. delicatulum, commonly known as the delicate spleenwort, is a species of fern belonging to the family Aspleniaceae.

It is a small, evergreen fern that typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as rock crevices or on tree trunks. The plant features delicate, lance-shaped leaves that are usually less than 10 centimeters in length. Its rhizome is short and produces a dense cluster of fronds.

This variety is often found in temperate regions and is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.
