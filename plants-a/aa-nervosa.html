Aa nervosa

Aa nervosa, commonly known as the Hawaiian tree fern, is a species native to the tropical regions of the Pacific, particularly Hawaii.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feathery fronds that give it a distinctive, tree-like appearance. The plant has a robust rhizome system that allows it to thrive in moist, shaded environments. Its leaves are bi-directionally pinnate, with a complex network of veins that contribute to its structural strength.

Aa nervosa plays an important ecological role in its native habitat, providing shelter and food for various native species.
