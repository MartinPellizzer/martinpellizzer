Arachnis bella

Arachnis bella, commonly known as the velvet tamarind, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, velvety leaves and small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces elongated, woody fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Its botanical features make it a unique addition to tropical gardens and greenhouses.
