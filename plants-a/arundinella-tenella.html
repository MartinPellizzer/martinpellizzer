Arundinella tenella

Arundinella tenella, commonly known as the slender reed, is a flowering plant species belonging to the grass family Poaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and Australia. The plant is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, spike-like inflorescences. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often covered with a fine layer of hairs.

Arundinella tenella is typically found in wetlands, marshes, and along the edges of water bodies, where it thrives in moist, nutrient-rich soils.
