Anthurium trinervium var. augustinum

Anthurium trinervium var. augustinum, commonly known as the Augustine anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a metallic sheen. The plant produces vibrant, spathaceous flowers in shades of red, orange, or yellow, which are often accompanied by a showy, leaf-like bract. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.

This variety is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and ease of care.
