Aster vagans

Aster vagans, commonly known as the wandering aster, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant produces showy blue to purple flowers that bloom in late summer and early fall. It thrives in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands.

Aster vagans is often used in native plant gardens for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
