Asplundia sphaerocephala

Asplundia sphaerocephala, commonly known as the globular fern, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its unique, rounded inflorescence. The plant features a short, stout rhizome with dense, clustered leaves that form a rosette. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, enclosed within a spadix surrounded by a spathe.

Asplundia sphaerocephala is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in warm, humid environments.
