Aquifolium spinosum

Aquifolium spinosum, commonly known as the spiny holly, is a deciduous shrub native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are typically spiny along the edges. The plant produces small, red berries that are an important food source for birds and other wildlife. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in woodland edges or hedgerows.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in temperate climates.
