Anisocampium × saitoanum

Anisocampium × saitoanum, commonly known as the hybrid golden aster, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its yellow-orange composite flowers that bloom in the summer months. The plant typically grows to a height of 30–60 cm and features narrow, grass-like leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated for its vibrant floral display.
