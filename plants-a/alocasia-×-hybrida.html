Alocasia × hybrida

Alocasia × hybrida, commonly known as the giant elephant ear, is a hybrid plant in the Araceae family.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can reach up to 1 meter in length. The plant originates from the tropical regions of Southeast Asia and is cultivated for its striking foliage. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Alocasia × hybrida requires well-draining soil and regular watering to maintain its health and vibrant appearance.
