Ardisia myriantha

Ardisia myriantha, commonly known as the clustered beautyberry, is a flowering plant in the family Ericaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Asia, including China, India, and Indonesia. The plant features glossy, dark green leaves that are opposite or clustered at the stem tips. Its small, white to pink flowers are followed by clusters of bright red berries, which are a key feature of the species.

Ardisia myriantha is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.
