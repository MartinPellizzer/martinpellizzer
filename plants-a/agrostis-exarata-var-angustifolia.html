Agrostis exarata var. angustifolia

Agrostis exarata var. angustifolia, commonly known as slender basket grass, is a perennial grass species native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

It typically grows in open, disturbed sites such as roadsides, fields, and meadows. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and is often found in sandy or loamy soils.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping or as a ground cover.
