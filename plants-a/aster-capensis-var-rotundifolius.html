Aster capensis var. rotundifolius

Aster capensis var. rotundifolius, commonly known as the round-leaved aster, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features showy, daisy-like flowers with purple to blue petals that bloom in late summer. The plant has rounded, velvety leaves that are typically arranged in a basal rosette. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
