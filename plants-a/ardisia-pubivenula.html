Ardisia pubivenula

Ardisia pubivenula, commonly known as the velvetleaf ardisia, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are typically elliptical in shape and covered in fine, velvety hairs. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers that are clustered in umbels, followed by bright red berries that are attractive to birds. Its root system is fibrous, and it thrives in moist, shaded environments.

Ardisia pubivenula is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and adaptability.
