Asphodelus ramosus subsp. ramosus

Asphodelus ramosus subsp.

ramosus, commonly known as the clustered asphodel, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa. It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces tall, branched inflorescences with pale yellow to orange flowers.

The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments. It is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and shrublands.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional uses in local cultures.
