Aspilia podophylla

Aspilia podophylla, commonly known as the African star grass, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking yellow-orange flower heads. The plant typically grows in open, grassy areas and is often found in disturbed soils. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are arranged in compact clusters.

Aspilia podophylla is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
