Ardisia rigidula

Ardisia rigidula, commonly known as the rigid leaf evergreen, is a small shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Ericaceae, which includes plants adapted to acidic soils. The plant features glossy, dark green leaves that are typically arranged in whorls around the stem. Its small, white or pinkish flowers are followed by bright red berries that are attractive to birds.
