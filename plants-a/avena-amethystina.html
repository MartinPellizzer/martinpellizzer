Avena amethystina

Avena amethystina, commonly known as purple oat, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and clusters of small, violet to purple flowers. The plant produces oval, dark purple to black seeds that are often used in gourmet and health food markets. It thrives in temperate climates and is cultivated for its ornamental value and nutritional content.

Avena amethystina is also valued for its potential medicinal properties.
