Anthemis collina

Anthemis collina, commonly known as mountain sandwort, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and western Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically reaches heights of about 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, often in clusters at the tips of its stems. Its leaves are usually narrow and linear, with a slightly hairy surface.

Anthemis collina thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or alpine environments.
