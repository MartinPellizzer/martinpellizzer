Adiantum glareosum var. monosorum

Adiantum glareosum var. monosorum, commonly known as the silver lace fern, is a delicate fern species native to Southeast Asia.

It features finely divided, lacy fronds that give it a distinctive, almost ethereal appearance. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and prefers well-draining, moist soil. Its leaves are typically a striking silver-green, with a waxy texture that helps reflect light.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its elegant foliage and air-purifying qualities.
