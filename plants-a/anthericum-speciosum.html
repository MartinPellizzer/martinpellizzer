Anthericum speciosum

Anthericum speciosum, commonly known as the showy arum, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the wetlands of eastern North America.

It features a distinctive inflorescence composed of a spadix surrounded by a showy, purple-bracted spathe. The plant has arrow-shaped, heart-lobed leaves that are typically green with a slight sheen. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in swamps and along stream banks.

Anthericum speciosum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in wetland ecosystems.
