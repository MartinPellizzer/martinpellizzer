Anthemis styriaca

Anthemis styriaca, commonly known as Styrian ragwort, is a flowering plant native to Central Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its yellow-orange composite flowers. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and rocky areas, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are upright and branching.

Anthemis styriaca is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
