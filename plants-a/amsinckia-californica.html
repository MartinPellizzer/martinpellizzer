Amsinckia californica

Amsinckia californica, commonly known as the California fiddleneck, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a biennial or short-lived perennial that grows up to 1.5 meters tall, featuring slender, branching stems covered in fine hairs. Amsinckia californica is adapted to dry, sunny environments and is often found in grasslands and disturbed soils.
