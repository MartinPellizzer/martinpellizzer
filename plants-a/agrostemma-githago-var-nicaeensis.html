Agrostemma githago var. nicaeensis

Agrostemma githago var. nicaeensis, commonly known as the Nicaean thornapple, is a variant of the common thornapple, native to southern Europe.

It is an annual herbaceous plant characterized by its erect stems and lobed leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its seeds are enclosed in a spiny capsule, which aids in seed dispersal.

This variety is often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides, showcasing its adaptability to various environments.
