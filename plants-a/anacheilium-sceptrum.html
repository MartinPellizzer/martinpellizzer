Anacheilium sceptrum

Anacheilium sceptrum, commonly known as the sacred crown, is a striking member of the Acanthaceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from deep red to purple, often resembling a crown, hence its common name. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are arranged oppositely along the stem, contributing to its ornamental appeal. Native to tropical regions, it thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full sunlight.

Its unique appearance and cultural significance make it a popular choice for both horticultural and ceremonial purposes.
