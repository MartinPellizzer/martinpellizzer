Astragalus syreitschikovii

Astragalus syreitschikovii, commonly known as the Syreitschikov's milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to central Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow or pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments, adapting to harsh conditions with deep root systems.

It is often found in rocky or sandy soils and plays a role in soil stabilization and ecological balance in its native habitat.
