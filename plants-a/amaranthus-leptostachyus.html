Amaranthus leptostachyus

Amaranthus leptostachyus, commonly known as the slender amaranth, is a herbaceous annual plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically green to reddish in color. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.

Amaranthus leptostachyus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
