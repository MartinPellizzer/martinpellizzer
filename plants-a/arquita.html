Arquita

Arquita, commonly known as the Mexican spiderwort, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and parts of Central America.

It belongs to the genus *Alternanthera* in the family Amaranthaceae. The plant features lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture and produces small, clustered flowers in shades of pink and white. Arquita thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
