Arachnis zhaoi

Arachnis zhaoi, commonly known as the Zhao's arachnis, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its elegant, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid tropical environments. Its flowers are usually small, with three petals and three sepals, often displaying a mix of white and pink hues.

Arachnis zhaoi is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in shaded, moist conditions.
