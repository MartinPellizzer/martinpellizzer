Adnaria martii

Adnaria martii, commonly known as the Mediterranean sea hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows in coastal areas and saline soils. The plant features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. Its leaves are ovate and often covered in a waxy coating to reduce water loss.

Adnaria martii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with saline tolerance.
