Aeschynanthus rejieae

Aeschynanthus rejieae, commonly known as the Rejie's ivy creeper, is a flowering plant native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant produces small, elliptical leaves that are glossy and arranged oppositely along the stems. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Aeschynanthus rejieae is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and showy blooms.
