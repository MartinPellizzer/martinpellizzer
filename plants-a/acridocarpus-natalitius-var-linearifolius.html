Acridocarpus natalitius var. linearifolius

Acridocarpus natalitius var. linearifolius, commonly known as the Natal flame tree, is a deciduous tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, fiery orange-red flowers that appear in clusters during the warmer months. The leaves are elongated and linear, giving the plant its specific epithet "linearifolius.". It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular ornamental plant in tropical and subtropical regions.

The tree is also valued for its fast growth and ability to provide shade in landscaping.
