Anthurium × pallidinervium

Anthurium × pallidinervium, commonly known as the pale-veined anthurium, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Araceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive pale green veining pattern. The plant produces striking spathes that can range in color from red to orange, often with a contrasting stripe. Native to tropical regions, it thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor and greenhouse cultivation.
