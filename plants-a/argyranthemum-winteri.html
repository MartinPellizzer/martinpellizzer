Argyranthemum winteri

Argyranthemum winteri, commonly known as the silver geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and showy, daisy-like flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders. Its compact growth habit and long blooming season add ornamental value to gardens.

Argyranthemum winteri is also valued for its drought tolerance and ability to attract pollinators.
