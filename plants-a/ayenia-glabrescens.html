Ayenia glabrescens

Ayenia glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth ayenia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and has small, narrow leaves that are densely packed. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, with ray florets surrounding a central disk.

Ayenia glabrescens is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
