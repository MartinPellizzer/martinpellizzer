Amorphophallus bulbifer var. atroviridimaculata

Amorphophallus bulbifer var. atroviridimaculata, commonly known as the black-spotted arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive arrow-shaped leaves with dark green markings. The plant produces striking, spathaceous inflorescences that are often used in ornamental horticulture. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil.

Its bulbous tubers can be used in traditional cuisine in some regions.
