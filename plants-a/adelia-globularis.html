Adelia globularis

Adelia globularis, commonly known as the globular milkbush, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, rounded growth habit and small, pale green leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and birds. Its succulent stems store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments.

Adelia globularis is often used in xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and ornamental appeal.
