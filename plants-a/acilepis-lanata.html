Acilepis lanata

Acilepis lanata, commonly known as the woolly acilepis, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its unique texture and ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
