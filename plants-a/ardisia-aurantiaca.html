Ardisia aurantiaca

Ardisia aurantiaca, commonly known as the red-fruited evergreen, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, dark green leaves and produces small, white flowers followed by bright orange-red berries. The plant is evergreen, with dense foliage that provides year-round coverage. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscape design.
