Alstonia scholaris var. blumei

Alstonia scholaris var. blumei, commonly known as the Blume's flame tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, bright red to orange-pink flowers that bloom in clusters, creating a dramatic visual display. The leaves are typically alternate, simple, and ovate, with a smooth surface and a pointed tip. The tree produces woody, capsule-like fruits that contain several seeds.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is well-suited to warm, humid climates.
