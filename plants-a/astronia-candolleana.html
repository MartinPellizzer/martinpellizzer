Astronia candolleana

Astronia candolleana, commonly known as the white starwood, is a shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compound leaves, which are usually opposite and have a rough texture. The plant produces small, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters, giving it its common name. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The fruit is a small, woody capsule that contains several seeds.
