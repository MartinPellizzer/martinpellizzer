Aspilia africana

Aspilia africana, commonly known as the African flame flower, is a flowering plant native to sub-Saharan Africa.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces bright orange to yellow flowers that bloom in late summer and early autumn. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a rough texture.

Aspilia africana is known for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional African medicine for various health purposes.
