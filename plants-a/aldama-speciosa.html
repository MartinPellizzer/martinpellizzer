Aldama speciosa

Aldama speciosa, commonly known as the showy Aldama, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its foliage consists of narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

Aldama speciosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
