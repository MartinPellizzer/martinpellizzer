Anstrutheria madagascariensis

Anstrutheria madagascariensis, commonly known as the Madagascar star orchid, is a rare and unique orchid species native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers that display vibrant colors. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories where it thrives on decaying organic matter. Its roots are specialized for absorbing nutrients from the forest floor, and it has adapted to the specific ecological conditions of its native habitat.
