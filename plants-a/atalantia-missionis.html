Atalantia missionis

Atalantia missionis, commonly known as the Mission fig, is a species of flowering plant in the family Rutaceae.

It is native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico, thriving in arid and semi-arid environments. The Mission fig is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical significance in early Spanish missions.
