Anthurium cucullispathum

Anthurium cucullispathum, commonly known as the hooded anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are usually a deep green with a glossy texture. The plant produces striking spathes that are typically bright red or orange, often with a contrasting green or yellow base. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, nutrient-rich soil.

Anthurium cucullispathum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in botanical gardens.
