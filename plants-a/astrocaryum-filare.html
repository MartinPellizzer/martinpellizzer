Astrocaryum filare

Astrocaryum filare, commonly known as the yellow palm or *palmier jaune*, is a species of flowering plant in the Arecaceae family.

It is native to South America, particularly Brazil, and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate leaves. The plant produces yellowish to orange fruits that contain a single seed, which is often used in traditional medicine. Astrocaryum filare is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been studied for its potential economic and medicinal applications.

Its growth habit and ecological role make it an important species in tropical ecosystems.
