Anthurium antioquiense

Anthurium antioquiense, commonly known as the Antioquian arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to Colombia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and striking spadix inflorescence. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its foliage is often a deep green with a waxy texture, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers surrounded by a colorful spathe.

Anthurium antioquiense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in botanical gardens.
