Arachnorchis heberleana

Arachnorchis heberleana, commonly known as the Heberlean spider orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Australia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, spider-like floral structure. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in forest understoreys. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish in color, with intricate patterns that aid in pollination by specific insect species.

Arachnorchis heberleana is a rare and endangered species, highlighting the importance of conservation efforts in its natural habitat.
