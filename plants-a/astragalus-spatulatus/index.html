Astragalus Spatulatus

Astragalus spatulatus is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the arid regions of western North America. It belongs to the legume family and is known for its small, spiny pods and pale yellow blooms. This species thrives in rocky soils and is often found in desert environments. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species. Conservation efforts are crucial to protect this unique and ecologically important plant.
