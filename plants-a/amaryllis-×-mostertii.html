Amaryllis × mostertii

Amaryllis × mostertii, commonly known as the hybrid amaryllis, is a hybrid plant resulting from crosses between different amaryllis species.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, pink, white, and yellow. The plant typically produces multiple stems with broad, strap-like leaves that are often a deep green. It thrives in warm, bright environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Amaryllis × mostertii is popular as a houseplant and for its ability to bloom reliably when cared for properly.
