Areca kinabaluensis

Areca kinabaluensis, commonly known as the Borneo areca, is a flowering plant native to the highlands of Borneo.

It belongs to the Arecaceae family, which includes palms, and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and pinnate leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in montane forests.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
