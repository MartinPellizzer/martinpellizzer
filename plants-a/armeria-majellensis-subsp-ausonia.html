Armeria majellensis subsp. ausonia

Armeria majellensis subsp.

ausonia, commonly known as the Ausonian thrift, is a low-growing, flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region. It produces dense clusters of pink or white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.

The plant has succulent, fleshy leaves and is well adapted to dry, rocky habitats. It is often found in coastal areas and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.

This subspecies is closely related to other members of the Amaranthaceae family.
