Asplenium brasiliense f. pseudoharpeoides

Asplenium brasiliense f. pseudoharpeoides, commonly known as the Brazilian spleenwort, is a fern belonging to the Aspleniaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped fronds that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. The plant exhibits a rhizomatous growth habit, with creeping stems that root at the nodes. Its spores are produced in small, clustered sori on the undersides of the fronds.

This fern is often found in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices.
