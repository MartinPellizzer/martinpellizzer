Asteriscus schimperi

Asteriscus schimperi, commonly known as the Ethiopian aster, is a flowering plant native to the highlands of Ethiopia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant produces small, white to purple florets arranged in a flat, star-like shape, giving it its common name. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in montane grasslands.

Asteriscus schimperi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
