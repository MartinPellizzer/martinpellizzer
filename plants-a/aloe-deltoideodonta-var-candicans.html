Aloe deltoideodonta var. candicans

Aloe deltoideodonta var. candicans, commonly known as the candle aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its upright, branching stems and fleshy, sword-shaped leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. The plant produces tall, spikelike inflorescences with bright yellow to orange flowers, which bloom in summer. Its leaves contain a gel-like substance that is widely used in skincare and medicinal applications.

It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
