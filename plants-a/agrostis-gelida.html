Agrostis gelida

Agrostis gelida, commonly known as the alpine bentgrass, is a perennial grass species native to cold climates.

It thrives in high-altitude environments and is often found in alpine tundras and rocky slopes. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It is adapted to withstand harsh weather conditions, including frost and strong winds.

Its botanical features make it an important component of alpine ecosystems.
