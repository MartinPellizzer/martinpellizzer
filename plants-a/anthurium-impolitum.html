Anthurium impolitum

Anthurium impolitum, commonly known as the velvet anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a velvety texture. The plant produces showy spadices, which are brightly colored, spadix-like inflorescences that can range from red to yellow. Anthurium impolitum thrives in humid, warm environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.

Its unique foliage and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
