Anthericum dielsii

Anthericum dielsii, commonly known as the Diels' onion, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its strap-like, grass-like leaves. The plant produces clusters of white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. Its bulbs are used in traditional medicine and are also cultivated for ornamental purposes.

Anthericum dielsii thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.
