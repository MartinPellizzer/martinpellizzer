Allophylus petiolulatus

Allophylus petiolulatus, commonly known as the small-leaf alder, is a deciduous shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its simple, opposite, and usually elliptic leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers in clusters, which are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds.
