Aster spectatissimus

Aster spectatissimus, commonly known as the showy aster, is a flowering plant native to the United States.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from white to purple, often with a yellow center. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and prairies. It belongs to the sunflower family (Asteraceae) and produces ray flowers that attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.

The leaves are usually alternate, lance-shaped, and covered in fine hairs.
