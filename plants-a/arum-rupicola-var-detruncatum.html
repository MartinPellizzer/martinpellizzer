Arum rupicola var. detruncatum

Arum rupicola var. detruncatum, commonly known as the dwarf arrowhead plant, is a tropical perennial that features arrow-shaped, glossy leaves.

It produces striking, spadix-like inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, typical of the Araceae family. The plant thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions. It has a rhizomatous growth habit, allowing it to spread and form dense colonies.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
