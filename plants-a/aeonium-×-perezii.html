Aeonium × perezii

Aeonium × perezii, commonly known as the Ghost Plant, is a hybrid succulent belonging to the Crassulaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact, rosette-shaped growth pattern and its striking, translucent or variegated foliage. The plant's leaves are often fleshy and thick, adapted to store water in arid environments. It produces vibrant, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the growing season.

Aeonium × perezii is native to the Canary Islands and is well-suited for rock gardens or as a container plant due to its low maintenance requirements.
