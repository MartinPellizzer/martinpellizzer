Aglaonema warburgii

Aglaonema warburgii, commonly known as the Variegated Chinese Evergreen, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated foliage with shades of green, white, and pink. The leaves are typically lance-shaped and can grow up to 30 cm in length. It is a member of the Araceae family, which includes many ornamental plants.

This plant thrives in indirect light and requires moderate watering, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
