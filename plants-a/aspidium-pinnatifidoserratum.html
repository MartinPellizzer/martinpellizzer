Aspidium pinnatifidoserratum

Aspidium pinnatifidoserratum, commonly known as the serrated spleenwort, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped fronds that are finely serrated along the edges. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its reproductive structures, including sori, are located on the underside of the fronds.

Aspidium pinnatifidoserratum is often found in temperate regions and is valued for its ornamental appearance in horticulture.
