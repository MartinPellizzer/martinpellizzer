Aletes megarrhiza

Aletes megarrhiza, commonly known as the giant leaf orchid, is a striking tropical plant characterized by its large, broad leaves and showy flowers.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is native to the humid regions of Southeast Asia. The plant's leaves are typically elongated and have a waxy surface, which helps in reducing water loss in its natural habitat. Its flowers are often brightly colored, with intricate patterns that attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.

Aletes megarrhiza is valued both for its ornamental beauty and its ecological role in supporting local biodiversity.
