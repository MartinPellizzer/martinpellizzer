Aloe lastii

Aloe lastii, commonly known as the Cape Town aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, often with a waxy coating. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with bright yellow flowers during the summer months. Aloe lastii is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its leaves to survive drought conditions.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and low maintenance requirements.
