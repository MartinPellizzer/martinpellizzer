Anthyllis aspalathoides

Anthyllis aspalathoides, commonly known as the honeycomb bur.

This plant is a member of the legume family and is native to South Africa. It is characterized by its dense, hairy foliage and clusters of small, yellow flowers. The plant produces distinctive, spiny pods that contain seeds, which are often used in traditional medicine.

It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in coastal and inland regions.
