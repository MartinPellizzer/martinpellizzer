Aeschynomene solitariiflora

Aeschynomene solitariiflora, commonly known as the solitary-flowered aeschynomene, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable plant for ecological studies and potential agricultural applications.
