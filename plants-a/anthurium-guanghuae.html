Anthurium guanghuae

Anthurium guanghuae, commonly known as the Guanghua anthurium, is a tropical plant native to China.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves and vibrant, spadix-like inflorescences. The plant thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth. Its foliage is typically dark green with a glossy texture, and the flowers are often reddish or orange in color.

Anthurium guanghuae is valued as an ornamental plant for its aesthetic appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
