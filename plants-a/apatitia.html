Apatitia

Apatitia, commonly known as water dock, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Asteraceae.

It consists of several species, most of which are perennial herbs native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere. Apatitia species are often found in wet or moist soils, such as along stream banks or in marshy areas.
