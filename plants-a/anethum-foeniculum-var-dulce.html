Anethum foeniculum var. dulce

Anethum foeniculum var. dulce, commonly known as sweet fennel, is a herbaceous plant characterized by its feathery, aromatic foliage and yellow flowers.

It is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in well-drained soil with full sun exposure. The plant produces small, white to yellow flowers that form umbels, typical of the Apiaceae family. Its seeds, often used as a spice, have a sweet, anise-like flavor.

Sweet fennel is also valued for its ornamental qualities and medicinal properties.
