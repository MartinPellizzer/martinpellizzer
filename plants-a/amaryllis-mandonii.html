Amaryllis mandonii

Amaryllis mandonii, commonly known as the Cape Bellflower, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple, often with a contrasting throat. The plant produces strap-like leaves that are broad and pointed, emerging from a basal rosette. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Amaryllis mandonii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and can be grown in gardens or as a houseplant.
