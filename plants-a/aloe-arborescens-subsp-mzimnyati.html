Aloe arborescens subsp. mzimnyati

Aloe arborescens subsp.

mzimnyati, commonly known as the Natal aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa. It features a branched stem with fleshy, dark green leaves that have white tubular teeth along the edges.

The plant produces bright yellow flowers in clusters during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its medicinal properties have been traditionally used for skin treatments and wound healing.
