Artemisia hallaisanensis var. philippinensis

Artemisia hallaisanensis var. philippinensis, commonly known as Philippine wormwood, is a perennial herb native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its aromatic, silver-gray foliage and small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and forest edges. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes many flowering plants with composite flower heads.

The species is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
