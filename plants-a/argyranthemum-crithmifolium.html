Argyranthemum crithmifolium

Argyranthemum crithmifolium, commonly known as the silverleaf geranium, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It is characterized by its silvery, velvety leaves that give it its common name, and its daisy-like flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.

Argyranthemum crithmifolium is also valued for its hardiness and low maintenance requirements.
