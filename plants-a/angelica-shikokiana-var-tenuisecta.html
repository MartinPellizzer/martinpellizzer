Angelica shikokiana var. tenuisecta

Angelica shikokiana var. tenuisecta, commonly known as the narrow-leaved angelica, is a perennial herb native to Japan.

It features tall, slender stems that can reach heights of up to 2 meters. The plant has large, pinnately compound leaves with finely divided segments, giving it a delicate, fern-like appearance. Its flowers are small, greenish-yellow, and arranged in dense, terminal umbels.

This variety is valued for its aromatic foliage and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes in gardens.
