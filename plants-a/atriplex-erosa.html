Atriplex erosa

Atriplex erosa, commonly known as saltbush, is a shrub native to arid and semi-arid regions of North America.

It is characterized by its small, fleshy leaves that are adapted to reduce water loss in dry environments. The plant produces greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in dense, spherical inflorescences. Its salt-tolerant nature makes it a valuable plant for stabilizing soils in saline areas.

Atriplex erosa is also used in traditional medicine and as forage for livestock.
