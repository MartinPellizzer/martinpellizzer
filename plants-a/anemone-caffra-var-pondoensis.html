Anemone caffra var. pondoensis

Anemone caffra var. pondoensis, commonly known as the Pondo anemone, is a South African plant belonging to the Ranunculaceae family.

It is characterized by its showy, pink to red flowers that bloom in spring and summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded areas such as forest floors or along streams. Its leaves are palmately divided, with a hairy texture that helps retain moisture.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and botanical collections.
