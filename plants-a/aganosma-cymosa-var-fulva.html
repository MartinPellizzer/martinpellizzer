Aganosma cymosa var. fulva

Aganosma cymosa var. fulva, commonly known as the red-flowered aganosma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange-red flowers that appear in cymes, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and have a glossy, dark green surface.

It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
