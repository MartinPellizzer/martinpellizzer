Alchemilla Vulgaris Subvar. Wichurae

Lady's mantle, scientifically known as Alchemilla vulgaris subvar. wichurae, is a hardy perennial plant native to temperate regions of Europe and Asia. It is prized for its delicate foliage and ability to thrive in partial shade and well-drained soil. This variant of lady's mantle is particularly noted for its dense, silver-green leaves that create a striking visual contrast. The plant is also valued for its medicinal properties and historical use in herbal remedies. With its low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for gardeners seeking a resilient and ornamental ground cover.
