Abrotanum pauciflorum

Abrotanum pauciflorum, commonly known as the few-flowered abrotanum, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small shrub that typically grows in dry, rocky habitats. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the warmer months. Its leaves are narrow and grayish-green, often covered in fine hairs.

The species is noted for its aromatic properties and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
