Anthurium cundinamarcense

Anthurium cundinamarcense, commonly known as the Cundinamarca arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to Colombia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces an inflorescence consisting of a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe. It thrives in humid, shady environments and requires well-draining soil.

Anthurium cundinamarcense is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant.
