Antidesma obovatum

Antidesma obovatum, commonly known as the African starfruit or yellow star.

It is a shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa, characterized by its opposite, oval-shaped leaves and clusters of small, yellowish flowers. The plant produces small, edible fruits that are often used in local cuisines. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value and fruit-bearing capacity make it a notable species in horticulture.
