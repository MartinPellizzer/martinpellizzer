Anisonema wrightianum

Anisonema wrightianum, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves and clusters of showy, violet-colored flowers. The plant thrives in humid, well-drained soil and prefers indirect sunlight. Its foliage is often glossy and dark green, adding to its ornamental appeal.

Anisonema wrightianum is popular as a houseplant due to its ease of care and long blooming season.
