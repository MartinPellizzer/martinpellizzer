Anguria speciosa

Anguria speciosa, commonly known as the showy cucumber, is a striking member of the Cucurbitaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, lobed leaves and vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers that range from yellow to orange. The plant produces elongated, edible fruits that are typically green with a waxy coating. It thrives in warm climates and requires full sun for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value and unique appearance, it is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
