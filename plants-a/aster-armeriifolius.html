Aster armeriifolius

Aster armeriifolius, commonly known as the narrow-leaved aster, is a herbaceous perennial native to the eastern United States.

It features upright, branched stems that bear numerous small, blue to violet flowers arranged in clusters. The leaves are narrow, lance-shaped, and often have a hairy texture, giving the plant a distinctive appearance. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or wetland habitats.

This plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and its role in supporting pollinators.
