Anthurium hansonianum

Anthurium hansonianum, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a reddish tinge. The plant produces vibrant red spathes that enclose its small, spadix-shaped flowers, which are typical of the Araceae family. It thrives in humid, warm environments with bright, indirect light.

Anthurium hansonianum is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and long-lasting blooms.
