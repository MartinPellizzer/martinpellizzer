Aspilia tanganyikensis

Aspilia tanganyikensis, commonly known as the Tanganyika torchflower, is a flowering plant native to the forests of Tanzania.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking yellow-orange inflorescence. The plant typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, with narrow, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are arranged in a spherical head, which is surrounded by bracts that resemble leaves.

Aspilia tanganyikensis is adapted to humid, shaded environments and is often found in montane forests.
