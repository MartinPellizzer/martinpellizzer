Achillea ptarmica var. longifolia

Achillea ptarmica var. longifolia, commonly known as narrowleaf yarrow, is a perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features slender, upright stems and basal leaves that are linear to lanceolate in shape. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in flat-topped umbels. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy addition to gardens.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable species for both ornamental and ecological purposes.
