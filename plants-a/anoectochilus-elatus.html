Anoectochilus elatus

Anoectochilus elatus, commonly known as the golden orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking golden-yellow flowers and is often found in shaded, humid environments. The plant has fleshy, dark green leaves that help it store water, adapting to its tropical habitat. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires high humidity for optimal growth.

Anoectochilus elatus is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
