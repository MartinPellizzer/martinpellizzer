Anthurium maguirei

Anthurium maguirei, commonly known as the Maguire's anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central America.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive spadix and spathe. The plant features glossy, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 cm in length. Its flowers are typically greenish-white with a prominent spadix, and it is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Anthurium maguirei prefers bright, indirect light and high humidity to thrive.
