Amomum petiolatum

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes other aromatic plants like ginger and turmeric. The plant features tall, slender stems with lance-shaped leaves that are often aromatic. Its flowering spikes produce small, pale green flowers that are followed by distinctive, three-lobed fruits.
