Astracantha multispina

Astracantha multispina, commonly known as the spiny globe thistle, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its spiny, spherical stems and leaves, which are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, blue or purple flowers that bloom in summer, adding a striking visual element to its appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is sometimes cultivated as an ornamental plant.
