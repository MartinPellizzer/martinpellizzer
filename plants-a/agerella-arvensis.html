Agerella arvensis

Agerella arvensis, commonly known as the field agrimony, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Rosaceae.

It is a small, herbaceous perennial that typically grows in disturbed soils and open habitats. Its fruit is a dry, schizocarp that splits into two one-seeded parts upon maturity.

Agerella arvensis is known for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally in herbal remedies.
