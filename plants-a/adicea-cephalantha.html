Adicea cephalantha

Adicea cephalantha, commonly known as the Cape mountain heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and blueberries. The plant is characterized by its dense, low-growing shrub form with small, leathery leaves. Its flowers are typically white or pale pink, arranged in clusters at the ends of the stems.
