Argyrolobium macrophyllum

Argyrolobium macrophyllum, commonly known as the large-leaved silver globular pea, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-white, globular flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has a compact, bushy growth habit with trifoliate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens for ornamental purposes.
