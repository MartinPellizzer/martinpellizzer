Aster ardense

Aster ardense, commonly known as the fiery aster, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its showy composite flowers. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces vibrant purple to red flowers in the fall. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, often growing in open woodlands or along roadsides.

Aster ardense is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, providing nectar for pollinators.
