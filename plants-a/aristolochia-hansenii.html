Aristolochia hansenii

Aristolochia hansenii, commonly known as Hansen's Dutchman's pipe, is a climbing vine native to South America.

It is characterized by its unique, pipe-shaped flowers that are often a deep purple or brownish color. The plant produces heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and a strong, aromatic scent. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in shaded, moist environments.

The genus Aristolochia is known for its complex floral structures and is part of the Aristolochiaceae family.
