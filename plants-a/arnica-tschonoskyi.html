Arnica tschonoskyi

Arnica tschonoskyi, commonly known as the Tschonosky's arnica, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the genus Arnica, which is part of the sunflower family, Asteraceae. The plant features hairy, basal leaves and produces yellow-orange flowers with a distinctive tubular shape. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in alpine environments.

Due to its medicinal properties, it has been historically used in traditional medicine for its anti-inflammatory and analgesic effects.
