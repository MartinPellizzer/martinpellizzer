Andromeda oleifolia

Andromeda oleifolia, commonly known as the silver leaf honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to the coastal regions of California and Oregon.

It is characterized by its small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in early spring. The leaves are elliptical, glossy, and often covered in a waxy coating that gives them a silvery appearance. This plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in rocky coastal areas.

It is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
