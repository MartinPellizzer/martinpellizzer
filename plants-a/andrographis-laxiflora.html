Andrographis laxiflora

Andrographis laxiflora, commonly known as the slender leaf andrographis, is a herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Acanthaceae and is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in whorled clusters along the stems. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Andrographis laxiflora is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
