Allium sannineum

Allium sannineum, commonly known as the purple onion, is a bulbous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features a cluster of round, purple or pinkish bulbs that are typically harvested for culinary use. The plant produces tall, slender stems with narrow, grass-like leaves. Its flowers are small, white or pale pink, and arranged in a spherical cluster at the top of the stem.

Allium sannineum is a hardy perennial that thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.
