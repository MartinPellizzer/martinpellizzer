Arctium

Arctium, commonly known as burdock, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its tall, spiny stems and large, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces purple or pink flowers that are arranged in a composite head, typical of the Asteraceae family. Burdock is native to Europe, Asia, and North America, and it thrives in disturbed soils and open habitats.

Its roots are often used in traditional medicine and culinary applications.
