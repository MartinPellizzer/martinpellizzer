Alphonseopsis

Alphonseopsis, commonly known as the Christmas cactus, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It belongs to the genus Schlumbergera and is characterized by its segmented, leaf-like stems that store water. The plant produces vibrant flowers in shades of red, pink, and white during the holiday season. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Alphonseopsis is popular as a houseplant due to its ease of care and festive blooming period.
