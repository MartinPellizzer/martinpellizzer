Aconitum brevicalcaratum var. brevicalcaratum

Aconitum brevicalcaratum var. brevicalcaratum, commonly known as the short-horned monkshood, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its striking, hood-shaped blue to purple flowers. The plant has dark green, palmately lobed leaves with a distinctive, waxy texture. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Due to its toxic alkaloids, it is important to handle the plant with care.
