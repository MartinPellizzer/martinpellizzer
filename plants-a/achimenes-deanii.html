Achimenes deanii

Achimenes deanii, commonly known as the Dean's achimenes, is a tropical perennial plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, pink, and white. The plant has heart-shaped, velvety leaves that are typically dark green in color. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Achimenes deanii is often cultivated as a decorative plant for gardens and indoor spaces due to its ornamental value.
