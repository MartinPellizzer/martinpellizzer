Adesmia furcata

Adesmia furcata, commonly known as the clustered burr legume, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in dry, rocky habitats. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in dense clusters, which are followed by distinctive, spiny pods. Its leaves are compound, consisting of several oval-shaped leaflets.

Adesmia furcata is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
