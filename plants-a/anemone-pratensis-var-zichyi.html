Anemone pratensis var. zichyi

Anemone pratensis var. zichyi, commonly known as the Zichy meadow anemone, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central Europe.

It typically grows in moist meadows and woodland edges, preferring partial to full shade. The plant features showy, white to pale pink flowers with five petals, blooming from late spring to early summer. Its leaves are basal, heart-shaped, and covered in fine hairs.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting pollinators.
