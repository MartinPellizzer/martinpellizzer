Adesmia ramosissima

Adesmia ramosissima, commonly known as the ramose adesmia, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly found in Argentina and Chile.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its shrub-like growth habit with multiple branches. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are typically pale yellow to white in color. Adesmia ramosissima is often found in dry grasslands and rocky areas, where it plays a role in stabilizing soil and supporting local biodiversity.
