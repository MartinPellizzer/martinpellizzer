Aspidium otarioides

Aspidium otarioides, commonly known as the Otarioid fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Athyriaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, pinnate fronds that are typically dark green and have a leathery texture. The plant produces spores in small, clustered sporangia located on the underside of its leaves. It is native to regions of South America, particularly in Brazil, and thrives in moist, shaded environments.

Aspidium otarioides is often found in humid forests and is valued for its ornamental appearance in horticultural settings.
