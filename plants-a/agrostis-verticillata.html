Agrostis verticillata

Agrostis verticillata, commonly known as slender meadowgrass, is a perennial grass species native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

It typically grows in open, moist habitats such as meadows, pastures, and wetlands. The plant has slender, tufted stems and produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green coloration.

Agrostis verticillata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
