Athanasia pinnatifida

Athanasia pinnatifida, commonly known as the golden pincushion, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense, spherical inflorescence composed of bright yellow ray flowers. The plant features narrow, pinnately arranged leaves that give it its scientific name. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Athanasia pinnatifida is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
