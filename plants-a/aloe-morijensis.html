Aloe morijensis

Aloe morijensis, commonly known as the Orange Tree Aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a reddish or orange tinge along the edges. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow flowers, which bloom in summer. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun exposure, making it suitable for arid or semi-arid environments.

Aloe morijensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
