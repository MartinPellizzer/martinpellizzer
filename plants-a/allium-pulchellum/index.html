Allium Pulchellum

Allium pulchellum, commonly known as the purple onion, is a striking plant native to the mountainous regions of southwestern China. It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of vibrant purple flowers that bloom in late summer. This species belongs to the Allium genus, which includes many edible and ornamental plants. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for gardens and botanical collections. Despite its beauty, it is often mistaken for other Allium species due to its similar appearance.
