Aira tenorei var. aristata

Aira tenorei var. aristata, commonly known as the dwarf aira, is a compact, grass-like plant native to the island of Socotra.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences with tiny, white to pale blue flowers. The plant is adapted to arid environments and thrives in well-drained soils. It is often found in rocky or sandy habitats, showcasing its resilience to harsh conditions.

Its unique appearance and limited distribution make it a notable species in botanical studies.
