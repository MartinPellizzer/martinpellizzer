Alstroemeria torta

Alstroemeria torta, commonly known as the twisted alstroemeria, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, orange, and yellow. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and a compact growth habit, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Alstroemeria torta is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in floral arrangements.
