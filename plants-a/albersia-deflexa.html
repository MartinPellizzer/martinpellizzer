Albersia deflexa

Albersia deflexa, commonly known as the zigzag lily, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, nodding flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as along stream banks or in dense forests. Its leaves are narrow and often exhibit a zigzag pattern along the stem, giving it its common name.

Albersia deflexa is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in humid conditions.
