Agalmyla tuberculata

Agalmyla tuberculata, commonly known as the rough agalmyla, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered white or pink flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental flowers make it a desirable addition to coastal and Mediterranean gardens.
