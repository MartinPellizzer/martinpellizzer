Arenaria incrassata

Arenaria incrassata, commonly known as the thick-leaved sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to arctic and alpine regions.

It typically thrives in rocky or sandy soils, showcasing its adaptability to harsh environments. The plant features dense, fleshy leaves that help conserve moisture, and small white or pink flowers appear in early summer. It is often found in tundra and mountainous areas across Europe, Asia, and North America.

Its botanical characteristics make it a resilient species suited for extreme climatic conditions.
