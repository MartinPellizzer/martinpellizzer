Andromeda montana

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and blueberries, and is characterized by its evergreen foliage and showy, bell-shaped flowers. Andromeda montana is often found in forest understories and alpine meadows, where it plays a role in stabilizing soil and supporting local biodiversity.
