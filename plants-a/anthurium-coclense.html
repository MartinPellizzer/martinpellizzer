Anthurium coclense

Anthurium coclense, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves and vibrant red spadix surrounded by a white spathe. The plant thrives in humid, warm environments and requires indirect sunlight to flourish. Its botanical features include thick, waxy foliage and a unique inflorescence structure typical of the Araceae family.

Anthurium coclense is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and air-purifying qualities.
