Anthurium perezfarrerae

Anthurium perezfarrerae, commonly known as the Pezferreira's anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically deep green with a glossy texture. The plant produces vibrant red spathes that enclose its yellow-orange spadix, making it a visually striking ornamental species. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium perezfarrerae is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal and is relatively easy to care for in the right conditions.
