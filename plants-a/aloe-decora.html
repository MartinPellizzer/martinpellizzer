Aloe decora

Aloe decora, commonly known as the decorative aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with red or purple margins. The plant produces tall, branched flower stalks that bear yellow flowers, adding ornamental value. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Aloe decora is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.
