Aporum obcordatum

Aporum obcordatum, commonly known as the obcordate aporum, is a flowering plant belonging to the Araceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant features large, heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white and green patterns. Its inflorescence consists of a spadix surrounded by a spathe, typical of the Araceae family.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking foliage.
