Acacia × mangiiformis

Acacia × mangiiformis, commonly known as the hybrid mangosteen, is a cross between *Acacia mangium* and *Acacia nilotica*.

It is a shrub or small tree native to tropical regions of Asia and Africa. The plant is characterized by its feathery, bipinnate leaves and yellow to golden-orange flowers. It produces small, round fruits that are typically consumed raw or used in traditional medicine.

The botanical features of this hybrid make it an important species in ecological and agricultural studies.
