Anthurium macrolonchium

Anthurium macrolonchium, commonly known as the long-spurred anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically deep green with a velvety texture. The plant produces dramatic, spadix-shaped inflorescences surrounded by a brightly colored spathe, often in shades of red or orange. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and requires well-draining soil.

Anthurium macrolonchium is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
