Adesmia calycosa

Adesmia calycosa, commonly known as the velvetleaf tree, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and showy, pea-like flowers. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, thriving in moist, well-drained soils.
