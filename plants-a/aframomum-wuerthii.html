Aframomum wuerthii

Aframomum wuerthii, commonly known as African cardamom, is a member of the Zingiberaceae family, known for its aromatic properties.

It is native to tropical Africa and thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant produces spindle-shaped, dark brown fruits that contain aromatic seeds used in traditional medicine and cuisine. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, often with a waxy texture.
