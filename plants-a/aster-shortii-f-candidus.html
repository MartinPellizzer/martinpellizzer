Aster shortii f. candidus

Aster shortii f. candidus, commonly known as the white aster, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its showy, white to pale yellow ray florets and compact, daisy-like flower heads. This cultivar is prized for its ornamental value and is often used in gardens for its delicate appearance. It typically grows in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

The plant is relatively low maintenance and is suitable for both container gardening and landscape beds.
