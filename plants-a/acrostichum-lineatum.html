Acrostichum lineatum

Acrostichum lineatum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf fern, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its feathery, compound fronds that are typically narrow and linear in shape. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found near water bodies. Its leaves are composed of multiple pinnae, which are small, lance-shaped segments.

Acrostichum lineatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in horticulture for its unique foliage.
