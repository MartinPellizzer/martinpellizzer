Achnatherum swallenii

Achnatherum swallenii, commonly known as Swallen's needlegrass, is a perennial grass native to the western United States.

It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. The plant has slender, wiry stems and small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny flowers. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green coloration.

Achnatherum swallenii plays a role in stabilizing soils and providing forage for wildlife in its native range.
