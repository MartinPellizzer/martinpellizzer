Argemone pleiacantha

Argemone pleiacantha, commonly known as the narrowleaf argemone, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and parts of the southwestern United States.

It is a herbaceous annual or perennial plant characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and showy yellow flowers. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous tiny, black seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and potential medicinal properties, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
