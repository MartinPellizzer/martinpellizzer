Arquita grandiflora

Arquita grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flower arquita, is a flowering plant native to the temperate regions of South America.

It is characterized by its showy, clustered inflorescences that produce pink to purple petals. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in partial to full sunlight. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.

Arquita grandiflora is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
