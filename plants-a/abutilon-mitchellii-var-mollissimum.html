Abutilon mitchellii var. mollissimum

Abutilon mitchellii var. mollissimum, commonly known as velvetleaf abutilon, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its fuzzy, velvety leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces showy, lantern-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to red. It is a deciduous shrub that thrives in moist, well-drained soils.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and ecological restoration projects.
