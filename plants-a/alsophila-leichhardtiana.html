Alsophila leichhardtiana

Alsophila leichhardtiana, commonly known as the Leichhardt tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to Australia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feathery fronds that can grow up to 3 meters in length. The plant has a distinctive, hollow stem that is covered in a thick layer of dead fronds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

The fronds are arranged in a spiral pattern, giving the plant a unique and elegant appearance.
