Anthurium × eduardi

Anthurium × eduardi, commonly known as the Velvet Monster, is a hybrid plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves covered in a velvety texture. The plant produces vibrant, spatulate-shaped spathes that can range in color from red to orange, often with a contrasting green or yellow midrib. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.

Anthurium × eduardi is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and long-lasting blooms.
