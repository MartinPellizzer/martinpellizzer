Arisaema robustum var. ovale

Arisaema robustum var. ovale, commonly known as the Chinese arrowroot or umbrella plant, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its unique, umbrella-like inflorescence. The plant features large, heart-shaped leaves and produces striking, spadix-like flowers surrounded by a modified leaf called a spathe. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The plant is also used in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.
