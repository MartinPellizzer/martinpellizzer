Aira varia

Aira varia, commonly known as the variable air grass, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the grass family (Poaceae) and is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often with a bluish-green hue.

Aira varia is typically found in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands.
