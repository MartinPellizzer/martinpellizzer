Anisophyllea trapezoidalis

Anisophyllea trapezoidalis, commonly known as the zigzag tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, zigzagging branches and dark green, elliptical leaves that are glossy and leathery in texture. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by round, red or orange fruits containing seeds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
