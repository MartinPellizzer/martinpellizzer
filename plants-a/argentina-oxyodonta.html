Argentina oxyodonta

Argentina oxyodonta, commonly known as the Andean bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Andes in South America.

Argentina oxyodonta is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable garden conditions.
