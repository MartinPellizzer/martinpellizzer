Adenocaulon integrifolium

Adenocaulon integrifolium, commonly known as the smoothleaf pearly everlasting, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and opposite, smooth, ovate leaves.
