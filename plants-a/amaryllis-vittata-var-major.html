Amaryllis vittata var. major

Amaryllis vittata var. major, commonly known as the giant tiger lily, is a striking bulbous plant native to South America.

It features large, showy flowers with vibrant orange petals adorned with dark red stripes, resembling a tiger's pattern. The plant produces tall, sturdy stems that can reach up to 3 meters in height, making it a dramatic addition to gardens. Its foliage consists of long, narrow leaves that are often mistaken for grass.

This species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for its striking floral display.
