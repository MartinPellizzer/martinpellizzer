Achyranthes breviflora

Achyranthes breviflora, commonly known as Indian knotweed, is a herbaceous plant native to Asia.

It is characterized by its erect, branched stems and small, greenish-white flowers that appear in clusters. The plant has alternate, heart-shaped leaves with a rough texture. It thrives in moist, fertile soils and is often found in wetlands or along riverbanks.

Achyranthes breviflora is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional medicine.
