Anthurium triangulum

Anthurium triangulum, commonly known as the triangle anthurium, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped leaves that are often a deep green with a glossy texture. The plant produces striking spathes, which are modified leaves that enclose the small, spadix-like inflorescence. Anthurium triangulum thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and as a decorative plant.
