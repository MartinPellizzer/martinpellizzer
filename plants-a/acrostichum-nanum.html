Acrostichum nanum

Acrostichum nanum, commonly known as the small fern or dwarf fern, is a species of fern native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is a small, terrestrial fern that typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or along stream banks. The plant has delicate, finely divided fronds that are usually less than 30 centimeters in length. Its rhizomes are short and produce clusters of small, spore-bearing leaves.

Acrostichum nanum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
