Achimenes dentoniana

Achimenes dentoniana, commonly known as the toothed achimenes, is a tropical flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers that come in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with serrated edges, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as a ornamental plant.

Achimenes dentoniana prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight for optimal growth.
