Anthurium subcaulescens

Anthurium subcaulescens, commonly known as the slender anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are deep green with a waxy texture. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-like inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, typically in shades of red or orange. It thrives in humid, warm environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium subcaulescens is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its aesthetic appeal and air-purifying qualities.
