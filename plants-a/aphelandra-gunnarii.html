Aphelandra gunnarii

Aphelandra gunnarii, commonly known as the Solomon Islands torch ginger, is a striking tropical plant native to the Pacific Islands.

It belongs to the ginger family (Zingiberaceae) and is characterized by its vibrant, flame-like inflorescences that range from yellow to orange-red. Aphelandra gunnarii is typically propagated through rhizomes or stem cuttings, making it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
