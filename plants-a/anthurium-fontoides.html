Anthurium fontoides

Anthurium fontoides, commonly known as the velvet leaf anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically deep green with a velvety texture. The plant produces striking spadices, which are inflorescences that resemble a spadix, often in shades of red or orange. Anthurium fontoides thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity and well-draining soil.

It is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and air-purifying qualities.
