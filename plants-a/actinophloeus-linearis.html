Actinophloeus linearis

Actinophloeus linearis, commonly known as the slender leaf orchid, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its slender, elongated leaves. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
