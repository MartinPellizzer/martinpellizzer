Acrostichum ludens

Acrostichum ludens, commonly known as the golden fern, is a species of fern native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the family Blechnaceae and is characterized by its fronds that are typically bright green with a metallic sheen. The golden fern is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional medicine by some indigenous cultures.
