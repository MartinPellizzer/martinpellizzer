Agrostis nemoralis

Agrostis nemoralis, commonly known as common bentgrass, is a perennial grass species native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodland floors and meadows. The plant has slender, upright stems that are often covered in fine, soft hairs. Its small, spike-like inflorescence produces numerous tiny, wind-pollinated flowers.

Agrostis nemoralis is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in challenging soil conditions.
