Anthurium wedelianum subsp. viridispadix

Anthurium wedelianum subsp.

viridispadix, commonly known as the Emerald Spathe Anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America. It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture.

The plant produces large, showy spathes that range in color from green to red, depending on the variety. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.

Its unique foliage and vibrant spathes make it a popular choice for indoor gardening and ornamental purposes.
