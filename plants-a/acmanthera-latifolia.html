Acmanthera latifolia

Acmanthera latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf acmanthera, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa.

It belongs to the family Acanthaceae and is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or streamside habitats.
