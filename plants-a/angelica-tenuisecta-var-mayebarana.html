Angelica tenuisecta var. mayebarana

Angelica tenuisecta var. mayebarana, commonly known as the Mayebarana angelica, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of China.

It features tall, slender stems that can reach up to 2 meters in height, often branching near the top. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in complex umbels, which bloom in late summer. Its leaves are deeply lobed and have a strong, aromatic scent, typical of the Angelica genus.

This variety is valued for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional Chinese medicine.
