Aphyllon multiflorum

Aphyllon multiflorum, commonly known as the many-flowered aphyllon, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its small, clustered yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. Native to southern Africa, it thrives in dry, rocky habitats.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological role, it is often found in gardens and natural reserves where it contributes to local biodiversity.
