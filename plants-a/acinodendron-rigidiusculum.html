Acinodendron rigidiusculum

Acinodendron rigidiusculum, commonly known as the rigid dwarf acacia, is a small shrub native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is characterized by its leguminous properties and nitrogen-fixing ability. The plant features small, compound leaves and produces yellow flowers that are clustered in spikes. It is adapted to arid environments and has a woody stem with a rigid texture.
