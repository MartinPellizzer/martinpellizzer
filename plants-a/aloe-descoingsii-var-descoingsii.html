Aloe descoingsii var. descoingsii

Aloe descoingsii var. descoingsii, commonly known as the Desert Aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, with a waxy coating to reduce water loss. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing yellow flowers, which bloom in late summer. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its leaves and stems.

Due to its hardiness and ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
