Aster laetevirens

Aster laetevirens, commonly known as the cheerful green aster, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features showy purple to blue flowers that bloom in late summer to early fall. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Aster laetevirens thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or wetland habitats.
