Armeria vulgaris var. planifolia

Armeria vulgaris var. planifolia, commonly known as the flat-leaved thrift, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces clusters of small, pink or white flowers atop slender stems. The leaves are linear and arranged in a rosette, giving the plant a compact, mat-like appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

The plant is noted for its hardiness and ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
