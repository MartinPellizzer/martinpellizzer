Aulomyrcia amethystina

Aulomyrcia amethystina, commonly known as the amethystina myrtle, is a flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments within tropical rainforests. Its foliage is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.

Aulomyrcia amethystina is also valued for its ornamental appeal due to its glossy leaves and delicate blooms.
