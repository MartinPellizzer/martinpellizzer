Ardisia lineata

Ardisia lineata, commonly known as the glossy arrowroot or red arrowroot, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia and the Pacific.

It is a shrub that typically grows up to 3 meters tall, featuring glossy, dark green leaves that are elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, white or pinkish flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by red, fleshy berries that are edible. Its ornamental value comes from its attractive foliage and vibrant fruit, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Ardisia lineata thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, preferring humid environments.
