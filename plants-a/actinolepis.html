Actinolepis

Actinolepis, commonly known as the golden star lily, is a flowering plant belonging to the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its striking, star-shaped yellow flowers. The plant features strap-like, broad leaves that are typically green with a waxy texture. Actinolepis thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable climates.
