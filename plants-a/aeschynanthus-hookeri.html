Aeschynanthus hookeri

Aeschynanthus hookeri, commonly known as the rainbow wonder, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, orange, and yellow. The plant has dark green, glossy leaves and a compact, bushy growth habit. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Aeschynanthus hookeri is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
