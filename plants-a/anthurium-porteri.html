Anthurium porteri

Anthurium porteri, commonly known as the velvet leaf anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically deep green with a velvety texture. The plant produces striking spathes that can range in color from white to red, depending on the variety. It thrives in humid, warm environments with bright, indirect light.

Anthurium porteri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in greenhouse settings.
