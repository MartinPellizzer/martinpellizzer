Arctotis hirsuta

Arctotis hirsuta, commonly known as the hairy arctotis, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture. Its flowers are bright yellow with red centers, blooming in summer.

It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and rockeries.
