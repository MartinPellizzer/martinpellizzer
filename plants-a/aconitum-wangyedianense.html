Aconitum wangyedianense

Aconitum wangyedianense, commonly known as the Wangye Mountain aconite, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to purple spires of flowers. The plant features large, palmately lobed leaves that are typically hairy and serrated. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its toxicity, it should be handled with care.
