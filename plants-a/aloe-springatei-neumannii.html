Aloe springatei-neumannii

Aloe springatei-neumannii, commonly known as the Neumann's aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a waxy coating, which helps it retain water in arid environments. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow-orange flowers, often blooming in summer. Its leaves contain a gel-like substance that is sometimes used for its potential medicinal properties.

It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for xeriscaping.
