Afrormosia angolensis

Afrormosia angolensis, commonly known as African teak, is a tropical tree native to Central and Southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, heavy wood that is highly valued for its strength and durability. The plant has compound leaves with long, slender leaflets and produces small, yellowish-green flowers. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in lowland rainforests.

The tree is also known for its rapid growth and is used in both commercial and traditional applications.
