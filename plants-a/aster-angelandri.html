Aster angelandri

Aster angelandri, commonly known as the Angelandri aster, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies, sunflowers, and other similar plants. The plant is characterized by its showy, violet-blue flowers that bloom in the summer months. It typically grows in rocky or alpine habitats, preferring well-drained soils and full sun exposure.

Aster angelandri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
