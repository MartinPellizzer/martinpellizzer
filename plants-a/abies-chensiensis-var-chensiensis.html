Abies chensiensis var. chensiensis

Abies chensiensis var. chensiensis, commonly known as the Chinese fir, is a species of evergreen conifer native to China.

It is characterized by its conical shape, dark green needles, and distinctive cone structure. The tree typically grows in mountainous regions with cool, moist climates. Its bark is thick and scaly, providing protection against environmental stress.

This variety is valued for its ecological role and is often used in reforestation efforts.
