Aloysia aloysioides

Aloysia aloysioides, commonly known as silverleaf sage, is a perennial shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray leaves and showy, tubular flowers that range in color from white to purple. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for xeriscaping. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and aromatic foliage.

Aloysia aloysioides is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
