Argyreia maingayi

Argyreia maingayi, commonly known as the velvet tangle or sacred lotus of the Philippines, is a climbing vine native to Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped, velvety leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. The plant produces striking, showy flowers that range from white to pink, with a distinctive waxy texture. It thrives in tropical climates and requires well-drained soil and partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value and cultural significance, it is cultivated in gardens and used in various ceremonial practices.
