Abutilon giganteum var. detonsum

Abutilon giganteum var. detonsum, commonly known as the giant velvetleaf, is a tropical plant characterized by its large, velvety leaves that are typically heart-shaped or ovate.

It produces showy, lantern-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, often appearing in clusters. The plant is native to Central and South America and thrives in warm, humid climates. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking foliage and floral display.

The leaves are also used in traditional medicine in some regions for their purported health benefits.
