Acaena nivalis

Acaena nivalis, commonly known as the snowflax, is a perennial herb native to the alpine regions of South America.

It is characterized by its low-growing habit and produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in the summer. Acaena nivalis is adapted to cold, high-altitude conditions and is often found in rocky soils and mountainous areas.
