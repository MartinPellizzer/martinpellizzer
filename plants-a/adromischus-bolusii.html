Adromischus bolusii

Adromischus bolusii, commonly known as the Bolus's crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a reddish tinge, especially when exposed to sunlight. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white or pink during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves to survive drought conditions.

Adromischus bolusii is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance and attractive appearance.
