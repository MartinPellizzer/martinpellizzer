Asplenium × longmenense

Asplenium × longmenense, commonly known as the Longmen shield fern, is a hybrid fern species native to China.

It belongs to the family Aspleniaceae and is characterized by its compact, evergreen foliage. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often found in mountainous regions. Its fronds are leathery and have a distinctive, shield-like appearance, with a dense arrangement of small, lance-shaped leaves.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in horticultural settings.
