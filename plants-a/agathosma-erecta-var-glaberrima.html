Agathosma erecta var. glaberrima

Agathosma erecta var. glaberrima, commonly known as silver bush or Cape geranium, is a small shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its aromatic, glandular leaves that emit a strong, resinous scent. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rocky or coastal environments.

Due to its medicinal properties, it has been used traditionally in South African herbal medicine.
