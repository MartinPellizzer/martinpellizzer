Ananas sativus var. lucidus

Ananas sativus var. lucidus, commonly known as the lucid pineapple, is a rare variant of the pineapple plant characterized by its glossy, waxy leaves and a unique, more elongated fruit shape compared to the standard pineapple.

It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is native to certain tropical regions of South America. The plant produces a spiky, colorful inflorescence that gives way to the edible, segmented fruit.

Its botanical features make it a subject of interest for horticulturists and plant enthusiasts due to its distinct appearance and potential for cultivation.
