Acinodendron schwackei

Acinodendron schwackei, commonly known as the African velvet tamarind, is a small evergreen tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant produces pods containing edible seeds, which are used in traditional medicine and as a food source. Its botanical features include a dense, rounded crown and rough, fissured bark.

Acinodendron schwackei is well adapted to dry climates and is often found in savanna and woodland habitats.
