Athyrium dielsii

Athyrium dielsii, commonly known as the Chinese maidenhair fern, is a species of fern native to East Asia.

It belongs to the family Athyriaceae and is characterized by its delicate, lace-like fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as forests and rocky slopes. Its spores are produced on the underside of its fertile fronds, which are often curled or rolled.

Athyrium dielsii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic foliage.
