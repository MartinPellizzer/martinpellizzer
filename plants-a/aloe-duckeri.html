Aloe duckeri

Aloe duckeri, commonly known as the dwarf aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, with a waxy coating to reduce water loss. The plant produces small, white or yellowish flowers during the warmer months, usually in spring or early summer. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Aloe duckeri is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its compact size and attractive appearance.
