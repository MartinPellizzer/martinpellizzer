Asemeia Hondurana

Asemeia hondurana is a rare and fascinating plant native to the tropical regions of Honduras. It belongs to the Apocynaceae family, known for its diverse and often strikingly beautiful members. This plant is characterized by its unique foliage and delicate flowers, which bloom in vibrant shades of pink and white. Due to habitat loss and limited distribution, it is considered endangered and requires conservation efforts. Its study offers valuable insights into the biodiversity of Central American flora.
