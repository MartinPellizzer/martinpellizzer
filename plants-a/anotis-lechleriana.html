Anotis lechleriana

Anotis lechleriana, commonly known as the yellow lichen orchid, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its unique, lichen-like growth form. The plant typically grows on rocky surfaces or tree trunks, forming a dense, mat-like structure. Its flowers are small, yellowish-green, and often clustered in dense inflorescences.

Anotis lechleriana is known for its ability to thrive in harsh, arid environments.
