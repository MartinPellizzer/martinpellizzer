Aspidium africanum

Aspidium africanum, commonly known as the African water fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Athyriaceae.

It is native to various regions in Africa and thrives in moist, shaded environments. The plant has pinnate fronds with a delicate, feathery appearance, and its spores are produced on the underside of the leaves. Aspidium africanum is often found near water sources such as streams and ponds.

It plays a role in maintaining soil stability and providing habitat for small aquatic organisms.
