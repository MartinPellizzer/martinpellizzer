Artemisia dolosa

Artemisia dolosa, commonly known as the silverleaf tarragon, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, dense hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late summer. Artemisia dolosa is often used in traditional medicine for its aromatic and antiseptic properties.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy and drought-tolerant species.
