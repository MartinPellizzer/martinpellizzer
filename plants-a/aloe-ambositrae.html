Aloe ambositrae

Aloe ambositrae, commonly known as the Ambositrae aloe, is a succulent plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a waxy coating. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing yellowish-green flowers. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, preferring well-drained soil.

Aloe ambositrae is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional medicinal uses.
