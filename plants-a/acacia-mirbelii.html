Acacia mirbelii

Acacia mirbelii, commonly known as the Mirbel's acacia, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows up to 5 meters in height. The plant features bipinnate leaves with numerous small leaflets and produces yellow globular flower heads. Its flowers are followed by woody pods that contain flat, oblong seeds.

Acacia mirbelii is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a resilient species in its native habitat.
