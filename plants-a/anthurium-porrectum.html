Anthurium porrectum

Anthurium porrectum, commonly known as the arrowhead plant, is a tropical species native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, glossy dark green leaves that are often adorned with striking white veins. The plant produces vibrant red spadices, which are actually modified leaves that enclose the true flowers. Anthurium porrectum thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

It is a popular ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and air-purifying properties.
