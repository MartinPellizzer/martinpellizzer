Anthurium longistrorsum

Anthurium longistrorsum, commonly known as the long-spurred anthurium, is a striking tropical plant native to Central America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped leaves and distinctive spadix inflorescence. The plant's unique feature is its elongated, curling spadix, which gives it its common name. It thrives in humid, warm environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Anthurium longistrorsum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
