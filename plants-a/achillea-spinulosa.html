Achillea spinulosa

Achillea spinulosa, commonly known as the thistle fleabane, is a flowering plant native to Europe and western Asia.

It is characterized by its finely divided, gray-green leaves and clusters of small, yellow to orange flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is often found in meadows and along roadsides.
