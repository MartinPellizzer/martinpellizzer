Alternanthera articulata f. capitellata

Alternanthera articulata f. capitellata, commonly known as the compact Alternanthera, is a compact, bushy perennial plant native to South America.

It features striking, velvety foliage that ranges in color from deep red to purple, depending on light conditions. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or white, which appear in clusters at the tips of the stems. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full sunlight.

This cultivar is popular in water gardens and as a container plant due to its ornamental leaves and ease of maintenance.
