Atriplex × northusanum

Atriplex × northusanum, commonly known as the saltbush hybrid, is a shrub native to arid and semi-arid regions of North America.

It is characterized by its succulent, salt-tolerant leaves that help it thrive in saline soils. The plant produces small, greenish flowers arranged in clusters, which are typically wind-pollinated. Its robust root system allows it to survive in harsh, dry environments.

Atriplex × northusanum is often used in restoration projects due to its ecological resilience and ability to stabilize soil.
