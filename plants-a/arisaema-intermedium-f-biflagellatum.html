Arisaema intermedium f. biflagellatum

Arisaema intermedium f. biflagellatum, commonly known as the biflagellate arisaema, is a variant of the common arisaema species.

It is a herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia, particularly China. The plant features a distinctive spadix surrounded by a modified leaf called a spathe. Its botanical characteristics include a short rhizome and palmately lobed leaves.

The unique morphological traits of this form make it a subject of interest in botanical studies and horticulture.
