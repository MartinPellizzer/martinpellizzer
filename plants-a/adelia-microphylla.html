Adelia microphylla

Adelia microphylla, commonly known as the small-leaved adelia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family, which includes eucalyptus and tea trees. The plant is characterized by its small, narrow leaves and produces clusters of white or pale pink flowers. It typically grows in dry, sandy soils and is often found in coastal regions.

Adelia microphylla is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience to harsh environmental conditions.
