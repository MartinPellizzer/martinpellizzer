Anthurium teimosoanum

Anthurium teimosoanum, commonly known as the velvet leaf anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a velvety texture. The plant produces striking spadices, which are inflorescences that resemble a spike and are often brightly colored. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Anthurium teimosoanum is popular as a ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and air-purifying properties.
