Arenaria polygonoides var. occulta

Arenaria polygonoides var. occulta, commonly known as the hidden sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to arctic and alpine regions.

It features small, clustered flowers that bloom in shades of white and pink, typically in late spring to early summer. The plant has a mat-forming growth habit with hairy stems and leaves that are often covered in fine, white pubescence. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is adapted to cold, harsh environments.

This variety is distinguished by its concealed or less prominent floral structures compared to other Arenaria species.
