Argorips

Argorips, commonly known as the silverleaf vine, is a climbing plant native to tropical regions.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a silvery underside, giving it its distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. It thrives in humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Argorips is often used in ornamental gardening for its aesthetic appeal and can also be grown as a houseplant.
