Alchornea Rugosa

Alchornea rugosa is a unique shrub native to tropical regions of the Americas. It is known for its distinctive waxy leaves that have a rough, textured surface. This plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in humid forests. Its foliage is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits. Alchornea rugosa remains a fascinating subject for botanists and herbalists alike.
