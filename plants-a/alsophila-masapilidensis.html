Alsophila masapilidensis

Alsophila masapilidensis, commonly known as the Masapilidensis tree fern, is a species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate fronds. The plant exhibits a unique growth pattern, with a robust rhizome system that supports its vertical development. Its fronds are typically dark green and can reach lengths of up to several meters.
