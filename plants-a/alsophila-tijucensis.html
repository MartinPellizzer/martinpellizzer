Alsophila tijucensis

Alsophila tijucensis, commonly known as the Tijucense tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to Brazil.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in tropical and subtropical forests. Its rhizome is robust, allowing it to anchor firmly in the soil while supporting its tall structure.

Alsophila tijucensis plays a role in forest ecosystems by providing habitat and contributing to biodiversity.
