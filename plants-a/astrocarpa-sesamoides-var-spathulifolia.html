Astrocarpa sesamoides var. spathulifolia

Astrocarpa sesamoides var. spathulifolia, commonly known as the broad-leafed astrocarpa, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features lanceolate to broadly ovate leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. Its flowers are typically yellow to orange, arranged in dense clusters, and bloom during the warmer months.

It is often found in moist, well-drained soils in open habitats such as grasslands and forest edges.
