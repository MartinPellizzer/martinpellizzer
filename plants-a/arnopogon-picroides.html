Arnopogon picroides

Arnopogon picroides, commonly known as the narrow-leafed dandelion, is a herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and opposite leaf arrangement. The plant typically grows in moist, disturbed soils and is often found in meadows and along riverbanks. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a distinctive basal rosette formation.

The flowers are yellow and produce small, wind-dispersed seeds that contribute to its widespread distribution.
