Abutilon scabridum

Abutilon scabridum, commonly known as the rough abutilon, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its hairy, rough-textured stems and broad, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, lantern-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Abutilon scabridum is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and blooms.
