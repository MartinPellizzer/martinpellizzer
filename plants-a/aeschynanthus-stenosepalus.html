Aeschynanthus stenosepalus

Aeschynanthus stenosepalus, commonly known as the narrow-sepal begonia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that are typically red or orange in color. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a glossy surface and a faintly hairy texture. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Aeschynanthus stenosepalus is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant blooms and compact growth habit.
