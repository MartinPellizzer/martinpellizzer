Astragalus arnacantha var. kutepovii

Astragalus arnacantha var. kutepovii, commonly known as the Kutepov's milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to central Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions.

It has been used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits, though further scientific research is needed to validate these uses.
