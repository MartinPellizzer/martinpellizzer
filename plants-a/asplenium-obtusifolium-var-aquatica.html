Asplenium obtusifolium var. aquatica

Asplenium obtusifolium var. aquatica, commonly known as the water spleenwort, is a type of fern that thrives in moist, shaded environments.

It is characterized by its dark green, glossy fronds that are broad and lance-shaped. This variety is typically found growing on damp rock surfaces or in shaded, moist soil. The plant has a compact growth habit and produces spores on the undersides of its leaves.

It is a hardy species often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in challenging conditions.
