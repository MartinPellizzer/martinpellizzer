Apocynum oliganthum

Apocynum oliganthum, commonly known as the Chinese dogwood or small-flowered dogwood, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the family Apocynaceae, which includes many milkweeds and related species. The plant is characterized by its small, white or pinkish flowers that bloom in clusters, typically in the spring. Its leaves are ovate and have a slightly rough texture, while its stems are often hairy.

Apocynum oliganthum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
