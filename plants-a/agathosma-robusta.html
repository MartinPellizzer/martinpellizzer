Agathosma robusta

Agathosma robusta, commonly known as wild fennel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, fern-like foliage and yellow flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its essential oils, derived from the leaves and stems, are valued for their medicinal and aromatic properties.

Agathosma robusta is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
