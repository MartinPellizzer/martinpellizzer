Aceratium megalospermum var. coriaceum

Aceratium megalospermum var. coriaceum, commonly known as the rough-seeded aceratium, is a rare and endemic plant species native to specific regions of southern Africa.

The plant typically thrives in arid or semi-arid environments, where it adapts to low moisture conditions through deep root systems and reduced leaf surface area.
