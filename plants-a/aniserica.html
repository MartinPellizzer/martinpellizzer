Aniserica

Aniserica, commonly known as star anise, is a deciduous shrub native to China and Vietnam.

It belongs to the family Schisandraceae and is characterized by its star-shaped, dark brown fruit that contains aromatic seeds. The plant produces small, green flowers that develop into the distinctive capsule-like fruits. The seeds are widely used in cooking and traditional medicine for their licorice-like flavor and medicinal properties.

It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its essential oils and spice.
