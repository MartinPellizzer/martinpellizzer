Allium schoenoprasum var. schoenoprasoides

Allium schoenoprasum var. schoenoprasoides, commonly known as the Siberian chive, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central Asia.

It features slender, grass-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color and emit a mild onion-like fragrance. The plant produces small, clustered white or pale purple flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and mild culinary uses in various cuisines.
