Anthurium angustilaminatum var. brevipes

Anthurium angustilaminatum var. brevipes, commonly known as the narrow-leaf anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its elongated, narrow leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy surface. The plant produces striking spadices, which are often red or orange, surrounded by a colorful spathe. It thrives in humid, warm environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.

Anthurium angustilaminatum var. brevipes is popular as a decorative plant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
