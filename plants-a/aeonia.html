Aeonia

Aeonia, commonly known as the "rock rose," is a genus of flowering plants in the family Scrophulariaceae.

These plants are native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere, including Europe, Asia, and North America. Many species are cultivated for their ornamental value and ability to tolerate drought and poor soil conditions.
