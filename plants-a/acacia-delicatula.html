Acacia delicatula

Acacia delicatula, commonly known as the slender wattle, is a small shrub native to Australia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its narrow, feathery leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers in spherical or cylindrical clusters, which are typical of the acacia genus. Acacia delicatula is often found in arid and semi-arid regions, where it adapts well to harsh environmental conditions.

Its seeds are enclosed in woody pods and can remain dormant in the soil for extended periods.
