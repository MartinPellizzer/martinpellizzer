Alternanthera longipes

Alternanthera longipes, commonly known as the red-stemmed alternanthera, is a striking aquatic plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red stems and lance-shaped leaves that can range from green to red, depending on light conditions. This plant thrives in shallow, slow-moving waters and is often used in aquascaping for its aesthetic appeal. It prefers moderate to high light and can grow both submerged and as a floating plant.

Its rapid growth rate makes it a popular choice for gardeners and aquarists alike.
