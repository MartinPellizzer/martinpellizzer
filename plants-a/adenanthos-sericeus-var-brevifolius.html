Adenanthos sericeus var. brevifolius

Adenanthos sericeus var. brevifolius, commonly known as the silver mulla mulla, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its narrow, silver-blue leaves that are covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, fragrant white or pink flowers during the spring and summer seasons. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and inland regions of southern Australia.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
