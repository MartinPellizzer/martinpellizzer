Aristida endomelas

Aristida endomelas, commonly known as the slender wiregrass, is a perennial grass native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It is drought-tolerant and can thrive in poor soil conditions.

Its botanical characteristics make it a hardy and adaptable species within its native range.
