Astroloba × skinneri

Astroloba × skinneri, commonly known as the Skinner's moonvase, is a hybrid succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular, star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of yellow and orange. The plant features thick, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a fine, powdery coating, which helps reduce water loss. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is favored by both novice and experienced gardeners.
