Aneilema trichocoleum

Aneilema trichocoleum, commonly known as the velvetleaf aneilema, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its broad, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine, short hairs, giving it a velvety texture. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom during the wet season. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Aneilema trichocoleum is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in some African cultures.
