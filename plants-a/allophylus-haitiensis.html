Allophylus haitiensis

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its smooth, gray bark and simple, opposite leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It is typically found in moist, well-drained soils in forested areas.
