Astragalus yunnanensis var. glabricalyx

Astragalus yunnanensis var. glabricalyx, commonly known as Yunnan milkvetch, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its slender, branching stems and small, yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or mountainous regions, thriving in well-drained soils. Its leaves are trifoliate, and it produces pods that contain several seeds.

This variety is noted for its hairy stems and leaves, which distinguish it from other Astragalus species.
