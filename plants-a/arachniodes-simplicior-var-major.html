Arachniodes simplicior var. major

Arachniodes simplicior var. major, commonly known as the giant bamboo fern, is a striking evergreen fern native to Southeast Asia.

It features large, arching fronds that can grow up to several meters in length, with a distinctive, bamboo-like structure. The plant has compound leaves with serrated margins and a pale green to bluish-green coloration. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, moist soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often used in tropical gardens and indoor spaces.
