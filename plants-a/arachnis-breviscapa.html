Arachnis breviscapa

Arachnis breviscapa, commonly known as the short-stemmed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, clustered flowers that are typically pale yellow or greenish-white. The plant prefers shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories. Its roots are adapted to absorb nutrients from the surrounding soil and decaying organic matter.

Arachnis breviscapa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
