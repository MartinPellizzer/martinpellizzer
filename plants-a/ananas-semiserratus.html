Ananas semiserratus

Ananas semiserratus, commonly known as the serrated pineapple, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its rosette of sword-shaped leaves. The plant produces a striking, spiky inflorescence that bears small, red flowers. While it is not typically cultivated for its fruit, it is valued for its ornamental qualities.

Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.
