Astragalus terlinguensis

Astragalus terlinguensis, commonly known as the Chinese milkvetch, is a perennial legume native to China.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellowish flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant produces cylindrical, woody pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Astragalus terlinguensis is valued for its medicinal properties in traditional Chinese medicine.
