Aristolochia coadunata var. bosschai

Aristolochia coadunata var. bosschai, commonly known as the Cape mountain arrowhead, is a climbing vine native to South Africa.

It features heart-shaped, arrowhead-shaped leaves with a distinctive veining pattern. The plant produces striking, tubular flowers that are often purple or greenish in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forested areas.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its unique foliage.
