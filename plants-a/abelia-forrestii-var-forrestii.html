Abelia forrestii var. forrestii

Abelia forrestii var. forrestii, commonly known as the Chinese abelia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features slender, arching stems with opposite, ovate to elliptic leaves that are dark green and slightly hairy on the underside. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white to pinkish flowers in late summer, which are borne on short pedicels. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained, moist soil.

This ornamental plant is valued for its attractive foliage and showy blooms, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
