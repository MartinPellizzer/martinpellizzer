Aster cymbalariae var. ionops

Aster cymbalariae var. ionops, commonly known as the yellow starwort, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of narrow, hairy leaves and produces clusters of small, yellow to orange flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, disturbed soils and is often found in meadows, roadsides, and along stream banks. It is an annual or biennial herb that blooms from late spring to early autumn.

The flowers attract various pollinators, including bees and butterflies, contributing to its ecological significance.
