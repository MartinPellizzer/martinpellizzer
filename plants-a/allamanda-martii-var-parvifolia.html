Allamanda martii var. parvifolia

Allamanda martii var. parvifolia, commonly known as the dwarf allamanda, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South America.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are oval-shaped and slightly hairy. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow, funnel-shaped flowers that bloom throughout the year. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

This variety is popular in ornamental gardening for its vibrant color and compact growth habit.
