Ardisia multilineata

Ardisia multilineata, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen or red-rooted ardisia, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, dark green elliptical leaves that are typically arranged in whorls at the base of the plant. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers that are followed by bright red, fleshy berries. Its roots are thick and fleshy, often used in traditional medicine.

Ardisia multilineata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in shaded garden settings.
