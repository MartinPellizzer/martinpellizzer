Artemisia kobstanica

Artemisia kobstanica, commonly known as Kobstan wormwood, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its aromatic, silver-green foliage and small, yellowish flowers that appear in clusters. The plant thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in alpine and subalpine habitats. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes many other well-known herbs and flowering plants.

Due to its medicinal properties, it has been used in traditional medicine for various health purposes.
