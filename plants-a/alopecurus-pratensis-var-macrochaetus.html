Alopecurus pratensis var. macrochaetus

Alopecurus pratensis var. macrochaetus, commonly known as the tall meadow foxtail, is a variant of the meadow foxtail grass.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa. The plant typically grows in moist grasslands, meadows, and along stream banks. Its tall, slender stems can reach up to 1 meter in height, and it produces distinctive, spike-like inflorescences.

The leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green color, and the plant is known for its ability to thrive in nutrient-rich soils.
