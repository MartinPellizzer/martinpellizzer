Arapatiella

Arapatiella, commonly known as the yellow-flowered arapatiella, is a member of the Apocynaceae family.

It is a shrub native to the Caribbean islands, particularly Cuba and Hispaniola. The plant is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves and clusters of small, yellowish flowers. Its fruit is a capsule that contains several seeds.
