Albuca prasina

Albuca prasina, commonly known as the Emerald Star, is a perennial bulbous plant native to southern Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically bright green, with a dense, tufted growth habit. The plant produces clusters of white or pale yellow flowers during the summer months, which are followed by small, spherical berries. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Albuca prasina is also valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
