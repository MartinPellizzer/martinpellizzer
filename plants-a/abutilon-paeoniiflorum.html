Abutilon paeoniiflorum

Abutilon paeoniiflorum, commonly known as the Chinese lantern plant, is a flowering shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its fuzzy, velvety leaves and showy, lantern-shaped bracts that come in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are often hidden beneath the colorful bracts. It thrives in warm, humid climates and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Abutilon paeoniiflorum is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its vibrant, long-lasting display.
