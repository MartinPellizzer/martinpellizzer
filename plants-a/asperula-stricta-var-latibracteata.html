Asperula stricta var. latibracteata

Asperula stricta var. latibracteata, commonly known as the broad-bract evergreen azalea, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy clusters of pink to red flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments and prefers acidic soil conditions. Its leaves are elliptical with a smooth texture, and the bracts are broader than those of the typical Asperula stricta.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and as a decorative plant in horticultural settings.
