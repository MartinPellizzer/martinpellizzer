Achras nigra

Achras nigra, commonly known as black palm, is a species of flowering plant in the family Arecaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its large, dark green leaves and produces small, fragrant white flowers that develop into black, fleshy fruits. The fruits are edible and have a sweet, tropical flavor, often used in local cuisines.

It is also valued for its ornamental appearance and is sometimes cultivated for its unique aesthetic appeal.
