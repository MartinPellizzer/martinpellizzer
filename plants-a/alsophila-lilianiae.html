Alsophila lilianiae

Alsophila lilianiae, commonly known as the Lilian's tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds. The plant has a slow growth rate and can reach heights of up to 10 meters in its natural habitat. Its rhizome is thick and fibrous, providing structural support and enabling the plant to thrive in moist, shaded environments.

The fronds are typically a deep green, with a leathery texture, and they contribute to the plant's distinctive appearance.
