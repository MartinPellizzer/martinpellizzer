Anthurium scherzerianum var. rotundispathaceum

Anthurium scherzerianum var. rotundispathaceum, commonly known as the red-veined anthurium, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves with deep green surfaces and prominent red veins. The plant produces vibrant, spathaceous flowers that are typically bright red or orange, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

This variety is popular in horticulture for its dramatic foliage and ease of care.
