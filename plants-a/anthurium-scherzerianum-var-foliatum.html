Anthurium scherzerianum var. foliatum

Anthurium scherzerianum var. foliatum, commonly known as the Velvet Leaf Anthurium, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its large, velvety, heart-shaped leaves that are dark green with a rich sheen. The plant produces striking spadices, which are typically red or orange, surrounded by a showy white spathe. It thrives in humid, warm environments with bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
