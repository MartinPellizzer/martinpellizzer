Alchemilla killipii

Alchemilla killipii, commonly known as Killip's lady's mantle, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its dense, silver-green foliage that often forms a mat-like structure. Alchemilla killipii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and unique floral display.
