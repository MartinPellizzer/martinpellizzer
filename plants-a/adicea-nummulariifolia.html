Adicea nummulariifolia

Adicea nummulariifolia, commonly known as the silver dollar plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, silver-green leaves that are oval-shaped and slightly textured. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
