Asplenium bradeanum

Asplenium bradeanum, commonly known as the Bradean shield fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its compact, shield-shaped fronds that are typically less than 10 centimeters in length. The plant thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found growing on rocks or tree trunks in the understory of tropical forests. Its leaves are usually dark green and have a leathery texture, with a distinctive pattern of veins that contribute to its ornamental appeal.

Asplenium bradeanum is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening and terrariums.
