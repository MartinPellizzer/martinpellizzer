Aster chingshuiensis

Aster chingshuiensis, commonly known as the Chingshui aster, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically produces small, blue to purple flowers arranged in a compact cluster. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
