Adesmia elongata

Adesmia elongata, commonly known as the slender legume, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is characterized by its distinctive pod fruits and nitrogen-fixing abilities. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, with compound leaves and small, clustered flowers. Its seeds are enclosed in long, slender pods that open to release the seeds.
