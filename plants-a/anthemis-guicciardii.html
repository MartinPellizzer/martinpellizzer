Anthemis guicciardii

Anthemis guicciardii, commonly known as the Guicciardii's fleabane, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that consist of ray and disk flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. It produces small, yellow to pale orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.
