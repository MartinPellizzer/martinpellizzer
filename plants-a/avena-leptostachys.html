Avena leptostachys

Avena leptostachys, commonly known as slender-stalked oat, is a perennial grass species native to South America.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Avena leptostachys is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
