Ahouai nitida

Ahouai nitida, commonly known as the slender bamboo, is a member of the grass family, Poaceae.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems that can reach heights of up to 10 meters. The plant has narrow, elongated leaves that are typically green and covered in a fine layer of wax. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in moist, well-drained soils.

Ahouai nitida is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping due to its graceful appearance.
