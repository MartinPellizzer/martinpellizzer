Astephania africana

Astephania africana, commonly known as the African starfish plant, is a succulent native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern, aiding in water storage. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange, which bloom during the warmer months. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to survive in arid conditions.

Astephania africana is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
