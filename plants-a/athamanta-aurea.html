Athamanta aurea

Athamanta aurea, commonly known as the golden bell heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and blueberries. The plant is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of small, bell-shaped yellow flowers. It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade.
