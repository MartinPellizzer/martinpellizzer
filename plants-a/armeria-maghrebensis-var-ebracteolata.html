Armeria maghrebensis var. ebracteolata

Armeria maghrebensis var. ebracteolata, commonly known as the Moroccan thrift, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit and clusters of small, white or pink flowers. The plant features narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

This variety is noted for its hardiness and ability to withstand harsh environmental conditions.
