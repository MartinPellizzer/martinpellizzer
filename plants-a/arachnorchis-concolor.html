Arachnorchis concolor

Arachnorchis concolor, commonly known as the greenhood orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Australia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its unique hood-shaped labellum. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests and understoreys. Its flowers are usually green or yellowish-green, with a distinctive pouch-like structure that aids in pollination.

Arachnorchis concolor is known for its delicate appearance and is often found in association with specific mycorrhizal fungi.
