Athanasia argentea

Athanasia argentea, commonly known as silver athanasia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Athanasia argentea is also valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
