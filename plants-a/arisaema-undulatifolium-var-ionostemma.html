Arisaema undulatifolium var. ionostemma

Arisaema undulatifolium var. ionostemma, commonly known as the wavy-leaf arisaema, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive inflorescence consisting of a spadix surrounded by a modified leaf called a spathe. The plant features wavy, heart-shaped leaves with a deep green color and a velvety texture. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or white in color.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique foliage structure.
