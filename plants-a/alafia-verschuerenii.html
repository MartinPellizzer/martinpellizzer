Alafia verschuerenii

Alafia verschuerenii, commonly known as the African violet, is a tropical plant native to East Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, thick leaves and showy, tubular flowers. Alafia verschuerenii is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
