Actinodaphne concinna

Actinodaphne concinna, commonly known as the small-leaf camphor tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae and is characterized by its aromatic, dark green leaves with a smooth, waxy surface.
