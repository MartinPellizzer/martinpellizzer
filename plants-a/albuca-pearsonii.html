Albuca pearsonii

Albuca pearsonii, commonly known as the pearson's albuca, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.
