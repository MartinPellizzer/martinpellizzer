Abrotanella submarginata

Abrotanella submarginata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved sandmat, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of North America.

Abrotanella submarginata is often found in disturbed areas and is considered a pioneer species in ecological succession.
