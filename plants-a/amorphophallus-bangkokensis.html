Amorphophallus bangkokensis

Amorphophallus bangkokensis, commonly known as the Bangkoksian arrowhead, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its unique inflorescence composed of a spadix and a spathe. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are large, arrowhead-shaped, and can reach up to 1 meter in length.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its striking foliage.
