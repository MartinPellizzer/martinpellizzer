Anthurium tolimense

Anthurium tolimense, commonly known as the Tolima anthurium, is a species native to the cloud forests of Colombia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped spathes that can range in color from red to orange, often with a contrasting green or yellow spathe. The plant produces small, clustered spadices that are typically green or yellow, housing the actual flowers. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Anthurium tolimense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in botanical gardens.
