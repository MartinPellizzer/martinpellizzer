Arthrostylidium haenkei

Arthrostylidium haenkei, commonly known as the rock orchid, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly found in regions of Argentina and Chile.

The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers with a distinctive spurred lip, which is a characteristic feature of many orchid species. Arthrostylidium haenkei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens for its unique appearance.
