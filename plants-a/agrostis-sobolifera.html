Agrostis sobolifera

Agrostis sobolifera, commonly known as the slender basket grass, is a perennial grass species native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows, wetlands, and grasslands. The plant has slender, tufted culms with narrow, grass-like leaves that are often hairy. Its inflorescence consists of a spike-like cluster of small, pale green to brown spikelets.

Agrostis sobolifera is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping and restoration projects.
