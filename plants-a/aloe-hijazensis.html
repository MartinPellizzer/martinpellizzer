Aloe hijazensis

Aloe hijazensis, commonly known as the Hijaz aloe, is a succulent plant native to the Arabian Peninsula.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a waxy coating. The plant produces tall, branched flower stalks that bear yellowish-green flowers. It thrives in arid environments and is well adapted to hot, dry climates.

Aloe hijazensis is often used in traditional medicine and as an ornamental plant.
