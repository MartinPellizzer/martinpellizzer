Adromischus mammillaris var. rubra

Adromischus mammillaris var. rubra, commonly known as the Ruby Glow or Red-veined Adromischus, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, rosette-shaped stems covered in small, rounded tubercles that are often tinged with a reddish hue. The leaves are fleshy and thick, typically green with prominent red veins, especially under direct sunlight. It produces delicate, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or purple during the growing season.

This plant is well-suited for rock gardens or as a container plant due to its compact size and ornamental appeal.
