Adesmia trijuga var. robustior

Adesmia trijuga var. robustior, commonly known as the robust adesmia, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers. The plant is typically found in tropical and subtropical regions, thriving in well-drained soils. The species is noted for its adaptability to various environmental conditions, making it a resilient plant in its native habitat.
