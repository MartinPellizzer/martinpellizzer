Anoplophytum stramineum

Anoplophytum stramineum, commonly known as the yellow sandmat, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of Central Asia.

The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences, often appearing in late summer. Anoplophytum stramineum is adapted to thrive in sandy soils and is often found in disturbed or dry habitats.
