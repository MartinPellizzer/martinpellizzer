Adenia lanceolata var. grandiflora

Adenia lanceolata var. grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered adenia, is a climbing shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a smooth texture and a distinctive, aromatic scent. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that are typically pink or red, attracting pollinators such as birds and insects. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
