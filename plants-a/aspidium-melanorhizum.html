Aspidium melanorhizum

Aspidium melanorhizum, commonly known as the black-root fern, is a species of polypodioid fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark, wiry rhizomes that give it its common name. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky crevices. Its fronds are usually pinnate, with a dark green coloration and a leathery texture.

Aspidium melanorhizum is valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
