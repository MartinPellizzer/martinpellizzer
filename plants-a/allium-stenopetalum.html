Allium stenopetalum

Allium stenopetalum, commonly known as the narrow-petaled garlic.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. The plant features a bulb composed of several fleshy, white, layered scales. Its leaves are grass-like, narrow, and often covered in a waxy coating.

The flowers are small, clustered in umbels, and typically have six petals that are narrow and pointed.
