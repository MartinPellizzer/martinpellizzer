Alsophila sinuata

Alsophila sinuata, commonly known as the Sinuous tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and a crown of feathery fronds that can reach several meters in length. The plant has a distinctive, sinuous or wavy outline, which gives it its common name. It thrives in humid, tropical environments and is often found in forest understories.

Alsophila sinuata plays an important ecological role by providing habitat and shelter for various forest organisms.
