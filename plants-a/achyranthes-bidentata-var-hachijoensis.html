Achyranthes bidentata var. hachijoensis

Achyranthes bidentata var. hachijoensis, commonly known as Hachijo sandmat, is a variant of the horse hoof plant native to Japan.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant characterized by its upright stems and palmately lobed leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in clusters, followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Its botanical features make it a notable species within the Polygonaceae family.
