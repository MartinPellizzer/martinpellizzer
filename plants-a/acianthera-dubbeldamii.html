Acianthera dubbeldamii

Acianthera dubbeldamii, commonly known as the Dubbeldam's orchid, is a species of orchid native to South America, particularly Brazil.

The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, allowing it to thrive in humid, shaded environments. Its roots are fibrous and often grow along tree trunks or in rocky crevices, adapting to its epiphytic lifestyle.

Acianthera dubbeldamii is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and unique floral structure.
