Alstroemeria crispata

Alstroemeria crispata, commonly known as the zigzag alstroemeria, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that come in shades of red, yellow, and orange. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and a creeping rhizome, allowing it to spread along the ground. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its ornamental value is further enhanced by its long-lasting blooms, which can persist for several weeks.
