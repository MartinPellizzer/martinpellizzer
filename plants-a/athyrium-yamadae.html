Athyrium yamadae

Athyrium yamadae, commonly known as the Yamada's sword fern, is a species of fern native to Japan.

It belongs to the family Athyriaceae and is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its spores are produced on the underside of the fronds in clusters called sori.

Athyrium yamadae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
