Acer Negundo F. Violaceum

Purple maple, scientifically known as Acer negundo f. violaceum, is a striking variation of the green ash tree that captivates with its vibrant purple foliage. This unique cultivar exhibits a range of colors from deep purples to soft blues, making it a popular choice for ornamental landscapes. Native to eastern North America, it thrives in moist soils and partial shade, adapting well to various climates. Its striking appearance has made it a favored plant for parks, gardens, and urban settings. Despite its beauty, it requires regular care to maintain its color and health.
