Achnatherum pinetorum

Achnatherum pinetorum, commonly known as needlegrass, is a perennial grass species native to North America.

It typically grows in open woodlands and pine forests, preferring well-drained soils. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces slender, spike-like inflorescences. It is often found in association with other grasses and forbs in mixed vegetation.

Achnatherum pinetorum plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various wildlife species.
