Agathosma pubigera

Agathosma pubigera, commonly known as the Cape daisy, is a succulent shrub native to South Africa.

It features small, daisy-like white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has a compact growth habit with silvery-green leaves covered in fine hairs. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Agathosma pubigera is often used in rock gardens and as a decorative plant due to its ornamental value.
