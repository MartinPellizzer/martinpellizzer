Agave cantala var. acuispina

Agave cantala var. acuispina, commonly known as the narrow-leaved agave, is a succulent plant native to Mexico.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically narrow and pointed, with a bluish-green hue. The plant produces a tall, branched inflorescence bearing yellowish-green flowers. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
