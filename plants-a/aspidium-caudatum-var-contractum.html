Aspidium caudatum var. contractum

Aspidium caudatum var. contractum, commonly known as the contracted water fern, is a variant of the water fern species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, closely spaced fronds that form a dense, bushy appearance. The plant typically grows in wet, shaded environments such as marshes and along stream banks. Its fronds are pinnate, with small, oval-shaped pinnae that are arranged in a regular pattern.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in aquatic gardens and water features.
