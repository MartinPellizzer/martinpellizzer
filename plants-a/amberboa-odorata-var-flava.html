Amberboa odorata var. flava

Amberboa odorata var. flava, commonly known as the yellow amberboa, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and thrives in well-drained soils. It is often used in rock gardens and as a border plant due to its ornamental value.

The species is noted for its resilience to drought and mild frost.
