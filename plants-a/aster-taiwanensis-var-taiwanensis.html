Aster taiwanensis var. taiwanensis

Aster taiwanensis var. taiwanensis, commonly known as the Taiwan aster, is a flowering plant native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as mountain forests and stream sides. Its flowers are usually blue to purple in color, with ray florets surrounding a central disk of tubular florets.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
