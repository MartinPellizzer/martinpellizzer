Alsine verna lusus strictiformis

Alsine verna lusus strictiformis, commonly known as the slender alsike, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Amaranthaceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It is often found in moist, disturbed soils in temperate regions.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in challenging environments.
