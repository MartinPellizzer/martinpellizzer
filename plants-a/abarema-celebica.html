Abarema celebica

Abarema celebica, commonly known as the Celebes acacia, is a flowering plant native to Indonesia.

The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in open, sunny habitats. It is valued for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
