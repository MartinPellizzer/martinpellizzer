Aloe compressa var. paucituberculata

Aloe compressa var. paucituberculata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features narrow, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, with a textured surface. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in summer. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for rock gardens and container cultivation.
