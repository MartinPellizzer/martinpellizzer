Andromeda calyculata var. linearis

Andromeda calyculata var. linearis, commonly known as the slender andromeda, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as mountain forests. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of small, white flowers with a distinctive reddish calyx. Its botanical characteristics include a woody stem and opposite leaf arrangement.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens with suitable conditions.
