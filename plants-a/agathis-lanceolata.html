Agathis lanceolata

Agathis lanceolata, commonly known as the lanceleaf agar.

It is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia, characterized by its elongated, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces large, fragrant flowers and is known for its valuable resinous sap. Its bark and wood have been used in traditional medicine and for crafting.

Agathis lanceolata is also valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance.
