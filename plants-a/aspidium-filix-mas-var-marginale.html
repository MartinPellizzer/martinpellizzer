Aspidium filix-mas var. marginale

Aspidium filix-mas var. marginale, commonly known as the marginal maidenhair fern, is a variant of the common maidenhair fern.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds that are typically light green with a distinct marginal stripe. The plant belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its fronds are bioluminescent in the dark, making it a unique and visually striking plant.
