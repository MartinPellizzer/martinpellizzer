Aureliana lucida

Aureliana lucida, commonly known as the radiant aurora, is a striking plant native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, waxy leaves that shimmer with a metallic sheen under sunlight. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale purple, which bloom in clusters during the rainy season. Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Aureliana lucida thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil.
