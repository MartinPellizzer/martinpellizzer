Agrimonia eupatoria var. rotundifolia

Agrimonia eupatoria var. rotundifolia, commonly known as round-leaf agrimony, is a perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features upright stems covered in fine hairs, with alternate, broadly ovate to round leaves that have serrated edges. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers in late summer, which are followed by red, fuzzy seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and sunny to partially shaded environments.

This variety is valued for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional herbal remedies.
