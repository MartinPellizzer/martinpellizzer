Ardisia capuronii

Ardisia capuronii, commonly known as the Philippine beautyberry, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 3 meters. The plant features opposite, elliptical leaves that are glossy and dark green in color. Its flowers are small, white, and clustered in dense inflorescences, followed by bright red, berry-like fruits.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and vibrant fruits.
