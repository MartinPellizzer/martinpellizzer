Adenosacme lawii

Adenosacme lawii, commonly known as the Philippine star anise.

It is a climbing shrub native to the Philippines, characterized by its aromatic, star-shaped fruits. The plant belongs to the family Lauraceae and is valued for its fragrant seeds used in traditional medicine and as a spice. Its leaves are evergreen and have a glossy appearance, while the flowers are small and white.

The fruit matures to a dark brown color and is often harvested for its essential oils and medicinal properties.
