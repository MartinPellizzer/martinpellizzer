Anaphalis sinica var. sinica

Anaphalis sinica var. sinica, commonly known as the silverleaf fleabane, is a flowering plant native to China.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces fluffy, white capitula resembling small clouds. The plant has silver-gray leaves covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss in arid environments. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or mountainous regions.

Its flowers attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies, contributing to ecological biodiversity.
