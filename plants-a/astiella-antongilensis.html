Astiella antongilensis

Astiella antongilensis, commonly known as the Madagascar orchid, is a rare and endemic species native to the island of Madagascar.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its unique floral structure and striking coloration. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in forest undergrowth.
