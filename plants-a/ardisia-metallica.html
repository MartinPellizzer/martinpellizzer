Ardisia metallica

Ardisia metallica, commonly known as the glossy privet, is a species of flowering plant in the family Ericaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and small, white flowers. The plant produces bright red berries that are attractive to birds, aiding in seed dispersal. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded to semi-shaded environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and landscapes around the world.
