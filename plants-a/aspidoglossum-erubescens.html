Aspidoglossum erubescens

Aspidoglossum erubescens, commonly known as the red-tipped aspidoglossum, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its strap-shaped, grass-like leaves. The plant produces striking clusters of tubular, red or orange flowers that bloom in summer. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant color and compact growth habit.
