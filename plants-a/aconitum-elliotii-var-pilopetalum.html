Aconitum elliotii var. pilopetalum

Aconitum elliotii var. pilopetalum, commonly known as the wooly petal aconite, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of China.

It features striking blue to purple flowers that bloom in late summer, typically from July to September. The plant has deeply lobed, dark green leaves with a distinctive hairy texture. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its dramatic floral display.
