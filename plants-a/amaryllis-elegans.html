Amaryllis elegans

Amaryllis elegans, commonly known as the elegant amaryllis, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, strap-like leaves and produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and containers. Its blooming period typically occurs in late spring to early summer, adding a vibrant touch to landscapes.

Amaryllis elegans is also relatively easy to care for, requiring minimal water and moderate temperatures for optimal growth.
