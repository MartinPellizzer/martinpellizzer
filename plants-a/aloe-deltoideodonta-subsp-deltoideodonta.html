Aloe deltoideodonta subsp. deltoideodonta

Aloe deltoideodonta subsp.

deltoideodonta, commonly known as the deltoid leaf aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa. It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, with a waxy coating to reduce water loss.

The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing yellow flowers, which attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their soothing properties.

This species is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its leaves and thriving in full sun.
