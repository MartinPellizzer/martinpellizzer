Anthemis haynaldii

Anthemis haynaldii, commonly known as the Haynald's fleabane, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that consist of both ray and disk florets. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is known for its yellow to orange flower color. It has narrow, hairy leaves and produces small, daisy-like blooms.

Anthemis haynaldii is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
