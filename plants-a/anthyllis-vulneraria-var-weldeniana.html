Anthyllis vulneraria var. weldeniana

Anthyllis vulneraria var. weldeniana, commonly known as the wounded healer or narrow-leaved catchfly, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, pink to purple flowers and narrow, linear leaves. This variety is native to parts of Europe and is often found in rocky or sandy soils. It has small, pod-like fruits that release seeds when mature.

The plant is known for its medicinal properties, traditionally used for its purported healing abilities.
