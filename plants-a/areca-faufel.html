Areca faufel

Areca faufel, commonly known as the red areca palm, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are followed by dark, round fruits containing a single seed. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to grow successfully.

The red areca palm is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
