Adenocarpus hispanicus subsp. gredensis

Adenocarpus hispanicus subsp.

gredensis, commonly known as the Gredense thornvetch, is a flowering plant native to the Iberian Peninsula. It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its legume fruits and nitrogen-fixing capabilities.

The plant typically grows in rocky or calcareous soils, thriving in Mediterranean climates. It features compound leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers that attract pollinators.
