Astragalus suidunensis

Astragalus suidunensis, commonly known as the Suidun milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions.

Astragalus suidunensis is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional Chinese medicine.
