Arisaema thunbergii var. heterophyllum

Arisaema thunbergii var. heterophyllum, commonly known as the Japanese umbrella plant, is a flowering plant native to Japan and parts of eastern Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, umbrella-like inflorescence composed of a spadix surrounded by a modified leaf called a spathe. The plant has heart-shaped, dark green leaves that are often hairy on the underside. It produces small, yellowish flowers that are typically hidden within the spathe.

This varietal form is known for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
