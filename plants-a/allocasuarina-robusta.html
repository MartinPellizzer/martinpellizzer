Allocasuarina robusta

Allocasuarina robusta, commonly known as the robust casuarina, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a small to medium-sized tree or shrub that typically grows in coastal and sandy soils. The plant is characterized by its unique needle-like leaves and small, woody cones that contain the seeds. It is well adapted to arid and saline environments, making it a hardy species.

The flowers are usually small and yellow, appearing in clusters, and attract various pollinators.
