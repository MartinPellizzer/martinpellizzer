Aldama Squarrosa

Aldama squarrosa is a unique flowering plant native to the arid regions of Mexico. It is characterized by its striking, showy blooms that range from white to pink in color. This plant thrives in rocky soils and is often found in desert environments. Its name comes from the Spanish word "alameda," meaning a grove or shady place. Despite its beauty, it remains a relatively obscure species in the botanical world.
