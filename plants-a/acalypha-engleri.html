Acalypha engleri

Acalypha engleri, commonly known as the velvetleaf or silverleaf acalypha, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia and Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves that range in color from silver to deep green. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically green or yellowish in color. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in rainforests.
