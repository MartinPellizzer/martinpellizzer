Asplenium setisectum

Asplenium setisectum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf rock fern, is a species of fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Aspleniaceae and is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as rocky crevices or forest floors. Its leaves are often finely divided, with a setaceous appearance, giving it a unique visual texture.

Asplenium setisectum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in horticultural settings.
