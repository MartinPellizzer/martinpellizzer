Astragalus chiukiangensis

Astragalus chiukiangensis, commonly known as Chinese milkvetch, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to China and is characterized by its upright stems and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are typically clustered in racemes. Astragalus chiukiangensis is known for its ability to thrive in arid and semi-arid environments, making it a resilient species.

It is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional Chinese medicine.
