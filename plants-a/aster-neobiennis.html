Aster neobiennis

Aster neobiennis, commonly known as the sand aster, is a flowering plant native to the western United States and parts of Mexico.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that consist of both ray and disk florets. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and thrives in arid or semi-arid environments. Its showy purple or pink flowers bloom in the summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Aster neobiennis is often found in open habitats like grasslands, deserts, and along riverbanks.
