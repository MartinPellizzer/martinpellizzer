Aloe pentagona var. spiralis

Aloe pentagona var. spiralis, commonly known as the spiral aloe, is a striking succulent native to South Africa.

It features a compact rosette of fleshy, blue-green leaves that spiral outward in a unique, almost helical pattern. The leaves are typically narrow and pointed, with a waxy surface that helps reduce water loss. It produces tall, branched inflorescences with clusters of yellow flowers during the summer months.

This plant is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
