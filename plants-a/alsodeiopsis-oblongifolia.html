Alsodeiopsis oblongifolia

Alsodeiopsis oblongifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf alsode, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features slender, upright stems that bear opposite, ovate to elliptic leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pale blue or purple, arranged in racemes. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and rocky habitats.

Its botanical characteristics make it a notable species in the Asteraceae family.
