Artemisia fedtschenkoana

Artemisia fedtschenkoana, commonly known as the Fedtschenko's wormwood, is a perennial shrub native to the high-altitude regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, silver-gray foliage. The plant produces small, yellowish flower heads that are typically clustered in dense, spherical shapes. It thrives in cold, dry climates and is often found in alpine and arctic environments.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes cultivated as an ornamental plant in gardens with well-drained soil.
