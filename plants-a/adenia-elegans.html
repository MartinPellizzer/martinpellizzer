Adenia elegans

Adenia elegans, commonly known as the elegant adenium, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, woody stems and striking, palmate leaves that are often hairy. The plant produces yellow flowers that are clustered in umbels, adding to its ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its unique appearance and drought tolerance, it is a popular choice for xeriscaping and rock gardens.
