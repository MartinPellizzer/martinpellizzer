Argyreia collinsiae

Argyreia collinsiae, commonly known as the pink angel's trumpets, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range from white to pink, often with a reddish hue at the base. The plant produces clusters of elongated, tubular flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and ovate with a waxy texture.

Argyreia collinsiae is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a decorative plant.
