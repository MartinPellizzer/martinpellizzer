Arenaria Patula Var. Robusta

Arenaria patula var. robusta is a low-growing flowering plant known for its dense, mat-like growth habit. It features small, white or pinkish flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer months. This variety is particularly hardy, thriving in a variety of soil types and tolerating harsh environmental conditions. It is often used in rock gardens and as a ground cover due to its resilience and aesthetic appeal. Its compact form and long blooming period make it a popular choice for both ornamental and ecological landscaping.
