Adiantum pulverulentum var. biserratum

Adiantum pulverulentum var. biserratum, commonly known as the serrated maidenhair fern, is a delicate and ornamental fern species native to Southeast Asia.

It features finely divided, dark green fronds with a serrated edge, giving it its common name. The plant thrives in shaded, moist environments and has a rhizomatous growth habit. Its fronds are typically bi- or tri-pinnate, with a feathery appearance that adds a refined touch to gardens or indoor spaces.

It is valued for its aesthetic appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
