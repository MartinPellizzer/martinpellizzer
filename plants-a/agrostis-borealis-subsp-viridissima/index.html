Agrostis Borealis Subsp. Viridissima

Agrostis borealis subsp. viridissima is a rare and resilient grass species found in cold climates. It thrives in nutrient-poor soils and is often seen in alpine and arctic regions. This subspecies is known for its dense, dark green foliage that can withstand harsh weather conditions. Its ecological importance lies in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for small animals. Despite its hardiness, it remains a subject of scientific interest due to its limited distribution.
