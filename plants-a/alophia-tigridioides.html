Alophia tigridioides

Alophia tigridioides, commonly known as the tiger lily, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the eastern United States.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from orange to yellow, often with a red or orange throat. The plant has strap-like, linear leaves that are typically a deep green. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Alophia tigridioides is known for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
