Agrostis leersii

Agrostis leersii, commonly known as slender wheatgrass, is a perennial grass species native to Europe, Asia, and Africa.

It typically grows in moist, temperate environments such as meadows and wetlands. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are usually less than 10 cm in length. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences with numerous tiny flowers.

Agrostis leersii is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil and support biodiversity.
