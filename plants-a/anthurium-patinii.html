Anthurium patinii

Anthurium patinii, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves and vibrant red spadix. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers surrounded by a colorful spathe, which is often the feature most appreciated by gardeners. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium patinii is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
