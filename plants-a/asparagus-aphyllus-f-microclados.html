Asparagus aphyllus f. microclados

Asparagus aphyllus f. microclados, commonly known as the miniature asparagus, is a unique variant of the asparagus plant characterized by its reduced foliage.

This form exhibits a compact growth habit with small, scale-like leaves that are densely packed along the stems. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It is adapted to thrive in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its distinctive appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
