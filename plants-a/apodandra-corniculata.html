Apodandra corniculata

Apodandra corniculata, commonly known as the horned orchid, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, horn-shaped inflorescence. The plant typically grows in tropical rainforests, thriving in humid and shaded environments. Its flowers are usually pale green or yellow, with a delicate, orchid-like appearance.

Apodandra corniculata is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
