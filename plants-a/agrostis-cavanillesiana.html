Agrostis cavanillesiana

Agrostis cavanillesiana, commonly known as the slender meadowgrass, is a perennial grass species native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows, grasslands, and rocky slopes. The plant has slender, grass-like leaves that are often tufted at the base, and it produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences. It is characterized by its fine texture and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.

Agrostis cavanillesiana is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
