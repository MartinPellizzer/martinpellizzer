Arisaema wallichianum f. propinquum

Arisaema wallichianum f. propinquum, commonly known as the narrowleaf arisaema, is a variant of the arrowhead plant native to the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its distinctive, arrow-shaped leaves and a spadix surrounded by a spathe. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its botanical features include a rhizomatous root system and showy, greenish-white inflorescences.

The foliage and floral structure make it a notable species in the Araceae family.
