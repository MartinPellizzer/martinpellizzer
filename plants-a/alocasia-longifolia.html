Alocasia longifolia

Alocasia longifolia, commonly known as the long-leaf alocasia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 3 feet in length. The plant has a thick, rhizomatous stem that helps it store water and nutrients. Its foliage is often a deep green with a glossy texture, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Alocasia longifolia thrives in humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
