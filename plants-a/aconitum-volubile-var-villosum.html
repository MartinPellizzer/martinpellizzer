Aconitum volubile var. villosum

Aconitum volubile var. villosum, commonly known as the hairy goat's beard, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, pendulous flowers that are typically blue to purple in color. The plant features a trailing growth habit, with hairy stems and large, palmately lobed leaves. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodlands and along stream banks.

Its toxic nature, due to the presence of potent alkaloids, makes it a plant to be handled with care.
