Ancipitia nelsonii

Ancipitia nelsonii, commonly known as the Nelson's antler plant, is a unique flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, antler-like inflorescences. The plant features lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of small, tubular flowers in shades of red and purple. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.
