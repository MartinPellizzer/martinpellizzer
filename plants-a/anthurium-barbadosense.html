Anthurium barbadosense

Anthurium barbadosense, commonly known as the Barbados anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the Caribbean island of Barbados.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-shaped inflorescences surrounded by colorful spatula-like bracts, which can range from red to orange. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Anthurium barbadosense is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and air-purifying properties.
