Alyssum stenophyllum

Alyssum stenophyllum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved alyssum, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant has small, narrow leaves that are typically covered in a fine layer of white hairs, giving it a silvery appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Alyssum stenophyllum is also valued for its ability to attract beneficial insects such as bees and butterflies.
