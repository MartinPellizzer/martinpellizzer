Astranthium

Astranthium, commonly known as the star-thorn, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers that often display vibrant colors, attracting various pollinators. The plants typically have hairy stems and leaves, and their flowers are arranged in dense, spherical clusters. Astranthium species are native to regions in Europe, Asia, and Africa, and they often grow in dry, rocky habitats.

These plants are valued for their ornamental appeal and are sometimes cultivated in gardens.
