Alchornea thozetiana

Alchornea thozetiana, commonly known as the African blackwood tree, is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub or tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its dark, almost black bark, which gives it its common name.
