Alepidea ciliaris var. moltenensis

Alepidea ciliaris var. moltenensis, commonly known as the Moltenes' goldenbush, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and yellowish-green foliage. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or pale yellow. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its ornamental appeal, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
