Acanthus polystachyus

Acanthus polystachyus, commonly known as the mountain acanthus, is a herbaceous perennial native to the Mediterranean region.

It features striking, spiny leaves with a velvety texture and large, showy inflorescences composed of numerous small, pale blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or limestone soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

It is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
