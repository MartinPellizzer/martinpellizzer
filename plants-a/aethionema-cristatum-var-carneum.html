Aethionema cristatum var. carneum

Aethionema cristatum var. carneum, commonly known as the pink rockcress, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, fleshy, blue to purple flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has a compact, mat-forming habit with hairy stems and leaves that are typically oval or lance-shaped. It thrives in well-drained, sandy or rocky soils and is often found in coastal areas.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to tolerate harsh environmental conditions.
