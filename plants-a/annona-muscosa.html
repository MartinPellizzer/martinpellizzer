Annona muscosa

Annona muscosa, commonly known as the velvet-apple or custard apple, is a tropical plant native to the Americas.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height. The plant produces large, fleshy, greenish-yellow fruits with a distinctive velvety texture. Its leaves are broad, ovate, and covered in a fine, soft pubescence.

The flowers are typically white or greenish-white, with a strong, sweet fragrance.
