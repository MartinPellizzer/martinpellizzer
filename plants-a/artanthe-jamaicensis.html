Artanthe jamaicensis

Artanthe jamaicensis, commonly known as Jamaican artanthe, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean, particularly Jamaica.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Artanthe jamaicensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
