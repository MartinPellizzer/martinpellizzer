Agrostis propinqua

Agrostis propinqua, commonly known as slender meadowgrass, is a perennial grass species native to temperate regions of Europe and Asia.

It typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows, wetlands, and grassy slopes. The plant has slender, tufted stems that can reach heights of up to 60 centimeters. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green color and a hairy underside.

The inflorescence consists of a spikelet cluster, producing small, wind-pollinated flowers.
