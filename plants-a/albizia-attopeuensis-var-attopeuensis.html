Albizia attopeuensis var. attopeuensis

Albizia attopeuensis var. attopeuensis, commonly known as the Attopeu Albizia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in Laos and Vietnam.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features bipinnate leaves, which are divided into small, feather-like segments, and produces fragrant, yellowish-white flowers arranged in racemose inflorescences. Its botanical significance lies in its role within local ecosystems and its potential uses in traditional medicine.

The species is also valued for its ornamental qualities and adaptability to various soil conditions.
