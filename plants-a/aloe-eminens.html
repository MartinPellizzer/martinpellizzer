Aloe eminens

Aloe eminens, commonly known as the tall aloe or Aloe ferox, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, sparsely branched stem and fleshy, sword-shaped leaves that are covered in a white powdery coating. The plant produces yellow-orange flowers in terminal panicles, which bloom during the summer months. Aloe eminens is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves and having a slow growth rate.

It is often used in traditional medicine and as a source of gel for skincare products.
