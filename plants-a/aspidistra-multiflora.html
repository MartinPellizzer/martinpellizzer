Aspidistra multiflora

Aspidistra multiflora, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen, is a species of flowering plant in the family Araceae.

It is native to China and is characterized by its dark green, glossy, strap-like leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in low-light conditions and is popular as a houseplant due to its hardiness and ability to tolerate a range of indoor environments.

Aspidistra multiflora is also valued for its air-purifying properties and its ability to thrive with minimal care.
