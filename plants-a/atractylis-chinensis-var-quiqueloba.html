Atractylis chinensis var. quiqueloba

Atractylis chinensis var. quiqueloba, commonly known as Chinese Atractylis, is a perennial herb native to China.

It features a robust rhizome system and produces tall, slender stems with hairy leaves that are often lobed or divided. The plant blooms with yellow flowers arranged in dense, spherical clusters called cymes. It is adapted to grow in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
