Anil angustifolia

Anil angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leafed anil, is a flowering plant native to parts of South America.

It belongs to the family Violaceae and is characterized by its slender, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, violet-colored flowers. Anil angustifolia is also valued for its ornamental appeal and has been studied for potential medicinal properties.
