Anemia californica

Anemia californica, commonly known as California poppy, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its vibrant orange to red flowers and delicate foliage. The plant is an annual herb that thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight. Its scientific name reflects its historical use in treating anemia, though this is not a primary botanical feature.

The California poppy is also known for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional herbal remedies.
