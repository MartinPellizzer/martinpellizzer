Arachnaria subarmata

Arachnaria subarmata, commonly known as the small-armed fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Blechnaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped fronds that are typically less than 30 cm in length. The plant has a creeping rhizome and produces small, clustered sporangia on the underside of its leaves. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in temperate rainforests.

Arachnaria subarmata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
