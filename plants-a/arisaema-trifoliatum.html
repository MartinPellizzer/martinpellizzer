Arisaema trifoliatum

Arisaema trifoliatum, commonly known as the three-leaved arrowhead, is a member of the Araceae family.

It is native to China and is characterized by its distinctive trifoliate leaves and spadix inflorescence. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream sides. Its foliage is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

The plant's unique morphology makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
