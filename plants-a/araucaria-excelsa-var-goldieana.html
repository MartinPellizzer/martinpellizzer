Araucaria excelsa var. goldieana

Araucaria excelsa var. goldieana, commonly known as the Goldie's Norfolk Island pine, is a variant of the Norfolk Island pine native to Norfolk Island.

It is a coniferous evergreen tree characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and needle-like leaves. The tree produces cones that are typically small and cylindrical, containing seeds that are dispersed by wind. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and full sunlight.

This variety is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its distinctive appearance and slow growth rate.
