Acacia dolichostachya

Acacia dolichostachya, commonly known as the long-stemmed acacia, is a shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, often branching stems and bipinnate leaves that are typically gray-green in color. The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in long, drooping inflorescences, which are a distinctive feature. It is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a resilient species in dry climates.

The plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
