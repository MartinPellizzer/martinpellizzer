Achnatherum richardsonii

Achnatherum richardsonii, commonly known as the Richardson's needlegrass, is a perennial grass species native to North America.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and sandy soils. It is adapted to arid environments and is often found in regions with low precipitation.

Achnatherum richardsonii plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various wildlife species.
