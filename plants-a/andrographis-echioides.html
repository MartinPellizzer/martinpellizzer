Andrographis echioides

Andrographis echioides, commonly known as the narrowleaf andrographis, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It belongs to the family Acanthaceae and is characterized by its slender, upright stems and opposite, lance-shaped leaves.
