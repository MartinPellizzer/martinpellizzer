Anthurium corallinum

Anthurium corallinum, commonly known as the coral anthurium, is a striking tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped, glossy green leaves that are often adorned with red or orange veins. The plant produces spadices, which are specialized inflorescences that consist of a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe. Anthurium corallinum thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

It is a popular ornamental plant due to its unique foliage and ability to purify indoor air.
