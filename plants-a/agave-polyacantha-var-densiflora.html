Agave polyacantha var. densiflora

Agave polyacantha var. densiflora, commonly known as the denseflower agave, is a succulent plant native to Mexico.

It features a rosette of thick, blue-gray leaves with prominent white teeth along the edges. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences that bear small, greenish-white flowers. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil with full sun exposure.

Its striking appearance makes it a popular choice for xeriscaping and rock gardens.
