Alsophila erubescens

Alsophila erubescens, commonly known as the red-stemmed tree fern, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk covered in a reddish-brown, fibrous sheath. The plant produces large, feather-like fronds that can grow up to several meters in length. Alsophila erubescens thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular ornamental plant in tropical gardens.
