Aechmea drakeana

Aechmea drakeana, commonly known as the Drake's bromeliad, is a flowering plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that form a central cup to collect water. The plant produces striking inflorescences with vibrant red or orange flowers that bloom in the spring. Its foliage is often adorned with silver or blue markings, adding to its ornamental value.

Aechmea drakeana is well-suited for indoor cultivation and requires bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.
