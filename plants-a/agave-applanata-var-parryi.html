Agave applanata var. parryi

Agave applanata var. parryi, commonly known as Parry's agave, is a species of flowering plant in the Asparagaceae family.

It is native to the arid regions of the southwestern United States and northern Mexico. The plant is well adapted to harsh, dry environments and is often used in xeriscaping due to its low water requirements.
