Amphorkis inermamphis

Amphorkis inermamphis, commonly known as the smooth-leaved amphorkis, is a unique member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its lanceolate, smooth leaves that are often slightly glossy in appearance. The plant produces small, pale green flowers with a distinctive, elongated spur. It is native to specific regions in Southeast Asia, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a delicate and specialized species within its ecosystem.
