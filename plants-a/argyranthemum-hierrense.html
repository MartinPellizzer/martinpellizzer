Argyranthemum hierrense

Argyranthemum hierrense, commonly known as the silverleaf gerbera, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking white or silver-colored petals and yellow centers. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its foliage is broad and slightly hairy, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Argyranthemum hierrense is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its vibrant blooms and adaptability to various growing conditions.
