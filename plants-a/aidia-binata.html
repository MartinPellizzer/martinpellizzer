Aidia binata

Aidia binata, commonly known as the silverleaf daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and bright yellow daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. It is a perennial shrub that can reach heights of up to 1 meter.

Aidia binata is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
