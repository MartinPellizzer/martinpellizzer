Arachniodes leuconeura

Arachniodes leuconeura, commonly known as the silver-veined bamboo or white-veined arachniodes, is a species of evergreen fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, arching fronds that are adorned with striking white veins against a dark green background. The plant thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical forests. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to form dense clumps, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

The unique foliage makes it a valuable addition to both indoor and outdoor plant collections.
