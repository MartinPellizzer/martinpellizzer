Arisaema japonicum var. angustifoliolatum

Arisaema japonicum var. angustifoliolatum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf arrowhead, is a herbaceous perennial native to East Asia.

It features a distinctive arrow-shaped leaf with narrow, lanceolate lobes, giving it its common name. The plant produces a spadix surrounded by a greenish-white spathe, typical of the Araceae family. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and ecological restoration projects.
