Anthemis tenuiloba var. ramifera

Anthemis tenuiloba var. ramifera, commonly known as the slender anthemis, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and medicinal properties. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. Its leaves are linear and aromatic, while its yellow flowers bloom in clusters.

It is valued for its potential use in traditional medicine and horticulture.
