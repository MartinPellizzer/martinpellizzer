Alchemilla amphisericea

Alchemilla amphisericea, commonly known as the silver leaf speedwell, is a perennial plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its low-growing habit and silvery-gray foliage. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. Its leaves are covered in fine, silky hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Alchemilla amphisericea is often found in rocky or gravelly soils and is adapted to dry, sunny environments.
