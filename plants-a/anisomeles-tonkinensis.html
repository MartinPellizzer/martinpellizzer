Anisomeles tonkinensis

Anisomeles tonkinensis, commonly known as the Vietnamese orchid, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are usually elliptical and arranged in a rosette pattern.

Anisomeles tonkinensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in horticultural settings.
