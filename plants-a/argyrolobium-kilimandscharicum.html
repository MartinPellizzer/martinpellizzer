Argyrolobium kilimandscharicum

Argyrolobium kilimandscharicum, commonly known as the Kilimanjaro silverlobed crotalaria, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of East Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is characterized by its legume fruits and nitrogen-fixing ability. The plant features distinctive silver-gray leaves with a hairy texture, and its flowers are typically yellow with a distinctive black or purple pattern.
