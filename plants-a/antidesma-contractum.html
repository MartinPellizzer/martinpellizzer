Antidesma contractum

Antidesma contractum, commonly known as the African snowberry, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a small to medium-sized shrub or tree that typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers followed by bright red berries that are attractive to birds. Its leaves are opposite, elliptical, and often have a slightly waxy texture.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
