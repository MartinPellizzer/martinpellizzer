Ascochilopsis myosurus

Ascochilopsis myosurus, commonly known as the hairy leaved orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its erect, hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine pubescence. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in a spike, which are typically borne on the upper part of the stem. It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia and is often found in moist, shaded environments.

Ascochilopsis myosurus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
