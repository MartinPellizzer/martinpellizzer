Anneslia reticulata

Anneslia reticulata, commonly known as the nettleleaf anneslia, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its delicate, blue to purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has finely divided, fern-like leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. It prefers well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.

Anneslia reticulata is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.
