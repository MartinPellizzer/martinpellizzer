Adenodolichos rhomboideus

Adenodolichos rhomboideus, commonly known as the broad-leafed milkvetch, is a perennial legume native to the Mediterranean region.

It features compound leaves with oval to rhomboidal leaflets, giving it its scientific name. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, rocky habitats.

Adenodolichos rhomboideus is valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it beneficial for soil enrichment.
