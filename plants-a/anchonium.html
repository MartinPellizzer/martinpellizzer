Anchonium

Anchonium, commonly known as the sea holly, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of the Mediterranean.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its spiky, blue or purple flower heads. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and thrives in saline and well-drained soils. Anchonium is often found growing along shores and in rocky habitats.

Its structure and adaptations make it well-suited for coastal environments.
