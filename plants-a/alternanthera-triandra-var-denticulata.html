Alternanthera triandra var. denticulata

Alternanthera triandra var. denticulata, commonly known as the denticulate Alternanthera, is a striking aquatic or semi-aquatic plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red and pink foliage, which intensifies under strong lighting conditions. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves with serrated edges, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in warm, tropical climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant in water gardens.

Its rapid growth rate makes it a popular choice for aquariums and pond landscapes.
