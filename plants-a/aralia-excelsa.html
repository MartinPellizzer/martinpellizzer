Aralia excelsa

Aralia excelsa, commonly known as the giant aralia, is a towering plant native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its massive, compound leaves that can reach several feet in length, showcasing a feathery, palm-like appearance. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in a spire-like cluster, which are followed by spherical, black berries. Aralia excelsa thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Its rapid growth and striking foliage make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in tropical climates.
