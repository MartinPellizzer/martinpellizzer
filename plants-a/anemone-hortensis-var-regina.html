Anemone hortensis var. regina

Anemone hortensis var. regina, commonly known as the Queen Anemone, is a perennial plant native to the eastern United States.

It features showy, single or double white or pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has finely divided, fern-like leaves that are hairy and gray-green in color. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers moist, well-drained soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and ability to naturalize in suitable conditions.
