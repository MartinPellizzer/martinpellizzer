Armeria pubigera

Armeria pubigera, commonly known as the silver tufted thrift, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces dense clusters of white or pale pink flowers. The plant features silver-gray foliage with fine, soft hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Armeria pubigera is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
