Arenaria trichophora var. angustifolia

Arenaria trichophora var. angustifolia, commonly known as narrowleaf sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to arctic and alpine regions.

It features small, clustered flowers that bloom in shades of white or pink, typically in early summer. The plant has thin, succulent leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, aiding in water retention. It thrives in well-drained, sandy or rocky soils and is often found in tundra or mountainous habitats.

Its botanical structure is adapted to withstand cold temperatures and strong winds.
