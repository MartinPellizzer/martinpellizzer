Abutilon periplocifolium var. caribaeum

Abutilon periplocifolium var. caribaeum, commonly known as the Caribaeum abutilon, is a shrub native to the Caribbean region.

It features heart-shaped, velvety leaves that are typically gray-green in color. The plant produces showy, lantern-shaped yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in coastal areas and disturbed soils.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardeners in tropical and subtropical regions.
