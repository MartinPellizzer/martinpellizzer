Ainsliaea spanocephala

Ainsliaea spanocephala, commonly known as the red-flowered ainsliaea, is a herbaceous perennial native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its striking red or pink flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and streambanks. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a velvety texture.

Ainsliaea spanocephala is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting local pollinators.
