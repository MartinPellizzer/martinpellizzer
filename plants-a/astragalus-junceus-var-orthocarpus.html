Astragalus junceus var. orthocarpus

Astragalus junceus var. orthocarpus, commonly known as the slender milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of North America.

It is a perennial herb with slender, branched stems and small, yellow to pale green flowers. The plant produces small, hairy pods that contain several seeds, typical of the Fabaceae family. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in disturbed or open habitats.

Its botanical features make it an important plant for ecological studies and potential medicinal research.
