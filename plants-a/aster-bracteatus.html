Aster bracteatus

Aster bracteatus, commonly known as the Chinese aster, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that range in color from white to purple. The plant typically grows in a compact, bushy form with hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes many other flowering plants with composite flower heads.

Aster bracteatus is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
