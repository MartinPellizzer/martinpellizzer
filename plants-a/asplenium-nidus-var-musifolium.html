Asplenium nidus var. musifolium

Asplenium nidus var. musifolium, commonly known as the basket fern, is a variant of the common shield fern.

It is characterized by its dense, overlapping fronds that form a compact, basket-like structure. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in tropical and subtropical regions. Its leaves are usually dark green and have a leathery texture.

This variant is prized in horticulture for its ornamental appearance and ability to thrive in indoor conditions.
