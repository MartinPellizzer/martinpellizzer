Avena caryophyllea var. aggregata

Avena caryophyllea var. aggregata, commonly known as the clustered oatgrass, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, grass-like leaves and produces small, white to pinkish flowers in clusters. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and is often found in rocky or disturbed soils. It has a fibrous root system and is adapted to thrive in dry, sunny environments.

Its botanical structure makes it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural study.
