Alsinanthemum

Alsinanthemum, commonly known as the common sandmat, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to regions of southern Europe and western Asia, often found in dry, sandy soils. The plant produces small, daisy-like flowers with yellow centers and white petals, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. Alsinanthemum is an annual plant, completing its life cycle within a single growing season.
