Aphelandra lateritia

Aphelandra lateritia, commonly known as the silverleaf torch plant, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves with a distinctive silvery underside. The plant produces striking, spathiflorous inflorescences with bright yellow flowers that resemble torches. It thrives in humid, warm environments and requires well-draining soil.

Aphelandra lateritia is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and showy blooms.
