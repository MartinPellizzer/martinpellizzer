Artemisia Camphorata Var. Pauciflora

Artemisia camphorata var. pauciflora is a lesser-known member of the sunflower family known for its aromatic properties and unique growth habits. This variant is characterized by its reduced flowering compared to its parent species, making it a distinctive subject of study in botanical research. Native to arid and semi-arid regions, it thrives in harsh environments where few plants can survive. Its essential oils have been explored for potential medicinal and commercial uses. Despite its limited distribution, it holds significant ecological value in its native habitats.
