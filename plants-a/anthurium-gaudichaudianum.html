Anthurium gaudichaudianum

Anthurium gaudichaudianum, commonly known as the giant anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length. The plant produces striking spadices, which are modified leaves that house the small, clustered flowers. It thrives in humid, warm environments with bright, indirect light.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor and greenhouse cultivation.
