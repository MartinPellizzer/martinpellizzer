Alsodeia martini

Alsodeia martini, commonly known as the pink alsike clover, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of western Asia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes known for their nitrogen-fixing abilities. Alsodeia martini is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in ecological restoration projects.
