Amalophyllon rubidum

Amalophyllon rubidum, commonly known as the crimson flame fern, is a rare and striking plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Andes.

It is characterized by its vibrant red foliage, which is due to the presence of unique pigments in its leaves. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and requires partial shade to flourish. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread slowly across rocky substrates.

Despite its beauty, Amalophyllon rubidum is highly sensitive to environmental changes, making it a challenging species to cultivate outside its natural habitat.
