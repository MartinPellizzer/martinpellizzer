Arisaema hatizyoense

Arisaema hatizyoense, commonly known as the Yunnan arrowhead, is a member of the Araceae family and is native to China.

It is characterized by its distinctive arrow-shaped leaves and a spadix surrounded by a modified leaf called a spathe. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as mountain forests. It produces small, greenish-white flowers that are followed by berries containing toxic seeds.

Due to its rarity and habitat specificity, it is considered an endangered species in its native range.
