Adiantum jarlegense

Adiantum jarlegense, commonly known as the Jarlegens' maidenhair fern, is a species of fern native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the family Adiantaceae and is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds that are typically bi- or tri-pinnate. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in mountainous regions with high humidity. Its leaves are usually dark green and have a distinctive, finely divided structure, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Adiantum jarlegense is valued for its aesthetic qualities and is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant or in botanical gardens.
