Anthurium patens

Anthurium patens, commonly known as the broad-leaved anthurium, is a tropical flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant produces striking spadices, which are the modified leaves that house the small, inconspicuous flowers. It thrives in humid, warm environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium patens is popular as a decorative plant due to its vibrant foliage and unique floral structure.
