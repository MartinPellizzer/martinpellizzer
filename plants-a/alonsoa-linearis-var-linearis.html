Alonsoa linearis var. linearis

Alonsoa linearis var. linearis, commonly known as the slender alonsoa, is a flowering plant native to the Andes region of South America.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in high-altitude grasslands and rocky slopes. The plant features slender, linear leaves and produces small, clustered inflorescences with showy, tubular flowers. Its flowers are often brightly colored, ranging from yellow to orange, and attract pollinators such as bees and hummingbirds.

Alonsoa linearis var. linearis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with suitable growing conditions.
