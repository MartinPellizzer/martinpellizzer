Ajax luteus

Ajax luteus, commonly known as the yellow ajax, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant produces bright yellow ray flowers surrounding a central disk of tubular flowers, making it visually striking. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, often growing in rocky or coastal areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
