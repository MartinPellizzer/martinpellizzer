Aeranthes tenella

Aeranthes tenella, commonly known as the slender spider orchid, is a small, herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, spider-like appearance. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as forest undergrowth or rocky areas. Its flowers are usually pale green or white, with intricate patterns that mimic the appearance of spiders.

Aeranthes tenella is prized for its unique morphology and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
