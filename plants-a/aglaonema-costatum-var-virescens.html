Aglaonema costatum var. virescens

Aglaonema costatum var. virescens, commonly known as the Emerald Queen, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features striking, broad, lance-shaped leaves with a vivid green color and prominent, waxy white veins. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and requires moderate watering to maintain its health. Its foliage is often used in interior landscaping due to its aesthetic appeal and air-purifying qualities.

Aglaonema costatum var. virescens is also valued for its resilience and adaptability to various indoor environments.
