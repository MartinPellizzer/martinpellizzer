Alchemilla filicaulis f. vestita

Alchemilla filicaulis f. vestita, commonly known as the velvetleaf lady's mantle, is a horticultural form of the lady's mantle plant.

It is characterized by its soft, velvety foliage that gives it a unique texture. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract beneficial insects.
