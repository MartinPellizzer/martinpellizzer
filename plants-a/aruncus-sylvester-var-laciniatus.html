Aruncus sylvester var. laciniatus

Aruncus sylvester var. laciniatus, commonly known as the cutleaf goat's beard, is a perennial plant native to eastern North America.

It features striking, feathery plumes of white flowers that appear in late spring to early summer. The plant has large, lobed, and deeply cut leaves that are often used in ornamental gardening. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland settings.

Its botanical characteristics make it a distinctive and showy addition to native plant gardens.
