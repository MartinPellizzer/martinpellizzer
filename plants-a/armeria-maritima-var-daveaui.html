Armeria maritima var. daveaui

Armeria maritima var. daveaui, commonly known as the coastal thrift, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of the United States.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically reaches heights of 10 to 20 centimeters. The plant features dense clusters of small, white or pink flowers that bloom in spring. Its leaves are narrow, grass-like, and often covered in a fine layer of white fuzz.

It thrives in sandy, well-drained soils and is adapted to salt spray and harsh coastal conditions.
