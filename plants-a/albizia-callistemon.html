Albizia callistemon

Albizia callistemon, commonly known as the red bottlebrush, is a shrub or small tree native to Australia.

It is characterized by its bright red, bottlebrush-like flowers that appear in clusters at the ends of its branches. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are often arranged in whorls around the stems. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscaping.

Albizia callistemon is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators such as birds and bees.
