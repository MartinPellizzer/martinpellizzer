Anthurium amplum

Anthurium amplum, commonly known as the giant anthurium, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 2 feet in length, with a deep green color and a velvety texture. The plant produces vibrant red spathes that enclose its yellow spadix, creating a dramatic floral display. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium amplum is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
