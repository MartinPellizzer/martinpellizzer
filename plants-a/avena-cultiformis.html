Avena cultiformis

Avena cultiformis, commonly known as the slender oat, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and compact, spike-like inflorescence. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. Its seeds are small and wind-dispersed, contributing to its ability to colonize new areas.

Avena cultiformis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
