Amaranthus spinosus var. purpurascens

Amaranthus spinosus var. purpurascens, commonly known as the purple thorn amaranth, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its spiny stems and lance-shaped leaves that are typically green with a reddish tinge. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from red to purple, adding visual interest to gardens. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a hardy choice for many climates.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional medicine in some cultures.
