Arischrada molucellae

Arischrada molucellae, commonly known as the Moluccan starflower, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the Molucca Islands in Indonesia.

It is characterized by its small, star-shaped flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pale pink. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves and grows in moist, shaded environments within tropical forests. It is known for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered endangered and requires conservation efforts to ensure its survival.
