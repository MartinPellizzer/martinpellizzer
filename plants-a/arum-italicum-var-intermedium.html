Arum italicum var. intermedium

Arum italicum var. intermedium, commonly known as the Italian arum or white arum, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to southern Europe.

It features striking, arrow-shaped leaves that are dark green with a glossy surface. The plant produces a spadix surrounded by a showy, white to pale pink spathe, which is typical of the Araceae family. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
