Arachniodes amabilis var. yakusimensis

Arachniodes amabilis var. yakusimensis, commonly known as the Yakusim fern, is a rare and striking member of the Dryopteridaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Japan, particularly Yakushima Island, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant is characterized by its large, elegant fronds that are often adorned with intricate, lace-like patterns. Its foliage is typically dark green with a silvery underside, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a valuable and somewhat elusive species in botanical collections.
