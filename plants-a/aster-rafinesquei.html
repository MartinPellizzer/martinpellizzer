Aster rafinesquei

Aster rafinesquei, commonly known as Rafinesque's aster, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the sunflower family (Asteraceae) and is characterized by its showy, purple to blue flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and woodlands. It produces both ray and disk flowers, which attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.

Rafinesque's aster is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting local wildlife.
