Acinos majoranifolius f. ozrensis

Acinos majoranifolius f. ozrensis, commonly known as the Ozren's thyme.

This plant is a variant of the common thyme, characterized by its compact growth habit and aromatic foliage. It is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in well-drained, sunny soils. The leaves are narrow and serrated, with a strong, pungent fragrance.

It is often used in traditional herbal medicine and as a culinary herb due to its essential oils and antioxidant properties.
