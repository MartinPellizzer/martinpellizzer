Amesia papillosa

Amesia papillosa, commonly known as the silky amesia, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the western United States.

It features soft, fuzzy foliage that gives it a distinctive, almost woolly appearance. The plant produces small, daisy-like white flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its delicate foliage and long-lasting blooms.
