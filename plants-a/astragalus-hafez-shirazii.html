Astragalus hafez-shirazii

Astragalus hafez-shirazii, commonly known as Hafez-Shirazii milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of Iran.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its small, yellowish flowers and compound leaves. The plant is adapted to dry climates, with deep roots that help it survive in poor soil conditions.

It plays a role in stabilizing soil and supporting local biodiversity in its native habitat.
