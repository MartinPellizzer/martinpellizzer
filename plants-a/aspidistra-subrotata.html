Aspidistra subrotata

Aspidistra subrotata, commonly known as the silver-veined aspidistra, is a tropical evergreen plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its dark green, lance-shaped leaves with distinctive silver veins. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, with a clumping growth habit. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, forming in a spadix enclosed by a leaf-like bract.

Aspidistra subrotata is well-suited for shady environments and is valued for its ornamental foliage in indoor and outdoor gardening.
