Annesorhiza wilmsii

Annesorhiza wilmsii, commonly known as the Wilmsii goldenrod, is a perennial herb native to the northeastern United States.

It is characterized by its bright yellow composite flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands and along stream banks.

Annesorhiza wilmsii plays an important role in supporting local pollinators and maintaining ecological balance in its habitat.
