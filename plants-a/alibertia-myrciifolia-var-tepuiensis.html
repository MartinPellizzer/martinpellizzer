Alibertia myrciifolia var. tepuiensis

Alibertia myrciifolia var. tepuiensis, commonly known as the tepui alibertia, is a perennial shrub native to the tepuis of southern Venezuela and northern Colombia.

The plant features compound leaves with serrated margins and small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in dense clusters.
