Amaranthus lividus proles oleraceus

Amaranthus lividus proles oleraceus, commonly known as purple amaranth, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and vibrant purple to red foliage, which becomes more pronounced as the plant matures. The plant produces small, inconspicuous green flowers that develop into three-angled, bead-like seeds. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a suitable candidate for both ornamental and edible gardening.

This species is also valued for its nutritional content, including high levels of protein and minerals.
