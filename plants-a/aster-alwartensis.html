Aster alwartensis

Aster alwartensis, commonly known as the Alwartensian aster, is a flowering plant native to specific regions in Eastern Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies, sunflowers, and other composite flowers. The plant is characterized by its showy, daisy-like blooms that typically range in color from white to purple. Its leaves are usually narrow and lance-shaped, arranged alternately along the stem.

Aster alwartensis thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in meadows and grassy areas.
