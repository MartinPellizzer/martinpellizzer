Aphelandra lasia

Aphelandra lasia, commonly known as the golden flame plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America.

It is characterized by its striking, elongated leaves that are often bright green with a metallic sheen. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant, white to yellowish flowers that bloom throughout the year. Aphelandra lasia thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and high humidity to flourish.

It is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its ornamental foliage and unique floral display.
