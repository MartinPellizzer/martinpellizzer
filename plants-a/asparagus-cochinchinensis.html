Asparagus cochinchinensis

Asparagus cochinchinensis, commonly known as Chinese asparagus fern, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its dense, feathery foliage and small white or greenish flowers. The plant produces small, round berries that turn red when mature, containing seeds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread easily in suitable conditions.
