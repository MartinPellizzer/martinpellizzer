Albuca minor

Albuca minor, commonly known as the small albuca, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a bulbous perennial that produces clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are often mistaken for grasses or sedges. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Albuca minor is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to grow in gardens.
