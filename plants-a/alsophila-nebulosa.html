Alsophila nebulosa

Alsophila nebulosa, commonly known as the cloud fern, is a species of tree fern native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and a crown of feathery, compound fronds that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in cloud forests. Its fronds are typically light green and can reach lengths of up to several meters.

Alsophila nebulosa plays an important role in its ecosystem by providing habitat and shelter for various forest organisms.
