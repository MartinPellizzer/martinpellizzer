Acianthera Rodrigoi

Acianthera rodrigoi is a rare and striking orchid native to the cloud forests of Brazil. This unique species is known for its vibrant purple and white flowers that bloom in the rainy season. It was named in honor of the Brazilian botanist Dr. Ronaldo Rodrigues. Due to habitat loss, it is considered endangered and requires conservation efforts. Its beauty and rarity make it a subject of interest for both scientists and plant enthusiasts.
