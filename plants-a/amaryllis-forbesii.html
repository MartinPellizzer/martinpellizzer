Amaryllis forbesii

Amaryllis forbesii, commonly known as the Forbes' amaryllis, is a species native to South Africa.

It is a bulbous plant that produces striking, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant typically blooms in summer and is characterized by its strap-like, evergreen leaves. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Amaryllis forbesii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
