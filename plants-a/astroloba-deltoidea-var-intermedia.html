Astroloba deltoidea var. intermedia

Astroloba deltoidea var. intermedia, commonly known as the Delta Starfish or Starfish Plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features distinctive, fleshy, paddle-shaped leaves that are often adorned with wavy edges and a blue-green hue. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange, typically during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for succulent collections.
