Aethionema speciosum

Aethionema speciosum, commonly known as the rockcress, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically reaches heights of about 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features basal leaves that are usually blue-gray in color, with a fuzzy texture. Its flowers are small, yellow, and borne in clusters, adding ornamental value to rock gardens and alpine settings.

Aethionema speciosum thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy choice for rockeries and borders.
