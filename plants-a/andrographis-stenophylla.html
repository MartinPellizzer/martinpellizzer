Andrographis stenophylla

Andrographis stenophylla, commonly known as narrow-leaf horseleaf, is a perennial herb native to southern Asia.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and opposite, ovate leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Andrographis stenophylla is noted for its medicinal properties, though it is not as widely used as other Andrographis species.
