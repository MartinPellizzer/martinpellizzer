Aulomyrcia paniculata

Aulomyrcia paniculata, commonly known as the Brazilian peppertree, is a flowering plant native to Brazil.

It is a small to medium-sized tree or shrub that typically grows up to 10 meters tall. The plant produces small, white or pinkish flowers arranged in panicles, which are visible during the flowering season. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and have a slightly serrated margin.

The fruit is a small, three-lobed capsule that contains several seeds.
