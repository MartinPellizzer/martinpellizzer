Asarum ruthii

Asarum ruthii, commonly known as the Himalayan wild ginger, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It belongs to the family Aristolochiaceae and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, fragrant flowers. Asarum ruthii thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories at high elevations.
