Antimima koekenaapensis

Antimima koekenaapensis, commonly known as the Koekenaap orchid, is a species of orchid native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stem and small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and prefers full sun to partial shade. It is a perennial herb that is often found in the Cape Floristic Region, a biodiversity hotspot.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
