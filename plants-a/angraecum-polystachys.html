Angraecum polystachys

Angraecum polystachys, commonly known as the Christmas star orchid, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers that bloom during the winter months. The plant produces long, pendant inflorescences with numerous small, white to pale blue flowers. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Angraecum polystachys is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
