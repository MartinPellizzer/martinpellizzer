Anthurium olfersianum var. leptostachyum

Anthurium olfersianum var. leptostachyum, commonly known as the narrow-stemmed anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically deep green with a glossy surface. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-shaped inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, often in shades of red or orange. It thrives in humid, warm environments with indirect light and requires well-draining soil.

This variety is popular among plant enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
