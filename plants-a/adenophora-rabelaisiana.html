Adenophora rabelaisiana

Adenophora rabelaisiana, commonly known as the Rabelaisian bellflower, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to China.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped, blue to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters and features narrow, lanceolate leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens or woodland settings.

Adenophora rabelaisiana is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for its delicate floral display.
