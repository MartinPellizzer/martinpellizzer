Acer Wilsonii Var. Kwangtungense

Kwangtung maple, scientifically known as Acer wilsonii var. kwangtungense, is a striking ornamental tree native to southern China. This species is celebrated for its vibrant fall foliage, which displays shades of red, orange, and yellow, making it a popular choice for landscaping. It thrives in temperate climates and prefers well-drained soil with partial to full sunlight. The tree's unique leaf shape and elegant structure add both aesthetic and ecological value to its surroundings. With its resilience and beauty, the Kwangtung maple remains a favorite among gardeners and botanists alike.
