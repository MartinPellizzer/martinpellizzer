Alsine tenuifolia var. arvatica

Alsine tenuifolia var. arvatica, commonly known as narrowleaf sandwort, is a low-growing annual plant native to North America.

It typically thrives in sandy or gravelly soils, often found in disturbed areas such as roadsides and fields. The plant has small, linear leaves and produces tiny white or pink flowers in clusters. It is characterized by its delicate appearance and ability to tolerate dry conditions.

Its botanical features make it a common sight in arid and semi-arid environments.
