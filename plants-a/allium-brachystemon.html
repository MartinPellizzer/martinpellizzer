Allium brachystemon

Allium brachystemon, commonly known as the narrow-leaved garlic.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe and western Asia. The plant features a bulbous stem with narrow, grass-like leaves. Its flowers are typically small and clustered in a spherical inflorescence.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and mild garlic-like fragrance.
