Agrostis biseta

Agrostis biseta, commonly known as the slender bentgrass, is a perennial grass species native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its fine, grass-like leaves and small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows, grasslands, and along stream banks. Its botanical features include a fibrous root system and small, oval-shaped leaves with a bluish-green hue.

Agrostis biseta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping and turfgrass applications.
