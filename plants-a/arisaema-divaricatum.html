Arisaema divaricatum

Arisaema divaricatum, commonly known as the Chinese arrowhead, is a member of the Araceae family.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant native to China, characterized by its distinctive arrow-shaped leaves. The plant produces a spadix inflorescence surrounded by a modified leaf called a spathe. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forested areas.

Its unique morphology and ornamental value make it a popular choice for botanical gardens and horticultural collections.
