Argostemma begoniaceum

Argostemma begoniaceum, commonly known as the velvetleaf begonia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Begoniaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and vibrant, showy flowers. The plant typically grows as a herbaceous perennial, often reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its foliage is often covered in a fine, velvety texture, giving it its common name.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is popular among gardeners for its ornamental value.
