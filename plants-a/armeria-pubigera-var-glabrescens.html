Armeria pubigera var. glabrescens

Armeria pubigera var. glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth sea thrift, is a low-growing evergreen flowering plant native to the coastal regions of Europe.

It features dense clusters of small, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has compact, silver-gray foliage that is covered in fine, soft hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained, sandy or gravelly soils and is tolerant of salt spray, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and ability to stabilize shoreline vegetation.
