Abies parlatorei

Abies parlatorei, commonly known as the Parlatore's fir, is a rare and endemic species native to the mountainous regions of southern China.

It is a coniferous evergreen tree characterized by its slender, conical shape and dark green, needle-like leaves. The tree produces cones that are typically small and cylindrical, containing seeds that are dispersed by wind. It thrives in cool, moist climates and is often found in high-altitude forests.

Due to its limited range and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native environment.
