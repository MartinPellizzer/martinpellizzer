Aulomyrcia albotomentosa

Aulomyrcia albotomentosa, commonly known as the white-tomentose myrcia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its small, white or pale green leaves covered in fine, silky hairs. It produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow.

Aulomyrcia albotomentosa is often found in humid, shaded environments within tropical rainforests.
