Agathosma schlechtendahliana

Agathosma schlechtendahliana, commonly known as the Cape citrus or African geranium, is a small, evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It features aromatic, narrow leaves with a distinctive citrus scent, which are often used in traditional medicine. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in spring, followed by orange to red fruits resembling small citrus fruits. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Due to its medicinal properties, it is also valued in aromatherapy and herbal remedies.
