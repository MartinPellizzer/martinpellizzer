Acouroa

Acouroa, commonly known as the golden bell tree, is a tropical plant native to parts of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, bell-shaped yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has compound leaves with elongated, narrow leaflets. Acouroa thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in lowland forests.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and ornamental landscaping.
