Aeonium manriqueorum

Aeonium manriqueorum, commonly known as the Manrique's Aeonium, is a succulent plant native to the Canary Islands.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with red or purple hues along the edges. The plant produces tall, branched stems with clusters of yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a container plant.

Its striking appearance and easy care make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
