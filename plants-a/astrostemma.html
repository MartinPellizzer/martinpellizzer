Astrostemma

Astrostemma, commonly known as the star-shaped flower or star vine, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Asclepiadaceae.

It is characterized by its unique, star-like flowers that range in color from white to pink and yellow. The plant typically grows as a climbing vine with opposite, heart-shaped leaves. Astrostemma species are native to regions of Africa, particularly South Africa, and thrive in warm, sunny environments.

They are often cultivated for their ornamental value and are popular in gardens and as houseplants.
