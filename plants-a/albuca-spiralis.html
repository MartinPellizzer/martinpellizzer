Albuca spiralis

Albuca spiralis, commonly known as the spiral albuca, is a bulbous plant native to southern Africa.

It features a cluster of narrow, grass-like leaves that spiral outward, creating a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white or pale yellow during the spring. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Albuca spiralis is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its unique foliage and delicate blooms.
