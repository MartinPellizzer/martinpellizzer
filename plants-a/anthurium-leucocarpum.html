Anthurium leucocarpum

Anthurium leucocarpum, commonly known as the white fruit anthurium, is a striking tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces vibrant red spathes that enclose its spadix, which bears small, inconspicuous flowers. Anthurium leucocarpum thrives in humid, warm environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and ornamental purposes.
