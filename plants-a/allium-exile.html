Allium exile

Allium exile, commonly known as the slender leek, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and small, clustered white or pale purple flowers. However, it is less commonly cultivated than other Allium species due to its specific growing requirements.
