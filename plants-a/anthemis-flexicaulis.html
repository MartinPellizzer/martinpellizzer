Anthemis flexicaulis

Anthemis flexicaulis, commonly known as the bentstem fleabane, is a flowering plant native to the western United States and parts of Canada.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. It produces small, yellow to orange flowers that bloom in the summer months.

Its hairy stems and leaves help it retain moisture in arid environments.
