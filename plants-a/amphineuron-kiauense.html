Amphineuron kiauense

Amphineuron kiauense, commonly known as the Kiuai orchid, is a rare and unique orchid species native to specific regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, often fragrant flowers that display a variety of colors. The plant typically features strap-like leaves and produces inflorescences with clustered blooms. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a valuable subject for botanical research and conservation efforts.
