Asteropeia multiflora

Asteropeia multiflora, commonly known as the many-flowered aster.

It is a flowering plant native to southern Africa, characterized by its clusters of small, star-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and is adapted to sandy soils. It produces numerous small flowers that bloom in shades of white and pink.

Asteropeia multiflora is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
