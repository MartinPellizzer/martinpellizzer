Aceratium archboldianum

Aceratium archboldianum, commonly known as the Archbold's aceratium, is a species of flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy yellow flowers. The plant produces small, oval leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to the mild, humid climate of its native habitat.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
