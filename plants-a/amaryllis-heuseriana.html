Amaryllis heuseriana

Amaryllis heuseriana, commonly known as the Cape Lily, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, often blooming in late summer to early autumn. The plant features strap-like, evergreen leaves that form a dense rosette, providing a lush backdrop for its vibrant blooms. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance make it a favored ornamental plant in many regions.
