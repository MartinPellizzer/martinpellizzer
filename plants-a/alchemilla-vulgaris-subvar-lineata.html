Alchemilla vulgaris subvar. lineata

Alchemilla vulgaris subvar. lineata, commonly known as the variegated lady's mantle, is a perennial plant native to Europe and western Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, shield-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. The plant produces clusters of small, greenish-yellow flowers in early summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Alchemilla vulgaris subvar. lineata is valued in gardens for its ornamental foliage and its ability to attract beneficial insects.
