Ardisia rivularis

Ardisia rivularis, commonly known as the stream ardisia, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, dark green elliptical leaves and produces small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is characterized by its bright red berries, which are edible but often used as ornamental features. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found near streams or in humid forests.

Ardisia rivularis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
