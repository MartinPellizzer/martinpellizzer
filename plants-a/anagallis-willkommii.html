Anagallis willkommii

Anagallis willkommii, commonly known as the willkomm's pink, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces delicate, five-petaled flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Anagallis willkommii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
