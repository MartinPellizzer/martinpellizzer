Asplenium stans

Asplenium stans, commonly known as the shield fern, is a small evergreen fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Aspleniaceae and is characterized by its compact, upright growth habit. The plant has lance-shaped, dark green fronds with a leathery texture and a distinctive shield-like appearance on the underside of its leaves. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in rocky or forested areas.

Asplenium stans is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated as a houseplant.
