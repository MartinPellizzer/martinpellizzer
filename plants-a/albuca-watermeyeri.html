Albuca watermeyeri

Albuca watermeyeri, commonly known as the Watermeyer's albuca, is a member of the Asparagaceae family and is native to southern Africa.

It is a bulbous perennial plant characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and clusters of pale yellow flowers. The plant produces small, rounded bulbs that are used for propagation. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate watering, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Albuca watermeyeri is appreciated for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation.
