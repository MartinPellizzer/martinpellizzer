Asplenium pechuelii

Asplenium pechuelii, commonly known as the Pechuel's spleenwort, is a small, evergreen fern belonging to the Aspleniaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. This plant is often found growing on rocks or tree trunks in moist, shaded environments. It has a compact growth habit and produces spore-bearing structures called sorocarps.

Asplenium pechuelii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
