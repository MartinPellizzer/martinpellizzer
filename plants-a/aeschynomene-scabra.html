Aeschynomene scabra

Aeschynomene scabra, commonly known as rough sicklegrass, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Americas. The plant is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, clustered leaves with a rough, hairy texture. Its flowers are typically pale yellow or greenish-yellow, arranged in small racemes.

Aeschynomene scabra is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in semi-arid environments.
