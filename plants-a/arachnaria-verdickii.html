Arachnaria verdickii

Arachnaria verdickii, commonly known as the silverleaf spiderplant, is a unique botanical species characterized by its striking foliage.

It features broad, waxy leaves that are typically a silvery-green color, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, white flowers in clusters, which are followed by spherical, berry-like fruits. Native to tropical regions, it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
