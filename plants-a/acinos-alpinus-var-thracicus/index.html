Acinos Alpinus Var. Thracicus

Alpine cinquefoil, scientifically known as Acinos alpinus var. thracicus, is a resilient flowering plant found in high-altitude regions. It thrives in rocky and mountainous environments where few other plants can survive. With its delicate yellow flowers and compact growth habit, it adds a touch of beauty to barren landscapes. This variant of the Alpine cinquefoil is particularly adapted to cold climates and poor soils. Its presence is a sign of ecological health in alpine ecosystems.
