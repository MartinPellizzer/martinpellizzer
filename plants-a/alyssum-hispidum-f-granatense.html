Alyssum hispidum f. granatense

Alyssum hispidum f. granatense, commonly known as the Granate Alyssum, is a compact, mat-forming perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features dense clusters of small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple, blooming throughout the summer. The plant has hairy, silver-green foliage that adds ornamental value to gardens. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its compact growth habit and long flowering period make it a popular choice for ornamental horticulture.
