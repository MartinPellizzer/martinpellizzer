Agauria callibotrys

Agauria callibotrys, commonly known as the golden rain tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its feathery, bipinnate leaves that give it a delicate, fern-like appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, creating a striking visual display. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in warm, humid climates.

Agauria callibotrys is also valued for its fast growth and adaptability to various soil types.
