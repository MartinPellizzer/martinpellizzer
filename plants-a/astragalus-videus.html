Astragalus videus

Astragalus videus, commonly known as yellow vetch, is a leguminous plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant is native to temperate regions of Europe, Asia, and North Africa. It has a deep root system that helps prevent soil erosion and improves soil fertility through nitrogen fixation.

Astragalus videus is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its adaptability and hardiness.
