Austrobryonia argillicola

Austrobryonia argillicola, commonly known as the clayland liverwort, is a small, mat-forming liverwort found in moist, shaded environments.

Austrobryonia argillicola plays a role in soil stabilization and provides habitat for microorganisms in its native range.
