Auricula-ursi kitaibeliana

Auricula-ursi kitaibeliana, commonly known as the alpine strawberry, is a low-growing perennial herb native to the high altitudes of the Alps and other mountainous regions.

It features rosettes of velvety, dark green leaves that are covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, pinkish-white flowers with a distinctive, sweet scent. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in rocky or alpine meadows.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is a popular subject in botanical studies and horticulture.
