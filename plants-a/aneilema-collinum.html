Aneilema collinum

Aneilema collinum, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows as a compact shrub, reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers indirect sunlight.

Aneilema collinum is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
