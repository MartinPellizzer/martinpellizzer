Aloe glabrescens

Aloe glabrescens, commonly known as the Cape aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, upright stem and large, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a bluish tint. The plant produces striking orange-red flowers during the summer months, which are clustered in racemes. Aloe glabrescens is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves.

It is often used in traditional medicine and as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and hardy nature.
