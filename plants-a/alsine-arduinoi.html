Alsine arduinoi

Alsine arduinoi, commonly known as the desert sandpenny, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a low-growing annual herb that thrives in arid and semi-arid environments. The plant produces small, white to pinkish flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are typically narrow and succulent, adapted to conserve water.

Alsine arduinoi is often found in sandy or gravelly soils, making it a characteristic species of desert ecosystems.
