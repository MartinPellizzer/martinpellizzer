Arrhenatherum pallens

Arrhenatherum pallens, commonly known as silver hair grass, is a perennial grass species native to temperate regions of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, silver-haired inflorescences that give it its common name. The plant typically grows in moist meadows, woodlands, and along stream banks. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue.

Arrhenatherum pallens plays a role in supporting local biodiversity by providing habitat and nectar for various insects.
