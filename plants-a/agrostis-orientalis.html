Agrostis orientalis

Agrostis orientalis, commonly known as oriental bentgrass, is a perennial grass species native to temperate regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its fine, tufted growth habit and slender, erect stems. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny florets. Its leaves are narrow, grass-like, and often have a bluish-green hue.

Agrostis orientalis is often found in meadows, grasslands, and along the edges of water bodies.
