Apostasia brunonis

Apostasia brunonis, commonly known as the black orchid, is a rare and striking member of the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in a spike, and it is typically found growing in shaded, moist environments. Apostasia brunonis is a terrestrial herb with a short rhizome and is often found in association with other epiphytic orchids.
