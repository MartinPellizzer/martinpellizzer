Acnistus Gesnerioides

Acnistus gesnerioides is a unique and striking plant known for its vibrant, showy flowers. Native to parts of South America, it thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. The plant features heart-shaped leaves and clusters of tubular blooms that range in color from white to pink. It is a member of the Apocynaceae family, which includes several toxic species. Despite its beauty, it requires careful handling due to its potentially harmful properties.
