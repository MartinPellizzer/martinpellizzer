Abutilon rotundifolium

Abutilon rotundifolium, commonly known as the woolly abutilon, is a shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its soft, fuzzy leaves that are rounded or heart-shaped. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its unique appearance and ease of cultivation contribute to its appeal among gardeners.
