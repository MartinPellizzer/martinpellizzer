Annesorhiza capensis

Annesorhiza capensis, commonly known as the Cape annesorhiza, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow and daisy-like.

Annesorhiza capensis is often used in rock gardens due to its drought tolerance and ornamental value.
