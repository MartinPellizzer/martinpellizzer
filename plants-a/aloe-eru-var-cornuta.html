Aloe eru var. cornuta

Aloe eru var. cornuta, commonly known as the horned aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that are often ribbed and adorned with spines. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences that bear yellow or orange flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

The unique horn-like structures on its leaves give it its distinctive appearance and contribute to its ornamental value.
