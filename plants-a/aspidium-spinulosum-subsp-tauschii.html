Aspidium spinulosum subsp. tauschii

Aspidium spinulosum subsp.

tauschii, commonly known as the Tausch's water fern, is a fern species native to parts of Europe and Asia. It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped fronds.

The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or along stream banks. Its spore-producing structures, called sori, are located on the underside of the fronds.

This subspecies is notable for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its graceful appearance.
