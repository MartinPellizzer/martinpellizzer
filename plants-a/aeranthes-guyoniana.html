Aeranthes guyoniana

Aeranthes guyoniana, commonly known as the Guyon's spider orchid, is a species of orchid native to the wet tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its unique, spider-like appearance, with elongated, slender petals that resemble the legs of a spider. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in the understory of rainforests. Its flowers are usually pale green or white, with a distinctive, intricate pattern on the petals.

Aeranthes guyoniana is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
