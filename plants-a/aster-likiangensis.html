Aster likiangensis

Aster likiangensis, commonly known as the Yunnan aster, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Yunnan Province in China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its showy, composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in high-altitude meadows and has lance-shaped leaves with a hairy texture. Its flowers bloom in late summer to early autumn, displaying a range of colors from white to purple.

Aster likiangensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic beauty.
