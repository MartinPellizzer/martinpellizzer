Aloe × selmarii

Aloe × selmarii, commonly known as the hybrid aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a bluish tint, often marked by white or yellowish teeth along the edges. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with small, yellowish-green flowers. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its leaves to survive dry conditions.

Aloe × selmarii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable growing conditions.
