Anacampseros angustata

Anacampseros angustata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and fleshy, narrow leaves that store water, enabling it to survive in arid conditions. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange, typically during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a valuable addition to drought-tolerant landscapes.
