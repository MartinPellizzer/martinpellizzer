Anemone biflora var. biflora

Anemone biflora var. biflora, commonly known as the two-flowered anemone, is a herbaceous perennial native to the eastern United States.

It features showy, white to pale pink flowers with a distinctive double layer of petal-like structures. The plant typically grows in moist, shady woodland habitats, often along stream banks or in thickets. Its leaves are palmately divided, with a coarse texture and a bluish-green hue.

The species is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits to pollinators.
