Anthericum tenellum

Anthericum tenellum, commonly known as the slender ladies' tresses, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the orchid family (Orchidaceae).

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and dense clusters of small, white or pale green flowers that resemble a tresses of hair. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and wet meadows. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often arranged in a rosette at the base.

Anthericum tenellum is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
