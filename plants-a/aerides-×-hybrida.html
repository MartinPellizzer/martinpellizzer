Aerides × hybrida

Aerides × hybrida, commonly known as the orchid hybrid, is a cross between species of the genus Aerides, resulting in a diverse range of ornamental varieties.

It is a epiphytic plant, typically found growing on trees in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features showy, fragrant flowers that come in a wide array of colors, often with intricate patterns. It requires bright, indirect light and consistent moisture to thrive.

Due to its hybrid nature, Aerides × hybrida exhibits a high degree of variability in growth habits and floral characteristics.
