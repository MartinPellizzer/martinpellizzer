Argostemma uniflorum var. pilosellum

Argostemma uniflorum var. pilosellum, commonly known as the hairy argostemma, is a perennial herb native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It features slender, upright stems covered in fine, soft hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, often with a slight greenish tinge. Its leaves are lance-shaped and also bear a hairy texture, contributing to its overall rugged appearance.

This species is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or along stream banks.
