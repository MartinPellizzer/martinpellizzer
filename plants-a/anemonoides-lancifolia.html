Anemonoides lancifolia

Anemonoides lancifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved anemone, is a herbaceous perennial native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features striking, showy flowers that are typically pink to red in color, with five to seven petals. The plant has slender, upright stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and alpine displays.

Its botanical characteristics make it a distinctive addition to both wild and cultivated landscapes.
