Astrantia ciliaris

Astrantia ciliaris, commonly known as the fringe flower or moonflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features showy, pale pink to white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are basal, heart-shaped, and covered in fine hairs.

Astrantia ciliaris is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
