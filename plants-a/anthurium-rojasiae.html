Anthurium rojasiae

Anthurium rojasiae, commonly known as the Rojasia anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Colombia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves and vibrant, spadix-like inflorescence. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its foliage is often a deep green with a glossy texture, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers surrounded by a colorful spathe.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is a sought-after specimen in botanical gardens and among plant enthusiasts.
