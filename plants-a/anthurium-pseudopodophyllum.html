Anthurium pseudopodophyllum

Anthurium pseudopodophyllum, commonly known as the velvet leaf anthurium, is a tropical flowering plant native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a velvety texture. The plant produces striking spadices, which are modified leaves that enclose the actual flowers. Anthurium pseudopodophyllum thrives in humid, warm environments with indirect light.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique foliage and ability to purify indoor air.
