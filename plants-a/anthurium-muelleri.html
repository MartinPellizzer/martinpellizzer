Anthurium muelleri

Anthurium muelleri, commonly known as the Mueller's arrowhead, is a tropical flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically bright green with a glossy texture. The plant produces striking spathes that are usually deep red or orange, enclosing a cluster of small, spadix-like flowers. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Anthurium muelleri is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and air-purifying qualities.
