Alocasia sinuata

Alocasia sinuata, commonly known as the Shiny-leaf Alocasia, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are glossy and often have a deep green color with a subtle metallic sheen. The plant belongs to the Araceae family, which includes other ornamental plants like caladiums and peace lilies. Alocasia sinuata thrives in humid, shady environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical landscapes.
