Anogeissus sericea var. nummularia

Anogeissus sericea var. nummularia, commonly known as the silver leaf acacia, is a shrub or small tree native to parts of Africa.

It is characterized by its finely haired, silver-gray leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in spikes, followed by woody pods containing flat, disc-shaped seeds. It thrives in arid or semi-arid environments and is adapted to withstand drought conditions.

The species is often used in traditional medicine and for its ornamental value in landscaping.
