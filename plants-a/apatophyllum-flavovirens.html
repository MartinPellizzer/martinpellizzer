Apatophyllum flavovirens

Apatophyllum flavovirens, commonly known as the yellow-green spurge, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, stem-like leaves and milky sap. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments. Its leaves are usually a mix of yellow and green, giving it its common name.
