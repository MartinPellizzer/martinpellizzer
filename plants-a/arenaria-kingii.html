Arenaria kingii

Arenaria kingii, commonly known as King's sandspurry, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial that thrives in sandy or rocky soils, often found in coastal areas. The plant produces small, white or pink flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are typically rounded and succulent, adapted to retain moisture in arid environments.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to stabilize soil in coastal ecosystems.
