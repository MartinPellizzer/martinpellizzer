Anthurium henryi

Anthurium henryi, commonly known as the Henry's arrowhead, is a striking tropical plant native to the Caribbean and Central America.

It features vibrant, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a glossy texture. The plant produces striking spadices, which are club-shaped inflorescences that are often red or orange in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture in its soil.

Anthurium henryi is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
