Agathyrsus cyaneus

Agathyrsus cyaneus, commonly known as the blue agathis, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, straight trunk and large, glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. Its wood is highly valued for its durability and fine grain.

Agathyrsus cyaneus plays an important role in the ecosystems of its native habitats.
