Ajax minor var. humilior

Ajax minor var. humilior, commonly known as the dwarf ajax, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color, and it produces small, pale blue or purple flowers during the summer months. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers. It is relatively drought-tolerant and requires minimal maintenance once established.

Its compact form and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for landscaping in arid or semi-arid climates.
