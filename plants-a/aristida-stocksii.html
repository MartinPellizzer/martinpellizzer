Aristida stocksii

Aristida stocksii, commonly known as the silvergrass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-gray foliage and compact growth habit, which makes it visually striking in gardens. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bloom in late summer, adding ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for xeriscaping and drought-tolerant landscapes.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for both ornamental and ecological gardening.
