Acronema chinense var. humile

Acronema chinense var. humile, commonly known as the small Chinese acronema, is a low-growing perennial herb native to eastern Asia.

It features small, opposite leaves that are typically elliptical or ovate in shape. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable species for ecological studies and horticultural use in shaded gardens.
