Adipera stahlii

Adipera stahlii, commonly known as the Stahli's orchid, is a species of orchid native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid with a distinctive inflorescence that bears several showy, violet-blue flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments within tropical forests. Its leaves are elongated and often covered in a dense layer of fine, white pubescence.

Adipera stahlii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated by enthusiasts of rare orchids.
