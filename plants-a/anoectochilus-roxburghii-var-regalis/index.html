Anoectochilus Roxburghii Var. Regalis

Regal orchid, scientifically known as Anoectochilus roxburghii var. regalis, is a rare and exquisite species native to the high-altitude regions of the Himalayas. This orchid is celebrated for its striking, velvety petals that display a vibrant blend of pink and white, creating a visually stunning appearance. It thrives in cool, moist environments and requires specific conditions to flourish, making it a sought-after plant among horticulturists. Due to its limited natural habitat and the threats posed by habitat destruction, the Regal orchid is classified as endangered. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this unique and beautiful flora for future generations.
