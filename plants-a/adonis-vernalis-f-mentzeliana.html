Adonis vernalis f. mentzeliana

Adonis vernalis f. mentzeliana, commonly known as the Mentzelian larkspur, is a herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Europe.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from white to blue or purple. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, thriving in well-drained soils and full sun. Its leaves are palmately divided, and the flowers are borne in dense clusters at the top of the stems.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and naturalized areas.
