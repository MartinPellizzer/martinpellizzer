Ayenia glabra

Ayenia glabra, commonly known as the smooth ayenia, is a perennial herb native to the eastern United States.

It typically grows in moist, woodland habitats and has slender, upright stems. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually white or pale pink, which bloom in the spring. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a smooth texture.

Ayenia glabra is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in native plant gardens.
