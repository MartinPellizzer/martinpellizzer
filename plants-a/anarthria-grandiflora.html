Anarthria grandiflora

Anarthria grandiflora, commonly known as the grandiflora anarthria, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant has compound leaves with serrated edges, and its stems are woody and often branched. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forest understories.

Anarthria grandiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
