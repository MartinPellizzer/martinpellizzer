Asplenium dombeyi

Asplenium dombeyi, commonly known as the Dombey's spleenwort, is a species of fern belonging to the family Aspleniaceae.

It is a small, terrestrial fern typically found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rock crevices. The plant has a distinctive morphology, featuring a creeping rhizome with clustered, lance-shaped leaves. Its fronds are usually dark green and have a serrated or toothed margin.

Asplenium dombeyi is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to cultivate in suitable conditions.
