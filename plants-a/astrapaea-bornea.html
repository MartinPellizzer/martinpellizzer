Astrapaea bornea

Astrapaea bornea, commonly known as the Bornean orchid, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Borneo.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. Astrapaea bornea thrives in humid, shaded environments, often growing in the understory of rainforests.

Its ornamental value makes it a sought-after species among horticulturists and plant enthusiasts.
