Argostemma rupestrinum

Argostemma rupestrinum, commonly known as the rock argostemma, is a perennial herb native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It typically grows in rocky or limestone habitats, showcasing its adaptability to harsh environments. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy, contributing to its distinctive appearance. Its flowers are small and clustered, usually in umbels, and range in color from white to pale yellow.

Argostemma rupestrinum is valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
