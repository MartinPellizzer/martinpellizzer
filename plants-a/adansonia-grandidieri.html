Adansonia grandidieri

Adansonia grandidieri, commonly known as the Royal Palm, is a striking endemic species native to Madagascar.

The plant belongs to the Malvaceae family and is adapted to the arid and semi-arid climates of its native habitat. Its flowers are typically white or pale yellow, and it produces large, fibrous fruits that contain seeds.
