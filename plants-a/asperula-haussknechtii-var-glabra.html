Asperula haussknechtii var. glabra

Asperula haussknechtii var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth asperula, is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to southern Europe.

It features small, oval-shaped leaves that are smooth and glossy, with a dark green hue. The plant produces clusters of delicate white or pale pink flowers in early spring. It thrives in partial to full shade and prefers well-drained, acidic soil.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in woodland gardens.
