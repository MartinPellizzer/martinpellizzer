Achimenes × naegelioides var. diamantina

Achimenes × naegelioides var. diamantina, commonly known as the Diamond Plant, is a hybrid ornamental plant native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and red, often with a distinctive speckled or dotted appearance. The plant has heart-shaped, glossy leaves and thrives in warm, humid environments. It is typically grown as a container plant due to its preference for well-drained soil and indirect sunlight.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for tropical gardens and indoor cultivation.
