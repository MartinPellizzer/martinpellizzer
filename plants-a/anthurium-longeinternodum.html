Anthurium longeinternodum

Anthurium longeinternodum, commonly known as the long internode anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its elongated, narrow leaves that can grow up to several feet in length, giving it a dramatic and exotic appearance. The plant produces striking spadices, which are typically bright red or orange, surrounded by a showy, leaf-like bract. It thrives in high humidity and indirect light, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique foliage and vibrant inflorescences make it a standout ornamental plant in both home and greenhouse settings.
