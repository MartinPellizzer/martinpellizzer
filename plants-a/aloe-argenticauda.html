Aloe argenticauda

Aloe argenticauda, commonly known as the silver-tailed aloe, is a striking succulent native to southern Africa.

It features tall, slender stems covered in dense, silver-gray foliage that gives it its distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of yellow flowers during the warmer months, adding a vibrant touch to its structure. Its leaves are thick and fleshy, adapted to store water in arid environments.

Aloe argenticauda is well-suited for rock gardens or xeriscaping due to its low maintenance and drought tolerance.
