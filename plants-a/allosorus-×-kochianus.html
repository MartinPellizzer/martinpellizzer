Allosorus × kochianus

Allosorus × kochianus, commonly known as the hybrid alpine orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces violet or purple flowers with a distinct lip, which is a specialized structure for attracting pollinators. Allosorus × kochianus thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine meadows at high elevations.

Its botanical significance lies in its role as a hybrid species that showcases the genetic diversity within the genus Allosorus.
