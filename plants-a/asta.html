Asta

Asta, commonly known as the asta plant, is a species of flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant, daisy-like flowers that typically range in color from white to yellow. The plant is native to various regions in Asia and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. Asta thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

Its growth habit is usually compact, with upright stems and small, lobed leaves.
