Amaryllis ignea

Amaryllis ignea, commonly known as the red amaryllis, is a striking bulbous plant native to South America.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from deep red to orange and yellow. The plant produces large, strap-like leaves that are evergreen in warmer climates. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Amaryllis ignea is often cultivated for its vibrant blooms and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
