Armeriastrum stocksii

Armeriastrum stocksii, commonly known as the coastal sand verbena, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of California and Baja California.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that thrives in sandy, well-drained soils and full sun. The plant produces small, pink to purple flowers in clusters during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are narrow and succulent, adapted to conserve moisture in arid environments.
