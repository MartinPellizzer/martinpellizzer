Astilbe japonica

Astilbe japonica, commonly known as Japanese feather reed, is a perennial plant native to East Asia.

It features upright, branching stems that bear plumes of small, fluffy flowers in shades of pink, red, or white. The plant has palmate, heart-shaped leaves that are dark green and finely textured. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it ideal for woodland gardens.

Astilbe japonica is valued for its ornamental foliage and delicate floral display.
