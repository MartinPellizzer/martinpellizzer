Asteracantha longifolia

Asteracantha longifolia, commonly known as the long-leaved asteracantha, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which is characterized by its composite flower heads and alternate leaf arrangement. The plant features slender, hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy as well. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, arranged in compact clusters, and bloom during the warmer months.

It is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments.
