Achyranthes colinsii

Achyranthes colinsii, commonly known as the African spurge, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous annual that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant features alternate, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs. Its small yellow flowers are clustered in dense, branched inflorescences.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed or open habitats.
