Arenaria Tetraquetra Var. Cavanillesiana

Arenaria tetraquetra var. cavanillesiana is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe. It is known for its small, white or pinkish flowers that bloom in early spring. This variant of Arenaria tetraquetra is particularly prized for its ornamental value and hardiness. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens. Due to its limited distribution, it is often considered a rare and sought-after species among horticultural enthusiasts.
