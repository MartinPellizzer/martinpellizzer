Ampelopsis aconitifolia f. glabra

Ampelopsis aconitifolia f. glabra, commonly known as the smooth leaf grapevine, is a variant of the Ampelopsis aconitifolia species.

It is a climbing vine characterized by its slender stems and compound leaves that are smooth and glabrous. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by blue-black berries. It is native to eastern Asia and thrives in moist, shady environments.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and ecological restoration projects.
