Astragalus yukselii

Astragalus yukselii, commonly known as Yuksel's milkvetch, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to Turkey and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. The plant produces pods that contain several seeds, typical of the Fabaceae family. It thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid environments.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and potentially endangered species.
