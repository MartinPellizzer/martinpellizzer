Abelia graebneriana

Abelia graebneriana, commonly known as the pink abelia, is a deciduous shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It features narrow, dark green leaves that turn reddish in the fall. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, tubular flowers in shades of pink and white during the summer months. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained, moist soil.

Abelia graebneriana is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various garden conditions.
