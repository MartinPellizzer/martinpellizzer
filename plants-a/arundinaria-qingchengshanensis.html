Arundinaria qingchengshanensis

Arundinaria qingchengshanensis, commonly known as the Qingchengshan bamboo, is a rare and endemic bamboo species native to China.

It is characterized by its tall, slender culms that can reach heights of up to 10 meters. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a valuable species for botanical research and conservation efforts.
