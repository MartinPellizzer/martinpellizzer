Anthericum liliago var. australe

Anthericum liliago var. australe, commonly known as the Australian lily or narrow-leaved lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Australia.

It features slender, grass-like leaves that are typically narrow and have a bluish-green hue. The plant produces delicate, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in summer, arranged in a loose cluster at the top of the stem. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along streambanks.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its elegant appearance.
