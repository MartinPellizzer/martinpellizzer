Artemisia niitakayamensis

Artemisia niitakayamensis, commonly known as the Japanese wormwood, is a perennial shrub native to Japan.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, silver-green foliage. The plant produces small, yellowish flower heads that are typically clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or rocky habitats.

Due to its medicinal properties, it has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
