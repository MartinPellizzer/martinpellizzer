Athyrium cyclosorum var. strictum

Athyrium cyclosorum var. strictum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf Japanese painted fern, is a species of fern native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped leaves that are typically green with a metallic sheen. The fronds are usually upright and compact, forming a dense, upright clump. It prefers shady, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
