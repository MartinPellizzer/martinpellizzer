Andropogon polystachyos

Andropogon polystachyos, commonly known as big bluestem, is a tall grass native to North America.

It is a key component of tallgrass prairie ecosystems and is characterized by its blue-green color during the growing season. The plant has a robust root system that helps prevent soil erosion and supports nutrient cycling. It produces feathery, spike-like inflorescences that are prominent in the fall.

Big bluestem is also valued for its drought tolerance and is used in restoration projects and as a forage crop.
