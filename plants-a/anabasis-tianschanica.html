Anabasis tianschanica

Anabasis tianschanica, commonly known as the Tian Shan sandmat, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It is a low-growing, herbaceous perennial that thrives in arid and semi-arid environments. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its root system is extensive, allowing it to survive in sandy and saline soils.

It is often found in dunes and rocky areas, playing a role in stabilizing soil and preventing erosion.
