Atractocarpus obscurinervius

Atractocarpus obscurinervius, commonly known as the dark-veined tamarind, is a member of the Rubiaceae family and is native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
