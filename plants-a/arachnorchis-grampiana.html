Arachnorchis grampiana

Arachnorchis grampiana, commonly known as the Grampian spider-orchid, is an epiphytic orchid native to Australia.

It is characterized by its unique, spider-like appearance, with slender, wiry stems and small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows on tree trunks or branches in humid, shaded environments. It has a complex root system adapted for absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
