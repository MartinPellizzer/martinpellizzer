Ageria cassena

Ageria cassena, commonly known as the Cape jasmine, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces fragrant white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer seasons. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and have a slightly serrated edge.

Ageria cassena is often found in coastal areas and is adapted to sandy or well-drained soils.
