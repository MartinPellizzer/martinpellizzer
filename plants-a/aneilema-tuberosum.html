Aneilema tuberosum

Aneilema tuberosum, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, tuberous roots that store water and nutrients, allowing it to thrive in dry conditions. The plant produces showy, violet-colored flowers that bloom in clusters, often with a white or pinkish hue. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and covered in a fine, waxy coating.

Aneilema tuberosum is widely cultivated as a decorative plant due to its attractive foliage and long-lasting blooms.
