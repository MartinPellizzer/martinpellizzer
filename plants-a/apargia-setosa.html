Apargia setosa

Apargia setosa, commonly known as the hairy false nettle, is a herbaceous plant native to eastern North America.

Its flowers are small and clustered in spikes, with both male and female flowers present on the same plant.

Apargia setosa is often found in rich, loamy soils and can thrive in partial to full shade.
