Anemone × palezieuxii

Anemone × palezieuxii, commonly known as the hybrid anemone, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features showy, white to pale pink flowers with a central cluster of yellow stamens, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has basal leaves that are palmately lobed and often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained, loamy soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Anemone × palezieuxii is valued in gardens for its ornamental beauty and adaptability to temperate climates.
