Asarum sieboldii f. chungbuensis

Asarum sieboldii f. chungbuensis, commonly known as the Chungbu Mountain asarum, is a variant of the asarum plant native to China.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, greenish-yellow flowers. The plant thrives in shaded, moist environments, typically found in mountainous regions. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine for their purported therapeutic properties.

Botanically, it belongs to the Aristolochiaceae family and is distinguished by its unique floral structure and growth habit.
