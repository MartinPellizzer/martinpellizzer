Asterocephalus strictus

Asterocephalus strictus, commonly known as the narrow-leaf asterhead, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, daisy-like flowers that are typically white or pale blue in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open grasslands and rocky areas.

Asterocephalus strictus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
