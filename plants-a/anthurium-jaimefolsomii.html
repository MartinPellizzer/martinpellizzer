Anthurium jaimefolsomii

Anthurium jaimefolsomii, commonly known as the Jaime Folsom anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Colombia.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically deep green with a glossy texture. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-shaped inflorescences surrounded by colorful spathe flowers, often in shades of red, orange, or yellow. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Anthurium jaimefolsomii is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
