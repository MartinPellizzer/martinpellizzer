Acalypha subsessilis var. glabra

Acalypha subsessilis var. glabra, commonly known as smoothleaf copperleaf, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its smooth, glabrous leaves that are often reddish or bronze in color. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically green or yellowish in hue. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests or along stream banks.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in tropical gardens.
