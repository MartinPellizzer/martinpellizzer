Achillea clavennae f. velebitica

Achillea clavennae f. velebitica, commonly known as the Velebit yarrow, is a perennial herb native to the Balkan Peninsula.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its finely divided, gray-green leaves and flat-topped clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or calcareous soils and is adapted to dry, sunny environments. It is often found in mountainous regions, particularly in the Dinaric Alps, where it thrives in open habitats.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional medicinal uses.
