Alstroemeria fimbriata

Alstroemeria fimbriata, commonly known as the fringed alstroemeria, is a perennial evergreen plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that come in a range of colors including red, orange, yellow, and pink. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, featuring strap-like leaves that are often variegated. Its rhizomatous root system allows it to thrive in well-drained soils, making it suitable for both garden beds and containers.

Alstroemeria fimbriata is valued for its long-lasting blooms and is often used in floral arrangements.
