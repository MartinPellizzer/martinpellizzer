Agrostis alba var. varia

Agrostis alba var. varia, commonly known as the silver hairgrass, is a variant of the common bentgrass.

It is a low-growing perennial grass native to temperate regions of Europe and Asia. The plant is characterized by its fine, silver-tinged leaves and compact growth habit. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows and along stream banks.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping and ecological restoration projects.
