Anthemis tenuisecta subsp. tenuisecta

Anthemis tenuisecta subsp.

tenuisecta, commonly known as the narrow-leaved tansy, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that consist of both ray and disk florets.

The plant typically grows in dry, sunny habitats such as rocky soils and open woodlands. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often with a silvery or grayish underside, and it produces small yellow flowers in the summer.

The species is valued for its aromatic properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
