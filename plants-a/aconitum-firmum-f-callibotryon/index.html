Aconitum Firmum F. Callibotryon

Aconitum firmum f. callibotryon is a striking variant of the aconite genus known for its vibrant blue to purple flowers. This plant is native to the mountainous regions of central and eastern Europe. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade. Its unique foliage and showy blooms make it a prized addition to gardens and botanical collections. Despite its beauty, it is highly toxic and requires careful handling.
