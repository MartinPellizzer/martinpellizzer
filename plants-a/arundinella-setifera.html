Arundinella setifera

Arundinella setifera, commonly known as the slender bamboo, is a perennial grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features slender, hollow stems that are typically green or slightly reddish in color. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spikelets, which are characteristic of the grass family. Its leaves are long, narrow, and often have a slight sheen.

Arundinella setifera is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its elegant appearance and adaptability to various soil conditions.
