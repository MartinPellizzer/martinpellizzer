Aspalathus aristata

Aspalathus aristata, commonly known as the sweet cycad, is a unique plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, unbranched stem and large, pinnately compound leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically yellow or green in color. Aspalathus aristata is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it resilient to drought.

Its slow growth rate and long lifespan make it a distinctive member of the cycad family.
