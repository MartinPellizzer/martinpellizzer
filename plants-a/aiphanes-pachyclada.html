Aiphanes pachyclada

Aiphanes pachyclada, commonly known as the broad-leaved papyrus, is a member of the Cyperaceae family, characterized by its grass-like appearance.

Aiphanes pachyclada is often used in restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil and filter water.
