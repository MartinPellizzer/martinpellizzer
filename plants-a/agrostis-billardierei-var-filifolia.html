Agrostis billardierei var. filifolia

Agrostis billardierei var. filifolia, commonly known as slender meadow grass, is a perennial grass species native to Australia.

It typically grows in coastal and sandy soils, often found in heathlands and open woodlands. The plant has slender, tufted stems with fine, thread-like leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of hairs. Its inflorescence consists of a dense cluster of spikelets, which are small and compact.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping due to its delicate appearance.
