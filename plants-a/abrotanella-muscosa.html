Abrotanella muscosa

Abrotanella muscosa, commonly known as woolly thyme or mossy thyme, is a low-growing perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, aromatic leaves covered in fine, white hairs that give it a woolly appearance. The plant produces clusters of tiny, pink to purple flowers that bloom in the spring and summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.
