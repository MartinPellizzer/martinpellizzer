Aloe nicholsii

Aloe nicholsii, commonly known as the Mountain Aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, often with a waxy coating. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with bright yellow flowers during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its leaves and stems.

Aloe nicholsii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable growing conditions.
