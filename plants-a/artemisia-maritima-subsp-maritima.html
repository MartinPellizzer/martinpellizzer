Artemisia maritima subsp. maritima

Artemisia maritima subsp.

maritima, commonly known as sea wormwood, is a perennial herb native to coastal regions of the Northern Hemisphere. It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, silky hairs.

The plant produces small, yellowish-green flower heads that are typically clustered in panicles. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in dune environments. Artemisia maritima subsp.

maritima is well adapted to salt spray and harsh maritime conditions.
