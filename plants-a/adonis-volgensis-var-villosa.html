Adonis volgensis var. villosa

Adonis volgensis var. villosa, commonly known as the hairy adonis, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features basal rosettes of soft, hairy, gray-green leaves that are often covered in fine pubescence. The plant produces showy, yellowish-white flowers in early summer, arranged in dense, terminal clusters. Its flowering stems are usually unbranched and can reach heights of up to 60 cm.

This species is adapted to rocky and arid environments, displaying a compact growth form to conserve moisture.
