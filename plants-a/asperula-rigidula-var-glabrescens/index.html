Asperula Rigidula Var. Glabrescens

Asperula rigidula var. glabrescens is a rare and delicate plant species native to specific regions of Europe. It is known for its compact growth habit and small, clustered flowers that bloom in early spring. This variety is distinguished by its smooth, glabrous leaves and stems, setting it apart from other asperula species. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in woodland areas or along stream banks. Due to its limited distribution and unique characteristics, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists alike.
