Alocasia jiewhoei

Alocasia jiewhoei, commonly known as the Jiewhoei arrowleaf, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves that can grow up to 3 feet in length, featuring a deep green color with prominent veins. The plant thrives in humid, shady environments and requires well-draining, rich soil to flourish. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique foliage and aesthetic appeal.

Alocasia jiewhoei is also valued for its air-purifying properties, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
