Arachne Cordifolia

Heartleaf arachne, scientifically known as Arachne cordifolia, is a striking member of the Araliaceae family native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia. This plant is recognized for its heart-shaped leaves and delicate, clustered white flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for both ornamental gardens and indoor cultivation. Despite its beauty, it requires specific care to thrive, including well-draining soil and indirect sunlight. Overall, it is a fascinating plant that adds a touch of elegance to any green space.
