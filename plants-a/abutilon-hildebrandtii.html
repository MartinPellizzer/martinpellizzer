Abutilon hildebrandtii

Abutilon hildebrandtii, commonly known as the woolly abutilon, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its fuzzy, velvety leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces showy, lantern-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Abutilon hildebrandtii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its unique foliage and attractive blooms.
