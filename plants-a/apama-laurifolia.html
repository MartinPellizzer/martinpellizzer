Apama laurifolia

Apama laurifolia, commonly known as the glossy laurel.

It is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant features broad, dark green, glossy leaves that are elliptical in shape. It produces small, fragrant white flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by small, red berries.

Apama laurifolia is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and pleasant aroma.
