Achimenes ignea

Achimenes ignea, commonly known as the flame violet, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant features heart-shaped, velvety leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Achimenes ignea is known for its ability to bloom throughout the growing season, making it a popular choice for tropical and subtropical gardens.
