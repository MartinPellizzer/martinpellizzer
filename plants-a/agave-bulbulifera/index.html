Agave Bulbulifera

Agave bulbulifera is a striking plant native to the arid regions of Mexico. Known for its unique appearance, it features thick, fleshy leaves that form a rosette shape. This species is also called the "Bulbuls' agave" due to its association with the bulbuls bird. It is popular among succulent enthusiasts for its architectural beauty and resilience. The plant is often cultivated for ornamental purposes and can thrive in well-drained soil with minimal water.
