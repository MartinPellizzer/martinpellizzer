Amphicosmia brunoniana

Amphicosmia brunoniana, commonly known as the golden star orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking yellow and green foliage, with flowers that resemble small stars, hence its common name. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest undergrowth. Its flowers are fragrant and attract various pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Amphicosmia brunoniana is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
