Aechmea thyrsiflora

Aechmea thyrsiflora, commonly known as the pineapple bromeliad, is a striking epiphytic plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its rosette of fleshy, grass-like leaves that form a central cup, which collects water and nutrients. The plant produces an inflorescence that bears showy, violet-colored flowers, followed by small, red berries. Its foliage is often adorned with a waxy bloom, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Aechmea thyrsiflora is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its adaptability and ornamental value.
