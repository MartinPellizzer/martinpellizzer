Anemia × espiritosantensis

Anemia × espiritosantensis, commonly known as the hybrid anemia plant, is a botanical hybrid that combines traits from its parent species.

It is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Brazil, and thrives in tropical climates. The plant features broad, lance-shaped leaves with a dark green color and produces small, fragrant white flowers. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to well-drained soil and full sun exposure.

Due to its hybrid nature, it exhibits variability in growth habits and floral characteristics.
