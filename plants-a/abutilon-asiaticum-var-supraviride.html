Abutilon asiaticum var. supraviride

Abutilon asiaticum var. supraviride, commonly known as the velvetleaf abutilon, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its velvety, fuzzy leaves and showy, lantern-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant typically grows as an annual or perennial herb, often reaching heights of up to 2 meters. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a common weed in agricultural fields.

Its rapid growth and adaptability contribute to its success in various environments.
