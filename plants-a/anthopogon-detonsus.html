Anthopogon detonsus

Anthopogon detonsus, commonly known as the slender everlasting.

It is a flowering plant native to South Africa, characterized by its compact, evergreen growth habit and small, needle-like leaves. The species produces clusters of tiny, pale blue to white flowers that bloom in spring. It is typically found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens, though it is not widely distributed beyond its native range.
