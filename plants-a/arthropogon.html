Arthropogon

Arthropogon, commonly known as grass or sedge, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Poaceae.

These plants are typically herbaceous and can be found in a variety of habitats, including wetlands and grasslands. Arthropogon species often play a crucial role in soil stabilization and as a food source for various herbivores.
