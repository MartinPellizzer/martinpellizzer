Agelanthus Discolor

Agelanthus fern, scientifically known as Agelanthus discolor, is a unique and lesser-known member of the fern family. Native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, this plant thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its delicate fronds display a striking color variation, ranging from light green to deep bronze. Despite its beauty, Agelanthus discolor remains underappreciated in mainstream horticulture. This article explores its characteristics, habitat, and potential for cultivation.
