Anthurium lucioi

Anthurium lucioi, commonly known as the Lucio's flamingo flower, is a tropical plant native to Colombia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves and vibrant, spadix-like inflorescence. The plant produces showy, colorful bracts that resemble petals, which are actually modified leaves. Anthurium lucioi thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor and greenhouse cultivation.
