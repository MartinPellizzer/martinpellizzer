Argyranthemum anethifolium

Argyranthemum anethifolium, commonly known as the silverleaf geranium, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray, anise-scented foliage and clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers. The plant thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil. It is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.

Argyranthemum anethifolium is also valued for its aromatic properties and is sometimes used in herbal remedies.
