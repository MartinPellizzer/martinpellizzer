Acrostichum varians var. contractum

Acrostichum varians var. contractum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf maidenhair fern, is a variant of the broad-leaf maidenhair fern.

It is a delicate, evergreen fern characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its fronds are usually bright green and have a feathery appearance, with small, toothed leaflets.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
