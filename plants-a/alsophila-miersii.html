Alsophila miersii

Alsophila miersii, commonly known as the Miers' tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds that can reach several meters in length. Alsophila miersii is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
