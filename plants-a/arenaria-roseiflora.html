Arenaria roseiflora

Arenaria roseiflora, commonly known as the pink sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the rocky and sandy soils of the Mediterranean region.

It features dense clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in early spring, adding a delicate floral display to its habitat. The plant has succulent, fleshy leaves that help it retain moisture in arid environments. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its compact growth habit and attractive flowers make it a popular ornamental plant in horticulture.
