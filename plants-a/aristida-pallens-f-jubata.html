Aristida pallens f. jubata

Aristida pallens f. jubata, commonly known as the silverhair grass, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its fine, silver-gray foliage. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as prairies and disturbed soils. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that are often tinged with purple or pink.
