Alyssum deltoideum

Alyssum deltoideum, commonly known as the heartleaf alyssum, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It is a low-growing annual or perennial herb with small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant features heart-shaped leaves and produces clusters of tiny flowers that bloom in early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Alyssum deltoideum is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
