Anemonidium canadense

Anemonidium canadense, commonly known as Canadian anemone, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features showy, white to pinkish flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are palmately lobed and often have a reddish or purple tint beneath.

Anemonidium canadense plays a role in supporting local pollinators and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
