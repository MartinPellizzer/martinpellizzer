Arum colocasioides

Arum colocasioides, commonly known as the arrowhead plant, is a tropical herbaceous plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its arrow-shaped leaves, which are typically a deep green with a glossy texture. The plant produces striking inflorescences that consist of a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Arum colocasioides is also valued for its ability to purify air, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
