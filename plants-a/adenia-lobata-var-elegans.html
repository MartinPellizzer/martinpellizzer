Adenia lobata var. elegans

Adenia lobata var. elegans, commonly known as the elegant adenia, is a striking evergreen shrub native to eastern Australia.

It features lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and glossy, often with a reddish or purple tinge on the underside. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that are typically red or orange, attracting nectar-feeding birds and insects. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and its ability to tolerate coastal conditions.
