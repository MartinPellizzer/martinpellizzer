Anthurium × hollandi

Anthurium × hollandi, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a hybrid plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped, brightly colored spathes that range from red to orange, often with a contrasting green or white spadix. The plant thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth. Its leaves are large, glossy, and typically have a deep green color with prominent veins.

Anthurium × hollandi is widely cultivated as a decorative plant due to its ornamental value and ability to thrive in indoor settings.
