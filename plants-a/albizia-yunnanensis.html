Albizia yunnanensis

Albizia yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan Albizia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to southern China.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant features bipinnate leaves that are often used in traditional Chinese medicine. It produces fragrant yellowish-white flowers that bloom in summer, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies.

Albizia yunnanensis is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
