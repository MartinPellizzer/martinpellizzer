Allium platystemon

Allium platystemon, commonly known as the narrowleaf garlic.

It is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region and belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family. The plant features grass-like leaves and produces small, spherical bulbs that are often used as a culinary herb. Its flowers are typically white or pale pink, arranged in a dense umbel.

Allium platystemon is valued for its aromatic leaves and is often cultivated in gardens for both ornamental and edible purposes.
