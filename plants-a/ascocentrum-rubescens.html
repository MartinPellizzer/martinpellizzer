Ascocentrum rubescens

Ascocentrum rubescens, commonly known as the red-flowered arrowroot, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its striking red bracts that enclose the small, white flowers. The plant features a rhizomatous root system and produces arrowhead-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green. It thrives in humid, tropical environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The plant's unique floral structure and vibrant color make it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardening.
