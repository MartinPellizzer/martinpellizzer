Athyriopsis concinna

Athyriopsis concinna, commonly known as the slender fern, is a small, delicate fern native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the family Athyriaceae and is characterized by its finely divided, feather-like fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its rhizomes are short and thick, supporting its compact growth habit.

Athyriopsis concinna is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in shaded garden settings.
