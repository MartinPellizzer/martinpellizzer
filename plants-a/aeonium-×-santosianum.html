Aeonium × santosianum

Aeonium × santosianum, commonly known as the Christmas Chandelier, is a hybrid succulent plant native to the Canary Islands.

It features rosettes of fleshy, blue-green leaves that are often adorned with red or purple margins. The plant produces tall, branching inflorescences with yellow flowers during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Due to its striking appearance, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
