Anthurium santaritensis

Anthurium santaritensis, commonly known as the Santa Rita anthurium, is a species of flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive spathe and spadix inflorescence. The plant features large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a vibrant green with a velvety texture. Its flowers, while not showy, play a crucial role in its reproductive cycle.

Anthurium santaritensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in warm, humid environments.
