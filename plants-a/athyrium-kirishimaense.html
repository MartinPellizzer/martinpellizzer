Athyrium kirishimaense

Athyrium kirishimaense, commonly known as the Kirishima holly fern, is a species of fern native to Japan.

It belongs to the family Athyriaceae and is characterized by its delicate, bi-colored fronds. The plant features a short, creeping rhizome with fiddlehead-like, coiled young fronds that unfurl into lanceolate leaves. Its foliage is often adorned with a striking combination of green and silvery-white on the underside.

This fern thrives in shaded, moist environments and is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.
