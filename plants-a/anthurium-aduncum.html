Anthurium aduncum

Anthurium aduncum, commonly known as the "Cobra Lily," is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive spathe and spadix inflorescence. The plant features large, heart-shaped leaves that are usually dark green with a glossy texture. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, enclosed within a brightly colored, leaf-like structure called a spathe.

Anthurium aduncum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in warm, humid environments.
