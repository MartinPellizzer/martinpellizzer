Asphodelus repens

Asphodelus repens, commonly known as the spreading asphodel, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa.

It features strap-like, grass-like leaves that are typically bluish-green in color. The plant produces tall spikes of bright yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry meadows and rocky areas.

Asphodelus repens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in garden landscapes.
