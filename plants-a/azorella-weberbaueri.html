Azorella weberbaueri

Azorella weberbaueri, commonly known as the cushion plant, is a member of the Apiaceae family and is native to the high Andes of South America.

Azorella weberbaueri is adapted to nutrient-poor soils and is often found in alpine and subarctic regions.
