Asplenium kohautianum

Asplenium kohautianum, commonly known as the Kohaut's spleenwort, is a small, epiphytic fern belonging to the family Aspleniaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant typically grows on tree trunks or rocks, relying on atmospheric moisture for nourishment. Its fronds are narrow and lance-shaped, with a dark green color and a leathery texture.

It is often found in humid, shaded environments, making it a characteristic species of tropical rainforests.
