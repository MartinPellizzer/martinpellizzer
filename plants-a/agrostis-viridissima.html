Agrostis viridissima

Agrostis viridissima, commonly known as green bentgrass, is a perennial grass species native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It is characterized by its fine, tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically dark green in color. This plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wet meadows, marshes, and along stream banks. Agrostis viridissima is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping or as a ground cover.

Its reproductive structures include small, spike-like inflorescences that produce numerous tiny seeds.
