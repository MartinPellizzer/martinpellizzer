Amaryllis punicea

Amaryllis punicea, commonly known as the fire lily, is a striking bulbous plant native to South America.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from red to orange, often with a contrasting purple or red stripe down the center. The plant produces large, strap-like leaves that are dark green and can grow up to 1 meter in length. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and containers.

Amaryllis punicea is known for its ability to bloom reliably when cared for properly, adding vibrant color to the landscape.
