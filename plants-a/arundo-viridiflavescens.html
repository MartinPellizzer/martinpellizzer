Arundo viridiflavescens

Arundo viridiflavescens, commonly known as the green-yellow reed, is a perennial grass native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems that can reach heights of up to 3 meters. The leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a distinct yellowish-green coloration on the underside. This plant thrives in moist environments such as wetlands and riverbanks.

It is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its attractive appearance and adaptability.
