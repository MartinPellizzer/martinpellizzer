Aloe andongensis

Aloe andongensis, commonly known as the Andong aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a slight blue or purple tint. The plant produces yellow flowers in clusters during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its leaves to survive droughts.

Aloe andongensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and medicinal properties.
