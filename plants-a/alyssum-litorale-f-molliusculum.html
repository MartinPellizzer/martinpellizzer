Alyssum litorale f. molliusculum

Alyssum litorale f. molliusculum, commonly known as the coastal alyssum, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features dense clusters of small, fragrant, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has succulent, fleshy leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs, aiding in water retention. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
