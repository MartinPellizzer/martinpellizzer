Actinidia umbelloides var. flabellifolia

Actinidia umbelloides var. flabellifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaf actinidia, is a species native to China.

It is a deciduous vine characterized by its compound leaves, which are often broad and lobed. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that appear in clusters, followed by edible berries that are typically yellow or orange. It thrives in temperate climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and fruit production.

The leaves are also used in traditional Chinese medicine.
