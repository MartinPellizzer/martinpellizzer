Argythamnia argentinensis

Argythamnia argentinensis, commonly known as the Argentine sandthrift, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Argentina.

Argythamnia argentinensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in stabilizing sandy soils.
