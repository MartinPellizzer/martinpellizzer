Agrostis signata var. gigantea

Agrostis signata var. gigantea, commonly known as the giant bentgrass, is a variant of the bentgrass species native to North America.

It is a low-growing perennial grass that typically forms dense, mat-like turf. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves with a bluish-green color and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It is often found in moist, well-drained soils in open areas such as meadows and wetlands.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in native plant gardens.
