Aloe rigens var. mortimeri

Aloe rigens var. mortimeri, commonly known as the Mortimer's Aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, green to blue-green leaves that are covered in a layer of white, powdery wax. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with small, yellowish flowers during the summer months. It is adapted to arid environments and requires well-draining soil to thrive.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in dry climates.
