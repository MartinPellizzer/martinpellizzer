Arisaema stracheyanum

Arisaema stracheyanum, commonly known as the Strachey's arrowhead, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive inflorescence composed of a spadix and a spathe. The plant features heart-shaped leaves and produces small, inconspicuous flowers. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Due to its unique morphology and ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens.
