Ageratina persetosa

Ageratina persetosa, commonly known as the stiff ageratum, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Ageratina persetosa thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking a low-maintenance, decorative plant.
