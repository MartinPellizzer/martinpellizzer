Astragalus xiqingshanicus

Astragalus xiqingshanicus, commonly known as the Qixingshan milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its erect, herbaceous stems and compound leaves. The plant produces small, yellow to green flowers arranged in clusters, which are typical of the genus Astragalus. It is often found in arid or semi-arid regions, thriving in well-drained soils.

Due to its limited distribution and ecological significance, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
