Actinidia rufa var. cordifolia

Actinidia rufa var. cordifolia, commonly known as the red actinidia or heartleaf actinidia, is a deciduous vine native to China.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and small, edible berries that ripen to a red color. The plant thrives in temperate climates and prefers partial shade to full sun. It has a climbing habit and can be trained on trellises or arbors.

Its ornamental value comes from its vibrant foliage and attractive fruit, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
