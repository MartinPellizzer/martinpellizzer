Achyranthes morongii

Achyranthes morongii, commonly known as Morong's smartweed, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is a herbaceous annual or biennial species that typically grows in moist, disturbed soils. The plant features upright stems with alternate, lance-shaped leaves that are often reddish in color. Its small, clustered flowers are usually pink or red, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits.

It is valued in traditional medicine for its potential anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties.
