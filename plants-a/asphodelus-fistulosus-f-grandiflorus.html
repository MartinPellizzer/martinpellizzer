Asphodelus fistulosus f. grandiflorus

Asphodelus fistulosus f. grandiflorus, commonly known as the giant bulbous asphodel, is a striking ornamental plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its tall, grass-like leaves and large, showy flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. The plant produces bulbous structures that store nutrients, allowing it to survive in dry conditions. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its unique appearance and resilience make it a valuable addition to both wild and cultivated landscapes.
