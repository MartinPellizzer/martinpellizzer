Alstroemeria pulchra var. bicolor

Alstroemeria pulchra var. bicolor, commonly known as the rainbow lily or the Peruvian lily, is a striking perennial plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that typically display a bicolor pattern, often with red, orange, and yellow hues. The plant features strap-like, grassy leaves and thrives in well-drained soil with partial to full sunlight. It is a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements due to its long-lasting blooms and ornamental value.

Alstroemeria pulchra var. bicolor is also valued for its ability to withstand cooler temperatures, making it suitable for temperate climates.
