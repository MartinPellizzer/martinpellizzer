Alsinidendron verticillatum

Alsinidendron verticillatum, commonly known as the clustered alsinidendron, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa and the Arabian Peninsula.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and clusters of small, pale yellow to greenish flowers. The plant has small, ovate leaves that are arranged in whorls around the stem, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky soils.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens in regions with suitable climates.
