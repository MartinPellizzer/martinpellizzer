Alternanthera bettzickiana f. aurea

Alternanthera bettzickiana f. aurea, commonly known as the golden alternanthera, is a striking ornamental plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, golden-orange foliage that stands out against its grass-like, narrow leaves. The plant typically grows as a compact, bushy shrub with a spreading habit. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full sunlight.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for water gardens and as a container plant.
