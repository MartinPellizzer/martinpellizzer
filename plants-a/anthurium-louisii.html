Anthurium louisii

Anthurium louisii, commonly known as the Louis's arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to the Caribbean and Central America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are often bright green with a metallic sheen. The plant produces spadices, which are inflorescences that consist of a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe. It thrives in humid, warm environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.

Anthurium louisii is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
