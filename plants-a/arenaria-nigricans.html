Arenaria nigricans

Arenaria nigricans, commonly known as black sandwort, is a flowering plant native to arctic and alpine regions.

It is characterized by its low-growing habit and produces small, white or pink flowers. The plant has fleshy, succulent leaves that help it survive in harsh, cold environments. It thrives in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in tundra and mountainous areas.

Arenaria nigricans plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for small invertebrates in its native ecosystems.
