Aster millefolius

Aster millefolius, commonly known as common yarrow, is a herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features a basal rosette of finely divided, feathery leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pinkish flowers arranged in flat-topped umbels. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in meadows and along roadsides.

Aster millefolius is known for its aromatic properties and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported healing benefits.
