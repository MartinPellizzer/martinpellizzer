Aloe oligospila

Aloe oligospila, commonly known as the narrow-leaved aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features upright, branched stems that bear fleshy, narrow, and pointed leaves. The leaves are covered in a layer of white, powdery bloom, which helps reduce water loss. It produces yellowish-green flowers in clusters during the summer months.

This plant is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
