Andropogon welwitschii

Andropogon welwitschii, commonly known as the tall bluestem, is a perennial grass native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and narrow, blue-green leaves that give it its common name. This plant thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and savannas. It plays an important role in the ecosystem by providing habitat and food for various wildlife species.

Andropogon welwitschii is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping.
