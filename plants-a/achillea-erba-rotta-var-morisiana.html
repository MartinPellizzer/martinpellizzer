Achillea erba-rotta var. morisiana

Achillea erba-rotta var. morisiana, commonly known as the Morisiana yarrow, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features finely divided, gray-green leaves and produces clusters of small, white to pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, sunny habitats such as rocky soils and grasslands. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscaping.
