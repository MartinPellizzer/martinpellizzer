Amaryllis gigantea

Amaryllis gigantea, commonly known as the giant amaryllis, is a striking bulbous plant native to South America.

It produces large, showy flowers in shades of red, white, and pink, often with a distinctive red or purple stripe down the center. The plant features strap-like, sword-shaped leaves that are broad and glossy. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and containers.

Amaryllis gigantea is known for its ability to bloom multiple times a year under optimal growing conditions.
