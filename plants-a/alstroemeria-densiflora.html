Alstroemeria densiflora

Alstroemeria densiflora, commonly known as the dense-flowered alstroemeria, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces clusters of showy, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, which bloom in late summer to early autumn. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.

Alstroemeria densiflora is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in floral arrangements.
