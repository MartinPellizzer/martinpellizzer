Agathosma riversdalensis

Agathosma riversdalensis, commonly known as Riverdal sage, is a small, evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its aromatic, needle-like leaves and small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and prefers full sun exposure. It is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

Its unique appearance and resilience make it a desirable plant for both ornamental and ecological purposes.
