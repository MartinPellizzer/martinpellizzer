Anil lotoides

Anil lotoides, commonly known as the blue lotus, is a striking aquatic plant native to the wetlands of Southeast Asia.

It features large, showy blue or purple flowers that bloom at night, making it a unique addition to water gardens. The plant has broad, heart-shaped leaves that float on the water's surface, while its roots anchor it in the muddy substrate. Anil lotoides is valued for its ornamental beauty and is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

Its blooming cycle and striking appearance make it a popular choice for both ecological and decorative purposes.
