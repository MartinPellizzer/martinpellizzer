Anneslia colletioides

Anneslia colletioides, commonly known as the golden crownbeard, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, yellow-orange inflorescences that resemble a crown. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves, and it produces small, tubular flowers that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Anneslia colletioides is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
