Athyrium filix-femina f. strictum

Athyrium filix-femina f. strictum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf lady fern, is a fern species characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds.

It belongs to the family Athyriaceae and is native to regions of Asia, including China and Japan. The plant features a compact, upright growth habit with dark green, lance-shaped leaves that are narrower than the typical form. It thrives in shady, moist environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.

The fronds are typically bi- or tri-pinnate, with a graceful, flowing appearance that enhances its aesthetic appeal.
