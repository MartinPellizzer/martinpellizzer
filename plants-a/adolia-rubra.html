Adolia rubra

Adolia rubra, commonly known as the red-stemmed adolia, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, reddish stems and small, clustered leaves. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to deep red, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in well-drained soils and is drought-tolerant, making it adaptable to harsh environments.

The leaves and flowers are sometimes used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.
