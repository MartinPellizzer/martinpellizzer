Asystasia malawiana

Asystasia malawiana, commonly known as the Malawi asystasia, is a flowering plant belonging to the Acanthaceae family.

It is native to eastern Africa, particularly Tanzania and Malawi, where it thrives in moist, shaded environments. The plant features upright, slender stems with opposite, ovate leaves that have a slightly hairy texture. Its showy, tubular flowers are typically blue or violet, arranged in spikes at the top of the stems.

Asystasia malawiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
