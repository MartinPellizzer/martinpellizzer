Anogra Pallida

Anogra pallida is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Andes. It is known for its pale, almost translucent petals that shimmer in the sunlight. This species thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found growing in rocky crevices. Due to its limited habitat and slow growth rate, it is considered vulnerable to environmental changes. Conservation efforts are crucial to ensure its survival in the wild.
