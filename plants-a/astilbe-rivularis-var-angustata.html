Astilbe rivularis var. angustata

Astilbe rivularis var. angustata, commonly known as narrowleaf water astilbe, is a herbaceous perennial native to eastern North America.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodland floors and stream sides. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces panicles of white to pinkish flowers in late summer. It has a fibrous root system and is known for its ability to thrive in wet soils.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking shade-tolerant plants.
