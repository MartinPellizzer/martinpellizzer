Adiantum roborowskii f. faberi

Adiantum roborowskii f. faberi, commonly known as the Faberi's maidenhair fern, is a varietal form of the common maidenhair fern.

It is a delicate, evergreen fern characterized by its finely divided, arching fronds that give it a graceful appearance. Native to the mountainous regions of China, this plant thrives in cool, humid environments with indirect light. Its leaves are typically dark green and have a leathery texture, which helps it retain moisture.

The plant is well-suited for shaded garden areas or as a houseplant, requiring well-draining soil and consistent moisture.
