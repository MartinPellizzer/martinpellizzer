Alectorolophus groenlandicus

Alectorolophus groenlandicus, commonly known as the Greenland lousewort, is a flowering plant native to the Arctic and subarctic regions.

It is a small herbaceous plant that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant produces clusters of small, pink or purple flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are often arranged in a rosette pattern and are covered in fine hairs.
