Ayenia tortilis

Ayenia tortilis, commonly known as the twisted ayenia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters. Ayenia tortilis is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils.

Its botanical structure supports its adaptation to arid environments, making it a resilient species in its native range.
