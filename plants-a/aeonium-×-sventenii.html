Aeonium × sventenii

Aeonium × sventenii, commonly known as the "Coral Cactus," is a hybrid succulent plant native to the Canary Islands.

It features rosettes of fleshy, triangular leaves that range in color from green to red, depending on light exposure. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is relatively drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with full sun.

Aeonium × sventenii is popular among succulent enthusiasts for its striking appearance and ease of care.
