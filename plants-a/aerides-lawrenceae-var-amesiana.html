Aerides lawrenceae var. amesiana

Aerides lawrenceae var. amesiana, commonly known as the Amesiana orchid, is a rare and exquisite orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant features long, arching inflorescences with numerous blooms, making it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in greenhouses or tropical gardens.
