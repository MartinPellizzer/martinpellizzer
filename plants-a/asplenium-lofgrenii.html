Asplenium lofgrenii

Asplenium lofgrenii, commonly known as Lofgren's spleenwort, is a small, evergreen fern native to the western United States.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky crevices. The plant has a compact rosette of lance-shaped, dark green leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its sporangia are clustered on the underside of the leaves, a characteristic feature of many ferns.

Asplenium lofgrenii is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in ecological habitats.
