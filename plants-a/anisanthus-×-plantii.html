Anisanthus × plantii

Anisanthus × plantii, commonly known as the hybrid anise plant, is a flowering plant in the Apiaceae family.

It is a cross between Anisanthus linearis and Anisanthus hispidus, resulting in a plant with distinctive aromatic foliage and small, yellowish flowers. The leaves are typically narrow and have a strong, licorice-like scent due to the presence of volatile oils. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and historical use in herbal medicine.
