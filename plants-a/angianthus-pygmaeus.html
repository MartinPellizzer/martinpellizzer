Angianthus pygmaeus

Angianthus pygmaeus, commonly known as the dwarf angianthus, is a small, flowering plant native to South Africa.

Due to its unique appearance and ornamental value, it is a popular choice for gardeners seeking to add a touch of exotic beauty to their landscapes.
