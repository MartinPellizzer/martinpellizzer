Ajax bicolor

Ajax bicolor, commonly known as the rainbow plant, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family, which is characterized by its succulent leaves and vibrant flowers. The plant features striking, colorful bracts that range from pink to purple, giving it its common name. It produces small, star-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.

Ajax bicolor thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
