Aspidosperma riedelii f. microphyllum

Aspidosperma riedelii f. microphyllum, commonly known as the small-leaved aspidosperma, is a shrub or small tree native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pink in color.
