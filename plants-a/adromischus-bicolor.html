Adromischus bicolor

Adromischus bicolor, commonly known as the two-color jade or the fairy lantern, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features small, fleshy, triangular leaves that are typically green with a reddish or purplish hue on the edges. The plant produces delicate, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Adromischus bicolor is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.
