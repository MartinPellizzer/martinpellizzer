Adiantopsis chlorophylla var. contracta

Adiantopsis chlorophylla var. contracta, commonly known as the narrow-leaf maidenhair fern, is a delicate fern species characterized by its finely divided, lance-shaped fronds.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia. The plant features small, bright green, fertile fronds that produce spores, typical of its reproductive strategy. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and requires high humidity and well-drained soil.

Its unique morphology and elegant appearance make it a popular choice for indoor and greenhouse cultivation.
