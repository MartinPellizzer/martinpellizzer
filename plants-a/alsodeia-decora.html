Alsodeia decora

Alsodeia decora, commonly known as the ornamental alsodeia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, clustered flowers that range from white to pink in color. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds, facilitating its propagation. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens and containers.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening due to its aesthetic appeal and adaptability.
