Anthurium cirinoi

Anthurium cirinoi, commonly known as the Cirino's flamingo flower, is a striking tropical plant native to Colombia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped spathe that ranges from red to orange in color. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers within a spadix, which is enclosed by the brightly colored spathe. Its foliage consists of large, glossy, dark green leaves that are often used in ornamental horticulture.

Anthurium cirinoi thrives in humid, warm environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.
