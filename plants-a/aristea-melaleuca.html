Aristea melaleuca

Aristea melaleuca, commonly known as the silver leaf aristea, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, mat-forming growth habit. The plant produces small, white to pinkish flowers that bloom in summer, adding ornamental value to gardens. Its leaves are silver-gray in color, covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss in arid conditions.

It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
