Ajax pseudonarcissus subsp. nobilis

Ajax pseudonarcissus subsp.

nobilis, commonly known as the noble daffodil, is a subspecies of the daffodil genus characterized by its elegant, trumpet-shaped flowers. It features bright yellow petals and a slender, upright stem that supports its showy blooms.

Native to southern Europe, this plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun. Its foliage consists of long, strap-like leaves that emerge in early spring.

The flowers typically bloom in late spring, adding a vibrant touch to gardens and natural landscapes.
