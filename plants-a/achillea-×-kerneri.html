Achillea × kerneri

Achillea × kerneri, commonly known as the hybrid yarrow, is a perennial flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its finely divided, fern-like leaves and flat, composite flower heads that range from white to pale pink. The plant typically grows to a height of 30–60 cm and thrives in well-drained soils in full sun.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and is often used in rock gardens and borders.
