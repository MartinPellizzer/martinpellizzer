Anthurium uasadiensis

Anthurium uasadiensis, commonly known as the velvet leaf anthurium, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are velvety to the touch and can vary in color from deep green to reddish-brown. The plant produces vibrant spadices, which are the spathes that enclose the true flowers, typically in shades of red or orange. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Anthurium uasadiensis is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
