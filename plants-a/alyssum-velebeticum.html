Alyssum velebeticum

Alyssum velebeticum, commonly known as the Canary Island alyssum, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Alyssum velebeticum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract beneficial insects.
