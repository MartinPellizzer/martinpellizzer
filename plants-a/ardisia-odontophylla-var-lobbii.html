Ardisia odontophylla var. lobbii

Ardisia odontophylla var. lobbii, commonly known as the Lobb's ardisia, is a compact evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, dark green elliptical leaves that are arranged alternately along its stems. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. Following flowering, it develops bright red, fleshy berries that are attractive to birds.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for shaded garden settings.
