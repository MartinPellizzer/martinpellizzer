Archontophoenix elegans

Archontophoenix elegans, commonly known as the silver crown palm, is a striking tropical plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and elegant, feather-like fronds that give it a graceful appearance. The plant produces large, showy inflorescences that are often used in ornamental horticulture. Its leaves are typically a deep green with a silvery underside, contributing to its ornamental value.

It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
