Anaphalis yunnanensis

Anaphalis yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan woollyhead, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its soft, fuzzy leaves and showy, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in alpine meadows and rocky areas at high altitudes. Its flowers are usually white or pale yellow, and they bloom during the summer months.

Anaphalis yunnanensis is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and is also studied for its ecological role in mountain ecosystems.
