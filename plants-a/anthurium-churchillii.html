Anthurium churchillii

Anthurium churchillii, commonly known as the Churchill's arrowhead, is a striking tropical plant native to Central America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often a vibrant green with a glossy texture. The plant produces striking spadices, which are modified leaves that enclose the actual flowers, typically in red or orange hues. Anthurium churchillii thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical landscapes.
