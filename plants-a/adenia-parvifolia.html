Adenia parvifolia

Adenia parvifolia, commonly known as the small-flowered adenia, is a species of flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Passifloraceae, which includes well-known genera like Passiflora. The plant is characterized by its woody stems and heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture. It produces small, white or greenish flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences.

Adenia parvifolia is often found in moist, shaded habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
