Aristida protensa

Aristida protensa, commonly known as the slender threeawn, is a grass species native to parts of Africa and the Middle East.

It is characterized by its fine, wiry stems and small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, contributing to its drought-resistant nature.

Aristida protensa is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil and support local wildlife.
