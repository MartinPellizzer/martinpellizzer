Aster dregei

Aster dregei, commonly known as the Cape aster, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features showy, daisy-like flowers that range in color from white to purple, often with a yellow center. Aster dregei typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
