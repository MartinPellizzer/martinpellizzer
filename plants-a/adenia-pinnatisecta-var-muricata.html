Adenia pinnatisecta var. muricata

Adenia pinnatisecta var. muricata, commonly known as the rough-leaved adenia, is a climbing shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its compound, pinnatisect leaves that are often covered in a rough, textured surface. The plant produces striking yellow flowers with prominent, fleshy petals, which are followed by distinctive, berry-like fruits. Its unique foliage and floral structure make it a notable species in botanical studies.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforest undergrowth.
