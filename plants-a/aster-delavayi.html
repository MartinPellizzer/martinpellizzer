Aster delavayi

Aster delavayi, commonly known as Delavay's aster, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically features showy blue to purple ray flowers surrounding a central disk of smaller florets. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine meadows.

Aster delavayi is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
