Acriopsis javanica var. floribunda

Acriopsis javanica var. floribunda, commonly known as the Javanese cowpea, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its erect, branched stems and palmately lobed leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in disturbed soils.

Its botanical features make it a useful species for ecological studies and potential agricultural applications.
