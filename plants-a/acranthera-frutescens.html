Acranthera frutescens

Acranthera frutescens, commonly known as the slender acranthera, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous species that typically grows in open, sandy or gravelly soils. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers arranged in a spike-like inflorescence. It is characterized by its erect, branched stems and narrow, linear leaves.

Acranthera frutescens is often found in disturbed habitats and is adapted to thrive in arid or semi-arid conditions.
