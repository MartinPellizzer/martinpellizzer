Afrothismia mhoroana

Afrothismia mhoroana, commonly known as the Mahango rock fig, is a rare and endemic plant species native to the arid regions of Namibia.

The plant produces small, inedible fruits that are rarely consumed by local wildlife. Its unique morphology and restricted distribution make it a subject of interest for botanists studying succulent flora.
