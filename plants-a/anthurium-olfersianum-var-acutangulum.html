Anthurium olfersianum var. acutangulum

Anthurium olfersianum var. acutangulum, commonly known as the Acute-angled Anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped spathes that can range in color from bright red to deep maroon. The plant produces striking, elongated spadices that are typically yellow or orange, contrasting with the spatulate leaves. It thrives in humid, warm environments with indirect light and requires well-draining soil.

This variety is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in indoor conditions.
