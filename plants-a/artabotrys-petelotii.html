Artabotrys petelotii

Artabotrys petelotii, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in pendulous clusters, resembling a cascade of small bells. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are ovate to elliptic in shape. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The fruit is a woody capsule that contains several seeds.
