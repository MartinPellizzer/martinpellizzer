Ardisia Zarceroana

Ardisia zarceroana is a rare and intriguing plant native to the cloud forests of Central America. It belongs to the Ericaceae family, known for its ornamental and ecological significance. This species is characterized by its glossy leaves and small, dark berries that attract various wildlife. Due to habitat loss, it faces threats to its survival in the wild. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this unique botanical treasure.
