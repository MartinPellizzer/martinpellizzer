Acidanthera lomatensis

Acidanthera lomatensis, commonly known as the mountain lomatia, is a flowering plant native to southern South America.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or coastal environments. The plant produces showy, pink to purple flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a hairy texture.

The species is noted for its ecological importance in its native habitat, supporting various pollinators and contributing to local biodiversity.
