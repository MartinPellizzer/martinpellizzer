Angraecum myrianthum

Angraecum myrianthum, commonly known as the Madagascar star orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale pink with a green throat. The plant has long, slender stems and produces numerous pendulous flowers that bloom during the rainy season. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture from the humid forest environment.

Angraecum myrianthum is a popular species among orchid enthusiasts due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
