Anemone wahlenbergii

Anemone wahlenbergii, commonly known as the Swedish anemone, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to northern Europe.

It features showy, white to pinkish flowers that bloom in early spring, typically from March to May. The plant has basal leaves that are palmately lobed and covered in a dense layer of fine, white hairs. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking native, ornamental plants.
