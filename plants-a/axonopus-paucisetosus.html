Axonopus paucisetosus

Axonopus paucisetosus, commonly known as the slender axonopus, is a grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its fine, tufted growth habit and narrow, blade-like leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or along stream banks. Its inflorescence consists of dense, cylindrical spikelets that produce small, wind-pollinated flowers.

Axonopus paucisetosus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping due to its low maintenance requirements.
