Artemisia schimperi

Artemisia schimperi, commonly known as Ethiopian wormwood, is a perennial shrub native to the highlands of Ethiopia.

It is characterized by its aromatic, silver-gray foliage and small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often growing at elevations above 2,000 meters. Its leaves contain essential oils that contribute to its strong, pungent scent.

Artemisia schimperi is also valued for its traditional medicinal uses in local communities.
