Actinostemon unciformis

Actinostemon unciformis, commonly known as the broad-leafed daisy, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa and the Middle East.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and serrated leaves. The plant features showy, daisy-like flowers with white petals and a central disc of yellow florets. Its leaves are broad, lance-shaped, and often have a waxy coating.

Actinostemon unciformis is typically found in arid or semi-arid environments, thriving in well-drained soils.
