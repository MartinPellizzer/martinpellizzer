Anneslia rupestris

Anneslia rupestris, commonly known as the rock anneslia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and is adapted to arid environments. It produces showy yellow flowers that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Its compact growth habit and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
