Anthistiria strigosa

Anthistiria strigosa, commonly known as the rough-stemmed anthistiria, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in grasslands and rocky outcrops, preferring well-drained soils. The plant features slender, hairy stems and small, lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture. Its flowers are clustered in dense inflorescences and are usually pale yellow to greenish in color.

Anthistiria strigosa is known for its adaptability to arid conditions and its role in supporting local insect populations.
