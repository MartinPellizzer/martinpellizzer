Argyreia verrucosohispida

Argyreia verrucosohispida, commonly known as the velvet bean or red rattleplant, is a climbing shrub native to tropical regions of Asia and Africa.

It features large, heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and prominent veins. The plant produces showy, red or orange flowers that are often clustered in racemes. Its fruit is a spiny capsule that contains several kidney-shaped seeds.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in various cultures.
