Amphinomia Genistoides

Amphinomia genistoides is a unique flowering plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa. It is known for its striking yellow flowers and its ability to thrive in harsh, dry environments. This species belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes many legumes and nitrogen-fixing plants. Its distinctive appearance makes it a popular choice for xeriscaping and rock gardens. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively obscure in mainstream horticulture.
