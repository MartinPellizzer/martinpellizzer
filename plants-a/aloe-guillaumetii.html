Aloe guillaumetii

Aloe guillaumetii, commonly known as the Guillaumet's aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a waxy coating, which helps it retain moisture in arid environments. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow flowers, blooming during the summer months. It is well adapted to rocky and sandy soils, thriving in full sun.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
