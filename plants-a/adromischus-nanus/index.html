Adromischus Nanus

Adromischus nanus is a small, succulent plant native to southern Africa. It is known for its compact, rounded shape and fleshy, textured leaves that resemble tiny succulents. This plant is popular among growers for its ease of care and ability to thrive in low-light conditions. Its unique appearance makes it a favorite for indoor gardens and rockeries. Despite its size, Adromischus nanus can produce small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white.
