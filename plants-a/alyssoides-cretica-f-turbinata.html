Alyssoides cretica f. turbinata

Alyssoides cretica f. turbinata, commonly known as the Turbinated Cretan Alyssum, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers. The leaves are linear to lanceolate, often with a slightly hairy texture. Native to the Mediterranean region, it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
