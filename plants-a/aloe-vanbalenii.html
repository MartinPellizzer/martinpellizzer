Aloe vanbalenii

Aloe vanbalenii, commonly known as the Van Balen aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, with a waxy coating to store water. The plant produces tall, branched flower stalks that bear yellowish-green flowers, which bloom in late winter to early spring. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Aloe vanbalenii is valued for its ornamental beauty and has some traditional uses in herbal medicine.
