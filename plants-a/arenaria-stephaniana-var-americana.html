Arenaria stephaniana var. americana

Arenaria stephaniana var. americana, commonly known as American sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to North America.

It typically thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in dry, open habitats such as dunes, roadsides, and disturbed areas. The plant has small, white or pink flowers that bloom in early spring, and its leaves are usually fleshy and succulent, adapted to conserve water. It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family, which includes other flowering plants with showy blooms.

This variety is known for its hardiness and ability to colonize areas with minimal care.
