Aponogeton elongatus

Aponogeton elongatus, commonly known as the long-stemmed arrowhead, is a aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features long, slender leaves that are arrowhead-shaped, typically floating on the water's surface. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom above the water, attracting pollinators. It thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments.

Aponogeton elongatus is often used in water gardens and pond decorations due to its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.
