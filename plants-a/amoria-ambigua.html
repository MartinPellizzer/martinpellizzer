Amoria ambigua

Amoria ambigua, commonly known as the ambiguous amoria, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Amoryaceae.

It is native to parts of Southeast Asia, particularly Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in humid, tropical environments and is often found in forest understories.
