Acmispon maritimus var. maritimus

Acmispon maritimus var. maritimus, commonly known as sea rocket, is a flowering plant native to coastal areas of the Mediterranean and Atlantic coasts.

It is a low-growing annual or biennial plant that thrives in sandy or gravelly soils. The plant features small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. Its leaves are pinnately divided and often covered in a fine layer of salt-excreting hairs.

It plays an important role in stabilizing dunes and providing habitat for coastal wildlife.
