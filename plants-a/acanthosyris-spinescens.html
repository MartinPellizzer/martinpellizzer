Acanthosyris spinescens

Acanthosyris spinescens, commonly known as the spiny acanthosyris, is a succulent plant native to the arid regions of South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, spiny foliage that serves as a defense mechanism against herbivores. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color, blooming in late summer. Its leaves are thick and fleshy, adapted to store water in its native desert environment.

Acanthosyris spinescens is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and resilience.
