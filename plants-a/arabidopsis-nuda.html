Arabidopsis nuda

Arabidopsis nuda, commonly known as the naked Arabidopsis, is a species of flowering plant in the Brassicaceae family.

It is a small, annual herb that typically grows to a height of about 10-20 centimeters. The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and small, white or pale yellow flowers. It is native to parts of Europe and western Asia.

Arabidopsis nuda is often studied in botanical research due to its simple genome and rapid life cycle.
