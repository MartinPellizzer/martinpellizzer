Alstroemeria ligtu var. heterophylla

Alstroemeria ligtu var. heterophylla, commonly known as the red alstroemeria, is a species native to South America, particularly Chile.

It is characterized by its vibrant red flowers with a striking contrast of white and yellow markings. The plant typically grows as a herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of about 30 to 60 centimeters. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
