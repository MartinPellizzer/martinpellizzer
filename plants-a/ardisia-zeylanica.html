Ardisia zeylanica

Ardisia zeylanica, commonly known as the Ceylon evergreen.

It is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Sri Lanka and parts of Southeast Asia. The plant features glossy, dark green leaves and produces small, white, fragrant flowers. Its berries are red when mature and contain seeds, making it an attractive ornamental plant.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
