Adenodolichos macrothyrsus

Adenodolichos macrothyrsus, commonly known as the large thyrse bean, is a climbing legume native to tropical Africa.

It features compound leaves with elongated leaflets and produces distinctive, showy flowers that are typically yellow or orange. The plant bears pods that contain several seeds, which are often used in traditional medicine. Its botanical characteristics include a robust root system and a tendency to form dense foliage, making it adaptable to various soil types.

It is valued for both its ornamental appeal and potential medicinal properties.
