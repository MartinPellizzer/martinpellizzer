Anthurium lancea

Anthurium lancea, commonly known as the lance-shaped anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its elongated, lanceolate leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy surface. The plant produces striking spadices, which are specialized inflorescences that give it its ornamental value. Anthurium lancea thrives in humid, warm environments with bright, indirect light.

Its unique foliage and exotic flowers make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
