Allium mobilense

Allium mobilense, commonly known as the Mobile onion, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a member of the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its basal rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves. Allium mobilense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in native plant gardens.
