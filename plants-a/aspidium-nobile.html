Aspidium nobile

Aspidium nobile, commonly known as the noble water fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds that are typically light green in color. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in rocky or forested areas. Its rhizomes are creeping and help anchor the plant while absorbing nutrients from the soil.

Aspidium nobile is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
