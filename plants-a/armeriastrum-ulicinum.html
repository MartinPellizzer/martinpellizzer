Armeriastrum ulicinum

Armeriastrum ulicinum, commonly known as the coastal sand verbena, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of California and Baja California.

It is a low-growing annual herb with slender stems and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant thrives in sandy, well-drained soils and is adapted to coastal climates with high salinity and strong winds.
