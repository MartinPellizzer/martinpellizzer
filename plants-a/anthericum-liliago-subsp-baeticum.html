Anthericum liliago subsp. baeticum

baeticum, commonly known as the Baetican lily, is a rare and endemic subspecies native to the Iberian Peninsula. It is a bulbous perennial plant characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and striking white or pale pink flowers.
