Adicea trichosanthes

Adicea trichosanthes, commonly known as the Chinese yam or wild yam, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Asia.

It belongs to the family Cucurbitaceae, which includes cucumbers and squash. The plant features heart-shaped leaves and produces yellow flowers that develop into oval, tuberous fruits. Its roots are often harvested for their medicinal properties.

Adicea trichosanthes is valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
