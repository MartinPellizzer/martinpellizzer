Acacia hebeclada subsp. tristis

Acacia hebeclada subsp.

tristis, commonly known as the slender acacia, is a shrub native to parts of southern Africa. It typically grows in coastal and sandy soils, thriving in well-drained environments.

The plant features small, bipinnate leaves and produces yellow flowers in globular heads. It is often used in ornamental gardening due to its hardy nature and attractive foliage.

The subspecies is distinguished by its slender growth habit and unique floral structure.
