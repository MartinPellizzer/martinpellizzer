Appendicula inermis

Appendicula inermis, commonly known as the slender bladderwort, is a small aquatic plant belonging to the family Lentibulariaceae.

It is characterized by its tiny, bladder-like structures called bladders, which are used to capture small aquatic organisms. Appendicula inermis is a carnivorous plant, relying on its bladders to supplement its nutrient intake in nutrient-poor waters.
