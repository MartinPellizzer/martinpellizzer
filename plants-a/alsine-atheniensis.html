Alsine atheniensis

Alsine atheniensis, commonly known as the Athens alsine, is a species of flowering plant in the family Amaranthaceae.

It is a small annual herb that typically grows in dry, open habitats such as rocky soils and disturbed areas. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered with fine hairs.

Alsine atheniensis is native to southern Europe, particularly Greece, and is adapted to Mediterranean climates.
