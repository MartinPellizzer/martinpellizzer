Adromischus liebenbergii subsp. liebenbergii

Adromischus liebenbergii subsp.

liebenbergii, commonly known as the "Coral Cactus," is a succulent plant native to South Africa. It features compact, rounded stems that are typically tinged with red or pink hues, especially under direct sunlight.

The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow to orange during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its fleshy leaves.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
