Actinella palmeri

Actinella palmeri, commonly known as Palmer's actinella, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils in arid environments. Its leaves are usually narrow and gray-green, adapted to reduce water loss.

Actinella palmeri is often found in desert scrub and chaparral habitats.
