Agathosma microcarpa

Agathosma microcarpa, commonly known as the Cape geranium, is a small, evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It features narrow, aromatic leaves that are typically covered in fine, white hairs. The plant produces small, tubular flowers in shades of pink to purple, which bloom in spring and summer. It is well adapted to dry, sandy soils and full sun exposure.

Due to its fragrant properties, it is often used in herbal medicine and as a source of essential oils.
