Amaryllis equestris var. flore-simplici

Amaryllis equestris var. flore-simplici, commonly known as the red spider lily, is a striking bulbous plant native to the eastern United States.

It features tall, strap-like leaves and produces clusters of vibrant red flowers with a distinctive spider-like appearance. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is a member of the Amaryllidaceae family.

This variety is known for its resilience and ability to naturalize in suitable climates.
