Abelia sanguinea

Abelia sanguinea, commonly known as the redleaf abelia, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern North America.

It features attractive, fragrant, tubular flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that turn vibrant red in the fall, adding ornamental value. It prefers partial shade to full sun and well-drained soil.

Abelia sanguinea is often used in gardens for its ornamental appeal and moderate care requirements.
