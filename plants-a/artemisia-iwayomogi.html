Artemisia iwayomogi

Artemisia iwayomogi, commonly known as Japanese mugwort, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, gray-green foliage. The plant produces small yellow flowers that are typically clustered in composite heads. Artemisia iwayomogi is often found in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and streambanks.
