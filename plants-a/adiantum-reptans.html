Adiantum reptans

Adiantum reptans, commonly known as the walking fern, is a species of fern that belongs to the family Adiantaceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive fronds that curl upward, giving it a unique, almost prehensile appearance. The plant is native to the southeastern United States and thrives in moist, shaded environments. Its rhizomes grow along the ground, allowing it to spread and form dense mats.

Adiantum reptans is often used in ornamental horticulture due to its attractive foliage and ease of cultivation.
