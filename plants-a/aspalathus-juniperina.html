Aspalathus juniperina

Aspalathus juniperina, commonly known as the silver leaf bush, is a small evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its narrow, silvery-gray leaves that provide a striking visual contrast to its small, bright yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and is adapted to sandy, well-drained soils. It is often used in ornamental gardening due to its drought tolerance and attractive foliage.

Aspalathus juniperina is also valued for its ability to thrive in harsh, coastal environments.
