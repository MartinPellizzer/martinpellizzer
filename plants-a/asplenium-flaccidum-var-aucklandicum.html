Asplenium flaccidum var. aucklandicum

Asplenium flaccidum var. aucklandicum, commonly known as the Auckland fern, is a fern native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the family Aspleniaceae and is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds that are typically light green in color. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories. Its rhizomes are fibrous and help anchor it to rocky or soil substrates.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated as a houseplant.
