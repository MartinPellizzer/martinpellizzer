Agave polianthes

Agave polianthes, commonly known as the milky agave, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a succulent plant characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are thick and fleshy, often with a bluish-green hue. The plant produces a tall flowering stalk that bears clusters of white or pale yellow flowers. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
