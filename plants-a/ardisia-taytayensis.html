Ardisia taytayensis

Ardisia taytayensis, commonly known as the Taytay arrowroot, is a shrub native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, white flowers that are followed by bright red berries. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
