Adenophora polyantha var. media

Adenophora polyantha var. media, commonly known as the Chinese bellflower, is a flowering plant native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in woodland areas and thrives in partial shade. It has lance-shaped leaves and produces a cluster of small, greenish-yellow flowers in the summer.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens.
