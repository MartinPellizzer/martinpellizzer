Amaryllis paranaensis

Amaryllis paranaensis, commonly known as the Parana amaryllis, is a flowering plant native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that come in shades of red, yellow, and white. The plant produces large, strap-like leaves that are often used for ornamental purposes. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Amaryllis paranaensis is popular among gardeners for its vibrant blooms and relatively easy care requirements.
