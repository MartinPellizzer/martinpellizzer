Abbotia Pumila

Abbotia pumila is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the southeastern united states. it belongs to the gentian family and is known for its small, pale yellow blooms. this plant thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories. due to habitat loss, it is considered endangered and requires conservation efforts. its unique appearance and ecological importance make it a fascinating subject for botanical study.
