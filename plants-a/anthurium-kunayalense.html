Anthurium kunayalense

Anthurium kunayalense, commonly known as the Yungas Anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the humid forests of Ecuador.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are often dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces an inflorescence consisting of a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe, which can range from white to reddish hues. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and requires high humidity and well-draining soil to flourish.

Anthurium kunayalense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in botanical gardens.
