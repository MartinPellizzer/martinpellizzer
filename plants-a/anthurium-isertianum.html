Anthurium isertianum

Anthurium isertianum, commonly known as the velvet leaf anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped, velvety green leaves. The plant produces striking spadix inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, typically in shades of red or orange. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Anthurium isertianum is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
