Amellus speciosus

Amellus speciosus, commonly known as the showy amellus, is a striking flowering plant native to the temperate regions of the northeastern United States.

The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks, where it thrives in partial to full shade. Its leaves are ovate with a smooth texture and a distinctive reddish or purple hue, adding to its ornamental value.

Amellus speciosus is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal and ecological benefits, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies.
