Aubrieta olympica

Aubrieta olympica, commonly known as the Olympic aubrieta, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Greece.

It features compact, mat-forming growth with clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has dark green, lance-shaped leaves that are arranged in a basal rosette. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens and alpine settings.

Aubrieta olympica is valued for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
