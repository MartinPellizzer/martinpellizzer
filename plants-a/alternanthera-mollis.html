Alternanthera mollis

Alternanthera mollis, commonly known as red leafAlternanthera, is a herbaceous plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety red or purple leaves that contrast with its green stems. The plant typically grows in wetland or marshy environments, thriving in moist, nutrient-rich soil. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are often overlooked in favor of its vibrant foliage.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in water gardens and as a houseplant.
