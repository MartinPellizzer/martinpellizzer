Anthurium lapoanum

Anthurium lapoanum, commonly known as the Lapoanum arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are often a deep green with a glossy texture. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-like inflorescences surrounded by colorful spathe petals, typically in shades of red or orange. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Anthurium lapoanum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in botanical gardens.
