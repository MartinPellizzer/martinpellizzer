Agrostula

Agrostula, commonly known as the marsh hawksbeard, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in wet or damp habitats such as marshes and meadows. The plant produces yellow composite flowers that consist of many small florets, typical of the daisy family. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs, and it can grow up to 1 meter tall.

Agrostula is known for its ability to thrive in moist environments, making it a common sight in wetland ecosystems.
