Abelia forrestii var. gracilenta

Abelia forrestii var. gracilenta, commonly known as the slender abelia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features slender, arching stems and produces clusters of fragrant, white to pinkish flowers in the summer. The leaves are ovate to elliptical, with a smooth texture and a glossy green surface. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained, moist soil.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various garden conditions.
