Acnistus spinescens

Acnistus spinescens, commonly known as the spiny acnistus, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree characterized by its woody stems and dense, thorny foliage. The plant produces small, white or greenish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are typically opposite, simple, and have a rough, sometimes hairy texture.

Acnistus spinescens is often found in disturbed habitats and is adapted to dry or semi-arid conditions.
