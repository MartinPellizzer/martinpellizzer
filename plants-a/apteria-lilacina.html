Apteria lilacina

Apteria lilacina, commonly known as the lilac-leafed apteria, is a species of flowering plant native to certain regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, lilac-hued leaves that give it its common name. The plant typically grows as a small shrub, reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters. Its flowers are small and clustered, often appearing in shades of white and pink.
