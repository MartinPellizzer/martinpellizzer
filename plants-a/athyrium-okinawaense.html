Athyrium okinawaense

Athyrium okinawaense, commonly known as the Okinawa shield fern, is a species of fern native to the Ryukyu Islands of Japan.

It belongs to the family Athyriaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, shield-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as forest undergrowth or rocky crevices. Its leaves are bioluminescent, a unique trait that aids in attracting certain nocturnal pollinators.

The plant's ornamental value has led to its cultivation in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
