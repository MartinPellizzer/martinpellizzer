Axanthes griffithiana

Axanthes griffithiana, commonly known as the Kashmir rose or Himalayan rose, is a flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It is a shrub that produces showy, pink to white flowers with a distinctive, fragrant scent. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves and is adapted to cold, mountainous climates. It is often found in rocky soils and is valued for its ornamental beauty.

Axanthes griffithiana is also noted for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal medicine.
