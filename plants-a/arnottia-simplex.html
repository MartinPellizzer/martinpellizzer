Arnottia simplex

Arnottia simplex, commonly known as the small-flowered bellflower, is a herbaceous perennial native to eastern North America.

It features delicate, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale blue, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along its stems. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Arnottia simplex is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance, providing nectar for pollinators.
