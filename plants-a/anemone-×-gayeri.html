Anemone × gayeri

Anemone × gayeri, commonly known as the hybrid anemone, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features showy, cup-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has finely divided, fern-like leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in well-drained soils and partial to full sunlight, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Anemone × gayeri is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to naturalize in suitable climates.
