Arabis pubicalyx var. soyensis

Arabis pubicalyx var. soyensis, commonly known as the soybean Arabis, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, white to pale purple flowers and basal rosette of hairy, lance-shaped leaves. The plant is native to East Asia and thrives in moist, well-drained soils. It is often found in forest understories and along streambanks.

Its ornamental value and adaptability make it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
