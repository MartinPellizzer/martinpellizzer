Agoseris frondifera

Agoseris frondifera, commonly known as the fronded goat's beard, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its basal rosette of finely divided, fern-like leaves. The plant produces showy, yellow to orange composite flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and woodland edges.

Agoseris frondifera is adapted to arid conditions and is considered a perennial herb.
