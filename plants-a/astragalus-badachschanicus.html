Astragalus badachschanicus

Astragalus badachschanicus, commonly known as the Badakshan milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia, particularly Afghanistan and Pakistan.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, pea-like flowers. The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments, thriving in well-drained soils.

It plays a role in soil stabilization and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
