Anthurium sarmentosum

Anthurium sarmentosum, commonly known as the creeping anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the Caribbean and northern South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant produces striking spathes that range in color from red to yellow, often with a contrasting pattern. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Anthurium sarmentosum is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
