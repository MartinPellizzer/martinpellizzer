Allophylus cobbe f. racemosus

Allophylus cobbe f. racemosus, commonly known as the racemose allophylus, is a botanical variant of the genus Allophylus.

It is a small evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia. The plant is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and small, oval-shaped leaves. Its flowers are typically arranged in racemose inflorescences, giving it its common name.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its foliage and seasonal flowering.
