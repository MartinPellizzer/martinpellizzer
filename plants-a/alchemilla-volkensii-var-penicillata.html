Alchemilla volkensii var. penicillata

Alchemilla volkensii var. penicillata, commonly known as the Penicillate Lady's Mantle, is a perennial plant native to East Africa.

It features basal rosettes of silver-green, velvety leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces upright flowering stems bearing clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Its ornamental value comes from its attractive foliage and delicate flowers, making it a popular choice for shaded garden settings.
