Adenia hederifolia

Adenia hederifolia, commonly known as the creeping Adenia, is a climbing shrub native to tropical Africa.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are followed by berries containing a single seed. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical characteristics make it an interesting subject for horticultural study and conservation efforts.
