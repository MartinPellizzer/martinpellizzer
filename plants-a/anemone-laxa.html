Anemone laxa

Anemone laxa, commonly known as the lax anemone, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to parts of Asia and North America.

It typically grows in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features showy, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its foliage consists of heart-shaped, lobed leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

Anemone laxa is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in partially shaded conditions.
