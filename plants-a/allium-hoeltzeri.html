Allium hoeltzeri

Allium hoeltzeri, commonly known as the Hoeltzer's onion, is a perennial bulbous plant native to Central Asia.

It features slender, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of pale purple to white flowers. The plant has a compact growth habit and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Allium hoeltzeri is drought-tolerant and suitable for rock gardens or as a border plant.
