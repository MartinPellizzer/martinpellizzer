Alstonia fragrans

Alstonia fragrans, commonly known as the fragrant alstonia or the Indian angel's trumpet, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. The plant has smooth, grayish bark and palmately lobed leaves that are opposite in arrangement. Its fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release numerous seeds.

Alstonia fragrans is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has traditional uses in some cultures.
