Arachne lanceolata

Arachne lanceolata, commonly known as the lanceleaf arachis, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its elongated, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are clustered in racemes, followed by pod-like fruits containing edible seeds. Arachne lanceolata is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional uses in local communities.
