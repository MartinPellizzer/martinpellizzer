Aeranthes brevivaginans

Aeranthes brevivaginans, commonly known as the narrow sheath orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a terrestrial herb with a short, stout rhizome and linear, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, white to pale green flowers arranged in a dense spike. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices.

Its botanical features reflect adaptations to its specific ecological niche within the tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.
