Amaryllis × costata

Amaryllis × costata, commonly known as the Costata hybrid, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that come in a range of colors, including red, orange, and yellow. The plant produces multiple flower stems from a single bulb, each bearing several blooms. Its foliage consists of strap-like, evergreen leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings.

Amaryllis × costata is well-suited for gardens and containers, thriving in full sun to partial shade.
