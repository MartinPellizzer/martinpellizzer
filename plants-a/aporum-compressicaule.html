Aporum compressicaule

Aporum compressicaule, commonly known as the narrow-stemmed aporum, is a tropical climbing plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, cylindrical stems that often trail along the ground or climb supports. The leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a smooth, waxy surface, while the flowers are small and clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant.
