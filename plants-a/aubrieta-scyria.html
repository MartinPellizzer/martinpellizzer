Aubrieta scyria

Aubrieta scyria, commonly known as the alpine aubrieta, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the mountains of Central Asia.

It features dense clusters of small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has silvery-gray foliage that is covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and alpine settings.

Aubrieta scyria is valued for its ornamental value and hardiness in cooler climates.
