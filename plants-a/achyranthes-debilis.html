Achyranthes debilis

Achyranthes debilis, commonly known as slender knotweed, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, greenish flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Achyranthes debilis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
