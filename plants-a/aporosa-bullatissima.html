Aporosa bullatissima

Aporosa bullatissima, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, glossy green foliage and clusters of showy, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in summer. The plant produces small, round fruits that are typically reddish-brown when mature. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Aporosa bullatissima is also valued for its use in traditional medicine and as a source of timber.
