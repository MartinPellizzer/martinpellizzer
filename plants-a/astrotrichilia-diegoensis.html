Astrotrichilia diegoensis

Astrotrichilia diegoensis, commonly known as Diego's aster.

It is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of South America, particularly in Argentina and Chile. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. It produces small, star-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or white, which bloom during the dry season.

Astrotrichilia diegoensis is adapted to survive in harsh, low-nutrient soils and can tolerate extreme temperature fluctuations.
