Abies fabri var. ziyuanensis

Abies fabri var. ziyuanensis, commonly known as the Ziyuan fir, is a rare and endemic conifer native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the genus Abies, which includes various species of fir trees characterized by their conical shape and needle-like leaves. The foliage of this variety is typically dark green and densely packed, contributing to its ornamental value. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in high-altitude forests.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a species of conservation concern.
