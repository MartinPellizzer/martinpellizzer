Astrophytum aureispinum

Astrophytum aureispinum, commonly known as the golden star cactus, is a species of cactus native to the Sonoran Desert in North America.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems that are covered in small, yellow spines. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months, which are followed by red, fleshy fruits. Its succulent structure allows it to store water, making it well adapted to arid environments.

The golden star cactus is often cultivated for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
