Aidia dilleniacea

Aidia dilleniacea, commonly known as the dilleniaceae or the dilleniaceae plant, is a flowering plant species native to South America.

It belongs to the family Dilleniaceae and is characterized by its showy, often brightly colored flowers. The plant typically has simple, alternate leaves that are usually ovate or elliptic in shape. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in moist, shaded environments.

The flowers are usually bisexual, with both stamens and pistils present, facilitating self-pollination.
