Anthurium libonianum

Anthurium libonianum, commonly known as the Libonian arrowroot, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive spathe and spadix inflorescence. The plant features broad, heart-shaped leaves with a waxy texture and deep green coloration. It thrives in humid, shady environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical landscapes.
