Agrostis gigantea var. gouxii

Agrostis gigantea var. gouxii, commonly known as Goux's silvergrass, is a perennial grass species native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-blue foliage and compact growth habit, making it a popular ornamental plant. The plant produces small, pale green to brownish spikelets that are clustered along the stems. It thrives in well-drained soils and full to partial sunlight, often used in landscaping for its ornamental value.

Its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements make it suitable for rock gardens and as a ground cover.
