Andropogon myosurus

Andropogon myosurus, commonly known as tall bluestem, is a perennial grass native to North America.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and narrow, blue-green leaves that turn reddish in the fall. This plant is often found in prairie ecosystems and is adapted to full sun and well-drained soils. It plays an important role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various wildlife species.

Andropogon myosurus is also valued for its ornamental appeal and use in landscaping.
