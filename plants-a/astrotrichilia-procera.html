Astrotrichilia procera

Astrotrichilia procera, commonly known as the giant candle plant, is a striking member of the Asteraceae family.

It is native to the arid regions of southern Africa, where it thrives in rocky and sandy soils. The plant is characterized by its tall, candle-like stems that can grow up to 3 meters in height. Its leaves are narrow and covered in a dense layer of fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.

The showy yellow flowers appear in clusters at the top of the plant, attracting pollinators and adding to its ornamental appeal.
