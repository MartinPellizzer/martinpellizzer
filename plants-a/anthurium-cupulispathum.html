Anthurium cupulispathum

Anthurium cupulispathum, commonly known as the cupulispathum anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive spathe and spadix inflorescence. The plant features broad, heart-shaped leaves that are usually dark green with a glossy texture. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, with the showy spathe serving as a protective structure for the spadix.

Anthurium cupulispathum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in humid, shaded environments.
