Ardisia sinoaustralis var. longicalyx

Ardisia sinoaustralis var. longicalyx, commonly known as the long-leaf Chinese evergreen, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green and arranged oppositely along the stems. The plant produces small, white to pale pink flowers followed by bright red berries, which are an important food source for birds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for shade gardens and indoor cultivation.
