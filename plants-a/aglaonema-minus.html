Aglaonema minus

Aglaonema minus, commonly known as the Chinese Evergreen, is a striking ornamental plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, variegated foliage that features shades of green, white, and pink. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and can grow up to 30 centimeters in length.

Aglaonema minus is also appreciated for its air-purifying qualities and its ability to add a touch of elegance to any space.
