Arisaema roxburghii var. kunstleri

Arisaema roxburghii var. kunstleri, commonly known as the Chinese arrowhead, is a herbaceous perennial native to Southeast Asia.

It features a distinctive, arrow-shaped leaf that is often deeply lobed, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces an unusual, spadix-like inflorescence surrounded by a showy, leaf-like spathe. Its botanical structure includes a rhizomatous root system that allows it to thrive in moist, shaded environments.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and unique flowering display.
