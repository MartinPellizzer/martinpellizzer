Asplenium ruta-muraria var. ohionis

Asplenium ruta-muraria var. ohionis, commonly known as the Ohio spleenwort, is a fern species native to North America.

It is a small, epiphytic or lithophytic plant that typically grows on rocks or tree trunks. The plant has delicate, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, hairy texture. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forested areas.

Its unique morphology and adaptability make it a notable species in botanical studies.
