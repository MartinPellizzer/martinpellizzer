Ayenia melastomifolia

Ayenia melastomifolia, commonly known as the Brazilian violet or violet of the Amazon, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its showy, violet-colored flowers. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in shaded forest understories. Its leaves are usually opposite and have a glossy, dark green appearance.

Ayenia melastomifolia is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
