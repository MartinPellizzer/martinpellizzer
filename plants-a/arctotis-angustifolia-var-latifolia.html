Arctotis angustifolia var. latifolia

Arctotis angustifolia var. latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf arctotis, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves that are slightly broader than the typical variety, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces vibrant, daisy-like flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and adaptability to various growing conditions.
