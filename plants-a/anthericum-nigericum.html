Anthericum nigericum

Anthericum nigericum, commonly known as the black anthericum, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Africa.

It features a rosette of dark green, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, hairy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white or greenish flowers arranged in a dense, spike-like inflorescence. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage.
