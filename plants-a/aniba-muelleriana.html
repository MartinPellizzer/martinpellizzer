Aniba muelleriana

Aniba muelleriana, commonly known as the Mueller's aniba, is a small evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurels and avocado trees. The plant features aromatic, lance-shaped leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. It produces small, yellowish flowers followed by elliptical, dark blue berries.

Aniba muelleriana is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been studied for its potential pharmacological properties.
