Aulax pallasia

Aulax pallasia, commonly known as the Pallas's aulax, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayas and parts of Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub that typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or yellowish in color. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a slightly serrated edge.

Aulax pallasia is valued for its ornamental potential and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
