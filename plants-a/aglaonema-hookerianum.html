Aglaonema hookerianum

Aglaonema hookerianum, commonly known as the Hooker's aglaonema, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features striking, variegated foliage with dark green leaves adorned with cream and red veins. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant. It thrives in indirect light and requires moderate watering to maintain its vibrant appearance.

Aglaonema hookerianum is valued for its ornamental qualities and air-purifying properties.
