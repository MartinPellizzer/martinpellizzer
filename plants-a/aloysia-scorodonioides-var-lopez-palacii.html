Aloysia scorodonioides var. lopez-palacii

Aloysia scorodonioides var. lopez-palacii, commonly known as Lopez-Palacii verbena, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Verbenaceae family and is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant, violet-blue flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in fine, soft hairs.
