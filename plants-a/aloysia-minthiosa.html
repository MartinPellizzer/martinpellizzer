Aloysia minthiosa

Aloysia minthiosa, commonly known as lemon verbena, is a perennial shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its fragrant, lemon-scented leaves and small, pale yellow flowers. The plant thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for cultivation in temperate climates. Its aromatic leaves are often used in herbal teas and as a natural remedy.

Aloysia minthiosa is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
