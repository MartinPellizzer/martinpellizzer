Arnebia cornuta var. grandiflora

Arnebia cornuta var. grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered betony, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its upright stems and clusters of pink or purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy perennial suitable for rock gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant flowers and compact growth habit.
