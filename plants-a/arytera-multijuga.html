Arytera multijuga

Arytera multijuga, commonly known as the multijugate artemisia, is a flowering plant in the Asteraceae family.

It is native to southern Africa, particularly South Africa, where it thrives in dry, sandy soils. The plant has silver-gray foliage due to dense pubescence, and its small, yellow flowers are clustered in composite heads. It is often used in traditional medicine for its purported anti-inflammatory properties.

The species is also valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
