Alyssum cuneifolium subsp. losanum

Alyssum cuneifolium subsp.

losanum, commonly known as Sierra wallflower, is a perennial herb native to the western United States. It typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils in mountainous regions.

The plant features small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink, often blooming in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, contributing to its characteristic appearance.

It is valued in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained conditions.
