Artemisia iwayomogi var. discolor

Artemisia iwayomogi var. discolor, commonly known as Japanese wormwood, is a perennial shrub native to East Asia.

It features aromatic, silvery-gray foliage with finely divided leaves that are often covered in a powdery bloom. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in panicles during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy addition to gardens.

Its medicinal properties have been utilized in traditional herbal remedies for centuries.
