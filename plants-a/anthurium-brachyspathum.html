Anthurium brachyspathum

Anthurium brachyspathum, commonly known as the broad-leaf anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces striking spadices, which are modified leaves that house the small, inconspicuous flowers. Anthurium brachyspathum thrives in humid, warm environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and ornamental purposes.
