Amyema Rubra

Amyema rubra is a rare and striking plant known for its vibrant red flowers and unique ecological role. Native to the eastern coast of Australia, it thrives in specific microhabitats within coastal heathlands. This species is highly sensitive to environmental changes, making it an indicator of ecosystem health. Its conservation status is threatened by habitat loss and climate change. Understanding and protecting Amyema rubra is crucial for preserving biodiversity in its native range.
