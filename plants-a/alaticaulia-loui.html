Alaticaulia loui

Alaticaulia loui, commonly known as the Lousia's alate caudex plant, is a unique succulent native to specific arid regions.

It is characterized by its swollen, ribbed caudex that stores water, allowing it to survive in dry conditions. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. Its leaves are modified into spines, reducing water loss and deterring herbivores.

Alaticaulia loui is adapted to grow in well-drained soils and requires minimal water, making it a resilient species in harsh environments.
