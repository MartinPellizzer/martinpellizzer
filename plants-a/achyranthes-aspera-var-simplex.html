Achyranthes aspera var. simplex

Achyranthes aspera var. simplex, commonly known as the slender knotweed, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and lanceolate leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. Its stems are often hairy, and it has a fibrous root system that can spread widely.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and waste areas.
