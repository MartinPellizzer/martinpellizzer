Astranthium reichei

Astranthium reichei, commonly known as the rock aster, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or limestone soils. The plant features basal leaves that are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small, blue to purple, and arranged in a dense, spherical inflorescence.

Astranthium reichei is known for its ability to thrive in arid conditions and is often found in coastal and mountainous areas.
