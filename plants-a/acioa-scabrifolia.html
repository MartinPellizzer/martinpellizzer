Acioa scabrifolia

Acioa scabrifolia, commonly known as the rough-leaved acioa, is a flowering plant belonging to the Rubiaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its hairy, rough-textured leaves and clusters of small, white or yellowish flowers. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
