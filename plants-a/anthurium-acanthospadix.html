Anthurium acanthospadix

Anthurium acanthospadix, commonly known as the spadix anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, elongated spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe, which is often a deep red or maroon. The plant produces small, clustered flowers on the spadix, which are typically hidden beneath the spathe. Its leaves are large, heart-shaped, and glossy, contributing to its ornamental value.

Anthurium acanthospadix thrives in humid, warm environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.
