Agave grisea var. obesispina

Agave grisea var. obesispina, commonly known as the fat spiny agave, is a succulent plant native to Mexico.

It features a thick, rosette-shaped stem covered in sharp, spiny leaves that are typically blue-gray in color. The leaves are fleshy and store water, allowing the plant to thrive in arid environments. It produces tall, branched flower stalks that bear small, greenish-white flowers.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively low maintenance once established.
