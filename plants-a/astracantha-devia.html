Astracantha devia

Astracantha devia, commonly known as the wandering star, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features slender, branching stems and small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, and it thrives in well-drained soils.

Astracantha devia is often found in rocky or sandy habitats and is adapted to arid environments.
