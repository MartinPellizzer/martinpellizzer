Artanema evrardii

Artanema evrardii, commonly known as the golden bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features showy, bell-shaped yellow flowers that bloom in late summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. The plant has hairy, upright stems and lance-shaped leaves with a slightly rough texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening due to its vibrant color and easy care requirements.
