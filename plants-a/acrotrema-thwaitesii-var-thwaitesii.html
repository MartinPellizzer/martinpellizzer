Acrotrema thwaitesii var. thwaitesii

Acrotrema thwaitesii var. thwaitesii, commonly known as the slender clubmoss, is a small evergreen plant belonging to the family Lycopodiaceae.

It is characterized by its compact, low-growing habit with whorled leaves that form dense, cushion-like structures.
