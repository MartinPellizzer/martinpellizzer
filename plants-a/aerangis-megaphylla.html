Aerangis megaphylla

Aerangis megaphylla, commonly known as the giant orchid, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can reach up to 15 cm in diameter, with vibrant colors ranging from pink to purple. The plant has thick, fleshy leaves that help it store water in its natural habitat. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Aerangis megaphylla is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is a popular choice in tropical gardens.
