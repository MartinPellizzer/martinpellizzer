Asplenium dimidiatum var. exhaustum

Asplenium dimidiatum var. exhaustum, commonly known as the exhausted spleenwort, is a fern belonging to the family Aspleniaceae.

It is a small, evergreen epiphytic fern that typically grows on tree trunks or rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant features a compact, rosette-like growth form with narrow, dark green, lance-shaped leaves. Its fronds are usually unbranched and have a distinctive, leathery texture.

This variety is noted for its ability to thrive in moist, nutrient-poor conditions, making it a common sight in tropical and subtropical regions.
