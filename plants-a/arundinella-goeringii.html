Arundinella goeringii

Arundinella goeringii, commonly known as the golden bamboo or Chinese bamboo, is a member of the grass family Poaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, hollow stems that are often used in traditional crafts and construction. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as along riverbanks and in mountainous regions. Its leaves are long and narrow, with a smooth, glossy surface that helps reduce water loss.

Arundinella goeringii is valued for its rapid growth and versatility, making it an important resource in various parts of Asia.
