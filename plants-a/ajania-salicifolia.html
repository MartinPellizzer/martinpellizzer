Ajania salicifolia

Ajania salicifolia, commonly known as the silverleaf ajania, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Africa.

It features basal leaves that are lance-shaped and covered in fine white hairs, giving them a silvery appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and alpine settings.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its attractive foliage and compact growth habit.
