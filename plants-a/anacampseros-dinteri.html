Anacampseros dinteri

Anacampseros dinteri, commonly known as the Dinter's ice plant, is a succulent native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white powder, which helps reduce water loss. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the warmer months, typically in spring or early summer. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
