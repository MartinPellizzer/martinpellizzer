Aspidium kiboschense

It is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds that are typically borne on long, slender stalks. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its spores are produced on the underside of the fronds in clusters called sori.

Aspidium kiboschense plays a role in maintaining soil stability and providing habitat for small organisms in its native ecosystems.
