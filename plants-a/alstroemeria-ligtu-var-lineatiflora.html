Alstroemeria ligtu var. lineatiflora

Alstroemeria ligtu var. lineatiflora, commonly known as the narrow-leaved alstroemeria, is a striking ornamental plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its long, narrow leaves and showy, tubular flowers that come in a range of colors including red, orange, and yellow. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters and thrives in well-drained soil and full sun. It is often cultivated for its vibrant blooms and is used in floral arrangements and gardens.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for both horticultural and ornamental purposes.
