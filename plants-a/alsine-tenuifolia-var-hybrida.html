Alsine tenuifolia var. hybrida

Alsine tenuifolia var. hybrida, commonly known as hybrid narrowleaf sandwort, is a low-growing annual plant native to arid and semi-arid regions.

It features small, succulent leaves that are typically narrow and arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces clusters of tiny, white or pink flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed areas such as roadsides and fields.

Its botanical characteristics make it well adapted to harsh environmental conditions.
