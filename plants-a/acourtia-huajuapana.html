Acourtia huajuapana

Acourtia huajuapana, commonly known as the Huajuapan daisy, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry habitats, showcasing its adaptability to arid conditions. Its yellow-orange petals and dark centers make it visually striking.
