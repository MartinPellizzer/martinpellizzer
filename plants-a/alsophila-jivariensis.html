Alsophila jivariensis

Alsophila jivariensis, commonly known as the Jivari tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, typically found in tropical rainforests. The botanical features of Alsophila jivariensis make it an important component of its native ecosystem.
