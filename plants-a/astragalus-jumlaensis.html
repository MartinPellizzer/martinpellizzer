Astragalus jumlaensis

Astragalus jumlaensis, commonly known as the Jumla milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Nepal.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow or pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions.

It plays a role in soil stabilization and is of interest for its potential medicinal properties.
