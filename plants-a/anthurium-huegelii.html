Anthurium huegelii

Anthurium huegelii, commonly known as the giant anthurium, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, glossy, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 2 feet in length. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-shaped inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, typically in shades of red, orange, or yellow. It thrives in high humidity and bright, indirect light, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique foliage and dramatic flowers make it a standout addition to any plant collection.
