Aurota

Aurota, commonly known as the golden bell, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Acanthaceae.

It is characterized by its striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, often with a contrasting green or red throat. Aurota thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.
