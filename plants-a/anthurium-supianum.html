Anthurium supianum

Anthurium supianum, commonly known as the Supian's anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are often a deep green with a glossy texture. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-like inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, typically in shades of red or orange. It thrives in humid, warm environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.

Anthurium supianum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated as a houseplant.
