Aster amabilis

Aster amabilis, commonly known as the showy aster, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and woodlands. Its vibrant purple to pink flowers bloom in the fall, attracting pollinators like butterflies and bees.

Aster amabilis is valued for its ornamental beauty and ecological benefits in natural landscapes.
