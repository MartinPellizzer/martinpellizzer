Athamanta chinensis

Athamanta chinensis, commonly known as Chinese starflower, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the family Campanulaceae and is characterized by its star-shaped flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a hairy surface.

Athamanta chinensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and has been used in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.
