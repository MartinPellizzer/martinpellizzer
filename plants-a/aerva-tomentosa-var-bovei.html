Aerva tomentosa var. bovei

Aerva tomentosa var. bovei, commonly known as the hairy aerva, is a variant of the aerva plant native to parts of Africa and Asia.

It is a shrub characterized by its dense, hairy foliage and small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments, showing adaptability to dry conditions. Its leaves are often covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.

The species is sometimes used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
