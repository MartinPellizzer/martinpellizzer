Aeschynomene nodulosa var. glabrescens

Aeschynomene nodulosa var. glabrescens, commonly known as the glabrous sicklepod, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod-like fruits. The plant typically grows as an annual herb, often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides. Its small, yellow flowers are followed by slender, hairy pods that contain several seeds.
