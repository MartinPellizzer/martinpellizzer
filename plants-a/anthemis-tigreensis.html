Anthemis tigreensis

Anthemis tigreensis, commonly known as the Tigrean anthemis, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of Ethiopia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant produces small, yellow to orange flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to the harsh climate of its native habitat.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
