Achillea lingulata var. buglossis

Achillea lingulata var. buglossis, commonly known as narrow-leaved yarrow, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features finely divided, silver-gray leaves that are often covered in a powdery bloom, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in flat-topped clusters, which bloom in early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its aromatic foliage and long flowering period make it both ornamental and ecologically valuable.
