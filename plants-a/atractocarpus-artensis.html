Atractocarpus artensis

Atractocarpus artensis, commonly known as the Artense tallow tree, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Central and South America, particularly in countries like Costa Rica and Panama. The tree is valued for its hard, durable wood and is often found in lowland rainforests.
