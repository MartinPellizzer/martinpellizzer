Armeria carratracensis

Armeria carratracensis, commonly known as the Canary Islands sandwort, is a flowering plant native to the island of Tenerife in the Canary Islands.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that typically reaches a height of about 10 to 20 centimeters. The plant features basal leaves that are linear and covered in fine hairs, and its flowers are small, white or pale pink, arranged in dense clusters.
