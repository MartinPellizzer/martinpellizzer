Alocasia sanderi

Alocasia sanderi, commonly known as the Sander's elephant ear, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 inches in length. The plant belongs to the Araceae family, which includes other well-known genera like Dieffenbachia and Caladium. Alocasia sanderi thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Its striking foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical landscapes.
