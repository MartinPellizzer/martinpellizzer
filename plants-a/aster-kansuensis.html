Aster kansuensis

Aster kansuensis, commonly known as the Kansu aster, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads and medicinal properties. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is found in mountainous regions. It features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, clustered blue or purple flowers.

Aster kansuensis is valued in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.
