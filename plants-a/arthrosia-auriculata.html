Arthrosia auriculata

Arthrosia auriculata, commonly known as the ear-shaped leaf orchid, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive ear-shaped leaves and small, delicate orchid-like flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, such as forest undergrowth. Its botanical features include a sympodial growth habit and rhizomatous roots.

Arthrosia auriculata is often found in rocky or mossy areas, where it thrives in nutrient-poor soils.
