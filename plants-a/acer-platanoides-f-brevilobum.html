Acer platanoides f. brevilobum

Acer platanoides f. brevilobum, commonly known as the narrow-lobed Norway maple, is a cultivar of the Norway maple tree.

It is characterized by its smaller, more delicate leaves compared to the typical Norway maple, with a distinctive lobing pattern. The plant is a deciduous tree that typically grows in temperate regions, preferring well-drained soils and full sun. Its ornamental value comes from its elegant foliage and seasonal color changes.

It is often used in landscaping for its aesthetic appeal and adaptability to various garden conditions.
