Artemisia × wolfii

Artemisia × wolfii, commonly known as the silver artemisia, is a hybrid shrub native to the southwestern United States.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which results from dense covering of fine, hair-like trichomes. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that appear in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy plant suitable for arid and semi-arid climates.

Artemisia × wolfii is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to stabilize soil in dry environments.
