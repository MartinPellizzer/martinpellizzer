Angelesia splendens

Angelesia splendens, commonly known as the golden flame tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, adding a vibrant splash of color to its environment. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds.
