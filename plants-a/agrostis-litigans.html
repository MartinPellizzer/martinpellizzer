Agrostis litigans

Agrostis litigans, commonly known as the slender bentgrass, is a low-growing perennial grass species native to temperate regions of Asia and Europe.

It typically forms dense, mat-like growth and thrives in moist, well-drained soils. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences. Its botanical features include a fibrous root system and a distinctive, tufted appearance.

Agrostis litigans is often found in meadows, along stream banks, and in disturbed sites.
