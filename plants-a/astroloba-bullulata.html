Astroloba bullulata

Astroloba bullulata, commonly known as the star bulb lily, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its compact, bulb-like structure and fleshy leaves. The plant produces striking, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
