Anthoxanthum japonicum var. sikokianum

Anthoxanthum japonicum var. sikokianum, commonly known as the Japanese meadow grass, is a low-growing perennial grass species native to Japan.

It features slender, tufted stems with narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically bluish-green in color. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences bearing numerous pale yellow to golden florets. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows and along stream banks.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in native plant gardens.
