Anthurium standleyi

Anthurium standleyi, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a striking tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces vibrant red spathes that enclose its spadix, creating a dramatic floral display. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium standleyi is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
