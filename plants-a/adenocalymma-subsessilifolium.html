Adenocalymma subsessilifolium

Adenocalymma subsessilifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leafed adenocalymma, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small, herbaceous shrub that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in dense clusters. Its leaves are narrow and sessile, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It is adapted to arid environments and is often found in coastal or inland regions with low rainfall.
