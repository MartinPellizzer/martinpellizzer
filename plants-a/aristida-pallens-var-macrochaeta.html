Aristida pallens var. macrochaeta

Aristida pallens var. macrochaeta, commonly known as the silky hairgrass, is a perennial grass native to arid and semi-arid regions of North America.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-gray foliage and dense, tufted growth habit. The plant produces slender, spike-like inflorescences that bear small, pale green to brown flowers. Its leaves are often covered with fine hairs, giving it a distinctive silvery appearance.

This grass is adapted to dry conditions and is often found in disturbed soils or along roadsides.
