Alsine valida

Alsine valida, commonly known as the slender alsike, is a low-growing annual herb native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in clusters, typically from June to August. The plant has alternate, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows and along riverbanks.

Alsine valida is known for its delicate appearance and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
