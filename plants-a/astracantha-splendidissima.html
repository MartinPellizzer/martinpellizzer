Astracantha splendidissima

Astracantha splendidissima, commonly known as the splendid star-thistle, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its spiny, bract-covered flower heads that resemble small stars, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in grassland habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens, though it can be invasive in certain regions.
