Achyronia rubrofusca

Achyronia rubrofusca, commonly known as the red-fleshed orchid, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking red and orange flowers, which bloom during the summer months. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, such as tropical rainforests. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture from the surrounding soil.

The orchid's unique coloration and habitat make it a notable species in botanical studies.
