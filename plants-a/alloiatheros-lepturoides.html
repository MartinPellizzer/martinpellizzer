Alloiatheros lepturoides

Alloiatheros lepturoides, commonly known as the slender-leaved alpine aster, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine meadows and rocky slopes. It has a compact growth habit and is adapted to withstand harsh weather conditions.
