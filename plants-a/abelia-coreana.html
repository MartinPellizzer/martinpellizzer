Abelia coreana

Abelia coreana, commonly known as Korean abelia, is a deciduous shrub native to East Asia.

It features showy, tubular flowers that range from white to pink, often with a red throat, blooming in late spring to early summer. Abelia coreana is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability in garden settings.
