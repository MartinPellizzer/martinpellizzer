Abelia coreana

Abelia coreana, commonly known as Korean abelia, is a deciduous shrub native to East Asia.

It features showy, tubular flowers that range from white to pink, often with a red throat, blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has opposite, ovate to elliptical leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained soil.

Abelia coreana is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability in garden settings.
