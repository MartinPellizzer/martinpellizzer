Arenaria cretica

Arenaria cretica, commonly known as Cretan sandwort, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically thrives in sandy or rocky soils. The plant produces small, white or pink flowers during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, adapted to withstand harsh environmental conditions.

Arenaria cretica is often found in coastal areas and is valued for its hardiness and ornamental appeal.
