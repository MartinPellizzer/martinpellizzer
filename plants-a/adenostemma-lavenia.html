Adenostemma lavenia

Adenostemma lavenia, commonly known as silverleaf bluemink, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in grasslands and open woodlands. The plant features narrow, silver-gray leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it its common name. Its small blue to purple flowers bloom in spikes, adding ornamental value to the landscape.

It is adapted to dry conditions and is often found in regions with a Mediterranean climate.
