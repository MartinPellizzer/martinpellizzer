Aloe laeta var. typica

Aloe laeta var. typica, commonly known as the silver aloe or silver leaf aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy, silver-gray leaves that are covered in a fine, powdery coating, which helps reduce water loss. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow flowers, typically blooming in spring. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal watering.

Its striking appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
