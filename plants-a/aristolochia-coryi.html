Aristolochia coryi

Aristolochia coryi, commonly known as the Chinese birthwort, is a flowering plant native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, heart-shaped leaves and its unique, intricate flowers that resemble a horn or pipe. The plant belongs to the Aristolochiaceae family and is known for its aromatic, often fragrant blossoms. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forests and along stream banks.

However, it contains toxic compounds and should be handled with care.
