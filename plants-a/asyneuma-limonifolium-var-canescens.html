Asyneuma limonifolium var. canescens

Asyneuma limonifolium var. canescens, commonly known as the hairy lemonleaf, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its yellow flowers and hairy, lemon-scented leaves. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers. It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.

Its unique appearance and aromatic properties make it a notable species in its native habitat.
