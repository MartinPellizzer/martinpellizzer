Astragalus mardabadensis

Astragalus mardabadensis, commonly known as Mardabad milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow or reddish flowers. The plant is adapted to dry environments, with deep root systems that help it survive in nutrient-poor soils.

It plays a role in soil stabilization and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
