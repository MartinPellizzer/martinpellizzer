Aneilema katangense

Aneilema katangense, commonly known as the Katanga arrowroot, is a flowering plant native to Central Africa.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its striking, arrowhead-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, greenish-white flowers surrounded by a modified leaf called a spathe. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.
