Ascoglossum

Ascoglossum, commonly known as the purple morning glory, is a flowering plant belonging to the Convolvulaceae family.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from purple to blue. The plant is native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas. Ascoglossum is a climbing vine that produces showy blooms during the early morning hours.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and floral arrangements.
