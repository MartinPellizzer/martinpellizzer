Arthrocnemum halocnemoides var. pterygospermum

Arthrocnemum halocnemoides var. pterygospermum, commonly known as the desert sandmat, is a succulent plant adapted to arid environments.

The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellowish in color.
