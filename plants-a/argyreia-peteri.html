Argyreia peteri

Argyreia peteri, commonly known as the red spider lily, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Amaryllis family (Amaryllidaceae) and is characterized by its striking, large, showy flowers that resemble spiders. The plant features strap-like leaves and produces clusters of flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Argyreia peteri is also noted for its ability to grow in a variety of soil types, making it adaptable to different garden environments.
