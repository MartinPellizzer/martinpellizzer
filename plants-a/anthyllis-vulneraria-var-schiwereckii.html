Anthyllis vulneraria var. schiwereckii

Anthyllis vulneraria var. schiwereckii, commonly known as the Schiwereck's burnet, is a perennial flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its dense clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It is typically found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
