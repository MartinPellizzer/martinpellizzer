Aglaia brassii

Aglaia brassii, commonly known as the Java Aglaia, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Magnoliaceae family and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers that are often clustered in inflorescences, and its fruits are capsules containing numerous seeds. Its growth habit makes it suitable for ornamental gardening in warm climates.
