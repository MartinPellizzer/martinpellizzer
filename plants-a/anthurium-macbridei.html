Anthurium macbridei

Anthurium macbridei, commonly known as the MacBride's flamingo flower, is a tropical plant native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped, brightly colored spathe that resembles a flower, though it is actually a modified leaf. The plant produces small, spadix-like inflorescences that are often hidden within the spathe. It thrives in humid, warm environments with bright, indirect light.

Anthurium macbridei is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its vibrant appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
