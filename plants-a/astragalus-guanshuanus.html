Astragalus guanshuanus

Astragalus guanshuanus, commonly known as the Guanshuan milkvetch, is a flowering plant species native to China.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its small, yellowish flowers and compound leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. It plays a role in soil stabilization and is of interest for its potential medicinal properties.

However, it is relatively rare and faces threats from habitat loss and overcollection.
