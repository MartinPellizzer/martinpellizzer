Aster philippinensis

Aster philippinensis, commonly known as the Philippine aster, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded areas and has hairy, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are usually purple or blue, with a yellow center, and bloom from September to November.

It is often found in mountainous regions and is valued for its ornamental beauty.
