Amaryllis × cronii

Amaryllis × cronii, commonly known as the hybrid amaryllis, is a striking bulbous plant native to South America.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, pink, and white. The plant produces large, strap-like leaves that are often used for ornamental purposes. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to bloom successfully.

Amaryllis × cronii is popular as a houseplant and for its ability to flower reliably during the holiday season.
