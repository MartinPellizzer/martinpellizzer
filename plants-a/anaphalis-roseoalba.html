Anaphalis roseoalba

Anaphalis roseoalba, commonly known as the silverleaf frostflower, is a flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its silvery-white, woolly foliage and delicate, pale pink to white flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine meadows and rocky slopes. Its unique appearance and habitat make it a striking example of high-altitude flora.

The species is also noted for its ability to withstand harsh climatic conditions.
