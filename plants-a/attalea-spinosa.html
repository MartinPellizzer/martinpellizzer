Attalea spinosa

Attalea spinosa, commonly known as the Brazilian buriti palm, is a tall, woody palm native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its large, fan-shaped leaves and smooth, dark green trunk. The plant produces small yellow flowers that develop into clusters of orange or red fruits. The fruits are edible and contain a nutritious, oily pulp.

It plays an important ecological role in its habitat, providing food for various wildlife species.
