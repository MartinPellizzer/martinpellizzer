Athyrium taiwanense

Athyrium taiwanense, commonly known as Taiwan shield fern, is a species of fern native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the family Athyriaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, shield-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its fronds are bi- or tri-pinnate, with a delicate, lace-like appearance.

Athyrium taiwanense is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
