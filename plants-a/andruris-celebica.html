Andruris celebica

Andruris celebica, commonly known as the Celebes orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Indonesia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its roots are adapted to absorb nutrients from decaying organic matter in the soil.

Andruris celebica is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
