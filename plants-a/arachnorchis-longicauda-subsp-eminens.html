Arachnorchis longicauda subsp. eminens

Arachnorchis longicauda subsp.

eminens, commonly known as the tall spider-orchid, is a rare and striking orchid species native to southeastern Australia. It is characterized by its long, slender inflorescence bearing numerous pale yellow to greenish-white flowers with intricate, spider-like structures.

The plant typically grows in heathlands and open forests, preferring well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade. Its unique morphology and limited distribution make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists.

Due to habitat loss and its rarity, it is considered vulnerable and requires careful protection.
