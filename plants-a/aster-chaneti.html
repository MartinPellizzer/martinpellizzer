Aster chaneti

Aster chaneti, commonly known as the Chanet's aster, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of central and southern Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies, sunflowers, and other composite flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or grassy habitats and produces small, clustered flowers in shades of white and purple. Its leaves are usually narrow and lance-shaped, and it blooms during the late summer and early autumn.

Aster chaneti is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
