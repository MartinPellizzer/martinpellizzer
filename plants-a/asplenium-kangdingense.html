Asplenium kangdingense

Asplenium kangdingense, commonly known as the Kangding spleenwort, is a species of fern native to the mountainous regions of western China.

It belongs to the family Aspleniaceae and is characterized by its small, compact growth habit. The plant typically has dark green, leathery fronds with a serrated or lobed margin. It thrives in shaded, moist environments, often found in rocky crevices or on tree trunks.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
