Asterochlaena morongii var. intermedia

Asterochlaena morongii var. intermedia, commonly known as the variable morong's aster.

It is a flowering plant native to certain regions of Southeast Asia. The plant features showy, daisy-like flowers with white petals and a central yellow disk. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.

Its botanical characteristics make it an interesting subject for study in ecological and horticultural contexts.
