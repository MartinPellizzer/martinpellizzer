Arabis pinetorum

Arabis pinetorum, commonly known as the pinegrass or mountain arabis, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in open, rocky, or sandy soils. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers in early spring. Its leaves are linear and grass-like, contributing to its common name.

Arabis pinetorum is often found in alpine and subalpine habitats, where it thrives in cool, moist environments.
