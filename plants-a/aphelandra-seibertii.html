Aphelandra seibertii

Aphelandra seibertii, commonly known as the golden torch plant, is a striking tropical plant native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its long, pendant inflorescences that bear bright yellow bracts, which resemble torches. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom sequentially along the inflorescence. Aphelandra seibertii thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

It is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
