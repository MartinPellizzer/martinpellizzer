Arctotis × regalis

Arctotis × regalis, commonly known as the African daisy, is a hybrid perennial plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, daisy-like flowers that range in color from yellow to orange and red. The plant typically grows to a height of 30–60 cm and prefers well-drained soil and full sun. Its foliage consists of narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs.

Arctotis × regalis is often cultivated for ornamental purposes in gardens and is relatively drought-tolerant once established.
