Arachnorchis montana

Arachnorchis montana, commonly known as the mountain spider orchid, is an epiphytic orchid native to southeastern Australia.

It typically grows on the trunks of eucalyptus trees in cool, moist environments. The plant features a slender, wiry stem with a single, narrow leaf. Its flowers are small, greenish-white, and have a distinctive spider-like appearance.

It blooms from late winter to early spring, adding a unique ornamental value to its habitat.
