Acrocomia totai

Acrocomia totai, commonly known as the giant palm or the red palm, is a striking tropical plant native to parts of South America.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like leaves that can reach impressive lengths. The plant produces distinctive yellow-orange flowers followed by edible, coconut-like fruits. Its botanical features make it a valuable species for both ornamental and agricultural purposes.

It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
