Asplenium hookerianum var. ornatum

Asplenium hookerianum var. ornatum, commonly known as the ornate spleenwort, is a fern species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Aspleniaceae and is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped leaves with a distinctive, ornate venation pattern. The plant typically grows on rocks or tree trunks, thriving in moist, shaded environments. Its fronds are usually small, ranging from 5 to 15 centimeters in length, and display a rich green color with a slightly glossy texture.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
