Anthurium chucantiense

Anthurium chucantiense, commonly known as the Chucantiense arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to Central America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture. The plant produces spadices, which are inflorescences that consist of a spadix surrounded by a colorful bract. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Anthurium chucantiense is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated as a houseplant.
