Ardisia celebica

Ardisia celebica, commonly known as the Java Christmasberry, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, dark green leaves and produces clusters of small, white, fragrant flowers. The plant is notable for its bright red, berry-like fruits that resemble Christmas ornaments, hence its common name. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forests.

Ardisia celebica is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
