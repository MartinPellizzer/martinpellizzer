Acanos spina

Acanos spina, commonly known as the spiny acanthus, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features upright, branched stems that are often covered in stiff, spiny hairs. The plant produces large, palmate leaves with serrated edges, and its flowers are typically arranged in dense, spiky clusters. Acanos spina thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy plant in Mediterranean climates.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
