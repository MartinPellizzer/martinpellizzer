Anaxagorea javanica var. javanica

Anaxagorea javanica var. javanica, commonly known as the Javanian ironwood, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Anacardiaceae and is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and sturdy, aromatic wood. The tree is valued for its hardiness and is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes or for its timber.
