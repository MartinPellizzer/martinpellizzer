Aloe erensii

Aloe erensii, commonly known as the Cape aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with white or yellowish markings. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with orange or red tubular flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for arid environments.

Aloe erensii is valued for its ornamental beauty and traditional medicinal uses.
