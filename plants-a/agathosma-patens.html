Agathosma patens

Agathosma patens, commonly known as the Cape Geranium, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its aromatic, hairy leaves and small, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. It is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and ornamental landscaping.
