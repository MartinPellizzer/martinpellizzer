Archontophoenix myolensis

Archontophoenix myolensis, commonly known as the Myola palm, is a tropical tree native to the island of New Caledonia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like leaves that form a crown at the top. The plant belongs to the Arecaceae family, which includes many other palm species. It typically grows in coastal areas and is adapted to the humid, subtropical climate of its native habitat.
