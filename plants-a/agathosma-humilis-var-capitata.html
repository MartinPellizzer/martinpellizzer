Agathosma humilis var. capitata

Agathosma humilis var. capitata, commonly known as Cape geranium, is a small, herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant produces small, rounded leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its aromatic foliage and delicate blooms make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
