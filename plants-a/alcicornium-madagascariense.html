Alcicornium madagascariense

Alcicornium madagascariense, commonly known as the Madagascar orchid, is a unique flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, often fragrant blooms. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, such as rainforests, where it thrives on nutrient-poor soil. Its roots are specialized for absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air and decaying organic matter.

Alcicornium madagascariense plays an important role in the local ecosystem and is a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists.
