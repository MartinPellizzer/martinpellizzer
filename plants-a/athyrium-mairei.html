Athyrium mairei

Athyrium mairei, commonly known as the Maire's sword fern, is a species of fern native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the family Athyriaceae and is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its rhizomes are often covered in a dense layer of brown, velvety scales.

Athyrium mairei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens with the right conditions.
