Anthyllis splendens

Anthyllis splendens, commonly known as the splendour anthyllis, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has bipinnate leaves and produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its showy blooms and hardy nature.
