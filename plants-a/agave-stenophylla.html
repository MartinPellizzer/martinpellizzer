Agave stenophylla

Agave stenophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leaf agave, is a succulent plant native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color and have a waxy texture. The plant produces a tall flower stalk that bears clusters of yellowish-green flowers. It is adapted to arid environments and stores water in its leaves and stem.

Agave stenophylla is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and resilience.
