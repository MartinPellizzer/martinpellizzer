Amaranthus aeruoides

Amaranthus aeruoides, commonly known as the slender amaranth, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy inflorescences. The plant features lance-shaped leaves and produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from red to orange. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Amaranthus aeruoides is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
