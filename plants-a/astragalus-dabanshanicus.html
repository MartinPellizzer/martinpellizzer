Astragalus dabanshanicus

Astragalus dabanshanicus, commonly known as Dabanshan milkvetch, is a flowering plant species native to China.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its hairy stems and small, pea-like flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or mountainous regions, adapting to harsh environmental conditions. Its seeds are encased in a leathery pod, which helps in seed dispersal.

Astragalus dabanshanicus is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphology and ecological significance in its native habitat.
