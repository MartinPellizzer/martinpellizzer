Alrawia

Alrawia, commonly known as the desert rose, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of Africa and the Middle East.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant has a woody stem and produces small, green, egg-shaped fruits that contain seeds. Alrawia is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens in hot, dry climates.
