Anaphalis margaritacea f. arachnoidea

Anaphalis margaritacea f. arachnoidea, commonly known as the silverleaf or frost flower, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a silvery appearance, and its small, white to pale yellow flower heads. The plant is native to the mountainous regions of East Asia, including China, Japan, and Korea. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in rocky or alpine habitats.

Its unique appearance and ecological adaptations make it a notable species in its native flora.
