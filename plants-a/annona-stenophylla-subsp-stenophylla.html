Annona stenophylla subsp. stenophylla

Annona stenophylla subsp.

stenophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leaf soursop, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Central and South America. It belongs to the Annonaceae family and is characterized by its palmate leaves and fragrant, yellowish-green flowers.

The plant produces a large, spiny fruit that is typically harvested when fully mature. The fruit contains a single large seed and is often used in local cuisines for its unique flavor.

Botanically, it is notable for its ornamental value and potential medicinal properties.
