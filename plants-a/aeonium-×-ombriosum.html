Aeonium × ombriosum

Aeonium × ombriosum, commonly known as the Rainbow Aeonium, is a hybrid succulent plant native to the Canary Islands.

It features rosettes of fleshy, thick leaves that display a striking range of colors, from deep greens to vibrant reds and blues. The plant produces tall, branching flower stalks that bear yellowish-orange flowers, adding to its ornamental value. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
