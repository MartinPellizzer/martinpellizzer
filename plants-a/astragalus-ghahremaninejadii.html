Astragalus ghahremaninejadii

Astragalus ghahremaninejadii, commonly known as the Ghahremaninejadi Astragalus, is a flowering plant species native to Iran.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, pea-like flowers. The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions.

Its root system is notable for its deep penetration, which helps it access water from deeper soil layers.
