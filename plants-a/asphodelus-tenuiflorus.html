Asphodelus tenuiflorus

Asphodelus tenuiflorus, commonly known as the slender-flowered asphodel, is a perennial herb native to southern Europe and North Africa.

It typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is often found in Mediterranean climates. The plant features strap-like, grass-like leaves and produces tall, slender stems with small, yellowish-green flowers. It is adapted to drought conditions and can thrive in poor soil environments.

Asphodelus tenuiflorus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in rock gardens.
