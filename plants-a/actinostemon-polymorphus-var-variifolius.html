Actinostemon polymorphus var. variifolius

Actinostemon polymorphus var. variifolius, commonly known as the variable-leaf actinostemon, is a species native to parts of Africa.

It is a succulent plant characterized by its fleshy, cylindrical stems and thick, waxy leaves. The leaves exhibit a wide variation in shape and size, which is reflected in its scientific name. This plant thrives in arid or semi-arid environments and is adapted to store water in its stems and leaves.

It produces small, greenish flowers that are often clustered at the ends of the stems.
