Astelma spirale

Astelma spirale, commonly known as the spiral astelma, is a unique plant characterized by its spiraling growth pattern.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves that spiral upward along its stem. Its flowers are small and clustered, often resembling a dense tuft at the top of the stem.

Astelma spirale is native to certain regions of South America and thrives in well-drained soil with partial sunlight.
