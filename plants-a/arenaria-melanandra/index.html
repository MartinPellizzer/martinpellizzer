Arenaria Melanandra

Black sandplant, scientifically known as Arenaria melanandra, is a resilient plant species adapted to harsh environments. It thrives in sandy and coastal areas where few other plants can survive. The plant's dark-colored foliage and compact growth make it uniquely suited to its habitat. Despite its tough conditions, it plays an important ecological role in stabilizing dunes and preventing erosion. Its distinctive appearance and hardy nature make it a fascinating subject for botanical study.
