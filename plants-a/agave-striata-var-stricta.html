Agave striata var. stricta

Agave striata var. stricta, commonly known as the narrow-leaved agave, is a succulent plant native to Mexico.

It features narrow, gray-green leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with small, white flowers that bloom in the spring. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.

Its striking appearance makes it a popular choice for xeriscaping and rock gardens.
