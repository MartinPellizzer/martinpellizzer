Ardisia penduliflora var. microsepala

Ardisia penduliflora var. microsepala, commonly known as the small-sepaled flame tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, pendulous red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters, creating a dramatic visual effect. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves that are opposite and ovate in shape. It produces small, fleshy berries that are typically dark red when mature.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes due to its vibrant floral display.
