Acubalus

Acubalus, commonly known as the silverleaf tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, silvery-green leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by edible, orange-colored fruits. Acubalus thrives in humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Its root system is fibrous, making it adaptable to various soil types.
