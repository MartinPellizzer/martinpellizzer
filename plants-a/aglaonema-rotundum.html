Aglaonema rotundum

Aglaonema rotundum, commonly known as the Round-leaf Chinese Evergreen, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, rounded leaves with striking silver or white veins. The plant is evergreen and thrives in low-light conditions, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening. It has a compact growth habit and is relatively easy to care for, requiring moderate watering and well-draining soil.

Aglaonema rotundum is also known for its air-purifying properties, contributing to improved indoor air quality.
