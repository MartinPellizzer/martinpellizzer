Aloe microstigma subsp. microstigma

Aloe microstigma subsp.

microstigma, commonly known as the narrow-leaved aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa. It features rosettes of fleshy, narrow, and often blue-green leaves that are covered in a whitish bloom.

The plant produces small, yellowish flowers on spikes during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its compact size and ornamental leaves make it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation.
