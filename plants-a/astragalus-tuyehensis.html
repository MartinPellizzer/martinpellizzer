Astragalus tuyehensis

Astragalus tuyehensis, commonly known as the Tuyeh mountain milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, pea-like flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Astragalus tuyehensis is known for its medicinal properties, often used in traditional Chinese medicine.

Its root system is valued for its potential health benefits and is a subject of ongoing botanical research.
