Aethusa leptophylla

Aethusa leptophylla, commonly known as the slender-leaved cow-wheat, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, yellow flowers and trifoliate leaves, which are typically narrow and elongated. This plant is native to Europe and parts of western Asia, often growing in disturbed soils and open habitats.
