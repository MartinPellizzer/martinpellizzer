Astilbe japonica var. angustifoliolata

Astilbe japonica var. angustifoliolata, commonly known as narrow-leaved Japanese astilbe, is a herbaceous perennial native to Japan.

It features delicate, feathery plumes of pink or white flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has finely cut, lance-shaped leaves that provide a lush, textured backdrop. It prefers partial to full shade and moist, well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for woodland gardens and shaded borders.
