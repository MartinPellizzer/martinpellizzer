Acrocephalus hensii

Acrocephalus hensii, commonly known as the Henslow's sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It typically grows in wet or damp environments such as marshes, ditches, and stream banks. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers.
