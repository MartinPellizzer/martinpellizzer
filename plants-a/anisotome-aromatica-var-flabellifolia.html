Anisotome aromatica var. flabellifolia

Anisotome aromatica var. flabellifolia, commonly known as the fragrant anisotome, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe.

It features aromatic, lanceolate leaves with a distinctive, often fragrant scent. The plant produces small, clustered white or pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. Its fibrous roots are commonly used in traditional herbal medicine.

The variety flabellifolia is distinguished by its broad, fan-shaped leaves, which give it a unique appearance.
