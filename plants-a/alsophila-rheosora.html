Alsophila rheosora

Alsophila rheosora, commonly known as the red-stemmed tree fern, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk that develops a reddish hue as it matures. The plant produces large, feather-like fronds that are arranged in a dense, upright fashion. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, typically found in tropical and subtropical forests.

Alsophila rheosora plays a role in providing habitat and shelter for various forest organisms.
