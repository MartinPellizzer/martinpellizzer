Azalea ciliicalyx

Azalea ciliicalyx, commonly known as the broadleaf azalea, is a flowering plant native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the Rhododendron genus and is characterized by its showy, clustered flowers that bloom in spring. The plant features glossy, elliptical leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a soft texture. It prefers acidic, well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

Its ornamental value lies in its vibrant floral display and adaptability to various climates.
