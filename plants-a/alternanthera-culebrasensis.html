Alternanthera culebrasensis

Alternanthera culebrasensis, commonly known as the red flame plant, is a species of flowering plant in the Amaranthaceae family.

It is native to South America, particularly Brazil, and is known for its striking red foliage. The plant features small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a decorative plant in aquatic and terrestrial gardens.
