Achillea Millefolium Var. Sudetica

Achillea millefolium var. sudetica is a lesser-known variant of the common yarrow plant. It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Europe, particularly the Sudetes. This plant is characterized by its finely divided leaves and clusters of small, white flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy addition to rock gardens. Its resilience and beauty make it a valuable species for both ecological and ornamental purposes.
