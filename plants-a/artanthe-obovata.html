Artanthe obovata

Artanthe obovata, commonly known as the broad-ovate artanthe, is a perennial herb native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its lanceolate to obovate leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or arid environments.

Artanthe obovata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in native plant landscaping.
