Aichryson × cumbrense

Aichryson × cumbrense, commonly known as the hybrid African violet, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, thick leaves that are typically green with white or silver markings. The plant produces small, showy flowers in shades of purple, pink, or white during the spring and summer months. It prefers well-draining soil and bright, indirect sunlight to thrive.

Due to its hybrid nature, it is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
