Aquilegia einseleana f. reichenbachii

Aquilegia einseleana f. reichenbachii, commonly known as the Reichenbach's columbine, is a hybrid form of the European columbine.

It is characterized by its showy, spurred flowers that range in color from blue to purple. The plant typically grows in woodland areas and prefers moist, well-drained soils. It has compound leaves with three to five leaflets, and its flowers bloom in early summer.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in garden landscapes.
