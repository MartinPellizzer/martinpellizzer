Asphodeline cilicica

Asphodeline cilicica, commonly known as the Cilician asphodel, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically bluish-green in color. The plant produces tall, flowering stems with small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in a spike. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or arid environments.

Asphodeline cilicica is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
