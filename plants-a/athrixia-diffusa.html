Athrixia diffusa

Athrixia diffusa, commonly known as the silver leaf tree, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a silvery or grayish underside, giving the plant its common name. It typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters.

The plant is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it drought-tolerant and suitable for xeriscaping.
