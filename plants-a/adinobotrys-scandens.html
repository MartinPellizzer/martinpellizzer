Adinobotrys scandens

Adinobotrys scandens, commonly known as the climbing hibiscus, is a woody vine native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, which bloom throughout the wet season. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a velvety texture.
