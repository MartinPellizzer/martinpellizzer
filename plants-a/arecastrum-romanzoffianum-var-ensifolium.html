Arecastrum romanzoffianum var. ensifolium

Arecastrum romanzoffianum var. ensifolium, commonly known as the swordleaf areca.

It is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia. The plant features elongated, sword-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. It produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually yellow or greenish-yellow in color.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is well-suited to warm, humid climates.
