Agathosma pungens

Agathosma pungens, commonly known as the Cape aster, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant has aromatic leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to dry, sunny environments.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in arid regions.
