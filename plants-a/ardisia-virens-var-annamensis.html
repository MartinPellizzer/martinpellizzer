Ardisia virens var. annamensis

Ardisia virens var. annamensis, commonly known as the Annamese evergreen ardisia, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, white flowers followed by bright red berries, which are an important food source for various bird species. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical forests.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for horticultural cultivation in warm climates.
