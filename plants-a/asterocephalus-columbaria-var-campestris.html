Asterocephalus columbaria var. campestris

Asterocephalus columbaria var. campestris, commonly known as the field asterocephalus, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as roadsides and meadows. It produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in the summer months.

Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often forming dense clusters at the base of the plant.
