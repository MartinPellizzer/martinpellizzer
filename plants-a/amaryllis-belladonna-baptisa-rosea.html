Amaryllis belladonna baptisa-rosea

Amaryllis belladonna baptisa-rosea, commonly known as the Roseate Amaryllis, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It produces vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of pink and white, often with a subtle rose hue. The plant features strap-like, narrow leaves that are typically a deep green color. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires ample sunlight to bloom successfully.

This variety is popular for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation in gardens and as a houseplant.
