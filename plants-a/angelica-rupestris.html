Angelica rupestris

Angelica rupestris, commonly known as the rock angelica, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Europe and western Asia.

It typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, where it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun. The plant features tall, slender stems that can reach up to 1.5 meters in height, adorned with large, compound leaves that are deeply lobed. Its flowers are small, greenish-yellow, and arranged in clusters, producing a distinctive, aromatic scent.

Angelica rupestris is valued for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional herbal remedies.
