Amaranthus tricolor var. polygamus

Amaranthus tricolor var. polygamus, commonly known as red leaf amaranth, is a vibrant annual plant characterized by its strikingly colored foliage.

It features leaves that display a mix of red, green, and yellow hues, creating a visually appealing appearance. The plant produces small, greenish flowers that are clustered in dense spikes, and it is often cultivated for ornamental purposes. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Its rapid growth and ability to attract pollinators make it both ecologically and aesthetically valuable.
