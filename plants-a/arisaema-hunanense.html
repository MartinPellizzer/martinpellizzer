Arisaema hunanense

Arisaema hunanense, commonly known as the Hunan arrowhead, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its distinctive arrow-shaped leaves. The plant produces an inflorescence consisting of a spadix surrounded by a spathe, typical of the Araceae family. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in Chinese medicine.
