Alternanthera nigripes

Alternanthera nigripes, commonly known as blackleaf alternanthera, is a striking aquatic plant native to South America.

It features dark green to almost black leaves that provide a dramatic contrast to its bright red or orange flower clusters. The plant thrives in shallow, slow-moving waters and requires moderate to high light conditions for optimal growth. Its foliage is often used in aquascaping for its aesthetic appeal and ease of maintenance.

It is also known for its ability to tolerate a range of water parameters, making it a popular choice among aquarium enthusiasts.
