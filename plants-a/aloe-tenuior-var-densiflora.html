Aloe tenuior var. densiflora

Aloe tenuior var. densiflora, commonly known as the dense-flowered aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, with a waxy coating to store water. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect sunlight.

Due to its compact growth habit, it is often used in rock gardens or as a decorative indoor plant.
