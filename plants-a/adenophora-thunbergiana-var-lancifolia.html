Adenophora thunbergiana var. lancifolia

Adenophora thunbergiana var. lancifolia, commonly known as the lanceleaf bellflower, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It features slender, lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale blue. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and conservation efforts.
