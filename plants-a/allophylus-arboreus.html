Allophylus arboreus

Allophylus arboreus, commonly known as the African holly, is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub or tree native to southern Africa.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a leathery texture. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. Its fruit is a small, smooth, black drupe that contains a single seed.

Allophylus arboreus is often used in ornamental horticulture due to its attractive appearance and hardy nature.
