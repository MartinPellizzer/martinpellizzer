Alyxia winckelii

Alyxia winckelii, commonly known as the Florida alyxia, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows in wetlands and along waterways. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in the spring. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and often have a slightly waxy texture.

Alyxia winckelii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting local wildlife.
