Arachne polypetala

Arachne polypetala, commonly known as the Persian bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped blue or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in temperate climates. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged alternately along the stem.
