Artanthe baccans

Artanthe baccans, commonly known as the scarlet globemallow, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its showy, pink to red, globe-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has narrow, gray-green leaves and grows in a compact, bushy form. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or arid habitats.
