Anoniodes nymanii

Anoniodes nymanii, commonly known as the African butter tree, is a flowering plant belonging to the Annonaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its large, simple, ovate leaves. The plant produces fragrant, yellowish-green flowers that are typically clustered in umbels. Its fruit is a smooth, round berry that turns from green to yellow when mature.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and has traditional uses in some African cultures.
