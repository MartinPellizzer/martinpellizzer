Aerangis spiculata

Aerangis spiculata, commonly known as the Spiny Aerangis, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and unique, spiny appearance. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in spikes, which give it its common name. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Aerangis spiculata is often cultivated for its ornamental value and resilience in arid environments.
