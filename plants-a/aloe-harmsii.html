Aloe harmsii

Aloe harmsii, commonly known as the Transkei aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a waxy texture. The plant produces tall, branched flower stalks that bear yellow-orange flowers, which bloom in late summer. Aloe harmsii is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its leaves to survive drought conditions.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional medicinal uses.
