Arachniodes rhomboidea var. yakusimensis

Arachniodes rhomboidea var. yakusimensis, commonly known as the Yakushima bracken fern, is a variant of the broad-leafed arachniodes species native to Japan.

It is characterized by its large, rhombic-shaped, dark green leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery pubescence. The plant thrives in shaded, humid environments, typically found in mountainous regions with rich, well-drained soil. Its fronds are divided into narrow segments, giving it a delicate, feathery appearance.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in shaded garden settings.
