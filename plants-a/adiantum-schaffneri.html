Adiantum schaffneri

Adiantum schaffneri, commonly known as the Schaffner's maidenhair fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Adiantaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Central and South America, particularly in countries like Brazil and Peru. The plant is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds that are typically bright green and have a feathery appearance. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires consistent moisture and well-draining soil.

Adiantum schaffneri is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
