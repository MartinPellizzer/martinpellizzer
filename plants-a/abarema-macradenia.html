Abarema macradenia

Abarema macradenia, commonly known as the purple crown bean, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is characterized by its leguminous properties and nitrogen-fixing ability. The plant features compound leaves and produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant purple flowers.

The species is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
