Astragalus francisquitensis var. lagunensis

Astragalus francisquitensis var. lagunensis, commonly known as the Lagunense Milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to specific regions of North America.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its small, pea-like flowers that typically bloom in late spring or early summer. The plant has compound leaves with numerous small leaflets and produces pods that contain several seeds.
