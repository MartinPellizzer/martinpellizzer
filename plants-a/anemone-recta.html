Anemone recta

Anemone recta, commonly known as the straight anemone, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in moist, woodland habitats. The plant features showy, white to pinkish flowers with five to seven petals, which bloom in early spring. Its leaves are palmately divided, with a waxy surface that helps retain moisture.

Anemone recta is often found in shaded areas and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
