Ardisia leschenaultii

Ardisia leschenaultii, commonly known as the Leschenault's beautyberry, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a member of the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers that are followed by bright purple berries. These berries are an important food source for various birds and mammals.
