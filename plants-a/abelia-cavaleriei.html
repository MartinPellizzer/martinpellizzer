Abelia cavaleriei

Abelia cavaleriei, commonly known as Chinese abelia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features fragrant, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink, blooming in late summer. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are dark green and glossy. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained soil.

Abelia cavaleriei is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability in garden settings.
