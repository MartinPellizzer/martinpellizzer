Anemone narcissiflora var. shikokiana

Anemone narcissiflora var. shikokiana, commonly known as the Shikokian anemone, is a striking perennial plant native to Japan.

It features showy, cup-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale pink, often with a yellow center. The plant produces large, palmately lobed leaves that are typically dark green and slightly hairy. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate, early spring blooms.
