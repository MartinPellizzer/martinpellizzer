Aristolochia setchuenensis

Aristolochia setchuenensis, commonly known as the Sichuan pipevine, is a climbing vine native to China.

It is characterized by its distinctive, heart-shaped leaves and intricate, pipe-like flowers that are often greenish-white in color. The plant produces small, berry-like fruits that turn red when mature. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forested areas.

Due to its unique morphology and ecological role, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
