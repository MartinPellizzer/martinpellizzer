Acianthera viridis

Acianthera viridis, commonly known as the green acianthera, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in racemes, which are often hidden among the foliage. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is a subject of interest for botanists and orchid enthusiasts alike.
