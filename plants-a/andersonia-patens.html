Andersonia patens

Andersonia patens, commonly known as the Cape York bell heath, is an evergreen shrub native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Ericaceae and is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers that are typically white or pink, blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and is often found in coastal heathlands.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
