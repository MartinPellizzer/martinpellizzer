Allophylus pervillei

Allophylus pervillei, commonly known as the African snowball, is a small evergreen shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, spherical clusters of white flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant has smooth, dark green leaves that are opposite in arrangement. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

Allophylus pervillei is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
