Aster decemflorus

Aster decemflorus, commonly known as the ten-flowered aster, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant produces showy purple to blue flowers that bloom in the late summer and early autumn. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, and it thrives in well-drained soils.

Aster decemflorus is often found in meadows and open woodlands, where it contributes to biodiversity and supports pollinators.
