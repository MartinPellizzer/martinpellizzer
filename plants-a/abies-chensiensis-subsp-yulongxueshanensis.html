It is characterized by its slender, pyramidal tree form and dark green, needle-like leaves that are arranged in spirals along the branches.

The cones are typically small and cylindrical, maturing to a reddish-brown color, and the tree produces a distinctive, aromatic resin. This subspecies thrives in cool, moist climates with well-drained soil, often growing in mixed forests at elevations above 3,000 meters.
