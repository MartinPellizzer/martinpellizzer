Ajania tibetica

Ajania tibetica, commonly known as the Tibetan ajania, is a flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Tibetan Plateau.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky and alpine environments, thriving in cold and arid conditions. Its leaves are usually gray-green and woolly, adapted to reduce water loss in harsh climates.

Ajania tibetica is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional medicinal uses in local communities.
