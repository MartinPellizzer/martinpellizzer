Acalypha somalium

Acalypha somalium, commonly known as the Somali copperleaf, is a flowering plant native to East Africa.

It is characterized by its striking copper-colored bracts that surround its small, inconspicuous flowers. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub, reaching heights of up to 1.5 meters. Its leaves are ovate with a waxy texture and a deep green color.

Acalypha somalium thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular ornamental plant in tropical and subtropical regions.
