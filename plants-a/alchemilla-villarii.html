Alchemilla villarii

Alchemilla villarii, commonly known as the lesser lady's mantle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its basal rosette of lobed, silver-green leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in panicles during the summer months. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas or along stream banks.

Alchemilla villarii is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine for its purported healing properties.
