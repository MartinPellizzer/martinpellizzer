Aloe mzimbana

Aloe mzimbana, commonly known as the Natal aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a waxy coating. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow flowers that bloom in summer. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Aloe mzimbana is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional medicinal uses.
