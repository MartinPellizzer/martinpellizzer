Anthurium huixtlense

Anthurium huixtlense, commonly known as the Huixtlense arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-like inflorescences that are often surrounded by colorful spathe. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and botanical collections.
