Artemisia gmelinii var. turtschaninoviana

Artemisia gmelinii var. turtschaninoviana, commonly known as the Turkestan wormwood, is a perennial shrub native to Central Asia.

It is characterized by its aromatic, silver-gray foliage and small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant thrives in dry, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions. It has a compact growth habit and is valued for its ornamental appeal and medicinal properties.

The leaves contain volatile oils, which contribute to its distinctive scent and potential therapeutic uses.
