Acer wuyuanense var. trichopodum

Acer wuyuanense var. trichopodum, commonly known as the Wuyuan maple, is a rare and unique variant of the Wuyuan maple species.

It is native to China, specifically the Wuyun Mountains in Jiangxi Province. The plant is characterized by its distinctive foliage, with leaves that are often deeply lobed and have a vibrant green color during the growing season. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Due to its limited distribution and ecological significance, it is considered a valuable species for botanical study and conservation efforts.
