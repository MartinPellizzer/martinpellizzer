Absinthium mollissimum

Absinthium mollissimum, commonly known as velvetleaf, is a herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are soft and velvety to the touch, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in summer, attracting various pollinators. It is often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides due to its adaptability.

Botanically, it is characterized by its robust growth and ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
