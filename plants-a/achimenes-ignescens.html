Achimenes ignescens

Achimenes ignescens, commonly known as the flame flower, is a striking tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that range from red to orange and yellow. The plant produces showy bracts that resemble petals, enhancing its ornamental appeal. It thrives in warm, humid environments and prefers well-drained soil.

Achimenes ignescens is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its unique appearance and ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
