Anthurium billdarcyi

Anthurium billdarcyi, commonly known as the Red-veined Anthurium, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves with deep red veins contrasting against its dark green foliage. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-shaped inflorescences that are typically surrounded by a showy spathe. It thrives in high humidity and indirect light, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long-lasting, colorful bracts that add visual interest to any space.
