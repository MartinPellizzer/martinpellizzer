Athyrium yokoscense var. kirishimaense

Athyrium yokoscense var. kirishimaense, commonly known as the Kirishima holly fern, is a rare and ornamental fern native to Japan.

It belongs to the Dryopteridaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, lace-like fronds that are typically dark green with a silvery underside.
