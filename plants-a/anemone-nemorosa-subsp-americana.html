Anemone nemorosa subsp. americana

Anemone nemorosa subsp.

americana, commonly known as the American wood anemone, is a herbaceous perennial native to North America. It features delicate, white or pale pink flowers with a distinctive, showy center, typically blooming in early spring.

The plant has trifoliate leaves that are heart-shaped and covered in a soft, hairy texture. It thrives in moist, shaded woodlands and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for woodland gardens and conservation efforts.
