Allamanda blanchetii

Allamanda blanchetii, commonly known as the yellow allamanda, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It is a climbing shrub that produces large, showy yellow flowers with a prominent, fleshy corona. The plant features glossy, dark green leaves that are typically ovate to lanceolate in shape. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Allamanda blanchetii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
