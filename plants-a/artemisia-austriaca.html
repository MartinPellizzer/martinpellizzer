Artemisia austriaca

Artemisia austriaca, commonly known as Austrian wormwood, is a perennial herb native to Central Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, silver-gray foliage. The plant produces small, yellowish flower heads that are clustered in dense, spherical shapes. It thrives in dry, well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or alpine environments.

Artemisia austriaca is valued for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally in herbal remedies.
