Arthromeris cyrtomioides

Arthromeris cyrtomioides, commonly known as the shield fern, is a species of fern native to the tropical regions of Africa and the Indian Ocean islands.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its compact, evergreen growth habit. The plant features a short, creeping rhizome with dense clusters of lanceolate, leathery leaves. Its fronds are typically dark green and have a distinctive, shield-like appearance.

Arthromeris cyrtomioides is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in shaded, moist environments.
