Actinodium

Actinodium, commonly known as the ghost flower, is a rare and fascinating plant belonging to the family Actinidiaceae.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia, particularly in countries like Vietnam and China. Actinodium is a climbing shrub that typically grows in shaded, humid environments.
