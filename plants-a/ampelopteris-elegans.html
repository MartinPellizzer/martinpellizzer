Ampelopteris elegans

Ampelopteris elegans, commonly known as the slender chain fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, linear fronds that grow in a cascading manner, making it a popular choice for hanging baskets or as a decorative plant. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit, with a creeping stem that produces new shoots along its length. Its leaves are typically bright green and can reach lengths of up to 1 meter.

Ampelopteris elegans prefers bright, indirect light and moderate humidity, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.
