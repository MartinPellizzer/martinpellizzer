Alternanthera cinerella

Alternanthera cinerella, commonly known as silver velvet plant, is a tropical perennial plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking silver-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, velvety hairs. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or reddish in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full sunlight.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and indoor plant collections.
