Aponogeton prolifer

Aponogeton prolifer, commonly known as the African water lettuce, is a aquatic plant native to Africa.

It has dark green, waxy leaves that are broad and heart-shaped, with a velvety texture. The plant thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments, often found in ponds and lakes. It produces small, white flowers that bloom in clusters, adding a delicate floral touch to its appearance.

Aponogeton prolifer is known for its rapid growth and ability to spread through both vegetative propagation and seed dispersal.
