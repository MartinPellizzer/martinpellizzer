Amyema bifurcata

Amyema bifurcata, commonly known as the red-flowered lilliput, is a small, flowering plant native to the eastern coast of Australia.

It is a member of the Myrtaceae family, which includes eucalyptus and tea trees. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to deep red, depending on the variety. Its leaves are narrow, lance-shaped, and often covered in a fine, powdery coating.

Amyema bifurcata is typically found in sandy soils and is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value.
