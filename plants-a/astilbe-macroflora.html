Astilbe macroflora

Astilbe macroflora, commonly known as the giant aster.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to eastern Asia, particularly China. The plant features upright, branched stems that bear clusters of soft, fluffy, pink or white flowers. Its foliage consists of dark green, serrated leaves that are often used in ornamental gardening.

Astilbe macroflora thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens.
