Aloe amoena

Aloe amoena, commonly known as the slender aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, narrow leaves that are typically blue-green in color, with white to yellowish marginal teeth. The plant produces clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in late summer or early autumn. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Aloe amoena is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional medicinal uses.
