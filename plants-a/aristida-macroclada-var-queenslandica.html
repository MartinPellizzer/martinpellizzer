Aristida macroclada var. queenslandica

Aristida macroclada var. queenslandica, commonly known as Queensland false grass, is a perennial grass species native to Australia.

It typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid regions. The plant has slender, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It is drought-tolerant and well adapted to harsh environmental conditions.

Its botanical features make it a valuable component of native plant communities in its habitat.
