Agathosma nigra

Agathosma nigra, commonly known as African geranium, is a perennial shrub native to South Africa.

It features aromatic, dark green leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. The plant produces small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rocky or coastal gardens.

Its essential oils are valued for their medicinal and aromatic properties.
