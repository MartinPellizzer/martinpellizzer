Aloe broomii

Aloe broomii, commonly known as the silver aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy, gray-green leaves that store water, adapting to arid environments. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and full sun, requiring minimal water once established.

Aloe broomii is prized for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in dry climates.
