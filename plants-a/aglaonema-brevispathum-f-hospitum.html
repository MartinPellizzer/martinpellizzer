Aglaonema brevispathum f. hospitum

Aglaonema brevispathum f. hospitum, commonly known as the Compact Aglaonema, is a striking ornamental plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features broad, dark green leaves with distinctive white and yellow variegation patterns. The plant is noted for its compact growth habit, making it ideal for indoor cultivation. It thrives in low to moderate light conditions and requires consistent moisture without being overwatered.

Its foliage remains vibrant with proper care, adding a decorative touch to any space.
