Anacamptis × nicodemi

Anacamptis × nicodemi, commonly known as the Nicodemi's orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to southern Europe.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its tall, erect stems and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically blooms in late spring to early summer, producing clusters of violet or purple flowers with intricate patterns. It thrives in well-drained soils and partial shade, often found in woodland or meadow habitats.

Anacamptis × nicodemi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its beauty and fragrance.
