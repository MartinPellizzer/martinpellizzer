Aloe mitriformis var. commelyni

Aloe mitriformis var. commelyni, commonly known as the Cape aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, dark green leaves that are adorned with white to yellowish marginal teeth. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellowish-green flowers during the summer months. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun exposure, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its striking appearance and drought tolerance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
