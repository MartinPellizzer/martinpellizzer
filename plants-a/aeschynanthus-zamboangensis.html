Aeschynanthus zamboangensis

Aeschynanthus zamboangensis, commonly known as the Zambangan flame vine, is a tropical climbing plant native to the Philippines.

It features striking, tubular red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as hummingbirds and bees. The plant has heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for hanging baskets and garden displays.
