Aeonium × Extinctum

Aeonium × extinctum is a striking succulent known for its dramatic rosettes of fleshy, brightly colored leaves. Native to the Canary Islands, this plant thrives in warm, dry climates and is prized for its unique appearance. Its leaves often display shades of red, pink, and orange, making it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts. Despite its beauty, it is relatively rare in the wild, leading to increased interest in cultivation. This article explores the care, characteristics, and growing conditions needed to successfully grow Aeonium × extinctum.
