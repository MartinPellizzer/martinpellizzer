Anthurium chlorocardium

Anthurium chlorocardium, commonly known as the green heart anthurium, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a waxy texture. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-shaped inflorescences that are often surrounded by a colorful spathe, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

Anthurium chlorocardium is popular in horticulture for its aesthetic value and relatively easy care requirements.
