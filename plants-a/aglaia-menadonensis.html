Aglaia menadonensis

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly Indonesia, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces yellowish-green fruits that contain smooth, oval seeds. It is often found in lowland tropical rainforests and is valued for its ornamental qualities.
