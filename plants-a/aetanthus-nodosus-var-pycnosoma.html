Aetanthus nodosus var. pycnosoma

Aetanthus nodosus var. pycnosoma, commonly known as the clustered nodding bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and clusters of bell-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of blue or purple. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, while its stems are often hairy.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
