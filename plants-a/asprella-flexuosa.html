Asprella flexuosa

Asprella flexuosa, commonly known as the waxy leaf lily, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, waxy-textured leaves that are often a deep green with a bluish tint. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Asprella flexuosa is also valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
