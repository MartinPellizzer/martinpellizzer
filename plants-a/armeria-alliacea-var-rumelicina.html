Armeria alliacea var. rumelicina

Armeria alliacea var. rumelicina, commonly known as the narrow-leaved thrift, is a perennial flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the family Caryophyllaceae and is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense clusters of white or pinkish flowers. The plant features linear, grass-like leaves and produces showy, bell-shaped blooms that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
