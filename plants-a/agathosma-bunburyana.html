Agathosma bunburyana

Agathosma bunburyana, commonly known as Cape citrus or bunderbush, is a small evergreen shrub native to the Western Cape of South Africa.

It is characterized by its aromatic, narrow leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in spring. The plant is well adapted to dry, sandy soils and is drought-tolerant, making it suitable for xeriscaping. It is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

Its essential oils are also valued in the fragrance and aromatherapy industries.
