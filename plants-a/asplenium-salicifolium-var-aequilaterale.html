Asplenium salicifolium var. aequilaterale

Asplenium salicifolium var. aequilaterale, commonly known as the narrow-leaf spleenwort, is a fern that belongs to the family Aspleniaceae.

It is characterized by its lanceolate, dark green leaves that are typically arranged in a compact rosette. The fronds are usually unlobed and have a smooth, leathery texture. This variety is native to temperate regions and thrives in moist, shaded environments.

It is often found growing on rocks or in shaded forest floors, showcasing its adaptability to various substrates.
