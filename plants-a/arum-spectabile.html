Arum spectabile

Arum spectabile, commonly known as the showy arum, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features a distinctive, heart-shaped leaf that can grow up to 2 meters in length. The plant produces an impressive inflorescence with a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The spathe can range in color from white to deep red, depending on the variety.
