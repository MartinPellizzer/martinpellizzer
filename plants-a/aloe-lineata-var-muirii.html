Aloe lineata var. muirii

Aloe lineata var. muirii, commonly known as the Muir's Aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, narrow leaves that are typically green with a reddish tinge, often adorned with white or yellowish marginal teeth. The plant produces striking, candle-like inflorescences that bear orange to red flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance, it is a popular choice for low-maintenance landscaping.
