Anthurium tapinostachyum

Anthurium tapinostachyum, commonly known as the velvet leaf anthurium, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are velvety to the touch and often display a deep green to dark red coloration. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-like inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, which can range from white to orange or red. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Anthurium tapinostachyum is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to add a dramatic visual impact to indoor spaces.
