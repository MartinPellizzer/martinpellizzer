Acrostemon xeranthemifolius

Acrostemon xeranthemifolius, commonly known as the narrow-leaved spurge or narrow-leaved arrowroot, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features slender, upright stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small and clustered in inflorescences, typically in shades of yellow or orange.

The plant is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its aesthetic appeal in gardens.
