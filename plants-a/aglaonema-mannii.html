Aglaonema mannii

Aglaonema mannii, commonly known as the Chinese Evergreen, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated foliage with shades of green, white, and pink. The plant has arrowhead-shaped leaves that are glossy and often have a waxy texture. It is a member of the Araceae family, which includes other ornamental plants like philodendrons and calla lilies.

Aglaonema mannii is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low light requirements and ability to thrive in humid environments.
