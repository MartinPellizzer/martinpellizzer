Adenosma grandiflora

Adenosma grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered adenosma, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink and violet. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, often reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a rough texture with a strong scent.

Adenosma grandiflora is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and along stream banks.
