Aspalathus plukenetiana

Aspalathus plukenetiana, commonly known as African red bush, is a small evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and vibrant red flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces small, oval-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green in color. It thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Aspalathus plukenetiana is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive foliage and seasonal blooms.
