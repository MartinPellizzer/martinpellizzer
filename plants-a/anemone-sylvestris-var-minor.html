Anemone sylvestris var. minor

Anemone sylvestris var. minor, commonly known as the lesser wood anemone, is a herbaceous perennial native to Europe.

It features delicate, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has trifoliate leaves with a hairy texture, and its stems are often reddish in color. It thrives in woodland environments and prefers moist, well-drained soils.

This species is often found in shaded areas and is part of the Ranunculaceae family.
