Arisaema serratum f. thunbergii

Arisaema serratum f. thunbergii, commonly known as the Thunberg's Chinese arum, is a variant of the Chinese arum characterized by its distinctive leaf morphology and inflorescence.

It is a perennial herb native to eastern Asia, particularly China, Korea, and Japan, often found in moist, shaded environments. The plant features a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe, which is typical of the Araceae family. Its leaves are typically arrow-shaped with serrated edges, contributing to its ornamental value.

The plant is also noted for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal medicine.
