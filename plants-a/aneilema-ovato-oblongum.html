Aneilema ovato-oblongum

Aneilema ovato-oblongum, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its velvety, waxy leaves that are ovate-oblong in shape. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of purple, pink, and white, often with a contrasting darker center. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Aneilema ovato-oblongum is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
