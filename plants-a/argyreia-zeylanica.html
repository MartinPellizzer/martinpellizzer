Argyreia zeylanica

Argyreia zeylanica, commonly known as the Indian hibiscus or red hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, pink, or white. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and a strong, aromatic scent. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Argyreia zeylanica is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
