Aerva arachnoidea

Aerva arachnoidea, commonly known as spider plant or spiderflower, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its distinctive, fluffy seed heads that resemble spider webs, which give it its common name. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that are clustered in dense, spherical inflorescences. It is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and ability to thrive in various conditions.

Aerva arachnoidea is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal practices.
