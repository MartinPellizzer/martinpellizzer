Asphodeliris falcata

Asphodeliris falcata, commonly known as the sword leaf asphodel, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Asphodelaceae and is characterized by its sword-shaped leaves that are usually narrow and grass-like. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing small, tubular flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Asphodeliris falcata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage and seasonal blooms.
