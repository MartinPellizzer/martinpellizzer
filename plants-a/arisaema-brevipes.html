Arisaema brevipes

Arisaema brevipes, commonly known as the short-footed arrowroot, is a member of the Araceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of China, India, and Indonesia. The plant features a short, rhizomatous stem with a single, large, heart-shaped leaf that is often covered in a dense layer of white, waxy hairs. Its inflorescence consists of a spadix surrounded by a spathe, which is typically green with a distinctive purple stripe.

Arisaema brevipes is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
