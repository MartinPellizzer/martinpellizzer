Arachnorchis × aestantha

Arachnorchis × aestantha, commonly known as the hybrid spider orchid, is a fascinating orchid species native to Australia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, spider-like appearance due to its elongated, thread-like petals and sepals. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forests or under dense vegetation. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellow, with intricate patterns that aid in pollination by specific insect species.

Arachnorchis × aestantha is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated by enthusiasts for its delicate and unusual beauty.
