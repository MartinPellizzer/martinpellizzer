Asterogyne martiana

Asterogyne martiana, commonly known as the Malabar starling tree, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of India and Sri Lanka.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree that can grow up to 15 meters in height. The plant is characterized by its dense foliage and distinctive star-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and elliptical, with a glossy green surface.

Asterogyne martiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
