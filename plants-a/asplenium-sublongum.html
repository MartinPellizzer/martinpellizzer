Asplenium sublongum

Asplenium sublongum, commonly known as the long-leaf spleenwort, is a species of fern belonging to the family Aspleniaceae.

It is characterized by its narrow, elongated fronds that are typically less than 30 centimeters in length. The plant has a distinctive structure with a creeping rhizome and small, lanceolate leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found growing on rocks or in shaded forest floors.

Asplenium sublongum is a hardy plant that is relatively easy to cultivate in suitable conditions.
