Aeschynomene glabrescens var. glabrescens

Aeschynomene glabrescens var. glabrescens, commonly known as partridge pea, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is a warm-season annual legume characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow-orange flowers. The plant has a robust root system that helps prevent soil erosion and improves soil fertility through nitrogen fixation. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it suitable for use in restoration projects and as a forage crop.

Its seeds are also a valuable food source for birds and small mammals.
