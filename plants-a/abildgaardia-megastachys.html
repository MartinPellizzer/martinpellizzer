Abildgaardia megastachys

Abildgaardia megastachys, commonly known as the giant flame tree, is a striking tropical plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its large, showy red flowers that bloom in clusters, creating a dramatic visual impact. The plant has compound leaves with long, narrow leaflets, and it produces woody pods that contain seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Due to its size and unique appearance, it is a popular choice for landscaping in tropical and subtropical regions.
