Agrostis macrothyrsa

Agrostis macrothyrsa, commonly known as the broad-panicle grass, is a perennial grass species native to Europe, Asia, and Africa.

It typically grows in open habitats such as meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils. The plant is characterized by its tufted growth habit and slender, erect stems that can reach heights of up to 60 cm. Its inflorescence consists of a single, broad, open panicle, which is often branched and covered with small spikelets.

The leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green color and a hairy underside.
