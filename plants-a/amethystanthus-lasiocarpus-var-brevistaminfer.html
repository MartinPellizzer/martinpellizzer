Amethystanthus lasiocarpus var. brevistaminfer

Amethystanthus lasiocarpus var. brevistaminfer, commonly known as the short-vested amethyst flower, is a rare and delicate plant native to specific regions of South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking violet to purple flowers, which bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant has a compact growth habit with hairy, glandular leaves that help it retain moisture in arid conditions. Its unique varietal name, *brevistaminfer*, refers to its shorter, more condensed stipules compared to the typical form.

Due to its limited distribution and specialized habitat, it is considered a rare and protected species in its native environment.
