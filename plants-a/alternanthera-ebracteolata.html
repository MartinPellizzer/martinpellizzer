Alternanthera ebracteolata

Alternanthera ebracteolata, commonly known as the red leafAlternanthera, is a striking aquatic plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red or pink foliage, which becomes more intense under strong light conditions. The plant has slender, branching stems and small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters. It thrives in shallow, warm waters and is often used in aquascaping for its aesthetic appeal.
