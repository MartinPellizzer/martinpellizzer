Anacyclus australis

Anacyclus australis, commonly known as the southern globe thistle, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb with a distinctive globular shape, featuring a dense cluster of blue or purple flowers atop a short stem. The plant has basal leaves that are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and rocky areas.

Anacyclus australis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
