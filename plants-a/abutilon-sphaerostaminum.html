Abutilon sphaerostaminum

Abutilon sphaerostaminum, commonly known as the globe hibiscus, is a shrub native to the Canary Islands.

It is characterized by its woolly, hairy stems and large, showy flowers that bloom in shades of pink, red, and white. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small, round seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its unique appearance and adaptability contribute to its presence in both natural and cultivated landscapes.
