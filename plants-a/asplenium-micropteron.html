Asplenium micropteron

Asplenium micropteron, commonly known as the small-leaf maidenhair fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Aspleniaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds that are typically less than 10 centimeters in length. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit, with creeping stems that produce new shoots along their length. Its leaves are usually dark green and have a leathery texture, with a distinctive, narrow shape.

Asplenium micropteron thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas or along stream banks.
