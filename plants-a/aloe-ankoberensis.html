Aloe ankoberensis

Aloe ankoberensis, commonly known as the Ankober aloe, is a succulent plant native to Ethiopia.

It belongs to the Asphodelaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are thick and fleshy. The leaves are typically green with a waxy coating, which helps the plant retain moisture in its arid habitat. It produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow flowers, which bloom in the spring.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional uses in local medicine.
