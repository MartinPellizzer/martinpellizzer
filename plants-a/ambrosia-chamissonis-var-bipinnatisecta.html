Ambrosia chamissonis var. bipinnatisecta

Ambrosia chamissonis var. bipinnatisecta, commonly known as the narrow-leaf Ambrosia, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its finely divided, bipinnatisect leaves that give it a feathery appearance. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in dense, branched clusters called panicles. It thrives in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils.
