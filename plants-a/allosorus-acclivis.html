Allosorus acclivis

Allosorus acclivis, commonly known as the mountain helleborine, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

The plant produces showy, greenish-yellow flowers with prominent, hood-like structures that enclose the reproductive organs.
