Anettea × duveenii

Anettea × duveenii, commonly known as the hybrid anettea, is a perennial plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its finely divided, fern-like leaves and produces small, daisy-like flowers in shades of white and yellow. This plant is native to the southeastern United States and thrives in moist, well-drained soils. It is often found in wetlands, stream banks, and other riparian habitats.

Anettea × duveenii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting pollinators.
