Alstroemeria nubigena

Alstroemeria nubigena, commonly known as the cloud alstroemeria, is a flowering plant native to the Andes mountains of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that come in a range of colors, including red, orange, and yellow. The plant typically grows in high-altitude regions, where it thrives in cool, moist climates. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a spiral pattern around the stem.

Alstroemeria nubigena is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
