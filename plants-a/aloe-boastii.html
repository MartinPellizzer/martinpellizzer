Aloe boastii

Aloe boastii, commonly known as the Cape aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, spindly stems and dense rosettes of fleshy, sword-shaped leaves. The leaves are typically green with a waxy coating and often have a red or purple tinge at the base. It produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow to orange flowers during the summer months.

This plant is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
