Aa lorentzii

Aa lorentzii, commonly known as the Lorentz's aroid, is a flowering plant native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive inflorescence consisting of a spadix surrounded by a spathe. The plant features broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white and green patterns. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Aa lorentzii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated as a houseplant.
