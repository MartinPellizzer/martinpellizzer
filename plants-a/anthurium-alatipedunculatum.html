Anthurium alatipedunculatum

Anthurium alatipedunculatum, commonly known as the broad-leaf anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often bright green with a waxy texture. The plant produces striking spadices, which are the modified leaves that enclose the true flowers. It thrives in humid, warm environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.

Anthurium alatipedunculatum is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and ability to purify indoor air.
