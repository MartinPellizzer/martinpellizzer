Astragalus schuschnasensis

Astragalus schuschnasensis, commonly known as Schuschnas' milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its small, yellow flowers and compound leaves. The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments, adapting to harsh conditions with its deep root system.

It is often found in rocky soils and is part of the diverse Astragalus genus, which includes many species with medicinal and ecological significance.
