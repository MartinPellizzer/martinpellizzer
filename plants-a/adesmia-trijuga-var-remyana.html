Adesmia trijuga var. remyana

Adesmia trijuga var. remyana, commonly known as the white-flowered legume, is a perennial shrub native to South America.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod-bearing fruits. The plant features trifoliate leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces small, white or pale pink flowers arranged in clusters. Its root system is well-developed, enabling it to thrive in various soil types.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential ecological benefits in nitrogen fixation.
