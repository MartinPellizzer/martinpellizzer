Aichryson bituminosum

Aichryson bituminosum, commonly known as the bituminous aichryson, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, thick leaves that store water, adapting to arid environments. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation.
