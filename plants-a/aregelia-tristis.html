Aregelia tristis

Aregelia tristis, commonly known as the weeping begonia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its trailing stems and clusters of small, pink or white flowers. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

Aregelia tristis is often used in hanging baskets or as a ground cover due to its cascading growth habit.
