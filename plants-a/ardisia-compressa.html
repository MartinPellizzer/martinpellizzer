Ardisia compressa

Ardisia compressa, commonly known as the glossy buckeye, is a deciduous shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are elliptical in shape and often have a waxy texture. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers in clusters, followed by bright red berries that are attractive to birds. Ardisia compressa thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to form dense thickets.
