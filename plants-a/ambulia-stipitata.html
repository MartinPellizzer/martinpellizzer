Ambulia stipitata

Ambulia stipitata, commonly known as the velvetleaf.

It is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia. The plant is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in a dense layer of fine, velvety hairs. Its flowers are small and clustered in spikes, typically yellow or greenish in color.

Ambulia stipitata is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands.
