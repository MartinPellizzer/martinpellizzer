Anguloa × macrantha

Anguloa × macrantha, commonly known as the hybrid anguloa, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Anguloa lutea and Anguloa macrantha, characterized by its large, showy flowers. The plant typically produces vibrant pink or red blooms with a distinctive, elongated shape. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Anguloa × macrantha is often cultivated for its ornamental value in tropical gardens and greenhouses.
