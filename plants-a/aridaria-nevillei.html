Aridaria nevillei

Aridaria nevillei, commonly known as the Neville's arid shrub, is a hardy plant adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.

It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is found in regions with low annual rainfall. The plant has small, waxy leaves that help reduce water loss through transpiration. It produces small, often inconspicuous flowers that are usually pale in color.

Aridaria nevillei plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for local wildlife in its native range.
