Aclisia celebica

Aclisia celebica, commonly known as the Celebes moonvine, is a climbing vine native to Indonesia.

It belongs to the family Convolvulaceae and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and fragrant, white to pale purple flowers. The plant produces small, fleshy fruits that contain seeds covered in a sticky substance. It thrives in tropical climates and is often found in humid, shaded environments.
