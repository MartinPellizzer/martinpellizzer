Areca dealbata

Areca dealbata, commonly known as the white areca palm, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and feathery, fan-shaped leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its leaves are often used in traditional crafts, and the palm is valued for its ornamental beauty in gardens and landscapes.

Areca dealbata is also known for its ability to purify the air, making it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor cultivation.
