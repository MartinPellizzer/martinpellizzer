Allium djimilense

Allium djimilense, commonly known as the Djimilense onion, is a species of flowering plant in the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is native to the arid regions of northern Africa, particularly Morocco and Algeria. The plant features a bulbous stem with narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of white or pale purple flowers. It is adapted to survive in dry conditions, with a deep root system that helps it access water.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and has some traditional uses in local communities.
