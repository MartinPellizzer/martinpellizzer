Aristida paraguayensis

Aristida paraguayensis, commonly known as Paraguay threeawn, is a grass species native to South America.

It is characterized by its slender, wiry stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces fluffy, three-awned inflorescences that are typical of the Aristida genus. It thrives in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils.

Its botanical features make it an important component of its native ecosystem.
