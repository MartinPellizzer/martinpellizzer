Azolla filiculoides subsp. filiculoides

Azolla filiculoides subsp.

filiculoides, commonly known as the water fern or mosquito fern, is a small, free-floating aquatic plant belonging to the family Araceae. It forms dense mats on the surface of freshwater bodies, often in slow-moving or still water.

The plant has small, palmately divided leaves and is capable of rapid vegetative propagation through budding. It is known for its ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, contributing to nutrient cycling in aquatic ecosystems.

This species is widely distributed in tropical and subtropical regions around the world.
