Alaticaulia luziaemariae

Alaticaulia luziaemariae, commonly known as the Luziaemariae tree, is a species native to the Atlantic Forest of Brazil.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree with smooth, dark bark and elliptical leaves that are typically arranged alternately along the branches. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters, which are followed by woody, capsule-like fruits containing several seeds.

Its botanical features make it an important component of the region's biodiversity and ecological balance.
