Aponogeton gracilis

Aponogeton gracilis, commonly known as the slender arrowhead, is a tropical aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features slender, ribbon-like leaves that float on the water surface, with long, thin roots that hang beneath the water. The plant produces small, white flowers that are typically submerged, adding a delicate touch to its aquatic habitat. It thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments with moderate to high light exposure.

Aponogeton gracilis is popular in aquascaping due to its elegant appearance and ease of care.
