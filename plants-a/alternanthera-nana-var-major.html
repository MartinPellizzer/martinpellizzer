Alternanthera nana var. major

Alternanthera nana var. major, commonly known as the dwarf Alternanthera, is a compact, low-growing herbaceous plant native to South America.

It features vibrant, colorful foliage that ranges from red to pink and purple, depending on light conditions. The leaves are ovate to lanceolate in shape and often have a velvety texture. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow, which appear in clusters at the leaf axils.

This plant is popular in aquatic and bog gardens due to its ornamental value and adaptability to moist environments.
