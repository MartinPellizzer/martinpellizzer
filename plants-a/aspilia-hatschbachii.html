Aspilia hatschbachii

Aspilia hatschbachii, commonly known as the African flame flower, is a striking herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features showy, tubular red flowers that bloom in summer, attracting pollinators such as birds and insects. The plant has a short, upright stem with lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its unique floral structure and vibrant color make it a notable species in the Asteraceae family.
