Alexitoxicon ochroleucum

Alexitoxicon ochroleucum, commonly known as the yellow-leaved saxifrage, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Saxifragaceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow foliage and small, clustered white or pink flowers that bloom in early summer. Native to alpine regions of Europe, this plant thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and cool climates. It has a compact growth habit and is often found in mountainous areas with high altitude.

Due to its specialized habitat, it is considered a rare and sensitive indicator of environmental health.
