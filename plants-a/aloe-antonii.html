Aloe antonii

Aloe antonii, commonly known as the mountain aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, covered in a layer of white powdery wax. The plant produces striking yellow flowers that bloom in summer, attracting pollinators like bees and birds. Aloe antonii is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its leaves to survive dry conditions.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
