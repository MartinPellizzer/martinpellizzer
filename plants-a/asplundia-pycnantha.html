Asplundia pycnantha

Asplundia pycnantha, commonly known as the clustered arrowroot, is a tropical plant belonging to the Araceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in regions like Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant features a short, stocky rhizome with dense clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers. Its leaves are typically broad and arrow-shaped, contributing to its common name.

Asplundia pycnantha is often cultivated for its ornamental value and as a source of starch.
