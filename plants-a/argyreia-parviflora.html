Argyreia parviflora

Argyreia parviflora, commonly known as the velvet flower or velvet leaf, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety purple flowers that bloom in clusters, often with a faintly scented aroma. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are typically a deep green color. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Argyreia parviflora is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and unique foliage.
