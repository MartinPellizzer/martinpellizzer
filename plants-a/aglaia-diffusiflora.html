Aglaia diffusiflora

Aglaia diffusiflora, commonly known as the spreading aglaia, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Meliaceae family, which includes mahogany and other tropical hardwoods. The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. Its flowers are small, fragrant, and typically yellow or greenish-yellow, arranged in clusters.
