Athyrium lewalleanum

Athyrium lewalleanum, commonly known as the Lepidote Athyrium, is a fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Athyriaceae and is characterized by its delicate, bipinnate fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its rhizomes are often covered in a layer of fine, scale-like structures, giving it its common name.

The foliage displays a range of colors from light green to deep bronze, depending on the light conditions.
