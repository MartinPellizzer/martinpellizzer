Aloe boiteaui

Aloe boiteaui, commonly known as the Cape aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are thick and fleshy, adapted for storing water in arid environments. The leaves are typically green with a waxy coating, and some varieties may have red or purple hues at the base. It produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow or orange flowers during the warmer months.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has mild medicinal properties.
