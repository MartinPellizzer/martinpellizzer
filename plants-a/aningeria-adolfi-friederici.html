Aningeria adolfi-friederici

Aningeria adolfi-friederici, commonly known as the African sandalwood, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the family Burseraceae and is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and lanceolate, leathery leaves. Due to overharvesting and habitat loss, it is also considered a threatened species in its native range.
