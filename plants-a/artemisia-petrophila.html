Artemisia petrophila

Artemisia petrophila, commonly known as desert sagebrush, is a perennial shrub native to arid and semi-arid regions of North America.

It is characterized by its aromatic, silvery-gray foliage and small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to harsh environmental conditions. It plays a vital role in stabilizing soils and providing habitat for various desert wildlife.

Its essential oils have been studied for potential medicinal and aromatic uses.
