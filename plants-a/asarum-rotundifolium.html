Asarum rotundifolium

Asarum rotundifolium, commonly known as Indian snakeroot, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the eastern United States.

It features heart-shaped, reddish-purple leaves that are often covered in fine, white hairs. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in a spike at the base of the leaves. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

The plant is valued for its aromatic properties and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
