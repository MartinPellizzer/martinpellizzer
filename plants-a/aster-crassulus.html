Aster crassulus

Aster crassulus, commonly known as the silver aster, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features compact, fleshy leaves that are covered in a silvery-gray coating, which helps reduce water loss in arid conditions. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white to violet, typically blooming in late summer to early autumn. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Due to its drought tolerance and ornamental appeal, Aster crassulus is often cultivated in xeriscapes and coastal gardens.
