Alstroemeria pulchra

Alstroemeria pulchra, commonly known as the red alstroemeria, is a striking perennial plant native to South America.

It belongs to the family Alstroemeriaceae and is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that come in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant features strap-like leaves that are typically dark green and slightly wavy in appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.

Alstroemeria pulchra is also valued for its long-lasting blooms and ability to tolerate cooler climates.
