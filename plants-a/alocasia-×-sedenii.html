Alocasia × sedenii

Alocasia × sedenii, commonly known as the giant elephant ear, is a hybrid plant that showcases striking, large, heart-shaped leaves with a glossy, dark green surface.

It originates from the cross between Alocasia odora and Alocasia macrorrhiza, resulting in a unique combination of traits from both parents. The plant is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia and thrives in warm, humid environments with high light exposure. Its foliage is often used in ornamental gardening for its dramatic visual impact.

Alocasia × sedenii requires well-draining soil and consistent moisture to maintain its health and vibrant appearance.
