Anthurium variegatum

Anthurium variegatum, commonly known as the Variegated Anthurium, is a tropical flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that display a mix of green, white, and red hues. The plant produces vibrant spadices, which are modified leaves that enclose the true flowers. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium variegatum is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
