Ayenia subtilis

Ayenia subtilis, commonly known as the slender ayenia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or stream banks. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, while its flowers consist of both ray and disk florets.

Ayenia subtilis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting pollinators.
