Arundinella sinensis

Arundinella sinensis, commonly known as Chinese bamboo or golden bamboo, is a perennial grass native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems that can grow up to 3 meters in height. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are usually green with a slight sheen. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters at the base of the stems.

Arundinella sinensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional Chinese medicine.
