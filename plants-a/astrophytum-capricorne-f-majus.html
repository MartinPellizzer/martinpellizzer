Astrophytum capricorne f. majus

Astrophytum capricorne f. majus, commonly known as the giant star cactus, is a unique cactus species native to the Chihuahuan Desert in Mexico.

It is characterized by its large, ribbed stems that can grow up to 3 meters in height, giving it a distinctive, tree-like appearance. The plant produces yellow flowers during the summer months, which are followed by red, spherical fruits. It has a slow growth rate and requires well-draining soil and minimal water to thrive.

Due to its striking appearance and rarity, it is often cultivated by cactus enthusiasts.
