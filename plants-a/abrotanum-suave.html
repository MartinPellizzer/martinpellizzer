Abrotanum suave

Abrotanum suave, commonly known as sweet everlasting, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, showy yellow flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant has aromatic leaves and stems, which are often used in herbal remedies. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

Its common name reflects the pleasant scent it emits and its ability to retain its appearance for long periods.
