Arctostaphylos Andersonii Var. Pallida

Arctostaphylos andersonii var. pallida is a rare and fascinating evergreen shrub native to the coastal regions of California. It is known for its distinctive pale pink to white flowers that bloom in late summer. This plant thrives in sandy soils and is often found in coastal chaparral habitats. Due to its limited range and habitat specificity, it is considered a species of conservation concern. Its unique appearance and ecological importance make it a noteworthy subject for botanical study.
