Alpinia tonkinensis

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like ginger and turmeric. The plant is characterized by its tall, rhizomatous growth and clusters of fragrant, pale yellow to white flowers. Its leaves are large, lance-shaped, and often have a distinct serrated edge.
