Arisaema formosanum f. stenophyllum

Arisaema formosanum f. stenophyllum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved Taiwanese arrowroot, is a variant of the Arisaema genus native to Taiwan.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and a distinctive, spadix-like inflorescence surrounded by a hooded spathe. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as mountain forests. Its botanical features include a tuberous rhizome and showy, often colorful floral structures.

It is valued both for its ornamental appeal and its traditional use in local herbal medicine.
