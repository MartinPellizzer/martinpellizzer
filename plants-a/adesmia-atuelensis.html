Adesmia atuelensis

Adesmia atuelensis, commonly known as the Atuel burrobrush, is a flowering plant native to Argentina.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its small, clustered yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. Its leaves are compound, consisting of several small leaflets, and it produces small, pod-like fruits.
