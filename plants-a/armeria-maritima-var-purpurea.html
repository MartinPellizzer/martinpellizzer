Armeria maritima var. purpurea

Armeria maritima var. purpurea, commonly known as the purple sandmat, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of the United States and Canada.

It is a low-growing perennial that thrives in sandy, well-drained soils and full sun. The plant produces clusters of small, pink to purple flowers that bloom in early summer. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that form a dense mat.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to stabilize coastal dunes.
