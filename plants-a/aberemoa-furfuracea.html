Aberemoa furfuracea

Aberemoa furfuracea, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical plant belonging to the Malvaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in a dense layer of fine, velvety hairs. The plant produces showy, yellow flowers that are often clustered in terminal inflorescences. It is native to Central and South America and thrives in warm, humid climates.

Due to its invasive nature, it is often considered a weed in agricultural settings.
