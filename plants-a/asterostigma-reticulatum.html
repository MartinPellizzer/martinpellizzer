Asterostigma reticulatum

Asterostigma reticulatum, commonly known as the net-spotted aster, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features small, showy florets arranged in a dense, often colorful cluster. Its leaves are typically narrow and slightly serrated, with a textured surface.

Asterostigma reticulatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic value.
