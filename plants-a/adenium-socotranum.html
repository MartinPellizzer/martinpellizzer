Adenium socotranum

Adenium socotranum, commonly known as the Socotra candelabra, is a unique plant native to the Socotra archipelago.

It is characterized by its thick, swollen trunk and branching structure that resembles a candlestick. The plant produces yellow or red flowers and has distinctive, waxy leaves that are adapted to the arid environment. It is a succulent that stores water in its trunk, allowing it to survive in dry conditions.

The plant is also known for its toxic sap and has cultural significance in the region.
