Aster unctuosus

Aster unctuosus, commonly known as the woolly aster, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its fuzzy, silver-gray leaves and showy, daisy-like flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and woodland edges. Its flowers are usually blue to purple in color and attract various pollinators.

Aster unctuosus is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in supporting local wildlife.
