Arachnorchis × exserta

Arachnorchis × exserta, commonly known as the hybrid spider orchid, is a fascinating member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a terrestrial herb with slender, wiry stems and small, greenish-brown pseudobulbs. The plant produces delicate, spider-like flowers with elongated petals and sepals, often in shades of green, brown, and purple. It thrives in shaded, moist environments, typically found in temperate regions of Australia.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
