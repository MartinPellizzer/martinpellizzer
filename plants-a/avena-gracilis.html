Avena gracilis

Avena gracilis, commonly known as slender oat, is a herbaceous annual plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features slender, cylindrical stems that are typically less than 30 centimeters in height. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences with numerous tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue.

Avena gracilis is often found in disturbed soils and is sometimes cultivated as a forage crop.
