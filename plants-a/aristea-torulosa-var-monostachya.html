Aristea torulosa var. monostachya

Aristea torulosa var. monostachya, commonly known as the narrow-leaved aristea, is a perennial grass-like plant native to southern Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and is often found in rocky or sandy soils. It is adapted to arid environments and is drought-tolerant, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in native plant gardens.
