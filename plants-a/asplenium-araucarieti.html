Asplenium araucarieti

Asplenium araucarieti, commonly known as the Araucaria spleenwort, is a fern belonging to the family Aspleniaceae.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that typically grows on the trunks of trees in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant has lance-shaped, serrated leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. It produces spores in specialized structures called sori, which are located on the underside of the leaves.

Asplenium araucarieti is often found in humid, shaded environments, contributing to the biodiversity of forest ecosystems.
