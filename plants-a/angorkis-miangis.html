Angorkis miangis

Angorkis miangis, commonly known as the silverleaf orchid, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and striking, pale-blue flowers that bloom during the cooler months. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot. Its unique appearance makes it a sought-after species among botanists and horticulturists.
