Avena bromoides var. microstachya

Avena bromoides var. microstachya, commonly known as slender oat, is a perennial grass species native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences with numerous short, bract-like flowers. It thrives in dry, rocky habitats and is often found in open woodlands and grasslands.

Its botanical features make it an important component of native plant communities and a valuable forage species.
