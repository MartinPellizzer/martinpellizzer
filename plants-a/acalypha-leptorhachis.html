Acalypha leptorhachis

Acalypha leptorhachis, commonly known as the velvetleaf or silky hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Pacific.

The plant has palmate leaves with a velvety texture, which contribute to its ornamental appeal.
