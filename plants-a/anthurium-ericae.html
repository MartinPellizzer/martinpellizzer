Anthurium ericae

Anthurium ericae, commonly known as the Erica Anthurium, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are often deep green with a waxy texture. The plant produces vibrant red spathes that enclose its yellow spadix, creating a dramatic floral display. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium ericae is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
