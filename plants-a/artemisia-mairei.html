Artemisia mairei

Artemisia mairei, commonly known as Maire's wormwood, is a perennial shrub native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, silver-gray foliage. The plant produces small, yellowish flower heads that bloom in the summer months. Artemisia mairei is often found in rocky or well-drained soils at higher elevations.

It is valued for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional Chinese medicine.
