Asplenium harpeodes var. incisum

Asplenium harpeodes var. incisum, commonly known as the incised shield fern, is a fern species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its intricate, finely divided fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests or rocky crevices. Its rhizomes are often covered in a dense, fibrous layer that aids in water retention.
