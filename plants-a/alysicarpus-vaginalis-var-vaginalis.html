Alysicarpus vaginalis var. vaginalis

Alysicarpus vaginalis var. vaginalis, commonly known as the slender burr sida, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Pacific Islands. The plant is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, trifoliate leaves. Its flowers are typically pink or purple and are followed by distinctive, spiny pods that contain small, round seeds.

This species is often found in disturbed soils and can be invasive in certain areas.
