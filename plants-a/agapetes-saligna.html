Agapetes saligna

Agapetes saligna, commonly known as the silverleaf agapetes, is a species of flowering plant in the Ericaceae family.

It is native to the temperate regions of South America, particularly in the Andes. Agapetes saligna is often found in moist, shaded environments such as cloud forests and is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.
