Aloysia looseri

Aloysia looseri, commonly known as the silverleaf honeysuckle, is a shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and fragrant, tubular flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant belongs to the Verbenaceae family and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

Its nectar attracts various pollinators, including bees and butterflies.
