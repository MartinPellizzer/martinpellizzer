Anthurium ocotepecense

Anthurium ocotepecense, commonly known as the Ocotlan anthurium, is a tropical plant native to southern Mexico.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves and vibrant, spatulate inflorescence. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in montane forests. Its foliage is glossy and dark green, with a unique veining pattern.

Anthurium ocotepecense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant.
