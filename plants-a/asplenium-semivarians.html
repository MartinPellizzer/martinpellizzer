Asplenium semivarians

Asplenium semivarians, commonly known as the narrow fern, is a species of fern native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the family Aspleniaceae and is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its rhizomes are wiry and often form dense clusters, allowing it to spread vegetatively.

The leaves are usually dark green and have a leathery texture, contributing to its ornamental appeal.
