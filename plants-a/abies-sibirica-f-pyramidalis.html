Abies sibirica f. pyramidalis

Abies sibirica f. pyramidalis, commonly known as the Siberian fir pyramidalis, is a compact, coniferous evergreen shrub with a conical shape.

It features needle-like leaves that are dark green and densely packed, giving it a dense appearance. The plant produces small, spherical cones that mature to a reddish-brown color. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant in gardens.

This variety is particularly valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in colder climates.
