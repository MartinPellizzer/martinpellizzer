Adenophora marsupiiflora var. crispata

Adenophora marsupiiflora var. crispata, commonly known as the crinkled bellflower, is a flowering plant native to China.

This perennial plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate blooms.
