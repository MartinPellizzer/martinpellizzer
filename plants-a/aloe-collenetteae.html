Aloe collenetteae

Aloe collenetteae, commonly known as the Collenette's aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, with a waxy coating to reduce water loss. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers during the warmer months, which are clustered in inflorescences. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal watering.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
