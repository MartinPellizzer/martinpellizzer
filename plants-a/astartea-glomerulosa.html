Astartea glomerulosa

Astartea glomerulosa, commonly known as the clustered starwort, is a herbaceous plant belonging to the Apiaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, clustered inflorescences that resemble stars, giving it its common name. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Astartea glomerulosa is known for its delicate white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.
