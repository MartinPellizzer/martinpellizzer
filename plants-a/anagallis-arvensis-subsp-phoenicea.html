Anagallis arvensis subsp. phoenicea

Anagallis arvensis subsp.

phoenicea, commonly known as the redstem mouse-ear, is a flowering plant characterized by its slender, hairy stems and small, pink to red flowers. It is native to southern Europe and parts of western Asia, often found in disturbed soils and open habitats.
