Agathosma cerefolia var. glandulosa

Agathosma cerefolia var. glandulosa, commonly known as the glandular agathosma, is a South African plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its aromatic, glandular leaves that emit a strong, resinous scent. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in fynbos vegetation. Its small, daisy-like flowers are yellow and appear in spring.

It is used in traditional medicine and as a source of essential oils due to its unique chemical composition.
