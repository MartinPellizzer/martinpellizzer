Adenocalymma fruticosum

Adenocalymma fruticosum, commonly known as the bushy adenocalymma, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows in coastal and sandy soils, often found in the Cape Province. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences, which attract various pollinators. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and often covered in fine hairs.

Adenocalymma fruticosum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
