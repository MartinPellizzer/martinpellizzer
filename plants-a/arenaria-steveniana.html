Arenaria steveniana

Arenaria steveniana, commonly known as Steven's sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and western Asia.

It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in alpine and subalpine habitats. The plant has small, clustered leaves and produces delicate white or pink flowers in early summer. It is characterized by its succulent appearance and ability to withstand harsh environmental conditions.

Arenaria steveniana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in rock gardens.
