Arenaria cerastiiformis

Arenaria cerastiiformis, commonly known as the sandstone pink, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial that thrives in rocky, well-drained soils. The plant produces small, pink to purple flowers during the spring and summer months. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are adapted to arid conditions.

It is often found in coastal areas and is valued for its ornamental appeal in rock gardens.
