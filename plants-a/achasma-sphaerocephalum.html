Achasma sphaerocephalum

Achasma sphaerocephalum, commonly known as the globe-headed achasma, is a unique member of the Apiaceae family.

It is characterized by its distinctive spherical or globe-like flower heads, which give it its common name. Achasma sphaerocephalum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens with well-drained soil.
