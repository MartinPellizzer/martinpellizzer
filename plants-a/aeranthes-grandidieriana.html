Aeranthes grandidieriana

Aeranthes grandidieriana, commonly known as the Madagascar orchid, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its flowers are usually pale green or yellow with intricate patterns, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits.

Aeranthes grandidieriana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
