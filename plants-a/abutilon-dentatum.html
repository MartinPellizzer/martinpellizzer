Abutilon dentatum

Abutilon dentatum, commonly known as the velvetleaf abutilon, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its fuzzy, velvety leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces showy, lantern-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to red. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in disturbed soils.

Abutilon dentatum is cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
