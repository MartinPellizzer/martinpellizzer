Astragalus melanotrichos

Astragalus melanotrichos, commonly known as the black-haired milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to central Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, pea-like flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is known for its deep root system that helps it survive in arid conditions.

Astragalus melanotrichos is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal medicine.
