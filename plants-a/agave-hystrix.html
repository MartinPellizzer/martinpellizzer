Agave hystrix

Agave hystrix, commonly known as the spiny leaf agave, is a succulent plant native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its thick, spiny leaves that form a rosette shape, with the spines varying in length and color. The plant produces a tall, branched flower stalk that can reach several meters in height, bearing yellowish-green flowers. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, storing water in its fleshy leaves.

Agave hystrix is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has cultural significance in traditional practices.
