Anthurium manabianum

Anthurium manabianum, commonly known as the Manabian arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to Central America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its glossy, heart-shaped leaves and striking spadix inflorescence. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its foliage is often dark green with a waxy texture, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers surrounded by a colorful spathe.

Anthurium manabianum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for indoor gardening.
