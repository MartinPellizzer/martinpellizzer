Apocynum × viride

Apocynum × viride, commonly known as green milkweed, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Apocynaceae family.

It is characterized by its smooth, dark green leaves and clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. The plant produces a milky sap that is toxic to many animals, including livestock. It thrives in a variety of soil types and is often found in disturbed areas such as roadsides and fields.

Green milkweed is an important plant for certain pollinators, though its toxicity limits its use in horticulture.
