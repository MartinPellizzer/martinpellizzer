Aloe × tricolor

Aloe × tricolor, commonly known as the rainbow aloe, is a hybrid succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated foliage that displays shades of green, white, and pink. The plant features thick, fleshy leaves that store water, a typical adaptation to arid environments. It produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow flowers, which bloom in late spring or early summer.

Aloe × tricolor is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.
