Amyema aneityensis

Amyema aneityensis, commonly known as the slender lomatia, is a rare and critically endangered plant species native to Western Australia.

It is a shrub characterized by its small, leathery leaves and unique, lantern-like inflorescences. The plant produces small, white flowers that are pollinated by insects, and its seeds are dispersed by wind. Due to habitat loss and limited distribution, it is highly vulnerable to environmental changes.

Conservation efforts are ongoing to protect and propagate this rare botanical species.
