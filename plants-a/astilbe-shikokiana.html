Astilbe shikokiana

Astilbe shikokiana, commonly known as the Japanese astilbe, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Japan.

It features upright, branched stems that bear clusters of delicate, feathery panicles in shades of pink, white, or red. The plant typically grows to a height of 2 to 3 feet and thrives in moist, well-drained soil. It prefers partial to full shade and is often used in woodland gardens for its ornamental foliage and flowering display.

Astilbe shikokiana is valued for its ability to add texture and color to shaded garden spaces.
