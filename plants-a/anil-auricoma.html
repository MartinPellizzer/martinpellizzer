Anil auricoma

Anil auricoma, commonly known as golden crown grass, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features slender, upright stems that are often covered in fine, silky hairs. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences with golden-yellow flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. Its leaves are narrow, lance-shaped, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Anil auricoma is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive foliage and vibrant floral display.
