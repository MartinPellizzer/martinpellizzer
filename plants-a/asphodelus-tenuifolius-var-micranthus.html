Asphodelus tenuifolius var. micranthus

Asphodelus tenuifolius var. micranthus, commonly known as the narrow-leaved asphodel, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, grass-like leaves and produces tall, branched stems with small, pale yellow or greenish flowers. The plant is adapted to dry, rocky soils and thrives in full sun, making it a hardy species suitable for coastal and arid gardens. Its flowers bloom in early summer, adding a delicate touch to the landscape.

The plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional herbal medicine.
