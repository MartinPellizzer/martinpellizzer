Anthyllis vulneraria f. pallida

Anthyllis vulneraria f. pallida, commonly known as the pale woundwort, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that typically reaches heights of 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and early summer. Its leaves are trifoliate, consisting of three oval-shaped leaflets, and are often covered in fine hairs.

The pale woundwort is native to Europe and is often found in dry, calcareous soils in open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas.
