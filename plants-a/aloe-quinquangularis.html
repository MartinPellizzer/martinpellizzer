Aloe quinquangularis

Aloe quinquangularis, commonly known as the Five-angle Aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a reddish tinge, forming a rosette shape. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow flowers during the summer months. Its leaves contain a gel-like substance that is often used for its soothing properties.

Aloe quinquangularis is relatively low maintenance and thrives in well-draining soil and full sunlight.
