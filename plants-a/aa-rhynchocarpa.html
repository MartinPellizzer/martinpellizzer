Aa rhynchocarpa

Aa rhynchocarpa, commonly known as the Hawaiian banyan, is a member of the Moraceae family.

It is a large, fast-growing tree that can reach heights of up to 30 meters. The plant has a distinctive trunk with multiple aerial roots that form a dense canopy. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and ovate with a glossy surface.

The fruit is a woody capsule that contains small, edible seeds.
