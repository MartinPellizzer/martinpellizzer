Actinodaphne obscurinervia

Actinodaphne obscurinervia, commonly known as the dark-veined actinodaphne, is a small evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae, which includes laurels, and is characterized by its aromatic foliage.
