Abies finhonnoskiana

Abies finhonnoskiana, commonly known as the Far Eastern fir, is a coniferous tree native to the mountainous regions of eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its conical shape, dark green needle-like leaves, and distinctive cone structures. The tree typically grows in cool, moist climates at high altitudes. Its bark is smooth and reddish-brown, becoming fissured with age.

This species plays an important ecological role in its native habitat, providing shelter and food for various wildlife species.
