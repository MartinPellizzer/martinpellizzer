Anthurium omarescobarii

Anthurium omarescobarii, commonly known as the Omarescobarii anthurium, is a rare tropical plant native to the understory of humid forests in South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its unique inflorescence consisting of a spadix and a spathe. The plant produces striking, heart-shaped leaves with a glossy surface and vibrant green coloration. Its flowers are typically small and clustered around the spadix, often hidden beneath the spathe.

Due to its rarity and ornamental value, it is highly sought after by collectors and horticulturists.
