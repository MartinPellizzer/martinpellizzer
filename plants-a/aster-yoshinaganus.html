Aster yoshinaganus

Aster yoshinaganus, commonly known as the Japanese aster, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and mountainous regions. Its flowers are usually purple or blue, with ray florets surrounding a central disk of tubular flowers.

Aster yoshinaganus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
