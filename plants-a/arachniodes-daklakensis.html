Arachniodes daklakensis

Arachniodes daklakensis, commonly known as the cloud fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, lace-like fronds that resemble spider webs, giving it its common name. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its leaves are usually bi- or tri-pinnate, with a soft, velvety texture.

Arachniodes daklakensis is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.
