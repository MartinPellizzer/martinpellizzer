Adicea rotundata

Adicea rotundata, commonly known as the roundleaf sandwort, is a small evergreen shrub native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is characterized by its compact, rounded form. The plant features small, dark green, oval-shaped leaves that are arranged in a dense rosette. It produces clusters of tiny, white or pale blue flowers during the spring and summer months.

Adicea rotundata is well adapted to arid conditions and requires minimal water once established.
