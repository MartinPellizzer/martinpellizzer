Aristolochia lindeniana var. lindeniana

Aristolochia lindeniana var. lindeniana, commonly known as the Lindenhof Dutchman's pipe, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive, heart-shaped leaves and its unique, pipe-like flowers that are often greenish or brownish in color. The plant belongs to the Aristolochiaceae family and is known for its complex, winding flower structure. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forests.

The species is also noted for its medicinal properties, though its use is limited due to potential toxicity.
