Astragalus saratagius subsp. sarimensis

Astragalus saratagius subsp.

sarimensis, commonly known as the Saratov milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia. It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers.

The subspecies is distinguished by its specific morphological traits, including the structure of its pods and seeds. It typically grows in dry, sandy or gravelly soils and is adapted to arid environments.
