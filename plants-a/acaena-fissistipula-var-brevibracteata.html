Acaena fissistipula var. brevibracteata

Acaena fissistipula var. brevibracteata, commonly known as the short-bracted New Zealand spinach, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in spring, followed by the development of woody, follicle-like fruits.
