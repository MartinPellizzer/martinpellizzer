Alchemilla pratensis var. heteropoda

Alchemilla pratensis var. heteropoda, commonly known as the variable lady's mantle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves that are typically covered in fine, silky hairs. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers in summer, which are clustered in dense, rounded inflorescences. It thrives in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and stream sides.

Its leaves and flowers are often used in traditional herbal remedies for their purported healing properties.
