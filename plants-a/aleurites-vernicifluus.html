Aleurites vernicifluus

Aleurites vernicifluus, commonly known as the Chinese Tung tree, is a deciduous tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its smooth, grey bark and palmate leaves with five to seven lobes. The tree produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are followed by spherical fruits containing hard, oily seeds. Its sap is historically used to make traditional lacquer, which is highly valued in craftsmanship.

The plant is also cultivated for its economic value due to the high oil content in its seeds.
