Acrostichum litanum

Acrostichum litanum, commonly known as the rattlesnake plantain, is a fern native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its tall, upright fronds that can reach up to six feet in height. The plant has a rhizomatous root system that allows it to spread vegetatively. Its leaves are pinnately compound, with a dark green color and a slightly glossy texture.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodlands and along stream banks.
