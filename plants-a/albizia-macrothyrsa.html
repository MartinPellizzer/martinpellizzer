Albizia macrothyrsa

Albizia macrothyrsa, commonly known as the large-throated Albizia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is characterized by its leguminous properties and nitrogen-fixing capabilities. The plant features compound leaves with elongated, narrow leaflets, and its flowers are typically white or pale pink, arranged in clustered inflorescences. Albizia macrothyrsa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and floral displays.

It thrives in well-drained soils and requires moderate to high levels of sunlight.
