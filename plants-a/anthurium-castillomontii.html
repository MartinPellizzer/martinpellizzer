Anthurium castillomontii

Anthurium castillomontii, commonly known as the Castillomontii arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically deep green with a glossy texture. The plant produces vibrant spadix flowers surrounded by a colorful spathe, which can range from red to orange in hue. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium castillomontii is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
