Abutilon venosum var. brevicalyx

Abutilon venosum var. brevicalyx, commonly known as the narrow-leaved abutilon, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features soft, hairy stems and heart-shaped leaves with a serrated margin. The plant produces fluffy, pale green to yellowish flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are typically smaller and more compact compared to other varieties of Abutilon.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil conditions.
