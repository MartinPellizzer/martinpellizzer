Anisomeles × intermedia

Anisomeles × intermedia, commonly known as the hybrid anisomeles, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Amaranthaceae and is characterized by its erect, branching stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white in color, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a suitable choice for ornamental gardening.

Due to its adaptability and aesthetic appeal, it is often cultivated in both garden and landscape settings.
