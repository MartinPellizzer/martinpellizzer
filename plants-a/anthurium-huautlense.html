Anthurium huautlense

Anthurium huautlense, commonly known as the Huautlense arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its distinctive arrow-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking spadices, which are the modified leaves that house the true flowers. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Anthurium huautlense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated as a houseplant.
