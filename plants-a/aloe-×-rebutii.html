Aloe × rebutii

Aloe × rebutii, commonly known as the hybrid aloe or the false aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a waxy coating, which helps it retain moisture in arid environments. The plant produces tall, branched stems with clusters of yellow flowers, usually blooming in summer. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and hardy nature.

Aloe × rebutii is also valued for its ability to thrive in well-drained soil and full sun conditions.
