Anthurium lancea var. ecostatum

Anthurium lancea var. ecostatum, commonly known as the lance-shaped anthurium, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its elongated, lanceolate leaves that are often dark green with a glossy sheen. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-like inflorescences surrounded by colorful, spatulate bracts that range from red to orange. It thrives in humid, warm environments with bright, indirect light.

This variety is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to purify indoor air.
