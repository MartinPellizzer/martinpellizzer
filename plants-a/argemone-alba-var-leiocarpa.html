Argemone alba var. leiocarpa

Argemone alba var. leiocarpa, commonly known as white argemone, is a variant of the argemone plant native to parts of Asia.

It is an annual herb characterized by its yellow flowers and smooth, oval-shaped seeds. The plant typically grows in dry, arid environments and is often found in disturbed soils. Its botanical features include simple, alternate leaves and a hairy stem.

The seeds are rich in oil and have been used traditionally for various purposes.
