Aloe affinis

Aloe affinis, commonly known as the narrow-leaf aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features rosettes of narrow, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a slight bluish tint. The plant produces upright, branched inflorescences bearing yellowish-green flowers. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.

Aloe affinis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional medicinal uses.
