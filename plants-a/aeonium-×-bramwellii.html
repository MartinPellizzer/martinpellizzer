Aeonium × bramwellii

Aeonium × bramwellii, commonly known as the Lemon Tree Aeonium, is a hybrid succulent plant belonging to the Crassulaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact, tree-like growth habit with thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a yellow or orange edge. Native to the Canary Islands, this plant is well adapted to arid environments and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine and have ornamental value in gardens.

Aeonium × bramwellii is relatively easy to care for, requiring minimal water and well-draining soil.
