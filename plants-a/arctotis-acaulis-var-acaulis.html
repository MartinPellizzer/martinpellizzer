Arctotis acaulis var. acaulis

Arctotis acaulis var. acaulis, commonly known as the purple coneflower, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and distinctive, daisy-like flowers that range in color from purple to pink. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces showy, tubular flowers that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its compact size and vibrant blooms make it a valuable addition to both wild and cultivated landscapes.
