Anthurium humile

Anthurium humile, commonly known as the small anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its dark green, glossy leaves and striking, heart-shaped spathes that range in color from white to red. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are surrounded by a colorful, leaf-like bract. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium humile is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
