Aristolochia frutescens

Aristolochia frutescens, commonly known as Dutchman's pipe, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its distinctive, pipe-shaped flowers that are often purple or greenish in color. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and thrives in moist, shaded environments. It belongs to the Aristolochiaceae family and is known for its complex floral structure.

However, it contains toxic compounds and should be handled with care.
