Asplenium × Badense

Asplenium × badense is a unique hybrid fern that combines traits from its parent species, Asplenium trichomanes and Asplenium bulbiferum. It is known for its distinctive appearance and adaptability to various environments. This plant is commonly found in shaded, moist areas where it thrives in the presence of high humidity. Its ability to form dense colonies makes it a popular choice for both ornamental and ecological purposes. Despite its beauty, it remains a relatively obscure species in the world of horticulture.
