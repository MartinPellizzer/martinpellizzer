Atylosia scarabaeoides var. typica

Atylosia scarabaeoides var. typica, commonly known as the scarab beetle plant, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy yellow-orange composite flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or subshrub, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
