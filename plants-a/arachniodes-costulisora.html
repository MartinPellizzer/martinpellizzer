Arachniodes costulisora

Arachniodes costulisora, commonly known as the silver lace fern, is a strikingly ornamental plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features delicate, lacy fronds that give it a unique, almost spider-web-like appearance. The plant has a creeping rhizome system that allows it to spread and form dense mats. Its foliage is typically dark green with a silvery underside, enhancing its visual appeal.

Arachniodes costulisora thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often used in tropical gardens and as a houseplant.
