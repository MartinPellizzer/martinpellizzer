Armeriastrum pterostegium

Armeriastrum pterostegium, commonly known as the narrowleaf sandmat, is a low-growing, mat-forming plant native to the southwestern United States.

It features small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are covered in fine, white hairs, giving it a silvery appearance. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid environments.

Its botanical characteristics make it well adapted to harsh, dry conditions.
