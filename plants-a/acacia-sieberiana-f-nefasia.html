Acacia sieberiana f. nefasia

Acacia sieberiana f. nefasia, commonly known as the slender acacia, is a shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, often multi-branched stems and bipinnate leaves that are reduced to small, scale-like structures. The plant produces yellow to orange spherical pods that contain flattened, winged seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in savanna and woodland habitats.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
