Andropogon asthenostachys

Andropogon asthenostachys, commonly known as slender bluestem, is a perennial grass native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It thrives in moist to wet soils and is often found in wetlands, marshes, and along stream banks.

Andropogon asthenostachys plays an important role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various wildlife species.
