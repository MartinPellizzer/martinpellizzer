Aizoon propinquum

Aizoon propinquum, commonly known as the dwarf dandelion, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with basal rosettes of narrow, hairy leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that develop into fluffy white seeds, typical of the Asteraceae family. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed or open habitats.

Aizoon propinquum is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
