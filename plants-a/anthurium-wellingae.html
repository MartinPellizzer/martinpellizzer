Anthurium wellingae

Anthurium wellingae, commonly known as the Welling's anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are deep green with a velvety texture. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-shaped inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, typically in shades of red or orange. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Anthurium wellingae is popular as a ornamental plant due to its unique foliage and long-lasting blooms.
