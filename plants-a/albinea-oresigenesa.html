Albinea oresigenesa

Albinea oresigenesa, commonly known as the Mexican whitebark.

This plant is a member of the Fabaceae family and is native to the high elevations of central Mexico. It is characterized by its compound leaves and produces small, white flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant has a shrub-like growth habit and is often found in rocky or well-drained soils.

Albinea oresigenesa is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and floral display.
