Albizia tenuiflora

Albizia tenuiflora, commonly known as slender-flowered albizia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its compound leaves, which are pinnately arranged and often have a feathery appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically pale yellow to cream in color, arranged in racemes. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas or disturbed sites.

Albizia tenuiflora is also valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some cultures.
