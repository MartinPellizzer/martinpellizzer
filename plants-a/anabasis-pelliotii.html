Anabasis pelliotii

Anabasis pelliotii, commonly known as the desert sandmat, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its prostrate growth habit and succulent stems. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It is well adapted to harsh desert environments, with mechanisms to conserve water and withstand high temperatures.

Anabasis pelliotii is often found in sandy soils and plays a role in stabilizing dunes in its native habitat.
