Ajax bicolor var. lorifolius

Ajax bicolor var. lorifolius, commonly known as the variegated ajax, is a striking evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It features glossy, lance-shaped leaves with a distinctive bicolor pattern of green and white, which adds visual interest to gardens. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers in spring, followed by red, berry-like fruits that attract birds. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value and hardiness, it is a popular choice for coastal and Mediterranean climates.
