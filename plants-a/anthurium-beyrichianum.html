Anthurium beyrichianum

Anthurium beyrichianum, commonly known as the Red Velvet Anthurium, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are deep green with a velvety texture. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-shaped inflorescences that range in color from red to orange, surrounded by a bright yellow spathe. It thrives in humid, warm environments with bright, indirect light.

Anthurium beyrichianum is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
