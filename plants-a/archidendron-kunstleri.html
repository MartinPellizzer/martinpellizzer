Archidendron kunstleri

Archidendron kunstleri, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a tropical tree native to Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, flame-colored flowers that bloom in clusters during the wet season. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces woody pods that contain seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The botanical features of Archidendron kunstleri make it a popular choice for landscaping in tropical and subtropical regions.
