Athamanta galatta

Athamanta galatta, commonly known as the golden star orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the eastern regions of Australia.

It features a compact, bushy growth habit with numerous small, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a white, waxy coating. The plant produces striking, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange, which bloom during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Athamanta galatta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
