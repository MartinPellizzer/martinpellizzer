Acinodendron cecidophorum

Acinodendron cecidophorum, commonly known as the rainforest acacia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in dense, humid forests and thrives in shaded environments. Its seeds are encased in hard, woody pods, which contribute to its ecological role in seed dispersal.
