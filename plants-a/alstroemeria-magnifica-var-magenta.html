Alstroemeria magnifica var. magenta

Alstroemeria magnifica var. magenta, commonly known as the magenta alstroemeria, is a striking ornamental plant native to South America.

It features showy, tubular flowers in shades of magenta and pink, with a distinctive, spurred shape that attracts pollinators. The plant has strap-like, narrow leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. It thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it suitable for gardens and floral arrangements.

Its botanical appeal lies in its vibrant color and unique floral structure, which make it a popular choice among horticulturists and florists.
