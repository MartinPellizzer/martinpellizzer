Aglaonema costatum var. viride

Aglaonema costatum var. viride, commonly known as the Emerald Beauty, is a striking ornamental plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant green, waxy leaves that often feature subtle variegation. The plant is a member of the Araceae family, known for its unique inflorescence consisting of a spadix and spathe. It thrives in low-light conditions and requires moderate humidity to maintain its lush foliage.

Its compact growth habit makes it an ideal choice for indoor cultivation and decorative purposes.
