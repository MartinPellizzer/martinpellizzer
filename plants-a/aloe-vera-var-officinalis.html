Aloe vera var. officinalis

Aloe vera var. officinalis, commonly known as the medicinal aloe, is a succulent plant native to the Arabian Peninsula.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The gel inside the leaves contains various bioactive compounds, including polysaccharides and enzymes, which contribute to its healing properties. It is widely cultivated for its soothing effects on skin conditions and its use in natural remedies.

The plant's resilience and versatility make it a popular choice for both ornamental and therapeutic purposes.
