Aspidium formosum

Aspidium formosum, commonly known as the Formosan water fern, is a species of fern native to East Asia.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. Its leaves are bi- or tri-pinnate, with small, rounded pinnae that give it a lacy appearance.

Aspidium formosum is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
