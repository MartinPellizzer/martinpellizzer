Andropogon nutans subvar. neesii

Andropogon nutans subvar. neesii, commonly known as Nees' bluestem, is a grass species native to parts of Africa and the Middle East.

It is characterized by its slender, blue-green foliage and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. It has a clump-forming growth habit and is often found in dry to mesic environments.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for ecological studies and potential use in restoration projects.
