Askofake recurva

Askofake recurva, commonly known as the spiral vine, is a climbing plant native to tropical regions.

It features long, flexible stems that twist around supports as it grows. Its leaves are heart-shaped and have a glossy, dark green surface. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to cover trellises or fences.
