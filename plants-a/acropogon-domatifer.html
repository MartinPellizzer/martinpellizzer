Acropogon domatifer

Acropogon domatifer, commonly known as the domatifer grass, is a flowering plant belonging to the grass family Poaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, linear leaves that are often hairy. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences bearing tiny, greenish flowers. It is typically found in moist, open habitats such as wetlands and along stream banks.
