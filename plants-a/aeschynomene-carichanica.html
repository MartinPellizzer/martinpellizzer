Aeschynomene carichanica

Aeschynomene carichanica, commonly known as the smallleaf aeschynomene, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its small, trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers. The plant is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in dry conditions, making it a hardy species. It has been used in traditional medicine and for forage in some regions.

Its ability to fix nitrogen makes it beneficial for soil enrichment.
