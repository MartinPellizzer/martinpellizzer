Amaryllis moelleri

Amaryllis moelleri, commonly known as the white amaryllis, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range from white to pale pink, often with a subtle red or purple tint at the base. The plant produces a single, upright stem bearing several blooms during the growing season. Its foliage consists of strap-like, dark green leaves that are broad and sturdy.

Amaryllis moelleri is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens or as a houseplant.
