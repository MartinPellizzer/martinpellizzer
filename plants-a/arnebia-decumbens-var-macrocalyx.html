Arnebia decumbens var. macrocalyx

Arnebia decumbens var. macrocalyx, commonly known as the large-flowered arnebia, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features creeping stems that root at the nodes, forming dense mats of foliage. The plant produces showy, pink to red flowers with five petals, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, adapting to arid and high-altitude environments.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
