Aquilegia buergeriana f. flavescens

Aquilegia buergeriana f. flavescens, commonly known as the Yellow Buerger's Columbine, is a hybrid form of the Buerger's Columbine.

It is characterized by its bright yellow petals that contrast with its dark green foliage. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and is native to regions of East Asia. It produces clusters of showy, spurred flowers in the spring.

The botanical features make it a desirable ornamental plant in gardens and natural landscapes.
