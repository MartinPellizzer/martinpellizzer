Anemone caroliniana

Anemone caroliniana, commonly known as the Carolina anemone, is a herbaceous perennial native to the eastern United States.

It features showy, white to pinkish flowers that bloom in early spring, often before the leaves fully emerge. The plant has a short, thick rhizome and basal leaves that are palmately lobed with a hairy texture. It thrives in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable addition to native plant gardens and ecological restoration projects.
