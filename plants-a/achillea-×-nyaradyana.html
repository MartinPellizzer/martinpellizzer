Achillea × nyaradyana

Achillea × nyaradyana, commonly known as the Hungarian yarrow, is a hybrid perennial plant native to Hungary.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its finely divided, feathery leaves and clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of 30–60 cm and thrives in full sun to partial shade.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in gardens for its attractive appearance and ability to attract pollinators.
