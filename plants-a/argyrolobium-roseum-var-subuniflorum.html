Argyrolobium roseum var. subuniflorum

Argyrolobium roseum var. subuniflorum, commonly known as the narrow-flowered silver globular vetch, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and spherical, clustered inflorescences. The plant produces small, pink to purple flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
