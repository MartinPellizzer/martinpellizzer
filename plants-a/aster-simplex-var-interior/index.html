Aster Simplex Var. Interior

Aster simplex var. interior is a unique and resilient plant native to the high elevations of the western United States. It thrives in rocky and alpine environments where few other species can survive. This variant of the common aster displays a compact growth habit with small, star-shaped flowers that bloom in late summer. Its ability to withstand harsh conditions makes it a symbol of hardiness in mountain ecosystems. Despite its modest appearance, it plays a vital role in supporting local wildlife and biodiversity.
