Argocoffeopsis spathulata

Argocoffeopsis spathulata, commonly known as the broad-leafed coffee tree, is a species native to tropical regions of South America.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree characterized by its broad, ovate leaves and fragrant, white flowers. The plant produces small, red berries that contain seeds used for coffee production. It thrives in humid, shaded environments with well-drained soil.

Due to its unique botanical features, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists alike.
