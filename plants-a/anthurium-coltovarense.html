Anthurium coltovarense

Anthurium coltovarense, commonly known as the velvet leaf anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a velvety texture. The plant produces clusters of spadix flowers surrounded by a colorful spathe, which can range from red to orange. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.

Anthurium coltovarense is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and air-purifying properties.
