Astroloba spiralis subsp. foliolosa

Astroloba spiralis subsp.

foliolosa, commonly known as the spiral leaf astroloba, is a succulent plant native to South Africa. It is characterized by its rosette-forming structure and spiral arrangement of its fleshy, bluish-green leaves.

The leaves are typically narrow and have a distinctive wavy or undulating edge. It produces tall, branched inflorescences with small, star-shaped yellow flowers during the summer months.

This species is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
