Aerangis monantha

Aerangis monantha, commonly known as the single-flowered orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly South Africa. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces a single, showy orchid flower with a distinctive lip. Its leaves are typically arranged in a rosette and are broad and fleshy, adapted for water storage.

Aerangis monantha is prized in horticulture for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
