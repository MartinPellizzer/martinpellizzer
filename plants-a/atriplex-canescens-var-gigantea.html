Atriplex canescens var. gigantea

Atriplex canescens var. gigantea, commonly known as giant saltbush, is a shrub native to the arid regions of North America.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage, which helps reduce water loss in harsh environments. The plant produces small, greenish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It has a deep root system that allows it to access water from deeper soil layers.

This species is often used in restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soils and thrive in saline conditions.
