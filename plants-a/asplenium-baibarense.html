Asplenium baibarense

Asplenium baibarense, commonly known as the Baibar Valley spleenwort, is a small, evergreen fern native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the family Aspleniaceae and is characterized by its compact, rosette-like growth habit. The plant features delicate, lance-shaped fronds that are typically dark green and covered in fine, scale-like structures. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often growing on rocky substrates or in forest understories.

Asplenium baibarense is a rare and lesser-known species, with limited documented distribution and ecological significance in its native habitat.
