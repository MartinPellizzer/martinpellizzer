Amaryllis belladonna var. haywardii

Amaryllis belladonna var. haywardii, commonly known as the Haywardii Amaryllis, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It produces large, showy flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, often with a distinctive yellow throat. The plant has strap-like, broad leaves that are typically green with a reddish tinge. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

This variety is popular among gardeners for its vibrant blooms and relatively easy care requirements.
