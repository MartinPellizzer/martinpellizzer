Ancistrocactus uncinatus

Ancistrocactus uncinatus, commonly known as the hooked ancistrocactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems that are typically green to blue-green in color. The plant produces clusters of yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. Its spines are short and hooked, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Ancistrocactus uncinatus is adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
