Ardisia synneura var. cataractorum

Ardisia synneura var. cataractorum, commonly known as the glossy buckeye, is a member of the Ericaceae family and is native to the eastern United States.

It is a deciduous shrub characterized by its dark green, glossy leaves and showy clusters of white flowers. The plant produces red, poisonous berries that are a feature of its botanical profile. It thrives in moist, acidic soils and is often found in woodland areas.

The species is notable for its ornamental value and ecological significance in supporting local wildlife.
