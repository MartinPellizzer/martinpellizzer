Artemisia clemensiana

Artemisia clemensiana, commonly known as silver sagebrush, is a perennial shrub native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, dense hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flower heads that appear in late spring to early summer. Artemisia clemensiana thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in arid and semi-arid environments.

It is part of the sunflower family (Asteraceae) and is known for its aromatic properties.
