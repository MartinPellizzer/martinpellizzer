Alhagi graecorum

Alhagi graecorum, commonly known as Greek senna, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers. The plant produces elongated pods that contain lens-shaped seeds. It is often found in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to arid environments.

Alhagi graecorum is also valued for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
