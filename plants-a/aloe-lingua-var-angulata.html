Aloe lingua var. angulata

Aloe lingua var. angulata, commonly known as the angular tongue aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a slight blue or grayish tint. The leaves are often narrow and have a distinctive angular appearance, giving the plant its common name. It produces small, yellow flowers that emerge from the axils of the leaves during the warmer months.

This plant is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
