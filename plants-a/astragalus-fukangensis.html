Astragalus fukangensis

Astragalus fukangensis, commonly known as the Fukang milkvetch, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its small, yellow flowers and compound leaves. The plant is typically found in arid and semi-arid regions, thriving in sandy or rocky soils. It plays a role in soil stabilization and is of interest for its potential medicinal properties.

Its seeds are often used in traditional Chinese medicine.
