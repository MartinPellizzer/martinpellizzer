Anthemis tinctoria var. gaudium-solis

Anthemis tinctoria var. gaudium-solis, commonly known as the Sun-Blush Marigold, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is characterized by its bright yellow to orange flowers, which are typically borne in clusters at the ends of the stems. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening due to its vibrant color and compact growth habit.
