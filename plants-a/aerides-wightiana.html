Aerides wightiana

Aerides wightiana, commonly known as the Wight's orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant typically features strap-like leaves and blooms during the warmer months. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.

Aerides wightiana is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
