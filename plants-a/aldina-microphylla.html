Aldina microphylla

Aldina microphylla, commonly known as the small-leaved Aldina, is a species of flowering plant native to South Africa.

The plant produces clusters of tubular, two-lipped flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
