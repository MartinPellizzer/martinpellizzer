Abrodictyum pseudorigidum

Abrodictyum pseudorigidum, commonly known as the rigid spike orchid, is a species of orchid native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem and dense clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments within tropical forests. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces a single, elongated inflorescence.

Abrodictyum pseudorigidum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
