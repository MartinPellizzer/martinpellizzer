Allium sphaerocephalon var. genuinum

Allium sphaerocephalon var. genuinum, commonly known as the globe onion, is a bulbous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features rounded, globe-like bulbs that are typically white or pale yellow in color. The plant produces tall, slender stems with narrow, grass-like leaves. Its flowers are small, clustered in umbels, and range in color from white to pale pink.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and mild onion-like flavor.
