Anthurium cabrerense

Anthurium cabrerense, commonly known as the Cabrerense Arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a glossy texture. The plant produces striking spadices, which are inflorescences that resemble a spadix, often surrounded by a colorful spathe. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.

Anthurium cabrerense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in botanical gardens.
