Agathosma denticulata

Agathosma denticulata, commonly known as the Cape spearmint, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, finely divided leaves and small yellow flowers. The plant thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in fynbos vegetation. It is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.

Agathosma denticulata is also cultivated for its essential oils, which are used in aromatherapy and perfumery.
