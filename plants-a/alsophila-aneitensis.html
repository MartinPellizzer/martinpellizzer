Alsophila aneitensis

Alsophila aneitensis, commonly known as the Aneitens Ridge tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to the mountainous regions of Central America.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds that can reach several meters in length. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in cloud forests and along stream banks. Its rhizome system is robust, allowing it to anchor firmly in rocky or clay soils.

Alsophila aneitensis plays an important ecological role in its habitat by providing shelter and food for various wildlife species.
