Aphloia theiformis var. purpurascens

Aphloia theiformis var. purpurascens, commonly known as the purple-flowered daisy bush, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It features dense, upright growth with narrow, leathery leaves that are typically gray-green in color. The plant produces showy, daisy-like flowers in shades of purple and white, which bloom in spring and summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal and Mediterranean climates.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
