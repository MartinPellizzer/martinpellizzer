Astragalus medicagineus

Astragalus medicagineus, commonly known as yellow milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to the western United States and parts of Canada.

It is a member of the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its yellow flowers and compound leaves. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. It has a deep root system that helps it survive in arid conditions.

Astragalus medicagineus is also valued for its role in ecological systems and as a forage plant for wildlife.
