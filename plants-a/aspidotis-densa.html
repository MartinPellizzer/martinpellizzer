Aspidotis densa

Aspidotis densa, commonly known as the African orchid or African violet, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom throughout the year under suitable conditions. Its leaves are dense, velvety, and often have a slightly hairy texture, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Aspidotis densa is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ability to thrive in indoor environments with moderate light and humidity.
