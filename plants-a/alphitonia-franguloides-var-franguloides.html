Alphitonia franguloides var. franguloides

Alphitonia franguloides var. franguloides, commonly known as the narrow-leaved alphitonia, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to eastern Australia.

It features elliptical, glossy, dark green leaves that are typically narrow and pointed. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers in clusters, which are followed by spherical, woody fruits containing edible seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and dry inland regions.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various environmental conditions.
