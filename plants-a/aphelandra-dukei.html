Aphelandra dukei

Aphelandra dukei, commonly known as the golden flame tree, is a tropical plant native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America.

It is characterized by its striking, elongated leaves that are often a deep green with a metallic sheen. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in the summer months. Aphelandra dukei thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its aesthetic appeal and unique foliage.
