Annona amazonica var. lancifolia

Annona amazonica var. lancifolia, commonly known as the Amazonian soursop, is a tropical plant native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is a small to medium-sized tree with smooth, dark green leaves that are elliptical in shape. The plant produces large, spiny fruits that are typically green when unripe and turn yellow or orange when mature. The fruit has a distinctive sweet and sour flavor, making it a popular food source in some regions.

It also has medicinal properties and is used in traditional remedies for various ailments.
