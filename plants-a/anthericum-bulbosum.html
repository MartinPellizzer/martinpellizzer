Anthericum bulbosum

Anthericum bulbosum, commonly known as the bulbous star-of-Bethlehem, is a perennial herb native to southern Europe and parts of western Asia.

It features a bulbous stem base and produces clusters of star-shaped, white or pale blue flowers. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and blooms from late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or rocky habitats.

Anthericum bulbosum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
