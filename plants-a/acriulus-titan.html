Acriulus titan

Acriulus titan, commonly known as the giant orchid, is a striking plant native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its massive, showy flowers that can reach up to two feet in diameter. The plant has a robust, woody stem with broad, dark green leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. Its flowers typically bloom in clusters and come in shades of white, yellow, and orange.

Acriulus titan is known for its strong fragrance, which attracts a variety of pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
