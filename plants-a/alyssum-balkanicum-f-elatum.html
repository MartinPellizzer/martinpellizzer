Alyssum balkanicum f. elatum

Alyssum balkanicum f. elatum, commonly known as the tall Balkan alyssum, is a perennial flowering plant native to the Balkan Peninsula.

It features compact, mat-forming growth with dense clusters of small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. The plant has hairy, linear leaves and produces numerous seeds in elongated pods. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
