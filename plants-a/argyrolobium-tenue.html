Argyrolobium tenue

Argyrolobium tenue, commonly known as the narrowleaf silverhead, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small annual herb that typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring, and its leaves are narrow and silver-gray in appearance. It is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and open fields.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat preferences, it is of interest to botanists and ecologists studying local flora.
