Albizia fastigiata var. glabra

Albizia fastigiata var. glabra, commonly known as the silky albizia, is a shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and smooth, pale green bark. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, typically in the spring. Its leaves are bipinnate, consisting of numerous small, oval leaflets.

This species is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its attractive appearance and ability to thrive in various soil conditions.
