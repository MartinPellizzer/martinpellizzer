Aster puniceus f. albiflorus

Aster puniceus f. albiflorus, commonly known as the white-flowered purple coneflower, is a cultivar of the purple coneflower species.

It is characterized by its striking white petals and deep purple central cone, creating a striking contrast. The plant is native to North America and thrives in well-drained soil and full sun. It is a perennial herb that produces showy flowers in the summer months.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for gardens and wildlife habitats.
