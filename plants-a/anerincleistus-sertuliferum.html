Anerincleistus sertuliferum

Anerincleistus sertuliferum, commonly known as the golden star orchid, is a tropical epiphytic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking yellow and green foliage. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers that bloom in spring, adding ornamental value to its appearance. It thrives in humid environments with indirect light and requires well-draining potting mix.

Due to its rarity and specific care requirements, it is a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
