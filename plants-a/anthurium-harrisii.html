Anthurium harrisii

Anthurium harrisii, commonly known as the Harris's anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the Caribbean islands.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces striking spadices, which are inflorescences that resemble a cluster of small, bright red flowers. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium harrisii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and unique floral structure.
