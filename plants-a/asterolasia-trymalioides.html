Asterolasia trymalioides

Asterolasia trymalioides, commonly known as the slender asterolasia, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows as an erect shrub with hairy stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are small and yellow, arranged in clusters, and bloom during the warmer months.

It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest undergrowth.
