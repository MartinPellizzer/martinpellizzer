Astragalus lanzhouensis

Astragalus lanzhouensis, commonly known as the Lanzhou milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant is typically found in arid and semi-arid regions, growing in rocky soils and mountainous areas. It plays a role in soil stabilization and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a vulnerable species.
