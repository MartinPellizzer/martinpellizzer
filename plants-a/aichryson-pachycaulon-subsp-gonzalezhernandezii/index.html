Aichryson Pachycaulon Subsp. Gonzalezhernandezii

Aichryson pachycaulon subsp. gonzalezhernandezii is a rare and striking succulent native to the Canary Islands. This unique plant features dense, compact growth with vibrant green leaves that often display a subtle blue or purple hue. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments. Its distinctive appearance and hardy nature make it a sought-after addition to rock gardens and succulent collections. Due to its limited distribution, it is also a species of conservation concern.
