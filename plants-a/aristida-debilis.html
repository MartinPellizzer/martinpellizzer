Aristida debilis

Aristida debilis, commonly known as slender dropseed, is a native grass species found in various regions of North America.

It is characterized by its fine, tufted growth habit and produces small, delicate panicles of flowers. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as prairies, meadows, and disturbed soils. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green coloration.

Aristida debilis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, providing habitat and food for native wildlife.
