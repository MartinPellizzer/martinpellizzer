Athrixia fontana

Athrixia fontana, commonly known as the rock orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, delicate flowers that are typically white or pale pink. The plant thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal or mountainous regions. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and the flowers are arranged in dense clusters at the ends of the stems.

Athrixia fontana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with suitable conditions.
