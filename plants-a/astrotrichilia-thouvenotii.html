Astrotrichilia thouvenotii

Astrotrichilia thouvenotii, commonly known as the African star grass, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

The plant typically grows in open grasslands and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces a fibrous root system that helps it survive in poor soil conditions.
