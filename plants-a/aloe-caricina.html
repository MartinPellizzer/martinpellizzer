Aloe caricina

Aloe caricina, commonly known as the desert aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that store water, allowing it to survive in arid conditions. The plant produces yellow flowers in clusters, typically during the summer months. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their soothing properties.

Aloe caricina is well adapted to dry climates and requires minimal water to thrive.
