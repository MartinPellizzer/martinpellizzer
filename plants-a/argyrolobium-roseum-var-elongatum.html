Argyrolobium roseum var. elongatum

Argyrolobium roseum var. elongatum, commonly known as the elongated silver globular veldtflower, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is characterized by its legume fruits and often nitrogen-fixing abilities. The plant features a compact, globular growth habit with silvery-white, silky-hairy foliage. Its flowers are pink to purple in color, arranged in dense clusters, and bloom during the cooler months.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
