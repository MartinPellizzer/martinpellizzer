Acetosella mollis

Acetosella mollis, commonly known as sweet violet, is a low-growing perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving them a velvety texture. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant purple flowers that bloom in early spring. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial shade, often found in woodland areas.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its delicate appearance and pleasant scent.
