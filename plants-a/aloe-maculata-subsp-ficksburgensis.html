Aloe maculata subsp. ficksburgensis

Aloe maculata subsp.

ficksburgensis, commonly known as the Ficksburg aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa. It features rosettes of fleshy, blue-green leaves with distinctive white or pale green markings.

The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow-orange flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Due to its ornamental value and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for drought-tolerant landscaping.
