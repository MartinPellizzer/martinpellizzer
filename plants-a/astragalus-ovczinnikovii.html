Astragalus ovczinnikovii

Astragalus ovczinnikovii, commonly known as the Ovczinnikov's milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments. It is known for its small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
