Anubias barteri var. nana

Anubias barteri var. nana, commonly known as dwarf anubias, is a compact aquatic plant native to Africa.

It features broad, dark green leaves that are slightly wavy and often have a reddish tint when exposed to strong lighting. The plant grows slowly and is well-suited for low-light aquarium environments. It is often used as a background plant due to its dense foliage and ease of care.

Anubias barteri var. nana is also known for its ability to thrive in a variety of water conditions and its resistance to algae growth.
