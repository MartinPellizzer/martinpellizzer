Aster tsarungensis

Aster tsarungensis, commonly known as the Tsa-renung aster, is a flowering plant native to the Tibetan Plateau.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in high-altitude meadows, where it thrives in cool, temperate climates. Its flowers are usually blue or purple, with ray florets surrounding a central disk.

Aster tsarungensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in alpine ecosystems.
