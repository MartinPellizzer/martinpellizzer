Anneslia grandiflora

Anneslia grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered anneslia, is a herbaceous perennial native to South America.

It features showy, violet-blue flowers that bloom in late summer and early autumn. The plant has opposite, lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and full to partial sunlight.

Anneslia grandiflora is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and botanical collections.
