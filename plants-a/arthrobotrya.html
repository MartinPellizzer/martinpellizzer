Arthrobotrya

Arthrobotrya, commonly known as the glassy fan fungus, is a type of lichen that belongs to the family Caloplacaceae.

It is characterized by its crust-like growth form and a distinctive, glassy appearance on its upper surface. These lichens are often used in ecological studies to assess air quality due to their sensitivity to environmental changes.
