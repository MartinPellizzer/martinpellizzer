Acanthostachys exilis

Acanthostachys exilis, commonly known as the slender acanthostachy, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spikelets, typical of the grass family. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or grasslands.
