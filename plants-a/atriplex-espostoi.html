Atriplex espostoi

Atriplex espostoi, commonly known as the coastal saltbush, is a shrub native to the arid and semi-arid regions of the western United States.

It is characterized by its succulent, salt-tolerant leaves that help it thrive in saline soils. The plant has small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its robust root system allows it to survive in harsh, dry environments.

Atriplex espostoi plays an important ecological role in stabilizing soils and providing habitat for wildlife.
