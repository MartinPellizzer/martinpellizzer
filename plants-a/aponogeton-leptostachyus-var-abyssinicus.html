Aponogeton leptostachyus var. abyssinicus

Aponogeton leptostachyus var. abyssinicus, commonly known as the Abyssinian arrowhead, is a tropical aquatic plant native to Africa.

It features arrowhead-shaped leaves that are typically bright green and slightly pointed. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom above the water surface. It thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments with moderate light.

Its unique morphology and ornamental value make it a popular choice for aquariums and water gardens.
