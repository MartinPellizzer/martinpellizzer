Arisaema pentaphyllum

Arisaema pentaphyllum, commonly known as the five-lobed arisaema, is a member of the Araceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of China and is characterized by its distinctive, ornate inflorescence. The plant features a short, stout rhizome with a crown of lance-shaped, dark green leaves. Its spadix is surrounded by a spathe that is typically white or pale yellow with a greenish base.

Arisaema pentaphyllum is often cultivated for its unique appearance and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
