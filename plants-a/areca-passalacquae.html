Areca passalacquae

Areca passalacquae, commonly known as the golden areca palm, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Arecaceae family and is characterized by its slender trunk and pinnate leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that develop into edible fruits. It thrives in tropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The golden areca palm is also valued for its ability to tolerate partial shade and its relatively low maintenance requirements.
