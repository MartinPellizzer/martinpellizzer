Anarrhinum fruticosum var. delonii

Anarrhinum fruticosum var. delonii, commonly known as the Delon's thornbush, is a shrub native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its spiny branches and small, greenish-yellow flowers that appear in clusters. The plant has small, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It is adapted to withstand drought conditions and thrives in well-drained soils.

Its botanical features make it a hardy and resilient species suitable for xeriscaping in dry environments.
