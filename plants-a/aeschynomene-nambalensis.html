Aeschynomene nambalensis

Aeschynomene nambalensis, commonly known as the Nambala burr legume, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or greenish in color. Its fruit is a dry, dehiscent pod that releases seeds when mature.

Aeschynomene nambalensis is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in semi-arid environments.
