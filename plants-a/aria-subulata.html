Aria subulata

Aria subulata, commonly known as the needleleaf aria, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, needle-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Aria subulata is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique foliage and low maintenance requirements.
