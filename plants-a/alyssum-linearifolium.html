Alyssum linearifolium

Alyssum linearifolium, commonly known as slenderleaf alyssum, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the western United States.

It features small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in spring and early summer. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a delicate appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Alyssum linearifolium is also valued for its ability to attract beneficial insects like bees and ladybugs.
