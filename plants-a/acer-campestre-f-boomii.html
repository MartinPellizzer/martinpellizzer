Acer campestre f. boomii

Acer campestre f. boomii, commonly known as the Boomi maple, is a cultivar of the common maple tree.

It is characterized by its striking red foliage, which becomes more intense as the season progresses. The plant is relatively compact, making it suitable for smaller garden spaces. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

This ornamental tree is valued for its vibrant autumn color and adaptability to various growing conditions.
