Anthericum fastigiatum

Anthericum fastigiatum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved ladies' tresses, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the orchid family (Orchidaceae).

It is characterized by its dense, spike-like inflorescence composed of small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and wet meadows. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often arranged in a rosette at the base.

Anthericum fastigiatum is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
