Anacamptis morio f. maculata

Anacamptis morio f. maculata, commonly known as the spotted orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to southern Europe.

It is characterized by its distinctive spotted leaves and flowers that range from pink to violet in color. The plant typically grows in grasslands and open woodlands, preferring well-drained soils. It blooms from late spring to early summer, producing upright spikes of fragrant, tubular flowers.

This orchid is often found in habitats with moderate light and is considered a valuable component of biodiversity in its native regions.
