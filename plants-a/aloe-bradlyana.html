Aloe bradlyana

Aloe bradlyana, commonly known as the Natal aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a reddish or purple tint along the edges. The plant produces tall, branched flower stalks that bear yellowish-green flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright sunlight.

Aloe bradlyana is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional medicinal uses.
