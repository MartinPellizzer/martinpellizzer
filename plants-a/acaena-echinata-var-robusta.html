Acaena echinata var. robusta

Acaena echinata var. robusta, commonly known as the robust bristly acaena, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny stems and clusters of small, yellowish flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces bright red, berry-like fruits that are edible for birds and other wildlife. It thrives in a variety of soil types but prefers well-drained conditions.

Its botanical features make it an important component of native ecosystems in its habitat.
