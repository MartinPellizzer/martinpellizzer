Adenostyles viridis

Adenostyles viridis, commonly known as the greenstem bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in rocky or dry soils. The plant features slender, upright stems and small, bell-shaped flowers that are usually blue or violet in color. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits.

Adenostyles viridis is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and slopes.
