Anthurium hacumense

Anthurium hacumense, commonly known as the Hacumense arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are often brightly colored, ranging from deep green to red. The plant produces small, spadix-like inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, which is typical of the Araceae family. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect sunlight for optimal growth.

Anthurium hacumense is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and air-purifying properties.
