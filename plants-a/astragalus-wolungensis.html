Astragalus wolungensis

Astragalus wolungensis, commonly known as the Wolung milkvetch, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, particularly in countries like China and Kazakhstan. The plant is characterized by its compound leaves and small, pea-like flowers that typically bloom in shades of yellow or pale purple. Astragalus wolungensis is known for its deep root system, which allows it to thrive in arid and semi-arid environments.

It plays a role in soil stabilization and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
