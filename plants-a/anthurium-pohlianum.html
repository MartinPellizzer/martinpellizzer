Anthurium pohlianum

Anthurium pohlianum, commonly known as the Pohlian's arrowhead, is a striking tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its unique inflorescence consisting of a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe. The plant features heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a glossy texture, and it produces vivid orange to red spadices that attract pollinators. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Anthurium pohlianum is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in botanical gardens.
