Asphodeline lutea

Asphodeline lutea, commonly known as the yellow asphodel, is a perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its tall, grass-like stems and bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are typically bluish-green in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Asphodeline lutea is also valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
