Astragalus storozhevae var. longiracemosa

Astragalus storozhevae var. longiracemosa, commonly known as the long raceme milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant features a slender, upright stem with small, bright yellow flowers arranged in elongated racemes. Its root system is extensive, allowing it to thrive in arid and semi-arid environments.

This variant is distinguished by its longer inflorescences compared to other varieties within the species.
