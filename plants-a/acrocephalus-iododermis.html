Acrocephalus iododermis

Acrocephalus iododermis, commonly known as the yellow flag iris, is a herbaceous plant native to wetland environments.

It features tall, slender stems that can reach up to 2 meters in height, often growing in dense clusters. The plant has narrow, sword-shaped leaves that are typically green with a slight bluish tinge. Its showy yellow flowers are arranged in panicles and bloom in late summer.

Acrocephalus iododermis is often found in marshes, pond edges, and other moist habitats across temperate regions.
