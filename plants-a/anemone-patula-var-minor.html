Anemone patula var. minor

Anemone patula var. minor, commonly known as the dwarf garden anemone, is a herbaceous perennial native to the eastern United States.

It features showy, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has finely divided, fern-like leaves that are typically basal and hairy. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.

This variety is prized for its delicate appearance and ability to naturalize in woodland gardens.
