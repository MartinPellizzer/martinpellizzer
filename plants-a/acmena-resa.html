Acmena resa

Acmena resa, commonly known as the flame tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, fiery orange-red flowers that bloom in clusters during the dry season. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a smooth surface. Its fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release small, hairy seeds.

Acmena resa is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.
