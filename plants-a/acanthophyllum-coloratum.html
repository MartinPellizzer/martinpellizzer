Acanthophyllum coloratum

Acanthophyllum coloratum, commonly known as the pink spurge or desert holly, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of Central Asia.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its compact growth habit and dense foliage. The plant produces small, pink or purple flowers that bloom in the summer months. It has adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments with minimal water availability.

Its succulent leaves and deep root system help it conserve water and thrive in desert conditions.
