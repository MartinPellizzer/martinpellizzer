Adiantum fovearum var. reductum

Adiantum fovearum var. reductum, commonly known as the lesser bird's nest fern, is a species of fern characterized by its delicate, fan-shaped fronds that resemble a bird's nest.

It is native to regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of India, China, and the Philippines. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing on trees or rocks in tropical forests. Its fronds are typically dark green with a waxy texture and can reach lengths of up to 30 centimeters.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is commonly cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.
