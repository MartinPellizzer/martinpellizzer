Adonis volgensis var. pilosa

Adonis volgensis var. pilosa, commonly known as the hairy adonis, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features upright stems covered in fine, soft hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces bright yellow flowers in early spring, which are typically arranged in dense, terminal clusters. Its leaves are basal, lance-shaped, and also covered in a fine pubescence.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in alpine or subalpine habitats.
