Artabotrys odoratissimus var. intermedius

Artabotrys odoratissimus var. intermedius, commonly known as the intermediate sweetshrub, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in panicles during the summer months. The plant has simple, opposite, and ovate leaves with a slightly rough texture. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.

Its ornamental value is due to its pleasant fragrance and attractive floral display.
