Aporum albayense

Aporum albayense, commonly known as the Philippine tree fern, is a species native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and a crown of large, feathery fronds that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is typically found in tropical rainforests. Its rhizome is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.

The plant plays an important role in its native ecosystem by providing habitat and contributing to forest biodiversity.
