Alternanthera brasiliensis

Alternanthera brasiliensis, commonly known as the red-stemmedAlternanthera, is a striking aquatic plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often a deep green. This plant thrives in freshwater environments and is popular in aquariums for its aesthetic appeal. It prefers moderate to high light conditions and can grow both submerged and as a floating plant.

Its rapid growth rate makes it an excellent choice for aquascaping but requires regular pruning to maintain its shape.
