Anthurium bantanum

Anthurium bantanum, commonly known as the Bantan arrowroot, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves and vibrant spadix. The plant produces a unique inflorescence consisting of a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Anthurium bantanum is also valued for its potential medicinal properties in traditional medicine.
