Anthyllis vulneraria var. hosmarensis

Anthyllis vulneraria var. hosmarensis, commonly known as the Hoamarensis Scotch thistle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its spiny, branched stems and small, yellowish-white flowers. The plant produces trifoliate leaves with hairy surfaces, and its root system is well-developed, enabling it to thrive in rocky and arid environments.

It is often found in coastal areas and is adapted to withstand strong winds and salt spray.
