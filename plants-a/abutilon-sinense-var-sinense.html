Abutilon sinense var. sinense

Abutilon sinense var. sinense, commonly known as Chinese velvetleaf, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 3 meters tall, characterized by its soft, fuzzy leaves and showy, lantern-shaped flowers. The plant produces yellow or orange flowers that bloom in clusters, typically during the summer months. Its leaves are alternate, ovate to heart-shaped, and covered in fine, silky hairs.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds.
