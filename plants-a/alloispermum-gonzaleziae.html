Alloispermum gonzaleziae

Alloispermum gonzaleziae, commonly known as the Gonzalezia cactus, is a rare and unique species native to the arid regions of northern Mexico.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its columnar stems and clusters of small, spiny leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months, adding a striking visual contrast to its green foliage. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species.
