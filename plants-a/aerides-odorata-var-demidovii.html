Aerides odorata var. demidovii

Aerides odorata var. demidovii, commonly known as the Demidov's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a pseudobulb that supports its growth and produces a single, showy spike of fragrant flowers. The flowers are typically white or pale pink, with a distinctive lip that is often marked with purple or brown. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
