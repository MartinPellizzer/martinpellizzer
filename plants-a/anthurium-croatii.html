Anthurium croatii

Anthurium croatii, commonly known as the Croatian anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves and vibrant, spadix-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its foliage is often a deep green with a glossy texture, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers surrounded by a colorful spathe.

Anthurium croatii is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated as a houseplant.
