Argemone mexicana subvar. subinermis

Argemone mexicana subvar. subinermis, commonly known as the narrowleaf castor bean, is a herbaceous annual plant native to the Americas.

It features slender, branching stems and lanceolate leaves with a slightly hairy surface. The plant produces striking yellow flowers that develop into spiny, capsule-like fruits containing toxic seeds. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid environments.

Due to its toxic properties, it is important to handle the plant with care.
