Acioa eketensis

Acioa eketensis, commonly known as the Eket wild bean, is a flowering plant native to West Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is characterized by its leguminous properties and nitrogen-fixing capabilities. The plant features trifoliate leaves and produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in moist, well-drained soils.

Due to its ecological and potential agricultural significance, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists.
