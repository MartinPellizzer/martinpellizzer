Aster yamazutae

Aster yamazutae, commonly known as the Japanese aster, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its flowers are usually purple or blue, with ray florets surrounding a central disk.

Aster yamazutae is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
