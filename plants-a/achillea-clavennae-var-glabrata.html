Achillea clavennae var. glabrata

Achillea clavennae var. glabrata, commonly known as the smooth yarrow, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features basal rosettes of silver-gray, linear leaves and produces clusters of tiny, white to pale yellow flower heads. The plant is characterized by its lack of leaf hairs, giving it a smooth appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or dry habitats.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
