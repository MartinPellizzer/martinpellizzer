Ascolepis menonguensis

Ascolepis menonguensis, commonly known as the Menongue orchid, is a flowering plant native to Central Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, pseudobulbous structure. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
