Ardisia clementis

Ardisia clementis, commonly known as the Christmas berry, is a flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, white flowers that are followed by bright red, berry-like fruits. These fruits are edible and often used in traditional medicine.
