Aloe pienaarii

Aloe pienaarii, commonly known as the Pienaar's aloe, is a striking succulent native to South Africa.

It features tall, spire-like rosettes of fleshy, blue-green leaves that are covered in a layer of white powdery wax. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with clusters of yellow-orange flowers, which bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a container plant.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
