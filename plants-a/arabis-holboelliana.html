Arabis holboelliana

Arabis holboelliana, commonly known as the Holboell's rockcress, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in rocky or gravelly soils and is often found in alpine environments.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens as an ornamental plant.
