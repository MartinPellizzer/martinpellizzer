Areca nibung

Areca nibung, commonly known as the betel palm, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, fan-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that eventually develop into clustered fruits. The fruits contain a single seed, which is used in various traditional and commercial applications.

Areca nibung is also valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in local cultures.
