Ancistrum acorna

Ancistrum acorna, commonly known as the golden spider plant, is a member of the Araceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant features striking, waxy leaves that are typically bright green with a metallic sheen. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by spadices, which are characteristic of the Araceae family.

Ancistrum acorna is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in indoor settings.
