Allium omiostema

Allium omiostema, commonly known as the narrow-leaved garlic.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. The plant produces small, spherical bulbs that are typically white or pale yellow in color. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it blooms with clusters of small, pale purple flowers.

Allium omiostema is often cultivated for its ornamental value and mild garlic-like fragrance.
