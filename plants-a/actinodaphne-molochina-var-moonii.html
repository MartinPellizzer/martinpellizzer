Actinodaphne molochina var. moonii

Actinodaphne molochina var. moonii, commonly known as the Moonii camphor tree, is a variant of the Actinodaphne molochina species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree characterized by its aromatic, glossy, and dark green leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by spherical, woody fruits containing smooth, dark seeds. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and medicinal properties.

The leaves and bark contain essential oils, which have been traditionally used in herbal remedies.
