Acranthera tomentosa

Acranthera tomentosa, commonly known as the woolly acranthera, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive, soft appearance. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the cooler months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Acranthera tomentosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique texture and color.
