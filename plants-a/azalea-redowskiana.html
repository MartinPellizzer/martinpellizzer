Azalea redowskiana

Azalea redowskiana, commonly known as the Redowskiana azalea, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of eastern Asia.

It belongs to the Rhododendron genus and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant typically grows in acidic, well-drained soils and thrives in partial to full shade. Its leaves are evergreen, elliptical, and have a leathery texture.

Azalea redowskiana is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to bloom reliably in suitable climates.
