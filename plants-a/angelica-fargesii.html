Angelica fargesii

Angelica fargesii, commonly known as the Yunnan angelica, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of western China.

It belongs to the Apiaceae family, which includes other aromatic plants like carrots and parsley. The plant features tall, slender stems that can reach up to 2 meters in height, crowned with compound umbels of small, white to greenish-white flowers. Its leaves are large, deeply lobed, and have a strong, spicy aroma.

Angelica fargesii is valued for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional Chinese medicine.
