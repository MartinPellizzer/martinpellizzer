Alstroemeria riedeliana

Alstroemeria riedeliana, commonly known as the Riedel's alstroemeria, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Chile and Argentina.

It belongs to the family Alstroemeriaceae and is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that come in a range of colors, including red, orange, and yellow. The plant typically grows as a herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 1 meter, and features strap-like leaves that are often variegated.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
