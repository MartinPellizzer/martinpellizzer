Anthurium lactiflorum

Anthurium lactiflorum, commonly known as the white anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a metallic sheen. The plant produces clusters of white spathes that enclose bright yellow spadices, creating a dramatic floral display. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.

Anthurium lactiflorum is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
