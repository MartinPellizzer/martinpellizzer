Argyrolobium brevicalyx

Argyrolobium brevicalyx, commonly known as the narrow-leaved silver globular pea, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its compact, globular growth habit and silver-gray foliage. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
