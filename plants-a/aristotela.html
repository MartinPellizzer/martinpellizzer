Aristotela

Aristotela, commonly known as the purple passionfruit, is a tropical vine native to South America.

It produces small, round fruits with a thick, purple rind and a sweet, aromatic pulp. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, white flowers. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

The fruit is often used in jams, juices, and desserts due to its unique flavor.
