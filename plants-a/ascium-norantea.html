Ascium norantea

Ascium norantea, commonly known as the silverleaf orchid, is a rare and striking plant native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and elongated, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in a silvery, waxy coating. The plant produces delicate, white or pale blue flowers that bloom in late spring, adding a unique aesthetic to its natural habitat.
