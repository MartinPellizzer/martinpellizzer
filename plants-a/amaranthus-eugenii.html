Amaranthus eugenii

Amaranthus eugenii, commonly known as the red amaranth, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant red or purple foliage and showy inflorescences. The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its striking color and texture. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for gardens and landscapes.

Amaranthus eugenii is also valued for its nutritional content, as its leaves are edible and rich in vitamins and minerals.
