Aglaonema siamense

Aglaonema siamense, commonly known as the Siam silver leaf plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves with striking silver and white veins, creating a visually appealing contrast. The plant thrives in indirect light and requires moderate humidity to maintain its vibrant foliage. It is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Aglaonema siamense is also noted for its air-purifying qualities, contributing to improved indoor air quality.
