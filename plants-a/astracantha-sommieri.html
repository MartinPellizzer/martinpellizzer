Astracantha sommieri

Astracantha sommieri, commonly known as the Sommier's thornveld, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its spiny, woody stems and small, tubular yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments. Its unique morphology and ecological role make it an important species in its native habitat.

It is also valued for its ornamental appeal and potential medicinal properties.
