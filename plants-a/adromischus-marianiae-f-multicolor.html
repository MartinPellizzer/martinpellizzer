Adromischus marianiae f. multicolor

Adromischus marianiae f. multicolor, commonly known as the multicolored moon cactus, is a unique hybrid plant that combines the vibrant colors of its parent species.

It is a succulent with thick, fleshy stems that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid conditions. The plant features a striking array of colors, including red, pink, and purple, which are most prominent in its stems. It is native to South Africa and is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.

Due to its hybrid nature, it is typically propagated through grafting rather than seeds.
