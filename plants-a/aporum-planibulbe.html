Aporum planibulbe

Aporum planibulbe, commonly known as the flat bulb plant, is a unique species characterized by its distinctive, flattened bulb structure.

This plant belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is native to certain regions of South America. Its botanical features include narrow, linear leaves and a showy inflorescence with pale yellow to white flowers. The bulb serves as a storage organ, allowing the plant to survive in dry conditions.
