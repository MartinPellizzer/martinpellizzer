Arisaema fargesii

Arisaema fargesii, commonly known as the Farges' arrowhead, is a herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of western China.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its unique inflorescence composed of a spadix surrounded by a modified leaf called a spathe. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a waxy surface, and its flowers are typically greenish-yellow with a distinct central spadix. Arisaema fargesii is often found in moist, shaded environments, growing in rocky or forested areas.

It is valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
