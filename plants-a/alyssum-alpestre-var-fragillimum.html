Alyssum alpestre var. fragillimum

Alyssum alpestre var. fragillimum, commonly known as the alpine alyssum, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It typically features compact, mat-forming growth with small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, contributing to its drought-resistant characteristics. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and alpine settings.

Its delicate appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking ornamental yet resilient plants.
