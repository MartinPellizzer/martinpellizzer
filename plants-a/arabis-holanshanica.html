Arabis holanshanica

Arabis holanshanica, commonly known as the Chinese rockcress, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. The plant is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. It thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in alpine or arid regions.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
