Aspilia goiazensis

Aspilia goiazensis, commonly known as the Goiania spiderflower, is a flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, spiky inflorescence. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats and is adapted to thrive in regions with seasonal rainfall. Its flowers are yellowish-green, with a central disc surrounded by ray florets.

Aspilia goiazensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
