Apocynum farwellii

Apocynum farwellii, commonly known as Farwell's milkweed, is a flowering plant native to the United States.

It is a member of the Apocynaceae family, which includes many milkweed species. The plant produces showy, pink to red flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a fuzzy texture.

The plant contains cardiac glycosides, making it toxic to livestock and humans if ingested.
