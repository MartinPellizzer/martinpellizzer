Atalantia lauterbachii

Atalantia lauterbachii, commonly known as the African star-of-Bethlehem, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has a short, woody stem and produces small, round, yellow fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its ornamental value and drought tolerance make it a desirable plant for xeriscaping.
