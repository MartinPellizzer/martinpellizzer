Athyrium asplenioides var. angustum

Athyrium asplenioides var. angustum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf water fern, is a fern species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Athyriaceae and is characterized by its finely divided, pinnate leaves that are typically narrow and elongated. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found near streams or in dense forest understories. Its fronds are usually dark green and glossy, with a delicate, feathery appearance.

The plant is valued in horticulture for its ornamental qualities and ability to thrive in humid conditions.
