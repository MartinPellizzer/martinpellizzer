Afroaster comptonii

Afroaster comptonii, commonly known as the Compton's aster, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its flowers are usually pink to purple in color, with ray florets surrounding a central disk.

Afroaster comptonii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
