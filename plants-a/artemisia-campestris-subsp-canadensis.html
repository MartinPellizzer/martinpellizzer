Artemisia campestris subsp. canadensis

Artemisia campestris subsp.

canadensis, commonly known as Canadian tarragon, is a perennial herb native to North America. It is characterized by its aromatic, silver-green foliage and small yellow flower heads that appear in the summer.

The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as fields and roadsides. It has lance-shaped leaves with a strong, camphor-like scent.

Canadian tarragon is often used in culinary applications, though it is less common than French tarragon.
