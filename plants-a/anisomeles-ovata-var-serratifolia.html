Anisomeles ovata var. serratifolia

Anisomeles ovata var. serratifolia, commonly known as the serrated-leaf anisomeles, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its palmate leaves, which are deeply lobed and have a serrated edge, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or greenish-yellow in color. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage.

The species is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
