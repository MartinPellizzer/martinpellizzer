Amaranthus retroflexus var. delilei

Amaranthus retroflexus var. delilei, commonly known as the redroot amaranth, is a species of flowering plant in the amaranth family.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and distinctive red stems, which are often covered in fine hairs. The leaves are typically lance-shaped and have a slightly rough texture. It produces small, greenish flowers that develop into three-winged seeds.

This plant is native to tropical and subtropical regions and is often found in disturbed soils or along roadsides.
