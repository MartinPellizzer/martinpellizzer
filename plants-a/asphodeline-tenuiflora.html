Asphodeline tenuiflora

Asphodeline tenuiflora, commonly known as the slender-flowered asphodel, is a perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, narrow leaves and spikes of small, white or pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments. It belongs to the Asphodelaceae family and is known for its resilience in harsh conditions.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and drought-tolerant landscapes.
