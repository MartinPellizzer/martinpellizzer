Aurinia halimifolia

Aurinia halimifolia, commonly known as the rock rose, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, fragrant yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has a compact growth habit with silvery-gray foliage that helps it retain moisture. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Its hardy nature and ornamental value make it a popular choice among gardeners.
