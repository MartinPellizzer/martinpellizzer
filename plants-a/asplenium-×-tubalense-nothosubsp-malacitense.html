Asplenium × tubalense nothosubsp. malacitense

Asplenium × tubalense nothosubsp.

malacitense, commonly known as the Malacitense spleenwort, is a hybrid fern species native to the Iberian Peninsula. It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit.

The fronds are typically dark green, with a leathery texture and a distinctive, narrow, lanceolate shape. It produces small, sporangia-bearing sorus clusters on the underside of its leaves.

This subspecies is notable for its adaptability to humid, shaded environments and its role in forest ecosystems.
