Agallostachys fastuosa

Agallostachys fastuosa, commonly known as the silver velvet tamarisk, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, silvery-gray foliage that provides a striking visual contrast. The plant produces small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for drought-tolerant landscaping.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental appeal make it suitable for rock gardens and borders.
