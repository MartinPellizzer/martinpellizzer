Allium thracicum

Allium thracicum, commonly known as the Thrace onion, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It is a member of the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and clusters of small, white or pale purple flowers. Allium thracicum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting pollinators.
