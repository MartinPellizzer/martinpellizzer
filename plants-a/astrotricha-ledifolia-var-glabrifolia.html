Astrotricha ledifolia var. glabrifolia

Astrotricha ledifolia var. glabrifolia, commonly known as the smoothleaf starwort, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Pacific Northwest of North America.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as stream banks and forest floors. The plant features a basal rosette of lanceolate, glabrous leaves and produces small, star-shaped white flowers in late spring to early summer. Its foliage is characterized by a smooth texture and a distinct, aromatic scent.

The species is valued for its ecological role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various invertebrates.
