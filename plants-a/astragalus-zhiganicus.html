Astragalus zhiganicus

Astragalus zhiganicus, commonly known as the Chinese milkvetch, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to China and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to pale purple. The plant produces pods that contain several seeds, which are often used in traditional Chinese medicine.
