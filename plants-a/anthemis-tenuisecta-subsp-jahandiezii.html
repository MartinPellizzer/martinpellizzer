Anthemis tenuisecta subsp. jahandiezii

Anthemis tenuisecta subsp.

jahandiezii, commonly known as the Persian tansy, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Iran. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of ray and disk florets.

The plant features finely divided, aromatic leaves and produces yellow flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or semi-arid habitats.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species within its native range.
