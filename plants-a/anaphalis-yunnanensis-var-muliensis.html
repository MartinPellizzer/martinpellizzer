Anaphalis yunnanensis var. muliensis

Anaphalis yunnanensis var. muliensis, commonly known as the Yunnan cudweed, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and small, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in alpine meadows and rocky slopes at high altitudes. It has narrow, linear leaves and produces numerous small yellow flowers that bloom in the summer.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its aesthetic appeal.
