Amaranthus rotundifolius

Amaranthus rotundifolius, commonly known as roundleaf amaranth, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its broad, rounded leaves and tall, branched inflorescences that produce small, clustered flowers. The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant foliage and showy blooms. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for gardens and landscapes.

Amaranthus rotundifolius is also valued for its nutritional content, as its leaves are edible and rich in vitamins and minerals.
