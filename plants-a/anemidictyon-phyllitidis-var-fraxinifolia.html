Anemidictyon phyllitidis var. fraxinifolia

Anemidictyon phyllitidis var. fraxinifolia, commonly known as the fraxinifolia variant of the genus Anemidictyon, is a unique plant species characterized by its distinctive leaf morphology.

Its leaves are typically ovate to elliptical in shape, with a smooth or slightly serrated margin. The plant exhibits a compact growth habit, often forming dense clusters of foliage. It is native to specific regions in southern Africa, where it thrives in well-drained, sandy soils.
