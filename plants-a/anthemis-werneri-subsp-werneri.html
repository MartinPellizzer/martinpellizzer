Anthemis werneri subsp. werneri

Anthemis werneri subsp.

werneri, commonly known as Werneri's fleabane, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads.

The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, with ray florets surrounding a central disc.

It is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
