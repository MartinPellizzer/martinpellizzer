Afraurantium

Afraurantium, commonly known as the African tulip.

It is a flowering plant native to southern Africa, characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 6 meters. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Afraurantium is well-suited for warm climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
