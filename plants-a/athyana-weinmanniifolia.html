Athyana weinmanniifolia

Athyana weinmanniifolia, commonly known as the silver leaf tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to the Caribbean.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage with a silvery underside, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by oval-shaped, woody fruits. It thrives in humid, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal or lowland forest habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens.
