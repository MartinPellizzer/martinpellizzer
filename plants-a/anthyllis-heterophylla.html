Anthyllis heterophylla

Anthyllis heterophylla, commonly known as the variable anthyllis, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive, often hairy foliage and small, clustered flowers. Anthyllis heterophylla is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and coastal areas.
