Artabotrys nicobarianus

Artabotrys nicobarianus, commonly known as the Nicobar starfruit, is a tropical plant native to the Andaman and Nicobar Islands.

It is a small, evergreen shrub that typically grows to a height of about 2 to 3 meters. The plant features simple, alternate leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a smooth, glossy surface. Its flowers are clustered in umbels and are usually white or pale yellow, emitting a fragrant scent.

The fruit is a small, star-shaped berry that turns from green to yellow when ripe, containing a single seed.
