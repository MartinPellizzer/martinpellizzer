Aloe cameronii var. dedzana

Aloe cameronii var. dedzana, commonly known as Cameron's aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a waxy coating, which helps it retain moisture in arid environments. The plant produces tall, branched flower stalks that bear yellow to orange flowers, adding ornamental value. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is often cultivated as a decorative plant in gardens and collections.
