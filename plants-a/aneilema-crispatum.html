Aneilema crispatum

Aneilema crispatum, commonly known as the curly-leaved aneilema, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, rolled or crisped leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Aneilema crispatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
