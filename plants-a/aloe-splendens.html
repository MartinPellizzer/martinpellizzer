Aloe splendens

Aloe splendens, commonly known as the splendour aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically bright green with a waxy coating. The plant produces tall, branched flower stalks that bear yellowish-green flowers, which bloom in late summer. Its leaves are used in traditional medicine and for their soothing properties.

Aloe splendens thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.
