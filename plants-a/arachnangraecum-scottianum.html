Arachnangraecum scottianum

Arachnangraecum scottianum, commonly known as the Scottish spider orchid, is a rare and endangered flowering plant native to the Scottish Highlands.

The plant typically grows in acidic, nutrient-poor soils and thrives in moist, shaded environments.
