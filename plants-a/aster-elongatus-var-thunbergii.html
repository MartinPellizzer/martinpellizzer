Aster elongatus var. thunbergii

Aster elongatus var. thunbergii, commonly known as the Thunberg's elongated aster, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to East Asia.

It features upright, branching stems covered in fine, hairy textures, and it produces showy, daisy-like flowers in shades of blue to purple. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in woodland or meadow habitats. Its foliage consists of lance-shaped leaves with serrated edges, and it blooms from late summer into early autumn.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits to pollinators.
