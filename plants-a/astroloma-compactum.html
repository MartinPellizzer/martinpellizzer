Astroloma compactum

Astroloma compactum, commonly known as the compact astroloma, is a small, evergreen shrub native to Western Australia.

It features dense, rounded foliage with small, glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces clusters of tiny, white, star-shaped flowers during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, showing drought tolerance.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
