Acianthus sphenochilus

Acianthus sphenochilus, commonly known as the Cape orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, rosette-like growth habit and produces striking, spurred flowers in shades of pink and purple. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and prefers full sun to partial shade. Its flowering period occurs during the summer months, and it is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Acianthus sphenochilus is a member of the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is known for its relatively easy care requirements.
