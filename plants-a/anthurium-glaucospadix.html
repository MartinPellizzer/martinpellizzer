Anthurium glaucospadix

Anthurium glaucospadix, commonly known as the white spadix anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, elongated spadix that is typically white or pale green in color, surrounded by a broad, glossy spathe. The plant belongs to the Araceae family, which is known for its unique floral structures consisting of a spadix and spathe. Anthurium glaucospadix thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical landscapes.
