Anthurium cornejoi

Anthurium cornejoi, commonly known as the "Coral Flame Anthurium," is a striking tropical plant native to Colombia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped leaves that range from deep green to red, depending on the light conditions. The plant produces long, spadix-like inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, typically in shades of red or orange. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium cornejoi is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
