Agave lemairei

Agave lemairei, commonly known as the Lemaire agave, is a succulent plant native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are typically blue-green in color with a waxy coating. The plant produces tall, branched flower stalks that bear yellow flowers, which bloom once in its lifetime. It thrives in arid environments and requires well-drained soil.

Agave lemairei is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.
