Atriplex fominii

Atriplex fominii, commonly known as Fomin's saltbush, is a shrub native to arid and semi-arid regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its succulent leaves and small, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is highly adapted to saline and alkaline soils, making it a valuable species for stabilizing degraded lands. Its leaves contain high levels of minerals, which have been used in traditional medicine.

Atriplex fominii plays an important ecological role in desert ecosystems by providing food and shelter for various wildlife species.
