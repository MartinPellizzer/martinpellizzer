Aeschynomene abyssinica

Aeschynomene abyssinica, commonly known as African velvetbean, is a perennial leguminous plant native to Africa.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces elongated, cylindrical pods containing small, dark seeds. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often found in disturbed soils.

Aeschynomene abyssinica is valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it useful in agricultural systems.
