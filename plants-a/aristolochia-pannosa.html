Aristolochia pannosa

Aristolochia pannosa, commonly known as the broadleaf Dutchman's pipe, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its unique, pipe-shaped flowers that are often dark purple or brown in color. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, hairy texture. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The species is part of the Aristolochiaceae family and is known for its complex floral structure and medicinal properties.
