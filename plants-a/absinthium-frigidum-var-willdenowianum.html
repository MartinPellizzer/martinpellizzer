Absinthium frigidum var. willdenowianum

Absinthium frigidum var. willdenowianum, commonly known as the frost absinth.

It is a perennial herb native to the alpine regions of Europe and Asia. The plant features slender, hairy stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine pubescence. Its flowers are small, blue to purple in color, and it produces a distinctive, aromatic compound known as absinthin.

This variety is valued for its medicinal properties and historical use in herbal remedies.
