Ardisia philippinensis

Ardisia philippinensis, commonly known as the Philippine ardisia, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the family Ericaceae and is characterized by its glossy, dark green elliptical leaves. The plant produces small, white to pinkish flowers followed by bright red berries that are attractive to birds. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in secondary forests and along stream banks.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for horticultural use in tropical regions.
