Arabis stelleri

Arabis stelleri, commonly known as Steller's rockcress, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other mustard family plants. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, where it thrives in well-drained soils. Its flowers are small and white or pale yellow, arranged in clusters at the ends of the stems.

Arabis stelleri is known for its resilience to cold climates and is often found at high altitudes.
