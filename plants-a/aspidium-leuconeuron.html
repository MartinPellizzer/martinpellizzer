Aspidium leuconeuron

Aspidium leuconeuron, commonly known as the silver fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Athyriaceae.

It is native to various regions in Asia, including China, Japan, and the Indian subcontinent. The plant features delicate, bipinnate fronds that are characterized by their silvery-white coloration on the underside. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The plant's unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
