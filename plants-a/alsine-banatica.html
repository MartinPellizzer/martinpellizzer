Alsine banatica

Alsine banatica, commonly known as the common alsike, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Caryophyllaceae.

It is a low-growing annual herb that typically reaches a height of about 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features simple, alternate leaves that are often hairy and have a bluish-green color. Its flowers are small, pink to purple in color, and arranged in clusters at the ends of the stems.

Alsine banatica is native to Europe and parts of Asia, and it thrives in moist, disturbed soils.
