Afzelia quanzensis

Afzelia quanzensis, commonly known as the Guangdong afzelia, is a deciduous tree native to southern China.

It belongs to the family Fabaceae and is characterized by its compound leaves and fragrant flowers. The plant is valued for its hard, heavy wood, which is used in traditional craftsmanship. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in lowland forests.
