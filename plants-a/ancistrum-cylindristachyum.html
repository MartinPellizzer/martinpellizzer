Ancistrum cylindristachyum

Ancistrum cylindristachyum, commonly known as the cylindrical-stalked anthurium, is a member of the Araceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant features large, heart-shaped leaves that are often brightly colored, with a glossy surface. Its inflorescence consists of a spadix surrounded by a modified leaf called a spathe.

It thrives in humid, warm environments and is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its striking appearance.
