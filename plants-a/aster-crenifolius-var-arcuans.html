Aster crenifolius var. arcuans

Aster crenifolius var. arcuans, commonly known as the curlyleaf aster, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the northeastern United States.

It features showy, purple to pink flower heads that bloom in late summer to early fall. The plant has finely toothed, lance-shaped leaves that are often curled or crinkled, giving it its common name. It typically grows in moist, shaded woodlands and thrives in well-drained soil.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable addition to native plant gardens and ecological restoration projects.
