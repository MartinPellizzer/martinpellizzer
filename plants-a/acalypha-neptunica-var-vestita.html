Acalypha neptunica var. vestita

Acalypha neptunica var. vestita, commonly known as the velvetleaf or silverleaf acalypha, is a striking ornamental plant native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its velvety, silver-green foliage that shimmers in the sunlight. The leaves are typically ovate to lanceolate in shape and often have a soft, fuzzy texture. It produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that are not particularly showy.

This plant thrives in warm, humid climates and is often used in landscaping for its unique leaf appearance.
