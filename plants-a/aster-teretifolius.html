Aster teretifolius

Aster teretifolius, commonly known as the narrow-leaved aster, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a rough texture, while its flowers are blue to purple in color.

Aster teretifolius is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, providing nectar for pollinators.
