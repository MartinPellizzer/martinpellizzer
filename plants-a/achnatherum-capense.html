Achnatherum capense

Achnatherum capense, commonly known as the Cape wild oat, is a perennial grass native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. The plant has slender, erect stems and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. Its leaves are narrow and linear, with a bluish-green color.

Achnatherum capense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
