Anoectochilus chapaensis

Anoectochilus chapaensis, commonly known as the Chapa orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that support its foliage and flowers. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are typically arranged in a raceme. It prefers shaded, moist habitats and is often found in forest understories.

Anoectochilus chapaensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
