Aconitum hemsleyanum var. hsiae

Aconitum hemsleyanum var. hsiae, commonly known as the Hsia's monkshood, is a striking perennial herb native to China.

It features tall, upright stems that bear large, hooded flowers in shades of blue to purple. The plant has palmately divided leaves with a distinctive, wavy margin. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Due to its toxicity, it should be handled with care.
