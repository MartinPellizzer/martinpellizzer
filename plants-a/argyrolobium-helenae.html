Argyrolobium helenae

Argyrolobium helenae, commonly known as Helen's silver globular.

It is a flowering plant native to South Africa, characterized by its distinctive spherical shape and silvery foliage. The plant produces showy, yellow flowers that bloom in late summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun exposure to flourish.

Due to its unique appearance and ornamental value, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.
