Aloysia barbata

Aloysia barbata, commonly known as the silverleaf honeysuckle, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and clusters of fragrant, tubular flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 6 meters. Its leaves are narrow and covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Aloysia barbata is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
