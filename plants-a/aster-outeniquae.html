Aster outeniquae

Aster outeniquae, commonly known as the Outeniqua aster, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Cape Region of South Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of small, daisy-like flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant features narrow, linear leaves that are typically arranged in a basal rosette. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Aster outeniquae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
