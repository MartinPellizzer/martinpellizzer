Amaranthus gangeticus var. rotundifolius

Amaranthus gangeticus var. rotundifolius, commonly known as red amaranth, is a species of flowering plant in the amaranth family.

It is characterized by its broad, rounded leaves and vibrant red or purple flower heads. This plant is native to the Indian subcontinent and thrives in warm, sunny climates. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and as a source of edible grains and leaves.

The plant is also valued for its ability to grow in poor soil conditions and its resilience to drought.
