Aciphylla lyallii

Aciphylla lyallii, commonly known as the Lyall's pincushion, is a perennial shrub native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that resembles a pincushion. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in rocky, alpine environments and is adapted to harsh climatic conditions.
