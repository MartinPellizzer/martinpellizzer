Anthurium kunthii

Anthurium kunthii, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a velvety texture. The plant produces vibrant red spathes that enclose a spadix, which is a unique floral structure typical of the Araceae family. Anthurium kunthii thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor and greenhouse cultivation.
