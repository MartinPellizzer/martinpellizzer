Alocasia lowii var. reticulata

Alocasia lowii var. reticulata, commonly known as the Reticulated Alocasia, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive reticulated pattern and deep green veins. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, rich soil to flourish. Its foliage is often adorned with a waxy coating, enhancing its aesthetic appeal.

Due to its ornamental value, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical landscapes.
