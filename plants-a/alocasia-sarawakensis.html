Alocasia sarawakensis

Alocasia sarawakensis, commonly known as the Sarawak Alocasia, is a tropical plant native to Borneo.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can reach up to 1 meter in diameter. The plant produces striking spathes that enclose its small, white flowers. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, nutrient-rich soil.

Alocasia sarawakensis is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its dramatic foliage.
