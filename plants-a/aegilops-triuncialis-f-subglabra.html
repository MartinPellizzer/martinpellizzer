Aegilops triuncialis f. subglabra

Aegilops triuncialis f. subglabra, commonly known as the smooth spikegrass, is a perennial grass species native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and spike-like inflorescences that bear small, smooth lemmaless florets. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments. Its leaves are narrow and hairy, contributing to its drought tolerance.

It is often found in naturalized populations in areas with mild winters and warm, dry summers.
