Amorphophallus angustispathus

Amorphophallus angustispathus, commonly known as the narrow-leafed giant arrowhead, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive inflorescence consisting of a spadix and spathe. The plant produces large, arrowhead-shaped leaves that can grow up to several meters in length. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.
