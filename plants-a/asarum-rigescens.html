Asarum rigescens

Asarum rigescens, commonly known as wild betony, is a perennial herb native to eastern North America.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine, white hairs. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in a loose umbel. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Its botanical characteristics make it a notable species within the Aristolochiaceae family.
