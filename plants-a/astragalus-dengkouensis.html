Astragalus dengkouensis

Astragalus dengkouensis, commonly known as the Dengkou milkvetch, is a perennial legume native to China.

It is characterized by its erect stems and small, yellowish flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has compound leaves with numerous small leaflets, typical of the Fabaceae family. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky or sandy soils.

Astragalus dengkouensis is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional Chinese medicine.
