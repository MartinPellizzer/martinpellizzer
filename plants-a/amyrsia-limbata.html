Amyrsia limbata

Amyrsia limbata, commonly known as the slender bignonia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Bignoniaceae family and is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically orange or yellow in color. The plant has opposite, simple leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, and it produces long, slender pods that contain seeds.

Amyrsia limbata is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant blooms and adaptability to various growing conditions.
