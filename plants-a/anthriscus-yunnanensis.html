Anthriscus yunnanensis

Anthriscus yunnanensis, commonly known as Yunnan cow parsley, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the carrot family (Apiaceae) and is characterized by its finely divided, fern-like leaves and small white or greenish flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shady environments and is often found in mountainous regions. It has a delicate, herbaceous appearance and is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes.

Anthriscus yunnanensis is also valued for its potential medicinal properties in traditional Chinese medicine.
