Aeschynomene tribuloides

Aeschynomene tribuloides, commonly known as slender goffer's weed, is a flowering plant in the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Americas. The plant is characterized by its small, trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in agricultural fields and along roadsides.

Aeschynomene tribuloides has a fibrous root system and is known for its ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, making it a valuable plant in ecological restoration efforts.
