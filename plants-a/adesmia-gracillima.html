Adesmia gracillima

Adesmia gracillima, commonly known as the slender adesmia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant typically grows in dry, arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. It has small, delicate flowers that are often pale or white in color.
