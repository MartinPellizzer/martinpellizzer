Artemisia dahurica

Artemisia dahurica, commonly known as Siberian wormwood, is a perennial herb native to the cold climates of Asia.

It features aromatic, silver-gray foliage with lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flower heads that are clustered in dense, spherical shapes. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in alpine or steppe regions.

Artemisia dahurica is valued for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
