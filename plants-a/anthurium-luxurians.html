Anthurium luxurians

Anthurium luxurians, commonly known as the velvet leaf anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are velvety to the touch, often with a deep green to dark red coloration. The plant produces striking spadices, which are bright red or yellow, surrounded by a showy, white or green spathe. It thrives in humid, warm environments and requires indirect sunlight for optimal growth.

Anthurium luxurians is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
