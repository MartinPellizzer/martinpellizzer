Agathosma krakadouwensis

Agathosma krakadouwensis, commonly known as the Klein Karoo thyme, is a small, aromatic shrub native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its small, needle-like leaves and clusters of tiny white flowers. The plant is well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, often growing in rocky soils. It is valued for its essential oils, which are used in aromatherapy and traditional medicine.
