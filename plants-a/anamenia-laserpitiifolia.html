Anamenia laserpitiifolia

Anamenia laserpitiifolia, commonly known as the red sandalwood, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark red to purple bark and heart-shaped leaves with a glossy surface. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink. Its wood is highly valued for its dense, durable quality and aromatic properties.

Anamenia laserpitiifolia plays an important role in traditional medicine and is also used in ornamental horticulture.
