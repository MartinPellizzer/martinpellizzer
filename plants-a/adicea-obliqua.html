Adicea obliqua

Adicea obliqua, commonly known as the zigzag plant, is a member of the Acanthaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its distinctive zigzagging stems. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Its leaves are opposite and oval-shaped, with a slightly hairy texture.

Adicea obliqua is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in shaded environments.
