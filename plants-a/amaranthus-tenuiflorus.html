Amaranthus tenuiflorus

Amaranthus tenuiflorus, commonly known as slenderflower amaranth, is an annual herbaceous plant native to the Americas.

It features slender, branching stems that can grow up to 1 to 2 meters in height. The plant produces small, greenish to reddish flowers arranged in dense clusters at the ends of the stems. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a slightly hairy texture.

Amaranthus tenuiflorus is known for its adaptability to various soil types and is often found in disturbed habitats.
