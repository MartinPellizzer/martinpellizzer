Aberemoa lepidota

Aberemoa lepidota, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical plant native to regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or pale yellow. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Due to its rapid growth and ability to spread, it can become an invasive species in certain environments.
