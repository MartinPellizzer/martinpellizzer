Anchusa pinardii

Anchusa pinardii, commonly known as the Narrow-leaved Anchusa, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe.

It belongs to the Boraginaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and striking blue to violet flowers.
