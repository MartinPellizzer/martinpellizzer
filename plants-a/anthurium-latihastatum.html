Anthurium latihastatum

Anthurium latihastatum, commonly known as the red-veined anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves with vibrant red veins against a deep green background. The plant produces clusters of small, spadix-like flowers surrounded by a colorful spathe. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Anthurium latihastatum is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
