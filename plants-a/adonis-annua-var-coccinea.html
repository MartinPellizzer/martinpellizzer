Adonis annua var. coccinea

Adonis annua var. coccinea, commonly known as the red globemallow, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a herbaceous annual with striking red-orange flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has palmately lobed leaves and produces capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in dry, sunny environments and is often found in disturbed soils.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive and ornamental species in its native habitat.
