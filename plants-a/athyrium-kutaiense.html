Athyrium kutaiense

Athyrium kutaiense, commonly known as the Kutai sword fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Athyriaceae and is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its rhizomes are often covered in a layer of fine, hairy structures.

The fronds are bi- or tri-pinnate, with a distinctive appearance that makes it recognizable in its natural habitat.
