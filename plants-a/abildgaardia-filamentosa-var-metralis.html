Abildgaardia filamentosa var. metralis

Abildgaardia filamentosa var. metralis, commonly known as the slender sandwort, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of southern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial that thrives in sandy or gravelly soils, often found in dune environments. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers arranged in dense clusters, which bloom during the cooler months. Its leaves are linear and thread-like, contributing to its delicate appearance.

The species is notable for its ability to withstand harsh coastal conditions, including salt spray and strong winds.
