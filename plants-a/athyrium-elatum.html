Athyrium elatum

Athyrium elatum, commonly known as the giant Japanese shield fern, is a species of fern native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its large, leathery, shield-shaped fronds that can grow up to 1.5 meters in length. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit and thrives in moist, shaded environments. Its leaves are bioluminescent in the dark, a unique trait among ferns.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in shaded garden settings.
