Aristotelia × fruserrata

Aristotelia × fruserrata, commonly known as the South Island kōwhai, is a hybrid plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Elaeocarpaceae family and is characterized by its yellow flowers and compound leaves. The plant typically grows in coastal and lowland areas, often in sunny, well-drained soils. It plays a role in local ecosystems by providing nectar for native birds and insects.

However, it is not commonly cultivated due to its specific environmental requirements.
