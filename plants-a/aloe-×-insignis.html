Aloe × insignis

Aloe × insignis, commonly known as the hybrid aloe or the African hybrid aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, sword-shaped leaves that are typically green with a reddish tinge, especially under stress. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow flowers, which bloom in the spring. Aloe × insignis is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Its resilience and ornamental value make it a popular choice for xeriscaping and indoor gardening.
