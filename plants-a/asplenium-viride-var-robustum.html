Asplenium viride var. robustum

Asplenium viride var. robustum, commonly known as the robust green spleenwort, is a species of fern belonging to the family Aspleniaceae.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen growth habit and features dark green, leathery fronds with a serrated margin. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as rock crevices or on tree trunks. Its spores are produced in small, clustered sori located on the underside of the fronds.

This variety is notable for its hardiness and adaptability to various microhabitats within its native range.
