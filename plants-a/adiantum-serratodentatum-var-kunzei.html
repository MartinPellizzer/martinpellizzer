Adiantum serratodentatum var. kunzei

Adiantum serratodentatum var. kunzei, commonly known as the serrated tooth fern, is a delicate fern species native to Southeast Asia.

It features finely divided, lanceolate fronds that are often tinged with silvery veins, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories. Its leaves are bi-lobed and have a serrated edge, contributing to its common name.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental qualities and air-purifying properties.
