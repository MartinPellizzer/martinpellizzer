Anthurium carmenense

Anthurium carmenense, commonly known as the Carmenense Philodendron, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive spathe and spadix inflorescence. The plant features broad, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a waxy texture. Anthurium carmenense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated as a houseplant.

It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and high humidity for optimal growth.
