Arachnites insengae

Arachnites insengae, commonly known as the silverleaf spider plant, is a unique member of the Asparagaceae family.

It is characterized by its dense clusters of small, silver-white flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant has strap-like, narrow leaves that are typically a vibrant green with a silvery sheen. It is native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia and thrives in well-drained, sandy soils.

Due to its ornamental value and ease of care, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.
