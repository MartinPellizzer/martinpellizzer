Aspidistra alata

Aspidistra alata, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen or broadleaf aspidistra, is a perennial evergreen plant native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the family Araceae and is characterized by its dark green, glossy, and broad lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or white, followed by bright red berries. It thrives in low-light conditions and is popular as a houseplant due to its hardiness and ease of care.

Aspidistra alata is also valued for its ability to purify indoor air and its ornamental foliage.
