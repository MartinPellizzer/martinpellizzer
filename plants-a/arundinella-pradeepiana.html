Arundinella pradeepiana

Arundinella pradeepiana, commonly known as the Pradeep's bamboo, is a flowering plant species native to the Indian subcontinent.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender culms that can reach heights of up to 3 meters. Arundinella pradeepiana is often found in moist, open habitats such as grasslands and riverbanks.
