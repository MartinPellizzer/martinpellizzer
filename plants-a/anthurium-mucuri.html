Anthurium mucuri

Anthurium mucuri, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a reddish or purple tinge. The plant produces vibrant red spathes that enclose its spadix, which is a unique floral structure typical of the Araceae family. Anthurium mucuri thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and ornamental plant collections.
