Ancistrocladus robertsoniorum

Ancistrocladus robertsoniorum, commonly known as the Robertson's ancistrocladus, is a rare shrub native to East Africa.

It belongs to the family Ancistrocladaceae and is characterized by its small, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of yellow flowers. The plant produces small, woody fruits that contain elliptical seeds. It is typically found in rocky or sandy soils in dry, woodland habitats.
