Anthyllis dillenii subsp. pulchella

Anthyllis dillenii subsp.

pulchella, commonly known as the beautiful anthyllis, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region. It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers and trifoliate leaves.

The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It is often found in open habitats such as coastal areas and disturbed ground.

Its botanical features make it an important species for both ecological studies and horticultural interest.
