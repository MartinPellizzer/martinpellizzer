Ardisia kalimbahin

Ardisia kalimbahin, commonly known as the Philippine beautyberry, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It is a small evergreen shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers followed by bright red, berry-like fruits that are edible for some animals but toxic to humans. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and have a glossy appearance, while its bark is smooth and grayish in color.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant fruit display.
