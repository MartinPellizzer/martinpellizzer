Androrchis tenera

Androrchis tenera, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Europe and western Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows. The plant features a slender, upright stem with a single, narrow leaf. Its flowers are small, greenish-yellow, and lack the typical orchid fragrance.

It is known for its delicate appearance and is often found in association with other orchid species in its habitat.
