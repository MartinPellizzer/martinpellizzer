Adenoncos major

Adenoncos major, commonly known as the giant African snail, is a large terrestrial gastropod mollusk native to East Africa.

It is characterized by its elongated, spiral-shaped shell that can grow up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant-like name may be a misidentification, as Adenoncos major is not a plant but an animal. It thrives in humid environments and is often found near vegetation.
