Arisaema ringens

Arisaema ringens, commonly known as the Chinese mountain arisaema, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its unique inflorescence consisting of a spadix surrounded by a spathe. The plant produces a single, showy white spathe that encloses a dense cluster of small, greenish-yellow flowers. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Arisaema ringens is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional Chinese medicine.
