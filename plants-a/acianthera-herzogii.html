Acianthera herzogii

Acianthera herzogii, commonly known as the Herzog's acianthera, is a species of orchid native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale pink. The plant typically blooms during the warmer months, and its flowers are often clustered in racemes. Acianthera herzogii prefers shaded, humid environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.
