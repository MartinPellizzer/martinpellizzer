Ardisia hasseltii

Ardisia hasseltii, commonly known as the glossy privet, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are elliptical in shape and often have a waxy texture. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers that are followed by bright red, fleshy berries. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Ardisia hasseltii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
