Aechmea triticina var. capensis

Aechmea triticina var. capensis, commonly known as the Cape wheatgrass bromeliad, is a striking epiphytic plant native to southern Africa.

It features a rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in a dense, woolly layer of white trichomes. The plant produces vibrant red or orange inflorescences that emerge from the center of the rosette. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and unique appearance.
