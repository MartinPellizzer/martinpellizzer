Alloberberis trifoliolata

Alloberberis trifoliolata, commonly known as the three-leaved berberis, is a shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features compound leaves with three leaflets, which are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, yellow flowers in clusters, followed by red berries that are edible for both humans and wildlife. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or arid environments.

Alloberberis trifoliolata is valued for its ornamental qualities and its ability to stabilize soil in challenging landscapes.
