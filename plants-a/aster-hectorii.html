Aster hectorii

Aster hectorii, commonly known as the Hector's aster, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, often in arid or semi-arid environments. It produces showy blue to purple flowers that bloom in the summer months.

Aster hectorii is valued for its ecological role in supporting pollinators and its use in native plant landscaping.
