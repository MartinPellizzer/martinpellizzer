Aeonium rubrolineatum

Aeonium rubrolineatum, commonly known as the Crimson Blushing Aeonium, is a striking succulent native to the Canary Islands.

It features rosettes of fleshy, triangular leaves that are typically green with red or pink hues along the edges. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow flowers during the spring or early summer. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with plenty of sunlight.

Its unique coloration and compact growth make it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
