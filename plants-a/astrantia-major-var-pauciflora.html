Astrantia major var. pauciflora

Astrantia major var. pauciflora, commonly known as the lesser aster or slender aster, is a herbaceous perennial native to Europe.

It features a basal rosette of narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of small, white to pale purple flowers in late summer. The plant has a fibrous root system and thrives in moist, well-drained soils. It is often found in woodland edges and meadows, where it contributes to biodiversity.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable addition to native plant gardens.
