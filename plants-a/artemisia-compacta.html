Artemisia compacta

Artemisia compacta, commonly known as silverleaf sage, is a perennial herb native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, dense hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flower heads that appear in late summer. It thrives in dry, well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or open habitats.

Its aromatic leaves and resilience make it a valuable plant in both ecological and horticultural contexts.
