Aloe inyangensis var. inyangensis

Aloe inyangensis var. inyangensis, commonly known as the Inyangensis Aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, with a waxy coating to reduce water loss. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing yellow to orange flowers, which attract pollinators such as bees and birds. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional medicinal uses in its native region.
