Anil vicioides

Anil vicioides, commonly known as the yellow vetch, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia and Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is characterized by its leguminous properties and ability to fix nitrogen in the soil. The plant features trifoliate leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in spring. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.

Anil vicioides is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
