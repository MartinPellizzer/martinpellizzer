Anthurium scottmorii

Anthurium scottmorii, commonly known as the Scott Morrison anthurium, is a striking tropical plant native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped spathes that can range in color from deep red to orange, often with contrasting patterns. The plant features a tall, slender stem with waxy, dark green leaves that are elliptical in shape. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Anthurium scottmorii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique and eye-catching foliage.
