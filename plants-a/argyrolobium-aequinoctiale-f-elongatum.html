Argyrolobium aequinoctiale f. elongatum

Argyrolobium aequinoctiale f. elongatum, commonly known as the elongated silver globular vetch, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a member of the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod-bearing fruits. The plant features small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring. Its distinctive feature is the elongated, silver-green pods that develop after flowering.
