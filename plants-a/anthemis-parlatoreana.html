Anthemis parlatoreana

Anthemis parlatoreana, commonly known as the Parlatore's fleabane, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in dry, sunny habitats such as rocky soils and grasslands. Its flowers are yellow and resemble daisies, with ray florets surrounding a central disk.

It is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
