Aloe macilenta

Aloe macilenta, commonly known as the slender aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features tall, thin, branching stems that are often covered in a layer of white, powdery wax. The plant produces clusters of small, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. Its leaves are narrow and fleshy, adapted to store water in arid environments.

Aloe macilenta is well-suited for rock gardens or as a container plant due to its drought tolerance and unique appearance.
