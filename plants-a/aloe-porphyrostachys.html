Aloe porphyrostachys

Aloe porphyrostachys, commonly known as the purple-stemmed aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with red or purple markings along the edges. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow flowers, which bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are used for their gel, which has medicinal properties and is often applied for skin care.

Aloe porphyrostachys is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
