Aloinopsis rosulata

Aloinopsis rosulata, commonly known as the Rosulate Aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy, blue-green leaves that store water, adapting to arid environments. The plant produces tall, branching inflorescences with orange to red flowers during the warmer months. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
