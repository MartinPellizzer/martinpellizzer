Armeria welwitschii

Armeria welwitschii, commonly known as the Welwitsch's thrift, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically forms dense clusters of foliage. The plant produces showy, pink or white flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its hardy nature and ornamental value make it a popular choice among gardeners.
