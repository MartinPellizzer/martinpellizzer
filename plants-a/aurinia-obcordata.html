Aurinia obcordata

Aurinia obcordata, commonly known as the heartleaf bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central and Eastern Europe.

It features attractive, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers that are typically blue or violet in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sun.

Aurinia obcordata is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and easy maintenance.
