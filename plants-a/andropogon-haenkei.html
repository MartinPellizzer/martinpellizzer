Andropogon haenkei

Andropogon haenkei, commonly known as switchgrass, is a perennial grass native to the eastern and central United States.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and narrow, grass-like leaves that are often bluish-green in color. This plant is highly adaptable, thriving in a variety of soil types and environmental conditions. It is widely used in ecological restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil and support biodiversity.

Additionally, switchgrass is a valuable source of biomass for renewable energy production.
