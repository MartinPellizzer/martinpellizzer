Alsophila denticulata

Alsophila denticulata, commonly known as the toothed tree fern, is a species native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia and the Pacific.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk covered in a fibrous, brownish bark. The plant produces large, feather-like fronds that can reach several meters in length. Alsophila denticulata thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Its unique appearance and ecological role make it a notable species in botanical studies.
