Alstonia longissima

Alstonia longissima, commonly known as the African angel or white angel tree, is a member of the Rubiaceae family.

It is a fast-growing, evergreen tree native to tropical regions of Africa. The plant is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and large, opposite, ovate leaves that are glossy and dark green. It produces small, fragrant white flowers followed by yellowish-orange fruits that contain seeds.

Alstonia longissima is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
