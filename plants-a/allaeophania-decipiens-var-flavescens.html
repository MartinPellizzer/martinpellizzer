Allaeophania decipiens var. flavescens

Allaeophania decipiens var. flavescens, commonly known as the yellow deceptive allaeophania, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers that bloom during the summer months. The plant has small, lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full sunlight.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological role, it is often found in forest understories and along stream banks.
