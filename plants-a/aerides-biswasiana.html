Aerides biswasiana

Aerides biswasiana, commonly known as the Bishwas orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers. The plant typically features dark green, strap-like leaves and blooms in shades of white, pink, and red. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Aerides biswasiana is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
