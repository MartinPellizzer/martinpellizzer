Alsophila pycnoneura

Alsophila pycnoneura, commonly known as the narrow-leaf tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its slender, upright trunk and feathery, compound fronds that give it a distinctive appearance. Its fronds are typically dark green and can grow quite long, contributing to its ornamental value.
