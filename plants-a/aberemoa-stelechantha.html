Aberemoa stelechantha

Aberemoa stelechantha, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine, velvety hairs. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or greenish in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in the understory of rainforests.

Its botanical features make it an important species in ecological studies and traditional medicine.
