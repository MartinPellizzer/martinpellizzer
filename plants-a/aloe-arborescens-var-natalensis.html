Aloe arborescens var. natalensis

Aloe arborescens var. natalensis, commonly known as Natal aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a branched stem with fleshy, triangular leaves that are green with white spots. The plant produces bright yellow flowers in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Its medicinal properties have been traditionally used for skin treatments and digestive health.
