Asterocephalus commutatus

Asterocephalus commutatus, commonly known as the variable asterhead, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in grasslands and rocky areas, thriving in well-drained soils. Its flowers are usually yellow or brown, with a distinctive, radiate structure.
