Anthurium straminopetiolum

Anthurium straminopetiolum, commonly known as the golden arrow plant, is a tropical flowering plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a metallic sheen. The plant produces striking spadices, which are the modified leaves that enclose the true flowers, often in shades of red or orange. It thrives in humid, warm environments with bright, indirect light.

Anthurium straminopetiolum is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
