Anthemis caulescens

Anthemis caulescens, commonly known as the slender everlasting, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments, showcasing adaptations to drought conditions. Its flowers are yellow and have a strong, often aromatic scent.

It is known for its hardy nature and is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes.
