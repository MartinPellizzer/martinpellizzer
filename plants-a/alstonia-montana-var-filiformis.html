Alstonia montana var. filiformis

Alstonia montana var. filiformis, commonly known as the slender alstonia, is a variant of the alstonia tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its narrow, elongated leaves and distinctive, elongated fruit pods. The plant is typically found in tropical and subtropical regions, growing in moist, shaded environments. Its botanical features include smooth, greyish bark and clusters of small, white flowers.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ecological importance in its native habitats.
