Aloe marginata

Aloe marginata, commonly known as the marginated aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features tall, branched stems covered in dense, gray-green leaves with white to yellow margins. The leaves are fleshy and adapted to store water, a characteristic typical of desert plants. It produces yellow flowers in clusters during the summer months.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional medicinal uses.
