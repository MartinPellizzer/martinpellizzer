Allium sanandajense

Allium sanandajense, commonly known as the Sanandaj garlic.

It is a bulbous plant native to the mountainous regions of western Iran. The plant produces small, white, spherical bulbs that are clustered together. It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic leaves and flowers.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
