Aglaonema treubii

Aglaonema treubii, commonly known as the Variegated Chinese Evergreen, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated foliage with shades of green, white, and yellow. The plant's leaves are typically broad and elliptical, often featuring intricate patterns that enhance its ornamental value. It thrives in low-light conditions and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Aglaonema treubii is popular as a houseplant due to its air-purifying qualities and ability to thrive indoors with minimal care.
