Artemisia campestris var. canadensis

Artemisia campestris var. canadensis, commonly known as Canadian tarragon, is a perennial herb native to North America.

It features aromatic, silver-green foliage with finely divided, needle-like leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Its essential oils, particularly those containing thymol, contribute to its medicinal and culinary uses.
