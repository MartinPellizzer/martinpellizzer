Astragalus chamaeleuce var. panguicensis

Astragalus chamaeleuce var. panguicensis, commonly known as the Panguicense milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to specific regions of China.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, pea-like flowers. The plant is typically found in alpine or rocky habitats, thriving in well-drained soils.
