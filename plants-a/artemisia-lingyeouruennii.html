Artemisia lingyeouruennii

Artemisia lingyeouruennii, commonly known as the Yunnan wormwood, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, silver-gray foliage. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions and thrives in well-drained soils. It produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in the summer months.

Due to its medicinal properties, it is sometimes used in traditional Chinese medicine.
