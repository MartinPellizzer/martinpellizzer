Alsophila formosana

Alsophila formosana, commonly known as the Formosa tree fern, is a species native to Taiwan.

Its fronds are typically dark green and can grow up to 3 meters in length, contributing to its striking appearance.

Alsophila formosana is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and is valued for its ornamental qualities.
