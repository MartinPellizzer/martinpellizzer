Achyranthes scandens

Achyranthes scandens, commonly known as climbing smartweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Polygonaceae family and is characterized by its slender, climbing stems that often twine around other vegetation. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers in clusters, which are followed by three-lobed seed capsules. Its leaves are alternate, ovate to rhombic in shape, and have a slightly rough texture.

Achyranthes scandens is often found in moist environments such as wetlands, ditches, and along stream banks.
