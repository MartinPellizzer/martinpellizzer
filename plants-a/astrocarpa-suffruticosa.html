Astrocarpa suffruticosa

Astrocarpa suffruticosa, commonly known as the silverleaf dandelion, is a perennial herb native to South America.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and yellow composite flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. It has a fibrous root system and produces small, wind-dispersed seeds.

Astrocarpa suffruticosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting pollinators.
