Aloe abyssinica

Aloe abyssinica, commonly known as the Abyssinian aloe, is a succulent plant native to Ethiopia.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a waxy coating, which helps it retain moisture in arid environments. The plant produces clusters of yellow flowers that bloom during the warmer months. Its root system is adapted to absorb water efficiently from the soil.

Aloe abyssinica is valued for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional remedies.
