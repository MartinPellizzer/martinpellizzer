Astragalus ordosicus

Astragalus ordosicus, commonly known as the Ordos milkvetch, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to arid and semi-arid regions of northern China, particularly in the Ordos region. The plant is characterized by its slender, branching stems and small, yellowish to brownish flowers that bloom in early summer. Its leaves are pinnately compound, with numerous small leaflets, and it produces cylindrical pods that contain several seeds.

Astragalus ordosicus is adapted to harsh environmental conditions, making it a resilient species in its native habitat.
