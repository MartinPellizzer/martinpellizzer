Achillea Millefolium Subsp. Sudetica

Achillea millefolium subsp. sudetica is a lesser-known variant of the common yarrow plant. It thrives in temperate climates and is native to parts of Europe and Asia. This subspecies is characterized by its finely divided leaves and clusters of small white or pale yellow flowers. It is often found in meadows, grasslands, and along roadsides. Despite its modest appearance, it plays an important role in supporting local pollinators and soil stability.
