Anoplophytum Strobilanthum

Anoplophytum strobilanthum is a unique flowering plant native to the Western Ghats of India. It is known for its striking purple and pink flowers that bloom in dense clusters during the monsoon season. This plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forested areas. Its leaves are dark green and have a velvety texture, making it visually distinct. Despite its beauty, it remains a relatively lesser-known species within the plant kingdom.
