Aloe consobrina

Aloe consobrina, commonly known as the Cape aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, sparsely branched stems and produces striking, orange-red flowers during the summer months. The plant has thick, fleshy leaves that store water, typical of many succulents. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it suitable for arid environments.

Aloe consobrina is also valued for its ornamental beauty and has been used in traditional medicine.
