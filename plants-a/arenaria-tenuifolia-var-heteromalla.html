Arenaria tenuifolia var. heteromalla

Arenaria tenuifolia var. heteromalla, commonly known as the slender sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to arctic and alpine regions.

It typically features small, narrow leaves and produces clusters of white or pink flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained, sandy or rocky soils and is adapted to cold climates. It is often found in tundra and mountainous environments where it plays a role in stabilizing soil and supporting local ecosystems.

Its botanical characteristics reflect its evolutionary adaptation to harsh, nutrient-poor conditions.
