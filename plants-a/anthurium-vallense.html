Anthurium vallense

Anthurium vallense, commonly known as the velvet anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the Andes region of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a velvety texture. The plant produces vibrant red spadices, which are the modified leaves that enclose the actual flowers. It thrives in humid, warm environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium vallense is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its ornamental foliage and unique inflorescence.
