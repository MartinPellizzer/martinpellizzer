Athanasia parviflora

Athanasia parviflora, commonly known as the small-flowered immortality, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, pale yellow flowers during the summer months. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is known for its hardy nature.

Athanasia parviflora is also valued in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
