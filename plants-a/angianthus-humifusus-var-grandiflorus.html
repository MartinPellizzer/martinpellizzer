Angianthus humifusus var. grandiflorus

Angianthus humifusus var. grandiflorus, commonly known as the broadflower angianthus, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically forms dense mats of foliage. The plant produces showy, yellow-orange inflorescences that are composed of numerous small, tubular flowers. It thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms and drought tolerance.
