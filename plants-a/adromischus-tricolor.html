Adromischus tricolor

Adromischus tricolor, commonly known as the Tricolor Stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, rosette-shaped growth habit and thick, fleshy leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid conditions. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, typically during the summer months. Its leaves are usually a vibrant green with red or purple markings, adding to its ornamental appeal.

Adromischus tricolor is well-suited for rock gardens or as a container plant due to its low maintenance requirements.
