Aspidoglossum angustissimum

Aspidoglossum angustissimum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved snake plant, is a member of the Asparagaceae family.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its upright, sword-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green color. The plant produces small, white flowers in a raceme, which are followed by dark blue berries. It is a succulent, meaning it stores water in its leaves and stems, making it drought-tolerant.

This plant is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and air-purifying properties.
