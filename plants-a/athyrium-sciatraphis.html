Athyrium sciatraphis

Athyrium sciatraphis, commonly known as the narrow-leaf Japanese painted fern, is a delicate fern native to East Asia.

It features intricate, finely divided fronds that are typically dark green with a silvery underside. The plant thrives in shady, moist environments and prefers well-drained, acidic soil. Its elegant appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

It reproduces through spores and is often cultivated for its aesthetic value in shaded garden settings.
