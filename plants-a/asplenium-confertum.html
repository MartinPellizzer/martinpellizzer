Asplenium confertum

Asplenium confertum, commonly known as the dense spleenwort, is a species of fern belonging to the family Aspleniaceae.

It is characterized by its compact, mat-forming growth habit and dark green, leathery fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as rock crevices or forest floors, and is often found in temperate regions. Its spores are produced on the underside of the fronds, which are arranged in a tight, clustered pattern.

Asplenium confertum is a hardy plant that is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
