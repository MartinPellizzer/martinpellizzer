Astragalus schachbuzensis

Astragalus schachbuzensis, commonly known as the Schachbuz milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments, adapting to harsh conditions with deep root systems.

It plays a role in soil stabilization and is of interest for its potential medicinal properties.
