Aethionema lipskyi

Aethionema lipskyi, commonly known as the Armenian candytuft, is a flowering plant native to the mountains of Armenia and Turkey.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces clusters of small, white or pink flowers in the spring. The plant has hairy, lance-shaped leaves and is adapted to rocky and arid environments. It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants.
