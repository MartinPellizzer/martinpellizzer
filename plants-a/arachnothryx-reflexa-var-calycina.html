Arachnothryx reflexa var. calycina

Arachnothryx reflexa var. calycina, commonly known as the calycine arachnothryx, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil, and thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant is characterized by its compound leaves and small, pea-like flowers that are typically greenish or yellowish in color. Its fruits are legumes that contain several seeds, and the plant is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
