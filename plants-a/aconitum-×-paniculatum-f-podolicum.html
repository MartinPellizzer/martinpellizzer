Aconitum × paniculatum f. podolicum

Aconitum × paniculatum f. podolicum, commonly known as the Podolic hybrid aconite, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central and Eastern Europe.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its tall, erect stem that can reach up to 2 meters in height. The plant produces showy blue to purple flowers arranged in a dense, conical inflorescence called a panicle. Its leaves are deeply lobed and have a distinctive, somewhat fern-like appearance.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or streamside habitats.
