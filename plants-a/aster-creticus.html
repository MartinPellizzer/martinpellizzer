Aster creticus

Aster creticus, commonly known as the Cretan aster, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features upright, slender stems that bear clusters of small, blue to purple flowers. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a slightly fuzzy appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

The Cretan aster is valued for its ornamental beauty and historical significance in traditional medicine.
