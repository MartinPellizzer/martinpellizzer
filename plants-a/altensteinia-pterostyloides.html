Altensteinia pterostyloides

Altensteinia pterostyloides, commonly known as the narrow-leaved altensteinia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
