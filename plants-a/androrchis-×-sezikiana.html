Androrchis × sezikiana

Androrchis × sezikiana, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a rare and fascinating plant resulting from the cross between Androrchis kiusianus and Androrchis spathaceus.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its striking violet and white flowers, which bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in forest undergrowth where it can thrive. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb nutrients from the soil efficiently.

This hybrid is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated by enthusiasts interested in rare orchids.
