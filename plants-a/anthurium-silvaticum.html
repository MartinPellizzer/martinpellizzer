Anthurium silvaticum

Anthurium silvaticum, commonly known as the velvet leaf anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a velvety texture. The plant produces clusters of small, spadix-like flowers surrounded by a colorful spathe, which can range from red to orange. Anthurium silvaticum thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical landscapes.
