Allionia pilosa var. parva

Allionia pilosa var. parva, commonly known as the dwarf allionia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a small, evergreen shrub characterized by its compact growth habit and dense foliage. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale pink flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its ornamental value lies in its attractive foliage and delicate blooms, which make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking low-maintenance, decorative plants.
