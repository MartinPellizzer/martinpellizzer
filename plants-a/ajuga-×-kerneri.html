Ajuga × kerneri

Ajuga × kerneri, commonly known as the carpet bugleweed, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It features dense, mat-forming foliage with heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and slightly hairy. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of blue or purple, which bloom in early summer. Ajuga × kerneri is well-suited for rock gardens and shaded areas due to its compact growth habit and ornamental appeal.

It is also valued for its ability to suppress weeds and improve soil structure in garden settings.
