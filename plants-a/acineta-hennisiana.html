Acineta hennisiana

Acineta hennisiana, commonly known as the Hennisian acineta, is a unique succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family, known for its diverse and often striking floral displays. The plant features a low-growing, mat-forming structure with fleshy, cylindrical stems that store water, adapting it to arid environments. Acineta hennisiana is prized by collectors for its compact size and distinctive appearance.
