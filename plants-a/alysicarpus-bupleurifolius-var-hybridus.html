Alysicarpus bupleurifolius var. hybridus

Alysicarpus bupleurifolius var. hybridus, commonly known as the hybrid bupleurifolius, is a member of the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits.

Its flowers are usually small and clustered, with a distinctive yellow or greenish coloration.
