Aloe succotrina var. saxigena

Aloe succotrina var. saxigena, commonly known as the rock aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a reddish or purple tinge along the edges. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with small, yellowish-green flowers. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its leaves and stems.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and resilience in dry climates.
