Anthurium brevispadix

Anthurium brevispadix, commonly known as the short-spadix anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a glossy texture. The plant produces an inflorescence consisting of a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe, which can range from white to red or yellow. Anthurium brevispadix thrives in humid, warm environments with bright, indirect light.

It is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and unique floral structure.
