Anoectochilus tetsuoi

Anoectochilus tetsuoi, commonly known as the Tetsuoi orchid, is a rare and endemic orchid species native to Japan.

It is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in late spring or early summer. The plant grows as a terrestrial orchid, often found in shaded, humid environments such as mountain forests. Its leaves are elliptical and dark green, with a waxy texture that helps retain moisture.

The species is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts due to its unique appearance and limited natural habitat.
