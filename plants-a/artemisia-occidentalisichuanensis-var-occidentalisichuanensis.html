Artemisia occidentalisichuanensis var. occidentalisichuanensis

Artemisia occidentalisichuanensis var. occidentalisichuanensis, commonly known as the Chinese wormwood, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of southwestern China.

It is characterized by its aromatic, silver-gray foliage with narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in panicles, typically blooming in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rocky or dry garden environments.

This variety is valued for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional Chinese medicine.
