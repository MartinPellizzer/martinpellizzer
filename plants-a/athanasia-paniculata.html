Athanasia paniculata

Athanasia paniculata, commonly known as the yellow panic grass, is a perennial grass species native to Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and is often found in open grasslands and disturbed soils. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it adaptable to various environmental conditions.

Its ornamental value has led to its cultivation in some regions for landscaping purposes.
