Abies excelsa var. eremita

Abies excelsa var. eremita, commonly known as the eremite fir, is a rare and unique variant of the white fir.

It is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and dense, dark green foliage. Native to the high altitudes of the Andes in South America, this plant thrives in cool, moist environments with well-drained soil. Its cones are typically small and clustered, and it produces both male and female cones on the same plant.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a delicate and endangered species.
