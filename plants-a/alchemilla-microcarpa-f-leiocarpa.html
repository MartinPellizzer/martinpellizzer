Alchemilla microcarpa f. leiocarpa

Alchemilla microcarpa f. leiocarpa, commonly known as the smooth-leaved lady's mantle, is a cultivar of the alchemilla genus.

It is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit and silver-green foliage. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It prefers moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in shaded, humid environments.
