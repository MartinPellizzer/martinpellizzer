Albuca avasimontana

Albuca avasimontana, commonly known as the Natal albuca, is a bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It produces narrow, grass-like leaves and clusters of white to yellow flowers during the summer months. The plant is adapted to dry conditions and thrives in well-drained soil. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ease of care.

Albuca avasimontana is a member of the Asparagaceae family, which includes many other bulbous and ornamental plants.
