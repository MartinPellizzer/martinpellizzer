Anacamptis × timbali nothosubsp.

timbali, commonly known as the Timbali orchid, is a hybrid plant native to the Mediterranean region. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its tall, upright growth habit.

The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white, typically arranged in a raceme. It thrives in sunny, well-drained habitats such as meadows and rocky soils.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for both horticultural gardens and naturalistic landscapes.
