Aspidium spinulosum subsp. euspinulosum

Aspidium spinulosum subsp.

euspinulosum, commonly known as the spinulose water fern, is a fern species native to Southeast Asia. It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds.

The plant typically grows in wet, shaded environments such as marshes and along stream banks. Its leaves are often covered with small, spiny projections, which give it its common name.
