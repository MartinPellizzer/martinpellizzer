Amaryllis belladonna baptisa-alba

Amaryllis belladonna baptisa-alba, commonly known as the white belladonna amaryllis, is a striking plant native to South Africa.

It features large, showy white flowers with a subtle pinkish tinge, blooming in late winter to early spring. The plant has strap-like, dark green leaves that are narrow and elongated. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

This species is prized for its elegant appearance and is often cultivated as a decorative indoor plant.
