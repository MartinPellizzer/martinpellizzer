Anthurium scherzerianum var. sanguineum

Anthurium scherzerianum var. sanguineum, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces vibrant red spathes that enclose bright yellow spadices, creating a dramatic floral display. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and high humidity.

This variety is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and ease of care.
