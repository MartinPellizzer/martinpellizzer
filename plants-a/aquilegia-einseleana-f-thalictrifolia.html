Aquilegia einseleana f. thalictrifolia

Aquilegia einseleana f. thalictrifolia, commonly known as the Thalictrifolia form of the Swiss waterdropwort, is a hybrid plant that exhibits characteristics of both Aquilegia einseleana and Thalictrum.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant with distinctive spurred flowers that range in color from white to pink. The foliage consists of palmately compound leaves, which are typically green and finely divided. This plant is often found in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream sides.

Its botanical interest lies in its hybrid nature and unique floral morphology.
