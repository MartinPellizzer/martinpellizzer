Anthericum tenue

Anthericum tenue, commonly known as slender mouse ear, is a low-growing perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Araceae and is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit. The plant features small, white, spadix-like flowers surrounded by a modified leaf called a spathe. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Anthericum tenue thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodlands and along stream banks.
