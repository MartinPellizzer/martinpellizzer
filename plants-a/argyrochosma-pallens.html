Argyrochosma pallens

Argyrochosma pallens, commonly known as the silverleaf saxifrage, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, powdery hairs. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring. It typically grows in rocky or gravelly habitats, often in alpine or subalpine regions.

Its botanical features make it well adapted to harsh, arid environments.
