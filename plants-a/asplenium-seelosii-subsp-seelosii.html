Asplenium seelosii subsp. seelosii

Asplenium seelosii subsp.

seelosii, commonly known as the rock fern, is a terrestrial fern native to Southeast Asia. It typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as rocky crevices and forest floors.

The plant features lance-shaped, dark green fronds with a serrated margin. It has a compact growth habit and is often found in humid, tropical climates.

Its rhizome is creeping, allowing it to spread and form dense mats in suitable habitats.
