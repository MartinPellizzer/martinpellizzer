Areca gracilis

Areca gracilis, commonly known as the slender areca palm, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, multi-stemmed trunk and feathery, palmate leaves that give it a graceful appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by elongated, three-lobed fruits. Areca gracilis thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and air-purifying properties.
