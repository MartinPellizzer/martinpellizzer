Aptosimum junceum

Aptosimum junceum, commonly known as slender false flax, is a flowering plant native to the western United States and parts of Canada.

It is a member of the Poaceae family, which includes grasses, and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. Aptosimum junceum is adapted to arid conditions and is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its resilience and ability to stabilize soils.
