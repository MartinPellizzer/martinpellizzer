Aster gracilipes

Aster gracilipes, commonly known as slenderleaf aster, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands. It produces small, pink to purple flowers that bloom in the summer months.

Its slender leaves and delicate appearance make it a distinctive species within its native range.
