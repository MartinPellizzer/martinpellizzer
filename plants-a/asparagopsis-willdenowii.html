Asparagopsis willdenowii

Asparagopsis willdenowii, commonly known as the red lily or red spider lily, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its striking red or orange flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests or along stream banks. Its leaves are strap-shaped and often have a waxy coating, while its rhizomes form dense clusters.

Asparagopsis willdenowii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is also used in traditional medicine in some cultures.
