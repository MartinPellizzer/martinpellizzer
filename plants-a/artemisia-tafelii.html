Artemisia tafelii

Artemisia tafelii, commonly known as the Tafel's wormwood, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, silver-gray foliage. The plant produces small, yellowish flower heads that are typical of the daisy family. It thrives in dry, well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Artemisia tafelii is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
