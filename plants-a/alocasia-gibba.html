Alocasia gibba

Alocasia gibba, commonly known as the umbrella plant, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often a deep green with a waxy texture. The plant features a thick, swollen stem and produces small, pale yellow flowers that are rarely seen. Alocasia gibba thrives in moist, shady environments and requires high humidity and well-draining soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical landscapes.
