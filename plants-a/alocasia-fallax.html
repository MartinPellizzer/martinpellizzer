Alocasia fallax

Alocasia fallax, commonly known as the Black Velvet Alocasia, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features large, heart-shaped leaves with a deep, velvety black color that contrasts beautifully with its green veins. The plant thrives in humid environments with bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and as a statement plant in ornamental settings.

Alocasia fallax is known for its rapid growth and ability to add a dramatic visual impact to any space.
