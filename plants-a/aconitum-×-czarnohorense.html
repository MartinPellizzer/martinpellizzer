Aconitum × czarnohorense

Aconitum × czarnohorense, commonly known as the Black Highland Aconite, is a hybrid perennial plant belonging to the Ranunculaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers, which bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, featuring palmate leaves with a coarse texture. Native to the mountainous regions of Central Europe, it thrives in moist, well-drained soils.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in shady, woodland environments.
