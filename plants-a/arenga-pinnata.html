Arenga pinnata

Arenga pinnata, commonly known as sugar palm, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a tall, slender tree that can grow up to 20 meters in height, with a straight trunk and large, pinnate leaves. The plant is characterized by its fibrous trunk, which is used to produce natural fibers called areca fiber. Its flowers are small and clustered, and it produces a type of fruit that is edible.

Arenga pinnata is also valued for its sap, which can be processed into various products.
