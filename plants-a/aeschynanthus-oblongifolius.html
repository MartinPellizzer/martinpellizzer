Aeschynanthus oblongifolius

Aeschynanthus oblongifolius, commonly known as the velvet tangerine.

It is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly Kenya and Tanzania. The plant is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage and clusters of small, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of yellow and orange. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant.
