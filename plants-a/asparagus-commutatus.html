Asparagus commutatus

Asparagus commutatus, commonly known as the smooth asparagus, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features slender, grass-like stems that grow close to the ground, often forming dense mats. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that bloom in late spring. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, and the plant is known for its ability to thrive in arid conditions.

Asparagus commutatus is often used in traditional medicine and is valued for its ornamental appeal in landscaping.
