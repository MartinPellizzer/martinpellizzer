Achras balata

Achras balata, commonly known as the rubber tree, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, palm-like leaves and produces a milky sap that is rich in latex. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil. Its trunk is typically smooth and can grow quite tall, reaching up to 20 meters in height.

The latex obtained from Achras balata has been historically used in the production of natural rubber.
