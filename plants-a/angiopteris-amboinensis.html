Angiopteris amboinensis

Angiopteris amboinensis, commonly known as the Amboina water fern, is a tropical fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, lobed fronds that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit, with creeping stems that produce new fronds along their length. Its leaves are typically bipinnate, with a delicate, feathery appearance.

Angiopteris amboinensis thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in humid tropical forests.
