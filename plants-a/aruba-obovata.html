Aruba obovata

Aruba obovata, commonly known as the broad-leafed aruban plant, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean region.

It is characterized by its broad, ovate leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery pubescence. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pale pink, typically blooming during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for coastal gardens.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is valued both for ornamental purposes and as a component of local ecosystems.
