Anthurium × grusonii

Anthurium × grusonii, commonly known as the Red Anthurium, is a hybrid plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are dark green with a velvety texture. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-shaped inflorescences surrounded by a showy, spatulate spathe that ranges from red to orange. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Anthurium × grusonii is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
