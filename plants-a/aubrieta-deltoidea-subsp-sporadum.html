Aubrieta deltoidea subsp. sporadum

Aubrieta deltoidea subsp.

sporadum, commonly known as the sporadic aubrieta, is a low-growing, flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region. It features compact, mat-forming growth with bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring.

The plant has gray-green, hairy leaves that are small and rounded, contributing to its drought-resistant characteristics. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in temperate climates.
