Ardisia tuirana

Ardisia tuirana, commonly known as the Tuirana beautyberry, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the Caribbean.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a smooth texture. The plant produces small, white flowers that are clustered in umbels, followed by bright red, berry-like fruits that attract birds. Ardisia tuirana thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade, making it suitable for shaded garden settings.

Its ornamental value comes from its striking foliage and colorful fruits, which add visual interest throughout the year.
