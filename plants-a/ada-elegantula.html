Ada elegantula

Ada elegantula, commonly known as the elegant lily, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. The plant typically grows to a height of 30 to 60 centimeters and features narrow, grass-like leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
