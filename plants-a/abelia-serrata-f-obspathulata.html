Abelia serrata f. obspathulata

Abelia serrata f. obspathulata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf abelia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to China.

It features small, ovate leaves that are serrated along the edges, giving it a distinctive texture. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white to pinkish flowers in late spring to early summer. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
