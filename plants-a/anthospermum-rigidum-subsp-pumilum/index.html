Anthospermum Rigidum Subsp. Pumilum

Anthospermum rigidum subsp. pumilum is a lesser-known species within the Rubiaceae family. It is characterized by its compact growth habit and small, clustered flowers. Native to parts of Southeast Asia, this plant thrives in humid, shaded environments. Despite its modest appearance, it holds ecological significance in its native habitats. Its unique morphology and limited distribution make it an intriguing subject for botanical study.
