Acianthera morenoi

Acianthera morenoi, commonly known as the Moreno's acianthera, is a rare orchid species native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, fragrant flowers with a distinctive coloration. The plant typically blooms during the cooler months, showcasing its adaptability to high-altitude environments. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered in a fine, velvety texture.
