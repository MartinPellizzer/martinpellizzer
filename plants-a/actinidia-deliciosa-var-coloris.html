Actinidia deliciosa var. coloris

Actinidia deliciosa var. coloris, commonly known as the colored kiwifruit, is a variant of the Chinese gooseberry native to China.

This plant is a vigorous, deciduous vine that thrives in temperate climates with well-drained soil. Its leaves are heart-shaped and have a velvety texture, while the fruit exhibits a range of vibrant colors, including red, yellow, and green. The fruit is typically smaller than the common kiwifruit and has a sweeter, more aromatic flavor.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and unique culinary qualities.
