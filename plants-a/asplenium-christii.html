Asplenium christii

Asplenium christii, commonly known as the African maidenhair fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, lace-like fronds that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit, allowing it to spread and form dense clumps. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in rocky or forested areas.

Asplenium christii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to care for in a home setting.
