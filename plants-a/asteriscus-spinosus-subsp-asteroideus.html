Asteriscus spinosus subsp. asteroideus

Asteriscus spinosus subsp.

asteroideus, commonly known as the starry asterisk, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its small, star-shaped white or pale blue flowers that bloom in late summer.

The plant has hairy, branched stems and narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
