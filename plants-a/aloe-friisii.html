Aloe friisii

Aloe friisii, commonly known as the Cape Aloe, is a striking succulent native to South Africa.

It features tall, narrow leaves that are often tinged with red or purple at the base, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces vibrant orange or yellow flowers in clusters during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for arid climates.

Its unique morphology and ornamental value make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
