Athyriopsis lushanensis

Athyriopsis lushanensis, commonly known as the Lushan rock fern, is a rare species native to China.

It belongs to the family Athyriaceae and is characterized by its compact, evergreen fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as rocky crevices and forest floors. Its leaves are leathery and have a distinctive, intricate pattern of veins.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a conservation concern.
