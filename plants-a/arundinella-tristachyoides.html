Arundinella tristachyoides

Arundinella tristachyoides, commonly known as the slender bamboo or bamboo grass, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

It belongs to the grass family (Poaceae) and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences with numerous tiny flowers, typical of many grass species. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along riverbanks.

Its growth habit and ecological role make it an important component of its native habitats.
