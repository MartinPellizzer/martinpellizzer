Arisaema polyphyllum

Arisaema polyphyllum, commonly known as the multileaved arrowhead, is a herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of eastern Asia.

It features a short, rhizomatous stem with large, heart-shaped leaves that are often deeply lobed. The plant produces striking, spadix-like inflorescences surrounded by a showy, hood-shaped spathe. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its unique morphology makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in humid climates.
