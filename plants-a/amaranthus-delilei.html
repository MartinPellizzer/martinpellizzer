Amaranthus delilei

Amaranthus delilei, commonly known as the Delile's amaranth, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and vibrant, showy bracts that range in color from red to orange. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for ornamental gardening.

Amaranthus delilei is also valued for its potential as a drought-tolerant plant in xeriscaping.
