Agrostis truncatula var. font-queri

Agrostis truncatula var. font-queri, commonly known as Font-Queri bentgrass, is a low-growing perennial grass species native to the Mediterranean region.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically bluish-green in color and have a fine texture. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas and rocky habitats.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes used in ornamental landscaping and ecological restoration projects.
