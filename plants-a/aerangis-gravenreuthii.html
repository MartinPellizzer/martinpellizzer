Aerangis gravenreuthii

Aerangis gravenreuthii, commonly known as the Gravenreuth's orchid, is a species native to South Africa.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a compact, rhizomatous growth habit. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in a dense spike, typically blooming in late spring. It prefers shady, moist habitats and is often found in forested areas.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
