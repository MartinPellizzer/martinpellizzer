Abutilon ramosum var. chevalieri

Abutilon ramosum var. chevalieri, commonly known as the velvetleaf abutilon, is a species of flowering plant in the mallow family.

It is characterized by its soft, hairy foliage and showy, lantern-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. Native to tropical regions of the Americas, this plant thrives in warm, humid climates. It is often cultivated as an ornamental due to its attractive appearance and ease of growth.

The plant's rapid growth and spreading habit make it suitable for use in gardens or as a ground cover.
