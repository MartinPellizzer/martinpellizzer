Acacia discolor var. glabra

Acacia discolor var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth acacia, is a shrub native to parts of Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, yellow flowers that appear in globular clusters. The leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a glabrous (smooth) surface, giving the plant its varietal name. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to dry, arid environments.

This plant is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
