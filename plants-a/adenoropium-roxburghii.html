Adenoropium roxburghii

Adenoropium roxburghii, commonly known as the Himalayan rosebay, is a shrub native to the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green leaves and showy, pink to red flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant is typically found in moist, well-drained soils at high altitudes. It has a woody stem and produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain seeds.

Adenoropium roxburghii is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often used in horticulture.
