Allamanda cathartica f. salicifolia

Allamanda cathartica f. salicifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf yellow allamanda, is a hybrid cultivar of the yellow allamanda.

It is a fast-growing, evergreen shrub or small tree native to South America. The plant is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped yellow flowers. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil.

This ornamental plant is often cultivated for its vibrant blooms and is relatively easy to care for in suitable climates.
