Amperea xiphoclada var. xiphoclada

Amperea xiphoclada var. xiphoclada, commonly known as the narrow-leaf amperea, is a perennial shrub native to southern Africa.

It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and slightly hairy. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers in clusters during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or coastal landscapes.

Its ornamental value lies in its elegant foliage and delicate floral display.
