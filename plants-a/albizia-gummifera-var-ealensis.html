Albizia gummifera var. ealensis

Albizia gummifera var. ealensis, commonly known as the Ealensis Albizia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compound leaves, which are often pinnately arranged and have a feathery appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink, arranged in racemes. Its bark is thick and exudes a sticky resin, which is one of its distinguishing features.

It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often found in moist, well-drained soils.
