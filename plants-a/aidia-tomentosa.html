Aidia tomentosa

Aidia tomentosa, commonly known as the wooly aidia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Aidia tomentosa is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique texture and seasonal blooms.
