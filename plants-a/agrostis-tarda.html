Agrostis tarda

Agrostis tarda, commonly known as silver hairgrass, is a perennial grass species native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-gray foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its inflorescence consists of a single, narrow spikelet that is often hidden among the leaves.

Agrostis tarda is valued in horticulture for its ornamental qualities and ability to thrive in humid conditions.
