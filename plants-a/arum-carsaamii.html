Arum carsaamii

Arum carsaamii, commonly known as the giant arrowhead, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It features large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 3 meters in length, often with a striking variegation of green and white. The plant produces a spadix inflorescence surrounded by a showy, petal-like spathe, typical of the Araceae family. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and requires high humidity and well-draining soil.

Its unique morphology makes it a popular choice for indoor cultivation and botanical gardens.
