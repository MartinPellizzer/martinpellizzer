Abutilon umbellatum

Abutilon umbellatum, commonly known as the velvetleaf or Chinese lantern plant, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and showy, lantern-shaped yellow-orange flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is a shrub or small tree, typically reaching heights of up to 3 meters. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Its foliage is soft and fuzzy, giving it a distinctive, velvety texture.
