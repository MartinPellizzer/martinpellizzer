Anthemis cretica

Anthemis cretica, commonly known as Crete tansy, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its yellow-orange composite flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is often found in coastal areas. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are erect and branching.

Anthemis cretica is valued for its aromatic properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
