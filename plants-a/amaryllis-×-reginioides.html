Amaryllis × reginioides

Amaryllis × reginioides, commonly known as the hybrid amaryllis, is a striking plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its large, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, pink, and white. The plant typically grows as a bulbous perennial, with strap-like leaves that emerge from the base of the plant.

Its blooming period usually occurs in the spring or early summer, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
