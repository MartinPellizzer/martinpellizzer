Annona klainei var. moandensis

Annona klainei var. moandensis, commonly known as the Moanda annona, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central Africa.

It is a small to medium-sized tree or shrub, typically reaching heights of up to 10 meters. The plant produces large, heart-shaped leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. Its flowers are white or pale green, and it bears yellow or orange fruits that are edible when ripe.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional uses in local medicine.
