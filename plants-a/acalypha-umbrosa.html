Acalypha umbrosa

Acalypha umbrosa, commonly known as the shadow plant, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, palmate leaves that are often dark green with a velvety texture. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically green or yellowish in color. It thrives in shady, moist environments and is often used in ornamental gardening.

Acalypha umbrosa is also valued for its ability to purify air and its use in traditional medicine.
