Anthurium oblongifolium

Anthurium oblongifolium, commonly known as the broad-leaf anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It features large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy surface. The plant produces striking spadices, which are the characteristic inflorescences of the Araceae family. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Anthurium oblongifolium is popular as a decorative plant due to its ornamental foliage and unique flowering structure.
