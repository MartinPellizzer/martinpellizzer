Argyranthemum sundingii

Argyranthemum sundingii, commonly known as the Swedish marguerite, is a flowering plant native to southern Scandinavia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers with white petals and a yellow center. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters and prefers well-drained soil and full sun. Its blooms appear in late summer to early autumn, adding a delicate touch to gardens.

Argyranthemum sundingii is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its attractive appearance and hardy nature.
