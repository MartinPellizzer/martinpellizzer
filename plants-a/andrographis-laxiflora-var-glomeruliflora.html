Andrographis laxiflora var. glomeruliflora

Andrographis laxiflora var. glomeruliflora, commonly known as the clustered-flower andrographis, is a variant of the andrographis plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and clusters of small, opposite leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in dense clusters, which are typical of the Andrographis genus. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

This variant is valued for its medicinal properties, similar to other members of the Andrographis family.
