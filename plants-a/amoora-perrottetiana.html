Amoora perrottetiana

Amoora perrottetiana, commonly known as the white sylhet maple, is a deciduous tree native to the northeastern regions of India.

It belongs to the Sapindaceae family and is characterized by its striking white to pale pink flowers that appear in clusters. The tree is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated for its timber.
