Alyssum gymnopodum

Alyssum gymnopodum, commonly known as the smoothleaf alyssum, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with dense clusters of small, fragrant white or pale yellow flowers. The plant features slender, hairy stems and narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Alyssum gymnopodum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract beneficial insects.
