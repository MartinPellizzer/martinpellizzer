Abronia fragrans var. elliptica

Abronia fragrans var. elliptica, commonly known as the fragrant abronia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its aromatic, tubular flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant features succulent, fleshy leaves that help it store water, adapting to its often arid habitat. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its unique appearance and pleasant fragrance make it a desirable addition to both gardens and botanical collections.
