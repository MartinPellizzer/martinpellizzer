Adelocaryum Malabaricum

Malabar caper, scientifically known as Adelocaryum malabaricum, is a unique and resilient plant native to the Western Ghats of India. This evergreen shrub is prized for its ornamental value and medicinal properties, making it a popular choice in both horticulture and traditional medicine. It features small, waxy leaves and clusters of yellow flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant is well adapted to rocky and dry environments, thriving in poor soil conditions. Due to its hardiness and aesthetic appeal, it is increasingly cultivated in gardens and botanical collections worldwide.
