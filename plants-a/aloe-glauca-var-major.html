Aloe glauca var. major

Aloe glauca var. major, commonly known as the giant aloe or Cape aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, fleshy, branching stems that can grow up to several meters in height. The plant produces large, showy, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in summer. Its leaves are thick and fleshy, adapted to store water in arid environments.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional medicinal uses.
