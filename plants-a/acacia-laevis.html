Acacia laevis

Acacia laevis, commonly known as the smooth wattle, is a shrub or small tree native to Australia.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is drought-tolerant, making it suitable for arid and semi-arid regions.

Acacia laevis is also valued for its role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for native wildlife.
