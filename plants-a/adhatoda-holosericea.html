Adhatoda holosericea

Adhatoda holosericea, commonly known as the Indian skullcap, is a flowering plant native to India and parts of Southeast Asia.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy surface, and its flowers are small, tubular, and usually pink or purple in color. It produces small, round fruits that contain several seeds.

The plant is often found in moist, well-drained soils in forested areas or along stream banks.
