Aspidistra viridiflora

Aspidistra viridiflora, commonly known as the green-flowered aspidistra, is a shade-tolerant evergreen perennial native to southern China.

It features dark, leathery, elliptical leaves that are glossy and typically dark green in color. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers with greenish veins, which bloom in spring. It is valued for its ornamental foliage and ability to thrive in low-light conditions.

Aspidistra viridiflora is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its resilience and aesthetic appeal.
