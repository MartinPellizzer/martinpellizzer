Achyropsis greenwayi

Achyropsis greenwayi, commonly known as the Greenway's achyrospermum, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Amaranthaceae and is characterized by its succulent, fleshy stems and leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow in color. It is adapted to survive in dry conditions, storing water in its thick, fleshy tissues.
