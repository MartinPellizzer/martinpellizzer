Allium obscurum

Allium obscurum, commonly known as purple onion, is a perennial herb native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Allium genus, which includes many edible and ornamental plants. The plant features basal leaves that are narrow and grass-like, with a purple tinge along the edges. Its flowers are clustered in umbels and are typically pale purple to white in color.

It is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential culinary uses.
