Aloe attenuata var. clariperla

Aloe attenuata var. clariperla, commonly known as the narrow-leaved aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features slender, upright stems and linear, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces clusters of yellowish-green flowers during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Its leaves are often used for their gel, which has various medicinal properties.
