Anacamptis × albuferensis

Anacamptis × albuferensis, commonly known as the Albufera orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to southern Europe, particularly Spain.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its tall, erect stems and clusters of fragrant, violet-blue flowers. The plant typically grows in calcareous soils and thrives in sunny, open habitats. Its blooming period occurs during the spring and early summer, adding vibrant color to its natural environment.

Anacamptis × albuferensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
