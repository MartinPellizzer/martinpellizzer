Andromeda polifolia var. angustifolia

Andromeda polifolia var. angustifolia, commonly known as narrowleaf wintergreen, is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to the temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

It features dense clusters of small, dark red to purple flowers that bloom in late winter or early spring. The leaves are elliptical, leathery, and dark green, often with a waxy coating. This plant thrives in acidic, moist soils and is often found in shaded forest understories.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
