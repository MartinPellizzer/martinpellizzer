Anemone anemonoides subsp. tenuis

Anemone anemonoides subsp.

tenuis, commonly known as the slender anemonoid anemone, is a herbaceous perennial native to parts of East Asia. It features delicate, showy flowers with five to nine white or pink petals, typically blooming in early spring.

The plant has finely divided, fern-like leaves that are often covered in a soft, downy texture. It prefers moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable addition to native plant collections and pollinator habitats.
