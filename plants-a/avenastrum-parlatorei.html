Avenastrum parlatorei

Avenastrum parlatorei, commonly known as the Parlatore's wormwood, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its slender, branched stems and small, yellowish flowers. The plant produces small, oblong seeds that are dispersed by wind, aiding in its spread across rocky and arid habitats. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, sunny environments.

Avenastrum parlatorei is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine for its potential health benefits.
