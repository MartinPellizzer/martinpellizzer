Anemone coelestina f. holophylla

Anemone coelestina f. holophylla, commonly known as the blue anemone, is a flowering plant belonging to the Ranunculaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet petals that resemble the petals of true anemones. The plant typically grows in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its foliage consists of trifoliate leaves with serrated edges.

The flowers bloom in early spring, adding a vibrant splash of color to its natural habitat.
