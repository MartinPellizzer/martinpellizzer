Agave ixtlioides

Agave ixtlioides, commonly known as the Mexican blue agave, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a succulent plant characterized by its rosette-shaped growth pattern and thick, waxy leaves. The leaves are typically blue-gray in color, with sharp, spiny margins. It produces a tall flower stalk that bears small, greenish-white flowers.

This plant is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
