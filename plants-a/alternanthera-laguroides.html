Alternanthera laguroides

Alternanthera laguroides, commonly known as red-stemmed alternanthera, is a herbaceous plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking red stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often variegated with green and white. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, typically green or yellow, and it is often grown for its ornamental foliage rather than its blooms.

It is a popular choice for water gardens and bog landscapes due to its vibrant color and adaptability.
