Alstonia vietnamensis

Alstonia vietnamensis, commonly known as Vietnamese alstonia, is a deciduous tree native to Southeast Asia, particularly Vietnam.

It belongs to the family Apocynaceae and is characterized by its smooth, gray bark and opposite, elliptical leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers followed by woody, capsule-like fruits containing numerous seeds.

Alstonia vietnamensis is often found in lowland evergreen forests and is valued for its ornamental qualities and traditional medicinal uses.
