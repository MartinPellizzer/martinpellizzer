Aster milanjiensis

Aster milanjiensis, commonly known as the Milanji aster, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of Ethiopia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in cool, temperate climates. Its flowers are usually blue to violet in color, with ray florets surrounding a central disk.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
