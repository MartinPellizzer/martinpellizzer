Anthurium obpyriforme

Anthurium obpyriforme, commonly known as the heartleaf anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its glossy, heart-shaped leaves that are typically deep green with a reddish or purple tint on the underside. The plant produces striking spadices, which are specialized inflorescences that resemble a spike of flowers. Anthurium obpyriforme thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.

Its unique foliage and ornamental value make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
