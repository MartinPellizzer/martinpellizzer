Argyroderma testiculare var. roseum

Argyroderma testiculare var. roseum, commonly known as the rose-flowered argyroderma, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, rounded rosettes of fleshy, silver-green leaves that are often adorned with a pink or rose-colored bloom. The plant produces showy, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink to red, which appear in spring. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in rocky, well-drained soils.
