Apocynum × viarum

Apocynum × viarum, commonly known as the hybrid dogbane, is a flowering plant belonging to the Apocynaceae family.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows in moist, open habitats such as wetlands and marshes. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are usually pink or red, and it has milky, latex-filled stems and leaves. Its seeds are enclosed in follicles that split open to release the seeds.

Apocynum × viarum is often found in the eastern United States and is considered an invasive species in some areas.
