Albizia multiflora var. sagasteguii

Albizia multiflora var. sagasteguii, commonly known as the Guanacaste tree, is a flowering plant native to Central America.

It is a deciduous tree that can grow up to 20 meters tall, featuring a broad, spreading canopy. The plant produces fragrant, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in clusters, typically in the spring. Its leaves are bipinnate, consisting of numerous small, oval-shaped leaflets.

This species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often used in landscaping and reforestation projects.
