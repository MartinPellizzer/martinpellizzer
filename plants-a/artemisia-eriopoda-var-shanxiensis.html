Artemisia eriopoda var. shanxiensis

Artemisia eriopoda var. shanxiensis, commonly known as Shanxi wormwood, is a perennial herb native to China.

It is characterized by its aromatic foliage and grayish-green leaves covered in fine, white hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers in clustered inflorescences, typical of the Asteraceae family. It thrives in dry, rocky, or sandy soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its medicinal properties, it has been used in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.
