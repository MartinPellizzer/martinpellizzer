Aster macrorrhizus

Aster macrorrhizus, commonly known as the large-rooted aster, is a perennial herb native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and serrated leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands. Its showy blue to purple flowers bloom in the late summer and early fall.

The roots are thick and fleshy, which help the plant store water and nutrients.
