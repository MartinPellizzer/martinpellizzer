Aster kumleini

Aster kumleini, commonly known as the Kummelini aster, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies, sunflowers, and other composite flowers. The plant features showy, purple to violet flowers that bloom in late summer and early autumn. It typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils at high elevations.

Aster kumleini is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
