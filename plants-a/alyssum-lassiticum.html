Alyssum lassiticum

Alyssum lassiticum, commonly known as the rock Alyssum, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in spring. The plant has succulent, gray-green leaves that are arranged in a dense, mat-like formation. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Alyssum lassiticum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
