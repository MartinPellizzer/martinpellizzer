Aegilops aucheri var. schultzii

Aegilops aucheri var. schultzii, commonly known as the Schultze's wild wheat, is a perennial grass native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the tribe Aegilop tribe within the grass family Poaceae. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences with pale green to brown florets. It is often found in rocky or gravelly soils and is adapted to dry, temperate climates.
