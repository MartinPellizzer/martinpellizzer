Anathallis margaritifera

Anathallis margaritifera, commonly known as the pearl orchid, is a flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking white or pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its inflorescence consists of a single spike with small, delicate flowers that resemble pearls.

Anathallis margaritifera is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its unique floral display.
