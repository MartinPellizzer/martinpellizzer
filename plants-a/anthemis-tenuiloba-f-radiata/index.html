Anthemis Tenuiloba F. Radiata

Radiate anthemis, scientifically known as Anthemis tenuiloba f. radiata, is a strikingly beautiful flowering plant native to the rocky regions of southern Europe and the Mediterranean. Its most distinctive feature is its vibrant yellow, daisy-like flowers that radiate outward in a sun-like pattern, giving it its common name. This plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes. With its delicate yet resilient nature, Radiate anthemis has become a favorite among gardeners seeking both aesthetic appeal and low maintenance. Its unique appearance and hardy characteristics make it a standout addition to any botanical collection.
