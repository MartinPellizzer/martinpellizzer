Albizia multiflora

Albizia multiflora, commonly known as the Chinese Albizia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to East Asia.

It features compound leaves with numerous small, oval leaflets that give it a feathery appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant, pale yellow flowers arranged in racemes, which bloom in the spring. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine.

Albizia multiflora prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.
