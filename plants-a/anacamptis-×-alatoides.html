Anacamptis × alatoides

Anacamptis × alatoides, commonly known as the hybrid green-winged orchid, is a fascinating plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is a natural hybrid between Anacamptis morio and Anacamptis pyramidalis, resulting in unique floral characteristics. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and woodland edges, preferring well-drained soils. Its flowers are usually greenish-yellow with distinctive dark markings, and it blooms from late spring to early summer.

This orchid is notable for its adaptability and is often found in regions across Europe.
