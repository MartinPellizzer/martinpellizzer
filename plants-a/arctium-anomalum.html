Arctium anomalum

Arctium anomalum, commonly known as the strange burdock, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and thistles, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Arctium anomalum is often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides, thriving in temperate climates.
