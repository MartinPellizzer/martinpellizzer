Asplenium × garhwalense nothosubsp. kumaonicum

Asplenium × garhwalense nothosubsp.

kumaonicum, commonly known as the Kumaon spleenwort, is a hybrid fern species native to the Himalayan region. It belongs to the family Aspleniaceae and is characterized by its compact, evergreen foliage.

The plant typically grows on rocks or tree trunks in moist, shaded environments. Its fronds are usually dark green and have a leathery texture.

This subspecies is of interest to botanists due to its unique hybrid origin and limited distribution.
