Amesia

Amesia, commonly known as frostweed, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, daisy-like flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. These plants are typically found in moist, open habitats such as meadows and stream banks. Amesia species are often used in ornamental gardening due to their attractive foliage and blooms.

They are also valued for their ability to thrive in cooler climates and their role in supporting pollinators.
