Aichryson dichotomum var. villosum

Aichryson dichotomum var. villosum, commonly known as the Woolly Aichryson, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features dense, woolly gray-green foliage that is covered in fine, soft hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, star-shaped yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its compact growth habit makes it an ideal choice for rock gardens or as a container plant.
