Allium × pseudonutans

Allium × pseudonutans, commonly known as the giant garlic or elephant garlic, is a hybrid plant in the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, bulbous stems and long, narrow leaves. The plant produces clusters of white, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. Native to Central Asia, it has been cultivated for its pungent, edible bulbs.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for both culinary use and ornamental gardening.
