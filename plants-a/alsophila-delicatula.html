Alsophila delicatula

Alsophila delicatula, commonly known as the delicate tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, gracefully tapering trunk and feathery, pinnate fronds that give it a distinctive, elegant appearance. The plant belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is known for its ability to thrive in humid, shaded environments. Its fronds are typically bright green and can grow up to several meters in length.

Alsophila delicatula is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and air-purifying qualities.
