Arthrochilium giganteum

Arthrochilium giganteum, commonly known as the giant rock orchid, is a striking epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can reach up to 15 centimeters in length, featuring vivid purple and white hues. The plant typically grows on tree trunks or rocky surfaces, thriving in humid tropical environments. Its leaves are broad and succulent, adapted to store moisture in its native habitat.

Arthrochilium giganteum is notable for its unique floral structure and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
