Albizia arunachalensis

Albizia arunachalensis, commonly known as the Arunachal Albizia, is a flowering plant native to the eastern Himalayas, particularly in Arunachal Pradesh.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that typically grows in moist, well-drained soils. The plant produces fragrant, fluffy pods that contain seeds, and its leaves are bipinnate, consisting of numerous small leaflets. Albizia arunachalensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.

Its ecological role includes providing habitat and food for various wildlife species in its native range.
