Asplenium × vinaniteloense

Asplenium × vinaniteloense, commonly known as the hybrid shield fern, is a naturally occurring hybrid between *Asplenium bulbiferum* and *Asplenium trichomanes*.

It is characterized by its dense, leathery fronds that are typically dark green with a glossy appearance. The plant produces small, spore-bearing structures called sori on the underside of its leaves. It thrives in shaded, moist environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its unique morphology and hybrid origin make it an interesting subject in botanical studies.
