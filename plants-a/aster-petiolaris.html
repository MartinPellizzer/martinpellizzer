Aster petiolaris

Aster petiolaris, commonly known as the slender aster, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, while its flowers are small and clustered in a rounded head.

Aster petiolaris is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits to pollinators.
