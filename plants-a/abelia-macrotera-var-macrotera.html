Abelia macrotera var. macrotera

Abelia macrotera var. macrotera, commonly known as the largeflower abelia, is a shrub native to eastern Asia, particularly China.

It features showy, tubular flowers that are typically pink or red, blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained soil.

This variety is valued for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
