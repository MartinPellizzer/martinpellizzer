Alcantarea vinicolor

Alcantarea vinicolor, commonly known as the purple crown cactus, is a species native to Brazil.

It is a member of the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its tall, columnar stems and vibrant purple flowers. The plant features spiny, ribbed stems that help it store water in arid environments. Its flowers, which bloom in shades of purple and pink, are typically produced at the top of the plant.

Alcantarea vinicolor is well adapted to tropical and subtropical climates, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
