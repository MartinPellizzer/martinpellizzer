Astronium fraxinifolium f. subglabrum

Astronium fraxinifolium f. subglabrum, commonly known as the smooth-leaved star apple, is a variant of the star apple tree native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its smooth, glabrous leaves that are opposite and ovate in shape. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by edible, star-shaped fruits that are rich in nutrients. The bark and leaves have been used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

Its botanical features make it an important species in ecological and horticultural contexts.
