Asparagus densiflorus

Asparagus densiflorus, commonly known as the asparagus fern, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It features feathery, delicate foliage that resembles ferns, though it is not a true fern. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white or greenish flowers that appear in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers bright, indirect light.

Asparagus densiflorus is often used as a decorative plant due to its attractive foliage and low maintenance requirements.
