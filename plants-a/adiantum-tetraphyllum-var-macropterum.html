Adiantum tetraphyllum var. macropterum

Adiantum tetraphyllum var. macropterum, commonly known as the broad-winged maidenhair fern, is a species of fern characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds.

It belongs to the family Adiantaceae and is native to regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of India and China. The plant is distinguished by its broad, lance-shaped pinnae and the presence of fertile fronds with spore-bearing sorus structures. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and horticultural collections.
