Achimenes chirita

Achimenes chirita, commonly known as the flame violet, is a tropical perennial plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange and yellow. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped blooms that are often used in floral arrangements. Its foliage consists of heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are typically dark green.

Achimenes chirita thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
