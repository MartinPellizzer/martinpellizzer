Anthurium × ortgiesii

Anthurium × ortgiesii, commonly known as the velvet leaf anthurium, is a hybrid plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a velvety texture. The plant produces striking spadices, which are specialized inflorescences that are usually bright red or orange in color. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium × ortgiesii is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and long-lasting blooms.
