Anemone takasagomontana

Anemone takasagomontana, commonly known as the Japanese anemone, is a herbaceous perennial native to Japan.

It features showy, white to pink flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. The plant has finely divided, fern-like leaves that are typically arranged in a basal rosette. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens.

Its delicate appearance and seasonal blooming make it a valuable addition to native plant collections.
