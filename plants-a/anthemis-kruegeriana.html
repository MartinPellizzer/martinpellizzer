Anthemis kruegeriana

Anthemis kruegeriana, commonly known as the Krueger's feathercup, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and aromatic properties. The plant features upright, branched stems covered in fine, hairy foliage. Its flowers are yellow and daisy-like, typically blooming in late spring to early summer.

It is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments.
