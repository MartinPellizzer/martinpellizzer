Aclisia elegans

Aclisia elegans, commonly known as the elegant acanthus, is a striking plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its large, palmate leaves with intricate, spiny margins. The plant produces showy, violet-blue flowers in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its architectural form and long-lasting blooms.
