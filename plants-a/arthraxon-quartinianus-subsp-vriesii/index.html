Arthraxon Quartinianus Subsp. Vriesii

Arthraxon quartinianus subsp. vriesii is a lesser-known grass species native to parts of Southeast Asia. It belongs to the Poaceae family and is often found in wetland and marshy environments. This subspecies is distinguished by its unique leaf morphology and growth habits. Despite its ecological significance, it remains under-researched and poorly documented. Understanding its biology and habitat can contribute to broader conservation efforts in its native regions.
