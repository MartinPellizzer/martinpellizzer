Agathosma cerefolia var. glabrata

Agathosma cerefolia var. glabrata, commonly known as African geranium, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, aromatic leaves that are typically gray-green in color. The plant produces small, pink or purple flowers during the warmer months. It is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its compact size and attractive foliage.

Its drought tolerance makes it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
