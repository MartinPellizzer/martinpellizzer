Anthriscus nitida

Anthriscus nitida, commonly known as slender chervil, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe.

It features finely divided, fern-like leaves that are aromatic and slightly hairy. The plant produces small, white, umbellate flowers that bloom in early summer. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in woodland areas or along stream banks.

Its delicate appearance and mild flavor make it a sought-after herb in culinary applications.
