Abrotanum virgatum

Abrotanum virgatum, commonly known as Virgin's balm, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically reaches a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant features small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. Its leaves are narrow and covered in fine hairs, giving them a slightly fuzzy texture.

Abrotanum virgatum is often found in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to thrive in sunny, well-drained environments.
