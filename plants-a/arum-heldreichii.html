Arum heldreichii

Arum heldreichii, commonly known as the Heldreich's arrowleaf arum, is a flowering plant native to the Balkan Peninsula.

It is characterized by its distinctive arrow-shaped leaves and a spadix surrounded by a spathe. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered on the spadix. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodlands and mountainous regions.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
