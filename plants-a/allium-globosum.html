Allium globosum

Allium globosum, commonly known as the globe onion, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features a bulbous stem that produces clusters of small, white, bell-shaped flowers. The plant has broad, grass-like leaves and is typically found in rocky or sandy soils. It blooms in late spring to early summer and is often used in rock gardens due to its ornamental value.

Allium globosum is relatively low maintenance and thrives in full sun.
