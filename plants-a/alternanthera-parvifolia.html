Alternanthera parvifolia

Alternanthera parvifolia, commonly known as small-leaved Alternanthera, is a herbaceous plant native to South America.

It features strikingly colored leaves that can range from red to purple, depending on light conditions. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or white. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for aquatic and bog gardens.
