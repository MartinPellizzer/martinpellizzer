Alternanthera calcicola

Alternanthera calcicola, commonly known as the limestone alternanthera, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It is characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves and showy, pinkish-red bracts that resemble flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained, calcareous soils and is often found in rocky or limestone-rich environments.
