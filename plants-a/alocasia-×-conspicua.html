Alocasia × conspicua

Alocasia × conspicua, commonly known as the giant elephant ear, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Araceae family.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped, dark green leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length. The plant originates from the tropical regions of Southeast Asia and is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and indoor settings. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Alocasia × conspicua requires well-draining soil and regular watering to maintain its health and vigor.
