Albizia burkartiana

Albizia burkartiana, commonly known as the Burkart's Albizia, is a species of legume native to Mexico and parts of Central America.

It is characterized by its compound leaves, which are typically pinnately arranged and have a feathery appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually pink or white, arranged in racemose inflorescences. Its pods are elongated and contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

Albizia burkartiana is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, growing in a variety of habitats including forests and open areas.
