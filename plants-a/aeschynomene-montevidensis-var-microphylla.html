Aeschynomene montevidensis var. microphylla

Aeschynomene montevidensis var. microphylla, commonly known as small-leaf aeschynomene, is a perennial forage legume native to South America.

It is characterized by its small, narrow leaves and produces small, pale yellow flowers arranged in racemes. The plant is often found in grasslands and disturbed soils, thriving in warm climates. It has a deep root system that helps in soil stabilization and nitrogen fixation.

This variety is valued for its high nutritional value and adaptability to various environmental conditions.
