Allium orosamium

Allium orosamium, commonly known as the Japanese leek or mountain garlic, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

The plant produces bulb-like structures that are often used in traditional Asian cuisine. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often cultivated for both ornamental and culinary purposes.
