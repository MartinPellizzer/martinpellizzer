Aniba hirsuta

Aniba hirsuta, commonly known as the hairy aniba, is a small evergreen tree native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurel and avocado trees. The plant is characterized by its smooth, dark green leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. It produces small, fragrant flowers followed by elliptical, dark purple fruits.

Aniba hirsuta is valued for its aromatic properties and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
