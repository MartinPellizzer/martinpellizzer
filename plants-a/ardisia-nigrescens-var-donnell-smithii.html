Ardisia nigrescens var. donnell-smithii

Ardisia nigrescens var. donnell-smithii, commonly known as the Donnell-Smith ardisia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and produces small, white flowers that are followed by bright red berries. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

The varietal form is distinguished by its unique leaf morphology and slightly different growth habits compared to the typical species.
