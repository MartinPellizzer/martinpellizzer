Alternanthera strictiuscula

Alternanthera strictiuscula, commonly known as the compact Alternanthera, is a herbaceous perennial native to South America.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and vibrant, colorful foliage that often displays shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow, which appear in clusters at the leaf axils. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This ornamental plant is popular in aquatic and bog gardens due to its striking appearance and adaptability.
