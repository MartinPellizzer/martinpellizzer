Adromischus robustus

Adromischus robustus, commonly known as the rock rose or stone plant, is a succulent species native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with red or purple undertones, arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink, white, or red during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it ideal for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its compact size and ornamental appearance make it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.
