Aizopsis rhodocarpa

Aizopsis rhodocarpa, commonly known as the redwood lily, is a striking evergreen shrub native to the eastern coast of South Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of vibrant red flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has lance-shaped, glossy green leaves that provide a lush backdrop to its showy inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and requires partial to full sunlight for optimal growth.

Aizopsis rhodocarpa is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to add color to coastal gardens.
