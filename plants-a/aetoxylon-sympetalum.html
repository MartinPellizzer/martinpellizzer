Aetoxylon sympetalum

Aetoxylon sympetalum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved sandalwood, is a unique plant species native to the rainforests of northeastern Queensland, Australia.

The plant produces woody fruits that contain smooth, glossy seeds, which are dispersed by wind. Aetoxylon sympetalum is adapted to humid, subtropical climates and thrives in well-drained, acidic soils.
