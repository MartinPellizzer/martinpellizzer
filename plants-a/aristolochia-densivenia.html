Aristolochia densivenia

Aristolochia densivenia, commonly known as the dense venation arrowhead, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves with prominent venation patterns and distinctive, fragrant, pipe-shaped flowers. The plant belongs to the Aristolochiaceae family, known for its complex and often deceptive floral structures. It thrives in humid, tropical environments and is often found in forest understories.

The species is also noted for its medicinal properties, though its use is limited due to potential toxicity.
