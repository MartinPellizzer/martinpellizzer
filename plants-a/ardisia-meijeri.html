Ardisia meijeri

Ardisia meijeri, commonly known as the Christmasberry, is a compact evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are elliptical in shape and emit a mild fragrance when crushed. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers followed by bright red berries that resemble Christmas ornaments, hence its common name. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained, moist soil.

Ardisia meijeri is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and seasonal berries.
