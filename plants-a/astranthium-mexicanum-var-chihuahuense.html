Astranthium mexicanum var. chihuahuense

Astranthium mexicanum var. chihuahuense, commonly known as the Chihuahua aster, is a flowering plant native to the Chihuahua region of Mexico.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its showy, purple to pink ray flowers and green, lance-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments, often in rocky soils. It produces small, cylindrical seed heads that are adapted for wind dispersal.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
