Aloe doddsiorum

Aloe doddsiorum, commonly known as the Cape aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces striking red or orange flowers during the summer months. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their healing properties.

Aloe doddsiorum is well-suited for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its low water requirements.
