Aster pekinensis

Aster pekinensis, commonly known as the Chinese aster, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically features showy, pink or purple flower clusters that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its ornamental value has led to its cultivation in various parts of the world.
