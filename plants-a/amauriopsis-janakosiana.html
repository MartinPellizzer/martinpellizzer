Amauriopsis janakosiana

Amauriopsis janakosiana, commonly known as the Janakosia orchid, is a species of flowering plant native to the Andean regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, showy flowers. The plant typically produces small, fragrant blooms in shades of white and purple, often with intricate patterns on the petals. It thrives in cool, humid environments and is often found in cloud forests at high elevations.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
