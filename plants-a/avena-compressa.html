Avena compressa

Avena compressa, commonly known as slender oat, is a perennial grass species native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and stream banks. The plant features narrow, compressed leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. Its seeds are often used in animal feed and have historical uses in traditional medicine.

Botanically, it is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit and ability to form dense stands.
