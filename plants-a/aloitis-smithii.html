Aloitis smithii

Aloitis smithii, commonly known as Smith's aloes, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aloe family (Xanthorrhoeaceae) and is characterized by its fleshy, spiny leaves that store water. The plant produces striking yellow-orange flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens or arid landscapes.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for succulent enthusiasts.
