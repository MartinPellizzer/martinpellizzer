Aniba excelsa

Aniba excelsa, commonly known as the Brazilian rosewood, is a tropical evergreen tree native to South America.

It is characterized by its dense, dark-colored wood, which is highly valued in the production of musical instruments. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are usually glossy and dark green in color. It produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow.

Aniba excelsa is also noted for its aromatic properties and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
