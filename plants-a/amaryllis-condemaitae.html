Amaryllis condemaitae

Amaryllis condemaitae, commonly known as the Christmas Amaryllis, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It produces large, showy flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, typically blooming during the winter months. The plant features strap-like, dark green leaves that are broad and sturdy. Its bulbous stem is thick and fleshy, storing nutrients for flowering.

Amaryllis condemaitae is popular as a ornamental plant due to its vibrant blooms and ease of cultivation.
