Aloe mangeaensis

Aloe mangeaensis, commonly known as the Cape aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with vibrant yellow flowers, typically blooming in late spring. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their soothing properties.

Aloe mangeaensis is well adapted to full sun and requires minimal water, making it a popular choice for xeriscaping.
