Armeria malinvaudii

Armeria malinvaudii, commonly known as the Malinvaud's thrift, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically reaches heights of about 10 to 20 centimeters. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pinkish flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are linear and grass-like, often covered in a fine layer of white hairs.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
