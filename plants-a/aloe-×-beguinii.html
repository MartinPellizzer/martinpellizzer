Aloe × beguinii

Aloe × beguinii, commonly known as the hybrid aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aloe genus and is a hybrid between Aloe aristata and Aloe barberae. The plant features rosettes of fleshy, pointed leaves that are typically green with white or yellow markings. It produces tall, branched inflorescences with clusters of yellow flowers.

Aloe × beguinii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in arid environments.
