Agathosma bicornuta

Agathosma bicornuta, commonly known as the Cape daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant has aromatic leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
