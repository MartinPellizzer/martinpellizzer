Anthurium retusum

Anthurium retusum, commonly known as the curly anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves with a waxy texture. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-like inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, which is often used in ornamental horticulture. It thrives in humid, warm environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.

Anthurium retusum is relatively low-maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
