Aneilema setiferum

Aneilema setiferum, commonly known as the velvetleaf or African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves and clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to purple. The plant thrives in humid, warm environments and prefers well-drained soil. It is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its attractive foliage and blooms.

Aneilema setiferum is also valued for its ornamental use in gardens and as a houseplant.
