Anemone narcissiflora subsp. chinensis

Anemone narcissiflora subsp.

chinensis, commonly known as the Chinese anemone, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to eastern Asia. It features showy, cup-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, often with a yellow center.

The plant has basal leaves that are palmately lobed and hairy, and it thrives in moist, shady environments. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

The subspecies is closely related to other anemone species, sharing similar botanical characteristics.
