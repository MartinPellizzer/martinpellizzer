Allium carinatum var. carneum

Allium carinatum var. carneum, commonly known as the red onion, is a bulbous plant belonging to the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is characterized by its elongated, fleshy bulbs that are typically red or pink in color. The plant produces tall, slender stems with narrow, grass-like leaves. Its flowers are clustered in umbels and are usually purple or white in color.

This variety is valued for its culinary use and ornamental appeal in gardens.
