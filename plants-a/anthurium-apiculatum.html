Anthurium apiculatum

Anthurium apiculatum, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces vibrant red spadices, which are the colorful, spadix-like structures that house the actual flowers. It thrives in humid, warm environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium apiculatum is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
