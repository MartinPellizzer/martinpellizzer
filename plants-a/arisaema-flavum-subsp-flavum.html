Arisaema flavum subsp. flavum

Arisaema flavum subsp.

flavum, commonly known as the yellow arrowhead, is a herbaceous plant native to eastern Asia. It features a distinctive, arrow-shaped leaf that is typically bright green with a yellowish tinge.

The plant produces a spadix surrounded by a spathe, which is often yellow or orange in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional medicinal uses in its native regions.
