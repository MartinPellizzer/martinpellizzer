Acianthera ochreata subsp. ochreata

Acianthera ochreata subsp.

ochreata, commonly known as the ochre orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to South America. It is a small, terrestrial orchid that typically grows in humid, shaded environments.

The plant features a slender, erect stem with several lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are yellowish-orange, with a distinctive labellum that is often adorned with a yellowish or reddish stripe.

This subspecies is noted for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
