Anthurium eichleri

Anthurium eichleri, commonly known as the Eichleri anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the Andes region of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are often brightly colored, ranging from deep green to reddish hues. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-shaped inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, which is a hallmark feature of the Anthurium genus. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in tropical gardens.
