Aspalathus squamosa

Aspalathus squamosa, commonly known as the silver bush, is a small, evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, silver-gray foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.

Aspalathus squamosa is valued for its ornamental qualities and is also used in traditional medicine.
