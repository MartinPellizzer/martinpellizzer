Asparagus royleanus

Asparagus royleanus, commonly known as the Kashmir asparagus, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its slender, branched stems and small, scale-like leaves. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Asparagus royleanus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its delicate foliage and floral display.
