Ardisia semicrenata var. angustissima

Ardisia semicrenata var. angustissima, commonly known as the narrow-leaf evergreen ardisia, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are narrow and lance-shaped, giving it a distinct appearance. The plant produces small, white flowers followed by red, fleshy berries that are attractive to birds. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical forests.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for horticultural use in shaded garden settings.
