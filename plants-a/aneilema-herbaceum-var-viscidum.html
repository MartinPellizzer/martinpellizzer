Aneilema herbaceum var. viscidum

Aneilema herbaceum var. viscidum, commonly known as the sticky aneilema, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features upright, slender stems that are often covered in a sticky, glandular coating. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink, with showy bracts that enhance its ornamental appeal. Its leaves are usually elliptical or ovate, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

This species is valued in horticulture for its unique appearance and ability to thrive in humid, shaded environments.
