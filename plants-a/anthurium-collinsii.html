Anthurium collinsii

Anthurium collinsii, commonly known as the red-tipped anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are usually dark green with a deep red or maroon base. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-like inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, which can range from white to red. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Anthurium collinsii is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
