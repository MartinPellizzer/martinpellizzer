Achoriphragma minutum

Achoriphragma minutum, commonly known as the small-flowered sandmat, is a low-growing, mat-forming perennial plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of North America.

It features small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color, and it has narrow, fleshy leaves adapted to conserve water. The plant thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in desert environments. It plays a role in stabilizing dunes and preventing soil erosion.

Achoriphragma minutum is also valued for its ornamental appeal in xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and unique appearance.
