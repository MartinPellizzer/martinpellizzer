Astragalus Bilobatoalatus

Astragalus bilobatoalatus is a rare and distinctive member of the legume family known for its unique floral structure. Native to the arid regions of western North America, this plant thrives in harsh desert environments. Its two-lobed petals and intricate floral morphology make it a subject of botanical interest. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this rare botanical gem.
