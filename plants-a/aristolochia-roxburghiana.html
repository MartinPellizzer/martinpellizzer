Aristolochia roxburghiana

Aristolochia roxburghiana, commonly known as the Indian birthwort, is a climbing vine native to South and Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, tubular, maroon-colored flowers that resemble a horn or a pipe. The plant has heart-shaped, dark green leaves and is often found in moist, shaded environments. It belongs to the Aristolochiaceae family and is known for its aromatic foliage.

However, it contains toxic compounds and should be handled with care.
