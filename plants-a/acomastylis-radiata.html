Acomastylis radiata

Acomastylis radiata, commonly known as the radiate acomastylis, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features slender, hairy stems and produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in a radiate pattern. Its leaves are typically linear and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Acomastylis radiata is often found in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun conditions.
