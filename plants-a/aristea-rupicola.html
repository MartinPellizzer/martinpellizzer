Aristea rupicola

Aristea rupicola, commonly known as the rock aristea, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Xanthorrhoeaceae and is characterized by its compact, cushion-like growth habit. The plant features dense clusters of narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color. It produces small, tubular flowers in shades of yellow and orange, which bloom in summer.

Aristea rupicola is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
