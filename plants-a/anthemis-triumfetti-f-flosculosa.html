Anthemis triumfetti f. flosculosa

Anthemis triumfetti f. flosculosa, commonly known as the lesser tansy, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its small, yellowish to pale orange composite flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as meadows and rocky soils. Its leaves are narrow and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

It is often found in temperate regions and is valued for its historical use in herbal medicine.
