Anthurium lanceolatum

Anthurium lanceolatum, commonly known as the lanceleaf anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its elongated, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces striking spadices, which are modified leaves that house the actual flowers, often in vibrant red or orange hues. Anthurium lanceolatum thrives in humid, warm environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Its unique foliage and colorful inflorescences make it a popular choice for indoor gardening and ornamental purposes.
