Acanthus dioscoridis var. strausii

Acanthus dioscoridis var. strausii, commonly known as the Straus's spiny acanthus, is a rare and ornamental plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, spiny leaves and showy, violet-blue bracts that resemble flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its unique foliage makes it a sought-after specimen in botanical gardens and specialized collections.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native range.
