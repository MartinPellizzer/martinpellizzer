Alchemilla triangularis

Alchemilla triangularis, commonly known as the triangular lady's mantle, is a perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive triangular-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Alchemilla triangularis is valued in gardens for its ornamental foliage and ability to attract beneficial insects.
