Adonis vernalis var. volgensis

Adonis vernalis var. volgensis, commonly known as the spring adonis, is a flowering plant belonging to the Ranunculaceae family.

It is characterized by its showy, yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in woodland or meadow habitats. It has basal leaves that are palmately lobed and a short, stout stem.

This variety is known for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its early blooming habit.
