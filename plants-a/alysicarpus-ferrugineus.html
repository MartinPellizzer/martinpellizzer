Alysicarpus ferrugineus

Alysicarpus ferrugineus, commonly known as the red sand mat, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, also known as the legume or pea family, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers followed by distinctive, inflated pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in sandy or well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas or disturbed habitats.

Its growth habit is low and sprawling, making it a common weed in agricultural fields.
