Aeschynomene oligantha

Aeschynomene oligantha, commonly known as smallleaf aeschynomene, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, pea-like flowers. The plant is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
