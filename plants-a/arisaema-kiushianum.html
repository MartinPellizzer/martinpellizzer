Arisaema kiushianum

Arisaema kiushianum, commonly known as the Kiushian arrowhead, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its unique leaf structure, which resembles an arrowhead. The plant produces a spadix inflorescence surrounded by a spathe, typical of many arum species. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation in suitable climates.
