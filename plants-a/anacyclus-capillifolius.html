Anacyclus capillifolius

Anacyclus capillifolius, commonly known as the slenderleaf dandelion, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a herbaceous perennial with a rosette of basal leaves and a single stem that bears yellow flowers. The plant has finely divided, hair-like leaves that give it its common name. It produces small, wind-dispersed seeds, typical of the Asteraceae family.

Anacyclus capillifolius is often found in dry, disturbed soils and is adapted to arid environments.
