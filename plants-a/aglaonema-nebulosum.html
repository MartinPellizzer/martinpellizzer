Aglaonema nebulosum

Aglaonema nebulosum, commonly known as the Cloudy Spotted Philodendron, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features vibrant, waxy leaves with a distinctive mottled pattern of silver and green, giving it its common name. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening. Its foliage is often used in ornamental horticulture due to its aesthetic appeal.

Aglaonema nebulosum is relatively low maintenance, requiring moderate watering and well-draining soil.
