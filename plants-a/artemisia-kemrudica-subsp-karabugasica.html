Artemisia kemrudica subsp. karabugasica

Artemisia kemrudica subsp.

karabugasica, commonly known as the Karabugaz Artemisia, is a perennial shrub native to Turkey. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and aromatic leaves.

The plant typically grows in rocky or dry habitats, showing adaptability to arid conditions. It produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in composite heads, typical of the family.

This subspecies is of interest for its potential medicinal properties and ecological role in its native environment.
