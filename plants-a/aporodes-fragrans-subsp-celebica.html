Aporodes fragrans subsp. celebica

Aporodes fragrans subsp.

celebica, commonly known as the Celebes fragrant orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family. It is native to the Indonesian island of Sulawesi, where it thrives in tropical rainforest environments.

The plant is characterized by its slender, erect stems and showy, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and arranged in a spiral pattern around the stem.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its unique fragrance and aesthetic beauty.
