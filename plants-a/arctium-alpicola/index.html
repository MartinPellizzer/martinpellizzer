Arctium Alpicola

Arctium alpicola is a rare and elusive plant native to the alpine regions of Europe. It thrives in rocky and high-altitude environments where few species can survive. Known for its distinctive purple flowers and spiky seed heads, it is both beautiful and hardy. This plant plays an important role in its ecosystem, supporting pollinators and contributing to biodiversity. Its conservation is crucial due to habitat loss and climate change threats.
