Aichryson porphyrogennetos

Aichryson porphyrogennetos, commonly known as the Porphyrogonetos Aichryson, is a succulent plant native to the Canary Islands.

It features small, fleshy, spiky leaves that are typically green with a reddish or purplish hue. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months, which are clustered in inflorescences. It is well adapted to dry, rocky environments and requires minimal water.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
