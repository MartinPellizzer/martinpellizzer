Anthurium ligulare

Anthurium ligulare, commonly known as the narrow-leaf anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are often a deep green with a glossy texture. The plant produces vibrant spathes that can range in color from red to pink, depending on the variety. It thrives in humid, warm environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Anthurium ligulare is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and unique flowering structure.
