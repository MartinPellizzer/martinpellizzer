Asplenium brisbaneense var. fissiloba

Asplenium brisbaneense var. fissiloba, commonly known as the Brisbane spleenwort, is a rare and endemic fern species native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Aspleniaceae and is characterized by its compact, evergreen foliage. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as rock crevices or forest floors. Its fronds are usually dark green, with a leathery texture and a distinctive, narrow shape.
