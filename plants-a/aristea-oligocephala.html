Aristea oligocephala

Aristea oligocephala, commonly known as the narrow-headed aristea, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, daisy-like flowers. The plant produces small, tubular florets surrounded by a series of narrow, grass-like leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
