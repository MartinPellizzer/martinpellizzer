Aster lowrieanus

Aster lowrieanus, commonly known as the lowriean aster, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and woodland edges. It produces showy blue to purple flowers that bloom in late summer to early fall.

Aster lowrieanus is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, providing nectar for pollinators.
