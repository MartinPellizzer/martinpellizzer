Aloe socialis

Aloe socialis, commonly known as the social aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy leaves that are typically gray-green in color with white or yellow markings. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences that bear clusters of yellow flowers. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Aloe socialis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional medicinal uses.
