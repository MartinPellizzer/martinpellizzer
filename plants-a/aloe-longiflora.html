Aloe longiflora

Aloe longiflora, commonly known as the long-flowered aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a waxy coating, which helps it store water. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer. Its root system is fibrous and adapted to thrive in well-drained soils.

Aloe longiflora is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional medicinal uses.
