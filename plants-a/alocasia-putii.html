Alocasia putii

Alocasia putii, commonly known as the White Velvet Alocasia, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in a dense, waxy white bloom, giving it a velvety appearance. The plant thrives in humid environments with bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil. Its foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and as a statement plant in tropical landscapes.

Alocasia putii is also noted for its ability to purify the air, making it both ornamental and beneficial for indoor environments.
