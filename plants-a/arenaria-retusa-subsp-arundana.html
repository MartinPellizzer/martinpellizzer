Arenaria retusa subsp. arundana

Arenaria retusa subsp.

arundana, commonly known as the smooth sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to arctic and alpine regions. It typically forms dense mats of tiny, succulent leaves that are covered in a fine layer of white hairs.

The plant produces small, white or pinkish flowers in early summer, which are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained, rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to cold climates.

Its compact growth habit and ability to survive in harsh environments make it a characteristic species of tundra and mountain habitats.
