Acacia crassiuscula var. latifolia

Acacia crassiuscula var. latifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaved acacia, is a shrub or small tree native to Australia.

It features compound leaves with broad, flat leaflets that are often pale green in color. The plant produces yellow globular flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to provide habitat for native wildlife.
