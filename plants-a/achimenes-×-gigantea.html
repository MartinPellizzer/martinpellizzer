Achimenes × gigantea

Achimenes × gigantea, commonly known as the giant achimenes, is a hybrid plant native to South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant features heart-shaped, velvety leaves and thrives in warm, humid conditions. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its vibrant blooms and foliage.

Its flowers are typically borne on long, branching stems, making it a popular choice for garden displays.
