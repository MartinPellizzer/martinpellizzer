Aloe bulbicaulis

Aloe bulbicaulis, commonly known as the bulb aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features a thick, fleshy stem that stores water, allowing it to survive in arid conditions. The plant produces clusters of narrow, pointed leaves that are typically gray-green in color. It blooms with yellow flowers during the summer months, adding ornamental value to its appearance.

Aloe bulbicaulis is well-suited for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements.
