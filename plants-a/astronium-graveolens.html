Astronium graveolens

Astronium graveolens, commonly known as the Brazilian cherry or *Pouteria graveolens*, is a tropical tree native to South America.

It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its large, glossy, elliptical leaves. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers followed by edible, fleshy fruits that are rich in nutrients. Its wood is valued for its durability and is used in construction and furniture-making.

The plant also has traditional medicinal uses in some regions.
