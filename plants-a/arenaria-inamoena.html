Arenaria inamoena

Arenaria inamoena, commonly known as the sandspoon, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Arctic and subarctic regions.

It thrives in rocky, sandy, and gravelly soils, often found in coastal and alpine environments. The plant produces small, white or pale blue flowers that bloom in early summer. Its leaves are typically narrow and linear, adapted to withstand harsh weather conditions.

Arenaria inamoena is valued for its hardiness and is often used in rock gardens or as a ground cover in cold climates.
