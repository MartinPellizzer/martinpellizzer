Agrostistachys sessilifolia var. graciliflora

Agrostistachys sessilifolia var. graciliflora, commonly known as the slender-flowered grassveld grass, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically bluish-green in color and grow in dense tufts. The plant produces small, white to pale pink flowers arranged in spike-like inflorescences, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a ground cover.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its delicate appearance and ability to attract pollinators.
