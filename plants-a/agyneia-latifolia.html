Agyneia latifolia

Agyneia latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf agyneia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions.

It features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery pubescence. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in warm climates.
