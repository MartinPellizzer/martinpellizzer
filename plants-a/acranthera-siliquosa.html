Acranthera siliquosa

Acranthera siliquosa, commonly known as the slender siliquous acranthera, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its flowering period occurs during the summer months, producing numerous yellow flowers that attract pollinators.

Acranthera siliquosa is often found in fynbos vegetation, showcasing its adaptation to the unique climate of the region.
