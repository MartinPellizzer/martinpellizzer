Alyssum iljinskae

Alyssum iljinskae, commonly known as the Iljin's alyssum, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale purple, blooming in late spring to early summer.
