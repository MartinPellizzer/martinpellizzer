Astragalus chrysostachys var. kopet-daghensis

Astragalus chrysostachys var. kopet-daghensis, commonly known as the Kopet-Dag astragalus, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its spiny stems and yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to harsh, dry environments. It produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds.
