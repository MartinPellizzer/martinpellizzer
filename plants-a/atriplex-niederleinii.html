Atriplex niederleinii

Atriplex niederleinii, commonly known as the Niederlein's saltbush, is a perennial shrub native to the arid regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves that are adapted to conserve water in harsh, dry environments. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in dense, spike-like inflorescences. It thrives in saline and alkaline soils, making it a valuable species for reclaiming degraded lands.

Atriplex niederleinii is also known for its ability to stabilize soil and prevent erosion in desert ecosystems.
