Anubias barteri var. caladiifolia

Anubias barteri var. caladiifolia, commonly known as the African water lettuce, is a hardy aquatic plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features broad, waxy, dark green leaves that are often crinkled and arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that emerge from the water surface. It is well adapted to slow-moving or still freshwater environments.

Its dense foliage makes it an excellent choice for aquariums and pond landscaping.
