Aparisthmium javanense

Aparisthmium javanense, commonly known as the Java climbing fern, is a tropical fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, arching fronds that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant features a complex system of rhizomes and stolons, allowing it to spread rapidly in its natural habitat. Its leaves are typically bi-lobed and have a delicate, feathery appearance.

Aparisthmium javanense thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
