Aspidistra lateralis

Aspidistra lateralis, commonly known as the silver lace aspidistra, is a perennial evergreen plant native to Japan.

It features dark green, leathery leaves with a distinctive silvery-white vein pattern that gives it its common name. The plant grows in a compact, upright form and is well adapted to low-light conditions. It prefers moist, well-drained soil and is often used in shaded garden settings.

Aspidistra lateralis is valued for its ornamental foliage and its ability to thrive indoors with minimal care.
