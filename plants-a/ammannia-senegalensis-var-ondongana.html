Ammannia senegalensis var. ondongana

Ammannia senegalensis var. ondongana, commonly known as the redstem smartweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Africa and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, pink to red flowers that bloom in summer. The leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and often have a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in moist, sunny environments and is often found in wetlands, along stream banks, and in disturbed soils.

The plant is known for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
