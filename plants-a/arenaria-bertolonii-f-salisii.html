Arenaria bertolonii f. salisii

Arenaria bertolonii f. salisii, commonly known as the Salisii's sandwort, is a flowering plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is a low-growing perennial herb characterized by its compact, mat-forming growth habit. The plant features small, white or pinkish flowers that bloom in early summer, typically from June to July. Its leaves are linear and arranged in a rosette pattern, adapted to thrive in sandy or rocky soils.

This subspecies is native to the Mediterranean region and is often found in dry, open habitats.
