Amaranthus blitum

Amaranthus blitum, commonly known as red root amaranth, is an annual herbaceous plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of small, red or yellow flowers. The plant has feathery, lance-shaped leaves that are often tinged with red or purple hues. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy species suitable for various climates.

Amaranthus blitum is also valued for its edible leaves, which are rich in nutrients and used in traditional cuisines.
