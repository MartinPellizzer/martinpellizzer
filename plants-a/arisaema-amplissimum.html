Arisaema amplissimum

Arisaema amplissimum, commonly known as the giant arrowhead, is a striking member of the Araceae family native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can reach up to 1 meter in diameter, giving it a dramatic appearance. The plant produces a spadix surrounded by a spathe, which is typically green or yellowish in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Due to its unique morphology and ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and by plant enthusiasts.
