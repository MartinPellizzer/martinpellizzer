Anacampseros cebennensis

Anacampseros cebennensis, commonly known as the Cebenna stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, rosette-forming growth habit and thick, fleshy leaves that store water, allowing it to survive in dry conditions. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
