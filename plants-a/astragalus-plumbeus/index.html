Astragalus Plumbeus

Astragalus plumbeus is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the arid regions of the western united states. it belongs to the legume family and is known for its small, pale blue to purple blooms that appear in late spring. this species thrives in rocky soils and is often found in desert environments where water is scarce. due to its limited range and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species. conservation efforts are crucial to protect this unique plant from further decline.
