Ansellia humilis

Ansellia humilis, commonly known as the dwarf arrowroot, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 1 meter. The plant features broad, arrow-shaped leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. Its flowers are small and clustered in inflorescences, usually in shades of white or pale yellow.

Ansellia humilis is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some culinary uses in certain regions.
