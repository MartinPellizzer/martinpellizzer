Allocarya californica var. debilis

Allocarya californica var. debilis, commonly known as the California dwarf flax.

It is a low-growing perennial herb native to the coastal regions of California. The plant features slender, branching stems covered in fine, silky hairs. Its small, blue to purple flowers appear in clusters during the spring and early summer.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats along the coast.
