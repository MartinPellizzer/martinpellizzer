Agropyron × yukonense

Agropyron × yukonense, commonly known as the Yukon wheatgrass, is a hybrid grass species native to North America.

It is characterized by its fine, tufted leaves and its ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions. This plant is often used in landscaping and erosion control due to its hardy nature and adaptability. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny grains.

Agropyron × yukonense is also valued for its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements.
