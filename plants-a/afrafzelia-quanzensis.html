Afrafzelia quanzensis

Afrafzelia quanzensis, commonly known as the Quanzhou flame tree, is a deciduous tree native to southern China.

It belongs to the family Leguminosae and is characterized by its striking red-orange flowers that bloom in the spring. The tree has a broad, spreading canopy and can grow up to 20 meters in height. Its leaves are bipinnate, and the fruit is a flattened pod containing several seeds.
