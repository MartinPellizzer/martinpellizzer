Asplenium solmsii

Asplenium solmsii, commonly known as the rock fern, is a small, evergreen fern native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in rocky or crevice habitats, showcasing its adaptability to harsh environments. The plant has delicate, lance-shaped fronds that are often covered in a fine, rusty-colored bloom, giving it a distinctive appearance. Its rhizomes are wiry and capable of forming dense colonies, which helps it thrive in rocky soil.

Asplenium solmsii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in shaded, moist conditions.
