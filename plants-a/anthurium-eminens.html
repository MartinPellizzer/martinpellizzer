Anthurium eminens

Anthurium eminens, commonly known as the eminens arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are often a deep green with a glossy texture. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-like inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, typically in shades of red or orange. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium eminens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.
