Asparagus aethiopicus var. natalensis

Asparagus aethiopicus var. natalensis, commonly known as Natal asparagus, is a perennial shrub native to southern Africa.

It features succulent, fleshy stems and leaves that are adapted to arid environments. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that develop into red, berry-like fruits. Its root system is extensive, allowing it to thrive in well-drained soils.

Natal asparagus is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in dry climates.
