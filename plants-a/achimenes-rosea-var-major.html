Achimenes rosea var. major

Achimenes rosea var. major, commonly known as the giant flame flower, is a tropical perennial plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from red to orange and yellow. The plant produces tubular, spurred flowers that bloom in clusters, often reaching several inches in length. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking vibrant, tropical blooms.
