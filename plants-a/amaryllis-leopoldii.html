Amaryllis leopoldii

Amaryllis leopoldii, commonly known as the Leopold's amaryllis, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, sturdy stems that bear large, showy flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant produces strap-like leaves that are broad and waxy, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and containers.

Due to its vibrant blooms and relatively easy care requirements, it is often cultivated for its aesthetic value.
