Aethionema chlorifolium

Aethionema chlorifolium, commonly known as the greenleaf rockcress, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically thrives in rocky or sandy soils. The plant features small, fleshy leaves that are usually green, hence its common name. Its flowers are typically bright yellow and appear in early spring.

It is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
