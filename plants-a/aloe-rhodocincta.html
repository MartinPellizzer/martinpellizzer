Aloe rhodocincta

Aloe rhodocincta, commonly known as the red-striped aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, slender stems that are often branched and covered in a dense layer of white, woolly hair. The leaves are fleshy, triangular, and arranged in a rosette, with a pale green color and prominent red or purple stripes along the edges. It produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow flowers that bloom in summer.

This plant is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens and arid landscapes.
