Aporosa mollis

Aporosa mollis, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical shrub belonging to the Malvaceae family.

It is characterized by its smooth, often velvety leaves that are typically oval to heart-shaped. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. Aporosa mollis is native to regions of Southeast Asia and is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and clearings.

It is noted for its adaptability and can thrive in a variety of soil types.
