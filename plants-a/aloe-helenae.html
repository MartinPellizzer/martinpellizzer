Aloe helenae

Aloe helenae, commonly known as Helen's aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, upright leaves that are typically green with white or yellowish markings. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow flowers during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves.

Aloe helenae is often cultivated for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
