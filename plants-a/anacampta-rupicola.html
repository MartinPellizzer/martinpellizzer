Anacampta rupicola

Anacampta rupicola, commonly known as the rock orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is characterized by its compact growth habit. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in late winter or early spring. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often forming dense clumps.

Anacampta rupicola is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
