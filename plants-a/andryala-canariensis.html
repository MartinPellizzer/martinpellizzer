Andryala canariensis

Andryala canariensis, commonly known as the Canary Islands holly, is a shrub native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the family Ericaceae and is characterized by its evergreen leaves and small, white flowers. The plant produces berries that are typically red or black when mature. It thrives in acidic soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
