Alocasia rex

Alocasia rex, commonly known as the regal alocasia, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 2 feet in diameter, often with a deep green color and intricate vein patterns. The plant thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light. It requires well-draining soil and regular watering to prevent root rot.

Alocasia rex is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its dramatic appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
