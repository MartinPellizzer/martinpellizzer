Astragalus delanensis subsp. baytopianus

Astragalus delanensis subsp.

baytopianus, commonly known as the Baytopian milkvetch, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Turkey. It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellowish flowers.

The plant typically grows in rocky or dry habitats, showcasing its adaptability to harsh environments. Its subspecies status highlights its distinct morphological and genetic traits compared to other Astragalus species.
