Astilbe thunbergii var. shikokiana

Astilbe thunbergii var. shikokiana, commonly known as the Shikoku astilbe, is a striking perennial plant native to Japan.

It features upright, branched stems that bear fluffy, plume-like panicles of pink to white flowers. The foliage is dark green and lance-shaped, providing a lush backdrop to the delicate blooms. It thrives in partial to full shade and moist, well-drained soil, making it ideal for woodland gardens.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and ability to add texture and color to shaded landscapes.
