Achillea holosericea

Achillea holosericea, commonly known as the silky yarrow, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its finely divided, silver-gray leaves and clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to high altitudes. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and alpine displays.
