Achnatherum chinense

Achnatherum chinense, commonly known as Chinese needlegrass, is a perennial grass species native to Asia.

It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. The plant has slender, erect stems that can reach heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are narrow and linear, with a rough, hairy texture.

The inflorescence consists of dense, spike-like clusters of small, spikelet-based flowers.
