Aspidistra arnautovii

Aspidistra arnautovii, commonly known as the Armenian aspidistra, is a species of flowering plant native to the Caucasus region.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its unique inflorescence consisting of a spadix and a spathe. The plant features dark green, lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and often marked with subtle veins. It thrives in shady, humid environments and is valued for its ornamental appeal.

Aspidistra arnautovii is relatively low-maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
