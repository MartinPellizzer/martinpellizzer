Aspalathus argentea var. glabriuscula

Aspalathus argentea var. glabriuscula, commonly known as silver bush or white clover, is a small shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-white leaves and small, pale yellow flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to fix nitrogen, improving soil fertility.

However, it is considered an invasive species in some regions due to its rapid spread.
