Avena neumeyeriana

Avena neumeyeriana, commonly known as the Neumeyer's oat, is a perennial grass species native to South America.

It is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green coloration.

Avena neumeyeriana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
