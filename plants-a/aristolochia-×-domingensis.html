Aristolochia × domingensis

Aristolochia × domingensis, commonly known as the Dominican birthwort, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean, particularly the Dominican Republic.

It is characterized by its unique, heart-shaped leaves and striking, pipe-shaped flowers that are often greenish with a purple or reddish tint. The plant belongs to the Aristolochiaceae family and is known for its complex chemical composition, including various aristolochic acids. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its exotic appearance.

However, it is important to note that some species within this genus are toxic and should be handled with care.
