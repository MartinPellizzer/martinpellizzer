Atractocarpus pterocarpon

Atractocarpus pterocarpon, commonly known as the thick-leafed sandarac tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Arecaceae, which includes palms, and is characterized by its robust, straight trunk and large, pinnate leaves.
