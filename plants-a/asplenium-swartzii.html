Asplenium swartzii

Asplenium swartzii, commonly known as the Swartz's spleenwort, is a fern belonging to the family Aspleniaceae.

It is a small, epiphytic or lithophytic plant, typically found growing on rocks or tree trunks in humid environments. The plant has slender, wiry stems with dense clusters of small, lance-shaped leaves. Its fronds are usually dark green and have a leathery texture, with spores produced on the underside of the leaves.

Asplenium swartzii is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, where it thrives in shaded, moist conditions.
