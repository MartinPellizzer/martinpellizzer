Anthurium consimile

Anthurium consimile, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a tropical flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are deep green with a velvety texture. The plant produces showy, spadix-based inflorescences surrounded by a bright red spathe, which is the primary ornamental feature. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.

Anthurium consimile is popular as a houseplant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
