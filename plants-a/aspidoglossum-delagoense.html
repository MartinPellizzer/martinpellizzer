Aspidoglossum delagoense

Aspidoglossum delagoense, commonly known as the African silver sword, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features a rosette of fleshy, silver-blue leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid conditions. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with small, tubular flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It is well adapted to full sun and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its striking appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a favored ornamental plant.
