Austromyrtus hillii

Austromyrtus hillii, commonly known as the Tasmanian myrtle, is a small evergreen shrub native to Tasmania.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family, which includes eucalyptus and tea trees. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are glossy green on top and lighter in color underneath. It produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in spring, followed by edible, red, fleshy berries.

Austromyrtus hillii is often found in moist, shaded areas of rainforests and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance.
