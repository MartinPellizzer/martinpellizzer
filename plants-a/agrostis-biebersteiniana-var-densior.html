Agrostis biebersteiniana var. densior

Agrostis biebersteiniana var. densior, commonly known as the dense hairgrass, is a perennial grass species native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and dense, fine-textured foliage. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows, wetlands, and along stream banks. Its inflorescence consists of a single, narrow spikelet that is often tinged with reddish hues.

This grass is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
