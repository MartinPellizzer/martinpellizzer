Acaena × ovina var. laxissima

Acaena × ovina var. laxissima, commonly known as the soft burr clover, is a low-growing, mat-forming plant native to New Zealand.

It features small, rounded leaves and produces numerous fluffy, yellowish seed heads that are characteristic of the genus Acaena. The plant is adapted to a variety of soil types and is often found in coastal and subalpine environments. Its dense growth habit makes it useful for erosion control and as a ground cover.

However, it can become invasive in certain areas, outcompeting native vegetation.
