Angraecoides Elliotii

Angraecoides elliotii is a rare and captivating orchid native to Madagascar. Known for its striking beauty and unique floral structure, it has fascinated botanists and plant enthusiasts alike. This species belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is often referred to as the "night-blooming orchid" due to its peculiar flowering habits. Its delicate petals and intricate patterns make it a prized specimen in horticulture. Despite its allure, Angraecoides elliotii remains elusive, thriving only in specific microhabitats within its native environment.
