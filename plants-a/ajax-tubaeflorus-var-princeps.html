Ajax tubaeflorus var. princeps

Ajax tubaeflorus var. princeps, commonly known as the Prince's Trumpet.

This plant is a flowering species native to tropical regions, characterized by its long, tubular flowers that are typically bright yellow or orange. The leaves are large, broad, and glossy, providing a striking contrast to the vibrant blooms. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is popular in botanical gardens and tropical landscapes.
