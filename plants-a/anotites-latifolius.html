Anotites latifolius

Anotites latifolius, commonly known as the broadleaf anotites, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy and arranged alternately along the stem. Its flowers are typically yellow or white, forming small clusters at the ends of the branches.
