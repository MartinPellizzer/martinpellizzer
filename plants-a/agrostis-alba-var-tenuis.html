Agrostis alba var. tenuis

Agrostis alba var. tenuis, commonly known as slender silvergrass, is a grass species native to temperate regions of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-haired leaves and tufted growth habit. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and riverbanks. Its inflorescence consists of a dense, cylindrical spikelet that is often tinged with silver or white.

Agrostis alba var. tenuis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping and ecological restoration projects.
