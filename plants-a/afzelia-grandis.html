Afzelia grandis

Afzelia grandis, commonly known as the African ironwood, is a large evergreen tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, heavy wood that is highly valued for its durability and strength. The tree has simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. It produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or white, followed by woody pods containing seeds.

Afzelia grandis is also known for its slow growth and long lifespan, making it an important species in both ecological and economic contexts.
