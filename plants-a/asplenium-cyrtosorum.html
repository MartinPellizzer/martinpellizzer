Asplenium cyrtosorum

Asplenium cyrtosorum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf rock fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Aspleniaceae.

It is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. This plant is often found growing on rocky surfaces or in shaded, moist environments. Its rhizomes are wiry and help it anchor to substrates such as stone or soil.

Asplenium cyrtosorum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in horticulture.
