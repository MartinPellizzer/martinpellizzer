Aster otagoensis

Aster otagoensis, commonly known as the Otago aster, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in alpine and subalpine regions, thriving in rocky and well-drained soils. Its flowers are usually blue to purple in color, with ray florets surrounding a central disk.

Aster otagoensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitat.
