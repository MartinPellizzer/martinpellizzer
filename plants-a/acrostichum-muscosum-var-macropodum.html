Acrostichum muscosum var. macropodum

Acrostichum muscosum var. macropodum, commonly known as the broadleaf fern, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a member of the fern family and is characterized by its large, leathery fronds that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense colonies.

The fronds are typically bipinnate, with a distinctive appearance that makes it a popular ornamental plant.
