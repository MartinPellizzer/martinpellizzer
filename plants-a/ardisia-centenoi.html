Ardisia centenoi

Ardisia centenoi, commonly known as the Christmas berry, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are elliptical in shape and emit a slight fragrance. The plant produces small, red berries that are edible and often used in traditional medicine. Ardisia centenoi thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and landscape design.
