Allocarya linifolia

Allocarya linifolia, commonly known as the slender leaf dandelion, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and serrated leaves. The plant typically grows in grasslands and rocky areas, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are linear and hairy, while the flowers are yellow and arranged in a single head.

Allocarya linifolia is known for its resilience and ability to thrive in arid conditions.
