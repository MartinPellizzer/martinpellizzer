Athamanta verticillata

Athamanta verticillata, commonly known as the spiral cypress, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its unique spiral arrangement of branches, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Athamanta verticillata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
