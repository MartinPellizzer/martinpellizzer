Alyssum huber-morathii

Alyssum huber-morathii, commonly known as the Morath's alyssum, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cress plants. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and clusters of small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. Alyssum huber-morathii typically blooms in late spring to early summer, and it thrives in well-drained soils with full sun exposure.

This species is often cultivated as a ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and delicate blooms.
