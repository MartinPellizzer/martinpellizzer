Acaulimalva Parnassiifolia

Acaulimalva parnassiifolia is a striking flowering plant known for its delicate, fern-like leaves and showy, pale pink to white blooms. Native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, it thrives in cool, moist environments with well-drained soil. This perennial plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is prized in gardens for its elegant appearance. Its unique foliage and floral structure make it a standout in botanical collections. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively rare in cultivation, making it a sought-after species among plant enthusiasts.
