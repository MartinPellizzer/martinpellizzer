Agathosma barbata

Agathosma barbata, commonly known as African geranium, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small, evergreen shrub that typically grows to a height of about 30 cm. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Agathosma barbata is also valued for its aromatic properties and is used in traditional medicine.
