Anemia oblongifolia var. presliana

Anemia oblongifolia var. presliana, commonly known as Preslia's anemia, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the family Amaranthaceae and is characterized by its elongated, lance-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in partial to full sunlight. Its flowers are small and clustered, often greenish-white in color, and it is known for its ornamental value.

The species is also valued for its potential medicinal properties in traditional herbal practices.
