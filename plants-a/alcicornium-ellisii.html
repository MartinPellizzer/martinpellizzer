Alcicornium ellisii

Alcicornium ellisii, commonly known as the silverleaf orchid, is a flowering plant native to the cloud forests of Central America.

It is characterized by its striking silver-gray foliage and delicate, fragrant orchid-like blooms. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil. Its flowers typically bloom in the spring and are pollinated by specific species of bees and moths.

Alcicornium ellisii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and private collections.
