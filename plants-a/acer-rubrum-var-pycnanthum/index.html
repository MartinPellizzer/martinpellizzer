Acer Rubrum Var. Pycnanthum

Acer rubrum var. pycnanthum is a unique variant of the red maple tree known for its striking autumn foliage. This plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is commonly found in eastern North America. Its leaves turn vibrant shades of red and orange, making it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping. The tree also produces small, red fruits that attract wildlife. Despite its beauty, it requires careful attention to soil conditions for optimal growth.
