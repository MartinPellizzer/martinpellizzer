Asphodeliris occidentalis

Asphodeliris occidentalis, commonly known as the western asphodel, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features strap-like leaves that are usually gray-green in color and can grow up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance, it is often cultivated in arid regions.
