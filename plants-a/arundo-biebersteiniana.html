Arundo biebersteiniana

Arundo biebersteiniana, commonly known as the silver reed, is a tall, perennial grass native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its slender, silver-gray leaves and tall, cylindrical flower spikes that bloom in summer. The plant is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its elegant appearance and drought tolerance. It prefers full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Its rapid growth and ability to form dense stands make it both a valuable and sometimes invasive species in certain environments.
