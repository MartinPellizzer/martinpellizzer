Antirrhinum orontium var. abyssinicum

Antirrhinum orontium var. abyssinicum, commonly known as the Abyssinian snapdragon, is a flowering plant native to Ethiopia.

It belongs to the Plantaginaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that come in shades of red, yellow, and orange. The plant typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are upright and branched.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
