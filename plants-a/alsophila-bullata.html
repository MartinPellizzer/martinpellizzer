Alsophila bullata

Alsophila bullata, commonly known as the broad-leaf tree fern, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk covered in fibrous, persistent roots. The plant has large, feather-like fronds that grow in a dense, upright arrangement. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Alsophila bullata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a decorative plant in gardens.
