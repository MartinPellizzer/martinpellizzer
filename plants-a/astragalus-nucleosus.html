Astragalus nucleosus

Astragalus nucleosus, commonly known as the small milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its small, yellow flowers and compound leaves. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is often found in prairies and open woodlands. Astragalus nucleosus has a deep root system that helps it survive in arid conditions.

It is also valued for its ecological role in supporting pollinators and soil stabilization.
