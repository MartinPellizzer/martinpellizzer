Acrostichum roezlii

Acrostichum roezlii, commonly known as the Zambian shield fern, is a member of the fern family Polypodiaceae.

It is native to tropical Africa, particularly Zambia, and thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit, with long, creeping stems that produce fronds. Its leaves are pinnately compound, with a distinctive shield-shaped base that gives it its common name.

It is valued for its ornamental appearance and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
