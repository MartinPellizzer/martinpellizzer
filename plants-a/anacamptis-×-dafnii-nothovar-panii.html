Anacamptis × dafnii nothovar. panii

Anacamptis × dafnii nothovar. panii, commonly known as the Panis orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and clusters of fragrant, violet-blue flowers with white markings. The plant typically blooms in late spring to early summer, producing showy, spurred orchid flowers that attract pollinators. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.

This nothovar is valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of care in suitable climates.
