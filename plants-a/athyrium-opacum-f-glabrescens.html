Athyrium opacum f. glabrescens

Athyrium opacum f. glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth-leafed Japanese shield fern, is a fern species characterized by its dense, dark green fronds with a smooth, glossy texture.

It belongs to the Athyriaceae family and is native to Japan, where it thrives in shaded, moist forest environments. The plant features a rhizomatous growth habit, with fertile fronds that produce sporangia on the underside of the leaves.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for woodland gardens and as a houseplant due to its elegant appearance and ease of care.
