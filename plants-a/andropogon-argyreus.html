Andropogon argyreus

Andropogon argyreus, commonly known as silver bluestem, is a perennial grass native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its silvery-blue foliage and tufted inflorescence, which gives it a striking appearance. The plant typically grows in open woodlands and prairies, preferring well-drained soils. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense stands.

Silver bluestem is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in stabilizing soil in native landscapes.
