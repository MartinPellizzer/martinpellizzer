Amauropelta Caucaensis

Amauropelta caucaensis is a rare and unique fern species native to the mountainous regions of Colombia. This plant belongs to the family Pterobranchiaceae and is known for its distinctive leaf morphology. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in cloud forests at high altitudes. Due to habitat loss and limited distribution, it is considered endangered. Studying Amauropelta caucaensis offers valuable insights into the biodiversity of tropical ecosystems.
