Aglaonema subundulatum

Aglaonema subundulatum, commonly known as the silver queen plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features striking, waxy, dark green leaves with prominent silver veins that create a shimmering effect. The plant is known for its compact growth habit and is often cultivated as a decorative indoor plant. It thrives in low light conditions and requires moderate watering to maintain its vibrant foliage.

Aglaonema subundulatum is also valued for its air-purifying qualities and ability to thrive in humid environments.
