Ardisia bawae

Ardisia bawae, commonly known as the Bawae beautyberry, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Ericaceae, which includes plants such as blueberries and heathers. The plant is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of small, white flowers that bloom in spring. Following flowering, it produces bright red berries that are edible for humans and birds.
