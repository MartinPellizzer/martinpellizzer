Asplenium serra var. camptosorum

Asplenium serra var. camptosorum, commonly known as the zigzag spleenwort, is a fern belonging to the family Aspleniaceae.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves that exhibit a distinctive zigzag or undulating pattern along their margins. This variety is typically found in moist, shaded environments such as rock crevices or forest floors. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit, allowing it to spread vegetatively.

Its botanical features make it a notable species in both horticulture and ecological studies.
