Arrhenatherum longifolium

Arrhenatherum longifolium, commonly known as slender wood oat, is a perennial grass species native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features slender, upright stems with long, narrow leaves that are often hairy. Its inflorescence consists of a dense spike of small, two-lobed spikelets.

It plays an important role in ecological systems by providing habitat and food for various invertebrates.
