Aster oharae

Aster oharae, commonly known as the Obara's aster, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its flowers are usually purple or blue, with ray florets surrounding a central disk.

Aster oharae is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
