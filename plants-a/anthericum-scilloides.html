Anthericum scilloides

Anthericum scilloides, commonly known as the narrow-leaved grass-of-Parnassus, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Araceae family.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and a distinctive inflorescence composed of a spadix surrounded by a spathe. Anthericum scilloides is often found in temperate regions of Europe and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local biodiversity.
