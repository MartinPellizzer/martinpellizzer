Aeschynomene ormocarpoides

Aeschynomene ormocarpoides, commonly known as smallleaf leadplant, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its small, trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers. The plant is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of habitats, including grasslands and woodlands.
