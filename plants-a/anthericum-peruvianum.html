Anthericum peruvianum

Anthericum peruvianum, commonly known as the Peruvian arum, is a herbaceous plant native to the Andean regions of South America.

It features a short, tuberous rhizome and produces a striking, umbrella-shaped inflorescence with a spathe and spadix. The plant's leaves are large, heart-shaped, and often have a waxy texture. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is valued for its ornamental appeal.

Anthericum peruvianum is also known for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal practices.
