Artemisia kanashiroi

Artemisia kanashiroi, commonly known as Japanese wormwood, is a perennial herb native to Japan.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, silver-gray foliage. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flower heads that are typically clustered in flat-topped clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or mountainous regions.

Due to its medicinal properties, it has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
