Areca jokowi

Areca jokowi, commonly known as the Jokowi areca palm, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Arecaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like leaves. The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and can thrive in warm, humid climates. Its growth is slow, making it suitable for indoor or outdoor gardens with proper care.

The leaves are typically a deep green, adding a lush aesthetic to its surroundings.
