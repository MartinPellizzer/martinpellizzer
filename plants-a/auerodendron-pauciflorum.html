Auerodendron pauciflorum

Auerodendron pauciflorum, commonly known as the few-flowered evergreen.

It is a small shrub native to southern Africa, characterized by its dense, leathery leaves and clusters of small, white flowers. The plant produces red, fleshy fruits that are adapted for dispersal by animals. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Auerodendron pauciflorum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its hardy nature and year-round foliage.
