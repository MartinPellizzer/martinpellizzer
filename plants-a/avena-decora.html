Avena decora

Avena decora, commonly known as the decorative oat, is a species of grass native to North America.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and delicate, feathery inflorescences that give it a graceful appearance. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its foliage is often blue-green in color, adding visual interest to gardens and natural landscapes.

Avena decora is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often used in landscaping for its aesthetic appeal.
