Aloe acutissima

Aloe acutissima, commonly known as the sharp-toothed aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, with sharp, spiny edges. The plant produces tall, branched flowering stems that bear yellow-orange flowers during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its leaves to survive dry conditions.

Aloe acutissima is often used in traditional medicine and as an ornamental plant due to its hardy nature and striking appearance.
