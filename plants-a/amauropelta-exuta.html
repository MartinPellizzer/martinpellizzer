Amauropelta exuta

Amauropelta exuta, commonly known as the broad-leafed shield fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its large, pinnately compound leaves that are often broad and leathery in texture. The fronds are typically dark green and have a distinctive, shield-like appearance, which gives the plant its common name. This fern is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia and is often found growing in moist, shaded environments.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
