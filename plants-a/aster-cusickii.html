Aster cusickii

Aster cusickii, commonly known as Cusick's aster, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the sunflower family (Asteraceae) and is characterized by its showy blue to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils in arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its leaves are usually narrow and gray-green, with a fuzzy texture that helps reduce water loss.

Cusick's aster is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and desert scrub.
