Aloinopsis schooneesii

Aloinopsis schooneesii, commonly known as the Schoonees aloes, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asphodelaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-shaped arrangement of fleshy, blue-green leaves. The plant produces tall, branched flower stalks that bear yellowish-white flowers, which bloom in late winter or early spring. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves and requiring minimal watering.

Due to its unique appearance and hardiness, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
