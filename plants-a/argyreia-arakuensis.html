Argyreia arakuensis

Argyreia arakuensis, commonly known as the red sandalwood or Araku sandalwood, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of India.

It belongs to the Convolvulaceae family and is characterized by its climbing habit and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers. The plant produces small, dark blue to black berries that contain seeds. Its wood is highly valued for its aromatic properties and is used in traditional crafts and incense.
