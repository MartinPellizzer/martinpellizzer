Aristida dewinteri

Aristida dewinteri, commonly known as Dewinter's threeawn, is a flowering grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and slender, wiry stems that often form dense clumps. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences composed of multiple bracts, which are typically covered in short, bristly hairs. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue and a rough texture.

Aristida dewinteri is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a resilient plant in dry conditions.
