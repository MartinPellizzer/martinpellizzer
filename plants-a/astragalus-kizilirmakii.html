Astragalus kizilirmakii

Astragalus kizilirmakii, commonly known as the Kizilirmak milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to Turkey.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, pea-like flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid conditions.

It plays a role in soil stabilization and is of interest for its potential medicinal properties.
