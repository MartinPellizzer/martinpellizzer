Apuleja asteroides

Apuleja asteroides, commonly known as the star-shaped nightshade, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its small, star-shaped flowers that typically bloom in the evening. The plant has dark green, oval-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It produces small, round berries that turn from green to red as they mature.

Apuleja asteroides is often grown in gardens for its ornamental value and nocturnal blooming habit.
