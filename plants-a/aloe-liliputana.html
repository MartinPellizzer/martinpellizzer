Aloe liliputana

Aloe liliputana, commonly known as the dwarf aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, narrow leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces small, yellow flowers on tall, branched inflorescences during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its leaves for survival.

Due to its compact size and ornamental appeal, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in rock gardens.
