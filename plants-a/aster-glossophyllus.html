Aster glossophyllus

Aster glossophyllus, commonly known as the woolly aster, is a flowering plant native to the Andes Mountains of South America.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces showy blue to purple flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in cool, high-altitude environments and is often found in rocky or alpine habitats.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is a subject of interest for botanists and horticulturists.
