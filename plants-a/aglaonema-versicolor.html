Aglaonema versicolor

Aglaonema versicolor, commonly known as the variegated Chinese evergreen, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, multicolored foliage, featuring shades of green, white, and pink. The leaves are typically broad and lance-shaped, with a smooth texture and a waxy appearance. This plant thrives in low-light conditions and requires moderate humidity to maintain its vibrant coloration.

It is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and air-purifying properties.
