Abutilon Indicum Var. Albescens

Abutilon indicum var. albescens is a unique and striking plant known for its soft, velvety leaves and delicate, lantern-like flowers. Native to tropical regions, this variant of the Indian mallow thrives in warm, humid climates with plenty of sunlight. Its white blooms add a subtle elegance to gardens and indoor spaces, making it a popular choice among plant enthusiasts. The plant's distinctive appearance and easy care requirements make it a favorite for both beginners and experienced gardeners. With its ornamental value and adaptability, Abutilon indicum var. albescens continues to captivate plant lovers worldwide.
