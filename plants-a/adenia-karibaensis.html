Adenia karibaensis

Adenia karibaensis, commonly known as the Kariba adenium, is a succulent shrub native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Passifloraceae and is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems and lobed leaves. The plant produces striking yellow flowers with prominent stamens, which are often surrounded by colorful bracts. It is adapted to arid environments, storing water in its stem to survive dry periods.

Adenia karibaensis is also noted for its medicinal properties in traditional African medicine.
