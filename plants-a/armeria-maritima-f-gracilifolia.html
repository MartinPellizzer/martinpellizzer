Armeria maritima f. gracilifolia

Armeria maritima f. gracilifolia, commonly known as the slender-leaved sea thrift, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of the northern hemisphere.

It is a low-growing perennial with dense clusters of white or pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant features narrow, linear leaves that are adapted to withstand salty sea air and strong coastal winds. It thrives in well-drained sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in dunes and cliff habitats.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to stabilize coastal environments.
