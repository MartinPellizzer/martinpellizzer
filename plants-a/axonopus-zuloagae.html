Axonopus zuloagae

Axonopus zuloagae, commonly known as the Zuloaga's axonopus, is a species of grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Poaceae family and is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit. The plant typically has narrow, grass-like leaves with a smooth texture and a bluish-green color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments.

Axonopus zuloagae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping and erosion control.
