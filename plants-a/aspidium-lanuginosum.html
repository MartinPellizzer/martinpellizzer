Aspidium lanuginosum

Aspidium lanuginosum, commonly known as the downy water fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, hairy fronds that give it a soft, downy appearance. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. Its rhizomes are often covered in a fine, silky layer of hair, contributing to its common name.

Aspidium lanuginosum is a perennial plant that thrives in temperate climates and is often found in moist, acidic soils.
