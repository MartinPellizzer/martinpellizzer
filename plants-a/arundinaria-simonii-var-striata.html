Arundinaria simonii var. striata

Arundinaria simonii var. striata, commonly known as the stripe bamboo, is a variety of bamboo characterized by its distinctive striped foliage.

It is native to eastern China and is known for its slender, upright growth habit. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often marked with contrasting green stripes. It is a hardy perennial that thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a landscape plant.
