Adenosma macrophylla

Adenosma macrophylla, commonly known as the large-leaf adenosma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its robust, herbaceous growth habit and large, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in forest understories.

Adenosma macrophylla is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
