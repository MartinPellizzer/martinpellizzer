Anabasis ferganica

Anabasis ferganica, commonly known as the Fergana saltbush, is a shrub native to Central Asia.

It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in saline soils. The plant has small, fleshy leaves that help it retain moisture in harsh conditions. Its flowers are typically yellow or greenish-yellow, appearing in clusters.

Anabasis ferganica is adapted to withstand extreme temperatures and low precipitation.
