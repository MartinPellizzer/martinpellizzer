Aspidium koordersii

Aspidium koordersii, commonly known as the Koorders' water fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or stream banks. Its rhizomes are creeping and produce clusters of small, spore-bearing leaves.

Aspidium koordersii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens with high humidity.
