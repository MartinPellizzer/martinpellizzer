Arenaria verna var. rubella

Arenaria verna var. rubella, commonly known as the pink sandwort, is a low-growing, mat-forming perennial plant native to arctic and alpine regions.

It features dense clusters of small, pink or red flowers that bloom in early spring, attracting pollinators like bees. The plant has slender, succulent stems and narrow, fleshy leaves adapted to cold, dry environments. It thrives in rocky soils and is often found in tundra and mountainous habitats.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and alpine plant collections.
