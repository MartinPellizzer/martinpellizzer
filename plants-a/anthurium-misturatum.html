Anthurium misturatum

Anthurium misturatum, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are dark green with a metallic sheen. The plant produces vibrant red spathes that enclose its yellow spadix, creating a dramatic floral display. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Anthurium misturatum is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
