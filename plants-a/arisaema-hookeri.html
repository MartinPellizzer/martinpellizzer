Arisaema hookeri

Arisaema hookeri, commonly known as the Hooker's arisaema, is a member of the Araceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of India and China. The plant features a distinctive underground tuber and a tall, leafy stem that bears a single, showy spadix surrounded by a spathe. Its foliage consists of large, heart-shaped leaves that are often deeply lobed.

Arisaema hookeri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens for its unique appearance.
