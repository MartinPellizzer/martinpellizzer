Anacamptis × semisaccata

Anacamptis × semisaccata, commonly known as the semi-saccate orchid, is a hybrid species within the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lanceolate, dark green leaves. The plant produces inflorescences with pendulous flowers that have a distinctive, spurred labellum. Its flowers are typically violet to purple in color, with a pale yellow or white lip.

This orchid thrives in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in woodland or meadow habitats.
