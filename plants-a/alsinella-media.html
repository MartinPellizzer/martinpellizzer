Alsinella media

Alsinella media, commonly known as the narrowleaf sandmat, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of North America.

It is a low-growing annual herb that typically reaches heights of 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, yellowish-green flowers in clusters. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is adapted to withstand drought conditions.

Alsinella media is often found in disturbed areas and is considered a characteristic species of desert environments.
