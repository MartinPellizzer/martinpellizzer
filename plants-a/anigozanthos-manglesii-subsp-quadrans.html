Anigozanthos manglesii subsp. quadrans

Anigozanthos manglesii subsp.

quadrans, commonly known as the Purple Flag, is a perennial shrub native to Western Australia. It is characterized by its striking purple to red flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring.

The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces showy, clustered inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for coastal gardens.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract native pollinators.
