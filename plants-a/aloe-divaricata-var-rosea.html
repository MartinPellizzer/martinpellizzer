Aloe divaricata var. rosea

Aloe divaricata var. rosea, commonly known as the Rose Aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically pink or red-tipped, giving it its striking appearance. The plant produces tall, branching inflorescences with small, tubular flowers that are often yellow or orange in color. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and ability to store water in its leaves.
