Alaticaulia brachyura

Alaticaulia brachyura, commonly known as the short-stemmed alate caudex plant, is a unique succulent native to arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems that store water, allowing it to survive in dry conditions. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the rainy season. Its leaves are typically reduced to small, spiny structures, which help minimize water loss.

Alaticaulia brachyura is well adapted to rocky soils and can thrive in full sun, making it a resilient species in harsh environments.
