Acriopsis liliifolia var. liliifolia

Acriopsis liliifolia var. liliifolia, commonly known as the slender cowplant, is a perennial herb native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in clusters during the spring and summer months. It thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and sagebrush steppe.

Its botanical features make it well adapted to arid environments and a valuable component of local ecosystems.
