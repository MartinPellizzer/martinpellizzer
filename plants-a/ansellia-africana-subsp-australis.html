Ansellia africana subsp. australis

Ansellia africana subsp.

australis, commonly known as the Australian arrowroot, is a tropical plant native to southern Africa. It is a rhizomatous herbaceous perennial that produces large, showy, white to pale yellow flowers.

The plant features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and swampy areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and greenhouses.
