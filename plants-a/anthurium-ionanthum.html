Anthurium ionanthum

Anthurium ionanthum, commonly known as the velvet leaf anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a velvety texture. The plant produces striking spadices, which are the characteristic inflorescences of the Araceae family, often in shades of red or orange. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium ionanthum is popular as a decorative plant due to its ornamental foliage and unique floral structures.
