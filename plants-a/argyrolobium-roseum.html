Argyrolobium roseum

Argyrolobium roseum, commonly known as the pink burr vetch.

It is a flowering plant native to southern Africa, characterized by its distinctive pink to purple flowers and hairy, branching stems. The plant produces small, spiny pods that contain seeds, which are often used for erosion control due to its hardy nature. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions.

The species is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in native landscapes.
