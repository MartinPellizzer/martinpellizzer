Aspidium ferox

Aspidium ferox, commonly known as the fierce water fern, is a species of fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Athyriaceae and is characterized by its robust, leathery fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands. Its spores are produced in sori that are clustered on the undersides of its leaves.

Aspidium ferox is known for its adaptability and can thrive in a variety of soil types as long as there is adequate moisture.
