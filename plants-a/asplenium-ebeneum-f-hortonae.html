Asplenium ebeneum f. hortonae

Asplenium ebeneum f. hortonae, commonly known as the Horton's spleenwort, is a fern belonging to the family Aspleniaceae.

It is characterized by its small, elongated, and often lobed leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. This fern is epiphytic, meaning it grows on the surfaces of trees or rocks without being parasitic. It prefers humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

The fronds are usually arranged in a rosette and can vary in shape depending on the environmental conditions.
