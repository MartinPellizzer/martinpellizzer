Achillea × schroeteri

Achillea × schroeteri, commonly known as the hybrid yarrow, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is a natural hybrid between Achillea millefolium (common yarrow) and Achillea ptarmica (chicory yarrow), resulting in a plant with intermediate characteristics. The plant features finely divided, feathery leaves and clusters of small, often white or pale yellow flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long bloom period and resilience to various environmental conditions.
