Anthericum platyphyllum var. michoacense

Anthericum platyphyllum var. michoacense, commonly known as the Michoacan broadleaf lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Mexico.

It features strap-like, broad leaves that are typically dark green and densely arranged around the base. The plant produces clusters of small, white, bell-shaped flowers during the spring and early summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
