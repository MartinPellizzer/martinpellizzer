Aralia hupehensis

Aralia hupehensis, commonly known as the Chinese angelica tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It features compound leaves that are typically pinnately arranged with serrated margins. The plant produces small, white to greenish flowers in clustered umbels, followed by red or orange berries. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
