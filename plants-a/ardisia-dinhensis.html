Ardisia dinhensis

Ardisia dinhensis, commonly known as Dinh's ardisia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in Vietnam and Cambodia. The plant is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and produces small, white to pinkish flowers. The fruit is typically red or orange, containing several seeds, and is often used in traditional medicine.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical forests.
