Astronia bulusanensis

Astronia bulusanensis, commonly known as the Bulusan aster, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in lowland areas and is often found in secondary forests. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow with a central disc.

Astronia bulusanensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and plays a role in local biodiversity.
