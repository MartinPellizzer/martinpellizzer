Agrimonia aitchisonii

Agrimonia aitchisonii, commonly known as the Kashmir agrimony, is a perennial herb native to the Himalayas.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its upright stems and serrated leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in summer, followed by distinctive, spiny fruits. It thrives in rocky and alpine environments, often found at high altitudes.

Agrimonia aitchisonii is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
