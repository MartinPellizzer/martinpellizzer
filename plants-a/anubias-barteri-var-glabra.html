Anubias barteri var. glabra

Anubias barteri var. glabra, commonly known as African water lettuce, is a hardy aquatic plant native to Africa.

It features broad, waxy, dark green leaves that are typically rounded and slightly pointed. The plant thrives in slow-moving or still freshwater environments and is often used in aquariums for its aesthetic appeal and ability to provide shelter for aquatic life. It has a rhizomatous root system that helps it anchor in substrate and spread vegetatively.

This variety is particularly noted for its adaptability and low maintenance requirements in both natural and artificial aquatic settings.
