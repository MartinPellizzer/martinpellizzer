Alternanthera altacruzensis

Alternanthera altacruzensis, commonly known as the red-stemmed Alternanthera, is a striking aquatic plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red stems and lance-shaped leaves that often display a mix of green and red hues. This plant thrives in shallow, slow-moving waters and is often used in aquascaping for its aesthetic appeal. It prefers moderate to high light conditions and can grow in a variety of substrates.

Its rapid growth rate makes it a popular choice for both freshwater and brackish water environments.
