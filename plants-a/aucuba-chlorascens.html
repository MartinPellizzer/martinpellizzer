Aucuba chlorascens

Aucuba chlorascens, commonly known as the golden aucuba, is a member of the family Cornaceae.

It is native to Japan and is characterized by its striking yellow-variegated leaves. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its foliage remains vibrant throughout the year, making it a popular ornamental plant in gardens.
