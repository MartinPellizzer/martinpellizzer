Acrostichum portoricense

Acrostichum portoricense, commonly known as the Puerto Rican grass.

It is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean, particularly found in Puerto Rico. This species belongs to the Poaceae family, which includes grasses, and is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded areas and is often found in coastal regions.

Its leaves are narrow and blade-like, with a smooth surface and a distinct midrib.
