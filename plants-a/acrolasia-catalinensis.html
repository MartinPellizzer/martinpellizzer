Acrolasia catalinensis

Acrolasia catalinensis, commonly known as the Catalina acrolasia, is a flowering plant native to the island of Catalina in California.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in coastal scrub habitats, thriving in well-drained sandy soils. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
