Ardisia brevipetiolata

Ardisia brevipetiolata, commonly known as the short-stemmed beautyberry, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features small, clustered white flowers that bloom in the spring, followed by bright blue berries in the fall. The leaves are elliptical, glossy, and have short petioles, giving the plant its characteristic appearance. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for shaded garden settings.

The plant is valued for its ornamental berries and is often used in landscape design.
