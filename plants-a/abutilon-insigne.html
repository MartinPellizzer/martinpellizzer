Abutilon insigne

Abutilon insigne, commonly known as the velvetleaf abutilon, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and showy, lantern-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant blooms and unique foliage. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is typically grown as an annual or tender perennial.

Abutilon insigne is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
