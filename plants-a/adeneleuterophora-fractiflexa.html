Adeneleuterophora fractiflexa

Adeneleuterophora fractiflexa, commonly known as the flexible leaf orchid, is a rare and delicate orchid species native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

The plant produces fragrant, pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, typically during the rainy season. Its unique floral structure and specific habitat requirements make it a challenging species to cultivate outside its natural range.
