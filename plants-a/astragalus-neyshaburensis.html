Astragalus neyshaburensis

Astragalus neyshaburensis, commonly known as the Neyshabur milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to Iran.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, pea-like flowers. The plant typically grows in arid and semi-arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions.

Its roots are known to have medicinal properties, making it of interest in traditional herbal medicine.
