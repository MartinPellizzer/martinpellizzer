Anthurium scabrinerve

Anthurium scabrinerve, commonly known as the rough-veined anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves with prominent, rough-textured veins. The plant produces striking spathes that can range in color from deep red to orange, often with a contrasting spadix. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.

Anthurium scabrinerve is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its vibrant foliage and unique floral structures.
