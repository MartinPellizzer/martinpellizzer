Alangium Ebenaceum Var. Tutela

Tutela alangium, scientifically known as Alangium ebenaceum var. tutela, is a rare and lesser-known plant species native to specific regions in Southeast Asia. This unique variant of the Alangium genus exhibits distinct morphological characteristics that set it apart from its relatives. Found primarily in tropical rainforests, it thrives in humid, shaded environments with rich soil. Its ornamental value and potential medicinal properties have sparked interest among botanists and herbalists. Despite its ecological importance, Tutela alangium remains under-researched and vulnerable to habitat loss.
