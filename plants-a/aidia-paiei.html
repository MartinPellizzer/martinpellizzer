Aidia paiei

Aidia paiei, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are typically clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in lowland forests or near water sources.

Due to its ornamental value, Aidia paiei is sometimes cultivated as a decorative plant in gardens and greenhouses.
