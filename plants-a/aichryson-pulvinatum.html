Aichryson pulvinatum

Aichryson pulvinatum, commonly known as the silver rose or silver star, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, rounded foliage with thick, fleshy leaves that are covered in a silvery-white wax. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation.
