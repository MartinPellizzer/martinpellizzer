Aristolochia jingiangensis

Aristolochia jingiangensis, commonly known as the Jingjiang arrowhead vine, is a member of the Aristolochiaceae family and is native to China.

It is characterized by its distinctive, heart-shaped leaves and unique, winding flowers that resemble a pipe or a horn. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are often enclosed in a complex, hollow structure. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
