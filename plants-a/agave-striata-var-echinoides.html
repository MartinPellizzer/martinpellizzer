Agave striata var. echinoides

Agave striata var. echinoides, commonly known as the spiny agave, is a succulent plant native to Mexico.

It features striking blue-gray foliage with sharp, spiny margins that provide a distinctive appearance. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with tubular, white to pale yellow flowers. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it suitable for arid climates.

Its unique morphology and ornamental value make it a popular choice for xeriscaping and rock gardens.
