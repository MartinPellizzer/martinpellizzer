Anthurium cupreonitens

Anthurium cupreonitens, commonly known as the red-tipped anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces vibrant red spathes that enclose its spadix, which is covered in tiny, bright orange flowers. It thrives in humid, warm environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium cupreonitens is popular as a decorative plant due to its ornamental foliage and unique flowering structure.
