Arachniodes chinensis var. japonica

Arachniodes chinensis var. japonica, commonly known as the Japanese spider fern, is a striking evergreen fern native to East Asia.

It features delicate, arching fronds that resemble spider legs, giving it its common name. The plant has a creeping rhizome system that allows it to spread and form dense mats. Its leaves are typically leathery and dark green, with a silvery underside that adds visual interest.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often used in ornamental horticulture for its unique appearance.
