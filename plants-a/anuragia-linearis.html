Anuragia linearis

Anuragia linearis, commonly known as the slender leaf orchid, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Anuragia linearis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
