Ardisia glanduligera

Ardisia glanduligera, commonly known as the glandular evergreen or Chinese beautyberry, is a species of flowering plant in the family Ericaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of small, white flowers.
