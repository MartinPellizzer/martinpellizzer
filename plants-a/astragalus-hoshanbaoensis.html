Astragalus hoshanbaoensis

Astragalus hoshanbaoensis, commonly known as the Hoshanbao milkvetch, is a flowering plant species native to China.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant is typically found in arid or semi-arid regions, where it thrives in sandy or rocky soils.

It plays a role in soil stabilization and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
