Anthurium barbourii

Anthurium barbourii, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the Caribbean and northern South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped, dark green leaves and its vibrant, spadix-like inflorescence that ranges in color from red to orange. The plant belongs to the Araceae family, which is known for its unique flower structures. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Anthurium barbourii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for with proper humidity and light conditions.
