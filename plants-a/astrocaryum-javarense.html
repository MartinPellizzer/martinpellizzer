Astrocaryum javarense

Astrocaryum javarense, commonly known as the Java palm, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a member of the Arecaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and pinnate leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that develop into edible fruits with a sweet, coconut-like flavor. Its fruits are often consumed raw or used in traditional cuisines.

Astrocaryum javarense is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in tropical gardens.
