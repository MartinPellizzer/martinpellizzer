Adromischus coleorum

Adromischus coleorum, commonly known as the "moonstone plant," is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, triangular leaves that are often tinged with red or purple, especially in full sun. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the spring. It is drought-tolerant and prefers well-draining soil, making it ideal for rock gardens or containers.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
