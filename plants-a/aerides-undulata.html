Aerides undulata

Aerides undulata, commonly known as the wavy orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a pseudobulb and linear leaves. The plant produces pendulous flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. It prefers shady, humid environments and well-draining soil.

Aerides undulata is appreciated for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation.
