Armeria bubanii

Armeria bubanii, commonly known as the mountain sandwort, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically forms dense mats of foliage. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pinkish flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is often found in alpine environments.

Due to its hardy nature, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens with suitable conditions.
