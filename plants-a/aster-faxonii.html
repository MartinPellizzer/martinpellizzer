Aster faxonii

Aster faxonii, commonly known as the Faxon's aster, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and medicinal properties. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces showy, violet-blue flowers in the fall. It thrives in moist, wooded areas and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.

Aster faxonii is also recognized for its potential in traditional herbal medicine.
