Acrostichum hybridum var. vulcani

Acrostichum hybridum var. vulcani, commonly known as the volcanic fern, is a variant of the hybrid fern species native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its large, leathery fronds that can grow up to several feet in length. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found near water sources in its natural habitat. Its spores are produced on the undersides of its leaves, contributing to its reproductive cycle.

This variant is notable for its adaptability to volcanic soils, which are typically nutrient-rich and well-draining.
