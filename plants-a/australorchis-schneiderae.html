Australorchis schneiderae

Australorchis schneiderae, commonly known as the Schneider's Austral Orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the eastern regions of Australia.

It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and heathlands. The plant features a slender, erect stem with several elongated, linear leaves. Its flowers are small, greenish-yellow, and clustered in a spike, often with a subtle fragrance.

This species is noted for its resilience and ability to thrive in nutrient-poor soils.
