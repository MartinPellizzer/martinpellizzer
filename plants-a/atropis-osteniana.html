Atropis osteniana

Atropis osteniana, commonly known as the Ostenian starflower, is a flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Andes in South America.

It is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale purple. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in cool, temperate climates. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often covered in a fine layer of silvery hair.

Atropis osteniana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens for its unique floral display.
