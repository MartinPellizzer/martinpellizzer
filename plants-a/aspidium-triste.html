Aspidium triste

It is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds that are typically green and sometimes tinged with a pale hue. Its reproductive structures, including sori, are located on the underside of the fronds, and it produces spores for propagation.

Aspidium triste is often found in rocky or forested areas, contributing to the ecological diversity of its habitat.
