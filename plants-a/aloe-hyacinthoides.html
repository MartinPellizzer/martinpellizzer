Aloe hyacinthoides

Aloe hyacinthoides, commonly known as the Cape aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, sword-shaped leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with clusters of tubular, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow to orange. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Aloe hyacinthoides is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
