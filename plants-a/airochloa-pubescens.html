Airochloa pubescens

Airochloa pubescens, commonly known as the hairy grass, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family Poaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas, particularly in areas with moist, well-drained soils. Airochloa pubescens is often found in open habitats such as grasslands, forest edges, and disturbed soils, where it thrives in full sun to partial shade.
