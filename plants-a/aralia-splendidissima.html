Aralia splendidissima

Aralia splendidissima, commonly known as the Japanese aralia, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features compound leaves that are pinnately arranged, with each leaflet being ovate and serrated. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers in umbellate clusters, which are followed by bright red berries. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade.

Its ornamental value comes from its attractive foliage and unique fruiting display.
