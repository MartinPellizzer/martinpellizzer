Acaena elongata subvar. pergrandis

Acaena elongata subvar. pergrandis, commonly known as the large-flowered spinster, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has small, spiny bracts surrounding its flowers, which are typical of the genus Acaena. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and have a rough texture.

This subshrub is often found in coastal and montane environments, where it thrives in well-drained soils.
