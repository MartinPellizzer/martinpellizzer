Ananassa sativa var. rubrolineata

Ananassa sativa var. rubrolineata, commonly known as the red-line pineapple, is a rare and ornamental variant of the pineapple plant.

It is characterized by its striking red and yellow striped leaves, which give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces a pineapple-like fruit, though it is often smaller and less sweet than the common variety. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil.

This variety is prized for its aesthetic value in horticulture and as a unique addition to botanical gardens.
