Agrostis spica-venti f. violacea

Agrostis spica-venti f. violacea, commonly known as the violet spike grass, is a variant of the spike grass species characterized by its striking violet-colored inflorescence.

It is a perennial grass that typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows and along roadsides. The plant features slender, erect stems with dense clusters of small, spike-like flowers. Its foliage is narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue.

This ornamental grass is valued for its aesthetic appeal and is often used in landscaping for its visual interest.
