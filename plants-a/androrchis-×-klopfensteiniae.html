Androrchis × klopfensteiniae

Androrchis × klopfensteiniae, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a cross between Androrchis macrantha and Androrchis luteus.

It is a terrestrial orchid native to the mountainous regions of Central and Eastern Europe. The plant produces tall, slender stems with clustered, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb nutrients from the forest floor.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in deciduous woodlands.
