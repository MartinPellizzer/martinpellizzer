Aloe pustuligemma

Aloe pustuligemma, commonly known as the bumpy aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that are covered in small, raised bumps or tubercles. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers that bloom in summer, adding ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
