Agoseris gracilens

Agoseris gracilens, commonly known as slender agoseris, is a perennial herb native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. Its leaves are narrow and linear, and its flowers are small, yellowish, and clustered in a flat-topped inflorescence.

Agoseris gracilens is often found in disturbed soils and is adapted to arid environments.
