Adiantum capillus-veneris var. magnificum

Adiantum capillus-veneris var. magnificum, commonly known as the "Maiden's Tresses," is a striking fern characterized by its delicate, lace-like fronds.

It belongs to the family Adiantaceae and is native to regions of Southeast Asia, including India and Sri Lanka. The plant features bipinnate leaves that are finely divided, giving it a feathery appearance. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found near water sources.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor and garden cultivation.
