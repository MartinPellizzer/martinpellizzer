Agrostis praticola

Agrostis praticola, commonly known as fine leaf bluegrass, is a cool-season grass native to North America.

It is characterized by its slender, blue-green blades and tufted growth habit. This plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in lawns, meadows, and along stream banks. It has a dense root system that helps prevent soil erosion.

Agrostis praticola is valued for its fine texture and ability to thrive in shaded conditions.
