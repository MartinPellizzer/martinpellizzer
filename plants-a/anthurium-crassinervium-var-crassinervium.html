Anthurium crassinervium var. crassinervium

Anthurium crassinervium var. crassinervium, commonly known as the broad-veined anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves with prominent, dark green veins. The plant produces striking spadices, which are typically deep red or orange, surrounded by a showy spathe. It thrives in humid, warm environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical landscapes.
