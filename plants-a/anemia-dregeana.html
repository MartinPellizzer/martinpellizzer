Anemia dregeana

Anemia dregeana, commonly known as the flame violet, is a striking plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range from red to orange and yellow, often with a dark purple or black center. The plant has lance-shaped, dark green leaves that are slightly hairy to the touch. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Anemia dregeana is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
