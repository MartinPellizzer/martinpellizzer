Alternanthera amabilis var. tricolor

Alternanthera amabilis var. tricolor, commonly known as the tri-colorAlternanthera, is a strikingly colorful aquatic plant native to South America.

It features vibrant leaves with a combination of red, pink, and green hues, making it a popular choice for aquariums. The plant thrives in warm, well-lit environments with moderate to high water flow. It is easy to care for and does not require frequent pruning, though it benefits from regular fertilization.

Its unique coloration and ornamental appeal make it a favorite among aquascapers.
