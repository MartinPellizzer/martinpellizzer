Aspidium filix-mas var. goldieanum

Aspidium filix-mas var. goldieanum, commonly known as the Goldie's shield fern, is a variant of the common shield fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is a perennial fern characterized by its distinctive, shield-shaped fronds that are typically dark green with a slightly glossy texture. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and prefers well-draining, rich soil. Its fronds are divided into smaller, lance-shaped segments, giving it a lush, feathery appearance.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
