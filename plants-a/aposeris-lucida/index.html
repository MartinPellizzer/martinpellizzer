Aposeris Lucida

Aposeris lucida is a striking member of the Amaryllidaceae family known for its vibrant, bell-shaped flowers. Native to southern Africa, this plant thrives in sunny, well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy environments. Its bright yellow to orange blooms appear in late summer, adding a splash of color to the landscape. The plant is prized for its ornamental value and is commonly cultivated in gardens and botanical collections. Despite its beauty, Aposeris lucida is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal practices.
