Athyrium barbae

Athyrium barbae, commonly known as the Barbae fern, is a species of flowering plant in the family Athyriaceae.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia, particularly in countries like Thailand and Vietnam. The plant is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds that are typically bioluminescent under certain conditions. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological significance, it is a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists.
