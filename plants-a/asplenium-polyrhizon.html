Asplenium polyrhizon

Asplenium polyrhizon, commonly known as the zigzag fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Aspleniaceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive, zigzagging fronds that give it its common name. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its rhizomes are wiry and often covered with small, scale-like structures.

Asplenium polyrhizon is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and ability to thrive in low-light conditions.
