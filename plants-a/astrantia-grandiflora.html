Astrantia grandiflora

Astrantia grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered aster, is a herbaceous perennial native to eastern Europe and western Asia.

It features showy, pink to purple flower heads that bloom in late summer to early autumn. The plant has basal leaves that are typically heart-shaped or ovate with toothed edges. Its flowering stems are usually unbranched and can grow up to 60 cm tall.

Astrantia grandiflora is well-suited for moist, shady garden environments and is often used in ornamental landscaping.
