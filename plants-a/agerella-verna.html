Agerella verna

Agerella verna, commonly known as the spring beauty, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It typically grows in moist, shaded woodlands and rocky slopes. The plant features delicate, clustered flowers that bloom in early spring, often with a pink or white color. Its leaves are basal, heart-shaped, and covered in fine hairs.

Agerella verna is known for its early blooming habit, making it an important indicator plant for the arrival of spring.
