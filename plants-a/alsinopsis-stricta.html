Alsinopsis stricta

Alsinopsis stricta, commonly known as the rigid alsin, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its upright, branching stems and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in spikes, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grassland or rocky habitats.

Alsinopsis stricta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
