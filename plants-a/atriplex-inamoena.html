Atriplex inamoena

Atriplex inamoena, commonly known as desert saltbush, is a shrub native to arid and semi-arid regions of North America.

It is characterized by its gray-green, succulent leaves that help reduce water loss in harsh environments. The plant produces small, greenish flowers arranged in clusters, which are typically wind-pollinated. It thrives in saline soils and is often found in desert scrub and coastal dunes.

Atriplex inamoena plays a vital role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various desert wildlife.
