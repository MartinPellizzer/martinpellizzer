Adesmia pseudogrisea

Adesmia pseudogrisea, commonly known as the silverleaf burr legume, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery pubescence. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in racemose inflorescences, followed by pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky soils.

Adesmia pseudogrisea is adapted to withstand drought conditions and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
