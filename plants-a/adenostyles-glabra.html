Adenostyles glabra

Adenostyles glabra, commonly known as the smooth adenostyle, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant features slender, upright stems and small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring. Its leaves are linear and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its drought-resistant nature.

Adenostyles glabra is often found in dry, open habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
