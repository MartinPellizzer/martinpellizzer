Acer Negundo Var. Violaceum

Negundo maple, scientifically known as Acer negundo var. violaceum, is a striking variant of the common negundo maple. This tree is native to parts of Asia and is known for its unique purple foliage that adds a vibrant touch to the landscape. Its delicate, lace-like leaves turn a striking shade of violet in the fall, making it a popular choice for ornamental planting. The plant thrives in moist environments and is often found near water bodies. With its distinctive appearance and adaptability, it is a fascinating subject for both botanists and garden enthusiasts.
