Androsace hirsuta

Androsace hirsuta, commonly known as the hairy androsace, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the family Primulaceae and is characterized by its dense, cushion-like growth habit. Due to its ornamental value and ease of cultivation, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and shady borders.
