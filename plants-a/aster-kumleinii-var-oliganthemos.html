Aster kumleinii var. oliganthemos

Aster kumleinii var. oliganthemos, commonly known as the narrow-leaved aster, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its slender stems and small, daisy-like flowers that bloom in late summer. The leaves are typically narrow and gray-green, with a fuzzy texture. This variety is part of the Asteraceae family, which includes many other flowering plants with composite flower heads.

It thrives in dry, rocky habitats and is often found in mountainous regions.
