Austromyrtus lasioclada

Austromyrtus lasioclada, commonly known as the slender myrtle, is a shrub native to eastern Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family, which includes eucalyptus and tea trees. The plant has small, ovate leaves that are typically covered in a fine, whitish bloom. Its flowers are usually white or pink and are borne in clusters.

Austromyrtus lasioclada is often found in coastal or sandy soils and is adapted to tolerate salt spray.
