Alyxia erythrosperma var. samoensis

Alyxia erythrosperma var. samoensis, commonly known as the Samoan coral tree, is a flowering plant native to the Pacific Islands.

It is characterized by its distinctive coral-like inflorescences and evergreen foliage. The plant thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in coastal areas or on rocky slopes. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, and it produces small, red berries that are an important food source for local wildlife.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in its native habitats.
