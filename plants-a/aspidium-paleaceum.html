Aspidium paleaceum

Aspidium paleaceum, commonly known as the pale fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped fronds that are typically light green in color. The plant produces sporangia on the undersides of its leaves, which are clustered in sori, a common feature among ferns. Aspidium paleaceum thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories.

Its reproductive structures are essential for its propagation and ecological role in woodland ecosystems.
