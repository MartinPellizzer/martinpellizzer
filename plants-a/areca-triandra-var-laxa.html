Areca triandra var. laxa

Areca triandra var. laxa, commonly known as the slender areca palm, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and pinnately compound leaves that are often feather-like in appearance. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences with both male and female flowers, typical of the Arecaceae family. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Its foliage provides shade and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
