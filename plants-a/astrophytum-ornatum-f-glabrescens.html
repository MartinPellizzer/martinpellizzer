Astrophytum ornatum f. glabrescens

Astrophytum ornatum f. glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth-leafed star cactus, is a rare and unique cactus species native to the Sonoran Desert.

It is characterized by its short, columnar stems that are typically covered with a smooth, waxy coating, distinguishing it from other members of the Astrophytum genus. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the spring and summer months, which are often followed by red or orange fruits. Its low-growing form and compact size make it a popular choice for rock gardens and arid landscapes.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is often cultivated in controlled environments to ensure its survival.
