Adenophora forrestii var. handeliana

Adenophora forrestii var. handeliana, commonly known as the Yunnan bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to China.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped, blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as mountain forests. Its leaves are lanceolate and hairy, while its stems are erect and often branched.
