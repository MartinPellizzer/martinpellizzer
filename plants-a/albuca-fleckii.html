Albuca fleckii

Albuca fleckii, commonly known as the Fleck's albuca, is a bulbous plant native to southern Africa.

It produces clusters of narrow, grass-like leaves and spikes of white to pale yellow flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes. It is known for its drought tolerance and ability to bloom reliably in mild climates.

Albuca fleckii is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
