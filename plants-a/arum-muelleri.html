Arum muelleri

Arum muelleri, commonly known as Mueller's arum, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its striking, arrow-shaped leaves and a spatulate inflorescence. The plant produces a spadix surrounded by a colorful, petal-like bract, which is typical of arum species. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Its unique morphology makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
