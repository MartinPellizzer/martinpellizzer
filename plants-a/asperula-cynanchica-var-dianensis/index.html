Asperula Cynanchica Var. Dianensis

Asperula cynanchica var. dianensis is a lesser-known but fascinating member of the Rubiaceae family. Native to the mountainous regions of eastern Europe and western Asia, this plant thrives in cool, shaded environments. Known for its delicate, fragrant flowers and evergreen foliage, it is a popular choice for gardeners seeking a hardy yet ornamental plant. Its ability to bloom throughout the growing season makes it a valuable addition to woodland gardens. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively obscure in mainstream horticultural circles.
