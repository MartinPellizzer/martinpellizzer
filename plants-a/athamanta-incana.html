Athamanta incana

Athamanta incana, commonly known as the silverleaf daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and bright yellow daisy-like flowers. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family and is known for its drought tolerance. It typically grows in well-drained soils and prefers full sun.

Athamanta incana is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and low maintenance requirements.
