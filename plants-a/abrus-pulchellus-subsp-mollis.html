Abrus pulchellus subsp. mollis

Abrus pulchellus subsp.

mollis, commonly known as the silky velvet bean, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Southeast Asia. It is characterized by its distinctive, showy red and black ornamental pods that contain toxic seeds.

The plant belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and produces a strong, unpleasant odor when the pods mature. Its hairy, velvety leaves and stems give it a unique tactile appearance.

Due to the toxicity of its seeds, it is important to handle the plant with care.
