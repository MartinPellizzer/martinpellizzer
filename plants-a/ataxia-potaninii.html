Ataxia potaninii

Ataxia potaninii, commonly known as the Chinese bellflower, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped flowers that range in color from blue to purple. The plant typically grows in moist environments such as forests and stream banks. Its leaves are opposite and ovate, with a rough texture.
