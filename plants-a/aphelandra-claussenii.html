Aphelandra claussenii

Aphelandra claussenii, commonly known as the golden flame plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the Caribbean.

It is characterized by its striking, elongated, lance-shaped leaves that are a deep green with a metallic sheen. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant, white flowers that bloom in the spring and summer months. Its ornamental value lies in its vibrant foliage and showy inflorescences.

It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
