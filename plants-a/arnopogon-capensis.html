Arnopogon capensis

Arnopogon capensis, commonly known as the Cape arrowgrass, is a perennial grass native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and along coastal areas.

Arnopogon capensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
