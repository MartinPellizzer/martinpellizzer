Alaticaulia mayaycu

Alaticaulia mayaycu, commonly known as the Mayan tree, is a species native to the tropical regions of Central America.

It is a deciduous tree that can grow up to 20 meters tall, featuring a straight trunk and a broad, rounded crown. The leaves are alternate, simple, and ovate, with a glossy green upper surface and a lighter underside. Its flowers are small and clustered, typically white or pale yellow, and it produces woody capsules containing several seeds.
