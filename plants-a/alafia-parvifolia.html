Alafia parvifolia

Alafia parvifolia, commonly known as the small-leaved alafia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is a member of the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and small, lance-shaped leaves. Due to its hardiness and aesthetic appeal, it is often cultivated in shaded or semi-shaded areas in gardens.
