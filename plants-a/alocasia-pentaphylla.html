Alocasia pentaphylla

Alocasia pentaphylla, commonly known as the Chinese Evergreen, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length. The plant has a rhizomatous root system, which allows it to store moisture and nutrients efficiently. It thrives in shady, humid environments with well-draining soil.

Alocasia pentaphylla is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and relatively low maintenance requirements.
