Anthurium neei

Anthurium neei, commonly known as the Neei Anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive inflorescence consisting of a spadix and spathe. The plant features broad, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. Its flowers are usually small and inconspicuous, with the showy spathe serving as the primary ornamental feature.

Anthurium neei is valued in horticulture for its aesthetic appeal and ability to thrive in humid, shaded environments.
