Athyrium koryoense

Athyrium koryoense, commonly known as the Korean shield fern, is a species of fern native to eastern Asia, particularly Korea and parts of China.

It belongs to the family Athyriaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, shield-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky slopes. Its leaves are bi-lobed and have a serrated edge, with a dark green coloration.

Athyrium koryoense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its elegant foliage.
