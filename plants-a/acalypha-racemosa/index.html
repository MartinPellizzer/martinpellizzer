Acalypha Racemosa

Acalypha racemosa is a unique flowering plant known for its striking foliage and ornamental value. Native to tropical regions, it thrives in warm, humid climates with plenty of sunlight. Its leaves are often colorful, featuring shades of red, orange, and yellow, making it a popular choice for gardeners. The plant produces clusters of small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in summer. Despite its beauty, it requires careful attention to soil and watering conditions to flourish.
