Antidesma rhynchophyllum

Antidesma rhynchophyllum, commonly known as the African starfruit or false starfruit, is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub or tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its opposite, simple, elliptic to ovate leaves. The plant is valued for its ornamental value and has some traditional uses in local medicine.
