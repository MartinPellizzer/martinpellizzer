Alstroemeria haemantha

Alstroemeria haemantha, commonly known as the red alstroemeria, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that come in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant has strap-like leaves that are typically green and arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Alstroemeria haemantha is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and floral arrangements.
