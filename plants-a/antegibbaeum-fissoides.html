Antegibbaeum fissoides

Antegibbaeum fissoides, commonly known as the fissured stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating to reduce water loss. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and arid environments.

Due to its drought tolerance and ornamental value, it is a popular choice for xeriscaping.
