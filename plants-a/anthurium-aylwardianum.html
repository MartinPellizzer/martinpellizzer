Anthurium aylwardianum

Anthurium aylwardianum, commonly known as the Red Velvet Anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety red spathes that enclose the bright yellow spadix, creating a dramatic floral display. The plant produces heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a glossy texture. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium aylwardianum is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental value and air-purifying properties.
