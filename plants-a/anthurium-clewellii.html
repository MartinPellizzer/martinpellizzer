Anthurium clewellii

Anthurium clewellii, commonly known as the Clewell's anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically deep green with a glossy texture. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-shaped inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, which can range from red to orange. It thrives in humid, warm environments with bright, indirect light.

Anthurium clewellii is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its aesthetic appeal and air-purifying qualities.
