Aquilegia columbiana

Aquilegia columbiana, commonly known as the Columbian columbine, is a herbaceous perennial native to the western United States.

It features striking blue to purple spurred flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has palmately divided leaves with a velvety texture and a deep green color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.

Aquilegia columbiana is valued in gardens for its ornamental beauty and ecological benefits to pollinators.
