Antirrhinum Linkianum Subsp. Cirrhigerum

Antirrhinum linkianum subsp. cirrhigerum is a rare and intriguing member of the plant family Plantaginaceae. Native to the high-altitude regions of eastern Africa, this species thrives in cool, moist environments. Its striking flowers, often in shades of pink and white, make it a standout in its ecosystem. Despite its beauty, it remains poorly studied due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity. This plant offers valuable insights into plant adaptation and biodiversity in mountainous regions.
