Afrothismia pusilla

Afrothismia pusilla, commonly known as the dwarf African cycad, is a small, slow-growing cycad native to South Africa.

It has a stout, unbranched trunk and pinnate leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces both male and female cones, which are essential for its reproduction. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Due to its unique appearance and conservation status, it is a valued ornamental plant in horticulture.
