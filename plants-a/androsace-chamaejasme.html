Androsace chamaejasme

Androsace chamaejasme, commonly known as the alpine mouse-ear, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and western Asia.

It belongs to the Primulaceae family and is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit.
