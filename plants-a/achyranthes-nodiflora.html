Achyranthes nodiflora

Achyranthes nodiflora, commonly known as the clustered knotweed, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Amaranthaceae and is characterized by its erect, herbaceous stems and alternate, ovate leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers in clusters, which are typically arranged in inflorescences at the nodes of the stems. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and waste areas.

Its botanical features make it a notable species in ecological studies and traditional medicine.
