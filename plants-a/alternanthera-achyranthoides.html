Alternanthera achyranthoides

Alternanthera achyranthoides, commonly known as red-stemmed Alternanthera, is a species of flowering plant in the family Amaranthaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America and is characterized by its striking red or purple stems and foliage. The plant typically grows as an annual herb, reaching heights of up to 60 cm, and features small, clustered flowers that are usually pink or white. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its vibrant coloration.

The leaves are usually lance-shaped and can vary in color from green to reddish, depending on light conditions.
