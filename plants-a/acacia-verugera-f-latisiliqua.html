Acacia verugera f. latisiliqua

Acacia verugera f. latisiliqua, commonly known as the broad-leaved acacia, is a shrub or small tree native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, spreading foliage and distinctive, flat, fan-shaped leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers in summer, which are clustered in spherical heads. It is well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a resilient species in harsh climates.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in regions with suitable climates.
