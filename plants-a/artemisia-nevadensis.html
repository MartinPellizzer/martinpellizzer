Artemisia nevadensis

Artemisia nevadensis, commonly known as silver bluestem, is a perennial herb native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and compact growth habit, often forming dense mats. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers in late summer, which are borne on slender, branching stalks. It thrives in dry, rocky soils and is commonly found in sagebrush steppe and desert environments.

Artemisia nevadensis plays a vital role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various wildlife species.
