Anthurium coleorrhiza

Anthurium coleorrhiza, commonly known as the red-tipped anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are deep green with a reddish or purplish hue on the underside. The plant produces vibrant red spadices, which are the modified leaves that enclose the small, yellowish flowers. Anthurium coleorrhiza thrives in humid, warm environments with bright, indirect light.

Its unique appearance and ornamental value make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
