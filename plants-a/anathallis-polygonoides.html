Anathallis polygonoides

Anathallis polygonoides, commonly known as the Brazilian star orchid, is a flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments within tropical rainforests. Its inflorescence consists of several showy, violet or purple flowers that bloom sequentially.

Anathallis polygonoides is noted for its unique morphology and is often found in association with specific mycorrhizal fungi.
