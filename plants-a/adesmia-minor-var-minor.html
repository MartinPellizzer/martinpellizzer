Adesmia minor var. minor

Adesmia minor var. minor, commonly known as the small adesmia or lesser adesmia, is a small shrub native to parts of South America.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, pea-like flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid conditions. It produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and lesser-known species within the Fabaceae family.
