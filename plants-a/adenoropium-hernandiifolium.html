Adenoropium hernandiifolium

It has small, leathery leaves that are opposite and elliptic in shape, often with a waxy coating. The plant produces small, white or greenish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its berries are typically red or yellow and contain sticky seeds that adhere to birds for dispersal.
