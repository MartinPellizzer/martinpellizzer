Acaena argentea var. gracillima

Acaena argentea var. gracillima, commonly known as silverweed, is a low-growing perennial plant native to New Zealand.

It features small, silver-gray leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. Its dense growth habit makes it an effective ground cover in ecological restoration projects.
