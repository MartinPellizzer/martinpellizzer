Anthurium glaziovii

Anthurium glaziovii, commonly known as the Glaziov's arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are often a deep green with a metallic sheen. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers surrounded by a spathe, which is typically a vibrant red or orange. It thrives in humid, warm environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.

Anthurium glaziovii is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
