Aneilema nudiflorum var. terminale

Aneilema nudiflorum var. terminale, commonly known as the terminal aneilema, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features slender, upright stems that are often hairy, and its leaves are simple, alternate, and elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color, arranged in clusters at the ends of the stems. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its unique foliage.
