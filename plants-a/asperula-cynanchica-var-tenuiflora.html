Asperula cynanchica var. tenuiflora

Asperula cynanchica var. tenuiflora, commonly known as slenderflower asperula, is a perennial shrub native to southern Europe.

It features small, oval leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. The plant produces delicate, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, often appearing in late spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental value make it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
