Alkanna graeca var. versicolor

Alkanna graeca var. versicolor, commonly known as the rainbow alkanna, is a striking perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its vibrant, multi-colored flowers that range from red to orange and yellow, creating a visually stunning display. The plant produces distinctive, spindle-shaped rootstocks that are often used in traditional medicine. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern.

Alkanna graeca var. versicolor thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.
