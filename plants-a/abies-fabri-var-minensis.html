Abies fabri var. minensis

Abies fabri var. minensis, commonly known as the miniature fir, is a rare and compact variant of the Fabri fir.

It is characterized by its dense, pyramidal shape and short, stiff needles that are typically a deep green. Native to the mountainous regions of China, this plant thrives in cool, moist climates with well-drained soil. Its slow growth rate makes it an ideal choice for small garden spaces or container cultivation.

Due to its limited distribution and unique morphology, it is considered a valuable species in botanical collections.
