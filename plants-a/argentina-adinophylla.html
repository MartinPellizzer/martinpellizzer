Argentina adinophylla

Argentina adinophylla, commonly known as the Argentine feather grass, is a member of the Poaceae family.

It is a low-growing perennial grass native to the grasslands of Argentina. The plant features slender, wiry stems and narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny florets.

Argentina adinophylla is adapted to dry, open habitats and is often found in disturbed soils.
