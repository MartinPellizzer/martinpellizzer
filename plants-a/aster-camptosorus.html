Aster camptosorus

Aster camptosorus, commonly known as the bent-stem aster, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and woodlands. Its stems are often bent or twisted, giving it its common name.

The flowers bloom in the fall, producing purple to pink ray florets surrounded by yellow disk florets.
