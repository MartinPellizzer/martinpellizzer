Arenaria emarginata var. macrosperma

Arenaria emarginata var. macrosperma, commonly known as the large-seeded sandwort, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Caryophyllaceae.

It is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit and produces small, white or pink flowers. The plant thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in coastal or arid environments. Its leaves are typically narrow and fleshy, adapted to conserve water.

The variety macrosperma is distinguished by its larger seeds compared to other varieties of Arenaria emarginata.
