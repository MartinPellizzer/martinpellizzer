Amorphophallus saraburensis

Amorphophallus saraburensis, commonly known as the Sarabura elephant's foot, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

The plant produces a single, showy inflorescence with white or pink spathes and yellow spadices, typical of the Araceae family.
