Acronychia simplicifolia

Acronychia simplicifolia, commonly known as the smooth-leaved acronychia, is a tropical tree native to northeastern Australia.

It is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are typically ovate to elliptic in shape. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by woody, spherical fruits. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
