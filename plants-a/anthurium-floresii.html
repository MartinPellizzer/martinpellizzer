Anthurium floresii

Anthurium floresii, commonly known as the Flores Anthurium, is a striking tropical plant native to the island of Flores in Indonesia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped leaves and spadix inflorescence. The plant produces clusters of small, inconspicuous flowers surrounded by a colorful spathe, which is often the most noticeable part of the plant. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Anthurium floresii is popular among plant enthusiasts for its ornamental value and unique foliage.
