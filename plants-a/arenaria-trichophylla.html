Arenaria trichophylla

Arenaria trichophylla, commonly known as the silky sandwort, is a low-growing flowering plant native to arctic and alpine regions.

It thrives in harsh environments such as rocky soils and sandy substrates, showcasing its adaptability to extreme conditions. The plant features small, rounded leaves that are covered in fine, silky hairs, which help reduce water loss. Its flowers are typically white or pale pink, arranged in clusters at the ends of stems.

Arenaria trichophylla plays a role in stabilizing soil in its native habitats and is often found in tundra and mountainous areas.
