Anthurium fatoense

Anthurium fatoense, commonly known as the Fatoense arrowhead, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-shaped inflorescences surrounded by colorful, modified leaves called spatulae. It thrives in high humidity and indirect light, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique foliage and exotic appearance make it a sought-after ornamental plant.
