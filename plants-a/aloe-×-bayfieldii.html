Aloe × bayfieldii

Aloe × bayfieldii, commonly known as the hybrid aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with white or yellowish markings. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with small, tubular flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. Aloe × bayfieldii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in arid conditions.

It requires well-draining soil and minimal water, making it suitable for xeriscaping.
