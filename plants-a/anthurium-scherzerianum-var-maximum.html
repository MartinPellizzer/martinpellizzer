Anthurium scherzerianum var. maximum

Anthurium scherzerianum var. maximum, commonly known as the giant anthurium, is a striking tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 3 feet in length, often with a deep green color and a velvety texture. The plant produces vibrant red spadices, which are the spiky, clustered inflorescences that give it its dramatic appearance. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture in its soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical landscapes.
