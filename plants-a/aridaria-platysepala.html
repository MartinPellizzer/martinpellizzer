Aridaria platysepala

Aridaria platysepala, commonly known as the flat-sepaled aridaria, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions.

It is characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves that help it conserve water in dry environments. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pale green or yellowish in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to withstand high temperatures and low precipitation.

Aridaria platysepala plays a role in stabilizing sandy soils and providing habitat for desert wildlife.
