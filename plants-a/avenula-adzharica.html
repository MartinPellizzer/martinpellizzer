Avenula adzharica

Avenula adzharica, commonly known as the Caucasian vetch, is a flowering plant native to the Caucasus region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in dry, rocky soils. The plant features slender stems and small, trifoliate leaves. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, appearing in clusters.

Avenula adzharica is often found in open habitats such as steppes and forest edges.
