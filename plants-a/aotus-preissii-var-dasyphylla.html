Aotus preissii var. dasyphylla

Aotus preissii var. dasyphylla, commonly known as the rough-leaved aotus, is a variant of the aotus plant native to specific regions of South America.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that are typically rough to the touch, providing a unique texture. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in tropical rainforests. Its foliage plays a crucial role in its photosynthetic efficiency and moisture retention.

The botanical features of this variant make it distinct from other aotus species, contributing to its ecological niche.
