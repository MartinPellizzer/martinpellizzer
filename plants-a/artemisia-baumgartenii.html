Artemisia baumgartenii

Artemisia baumgartenii, commonly known as the Tibetan wormwood, is a perennial shrub native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its aromatic, silver-gray foliage and small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant thrives in cold, dry environments and is often found in alpine meadows and rocky slopes. Its leaves contain essential oils that contribute to its strong, minty scent.

Artemisia baumgartenii is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional Tibetan medicine.
