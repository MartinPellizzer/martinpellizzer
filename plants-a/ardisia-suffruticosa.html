Ardisia suffruticosa

Ardisia suffruticosa, commonly known as the red-berried evergreen, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features small, glossy, dark green leaves and produces bright red berries that are visually striking. The plant has a compact growth habit and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in shaded to partially shaded environments and prefers moist, well-drained soil.

Ardisia suffruticosa is also valued for its ability to attract birds due to its edible berries.
