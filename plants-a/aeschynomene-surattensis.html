Aeschynomene surattensis

Aeschynomene surattensis, commonly known as Surat mesquite, is a shrub or small tree native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, pea-like flowers. The plant is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a resilient species in challenging climates.

It plays a role in soil stabilization and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects due to its hardy nature.
