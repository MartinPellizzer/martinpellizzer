Astrophytum tulense

Astrophytum tulense, commonly known as the "Cactus of the Desert," is a species of cactus native to the Sonoran Desert.

It is characterized by its short, thick stems that are ribbed and covered in white, woolly spines. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months, which are typically short-lived but striking. It is a succulent plant, capable of storing water in its stem to survive in arid conditions.

Astrophytum tulense is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and hardy nature.
