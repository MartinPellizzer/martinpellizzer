Acisanthera nana

Acisanthera nana, commonly known as the dwarf acisanthera, is a small, herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It typically grows to a height of only a few inches, with compact foliage and delicate, clustered flowers. The plant prefers well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers. Its flowers are usually white or pale yellow, adding a subtle elegance to its appearance.

Due to its small size and ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
