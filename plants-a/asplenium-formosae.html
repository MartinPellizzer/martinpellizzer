Asplenium formosae

Asplenium formosae, commonly known as the Formosa spleenwort, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Aspleniaceae and is characterized by its small, compact growth habit. The plant typically has dark green, leathery fronds with a serrated or toothed margin. It thrives in shaded, moist environments, often found in forest understories.

Its reproductive structures, including sporangia, are located on the underside of the fronds, typical of ferns.
