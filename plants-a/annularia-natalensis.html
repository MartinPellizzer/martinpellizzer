Annularia natalensis

Annularia natalensis, commonly known as Natal ringweed, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves arranged in a rosette pattern, which help it store water in arid environments. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring, adding a delicate touch to its appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Due to its unique morphology and adaptability, it is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
