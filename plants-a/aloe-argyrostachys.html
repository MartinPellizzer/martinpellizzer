Aloe argyrostachys

Aloe argyrostachys, commonly known as the silver aloe or woolly aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, silver-gray foliage that features soft, woolly textures on the leaves. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow flowers during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil with full sun exposure.

Aloe argyrostachys is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
