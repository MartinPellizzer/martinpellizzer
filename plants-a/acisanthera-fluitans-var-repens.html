Acisanthera fluitans var. repens

Acisanthera fluitans var. repens, commonly known as floating bladderwort, is a aquatic plant characterized by its unique adaptations for life in water.

Its flowers are small and typically white or pale purple, arranged in clusters that emerge above the water surface.
