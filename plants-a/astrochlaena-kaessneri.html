Astrochlaena kaessneri

Astrochlaena kaessneri, commonly known as the starleaf arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive arrowhead-shaped leaves that are often glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

Due to its unique foliage, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant in shaded garden settings.
