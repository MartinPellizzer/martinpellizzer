Armeria plantaginea var. praecox

Armeria plantaginea var. praecox, commonly known as early sea thrift, is a flowering plant native to the coastal areas of the British Isles.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically reaches heights of 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features dense clusters of white or pink flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are linear and slightly succulent, adapted to withstand salty sea air and coastal conditions.

It thrives in well-drained, sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in dunes and rocky shores.
