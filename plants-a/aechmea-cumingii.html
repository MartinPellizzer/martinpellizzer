Aechmea cumingii

Aechmea cumingii, commonly known as the pineapple bromeliad, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that form a central cup, which collects water and nutrients. Aechmea cumingii is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
