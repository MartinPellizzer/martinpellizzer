Achillea × dragomanii

Achillea × dragomanii, commonly known as the hybrid yarrow, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Balkans.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its flat, composite flower heads that range in color from white to pink. The plant produces feathery, aromatic leaves that are finely divided and covered in a soft, silvery down. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in grasslands and rocky areas.

Achillea × dragomanii is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
