Aloe benishangulana

Aloe benishangulana, commonly known as the Ethiopian aloe, is a succulent plant native to the highlands of Ethiopia.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that are typically gray-green in color, often with a waxy coating. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing yellow flowers, which bloom in the spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Aloe benishangulana is valued for its hardiness and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
