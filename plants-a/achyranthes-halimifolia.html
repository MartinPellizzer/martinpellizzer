Achyranthes halimifolia

Achyranthes halimifolia, commonly known as red sandreed, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a herbaceous annual that typically grows in sandy or coastal soils. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from pink to red, which bloom in late summer. Its leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and often covered in fine hairs.

It is adapted to thrive in arid environments and is often found along dunes or in disturbed soils.
