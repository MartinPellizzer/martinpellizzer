Acalypha reticulata var. goudotiana

Acalypha reticulata var. goudotiana, commonly known as the Goudot's spurge, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, palmate leaves with a reticulated pattern, giving it a unique appearance. The plant produces small, clustered yellow flowers that are often surrounded by bracts. It is typically found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been introduced to other tropical regions for horticultural purposes.
