Asphodeline brevicaulis

Asphodeline brevicaulis, commonly known as the narrowleaf asphodel, is a perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features slender, grass-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color and covered in fine hairs. The plant produces tall, erect stems topped with clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is drought-tolerant, making it suitable for rock gardens or arid landscapes.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a desirable ornamental plant.
