Aneilema johnstonii

Aneilema johnstonii, commonly known as the Johnston's arrowroot, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive inflorescence composed of a spadix and a spathe. The plant produces strikingly colored flowers, often with a green or yellow spathe enclosing a central spadix. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in swampy or marshy areas.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
