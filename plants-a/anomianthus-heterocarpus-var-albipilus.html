Anomianthus heterocarpus var. albipilus

Anomianthus heterocarpus var. albipilus, commonly known as the white-haired anomianthus, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and daisy-like appearance. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Its unique white hairy appearance on the stems and leaves distinguishes it from other varieties within the species.
