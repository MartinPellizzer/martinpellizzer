Andromeda japonica

Andromeda japonica, commonly known as Japanese andromeda, is a deciduous shrub native to Japan and Korea.

It features small, dark green leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a leathery texture. The plant produces clusters of bell-shaped, white flowers in early spring, which are highly fragrant. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

Andromeda japonica is often used in ornamental horticulture for its attractive foliage and spring blooms.
