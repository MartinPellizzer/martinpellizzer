Antiaris toxicaria var. welwitschii

Antiaris toxicaria var. welwitschii, commonly known as the giant arrowroot or African giant star.

This variant is a large, fast-growing shrub or small tree native to the tropical regions of Africa. It is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that are typically green with a reddish or purplish tinge on the underside. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers followed by orange or red berries containing toxic seeds.

Due to its rapid growth and ornamental value, it is often cultivated in warm climates for its unique appearance.
