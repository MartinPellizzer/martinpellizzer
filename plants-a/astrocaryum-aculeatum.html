Astrocaryum aculeatum

Astrocaryum aculeatum, commonly known as the Brazilian buriti, is a tall, palm-like tree native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its smooth, gray bark and large, fan-shaped leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. The plant produces yellowish-orange fruits that contain edible seeds, which are rich in nutrients. Its roots and leaves are also utilized in local remedies for their purported health benefits.

Astrocaryum aculeatum plays an important ecological role in its habitat, providing food and shelter for various wildlife species.
