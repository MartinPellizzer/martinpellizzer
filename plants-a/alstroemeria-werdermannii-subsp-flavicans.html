Alstroemeria werdermannii subsp. flavicans

Alstroemeria werdermannii subsp.

flavicans, commonly known as the yellow-flowered alstroemeria, is a South American species native to Chile and Argentina. It is a herbaceous perennial that produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of yellow and orange.

The plant features strap-like leaves that are often variegated with white and green markings. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
