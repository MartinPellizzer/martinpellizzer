Aegilops cylindrica var. multiaristata

Aegilops cylindrica var. multiaristata, commonly known as the slender wheatgrass, is a perennial grass native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its slender, cylindrical stems and dense, tufted foliage. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous bristle-tipped florets. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed areas or along roadsides.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
