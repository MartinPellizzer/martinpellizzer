Adenogyne pachystachya

Adenogyne pachystachya, commonly known as the giant pachystachya, is a striking member of the Acanthaceae family.

It is native to the arid regions of South America, particularly in countries like Peru and Bolivia. This plant is characterized by its tall, branched stems and showy, tubular flowers that range in color from purple to red. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens that can provide the necessary heat and sunlight.
