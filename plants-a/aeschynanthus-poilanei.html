Aeschynanthus poilanei

Aeschynanthus poilanei, commonly known as the flame flower, is a tropical shrub native to West Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange, often resembling flames. The plant produces small, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. It thrives in humid, warm environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Aeschynanthus poilanei is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its vibrant floral display.
