Agrostis tenuiculmis var. recta

Agrostis tenuiculmis var. recta, commonly known as slender bentgrass, is a perennial grass species native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its fine, tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows, dunes, and coastal areas. Its flowering stems bear small, spike-like inflorescences with numerous pale green to brownish spikelets.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping or as a ground cover.
