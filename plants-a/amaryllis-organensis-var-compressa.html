Amaryllis organensis var. compressa

Amaryllis organensis var. compressa, commonly known as the compact amaryllis, is a striking plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and produces large, showy flowers in shades of red, pink, and white. The leaves are broad, strap-like, and often form a dense rosette at the base of the plant. This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.

It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
