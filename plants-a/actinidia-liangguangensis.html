Actinidia liangguangensis

Actinidia liangguangensis, commonly known as the Liangguang actinidia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Actinidiaceae.

It is native to China, specifically found in the Guangdong and Guangxi provinces. This vine produces small, greenish-white flowers and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves. The plant is often grown for its ornamental value and has potential uses in traditional medicine.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural study.
