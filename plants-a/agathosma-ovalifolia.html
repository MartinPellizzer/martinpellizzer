Agathosma ovalifolia

Agathosma ovalifolia, commonly known as African germander, is a small evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are aromatic and often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Agathosma ovalifolia is valued for its medicinal properties and ornamental appeal.
