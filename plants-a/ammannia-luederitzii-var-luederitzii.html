Ammannia luederitzii var. luederitzii

Ammannia luederitzii var. luederitzii, commonly known as the Luederitz's ammannia, is a perennial shrub native to southern Africa.

It features upright, branching stems that are often covered in fine, white hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, pink to red flowers that bloom in summer, attracting pollinators like bees and birds. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a slightly serrated margin.

This species is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a hardy plant in dry climates.
