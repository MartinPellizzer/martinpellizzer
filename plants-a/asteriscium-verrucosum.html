Asteriscium verrucosum

Asteriscium verrucosum, commonly known as the rough asterisk, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, surrounded by bracts that give it a star-like appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun, making it a suitable candidate for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Its unique morphology and ornamental value make it a desirable addition to botanical collections.
