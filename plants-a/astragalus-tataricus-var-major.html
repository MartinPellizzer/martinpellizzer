Astragalus tataricus var. major

Astragalus tataricus var. major, commonly known as the Tatarian milkvetch, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant is known for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it beneficial for soil enrichment.

It typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is valued for its medicinal properties in traditional medicine.
