Abutilon arborescens

Abutilon arborescens, commonly known as the Chinese lantern plant, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its velvety, maple-like leaves that are typically heart-shaped or ovate. The plant produces clusters of vibrant orange to red, lantern-shaped flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. These flowers are followed by inflated, papery seed pods that persist through winter.

It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.
