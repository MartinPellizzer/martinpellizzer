Asplenium simonsianum

Asplenium simonsianum, commonly known as the Simonsian holly fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Aspleniaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, particularly in countries such as Kenya and Tanzania. The plant is characterized by its compact, dense foliage and small, lance-shaped fronds that are usually dark green in color. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

The plant's rhizomes are creeping and help it spread across the forest floor, contributing to its ecological role in soil stabilization.
