Aragallus spicatus

Aragallus spicatus, commonly known as the spicate aragallus, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Polygonaceae.

It is characterized by its upright, branched stems and clusters of small, greenish-yellow flowers. Aragallus spicatus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
