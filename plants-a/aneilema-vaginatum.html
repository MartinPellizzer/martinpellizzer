Aneilema vaginatum

Aneilema vaginatum, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, tuberous roots and its attractive, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of purple, pink, and white. The plant has lance-shaped, dark green leaves that are often covered in a fine, hairy layer. It thrives in humid, warm environments and is widely cultivated as a houseplant.

Aneilema vaginatum is also valued for its ability to purify indoor air and enhance the aesthetic of indoor spaces.
