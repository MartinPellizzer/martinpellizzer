Aloysia salsoloides

Aloysia salsoloides, commonly known as silverleaf sage, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and spikes of pale blue to violet flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Aloysia salsoloides is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive appearance and drought tolerance.
