Aegopodium angelicifolium

Aegopodium angelicifolium, commonly known as angelica leaf groundsel, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in woodland edges and moist meadows. The plant features finely divided, fern-like leaves that are aromatic and often used in traditional herbal medicine. Its small yellow flowers bloom in clusters, attracting various pollinators.

The species is noted for its medicinal properties, particularly in treating respiratory and digestive ailments.
