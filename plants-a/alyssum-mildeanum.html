Alyssum mildeanum

Alyssum mildeanum, commonly known as the silverleaf alyssum, is a low-growing perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features dense clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring and summer. The plant has silver-gray foliage due to a thick layer of fine hairs, which helps it retain moisture in arid conditions. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Alyssum mildeanum is also valued for its ability to attract beneficial insects like butterflies and bees.
