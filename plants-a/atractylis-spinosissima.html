Atractylis spinosissima

Atractylis spinosissima, commonly known as the spiny Atractylis, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its spiny stems and rough, hairy leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in dense clusters, which bloom in the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or dry habitats.

Atractylis spinosissima is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
