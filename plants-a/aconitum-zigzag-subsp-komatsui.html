Aconitum zigzag subsp. komatsui

Aconitum zigzag subsp.

komatsui, commonly known as the Komatsui larkspur, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to eastern Asia. It is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers that bloom in summer.

The plant has lance-shaped leaves with a zigzag pattern along the edges, giving it its common name. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Due to its toxic alkaloids, it should be handled with care.
