Alyxia reinwardtii var. lucida

Alyxia reinwardtii var. lucida, commonly known as the shining alyxia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in humid, lowland forest environments. The plant features opposite, elliptical leaves that are usually glossy and dark green. Its flowers are small, fragrant, and typically white or pale yellow, arranged in clusters.
