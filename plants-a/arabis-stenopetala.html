Arabis stenopetala

Arabis stenopetala, commonly known as narrow-petaled rockcress, is a perennial flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other cruciferous plants. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, typically blooming in early spring. Its leaves are basal, rosette-like, and covered in a fine layer of white hairs.

Arabis stenopetala is often found in rocky or gravelly soils and is adapted to harsh alpine environments.
