Atelianthus veronicoides

Atelianthus veronicoides, commonly known as the silverleaf veronicoides, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, mat-forming growth habit. The plant features silvery-gray leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. Its small, daisy-like flowers bloom in shades of white and yellow, typically during the spring and summer months.

It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its attractive foliage and hardy nature.
