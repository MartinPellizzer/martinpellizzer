Arthrostemma hirtellum

Arthrostemma hirtellum, commonly known as the hairy arthrostemma, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Acanthaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its erect, branched stems and opposite leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
