Aloe hybrida stella

Aloe hybrida stella, commonly known as the Star Aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of fleshy, bluish-green leaves that are adorned with white to yellow marginal teeth. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing yellow or orange flowers during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with ample sunlight.

Its compact size and ornamental appearance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
