Actinodaphne forrestii

Actinodaphne forrestii, commonly known as the Yunnan laurel, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is native to southern China, particularly Yunnan Province, and is characterized by its aromatic, evergreen leaves. The plant typically grows as a small to medium-sized tree, reaching heights of up to 10 meters. Its flowers are small and clustered, often appearing in the axils of the leaves, and it produces dark, glossy berries.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is also used in traditional medicine in some regions.
