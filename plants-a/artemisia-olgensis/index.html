Artemisia Olgensis

Artemisia olgensis is a hardy perennial plant native to the cold regions of Asia. It is known for its aromatic foliage and resilience to harsh climates. This species thrives in well-drained soils and full sun exposure. Its silver-green leaves are often used in traditional medicine and herbal remedies. Artemisia olgensis remains a valuable plant for both ecological and cultural purposes.
