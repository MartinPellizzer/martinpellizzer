Aeonium × exsul

Aeonium × exsul, commonly known as the Ghost Plant, is a hybrid succulent native to the Canary Islands.

It features rosettes of fleshy, blue-green leaves that are often covered in a fine, powdery coating. The plant produces vibrant red or orange flowers during the spring or summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its striking appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a favored ornamental plant among succulent enthusiasts.
