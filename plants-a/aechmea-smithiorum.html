Aechmea smithiorum

Aechmea smithiorum, commonly known as the Smith's Bromeliad, is a species of flowering plant in the Bromeliaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that form a central cup, which collects water and nutrients. Its flowers are typically red or orange, emerging from the center of the rosette.

Aechmea smithiorum is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant foliage and long-lasting blooms.
