Asterochaete angustifolia

Asterochaete angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf aster.

It is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads and narrow, linear leaves. Native to South America, particularly Brazil, it thrives in moist, shaded environments. The plant produces small, purple to pink flowers that bloom in the summer months.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ecological benefits in garden settings.
