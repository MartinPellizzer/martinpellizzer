Anguria ovata

Anguria ovata, commonly known as the oval cucumber, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Cucurbitaceae family, which includes other cucurbits like pumpkins and melons. The plant produces elongated, oval-shaped fruits that are typically green when mature. Its leaves are broad and heart-shaped, with a rough texture.
