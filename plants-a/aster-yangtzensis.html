Aster yangtzensis

Aster yangtzensis, commonly known as the Yangtze aster, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and robust growth habits. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as mountainous regions and forest floors. Its leaves are lance-shaped with a rough texture, and its flowers bloom in shades of purple and blue.

Aster yangtzensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional medicinal uses in Chinese herbal medicine.
