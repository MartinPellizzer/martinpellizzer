Astronia intermedia

Astronia intermedia, commonly known as the white aster or white star, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers. Astronia intermedia is often found in moist, open habitats such as grasslands and wetlands.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting pollinators.
