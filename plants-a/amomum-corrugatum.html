Amomum corrugatum

Amomum corrugatum, commonly known as the rough nutmeg or zigzag ginger, is a perennial herb native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes other aromatic plants like ginger and cardamom. The plant is characterized by its rhizomatous growth and dark brown, aromatic seeds that are used in traditional medicine and cuisine. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is valued for its medicinal properties and distinctive flavor.
