Arisaema anomalum

Arisaema anomalum, commonly known as the narrowleaf arisaema, is a member of the Araceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like China, India, and Malaysia. The plant is characterized by its distinctive, narrow leaves and a striking spadix surrounded by a spathe. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions.

Arisaema anomalum is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its unique foliage and flowering structure.
