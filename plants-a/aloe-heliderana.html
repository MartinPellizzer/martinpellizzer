Aloe heliderana

Aloe heliderana, commonly known as the Cape aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, spiky leaves that are fleshy and covered in a whitish, powdery coating called bloom. The plant produces vibrant orange or yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Aloe heliderana is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional medicinal uses.
