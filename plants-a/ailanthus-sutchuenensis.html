Ailanthus sutchuenensis

Ailanthus sutchuenensis, commonly known as the Chinese empress tree, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It belongs to the Simarouba family and is characterized by its compound leaves and showy yellow flowers. The tree produces small, round fruits that are dispersed by wind. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and rapid growth rate.

However, it can become invasive in certain environments if not managed properly.
