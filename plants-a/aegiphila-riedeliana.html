Aegiphila riedeliana

Aegiphila riedeliana, commonly known as the Riedel's shrub, is a species of flowering plant native to the Andean regions of South America.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in high-altitude environments and is often found in rocky or mountainous terrain.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
