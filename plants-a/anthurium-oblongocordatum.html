Anthurium oblongocordatum

Anthurium oblongocordatum, commonly known as the heart-shaped anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically deep green with a glossy texture. The plant produces striking spadices, which are modified leaves that house the tiny, inconspicuous flowers. Anthurium oblongocordatum thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical landscapes.
