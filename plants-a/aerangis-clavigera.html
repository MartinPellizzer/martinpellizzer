Aerangis clavigera

Aerangis clavigera, commonly known as the sword orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, sword-shaped leaves and clusters of fragrant, white or pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
