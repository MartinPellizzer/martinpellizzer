Amaranthus rosengurttii

Amaranthus rosengurttii, commonly known as the Rosengurttii amaranth, is a species of flowering plant in the amaranth family.

It is native to South America, particularly Brazil, and is characterized by its vibrant red and purple flower clusters. The plant produces small, triangular seeds that are often used in traditional cuisines. It thrives in warm climates and well-drained soils, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Due to its ornamental value and nutritional benefits, it has gained attention in both horticulture and agriculture.
