Aster microcephalus var. yezoensis

Aster microcephalus var. yezoensis, commonly known as Yezo aster, is a perennial herb native to Japan.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and small, clustered blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream sides. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental qualities and ability to thrive in cool, humid climates.
