Acanthocereus floridanus

Acanthocereus floridanus, commonly known as the Florida night bloomer cactus, is a species native to the southeastern United States.

It is a columnar cactus with ribbed stems and produces white, fragrant flowers at night. The plant has spiny areoles and is adapted to arid environments. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil.

Its unique blooming pattern makes it a popular choice for xeriscaping and desert gardens.
