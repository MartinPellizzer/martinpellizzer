Adesmia inflexa var. brevisetosa

Adesmia inflexa var. brevisetosa, commonly known as the short-setose legume, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, trifoliate leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in dense clusters, which are followed by elongated, bean-like pods. It thrives in dry, open habitats and is often found in arid or semi-arid regions.

Due to its unique morphology and ecological adaptations, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists.
