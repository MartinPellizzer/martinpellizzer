Alsophila nilgirensis

Alsophila nilgirensis, commonly known as the Nilgiri brake, is a species of tree fern native to the Western Ghats of India.

It belongs to the family Dicksoniaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate fronds. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its rhizomes are fibrous and can be used in traditional medicine.

The fronds are typically evergreen, contributing to its ornamental value in horticulture.
