Astilbe chinensis var. longicarpa

Astilbe chinensis var. longicarpa, commonly known as the Chinese astilbe, is a perennial herb native to China.

It features upright, slender stems that bear panicles of fluffy, pale pink to white flowers. The plant has palmately divided, dark green leaves that are lance-shaped and often covered in a fine, silky hair. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Astilbe chinensis var. longicarpa is valued for its ornamental foliage and delicate floral display in garden settings.
