Adenophora thunbergiana f. lancifolia

Adenophora thunbergiana f. lancifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved bellflower, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It features slender stems that often grow in clumps, and its leaves are lance-shaped, typically with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. The plant produces bell-shaped flowers that are usually white or pale blue, blooming in summer. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Its botanical characteristics make it a desirable plant for both ornamental gardens and ecological restoration projects.
