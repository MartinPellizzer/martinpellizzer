Angelonia linearifolia

Angelonia linearifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf angelonia, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its slender, linear leaves and spikes of bright, tubular flowers that bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant is often cultivated for its long-lasting blooms and drought tolerance, making it suitable for gardens and landscapes. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Angelonia linearifolia is a member of the Plantaginaceae family, which includes other ornamental and medicinal plants.
