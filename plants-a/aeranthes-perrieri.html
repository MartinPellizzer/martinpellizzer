Aeranthes perrieri

Aeranthes perrieri, commonly known as the Perrier's orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a terrestrial orchid native to the wet tropical forests of Madagascar. The plant features a slender, leafy stem with several elongated, strap-like leaves. Its flowers are small and clustered, typically in a raceme, with white or pale green petals.

Aeranthes perrieri thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found near streams or in dense undergrowth.
