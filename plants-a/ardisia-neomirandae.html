Ardisia neomirandae

Ardisia neomirandae, commonly known as the New Miranda beauty, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and produces small, white flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The fruit is a bright red berry, which contains several small seeds and is an important food source for local wildlife.
