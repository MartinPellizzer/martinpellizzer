Aloitis propinqua

Aloitis propinqua, commonly known as the Narrow-leaved Ice Plant, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features small, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, adapted to store water in arid environments. The plant produces clusters of tiny, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or purple during the spring and summer months. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for low-maintenance landscaping.
