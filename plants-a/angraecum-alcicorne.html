Angraecum alcicorne

Angraecum alcicorne, commonly known as the horned angraecum, is a rare and striking orchid native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stem and large, showy white flowers with a distinctive yellow lip. The plant produces a single flower per stem, which opens at night, attracting specific nocturnal pollinators. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.
