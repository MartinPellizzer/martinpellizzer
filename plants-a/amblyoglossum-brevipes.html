Amblyoglossum brevipes

Amblyoglossum brevipes, commonly known as the short-lipped ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in wetlands and moist soils, exhibiting a herbaceous growth habit. Its small, tubular flowers are usually pink to purple in color, and it produces seeds in achenes.
