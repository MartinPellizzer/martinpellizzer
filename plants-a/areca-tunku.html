Areca tunku

Areca tunku, commonly known as the "Tunku areca," is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Arecaceae family, which includes palms, and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically green or yellowish in color. Areca tunku is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.

Its growth habit makes it suitable for both indoor and outdoor cultivation in warm climates.
