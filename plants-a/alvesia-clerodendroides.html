Alvesia clerodendroides

Alvesia clerodendroides, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a tropical plant native to southern Africa.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. Its flowers are clustered in panicles and are typically bright yellow or orange, attracting pollinators like bees and birds.

The fruit is a small, woody capsule that splits open to release seeds.
