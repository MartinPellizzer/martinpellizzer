Adenandra intermedia

Adenandra intermedia, commonly known as the Natal hibiscus, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and yellow. The plant has hairy stems and leaves that are ovate to elliptical in shape. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Adenandra intermedia is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and is relatively drought-tolerant once established.
