Argyrolobium schimperianum

Argyrolobium schimperianum, commonly known as the silver globular pea, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in globular clusters. The plant has hairy stems and leaves, and its pods contain small, round seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
