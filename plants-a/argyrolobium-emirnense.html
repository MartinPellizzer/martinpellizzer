Argyrolobium emirnense

Argyrolobium emirnense, commonly known as the Emirnense silverlobed burr.

It is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of southwestern Asia, particularly Iran. The plant features a low-growing, mat-forming habit with hairy stems and small, yellowish flowers. Its leaves are typically narrow and covered in fine, silvery hairs, which help reduce water loss in its dry habitat.

The plant is adapted to survive in harsh, semi-desert conditions, making it a resilient species within its native range.
