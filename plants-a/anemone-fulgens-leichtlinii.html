Anemone fulgens leichtlinii

Anemone fulgens leichtlinii, commonly known as the showy anemone, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central America.

It features showy, pink to red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has palmately divided, lobed leaves that are typically hairy and covered in fine pubescence. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for woodland gardens and native plant collections.
