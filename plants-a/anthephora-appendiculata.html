Anthephora appendiculata

Anthephora appendiculata, commonly known as the slender anthephora, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small, herbaceous perennial that typically grows in open, sunny habitats. The plant features delicate, linear leaves and produces small, clustered flowers with showy, white petals. Its botanical characteristics include a fibrous root system and a branching stem.
