Abrodictyum saxatile

Abrodictyum saxatile, commonly known as the rock orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, often in arid or semi-arid environments. The plant features a basal rosette of linear leaves and produces tall, slender flowering stems. Its flowers are small, greenish-white, and have a delicate, orchid-like appearance.

It is adapted to survive in harsh conditions with minimal water availability.
