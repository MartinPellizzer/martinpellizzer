Astrocarpa sesamoides var. cochleariifolia

Astrocarpa sesamoides var. cochleariifolia, commonly known as the spiral-leaved sesamoid, is a unique plant species characterized by its distinctive spiral arrangement of leaves.

The flowers are small and yellow, appearing in a clustered inflorescence, contributing to its ornamental value.
