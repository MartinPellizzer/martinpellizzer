Atragene moisseenkoi

Atragene moisseenkoi, commonly known as the Cape orchid, is a South African plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in late summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces elongated, pendant-like flower spikes. It thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation areas.
