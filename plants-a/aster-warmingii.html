Aster warmingii

Aster warmingii, commonly known as the Chinese aster, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in temperate regions and features showy, colorful blooms that attract pollinators. Its leaves are usually narrow and lance-shaped, and it thrives in well-drained soil.

Aster warmingii is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens.
