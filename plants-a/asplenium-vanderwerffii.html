Asplenium vanderwerffii

Asplenium vanderwerffii, commonly known as Vanderwerff's spleenwort, is a species of moss belonging to the family Aspleniaceae.

It is characterized by its small, delicate, and often clustered growth habit, with leaves that are typically narrow and spirally arranged. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found on rock surfaces or in crevices. Its reproductive structures, known as sporangia, are borne on short stalks at the tips of the leaves.

Asplenium vanderwerffii is a relatively rare and specialized species, often indicating specific ecological conditions in its habitat.
