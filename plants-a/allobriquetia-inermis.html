Allobriquetia inermis

Allobriquetia inermis, commonly known as the slender bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the Andes Mountains of South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in high-altitude grasslands and rocky areas. The plant features slender stems and produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of blue or purple. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often covered in a fine layer of white hairs.

Allobriquetia inermis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with suitable growing conditions.
