Ascolepis bellidiflora

Ascolepis bellidiflora, commonly known as the bellidiflora orchid, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its striking, bell-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its flowers are usually a vibrant yellow or orange, with a distinctive, hooded lip that attracts pollinators.

Ascolepis bellidiflora is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
