Aechmea rupestris

Aechmea rupestris, commonly known as the rock aechmea, is a bromeliad native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that form a central cup to collect water. The plant produces vibrant orange to red flowers that bloom from the center of the rosette. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light.

Aechmea rupestris is often used in landscaping due to its ornamental value and adaptability to various growing conditions.
