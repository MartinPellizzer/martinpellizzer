Artanthe mollicoma f. major

Artanthe mollicoma f. major, commonly known as the soft-leaved artanthe, is a perennial herb native to the western United States.

It features soft, velvety leaves that are typically gray-green in color, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, showy flowers in shades of pink and purple, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and drought tolerance.
