Asphodelus szovitsii

Asphodelus szovitsii, commonly known as the Szovits' asphodel, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the family Asphodelaceae and is characterized by its sword-shaped leaves and tall, branched inflorescences. The plant produces showy, often yellow or orange flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or arid environments.

Due to its ornamental value and limited distribution, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
