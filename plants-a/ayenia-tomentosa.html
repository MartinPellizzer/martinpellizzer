Ayenia tomentosa

Ayenia tomentosa, commonly known as the wooly ayenia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly covering on its stems and leaves, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, clustered white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or forested areas.

Ayenia tomentosa is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
