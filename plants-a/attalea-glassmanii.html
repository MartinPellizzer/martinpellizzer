Attalea glassmanii

Attalea glassmanii, commonly known as the glassmanii palm, is a species of palm native to the Amazon Basin.

It is a tall, slender tree that can reach heights of up to 20 meters. The plant has a smooth, grayish trunk with a distinctive crown of pinnate leaves. Its leaves are large, feathery, and can span several meters in width.

The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that develop into clusters of berries, which are dispersed by birds and other animals.
