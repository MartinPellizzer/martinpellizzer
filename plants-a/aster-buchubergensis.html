Aster buchubergensis

Aster buchubergensis, commonly known as the Buchuberg aster, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically produces small, violet-blue flowers arranged in a cluster, with narrow, grass-like leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a suitable candidate for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species in its native habitat.
