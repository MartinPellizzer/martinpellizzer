Ajax festalis var. plenus

Ajax festalis var. plenus, commonly known as the Double Ajax, is a hybrid cultivar of the African daisy, known for its showy, double-flowered appearance.

It features dense clusters of white petals that resemble small, rounded blossoms, giving it a lush, full look. The plant is native to South Africa and thrives in full sun with well-draining soil. It is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to bloom throughout the growing season.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for both container gardening and landscape design.
