Alopecurus tenuis

Alopecurus tenuis, commonly known as slender meadow grass, is a flowering plant belonging to the grass family Poaceae.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that typically forms dense, tufted clumps. The plant has slender, hairless stems and narrow, linear leaves that are often slightly rolled. Its small, spike-like inflorescence produces numerous tiny flowers, which are wind-pollinated.

Alopecurus tenuis is commonly found in moist meadows, pastures, and along stream banks in temperate regions.
