Archangelica keiskei

Archangelica keiskei, commonly known as the Japanese angelica root, is a herbaceous perennial native to Japan.

It belongs to the Apiaceae family, which includes carrots and parsley, and is characterized by its tall, hollow stems and compound leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in umbels, typical of its family, and is often cultivated for its aromatic roots. Due to its medicinal properties, it has been used in traditional Japanese medicine for various health benefits.
