Asperula cynanchica var. ebusitana

Asperula cynanchica var. ebusitana, commonly known as Ebusi azalea, is a variant of the evergreen azalea native to Japan.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically features dark green, leathery leaves and produces clusters of pink to red flowers. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and shaded borders.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in humid, coastal environments.
