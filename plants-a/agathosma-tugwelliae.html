Agathosma tugwelliae

Agathosma tugwelliae, commonly known as the Cape rosemary, is a small evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, needle-like leaves that emit a distinctive scent. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and prefers full sun exposure.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is often cultivated in gardens and used in traditional herbal remedies.
