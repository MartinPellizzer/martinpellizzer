Agrostis gracililaxa subsp. gracililaxa

Agrostis gracililaxa subsp.

gracililaxa, commonly known as slender stemmed bentgrass, is a grass species native to parts of Europe and Asia. It is characterized by its fine, tufted growth habit and slender, slightly compressed stems.

The leaves are narrow, with a bluish-green color, and the plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows and along stream banks.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and is used in landscaping and turfgrass applications.
