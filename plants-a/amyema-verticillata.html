Amyema verticillata

Amyema verticillata, commonly known as the spiral leaf orchid, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Orchidaceae and is characterized by its distinctive spiral arrangement of leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or rainforest habitats.
