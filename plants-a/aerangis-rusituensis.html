Aerangis rusituensis

Aerangis rusituensis, commonly known as the Rusituensis Orchid, is a rare and endemic species found in the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its intricate, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and purple. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil to flourish. Its unique floral structure and limited distribution make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists.

Due to habitat destruction, Aerangis rusituensis is considered vulnerable and is often protected in conservation programs.
