Astilbe virescens

Astilbe virescens, commonly known as green astilbe, is a herbaceous perennial native to East Asia.

It features upright, branching stems that bear clusters of soft, feathery, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of 2 to 3 feet and thrives in partial to full shade. Its foliage is lance-shaped and often turns a vibrant red in the fall.

Astilbe virescens prefers moist, well-drained soil and is commonly used in shaded garden landscapes.
