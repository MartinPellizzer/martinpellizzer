Ananassa lucida

Ananassa lucida, commonly known as the shining ananassia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the family Annonaceae and is characterized by its compound leaves and fragrant flowers. The plant produces small, green fruits that mature to a yellowish color, containing a single seed. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens.
