Aralia wangshanensis

Aralia wangshanensis, commonly known as the Wangshan aralia, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Araliaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in mountainous regions. Its berries are edible and have been used in traditional Chinese medicine.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological significance in its native habitat.
