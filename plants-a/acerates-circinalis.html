Acerates circinalis

Acerates circinalis, commonly known as the spiral tree, is a unique species characterized by its distinctive spiral growth pattern.

This plant exhibits a helical arrangement of its branches, which gives it an otherworldly appearance. It is native to certain regions of Southeast Asia, where it thrives in tropical climates. The leaves are typically elongated and narrow, with a smooth texture and a vibrant green color.

Due to its unusual morphology, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant in botanical gardens.
