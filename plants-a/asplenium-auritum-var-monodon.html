Asplenium auritum var. monodon

Asplenium auritum var. monodon, commonly known as the ear-shaped maidenhair fern, is a variant of the common maidenhair fern.

It is a small, evergreen fern that typically grows in shaded, moist environments. The plant features delicate, finely divided fronds with a distinctive ear-like shape on the pinnae. It is native to parts of Asia and is often found in rocky or forested areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and shaded outdoor landscapes.
