Azima diacantha

Azima diacantha, commonly known as the African star-of-Bethlehem, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, star-shaped white flowers. The plant has strap-like, dark green leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
