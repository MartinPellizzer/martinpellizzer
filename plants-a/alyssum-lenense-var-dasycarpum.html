Alyssum lenense var. dasycarpum

Alyssum lenense var. dasycarpum, commonly known as the woolly alyssum, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features dense clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has hairy, gray-green foliage that helps it retain moisture in arid conditions. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its compact form and long blooming period make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
