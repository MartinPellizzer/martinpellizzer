Ajania fastigiata

Ajania fastigiata, commonly known as the silverleaf ajania, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and silvery-gray foliage, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Ajania fastigiata is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
