Aliciella micromeria

Aliciella micromeria, commonly known as the small-leaved aliciella, is a small shrub native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its tiny, elliptical leaves that are usually less than 1 centimeter in length. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. Its fruit is a small, dry capsule that contains several seeds.

Aliciella micromeria is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid environments.
