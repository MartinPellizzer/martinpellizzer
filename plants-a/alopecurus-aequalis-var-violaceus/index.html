Alopecurus Aequalis Var. Violaceus

Alopecurus aequalis var. violaceus is a strikingly beautiful plant known for its vibrant purple flowers. Native to parts of Europe and Asia, it thrives in moist, well-drained soils. This variant of the common mouse-ear grass is often found in meadows and along riverbanks. Its unique coloration makes it a popular choice for gardens and natural landscapes. Despite its ornamental appeal, it also plays an important role in supporting local wildlife.
