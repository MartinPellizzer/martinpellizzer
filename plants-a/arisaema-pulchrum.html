Arisaema pulchrum

Arisaema pulchrum, commonly known as the beautiful arum, is a member of the Araceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like China, Indonesia, and the Philippines. The plant features a striking, arrow-shaped leaf with a glossy surface and a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The plant's unique floral structure includes both male and female flowers, which are pollinated by insects.
