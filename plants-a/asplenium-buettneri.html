Asplenium buettneri

Asplenium buettneri, commonly known as the Button Fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Aspleniaceae and is characterized by its compact, rosette-like growth habit. The plant features delicate, lance-shaped fronds that are often covered in fine, silky hairs. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found growing on tree trunks or rocks.

Asplenium buettneri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated as a houseplant.
