Adnaria montana

Adnaria montana, commonly known as the Mediterranean sea holly, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of the Mediterranean.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils along the shoreline. The plant produces small, blue or violet flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are narrow and succulent, adapted to withstand salty sea air and coastal conditions.

Adnaria montana is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to thrive in harsh maritime environments.
