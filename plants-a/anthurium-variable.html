Anthurium variable

Anthurium variable, commonly known as the variable flamingo flower, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that can vary in color from deep green to reddish hues. The plant produces vibrant spadices, which are specialized inflorescences that resemble flowers but are actually modified leaves. Anthurium variable thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and ornamental purposes.
