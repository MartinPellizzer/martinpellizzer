Aristolochia congolana

Aristolochia congolana, commonly known as the Congo birthvane, is a flowering plant native to Central and Eastern Africa.

It belongs to the Aristolochiaceae family and is characterized by its unique, tubular, and often heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, hooded flowers that are typically greenish or brownish in color. It is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional African medicine.

However, it contains toxic compounds and should be handled with care.
