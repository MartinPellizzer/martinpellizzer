Anthurium scherzerianum var. roseum

Anthurium scherzerianum var. roseum, commonly known as the rose-colored anthurium, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped, velvety leaves that are typically deep green with a reddish tinge. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-shaped inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, often in shades of pink or red. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

This variety is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
