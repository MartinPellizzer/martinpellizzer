Anubias pynaertii

Anubias pynaertii, commonly known as the African water fern, is a hardy aquatic plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features dark green, leathery leaves that are typically elongated and slightly wavy in appearance. The plant grows from a rhizome and can thrive in a variety of water conditions, making it popular in aquariums. It is known for its ability to tolerate low light and poor water quality.

Anubias pynaertii is also appreciated for its ornamental value and ease of maintenance in both freshwater and brackish environments.
