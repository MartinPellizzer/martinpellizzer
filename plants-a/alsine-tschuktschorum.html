Alsine tschuktschorum

Alsine tschuktschorum, commonly known as the Tschuktschorum sandwort, is a low-growing annual plant native to arctic and alpine regions of Europe and Asia.

It typically reaches heights of 5 to 15 centimeters and features small, white or pinkish flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has a rosette of narrow, fleshy leaves that help it conserve moisture in harsh environments. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in tundra and rocky habitats.

Its botanical structure is adapted to withstand cold temperatures and strong winds.
