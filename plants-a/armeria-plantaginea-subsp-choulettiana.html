Armeria plantaginea subsp. choulettiana

Armeria plantaginea subsp.

choulettiana, commonly known as the Choulett's thrift, is a low-growing, flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region. It features dense clusters of small, white or pink flowers that bloom in early summer.

The plant has linear, grass-like leaves and is often found in rocky or coastal areas. It is a perennial species that thrives in well-drained soils and full sun.

Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
