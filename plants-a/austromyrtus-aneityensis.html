Austromyrtus aneityensis

Austromyrtus aneityensis, commonly known as the Tasmanian myrtle, is a small evergreen shrub native to Tasmania.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family, which includes eucalypts and tea trees. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves with a glossy, dark green upper surface and a lighter underside. Its small, fragrant white flowers are typically arranged in clusters and bloom in spring.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its hardy nature and aromatic foliage.
