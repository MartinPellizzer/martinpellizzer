Ayenia corchorifolia

Ayenia corchorifolia, commonly known as the silky ayenia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features slender, hairy stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its showy yellow flowers bloom in the spring and attract various pollinators.

Ayenia corchorifolia is typically found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky slopes.
