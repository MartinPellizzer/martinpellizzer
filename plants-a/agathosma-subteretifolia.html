Agathosma subteretifolia

Agathosma subteretifolia, commonly known as the silver leaf bush, is a succulent shrub native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its narrow, silver-green leaves that are covered in fine, white hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, typically blooming in spring. It is well adapted to arid environments and can thrive in full sun with minimal water.

Its aromatic leaves have been used traditionally for their medicinal properties.
