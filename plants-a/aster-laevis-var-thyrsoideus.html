Aster laevis var. thyrsoideus

Aster laevis var. thyrsoideus, commonly known as the smooth aster, is a herbaceous perennial native to North America.

It features showy, daisy-like flowers that bloom in late summer to early fall, typically in shades of purple, pink, or white. The plant has smooth, lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soils.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in garden landscapes for its vibrant floral display.
