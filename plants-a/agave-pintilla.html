Agave pintilla

Agave pintilla, commonly known as the Pintilla agave, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a succulent plant characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are thick and fleshy. The leaves are typically blue-green in color with a waxy coating, which helps reduce water loss. It produces a tall flower stalk that bears small, white flowers, often used in ornamental gardening.

This plant is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
