Amaryllis longifolia var. maxima

Amaryllis longifolia var. maxima, commonly known as the giant amaryllis, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features large, showy flowers that can reach up to 12 inches in diameter, typically in shades of white, pink, or red. The plant produces strap-like, narrow leaves that are dark green and often persist through the winter. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and containers.

This variety is prized for its bold blooms and relatively easy care requirements.
