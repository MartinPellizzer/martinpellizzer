Asphodeliris racemosa

Asphodeliris racemosa, commonly known as the clustered asphodel, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in grassy areas and along roadsides, thriving in well-drained soils. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces tall, branched stems with showy, pale yellow to greenish-white flowers. Its inflorescence is a raceme, which consists of multiple small, clustered flowers.

Asphodeliris racemosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens for its unique appearance.
