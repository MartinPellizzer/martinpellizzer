Abalon albiflorum var. pumilum

Abalon albiflorum var. pumilum, commonly known as the dwarf whiteflower abalon, is a compact shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has narrow, leathery leaves that are typically gray-green in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance, it is a popular choice for xeriscaping.
