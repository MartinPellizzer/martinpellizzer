Adenocarpus decorticans subsp. speciosus

Adenocarpus decorticans subsp.

speciosus, commonly known as the ornamental sicklepod, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its slender, branching stems and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow.

The plant produces distinctive, sickle-shaped fruits that encase its seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open grasslands and disturbed habitats.

Due to its unique appearance, it is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes.
