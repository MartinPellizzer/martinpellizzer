Agave vivipara var. marginata

Agave vivipara var. marginata, commonly known as the marginata agave, is a succulent plant native to Mexico.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color with white margins. The plant produces offsets, or "pups," along its base, which is a characteristic of its viviparous growth habit. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, requiring minimal water once established.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
