Avena editissima

Avena editissima, commonly known as slender oat, is a flowering plant native to the United States.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny florets. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands and along stream banks.

Avena editissima is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, providing food and habitat for various wildlife species.
