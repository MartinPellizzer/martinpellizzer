Anthoxanthum odoratum var. glaberrimum

Anthoxanthum odoratum var. glaberrimum, commonly known as sweet vernal grass, is a flowering plant characterized by its bright yellow inflorescences.

It belongs to the grass family (Poaceae) and is native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and stream banks. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a smooth texture, and it produces small, wind-pollinated flowers.

The species is noted for its aromatic scent, which becomes more pronounced when the plant is dried.
