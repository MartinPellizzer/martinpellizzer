Abutilon tubulosum var. breviflorum

Abutilon tubulosum var. breviflorum, commonly known as the velvetleaf abutilon, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its fuzzy, velvety leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters, often followed by distinctive, spiny seed pods. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in disturbed soils or along roadsides.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening due to its unique texture and visual appeal.
