Aklema nudiflora

Aklema nudiflora, commonly known as the naked-flower aklema, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and small, clustered flowers that are usually white or pale yellow. It blooms during the cooler months, often in spring or early summer.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
