Arisaema ciliatum

Arisaema ciliatum, commonly known as the curly-leaved arisaema, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its distinctive, spirally arranged leaves that give it a unique appearance. The plant produces a spadix surrounded by a spathe, which is typical of the Araceae family. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Arisaema ciliatum is also known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its attractive foliage.
