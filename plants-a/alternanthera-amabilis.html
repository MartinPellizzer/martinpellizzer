Alternanthera amabilis

Alternanthera amabilis, commonly known as the red-veined or red-leaf alternate, is a strikingly colorful aquatic plant native to South America.

It features vibrant red veins contrasting with its bright green leaves, making it a popular choice for aquariums and water gardens. The plant thrives in warm, tropical climates and requires moderate to high light levels for optimal growth. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value rather than for its edible parts.

Its rapid growth rate and ease of propagation make it a favored plant among aquatic hobbyists.
