Arundinella gigantea

Arundinella gigantea, commonly known as giant bamboo, is a perennial grass native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems that can grow up to 10 meters in height. The plant has long, narrow leaves that are typically arranged in a spiral pattern around the stem. It produces small, clustered inflorescences with tiny, wind-pollinated flowers.

Arundinella gigantea is often used in ornamental horticulture due to its striking appearance and rapid growth rate.
