Abrotanum brachylobium

Abrotanum brachylobium, commonly known as the narrow-leafed abrotanum, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in dry, sandy soils. The plant features small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. Its leaves are narrow and often have a slightly hairy texture.

It is valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
