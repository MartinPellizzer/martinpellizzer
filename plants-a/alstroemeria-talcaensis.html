Alstroemeria talcaensis

Alstroemeria talcaensis, commonly known as the Talca Valley alstroemeria, is a flowering plant native to Chile.

It belongs to the family Alstroemeriaceae and is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that come in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant typically grows in moist, temperate climates and is often found in coastal areas. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a spiral pattern around the stem.

Alstroemeria talcaensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is cultivated in gardens and floral arrangements.
