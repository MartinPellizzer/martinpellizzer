Asplenium bullatum var. shikokianum

Asplenium bullatum var. shikokianum, commonly known as the Shikokian shield fern, is a rare and delicate fern species native to Japan.

It belongs to the family Aspleniaceae and is characterized by its compact, rosette-like growth habit. The fronds are typically short and broad, with a leathery texture and a distinctive, shield-shaped appearance. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in rocky or forested areas.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered plant.
