Amomum lepicarpum

Amomum lepicarpum, commonly known as the small-fruited cardamom, is a tropical plant belonging to the ginger family, Zingiberaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in regions of India, Nepal, and Bhutan. The plant produces small, aromatic fruits that are used in traditional medicine and as a spice. Its rhizomes are also utilized for their medicinal properties.
