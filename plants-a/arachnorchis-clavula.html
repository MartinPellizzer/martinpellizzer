Arachnorchis clavula

Arachnorchis clavula, commonly known as the narrow-leafed spider orchid, is an epiphytic orchid native to eastern Australia.

It typically grows on the bark of trees in rainforest or moist woodland habitats. The plant features a slender, wiry stem with narrow, elongated leaves. Its flowers are small, greenish-white, and have a distinctive spider-like appearance.

It blooms between September and November, producing a cluster of fragrant, tubular flowers.
