Astilbe shikokiana var. shikokiana

Astilbe shikokiana var. shikokiana, commonly known as the Shikoku astilbe, is a perennial herb native to Japan.

It features upright, branched stems that bear clusters of small, lace-like, pinkish-white flowers. The plant has heart-shaped, dark green leaves that are slightly serrated along the edges. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens.

Its ornamental value lies in its delicate foliage and showy floral display.
