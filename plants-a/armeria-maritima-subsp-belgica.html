Armeria maritima subsp. belgica

Armeria maritima subsp.

belgica, commonly known as the Belgian sea thrift, is a low-growing perennial plant native to coastal regions of Europe. It features dense clusters of white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in early summer.

The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is adapted to sandy or gravelly soils. It is often found in dune environments and is resistant to salt spray and wind.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in stabilizing coastal habitats.
