Amaryllis chloroleuca

Amaryllis chloroleuca, commonly known as the white amaryllis, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking white, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has strap-like, narrow leaves that are typically a bright green color. It is a bulbous plant that thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.

Amaryllis chloroleuca is popular in gardens for its elegant appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
