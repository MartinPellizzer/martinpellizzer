Alternanthera multicaulis

Alternanthera multicaulis, commonly known as red-stemmed alternanthera, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red or purple stems and lance-shaped leaves that can vary in color depending on light conditions. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow, arranged in spikes. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full sunlight.

This species is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its striking foliage and ease of care.
