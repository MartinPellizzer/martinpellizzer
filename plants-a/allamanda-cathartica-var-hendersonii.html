Allamanda cathartica var. hendersonii

Allamanda cathartica var. hendersonii, commonly known as Henderson's allamanda, is a striking tropical shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range from yellow to orange in color. The plant features glossy, dark green leaves that are opposite and elliptical in shape. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
