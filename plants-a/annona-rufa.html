Annona rufa

Annona rufa, commonly known as the red annona, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Annonaceae family and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are ovate to elliptical in shape. The plant produces fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers with a distinctive, fleshy calyx. Its fruit is a rough, green, spiny berry that turns red when mature, containing several large, white seeds.

Annona rufa is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
