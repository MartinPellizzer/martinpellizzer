Acianthus reniformis

Acianthus reniformis, commonly known as the kidney-shaped orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its unique kidney-shaped leaves, which are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces striking, showy flowers in shades of yellow and orange, often with intricate patterns. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Acianthus reniformis is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
