Adesmia ciliata

Adesmia ciliata, commonly known as the silky adesmia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features small, delicate flowers that are usually pink or white in color. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have soft, hairy margins.

Adesmia ciliata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
