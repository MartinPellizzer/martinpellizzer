Abelia chinensis var. lipoensis

Abelia chinensis var. lipoensis, commonly known as the Lipoensis Abelia, is a flowering shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its ornamental, fragrant, and showy flowers that bloom in clusters of white to pinkish-red. The plant features opposite, simple, and elliptical leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers moist, well-drained soil.

This variety is popular in gardens for its attractive appearance and adaptability to various growing conditions.
