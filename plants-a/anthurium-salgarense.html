Anthurium salgarense

Anthurium salgarense, commonly known as the Salgarense arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to the Caribbean islands.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves with a deep green color and prominent veins. The plant produces striking spadices, which are the modified leaves that house the actual flowers. Anthurium salgarense thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and air-purifying properties.
