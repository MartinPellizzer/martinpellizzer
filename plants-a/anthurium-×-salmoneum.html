Anthurium × salmoneum

Anthurium × salmoneum, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Araceae family.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a reddish or maroon tint. The plant produces vibrant, spathiflorous inflorescences with a spathe that ranges from red to orange, often with a contrasting green spadix. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.

Anthurium × salmoneum is popular as a decorative plant due to its ornamental foliage and long-lasting flowers.
