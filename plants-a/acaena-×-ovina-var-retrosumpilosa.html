Acaena × ovina var. retrosumpilosa

Acaena × ovina var. retrosumpilosa, commonly known as the silver mat grass, is a low-growing perennial plant native to South America.

It features dense, mat-forming growth with silver-gray foliage due to a waxy coating on its leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in summer, followed by spiny, spherical fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often used in erosion control and ornamental landscaping.

Its botanical characteristics make it well-suited for coastal and dry garden environments.
