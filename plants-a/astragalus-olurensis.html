Astragalus olurensis

Astragalus olurensis, commonly known as the Persian milkvetch, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to regions of Central Asia, including parts of Iran and Afghanistan. The plant features compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces small, yellow to reddish-purple flowers. It is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid environments.

Astragalus olurensis is valued for its role in soil stabilization and its potential medicinal properties.
