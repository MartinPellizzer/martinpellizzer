Aneilema siliculosum var. gracilescens

Aneilema siliculosum var. gracilescens, commonly known as the slender spike rush, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It typically grows in wetland environments, such as marshes and along stream banks, where it thrives in moist, nutrient-rich soils. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces spike-like inflorescences that bear small, greenish flowers. It is characterized by its slender stem and is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil and support aquatic ecosystems.

Its botanical structure reflects adaptations to aquatic or semi-aquatic habitats, making it an important component of wetland flora.
