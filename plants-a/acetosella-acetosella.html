Acetosella acetosella

Acetosella acetosella, commonly known as sorrel, is a low-growing perennial plant belonging to the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, heart-shaped leaves that are typically reddish or purple in color, which help in distinguishing it from similar species. Acetosella acetosella is valued for its edible leaves, which have a tangy, sour flavor due to the presence of oxalic acid.
