Acantholimon nuratavicum

Acantholimon nuratavicum, commonly known as the Nurata Valley thorned gentian, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the family Gentianaceae and is characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves and striking blue or violet flowers.
