Arum digitatum

Arum digitatum, commonly known as the Chinese arrowhead, is a herbaceous plant native to East Asia.

It features arrow-shaped leaves that are often bright green with a glossy texture. The plant produces striking spadices, which are inflorescences that resemble a spadix with a spathe. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in wetlands or along streams.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some cultures.
