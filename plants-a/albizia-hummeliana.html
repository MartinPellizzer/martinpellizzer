Albizia hummeliana

Albizia hummeliana, commonly known as the Chinese honeylocust, is a deciduous leguminous tree native to China.

It is characterized by its compound leaves that are often feathery and bipinnate, giving it a delicate appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, which bloom in the spring. Its pods are elongated and contain several seeds, and the tree is known for its rapid growth and adaptability to various soil types.

Albizia hummeliana is also valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
