Anguillaria crenulata

Anguillaria crenulata, commonly known as the zigzag orchid, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, zigzag-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or white in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Anguillaria crenulata is often cultivated for its ornamental value in botanical gardens and greenhouses.
