Axonopus rupestris

Axonopus rupestris, commonly known as the rock axonopus, is a perennial grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in well-drained environments. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences.

Axonopus rupestris is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to stabilize soil in rocky landscapes.
