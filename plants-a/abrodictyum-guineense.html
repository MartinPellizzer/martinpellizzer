Abrodictyum guineense

Abrodictyum guineense, commonly known as the African orchid or Guinea orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly found in countries like Guinea, Nigeria, and Cameroon. The plant is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of yellow and orange. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and requires a warm, humid environment.

Abrodictyum guineense is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is appreciated in horticultural circles for its beauty and resilience.
