Aneilema paludosum subsp. pauciflorum

Aneilema paludosum subsp.

pauciflorum, commonly known as the swamp aneilema, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to wetland habitats in tropical Africa. It typically grows in marshy or swampy areas, thriving in high humidity and waterlogged soils.

The plant features linear to lanceolate leaves that are usually arranged in a basal rosette. Its flowers are small and clustered in spike-like inflorescences, often with a pale green or yellowish hue.

It plays a role in wetland ecosystems by stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various organisms.
