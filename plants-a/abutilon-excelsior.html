Abutilon excelsior

Abutilon excelsior, commonly known as the velvetleaf abutilon, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a soft, velvety texture. The plant produces clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Abutilon excelsior is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and showy blooms.
