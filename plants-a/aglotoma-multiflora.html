Aglotoma multiflora

Aglotoma multiflora, commonly known as the many-flowered aglotoma, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in dense clusters, which bloom during the summer months. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often covered in fine hairs.

Aglotoma multiflora is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it resilient to drought conditions.
