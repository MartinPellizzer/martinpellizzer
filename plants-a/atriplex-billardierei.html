Atriplex billardierei

Atriplex billardierei, commonly known as the narrowleaf saltbush, is a shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its small, scale-like leaves that are adapted to reduce water loss in arid environments. The plant produces small greenish flowers that are typically clustered in compact inflorescences. It thrives in saline and alkaline soils, making it an important component of coastal and inland saltbush communities.

Atriplex billardierei plays a role in stabilizing soils and providing habitat for native wildlife.
