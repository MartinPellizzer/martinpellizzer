Adromischus filicaulis

Adromischus filicaulis, commonly known as the narrow-leafed moonstone, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features small, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a bluish or purplish tinge, arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces clusters of tiny, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the warmer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with plenty of sunlight.

Adromischus filicaulis is popular among succulent enthusiasts for its compact size and ornamental appeal.
