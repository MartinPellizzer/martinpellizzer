Arctium cynarodes

Arctium cynarodes, commonly known as the lesser burdock, is a flowering plant in the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, hairy stems and large, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, purple flowers that develop into spiny, bur-like fruits. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, it thrives in disturbed soils and open habitats.

Its botanical features make it identifiable and often found in meadows and along roadsides.
