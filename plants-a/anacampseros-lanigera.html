Anacampseros lanigera

Anacampseros lanigera, commonly known as the woolly anacampseros, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly covering of white to silver hairs on its stems and leaves, which helps it retain moisture in arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for xerophytic gardens.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
