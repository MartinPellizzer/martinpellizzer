Alectorolophus alectorolophus

Alectorolophus alectorolophus, commonly known as the golden rain tree, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and showy yellow flowers. The plant produces small, flat pods that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its rapid growth and vibrant floral display.
