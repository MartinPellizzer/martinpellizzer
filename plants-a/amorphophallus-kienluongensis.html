Amorphophallus kienluongensis

Amorphophallus kienluongensis, commonly known as the Kien Luong arrowhead, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its spadix and spathe floral structures. The plant produces a large, leafy stem that can grow up to several meters in height, supporting its striking inflorescence. Its leaves are typically arrowhead-shaped, giving it its common name, and it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

The species is notable for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its unique foliage and floral display.
