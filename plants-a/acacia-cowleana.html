Acacia cowleana

Acacia cowleana, commonly known as the Christmas tree acacia, is a shrub or small tree native to Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, feathery foliage and yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces fluffy, pod-like fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy regions.

Acacia cowleana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping.
