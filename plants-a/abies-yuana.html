Abies yuana

Abies yuana, commonly known as the Chinese fir, is a coniferous evergreen tree native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its conical shape, dark green needles, and distinctive, rounded cones. The tree typically grows in mountainous regions with cool, moist climates. Its bark is thick and scaly, providing protection against harsh environmental conditions.

Abies yuana is valued for its timber and is also used in horticulture for its ornamental appeal.
