Aspidistra subrotata var. subrotata

Aspidistra subrotata var. subrotata, commonly known as the broad-leaf aspidistra, is a species native to southern China and northern Vietnam.

It is characterized by its dark green, broad, and lance-shaped leaves that are often slightly wavy along the edges. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments and is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its tolerance of low light conditions. Its foliage remains evergreen throughout the year, making it a popular choice for ornamental purposes.

The plant's botanical features include a rhizomatous root system and small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually not showy.
