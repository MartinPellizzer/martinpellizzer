Achras Montana

Achras montana is a unique and striking plant native to the Andes mountains of South America. Known for its large, spiny leaves and showy yellow flowers, it stands out in its natural habitat. This plant thrives in high-altitude environments with cool temperatures and well-drained soil. Its distinctive appearance has made it a popular choice for botanical gardens and exotic plant collectors. Despite its beauty, Achras montana remains relatively obscure in mainstream horticulture.
