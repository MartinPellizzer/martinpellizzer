Atroxima liberica

Atroxima liberica, commonly known as the African coffee plant, is a species native to tropical Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height. The plant produces small, dark green leaves and bears red fruits containing seeds used for coffee production. Its botanical features include a woody stem, opposite leaf arrangement, and a tropical climate preference.

It is closely related to other coffee species and plays a significant role in regional agriculture.
