Aspidoglossum carinatum

Aspidoglossum carinatum, commonly known as the African daisy, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features rosettes of narrow, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces striking yellow flowers with a red center, which bloom in summer. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil.

Aspidoglossum carinatum is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and ease of care.
