Artemisia opulenta f. laciniata

Artemisia opulenta f. laciniata, commonly known as the fragrant fringed sage, is a flowering plant in the sunflower family.

It is characterized by its finely dissected, fern-like leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late summer. It is native to the southeastern United States and thrives in well-drained soils.

Its aromatic foliage and ornamental value make it a popular choice for gardens and landscaping.
