Astragalus sphaerocarpus

Astragalus sphaerocarpus, commonly known as the globe milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of central Asia.

It is a member of the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its small, yellow pea-shaped flowers.
