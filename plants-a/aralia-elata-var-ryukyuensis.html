Aralia elata var. ryukyuensis

Aralia elata var. ryukyuensis, commonly known as the Ryukyu aralia, is a perennial shrub native to the Ryukyu Islands of Japan.

It features compound leaves with long, slender leaflets that are often arranged in a feather-like pattern. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers in umbel-like clusters, which are followed by red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in its native habitat.
