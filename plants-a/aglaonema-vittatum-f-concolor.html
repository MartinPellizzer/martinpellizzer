Aglaonema vittatum f. concolor

Aglaonema vittatum f. concolor, commonly known as the Variegated Chinese Evergreen, is a striking ornamental plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, dark green leaves with prominent, waxy white veins that create a distinctive, eye-catching pattern. The plant is a member of the Araceae family, known for its tropical appearance and air-purifying properties. It thrives in low-light conditions and requires moderate humidity and well-draining soil.

Its foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.
