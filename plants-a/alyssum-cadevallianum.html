Alyssum cadevallianum

Alyssum cadevallianum, commonly known as the Cape Verdean alyssum, is a flowering plant native to the Cape Verde Islands.

It is a low-growing annual herb with small, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant features dense, mat-forming foliage that is often covered in fine, soft hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Alyssum cadevallianum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
