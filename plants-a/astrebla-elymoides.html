Astrebla elymoides

Astrebla elymoides, commonly known as the slender grasshopper grass, is a perennial grass native to Australia.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny flowers. It is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, showing drought tolerance.

Astrebla elymoides is often found in sandy soils and is used in some regions for forage or land stabilization.
