Ardisia Parvissima

Ardisia parvissima is a small evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia. It is known for its glossy dark green leaves and clusters of small, bright red berries. This plant is often cultivated as an ornamental due to its attractive appearance and compact growth habit. It thrives in shaded environments and requires well-drained soil to flourish. Despite its beauty, it can be invasive in some areas if not properly managed.
