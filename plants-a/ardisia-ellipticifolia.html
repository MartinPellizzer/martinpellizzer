Ardisia ellipticifolia

Ardisia ellipticifolia, commonly known as the elliptic-leaf beautyberry, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves that are dark green in color. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale pink flowers, which are followed by bright red berries. These berries are an important food source for various bird species.

Ardisia ellipticifolia is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
