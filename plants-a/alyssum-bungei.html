Alyssum bungei

Alyssum bungei, commonly known as the rock allyssum, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically reaches heights of 10 to 20 centimeters. The plant features dense clusters of small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. Its foliage consists of narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

Alyssum bungei thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and alpine landscapes.
