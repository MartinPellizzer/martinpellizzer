Anthurium tetragonum var. yucatanense

Anthurium tetragonum var. yucatanense, commonly known as the Yucatan anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central America.

It features broad, dark green, heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture. The plant produces striking, spadix-shaped inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor and greenhouse cultivation.
