Aster linodes

Aster linodes, commonly known as the slender aster, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads composed of ray and disk florets. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and woodlands. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture, while its flowers are blue to purple in color.

Aster linodes is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, providing nectar for pollinators.
