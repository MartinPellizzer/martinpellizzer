Asplenium mannii

Asplenium mannii, commonly known as the African shield fern, is a species of fern native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Aspleniaceae and is characterized by its compact, evergreen foliage. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its leaves are leathery and shield-shaped, with a distinctive dark green color and a rough texture.

Asplenium mannii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated as a houseplant.
