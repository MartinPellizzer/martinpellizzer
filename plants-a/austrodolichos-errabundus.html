Austrodolichos errabundus

Austrodolichos errabundus, commonly known as the wandering cowpea, is a leguminous plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, trailing stems and small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant produces elongated pods containing several seeds, which are typically small and angular. Austrodolichos errabundus is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, showcasing a remarkable ability to thrive in poor soil conditions.
