Acetosella Caesia

Acetosella caesia is a small, delicate plant known for its vibrant blue flowers. It thrives in moist, acidic soils and is often found in woodland areas. This species belongs to the Primulaceae family and is native to parts of Europe and Asia. Its unique coloration makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking a touch of color. Despite its beauty, it is often overlooked due to its modest size.
