Ardisia velutina

Ardisia velutina, commonly known as velvet ardisia, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, white to pale pink flowers that are clustered in spikes, followed by bright red berries. It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in shaded environments.

Ardisia velutina is also noted for its aromatic foliage and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
