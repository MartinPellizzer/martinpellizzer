Alonsoa procumbens

Alonsoa procumbens, commonly known as the creeping alonsoa, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that typically forms dense mats on the ground. The plant produces small, showy flowers that are often violet or purple in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
