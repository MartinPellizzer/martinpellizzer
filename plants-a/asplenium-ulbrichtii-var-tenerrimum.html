Asplenium ulbrichtii var. tenerrimum

Asplenium ulbrichtii var. tenerrimum, commonly known as the slender rock fern, is a rare and unique fern species native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, slender fronds that often grow in dense clusters on rocky surfaces. The plant exhibits a compact growth habit with small, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and slightly glossy. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions with high moisture levels.

Due to its limited distribution and fragile nature, it is considered a rare and endangered species in its native habitat.
