Aster magellanicus

Aster magellanicus, commonly known as the Magellanic aster, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its showy blue to purple flowers that bloom in the summer months. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss in its native environment.

Aster magellanicus is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
