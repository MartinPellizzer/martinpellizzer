Astrotricha pterocarpa

Astrotricha pterocarpa, commonly known as the winged bursage, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features distinctive, hairy, and often winged bracts that enclose its small, yellowish flowers. Its leaves are typically narrow and arranged alternately along the stem.

Astrotricha pterocarpa is adapted to arid environments, showcasing drought-resistant traits such as succulent stems and reduced leaf surfaces.
