Adicea gnidioides

Adicea gnidioides, commonly known as the Mediterranean thyme.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe and western Asia. The plant features small, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of pink to purple, typically in the spring and summer. Its leaves are narrow and aromatic, often used in traditional herbal remedies.

Adicea gnidioides thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy plant suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.
