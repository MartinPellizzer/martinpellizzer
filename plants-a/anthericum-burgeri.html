Anthericum burgeri

Anthericum burgeri, commonly known as the Burger's lily, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit, which allows it to spread and form dense ground cover. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Anthericum burgeri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
