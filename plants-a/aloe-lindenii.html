Aloe lindenii

Aloe lindenii, commonly known as the desert aloe, is a succulent plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It features fleshy, upright leaves that are typically blue-green in color, with a waxy coating to reduce water loss. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with small, tubular flowers that range from yellow to orange in hue. Aloe lindenii is well adapted to hot, dry climates and requires minimal water once established.

Its resilience makes it a popular choice for xeriscaping and rock gardens.
