Artemisia erlangshanensis

Artemisia erlangshanensis, commonly known as the Erlangshan wormwood, is a perennial shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, gray-green foliage. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters, typical of the genus Artemisia. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in alpine or mountainous regions.

Due to its medicinal properties, it has been used in traditional Chinese medicine for various health purposes.
