Anchusa mexicana

Anchusa mexicana, commonly known as Mexican blueweed, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a perennial herb with upright stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Anchusa mexicana is often found in rocky soils and open habitats, such as grasslands and disturbed areas.

It has been used traditionally in herbal medicine for its purported medicinal properties.
