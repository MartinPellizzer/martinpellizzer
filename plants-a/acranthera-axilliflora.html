Acranthera axilliflora

Acranthera axilliflora, commonly known as the axillary-flowered acranthera, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in grasslands and open woodlands. The plant features slender stems and small, clustered flowers that bloom in shades of white and pale pink. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often arranged in a basal rosette.

Acranthera axilliflora is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
