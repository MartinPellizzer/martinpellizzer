Ammoseris Sarmentosus

Ammoseris sarmentosus is a unique flowering plant native to arid regions of Africa. It is known for its striking blue or violet flowers and its ability to thrive in harsh, dry environments. This plant belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is often found in rocky soils and coastal areas. Its creeping growth habit and fragrant blooms make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
