Asplenium csikii

Asplenium csikii, commonly known as the Csik's spleenwort, is a small, terrestrial fern belonging to the family Aspleniaceae.

It is characterized by its compact, rosette-like growth habit and dark green, leathery fronds. The plant typically produces spore-bearing structures called sori on the underside of its leaves. Asplenium csikii is often found in moist, shaded environments such as rock crevices or forest floors.

Its unique morphology and habitat preferences make it an interesting subject for botanical study.
