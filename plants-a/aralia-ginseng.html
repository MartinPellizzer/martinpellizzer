Aralia ginseng

Aralia ginseng, commonly known as American ginseng, is a herbaceous plant native to eastern North America.

It belongs to the Araliaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, greenish-white flowers. The plant produces red berries that contain seeds, and its roots are the primary part used in traditional medicine. Aralia ginseng thrives in shady, moist environments and is often found in forested areas.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for both ecological and medicinal purposes.
