Athanasia calva

Athanasia calva, commonly known as the white daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking white petals and yellow central disc, which give it a daisy-like appearance. The plant typically grows in dry, sandy soils and is often found in grassland habitats. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Athanasia calva is also valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
