Anthurium camiloi

Anthurium camiloi, commonly known as the Camilo's arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to Central America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are often a deep green with a metallic sheen. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-shaped flowers surrounded by a colorful spathe, typically in red or orange hues. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect sunlight for optimal growth.

Anthurium camiloi is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
