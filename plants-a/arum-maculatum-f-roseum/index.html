Arum Maculatum F. Roseum

Arum maculatum f. roseum is a striking variant of the common white arum known for its unique pinkish hues. This plant features heart-shaped leaves and a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas. The roseum form adds a subtle elegance to its already dramatic appearance. Its beauty makes it a sought-after addition to botanical gardens and specialty collections.
