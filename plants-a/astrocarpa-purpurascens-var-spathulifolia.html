Astrocarpa purpurascens var. spathulifolia

Astrocarpa purpurascens var. spathulifolia, commonly known as the purple-flowered astrocarpa, is a striking member of the Acanthaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil, and thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that range in color from deep purple to violet, often with contrasting white or yellow markings. Its leaves are typically ovate to spatulate in shape, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

This species is valued in horticulture for its vibrant blooms and is often cultivated in shaded garden settings.
