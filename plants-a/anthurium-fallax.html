Anthurium fallax

Anthurium fallax, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are dark green with a reddish sheen. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-shaped inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, typically in shades of red or orange. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium fallax is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and long-lasting blooms.
