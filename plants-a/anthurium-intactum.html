Anthurium intactum

Anthurium intactum, commonly known as the "Intactum Arrowhead," is a tropical plant native to Central America.

It features large, heart-shaped leaves with a vibrant green color and a waxy texture. The plant produces striking spadices, which are the characteristic spathes of the Araceae family. Anthurium intactum thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for indoor gardening and ornamental purposes.
