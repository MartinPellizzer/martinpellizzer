Acuan bicornutum

Acuan bicornutum, commonly known as the two-horned water lily, is a aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features broad, floating leaves that provide shade and habitat for aquatic life. The plant produces distinctive white flowers with two prominent, horn-like projections on the petals. Its roots anchor in the mud at the bottom of water bodies, while its stems support the foliage.

Acuan bicornutum plays a vital role in maintaining water quality and supporting local biodiversity.
