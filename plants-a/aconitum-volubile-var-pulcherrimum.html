Aconitum volubile var. pulcherrimum

Aconitum volubile var. pulcherrimum, commonly known as the beautiful climbing aconite, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It features attractive, blue to purple bell-shaped flowers that bloom in summer, adding ornamental value to gardens. The plant has a fibrous root system and possesses a distinctive, often toxic, blue-green stem with prominent ridges. Its leaves are deeply lobed and can grow up to 15 cm in length, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Due to its toxicity, it should be handled with care and kept away from children and pets.
