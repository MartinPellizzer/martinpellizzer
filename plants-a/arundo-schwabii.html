Arundo schwabii

Arundo schwabii, commonly known as the Schwab's reed, is a tall, perennial grass native to southern Africa.

It features narrow, blade-like leaves and can grow up to 3 meters in height. The plant produces small, clustered flower heads that are typically green or brown in color. It is often found in wetland areas and along the edges of water bodies.

Botanically, it is characterized by its robust rhizomes and its ability to thrive in moist to wet soil conditions.
