Aglaonema princeps

Aglaonema princeps, commonly known as the silver queen plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated foliage featuring a mix of white, silver, and green hues. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and often have a waxy texture. It thrives in low-light conditions and requires moderate humidity to maintain its vibrant appearance.

Aglaonema princeps is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and air-purifying qualities.
