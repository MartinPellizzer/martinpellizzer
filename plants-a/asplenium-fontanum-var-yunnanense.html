Asplenium fontanum var. yunnanense

Asplenium fontanum var. yunnanense, commonly known as the Yunnan shield fern, is a rare fern species native to China.

It belongs to the family Aspleniaceae and is characterized by its compact, evergreen foliage. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as mountain forests. Its fronds are leathery and have a distinctive, shield-like shape, with spores produced on the underside of the leaves.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
