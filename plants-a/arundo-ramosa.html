Arundo ramosa

Arundo ramosa, commonly known as the slender reed, is a perennial grass native to parts of Asia and Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems that can grow up to 2 meters in height. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are typically green and slightly hairy. It thrives in moist environments such as wetlands, marshes, and along waterways.

Arundo ramosa is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil and filter water.
