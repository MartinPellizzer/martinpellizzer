Avena fatua var. alta

Avena fatua var. alta, commonly known as tall oat, is a variant of the common oat species characterized by its taller growth habit.

It is a perennial grass that typically grows in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions. The plant produces spikelets that contain small, oval grains, which are often dispersed by wind. It is known for its adaptability and can be found in agricultural fields and along roadsides.

Botanically, it is distinguished by its elongated inflorescence and robust root system.
