Arisaema heterophyllum var. nigropunctatum

Arisaema heterophyllum var. nigropunctatum, commonly known as the black-spotted arrowhead, is a member of the Araceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its distinctive arrow-shaped leaves with dark purple spots. The plant produces a spadix surrounded by a spathe, typical of the arum family. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for botanical gardens and private collections.
