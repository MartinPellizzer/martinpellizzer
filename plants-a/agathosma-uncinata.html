Agathosma uncinata

Agathosma uncinata, commonly known as African sage, is a perennial shrub native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Rutaceae and is characterized by its aromatic, small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in spring, followed by small, round, red fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
