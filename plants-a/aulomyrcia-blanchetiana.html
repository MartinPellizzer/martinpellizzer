Aulomyrcia blanchetiana

Aulomyrcia blanchetiana, commonly known as the golden flame tree, is a species of flowering plant native to the Caribbean.

It is a deciduous tree that can grow up to 15 meters tall, characterized by its striking golden-yellow foliage. The leaves are simple, alternate, and have a smooth texture, while the flowers are small and clustered in panicles. It produces small, round fruits that contain several seeds.

This plant thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
