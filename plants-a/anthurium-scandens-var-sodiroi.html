Anthurium scandens var. sodiroi

Anthurium scandens var. sodiroi, commonly known as the climbing anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a glossy texture. The plant produces bright red spadices surrounded by colorful, showy bracts, which are often mistaken for flowers. It thrives in humid, warm environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.

Anthurium scandens var. sodiroi is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its ornamental foliage and unique inflorescence.
