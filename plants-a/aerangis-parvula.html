Aerangis parvula

Aerangis parvula, commonly known as the small orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a small, herbaceous plant with delicate, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of white and pale pink. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as rainforests or under the canopy of larger trees. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, and it produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients.

Aerangis parvula is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
