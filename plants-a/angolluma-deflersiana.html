Angolluma deflersiana

Angolluma deflersiana, commonly known as the Flinders lily, is a striking evergreen shrub native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Xanthorrhoeaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of vibrant yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun, making it well-suited to arid and semi-arid environments. The leaves are narrow and grass-like, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
