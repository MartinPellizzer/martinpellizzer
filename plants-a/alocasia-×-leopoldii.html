Alocasia × leopoldii

Alocasia × leopoldii, commonly known as the Leopard Alocasia, is a hybrid plant that combines traits from several Alocasia species.

It is characterized by its striking, deeply veined, heart-shaped leaves that are often a mix of green, silver, and purple. The plant is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, where it thrives in humid, shady environments. Alocasia × leopoldii prefers well-draining soil and consistent moisture, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its ornamental value lies in its unique foliage, which adds a dramatic touch to any indoor or outdoor space.
