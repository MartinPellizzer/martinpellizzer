Anthurium consanguineum

Anthurium consanguineum, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves and vibrant red spadix, which is surrounded by a colorful spathe. The plant belongs to the Araceae family and is known for its ability to thrive in humid, warm environments. Its foliage is often used in ornamental horticulture due to its aesthetic appeal.

Anthurium consanguineum requires indirect light and well-draining soil to flourish.
