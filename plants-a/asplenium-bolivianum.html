Asplenium bolivianum

Asplenium bolivianum, commonly known as the Bolivian spleenwort, is a small, epiphytic fern native to South America.

It typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often divided into smaller segments. Its fronds are usually dark green and possess a delicate, moss-like appearance.

Asplenium bolivianum is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
