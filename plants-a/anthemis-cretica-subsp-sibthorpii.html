Anthemis cretica subsp. sibthorpii

Anthemis cretica subsp.

sibthorpii, commonly known as the Sibthorpi's tansy, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and yellowish-green foliage.

The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or pale yellow, which bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or arid environments.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
