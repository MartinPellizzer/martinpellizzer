Albizia duclouxii

Albizia duclouxii, commonly known as the Ducloux's mimosa, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant features bipinnate leaves with small, delicate leaflets that give it a feathery appearance. It produces yellowish-white flowers in clusters, which bloom in the spring and summer.

Albizia duclouxii is often used in traditional medicine and is also valued for its ornamental qualities.
