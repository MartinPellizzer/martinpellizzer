Athanasia indivisa

Athanasia indivisa, commonly known as the Immortality Plant, is a member of the Asteraceae family.

It is native to the Canary Islands and is characterized by its striking yellow-orange flowers and silver-green foliage. The plant produces small, oval-shaped fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value and unique appearance, it is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
