Astragalus sphaeranthus

Astragalus sphaeranthus, commonly known as globe milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of North America.

It is characterized by its spherical, inflated seed pods that give it its common name. The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and is adapted to drought conditions. It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and has small, yellow to green flowers.

Astragalus sphaeranthus is often found in steppe and shrubland habitats.
