Aphelexis filiformis

Aphelexis filiformis, commonly known as the slender milkweed, is a flowering plant native to parts of North America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in dry, open habitats such as prairies and rocky soils. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually pink or purple in color. Its leaves are narrow and linear, and the stems are often covered in fine, silky hairs.

Aphelexis filiformis is known for its milky sap, which is a characteristic feature of the milkweed genus.
