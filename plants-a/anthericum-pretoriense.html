Anthericum pretoriense

Anthericum pretoriense, commonly known as the Pretoria arum, is a herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It features a distinctive inflorescence composed of a spadix surrounded by a showy, greenish-white spathe. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in a dense cluster along the spadix. Its leaves are typically broad and arrow-shaped, with a smooth, waxy surface.

Anthericum pretoriense is often found in moist, shaded environments and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
