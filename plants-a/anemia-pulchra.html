Anemia pulchra

Anemia pulchra, commonly known as the beautiful anemia, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from white to deep pink. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest floors or along stream banks. Its leaves are broad and often have a slightly waxy texture, which helps retain moisture.

Anemia pulchra is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in humid climates.
