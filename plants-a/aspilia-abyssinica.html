Aspilia abyssinica

Aspilia abyssinica, commonly known as the Abyssinian torch, is a flowering plant native to Ethiopia.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking yellow-orange inflorescence. The plant features a tall, erect stem with narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often growing in rocky soils.

Aspilia abyssinica is valued for its ornamental beauty and has traditional uses in local communities.
