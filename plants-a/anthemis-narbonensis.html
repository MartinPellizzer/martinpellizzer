Anthemis narbonensis

Anthemis narbonensis, commonly known as the Narbonne anthemis, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or arid environments. The plant produces small, yellow to orange flowers that bloom in clusters, often in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a slightly hairy texture.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
