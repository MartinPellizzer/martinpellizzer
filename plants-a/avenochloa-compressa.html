Avenochloa compressa

Avenochloa compressa, commonly known as slender panicgrass, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It typically grows in wetland environments and is characterized by its compressed, flat stems and narrow leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear minute, wind-pollinated flowers. It is often found in marshes, swamps, and along the edges of water bodies.

Botanically, it is valued for its ecological role in stabilizing wetland soils and providing habitat for various wildlife species.
