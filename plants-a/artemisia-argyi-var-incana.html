Artemisia argyi var. incana

Artemisia argyi var. incana, commonly known as Chinese mugwort, is a perennial shrub native to East Asia.

It features silvery-gray, fuzzy foliage that is highly aromatic, with narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in panicles during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in dry, open habitats.

Due to its medicinal properties, it has been used in traditional Chinese medicine for centuries.
