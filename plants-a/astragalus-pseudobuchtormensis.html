Astragalus pseudobuchtormensis

Astragalus pseudobuchtormensis, commonly known as the Tien Shan milkvetch, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, often growing in rocky soils at high elevations.

It plays a role in soil stabilization and is of interest for its potential medicinal properties.
