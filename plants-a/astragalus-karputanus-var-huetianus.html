Astragalus karputanus var. huetianus

Astragalus karputanus var. huetianus, commonly known as the Karpuan milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to parts of Central Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant is typically found in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid conditions. It plays a role in soil stabilization and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.

Its seeds are often dispersed by wind, contributing to its distribution across its native range.
