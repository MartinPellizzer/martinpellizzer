Aristea nandiensis

Aristea nandiensis, commonly known as the Nandi's aristea, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, daisy-like flowers in shades of yellow to orange. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance, it is often cultivated in arid regions.
