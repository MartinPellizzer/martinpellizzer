Aloe reflexa

Aloe reflexa, commonly known as the narrow-leaf aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features tall, spindly stems with narrow, fleshy leaves that are often tinged with red or purple at the base. The leaves are covered in a thick, waxy cuticle that helps it retain moisture in arid environments. It produces clusters of yellow flowers during the summer months, which are followed by small, red berries.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in rock gardens or as a houseplant.
