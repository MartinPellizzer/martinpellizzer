Aster philippii

Aster philippii, commonly known as the Philippi's aster, is a flowering plant native to the western United States and parts of Canada.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies, sunflowers, and other composite flowers. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and rocky slopes. Its flowers are usually purple to blue in color, with ray florets surrounding a central disk of tubular flowers.

Aster philippii is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting local pollinators.
