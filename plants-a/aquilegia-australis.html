Aquilegia australis

Aquilegia australis, commonly known as the New Zealand columbine, is a perennial herb native to New Zealand.

It features striking blue or purple spurred flowers that bloom in spring and summer. The plant has compound leaves with serrated edges and grows up to 1 meter tall. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.

Its unique floral structure attracts pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
