Avena capillaris var. elegantissima

Avena capillaris var. elegantissima, commonly known as the slender oat, is a perennial grass species native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features slender, upright stems with fine, needle-like leaves that are typically bluish-green in color. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences bearing numerous tiny florets, which are wind-pollinated. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows, woodlands, and along stream banks.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, supporting a range of pollinators and wildlife.
