Asparagopsis sinica

Asparagopsis sinica, commonly known as Chinese asparagus fern, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the family Asparagaceae and is characterized by its compact, clumping growth habit. The plant features long, arching leaves that are typically bright green and strap-shaped. It produces small, inconspicuous white or greenish flowers followed by red berries.

Asparagopsis sinica is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and low maintenance requirements.
