Adesmia filicaulis

Adesmia filicaulis, commonly known as the slender leaf legume, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes many legumes known for their nitrogen-fixing abilities. The plant features trifoliate leaves and small, pod-like fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in tropical and subtropical regions, often found in dry, open habitats.

Its botanical characteristics make it an important species in ecological studies and traditional medicine.
