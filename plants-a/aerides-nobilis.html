Aerides nobilis

Aerides nobilis, commonly known as the noble orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem and large, showy flowers that are typically white or pale pink. The plant produces a single inflorescence with multiple blooms, and its roots are adapted to absorb moisture from the air. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting mix.

Aerides nobilis is often cultivated for its ornamental value in tropical gardens and greenhouses.
