Aerides compressa

Aerides compressa, commonly known as the compressed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, dense growth habit and produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its leaves are usually dark green and arranged in a rosette pattern, providing structural support.

Aerides compressa is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
