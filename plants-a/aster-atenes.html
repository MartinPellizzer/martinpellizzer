Aster atenes

Aster atenes, commonly known as the Atenes aster, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies, sunflowers, and other composite flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry soils and is characterized by its showy, purple to blue flower heads. Its leaves are usually gray-green and covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.

Aster atenes is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in poor soil conditions.
