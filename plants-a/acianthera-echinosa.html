Acianthera echinosa

Acianthera echinosa, commonly known as the spiny acianthera, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen growth habit and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. Acianthera echinosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and private collections.
