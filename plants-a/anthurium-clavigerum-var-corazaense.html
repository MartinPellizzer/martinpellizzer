Anthurium clavigerum var. corazaense

Anthurium clavigerum var. corazaense, commonly known as the Corazasense Anthurium, is a tropical plant native to parts of Central America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-shaped inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, often in shades of red or orange. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

This variety is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
