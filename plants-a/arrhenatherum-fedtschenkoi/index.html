Arrhenatherum Fedtschenkoi

Arrhenatherum fedtschenkoi is a rare grass species native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. It thrives in high-altitude environments where few plants can survive. This plant is characterized by its slender stems and narrow leaves that are adapted to cold and arid conditions. Due to its limited distribution, it is considered endangered and faces threats from habitat loss and climate change. Studying Arrhenatherum fedtschenkoi provides valuable insights into plant resilience in extreme climates.
