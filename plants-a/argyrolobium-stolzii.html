Argyrolobium stolzii

Argyrolobium stolzii, commonly known as the Stolz's silver globular veldtflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has a compact, cushion-like growth habit with hairy stems and leaves, adapted to arid and semi-arid environments. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open grasslands and rocky areas.

Argyrolobium stolzii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
