Avena aristelliformis

Avena aristelliformis, commonly known as the fairy grass, is a perennial grass species native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like foliage and small, clustered inflorescences that resemble miniature pine trees. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to dry, sunny environments. Its unique appearance has made it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Avena aristelliformis is also valued for its ability to stabilize soil and prevent erosion in arid landscapes.
