Avenastrum pratense f. spiculosum

Avenastrum pratense f. spiculosum, commonly known as the spiky meadow grass, is a flowering plant belonging to the Poaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands.

This variety is distinguished by its elongated, spiculate inflorescence, which gives it its specific epithet.
