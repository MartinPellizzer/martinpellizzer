Aphanocalyx hedinii

Aphanocalyx hedinii, commonly known as the Hedin's flame tree, is a small evergreen tree native to China.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves and clusters of small, yellow flowers. The plant produces small, flat pods that contain seeds, typical of the legume family.

It thrives in well-drained soil and is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its attractive foliage and flowering habit.
