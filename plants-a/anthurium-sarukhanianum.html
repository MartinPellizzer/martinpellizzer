Anthurium sarukhanianum

Anthurium sarukhanianum, commonly known as the Sarukhanian anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the Andean regions of South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves and vibrant, spadix-like inflorescence. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its foliage is often dark green with a glossy texture, and it produces colorful flowers that range from red to yellow.

Anthurium sarukhanianum is prized for its ornamental value and is commonly cultivated as a houseplant.
