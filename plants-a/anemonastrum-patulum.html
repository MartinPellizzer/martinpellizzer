Anemonastrum patulum

Anemonastrum patulum, commonly known as the broadleaf sandmat, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
