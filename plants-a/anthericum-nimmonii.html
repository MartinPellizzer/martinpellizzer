Anthericum nimmonii

Anthericum nimmonii, commonly known as the Nimmon's bellflower, is a rare and endangered plant species native to a limited area in the United Kingdom.

It is a perennial herb with a rosette of broad, lance-shaped leaves that form a dense cluster near the ground. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers that are typically blue or purple in color, blooming in late summer. Its botanical features include a fibrous root system and a short, stout stem that supports its floral display.
