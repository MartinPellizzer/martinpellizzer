Acianthera asaroides

Acianthera asaroides, commonly known as the asaroides orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, moist environments. The plant features a short, rhizomatous stem with several narrow, grass-like leaves. Its flowers are small and greenish-white, often with a faintly colored lip.

It is native to parts of South America, particularly Brazil, and is noted for its delicate appearance and specific habitat requirements.
