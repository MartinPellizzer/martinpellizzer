Aciphylla subflabellata

Aciphylla subflabellata, commonly known as the flabellate pincushion, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage and compact, cushion-like growth habit. Due to its unique appearance and ecological significance, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
