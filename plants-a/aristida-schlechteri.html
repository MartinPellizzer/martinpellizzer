Aristida schlechteri

Aristida schlechteri, commonly known as the slender grass or Schlechter's grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. The plant has narrow, erect leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences that are often clustered at the tips of the stems. Its botanical features include a fibrous root system and a tufted growth habit.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in native plant landscaping.
