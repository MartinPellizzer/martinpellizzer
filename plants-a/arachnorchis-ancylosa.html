Arachnorchis ancylosa

Arachnorchis ancylosa, commonly known as the golden spider-orchid, is an epiphytic orchid native to Australia.

It produces striking golden-yellow flowers that resemble spiders, hence its common name. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. Its roots are specialized for absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air.

It is a rare and protected species, found primarily in the southeastern regions of Australia.
