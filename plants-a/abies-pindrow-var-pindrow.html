Abies pindrow var. pindrow

Abies pindrow var. pindrow, commonly known as the Kashmiri fir, is a coniferous tree native to the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its slender, pyramidal shape and dark green, needle-like leaves. The tree produces cone-like structures that contain seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in cool, moist climates and is often found at high altitudes.

The bark is typically grayish-brown and becomes fissured with age.
