Aristea congesta

Aristea congesta, commonly known as the clustered arrowhead, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features dense clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. It is a perennial herb that thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Its unique appearance and adaptability make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
