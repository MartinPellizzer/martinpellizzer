Abacopteris presliana

Abacopteris presliana, commonly known as the giant fan palm, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, fan-shaped leaves that can reach several meters in diameter. The plant has a slender, unbranched trunk that can grow up to 10 meters tall. Its reproductive structures include small, clustered inflorescences with both male and female flowers.

The species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to warm, humid climates.
