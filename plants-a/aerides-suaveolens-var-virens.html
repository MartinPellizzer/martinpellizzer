Aerides suaveolens var. virens

Aerides suaveolens var. virens, commonly known as the fragrant orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its elegant, greenish-yellow flowers with a sweet, spicy fragrance. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, often with a waxy texture.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
