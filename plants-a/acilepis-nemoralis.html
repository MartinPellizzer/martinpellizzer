Acilepis nemoralis

Acilepis nemoralis, commonly known as the European rock rose, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Rosaceae.

It is characterized by its woody stems and dense clusters of small, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in spring. The plant is native to Europe and parts of western Asia, often found in rocky or limestone soils. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.
