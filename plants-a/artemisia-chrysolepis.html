Artemisia chrysolepis

Artemisia chrysolepis, commonly known as golden thread, is a shrub native to the western United States and parts of Canada.

It is characterized by its aromatic, silver-gray foliage and small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant thrives in dry, sunny environments and is often found in chaparral and woodland habitats. Its leaves contain essential oils that contribute to its strong, distinctive scent.

Artemisia chrysolepis is also valued for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
