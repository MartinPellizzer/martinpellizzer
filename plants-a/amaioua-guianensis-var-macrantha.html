Amaioua guianensis var. macrantha

Amaioua guianensis var. macrantha, commonly known as the Guianese amaioua, is a species native to the rainforests of South America.

It is a deciduous tree that can grow up to 30 meters tall, characterized by its smooth, grey bark and large, elliptical leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers followed by elongated, woody fruits containing numerous seeds.

It is often found in moist, shaded environments and is valued for its ornamental and ecological significance in tropical ecosystems.
