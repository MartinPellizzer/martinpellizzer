Aglaia bulusanensis

Aglaia bulusanensis, commonly known as the Bulusan aglaia, is a tropical tree native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Magnoliaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, compound leaves and showy yellow flowers. The plant produces small, woody fruits that contain several seeds. It is often found in lowland dipterocarp forests and is valued for its timber and medicinal properties.

Its botanical features make it an important component of the region's biodiversity.
