Aloe monticola

Aloe monticola, commonly known as the mountain aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, with a waxy coating that helps it retain moisture. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow flowers, which bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-draining soil and full sun, making it suitable for arid and semi-arid climates.

Aloe monticola is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional medicinal uses.
