Atropa revoluta

Atropa revoluta, commonly known as the purple star lily, is a striking perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features a basal rosette of lance-shaped, dark green leaves with a velvety texture. The plant produces striking clusters of pale purple to violet star-shaped flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Due to its ornamental value and toxic properties, it requires careful handling.
