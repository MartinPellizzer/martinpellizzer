Anthurium xanthosomifolium

Anthurium xanthosomifolium, commonly known as the golden-leaf anthurium, is a member of the Araceae family and is native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, broad, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a vibrant yellow-green color. The plant produces dramatic, spadix-like inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, often in shades of red or orange. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil.

Anthurium xanthosomifolium is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
