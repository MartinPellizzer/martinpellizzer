Anthemis melanoloma subsp. trapezuntica

Anthemis melanoloma subsp.

trapezuntica, commonly known as the Trapezuntic Anthemis, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family. It is characterized by its small, yellowish to brownish composite flowers and narrow, linear leaves.

Native to the eastern Mediterranean region, this subspecies thrives in rocky and arid environments. It typically grows in open habitats such as slopes and coastal areas.

The plant is noted for its aromatic properties and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
