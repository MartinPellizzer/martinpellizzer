Armeria alpina subsp. pubinervis

Armeria alpina subsp.

pubinervis, commonly known as the alpine thrift, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe. It features dense clusters of small, white or pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.

The plant has linear, grass-like leaves that are typically covered in fine, soft hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in alpine meadows and rocky habitats.

Its botanical characteristics make it well adapted to cold, high-altitude environments.
