Aichryson mollii

Aichryson mollii, commonly known as the Woolly Aichryson, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly gray-green foliage that covers its compact, rounded stems. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers during the summer months, which are followed by spherical, red berries. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Aichryson mollii is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
