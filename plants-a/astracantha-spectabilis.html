Astracantha spectabilis

Astracantha spectabilis, commonly known as the showy thornveld, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its distinctive, spiny bracts that enclose its yellow-orange flowers. The plant typically grows in grasslands and rocky areas, thriving in full sun. Its striking appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

The flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
