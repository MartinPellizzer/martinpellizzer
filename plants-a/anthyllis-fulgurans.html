Anthyllis fulgurans

Anthyllis fulgurans, commonly known as the flame vetch, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial shrub that produces bright yellow flowers, which bloom in early summer. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and is often found in rocky or sandy soils. It is adapted to arid environments and is drought-tolerant.

Anthyllis fulgurans plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various insects.
