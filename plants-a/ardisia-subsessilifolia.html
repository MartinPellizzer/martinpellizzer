Ardisia subsessilifolia

Ardisia subsessilifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved beautyberry, is a species of flowering plant in the family Ericaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are elliptical in shape.
