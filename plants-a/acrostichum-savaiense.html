Acrostichum savaiense

Acrostichum savaiense, commonly known as the Savaiʻi fern, is a species of tree fern native to the island of Savaiʻi in Samoa.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its large, tree-like structure with a stout, fibrous trunk. The plant has feather-like fronds that are typically dark green and can grow to considerable lengths. It thrives in humid, tropical environments and is often found in coastal areas and lowland forests.

The species is valued for its ornamental appearance and ecological importance in its native habitat.
