Asplenium colubrinum var. taeniophyllum

Asplenium colubrinum var. taeniophyllum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf rock fern, is a variant of the common rock fern.

It is a small, evergreen fern that typically grows in rocky or shaded environments. The plant features slender, leathery leaves with a distinctive narrow, elongated shape. Its fronds are usually dark green and have a finely serrated edge.

This variety is often found in limestone soils and is adapted to thrive in humid, cool climates.
