Abelia spathulata var. colorata

Abelia spathulata var. colorata, commonly known as the Chinese abelia or pink abelia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its oval-shaped leaves and clusters of fragrant, tubular flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The flowers are typically pink or white, with a distinctive red throat, adding visual interest to the plant. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained, moist soil.

This ornamental plant is valued for its showy blooms and adaptability to various garden conditions.
