Alsophila quadrata var. ivohibensis

Alsophila quadrata var. ivohibensis, commonly known as the Ivohibena tree fern, is a species native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnately compound fronds. The plant exhibits a slow growth rate and can reach heights of up to 10 meters in suitable conditions. Its rhizomes are thick and fibrous, providing structural support and aiding in nutrient absorption.

The fronds are evergreen, with a dark green coloration that contributes to its ornamental value in tropical gardens.
