Anchietea pyrifolia var. hilariana

Anchietea pyrifolia var. hilariana, commonly known as the Hilarian flame tree, is a striking tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters, creating a vibrant display during the flowering season. The plant has palmate leaves with lobed margins, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes in tropical and subtropical regions.
