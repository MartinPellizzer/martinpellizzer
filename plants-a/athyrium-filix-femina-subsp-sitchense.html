Athyrium filix-femina subsp. sitchense

Athyrium filix-femina subsp.

sitchense, commonly known as the Sichuan lady fern, is a rare and ornamental fern species native to China. It belongs to the Dryopteridaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, bipinnate fronds that display a graceful, feathery appearance.
