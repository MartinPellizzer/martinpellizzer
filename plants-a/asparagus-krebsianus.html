Asparagus krebsianus

Asparagus krebsianus, commonly known as the dwarf asparagus fern, is a low-growing perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features feathery, arching foliage composed of small, needle-like leaves that give it a delicate, fern-like appearance. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers in clusters, which are followed by small, red berries. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its compact size and attractive foliage make it a versatile addition to both indoor and outdoor gardens.
