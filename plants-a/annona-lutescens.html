Annona lutescens

Annona lutescens, commonly known as the yellow annona, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is a small to medium-sized tree or shrub that typically grows up to 5 meters in height. The plant features simple, alternate, ovate to elliptic leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. Its flowers are usually yellow or greenish-yellow, with a distinctive, fleshy calyx that persists after flowering.

The fruit is a spherical, yellow or orange berry that contains a single large seed, making it a unique and attractive botanical specimen.
