Aster ledifolius

Aster ledifolius, commonly known as the narrow-leaved aster, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America.

It typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and woodland edges. The plant features slender stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy. Its flowers are blue to purple in color, with five petals and numerous yellow stamens.

Aster ledifolius is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, providing nectar for pollinators.
