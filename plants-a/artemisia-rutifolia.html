Artemisia rutifolia

Artemisia rutifolia, commonly known as roughleaf sage, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows in arid and semi-arid environments. The plant features silvery-gray foliage and produces small, yellowish-green flower clusters. It has a strong, aromatic scent and is often used in traditional medicine.

Artemisia rutifolia is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in poor soil conditions.
