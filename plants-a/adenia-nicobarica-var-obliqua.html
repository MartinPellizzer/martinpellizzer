Adenia nicobarica var. obliqua

Adenia nicobarica var. obliqua, commonly known as the Nicobar Adenia, is a climbing shrub native to the Nicobar Islands.

It belongs to the family Passifloraceae and is characterized by its woody stems and compound leaves. The plant produces striking flowers with five sepals and five petals, often with a distinctive corona. Its fruits are fleshy berries that contain seeds, and it thrives in humid, tropical environments.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and has some traditional medicinal uses in local communities.
