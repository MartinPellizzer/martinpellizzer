Acuan velutinum

Acuan velutinum, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical plant native to regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine, velvety pubescence. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in terminal panicles. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release seeds.

Acuan velutinum is often found in wetlands and along riverbanks, where it thrives in moist, nutrient-rich soils.
