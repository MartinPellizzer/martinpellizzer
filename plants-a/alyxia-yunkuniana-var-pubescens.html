Alyxia yunkuniana var. pubescens

Alyxia yunkuniana var. pubescens, commonly known as the hairy alyxia, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has hairy foliage, which gives it its common name and helps in identifying it in the field. It thrives in humid, well-drained soils and is often found in forest understories.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional uses in local medicine.
