Argyrolobium lanceolatum

Argyrolobium lanceolatum, commonly known as the slender silver globular pea, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing, mat-forming perennial that produces clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves and is characterized by its distinctive silver-blue foliage. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance, it is cultivated in gardens in regions with a Mediterranean climate.
