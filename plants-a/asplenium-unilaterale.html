Asplenium unilaterale

Asplenium unilaterale, commonly known as the one-sided spleenwort, is a species of fern belonging to the family Aspleniaceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive, asymmetrical fronds that give it its common name. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often on rocks or soil in temperate regions. Its leaves are simple, with a lanceolate shape and a serrated margin.

Asplenium unilaterale is a small, terrestrial fern that is often found in forest understories and is relatively easy to cultivate in shaded garden settings.
