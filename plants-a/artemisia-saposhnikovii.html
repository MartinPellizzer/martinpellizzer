Artemisia saposhnikovii

Artemisia saposhnikovii, commonly known as Chinese wormwood, is a perennial herb native to Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, silver-green foliage. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions. Its leaves are lance-shaped and emit a strong, bitter odor when crushed.

Artemisia saposhnikovii is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional Chinese medicine.
