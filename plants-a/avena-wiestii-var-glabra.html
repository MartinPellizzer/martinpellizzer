Avena wiestii var. glabra

Avena wiestii var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth oat, is a perennial grass native to North America.

It is characterized by its slender, hairless stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It thrives in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands.

Its botanical features make it an important species for ecological studies and habitat restoration.
