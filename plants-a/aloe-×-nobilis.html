Aloe × nobilis

Aloe × nobilis, commonly known as the hybrid aloe or tiger aloe, is a succulent plant belonging to the Asphodelaceae family.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are thick, fleshy, and often have a waxy coating. The leaves are typically green with distinctive white or yellowish markings, giving it a striking appearance. It produces tall, branched inflorescences with clusters of yellow or orange flowers.

This plant is native to South Africa and is cultivated for its ornamental value and medicinal properties.
