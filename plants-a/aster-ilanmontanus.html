Aster ilanmontanus

Aster ilanmontanus, commonly known as the Ilanmontan aster, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to specific regions in Asia, particularly in the mountainous areas of China and Korea. The plant features showy, daisy-like flowers with purple to blue petals, often surrounded by white ray florets. It typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in temperate climates.

The leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
