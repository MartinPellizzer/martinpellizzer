Anulocaulis leiosolenus

Anulocaulis leiosolenus, commonly known as the smooth-stemmed anulocaulis, is a member of the Gesneriaceae family and is native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its slender, cylindrical stems that are often covered in a smooth, waxy cuticle. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically bright yellow or orange in color. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a glossy surface.

Anulocaulis leiosolenus is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
