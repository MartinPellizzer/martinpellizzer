Aloe aageodonta

Aloe aageodonta, commonly known as the Cape aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that store water, a characteristic common among succulents. The plant produces tall, branching inflorescences with yellow or orange flowers. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their soothing properties.

Aloe aageodonta prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for arid environments.
