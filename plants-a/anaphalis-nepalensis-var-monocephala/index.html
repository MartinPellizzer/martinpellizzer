Anaphalis Nepalensis Var. Monocephala

Anaphalis nepalensis var. monocephala is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the high altitudes of Nepal. It is known for its unique appearance, featuring a single, prominent flower atop a slender stem. This variety belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its woolly foliage. Due to its limited habitat and environmental sensitivity, it is considered a vulnerable species. Conservation efforts are crucial to protect this rare botanical gem from habitat loss and climate change.
