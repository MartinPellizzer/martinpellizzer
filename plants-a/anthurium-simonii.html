Anthurium simonii

Anthurium simonii, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped spathes that range from red to orange in color. The plant produces small, green spadices that are typically hidden within the spatulate leaf. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium simonii is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its striking foliage and long-lasting blooms.
