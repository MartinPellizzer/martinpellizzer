Astilbe astilboides

Astilbe astilboides, commonly known as the white feather astilbe, is a deciduous perennial shrub native to the mountainous regions of China.

It features finely divided, fern-like leaves that are dark green and glossy, providing a striking visual contrast to its delicate, plume-like inflorescences. The flowers, which bloom in late spring to early summer, are typically white or pale pink and are borne on tall, slender stalks. This plant thrives in partial to full shade and prefers moist, well-drained soil rich in organic matter.

It is a popular choice for gardeners seeking to add texture and color to woodland or shaded garden settings.
