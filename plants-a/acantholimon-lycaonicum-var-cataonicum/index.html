Acantholimon Lycaonicum Var. Cataonicum

Acantholimon lycaonicum var. cataonicum is a rare and delicate plant native to the mountainous regions of eastern Turkey. It thrives in rocky, arid environments where few other species can survive. This variant is distinguished by its unique leaf structure and pale blue flowers that bloom in early spring. Due to habitat loss and climate change, it faces increasing threats to its survival. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this rare botanical gem.
