Aloinopsis setifera

Aloinopsis setifera, commonly known as the narrow-leaved aloinopsis, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asphodelaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-forming growth pattern. The plant features narrow, blue-green leaves with white markings, and it produces yellow flowers in spring. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with full sun exposure.

Aloinopsis setifera is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and low maintenance requirements.
