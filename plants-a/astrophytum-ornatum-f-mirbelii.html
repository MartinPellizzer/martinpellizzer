Astrophytum ornatum f. mirbelii

Astrophytum ornatum f. mirbelii, commonly known as the Mirbel's moon cactus, is a unique cactus species native to the United States.

It is characterized by its short, spherical stem that is often covered in a dense layer of white, woolly hairs. The plant produces yellow flowers during the summer months, which are followed by small, red fruits. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it well-suited for arid environments.

Its distinctive appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for succulent collectors and desert gardens.
