Aridaria viridiflora

Aridaria viridiflora, commonly known as the green-flowered desert shrub, is a resilient plant adapted to arid environments.

It features narrow, waxy leaves that reduce water loss and enhance drought tolerance. The plant produces small, bright green flowers that bloom during brief rainy seasons. Its root system is extensive, allowing it to access deep groundwater sources.

Aridaria viridiflora plays a crucial role in stabilizing sandy soils and supporting local wildlife in desert ecosystems.
