Agelaea wallichii

Agelaea wallichii, commonly known as the Himalayan orchid, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its elegant, showy flowers. The plant typically grows in high-altitude environments, often in rocky or forested areas. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern, while its flowers are usually pink or white.

Agelaea wallichii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
