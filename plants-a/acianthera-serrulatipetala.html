Acianthera serrulatipetala

Acianthera serrulatipetala, commonly known as the serrated petal orchid, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, serrated petals and a delicate, fragrant flower structure. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments within the rainforests of countries like Brazil and Peru. Its floral morphology includes a slender stem with clustered blooms, and it is often found in association with other epiphytic orchids.

Botanically, it belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological significance in its native habitat.
