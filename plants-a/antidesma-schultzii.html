Antidesma schultzii

Antidesma schultzii, commonly known as the African starfruit tree, is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub or tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and opposite, ovate to elliptical leaves. The fruit is valued for its tangy flavor and is used in local cuisines and traditional medicine.
