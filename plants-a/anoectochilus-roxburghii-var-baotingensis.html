Anoectochilus roxburghii var. baotingensis

Anoectochilus roxburghii var. baotingensis, commonly known as the Baotingen orchid, is a rare and endemic species native to China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and attractive, lanceolate leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers with a distinctive lip that is often yellowish or reddish in color. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found in forest understories at high altitudes.
