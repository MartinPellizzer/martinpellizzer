Arbutus × reyorum

Arbutus × reyorum, commonly known as the California madrone.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to the western United States. The plant features smooth, peeling bark that ranges from red to gray in color. Its leaves are glossy and dark green, turning red in the fall.

The plant produces clusters of white or pinkish flowers in the spring, followed by red or orange berries.
