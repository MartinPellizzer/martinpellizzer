Anthurium pangoanum

Anthurium pangoanum, commonly known as the Pangoanum arrowhead, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, glossy green leaves that are often adorned with intricate patterns of veins. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-like inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, typically in shades of red or orange. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and botanical collections.
