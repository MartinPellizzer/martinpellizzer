Alternanthera williamsii

Alternanthera williamsii, commonly known as the red-veined sorrel, is a striking aquatic plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red veins contrasting with its dark green leaves. The plant thrives in warm, tropical climates and is often cultivated in freshwater aquariums. It prefers moderate to high light conditions and can grow both submerged and as a floating plant.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for aquascaping and water garden designs.
