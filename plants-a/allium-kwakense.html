Allium kwakense

Allium kwakense, commonly known as the Kwakense onion, is a perennial bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and clusters of white to pale pink flowers. The plant produces small, round bulbs that are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Allium kwakense is valued for its ornamental appeal and medicinal properties.
