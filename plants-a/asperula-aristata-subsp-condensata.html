Asperula aristata subsp. condensata

Asperula aristata subsp.

condensata, commonly known as the dense asperula, is a compact evergreen shrub native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe. It features small, elliptical leaves that are densely packed, giving the plant a lush, bushy appearance.

The flowers are typically white or pale pink, arranged in clusters and blooming in early spring. It prefers well-drained, acidic soils and partial shade, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in cooler climates.
