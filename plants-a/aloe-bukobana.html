Aloe bukobana

Aloe bukobana, commonly known as the Bukoba aloe, is a succulent plant native to Tanzania.

It belongs to the Asphodelaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are thick and fleshy. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. Aloe bukobana is adapted to arid environments and stores water in its leaves for survival.

Its unique appearance and hardiness make it a popular choice for succulent gardens and collections.
