Anthurium morae

Anthurium morae, commonly known as the Morae anthurium, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a glossy texture. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-shaped inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, often in red, orange, or yellow hues. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Anthurium morae is popular as a decorative plant due to its ornamental foliage and unique floral structure.
