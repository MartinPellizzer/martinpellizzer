Afrocalliandra gilbertii

Afrocalliandra gilbertii, commonly known as Gilbert's calliandra, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

Afrocalliandra gilbertii is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its attractive foliage and floral display.
