Aglaonema grande

Aglaonema grande, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, glossy, dark green leaves with striking white and cream variegation. The plant thrives in low-light conditions and is known for its ability to purify indoor air. Its foliage remains vibrant throughout the year, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Aglaonema grande is relatively easy to care for, requiring moderate watering and well-draining soil.
