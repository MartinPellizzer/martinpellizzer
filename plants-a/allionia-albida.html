Allionia albida

Allionia albida, commonly known as the white allionia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllis family and is characterized by its striking white, star-shaped flowers. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of tubular flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Allionia albida is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
