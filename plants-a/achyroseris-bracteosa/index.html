Achyroseris Bracteosa

Achyroseris bracteosa is a striking desert plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico. It is known for its vibrant yellow flowers and ability to thrive in harsh, arid environments. This species belongs to the sunflower family and is often found in rocky soils and open grasslands. Its unique adaptations allow it to survive extreme temperatures and limited water availability. The plant plays an important role in supporting local pollinators and maintaining ecosystem balance.
