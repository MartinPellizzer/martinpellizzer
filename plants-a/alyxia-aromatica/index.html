Alyxia Aromatica

Sweet alyssum, scientifically known as Alyxia aromatica, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas. It is prized for its delicate, fragrant blooms that range from white to pink and lavender. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and is often used in ornamental gardening for its aesthetic appeal. Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for rock gardens, borders, and containers. Despite its beauty, it requires well-drained soil and partial shade to flourish optimally.
