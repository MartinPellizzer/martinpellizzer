Agapanthus africanus var. maximus

Agapanthus africanus var. maximus, commonly known as the African lily or blue agapanthus, is a striking perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, upright stems that bear large, showy clusters of blue or violet flowers, which bloom in summer. The plant has strap-like, evergreen leaves that form a dense rosette at the base. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
