Albizia minahassae var. ledermannii

Albizia minahassae var. ledermannii, commonly known as the Ledermann's Albizia, is a flowering plant native to Indonesia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its pod fruits and nitrogen-fixing capabilities. The plant features compound leaves with small, oval leaflets and produces fragrant, fluffy yellow or white flowers. It thrives in tropical climates and is often found in forested areas.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and for ecological purposes.
