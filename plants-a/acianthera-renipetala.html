Acianthera renipetala

Acianthera renipetala, commonly known as the wrinkled leaf orchid, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its unique, wrinkled leaves and small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant prefers shaded, humid environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions. It has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients.

Acianthera renipetala is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or shaded gardens.
