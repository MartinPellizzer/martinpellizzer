Anemonastrum tenuicaule

Anemonastrum tenuicaule, commonly known as the slender-stemmed anemone, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of North America.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that consist of both ray and disk florets. The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and is adapted to withstand drought conditions. It produces small, yellowish to white flowers that bloom in the spring or early summer.

Anemonastrum tenuicaule is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and open fields.
