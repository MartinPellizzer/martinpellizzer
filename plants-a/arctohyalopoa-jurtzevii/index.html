Arctohyalopoa Jurtzevii

Arctohyalopoa jurtzevii is a rare and enigmatic flowering plant native to the remote highlands of Southeast Asia. This unique species belongs to the orchid family and is known for its striking, intricate blooms that resemble delicate lace. Due to its limited distribution and fragile habitat, it is considered critically endangered by conservationists. Despite its beauty, little is known about its ecological role or reproductive habits.
