Areca triandra var. pumila

Areca triandra var. pumila, commonly known as the dwarf areca palm, is a compact variety of the areca palm native to Southeast Asia.

It features slender, arching fronds that give it a graceful appearance, making it suitable for indoor or outdoor cultivation. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences with yellowish-white flowers, followed by bright orange fruits. It prefers well-drained soil and bright, indirect light, requiring minimal maintenance.

Its slow growth rate makes it an ideal choice for small spaces or as a decorative accent plant.
