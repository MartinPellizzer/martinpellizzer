Aerides multiflora

Aerides multiflora, commonly known as the many-flowered orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically produces multiple flower spikes, each bearing numerous blooms, which give it its common name. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Aerides multiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
