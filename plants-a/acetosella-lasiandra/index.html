Acetosella Lasiandra

Acetosella lasiandra is a small, mat-forming plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and western Asia. It is known for its delicate, clustered leaves and tiny white flowers that bloom in early spring. This species thrives in moist, acidic soils and is often found in shaded, forested areas. Due to its limited distribution and specialized habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered plant. Conservation efforts are crucial to protect this unique species from habitat loss and environmental changes.
