Athyrium austroyunnanense

Athyrium austroyunnanense, commonly known as the Yunnan lady fern, is a species of fern native to China.

It belongs to the family Athyriaceae and is characterized by its delicate, bipinnate fronds that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. The plant prefers moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions with cool, humid climates. Its rhizomes are creeping and produce new fronds throughout the growing season.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens.
