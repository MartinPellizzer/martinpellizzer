Aeonium × edgari

Aeonium × edgari, commonly known as the Crown of Thorns, is a hybrid succulent plant belonging to the Crassulaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact, rosette-shaped growth pattern and fleshy, thick leaves that are often blue-green in color. The plant produces showy, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink, red, or orange during the spring and summer months. Native to the Canary Islands, it is well adapted to arid and rocky environments.

Aeonium × edgari is popular as a decorative plant due to its aesthetic appeal and low maintenance requirements.
