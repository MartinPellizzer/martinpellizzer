Albizia robinsonii

Albizia robinsonii, commonly known as the silky albizia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its compound leaves, which are pinnately arranged and often covered in fine, silky hairs. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically pale yellow or cream in color. Its fruit is a flat, pod-like structure that splits open to release seeds.

Albizia robinsonii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
