Aralia houheensis

Aralia houheensis, commonly known as the Chinese aralia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features compound leaves with a distinctive, feathery appearance, and its flowers are small, greenish-white, and clustered in umbels. The plant produces berries that are typically red or black when mature. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adaptable to a variety of soil types.

Aralia houheensis is also valued in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.
