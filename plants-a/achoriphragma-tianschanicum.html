Achoriphragma tianschanicum

Achoriphragma tianschanicum, commonly known as the Tianshan sandwort, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, particularly the Tianshan range.

The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink, blooming in the summer months. Its leaves are succulent and fleshy, adapted to store water in its native habitat.
