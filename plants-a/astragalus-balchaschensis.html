Astragalus balchaschensis

Astragalus balchaschensis, commonly known as the Balchaschen milkvetch, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to Central Asia and is typically found in arid and semi-arid regions. The plant is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellowish flowers that bloom in early summer. It has adapted to survive in harsh environments with limited water availability.

Astragalus balchaschensis plays a role in soil stabilization and is of interest for its potential medicinal properties.
