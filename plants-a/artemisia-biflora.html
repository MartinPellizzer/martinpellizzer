Artemisia biflora

Artemisia biflora, commonly known as silverleaf sage, is a perennial shrub native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flower heads that appear in late summer. It thrives in dry, well-drained soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid regions.

Artemisia biflora is valued for its aromatic properties and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
