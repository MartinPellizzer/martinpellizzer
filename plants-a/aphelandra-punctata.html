Aphelandra punctata

Aphelandra punctata, commonly known as the spotted flame plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, elongated leaves that are often variegated with yellow and green markings. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, orange-red flowers that bloom throughout the year in warm climates. Its foliage and blooms make it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical landscapes.

Aphelandra punctata thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture to maintain its vibrant appearance.
