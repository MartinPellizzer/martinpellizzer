Anthocercis × tenuipes

Anthocercis × tenuipes, commonly known as the slender-leafed orchid, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, elongated leaves and small, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pale yellow. Native to the subtropical regions of Southeast Asia, this plant thrives in moist, shaded environments. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its elegant appearance and unique floral structure.

The species is also noted for its ability to adapt to various soil types, making it a versatile choice for horticultural purposes.
