Andropogon Rubens

Andropogon rubens is a striking grass species native to the southeastern united states. it is known for its vibrant crimson plumes that appear in late summer and fall. this plant thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for landscapes and gardens. its ornamental value has led to its use in native plant gardens and restoration projects. andropogon rubens also plays an important role in supporting local wildlife and pollinators.
