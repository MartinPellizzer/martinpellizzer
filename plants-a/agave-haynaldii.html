Agave haynaldii

Agave haynaldii, commonly known as the narrow-leaved agave, is a succulent plant native to the arid regions of Mexico.

It features a rosette of narrow, gray-green leaves that are often covered in a whitish bloom, which helps reduce water loss. The plant produces a tall flower stalk bearing yellowish-green flowers, which bloom in late spring or early summer. It is well adapted to drought conditions and thrives in full sun.

Its striking appearance makes it a popular choice for xeriscaping and rock gardens.
