Arundo vallatoria var. variegata

Arundo vallatoria var. variegata, commonly known as the variegated giant reed, is a striking ornamental grass native to southern Europe.

It features tall, slender culms with striking variegated foliage that displays shades of green, white, and yellow. The plant thrives in wet or moist environments, often found near water bodies such as ponds and streams. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a fibrous texture that contributes to its structural appeal.

It is valued for its aesthetic qualities and ability to provide habitat for wildlife in wetland ecosystems.
