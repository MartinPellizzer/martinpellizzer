Anil filifolia

Anil filifolia, commonly known as the silky filifolia, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its slender, branching stems and small, narrow leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are typically aromatic and have been used in traditional herbal remedies.

Anil filifolia thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a suitable plant for rock gardens and dry landscapes.
