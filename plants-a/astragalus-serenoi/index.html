Astragalus Serenoi

Astragalus serenoi is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the high elevations of the Sierra Nevada mountains. It thrives in rocky soils and cool, moist environments where few other plants can survive. This species is known for its small, pale purple blooms that appear in late summer. Due to habitat loss and climate change, Astragalus serenoi is facing increasing threats to its survival. Conservation efforts are underway to protect and preserve this unique and endangered species.
