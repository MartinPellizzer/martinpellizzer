Anil nummulariifolia

Anil nummulariifolia, commonly known as the silver leaf tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, round, silvery-gray leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are often clustered together. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Anil nummulariifolia is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.
