Albizia retusa subsp. morobei

Albizia retusa subsp.

The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant, cream-colored flowers, which bloom during the wet season. Its leaves are bipinnate, consisting of numerous small leaflets, and it has a fibrous root system that helps stabilize soil.
