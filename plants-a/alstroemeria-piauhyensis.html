Alstroemeria piauhyensis

Alstroemeria piauhyensis, commonly known as the Piauhy lily, is a perennial flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Alstroemeriaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, orange, and yellow. The plant has strap-like leaves and produces inflorescences that are often used in floral arrangements.

It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
