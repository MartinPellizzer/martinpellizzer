Aphelandra atrovirens

Aphelandra atrovirens, commonly known as the black-leafed flame plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, glossy leaves and striking, orange-red bracts that resemble flowers. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and high humidity to flourish.

Aphelandra atrovirens is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its vibrant appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
