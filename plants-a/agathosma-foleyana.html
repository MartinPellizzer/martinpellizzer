Agathosma foleyana

Agathosma foleyana, commonly known as the Cape citrus or foley's agathosma, is a small shrub native to the Western Cape region of South Africa.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family, which includes citrus plants, and is characterized by its aromatic, narrow leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are followed by elliptical, greenish-yellow fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun, making it suitable for rocky or sandy environments.

Due to its unique appearance and fragrance, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
