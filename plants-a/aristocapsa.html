Aristocapsa

Aristocapsa, commonly known as the golden crown, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the family Acanthaceae and is characterized by its striking, showy inflorescences. The plant features lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of yellow or orange flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.
