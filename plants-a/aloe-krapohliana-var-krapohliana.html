Aloe krapohliana var. krapohliana

Aloe krapohliana var. krapohliana, commonly known as the Krapohliana aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically gray-green in color, often with a waxy coating to reduce water loss. The plant produces tall, branched stems with clusters of yellow flowers during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its leaves and stems.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and resilience in dry conditions.
