Arachniodes × mitsuyoshiana

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its delicate, lace-like fronds that resemble spider webs. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as forests or rocky crevices. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread slowly and form dense mats.
