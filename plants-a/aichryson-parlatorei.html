Aichryson parlatorei

Aichryson parlatorei, commonly known as the Parlatorei Aichryson, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, ribbed stems and small, spiny leaves that help it store water in arid environments. The plant produces yellow flowers during the summer months, adding a striking visual element to its appearance. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
