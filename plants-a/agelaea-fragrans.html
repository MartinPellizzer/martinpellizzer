Agelaea fragrans

Agelaea fragrans, commonly known as the sweet-scented orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its fragrant, showy flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its leaves are long and strap-like, and it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Agelaea fragrans is valued for its ornamental beauty and aromatic properties.
