Audibertiella incana

Audibertiella incana, commonly known as the silky lichen, is a member of the family Parmeliaceae.

It is a foliose lichen, characterized by its leaf-like thallus that is typically pale green to yellowish in color. Its surface is often covered with a fine, silky layer of hairs, which gives it its common name.

Audibertiella incana is typically found on bark and rock surfaces in temperate forest environments.
