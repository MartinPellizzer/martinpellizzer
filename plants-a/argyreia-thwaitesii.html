Argyreia thwaitesii

Argyreia thwaitesii, commonly known as the velvet tangle or sweet tangle, is a climbing shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage and clusters of small, white, star-shaped flowers. The plant produces bright red, fleshy berries that are edible but contain toxic seeds. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in forested areas.

Its rapid growth and attractive appearance make it a popular ornamental plant in horticulture.
