Aloe chlorantha

Aloe chlorantha, commonly known as the green aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically a deep green color, often with a bluish or grayish tint. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers during the spring and summer months. Its leaves contain a gel-like substance that is used in traditional medicine and skincare products.

Aloe chlorantha is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its leaves to survive in dry conditions.
