Aeonium palmense

Aeonium palmense, commonly known as the "Tree Aeonium," is a succulent plant native to the Canary Islands.

It features a rosette of fleshy, blue-green leaves that form a compact, tree-like structure. The plant produces tall, branched flower stalks that bear yellow flowers in the spring. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with ample sunlight.

Aeonium palmense is popular in rock gardens and as a decorative houseplant due to its unique appearance.
