Acrostichum nummulariifolium var. subpeltatum

Acrostichum nummulariifolium var. subpeltatum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf fern, is a variant of the broad-leaf fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial fern characterized by its finely divided, narrow leaves that form a dense, mat-like growth. The plant prefers moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its fronds are typically bright green and can reach lengths of up to 1 meter.

The species is valued for its ornamental appearance and is commonly cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
