Aquilegia Coerulea Subsp. Albiflora

Aquilegia coerulea subsp. albiflora is a rare and beautiful plant native to the mountainous regions of western North America. Known for its striking white flowers and delicate foliage, it stands out among other members of the genus Aquilegia. This subspecies thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in shaded forest understories. Its unique appearance makes it a popular subject for botanists and gardeners alike. Conservation efforts are crucial to protect this fragile species from habitat loss and climate change.
