Adiantum orosiense

Adiantum orosiense, commonly known as the Orosi maidenhair fern, is a species of fern native to the mountainous regions of Central America.

It belongs to the family Adiantaceae and is characterized by its delicate, lace-like fronds that are typically bi-lobed. The plant prefers moist, shaded environments and is often found in cloud forests. Its rhizomes are creeping and produce numerous erect, slender stems.

Adiantum orosiense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in horticultural settings.
