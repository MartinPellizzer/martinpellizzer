Aster palmeri

Aster palmeri, commonly known as Palmer's aster, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a member of the sunflower family (Asteraceae) and is characterized by its showy purple to blue floral heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in desert or semi-desert environments. It produces both ray and disk flowers, with the ray flowers being the most prominent and colorful.

Aster palmeri is often used in native plant gardens due to its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
