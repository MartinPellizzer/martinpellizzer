Arctostaphylos Adenotricha

Arctostaphylos adenotricha is a rare evergreen shrub native to the western United States. It is known for its dense, prickly foliage and ability to thrive in harsh, dry environments. This plant is often found in chaparral and coastal scrub habitats. Its unique adaptations make it an important species for local ecosystems. Due to habitat loss, it faces increasing threats to its survival.
