Althaea Haussknechtii

Althaea haussknechtii is a rare and lesser-known species of flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. It belongs to the mallow family, known for its showy blooms and medicinal properties. This plant is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and vibrant pink or purple flowers that bloom in late summer. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered endangered and requires conservation efforts. Its unique appearance and ecological significance make it a fascinating subject for botanical study.
