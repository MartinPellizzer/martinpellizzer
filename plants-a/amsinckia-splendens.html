Amsinckia splendens

Amsinckia splendens, commonly known as the golden crownbeard, is a flowering plant native to California and Baja California.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking yellow ray flowers and silver-green foliage. The plant produces small, spiny bracts that surround its flowering heads, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Amsinckia splendens is a hardy perennial that is well adapted to Mediterranean climates.
