Aloe × caesia

Aloe × caesia, commonly known as the hybrid aloe or the hybrid aloe vera, is a naturally occurring hybrid between Aloe vera and Aloe arborescens.

It is characterized by its tall, branching stems and large, fleshy, greenish-yellow leaves that are often covered in small teeth. The plant produces showy orange or red flowers in clusters, which appear at the ends of the stems. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for xeriscaping and container gardening.

Aloe × caesia is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some medicinal uses similar to its parent species.
