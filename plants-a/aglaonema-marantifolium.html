Aglaonema marantifolium

Aglaonema marantifolium, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated foliage with shades of green, white, and pink. The plant features thick, waxy leaves that are arranged in a spiral pattern along its upright stem. It thrives in low-light conditions and requires moderate humidity and well-draining soil.

Aglaonema marantifolium is popular as a houseplant due to its aesthetic appeal and air-purifying qualities.
