Astrocaryum standleyanum

Astrocaryum standleyanum, commonly known as the red pepper palm, is a flowering plant native to the Amazon rainforest.

It belongs to the Arecaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and pinnate leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that develop into edible, red berries. These berries are rich in nutrients and are often used in traditional medicine and cuisine.
