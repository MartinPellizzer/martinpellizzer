Anacampseros sempervirens

Anacampseros sempervirens, commonly known as the evergreen stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, thick leaves that are typically blue-green in color, adapted to store water in arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit and low maintenance requirements make it ideal for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.
