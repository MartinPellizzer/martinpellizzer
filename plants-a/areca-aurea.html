Areca aurea

Areca aurea, commonly known as the golden areca palm, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like leaves that give it a striking appearance. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in tropical and subtropical regions.

Areca aurea is also known for its ability to purify indoor air, making it a beneficial addition to homes and offices.
