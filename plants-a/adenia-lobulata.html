Adenia lobulata

Adenia lobulata, commonly known as the African star gooseberry, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Passifloraceae and is characterized by its woody stems and alternate leaves. Its leaves are typically ovate with a heart-shaped base, and the plant is often found in moist, wooded areas.

Adenia lobulata is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used traditionally in some regions for its medicinal properties.
