Abelmoschus praecox var. pubescens

Abelmoschus praecox var. pubescens, commonly known as okra, is an annual herbaceous plant native to Africa.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its spiny stems and large, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces green, fibrous pods that mature to a yellow or red color, containing numerous seeds. It thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
