Amphiblemma gossweileri var. gossweileri

Amphiblemma gossweileri var. gossweileri, commonly known as the Cape snowbush, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, fleshy foliage and produces clusters of small, white, star-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments. It is often found in coastal regions and has a compact, bushy growth habit.
