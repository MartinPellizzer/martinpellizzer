Argyrolobium connatum

Argyrolobium connatum, commonly known as the white globular pea, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its spherical, white seed pods that contain several small, round seeds. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Argyrolobium connatum is a perennial herb that is valued for its ornamental appearance and ecological importance.
