Anthurium harrisii var. pulchrum

Anthurium harrisii var. pulchrum, commonly known as the red-veined anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves with deep green foliage and prominent red veins. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-shaped inflorescences surrounded by a showy, white to pink spathe. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated as a houseplant.
