Adoketon maritimum

Adoketon maritimum, commonly known as sea holly, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of Europe and western Asia.

It is characterized by its spiny, blue or purple flowers and silver-gray, fuzzy leaves that help reduce water loss in salty environments. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dunes and rocky shores. Its unique appearance makes it a popular ornamental plant in gardens.

Adoketon maritimum plays a role in stabilizing coastal ecosystems by preventing soil erosion.
