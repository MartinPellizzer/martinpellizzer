Anthurium juanguillermoi

Anthurium juanguillermoi, commonly known as the Juan Guillermo's arrowhead, is a striking tropical plant native to Colombia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped leaves that often display a deep green with red or purple veins. The plant produces a spadix, which is a unique inflorescence typical of the Araceae family, surrounded by a colorful spathe. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor and greenhouse cultivation.
