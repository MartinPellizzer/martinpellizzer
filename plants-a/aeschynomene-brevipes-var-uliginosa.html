Aeschynomene brevipes var. uliginosa

Aeschynomene brevipes var. uliginosa, commonly known as marsh sesban, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a herbaceous annual with slender, erect stems and small, clustered leaves. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that are typically clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in wet, marshy habitats and is often found in disturbed soils.

Its seeds are dispersed by wind, contributing to its ability to colonize new areas.
