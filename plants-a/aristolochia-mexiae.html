Aristolochia mexiae

Aristolochia mexiae, commonly known as the Mexican birthwort, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and parts of Central America.

It is characterized by its unique, pipe-shaped flowers that are often greenish or reddish in color. The plant belongs to the Aristolochiaceae family and is known for its complex, intricate floral structure. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a rough texture.

The plant contains various chemical compounds, some of which have been studied for potential medicinal properties.
