Adhatoda Engleriana

Blue trumpet, scientifically known as Adhatoda engleriana, is a striking plant native to tropical regions of Asia. It is renowned for its vibrant blue and purple trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. This evergreen shrub is often cultivated for its ornamental value and medicinal properties. The plant has been traditionally used in Ayurvedic and folk medicine for its therapeutic benefits. Its unique appearance and ecological significance make it a fascinating subject for botanical study.
