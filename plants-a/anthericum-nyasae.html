Anthericum nyasae

Anthericum nyasae, commonly known as the Nyasaland arrowhead, is a perennial herb native to the wetlands of East Africa.

It features arrowhead-shaped leaves that are often submerged or floating, adapted to aquatic environments. The plant produces small, white flowers arranged in a spike, which bloom during the wet season. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense colonies in shallow water.

Anthericum nyasae plays a role in stabilizing aquatic ecosystems and providing habitat for various aquatic organisms.
