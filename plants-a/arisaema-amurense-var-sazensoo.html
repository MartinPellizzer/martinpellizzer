Arisaema amurense var. sazensoo

Arisaema amurense var. sazensoo, commonly known as the Sazensoo arrowroot, is a herbaceous plant native to East Asia.

It features a distinctive underground tuber that is often used in traditional medicine and as a food source. The plant has a striking, spadix-shaped inflorescence surrounded by a greenish-white spathe. Its leaves are typically large, heart-shaped, and have a velvety texture.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and medicinal properties in traditional herbal practices.
