Aster tenue

Aster tenue, commonly known as slender aster, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America.

It typically grows in open woodlands and meadows, preferring well-drained soils. The plant features slender, branching stems that are covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small, blue to purple, and bloom in late summer to early fall.

Aster tenue is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, providing nectar for pollinators.
