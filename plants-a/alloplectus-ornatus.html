Alloplectus ornatus

Alloplectus ornatus, commonly known as the golden star lily, is a striking ornamental plant native to South America.

It features showy, star-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, often with a contrasting central stripe. The plant has narrow, strap-like leaves that are typically dark green and arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Alloplectus ornatus is valued for its vibrant blooms and unique foliage, adding both color and texture to gardens.
