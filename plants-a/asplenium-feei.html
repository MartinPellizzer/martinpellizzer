Asplenium feei

Asplenium feei, commonly known as the rock fern, is a small, evergreen fern native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows on rocky substrates and in shaded, humid environments. The plant has delicate, lance-shaped leaves that are often clustered at the base. Its fronds are usually dark green and have a leathery texture.

Asplenium feei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
