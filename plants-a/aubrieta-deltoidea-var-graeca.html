Aubrieta deltoidea var. graeca

Aubrieta deltoidea var. graeca, commonly known as Greek aubrieta, is a low-growing, mat-forming perennial native to the Mediterranean region.

It features compact clusters of small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has silvery-gray, fuzzy foliage that adds visual interest throughout the growing season. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it ideal for rock gardens or borders.

Its compact size and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
