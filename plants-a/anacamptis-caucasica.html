Anacamptis caucasica

Anacamptis caucasica, commonly known as the Caucasian lady's tresses, is a species of orchid native to the mountainous regions of the Caucasus.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green leaves and its distinctive, spiraled inflorescence of pale blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as forest floors and rocky slopes. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is known for its delicate, fragrant blooms.

Anacamptis caucasica is a perennial herb that thrives in temperate climates with well-drained soil.
