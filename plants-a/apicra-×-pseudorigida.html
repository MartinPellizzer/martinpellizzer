Apicra × pseudorigida

Apicra × pseudorigida, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a natural hybrid between Apicra and Pseudorigida species.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces small, clustered blooms with intricate patterns on the petals and sepals. It thrives in well-drained, acidic soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated by enthusiasts of rare and exotic plants.
