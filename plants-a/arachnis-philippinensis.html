Arachnis philippinensis

Arachnis philippinensis, commonly known as the Philippine arachnid vine, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its climbing habit and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are often clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is typically found in lowland forests.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
