Amaryllis fucata

Amaryllis fucata, commonly known as the black amaryllis, is a striking plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dark purple to black flowers, which contrast beautifully with its strap-like, dark green foliage. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped blooms that can reach up to 10 inches in length. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Amaryllis fucata is a popular choice for gardeners seeking a dramatic and exotic ornamental plant.
