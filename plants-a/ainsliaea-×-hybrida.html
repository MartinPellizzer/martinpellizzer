Ainsliaea × hybrida

Ainsliaea × hybrida, commonly known as the hybrid evergreen shrub, is a botanical hybrid resulting from the cross between Ainsliaea argyrophylla and Ainsliaea sinensis.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, butterfly-like flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters and thrives in well-drained, acidic soils. It is native to regions of China and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.

Ainsliaea × hybrida is relatively low maintenance and prefers partial to full sun exposure.
