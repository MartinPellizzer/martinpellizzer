Aloe venosa

Aloe venosa, commonly known as the Zebra Aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, striped leaves that give it its common name. The leaves are thick and fleshy, adapted to store water in arid environments. It produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow-orange flowers.

Aloe venosa is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable growing conditions.
