Adenophora chanetii

Adenophora chanetii, commonly known as the Chinese bellflower, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped, blue or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and has hairy stems and leaves. Its flowers bloom in summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
