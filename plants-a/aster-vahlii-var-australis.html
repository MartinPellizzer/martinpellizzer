Aster vahlii var. australis

Aster vahlii var. australis, commonly known as the Australian aster, is a perennial herb native to Australia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and alternate leaves. The plant typically grows in dry, sandy soils and is often found in coastal areas. Its flowers are usually blue to violet in color, with ray florets surrounding a central disk.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
