Alternanthera achyrantha var. echinata

Alternanthera achyrantha var. echinata, commonly known as the spiked Alternanthera, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, feather-like leaves that often display vibrant red, pink, or purple hues. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This species is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and ease of care.
