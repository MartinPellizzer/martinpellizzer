Asparagus equisetifolius

Asparagus equisetifolius, commonly known as the slender asparagus, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asparagaceae family.

It is native to southern Europe and western Asia, often found in rocky or coastal areas. The plant has a distinctive appearance with slender, branching stems and small, needle-like leaves. Its flowers are typically greenish-white and appear in clusters, blooming in spring.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
