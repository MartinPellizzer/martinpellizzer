Alternanthera ramosissima var. reptans

Alternanthera ramosissima var. reptans, commonly known as the red-leaf alternanthera, is a striking aquatic plant native to South America.

It features vibrant red or pink foliage that intensifies under strong lighting conditions. The plant has slender, branching stems and small, opposite leaves that are often serrated along the edges. It thrives in warm, tropical climates and is popular in freshwater aquariums for its aesthetic appeal.

Due to its rapid growth, it requires regular pruning to maintain its shape and prevent it from overtaking other plants.
