Alyssum harputicum

Alyssum harputicum, commonly known as the Harput alyssum, is a flowering plant native to Turkey.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. It is often found growing in rocky or arid environments due to its drought-tolerant nature.

Alyssum harputicum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping.
