Abelia splendens

Abelia splendens, commonly known as the Chinese abelia, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features clusters of fragrant, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink, depending on the cultivar. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained, acidic soil.

Abelia splendens is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
