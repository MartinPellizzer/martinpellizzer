Asplenium longjinense

Asplenium longjinense, commonly known as the Longjin fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the family Aspleniaceae and is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine in its native regions.

The leaves are usually dark green and have a finely divided, feathery appearance.
