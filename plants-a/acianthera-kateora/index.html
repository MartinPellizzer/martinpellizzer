Acianthera Kateora

Acianthera kateora is a rare and intriguing orchid native to the cloud forests of South America. It is known for its delicate, violet-hued flowers that bloom in the early spring. This species thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires specific conditions to flourish. Due to habitat loss, it is now classified as endangered by conservationists. Its unique beauty and ecological significance make it a subject of great interest in botanical research.
