Areca hutchinsoniana

Areca hutchinsoniana, commonly known as the red areca palm, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and pinnate leaves that are dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or white in color. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Areca hutchinsoniana is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and air-purifying properties.
