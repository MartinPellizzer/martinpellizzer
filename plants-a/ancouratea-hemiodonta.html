Ancouratea hemiodonta

Ancouratea hemiodonta, commonly known as the Brazilian nightshade, is a member of the Solanaceae family.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its hairy stems and ovate, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale purple. Its fruits are berries that mature to a deep red color, containing several seeds.
