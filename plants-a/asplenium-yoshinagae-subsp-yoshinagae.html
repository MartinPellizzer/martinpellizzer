Asplenium yoshinagae subsp. yoshinagae

Asplenium yoshinagae subsp.

yoshinagae, commonly known as Yoshinaga's spleenwort, is a rare and specialized fern species native to Japan. It belongs to the family Aspleniaceae and is characterized by its small, clustered growth habit and delicate fronds.

The plant typically grows on rocky substrates in humid, shaded environments, reflecting its preference for cool and moist conditions. Its morphology includes narrow, serrated leaves that are often dark green and glossy.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a botanically significant and ecologically unique species.
