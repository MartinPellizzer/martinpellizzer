Anthurium tachiranum

Anthurium tachiranum, commonly known as the Tachira arrowroot, is a member of the Araceae family.

It is native to South America, specifically Venezuela and Colombia. The plant features large, heart-shaped leaves with a waxy texture and a deep green color. Its inflorescence consists of a spadix surrounded by a spathe, typical of the Araceae family.

Anthurium tachiranum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to humid, shaded environments.
