Agathis motleyi

Agathis motleyi, commonly known as the Motley's agathis, is a tropical evergreen tree belonging to the Araucariaceae family.

It is native to the rainforests of Central and South America, particularly in regions of Mexico and Central America. The tree is characterized by its tall, straight trunk and dense, dark green foliage. Its bark is thick and fibrous, providing structural support and protection against environmental stressors.

The plant produces large, conical cones and is valued for its durable wood, though it is also studied for its ecological role in tropical ecosystems.
