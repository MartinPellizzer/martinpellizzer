Asperula gunnii

Asperula gunnii, commonly known as the Japanese azalea, is a species of evergreen shrub native to Japan.

It belongs to the family Ericaceae and is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit. The plant produces clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink, white, and red during the spring. Its leaves are glossy and dark green, providing a striking contrast to the vibrant blooms.

Asperula gunnii is well-suited for shaded garden environments and is valued for its ornamental beauty.
