Auganthus listeri

Auganthus listeri, commonly known as the Cape gooseberry or Physalis peruviana, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive inflated calyx that encloses the fruit, resembling a small lantern. The plant produces yellow-orange berries that are often used in culinary applications. It thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil.

Auganthus listeri is also valued for its ornamental appeal and medicinal properties.
