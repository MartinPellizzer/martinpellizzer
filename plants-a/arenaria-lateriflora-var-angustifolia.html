Arenaria lateriflora var. angustifolia

Arenaria lateriflora var. angustifolia, commonly known as narrowleaf sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to arctic and alpine regions.

It features small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pink, blooming in late spring to early summer. The leaves are linear and narrow, adapted to withstand cold and harsh environmental conditions. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

This plant plays a role in stabilizing soil in its native environments and is of interest to botanists studying plant adaptations to extreme climates.
