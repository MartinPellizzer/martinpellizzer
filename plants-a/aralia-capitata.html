Aralia capitata

Aralia capitata, commonly known as the umbrella tree, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, umbrella-like compound leaves that can reach up to 3 meters in diameter. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in an umbel, which are followed by bright red berries. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Its rapid growth and striking foliage make it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
