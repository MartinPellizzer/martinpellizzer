Agathis endertii

Agathis endertii, commonly known as the Endert's agathis, is a species of flowering plant in the family Araucariaceae.

It is native to the tropical rainforests of Central and South America, particularly in regions of Mexico, Guatemala, and Panama. This evergreen tree can grow up to 50 meters tall, featuring a straight trunk and a dense, umbrella-shaped crown. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and lanceolate, with a leathery texture and a dark green color.

The plant produces large, conifer-like cones and is valued for its durable, aromatic wood.
