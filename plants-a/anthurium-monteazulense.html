Anthurium monteazulense

Anthurium monteazulense, commonly known as the Monte Azul anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves and vibrant spadix. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers surrounded by a colorful spathe. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Anthurium monteazulense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated as a houseplant.
