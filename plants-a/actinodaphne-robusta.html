Actinodaphne robusta

Actinodaphne robusta, commonly known as the robust daphne, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae and is characterized by its aromatic, glossy leaves and small, fragrant flowers. Actinodaphne robusta plays an important ecological role in its native habitats by providing shelter and food for various wildlife species.
