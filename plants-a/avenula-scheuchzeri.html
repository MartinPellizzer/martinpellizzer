Avenula scheuchzeri

Avenula scheuchzeri, commonly known as the Scheuchzer's sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like stems and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spikelets, typical of sedges. It thrives in moist, often wet habitats such as marshes and bogs.

Avenula scheuchzeri is typically found in temperate regions and is often associated with calcareous soils.
