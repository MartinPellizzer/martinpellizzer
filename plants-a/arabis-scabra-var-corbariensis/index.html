Arabis Scabra Var. Corbariensis

Arabis scabra var. corbariensis is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to a small region in France. It is known for its low-growing, mat-forming habit and clusters of white or pink flowers that bloom in early spring. This variety is particularly valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in rocky or sandy soils. Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a vulnerable species by conservationists. Its unique characteristics make it a subject of interest for botanists and gardeners alike.
