Ardisia patentiradiosa

Ardisia patentiradiosa, commonly known as the broad-leaf evergreen ardisia, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are broad and elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, white flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences, followed by bright red berries that are attractive to birds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical forests.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
