Alisma jianshiensis

Alisma jianshiensis, commonly known as the Jianshi water plant, is a rare aquatic plant native to China.

It typically grows in shallow freshwater habitats such as ponds and marshes. The plant has long, slender leaves that float on the water surface, while its roots are submerged. Its flowers are small and white, arranged in clusters, and bloom during the summer months.

Alisma jianshiensis is valued for its ecological role in maintaining water quality and supporting aquatic biodiversity.
