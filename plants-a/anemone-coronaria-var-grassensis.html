Anemone coronaria var. grassensis

Anemone coronaria var. grassensis, commonly known as the grassland anemone, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features showy, cup-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple, typically blooming in early to mid-summer. The plant has hairy, lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or disturbed habitats.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable addition to gardens and natural landscapes.
