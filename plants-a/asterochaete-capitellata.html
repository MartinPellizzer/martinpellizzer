Asterochaete capitellata

Asterochaete capitellata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved aster.

It is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads and opposite leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in temperate climates. Its flowers are usually blue to purple in color, with ray florets surrounding a central disk.

Asterochaete capitellata is often found in meadows and woodland edges, contributing to the biodiversity of its habitat.
