Alstonia actinophylla

Alstonia actinophylla, commonly known as the flame tree, is a flowering plant native to eastern Australia.

It is characterized by its striking, fiery red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer seasons. The tree has a distinctive, often twisted trunk and can grow quite tall, reaching up to 30 meters in height. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and typically have a velvety texture.

The plant is well adapted to coastal and sandy soils, making it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.
