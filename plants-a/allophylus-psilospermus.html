Allophylus psilospermus

Allophylus psilospermus, commonly known as the smooth-seeded alderleaf, is a small evergreen shrub native to parts of Southeast Asia.

The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by capsule fruits that contain smooth, elongated seeds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
