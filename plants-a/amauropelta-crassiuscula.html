Amauropelta crassiuscula

Amauropelta crassiuscula, commonly known as the broad-leafed shield fern, is a species of fern native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, shield-shaped fronds that are typically broad and leathery in texture. The plant has a robust rhizome system that allows it to thrive in moist, shaded environments. Its leaves are divided into multiple pinnae, giving it a distinctive and intricate appearance.

Amauropelta crassiuscula is often found in dense forests, where it plays a role in maintaining soil moisture and supporting local biodiversity.
