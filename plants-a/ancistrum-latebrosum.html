Ancistrum latebrosum

Ancistrum latebrosum, commonly known as the Brazilian sword fern, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its large, sword-shaped leaves that can grow up to several feet in length. The plant produces striking inflorescences with a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe, which is typical of the Araceae family. It thrives in humid, shady environments and requires consistent moisture and well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded gardens.
