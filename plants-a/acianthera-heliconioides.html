Acianthera heliconioides

Acianthera heliconioides, commonly known as the Heliconoides orchid, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that are typically violet or purple in color. The plant produces a single, showy spike with numerous small, clustered blooms. It thrives in humid, tropical environments and requires well-draining soil.

Acianthera heliconioides is often cultivated for its ornamental value in botanical gardens and greenhouses.
