Adiantum bonii

Adiantum bonii, commonly known as the velvet fern, is a delicate fern species native to Southeast Asia.

It features finely divided, silvery-green fronds that give it a soft, velvety appearance. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its fronds are bioluminescent at night, a unique characteristic that aids in its survival.

Adiantum bonii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and ease of care.
