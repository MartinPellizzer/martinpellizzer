Asplenium chlorophyllum

Asplenium chlorophyllum, commonly known as the green maidenhair fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Aspleniaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds that are typically bright green in color. This plant is native to various regions in the Americas, particularly in moist, shaded environments. It thrives in humid conditions and is often found in forests or along stream banks.

The plant's ability to tolerate low light makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
