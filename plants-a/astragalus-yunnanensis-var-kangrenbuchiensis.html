Astragalus yunnanensis var. kangrenbuchiensis

Astragalus yunnanensis var. kangrenbuchiensis, commonly known as the Yunnan milkvetch variant, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its upright growth habit and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in racemes, typical of the genus Astragalus. It is often found in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions.

This variety is of interest in botanical studies due to its unique morphology and potential ecological roles.
