Amaranthus × cacciatoi

Amaranthus × cacciatoi, commonly known as the red amaranth, is a hybrid plant in the Amaranthaceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant red to purple foliage and erect, branching stems. The plant produces small, green flowers that develop into triangular, inedible seeds. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a hardy annual in many climates.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its striking color and drought tolerance.
