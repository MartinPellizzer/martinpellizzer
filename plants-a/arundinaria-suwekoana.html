Arundinaria suwekoana

Arundinaria suwekoana, commonly known as the Suwekoan bamboo, is a grass-like plant native to eastern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in tropical and subtropical climates. It is often found in open woodlands and along riverbanks.

Arundinaria suwekoana is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional construction and crafts in its native regions.
