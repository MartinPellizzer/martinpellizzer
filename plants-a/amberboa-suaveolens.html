Amberboa suaveolens

Amberboa suaveolens, commonly known as the sweet-scented amberboa, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its aromatic, yellowish flowers and woody stems. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are narrow and often covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Amberboa suaveolens is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been cultivated for its pleasant fragrance.
