Alternanthera boliviana subsp. amentacea

Alternanthera boliviana subsp.

amentacea, commonly known as the silver feather plant, is a striking aquatic plant native to South America. It is characterized by its delicate, hair-like leaves that give it a feathery appearance.

The plant thrives in shallow, slow-moving freshwater environments and prefers moderate to high light conditions. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in aquariums and ponds.

Its growth habit is typically rhizomatous, allowing it to spread easily in suitable conditions.
