Armeria fasciculata var. aristata

Armeria fasciculata var. aristata, commonly known as the tufted thrift, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit and produces clusters of pink or white flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or coastal environments. Its leaves are linear and gray-green, while the stems are upright and covered in fine hairs.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to tolerate harsh environmental conditions.
