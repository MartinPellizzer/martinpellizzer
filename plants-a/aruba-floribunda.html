Aruba floribunda

Aruba floribunda, commonly known as the coastal morning glory, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean.

It is characterized by its trumpet-shaped, violet-blue flowers that bloom in clusters along the stems. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and thrives in coastal environments, tolerating salt spray and sandy soils. It is often found in coastal dunes and along the shores of islands like Aruba.

Its rapid growth and showy flowers make it a popular ornamental plant in tropical regions.
