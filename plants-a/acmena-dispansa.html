Acmena dispansa

Acmena dispansa, commonly known as the red-flowered acmena, is a species of flowering plant native to eastern Australia.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub that typically grows in coastal and subtropical regions. The plant features glossy, ovate leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. Its most striking characteristic is its showy, red or pinkish-red tubular flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months.

Acmena dispansa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
