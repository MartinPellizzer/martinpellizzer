Azalea grandis

Azalea grandis, commonly known as the Chinese azalea, is a species of flowering plant in the Ericaceae family.

It is native to eastern Asia, particularly China, Japan, and Korea. The plant is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that come in a range of colors including pink, red, and white. Azalea grandis typically grows as an evergreen shrub, reaching heights of up to 3 meters.

Its leaves are elliptical and dark green, providing a striking contrast to its vibrant floral displays.
