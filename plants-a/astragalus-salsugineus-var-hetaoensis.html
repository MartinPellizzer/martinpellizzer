Astragalus salsugineus var. hetaoensis

Astragalus salsugineus var. hetaoensis, commonly known as the Hetao milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to the semi-arid regions of northern China.

It is characterized by its small, yellow pea-like flowers and trifoliate leaves, typical of the Fabaceae family. This variant is adapted to sandy and saline soils, making it a resilient species in harsh environments. It plays a role in stabilizing soil and preventing erosion in its native habitat.

The plant is also of interest for its potential in ecological restoration projects.
