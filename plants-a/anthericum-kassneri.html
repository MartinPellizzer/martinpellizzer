Anthericum kassneri

Anthericum kassneri, commonly known as the Kassner's arum, is a striking tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It features a distinctive inflorescence consisting of a spadix surrounded by a showy, often colorful spathe. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in a dense, cylindrical cluster on the spadix. Its leaves are typically broad and heart-shaped, with a waxy texture.

Anthericum kassneri is often cultivated for its ornamental value in shaded garden environments.
