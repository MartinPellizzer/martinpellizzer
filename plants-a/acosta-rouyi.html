Acosta rouyi

Acosta rouyi, commonly known as the Yunnan rhododendron, is a species of evergreen shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its dense, leathery leaves and showy, bell-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions with acidic soils, thriving in cool, humid climates. Its flowers, which range in color from white to pink, bloom in late spring to early summer.

Acosta rouyi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and specialized collections.
