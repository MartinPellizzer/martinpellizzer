Astragalus Micranthus Var. Seatonii

Astragalus micranthus var. seatonii is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of western North America. It is known for its small, pale purple flowers and its unique ecological adaptations to harsh, alpine environments. This variety is often found in rocky soils and open meadows at high elevations. Due to habitat loss and climate change, it faces increasing threats to its survival. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this beautiful and ecologically important species.
