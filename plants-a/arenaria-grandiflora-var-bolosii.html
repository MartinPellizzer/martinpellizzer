Arenaria grandiflora var. bolosii

Arenaria grandiflora var. bolosii, commonly known as Bolosii's sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the western United States.

It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in alpine and subalpine environments. The plant features small, white to pink flowers that bloom in early spring, attracting pollinators such as bees. Its leaves are typically succulent and arranged in a rosette pattern, aiding in water retention.

This variety is noted for its hardiness and ability to withstand harsh environmental conditions.
