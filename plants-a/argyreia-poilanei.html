Argyreia poilanei

Argyreia poilanei, commonly known as the velvet tangle or purple flame vine, is a climbing plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage and clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from purple to red. The plant produces berries that are typically orange or yellow when mature, containing seeds that are dispersed by animals. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
