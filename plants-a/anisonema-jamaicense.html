Anisonema jamaicense

Anisonema jamaicense, commonly known as Jamaican anise or wild anise, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean, particularly Jamaica.

It belongs to the Apiaceae family, which includes carrots and parsley, and is characterized by its aromatic, compound leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that develop into small, oval fruits with a distinctive anise-like scent.

It is often found in moist, shaded areas and is valued for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal remedies.
