Astracantha sofarensis

Astracantha sofarensis, commonly known as the Socotra starfish flower, is a unique plant endemic to the Socotra Archipelago.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its distinctive star-shaped flowers. The plant has thick, fleshy leaves adapted to arid conditions, and its flowers are typically pale yellow or white. It grows in rocky and sandy soils, often in coastal areas.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and vulnerable species.
