Aconogonon sibiricum

Aconogonon sibiricum, commonly known as Siberian bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the cold climates of Siberia and Central Asia.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped blue or purple flowers that bloom in summer. Aconogonon sibiricum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
