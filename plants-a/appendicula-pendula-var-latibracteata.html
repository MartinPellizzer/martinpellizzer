Appendicula pendula var. latibracteata

Appendicula pendula var. latibracteata, commonly known as the broad-bract appendicula, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the family Campanulaceae and is characterized by its pendulous, bell-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while the bracts are notably broad and leaf-like.
