Aster langaoensis

Aster langaoensis, commonly known as the Langao aster, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded areas and has narrow, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are usually blue or purple, with ray florets surrounding a central disk.

Aster langaoensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and plays a role in local biodiversity.
