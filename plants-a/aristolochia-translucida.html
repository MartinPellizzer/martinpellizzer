Aristolochia translucida

Aristolochia translucida, commonly known as the translucent arrowhead vine, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive, heart-shaped leaves and striking, hooded flowers that range in color from green to purple. The plant belongs to the Aristolochiaceae family and is known for its complex, winding floral structure. Its vines can grow up to several meters in length, often climbing or trailing along supports.

However, it contains toxic compounds and should be handled with care.
