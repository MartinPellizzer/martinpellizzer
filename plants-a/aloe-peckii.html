Aloe peckii

Aloe peckii, commonly known as the Peck's aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with white or yellowish markings. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with small, tubular flowers that range in color from yellow to red. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its leaves to survive dry conditions.

Aloe peckii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional uses in herbal medicine.
