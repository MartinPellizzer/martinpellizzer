Anthurium scherzerianum

Anthurium scherzerianum, commonly known as the Scherzer's anthurium, is a tropical flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green, often with a reddish or purple tint along the veins. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-shaped inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, typically in shades of red, orange, or yellow. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium scherzerianum is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and long-lasting blooms.
