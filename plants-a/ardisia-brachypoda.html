Ardisia brachypoda

Ardisia brachypoda, commonly known as the short-stalked beautyberry, is a shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the family Ericaceae and is characterized by its small, dark purple berries that appear in late summer. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth texture and a slightly glossy appearance. It produces fragrant white flowers in clusters during the spring.
