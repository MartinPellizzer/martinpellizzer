Acacia subcaerulea var. parvifolia

Acacia subcaerulea var. parvifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved wattle, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is drought-tolerant, making it suitable for arid and semi-arid environments.
