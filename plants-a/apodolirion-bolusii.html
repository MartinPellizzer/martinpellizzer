Apodolirion bolusii

Apodolirion bolusii, commonly known as the Bolus's lily, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from pink to purple, often with intricate patterns. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It is a perennial, producing foliage in early spring before blooming in late summer.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens, though it requires well-drained soil and minimal water.
