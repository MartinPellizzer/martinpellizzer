Arthraxon lanceolatus var. glabratus

Arthraxon lanceolatus var. glabratus, commonly known as the smooth arrowhead, is a perennial aquatic plant native to Australia.

It features slender, lance-shaped leaves that are smooth and often floating or submerged. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish in color. It thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments.

Its rapid growth makes it both a valuable aquatic plant and a potential invasive species in non-native habitats.
