Anil heterotricha

Anil heterotricha, commonly known as the spiral liverwort, is a type of non-vascular plant belonging to the family Ptilidiaceae.

It is characterized by its spiral arrangement of leaves, which gives it a distinctive appearance. This liverwort typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Anil heterotricha plays a role in soil stabilization and provides habitat for microorganisms in its ecosystem.
