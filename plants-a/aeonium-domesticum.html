Aeonium domesticum

Aeonium domesticum, commonly known as the "Tree Aeonium," is a succulent plant native to the Canary Islands.

It features a rosette of thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with red or purple hues at the edges. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow flowers during the spring or early summer. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with plenty of sunlight.

Aeonium domesticum is popular in rock gardens and as a container plant due to its compact growth habit and ornamental appeal.
