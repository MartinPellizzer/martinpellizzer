Anisotome filifolia

Anisotome filifolia, commonly known as the slender leaf grass, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features narrow, linear leaves that are often arranged in a rosette pattern, with a distinctive silver-gray appearance due to fine pubescence. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers in spike-like clusters during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements.
