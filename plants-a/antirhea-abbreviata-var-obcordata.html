Antirhea abbreviata var. obcordata

Antirhea abbreviata var. obcordata, commonly known as the heart-shaped antirhea, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub characterized by its lanceolate to ovate leaves with a distinctive heart-shaped base. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white or pale yellow flowers. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in shaded, moist environments.

Its botanical features make it a valued species in horticulture and traditional medicine.
