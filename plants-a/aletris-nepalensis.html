Aletris nepalensis

Aletris nepalensis, commonly known as the Nepalese grass-of-parnassus, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Himalayan regions of Nepal and northern India.

Aletris nepalensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with suitable conditions.
