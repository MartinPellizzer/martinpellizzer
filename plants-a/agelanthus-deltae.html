Agelanthus deltae

Agelanthus deltae, commonly known as the delta agelanthus, is a rare and specialized plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It is a succulent shrub characterized by its fleshy, water-storing leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant thrives in hot, dry environments and is adapted to survive with minimal water.
