Angraecum cornigerum

Angraecum cornigerum, commonly known as the horned angraecum, is a species of orchid native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, showy flowers that bloom at night. The plant produces white, fragrant flowers with long, narrow petals and a distinctive spur. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Angraecum cornigerum is a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts due to its striking appearance and unique blooming habits.
