Artemisia globosa

Artemisia globosa, commonly known as globular wormwood, is a flowering plant in the sunflower family.

It is native to the western United States and grows in dry, rocky habitats. The plant has silver-gray foliage with small, rounded leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. It produces tiny, yellowish flowers that bloom in late summer.

Artemisia globosa is often used in herbal medicine and as a natural insect repellent.
