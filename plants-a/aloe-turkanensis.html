Aloe turkanensis

Aloe turkanensis, commonly known as the Turkish aloe, is a succulent plant native to Turkey.

It belongs to the Asphodelaceae family and is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that store water, adapting it to arid environments. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow flowers, which bloom in the spring. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their soothing properties.

Aloe turkanensis is valued for its resilience and ornamental appeal in dry climates.
