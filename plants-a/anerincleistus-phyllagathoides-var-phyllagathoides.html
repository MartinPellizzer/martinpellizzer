Anerincleistus phyllagathoides var. phyllagathoides

Anerincleistus phyllagathoides var. phyllagathoides, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves and clusters of showy, long-lasting blooms in shades of purple, pink, and white. The plant typically grows as a compact, upright shrub with a dense foliage structure. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate to bright indirect light for optimal growth.
