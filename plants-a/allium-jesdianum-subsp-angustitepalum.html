Allium jesdianum subsp. angustitepalum

Allium jesdianum subsp.

angustitepalum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf garlic. It is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

The plant features strap-shaped, grass-like leaves and produces umbel-like clusters of small, white or pale purple flowers. Its bulbs are bulbous and often used in traditional medicine and cuisine.

The species is valued for its aromatic properties and adaptability to harsh environments.
