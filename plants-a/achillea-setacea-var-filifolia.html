Achillea setacea var. filifolia

Achillea setacea var. filifolia, commonly known as the slender yarrow, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It features finely divided, silver-gray leaves that are linear to filiform, giving it a delicate, feathery appearance. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in flat-topped clusters, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or alpine environments.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
