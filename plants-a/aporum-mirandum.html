Aporum mirandum

Aporum mirandum, commonly known as the Enchanted Orchid, is a rare and striking member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its intricate, lace-like petals that shimmer with a unique iridescent hue under sunlight. Native to the misty highlands of Southeast Asia, this plant thrives in cool, humid environments with rich, well-draining soil. Its flowers bloom only during the late evening, releasing a subtle fragrance that attracts nocturnal pollinators.

Due to its rarity and beauty, Aporum mirandum is highly sought after by botanists and horticulturists alike.
