Aconitopsis aconiti

It is native to regions of Europe and Asia, typically found in moist, woodland habitats. The plant produces showy, blue to violet flowers with five petals, and its leaves are deeply lobed. It contains toxic alkaloids, making it potentially dangerous to humans and animals.
