Aglaonema crispum

Aglaonema crispum, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves with striking white and silver veins. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening. Its foliage remains vibrant throughout the year, adding aesthetic value to any space.

Aglaonema crispum is also noted for its air-purifying properties, contributing to improved indoor air quality.
