Aralia leschenaultii

Aralia leschenaultii, commonly known as the Chinese angelica or white aralia, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features compound leaves with long, slender leaflets that give it a feathery appearance. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in umbels, which are typically followed by dark blue or black berries. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its attractive foliage and unique fruiting habit.
