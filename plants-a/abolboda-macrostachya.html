Abolboda macrostachya

Abolboda macrostachya, commonly known as the giant flame bamboo, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems that can reach heights of up to 10 meters. The plant produces large, bright red bracts that resemble flowers, which are actually modified leaves. These bracts are often used in ornamental horticulture due to their vibrant color and unique appearance.

Abolboda macrostachya thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
