Ajax pseudonarcissus var. nobilis

Ajax pseudonarcissus var. nobilis, commonly known as the noble daffodil, is a variety of the daffodil genus characterized by its striking yellow flowers.

It features a tall, slender stem with a single, large, trumpet-shaped bloom that is often more elongated than in other varieties. The plant is native to southern Europe and thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight. It is a perennial bulb plant that blooms in early spring, adding vibrant color to gardens.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening due to its elegance and hardiness.
