Acacia kamerunensis

The plant produces yellow flowers in compact globular heads, which are followed by flat, pod-like fruits containing seeds. Acacia kamerunensis is adapted to dry and semi-arid environments, making it a resilient species in tropical savanna ecosystems.

It plays a role in local ecosystems and has been used traditionally for various purposes, including medicinal and construction applications.
