Adesmia salamancensis

Adesmia salamancensis, commonly known as the Salamanca burro bean, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, pea-like flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid conditions. Its seed pods are woody and contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind or animals.
