Anthurium tuquesense

Anthurium tuquesense, commonly known as the Tuquesense arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive spathe and spadix inflorescence. The plant features broad, arrow-shaped leaves that are often a deep green with a waxy texture. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor cultivation and botanical gardens.
