Acianthera beyrodtiana

Acianthera beyrodtiana, commonly known as the golden twayblade, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to South America.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid with a distinctive golden-yellow inflorescence that resembles a twayblade. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and grasslands. Its flowers are short-lived but attract various pollinators, including bees and flies.
