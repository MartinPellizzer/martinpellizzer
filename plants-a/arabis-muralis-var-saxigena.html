Arabis muralis var. saxigena

Arabis muralis var. saxigena, commonly known as the rock arabis, is a perennial flowering plant native to rocky and alpine environments in Europe.

It produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has basal leaves that are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and alpine settings.

Its hardy nature and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking low-maintenance, resilient plants.
