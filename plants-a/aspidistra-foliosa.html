Aspidistra foliosa

Aspidistra foliosa, commonly known as the Japanese evergreen, is a striking evergreen plant native to Japan and Korea.

It features broad, dark green, glossy leaves that are lance-shaped and often have a waxy texture. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening. Its foliage remains lush throughout the year, adding a touch of elegance to any space.

Aspidistra foliosa is also valued for its resilience and minimal care requirements.
