Arenaria spathulata var. oranensis

Arenaria spathulata var. oranensis, commonly known as the Oranese sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to arid and semi-arid regions.

It features succulent, fleshy leaves that help it retain moisture in dry environments. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers arranged in clusters, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Its adaptability makes it a hardy choice for xeriscaping and rock gardens.
