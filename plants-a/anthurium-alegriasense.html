Anthurium alegriasense

Anthurium alegriasense, commonly known as the Alegriasense Flame, is a striking tropical plant native to the humid lowland forests of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped leaves that can range in color from deep green to reddish hues. The plant produces showy spadices, which are the inflorescences that resemble flowers, though they are actually modified leaves. Anthurium alegriasense thrives in warm, moist environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for both ornamental gardening and botanical study.
