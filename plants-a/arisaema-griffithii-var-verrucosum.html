Arisaema griffithii var. verrucosum

Arisaema griffithii var. verrucosum, commonly known as the spiny arrowhead, is a member of the Araceae family.

It is characterized by its distinctive, arrow-shaped leaves with a serrated edge. The plant produces an unusual, spiny structure that resembles a flower, which is actually a spadix enclosed by a spathe. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in mountainous regions of Asia.

This variant is notable for its unique morphology and is sometimes cultivated for its ornamental value.
