Aloe ciliaris var. flanaganii

Aloe ciliaris var. flanaganii, commonly known as the Flanagan's aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, blue-green leaves with white teeth along the edges. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow flowers during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
