Artemisia commutata var. pubescens

Artemisia commutata var. pubescens, commonly known as silver bluestem, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its finely divided, silver-gray leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in composite inflorescences, which are typical of the Asteraceae family. It thrives in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils.

Its aromatic foliage and drought tolerance make it an important species in ecological restoration projects.
