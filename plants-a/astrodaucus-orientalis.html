Astrodaucus orientalis

Astrodaucus orientalis, commonly known as the oriental starflower, is a herbaceous perennial native to Central Asia.

It features showy, star-shaped yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture, and it grows in clumps from a short, fibrous root system. It prefers well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade, making it adaptable to various garden conditions.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant color and unique floral structure.
