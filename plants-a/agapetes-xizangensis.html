Agapetes xizangensis

Agapetes xizangensis, commonly known as the Yunnan silver fir, is a species of evergreen shrub native to the mountainous regions of southwest China.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and small, bell-shaped flowers.
