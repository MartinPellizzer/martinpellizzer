Alocasia uhinkii

Alocasia uhinkii, commonly known as the Black Velvet Alocasia, is a striking ornamental plant native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It features large, heart-shaped leaves with a deep, velvety black color that contrasts beautifully with its white veins. The plant thrives in high humidity and indirect light, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation. Its foliage is often used in horticultural displays due to its dramatic appearance.

Alocasia uhinkii requires well-draining soil and consistent moisture to maintain its lush, vibrant look.
