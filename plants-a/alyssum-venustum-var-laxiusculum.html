Alyssum venustum var. laxiusculum

Alyssum venustum var. laxiusculum, commonly known as the slender alpine alyssum, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It features small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pale pink, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has fine, hairy leaves and a compact growth habit, making it well-suited for rock gardens and alpine settings. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, and is often used in horticultural displays for its ornamental value.

Its botanical characteristics make it a hardy and resilient plant adapted to harsh, high-altitude environments.
