Aegiphila cuatrecasasii

Aegiphila cuatrecasasii, commonly known as the Cuatrecasas' aegiphila, is a shrub native to the cloud forests of central Mexico.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and requires partial to full shade.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
