Anemone multifida

Anemone multifida, commonly known as the divided anemone, is a herbaceous perennial native to eastern North America.

It features showy, white to pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has finely divided, fern-like leaves that are typically bluish-green in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.

Anemone multifida is often found in woodland habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
