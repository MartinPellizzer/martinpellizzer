Angraecum multiflorum

Angraecum multiflorum, commonly known as the Madagascar star orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its long, pendant inflorescence bearing numerous white or pale pink flowers. The plant produces fragrant, star-shaped blooms that are highly attractive to nocturnal pollinators such as moths. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Angraecum multiflorum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique floral structure.
