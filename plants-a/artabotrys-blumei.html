Artabotrys blumei

Artabotrys blumei, commonly known as the fragrant starfruit tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Annonaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, fragrant, star-shaped flowers. The plant produces a yellowish fruit that is often used in traditional medicine. Its foliage consists of alternate, simple, and ovate leaves with a smooth texture.
