Ascocentrum hendersonianium

Ascocentrum hendersonianium, commonly known as the Henderson's arrowroot.

It is a herbaceous plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly found in the Philippines. The plant features strap-like, fleshy leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. It produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in a spike inflorescence.

The rhizomes of this plant are valued for their starchy content and have been traditionally used as a food source.
