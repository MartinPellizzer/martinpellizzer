Afrodaphne fruticosa

Afrodaphne fruticosa, commonly known as the African star jasmine, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its aromatic, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters, often with a star-like appearance. The plant has small, elliptical leaves that are usually glossy and dark green. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Afrodaphne fruticosa is often cultivated for its ornamental value and pleasant fragrance.
