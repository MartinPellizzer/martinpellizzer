Argyroxiphium caligini

Argyroxiphium caligini, commonly known as the cloud forest ginger, is a rare plant native to the high-altitude regions of Hawaii.

It is characterized by its striking silver-gray foliage, which reflects the humid and misty conditions of its habitat.
