Aster microspermus

Aster microspermus, commonly known as the small-seeded aster, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and woodland edges. Its small, showy flowers bloom in the late summer to early fall, producing both ray and disk florets.

The species is noted for its delicate appearance and is often found in shaded or partially shaded environments.
