Astrotricha floccosa var. latifolia

Astrotricha floccosa var. latifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaved star-plant, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive star-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. The leaves are broad and often hairy, giving the plant a soft, fuzzy appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open grasslands or rocky areas.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage and flowers.
