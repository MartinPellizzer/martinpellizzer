Anil dissitiflora

Anil dissitiflora, commonly known as the purple morning glory, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking violet to purple flowers that bloom in the early morning. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded areas.

Anil dissitiflora is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
