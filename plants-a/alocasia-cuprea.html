Alocasia cuprea

Alocasia cuprea, commonly known as the Red-veined Alocasia, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often a deep green with prominent red veins. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and as a decorative plant.

Alocasia cuprea is relatively easy to care for, making it accessible to both novice and experienced plant enthusiasts.
