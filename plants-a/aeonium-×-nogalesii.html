Aeonium × nogalesii

Aeonium × nogalesii, commonly known as the Mexican Tree Aeonium, is a succulent plant native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped foliage, with thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green or blue-green in color. The plant produces tall, branching inflorescences that bear yellow flowers, which bloom in the spring. Aeonium × nogalesii is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its leaves and stems.

It is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and ornamental appearance.
