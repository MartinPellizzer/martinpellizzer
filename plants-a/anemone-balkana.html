Anemone balkana

Anemone balkana, commonly known as Balkan anemone, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the Balkan Peninsula.

It typically grows in moist, shaded woodlands and mountainous regions. The plant features showy, white or pinkish flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are palmately lobed, and the plant has a fibrous root system.

Anemone balkana is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitat.
