Anoda decumbens

Anoda decumbens, commonly known as the prostrate morning glory, is a herbaceous annual plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its prostrate growth habit, with slender stems that often root at the nodes. The plant produces small, showy flowers that range in color from blue to violet, typically blooming in the morning. Its leaves are ovate to lanceolate, with hairy surfaces, and it thrives in moist, well-drained soils.

Anoda decumbens is often found in disturbed areas and is sometimes cultivated for its ornamental value.
