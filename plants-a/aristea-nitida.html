Aristea nitida

Aristea nitida, commonly known as the silver feather plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features dense, silver-gray foliage that forms a compact, cushion-like growth habit. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink or purple during the spring and summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil with plenty of sunlight.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
