Antigramma Sibirica

Antigramma sibirica is a rare and enigmatic plant native to the cold climates of Siberia. It thrives in harsh, arid environments where few other species can survive. Its unique appearance features delicate, waxy leaves that reflect the harsh sunlight. Despite its resilience, it remains poorly understood by botanists due to its elusive nature. This plant offers valuable insights into adaptation strategies in extreme climates.
