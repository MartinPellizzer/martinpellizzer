Allocasuarina torulosa

Allocasuarina torulosa, commonly known as the narrow-leaf she-oak, is a shrub or small tree native to southeastern Australia.

It is characterized by its unique, needle-like phyllodes that are often spirally arranged along the stems. The plant produces small, spherical flowers that are typically greenish or yellowish in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and inland regions.

Allocasuarina torulosa is valued for its hardiness and is used in horticulture and reforestation projects.
