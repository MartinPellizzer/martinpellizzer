Asplundia gigantea

Asplundia gigantea, commonly known as the giant spider plant, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features long, arching leaves that can grow up to several feet in length, giving it a dramatic appearance. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are borne on tall, slender stalks. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense clusters.

Asplundia gigantea is well-suited for both indoor and outdoor cultivation, thriving in humid, warm environments.
