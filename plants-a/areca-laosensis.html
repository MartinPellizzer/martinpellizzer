Areca laosensis

Areca laosensis, commonly known as the Laos areca palm, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Arecaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and pinnate leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow-green in color. Areca laosensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.

Its growth habit makes it suitable for tropical and subtropical climates.
