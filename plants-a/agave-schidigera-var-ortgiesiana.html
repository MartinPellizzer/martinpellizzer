Agave schidigera var. ortgiesiana

Agave schidigera var. ortgiesiana, commonly known as the Ortgies' agave, is a succulent plant native to the arid regions of Mexico.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped growth pattern and thick, fleshy leaves that are often blue-green in color. The plant produces a tall flowering stalk that bears clusters of white to pale yellow flowers. It is adapted to survive in hot, dry climates with minimal water requirements.

Its unique appearance and resilience make it a popular choice for xeriscaping and rock gardens.
