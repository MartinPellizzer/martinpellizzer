Albuca hereroensis

Albuca hereroensis, commonly known as the Herero bulbous plant, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its bulbous stem and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Albuca hereroensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
