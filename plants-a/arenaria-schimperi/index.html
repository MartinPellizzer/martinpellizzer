Arenaria Schimperi

Arenaria schimperi is a rare flowering plant native to the high altitudes of Ethiopia. It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family, known for its vibrant and delicate blooms. This species thrives in rocky and arid environments, showcasing remarkable adaptability to harsh conditions. Its small, white flowers appear in spring, adding a subtle beauty to its rugged habitat. Due to its limited distribution and habitat threats, it is considered a vulnerable species requiring conservation efforts.
