Astragalus sapozhnikovii

Astragalus sapozhnikovii, commonly known as the Siberian milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It is a member of the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid environments. It has a deep root system that helps it survive in nutrient-poor soils.
