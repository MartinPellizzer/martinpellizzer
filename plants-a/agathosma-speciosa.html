Agathosma speciosa

Agathosma speciosa, commonly known as sweet everlasting, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, small yellow flowers and silver-gray foliage. The plant is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy perennial in suitable climates.

Its fragrance and resilience make it a popular choice for gardens and herbal applications.
