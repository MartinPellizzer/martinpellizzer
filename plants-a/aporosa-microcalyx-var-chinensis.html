Aporosa microcalyx var. chinensis

Aporosa microcalyx var. chinensis, commonly known as the Chinese aporosa, is a flowering plant native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters, typically in spring. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical in shape. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
