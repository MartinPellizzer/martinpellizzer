Asplenium actiniopteroides

Asplenium actiniopteroides, commonly known as the silver maidenhair fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Aspleniaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds that give it a feathery appearance. Native to tropical and subtropical regions, this plant thrives in moist, shaded environments. The fronds are typically dark green with a silvery underside, contributing to its ornamental value.

It is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ability to tolerate low light conditions and its attractive foliage.
