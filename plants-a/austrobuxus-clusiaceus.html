Austrobuxus clusiaceus

Austrobuxus clusiaceus, commonly known as the Australian boxwood, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Clusiaceae and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, oval-shaped, and have a glossy, dark green appearance.
