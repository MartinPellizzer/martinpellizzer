Aquilegia buergeriana f. viridialba

Aquilegia buergeriana f. viridialba, commonly known as the white-flowered columbine, is a rare and striking variant of the common columbine.

It is characterized by its pale green to white petals, which contrast with its deep green foliage. This cultivar is prized for its unique coloration and is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

The plant is a perennial, producing showy flowers in early to mid-summer.
