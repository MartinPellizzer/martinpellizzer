Aylthonia

Aylthonia, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Aylthonia is often found in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests and riverbanks.

It is also known for its ability to thrive in disturbed soils and can be invasive in certain regions.
