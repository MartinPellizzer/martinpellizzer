Astilbe pedunculata

Astilbe pedunculata, commonly known as the upright astilbe, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to eastern Asia.

It features upright, branched stems that bear clusters of small, panicle-like flowers. The foliage is typically dark green and lance-shaped, providing a striking contrast to the delicate blooms. It thrives in partial shade and moist, well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to add texture and color to shaded landscapes.
