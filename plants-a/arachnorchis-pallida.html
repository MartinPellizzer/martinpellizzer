Arachnorchis pallida

Arachnorchis pallida, commonly known as the pale orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, pale green to white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests and wetlands. Its flowers are usually arranged in a loose spike and are pollinated by insects.

Arachnorchis pallida is a rare species, often found in specific microhabitats within its native range.
