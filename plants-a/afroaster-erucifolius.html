Afroaster erucifolius

Afroaster erucifolius, commonly known as the African aster, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows in shrubland and grassland habitats, thriving in well-drained soils. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often with a slightly hairy texture.

Afroaster erucifolius is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
