Armeria morisii var. gussonei

Armeria morisii var. gussonei, commonly known as the Gussone's thrift, is a low-growing, mat-forming plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features dense clusters of small, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring. The leaves are linear and gray-green, with a woolly texture, and the plant is drought-tolerant once established. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and ability to withstand harsh environmental conditions.
