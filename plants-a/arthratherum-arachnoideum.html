Arthratherum arachnoideum

Arthratherum arachnoideum, commonly known as the spider-like grass, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, creeping stems that often form dense mats on the ground. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences bearing tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a slightly hairy surface.

Arthratherum arachnoideum is adapted to rocky and arid environments, exhibiting drought tolerance and rapid growth in suitable conditions.
