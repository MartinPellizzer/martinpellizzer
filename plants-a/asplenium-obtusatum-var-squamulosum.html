Asplenium obtusatum var. squamulosum

Asplenium obtusatum var. squamulosum, commonly known as the woolly spleenwort, is a fern species native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its compact, rosette-like growth habit and dark green, leathery fronds. The leaves are typically short and broad, with a distinctive woolly texture on the underside. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as rocky crevices and forest floors.

This variety is notable for its unique morphology and is often found in humid, temperate regions.
