Asplenium marinum var. incisum

Asplenium marinum var. incisum, commonly known as the coastal spleenwort, is a variety of the common spleenwort.

It is a small, evergreen fern that typically grows in moist, shaded environments along coastal areas. The plant features delicate, lance-shaped fronds with a serrated or incised margin, which is characteristic of the var. incisum form. Its rhizomes are often found in rocky or sandy soils near the shoreline.

This variety is well adapted to salt spray and is frequently found in maritime habitats.
