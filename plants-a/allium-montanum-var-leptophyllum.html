Allium montanum var. leptophyllum

Allium montanum var. leptophyllum, commonly known as the slender bulb onion, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of western North America.

It features a basal rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves and produces tall, slender flowering stems that bear small, white to pale purple flowers. The plant's bulbs are small and often clustered, with a mild onion flavor. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable candidate for rock gardens and alpine settings.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and culinary potential in certain regional cuisines.
