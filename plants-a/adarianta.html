Adarianta

Adarianta, commonly known as the star jasmine, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features small, fragrant white or light pink flowers that bloom in clusters, often emitting a sweet aroma. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and a climbing or shrub-like growth habit. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Adarianta is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in landscaping and as a houseplant.
