Aristida venustula var. scabrifolia

Aristida venustula var. scabrifolia, commonly known as the velvetgrass, is a perennial grass native to southern Africa.

It features slender, wiry stems and narrow, grass-like leaves with a distinctive velvety texture. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that are often adorned with soft, fluffy bristles. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
