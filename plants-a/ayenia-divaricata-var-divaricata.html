Ayenia divaricata var. divaricata

Ayenia divaricata var. divaricata, commonly known as the variegated ayenia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage with variegated patterns of green, yellow, and white. The plant produces small, daisy-like flowers in shades of yellow and orange, typically blooming in the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking ornamental foliage.

Due to its unique appearance, it is often cultivated for decorative purposes in both gardens and as a houseplant.
