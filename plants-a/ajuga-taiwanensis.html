Ajuga taiwanensis

Ajuga taiwanensis, commonly known as Taiwan bugleweed, is a perennial herb native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the family Plantaginaceae and is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit. The plant features dark green, ovate leaves with serrated edges and produces clusters of small, blue to purple flowers in early summer. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Ajuga taiwanensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in native plant gardens.
