Armeria mauritanica var. eumauritanica

Armeria mauritanica var. eumauritanica, commonly known as the Moroccan thrift, is a low-growing evergreen perennial native to the Mediterranean region.

It features dense clusters of small, white or pinkish flowers that bloom in spring and summer. The plant has silver-gray foliage with a fuzzy texture, and its stems are typically prostrate or spreading. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in coastal and arid environments.
