Anthurium microspadix

Anthurium microspadix, commonly known as the dwarf anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a vibrant green. The plant produces small, spadix-like inflorescences, which are surrounded by a colorful spathe. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Anthurium microspadix is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
