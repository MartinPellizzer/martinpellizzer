Acer platanoides var. lobatum

Acer platanoides var. lobatum, commonly known as the European maple or Norway maple, is a deciduous tree native to Europe.

It is characterized by its broad, palmate leaves that are typically lobed and have a smooth, dark green upper surface. The tree produces small, yellowish-green flowers in early spring, followed by winged samaras that disperse its seeds. It thrives in a variety of soil types and is often found in urban environments due to its tolerance of pollution and compacted soils.

The species is known for its fast growth and adaptability, making it a popular ornamental tree.
