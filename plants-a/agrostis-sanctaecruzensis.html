Agrostis sanctaecruzensis

Agrostis sanctaecruzensis, commonly known as the San Cruzen's bentgrass, is a perennial grass species native to the western United States.

It typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands. The plant has slender, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It is adapted to temperate climates and is often found in areas with well-drained soil.

Its botanical features make it a subject of interest in ecological and botanical studies.
