Ammocharis taveliana

Ammocharis taveliana, commonly known as the Taveliana orchid, is a species of flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces small, green leaves and has a compact, bulb-like structure that helps it survive in arid conditions. Its flowers are typically white or pale yellow with a distinctive, intricate pattern.

Ammocharis taveliana is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and ease of cultivation.
