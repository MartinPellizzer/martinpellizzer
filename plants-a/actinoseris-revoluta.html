Actinoseris revoluta

Actinoseris revoluta, commonly known as the spiral cactus, is a species of flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its spiny, cylindrical stems that often form dense clusters. The plant produces vibrant yellow or orange flowers during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are reduced to spines, and it has a shallow root system adapted to arid environments.

Actinoseris revoluta is a slow-growing plant that thrives in full sun and well-draining soil.
