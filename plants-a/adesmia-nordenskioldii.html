Adesmia nordenskioldii

Adesmia nordenskioldii, commonly known as the white-flowered burro's tail, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces small, white, tubular flowers clustered in dense inflorescences. The plant has succulent, fleshy leaves that help it store water, adapting it to arid environments. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its ornamental value comes from its delicate appearance and ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
