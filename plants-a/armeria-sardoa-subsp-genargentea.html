Armeria sardoa subsp. genargentea

Armeria sardoa subsp.

genargentea, commonly known as the silverleaf thrift, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region. It features dense clusters of small, white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.

The leaves are typically silver-gray in color, covered in fine hairs that help reduce water loss. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
