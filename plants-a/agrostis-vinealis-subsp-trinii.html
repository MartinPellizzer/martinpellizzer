Agrostis vinealis subsp. trinii

Agrostis vinealis subsp.

trinii, commonly known as slender meadowgrass, is a grass species native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa. It is a low-growing perennial plant that typically forms dense mats on sandy or gravelly soils.

The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It is often found in open, disturbed habitats such as roadsides, dunes, and pastures.

Its botanical features make it well adapted to harsh environmental conditions.
