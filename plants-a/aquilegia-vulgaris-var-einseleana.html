Aquilegia vulgaris var. einseleana

Aquilegia vulgaris var. einseleana, commonly known as the Einseleana columbine, is a cultivated variant of the common columbine.

It is characterized by its striking blue and purple flowers, which are arranged in intricate spurred blossoms. The plant is a herbaceous perennial that thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil. Its foliage consists of palmate leaves with finely toothed edges, adding to its ornamental appeal.

This variety is valued in gardens for its vibrant color and ability to attract pollinators like butterflies and bees.
