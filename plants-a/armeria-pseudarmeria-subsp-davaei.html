Armeria pseudarmeria subsp. davaei

Armeria pseudarmeria subsp.

davaei, commonly known as the Davae's thrift, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region. It features dense clusters of small, white or pinkish flowers that bloom in early spring.

The plant has basal leaves that are linear and often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking drought-tolerant plants.
