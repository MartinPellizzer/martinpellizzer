Abelia densipila

Abelia densipila, commonly known as the denseleaf abelia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, fragrant, white to pinkish flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has dense, dark green foliage that provides a lush appearance throughout the growing season. It thrives in partial to full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for garden landscapes.

Abelia densipila is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
