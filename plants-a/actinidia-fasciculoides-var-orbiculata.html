Actinidia fasciculoides var. orbiculata

Actinidia fasciculoides var. orbiculata, commonly known as the round-leaf kiwifruit, is a deciduous vine native to China.

It produces small, edible fruits with a hairy, brown skin and a sweet, aromatic pulp. The plant has compound leaves that are oval to round in shape, with a smooth margin. It thrives in temperate climates and requires a cold winter dormancy period for fruit production.

The variety is valued for its unique flavor and nutritional content, making it a cultivar of interest in horticulture.
