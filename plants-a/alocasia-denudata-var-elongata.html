Alocasia denudata var. elongata

Alocasia denudata var. elongata, commonly known as the Giant Taro or Longleaf Alocasia, is a striking tropical plant characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 3 feet in length.

The foliage is typically a deep green with a velvety texture and prominent veins, adding to its ornamental appeal. This variant is a cultivar of Alocasia denudata, which is native to Southeast Asia, and it thrives in warm, humid climates with high light exposure. It requires well-draining soil and consistent moisture to flourish, making it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.

The plant is also known for its ability to purify the air, contributing to its popularity among plant enthusiasts.
