Avena melillensis

Avena melillensis, commonly known as the Melilla oat, is a perennial grass species native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences with numerous tiny florets. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or arid environments.

Avena melillensis is valued for its ecological role in stabilizing soils and supporting local biodiversity.
