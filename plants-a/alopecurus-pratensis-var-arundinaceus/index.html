Alopecurus Pratensis Var. Arundinaceus

Alopecurus pratensis var. arundinaceus is a variant of the meadow foxtail known for its unique growth characteristics. This plant thrives in moist, nutrient-rich soils and is commonly found in wet meadows and marshy areas. Its slender stems and dense foliage make it a distinctive feature of its habitat. While it shares similarities with its parent species, it exhibits a more robust and grass-like appearance. This variety plays an important role in supporting local biodiversity and ecosystem health.
