Aulomyrcia cassinioides var. glabrata

Aulomyrcia cassinioides var. glabrata, commonly known as the smoothleaf aulomyrcia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a small shrub characterized by its simple, lance-shaped leaves that are smooth and glabrous. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences bearing white to pale yellow flowers. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in understory layers of tropical forests.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for horticultural and ecological studies.
