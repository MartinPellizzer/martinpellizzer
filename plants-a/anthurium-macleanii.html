Anthurium macleanii

Anthurium macleanii, commonly known as the MacLean's anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-shaped inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, which can range from red to yellow. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Anthurium macleanii is popular as a decorative plant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
