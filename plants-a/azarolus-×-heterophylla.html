Azarolus × heterophylla

Azarolus × heterophylla, commonly known as the hybrid azarolus, is a unique plant resulting from the cross between two species of the genus Azarolus.

It is characterized by its striking, often variegated foliage that displays a mix of green, white, and sometimes pink hues. The plant typically has a compact growth habit with dense, waxy leaves that are resistant to pests and diseases. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to maintain its vibrant appearance.

Azarolus × heterophylla is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental value and low maintenance requirements.
