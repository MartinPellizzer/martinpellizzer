Andromeda jamaicensis var. myrsinifolia

Andromeda jamaicensis var. myrsinifolia, commonly known as Jamaican andromeda, is a flowering plant belonging to the Ericaceae family.

It is characterized by its evergreen foliage, with dark green, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, white pubescence. The plant produces clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically pink or red in color, blooming in the spring. It thrives in moist, acidic soils and is typically found in shaded, woodland environments.

This variant is notable for its ornamental value and ecological importance in its native habitat.
