Aphelandra harleyi

Aphelandra harleyi, commonly known as the golden flame plant, is a tropical shrub native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, elongated leaves that are often a deep green with a metallic sheen. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in the evening. It thrives in humid, warm environments with bright, indirect light.

Aphelandra harleyi is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
