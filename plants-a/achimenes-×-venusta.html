Achimenes × venusta

Achimenes × venusta, commonly known as the hybrid gloxinia, is a tropical perennial plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, pink, and purple. The plant produces showy bracts that often resemble its true flowers, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in warm, humid conditions and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Achimenes × venusta is often cultivated as a houseplant or in greenhouses due to its sensitivity to cold temperatures.
