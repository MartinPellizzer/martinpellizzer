Archidendron solomonense

Archidendron solomonense, commonly known as Solomon's legume, is a tropical shrub native to the Solomon Islands.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in lowland forest areas. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, displaying a range of colors from white to pink.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has limited use in traditional medicine.
