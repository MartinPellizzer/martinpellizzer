Alocasia vulcanica

Alocasia vulcanica, commonly known as the Vulcan's Alocasia, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length, often with a dark green to almost black coloration. The plant produces striking white to pale yellow spathes that enclose its small, inconspicuous flowers. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Alocasia vulcanica is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
