Andriapetalum yolombo

Andriapetalum yolombo, commonly known as the Yolombo rock aster, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of numerous small florets. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils, thriving in well-drained environments. Its leaves are usually narrow and linear, while its flowers are small and clustered in dense inflorescences.

The species is notable for its ability to withstand harsh environmental conditions, making it a resilient component of its native ecosystem.
