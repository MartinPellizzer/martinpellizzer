Ardisia clemensii

Ardisia clemensii, commonly known as the Christmas berry, is a small evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It features glossy, dark green leaves and produces small, red berries that are edible for humans and animals. The plant belongs to the Myrsinaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and ornamental appeal. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Ardisia clemensii is often cultivated for its attractive foliage and seasonal fruiting.
