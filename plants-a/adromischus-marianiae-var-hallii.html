Adromischus marianiae var. hallii

Adromischus marianiae var. hallii, commonly known as the Hall's moon cactus, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a bluish or purplish tint. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with plenty of sunlight.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for succulent enthusiasts.
