Arundinaria variegata var. tsumorii

Arundinaria variegata var. tsumorii, commonly known as the Japanese bamboo or Tsumorii bamboo, is a variant of the bamboo genus Arundinaria.

It is characterized by its slender, green to yellow-green culms that often exhibit a variegated pattern. The plant is native to Japan and is typically found in moist, shaded environments. Its leaves are long and narrow, with a smooth texture and a bluish-green hue.

This bamboo is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic and structural qualities.
