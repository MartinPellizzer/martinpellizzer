Ambulia

Ambulia, commonly known as the silverleaf vine, is a climbing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a silvery underside, giving it its distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters, typically in shades of white and pink. Its rapid growth habit makes it suitable for trellises or as a ground cover in shaded areas.

Ambulia is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in humid conditions.
