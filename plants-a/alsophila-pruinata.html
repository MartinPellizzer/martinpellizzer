Alsophila pruinata

Alsophila pruinata, commonly known as the silver tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk covered in a dense layer of silvery, scale-like structures. The fronds are pinnately compound, with a distinctive grayish-white coloration on the underside. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

This plant plays an important ecological role in its habitat, providing shelter and food for various organisms.
