Anthurium hoehnei

Anthurium hoehnei, commonly known as the Hoehne's anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves and vibrant, spadix-like inflorescence. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, clinging to trees in its natural habitat. Its foliage is often deep green with a glossy texture, and the flowers are usually bright red or orange, attracting pollinators.

Anthurium hoehnei is appreciated for its ornamental value and is cultivated in warm, humid environments.
