Anathallis attenuata

Anathallis attenuata, commonly known as the slender anathallist, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, pendant-like flowers that hang from the plant's stems. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in cloud forests. Its flowers are usually white or pale green, with a unique structure that resembles a small, elongated bell.

Anathallis attenuata is noted for its rarity and is often found in specific microhabitats within its native range.
