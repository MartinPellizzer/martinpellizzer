Astephanus cordifolius

Astephanus cordifolius, commonly known as the heart-leaf astephanus, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves, which are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its unique foliage and ornamental value make it a sought-after plant among horticulturists.
