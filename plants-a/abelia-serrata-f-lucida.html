Abelia serrata f. lucida

Abelia serrata f. lucida, commonly known as the shining abelia, is a hybrid shrub characterized by its striking, glossy, dark green leaves.

It produces clusters of fragrant, tubular flowers in shades of white and pink, which bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant is native to the southeastern United States and thrives in partial shade to full sun, preferring well-drained, acidic soil. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various garden conditions.

Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for borders, hedges, or container gardening.
