Arum rauwolffii

Arum rauwolffii, commonly known as the giant arrowhead, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 2 meters in length. The plant produces striking purple spadices surrounded by a white or green spathe, which is typical of the Araceae family. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found near water bodies.

Its botanical features make it both ornamental and ecologically significant in its native habitat.
