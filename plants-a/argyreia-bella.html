Argyreia bella

Argyreia bella, commonly known as the silver leaf nightshade, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking silver-gray foliage and clusters of small, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other nightshades like tomatoes and peppers. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

However, it contains toxic compounds and should be handled with care.
