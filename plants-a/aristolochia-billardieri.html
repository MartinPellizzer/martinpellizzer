Aristolochia billardieri

Aristolochia billardieri, commonly known as the Brazilian birthwort, is a climbing vine native to South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, dark green leaves and distinctive, pipe-shaped flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color. The plant produces small, reddish-brown berries that contain the seeds. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in shaded, moist environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens, though it can be invasive in certain regions.
