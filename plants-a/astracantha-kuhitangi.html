Astracantha kuhitangi

Astracantha kuhitangi, commonly known as the Kuhitangi burr bush, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, spiny, globe-shaped fruits. The plant has narrow, grey-green leaves and produces yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in coastal and dry inland areas, often growing in sandy or rocky soils.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is of conservation interest in its native habitat.
