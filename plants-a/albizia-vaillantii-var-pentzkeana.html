Albizia vaillantii var. pentzkeana

Albizia vaillantii var. pentzkeana, commonly known as the pink-veined albizia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compound leaves with distinctive pink veins, which give it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters, typically blooming in the summer months. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Albizia vaillantii var. pentzkeana is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it beneficial for soil enrichment.
