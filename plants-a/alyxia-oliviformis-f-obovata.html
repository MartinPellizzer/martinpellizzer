Alyxia oliviformis f. obovata

Alyxia oliviformis f. obovata, commonly known as the broad-leafed alyxia, is a variant of the alyxia plant characterized by its obovate leaves.

It is a flowering plant native to regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of Malaysia and Indonesia. The leaves are typically dark green and glossy, with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually white or pale yellow, clustered in inflorescences.

It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in lowland rainforests or secondary growth areas.
