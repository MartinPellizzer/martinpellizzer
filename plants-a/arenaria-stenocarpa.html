Arenaria stenocarpa

Arenaria stenocarpa, commonly known as the narrow-fruit sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to arctic and alpine regions.

It belongs to the family Caryophyllaceae and is characterized by its small, clustered flowers that bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to cold climates.

Its compact growth habit makes it a hardy addition to rock gardens and alpine landscapes.
