Anthemis cretica subsp. spruneri

Anthemis cretica subsp.

spruneri, commonly known as the Crete tansy, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region. It typically grows in dry, rocky soils and thrives in full sun.

The plant features aromatic, narrow leaves and produces small, yellowish-white flowers in clusters. It is part of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Its botanical characteristics make it well adapted to arid and coastal environments.
