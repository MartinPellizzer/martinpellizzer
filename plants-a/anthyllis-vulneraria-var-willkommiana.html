Anthyllis vulneraria var. willkommiana

Anthyllis vulneraria var. willkommiana, commonly known as the narrow-leaved catchfly, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that typically reaches a height of about 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features small, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer, and its leaves are narrow and linear. It thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and coastal areas.

The species is characterized by its distinctive seed pods that split open to release its seeds.
