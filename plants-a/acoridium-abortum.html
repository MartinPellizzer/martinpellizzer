Acoridium abortum

Acoridium abortum, commonly known as the aborted acoridium, is a rare and enigmatic plant belonging to the Acoraceae family.

It is characterized by its unique, narrow leaves that often exhibit a striking bluish-green hue. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically clustered in spike-like inflorescences. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a species of conservation concern.
