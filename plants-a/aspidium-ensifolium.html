Aspidium ensifolium

Aspidium ensifolium, commonly known as the sword fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its elongated, lance-shaped fronds that resemble a sword, hence its common name. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forests and rocky crevices. Its sporangia are clustered in sori, which are located on the underside of the fronds.

Aspidium ensifolium is a perennial plant that can thrive in a variety of temperate climates.
