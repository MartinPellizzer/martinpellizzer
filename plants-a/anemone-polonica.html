Anemone polonica

Anemone polonica, commonly known as the Polish anemone, is a herbaceous perennial native to Europe.

It features showy, white or pinkish-white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has a short, thick rhizome and basal leaves that are palmately lobed. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking native flora.
