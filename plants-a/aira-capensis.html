Aira capensis

Aira capensis, commonly known as the African feather grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery plumes that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue.

Aira capensis is often used in ornamental gardening for its aesthetic appeal and drought tolerance.
