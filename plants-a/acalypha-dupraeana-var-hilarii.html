Acalypha dupraeana var. hilarii

Acalypha dupraeana var. hilarii, commonly known as the velvetleaf or flameleaf plant, is a species native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage with leaves that often display shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant has a bushy growth habit and can reach heights of up to 2 meters. Its leaves are typically velvety to the touch, giving it its common name.

It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its vibrant foliage.
