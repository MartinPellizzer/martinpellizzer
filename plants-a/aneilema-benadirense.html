Aneilema benadirense

Aneilema benadirense, commonly known as the Benin flame plant, is a striking tropical shrub native to western Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant red and orange floral bracts that surround small, inconspicuous flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in the understory of rainforests. Its leaves are usually dark green and glossy, with a slightly serrated edge.

Aneilema benadirense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
