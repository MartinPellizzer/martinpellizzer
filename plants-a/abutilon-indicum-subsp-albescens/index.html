Abutilon Indicum Subsp. Albescens

Abutilon indicum subsp. albescens is a unique plant species known for its striking white flowers and fuzzy leaves. It belongs to the Malvaceae family, which includes hibiscus and cotton. This subspecies is native to parts of Asia and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes. The plant's ability to thrive in varied conditions contributes to its widespread cultivation.
