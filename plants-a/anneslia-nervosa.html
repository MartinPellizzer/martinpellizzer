Anneslia nervosa

Anneslia nervosa, commonly known as the slender anneslia, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, blue to violet flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Anneslia nervosa is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens for its striking floral display.
