Aglaia grandifoliola

Aglaia grandifoliola, commonly known as the large-leaf aglaia, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, elongated leaves that can grow up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or greenish-yellow in color. Its fruit is a fleshy, orange-colored berry that contains several seeds.

Aglaia grandifoliola is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has traditional uses in local medicine.
