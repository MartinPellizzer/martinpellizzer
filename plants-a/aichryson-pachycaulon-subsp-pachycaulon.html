Aichryson pachycaulon subsp. pachycaulon

Aichryson pachycaulon subsp.

pachycaulon, commonly known as the thick-stemmed aichryson, is a succulent shrub native to South Africa. It features dense, fleshy stems that store water, enabling it to thrive in arid environments.

The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. Its leaves are typically small and often covered in a fine layer of white wax, which helps reduce water loss.

It is well-suited for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and ornamental appeal.
