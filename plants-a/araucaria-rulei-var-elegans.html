Araucaria rulei var. elegans

Araucaria rulei var. elegans, commonly known as the elegant Norfolk pine, is a rare and unique variant of the Norfolk pine species.

It is characterized by its slender, upright trunk and feather-like foliage, which gives it a delicate, tree-like appearance. Native to a restricted area in New Caledonia, this plant thrives in humid, tropical climates with well-drained soil. Its slow growth rate and ornamental value make it a sought-after specimen in botanical gardens and private collections.

Due to its limited natural range, conservation efforts are essential to protect this rare botanical treasure.
