Argyroderma angustipetalum

Argyroderma angustipetalum, commonly known as the narrow-petaled silverleaf.

It is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly found in Argentina and Chile. The plant is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and small, delicate white or pale pink flowers. It thrives in arid or semi-arid environments and is often found in rocky soils.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
