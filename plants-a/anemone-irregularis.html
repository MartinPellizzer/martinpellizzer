Anemone irregularis

Anemone irregularis, commonly known as the irregular anemone, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, irregularly shaped petals that range in color from white to pale pink. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, preferring well-drained soil and full sun exposure. Its flowers bloom in early spring, adding a delicate touch to the landscape.

Anemone irregularis is often found in meadows and open slopes at high altitudes.
