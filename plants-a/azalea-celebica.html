Azalea celebica

Azalea celebica, commonly known as the Celebes azalea, is a flowering plant native to the Indonesian island of Sulawesi.

It belongs to the Rhododendron genus and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant is evergreen, with glossy, oval-shaped leaves that provide a striking backdrop to its blooms. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soil and requires partial shade to full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its ornamental value has led to its cultivation in various horticultural settings around the world.
