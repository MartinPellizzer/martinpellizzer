Anisacanthus insignis var. linearis

Anisacanthus insignis var. linearis, commonly known as the linear leaf flame flower, is a deciduous shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It features narrow, linear leaves that are typically 2 to 4 inches long, giving it a distinctive, elegant appearance. The plant produces vibrant orange-red tubular flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for xeriscaping.

Its drought tolerance and showy blooms make it a valuable addition to native gardens.
