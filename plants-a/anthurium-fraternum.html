Anthurium fraternum

Anthurium fraternum, commonly known as the "Sister's Anthurium," is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically deep green with a waxy texture. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-shaped flowers surrounded by a colorful spathe, often in shades of red, orange, or yellow. It thrives in humid, warm environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium fraternum is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
