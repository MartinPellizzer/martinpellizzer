Annona senegalensis var. deltoides

Annona senegalensis var. deltoides, commonly known as the African sugar apple, is a tropical shrub native to West Africa.

It produces a large, green, fleshy fruit with a sweet, aromatic pulp that is edible. The plant has smooth, dark green leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value as well as its fruit. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

The fruit is rich in nutrients and is used in both culinary and traditional medicinal applications.
