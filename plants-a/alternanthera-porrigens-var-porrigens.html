Alternanthera porrigens var. porrigens

Alternanthera porrigens var. porrigens, commonly known as the red-leaf alternanthera, is a vibrant aquatic plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking red or pink foliage, which intensifies under strong lighting and nutrient-rich conditions. This plant thrives in tropical and subtropical freshwater environments, often found in slow-moving streams and marshes. Its leaves are lance-shaped and can grow up to 10 centimeters in length, with a velvety texture.

It is popular in aquariums for its aesthetic appeal and ability to provide habitat for small aquatic life.
