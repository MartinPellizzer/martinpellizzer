Amaranthus tricolor var. acutus

Amaranthus tricolor var. acutus, commonly known as red leaf amaranth, is a strikingly colorful plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its vibrant, multicolored leaves that range from red, orange, and yellow to green. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or brown. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its foliage is often used in culinary applications, adding both visual appeal and nutritional value to dishes.
