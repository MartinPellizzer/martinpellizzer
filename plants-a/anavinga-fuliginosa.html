Anavinga fuliginosa

Anavinga fuliginosa, commonly known as the black anavinga, is a tropical plant native to parts of South America.

It is characterized by its dark, almost black, bark and its ability to thrive in humid, shaded environments. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged alternately along its stem. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are often green or reddish in color.

Anavinga fuliginosa is valued for its medicinal properties, particularly in traditional herbal remedies.
