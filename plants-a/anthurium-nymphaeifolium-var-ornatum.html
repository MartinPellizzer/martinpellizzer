Anthurium nymphaeifolium var. ornatum

Anthurium nymphaeifolium var. ornatum, commonly known as the Water Lily Anthurium, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often a deep green with a waxy texture. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-shaped inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, typically in shades of red or orange. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires consistent moisture and high humidity to flourish.

Its unique foliage and dramatic blooms make it a popular choice for indoor and greenhouse cultivation.
