Astranthium xylopodum

Astranthium xylopodum, commonly known as the narrowleaf starflower, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features showy, star-shaped yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. Its leaves are narrow and lanceolate, often with a slightly hairy texture.

Astranthium xylopodum is typically found in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid environments.
