Aucuba confertiflora

Aucuba confertiflora, commonly known as the denseflower aucuba, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its evergreen foliage, with dark green, glossy leaves that are typically elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, clustered yellowish-white flowers that are borne on short pedicels. It thrives in shaded to partially shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Aucuba confertiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in horticulture for its attractive foliage and resilience.
