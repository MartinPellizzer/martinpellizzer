Adesmia tunuianica

Adesmia tunuianica, commonly known as the Tunuian legume, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of northern Africa.

It is a small shrub characterized by its slender, often branched stems and small, compound leaves. The plant produces clusters of tiny, yellowish-white flowers, which are typical of the Fabaceae family. Its fruit is a legume pod that contains several seeds, adapted for dispersal by wind or animals.

Adesmia tunuianica is well-suited to harsh environments, showing resilience to drought and poor soil conditions.
