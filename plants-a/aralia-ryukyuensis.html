Aralia ryukyuensis

Aralia ryukyuensis, commonly known as the Ryukyu aralia, is a flowering plant native to the Ryukyu Islands of Japan.

It is a member of the Araliaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and small white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments within mountainous regions. Its berries are edible and have been used in traditional cuisine.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological significance in its native habitat.
