Aster sonderi

Aster sonderi, commonly known as the Sonder's aster, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in sunny environments. Its flowers are usually purple or blue, with a central disk surrounded by ray florets.

Aster sonderi is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and botanical collections.
