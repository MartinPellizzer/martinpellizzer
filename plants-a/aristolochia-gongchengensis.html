Aristolochia gongchengensis

Aristolochia gongchengensis, commonly known as the Guangxi arrowhead vine, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Aristolochiaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, heart-shaped leaves and unique, pipe-shaped flowers. The plant is often found in humid, shaded environments within its natural range. Its flowers are typically greenish-yellow with a strong, musty odor, which attracts specific pollinators.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal potential, it has gained attention in horticultural and botanical circles.
