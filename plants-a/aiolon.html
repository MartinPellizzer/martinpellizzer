Aiolon

Aiolon, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies, sunflowers, and ragworts. The plant features showy, daisy-like flowers with yellow centers and white to pink petals. It typically grows in grasslands and rocky areas, thriving in full sun.
