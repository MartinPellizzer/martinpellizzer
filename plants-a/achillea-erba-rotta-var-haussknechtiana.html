Achillea erba-rotta var. haussknechtiana

Achillea erba-rotta var. haussknechtiana, commonly known as the Haussknechtian yarrow, is a perennial herb native to southern Europe.

It features finely divided, feathery leaves and produces clusters of tiny, flat, white or pale yellow flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders. It is known for its aromatic foliage and is often used in herbal medicine.

This variety is noted for its compact growth habit and is a hardy plant that attracts beneficial insects.
