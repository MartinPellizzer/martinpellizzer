Ardisia lenticellata

Ardisia lenticellata, commonly known as the smooth-leafed evergreen.

It is a shrub native to Southeast Asia, characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of small, white flowers. The plant produces bright red berries that are edible for humans but toxic to some animals. Ardisia lenticellata thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its botanical features make it a popular ornamental plant in horticulture.
