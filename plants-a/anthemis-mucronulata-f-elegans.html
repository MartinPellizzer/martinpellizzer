Anthemis mucronulata f. elegans

Anthemis mucronulata f. elegans, commonly known as the elegant cudweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its small, yellowish-green composite flowers and hairy, lance-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as meadows and rocky areas. It is native to regions of southern Europe and parts of North Africa.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
