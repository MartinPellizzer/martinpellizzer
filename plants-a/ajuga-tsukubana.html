Ajuga tsukubana

Ajuga tsukubana, commonly known as Japanese bugleweed, is a low-growing perennial herb native to Japan.

It features dense clusters of small, dark purple to blue flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs, giving it a soft texture. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Ajuga tsukubana is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to form dense ground cover.
