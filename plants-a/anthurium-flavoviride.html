Anthurium flavoviride

Anthurium flavoviride, commonly known as the yellow-green anthurium, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped leaves that range from bright green to yellow-green in color. The plant produces spadices, which are inflorescences that consist of a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe. Anthurium flavoviride thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and ornamental purposes.
