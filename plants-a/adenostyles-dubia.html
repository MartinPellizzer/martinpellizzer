Adenostyles dubia

Adenostyles dubia, commonly known as the slender bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features upright, branched stems covered in fine, hairy hairs. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically blue or violet in color, blooming from late spring to early summer. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often with a slightly hairy surface.

Adenostyles dubia is well adapted to dry, rocky soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and scrublands.
