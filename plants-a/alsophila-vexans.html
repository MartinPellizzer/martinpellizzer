Alsophila vexans

Alsophila vexans, commonly known as the slender tree fern, is a species native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and a crown of pinnate fronds. The plant has a robust rhizome system that supports its growth in moist, shaded environments. Its fronds are typically dark green and can reach lengths of up to 2 meters.
