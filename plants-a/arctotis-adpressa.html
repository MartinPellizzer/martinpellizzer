Arctotis adpressa

Arctotis adpressa, commonly known as the prostrate arctotis, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically forms dense mats of foliage. The plant produces showy, daisy-like flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, which bloom throughout the growing season. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its compact growth habit and vibrant blooms make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
