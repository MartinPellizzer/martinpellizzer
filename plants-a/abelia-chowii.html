Abelia chowii

Abelia chowii, commonly known as Chinese abelia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features attractive, fragrant, tubular flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are usually dark green and slightly hairy. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained soil.

Abelia chowii is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability in garden settings.
