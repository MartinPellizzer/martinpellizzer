Acis Autumnalis

Acis autumnalis is a small, delicate flowering plant known for its vibrant autumn blooms. It thrives in woodland and shaded areas, preferring moist, well-drained soil. This species is often overlooked but plays an important role in supporting local pollinators. Its flowers, which range from white to pale pink, add a subtle beauty to the forest floor. Despite its modest appearance, Acis autumnalis is a valuable addition to native plant gardens and conservation efforts.
