Aloe sladeniana

Aloe sladeniana, commonly known as the Sladen's aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asphodelaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are thick and fleshy. The leaves are typically green with a reddish or purple tint, especially when exposed to full sun. It produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow flowers that bloom in spring.

This plant is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
