Acianthera atrata

Acianthera atrata, commonly known as the dark violet orchid, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its dark purple to blackish petals and sepals, which give it its common name. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments such as rainforests. It has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients.

Acianthera atrata produces small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting various pollinators.
