Abutilon bicolor

Abutilon bicolor, commonly known as the velvetleaf abutilon, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its fuzzy, velvety leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces showy, lantern-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in warm climates and is often found in disturbed soils or along roadsides.

Abutilon bicolor is also known for its ability to grow as a shrub or small tree, depending on environmental conditions.
