Astragalus lamarckii subsp. bombycalyx

Astragalus lamarckii subsp.

bombycalyx, commonly known as the Chinese milkvetch, is a perennial legume native to China. It is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in late spring.

The plant thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in grasslands and rocky soils. It has a deep root system that helps it survive in dry conditions.

This subspecies is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional Chinese medicine.
