Arisaema shimienense

Arisaema shimienense, commonly known as the Shimien arrowhead, is a rare and striking member of the Araceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Sichuan, China, where it thrives in cool, moist environments. The plant features a distinctive, arrow-shaped leaf that is often covered in a dense layer of fine hairs. Its inflorescence consists of a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe, which adds to its ornamental appeal.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species in the wild.
