Aristolochia deltantha var. laheyana

Aristolochia deltantha var. laheyana, commonly known as the Florida Dutchman's pipe, is a climbing vine native to the southeastern United States.

It features heart-shaped, aromatic leaves with a distinctive waxy texture. The plant produces unique, hooded flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in swampy areas.

Its botanical characteristics make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural study.
