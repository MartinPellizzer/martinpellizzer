Abelia serrata var. integerrima

Abelia serrata var. integerrima, commonly known as the smooth abelia, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features upright stems with opposite, ovate to elliptic leaves that are typically smooth and dark green on top. The plant produces clusters of tubular, white to pale pink flowers in the summer, which attract pollinators like bees and butterflies. It thrives in partial to full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for garden landscapes.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental value make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
