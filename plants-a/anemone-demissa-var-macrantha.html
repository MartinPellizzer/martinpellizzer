Anemone demissa var. macrantha

Anemone demissa var. macrantha, commonly known as the showy anemone, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the eastern United States.

It features basal leaves that are broadly ovate with a wavy margin and a hairy surface. The plant produces showy, clustered flowers in shades of white to pink, with five to seven petals and a prominent central cone. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for native gardens and ecological restoration projects.
