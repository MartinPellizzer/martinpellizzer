Amaryllis blanda

Amaryllis blanda, commonly known as the dwarf amaryllis, is a species of flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a bulbous perennial that produces clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, and red. The plant has strap-like, grassy leaves that emerge from the base of the bulb. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Amaryllis blanda is popular for its ease of care and vibrant, long-lasting blooms.
