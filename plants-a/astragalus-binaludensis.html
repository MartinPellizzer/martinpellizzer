Astragalus binaludensis

Astragalus binaludensis, commonly known as the Binalud milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of Iran.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its small, yellowish flowers and compound leaves. The plant is adapted to survive in dry, rocky environments, showcasing a deep root system for water absorption.

It plays a role in stabilizing soil and supporting local biodiversity in its native habitat.
