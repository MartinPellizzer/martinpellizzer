Astrocaryum macrocalyx

Astrocaryum macrocalyx, commonly known as the yellow murua, is a species of palm native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate leaves that can reach several meters in length. The plant produces yellow to orange fruits that are clustered in bunches, containing edible seeds. It thrives in humid, tropical climates with well-drained soil.

The botanical features of Astrocaryum macrocalyx make it an important component of the rainforest ecosystem.
