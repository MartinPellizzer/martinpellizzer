Agathosma geniculata

Agathosma geniculata, commonly known as silver leaf bush, is a small shrub native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, silver-gray leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are followed by orange-red fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.

Due to its unique appearance and fragrance, it is occasionally cultivated for ornamental purposes.
