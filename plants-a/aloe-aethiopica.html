Aloe aethiopica

Aloe aethiopica, commonly known as the African aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a white or yellowish margin. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. Its gel is used for its soothing and healing properties, making it popular in traditional medicine.

Aloe aethiopica is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil and full sunlight.
