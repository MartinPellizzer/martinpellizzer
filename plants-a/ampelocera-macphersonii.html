Ampelocera macphersonii

Ampelocera macphersonii, commonly known as the Macpherson's spider-orchid, is a rare and unique orchid species native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its intricate, spider-like appearance, with long, slender petals that resemble the legs of a spider. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in semi-arid environments. Its flowers are usually pale green or yellowish, with a distinctive, narrow shape.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a vulnerable species.
