Ascolepis tenuior

Ascolepis tenuior, commonly known as the slender sandwort, is a low-growing, mat-forming plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Montiaceae and is characterized by its small, succulent leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white wax. Due to its hardy nature, it is often used in xeriscaping and as a ground cover in dry landscapes.
