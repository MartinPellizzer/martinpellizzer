Actinostemon communis var. spathularis

Actinostemon communis var. spathularis, commonly known as the narrowleaf actinostemon, is a herbaceous perennial native to parts of Asia.

It features slender, upright stems with lance-shaped leaves that are typically less than 10 cm in length. The plant produces small, clustered flowers with prominent, spatulate-shaped bracts that give it its common name. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Its ornamental value lies in its delicate foliage and unique floral structure.
