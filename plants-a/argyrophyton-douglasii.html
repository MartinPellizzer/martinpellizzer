Argyrophyton douglasii

Argyrophyton douglasii, commonly known as the Douglas's silverleaf.

It is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico. The plant is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, white hairs. It produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the spring.

The species is adapted to arid environments and is often found in rocky soils and open woodlands.
