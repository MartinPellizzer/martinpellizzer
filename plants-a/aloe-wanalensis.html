Aloe wanalensis

Aloe wanalensis, commonly known as the Wanalensis Aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a waxy coating, which helps it retain moisture in arid conditions. The plant produces striking, tall flower stalks with yellow flowers, which bloom during the warmer months. It is well adapted to rocky and sandy soils, making it a hardy choice for xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and resilience make it a popular plant among succulent enthusiasts.
