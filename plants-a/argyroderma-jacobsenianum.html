Argyroderma jacobsenianum

Argyroderma jacobsenianum, commonly known as the Jacobson's silverleaf.

It is a perennial herb native to South Africa, characterized by its silvery, hairy leaves that reflect light and reduce water loss. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky soils.

Its unique morphology and adaptability make it an interesting subject for botanical study.
