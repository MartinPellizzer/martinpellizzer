Alstonia venenata var. pubescens

Alstonia venenata var. pubescens, commonly known as the hairy alstonia, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its smooth, dark grey bark and oval-shaped leaves with a leathery texture. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers followed by orange-red berries that contain toxic seeds. It is often found in lowland forests and is valued for its ornamental appeal.

Due to its toxicity, it should be handled with care.
