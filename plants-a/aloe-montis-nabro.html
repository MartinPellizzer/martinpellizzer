Aloe montis-nabro

Aloe montis-nabro, commonly known as the Nabro mountain aloe, is a succulent plant native to the high altitudes of the Ethiopian Highlands.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that store water, adapted to its arid environment. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow flowers, typical of the Aloe genus. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their soothing properties.

Aloe montis-nabro thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for rocky or mountainous habitats.
