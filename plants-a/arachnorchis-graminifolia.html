Arachnorchis graminifolia

Arachnorchis graminifolia, commonly known as the grass-leaf spider orchid, is an epiphytic orchid native to Australia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and delicate, spider-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks in moist, shaded environments. Its flowers are usually pale green or yellow, with intricate patterns that aid in pollination by specific insects.

Arachnorchis graminifolia is a rare and endangered species, requiring careful conservation efforts to ensure its survival.
