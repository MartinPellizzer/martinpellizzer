Abelia tyaihyoni var. mosanensis

Abelia tyaihyoni var. mosanensis, commonly known as the Mosanensis Abelia, is a compact, flowering shrub native to China.

It features narrow, dark green leaves that are typically arranged in opposite pairs along the stems. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white to pinkish flowers in the summer, which attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained, moist soil.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
