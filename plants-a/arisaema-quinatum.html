Arisaema quinatum

Arisaema quinatum, commonly known as the Chinese arrowhead, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive arrow-shaped leaves and striking, spadix-bearing inflorescence. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. Its underground tuberous roots are sometimes used in traditional medicine.

Arisaema quinatum is also valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
