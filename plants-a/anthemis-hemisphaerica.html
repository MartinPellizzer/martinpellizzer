Anthemis hemisphaerica

Anthemis hemisphaerica, commonly known as the spherical anthemis, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in late summer and early autumn. Its spherical shape is unique, with tightly packed flower heads forming a compact, rounded structure.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.
