Athanasia squarrosa

Athanasia squarrosa, commonly known as the squarrose everlasting flower, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant is known for its showy, long-lasting inflorescences that consist of numerous small, tubular flowers. Athanasia squarrosa typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in coastal or mountainous regions.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its drought tolerance and vibrant blooms.
