Aralia beccarii

Aralia beccarii, commonly known as the Beccari's aralia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, compound leaves that can reach several feet in length. The plant produces small, white flowers arranged in an umbel, which are followed by spherical, berry-like fruits. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for botanical gardens and shaded landscapes.
