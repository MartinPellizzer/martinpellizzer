Artanthe cearensis

Artanthe cearensis, commonly known as the Brazilian aster, is a flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers. The plant features showy, violet-blue flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, giving them a slightly fuzzy texture.

Artanthe cearensis is often found in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils.
