Aloe richtersveldensis

Aloe richtersveldensis, commonly known as the Richtersveld aloe, is a succulent plant native to the arid regions of South Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are thick and fleshy, adapted for water storage in its harsh environment. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing yellow flowers, which bloom during the rainy season. Its leaves contain a gel-like substance that has been used for its healing properties.

Aloe richtersveldensis is also noted for its resilience to extreme temperatures and drought conditions.
