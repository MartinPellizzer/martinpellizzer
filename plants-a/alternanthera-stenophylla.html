Alternanthera stenophylla

Alternanthera stenophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leaf alternanthera, is a species of flowering plant in the Amaranthaceae family.

It is native to South America, particularly Brazil, and is characterized by its striking red or purple foliage. The plant typically grows as an annual herb, reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters. Its leaves are narrow and often display vibrant coloration, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.
