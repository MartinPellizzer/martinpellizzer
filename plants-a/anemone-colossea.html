Anemone colossea

Anemone colossea, commonly known as the meadow anemone, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features delicate, white to pale pink flowers with a distinctive, rounded center, blooming in early spring. The plant has basal leaves that are palmately lobed and covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows and woodland edges.

Anemone colossea is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting pollinators.
