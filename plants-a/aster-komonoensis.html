Aster komonoensis

Aster komonoensis, commonly known as the Komono aster, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and medicinal properties. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. Its flowers are usually blue to purple in color, with narrow, linear leaves.

It is valued both for its ornamental appeal and traditional use in herbal medicine.
