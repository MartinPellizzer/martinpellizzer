Alocasia nicolsonii

Alocasia nicolsonii, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features large, heart-shaped leaves with a dark green upper surface and a lighter, often variegated underside. The plant is prized for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in low-light conditions. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense foliage.

Botanically, it belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its unique inflorescence structure.
