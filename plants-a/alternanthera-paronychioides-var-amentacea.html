Alternanthera paronychioides var. amentacea

Alternanthera paronychioides var. amentacea, commonly known as the golden thread plant, is a tropical perennial known for its striking, cascading inflorescences.

It features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in dense, pendulous clusters, creating a visually appealing display. The plant has succulent, fleshy leaves that are often tinged with red or purple, adding to its ornamental value. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings due to its delicate appearance and ease of care.
