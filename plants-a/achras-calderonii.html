Achras calderonii

Achras calderonii, commonly known as the dragon blood tree, is a unique succulent plant native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the Arecaceae family and is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves and a tall, unbranched stem. The plant produces a red, resinous sap that has been historically used for medicinal and dye purposes. Its growth habit is slow, and it can live for several decades under suitable conditions.

The species is also noted for its ability to thrive in arid environments with minimal water requirements.
