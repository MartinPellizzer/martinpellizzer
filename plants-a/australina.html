Australina

Australina, commonly known as the Australian tea tree, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small white or pink flowers. The plant is well adapted to the dry and sunny conditions of its native habitat. It is often used in traditional Aboriginal medicine and has become popular in modern aromatherapy.

Its essential oil, extracted from the leaves, is known for its antiseptic and anti-inflammatory properties.
