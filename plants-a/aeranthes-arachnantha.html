Aeranthes arachnantha

Aeranthes arachnantha, commonly known as the spider orchid, is a striking member of the Orchidaceae family native to South Africa.

This plant is characterized by its unique, spider-like appearance, with long, slender leaves and intricate, delicate flowers that resemble a spider's web. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions. The flowers are typically pale green or white, with a distinctive, tubular shape that attracts specific pollinators.

Due to its rarity and aesthetic appeal, it is a prized species among orchid enthusiasts and conservationists.
