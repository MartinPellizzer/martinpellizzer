Anoplon Tournefortii

Anoplon tournefortii is a rare and unique plant native to southern France and parts of North Africa. It thrives in rocky and arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. This species is known for its distinctive yellow flowers and compact growth habit. Due to its limited distribution, it is considered endangered and requires conservation efforts. Its beauty and ecological significance make it a subject of interest for botanists and nature enthusiasts alike.
