Alternanthera denticulata

Alternanthera denticulata, commonly known as the toothed Alternanthera, is a species of flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage that often displays shades of red, pink, and orange. The plant features small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow, and it thrives in moist, well-drained soil. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its vibrant leaves and is popular in aquatic and bog gardens.
