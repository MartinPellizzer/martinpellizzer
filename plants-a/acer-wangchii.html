Acer wangchii

Acer wangchii, commonly known as the Yunnan maple, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange foliage in the autumn, which makes it a popular ornamental plant. The leaves are typically palmately lobed, with a serrated margin and a smooth, glossy surface. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, often growing in mountainous regions.

The tree produces small, red flowers in early spring, followed by winged samaras as its fruit.
