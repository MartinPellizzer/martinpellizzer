Aster fischerianus

Aster fischerianus, commonly known as the Fischer's aster, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads and medicinal properties. The plant typically grows in alpine environments, thriving in well-drained soils and full sun exposure. Its blue to violet flower heads are composed of numerous tiny florets, and it produces a distinctive, aromatic scent.

Aster fischerianus is valued for its ornamental beauty and has been used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
