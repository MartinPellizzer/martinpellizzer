Acer Platanoides F. Palmatifidum

Acer platanoides f. palmatifidum is a unique variant of the Norway maple known for its distinctive palmate leaves. This cultivar showcases a striking foliage pattern that sets it apart from its parent species. It is often admired for its ornamental value in gardens and urban landscapes. The plant thrives in temperate climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth. Its foliage changes color with the seasons, adding visual interest throughout the year.
