Achillea baltae

Achillea baltae, commonly known as the Balkan yarrow, is a flowering plant native to the Balkan Peninsula.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Its foliage consists of finely divided, aromatic leaves, and it produces small, white or pale yellow flowers.

Achillea baltae is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used traditionally in herbal medicine.
