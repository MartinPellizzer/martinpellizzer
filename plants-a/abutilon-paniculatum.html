Abutilon paniculatum

Abutilon paniculatum, commonly known as the Chinese lantern plant, is a flowering shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fuzzy, velvety leaves and showy, lantern-shaped yellow or orange flowers. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, preferring well-drained soil and full sun. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its distinctive foliage and vibrant blooms.

The species is also used in traditional medicine in some parts of its native range.
