Ageratina Tenuis

Ageratina tenuis is a delicate flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas. It is known for its small, fluffy white flowers that bloom in clusters. This plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories. Its foliage is typically narrow and lance-shaped, adding a unique texture to its appearance. Despite its modest size, Ageratina tenuis plays an important role in supporting local pollinators and ecosystems.
