Anthemis sibthorpii

Anthemis sibthorpii, commonly known as the Sierra foothill ragwort, is a flowering plant native to California.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky soils and is adapted to arid environments. It features yellow flowers that bloom in summer, and its leaves are often covered in fine hairs.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species within its range.
