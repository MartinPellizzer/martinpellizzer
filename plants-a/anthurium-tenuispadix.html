Anthurium tenuispadix

Anthurium tenuispadix, commonly known as the narrow-leaf anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its elongated, narrow leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces striking spadices, which are inflorescences that resemble a spike, often with a deep red or orange spathe. It thrives in humid, warm environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium tenuispadix is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
