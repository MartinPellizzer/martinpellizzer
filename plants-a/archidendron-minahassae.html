Archidendron minahassae

Archidendron minahassae, commonly known as the Minahassa legume, is a tropical plant native to Indonesia.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or greenish-yellow in color. Its leaves are pinnately compound, consisting of several oval-shaped leaflets.

The plant is often found in moist, well-drained soils in lowland areas.
