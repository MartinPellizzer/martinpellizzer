Angelonia eriostachys

Angelonia eriostachys, commonly known as the tropical angelonia, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact, upright growth habit and its ability to produce long-lasting blooms throughout the growing season. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of tubular, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of red, pink, and white. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and containers.

Angelonia eriostachys is also valued for its drought tolerance and resistance to common pests and diseases.
