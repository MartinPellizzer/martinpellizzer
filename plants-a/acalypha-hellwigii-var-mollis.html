Acalypha hellwigii var. mollis

Acalypha hellwigii var. mollis, commonly known as silky acalypha, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its soft, velvety leaves that give it a distinctive, almost fuzzy appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in humid, warm environments and is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking a unique and visually striking foliage plant.
