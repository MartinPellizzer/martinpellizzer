Artemisia yadongensis

Artemisia yadongensis, commonly known as the Yadong wormwood, is a perennial shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, silver-gray foliage. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in dry, rocky habitats and is often found in alpine regions.

Due to its medicinal properties, it has been traditionally used in Chinese herbal medicine.
