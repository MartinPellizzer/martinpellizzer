Aster velutinus

Aster velutinus, commonly known as the downy aster, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and woodlands. Its leaves are hairy and gray-green, while its flowers range from white to pinkish-purple.

Aster velutinus is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, providing nectar for pollinators.
