Athamanta turbith

Athamanta turbith, commonly known as the Turbith or Turbith plant, is a species of flowering plant native to certain regions of southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Acanthaceae and is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Athamanta turbith is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
