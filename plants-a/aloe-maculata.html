Aloe maculata

Aloe maculata, commonly known as the "Beaded Aloe," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, gray-green leaves with distinctive white or yellow tubercles. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with ample sunlight.

Aloe maculata is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
