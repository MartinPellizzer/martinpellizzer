Asperula longiflora var. aristata

Asperula longiflora var. aristata, commonly known as the aristate evergreen shrub, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe.

It features small, dark green, leathery leaves that are typically elliptical in shape. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant, white flowers in spring, which are followed by red, hairy berries. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a border plant.

Its hardy nature and ornamental value make it a popular choice in horticulture.
