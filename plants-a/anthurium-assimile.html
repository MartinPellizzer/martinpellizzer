Anthurium assimile

Anthurium assimile, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped spathes that range in color from deep red to orange. The plant produces small, green spadices that are clustered within the spatulate leaf-like structure. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium assimile is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
