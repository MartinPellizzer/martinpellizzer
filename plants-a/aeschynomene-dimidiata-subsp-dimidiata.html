Aeschynomene dimidiata subsp. dimidiata

Aeschynomene dimidiata subsp.

dimidiata, commonly known as partridge pea, is a flowering plant native to the Americas. It is a perennial legume characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers.

The plant is known for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it beneficial for soil fertility. It thrives in a variety of soil types and is often found in disturbed areas.

Its seeds are an important food source for birds and small mammals.
