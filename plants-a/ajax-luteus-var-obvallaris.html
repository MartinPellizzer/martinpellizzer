Ajax luteus var. obvallaris

Ajax luteus var. obvallaris, commonly known as the yellow arrowhead, is a striking aquatic plant characterized by its bright yellow to orange flowers that bloom in late summer.

It features long, slender, arrow-shaped leaves that are typically bright green and often have a waxy texture. The plant thrives in shallow, slow-moving freshwater environments, such as ponds and marshes. It is known for its ability to spread through rhizomes, forming dense underwater colonies.

Its vibrant color makes it a popular choice for ornamental water gardens.
