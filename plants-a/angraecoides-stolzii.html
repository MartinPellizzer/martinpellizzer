Angraecoides stolzii

Angraecoides stolzii, commonly known as the Stolzii orchid, is a species of orchid native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that bloom at night. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its flowers are usually white or pale yellow, with intricate patterns that attract specific pollinators like moths.

Angraecoides stolzii is noted for its rarity and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts due to its unique appearance and blooming habits.
