Anthyllis vulneraria f. rubens

Anthyllis vulneraria f. rubens, commonly known as the red clover, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and purple or pinkish-red flowers that bloom in spring. The plant is native to Europe and parts of western Asia, often found in grassy areas and disturbed soils. It has a taproot system and is known for its ability to fix nitrogen, improving soil fertility.

This variety is distinguished by its more intense red coloration compared to the typical white or pink flowers of the species.
