Aralia elegans

Aralia elegans, commonly known as the Japanese arrowhead, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its compound, feather-like leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, white or greenish flowers arranged in an umbel, which are followed by bright red berries. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade.

Aralia elegans is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively low maintenance.
