Ardisia austroasiatica

Ardisia austroasiatica, commonly known as the Asian beautyberry, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia and features small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces bright red berries that are an important food source for various birds and small mammals. It typically grows as an evergreen shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters.

Its leaves are glossy and dark green, with a smooth texture and a slightly aromatic scent when crushed.
