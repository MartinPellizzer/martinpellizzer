Alsophila minor

Alsophila minor, commonly known as the lesser tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and a crown of feather-like fronds. The plant has a slow growth rate and can live for several decades. Its fronds are typically bright green and can reach lengths of up to 3 meters.

Alsophila minor is often found in humid, shaded environments such as tropical forests.
