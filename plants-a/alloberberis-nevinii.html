Alloberberis nevinii

Alloberberis nevinii, commonly known as the Nevin's berberis, is a shrub native to the mountainous regions of southern China.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and dense foliage of small, leathery leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers in early spring, followed by red berries that are edible for birds. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Alloberberis nevinii is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
