Astragalus microcystis

Astragalus microcystis, commonly known as the small-cyst milkvetch, is a flowering plant species belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its small, yellow flowers and trifoliate leaves, typical of many Astragalus species. The plant is native to arid and semi-arid regions of central Asia, including parts of China and Mongolia. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in steppe and desert environments.

Astragalus microcystis is known for its ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, contributing to soil fertility in its native habitats.
