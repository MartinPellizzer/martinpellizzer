Anthurium victoriense

Anthurium victoriense, commonly known as the "Victoria's arrowhead," is a striking tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves with a glossy, dark green surface and prominent veins. The plant produces striking spadices, which are modified leaves that enclose the actual flowers, often in vibrant red or orange hues. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Anthurium victoriense is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
