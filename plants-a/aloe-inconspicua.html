Aloe inconspicua

Aloe inconspicua, commonly known as the dwarf aloe, is a small, compact succulent native to southern Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, narrow leaves that are typically green with a bluish tinge. The plant produces yellow flowers on slender stems during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its leaves.

Aloe inconspicua is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its low maintenance and attractive appearance.
