Aloe ferox var. xanthostachys

Aloe ferox var. xanthostachys, commonly known as the yellow-stemmed aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, with a waxy coating to store water. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing yellow to orange flowers, which attract pollinators such as bees and birds. Its rhizomatous root system allows it to spread and form dense colonies in its natural habitat.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional medicinal uses.
