Ammi meoides

Ammi meoides, commonly known as hoary alyssum, is a flowering plant native to the western United States and parts of Canada.

It is a low-growing annual herb with grayish-white, woolly foliage that helps it retain moisture in arid environments. Ammi meoides is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and roadsides.
