Anoda speciosa

Anoda speciosa, commonly known as the showy blueweed, is a flowering plant native to the southern United States.

It is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has palmate leaves with five to seven lobes, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soils.

Anoda speciosa is often found in prairies, roadsides, and disturbed areas, contributing to ecological diversity in its habitat.
