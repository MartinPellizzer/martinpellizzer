Arabis camtschatica

Arabis camtschatica, commonly known as the Siberian rockcress, is a flowering plant native to the Arctic and alpine regions of Eurasia.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and is adapted to cold climates. It produces small, white or yellow flowers that bloom in early spring.
