Adenocalymma apparicioanum

Adenocalymma apparicioanum, commonly known as the pink burr daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its distinctive pink to purple flower heads. The plant produces bract-like structures that enclose the flowers, giving it a unique appearance. It typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is adapted to arid environments.

Adenocalymma apparicioanum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.
