Asplenium chondrophyllum

Asplenium chondrophyllum, commonly known as the rock spleenwort, is a small, epiphytic fern that thrives in moist, shaded environments.

It is characterized by its compact, clustered growth and dark green, lance-shaped leaves. The plant typically has a short, creeping rhizome with dense, overlapping fronds. It is often found growing on rock surfaces or tree trunks in temperate regions.

Asplenium chondrophyllum is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
