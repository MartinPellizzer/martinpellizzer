Anil atriceps

Anil atriceps, commonly known as the black anil, is a striking plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its dark, almost black, velvety leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale purple, which contrast beautifully with its dark foliage. Anil atriceps thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in the understory of rainforests.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in warm climates.
