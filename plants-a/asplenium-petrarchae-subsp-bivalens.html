Asplenium petrarchae subsp. bivalens

Asplenium petrarchae subsp.

bivalens, commonly known as the Petrarch's spleenwort, is a fern belonging to the family Aspleniaceae. It is a small, evergreen epiphyte that typically grows on tree trunks or rocks in moist, shaded environments.

The plant features a compact rosette of lanceolate, dark green leaves with a distinctive midrib. Its spores are produced in sori located on the underside of the leaves, typical of ferns.

This subspecies is noted for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in shaded garden settings.
