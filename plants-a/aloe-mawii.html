Aloe mawii

Aloe mawii, commonly known as the red aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, upright leaves that are typically green with red or purple margins. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Aloe mawii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
