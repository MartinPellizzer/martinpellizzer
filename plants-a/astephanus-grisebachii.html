Astephanus grisebachii

Astephanus grisebachii, commonly known as the grey star grass, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its small, daisy-like flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in grasslands and rocky areas, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-gray hue that gives it its common name.

Astephanus grisebachii is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive appearance and hardy nature.
