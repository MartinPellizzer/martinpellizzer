Astrophytum quadratum

Astrophytum quadratum, commonly known as the Star Cactus, is a member of the Cactaceae family native to the deserts of Mexico.

It is characterized by its distinctive star-shaped body, which is covered in small, rounded tubercles. The plant produces yellow to orange flowers during the summer months, typically at night. It has a shallow root system adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for succulent collectors.
