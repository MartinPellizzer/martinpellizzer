Aloe barbadensis var. chinensis

Aloe barbadensis var. chinensis, commonly known as Chinese aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern China.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a waxy texture. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow flowers during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Its gel is used in traditional medicine and skincare products for its purported healing properties.
