Arisaema oblanceolatum

Arisaema oblanceolatum, commonly known as the broadleaf arrowhead, is a herbaceous plant native to the wetlands and marshy areas of East Asia.

It features a distinctive, arrow-shaped leaf that is broad and often variegated with white and green patterns. The plant produces a spadix surrounded by a hooded spathe, a characteristic feature of the Arisaema genus. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in swampy or boggy habitats.

Its unique morphology and ecological preferences make it a notable species in wetland ecosystems.
