Amauropelta boliviana

Amauropelta boliviana, commonly known as the Bolivian shield fern, is a unique member of the Pteridaceae family.

It is characterized by its distinctive, shield-shaped fronds that are typically leathery and dark green. The plant has a compact growth habit, with fronds emerging from a central rhizome. Native to the Andean regions of Bolivia, it thrives in moist, shaded environments.

Its botanical features make it a subject of interest for both horticulturists and botanists.
