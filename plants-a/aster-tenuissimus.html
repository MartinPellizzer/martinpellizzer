Aster tenuissimus

Aster tenuissimus, commonly known as slender aster, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that consist of both ray and disk flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands. It produces small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in the late summer and early fall.

The leaves are narrow and grass-like, often forming a basal rosette.
