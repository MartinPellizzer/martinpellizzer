Acianthera translucida

Acianthera translucida, commonly known as the translucent orchid, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, translucent petals that give it a unique appearance. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments within tropical forests. Its flowers are small and often clustered, with a distinct fragrance that attracts pollinators.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native range.
