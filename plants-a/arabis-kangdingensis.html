Arabis kangdingensis

Arabis kangdingensis, commonly known as the Kangding rockcress, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of Sichuan and Yunnan in China.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other well-known flowering plants. This species is characterized by its low-growing habit, with basal leaves and small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in early spring.
