Astragalus lioui

Astragalus lioui, commonly known as the Chinese milkvetch, is a perennial legume native to China.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellowish flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant produces cylindrical pods that contain several seeds, typical of the Fabaceae family. It is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid environments.

Astragalus lioui is valued for its medicinal properties in traditional Chinese medicine.
