Ajax pseudonarcissus var. pleurus

Ajax pseudonarcissus var. pleurus, commonly known as the Dutch bulbous iris, is a perennial plant native to southern Europe.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces striking blue or violet flowers with a distinctive yellow beard on the lower petals. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and easy maintenance.

The varietal name 'pleurus' refers to the thickened base of the flower petals, a characteristic feature of this cultivar.
