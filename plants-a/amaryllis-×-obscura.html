Amaryllis × obscura

Amaryllis × obscura, commonly known as the hybrid amaryllis, is a striking bulbous plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, pink, and white. The plant produces strap-like leaves that are typically broad and dark green. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires ample sunlight to bloom successfully.

Amaryllis × obscura is popular as a ornamental plant due to its vibrant flowers and relatively easy care requirements.
