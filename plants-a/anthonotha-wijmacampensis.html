Anthonotha wijmacampensis

Anthonotha wijmacampensis, commonly known as the Wijmacamp's antelope's foot, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in open grasslands. Its leaves are basal, succulent, and arranged in a rosette pattern, while its flowers are small and yellow.

It is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its unique appearance.
