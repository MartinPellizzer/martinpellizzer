Anthurium ravenii

Anthurium ravenii, commonly known as the black anthurium, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its dark, velvety foliage and vibrant red spadix, which is surrounded by a broad, heart-shaped spathe. The plant thrives in humid, warm environments with indirect light and well-draining soil. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and as a decorative plant.

Anthurium ravenii is also valued for its air-purifying properties and ability to thrive in low-light conditions.
