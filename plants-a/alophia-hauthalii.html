Alophia hauthalii

Alophia hauthalii, commonly known as the Hauthal's lily, is a striking bulbous plant native to the Canary Islands.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of white, yellow, and orange. The plant is adapted to grow in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and rockeries.
