Armeria maritima var. leucocephala

Armeria maritima var. leucocephala, commonly known as sea thrift, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of the northern hemisphere.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen foliage and clusters of white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and is often found in maritime climates. It is a hardy perennial that is tolerant of salt spray and wind.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological benefits, it is often used in coastal landscaping and restoration projects.
