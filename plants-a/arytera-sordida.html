Arytera sordida

Arytera sordida, commonly known as the dirty bellflower, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant features small, bell-shaped flowers that are usually a dull yellow or brownish color. Its leaves are narrow and often covered in a fine, powdery coating.

Arytera sordida is known for its ability to thrive in arid environments and is often found in mountainous regions.
