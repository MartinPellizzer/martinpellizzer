Alsophila dregei

Alsophila dregei, commonly known as the Drege's tree fern, is a species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds that can reach several meters in length. The plant belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is typically found in moist, shaded environments. Its rhizome is thick and woody, supporting the growth of the fronds above.

Alsophila dregei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical and subtropical gardens.
