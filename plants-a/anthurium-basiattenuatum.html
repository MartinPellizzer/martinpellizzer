Anthurium basiattenuatum

Anthurium basiattenuatum, commonly known as the dwarf anthurium, is a tropical flowering plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces striking spadix inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, often in shades of red, orange, or yellow. It thrives in humid, warm environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium basiattenuatum is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and long-lasting blooms.
