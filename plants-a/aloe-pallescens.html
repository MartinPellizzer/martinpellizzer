Aloe pallescens

Aloe pallescens, commonly known as the silver aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically gray-green in color, covered in a powdery coating. The plant produces clusters of yellow flowers during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil.

Aloe pallescens is often used in ornamental gardening due to its striking appearance and low maintenance requirements.
