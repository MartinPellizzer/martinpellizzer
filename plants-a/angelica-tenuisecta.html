Angelica tenuisecta

Angelica tenuisecta, commonly known as the slender angelica, is a member of the Apiaceae family, characterized by its aromatic foliage and umbellate flowers.

It is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of central and southern Europe. The plant features slender, hollow stems with finely divided, fern-like leaves that are often covered in a soft, white down. Its small, greenish-white flowers grow in umbrella-shaped clusters and bloom in late summer.

Angelica tenuisecta is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
