Amomum striatum

Amomum striatum, commonly known as the zigzag ginger, is a herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes other gingers and turmeric. The plant is characterized by its aromatic, striated rhizomes that are often used in traditional medicine. Its leaves are long and narrow, with a prominent midrib and a waxy surface.

Amomum striatum is valued for its medicinal properties, particularly in treating digestive ailments and respiratory conditions.
