Aconitum lycoctonum lusus glabriusculum

Aconitum lycoctonum lusus glabriusculum, commonly known as the smooth aconite, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of central and southern Europe.

It features striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer, typically from July to September. The plant has dark green, heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and is characterized by its tall, sparsely branched stems. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Due to its toxic alkaloids, it is important to handle the plant with care and avoid ingestion.
