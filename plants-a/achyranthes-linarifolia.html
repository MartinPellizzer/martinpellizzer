Achyranthes linarifolia

Achyranthes linarifolia, commonly known as slender knotweed, is a herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia and Africa.

It typically grows in moist, disturbed soils and can be found in wetlands, riverbanks, and marshy areas. The plant has erect, branching stems that are often covered with fine hairs. Its leaves are alternate, ovate to lanceolate, and have a slightly rough texture.

The flowers are small, greenish-white, and clustered in dense inflorescences, which are often subtended by bracts.
