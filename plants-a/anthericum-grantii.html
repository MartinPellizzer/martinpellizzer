Anthericum grantii

Anthericum grantii, commonly known as the Grant's lily, is a striking plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of white or pale pink flowers. The plant features strap-like, lance-shaped leaves that are often tinged with red or purple at the base. Anthericum grantii thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its elegant appearance and long blooming period.
