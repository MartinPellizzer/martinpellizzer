Astartea heteranthera

Astartea heteranthera, commonly known as the starry bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that typically range in color from white to lavender. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits containing numerous seeds.
