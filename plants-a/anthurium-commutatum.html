Anthurium commutatum

Anthurium commutatum, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a striking tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped spathes that range in color from deep red to maroon. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers inside the spathe, which are typically hidden from view. Its foliage consists of large, glossy, dark green leaves with a veined pattern.

Anthurium commutatum thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant.
