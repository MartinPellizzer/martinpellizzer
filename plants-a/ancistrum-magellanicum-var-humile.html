Ancistrum magellanicum var. humile

Ancistrum magellanicum var. humile, commonly known as the dwarf magellanic arrowroot, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, arrow-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, spatulate inflorescences with a central spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated as a houseplant or in botanical gardens.
