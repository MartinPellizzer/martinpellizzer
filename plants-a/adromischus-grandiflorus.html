Adromischus grandiflorus

Adromischus grandiflorus, commonly known as the "Lion's Tail" or "Coral Cactus," is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, ribbed stems that are typically green or reddish in color, and it produces clusters of small, bright pink or red flowers during the summer months. The plant is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick, fleshy leaves.

It is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
