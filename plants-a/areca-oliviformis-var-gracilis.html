Areca oliviformis var. gracilis

Areca oliviformis var. gracilis, commonly known as the slender areca palm, is a striking tropical plant characterized by its slender, upright trunk and feathery, pinnate leaves.

It belongs to the Arecaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia, where it thrives in warm, humid climates. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically followed by edible coconut-like fruits. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in tropical and subtropical regions.

It requires well-drained soil and regular watering to flourish.
