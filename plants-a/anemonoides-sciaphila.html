Anemonoides sciaphila

Anemonoides sciaphila, commonly known as the shade anemone, is a delicate flowering plant native to the temperate regions of East Asia.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodland floors and rocky crevices. The plant features heart-shaped, dark green leaves and produces small, pale yellow or white flowers with five petals. It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family, which includes other notable genera like Ranunculus and Clematis.

Due to its preference for cool, dim conditions, it is often cultivated in garden settings with partial shade.
