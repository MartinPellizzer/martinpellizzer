Arisaema chuanxiense

Arisaema chuanxiense, commonly known as the Chuanxi arrowhead, is a member of the Araceae family, characterized by its unique inflorescence structure.

It is native to China, specifically the Chuanxi region, and thrives in moist, shaded environments. The plant features a short, rhizomatous stem with large, arrowhead-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green. Its flowers are clustered in a spadix surrounded by a spathe, a common feature among arum plants.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has traditional uses in Chinese medicine.
