Acrothamnus

Acrothamnus, commonly known as the golden weed or silverleaf bush, is a genus of flowering plants in the daisy family, Asteraceae.

Acrothamnus species typically grow in arid or semi-arid environments, showcasing adaptations to drought and poor soil conditions.
