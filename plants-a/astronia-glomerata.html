Astronia glomerata

Astronia glomerata, commonly known as the clustered Astronia, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a rough texture.

The flowers are small and clustered, forming dense inflorescences that give the plant its common name.
