Arachniodes blinii

Arachniodes blinii, commonly known as the silver lace fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, lacy fronds that give it a distinctive, almost spider-web-like appearance. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit and thrives in shady, humid environments. Its leaves are typically bi- or tri-pinnate, with finely divided segments that create a soft, flowing texture.

Arachniodes blinii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its aesthetic appeal and ability to thrive in indoor settings.
