Anacamptis × cortesii

Anacamptis × cortesii, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a naturally occurring hybrid between Anacamptis morio and Anacamptis pyramidalis.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe, particularly in Spain and Italy. The plant produces tall, erect stems with showy, fragrant flowers that are typically violet or purple in color. Its inflorescence consists of a raceme of spurred orchid flowers, which bloom in late spring to early summer.

Anacamptis × cortesii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and fragrance.
