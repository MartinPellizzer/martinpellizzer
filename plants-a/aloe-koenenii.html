Aloe koenenii

Aloe koenenii, commonly known as the Cape aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stem and fleshy, sword-shaped leaves that are often covered in a white powdery substance. The plant produces clusters of yellow flowers at the top of the stem, which bloom in spring. Aloe koenenii is adapted to arid environments and stores water in its thick leaves.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some medicinal uses in traditional medicine.
