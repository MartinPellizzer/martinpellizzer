Aruncus dioicus var. subrotundatus

Aruncus dioicus var. subrotundatus, commonly known as the rounded goat's beard, is a herbaceous perennial native to eastern North America.

It features striking, feathery, white to pale pink inflorescences that appear in early summer. The plant has lance-shaped, dark green leaves with a rough texture and serrated edges. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and ecological restoration projects.
