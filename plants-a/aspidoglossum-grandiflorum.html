Aspidoglossum grandiflorum

Aspidoglossum grandiflorum, commonly known as the golden torch plant, is a member of the Asparagaceae family and is native to South Africa.

It is a succulent shrub characterized by its upright growth habit and striking, orange-yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant features fleshy, strap-shaped leaves that are typically gray-green in color, providing a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Aspidoglossum grandiflorum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ease of care in indoor environments.
