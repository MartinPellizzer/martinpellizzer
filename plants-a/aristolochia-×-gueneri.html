Aristolochia × gueneri

Aristolochia × gueneri, commonly known as the purple swallowwort, is a flowering plant in the family Aristolochiaceae.

It is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Aristolochia durangensis and Aristolochia mollis. The plant is characterized by its distinctive, pipe-shaped flowers that are typically purple or violet in color. It is native to Mexico and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

The leaves are heart-shaped and have a rough texture, while the plant produces small, berry-like fruits that contain the seeds.
