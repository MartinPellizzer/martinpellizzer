Anthyllis polycephala var. fontanesii

Anthyllis polycephala var. fontanesii, commonly known as the Fontanesii anthyllis, is a perennial flowering plant native to southern Europe.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense clusters of small, yellowish-white flowers. The plant produces trifoliate leaves and has a deep root system that helps it thrive in poor soils.

It is often found in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas.
