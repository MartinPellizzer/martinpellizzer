Anthurium rubidum

Anthurium rubidum, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a striking tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a reddish tinge. The plant produces showy spadices, which are brightly colored, often red or orange, surrounded by a modified leaf called a spathe. Anthurium rubidum thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

It is a popular ornamental plant due to its long-lasting, colorful inflorescences.
