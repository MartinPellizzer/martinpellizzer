Argyrophyllum lanceolatoellipticum

Argyrophyllum lanceolatoellipticum, commonly known as the lanceolate elliptic silverleaf.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the arid regions of western North America. The plant is characterized by its silver-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, reflective hairs. Its flowers are small and typically white or pale yellow, arranged in clusters.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to harsh, dry environments.
