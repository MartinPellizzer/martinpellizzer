Arctotis pectinata

Arctotis pectinata, commonly known as the silverleaf arctotis, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and vibrant, daisy-like flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant typically grows as a perennial shrub, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming period, which extends from spring to autumn.
