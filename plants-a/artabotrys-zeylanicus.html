Artabotrys zeylanicus

Artabotrys zeylanicus, commonly known as the sweet shower tree, is a tropical flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters, emitting a sweet, honey-like scent. The plant produces small, round, green fruits that turn red when mature, containing seeds surrounded by a fleshy pulp. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and elliptical, with a smooth texture and a dark green upper surface.

The tree is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine for its various therapeutic properties.
