Arachnothryx costaricensis

Arachnothryx costaricensis, commonly known as the Costa Rican spider tree, is a flowering plant native to Central America.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and distinctive spiny thorns. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by pod fruits containing several seeds. It thrives in tropical and subtropical regions, often found in lowland forests and along stream banks.

Due to its unique morphology and ecological role, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
