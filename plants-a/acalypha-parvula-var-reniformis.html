Acalypha parvula var. reniformis

Acalypha parvula var. reniformis, commonly known as the round-leaf copperleaf, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features small, round or heart-shaped leaves that are often brightly colored, ranging from red to orange. The plant produces clusters of tiny, pale yellow flowers that are followed by distinctive, papery bracts. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often used in ornamental gardening.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.
