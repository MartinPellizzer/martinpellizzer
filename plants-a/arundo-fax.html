Arundo fax

Arundo fax, commonly known as the giant reed, is a tall, perennial grass native to the Mediterranean region.

It features narrow, sword-shaped leaves that can grow up to 3 meters in height. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers in panicles during the summer months. It is often used in landscaping for erosion control and as a ornamental plant.

However, it can become invasive in certain environments if not managed properly.
