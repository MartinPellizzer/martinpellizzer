Arabis belmuntis

Arabis belmuntis, commonly known as the Belmunt's rockcress, is a perennial flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other cruciferous plants. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in alpine or subalpine environments. Its flowers are small, white or pale yellow, and appear in clusters, often in spring.
