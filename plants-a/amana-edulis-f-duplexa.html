Amana edulis f. duplexa

Amana edulis f. duplexa, commonly known as the double-flowered amana, is a unique cultivar of the amana plant characterized by its distinctive double blooms.

It belongs to the family Amaranthaceae and is native to tropical regions of South America. The plant features striking, showy flowers that are often used in ornamental gardening. Its foliage is dark green and slightly hairy, adding to its visual appeal.

This variety is valued for both its aesthetic qualities and its potential nutritional benefits.
