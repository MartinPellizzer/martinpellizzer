Acaena stricta

Acaena stricta, commonly known as the slender burr plant, is a perennial shrub native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the family Rosaceae and is characterized by its dense, spiny foliage and small, yellowish flowers. The plant produces bright red, hairy berries that are an important food source for native birds. Its growth habit is typically upright, with multiple stems forming a bushy appearance.
