Anthurium scherzerianum var. gandavense

Anthurium scherzerianum var. gandavense, commonly known as the Gandavense Philodendron, is a tropical flowering plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces striking spadix flowers enclosed within a vibrant, often red or orange spathe. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

This variety is popular among indoor gardeners for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
