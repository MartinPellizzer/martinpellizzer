Anisotes ukambanensis

Anisotes ukambanensis, commonly known as the African arrowhead, is a member of the Araceae family.

It is a herbaceous plant native to eastern Africa, particularly Tanzania and Kenya. The plant features arrow-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers surrounded by a spathe, typical of the Araceae family.

Anisotes ukambanensis is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage.
