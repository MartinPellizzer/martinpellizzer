Argyrolobium nitens

Argyrolobium nitens, commonly known as the silver globular pea, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its spherical, silver-green seed pods that contain several seeds. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and hardy nature, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
