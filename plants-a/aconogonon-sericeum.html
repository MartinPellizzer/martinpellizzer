Aconogonon sericeum

Aconogonon sericeum, commonly known as silverleaf aconogonon, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its soft, silvery foliage that gives it its common name. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in summer. It is typically found in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.

Aconogonon sericeum is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its attractive appearance and low maintenance requirements.
