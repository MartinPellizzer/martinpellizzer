Asparagus sinensis

Asparagus sinensis, commonly known as Chinese asparagus fern, is a perennial plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its arching, feather-like leaves that form a dense, fern-like appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are followed by bright red berries. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its foliage provides a lush, green texture that adds visual interest to gardens and indoor spaces.
