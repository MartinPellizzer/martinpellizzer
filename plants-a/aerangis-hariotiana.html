Aerangis hariotiana

Aerangis hariotiana, commonly known as the Hariot's orchid, is a species of orchid native to the mountainous regions of Borneo.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink with a yellowish lip. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are strap-like and arranged in a fan shape, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
