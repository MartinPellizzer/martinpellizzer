Aristida elongata

Aristida elongata, commonly known as slender dropseed, is a perennial grass species native to the United States and parts of Mexico.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences that are typically pale green to brown in color. The plant is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it suitable for xeriscaping and native plant gardens. Its fine texture and upright growth habit make it an attractive ornamental plant.

Aristida elongata plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various insects and small animals.
