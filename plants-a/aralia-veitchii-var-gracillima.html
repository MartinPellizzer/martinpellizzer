Aralia veitchii var. gracillima

Aralia veitchii var. gracillima, commonly known as the slender aralia, is a deciduous shrub native to the mountainous regions of China.

It features compound leaves that are finely divided, giving it a delicate, fern-like appearance. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers in umbel-like clusters, which are followed by spherical, red berries. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its graceful form and seasonal foliage changes.
