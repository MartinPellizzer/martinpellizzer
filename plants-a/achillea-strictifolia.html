Achillea strictifolia

Achillea strictifolia, commonly known as narrowleaf yarrow, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe and parts of western Asia.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a common sight in rocky or dry meadows.

Its aromatic foliage and long blooming period make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and ecological habitats.
