Afrosolen schimperi

Afrosolen schimperi, commonly known as the African starflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the highlands of Ethiopia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of numerous small florets. The plant features a basal rosette of narrow, gray-green leaves with hairy surfaces. Its flowering period occurs during the dry season, producing yellow-orange capitula that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Afrosolen schimperi is valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional uses in local medicine.
