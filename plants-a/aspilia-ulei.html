Aspilia ulei

Aspilia ulei, commonly known as the African flame flower, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking, bright orange to red flower heads. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs.

Aspilia ulei is adapted to arid environments and thrives in well-drained soils.
