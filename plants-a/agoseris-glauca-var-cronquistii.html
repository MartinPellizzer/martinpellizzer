Agoseris glauca var. cronquistii

Agoseris glauca var. cronquistii, commonly known as the Cronquist's blueweed, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and has blue to violet petals with yellow centers. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces small, herbaceous stems.

It is often found in open areas such as grasslands and disturbed soils.
