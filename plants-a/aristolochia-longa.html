Aristolochia longa

Aristolochia longa, commonly known as the long-pod Dutchman's pipe, is a climbing vine native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, pipe-shaped flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color. The plant produces heart-shaped leaves with a rough, hairy texture. Its fruit is a elongated, fleshy capsule that contains numerous small, dark seeds.

The plant is part of the Aristolochiaceae family and is known for its complex chemical composition.
