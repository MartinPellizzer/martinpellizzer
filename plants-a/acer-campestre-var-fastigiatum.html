Acer campestre var. fastigiatum

Acer campestre var. fastigiatum, commonly known as the columnar hedge maple, is a compact, upright shrub or small tree native to Europe.

It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath, turning red or orange in autumn. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in early spring, followed by red or orange samaras. It is often used as a decorative hedge or screens due to its dense growth habit and ornamental value.

This variety is relatively low-maintenance and tolerant of urban conditions.
