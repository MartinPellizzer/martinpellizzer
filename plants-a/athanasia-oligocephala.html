Athanasia oligocephala

Athanasia oligocephala, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies, sunflowers, and thistles. The plant is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and clusters of small, yellow flowers surrounded by white ray florets. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Athanasia oligocephala is also valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
