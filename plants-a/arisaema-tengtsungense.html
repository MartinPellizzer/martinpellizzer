Arisaema tengtsungense

Arisaema tengtsungense, commonly known as the Tengtsung arrowhead, is a member of the Araceae family.

It is native to China, specifically the Yunnan Province, and thrives in moist, shaded forest environments. The plant features a distinctive arrowhead-shaped leaf and produces a spadix surrounded by a spathe, typical of its genus. Its botanical characteristics include a rhizomatous root system and a short, stout stem.

Arisaema tengtsungense is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in local medicine.
