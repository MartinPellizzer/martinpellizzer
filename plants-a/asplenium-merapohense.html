Asplenium merapohense

Asplenium merapohense, commonly known as the Merapoh fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Aspleniaceae and is characterized by its compact, upright growth habit. The plant features lance-shaped, serrated leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Asplenium merapohense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant.
