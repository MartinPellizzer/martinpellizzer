Aspalathus dahlgrenii

Aspalathus dahlgrenii, commonly known as the Dalhgren's bushveld pea, is a small, evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It typically grows in the fynbos vegetation of the Western and Eastern Cape provinces. The plant features narrow, leathery leaves and produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It is adapted to nutrient-poor soils and requires well-drained conditions to thrive.

Aspalathus dahlgrenii is often found in rocky outcrops and along coastal areas.
