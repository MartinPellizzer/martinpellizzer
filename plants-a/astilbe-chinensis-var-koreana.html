Astilbe chinensis var. koreana

Astilbe chinensis var. koreana, commonly known as Korean astilbe, is a herbaceous perennial native to East Asia.

It features finely divided, feathery foliage that adds texture to garden landscapes. The plant produces panicles of pale pink to white flowers in late spring to early summer. It prefers partial to full shade and moist, well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for woodland gardens and shaded borders.
