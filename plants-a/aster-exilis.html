Aster exilis

Aster exilis, commonly known as the slender aster, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its delicate, slender stems and small, daisy-like flowers that bloom in the fall. The plant typically grows in open, sunny areas such as meadows and prairies. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often covered in fine hairs.

Aster exilis is a hardy perennial that thrives in well-drained soils and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
