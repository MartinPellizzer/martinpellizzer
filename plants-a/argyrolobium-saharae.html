Argyrolobium saharae

Argyrolobium saharae, commonly known as the Saharan silverlobelia, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of North Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and small, showy flowers. The plant is adapted to survive in harsh desert conditions, with deep root systems that help it access water from deeper soil layers. Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a vulnerable species.
