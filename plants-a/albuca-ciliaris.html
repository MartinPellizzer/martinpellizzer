Albuca ciliaris

Albuca ciliaris, commonly known as the Cape tulip, is a bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to yellow and orange. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery dusting. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Albuca ciliaris is known for its drought tolerance and ability to bloom reliably in suitable conditions.
