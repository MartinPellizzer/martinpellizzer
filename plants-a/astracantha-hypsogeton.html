Astracantha hypsogeton

Astracantha hypsogeton, commonly known as the mountain aster, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Africa.

It typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and is adapted to high-altitude environments. The plant features a basal rosette of narrow, silvery-green leaves that are covered in fine hairs. Its flowering stems bear clusters of small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in late summer.

Astracantha hypsogeton is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in rock gardens or alpine settings.
