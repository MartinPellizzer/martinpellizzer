Asplenium fieldingianum

Asplenium fieldingianum, commonly known as the fielding's spleenwort, is a small, terrestrial fern belonging to the family Aspleniaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped leaves that are typically less than 10 cm in length. The plant produces small, clustered spore cases known as soruses on the underside of its fronds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and rocky crevices.

Asplenium fieldingianum is often found growing on the ground or on rocks, preferring cool and humid conditions.
