Alternanthera vestita

Alternanthera vestita, commonly known as the velvetleaf alternanthera, is a striking aquatic plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its bright red or orange foliage, which becomes more intense under strong light conditions. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture, giving it its common name. It thrives in warm, shallow waters and is popular in aquariums for its vibrant coloration.

Alternanthera vestita is relatively easy to care for, making it a favored choice for both beginner and experienced aquarists.
