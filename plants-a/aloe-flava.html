Aloe flava

Aloe flava, commonly known as the yellow aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically yellowish-green in color, often with a waxy coating. The plant produces upright, branched flower stalks that bear yellow to orange flowers, which bloom in spring. Aloe flava is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its leaves to survive drought conditions.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for both ornamental and medicinal purposes.
