Anthurium losencuentrosense

Anthurium losencuentrosense, commonly known as the Los Encuentros arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to the humid lowland forests of Colombia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves with a deep green hue and prominent veins. The plant produces a spadix inflorescence surrounded by a colorful spathe, which varies in shade depending on the cultivar. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

Anthurium losencuentrosense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.
