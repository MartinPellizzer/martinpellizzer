Andruris khasiana

Andruris khasiana, commonly known as Khasi orchid, is a species of orchid native to the northeastern region of India.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in the undergrowth of forests.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
