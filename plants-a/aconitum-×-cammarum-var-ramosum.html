Aconitum × cammarum var. ramosum

Aconitum × cammarum var. ramosum, commonly known as the Alpine garden aconite, is a hybrid perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems that bear clusters of blue to purple flowers. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and is known for its ornamental value in gardens. It thrives in cool, moist environments and prefers well-drained soil.

Due to its toxic properties, it should be handled with care.
