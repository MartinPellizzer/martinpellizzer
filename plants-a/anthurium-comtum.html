Anthurium comtum

Anthurium comtum, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are usually dark green with a reddish or purplish tinge. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-shaped inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, which can range from white to red. It thrives in humid, warm environments with bright, indirect light.

Anthurium comtum is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
