Anthodon grandiflorum

Anthodon grandiflorum, commonly known as the large-flowered anthodon, is a herbaceous perennial native to South America.

It features showy, violet-blue flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with a hairy texture, and it thrives in moist, well-drained soils. It is often found in wetlands and along streambanks, where it plays a role in stabilizing soil and supporting local biodiversity.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardeners interested in native plants.
