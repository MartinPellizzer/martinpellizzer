Anithista flava

Anithista flava, commonly known as the yellow sandwort, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of North America.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically reaches a height of 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in the spring. Its leaves are fleshy and succulent, adapted to conserve water in dry environments.

Anithista flava is often found in sandy or gravelly soils, thriving in well-drained conditions.
