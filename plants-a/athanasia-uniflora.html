Athanasia uniflora

Athanasia uniflora, commonly known as the single-flower immortality, is a striking plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its unique, solitary yellow flower that appears at the top of a tall, sparsely branched stem. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a hardy addition to arid gardens.

Due to its resilience and ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a decorative plant in dry climates.
