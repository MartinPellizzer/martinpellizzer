Argyranthemum lemsii

Argyranthemum lemsii, commonly known as the silverleaf geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its silvery-gray leaves and showy, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows as a low shrub, reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters. Its flowers, which range in color from white to pink and purple, bloom throughout the growing season.

Argyranthemum lemsii is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its attractive foliage and long-lasting blooms.
