Alternanthera nahui

Alternanthera nahui, commonly known as Mexican flamebeard, is a flowering plant native to Central America.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy inflorescences. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are often bright red or orange, adding visual interest to gardens. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil.

Alternanthera nahui is often cultivated for its ornamental value and can be grown as an annual in temperate climates.
