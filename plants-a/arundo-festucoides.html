Arundo festucoides

Arundo festucoides, commonly known as narrowleaf reed, is a grass-like plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like blades and grows in dense clumps. The plant typically reaches heights of 1 to 2 meters and has a fibrous root system. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found near water bodies.

Its botanical features make it adaptable to various environmental conditions.
