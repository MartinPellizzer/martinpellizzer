Anthurium carrasquillanum

Anthurium carrasquillanum, commonly known as the Carrasquillanum anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces striking spadix flowers surrounded by a vibrant spathe, which can range in color from red to orange. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect sunlight for optimal growth.

Anthurium carrasquillanum is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and air-purifying properties.
