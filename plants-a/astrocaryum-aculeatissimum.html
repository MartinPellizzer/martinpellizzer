Astrocaryum aculeatissimum

Astrocaryum aculeatissimum, commonly known as the spiny palm, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and a crown of long, narrow leaves. The plant produces yellow or orange fruits that are edible and often used in local cuisines. Its unique spines and robust growth make it well adapted to its natural habitat.

The species is also valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological importance in its native ecosystems.
