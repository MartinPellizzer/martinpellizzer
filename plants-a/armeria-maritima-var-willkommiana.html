Armeria maritima var. willkommiana

Armeria maritima var. willkommiana, commonly known as the coastal thrift, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of Europe and North America.

It is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit and produces clusters of pink or white flowers in early spring. The plant thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is highly tolerant of salt spray and strong winds. Its leaves are linear and evergreen, providing year-round interest in coastal landscapes.

It is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to stabilize dunes.
