Astracantha elbistanica

Astracantha elbistanica, commonly known as the Elbistan thornvetch, is a flowering plant native to Turkey.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its spiny stems and small, pea-like flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions.

Its seeds are enclosed in pods and are often dispersed by wind, contributing to its survival in its native habitat.
