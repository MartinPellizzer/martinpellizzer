Alsine austriaca var. brachycarpa

Alsine austriaca var. brachycarpa, commonly known as the Austrian sandwort, is a low-growing, annual plant native to central Europe.

It features small, pale green to blue-green leaves that are typically opposite and covered in fine hairs. The plant produces tiny, clustered flowers that are usually pink or purple, blooming in early summer. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in disturbed areas or along roadsides.

Its compact form and drought tolerance make it a hardy species adapted to harsh environments.
