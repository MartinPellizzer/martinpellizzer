Acacia diptera var. eriocarpa

Acacia diptera var. eriocarpa, commonly known as the silky acacia, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant features small, yellow, pea-like flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are often covered in a fine, silky hair, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It is well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a resilient species in dry regions.
