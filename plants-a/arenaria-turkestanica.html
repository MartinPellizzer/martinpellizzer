Arenaria turkestanica

Arenaria turkestanica, commonly known as the Turkestan sandwort, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It typically grows in arid and semi-arid environments, such as sandy soils and rocky areas. The plant has small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink, often blooming in early spring. Its leaves are usually fleshy and succulent, adapted to conserve water in dry conditions.

Arenaria turkestanica is often found in steppes and along the edges of deserts, where it plays a role in stabilizing soil and supporting local biodiversity.
