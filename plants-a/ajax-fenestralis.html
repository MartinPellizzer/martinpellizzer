Ajax fenestralis

Ajax fenestralis, commonly known as the fenestrated ajax, is a unique plant characterized by its distinctive fenestrations or leaf openings.

It belongs to the family Araliaceae and is native to the tropical regions of South America. The plant features compound leaves with a striking, lace-like appearance due to its numerous leaflets and perforations. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and botanical collections.
