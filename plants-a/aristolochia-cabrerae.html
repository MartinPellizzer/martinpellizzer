Aristolochia cabrerae

Aristolochia cabrerae, commonly known as the Cabrera's Dutchman's pipe, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Aristolochiaceae family and is characterized by its unique, tubular, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically greenish-yellow with a distinctive hooded shape. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in shaded, moist environments.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
