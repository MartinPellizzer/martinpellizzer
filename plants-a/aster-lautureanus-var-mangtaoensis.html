Aster lautureanus var. mangtaoensis

Aster lautureanus var. mangtaoensis, commonly known as the Mangtao aster, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the sunflower family (Asteraceae) and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that range in color from white to pale purple. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories or along stream banks. Its leaves are lance-shaped with serrated edges, and it produces small, fluffy seeds that are dispersed by wind.

This variant is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting pollinators.
