Alternanthera nesiotes

Alternanthera nesiotes, commonly known as the red-leaf alternanthera, is a striking aquatic plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red or pink foliage, which becomes more pronounced under strong light conditions. This plant thrives in shallow, slow-moving freshwater environments and is often used in aquariums for its aesthetic appeal. Its leaves are opposite and ovate, with a smooth texture and a tendency to float on the water surface.

Alternanthera nesiotes is also known for its rapid growth rate, making it a popular choice for pond and aquarium landscaping.
