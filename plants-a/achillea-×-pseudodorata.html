Achillea × pseudodorata

Achillea × pseudodorata, commonly known as the hybrid yarrow, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its finely divided, feathery leaves and flat, composite flower heads that range in color from white to pale yellow. This plant is native to the western United States and thrives in well-drained soils in full sun. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.

Achillea × pseudodorata is also valued for its hardiness and low maintenance requirements in garden settings.
