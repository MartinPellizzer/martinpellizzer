Anarthria prolifera

Anarthria prolifera, commonly known as the creeping broomweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its prostrate growth habit and small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant produces small, oval seeds that are dispersed by wind. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed areas.

Anarthria prolifera is a member of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.
