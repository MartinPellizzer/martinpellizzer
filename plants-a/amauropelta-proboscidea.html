Amauropelta proboscidea

Amauropelta proboscidea, commonly known as the proboscideous shield fern, is a unique species of fern characterized by its distinctive, shield-shaped fronds.

The plant features a short, creeping rhizome with dense, overlapping leaves that form a protective, umbrella-like structure. Its reproductive structures are concealed within the fronds, and it thrives in moist, shaded environments.
