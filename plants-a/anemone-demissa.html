Anemone demissa

Anemone demissa, commonly known as the small anemone, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features delicate, white to pinkish flowers with five petals, often accompanied by yellow stamens. Its leaves are heart-shaped and have a hairy texture.

Anemone demissa is valued for its ornamental appeal and plays a role in supporting local pollinators.
