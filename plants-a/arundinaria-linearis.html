Arundinaria linearis

Arundinaria linearis, commonly known as the bamboo grass or slender bamboo, is a member of the grass family Poaceae.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks that can reach heights of up to 6 meters. The plant features narrow, linear leaves that are typically green and have a smooth texture. It is native to eastern Asia and is often found in moist, shaded environments.

Arundinaria linearis is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
