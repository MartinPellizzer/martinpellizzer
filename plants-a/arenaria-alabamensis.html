Arenaria alabamensis

Arenaria alabamensis, commonly known as Alabama sandwort, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically thrives in sandy or gravelly soils. The plant produces small, white or pinkish flowers during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are usually fleshy and succulent, adapted to conserve moisture in arid conditions.

It is often found in open, disturbed areas such as roadsides and dunes.
