Alternanthera denticulata var. major

Alternanthera denticulata var. major, commonly known as the red-leaf alternanthera, is a striking aquatic plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red or pink foliage, which intensifies under strong lighting and nutrient-rich conditions. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often adorned with intricate vein patterns. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, making it a popular choice for water gardens and pond landscapes.

Its ornamental value is further enhanced by its ability to produce small, delicate white flowers.
