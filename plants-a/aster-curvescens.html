Aster curvescens

Aster curvescens, commonly known as the curly aster, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, curling leaves and produces showy purple or pink flowers in the fall. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland habitats.

Aster curvescens is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits to pollinators.
