Astrantia major var. minor

Astrantia major var. minor, commonly known as lesser masterwort, is a herbaceous perennial native to Europe.

It features upright, branching stems that bear clusters of small, white to pale pink flowers. The plant has palmate leaves with five to seven lobes, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers moist, well-drained soil.

This species is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
