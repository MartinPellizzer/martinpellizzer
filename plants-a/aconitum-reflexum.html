Aconitum reflexum

Aconitum reflexum, commonly known as the reflexed monkshood, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It features striking, hood-shaped blue to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has dark green, doubly serrated leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It prefers moist, well-drained soils in shaded to partially shaded environments.

Aconitum reflexum is part of the Ranunculaceae family and contains toxic alkaloids, making it potentially dangerous if ingested.
