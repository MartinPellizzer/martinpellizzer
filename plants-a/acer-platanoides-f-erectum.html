Acer platanoides f. erectum

Acer platanoides f. erectum, commonly known as the European maple or Norway maple, is a cultivar of the Norway maple (Acer platanoides).

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and is often used as a ornamental tree in gardens and urban landscapes. The leaves are typically heart-shaped with a serrated margin, and they turn vibrant shades of yellow and red in the fall. It produces small, red flowers in early spring followed by winged seeds.

This cultivar is valued for its dense foliage and adaptability to a variety of soil conditions.
