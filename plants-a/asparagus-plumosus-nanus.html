Asparagus plumosus nanus

Asparagus plumosus nanus, commonly known as the silver feather asparagus, is a compact, low-growing perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features delicate, fern-like foliage with silvery-white undersides, giving it a striking, otherworldly appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous green flowers that give way to fluffy, white seed heads. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Due to its slow growth and ornamental appeal, it is often cultivated as a decorative plant in arid and Mediterranean climates.
