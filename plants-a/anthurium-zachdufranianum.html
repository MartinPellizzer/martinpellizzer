Anthurium zachdufranianum

Anthurium zachdufranianum, commonly known as the Red-veined Anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves with deep red veins against a dark green background. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-shaped inflorescences that are often surrounded by colorful spatulate bracts. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Anthurium zachdufranianum is a popular ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
