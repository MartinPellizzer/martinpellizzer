Astilbe thunbergii var. taquetii

Astilbe thunbergii var. taquetii, commonly known as the Taquet's feather fern, is a compact, herbaceous perennial native to Japan.

It features delicate, plume-like inflorescences that bloom in shades of pink to white, adding a subtle elegance to gardens. The plant has lance-shaped, dark green leaves that are arranged in a dense, upright cluster. It thrives in moist, shady conditions and prefers well-drained, humus-rich soil.

This variety is valued for its ornamental foliage and modest floral display, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens.
