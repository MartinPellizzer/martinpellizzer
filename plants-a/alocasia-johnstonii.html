Alocasia johnstonii

Alocasia johnstonii, commonly known as the Johnston's arrow leaf, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features large, heart-shaped leaves with a deep green color and a velvety texture. The plant is characterized by its unique arrow-like leaf shape and often has a reddish tint on the underside of its foliage. Alocasia johnstonii thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

It is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
