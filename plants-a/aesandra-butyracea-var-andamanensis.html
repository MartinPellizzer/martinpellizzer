Aesandra butyracea var. andamanensis

Aesandra butyracea var. andamanensis, commonly known as the Andaman butter tree, is a tropical plant native to the Andaman Islands.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and elliptical leaves that are glossy on the upper surface. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by edible, fleshy fruits that are rich in oils and nutrients. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Due to its unique botanical features and ecological importance, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
