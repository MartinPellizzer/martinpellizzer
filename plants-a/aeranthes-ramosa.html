Aeranthes ramosa

Aeranthes ramosa, commonly known as the ramose orchid, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, branching inflorescence. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and green, often with intricate patterns. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil.

Aeranthes ramosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
