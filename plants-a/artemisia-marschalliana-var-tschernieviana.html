Artemisia marschalliana var. tschernieviana

Artemisia marschalliana var. tschernieviana, commonly known as the Tschernievena wormwood, is a perennial herb native to Central Asia.

It features gray-green, aromatic foliage with narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are densely covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flower heads that are clustered in compact, spherical inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in arid or semi-arid environments.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
