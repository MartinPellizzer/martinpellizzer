Angelica anomala var. kawakamii

Angelica anomala var. kawakamii, commonly known as Kawakamii angelica, is a perennial herb native to Japan.

It belongs to the family Apiaceae, which includes many aromatic and medicinal plants. The plant features finely divided, fern-like leaves and produces small, greenish-yellow flowers in umbels. It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and streamside areas.
