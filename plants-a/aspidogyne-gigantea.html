Aspidogyne gigantea

Aspidogyne gigantea, commonly known as the giant aspidogyne, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, waxy leaves that can grow up to several feet in length. The plant produces clusters of small, greenish-white flowers that are often overlooked due to their modest appearance. Its unique morphology makes it a subject of interest in botanical studies and horticulture.

Aspidogyne gigantea is typically found in humid, shaded environments within dense tropical forests.
