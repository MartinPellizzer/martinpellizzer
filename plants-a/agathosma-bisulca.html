Agathosma bisulca

Agathosma bisulca, commonly known as African geranium, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features small, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-gray in color, adapted to store water in arid environments. The plant produces clusters of small, pink or purple flowers during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Due to its drought tolerance and ornamental value, it is a popular choice for low-maintenance landscaping.
