Alchornea villosa var. latisepala

Alchornea villosa var. latisepala, commonly known as the broad-sepaled alchornea, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa and the Americas.

It features opposite, ovate leaves that are typically covered in dense, soft pubescence. The flowers are small, clustered in inflorescences, and have showy, broad sepals that give the plant its varietal name. It produces small, spherical fruits that contain several seeds.

This plant is often found in moist, shaded environments and is valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional uses.
