Acacia cincinnata

Acacia cincinnata, commonly known as the slender wattle, is a shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its feathery, yellow flowers that bloom in winter and spring. The plant has small, narrow leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy regions.

Acacia cincinnata is also valued for its hardy nature and ability to withstand drought conditions.
