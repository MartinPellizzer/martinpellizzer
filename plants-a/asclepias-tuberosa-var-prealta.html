Asclepias tuberosa var. prealta

Asclepias tuberosa var. prealta, commonly known as the Mexican butterflyweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Mexico.

It features showy, bright orange to red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs. It is a member of the milkweed family (Asclepiadaceae) and is known for its milky sap.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and its role in supporting pollinators like butterflies.
