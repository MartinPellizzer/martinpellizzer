Alyssum oxycarpum

Alyssum oxycarpum, commonly known as sharpseed alyssum, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing annual or biennial herb with small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Alyssum oxycarpum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
