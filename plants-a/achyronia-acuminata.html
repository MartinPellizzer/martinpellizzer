Achyronia acuminata

Achyronia acuminata, commonly known as the slender lily, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Orchidaceae, which includes over 20,000 species of orchids. The plant is characterized by its tall, slender stem and showy, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. Its leaves are linear and arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem.

Achyronia acuminata is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a cut flower.
