Astracantha antabica

Astracantha antabica, commonly known as the Antabikascent, is a flowering plant native to the Arabian Peninsula.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features spiny, cylindrical stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, arranged in dense clusters.

Astracantha antabica is adapted to arid environments, showcasing resilience to drought and high temperatures.
