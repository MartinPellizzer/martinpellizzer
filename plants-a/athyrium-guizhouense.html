Athyrium guizhouense

Athyrium guizhouense, commonly known as Guizhou shield fern, is a species of fern native to China.

It belongs to the Athyriaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, shield-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in mountainous regions. Its leaves are bi- or tri-pinnate, with a delicate, lace-like appearance.

Athyrium guizhouense is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
