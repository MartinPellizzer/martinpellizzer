Amaranthus × turicensis

Amaranthus × turicensis, commonly known as the red leaf amaranth, is a hybrid species native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, feather-like leaves that range in color from red to purple, making it a striking ornamental plant. The plant produces small, greenish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, preferring warm climates.

Its rapid growth and ability to tolerate drought make it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
