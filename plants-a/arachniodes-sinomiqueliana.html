Arachniodes sinomiqueliana

Arachniodes sinomiqueliana, commonly known as the Chinese spider plant, is a species of evergreen fern native to China.

It features gracefully arching, dark green fronds that are covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous spore-bearing structures called sori on the underside of its leaves. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often used in ornamental horticulture for its elegant foliage.

Its slow growth rate makes it suitable for indoor cultivation and rock gardens.
