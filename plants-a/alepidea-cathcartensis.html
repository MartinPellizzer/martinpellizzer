Alepidea cathcartensis

Alepidea cathcartensis, commonly known as the Natal umbrella tree, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

The plant features compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in summer. Alepidea cathcartensis is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, contributing to soil fertility.
