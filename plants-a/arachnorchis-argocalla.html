Arachnorchis argocalla

Arachnorchis argocalla, commonly known as the golden spider orchid, is an epiphytic orchid native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Orchidaceae and is characterized by its unique, spider-like floral structure. The plant produces bright yellow flowers that are highly attractive to pollinators such as bees and wasps. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining substrates.

Arachnorchis argocalla is known for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in specialized horticultural settings.
