Asteropyrum cavaleriei

Asteropyrum cavaleriei, commonly known as the Chinese aster, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers. The plant is characterized by its showy, purple or pink flower heads that resemble daisies. It typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in temperate climates.

Asteropyrum cavaleriei is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
