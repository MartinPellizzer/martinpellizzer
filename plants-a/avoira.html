Avoira

Avoira, commonly known as the sacred lotus, is a water-loving plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that bloom on long, erect stems above the water surface. The plant has broad, heart-shaped leaves that float on the water or are submerged beneath it. Avoira is often cultivated for its ornamental value and holds cultural significance in many Asian countries.

Its botanical name, *Nelumbo nucifera*, reflects its historical and symbolic importance in various traditions.
