Aniseia martinicensis var. nitens

Aniseia martinicensis var. nitens, commonly known as the brightleaf hibiscus, is a tropical shrub native to the Caribbean.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The leaves are broad, heart-shaped, and often have a glossy appearance. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
