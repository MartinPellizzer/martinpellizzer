Adiantum mindanaoense

Adiantum mindanaoense, commonly known as the Mindanao maidenhair fern, is a species of fern native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the family Adiantaceae and is characterized by its delicate, bipinnate fronds that are typically dark green with a metallic sheen. The plant thrives in shaded, moist environments, often found in forest understories. Its rhizomes are creeping and produce roots that help anchor it to rocky or soil substrates.

Adiantum mindanaoense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
