Asplenium salwinense

Asplenium salwinense, commonly known as the Salwin water fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Aspleniaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds that are typically green and glossy. This plant is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in regions like Myanmar, Thailand, and Vietnam. Asplenium salwinense thrives in moist, shaded environments, often growing on rocks or in forest undergrowth.

Its botanical features make it a subject of interest for both horticultural and scientific study.
