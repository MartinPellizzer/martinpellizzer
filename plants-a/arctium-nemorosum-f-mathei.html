Arctium nemorosum f. mathei

Arctium nemorosum f. mathei, commonly known as the Mathe's burdock, is a variant of the common burdock native to Europe.

It is a biennial herbaceous plant characterized by its tall, hairy stems and large, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces spiny, spherical seed heads that are adapted for wind dispersal. Its flowers are yellow and clustered in umbels, typical of the Asteraceae family.

The species is often found in disturbed soils and open habitats, showcasing its resilience and adaptability.
