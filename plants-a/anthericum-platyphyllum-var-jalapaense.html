Anthericum platyphyllum var. jalapaense

Anthericum platyphyllum var. jalapaense, commonly known as the Jalapa flat-leaf arum, is a tropical plant native to Central America.

It is characterized by its broad, arrow-shaped leaves and striking, spadix-bearing inflorescence. The plant produces a fragrant, white to green spathe that surrounds the spadix, which is typical of the Araceae family. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in humid tropical forests.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
