Ajax bicolor var. breviflos

Ajax bicolor var. breviflos, commonly known as the short-flowered ajax, is a member of the Asteraceae family.

It is native to the southern United States, particularly Texas and New Mexico. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered yellow flowers. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, often found in open, rocky habitats.

Its botanical characteristics make it a distinctive and resilient wildflower.
