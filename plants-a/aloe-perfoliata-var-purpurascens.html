Aloe perfoliata var. purpurascens

Aloe perfoliata var. purpurascens, commonly known as the purple-flowered aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, gray-green leaves that are often tinged with purple, especially at the base. The plant produces striking, upward-facing inflorescences with bright orange or yellow flowers. Its leaves have a waxy coating that helps reduce water loss, making it well adapted to arid environments.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ease of care in dry climates.
