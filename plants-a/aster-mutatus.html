Aster mutatus

Aster mutatus, commonly known as the Variable Aster, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands. It features showy purple to blue flowers that bloom in the summer months.

Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinct appearance.
