Adenostylis violascens

Adenostylis violascens, commonly known as the violet-flowered grass.

It is a perennial herb native to New Zealand, characterized by its tufted growth habit and slender, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, violet or purple flowers arranged in dense spikelets, which are typically borne on long, slender stalks. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forest margins or along stream banks.

Adenostylis violascens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
