Aloe eylesii

Aloe eylesii, commonly known as the Eyles' aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asphodelaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-shaped arrangement of fleshy, sword-like leaves. The leaves are typically green with a bluish tint and have a waxy surface to reduce water loss. It produces clusters of yellowish-green flowers during the summer months.

This plant is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
