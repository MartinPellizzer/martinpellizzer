Alocasia lowii

Alocasia lowii, commonly known as the Black Velvet Alocasia, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark, velvety leaves that range from deep green to nearly black in color. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit and produces small, pale green flowers that are not typically visible above the foliage. Alocasia lowii thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor and greenhouse cultivation.
