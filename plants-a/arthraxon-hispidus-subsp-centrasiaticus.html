Arthraxon hispidus subsp. centrasiaticus

Arthraxon hispidus subsp.

centrasiaticus, commonly known as the Central Asian rough grass, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central Asia. It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its tufted growth habit and hairy stems.
