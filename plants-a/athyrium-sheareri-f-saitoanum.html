Athyrium sheareri f. saitoanum

Athyrium sheareri f. saitoanum, commonly known as the Saito's Japanese shield fern, is a fern species native to Japan.

It belongs to the Dryopteridaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, shield-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its fronds are often divided into segments with a serrated edge, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

This fern is valued in horticulture for its elegant appearance and adaptability to various soil types.
