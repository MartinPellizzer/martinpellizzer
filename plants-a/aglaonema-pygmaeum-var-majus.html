Aglaonema pygmaeum var. majus

Aglaonema pygmaeum var. majus, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen or the prayer plant, is a compact, ornamental foliage plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features striking, variegated leaves with a mix of green, white, and pink hues, often with a waxy texture. The plant thrives in low-light conditions and prefers well-draining soil, making it ideal for indoor cultivation. It is known for its ability to purify the air and is often used as a decorative houseplant.

Its foliage remains vibrant throughout the year, requiring minimal maintenance.
