Androsaemum grandifolium

Androsaemum grandifolium, commonly known as the large-leaved angel's trumpet, is a striking plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to yellow and orange. The plant has thick, waxy leaves that are typically broad and heart-shaped, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in warm, sunny environments and prefers well-drained soil.

Due to its toxic properties, it should be handled with care and kept away from pets and children.
