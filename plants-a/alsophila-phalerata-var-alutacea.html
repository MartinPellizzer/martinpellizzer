Alsophila phalerata var. alutacea

Alsophila phalerata var. alutacea, commonly known as the narrow-leaf tree fern, is a variant of the tree fern species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features a tall, slender trunk with a crown of feathery fronds that give it a distinctive, elegant appearance. The plant has a slow growth rate and thrives in humid, shaded environments with well-drained soil. Its fronds are typically dark green and have a narrow, linear shape, contributing to its ornamental value.

This variety is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal in botanical gardens and as a landscape plant.
