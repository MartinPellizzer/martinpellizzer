Agrostis volkensii

Agrostis volkensii, commonly known as Volkens' bentgrass, is a perennial grass species native to East Africa.

It typically grows in open, grassy areas and is often found in mountainous regions. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spikelet-based flowers. It is well adapted to dry and semi-arid conditions.

Its botanical characteristics make it an important component of local ecosystems and a subject of interest in botanical studies.
