Achimenes haageana

Achimenes haageana, commonly known as the showy achimenes, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, orange, and yellow. The plant has heart-shaped, velvety leaves that are typically dark green. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Achimenes haageana is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its vibrant blooms and ease of care.
