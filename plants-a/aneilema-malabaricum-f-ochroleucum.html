Aneilema malabaricum f. ochroleucum

Aneilema malabaricum f. ochroleucum, commonly known as the white-leafed aneilema, is a variant of the Aniseleaf plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its striking white to pale green foliage, which contrasts with the typical green leaves of the species. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its flowers are small and clustered, often appearing in shades of yellow or green, and it thrives in moist, shaded environments.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and is often used in ornamental gardens and as a houseplant.
