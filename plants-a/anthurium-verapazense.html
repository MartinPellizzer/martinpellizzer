Anthurium verapazense

Anthurium verapazense, commonly known as the Verapaz arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to Central America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-like inflorescences that are often surrounded by a colorful spathe. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium verapazense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated as a houseplant.
