Acrorumohra yoroi

Acrorumohra yoroi, commonly known as the golden spire orchid, is a rare and striking member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stem and clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring. Native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia, this plant thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its unique floral structure and vivid coloration make it a subject of interest for botanists and horticulturists.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered endangered and requires conservation efforts to ensure its survival.
