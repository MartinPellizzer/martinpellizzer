Aster lateriflorus

Aster lateriflorus, commonly known as the narrow-flowered aster, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and opposite leaf arrangement. The plant features slender, upright stems covered with grayish-green, hairy leaves. Its flowers are typically blue to purple, with ray florets that bloom in late summer to early fall.

Aster lateriflorus is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes.
