Aster amoenus

Aster amoenus, commonly known as the Chinese aster, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that come in a variety of colors, including pink, red, and white. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are usually hairy as well.

Aster amoenus is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a cut flower.
