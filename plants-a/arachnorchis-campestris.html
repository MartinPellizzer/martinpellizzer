Arachnorchis campestris

Arachnorchis campestris, commonly known as the field spider-orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to southeastern Australia.

It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and heathlands. The plant features a slender, erect stem with a single, showy flower that is usually pale yellow to greenish-white in color. Its flowers have a distinctive spider-like appearance, giving it its common name.

Arachnorchis campestris is a perennial herb that blooms during the autumn season.
