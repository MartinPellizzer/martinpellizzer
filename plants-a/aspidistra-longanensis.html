Aspidistra longanensis

Aspidistra longanensis, commonly known as the longan leaf aspidistra, is a perennial evergreen plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its dark green, leathery, lance-shaped leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color, followed by the development of berries. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its tolerance of low light and ability to thrive in humid conditions.

Aspidistra longanensis is valued for its ornamental foliage and air-purifying qualities.
