Arisaema inkiangense

Arisaema inkiangense, commonly known as the Yunnan arrowhead, is a striking tropical plant native to China.

It is characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves and distinctive, spadix-like inflorescence. The plant produces a single, elongated flower spike surrounded by a modified leaf called a spathe. Arisaema inkiangense thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Its unique morphology and ornamental value make it a popular choice for botanical gardens and conservation efforts.
