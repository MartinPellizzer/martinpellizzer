Achimenes bodmeri

Achimenes bodmeri, commonly known as the golden trumpet vine, is a tropical flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from orange to red, often with a yellow throat. The plant produces showy, pendulous blooms that attract pollinators such as hummingbirds and bees. Its leaves are heart-shaped and glossy, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Achimenes bodmeri thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
