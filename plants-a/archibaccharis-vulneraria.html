Archibaccharis vulneraria

Archibaccharis vulneraria, commonly known as the wounded bough.

It is a shrub native to the southeastern United States, particularly Florida and the Caribbean. The plant is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, white or pink flowers. It is often found in wetland environments, such as swamps and marshes.

The species is noted for its ability to thrive in moist, acidic soils and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
