Agathosma melaleucoides

Agathosma melaleucoides, commonly known as silver leaf agathosma, is a South African plant belonging to the Rutaceae family.

It is characterized by its aromatic, silver-gray foliage and small, white to yellowish flowers. The plant is often found in rocky, well-drained soils in the Western and Eastern Cape provinces. It is valued for its essential oils, which are used in perfumery and aromatherapy.

Due to habitat loss and overharvesting, it is also considered a threatened species.
