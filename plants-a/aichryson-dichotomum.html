Aichryson dichotomum

Aichryson dichotomum, commonly known as the dichotomous aichryson, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, shrub-like growth with dense, fleshy leaves that are typically gray-green in color. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the warmer months, adding a delicate touch to its appearance. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
