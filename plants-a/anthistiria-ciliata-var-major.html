Anthistiria ciliata var. major

Anthistiria ciliata var. major, commonly known as the woolly antelope's foot, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, hairy foliage that gives it a woolly appearance, and its leaves are typically gray-green in color. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
