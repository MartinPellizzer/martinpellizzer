Arthrosolen anthylloides

Arthrosolen anthylloides, commonly known as the silverleaf daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features silvery-gray foliage and produces showy yellow flowers with white ray florets. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Arthrosolen anthylloides is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
