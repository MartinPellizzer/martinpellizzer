Aristolochia jinshanensis

Aristolochia jinshanensis, commonly known as the Jinshan birthwort, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Aristolochiaceae family and is characterized by its unique, tubular, and often fragrant flowers. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces small, greenish-yellow fruits. It is typically found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
