Arabis hookeri

Arabis hookeri, commonly known as Hooker's rockcress, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other well-known flowering plants. The plant is characterized by its low-growing habit, with basal leaves and small, white or pinkish flowers that bloom in early spring.
