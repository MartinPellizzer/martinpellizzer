Arnoglossum lanceolatum var. ramosum

Arnoglossum lanceolatum var. ramosum, commonly known as the branched arrowhead, is a perennial herb native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and opposite leaf arrangement. The plant features lanceolate leaves with a distinctive serrated margin and produces yellow flowers in the summer. It thrives in moist, wooded habitats and is often found in shaded or partially shaded environments.

Its botanical features make it a notable species for ecological and botanical study.
