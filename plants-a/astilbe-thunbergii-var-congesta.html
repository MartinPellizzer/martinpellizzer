Astilbe thunbergii var. congesta

Astilbe thunbergii var. congesta, commonly known as the Japanese feather reed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Japan.

It features upright, branched stems that bear dense clusters of small, lace-like flowers in shades of white, pink, or red. The foliage consists of dark green, serrated leaves that add visual interest throughout the growing season. It thrives in partial to full shade and moist, well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in challenging garden conditions.
