Astronidium fraternum

Astronidium fraternum, commonly known as the pink star cactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its short, branching stems covered in dense, pale green spines. The plant produces vibrant pink to red flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a decorative plant in arid gardens.
