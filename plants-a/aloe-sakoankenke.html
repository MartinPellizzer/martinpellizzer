Aloe sakoankenke

Aloe sakoankenke, commonly known as the Sakoankenke aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asphodelaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are thick and fleshy. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with small, yellowish-green flowers. Its leaves contain a gel-like substance that is used in traditional medicine and skincare.

Aloe sakoankenke is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water to thrive.
