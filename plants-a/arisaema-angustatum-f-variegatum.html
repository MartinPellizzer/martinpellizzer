Arisaema angustatum f. variegatum

Arisaema angustatum f. variegatum, commonly known as the variegated narrowleaf arrowroot, is a striking ornamental plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features a distinctive, umbrella-shaped inflorescence with spadices that are often brightly colored, typically in shades of yellow or orange. The plant has narrow, arrowhead-shaped leaves that are usually variegated with white or cream markings. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal in gardens.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for tropical and subtropical landscapes.
