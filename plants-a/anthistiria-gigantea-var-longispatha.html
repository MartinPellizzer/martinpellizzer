Anthistiria gigantea var. longispatha

Anthistiria gigantea var. longispatha, commonly known as the long-spurred anthistiria, is a striking member of the Apocynaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically display a combination of red, orange, and yellow hues. This variant is native to certain regions of South America and thrives in tropical and subtropical climates.
