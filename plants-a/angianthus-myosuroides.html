Angianthus myosuroides

Angianthus myosuroides, commonly known as the yellow sand verbena, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

The plant has slender, branching stems and small, narrow leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Angianthus myosuroides is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
