Alchemilla grisebachiana

Alchemilla grisebachiana, commonly known as the gray alchemilla, is a perennial plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers in early summer, which are clustered in dense inflorescences.

Alchemilla grisebachiana is often found in rocky or gravelly soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.
