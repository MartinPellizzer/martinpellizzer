Acidanthera brachystachys

Acidanthera brachystachys, commonly known as the dwarf spider orchid, is a small, terrestrial orchid native to southern Africa.

It features delicate, spider-like flowers that are typically white or pale pink, with a distinctive, narrow labellum. The plant grows from a rhizomatous root system and produces compact inflorescences with few to several flowers. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
