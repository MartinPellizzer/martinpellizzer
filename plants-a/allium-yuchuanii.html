Allium yuchuanii

Allium yuchuanii, commonly known as the Yuchuan garlic, is a bulbous plant native to China.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, often purple or red flowers. The plant produces small, clustered bulbs that are used in traditional Chinese medicine. Its leaves are typically grass-like and can grow up to several feet in height.

Allium yuchuanii is valued for its medicinal properties and is also cultivated for ornamental purposes.
