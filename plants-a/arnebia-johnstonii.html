Arnebia johnstonii

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, particularly found in countries like Kazakhstan and Uzbekistan. Arnebia johnstonii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
