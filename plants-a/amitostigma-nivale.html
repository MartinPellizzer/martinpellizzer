Amitostigma nivale

Amitostigma nivale, commonly known as the snow lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of North America.

It is characterized by its delicate, white, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils at high elevations. Its leaves are basal, grass-like, and often covered in a fine, silvery down.

Amitostigma nivale is a low-growing plant that thrives in cold climates and is often found in alpine and subalpine habitats.
