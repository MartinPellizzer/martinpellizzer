Aphelandra chaponensis

Aphelandra chaponensis, commonly known as the Chapon's flame plant, is a tropical shrub native to the Caribbean.

The plant features elongated, lance-shaped leaves that are often dark green with a glossy texture. Its flowers are typically yellow with red or orange bracts, creating a dramatic visual effect.

Aphelandra chaponensis is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in warm, humid climates.
