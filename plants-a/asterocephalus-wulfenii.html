Asterocephalus wulfenii

Asterocephalus wulfenii, commonly known as the Wulfen's aster, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its flowers are usually purple or pink, with ray florets surrounding a central disk of tubular flowers.

Asterocephalus wulfenii is often found in fynbos vegetation, contributing to the biodiversity of its native habitat.
