Arctotis dentata

Arctotis dentata, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, daisy-like flowers that range in color from yellow to red. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves with serrated edges, which contribute to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Arctotis dentata is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
