Aglaonema oblanceolatum

Aglaonema oblanceolatum, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with striking white or cream-colored veins. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening. Its foliage remains vibrant throughout the year, adding a decorative touch to any space.

Aglaonema oblanceolatum is also valued for its air-purifying qualities and ease of care.
