Aruncus vulgaris var. tomentosus

Aruncus vulgaris var. tomentosus, commonly known as the fuzzy goat's beard, is a perennial herb native to eastern North America.

It features tall, slender stems that bear clusters of small, white to pale pink flowers arranged in panicles. The plant has soft, hairy foliage that gives it a distinctive, fluffy appearance. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodlands and stream banks.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and natural landscapes.
