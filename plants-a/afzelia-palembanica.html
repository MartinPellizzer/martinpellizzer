Afzelia palembanica

Afzelia palembanica, commonly known as the Palembang ironwood, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Fabaceae and is characterized by its dense, heavy wood that is highly valued for its durability and strength. The tree has compound leaves with alternating leaflets and produces small, yellowish-white flowers arranged in racemes. Its fruit is a legume pod that contains several flat, winged seeds.

Afzelia palembanica is often found in lowland rainforests and is known for its slow growth and long lifespan.
