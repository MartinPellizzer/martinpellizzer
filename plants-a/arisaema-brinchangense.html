Arisaema brinchangense

Arisaema brinchangense, commonly known as the Bintangor arrowroot, is a flowering plant native to the rainforests of Borneo.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive spathe and spadix inflorescence. The plant features a rhizomatous root system and produces large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 cm in diameter. Its flowers are typically greenish-yellow, with a spadix that bears both male and female flowers.

Arisaema brinchangense is often found in shaded, moist environments and is valued for its ornamental and potential medicinal properties.
