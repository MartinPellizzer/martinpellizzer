Abelia integerrima

Abelia integerrima, commonly known as the smooth abelia, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern North America.

It features attractive, fragrant, tubular flowers that bloom in late summer, typically in shades of pink and white. The plant has smooth, glossy leaves that provide ornamental value throughout the growing season. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers moist, well-drained soil.

Abelia integerrima is valued for its hardiness and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
