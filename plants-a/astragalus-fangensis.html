Astragalus fangensis

Astragalus fangensis, commonly known as Fang's milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its small, yellowish flowers and compound leaves. The plant is typically found in arid or semi-arid regions, where it thrives in well-drained soils. Astragalus fangensis is known for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional Chinese medicine.

Its seeds are often used as a source of protein and have been studied for their potential health benefits.
