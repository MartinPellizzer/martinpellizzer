Acrostichum strictum

Acrostichum strictum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf brake, is a tropical fern native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its narrow, leathery fronds that grow in a compact, upright form. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories. Its rhizomes are fibrous and can form dense colonies, contributing to its ability to spread and colonize new areas.

The fronds are typically divided into pairs of pinnae, giving it a distinctive appearance.
