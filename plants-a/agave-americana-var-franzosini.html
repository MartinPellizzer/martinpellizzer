Agave americana var. franzosini

Agave americana var. franzosini, commonly known as the Franzosini agave, is a succulent plant native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its thick, blue-green leaves that are often lined with white teeth along the edges. The plant produces a tall flower stalk that bears small, white flowers, which bloom in late spring or early summer. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

This variety is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping due to its striking appearance.
