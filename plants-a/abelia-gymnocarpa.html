Abelia gymnocarpa

Abelia gymnocarpa, commonly known as smooth-fruit abelia, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern North America.

It features slender, arching branches and is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant produces small, smooth, yellowish fruits that are edible for certain bird species. Abelia gymnocarpa thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained soil.

It is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various growing conditions.
