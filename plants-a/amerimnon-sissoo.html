Amerimnon sissoo

Amerimnon sissoo, commonly known as Indian Sissoo, is a deciduous tree native to the Indian subcontinent.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and showy, pink or purple flowers. The tree produces long, flat pods that contain several seeds, which are often used in traditional medicine.

It is valued for its hard, durable wood and is also used in agroforestry for soil stabilization.
