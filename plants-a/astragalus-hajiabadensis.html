Astragalus hajiabadensis

Astragalus hajiabadensis, commonly known as Hajiabad milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of Iran.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its small, yellow flowers and compound leaves. The plant is adapted to survive in dry, rocky environments, showcasing deep root systems to access groundwater.

It plays a role in soil stabilization and is of interest for its potential ecological and medicinal properties.
