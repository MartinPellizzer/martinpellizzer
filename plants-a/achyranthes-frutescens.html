Achyranthes frutescens

Achyranthes frutescens, commonly known as horse radish or wild radish, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is an annual or biennial herb that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters. The plant has a taproot system with a strong, aromatic root that is often used in traditional medicine.
