Anthurium combeimense

Anthurium combeimense, commonly known as the Combeimense arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive inflorescence consisting of a spadix and a spathe. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are typically deep green with a waxy texture. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are surrounded by a showy, colorful spathe.

Anthurium combeimense is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and ability to thrive in humid environments.
