Arnebia tibetana

Arnebia tibetana, commonly known as Tibetan gentian, is a flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Tibetan Plateau.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky and arid environments, adapting to the harsh conditions of its mountainous habitat. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, while its stems are sturdy and often branched.

Arnebia tibetana is valued for its ornamental beauty and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
