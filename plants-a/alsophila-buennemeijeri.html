Alsophila buennemeijeri

Alsophila buennemeijeri, commonly known as the Buenten's tree fern, is a species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds. The plant has a distinctive crown of pinnate leaves that can reach several meters in length. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.
