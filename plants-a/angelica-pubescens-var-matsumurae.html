Angelica pubescens var. matsumurae

Angelica pubescens var. matsumurae, commonly known as Japanese angelica or Matsumura's angelica, is a perennial herb native to Japan.

It belongs to the Apiaceae family, characterized by its aromatic foliage and umbel-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, with finely divided, fern-like leaves that are covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are white to pale green, and it produces small, dark brown berries.

The plant is valued for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional herbal remedies.
