Aster gmelinii

Aster gmelinii, commonly known as the Siberian aster, is a flowering plant native to the cold climates of Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of small, blue to purple flowers. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is often found in alpine and tundra environments. It is well adapted to harsh conditions, including low temperatures and poor soil.

Aster gmelinii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in challenging climates.
