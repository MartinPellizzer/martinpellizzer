Anotis juniperifolia

Anotis juniperifolia, commonly known as the coastal anotis, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows in coastal areas, characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its leaves are narrow and leathery, adapted to withstand salt spray and strong winds.

Anotis juniperifolia is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often used in coastal landscaping.
