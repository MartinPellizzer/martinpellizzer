Athyrium × lantangense

Athyrium × lantangense, commonly known as the Lantang fern, is a hybrid species within the Dryopteridaceae family.

It is characterized by its elegant, bipinnate fronds that are typically dark green with a glossy appearance. The plant thrives in shaded, moist environments, often found in temperate forests and mountainous regions. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense colonies.

Athyrium × lantangense is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and ability to tolerate a range of soil conditions.
