Arrabidaea rhodantha f. puberula

Arrabidaea rhodantha f. puberula, commonly known as the silky arrabidaea, is a flowering plant native to the humid forests of Brazil.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive silvery-white pubescence on its stems and leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or pale purple in color. Its leaves are trifoliate, with each leaflet being oval-shaped and hairy.
