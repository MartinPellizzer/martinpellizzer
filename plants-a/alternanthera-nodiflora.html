Alternanthera nodiflora

Alternanthera nodiflora, commonly known as the red-veined plant or red-veined alternate, is a herbaceous plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking red veins that run through its bright green leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters along the stems. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for aquatic and terrestrial gardens.
