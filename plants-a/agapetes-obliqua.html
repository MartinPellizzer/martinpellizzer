Agapetes obliqua

Agapetes obliqua, commonly known as the zigzag heath, is a species of flowering plant in the Ericaceae family.

It is native to the Andean regions of South America, particularly in countries like Ecuador and Peru. Agapetes obliqua is adapted to high-altitude environments and thrives in cool, moist, acidic soils.
