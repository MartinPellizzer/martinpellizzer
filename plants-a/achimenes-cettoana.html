Achimenes cettoana

Achimenes cettoana, commonly known as the velvetleaf begonia, is a tropical flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its broad, velvety leaves and showy, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. It thrives in warm, humid environments and prefers well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking vibrant, exotic blooms.
