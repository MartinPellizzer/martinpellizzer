Aster jamaicensis

Aster jamaicensis, commonly known as the Jamaican aster, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean islands, particularly Jamaica.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. Its flowers are usually blue to purple in color, with ray florets surrounding a central disk of tubular flowers.

Aster jamaicensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
