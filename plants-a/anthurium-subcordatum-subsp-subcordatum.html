Anthurium subcordatum subsp. subcordatum

Anthurium subcordatum subsp.

subcordatum, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a tropical flowering plant native to Central and South America. It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped leaves that are dark green with a glossy texture.

The plant produces striking spathes that range in color from deep red to orange, often with a contrasting white or yellow lip. It thrives in humid, warm environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.

Its unique foliage and colorful inflorescences make it a popular choice for indoor gardening and ornamental purposes.
