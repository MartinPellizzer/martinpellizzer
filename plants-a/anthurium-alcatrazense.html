Anthurium alcatrazense

Anthurium alcatrazense, commonly known as the Alcatraz Anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the island of Alcatraz in the Philippines.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a metallic sheen. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-shaped inflorescences that are often surrounded by a colorful spathe. It thrives in humid, warm environments with bright, indirect light.

Due to its unique appearance, it is a popular choice for ornamental gardening and plant collections.
