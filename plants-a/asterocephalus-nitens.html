Asterocephalus nitens

Asterocephalus nitens, commonly known as the star-headed orchid, is a rare and unique species native to the arid regions of Australia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, star-shaped floral structure. The plant produces small, pale yellow to greenish flowers that bloom during the cooler months. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, adapted to conserve water in its harsh environment.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a vulnerable species.
