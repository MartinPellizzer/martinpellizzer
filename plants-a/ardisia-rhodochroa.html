Ardisia rhodochroa

Ardisia rhodochroa, commonly known as the red-flowered ardisia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of small, red berries that appear in the fall. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
