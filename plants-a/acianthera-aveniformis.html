Acianthera aveniformis

Acianthera aveniformis, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces delicate, greenish-white flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in shaded, humid environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and by plant enthusiasts.
