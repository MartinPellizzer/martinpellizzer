Aotus lanigera var. incana

Aotus lanigera var. incana, commonly known as the woolly monkey tree.

This plant is a climbing shrub native to the tropical regions of South America. It features dense, woolly foliage that gives it its common name. The leaves are typically broad and elliptical, with a rough, hairy texture.

It produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by small, round fruits that are edible for certain wildlife.
