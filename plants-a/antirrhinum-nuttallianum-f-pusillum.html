Antirrhinum nuttallianum f. pusillum

Antirrhinum nuttallianum f. pusillum, commonly known as the dwarf snapdragon, is a flowering plant belonging to the Plantaginaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and small, tubular flowers that typically range in color from pink to red. The plant produces numerous small, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
