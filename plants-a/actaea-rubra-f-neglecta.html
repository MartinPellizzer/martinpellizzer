Actaea rubra f. neglecta

Actaea rubra f. neglecta, commonly known as the red baneberry.

This variant of the red baneberry is a herbaceous perennial native to eastern North America. It features striking red berries that contrast with its white, lace-like foliage. The plant is part of the Ranunculaceae family and thrives in moist, wooded areas.

Its ornamental value is heightened by its unique appearance and seasonal color changes.
