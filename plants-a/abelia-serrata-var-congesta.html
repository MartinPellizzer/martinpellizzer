Abelia serrata var. congesta

Abelia serrata var. congesta, commonly known as the Chinese Abelia or clustered Abelia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, fragrant, white to pinkish flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. The plant has glossy, ovate leaves with serrated edges, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained, moist soil.

This variety is valued for its compact growth habit and ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
