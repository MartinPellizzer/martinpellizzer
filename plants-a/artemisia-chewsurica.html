Artemisia chewsurica

Artemisia chewsurica, commonly known as Chinese wormwood, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It belongs to the sunflower family (Asteraceae) and is characterized by its aromatic, silver-gray foliage. The plant produces small, yellowish flower heads that are typically clustered in compact spherical shapes. Artemisia chewsurica is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

Its essential oils contain compounds like thujone and camphor, which contribute to its distinctive scent and potential medicinal properties.
