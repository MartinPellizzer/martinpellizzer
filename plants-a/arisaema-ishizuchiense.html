Arisaema ishizuchiense

Arisaema ishizuchiense, commonly known as the Ishizuchi arisaema, is a rare and unique plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive inflorescence composed of a spadix and a spathe. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded forest environments, preferring well-drained soil. Its foliage consists of broad, heart-shaped leaves that contribute to its ornamental appeal.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native range.
