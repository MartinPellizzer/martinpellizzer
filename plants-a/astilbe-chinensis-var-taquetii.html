Astilbe chinensis var. taquetii

Astilbe chinensis var. taquetii, commonly known as the Chinese astilbe, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to China.

It features upright, slender stems that bear panicles of small, fragrant, pinkish-white flowers. The plant has lance-shaped, dark green leaves that are arranged in a basal rosette. It thrives in partial shade and moist, well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens.

Its ornamental value comes from its delicate foliage and showy floral display.
