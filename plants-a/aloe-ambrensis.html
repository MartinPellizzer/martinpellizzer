Aloe ambrensis

Aloe ambrensis, commonly known as the Ambrensis Aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that store water, adapted to its arid environment. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow-orange flowers during the warmer months. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their soothing properties.

Aloe ambrensis thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for xeriscaping.
