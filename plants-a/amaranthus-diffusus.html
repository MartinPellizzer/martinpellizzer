Amaranthus diffusus

Amaranthus diffusus, commonly known as redroot amaranth, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and vibrant red or purple foliage. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically green or yellow in color. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a hardy annual in many temperate regions.

Amaranthus diffusus is also valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine by some indigenous cultures.
