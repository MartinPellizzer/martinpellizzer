Aster rubricaulis

Aster rubricaulis, commonly known as the redstem aster, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and woodlands. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers range from white to pinkish-red.

Aster rubricaulis plays a role in supporting local pollinators and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
