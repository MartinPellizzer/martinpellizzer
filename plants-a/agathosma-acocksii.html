Agathosma acocksii

Agathosma acocksii, commonly known as the Cape garlic.

It is a perennial shrub native to South Africa, characterized by its aromatic, narrow leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family and is often found in rocky, well-drained soils. It is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal medicine.

Its unique scent and resilience make it a notable species in its native habitat.
