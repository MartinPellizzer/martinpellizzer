Aloe descoingsii

Aloe descoingsii, commonly known as the Desert Aloe, is a succulent plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, often with a waxy coating to reduce water loss. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing yellow to orange flowers, which attract pollinators like bees and birds. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Due to its striking appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and arid landscapes.
