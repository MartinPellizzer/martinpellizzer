Aster adenolepis

Aster adenolepis, commonly known as the woolly aster, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant is known for its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. It produces showy blue to purple flowers that bloom in the summer months.

Aster adenolepis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
