Antirrhinum orontium f. elegans

Antirrhinum orontium f. elegans, commonly known as the elegant snapdragon, is a cultivar of the snapdragon species characterized by its refined and delicate floral form.

It belongs to the family Plantaginaceae and is native to the Mediterranean region. The plant typically produces tall, branched stems with clusters of bright red or orange tubular flowers. Its foliage is lance-shaped and covered in a fine, silky hair, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

This cultivar is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms and adaptability to various growing conditions.
