Anthurium gracilispadix

Anthurium gracilispadix, commonly known as the slender-spadix anthurium, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its elongated, spadix-shaped inflorescence, which is often surrounded by a large, colorful spathe. The plant's foliage consists of broad, glossy, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 cm in length. It thrives in humid, warm environments with bright, indirect light.

Anthurium gracilispadix is prized for its ornamental value and is commonly cultivated as a houseplant.
