Apocynum xylosteaceum

Apocynum xylosteaceum, commonly known as the Canadian milkweed, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family, which includes many species with milky sap. The plant produces showy, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are typically oval and have a slightly hairy texture.

The plant is known for its toxic properties, particularly due to the presence of cardiac glycosides in its sap and seeds.
