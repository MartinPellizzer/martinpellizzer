Aster nummulariifolius

Aster nummulariifolius, commonly known as the silverleaf aster, is a perennial herb native to the northeastern United States.

It features small, white to pale purple flowers that bloom in late summer and early fall. The plant has finely divided, silver-gray leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, providing nectar for pollinators.
