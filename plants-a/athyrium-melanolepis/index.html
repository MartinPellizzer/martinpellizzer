Athyrium Melanolepis

Athyrium melanolepis is a unique fern species known for its striking dark green fronds. Native to the forests of Asia, it thrives in humid, shaded environments. This plant is often admired for its elegant, feather-like structure and lush foliage. Its name reflects the dark pigmentation that characterizes its leaves. Growing both as an epiphyte and a terrestrial plant, it offers versatility in cultivation.
