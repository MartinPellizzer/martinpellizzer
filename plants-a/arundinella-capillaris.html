Arundinella capillaris

Arundinella capillaris, commonly known as the slender bamboo or capillary bamboo, is a member of the grass family Poaceae.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its slender, creeping stems that often form dense mats. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spikes, typical of grasses.

Arundinella capillaris is native to tropical and subtropical regions and is often found in moist, shaded environments.
