Adenia reticulata

Adenia reticulata, commonly known as the net-veined adenium, is a succulent shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems and leaves with a distinctive net-like venation pattern. The plant produces yellow flowers that are clustered in terminal inflorescences, followed by orange or red fruits. It is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, storing water in its swollen stems.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
