Aster ageratoides var. yezoensis

Aster ageratoides var. yezoensis, commonly known as Yezo aster, is a perennial herb native to Japan.

It features slender, upright stems with opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, daisy-like flowers in shades of white to pale blue, typically blooming in late summer to early autumn. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its delicate appearance and ability to attract pollinators.
