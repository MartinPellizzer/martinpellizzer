Adenocalymma comosum var. lanceolatum

Adenocalymma comosum var. lanceolatum, commonly known as the slender bristled everlasting, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit. The plant produces small, daisy-like flowers in shades of yellow and orange, which bloom in spring. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, giving it the variety name *lanceolatum*.
