Ajax pseudonarcissus

Ajax pseudonarcissus, commonly known as the lesser narcissus, is a member of the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is a bulbous plant native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa. The plant produces clusters of small, white, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring. Its leaves are strap-shaped and emerge from the base of the plant.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to grow in well-drained soil.
