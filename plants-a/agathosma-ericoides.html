Agathosma ericoides

Agathosma ericoides, commonly known as the silver leaf agathosma, is a small evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Rutaceae and is characterized by its aromatic, silver-green leaves that are covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in spring, followed by small, spherical fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in fynbos vegetation.
