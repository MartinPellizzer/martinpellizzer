Alternanthera caribaea

Alternanthera caribaea, commonly known as red flame or red beauty, is a striking aquatic plant native to South America.

It features vibrant red to pink foliage that intensifies under moderate to high light conditions. The plant has slender, branching stems and small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters. It thrives in tropical and subtropical freshwater environments, often found in slow-moving rivers and lakes.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for aquariums and water gardens.
