Amerosedum leibergii

Amerosedum leibergii, commonly known as the Leiberg's stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to the arid regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Crassulaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, thick leaves that store water, enabling it to survive in dry conditions.
