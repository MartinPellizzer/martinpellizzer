Alchemilla lucida

Alchemilla lucida, commonly known as the shining lady's mantle, is a perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its dense, silver-green foliage that shimmers in the sunlight. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers in clusters during the summer months. Its leaves are often used in traditional herbal medicine for their purported soothing properties.

Alchemilla lucida thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or meadow environments.
