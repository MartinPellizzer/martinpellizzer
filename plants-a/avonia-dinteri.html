Avonia dinteri

Avonia dinteri, commonly known as the Dinter's avonia, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, branching structure and fleshy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically green with a slight reddish tinge. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are usually yellow or orange, appearing in late spring or early summer. Avonia dinteri thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect sunlight to flourish.

It is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for succulent enthusiasts.
