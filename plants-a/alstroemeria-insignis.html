Alstroemeria insignis

Alstroemeria insignis, commonly known as the Peruvian lily, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the family Alstroemeriaceae and is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant has strap-like, narrow leaves and produces showy inflorescences that bloom in spring or summer. It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its attractive appearance and long-lasting blooms.

Alstroemeria insignis is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
