Alstroemeria pallens

Alstroemeria pallens, commonly known as the white alstroemeria, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the family Alstroemeriaceae and is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that typically range in color from white to pink. The plant produces long, strap-like leaves and thrives in well-drained soil with full sun to partial shade. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and floral arrangements.

Alstroemeria pallens is also known for its long-lasting blooms and adaptability to various growing conditions.
