Alyxia palauensis

It belongs to the family Apocynaceae and is characterized by its small, evergreen leaves and showy, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. Alyxia palauensis plays a role in the ecological dynamics of its habitat, contributing to nutrient cycling and providing food for local pollinators and birds.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native range.
