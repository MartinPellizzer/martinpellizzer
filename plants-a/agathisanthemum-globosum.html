Agathisanthemum globosum

Agathisanthemum globosum, commonly known as the globe agathis, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in a spherical shape, giving it its common name. It typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters.

The leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
