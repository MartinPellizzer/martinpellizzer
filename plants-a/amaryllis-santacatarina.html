Amaryllis santacatarina

Amaryllis santacatarina, commonly known as the Santa Catarina amaryllis, is a striking bulbous plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its large, showy flowers that come in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant produces strap-like leaves that are typically dark green and quite broad. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for gardeners in warmer climates.

Its blooming period usually occurs in the spring, adding a vibrant touch to gardens during that season.
