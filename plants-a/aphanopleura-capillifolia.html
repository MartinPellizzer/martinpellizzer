Aphanopleura capillifolia

Aphanopleura capillifolia, commonly known as the slender-leaved rock cress, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a small, perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils. The plant features delicate, thread-like leaves and produces small, white or pale yellow flowers. It is often found in open, sunny habitats such as cliffs and slopes.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species in many parts of its range.
