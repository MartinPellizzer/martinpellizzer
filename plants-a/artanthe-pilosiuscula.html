Artanthe pilosiuscula

Artanthe pilosiuscula, commonly known as the silky artanthe, is a low-growing perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features silvery-gray foliage covered in fine, soft hairs, which give it its common name. The plant produces small, pink to purple flowers that bloom in early spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Its ornamental value lies in its attractive foliage and delicate floral display.
