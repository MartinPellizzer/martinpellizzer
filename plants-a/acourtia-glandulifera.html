Acourtia glandulifera

Acourtia glandulifera, commonly known as the glandular acourtia, is a flowering plant native to California and Baja California.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in coastal areas. It features small, glandular-dotted leaves and produces yellow to orange flowers.

Acourtia glandulifera is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
