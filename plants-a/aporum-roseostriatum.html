Aporum roseostriatum

Aporum roseostriatum, commonly known as the red-striated aporum, is a striking plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often adorned with red or pink veins, giving it a vibrant and eye-catching appearance. The plant belongs to the Araceae family, which includes many ornamental plants known for their unique foliage and flowering structures.
