Athanasia bremeri

Athanasia bremeri, commonly known as the Bremer's immortality, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in spring. Athanasia bremeri is known for its resilience, thriving in arid and rocky environments.

Its common name reflects the belief that it symbolizes eternal life due to its hardy nature and ability to survive harsh conditions.
