Agrostis stolonifera f. pontica

Agrostis stolonifera f. pontica, commonly known as the Pontic meadowgrass, is a variant of the common bentgrass.

It is a low-growing perennial grass characterized by its fine, tufted foliage and creeping stolons. This plant typically thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wet meadows and along stream banks. Its botanical features include narrow leaves and small, spike-like inflorescences.

It plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various invertebrates.
