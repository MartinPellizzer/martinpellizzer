Arundinaria simonii

Arundinaria simonii, commonly known as Chinese bamboo or black bamboo, is a species of grass native to China.

It is characterized by its tall, slender culms that can reach heights of up to 10 meters. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or brown in color. Its leaves are long and narrow, with a smooth texture and a bluish-green hue.

Arundinaria simonii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional Chinese medicine.
