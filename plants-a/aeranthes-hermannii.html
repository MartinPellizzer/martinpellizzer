Aeranthes hermannii

Aeranthes hermannii, commonly known as Hermann's spider orchid, is a tropical plant native to the Caribbean.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its intricate, spider-like appearance. The plant features a short, erect stem with a single, showy flower that has a distinctive, intricate pattern of petals and sepals. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique beauty and rarity, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
