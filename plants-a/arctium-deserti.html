Arctium deserti

Arctium deserti, commonly known as the desert burdock, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features a tall, erect stem with spiny bracts that protect its small, yellow flowers. Its leaves are large, heart-shaped, and covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.

Arctium deserti is adapted to survive in harsh environments, making it a resilient species in desert ecosystems.
