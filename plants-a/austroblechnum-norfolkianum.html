Austroblechnum norfolkianum

Austroblechnum norfolkianum, commonly known as the Norfolk Island holly fern, is a species of fern native to Norfolk Island.

It belongs to the family Blechnaceae and is characterized by its evergreen, leathery fronds that are deeply divided. The species is of conservation interest due to its limited distribution and sensitivity to environmental changes.
