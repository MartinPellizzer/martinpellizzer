Aspalathus pungens

Aspalathus pungens, commonly known as the Cape Flats tea plant, is a small evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It features small, leathery leaves that are dark green in color and have a slightly serrated edge. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer months. Its unique properties make it a popular choice for herbal teas, particularly in the region of the Western Cape.

The plant thrives in well-drained soils and requires a sunny to partially shaded environment.
