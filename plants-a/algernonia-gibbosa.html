Algernonia gibbosa

Algernonia gibbosa, commonly known as the waxy leaf orchid, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its glossy, waxy leaves that are often shaped in a lanceolate form. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. Algernonia gibbosa thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, nutrient-rich soil.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique foliage and subtle floral display.
