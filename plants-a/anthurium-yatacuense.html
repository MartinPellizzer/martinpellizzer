Anthurium yatacuense

Anthurium yatacuense, commonly known as the Yatacuense arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to the Amazon rainforest of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are often a deep green with a waxy texture. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-shaped inflorescences that are surrounded by a colorful spathe, typically in shades of red or orange. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium yatacuense is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in botanical gardens.
