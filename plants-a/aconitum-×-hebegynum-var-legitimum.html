Aconitum × hebegynum var. legitimum

Aconitum × hebegynum var. legitimum, commonly known as the hybrid aconite, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Ranunculaceae family.

It features tall, sparsely branched stems that can reach up to 2 meters in height, adorned with large, showy blue to purple flowers. The plant has palmately compound leaves with three to five leaflets, and its roots are tuberous, typical of the genus Aconitum. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade, often found in woodland areas.

The plant is noted for its ornamental value and is cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
