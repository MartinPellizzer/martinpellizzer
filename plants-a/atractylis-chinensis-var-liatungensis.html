Atractylis chinensis var. liatungensis

Atractylis chinensis var. liatungensis, commonly known as the Liatung golden burr, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its hairy, rhizomatous growth habit. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in composite heads, which are typical of the daisy family. Its leaves are typically lanceolate and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows and grasslands.
