Ascolepis protea var. tuberosa

Ascolepis protea var. tuberosa, commonly known as the tuberous protea, is a striking South African plant belonging to the Proteaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, showy, spherical inflorescence composed of numerous white to pinkish flowers. The plant produces a unique tuberous root system that stores nutrients and water, aiding in its survival in dry conditions. Its flowers are typically visited by pollinators such as bees and birds, contributing to its ecological significance.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and floral arrangements.
