Aquilegia chrysantha

Aquilegia chrysantha, commonly known as the golden monkeyflower, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its showy, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in woodland or open areas. Its leaves are trifoliate, with three leaflets that are palmately arranged.
