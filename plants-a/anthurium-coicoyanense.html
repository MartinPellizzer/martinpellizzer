Anthurium coicoyanense

Anthurium coicoyanense, commonly known as the Coicoyanense arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces striking spathes that can range in color from deep red to orange, often contrasting with its foliage. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Anthurium coicoyanense is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in botanical gardens.
