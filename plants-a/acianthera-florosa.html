Acianthera florosa

Acianthera florosa, commonly known as the showy orchid, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern around the base of the plant.

Acianthera florosa prefers moist, well-drained soils and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.
