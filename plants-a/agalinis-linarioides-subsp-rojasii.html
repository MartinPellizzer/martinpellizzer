Agalinis linarioides subsp. rojasii

Agalinis linarioides subsp.

rojasii, commonly known as the Mexican everlasting pea, is a flowering plant native to Mexico. It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its typical pea-like flowers and compound leaves.
