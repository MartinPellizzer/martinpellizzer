Arnocrinum preisii

Arnocrinum preisii, commonly known as the purple tansy, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its showy, purple to pinkish-yellow composite flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has finely divided, fern-like leaves and is native to parts of Europe and western Asia. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Arnocrinum preisii is often found in wetlands, stream banks, and other damp habitats.
