Aralia weberbaueri

Aralia weberbaueri, commonly known as the Hawaiian aralia, is a flowering plant native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It is a member of the Araliaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and clusters of small white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded areas of tropical forests. It produces small, dark berries that are an important food source for local wildlife.
