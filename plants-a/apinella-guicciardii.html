Apinella guicciardii

Apinella guicciardii, commonly known as the Guicciardii's apinella, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, clustered leaves. The plant produces delicate, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in late spring. It prefers well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Apinella guicciardii is valued for its ornamental appeal and low maintenance requirements.
