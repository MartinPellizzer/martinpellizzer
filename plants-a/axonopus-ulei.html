Axonopus ulei

Axonopus ulei, commonly known as the slender axonopus, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or wetlands. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, inconspicuous flowers. It is often found in low-light conditions and thrives in humid climates.

Axonopus ulei is valued for its ability to stabilize soil and prevent erosion in wetland areas.
