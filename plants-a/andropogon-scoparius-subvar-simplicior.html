Andropogon scoparius subvar. simplicior

Andropogon scoparius subvar. simplicior, commonly known as little bluestem, is a native grass species found in North America.

It is characterized by its fine, blue-green foliage and tufted inflorescence, which becomes reddish-brown in maturity. This plant thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in prairies and open woodlands. It plays a vital role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various wildlife species.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for native plant gardens and restoration projects.
