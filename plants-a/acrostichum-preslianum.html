Acrostichum preslianum

Acrostichum preslianum, commonly known as the Presleian grass.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia. The plant features long, narrow leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are clustered in spikes.

Acrostichum preslianum is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or wetlands.
