Athrotaxis × doniana

Athrotaxis × doniana, commonly known as the Tasmanian pencil pine, is a hybrid conifer native to Tasmania.

It is a small to medium-sized tree with a distinctive, narrow, conical shape. The plant produces small, scale-like leaves that are arranged spirally along its branches. It flowers in late summer, producing yellowish-green cones that contain seeds.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and parks.
