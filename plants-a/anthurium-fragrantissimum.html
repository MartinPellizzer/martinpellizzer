Anthurium fragrantissimum

Anthurium fragrantissimum, commonly known as the fragrant anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped leaves that can range in color from deep green to variegated shades. The plant produces striking spadices, which are modified leaves that enclose the true flowers, often in shades of red, orange, or yellow. Anthurium fragrantissimum is known for its pleasant, spicy fragrance, which is more pronounced in certain varieties.

It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect sunlight for optimal growth.
