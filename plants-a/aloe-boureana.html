Aloe boureana

Aloe boureana, commonly known as the snow aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy, bluish-green leaves that are covered in a powdery white coating. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its leaves and requiring minimal watering.

Aloe boureana is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable growing conditions.
