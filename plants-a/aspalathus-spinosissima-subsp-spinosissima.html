Aspalathus spinosissima subsp. spinosissima

Aspalathus spinosissima subsp.

spinosissima, commonly known as the spiny aspalathus, is a shrub native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its woody stems and small, needle-like leaves.

The plant produces yellow flowers that are typically clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological role, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists alike.
