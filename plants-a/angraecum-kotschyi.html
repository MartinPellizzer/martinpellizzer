Angraecum kotschyi

Angraecum kotschyi, commonly known as the Madagascar star orchid, is a climbing orchid native to Madagascar.

It features long, slender stems and produces striking, star-shaped flowers with white petals and a deep purple lip. The plant is adapted to grow in humid, shaded environments, often found in forest understories. Its flowers are pollinated by moths, which are attracted to its strong nocturnal fragrance.

Angraecum kotschyi is valued for its ornamental beauty and is relatively easy to care for in the right conditions.
