Amorphophallus tenuistylis

Amorphophallus tenuistylis, commonly known as the narrow-styled arrowhead, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its unique inflorescence consisting of a spadix and a spathe. The plant produces a large, white or pale pink spathe that encloses a tall, spiky spadix, which bears numerous tiny flowers. It is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in moist, shaded environments.

The plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its foliage and floral display.
