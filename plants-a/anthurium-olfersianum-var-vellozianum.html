Anthurium olfersianum var. vellozianum

Anthurium olfersianum var. vellozianum, commonly known as the Vellozianum anthurium, is a tropical flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its glossy, heart-shaped leaves and vibrant, spadix-shaped inflorescences that range in color from red to orange. The plant thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light. It requires well-draining soil and regular watering to maintain its health.

This variety is popular among collectors for its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
