Anemone × mixta

Anemone × mixta, commonly known as the hybrid anemone, is a perennial herbaceous plant that exhibits characteristics of both Anemone coronaria and Anemone hupehensis.

It produces showy, single or double flowers in shades of white, pink, and red, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant features sturdy, upright stems with basal leaves that are palmately lobed and hairy. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden borders and rock gardens.

Anemone × mixta is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
