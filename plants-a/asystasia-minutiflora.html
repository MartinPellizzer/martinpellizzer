Asystasia minutiflora

Asystasia minutiflora, commonly known as the dwarf asystasia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 20-30 centimeters. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth texture and a light green color. Its small, tubular flowers are usually violet or purple in color and bloom in clusters.

Asystasia minutiflora is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its compact size and attractive foliage.
