Athyrium shaaxiense

Athyrium shaaxiense, commonly known as the Shaanxi Athyrium, is a fern species native to China.

It belongs to the family Athyriaceae and is characterized by its delicate, feather-like fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist forest environments, preferring well-drained soil. Its spore-bearing structures, called sori, are located on the underside of the fronds.

Athyrium shaaxiense is valued for its ornamental appeal and plays a role in the ecological diversity of its native habitat.
