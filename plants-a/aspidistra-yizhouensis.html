Aspidistra yizhouensis

Aspidistra yizhouensis, commonly known as the Yizhou aspidistra, is a rare evergreen perennial plant native to China.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its dark green, strap-like leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are surrounded by a spadix and a spathe, typical of the Araceae family. It thrives in shady, humid environments and prefers well-draining, acidic soil.
