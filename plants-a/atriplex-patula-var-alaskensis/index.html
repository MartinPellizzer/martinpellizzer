Atriplex Patula Var. Alaskensis

Alaska saltbush, scientifically known as Atriplex patula var. alaskensis, is a hardy shrub native to the arid and semi-arid regions of Alaska. This resilient plant thrives in saline and alkaline soils, making it a vital component of its harsh ecosystem. Its silver-green foliage and compact growth habit allow it to withstand extreme weather conditions. The Alaska saltbush plays a crucial role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for local wildlife. Due to its unique adaptations, it is an important species for ecological restoration in northern environments.
