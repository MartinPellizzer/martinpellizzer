Areca subacaulis

Areca subacaulis, commonly known as the slender areca palm, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, unbranched trunk and feathery, compound leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that eventually develop into three-lobed fruits. Areca subacaulis thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in tropical and subtropical regions.
