Asplenium abscissum var. ruizianum

Asplenium abscissum var. ruizianum, commonly known as the Ruiz's shield fern, is a variant of the shield fern genus Asplenium.

It is a small, evergreen fern typically found in shaded, moist environments, often in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features a creeping rhizome with dense clusters of small, shield-shaped leaves. Its fronds are usually dark green and have a leathery texture, with a distinctive, narrow, and elongated shape.

This variety is notable for its adaptability to various soil types and its ability to thrive in low-light conditions.
