Anoectochilus moulmeinensis

Anoectochilus moulmeinensis, commonly known as the Moulmein orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its epiphytic growth habit, typically found on trees in humid tropical forests. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are usually white or pale pink in color. It has lance-shaped leaves that are usually clustered at the base of the plant.

Anoectochilus moulmeinensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
