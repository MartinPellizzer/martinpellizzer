Astracantha talarensis

Astracantha talarensis, commonly known as the Talarensis thistle, is a flowering plant native to the island of Socotra.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its distinctive spiny leaves and purple flowers. The plant is adapted to the arid and rocky environment of Socotra, showcasing unique botanical features that aid in its survival. Its morphology includes a tall, erect stem with basal leaves that are deeply lobed and covered in sharp spines.

Astracantha talarensis is of interest to botanists due to its endemic status and unique adaptations to its harsh habitat.
