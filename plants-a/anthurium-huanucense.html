Anthurium huanucense

Anthurium huanucense, commonly known as the Huanuco arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to the Amazon rainforest of Peru.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves with a vibrant green color and a distinctive arrowhead pattern. The plant produces striking spadices, which are the modified leaves that house the tiny, inconspicuous flowers. Anthurium huanucense thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, nutrient-rich soil.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological significance, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation programs.
