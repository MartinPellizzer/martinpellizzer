Asparagus tuberculatus

Asparagus tuberculatus, commonly known as the smooth asparagus or the cushion asparagus, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features small, fleshy, spiny leaves that are arranged in a dense, cushion-like structure. The plant produces yellow flowers during the summer months, which are followed by small, red berries. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Asparagus tuberculatus is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and hardy nature.
