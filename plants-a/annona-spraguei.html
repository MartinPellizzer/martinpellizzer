Annona spraguei

Annona spraguei, commonly known as the yellow annona, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is a small, deciduous tree or shrub that typically grows to a height of 3 to 5 meters. The plant produces large, heart-shaped leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. Its flowers are fragrant, white or pale yellow, and appear in clusters.

The fruit is a large, green, spiny berry that turns yellow when ripe, and is edible when mature.
