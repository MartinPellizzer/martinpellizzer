Abies fargesii var. tieghemii

Abies fargesii var. tieghemii, commonly known as the Tieghem's fir, is a variant of the fargesii fir native to the mountainous regions of western China.

It is a coniferous evergreen tree characterized by its pyramidal shape and needle-like leaves that are blue-green in color. The tree produces small, rounded cones that mature to a reddish-brown hue. It thrives in cool, moist climates and is often found in high-altitude forests.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for both ecological and ornamental purposes.
