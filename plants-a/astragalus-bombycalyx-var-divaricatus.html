Astragalus bombycalyx var. divaricatus

Astragalus bombycalyx var. divaricatus, commonly known as the Chinese milkvetch, is a perennial legume native to China.

It is characterized by its compound leaves with small, oval leaflets and produces yellow flowers in clusters. The plant is known for its ability to fix nitrogen through symbiotic relationships with rhizobia bacteria in its root system. It thrives in dry, well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and rocky areas.

This species is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional Chinese medicine.
