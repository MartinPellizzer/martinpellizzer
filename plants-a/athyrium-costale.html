Athyrium costale

Athyrium costale, commonly known as the narrow-leaved lady fern, is a delicate fern species native to eastern Asia.

It features finely divided, feathery fronds that are typically bright green and slightly hairy along the veins. The plant grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its rhizomes are creeping and produce numerous upright stems.

Athyrium costale is valued for its ornamental foliage and is often cultivated in gardens with high humidity.
