Achimenes × boeckmannii

Achimenes × boeckmannii, commonly known as the hybrid flame violet, is a tropical perennial plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from red to orange and yellow. The plant features heart-shaped, dark green leaves and produces tuberous roots that help it survive in its natural habitat. It thrives in warm, humid conditions and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This species is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking floral display.
