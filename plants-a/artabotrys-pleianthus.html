Artabotrys pleianthus

Artabotrys pleianthus, commonly known as the white flame tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy white flowers that bloom in clusters, often resembling flames. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are ovate to elliptical in shape. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The bark and leaves have been used in traditional medicine for their potential health benefits.
