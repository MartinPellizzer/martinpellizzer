Amalia grandiflora

Amalia grandiflora, commonly known as the grandiflora amalia, is a flowering plant native to specific regions in South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along its stem. Amalia grandiflora thrives in well-drained soil and prefers a sunny to partially shaded environment.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
