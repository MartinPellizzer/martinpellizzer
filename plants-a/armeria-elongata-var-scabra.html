Armeria elongata var. scabra

Armeria elongata var. scabra, commonly known as the roughleaf thrift, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces clusters of white, star-shaped flowers. The plant features linear, hairy leaves that are rough to the touch, giving it its scientific name. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its hardy nature and ornamental value make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking drought-tolerant plants.
