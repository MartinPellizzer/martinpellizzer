Aspalathoides cytisi

Aspalathoides cytisi, commonly known as the Cape chestnut, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in arid conditions, making it well adapted to the local climate. Its seeds are encased in a pod and are often used in traditional medicine.
