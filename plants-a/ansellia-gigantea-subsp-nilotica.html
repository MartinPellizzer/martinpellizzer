Ansellia gigantea subsp. nilotica

Ansellia gigantea subsp.

nilotica, commonly known as the giant arrowroot, is a tropical plant native to Africa and parts of Asia. It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves that can grow up to 2 meters in length.

The plant produces striking inflorescences with a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe. Its rhizomes are edible and used in various culinary applications.

The species is valued for both its ornamental beauty and its utility in traditional medicine and food.
