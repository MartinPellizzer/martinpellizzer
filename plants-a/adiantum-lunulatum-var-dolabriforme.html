Adiantum lunulatum var. dolabriforme

Adiantum lunulatum var. dolabriforme, commonly known as the sickle moon fern, is a delicate fern species native to Southeast Asia.

It features finely divided, lance-shaped fronds that are often described as resembling a sickle or crescent. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is characterized by its intricate, lace-like appearance. Its fronds are typically light green and have a slightly curled, wavy margin.

This variety is prized in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in indoor settings with proper care.
