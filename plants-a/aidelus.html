Aidelus

Aidelus, commonly known as the silverleaf plant, is a flowering shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features silvery-gray foliage that reflects sunlight, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its ornamental value is complemented by its ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
