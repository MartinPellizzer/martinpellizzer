Actinidia chinensis f. jinggangshanensis

Actinidia chinensis f. jinggangshanensis, commonly known as the Jinggangshan kiwifruit, is a unique variant of the Chinese Actinidia species.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Jiangxi Province in China, where it thrives in cool, humid climates. The plant is a deciduous vine that produces small, hairy fruits with a distinctive flavor profile. Its botanical characteristics include heart-shaped leaves with a serrated margin and a climbing growth habit.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential as a fruit crop.
