Aster bellidiastrum

Aster bellidiastrum, commonly known as the slender aster, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads composed of ray and disk florets. The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, while its flowers are small and clustered, often appearing in shades of purple or blue.

Aster bellidiastrum plays a role in supporting local pollinators and is sometimes used in native plant landscaping due to its ornamental value.
