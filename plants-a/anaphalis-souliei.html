Anaphalis souliei

Anaphalis souliei, commonly known as the silver hair grass, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense, white, woolly foliage that resembles a cloud. The plant produces small, inconspicuous yellow flowers that are clustered in a spherical inflorescence. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
