Anemone uralensis

Anemone uralensis, commonly known as the Ural anemone, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features showy, white to pinkish flowers that bloom in early spring, typically from March to May. The plant has a short, thick rhizome and basal leaves that are palmately divided with serrated edges. Its flowers are actinomorphic, with five to seven petals and numerous stamens, making it a striking addition to alpine gardens.

Anemone uralensis thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or forested habitats.
