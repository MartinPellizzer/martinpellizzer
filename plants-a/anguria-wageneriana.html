Anguria wageneriana

Anguria wageneriana, commonly known as the Wagener's cucumber, is a species of flowering plant in the Cucurbitaceae family.

It is native to South America, particularly Brazil, and is characterized by its trailing growth habit and hairy stems. Anguria wageneriana is often cultivated for its ornamental value and as a source of edible produce in its native region.
