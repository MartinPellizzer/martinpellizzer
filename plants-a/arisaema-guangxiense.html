Arisaema guangxiense

Arisaema guangxiense, commonly known as Guangxi arrowroot, is a flowering plant native to southern China.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its unique, spadix-like inflorescence surrounded by a colorful spathe. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream sides. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional Chinese medicine for their purported health benefits.

The botanical features of Arisaema guangxiense make it an interesting subject for both horticultural and scientific study.
