Annona dolichophylla

Annona dolichophylla, commonly known as the long-leaf annona, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is a small tree or shrub that typically grows to a height of about 3 to 5 meters. The plant produces large, heart-shaped leaves that are usually green and glossy. Its flowers are white or pale yellow and are followed by large, round fruits that are green when unripe and turn yellow when mature.

The fruit is edible and has a sweet, creamy texture, making it a valued species in some regions.
