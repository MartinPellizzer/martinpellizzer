Aponogeton abyssinicus var. albiflorus

Aponogeton abyssinicus var. albiflorus, commonly known as the whiteflower water arrowhead, is a rare aquatic plant native to Ethiopia.

It features striking white flowers that contrast with its dark green, arrowhead-shaped leaves. The plant thrives in slow-moving freshwater environments with moderate to high light conditions. Its roots are often used in traditional medicine, and it is prized for its ornamental value in aquariums.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species.
