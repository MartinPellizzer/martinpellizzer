Astragalus lichiangensis

Astragalus lichiangensis, commonly known as the Yunnan milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Yunnan Province in China.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to pale purple. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils at high elevations, often in alpine or subalpine habitats. It has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces pods that contain several seeds.

Astragalus lichiangensis is valued for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional Chinese medicine.
