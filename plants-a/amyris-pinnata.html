Amyris pinnata

Amyris pinnata, commonly known as the flame tree or pinnate flame tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compound leaves with long, slender leaflets that give it a feathery appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or orange, which bloom in clusters. Amyris pinnata is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.

It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
