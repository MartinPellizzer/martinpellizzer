Acomastylis elata var. humilis

Acomastylis elata var. humilis, commonly known as the slender acomastylis, is a low-growing succulent native to southern Africa.

It features compact, fleshy stems that are often covered in fine, white hairs. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique morphology and adaptability make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
