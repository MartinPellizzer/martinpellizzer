Astracantha florulenta

Astracantha florulenta, commonly known as the star-thorned bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features distinctive, tubular flowers that range in color from white to purple, often with a yellow throat. The plant has spiny, leaf-like structures called phyllaries that surround the flower heads, giving it a unique appearance. Its leaves are typically narrow and lance-shaped, with a rough texture.

Astracantha florulenta thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy addition to rock gardens and dry landscapes.
