Anuragia Quadrifolia

Four-leaved clover, scientifically known as Anuragia quadrifolia, is a rare and mystical plant that has captured the imagination of people for centuries. While most clovers have three leaves, this unique species occasionally develops four, making it a symbol of luck and fortune in many cultures. Found primarily in temperate regions, it thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often associated with folklore and superstition. Its delicate appearance and rarity have made it a popular subject in literature, art, and even modern media. Despite its symbolic significance, the plant remains a fascinating subject of botanical study due to its genetic anomalies and ecological role.
