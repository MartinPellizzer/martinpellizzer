Agave congesta

Agave congesta, commonly known as the dense agave, is a succulent plant native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces a tall, branched inflorescence with numerous white or yellowish flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for arid environments.

Agave congesta is often used in xeriscaping due to its low water requirements and ornamental value.
