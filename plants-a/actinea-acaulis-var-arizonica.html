Actinea acaulis var. arizonica

Actinea acaulis var. arizonica, commonly known as Arizona star moss, is a low-growing, mat-forming plant native to the southwestern United States.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its compact, cushion-like growth habit. The plant features small, ovate to elliptical leaves that are typically dark green and have a leathery texture. Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is sometimes cultivated as an ornamental plant in xeriscapic gardens.
