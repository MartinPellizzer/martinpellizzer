Artemisia pabularis

Artemisia pabularis, commonly known as silverleaf sage, is a perennial herb native to the western United States and parts of Canada.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, dense hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late summer and early fall. It thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in open habitats such as meadows and grasslands.

Artemisia pabularis is valued for its aromatic properties and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
