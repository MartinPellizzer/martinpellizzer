Aralia montana

Aralia montana, commonly known as the mountain aralia, is a deciduous shrub native to the eastern United States.

It features compound leaves that are typically palmately lobed, with a feathery appearance. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers in umbels, which are followed by bright red berries. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.

Its botanical characteristics make it a distinctive and ornamental plant in native gardens.
