Alysicarpus mahbubnagarensis

Alysicarpus mahbubnagarensis, commonly known as Mahbubnagar burr grass, is a flowering plant native to the Indian subcontinent.

The plant typically grows in dry, arid regions and is adapted to withstand harsh environmental conditions. It plays a role in soil stabilization and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
