Arisaema ilanense

Arisaema ilanense, commonly known as the Ilan arrowhead, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its spadix and spathe structure. The plant features a tuberous root system and broad, arrow-shaped leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value and medicinal properties make it a notable species in botanical studies.
