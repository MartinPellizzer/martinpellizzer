Astroloma microdonta

Astroloma microdonta, commonly known as the small-flowered astroloma, is a flowering plant native to Western Australia.

It is a low-growing shrub with small, glandular leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of tiny, white to pale pink flowers during the warmer months. It is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, showing drought tolerance.

Astroloma microdonta is often found in sandy or gravelly soils in coastal and inland regions.
