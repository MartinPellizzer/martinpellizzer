Asterotrichion discolor

Asterotrichion discolor, commonly known as the spotted asterotrichion, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its small, daisy-like flowers that typically display a mix of white and yellow petals. The plant produces small, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a fuzzy appearance. It is native to certain regions of South America and thrives in moist, temperate climates.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens, though it is more commonly found in the wild.
