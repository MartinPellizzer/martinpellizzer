Arum zeleborii

Arum zeleborii, commonly known as the Zelbor's arum, is a flowering plant native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive inflorescence composed of a spadix and a spathe. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are typically dark green with a velvety texture. Its flowers are small and clustered around the spadix, which is often yellow or green in color.

Arum zeleborii is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its striking foliage and unique floral structure.
