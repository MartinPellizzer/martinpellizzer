Acianthus vulcanicus

Acianthus vulcanicus, commonly known as the volcanic orchid, is a rare and unique plant native to volcanic regions.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from deep purples to vivid reds. The plant thrives in nutrient-rich, volcanic soil and requires high levels of sunlight. Its roots are adapted to absorb minerals from the rocky substrate, showcasing its resilience.
