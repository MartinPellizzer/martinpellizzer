Asphodeline globifera

Asphodeline globifera, commonly known as the globe asphodel, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features a rosette of narrow, gray-green leaves that are often covered in a fine, powdery coating. The plant produces tall, spiky flower stalks that bear clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and arid landscapes.

Due to its drought tolerance and ornamental value, it is a popular choice for xeriscaping.
