Anthurium bogotense var. concinnatum

Anthurium bogotense var. concinnatum, commonly known as the velvet leaf anthurium, is a striking tropical plant native to the cloud forests of Colombia.

It features large, heart-shaped leaves that are velvety and dark green, with a prominent midrib and a distinctive red or purple spathe. The plant produces showy, spadix-like inflorescences that are often surrounded by a colorful spathe, contributing to its ornamental value. It thrives in high humidity and indirect light, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Its unique foliage and vibrant flowers make it a sought-after addition to botanical collections and home gardens.
