Acareosperma spireanum

Acareosperma spireanum, commonly known as the spiral spirea, is a flowering plant belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its distinctive spirally arranged inflorescences, which give it a unique and ornamental appearance. The plant produces small, white to pinkish flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are typically narrow and serrated, providing a complementary texture to the floral display.

Native to certain regions of Europe, it is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic value and ease of care.
