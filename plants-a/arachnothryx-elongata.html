Arachnothryx elongata

Arachnothryx elongata, commonly known as the long-stemmed arachnothryx, is a species of flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its climbing habit and compound leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or greenish-yellow in color. Its fruit is a legume pod that contains several seeds, which are dispersed by wind or animals.

Arachnothryx elongata is often found in humid, lowland forests and is valued for its ornamental potential in horticulture.
