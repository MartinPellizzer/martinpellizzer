Arrabidaea claussenii

Arrabidaea claussenii, commonly known as the Cape barbed wire vine, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its distinctive, barbed pods. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to pink, often appearing in clusters. The species is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
