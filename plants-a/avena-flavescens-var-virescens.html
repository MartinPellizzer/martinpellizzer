Avena flavescens var. virescens

Avena flavescens var. virescens, commonly known as green oat, is a variant of the oat species characterized by its distinct greenish hue.

It is a perennial grass native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, with slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. Its inflorescence consists of a spikelet-bearing panicle, which produces small, two-lobed flowers.

The botanical features of this variety make it valuable for both ornamental and ecological purposes.
