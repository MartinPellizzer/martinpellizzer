Amaryllis kromeri

Amaryllis kromeri, commonly known as the Kromer's amaryllis, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers. The plant typically produces clusters of vibrant red or orange blooms during the summer months. Its foliage consists of broad, strap-like leaves that emerge from a bulbous base.

Amaryllis kromeri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
