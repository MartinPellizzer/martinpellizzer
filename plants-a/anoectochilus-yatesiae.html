Anoectochilus yatesiae

Anoectochilus yatesiae, commonly known as the Yate's slipper orchid, is a rare and endangered species native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid with a distinctive slipper-shaped flower that is typically greenish-yellow in color. The plant has a short, thick rhizome and produces delicate, hairy leaves that help it survive in its cool, shaded habitat. It thrives in humid, mossy environments and is often found growing on rocky substrates.
