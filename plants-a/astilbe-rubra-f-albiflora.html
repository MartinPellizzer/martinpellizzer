Astilbe rubra f. albiflora

Astilbe rubra f. albiflora, commonly known as the white-flowered smooth astilbe, is a cultivar of the smooth astilbe species.

It is characterized by its elegant, plume-like inflorescences that are predominantly white in color. The plant features lance-shaped, dark green foliage that provides a striking contrast to its blooms. It thrives in partial to full shade and prefers moist, well-drained soil.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal in shaded garden settings.
