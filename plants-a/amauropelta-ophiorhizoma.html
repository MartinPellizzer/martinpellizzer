Amauropelta ophiorhizoma

Amauropelta ophiorhizoma, commonly known as the golden shield fern, is a unique member of the Pteridaceae family.

It is characterized by its distinctive, shield-shaped fronds that are typically bright yellow to golden in color. The plant produces sporangia on the underside of its fronds, which are clustered in sori, a common feature among ferns. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for horticultural displays.
