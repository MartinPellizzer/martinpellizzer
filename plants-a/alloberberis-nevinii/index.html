Alloberberis Nevinii

Alloberberis nevinii is a rare and intriguing shrub native to the mountainous regions of southern California. It belongs to the barberry family, known for its ornamental value and ecological importance. This plant is characterized by its dense, spiny foliage and bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring. Due to habitat loss and limited distribution, it is considered endangered and requires conservation efforts. Its unique features make it a subject of interest for botanists and nature enthusiasts alike.
