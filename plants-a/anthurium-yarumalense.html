Anthurium yarumalense

Anthurium yarumalense, commonly known as the Yarumalense arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to Colombia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces a spadix inflorescence, which is typical of the genus Anthurium. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.
