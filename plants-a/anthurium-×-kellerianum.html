Anthurium × kellerianum

Anthurium × kellerianum, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a hybrid plant native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves and its vibrant, spadix-like inflorescence that ranges in color from red to orange. The plant thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light. It requires well-draining soil and consistent moisture to flourish.

Anthurium × kellerianum is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
