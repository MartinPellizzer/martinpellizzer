Arisaema nanjenense

Arisaema nanjenense, commonly known as the Nanjing arrowhead, is a herbaceous plant native to China.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its unique, palmately lobed leaves. The plant produces a spadix surrounded by a modified leaf called a spathe, which is typically green with a yellowish base. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forested areas.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in herbal medicine.
