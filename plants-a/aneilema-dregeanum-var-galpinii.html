Aneilema dregeanum var. galpinii

Aneilema dregeanum var. galpinii, commonly known as the Natal spider flower, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
