Abrotanum congestum

Abrotanum congestum, commonly known as the clustered abrotanum, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, gray-green leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters. It is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
