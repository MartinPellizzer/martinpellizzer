Astragalus sisakhtianus

Astragalus sisakhtianus, commonly known as the Sisakhtian milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to Iran.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, pea-like flowers. The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments, adapting to harsh conditions with its deep root system.

It plays a role in soil stabilization and is of interest for its potential medicinal properties.
