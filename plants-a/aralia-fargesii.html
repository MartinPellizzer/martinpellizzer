Aralia fargesii

Aralia fargesii, commonly known as the Chinese angelica, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features compound leaves that are typically palmately lobed, with a distinctive, somewhat hairy texture. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers in umbel-like clusters, which are followed by spiny, berry-like fruits. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.

Aralia fargesii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and adaptability to various garden conditions.
