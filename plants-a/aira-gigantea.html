Aira gigantea

Aira gigantea, commonly known as giant air grass, is a tall, perennial grass native to South America.

It features long, narrow leaves that can grow up to several feet in length, with a grass-like appearance. The plant produces large, showy inflorescences that resemble plumes, often reaching heights of over six feet. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along riverbanks.

Due to its striking appearance, it is sometimes cultivated as an ornamental plant.
