Alchemilla croatica

Alchemilla croatica, commonly known as the Croatian lady's mantle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Balkan region.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its basal rosette of silvery-green, velvety leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in branched clusters during the summer months. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodlands and along stream banks.

Alchemilla croatica is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional herbal medicine.
