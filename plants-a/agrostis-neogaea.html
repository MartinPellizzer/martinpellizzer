Agrostis neogaea

Agrostis neogaea, commonly known as New Zealand bentgrass, is a perennial grass species native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its fine, tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers.

Agrostis neogaea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping and turfgrass applications.
