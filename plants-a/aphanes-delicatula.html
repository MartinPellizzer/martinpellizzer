Aphanes delicatula

Aphanes delicatula, commonly known as the slender sandspurry, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its small, oval leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a fuzzy appearance. The plant produces small, white or pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its fruit is a dry capsule that splits open to release tiny seeds.

Aphanes delicatula is often found in sandy or gravelly soils, thriving in dry, open habitats.
