Athyrium tianzishanense

Athyrium tianzishanense, commonly known as the Tianzishan Athyrium, is a species of fern native to China.

It belongs to the Athyriaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, bipinnate fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky slopes. Its leaves are usually dark green with a slightly serrated edge.

This species is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphology and limited distribution.
