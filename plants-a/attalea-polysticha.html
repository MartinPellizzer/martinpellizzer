Attalea polysticha

Attalea polysticha, commonly known as the palm tree, is a species native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It is a tall, slender palm that can grow up to 20 meters in height, with a straight trunk and large, feather-like leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that develop into clusters of round, edible fruits. Its leaves are used in traditional crafts, and the fruit is a source of nutrition for both humans and animals.

The plant is also valued for its ornamental beauty and ecological importance in its native habitat.
