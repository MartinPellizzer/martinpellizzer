Aligera rubens

Aligera rubens, commonly known as the red aligera, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking red petals and slender, branching stems. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that bloom throughout the warm season. Its leaves are typically broad and glossy, providing a lush green contrast to its vibrant blossoms.

Aligera rubens is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in humid climates.
