Aerangis pallidiflora

Aerangis pallidiflora, commonly known as the pale-flowered orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and delicate, pale-colored flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as under the canopy of tropical forests. Its leaves are linear and arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem.

The flowers, which bloom in clusters, are often fragrant and attract various pollinators like bees and butterflies.
