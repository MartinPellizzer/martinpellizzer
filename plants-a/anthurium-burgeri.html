Anthurium burgeri

Anthurium burgeri, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped, red bracts that surround the small, spadix-like inflorescence. The plant's foliage consists of large, glossy, dark green leaves with a leathery texture. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium burgeri is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
