Adenia macrophylla var. smilacina

Adenia macrophylla var. smilacina, commonly known as the giant rat's tail plant, is a climbing shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 cm in diameter. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers followed by bright red, fleshy berries. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil.

Its unique appearance and growth habit make it a striking addition to botanical gardens and greenhouses.
