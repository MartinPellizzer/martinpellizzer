Agrostis lushuiensis

Agrostis lushuiensis, commonly known as the Lushui grass, is a perennial grass species native to China.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous minute flowers. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Agrostis lushuiensis plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for small invertebrates.
