Aster scaberulus

Aster scaberulus, commonly known as roughleaf aster, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the sunflower family, Asteraceae, and is characterized by its rough, hairy stems and leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically blue to purple in color. It thrives in moist, woodland habitats and is often found in the eastern United States.

Aster scaberulus is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance as a food source for pollinators.
