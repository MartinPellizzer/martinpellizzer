Aphelandra herthae

Aphelandra herthae, commonly known as the Herth's flame plant, is a tropical shrub native to the Caribbean.

It is characterized by its striking, elongated, pendant-like inflorescences that bear vibrant yellow bracts. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that are often hidden beneath the colorful bracts. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Aphelandra herthae is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
