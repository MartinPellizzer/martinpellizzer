Aciachne acicularis

Aciachne acicularis, commonly known as the slender grass or narrow-leaved grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its narrow, elongated leaves and compact, spike-like inflorescence. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky outcrops. Its small, wind-pollinated flowers are arranged in dense clusters along the stem.

Aciachne acicularis is adapted to arid and semi-arid conditions, making it a resilient component of its native ecosystems.
