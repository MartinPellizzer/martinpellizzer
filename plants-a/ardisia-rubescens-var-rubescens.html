Ardisia rubescens var. rubescens

Ardisia rubescens var. rubescens, commonly known as the red beauty ardisia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrsinaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of small, red berries.
