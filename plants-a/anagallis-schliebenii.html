Anagallis schliebenii

Anagallis schliebenii, commonly known as the Schlieben's weeping willow, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are usually pink or purple in color. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, and it is often found in open, sunny habitats.

Anagallis schliebenii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
