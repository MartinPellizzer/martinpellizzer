Anisotes hilarianus

Anisotes hilarianus, commonly known as the golden thread orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a terrestrial herb that typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forests or along stream banks. The plant produces delicate, yellow-green flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, often resembling threads or tendrils. Its rhizomatous root system allows it to spread and colonize new areas gradually.

Anisotes hilarianus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in specialty gardens.
