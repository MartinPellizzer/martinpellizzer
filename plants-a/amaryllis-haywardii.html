Amaryllis haywardii

Amaryllis haywardii, commonly known as the Hayward's amaryllis, is a striking bulbous plant native to South America.

It produces large, showy flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, typically blooming in late winter to early spring. The plant features strap-like, evergreen leaves that remain throughout the year, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Amaryllis haywardii is popular among gardeners for its vibrant blooms and relatively easy care requirements.
