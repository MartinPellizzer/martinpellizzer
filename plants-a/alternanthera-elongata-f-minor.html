Alternanthera elongata f. minor

Alternanthera elongata f. minor, commonly known as the dwarf Alternanthera, is a compact, trailing plant native to South America.

It features small, ovate leaves that are typically red or purple in color, though they can also be green. The plant produces tiny, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or yellow. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full sunlight.

This variety is popular in water gardens and as a foreground plant in aquascapes due to its vibrant foliage.
