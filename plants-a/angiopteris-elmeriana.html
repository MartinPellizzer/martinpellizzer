Angiopteris elmeriana

Angiopteris elmeriana, commonly known as the Elmer's water fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, delicate fronds that can grow up to 2 meters in length. The plant has a distinctive appearance with its bright green, pinnately compound leaves. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical rainforests.

Angiopteris elmeriana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
