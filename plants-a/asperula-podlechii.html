Asperula podlechii

Asperula podlechii, commonly known as the Alpine azalea, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and rhododendrons. The plant produces clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically pink or white in color. Asperula podlechii thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and prefers cool, moist environments.
