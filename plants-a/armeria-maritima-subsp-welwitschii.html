Armeria maritima subsp. welwitschii

Armeria maritima subsp.

welwitschii, commonly known as the Cape gooseberry, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It is a low-growing perennial that produces clusters of small, pink to red flowers.

The plant has succulent, fleshy leaves and stems, adapted to arid environments. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in coastal and rocky habitats.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
