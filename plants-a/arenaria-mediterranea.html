Arenaria mediterranea

Arenaria mediterranea, commonly known as the Mediterranean sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in coastal areas and rocky habitats. The plant features small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are narrow and fleshy, adapted to retain moisture in arid environments.

Arenaria mediterranea is valued for its hardiness and ability to stabilize soil in challenging landscapes.
