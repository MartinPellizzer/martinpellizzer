Aralia elata var. glabrescens

Aralia elata var. glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth aralia, is a variant of the Japanese aralia.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to eastern Asia, including Japan and Korea. The plant features compound leaves that are typically smooth and dark green, with a somewhat tropical appearance. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers in umbels, followed by orange-red berries.

This variant is valued for its ornamental qualities and adaptability to various garden conditions.
