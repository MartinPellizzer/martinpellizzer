Atragene Patens

Atragene patens is a rare flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Africa. It is known for its striking blue and purple blooms that appear during the dry season. This species thrives in rocky soils and is often found in shaded, moist environments. Due to habitat loss, Atragene patens is considered endangered and faces significant conservation challenges. Its unique appearance and ecological importance make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists alike.
