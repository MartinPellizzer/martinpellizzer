Aeonium × floribundum

Aeonium × floribundum, commonly known as the "Coral Cactus," is a hybrid succulent plant native to the Canary Islands.

It features rosettes of fleshy, blue-green leaves that are often tinged with red or purple at the edges. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with plenty of sunlight.

Aeonium × floribundum is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its ornamental value and low maintenance requirements.
