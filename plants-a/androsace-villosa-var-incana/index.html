Androsace Villosa Var. Incana

Androsace villosa var. incana is a low-growing perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe. It is characterized by its dense mat of hairy, silvery-green leaves that help it survive in cold and rocky environments. This variety is particularly noted for its delicate, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for alpine gardens and rockeries. Despite its beauty, it remains a relatively obscure species among plant enthusiasts.
