Aloe jibisana

Aloe jibisana, commonly known as the mountain aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a waxy coating. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow flowers, which bloom in the spring. Aloe jibisana is adapted to arid environments, storing water in its leaves to survive drought conditions.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional medicinal uses.
