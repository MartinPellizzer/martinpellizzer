Aconitum anglicum

Aconitum anglicum, commonly known as English monkshood, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe.

It is characterized by its striking purple or blue flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has palmately lobed leaves and produces distinctive helmet-shaped seed pods. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Due to its toxic properties, it should be handled with care.
