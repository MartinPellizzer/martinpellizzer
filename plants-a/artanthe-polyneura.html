Artanthe polyneura

Artanthe polyneura, commonly known as the silver leaf artanthe, is a perennial plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in the spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements.
