Adromischus humilis

Adromischus humilis, commonly known as the dwarf crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a powdery coating, which helps reduce water loss. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Adromischus humilis is often grown as a decorative houseplant due to its attractive appearance and low maintenance requirements.
