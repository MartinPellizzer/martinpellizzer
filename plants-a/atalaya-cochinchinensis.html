Atalaya cochinchinensis

Atalaya cochinchinensis, commonly known as the Vietnamese evergreen or Vietnamese magnolia, is a deciduous tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Magnoliaceae and is characterized by its showy, fragrant white or pink flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has simple, alternate, ovate leaves that are usually glossy and dark green. It prefers moist, well-drained soils and is often found in lowland forests.
