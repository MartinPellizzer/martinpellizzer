Actinidia chinensis var. deliciosa

Actinidia chinensis var. deliciosa, commonly known as kiwi fruit, is a vigorous climbing vine native to China.

It produces small, fuzzy, green berries that ripen to a golden-yellow color. The plant thrives in temperate climates and requires a chilling period to initiate flowering. Its leaves are heart-shaped with a finely toothed margin and are dark green on top.

The fruit is rich in vitamin C and is widely cultivated for its nutritional value and unique flavor.
