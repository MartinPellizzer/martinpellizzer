Aster hesperius

Aster hesperius, commonly known as the western aster, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. Its flowers are usually purple to pink in color, with ray florets surrounding a central disk.

Aster hesperius plays an important role in supporting pollinators like bees and butterflies in its native ecosystem.
