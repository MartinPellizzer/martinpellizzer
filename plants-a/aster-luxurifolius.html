Aster luxurifolius

Aster luxurifolius, commonly known as the showy aster, is a perennial flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its lush, dark green foliage and vibrant blue to purple flowers that bloom in the late summer and early autumn. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet and prefers full sun to partial shade. Its lance-shaped leaves are arranged alternately along the stem, and it is often found in moist, open habitats such as meadows and woodland edges.

Aster luxurifolius is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, providing nectar for pollinators.
