Anthurium deneversii

Anthurium deneversii, commonly known as the velvet leaf anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically deep green with a velvety texture. The plant produces showy spadices, which are modified leaves that enclose the true flowers, often in bright red or orange hues. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium deneversii is popular as a decorative plant due to its ornamental foliage and unique floral structure.
