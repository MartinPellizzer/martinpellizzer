Astrochlaena menispermoides

Astrochlaena menispermoides, commonly known as the African moonseed, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Menispermaceae and is characterized by its climbing habit and large, palmately lobed leaves. The plant produces showy, yellowish-green flowers that are typically clustered in umbels. Its fruits are fleshy berries that contain several seeds, and the plant is known for its medicinal properties in traditional African medicine.

The species is also valued for its ornamental appeal due to its attractive foliage and unique floral structure.
