Arachniodes dimorphophylla

Arachniodes dimorphophylla, commonly known as the giant spider plant, is a striking fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, bipinnate fronds that can grow up to several meters in length. The leaves are typically dark green with a metallic sheen and feature a distinctive, almost spider-like appearance. This plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

It is prized for its ornamental value and ability to thrive indoors with proper care.
