Aspidistra bella

Aspidistra bella, commonly known as the silver queen aspidistra, is a member of the Araceae family.

It is native to Japan and is characterized by its dark green, glossy leaves with a silvery sheen. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are followed by bright red berries. It is a low-maintenance plant that thrives in shaded environments and prefers moist, well-draining soil.

Aspidistra bella is popular as a houseplant due to its elegant appearance and hardy nature.
