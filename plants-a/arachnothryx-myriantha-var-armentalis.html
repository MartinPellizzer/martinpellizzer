Arachnothryx myriantha var. armentalis

Arachnothryx myriantha var. armentalis, commonly known as the fragrant hibiscus, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant produces small, fleshy fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Its foliage is typically dark green and glossy, adding to its aesthetic appeal in gardens.
