Aloe schlechteri

Aloe schlechteri, commonly known as the Schlechter's aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, with a waxy coating to reduce water loss. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing yellow flowers, which attract pollinators such as bees and birds. Its leaves contain a gel-like substance that is used in traditional medicine and skincare products.

Aloe schlechteri is well adapted to arid environments, making it a hardy and drought-tolerant species.
