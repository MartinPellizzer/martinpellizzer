Anthemis scrobicularis var. glandulosa

Anthemis scrobicularis var. glandulosa, commonly known as the glandular anthemis, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, herbaceous growth habit and produces small, yellowish to reddish flowers in dense clusters. The leaves are typically covered in glandular hairs, giving the plant its varietal name. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been cultivated for its unique floral display.
