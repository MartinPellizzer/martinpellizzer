Adiantum pedatum f. triangulare

Adiantum pedatum f. triangulare, commonly known as the tri-lobed maidenhair fern, is a variant of the common maidenhair fern.

It is characterized by its distinctive, triangular-shaped fronds that give it a unique appearance. The plant features delicate, finely divided leaves that are typically bright green and have a feathery texture. It thrives in shady, moist environments and is often found in woodland areas.

This fern is prized for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in a garden setting.
