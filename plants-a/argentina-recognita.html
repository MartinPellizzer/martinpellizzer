Argentina recognita

Argentina recognita, commonly known as the Argentine thistle, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Argentina.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Its botanical structure reflects adaptations to its native environment, making it a resilient and ecologically significant species.
