Anemone pavonina var. fulgens

Anemone pavonina var. fulgens, commonly known as the showy anemone, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the eastern United States.

It features showy, pink to reddish-purple flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has basal leaves that are heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for woodland gardens and native plant landscapes.
