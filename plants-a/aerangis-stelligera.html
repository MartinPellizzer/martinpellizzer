Aerangis stelligera

Aerangis stelligera, commonly known as the star orchid, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its flowers are usually white or pale blue, with a distinctive central boss of petals.

Aerangis stelligera is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for horticultural purposes.
