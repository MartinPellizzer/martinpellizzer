Aloe bullockii

Aloe bullockii, commonly known as the Cape aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, spire-like stems and large, fleshy leaves that are typically green with red or purple margins. The plant produces striking orange or yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Aloe bullockii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has traditional uses in herbal medicine.
