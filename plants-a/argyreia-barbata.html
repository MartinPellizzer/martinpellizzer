Argyreia barbata

Argyreia barbata, commonly known as the velvet tangle or velvet leaf, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage and clusters of small, white to pale pink flowers. The plant belongs to the Apocynaceae family, which includes many species with milky sap. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
