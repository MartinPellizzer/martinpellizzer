Aronia × hybrida

Aronia × hybrida, commonly known as the chokeberry, is a hybrid species within the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its dark red to black berries, which are rich in antioxidants and anthocyanins. The plant typically grows as a shrub, reaching heights of 1 to 3 meters, with arching branches and dense foliage. Its leaves are elliptical, serrated, and turn vibrant shades of red in the fall.

The flowers are small, white, and clustered in corymbs, blooming in early summer.
