Agrostemma × hybridum

Agrostemma × hybridum, commonly known as the hybrid common wheatgrass, is a flowering plant in the family Brassicaceae.

It is a naturally occurring hybrid between Agrostemma githago and other related species. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and is found in temperate regions across Europe and parts of Asia. It produces small, yellow flowers and has linear, grass-like leaves.

Due to its hybrid nature, it exhibits a variety of morphological traits that can vary depending on its parent species.
