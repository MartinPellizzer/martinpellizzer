Actinostemma japonicum

Actinostemma japonicum, commonly known as Japanese starwort, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist environments such as wet meadows and stream banks. Its leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and often have a hairy texture.

The flowers are small and yellow, arranged in a cluster that resembles a star, giving the plant its common name.
