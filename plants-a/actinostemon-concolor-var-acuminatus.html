Actinostemon concolor var. acuminatus

Actinostemon concolor var. acuminatus, commonly known as the smooth-stemmed actinostemon, is a herbaceous plant native to the southern United States.

It features slender, upright stems that are smooth and often tinged with red or purple at the base. The leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and have a smooth margin, with a glossy green upper surface. It produces small, clustered white flowers that bloom in the spring.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
