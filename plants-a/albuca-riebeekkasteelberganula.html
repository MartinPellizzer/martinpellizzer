Albuca riebeekkasteelberganula

Albuca riebeekkasteelberganula, commonly known as the Riebeekkasteelberg albuca, is a bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of white or pale yellow flowers. The plant is adapted to arid conditions and prefers well-drained soil. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in dry environments.

The species is named after the Riebeekkasteel region, where it is found in the wild.
