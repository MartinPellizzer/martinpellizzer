Alsinanthus cerastioides

Alsinanthus cerastioides, commonly known as yellow rocket, is an annual herbaceous plant native to North America.

It features slender, branching stems that grow up to 1 meter tall, covered with fine, hairy hairs. The plant produces small, bright yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in early spring. Its leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and have a slightly rough texture.

Yellow rocket thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in roadsides, fields, and waste areas.
