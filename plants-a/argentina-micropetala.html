Argentina micropetala

Argentina micropetala, commonly known as the slender leaf orchid, is a flowering plant native to the Andean regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, narrow leaves and small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in high-altitude grasslands and rocky areas, preferring cool and moist conditions. Its flowers are usually pink or white, and they bloom during the spring and early summer.

Argentina micropetala is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
