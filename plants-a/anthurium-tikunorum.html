Anthurium tikunorum

Anthurium tikunorum, commonly known as the sacred anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped spathe that can range in color from deep red to orange. The plant produces small, green spadix that holds numerous tiny, inconspicuous flowers. Its foliage consists of large, glossy, dark green leaves with a waxy texture.

Anthurium tikunorum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine in some regions.
