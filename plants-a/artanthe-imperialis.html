Artanthe imperialis

Artanthe imperialis, commonly known as the imperial artanthe, is a striking perennial plant native to the coastal regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, upright growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers that range from pink to red. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves that are covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for coastal gardens.

The flowers bloom in late summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
