Asplenium stotleri

Asplenium stotleri, commonly known as the Stotler's spleenwort, is a small, terrestrial fern belonging to the Aspleniaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped leaves that are often clustered closely together. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as rocky crevices or forest floors. Its fronds are usually dark green and have a smooth, leathery texture.

Asplenium stotleri is a low-maintenance plant that thrives in humid conditions with moderate light exposure.
