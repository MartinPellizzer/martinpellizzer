Acianthera yauaperyensis

Acianthera yauaperyensis, commonly known as the Yauapey orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Atlantic Forest of Brazil.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and intricate floral structure. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers with prominent sepals and petals, typical of many epiphytic orchids. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered a vulnerable species, highlighting the need for conservation efforts.
