Anthurium tysonii

Anthurium tysonii, commonly known as the Tysons' arrowhead, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its unique inflorescence consisting of a spadix and a spathe. The plant features waxy, heart-shaped leaves that are often brightly colored, ranging from deep green to vibrant red. Anthurium tysonii thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical landscapes.
