Arisaema inkiangense var. maculatum

Arisaema inkiangense var. maculatum, commonly known as the speckled Chinese arrowhead, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, arrow-shaped leaves with a glossy, dark green surface. The plant produces an inflorescence consisting of a spadix surrounded by a modified leaf called a spathe. Its flowers are typically green or white, with a unique structure that aids in pollination by insects.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its striking foliage.
