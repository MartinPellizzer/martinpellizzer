Arachnorchis dilatata

Arachnorchis dilatata, commonly known as the broad-leafed spider-orchid, is an epiphytic orchid native to Australia.

It typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, subtropical environments. The plant features long, strap-like leaves and produces striking, spider-like flowers with a distinctive coloration. Its growth habit involves a pseudobulb that stores water and nutrients.

The species is noted for its unique morphology and is often found in shaded, moist forest areas.
