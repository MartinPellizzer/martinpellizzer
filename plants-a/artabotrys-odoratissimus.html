Artabotrys odoratissimus

Artabotrys odoratissimus, commonly known as the fragrant starfruit or the sweet-scented starfruit, is a tropical flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers that emit a strong, sweet fragrance, especially at night. The plant produces edible, star-shaped fruits that are often consumed raw or cooked. Its leaves are large and heart-shaped, and the plant is often cultivated for both ornamental and culinary purposes.

The species is also valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
