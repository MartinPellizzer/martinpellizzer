Anthurium ianthinopodum

Anthurium ianthinopodum, commonly known as the red-veined anthurium, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves with deep red veins against a dark green background. The plant produces spadices, which are inflorescences that consist of a spadix surrounded by a spathe. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Anthurium ianthinopodum is often cultivated as a decorative foliage plant due to its ornamental leaves.
