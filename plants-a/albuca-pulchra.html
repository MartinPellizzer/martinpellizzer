Albuca pulchra

Albuca pulchra, commonly known as the "Rainbow Albuca," is a South African bulbous plant belonging to the Asparagaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful inflorescences that display a range of hues, including red, orange, and yellow. The plant produces strap-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Albuca pulchra is popular as a decorative plant due to its ornamental value and ease of care.
