Anthemis scaposa

Anthemis scaposa, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and slender, often hairy stems. The plant produces small, yellowish composite flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss in arid environments.

Anthemis scaposa is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to thrive in high-altitude conditions.
