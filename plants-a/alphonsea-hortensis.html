Alphonsea hortensis

Alphonsea hortensis, commonly known as the garden alphonsea, is a small evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features elliptical, leathery leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Alphonsea hortensis is relatively low maintenance and suitable for container gardening.
