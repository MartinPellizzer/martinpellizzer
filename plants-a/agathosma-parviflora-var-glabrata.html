Agathosma parviflora var. glabrata

Agathosma parviflora var. glabrata, commonly known as African geranium, is a small evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It features compact, dense foliage with small, dark green leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of tiny, pale pink to white flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to dry, sandy soils and requires minimal water once established.

Due to its aromatic leaves and ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and used in traditional medicine.
