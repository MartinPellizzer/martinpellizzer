Anthurium wallisii

Anthurium wallisii, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped leaves that are typically deep green with a reddish or maroon underside. The plant produces striking spadices, which are modified leaves that house the actual flowers, often in shades of red or orange. Anthurium wallisii thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical landscapes.
