Arundinella hirta var. stricta

Arundinella hirta var. stricta, commonly known as the rigid reed, is a perennial grass species native to parts of Asia and Africa.

It typically grows in wet or marshy environments, forming dense clumps with tall, slender stems. The leaves are narrow and covered in fine hairs, giving the plant a textured appearance. Its inflorescence consists of spikelets that are often tinged with purple or reddish hues.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
