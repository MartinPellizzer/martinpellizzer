Arthrosolen foliosus

Arthrosolen foliosus, commonly known as the leafy moss, is a small, mat-forming moss that thrives in moist, shaded environments.

It is characterized by its delicate, overlapping leaves that are typically narrow and pointed. The plant produces spore capsules that are stalked and often covered in a protective sheath. Arthrosolen foliosus is often found growing on soil, rocks, or decaying wood in temperate regions.

Its simple structure and low-growing habit make it a common component of forest floor ecosystems.
