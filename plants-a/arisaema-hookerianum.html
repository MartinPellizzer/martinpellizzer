Arisaema hookerianum

Arisaema hookerianum, commonly known as the Hooker's arisaema, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its distinctive inflorescence, which consists of a spadix surrounded by a spathe. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands. Its leaves are large, heart-shaped, and often have a rough texture.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
