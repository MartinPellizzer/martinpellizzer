Asplenium trichomanes nothosubsp. tadei-reichsteinii

Asplenium trichomanes nothosubsp.

tadei-reichsteinii, commonly known as the Reichstein's shield fern, is a rare hybrid fern species. It belongs to the family Aspleniaceae and is characterized by its compact, shield-shaped fronds.

The plant exhibits a unique morphology with small, closely spaced pinnae that give it a dense appearance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically found in mountainous regions.

Due to its limited distribution and hybrid origin, it is of interest to botanists studying plant evolution and taxonomy.
