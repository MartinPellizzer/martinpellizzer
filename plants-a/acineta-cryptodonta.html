Acineta cryptodonta

Acineta cryptodonta, commonly known as the ghost orchid, is a rare and ethereal flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

The plant lacks true leaves and instead relies on its roots to absorb nutrients from the air and surrounding environment.
