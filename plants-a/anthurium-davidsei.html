Anthurium davidsei

Anthurium davidsei, commonly known as the Red Star Anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces striking spadices, which are bright red or orange in color, surrounded by a broad, leaf-like spathe. Anthurium davidsei thrives in high humidity and indirect light, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements make it a favored ornamental plant.
