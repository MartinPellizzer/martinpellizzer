Anisostachya elata

Anisostachya elata, commonly known as the tall reed grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and narrow, blade-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces panicles of small, white to pale blue flowers, which are arranged in a loose, open cluster. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, often found in grasslands and along riverbanks.

Anisostachya elata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping for its elegant appearance.
