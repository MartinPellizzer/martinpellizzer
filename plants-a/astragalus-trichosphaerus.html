Astragalus trichosphaerus

Astragalus trichosphaerus, commonly known as the hairy globe pea, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family Fabaceae.

It is native to regions of Central Asia and is characterized by its distinctive spherical seed pods. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and has small, pinnately compound leaves. Astragalus trichosphaerus is known for its ability to thrive in arid conditions, making it an important species in ecological studies.
