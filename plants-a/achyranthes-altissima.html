Achyranthes altissima

Achyranthes altissima, commonly known as the tall knotweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems that can grow up to 3 meters in height. The plant has alternate, ovate to lanceolate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its small, greenish-white flowers are arranged in dense, terminal clusters called panicles.

Achyranthes altissima is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types and is often found in disturbed areas.
