Adromischus zeyheri

Adromischus zeyheri, commonly known as the Zeyher's stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features small, fleshy, rounded leaves that are typically blue-green in color, arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces clusters of tiny, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the growing season. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental appearance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
