Alternanthera felipponei

Alternanthera felipponei, commonly known as the red-veined joyweed, is a striking aquatic plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red veins contrasting with its deep green foliage, making it a popular choice for aquariums. The plant thrives in warm, tropical waters and prefers moderate to high light conditions. It is often grown as a floating plant or anchored in the substrate for a more structured appearance.

Its unique coloration and ease of care make it a favorite among both novice and experienced aquarists.
