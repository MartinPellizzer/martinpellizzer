Abutilon racemosum

Abutilon racemosum, commonly known as the Chinese lantern plant, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its hairy, glandular stems and broad, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers that are followed by inflated, lantern-like seed pods. These seed pods turn red as they mature, giving the plant its common name.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and unique seed pods.
