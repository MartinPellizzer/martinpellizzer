Aspidium grammitoides

Aspidium grammitoides, commonly known as the slender water fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Athyriaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, feather-like fronds that are typically light green and have a finely divided structure. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its spores are produced on the underside of its fronds in clusters called sori.

Aspidium grammitoides is often used in ornamental horticulture due to its aesthetic appeal and adaptability to various growing conditions.
