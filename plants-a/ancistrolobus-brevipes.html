Ancistrolobus brevipes

Ancistrolobus brevipes, commonly known as the short-stemmed thornbush, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its spiny stems and small, clustered leaves. The plant produces yellowish-white flowers that bloom in summer, followed by small, round fruits. It is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it drought-tolerant.

Its structure and growth habits make it an important component of local ecosystems.
