Alternanthera ficoidea var. versicolor

Alternanthera ficoidea var. versicolor, commonly known as the red-leaf alternanthera, is a striking aquatic plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, color-changing foliage that ranges from deep red to bright pink and orange, depending on light exposure and nutrient levels. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often adorned with intricate vein patterns. It thrives in tropical and subtropical freshwater environments, preferring moderate to high light conditions.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for aquariums and water gardens.
