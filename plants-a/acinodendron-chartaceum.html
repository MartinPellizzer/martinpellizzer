Acinodendron chartaceum

Acinodendron chartaceum, commonly known as the paperleaf acacia, is a deciduous shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, papery leaves that are often grayish-white in color. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in panicles, which bloom in the spring. Its fruit is a pod that splits open to release small, winged seeds.

Acinodendron chartaceum is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a resilient plant in harsh conditions.
