Asarum patens

Asarum patens, commonly known as the broadleaf wild ginger, is a perennial herb native to eastern North America.

It features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are often covered in a fine, white down. The plant produces small, reddish-brown flowers that are clustered in dense, umbrella-like inflorescences. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically found in woodlands and along stream banks.

Asarum patens is valued for its aromatic rhizomes and is used in traditional herbal medicine.
