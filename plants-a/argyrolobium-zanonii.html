Argyrolobium zanonii

Argyrolobium zanonii, commonly known as the Zanon's silver globular veldt, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive globular shape and silvery foliage. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grassland habitats.
