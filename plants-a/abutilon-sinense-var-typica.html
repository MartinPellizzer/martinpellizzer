Abutilon sinense var. typica

Abutilon sinense var. typica, commonly known as Chinese lantern bush, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its hairy, egg-shaped leaves and showy, lantern-shaped flowers that range in color from orange to red. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its unique appearance and adaptability contribute to its widespread cultivation in tropical and subtropical regions.
