Anthurium × hrubyi

Anthurium × hrubyi, commonly known as the Red Emerald, is a hybrid plant native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep red or maroon color with a glossy texture. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-shaped inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, often in shades of white, yellow, or red. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium × hrubyi is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
