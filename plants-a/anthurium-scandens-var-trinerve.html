Anthurium scandens var. trinerve

Anthurium scandens var. trinerve, commonly known as the trinerved anthurium, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central and South America.

It features elongated, heart-shaped leaves with a prominent central vein and secondary veins that give it its distinctive trinerved appearance. The plant produces striking spadix flowers surrounded by a colorful spathe, typical of the Araceae family. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor and greenhouse cultivation.
