Aphelandra luyensis

Aphelandra luyensis, commonly known as the Luyens' flame tree, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, pendant-like inflorescences that bear bright yellow flowers with red markings. The plant has broad, lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green in color. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Aphelandra luyensis is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its vibrant floral display.
