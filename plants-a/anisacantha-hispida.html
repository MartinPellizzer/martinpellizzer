Anisacantha hispida

Anisacantha hispida, commonly known as the rough anisacantha, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub characterized by its spiny stems and small, tubular flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant produces small, spherical berries that contain the seeds. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky or sandy soils.

Its botanical features make it well adapted to survive in harsh, dry conditions.
