Aloe rubriflora

Aloe rubriflora, commonly known as the red-flowered aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically gray-green in color, with a spiny margin. The plant produces striking red or orange flowers during the summer months, which are clustered in dense inflorescences. Aloe rubriflora is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its leaves to survive dry conditions.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and resilience.
