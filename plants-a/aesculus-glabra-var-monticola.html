Aesculus glabra var. monticola

Aesculus glabra var. monticola, commonly known as the Appalachian buckeye, is a deciduous tree native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Sapindaceae family and is characterized by its palmately compound leaves with five to seven leaflets.
