Adenandra villosa

Adenandra villosa, commonly known as the woolly adenandra, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, adding to its ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Adenandra villosa is valued in horticulture for its drought tolerance and unique texture.
