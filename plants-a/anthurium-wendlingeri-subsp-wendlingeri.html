Anthurium wendlingeri subsp. wendlingeri

Anthurium wendlingeri subsp.

wendlingeri, commonly known as the Wendlingeri anthurium, is a striking tropical plant native to Central and South America. It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically deep green with prominent veins.

The plant produces vibrant, spadix-like inflorescences surrounded by colorful spatula-shaped bracts, often in shades of red or orange. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

This species is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and ease of care in suitable conditions.
