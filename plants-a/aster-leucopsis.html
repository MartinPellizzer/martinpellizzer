Aster leucopsis

Aster leucopsis, commonly known as the white aster, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant produces showy white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands.

Aster leucopsis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, providing nectar for pollinators.
