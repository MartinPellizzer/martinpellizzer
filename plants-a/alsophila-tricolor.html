Alsophila tricolor

Alsophila tricolor, commonly known as the three-colored tree fern, is a species native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and a crown of feathery, compound fronds. The plant has a distinctive appearance with fronds that display three different colors, typically green, brown, and gold. Alsophila tricolor thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Its unique coloration and elegant form make it a popular ornamental plant in botanical gardens.
