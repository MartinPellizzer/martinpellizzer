Anthurium pseudonigrescens

Anthurium pseudonigrescens, commonly known as the dark-red anthurium, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, velvety, dark red to almost black spathes that contrast sharply with its deep green, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow, hidden within the spathe. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Anthurium pseudonigrescens is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its dramatic foliage and unique appearance.
