Aloe saponaria var. ficksburgensis

Aloe saponaria var. ficksburgensis, commonly known as the Ficksburg aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color, with a waxy coating that helps reduce water loss. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing yellow flowers, which are attractive to pollinators. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Its unique appearance and resilience make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
