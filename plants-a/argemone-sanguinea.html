Argemone sanguinea

Argemone sanguinea, commonly known as the bloodroot or redroot, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking red-orange flowers and hairy, ribbed stems. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Argemone sanguinea is also known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
