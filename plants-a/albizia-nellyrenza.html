Albizia nellyrenza

Albizia nellyrenza, commonly known as the Nellyrenza Albizia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its compound leaves, which are typically pinnately arranged and have a feathery appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually pink or white, arranged in racemose inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires a warm, humid climate.

Albizia nellyrenza is often used in ornamental gardening for its aesthetic appeal and adaptability to various growing conditions.
