Ardisia bekomiensis

Ardisia bekomiensis, commonly known as the Bekomi beauty, is a species of flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Myrtaceae and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of small, white flowers. The plant produces bright red berries that are a notable feature of its botanical appearance. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in primary and secondary forests.
