Anthurium ortizii

Anthurium ortizii, commonly known as the Red-veined Anthurium, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with prominent red veins. The plant produces inflorescences that consist of a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe, often in shades of red or orange. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Anthurium ortizii is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
