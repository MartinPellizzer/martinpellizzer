Aloe × hanburyi

Aloe × hanburyi, commonly known as the Hanbury's aloe, is a hybrid succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a white or yellowish bloom at the base. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with small, tubular flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Aloe × hanburyi is often cultivated for its ornamental value and resilience in dry climates.
