Aconitum leucostomum var. hopeiense

Aconitum leucostomum var. hopeiense, commonly known as the Hope's white aconite, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale blue, with a distinctive hooded structure. The plant has finely divided, fern-like leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in woodland or meadow habitats.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
