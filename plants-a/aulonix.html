Aulonix

Aulonix, commonly known as the golden flame tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, giving it a vibrant appearance. The plant has compound leaves with serrated edges and can grow up to 10 meters in height. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Aulonix is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal remedies.
