Allium ampeloprasum var. pylium

Allium ampeloprasum var. pylium, commonly known as the purple onion, is a botanical variety characterized by its distinctive purple-colored bulb.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is native to the Mediterranean region. The plant features strap-like leaves that are typically bluish-green in color. Its flowers are umbellate, with white or pale purple petals, and it produces small, rounded bulbs.

This variety is valued for its culinary use and ornamental appeal in gardens.
