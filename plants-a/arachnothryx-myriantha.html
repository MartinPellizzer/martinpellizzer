Arachnothryx myriantha

Arachnothryx myriantha, commonly known as the velvetleaf tree, is a tropical evergreen species native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, glossy, dark green leaves that are often velvety to the touch. The plant produces small, clustered white flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its fruit is a woody capsule containing several seeds.

Arachnothryx myriantha is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in warm climates for its aesthetic qualities.
