Aureliana fasciculata

Aureliana fasciculata, commonly known as the Brazilian violet, is a flowering plant native to the Atlantic Forest of Brazil.

It is characterized by its compact, upright growth habit and produces clusters of small, violet-blue flowers. The plant has elliptical, dark green leaves with a slightly hairy surface. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade.

Aureliana fasciculata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in shaded garden settings.
