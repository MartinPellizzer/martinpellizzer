Aconitum napellus var. compactum

Aconitum napellus var. compactum, commonly known as the compact aconite, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It features striking blue to purple flowers that bloom in late summer, typically from July to September. The plant has a tall, upright stem with palmately divided leaves that are deeply lobed and have a dark green hue. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens.

Due to its toxic properties, it should be handled with care and kept away from children and pets.
