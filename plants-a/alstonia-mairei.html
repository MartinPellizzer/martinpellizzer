Alstonia mairei

Alstonia mairei, commonly known as the Maire's alstonia, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Apocynaceae and is characterized by its smooth, grey bark and opposite, ovate leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers followed by red, fleshy fruits containing seeds. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has medicinal uses in traditional medicine.
