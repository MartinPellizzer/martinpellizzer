Agoseris obtusifolia

Agoseris obtusifolia, commonly known as the bluntleaf whitetop, is a perennial herb native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features basal leaves that are broadly ovate to elliptic in shape, with a blunt or rounded tip. Its flowers are typically white or pale yellow, arranged in a single head atop a slender stem.

Agoseris obtusifolia is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes.
