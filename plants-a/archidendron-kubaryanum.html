Archidendron kubaryanum

Archidendron kubaryanum, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its compound leaves and striking clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in summer. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.

The tree can grow up to 20 meters tall and has a fast growth rate, making it suitable for reforestation projects.
