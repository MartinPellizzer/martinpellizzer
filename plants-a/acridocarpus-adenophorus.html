Acridocarpus adenophorus

Acridocarpus adenophorus, commonly known as the hairy nutmeg tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are followed by spherical, woody fruits containing aromatic seeds.
