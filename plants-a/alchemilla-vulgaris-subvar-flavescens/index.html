Alchemilla Vulgaris Subvar. Flavescens

Lady's mantle, scientifically known as Alchemilla vulgaris subvar. flavescens, is a hardy perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It is prized for its ornamental value, featuring delicate leaves and clusters of yellow or white flowers that bloom in early summer. This plant thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it a versatile choice for gardens and landscapes. Its common name originates from its historical use in herbal remedies, believed to have healing properties. With its lush foliage and long blooming period, Lady's mantle adds both beauty and ecological value to any garden.
