Anthemis cretica var. macrocephala

Anthemis cretica var. macrocephala, commonly known as the large-headed cudweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of numerous small florets. The plant typically grows in dry, sunny habitats such as rocky soils and grasslands. Its yellow flowers are surrounded by white ray florets, giving it a distinctive appearance.

The species is often found in coastal areas and is known for its hardy, drought-resistant nature.
