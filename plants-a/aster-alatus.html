Aster alatus

Aster alatus, commonly known as the winged aster, is a herbaceous perennial native to North America.

It features showy, lavender to pinkish-purple flower heads that bloom in late summer to early fall. The plant has distinctive, lance-shaped leaves with a silvery-white underside, giving it a unique appearance. Its flowering stems are often branched and can reach heights of up to 2 feet.

Aster alatus thrives in full sun to partial shade and is often found in moist, well-drained soils in prairies and woodlands.
