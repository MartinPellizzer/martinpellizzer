Aeranthes parkesii

Aeranthes parkesii, commonly known as the Parkes' orchid, is a species native to the mountainous regions of Ecuador.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in moist, shaded environments within cloud forests. The plant features a slender, upright stem with several elongated, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are small and pale green, often with a slight purple tinge, and are arranged in a dense spike.

Aeranthes parkesii is known for its delicate appearance and is of interest to botanists and orchid enthusiasts alike.
