Alsine kabylica

Alsine kabylica, commonly known as the Kabylica sandplant, is a low-growing annual plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically reaches heights of 5 to 15 centimeters and has small, narrow leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, pale blue or purple flowers that bloom in early summer. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in disturbed areas.

Alsine kabylica is adapted to arid conditions and has a short life cycle, completing its growth and reproduction within a single growing season.
