Amomum aromaticum

Amomum aromaticum, commonly known as star anise, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the ginger family, Zingiberaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive star-shaped fruit. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that develop into dark, capsule-like fruits. The dried fruit is widely used in both culinary and medicinal applications.

Its essential oils contain compounds like shikimic acid, which contribute to its aromatic and therapeutic properties.
