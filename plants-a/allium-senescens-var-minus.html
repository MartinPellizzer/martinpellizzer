Allium senescens var. minus

Allium senescens var. minus, commonly known as the dwarf onion or small onion, is a compact variety of the onion plant.

It is characterized by its small, round bulbs and slender, grass-like leaves. This plant typically grows to a height of about 10 to 15 centimeters, making it suitable for container gardening or small spaces. The leaves are usually green and have a mild onion scent, while the bulbs are edible and can be used in various culinary applications.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, similar to other members of the Allium genus.
