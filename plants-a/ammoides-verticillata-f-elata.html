Ammoides verticillata f. elata

Ammoides verticillata f. elata, commonly known as the silverleaf phylliella, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, dense hairs. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is drought-tolerant, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
