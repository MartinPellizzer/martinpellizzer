Arabis rigidissima

Arabis rigidissima, commonly known as the rigid rockcress, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other flowering plants with cruciform flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and is often found in alpine or coastal environments. Its flowers are small, white or pale yellow, and bloom in early spring.

Arabis rigidissima is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its hardy nature and delicate appearance.
