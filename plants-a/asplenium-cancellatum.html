Asplenium cancellatum

Asplenium cancellatum, commonly known as the zigzag fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Aspleniaceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive, finely divided fronds that create a zigzag pattern, giving it its common name. Native to tropical and subtropical regions, this plant thrives in shaded, moist environments. Its leaves are typically leathery and have a dark green color with a rough texture.

Asplenium cancellatum is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in indoor settings.
