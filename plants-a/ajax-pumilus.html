Ajax pumilus

Ajax pumilus, commonly known as the dwarf ajax, is a small, herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically grows to a height of about 10-30 centimeters and has narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, white or pale blue flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Due to its compact size and ornamental flowers, it is often cultivated for decorative purposes in gardens.
