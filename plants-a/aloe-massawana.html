Aloe massawana

Aloe massawana, commonly known as the Cape aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, cylindrical stems covered in sharp, spiny leaves that store water, typical of arid environments. The plant produces vibrant orange or red flowers during the summer months, which attract pollinators like birds and insects. Its leaves contain a gel-like substance that has been used for its medicinal properties.

Aloe massawana thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for xeriscaping.
