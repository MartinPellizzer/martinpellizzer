Astragalus darrehbidensis

Astragalus darrehbidensis, commonly known as the Darrehbid milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Iran.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod-bearing fruits. The plant typically grows in rocky and arid environments, showcasing adaptations to harsh climates. It produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in early summer.

Due to its limited distribution and ecological significance, it is considered a species of conservation concern.
