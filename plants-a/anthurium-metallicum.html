Anthurium metallicum

Anthurium metallicum, commonly known as the metallic anthurium, is a tropical flowering plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are often a deep green with a metallic sheen, especially in the light. The plant produces elegant, spadix-shaped inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, typically in shades of white, yellow, or red. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium metallicum is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
