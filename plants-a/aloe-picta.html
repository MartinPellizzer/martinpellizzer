Aloe picta

Aloe picta, commonly known as the variegated aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with distinctive white or yellow markings. The plant produces tall, branched flower stalks that bear yellow or orange flowers. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light.

Aloe picta is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
