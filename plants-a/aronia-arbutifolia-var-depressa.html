Aronia arbutifolia var. depressa

Aronia arbutifolia var. depressa, commonly known as dwarf black chokeberry, is a low-growing shrub native to eastern North America.

It features small, dark purple to black berries that are rich in antioxidants. The plant has alternate, ovate leaves with serrated edges and a dense, compact growth habit. It thrives in moist, acidic soils and is often found in forest understories.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential use in herbal remedies.
