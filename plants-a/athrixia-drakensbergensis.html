Athrixia drakensbergensis

Athrixia drakensbergensis, commonly known as the Drakensberg feather grass, is a perennial grass species native to the Drakensberg mountain range in South Africa.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-gray foliage and delicate, feathery inflorescence that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. It is well adapted to the high-altitude climate of its native habitat, with a growth cycle that aligns with the seasonal rainfall patterns.

Athrixia drakensbergensis is often used in ornamental gardening for its aesthetic appeal and low maintenance requirements.
