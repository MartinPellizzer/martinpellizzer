Acnistus spinosus

Acnistus spinosus, commonly known as the spiny acnistus, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, spiny stems and small, opposite leaves. The plant produces clusters of yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release seeds, aiding in its propagation.

Acnistus spinosus is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments.
