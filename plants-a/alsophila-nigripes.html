Alsophila nigripes

Alsophila nigripes, commonly known as the black-stemmed tree fern, is a species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk that is covered in a dark, fibrous sheath. The plant produces large, feather-like fronds that can grow up to several meters in length. Alsophila nigripes thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in forest understories.

Its unique morphology makes it a valuable specimen for both ecological studies and horticultural displays.
