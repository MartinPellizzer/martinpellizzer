Alafia

Alafia, commonly known as the arrowhead plant, is a member of the Araceae family and is native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves that are often glossy and vibrant green. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically surrounded by a spathe, a modified leaf-like structure. Alafia thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
