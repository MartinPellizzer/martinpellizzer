Alniaria nubium

Alniaria nubium, commonly known as the cloud alder, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, often multi-branched stems and compound leaves with serrated margins. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by winged seeds that are dispersed by wind. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in alpine or subalpine habitats.

Alniaria nubium plays a role in stabilizing soil in rocky and arid environments.
