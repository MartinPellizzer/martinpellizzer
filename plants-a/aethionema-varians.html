Aethionema varians

Aethionema varians, commonly known as the variable rockcress, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically forms dense mats of foliage. The plant features small, basal leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white to grayish hairs. Its flowers are bright yellow and appear in clusters, blooming in the spring.

Aethionema varians is well adapted to rocky and arid environments, making it a resilient and hardy species.
