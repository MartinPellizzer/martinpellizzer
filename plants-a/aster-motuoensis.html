Aster motuoensis

Aster motuoensis, commonly known as the Motuo aster, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of Yunnan Province in China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its showy, composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, showcasing its adaptation to harsh climates. Its flowers bloom in late summer, producing a striking display of purple and white hues.

Due to its limited distribution and unique ecological niche, Aster motuoensis is considered a rare and valuable species in botanical studies.
