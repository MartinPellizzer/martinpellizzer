Avena orientalis var. flavescens

Avena orientalis var. flavescens, commonly known as the yellow oatgrass, is a perennial grass native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, wiry stems and produces small, yellowish to golden flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves with a slight bluish-green hue. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
