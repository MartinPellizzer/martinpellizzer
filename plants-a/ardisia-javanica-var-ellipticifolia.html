Ardisia javanica var. ellipticifolia

Ardisia javanica var. ellipticifolia, commonly known as the Javanese beautyberry, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, elliptical-shaped leaves that give it its varietal name. The plant produces small, clustered white flowers that are followed by bright red berries, which are a notable feature. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical forests.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for horticultural use in warm climates.
