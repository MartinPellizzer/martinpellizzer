Artemisia astrachanica

Artemisia astrachanica, commonly known as Astrachan wormwood, is a perennial herb native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, gray-green foliage. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in steppe and semi-desert environments.

Artemisia astrachanica is valued for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally in herbal remedies.
