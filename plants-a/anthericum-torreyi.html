Anthericum torreyi

Anthericum torreyi, commonly known as Torrey's lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its unique inflorescence consisting of a spadix surrounded by a spathe. The plant features strap-like, lanceolate leaves that are typically green and have a smooth texture. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, clustered around the spadix, which is often yellow or green in color.

Anthericum torreyi thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
