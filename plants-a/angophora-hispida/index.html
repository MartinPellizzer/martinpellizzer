Angophora Hispida

Angophora hispida is a unique species of flowering plant native to eastern Australia. It is known for its distinctive appearance, featuring rough, hairy leaves and vibrant red flowers. This plant thrives in coastal and heathland environments, often found in sandy soils. Its ecological importance makes it a valuable component of local ecosystems. Due to its hardy nature, it is also popular among horticulturists and gardeners.
