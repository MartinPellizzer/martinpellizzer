Anathallis gracilenta

Anathallis gracilenta, commonly known as the slender anathallist, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, elongated inflorescence and small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded forest environments, often epiphytic on tree branches. Its botanical features include lanceolate leaves and a unique floral structure that facilitates specific pollination mechanisms.

Anathallis gracilenta is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological significance in its native habitat.
