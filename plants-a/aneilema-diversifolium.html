Aneilema diversifolium

Aneilema diversifolium, commonly known as the variable-leaf aneilema, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its diverse leaf shapes, which range from ovate to lanceolate, often with serrated margins. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the wet season. Its leaves are typically alternate and can vary in size and texture depending on environmental conditions.

Aneilema diversifolium thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
