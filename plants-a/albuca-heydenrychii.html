Albuca heydenrychii

Albuca heydenrychii, commonly known as the Cape tulip, is a bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features striking, showy flowers that resemble tulips, typically in shades of yellow and orange. The plant has linear, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of tubular blooms. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Albuca heydenrychii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
