Aloe nyeriensis

Aloe nyeriensis, commonly known as the Nyeri aloe, is a succulent plant native to East Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are fleshy and covered in a whitish powdery coating. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow-orange flowers, typically blooming in the summer months. Aloe nyeriensis is adapted to arid environments, storing water in its leaves to survive dry conditions.

It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its striking appearance and hardy nature.
