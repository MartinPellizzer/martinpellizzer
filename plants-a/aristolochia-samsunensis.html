Aristolochia samsunensis

Aristolochia samsunensis, commonly known as the Samsun birthwort, is a flowering plant native to Turkey.

It is characterized by its unique, tubular, and often heart-shaped leaves, which are typically green with a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers that are usually dark purple or maroon in color. It belongs to the Aristolochiaceae family, known for its complex and often deceptive floral structures.

Due to its medicinal properties and ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and traditional herbal practices.
