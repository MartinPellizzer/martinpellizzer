Aspidium excellens

Aspidium excellens, commonly known as the elegant water fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its delicate, feather-like fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream sides. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

Aspidium excellens is valued both for its ornamental appeal and its ecological role in maintaining wetland habitats.
