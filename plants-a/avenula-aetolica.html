Avenula aetolica

Avenula aetolica, commonly known as the Aeolian grass, is a perennial grass species native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny florets. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and rocky habitats.

Avenula aetolica is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in stabilizing soil in marginal environments.
