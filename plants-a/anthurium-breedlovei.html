Anthurium breedlovei

Anthurium breedlovei, commonly known as the Red-veined Anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the Caribbean islands.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are often adorned with deep red veins. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-shaped inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, typically in shades of red or orange. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is popular among plant enthusiasts and collectors.
