Aster × amethystinus f. leucos

Aster × amethystinus f. leucos, commonly known as the white aster, is a hybrid perennial plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that typically feature white petals and a yellow central disk. The plant produces small, cylindrical flower heads that bloom in late summer to early autumn. It thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

Its ornamental value lies in its delicate appearance and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
