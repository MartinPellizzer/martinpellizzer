Artemisia elegantissima

Artemisia elegantissima, commonly known as silver artemisia, is a perennial shrub native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Its aromatic leaves contain volatile oils, which contribute to its strong scent and medicinal properties.
