Amaryllis purpurea var. major

Amaryllis purpurea var. major, commonly known as the giant purple amaryllis, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically deep purple or red, with a speckled pattern on the petals. The plant produces large, strap-like leaves that are often bluish-green in color. It thrives in warm, sunny environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

This variety is popular for its dramatic blooms and is often cultivated as a decorative plant.
