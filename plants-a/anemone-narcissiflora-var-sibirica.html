Anemone narcissiflora var. sibirica

Anemone narcissiflora var. sibirica, commonly known as the Siberian anemone, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the temperate regions of Asia.

It features showy, white to pale yellow flowers that resemble daisies, typically blooming in early spring. The plant has a short, stout rhizome and produces basal leaves that are lobed and hairy. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for woodland gardens and naturalized landscapes.
