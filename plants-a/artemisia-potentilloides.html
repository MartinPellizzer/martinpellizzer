Artemisia potentilloides

Artemisia potentilloides, commonly known as silver bluestem, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and slender, upright growth habit. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in panicles during the summer months. It thrives in dry, well-drained soils and is often found in prairie and steppe habitats.

Artemisia potentilloides is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in stabilizing soil in disturbed areas.
