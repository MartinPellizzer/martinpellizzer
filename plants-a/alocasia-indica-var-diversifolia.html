Alocasia indica var. diversifolia

Alocasia indica var. diversifolia, commonly known as the variegated elephant ear, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that often feature a striking combination of green, white, and yellow variegation. The plant thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light to maintain its vibrant foliage. Its leaves can grow up to 3 feet in diameter, making it a dramatic addition to any indoor or outdoor space.

Alocasia indica var. diversifolia is a popular choice for those seeking a bold, exotic look in their plant collection.
