Arisaema danzhuense

Arisaema danzhuense, commonly known as the Danzhou arrowhead, is a herbaceous plant native to China.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its distinctive arrow-shaped leaves and spadix inflorescence. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as mountain forests and rocky areas. Its underground rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

The flowers are usually greenish-white, with a unique structure that aids in pollination by insects.
