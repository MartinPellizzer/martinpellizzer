Asterosedum obtusifolium

Asterosedum obtusifolium, commonly known as the bluntleaf stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to the arid regions of North America.

It features fleshy, thick leaves that are typically gray-green in color, adapted to store water and reduce transpiration. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or white during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit and low maintenance requirements make it an ideal plant for drought-tolerant landscapes.
