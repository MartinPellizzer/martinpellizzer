Alstonia reineckeana

Alstonia reineckeana, commonly known as the African angel's trumpet, is a striking tropical plant native to eastern Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. The plant produces woody, branching stems and has glossy, dark green leaves that are opposite in arrangement. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
