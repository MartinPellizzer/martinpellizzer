Anthurium nymphaeifolium var. cochleatum

Anthurium nymphaeifolium var. cochleatum, commonly known as the spiral leaf anthurium, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its uniquely spiraled, lanceolate leaves that give it a distinctive and ornamental appearance. The plant produces strikingly colorful spathes that range from red to orange, often with a contrasting yellow or white interior. It thrives in humid, warm environments with bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is popular among plant enthusiasts for its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
