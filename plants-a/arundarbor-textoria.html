Arundarbor textoria

Arundarbor textoria, commonly known as the silverleaf orchid, is a flowering plant native to the temperate regions of the northern hemisphere.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and lance-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine, silvery down. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale pink during the spring and early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and partial shade, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and woodland settings.
