Acinodendron paniculatum

Acinodendron paniculatum, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy yellow-orange flowers that appear in paniculate inflorescences, creating a striking visual display. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are typically ovate or elliptic in shape. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it suitable for ornamental cultivation in warm climates.

The tree is also valued for its rapid growth and ability to provide shade in urban and rural landscapes.
