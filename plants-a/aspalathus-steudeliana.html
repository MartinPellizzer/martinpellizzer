Aspalathus steudeliana

Aspalathus steudeliana, commonly known as the silver leaf bush, is a small evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its narrow, silvery-green leaves that are covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in clusters during the winter months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for coastal and rocky garden environments.

Aspalathus steudeliana is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
