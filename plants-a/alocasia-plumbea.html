Alocasia plumbea

Alocasia plumbea, commonly known as the silver leaf alocasia, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often a striking combination of deep green and silvery white. The plant thrives in humid, shady environments and requires well-draining, nutrient-rich soil. Its foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and as a decorative plant in tropical landscapes.

Alocasia plumbea is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
