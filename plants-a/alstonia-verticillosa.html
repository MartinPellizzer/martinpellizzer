Alstonia verticillosa

Alstonia verticillosa, commonly known as the clustered alstonia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is a deciduous tree that can grow up to 15 meters tall, characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and opposite, ovate leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers followed by woody, capsule-like fruits containing numerous seeds.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
