Anthurium oaxacamonticola

Anthurium oaxacamonticola, commonly known as the Oaxaca anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves and vibrant spadix inflorescence. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its foliage is often a deep green with a glossy texture, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers surrounded by a colorful spathe.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in botanical gardens.
