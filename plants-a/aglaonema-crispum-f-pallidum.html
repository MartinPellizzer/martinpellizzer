Aglaonema crispum f. pallidum

Aglaonema crispum f. pallidum, commonly known as the Variegated Aglaonema, is a tropical evergreen plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated leaves that display shades of white, cream, and green. The plant has a compact growth habit with upright, lance-shaped foliage. It thrives in low to moderate light conditions and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is widely cultivated as a houseplant and in tropical gardens.
