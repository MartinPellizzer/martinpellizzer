Ambrosia purpurascens

Ambrosia purpurascens, commonly known as the purple ambrosia, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and ragweed, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant produces small, yellow to orange flowers that are often surrounded by bracts, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in dry, sunny environments and is adapted to arid conditions.

Ambrosia purpurascens is known for its rapid growth and ability to colonize disturbed soils.
