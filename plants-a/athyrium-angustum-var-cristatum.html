Athyrium angustum var. cristatum

Athyrium angustum var. cristatum, commonly known as the narrow fern with a crest, is a striking variant of the narrow fern.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often adorned with a distinctive crest or ridge along the midrib. This varietal form is native to regions of East Asia, including China and Japan. The plant thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and botanical collections.
