Arthrostemma salzmannii

Arthrostemma salzmannii, commonly known as Salzmann's arthrostema, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Acanthaceae and is characterized by its opposite, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of tubular, two-lipped flowers. The species is also noted for its potential medicinal properties in traditional herbal practices.
