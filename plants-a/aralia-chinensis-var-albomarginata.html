Aralia chinensis var. albomarginata

Aralia chinensis var. albomarginata, commonly known as the white-margin aralia, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features compound leaves with a distinctive white or cream-colored margin, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in umbels, which are followed by red berries. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a suitable ornamental plant in gardens.

Its foliage and unique appearance make it a popular choice for shady garden spaces.
