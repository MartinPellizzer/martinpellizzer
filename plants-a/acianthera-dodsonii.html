Acianthera dodsonii

Acianthera dodsonii, commonly known as the Dodson's acianthera, is a rare orchid species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, fragrant flowers with a distinctive violet hue. Its flowers are pollinated by nocturnal moths, which are attracted to its strong scent.
