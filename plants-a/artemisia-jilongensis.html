Artemisia jilongensis

Artemisia jilongensis, commonly known as the Jilong wormwood, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, silver-gray foliage. The plant produces small, yellowish flower heads that are clustered in composite inflorescences. Artemisia jilongensis is typically found in arid or semi-arid regions, thriving in well-drained soils.

It is often used in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.
