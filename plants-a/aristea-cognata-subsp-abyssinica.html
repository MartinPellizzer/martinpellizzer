Aristea cognata subsp. abyssinica

Aristea cognata subsp.

abyssinica, commonly known as the Abyssinian aristea, is a flowering plant native to the highlands of Ethiopia. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, rosette-like growth habit.

The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple in color, arranged in dense clusters. It is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, thriving in well-drained soils.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in rock gardens.
