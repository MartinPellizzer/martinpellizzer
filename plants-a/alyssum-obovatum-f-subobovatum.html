Alyssum obovatum f. subobovatum

Alyssum obovatum f. subobovatum, commonly known as the narrow-ovate alyssum, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is a herbaceous perennial characterized by its compact growth habit and clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The subspecies subobovatum exhibits slightly elongated, ovate leaves compared to the typical form of A. obovatum. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

This plant is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
