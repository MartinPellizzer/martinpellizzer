Anthurium pucuroense

Anthurium pucuroense, commonly known as the Pucuroense Arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to the Amazon rainforest of Peru.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves with a glossy surface. The plant produces unique spadices, which are inflorescences that consist of a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe. It thrives in humid, shady environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Anthurium pucuroense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated as a houseplant.
