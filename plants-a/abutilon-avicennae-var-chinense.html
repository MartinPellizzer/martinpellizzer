Abutilon avicennae var. chinense

Abutilon avicennae var. chinense, commonly known as Chinese velvetleaf, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height. The plant is characterized by its ovate to lanceolate leaves, which are typically hairy and have a velvety texture. Its flowers are yellow and occur in clusters, often with a distinctive shape.

The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small, dry seeds.
