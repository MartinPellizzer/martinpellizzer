Aloe deltoidea

Aloe deltoidea, commonly known as the delta aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, triangular leaves that are often tinged with red or purple at the edges. The plant produces yellow-orange flowers in summer, which are borne on tall, branching inflorescences. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it suitable for arid environments.

Aloe deltoidea is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional uses in herbal medicine.
