Allium aksekiense

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its large, fleshy bulbs and strap-like leaves. The plant produces clusters of pale pink to white flowers with six petals, typical of the Allium genus. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to temperate climates.
