Astrantia major subsp. carinthiaca

Astrantia major subsp.

carinthiaca, commonly known as Carpathian aster, is a perennial herb native to Central and Eastern Europe. It features upright stems with narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically gray-green in color.

The plant produces showy, flat-topped clusters of white to pale pink flowers in late summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.

This species is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
