Asplenium taiwanense

Asplenium taiwanense, commonly known as the Taiwan spleenwort, is a species of fern native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the family Aspleniaceae and is characterized by its compact, rosette-like growth habit. The plant features delicate, lance-shaped fronds that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically found in forest understories.

Asplenium taiwanense is valued for its ornamental appeal and plays a role in the ecological diversity of its native habitat.
