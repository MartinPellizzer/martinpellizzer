Aizoanthemum

Aizoanthemum, commonly known as the ice plant, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Aizoaceae.

These plants are native to South Africa and are characterized by their succulent leaves and vibrant, showy flowers. They are typically found in arid and semi-arid environments, adapted to thrive in hot, dry conditions. Aizoanthemum species are often used in xeriscaping due to their drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements.

Their unique appearance and ability to store water in their leaves make them popular among gardeners and botanists alike.
