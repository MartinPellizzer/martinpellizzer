Asplenium longissimum

Asplenium longissimum, commonly known as the long-leaf spleenwort, is a species of fern belonging to the family Aspleniaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, evergreen fronds that can grow up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often on rocks or tree trunks. Its spores are produced on the underside of the fronds in clusters called sori.

Asplenium longissimum is widely distributed across temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.
