Arenaria rosanoi

Arenaria rosanoi, commonly known as the Rosano sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of central and southern Europe.

It typically thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in alpine or subalpine environments. The plant features small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in early summer, adding a delicate touch to its habitat. Its leaves are usually succulent and arranged in a rosette pattern, helping it conserve moisture in harsh conditions.

Arenaria rosanoi is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to stabilize soil in rocky terrains.
