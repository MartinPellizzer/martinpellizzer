Asplenium bourgaei

Asplenium bourgaei, commonly known as the Bourgai's shield fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Aspleniaceae and is characterized by its compact, rosette-like growth habit. The plant features lanceolate, serrated leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Asplenium bourgaei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant.
