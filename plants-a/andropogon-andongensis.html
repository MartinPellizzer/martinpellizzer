Andropogon andongensis

Andropogon andongensis, commonly known as the Andong grass, is a perennial grass species native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Poaceae family, which includes many other grasses. The plant is characterized by its tall, slender stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. It typically grows in moist, open habitats such as wetlands and forest edges.

Andropogon andongensis plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various wildlife species.
