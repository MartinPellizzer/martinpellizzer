Abies nordmanniana var. eichleri

Abies nordmanniana var. eichleri, commonly known as the Eichler fir, is a rare variant of the Nordmann fir.

It is native to the Caucasus region and is characterized by its conical shape and dark green needles. The plant has a slow growth rate and is often found in high-altitude forests. Its cones are typically reddish-brown and release seeds in the fall.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a valuable species in botanical studies.
