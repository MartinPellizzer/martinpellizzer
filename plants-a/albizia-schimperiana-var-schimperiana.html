Albizia schimperiana var. schimperiana

Albizia schimperiana var. schimperiana, commonly known as the African silk tree, is a leguminous shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its bipinnate leaves, which are often compound and feathery in appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant, cream-colored flowers arranged in dense, globular clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to semi-arid to moist tropical climates.

Albizia schimperiana var. schimperiana is also valued for its ornamental appeal and potential use in traditional medicine.
