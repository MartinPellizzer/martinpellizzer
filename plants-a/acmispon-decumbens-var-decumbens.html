Acmispon decumbens var. decumbens

Acmispon decumbens var. decumbens, commonly known as slender blueweed, is a low-growing annual plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It features slender, prostrate stems that often root at the nodes, forming dense mats on the ground. The plant produces small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are alternate, narrow, and often covered in fine hairs.

It is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a common weed in disturbed soils.
