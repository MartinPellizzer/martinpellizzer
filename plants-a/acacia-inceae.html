Acacia inceae

Acacia inceae, commonly known as the Ince's acacia, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow to golden-orange flowers that bloom in clusters. Acacia inceae is valued for its ecological role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for native wildlife.
