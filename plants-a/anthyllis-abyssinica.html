Anthyllis abyssinica

Anthyllis abyssinica, commonly known as the Abyssinian burr.

It is a flowering plant native to Ethiopia and belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae. The plant is characterized by its hairy stems and yellow or orange flowers that bloom in spring. Its seed pods are long and cylindrical, containing numerous small, hard seeds.

Anthyllis abyssinica is adapted to arid environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
