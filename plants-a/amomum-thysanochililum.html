Amomum thysanochililum

Amomum thysanochililum, commonly known as the golden spike orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and distinctive golden-yellow spikes that emerge from the foliage. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically arranged in dense clusters. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in tropical gardens and greenhouses.
