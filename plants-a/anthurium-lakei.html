Anthurium lakei

Anthurium lakei, commonly known as the Lake's arrowhead, is a tropical flowering plant native to the lowland rainforests of Central America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are often brightly colored with a glossy texture. The plant produces spadices, which are inflorescences that resemble a spike, and these are typically surrounded by a colorful spathe. Anthurium lakei thrives in humid, warm environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Its unique foliage and ornamental value make it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical plant collections.
