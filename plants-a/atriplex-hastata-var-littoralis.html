Atriplex hastata var. littoralis

Atriplex hastata var. littoralis, commonly known as saltbush, is a halophytic shrub adapted to coastal and saline environments.

It possesses succulent leaves that help reduce water loss in arid conditions. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dunes or salt marshes. Its small, greenish flowers are typically clustered in dense inflorescences.

This variety is notable for its ability to tolerate high salinity and wind exposure.
