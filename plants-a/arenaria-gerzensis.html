Arenaria gerzensis

Arenaria gerzensis, commonly known as Gerzen's sandwort, is a flowering plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is native to Central Asia and is typically found in arid or semi-arid environments. The plant is characterized by its low-growing habit, with small, fleshy leaves and clusters of white or pink flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to withstand drought conditions.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species in its native range.
