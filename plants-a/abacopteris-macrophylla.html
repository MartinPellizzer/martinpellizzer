Abacopteris macrophylla

Abacopteris macrophylla, commonly known as the giant fan palm, is a striking tropical plant characterized by its large, fan-shaped leaves.

It is native to Southeast Asia and thrives in warm, humid climates with well-drained soil. The plant features a slender trunk that can grow up to several meters in height, supporting its expansive fronds. Its leaves are typically green and can reach lengths of over two meters, making it a prominent feature in its natural habitat.

Abacopteris macrophylla is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its unique appearance and resilience.
