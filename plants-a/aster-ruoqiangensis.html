Aster ruoqiangensis

Aster ruoqiangensis, commonly known as the Ruoqiang aster, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of Xinjiang, China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is adapted to harsh, dry environments. Its flowers are usually blue or purple, and it blooms during the summer months.

Due to its unique habitat and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
