Alnus × hosoii

Alnus × hosoii, commonly known as the Hosoii alder, is a hybrid species within the Betulaceae family.

It is native to Japan and is characterized by its small, ovate leaves and clusters of yellowish-green catkins. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions. It plays a role in soil stabilization and provides habitat for various wildlife species.

Alnus × hosoii is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, enhancing soil fertility.
