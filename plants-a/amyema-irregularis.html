Amyema irregularis

Amyema irregularis, commonly known as the irregular leaf amyema, is a species of flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Myrtaceae, which includes eucalyptus and other aromatic plants. The plant is characterized by its irregularly shaped leaves and small, clustered white flowers. It typically grows in dry, sandy soils and is often found in coastal regions.

Amyema irregularis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
