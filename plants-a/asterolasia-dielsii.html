Asterolasia dielsii

Asterolasia dielsii, commonly known as the rock aster, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads and medicinal properties. The plant typically grows on rocky outcrops and has a low, sprawling growth habit with hairy leaves. Its flowers are small and clustered, often displaying shades of purple or pink, and bloom during the cooler months.

Asterolasia dielsii is valued for its resilience in harsh environments and has been used in traditional medicine for various ailments.
