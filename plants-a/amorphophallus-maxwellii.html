Amorphophallus maxwellii

Amorphophallus maxwellii, commonly known as the Maxwell's arrowleaf, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of western Africa.

It is characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves and a distinctive, often spiky stem. The plant produces a large, white, spathaceous flower that is typically borne on a tall, leafless stalk. It is a member of the Araceae family, which includes many ornamental and economically important plants.
