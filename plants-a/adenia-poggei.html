Adenia poggei

Adenia poggei, commonly known as the red-stemmed Adenia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 2 to 5 meters. The plant features rough, reddish-brown bark and produces striking yellow flowers with prominent stamens. Its leaves are usually elliptical or ovate, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

Adenia poggei is often found in rocky or coastal areas, where it thrives in well-drained soils.
