Aerides mitrata

Aerides mitrata, commonly known as the helmet orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, helmet-shaped flower that gives it its common name. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in tropical rainforests. Its flowers are usually greenish-yellow with a distinctive red or purple lip.

Aerides mitrata thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
