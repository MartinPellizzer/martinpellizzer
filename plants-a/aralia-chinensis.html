Aralia chinensis

Aralia chinensis, commonly known as Chinese angelica, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features compound leaves with feather-like lobes and produces small, greenish-white flowers in umbel-like clusters. The plant is characterized by its milky sap and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Aralia chinensis is also valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
