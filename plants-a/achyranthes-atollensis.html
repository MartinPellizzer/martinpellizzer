Achyranthes atollensis

Achyranthes atollensis, commonly known as the atoll smartweed, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of the Indian Ocean.

It is characterized by its erect, branched stems and small, greenish-white flowers that appear in clusters. The plant typically grows in saline and brackish water environments, such as mangrove swamps and tidal flats. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Achyranthes atollensis plays a role in stabilizing coastal soils and providing habitat for various small organisms.
