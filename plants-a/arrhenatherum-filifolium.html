Arrhenatherum filifolium

Arrhenatherum filifolium, commonly known as slender feathergrass, is a perennial grass species native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its fine, needle-like leaves that are typically green and slightly hairy. The plant produces slender, spike-like inflorescences that bear small, greenish to brownish flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows, wetlands, and along riverbanks.

Arrhenatherum filifolium is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, supporting various insect and bird species.
