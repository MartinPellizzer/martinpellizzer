Asplenium unilaterale var. amoenum

Asplenium unilaterale var. amoenum, commonly known as the one-sided spleenwort, is a fern belonging to the family Aspleniaceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive morphology, featuring a single, elongated fertile frond on one side of the plant. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as rock crevices or forest floors. Its leaves are usually narrow and lance-shaped, with a dark green coloration.

This variety is often found in temperate regions and is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.
