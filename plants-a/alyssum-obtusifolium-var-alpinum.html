Alyssum obtusifolium var. alpinum

Alyssum obtusifolium var. alpinum, commonly known as alpine alyssum, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It features compact, mat-forming growth with dense clusters of small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink. The foliage consists of rounded, leathery leaves that are typically gray-green in appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and alpine settings.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to tolerate cold climates.
