Apocynum × sarniense

Apocynum × sarniense, commonly known as the hybrid dogbane, is a perennial shrub native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family, which includes milkweeds and other toxic plants. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of bell-shaped, white or pink flowers. Its stems are often covered in fine hairs, and it produces follicles containing toxic seeds.

The plant contains cardiac glycosides, which are toxic to humans and animals.
