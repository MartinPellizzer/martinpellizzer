Alyssum numidicum

Alyssum numidicum, commonly known as the Moroccan alyssum, is a flowering plant native to North Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant has finely divided, silvery-gray foliage that adds visual interest to gardens. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Alyssum numidicum is also valued for its ability to attract beneficial insects like bees and butterflies.
