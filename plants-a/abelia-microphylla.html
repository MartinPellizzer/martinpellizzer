Abelia microphylla

Abelia microphylla, commonly known as the dwarf abelia, is a compact evergreen shrub native to China.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white to pinkish flowers in the summer months. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and well-drained soil.

Abelia microphylla is prized for its ornamental value and adaptability to various garden conditions.
