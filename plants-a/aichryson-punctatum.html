Aichryson punctatum

Aichryson punctatum, commonly known as the stonecrop jade plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, thick leaves that are typically green with a waxy coating, adapted to store water. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the growing season. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental appearance make it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
