Aspidistra semiaperta var. globulifera

Aspidistra semiaperta var. globulifera, commonly known as the globular aspidistra, is a rare and unique variant of the aspidistra genus.

It is characterized by its distinctive, rounded leaves that are often broader and more compact compared to typical aspidistra species. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences with white flowers, which are not commonly seen in other aspidistra varieties. It thrives in low-light conditions and prefers moist, well-draining soil, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.

Due to its rarity and specific care requirements, it is a sought-after plant among botanical enthusiasts.
