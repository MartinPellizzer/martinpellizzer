Agathisanthemum chlorophyllum

Agathisanthemum chlorophyllum, commonly known as the green agathisanthemum, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in horticulture for its delicate appearance.
