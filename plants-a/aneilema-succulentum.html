Aneilema succulentum

Aneilema succulentum, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette-forming leaves that are often fleshy and succulent, adapted to store water. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of purple, pink, and white, which bloom from the leaf axils. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Aneilema succulentum is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
