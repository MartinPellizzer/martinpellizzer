Aneilema gramineum var. typicum

Aneilema gramineum var. typicum, commonly known as the slender swamp lily, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It typically grows in wet, swampy areas and has linear, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, white or pale green flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the rainy season. It is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit and is often found in marshy or disturbed habitats.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological role in wetland ecosystems.
