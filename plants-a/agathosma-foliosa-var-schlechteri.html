Agathosma foliosa var. schlechteri

Agathosma foliosa var. schlechteri, commonly known as Schlechter's agathosma, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, needle-like leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in spring. It is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its compact size and fragrant foliage.

This variety is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
