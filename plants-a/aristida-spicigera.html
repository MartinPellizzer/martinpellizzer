Aristida spicigera

Aristida spicigera, commonly known as the slender dropseed, is a grass species native to North America.

It is characterized by its fine, wiry leaves and compact, spike-like inflorescences. This plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as prairies and sandy soils. It produces small, pale green to brown flowers that are arranged in dense clusters.

Aristida spicigera is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
