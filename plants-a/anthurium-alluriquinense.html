Anthurium alluriquinense

Anthurium alluriquinense, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a striking tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its unique inflorescence consisting of a spadix and spathe. The plant features vibrant, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. Its flowers are notable for their bright red spadix, which is often surrounded by a colorful spathe.

Anthurium alluriquinense thrives in humid, warm environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal.
