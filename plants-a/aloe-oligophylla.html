Aloe oligophylla

Aloe oligophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leaf aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and narrow, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a pale blue or white bloom. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in spring, adding ornamental value. Aloe oligophylla is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves.

It is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
