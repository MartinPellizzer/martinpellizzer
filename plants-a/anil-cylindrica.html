Anil cylindrica

Anil cylindrica, commonly known as the slender spurge, is a herbaceous plant native to parts of Africa and Asia.

It features a cylindrical stem that is often ribbed and covered with a milky sap. The plant has small, opposite leaves that are usually elliptical in shape. It produces clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months.

Anil cylindrica is typically found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas.
