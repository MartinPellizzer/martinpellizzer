Anthurium shinumas

Anthurium shinumas, commonly known as the Shimanami anthurium, is a rare and exotic plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its unique inflorescence consisting of a spadix and a spathe. The plant features vibrant, heart-shaped leaves that are often a deep green with a glossy texture. Its flowers, though small, are surrounded by a strikingly colorful spathe that can range from red to orange.

Anthurium shinumas is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouse environments due to its specific care requirements.
