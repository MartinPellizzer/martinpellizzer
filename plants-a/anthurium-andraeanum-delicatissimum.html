Anthurium andraeanum delicatissimum

Anthurium andraeanum delicatissimum, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Colombia.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped, brightly colored spathe that can range from red to white or pink. The plant produces small, green spadices that house its actual flowers, which are clustered in dense, cylindrical structures. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Its foliage is glossy and dark green, adding to its ornamental value in indoor settings.
