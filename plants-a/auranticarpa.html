Auranticarpa

Auranticarpa, commonly known as the golden fruit tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy yellow-orange flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces edible fruits that are rich in nutrients and have a sweet, tangy flavor. Its leaves are broad and glossy, providing a striking visual contrast to the vibrant flowers.

Auranticarpa is often cultivated for both its ornamental value and its edible produce.
