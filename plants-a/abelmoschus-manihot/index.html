Abelmoschus Manihot

Abelmoschus manihot is a versatile plant known for its edible roots and unique properties. It is commonly referred to as the African breadfruit or arrowroot plant. This plant thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is widely cultivated for its nutritional value. Its starch is used in food products and industrial applications. Abelmoschus manihot plays an important role in both agriculture and local economies.
