Abacosa canescens

Abacosa canescens, commonly known as the hairy abacosa, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of southern Africa.

Abacosa canescens is often found growing on rocky outcrops and in sandy soils, showcasing its adaptability to extreme conditions.
