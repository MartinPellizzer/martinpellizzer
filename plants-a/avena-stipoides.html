Avena stipoides

Avena stipoides, commonly known as slender oat, is a perennial grass native to North America.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and compact inflorescence that resembles a spikelet. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands. Its seeds are small and produce a delicate, fibrous husk.

Avena stipoides is valued for its ecological role in supporting pollinators and stabilizing soil.
