Achillea distans f. angustissima

Achillea distans f. angustissima, commonly known as narrow-leaved yarrow, is a perennial herb native to southern Europe and parts of western Asia.

It features finely divided, linear leaves that are often described as narrow and grass-like. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in flat-topped umbels. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its delicate foliage and prolonged flowering period.
