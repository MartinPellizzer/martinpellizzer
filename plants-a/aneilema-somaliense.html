Aneilema somaliense

Aneilema somaliense, commonly known as the Somali arrowhead, is a flowering plant native to Somalia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its arrow-shaped leaves and striking white flowers. The plant typically grows in wet, marshy areas and thrives in humid conditions. It has a short stem and produces small, clustered inflorescences.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological significance, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
