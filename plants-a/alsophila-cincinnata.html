Alsophila cincinnata

Alsophila cincinnata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to tropical regions of Asia and the Pacific.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its slender, upright trunk and feathery fronds. The plant has a distinctive crown of long, narrow, and often drooping leaves that give it a graceful appearance. Its rhizome is robust, allowing it to grow in shaded, moist environments.

Alsophila cincinnata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
