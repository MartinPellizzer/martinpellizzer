Aloe johnstonii

Aloe johnstonii, commonly known as Johnston's aloe, is a succulent plant native to the island of Socotra.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, often with a waxy coating. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow flowers during the warmer months. It thrives in arid environments and is adapted to withstand drought conditions.

Its unique appearance and resilience make it a notable species in botanical studies.
