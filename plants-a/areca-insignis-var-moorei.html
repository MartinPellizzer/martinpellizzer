Areca insignis var. moorei

Areca insignis var. moorei, commonly known as the Moore's areca palm, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a member of the Arecaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and feather-like leaves. The plant typically grows up to 10 meters in height and thrives in warm, humid climates. Its leaves are long and pinnately compound, giving it a distinctive, elegant appearance.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and adaptability to various growing conditions.
