Adenia penangiana var. parvifolia

Adenia penangiana var. parvifolia, commonly known as the small-flowered Adenia, is a climbing shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces yellowish-green fruits that are typically consumed by local wildlife. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value and rarity, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
