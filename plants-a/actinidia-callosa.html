Actinidia callosa

Actinidia callosa, commonly known as the Chinese mayapple, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. The plant produces edible berries that ripen to a bright orange-red color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.

Actinidia callosa is valued for its ornamental qualities and historical use in traditional medicine.
