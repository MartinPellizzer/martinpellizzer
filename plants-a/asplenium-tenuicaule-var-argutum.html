Asplenium tenuicaule var. argutum

Asplenium tenuicaule var. argutum, commonly known as the narrow-stemmed spleenwort, is a fern species characterized by its delicate, linear leaves that are often slightly curled.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as rock crevices or along stream banks. The plant has a compact, mat-forming growth habit with short, wiry rhizomes. Its fronds are usually dark green and have a smooth, glossy surface.

This variety is notable for its adaptability to humid conditions and its role in forest ecosystems.
