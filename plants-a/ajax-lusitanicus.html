Ajax lusitanicus

Ajax lusitanicus, commonly known as the Portuguese sword fern, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its sword-shaped, evergreen fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and rocky areas. Its rhizomes are fibrous and help it spread vegetatively.
