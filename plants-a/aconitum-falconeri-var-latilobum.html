Aconitum falconeri var. latilobum

Aconitum falconeri var. latilobum, commonly known as the Falconeri Aconite, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Himalayas.

It features striking blue to purple flowers that bloom in late summer, typically from July to September. The plant has finely divided, fern-like leaves that are arranged alternately along its stem. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine meadows and rocky slopes.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and by plant enthusiasts.
