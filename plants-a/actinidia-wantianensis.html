Actinidia wantianensis

Actinidia wantianensis, commonly known as the Yunnan actinidia, is a deciduous vine native to China.

It belongs to the Actinidiaceae family and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are usually ovate to elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, yellow-green flowers that are clustered in umbels, followed by edible berries that are similar in appearance to kiwifruit. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and as a source of food for wildlife.

The species is also valued in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.
