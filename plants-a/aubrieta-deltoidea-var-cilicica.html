Aubrieta deltoidea var. cilicica

Aubrieta deltoidea var. cilicica, commonly known as the Cilician aubrieta, is a flowering plant native to the eastern Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces vibrant clusters of yellow flowers in early spring. The plant has compact, evergreen foliage that is typically blue-gray in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

This variety is prized for its hardiness and ornamental value in horticulture.
