Artemisia shansiensis

Artemisia shansiensis, commonly known as Chinese wormwood, is a perennial shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, silver-gray foliage. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flower heads that are clustered in dense arrays. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, rocky habitats.

Artemisia shansiensis is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional Chinese medicine.
