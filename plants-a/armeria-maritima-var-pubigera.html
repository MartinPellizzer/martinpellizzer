Armeria maritima var. pubigera

Armeria maritima var. pubigera, commonly known as sea thrift, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of Europe and North America.

It is characterized by its dense, low-growing clusters of pink or white flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are adapted to withstand salt spray and sandy soils. It thrives in well-drained, sunny environments and is often found along shorelines and dunes.

Its hardy nature makes it a popular choice for coastal gardens and erosion control.
