Athanasia rotundifolia

Athanasia rotundifolia, commonly known as the round-leafed everlasting.

It is a flowering plant native to South Africa, characterized by its dense clusters of small, white, tubular flowers. The plant is perennial and thrives in well-drained, sandy soils. Its leaves are round and succulent, adapted to conserve water in arid environments.

Athanasia rotundifolia is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to bloom throughout the year.
