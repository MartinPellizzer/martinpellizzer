Allocasuarina tessellata

Allocasuarina tessellata, commonly known as the coastal sheoak, is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub or tree native to southeastern Australia.

The plant produces small, woody cones that contain seeds, typical of the Casuarinaceae family. It thrives in sandy or well-drained soils and is often found in coastal dunes and heathlands.
