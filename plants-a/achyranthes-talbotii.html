Achyranthes talbotii

Achyranthes talbotii, commonly known as the Talbot's smartweed, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a herbaceous annual that typically grows in moist, disturbed soils such as along roadsides and in wetlands. The plant features alternate, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in dense clusters.
