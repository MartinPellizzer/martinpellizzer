Anthericum vestitum

Anthericum vestitum, commonly known as the velvet fern, is a unique plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, feathery foliage that resembles a soft, velvety texture. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in late summer, adding a delicate touch to its appearance. Its leaves are typically clustered at the base, forming a compact rosette structure.

Anthericum vestitum is adapted to grow in dry, sandy soils and is often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.
