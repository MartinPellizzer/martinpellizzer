Arisaema japonicum var. brachyspatha

Arisaema japonicum var. brachyspatha, commonly known as the Japanese threadleaf arum, is a herbaceous perennial native to Japan.

It features a distinctive inflorescence with a spadix surrounded by a spathe that is narrow and thread-like. The plant has a short, rhizomatous stem and produces dark green, heart-shaped leaves. It blooms in early summer, with the spadix bearing small, greenish-white flowers.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to moist, shady environments.
