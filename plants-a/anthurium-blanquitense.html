Anthurium blanquitense

Anthurium blanquitense, commonly known as the white anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central America.

It is characterized by its striking, waxy, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces vibrant, spatulate-shaped flowers in shades of white to pale pink, which are often surrounded by a colorful spadix. It thrives in humid, warm environments and requires indirect sunlight for optimal growth.

Anthurium blanquitense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated as a houseplant or in botanical gardens.
