Aplotaxis Frolowii

Aplotaxis frolowii is a rare and striking succulent native to southern Africa. It is known for its unique, spiny leaves that form a compact rosette shape. This plant thrives in arid and rocky environments, showcasing remarkable drought resistance. Its vibrant yellow flowers appear during the rainy season, adding a burst of color to its surroundings. Due to its limited distribution and habitat threats, it is considered a vulnerable species requiring conservation efforts.
