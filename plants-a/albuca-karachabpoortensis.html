Albuca karachabpoortensis

Albuca karachabpoortensis, commonly known as the Karachabpoort albuca, is a perennial bulbous plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and clusters of white or pale yellow flowers. Its ornamental value lies in its elegant foliage and delicate floral display.
