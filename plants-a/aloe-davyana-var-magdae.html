Aloe davyana var. magdae

Aloe davyana var. magdae, commonly known as the Magda's Aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, often with a waxy coating. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow-orange flowers during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Its foliage is often used in traditional medicine and as a source of gel for skincare applications.
