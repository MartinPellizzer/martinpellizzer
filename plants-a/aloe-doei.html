Aloe doei

Aloe doei, commonly known as the Cape aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features striking, tall, branched stems that can grow up to 3 meters in height. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow flowers during the summer months. Its leaves are thick, fleshy, and often have a waxy coating to store water.

Aloe doei is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
