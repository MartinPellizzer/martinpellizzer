Alsophila robertsiana

Alsophila robertsiana, commonly known as the Roberts' tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds. The plant has a slow growth rate and can reach heights of up to 10 meters in optimal conditions. Its rhizome is thick and fibrous, providing structural support and aiding in water absorption.

Alsophila robertsiana is often found in humid, shaded environments such as tropical rainforests.
