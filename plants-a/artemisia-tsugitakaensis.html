Artemisia tsugitakaensis

Artemisia tsugitakaensis, commonly known as the Japanese wormwood, is a perennial shrub native to Japan.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, silver-gray foliage. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flower heads that are typically clustered in dense spherical inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes used in traditional medicine and as an ornamental plant.
