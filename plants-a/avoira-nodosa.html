Avoira nodosa

Avoira nodosa, commonly known as the nodular orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its unique, clustered growth pattern and its delicate, often fragrant flowers. Native to tropical regions, this plant thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture from the surrounding soil.

The plant's nodular structure is a distinctive botanical feature that aids in its survival in competitive ecosystems.
