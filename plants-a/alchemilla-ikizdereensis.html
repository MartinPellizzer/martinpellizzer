Alchemilla ikizdereensis

Alchemilla ikizdereensis, commonly known as the Ikizdere ladies' mantle, is a perennial herb native to Turkey.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its basal rosette of soft, hairy leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in early summer, which are clustered in dense, upright inflorescences. Its foliage is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.

Alchemilla ikizdereensis thrives in moist, shaded environments and is valued for its ornamental and medicinal potential.
