Astronia sessilis

Astronia sessilis, commonly known as the sessile astronia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, featuring opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. Its flowers are small and clustered in heads, often displaying a range of colors from white to yellow.

Astronia sessilis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
