Anthericum indutum

Anthericum indutum, commonly known as the velvet fern, is a unique plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a soft, velvety appearance. The plant features small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in arid and rocky environments, demonstrating remarkable adaptability to harsh conditions.

Its distinctive appearance and ecological role make it a notable species in its native habitat.
