Artemisia princeps var. orientalis

Artemisia princeps var. orientalis, commonly known as Japanese mugwort, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its aromatic, silver-gray foliage and small, inconspicuous yellow flowers. The plant thrives in temperate climates and is often found in moist, shaded environments. Its leaves contain volatile oils that contribute to its strong, medicinal scent.

It is widely used in traditional medicine and as a source of natural compounds for various applications.
