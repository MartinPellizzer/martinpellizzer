Aspidium dregei

Aspidium dregei, commonly known as the African shield fern, is a species of fern native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its shield-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its fronds are usually evergreen and can reach lengths of up to 30 centimeters.

Aspidium dregei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its lush, fern-like appearance.
