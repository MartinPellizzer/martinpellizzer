Aristida boninensis

Aristida boninensis, commonly known as the Bonin grass, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family Poaceae.

It is native to the Bonin Islands, a group of islands located in the Pacific Ocean. The plant is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and small, spike-like inflorescences. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green coloration.

Aristida boninensis is often found in sandy or coastal habitats, thriving in well-drained soils.
