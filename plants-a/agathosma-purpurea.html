Agathosma purpurea

Agathosma purpurea, commonly known as the purple agathosma, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, purple to pinkish flowers arranged in compact heads. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in sunny conditions. Its leaves are aromatic and often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

Agathosma purpurea is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its visual charm.
