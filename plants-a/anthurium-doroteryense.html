Anthurium doroteryense

Anthurium doroteryense, commonly known as the Dorotery's arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped leaves that can vary in color from deep green to reddish hues. The plant produces striking spadices, which are the modified leaves that house the small, inconspicuous flowers. Anthurium doroteryense thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and ornamental purposes.
