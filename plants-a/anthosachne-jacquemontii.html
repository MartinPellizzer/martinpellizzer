Anthosachne jacquemontii

Anthosachne jacquemontii, commonly known as Jacquemont's sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and related plants. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a triangular cross-section characteristic of many sedges.
