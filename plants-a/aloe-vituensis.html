Aloe vituensis

Aloe vituensis, commonly known as the red aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically red or purple in color, with a waxy coating that helps it retain moisture. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow flowers, which bloom in the spring. Its leaves contain a gel-like substance that is often used for its soothing properties.

Aloe vituensis is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water to thrive.
