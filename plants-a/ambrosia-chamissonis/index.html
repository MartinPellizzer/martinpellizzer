Ambrosia Chamissonis

Ambrosia chamissonis is a flowering plant native to the western united states. it belongs to the asteraceae family and is commonly known as chamisson's ambrosia. this plant is characterized by its small yellow flowers and feathery leaves. it thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often found in coastal areas. due to its invasive nature, it can pose a threat to native plant communities.
