Artanthe albida

Artanthe albida, commonly known as the white artanthe, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It features delicate, woolly leaves that are typically gray-green in color. The plant produces small, showy white to pale pink flowers that bloom in spring. It prefers well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.

Artanthe albida is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.
