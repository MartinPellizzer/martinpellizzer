Anemone lerinensis

Anemone lerinensis, commonly known as the Lerins anemone, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its delicate, white to pinkish flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and rocky soils, preferring well-drained conditions. Its leaves are basal, heart-shaped, and often covered in fine hairs.

Anemone lerinensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
