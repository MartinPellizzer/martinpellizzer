Anthurium cuasicanum

Anthurium cuasicanum, commonly known as the copperleaf anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a metallic sheen. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-shaped inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, often in shades of red or orange. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Anthurium cuasicanum is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
