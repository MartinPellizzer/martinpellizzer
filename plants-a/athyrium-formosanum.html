Athyrium formosanum

Athyrium formosanum, commonly known as the Taiwan fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the Dryopteridaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, lace-like fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky slopes. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

The plant's ornamental value makes it a popular choice for horticultural cultivation.
