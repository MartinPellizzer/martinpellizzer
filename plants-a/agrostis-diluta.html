Agrostis diluta

Agrostis diluta, commonly known as slender wheatgrass, is a perennial grass native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

It typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows, grasslands, and along stream banks. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences with numerous florets. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of soil types.

Agrostis diluta is valued for its resilience and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
