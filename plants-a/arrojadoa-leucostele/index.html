Arrojadoa Leucostele

Arrojadoa leucostele is a rare and striking plant native to the cloud forests of Costa Rica. Its unique white stems and vibrant green leaves make it a standout among its botanical relatives. This species is known for its slow growth and adaptability to high-altitude environments. Due to habitat loss, it is now considered endangered and requires conservation efforts. Studying Arrojadoa leucostele offers insights into the ecological resilience of tropical flora.
