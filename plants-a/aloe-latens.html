Aloe latens

Aloe latens, commonly known as the Cape aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, gray-green leaves that are covered in a powdery coating. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow flowers during the summer months. It is adapted to arid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Aloe latens is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some medicinal uses in traditional practices.
