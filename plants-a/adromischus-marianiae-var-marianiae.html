Adromischus marianiae var. marianiae

Adromischus marianiae var. marianiae, commonly known as the Rosy Red Jade, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, rosette-shaped leaves that are typically pink to reddish in color, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or white during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Due to its unique aesthetics and low maintenance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
