Abelia macrotera var. mairei

Abelia macrotera var. mairei, commonly known as Maire's abelia, is a compact, deciduous shrub native to China.

It features fragrant, tubular flowers that range from white to pink, blooming in late summer. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and slightly hairy. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for garden borders or containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its attractive foliage and long-lasting blooms.
