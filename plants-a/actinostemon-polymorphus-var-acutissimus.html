Actinostemon polymorphus var. acutissimus

Actinostemon polymorphus var. acutissimus, commonly known as the sharp-leaved actinostemon, is a species native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its slender stems and lance-shaped leaves with acute tips. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments.

Its ornamental value makes it a sought-after plant in horticulture, particularly for its delicate foliage and seasonal blooms.
