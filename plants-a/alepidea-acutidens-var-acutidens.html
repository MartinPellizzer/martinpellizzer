Alepidea acutidens var. acutidens

Alepidea acutidens var. acutidens, commonly known as the narrow-leaved rock heath, is a perennial shrub native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments. The plant features small, narrow leaves and produces clusters of white or pale yellow flowers. It is often found in coastal and mountainous regions, where it thrives in full sun.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its hardy nature.
