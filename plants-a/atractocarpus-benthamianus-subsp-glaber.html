Atractocarpus benthamianus subsp. glaber

glaber, commonly known as the smooth-leafed tamarind, is a member of the Rubiaceae family and is native to tropical regions of Africa. It is characterized by its large, glossy, ovate leaves that are typically smooth on both surfaces.
