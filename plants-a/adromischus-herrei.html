Adromischus herrei

Adromischus herrei, commonly known as the "Horned Cactus," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features small, fleshy, spiny stems that resemble horns, giving it its common name. The plant produces clusters of tiny, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the spring and summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil with plenty of sunlight.

Adromischus herrei is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
