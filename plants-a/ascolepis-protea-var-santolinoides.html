Ascolepis protea var. santolinoides

Ascolepis protea var. santolinoides, commonly known as the Santolinoides protea, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and is adapted to sandy soils. It has narrow, linear leaves and produces a single flower head per stem.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
