Artemisia daghestanica

Artemisia daghestanica, commonly known as the Daghestan wormwood, is a perennial herb native to the Caucasus region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, gray-green foliage. The plant produces small, yellowish flower heads that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in alpine and steppe habitats.

Due to its medicinal properties, it has been used traditionally in herbal remedies.
