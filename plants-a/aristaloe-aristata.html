Aristaloe aristata

Aristaloe aristata, commonly known as the desert candle, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features a cylindrical, branching stem that resembles a candle, covered in dense, woolly gray-green foliage. The plant produces tall, spiky inflorescences with showy yellow flowers during the warmer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it suitable for arid and semi-arid environments.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice for xeriscaping and rock gardens.
