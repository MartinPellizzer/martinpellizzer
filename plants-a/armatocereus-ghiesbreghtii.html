Armatocereus ghiesbreghtii

Armatocereus ghiesbreghtii, commonly known as the Ghiesbreght's night bloomer, is a species of cactus native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, cylindrical stems covered in clusters of spines. The plant produces large, fragrant white flowers that open at night, attracting nocturnal pollinators. It thrives in arid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is often cultivated in controlled environments to ensure its survival.
