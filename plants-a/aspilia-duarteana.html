Aspilia duarteana

Aspilia duarteana, commonly known as Duarte's torchflower, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking, spiky inflorescence. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are surrounded by bracts resembling torches. It thrives in dry, rocky habitats and is often found in arid regions.

Aspilia duarteana is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological significance in its native environment.
