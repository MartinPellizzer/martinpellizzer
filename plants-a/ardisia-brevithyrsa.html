Ardisia brevithyrsa

Ardisia brevithyrsa, commonly known as the narrow-leaf evergreen ardisia, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a waxy texture. The plant produces small, white flowers followed by bright red berries, which are edible but often consumed by birds. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.

Ardisia brevithyrsa is also noted for its ability to tolerate partial shade and well-drained soil.
