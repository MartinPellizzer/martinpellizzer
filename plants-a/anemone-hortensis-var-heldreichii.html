Anemone hortensis var. heldreichii

Anemone hortensis var. heldreichii, commonly known as the Heldreich's anemone, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Balkan Peninsula.

It features showy, cup-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in early spring. The plant has finely divided, fern-like leaves that are hairy and emit a mild scent. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
