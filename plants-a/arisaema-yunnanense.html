Arisaema yunnanense

Arisaema yunnanense, commonly known as the Yunnan arisaema, is a herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Yunnan Province in China.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its unique inflorescence consisting of a spadix surrounded by a spathe. The plant typically features a short rhizome with a rosette of heart-shaped leaves that are often hairy and have a waxy texture. Its flowers are unisexual, with separate male and female flowers on the spadix, a common trait in the Araceae family.

Arisaema yunnanense is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has traditional uses in Chinese medicine.
