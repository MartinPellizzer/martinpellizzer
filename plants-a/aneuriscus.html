Aneuriscus

Aneuriscus, commonly known as the desert hibiscus, is a genus of flowering plants in the mallow family, Malvaceae.

These plants are native to arid and semi-arid regions of Africa, the Middle East, and parts of Asia. They are characterized by their succulent stems and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to red. Aneuriscus species are often used in traditional medicine and landscaping due to their drought tolerance and ornamental value.

The leaves are typically lance-shaped and arranged alternately along the stem.
