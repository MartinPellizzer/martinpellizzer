Astragalus yangtzeanus

Astragalus yangtzeanus, commonly known as the Yangtze milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant is typically found in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. It plays a role in soil stabilization and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.

Its ecological significance highlights its importance in local biodiversity.
