Adenophora thunbergiana f. angustifolia

Adenophora thunbergiana f. angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved bellflower, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It features lance-shaped leaves that are typically narrow and pointed, contributing to its distinctive appearance. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers that are usually blue or purple in color, blooming in summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or streamside habitats.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability in garden settings.
