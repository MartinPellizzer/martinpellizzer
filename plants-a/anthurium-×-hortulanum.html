Anthurium × hortulanum

Anthurium × hortulanum, commonly known as the flamingo flower, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Araceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped spathe that comes in a variety of colors, including red, orange, and pink. The plant's spadix, which is the central, column-like structure, is often covered in tiny, inconspicuous flowers. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect sunlight for optimal growth.

Anthurium × hortulanum is popular as a decorative plant due to its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
