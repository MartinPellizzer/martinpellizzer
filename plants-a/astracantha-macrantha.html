Astracantha macrantha

Astracantha macrantha, commonly known as the large-flowered astracantha, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features upright, branched stems covered in soft, hairy foliage. Its showy, yellow to orange flowers are arranged in dense clusters, making it a striking addition to gardens.

Astracantha macrantha thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
