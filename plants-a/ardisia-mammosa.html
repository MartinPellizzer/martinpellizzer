Ardisia mammosa

Ardisia mammosa, commonly known as the glossy privet, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a small evergreen shrub or tree that can grow up to 6 meters in height. The plant features opposite, elliptical leaves that are glossy and dark green on top, with a lighter underside. Its small, white flowers are clustered in umbels and are followed by bright red berries.

Ardisia mammosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
