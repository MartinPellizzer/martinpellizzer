Adiantum palmense

Adiantum palmense, commonly known as the palm fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Adiantaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, feather-like fronds that are typically bright green and have a slender, elongated shape. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is native to parts of South America, particularly Brazil. Its rhizomes are often covered in a fine layer of white, powdery spores, which are released into the air for reproduction.

Adiantum palmense is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its elegant appearance and ability to thrive in humid indoor conditions.
