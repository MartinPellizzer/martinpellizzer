Areca aliceae

Areca aliceae, commonly known as the Alice's areca palm, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Arecaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and feather-like leaves. The plant produces small, clustered white flowers that are followed by bright red fruits. Areca aliceae is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adaptable to various soil types.

It prefers warm, humid climates and is relatively low maintenance once established.
