Ardisia vernicosa

Ardisia vernicosa, commonly known as the glossy privet, is a flowering plant species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and small, clustered white flowers that appear in spring. The plant produces bright red berries that are attractive to birds, aiding in seed dispersal. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Ardisia vernicosa is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various soil types.
