Agave gracilis

Agave gracilis, commonly known as the slender agave, is a succulent plant native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces a tall, branched inflorescence with yellow flowers, which bloom once in its lifetime. It is adapted to arid environments and stores water in its fleshy leaves.

Agave gracilis is often used in xeriscaping due to its low water requirements and ornamental appearance.
