Anthurium terryae

Anthurium terryae, commonly known as Terry's anthurium, is a rare and striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped leaves that are often a deep green with a waxy texture. The plant produces striking spadices, which are modified leaves that enclose the true flowers, typically in shades of red or orange. Anthurium terryae thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its unique appearance makes it a prized specimen among collectors and horticulturists.
