Alstroemeria bredemeyeriana

Alstroemeria bredemeyeriana, commonly known as the Yellow Alstroemeria, is a striking flowering plant native to South America, particularly found in regions of Argentina and Chile.

It belongs to the family Alstroemeriaceae and is characterized by its vibrant yellow and orange blooms, which are often adorned with intricate patterns. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of tubular flowers that attract pollinators such as bees and hummingbirds.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
