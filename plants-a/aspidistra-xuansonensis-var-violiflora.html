Aspidistra xuansonensis var. violiflora

Aspidistra xuansonensis var. violiflora, commonly known as the purple-flowered aspidistra, is a rare evergreen herb native to China.

It features dark green, lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces striking violet or purple flowers that bloom in late spring. It prefers shady, moist environments and is often found in rocky or forested areas.

Due to its rarity, it is considered a valuable species in botanical studies and conservation efforts.
