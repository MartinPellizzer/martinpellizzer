Ascocentrum pusillum

Ascocentrum pusillum, commonly known as the small ascocentrum, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant has narrow, dark green leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and green. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in terrariums or as a houseplant.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is relatively rare in the wild.
