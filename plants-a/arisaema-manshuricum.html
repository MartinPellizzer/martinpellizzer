Arisaema manshuricum

Arisaema manshuricum, commonly known as the Manchurian arrowhead, is a herbaceous plant native to eastern Asia.

It features a tuberous rhizome and produces a single, strikingly shaped leaf that resembles an arrowhead. The plant blooms with a spadix surrounded by a spathe, typical of the Araceae family. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value makes it a sought-after plant in horticulture.
