Aspidium nantoense

Aspidium nantoense, commonly known as the Nanto fern, is a species of polypodioid fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its delicate, feather-like fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its rhizome is creeping and often covered with a layer of brown, scaly tissue.

Aspidium nantoense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic foliage.
