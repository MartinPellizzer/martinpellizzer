Anaphalis viscosissima

Anaphalis viscosissima, commonly known as the sticky aster, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its woolly, silver-gray foliage. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are typically clustered in a spherical shape. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Its sticky leaves and showy inflorescences make it a distinctive and ornamental species.
