Asphodelus deseglisei

Asphodelus deseglisei, commonly known as the Desert Asphodel, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of North Africa and the Middle East.

It belongs to the Asphodelaceae family, which includes a variety of succulent and grass-like plants. The plant produces striking, tall spikes of pale yellow or greenish flowers, which bloom in late summer. Its leaves are narrow and fleshy, adapted to store water in its native dry environment.

Asphodelus deseglisei is often found in rocky soils and is well-suited to xerophytic conditions.
