Anthemis austriaca f. latiloba

Anthemis austriaca f. latiloba, commonly known as the Austrian anthemis, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its compact, herbaceous growth habit and produces small, yellow to orange composite flowers. The leaves are typically linear to lanceolate, with a slightly hairy surface. This variety is native to Central Europe and thrives in sunny, well-drained soils.

It is often found in meadows and rocky habitats, showcasing its adaptability to various environmental conditions.
