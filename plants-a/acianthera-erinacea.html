Acianthera erinacea

Acianthera erinacea, commonly known as the hairy acianthera, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, hairy stems and small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in tropical or subtropical regions. Its leaves are usually elongated and covered in fine pubescence, contributing to its unique appearance.

Acianthera erinacea is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
