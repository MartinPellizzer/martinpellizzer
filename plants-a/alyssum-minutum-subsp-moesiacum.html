Alyssum minutum subsp. moesiacum

Alyssum minutum subsp.

moesiacum, commonly known as the Moesian dwarf alyssum, is a low-growing perennial herb native to the Balkan region. It features dense clusters of small, fragrant white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in early spring.

The plant has slender, branched stems and narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and long flowering period.
