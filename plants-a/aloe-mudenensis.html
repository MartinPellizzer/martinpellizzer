Aloe mudenensis

Aloe mudenensis, commonly known as the Muden's aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a waxy coating. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing yellow-orange flowers. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, requiring minimal water and well-drained soil.

Aloe mudenensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional medicinal uses.
