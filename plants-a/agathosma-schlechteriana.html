Agathosma schlechteriana

Agathosma schlechteriana, commonly known as the Cape daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

Its leaves are aromatic, often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

Agathosma schlechteriana thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
