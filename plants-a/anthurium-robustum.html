Anthurium robustum

Anthurium robustum, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped spathe that can range in color from deep red to orange. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers inside the spathe, which are typically green or yellow. Anthurium robustum thrives in humid, warm environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Its striking foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and ornamental purposes.
