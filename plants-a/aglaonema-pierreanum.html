Aglaonema pierreanum

Aglaonema pierreanum, commonly known as the Pierre's Chinese evergreen, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated leaves that display shades of green, white, and pink. The plant belongs to the Araceae family and is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions. Its foliage is often used in ornamental horticulture due to its aesthetic appeal.

Aglaonema pierreanum is also valued for its air-purifying properties and ease of care.
