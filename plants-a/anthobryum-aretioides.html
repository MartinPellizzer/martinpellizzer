Anthobryum aretioides

Anthobryum aretioides, commonly known as the rock beard moss, is a small, acrocarpous moss that typically grows on shaded, moist rock surfaces.

It has a dense, tufted growth form with closely spaced, elongated leaves that are often recurved at the tips. The leaves are usually dark green and have a smooth, shiny surface. The plant produces small, yellowish to reddish capsules that are borne on short, erect stalks.

It is often found in mountainous regions and is adapted to thrive in harsh, rocky environments.
