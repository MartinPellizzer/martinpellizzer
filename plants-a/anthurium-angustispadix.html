Anthurium angustispadix

Anthurium angustispadix, commonly known as the narrow-spadix anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, elongated spadix surrounded by a vibrant, colorful spathe. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its leaves are usually broad and glossy, with a deep green color.

Anthurium angustispadix is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
