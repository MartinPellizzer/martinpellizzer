Anomatheca grandiflora

Anomatheca grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered anomatheca, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South America.

It features erect, branching stems covered in soft, hairy foliage. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a suitable candidate for rock gardens or containers.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
