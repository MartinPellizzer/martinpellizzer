Austromyrtus gonoclada

Austromyrtus gonoclada, commonly known as the Tasmanian tea tree, is a shrub native to the island of Tasmania.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, white flowers. The plant produces woody capsules that contain numerous small, hard seeds. It thrives in coastal and dry inland areas, often growing in sandy or rocky soils.

Austromyrtus gonoclada is valued for its essential oils, which are used in aromatherapy and traditional medicine.
