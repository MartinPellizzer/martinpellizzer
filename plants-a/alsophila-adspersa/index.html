Alsophila Adspersa

Alsophila adspersa is a species of tree fern native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia. It is known for its tall, slender trunk and feathery fronds that give it a distinctive, elegant appearance. This plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories. Its unique structure makes it a popular choice for both ornamental gardening and ecological restoration projects. The plant plays an important role in supporting local biodiversity and maintaining forest ecosystems.
