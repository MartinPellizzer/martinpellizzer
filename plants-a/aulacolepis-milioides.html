Aulacolepis milioides

Aulacolepis milioides, commonly known as the millet grass, is a flowering plant belonging to the grass family, Poaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and Australia. The plant is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and small, spikelet-based inflorescences. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green coloration.

Aulacolepis milioides is often used in ornamental gardening due to its aesthetic appeal and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
