Astragalus Varius F. Albiflorus

Astragalus varius f. albiflorus is a rare and striking variant of the common milkvetch known for its distinctive white flowers. This plant thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its unique floral display makes it a prized subject for botanists and gardeners alike. The plant's genetic variation highlights the diversity within the Astragalus genus. Despite its beauty, it remains a elusive and underappreciated member of the legume family.
