Adenostemma caffrum var. longifolium

Adenostemma caffrum var. longifolium, commonly known as the long-leaved adenostemma, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Acanthaceae and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and opposite, lance-shaped leaves. Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens, though it can be invasive in certain regions.
