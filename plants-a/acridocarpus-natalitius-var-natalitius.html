Acridocarpus natalitius var. natalitius

Acridocarpus natalitius var. natalitius, commonly known as Natal palm, is a tropical plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small to medium-sized palm tree that typically grows up to 10 meters in height. The plant has a slender trunk with a smooth, greyish-brown bark and produces feather-like leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern. Its flowers are small and yellowish, and it produces fruit that is initially green and turns red when mature.

The plant is well adapted to coastal and sandy soils and is often used in ornamental landscaping.
