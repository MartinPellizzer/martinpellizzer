Anguloa × ruckeri var. speciosa

Anguloa × ruckeri var. speciosa, commonly known as the showy anguloa, is a hybrid orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from pink to purple, with intricate patterns on the petals. The plant thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, often covered in a fine, hairy texture.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
