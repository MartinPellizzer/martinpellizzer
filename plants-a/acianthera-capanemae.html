Acianthera capanemae

Acianthera capanemae, commonly known as the Capanema violet, is a rare orchid species native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking violet-blue flowers. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments within the Atlantic Forest biome. Its flowers are relatively small but highly fragrant, attracting specific pollinators.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered endangered and is protected in several conservation programs.
