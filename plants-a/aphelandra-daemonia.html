Aphelandra daemonia

Aphelandra daemonia, commonly known as the sacred flame plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the Caribbean and Central America.

It is characterized by its striking, pendulous inflorescences that resemble flames, with vibrant yellow bracts surrounding small white flowers. The plant has lance-shaped, dark green leaves that are glossy and arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity and well-draining soil to flourish.

Aphelandra daemonia is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is popular in indoor gardening.
