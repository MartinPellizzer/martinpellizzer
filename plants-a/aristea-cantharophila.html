Aristea cantharophila

Aristea cantharophila, commonly known as the sand mat, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered yellow flowers. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid environments.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens with well-draining soil.
