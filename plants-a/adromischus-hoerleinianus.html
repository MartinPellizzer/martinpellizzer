Adromischus hoerleinianus

Adromischus hoerleinianus, commonly known as the Hoerlein's moon cactus, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features small, fleshy, rounded leaves that are typically green with red or purple hues. The plant produces delicate, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the growing season. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect sunlight.

Due to its unique appearance and slow growth, it is a popular choice for succulent enthusiasts.
