Aechmea lamarchei

Aechmea lamarchei, commonly known as the silver pine or silver rosette, is a striking bromeliad native to the tropical rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its dense, compact rosette of long, narrow, silver-green leaves that form a central cup to collect rainwater. The plant's foliage is often covered in a fine, waxy coating that gives it its distinctive appearance. It produces striking inflorescences with bright red flowers that bloom in the spring.

Aechmea lamarchei is a popular ornamental plant due to its unique look and relatively low maintenance requirements.
