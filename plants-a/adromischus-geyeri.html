Adromischus geyeri

Adromischus geyeri, commonly known as the Geyeri stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a waxy coating, adapted to store water in arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or purple during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit and attractive foliage make it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.
