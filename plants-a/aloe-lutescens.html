Aloe lutescens

Aloe lutescens, commonly known as the yellow aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically bright yellow to green in color. The plant produces striking orange or red flowers in clusters during the spring or summer. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil.

Aloe lutescens is often cultivated for its ornamental value and medicinal properties.
