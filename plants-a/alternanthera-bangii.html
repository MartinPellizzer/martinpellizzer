Alternanthera bangii

Alternanthera bangii, commonly known as red arrowhead, is a striking aquatic plant native to South America.

It features bright red or pink foliage that becomes more intense under strong light conditions. The plant has slender, branching stems that often trail or float on the water surface. Its leaves are arrow-shaped and can be submerged or emergent depending on the water level.

It is popular in aquariums for its vibrant color and ease of care.
