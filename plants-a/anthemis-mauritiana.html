Anthemis mauritiana

Anthemis mauritiana, commonly known as the Mauritius daisy, is a flowering plant native to the island of Mauritius.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and has adapted to the tropical climate of its native habitat. Its foliage is covered in fine, hairy structures that help reduce water loss.

It is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
