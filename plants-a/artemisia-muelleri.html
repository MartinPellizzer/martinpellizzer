Artemisia muelleri

Artemisia muelleri, commonly known as Mueller's wormwood, is a perennial shrub native to the arid regions of Australia.

It features silvery-gray foliage with small, aromatic leaves that are covered in fine, fuzzy hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flower clusters that bloom in late summer. It is adapted to dry, well-drained soils and is drought-tolerant.

Artemisia muelleri is often used in traditional medicine and has a strong, pungent aroma.
