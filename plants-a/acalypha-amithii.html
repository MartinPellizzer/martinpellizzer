Acalypha amithii

Acalypha amithii, commonly known as the red-flowered iron plant, is a striking ornamental shrub native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, rust-colored flowers that bloom in dense clusters, often surrounded by striking red bracts. The plant features palmate leaves with lobed margins, giving it a distinctive, tropical appearance. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Acalypha amithii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
