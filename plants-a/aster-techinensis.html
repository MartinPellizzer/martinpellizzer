Aster techinensis

Aster techinensis, commonly known as the Chinese aster, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically features showy, purple or pink flowers that bloom in the summer months. It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its vibrant colors and adaptability to various soil types.

Aster techinensis is also valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
