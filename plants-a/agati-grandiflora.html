Agati grandiflora

Agati grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered agati, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Meliaceae, which includes mahogany and other valuable hardwoods. The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. It produces woody fruits that contain flat, edible seeds.

Agati grandiflora is valued for its durable timber and is also used in traditional medicine.
