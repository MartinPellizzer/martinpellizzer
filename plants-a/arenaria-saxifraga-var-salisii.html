Arenaria saxifraga var. salisii

Arenaria saxifraga var. salisii, commonly known as the Salisii sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It thrives in rocky and arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. The plant features small, rounded leaves and produces clusters of white or pink flowers, typically blooming in early summer. Its botanical structure includes a compact rosette form with hairy stems, aiding in water retention.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in stabilizing soil in alpine habitats.
