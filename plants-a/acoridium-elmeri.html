Acoridium elmeri

Acoridium elmeri, commonly known as the Elmer's arrowhead, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its unique inflorescence consisting of a spadix and a spathe. The plant typically grows in wetlands and marshy areas, preferring moist and shaded environments. Its leaves are arrowhead-shaped, which gives it its common name, and it produces small, greenish-white flowers.

Acoridium elmeri is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
