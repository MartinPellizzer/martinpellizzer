Adenostyles glabra var. australis

Adenostyles glabra var. australis, commonly known as the Australian smooth adenostyle, is a flowering plant native to southern Australia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. The plant features slender, upright stems with small, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are white or pale pink, arranged in dense clusters at the ends of the stems.

It is known for its ability to thrive in arid conditions and is often used in native gardens for its ornamental value.
