Arundarbor griffithiana

Arundarbor griffithiana, commonly known as the Himalayan orchid, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Himalayas.

The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in cool, moist climates. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces a single, showy flower per stem.
