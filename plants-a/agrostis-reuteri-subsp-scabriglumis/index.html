Agrostis Reuteri Subsp. Scabriglumis

Scabrous bentgrass, scientifically known as Agrostis reuteri subsp. scabriglumis, is a unique species of grass that thrives in specific ecological niches. It is characterized by its rough, textured leaves and compact growth habit, which help it survive in harsh environments. Found primarily in Europe and parts of Asia, this plant is often overlooked due to its modest appearance. Despite its unassuming look, it plays an important role in maintaining soil stability and supporting local biodiversity. Its resilience and adaptability make it a subject of interest for botanists and ecologists alike.
