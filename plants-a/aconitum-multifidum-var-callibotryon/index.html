Aconitum Multifidum Var. Callibotryon

Aconitum multifidum var. callibotryon is a striking member of the buttercup family known for its dramatic purple and white spires of flowers. Native to the mountainous regions of Europe, this plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and cool climates. Its showy blooms appear in late summer, attracting pollinators and adding a dramatic touch to gardens. Despite its beauty, it is highly toxic and should be handled with care. This variety is prized by horticulturists for its ornamental value and resilience in challenging environments.
