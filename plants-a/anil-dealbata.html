Anil dealbata

Anil dealbata, commonly known as the white flame tree, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy white or pink flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. The plant has compound leaves composed of several oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing as a tree or shrub.

Its rapid growth and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for landscaping in warm regions.
