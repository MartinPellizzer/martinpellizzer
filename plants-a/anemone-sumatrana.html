Anemone sumatrana

Anemone sumatrana, commonly known as the Sumatran anemone, is a flowering plant native to the Indonesian island of Sumatra.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant features a short, hairy stem and palmate leaves with a hairy underside. Its flowers are usually borne in clusters at the top of the stem, and it thrives in moist, shady environments.

Anemone sumatrana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
