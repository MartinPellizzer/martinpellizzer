Aloe flabelliformis

Aloe flabelliformis, commonly known as the fan aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette of fleshy, fan-shaped leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The leaves are covered in a powdery white coating, which helps reduce water loss in arid environments. It produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow flowers during the summer months.

This plant is well adapted to dry conditions and is often used in xeriscaping due to its low water requirements.
