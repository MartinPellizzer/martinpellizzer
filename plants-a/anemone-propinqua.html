Anemone propinqua

Anemone propinqua, commonly known as the small anemone, is a herbaceous perennial native to the eastern United States.

It features delicate, white to pale pink flowers with a distinctive center that resembles a crown. The plant has basal leaves that are heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Anemone propinqua is often found in woodland habitats and is valued for its early spring blooms.
