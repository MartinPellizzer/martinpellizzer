Anthurium andraeanum var. atrosanguineum

Anthurium andraeanum var. atrosanguineum, commonly known as the Black Velvet Anthurium, is a striking tropical plant native to Colombia.

It is characterized by its dark, velvety leaves that contrast beautifully with its vibrant red spadix. The plant produces showy, spadix-shaped inflorescences that are often surrounded by a white or cream-colored spathe. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

This variety is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
