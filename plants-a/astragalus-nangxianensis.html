Astragalus nangxianensis

Astragalus nangxianensis, commonly known as the Nangxian milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its hairy stems and small, pea-like flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.
