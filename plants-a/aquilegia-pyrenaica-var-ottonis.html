Aquilegia pyrenaica var. ottonis

Aquilegia pyrenaica var. ottonis, commonly known as the Pyrenean columbine, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Pyrenees region.

It features striking blue to purple spurred flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has palmate leaves with five to seven lobes, and its stems are covered in fine hairs. It thrives in woodland and rocky habitats, preferring well-drained soil and partial shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting pollinators.
