Artanthe Cernua

Artanthe, scientifically known as Artanthe cernua, is a strikingly beautiful flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of central and southern Europe. This low-growing perennial is prized for its vibrant, daisy-like blooms that range from pink to red, adding a splash of color to gardens and natural landscapes. It thrives in well-drained soils and full to partial sunlight, making it a versatile choice for rock gardens and borders. With its delicate appearance and easy care requirements, Artanthe cernua has become a popular ornamental plant among horticulturists and garden enthusiasts. Its unique floral structure and seasonal display make it a standout in the botanical world.
