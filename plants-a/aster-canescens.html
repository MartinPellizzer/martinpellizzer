Aster canescens

Aster canescens, commonly known as the gray aster, is a flowering plant native to the western United States and parts of Mexico.

It is a member of the sunflower family (Asteraceae) and is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and showy purple to blue flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid environments. It produces small, wind-pollinated flowers that bloom in the summer months.

Aster canescens is often found in open areas such as grasslands and slopes, where it contributes to local biodiversity.
