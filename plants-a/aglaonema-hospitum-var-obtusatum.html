Aglaonema hospitum var. obtusatum

Aglaonema hospitum var. obtusatum, commonly known as the Broad-leaf Aglaonema, is a tropical evergreen plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves that are typically a striking combination of green and white or cream patterns. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening. Its foliage is often used in ornamental horticulture due to its aesthetic appeal.

Aglaonema hospitum var. obtusatum prefers moist, well-draining soil and moderate humidity for optimal growth.
