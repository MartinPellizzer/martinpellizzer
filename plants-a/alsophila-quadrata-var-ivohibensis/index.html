Alsophila Quadrata Var. Ivohibensis

Ivohibena tree fern, scientifically known as Alsophila quadrata var. ivohibensis, is a unique and ancient plant species native to Madagascar. This rare fern thrives in the humid, shaded environments of tropical rainforests, where it plays a crucial role in the ecosystem. Its distinctive appearance, with tall, slender fronds and a robust trunk, makes it easily recognizable among other fern species. The plant is also valued for its ecological significance and has been the focus of conservation efforts due to habitat loss. Understanding and protecting the Ivohibena tree fern is essential for preserving biodiversity in Madagascar.
