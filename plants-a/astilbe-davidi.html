Astilbe davidi

Astilbe davidi, commonly known as Chinese fern-ferns, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its upright, branching stems that bear clusters of delicate, plume-like flowers in shades of pink, red, and white. The plant's foliage consists of heart-shaped, dark green leaves that are often adorned with a silvery underside. Astilbe davidi thrives in shady, moist environments and is well-suited for woodland gardens.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to add texture and color to shaded landscapes.
