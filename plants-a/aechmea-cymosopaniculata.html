Aechmea cymosopaniculata

Aechmea cymosopaniculata, commonly known as the panicle-bush bromeliad, is a striking epiphytic plant native to South America.

It features a rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves that form a central rosette, which collects rainwater. The plant produces an inflorescence of bright red bracts that enclose small, white flowers. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Its unique structure and vibrant appearance make it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
