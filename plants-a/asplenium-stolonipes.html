Asplenium stolonipes

Asplenium stolonipes, commonly known as the running spleenwort, is a fern belonging to the family Aspleniaceae.

It is a small, terrestrial fern that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky crevices. The plant features a creeping rhizome with dense clusters of small, lance-shaped leaves. Its fronds are usually dark green and have a leathery texture, with spores produced on the underside of the leaves.

Asplenium stolonipes is often found in temperate regions and is known for its ability to spread through stolons, forming dense mats.
