Aquilegia Vulgaris Var. Sylvestris

Aquilegia vulgaris var. sylvestris is a striking wildflower known for its delicate, bell-shaped blooms. Native to parts of Europe and western Asia, this variety thrives in woodland and meadow environments. Its vibrant red and purple petals create a vivid contrast against its lush green foliage. The plant is often admired for its ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies. Its unique structure and beauty make it a popular choice for both gardens and natural habitats.
