Anthurium engleri

Anthurium engleri, commonly known as the Engler's anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically deep green with a glossy texture. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-shaped inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, often in shades of red, orange, or yellow. Anthurium engleri thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

It is a popular ornamental plant appreciated for its unique foliage and long-lasting floral displays.
