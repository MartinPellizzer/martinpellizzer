Aequatorium fabrisii

Aequatorium fabrisii, commonly known as the Fabrisii's bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe.

It is characterized by its striking bell-shaped flowers that range in color from pale blue to violet. The plant typically grows in rocky soils and thrives in cool, humid environments. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often covered in a fine layer of white hairs.
