Aster sodiroi

Aster sodiroi, commonly known as the Sierra Madre aster, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Mexico.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky and well-drained soils, thriving in high-altitude environments. Its flowers are usually blue to purple in color, and it blooms during the summer months.

Aster sodiroi is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitat.
