Abronia robusta

Abronia robusta, commonly known as the rock beard or coastal beard, is a flowering plant native to the western United States and Baja California.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to purple, often blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to coastal and inland desert environments.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and sensitive species.
