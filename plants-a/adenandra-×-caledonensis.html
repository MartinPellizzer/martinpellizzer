Adenandra × caledonensis

Adenandra × caledonensis, commonly known as the Natal adenandra, is a hybrid evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Proteaceae and is characterized by its dense, leathery foliage and showy, cone-like inflorescences. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that are clustered in cylindrical heads. It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Adenandra × caledonensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in horticulture for its attractive appearance and hardy nature.
