Aloe × lapaixii

Aloe × lapaixii, commonly known as the hybrid aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is a natural hybrid between Aloe ferox and Aloe polyphylla, resulting in a unique combination of traits from both parents. The plant typically features tall, slender stems with spirally arranged leaves that are often red-tinged at the base. Its flowers are small and clustered in inflorescences, usually producing a yellow or orange hue.

Aloe × lapaixii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking appearance.
