Abelia schischkinii

Abelia schischkinii, commonly known as the Schischkin abelia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features showy, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink, often with a red throat, blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and turn reddish in the fall. It thrives in partial to full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental value make it suitable for borders or container gardening.
