Aragoa × funzana

Aragoa × funzana, commonly known as the hybrid aragoa, is a naturally occurring hybrid between Aragoa parviflora and Aragoa funzana.

It is a shrub native to the eastern United States, particularly in the southeastern region. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters and features narrow, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are small, white, and clustered in dense inflorescences, often blooming in late spring to early summer.

Aragoa × funzana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often found in moist, wooded habitats.
