Annularia

Annularia, commonly known as the ringed orchid, is a genus of flowering plants belonging to the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is characterized by its unique floral structure, featuring a ring-like arrangement of petals and sepals. The plants are typically found in tropical and subtropical regions, thriving in humid, shaded environments. Annularia species often have delicate, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pale pink.

Their root systems are specialized for nutrient absorption, adapting to the specific conditions of their native habitats.
