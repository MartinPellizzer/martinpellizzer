Adenandra brachyphylla var. heterophylla

Adenandra brachyphylla var. heterophylla, commonly known as the variable-leaf adenandra, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its diverse leaf shapes, ranging from ovate to elliptical, which vary depending on the habitat. The plant typically grows in coastal and sandy soils, thriving in well-drained environments. Its small, white flowers are clustered in terminal inflorescences, adding ornamental value.

The species is valued for its adaptability and is often used in horticultural settings for its aesthetic appeal.
