Anemone nemorosa f. nitida

Anemone nemorosa f. nitida, commonly known as the silver anemone, is a decorative perennial plant native to Europe.

It features finely cut, silvery-white leaves that give it its distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, pale pink to white flowers in early spring. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Its delicate foliage and early blooming habit make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking a shade-tolerant ornamental plant.
