Alphitonia obtusifolia var. tenuis

Alphitonia obtusifolia var. tenuis, commonly known as the narrow-leaved alphitonia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree or shrub that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. Its flowers are small, white or pale yellow, and are clustered in panicle-like inflorescences.

The fruit is a small, woody capsule that contains several seeds.
