Athyrium nigritianum

Athyrium nigritianum, commonly known as the Black Forest Athyrium, is a species of fern native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the family Athyriaceae and is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest understories or rocky crevices. Its leaves are bi- or tri-pinnate, with a dark green hue and a finely divided structure.

Athyrium nigritianum is valued for its ornamental appeal and plays a role in the ecological balance of its native habitats.
