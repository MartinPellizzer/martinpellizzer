Astrocaryum sociale

Astrocaryum sociale, commonly known as the Brazilian buriti palm, is a tall, slender palm native to the Amazon basin.

It is characterized by its large, fan-shaped leaves and produces clusters of small, yellowish flowers. The plant's fruit is a dark brown, oval drupe that contains a single seed. The trunk is fibrous and can grow up to 20 meters in height, making it a significant component of the rainforest canopy.

It plays an important ecological role by providing food and habitat for various wildlife species.
