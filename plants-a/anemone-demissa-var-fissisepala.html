Anemone demissa var. fissisepala

Anemone demissa var. fissisepala, commonly known as the fissisepal anemone, is a herbaceous perennial native to the Pacific Northwest of North America.

It features showy, white to pale pink flowers with deeply notched petals that give it a distinctive, almost fern-like appearance. The plant typically grows in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and streambanks. Its leaves are palmately divided, with a soft, velvety texture.

It is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in partial shade.
