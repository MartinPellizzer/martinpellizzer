Alternanthera ficoidea var. normalis

Alternanthera ficoidea var. normalis, commonly known as red arrowhead, is a striking aquatic plant native to South America.

It features vibrant red or pink foliage that becomes more intense under bright light and high nutrient conditions. The plant has long, arrow-shaped leaves that are often used in aquariums for their aesthetic appeal. It thrives in tropical freshwater environments and requires moderate to high water flow.

Its growth habit is bushy, making it suitable for background or midground planting in planted tanks.
