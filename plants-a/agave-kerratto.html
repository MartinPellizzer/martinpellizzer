Agave kerratto

Agave kerratto, commonly known as the Kerratto agave, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a succulent plant characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces a tall flower stalk that bears yellow flowers, which bloom in the summer months. It is adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Its leaves are often used in traditional crafts and as a source of fiber.
