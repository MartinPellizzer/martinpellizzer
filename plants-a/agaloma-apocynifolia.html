Agaloma apocynifolia

Agaloma apocynifolia, commonly known as the African milk tree, is a member of the Apocynaceae family, characterized by its milky sap and showy flowers.

It is native to southern Africa, where it thrives in dry, rocky habitats. The plant has opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are typically clustered in umbels and range in color from white to pink, attracting various pollinators.

The plant is also known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its unique appearance.
