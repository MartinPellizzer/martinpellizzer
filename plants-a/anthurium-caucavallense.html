Anthurium caucavallense

Anthurium caucavallense, commonly known as the Caucavalense arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to the humid lowland forests of South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive spathe and spadix inflorescence. The plant features broad, arrowhead-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. It thrives in warm, moist environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium caucavallense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated as a houseplant.
