Aruncus sylvester var. subrotundatus

Aruncus sylvester var. subrotundatus, commonly known as the rounded goat's beard, is a herbaceous perennial native to the northeastern United States.

It features striking, feathery plumes of white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has large, palmately lobed leaves that are deeply cut and often have a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland habitats.

Its botanical characteristics make it a distinctive and ornamental addition to gardens and natural landscapes.
