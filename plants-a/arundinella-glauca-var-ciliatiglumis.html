Arundinella glauca var. ciliatiglumis

Arundinella glauca var. ciliatiglumis, commonly known as the silky bamboo or silky reed, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, reed-like stems that can grow up to several meters in height. The leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue, and the inflorescences are paniculate, bearing small, pale green to yellowish flowers. This variety is distinguished by its finely ciliate (fringed) glumes, which give it its specific epithet.

It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its elegant appearance and adaptability to various soil conditions.
