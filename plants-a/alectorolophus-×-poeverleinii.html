Alectorolophus × poeverleinii

Alectorolophus × poeverleinii, commonly known as the hybrid lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It belongs to the Liliaceae family and is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves and showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows to a height of 30–60 cm and produces small, elongated seed pods that contain numerous seeds.

It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
