Arachnis celebica

Arachnis celebica, commonly known as the Celebes arachnid, is a climbing plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy inflorescences. The plant features heart-shaped leaves and produces small, green flowers surrounded by a white spathe. It thrives in humid, tropical environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant.
