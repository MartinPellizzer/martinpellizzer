Argyranthemum pinnatifidum var. succulentum

Argyranthemum pinnatifidum var. succulentum, commonly known as the succulent marigold, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, succulent leaves and showy, daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for xeriscaping and rock gardens. Its compact growth habit and ornamental value make it a popular choice for both gardeners and horticultural enthusiasts.

The variety is noted for its drought tolerance and ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
