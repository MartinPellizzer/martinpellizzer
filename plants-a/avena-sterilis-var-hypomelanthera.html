Avena sterilis var. hypomelanthera

Avena sterilis var. hypomelanthera, commonly known as the slender oat, is a perennial grass species native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences with pale green to yellowish flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or dry habitats.

Due to its low-growing habit and ornamental appeal, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
