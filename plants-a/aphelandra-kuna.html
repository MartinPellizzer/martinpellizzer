Aphelandra kuna

Aphelandra kuna, commonly known as the Kuna candleplant, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Arecaceae family, which includes palms, and is characterized by its tall, slender stem and long, narrow leaves. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical landscapes.
