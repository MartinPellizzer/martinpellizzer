Alsophila microdonta

Alsophila microdonta, commonly known as the dwarf chain fern, is a species of tree fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, dense fronds that form a rosette-like structure. The plant has a short, stout trunk covered in a fibrous, brownish skin. Its fronds are typically dark green and have a leathery texture.

Alsophila microdonta is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and ability to thrive in shaded environments.
