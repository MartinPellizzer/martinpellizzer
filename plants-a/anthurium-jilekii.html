Anthurium jilekii

Anthurium jilekii, commonly known as the Jilekii arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are often brightly colored. The plant produces a spadix, which is a type of inflorescence, surrounded by a colorful spathe. It thrives in humid, warm environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.

Anthurium jilekii is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
