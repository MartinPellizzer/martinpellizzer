Aerides suavissima

Aerides suavissima, commonly known as the sweet-scented orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its fragrant, white to pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest undergrowth. Its leaves are broad and grass-like, and it produces clusters of small, delicate blooms.

Aerides suavissima is valued for its ornamental beauty and is cultivated for its pleasant aroma.
