Arctotis angustifolia var. angustifolia

Arctotis angustifolia var. angustifolia, commonly known as the narrowleaf arctotis, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces vibrant blue or purple flowers in the summer months. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming period and drought tolerance.
