Angraecum floribundum

Angraecum floribundum, commonly known as the Madagascar star orchid, is a striking epiphytic orchid native to Madagascar.

It features long, pendulous inflorescences with showy, white to pale pink flowers that have a distinctive star-like appearance. The plant produces fragrant, tubular flowers that attract nectar-feeding moths, particularly the hawkmoth *Xanthopan morgani*. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media.

Angraecum floribundum is prized for its ornamental value and unique floral structure.
