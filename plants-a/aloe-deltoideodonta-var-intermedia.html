Aloe deltoideodonta var. intermedia

Aloe deltoideodonta var. intermedia, commonly known as the Narrow-leaf Aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features linear, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow flowers during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and resilience in dry conditions.
