Alsophila scaberulipes

Alsophila scaberulipes, commonly known as the rough-stemmed tree fern, is a species native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feathery fronds that can reach several meters in length. The plant has a distinctive rough, scaly texture on its trunk, which gives it its common name. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in lowland rainforests.

Alsophila scaberulipes plays an important ecological role by providing habitat and shelter for various forest organisms.
