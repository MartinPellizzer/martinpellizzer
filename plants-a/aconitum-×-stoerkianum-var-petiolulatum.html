Aconitum × stoerkianum var. petiolulatum

Aconitum × stoerkianum var. petiolulatum, commonly known as the petiolulate aconite, is a hybrid plant in the Ranunculaceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems that bear large, showy blue to purple flowers. The leaves are deeply lobed and have a distinctive, almost fern-like appearance. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and botanical collections.
