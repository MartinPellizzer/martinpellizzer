Altheria madagascariensis

Altheria madagascariensis, commonly known as the Madagascar star orchid, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, star-shaped blooms. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are elongated and dark green, while its flowers range in color from white to pink and purple.

Altheria madagascariensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
