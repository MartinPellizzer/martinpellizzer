Aphelandra storkii

Aphelandra storkii, commonly known as the Stork's bill or stork plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Central and South America.

It features striking, elongated, waxy leaves that are often dark green with a metallic sheen. The plant produces showy, pendulous inflorescences with vibrant yellow bracts that resemble flowers. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and humidity.

Aphelandra storkii is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded outdoor gardens.
