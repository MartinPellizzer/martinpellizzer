Abies alba var. nana

Abies alba var. nana, commonly known as the dwarf Norway spruce, is a compact, low-growing variant of the Norway spruce.

It features dense, dark green needles that are shorter than those of the typical species. The plant produces small, spherical cones that mature to a reddish-brown color. It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This variety is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and compact growth habit.
