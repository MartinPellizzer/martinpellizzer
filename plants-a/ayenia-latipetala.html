Ayenia latipetala

Ayenia latipetala, commonly known as the broad-petaled ayenia, is a species of flowering plant native to the high Andes of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and violet. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils at elevations between 3,000 and 5,000 meters. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces small, elongated fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Ayenia latipetala is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in alpine gardens.
