Astrochlaena volkensii

Astrochlaena volkensii, commonly known as the African star orchid, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its unique, star-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are broad and grass-like, while its flowers are fragrant and attract various pollinators.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
