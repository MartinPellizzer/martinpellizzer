Anthurium paulmaasii

Anthurium paulmaasii, commonly known as the Paulmaasii anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Colombia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped spathes that come in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant features a striking, glossy, dark green foliage with prominent veins. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium paulmaasii is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
