Agrostophyllum bimaculatum

Agrostophyllum bimaculatum, commonly known as the two-spotted grass.

It is a flowering plant species native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia. The plant is characterized by its grass-like appearance with narrow, linear leaves and small, clustered flowers. It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate foliage and floral display.
