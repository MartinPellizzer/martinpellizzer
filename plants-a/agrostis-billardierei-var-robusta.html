Agrostis billardierei var. robusta

Agrostis billardierei var. robusta, commonly known as the robust tussock grass, is a perennial grass species native to Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, tufted growth habit and can reach heights of up to 60 cm. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands and grassy areas.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping.
