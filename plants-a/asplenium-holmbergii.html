Asplenium holmbergii

Asplenium holmbergii, commonly known as the Holmberg's spleenwort, is a small, evergreen fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Aspleniaceae and is characterized by its compact, rosette-like growth habit. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are usually dark green and densely arranged around the central stem. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found growing on rock surfaces or in shaded forest floors.

Asplenium holmbergii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant.
