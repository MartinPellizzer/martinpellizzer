Aloe darainensis

Aloe darainensis, commonly known as the Darain aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asphodelaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are thick and fleshy. The leaves are typically green with a bluish tint and have a waxy coating to reduce water loss. It produces tall, branched flower stalks that bear yellowish-green flowers, which bloom in the summer months.

This plant is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
