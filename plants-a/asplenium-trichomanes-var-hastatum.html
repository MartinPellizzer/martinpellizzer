Asplenium trichomanes var. hastatum

Asplenium trichomanes var. hastatum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf maidenhair fern, is a variant of the common maidenhair fern.

It is a small, evergreen fern that typically grows in shaded, moist environments. The leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a distinctive, finely divided structure. It has delicate, feathery fronds that are usually dark green and glossy.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
