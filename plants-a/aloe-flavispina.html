Aloe flavispina

Aloe flavispina, commonly known as the yellow-spined aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features spiny, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a yellowish or reddish tinge along the edges. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with clusters of yellow to orange flowers. Aloe flavispina is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick, fleshy leaves.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and resilience.
