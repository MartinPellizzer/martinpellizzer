Athanasia sessiliflora

Athanasia sessiliflora, commonly known as the rock lily, is a perennial flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of yellow to orange. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often clustered at the base of the plant.

Athanasia sessiliflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its resilience and beauty.
