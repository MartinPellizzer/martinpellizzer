Astronia rolfei

Astronia rolfei, commonly known as the Rolfe's aster, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its flowers are usually yellow to orange in color, with ray florets surrounding a central disk.

Astronia rolfei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
