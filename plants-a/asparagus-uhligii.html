Asparagus uhligii

Asparagus uhligii, commonly known as the Chinese asparagus, is a perennial herb native to China.

It features slender, arching stems that produce small, inconspicuous green flowers. The plant is characterized by its pinnately compound leaves, which are often used in traditional Chinese medicine. Its roots are valued for their medicinal properties, including potential anti-inflammatory and antioxidant effects.

Asparagus uhligii thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a versatile addition to gardens and landscapes.
