Alloxylon flammeum

Alloxylon flammeum, commonly known as the flame tree, is a striking tropical tree native to northern Australia.

It is characterized by its showy, fiery orange-red flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. The plant has a rugged, fibrous bark that becomes more prominent as it matures. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a rough texture.

The flame tree is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in arid conditions.
