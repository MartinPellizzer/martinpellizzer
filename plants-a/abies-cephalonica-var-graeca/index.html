Abies Cephalonica Var. Graeca

Abies cephalonica var. graeca is a rare and striking conifer native to the mountainous regions of northern Greece. This variant of the Greek fir is distinguished by its dense, pyramidal shape and dark, glossy needles. It thrives in cool, moist climates and is often found in high-altitude forests. Its unique appearance makes it a valuable species for both ecological and ornamental purposes. Conservation efforts are crucial to protect this plant from habitat loss and climate change.
