Artabotrys crassifolius

Artabotrys crassifolius, commonly known as the broad-leafed starfruit tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and clusters of fragrant white flowers. The plant produces small, yellow fruits that resemble star-shaped berries, hence its common name. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and edible fruit.

The tree is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal medicine.
