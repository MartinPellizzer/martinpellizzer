Armeria longearistata

Armeria longearistata, commonly known as the long-leaved thrift, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the family Caryophyllaceae and is characterized by its upright growth habit and dense clusters of small, white or pink flowers. Armeria longearistata is also valued for its ability to withstand harsh environmental conditions, including salt spray and drought.
