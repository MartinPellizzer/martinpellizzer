Ardisia subcuneifolia

Ardisia subcuneifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf beautyberry, is a species of flowering plant in the family Ericaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Indonesia and the Philippines. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and berries.
