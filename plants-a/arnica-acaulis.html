Arnica acaulis

Arnica acaulis, commonly known as the upright arnica, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Europe and western Asia.

It is characterized by its erect, unbranched stem that can grow up to 60 cm in height. The plant produces yellow-orange flowers with tubular corollas, which bloom from June to August. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, and it thrives in dry, sunny environments.

Arnica acaulis is often found in alpine meadows and rocky slopes at high elevations.
