Anthurium ricaurtense

Anthurium ricaurtense, commonly known as the Ricaurte's arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive spathe and spadix flower structures. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy, dark green leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Anthurium ricaurtense is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in botanical gardens.
