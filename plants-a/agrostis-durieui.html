Agrostis durieui

Agrostis durieui, commonly known as the Durieu's bentgrass, is a species of grass native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial plant with fine, slender leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in grassland habitats. It has a clump-forming growth habit and is characterized by its dense, mat-like appearance.

Agrostis durieui is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in horticultural settings.
