Aeonium × bravoanum

Aeonium × bravoanum, commonly known as the Bravo Aeonium, is a hybrid succulent plant belonging to the Crassulaceae family.

It is characterized by its rosette-forming structure, with thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces vibrant red or orange flowers during the summer months, adding visual interest to its appearance. Native to the Canary Islands, it thrives in well-draining soil and full sunlight.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in rock gardens.
