Aponogeton tenuispicatus

Aponogeton tenuispicatus, commonly known as the narrow-spined arrowhead, is a tropical aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features elongated, arrow-shaped leaves that are often submerged, with a distinctive narrow spines along the leaf margins. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom above the water surface, adding ornamental value to ponds and water gardens. It thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments with moderate to high light exposure.

Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense underwater foliage.
