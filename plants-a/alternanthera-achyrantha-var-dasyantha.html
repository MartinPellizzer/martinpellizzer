Alternanthera achyrantha var. dasyantha

Alternanthera achyrantha var. dasyantha, commonly known as the red-leaf alternanthera, is a striking aquatic or semi-aquatic plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red to purple foliage, which becomes more intense under bright light conditions. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a textured appearance. It produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are typically borne on slender stalks.

This variety is popular in aquariums and wetland gardens for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a range of water conditions.
