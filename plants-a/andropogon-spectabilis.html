Andropogon spectabilis

Andropogon spectabilis, commonly known as purple top grass, is a tall perennial grass native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its striking purple plumes that appear at the top of its slender, green stalks. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands, meadows, and along stream banks. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a smooth texture and a bluish-green color.

Andropogon spectabilis plays an important role in ecological systems by providing habitat and food for various wildlife species.
