Acilepis anaimudica

Acilepis anaimudica, commonly known as the Anaimudi acilepis, is a flowering plant native to the Western Ghats of India.

It is a shrub that typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often found in dense forests. The plant features small, clustered flowers that are usually white or pale yellow in color. Its leaves are simple and alternate, with a glossy surface and serrated edges.

Acilepis anaimudica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
