Anthurium undatum

Anthurium undatum, commonly known as the velvet leaf anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its dark green, velvety leaves that are typically heart-shaped or arrow-shaped. The plant produces striking spadices, which are often bright red or orange, surrounded by a showy spathe. Anthurium undatum thrives in humid, warm environments with bright, indirect light.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and as a houseplant.
