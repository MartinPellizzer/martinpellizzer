Aloe trachyticola

Aloe trachyticola, commonly known as the Cape aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its spiky, fleshy leaves that are often tinged with red or purple at the base. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellowish-green flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun exposure.

Aloe trachyticola is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its hardy nature.
