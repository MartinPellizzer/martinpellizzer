Aloe rhodacantha

Aloe rhodacantha, commonly known as the red aloe or red-tipped aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with red or pinkish tips. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing yellow flowers, which attract pollinators like bees and birds. Aloe rhodacantha is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil with full sun exposure.

Its striking appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
