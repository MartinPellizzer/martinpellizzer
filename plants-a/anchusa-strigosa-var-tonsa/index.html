Anchusa Strigosa Var. Tonsa

Anchusa strigosa var. tonsa is a striking member of the Boraginaceae family known for its vibrant blue to purple flowers. Native to southern Europe, this plant thrives in rocky and dry environments. Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders. The variety 'tonsa' is distinguished by its more robust stems and larger blooms compared to the typical form. This hardy perennial adds both color and texture to any garden setting.
