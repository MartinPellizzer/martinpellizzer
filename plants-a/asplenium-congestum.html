Asplenium congestum

Asplenium congestum, commonly known as the clustered spleenwort, is a small, evergreen fern native to the southeastern United States.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant has a compact, rosette-like growth form with closely packed, lance-shaped leaves. Its fronds are usually dark green and have a leathery texture.

Asplenium congestum is characterized by its dense, clustered appearance and is often found growing on rocks or tree trunks.
