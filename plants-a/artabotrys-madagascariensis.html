Artabotrys madagascariensis

Artabotrys madagascariensis, commonly known as the Madagascar candlewood, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Anacardiaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, candle-like inflorescences. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that are often clustered in elongated, candle-shaped clusters.
