Astroloba dodsoniana

Astroloba dodsoniana, commonly known as the star lily, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asphodelaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are often blue-gray in color. The plant produces striking, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and arid landscapes.
