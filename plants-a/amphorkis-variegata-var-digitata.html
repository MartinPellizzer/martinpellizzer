Amphorkis variegata var. digitata

Amphorkis variegata var. digitata, commonly known as the variegated amphorkis, is a unique variant of the amphorkis genus.

It is characterized by its striking variegation in leaf patterns, which often includes shades of white, yellow, and green. The plant exhibits a compact growth habit with elongated, lance-shaped leaves that are often adorned with intricate markings. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires moderate to bright indirect light for optimal growth.

This variety is prized in horticulture for its ornamental value and distinctive foliage.
