Astrocaryum munbaca

Astrocaryum munbaca, commonly known as the pink palm, is a tropical plant native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate leaves that can reach up to 3 meters in length. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by edible, orange-colored fruits that are rich in nutrients. Its unique appearance and ecological significance make it an important species in its native habitat.

The plant is also valued for its ornamental qualities and traditional uses in local communities.
