Astilbe crenatiloba

Astilbe crenatiloba, commonly known as the serrated astilbe, is a herbaceous perennial native to the mountainous regions of China.

It features delicate, feather-like panicles of pale pink to white flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has lance-shaped, dark green leaves with serrated edges, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in partial to full shade and prefers moist, well-drained soil.

Astilbe crenatiloba is valued in gardens for its elegant appearance and ability to add color to shaded landscapes.
