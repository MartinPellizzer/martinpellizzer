Anthurium polyrrhizon

Anthurium polyrrhizon, commonly known as the red-veined anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped leaves with striking red veins against a deep green background. The plant produces spadices, which are inflorescences that are typically green with a red or orange spathe. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.

Anthurium polyrrhizon is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
