Alternanthera costaricensis

Alternanthera costaricensis, commonly known as the Costa Rican Alternanthera, is a striking aquatic or semi-aquatic plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red, pink, or purple foliage, which becomes more intense under strong light conditions. The plant typically grows in slow-moving freshwater environments such as ponds and wetlands. Its leaves are opposite, ovate to lanceolate, and often have a waxy texture.

It is popular in aquariums for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of water conditions.
