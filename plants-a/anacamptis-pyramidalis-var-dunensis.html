Anacamptis pyramidalis var. dunensis

Anacamptis pyramidalis var. dunensis, commonly known as the Dun Valley Orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender stems that bear a dense cluster of violet to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in calcareous soils and thrives in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and coastal areas. Its flowers are arranged in a pyramidal shape, giving it the species name "pyramidalis.".

The variety 'dunensis' is distinguished by its specific geographic distribution and slightly different floral morphology compared to other variants of the species.
