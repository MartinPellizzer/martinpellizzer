Aeonium × banaresii

Aeonium × banaresii, commonly known as the Orange Tree Aeonium, is a succulent plant native to the Canary Islands.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with red or orange hues, especially under sunlight. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences that bear yellow flowers in the spring. It is a hybrid between Aeonium arborense and Aeonium haworthii, known for its striking appearance and relatively low maintenance.

Aeonium × banaresii is well-suited for rock gardens or as a container plant due to its compact size and ornamental value.
