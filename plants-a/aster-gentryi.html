Aster gentryi

Aster gentryi, commonly known as Gentry's aster, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in arid or semi-arid environments. Its flowers are usually blue to purple in color, with ray florets surrounding a central disk.

Aster gentryi is known for its ornamental value and is often found in wildflower gardens and natural habitats.
