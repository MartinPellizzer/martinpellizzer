Andrographis cordifolia

Andrographis cordifolia, commonly known as heartleaf andrographis, is a herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Acanthaceae and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and slender, branching stems. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color. It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest undergrowth.

Andrographis cordifolia is valued for its medicinal properties, particularly in traditional medicine systems.
