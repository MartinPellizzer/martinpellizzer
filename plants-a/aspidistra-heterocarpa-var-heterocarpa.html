Aspidistra heterocarpa var. heterocarpa

Aspidistra heterocarpa var. heterocarpa, commonly known as the broad-leaf aspidistra, is a striking evergreen plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It features dark green, broad, and lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and often marked with silver veins. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are typically borne on a spadix surrounded by a spathe. It thrives in low-light conditions and prefers moist, well-draining soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and shaded outdoor spaces.
