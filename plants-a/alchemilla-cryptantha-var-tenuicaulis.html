Alchemilla cryptantha var. tenuicaulis

Alchemilla cryptantha var. tenuicaulis, commonly known as slender-stemmed lady's mantle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of central and eastern Europe.

It features rosettes of lance-shaped, hairy leaves that are often tinged with silver, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in terminal panicles during the summer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or meadow habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal potential, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
