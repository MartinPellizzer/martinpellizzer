Aspalathus dubia

Aspalathus dubia, commonly known as the Cape daisy, is a species native to South Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows in coastal areas and sandy soils. The plant features small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often covered in fine hairs.

Aspalathus dubia is adapted to withstand coastal conditions, including salt spray and strong winds.
