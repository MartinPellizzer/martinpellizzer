Arrabidaea subverticillata

Arrabidaea subverticillata, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a deciduous tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, bright red flowers that appear in clusters, often resembling flames. The plant has pinnate leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets, and its bark is smooth and greyish in color. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it a popular ornamental plant in tropical and subtropical regions.

The tree is also valued for its fast growth and ability to provide shade in landscaping.
