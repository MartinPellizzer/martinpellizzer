Astilbe × alba

Astilbe × alba, commonly known as the white feather astilbe, is a hybrid perennial plant native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its upright, branched stems that bear plume-like inflorescences of white to pale pink flowers. The plant thrives in partial to full shade and prefers moist, well-drained soil. Its foliage is typically dark green and textured, adding visual interest throughout the growing season.

Astilbe × alba is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in woodland settings.
