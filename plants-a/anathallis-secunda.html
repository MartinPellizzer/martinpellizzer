Anathallis secunda

Anathallis secunda, commonly known as the velvet orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its unique, hairy appearance, with leaves covered in dense, velvety pubescence. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are often clustered together in inflorescences. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, typically found in rainforests.

Anathallis secunda is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
