Arundinella effusa

Arundinella effusa, commonly known as the slender bamboo, is a perennial herb native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, hollow stems that resemble bamboo, often growing in dense clumps. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in spike-like inflorescences. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a smooth surface and a mild aromatic scent.

Arundinella effusa is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
