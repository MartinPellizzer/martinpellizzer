Arisaema petelotii

Arisaema petelotii, commonly known as the Petelot's arisaema, is a species of flowering plant in the Araceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Thailand and Vietnam. The plant is characterized by its distinctive leaf structure, featuring a large, heart-shaped leaf with a prominent midrib. Its inflorescence consists of a spadix surrounded by a spathe, typical of the Araceae family.

Arisaema petelotii is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its unique foliage and striking floral display.
