Artanthe mitis

Artanthe mitis, commonly known as the silverleaf artanthe, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and showy, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes. Its leaves are covered in fine, silky hairs that give it a distinctive appearance.

Artanthe mitis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its visual appeal.
