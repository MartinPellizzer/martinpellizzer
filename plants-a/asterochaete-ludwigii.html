Asterochaete ludwigii

Asterochaete ludwigii, commonly known as the Ludwig's aster, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in grasslands and rocky areas, thriving in well-drained soils. Its flowers are daisy-like, with white petals and a yellow center, blooming in spring and summer.

Asterochaete ludwigii is known for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
