Arisaema koidzumianum

Arisaema koidzumianum, commonly known as the Koidzumi arrowhead, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

It features a distinctive arrowhead-shaped leaf that is often deeply lobed, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces a spadix surrounded by a spathe, a characteristic feature of the Araceae family. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its unique morphology and limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists alike.
