Aconogonon valerii

Aconogonon valerii, commonly known as the valerian vine, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a climbing perennial that produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and thrives in warm, humid environments. It is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and pleasant scent.

Aconogonon valerii is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal remedies.
