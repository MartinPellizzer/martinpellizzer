Alternanthera paronychioides var. robusta

Alternanthera paronychioides var. robusta, commonly known as the red-stemmed Alternanthera, is a striking aquatic plant characterized by its vivid red or pink stems and lance-shaped leaves.

It is native to South America and thrives in freshwater environments with moderate to high light conditions. The plant is known for its rapid growth rate, making it a popular choice for pond and aquarium landscaping. Its foliage often displays a striking contrast between the deep green leaves and the colorful stems.

It is relatively easy to care for, requiring regular pruning to maintain its shape and prevent overcrowding.
