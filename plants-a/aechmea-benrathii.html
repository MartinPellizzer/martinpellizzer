Aechmea benrathii

Aechmea benrathii, commonly known as the Benrath's bromeliad, is a flowering plant native to the cloud forests of South America.

It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaf structure that collects water. The plant features a striking inflorescence with brightly colored bracts that surround the small, white flowers. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Aechmea benrathii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its aesthetic appeal and unique foliage.
