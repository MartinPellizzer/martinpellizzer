Alocasia hainanensis

Alocasia hainanensis, commonly known as the Hainan elephant ear, is a tropical plant native to southern China.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 50 cm in length. The plant belongs to the Araceae family, which includes many ornamental and edible species. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, fertile soil.

Due to its striking foliage, it is often cultivated as a decorative plant in gardens and indoor spaces.
