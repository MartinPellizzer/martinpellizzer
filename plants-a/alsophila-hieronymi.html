Alsophila hieronymi

Alsophila hieronymi, commonly known as the Hieronymi's tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, unbranched trunk and large, pinnately divided fronds that can reach several meters in length. The plant belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is known for its slow growth and long lifespan. Its fronds are typically green on the upper surface and lighter in color on the underside, with sporangia developing on the margins of the fronds.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens and greenhouses.
