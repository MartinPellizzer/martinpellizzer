Achimenes ichthyostoma

Achimenes ichthyostoma, commonly known as the silverleaf achimenes, is a tropical perennial plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, pink, and white. The plant features heart-shaped, velvety leaves that are often covered in a silvery or grayish bloom, giving it its common name. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Achimenes ichthyostoma is often cultivated as a decorative plant for gardens and indoor spaces due to its ornamental value.
