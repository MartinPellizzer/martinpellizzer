Artabotrys klainei

Artabotrys klainei, commonly known as the golden flame tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, pendulous yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, creating a dramatic visual display. The plant has simple, ovate leaves with a smooth texture and dark green color. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release small, hairy seeds.
