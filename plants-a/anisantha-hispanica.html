Anisantha hispanica

Anisantha hispanica, commonly known as the Spanish anisantha, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its small, daisy-like flowers that typically bloom in the summer months. The plant has linear, grass-like leaves and produces small, oval-shaped seeds. It is native to the Iberian Peninsula and thrives in sunny, well-drained soils.

Anisantha hispanica is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed areas.
