Atropis distans f. glaberrima

Atropis distans f. glaberrima, commonly known as the glabrous atropis, is a unique variant of the Atropis distans species.

It is characterized by its smooth, hairless surface, distinguishing it from other forms of the plant. This fern-like plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its leaves are elongated and feathery, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

The plant's botanical structure reflects adaptations to its specific ecological niche, making it a subject of interest in botanical studies.
