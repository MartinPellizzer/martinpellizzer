Aristolochia schweinfurthii

Aristolochia schweinfurthii, commonly known as the African birthwort, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, aromatic leaves and distinctive, hooded flowers that resemble a pipe or a skull. The plant belongs to the Aristolochiaceae family and is known for its complex chemical composition, including various alkaloids. It thrives in humid, shady environments and is often found in forests and along riverbanks.

Due to its ornamental value and traditional medicinal uses, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and herbal practices.
