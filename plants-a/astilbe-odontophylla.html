Astilbe odontophylla

Astilbe odontophylla, commonly known as the toothed astilbe, is a herbaceous perennial native to eastern Asia.

It features upright, lance-shaped leaves that are often bluish-green in color. The plant produces panicles of pink to white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, shady conditions and prefers well-drained, loamy soil.

Astilbe odontophylla is valued in gardens for its ornamental foliage and delicate floral display.
