Aphanelytrum peruvianum

Aphanelytrum peruvianum, commonly known as the Peruvian feather grass, is a perennial grass native to South America.

It is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and fine, silky leaves that give it a delicate appearance. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that are often mistaken for foliage due to their subtle structure. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along streams.

Aphanelytrum peruvianum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is used in landscaping for its soft, flowing texture.
