Anotis teysmanniana

Anotis teysmanniana, commonly known as the mountain daisy, is a flowering plant native to New Guinea and surrounding regions.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and forest margins. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, while its stems are erect and often branched.

Anotis teysmanniana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
