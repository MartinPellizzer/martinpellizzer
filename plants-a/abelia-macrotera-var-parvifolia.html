Abelia macrotera var. parvifolia

Abelia macrotera var. parvifolia, commonly known as the dwarf abelia, is a compact shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It features small, white to pinkish flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The leaves are ovate, glossy, and have a slightly serrated edge. It prefers partial shade to full sun and well-drained soil.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in USDA zones 6 to 9.
