Amphilophis intermedia var. acidula

Amphilophis intermedia var. acidula, commonly known as the sour apple tree, is a member of the Rosaceae family and is native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree characterized by its elliptical, leathery leaves and fragrant white flowers.
