Adiantum cuneatum foliis-variegatis

Adiantum cuneatum foliis-variegatis, commonly known as the variegated maidenhair fern, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, fern-like fronds that are often variegated with shades of green and gold. The plant features pinnate leaves with a distinctive, finely divided structure. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, moist soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardening.
