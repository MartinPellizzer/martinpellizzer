Anil nigritana

Anil nigritana, commonly known as the African blackwood, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its dark, heavy wood and can grow up to 25 meters in height. The leaves are alternate, simple, and elliptic in shape, with a smooth margin. It produces small, fragrant yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by small, round fruits.

The plant is valued for its hard timber and is also used in traditional medicine.
