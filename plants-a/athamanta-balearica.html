Athamanta balearica

Athamanta balearica, commonly known as the Balearic bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the Balearic Islands.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in rocky or coastal habitats. The plant produces showy blue or violet flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it has a compact growth habit.

Athamanta balearica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often found in gardens and naturalized areas.
