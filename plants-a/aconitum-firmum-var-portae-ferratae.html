Aconitum firmum var. portae-ferratae

Aconitum firmum var. portae-ferratae, commonly known as the Swiss Alps aconite, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the alpine regions of Europe.

It features striking blue to purple flowers that bloom in late summer, typically from July to August. The plant has a robust root system and lance-shaped leaves that are deeply lobed. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is often found in mountainous habitats.

Due to its toxic properties, it should be handled with care.
