Asplenium fontanum var. exiguum

Asplenium fontanum var. exiguum, commonly known as the small shield fern, is a compact fern species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features small, leathery, shield-shaped fronds that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant thrives in humid environments with indirect light and well-draining, moist soil. Its rhizomes are short and help anchor it to rocky or shaded surfaces.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in shaded garden settings.
