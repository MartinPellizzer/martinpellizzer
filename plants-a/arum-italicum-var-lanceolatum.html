Arum italicum var. lanceolatum

Arum italicum var. lanceolatum, commonly known as the Italian arum or arrowleaf arum, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features a distinctive arrow-shaped leaf with a deep green color and a prominent midrib. The plant produces a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe, which is typical of the Araceae family. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires consistently moist soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in tropical gardens and indoor spaces.
