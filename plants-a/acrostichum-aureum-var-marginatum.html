Acrostichum aureum var. marginatum

Acrostichum aureum var. marginatum, commonly known as the golden marginal fern, is a variant of the golden fern native to tropical regions.

It is a herbaceous plant with fronds that have a distinctive golden margin, contrasting with its dark green central portion. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks. Its leaves are typically divided into narrow, elongated segments, giving it a feathery appearance.

The golden marginal fern is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with high humidity.
