Alsophila truncata

Alsophila truncata, commonly known as the dwarf tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, unbranched trunk and a crown of feathery, compound fronds. The fronds are typically dark green and can reach lengths of up to 2 meters. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

The plant has a slow growth rate and is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.
