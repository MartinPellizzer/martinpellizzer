Alocasia eminens

Alocasia eminens, commonly known as the giant arrowroot, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length, with a deep green color and prominent veins. The plant belongs to the Araceae family, which includes other ornamental plants like calla lilies. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Alocasia eminens is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its impressive foliage and ability to add a dramatic touch to indoor or outdoor spaces.
