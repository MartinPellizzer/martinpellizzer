Astragalus kalatehensis

Astragalus kalatehensis, commonly known as Kalateh Mountain milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Iran.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its erect stem and pinnately compound leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in summer, and its seeds are enclosed in hairy pods.

Astragalus kalatehensis is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a resilient species in its native habitat.
