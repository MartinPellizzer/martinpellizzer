Aster graveolens var. oliganthus

Aster graveolens var. oliganthus, commonly known as the Chinese aster, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant typically grows to a height of 30 to 60 centimeters and features narrow, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.

The flowers are often used in ornamental gardening and can also be found in traditional herbal medicine.
