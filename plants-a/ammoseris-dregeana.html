Ammoseris dregeana

Ammoseris dregeana, commonly known as the desert lily, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in arid and semi-arid environments. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of white, yellow, or orange. It is adapted to survive in drought conditions with a deep root system.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitat.
