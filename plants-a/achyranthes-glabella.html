Achyranthes glabella

Achyranthes glabella, commonly known as the blunt achyranthes, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a member of the Amaranthaceae family and typically grows as an annual herb. The plant features upright stems and lance-shaped leaves with a smooth texture. Its small, clustered flowers are usually greenish or reddish in color.

Achyranthes glabella is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
