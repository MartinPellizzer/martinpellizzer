Alsine brachypetala

Alsine brachypetala, commonly known as narrowleaf sandwort, is a small annual herb native to arid and semi-arid regions of North America.

It typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and is adapted to drought conditions. The plant has small, fleshy leaves and produces clusters of tiny white flowers. Its flowering period occurs in late spring to early summer.

Alsine brachypetala is often found in disturbed areas and is considered a characteristic species of dry, open habitats.
