Astragalus haiyuanensis

Astragalus haiyuanensis, commonly known as the Haiyuan milkvetch, is a flowering plant species native to China.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. This plant is typically found in arid and semi-arid regions, thriving in rocky and sandy soils.

Astragalus haiyuanensis plays a role in soil stabilization and is of interest for its potential medicinal properties.
