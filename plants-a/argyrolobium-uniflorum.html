Argyrolobium uniflorum

Argyrolobium uniflorum, commonly known as the single-flowered silver globular pea, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its globular, silver-gray foliage and a single, showy white or pale pink flower that appears at the end of each stem. The plant belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and it has a distinctive, compact growth habit. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and unique appearance, it is cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
