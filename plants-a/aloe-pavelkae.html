Aloe pavelkae

Aloe pavelkae, commonly known as the Cape Peninsula aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, branching stems and fleshy, sword-shaped leaves that are typically green with a reddish tinge. The plant produces striking red or orange flowers in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Aloe pavelkae is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable growing conditions.
