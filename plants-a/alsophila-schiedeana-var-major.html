Alsophila schiedeana var. major

Alsophila schiedeana var. major, commonly known as the giant tree fern, is a striking member of the Cyatheaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly in countries like Brazil and Peru. The plant features a tall, unbranched trunk covered in persistent, fibrous roots, with a crown of large, feather-like fronds that can grow up to several meters in length. Its fronds are typically dark green and have a leathery texture, contributing to its ornamental value.

This species is often cultivated in warm, humid climates for its aesthetic appeal and ability to thrive in shaded environments.
