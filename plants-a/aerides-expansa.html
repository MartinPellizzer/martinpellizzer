Aerides expansa

Aerides expansa, commonly known as the broad-leafed orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, leathery leaves and produces fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, thriving in humid, warm environments. Its flowers bloom in clusters and are often used in floral arrangements due to their beauty.

Aerides expansa is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
