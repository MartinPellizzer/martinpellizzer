Aerides arachnites

Aerides arachnites, commonly known as the spider orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in the Philippines and Indonesia. This terrestrial orchid features slender, hairy pseudobulbs and produces delicate, spider-like flowers with intricate patterns. The plant typically blooms in spring, and its flowers are often pale green or yellow with red markings.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
