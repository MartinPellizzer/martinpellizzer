Astragalus kohrudicus

Astragalus kohrudicus, commonly known as the Kohrud milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Iran.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, pea-like flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky and arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions.

It plays a role in soil stabilization and is of interest for its potential ecological and medicinal properties.
