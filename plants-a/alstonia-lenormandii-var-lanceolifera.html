Alstonia lenormandii var. lanceolifera

Alstonia lenormandii var. lanceolifera, commonly known as the lance-leaf alstonia, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It features large, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces clusters of small, white, fragrant flowers followed by elongated, woody pods containing flat, winged seeds. It thrives in humid climates with well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in tropical and subtropical gardens.
