Anacampseros arachnoides

Anacampseros arachnoides, commonly known as the woolly ice plant, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, blue-green leaves covered in a dense layer of fine, white hairs. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Due to its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements, it is well-suited for arid and semi-arid environments.
