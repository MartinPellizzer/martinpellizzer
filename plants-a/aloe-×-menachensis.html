Aloe × menachensis

Aloe × menachensis, commonly known as the hybrid aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is a naturally occurring hybrid between Aloe ferox and Aloe barberae, resulting in a plant with unique characteristics. The leaves are thick, fleshy, and often have a serrated edge, adapted to store water in arid environments. It produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow flowers, typically blooming in spring.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional uses in medicine.
