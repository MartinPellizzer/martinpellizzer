Areca jugahpunya

Areca jugahpunya, commonly known as the red areca palm, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feathery leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by edible fruits that are often used in traditional cuisine. Areca jugahpunya thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in tropical regions.
