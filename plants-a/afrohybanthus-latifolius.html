Afrohybanthus latifolius

Afrohybanthus latifolius, commonly known as the broadleaf hybanthus, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in coastal areas and sandy soils. The plant produces small, fragrant white or yellow flowers that bloom throughout the year. Its leaves are broad and oval, with a smooth texture and a slightly waxy surface.

Afrohybanthus latifolius is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
