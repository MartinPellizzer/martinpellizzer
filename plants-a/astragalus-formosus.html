Astragalus formosus

Astragalus formosus, commonly known as the Taiwan milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. It is known for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, contributing to soil fertility.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
