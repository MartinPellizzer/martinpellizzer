Aeonium decorum var. alucense

Aeonium decorum var. alucense, commonly known as the Alucense Aeonium, is a succulent plant native to the Canary Islands.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, with a waxy texture. The plant produces striking pink to red flowers on tall, branching stems during the spring or early summer. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its unique appearance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
