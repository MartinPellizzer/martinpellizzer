Anisomeris ribesioides var. villosula

Anisomeris ribesioides var. villosula, commonly known as the hairy anisomeris, is a succulent plant native to South America.

It features fleshy, ribbed stems that are densely covered in fine, white hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its adaptability to harsh conditions makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
