Anthurium hookeri

Anthurium hookeri, commonly known as the Hooker's anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped spathe that comes in a variety of colors, including red, orange, and yellow. The plant's leaves are typically large, glossy, and deeply veined, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in humid, warm environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium hookeri is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its striking appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.
