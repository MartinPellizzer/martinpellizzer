Alternanthera paronychioides var. boliviana

Alternanthera paronychioides var. boliviana, commonly known as the Bolivian Alternanthera, is a striking aquatic plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red and pink foliage, which intensifies under strong light conditions. The plant typically grows in shallow waters, forming dense, bushy clusters that provide excellent habitat for small aquatic life. Its leaves are opposite and ovate, with a smooth surface and a slightly serrated edge.

This variety is popular in aquariums for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a range of water conditions.
