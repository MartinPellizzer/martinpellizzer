Anethum

Anethum, commonly known as dill, is a herbaceous plant belonging to the Apiaceae family.

It is characterized by its finely divided, feathery leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers that form umbrella-like clusters. The plant is native to southern Europe and western Asia, and it thrives in temperate climates. Dill is widely used in culinary applications for its aromatic seeds and leaves.

Its botanical features make it a valuable addition to both gardens and kitchens.
