Anarthrophyllum andicola

Anarthrophyllum andicola, commonly known as the Andean moss plant, is a species of moss that thrives in high-altitude environments.

It is native to the Andes Mountains and is adapted to cool, moist conditions. This plant is characterized by its dense, cushion-like growth form, which helps it retain moisture and withstand strong winds. It plays a vital role in stabilizing soil in its native habitat.

Anarthrophyllum andicola is often found in rocky or alpine areas where other plants struggle to survive.
