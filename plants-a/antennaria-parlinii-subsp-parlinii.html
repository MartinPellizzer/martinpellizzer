Antennaria parlinii subsp. parlinii

Antennaria parlinii subsp.

parlinii, commonly known as Parlin's mouse-ear, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the western United States. It belongs to the family Compositae (Asteraceae) and is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit.
