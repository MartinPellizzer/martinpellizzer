Anthemis Tenuiloba Var. Radiata

Radiate anthemis, scientifically known as Anthemis tenuiloba var. radiata, is a striking member of the Asteraceae family that captivates with its intricate floral structure. Native to the arid regions of southern Africa, this plant thrives in harsh conditions with minimal water and intense sunlight. Its unique appearance features small, daisy-like flowers with a vibrant yellow center and delicate white petals that radiate outward, giving it its name. Despite its modest size, Radiate anthemis holds cultural and medicinal significance in local communities. This resilient plant serves as both an ornamental and a symbol of adaptability in challenging environments.
