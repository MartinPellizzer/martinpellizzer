Ajuga iva var. robertiana

Ajuga iva var. robertiana, commonly known as carpet bugleweed, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the eastern United States.

It features dense, mat-forming foliage with heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and slightly hairy. The plant produces small, tubular flowers in shades of blue to purple, which bloom in early to mid-summer. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Its compact growth habit makes it an excellent ground cover for erosion control and garden landscaping.
