Arachnorchis Rigida

Arachnorchis rigida is a unique and striking orchid native to the rainforests of eastern Australia. Known for its intricate, spider-like flowers, it captivates botanists and nature enthusiasts alike. This plant thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires specific conditions to flourish. Its conservation status is of great concern due to habitat loss and environmental changes. Studying Arachnorchis rigida offers valuable insights into the biodiversity of Australian flora.
