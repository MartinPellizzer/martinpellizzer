Astragalus nanfengensis

Astragalus nanfengensis, commonly known as the Nanfeng milkvetch, is a flowering plant species native to China.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its erect stems and small, pea-like flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its small, oval-shaped leaves are often covered in fine hairs, and it produces pods that contain several seeds.

Astragalus nanfengensis is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphology and potential ecological significance in its native habitat.
