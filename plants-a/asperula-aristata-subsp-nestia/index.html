Asperula Aristata Subsp. Nestia

Asperula aristata subsp. nestia is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of western China. It is known for its small, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white during the spring. This subspecies is often found in rocky habitats and is adapted to cool, moist environments. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species. Conservation efforts are crucial to ensure its survival in the wild.
