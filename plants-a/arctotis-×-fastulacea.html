Arctotis × fastulacea

Arctotis × fastulacea, commonly known as the African daisy, is a hybrid perennial plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, daisy-like flowers that come in a range of colors including red, orange, yellow, and pink. The plant typically grows to a height of 30–60 cm and produces showy, single or double flowers with a central yellow disk. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.

Its botanical features make it both ornamental and ecologically valuable in its native habitat.
