Asterolinon

Asterolinon, commonly known as the silverleaf tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, silvery-green leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. Asterolinon is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.

Its growth habit is usually shrub-like, with a dense canopy that provides shade in its natural habitat.
