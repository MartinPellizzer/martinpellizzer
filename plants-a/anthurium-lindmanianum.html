Anthurium lindmanianum

Anthurium lindmanianum, commonly known as the Lindman's anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a reddish or purple sheen. The plant produces vibrant red spathes that enclose its small, spadix-like inflorescence, which is a common feature among Anthurium species. It thrives in humid, warm environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.

The plant is popular as a decorative foliage plant due to its ornamental leaves and relatively easy care requirements.
