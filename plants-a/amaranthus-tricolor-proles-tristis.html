Amaranthus tricolor proles tristis

Amaranthus tricolor proles tristis, commonly known as red root amaranth, is a species of flowering plant in the amaranth family.

It is characterized by its triangular, ribbed stems and leaves that can vary in color from green to red or purple. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically clustered in dense inflorescences. It is native to the Americas and is often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
