Anthericum recurvifolium

Anthericum recurvifolium, commonly known as the recurved lily of the valley, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It features a rosette of narrow, recurved leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces clusters of small, bell-shaped white flowers in late spring. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Its botanical characteristics make it a distinctive and ornamental plant in its native habitat.
