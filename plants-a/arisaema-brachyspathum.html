Arisaema brachyspathum

Arisaema brachyspathum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved arrowhead, is a herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its unique inflorescence consisting of a spadix surrounded by a spathe. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream sides. Its leaves are heart-shaped with a narrow base, and it produces striking, often colorful flowers.

Arisaema brachyspathum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
