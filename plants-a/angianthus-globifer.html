Angianthus globifer

Angianthus globifer, commonly known as the globe angianthus, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Acanthaceae and is characterized by its showy, clustered flowers that bloom in shades of pink and purple. The plant features upright stems with lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Angianthus globifer is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
