Argyrochosma jonesii

Argyrochosma jonesii, commonly known as Jones' silverleaf.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the western United States, particularly found in rocky and arid environments. The plant features silver-gray foliage due to dense pubescence, which helps reflect sunlight and reduce water loss. Its flowers are typically small and yellow, arranged in clusters, and bloom in early summer.

It is adapted to thrive in well-drained soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats.
