Andromeda cassinefolia

Andromeda cassinefolia, commonly known as the Florida andromeda, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows in wet, acidic soils along the edges of swamps and marshes. The plant features showy, pink to red flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. Its leaves are opposite, elliptical, and have a glossy appearance.

Andromeda cassinefolia plays an important role in wetland ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various wildlife species.
