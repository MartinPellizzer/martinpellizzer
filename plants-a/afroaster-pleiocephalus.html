Afroaster pleiocephalus

Afroaster pleiocephalus, commonly known as the African aster, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and produces showy yellow-orange flowers. Its foliage consists of narrow, linear leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem.

Afroaster pleiocephalus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
