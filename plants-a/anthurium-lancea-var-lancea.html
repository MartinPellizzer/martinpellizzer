Anthurium lancea var. lancea

Anthurium lancea var. lancea, commonly known as the lance-leaf anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its elongated, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy surface. The plant produces striking, spadix-shaped inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, often in shades of red, orange, or yellow. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium lancea var. lancea is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and long-lasting blooms.
