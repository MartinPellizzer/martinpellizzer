Anthurium marleenianum

Anthurium marleenianum, commonly known as the Marleen's Arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are often a deep green with a reddish or purple tint. The plant produces inflorescences consisting of a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe, which can range from white to pink or red. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Anthurium marleenianum is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and air-purifying qualities.
