Agrostis rigescens

Agrostis rigescens, commonly known as rigid bentgrass, is a perennial grass species native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It typically grows in moist, temperate climates and is often found in meadows, grasslands, and along stream banks. The plant has slender, upright stems that are often covered in fine, silky hairs. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green coloration.

Agrostis rigescens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping and turfgrass management.
