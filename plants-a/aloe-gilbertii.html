Aloe gilbertii

Aloe gilbertii, commonly known as Gilbert's aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a waxy coating, which helps it store water. The plant produces tall, branched flower stalks that bear yellowish-green flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Aloe gilbertii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
