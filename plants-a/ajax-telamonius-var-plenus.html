Ajax telamonius var. plenus

Ajax telamonius var. plenus, commonly known as the Full-flowered Ajax, is a striking perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense, showy flower heads that bloom in shades of pink to red. The plant features narrow, upright leaves and produces small, fluffy seeds that are dispersed by the wind. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming period, typically from late spring to early autumn.
