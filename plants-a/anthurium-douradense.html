Anthurium douradense

Anthurium douradense, commonly known as the Douradense Arrowhead, is a striking tropical plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its unique inflorescence consisting of a spadix and a spathe. The plant features broad, arrow-shaped leaves that are often a vibrant green with a waxy texture. Its flowers are typically small and inconspicuous, with the showy spathe serving as the primary ornamental feature.

Anthurium douradense is valued in horticulture for its foliage and is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant.
