Aloe roeoeslii

Aloe roeoeslii, commonly known as the Cape aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asphodelaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are thick and fleshy. The leaves are typically green with white or yellowish markings, and they can grow up to 30 centimeters in length. This plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in summer, and it is well adapted to arid environments.

Aloe roeoeslii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional medicinal uses.
