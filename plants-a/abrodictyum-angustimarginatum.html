Abrodictyum angustimarginatum

Abrodictyum angustimarginatum, commonly known as the narrow-margin orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and linear, grass-like leaves. The plant produces delicate, pale green to yellowish flowers arranged in a raceme, which bloom during the cooler months. It prefers well-drained soil and partial shade, often growing in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species in its native range.
