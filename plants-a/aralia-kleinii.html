Aralia kleinii

Aralia kleinii, commonly known as the dwarf aralia, is a perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of East Asia.

It belongs to the Araliaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, clustered white flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30-60 cm, making it a compact and ornamental choice for gardens. Its foliage is often variegated, adding visual interest, while its growth habit is upright and bushy.

Aralia kleinii prefers well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it suitable for woodland gardens.
