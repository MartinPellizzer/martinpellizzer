Adhatoda camerunensis

Adhatoda camerunensis, commonly known as African Trumpet or Cameroun Trumpet, is a perennial shrub native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Acanthaceae and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from purple to pink. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy surface and produces small, capsule-like fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forest understories or along streambanks.

Adhatoda camerunensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and has traditional medicinal uses in some African communities.
