Angiopteris howii

Angiopteris howii, commonly known as the Howii water fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, feathery fronds that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant has a distinctive, creeping rhizome system that allows it to spread along the ground. Its leaves are typically bipinnate, with a delicate, lace-like appearance.

Angiopteris howii thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found near streams or in dense forest undergrowth.
