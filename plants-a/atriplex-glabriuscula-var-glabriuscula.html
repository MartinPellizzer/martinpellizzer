Atriplex glabriuscula var. glabriuscula

Atriplex glabriuscula var. glabriuscula, commonly known as silverleaf chenopod, is a shrub native to arid and semi-arid regions of North America.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, scale-like hairs that reflect sunlight and reduce water loss. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in clusters, and its seed capsules are often used as a food source by birds and small mammals. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to withstand drought and high salinity.

This species plays an important ecological role in stabilizing soils and providing habitat in desert ecosystems.
