Aster kawaguchii

Aster kawaguchii, commonly known as the Kawaguchii aster, is a perennial herb native to Japan.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that bloom in late summer to early fall. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in woodland or mountainous areas. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers range in color from white to pale blue.

Aster kawaguchii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate beauty.
