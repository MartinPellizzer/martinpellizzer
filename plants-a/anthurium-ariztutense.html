Anthurium ariztutense

Anthurium ariztutense, commonly known as the Mexican flamingo flower, is a tropical plant native to Mexico and Central America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves and vibrant, spadix-like inflorescences that range in color from red to orange. The plant thrives in humid, warm environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. It is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its ornamental foliage and unique floral structure.

Anthurium ariztutense is also valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
