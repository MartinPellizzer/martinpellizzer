Andinia ricii

Andinia ricii, commonly known as the golden star, is a striking flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its bright yellow, star-shaped flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has a short, upright stem with broad, lance-shaped leaves that are a deep green. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

Andinia ricii is valued for its ornamental beauty and ease of cultivation.
