Arachnorchis rigida

Arachnorchis rigida, commonly known as the rigid spider-orchid, is an epiphytic orchid native to Australia.

It is characterized by its unique, spider-like inflorescence that gives it its common name. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers with a distinctive, intricate pattern on the petals. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found growing on trees or rocks.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
