Ambulia trichophylla

Ambulia trichophylla, commonly known as the hairy leaf ambulia, is a unique species of flowering plant native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, hairy foliage and small, clustered flowers that bloom in shades of white and pale yellow. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests. Its leaves are broad and have a velvety texture, while its stems are woody and somewhat creeping.

Ambulia trichophylla is of interest to botanists due to its unusual morphology and potential medicinal properties.
