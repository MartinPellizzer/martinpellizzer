Aglaonema subarborescens

Aglaonema subarborescens, commonly known as the Shiny Arrowroot or Emerald Beauty, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features dark green, glossy, arrowhead-shaped leaves with striking variegation of white and cream patterns. The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit and can reach heights of up to 3 meters in its natural habitat. It produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers, though these are rarely seen in cultivation.

Aglaonema subarborescens is valued for its ornamental foliage and is commonly cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.
