Amauropelta deminuta

Amauropelta deminuta, commonly known as the small shield fern, is a small, terrestrial fern native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and rocky crevices. The plant has a short, creeping rhizome with closely spaced, shield-shaped fronds. Its fronds are usually less than 10 centimeters in length and have a delicate, feathery appearance.

Amauropelta deminuta is characterized by its unique morphology and is often found in humid, tropical climates.
