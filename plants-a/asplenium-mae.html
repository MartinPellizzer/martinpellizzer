Asplenium mae

Asplenium mae, commonly known as the rock fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Aspleniaceae.

It is characterized by its small, leathery leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found on rocky surfaces or in crevices. Its fronds are usually dark green and have a rough texture, which helps it retain moisture.

Asplenium mae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with suitable conditions.
