Asarum sieboldii var. seoulense

Asarum sieboldii var. seoulense, commonly known as Seoul asarum, is a perennial herb native to Korea.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive waxy texture and a strong, aromatic scent. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical characteristics make it a notable species in the Aristolochiaceae family.
