Areca dicksonii

Areca dicksonii, commonly known as the giant areca palm, is a tall, slender palm native to Southeast Asia.

It features a tall, unbranched trunk with a smooth, grayish-brown surface. The plant produces large, feather-like leaves that can reach up to 5 meters in length. Its flowers are small and clustered in spadices, typically yellow-green in color.

Areca dicksonii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to tropical and subtropical climates.
