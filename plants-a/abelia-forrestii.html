Abelia forrestii

Abelia forrestii, commonly known as the Chinese abelia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features slender, arching stems and produces clusters of fragrant, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, and red. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery down. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained soil.

Abelia forrestii is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various garden conditions.
