Althaea Insularis

Althaea insularis is a rare flowering plant native to the remote islands of the Mediterranean. It thrives in coastal environments where salty winds and sandy soils create unique growing conditions. Known for its showy, pinkish-white flowers, this species is a striking addition to any natural landscape. Due to its limited habitat, it is considered endangered and requires careful conservation efforts. Its beauty and ecological significance make it a subject of interest for botanists and nature enthusiasts alike.
