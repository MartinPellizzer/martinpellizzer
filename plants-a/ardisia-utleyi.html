Ardisia utleyi

Ardisia utleyi, commonly known as the Florida beautyberry, is a small evergreen shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that provide a striking contrast to its clusters of vibrant purple berries. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in the spring, followed by the showy fruits that persist into the winter. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded to partially shaded environments.

Ardisia utleyi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly used in landscaping and garden settings.
