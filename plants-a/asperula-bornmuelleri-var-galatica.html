Asperula bornmuelleri var. galatica

Asperula bornmuelleri var. galatica, commonly known as the Galician azalea, is a compact evergreen shrub native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It features dense, dark green foliage and produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of pink and white during late spring. The plant thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in garden landscapes for its neat growth habit.

Asperula bornmuelleri var. galatica is relatively low maintenance and resistant to common pests and diseases.
