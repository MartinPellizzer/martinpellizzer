Abies delavayi subsp. nukiangensis

Abies delavayi subsp.

nukiangensis, commonly known as the Nukiang fir, is a rare evergreen conifer native to the eastern Himalayas. It is characterized by its slender, conical shape and dark green, needle-like leaves that are arranged in spirals along the branches.

The cones are typically slender and pendulous, containing small, winged seeds. This subspecies is adapted to high-altitude environments, thriving in cool, moist climates with well-drained soil.

Its botanical features make it an important species for ecological studies in alpine ecosystems.
