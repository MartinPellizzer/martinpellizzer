Anthurium baguense

Anthurium baguense, commonly known as the Baguense arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a glossy texture. The plant produces vibrant spadices, which are modified leaves that enclose the true flowers, often in shades of red or orange. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Anthurium baguense is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
