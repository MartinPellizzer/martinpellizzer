Alsophila physolepidota

Alsophila physolepidota, commonly known as the broad-leaf tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feathery fronds that can reach several meters in length. The plant has a robust root system that helps it thrive in moist, shaded environments. Its spores are produced on the undersides of its fronds, contributing to its reproductive cycle.

Alsophila physolepidota plays an important ecological role in tropical rainforests, providing habitat and contributing to biodiversity.
