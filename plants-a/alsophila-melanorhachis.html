Alsophila melanorhachis

Alsophila melanorhachis, commonly known as the black-stemmed tree fern, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a tall, slender fern with a distinctive, unbranched trunk that is dark brown to black in color. The plant features a crown of large, pinnate fronds that can grow up to several meters in length. Its rhizomes are thick and fibrous, supporting its upright growth habit.

Alsophila melanorhachis is often found in humid, shaded environments, such as tropical rainforests.
