Asplenium poolii var. poolii

Asplenium poolii var. poolii, commonly known as the Pool's spleenwort, is a species of fern belonging to the family Aspleniaceae.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen growth habit and its ability to thrive in shaded, moist environments. The plant typically features small, lance-shaped leaves that are often densely packed along its rhizome. It is native to regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of China and the Philippines.

Asplenium poolii var. poolii is often found growing on rock surfaces or in crevices, demonstrating its adaptability to rocky substrates.
