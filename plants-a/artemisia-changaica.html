Artemisia changaica

Artemisia changaica, commonly known as Chang's wormwood, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, silver-gray foliage. The plant produces small, yellowish flower heads that are typically clustered in dense, spherical shapes. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, rocky habitats.

Due to its medicinal properties, it has been used in traditional Chinese medicine for various health purposes.
