Anthurium scherzerianum var. stipitatum

Anthurium scherzerianum var. stipitatum, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are deep green with a reddish tinge. The plant produces vibrant red spathes that enclose its small, spadix-shaped inflorescence. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical landscapes.
