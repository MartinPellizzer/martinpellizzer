Anthurium × froebelii

Anthurium × froebelii, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Araceae family.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped, velvety red bracts that surround the spadix, which is typically yellow or green. Native to the tropical regions of South America, it thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light. The plant produces small, white flowers that are often hidden beneath the colorful bracts.

Its foliage consists of large, glossy, dark green leaves that add to its ornamental value.
