Agrostis philippiana

Agrostis philippiana, commonly known as Philippine bentgrass, is a perennial grass species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fine, tufted growth habit and slender, blue-green leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in open areas such as grasslands and forest margins. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny flowers.

Agrostis philippiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping and turfgrass applications.
