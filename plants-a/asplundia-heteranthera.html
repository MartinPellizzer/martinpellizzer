Asplundia heteranthera

Asplundia heteranthera, commonly known as the heteranthera arrowroot, is a flowering plant belonging to the Araceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Central and South America, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant features a short, stocky rhizome with broad, arrowhead-shaped leaves that are often covered in a waxy cuticle. Its inflorescence consists of a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe, typical of the Araceae family.

Asplundia heteranthera is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been cultivated for both horticultural and medicinal purposes.
