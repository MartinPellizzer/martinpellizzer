Alyxia tropica

Alyxia tropica, commonly known as the tropical alyxia, is a flowering plant native to regions of Southeast Asia and the Pacific Islands.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in tropical and subtropical climates. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually white or pale yellow, arranged in clusters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a leathery texture.

Alyxia tropica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
