Aridarum zygosetum

Aridarum zygosetum, commonly known as the desert moss, is a specialized plant adapted to arid environments.

It belongs to the family Zygostomataceae and is characterized by its compact, cushion-like growth form. The plant features small, dark green leaves that are tightly arranged to minimize water loss. It thrives in rocky or sandy soils with low moisture levels.

Due to its resilience, it plays a vital role in stabilizing soil in desert ecosystems.
