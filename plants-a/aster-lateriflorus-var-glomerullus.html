Aster lateriflorus var. glomerullus

Aster lateriflorus var. glomerullus, commonly known as the clustered leaf aster, is a perennial herb native to the eastern United States.

It features lance-shaped leaves that are usually arranged alternately along the stem. The plant produces small, daisy-like flowers with white ray florets and yellow disk florets, typically blooming in late summer to early fall. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable species for ecological restoration and native plant gardens.
