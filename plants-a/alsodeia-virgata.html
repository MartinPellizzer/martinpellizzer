Alsodeia virgata

Alsodeia virgata, commonly known as the silverleaf alsodeia, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in dry, rocky soils. The plant features slender, branched stems covered in silvery-gray hairs, giving it its common name. Its small, pale blue or violet flowers bloom in late spring to early summer.

Alsodeia virgata is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and along coastal areas.
