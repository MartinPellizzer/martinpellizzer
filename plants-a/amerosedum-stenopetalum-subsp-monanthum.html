Amerosedum stenopetalum subsp. monanthum

Amerosedum stenopetalum subsp.

monanthum, commonly known as the narrow-petaled stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its fleshy, blue-green leaves and produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white and pink.

The plant is typically found in rocky or sandy soils, thriving in arid or semi-arid environments. It is a low-growing perennial that forms dense mats, making it well-suited for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its drought tolerance and ornamental appeal make it a popular choice for succulent enthusiasts.
