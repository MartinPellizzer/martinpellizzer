Adesmia leptobotrys

Adesmia leptobotrys, commonly known as the slender-leaved adesmia, is a shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is characterized by its leguminous properties and nitrogen-fixing capabilities. The plant features small, trifoliate leaves and produces yellow flowers that are clustered in racemes. Its fruit is a legume pod containing several seeds.

Adesmia leptobotrys is often found in moist, well-drained soils in forest understories or along stream banks.
