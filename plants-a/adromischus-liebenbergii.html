Adromischus liebenbergii

Adromischus liebenbergii, commonly known as the "Lion's Tail" or "Lion's Ear," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, dark green leaves that are often tinged with red or purple, especially when exposed to sunlight. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.

Adromischus liebenbergii is popular among succulent enthusiasts for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
