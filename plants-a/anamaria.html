Anamaria

Anamaria, commonly known as the silverleaf nightshade, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It features a bushy growth habit with silver-gray leaves that have a velvety texture. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale purple. Its berries are toxic and should not be consumed by humans or animals.

Anamaria is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens due to its striking foliage.
