Albuca garuensis

Albuca garuensis, commonly known as the Garu Valley onion, is a member of the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is a bulbous plant native to South Africa, specifically the Eastern Cape province. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of white to pale yellow flowers. Its bulbs are edible and have a mild onion-like flavor.

Albuca garuensis is adapted to grow in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
