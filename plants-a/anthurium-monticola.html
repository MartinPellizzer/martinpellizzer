Anthurium monticola

Anthurium monticola, commonly known as the mountain anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are often a deep green with a glossy texture. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-shaped inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, typically in shades of red or orange. Anthurium monticola thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and botanical collections.
