Aster meritus

Aster meritus, commonly known as the purple coneflower, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features showy purple petals surrounding a central disc of yellow florets, and it produces fluffy seeds that are dispersed by the wind. Aster meritus thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for native gardens.

It also serves as a valuable food source for pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
