Anthurium chamberlainii

Anthurium chamberlainii, commonly known as the Crimson Velvet Anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are deep green with a velvety texture. The plant produces vibrant red spathes that enclose its small, spadix-like inflorescence, making it a popular ornamental species. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Anthurium chamberlainii is valued for its aesthetic appeal and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in botanical gardens.
