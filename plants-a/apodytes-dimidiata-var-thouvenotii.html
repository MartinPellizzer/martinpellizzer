Apodytes dimidiata var. thouvenotii

Apodytes dimidiata var. thouvenotii, commonly known as the Cape tulip, is a succulent shrub native to South Africa.

It features striking, showy flowers that are typically red or orange with a yellow throat, resembling tulips. The plant has fleshy, strap-like leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating to reduce water loss. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for arid and semi-arid climates.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and resilience in dry conditions.
