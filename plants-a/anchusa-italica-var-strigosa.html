Anchusa italica var. strigosa

Anchusa italica var. strigosa, commonly known as the woolly blueweed, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its hairy, silver-green foliage and spikes of bright blue or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry soils and thrives in full sun. It is often found in coastal areas and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
