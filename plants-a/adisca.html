Adisca

Adisca, commonly known as the red spider lily, is a striking ornamental plant native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant red, spider-like flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant features strap-like, dark green leaves that emerge from a bulbous base. Adisca thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its unique appearance and seasonal blooming make it a notable addition to botanical collections.
