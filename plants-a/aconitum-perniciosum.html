Aconitum perniciosum

Aconitum perniciosum, commonly known as the deadly nightshade, is a member of the Ranunculaceae family.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in moist, shady environments. The plant produces striking blue or purple flowers, which are followed by egg-shaped berries. Its roots and other parts contain toxic alkaloids, making it highly dangerous to humans and animals.

Despite its toxicity, it is sometimes cultivated for its ornamental value.
