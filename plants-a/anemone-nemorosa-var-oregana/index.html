Anemone Nemorosa Var. Oregana

Wood anemone, scientifically known as Anemone nemorosa var. oregana, is a delicate woodland flower that blooms in early spring. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest floors and along stream banks. With its white petals and a central yellow cluster, it resembles a miniature lily. This plant is native to the Pacific Northwest and is a symbol of the region's natural beauty. Its short flowering period makes it a fleeting but cherished sight in the wild.
