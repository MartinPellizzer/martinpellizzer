Acacia Deanei Subsp. Paucijuga

Acacia deanei subsp. paucijuga is a rare and striking plant native to the arid regions of Western Australia. It is known for its unique appearance, featuring feathery leaves and clusters of golden-yellow flowers. This subspecies thrives in harsh, dry environments, showcasing remarkable adaptability to extreme conditions. Its ecological importance includes providing nectar for native pollinators and supporting local wildlife. Due to its limited distribution and habitat threats, conservation efforts are crucial for its survival.
