Agrostis alba f. amethystina

Agrostis alba f. amethystina, commonly known as the amethyst grass, is a variant of the silver hair grass.

It is a low-growing perennial grass that features distinctive silver-blue foliage and dense, fluffy seed heads. The plant is native to Europe and is often found in grassy areas, meadows, and along roadsides. Its compact growth habit and ornamental seed heads make it a desirable choice for garden landscapes.

Botanically, it is characterized by its fine, wiry leaves and its ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
