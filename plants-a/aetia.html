Aetia

Aetia, commonly known as the red-flowered bell heather, is a flowering plant belonging to the Ericaceae family.

It is native to southern Africa and is characterized by its compact, evergreen shrub form. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically red or pink in color, which bloom in spring and summer. Aetia thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and horticultural collections.
