Anthyllis vulneraria var. dillenii

Anthyllis vulneraria var. dillenii, commonly known as the Dillenii Scotch thistle, is a perennial flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its erect, spiny stems and small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has finely divided, fern-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, rocky habitats.

This variety is known for its hardy nature and ability to withstand harsh environmental conditions.
