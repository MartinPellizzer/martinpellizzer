Aglaonema nieuwenhuisii

Aglaonema nieuwenhuisii, commonly known as the Nieuwenhuis' evergreen arum, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated foliage with shades of green, white, and pink. The plant belongs to the Araceae family, which includes other ornamental plants like peace lilies and calla lilies. Its leaves are typically broad and heart-shaped, with a waxy texture that helps it retain moisture.

Aglaonema nieuwenhuisii thrives in low-light conditions and is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant.
