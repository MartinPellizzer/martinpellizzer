Adenia mannii

Adenia mannii, commonly known as the African butter tree, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a woody shrub or small tree that can grow up to 6 meters in height. The plant produces large, showy yellow flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a waxy coating.

The fruit is a fleshy, orange-red berry that contains several seeds.
