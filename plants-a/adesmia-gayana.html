Adesmia gayana

Adesmia gayana, commonly known as the yellow burr legume, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is known for its woody stems and small, spiny fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in dry, arid environments and is often found in disturbed soils.

Adesmia gayana is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
