Allosorus Aurantiacus

Red spider orchid, scientifically known as Allosorus aurantiacus, is a rare and striking flowering plant native to the tropical rainforests of northern Queensland, Australia. This orchid is named for its distinctive red, spider-like appearance, with intricate, thread-like flowers that resemble a web of delicate filaments. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing on the trunks of rainforest trees. Despite its beauty, the Red spider orchid is highly sensitive to environmental changes and faces threats from habitat loss and climate change. Conservation efforts are crucial to ensure its survival in the wild.
