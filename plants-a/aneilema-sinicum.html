Aneilema sinicum

Aneilema sinicum, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are often variegated with silver or white markings. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or white in color. It thrives in humid environments and prefers bright, indirect light.

Aneilema sinicum is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and relatively low maintenance requirements.
