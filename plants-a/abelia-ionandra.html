Abelia ionandra

Abelia ionandra, commonly known as the Chinese abelia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features showy, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink, blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are typically dark green and slightly hairy. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained soil.

Abelia ionandra is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various garden conditions.
