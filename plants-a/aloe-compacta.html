Aloe compacta

Aloe compacta, commonly known as the dwarf aloe or compact aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of thick, fleshy, blue-green leaves that are covered in a layer of white powdery bloom. The plant produces small, yellow flowers on a tall stem during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with plenty of sunlight.

Aloe compacta is often used in rock gardens and as a decorative indoor plant due to its compact size and attractive appearance.
