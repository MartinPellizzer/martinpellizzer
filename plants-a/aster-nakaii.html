Aster nakaii

Aster nakaii, commonly known as the Japanese aster, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its showy blue to purple flowers that bloom in the summer months. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Aster nakaii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens and landscapes.
