Arisaema madhuanum

Arisaema madhuanum, commonly known as the Madhuana arrowhead, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the Himalayas.

It features a distinctive arrowhead-shaped leaf that is often brightly colored, with a deep green to dark purple hue. The plant produces a spadix enclosed within a spathe, a characteristic feature of the Araceae family. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its unique morphology and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists alike.
