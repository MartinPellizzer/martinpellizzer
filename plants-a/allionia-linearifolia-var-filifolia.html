Allionia linearifolia var. filifolia

Allionia linearifolia var. filifolia, commonly known as the threadleaf allionia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, linear leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale pink flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and rockeries.
