Andrachne orbiculata

Andrachne orbiculata, commonly known as the roundleaf sandmat, is a flowering plant belonging to the Amaranthaceae family.

It is native to arid and semi-arid regions of southern Africa, particularly South Africa. The plant is characterized by its orbicular (rounded) leaves and small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in coastal or inland desert environments.

Due to its hardy nature, it is well adapted to withstand drought and high temperatures.
