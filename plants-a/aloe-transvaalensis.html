Aloe transvaalensis

Aloe transvaalensis, commonly known as the Transvaal aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with white or yellow markings. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing yellow or orange flowers. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Its leaves are often used for their medicinal properties.
