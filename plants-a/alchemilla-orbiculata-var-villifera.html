Alchemilla orbiculata var. villifera

Alchemilla orbiculata var. villifera, commonly known as the velvet cinquefoil, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features a low-growing rosette of soft, velvety, spoon-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers during the summer months, which are borne on slender, upright stems. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodland floors and stream banks.

Its leaves and flowers contain compounds that have been historically used in traditional herbal remedies.
