Argyreia capitiformis var. minor

Argyreia capitiformis var. minor, commonly known as the red balloon plant, is a flowering vine native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, inflated, balloon-like fruits that turn red when mature. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped white to pink flowers that attract pollinators. Its leaves are heart-shaped and have a velvety texture.

It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
