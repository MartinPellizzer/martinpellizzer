Aster iodochrous

Aster iodochrous, commonly known as the iodochrous aster, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands. Its flowers are usually blue to purple in color, with ray florets surrounding a central disk.

Aster iodochrous is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, providing nectar for pollinators.
