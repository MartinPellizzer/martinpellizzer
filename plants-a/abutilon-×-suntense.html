Abutilon × suntense

Abutilon × suntense, commonly known as the velvetleaf abutilon, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Malvaceae family.

It is characterized by its soft, velvety leaves that give it a distinctive tactile quality. The plant produces showy, pendulous flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, often resembling lanterns. It is native to the southeastern United States and thrives in warm, humid climates.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes for its visual appeal.
