Anthurium × vanwambeckeanum

Anthurium × vanwambeckeanum, commonly known as the velvet leaf anthurium, is a hybrid plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically deep green with a velvety texture. The plant produces striking spadices, which are modified leaves that enclose the small, inconspicuous flowers. It thrives in humid, warm environments with bright indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium × vanwambeckeanum is popular as a decorative plant due to its ornamental foliage and ease of care.
