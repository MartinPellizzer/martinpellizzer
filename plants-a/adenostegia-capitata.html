Adenostegia capitata

Adenostegia capitata, commonly known as the woolly goldenbush, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, bright yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.

Adenostegia capitata is valued for its ornamental qualities and ability to tolerate harsh environmental conditions.
