Arthropteris archboldiae

Arthropteris archboldiae, commonly known as the Archbold's water fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Athyriaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds that are typically divided into small, pinnae-like segments. The plant is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of Indonesia and the Philippines. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found near streams or in dense forest undergrowth.

Its unique morphology and habitat preferences make it an interesting subject for botanical study.
