Aristolochia brasiliensis var. macrophylla

Aristolochia brasiliensis var. macrophylla, commonly known as Brazilian birthwort, is a climbing vine native to tropical South America.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a velvety texture and prominent veins. The plant produces unique, pipe-shaped flowers that are typically greenish-brown with a strong, earthy aroma. Its roots and leaves contain various alkaloids, some of which have been studied for potential medicinal properties.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is often cultivated in warm climates.
