Anthurium corrientense

Anthurium corrientense, commonly known as the "Crimson Velvet" anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are deep green with a velvety texture. The plant produces clusters of bright red spadices, which are the modified leaves that enclose the true flowers. Anthurium corrientense thrives in humid, warm environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical landscapes.
