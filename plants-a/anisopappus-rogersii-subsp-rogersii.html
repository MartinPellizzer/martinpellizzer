Anisopappus rogersii subsp. rogersii

Anisopappus rogersii subsp.

rogersii, commonly known as the desert gourd, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico. It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and alternate leaves.
