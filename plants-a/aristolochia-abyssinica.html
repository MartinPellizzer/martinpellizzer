Aristolochia abyssinica

Aristolochia abyssinica, commonly known as the Abyssinian birthwort, is a climbing vine native to East Africa.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and distinctive, complex, pipe-shaped flowers that are often purple or greenish in color. The plant contains various bioactive compounds, some of which have been studied for their medicinal properties. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

However, due to its toxic nature, it should be handled with care.
