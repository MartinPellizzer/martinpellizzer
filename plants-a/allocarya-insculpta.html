Allocarya insculpta

Allocarya insculpta, commonly known as the sculptured rock cress, is a perennial flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many plants with characteristic cruciform flowers. Allocarya insculpta is often found in open slopes and among scree, where it thrives in well-drained conditions.
