Ardisia merrillii var. merrillii

Ardisia merrillii var. merrillii, commonly known as the Philippine beautyberry, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of small, white flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant produces bright red, berry-like fruits that are an important food source for local wildlife. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation programs.
