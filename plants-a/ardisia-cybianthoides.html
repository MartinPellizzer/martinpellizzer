Ardisia cybianthoides

Ardisia cybianthoides, commonly known as the flame tree or the Christmas tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and striking red or orange flowers that appear in clusters. The plant produces small, dark blue to black berries that are edible but often have a bitter taste. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The botanical features of Ardisia cybianthoides make it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes in tropical regions.
