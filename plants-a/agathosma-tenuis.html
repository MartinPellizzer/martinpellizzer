Agathosma tenuis

Agathosma tenuis, commonly known as the narrowleaf buchu, is a small evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its aromatic, narrow leaves that emit a strong, peppery scent when crushed. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in coastal regions. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are followed by oval, woody fruits.

Agathosma tenuis is valued for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally in South African herbal medicine.
