Abutilon sachetianum

Abutilon sachetianum, commonly known as the velvetleaf abutilon, is a shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its hairy, velvety leaves that give it a distinctive texture. The plant produces showy, lantern-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Abutilon sachetianum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
