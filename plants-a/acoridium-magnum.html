Acoridium magnum

Acoridium magnum, commonly known as the giant arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves that can grow up to 3 meters in length. The plant thrives in wet, swampy environments and is often found near water bodies. It has a rhizomatous root system that helps it spread and colonize new areas.

Its botanical features make it an important species in wetland ecosystems.
