Arisaema heterocephalum subsp. okinawaense

Arisaema heterocephalum subsp.

okinawaense, commonly known as the Okinawan arrowhead, is a member of the Araceae family. It is native to the Ryukyu Islands of Japan, where it thrives in moist, shaded environments.

The plant features a distinctive, arrow-shaped leaf that is often variegated with white and green markings. Its inflorescence consists of a spadix surrounded by a modified leaf called a spathe.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens.
