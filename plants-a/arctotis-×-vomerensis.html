Arctotis × vomerensis

Arctotis × vomerensis, commonly known as the African daisy, is a hybrid perennial plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, daisy-like flowers that range in color from yellow to red. The plant typically grows to a height of 30–60 cm, featuring narrow, grass-like leaves and showy composite flower heads. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Arctotis × vomerensis is also valued for its drought tolerance and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
