Aquilegia vulgaris var. elegans

Aquilegia vulgaris var. elegans, commonly known as the elegant columbine, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe.

It features showy, spurred flowers in shades of red, purple, and blue, which attract pollinators like bees and butterflies. The plant has compound, trifoliate leaves with finely toothed margins, typical of the Ranunculaceae family. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, often found in woodland gardens.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking both beauty and ecological benefit.
