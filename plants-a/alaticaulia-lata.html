Alaticaulia lata

Alaticaulia lata, commonly known as the broad-leafed alaticaulia, is a species of flowering plant native to certain regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often arranged alternately along the stem. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. Alaticaulia lata thrives in humid, tropical environments and is often found in lowland forests.

Its botanical features make it an important species for ecological studies and potential medicinal research.
