Andropogon × taiwanensis

Andropogon × taiwanensis, commonly known as the Taiwan bluestem, is a hybrid grass species native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, blue-green leaves. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers.

Andropogon × taiwanensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in stabilizing soil in native landscapes.
