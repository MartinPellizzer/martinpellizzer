Acrostichum firmum

Acrostichum firmum, commonly known as the hardy brake fern, is a robust fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its large, leathery fronds that can grow up to several feet in length. The plant thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in humid climates. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense colonies.

Botanically, it belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to tolerate various soil conditions.
