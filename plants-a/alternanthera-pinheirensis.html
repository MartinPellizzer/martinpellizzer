Alternanthera pinheirensis

Alternanthera pinheirensis, commonly known as the red-stemmed Alternanthera, is a species of flowering plant in the Amaranthaceae family.

It is native to South America, particularly Brazil, and is characterized by its striking red stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its vibrant foliage.

Due to its attractive appearance, it is popular in aquatic and wetland gardens.
