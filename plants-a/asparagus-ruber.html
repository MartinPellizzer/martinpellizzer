Asparagus ruber

Asparagus ruber, commonly known as red asparagus, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features slender, reddish stems that are covered in small, scale-like leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in umbels, which are typically followed by red berries. Its foliage and stems are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

Asparagus ruber thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.
