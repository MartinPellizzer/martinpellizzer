Agrostis × dimorpholemma

Agrostis × dimorpholemma, commonly known as the two-lobed bentgrass, is a hybrid grass species that exhibits distinct morphological features.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves with a bluish-green color. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in temperate regions. Its inflorescence consists of a single spikelet, which is often covered in a distinctive two-lobed bract.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping and turfgrass applications.
