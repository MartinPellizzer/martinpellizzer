Agrostis tenuis var. stolonifera

Agrostis tenuis var. stolonifera, commonly known as slender meadowgrass, is a cool-season grass species native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its fine, grass-like leaves and dense, mat-forming growth habit, which allows it to thrive in moist, well-drained soils. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny, spikelet-based flowers. It is often found in lawns, meadows, and along stream banks, where it contributes to soil stabilization.

Its stoloniferous growth pattern enables it to spread vegetatively, making it a persistent and resilient plant in suitable environments.
