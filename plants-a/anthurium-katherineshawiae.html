Anthurium katherineshawiae

Anthurium katherineshawiae, commonly known as Katherine Shaw's anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped spathe that ranges in color from deep red to orange, often with a contrasting green spadix. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, nutrient-rich soil. It produces small, green berries that are edible but not commonly consumed by humans.

Anthurium katherineshawiae is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in greenhouse settings.
