Achimenes autumnalis

Achimenes autumnalis, commonly known as the autumn bellflower, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of red, pink, and purple. The plant features heart-shaped leaves and thrives in moist, well-drained soil. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and containers.

Achimenes autumnalis is known for its ability to bloom throughout the growing season, making it a popular choice for summer gardens.
