Aristida adscensionis f. modesta

Aristida adscensionis f. modesta, commonly known as the modest grass, is a variant of the species Aristida adscensionis, which belongs to the grass family Poaceae.

This plant is characterized by its slender, erect stems and fine, linear leaves that give it a delicate appearance. It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. The inflorescence consists of a compact, cylindrical spikelet that is often slightly hairy.

This grass is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in native plant gardens.
