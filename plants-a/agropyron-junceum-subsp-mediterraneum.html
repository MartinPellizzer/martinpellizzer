Agropyron junceum subsp. mediterraneum

Agropyron junceum subsp.

mediterraneum, commonly known as Mediterranean needlegrass, is a perennial grass species native to the Mediterranean region. It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves.

The plant typically grows in rocky or dry soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny flowers.

This species is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its hardiness and ability to stabilize soil.
