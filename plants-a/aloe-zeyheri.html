Aloe zeyheri

Aloe zeyheri, commonly known as the Zeyher's aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a distinctive red or purple tinge along the edges. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing yellow flowers, which attract pollinators like bees and birds. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it drought-tolerant.

Aloe zeyheri is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional medicinal uses.
