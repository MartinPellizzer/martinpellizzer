Aira lenzei

Aira lenzei, commonly known as the slender false oat, is a flowering plant belonging to the grass family Poaceae.

It is native to parts of Asia, including China, Japan, and Korea, and thrives in moist, temperate environments. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, white or pale blue flowers. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue, and it often grows in dense clusters.

Aira lenzei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
