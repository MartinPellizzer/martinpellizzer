Anathallis tigridens

Anathallis tigridens, commonly known as the tiger orchid, is a striking epiphytic orchid native to the Atlantic Forest of Brazil.

It features slender, wiry stems and produces large, showy flowers with a distinctive tiger-like pattern on the petals. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, organic substrates for optimal growth. Its flowers are typically white or pale pink with deep red or orange markings, enhancing its ornamental value.

Anathallis tigridens is notable for its unique morphology and ecological significance in tropical ecosystems.
