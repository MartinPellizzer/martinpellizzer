Aster luzonensis

Aster luzonensis, commonly known as the Luzon aster, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions and is adapted to cool, humid climates. Its flowers are usually blue to purple in color, and it produces small, wind-dispersed seeds.

Aster luzonensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also studied for its potential ecological roles in its native habitat.
