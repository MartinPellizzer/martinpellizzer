Aloe reitzii

Aloe reitzii, commonly known as the Reitz's aloe, is a striking succulent native to South Africa.

It features tall, branching stems covered in thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces vibrant orange or yellow flowers during the summer months, adding to its ornamental appeal. Its leaves contain a gel-like substance that is often used for its soothing properties.

Aloe reitzii thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for xeriscaping and rock gardens.
