Amphorkis discolor

Amphorkis discolor, commonly known as the spotted orchid, is a species of orchid native to Africa, particularly South Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, spotted leaves and pale green to white flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in arid or semi-arid environments. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is known for its unique floral structure.

The species is often found in open grasslands and along riverbanks, where it contributes to local biodiversity.
