Aegle Marmelos Var. Tamilnadensis

Aegle marmelos var. tamilnadensis is a lesser-known variant of the wood apple tree native to southern India. This unique plant is characterized by its distinctive fruit and medicinal properties valued in traditional Ayurvedic practices. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often found in forested regions across Tamil Nadu. The tree is not only ornamental but also holds cultural significance in local communities. Its cultivation is gaining interest due to its potential health benefits and ecological importance.
