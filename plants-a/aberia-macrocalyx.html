Aberia macrocalyx

Aberia macrocalyx, commonly known as the large-flowered aberia, is a member of the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its distinctive, showy white or pale purple flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant features palmately compound leaves with five to seven leaflets. Native to tropical regions, it thrives in warm, humid climates with well-drained soil.

Its botanical structure supports its role as both an ornamental plant and a potential source of medicinal compounds.
