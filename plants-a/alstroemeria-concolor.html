Alstroemeria concolor

Alstroemeria concolor, commonly known as the red-flowered alstroemeria, is a perennial plant native to South America.

It belongs to the family Alstroemeriaceae and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, featuring lance-shaped leaves and a rhizomatous root system. Its flowers are often used in floral arrangements due to their long-lasting nature and vibrant hues.

Alstroemeria concolor thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade conditions.
