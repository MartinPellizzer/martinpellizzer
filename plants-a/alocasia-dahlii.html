Alocasia dahlii

Alocasia dahlii, commonly known as the Black Velvet Alocasia, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features large, dark green, heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and deep, rich coloration. The plant is part of the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive inflorescence consisting of a spadix and spathe. Alocasia dahlii thrives in humid, shady environments and requires well-draining, fertile soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor and greenhouse cultivation.
