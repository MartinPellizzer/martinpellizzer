Alsine arctica

Alsine arctica, commonly known as Arctic sandwort, is a flowering plant native to Arctic and alpine regions.

It is a low-growing perennial that thrives in harsh, cold environments. The plant has small, white or pinkish flowers that bloom in early summer. Its leaves are typically succulent and adapted to retain moisture in dry conditions.

Alsine arctica plays an important role in stabilizing soil in its extreme habitat.
