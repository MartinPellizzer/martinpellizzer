Aloe ruspoliana var. dracaeniformis

Aloe ruspoliana var. dracaeniformis, commonly known as the Dragon Tree Aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with red or purple edges. The plant produces a tall, branched inflorescence with yellow flowers, which bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are often used for their gel, which has various medicinal properties.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in arid conditions.
