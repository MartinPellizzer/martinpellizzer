Ajax telamonius var. grandiplenus

Ajax telamonius var. grandiplenus, commonly known as the giant corymbia, is a striking plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its large, showy white or pink flowers that appear in dense, rounded clusters. The plant has thick, leathery leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for warm climates.

This variant is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
