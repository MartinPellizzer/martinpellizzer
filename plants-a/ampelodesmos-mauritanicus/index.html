Ampelodesmos Mauritanicus

Ampelodesmos mauritanicus is a unique flowering plant native to arid regions of North Africa. It is known for its distinctive appearance and ability to thrive in harsh desert conditions. The plant features long, trailing stems that often cascade from rocky outcrops. Its small, pale flowers bloom during brief periods of rainfall. Despite its delicate look, it is highly resilient and adapted to extreme environments.
