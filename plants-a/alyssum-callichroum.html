Alyssum callichroum

Alyssum callichroum, commonly known as the rock alyssum, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant has small, narrow leaves and thrives in well-drained soil and full sun. It is often used in rock gardens and as a ground cover due to its hardy nature and ornamental value.

Alyssum callichroum is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
