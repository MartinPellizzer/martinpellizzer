Alsophila percrassa

Alsophila percrassa, commonly known as the giant tree fern, is a striking member of the Cyatheaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia and the Pacific Islands. The plant features a tall, unbranched trunk covered in persistent, fibrous roots. Its fronds are large, feather-like, and can grow up to several meters in length.

Alsophila percrassa plays an important ecological role in its habitat, providing shelter and food for various species.
