Achillea × incognita

Achillea × incognita, commonly known as the hybrid yarrow, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, feathery foliage and flat, composite flower heads that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows in dry, sunny habitats such as meadows and rocky slopes. It produces small, herbaceous stems that are often covered in fine hairs.

Achillea × incognita is valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional medicinal uses.
