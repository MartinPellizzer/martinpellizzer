Anemone × zichyi

Anemone × zichyi, commonly known as the hybrid anemone, is a perennial flowering plant that combines characteristics of both *Anemone hupehensis* and *Anemone japonica*.

It produces showy, single or double flowers in shades of white, pink, and red, typically blooming in early to mid-summer. The plant has finely divided, fern-like leaves that are usually bluish-green in color. It thrives in partial to full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to naturalize and spread through rhizomes.
