Agathosma pallens

Agathosma pallens, commonly known as the silver leaf agathosma, is a succulent shrub native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, silver-gray foliage. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires minimal water, making it drought-tolerant.

Its unique appearance and fragrance make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
