Aspilia gondensis

Aspilia gondensis, commonly known as the Gondweaspen, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in dense clusters. Aspilia gondensis is typically found in rocky or sandy soils in arid regions.
