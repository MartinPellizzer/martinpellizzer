Alsophila Camerooniana Var. Congi

Alsophila camerooniana var. congi is a striking species of tree fern native to the humid forests of Cameroon. It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feathery fronds that give it a dramatic appearance. This plant thrives in warm, moist environments and is often found in shaded, tropical locations. Its unique morphology makes it a popular choice for botanical gardens and conservation efforts. Due to habitat loss, it is also considered a vulnerable species in its natural range.
