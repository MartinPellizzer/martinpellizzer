Aerides ruckeri

Aerides ruckeri, commonly known as the Rucker's orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces showy, fragrant flowers with a distinctive coloration. The plant typically features dark green, lance-shaped leaves and thrives in warm, humid environments. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in a home setting.

Aerides ruckeri is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is well-suited for indoor gardening.
