Aegopordon

Aegopordon, commonly known as goatweed, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Apiaceae.

These plants are characterized by their aromatic foliage and small, often clustered flowers. They typically have finely divided leaves and produce fluffy, white or pale yellow blooms. Aegopordon species are often found in dry, rocky or sandy soils across the Mediterranean and parts of Asia.
