Abrus schimperi subsp. oblongus

Abrus schimperi subsp.

oblongus, commonly known as the narrow-leaf velvet bean, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family. It is characterized by its slender, elongated leaves and distinctive, showy red-and-black seed pods.
