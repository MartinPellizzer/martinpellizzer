Aloe branddraaiensis

Aloe branddraaiensis, commonly known as the Branddraai aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, with a waxy coating to reduce water loss. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing yellow flowers, which attract pollinators such as bees and birds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for arid and semi-arid environments.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is often cultivated in gardens and used in traditional medicine.
