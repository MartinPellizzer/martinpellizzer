Aglaonema simplex f. nieuwenhuisii

Aglaonema simplex f. nieuwenhuisii, commonly known as the Nieuwenhuisii Variability, is a compact, evergreen shrub characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves with striking white and cream variegation.

Native to Southeast Asia, this plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and prefers well-draining, fertile soil. It is a popular houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and low maintenance requirements. The variegation is due to genetic factors that affect chlorophyll production, resulting in unique leaf patterns.

It is relatively resistant to pests and diseases, making it a versatile choice for indoor gardening.
