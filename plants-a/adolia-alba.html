Adolia alba

Adolia alba, commonly known as the white adolia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are typically bright green and glossy. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and requires partial to full sunlight.

Adolia alba is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
