Aira gayana

Aira gayana, commonly known as the slender false wheat, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, pale blue or white flowers arranged in dense, spike-like inflorescences. It is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils.
