Anthurium dictyophyllum

Anthurium dictyophyllum, commonly known as the velvet leaf anthurium, is a striking tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a velvety texture. The plant produces striking spadices, which are spathes that enclose the small, clustered flowers. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.

Anthurium dictyophyllum is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
