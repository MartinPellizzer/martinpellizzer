Adiantum smithianum var. shensiense

Adiantum smithianum var. shensiense, commonly known as the Shensi maidenhair fern, is a delicate fern native to China.

It features finely divided, arching fronds with a pale green hue and a distinctive, lace-like appearance. The plant thrives in shady, moist environments and is often found in forest understories. Its fronds are bioluminescent in the dark, emitting a faint blue glow.

This unique trait makes it a rare and fascinating specimen in the botanical world.
