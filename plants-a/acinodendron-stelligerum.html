Acinodendron stelligerum

Acinodendron stelligerum, commonly known as the sea hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of the Indo-Pacific.

It is characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves and showy, five-petaled flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant thrives in saline and brackish environments, making it well adapted to coastal and mangrove ecosystems. Acinodendron stelligerum plays a role in stabilizing shorelines and providing habitat for marine and terrestrial organisms.
