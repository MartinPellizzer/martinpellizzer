Anthurium coleomischum

Anthurium coleomischum, commonly known as the velvet leaf anthurium, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are velvety to the touch, giving it its common name. The plant produces striking spadices, which are often bright red or orange, surrounded by a colorful spathe. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium coleomischum is popular as a decorative plant due to its ornamental foliage and unique flowering structure.
