Aerva burchellii

Aerva burchellii, commonly known as the African star grass, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features slender, upright stems and narrow, grass-like leaves that are often hairy. The plant produces small, white or pale blue flowers arranged in spikes, typically blooming in summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open grasslands and along riverbanks.

Aerva burchellii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
