Aeonium saundersii

Aeonium saundersii, commonly known as the "Coral Cactus," is a succulent plant native to the Canary Islands.

It features rosettes of fleshy, spoon-shaped leaves that are typically green with red or pink hues along the edges. The plant produces tall, branching inflorescences that bear yellow flowers, which bloom in spring. Aeonium saundersii is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil with full sun exposure.

It is popular as a decorative plant due to its striking appearance and low maintenance requirements.
