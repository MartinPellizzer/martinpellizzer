Adenostephanus sellowii

Adenostephanus sellowii, commonly known as the Brazilian star grass, is a perennial herb native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its star-shaped inflorescences. The plant typically grows in grasslands and open areas, thriving in well-drained soils. Its leaves are lanceolate and often covered with fine hairs, while its flowers are small and yellow.

Adenostephanus sellowii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in horticultural settings.
