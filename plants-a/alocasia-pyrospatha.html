Alocasia pyrospatha

Alocasia pyrospatha, commonly known as the flame leaf alocasia, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often adorned with bright red veins, giving it a fiery appearance. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, nutrient-rich soil. Its foliage is not only visually appealing but also plays a role in its natural habitat by providing shelter for small animals and insects.

Alocasia pyrospatha is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
