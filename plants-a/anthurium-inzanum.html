Anthurium inzanum

Anthurium inzanum, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a striking tropical plant native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped spathes that can range in color from deep red to orange. The plant produces small, greenish-white spadices that are clustered within the spatulate leaf-like structure. Anthurium inzanum thrives in humid, shady environments and requires well-draining, nutrient-rich soil.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is popular in horticulture due to its unique and eye-catching foliage.
