Alyssum pleiospermum

Alyssum pleiospermum, commonly known as the desert alyssum, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a low-growing annual or biennial plant with small, fragrant white or pale pink flowers. The plant has hairy, narrow leaves and produces numerous small seeds in elongated pods. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, showcasing adaptations to drought and poor soil conditions.

Its botanical features make it a hardy and resilient species well-suited for xeriscaping and ecological restoration projects.
