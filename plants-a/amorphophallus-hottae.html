Amorphophallus hottae

Amorphophallus hottae, commonly known as the giant red veined arrowhead, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves with prominent red veins. The plant produces a unique inflorescence consisting of a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe. It is notable for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in humid, shaded environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a decorative plant in tropical gardens.
