Athyrium coreanum

Athyrium coreanum, commonly known as the Korean lady fern, is a species of fern native to East Asia.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its finely divided, delicate fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky slopes. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine in some Asian countries.
