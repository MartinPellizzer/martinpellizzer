Aster Uniflorus

Aster uniflorus is a unique flowering plant known for its single bloom per stem. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas. This species is part of the Asteraceae family, which includes many familiar flowers like daisies and sunflowers. Its delicate appearance and rare occurrence make it a subject of interest for botanists and nature enthusiasts. Despite its beauty, Aster uniflorus remains a little-known plant in the broader botanical world.
