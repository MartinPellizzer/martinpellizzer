Acalypha gracilis var. divaricata

Acalypha gracilis var. divaricata, commonly known as the slender leaf spurflower, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its delicate, slender leaves that are often tinged with red or purple at the edges. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in humid, shady environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated as a decorative plant in gardens.
