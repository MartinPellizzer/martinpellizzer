Anserina foetida

Anserina foetida, commonly known as the stinking nightshade, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces small, greenish-white flowers with a strong, unpleasant odor. The plant's leaves are simple, alternate, and have a rough texture. Its fruit is a fleshy, orange-red berry that matures in autumn.

Anserina foetida is often found in rocky or disturbed soils and is adapted to arid environments.
