Anthemis montana var. columnae

Anthemis montana var. columnae, commonly known as the columnar anthemis, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features a compact, upright growth habit with narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically gray-green in color. The plant produces small, yellow-rayed flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and alpine landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value and hardy nature, it is often cultivated in gardens with rocky or arid conditions.
