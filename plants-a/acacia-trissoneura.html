Acacia trissoneura

Acacia trissoneura, commonly known as the silky oaks, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family.

It is native to Australia and is characterized by its distinctive, feathery foliage and showy yellow flowers. The plant is a deciduous tree that can grow up to 30 meters tall, with a broad, spreading canopy. Its bark is smooth and grayish, while its leaves are bipinnate, consisting of numerous small, rounded leaflets.

Acacia trissoneura plays an important role in its native ecosystem, providing habitat and food for various wildlife species.
