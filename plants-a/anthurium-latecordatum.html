Anthurium latecordatum

Anthurium latecordatum, commonly known as the latecordatum arrowhead plant, is a tropical species native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are often a deep green with a glossy texture. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-like inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, typically in shades of red or orange. It thrives in humid, warm environments with indirect light and requires well-draining soil.

Anthurium latecordatum is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
