Amaryllis elegans var. divifrancisci

Amaryllis elegans var. divifrancisci, commonly known as the Divifrancisci amaryllis, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It produces tall, sturdy stems with large, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are typically bright green and covered in a waxy coating. It blooms in late winter to early spring, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
