Anthurium galeottii

Anthurium galeottii, commonly known as the black anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its dark, glossy, heart-shaped leaves and striking red spathes that enclose the spadix. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its foliage is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.

Anthurium galeottii is a popular ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
