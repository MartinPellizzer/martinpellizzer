Aciotis indecora var. macrophylla

Aciotis indecora var. macrophylla, commonly known as the large-leaf aciotis, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features large, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, arranged in clusters at the ends of the stems.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and open woodlands.
