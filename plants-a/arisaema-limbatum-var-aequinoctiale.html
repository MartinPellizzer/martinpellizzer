Arisaema limbatum var. aequinoctiale

Arisaema limbatum var. aequinoctiale, commonly known as the equinox arisaema, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of southern China.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive inflorescence consisting of a spadix surrounded by a modified leaf called a spathe. The plant produces striking, often colorful spathe that can range from green to reddish hues, depending on the variety and environmental conditions. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense clumps in suitable habitats.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage and floral display.
