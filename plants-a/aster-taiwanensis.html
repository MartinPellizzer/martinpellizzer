Aster taiwanensis

Aster taiwanensis, commonly known as the Taiwan aster, is a flowering plant native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically produces showy blue to purple flowers, which bloom in the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Aster taiwanensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
