Aster × amethystinus f. leucerythros

Aster × amethystinus f. leucerythros, commonly known as the Purple Coneflower with White Petals, is a hybrid perennial plant native to North America.

It features showy, daisy-like flowers with violet-blue petals and a prominent white central disk. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its foliage is lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Botanically, it is valued for its hardiness and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
