Aerangis luteoalba var. luteoalba

Aerangis luteoalba var. luteoalba, commonly known as the yellow-white orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and lance-shaped leaves that are typically bright green. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

This orchid is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in the right conditions.
