Acianthera micrantha

Acianthera micrantha, commonly known as the small-flowered acianthera, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a terrestrial herb with a sympodial growth habit, characterized by its rhizomatous structure. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. Its leaves are lanceolate and arranged in a basal rosette, often with a waxy surface.

Native to tropical regions of South America, it thrives in humid, shaded environments.
