Anacheilium brachychilum

Anacheilium brachychilum, commonly known as the short-chilled anacheilium, is a flowering plant belonging to the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia, particularly in countries like Thailand and Vietnam. This species is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in spring. The plant prefers shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.
