Aloe straussii

Aloe straussii, commonly known as the Strassburger aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically gray-green in color, with a distinctive waxy coating. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in summer, adding ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for arid or semi-arid climates.

Aloe straussii is often used in rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements.
