Aristolochia fordiana

Aristolochia fordiana, commonly known as the Chinese birthwort, is a flowering plant native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, heart-shaped leaves and its unique, pipe-shaped flowers that are often greenish or brownish in color. The plant belongs to the Aristolochiaceae family and is known for its complex chemical composition, including various alkaloids. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has traditional uses in Chinese medicine.

However, it is important to note that some species of Aristolochia are toxic and should be handled with care.
