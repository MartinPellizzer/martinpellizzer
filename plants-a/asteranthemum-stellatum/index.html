Asteranthemum Stellatum

Starflower, scientifically known as Asteranthemum stellatum, is a strikingly beautiful plant native to South America. It is cherished for its vibrant, star-shaped blooms that resemble tiny suns. This perennial plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardeners. Its unique appearance and easy care requirements have made it a favorite in both ornamental and container gardening. With its ability to bloom throughout the growing season, the Starflower adds a touch of elegance and color to any landscape.
