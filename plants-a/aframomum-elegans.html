Aframomum elegans

Aframomum elegans, commonly known as the elegant cardamom, is a tropical herbaceous plant native to West Africa.

It belongs to the ginger family (Zingiberaceae) and is characterized by its aromatic, clustered inflorescences and broad, lance-shaped leaves. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is adapted to humid tropical climates.
