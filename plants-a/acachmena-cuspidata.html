Acachmena cuspidata

Acachmena cuspidata, commonly known as the prickly pear cactus, is a species of cactus native to the arid regions of the Americas.

The plant produces vibrant flowers, typically in shades of yellow, orange, or red, which bloom in the spring. Its fruit, known as a tuna, is edible and has been used by indigenous peoples for food and medicine.
