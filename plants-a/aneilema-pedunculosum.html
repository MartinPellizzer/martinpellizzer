Aneilema pedunculosum

Aneilema pedunculosum, commonly known as the African violet, is a tropical plant native to eastern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking violet or purple flowers that bloom on long, slender peduncles. The plant has fleshy, dark green leaves that are often velvety to the touch. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Aneilema pedunculosum is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
