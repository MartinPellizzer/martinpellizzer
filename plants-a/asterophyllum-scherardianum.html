Asterophyllum scherardianum

Asterophyllum scherardianum, commonly known as the Scherardian asterophyllum, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest undergrowth. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged alternately along the stem.

The flowers are small and yellow, often grouped into compact clusters, and bloom during the warmer months.
