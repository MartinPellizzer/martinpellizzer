Ammannia salsuginosa

Ammannia salsuginosa, commonly known as saltbush, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions.

It is characterized by its succulent, often blue-green leaves that help reduce water loss. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or red in color. It thrives in saline soils and is often found in coastal areas or salt flats.

Ammannia salsuginosa is valued for its hardiness and ability to stabilize soil in harsh environments.
