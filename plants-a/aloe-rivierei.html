Aloe rivierei

Aloe rivierei, commonly known as the Riviere's aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, with a waxy coating that helps it retain moisture. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow-orange flowers, which bloom in the spring. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Aloe rivierei is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional medicinal uses.
