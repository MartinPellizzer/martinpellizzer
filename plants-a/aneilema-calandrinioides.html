Aneilema calandrinioides

Aneilema calandrinioides, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette-like arrangement of velvety, dark green leaves. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of purple, pink, and white, often with a contrasting central stripe. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular houseplant.

Its botanical features make it both ornamental and relatively easy to care for in a home environment.
