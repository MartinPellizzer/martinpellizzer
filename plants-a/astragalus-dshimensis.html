Astragalus dshimensis

Astragalus dshimensis, commonly known as Chinese milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, pea-like flowers. The plant is known for its deep root system, which helps it thrive in arid and semi-arid environments. It produces small, hard seeds that are often dispersed by wind.

Astragalus dshimensis is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional Chinese medicine.
