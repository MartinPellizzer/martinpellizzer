Acalypha commersoniana var. acutifolia

Acalypha commersoniana var. acutifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved spurflower, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its finely serrated, lance-shaped leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers with vibrant red bracts that attract pollinators. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking a unique foliage plant.
