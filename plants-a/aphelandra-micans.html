Aphelandra micans

Aphelandra micans, commonly known as the silver-leaf croton, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its striking, glossy, dark green leaves with a silvery sheen. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are surrounded by a spathe, typical of the Araceae family. Aphelandra micans thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Its ornamental foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical landscapes.
