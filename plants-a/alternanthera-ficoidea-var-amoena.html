Alternanthera ficoidea var. amoena

Alternanthera ficoidea var. amoena, commonly known as red-veined sorrel, is a striking aquatic or semi-aquatic plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red veins contrasting with its dark green, heart-shaped leaves. This plant thrives in warm, humid conditions and is often cultivated as a decorative plant in ponds or water gardens. It produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters.

Its rapid growth rate makes it a popular choice for aquascaping and water feature design.
