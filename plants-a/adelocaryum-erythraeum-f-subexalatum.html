Adelocaryum erythraeum f. subexalatum

Adelocaryum erythraeum f. subexalatum, commonly known as the Red Sea spurge, is a rare succulent plant native to the coastal regions of the Red Sea.

The plant produces small, yellowish flowers during the warmer months, which are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds.
