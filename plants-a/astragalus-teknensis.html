Astragalus teknensis

Astragalus teknensis, commonly known as Teknish milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant is adapted to harsh, dry environments, with deep root systems that allow it to survive in poor soil conditions. It plays a role in stabilizing soil and supporting local ecosystems.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
