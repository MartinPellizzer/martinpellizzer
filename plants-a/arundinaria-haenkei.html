Arundinaria haenkei

Arundinaria haenkei, commonly known as the bamboo palm, is a tropical evergreen plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Arecaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and feather-like leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by dark blue berries. It thrives in humid environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Its growth habit makes it suitable for both indoor and outdoor settings.
