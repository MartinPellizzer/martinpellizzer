Alsine borealis

Alsine borealis, commonly known as the Arctic sandwort, is a flowering plant native to the Arctic and subarctic regions.

It is a low-growing perennial that thrives in cold, nutrient-poor environments. The plant has small, fleshy leaves that help it conserve water in harsh conditions. Its flowers are typically white or pink and bloom in the summer months.

Alsine borealis plays an important role in stabilizing soil in its extreme habitat.
