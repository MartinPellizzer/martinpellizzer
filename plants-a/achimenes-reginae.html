Achimenes reginae

Achimenes reginae, commonly known as the princess flower, is a tropical perennial plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, pink, and purple. The plant produces showy bracts that often resemble the actual flowers, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in warm, humid conditions and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Achimenes reginae is often cultivated as a bedding plant or in containers due to its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
