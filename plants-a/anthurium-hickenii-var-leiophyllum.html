Anthurium hickenii var. leiophyllum

Anthurium hickenii var. leiophyllum, commonly known as the white anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central America.

It is characterized by its striking, waxy, white spathes that contrast with its dark green, leathery leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically hidden within the spathe. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value in both indoor and outdoor settings.
