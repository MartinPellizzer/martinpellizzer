Anisophyllum scutelligerum

Anisophyllum scutelligerum, commonly known as the silver leaf plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flower heads that are typically clustered in dense inflorescences. It is native to arid and semi-arid regions of southern Africa, where it thrives in well-drained soils.

Anisophyllum scutelligerum is often used in traditional medicine and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
