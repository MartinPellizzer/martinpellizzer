Athyrium welwitschii

Athyrium welwitschii, commonly known as the Welwitsch's black fern, is a species of fern native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Athyriaceae and is characterized by its dark, glossy fronds that are often adorned with intricate patterns. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its rhizomes are thick and fleshy, allowing it to store water and nutrients efficiently.

Athyrium welwitschii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
