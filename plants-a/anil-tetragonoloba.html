Anil tetragonoloba

Anil tetragonoloba, commonly known as the Indian nettle or *Urena lobata*, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features palmate leaves with lobed margins, typically arranged in a basal rosette. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed habitats.

Anil tetragonoloba is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
