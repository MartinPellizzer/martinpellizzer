Aloe hanburiana

Aloe hanburiana, commonly known as the Hanbury's aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a waxy coating, which helps it store water. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with clusters of yellow flowers, blooming in late winter to early spring. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their soothing properties.

Aloe hanburiana thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.
