Alsophila tenuisecta

Alsophila tenuisecta, commonly known as the slender tree fern, is a species native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and a crown of feathery fronds that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant has a rhizomatous root system that helps it anchor in moist, shaded environments. Its fronds are typically arranged in a spiral pattern, contributing to its ornamental value.

Alsophila tenuisecta plays an important ecological role in forest ecosystems, providing habitat and contributing to biodiversity.
