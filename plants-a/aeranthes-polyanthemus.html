Aeranthes polyanthemus

Aeranthes polyanthemus, commonly known as the yellow orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Caribbean.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow petals and a compact, pseudobulbous structure. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in shaded, humid environments. Its flowers are fragrant and bloom in clusters, attracting various pollinators.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
