Arisaema eminens

Arisaema eminens, commonly known as the giant arrowhead, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length. The plant produces an unusual inflorescence consisting of a spadix enclosed by a spathe, typical of the Araceae family. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for botanical gardens and greenhouse cultivation.
