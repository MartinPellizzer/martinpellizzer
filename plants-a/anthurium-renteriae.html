Anthurium renteriae

Anthurium renteriae, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves and vibrant red spathes. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are surrounded by a colorful bract, which is often the primary ornamental feature. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium renteriae is popular as a houseplant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
