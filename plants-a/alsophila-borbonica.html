Alsophila borbonica

Alsophila borbonica, commonly known as the Bourbon tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and feathery fronds. The plant has a distinctive crown of pinnate fronds that can reach several meters in length. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.
