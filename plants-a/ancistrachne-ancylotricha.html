Ancistrachne ancylotricha

Ancistrachne ancylotricha, commonly known as the narrow-leaved grass, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to certain regions of Africa.

It belongs to the grass family (Poaceae) and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils.
