Achyranthes shahii

Achyranthes shahii, commonly known as Shahi achyranthes, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Amaranthaceae.

It is native to the Indian subcontinent and thrives in tropical and subtropical climates. The plant features erect, branched stems covered with soft, hairy leaves that are ovate to lanceolate in shape. Its flowers are small and clustered in panicles, typically displaying shades of pink or red.

The plant is often found in disturbed soils and is used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
