Aberemoa odorata

Aberemoa odorata, commonly known as the fragrant evergreen, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant produces small, round, red fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Aberemoa odorata is often cultivated for its ornamental value and pleasant scent.
