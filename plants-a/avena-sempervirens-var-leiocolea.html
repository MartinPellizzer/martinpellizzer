Avena sempervirens var. leiocolea

Avena sempervirens var. leiocolea, commonly known as the silky oat, is a perennial grass native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, upright stems covered in soft, silky hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences with pale green to golden florets. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often used in ornamental landscaping for its delicate, feathery look.
