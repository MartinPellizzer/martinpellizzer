Anthurium camposii

Anthurium camposii, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces vibrant red spadices, which are the showy inflorescences that house the small, inconspicuous true flowers. Anthurium camposii thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical landscapes.
