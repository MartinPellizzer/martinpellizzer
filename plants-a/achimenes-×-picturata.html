Achimenes × picturata

Achimenes × picturata, commonly known as the painted achimenes, is a hybrid perennial plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful, and often bicolor flowers that come in shades of red, orange, yellow, and white. The plant features velvety, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a slight waxy texture. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Achimenes × picturata is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant blooms and compact growth habit.
