Acer ornatum f. circumlobatum

Acer ornatum f. circumlobatum, commonly known as the Japanese maple with lobed leaves, is a cultivar of the species *Acer ornatum*.

It is characterized by its distinctive, lobed foliage that emerges in a vibrant red or orange hue in the spring. The plant is a deciduous tree or shrub, typically reaching heights of 3 to 6 meters. Its leaves are ovate to elliptical in shape, with finely serrated margins and a glossy texture.

It thrives in partial shade to full sun and is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
