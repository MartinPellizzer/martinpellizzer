Abies fargesii subsp. sutchuenensis

Abies fargesii subsp.

sutchuenensis, commonly known as the Sichuan fir, is a coniferous evergreen tree native to the mountainous regions of southwestern China. It is characterized by its slender, conical shape and dark green, needle-like leaves that are arranged in spirals along the branches.

The tree produces cone-like structures that contain seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in cool, moist climates and is often found in high-altitude forests.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitat.
