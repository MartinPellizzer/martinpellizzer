Agapanthus umbellatus var. globosus

Agapanthus umbellatus var. globosus, commonly known as the globular agapanthus, is a striking perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features dense, spherical clusters of blue or purple flowers that bloom in summer, creating a dramatic visual impact. The plant has strap-shaped, evergreen leaves that form a rosette at the base, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its unique flower structure and vibrant color make it a popular choice for both garden displays and cut flower arrangements.
