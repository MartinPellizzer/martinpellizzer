Anthericum senussiorum

Anthericum senussiorum, commonly known as the Senussi lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North Africa.

It features a basal rosette of narrow, sword-shaped leaves and produces tall, upright flowering stems. The plant's flowers are typically white or pale pink, arranged in clusters at the top of the stem. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in arid or semi-arid environments.

Anthericum senussiorum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its elegant appearance.
