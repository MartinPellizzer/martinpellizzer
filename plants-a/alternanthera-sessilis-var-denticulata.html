Alternanthera sessilis var. denticulata

Alternanthera sessilis var. denticulata, commonly known as the denticulateAlternanthera, is a striking aquatic plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its bright red or pink foliage, which intensifies under strong lighting conditions. The plant features small, rounded leaves with finely serrated edges, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It thrives in medium to high light environments and is often used in planted tanks for its ornamental value.

This variant is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice among aquarium enthusiasts.
