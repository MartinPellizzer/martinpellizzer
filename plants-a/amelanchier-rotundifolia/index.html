Amelanchier Rotundifolia

Amelanchier rotundifolia, also known as the roundleaf serviceberry, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern North America. It is prized for its early spring blooms and edible berries that ripen in late summer. This plant thrives in woodland habitats and is often found in moist, well-drained soils. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and natural landscapes. With its delicate flowers and vibrant fall foliage, it plays an important role in local ecosystems.
