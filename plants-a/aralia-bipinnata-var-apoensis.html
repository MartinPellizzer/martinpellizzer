Aralia bipinnata var. apoensis

Aralia bipinnata var. apoensis, commonly known as the Apo aralia, is a tropical plant native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its large, compound leaves that are bipinnately divided, giving it a feathery appearance. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in umbels, which are followed by spherical, spiny fruits. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
