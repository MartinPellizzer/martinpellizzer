Asparagus acocksii

Asparagus acocksii, commonly known as the Cape asparagus, is a perennial shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, spiny foliage and produces small, greenish-white flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for arid and semi-arid environments. Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

The plant is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional African medicine.
