Aromadendron yunnanense

Aromadendron yunnanense, commonly known as Yunnan aromatic tree, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to southwestern China.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant is known for its fragrant foliage, which emits a pleasant scent, particularly when crushed. It typically grows in humid, shaded environments within mountainous regions.
