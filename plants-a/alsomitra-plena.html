Alsomitra plena

Alsomitra plena, commonly known as the full-flowered alsomitra, is a climbing vine native to tropical regions of Asia.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, which bloom throughout the growing season. Alsomitra plena thrives in humid, warm climates and requires support for its climbing habit.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and greenhouses.
