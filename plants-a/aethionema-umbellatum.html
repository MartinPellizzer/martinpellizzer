Aethionema umbellatum

Aethionema umbellatum, commonly known as the rock cabbage, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces clusters of small, bright yellow flowers in the spring. The plant has fleshy, lobed leaves that are adapted to survive in dry, rocky environments. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its compact growth habit and vibrant blooms make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
