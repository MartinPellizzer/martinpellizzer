Abelia schumannii

Abelia schumannii, commonly known as Schumann's abelia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features showy, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink, blooming in late summer. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are typically dark green and covered in fine hairs. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained soil.

Abelia schumannii is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various garden conditions.
