Aichryson laxum

Aichryson laxum, commonly known as the silver leaf aichryson, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, silver-gray leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern, providing a striking visual appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the warmer months, typically in spring or summer. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a container plant.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
