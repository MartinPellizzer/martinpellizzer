Aloe ballii

Aloe ballii, commonly known as the spiral aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its unique spiral arrangement of fleshy, tubular leaves that form a compact rosette. The leaves are typically green with a powdery white coating, and they can grow up to 30 centimeters in length. It produces yellowish-green flowers in summer, which are borne on tall, branching inflorescences.

Aloe ballii is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
