Armeria mauritanica var. safiensis

Armeria mauritanica var. safiensis, commonly known as the Safien's thrift, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region, particularly Morocco.

It is a low-growing perennial that forms dense, compact clusters of foliage. The plant produces small, white or pinkish flowers that bloom in early spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its hardy nature and ornamental value make it a popular choice for gardeners in temperate climates.
