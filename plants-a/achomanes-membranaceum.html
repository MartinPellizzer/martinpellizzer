Achomanes membranaceum

Achomanes membranaceum, commonly known as the paperleaf daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its delicate, membranous leaves and small, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its attractive foliage and blooms.

However, it is not commonly found in cultivation outside its native range.
