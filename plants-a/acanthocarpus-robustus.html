Acanthocarpus robustus

Acanthocarpus robustus, commonly known as the African burr bush, is a perennial shrub native to southern Africa.

Its seeds are encased in spiny, woody pods, which contribute to its common name and aid in seed dispersal.

Acanthocarpus robustus is also valued for its potential in soil stabilization and as a forage plant for livestock.
