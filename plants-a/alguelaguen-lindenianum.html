Alguelaguen lindenianum

Alguelaguen lindenianum, commonly known as the silverleaf linden, is a deciduous tree native to the temperate regions of South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves that turn a striking silver-white color in the fall. The tree produces small, fragrant yellow flowers in the spring, which are followed by clusters of small, red berries. Its bark is smooth and gray, with shallow fissures, and it can grow up to 20 meters in height.

This plant is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often used in gardens for its seasonal color changes.
