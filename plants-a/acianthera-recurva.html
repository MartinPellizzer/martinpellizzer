Acianthera recurva

Acianthera recurva, commonly known as the recurved acianthera, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Due to its delicate appearance and specific habitat requirements, it is a relatively rare and endangered species.
