Albuca homblei

Albuca homblei, commonly known as the Cape Town albuca, is a bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of white or pale yellow flowers. The plant is adapted to arid environments and requires well-drained soil. It is often grown as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and flowering display.

Albuca homblei is relatively easy to cultivate in gardens with full sun and minimal water.
