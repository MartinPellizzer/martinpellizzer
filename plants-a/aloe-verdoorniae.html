Aloe verdoorniae

Aloe verdoorniae, commonly known as the Verdoorn's aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are thick and fleshy, adapted to store water in arid environments. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing yellow flowers, which attract pollinators such as bees and birds. Its leaves contain a gel-like substance that is used in traditional medicine and skincare products.

Aloe verdoorniae is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
