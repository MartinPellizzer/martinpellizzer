Asarum unzen f. luteoviride

Asarum unzen f. luteoviride, commonly known as the yellow-green asarum, is a variant of the asarum genus native to East Asia.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, yellow-green flowers. The plant thrives in shaded, moist environments, often found in forest understories. Its foliage is typically a deep green with a slight waxy texture, and the flowers emit a mild, aromatic scent.

This variant is valued in traditional medicine for its purported therapeutic properties.
