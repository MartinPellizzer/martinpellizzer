Alstroemeria foliosa

Alstroemeria foliosa, commonly known as the leafy alstroemeria, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the family Alstroemeriaceae and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant has long, narrow leaves that are often arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem. It is typically grown as a perennial in temperate climates and prefers well-drained soil and partial shade.

Alstroemeria foliosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in garden landscapes and floral arrangements.
