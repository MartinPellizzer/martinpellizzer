Atragene

Atragene, commonly known as the bluebell, is a flowering plant belonging to the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is native to the Mediterranean region and is characterized by its bell-shaped, blue or purple flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30-60 cm and features strap-like leaves. Atragene thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its early spring blooming habit.
