Antidesma oblongatum

Antidesma oblongatum, commonly known as the African starfruit or yellow starfruit, is a shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical or oblong in shape. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in clusters, followed by orange to red berries that are edible. The fruit is sometimes used in traditional medicine and as a source of food in its native regions.
