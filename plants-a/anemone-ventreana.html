Anemone ventreana

Anemone ventreana, commonly known as the Italian anemone, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features showy, pink to purple flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are palmately lobed and covered in a fine, white down.

Anemone ventreana is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting local pollinators.
