Astragalus dagestanicus

Astragalus dagestanicus, commonly known as Dagestan milkvetch, is a perennial herb native to the Caucasus region.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky soils. It plays a role in soil stabilization and is part of the diverse flora of its native habitat.

Due to its limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
