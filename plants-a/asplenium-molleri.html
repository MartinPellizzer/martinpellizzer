Asplenium molleri

Asplenium molleri, commonly known as the ebony spleenwort, is a fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Aspleniaceae and is characterized by its dark green, leathery fronds. The plant typically grows on shaded, moist rocks or soil, often in forested areas. Its rhizomes are wiry and help anchor it to its substrate.

Asplenium molleri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant.
