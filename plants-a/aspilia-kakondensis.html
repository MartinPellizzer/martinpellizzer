Aspilia kakondensis

Aspilia kakondensis, commonly known as the African torch flower, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking, spherical flower heads that resemble torches. The plant typically grows in open woodland and grassland habitats, preferring well-drained soils. Its flowers are yellow to orange in color and are surrounded by pale green bracts.

Aspilia kakondensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some African cultures.
