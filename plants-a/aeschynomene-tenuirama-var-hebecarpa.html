Aeschynomene tenuirama var. hebecarpa

Aeschynomene tenuirama var. hebecarpa, commonly known as the slenderleaf aeschynomene, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, often hairy stems and trifoliate leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers arranged in dense clusters, followed by narrow, elongated pods that contain several seeds. It is typically found in tropical and subtropical regions, often in disturbed soils or along roadsides.

This variant is notable for its adaptability and potential use in ecological restoration projects.
