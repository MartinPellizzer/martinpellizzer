Abutilon schinzii

Abutilon schinzii, commonly known as the silverleaf abutilon, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, featuring soft, silvery-gray foliage that gives it its common name. The plant produces showy, lantern-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, or red, which bloom in the spring and summer. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in fine, soft hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Abutilon schinzii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and vibrant flowers.
