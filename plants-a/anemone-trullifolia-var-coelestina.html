Anemone trullifolia var. coelestina

Anemone trullifolia var. coelestina, commonly known as the celestial anemone, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It features showy, single or double flowers with petals that range from white to pale blue, often with a distinctive violet or purple center. The plant has finely divided, fern-like leaves that are usually basal and clustered at the base. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate, ethereal blooms.
