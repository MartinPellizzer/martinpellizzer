Aglaonema nicobaricum var. luteum

Aglaonema nicobaricum var. luteum, commonly known as the Yellow-veined Aglaonema, is a striking ornamental plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves with prominent yellow veins, creating a vibrant and eye-catching appearance. The plant thrives in low-light conditions and prefers indirect sunlight, making it ideal for indoor cultivation. Its foliage remains evergreen, providing year-round visual interest.

Aglaonema nicobaricum var. luteum is also noted for its tolerance to moderate humidity and well-draining soil.
