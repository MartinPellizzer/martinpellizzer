Asystasia atriplicifolia

Asystasia atriplicifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf asystasia, is a flowering plant belonging to the Acanthaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Asia, including India, Malaysia, and the Philippines. Asystasia atriplicifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
