Arabis stelleri subsp. japonica

Arabis stelleri subsp.

japonica, commonly known as Japanese rockcress, is a perennial flowering plant native to East Asia. It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other flowering plants with small, cruciform flowers.

The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and is adapted to temperate climates. Its flowers are white or pale yellow and bloom in early spring.

It is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
