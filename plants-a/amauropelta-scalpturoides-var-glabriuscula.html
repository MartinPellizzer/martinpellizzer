Amauropelta scalpturoides var. glabriuscula

Amauropelta scalpturoides var. glabriuscula, commonly known as the smooth-leaved shield fern, is a variant of the shield fern species native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its shield-shaped fronds that are typically smooth and glabrous, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant's leaves are usually divided into a series of segments, forming a dense, compact structure. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Its botanical features make it a unique subject for both horticultural and ecological study.
