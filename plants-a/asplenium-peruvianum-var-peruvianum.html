Asplenium peruvianum var. peruvianum

Asplenium peruvianum var. peruvianum, commonly known as the Peruvian spleenwort, is a species of fern belonging to the Aspleniaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen foliage and is typically found growing on rock surfaces or in shaded, moist environments. The plant features small, lance-shaped leaves that are often clustered in dense rosettes, with a dark green coloration. It is native to South America, particularly Peru, and thrives in humid, temperate climates.

Due to its unique morphology and ecological niche, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists alike.
