Aconitum likiangense

Aconitum likiangense, commonly known as the Yunnan monkshood, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Yunnan, China.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue or purple spurred flowers. The plant has palmately compound leaves with a distinctive lobed structure. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in forested areas at high altitudes.

Due to its toxic alkaloids, it is important to handle the plant with care.
