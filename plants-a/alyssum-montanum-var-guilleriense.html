Alyssum montanum var. guilleriense

Alyssum montanum var. guilleriense, commonly known as the Sierra alyssum, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically forms dense mats of foliage. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of white, pink, or purple, which bloom in early spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and alpine settings.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental flowers make it a valued plant in horticulture.
