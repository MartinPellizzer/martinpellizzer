Agathis celebica

Agathis celebica, commonly known as the Celebes ironwood, is a large evergreen tree native to the tropical rainforests of Indonesia.

It belongs to the family Araucariaceae and is characterized by its tall, straight trunk and dense, dark green foliage. The tree produces large, cone-like structures that contain its seeds, which are dispersed by wind. Its wood is highly valued for its durability and strength, making it an important resource in local economies.

Agathis celebica also plays a significant role in the ecological balance of its habitat, providing shelter and food for various wildlife species.
