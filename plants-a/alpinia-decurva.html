Alpinia decurva

Alpinia decurva, commonly known as the black ginger or Chinese wild ginger, is a perennial herb native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes other economically important plants like ginger and turmeric. Alpinia decurva is also cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its attractive foliage and unique appearance.
