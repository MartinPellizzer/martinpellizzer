Agave manguai

Agave manguai, commonly known as the Mexican blue agave, is a succulent plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-shaped growth pattern. The plant features thick, blue-green leaves with white teeth along the edges, which help reduce water loss. It produces a tall flower stalk that bears clusters of white or pale blue flowers.

Agave manguai is well adapted to arid environments and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
