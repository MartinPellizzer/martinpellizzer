Angraecum grandidierianum

Angraecum grandidierianum, commonly known as the Madagascar star orchid, is a rare and striking epiphytic orchid native to Madagascar.

It features long, narrow leaves and produces large, fragrant, white flowers with prominent violet markings. The plant is characterized by its unique inflorescence, which consists of a raceme of showy, spurred flowers. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires high air humidity and good drainage.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is often cultivated in specialized orchid collections.
