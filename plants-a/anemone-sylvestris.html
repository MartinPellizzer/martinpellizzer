Anemone sylvestris

Anemone sylvestris, commonly known as the wood anemone, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in woodland areas and thrives in partial to full shade. The plant features showy white or pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring, often before the leaves emerge. Its leaves are heart-shaped and have a distinctive veining pattern.

Anemone sylvestris is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in shady garden landscapes.
