Aster dianchuanensis

Aster dianchuanensis, commonly known as the Dianchuan aster, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Yunnan Province in China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and medicinal properties. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces blue to violet flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in high-altitude environments with cool temperatures and well-drained soil.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological significance, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
