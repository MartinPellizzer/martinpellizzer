Anthemis biebersteiniana

Anthemis biebersteiniana, commonly known as the Alpine ragwort, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and western Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in alpine and subalpine habitats, thriving in well-drained, rocky soils. Its yellow-orange flowers bloom in late summer, and the plant is often found in open areas with full sunlight.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and has also been used in traditional herbal medicine.
