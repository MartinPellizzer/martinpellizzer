Aneilema pusillum subsp. pusillum

Aneilema pusillum subsp.

pusillum, commonly known as the small aneilema, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Africa. It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its arrow-shaped leaves and small, clustered inflorescences.

The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands. Its flowers are usually greenish-white with a spathe and spadix structure typical of the Araceae family.

The subspecies is distinguished by its compact size and specific habitat preferences.
