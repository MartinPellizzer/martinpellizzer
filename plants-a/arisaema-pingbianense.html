Arisaema pingbianense

Arisaema pingbianense, commonly known as the Pingbian arrowhead, is a rare and endangered plant species native to China.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its distinctive arrow-shaped leaves and spadix inflorescence. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as mountain forests. Its unique morphology and limited distribution make it a subject of interest for botanical research.

Conservation efforts are crucial to protect this rare species from habitat loss and over-collection.
