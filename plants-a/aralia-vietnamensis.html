Aralia vietnamensis

Aralia vietnamensis, commonly known as the Vietnamese aralia, is a flowering plant native to Vietnam.

It belongs to the Araliaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and clusters of small white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments in the understory of tropical forests. Its berries are dark blue to black and contain seeds that are dispersed by birds.

Aralia vietnamensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional medicinal uses in local cultures.
