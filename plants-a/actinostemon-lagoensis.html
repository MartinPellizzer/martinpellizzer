Actinostemon lagoensis

Actinostemon lagoensis, commonly known as the lake aster, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically features showy, daisy-like blooms with yellow central disks and white to pink petals. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found near water bodies.

Its botanical features make it a valuable addition to gardens and ecological restoration projects.
