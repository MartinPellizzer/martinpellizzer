Acrostichum leptophlebium

Acrostichum leptophlebium, commonly known as the narrow-leaf fern, is a species of fern native to tropical regions of Asia and the Pacific.

It is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped fronds that are typically green and glossy. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found near water bodies. Its reproductive structures, called sori, are located on the underside of the fronds.

Acrostichum leptophlebium is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens.
