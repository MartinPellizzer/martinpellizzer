Aster dumosus var. strictior

Aster dumosus var. strictior, commonly known as the compact aster, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the western United States.

It features dense, mat-forming growth with small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has hairy, gray-green leaves that are lance-shaped and arranged in a compact rosette. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its compact form and drought tolerance make it a valuable addition to native plant gardens.
