Artabotrys aereus

Artabotrys aereus, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, pendulous yellow flowers that resemble shower-like clusters, which bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has simple, opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth surface and a slightly glossy appearance. Its bark is smooth and grayish-brown, often with a faintly aromatic scent.

Artabotrys aereus is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in warm climates for its vibrant floral display.
