Alyssum smolikanum f. glabrum

Alyssum smolikanum f. glabrum, commonly known as the smooth alyssum, is a compact, low-growing plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The leaves are narrow and slightly succulent, with a soft, velvety texture. This variety is noted for its lack of hairy foliage, giving it a smooth appearance.

It is often used in rock gardens and as a border plant due to its ornamental value and ease of cultivation.
