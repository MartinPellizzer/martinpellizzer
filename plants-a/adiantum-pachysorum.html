Adiantum pachysorum

Adiantum pachysorum, commonly known as the broad-leaf maidenhair fern, is a delicate and elegant fern native to the southeastern United States.

It features finely divided, arching fronds with a distinctive silvery-white underside, which gives it a shimmering appearance. The plant thrives in shady, moist environments and prefers well-draining, acidic soil. Its foliage is evergreen, maintaining a lush green color throughout the year.

Adiantum pachysorum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in shaded garden settings or as a houseplant.
