Allantodia × Yaku-Montana

Allantodia × yaku-montana is a unique hybrid plant that combines traits from its parent species, Allantodia yakuensis and Allantodia montana. It is native to the mountainous regions of Japan, where it thrives in cool, moist environments. This plant is known for its striking foliage, featuring vibrant green leaves with a slight metallic sheen. Its ornamental value has made it a popular choice among gardeners and botanists alike. Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and valuable species in the plant world.
