Asplenium pocsii

Asplenium pocsii, commonly known as the Pocsii spleenwort, is a small, terrestrial fern native to the mountainous regions of Central America.

It belongs to the family Aspleniaceae and is characterized by its compact, evergreen foliage. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often on rock surfaces or in forest understories. Its fronds are usually narrow and elongated, with a dark green color and a serrated margin.

Asplenium pocsii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in shaded garden settings.
