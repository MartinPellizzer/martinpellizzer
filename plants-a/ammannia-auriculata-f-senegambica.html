Ammannia auriculata f. senegambica

Ammannia auriculata f. senegambica, commonly known as the African shield fern, is a variant of the genus Ammannia native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its distinctive, shield-shaped leaves that are usually dark green with a metallic sheen. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or red in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full sunlight.

This fern is often used in ornamental gardening due to its unique foliage and adaptability to various growing conditions.
