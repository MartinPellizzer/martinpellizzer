Artemisiastrum

Artemisiastrum, commonly known as silverleaf sage, is a genus of flowering plants in the sunflower family, Asteraceae.

It is native to North America and is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which gives it its common name. The plants typically have narrow, aromatic leaves and produce small, yellowish flower heads. Artemisiastrum species are often found in dry, open habitats such as prairies and rocky slopes.

They are valued for their ornamental appeal and traditional uses in herbal medicine.
