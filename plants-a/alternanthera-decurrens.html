Alternanthera decurrens

Alternanthera decurrens, commonly known as red arrowhead, is a striking aquatic or semi-aquatic plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its elongated, narrow leaves that often display vibrant red or pink hues, especially when exposed to strong light. The plant thrives in warm, shallow waters and is popular in ornamental ponds and aquariums. Its rapid growth rate makes it an effective choice for water gardeners seeking to control algae growth.

The flowers, which are small and inconspicuous, typically appear in clusters and are followed by small, round seeds.
