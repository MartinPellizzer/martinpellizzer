Abacosa onobrychioides

Abacosa onobrychioides, commonly known as the goldenweed, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a herbaceous annual with slender, erect stems and small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has pinnately divided leaves, which are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and fields.

Abacosa onobrychioides is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
