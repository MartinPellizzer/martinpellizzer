Atractylodes lancea var. simplicifolia

Atractylodes lancea var. simplicifolia, commonly known as Chinese yarrow or simply yarrow, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It features a robust rhizomatous root system and upright, branched stems that are often hairy. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in dense, flat-topped clusters called umbels. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a strong, aromatic scent.

It is commonly used in traditional medicine for its purported digestive and anti-inflammatory properties.
