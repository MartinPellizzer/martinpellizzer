Aretia pubescens subsp. cylindrica

Aretia pubescens subsp.

cylindrica, commonly known as the cylindrical aretia, is a flowering plant native to South America. It is characterized by its upright growth habit and hairy, cylindrical stems.

The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its foliage consists of narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine pubescence.
