Areca minuta

Areca minuta, commonly known as the dwarf areca palm, is a small, ornamental palm native to Southeast Asia.

It features a slender, unbranched trunk that grows to a height of about 2 to 3 meters. The plant has pinnate leaves that are dark green and often have a reddish tint on the underside. It produces small, white flowers that develop into three-winged fruits.

Areca minuta is well-suited for indoor cultivation and requires bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.
