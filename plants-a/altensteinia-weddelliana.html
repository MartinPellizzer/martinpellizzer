Altensteinia weddelliana

Altensteinia weddelliana, commonly known as the Weddell's altensteinia, is a rare and specialized fern that thrives in specific tropical and subtropical environments.

It is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds that often exhibit a unique bioluminescent quality under certain conditions. The plant is typically found in moist, shaded habitats such as forest understories or along stream banks. Its reproductive structures, including spore-bearing sorus, are arranged in distinctive patterns along the fronds.
