Adinandra crenulata

Adinandra crenulata, commonly known as the serrated adenium, is a shrub native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Apocynaceae and is characterized by its thick, often milky sap and branching structure. The plant features elliptical leaves with serrated edges and produces small, yellowish-green flowers. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release seeds.
