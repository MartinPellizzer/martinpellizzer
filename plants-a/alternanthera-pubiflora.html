Alternanthera pubiflora

Alternanthera pubiflora, commonly known as the velvetleaf joyweed, is a species of flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from red to orange and yellow. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine, velvety hairs, giving it a distinctive texture. It thrives in moist, tropical environments and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and ecological restoration projects.
