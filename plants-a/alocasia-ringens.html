Alocasia ringens

Alocasia ringens, commonly known as the prayer plant, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, arrow-shaped leaves that are often a deep green with contrasting veins. The plant is known for its unique leaf movement, where the leaves close at night and open in the morning. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

Alocasia ringens is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
