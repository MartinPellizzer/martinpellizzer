Allium tristissimum

Allium tristissimum, commonly known as weedy leek, is a flowering plant in the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, and it thrives in temperate climates. The plant produces small, white, bulbous flowers that bloom in the summer. Its leaves are long, narrow, and have a mild garlic-like odor.
