Aster linosyris subsp. armoricanus

Aster linosyris subsp.

armoricanus, commonly known as the Armorican aster, is a perennial herb native to Europe. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and cylindrical stems.

The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, blue or violet flowers in the fall.

Its botanical structure supports its role in ecological systems, providing nectar for pollinators and seeds for birds.
