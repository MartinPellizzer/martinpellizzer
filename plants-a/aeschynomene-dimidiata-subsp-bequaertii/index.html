Aeschynomene Dimidiata Subsp. Bequaertii

Aeschynomene dimidiata subsp. bequaertii is a lesser-known legume native to parts of Africa and Asia. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, making it a valuable plant for dryland agriculture. This subspecies is known for its ability to fix nitrogen, improving soil fertility in nutrient-poor soils. Its hardy nature and adaptability make it an important resource for sustainable farming practices. Despite its benefits, it remains underutilized in many regions where it could significantly contribute to food security.
