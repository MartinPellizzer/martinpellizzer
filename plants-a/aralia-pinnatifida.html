Aralia pinnatifida

Aralia pinnatifida, commonly known as Japanese aralia, is a deciduous shrub native to East Asia.

It features compound leaves with pinnate arrangements, giving it a feathery appearance. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers in umbel-like clusters, followed by red berries that are edible for humans and birds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
