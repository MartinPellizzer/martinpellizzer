Aloe niensiensis

Aloe niensiensis, commonly known as the Namibian aloe, is a succulent plant native to Namibia.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, with a waxy coating to reduce water loss. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow flowers, which bloom in the summer months. Aloe niensiensis is adapted to arid environments and stores water in its leaves for survival.

It is often used in traditional medicine and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
