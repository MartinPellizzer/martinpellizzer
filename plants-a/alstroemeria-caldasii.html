Alstroemeria caldasii

Alstroemeria caldasii, commonly known as the Caldasii Alstroemeria, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly found in Colombia and Ecuador.

It belongs to the family Alstroemeriaceae and is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that come in a range of colors, including red, orange, and yellow. The plant has strap-like, narrow leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and floral arrangements.

It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers partial to full sunlight, making it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.
