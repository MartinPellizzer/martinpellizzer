Arenaria longistyla

Arenaria longistyla, commonly known as the long-styled sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to arctic and alpine regions.

It belongs to the family Caryophyllaceae and is characterized by its small, clustered flowers and succulent leaves. The plant thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is often found in tundra and mountainous environments. Its elongated stamens and pistil make it distinctive among its relatives.
