Aloe meyeri

Aloe meyeri, commonly known as Meyer's aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color with a waxy coating. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing yellow flowers during the summer months. Its leaves contain a gel-like substance that is often used for its soothing properties.

Aloe meyeri is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
