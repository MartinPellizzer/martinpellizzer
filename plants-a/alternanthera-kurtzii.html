Alternanthera kurtzii

Alternanthera kurtzii, commonly known as the red-leaf alternanthera, is a striking aquatic plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red or pink foliage, which becomes more intense under bright light conditions. The plant has thin, branching stems and small, ovate leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. It thrives in freshwater environments and is popular among aquarium enthusiasts for its ornamental value.

Alternanthera kurtzii prefers moderate to high water temperatures and can be grown in both submerged and emersed forms.
