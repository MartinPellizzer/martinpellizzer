Acianthera per-dusenii

Acianthera per-dusenii, commonly known as the Per-dusen's acianthera, is a species of orchid native to South America, particularly Brazil.

The plant belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is known for its intricate floral structure. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in cloud forests.
