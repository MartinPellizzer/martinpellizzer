Aubrieta deltoidea subsp. sicula

Aubrieta deltoidea subsp.

sicula, commonly known as Sicilian rockcress, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region. It features compact, mat-forming growth with small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring.

The plant has hairy, linear leaves that are typically bluish-green in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and alpine settings.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to tolerate drought and poor soil conditions.
