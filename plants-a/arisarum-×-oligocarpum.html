Arisarum × oligocarpum

Arisarum × oligocarpum, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen, is a hybrid plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its striking, arrow-shaped leaves and colorful spadices. The plant typically grows as an evergreen perennial, often reaching heights of up to 1 meter. It thrives in shady, humid environments and is popular as a ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal.

Its botanical features make it a unique subject for both horticultural and scientific study.
