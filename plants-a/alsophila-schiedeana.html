Alsophila schiedeana

Alsophila schiedeana, commonly known as the Schiede's tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds that can reach several meters in length. The plant belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is known for its slow growth and longevity. Its rhizome is thick and fibrous, providing structural support and anchorage.

Alsophila schiedeana thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.
