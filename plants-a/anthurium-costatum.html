Anthurium costatum

Anthurium costatum, commonly known as the velvet leaf anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are dark green with a velvety texture. The plant produces spadices, which are inflorescences that resemble spikes, often surrounded by a colorful spathe. Anthurium costatum thrives in humid, warm environments with indirect light.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and ornamental purposes.
