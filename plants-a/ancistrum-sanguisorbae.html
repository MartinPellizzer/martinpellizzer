Ancistrum sanguisorbae

Ancistrum sanguisorbae, commonly known as the bloodroot orchid, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its striking, often red or orange-colored flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests or undergrowth. Ancistrum sanguisorbae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
