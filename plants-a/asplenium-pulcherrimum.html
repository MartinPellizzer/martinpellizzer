Asplenium pulcherrimum

Asplenium pulcherrimum, commonly known as the beautiful spleenwort, is a species of fern belonging to the family Aspleniaceae.

It is a small, terrestrial fern that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky crevices. The plant has slender, creeping rhizomes and produces delicate, lance-shaped fronds with a bright green color. Its spore-bearing structures, called sori, are located on the underside of the fronds, arranged in a distinctive pattern.

Asplenium pulcherrimum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage.
