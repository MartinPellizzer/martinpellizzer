Athyrium yuyangense

Athyrium yuyangense, commonly known as the Yuyang fern, is a species native to China.

It belongs to the Dryopteridaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, bi-lobed fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as forest undergrowth. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional Chinese medicine for their purported health benefits.

The foliage displays a vibrant green color, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
