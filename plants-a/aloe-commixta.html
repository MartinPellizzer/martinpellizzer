Aloe commixta

Aloe commixta, commonly known as the hybrid aloe or false aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, spathaceous leaves that are typically green with red or purple margins. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing yellowish-orange flowers. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable growing conditions.

Aloe commixta is also valued for its potential medicinal properties, though its use is less common compared to other aloe species.
