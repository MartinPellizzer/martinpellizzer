Asteriscus spinosus var. subacaulis

Asteriscus spinosus var. subacaulis, commonly known as the narrow-stemmed asteriscus, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its upright, sparsely branched stems and small, clustered flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink or purple. The leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
