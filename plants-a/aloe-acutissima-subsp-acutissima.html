Aloe acutissima subsp. acutissima

Aloe acutissima subsp.

acutissima, commonly known as the Cape aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa. It features rosettes of fleshy, spiky leaves that are green with white markings.

The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow flowers during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Its leaves are often used for their medicinal properties and ornamental value.
