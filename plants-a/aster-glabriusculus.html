Aster glabriusculus

Aster glabriusculus, commonly known as the smooth aster, is a herbaceous perennial native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and medicinal properties. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as prairies and rocky areas. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered with fine, short hairs, while its flowers are small and clustered in a flat-topped inflorescence.

Aster glabriusculus is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
