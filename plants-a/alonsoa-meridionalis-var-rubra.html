Alonsoa meridionalis var. rubra

Alonsoa meridionalis var. rubra, commonly known as the red-flowered alonsoa, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly found in regions of Argentina and Uruguay.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its vibrant blooms.
