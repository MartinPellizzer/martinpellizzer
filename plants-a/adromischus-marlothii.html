Adromischus marlothii

Adromischus marlothii, commonly known as the Marble Plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a bluish or purplish tint, often marked with distinctive white or silver patterns. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its leaves and requiring minimal watering.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardening.
