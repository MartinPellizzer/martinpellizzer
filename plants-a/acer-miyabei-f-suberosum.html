Acer miyabei f. suberosum

Acer miyabei f. suberosum, commonly known as the Japanese maple with red bark, is a unique cultivar of the maple tree.

It is characterized by its smooth, reddish-brown trunk that becomes more prominent with age. The plant produces delicate, palmate leaves that turn vibrant red in the fall. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained, acidic soil.

This ornamental tree is prized for its striking bark and seasonal color changes.
