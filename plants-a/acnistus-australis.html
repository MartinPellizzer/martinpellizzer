Acnistus australis

Acnistus australis, commonly known as the Australian starfruit or sandmat, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows in dry, sandy soils and is often found in coastal areas. The plant produces small, star-shaped yellow flowers that bloom in spring. Its fruit is a capsule that contains numerous small, hard seeds.

Acnistus australis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage and floral display.
