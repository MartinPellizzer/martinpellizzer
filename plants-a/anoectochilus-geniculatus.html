Anoectochilus geniculatus

Anoectochilus geniculatus, commonly known as the zigzag orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, zigzag-shaped inflorescence and small, intricate flowers with a distinctive coloration. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest undergrowth. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern.

Anoectochilus geniculatus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for horticultural purposes.
