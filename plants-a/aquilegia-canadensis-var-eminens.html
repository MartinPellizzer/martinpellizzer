Aquilegia canadensis var. eminens

Aquilegia canadensis var. eminens, commonly known as the tall Canadian columbine, is a perennial herb native to eastern North America.

It features striking blue to purple spurred petals and bright yellow anthers, which attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet, with palmate leaves divided into five segments. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers moist, well-drained soil.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and ecological benefits in native gardens.
