Alternanthera polygonoides var. austrotrinitatis

Alternanthera polygonoides var. austrotrinitatis, commonly known as the Trinidadian Alternanthera, is a striking aquatic plant native to South America.

It features vibrant red and pink foliage that intensifies under strong lighting conditions. The plant has small, lance-shaped leaves that are often arranged oppositely along its stems. It thrives in tropical freshwater environments and is popular in aquariums for its ornamental value.

This variety is also known for its ability to tolerate a range of water conditions, making it a versatile choice for aquascaping.
