Aerangis thomsonii

Aerangis thomsonii, commonly known as Thomson's orchid, is a species of orchid native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant typically blooms during the summer months, adding ornamental value to gardens. It prefers well-drained soil and bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements, it is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
