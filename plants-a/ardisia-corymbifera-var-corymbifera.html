Ardisia corymbifera var. corymbifera

Ardisia corymbifera var. corymbifera, commonly known as the Christmasberry, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, dark green elliptical leaves and produces clusters of small, white, fragrant flowers. The plant is notable for its ornamental berries, which turn bright red when mature, resembling Christmas ornaments. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Ardisia corymbifera var. corymbifera is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal in gardens and as a houseplant.
