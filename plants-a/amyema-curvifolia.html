Amyema curvifolia

Amyema curvifolia, commonly known as the zigzag lily, is a unique flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, spiral-shaped leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces clusters of showy, white or pink flowers that bloom during the warmer months. Amyema curvifolia thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Its ornamental value makes it a sought-after plant for botanical gardens and specialized collections.
