Aristolochia dielsiana

Aristolochia dielsiana, commonly known as the Chinese birthwort, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Aristolochiaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, pipe-shaped flowers that are often green or purple in color. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that attract insects. It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and along stream banks.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens, though it can be invasive in certain regions.
