Acosmium diffusissimum

Acosmium diffusissimum, commonly known as the broadleaf cowpea, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes known for their nitrogen-fixing capabilities. The plant features compound leaves with multiple leaflets and produces small, pod-like fruits containing edible seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often used as a forage crop.

Its botanical characteristics make it an important species in both agricultural and ecological contexts.
