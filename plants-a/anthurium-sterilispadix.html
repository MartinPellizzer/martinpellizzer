Anthurium sterilispadix

Anthurium sterilispadix, commonly known as the spadix anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful spadix, which is surrounded by a large, often brightly colored spathe. The plant belongs to the Araceae family, known for its unique floral structure consisting of a spadix and spathe. It thrives in humid, warm environments with bright, indirect light.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and botanical collections.
