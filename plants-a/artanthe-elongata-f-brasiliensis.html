Artanthe elongata f. brasiliensis

Artanthe elongata f. brasiliensis, commonly known as the Brazilian artanthe, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its delicate foliage and prolonged flowering period.
