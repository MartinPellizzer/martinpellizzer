Arracacia hartwegii

Arracacia hartwegii, commonly known as the Hartweg's arrowroot, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to South America.

It belongs to the family Araceae, which includes plants such as calla lilies and peace lilies. The plant is characterized by its broad, arrow-shaped leaves and produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some areas.
