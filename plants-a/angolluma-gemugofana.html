Angolluma gemugofana

Angolluma gemugofana, commonly known as the Moonlight Fern, is a striking plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Andes.

It is characterized by its delicate, lacy fronds that shimmer with a silvery sheen under sunlight. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for botanical gardens and specialized collections.

Angolluma gemugofana plays a vital role in its ecosystem by providing habitat for various microorganisms and small invertebrates.
