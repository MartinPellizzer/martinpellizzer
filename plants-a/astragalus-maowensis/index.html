Astragalus Maowensis

Astragalus maowensis is a rare flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of China. It belongs to the legume family and is known for its delicate, yellowish flowers. This species is considered endangered due to habitat loss and over-collection. Its unique characteristics make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists. Protecting Astragalus maowensis is crucial for preserving biodiversity in its natural habitat.
