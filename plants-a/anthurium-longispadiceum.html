Anthurium longispadiceum

Anthurium longispadiceum, commonly known as the long-spadix anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, elongated spadix surrounded by a vibrant, waxy spatula. The plant produces large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically deep green with a glossy surface. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Anthurium longispadiceum is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and unique floral structure.
