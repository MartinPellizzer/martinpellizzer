Adenocalymma comosum

Adenocalymma comosum, commonly known as the silky flax or silky everlasting, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

Its leaves are linear and often covered in a similar silken coating, contributing to its ornamental value.

Adenocalymma comosum is often grown in gardens for its aesthetic appeal and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
