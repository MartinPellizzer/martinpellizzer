Arachnorchis longiclavata

Arachnorchis longiclavata, commonly known as the long-clawed spider orchid, is an endemic species of Australia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its unique floral structure. The plant produces showy, spider-like flowers with long, slender sepals and petals. It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils in arid and semi-arid regions.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and vulnerable species.
