Aronia × floribunda

Aronia × floribunda, commonly known as the red chokeberry, is a hybrid shrub native to North America.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny growth habit and its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types. The plant produces small, dark red berries that are rich in antioxidants and are often used in culinary and medicinal applications. Its leaves are typically oval-shaped with serrated edges, and it blooms with clusters of white flowers in the spring.

Aronia × floribunda is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, providing food for birds and other wildlife.
