Araucaria dombeyi

Araucaria dombeyi, commonly known as the Dombey's Araucaria, is a coniferous evergreen tree native to South America.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and a crown of long, drooping branches. The tree produces small, cone-like structures that contain seeds, which are dispersed by wind. Its foliage consists of needle-like leaves that are typically dark green and arranged in a spiral pattern.

Araucaria dombeyi is often found in humid, temperate climates and is valued for its ornamental appearance and ecological significance.
