Armeria bigerrensis subsp. bigerrensis

Armeria bigerrensis subsp.

bigerrensis, commonly known as the narrow-leaved thrift, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region. It is characterized by its compact, low-growing habit and dense clusters of white or pinkish flowers.

The plant features basal leaves that are linear to narrowly elliptic, often with a bluish-gray hue. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its botanical features make it a valuable addition to both horticultural and ecological settings.
