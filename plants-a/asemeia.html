Asemeia

Asemeia, commonly known as the common hawksbeard, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its yellow flowers that resemble a hawk's beak, hence its common name. The plant typically grows in meadows and along roadsides, preferring well-drained soil. Asemeia has lance-shaped leaves and produces seeds in a fluffy, composite flower head.

It is often found in temperate regions across Europe and parts of Asia.
