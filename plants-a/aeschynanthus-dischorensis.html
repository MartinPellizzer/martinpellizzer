Aeschynanthus dischorensis

Aeschynanthus dischorensis, commonly known as the flame violet, is a member of the Gesneriaceae family and is native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is an evergreen shrub characterized by its trailing growth habit and vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. Aeschynanthus dischorensis is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often grown as a houseplant or in hanging baskets.
