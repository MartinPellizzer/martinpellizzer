Aspidopterys cavaleriei

Aspidopterys cavaleriei, commonly known as the Chinese cycad, is a species of cycad native to China.

It is characterized by its stout, unbranched stem and large, pinnately compound leaves that are typically bright green. The plant produces cone-like structures, with male and female cones occurring on separate plants. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in forested areas.

Due to its ornamental value and rarity, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
