Avena breviaristata

Avena breviaristata, commonly known as the short-awned oat, is a grass species native to the northern regions of the United States and southern Canada.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spikelet-based flowers that are typically greenish or brownish in color. Avena breviaristata is often found in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands.

Its botanical features make it an important component of ecological studies and conservation efforts in its native range.
