Abelia ionostachya

Abelia ionostachya, commonly known as the Greek Abelia, is a deciduous shrub native to southern Europe.

It features clusters of fragrant, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that turn red in the fall. It prefers well-drained soil and full to partial sun.

Abelia ionostachya is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
