Asplenium tenuiculum

Asplenium tenuiculum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf maidenhair fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Aspleniaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds that are typically dark green and have a feathery appearance. This plant prefers shady, moist environments and is often found in forest understories or on rock surfaces. Its rhizomes are wiry and can form dense colonies, contributing to its ability to thrive in various habitats.

Asplenium tenuiculum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to care for in the right conditions.
