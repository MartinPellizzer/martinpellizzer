Ardisia crenata var. taquetii

Ardisia crenata var. taquetii, commonly known as the Taquet's evergreen ardisia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, dark green elliptical leaves that are typically arranged in whorls along the stems. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers followed by bright red, spherical berries that are edible for some bird species. It thrives in partial to full shade and prefers well-drained, moist soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
