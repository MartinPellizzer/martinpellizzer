Astragalus kopetdaghi

Astragalus kopetdaghi, commonly known as the Kopetdag milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, pea-like flowers. The plant typically grows in arid and semi-arid environments, adapting to harsh conditions with deep root systems. It plays a role in soil stabilization and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.

Its ecological significance is notable in its native habitats.
