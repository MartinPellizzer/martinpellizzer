Atriplex centralasiatica

Atriplex centralasiatica, commonly known as the Central Asian saltbush, is a shrub native to Central Asia.

It is characterized by its succulent leaves and small, greenish-yellow flowers. The plant is adapted to arid and saline environments, making it a hardy species in harsh conditions. Its leaves are often coated with a waxy layer to reduce water loss.

Atriplex centralasiatica plays a role in stabilizing soils and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
