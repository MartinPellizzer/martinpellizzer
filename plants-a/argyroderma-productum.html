Argyroderma productum

Argyroderma productum, commonly known as the silverleaf tree, is a species of flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray leaves, which are covered in a dense layer of fine, reflective hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters, typically during the spring and summer seasons. Its bark is thick and fissured, providing a rugged appearance.

Argyroderma productum is often found in dry, arid environments and is adapted to thrive in regions with low rainfall.
