Ajax luteus var. major

Ajax luteus var. major, commonly known as the golden ajax, is a striking perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features showy, orange-yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and grows in clumps, reaching heights of up to 2 feet. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Its vibrant color and compact size make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
