Aloe officinalis var. officinalis

Aloe officinalis var. officinalis, commonly known as the common aloe, is a succulent plant native to the arid regions of Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with white or yellowish markings. The plant produces a spiky, branched inflorescence that bears yellow flowers. Its leaves contain a gel-like substance used in traditional medicine and skincare.

It is well adapted to dry environments, storing water in its leaves and having a shallow root system.
