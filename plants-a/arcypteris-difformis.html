Arcypteris difformis

Arcypteris difformis, commonly known as the variable fern.

It is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae, characterized by its delicate, feather-like fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as tropical and subtropical forests. Its rhizomes are creeping and produce both sterile and fertile fronds.

The spores are released from the sori, which are located on the underside of the leaves.
