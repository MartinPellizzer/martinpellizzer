Ammannia teres var. exauriculata

Ammannia teres var. exauriculata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved ammannia, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features upright, branching stems that often bear clusters of small, pinkish to red flowers. The leaves are lance-shaped and typically have a smooth or slightly toothed margin. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to stabilize soil in aquatic environments.
