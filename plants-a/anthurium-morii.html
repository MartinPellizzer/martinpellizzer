Anthurium morii

Anthurium morii, commonly known as the Morii arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive spathe and spadix inflorescence. The plant features heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a velvety texture and prominent veins. It thrives in humid, warm environments with bright, indirect light.

Anthurium morii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated as a houseplant.
