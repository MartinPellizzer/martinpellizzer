Asplenium fournieri

Asplenium fournieri, commonly known as the Fournier's spleenwort, is a small, evergreen fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Aspleniaceae and is characterized by its compact, rosette-like growth habit. The plant features finely divided, lanceolate leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. It thrives in shaded, moist environments, often growing on rocks or tree trunks.

Asplenium fournieri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in humid indoor settings.
