Ardisia murtonii

Ardisia murtonii, commonly known as the Murton's evergreen, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Myrtle family (Myrtaceae) and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, white flowers that are followed by bright red berries, which are an important food source for various wildlife. Ardisia murtonii thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for horticultural use in tropical gardens.
