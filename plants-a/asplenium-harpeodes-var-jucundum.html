Asplenium harpeodes var. jucundum

Asplenium harpeodes var. jucundum, commonly known as the golden hartscale, is a fern species native to parts of Asia, including China and Japan.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen fronds that are typically bright green with a slightly glossy texture. The plant produces small, spore-bearing structures called sorangs on the undersides of its leaves. It thrives in shady, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardening.
