Abelia macrotera var. zabelioides

Abelia macrotera var. zabelioides, commonly known as Zabel's abelia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features showy, fragrant, tubular flowers that bloom in late summer, typically in shades of pink and white. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are dark green and covered in fine hairs. It prefers partial shade to full sun and well-drained soil, making it adaptable to various garden conditions.

Zabel's abelia is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in temperate climates.
