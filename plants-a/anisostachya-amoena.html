Anisostachya amoena

Anisostachya amoena, commonly known as the purple lovegrass, is a perennial grass native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and slender, wiry stems that often curl at the tips. The plant produces fluffy, purple-tinted inflorescences that are both ornamental and wind-dispersed. Its leaves are narrow, blue-green in color, and have a slight sheen.

Anisostachya amoena is adapted to arid and semi-arid conditions, making it a hardy choice for drought-tolerant landscaping.
