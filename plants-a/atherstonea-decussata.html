Atherstonea decussata

Atherstonea decussata, commonly known as the clustered starwort, is a small, herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream sides. The plant has a basal rosette of lanceolate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small, white or pale yellow, and arranged in a loose cluster at the tip of the stem.

Atherstonea decussata is characterized by its simple, alternate leaf arrangement and is often found in calcareous soils.
