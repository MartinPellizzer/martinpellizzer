Anthurium diabloense

Anthurium diabloense, commonly known as the Diablo Anthurium, is a rare tropical plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves and vibrant spadix. The plant produces colorful, spathe-like bracts that can range from red to orange, enhancing its ornamental value. It thrives in humid, shaded environments with well-draining soil.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native range.
