Aloe bella

Aloe bella, commonly known as the dwarf aloe or miniature aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, upright leaves that are typically green with a slight bluish tint. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the summer months, which are followed by red, three-lobed seed pods. Aloe bella thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Its compact size and ornamental appearance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
