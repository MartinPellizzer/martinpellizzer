Ayenia ardua

Ayenia ardua, commonly known as the hardy ayenia, is a perennial shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of small, showy white flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has a compact growth habit and is well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments. Its leaves are typically leathery and dark green, providing year-round visual interest.

Ayenia ardua is often used in rock gardens and as a drought-tolerant ornamental plant.
