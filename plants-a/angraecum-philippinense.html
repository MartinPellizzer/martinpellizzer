Angraecum philippinense

Angraecum philippinense, commonly known as the Philippine angraecum, is a species of orchid native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and large, showy flowers that are typically white or pale pink. The plant produces intricate, spurred flowers that are adapted for pollination by specific nocturnal moths. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Angraecum philippinense is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
