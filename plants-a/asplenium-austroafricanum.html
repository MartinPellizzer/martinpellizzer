Asplenium austroafricanum

Asplenium austroafricanum, commonly known as the African shield fern, is a species of fern native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Aspleniaceae and is characterized by its delicate, shield-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky crevices. Its rhizomes are often covered in a dense layer of rhizome scales, which contribute to its unique appearance.

Asplenium austroafricanum is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
