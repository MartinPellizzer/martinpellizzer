Anemia ciliata

Anemia ciliata, commonly known as the silky leaf plant, is a perennial herb native to tropical regions.

It features slender, hairy stems and broad, heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and traditional herbal use.
