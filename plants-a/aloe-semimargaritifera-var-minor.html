Aloe semimargaritifera var. minor

Aloe semimargaritifera var. minor, commonly known as the dwarf aloe or small bead aloe, is a compact, succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, gray-green leaves that are adorned with white tubercles, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow flowers during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or container cultivation.

Its slow growth rate and ornamental appeal make it a popular choice for collectors and gardeners.
