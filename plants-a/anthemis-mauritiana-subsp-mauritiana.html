Anthemis mauritiana subsp. mauritiana

Anthemis mauritiana subsp.

mauritiana, commonly known as the Mauritius dandelion, is a flowering plant native to the island of Mauritius. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its yellow composite flowers and hairy, lance-shaped leaves.

The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. It produces small, wind-dispersed seeds that contribute to its propagation.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered vulnerable and requires conservation efforts to ensure its survival.
