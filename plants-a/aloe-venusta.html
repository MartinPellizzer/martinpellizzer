Aloe venusta

Aloe venusta, commonly known as the "Cape aloe," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of fleshy, pointed leaves that are pale green with a waxy coating. The plant produces tall, branched stems with yellow-orange flowers during the summer months. Its leaves contain a gel-like substance that is used in traditional medicine and skincare products.

Aloe venusta is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight.
