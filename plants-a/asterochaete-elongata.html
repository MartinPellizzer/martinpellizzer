Asterochaete elongata

Asterochaete elongata, commonly known as the elongated aster.

It is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its daisy-like composite flowers. The plant is native to certain regions of Africa and is often found in grasslands and rocky areas. Its leaves are typically narrow and lance-shaped, while its flowers consist of both ray and disk florets.

Asterochaete elongata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
