Atraphaxis afghanica

Atraphaxis afghanica, commonly known as the Afghan desert thorn, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of Central Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that thrives in hot, dry environments with poor soil conditions. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters, typically during the warmer months. Its leaves are small and often reduced to spines, which help reduce water loss.
