Arenaria longiseta

Arenaria longiseta, commonly known as the long-hair sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to arctic and alpine regions.

It typically thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to harsh environmental conditions. The plant features small, white or pinkish flowers that bloom in early summer. Its leaves are often covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Arenaria longiseta plays a role in stabilizing soil in its native habitats and is of interest to botanists studying plant adaptations to extreme climates.
