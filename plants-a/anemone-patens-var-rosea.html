Anemone patens var. rosea

Anemone patens var. rosea, commonly known as the Roseleaf Anemone, is a herbaceous perennial native to the eastern United States.

It features showy, pink to rose-colored flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has a basal rosette of heart-shaped leaves and produces erect, branched stems. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade.

Its delicate appearance makes it a popular choice for woodland gardens and naturalized areas.
