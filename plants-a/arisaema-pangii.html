Arisaema pangii

Arisaema pangii, commonly known as the Bornean cobra lily, is a member of the Araceae family.

It is native to Borneo and is characterized by its striking, hooded spathe and a spadix that bears both male and female flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist forest understories and is often found in montane regions. Its foliage consists of large, heart-shaped leaves that are usually a deep green.

Arisaema pangii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens.
