Arenaria serpyllifolia var. gracillima

Arenaria serpyllifolia var. gracillima, commonly known as slender sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

It typically features small, needle-like leaves and clusters of white or pale pink flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to tolerate harsh environmental conditions.

Its botanical characteristics make it a hardy choice for rock gardens and alpine landscapes.
