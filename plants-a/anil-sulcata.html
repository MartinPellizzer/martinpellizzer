Anil sulcata

Anil sulcata, commonly known as the African violet, is a tropical plant native to East Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves and vibrant, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its foliage is often velvety and dark green, with a distinctive central stem.

Anil sulcata is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
