Aspidistra punctata var. albomaculata

Aspidistra punctata var. albomaculata, commonly known as the white-spotted aspidistra, is a perennial evergreen plant native to southern China.

It features dark green, lance-shaped leaves with distinctive white or pale green spots along the veins. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and is often grown as a decorative foliage plant. It prefers shady, humid conditions and well-draining soil.

Its ornamental value lies in its striking leaf patterns and ability to thrive in low-light environments.
