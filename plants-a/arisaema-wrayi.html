Arisaema wrayi

Arisaema wrayi, commonly known as the red-tailed arisaema, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, umbrella-shaped inflorescence with brightly colored bracts. The plant has a short rhizomatous stem and produces small, greenish-white flowers. Its foliage consists of large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a waxy cuticle.

Arisaema wrayi is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its striking appearance and unique growth habit.
