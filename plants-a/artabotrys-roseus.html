Artabotrys roseus

Artabotrys roseus, commonly known as the pink trumpet tree, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, pink to purple trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has opposite, simple, and ovate leaves with a smooth surface. It thrives in tropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release its seeds.
