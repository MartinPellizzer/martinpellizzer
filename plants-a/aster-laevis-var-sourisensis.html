Aster laevis var. sourisensis

Aster laevis var. sourisensis, commonly known as the Sourisensis Aster, is a perennial herb native to specific regions of North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of ray and disk florets. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands. Its leaves are generally smooth and lanceolate, while the flowers are blue to purple in color.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting pollinators.
