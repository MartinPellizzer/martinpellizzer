Andropogon sorghum var. elegans

Andropogon sorghum var. elegans, commonly known as the elegant sorghum, is a perennial grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes for its visual appeal.
