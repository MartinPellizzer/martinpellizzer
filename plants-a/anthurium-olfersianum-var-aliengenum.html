Anthurium olfersianum var. aliengenum

Anthurium olfersianum var. aliengenum, commonly known as the Alien Anthurium, is a rare and exotic variant of the Anthurium genus native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its striking, elongated spathes that can range in color from deep red to vibrant orange, often with intricate patterns. The plant produces small, greenish-white spadices that are typical of the Anthurium family. It thrives in high humidity and warm temperatures, requiring indirect light and well-draining soil.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is a sought-after specimen among collectors and horticulturists.
