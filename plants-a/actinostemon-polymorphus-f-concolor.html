Actinostemon polymorphus f. concolor

Actinostemon polymorphus f. concolor, commonly known as the variegated actinostemon, is a rare and unique cultivar of the Actinostemon genus.

It is characterized by its striking variegated foliage, featuring shades of green, white, and yellow. The plant is native to South Africa and is typically found in rocky, well-drained soils. It has a compact growth habit and produces small, clustered flowers in shades of pink and white.

Due to its ornamental value and limited availability, it is a sought-after plant among collectors and gardeners.
