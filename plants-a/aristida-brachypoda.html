Aristida brachypoda

Aristida brachypoda, commonly known as blue grama, is a perennial grass native to North America.

It is characterized by its fine, blue-green foliage and compact growth habit. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny flowers. It thrives in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and prairies.

Blue grama is an important component of native plant communities and is often used in restoration projects.
