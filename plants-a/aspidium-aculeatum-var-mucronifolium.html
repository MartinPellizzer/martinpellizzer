Aspidium aculeatum var. mucronifolium

Aspidium aculeatum var. mucronifolium, commonly known as the blunt-leaf water fern, is a variant of the common water fern.

It is a perennial aquatic plant native to tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features delicate, pinnately divided fronds that are typically submerged or floating. Its spores are produced in sori located on the undersides of the fronds.

This variety is often found in still or slow-moving freshwater habitats.
