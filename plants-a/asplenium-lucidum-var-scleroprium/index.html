Asplenium Lucidum Var. Scleroprium

Scleroprium fern, scientifically known as Asplenium lucidum var. scleroprium, is a rare and unique member of the fern family. Native to specific regions in Southeast Asia, this plant thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its distinctive appearance features dense, leathery fronds that give it a striking visual appeal. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively unknown to the general public. This article explores its characteristics, habitat, and conservation status.
