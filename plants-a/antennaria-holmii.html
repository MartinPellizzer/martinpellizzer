Antennaria holmii

Antennaria holmii, commonly known as the Holm's cinquefoil, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Arctic and subarctic regions.

It features a mat-forming growth habit with small, hairy leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to cold, harsh environments. It is often found in alpine tundra and coastal areas where it contributes to soil stabilization.

Antennaria holmii is an important component of the flora in its native habitats, showcasing resilience to extreme climatic conditions.
