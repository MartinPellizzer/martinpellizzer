Arachniodes polyodon

Arachniodes polyodon, commonly known as the giant bamboo fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, pinnately compound leaves that can reach lengths of up to 3 meters. The plant features a robust rhizome system that allows it to thrive in moist, shaded environments. Its fronds are often described as having a delicate, lacy appearance due to the fine, thread-like veins.

Arachniodes polyodon is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to create a lush, tropical effect in gardens.
