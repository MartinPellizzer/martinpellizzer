Achimenes gloxiniiflora

Achimenes gloxiniiflora, commonly known as the gloxinia orchid, is a tropical perennial plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that resemble those of orchids, with vibrant colors such as red, pink, and purple. The plant has heart-shaped, velvety leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Achimenes gloxiniiflora is often cultivated as a ornamental plant for its decorative foliage and long-lasting blooms.
