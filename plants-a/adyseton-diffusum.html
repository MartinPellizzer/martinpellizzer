Adyseton diffusum

Adyseton diffusum, commonly known as the broadleaf mallow, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves and clusters of pink or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. It is often found in grasslands and along riverbanks, where it plays a role in stabilizing soil and supporting local wildlife.

Adyseton diffusum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and blooms.
