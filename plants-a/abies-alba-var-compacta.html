Abies alba var. compacta

Abies alba var. compacta, commonly known as the compact fir, is a compact, slow-growing variant of the European silver fir.

It is characterized by its dense, pyramidal shape and dark green, needle-like foliage. The plant features short, stout shoots and cones that are typically reddish-brown in color. It is native to the mountainous regions of Central and Southern Europe.

This variety is often cultivated as an ornamental plant in gardens and landscapes due to its unique appearance and hardy nature.
