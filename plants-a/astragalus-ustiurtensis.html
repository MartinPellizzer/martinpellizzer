Astragalus ustiurtensis

Astragalus ustiurtensis, commonly known as the Ustiurt milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its small, yellowish flowers and compound leaves. The plant is adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments, often growing in sandy or gravelly soils. Its root system is deep and extensive, allowing it to access water from deeper soil layers.

Astragalus ustiurtensis plays a role in stabilizing soils and supporting local ecosystems in its native habitat.
