Antirrhinum aparinoides

Antirrhinum aparinoides, commonly known as the slender toadflax, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces delicate, tubular flowers in shades of yellow and orange. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and is often found in rocky or sandy soils. It belongs to the Plantaginaceae family and is characterized by its unique floral structure.
