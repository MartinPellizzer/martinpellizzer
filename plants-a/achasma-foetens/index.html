Achasma Foetens

Achasma foetens is a rare and peculiar plant known for its distinctive appearance and strong, unpleasant odor. It thrives in specific, often remote environments where moisture and shade are abundant. Despite its unappealing scent, it plays a unique role in its ecosystem. The plant's structure consists of a short, woody stem and clusters of small, greenish flowers. Its existence remains a subject of fascination for botanists and nature enthusiasts alike.
