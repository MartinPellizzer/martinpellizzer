Argyrocome stipitata

Argyrocome stipitata, commonly known as the silver-stemmed orchid, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive silver-gray stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical forests.

Due to its unique appearance and ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens.
