Alstonia glabriflora

Alstonia glabriflora, commonly known as the smooth-flowered alstonia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a deciduous tree that can grow up to 20 meters tall, featuring a straight trunk and smooth, greyish bark. The leaves are alternate, simple, and elliptical, with a glossy green upper surface and a pale underside. Its flowers are small, white, and clustered in panicles, blooming in the summer months.

The plant produces woody pods that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.
