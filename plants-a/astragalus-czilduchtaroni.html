Astragalus czilduchtaroni

Astragalus czilduchtaroni, commonly known as the Czilduchtaroni milkvetch, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and small, pea-like flowers that typically range in color from white to pale pink. It plays a role in soil stabilization and is of interest for its potential medicinal properties.
