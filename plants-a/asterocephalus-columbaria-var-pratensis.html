Asterocephalus columbaria var. pratensis

Asterocephalus columbaria var. pratensis, commonly known as the meadow aster.

This plant is a herbaceous species belonging to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its showy composite flower heads. It typically features yellow disk flowers surrounded by pale blue to white ray florets, which attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. The plant grows in open, moist habitats like meadows and wetlands, often forming dense colonies.

Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive botanical appearance.
