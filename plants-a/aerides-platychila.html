Aerides platychila

Aerides platychila, commonly known as the broad-leafed orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant features long, narrow leaves and produces clusters of fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
