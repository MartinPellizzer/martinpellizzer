Alchemilla argyrophylla f. thorei

Alchemilla argyrophylla f. thorei, commonly known as silverleaf lady's mantle, is a low-growing perennial plant characterized by its silvery-gray foliage.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers in spring, which are followed by bright red bracts that persist into autumn. Its leaves are covered in dense, fine hairs that give them a distinctive silver appearance.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental qualities and ability to thrive in well-drained, rocky soils.
