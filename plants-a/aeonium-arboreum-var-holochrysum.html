Aeonium arboreum var. holochrysum

Aeonium arboreum var. holochrysum, commonly known as the "Golden Tree Aeonium," is a striking succulent native to the Canary Islands.

It features a tree-like structure with dense, fleshy rosettes of bright yellow to orange-green leaves that are covered in a waxy, powdery coating. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with small, star-shaped yellow flowers during the spring. Its compact growth habit and vibrant foliage make it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.

It thrives in well-draining soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
