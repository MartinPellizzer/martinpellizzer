Aloe linearifolia

Aloe linearifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features tall, spindly stems with narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. The plant produces yellow-orange flowers in clusters during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Its leaves are used in traditional medicine and for their soothing gel properties.
