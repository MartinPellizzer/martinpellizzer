Alsodeia angustifolia

Alsodeia angustifolia, commonly known as narrowleaf alsodeia, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing annual herb with slender, branching stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of tiny, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed areas such as roadsides and fields.

Alsodeia angustifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in wildflower gardens.
