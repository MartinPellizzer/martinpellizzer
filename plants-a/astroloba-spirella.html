Astroloba spirella

Astroloba spirella, commonly known as the spiral aster.

It is a succulent plant native to South Africa, characterized by its unique spiral arrangement of leaves. The plant features dense, fleshy foliage that forms a compact rosette, often with a reddish or purple hue. It produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, or purple during the warmer months.

Astroloba spirella is well-suited for rock gardens or dry environments due to its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements.
