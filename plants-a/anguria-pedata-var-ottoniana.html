Anguria pedata var. ottoniana

Anguria pedata var. ottoniana, commonly known as the Otto's broadleaf cucumber, is a variant of the broadleaf cucumber species.

It is characterized by its large, broad, lobed leaves that are typically heart-shaped. The plant produces yellow flowers that develop into elongated, cylindrical fruits. It thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This variety is often cultivated for its unique foliage and edible fruits.
