Androsace lanuginosa var. leichtlinii

Androsace lanuginosa var. leichtlinii, commonly known as the downy androsace, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the alpine regions of Europe.

It features dense, mat-forming foliage with soft, velvety leaves covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in rocky or gravelly soils.

Due to its ornamental value and habitat specificity, it is a sought-after plant in rock gardens and alpine collections.
