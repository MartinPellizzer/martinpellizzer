Astilbe formosa

Astilbe formosa, commonly known as the Japanese feather reed, is a herbaceous perennial native to East Asia.

It features delicate, plume-like inflorescences that range in color from white to pink and purple, depending on the variety. The plant thrives in partial to full shade and prefers moist, well-drained soil. Its foliage is typically dark green and fern-like, adding a lush texture to garden landscapes.

Astilbe formosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in woodland or shaded garden settings.
