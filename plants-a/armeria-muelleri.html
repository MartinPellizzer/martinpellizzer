Armeria muelleri

Armeria muelleri, commonly known as Mueller's thrift, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces clusters of white or pink flowers in spring. The plant has succulent leaves that are fleshy and covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its hardy nature and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking drought-resistant plants.
