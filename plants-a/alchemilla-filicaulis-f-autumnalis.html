Alchemilla filicaulis f. autumnalis

Alchemilla filicaulis f. autumnalis, commonly known as autumnal lady's mantle, is a horticultural form of the alchemilla genus.

It is characterized by its silver-green, spoon-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, fuzzy hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers in late summer to early autumn. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in woodland gardens.
