Ardisia spectabilis

Ardisia spectabilis, commonly known as the showy ardisia, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Ericaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas, particularly Central and South America. Ardisia spectabilis is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and showy fruit.
