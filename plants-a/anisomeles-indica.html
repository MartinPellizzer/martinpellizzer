Anisomeles indica

Anisomeles indica, commonly known as the Indian hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, featuring broad, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, or red, which bloom throughout the year in warm climates. Its botanical features include a woody stem, simple leaves, and a sympodial growth pattern.

Anisomeles indica is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
