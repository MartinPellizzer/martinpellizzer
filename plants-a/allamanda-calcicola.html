Allamanda calcicola

Allamanda calcicola, commonly known as the limestone allamanda, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in limestone soils, showcasing its adaptability to alkaline conditions. The plant produces large, showy yellow flowers that are often followed by orange or red fruits. Its leaves are elliptical and glossy, providing a striking contrast to the vibrant blooms.

Allamanda calcicola is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in specific soil types.
