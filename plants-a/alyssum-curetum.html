Alyssum curetum

Alyssum curetum, commonly known as lesser alyssum, is a low-growing annual plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in the spring and early summer. The plant has hairy, linear leaves and a branched stem, often growing close to the ground. It is often found in disturbed soils, along roadsides, and in dry, open habitats.

Alyssum curetum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in garden settings for its delicate appearance.
