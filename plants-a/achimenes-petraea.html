Achimenes petraea

Achimenes petraea, commonly known as the rock achimenes, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and Central America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of red, pink, and purple. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and thrives in well-drained soil and full sun. It is often grown as an ornamental plant in gardens and greenhouses.

Its blooming period extends from summer to autumn, making it a popular choice for seasonal floral displays.
