Anthurium flagellum

Anthurium flagellum, commonly known as the whip anthurium, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its long, narrow, and often pendulous leaves that can grow up to several feet in length. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-shaped inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, typically in shades of red, orange, or yellow. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and requires well-draining soil.

Anthurium flagellum is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
