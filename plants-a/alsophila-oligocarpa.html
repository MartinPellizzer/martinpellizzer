Alsophila oligocarpa

Alsophila oligocarpa, commonly known as the slender tree fern, is a member of the Cyatheaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a tall, upright fern that can grow up to 10 meters in height, characterized by its slender trunk and large, pinnate fronds. The plant has a fibrous root system and produces sporangia on the undersides of its fronds, which are clustered in strobila. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical forests.

Alsophila oligocarpa is valued for its ornamental appearance and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
