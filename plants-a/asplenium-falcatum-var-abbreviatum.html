Asplenium falcatum var. abbreviatum

Asplenium falcatum var. abbreviatum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf maidenhair fern, is a variant of the common maidenhair fern characterized by its more compact and narrower foliage.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia. The plant features delicate, finely divided fronds that are typically bright green and have a feathery appearance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and requires high humidity and well-draining soil.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
