Abelia spathulata f. lucida

Abelia spathulata f. lucida, commonly known as the shining abelia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to China.

It features narrow, elliptical leaves that are glossy and dark green, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale pink flowers in the summer, which attract pollinators like bees and butterflies. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained, acidic soil.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability in garden settings.
