Acaste venusta

Acaste venusta, commonly known as the elegant acasta, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Acanthaceae and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to purple. The plant typically grows as a shrub, reaching heights of up to 1 meter, with lance-shaped leaves that are arranged oppositely along the stems. Its flowers are pollinated primarily by birds, especially sunbirds, due to their bright colors and nectar-rich structure.

Acaste venusta is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
