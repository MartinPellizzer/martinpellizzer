Aerangis fuscata

Aerangis fuscata, commonly known as the dark orchid, is a species of orchid native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dark violet to purple flowers, which are typically clustered in loose racemes. The plant has a slender, erect stem and produces leaves that are ovate to lanceolate in shape. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forested areas.

The flowers emit a subtle fragrance, attracting specific pollinators such as bees and moths.
