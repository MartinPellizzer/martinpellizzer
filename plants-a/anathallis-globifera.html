Anathallis globifera

Anathallis globifera, commonly known as the globeflower orchid, is a species of orchid native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It is characterized by its unique, globular inflorescence that resembles a small ball, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that are clustered together on a single stem. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, typically in tropical rainforests.

Anathallis globifera is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
