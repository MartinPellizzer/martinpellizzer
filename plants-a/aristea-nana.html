Aristea nana

Aristea nana, commonly known as dwarf carpetgrass, is a low-growing perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features dense, mat-forming growth with small, narrow leaves that are typically bluish-green in color. The plant produces clusters of tiny, white or pale blue flowers during the summer months. It is well adapted to rocky and sandy soils and thrives in full sun.

Due to its compact form and ornamental appeal, it is often used in rock gardens and as a ground cover.
