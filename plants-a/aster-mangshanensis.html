Aster mangshanensis

Aster mangshanensis, commonly known as the Mangshan aster, is a rare flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant is known for its vibrant blue to purple flowers, which bloom in the summer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is typically found in mountainous regions.
