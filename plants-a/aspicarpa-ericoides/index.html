Aspicarpa Ericoides

Aspicarpa ericoides is a small, evergreen shrub native to the mountainous regions of South America. It is known for its dense, compact growth and its ability to thrive in harsh, high-altitude environments. The plant produces small, red berries that are both ornamental and edible. Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice for rock gardens and alpine landscapes. Aspicarpa ericoides is also valued for its ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
