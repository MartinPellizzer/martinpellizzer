Asplenium lepturus f. lepturus

Asplenium lepturus f. lepturus, commonly known as the narrow-leaved spleenwort, is a fern belonging to the family Aspleniaceae.

It is a small, epiphytic or lithophytic plant, typically found growing on rocks or tree trunks in moist, shaded environments. The plant has slender, linear leaves that are usually less than 10 centimeters in length. Its fronds are usually dark green and possess a delicate, intricate structure typical of many ferns.

This fern is often found in temperate regions and is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.
