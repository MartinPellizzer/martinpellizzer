Anthurium pluricostatum

Anthurium pluricostatum, commonly known as the multi-costate anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are often deeply veined and glossy in appearance. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-shaped inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, which can range from red to orange in hue. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Anthurium pluricostatum is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and long-lasting blooms.
