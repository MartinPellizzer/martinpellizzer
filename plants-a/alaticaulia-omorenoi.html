Alaticaulia omorenoi

It is characterized by its distinctive, flattened, winged stems that help reduce water loss in its harsh environment. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom during the brief wet season, attracting pollinators such as bees and moths. Its leaves are narrow and covered in a waxy cuticle, further aiding in moisture retention.
