Aspidistra lingyunensis

Aspidistra lingyunensis, commonly known as the Lingyunensis Snake Plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asparagaceae.

It is native to China, specifically the Yunnan province, and is characterized by its upright, dark green leaves with a glossy texture. The plant produces small, clustered white flowers that bloom in the spring. It is a perennial evergreen, capable of thriving in low-light conditions and requiring minimal water.

Its ornamental value and hardiness make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
