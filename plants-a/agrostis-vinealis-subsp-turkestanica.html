Agrostis vinealis subsp. turkestanica

Agrostis vinealis subsp.

turkestanica, commonly known as the Turkestan meadowgrass, is a perennial grass species native to Central Asia. It typically grows in temperate climates and is often found in grasslands, meadows, and along roadsides.

The plant has fine, wiry leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It is well adapted to poor soils and can thrive in both sunny and partially shaded environments.

Its presence is often indicative of disturbed or semi-natural habitats.
