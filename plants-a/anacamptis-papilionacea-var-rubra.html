Anacamptis papilionacea var. rubra

Anacamptis papilionacea var. rubra, commonly known as the red orchid, is a species of orchid native to southern Europe.

It is characterized by its striking red and purple flowers, which bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and meadows. It has a slender, erect stem with a few leaves and produces a raceme of showy, spurred flowers.

This variety is notable for its vibrant coloration and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
