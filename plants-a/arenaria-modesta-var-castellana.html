Arenaria modesta var. castellana

Arenaria modesta var. castellana, commonly known as the Castellana sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It thrives in rocky and sandy soils, often found in coastal and mountainous regions. The plant features small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are typically succulent, adapted to withstand arid conditions.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to stabilize soil in challenging environments.
