Aloe gracilicaulis

Aloe gracilicaulis, commonly known as the slender-stemmed aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features upright, branching stems that are typically slender and covered in a layer of white, powdery wax. Its leaves are fleshy, lance-shaped, and often have a bluish-green hue with a waxy coating. The plant produces yellowish-orange flowers in terminal clusters, which bloom during the summer months.

It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
