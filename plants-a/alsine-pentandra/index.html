Alsine Pentandra

Alsine pentandra is a small, annual herb commonly found in wetland and marshy environments across temperate regions. It is known for its delicate, grass-like appearance and its ability to thrive in nutrient-rich, moist soils. This plant plays an important role in stabilizing soil and supporting local biodiversity. Despite its modest size, it has been the subject of various ecological and botanical studies. Its resilience and adaptability make it a fascinating subject for those interested in plant life.
