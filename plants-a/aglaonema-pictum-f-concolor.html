Aglaonema pictum f. concolor

Aglaonema pictum f. concolor, commonly known as the Variegated Chinese Evergreen, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated foliage featuring shades of green, white, and cream. The plant has broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often marked with intricate patterns. It is a popular indoor plant due to its low maintenance and ability to thrive in low-light conditions.

Botanically, it belongs to the Araceae family and is known for its rhizomatous growth habit.
