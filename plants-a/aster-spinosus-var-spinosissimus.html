Aster spinosus var. spinosissimus

Aster spinosus var. spinosissimus, commonly known as the spiny aster, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the eastern United States.

It features coarse, spiny stems and lance-shaped leaves with rough textures. The plant produces showy, daisy-like flowers in shades of purple and blue during the late summer and early fall. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soils, making it a hardy addition to native gardens.

Its ecological value includes providing nectar for pollinators and habitat for small invertebrates.
