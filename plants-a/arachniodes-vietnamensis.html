Arachniodes vietnamensis

Arachniodes vietnamensis, commonly known as the Vietnamese spider fern, is a striking evergreen fern native to the forests of Vietnam.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its large, intricate fronds that resemble spider webs, hence its common name. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit and produces spores on the underside of its leaves. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in the undergrowth of tropical and subtropical forests.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and valuable species in botanical collections.
