Anthurium striolatum

Anthurium striolatum, commonly known as the zigzag anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive zigzag pattern along the midrib. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-like inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, typically in shades of red, orange, or yellow. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Anthurium striolatum is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and long-lasting blooms.
