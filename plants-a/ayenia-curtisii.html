Ayenia curtisii

Ayenia curtisii, commonly known as the Curtis's ayenia, is a flowering plant native to the southern United States and northern Mexico.

Ayenia curtisii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its drought tolerance and visual interest.
