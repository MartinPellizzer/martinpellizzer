Asplenium neohainanense

Asplenium neohainanense, commonly known as the Neohainan Shield Fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Aspleniaceae and is characterized by its compact, clump-forming growth habit. The fronds are usually lanceolate, with a dark green, leathery texture and a finely serrated margin. It thrives in shaded, moist environments, often found in forest undergrowth.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is occasionally cultivated in horticulture.
