Achyranthes bastosiana

Achyranthes bastosiana, commonly known as the Natal kochia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous annual that typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as roadsides and fields. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are ovate to lanceolate in shape. Its small, greenish-white flowers are clustered in dense, spike-like inflorescences.

It produces small, dry fruits known as schizocarps, which disperse by wind.
