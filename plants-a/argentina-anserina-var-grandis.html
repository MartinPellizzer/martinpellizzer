Argentina anserina var. grandis

Argentina anserina var. grandis, commonly known as the giant water shield, is a aquatic plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its broad, floating leaves that are typically green with a waxy surface. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments.

Its leaves help to stabilize water surfaces and provide habitat for aquatic organisms.
