Angelica saxicola

Angelica saxicola, commonly known as the rock angelica, is a perennial herb native to arctic and alpine regions of Europe and Asia.

It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to cold, high-altitude environments. The plant features tall, slender stems covered in fine hairs and produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in complex umbels. Its leaves are deeply lobed and have a strong, aromatic scent.

Angelica saxicola is valued for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional herbal remedies.
