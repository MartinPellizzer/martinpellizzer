Alloxylon

Alloxylon, commonly known as the silky oak, is a large evergreen tree native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Sterculiaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, heart-shaped leaves and showy yellow flowers. The tree produces woody pods containing flat, edible seeds that are often used in local cuisines. Its bark is thick and deeply furrowed, providing a rugged appearance.

Alloxylon is also valued for its hard, durable wood, which is used in construction and furniture-making.
