Arisaema jethompsonii

Arisaema jethompsonii, commonly known as Thompson's arisaema, is a rare flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive inflorescence consisting of a spadix surrounded by a modified leaf called a spathe. The plant produces a single, showy spathe that is typically green or yellowish in color, with a spadix that bears small, clustered flowers. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a critically endangered species.
