Aretia intermedia

Aretia intermedia, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow in color. It is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and agricultural fields.

Aretia intermedia is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
