Astragalus esferayenicus

Astragalus esferayenicus, commonly known as the Esferayen milkvetch, is a flowering plant species native to the mountainous regions of Iran.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its small, yellowish flowers and compound leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky and arid environments, showcasing adaptations to harsh conditions. Its root system is deep, allowing it to access water from lower soil layers.

Astragalus esferayenicus plays a role in stabilizing soil and supporting local biodiversity in its native habitat.
