Anthurium gruesoi

Anthurium gruesoi, commonly known as the Guerresi anthurium, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically deep green with a waxy texture. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-like inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, often in shades of red, orange, or yellow. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and botanical collections.
