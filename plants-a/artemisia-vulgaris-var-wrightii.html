Artemisia vulgaris var. wrightii

Artemisia vulgaris var. wrightii, commonly known as Wright's mugwort, is a perennial herb native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, silver-gray foliage. The plant produces small, yellow flower heads that are typically clustered in composite inflorescences. It thrives in dry, disturbed soils and is often found in prairies and open woodlands.

Wright's mugwort is known for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally in herbal remedies.
