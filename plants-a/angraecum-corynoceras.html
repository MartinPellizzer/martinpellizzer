Angraecum corynoceras

Angraecum corynoceras, commonly known as the Madagascar star orchid, is a striking epiphytic orchid native to Madagascar.

It features a tall, slender stem with numerous clustered flowers that bloom in a striking white and purple coloration. The plant produces long, narrow leaves and has a unique inflorescence with a central spadix surrounded by a spathe. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Angraecum corynoceras is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in tropical greenhouses.
