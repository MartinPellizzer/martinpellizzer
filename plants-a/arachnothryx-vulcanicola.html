Arachnothryx vulcanicola

Arachnothryx vulcanicola, commonly known as the volcanic spider plant, is a rare flowering plant native to volcanic regions.

It is characterized by its unique, spiky leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant thrives in nutrient-rich, well-drained soils found in volcanic soils. It is adapted to high-altitude environments with low humidity and intense sunlight.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a conservation concern.
