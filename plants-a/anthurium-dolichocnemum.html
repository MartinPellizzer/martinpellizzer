Anthurium dolichocnemum

Anthurium dolichocnemum, commonly known as the long-stemmed anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are often brightly colored, ranging from deep green to variegated patterns. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-shaped inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, which is typical of the Araceae family. It thrives in humid, warm environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Anthurium dolichocnemum is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
