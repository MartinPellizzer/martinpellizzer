Aeranthes longipes

Aeranthes longipes, commonly known as the long-stemmed orchid, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest understories. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish in color, with a distinctive elongated spur.

Aeranthes longipes is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in botanical gardens and greenhouses.
