Anthurium rubrifructum

Anthurium rubrifructum, commonly known as the red-fruited anthurium, is a striking tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped leaves that can range in color from deep green to reddish tones. The plant produces clusters of small, white spadices surrounded by a colorful spathe, which adds to its ornamental appeal. Its fruit, which is red and fleshy, is edible but not commonly cultivated for consumption.

Anthurium rubrifructum thrives in humid, warm environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.
