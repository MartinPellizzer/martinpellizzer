Aristea pusilla subsp. robustior

Aristea pusilla subsp.

robustior, commonly known as the dwarf aristea, is a small, mat-forming succulent native to South Africa. It features dense, woolly foliage that is typically green to bluish-gray in color.

The plant produces small, tubular flowers in shades of yellow to orange, often blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental appeal make it a valuable addition to arid landscape designs.
