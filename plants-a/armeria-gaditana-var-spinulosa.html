Armeria gaditana var. spinulosa

Armeria gaditana var. spinulosa, commonly known as the spinous thrift, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen foliage and clusters of white or pinkish flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are linear and slightly hairy, while the stems are often covered in small, spiny protrusions.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in coastal and dry garden environments.
