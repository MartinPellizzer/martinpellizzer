Aubrieta gracilis var. elongata

Aubrieta gracilis var. elongata, commonly known as the slender aubrieta, is a low-growing perennial plant native to southern Europe.

It features compact, mat-forming growth with clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has small, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine, silvery fuzz, enhancing its ornamental appeal. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

This variety is valued for its hardiness and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
