Aeschynomene deamii

Aeschynomene deamii, commonly known as the yellow burr legume, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers. The plant produces small, woody pods that contain several seeds, which are often used in traditional medicine.

Aeschynomene deamii is also valued for its ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, making it a useful plant in sustainable agriculture.
