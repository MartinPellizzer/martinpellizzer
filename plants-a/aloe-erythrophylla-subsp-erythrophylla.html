Aloe erythrophylla subsp. erythrophylla

Aloe erythrophylla subsp.

erythrophylla, commonly known as the red-leaved aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa. It is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are typically reddish or purple in color, often with a waxy coating.

The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing yellowish-green flowers. It thrives in arid or semi-arid conditions and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated as a decorative plant in gardens and collections.
