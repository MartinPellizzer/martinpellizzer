Abies Sibirica Subsp. Semenovii

Abies sibirica subsp. semenovii is a rare and ancient conifer native to the mountainous regions of central Asia. This subspecies is distinguished by its narrow, needle-like leaves and compact, conical shape. It thrives in cold, high-altitude environments where few plants can survive. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species. Its unique characteristics make it an important subject for botanical research and conservation efforts.
