Aniselytron agrostoides var. formosana

Aniselytron agrostoides var. formosana, commonly known as the Formosan grass, is a perennial grass species native to Taiwan.

It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and forest edges. The plant has slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves with a slightly rough texture. Its inflorescence consists of a spike-like cluster of small, two-lipped flowers.

This variety is notable for its adaptability to various soil types and its role in local ecosystems.
