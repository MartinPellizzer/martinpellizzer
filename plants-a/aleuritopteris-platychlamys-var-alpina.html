Aleuritopteris platychlamys var. alpina

Aleuritopteris platychlamys var. alpina, commonly known as the alpine shield fern, is a variant of the broad-chlamys shield fern.

It is a fern native to mountainous regions of Asia, including Japan and the Himalayas. The plant is characterized by its large, shield-shaped fronds that are typically divided into segments. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in rocky or shaded habitats.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive and ornamental species in horticulture.
