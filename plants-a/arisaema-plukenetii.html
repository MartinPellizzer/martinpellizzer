Arisaema plukenetii

Arisaema plukenetii, commonly known as the Chinese cobra lily, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, hooded spathe and spadix inflorescence. The plant features a short, rhizomatous stem with large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. Its flowers are typically yellow or green, with a central spadix that bears numerous tiny, clustered flowers.

Arisaema plukenetii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
