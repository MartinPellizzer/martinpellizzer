Achillea ptarmica var. tubulosa

Achillea ptarmica var. tubulosa, commonly known as the tubular yarrow, is a perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features finely divided, fern-like leaves and produces clusters of small, tubular yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, sunny habitats such as meadows and rocky slopes. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in herbal medicine.

Its flowering period usually occurs in late spring to early summer.
