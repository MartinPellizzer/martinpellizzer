Abutilon grandiflorum var. grandiflorum

Abutilon grandiflorum var. grandiflorum, commonly known as the Chinese lantern plant, is a species native to China and Southeast Asia.

It is a deciduous shrub characterized by its soft, hairy leaves and showy, lantern-shaped flowers that range in color from orange to red. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It produces small, round fruits that are edible but not commonly consumed by humans.

The plant is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
