Adenophora trachelioides f. puberula

Adenophora trachelioides f. puberula, commonly known as the pubescent bellflower, is a flowering plant belonging to the Campanulaceae family.

It is characterized by its hairy, pubescent leaves and stems, which give it its specific epithet "puberula.". The plant produces bell-shaped, white to pale blue flowers that bloom in late summer. This ornamental plant is valued for its delicate appearance and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
