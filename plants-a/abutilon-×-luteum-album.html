Abutilon × luteum-album

Abutilon × luteum-album, commonly known as the yellow velvet bush-honeysuckle, is a hybrid plant in the Malvaceae family.

It is characterized by its soft, hairy leaves and showy, lantern-shaped yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 6 meters. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden landscapes.
