Anthurium flexuosum

Anthurium flexuosum, commonly known as the curlleaf anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are often adorned with intricate patterns of veins. The plant produces bright red spadices, which are the modified leaves that enclose the actual flowers. Anthurium flexuosum thrives in humid, warm environments with indirect light.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and ornamental purposes.
