Ancistrochilus thailandicus

Ancistrochilus thailandicus, commonly known as the Thai orchid, is a species of orchid native to Thailand.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and strikingly colorful flowers. The plant typically produces small, fragrant blooms in shades of white, yellow, and pink, often with intricate patterns on the petals. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forested areas with well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
