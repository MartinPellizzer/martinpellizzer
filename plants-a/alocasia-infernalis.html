Alocasia infernalis

Alocasia infernalis, commonly known as the Infernal Alocasia, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, dark green, arrow-shaped leaves that often have a velvety texture. The plant produces small, white, spadix-like flowers surrounded by a colorful spathe, typical of the Araceae family. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, rich soil.

Due to its unique appearance, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and botanical collections.
