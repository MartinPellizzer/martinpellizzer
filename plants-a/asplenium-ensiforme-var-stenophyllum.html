Asplenium ensiforme var. stenophyllum

Asplenium ensiforme var. stenophyllum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf maidenhair fern, is a delicate fern species native to parts of Asia.

It features finely divided, lance-shaped fronds that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant has a compact growth habit, making it suitable for small spaces or as a houseplant. It prefers shady, humid environments and well-draining soil.

Its unique appearance and ease of care make it a popular choice among fern enthusiasts.
