Avena hexantha

Avena hexantha, commonly known as the slender oat, is a perennial grass species native to South America.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. Avena hexantha is adapted to a variety of soil types and can thrive in both moist and dry conditions.

It is often found in grasslands and along stream banks, contributing to soil stabilization and biodiversity.
