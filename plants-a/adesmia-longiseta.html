Adesmia longiseta

Adesmia longiseta, commonly known as the long-hair adesmia, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and small, trifoliate leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences, often resembling a raceme. Its fruit is a legume pod that contains several small, rounded seeds.

Adesmia longiseta is typically found in arid or semi-arid regions, where it thrives in well-drained soils.
