Ardisia dentata

Ardisia dentata, commonly known as the toothed evergreen or Chinese evergreen, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces small, white flowers followed by bright red berries, which are a key feature in its ornamental value. Ardisia dentata is valued for its aesthetic appeal and ability to tolerate low light conditions.
