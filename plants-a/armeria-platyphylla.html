Armeria platyphylla

Armeria platyphylla, commonly known as the flat-leaved thrift, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically forms dense mats of foliage. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of small, white or pink flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy addition to rock gardens.

Its botanical characteristics make it suitable for coastal and arid landscapes.
