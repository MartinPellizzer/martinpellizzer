Angelica saxicola var. saxicola

Angelica saxicola var. saxicola, commonly known as the rock angelica, is a perennial herb native to rocky and alpine environments in Europe and Asia.

It typically grows in well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. The plant features tall, slender stems with large, compound leaves that are deeply lobed and aromatic. Its flowers are small, greenish-yellow, and arranged in complex umbels, typical of theApiaceae family.

It is valued for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional herbal remedies.
