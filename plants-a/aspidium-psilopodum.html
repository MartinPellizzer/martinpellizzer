Aspidium psilopodum

Aspidium psilopodum, commonly known as the slender fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, feather-like fronds that are typically light green and covered in fine hairs. This plant prefers moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its rhizomes are creeping and help in anchoring the plant while absorbing nutrients from the soil.

Aspidium psilopodum plays a role in maintaining the ecological balance of its habitat by providing shelter and food for various microorganisms and small fauna.
