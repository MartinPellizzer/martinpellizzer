Anguria multiflora

Anguria multiflora, commonly known as the multiflora melon, is a vine-like plant native to tropical regions.

It produces large, green, hairy fruits that are typically harvested when they turn yellow or orange. The plant has palmately lobed leaves and can grow up to several meters in length. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

The fruit is often used in traditional medicine and as a source of edible seeds.
