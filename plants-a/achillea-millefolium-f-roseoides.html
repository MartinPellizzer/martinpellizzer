Achillea millefolium f. roseoides

Achillea millefolium f. roseoides, commonly known as the rose-like yarrow, is a horticultural form of the common yarrow.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant pink to rose-purple flower heads. The plant features finely divided, feathery leaves that give it a delicate appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
