Areca henrici

Areca henrici, commonly known as the Chinese areca palm, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and feather-like leaves that give it a distinctive, elegant appearance. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences that contain both male and female flowers, typical of many palm species. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine and as a source of fiber.

Areca henrici thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
