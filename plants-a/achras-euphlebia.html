Achras euphlebia

Achras euphlebia, commonly known as the desert date tree, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of Africa and the Arabian Peninsula.

The plant produces yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by fruit that resembles a large, spiky coconut.
