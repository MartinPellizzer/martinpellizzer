Aglaonema schottianum var. winkleri

Aglaonema schottianum var. winkleri, commonly known as the Winkler's aglaonema, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features striking, variegated leaves with a mix of green, white, and red hues, which are often speckled or streaked. The plant is known for its compact growth habit and is widely cultivated as a decorative houseplant. It prefers indirect light and well-draining soil, making it suitable for indoor environments.

Its foliage remains vibrant throughout the year, adding a touch of color to any space.
