Ascogrammitis angustipes

Ascogrammitis angustipes, commonly known as the narrow-leaf chain fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, arching fronds that are typically less than 30 centimeters in length. Ascogrammitis angustipes is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in indoor settings.
