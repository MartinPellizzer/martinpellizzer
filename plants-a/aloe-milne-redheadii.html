Aloe milne-redheadii

Aloe milne-redheadii, commonly known as the red-headed aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette of thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with red or orange markings. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing yellow to orange flowers. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its ornamental value and hardy nature, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
