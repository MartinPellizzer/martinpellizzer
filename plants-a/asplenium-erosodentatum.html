Asplenium erosodentatum

Asplenium erosodentatum, commonly known as the narrow-crested maidenhair fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Aspleniaceae and is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky crevices. Its leaves are usually dark green and have a soft, feathery appearance.

Asplenium erosodentatum is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
