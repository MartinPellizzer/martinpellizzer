Aloe chinensis

Aloe chinensis, commonly known as Chinese aloe, is a succulent plant native to China and parts of Southeast Asia.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a waxy coating, which helps it retain moisture in arid environments. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow or orange flowers, which bloom in the spring or early summer. Its leaves contain a gel-like substance that has been used traditionally for its soothing and healing properties.

Aloe chinensis is well adapted to grow in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a hardy plant suitable for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.
