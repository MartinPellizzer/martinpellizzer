Aristolochia chapmaniana

Aristolochia chapmaniana, commonly known as Chapman's Dutchman's pipe, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its unique, pipe-shaped flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color. The plant produces heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and emits a mild, sweet scent. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

However, it contains toxic compounds and should be handled with care.
