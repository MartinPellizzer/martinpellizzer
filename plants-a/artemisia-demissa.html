Artemisia demissa

Artemisia demissa, commonly known as silverleaf sage, is a perennial herb native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late summer. Artemisia demissa thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in dry, rocky habitats.

It is valued for its aromatic properties and historical use in traditional medicine.
