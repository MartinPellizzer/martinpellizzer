Asterochaete arundinacea

Asterochaete arundinacea, commonly known as the bamboo grass or bamboo reed, is a member of the Poaceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant is native to tropical and subtropical regions, particularly in Southeast Asia. It is often used in traditional landscaping and as a decorative plant due to its aesthetic appeal.

Its growth habit makes it suitable for erosion control and as a living fence.
