Alchemilla arvensis var. eucornucopioides

Alchemilla arvensis var. eucornucopioides, commonly known as the narrow-leaved lady's mantle, is a variant of the lady's mantle plant native to Europe.

It is a low-growing perennial that features dense, velvety foliage with a silver-green hue. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers during the summer months. Its leaves are typically narrow and lance-shaped, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

This variety is often found in moist, shaded habitats such as woodland edges and stream banks.
