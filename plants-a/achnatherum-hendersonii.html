Achnatherum hendersonii

Achnatherum hendersonii, commonly known as Henderson's needlegrass, is a perennial grass species native to the western United States.

It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as sagebrush steppe and grasslands. The plant has slender, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It is adapted to arid conditions and has deep root systems to access water.

Its botanical features make it an important component of native plant communities in its range.
