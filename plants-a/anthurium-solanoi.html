Anthurium solanoi

Anthurium solanoi, commonly known as the Solano anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-shaped flowers surrounded by a colorful spathe, typically in red or orange hues. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium solanoi is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and long-lasting blooms.
