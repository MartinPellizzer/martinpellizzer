Arenaria juressi

Arenaria juressi, commonly known as the Jussie's sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It typically thrives in rocky and arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. The plant features small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink, blooming in early summer. Its leaves are linear and succulent, helping to store moisture in its native habitat.

Arenaria juressi is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in stabilizing soil in sandy and rocky terrains.
