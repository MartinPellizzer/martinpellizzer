Agathosma mundii

Agathosma mundii, commonly known as the Cape geranium, is a small, evergreen shrub native to the Western Cape of South Africa.

It is characterized by its aromatic, needle-like leaves and small, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to purple. The plant thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and requires full sun to partial shade. It is often used in rock gardens and as a cut flower due to its ornamental value.

Agathosma mundii is also valued for its essential oils, which are used in aromatherapy and traditional medicine.
