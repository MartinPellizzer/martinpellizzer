Agathosma juncea

Agathosma juncea, commonly known as Cape aster, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal regions. It has aromatic leaves that are used in traditional medicine and aromatherapy.

Its resilience to harsh climates makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
