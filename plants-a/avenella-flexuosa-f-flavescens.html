Avenella flexuosa f. flavescens

Avenella flexuosa f. flavescens, commonly known as the yellow-flowered avenella, is a flowering plant belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its slender, arching stems and small, clustered flowers. The flowers are typically yellow or orange, with five petals, and bloom in late spring to early summer. The leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a hairy texture, often with a reddish or purplish hue.

This variety is native to parts of Europe and is cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.
