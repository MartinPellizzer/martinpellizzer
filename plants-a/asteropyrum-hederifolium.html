Asteropyrum hederifolium

Asteropyrum hederifolium, commonly known as the ivy-leaved aster, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, ivy-like leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, clustered yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, it thrives in moist, shaded environments.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and ecological restoration projects.
