Aeonium × Riosjordanae

Aeonium × riosjordanae is a striking hybrid succulent known for its vibrant, colorful rosettes. Native to the Canary Islands, it thrives in arid and rocky environments. This plant is prized for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements. Its foliage often displays shades of pink, red, and purple, making it a popular choice for rock gardens. Despite its beauty, it requires careful care to avoid overwatering and root rot.
