Arthrostylis

Arthrostylis, commonly known as the grassy orchid, is a genus of flowering plants in the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

These plants are native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia, Australia, and the Pacific Islands. Arthrostylis species typically have slender, grass-like leaves and produce small, delicate flowers in shades of white, yellow, or green. They are often found in wet or marshy habitats, thriving in humid environments.
