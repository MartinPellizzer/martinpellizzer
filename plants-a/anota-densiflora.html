Anota densiflora

Anota densiflora, commonly known as the dense-flowered anota, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to certain regions of South America.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories or along stream banks. The plant features slender, upright stems covered in dense clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Anota densiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
