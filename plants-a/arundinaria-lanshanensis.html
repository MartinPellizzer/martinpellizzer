Arundinaria lanshanensis

Arundinaria lanshanensis, commonly known as the Lan Shan bamboo, is a species of bamboo native to China.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender stems that can reach heights of up to 10 meters. The plant is known for its rapid growth and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and structural strength. Its leaves are long and narrow, with a smooth texture, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers.

Arundinaria lanshanensis is adapted to a variety of soil types and is relatively drought-tolerant once established.
