Astroloba foliolosa

Astroloba foliolosa, commonly known as the star leafed bulbous plant, is a succulent native to southern Africa.

It features a bulbous base with thick, fleshy leaves that are often ribbed and covered in a waxy coating. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. Its succulent nature allows it to store water, making it drought-tolerant.

It thrives in well-draining soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and arid landscapes.
