Aerides pedunculata

Aerides pedunculata, commonly known as the orchid with clustered flowers, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Thailand and Malaysia. The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. Its pseudobulbs are cylindrical, and it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Aerides pedunculata is popular among orchid growers for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
