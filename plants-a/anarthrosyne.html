Anarthrosyne

Anarthrosyne, commonly known as the leafless orchid, is a rare and unique flowering plant belonging to the orchid family.

It is characterized by its lack of leaves, which sets it apart from most other orchid species. Instead of leaves, it has a short stem that supports its single, showy flower. The plant is typically found in specific tropical and subtropical regions where it thrives in shaded, moist environments.

Due to its specialized habitat and limited distribution, the Anarthrosyne is considered a delicate and endangered species.
