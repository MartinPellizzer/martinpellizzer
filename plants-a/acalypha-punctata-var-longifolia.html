Acalypha punctata var. longifolia

Acalypha punctata var. longifolia, commonly known as the velvetleaf or red leaf acalypha, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety foliage that ranges from deep green to reddish hues. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in warm, humid climates and prefers well-drained soil.

Acalypha punctata var. longifolia is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive leaves and ease of care.
