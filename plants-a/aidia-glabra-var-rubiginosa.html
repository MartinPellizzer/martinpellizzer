Aidia glabra var. rubiginosa

Aidia glabra var. rubiginosa, commonly known as the red-stemmed aidia, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, upright stems that are often reddish in color, especially when mature. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale pink, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, giving them a slightly rough texture.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
