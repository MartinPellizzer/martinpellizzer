Albuca nelsonii

Albuca nelsonii, commonly known as the Nelson's albuca, is a bulbous plant native to southern Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of white to pale yellow flowers. The plant is adapted to arid environments, with a dormant period during the dry season. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water once established.

Albuca nelsonii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ease of care.
