Anotis nummulariiformis

Anotis nummulariiformis, commonly known as the silver dollar plant, is a succulent species native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, round leaves that are typically silver or gray in color, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its compact growth habit makes it an ideal choice for rock gardens or as a container plant.
