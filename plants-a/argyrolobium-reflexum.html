Argyrolobium reflexum

Argyrolobium reflexum, commonly known as the silverlobe burr vetch.

It is a flowering plant native to southern Africa, characterized by its silver-gray foliage and distinctive burr-like seed pods. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring, and it thrives in arid and semi-arid environments. It has a low-growing habit with creeping stems and is often found in open grasslands and rocky areas.

The species is adapted to drought conditions, making it a resilient plant in harsh climates.
