Alocasia augustiana

Alocasia augustiana, commonly known as the velvet leaf alocasia, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are dark green with a velvety texture. The plant belongs to the Araceae family, which includes other ornamental plants like calla lilies. Alocasia augustiana thrives in humid, shady environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and as a decorative plant in tropical landscapes.
