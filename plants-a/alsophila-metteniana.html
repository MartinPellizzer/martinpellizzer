Alsophila metteniana

Alsophila metteniana, commonly known as the Metteniana tree fern, is a species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and a crown of feathery fronds that can reach significant lengths. The plant belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is known for its slow growth rate and long lifespan. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine, and it plays a role in stabilizing soil in its native habitats.

The fronds are typically dark green and have a leathery texture, contributing to its ornamental value.
