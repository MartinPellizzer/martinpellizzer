Aster shortii var. camptosorus

Aster shortii var. camptosorus, commonly known as the bent-stem aster, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the western United States.

It features slender, hairy stems that often curve downward, giving it its distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of blue to purple, typically blooming in late summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its overall rugged appearance.

This species is adapted to rocky and dry habitats, showcasing its resilience in arid environments.
