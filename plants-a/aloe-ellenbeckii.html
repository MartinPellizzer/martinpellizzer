Aloe ellenbeckii

Aloe ellenbeckii, commonly known as the Cape aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asphodelaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are thick and fleshy. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow to orange flowers during the summer months. Its leaves contain a gel-like substance that is used in traditional medicine and skincare products.

Aloe ellenbeckii is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water to survive.
