Agrostis karsensis

Agrostis karsensis, commonly known as Kars bentgrass, is a low-growing perennial grass native to Turkey.

It typically forms dense, mat-like growths and thrives in moist, well-drained soils. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences. It is often found in mountainous regions and is adapted to cool, temperate climates.

Its botanical characteristics make it a subject of interest in ecological and taxonomic studies.
