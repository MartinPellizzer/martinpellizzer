Aloe albiflora

Aloe albiflora, commonly known as the white-flowered aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, with a waxy coating to store water. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers during the spring and summer seasons. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect sunlight.

Aloe albiflora is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
