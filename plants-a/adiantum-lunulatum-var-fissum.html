Adiantum lunulatum var. fissum

Adiantum lunulatum var. fissum, commonly known as the moonlight fern, is a delicate and elegant fern species native to parts of Asia.

It features finely divided, arching fronds that are typically light green with a silvery-blue hue on the underside. The plant thrives in shady, moist environments and is often found growing on rocks or in shaded forest floors. Its fronds are characterized by their narrow, lance-shaped segments and a distinctive, almost translucent appearance.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
