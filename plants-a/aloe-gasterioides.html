Aloe gasterioides

Aloe gasterioides, commonly known as the horned aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features striking, spiky, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, with a waxy coating to store water. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Aloe gasterioides is also valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
