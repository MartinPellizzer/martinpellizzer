Angraecum distichum var. grandifolium

Angraecum distichum var. grandifolium, commonly known as the giant angraecum, is a striking orchid native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its large, leathery leaves that can grow up to 30 inches in length, providing a dramatic visual impact. The plant produces fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom at night, attracting specific moths for pollination. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Due to its rarity and specific care requirements, it is a prized specimen among orchid enthusiasts.
