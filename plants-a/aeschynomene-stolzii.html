Aeschynomene stolzii

Aeschynomene stolzii, commonly known as yellow burr gourd, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its hairy stems and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers followed by woody, spiny fruits that contain seeds. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in semi-arid environments.

Aeschynomene stolzii is also valued for its potential in soil stabilization and as a forage crop.
