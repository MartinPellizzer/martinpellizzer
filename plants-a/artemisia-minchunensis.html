Artemisia minchunensis

Artemisia minchunensis, commonly known as the Minchun wormwood, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, silver-gray foliage. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in composite heads, typical of the daisy family. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or dry habitats.

Due to its medicinal properties, it has been used in traditional Chinese medicine for various health purposes.
