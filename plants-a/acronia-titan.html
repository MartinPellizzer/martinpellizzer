Acronia titan

Acronia titan, commonly known as the giant flame vine, is a striking member of the Apocynaceae family, native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

This plant is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from fiery red to deep orange, often resembling a flame. Its leaves are broad and glossy, providing a lush backdrop to its vibrant blooms. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Acronia titan is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
