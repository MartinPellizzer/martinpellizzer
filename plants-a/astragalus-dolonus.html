Astragalus dolonus

Astragalus dolonus, commonly known as the narrowleaf milkvetch, is a perennial flowering plant native to the western United States and parts of Canada.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and pea-like flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid environments. Astragalus dolonus has small, cylindrical seed pods that release tiny seeds when mature.

It plays a role in stabilizing soil and supporting local biodiversity in its native range.
