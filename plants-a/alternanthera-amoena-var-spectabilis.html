Alternanthera amoena var. spectabilis

Alternanthera amoena var. spectabilis, commonly known as the red-leaf alternanthera, is a striking aquatic plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red or pink foliage, which becomes more intense under strong light conditions. The plant typically grows in shallow water and thrives in warm, tropical climates. It has small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in summer.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for aquariums and water gardens.
