Arctotis humilis

Arctotis humilis, commonly known as the dwarf arctotis, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial shrub that typically reaches a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant produces daisy-like flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, which bloom throughout the growing season. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its compact size and vibrant blooms make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
