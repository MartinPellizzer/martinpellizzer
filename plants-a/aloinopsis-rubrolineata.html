Aloinopsis rubrolineata

Aloinopsis rubrolineata, commonly known as the red-line aloinopsis, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, cylindrical stems covered in sparse, blue-gray leaves that are often marked with reddish or purplish stripes. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
