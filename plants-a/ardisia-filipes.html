Ardisia filipes

Ardisia filipes, commonly known as the slender flame tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, elliptical leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers followed by bright red berries that are edible for humans but toxic to livestock. Ardisia filipes is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in shaded environments.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for garden landscapes in warm climates.
