Apochloa Steyermarkii

Apochloa steyermarkii is a rare and lesser-known grass species native to the southern United States. It thrives in wetland environments and is often found in marshy areas along the Gulf Coast. This plant is characterized by its slender stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a vulnerable species by conservationists. Studying Apochloa steyermarkii provides valuable insight into the ecological health of its habitat.
