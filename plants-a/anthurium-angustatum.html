Anthurium angustatum

Anthurium angustatum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive spathe and spadix inflorescence. The plant features elongated, narrow leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. It thrives in humid, warm environments and requires indirect sunlight for optimal growth.

Anthurium angustatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated as a houseplant.
