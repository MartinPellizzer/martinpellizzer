Asterotricha

Asterotricha, commonly known as the silky aster, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, hairy stems and leaves, which give it a soft, velvety appearance. The flowers are typically small and clustered in composite heads, resembling daisies. Native to regions of South America, Asterotricha species are often found in grasslands and open areas.

These plants are valued for their ornamental appeal and are sometimes cultivated in gardens for their aesthetic qualities.
