Alocasia acuta

Alocasia acuta, commonly known as the sharp-leaf alocasia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, arrow-shaped leaves that are often a deep green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. Alocasia acuta thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

It is a popular ornamental plant due to its unique foliage and ability to add a dramatic visual element to indoor or outdoor gardens.
