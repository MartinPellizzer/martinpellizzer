Achyranthes mangarevica

Achyranthes mangarevica, commonly known as the slender knotweed, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters and features upright stems that are often hairy. The leaves are lance-shaped, with a rough texture and a prominent midrib. Its small, greenish-white flowers are clustered in dense, terminal spikes, and it produces small, dry fruits containing single seeds.

This plant is often found in moist, disturbed soils and is adapted to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
