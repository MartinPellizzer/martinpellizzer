Anemia trichorhiza var. paraguariensis

Anemia trichorhiza var. paraguariensis, commonly known as mate, is a South American plant native to the Paraguay and Brazil regions.

It belongs to the genus Anemia in the family Polygonaceae, characterized by its unique leaf structure and growth habits. The plant is typically shrub-like, with trifoliate leaves and produces small, greenish flowers. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in humid tropical forests.
