Aulomyrcia pauciflora

Aulomyrcia pauciflora, commonly known as the few-flowered aulomyrcia, is a member of the Myrtaceae family.

It is a shrub or small tree native to Brazil, typically found in moist tropical forests. The plant is characterized by its small, clustered flowers that are usually white or pale pink in color. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a glossy surface.
