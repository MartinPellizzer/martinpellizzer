Acalypha schiedeana var. purpusiana

Acalypha schiedeana var. purpusiana, commonly known as the Mexican copperleaf, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and Central America.

It is characterized by its striking, bright red bracts that surround small yellow flowers, creating a visually appealing display.
