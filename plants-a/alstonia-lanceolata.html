Alstonia lanceolata

Alstonia lanceolata, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its large, lance-shaped leaves and striking clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant produces long, slender pods containing flat, winged seeds. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular ornamental species.

Its rapid growth and showy appearance make it a valuable addition to gardens and landscapes.
