Astragalus uttaranchalensis

Astragalus uttaranchalensis, commonly known as the Uttarakhand milkvetch, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is native to the Himalayan region, specifically found in the state of Uttarakhand in India. The plant is characterized by its slender stems, trifoliate leaves, and small, yellowish flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in rocky and alpine environments, often growing at high altitudes.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
