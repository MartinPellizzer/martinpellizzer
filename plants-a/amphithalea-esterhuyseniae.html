Amphithalea esterhuyseniae

Amphithalea esterhuyseniae, commonly known as the Esterhuysenia starfish, is a unique member of the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers that resemble the arms of a starfish, giving it its common name. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in a clustered inflorescence. It is native to South Africa and thrives in dry, sandy environments.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive and visually appealing addition to gardens and botanical collections.
