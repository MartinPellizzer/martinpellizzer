Adiantum elegantulum

Adiantum elegantulum, commonly known as the silver lace fern, is a delicate fern native to Southeast Asia.

It features finely divided, lacy fronds that give it a distinctive, ornamental appearance. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires consistent moisture and well-draining soil. Its fronds are typically a deep green, with a silvery sheen on the underside.

Adiantum elegantulum is prized for its elegant foliage and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.
