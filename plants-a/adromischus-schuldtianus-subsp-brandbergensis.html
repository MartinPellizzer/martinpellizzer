Adromischus schuldtianus subsp. brandbergensis

Adromischus schuldtianus subsp.

brandbergensis, commonly known as the Brandberg ice plant, is a succulent plant native to South Africa. It is characterized by its fleshy, rounded leaves that are often covered in a fine, white powder, which helps reduce water loss.

The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or purple during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
