Aconitum semigaleatum var. ichangense

Aconitum semigaleatum var. ichangense, commonly known as the Ichang monkshood, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its striking, hood-shaped blue to purple flowers. The plant features compound leaves with serrated edges and a robust root system. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Due to its toxicity, it should be handled with care.
