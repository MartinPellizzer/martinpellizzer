Arenaria conimbricensis

Arenaria conimbricensis, commonly known as the Azores sandwort, is a flowering plant native to the Azores Islands.

It is a low-growing perennial species that thrives in sandy or rocky soils. The plant produces small, white or pinkish flowers during the spring and summer months. It has basal leaves that are typically linear or lance-shaped.

This species is adapted to coastal and maritime environments, showing resilience to salt spray and wind.
