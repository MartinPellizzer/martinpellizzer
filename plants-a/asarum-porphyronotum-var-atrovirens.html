Asarum porphyronotum var. atrovirens

Asarum porphyronotum var. atrovirens, commonly known as the blackleaf asarum, is a perennial herb native to eastern China.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a velvety texture and prominent veins. The plant produces small, reddish-brown flowers that are clustered in a spire-like structure. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical characteristics make it a notable species in the Aristolochiaceae family.
