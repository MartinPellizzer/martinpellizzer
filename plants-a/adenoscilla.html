Adenoscilla

Adenoscilla, commonly known as the ghost flower, is a rare and elusive plant native to the high altitudes of southern Africa.

The plant has a unique structure with a single, slender stem and a cluster of tiny, lantern-like blooms that emit a faint bioluminescent glow. Adenoscilla thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in rocky or alpine regions.
