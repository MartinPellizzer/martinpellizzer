Aulomyrcia tetramera

Aulomyrcia tetramera, commonly known as the four-parted myrcia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its small, aromatic leaves and clusters of white or pale yellow flowers. It produces small, woody fruits that contain several seeds.

Aulomyrcia tetramera is often found in humid, shaded environments within tropical forests.
