Anthurium wattii

Anthurium wattii, commonly known as the Watt's anthurium, is a species of flowering plant native to the tropical rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces striking spadices, which are modified leaves that enclose the true flowers, often in bright red or orange hues. Anthurium wattii thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its unique foliage and colorful inflorescences make it a popular choice for indoor gardening and botanical collections.
