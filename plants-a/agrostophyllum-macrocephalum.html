Agrostophyllum macrocephalum

Agrostophyllum macrocephalum, commonly known as the large-headed agrostophyllum, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces showy yellow to orange composite flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands.

Agrostophyllum macrocephalum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
