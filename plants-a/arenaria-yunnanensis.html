Arenaria yunnanensis

Arenaria yunnanensis, commonly known as Yunnan sandwort, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Yunnan Province in China.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit. The plant features small, white or pink flowers that bloom in early summer, typically arranged in clusters. Its leaves are usually narrow and fleshy, adapted to withstand the arid and rocky conditions of its natural habitat.

Arenaria yunnanensis is often found in alpine meadows and is valued for its ornamental appeal in garden settings.
