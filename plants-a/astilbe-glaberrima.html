Astilbe glaberrima

Astilbe glaberrima, commonly known as the smooth astilbe, is a herbaceous perennial native to eastern North America.

It features delicate, feathery plumes of pink or white flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has lance-shaped, dark green leaves that are smooth and glossy. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland settings.

Astilbe glaberrima is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to add texture and color to shaded gardens.
