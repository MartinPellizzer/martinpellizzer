Alyssum elegans

Alyssum elegans, commonly known as slender alyssum, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with dense clusters of small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. The plant features silver-gray foliage with soft, fuzzy leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Alyssum elegans thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its compact size and long blooming period make it a valuable addition to both ornamental and ecological landscapes.
