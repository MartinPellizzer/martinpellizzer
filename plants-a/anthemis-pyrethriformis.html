Anthemis pyrethriformis

Anthemis pyrethriformis, commonly known as the pyrethrum-like anthemis, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its flowers are small and yellow, often arranged in a flat, disc-like structure.

It is known for its aromatic properties and has been used in traditional medicine.
