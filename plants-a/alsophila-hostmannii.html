Alsophila hostmannii

Alsophila hostmannii, commonly known as the hostmann's tree fern, is a member of the Cyatheaceae family and is native to New Zealand.

It is a tall, slender tree fern characterized by its pinnate fronds and a slender, unbranched trunk. The plant has a slow growth rate and can live for several decades, reaching heights of up to 10 meters. Its fronds are typically dark green and have a smooth, waxy texture.

Alsophila hostmannii thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forested areas.
