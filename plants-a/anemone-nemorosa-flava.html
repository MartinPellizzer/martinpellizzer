Anemone nemorosa flava

Anemone nemorosa flava, commonly known as the yellow wood anemone, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe.

It produces delicate, cup-shaped flowers in shades of yellow to white, typically blooming in early spring. The plant has palmate, lobed leaves that are hairy and appear before the flowers. It thrives in woodland habitats and prefers moist, well-drained soil.

Anemone nemorosa flava is often found in shaded areas and is a characteristic species of temperate forests.
