Amaranthus tricolor var. melancholicus

Amaranthus tricolor var. melancholicus, commonly known as the purple leaf panicum, is a striking ornamental plant characterized by its vibrant foliage.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is native to the southeastern United States. The plant features feathery, grass-like leaves that range in color from deep purple to bronze, creating a dramatic visual effect. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Its ability to attract birds and butterflies adds ecological value to the environment.
