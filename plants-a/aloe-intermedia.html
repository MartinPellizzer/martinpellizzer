Aloe intermedia

Aloe intermedia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of narrow, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces yellow flowers on slender stems, which bloom during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil.

Aloe intermedia is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and low maintenance requirements.
