Acer campestre var. josifovicii

Acer campestre var. josifovicii, commonly known as the Josifovicii hedge maple, is a variety of the common maple.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and is often used as a hedge or screen plant. The leaves are typically oval to ovate, with a serrated margin and a bronze to dark green coloration. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers in early spring, followed by red or orange samaras.

This variety is valued for its hardiness and adaptability to various soil types and light conditions.
