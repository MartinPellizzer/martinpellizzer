Anomospermum chloranthum subsp. pacificum

Anomospermum chloranthum subsp.

pacificum, commonly known as the Pacific anomospermum, is a shrub native to the coastal regions of California. It is characterized by its small, greenish-yellow flowers and narrow, lance-shaped leaves.

The plant is adapted to sandy, well-drained soils and is often found in dune environments. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain the seeds.

This subspecies is of interest to botanists due to its unique ecological adaptations and limited distribution.
