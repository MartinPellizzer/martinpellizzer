Asplenium nitidum

Asplenium nitidum, commonly known as the shining spleenwort, is a small, evergreen fern belonging to the family Aspleniaceae.

It is characterized by its narrow, elongated leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant has a delicate appearance with a light green to bluish-green foliage that often has a waxy coating. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found growing on rocks or soil in temperate regions.

Asplenium nitidum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
