Albizia polyantha

Albizia polyantha, commonly known as the pink albizia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its compound leaves, which are usually pinnate and have a delicate, feathery appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or white, arranged in racemes. Albizia polyantha is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.

It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
