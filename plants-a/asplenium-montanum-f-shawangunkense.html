Asplenium montanum f. shawangunkense

Asplenium montanum f. shawangunkense, commonly known as the Shawangunk spleenwort, is a fern native to the eastern United States.

It is a small, epiphytic fern that typically grows on the bark of trees in humid, shaded environments. The plant has lance-shaped, serrated leaves that are dark green and velvety to the touch. It belongs to the Aspleniaceae family and is characterized by its compact, clustered growth habit.

This fern is often found in rocky outcrops and forested areas, where it thrives in moist, nutrient-rich conditions.
