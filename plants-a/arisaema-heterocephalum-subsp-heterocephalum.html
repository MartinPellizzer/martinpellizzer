Arisaema heterocephalum subsp. heterocephalum

Arisaema heterocephalum subsp.

heterocephalum, commonly known as the heterocephalum arisaema, is a species of flowering plant in the Araceae family. It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Vietnam and Thailand.

The plant is characterized by its distinctive, spadix-like inflorescence surrounded by a colorful spathe. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
