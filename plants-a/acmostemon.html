Acmostemon

Acmostemon, commonly known as the common thyme.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region. The plant features small, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or white, arranged in dense clusters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in fine hairs, giving them a slightly fuzzy texture.

Acmostemon is often used in culinary applications and traditional medicine due to its aromatic properties.
