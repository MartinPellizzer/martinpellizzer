Ageratina schaffneri

Ageratina schaffneri, commonly known as Schaffner's blueweed, is a flowering plant native to Central America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The small, blue to purple flowers bloom in clusters, making it an attractive plant for ecological gardens.
