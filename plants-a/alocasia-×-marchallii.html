Alocasia × marchallii

Alocasia × marchallii, commonly known as the Congo giant, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Araceae family.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length, often with a deep green color and a glossy texture. The plant produces striking white spathes with bright yellow spadices, creating an eye-catching floral display. Native to tropical regions of Africa, it thrives in humid, warm environments with high light exposure.

Alocasia × marchallii is a popular ornamental plant due to its dramatic foliage and ease of care in suitable conditions.
