Anisifolium alatum

Anisifolium alatum, commonly known as the winged fern, is a unique plant characterized by its distinctive foliage.

Its leaves are bipinnately compound, with each leaflet having a wing-like structure that gives it its common name. The plant exhibits a creeping growth habit, often forming dense mats on the forest floor. It thrives in shaded, moist environments, typically found in temperate rainforests.

The botanical features of Anisifolium alatum make it a valuable component of understory vegetation in its native habitat.
