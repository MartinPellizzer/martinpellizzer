Apodytes dimidiata var. acutifolia

Apodytes dimidiata var. acutifolia, commonly known as the Cape daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and showy yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping. It is a member of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental value make it a popular choice for gardeners in arid climates.
