Andrographis lineata

Andrographis lineata, commonly known as the linearandrographis, is a perennial herb native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands.

Andrographis lineata is valued for its medicinal properties, particularly in traditional herbal medicine.
