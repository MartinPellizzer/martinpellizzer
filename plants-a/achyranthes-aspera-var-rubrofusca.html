Achyranthes aspera var. rubrofusca

Achyranthes aspera var. rubrofusca, commonly known as red-hair sida, is a shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its erect stems and small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has alternate, ovate leaves with hairy surfaces, contributing to its rough texture. Its root system is fibrous, and it thrives in well-drained soils.

It is often found in disturbed areas and is used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
