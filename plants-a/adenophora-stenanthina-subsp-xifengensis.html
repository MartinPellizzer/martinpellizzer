Adenophora stenanthina subsp. xifengensis

Adenophora stenanthina subsp.

xifengensis, commonly known as the Xifeng bellflower, is a perennial herb native to China. It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped, white to pale blue flowers.

The plant typically grows in mountainous regions with well-drained soil and partial shade. Its leaves are lanceolate and covered with fine hairs, while its stems are erect and slightly woody.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
