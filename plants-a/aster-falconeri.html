Aster falconeri

Aster falconeri, commonly known as the Falconeri's aster, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers that bloom in the summer months. The plant typically grows in rocky, arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its leaves are narrow and often covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.

Aster falconeri is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in specialized gardens.
