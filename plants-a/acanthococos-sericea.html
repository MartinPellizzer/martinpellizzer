Acanthococos sericea

Acanthococos sericea, commonly known as the silky acanthococos, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Acanthocococaceae and is characterized by its soft, silvery foliage. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its unique appearance and habitat make it an interesting subject for botanical study.
