Aethionema schistosum

Aethionema schistosum, commonly known as the rockcress, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically reaches a height of about 10-20 centimeters. The plant features basal leaves that are lance-shaped and covered in a fine, powdery coating. Its flowers are small, yellow, and appear in clusters, blooming in early spring.

Aethionema schistosum is well adapted to rocky and dry environments, making it a hardy plant suitable for rock gardens.
