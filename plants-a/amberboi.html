Amberboi

Amberboi, commonly known as the golden root, is a perennial herb native to the temperate regions of North America.

It features a fibrous root system that is rich in bioactive compounds, including alkaloids and flavonoids. The plant typically grows to a height of 30-60 cm, with lance-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine, waxy coating. Its flowers are small, yellowish-green, and appear in clusters during the late spring to early summer.

The plant thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for medicinal and ornamental gardening.
