Acelica holosericea

Acelica holosericea, commonly known as the silky acacia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery foliage and small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for arid or semi-arid climates. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

However, it is important to note that it may have limited cultivation due to its specific environmental requirements.
