Acetosella alsinoides

Acetosella alsinoides, commonly known as lesser sorrel, is a low-growing perennial herb belonging to the Primulaceae family.

It is characterized by its rosette of oval, dark green leaves with a reddish tinge and small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring. Acetosella alsinoides is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to thrive in shaded environments.
