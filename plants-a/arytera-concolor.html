Arytera concolor

Arytera concolor, commonly known as the smoothleaf bellflower, is a perennial herb native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped, blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist meadows and streambanks, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are smooth and lance-shaped, with a distinct silver-gray underside.
