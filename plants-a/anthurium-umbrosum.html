Anthurium umbrosum

Anthurium umbrosum, commonly known as the dark anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its dark green, velvety leaves that are often variegated with shades of green and silver. The plant produces striking spadices, which are the modified leaves that enclose the true flowers. Anthurium umbrosum thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires high levels of indirect light.

Its unique foliage and elegant inflorescence make it a popular choice for indoor gardening and ornamental purposes.
