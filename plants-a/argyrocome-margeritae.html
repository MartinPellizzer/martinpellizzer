Argyrocome margeritae

Argyrocome margeritae, commonly known as the silver marguerite.

It is a flowering plant native to South Africa, characterized by its silver-gray foliage and daisy-like flowers. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family and is known for its ornamental value in gardens. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, requiring minimal water once established.

The flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
