Asystasia schimperi var. grantii

Asystasia schimperi var. grantii, commonly known as the silver stripe asystasia, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features striking, lance-shaped leaves with a distinctive silver-green stripe running along the midrib. The plant produces small, tubular flowers in shades of violet or purple, typically clustered in spikes. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental foliage.

This variant is notable for its compact growth habit and adaptability to various soil types.
