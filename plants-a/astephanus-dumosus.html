Astephanus dumosus

Astephanus dumosus, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats and has a low, spreading growth habit. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, while its stems are often covered in fine hairs.

Astephanus dumosus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
