Anthericum carnosum

Anthericum carnosum, commonly known as the woolly nettle, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which are typically borne on long, slender stalks. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in rocky or woodland areas.

The species is notable for its ability to tolerate poor soil conditions and its role in supporting local biodiversity.
