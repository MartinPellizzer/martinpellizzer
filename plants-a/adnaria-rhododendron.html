Adnaria rhododendron

Adnaria rhododendron, commonly known as the purple rhododendron, is a flowering plant belonging to the Ericaceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, clustered flowers that range in color from pink to deep purple. Adnaria rhododendron is also noted for its ecological importance, providing nectar for pollinators and habitat for various wildlife species.
