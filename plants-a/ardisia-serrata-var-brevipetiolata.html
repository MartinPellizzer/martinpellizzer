Ardisia serrata var. brevipetiolata

Ardisia serrata var. brevipetiolata, commonly known as the short-petioled flamewood, is a compact evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, dark green elliptical leaves with a slightly serrated margin. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers followed by bright red berries that are an attractive feature. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for shaded garden settings.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and seasonal color changes.
