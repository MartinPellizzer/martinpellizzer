Aria grandifolia

Aria grandifolia, commonly known as the large-flowered aria, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that typically bloom in shades of yellow and orange. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery pubescence. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in the understory of rainforests.

Aria grandifolia is valued both for its ornamental beauty and its role in local ecosystems.
