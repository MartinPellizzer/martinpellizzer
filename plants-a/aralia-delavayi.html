Aralia delavayi

Aralia delavayi, commonly known as the Chinese angelica or Delavay's aralia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features compound leaves with a distinctive palmate shape, often divided into several lobes. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers in umbels, which are followed by dark blue berries. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial shade.

Aralia delavayi is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine.
