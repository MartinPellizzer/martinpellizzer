Arenaria megalantha

Arenaria megalantha, commonly known as the giant sandwort, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of central Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically thrives in rocky or sandy soils. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pinkish flowers during the summer months. Its leaves are usually succulent and adapted to withstand arid conditions.

Arenaria megalantha is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in stabilizing soil in harsh environments.
