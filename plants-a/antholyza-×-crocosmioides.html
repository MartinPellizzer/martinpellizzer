Antholyza × crocosmioides

Antholyza × crocosmioides, commonly known as the hybrid lilies, is a cross between Antholyza and Crocosmia species.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, with narrow, sword-shaped leaves. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained, fertile soil.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking vibrant, long-lasting blooms.
