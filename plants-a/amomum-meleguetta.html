Amomum meleguetta

Amomum meleguetta, commonly known as the small cardamom or lesser cardamom, is a member of the ginger family (Zingiberaceae).

It is a perennial herb native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in regions of India, Thailand, and Indonesia. The plant produces small, aromatic berries that are used in traditional medicine and as a spice. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a distinctive, fragrant aroma.

The rhizomes and fruits are the primary parts used for their medicinal and culinary properties.
