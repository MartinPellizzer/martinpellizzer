Aloe glabrata var. concolor

Aloe glabrata var. concolor, commonly known as the smooth aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, covered in a waxy coating to reduce water loss. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellowish-orange flowers, which bloom in late summer. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their soothing properties.

It is well-suited for arid climates and requires minimal water once established.
