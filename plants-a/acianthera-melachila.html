Acianthera melachila

Acianthera melachila, commonly known as the dark green orchid, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its dark green, lance-shaped leaves and produces striking, violet to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in forest understories. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is known for its delicate, fragrant blooms.
