Aloe plicatilis

Aloe plicatilis, commonly known as the pleated aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are pleated and often covered in a waxy coating to reduce water loss. The leaves are typically blue-green in color and can grow up to 1 meter in height. It produces yellow flowers in clusters during the spring and summer seasons.

This plant is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
