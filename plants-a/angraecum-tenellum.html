Angraecum tenellum

Angraecum tenellum, commonly known as the slender angraecum, is a species of orchid native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit and produces showy, white, fragrant flowers. The plant typically blooms during the dry season, with its inflorescence bearing several small, pendulous flowers. Its leaves are narrow and arranged in a rosette, and it thrives in bright, indirect light with high humidity.

Angraecum tenellum is valued in horticulture for its elegant appearance and unique flowering characteristics.
