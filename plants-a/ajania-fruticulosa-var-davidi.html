Ajania fruticulosa var. davidi

Ajania fruticulosa var. davidi, commonly known as David's ajania, is a shrub native to the mountainous regions of central and southern China.

It features dense, evergreen foliage with dark green, leathery leaves that are often covered in a fine, white powdery coating. The plant produces small, pinkish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

Its ornamental value lies in its compact growth habit and seasonal floral display.
