Antholyza pulchra

Antholyza pulchra, commonly known as the pretty antholyza, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers with bright yellow centers and white petals. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its delicate appearance and long blooming period.

Antholyza pulchra is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
