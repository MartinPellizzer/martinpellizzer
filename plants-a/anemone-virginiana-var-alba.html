Anemone virginiana var. alba

Anemone virginiana var. alba, commonly known as white wood anemone, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to eastern North America.

It features a basal rosette of heart-shaped, white, and often hairy leaves. The plant produces showy, white, five-petaled flowers that bloom in early spring. It thrives in moist, shady woodlands and is often found in forest understories.

The species is noted for its delicate appearance and its role in early succession plant communities.
