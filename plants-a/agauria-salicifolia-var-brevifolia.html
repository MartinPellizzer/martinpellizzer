Agauria salicifolia var. brevifolia

Agauria salicifolia var. brevifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved willow-leaf agauria, is a shrub native to the Andes Mountains.

It is characterized by its slender, willow-like leaves that are typically shorter and narrower than those of the typical species. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. It thrives in high-altitude environments with cool, moist conditions.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural study.
