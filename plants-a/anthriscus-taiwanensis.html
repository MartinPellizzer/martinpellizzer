Anthriscus taiwanensis

Anthriscus taiwanensis, commonly known as Taiwan cow parsley, is a flowering plant native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the carrot family (Apiaceae) and is characterized by its finely divided, fern-like leaves and small white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream sides. It is a perennial herb that can reach heights of up to 60 centimeters.

Its botanical features make it an important species for ecological studies in its native habitat.
