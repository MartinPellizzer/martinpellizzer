Arctotis cineraria

Arctotis cineraria, commonly known as the blue mistflower, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers that bloom in late summer and early autumn. The plant typically grows as a short-lived perennial, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, giving them a slightly fuzzy texture.

Arctotis cineraria thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
