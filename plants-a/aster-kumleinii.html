Aster kumleinii

Aster kumleinii, commonly known as the Kumealin's aster, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky, arid environments and is adapted to dry conditions. It features narrow, gray-green leaves and produces showy blue to purple flowers in the fall.

Aster kumleinii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting pollinators.
