Alstroemeria caldasiana

Alstroemeria caldasiana, commonly known as the Caldasian lily, is a striking flowering plant native to the Andean regions of Colombia.

It belongs to the family Alstroemeriaceae and is characterized by its vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers that come in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant typically grows in high-altitude environments, thriving in cool, moist conditions with well-drained soil. Its foliage consists of long, narrow leaves that are often arranged in a rosette pattern.

Alstroemeria caldasiana is valued not only for its ornamental beauty but also for its ecological importance in its native habitat.
