Arisaema latisectum

Arisaema latisectum, commonly known as the broad-lobed arisaema, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of southern China.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive inflorescence consisting of a spadix surrounded by a spathe. The plant features a short, rhizomatous stem with large, heart-shaped leaves that are deeply lobed, giving it its common name. Its flowers are typically greenish-yellow, with a spadix that bears both male and female flowers.

Arisaema latisectum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional Chinese medicine.
