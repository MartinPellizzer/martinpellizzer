Afrotysonia

Afrotysonia, commonly known as the African star orchid, is a genus of flowering plants native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers. The plants typically grow in shaded, moist environments, often in forest undergrowth. Afrotysonia species are known for their vibrant colors, ranging from purple to pink and white.
