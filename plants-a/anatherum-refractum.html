Anatherum refractum

Anatherum refractum, commonly known as the refractum grass, is a perennial grass species native to certain regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences that are typically wind-pollinated. Its botanical features include a fibrous root system and a high tolerance for arid conditions.

Anatherum refractum is often found in open grasslands and along disturbed soils.
