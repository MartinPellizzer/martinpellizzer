Artemisia orientaliyunnanensis

Artemisia orientaliyunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan wormwood, is a perennial shrub native to the mountainous regions of Yunnan Province in China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, silver-green foliage with narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in composite heads, typical of the daisy family. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its medicinal properties, it is sometimes used in traditional Chinese medicine.
