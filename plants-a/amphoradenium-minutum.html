Amphoradenium minutum

Amphoradenium minutum, commonly known as the miniature bottle tree, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its cylindrical, bottle-shaped stems that store water, allowing it to survive in arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped yellow flowers during the warmer months. Its leaves are usually small and may be present or reduced, depending on the species.

It is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its low maintenance and unique appearance.
