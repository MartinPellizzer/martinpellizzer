Aceras formosum

Aceras formosum, commonly known as the Chinese bellflower, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its bell-shaped, blue to purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full sunlight.

Aceras formosum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
