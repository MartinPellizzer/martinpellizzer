Abutilon marlothii

Abutilon marlothii, commonly known as the velvetleaf abutilon, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height, characterized by its velvety, hairy stems and broad, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it adaptable to various environmental conditions.

Its ornamental value comes from its attractive foliage and the subtle beauty of its flowers.
