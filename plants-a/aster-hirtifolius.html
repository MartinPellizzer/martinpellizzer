Aster hirtifolius

Aster hirtifolius, commonly known as the hairyleaf aster, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and woodlands. Its leaves are covered in fine, stiff hairs, giving it its common name.

The showy purple or blue flowers bloom in the fall, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies.
