Anoectochilus lowii var. virescens

Anoectochilus lowii var. virescens, commonly known as the green-flowered orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dark green, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-draining, loose soil.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in terrariums or as a houseplant.
