Aster linifolius

Aster linifolius, commonly known as slenderleaf aster, is a perennial herb native to North America.

It is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and clusters of small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in the fall. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and woodland edges. It belongs to the sunflower family (Asteraceae) and is known for its ornamental value in gardens.

Aster linifolius is also valued for its ecological role, providing nectar for pollinators.
