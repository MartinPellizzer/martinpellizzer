Arachnorchis septuosa

Arachnorchis septuosa, commonly known as the spider orchid, is an exquisite member of the Orchidaceae family native to Australia.

This plant is characterized by its unique, spider-like appearance, with long, slender petals and a distinctive, club-shaped labellum. It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in sunny to partially shaded environments. The flowers are usually pale yellow to greenish-white, with intricate patterns that aid in pollination by specific insects.

Arachnorchis septuosa is a perennial herb that is often found in heathlands and open woodlands across southeastern Australia.
