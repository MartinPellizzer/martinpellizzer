Abarema harmsii

Abarema harmsii, commonly known as the pink stinkwood, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and showy, pinkish flowers. The plant is typically shrub-like, with a woody stem and can grow up to several meters in height. It is adapted to humid environments and is often found in forest understories.
