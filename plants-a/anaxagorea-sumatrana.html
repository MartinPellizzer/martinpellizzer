Anaxagorea sumatrana

Anaxagorea sumatrana, commonly known as the Sumatran ironwood, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia, particularly Indonesia.

Anaxagorea sumatrana is also noted for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types and its role in local ecosystems.
