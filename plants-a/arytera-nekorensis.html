Arytera nekorensis

Arytera nekorensis, commonly known as the Nekor island bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the Ryukyu Islands of Japan.

It is a perennial herb with slender stems and small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale blue. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories or along stream banks. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss in humid conditions.

Arytera nekorensis is of interest to botanists due to its unique floral structure and limited distribution.
