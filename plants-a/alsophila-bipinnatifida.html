Alsophila bipinnatifida

Alsophila bipinnatifida, commonly known as the giant chain fern, is a species of tree fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, unbranched trunk and large, pinnate fronds that can reach several meters in length. The fronds are bipinnate, meaning they have a secondary division of pinnae, giving the plant a feathery appearance. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

This plant plays an important ecological role in its habitat, providing shelter and food for various organisms.
