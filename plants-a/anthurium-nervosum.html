Anthurium nervosum

Anthurium nervosum, commonly known as the velvet leaf anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a velvety texture. The plant produces striking spadix flowers surrounded by a colorful spathe, which can range from red to orange. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.

Anthurium nervosum is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its aesthetic appeal and air-purifying properties.
