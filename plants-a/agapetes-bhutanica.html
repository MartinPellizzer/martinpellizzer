Agapetes bhutanica

Agapetes bhutanica, commonly known as the Bhutan silver leaf.

It is a species of shrub belonging to the Ericaceae family, native to the mountainous regions of Bhutan. The plant is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are densely covered with silvery-white hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. It typically grows at high altitudes, often in shaded and moist environments.

Agapetes bhutanica is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
