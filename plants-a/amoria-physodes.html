Amoria physodes

Amoria physodes, commonly known as the silverleaf lily, is a striking perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in a silvery, powdery coating, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of pale yellow to white flowers with prominent, showy stamens. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value and unique foliage, Amoria physodes is often cultivated for its visual appeal in horticultural settings.
