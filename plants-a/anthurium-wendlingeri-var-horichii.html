Anthurium wendlingeri var. horichii

Anthurium wendlingeri var. horichii, commonly known as the Horichii Philodendron, is a tropical plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves with a deep green color and prominent veins. The plant produces clusters of small, inconspicuous flowers surrounded by a spathe, which is often brightly colored. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity and well-draining soil.

This variety is popular among indoor gardeners for its ornamental foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
