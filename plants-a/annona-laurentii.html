Annona laurentii

Annona laurentii, commonly known as the Laurentian soursop, is a tropical plant native to Central America and the Caribbean.

It is a small to medium-sized tree with smooth, greyish bark and large, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces a fragrant, spiky fruit that is typically harvested when fully mature. The fruit's interior contains a white, juicy pulp with numerous black seeds.

It is valued for its ornamental beauty and edible fruit, which has a sweet and tangy flavor.
