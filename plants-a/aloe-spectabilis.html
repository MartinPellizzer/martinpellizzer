Aloe spectabilis

Aloe spectabilis, commonly known as the showy aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, with a waxy coating to reduce water loss. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with clusters of yellow flowers, which bloom in late spring or early summer. Its leaves contain a gel-like substance that is used in traditional medicine and skincare products.

Aloe spectabilis is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
