Aerides tessellata

Aerides tessellata, commonly known as the tessellated orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, mottled green and yellow leaves and its fragrant, showy flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its flowers are usually pink or purple, with a distinctive pattern of markings.

Aerides tessellata is valued for its ornamental beauty and is commonly cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
