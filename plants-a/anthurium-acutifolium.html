Anthurium acutifolium

Anthurium acutifolium, commonly known as the sharp-leaved anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped, glossy green leaves that are often adorned with a striking white or cream-colored margin. The plant produces vibrant spadices, which are modified leaves that enclose the actual flowers, typically in shades of red or orange. Anthurium acutifolium thrives in humid, warm environments and requires indirect light to flourish.

Its unique foliage and dramatic inflorescences make it a popular choice for indoor gardening and ornamental purposes.
