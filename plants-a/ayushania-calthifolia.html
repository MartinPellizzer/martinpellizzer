Ayushania calthifolia

Ayushania calthifolia, commonly known as the Himalayan bellflower, is a perennial herb native to the alpine regions of the Himalayas.

It belongs to the family Caryophyllaceae and is characterized by its delicate, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are erect and often branched.

Ayushania calthifolia is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens for its unique floral display.
