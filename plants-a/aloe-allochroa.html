Aloe allochroa

Aloe allochroa, commonly known as the variegated aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with white or yellowish variegation. The plant produces tall, branched flower stalks that bear yellow flowers, which bloom in the spring. Aloe allochroa is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and hardy nature.
