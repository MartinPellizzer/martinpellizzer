Arenaria Gypsostrata

Arenaria gypsostrata is a rare and resilient plant native to the arid regions of central Asia. It thrives in harsh, rocky environments where few other species can survive. This low-growing plant is known for its small, silver-gray leaves that help reduce water loss. Its flowers, which bloom in shades of white and pink, add a delicate beauty to the otherwise barren landscape. Due to its unique adaptations, it plays an important role in stabilizing desert soils and supporting local ecosystems.
