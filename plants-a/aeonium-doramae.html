Aeonium doramae

Aeonium doramae, commonly known as the Dragon's Blood Aeonium, is a succulent plant native to the Canary Islands.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-gray in color, with a waxy texture. The plant produces striking red or orange flowers on tall, branching stems during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a standout in both indoor and outdoor settings.
