Atragene indica

Atragene indica, commonly known as the Indian sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to Asia.

It features small, white or pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has succulent, fleshy leaves that help it retain moisture in arid conditions. It typically thrives in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Atragene indica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
