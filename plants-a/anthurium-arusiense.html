Anthurium arusiense

Anthurium arusiense, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Colombia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped spathe and spadix. The plant produces striking red bracts that surround a yellow spadix, making it a popular ornamental species. It thrives in humid, warm environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Its unique floral structure is adapted for pollination by insects and birds.
