Adesmia pugionata

Adesmia pugionata, commonly known as the swordleaf burr legume, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its narrow, sword-shaped leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces distinctive, spiny pods that contain seeds, which are adapted for dispersal by animals. It thrives in dry, arid environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.

Adesmia pugionata is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
