Aconitum × dragulescuanum

Aconitum × dragulescuanum, commonly known as the hybrid aconite, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Ranunculaceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, upright stems that bear large, showy blue to purple flowers. The plant has palmately compound leaves with three to five leaflets, and its roots are toxic due to the presence of alkaloids. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

This hybrid is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and botanical collections.
