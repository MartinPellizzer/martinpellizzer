Amethystea corymbosa

Amethystea corymbosa, commonly known as the purple cluster flower, is a striking plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, corymbs of violet-blue flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant features lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture, and its stems are often covered in a fine, silvery bloom. Amethystea corymbosa thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Its unique coloration and compact growth habit make it a valuable addition to both cultivated and natural landscapes.
