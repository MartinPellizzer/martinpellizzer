Anthurium ivanportillae

Anthurium ivanportillae, commonly known as the Ivanportillae arrowhead, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its unique inflorescence consisting of a spadix and a spathe. The plant features broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often a deep green with a glossy texture. Its flowers are typically small and clustered around the spadix, with the spathe often displaying vibrant colors.

Anthurium ivanportillae thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to flourish.
