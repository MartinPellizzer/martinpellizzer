Avena sterilis f. aprica

Avena sterilis f. aprica, commonly known as the spring oat, is a flowering plant belonging to the grass family Poaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny florets. It is typically found in open, disturbed habitats such as roadsides and fields.
