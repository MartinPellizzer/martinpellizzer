Ascochilus capricornis

Ascochilus capricornis, commonly known as the Cape orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, spurred flowers that are typically violet or purple in color. The plant features a basal rosette of leaves and produces inflorescences with several small, showy blooms. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens, though it requires specific care to flourish.
