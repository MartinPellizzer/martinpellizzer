Adromischus × fosteri

Adromischus × fosteri, commonly known as the Foster's moon cactus, is a hybrid succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, rounded shape and vibrant, often colorful foliage that resembles a moon's surface. The plant features thick, fleshy stems that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid conditions. Its leaves are typically arranged in a rosette pattern, providing structural support and efficient light capture.

Adromischus × fosteri is prized for its ornamental value and ease of care, making it a popular choice for succulent enthusiasts.
