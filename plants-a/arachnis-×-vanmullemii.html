Arachnis × vanmullemii

Arachnis × vanmullemii, commonly known as the Van Mullem's arachnis, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Indonesia and Malaysia. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in humid, shady environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in tropical gardens and greenhouses.
