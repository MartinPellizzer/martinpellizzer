Aganosma cymosa var. cymosa

Aganosma cymosa var. cymosa, commonly known as the clustered aganosma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. The leaves are typically ovate to elliptic in shape, with a smooth margin and a glossy surface. It prefers moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it suitable for shaded garden environments.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in horticultural displays.
