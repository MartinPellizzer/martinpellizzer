Acalypha reticulata var. rubra

Acalypha reticulata var. rubra, commonly known as red leaf spurges, is a striking ornamental plant native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its vibrant red or orange bracts that surround small yellow flowers, creating a striking visual display. The plant has palmately lobed leaves that are often covered in a fine, silky hair, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in warm, humid climates and prefers well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
