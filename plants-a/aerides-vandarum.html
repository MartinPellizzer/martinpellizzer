Aerides vandarum

Aerides vandarum, commonly known as the Vandarum orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow, often with a slight greenish tinge. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and requires partial shade for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
