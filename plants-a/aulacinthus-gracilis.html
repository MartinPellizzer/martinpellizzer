Aulacinthus gracilis

Aulacinthus gracilis, commonly known as the slender bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its delicate, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from blue to purple. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry soils and prefers full sun exposure. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, contributing to its overall slender appearance.

Aulacinthus gracilis is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ease of care.
