Agrostis rubra var. minor

Agrostis rubra var. minor, commonly known as red brome, is a grass species native to western North America.

It is characterized by its fine, tufted growth habit and reddish-purple inflorescence. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. It produces small, spike-like clusters of flowers that are often tinged with red or purple hues.

Agrostis rubra var. minor is an important component of native plant communities and is often used in restoration projects.
