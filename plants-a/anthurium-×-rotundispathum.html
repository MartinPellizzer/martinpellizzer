Anthurium × rotundispathum

Anthurium × rotundispathum, commonly known as the velvetleaf anthurium, is a hybrid plant native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a velvety texture. The plant produces striking spathes that can range in color from deep red to orange, often with a contrasting midrib. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Anthurium × rotundispathum is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and long-lasting blooms.
