Anaphalis royleana

Anaphalis royleana, commonly known as the frost plant or silverweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to the Himalayas.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its silvery, fuzzy foliage and small, daisy-like flowers. Anaphalis royleana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
