Aerides flavescens

Aerides flavescens, commonly known as the yellow orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that range in color from pale yellow to orange. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid tropical environments. Its leaves are broad and strap-like, often arranged in a rosette pattern.

Aerides flavescens is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and ability to thrive in controlled indoor settings.
