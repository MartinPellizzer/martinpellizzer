Aridaria luteoalba

Aridaria luteoalba, commonly known as the yellow-white aridaria, is a succulent plant adapted to arid environments.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that store water, allowing it to survive in dry conditions. The plant produces small, bright yellow flowers during the warm season. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun exposure.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for xeriscaping in desert gardens.
