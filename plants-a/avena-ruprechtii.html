Avena ruprechtii

Avena ruprechtii, commonly known as the Ruprecht's oat, is a perennial grass species native to South America.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spikelet-bearing inflorescences that are typically green or brown in color. It thrives in moist, temperate climates and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Avena ruprechtii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in native plant gardens.
