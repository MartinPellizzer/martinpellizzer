Arisaema victoriae

Arisaema victoriae, commonly known as the Victoria's arisaema, is a tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its unique inflorescence consisting of a spadix and a spathe. The plant produces striking, umbrella-shaped leaves that can reach up to 1 meter in diameter. Its flowers are typically yellow or green, with a spadix that bears both male and female flowers.

Arisaema victoriae is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
