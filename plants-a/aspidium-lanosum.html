Aspidium lanosum

Aspidium lanosum, commonly known as the downy water fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Athyriaceae.

It is characterized by its soft, hairy fronds that give it a distinctive, velvety appearance. Native to tropical and subtropical regions, it thrives in moist, shaded environments. The plant has pinnate leaves with small, ovate pinnae and produces sporangia on the underside of its fronds.

It is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and can be found in both wild and cultivated settings.
