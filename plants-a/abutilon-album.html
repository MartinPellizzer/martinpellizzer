Abutilon album

Abutilon album, commonly known as the velvetleaf or white abutilon, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its broad, velvety leaves that are typically dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces showy, lantern-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink or red. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in disturbed areas or along roadsides.

Abutilon album is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
