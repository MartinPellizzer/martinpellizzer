Agave cocui var. laguayrensis

Agave cocui var. laguayrensis, commonly known as the Laguayrense agave, is a species native to Mexico, specifically the state of Oaxaca.

It is a succulent plant characterized by its robust, spiky leaves that form a dense rosette. The plant produces a tall flowering stalk that bears small, greenish-white flowers. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, requiring minimal water and full sun exposure.

Due to its unique appearance and hardiness, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
