Achnatherum mattheii

Achnatherum mattheii, commonly known as the slender needlegrass, is a perennial grass native to arid and semi-arid regions of North America.

It typically grows in open habitats such as grasslands, disturbed soils, and rocky slopes. The plant has slender, erect stems with narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its inflorescence consists of a spike-like arrangement of small, spikelet-bearing branches.

Achnatherum mattheii is well adapted to drought conditions, with deep root systems that help it survive in nutrient-poor environments.
