Albuca toxicaria

Albuca toxicaria, commonly known as the toxic arrow lily, is a bulbous plant native to southern Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of white or pale yellow flowers. The plant contains toxic alkaloids, making it potentially harmful to humans and animals. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its toxicity, it should be handled with care and kept away from children and pets.
