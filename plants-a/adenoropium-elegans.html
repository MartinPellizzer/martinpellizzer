Adenoropium elegans

Adenoropium elegans, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, linear leaves and small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest understories. Its root system is fibrous, and it often forms rhizomes that help in propagation.

The flowers are usually white or pale pink, with a unique structure that aids in pollination by specific insect species.
