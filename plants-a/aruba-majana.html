Aruba majana

Aruba majana, commonly known as the Aruba starfruit, is a tropical plant native to the island of Aruba.

It is a small shrub that produces small, yellow-orange fruits with a unique star-shaped appearance. The plant thrives in warm, sunny climates with well-drained soil. Its leaves are oval-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.

The fruit is typically harvested when it turns a vibrant yellow, indicating maturity.
