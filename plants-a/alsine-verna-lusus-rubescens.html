Alsine verna lusus rubescens

Alsine verna lusus rubescens, commonly known as redstem alsike, is a flowering plant in the family Brassicaceae.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in disturbed soils and meadows. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in early summer. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often have a hairy texture.
