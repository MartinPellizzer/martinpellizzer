Anthurium scherzerianum var. gallicum

Anthurium scherzerianum var. gallicum, commonly known as the French anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped, glossy green leaves and its showy, spadix-like inflorescence surrounded by a vivid red spathe. The plant thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil. It is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and unique floral structure.

Anthurium scherzerianum var. gallicum requires consistent moisture and high humidity to maintain its health and vibrant appearance.
