Aulomyrcia lancifolia

Aulomyrcia lancifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved myrcia, is a shrub native to the rainforests of Brazil.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves and small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces elliptical, woody berries that are typically red when mature. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in secondary growth areas.

Aulomyrcia lancifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional medicinal uses.
