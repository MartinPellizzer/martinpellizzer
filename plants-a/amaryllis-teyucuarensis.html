Amaryllis teyucuarensis

Amaryllis teyucuarensis, commonly known as the Teyucuarensis amaryllis, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a bulbous plant that produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant typically blooms in late spring or early summer, and its foliage consists of strap-like, grassy leaves. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.

This species is valued for its ornamental beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
