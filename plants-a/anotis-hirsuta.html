Anotis hirsuta

Anotis hirsuta, commonly known as the hairy anotis, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to southern Africa, particularly South Africa, where it thrives in rocky and sandy soils. The plant features small, hairy leaves and produces yellowish flowers that bloom in late summer. It is often found in coastal and mountainous regions, adapting well to dry and sunny conditions.

Anotis hirsuta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
