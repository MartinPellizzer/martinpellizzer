Amaranthus rajasekharii

Amaranthus rajasekharii, commonly known as the Indian lovegrass, is a species of flowering plant in the amaranth family.

It is native to India and is characterized by its upright growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically green or yellowish in color. It is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and roadsides.

Amaranthus rajasekharii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional landscaping.
