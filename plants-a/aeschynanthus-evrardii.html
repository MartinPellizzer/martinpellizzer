Aeschynanthus evrardii

Aeschynanthus evrardii, commonly known as the flame orchid, is a tropical shrub native to western Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular red flowers that resemble tiny flames, often clustered in groups of three. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are glossy and dark green, providing a striking contrast to its vibrant blooms. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity to flourish.

Aeschynanthus evrardii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique and eye-catching appearance.
