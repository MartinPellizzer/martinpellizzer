Adenocarpus anagyrifolius var. leiocarpus

Adenocarpus anagyrifolius var. leiocarpus, commonly known as the smooth-seeded horsebean, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its legume fruits and nitrogen-fixing abilities. The plant features compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces cylindrical, smooth-seeded pods. It is often found in grassland and savanna habitats, thriving in well-drained soils.

This variant is distinguished by its leathery, dark green foliage and its ability to adapt to dry conditions.
