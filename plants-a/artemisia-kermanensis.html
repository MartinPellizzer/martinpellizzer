Artemisia kermanensis

Artemisia kermanensis, commonly known as Kerman wormwood, is a flowering plant in the sunflower family.

It is native to the arid regions of Iran and is characterized by its aromatic, silver-gray foliage. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Due to its medicinal properties, it has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
