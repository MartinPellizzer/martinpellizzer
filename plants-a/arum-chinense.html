Arum chinense

Arum chinense, commonly known as Chinese arrowroot, is a herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features a short, rhizomatous stem with large, heart-shaped leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. The plant produces a spadix surrounded by a spathe, which is characteristic of the Araceae family. Its flowers are typically yellow or green, and it is often grown for its ornamental value.

The plant is also valued for its starchy roots, which can be used as a food source.
