Acer Pictum F. Mono

Acer pictum f. mono is a unique variant of the sugar maple known for its striking single-trunk growth. This rare form exhibits a distinct, almost columnar shape that sets it apart from its more common multi-trunk relatives. It is prized by gardeners and landscapers for its elegant appearance and adaptability to various climates. The plant's foliage displays the typical maple colors, transitioning through vibrant shades in autumn. Despite its beauty, it remains a relatively uncommon specimen in the horticultural world.
