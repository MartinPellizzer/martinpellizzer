Aster × lutescens

Aster × lutescens, commonly known as the yellow wood aster, is a hybrid perennial plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its showy yellow to orange flowers that bloom in late summer and early fall. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet and features narrow, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Aster × lutescens is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
