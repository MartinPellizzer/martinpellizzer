Agoseris gracilens var. greenei

Agoseris gracilens var. greenei, commonly known as Greene's slender agoseris, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of ray and disk florets. This perennial herb typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. Its slender stems and small, daisy-like flowers are adapted to arid environments.

The plant's botanical features make it an important component of its native ecosystem.
