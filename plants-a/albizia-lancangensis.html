Albizia lancangensis

Albizia lancangensis, commonly known as the Lancang Albizia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like China, Vietnam, and Thailand.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and showy, fluffy flower clusters. The plant is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and along riverbanks. Its root system has the ability to fix nitrogen, making it beneficial for soil enrichment.

Albizia lancangensis is also valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional uses in local medicine.
