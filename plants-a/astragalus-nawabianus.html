Astragalus nawabianus

Astragalus nawabianus, commonly known as Nawab's milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to Afghanistan and Pakistan.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow or orange flowers. The plant typically grows in arid and semi-arid environments, adapting to harsh conditions with its deep root system.

It plays a role in soil stabilization and is of interest for its potential medicinal properties.
