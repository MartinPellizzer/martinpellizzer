Alstroemeria ligtu subsp. splendens

Alstroemeria ligtu subsp.

splendens, commonly known as the Splendour Alstroemeria, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Chile and Argentina. It belongs to the family Alstroemeriaceae and is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that come in a range of colors including red, orange, and yellow. The plant typically grows as a herbaceous perennial with strap-like leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it suitable for gardens and floral arrangements.
