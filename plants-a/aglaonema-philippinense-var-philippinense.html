Aglaonema philippinense var. philippinense

Aglaonema philippinense var. philippinense, commonly known as the Philippine evergreen arum, is a perennial herb native to the Philippines.

It features striking, arrowhead-shaped leaves that are typically variegated with shades of green, white, and cream. The plant belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its spatheous inflorescence, which includes a colorful spathe and a spadix. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal.

The leaves are also known for their ability to purify indoor air, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
