Abrahamia sericea

Abrahamia sericea, commonly known as the silky abrahamia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and hairy foliage. The plant produces small, daisy-like flowers that are typically pale yellow to white in color. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats.
