Anemone oligantha

Anemone oligantha, commonly known as the alpine anemone, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, preferring well-drained soils and full sun exposure. Its leaves are basal, forming a rosette shape, and are usually covered in a fine layer of white hairs.

Anemone oligantha is a hardy perennial that thrives in cooler climates and is often found in high-altitude meadows.
