Aloinopsis jamesii

Aloinopsis jamesii, commonly known as the James' aloinopsis, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, rounded rosettes of fleshy, gray-green leaves that store water, adapting to arid environments. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with small, yellowish-green flowers during the summer months. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, requiring minimal water to prevent root rot.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
