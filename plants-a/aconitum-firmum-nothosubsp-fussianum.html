Aconitum firmum nothosubsp. fussianum

Aconitum firmum nothosubsp.

fussianum, commonly known as the Fussen aconite, is a hybrid plant native to the Alps. It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers.
