Arctotis scapiformis

Arctotis scapiformis, commonly known as the narrow-leaved arctotis, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, daisy-like flowers. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces bright yellow to orange flowers with a distinct central disk. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Arctotis scapiformis is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
