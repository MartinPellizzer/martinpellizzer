Adenostemma natalense

Adenostemma natalense, commonly known as Natal grass or Natal star grass, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its erect, fibrous stem and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in composite heads, typical of the daisy family. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and open woodlands.

Adenostemma natalense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
