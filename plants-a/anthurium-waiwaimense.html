Anthurium waiwaimense

Anthurium waiwaimense, commonly known as the Waiwai mountain anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves and vibrant spathe. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often found in montane forests. Its flowers are usually greenish-white with a spadix, and it produces berries that are edible but not commonly consumed by humans.

Anthurium waiwaimense is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant.
