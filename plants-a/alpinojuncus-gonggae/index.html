Alpinojuncus Gonggae

Gonggae rush, scientifically known as Alpinojuncus gonggae, is a unique and lesser-known plant species found primarily in high-altitude regions of Asia. This grass-like plant is characterized by its slender, grass-like stems and narrow leaves that thrive in cold, moist environments. Despite its delicate appearance, it plays an important ecological role in its native habitats by stabilizing soil and providing habitat for small organisms. Its limited distribution and fragile nature make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists. Due to habitat loss and climate change, the survival of this rare plant is increasingly threatened.
