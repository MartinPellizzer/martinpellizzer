Aspidistra opaca var. opaca

Aspidistra opaca var. opaca, commonly known as the Japanese evergreen, is a shade-tolerant plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its dark green, elliptical leaves with a glossy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically hidden within a spadix surrounded by a spathe. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and requires moderate to low light conditions.

Its ability to thrive in low-light environments makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
