Aster cordineri

Aster cordineri, commonly known as the Cordineri's aster, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry habitats, showcasing its adaptability to harsh environments. Its flowers are usually blue to purple in color, with ray florets surrounding a central disk.

Aster cordineri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
