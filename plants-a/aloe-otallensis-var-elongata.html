Aloe otallensis var. elongata

Aloe otallensis var. elongata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features elongated, narrow leaves that are typically covered in a pale blue-gray waxy coating. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow flowers during the warmer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with plenty of sunlight.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and arid landscapes.
