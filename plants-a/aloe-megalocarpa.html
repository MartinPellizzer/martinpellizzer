Aloe megalocarpa

Aloe megalocarpa, commonly known as the giant aloe or mountain aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, upright growth habit and thick, fleshy leaves that store water, typical of arid environments. The plant produces large, yellowish-green flower clusters that bloom in late winter or early spring. Its leaves are often ribbed and have a waxy coating, which helps reduce water loss.

Aloe megalocarpa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
