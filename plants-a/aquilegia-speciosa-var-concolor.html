Aquilegia speciosa var. concolor

Aquilegia speciosa var. concolor, commonly known as the Colorado leopard's-bane, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a member of the Ranunculaceae family, characterized by its showy, spurred petals and compound leaves.
