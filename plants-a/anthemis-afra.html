Anthemis afra

Anthemis afra, commonly known as African pyrethrum, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is known for its aromatic foliage and is often used in traditional medicine. Its flowers contain natural pyrethrin compounds, which are used in organic pest control.

Anthemis afra thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a hardy and drought-tolerant species.
