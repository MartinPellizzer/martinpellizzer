Alsophila nitida

Alsophila nitida, commonly known as the silver fern, is a species of tree fern native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and feathery, bipinnate fronds that give it a distinctive appearance. The fronds are often covered in a fine, silvery down, which contributes to its common name. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Alsophila nitida is valued for its ornamental qualities and plays an important ecological role in its native habitat.
