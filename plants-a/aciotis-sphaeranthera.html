Aciotis sphaeranthera

Aciotis sphaeranthera, commonly known as the spherical-flowered aciotis, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in dense, spherical clusters, giving it its common name. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.

Its foliage consists of narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.
