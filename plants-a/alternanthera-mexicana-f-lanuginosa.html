Alternanthera mexicana f. lanuginosa

Alternanthera mexicana f. lanuginosa, commonly known as the velvety Alternanthera, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its soft, velvety foliage that gives it a distinctive texture. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or red in color. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for gardeners and plant enthusiasts.
