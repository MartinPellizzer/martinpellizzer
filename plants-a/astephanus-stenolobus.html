Astephanus stenolobus

Astephanus stenolobus, commonly known as the narrow-lobed stephanus, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant produces small, daisy-like flowers with yellow disc florets and white to pale blue ray florets. Its leaves are narrow and often have a slightly hairy texture.

It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.
