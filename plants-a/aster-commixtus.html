Aster commixtus

Aster commixtus, commonly known as the purple coneflower, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its showy purple or pink flowers that bloom in the summer months. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet and has narrow, grass-like leaves. Its scientific name reflects its composite flower structure, which consists of both ray and disc florets.

Aster commixtus is a hardy perennial that thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.
