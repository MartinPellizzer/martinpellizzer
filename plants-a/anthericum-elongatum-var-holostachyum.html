Anthericum elongatum var. holostachyum

Anthericum elongatum var. holostachyum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved sweetflag, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically arranged in a dense rosette, with a fibrous root system. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in a spike, which are borne on slender, erect stems. It thrives in moist, shady environments such as wet meadows and marshy areas.

The species is characterized by its aromatic foliage and is often used in traditional herbal medicine.
