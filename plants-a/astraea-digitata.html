Astraea digitata

Astraea digitata, commonly known as the starry starwort, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in a star-like pattern, giving it its common name. Its leaves are typically narrow and lance-shaped, with a slightly hairy surface.

Astraea digitata is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks.
