Allium siskiyouense

Allium siskiyouense, commonly known as the Siskiyou garlic, is a species of flowering plant in the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is native to the western United States, specifically found in Oregon and California. This perennial herb has bulbous stems and produces clusters of white to pale purple flowers. The plant is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and is often found in open, rocky habitats.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional herbal medicine.
