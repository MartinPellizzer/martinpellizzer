Amyema seemeniana subsp. flexuosa

Amyema seemeniana subsp.

flexuosa, commonly known as the zigzag hakea, is a shrub native to southern Australia. It is characterized by its distinctive zigzagging stems and small, needle-like leaves.

The plant produces bright red or orange flowers in spring, which are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to dry, sunny environments.

This subspecies is often used in native gardens for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
