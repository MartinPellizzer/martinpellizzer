Aristida graciliflora var. robusta

Aristida graciliflora var. robusta, commonly known as the robust slendergrass, is a perennial grass species native to South Africa.

It features slender, erect stems with narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically hairy along the edges. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, bract-like flowers, which are often green or pale brown in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open grasslands or rocky areas.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to stabilize soil in dry environments.
