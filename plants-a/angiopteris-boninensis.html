Angiopteris boninensis

Angiopteris boninensis, commonly known as the Bonin shield fern, is a tropical fern native to the Bonin Islands.

It belongs to the family Athyriaceae and is characterized by its large, shield-shaped fronds. The plant has a creeping rhizome and produces sporangia on the undersides of its leaves. Its leaves are typically dark green and can grow up to several meters in length.

Angiopteris boninensis is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices.
