Armeria plantaginea subsp. oretana

Armeria plantaginea subsp.

oretana, commonly known as the Oretan thrift, is a low-growing, evergreen perennial that belongs to the family Caryophyllaceae. It is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in well-drained, rocky soils.

The plant produces clusters of small, white or pink flowers that bloom in spring and early summer. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically covered in a dense layer of fine, white hairs.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in coastal and dry garden environments.
