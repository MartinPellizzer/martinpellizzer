Argyroderma litorale

Argyroderma litorale, commonly known as the coastal silverleaf.

It is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of southern Africa. The plant is characterized by its silver-green leaves and small, pale yellow flowers. It thrives in sandy, well-drained soils and is often found in dune environments.

Argyroderma litorale plays a role in stabilizing coastal dunes and providing habitat for local wildlife.
