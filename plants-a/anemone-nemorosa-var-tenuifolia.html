Anemone nemorosa var. tenuifolia

Anemone nemorosa var. tenuifolia, commonly known as the slenderleaf wood anemone, is a herbaceous perennial native to Europe.

It features delicate, white or pale pink flowers with five petals, typically blooming in early spring. The plant has finely divided, fern-like leaves that are narrow and elongated, giving it its common name. It thrives in woodland environments, preferring moist, well-drained soils.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and early season blooms.
