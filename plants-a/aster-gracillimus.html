Aster gracillimus

Aster gracillimus, commonly known as the slender aster, is a herbaceous perennial native to the eastern United States.

It features slender, upright stems that produce small, daisy-like flowers in shades of blue to purple. The plant has finely divided, gray-green leaves that are often covered in a powdery coating. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sun.

Aster gracillimus is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting pollinators.
