Aeschynomene aristata

Aeschynomene aristata, commonly known as the hairy leaf desmodium, is a herbaceous annual plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellow flowers. The plant is often found in disturbed soils and is used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

Its ability to fix nitrogen makes it valuable in agricultural systems as a green manure crop.
