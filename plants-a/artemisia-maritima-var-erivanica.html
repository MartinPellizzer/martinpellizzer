Artemisia maritima var. erivanica

Artemisia maritima var. erivanica, commonly known as the Erivan thyme-verbena, is a perennial herb native to coastal regions of Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its aromatic, silver-gray foliage and small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in summer. The plant thrives in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in maritime environments. It is valued for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally in herbal remedies.

Its resilience to salt and wind makes it an important species in coastal vegetation.
