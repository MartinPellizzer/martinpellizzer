Aloe ithya

Aloe ithya, commonly known as the Cape aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, upright growth habit and striking, sword-shaped leaves that are typically green with red or purple markings. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing yellow flowers, which attract pollinators such as birds and insects. Aloe ithya is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its fleshy leaves and having a shallow root system.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine for its healing properties.
