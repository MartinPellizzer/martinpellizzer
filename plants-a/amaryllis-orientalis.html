Amaryllis orientalis

Amaryllis orientalis, commonly known as the oriental amaryllis, is a bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It produces striking, showy flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, often with a distinctive striped or spotted pattern. The plant features strap-like, narrow leaves that are typically evergreen. It blooms in late spring to early summer, and the flowers can last for several weeks.

Amaryllis orientalis is popular in gardens and as a houseplant due to its vibrant appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
