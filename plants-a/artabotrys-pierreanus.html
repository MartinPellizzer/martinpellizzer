Artabotrys pierreanus

Artabotrys pierreanus, commonly known as the African star of Bethlehem, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant, white flowers that resemble stars, which bloom in clusters at the ends of branches. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are ovate to elliptical in shape. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight.
