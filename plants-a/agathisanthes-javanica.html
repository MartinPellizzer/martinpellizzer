Agathisanthes javanica

Agathisanthes javanica, commonly known as the Java orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its inflorescence consists of a spike bearing several white or pale pink flowers.

Agathisanthes javanica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
