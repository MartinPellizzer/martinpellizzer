Anthurium × wittmackii

Anthurium × wittmackii, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Araceae family.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves and vibrant, spadix-like inflorescence that ranges from red to orange. Native to tropical regions of South America, it thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light. The plant is popular as a decorative foliage plant due to its ornamental leaves and long-lasting blooms.

Its botanical features make it a sought-after species in both horticulture and botanical research.
