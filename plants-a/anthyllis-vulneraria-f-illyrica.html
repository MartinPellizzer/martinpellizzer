Anthyllis vulneraria f. illyrica

Anthyllis vulneraria f. illyrica, commonly known as the Illyrian burnet, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its dense, hairy foliage and clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. This subspecies is native to the Balkan Peninsula, particularly in regions of Croatia, Bosnia and Herzegovina, and Albania. It thrives in dry, sunny habitats such as rocky slopes and grasslands.

The plant is noted for its ornamental value and is often used in gardens for its compact growth and long-lasting blooms.
