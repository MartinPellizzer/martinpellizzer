Aeschynomene pulchra

Aeschynomene pulchra, commonly known as the beautiful aeschynomene, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its slender, branching stems and small, delicate leaves. The plant produces clusters of pea-like flowers that range in color from white to pink, often attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
