Aloe glauca var. spinosior

Aloe glauca var. spinosior, commonly known as the spiny aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, often with a waxy coating. The leaves are adorned with sharp, spiny margins that distinguish this variety from other aloe species. It produces clusters of yellow flowers during the summer months, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

This plant is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
