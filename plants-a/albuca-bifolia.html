Albuca bifolia

Albuca bifolia, commonly known as the two-leaved albuca, is a bulbous perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features striking, narrow, grass-like leaves that are often bluish-green in color. The plant produces tall, branched stems with clusters of white or pale yellow flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Albuca bifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
