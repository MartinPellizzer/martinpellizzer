Afrocanthium ngonii

Afrocanthium ngonii, commonly known as the Congo flame tree, is a striking tropical plant native to Central Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy red flowers that bloom in clusters, creating a dramatic visual impact. The plant has a woody stem with a rough, bark-like texture and can grow up to several meters in height. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a serrated edge.

Afrocanthium ngonii thrives in humid, warm climates and is often found in forested areas or near rivers.
