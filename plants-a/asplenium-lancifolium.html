Asplenium lancifolium

Asplenium lancifolium, commonly known as the sword fern, is a species of evergreen fern characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves.

It belongs to the family Aspleniaceae and is native to various regions in Asia, including China, Japan, and India. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky crevices. Its fronds are usually dark green and can reach lengths of up to 30 centimeters.

Asplenium lancifolium is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to cultivate in suitable conditions.
