Acalypha seemannii

Acalypha seemannii, commonly known as the velvetleaf or red-rooted acalypha, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

The plant typically grows as an annual or perennial herb, reaching heights of up to 1 meter.
