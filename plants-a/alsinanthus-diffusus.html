Alsinanthus diffusus

Alsinanthus diffusus, commonly known as the spreading alsinanthus, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing annual herb that typically reaches heights of about 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features slender stems and small, pale blue or violet flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are alternate, linear, and often covered with fine hairs.

Alsinanthus diffusus is adapted to dry, sunny environments and is often found in disturbed soils.
