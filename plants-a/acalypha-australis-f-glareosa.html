Acalypha australis f. glareosa

Acalypha australis f. glareosa, commonly known as the glossy abutilon, is a variant of the abutilon plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its striking, glossy green foliage that shimmers in sunlight. The plant produces showy, lantern-shaped yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Its unique appearance and ease of care contribute to its popularity among gardeners.
