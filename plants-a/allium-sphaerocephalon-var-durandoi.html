Allium sphaerocephalon var. durandoi

Allium sphaerocephalon var. durandoi, commonly known as the Durando's globe onion, is a perennial bulbous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features spherical, grayish-purple bulbous stems that are covered in a fibrous sheath. The plant produces small, white or pale purple flowers arranged in a dense, spherical cluster at the top of the stem. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often with a slight bluish tint.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
