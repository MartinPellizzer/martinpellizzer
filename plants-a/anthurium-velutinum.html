Anthurium velutinum

Anthurium velutinum, commonly known as the velvet anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are velvety to the touch, giving it its common name. The plant produces bright red spathes with yellow spadices, which are its showy inflorescences. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Anthurium velutinum is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and unique floral structure.
