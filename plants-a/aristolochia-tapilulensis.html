Aristolochia tapilulensis

Aristolochia tapilulensis, commonly known as the Tapilulensis Dutchman's Pipe, is a member of the Aristolochiaceae family.

It is native to Central America, particularly found in Mexico and parts of Guatemala. The plant is characterized by its unique, pipe-shaped flowers that are often green or brown in color. The leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a rough texture.

It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in moist, shaded environments.
