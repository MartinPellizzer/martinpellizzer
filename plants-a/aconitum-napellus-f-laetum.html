Aconitum napellus f. laetum

Aconitum napellus f. laetum, commonly known as the Alpineaconite, is a variant of the European aconite.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the alpine regions of Europe. The plant features showy blue to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are palmately lobed and have a distinctive, waxy texture.

It is adapted to grow in rocky, well-drained soils at high altitudes.
