Arisaema serratum var. maximowiczii

Arisaema serratum var. maximowiczii, commonly known as the Sichuan arrowhead, is a variant of the arrowhead plant native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, heart-shaped leaves and spadix inflorescence typical of the Araceae family. The plant produces striking, yellowish-white spadix surrounded by a greenish-white spathe. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions of China.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
