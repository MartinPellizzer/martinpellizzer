Asparagus ecklonii

Asparagus ecklonii, commonly known as the mountain asparagus, is a perennial shrub native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and stout, spiny stems. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers in clusters, which are followed by bright red berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.

Asparagus ecklonii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and hardy nature.
