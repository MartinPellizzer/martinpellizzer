Arenaria tejedensis

Arenaria tejedensis, commonly known as the Tejed sandflower, is a flowering plant native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It thrives in sandy and rocky soils, typically found in coastal and inland arid environments. The plant produces small, white or pink flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are narrow and succulent, adapted to conserve water in dry conditions.

Arenaria tejedensis is an important species for stabilizing dunes and supporting local biodiversity.
