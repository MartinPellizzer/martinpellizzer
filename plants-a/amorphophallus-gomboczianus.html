Amorphophallus gomboczianus

Amorphophallus gomboczianus, commonly known as the Hungarian giant hogweed, is a striking plant native to the Carpathian region of Central Europe.

Its rhizomatous root system allows it to spread vegetatively, making it an aggressive colonizer in its native habitat.
