Aster spinosus var. jaliscensis

Aster spinosus var. jaliscensis, commonly known as the Jalisco spiny aster, is a perennial herb native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its spiny stems and hairy leaves. The plant produces showy, purple to blue flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and woodland edges.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
