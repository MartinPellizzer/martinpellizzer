Asplundia trilobulata

Asplundia trilobulata, commonly known as the three-lobed arrowroot, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its arrow-shaped leaves and striking inflorescence. The plant produces a starchy root that is used in culinary applications. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil.

Asplundia trilobulata is often cultivated for its ornamental value as well as its edible root.
