Achradotypus vieillardii

Achradotypus vieillardii, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its compact rosette of fleshy, velvety leaves. Due to its ornamental value, it is widely cultivated as a houseplant.
