Athyrium filix-femina f. rectangulare

Athyrium filix-femina f. rectangulare, commonly known as the rectangular maidenhair fern, is a variant of the common maidenhair fern.

It is characterized by its distinctive, elongated, and rectangular-shaped fronds. The plant features delicate, finely divided leaves that are typically bright green and have a silky texture. It thrives in shady, moist environments and is often found in woodland areas.

This fern is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
