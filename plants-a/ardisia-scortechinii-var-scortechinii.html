Ardisia scortechinii var. scortechinii

Ardisia scortechinii var. scortechinii, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small evergreen shrub characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of small, white flowers. The plant produces red, fleshy berries that are a key feature of its botanical profile. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Its foliage and fruit make it a notable species in both horticultural and ecological contexts.
