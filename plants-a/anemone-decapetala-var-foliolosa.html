Anemone decapetala var. foliolosa

Anemone decapetala var. foliolosa, commonly known as the leafy anemone, is a herbaceous perennial native to the eastern United States.

It features showy, white to pale pink flowers with ten petals, arranged in a star-like pattern. The leaves are deeply lobed and often have a distinctive, feathery appearance. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting local pollinators.
