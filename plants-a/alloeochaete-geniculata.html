Alloeochaete geniculata

Alloeochaete geniculata, commonly known as the zigzag grass, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive zigzagging stems, which give it its common name. The plant typically grows in grassland habitats and is often found in areas with well-drained soils. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spikelets.

Alloeochaete geniculata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique growth form.
