Armeria rouyana f. littorea

Armeria rouyana f. littorea, commonly known as the sea thrift, is a low-growing perennial plant native to coastal regions of Europe.

It features dense clusters of small, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has succulent, silver-gray foliage that is adapted to withstand salt spray and strong winds. It thrives in well-drained, sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in dune environments.

This variety is valued for its hardiness and ability to stabilize coastal soils.
