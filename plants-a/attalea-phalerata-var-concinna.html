Attalea phalerata var. concinna

Attalea phalerata var. concinna, commonly known as the pink palm or pink pachypodium, is a species of flowering plant in the Arecaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feathery leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. It produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or white in color.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to grow in well-drained soils with ample sunlight.
