Austrocallerya

Austrocallerya, commonly known as Chinese wisteria, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and showy, pendulous flowers. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its rapid growth and attractive foliage.

However, it can become invasive in some regions if not managed properly.
