Aleuritopteris leptolepis

Aleuritopteris leptolepis, commonly known as the slender fern, is a species of fern native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, bipinnate fronds that are typically light green and have a feathery appearance. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky crevices. Its reproductive structures, including sori, are located on the underside of the fronds.

Aleuritopteris leptolepis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
