Asarum nipponicum var. nankaiense

Asarum nipponicum var. nankaiense, commonly known as the Nankai asarum, is a perennial herb native to Japan.

It belongs to the Aristolochiaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, hooded flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.

Due to its medicinal properties, it has been used in traditional Japanese medicine.
