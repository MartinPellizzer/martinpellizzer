Aichryson laxum subsp. laxum

Aichryson laxum subsp.

laxum, commonly known as the silver leaf aichryson, is a succulent plant native to South Africa. It features dense, silver-gray foliage with a compact, shrub-like growth habit.

The leaves are fleshy and arranged in a rosette pattern, adapted for water storage. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers during the summer months.

This species is well-suited for rock gardens and dry landscapes due to its drought tolerance and ornamental appearance.
