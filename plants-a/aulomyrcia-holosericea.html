Aulomyrcia holosericea

Aulomyrcia holosericea, commonly known as the silverleaf acanthus, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking silver-green leaves with a velvety texture and its showy spikes of violet-blue flowers. The plant belongs to the family Acanthaceae and is known for its ornamental value in gardens. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for drought-tolerant landscaping.

Its unique foliage and vibrant blooms make it a standout in both natural and cultivated settings.
