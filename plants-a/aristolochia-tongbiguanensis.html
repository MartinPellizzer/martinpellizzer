Aristolochia tongbiguanensis

Aristolochia tongbiguanensis, commonly known as the Tongbiguan arrowhead vine, is a climbing herbaceous plant native to China.

It belongs to the Aristolochiaceae family and is characterized by its unique, heart-shaped leaves and intricate, pipe-shaped flowers. The plant produces a fragrant, waxy bloom that resembles a pipe or a horn, which is a distinctive feature of the genus Aristolochia. Its flowers are typically greenish-yellow with a purple or brownish hue, and they serve as a specialized structure for attracting pollinators.

The plant is of interest in botanical studies due to its morphological adaptations and potential medicinal properties.
