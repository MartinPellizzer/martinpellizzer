Asteriscus spinosus

Asteriscus spinosus, commonly known as the spiny asteriscus, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its small, spiny stems and clusters of tiny, star-shaped yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to arid environments. Its foliage consists of narrow, gray-green leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white hairs.

Asteriscus spinosus is valued in landscaping for its drought tolerance and ornamental appeal.
