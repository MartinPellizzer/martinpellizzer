Arum simlense

Arum simlense, commonly known as the Giant Taro, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features large, arrow-shaped leaves that can grow up to 2 meters in length, giving it a dramatic appearance. The plant produces a spadix surrounded by a showy, purple spathe, which is typical of the Araceae family. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found near water bodies.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for tropical gardens and indoor cultivation.
