Aeridostachya purpureocentrum

Aeridostachya purpureocentrum, commonly known as the purple-centered orchid, is a tropical orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking violet and purple flower spikes that emerge from a rhizomatous stem. The plant produces fragrant, showy flowers with a distinctive purple center, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light and well-draining soil.

Due to its specific care requirements, it is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
