Astrotrichilia masoalensis

Astrotrichilia masoalensis, commonly known as the Masoalensis starhairs, is a unique plant species native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and specialized structures. The plant features finely divided, feathery leaves that contribute to its distinctive appearance. Its flowers are typically small and clustered, often displaying a range of colors from white to yellow.

Astrotrichilia masoalensis is adapted to the humid and tropical conditions of its native habitat, showcasing remarkable botanical resilience.
