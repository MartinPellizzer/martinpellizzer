Anemia jaliscana var. integrifolia

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its showy, white or pink petals. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests or along stream banks. Its leaves are palmately lobed and have a smooth or slightly toothed margin.
