Armeria filicaulis subsp. alfacarensis

Armeria filicaulis subsp.

alfacarensis, commonly known as the Alfacar alpine thrift, is a perennial herb native to the Iberian Peninsula. It typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils in alpine and subalpine environments.

The plant features dense, silver-gray foliage and produces clusters of pinkish-white flowers in early summer. It is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and compact growth habit.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in mountainous habitats.
