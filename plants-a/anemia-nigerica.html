Anemia nigerica

Anemia nigerica, commonly known as the black anemone, is a flowering plant native to the wetlands of Southeast Asia.

It features striking dark purple to black petals that contrast with its bright yellow stamens. The plant has a short, stout stem with palmate leaves that are deeply lobed. It blooms in late spring to early summer, producing clusters of showy flowers.

Anemia nigerica is adapted to moist environments and is often found in marshy areas or along the edges of water bodies.
