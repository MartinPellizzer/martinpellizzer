Alstroemeria × zebrina

Alstroemeria × zebrina, commonly known as the Zebra lily, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Alstroemeriaceae family.

It is native to South America and is characterized by its striking, colorful, and often variegated foliage. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, yellow, and white, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It is a perennial herb that thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
