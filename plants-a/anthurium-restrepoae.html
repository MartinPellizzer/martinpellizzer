Anthurium restrepoae

Anthurium restrepoae, commonly known as the Restrepo's anthurium, is a striking tropical plant native to Colombia.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its unique inflorescence composed of a spadix and a spathe. The plant features large, heart-shaped leaves with a glossy green surface and prominent veins. Its flowers are typically green or white, with a spadix that can bear both male and female flowers.

Anthurium restrepoae is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated as a houseplant.
