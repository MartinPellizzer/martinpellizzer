Antholyza hirta

Antholyza hirta, commonly known as hairy antholyza, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small shrub that typically grows in dry, rocky habitats. The plant features narrow, hairy leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters. It is often found in coastal and inland regions with well-drained soils.

Antholyza hirta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
