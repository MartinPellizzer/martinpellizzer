Aristida perniciosa

Aristida perniciosa, commonly known as the purple threeawn, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a member of the grass family (Poaceae) and is characterized by its tufted growth habit and slender, erect stems. Aristida perniciosa is often found in disturbed areas and is adapted to arid environments.
