Anoectochilus lowii

Anoectochilus lowii, commonly known as the golden orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its rhizomatous growth and produces small, greenish-white flowers with a distinctive yellow lip. The plant thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found in forest undergrowth. It has lance-shaped leaves that are usually clustered at the base.

Anoectochilus lowii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional medicine.
