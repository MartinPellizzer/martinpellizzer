Anthemis dumetorum

Anthemis dumetorum, commonly known as common yarrow, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its finely divided, feathery leaves and clusters of small, yellowish flowers. Native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa, it thrives in a variety of habitats, including meadows and disturbed soils. The plant is often found in dry, sunny locations and is known for its aromatic, sometimes slightly bitter scent.

It has been used historically in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
