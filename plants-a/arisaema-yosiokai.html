Arisaema yosiokai

Arisaema yosiokai, commonly known as the Yosioka arrowhead, is a rare and ornamental plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its unique inflorescence consisting of a spadix surrounded by a spathe. The plant features heart-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green, often with a waxy texture. It thrives in shady, moist environments and is often found in forested areas.

Due to its rarity and aesthetic appeal, it is highly valued in botanical gardens and conservation efforts.
