Avoira conanam

Avoira conanam, commonly known as the golden flame tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. The plant has compound leaves composed of several oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Avoira conanam is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and public spaces.
