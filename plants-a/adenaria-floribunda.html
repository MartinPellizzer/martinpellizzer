Adenaria floribunda

Adenaria floribunda, commonly known as the clustered lady's tresses, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its dense, spike-like inflorescence. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are often clustered in dense groups. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests or rocky areas.

Adenaria floribunda is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
