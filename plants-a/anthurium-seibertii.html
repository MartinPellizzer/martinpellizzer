Anthurium seibertii

Anthurium seibertii, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are deep green with a reddish tinge. The plant produces vibrant orange spadices, which are the modified leaves that enclose the true flowers. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium seibertii is popular as a decorative plant due to its ornamental foliage and unique floral structure.
