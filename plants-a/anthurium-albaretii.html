Anthurium albaretii

Anthurium albaretii, commonly known as the white anthurium, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically bright green with a waxy texture. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-shaped inflorescences surrounded by a showy spathe, often in shades of white or pale pink. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium albaretii is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
