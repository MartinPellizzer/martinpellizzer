Alternanthera sessilis var. amoena

Alternanthera sessilis var. amoena, commonly known as the red-stemmedAlternanthera, is a striking aquatic plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red stems and lance-shaped leaves that often display a deep green to reddish hue. This variety thrives in freshwater environments and is popular in aquariums for its ornamental value. It prefers moderate to high light conditions and can grow in both submerged and floating forms.

Its compact growth habit makes it an excellent choice for foreground or midground planting in planted tanks.
