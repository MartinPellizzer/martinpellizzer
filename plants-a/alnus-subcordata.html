Alnus subcordata

It belongs to the Betulaceae family and is characterized by its alternate, simple, and ovate leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in catkins, which are typically wind-pollinated. Its seeds are enclosed in small, woody capsules that release seeds when mature.
