Angraecum compressicaule

Angraecum compressicaule, commonly known as the compressed-stem orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stem and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers with long, slender petals and a prominent lip. It is a lithophyte, typically growing on rock surfaces or in crevices, and thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Angraecum compressicaule is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
