Acer Pictum Var. Mono

Acer pictum var. mono is a strikingly beautiful maple tree known for its vibrant red leaves. It is a variant of the striped maple, native to the eastern United States. This tree thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forested areas. Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively rare in the wild.
