Astronium lecointei var. tomentosum

Astronium lecointei var. tomentosum, commonly known as the velvet tree, is a species native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its smooth, waxy trunk covered in a dense layer of fine, velvety hair, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in terminal panicles. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and elliptic in shape, with a glossy surface.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine by indigenous communities.
