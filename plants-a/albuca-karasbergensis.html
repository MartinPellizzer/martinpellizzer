Albuca karasbergensis

Albuca karasbergensis, commonly known as the Karasberg albuca, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces tall, branched stems that bear small, white to pale yellow flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens.

Due to its ornamental value and unique appearance, it is often cultivated by succulent enthusiasts.
