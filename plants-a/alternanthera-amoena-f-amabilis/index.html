Alternanthera Amoena F. Amabilis

Red alternanthera, scientifically known as Alternanthera amoena f. amabilis, is a strikingly beautiful flowering plant native to South America. It is prized for its vibrant red foliage and clusters of tiny, showy flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. This plant thrives in moist, well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardeners and landscapers. Its ornamental value extends beyond its appearance, as it also attracts pollinators like bees and butterflies. With proper care, red alternanthera can become a standout feature in both indoor and outdoor settings.
