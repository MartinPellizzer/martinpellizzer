Actinostemon glabrescens var. tenuifolius

Actinostemon glabrescens var. tenuifolius, commonly known as the narrow-leaved actinostemon, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features slender, upright stems that bear opposite, linear leaves with a smooth, glabrous surface. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in dense, terminal clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
