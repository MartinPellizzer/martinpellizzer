Albizia odoratissima var. subhedarii

Albizia odoratissima var. subhedarii, commonly known as the Chinese mimosa or sweet acacia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant is noted for its fragrant, fluffy yellow flowers that bloom in spring, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for ornamental gardening.

Its rapid growth and adaptability make it a popular choice for erosion control and landscape planting.
