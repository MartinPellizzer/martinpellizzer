Astrotricha ledifolia

Astrotricha ledifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leafed aster.

It is a herbaceous perennial native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. The plant features small, blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are narrow and often have a silvery or grayish underside.

Astrotricha ledifolia is adapted to rocky and well-drained soils, making it a hardy species in arid environments.
