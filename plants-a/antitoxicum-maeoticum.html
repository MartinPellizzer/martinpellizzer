Antitoxicum maeoticum

Antitoxicum maeoticum, commonly known as the Crimean bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the Black Sea region.

It is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers and lance-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, sandy soils along coastal areas and wetlands. It belongs to the Campanulaceae family, which includes other bellflowers.

Due to its habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species in its native range.
