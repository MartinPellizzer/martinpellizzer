Amomum citratum

Amomum citratum, commonly known as the citron ginger or lemon ginger, is a perennial herb native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes other aromatic plants like ginger and turmeric. Amomum citratum is valued for its aromatic properties and is used in traditional medicine and as a spice.
