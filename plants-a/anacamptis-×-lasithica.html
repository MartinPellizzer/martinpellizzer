Anacamptis × lasithica

Anacamptis × lasithica, commonly known as the Lasithi orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to the island of Crete.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its tall, erect stems and clusters of fragrant, violet-colored flowers. The plant typically blooms in late spring to early summer, producing showy, spurred orchid flowers that are attractive to pollinators. It thrives in sunny, well-drained habitats such as rocky slopes and grasslands.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
