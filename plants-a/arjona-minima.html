Arjona minima

Arjona minima, commonly known as the dwarf arjona, is a small evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It typically grows to a height of less than one meter, featuring dense, leathery leaves that are elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. Its compact size and attractive foliage make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Arjona minima thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate to bright light for optimal growth.
