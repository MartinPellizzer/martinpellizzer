Aster pleiocephalus

Aster pleiocephalus, commonly known as the showy aster, is a perennial flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads composed of ray and disk flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet and features vibrant blue to purple flower heads that bloom in late summer to early fall. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Aster pleiocephalus is well adapted to moist, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade.
