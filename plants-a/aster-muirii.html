Aster muirii

Aster muirii, commonly known as the Muir's aster, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of central Chile.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads and diverse species. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in high-altitude environments. Its flowers are usually blue to purple in color, with ray florets surrounding a central disk.

Aster muirii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitat.
