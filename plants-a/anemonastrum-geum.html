Anemonastrum geum

Anemonastrum geum, commonly known as the Chinese bellflower, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Anemonastrum geum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in a variety of garden conditions.
