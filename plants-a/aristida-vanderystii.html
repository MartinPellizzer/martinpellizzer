Aristida vanderystii

Aristida vanderystii, commonly known as Vanderyst's grass, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family Poaceae.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
