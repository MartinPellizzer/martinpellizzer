Astrochlaena ledermannii

Astrochlaena ledermannii, commonly known as the Ledermann's aster, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its flowers are usually small and yellow, with a distinctive central disc surrounded by ray florets.

Astrochlaena ledermannii is often found in fynbos vegetation, contributing to the biodiversity of its native habitat.
