Argyrolobium speciosum

Argyrolobium speciosum, commonly known as the silver globular pea, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive silver-gray foliage and spherical, clustered flowers that range from white to pale pink. The plant belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and it has a bushy growth habit. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and botanical collections for its unique appearance.
