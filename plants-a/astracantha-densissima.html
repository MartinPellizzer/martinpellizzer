Astracantha densissima

Astracantha densissima, commonly known as the dense thorned milkweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its hairy, upright stems and clusters of small, greenish-yellow flowers. The plant produces a milky sap that is toxic to many animals, including mammals and birds. Its leaves are opposite, oval-shaped, and often have a waxy coating.

Astracantha densissima thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, making it a hardy species adapted to dry conditions.
