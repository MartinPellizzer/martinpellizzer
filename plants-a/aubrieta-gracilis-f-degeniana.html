Aubrieta gracilis f. degeniana

Aubrieta gracilis f. degeniana, commonly known as the Degen's slender aubrieta, is a compact, low-growing perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, typically in clusters along the stems. The plant has finely divided, silvery-gray foliage that adds visual interest throughout the growing season. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and alpine settings.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in colder climates.
