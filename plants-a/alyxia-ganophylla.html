Alyxia ganophylla

Alyxia ganophylla, commonly known as the red-leaf alyxia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive red or orange-red leaves, which are often clustered at the base of the plant. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, arranged in clusters. Alyxia ganophylla thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for horticultural use in shaded garden settings.
