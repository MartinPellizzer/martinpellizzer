Androstoma empetrifolium

Androstoma empetrifolium, commonly known as the rock rose, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its dense, shrub-like growth habit and produces showy, pink or purple flowers. The plant has small, leathery leaves that are adapted to withstand arid conditions. It thrives in rocky or well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas.

Androstoma empetrifolium is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in harsh environments.
