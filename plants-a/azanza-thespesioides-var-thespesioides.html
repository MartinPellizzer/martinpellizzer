Azanza thespesioides var. thespesioides

Azanza thespesioides var. thespesioides, commonly known as the Thai hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height, characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and showy, five-petaled flowers. The flowers are typically bright red or orange, with a prominent yellow center, and bloom during the summer months. It prefers warm, humid climates and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

The plant is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
