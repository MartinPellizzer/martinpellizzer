Anathallis microtis

Anathallis microtis, commonly known as the small-lobed spider orchid, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the rainforests of Queensland, Australia.

The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments and relies on specific mycorrhizal fungi for nutrient uptake. Due to habitat loss, Anathallis microtis is considered endangered and is protected under Australian conservation laws.
