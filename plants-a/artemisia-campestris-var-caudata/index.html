Artemisia Campestris Var. Caudata

Artemisia campestris var. caudata is a hardy perennial plant known for its feathery foliage and aromatic qualities. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders. This variant of Artemisia is distinguished by its long, slender leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. Its ability to tolerate drought and poor soil conditions makes it a versatile addition to many landscapes. The plant's unique look and low maintenance requirements have made it a favorite among gardeners and landscapers alike.
