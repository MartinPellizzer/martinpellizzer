Aralia elata f. rotundata

Aralia elata f. rotundata, commonly known as the rounded-leaf aralia, is a variant of the Japanese aralia, characterized by its broad, rounded leaves.

This plant is native to Japan and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 6 meters. The leaves are usually dark green and have a smooth texture, while the plant produces small, white flowers in umbels.

It thrives in partial shade to full sun and is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for gardens.
