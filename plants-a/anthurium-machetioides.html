Anthurium machetioides

Anthurium machetioides, commonly known as the velvet leaf anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are velvety to the touch. The plant produces bright red spathes that enclose small, inconspicuous flowers. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Anthurium machetioides is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental foliage.
