Acer Campbellii Subsp. Wilsonii

Acer campbellii subsp. wilsonii is a rare and striking member of the maple family known for its vibrant foliage. Native to the mountainous regions of China, this subspecies thrives in cool, moist environments with well-drained soil. Its leaves turn brilliant shades of red and orange in the fall, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening. Despite its beauty, it is relatively uncommon in cultivation due to its specific growing requirements. This unique plant offers a glimpse into the diverse world of maple species and their ecological significance.
