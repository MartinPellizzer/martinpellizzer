Anthoshorea ochracea

Anthoshorea ochracea, commonly known as the yellow sandwort, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of southern Africa.

It is a small, herbaceous annual that typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils. The plant features simple, opposite leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters. Its botanical characteristics include a compact growth habit and a high tolerance for drought conditions.

Anthoshorea ochracea is often found in disturbed soils and is adapted to thrive in harsh, nutrient-poor environments.
