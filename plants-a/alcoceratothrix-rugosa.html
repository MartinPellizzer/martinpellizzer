Alcoceratothrix rugosa

Alcoceratothrix rugosa, commonly known as the rough-hairy orchid, is a species of flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its hairy, rough-textured leaves and distinctive floral structure. Native to South America, particularly Brazil, this orchid thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that bloom during the warmer months.

Its unique morphology and habitat preferences make it an interesting subject for botanical study.
