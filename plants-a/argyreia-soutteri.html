Argyreia soutteri

Argyreia soutteri, commonly known as the velvet tassel plant, is a climbing shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage and clusters of small, white, fragrant flowers. The plant produces distinctive, showy, red or orange berries that are often used in ornamental gardening. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Due to its attractive appearance, it is often cultivated for decorative purposes in gardens and landscapes.
