Alchemilla pickwellii

Alchemilla pickwellii, commonly known as Pickwell's lady's mantle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of western North America.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves that are covered in a dense layer of fine, silky hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in clusters during the summer months. Its leaves are often used in traditional herbal medicine for their purported soothing properties.

Alchemilla pickwellii thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or alpine habitats.
