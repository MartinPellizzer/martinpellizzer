Aerides emericii

Aerides emericii, commonly known as the Emerald Orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and strikingly green foliage, which gives it its common name. The plant produces fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers with a distinctive spurred lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Aerides emericii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in a home setting.
