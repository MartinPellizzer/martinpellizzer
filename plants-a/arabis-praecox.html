Arabis praecox

Arabis praecox, commonly known as early rockcress, is a flowering plant in the Brassicaceae family.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, typically found in rocky or gravelly soils. The plant produces small, white or pink flowers that bloom early in the spring. Its leaves are alternate, linear, and often covered in fine hairs.

Arabis praecox is often used in rock gardens due to its hardy nature and ornamental value.
