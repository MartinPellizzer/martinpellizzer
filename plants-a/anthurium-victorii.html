Anthurium victorii

Anthurium victorii, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped spathe that ranges from deep red to orange in color. The plant produces small, green spathes that enclose its spadix, which bears tiny, inconspicuous flowers. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Its unique foliage and colorful blooms make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
