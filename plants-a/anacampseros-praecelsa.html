Anacampseros praecelsa

Anacampseros praecelsa, commonly known as the tall stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-gray in color, adapted to store water in arid environments. The plant produces tall, branched stems with small, star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of yellow and orange. It thrives in well-draining soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a desirable addition to xeriscapic landscapes.
