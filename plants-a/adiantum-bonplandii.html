Adiantum bonplandii

Adiantum bonplandii, commonly known as the Bonpland's maidenhair fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Adiantaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, bipinnate fronds that are typically light green with a silvery underside. Native to Central and South America, this plant thrives in moist, shaded environments. Its rhizomes are often covered in a dense, silky layer of hair, contributing to its common name.

Adiantum bonplandii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
