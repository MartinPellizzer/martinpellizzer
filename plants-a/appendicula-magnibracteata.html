Appendicula magnibracteata

Appendicula magnibracteata, commonly known as the broad-bract appendicula, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

It belongs to the family Montiaceae and is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense foliage. Appendicula magnibracteata is often found in rocky or gravelly soils at elevations above 3,000 meters.
