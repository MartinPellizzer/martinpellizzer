Attalea crassispatha

Attalea crassispatha, commonly known as the Amazonian palm or the giant palm, is a tall, slender palm native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its large, pinnate leaves that can reach lengths of up to 6 meters. The plant produces large, yellowish-white flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its fruit is a large, fibrous drupe that contains a single seed.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and has ecological importance in its native habitat.
