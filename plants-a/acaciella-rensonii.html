Acaciella rensonii

Acaciella rensonii, commonly known as the Renson's acacia, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It is a small, shrub-like species that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are bipinnate, consisting of multiple pairs of small, feather-like leaflets.
