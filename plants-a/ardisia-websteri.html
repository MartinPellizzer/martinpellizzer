Ardisia websteri

Ardisia websteri, commonly known as the Webster's evergreen ardisia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of Indonesia and the Philippines. Ardisia websteri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
