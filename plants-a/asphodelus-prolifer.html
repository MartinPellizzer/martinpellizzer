Asphodelus prolifer

Asphodelus prolifer, commonly known as the clustered asphodel, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asparagaceae.

It is native to regions of southern Europe and North Africa, where it thrives in dry, rocky habitats. The plant produces tall, erect stems with narrow, grass-like leaves and clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant is often used in ornamental gardening due to its resilience and attractive floral display.
