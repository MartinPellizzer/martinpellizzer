Aster welshii

Aster welshii, commonly known as Welsh aster, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of western North America.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in open, rocky habitats at high elevations. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of small, blue to purple flowers in the fall. It is part of the Asteraceae family, known for its composite flower heads.

Welsh aster is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in alpine ecosystems.
