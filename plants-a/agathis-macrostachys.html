Agathis macrostachys

Agathis macrostachys, commonly known as the giant balsa tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the western Pacific islands.

It belongs to the family Araucariaceae and is characterized by its tall, straight trunk and large, feather-like leaves. The tree produces large, cone-like fruits that contain winged seeds, aiding in wind dispersal. Its wood is lightweight and has been historically valued for its use in model building and other crafts.
