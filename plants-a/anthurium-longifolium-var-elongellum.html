Anthurium longifolium var. elongellum

Anthurium longifolium var. elongellum, commonly known as the elongated anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 inches in length, with a deep green coloration and prominent veins. The plant produces striking spadices, which are often red or orange, surrounded by a showy white spathe. It thrives in high humidity and indirect light, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique foliage and vibrant inflorescences make it a standout ornamental plant.
