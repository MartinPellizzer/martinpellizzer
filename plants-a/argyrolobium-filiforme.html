Argyrolobium filiforme

Argyrolobium filiforme, commonly known as the slender silver globular veldt, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in grassland habitats, often in areas with well-drained soils. The plant features small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces small, spherical seed pods that contain numerous seeds.

Argyrolobium filiforme is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
