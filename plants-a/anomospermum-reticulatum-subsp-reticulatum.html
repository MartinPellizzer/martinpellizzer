Anomospermum reticulatum subsp. reticulatum

Anomospermum reticulatum subsp.

reticulatum, commonly known as the reticulated ghost tree, is a shrub or small tree native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its distinctive reticulate (net-like) pattern on its bark and leaves.

The plant has small, clustered leaves and produces yellowish-green flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky soils.

Its unique appearance and adaptability make it a notable species in its native habitat.
