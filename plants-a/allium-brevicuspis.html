Allium brevicuspis

Allium brevicuspis, commonly known as the short-bract onion, is a species of flowering plant in the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is native to southern Europe and western Asia, typically found in grasslands and rocky soils. The plant produces small, bulbous stems with narrow, grass-like leaves. Its flowers are clustered in umbels, featuring white or pale purple petals.
