Aloe deltoideodonta var. typica

Aloe deltoideodonta var. typica, commonly known as the deltoid leaf aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy, sword-shaped leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow-orange flowers during the summer months. Its leaves contain a gel-like substance that is often used for its soothing properties.

It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.
