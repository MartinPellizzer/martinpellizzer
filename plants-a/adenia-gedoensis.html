Adenia gedoensis

Adenia gedoensis, commonly known as the East African milk bush, is a flowering plant native to eastern Africa.

It belongs to the family Passifloraceae and is characterized by its woody stems and large, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically greenish-white with prominent stamens. It is adapted to grow in dry, rocky habitats and is often found in savanna and woodland areas.

Adenia gedoensis is also known for its latex-like sap, which can be toxic and is used in traditional medicine.
