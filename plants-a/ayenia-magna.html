Ayenia magna

Ayenia magna, commonly known as the large ayenia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features showy, yellow-orange ray flowers surrounding a central disk of smaller florets. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and open woodlands.

Ayenia magna is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting pollinators.
