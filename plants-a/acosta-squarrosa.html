Acosta squarrosa

Acosta squarrosa, commonly known as the woolly acosta, is a flowering plant native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly covering on its stems and leaves, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. It thrives in high-altitude environments with cool temperatures and well-drained soils.

Due to its unique morphology and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and plant enthusiasts.
