Alsophila elongata

Alsophila elongata, commonly known as the slender tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate fronds that can reach several meters in length. The plant has a distinctive crown of feathery fronds that give it a graceful, elegant appearance. Alsophila elongata thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests and along stream banks.

Its growth is slow, and it can live for many decades, making it a long-lived component of its ecosystem.
