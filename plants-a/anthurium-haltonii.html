Anthurium haltonii

Anthurium haltonii, commonly known as the Haltonii Philodendron, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are often a deep green with a glossy texture. The plant produces small, spadix-like inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, which can range from white to red or purple. It prefers bright, indirect light and consistently moist, well-draining soil.

Anthurium haltonii is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
