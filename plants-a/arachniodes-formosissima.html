Arachniodes formosissima

Arachniodes formosissima, commonly known as the Taiwan spider fern, is a striking evergreen fern native to Southeast Asia.

It features large, leathery, deeply divided fronds that give it a distinctive, almost spider-like appearance. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical forests. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense mats.

The fronds are typically dark green with a waxy texture, and they can grow up to several feet in length.
