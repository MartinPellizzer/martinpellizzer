Arthrosia capillaris

Arthrosia capillaris, commonly known as the slender leaf orchid, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, grass-like leaves and small, clustered orchid-like flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests or wetlands. Its botanical features include a rhizomatous root system and a unique inflorescence structure.

Arthrosia capillaris is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, where it thrives in humid conditions.
