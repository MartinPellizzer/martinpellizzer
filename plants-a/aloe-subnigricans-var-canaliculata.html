Aloe subnigricans var. canaliculata

Aloe subnigricans var. canaliculata, commonly known as the canyon aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, upright stems with narrow, fleshy leaves that are often blue-green in color. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with clusters of yellowish-green flowers. Its leaves have a distinctive ribbed structure and are covered in small, raised bumps called tubercles.

It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves to survive dry conditions.
