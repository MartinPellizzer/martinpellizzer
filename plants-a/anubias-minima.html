Anubias minima

Anubias minima, commonly known as the miniature anubias, is a compact aquatic plant native to Africa.

It features broad, dark green leaves that are typically ovate to elliptical in shape. This plant is well adapted to low-light conditions and can thrive in a variety of water conditions. It is often used in aquariums for its aesthetic appeal and ability to provide shelter for small aquatic creatures.

Anubias minima is also known for its slow growth rate and ease of care, making it a popular choice for beginner aquarists.
