Adenanthos meisneri var. velutinus

Adenanthos meisneri var. velutinus, commonly known as the velvet leaf adenanthos, is a shrub native to southern Australia.

It is characterized by its soft, velvety foliage, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens, though it requires careful management to prevent it from becoming invasive.
