Anisachne gracilis

Anisachne gracilis, commonly known as slender flatweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the grass family (Poaceae) and is characterized by its slender, creeping growth habit. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish or brownish in color. Its leaves are narrow, flat, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Anisachne gracilis is often found in moist soils along stream banks and in wetlands, where it plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for small organisms.
