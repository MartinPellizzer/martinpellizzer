Aster willkommii

Aster willkommii, commonly known as the Willkomm's aster, is a flowering plant native to the high altitudes of South America, particularly in the Andes.

It is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that bloom in shades of purple and white, typically during the summer months. The plant produces small, cylindrical seed heads that contain numerous tiny seeds, aiding in its dispersal. Aster willkommii thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often growing in rocky or alpine environments.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for both ecological studies and horticultural cultivation.
