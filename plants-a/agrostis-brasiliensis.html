Agrostis brasiliensis

Agrostis brasiliensis, commonly known as Brazilian bentgrass, is a grass species native to South America.

It is a low-growing perennial plant that typically forms dense mats on the ground. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetland or marshy areas.

Botanically, it is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit and is adapted to humid tropical climates.
