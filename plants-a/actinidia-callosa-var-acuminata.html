Actinidia callosa var. acuminata

Actinidia callosa var. acuminata, commonly known as the fuzzy actinidia or the Chinese gooseberry, is a deciduous vine native to China.

It is characterized by its hairy, ovate leaves and yellowish-green flowers that appear in early summer. The plant produces small, green berries that mature to a bright red color in late summer. These berries are edible and have a tart flavor, often used in jams and beverages.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various soil types.
