Aloe deltoideodonta var. brevifolia

Aloe deltoideodonta var. brevifolia, commonly known as the short-leaved aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, fleshy leaves that are shorter and broader compared to other aloe species, often with a bluish-green hue. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers in terminal spikes, which bloom during the warmer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique morphology and hardy nature make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
