Anthurium oreophilum

Anthurium oreophilum, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a reddish or purple tinge. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-shaped inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, often in shades of red, orange, or yellow. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Anthurium oreophilum is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
