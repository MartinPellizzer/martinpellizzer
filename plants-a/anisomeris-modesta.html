Anisomeris modesta

Anisomeris modesta, commonly known as the desert milkweed, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its small, showy pink or purple flowers that bloom in the spring and summer. The plant has a low, spreading growth habit with hairy stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in sandy or rocky soils.

Anisomeris modesta is adapted to withstand drought and high temperatures, making it a resilient species in desert ecosystems.
