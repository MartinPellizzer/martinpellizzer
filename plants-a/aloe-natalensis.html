Aloe natalensis

Aloe natalensis, commonly known as Natal aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a waxy coating. The plant produces tall, branched flower stalks that bear yellow-orange flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Aloe natalensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and has some traditional medicinal uses.
