Anotites tenerrimus

Anotites tenerrimus, commonly known as the slender waterwheel, is a perennial aquatic plant native to North America.

It typically grows in shallow freshwater environments such as ponds and slow-moving streams. The plant has whorled leaves that are narrow and strap-like, often with a bluish-green hue. Its flowers are small, yellow, and clustered in whorls around the stem, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Anotites tenerrimus plays a role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various invertebrates and fish.
