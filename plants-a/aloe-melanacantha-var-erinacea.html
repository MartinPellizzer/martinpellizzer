Aloe melanacantha var. erinacea

Aloe melanacantha var. erinacea, commonly known as the spiny aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color and covered with spines. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its rosette growth habit and ability to store water make it well adapted to arid environments.

The variety is distinguished by its dense, spiny margins and compact form.
