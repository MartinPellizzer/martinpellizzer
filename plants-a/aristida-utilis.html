Aristida utilis

Aristida utilis, commonly known as slender dropseed, is a perennial grass native to North America.

It typically grows in open, sunny areas such as prairies and disturbed soils. The plant has slender, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to stabilize soil.

Its botanical features make it a hardy and low-maintenance plant for landscaping.
