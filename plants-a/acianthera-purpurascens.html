Acianthera purpurascens

Acianthera purpurascens, commonly known as the purple-flowered orchid, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant purple or pinkish flowers. The plant typically grows in humid, tropical environments, often in shaded forest understories. Its roots are adapted for epiphytic growth, allowing it to thrive on tree trunks or branches.

The flowers are usually small and clustered, with intricate patterns that attract specific pollinators.
