Adelocaryum nebulicola

Adelocaryum nebulicola, commonly known as the cloud-spore plant, is a rare and enigmatic species native to high-altitude regions of South America.

The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in cloud forests where it receives consistent humidity. Its leaves are broad and waxy, adapted to retain moisture in its arid habitat.

Due to its elusive nature and limited distribution, Adelocaryum nebulicola remains a subject of ongoing botanical research.
