Anthurium penae

Anthurium penae, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive spadix and spathe. The plant features large, heart-shaped leaves that are dark green with a velvety texture. Its vibrant, crimson spadix is surrounded by a showy, white spathe, making it a popular ornamental plant.

Anthurium penae thrives in humid, warm environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.
