Acetosa lunaria

Acetosa lunaria, commonly known as silver vetch, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, hairy stems and small, white to pale blue flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has small, oval leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a silvery appearance. It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and coastal areas.

Acetosa lunaria is also known for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it an important plant in soil enrichment.
