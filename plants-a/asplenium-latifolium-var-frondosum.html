Asplenium latifolium var. frondosum

Asplenium latifolium var. frondosum, commonly known as the broad-leaf maidenhair fern, is a variant of the broad-leaf spleenwort.

It is a member of the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often on rock surfaces or in crevices. Its fronds are usually dark green and have a finely toothed margin.
