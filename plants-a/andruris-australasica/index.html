Andruris Australasica

Andruris australasica is a rare and enigmatic plant native to the unique ecosystems of Australia and New Zealand. It thrives in specific microhabitats, often found in shaded, moist environments with rich organic soil. Despite its limited distribution, it plays a vital role in its local ecosystem by supporting pollinators and contributing to biodiversity. Its delicate flowers and distinctive leaf structure make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists. However, habitat loss and climate change pose significant threats to its survival.
