Arundarbor ritchiei

Arundarbor ritchiei, commonly known as the silverleaf orchid, is a rare and striking plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Andes.

It is characterized by its elegant, silver-gray foliage and delicate, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of white and pale pink. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil. Its unique morphology makes it a subject of interest for botanists and horticulturists alike.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered endangered and is often cultivated in botanical gardens for conservation efforts.
