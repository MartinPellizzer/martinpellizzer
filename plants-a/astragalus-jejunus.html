Astragalus jejunus

Astragalus jejunus, commonly known as the slender milkvetch, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the western United States and parts of Canada.

It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. The plant features slender stems and small, compound leaves with a hairy texture. Its flowers are usually pink to purple, arranged in clusters, and bloom in early summer.

Astragalus jejunus is known for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional herbal remedies.
