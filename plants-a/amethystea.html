Amethystea

Amethystea, commonly known as the purple flame orchid, is a striking plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its vibrant purple and pink petals that resemble the color of amethyst. The plant features a slender, upright stem with clustered flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and glossy, providing a striking contrast to its colorful blooms.

Amethystea thrives in moist, well-drained soil and requires partial to full sunlight for optimal growth.
