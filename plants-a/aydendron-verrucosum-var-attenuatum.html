Aydendron verrucosum var. attenuatum

Aydendron verrucosum var. attenuatum, commonly known as the slender blistered wood, is a rare and specialized plant species characterized by its unique morphological features.

It typically exhibits a shrub-like growth form with woody stems covered in small, wart-like protrusions. The leaves are usually small, elliptical, and have a leathery texture, while the flowers are small and clustered in dense inflorescences. This variant is adapted to specific ecological niches, often found in rocky or disturbed soils.
