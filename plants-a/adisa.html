Adisa

Adisa, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the genus Saintpaulia and is characterized by its vibrant, velvety petals and compact rosette-shaped leaves. The plant thrives in humid, warm environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Adisa is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and ease of care.

Its flowers come in a wide range of colors, including pink, purple, white, and blue.
