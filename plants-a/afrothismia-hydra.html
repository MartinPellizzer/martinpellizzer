Afrothismia hydra

Afrothismia hydra, commonly known as the African hydra plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that store water, adapting to its arid environment. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the spring. It has a compact growth habit and is often grown as an ornamental plant.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
