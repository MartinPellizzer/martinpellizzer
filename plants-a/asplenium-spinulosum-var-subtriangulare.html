Asplenium spinulosum var. subtriangulare

Asplenium spinulosum var. subtriangulare, commonly known as the triangular spleenwort, is a fern species native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its compact, evergreen foliage. The leaves are typically triangular in shape, with a serrated or spinulose margin, giving it its scientific name. It thrives in shaded, moist environments, often found on rock surfaces or in forest understories.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is occasionally cultivated in shady gardens.
