Aristea elliptica

Aristea elliptica, commonly known as the narrowleaf aristea, is a perennial succulent native to South Africa.

It features compact, rosette-forming leaves that are narrow and elliptical, often with a bluish-gray hue. The plant produces small, tubular flowers in shades of pink or purple, typically blooming in summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its drought tolerance and ornamental appeal make it a popular choice for low-maintenance landscaping.
