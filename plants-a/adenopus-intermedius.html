Adenopus intermedius

Adenopus intermedius, commonly known as the African milk tree, is a member of the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is a shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa, characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and opposite, ovate leaves. The species is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it resilient to drought conditions.
