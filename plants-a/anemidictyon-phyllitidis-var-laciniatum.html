Anemidictyon phyllitidis var. laciniatum

Anemidictyon phyllitidis var. laciniatum, commonly known as the lace-leaf anemidictyon, is a rare and delicate fern species native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its finely divided, lacy fronds that give it a distinctive and ornamental appearance. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical forests. Its leaves are typically bioluminescent, a unique trait that aids in attracting nocturnal pollinators.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a conservation concern.
