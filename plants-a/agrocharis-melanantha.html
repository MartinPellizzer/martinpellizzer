Agrocharis melanantha

Agrocharis melanantha, commonly known as the dark-stemmed piper, is a flowering plant belonging to the Piperaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Central and South America, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant features slender, dark green leaves with a rough texture and produces small, greenish-white flowers clustered in spikes. Its fruit is a small, three-lobed berry that contains several seeds.

Agrocharis melanantha is often found in lowland rainforests and is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential medicinal properties.
