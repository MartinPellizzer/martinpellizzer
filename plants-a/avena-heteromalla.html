Avena heteromalla

Avena heteromalla, commonly known as the slender oat, is a grass species native to South America.

It is characterized by its slender, erect growth habit and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences with numerous florets, typical of the Poaceae family. Its flowers are usually greenish or pale yellow, and it is often found in grasslands and disturbed soils.

Avena heteromalla is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
