Astrochlaena delamereana

Astrochlaena delamereana, commonly known as the Delamere's aster, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in grasslands and rocky areas, preferring well-drained soils. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, with ray florets surrounding a central disk.

Astrochlaena delamereana is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive blooms.
