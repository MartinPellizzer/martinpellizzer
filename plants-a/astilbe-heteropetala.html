Astilbe heteropetala

Astilbe heteropetala, commonly known as the variable featherflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, plume-like inflorescences that bloom in shades of pink, red, and white. The plant features lance-shaped, compound leaves that are often covered in fine, silky hairs. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is well-suited for woodland gardens.

Astilbe heteropetala is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to add color to shaded landscapes.
