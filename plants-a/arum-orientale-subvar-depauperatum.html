Arum orientale subvar. depauperatum

Arum orientale subvar. depauperatum, commonly known as the slender arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its elongated, arrow-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy surface. The plant produces small, white flowers that are clustered in an inflorescence called a spadix, surrounded by a modified leaf called a spathe. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in swampy or wetland habitats.

Due to its unique morphology and ecological niche, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists alike.
