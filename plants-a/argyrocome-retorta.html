Argyrocome retorta

Argyrocome retorta, commonly known as the twisted silverleaf.

It is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of South America, particularly in Argentina and Chile. The plant is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and twisted, often branching stems. It produces small, white or pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months.

Argyrocome retorta is adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments with minimal water availability.
