Aperula polyantha

Aperula polyantha, commonly known as the Chinese bellflower, is a flowering plant native to China.

It is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that come in a variety of colors, including blue, purple, and white. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sun. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a smooth texture.

Aperula polyantha is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
