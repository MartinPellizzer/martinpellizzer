Achratinis

Achratinis, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 10 meters in height. The plant is characterized by its clusters of yellow, fragrant flowers that bloom in the spring. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and ovate with a smooth margin.

Achratinis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
