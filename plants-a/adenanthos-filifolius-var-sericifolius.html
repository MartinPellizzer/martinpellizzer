Adenanthos filifolius var. sericifolius

Adenanthos filifolius var. sericifolius, commonly known as the silky leaf adenanthos, is a shrub native to southern Australia.

It features narrow, linear leaves that are covered in a fine, silky white pubescence, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers in late winter to early spring, which are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to full sun, making it suitable for coastal and sandy environments.

Due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance, it is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
