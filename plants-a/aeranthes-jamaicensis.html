Aeranthes jamaicensis

Aeranthes jamaicensis, commonly known as the Jamaican orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Jamaica and parts of the Caribbean.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest understories. Its leaves are usually grass-like and arranged in a rosette pattern.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
