Aspidistra elatior var. attenuata

Aspidistra elatior var. attenuata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf evergreen aspidistra, is a variant of the aspidistra plant characterized by its elongated, narrow leaves.

It is a perennial evergreen plant native to southern China and is well adapted to low-light conditions. The leaves are typically dark green and have a glossy appearance, with a slightly wavy margin. It is valued for its ornamental foliage and ability to thrive in shaded environments.

This variety is often used in indoor gardening due to its resilience and aesthetic appeal.
