Artemisia oxycephala f. taiyangensis

Artemisia oxycephala f. taiyangensis, commonly known as the Taiyangensis Artemisia, is a variant of the common wormwood species.

It is a herbaceous plant native to China, characterized by its aromatic, silver-gray foliage. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flower heads that are typical of the Asteraceae family. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid regions.

Its botanical features make it a subject of interest in both traditional medicine and horticultural studies.
