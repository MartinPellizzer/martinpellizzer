Asplenosorus castaneoviridis

Asplenosorus castaneoviridis, commonly known as the golden-stem fern, is a species of fern native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its glossy, dark green fronds with a distinctive golden hue on the underside. The fronds are pinnately compound, with lance-shaped pinnae that give the plant its elegant, trailing appearance.
