Aloe rigens var. glabrescens

Aloe rigens var. glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, bluish-green leaves that are covered in a thin, smooth cuticle, distinguishing it from other aloe varieties. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow flowers during the summer months. It is adapted to arid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in dry conditions.
