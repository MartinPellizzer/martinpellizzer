Aristolochia buchtienii

Aristolochia buchtienii, commonly known as the Chinese birthwort, is a climbing vine native to China.

It features heart-shaped, waxy leaves and produces unique, pipe-shaped flowers that are often green or brownish in color. The plant is part of the Aristolochiaceae family and is known for its complex chemical composition. It contains various compounds, some of which have been studied for potential medicinal properties.

However, it is important to note that some species within this genus are toxic and should be handled with care.
