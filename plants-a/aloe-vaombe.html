Aloe vaombe

Aloe vaombe, commonly known as the Mozambique aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a waxy coating. The plant produces tall, branching inflorescences with yellow-orange flowers during the dry season. Its leaves contain a gel-like substance that is used in traditional medicine.

Aloe vaombe is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water to thrive.
