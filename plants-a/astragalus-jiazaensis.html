Astragalus jiazaensis

Astragalus jiazaensis, commonly known as the Jiaza Mountain milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to China, specifically found in the Xinjiang region.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its small, yellow flowers and compound leaves. The plant is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, showcasing resilience to drought and extreme temperatures.

Astragalus jiazaensis plays a role in local ecosystems by contributing to soil stabilization and providing nectar for pollinators.
