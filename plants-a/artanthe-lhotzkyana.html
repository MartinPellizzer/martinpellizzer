Artanthe lhotzkyana

Artanthe lhotzkyana, commonly known as the Tibetan artanthe, is a flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It belongs to the family Amaranthaceae and is characterized by its erect, branched stems and clustered inflorescences. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or white, with bracts that are often more showy. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, adapted to withstand cold and arid conditions.

Artanthe lhotzkyana is valued for its ornamental potential and is sometimes cultivated in alpine gardens.
