Angianthus strictus

Angianthus strictus, commonly known as the narrowleaf angianthus, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It features upright, narrow leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late spring. It prefers well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Its botanical characteristics make it an attractive addition to native plant collections.
