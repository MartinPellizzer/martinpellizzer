Asteriastigma

Asteriastigma, commonly known as the star-leaf aster, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are typically blue or purple, with ray florets that resemble petals.

Asteriastigma is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
