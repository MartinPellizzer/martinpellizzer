Andropogon integer

Andropogon integer, commonly known as tallgrass bluestem, is a perennial grass native to the central and eastern United States.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and narrow, bluish-green leaves that are often hairy. This plant is well adapted to prairie ecosystems and thrives in full sun with well-drained soils. It plays an important role in soil stabilization and provides habitat for various wildlife species.

Andropogon integer is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is used in native plant landscaping.
