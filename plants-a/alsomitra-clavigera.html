Alsomitra clavigera

Alsomitra clavigera, commonly known as the Christmas star or star of Bethlehem, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers that bloom in white or pink during the winter months. The plant produces small, fleshy fruits that are edible and contain seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, often growing in rocky or coastal areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
