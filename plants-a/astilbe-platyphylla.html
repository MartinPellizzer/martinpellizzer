Astilbe platyphylla

Astilbe platyphylla, commonly known as the broadleaf astilbe, is a herbaceous perennial native to eastern Asia.

It features dense, upright clusters of small, feathery flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant has heart-shaped, dark green leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery down. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is well-suited for woodland gardens.

Astilbe platyphylla is valued for its ornamental foliage and its ability to add texture and color to the garden landscape.
