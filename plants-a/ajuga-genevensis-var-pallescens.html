Ajuga genevensis var. pallescens

Ajuga genevensis var. pallescens, commonly known as the pale ajuga, is a low-growing perennial plant native to parts of Europe.

It features dark green, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, powdery coating. The plant produces clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of blue or purple during the spring. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Its foliage and flowers make it a desirable plant for ornamental gardening and ecological habitats.
