Alysicarpus muelleri var. clementii

Alysicarpus muelleri var. clementii, commonly known as the slender cowpea, is a tropical legume characterized by its climbing habit and trifoliate leaves.

It produces small, yellow flowers followed by elongated, cylindrical pods containing lens-shaped seeds. The plant is native to Australia and thrives in warm, humid climates with well-drained soils. It is often used as a forage crop due to its high nutritional value for livestock.

Its botanical features make it an important species in both agricultural and ecological contexts.
