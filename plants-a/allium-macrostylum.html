Allium macrostylum

Allium macrostylum, commonly known as the large-headed garlic.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Amaryllidaceae family. The plant produces spherical bulbous structures that are often used in traditional medicine. Its leaves are grass-like and can grow up to 30 centimeters in length.

The flowers are typically white or pale purple and bloom in clusters at the top of the stem.
