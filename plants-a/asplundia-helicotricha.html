Asplundia helicotricha

Asplundia helicotricha, commonly known as the spiral-haired asplundia, is a herbaceous plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Araceae, which includes many species with distinctive inflorescences. The plant is characterized by its spiral arrangement of leaves, giving it its common name. Its leaves are typically broad and have a waxy texture, while its flowers are small and clustered in a spadix, typical of the Araceae family.

Asplundia helicotricha is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or along stream banks.
