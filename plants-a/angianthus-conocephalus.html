Angianthus conocephalus

Angianthus conocephalus, commonly known as the desert lily, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant produces a distinctive, conical seed head that gives it its scientific name. It is adapted to survive in dry conditions with deep roots and succulent leaves.

Angianthus conocephalus is often found in rocky soils and is a symbol of resilience in harsh environments.
