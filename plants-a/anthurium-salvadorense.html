Anthurium salvadorense

Anthurium salvadorense, commonly known as the Salvadoran anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped spathe and spadix. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in the understory of tropical rainforests. Its leaves are large, glossy, and dark green, with a velvety texture.

Anthurium salvadorense is prized for its ornamental value and is cultivated for its striking foliage and unique floral structure.
