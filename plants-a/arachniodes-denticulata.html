Arachniodes denticulata

Arachniodes denticulata, commonly known as the toothed shield fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its finely toothed, lanceolate fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky crevices. Its rhizomes are creeping and produce multiple fertile and sterile fronds.
