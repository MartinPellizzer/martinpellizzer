Aglaonema nitidum var. helferi

Aglaonema nitidum var. helferi, commonly known as the Helferi Maranta, is a tropical evergreen plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features striking, waxy, dark green leaves with intricate patterns of white and cream, resembling the markings of a maranta. The plant is known for its compact growth habit and is often cultivated as a decorative indoor plant. It thrives in indirect light and requires moderate humidity and well-draining soil.

Its foliage remains vibrant throughout the year, making it a popular choice for interior landscaping.
