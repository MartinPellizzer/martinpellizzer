Aulomyrcia densa

Aulomyrcia densa, commonly known as the dense myrcia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in humid forests.

Aulomyrcia densa is valued for its aromatic properties and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
