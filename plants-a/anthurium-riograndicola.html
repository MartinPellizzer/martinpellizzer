Anthurium riograndicola

Anthurium riograndicola, commonly known as the Rio Grande anthurium, is a tropical plant native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are often dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-like inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, typically in shades of red or orange. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.

Anthurium riograndicola is popular as a decorative plant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
