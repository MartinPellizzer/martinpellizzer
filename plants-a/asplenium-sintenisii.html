Asplenium sintenisii

Asplenium sintenisii, commonly known as the Sintenisi's spleenwort, is a small, evergreen fern belonging to the Aspleniaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and have a smooth, glossy surface. This species is native to tropical and subtropical regions, particularly in parts of Southeast Asia and the Pacific Islands. Asplenium sintenisii thrives in moist, shaded environments, often growing on rocks or tree trunks.

Its unique morphology and ecological adaptability make it an interesting subject for botanical study.
