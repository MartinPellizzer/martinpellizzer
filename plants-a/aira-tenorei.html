Aira tenorei

Aira tenorei, commonly known as the Tuscan grass or Italian grass, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters, featuring slender, grass-like leaves that are often blue-green in color. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy and drought-tolerant species.

Aira tenorei is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its attractive appearance and low maintenance requirements.
