Arenaria Funiculata

Arenaria funiculata is a small flowering plant native to the rocky and alpine regions of Europe and Asia. It thrives in harsh environments, often growing on stone or in crevices where other plants struggle to survive. The plant is known for its delicate white or pink flowers that bloom in early spring. Its compact growth habit and resilience make it a popular choice for rock gardens and alpine displays. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively obscure in the broader botanical world.
