Aerangis brachyceras

Aerangis brachyceras, commonly known as the narrow-capped orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, upright growth habit and features a single, narrow-spurred flower with a distinctive, short, and narrow lip. The plant has a fibrous root system and thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories. Its flowers are typically pale green to yellowish in color, with a subtle fragrance.

Aerangis brachyceras is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in specialty orchid collections.
