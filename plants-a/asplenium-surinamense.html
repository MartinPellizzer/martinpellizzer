Asplenium surinamense

Asplenium surinamense, commonly known as the Surinam spleenwort, is a small, evergreen fern native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the family Aspleniaceae and is characterized by its compact, rosette-like growth habit. The plant has delicate, lance-shaped fronds that are typically dark green and covered in fine hairs. It thrives in shaded, moist environments, often growing on rocks or tree trunks.

Asplenium surinamense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant.
