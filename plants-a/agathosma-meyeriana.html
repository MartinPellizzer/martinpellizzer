Agathosma meyeriana

Agathosma meyeriana, commonly known as Cape geranium, is a succulent shrub native to the Western Cape region of South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, clustered flowers and fleshy, aromatic leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. It is known for its medicinal properties, with various parts used in traditional herbal medicine.

Its resilience to drought makes it a popular choice for xeriscaping in arid environments.
