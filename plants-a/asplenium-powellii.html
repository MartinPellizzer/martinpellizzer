Asplenium powellii

Asplenium powellii, commonly known as Powell's shield fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to the western United States.

It belongs to the family Aspleniaceae and is characterized by its compact, shield-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and rocky areas. Its rhizomes are short and produce dense clusters of leaves.

The fronds are usually dark green and have a leathery texture, making it a distinctive and hardy ornamental plant.
