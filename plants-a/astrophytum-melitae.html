Astrophytum melitae

Astrophytum melitae, commonly known as the Canary Island cactus or the star cactus, is a species of flowering plant in the family Cactaceae.

It is native to the Canary Islands and is characterized by its short, cylindrical stem covered in radial spines. The plant produces yellow flowers during the summer months, which are typically borne at the top of the stem. It is a slow-growing plant that thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.

Due to its unique appearance and hardiness, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
