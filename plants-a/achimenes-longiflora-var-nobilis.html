Achimenes longiflora var. nobilis

Achimenes longiflora var. nobilis, commonly known as the noble achimenes, is a tropical herbaceous plant native to South America.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, which bloom throughout the growing season. The plant has heart-shaped, dark green leaves and produces tuberous roots that allow it to survive periods of drought. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated as a decorative plant in gardens and greenhouses.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for floral arrangements and ornamental horticulture.
