Actinidia guilinensis

Actinidia guilinensis, commonly known as Guilin kiwifruit, is a deciduous vine native to China.

It belongs to the Actinidiaceae family and is closely related to the kiwifruit species. The plant produces small, edible berries that are typically green and have a fuzzy skin. It thrives in temperate climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

The botanical characteristics of Actinidia guilinensis make it an interesting subject for both horticultural and ecological studies.
