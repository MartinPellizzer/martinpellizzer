Albizia cauliflora

Albizia cauliflora, commonly known as the tropical albizia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its woody structure and compound leaves that are often pinnately arranged. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Albizia cauliflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its timber.
