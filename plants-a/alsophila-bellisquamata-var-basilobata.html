Alsophila bellisquamata var. basilobata

Alsophila bellisquamata var. basilobata, commonly known as the Basilobate Tree Fern, is a species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and a crown of feathery, compound fronds that give it a distinctive, elegant appearance. The plant's fronds are typically bright green and can grow quite long, adding a lush, tropical feel to its environment. It thrives in humid, shaded areas and is often found in forest understories.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
