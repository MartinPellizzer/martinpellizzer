Aberemoa furfuracea var. jonasiana

Aberemoa furfuracea var. jonasiana, commonly known as the Jonas's velvetleaf, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in a dense layer of fine, velvety hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are typically clustered in leaf axils. Its growth habit is herbaceous, with a sprawling or upright form depending on environmental conditions.

The species is often found in moist, shaded habitats such as forests and wetlands.
