Aconitum funckianum var. pubescens

Aconitum funckianum var. pubescens, commonly known as the pubescent monkshood, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe.

It is characterized by its striking blue to purple spurred flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has dark green, heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive veining pattern. It prefers moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.

Due to its toxic nature, it should be handled with care.
