Anthurium decursivum

Anthurium decursivum, commonly known as the arrowhead plant, is a tropical flowering plant native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, dark green leaves that are often glossy and have a distinctive arrowhead shape. The plant produces striking spadix inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, typically in shades of red, orange, or yellow. It thrives in humid, warm environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.

Anthurium decursivum is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
