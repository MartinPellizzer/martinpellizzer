Alstroemeria malmeana

Alstroemeria malmeana, commonly known as the Malme's lily, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Chile and Argentina.

It belongs to the family Alstroemeriaceae and is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that come in a variety of colors including red, yellow, and orange. The plant typically grows as a herbaceous perennial, featuring strap-like leaves and a tall, slender stem.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and as a cut flower.
