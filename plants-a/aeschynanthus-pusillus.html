Aeschynanthus pusillus

Aeschynanthus pusillus, commonly known as the dwarf wax plant, is a small, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically bright red or orange. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves that are opposite in arrangement, and it thrives in bright, indirect light. It is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.

Aeschynanthus pusillus is also known for its ability to bloom throughout the year under suitable conditions.
