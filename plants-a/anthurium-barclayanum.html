Anthurium barclayanum

Anthurium barclayanum, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a tropical flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces vibrant red spathes that enclose its spadix, which is covered in tiny, bright red flowers. Anthurium barclayanum thrives in humid, warm environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

It is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and unique floral structure.
