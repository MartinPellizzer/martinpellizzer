Apium leptophyllum var. latisectum

Apium leptophyllum var. latisectum, commonly known as the broad-leaved celery, is a variant of the celery plant characterized by its wide, flat leaves.

It belongs to the Apiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and vegetables. The plant typically grows in moist, temperate climates and is often found near water bodies. Its leaves are edible and have a mild, slightly sweet flavor, making it a valuable addition to culinary dishes.
