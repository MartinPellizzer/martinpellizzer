Apocynum macounii

Apocynum macounii, commonly known as Macoun's milkweed, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family, which includes many milkweed species. The plant is characterized by its woody stems and opposite, lance-shaped leaves. It produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in summer.

The plant contains milky sap, which is a common feature among members of its family.
