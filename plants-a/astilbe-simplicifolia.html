Astilbe simplicifolia

Astilbe simplicifolia, commonly known as white feather astilbe, is a herbaceous perennial native to East Asia.

It features finely divided, feathery leaves that create a delicate, lace-like appearance. The plant produces panicles of small, white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in partial to full shade and prefers moist, well-drained soil.

Astilbe simplicifolia is valued in gardens for its ornamental foliage and subtle floral display.
