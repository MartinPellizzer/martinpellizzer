Aloe morogoroensis

Aloe morogoroensis, commonly known as the East African aloe, is a succulent plant native to Tanzania.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a waxy texture. The plant produces tall, branched flower stalks that bear yellowish-green flowers. It is adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Its leaves contain a gel-like substance that has been used for various traditional and medicinal purposes.
