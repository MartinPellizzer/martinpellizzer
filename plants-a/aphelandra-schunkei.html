Aphelandra schunkei

Aphelandra schunkei, commonly known as the silver velvet plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves with a velvety texture and striking, pendulous flowers that range from yellow to orange in color. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. It is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and ability to purify indoor air.

Aphelandra schunkei is relatively low-maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardeners.
