Anisotome pilifera

Anisotome pilifera, commonly known as the hairy anisotome, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Apiaceae family, which includes carrots and parsley, and is characterized by its finely divided, fern-like leaves. Anisotome pilifera is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
