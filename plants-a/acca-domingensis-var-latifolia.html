Acca domingensis var. latifolia

Acca domingensis var. latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf apple or *Pitahaya blanca*, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Colombia and Ecuador.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its broad, ovate leaves and showy, pinkish-white flowers. The plant produces edible fruits that are typically white or pale yellow, with a sweet, delicate flavor.

It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and fruit.
