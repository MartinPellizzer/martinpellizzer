Anemone blanda

Anemone blanda, commonly known as the windflower, is a low-growing perennial plant native to Europe and western Asia.

It features delicate, cup-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple, typically blooming in early spring. The plant has basal leaves that are heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs, and its stems are usually short and hairy. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in woodland areas or along stream banks.

Anemone blanda is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in gardens for its early bloom and soft, delicate appearance.
