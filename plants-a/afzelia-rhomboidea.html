Afzelia rhomboidea

Afzelia rhomboidea, commonly known as the African ironwood, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, heavy wood that is highly valued for its durability and strength. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in panicles, followed by woody capsules containing seeds.

Afzelia rhomboidea is often found in dry, open woodlands and is known for its slow growth and long lifespan.
