Anthyllis subdinarica

Anthyllis subdinarica, commonly known as the Balkan broom.

It is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is native to the Balkan Peninsula. The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, yellowish flowers. Its leaves are trifoliate, consisting of three oval-shaped leaflets.
