Aristolochia kaempferi f. lineata

Aristolochia kaempferi f. lineata, commonly known as the Japanese arrowhead vine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, arrowhead-like leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. The plant produces distinctive, saddle-shaped flowers that are typically green or purple in color. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for tropical gardens and indoor spaces.
