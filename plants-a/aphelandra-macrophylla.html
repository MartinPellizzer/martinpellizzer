Aphelandra macrophylla

Aphelandra macrophylla, commonly known as the giant flame plant, is a striking tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, glossy, dark green leaves that can grow up to 3 feet in length. The plant produces vibrant, spathiflorous inflorescences with bright orange or red bracts that resemble flames. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Aphelandra macrophylla is often cultivated as a decorative plant for its dramatic foliage and long-lasting, showy flowers.
