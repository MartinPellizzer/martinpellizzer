Astelia Australiana

Astelia australiana is a striking plant native to the southern regions of New Zealand. It belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is known for its elegant, sword-like leaves. This species thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking a touch of the exotic. Despite its beauty, it remains a relatively obscure plant in the broader horticultural world.
