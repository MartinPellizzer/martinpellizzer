Aristolochia polyrrhizos

Aristolochia polyrrhizos, commonly known as the Chinese birthwort, is a flowering plant native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, hood-shaped flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with a rough texture and is often found in moist, shaded environments. It belongs to the Aristolochiaceae family and is known for its medicinal uses in traditional Chinese medicine.

However, it contains toxic compounds and should be handled with care.
