Athyrium habaense

Athyrium habaense, commonly known as the Haba fern, is a species of fern native to China.

It belongs to the family Athyriaceae and is characterized by its delicate, feather-like fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

The leaves are bi-directionally pinnate, with a distinctive appearance that makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
