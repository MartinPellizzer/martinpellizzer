Astragalus khokhrjakovii

Astragalus khokhrjakovii, commonly known as the Khokhrjakov's milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments, showcasing adaptations to dry conditions. It is known for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, contributing to soil fertility.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and potentially endangered species.
