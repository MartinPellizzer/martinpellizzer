Asteridea multiceps

Asteridea multiceps, commonly known as the starflower aster, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its showy, star-shaped flowers that typically bloom in shades of purple and white. The plant features alternate, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs. Native to temperate regions, it thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight.

Asteridea multiceps is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
