Anemone patens var. kryloviana

Anemone patens var. kryloviana, commonly known as the Kryloviana Anemone, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to eastern Europe.

It features showy, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has finely divided, fern-like leaves that are typically basal and hairy. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate, lace-like foliage and blooms.
