Artemisia × jaeggiana

Artemisia × jaeggiana, commonly known as wormwood.

It is a hybrid shrub native to the mountainous regions of Europe and western Asia. The plant is characterized by its aromatic, silver-gray foliage and small, yellowish-green flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in alpine and rocky habitats.

Artemisia × jaeggiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in herbal medicine.
