Anisopappus chinensis var. macrocephala

Anisopappus chinensis var. macrocephala, commonly known as the large-headed goldenweed, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy yellow composite flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and has narrow, linear leaves. It produces small, fluffy seeds that are dispersed by wind, aiding in its propagation.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
