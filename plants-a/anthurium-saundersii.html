Anthurium saundersii

Anthurium saundersii, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped, glossy green leaves and its vibrant red spadix surrounded by a white spathe. The plant thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light. It is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental value.

Anthurium saundersii requires well-draining soil and consistent moisture to flourish.
