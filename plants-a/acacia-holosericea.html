Acacia holosericea

Acacia holosericea, commonly known as the silky acacia, is a shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, often spiny stems and bipinnate leaves that are finely divided. The plant produces yellow flowers in globular inflorescences, which are followed by flat, linear pods. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often growing on rocky soils.

Acacia holosericea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
