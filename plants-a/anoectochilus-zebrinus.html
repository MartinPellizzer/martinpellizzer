Anoectochilus zebrinus

Anoectochilus zebrinus, commonly known as the zebra orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, strap-shaped leaves and striking, showy flowers that often feature a distinctive zebra-like pattern. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often near streams or in forest undergrowth. Its flowers are usually fragrant and bloom in summer, attracting various pollinators.

Anoectochilus zebrinus is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
