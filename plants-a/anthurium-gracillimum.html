Anthurium gracillimum

Anthurium gracillimum, commonly known as the slender anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive spatulate leaves and showy inflorescences. The plant typically features elongated, glossy green leaves with a prominent midrib and a narrow, pointed tip. Its flowers are composed of a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe, often in shades of white, pink, or red.

Anthurium gracillimum thrives in humid, warm environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.
