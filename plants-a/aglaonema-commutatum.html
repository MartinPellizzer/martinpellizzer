Aglaonema commutatum

Aglaonema commutatum, commonly known as the Chinese Evergreen, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves with striking white and silver variegation. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions, making it a popular houseplant. Its foliage is often used in ornamental horticulture for its aesthetic appeal.

Aglaonema commutatum is also valued for its air-purifying properties and ease of care.
