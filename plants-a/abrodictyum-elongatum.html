Abrodictyum elongatum

Abrodictyum elongatum, commonly known as the long-stemmed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces pendant flowers that are typically yellow or greenish-yellow in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil.

Abrodictyum elongatum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique floral structure.
