Amaryllis insignis

Amaryllis insignis, commonly known as the giant amaryllis, is a striking bulbous plant native to South America.

It produces large, showy flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, often with a distinctive yellow or green stripe down the center. The plant thrives in warm, sunny environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its foliage consists of broad, strap-like leaves that emerge from the base of the plant.

Amaryllis insignis is popular as a decorative plant due to its dramatic blooms and relatively easy care requirements.
