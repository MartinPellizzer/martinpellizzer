Aristolochia odoratissima var. glaziovii

Aristolochia odoratissima var. glaziovii, commonly known as the Glaziov's Dutchman's pipe, is a climbing vine native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its distinctive, tubular, heart-shaped leaves and its fragrant, pale green to white flowers. The plant belongs to the Aristolochiaceae family and is known for its unique floral morphology. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

The species is also noted for its medicinal properties, though it contains toxic compounds that require careful handling.
