Antheischima excelsa

Antheischima excelsa, commonly known as the towering flamebush, is a striking shrub or small tree native to certain regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem and clusters of bright red, tubular flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with a slightly waxy texture, and its bark is smooth and often reddish in color. Antheischima excelsa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.

It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it a hardy plant in suitable climates.
