Anthurium luzmariae

Anthurium luzmariae, commonly known as the Luzmaria anthurium, is a striking tropical plant native to Colombia.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its unique inflorescence consisting of a spadix and a spathe. The plant features broad, dark green, heart-shaped leaves that are glossy and textured. Its flowers are typically bright red or orange, with a spadix that is often shorter than the spathe.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in greenhouse environments.
