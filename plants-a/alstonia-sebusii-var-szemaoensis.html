Alstonia sebusii var. szemaoensis

Alstonia sebusii var. szemaoensis, commonly known as the Szemao alstonia, is a variant of the alstonia tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is a deciduous tree characterized by its smooth, grey bark and elongated, elliptical leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers followed by woody, capsule-like fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in lowland forests.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation areas.
