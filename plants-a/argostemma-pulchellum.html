Argostemma pulchellum

Argostemma pulchellum, commonly known as the velvetleaf arrowhead, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It belongs to the family Araceae, characterized by its unique inflorescence consisting of a spadix surrounded by a spathe. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves and produces small, greenish-white flowers. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Its ornamental value has led to its cultivation in some horticultural settings.
