Alternanthera bettzickiana f. spathulata

Alternanthera bettzickiana f. spathulata, commonly known as the broadleaf Alternanthera, is a striking aquatic plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves that often display vibrant red or pink hues, especially when exposed to strong light. The plant thrives in shallow, slow-moving waters and is often used in aquariums for its ornamental value. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow in color.

Its foliage makes it a popular choice for creating lush, colorful backgrounds in planted tanks.
