Amarenus patens

Amarenus patens, commonly known as the slender amaranth, is a flowering plant native to parts of South America.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family, which includes many species known for their vibrant flowers and edible seeds. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or yellowish in color.

The leaves are narrow and grass-like, contributing to its overall slender appearance.
