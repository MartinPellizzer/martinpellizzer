Aruncus sylvester var. astilboides

Aruncus sylvester var. astilboides, commonly known as the silverleaf aruncus, is a herbaceous perennial native to eastern North America.

It features tall, slender stems that bear clusters of small, white to pale pink flowers, which bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has finely divided, fern-like leaves that are often covered in a silvery or grayish hue, giving it its common name. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodlands and streamside areas.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to add texture and interest to shaded garden spaces.
