Aizoanthemum dinteri

Aizoanthemum dinteri, commonly known as the Dinter's ice plant, is a succulent species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, blue-green leaves that are covered in a fine, white powdery coating, known as a cuticle, which helps reduce water loss. The plant produces small, star-shaped yellow flowers during the winter months, adding a delicate floral display. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
