Avena preissia

Avena preissia, commonly known as the desert oat or Preiss' oat, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of North America.

It is a member of the grass family (Poaceae) and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences with numerous tiny florets, typically in shades of green or brown.
