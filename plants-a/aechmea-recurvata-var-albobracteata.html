Aechmea recurvata var. albobracteata

Aechmea recurvata var. albobracteata, commonly known as the white bract aechmea, is a bromeliad native to South America.

It is characterized by its rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves that form a central cup to collect rainwater. The plant produces striking inflorescences with bright red flowers surrounded by white bracts. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often used in ornamental gardening.

Its foliage and floral display make it a popular choice for tropical landscapes.
