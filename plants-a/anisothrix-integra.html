Anisothrix integra

Anisothrix integra, commonly known as the velvetleaf or velvetleaf hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its large, velvety leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a soft texture. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and yellow. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in moist, shaded environments.

Anisothrix integra is valued both for its ornamental appeal and its role in traditional medicine.
