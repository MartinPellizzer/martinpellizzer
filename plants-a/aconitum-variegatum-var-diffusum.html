Aconitum variegatum var. diffusum

Aconitum variegatum var. diffusum, commonly known as the variable monkshood, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its striking, hood-shaped blue to purple flowers. The plant features palmately compound leaves with a distinctive variegated pattern of green and white. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Due to its toxic alkaloids, it should be handled with care.
