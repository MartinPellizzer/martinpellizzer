Aralia wallichiana

Aralia wallichiana, commonly known as the Chinese angel's trumpets, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to the Himalayas.

It features compound leaves that are pinnately arranged, with each leaflet being ovate to lanceolate in shape. The plant produces clusters of white or pale yellow flowers that are often fragrant and resemble small trumpets. Its fruit is a red, fleshy berry that contains several seeds.

Aralia wallichiana is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and floral display.
