Astragalus skorniakowii

Astragalus skorniakowii, commonly known as the Skorniakov's milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Astragalus skorniakowii is also valued for its ecological role in stabilizing soil and supporting local wildlife.
