Aster woldenii

Aster woldenii, commonly known as the Wolden's aster, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and rocky slopes. Its flowers are usually purple to blue in color, with ray florets surrounding a central disk.

Aster woldenii is known for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
