Arenaria Michauxii

Arenaria michauxii is a low-growing flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of eastern North America. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in alpine or subalpine habitats. This species is known for its delicate, white or pinkish flowers that bloom in early spring. Its compact growth habit makes it well suited for rock gardens and shady woodland settings. Despite its beauty, Arenaria michauxii remains relatively uncommon and is often overlooked in favor of more showy wildflowers.
