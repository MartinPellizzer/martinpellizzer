Acer × bornmuelleri

Acer × bornmuelleri, commonly known as the red maple hybrid, is a deciduous tree that exhibits a range of ornamental features.

It is a natural hybrid between Acer rubrum (red maple) and Acer saccharum (sugar maple), resulting in a plant with unique genetic characteristics. The tree typically displays vibrant autumn foliage, ranging from red to orange and yellow, making it a popular choice for landscaping. It prefers moist, well-drained soils and partial to full sun, thriving in temperate climates.

Due to its hardiness and aesthetic appeal, Acer × bornmuelleri is widely cultivated in gardens and urban environments.
