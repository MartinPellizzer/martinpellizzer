Alocasia rapiformis

Alocasia rapiformis, commonly known as the arrow leaf alocasia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves that can grow up to 3 feet in length. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit and thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its foliage is often a deep green with a glossy texture.

Alocasia rapiformis is popular as a houseplant due to its striking appearance and air-purifying qualities.
