Albizia simeonis

Albizia simeonis, commonly known as the silky albizia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves and clusters of small, white to pale pink flowers. The plant produces flat, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas or disturbed sites.

Albizia simeonis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
