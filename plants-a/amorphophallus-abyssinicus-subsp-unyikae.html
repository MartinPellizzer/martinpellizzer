Amorphophallus abyssinicus subsp. unyikae

Amorphophallus abyssinicus subsp.

unyikae, commonly known as the Abyssinian giant arrowroot, is a tuberous plant native to Ethiopia. It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its large, fleshy underground tubers.

The plant produces a striking inflorescence with a spadix and a spathe, typical of the Araceae family. It thrives in humid, tropical environments and is often found in forested areas.

Its botanical features make it a unique and valuable species for both ecological and horticultural study.
