Aloe margaritifera var. maxima

Aloe margaritifera var. maxima, commonly known as the giant aloe or mountain aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a waxy coating. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing yellow or orange flowers. Its leaves are often used for their gel, which has medicinal properties.

It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.
