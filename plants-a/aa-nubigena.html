Aa nubigena

Aa nubigena, commonly known as the cloud fern, is a rare and elusive plant species native to the high-altitude regions of Madagascar.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds that resemble clouds, hence its common name. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is typically found in cloud forests. Aa nubigena has a unique reproductive system, producing spores rather than seeds, which contributes to its limited distribution.

Due to habitat loss and its reclusive nature, it is considered a vulnerable species.
