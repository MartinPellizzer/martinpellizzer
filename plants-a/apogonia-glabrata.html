Apogonia glabrata

Apogonia glabrata, commonly known as the smooth apogonia, is a unique flowering plant native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and distinctive, elongated leaves that are typically smooth and glabrous. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are often clustered in dense inflorescences. Its botanical significance lies in its role as a rare species within the Apogonaceae family.

Due to habitat loss, Apogonia glabrata is considered endangered and requires conservation efforts to ensure its survival.
