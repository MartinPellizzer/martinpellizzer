Anemone praecox

Anemone praecox, commonly known as the early anemone, is a herbaceous perennial native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features showy, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring, typically from April to May. The plant has basal leaves that are palmately lobed and covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Anemone praecox is often found in woodland areas and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
