Aganosma Cymosa Var. Elegans

Aganosma cymosa var. elegans is a striking member of the Rubiaceae family known for its delicate, fragrant flowers. Native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia, this plant thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its unique foliage and floral display make it a sought-after addition to both botanical gardens and private collections. The variety 'elegans' stands out for its refined appearance and subtle elegance. Cultivated with care, it offers a captivating blend of beauty and botanical interest.
