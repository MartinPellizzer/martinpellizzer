Anticharis linearis var. azurea

Anticharis linearis var. azurea, commonly known as the Azure Linear Caper, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and clusters of small, blue or violet flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or coastal areas. Its ornamental value comes from its delicate foliage and long-lasting blooms.

It is also valued for its resilience and low maintenance requirements.
