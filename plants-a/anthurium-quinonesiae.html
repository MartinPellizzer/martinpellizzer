Anthurium quinonesiae

Anthurium quinonesiae, commonly known as the Quinonesia anthurium, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its unique inflorescence consisting of a spadix and a spathe. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that can grow up to 30 centimeters in length. Its flowers are typically bright red or orange, with a central spadix that attracts pollinators.

Anthurium quinonesiae is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in humid, shaded environments.
