Aristolochia lawrenceae

Aristolochia lawrenceae, commonly known as the Mexican Dutchman's pipe, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its unique, pipe-shaped flowers that are typically greenish-yellow with a distinct, intricate pattern. The plant belongs to the Aristolochiaceae family and is known for its aromatic, somewhat spicy scent. Its leaves are heart-shaped and often have a velvety texture.

The species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental flowers and is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant.
