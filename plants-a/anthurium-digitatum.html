Anthurium digitatum

Anthurium digitatum, commonly known as the velvet leaf anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically deep green with a velvety texture. The plant produces striking spadix inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, which can range from red to orange. It thrives in humid, warm environments with bright, indirect light.

Anthurium digitatum is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and air-purifying qualities.
