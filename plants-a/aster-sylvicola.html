Aster sylvicola

Aster sylvicola, commonly known as the wood aster, is a perennial herb native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, wooded areas and has lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture. Its flowers are blue to purple in color and bloom in the late summer to early fall.

The wood aster is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting local pollinators.
