Azalea Multicolor

Azalea multicolor is a vibrant evergreen shrub known for its stunning array of flower colors. It thrives in acidic soils and partial shade, making it a popular choice for gardeners. The plant produces clusters of showy blooms that range from pink to red and even white. Its foliage remains lush throughout the year, adding year-round beauty to landscapes. Azalea multicolor is not only ornamental but also attracts pollinators like bees and butterflies.
