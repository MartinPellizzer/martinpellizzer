Arundarbor beecheyana

Arundarbor beecheyana, commonly known as the Silverleaf Beech, is a deciduous tree native to the temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

It is characterized by its broad, ovate leaves that turn a striking yellow in the fall. The tree produces small, inconspicuous green flowers in the spring, followed by edible, spherical fruits that are a food source for various wildlife. Its bark is smooth and light gray, becoming fissured with age.

Arundarbor beecheyana is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types.
