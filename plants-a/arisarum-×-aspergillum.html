Arisarum × aspergillum

Arisarum × aspergillum, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Araceae family.

It is characterized by its striking, dark green, glossy foliage with prominent veins. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers surrounded by a spadix, typical of the Araceae family. It thrives in low-light conditions and requires consistent moisture and high humidity.

Arisarum × aspergillum is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental appeal and ease of care.
