Asplenium wichurae

Asplenium wichurae, commonly known as the shield fern, is a species of fern native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the family Aspleniaceae and is characterized by its compact, upright growth habit. The plant features lance-shaped, dark green leaves with a leathery texture and a distinctive, shield-like appearance on the underside. It thrives in shady, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Asplenium wichurae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated as a houseplant in many regions.
