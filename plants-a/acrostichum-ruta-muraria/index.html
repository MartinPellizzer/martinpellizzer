Acrostichum Ruta-Muraria

Ruta muraria, scientifically known as Acrostichum ruta-muraria, is a hardy and resilient plant native to arid and rocky environments. This unique species thrives in regions with limited water availability, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its compact growth habit and distinctive foliage make it a striking addition to rocky landscapes. Despite its tough exterior, the plant possesses medicinal properties that have been recognized for centuries. With its ability to survive in extreme climates, Ruta muraria remains an important symbol of botanical resilience.
