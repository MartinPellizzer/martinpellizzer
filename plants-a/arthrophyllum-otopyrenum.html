Arthrophyllum otopyrenum

Arthrophyllum otopyrenum, commonly known as the mountain holly.

It is a shrub native to the high altitudes of South America, particularly in the Andes. The plant has small, elliptical leaves that are typically arranged in a spiral pattern around the stems. Its flowers are small and greenish-white, often clustered in dense inflorescences.

The plant is adapted to cold, dry environments and is often found in rocky or alpine habitats.
