Acalypha sericea var. sericea

Acalypha sericea var. sericea, commonly known as velvetleaf or silky ironweed, is a herbaceous annual plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features palmate leaves with a soft, velvety texture due to the dense covering of fine hairs. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in moist, disturbed soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Its rapid growth and ability to spread through seeds make it an aggressive weed in many habitats.
