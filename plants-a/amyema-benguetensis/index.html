Amyema Benguetensis

Amyema benguetensis is a rare and unique plant species native to the Philippines. It belongs to the family Myrtaceae, which includes eucalyptus and other aromatic plants. This plant is characterized by its small size and distinctive leaf structure. It thrives in specific microhabitats, making it highly vulnerable to environmental changes. Due to its limited distribution and ecological significance, conservation efforts are crucial for its survival.
