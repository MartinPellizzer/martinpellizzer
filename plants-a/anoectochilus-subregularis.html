Anoectochilus subregularis

Anoectochilus subregularis, commonly known as the pink-stemmed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique pinkish stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or white in color. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.

Anoectochilus subregularis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
