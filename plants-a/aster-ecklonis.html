Aster ecklonis

Aster ecklonis, commonly known as the Cape aster, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. It produces showy blue to purple flowers that bloom in the summer months.

Aster ecklonis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
