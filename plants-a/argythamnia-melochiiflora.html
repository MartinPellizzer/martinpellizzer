Argythamnia melochiiflora

Argythamnia melochiiflora, commonly known as the melochiiflorous argythamnia, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features silver-gray foliage with small, narrow leaves that help reduce water loss in dry environments. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, arranged in dense clusters, and bloom during the dry season.
