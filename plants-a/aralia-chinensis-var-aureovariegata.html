Aralia chinensis var. aureovariegata

Aralia chinensis var. aureovariegata, commonly known as the golden variegated aralia, is a striking ornamental plant native to China.

It features large, palmately compound leaves with distinctive yellow and white variegation that enhance its visual appeal. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers in umbel-like clusters, followed by dark blue berries that are attractive to birds. It thrives in partial to full shade and prefers moist, well-drained soil.

This variety is valued for its ornamental foliage and adaptability to a range of garden conditions.
