Acrostichum wallii

It is a large, evergreen fern that can grow up to 10 meters in height, characterized by its tall, unbranched trunk and a crown of fronds. The plant has a rhizome that produces a single, sturdy trunk, which is covered with a thick, fibrous layer of old leaf bases. Its fronds are pinnately compound, with a dark green, leathery texture and a distinctive, glossy appearance.

Acrostichum wallii thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forested areas across New Zealand.
