Acalypha haploclada

Acalypha haploclada, commonly known as the silverleaf acalypha, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive silver-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires moderate to high levels of sunlight.

Acalypha haploclada is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive appearance and resilience.
