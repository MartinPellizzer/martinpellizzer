Alsophila camerooniana var. congi

Alsophila camerooniana var. congi, commonly known as the Cameroon tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as rainforests. Its rhizome is robust, supporting the growth of the trunk and roots.

The fronds are usually dark green and can reach lengths of several meters, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
