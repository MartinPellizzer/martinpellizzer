Anguria umbrosa var. dentata

Anguria umbrosa var. dentata, commonly known as the shadow melon, is a unique variant of the anguria species characterized by its dark green, deeply lobed leaves.

This plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in the early morning and are followed by orange-red fruits with a sweet, slightly tangy flavor. The leaves are covered in a fine, velvety texture that helps in capturing moisture from the humid environment. It thrives in shaded, moist conditions and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its distinctive leaf shape and ornamental value make it a popular choice for shady garden spaces.
