Aster quelpaertensis

Aster quelpaertensis, commonly known as the Jeju aster, is a flowering plant native to the island of Jeju in South Korea.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in coastal areas. It produces small, white to pinkish flowers that bloom in the summer months.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
