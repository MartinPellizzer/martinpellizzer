Anthurium roezlii

Anthurium roezlii, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves and vibrant red spadix. The plant produces colorful spathes that surround the spadix, which is a modified leaf that bears the flowers. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.

Anthurium roezlii is popular as a decorative plant due to its ornamental foliage and unique floral structure.
