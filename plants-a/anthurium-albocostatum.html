Anthurium albocostatum

Anthurium albocostatum, commonly known as the white-striped anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive white or cream-colored stripe running down the center. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-shaped inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, typically in shades of red or orange. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium albocostatum is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
