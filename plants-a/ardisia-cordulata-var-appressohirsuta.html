Ardisia cordulata var. appressohirsuta

Ardisia cordulata var. appressohirsuta, commonly known as the glossy evergreen shrub, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features small, dark green, glossy leaves that are typically elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, white flowers that are followed by bright red, fleshy berries. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in warm climates.
