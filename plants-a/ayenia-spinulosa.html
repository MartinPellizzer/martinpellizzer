Ayenia spinulosa

Ayenia spinulosa, commonly known as the spiny ayenia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features small, tubular flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color, arranged in a cluster. Its leaves are usually narrow and slightly hairy, and it often grows in dry, rocky habitats.

Ayenia spinulosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
