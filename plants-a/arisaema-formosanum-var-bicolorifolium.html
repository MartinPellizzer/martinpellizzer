Arisaema formosanum var. bicolorifolium

Arisaema formosanum var. bicolorifolium, commonly known as the Rainbow Arum, is a striking tropical plant native to Taiwan.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that display a unique bicolor pattern, often with green and white or pink hues. The plant produces an inflorescence that resembles a spadix, surrounded by a colorful spathe. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The species is also notable for its medicinal uses in traditional Chinese medicine.
