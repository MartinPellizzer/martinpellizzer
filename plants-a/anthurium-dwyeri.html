Anthurium dwyeri

Anthurium dwyeri, commonly known as the Dwyer's arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are often a deep green with a glossy texture. The plant produces spadices, which are specialized inflorescences that consist of a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe. Anthurium dwyeri thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and botanical collections.
