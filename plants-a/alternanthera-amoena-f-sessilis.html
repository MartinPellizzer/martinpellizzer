Alternanthera amoena f. sessilis

Alternanthera amoena f. sessilis, commonly known as the compact alternanthera, is a compact, trailing plant native to South America.

It features striking, lance-shaped leaves that are typically red or purple, with a contrasting green underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually white or pale pink. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist soil.

This variety is popular in aquariums and as a garden plant due to its vibrant foliage and ease of care.
