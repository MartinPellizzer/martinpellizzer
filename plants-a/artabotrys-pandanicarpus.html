Artabotrys pandanicarpus

Artabotrys pandanicarpus, commonly known as the pandan-like tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, palm-like leaves and clusters of fragrant, white flowers. The plant produces small, woody fruits that are often used in traditional medicine. Its foliage is widely utilized for its aromatic properties in cooking and crafts.

The species is valued for both its ornamental beauty and practical applications in local cultures.
