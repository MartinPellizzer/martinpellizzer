Alsophila albidopaleata

Alsophila albidopaleata, commonly known as the white-veined tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and a crown of feathery, light green fronds. The fronds are typically bipinnate, with a distinctive white midrib that contrasts with the green tissue. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical forests.

This plant plays an important ecological role in its habitat by providing shelter and food for various organisms.
