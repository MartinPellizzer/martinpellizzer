Asterolasia asteriscophora

Asterolasia asteriscophora, commonly known as the starry aster, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features showy, daisy-like flowers with white petals and yellow centers, typically blooming during the rainy season. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or grasslands.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
