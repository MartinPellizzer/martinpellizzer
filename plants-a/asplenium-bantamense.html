Asplenium bantamense

Asplenium bantamense, commonly known as the Bantam spleenwort, is a small, evergreen fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Aspleniaceae and is characterized by its compact, dark green fronds that are often clustered closely together. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its rhizomes are short and produce small, lance-shaped leaves with a serrated margin.

Asplenium bantamense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in shaded garden settings.
