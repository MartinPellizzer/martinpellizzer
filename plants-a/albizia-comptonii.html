Albizia comptonii

Albizia comptonii, commonly known as the Compton's silktree, is a deciduous leguminous shrub or small tree native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its pinnately compound leaves and showy, fragrant flowers that range from white to pink. The plant produces elongated, flat pods that contain several seeds, and it has a shallow root system adapted to well-drained soils.

Albizia comptonii is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and can thrive in full sun to partial shade.
