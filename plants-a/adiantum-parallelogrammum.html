Adiantum parallelogrammum

Adiantum parallelogrammum, commonly known as the button fern, is a delicate evergreen fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features feathery, finely divided fronds that resemble buttons, giving it its common name. The plant thrives in humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil. Its rhizomes spread horizontally, allowing it to form dense mats or clusters.

Adiantum parallelogrammum is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its elegant appearance and air-purifying qualities.
