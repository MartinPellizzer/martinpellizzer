Artemisia Junnanica

Chinese wormwood, scientifically known as Artemisia junnanica, is a hardy perennial shrub native to the arid regions of northern China. It thrives in rocky and sandy soils, often found in mountainous and semi-desert environments. This plant is valued for its aromatic foliage and has been used in traditional medicine for centuries. Its resilience makes it a common sight in harsh climates where few other plants can survive. Despite its tough appearance, it plays an important ecological role in stabilizing soil and supporting local wildlife.
