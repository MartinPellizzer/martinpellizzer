Ateleia acunae

Ateleia acunae, commonly known as the Brazilian starfruit or golden starfruit, is a member of the Rutaceae family.

It is a small, deciduous tree native to the Atlantic Forest region of Brazil. The plant is characterized by its elliptical, leathery leaves and small, fragrant white flowers. Its fruit is a yellow, star-shaped berry that ripens in late summer.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and has some traditional medicinal uses.
