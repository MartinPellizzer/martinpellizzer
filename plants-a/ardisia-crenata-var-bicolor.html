Ardisia crenata var. bicolor

Ardisia crenata var. bicolor, commonly known as the variegated evergreen ardisia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, dark green leaves with striking white or cream-colored margins, creating a striking visual contrast. The plant produces small, red berries that are attractive to birds, aiding in seed dispersal. It thrives in partial to full shade and prefers moist, well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
