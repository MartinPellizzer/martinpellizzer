Amauropelta christensenii

Amauropelta christensenii, commonly known as the Christensen's shield fern, is a rare and specialized fern species native to the arid regions of western North America.

It is characterized by its distinctive, shield-shaped fronds that are often covered in fine, hairy structures. The plant thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to withstand extreme temperatures and limited moisture. Its reproductive structures are concealed within the fronds, contributing to its elusive nature.

Due to habitat loss and limited distribution, it is considered a vulnerable species requiring conservation efforts.
