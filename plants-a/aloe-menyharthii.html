Aloe menyharthii

Aloe menyharthii, commonly known as the White Aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, branching stems and dense clusters of narrow, pointed leaves that are typically white to pale green in color. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers in terminal panicles, which bloom during the summer months. Aloe menyharthii is adapted to arid environments and requires well-draining soil and minimal water.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional medicinal uses.
