Aeollanthus tuberosus

Aeollanthus tuberosus, commonly known as the tuberous aeollanthus, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its rosette-forming foliage and showy yellow flowers. The plant produces underground tubers that are sometimes used in traditional medicine. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal potential, it is cultivated in some regions for horticultural purposes.
