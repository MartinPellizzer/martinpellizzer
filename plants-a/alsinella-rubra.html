Alsinella rubra

Alsinella rubra, commonly known as the red alsinella, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, red-orange ray flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often forming a basal rosette.

Alsinella rubra is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
