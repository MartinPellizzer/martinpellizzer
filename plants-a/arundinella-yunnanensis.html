Arundinella yunnanensis

Arundinella yunnanensis, commonly known as Yunnan bamboo, is a flowering plant belonging to the grass family Poaceae.

It is native to China, particularly in the Yunnan province, where it thrives in mountainous regions. The plant is characterized by its tall, slender stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain tiny, wind-pollinated flowers.

Arundinella yunnanensis is often used in traditional Chinese medicine and is valued for its structural strength and ornamental qualities.
