Abutilon haenkeanum

Abutilon haenkeanum, commonly known as the velvetleaf abutilon, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its fuzzy, velvety leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces showy, lantern-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, which bloom throughout the growing season. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Its adaptability makes it suitable for both indoor and outdoor environments.
