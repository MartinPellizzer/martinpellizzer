Adenophora trachelioides subsp. giangsuensis

Adenophora trachelioides subsp.

giangsuensis, commonly known as the Jiangsu bellflower, is a perennial herb native to China. It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped, blue to purple flowers.

The plant typically grows in moist, shady environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its leaves are lanceolate and often have a hairy texture.
