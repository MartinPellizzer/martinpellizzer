Acinodendron thyrsoideum

Acinodendron thyrsoideum, commonly known as the slender acacia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small, shrub-like tree that typically grows in coastal and sandy soils. The plant features simple, alternate leaves and produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in panicles. Its fruit is a legume that contains several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

Acinodendron thyrsoideum is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in local ecosystems.
