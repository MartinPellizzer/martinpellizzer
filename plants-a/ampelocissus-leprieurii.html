Ampelocissus leprieurii

Ampelocissus leprieurii, commonly known as the African fig-leaf plant, is a climbing shrub native to southern Africa.

It features large, heart-shaped leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by edible fig-like fruits. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often grown as an ornamental plant.

Its ability to climb makes it suitable for trellises or as a ground cover in gardens.
