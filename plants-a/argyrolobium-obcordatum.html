Argyrolobium obcordatum

Argyrolobium obcordatum, commonly known as the silverleaf burr.

It is a flowering plant native to southern Africa, particularly South Africa, where it thrives in arid and semi-arid regions. The plant is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in spring. Its seed pods are woody and contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.
