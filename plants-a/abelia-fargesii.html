Abelia fargesii

Abelia fargesii, commonly known as the Chinese abelia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features slender, arching branches and produces clusters of fragrant, tubular flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are often adorned with a silvery underside. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained soil.

Abelia fargesii is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various garden conditions.
