Aerangis grantii

Aerangis grantii, commonly known as the Grant's orchid, is a tropical orchid native to the island of Madagascar.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters of up to 20 individual blooms. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are typically broad and lance-shaped, and it prefers bright, indirect light with high humidity.

Aerangis grantii is a popular choice for indoor cultivation due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
