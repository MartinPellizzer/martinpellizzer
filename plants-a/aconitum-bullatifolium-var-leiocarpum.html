Aconitum bullatifolium var. leiocarpum

Aconitum bullatifolium var. leiocarpum, commonly known as the smooth-carpel aconite, is a member of the Ranunculaceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem and large, showy blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are palmately lobed, with a deep green color and a rough texture.

This variant is notable for its reduced bracts and smoother carpels compared to other varieties of Aconitum.
