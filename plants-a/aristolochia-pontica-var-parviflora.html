Aristolochia pontica var. parviflora

Aristolochia pontica var. parviflora, commonly known as the small-flowered birthwort, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe and parts of western Asia.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces unique, maroon-colored, pipe-shaped flowers that are characteristic of the Aristolochiaceae family. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
