Astilbe thunbergii var. bandaica

Astilbe thunbergii var. bandaica, commonly known as the Bandaica false spirea, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Japan.

It features finely divided, fern-like leaves that are often bluish-green in color. The plant produces panicles of pink to rose-colored flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and suitability for woodland gardens.
