Acacia × tristis

Acacia × tristis, commonly known as the weeping acacia, is a hybrid plant resulting from the cross between Acacia robusta and Acacia decurrens.

The plant produces yellow globular flowers, which are followed by flat, leathery pods containing seeds. Native to Australia, it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular ornamental species.

Its unique appearance and adaptability have contributed to its widespread cultivation in gardens and landscapes.
