Aichryson pachycaulon

Aichryson pachycaulon, commonly known as the woolly aichryson, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that covers its compact, rounded stems. The plant has thick, fleshy leaves that store water, allowing it to survive in arid conditions. It produces small, yellow flowers during the summer months.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
