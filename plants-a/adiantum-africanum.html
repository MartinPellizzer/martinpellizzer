Adiantum africanum

Adiantum africanum, commonly known as the African maidenhair fern, is a species of fern native to Africa.

It belongs to the family Adiantaceae and is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds that are typically bioluminescent in the dark. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas. Its leaves are usually bright green and have a distinctive, lace-like appearance.

Adiantum africanum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant.
