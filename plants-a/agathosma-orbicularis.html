Agathosma orbicularis

Agathosma orbicularis, commonly known as the roundleaf buchu, is a small shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its aromatic, oval-shaped leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. The plant belongs to the Rutaceae family and is known for its distinctive scent, which resembles that of citrus. It thrives in coastal areas and is adapted to sandy, well-drained soils.

Agathosma orbicularis is also valued for its essential oils, which are used in aromatherapy and natural remedies.
