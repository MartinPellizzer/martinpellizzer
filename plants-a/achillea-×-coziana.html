Achillea × coziana

Achillea × coziana, commonly known as the hybrid garden yarrow, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features finely divided, feathery leaves and flat, composite flower heads that range from white to pale pink. The plant typically grows to a height of 30–60 cm and thrives in well-drained soil and full sun. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract beneficial insects.

Achillea × coziana is often used in gardens for its hardiness and low maintenance requirements.
