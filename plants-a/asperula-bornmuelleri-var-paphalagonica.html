Asperula bornmuelleri var. paphalagonica

Asperula bornmuelleri var. paphalagonica, commonly known as the Paphlagonian azalea, is a flowering shrub native to the mountainous regions of Greece.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers.
