Ardisia belizensis

Ardisia belizensis, commonly known as the Belize flame tree, is a species native to Central America, particularly Belize.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub or tree that can grow up to 10 meters tall. The plant features glossy, dark green leaves that are opposite in arrangement and have a slightly waxy texture. Its flowers are small, white, and clustered in dense inflorescences, followed by bright red berries that are an important food source for local wildlife.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological significance in its native habitat.
