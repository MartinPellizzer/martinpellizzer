Azalea makinoi var. muranoana

Azalea makinoi var. muranoana, commonly known as the Murano azalea, is a compact evergreen shrub native to Japan.

It features dense, dark green foliage and produces clusters of vibrant, funnel-shaped flowers in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant is characterized by its small, leathery leaves and is well adapted to humid, acidic soils. It thrives in partial shade and requires consistent moisture for optimal growth.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
