Artemisia ordosica

Artemisia ordosica, commonly known as the Ordos wormwood, is a perennial shrub native to arid and semi-arid regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its gray-green, aromatic foliage and small, yellowish flower heads. The plant is adapted to harsh environments, with deep root systems that help it survive in dry conditions. It contains essential oils and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

Artemisia ordosica is also valued in horticulture for its ornamental appearance and resilience.
