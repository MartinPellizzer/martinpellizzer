Armeria maritima var. profilicaulis

Armeria maritima var. profilicaulis, commonly known as the sea thrift, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

It is a low-growing perennial with dense clusters of white or pink flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has needle-like leaves and is well adapted to salty, windy environments. It thrives in well-drained sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in dunes and coastal grasslands.

Its hardy nature makes it a popular choice for coastal gardens and erosion control.
