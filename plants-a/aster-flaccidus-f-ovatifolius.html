Aster flaccidus f. ovatifolius

Aster flaccidus f. ovatifolius, commonly known as the broadleaf aster, is a variant of the flaccid aster species.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the northeastern United States. The plant features ovate to broadly ovate leaves that are usually hairy and have a bluish-green hue. Its flowering stems bear clusters of small, white to purple flowers that bloom in late summer.

The botanical characteristics of this plant make it a distinctive and ornamental addition to gardens and natural habitats.
