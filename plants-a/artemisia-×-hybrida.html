Artemisia × hybrida

Artemisia × hybrida, commonly known as mugwort or garden mugwort, is a hybrid perennial plant in the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its gray-green, aromatic foliage and slender, often branched stems. The plant produces small, yellowish to brownish flower heads that are typically clustered in dense, spherical clusters. Artemisia × hybrida is native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
