Anthericum nataglencoense

Anthericum nataglencoense, commonly known as the Natal grass, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in grassy areas and along the edges of forests, preferring well-drained soils. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered inflorescences. Its flowers are usually greenish or brownish, with a subtle fragrance.

Anthericum nataglencoense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
