Acer platanoides var. lobelii

Acer platanoides var. lobelii, commonly known as the lobed maple, is a variant of the Norway maple.

It is characterized by its large, palmately lobed leaves that are typically dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in early spring, which are followed by winged seeds. It is native to Europe and has been introduced to various parts of the world.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and adaptability to different soil types.
