Abutilon calliphyllum

Abutilon calliphyllum, commonly known as the silky abutilon, is a perennial shrub native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its soft, hairy leaves and showy, lantern-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in partial to full sun. It is often found in woodlands and along stream banks, where it contributes to the local biodiversity.

The silky abutilon is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage and flowers.
