Anasser laniti

Anasser laniti, commonly known as the slender marsh grass, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to wetland and marshy environments.

It typically grows in shallow water or saturated soil, showcasing its adaptability to aquatic conditions. The plant has slender, erect stems that are often reddish in color, and its leaves are narrow and grass-like. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, which are typically greenish or brownish in hue.

Anasser laniti plays a vital role in stabilizing wetland ecosystems by preventing erosion and providing habitat for various aquatic organisms.
