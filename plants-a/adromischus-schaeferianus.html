Adromischus schaeferianus

Adromischus schaeferianus, commonly known as the Schaefer's ice plant, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, triangular leaves that are often covered in a fine, powdery coating, which helps reduce water loss. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Its compact growth habit and attractive foliage make it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
