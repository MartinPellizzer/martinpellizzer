Aster sinensis

Aster sinensis, commonly known as Chinese aster, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant typically grows to a height of 30 to 60 centimeters and features narrow, linear leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.

Aster sinensis is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in traditional Chinese medicine.
