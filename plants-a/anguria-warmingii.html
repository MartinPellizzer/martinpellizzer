Anguria warmingii

Anguria warmingii, commonly known as the Chinese nightshade, is a member of the Solanaceae family.

It is a flowering plant native to China, typically found in forested areas. The plant features broad, ovate leaves and produces clusters of small, white or purple flowers. Its berries are edible when ripe but contain toxic compounds in their unripe state.

Anguria warmingii is valued both for its ornamental appeal and its traditional medicinal uses.
