Asplenium azoricum

Asplenium azoricum, commonly known as the Azorean spleenwort, is a small, terrestrial fern native to the Azores.

It typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as rock crevices and forest floors. The plant has slender, upright fronds that are usually less than 20 centimeters in length. Its leaves are dark green and have a leathery texture, with a distinctive, narrow shape.

Asplenium azoricum is adapted to humid climates and is often found in mountainous regions with high humidity.
