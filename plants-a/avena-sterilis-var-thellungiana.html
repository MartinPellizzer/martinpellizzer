Avena sterilis var. thellungiana

Avena sterilis var. thellungiana, commonly known as the Swedish oat, is a perennial grass species native to Europe.

It is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is often found in moist, calcareous soils. It is closely related to cultivated oats and has been used in ecological restoration projects.

Its botanical features make it an important species for studying grassland ecosystems.
