Aegilops crithodium

Aegilops crithodium, commonly known as the slender wheatgrass, is a grass species native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences with tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It is often found in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to arid environments.

Aegilops crithodium is sometimes used in horticulture for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
