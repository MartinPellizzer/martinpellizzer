Asemeia acuminata

Asemeia acuminata, commonly known as the slender milkweed, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and opposite, heart-shaped leaves. Asemeia acuminata plays an important role in supporting pollinators, particularly butterflies and bees.
