Aloe perfoliata var. serrulata

Aloe perfoliata var. serrulata, commonly known as the serrated aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features upright, branching stems that bear narrow, fleshy leaves with serrated edges. The leaves are typically blue-green in color and have a waxy coating that helps reduce water loss. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers in clusters during the warmer months.

This species is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
