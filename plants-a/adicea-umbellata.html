Adicea umbellata

Adicea umbellata, commonly known as the umbrella plant, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers that resemble an umbrella. The plant produces small, round fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate to high levels of sunlight.

Adicea umbellata is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable climates.
