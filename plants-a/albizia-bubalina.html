Albizia bubalina

Albizia bubalina, commonly known as the African silk tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is characterized by its leguminous properties and nitrogen-fixing capabilities. Albizia bubalina is also valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional uses in various African cultures.
