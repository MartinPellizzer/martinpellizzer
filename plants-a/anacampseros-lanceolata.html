Anacampseros lanceolata

Anacampseros lanceolata, commonly known as the lanceolate stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, lance-shaped leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or purple during the warmer months. It is often found growing on rocky outcrops or in sandy soils.

Its compact growth habit and attractive foliage make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
