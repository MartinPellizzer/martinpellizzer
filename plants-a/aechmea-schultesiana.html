Aechmea schultesiana

Aechmea schultesiana, commonly known as the Schultesi's bromeliad, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that form a central cup to collect water. The plant produces striking inflorescences with colorful bracts that can range from red to yellow. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in cloud forests.

Aechmea schultesiana is appreciated for its ornamental value and is cultivated as a houseplant in many parts of the world.
