Apocynum × medium var. leuconeuron

Apocynum × medium var. leuconeuron, commonly known as the whiteflower dogbane, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family, which includes many plants with milky sap and showy flowers. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of white to pale yellow flowers. Its fruit is a follicle that splits open to release seeds with silky hairs.

It is often found in moist woodlands and thrives in partial shade to full sun.
