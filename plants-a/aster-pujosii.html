Aster pujosii

Aster pujosii, commonly known as the Pujos' aster, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies, sunflowers, and other composite flowers. The plant is characterized by its showy, blue to violet-colored flowers that bloom in late summer and early autumn. Aster pujosii typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in full sun.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
