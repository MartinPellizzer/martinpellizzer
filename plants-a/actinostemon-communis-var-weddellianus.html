Actinostemon communis var. weddellianus

Actinostemon communis var. weddellianus, commonly known as the Weddellian starflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the southern regions of South America.

It features a basal rosette of elliptic to ovate, glaucous leaves that are covered in fine, white hairs. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white and pale yellow during the late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sun exposure.

Its botanical structure makes it an attractive addition to rock gardens and woodland borders.
