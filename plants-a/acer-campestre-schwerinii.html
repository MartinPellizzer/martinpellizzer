Acer campestre schwerinii

Acer campestre schwerinii, commonly known as the Schwerin hedge maple, is a cultivar of the common maple tree.

It is characterized by its dense, upright growth habit and is often used as a hedge or privacy screen. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers in early spring, followed by red or orange-red samaras in the fall.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and adaptability to various soil conditions.
