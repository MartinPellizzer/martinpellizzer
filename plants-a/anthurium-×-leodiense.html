Anthurium × leodiense

Anthurium × leodiense, commonly known as the flame anthurium, is a hybrid plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces vibrant red spathes that enclose bright yellow spadices, creating a visually stunning display. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium × leodiense is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
