Alyssum besseri

Alyssum besseri, commonly known as Sierra Lily, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family and is characterized by its dense clusters of small, fragrant white or pink flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in full sun. It has narrow, linear leaves and produces seed pods that split open to release tiny seeds.

Alyssum besseri is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
