Alstroemeria linifolia

Alstroemeria linifolia, commonly known as the silverleaf alstroemeria, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the family Alstroemeriaceae and is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces clusters of colorful, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and floral arrangements.

Alstroemeria linifolia prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
