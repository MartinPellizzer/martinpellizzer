Alyssum calycinum var. virescens

Alyssum calycinum var. virescens, commonly known as greenleaf alyssum, is a low-growing perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features dense clusters of small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically a vibrant green, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Its compact growth habit and long flowering period make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking a hardy, decorative plant.
