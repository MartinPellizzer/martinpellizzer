Anthurium cotobrusii

Anthurium cotobrusii, commonly known as the Red Anthurium, is a striking tropical plant native to Colombia.

It features vibrant, heart-shaped leaves with a deep red coloration, which is a notable botanical characteristic. The plant produces striking spadices, which are the inflorescences that give it its ornamental appeal. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Its unique foliage and exotic appearance make it a popular choice for indoor gardening and botanical collections.
