Ayenia aspera

Ayenia aspera, commonly known as the rough ayenia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, gray-green leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a rough texture. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, with a central disk surrounded by ray florets.

Ayenia aspera is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments.
