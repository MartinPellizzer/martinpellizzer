Astilbe chinensis var. pumila

Astilbe chinensis var. pumila, commonly known as the dwarf Chinese astilbe, is a herbaceous perennial native to China.

It features upright, branched stems that bear clusters of small, fragrant, pinkish-white flowers. The plant has finely divided, fern-like foliage that adds texture to garden landscapes. It thrives in partial to full shade and moist, well-drained soil.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in woodland gardens.
