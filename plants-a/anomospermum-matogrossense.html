Anomospermum matogrossense

Anomospermum matogrossense, commonly known as the Matogrossense bursera, is a tropical plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Burseraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic resinous sap. The plant typically grows as a small tree or shrub, with simple, alternate leaves that are often glossy. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, while its fruit is a capsule that releases seeds upon maturity.

The species is valued for its ecological role and traditional uses in local communities.
