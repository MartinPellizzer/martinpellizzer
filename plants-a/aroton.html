Aroton

Aroton, commonly known as the golden bell, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its distinctive, pendulous yellow flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, round fruits that mature to a dark brown color. Aroton thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant flowers and drought tolerance.
