Allium brevistylum

Allium brevistylum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved garlic.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the western United States, particularly California. The plant features basal rosettes of narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers in the summer. It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is often found in dry, open habitats.

Allium brevistylum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in native plant gardens.
