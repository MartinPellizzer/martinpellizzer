Aeschynomene minutiflora subsp. minutiflora

Aeschynomene minutiflora subsp.

minutiflora, commonly known as small-flower aeschynomene, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa and Asia. It features slender, erect stems that are often branched and covered with fine hairs.

The plant produces small, pale blue or white flowers arranged in clusters, which are typically less than 1 centimeter in diameter. Its leaves are narrow and opposite, with a slightly hairy surface.

It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
