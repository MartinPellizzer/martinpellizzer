Alsine uralensis

Alsine uralensis, commonly known as the Siberian sandwort, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Caryophyllaceae.

It is native to arctic and alpine regions of Eurasia, including parts of Russia, Mongolia, and northern China. The plant typically grows in dry, sandy or rocky soils and is adapted to harsh environmental conditions. It produces small, white or pink flowers that bloom in early summer.

Its leaves are usually narrow and fleshy, which helps it conserve water in its cold, arid habitat.
