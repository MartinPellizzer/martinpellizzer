Anemone brachystema

Anemone brachystema, commonly known as the Japanese anemone, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet. The plant features showy, white to pink flowers with a central cluster of stamens, which bloom in late summer to early autumn. Its leaves are palmately divided, with a coarse texture and a bluish-green color.

The plant thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.
