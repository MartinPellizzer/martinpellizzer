Abutilon asiaticum var. albescens

Abutilon asiaticum var. albescens, commonly known as the white Chinese lantern, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its fuzzy, ovate leaves and showy, lantern-shaped flowers that range from yellow to orange in color. The plant produces capsules that contain numerous small, wind-dispersed seeds. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its unique appearance and seasonal blooming make it a distinctive addition to gardens and landscapes.
