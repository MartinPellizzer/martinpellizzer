Ammannia linearipetala

Ammannia linearipetala, commonly known as the slender leaf ammannia, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its slender, linear leaves and small, clustered flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant typically grows in wetland habitats, such as marshes and along the edges of ponds. It produces small, berry-like fruits that are edible for some bird species.

Ammannia linearipetala is often found in moist, sunny areas and is known for its ornamental value in wetland gardens.
