Anemone bodinieri

Anemone bodinieri, commonly known as the Chinese anemone, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to China.

It features showy, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has basal leaves that are palmately lobed, often with a hairy texture. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Anemone bodinieri is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to naturalize in suitable climates.
