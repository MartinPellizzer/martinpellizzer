Aster laevis var. simplex

Aster laevis var. simplex, commonly known as the Smooth Aster, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that typically bloom in late summer and early fall. The plant has smooth, lance-shaped leaves and thrives in full sun to partial shade. It is often found in prairies, meadows, and along roadsides.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting pollinators.
