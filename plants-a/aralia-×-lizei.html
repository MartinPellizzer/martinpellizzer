Aralia × lizei

Aralia × lizei, commonly known as the Chinese aralia, is a hybrid plant that combines traits from various species of the Aralia genus.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to eastern Asia, particularly China and Korea. The plant features compound leaves with a feathery appearance, often arranged in a whorled pattern along the stems. Its flowers are small and greenish-white, typically appearing in clusters, and it produces berries that are edible for some wildlife.

Aralia × lizei is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and adaptability to a range of growing conditions.
