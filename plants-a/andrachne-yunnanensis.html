Andrachne yunnanensis

Andrachne yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan sandwort, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Yunnan Province in China.

It belongs to the family Montiaceae and is characterized by its low-growing, sprawling habit with small, fleshy leaves. The plant produces clusters of tiny, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in the summer months. Due to its unique morphology and limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
