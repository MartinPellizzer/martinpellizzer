Acalypha integrifolia var. gracilipes

Acalypha integrifolia var. gracilipes, commonly known as the slender-leaf copperleaf, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, palmate leaves that are typically bright green with a reddish or purplish tinge on the underside.
