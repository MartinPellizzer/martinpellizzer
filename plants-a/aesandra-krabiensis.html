Aesandra krabiensis

Aesandra krabiensis, commonly known as the Krabi sand orchid, is a flowering plant native to Thailand.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in the dry season. The plant thrives in sandy soils and is often found in coastal areas and limestone hills. It has linear leaves and produces yellow or greenish-yellow blooms.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
