Achillea × submicrantha

Achillea × submicrantha, commonly known as the hybrid yarrow, is a perennial plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its finely divided, feather-like leaves and flat, composite flower heads that range in color from white to pink. Native to the Mediterranean region, it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun. The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and historical use in herbal medicine.

Its flowering period typically occurs in late spring to early summer.
