Aster neesii

Aster neesii, commonly known as the Nees' aster, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayas.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in alpine and subalpine regions, thriving in cool, moist environments. Its flowers are usually blue to violet in color, with ray florets surrounding a central disk.

Aster neesii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
