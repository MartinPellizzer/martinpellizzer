Aretiastrum aschersonianum

Aretiastrum aschersonianum, commonly known as the silver hair grass, is a perennial grass native to South America.

It is characterized by its dense, silver-gray foliage and tufted growth habit. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is often found in coastal regions. Its narrow, grass-like leaves are covered in fine, silky hairs that give it a distinctive appearance.

It is valued in landscaping for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
