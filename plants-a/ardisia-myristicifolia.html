Ardisia myristicifolia

Ardisia myristicifolia, commonly known as the fragrant ardisia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and small, clustered white flowers that emit a subtle fragrance. The plant produces bright red berries that are edible but often have a tart flavor. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Ardisia myristicifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in gardens.
