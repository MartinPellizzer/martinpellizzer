Aloe Microstigma Subsp. Framesii

Coral aloe, scientifically known as Aloe microstigma subsp. framesii, is a striking succulent native to the arid regions of southern Africa. Its unique appearance, characterized by its coral-like red and orange bracts, makes it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts. This plant thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for xeriscaping and rock gardens. Despite its beauty, it is relatively rare in the wild and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. With proper care, coral aloe can flourish and add a vibrant touch to any indoor or outdoor space.
