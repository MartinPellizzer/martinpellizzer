Aristida lanigera

Aristida lanigera, commonly known as silky grass or silky lovegrass, is a perennial grass species native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its dense, tufted growth habit and fine, silky-haired foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that are often tinged with reddish or purplish hues. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open grasslands and disturbed sites.

Aristida lanigera is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to stabilize soil in arid environments.
