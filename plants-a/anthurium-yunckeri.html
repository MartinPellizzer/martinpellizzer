Anthurium yunckeri

Anthurium yunckeri, commonly known as the Yuncker's arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are often a deep green with a glossy texture. The plant produces vibrant red spadices, which are the modified leaves that enclose the true flowers. Anthurium yunckeri thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

It is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
