Anemone alchemillifolia var. caffra

Anemone alchemillifolia var. caffra, commonly known as the Cape anemone, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It features a basal rosette of lance-shaped, hairy leaves that are typically gray-green in color. The plant produces showy, white to pale pink flowers with five to seven petals, which bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its botanical characteristics make it a distinctive and ornamental addition to native plant collections.
