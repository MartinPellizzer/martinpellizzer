Ardisia martiana

Ardisia martiana, commonly known as the red-flowered ardisia, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are elliptical in shape and arranged oppositely on the stems. The plant produces small, white flowers followed by bright red, fleshy berries that are attractive to birds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in warm climates.
