Asplenium formosum var. incultum

Asplenium formosum var. incultum, commonly known as the Taiwan spleenwort, is a fern native to Southeast Asia, particularly Taiwan.

It belongs to the Aspleniaceae family and is characterized by its compact, evergreen foliage. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky crevices. Its fronds are leathery and often have a slightly serrated edge.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in shady gardens.
