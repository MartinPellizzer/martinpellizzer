Aristida pallens f. breviaristata

Aristida pallens f. breviaristata, commonly known as the narrowleaf false lovegrass, is a flowering plant in the grass family, Poaceae.

It is a perennial species characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It is native to arid and semi-arid regions of North America, where it thrives in sandy or gravelly soils.

This variety is often used in restoration projects due to its drought tolerance and ability to stabilize soil.
