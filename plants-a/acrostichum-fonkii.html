Acrostichum fonkii

Acrostichum fonkii, commonly known as the Chinese fern, is a species of fern native to China.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional Chinese medicine for their purported health benefits.

The spore-producing structures, called sori, are located on the underside of the fronds.
