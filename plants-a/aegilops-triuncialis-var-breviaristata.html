Aegilops triuncialis var. breviaristata

Aegilops triuncialis var. breviaristata, commonly known as the slender wild rye, is a perennial grass native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences with pale green to brownish flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed areas or along roadsides.

This variety is notable for its potential in forage and erosion control due to its hardy nature.
