Artabotrys setulosus

Artabotrys setulosus, commonly known as the hairy artabotrys, is a tropical flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, often giving off a strong, sweet scent. The plant has palmately lobed leaves with hairy surfaces, contributing to its common name. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release small, hairy seeds.
