Aloe microdonta

Aloe microdonta, commonly known as the narrow-leaved aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of narrow, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a slight blue tint. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing yellow flowers during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it suitable for arid and semi-arid environments.

Its leaves are used in traditional medicine and for their gel, which has soothing properties.
