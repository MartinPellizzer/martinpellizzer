Anatherostipa venusta

Anatherostipa venusta, commonly known as the showy feather grass, is a perennial grass species native to South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, silver-blue foliage and distinctive, feathery inflorescences that bloom in late spring. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. Its slender stems and fine leaves contribute to its ornamental appeal in gardens.

Anatherostipa venusta is valued for its drought tolerance and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
