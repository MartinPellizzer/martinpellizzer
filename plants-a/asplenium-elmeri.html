Asplenium elmeri

Asplenium elmeri, commonly known as Elmer's spleenwort, is a small, terrestrial fern native to the western United States.

It belongs to the family Aspleniaceae and is characterized by its compact, rosette-like growth habit. The plant has dark green, leathery leaves that are typically divided into narrow, lance-shaped segments. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices.

Asplenium elmeri is often found in association with other epiphytic and lithophytic plants in its native habitat.
