Aerides pusilla

Aerides pusilla, commonly known as the miniature orchid, is a small epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features delicate, linear leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant is well adapted to grow on trees, drawing moisture and nutrients from the air. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Due to its compact size and ornamental value, it is a popular choice for indoor cultivation.
