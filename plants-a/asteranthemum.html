Asteranthemum

Asteranthemum, commonly known as the starflower, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to South America and is characterized by its showy, star-shaped blooms that come in a variety of colors, including white, yellow, and purple. The plant typically grows as an annual or perennial herb with hairy, lance-shaped leaves. Asteranthemum thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.

Its compact growth habit and long blooming period make it a valuable addition to both ornamental and ecological landscapes.
