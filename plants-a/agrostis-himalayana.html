Agrostis himalayana

Agrostis himalayana, commonly known as Himalayan meadowgrass, is a perennial grass species native to the mountainous regions of the Himalayas.

It typically grows in high-altitude meadows and alpine environments, where it thrives in cool, moist conditions. The plant has slender, tufted stems and produces small, spike-like inflorescences with numerous tiny florets. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green coloration.

Agrostis himalayana plays an important role in stabilizing soil and supporting local biodiversity in its native habitat.
