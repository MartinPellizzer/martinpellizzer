Anthurium palosecense

Anthurium palosecense, commonly known as the Palosecense Arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy surface. The plant produces striking spadices, which are its inflorescences, often in shades of red or orange. Anthurium palosecense prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.

It is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its aesthetic appeal and air-purifying qualities.
