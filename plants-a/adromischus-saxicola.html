Adromischus saxicola

Adromischus saxicola, commonly known as the rock stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, rounded leaves that are typically green with a bluish tint, arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or white during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Due to its compact size and drought tolerance, it is a popular choice for low-maintenance gardening.
