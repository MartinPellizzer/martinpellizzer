Asplenium Sphenolobium

Cushion fern, scientifically known as Asplenium sphenolobium, is a unique and resilient plant native to the wet tropical regions of Southeast Asia. Its compact, rounded growth form resembles a miniature forest, making it a striking addition to any indoor or outdoor garden. This fern thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found growing on rocks or in crevices, where it clings tightly to its substrate. With its intricate fronds and ability to flourish in low-light conditions, it has become a popular choice for both botanists and plant enthusiasts. Its adaptability and aesthetic appeal make it a fascinating subject for study and cultivation.
