Alyssum Calycinum Var. Psilocarpum

Alyssum calycinum var. psilocarpum is a rare and intriguing plant native to specific regions of southern Europe. It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, known for its aromatic and often edible members. This variant is distinguished by its unique floral structure and subtle chemical composition. While not widely cultivated, it has attracted interest from botanists and herbalists alike. Its potential medicinal properties remain a subject of ongoing research and fascination.
