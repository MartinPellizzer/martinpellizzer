Anthurium pachyspathum

Anthurium pachyspathum, commonly known as the giant arrowhead plant, is a tropical herbaceous plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 3 feet in length, with a dark green upper surface and a lighter, sometimes variegated underside. The plant produces striking spadices, which are brightly colored, spadix-like structures that serve as the floral inflorescence. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Anthurium pachyspathum is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and air-purifying qualities.
