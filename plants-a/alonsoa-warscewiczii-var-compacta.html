Alonsoa warscewiczii var. compacta

Alonsoa warscewiczii var. compacta, commonly known as the compact alonsoa, is a flowering plant native to the Andes region of South America.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and produces clusters of small, showy flowers. The plant typically thrives in high-altitude environments with cool, moist conditions. Its leaves are often hairy and arranged in a rosette pattern, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental qualities and adaptability to specific garden conditions.
