Ammannia praetermissa

Ammannia praetermissa, commonly known as the slender ammannia, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its upright stems and small, clustered flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Ammannia praetermissa is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in supporting local wildlife.
