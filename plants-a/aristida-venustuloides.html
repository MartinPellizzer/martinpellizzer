Aristida venustuloides

Aristida venustuloides, commonly known as the elegant grass or velvet grass, is a flowering plant belonging to the grass family Poaceae.

It is characterized by its fine, wiry leaves and compact, spike-like inflorescence. Native to southern Africa, this species thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in coastal or inland regions. The plant produces small, pale green to brown flowers that are typically wind-pollinated.

Its delicate appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in suitable climates.
