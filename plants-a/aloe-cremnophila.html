Aloe cremnophila

Aloe cremnophila, commonly known as the Cape aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, upright growth habit and produces clusters of bright yellow flowers. The plant has thick, fleshy leaves that store water, adapting it to arid environments. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Aloe cremnophila is also valued for its medicinal properties, similar to other aloe species.
