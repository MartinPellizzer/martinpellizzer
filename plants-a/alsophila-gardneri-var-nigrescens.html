Alsophila gardneri var. nigrescens

Alsophila gardneri var. nigrescens, commonly known as the black-stemmed tree fern, is a variant of the tree fern genus Alsophila.

It is characterized by its dark, almost black, trunk that contrasts with its feathery fronds. Native to tropical regions, this plant thrives in humid, shaded environments. The fronds are typically bioluminescent, adding a unique visual element to its appearance.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental qualities and ability to create a dramatic focal point in gardens.
