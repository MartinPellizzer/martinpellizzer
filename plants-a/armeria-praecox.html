Armeria praecox

Armeria praecox, commonly known as early thrift, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces clusters of white or pink flowers in early spring. The plant has linear, grass-like leaves and is often found in rocky or coastal areas. It is well adapted to harsh environments due to its drought tolerance and ability to thrive in poor soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden landscapes.
