Aretia heeri

Aretia heeri, commonly known as the Cape mountain heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its dense, shrub-like growth habit. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or white, which bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and is often found in rocky or mountainous regions.

Aretia heeri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
