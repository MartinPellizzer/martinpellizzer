Amyris bipinnata

Amyris bipinnata, commonly known as the flame tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, fiery orange to red flowers that bloom in clusters during the dry season. The plant has compound leaves with two pairs of leaflets, giving it a feathery appearance. Its bark is smooth and reddish-brown, and it can grow up to 20 meters tall.

Amyris bipinnata is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
