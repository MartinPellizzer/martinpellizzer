Alchemilla paracompactilis

Alchemilla paracompactilis, commonly known as the compact alchemilla, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit. The plant features basal leaves that are typically rounded and covered in fine hairs, with a soft, silvery appearance. It produces small, greenish-yellow flowers in clusters during the summer months.

Alchemilla paracompactilis is often found in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream sides.
