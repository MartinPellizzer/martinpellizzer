Alternanthera ramosissima

Alternanthera ramosissima, commonly known as the red flame plant, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red, pink, or orange flowers that bloom in clusters, often resembling flames. The plant has slender, branching stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically green with a reddish tinge. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Alternanthera ramosissima is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its striking floral display.
