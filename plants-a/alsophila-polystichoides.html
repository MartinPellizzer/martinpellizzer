Alsophila polystichoides

Alsophila polystichoides, commonly known as the bamboo fern, is a species of tree fern native to Southeast Asia.

It features a slender, unbranched trunk that is covered with a smooth, dark brown to blackish bark. The plant produces large, feathery fronds that can grow up to several meters in length, giving it a distinctive, elegant appearance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical forests.

The fronds are typically bipinnate, with a delicate, lace-like structure that contributes to its ornamental value.
