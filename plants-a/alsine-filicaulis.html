Alsine filicaulis

Alsine filicaulis, commonly known as the fern-leaved sandwort, is a low-growing annual plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, oval leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a fuzzy appearance. The plant produces clusters of tiny, white flowers that bloom in early summer. Its root system is fibrous and adapted to thrive in sandy or rocky soils.

Alsine filicaulis is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and fields.
