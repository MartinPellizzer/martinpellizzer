Adicea Wightii

Adicea wightii is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of southern India. It is known for its striking blue-violet blooms that appear during the spring season. This species thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories. Due to habitat loss and over-collection, Adicea wightii is now considered endangered. Conservation efforts are underway to protect and propagate this unique botanical gem.
