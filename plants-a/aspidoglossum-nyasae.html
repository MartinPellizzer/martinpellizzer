Aspidoglossum nyasae

Aspidoglossum nyasae, commonly known as the Nyasae snake plant, is a perennial herb native to East Africa.

It features upright, sword-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, which are usually greenish-white in color. It is adapted to thrive in well-drained soil and bright, indirect light.

Aspidoglossum nyasae is valued for its air-purifying qualities and low maintenance requirements, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
