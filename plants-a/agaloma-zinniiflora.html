Agaloma zinniiflora

Agaloma zinniiflora, commonly known as the flame flower, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular red flowers that resemble flames, which bloom in clusters during the warmer months. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves that are opposite in arrangement, and it thrives in humid, well-drained soils. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.

Agaloma zinniiflora is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal remedies.
