Aloe brandhamii

Aloe brandhamii, commonly known as Brandham's aloe, is a succulent plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically pale green with a waxy coating, adapted to store water in its native environment. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with small, tubular flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. Aloe brandhamii is valued for its hardiness and ability to thrive in low-water conditions, making it a popular choice for xeriscaping.

Its botanical characteristics make it well-suited for cultivation in dry climates with minimal care.
