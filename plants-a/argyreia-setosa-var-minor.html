Argyreia setosa var. minor

Argyreia setosa var. minor, commonly known as the velvet flower or velvet leaf.

It is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia, characterized by its velvety, dark green foliage. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, often attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
