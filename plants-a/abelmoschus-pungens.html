Abelmoschus pungens

Abelmoschus pungens, commonly known as the prickly okra, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its spiny stems and heart-shaped leaves, which are covered in fine hairs. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous edible seeds, often used in culinary applications. It thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Abelmoschus pungens is also valued for its ornamental appeal and medicinal properties.
