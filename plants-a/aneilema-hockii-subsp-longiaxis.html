Aneilema hockii subsp. longiaxis

Aneilema hockii subsp.

longiaxis, commonly known as the long-axis aneilema, is a species native to tropical Africa. It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its upright growth habit and lanceolate leaves.

The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it a subject of interest in both horticultural and ecological studies.
