Asplenium alternifrons

Asplenium alternifrons, commonly known as the silver maidenhair fern, is a species of fern native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds that are typically silver-white in color, contrasting with its dark green veins. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories. Its leaves are pinnately compound, with each pinna bearing a row of small, toothed lobes.

Asplenium alternifrons is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appearance and ability to thrive in indoor conditions with proper care.
