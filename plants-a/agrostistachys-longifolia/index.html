Agrostistachys Longifolia

Agrostistachys longifolia is a rare and striking plant native to the mountainous regions of southern China. Known for its long, slender leaves and delicate, bell-shaped flowers, it is a member of the grass family. This species thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-drained soil to flourish. Due to its limited habitat and slow growth, it is considered a conservation concern. Its unique appearance makes it a prized addition to specialized botanical gardens and collections.
