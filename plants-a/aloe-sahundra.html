Aloe sahundra

Aloe sahundra, commonly known as the Cape aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a waxy coating. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow or orange flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Aloe sahundra is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional medicinal uses.
