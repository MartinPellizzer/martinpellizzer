Athyrium × pseudoiseanum

Athyrium × pseudoiseanum, commonly known as the Japanese painted fern, is a hybrid fern species that exhibits a striking combination of foliage colors.

It features delicate, lace-like fronds with a distinctive pattern of light and dark green, creating a visually appealing appearance. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread slowly, forming dense clusters over time.

While it is not a true fern in the strictest sense, it is widely cultivated for its ornamental value and ease of care.
