Agave todaroi

Agave todaroi, commonly known as the Mexican Blue Agave, is a succulent plant native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped growth pattern and thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-gray in color. The plant produces a tall flowering stalk that bears small, greenish-white flowers. It is adapted to arid environments and stores water in its leaves and stem.

Agave todaroi is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its striking appearance and hardiness.
