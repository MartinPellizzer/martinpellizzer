Armeria maritima f. aristulata

Armeria maritima f. aristulata, commonly known as the sea thrift or coastal thrift, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of Europe and North America.

It is a low-growing, mat-forming perennial with dense, compact clusters of white or pink flowers. The plant thrives in salty, well-drained soils and is often found in dunes and rocky coastal areas. Its leaves are narrow, fleshy, and succulent, adapted to retain moisture in arid environments.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to stabilize coastal soils.
