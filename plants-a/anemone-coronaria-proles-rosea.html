Anemone coronaria proles rosea

Anemone coronaria proles rosea, commonly known as the rose-flowered windflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features showy, cup-shaped flowers that range in color from pink to white, with a distinctive red or purple center. The plant has finely divided, fern-like leaves that are hairy and gray-green in appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, often growing in rocky or dry habitats.

Anemone coronaria proles rosea is valued for its ornamental beauty and is commonly cultivated in gardens.
