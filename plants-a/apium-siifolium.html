Apium siifolium

Apium siifolium, commonly known as sea holly, is a flowering plant belonging to the Apiaceae family.

It is characterized by its spiky, blue or purple flowers and feathery leaves. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and saline soils, showcasing its adaptability to harsh environments. Its root system helps stabilize sandy or rocky soils, making it valuable in ecological restoration.

Apium siifolium is also known for its aromatic properties and has been used in traditional medicine.
