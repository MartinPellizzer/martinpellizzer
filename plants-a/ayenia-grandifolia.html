Ayenia grandifolia

Ayenia grandifolia, commonly known as the large-flowered ayenia, is a species of flowering plant native to the Andean regions of South America.

It belongs to the family Rosaceae, which includes roses and other shrubs. The plant is characterized by its showy, white to pink flowers that bloom in spring. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a serrated edge.

Ayenia grandifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
