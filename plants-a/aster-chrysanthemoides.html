Aster chrysanthemoides

Aster chrysanthemoides, commonly known as the Chinese aster, is a herbaceous plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features showy, daisy-like flowers that range in color from white to purple, depending on the variety. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs.

Aster chrysanthemoides is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its vibrant blooms and adaptability to various growing conditions.
