Asteropeia densiflora

Asteropeia densiflora, commonly known as the dense-flowered aster.

It is a flowering plant native to South Africa, characterized by its compact growth habit and dense clusters of small, star-shaped flowers. The plant typically blooms in the spring and summer, producing a variety of colors including white, pink, and purple. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Asteropeia densiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
