Arrabidaea ovalifolia

Arrabidaea ovalifolia, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its velvety, waxy leaves and clusters of small, showy flowers that range in color from white to purple. The plant is typically evergreen and thrives in moist, well-drained soil. It is popular as a houseplant due to its ability to bloom throughout the year under proper care.

Its botanical features make it a favorite among indoor gardeners and botanists alike.
