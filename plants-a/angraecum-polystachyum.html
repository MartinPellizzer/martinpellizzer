Angraecum polystachyum

Angraecum polystachyum, commonly known as the Madagascar star orchid, is a species of orchid native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale blue with a deep purple or violet throat. The plant produces long, pendulous inflorescences bearing numerous small, fragrant blooms. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Angraecum polystachyum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
