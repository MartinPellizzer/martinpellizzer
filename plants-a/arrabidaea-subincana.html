Arrabidaea subincana

Arrabidaea subincana, commonly known as the Cape jasmine, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small to medium-sized shrub that typically grows in coastal areas and sandy soils. The plant produces fragrant white or pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the warmer months. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and often have a hairy texture.
