Aster taoyuenensis

Aster taoyuenensis, commonly known as the Taoyuan aster, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and woodland edges. Its flowers bloom in the summer and are usually purple or blue in color.

Aster taoyuenensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
