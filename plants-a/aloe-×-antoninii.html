Aloe × antoninii

Aloe × antoninii, commonly known as the hybrid aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a reddish or purple tinge on the edges. The plant produces yellow flowers in clusters during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil.

Aloe × antoninii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some medicinal uses.
