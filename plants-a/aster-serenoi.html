Aster serenoi

Aster serenoi, commonly known as the mountain aster, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Andes.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in cool, temperate climates. Its vibrant blue to purple flowers bloom in the summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Aster serenoi is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its hardy nature and visual appeal.
