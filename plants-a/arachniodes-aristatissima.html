Arachniodes aristatissima

Arachniodes aristatissima, commonly known as the hairy shield fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, leathery, dark green fronds that are often covered in fine, silky hairs. The fronds are typically divided into lance-shaped segments, giving the plant a distinctive, elegant appearance. It prefers shady, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

The plant's unique morphology and ornamental value make it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.
