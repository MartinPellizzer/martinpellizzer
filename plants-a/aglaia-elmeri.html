Aglaia elmeri

Aglaia elmeri, commonly known as the yellow mangosteen, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

The tree produces edible fruits that are typically yellow or orange in color, with a thick, leathery rind. Its bark and leaves contain various bioactive compounds, making it of interest in traditional medicine.
