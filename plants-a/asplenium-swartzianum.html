Asplenium swartzianum

Asplenium swartzianum, commonly known as the Swartz's shield fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to tropical regions of Africa and Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Aspleniaceae and is characterized by its compact, rosette-like growth habit. The plant features lanceolate, serrated leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in rocky or forested areas.

Asplenium swartzianum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant.
