Arundo donax f. versicolor

Arundo donax f. versicolor, commonly known as the variegated giant reed, is a striking variant of the common reed.

It is characterized by its striking variegated foliage, featuring combinations of green, white, and yellow hues. This ornamental grass is native to southern Europe and thrives in wet or moist environments. It can grow quite tall, often reaching heights of up to 6 meters.

Its foliage is narrow and grass-like, with a distinctive silver-blue tint that adds visual interest to gardens and water features.
