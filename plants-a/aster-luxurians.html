Aster luxurians

Aster luxurians, commonly known as the showy aster, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and robust root systems. The plant features upright, branching stems covered in fine hairs, and produces vibrant blue to purple flowers in the late summer and early fall. Its foliage consists of narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fuzzy texture.

Aster luxurians thrives in full sun to partial shade and is well-suited for moist, well-drained soils.
