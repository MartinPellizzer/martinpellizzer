Aeranthes parahybunensis

Aeranthes parahybunensis, commonly known as the Parahyba orchid, is a species native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments within the Atlantic Forest biome. Its leaves are elongated and often covered in a fine, waxy coating.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
