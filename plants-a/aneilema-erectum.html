Aneilema erectum

Aneilema erectum, commonly known as the upright aneilema, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, featuring upright stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are often covered in fine hairs, giving them a slightly fuzzy texture.

Aneilema erectum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and flowering habit.
