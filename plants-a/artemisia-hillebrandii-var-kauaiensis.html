Artemisia hillebrandii var. kauaiensis

Artemisia hillebrandii var. kauaiensis, commonly known as Kauai wormwood, is a perennial shrub native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It is characterized by its aromatic, silver-gray foliage and small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant thrives in dry, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and upland areas. It plays a role in traditional Hawaiian medicine and is used for its purported health benefits.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
