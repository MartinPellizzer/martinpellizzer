Aspidistra cyathiflora subsp. cyathiflora

Aspidistra cyathiflora subsp.

cyathiflora, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen, is a species native to Southeast Asia. It is characterized by its dark green, glossy, and strap-shaped leaves that grow in a rosette formation.

The plant produces small, white, spathiflorous flowers that are typically hidden within a leaf-like bract. It is a shade-tolerant plant that thrives in humid, cool environments.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is widely cultivated as a houseplant.
