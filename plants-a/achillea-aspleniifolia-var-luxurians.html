Achillea aspleniifolia var. luxurians

Achillea aspleniifolia var. luxurians, commonly known as the fern-leaved yarrow, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features finely dissected, fern-like leaves that are bright green and often covered in a silvery bloom. The plant produces flat, composite flower heads composed of tiny white to pale yellow florets. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its delicate appearance and extended blooming period add ornamental value to garden landscapes.
