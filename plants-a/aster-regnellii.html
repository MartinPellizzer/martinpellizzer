Aster regnellii

Aster regnellii, commonly known as the Regnell's aster, is a flowering plant native to the western United States and parts of Canada.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and produces vibrant blue to violet flowers. It has narrow, lance-shaped leaves and is often found in open woodlands and meadows.

Aster regnellii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance as a nectar source for pollinators.
