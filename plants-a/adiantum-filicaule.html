Adiantum filicaule

Adiantum filicaule, commonly known as the maidenhair fern, is a species of fern characterized by its delicate, lace-like fronds.

It belongs to the family Adiantaceae and is native to various regions in the Americas. The plant features finely divided, bi-pinnate leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. Adiantum filicaule thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its unique morphology and ornamental appearance make it a popular choice for horticultural cultivation.
