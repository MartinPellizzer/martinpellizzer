Argyrolobium collinum

Argyrolobium collinum, commonly known as the silver globular pea, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its spherical, silver-gray seed pods that contain small, round seeds. The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in dry, open habitats. It produces yellow flowers that bloom in spring, adding a striking visual contrast to its silver foliage.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat preferences, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens for its ornamental value.
