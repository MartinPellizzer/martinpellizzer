Anthurium maximiliani

Anthurium maximiliani, commonly known as the Red Anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped spathes that range from red to orange, often with a contrasting green or white inner surface. The plant produces striking, spadix-shaped inflorescences that are surrounded by the colorful spathe. It thrives in humid, warm environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium maximiliani is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
