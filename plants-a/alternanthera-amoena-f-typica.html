Alternanthera amoena f. typica

Alternanthera amoena f. typica, commonly known as the red-leaf alternanthera, is a strikingly ornamental plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red or pink foliage, which contrasts beautifully with its slender, branching stems. This species thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full sunlight. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often display a gradient of colors, ranging from deep red to maroon.

It is widely cultivated in water gardens and as a container plant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
