Acinodendron reticulatum

Acinodendron reticulatum, commonly known as the net-veined acacia, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its compound leaves with a distinctive net-like venation pattern. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its fruit is a legume pod containing several seeds.

It is often found in dry savannas and is adapted to arid conditions.
