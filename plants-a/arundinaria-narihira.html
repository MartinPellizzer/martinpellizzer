Arundinaria narihira

Arundinaria narihira, commonly known as the Japanese bamboo or Narihira bamboo, is a species of grass native to Japan.

It is characterized by its tall, slender culms that can grow up to several meters in height. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are typically a deep green color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments.

Arundinaria narihira is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
