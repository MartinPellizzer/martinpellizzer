Aloe barendii

Aloe barendii, commonly known as the Barend's aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that are typically gray-green in color, with prominent white teeth along the edges. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences that bear yellow to orange flowers. It is adapted to arid environments, storing water in its leaves to survive drought conditions.

Aloe barendii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional medicinal uses.
