Ancistrocladus tanzaniensis

Ancistrocladus tanzaniensis, commonly known as Tanzanian ancientwood, is a rare and unique plant species native to Tanzania.

It belongs to the family Ancistrocladaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, woody structure and small, clustered leaves. The plant exhibits a slow growth rate and is often found in rocky or semi-arid habitats. Due to its limited distribution and ecological significance, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
