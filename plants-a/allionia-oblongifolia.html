Allionia oblongifolia

Allionia oblongifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf allionia, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features basal rosettes of narrow, oblong leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink to purple, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and delicate blooms.
