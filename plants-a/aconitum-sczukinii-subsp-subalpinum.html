Aconitum sczukinii subsp. subalpinum

Aconitum sczukinii subsp.

subalpinum, commonly known as the subalpine aconite, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its tall, sparsely branched stem and striking blue to purple flowers.

subalpinum is adapted to cold, high-altitude environments and is often found in alpine meadows and rocky slopes.
