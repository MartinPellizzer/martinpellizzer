Anthanema capitata

Anthanema capitata, commonly known as the Cape daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and produces small, daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Anthanema capitata is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
