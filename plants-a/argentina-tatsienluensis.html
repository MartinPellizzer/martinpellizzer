Argentina tatsienluensis

Argentina tatsienluensis, commonly known as the Tatsienlu endemic plant, is a unique species native to the Tatsienlu region of China.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically found in mountainous areas. Its botanical significance lies in its restricted distribution and potential ecological role in its native habitat.

Due to its limited range, it is considered a rare and vulnerable species.
