Asimina manasota

Asimina manasota, commonly known as the Florida yellow cucumber.

It is a deciduous shrub native to the southeastern United States, particularly Florida and parts of Georgia. The plant produces yellow, cucumber-like fruits that are edible when ripe, though they are not commonly consumed by humans. Its leaves are simple and alternate, with a rough texture and a distinct aromatic scent.

The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands and along stream banks.
