Alsinopsis brevifolia

Alsinopsis brevifolia, commonly known as the dwarf silverleaf.

It is a succulent plant native to South Africa, characterized by its compact growth habit and silver-gray foliage. The leaves are fleshy and arranged in a rosette pattern, which helps it store water in arid environments. It produces small, yellow flowers during the summer months, adding a delicate touch to its appearance.

This plant is well-suited for rock gardens or containers due to its low maintenance and drought tolerance.
