Alstonia brassii

Alstonia brassii, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy red flowers that bloom in clusters, adding vibrant color to the landscape. The plant has compound leaves with elongated leaflets and produces woody pods containing flat, winged seeds. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it suitable for ornamental planting in warm climates.

The tree is also valued for its rapid growth and ability to provide shade in urban and rural settings.
