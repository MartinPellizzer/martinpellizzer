Alsine preslii

Alsine preslii, commonly known as Presli's alsike, is a low-growing annual herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in clusters, typically from late spring to early summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and thrives in well-drained soils. It is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and fields.

Its botanical characteristics make it a common species in ecological studies of Mediterranean flora.
