Asplenium lobangense

Asplenium lobangense, commonly known as the Philippine shield fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Aspleniaceae family and is characterized by its shield-shaped fronds that are typically dark green and leathery in texture. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its rhizomes are fibrous and help anchor it to rocky or soil substrates.

Asplenium lobangense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a decorative plant.
