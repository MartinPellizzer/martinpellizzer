Abies nordmanniana subsp. nordmanniana

Abies nordmanniana subsp.

nordmanniana, commonly known as the Nordmann fir, is a coniferous evergreen tree native to the mountainous regions of the Caucasus and parts of Turkey. It is characterized by its dense, pyramidal shape and dark green, needle-like leaves that are slightly twisted.

The cones are typically pendulous, with elongated, winged seeds that are dispersed by wind. This species is well adapted to cold climates and can thrive in a variety of soil types, including acidic and well-drained soils.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental tree due to its attractive appearance and hardiness.
