Andropogon bisquamulatus var. argyrophoeus

Andropogon bisquamulatus var. argyrophoeus, commonly known as silverleaf bluestem, is a variant of the grass species native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which gives it its distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in open woodlands and prairies, thriving in well-drained soils. Its inflorescence consists of dense, spike-like clusters of small, blue to purple flowers.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting local wildlife.
