Agrostis fukuyamae

Agrostis fukuyamae, commonly known as the Fukuyama fine grass, is a species of grass native to Japan.

It is characterized by its fine, tufted foliage and slender, upright growth habit. This plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and mountainous regions. Agrostis fukuyamae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.

Its botanical features make it a notable species within the Poaceae family.
