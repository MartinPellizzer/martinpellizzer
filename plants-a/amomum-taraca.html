Amomum taraca

Amomum taraca, commonly known as the Chinese cardamom, is a perennial herb native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes ginger and turmeric. The plant features erect, aromatic stems with lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small and clustered in inflorescences, typically greenish-white in color.

The rhizomes of Amomum taraca are used in traditional medicine and as a spice, valued for their aromatic and medicinal properties.
