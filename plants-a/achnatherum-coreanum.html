Achnatherum coreanum

Achnatherum coreanum, commonly known as Korean needlegrass, is a perennial grass native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences that contain both male and female flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows and along stream banks.

Achnatherum coreanum plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various insects and small animals.
