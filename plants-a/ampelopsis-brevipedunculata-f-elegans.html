Ampelopsis brevipedunculata f. elegans

Ampelopsis brevipedunculata f. elegans, commonly known as the elegant grape ivy, is a cultivar of the Ampelopsis genus native to China.

It is a vigorous climbing vine characterized by its trailing habit and heart-shaped, dark green foliage. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in late summer, followed by blue-black berries that are attractive to birds. It thrives in partial to full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for trellises or hanging baskets.

Its ornamental value comes from its lush foliage and seasonal flowering, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
