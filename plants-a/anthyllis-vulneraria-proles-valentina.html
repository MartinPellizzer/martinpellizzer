Anthyllis vulneraria proles valentina

Anthyllis vulneraria proles valentina, commonly known as the Valentina Scotch bonnet, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its legume-like pods and nitrogen-fixing ability. The plant features small, yellowish-white flowers arranged in dense, spike-like clusters. Its leaves are trifoliate, with three oval-shaped leaflets.
