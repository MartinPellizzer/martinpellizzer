Atkinsia

Atkinsia, commonly known as the silverleaf dandelion, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of silvery-gray, woolly leaves that help it retain moisture in arid conditions. The plant produces small, yellow composite flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are often used in traditional herbal medicine for their purported anti-inflammatory properties.

Atkinsia is a hardy perennial that thrives in well-drained soils and full sun.
