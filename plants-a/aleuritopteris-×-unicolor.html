Aleuritopteris × unicolor

Aleuritopteris × unicolor, commonly known as the hybrid water fern, is a fern species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a sterile hybrid between Aleuritopteris pterodonta and Aleuritopteris macgillivrayi, resulting in unique morphological characteristics. The plant features large, dichotomously branched fronds with a distinctive dark green color and a metallic sheen. Its leaves are typically pinnately divided and can grow up to several feet in length.

Aleuritopteris × unicolor thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.
