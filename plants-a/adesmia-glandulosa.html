Adesmia glandulosa

Adesmia glandulosa, commonly known as the glandular adesmia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, and it produces pod-like fruits that contain several seeds.

Adesmia glandulosa is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
