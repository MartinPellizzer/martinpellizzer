Atylosia reticulata subsp. maritima

Atylosia reticulata subsp.

maritima, commonly known as sea holly, is a perennial plant native to coastal regions of the Mediterranean. It features striking blue to purple composite flowers that bloom in late summer.

The plant has a rosette of silver-green, woolly leaves that help it retain moisture in arid conditions. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, often found in coastal dunes and rocky shores.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for xeriscaping and coastal gardens.
