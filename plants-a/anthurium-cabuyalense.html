Anthurium cabuyalense

Anthurium cabuyalense, commonly known as the Cabuyalense arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to the Amazon rainforest of South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive spathe and spadix inflorescence. The plant features broad, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a waxy texture. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Anthurium cabuyalense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated as a houseplant in tropical and subtropical regions.
