Appunia debilis

Appunia debilis, commonly known as the slender appunia, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to arid and semi-arid regions of southern Africa, particularly in countries like Namibia and South Africa. The plant is characterized by its small, daisy-like flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, typically blooming during the warmer months. It has a low-growing habit with narrow, linear leaves that help reduce water loss in its harsh environment.

Appunia debilis is often found in rocky soils and is adapted to withstand drought conditions.
