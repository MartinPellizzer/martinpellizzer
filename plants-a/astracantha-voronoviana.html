Astracantha voronoviana

Astracantha voronoviana, commonly known as the Voronov's thornveld, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asphodelaceae family and is characterized by its dense, spiny foliage and clusters of small, yellowish flowers. The plant is adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick, fleshy leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires minimal water, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is sometimes cultivated as an ornamental plant.
