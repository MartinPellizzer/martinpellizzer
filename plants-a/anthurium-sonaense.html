Anthurium sonaense

Anthurium sonaense, commonly known as the Sonora anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-like inflorescences that are often surrounded by colorful spathe. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Anthurium sonaense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated as a houseplant.
