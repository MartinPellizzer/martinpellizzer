Ammannia tolypobotrys f. tolypobotrys

Ammannia tolypobotrys f. tolypobotrys, commonly known as the redroot ammannia, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its erect, herbaceous stems that often exhibit a reddish hue, particularly at the base. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or red in color, which bloom during the warmer months. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a slightly pubescent surface.

This species is adapted to moist environments and is often found in wetlands or along riverbanks.
