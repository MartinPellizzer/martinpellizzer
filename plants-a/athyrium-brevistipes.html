Athyrium brevistipes

Athyrium brevistipes, commonly known as the short-stemmed Japanese shield fern, is a species of fern native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, upright growth habit and its distinctive, shield-shaped fronds. The plant typically has dark green, lanceolate leaves with a serrated margin and a short, sturdy rhizome. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Athyrium brevistipes is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its elegant foliage.
