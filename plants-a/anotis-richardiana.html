Anotis richardiana

Anotis richardiana, commonly known as the Cape daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant typically grows as a shrub or subshrub, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are usually narrow and slightly hairy, while the flowers are arranged in dense clusters.

Anotis richardiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
