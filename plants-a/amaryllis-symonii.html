Amaryllis symonii

Amaryllis symonii, commonly known as the Cape lily, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to deep red, often with contrasting stripes. The plant produces a tall, sturdy stem that can reach up to 2 meters in height, adorned with narrow, grass-like leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and containers.

Amaryllis symonii is known for its long blooming period, typically from late winter to early spring.
