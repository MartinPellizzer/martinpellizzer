Azorella crenata

It is native to the high-altitude regions of South America, particularly the Andes, where it thrives in cold and arid environments. Its leaves are typically fleshy and dark green, and it produces small, clustered flowers in shades of white or pink.

Azorella crenata is well adapted to extreme climates and plays a role in stabilizing soil in its native habitat.
