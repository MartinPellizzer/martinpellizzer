Acanthopanax leucorrhizus

Acanthopanax leucorrhizus, commonly known as the yellow arrowhead, is a deciduous shrub native to East Asia.

It belongs to the family Araliaceae and is characterized by its compound leaves and clusters of small white flowers. The plant produces red berries that are edible for birds and small mammals. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
