Axonopus plumosus

Axonopus plumosus, commonly known as the silky grass or velvetgrass, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit and its fine, soft, hair-like foliage that gives it a velvety appearance.
