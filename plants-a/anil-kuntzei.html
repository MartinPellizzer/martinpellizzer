Anil kuntzei

Anil kuntzei, commonly known as the desert dandelion, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble a single bloom. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that develop into fluffy, white seeds, which are dispersed by the wind. Its leaves are typically gray-green, covered in a waxy cuticle to reduce water loss.
