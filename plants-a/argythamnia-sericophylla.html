Argythamnia sericophylla

Argythamnia sericophylla, commonly known as the silky leaf desert-thistle, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of Australia.

It is characterized by its silver-green, silky-haired leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant is adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments with minimal water availability. Its growth habit is typically low and sprawling, with a compact structure.

It plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for small desert fauna.
