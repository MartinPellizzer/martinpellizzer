Anil Gracilis

Anil gracilis is a delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. It is known for its slender stems and clusters of small, pale blue flowers that bloom in early summer. This species thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in rocky soils along stream banks. Due to its rarity and unique appearance, it is a favorite among botanists and horticulturists. Conservation efforts are ongoing to protect this plant from habitat loss and over-collection.
