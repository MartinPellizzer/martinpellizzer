Adenodolichos harmsianus

Adenodolichos harmsianus, commonly known as the Harms' cowpea, is a leguminous plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its climbing habit, with trifoliate leaves and small, white to pale purple flowers. The plant produces elongated, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in warm, tropical climates and is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and fallow fields.

Its ability to fix nitrogen makes it valuable for soil enrichment in agricultural settings.
