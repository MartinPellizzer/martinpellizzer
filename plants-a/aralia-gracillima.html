Aralia gracillima

Aralia gracillima, commonly known as the laceleaf aralia, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its finely divided, feathery leaves that give it a delicate, lacy appearance. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers in umbels, which are followed by dark blue berries. It thrives in partial to full shade and prefers moist, well-drained soil.

Aralia gracillima is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
