Arctium outichaschense

Arctium outichaschense, commonly known as the Siberian burdock.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. The plant features a robust, hairy stem that can grow up to 1.5 meters in height. Its leaves are large, heart-shaped, and covered in fine, white hairs.

The flowers are purple and clustered in umbels, followed by the characteristic bur-like seed heads that aid in wind dispersal.
