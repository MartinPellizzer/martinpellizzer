Acianthera sicula

Acianthera sicula, commonly known as the Sicilian orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Mediterranean region, particularly Sicily.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces delicate, pinkish-white flowers with a distinct, sweet fragrance. Acianthera sicula is a rare species, often found in open, sunny habitats such as rocky soils and forest edges.
