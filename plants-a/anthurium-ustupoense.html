Anthurium ustupoense

Anthurium ustupoense, commonly known as the Ustupoense arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive inflorescence consisting of a spadix and a spathe. The plant features broad, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are often surrounded by a showy, modified leaf called a spathe.

Anthurium ustupoense is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ability to thrive in low-light conditions.
