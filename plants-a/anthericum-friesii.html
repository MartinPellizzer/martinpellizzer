Anthericum friesii

Anthericum friesii, commonly known as the African arum, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features a rhizomatous root system and produces large, heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive purple sheath. The plant's inflorescence consists of a spadix surrounded by a showy, greenish-white spathe. It blooms in spring and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Anthericum friesii prefers moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade.
