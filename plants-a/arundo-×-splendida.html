Arundo × splendida

Arundo × splendida, commonly known as the giant reed, is a hybrid grass species native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems that can grow up to 6 meters in height, with long, narrow leaves that are often silver-white in color. The plant produces fluffy, feathery seed heads that are highly visible during the flowering season. It thrives in moist environments and is often found near water sources such as rivers and wetlands.

Due to its rapid growth and adaptability, it is both a valuable ornamental plant and a potential invasive species in certain regions.
