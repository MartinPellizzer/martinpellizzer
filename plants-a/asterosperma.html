Asterosperma

Asterosperma, commonly known as the star-seed plant, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that typically feature yellow or white petals surrounding a central disk. The plant is native to various regions in Africa, Asia, and Australia, often growing in dry, rocky habitats.
