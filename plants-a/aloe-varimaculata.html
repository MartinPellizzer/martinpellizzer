Aloe varimaculata

Aloe varimaculata, commonly known as the Zebra Aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features striking, narrow, grass-like leaves that are often marked with distinctive white or yellow stripes. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with orange to red flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires plenty of sunlight.

Aloe varimaculata is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
