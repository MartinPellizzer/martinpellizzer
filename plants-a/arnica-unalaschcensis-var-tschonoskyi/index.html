Arnica Unalaschcensis Var. Tschonoskyi

Tschonosky's arnica, scientifically known as Arnica unalaschcensis var. tschonoskyi, is a rare and resilient plant native to the mountainous regions of Alaska. It thrives in harsh, alpine environments where few species can survive. This variant of arnica is distinguished by its unique floral structure and medicinal properties that have intrigued botanists for decades. Its limited distribution makes it a subject of conservation interest and scientific study. Despite its small size, it plays a vital role in its ecosystem and holds cultural significance for local communities.
