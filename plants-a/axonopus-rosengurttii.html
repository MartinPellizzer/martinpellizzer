Axonopus rosengurttii

Axonopus rosengurttii, commonly known as the Rosengurtt's axonopus, is a species of grass native to Southeast Asia.

It is a low-growing, mat-forming plant that thrives in moist, shaded environments. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves with a dark green color and a smooth texture. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically clustered in spikelets.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
