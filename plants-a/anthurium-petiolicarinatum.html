Anthurium petiolicarinatum

Anthurium petiolicarinatum, commonly known as the velvet leaf anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are dark green with a velvety texture. The plant produces clusters of small, spadix-like flowers surrounded by a vibrant spathe, typically in shades of red or orange. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Anthurium petiolicarinatum is a popular ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and air-purifying qualities.
