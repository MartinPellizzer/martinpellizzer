Ajax hispanicus

Ajax hispanicus, commonly known as the Spanish hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to deep pink. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters and has serrated, heart-shaped leaves. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

The flowers bloom in summer and are often used in traditional herbal remedies.
