Aster jussiei

Aster jussiei, commonly known as the Jussie's aster, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies, sunflowers, and other composite flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to arid environments. Its flowers are usually blue to purple in color, with ray florets surrounding a central disk of tubular flowers.

Aster jussiei is often found in open woodlands and grasslands, where it contributes to the biodiversity of its habitat.
