Alsodeia thoreliana

Alsodeia thoreliana, commonly known as the Thoreliana sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically features small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has a compact, mat-forming growth habit with dense clusters of foliage. Its leaves are usually narrow and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Alsodeia thoreliana is well adapted to sandy or gravelly soils and thrives in full sun.
