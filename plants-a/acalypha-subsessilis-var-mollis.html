Acalypha subsessilis var. mollis

Acalypha subsessilis var. mollis, commonly known as silky ironweed, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its soft, silky-hairy leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically green or yellowish in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or partially shaded environments.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
