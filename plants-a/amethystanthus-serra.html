Amethystanthus serra

Amethystanthus serra, commonly known as the serrated amethyst flower, is a striking plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant purple flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant features narrow, serrated leaves that give it its common name. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Amethystanthus serra is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and botanical collections.
