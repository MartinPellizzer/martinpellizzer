Achillea barrelieri subsp. elegans

Achillea barrelieri subsp.

elegans, commonly known as the elegant yarrow, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region. It features a compact growth habit with basal leaves that are lanceolate and covered in fine hairs.

The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in flat-topped inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its fragrant foliage and long flowering period.
