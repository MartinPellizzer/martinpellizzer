Asphodelus ozanonii

Asphodelus ozanonii, commonly known as the Ottoman asphodel, is a flowering plant native to Turkey.

It belongs to the family Asphodelaceae and is characterized by its grass-like leaves and striking yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to Mediterranean climates. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain its seeds.

Asphodelus ozanonii is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
