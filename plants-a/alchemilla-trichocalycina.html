Alchemilla trichocalycina

Alchemilla trichocalycina, commonly known as the woolly lady's mantle, is a perennial plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its fuzzy, silver-green foliage. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or meadow habitats.
