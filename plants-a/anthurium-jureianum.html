Anthurium jureianum

Anthurium jureianum, commonly known as the Jureianum arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped leaves that can range in color from deep green to dark red. The plant produces striking spadices, which are modified leaves that enclose the actual flowers, often in shades of yellow or orange. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Anthurium jureianum is popular as a decorative plant due to its ornamental foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
