Alliaria muelleri

Alliaria muelleri, commonly known as the slender garlic chive, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia and Australia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cress plants. The plant features slender, grass-like leaves and small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It is often grown as an ornamental and has a mild garlic scent.

Alliaria muelleri is also valued for its edible leaves, which can be used in cooking.
