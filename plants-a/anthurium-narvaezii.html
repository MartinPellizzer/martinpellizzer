Anthurium narvaezii

Anthurium narvaezii, commonly known as the velvet anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Colombia.

It is characterized by its striking, waxy, heart-shaped leaves that are often a deep green with a velvety texture. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-shaped inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, typically in shades of red or orange. It thrives in humid, warm environments with bright, indirect light.

Anthurium narvaezii is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
