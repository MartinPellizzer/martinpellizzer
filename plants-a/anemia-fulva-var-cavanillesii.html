Anemia fulva var. cavanillesii

Anemia fulva var. cavanillesii, commonly known as the Cavanilles' Anemia, is a variant of the Anemia fulva species.

It is a flowering plant characterized by its striking orange to red inflorescences. The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments, thriving in well-drained soils. Its leaves are usually narrow and lance-shaped, with a slightly hairy surface.

This variant is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant floral display.
