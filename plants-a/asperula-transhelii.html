Asperula transhelii

Asperula transhelii, commonly known as the Transheliola shrub, is a low-growing evergreen perennial native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe.

It features dense, compact foliage with small, leathery leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces clusters of tiny, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white or pale pink during the spring and early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or alpine settings.

Its hardy nature and ornamental appeal make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking a resilient, attractive shrub.
