Aeschynanthus maculatus

Aeschynanthus maculatus, commonly known as the spotted ivy leaf.

This evergreen shrub is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves with distinctive white or pale spots. It produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in late winter to early spring. The plant is often grown as a houseplant due to its compact size and ornamental appeal.

It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.
