Aster hortensis

Aster hortensis, commonly known as the garden aster, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that bloom in shades of purple, pink, and white. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are sturdy and often branched.

Aster hortensis is native to Europe and is widely cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.
