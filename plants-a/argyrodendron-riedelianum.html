Argyrodendron riedelianum

Argyrodendron riedelianum, commonly known as the silverwood tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and showy yellow flowers.
