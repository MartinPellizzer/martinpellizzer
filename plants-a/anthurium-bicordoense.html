Anthurium bicordoense

Anthurium bicordoense, commonly known as the Bicordo arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to the Amazon rainforest.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its unique inflorescence consisting of a spadix and spathe. The plant features large, heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and deep green coloration. Its striking red spadix is often surrounded by a white spathe, making it a popular ornamental plant.

Anthurium bicordoense thrives in humid, shady environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
