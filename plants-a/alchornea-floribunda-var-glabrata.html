Alchornea floribunda var. glabrata

Alchornea floribunda var. glabrata, commonly known as the smooth alchornea, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features simple, opposite, ovate to elliptic leaves that are typically smooth and glabrous. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers in terminal clusters, which are often borne on short pedicels. Its bark is fibrous and can be harvested for traditional uses.

The species is adapted to thrive in humid, shaded environments within its native habitat.
