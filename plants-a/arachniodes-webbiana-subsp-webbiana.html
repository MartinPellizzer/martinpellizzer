Arachniodes webbiana subsp. webbiana

Arachniodes webbiana subsp.

webbiana, commonly known as the silver fern, is a perennial herb native to Southeast Asia. It is characterized by its distinctive fronds that resemble a spider's web, giving it its scientific name.

The plant has a rhizomatous root system and prefers shady, moist environments. Its leaves are typically bipinnate, with a silvery sheen on the underside.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and air-purifying qualities.
