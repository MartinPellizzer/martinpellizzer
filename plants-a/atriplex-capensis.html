Atriplex capensis

Atriplex capensis, commonly known as the Cape saltbush, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its succulent leaves and small, greenish-yellow flowers. The plant is adapted to arid and saline environments, making it highly drought-resistant. It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is often found in coastal and inland sandy areas.

Its ability to thrive in poor soils makes it an important plant in ecological restoration projects.
