Astragalus sata-kandaoensis

Astragalus sata-kandaoensis, commonly known as the Sata-kandao milkvetch, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets. The plant produces yellow to orange flowers in racemes, which bloom in the summer months. Native to the mountainous regions of central Asia, it thrives in arid and semi-arid environments.

Due to its medicinal properties, it is sometimes cultivated for traditional herbal use.
