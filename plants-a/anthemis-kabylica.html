Anthemis kabylica

Anthemis kabylica, commonly known as the Kabylian anthemis, is a flowering plant native to the Balkan region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry habitats, showcasing its adaptability to harsh environments. Its yellow flowers are often accompanied by finely divided, linear leaves.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
