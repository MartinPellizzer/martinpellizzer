Alternanthera nigriceps

Alternanthera nigriceps, commonly known as black-headed alternate, is a striking aquatic plant native to South America.

It features elongated, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a contrasting reddish or blackish hue on the underside. The plant thrives in shallow, slow-moving waters and prefers moderate to high light conditions. Its unique coloration makes it a popular choice for aquariums and water gardens.

It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are often overlooked in favor of its ornamental foliage.
