Aporum araneum

Aporum araneum, commonly known as the spider plant, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are typically green with a waxy coating. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, which bloom in spring. It is a hardy species that thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Aporum araneum is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
