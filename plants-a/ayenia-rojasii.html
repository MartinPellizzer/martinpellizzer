Ayenia rojasii

Ayenia rojasii, commonly known as the Rosy Broom.

It is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Argentina and Chile. The plant features dense, bushy growth with bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring. Its leaves are narrow and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Ayenia rojasii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
