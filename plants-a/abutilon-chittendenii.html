Abutilon chittendenii

Abutilon chittendenii, commonly known as the Chittenden's abutilon, is a species of flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments. The plant features heart-shaped leaves and produces showy, lantern-shaped flowers in shades of yellow to orange. It has a fibrous root system that helps it survive in dry conditions.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens in suitable climates.
