Athyrium wuyishanense

Athyrium wuyishanense, commonly known as the Wuyi Mountain Athyrium, is a species of fern native to China.

It belongs to the Athyriaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors and rocky crevices. Its spore-bearing structures, called sori, are located on the underside of the fronds.

Athyrium wuyishanense is valued for its ornamental appeal and plays a role in the ecological balance of its native habitat.
