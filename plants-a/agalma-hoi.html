Agalma hoi

Agalma hoi, commonly known as the sacred flame orchid, is a rare and striking plant native to the high-altitude regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant red and orange flowers, which resemble flames, giving it its evocative common name. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and requires partial shade to flourish. Its leaves are broad and waxy, adapted to retain moisture in its native habitat.

Agalma hoi is also noted for its unique fragrance, which is said to emit a subtle, spicy aroma.
