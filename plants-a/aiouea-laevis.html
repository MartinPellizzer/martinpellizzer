Aiouea laevis

Aiouea laevis, commonly known as the smooth aiouea, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae, which includes laurels and cinnamon trees. The plant is characterized by its smooth, pale bark and elliptical, dark green leaves. It produces small, white flowers followed by spherical, woody fruits containing smooth, oval seeds.

Aiouea laevis is often found in lowland forests and is valued for its ornamental and potential medicinal properties.
