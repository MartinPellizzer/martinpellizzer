Anthurium cucullatum

Anthurium cucullatum, commonly known as the hooded anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are often dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-shaped inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, which can range from red to yellow. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.

Anthurium cucullatum is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
