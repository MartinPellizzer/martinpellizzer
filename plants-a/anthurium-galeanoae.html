Anthurium galeanoae

Anthurium galeanoae, commonly known as the Galeano's anthurium, is a striking tropical plant native to Colombia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces vibrant spadices, which are the modified leaves that enclose the true flowers, often in shades of red or orange. It thrives in humid, warm environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.

Anthurium galeanoae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in botanical gardens.
