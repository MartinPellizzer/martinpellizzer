Assonia rotundifolia

Assonia rotundifolia, commonly known as the round-leafed assonia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, daisy-like flowers that bloom in shades of yellow and white. The plant features rounded, velvety leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Assonia rotundifolia is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
