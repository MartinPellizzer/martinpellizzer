Aster biennis

Aster biennis, commonly known as the Chinese aster, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, pink, and purple. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet and has lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stems. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.

The flowers are composed of a central disk surrounded by ray florets, which are the showy petals visible to the eye.
