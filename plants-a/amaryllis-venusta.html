Amaryllis venusta

Amaryllis venusta, commonly known as the elegant amaryllis, is a striking bulbous plant native to South America.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that come in shades of red, white, and pink, often with a contrasting throat. The plant produces strap-like, dark green leaves that are broad and fleshy, adapted for storing water. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and containers.

Amaryllis venusta is known for its long blooming period and ability to flower multiple times throughout the growing season.
