Adenophora polyantha f. eriocaulis

Adenophora polyantha f. eriocaulis, commonly known as the hairy bellflower, is a flowering plant belonging to the Campanulaceae family.

It is characterized by its bell-shaped, white to pale blue flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant features hairy stems and leaves, which give it its common name. It is native to eastern Asia, particularly China, where it thrives in woodland and meadow environments.

This cultivar is often appreciated for its ornamental value in gardens due to its delicate appearance and long blooming period.
