Anacampseros ciliaris

Anacampseros ciliaris, commonly known as the silver leaf ice plant, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, triangular leaves that are covered in a fine, white powdery coating, which helps reduce water loss. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or purple during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its ability to store water in its leaves makes it drought-tolerant and suitable for xeriscaping.
