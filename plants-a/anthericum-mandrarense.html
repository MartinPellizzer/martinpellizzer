Anthericum mandrarense

Anthericum mandrarense, commonly known as the mandrake lily, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It belongs to the Amaryllis family and is characterized by its striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to deep purple. Anthericum mandrarense is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
