Adenandra brachyphylla

Adenandra brachyphylla, commonly known as the narrow-leaved adenandra, is a small evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Proteaceae and is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit. The plant features narrow, leathery leaves that are typically arranged in whorls around the stems. Its flowers are small and white, often clustered in dense, spherical inflorescences.
