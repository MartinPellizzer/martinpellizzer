Agelanthus Crassifolius

Broadleaf agelanthus, scientifically known as Agelanthus crassifolius, is a unique and resilient plant native to specific regions of Africa. It is characterized by its broad, waxy leaves that help it conserve moisture in arid environments. This species thrives in rocky soils and is often found in semi-desert landscapes. Its ability to withstand harsh conditions makes it an important part of its ecosystem. Despite its hardiness, it remains a lesser-known but ecologically significant plant.
