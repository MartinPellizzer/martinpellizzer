Anthurium gerardoi

Anthurium gerardoi, commonly known as the velvet leaf anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are dark green with a velvety texture. The plant produces vibrant red spadices, which are the showy parts of its inflorescence, surrounded by a spathe. Anthurium gerardoi thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Its unique foliage and colorful flowers make it a popular choice for indoor gardening and ornamental purposes.
