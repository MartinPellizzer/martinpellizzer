Adenophora stricta var. qinglongshanica

Adenophora stricta var. qinglongshanica, commonly known as the Qinglongshan bellflower, is a perennial herb native to China.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with a hairy texture, and its stems are often covered in fine pubescence. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is typically found in mountainous regions.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
