Athanasia mundii

Athanasia mundii, commonly known as the African ironwood, is a small, evergreen shrub native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has a deep root system, allowing it to thrive in poor, sandy soils and withstand prolonged droughts. Its hard, heavy wood is highly valued for its durability and resistance to decay.

Athanasia mundii plays an important ecological role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for local wildlife.
