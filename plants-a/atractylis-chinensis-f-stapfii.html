Atractylis chinensis f. stapfii

Atractylis chinensis f. stapfii, commonly known as the Chinese turmeric or Chinese ginger, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit. The plant features aromatic leaves with a distinct yellowish-green coloration and produces small, yellowish composite flowers. It is often cultivated for its rhizomes, which are used in traditional medicine and culinary applications.

The foliage is typically hairy, and the plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils.
