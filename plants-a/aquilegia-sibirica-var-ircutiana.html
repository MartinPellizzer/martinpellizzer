Aquilegia sibirica var. ircutiana

Aquilegia sibirica var. ircutiana, commonly known as the Siberian delphinium, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features showy, spurred flowers that range in color from blue to purple, often with a contrasting white or yellow base. The plant has compound leaves with finely divided foliage, typical of the Ranunculaceae family. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and alpine displays.
