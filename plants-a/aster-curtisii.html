Aster curtisii

Aster curtisii, commonly known as Curtis' aster, is a flowering plant native to the United States.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers. This perennial plant typically grows in open woodlands and prairies, preferring well-drained soils. Its showy blue to purple flowers bloom in the late summer and early fall.

Curtis' aster is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting pollinators.
