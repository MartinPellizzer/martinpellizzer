Amaranthus tristis var. leptostachys

Amaranthus tristis var. leptostachys, commonly known as slender amaranth, is a herbaceous annual plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically green or red in color. It thrives in a variety of soil types and is often found in disturbed areas such as roadsides and fields.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional medicine in some cultures.
