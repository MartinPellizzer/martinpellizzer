Aloe × imerinensis

Aloe × imerinensis, commonly known as the hybrid aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is a hybrid between Aloe aristata and Aloe ferox, resulting in a plant with unique characteristics. The leaves are fleshy, spiny, and often have a bluish-green hue. It produces yellow flowers during the summer months, adding ornamental value.

This plant is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it suitable for xeriscaping.
