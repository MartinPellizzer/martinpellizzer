Artemisia billardiereana

Artemisia billardiereana, commonly known as the grey needlebill, is a perennial shrub native to southeastern Australia.

It is characterized by its aromatic, silver-gray foliage and small, inconspicuous yellow flowers. The plant thrives in dry, well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands and grasslands. Its leaves contain volatile oils that contribute to its strong, minty scent.

Artemisia billardiereana is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
