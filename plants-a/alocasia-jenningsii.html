Alocasia jenningsii

Alocasia jenningsii, commonly known as the Blue Velvet Alocasia, is a striking ornamental plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features large, heart-shaped leaves with a deep blue-green hue and prominent veins, giving it a dramatic appearance. The plant thrives in humid, shady environments and requires well-draining, rich soil to flourish. It is a member of the Araceae family, characterized by its unique inflorescence consisting of a spadix and spathe.

Alocasia jenningsii is popular in tropical gardens and as a houseplant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
