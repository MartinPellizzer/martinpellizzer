Antirrhinum murale var. mauritii

Antirrhinum murale var. mauritii, commonly known as the Mauritius wallflower, is a flowering plant native to the island of Mauritius.

It is a member of the Plantaginaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant yellow or orange flowers.
