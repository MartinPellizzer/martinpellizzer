Aloe cyrillei

Aloe cyrillei, commonly known as the Cape aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, narrow leaves that are typically green with a reddish or purple tint along the edges. The plant produces striking red or orange flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Aloe cyrillei is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional medicinal uses.
