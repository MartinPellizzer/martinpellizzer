Achyranthes aspera var. pubescens

Achyranthes aspera var. pubescens, commonly known as the hairy cowhage, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Amaranthaceae and is characterized by its pubescent stems and leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in spikes, which are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in agricultural fields and along roadsides.

The species is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
