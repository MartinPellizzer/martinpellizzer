Aristolochia japonica

Aristolochia japonica, commonly known as Japanese arrowroot or Japanese birthwort, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is a perennial vine characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and showy, fragrant flowers that range in color from greenish-white to purple. The plant produces capsule fruits containing numerous small, dark seeds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

However, it contains toxic compounds and should be handled with care.
