Anemone apennina var. pallida

Anemone apennina var. pallida, commonly known as the pale anemone, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features showy, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has basal leaves that are palmately lobed and covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and alpine settings.
