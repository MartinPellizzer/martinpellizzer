Alocasia cuspidata

Alocasia cuspidata, commonly known as the arrow leaf alocasia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 3 feet in length. The plant features a rhizomatous root system and produces striking, spadix-shaped inflorescences. It thrives in humid, shaded environments with well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in tropical gardens.
