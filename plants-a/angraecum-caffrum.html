Angraecum caffrum

Angraecum caffrum, commonly known as the Cape orchid, is a species of orchid native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem and large, showy flowers that bloom in winter. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences with numerous white or pale green flowers that have a distinct, sweet scent. Angraecum caffrum is adapted to grow in rocky or sandy soils and requires bright light and well-draining conditions.

It is a popular ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and unique flowering pattern.
