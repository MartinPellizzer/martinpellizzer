Anathallis muscoidea

Anathallis muscoidea, commonly known as the mossy anathallis, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in tropical rainforests. Its leaves are usually moss-like, contributing to its common name.

Anathallis muscoidea is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is known for its unique floral structure and ecological significance.
