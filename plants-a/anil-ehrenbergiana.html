Anil ehrenbergiana

Anil ehrenbergiana, commonly known as the desert star cactus, is a succulent plant native to the arid regions of the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It features a distinctive, spiny, globular stem that stores water, allowing it to survive in harsh, dry environments. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months, adding a vibrant touch to its otherwise muted appearance. Its leaves are reduced to spines, which help reduce water loss and deter herbivores.

Anil ehrenbergiana is well adapted to full sun and requires minimal water, making it a hardy choice for xeriscaping.
