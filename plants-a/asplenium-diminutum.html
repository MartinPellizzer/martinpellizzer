Asplenium diminutum

Asplenium diminutum, commonly known as the small spleenwort, is a small, epiphytic fern belonging to the family Aspleniaceae.

It typically grows on tree trunks or rocks in moist, shaded environments. The plant has delicate, lance-shaped leaves that are usually less than 5 centimeters in length. Its fronds are often clustered closely together, forming a compact rosette.

Asplenium diminutum is characterized by its small size and is often found in temperate and tropical regions.
