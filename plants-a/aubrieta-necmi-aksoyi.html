Aubrieta necmi-aksoyi

Aubrieta necmi-aksoyi, commonly known as the Turkish rockcress, is a low-growing perennial plant native to Turkey.

Aubrieta necmi-aksoyi is often cultivated for its ornamental value in rock gardens and as a hardy ground cover.
