Astrochlaena tubiflora

Astrochlaena tubiflora, commonly known as the tuberous aster, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its tuberous roots and showy, star-shaped flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its foliage consists of lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

Astrochlaena tubiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
