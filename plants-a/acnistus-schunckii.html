Acnistus schunckii

Acnistus schunckii, commonly known as the velvetleaf or false hibiscus, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture, giving it its common name. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along riverbanks.

Acnistus schunckii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
