Aegiphila caucensis

Aegiphila caucensis, commonly known as Caucasian aegiphila, is a flowering plant native to the Caucasus region.

It is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

Aegiphila caucensis has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem, with a silvery-gray upper surface and a lighter green underside.
