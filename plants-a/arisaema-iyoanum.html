Arisaema iyoanum

Arisaema iyoanum, commonly known as the Japanese mountain arum, is a herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Japan.

It features a distinctive, arrow-shaped leaf that is often covered in a dense layer of fine hairs. The plant produces a spadix surrounded by a spathe, which is typically green with a white or yellowish tint. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forested areas.

Arisaema iyoanum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
