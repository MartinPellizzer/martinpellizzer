Albizia papuensis

Albizia papuensis, commonly known as the Papuan Albizia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its root system is capable of fixing nitrogen, contributing to soil fertility.

Albizia papuensis is often found in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests and along riverbanks.
