Astragalus mirus

Astragalus mirus, commonly known as the Chinese milkvetch, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to China and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow or pink flowers. The plant produces small, lens-shaped pods that contain several seeds. It is often found in arid and semi-arid regions, thriving in well-drained soils.

Astragalus mirus is also valued in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.
