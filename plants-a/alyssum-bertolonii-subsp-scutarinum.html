Alyssum bertolonii subsp. scutarinum

Alyssum bertolonii subsp.

scutarinum, commonly known as the rock alissum, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and North Africa. It is a low-growing perennial herb with small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink.

The plant produces clusters of four to six flowers on short pedicels, and its leaves are typically narrow and linear. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
