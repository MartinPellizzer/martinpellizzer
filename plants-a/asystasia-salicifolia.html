Asystasia salicifolia

Asystasia salicifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf asystasia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that belongs to the Acanthaceae family. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth texture and a bluish-green hue. It produces small, tubular flowers in clusters, typically in shades of purple or pink.

Asystasia salicifolia is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
