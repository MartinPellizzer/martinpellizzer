Amethystanthus koroensis

Amethystanthus koroensis, commonly known as the Koroense amethyst flower, is a striking member of the Acanthaceae family native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its vivid purple to violet flowers that bloom in clusters, creating a striking visual display. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its foliage consists of narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, powdery bloom.

Amethystanthus koroensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique and vibrant floral display.
