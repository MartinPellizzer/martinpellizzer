Aeonium × lowei

Aeonium × lowei, commonly known as the "Sunburst Aeonium," is a hybrid succulent plant native to the Canary Islands.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy leaves that range from green to red, depending on light exposure. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow flowers in the spring. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with full sun.

Aeonium × lowei is popular in rock gardens and as a decorative houseplant due to its striking appearance and ease of care.
