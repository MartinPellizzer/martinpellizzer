Ardisia cymosa var. cymosa

Ardisia cymosa var. cymosa, commonly known as the glossy wood-sorrel, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are typically oval to elliptic in shape. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers arranged in cymes, which are clusters that open sequentially. Its fruit is a bright red, fleshy berry that contains several seeds.

This species is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and seasonal flowering.
