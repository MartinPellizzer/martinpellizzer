Asplenium splendens

Asplenium splendens, commonly known as the Boston fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It features feathery, arching fronds that are typically bright green and slightly glossy. The plant has a rhizomatous root system that allows it to spread and form dense clumps. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires consistent moisture and well-draining soil.

Its elegant appearance makes it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor ornamental gardening.
