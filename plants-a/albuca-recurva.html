Albuca recurva

Albuca recurva, commonly known as the zigzag albuca, is a striking bulbous plant native to southern Africa.

It features long, grass-like leaves that curl and twist, giving it its distinctive zigzag appearance. The plant produces clusters of white or pale yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Albuca recurva is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to care for once established.
