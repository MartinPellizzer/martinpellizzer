Aglaonema decurrens

Aglaonema decurrens, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated foliage with dark green leaves streaked in silver and white. The plant thrives in low-light conditions and is highly tolerant of indoor environments. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and can grow up to 30 cm in length.

Aglaonema decurrens is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and air-purifying properties.
