Aspidium marginale var. elegans

Aspidium marginale var. elegans, commonly known as the elegant water fern, is a variant of the common water fern, characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds.

It is typically found in moist, shaded environments such as stream banks and forest floors. The plant belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is known for its rhizomatous growth habit. Its fronds are usually bi- or tri-pinnate, with a graceful, arching appearance.

This variety is often appreciated for its ornamental value in aquatic and humid garden settings.
