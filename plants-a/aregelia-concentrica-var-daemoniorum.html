Aregelia concentrica var. daemoniorum

Aregelia concentrica var. daemoniorum, commonly known as the Devil's Orchid, is a rare and striking member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of South America, particularly in Ecuador and Peru, where it thrives in cool, humid climates. The plant is characterized by its intricate, spiral-shaped inflorescence and vibrant, jewel-toned flowers that range from deep purples to bright yellows. Its unique morphology and vivid coloration make it a subject of fascination for botanists and horticulturists alike.
