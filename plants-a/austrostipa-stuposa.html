Austrostipa stuposa

Austrostipa stuposa, commonly known as the slender speargrass, is a grass species native to southern Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect growth habit and spikelets that are usually pale green to brown in color. The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in open habitats such as plains and hillsides. Austrostipa stuposa is a perennial species that produces small, wind-dispersed seeds.
