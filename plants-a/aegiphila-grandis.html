Aegiphila grandis

Aegiphila grandis, commonly known as the large-leaved aegiphila, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean region.

Aegiphila grandis is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.

Morphology

Its flowers are small, clustered in inflorescences, with white to pale pink petals, blooming during the dry season, and are pollinated primarily by insects.
