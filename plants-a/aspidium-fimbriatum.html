Aspidium fimbriatum

Aspidium fimbriatum, commonly known as the fringed water fern, is a species of aquatic fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its finely divided, feathery fronds that give it a delicate, lacy appearance. Aspidium fimbriatum plays a role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat for small aquatic organisms and contributing to water filtration.
