Arachnorchis amnicola

Arachnorchis amnicola, commonly known as the river orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Orchidaceae and is characterized by its slender, grass-like appearance. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as along riverbanks and in forested areas.

Arachnorchis amnicola is notable for its unique floral structure and is often found in regions with high humidity and cool temperatures.
