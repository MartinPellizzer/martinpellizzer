Asphodeliris calyculata

Asphodeliris calyculata, commonly known as the Cape iris, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces striking, tubular flowers that range in color from white to violet. The plant typically grows in well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. It is often found in rocky or sandy habitats, showcasing its adaptability to arid environments.

Asphodeliris calyculata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in gardens.
