Agathosma linifolia

Agathosma linifolia, commonly known as sweet everlasting, is a perennial shrub native to South Africa.

It features aromatic, needle-like leaves that are often used in herbal teas and traditional medicine. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring, adding a subtle ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its essential oils are valued for their fragrance and potential therapeutic properties.
