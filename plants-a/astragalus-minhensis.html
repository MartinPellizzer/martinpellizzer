Astragalus minhensis

Astragalus minhensis, commonly known as the Min River milkvetch, is a perennial legume native to China.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its root system is deep, allowing it to access water from lower soil layers.

Astragalus minhensis is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional Chinese medicine.
