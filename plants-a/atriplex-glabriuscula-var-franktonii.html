Atriplex glabriuscula var. franktonii

Atriplex glabriuscula var. franktonii, commonly known as Frankton's saltbush, is a shrub native to arid and semi-arid regions of Australia.

It is characterized by its small, scale-like leaves that reduce water loss, making it well adapted to dry environments. The plant produces small greenish flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in saline soils and is often found in coastal and inland dune areas.

Its ecological importance includes providing habitat and food for various wildlife species.
