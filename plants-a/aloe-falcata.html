Aloe falcata

Aloe falcata, commonly known as the sword aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features tall, narrow, sword-shaped leaves that are often bluish-green in color. The plant produces clusters of yellow flowers during the summer months, which are followed by red, fleshy fruits. Aloe falcata is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Its leaves contain a gel-like substance that is sometimes used for its medicinal properties.
