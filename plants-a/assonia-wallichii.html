Assonia wallichii

Assonia wallichii, commonly known as the Kashmir rose or Himalayan rose, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayas.

It is a shrub that typically grows in high-altitude regions, reaching heights of up to 2 meters. The plant produces showy, pink or white flowers that bloom in spring and summer. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a slightly serrated edge.

Assonia wallichii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
