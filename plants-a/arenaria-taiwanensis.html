Arenaria taiwanensis

Arenaria taiwanensis, commonly known as the Taiwan sandwort, is a flowering plant native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its small, delicate flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, often in alpine or mountainous regions. It has basal leaves that are linear and may be covered with fine hairs.

The flowers are usually pink or white and bloom in early spring.
