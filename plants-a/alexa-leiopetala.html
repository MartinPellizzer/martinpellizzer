Alexa leiopetala

Alexa leiopetala, commonly known as the slender leaf orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and yellow. The plant has linear, narrow leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern, giving it a unique appearance. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species in its natural range.
