Amaryllis nelsonii

Amaryllis nelsonii, commonly known as the Nelson's amaryllis, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, strap-like leaves and produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant blooms in late summer to early autumn, adding vibrant color to gardens. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Amaryllis nelsonii is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking bold, seasonal blooms.
