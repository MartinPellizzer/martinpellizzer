Asperula aristata subsp. laevis

Asperula aristata subsp.

laevis, commonly known as the smooth asperula, is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to the mountainous regions of Europe. It features small, oval-shaped leaves that are smooth and glossy, with a dark green hue.

The plant produces clusters of tiny, white to pale pink flowers in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a ground cover.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking a hardy, decorative plant.
