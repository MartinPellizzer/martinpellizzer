Amethystanthus effusus var. leucanthus

Amethystanthus effusus var. leucanthus, commonly known as the white amethyst flower, is a striking member of the Acanthaceae family.

It is native to southern Africa, particularly South Africa, where it thrives in rocky, well-drained soils. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
