Ajania fruticulosa var. kuenlunica

Ajania fruticulosa var. kuenlunica, commonly known as the Kuenlun ajania, is a shrub native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense foliage. The plant features small, narrow leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white hair, giving it a silvery appearance. Its flowers are typically blue to purple in color, arranged in clusters, and bloom in the summer months.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its hardy nature and visual interest.
