Artemisia fulgens var. meiguensis

Artemisia fulgens var. meiguensis, commonly known as the Meigu golden sage, is a perennial shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its aromatic, silvery-green foliage and bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping. Its essential oils contain compounds with potential medicinal properties.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in native plant landscapes.
