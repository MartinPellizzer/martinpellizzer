Amaranthus × ozanonii

Amaranthus × ozanonii, commonly known as the red leaf amaranth, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Amaranthaceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant red foliage, which becomes more intense as the plant matures. The leaves are typically narrow and lance-shaped, with a textured surface that adds to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

This plant is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators and its resilience to various environmental conditions.
