Alchemilla sylvestris var. micans

Alchemilla sylvestris var. micans, commonly known as the silver-sprayed lady's mantle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It is characterized by its dense, silver-white fuzzy foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in panicles during the summer months. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Its leaves are used in traditional herbal medicine for their mild astringent properties.
