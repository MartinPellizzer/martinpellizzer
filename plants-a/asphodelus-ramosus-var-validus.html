Asphodelus ramosus var. validus

Asphodelus ramosus var. validus, commonly known as the narrow-leaved asphodel, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellowish-green flowers. It is well adapted to arid environments and has a relatively long flowering period.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in challenging climates.
