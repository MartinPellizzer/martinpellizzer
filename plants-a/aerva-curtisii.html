Aerva curtisii

Aerva curtisii, commonly known as the Indian spurge or Curtis' spurge, is a flowering plant native to the Indian subcontinent.

It belongs to the family Amaranthaceae and is characterized by its erect, branching stems and small, clustered leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in summer, and its seeds are dispersed by wind. Aerva curtisii is often found in dry, disturbed areas and is adapted to arid conditions.
