Arceuthobium campylopodum var. cryptopodum

Arceuthobium campylopodum var. cryptopodum, commonly known as the cryptopod seed tickweed, is a parasitic plant that relies entirely on its host for survival.

It belongs to the Orobanchaceae family and lacks chlorophyll, making it incapable of photosynthesis. Due to its parasitic nature, it is rarely seen above ground, thriving in shaded, nutrient-poor environments.
