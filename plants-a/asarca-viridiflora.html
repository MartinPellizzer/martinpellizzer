Asarca viridiflora

Asarca viridiflora, commonly known as green-flowered asarum, is a perennial herb native to the forests of eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in temperate regions. Its rhizomatous root system allows it to spread and form dense colonies.

Asarca viridiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
