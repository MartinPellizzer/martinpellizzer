Aronicum carpaticum

Aronicum carpaticum, commonly known as Carpathian arnica, is a perennial herb native to the Carpathian Mountains.

It features upright, branched stems covered in fine, hairy hairs. The plant produces yellow-orange flowers in clusters, which bloom in late summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly serrated edge.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or meadow habitats.
