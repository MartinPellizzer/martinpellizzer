Aeonium × praegeri

Aeonium × praegeri, commonly known as the "Coral Cactus," is a hybrid succulent plant native to the Canary Islands.

It features rosettes of fleshy, triangular leaves that range in color from green to red, depending on light exposure. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow flowers in the spring. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with full sun.

Aeonium × praegeri is popular in rock gardens and as a decorative indoor plant due to its striking appearance and low maintenance requirements.
