Acalypha pauciflora f. umbrosa

Acalypha pauciflora f. umbrosa, commonly known as the shade-loving acalypha, is a variant of the acalypha plant characterized by its unique foliage and growth habits.

It is native to tropical regions and thrives in low-light conditions, making it suitable for indoor cultivation. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and often displays a striking coloration, ranging from deep green to variegated patterns. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow, which are not the primary ornamental feature.

This foliage plant is valued for its aesthetic appeal and ability to adapt to shaded environments.
