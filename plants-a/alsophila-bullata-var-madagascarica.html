Alsophila bullata var. madagascarica

Alsophila bullata var. madagascarica, commonly known as the Madagascar tree fern, is a species native to Madagascar.

It is a member of the Cyatheaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate fronds. The plant has a robust rhizome system that allows it to thrive in humid, shaded environments. Its fronds are typically dark green and can grow up to several meters in length.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
