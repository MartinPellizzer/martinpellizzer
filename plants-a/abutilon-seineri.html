Abutilon seineri

Abutilon seineri, commonly known as the velvetleaf abutilon, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and fluffy, lantern-like seed pods that turn from green to brown as they mature. The plant produces showy yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, adding ornamental value to gardens. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal.

Abutilon seineri is also used in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
