Achimenes carminata-splendens

Achimenes carminata-splendens, commonly known as the flame violet, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range from red to orange and yellow, often with a contrasting central spot. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are dark green and slightly hairy. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Achimenes carminata-splendens is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
