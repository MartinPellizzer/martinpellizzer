Armeria foucaudii

Armeria foucaudii, commonly known as the rock sandwort, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, low-growing habit and produces clusters of white or pink flowers. The plant thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are densely packed.

Armeria foucaudii is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance in gardens.
