Anisostachya atrorubra

Anisostachya atrorubra, commonly known as the purple-red grass, is a tall, perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking purple to reddish hues in its foliage and inflorescence, which give it its common name. The plant typically grows in open woodlands and grasslands, where it thrives in well-drained soils. Its inflorescence is a dense, cylindrical panicle with long, slender branches.

Anisostachya atrorubra is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant color and architectural form.
