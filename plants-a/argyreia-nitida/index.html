Argyreia Nitida

Argyreia nitida, commonly known as the silver vine, is a tropical climbing plant native to Southeast Asia. It is prized for its striking silver-green foliage and its ability to grow rapidly along supports. This plant is often used in ornamental gardening due to its aesthetic appeal and ease of care. Its leaves are also known to be mildly toxic to cats, making it important for pet owners to be aware. Despite its beauty, it requires proper care to thrive in various environments.
