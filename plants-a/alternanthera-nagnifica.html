Alternanthera nagnifica

Alternanthera nagnifica, commonly known as the red flame plant, is a strikingly colorful aquatic plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red and orange leaves that often change with environmental conditions. The plant thrives in shallow, warm waters and prefers moderate to high light levels. Its foliage is often used in ornamental pond settings due to its aesthetic appeal.

It is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for aquariums and water gardens.
