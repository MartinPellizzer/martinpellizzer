Aristida uniplumis var. neesii

Aristida uniplumis var. neesii, commonly known as the Nees's three-awn, is a grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, wiry stems and small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue.

It is often used in ornamental gardening due to its delicate appearance and drought tolerance.
