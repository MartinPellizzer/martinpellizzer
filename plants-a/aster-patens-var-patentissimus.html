Aster patens var. patentissimus

Aster patens var. patentissimus, commonly known as the showy aster, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its vibrant blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer and early fall. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and woodland edges. It has a spreading growth habit with hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators like butterflies and bees.
