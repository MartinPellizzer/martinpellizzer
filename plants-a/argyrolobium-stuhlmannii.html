Argyrolobium stuhlmannii

Argyrolobium stuhlmannii, commonly known as the silver globular pea, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive silver-gray foliage and spherical, globular seed pods. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its unique appearance and ornamental value, it is cultivated in some regions as a decorative plant.
