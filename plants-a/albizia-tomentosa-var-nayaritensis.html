Albizia tomentosa var. nayaritensis

Albizia tomentosa var. nayaritensis, commonly known as the Nayarit Albizia, is a shrub or small tree native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, fragrant flowers. The plant is known for its hairy, grayish foliage and produces pods that contain seeds. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often found in dry, rocky soils.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential ecological benefits in native habitats.
