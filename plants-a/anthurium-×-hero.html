Anthurium × hero

Anthurium × hero, commonly known as the Red Knight, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Araceae family.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a reddish tint. The plant produces vibrant, spathiflorous inflorescences with bright orange to red spathes and yellow spadices. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Anthurium × hero is popular as a decorative plant due to its ornamental foliage and long-lasting blooms.
