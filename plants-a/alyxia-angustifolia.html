Alyxia angustifolia

Alyxia angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf alyxia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a climbing shrub that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features small, elliptical leaves that are often arranged alternately along the stems. Its flowers are usually white or pale yellow, clustered in panicles, and are fragrant.

Alyxia angustifolia is often found in moist, shaded habitats such as forests and along stream banks.
