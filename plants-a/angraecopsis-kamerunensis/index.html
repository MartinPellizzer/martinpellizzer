Angraecopsis Kamerunensis

Angraecopsis kamerunensis is a rare and striking member of the orchid family found in the mountainous regions of Cameroon. This unique plant features elegant, fragrant flowers that bloom at night, attracting specific pollinators in its native habitat. Its delicate appearance belies its resilience, thriving in challenging environmental conditions. Due to habitat loss and limited distribution, it is considered endangered and requires conservation efforts. Studying Angraecopsis kamerunensis offers valuable insights into ecological adaptation and biodiversity preservation.
