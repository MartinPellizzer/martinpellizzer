Asclepias grantii

Its leaves are opposite and oval-shaped, with a hairy texture that helps reduce water loss in arid environments.

Asclepias grantii plays a crucial role in supporting local ecosystems by providing nectar for pollinators and serving as a host plant for monarch caterpillars.
