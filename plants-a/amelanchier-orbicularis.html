Amelanchier orbicularis

Amelanchier orbicularis, commonly known as the rounded serviceberry, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to eastern North America.

It is characterized by its oval-shaped leaves and produces small, edible berries that ripen in the summer. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in partial to full sun. It is valued for its ornamental qualities, including its spring blossoms and autumn foliage.

Amelanchier orbicularis is also an important food source for wildlife and birds.
