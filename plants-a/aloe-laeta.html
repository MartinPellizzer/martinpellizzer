Aloe laeta

Aloe laeta, commonly known as the silver aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically pale green with a silvery-blue hue due to a waxy coating. The plant produces tall, branched flower stalks that bear yellowish-green flowers, which bloom in spring. Aloe laeta is adapted to arid environments, storing water in its leaves to survive drought conditions.

Its leaves are often used for their gel, which has mild healing properties.
