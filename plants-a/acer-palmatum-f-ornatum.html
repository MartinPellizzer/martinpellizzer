Acer palmatum f. ornatum

Acer palmatum f. ornatum, commonly known as the Japanese maple with ornate leaves, is a cultivar of the Japanese maple tree.

It is characterized by its finely dissected, lace-like foliage that displays a striking range of colors throughout the growing season. The plant is native to Japan and thrives in partial shade with well-drained soil. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens and landscapes for its aesthetic value.

This variety is relatively slow-growing and requires minimal pruning to maintain its natural form.
