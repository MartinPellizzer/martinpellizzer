Asarca semibarbata

Asarca semibarbata, commonly known as the semi-barbed asarum, is a perennial herb native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Aristolochiaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and showy, hooded flowers. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its flowers are typically greenish-yellow with a distinctive, intricate pattern.

Asarca semibarbata is known for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally in herbal remedies.
