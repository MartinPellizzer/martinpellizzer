Amaranthus neei

Amaranthus neei, commonly known as the narrowleaf amaranth, is a species of flowering plant in the amaranth family.

It is native to Central and South America and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from green to red, depending on the variety. Amaranthus neei is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.

It is also valued for its potential use in traditional medicine and as a source of edible seeds.
