Acrostichum lomarioides

Acrostichum lomarioides, commonly known as the giant fern or tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its large, feather-like fronds that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant has a thick, woody trunk that supports its massive crown of leaves. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine.

The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and plays an important role in tropical ecosystems.
