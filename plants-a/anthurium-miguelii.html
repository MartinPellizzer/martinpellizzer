Anthurium miguelii

Anthurium miguelii, commonly known as the Miguel's arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive inflorescence consisting of a spadix and a spathe. The plant features broad, arrow-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a waxy texture. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers surrounded by a showy, brightly colored spathe.

Anthurium miguelii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and thrives in humid, warm environments with indirect light.
