Armatocereus laetus

Armatocereus laetus, commonly known as the bright star cactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems that are covered in dense, white spines. The plant produces large, showy flowers that are typically pale yellow to orange in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its striking appearance, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
