Alyssum balkanicum var. depressum

Alyssum balkanicum var. depressum, commonly known as the Balkan Alyssum, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Balkan region.

It features small, clustered white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring and early summer. The plant has hairy, linear leaves that are typically arranged in a basal rosette. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its compact form and fragrant blooms make it a valuable addition to ornamental horticulture.
