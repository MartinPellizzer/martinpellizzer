Angostura martiana

Angostura martiana, commonly known as the Andean lily, is a flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Andes in South America.

It is characterized by its striking blue-violet flowers and sword-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky soils and cool, moist environments. It has a short stem and produces showy inflorescences that bloom in the spring.

Angostura martiana is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens in temperate regions.
