Amaryllis × haylockii

Amaryllis × haylockii, commonly known as the hybrid amaryllis, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It is a hybrid between Amaryllis belladonna and Amaryllis paradisicola, resulting in a unique combination of traits from both parents. The plant produces large, showy flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, often with intricate patterns. Its foliage consists of broad, strap-like leaves that are dark green and evergreen.

This species is popular in gardens and as a potted plant due to its vibrant blooms and relatively easy care requirements.
