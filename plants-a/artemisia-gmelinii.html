Artemisia gmelinii

Artemisia gmelinii, commonly known as Siberian wormwood, is a perennial herb native to the cold climates of Asia and Europe.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and aromatic, finely divided leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that appear in late summer, followed by seeds dispersed by wind. Artemisia gmelinii is often found in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to harsh environmental conditions.

It has been used traditionally in herbal medicine for its purported health benefits.
