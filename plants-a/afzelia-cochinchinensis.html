Afzelia cochinchinensis

Afzelia cochinchinensis, commonly known as the Vietnamese rosewood, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Fabaceae and is characterized by its dense, dark-colored wood that is highly valued for its strength and beauty. The plant has bipinnate leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in racemes. Its fruit is a legume pod containing flat, winged seeds.
