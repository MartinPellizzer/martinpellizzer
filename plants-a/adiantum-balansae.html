Adiantum balansae

Adiantum balansae, commonly known as the Balansae maidenhair fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Adiantaceae and is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories. Its fronds are pinnately divided with a distinctive, almost lace-like appearance.

Adiantum balansae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
