Arundinaria gigantea var. tecta

Arundinaria gigantea var. tecta, commonly known as the Chinese bamboo or giant bamboo, is a species native to China.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems that can grow up to 10 meters in height. The plant has long, narrow leaves that are typically green on the upper surface and lighter in color on the underside. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and is used in traditional Chinese landscaping.
