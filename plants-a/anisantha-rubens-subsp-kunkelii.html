Anisantha rubens subsp. kunkelii

Anisantha rubens subsp.

kunkelii, commonly known as the Kunkel's bluegrass, is a perennial grass native to the central and eastern United States. It is characterized by its fine, blue-green foliage and compact growth habit, making it a desirable ornamental plant.

The plant typically grows in full sun to partial shade and thrives in well-drained soils. Its flowering spikes produce small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Anisantha rubens subsp.

kunkelii is valued for its low maintenance requirements and ability to form dense, attractive turf-like lawns.
