Ajuga grandiflora

Ajuga grandiflora, commonly known as great bugleweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features rhizomatous growth and produces clusters of small, tubular blue or purple flowers. The plant has heart-shaped, dark green leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Ajuga grandiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to stabilize soil in garden settings.
