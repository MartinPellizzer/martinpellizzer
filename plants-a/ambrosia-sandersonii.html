Ambrosia sandersonii

Ambrosia sandersonii, commonly known as the sanderson's ambrosia, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant typically grows in arid and semi-arid environments, thriving in well-drained soils. It produces small, yellowish to greenish flowers that bloom in the spring and summer months.

Ambrosia sandersonii is adapted to withstand drought conditions, making it a resilient species in its native habitat.
