Arachniodes spectabilis

Arachniodes spectabilis, commonly known as the showy spider fern, is a striking evergreen fern native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, leathery, dark green fronds that can grow up to 2 meters in length. The fronds are divided into narrow, finger-like segments that give the plant its distinctive, spider-like appearance. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in shaded gardens and as a houseplant.
