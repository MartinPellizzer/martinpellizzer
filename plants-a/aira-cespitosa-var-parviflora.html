Aira cespitosa var. parviflora

Aira cespitosa var. parviflora, commonly known as slender false oat, is a perennial grass native to North America.

It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and disturbed soils. The plant forms dense clumps with narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, pale blue or greenish flower spikes. Its inflorescence consists of a spikelet cluster at the tip of each stem.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in native plant gardens.
