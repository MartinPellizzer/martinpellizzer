Aloe fosteri

Aloe fosteri, commonly known as the Cape Town aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Aloe fosteri is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional medicinal uses.
