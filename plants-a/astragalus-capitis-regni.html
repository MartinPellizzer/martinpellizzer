Astragalus capitis-regni

Astragalus capitis-regni, commonly known as the crown of the kingdom, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters. The plant produces small, lens-shaped pods that contain several seeds. Native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, it thrives in arid and semi-arid environments.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for study in both ecological and horticultural contexts.
