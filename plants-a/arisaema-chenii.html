Arisaema chenii

Arisaema chenii, commonly known as the Chinese cobra lily, is a herbaceous plant native to China.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its striking, arrow-shaped leaves and distinctive inflorescence. The plant produces a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe, which resembles a cobra's hood, giving it its common name. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Arisaema chenii is valued both for its ornamental beauty and traditional medicinal uses.
