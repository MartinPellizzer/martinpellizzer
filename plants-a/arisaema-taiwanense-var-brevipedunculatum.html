Arisaema taiwanense var. brevipedunculatum

Arisaema taiwanense var. brevipedunculatum, commonly known as the Taiwan arrowhead, is a unique plant native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, arrow-shaped leaves. The plant produces a striking inflorescence with a spadix surrounded by a modified leaf called a spathe. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Its botanical features make it a notable species in the study of tropical and subtropical flora.
