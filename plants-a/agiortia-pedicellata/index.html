Agiortia Pedicellata

Agiortia pedicellata is a rare and elusive plant native to the mountainous regions of Greece. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded, forested areas. This species is known for its delicate, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring. Due to its limited habitat and declining populations, it is considered a vulnerable species. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this unique botanical treasure.
