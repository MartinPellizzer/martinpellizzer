Adelia reticulata

Adelia reticulata, commonly known as the net-veined adelia, is a flowering plant native to parts of southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Acanthaceae and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a distinctive net-like venation pattern.

Adelia reticulata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is occasionally cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
