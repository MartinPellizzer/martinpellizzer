Aponogeton gottlebei

Aponogeton gottlebei, commonly known as the Gottleb's arrowhead, is a aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features arrowhead-shaped leaves that are typically bright green and have a waxy texture. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments.

Aponogeton gottlebei is often cultivated for its ornamental value in ponds and water gardens.
