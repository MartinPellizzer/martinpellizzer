Asterolasia asteriscophora subsp. asteriscophora

Asterolasia asteriscophora subsp.

asteriscophora, commonly known as the narrow-leaf asterolasia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads.

The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest undergrowth. It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in a compact cluster.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
