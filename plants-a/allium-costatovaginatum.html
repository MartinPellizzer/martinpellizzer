Allium costatovaginatum

Allium costatovaginatum, commonly known as the variable garlic.

It is a flowering plant in the Amaryllidaceae family, characterized by its bulbous structure and narrow, grass-like leaves. Due to its ornamental value and potential medicinal properties, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
