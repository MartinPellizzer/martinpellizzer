Anthodon laxiflorum

Anthodon laxiflorum, commonly known as the broad-leaved thistle, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features large, spoon-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, arranged in a compact cluster.

Anthodon laxiflorum is often found in grasslands and rocky areas, thriving in well-drained soils.
