Aspidistra arnautovii var. angustifolia

Aspidistra arnautovii var. angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved aspidistra, is a species of flowering plant in the family Araceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Myanmar and Thailand. The plant is characterized by its dark green, narrow, strap-like leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color.

This variety is valued for its ornamental foliage and is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its tolerance of low light conditions.
