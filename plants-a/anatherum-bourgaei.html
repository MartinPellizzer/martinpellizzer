Anatherum bourgaei

Anatherum bourgaei, commonly known as the African grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it suitable for arid and semi-arid environments.

Anatherum bourgaei is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
