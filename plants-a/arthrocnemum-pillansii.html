Arthrocnemum pillansii

Arthrocnemum pillansii, commonly known as the desert sandmat, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming structure with fleshy, succulent leaves that help it conserve water. Arthrocnemum pillansii is well adapted to extreme desert conditions, making it a resilient species in harsh environments.
