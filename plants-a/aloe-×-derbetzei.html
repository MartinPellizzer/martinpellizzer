Aloe × derbetzei

Aloe × derbetzei, commonly known as the hybrid aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Aloe ferox and Aloe arborescens, combining traits from both parent plants. The plant features thick, fleshy leaves that are often green with red or purple margins, and it produces yellow flowers in clusters during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Aloe × derbetzei is popular as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and low maintenance requirements.
