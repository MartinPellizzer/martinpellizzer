Achyronia pungens

Achyronia pungens, commonly known as the spiny hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant has thick, waxy leaves and produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in humid, well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in some regions for its vibrant blooms.
