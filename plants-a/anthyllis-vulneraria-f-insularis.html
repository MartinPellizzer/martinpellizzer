Anthyllis vulneraria f. insularis

Anthyllis vulneraria f. insularis, commonly known as the Island woundwort, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that typically thrives in dry, rocky or sandy soils. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in dense clusters, which bloom in early summer. Its leaves are trifoliate, consisting of three oval-shaped leaflets, and it has a distinctive appearance that helps it stand out in its native habitat.
