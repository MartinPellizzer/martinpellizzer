Asplenium kansuense

Asplenium kansuense, commonly known as the Kansu spleenwort, is a small, terrestrial fern native to Asia, particularly found in regions of China, India, and Nepal.

It belongs to the family Aspleniaceae and is characterized by its delicate, evergreen foliage. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its fronds are lance-shaped and often have a smooth or slightly serrated margin.

Asplenium kansuense is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in shaded gardens.
