Anthurium calimense

Anthurium calimense, commonly known as the velvet leaf anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are dark green with a velvety texture. The plant produces bright red spadices, which are the inflorescences that house the small, yellowish true flowers. Anthurium calimense thrives in humid, warm environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.

It is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its ornamental foliage and unique floral structure.
