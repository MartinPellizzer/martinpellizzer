Alyssum arcticum

Alyssum arcticum, commonly known as Arctic Alyssum, is a flowering plant native to Arctic and alpine regions.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically reaches heights of 5 to 15 centimeters. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually white or pale pink, blooming in late summer. Its leaves are narrow and hairy, adapted to withstand cold and harsh environmental conditions.

Alyssum arcticum is often found in rocky soils and is a hardy species that thrives in nutrient-poor environments.
