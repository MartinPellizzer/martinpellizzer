Aeschynomene debilis

Aeschynomene debilis, commonly known as slender aeschynomene, is a small, herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, pea-like flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, disturbed soils and is often found in grasslands and along stream banks.

It is also known for its ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, making it a valuable plant in ecological restoration projects.
