Aerangis rhipsalisocia

Aerangis rhipsalisocia, commonly known as the ghost orchid, is a rare and ethereal flowering plant native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is an epiphytic orchid, meaning it grows on the surface of trees or rocks without deriving nutrients from them. Its delicate, white flowers resemble ghostly apparitions, giving it its hauntingly beautiful name. The plant has a unique appearance with long, slender stems and small, clustered leaves.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is highly sought after by botanists and conservationists.
