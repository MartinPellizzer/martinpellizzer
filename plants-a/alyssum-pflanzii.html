Alyssum pflanzii

Alyssum pflanzii, commonly known as the Sierra foothill alyssum, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, arranged in clusters. Alyssum pflanzii is often found in rocky soils and dry habitats, thriving in full sun.

Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and as a ground cover in arid landscapes.
