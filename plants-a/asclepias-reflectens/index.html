Asclepias Reflectens

Asclepias reflectens, also known as the reflective milkweed, is a unique and fascinating plant native to the southeastern United States. This species is part of the milkweed family, which plays a crucial role in supporting monarch butterfly populations. Its striking white flowers and reflective leaves make it a standout in any garden or natural setting. Despite its beauty, it is often overlooked due to its less common distribution and subtle appearance. Understanding and cultivating Asclepias reflectens can contribute to both ecological conservation and botanical appreciation.
