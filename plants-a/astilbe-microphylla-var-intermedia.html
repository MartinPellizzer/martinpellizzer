Astilbe microphylla var. intermedia

Astilbe microphylla var. intermedia, commonly known as the silver lace astilbe, is a herbaceous perennial native to eastern Asia.

It features delicate, plume-like inflorescences that range in color from white to pink and lavender. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically a striking silver-green, adding visual interest to gardens. It thrives in partial shade and moist, well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to bloom late into the summer season.
