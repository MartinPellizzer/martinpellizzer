Alsinopsis glabra

Alsinopsis glabra, commonly known as the smooth alsin, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant features erect stems with narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small, yellowish-green, and clustered in umbels, blooming in late spring to early summer.

Alsinopsis glabra is adapted to arid environments and is often found in fynbos vegetation.
