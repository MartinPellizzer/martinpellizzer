Alsine verna var. hirta

Alsine verna var. hirta, commonly known as hairy alsike, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a member of the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves. The plant produces small, greenish flowers that are typically clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows and along stream banks.

Alsine verna var. hirta is an annual plant that blooms from late spring to early summer.
