Agrostistachys africana

Agrostistachys africana, commonly known as the African grassveld grass, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in open grasslands and rocky outcrops, thriving in full sun and well-drained soils. The plant features slender, erect stems with narrow, grass-like leaves that are often hairy. Its flowering spikes are usually short and bear small, inconspicuous flowers.

It plays a role in local ecosystems by providing habitat and forage for various wildlife species.
