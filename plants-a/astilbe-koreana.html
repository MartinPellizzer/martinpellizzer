Astilbe koreana

Astilbe koreana, commonly known as Korean astilbe, is a herbaceous perennial native to Korea.

It features upright, branching stems that bear delicate, feathery panicles of pink to white flowers. The plant has heart-shaped, dark green leaves that are slightly toothed along the edges. It thrives in partial to full shade and moist, well-drained soil.

Astilbe koreana is valued for its ornamental foliage and long-lasting blooms that add color to woodland gardens.
