Atriplex spatiosa

Atriplex spatiosa, commonly known as the desert saltbush, is a shrub native to arid and semi-arid regions of North America.

It is characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves that help it retain moisture in harsh environments. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in dense, spherical inflorescences. Its salt-tolerant nature allows it to thrive in soils with high salinity.

Atriplex spatiosa plays an important role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for desert wildlife.
