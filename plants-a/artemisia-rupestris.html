Artemisia rupestris

Artemisia rupestris, commonly known as cliff-rose or rock sage, is a perennial shrub native to North America.

It is characterized by its aromatic, silver-gray foliage and small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in summer. The plant thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid environments. Its leaves contain essential oils that contribute to its strong, medicinal scent.

Artemisia rupestris is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
