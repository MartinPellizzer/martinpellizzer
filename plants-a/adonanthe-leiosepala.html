Adonanthe leiosepala

Adonanthe leiosepala, commonly known as the velvetleaf orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, velvety leaves and striking, orchid-like flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Adonanthe leiosepala is also valued for its potential medicinal properties in traditional medicine.
