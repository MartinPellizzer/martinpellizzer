Aconitum × paniculatum var. prutense

Aconitum × paniculatum var. prutense, commonly known as the Prut Valley aconite, is a herbaceous perennial native to the Carpathian region.

It features tall, upright stems that bear large, showy blue to violet flowers arranged in panicle-like clusters. The plant has palmately divided, dark green leaves with a coarse texture. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Due to its toxicity, it should be handled with care.
