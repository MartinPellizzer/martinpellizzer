Acalypha scandens var. mollis

Acalypha scandens var. mollis, commonly known as velvetleaf or woolly leaf, is a tropical plant characterized by its soft, velvety leaves.

The foliage is typically dark green with a dense, fuzzy texture that gives it a distinctive appearance. It is a climbing or trailing plant that thrives in warm, humid climates. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by spiny, three-lobed seed capsules.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its unique leaf structure and adaptability to various growing conditions.
