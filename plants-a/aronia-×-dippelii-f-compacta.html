Aronia × dippelii f. compacta

Aronia × dippelii f. compacta, commonly known as the compact black chokeberry, is a compact shrub native to North America.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its dense, upright growth habit. The plant produces small, dark purple to black berries that are rich in antioxidants. Its foliage is typically dark green with a slightly glossy appearance.

It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes and as a source of edible berries.
