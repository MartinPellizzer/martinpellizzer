Arisaema candidissimum

Arisaema candidissimum, commonly known as the white arisaema, is a member of the Araceae family.

It is native to parts of Asia, including China, India, and the Himalayas. The plant is characterized by its striking white spadix surrounded by a pale green spathe. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions.

The botanical features of Arisaema candidissimum make it a unique and visually appealing ornamental plant.
