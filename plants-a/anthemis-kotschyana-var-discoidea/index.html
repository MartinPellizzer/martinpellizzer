Anthemis Kotschyana Var. Discoidea

Anthemis kotschyana var. discoidea is a striking member of the Asteraceae family known for its vibrant, daisy-like flowers. Native to the high altitudes of the Eastern Mediterranean and parts of Asia, this plant thrives in rocky and arid environments. Its unique double-flowered form and bright yellow petals make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking both beauty and resilience. The plant's ability to withstand harsh conditions highlights its adaptability and ecological significance. With its historical use in traditional medicine, Anthemis kotschyana var. discoidea remains a fascinating subject for botanists and horticultural enthusiasts alike.
