Acrotrema arnottianum

Acrotrema arnottianum, commonly known as the narrow-leafed sandwort, is a small, herbaceous plant native to southern Australia.

It typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils, often in arid or semi-arid environments. The plant has slender, upright stems with narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small, white or pale pink, and are arranged in clusters at the tips of the stems.

Acrotrema arnottianum is adapted to withstand drought and high temperatures, making it a resilient species in its native habitat.
