Alpinia roscoeana

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes other well-known gingers such as turmeric and cardamom. The plant features long, narrow leaves and produces clusters of fragrant, pale yellow to white flowers. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine and culinary applications.
