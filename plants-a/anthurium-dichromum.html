Anthurium dichromum

Anthurium dichromum, commonly known as the black anthurium, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its dark, velvety leaves that contrast beautifully with its bright red spadix, which is surrounded by a white spathe. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its unique foliage and dramatic inflorescence make it a popular choice for indoor gardening and ornamental purposes.

Anthurium dichromum is also valued for its air-purifying properties and ability to thrive in low-light conditions.
