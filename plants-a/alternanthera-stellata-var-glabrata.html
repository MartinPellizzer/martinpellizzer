Alternanthera stellata var. glabrata

Alternanthera stellata var. glabrata, commonly known as the smooth Alternanthera, is a compact, ornamental plant native to South America.

It features striking, velvety leaves that range in color from deep red to bronze, depending on light exposure. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in late summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Its foliage makes it a popular choice for water gardens and bog landscapes.
