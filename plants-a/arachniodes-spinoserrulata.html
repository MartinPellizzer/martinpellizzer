Arachniodes spinoserrulata

Arachniodes spinoserrulata, commonly known as the spiny serrated fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, pinnately compound leaves with serrated margins and spiny structures along the leaflets. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments such as forest understories. Its fronds are often arching, giving it a graceful appearance.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and adaptability to indoor growing conditions.
