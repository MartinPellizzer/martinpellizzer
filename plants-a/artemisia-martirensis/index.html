Artemisia Martirensis

Artemisia martirensis is a rare and resilient plant native to the arid regions of northern Mexico. It thrives in harsh, dry environments where few other species can survive. This shrub is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and strong aromatic scent. Its unique adaptations make it an important part of the local ecosystem. Due to habitat loss, it is now considered endangered and requires conservation efforts to ensure its survival.
