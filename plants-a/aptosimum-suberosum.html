Aptosimum suberosum

Aptosimum suberosum, commonly known as the red-stemmed sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and grass-like plants. The plant is characterized by its slender, reddish stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. It typically grows in wet or moist habitats such as marshes and stream banks.
