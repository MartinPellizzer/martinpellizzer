Anthurium sellowianum

Anthurium sellowianum, commonly known as the Sellow's anthurium, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped spathes that come in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant produces small, greenish-white spadices inside the spathe, which are the actual flowers. It thrives in humid, warm environments with bright, indirect light.

Anthurium sellowianum is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
