Anthriscus intermedia

Anthriscus intermedia, commonly known as cow parsley, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a member of the Apiaceae family, which includes carrots and parsley, and is characterized by its finely divided, fern-like leaves. Anthriscus intermedia is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting pollinators.
