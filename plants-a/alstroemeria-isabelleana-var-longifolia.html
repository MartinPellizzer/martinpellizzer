Alstroemeria isabelleana var. longifolia

Alstroemeria isabelleana var. longifolia, commonly known as the long-flowered isabelleana alstroemeria, is a species native to South America.

It is characterized by its elongated, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to red, often with intricate patterns. The plant typically grows as a herbaceous perennial with strap-like leaves that are dark green and glossy. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long-lasting blooms and adaptability to various growing conditions.
