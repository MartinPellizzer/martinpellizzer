Aglaonema birmanicum

Aglaonema birmanicum, commonly known as the Chinese Evergreen, is a tropical evergreen plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves with striking white and silver variegation. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions, making it a popular houseplant. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by orange-red berries.

Aglaonema birmanicum is relatively low maintenance and resistant to pests, contributing to its widespread cultivation.
