Asterochaete angustissima

Asterochaete angustissima, commonly known as the narrow-leaved aster.

It is a flowering plant native to parts of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, daisy-like flowers in shades of white and yellow.

Asterochaete angustissima is often found in moist, shaded areas within tropical forests.
