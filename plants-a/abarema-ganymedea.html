Abarema ganymedea

Abarema ganymedea, commonly known as the silverleaf acacia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes and is characterized by its pod fruits. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often found in open woodland and savanna habitats.

Abarema ganymedea is also valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
