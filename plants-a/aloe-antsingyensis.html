Aloe antsingyensis

Aloe antsingyensis, commonly known as the Madagascar aloe, is a succulent plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that store water, typical of plants in arid environments. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellowish-green flowers, which bloom during the rainy season. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their soothing properties.

Aloe antsingyensis is adapted to withstand drought and thrives in well-drained soil.
