Artemisia elegantissima var. kumaonensis

Artemisia elegantissima var. kumaonensis, commonly known as the Kumaon wormwood, is a perennial herb native to the Himalayan regions of India and Nepal.

It is characterized by its aromatic, silver-gray foliage and slender, branched stems. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that appear in panicles during the summer months. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in alpine meadows and forested areas.

Due to its medicinal properties, it is occasionally used in traditional herbal remedies.
