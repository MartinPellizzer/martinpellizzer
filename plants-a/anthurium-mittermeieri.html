Anthurium mittermeieri

Anthurium mittermeieri, commonly known as the Mittermeier's anthurium, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 inches in length, with a glossy, dark green surface. The plant produces spadices, which are inflorescences consisting of a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe, often in shades of red or orange. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Anthurium mittermeieri is popular among collectors for its ornamental value and unique foliage.
