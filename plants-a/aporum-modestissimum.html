Aporum modestissimum

Aporum modestissimum, commonly known as the modest aporum, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family, which includes plants like philodendrons and callas. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a glossy surface and produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically white or green. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its delicate appearance and specific care needs, it is often cultivated as a houseplant.
