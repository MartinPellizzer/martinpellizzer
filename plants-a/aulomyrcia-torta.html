Aulomyrcia torta

Aulomyrcia torta, commonly known as the zigzag fern, is a tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, undulating fronds that give it its common name. The plant has a compact, bushy growth habit with dark green, leathery leaves. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Aulomyrcia torta is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and air-purifying qualities.
