Anthurium saccardoi

Anthurium saccardoi, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped spathe that ranges from deep red to orange, often with a contrasting green spadix in the center. The plant thrives in humid, warm environments with indirect light and well-draining soil. Its foliage consists of dark green, glossy leaves that are broad and lance-shaped.

Anthurium saccardoi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.
