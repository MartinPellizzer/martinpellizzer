Atriplex halimus var. schweinfurthii

Atriplex halimus var. schweinfurthii, commonly known as the silverleaf tamarisk, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, dense hairs. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in terminal panicles. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it suitable for arid and semi-arid environments.

This variety is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to stabilize soil.
