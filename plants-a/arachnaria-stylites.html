Arachnaria stylites

Arachnaria stylites, commonly known as the spiky column plant, is a unique species characterized by its tall, slender stem covered in sharp, needle-like structures.

This plant thrives in arid environments, showcasing adaptations such as reduced leaves to minimize water loss. Its stem serves as a storage organ for water and nutrients, enabling survival in harsh conditions. The plant's structure also provides protection against herbivores due to its thorny exterior.

Botanically, it belongs to the family Cactaceae, sharing traits with other succulents.
