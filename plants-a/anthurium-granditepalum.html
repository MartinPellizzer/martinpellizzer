Anthurium granditepalum

Anthurium granditepalum, commonly known as the giant spathiphyllum, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length, providing a lush, green backdrop for its showy inflorescence. The plant produces vibrant, colorful spathes that range from red to orange, often with a contrasting white or yellow interior, which are highly ornamental. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Due to its dramatic appearance, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical landscapes.
