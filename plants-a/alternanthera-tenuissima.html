Alternanthera tenuissima

Alternanthera tenuissima, commonly known as red leaf alternate, is a species of flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking red or pink foliage, which becomes more vivid under intense sunlight. The plant produces small, inconspicuous yellow flowers that appear in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in warm, humid conditions and is often grown as an ornamental plant in water gardens or as a houseplant.

Its rapid growth and vibrant color make it a popular choice for aquascaping and indoor gardening.
