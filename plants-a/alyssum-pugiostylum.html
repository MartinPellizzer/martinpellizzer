Alyssum pugiostylum

Alyssum pugiostylum, commonly known as the rock allyssum, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically reaches heights of 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features dense clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often covered in fine hairs.

Alyssum pugiostylum thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
