Astrophytum gracillimum

Astrophytum gracillimum, commonly known as the slender star cactus, is a small, columnar cactus native to the arid regions of Mexico.

It features a short, cylindrical stem with radial spines and a distinctive star-shaped crown of flowers. The plant has a dense covering of white, woolly hairs that help reduce water loss in its harsh environment. It blooms with yellow or orange flowers during the summer months.

Astrophytum gracillimum is well adapted to drought and requires minimal care, making it a popular choice for succulent collectors.
