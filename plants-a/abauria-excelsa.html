Abauria excelsa

Abauria excelsa, commonly known as the giant water lily, is a striking aquatic plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, floating leaves that can reach up to 3 meters in diameter, providing a unique habitat for various aquatic organisms.
