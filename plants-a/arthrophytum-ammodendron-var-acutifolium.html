Arthrophytum ammodendron var. acutifolium

Arthrophytum ammodendron var. acutifolium, commonly known as the sharpleaf stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its fleshy, spiny stems and narrow, pointed leaves that help reduce water loss. The plant produces small, star-shaped yellow flowers during the summer months. It is well adapted to hot, dry environments and requires minimal water once established.

Its hardy nature makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
