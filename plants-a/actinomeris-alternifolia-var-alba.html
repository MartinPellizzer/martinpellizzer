Actinomeris alternifolia var. alba

Actinomeris alternifolia var. alba, commonly known as the white-flowered actinomeris, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to certain regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its alternate leaf arrangement and produces small, white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has slender, upright stems and possesses a fibrous root system. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it a subject of interest in both horticultural and ecological studies.
