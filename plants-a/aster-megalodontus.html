Aster megalodontus

Aster megalodontus, commonly known as the giant aster, is a species of flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its large, showy composite flowers that typically bloom in the late summer and early autumn. The plant produces both ray and disk flowers, with the central disk being densely packed with small, tubular florets. Native to temperate regions, it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun exposure.

Its foliage consists of lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
