Androrchis × haussknechtii

Androrchis × haussknechtii, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a natural hybrid between Androrchis scaberulus and Androrchis nutans.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. The plant produces tall, slender stems with clustered, greenish-white flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, typically arranged in a basal rosette.

It thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
