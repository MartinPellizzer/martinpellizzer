Adenostyles × eginensis

Adenostyles × eginensis, commonly known as the hybrid between Adenostyles and Eginum, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features alternate, lance-shaped leaves with a hairy texture and a distinct, fragrant aroma. Its flowers are small, clustered in dense inflorescences, and are usually pink or white in color.

Adenostyles × eginensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting pollinators.
