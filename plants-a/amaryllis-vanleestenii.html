Amaryllis vanleestenii

Amaryllis vanleestenii, commonly known as the Van Leesten amaryllis, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and red, often with a contrasting stripe. The plant produces thick, fleshy leaves that are typically broad and strap-like. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This species is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its vibrant blooms.
