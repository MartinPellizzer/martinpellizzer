Ansonia salicifolia

Ansonia salicifolia, commonly known as the silverleaf ansonia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, silvery-gray foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The leaves are lance-shaped and often have a waxy texture, which helps reduce water loss in its native habitat. It produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in the summer months.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
