Arachnorchis bryceana

Arachnorchis bryceana, commonly known as the Bryce Canyon spider orchid, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the United States, specifically found in Utah's Bryce Canyon National Park.

It is a terrestrial orchid that thrives in high-altitude environments, typically growing in rocky, well-drained soils. The plant produces intricate, spider-like flowers that are typically pale green or yellowish in color, with a unique structure that aids in pollination by specific insects. Its small size and specialized habitat make it highly sensitive to environmental changes.
