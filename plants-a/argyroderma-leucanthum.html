Argyroderma leucanthum

Argyroderma leucanthum, commonly known as the silverleaf dandelion, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers. It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils.

Its leaves are hairy and have a distinctive silvery appearance, which helps to reflect sunlight and reduce water loss.
