Aichryson × bramwellii

Aichryson × bramwellii, commonly known as the hybrid African violet, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It features dense clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, which bloom throughout the growing season. The plant has thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a waxy texture, adapted to store water. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Due to its hybrid nature, it is typically propagated through leaf cuttings rather than seeds.
