Asplenium luzoniense

Asplenium luzoniense, commonly known as the Luzon shield fern, is a species of fern native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the family Aspleniaceae and is characterized by its shield-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as forests and rocky crevices. Its rhizomes are often covered in a dense layer of rhizome scales, which help in water retention.

Asplenium luzoniense is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
