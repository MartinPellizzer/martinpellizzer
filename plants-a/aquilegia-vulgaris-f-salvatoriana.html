Aquilegia vulgaris f. salvatoriana

Aquilegia vulgaris f. salvatoriana, commonly known as the Salvatoriana columbine, is a hybrid variety of the common columbine.

It is characterized by its striking, double flowers with a rich violet-blue color and a prominent yellow center. The plant is a herbaceous perennial that thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade. Its foliage consists of deeply lobed, dark green leaves that add visual interest throughout the growing season.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in garden landscapes for its vibrant blooms and unique floral structure.
