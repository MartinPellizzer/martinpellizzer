Anthurium × prochaskaianum

Anthurium × prochaskaianum, commonly known as the Red-veined Anthurium, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Araceae family.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves with deep red veins against a dark green background. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-shaped inflorescences that are often surrounded by a colorful spathe. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Anthurium × prochaskaianum is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
