Atraphaxis inermis

Atraphaxis inermis, commonly known as the desert lily, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

The plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, often blooming in the spring.
