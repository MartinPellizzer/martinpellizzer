Aichryson × domesticum

Aichryson × domesticum, commonly known as the African daisy or the blue African daisy, is a hybrid succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, rounded growth with dense clusters of blue or purple flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, providing a striking visual contrast to its flowers. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
