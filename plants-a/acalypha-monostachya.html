Acalypha monostachya

Acalypha monostachya, commonly known as the silky filbert or silky salsify, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its soft, silky foliage and clusters of small, pale yellow flowers. The plant produces elongated, tubular fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or near water bodies.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
