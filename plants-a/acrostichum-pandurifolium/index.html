Acrostichum Pandurifolium

Acrostichum pandurifolium is a unique fern species native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia. It is known for its distinctive, elongated leaves that resemble the shape of a drum, giving it its common name. This plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found near water sources. Its intricate fronds make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively unknown to the general public.
