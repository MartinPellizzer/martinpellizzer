Astronia grandiflora

Astronia grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered astronia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows up to 3 meters in height. The plant produces showy, pale yellow or white flowers that are clustered in terminal inflorescences. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Astronia grandiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
