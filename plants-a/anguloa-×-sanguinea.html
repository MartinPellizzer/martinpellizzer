Anguloa × sanguinea

Anguloa × sanguinea, commonly known as the red anguloa, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the Andean regions of South America, where it thrives in high-altitude environments. The plant is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically feature bright red petals and a contrasting yellow lip. Its growth habit is epiphytic, meaning it grows on the surface of trees or rocks rather than in soil.

Anguloa × sanguinea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
