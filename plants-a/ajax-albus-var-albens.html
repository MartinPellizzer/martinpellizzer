Ajax albus var. albens

Ajax albus var. albens, commonly known as the white ajax or white alba ajax, is a variant of the Ajax albus species.

It is a flowering plant native to certain regions of Europe, characterized by its white or pale-colored flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight. Its botanical features include narrow, linear leaves and a compact growth habit.

It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its delicate appearance and seasonal blooms.
