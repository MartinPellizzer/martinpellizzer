Agrostis roylei

Agrostis roylei, commonly known as the Himalayan meadowgrass, is a perennial grass species native to the high-altitude regions of the Himalayas.

It typically grows in alpine meadows and rocky slopes, thriving in cool, moist environments. The plant has slender, tufted stems and produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny florets. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green coloration.

Agrostis roylei plays an important ecological role in stabilizing soil and supporting local biodiversity in its native habitat.
