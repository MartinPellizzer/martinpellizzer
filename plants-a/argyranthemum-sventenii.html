Argyranthemum sventenii

Argyranthemum sventenii, commonly known as the silver geranium, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-white foliage and showy, daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or perennial, reaching heights of up to 3 feet. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
