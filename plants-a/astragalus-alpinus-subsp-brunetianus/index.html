Astragalus Alpinus Subsp. Brunetianus

Astragalus alpinus subsp. brunetianus is a rare and resilient plant species found in high-altitude regions of the Alps. It belongs to the legume family and is known for its small, delicate flowers that bloom in late summer. This subspecies is adapted to harsh mountain environments with low temperatures and limited soil nutrients. Its limited distribution makes it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists. Protecting this unique plant is crucial for preserving biodiversity in alpine ecosystems.
