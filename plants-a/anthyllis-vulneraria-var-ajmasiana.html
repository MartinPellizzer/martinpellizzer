Anthyllis vulneraria var. ajmasiana

Anthyllis vulneraria var. ajmasiana, commonly known as the Ajmasian broom, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, often found in alpine or arctic regions. Its leaves are trifoliate, consisting of three small, oval-shaped leaflets.

This variety is known for its hardy nature and ability to thrive in harsh environmental conditions.
