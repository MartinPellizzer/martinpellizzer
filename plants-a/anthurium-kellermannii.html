Anthurium kellermannii

Anthurium kellermannii, commonly known as the velvet leaf anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, velvety, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a metallic sheen. The plant produces striking spadices, which are inflorescences that resemble a spike, often in shades of red or yellow. It thrives in humid, warm environments with bright, indirect light.

Anthurium kellermannii is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
