Anthemis victoriae

Anthemis victoriae, commonly known as the Victoria's anthemis, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant produces small yellow to orange flowers that are often surrounded by white or pale green ray florets. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun, making it a suitable candidate for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and long blooming period.
