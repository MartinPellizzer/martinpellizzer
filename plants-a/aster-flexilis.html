Aster flexilis

Aster flexilis, commonly known as the flexible aster, is a herbaceous perennial native to the eastern United States.

It features showy, daisy-like flowers that range in color from white to purple, blooming in late summer to early fall. The plant has alternate, lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy, providing a textured appearance. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sun.

Aster flexilis is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
