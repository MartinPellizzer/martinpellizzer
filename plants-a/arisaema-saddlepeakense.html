Arisaema saddlepeakense

Arisaema saddlepeakense, commonly known as the Saddle Peak Arisaema, is a member of the Araceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia, particularly found in the Saddle Peak area of Taiwan. The plant features a distinctive underground tuber and produces a striking inflorescence with a spadix surrounded by a spathe. Its leaves are typically arrow-shaped and have a waxy texture, adapted to the humid, shaded environments it inhabits.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has cultural significance in certain local communities.
