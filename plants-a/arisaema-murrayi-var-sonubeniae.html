Arisaema murrayi var. sonubeniae

Arisaema murrayi var. sonubeniae, commonly known as the Sonubenia Arisaema, is a rare and unique species native to specific regions of China.

It is characterized by its distinctive, leaf-like bracts that enclose the spadix, a feature typical of the Araceae family. The plant produces small, white to greenish flowers arranged in a dense cluster on the spadix. Its foliage consists of broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often a deep green.

This variant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in specialized botanical gardens.
