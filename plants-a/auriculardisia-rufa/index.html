Auriculardisia Rufa

Auriculardisia rufa is a rare and striking plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia. It is known for its vibrant red flowers that resemble small ears, giving it its scientific name. This unique species thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical forests. Due to habitat loss and over-collection, it is now considered endangered. Conservation efforts are underway to protect and propagate this beautiful and rare flora.
