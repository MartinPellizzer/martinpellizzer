Aporum dalatense

Aporum dalatense, commonly known as the Dalat orchid, is a species of orchid native to Vietnam.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Aporum dalatense is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
