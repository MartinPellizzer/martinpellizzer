Aloe hyacinthoides var. zeylanica

Aloe hyacinthoides var. zeylanica, commonly known as the Zeylanica Aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with red or purple margins. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing clusters of small, tubular flowers in shades of yellow to orange. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

This species is valued both for its ornamental appeal and its traditional medicinal uses.
