Acer pseudoplatanus f. handjeryi

Acer pseudoplatanus f. handjeryi, commonly known as the Handjeryi maple, is a rare and unique variant of the common maple.

It is characterized by its distinctive foliage, featuring leaves with a striking combination of green and red hues. The plant exhibits a compact growth habit, making it suitable for smaller garden spaces. Its ornamental value lies in its vibrant autumn colors and unusual leaf patterns.

This form is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal in horticultural settings.
