Archeria hirtella

Archeria hirtella, commonly known as the hairy archeria, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and hairy leaves, which give it its common name. The plant produces small, yellowish to reddish flowers that are typically clustered in a dense inflorescence. It is native to regions of North America, particularly in the western United States.

Archeria hirtella is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas.
