Aira Tenera Var. Biaristata

Aira tenera var. biaristata is a rare and delicate plant known for its unique, feathery appearance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas. This variety is prized for its soft, silver-green foliage that shimmers in the light. Its small, pale flowers add a subtle elegance to any garden or natural setting. Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a valuable and sought-after species among plant enthusiasts.
