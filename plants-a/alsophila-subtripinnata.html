Alsophila subtripinnata

Alsophila subtripinnata, commonly known as the bamboo fern, is a species of tree fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and a crown of feathery, compound fronds. The plant has a robust root system that helps it thrive in moist, shaded environments. Its fronds are typically bioluminescent, emitting a soft glow at night.

This unique feature makes it a striking addition to tropical landscapes and a subject of botanical interest.
