Aulomyrcia lucida var. parvifolia

Aulomyrcia lucida var. parvifolia, commonly known as the small-leaved shining aulomyrcia, is a variant of the aulomyrcia plant native to specific regions in South America.

It is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are usually glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in cloud forests.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural study.
