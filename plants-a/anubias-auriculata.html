Anubias auriculata

Anubias auriculata, commonly known as the ear-shaped anubias, is a hardy aquatic plant native to Africa.

It features broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often green to dark green in color. The plant thrives in low to moderate light conditions and is well-suited for freshwater aquariums. It is known for its ability to tolerate a wide range of water parameters and is relatively easy to care for.

Anubias auriculata is often used as a background plant due to its dense foliage and aesthetic appeal.
