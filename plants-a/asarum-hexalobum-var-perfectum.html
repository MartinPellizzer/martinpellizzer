Asarum hexalobum var. perfectum

Asarum hexalobum var. perfectum, commonly known as the Pacific asarum, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Pacific Northwest of North America.

It features heart-shaped, glossy, dark green leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine, white hairs. The plant produces small, reddish-brown flowers that are clustered in umbels, which are typically hidden beneath the foliage. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in local ecosystems.
