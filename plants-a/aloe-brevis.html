Aloe brevis

Aloe brevis, commonly known as the dwarf aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features short, compact stems and fleshy, bluish-green leaves that are covered in a white powdery coating. The plant produces small, yellow flowers on branched inflorescences during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its leaves for survival.

Aloe brevis is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
