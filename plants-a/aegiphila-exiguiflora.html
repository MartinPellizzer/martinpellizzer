Aegiphila exiguiflora

Aegiphila exiguiflora, commonly known as the narrow-flowered aegiphila, is a flowering plant native to the Andean regions of South America.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its small, tubular flowers that are typically pale purple or white in color. It grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in high-altitude environments.
