Aeranthes recta var. recurva

Aeranthes recta var. recurva, commonly known as the recurved spider orchid, is a striking orchid species native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, recurved leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces delicate, spider-like flowers in shades of yellow and green, often with intricate patterns. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat requirements, it is a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
