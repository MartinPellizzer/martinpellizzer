Alyssum Calycinum Subsp. Psilocarpum

Alyssum calycinum subsp. psilocarpum is a rare and lesser-known plant species belonging to the Brassicaceae family. Native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe, it thrives in rocky and well-drained soils. This subspecies is distinguished by its delicate, fragrant flowers and compact growth habit. Despite its beauty, it remains underappreciated and poorly documented in botanical studies. Understanding its ecology and conservation status is crucial for its preservation in the wild.
