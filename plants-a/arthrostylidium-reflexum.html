Arthrostylidium reflexum

Arthrostylidium reflexum, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a species of flowering plant in the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil, and thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant features slender, erect stems that bear small, greenish-white flowers arranged in loose clusters. Its leaves are elongated and often have a waxy coating, which helps reduce water loss in its native habitat.

Arthrostylidium reflexum is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
