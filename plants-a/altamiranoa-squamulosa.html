Altamiranoa squamulosa

Altamiranoa squamulosa, commonly known as the velvetleaf orchid, is a striking tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves that are often covered in fine, hairy scales. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, which bloom in clusters along the stems. Altamiranoa squamulosa thrives in warm, humid environments and is often found in rainforests or along riverbanks.

Its unique foliage and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in tropical regions.
