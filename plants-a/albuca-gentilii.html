Albuca gentilii

Albuca gentilii, commonly known as the Natal lily, is a bulbous plant native to southern Africa.

It features striking, white, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant has strap-like, grassy leaves that are typically green or bluish-gray in color. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Albuca gentilii is relatively low maintenance and attracts pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
