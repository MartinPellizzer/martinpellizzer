Asterochlaena platyloba var. genuina

Asterochlaena platyloba var. genuina, commonly known as the narrow-leaf star-knife.

It is a perennial herb native to southern China, characterized by its slender, lance-shaped leaves and small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in a cluster. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest understories. Its leaves are often covered in fine hairs, and the stems are usually hairy as well.

It is valued in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported medicinal properties.
