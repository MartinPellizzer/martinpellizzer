Agathis robusta var. palmerstonii

Agathis robusta var. palmerstonii, commonly known as the Palmerston's ironwood, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the rainforests of northern Queensland, Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and can grow up to 40 meters in height, with a broad, spreading crown. The leaves are alternate, simple, and elliptical, with a leathery texture and a prominent midrib. Its flowers are small, yellowish-green, and clustered in terminal spikes, while the fruit is a woody capsule containing numerous seeds.

This species is valued for its hard, durable wood, which is used in construction and furniture-making.
