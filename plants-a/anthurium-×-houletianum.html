Anthurium × houletianum

Anthurium × houletianum, commonly known as the flamingo flower, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Araceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant, brightly colored spathe that resembles a flower, though it is actually a modified leaf. The plant's inflorescence consists of a spadix surrounded by the showy spathe, which is often used in ornamental horticulture. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect light to flourish.

Anthurium × houletianum is prized for its long-lasting, colorful bracts that can last several weeks.
