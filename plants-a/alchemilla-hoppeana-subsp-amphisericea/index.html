Alchemilla Hoppeana Subsp. Amphisericea

Alpine lady's mantle, scientifically known as Alchemilla hoppeana subsp. amphisericea, is a striking plant native to the high altitudes of the Alps. It is characterized by its dense, silvery-white foliage that shimmers in the sunlight. This plant thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in rocky or gravelly soils. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for alpine gardens and rockeries. Despite its beauty, it is a rare and delicate species that requires careful cultivation to flourish.
