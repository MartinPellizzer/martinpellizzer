Argyrocome apiculata

Argyrocome apiculata, commonly known as the silverleaf tree, is a species of flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-white leaves that are covered in fine, reflective hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. Its bark is smooth and grayish, often exuding a mild aromatic scent.

Argyrocome apiculata is typically found in sandy or rocky soils and thrives in full sun conditions.
