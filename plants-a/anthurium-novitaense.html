Anthurium novitaense

Anthurium novitaense, commonly known as the Novitaense Arrowhead, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often a deep green with a glossy texture. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-shaped inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, typically in shades of red, orange, or yellow. It thrives in humid, warm environments with bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil.

Anthurium novitaense is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
