Aira Tenorei Var. Biaristata

Aira tenorei var. biaristata is a rare and striking plant native to the Canary Islands. It is known for its unique, feather-like foliage that gives it a dramatic and ornamental appearance. This variant of Aira tenorei is highly sought after by collectors and botanists alike. Its delicate structure and vibrant colors make it a standout in any garden or collection. Despite its beauty, it remains a challenging plant to cultivate due to its specific environmental needs.
