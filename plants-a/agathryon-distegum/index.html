Agathryon Distegum

Agathryon distegum is a rare and striking plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia. It is known for its unique appearance, featuring vibrant purple and red flowers that bloom in late spring. This plant thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in shaded forest understories. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a conservation concern. Its beauty and ecological significance make it a subject of interest for botanists and nature enthusiasts alike.
