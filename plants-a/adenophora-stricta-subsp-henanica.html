Adenophora stricta subsp. henanica

Adenophora stricta subsp.

henanica, commonly known as the Henan bellflower, is a perennial herb native to China. It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and bell-shaped flowers.

The plant typically produces blue to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are opposite, lanceolate, and covered with fine hairs.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in moist meadows and forest edges.
