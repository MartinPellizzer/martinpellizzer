Astracantha zachlensis

Astracantha zachlensis, commonly known as the mountain bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the high altitudes of the Eastern Himalayas.

Astracantha zachlensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in alpine gardens.
