Acrotrema

Acrotrema, commonly known as the "butterfly fern," is a small, epiphytic fern belonging to the family Hymenophyllaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, feather-like fronds that resemble the wings of a butterfly, hence its common name. Its intricate frond structure and unique appearance make it a popular specimen in botanical collections.
