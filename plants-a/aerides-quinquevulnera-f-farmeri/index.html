Aerides Quinquevulnera F. Farmeri

Farmer's aerides, scientifically known as Aerides quinquevulnera f. farmeri, is a rare and captivating orchid species native to Southeast Asia. This unique plant is prized for its striking floral display and ornamental value among horticultural enthusiasts. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires careful attention to maintain its health and beauty. The variety 'farmeri' is particularly notable for its vibrant colors and distinctive foliage. Due to its limited natural habitat, it is often cultivated in greenhouses or specialized gardens.
