Aconitum × hakusanense

Aconitum × hakusanense, commonly known as the Japanese monkshood, is a hybrid perennial plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to purple spurred flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, featuring palmate leaves with a coarse texture. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its dramatic foliage and long-lasting blooms.
