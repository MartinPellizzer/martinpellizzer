Armeriastrum restiaceum

Armeriastrum restiaceum, commonly known as the coastal sandverbena, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of southern California and Baja California.

Armeriastrum restiaceum plays an important ecological role in stabilizing dunes and providing habitat for native insects and birds.
