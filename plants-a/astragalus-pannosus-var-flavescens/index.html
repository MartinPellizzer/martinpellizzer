Astragalus Pannosus Var. Flavescens

Astragalus pannosus var. flavescens is a rare and striking plant native to the arid regions of the southwestern united states. it belongs to the legume family and is known for its unique yellowish flowers that bloom in summer. this variant of astragalus is particularly valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological significance. it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy choice for xeriscaping. due to its limited distribution, it is often considered a conservation concern.
