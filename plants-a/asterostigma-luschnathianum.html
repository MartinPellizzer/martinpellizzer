Asterostigma luschnathianum

Asterostigma luschnathianum, commonly known as the Luschnathian aster, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that typically bloom in the summer months. The plant produces small, oval-shaped fruits called cypsela, which are often hairy and contain the seeds. Native to the Mediterranean region, it thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
