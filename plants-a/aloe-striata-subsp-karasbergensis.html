Aloe striata subsp. karasbergensis

Aloe striata subsp.

karasbergensis, commonly known as the Karasberg aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa. It features rosettes of fleshy, blue-green leaves that are covered in fine white hairs.

The plant produces upright spikes of yellow flowers during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil.

Its compact size and ornamental appearance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
