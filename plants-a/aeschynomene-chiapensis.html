Aeschynomene chiapensis

Aeschynomene chiapensis, commonly known as Chiapanese aeschynomene, is a flowering plant native to Central America.

Aeschynomene chiapensis is also noted for its potential in ecological restoration due to its nitrogen-fixing ability.
