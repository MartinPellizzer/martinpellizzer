Austroamericium brasiliense

Austroamericium brasiliense, commonly known as the Brazilian star orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments within the Atlantic Forest biome. It has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients.

Its flowers are often brightly colored, featuring a combination of yellow, orange, and red hues.
