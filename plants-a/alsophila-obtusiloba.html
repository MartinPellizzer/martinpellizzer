Alsophila obtusiloba

Alsophila obtusiloba, commonly known as the broad-leaf tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to tropical regions of Asia and Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds that can reach several meters in length. The plant has a distinctive crown of compound leaves that give it a graceful, ornamental appearance. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

The fronds are typically dark green and have a leathery texture, contributing to its visual appeal.
