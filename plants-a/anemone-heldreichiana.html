Anemone heldreichiana

Anemone heldreichiana, commonly known as the Balkan anemone, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of the Balkans.

It features showy, white to pinkish flowers that bloom in early spring, typically from April to May. The plant has basal leaves that are deeply lobed and covered in a fine, silky down. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forests and along stream banks.

Anemone heldreichiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with appropriate conditions.
