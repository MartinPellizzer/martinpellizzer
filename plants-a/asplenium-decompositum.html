Asplenium decompositum

Asplenium decompositum, commonly known as the broken fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Aspleniaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds that give it a lacy appearance. Native to tropical and subtropical regions, this plant thrives in moist, shaded environments. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine, and it is sometimes cultivated as an ornamental plant.

The plant's unique morphology and adaptability make it a subject of interest in both botanical research and horticulture.
