Asphodeliris nutans

Asphodeliris nutans, commonly known as the drooping asphodel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asphodelaceae family, which includes a variety of grass-like plants. The plant produces tall, slender stems that bear clusters of small, white or pale blue flowers. Its leaves are linear and grass-like, often with a slight sheen.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
