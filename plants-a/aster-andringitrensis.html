Aster andringitrensis

Aster andringitrensis, commonly known as the Andes aster, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Andes Mountains.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies, sunflowers, and other composite flowers. The plant is characterized by its showy, violet-blue blossoms that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine meadows.

Due to its unique habitat and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
