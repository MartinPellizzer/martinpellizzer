Allium kopetdagense

Allium kopetdagense, commonly known as the Kopetdag garlic, is a bulbous plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and spherical bulbs. The plant produces small, white to pinkish flowers arranged in a spherical inflorescence. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often growing in rocky soils.

Due to its ornamental value and potential medicinal properties, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
