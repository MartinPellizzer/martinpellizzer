Astrochlaena malvacea var. epedunculata

Astrochlaena malvacea var. epedunculata, commonly known as the slender hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family, which includes hibiscus and cotton plants. The plant features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically pink or white, with five petals and a prominent central stamen. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a soft, hairy texture.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along riverbanks.
