Angiopteris rahaoensis

Angiopteris rahaoensis, commonly known as the Rahao fern, is a species of tree fern native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the family Angiopteridaceae and is characterized by its tall, unbranched trunk covered in a dense layer of persistent, fibrous roots. The fronds are large, pinnately compound, and have a distinctive, often glossy appearance. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

The plant plays an important ecological role in its native habitat, contributing to soil stability and providing habitat for various organisms.
