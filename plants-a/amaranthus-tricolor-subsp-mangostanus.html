Amaranthus tricolor subsp. mangostanus

Amaranthus tricolor subsp.

mangostanus, commonly known as the purple leaf amaranth, is a species of flowering plant in the Amaranthaceae family. It is characterized by its vibrant, multicolored leaves that range from green to red, purple, and yellow.

The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow, and it is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in warm climates and is relatively drought-tolerant, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

The plant is also valued for its potential nutritional benefits, as its leaves are edible and rich in nutrients.
