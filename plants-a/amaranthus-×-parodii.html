Amaranthus × parodii

Amaranthus × parodii, commonly known as the red leaf amaranth, is a hybrid species of the amaranth family (Amaranthaceae).

It is characterized by its vibrant, feather-like leaves that range in color from deep red to purple, often with a striking contrast against its green stems. The plant is native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico, thriving in arid and semi-arid climates. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow, and it is often grown as an ornamental plant for its aesthetic appeal.

Amaranthus × parodii is also valued for its resilience, requiring minimal water and care once established.
