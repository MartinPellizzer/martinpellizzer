Aglaonema philippinense var. densinervium

Aglaonema philippinense var. densinervium, commonly known as the Philippine aglaonema, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the Philippines.

It features striking, dark green leaves with prominent, light green veins, giving it a distinctive and ornamental appearance. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters and has a compact, bushy growth habit. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage.

The leaves are leathery and have a waxy texture, which helps the plant retain moisture in its natural habitat.
