Aethusa pinnata

Aethusa pinnata, commonly known as the common cowparsley, is a biennial plant belonging to the Apiaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, hairy stems and fern-like, pinnately divided leaves. The plant produces small, white to greenish-yellow flowers arranged in umbrella-shaped clusters, typical of the carrot family. It is often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides, thriving in temperate climates.
