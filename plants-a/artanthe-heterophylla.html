Artanthe heterophylla

Artanthe heterophylla, commonly known as the variable artanthe, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the family Amaranthaceae and is characterized by its striking, showy inflorescences. The plant exhibits variable leaf morphology, with some leaves being lanceolate and others ovate, depending on environmental conditions. It thrives in rocky soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.

Its adaptability and ornamental value make it a popular choice for native plant gardens.
