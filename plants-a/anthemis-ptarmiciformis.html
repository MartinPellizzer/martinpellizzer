Anthemis ptarmiciformis

Anthemis ptarmiciformis, commonly known as the narrowleaf tansy, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to regions of southern Europe and western Asia, where it typically grows in dry, rocky habitats. The plant produces small, yellowish flower heads that are composed of numerous tiny florets, typical of the daisy family. Its leaves are narrow and often covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

The species is valued for its aromatic properties and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
