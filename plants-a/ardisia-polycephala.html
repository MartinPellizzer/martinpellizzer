Ardisia polycephala

Ardisia polycephala, commonly known as the clustered evergreen ardisia, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are opposite in arrangement and have a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by bright red berries that are attractive to birds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
