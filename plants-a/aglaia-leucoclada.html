Aglaia leucoclada

Aglaia leucoclada, commonly known as white aglaia, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Meliaceae, also known as the mahogany family. The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are typically ovate to elliptical in shape. Its flowers are small and clustered, often greenish-white in color, and it produces woody capsules as fruit.
