Appendicula vieillardii

Appendicula vieillardii, commonly known as the Sierra Madre bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Mexico.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale blue. Appendicula vieillardii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in rock gardens or alpine settings.
