Agapetes brachypoda var. brachypoda

Agapetes brachypoda var. brachypoda, commonly known as the short-stemmed agapete, is a shrub native to the temperate regions of South America.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and blueberries, and is characterized by its evergreen foliage.
