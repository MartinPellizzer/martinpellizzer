Agoseris heterophylla var. californica

Agoseris heterophylla var. californica, commonly known as the California agoseris, is a flowering plant native to California.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. It features basal leaves that are often lobed or toothed, and its yellow flowers bloom in summer.

This variety is known for its variability in leaf shape and size, which contributes to its adaptability in different environments.
