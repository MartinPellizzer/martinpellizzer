Achillea angustissima

Achillea angustissima, commonly known as narrowleaf yarrow, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its finely divided, feathery leaves and clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and thrives in full sun conditions. It is often found in grasslands, woodland edges, and disturbed areas.

Achillea angustissima is known for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally for its purported healing benefits.
