Astrotricha floccosa var. angustifolia

Astrotricha floccosa var. angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved starwort, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its small, white to pale yellow flowers that are clustered in star-like formations, giving it its common name. The plant typically grows in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in meadows and along streambanks. Its leaves are narrow and covered in fine, silky hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

It is a perennial herb that blooms from late spring to early summer.
