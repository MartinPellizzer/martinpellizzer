Alternanthera axillaris

Alternanthera axillaris, commonly known as the red-stemmed Alternanthera, is a herbaceous plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking red stems and lance-shaped leaves that often display a vibrant red or purple hue. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade. It is often used in water gardens and as a decorative plant due to its ornamental foliage.

Alternanthera axillaris is known for its rapid growth and ability to spread through rhizomes, making it a popular choice for pond edges and bog gardens.
