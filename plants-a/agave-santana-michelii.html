Agave santana-michelii

Agave santana-michelii, commonly known as the Santa Michelii agave, is a species native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped growth pattern and thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces a tall flowering stalk that bears clusters of yellowish-green flowers. It is adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Due to its unique appearance and resilience, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
