Artemisia argyi var. argyi

Artemisia argyi var. argyi, commonly known as Chinese mugwort, is a perennial shrub native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray, aromatic foliage and small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often growing in temperate climates. It is widely used in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.

Its essential oils contain compounds like thujone and camphor, which contribute to its medicinal and aromatic properties.
