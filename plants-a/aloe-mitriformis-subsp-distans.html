Aloe mitriformis subsp. distans

Aloe mitriformis subsp.

distans, commonly known as the narrow-leaved aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa. It features narrow, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a slight reddish tinge.

The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing yellow flowers, which bloom in summer. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves.

This subspecies is often cultivated for its ornamental value and resilience in dry conditions.
