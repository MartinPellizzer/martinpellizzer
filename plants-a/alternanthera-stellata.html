Alternanthera stellata

Alternanthera stellata, commonly known as the starred Alternanthera, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, star-shaped pink or red flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and thrives in moist, well-drained soils. It is often grown as an ornamental plant in gardens and water features.

Alternanthera stellata is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
