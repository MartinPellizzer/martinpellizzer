Aconitum × sprengelii

Aconitum × sprengelii, commonly known as Sprengel's monkshood, is a hybrid perennial plant belonging to the Ranunculaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking blue or purple spurred flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant features palmately divided, dark green leaves with a velvety texture. Native to Central Europe, it thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial shade.

It is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and historical use in traditional medicine.
