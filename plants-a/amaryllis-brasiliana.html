Amaryllis brasiliana

Amaryllis brasiliana, commonly known as the Brazilian amaryllis, is a bulbous plant native to South America.

It produces striking, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant features strap-like leaves that are often variegated with green and white markings. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Amaryllis brasiliana is popular as a ornamental plant due to its vibrant blooms and relatively easy care requirements.
