Asplenium nigripes var. solenopteris

Asplenium nigripes var. solenopteris, commonly known as the narrow-leaved spleenwort, is a fern species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Aspleniaceae and is characterized by its small, narrow, and often dark green leaves. The plant typically grows on rocks or soil in shaded, moist environments. Its fronds are usually pinnately divided, with a distinctive, elongated shape.

It is a hardy species that thrives in humid climates and is often found in forest understories.
