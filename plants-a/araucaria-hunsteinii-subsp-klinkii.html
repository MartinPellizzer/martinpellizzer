Araucaria hunsteinii subsp. klinkii

Araucaria hunsteinii subsp.

klinkii, commonly known as the Klinkii monkey puzzle tree, is a coniferous evergreen tree native to southern China. It belongs to the Araucariaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, spire-like growth habit and dense, needle-like foliage.

The tree produces cones that contain both male and female reproductive structures, typical of its family. Its bark is thick and fissured, providing protection against environmental stressors.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological significance in its native habitat.
