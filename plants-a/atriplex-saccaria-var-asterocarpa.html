Atriplex saccaria var. asterocarpa

Atriplex saccaria var. asterocarpa, commonly known as the silverleaf saltbush, is a shrub native to arid and semi-arid regions of North America.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage, which reflects sunlight and reduces water loss. The plant has small, scale-like leaves and produces clusters of greenish-yellow flowers. It is adapted to saline and alkaline soils, making it a valuable plant for stabilizing soils in harsh environments.

Its ecological importance includes providing food and habitat for various wildlife species.
