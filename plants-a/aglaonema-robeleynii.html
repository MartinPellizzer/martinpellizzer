Aglaonema robeleynii

Aglaonema robeleynii, commonly known as the Robeleynii Chinese evergreen, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated foliage with shades of green, white, and pink. The leaves are typically broad and lance-shaped, often featuring intricate patterns that enhance its ornamental appeal. This plant is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its tolerance of low light and moderate watering requirements.

It belongs to the Araceae family, which includes other popular houseplants like peace lilies and philodendrons.
