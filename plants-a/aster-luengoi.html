Aster luengoi

Aster luengoi, commonly known as the Luengoi aster, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in high-altitude grasslands and has small, blue to purple flower heads that bloom in the summer. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often covered in fine hairs, and it is adapted to cool, moist environments.

Aster luengoi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
