Adinobotrys vastus

Adinobotrys vastus, commonly known as the giant pandanus, is a flowering plant native to the Pacific Islands.

It is a member of the Pandanaceae family and is characterized by its large, sword-shaped leaves that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. Its roots are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

The plant thrives in humid, coastal environments and is adapted to saline soils.
