Agapetes filicicola

Agapetes filicicola, commonly known as the ferny agapete, is a species of evergreen shrub native to the mountainous regions of South America.

Agapetes filicicola is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens for its unique foliage and ecological significance.
