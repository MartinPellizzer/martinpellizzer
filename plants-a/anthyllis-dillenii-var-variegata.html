Anthyllis dillenii var. variegata

Anthyllis dillenii var. variegata, commonly known as the variegated broom.

This plant is a member of the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive variegated foliage with white and green patterns. It is a low-growing shrub that produces small, yellow flowers in early spring. Native to southern Europe, it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
