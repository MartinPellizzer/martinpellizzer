Anthurium × selloanum

Anthurium × selloanum, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a hybrid plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red spathes and elongated, heart-shaped spatula. The plant thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect sunlight for optimal growth. Its foliage is dark green and glossy, adding to its ornamental appeal.

Anthurium × selloanum is popular as a houseplant due to its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
