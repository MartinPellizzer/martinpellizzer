Allium × Proliferum

Allium × proliferum is a striking hybrid plant known for its vibrant, globe-shaped blooms. It belongs to the Allium genus, which includes popular species like onions and garlic. This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes. Its flowers come in a range of colors, from white to pink and purple. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a versatile addition to any garden.
