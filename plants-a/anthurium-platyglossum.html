Anthurium platyglossum

Anthurium platyglossum, commonly known as the flat-tongued anthurium, is a tropical flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped spathe that surrounds the spadix. The plant produces striking, colorful inflorescences that are often used in ornamental horticulture. Its leaves are typically broad and glossy, with a deep green color.

Anthurium platyglossum thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.
