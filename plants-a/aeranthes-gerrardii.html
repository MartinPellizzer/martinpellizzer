Aeranthes gerrardii

Aeranthes gerrardii, commonly known as the Cape orchid, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, colorful floral structures. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its flowers are often fragrant and attract various pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and botanical collections worldwide.
