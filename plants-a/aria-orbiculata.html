Aria orbiculata

Aria orbiculata, commonly known as the globe lily, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its round, orbicular leaves and striking, showy flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters and prefers well-drained soil and full sun. It is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for.

Aria orbiculata is also known for its ability to thrive in coastal areas due to its tolerance for salt spray.
