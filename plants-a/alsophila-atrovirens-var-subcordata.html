Alsophila atrovirens var. subcordata

Alsophila atrovirens var. subcordata, commonly known as the dark green tree fern, is a variant of the tree fern species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and a crown of feathery, pinnate fronds that give it a lush, ornamental appearance. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in rainforests or along stream banks. Its fronds are typically dark green, with a leathery texture, and the plant can grow to significant heights over time.

Alsophila atrovirens var. subcordata is valued for its aesthetic appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a decorative plant in suitable climates.
