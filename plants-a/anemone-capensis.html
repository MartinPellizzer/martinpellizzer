Anemone capensis

Anemone capensis, commonly known as the Cape anemone, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in coastal areas and sandy soils. The plant features showy, white to pinkish flowers that bloom in spring and summer. Its foliage consists of large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

Anemone capensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
