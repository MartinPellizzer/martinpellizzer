Anemone campanella

Anemone campanella, commonly known as the bell anemone, is a herbaceous perennial native to the eastern United States.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in early spring. The plant has a short, upright stem with palmately divided leaves that are deeply lobed. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens.

Its delicate appearance and early bloom make it a valuable addition to native plant landscapes.
