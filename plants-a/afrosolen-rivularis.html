Afrosolen rivularis

Afrosolen rivularis, commonly known as the river sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in wetland environments, such as marshes and riverbanks, where it thrives in moist, nutrient-rich soils. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that form dense clumps, and it produces small, inconspicuous greenish flowers that bloom in summer. Afrosolen rivularis is often found in shallow water or along the edges of water bodies.
