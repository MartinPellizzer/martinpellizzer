Anthurium breviscapum

Anthurium breviscapum, commonly known as the short-spurred anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-shaped inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, typically in shades of red, orange, or yellow. It thrives in humid, warm environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium breviscapum is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and unique floral structure.
