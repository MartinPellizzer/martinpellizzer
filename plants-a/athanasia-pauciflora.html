Athanasia pauciflora

Athanasia pauciflora, commonly known as the sparse-flowered immortality, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant produces small, yellow to orange flowers arranged in compact clusters, which bloom during the warmer months. It is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments.

Athanasia pauciflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in challenging conditions.
