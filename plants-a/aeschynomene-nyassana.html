Aeschynomene nyassana

Aeschynomene nyassana, commonly known as African striga, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellow flowers. The plant is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions. It plays a role in nitrogen fixation due to its symbiotic relationship with rhizobia bacteria.

However, it is also considered a weed in agricultural settings.
