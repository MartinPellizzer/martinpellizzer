Avena glabrescens

Avena glabrescens, commonly known as the slender oat, is a perennial grass native to the western United States.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences with numerous tiny flowers. The plant is characterized by its smooth, glabrous surface and is often found in dry, rocky habitats. It has a fibrous root system and is adapted to arid environments.

Avena glabrescens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in native plant gardens.
