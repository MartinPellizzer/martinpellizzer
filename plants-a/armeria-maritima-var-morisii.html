Armeria maritima var. morisii

Armeria maritima var. morisii, commonly known as the Morisii thrift, is a low-growing, evergreen perennial that thrives in coastal environments.

It features dense clusters of white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are typically covered in a fine layer of white fuzz. It is well adapted to salty air and sandy soils, making it a hardy choice for coastal gardens.

This variety is native to the Pacific coast of North America and is often used in xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance.
