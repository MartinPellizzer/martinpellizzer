Alsophila walkerae var. tripinnata

Alsophila walkerae var. tripinnata, commonly known as the Tripinnate Tree Fern, is a member of the Cyatheaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of Indonesia and Malaysia. The plant features a tall, unbranched trunk with a fibrous, spongy cortex. Its fronds are pinnately compound, with a distinctive tripinnate structure, giving it a lush, feathery appearance.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.
