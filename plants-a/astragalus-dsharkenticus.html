Astragalus dsharkenticus

Astragalus dsharkenticus, commonly known as the Dsharkent Astragalus, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, particularly in Kazakhstan and Uzbekistan. The plant is characterized by its small, yellowish flowers and compound leaves that are typical of the genus Astragalus. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often growing in rocky soils.
