Astragalus spinosus f. aegyptiacus

Astragalus spinosus f. aegyptiacus, commonly known as the Egyptian milkvetch, is a variant of the spiny milkvetch species.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant characterized by its spiny stems and compound leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in umbel-like clusters. It is native to arid and semi-arid regions of the Middle East and North Africa.

This subspecies is adapted to dry environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
