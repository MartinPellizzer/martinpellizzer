Athanasia pumila

Athanasia pumila, commonly known as the dwarf everlasting or small everlasting, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing, mat-forming perennial that produces small, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and red. The plant is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage and is often found in rocky or sandy soils. It thrives in full sun and is drought-tolerant, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Athanasia pumila is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
