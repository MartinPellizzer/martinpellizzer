Aspidium natalense

Aspidium natalense, commonly known as Natal shield fern, is a species of fern native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Athyriaceae and is characterized by its dense, evergreen fronds that form a compact, shield-like structure. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky outcrops. Its fronds are divided into lance-shaped segments with a serrated margin.

Aspidium natalense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its lush, tropical appearance.
