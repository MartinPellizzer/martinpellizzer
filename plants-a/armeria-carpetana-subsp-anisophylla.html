Armeria carpetana subsp. anisophylla

Armeria carpetana subsp.

anisophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leaved thrift, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region. It is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit and produces clusters of white or pale pink flowers.

The leaves are typically narrow and linear, which distinguishes it from other members of the Armeria species. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or coastal environments.

This subspecies is notable for its hardy nature and ability to withstand harsh climatic conditions.
