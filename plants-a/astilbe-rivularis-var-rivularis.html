Astilbe rivularis var. rivularis

Astilbe rivularis var. rivularis, commonly known as the river astilbe, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to eastern Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodland floors and stream banks. The plant features upright, branched stems that bear clusters of small, lace-like, panicle-shaped flowers in shades of pink, white, or red. Its foliage is finely divided, with a deep green color that adds visual interest throughout the growing season.

Astilbe rivularis var. rivularis is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in partially shaded conditions.
