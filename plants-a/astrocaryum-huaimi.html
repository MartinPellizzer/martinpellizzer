Astrocaryum huaimi

Astrocaryum huaimi, commonly known as the Huaimi palm, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil.

It is a medium-sized palm characterized by its slender trunk and pinnate leaves that can reach several meters in length. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in branched inflorescences, followed by clustered fruits that are typically reddish when mature. Its botanical features make it an important component of the rainforest ecosystem, contributing to biodiversity and ecological balance.

The species is also valued for its ornamental appeal and potential economic uses in local communities.
