Anthurium × rosiflorum

Anthurium × rosiflorum, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a hybrid plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces vibrant red spathes that enclose the spadix, which is covered in small, yellowish flowers. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Anthurium × rosiflorum is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and ability to purify indoor air.
