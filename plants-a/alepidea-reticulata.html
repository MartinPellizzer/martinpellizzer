Alepidea reticulata

Alepidea reticulata, commonly known as the net-veined daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, daisy-like flowers arranged in a compact cluster. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces yellow flowers with white ray florets. It is often found in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.

Alepidea reticulata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
