Aloe speciosa

Aloe speciosa, commonly known as the showy aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a waxy coating, and it produces striking, upward-facing yellow flowers. The plant is characterized by its rosette growth pattern and is well adapted to arid environments. It thrives in full sun and requires minimal water, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Aloe speciosa is also valued for its medicinal properties, similar to other aloe species.
