Anthurium fendleri

Anthurium fendleri, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces vibrant red spathes that enclose its spadix, which is a cluster of tiny flowers. Anthurium fendleri thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

It is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and unique flowering structure.
