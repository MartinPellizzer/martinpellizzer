Adenia natalensis

Adenia natalensis, commonly known as Natal Adenia, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant yellow flowers, which are followed by distinctive, fleshy, red berries. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a rough texture.

Adenia natalensis is adapted to grow in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forest understories.
