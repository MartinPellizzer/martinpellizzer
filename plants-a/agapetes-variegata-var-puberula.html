Agapetes variegata var. puberula

Agapetes variegata var. puberula, commonly known as the variegated silverleaf evergreen, is a species of flowering plant in the Ericaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of South America, particularly in Ecuador and Peru. The flowers are small and white, clustered in dense inflorescences, and bloom during the cooler months of the year.
