Anthyllis vulneraria subproles maritima

Anthyllis vulneraria subproles maritima, commonly known as the sea hawthorn, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family Fabaceae.

It is a low-growing perennial shrub that thrives in coastal environments, particularly on sandy or gravelly soils.
