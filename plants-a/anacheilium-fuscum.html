Anacheilium fuscum

Anacheilium fuscum, commonly known as the dark-veined orchid, is a species of orchid native to the mountainous regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dark green leaves and striking, dark purple to black flowers with intricate veining. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in areas with high altitude and cool temperatures. Its flowers bloom during the summer months, adding a unique aesthetic to its natural habitat.

Anacheilium fuscum is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated by enthusiasts of rare orchids.
