Asphodelus grandiflorus

Asphodelus grandiflorus, commonly known as the giant asphodel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in grasslands and rocky areas, reaching heights of up to 2 meters. The plant produces tall, erect stems with narrow, grass-like leaves and clusters of bright yellow flowers. Its inflorescence consists of numerous small, tubular flowers that bloom in spring.

Asphodelus grandiflorus is well adapted to dry conditions and is often used in ornamental gardening for its striking appearance.
