Acrorumohra

Acrorumohra, commonly known as the silverleaf orchid, is a striking member of the orchid family.

It is characterized by its silvery-green foliage and delicate, fragrant flowers that bloom in spring. Native to the humid rainforests of Southeast Asia, this plant thrives in warm, moist environments. Its roots are adapted to absorb nutrients from the rich, organic soil of its natural habitat.

The plant's unique appearance and aromatic blooms make it a popular choice for botanical gardens and enthusiasts.
