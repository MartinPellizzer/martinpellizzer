Angiopteris danaeoides

Angiopteris danaeoides, commonly known as the Danae fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, pinnately compound fronds that can grow up to 2 meters in length. The plant has a robust rhizome system that allows it to thrive in moist, shaded environments. Its leaves are typically dark green and have a leathery texture.

Angiopteris danaeoides is often cultivated for its ornamental value in tropical gardens and greenhouses.
