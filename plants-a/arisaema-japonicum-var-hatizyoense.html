Arisaema japonicum var. hatizyoense

Arisaema japonicum var. hatizyoense, commonly known as the Hatizyo Japanese Arum, is a herbaceous perennial native to Japan.

It features a distinctive, spadix-shaped inflorescence surrounded by a broad, heart-shaped spathe. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or along stream banks. Its foliage consists of palmately lobed leaves that are often dark green with a glossy appearance.

The species is valued for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage and dramatic flowering structure.
