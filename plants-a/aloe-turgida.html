Aloe turgida

Aloe turgida, commonly known as the red aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with red or purple markings. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow flowers, which bloom in summer. Its leaves contain a gel-like substance that is often used for its soothing properties.

Aloe turgida is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
