Arisaema yunnanense var. aridum

Arisaema yunnanense var. aridum, commonly known as the Yunnan arum, is a perennial herb native to arid regions of Yunnan Province in China.

It features a rhizomatous stem with large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically green with a waxy texture. The plant produces an inflorescence consisting of a spadix surrounded by a modified leaf called a spathe. Its flowers are unisexual, with separate male and female flowers on the spadix.

This species is adapted to dry environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
