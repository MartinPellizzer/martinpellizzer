Arenaria bhutanica

Arenaria bhutanica, commonly known as the Bhutan sandwort, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayan region, specifically Bhutan.

It is a low-growing herbaceous plant that typically thrives in rocky and alpine environments. The species features small, clustered flowers that are usually white or pale pink, blooming in the spring. Its leaves are linear and succulent, adapted to retain moisture in its arid habitat.

Arenaria bhutanica plays a role in stabilizing soil in mountainous regions and is of interest to botanists studying alpine flora.
