Artemisia potentilloides var. potentilloides

Artemisia potentilloides var. potentilloides, commonly known as silver bluestem, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the United States.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, silky hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in panicles during the summer months. It thrives in dry, well-drained soils and is often found in prairie and steppe habitats.

Its aromatic leaves and drought tolerance make it a valuable plant for ecological restoration and ornamental use.
