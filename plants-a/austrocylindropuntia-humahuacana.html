Austrocylindropuntia humahuacana

Austrocylindropuntia humahuacana, commonly known as the Huahuan cactus, is a species of cactus native to southern Mexico.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems that are typically green and covered in spines. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months, which are followed by red, spherical fruits. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky soils.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological role, it is a notable species within the Cactaceae family.
