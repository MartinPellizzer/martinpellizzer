Astragalus chodshamastonicus

Astragalus chodshamastonicus, commonly known as the Chodshamastonic milkvetch, is a flowering plant species native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, pea-like flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing adaptations to harsh climates. Its root system is often deep, allowing it to access water from lower soil layers.
