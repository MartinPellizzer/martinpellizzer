Aira altissima

Aira altissima, commonly known as giant air grass, is a tall, flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its dense, grass-like foliage and can grow up to 6 feet in height. The plant produces spike-like inflorescences that bear small, white to greenish flowers. It thrives in moist, sunny environments and is often found in wetlands and along stream banks.

Botanically, it is notable for its rhizomatous growth and ability to form dense stands.
