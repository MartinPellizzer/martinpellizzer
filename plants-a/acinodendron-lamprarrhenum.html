Acinodendron lamprarrhenum

Acinodendron lamprarrhenum, commonly known as the red-flowered acacia, is a shrub native to northern Australia.

Its most distinctive feature is its bright red flowers, which are clustered in dense, spherical inflorescences.

The plant is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a resilient component of its native ecosystem.
