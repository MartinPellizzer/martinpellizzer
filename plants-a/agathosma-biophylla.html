Agathosma biophylla

Agathosma biophylla, commonly known as African geranium, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, aromatic leaves and small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant belongs to the family Geraniaceae and is well adapted to arid environments. It is often used in traditional medicine and as an ornamental plant due to its hardy nature and attractive appearance.

The leaves contain essential oils that contribute to its distinctive fragrance and medicinal properties.
