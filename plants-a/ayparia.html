Ayparia

Ayparia, commonly known as the silverleaf plant, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the family Amaranthaceae and is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage that reflects sunlight, helping it retain moisture in harsh environments. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically green or reddish in color. Ayparia is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.

Its adaptability to dry conditions makes it a valuable species for ecological restoration projects.
