Aloe deltoideodonta subsp. brevifolia

Aloe deltoideodonta subsp.

brevifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa. It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically shorter and broader than those of other aloe species.

The plant produces tall, branched flower stalks that bear yellowish-green flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its leaves contain a gel-like substance that has been used for various medicinal purposes.
