Abies veitchii

Abies veitchii, commonly known as the Veitch fir, is a coniferous evergreen tree native to the mountainous regions of China.

It is characterized by its slender, pyramidal shape and dark green, needle-like foliage. The tree produces small, spherical cones that mature to a reddish-brown color. Its bark is smooth and grayish-white, becoming fissured with age.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
