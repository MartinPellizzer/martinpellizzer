Aristogeitonia monophylla

Aristogeitonia monophylla, commonly known as the single-leaf arrowhead, is a unique aquatic plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its single, arrow-shaped leaf that floats on the water surface. The plant has a slender, branching stem that often extends into the water, supporting the leaf. Its leaves are typically a vibrant green and can grow up to 10 centimeters in length.

This species is often cultivated in ponds and water gardens for its ornamental value and ability to provide habitat for aquatic life.
