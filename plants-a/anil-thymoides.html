Anil thymoides

Anil thymoides, commonly known as Indian thyme, is a flowering plant native to the Indian subcontinent.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant features small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Its leaves are aromatic and have a thyme-like scent, making it useful in traditional medicine.

Anil thymoides is often grown for its ornamental value and medicinal properties.
