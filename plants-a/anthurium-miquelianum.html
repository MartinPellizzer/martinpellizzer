Anthurium miquelianum

Anthurium miquelianum, commonly known as the Miquelian anthurium, is a tropical plant native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces striking spadix inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, which can range from red to orange. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect sunlight for optimal growth.

Anthurium miquelianum is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
