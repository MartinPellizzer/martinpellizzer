Arenaria conimbricensis var. loscosii

Arenaria conimbricensis var. loscosii, commonly known as the Alentejo sandwort, is a flowering plant native to Portugal.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that thrives in sandy and arid environments. The plant produces small, white or pink flowers during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are typically linear and adapted to reduce water loss.

It is often found in coastal dunes and other dry, nutrient-poor soils.
