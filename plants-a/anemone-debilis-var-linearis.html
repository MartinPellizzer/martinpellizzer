Anemone debilis var. linearis

Anemone debilis var. linearis, commonly known as the slender anemone, is a herbaceous perennial native to the eastern United States.

It features delicate, fern-like foliage and produces showy, white to pale pink flowers with a distinctive central tuft of stamens. The plant typically grows to a height of 6 to 12 inches and thrives in partial to full shade. It prefers moist, well-drained soil and is often found in woodland environments.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and ability to naturalize in suitable conditions.
