Agelanthus krausei

Agelanthus krausei, commonly known as the Cape honeysuckle, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 6 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs.

Agelanthus krausei is well adapted to arid environments and thrives in full sun.
