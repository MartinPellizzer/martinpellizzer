Alchemilla sericea subsp. chlorosericea

Alchemilla sericea subsp.

chlorosericea, commonly known as silverleaf lady's mantle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It is characterized by its silvery, fuzzy leaves that give it a distinctive appearance.

The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in clusters, typically blooming in early summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

This subspecies is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
