Ajax rudbeckii

Ajax rudbeckii, commonly known as the Swedish buttercup, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to northern Europe.

It features showy yellow flowers with a distinctive cup-shaped structure, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has smooth, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or wetland habitats.

Ajax rudbeckii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, supporting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
