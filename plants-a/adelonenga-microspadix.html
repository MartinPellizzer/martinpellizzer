Adelonenga microspadix

Adelonenga microspadix, commonly known as the small-spadix cycad, is a unique plant belonging to the Cycadaceae family.

It is characterized by its stout, unbranched trunk and a dense, spadix-like structure that bears both male and female flowers. The plant produces large, feather-like leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, requiring well-drained soil and plenty of sunlight.

Due to its slow growth and conservation status, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
