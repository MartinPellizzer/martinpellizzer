Alternanthera bettzickiana f. kurzii

Alternanthera bettzickiana f. kurzii, commonly known as the red-leaf alternanthera, is a striking aquatic plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red or pink foliage, which intensifies under strong light conditions. This plant thrives in shallow, slow-moving waters and prefers soft, slightly acidic water with a moderate to high level of nutrients. It is often cultivated in ponds and water gardens for its ornamental value.

The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit, allowing it to spread and form dense underwater beds.
