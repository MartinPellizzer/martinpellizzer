Andropogon nigrum

Andropogon nigrum, commonly known as black bluestem, is a perennial grass native to North America.

It is characterized by its dark purple or blackish stems, which give it its common name. The plant typically grows in open woodlands and prairies, thriving in well-drained soils. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green color.

Andropogon nigrum plays an important role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various wildlife species.
