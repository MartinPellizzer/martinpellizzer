Astragalus divnogorskajae

Astragalus divnogorskajae, commonly known as the Divnogorskaja milkvetch, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its small, yellowish flowers and trifoliate leaves, typical of many Astragalus species. Native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, this plant thrives in arid and semi-arid environments. It plays a role in soil stabilization and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and potentially endangered species.
