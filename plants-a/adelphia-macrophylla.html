Adelphia macrophylla

Adelphia macrophylla, commonly known as the large-leaved adelphia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Central America and northern South America.

Adelphia macrophylla is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in warm climates for its attractive foliage and floral display.
