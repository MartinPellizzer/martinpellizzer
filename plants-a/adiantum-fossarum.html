Adiantum fossarum

Adiantum fossarum, commonly known as the water fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Adiantaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds that are typically bright green and have a feathery appearance. This plant thrives in moist, shady environments, often found near streams or in damp woodlands. Its rhizomes are usually subterranean, allowing it to spread vegetatively.

Adiantum fossarum is notable for its ability to tolerate waterlogged soils, making it a common sight in wetland habitats.
