Adromischus mammillaris var. filicaulis

Adromischus mammillaris var. filicaulis, commonly known as the "Lemon Tree" or "Lemon Leaf" plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy, light green leaves that are often tinged with red or purple at the edges, especially in cooler temperatures. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect sunlight.

Due to its compact growth habit, it is well-suited for container gardening and indoor spaces.
