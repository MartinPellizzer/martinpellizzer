Aquilegia hybrida var. bicolor

Aquilegia hybrida var. bicolor, commonly known as the two-colored columbine, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe.

It features showy, spurred flowers that come in a striking combination of red and yellow, with the outer petals being red and the inner ones yellow. The plant typically grows to a height of 30 to 60 centimeters and thrives in well-drained, fertile soil. It prefers full sun to partial shade and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.

The leaves are deeply lobed and have a distinctive, fern-like appearance.
