Aspasia variegata

Aspasia variegata, commonly known as the variegated aspasia, is a member of the Araliaceae family.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its striking, variegated foliage that features shades of white, yellow, and green. Native to tropical and subtropical regions, it thrives in humid, well-drained soils. The plant produces small, white flowers that are typically followed by red berries.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardening.
