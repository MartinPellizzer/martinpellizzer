Astronium lecointei f. tomentosum

Astronium lecointei f. tomentosum, commonly known as the tomentose sycamore, is a tropical tree species native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a dense, woolly pubescence. The tree produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, followed by oval, woody fruits containing a single seed. Its bark is thick and fissured, providing structural support and contributing to its resilience in humid, tropical environments.

This species is valued for its timber and has ecological significance in its native habitats.
