Abarema microcalyx var. enterolobioides

Abarema microcalyx var. enterolobioides, commonly known as the small-flowered abarema, is a species of flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its small, delicate flowers and trifoliate leaves. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in moist, shaded environments. It produces pods that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind or animals.

This variant is noted for its ornamental value and potential use in ecological restoration projects.
