Adesmia muricata var. rionegrensis

Adesmia muricata var. rionegrensis, commonly known as the Rionegro thornvetch, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Colombia.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid conditions. Its leaves are trifoliate, with small, oval-shaped leaflets.

The species is of interest in botanical studies due to its unique morphology and ecological adaptations.
