Anthurium lucorum

Anthurium lucorum, commonly known as the red-tipped anthurium, is a tropical flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are dark green with a reddish-purple margin. The plant produces vibrant red spathes that enclose its yellow-green spadix, creating a dramatic visual display. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium lucorum is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and long-lasting blooms.
