Abdominea minimiflora

Abdominea minimiflora, commonly known as the miniature flower plant, is a small, herbaceous species native to arid regions.

It features delicate, clustered flowers that are typically pale yellow or greenish in color. The plant has a compact growth habit with short, branching stems and small, narrow leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Its unique morphology and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice for rock gardens and drought-tolerant landscapes.
