Artemisia royleana

Artemisia royleana, commonly known as Indian wormwood, is a flowering plant in the sunflower family.

It is native to regions of Asia, including India, Pakistan, and China. The plant is characterized by its aromatic, silver-gray foliage and small yellow flowers. It thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid environments.

Artemisia royleana has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
