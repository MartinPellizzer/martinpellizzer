Anacampseros stellata

Anacampseros stellata, commonly known as the star cactus, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a compact, rounded stem with star-shaped, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a bluish tint. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the summer months, adding a vibrant touch to its appearance. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation.
