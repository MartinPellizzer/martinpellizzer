Aglaonema simplex f. inaequale

Aglaonema simplex f. inaequale, commonly known as the Variegated Chinese Evergreen, is a cultivar of the Aglaonema genus native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated foliage with shades of green, white, and cream, which adds visual interest to indoor spaces. The leaves are typically broad, lance-shaped, and have a smooth texture, often with a slightly waxy appearance. This plant thrives in low to moderate light conditions and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

It is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its air-purifying qualities and ease of care.
