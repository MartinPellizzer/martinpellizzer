Aloe haroniensis

Aloe haroniensis, commonly known as the Haroniensis Aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a waxy coating, which helps it retain moisture in arid environments. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow flowers, often blooming in spring. Its leaves contain a gel-like substance that is used in traditional medicine and skincare products.

Aloe haroniensis is well adapted to dry climates and requires minimal water to thrive.
