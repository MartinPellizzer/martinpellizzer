Asplenium maxonii

Asplenium maxonii, commonly known as the African shield fern, is a species of fern native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the family Aspleniaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, shield-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its rhizomes are often covered in a dense, velvety layer of brownish hairs.

Asplenium maxonii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant.
