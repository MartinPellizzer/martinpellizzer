Athyrium nitens

Athyrium nitens, commonly known as the silver lace fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, lacy fronds that are typically dark green with a silvery underside. The plant thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its rhizomes are creeping and help it spread across forest floors.

Athyrium nitens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
