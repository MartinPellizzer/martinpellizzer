Ajax Bicolor

Ajax bicolor is a unique and striking plant known for its vibrant, bi-colored flowers. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is native to specific regions in the southern United States. The plant features bright yellow petals that contrast sharply with its deep red centers, creating a visually captivating display. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardeners. Its ornamental value and hardy nature have made it a favorite in both wild and cultivated landscapes.
