Anthurium arbelaezii

Anthurium arbelaezii, commonly known as the Cauca anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Colombia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves and vibrant, spadix-like inflorescence. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. It thrives in humid, shaded environments with high levels of indirect light.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and botanical collections.
