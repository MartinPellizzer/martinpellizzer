Anthurium narae

Anthurium narae, commonly known as the Narae anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its unique inflorescence consisting of a spadix and a spathe. The plant features large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. Its flowers are usually bright red or orange, surrounded by a white spathe, making it visually striking.

Anthurium narae is often cultivated for its ornamental value in indoor and greenhouse settings.
