Aster siyuanensis

Aster siyuanensis, commonly known as the Siyuan aster, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically features showy blue to purple flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
