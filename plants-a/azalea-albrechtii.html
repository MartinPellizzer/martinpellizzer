Azalea albrechtii

Azalea albrechtii, commonly known as the Chinese azalea, is a species of flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Rhododendron genus and is characterized by its showy, clustered flowers that range in color from white to deep pink. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are dark green, glossy, and elliptical in shape, providing a striking contrast to its vibrant blooms.

Azalea albrechtii thrives in acidic, well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
