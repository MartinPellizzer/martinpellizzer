Astridia vanheerdei

Astridia vanheerdei, commonly known as the Van Heerde's aster, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and prefers full sun exposure. Its flowers are usually blue to purple in color, with a central disk surrounded by ray florets.

Astridia vanheerdei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its attractive blooms.
