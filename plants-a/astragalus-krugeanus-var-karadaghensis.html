Astragalus krugeanus var. karadaghensis

Astragalus krugeanus var. karadaghensis, commonly known as the Karadagh milkvetch, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its erect growth habit and trifoliate leaves, with small, yellowish to brownish flowers clustered in racemes. Native to the mountainous regions of the Caucasus, this species thrives in rocky and arid environments. The plant has a deep root system, which helps it survive in nutrient-poor soils.

Its ecological importance includes nitrogen fixation, contributing to soil fertility in its native habitat.
