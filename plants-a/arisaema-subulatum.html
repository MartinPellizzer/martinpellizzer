Arisaema subulatum

Arisaema subulatum, commonly known as the needleleaf arisaema, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia, particularly China.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its unique inflorescence consisting of a spadix surrounded by a spathe. The plant features narrow, needle-like leaves that give it its common name, and it produces small, greenish-white flowers. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Arisaema subulatum is appreciated for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
