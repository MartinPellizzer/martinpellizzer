Aglaia Rimosa

Aglaia rimosa is a striking tropical plant known for its vibrant, colorful flowers that attract a variety of pollinators. Native to Southeast Asia, this species thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in forested areas. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and botanical collections. The plant's leaves are large and glossy, adding to its visual appeal. With its ecological and aesthetic value, Aglaia rimosa plays an important role in both natural and cultivated environments.
