Arundinaria virens var. tenuifolia

Arundinaria virens var. tenuifolia, commonly known as the slender bamboo, is a variety of the green bamboo species native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, graceful stems that can grow up to several meters in height. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers, which are typically borne in clusters. Its leaves are narrow and elongated, contributing to its delicate appearance.

This variety is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.
