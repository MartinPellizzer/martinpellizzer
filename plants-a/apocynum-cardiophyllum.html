Apocynum cardiophyllum

Apocynum cardiophyllum, commonly known as the red spider lily, is a flowering plant native to eastern North America.

It is characterized by its striking red-orange flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has elliptical, dark green leaves and produces capsule fruits that contain toxic seeds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or wetland habitats.

Due to its toxicity, it should be handled with care.
