Agathosma microcalyx

Agathosma microcalyx, commonly known as Cape garlic.

It is a small, evergreen shrub native to the Western Cape region of South Africa. The plant features narrow, aromatic leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in summer. It is often cultivated for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.

Agathosma microcalyx thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.
