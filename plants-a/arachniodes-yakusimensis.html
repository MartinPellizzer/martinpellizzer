Arachniodes yakusimensis

Arachniodes yakusimensis, commonly known as the Yakushima fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to Japan.

It is characterized by its large, leathery, deeply lobed leaves that can reach lengths of up to 1 meter. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, typically found in mountainous regions with high rainfall. Its fronds are often adorned with a fine, silky down, giving it a distinctive appearance.

This fern is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in shady gardens and greenhouses.
