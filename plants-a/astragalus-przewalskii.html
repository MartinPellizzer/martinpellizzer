Astragalus przewalskii

Astragalus przewalskii, commonly known as the Przewalski's milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its yellow flowers and compound leaves. The plant is known for its deep root system, which allows it to thrive in arid and rocky environments.

It plays an important ecological role in stabilizing soil and supporting local biodiversity.
