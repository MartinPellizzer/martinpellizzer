Alchemilla fagetii

Alchemilla fagetii, commonly known as the wood sorrel, is a low-growing perennial plant native to temperate regions of Europe.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, yellowish flowers. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas. Its foliage is covered in fine, silky hairs, which give it a distinctive appearance.
