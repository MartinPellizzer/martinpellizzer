Anemone × media

Anemone × media, commonly known as the wood anemone, is a hybrid perennial plant native to temperate regions of Europe and Asia.

It features showy, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring, typically from March to May. The plant has finely divided, fern-like leaves that emerge from a rhizomatous root system. It thrives in moist, shaded woodland environments and is often found in forest understories.

Anemone × media is valued in gardens for its delicate appearance and early season blooms.
