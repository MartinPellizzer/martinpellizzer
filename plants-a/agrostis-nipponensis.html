Agrostis nipponensis

Agrostis nipponensis, commonly known as Japanese meadowgrass, is a perennial grass species native to East Asia.

It typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows, grasslands, and along stream banks. The plant has slender, erect stems and produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny florets. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green coloration.

Agrostis nipponensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping and restoration projects.
