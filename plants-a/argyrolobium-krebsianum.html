Argyrolobium krebsianum

Argyrolobium krebsianum, commonly known as the silver globular pea, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive silver-gray foliage and spherical seed pods. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance and ability to fix nitrogen in the soil.
