Athyrocarpus pseudomonosperma

Athyrocarpus pseudomonosperma, commonly known as the single-seeded banyan, is a member of the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is a large, evergreen tree native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia. The plant is characterized by its thick, buttressed roots and a broad, umbrella-shaped canopy. Its fruit is a single-seeded capsule that splits open to release its seed.
