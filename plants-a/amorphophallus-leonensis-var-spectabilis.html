Amorphophallus leonensis var. spectabilis

Amorphophallus leonensis var. spectabilis, commonly known as the showy giant arrowhead, is a striking tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 3 meters in length, making it a dramatic addition to any garden. The plant produces a unique, often spiky inflorescence that resembles a phallic shape, hence its common name. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This variant is particularly valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its bold foliage and distinctive floral display.
