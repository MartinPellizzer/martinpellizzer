Athruphyllum

Athruphyllum, commonly known as the silver fern, is a unique plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the fern family and is characterized by its distinctive, feathery fronds that resemble a silver crown. The plant's leaves are composed of numerous small, overlapping pinnae that give it a shimmering appearance when sunlight reflects off them. Athruphyllum thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

It plays an important ecological role in its native habitat, providing shelter and food for various wildlife species.
