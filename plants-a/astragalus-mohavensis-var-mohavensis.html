Astragalus mohavensis var. mohavensis

Astragalus mohavensis var. mohavensis, commonly known as Mohave milkvetch, is a perennial legume native to the Mojave Desert.

It typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and thrives in arid environments. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval leaflets and produces yellow to reddish-purple flowers. It is adapted to withstand extreme temperatures and limited water availability.

Its seeds are often dispersed by wind, contributing to its distribution across desert regions.
