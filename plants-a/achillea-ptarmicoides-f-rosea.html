Achillea ptarmicoides f. rosea

Achillea ptarmicoides f. rosea, commonly known as the pink yarrow, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is a cultivar of the common yarrow, characterized by its pale pink to rose-colored flowers. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats and is native to parts of Europe. It features finely divided, gray-green foliage and produces small, daisy-like flowers in late spring to early summer.

This ornamental plant is valued for its hardiness and ability to thrive in well-drained soil.
