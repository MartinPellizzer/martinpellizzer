Aloe cuspidata

Aloe cuspidata, commonly known as the Cape aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, spindly stems with narrow, pointed leaves that are often covered in a whitish bloom. The leaves are fleshy and store water, allowing the plant to thrive in arid conditions. It produces yellowish-green flowers in clusters at the top of the stem during the warmer months.

This species is valued for its ornamental beauty and has some traditional medicinal uses.
