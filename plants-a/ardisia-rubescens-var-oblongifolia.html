Ardisia rubescens var. oblongifolia

Ardisia rubescens var. oblongifolia, commonly known as the red-berried evergreen, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features elliptical, glossy leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces small, white flowers followed by bright red berries that are attractive to birds. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for ornamental gardens.

Its compact growth habit and seasonal color changes make it a popular choice for landscaping.
