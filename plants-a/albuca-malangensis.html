Albuca malangensis

Albuca malangensis, commonly known as the Malang bulb, is a bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its compact, fleshy bulbs and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Albuca malangensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ease of care.
