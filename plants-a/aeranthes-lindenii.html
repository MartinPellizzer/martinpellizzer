Aeranthes lindenii

Aeranthes lindenii, commonly known as the Lindenberg's orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically feature a combination of purple and white hues. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot. Its roots are specialized for absorbing nutrients from the surrounding substrate, a common adaptation in orchids.

Aeranthes lindenii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in botanical gardens and private collections.
