Acosta capillata

Acosta capillata, commonly known as the slender acosta, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the family Poaceae, which includes grasses, and is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often covered in fine hairs.
