Albuca engleriana

Albuca engleriana, commonly known as the Engler's albuca, is a bulbous plant native to southern Africa.

It features strap-shaped leaves that are typically bright green with a waxy coating. The plant produces clusters of white or pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Albuca engleriana is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ease of care.
