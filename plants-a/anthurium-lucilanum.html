Anthurium lucilanum

Anthurium lucilanum, commonly known as the Lucilanum Arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive inflorescence composed of a spadix and a spathe. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Anthurium lucilanum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in botanical gardens.
