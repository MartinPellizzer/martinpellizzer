Aristida gracilior

Aristida gracilior, commonly known as slender threeawn, is a grass species native to North America.

It is characterized by its slender, wiry stems and small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as prairies and disturbed soils. Its leaves are narrow and often covered with fine hairs.

It plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for small invertebrates.
