Astragalus gambelianus

Astragalus gambelianus, commonly known as the Gambel's milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow pea-like flowers. Astragalus gambelianus is also valued for its ecological importance, supporting various pollinators and wildlife.
