Arundinaria niitakayamensis

Arundinaria niitakayamensis, commonly known as the Niitakayamensis bamboo, is a rare and native species of bamboo found primarily in Japan.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its slender, clumping growth habit. The plant produces long, narrow leaves that are typically a bright green color, and its culms are hollow and segmented.
