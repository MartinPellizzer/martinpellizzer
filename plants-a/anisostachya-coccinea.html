Anisostachya coccinea

Anisostachya coccinea, commonly known as the red grass or red-stemmed grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking red-colored stems and leaves, which are often adorned with fine hairs. The plant produces panicles of small, delicate flowers that are typically pink or red in color. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Anisostachya coccinea is valued for its vibrant appearance and its ability to add color to landscapes throughout the growing season.
