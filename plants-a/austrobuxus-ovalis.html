Austrobuxus ovalis

Austrobuxus ovalis, commonly known as the oval buxus, is a shrub native to southern Australia.

It features dense, evergreen foliage with oval-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green color. The plant produces small, white flowers in spring, which are clustered in inflorescences. Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for use in hedges and topiary.

Austrobuxus ovalis is well adapted to dry conditions and thrives in well-drained soils.
