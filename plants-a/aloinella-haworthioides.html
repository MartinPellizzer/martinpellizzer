Aloinella haworthioides

Aloinella haworthioides, commonly known as the Haworthia-like Aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features dense, fleshy leaves that are typically bluish-green with white or yellow markings. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the spring. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Its compact growth habit and attractive foliage make it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation.
