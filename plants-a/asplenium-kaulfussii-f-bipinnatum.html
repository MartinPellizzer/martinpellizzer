Asplenium kaulfussii f. bipinnatum

Asplenium kaulfussii f. bipinnatum, commonly known as the bipinnate spleenwort, is a fern species characterized by its finely divided, bipinnate fronds.

It belongs to the family Aspleniaceae and is native to tropical and subtropical regions. The plant typically grows as a terrestrial or epiphytic fern, often found on rocks or tree trunks. Its leaves are dark green, glossy, and arranged in a compact rosette, with a delicate, feathery appearance.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in shaded garden environments.
