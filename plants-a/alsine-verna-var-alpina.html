Alsine verna var. alpina

Alsine verna var. alpina, commonly known as alpine sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to alpine regions of Europe and Asia.

It typically features small, white or pinkish flowers arranged in dense clusters, which bloom in early summer. The plant has a mat-forming growth habit, with numerous slender stems that root at the nodes. Its leaves are usually narrow and fleshy, adapted to retain moisture in arid, high-altitude environments.

Alsine verna var. alpina is often found in rocky soils and is a characteristic component of alpine vegetation.
