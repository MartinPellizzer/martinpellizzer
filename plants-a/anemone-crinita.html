Anemone crinita

Anemone crinita, commonly known as the curly top anemone, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It features showy, pink to purple flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has a short, hairy stem and palmately divided, lobed leaves. Its flowers are surrounded by feathery, white bracts that give it a distinctive appearance.

Anemone crinita thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodlands and along stream banks.
