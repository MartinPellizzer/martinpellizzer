Aglaonema philippinense var. stenophyllum

Aglaonema philippinense var. stenophyllum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf aglaonema, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the Philippines.

It features striking, dark green, lance-shaped leaves with a prominent central vein and a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are rarely seen in cultivation. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate watering to maintain its vibrant foliage.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and ornamental landscapes.
