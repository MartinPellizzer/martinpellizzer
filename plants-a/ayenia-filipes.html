Ayenia filipes

Ayenia filipes, commonly known as the silky ayenia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features slender, creeping stems with narrow, silvery-gray leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. Its flowers are small and yellow, arranged in clusters, and bloom in the spring.

Ayenia filipes is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive foliage and low maintenance requirements.
