Arundinaria fargesii

Arundinaria fargesii, commonly known as Farges bamboo, is a species of grass native to China.

It is characterized by its tall, slender culms that can reach heights of up to 6 meters. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are typically a deep green color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Arundinaria fargesii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is used in traditional Chinese horticulture.
