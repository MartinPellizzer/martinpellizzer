Anthemis biebersteiniana f. pectinata

Anthemis biebersteiniana f. pectinata, commonly known as the pectinate anthemis, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features finely pectinated (bristle-like) leaves, which give it its specific epithet. Its flowers are typically yellow with a central disk, typical of the genus Anthemis.

It thrives in cool, dry climates and is often found in alpine meadows.
