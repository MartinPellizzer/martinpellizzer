Areca gurita

Areca gurita, commonly known as the areca palm, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and feathery, compound leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences that eventually develop into fibrous fruits. Areca gurita is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.

Its adaptability to various soil types makes it a popular choice for landscaping in warm climates.
