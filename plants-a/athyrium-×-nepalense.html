Athyrium × nepalense

Athyrium × nepalense, commonly known as the hybrid fern, is a deciduous fern native to the Himalayas.

It belongs to the family Athyriaceae and is characterized by its finely divided, lance-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its spores are released from fertile sporangia located on the underside of the fronds.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate foliage.
