Anacamptis × eccarii

Anacamptis × eccarii, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a natural hybrid between Anacamptis morio and Anacamptis pyramidalis.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe, particularly in regions such as Italy and Spain. The plant produces tall, spike-like inflorescences with showy, violet to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are grass-like, arranged in a basal rosette, and the roots are fibrous.

This orchid is often found in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and meadows.
