Anthoshorea cordata

Anthoshorea cordata, commonly known as the heartleaf tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often smooth and glossy. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. Its fruit is a capsule that contains several seeds.

Anthoshorea cordata is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
