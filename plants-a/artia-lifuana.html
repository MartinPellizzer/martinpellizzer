Artia lifuana

Artia lifuana, commonly known as the flame vine, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, trailing stems and clusters of bright orange to red tubular flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is well-suited for hanging baskets or trellises. Its leaves are broad and heart-shaped, providing a lush green backdrop to its vibrant blooms.

Artia lifuana thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil and regular watering.
