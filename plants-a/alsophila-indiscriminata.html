Alsophila indiscriminata

Alsophila indiscriminata, commonly known as the slender tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnately compound fronds. The plant exhibits a slow growth rate and can reach heights of up to 10 meters in optimal conditions. Its rhizome system supports the development of its sturdy trunk and contributes to its longevity.

The fronds are typically bright green and provide the plant with a distinctive, elegant appearance.
