Aleuritopteris formosana

Aleuritopteris formosana, commonly known as the Formosan water fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, pinnate fronds that are often divided into numerous pinnae. The plant typically grows in wet, shaded environments such as forests and along stream banks. Its rhizomes are creeping and help in the spread of the plant.

The fronds are usually a deep green and can reach lengths of up to 2 meters.
