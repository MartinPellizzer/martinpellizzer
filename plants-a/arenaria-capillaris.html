Arenaria capillaris

Arenaria capillaris, commonly known as sand sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to arctic and alpine regions.

It features small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has slender, hairless stems and small, fleshy leaves that help it retain moisture in harsh environments. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in tundra and mountainous habitats.

Its botanical structure is adapted to withstand cold temperatures and strong winds.
