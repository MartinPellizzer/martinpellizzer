Athyrium irayense

Athyrium irayense, commonly known as the Madagascar fern, is a species of fern native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Dryopteridae family and is characterized by its delicate, lace-like fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest understories. Its rhizomes are creeping and produce a rosette of pinnate leaves.

The fronds are bi- or tri-pinnate, with a distinctive, elegant appearance that makes it a popular ornamental plant.
