Acetosella Beringensis

Acetosella beringensis is a small, hardy plant native to the cold climates of Alaska and Siberia. It thrives in nutrient-poor, acidic soils and is often found in tundra and boreal forest environments. This species is part of the rockcress family and is known for its delicate, flowering stems and small, bright green leaves. Despite its modest appearance, it plays an important role in its ecosystem by providing food for local wildlife. Its resilience in harsh conditions makes it a fascinating subject for botanical study.
