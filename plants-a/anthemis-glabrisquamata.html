Anthemis glabrisquamata

Anthemis glabrisquamata, commonly known as the smooth-scaled everlasting, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant produces small, yellow disk flowers surrounded by pale blue to white ray florets. It is known for its aromatic properties and is often used in herbal remedies.

The leaves are typically narrow and covered in a fine, powdery coating, contributing to its common name.
