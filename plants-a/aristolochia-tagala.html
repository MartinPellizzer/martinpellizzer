Aristolochia tagala

Aristolochia tagala, commonly known as the Philippine birthwort, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It is a woody vine characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and distinctive, intricate flowers that resemble a pipe or a crown. The plant belongs to the Aristolochiaceae family and is known for its complex, tubular flowers that attract specific pollinators.

Its roots and leaves have been traditionally used in herbal medicine, though the plant contains toxic compounds that require careful handling.
