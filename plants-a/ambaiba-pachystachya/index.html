Ambaiba Pachystachya

Mexican flame plant, scientifically known as Ambaiba pachystachya, is a strikingly vibrant member of the Amaranthaceae family native to the arid regions of Mexico. With its fiery red and orange blooms, it captivates gardeners and botanists alike with its intense color palette and unique foliage. This plant thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a hardy choice for desert gardens and xeriscaping projects. Its ability to flourish in harsh conditions highlights its resilience and adaptability. Despite its bold appearance, it remains relatively easy to care for, offering both aesthetic value and ecological benefits.
