Anemone canadensis

Anemone canadensis, commonly known as the Canadian anemone, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America.

It features a basal rosette of hairy, lobed leaves and produces showy, white to pale pink flowers with a central yellow stamen. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. It blooms from late spring to early summer, and its flowers attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.

Anemone canadensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in native plant gardens.
