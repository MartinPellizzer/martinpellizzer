Arachnites Alpinus

Arachnites alpinus is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to high-altitude regions of the Alps. It is known for its small, white petals that resemble spider webs, giving it its unique name. This plant thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in rocky crevices or mountain meadows. Due to its fragile nature, it is highly sensitive to climate change and habitat loss. Conservation efforts are crucial to ensure its survival in the wild.
