Asplenium glanduliserrulatum

Asplenium glanduliserrulatum, commonly known as the glandular-serrated spleenwort, is a member of the Aspleniaceae family and is characterized by its compact, evergreen appearance.

It typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often found on rock surfaces or in crevices. The plant features lanceolate leaves with serrated margins and small, glandular dots on the leaf surface, giving it its scientific name. Its reproductive structures, known as sori, are located on the underside of the fronds.

Asplenium glanduliserrulatum is a hardy, low-maintenance plant that is well-suited for indoor cultivation.
