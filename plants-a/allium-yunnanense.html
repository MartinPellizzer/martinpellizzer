Allium yunnanense

Allium yunnanense, commonly known as Yunnan garlic, is a species of flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its bulbous stem and clusters of white or pale purple flowers. The plant produces small, rounded bulbs that are often used in traditional Chinese medicine. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in mountainous regions.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for both horticultural and medicinal studies.
