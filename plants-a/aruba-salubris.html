Aruba salubris

Aruba salubris, commonly known as the Aruban health plant, is a flowering plant native to the island of Aruba.

It belongs to the family Acanthaceae and is characterized by its striking blue-violet flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture, and it thrives in well-drained sandy soils. It is often found growing along coastal areas and in dry, sunny habitats.
