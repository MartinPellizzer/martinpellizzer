Amomum jingxiense

Amomum jingxiense, commonly known as the Jingxi cardamom, is a member of the Zingiberaceae family, native to China.

It is characterized by its aromatic, fragrant leaves and distinctive, spadix-like inflorescence. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments within mountainous regions. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine for their purported digestive and anti-inflammatory properties.

The botanical features of A. jingxiense make it a unique and valuable species within the genus Amomum.
