Artabotrys velutinus var. sphaerocarpa

Artabotrys velutinus var. sphaerocarpa, commonly known as the velvet wax tree, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its smooth, velvety bark and clusters of fragrant, white flowers that bloom during the rainy season. The plant produces woody, spherical fruits that are covered in a thick, waxy coating. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and have a leathery texture with a distinctive aromatic scent.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
