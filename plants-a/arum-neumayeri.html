Arum neumayeri

Arum neumayeri, commonly known as the Neumayer's arrowleaf, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its unique inflorescence composed of a spadix and a spathe. The plant features broad, arrow-shaped leaves that are often a deep green with a glossy texture. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or white, surrounded by the showy spathe.

Arum neumayeri is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.
