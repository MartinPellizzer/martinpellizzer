Acranthera Virescens

Acranthera virescens is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America. It is known for its striking greenish-yellow blooms that appear during the wet season. This species belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is often found in humid, shaded forest understories. Due to habitat loss, it is considered endangered and requires conservation efforts to survive. Its unique appearance and ecological importance make it a subject of interest for botanists and nature enthusiasts alike.
