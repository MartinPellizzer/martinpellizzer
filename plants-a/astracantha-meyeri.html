Astracantha meyeri

Astracantha meyeri, commonly known as Meyer's thornveld, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, spiny foliage and compact growth habit, which helps it survive in arid environments. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in summer, adding a striking contrast to its rough texture. Its leaves are thick and fleshy, adapted for water storage, and it thrives in well-drained soil.

Astracantha meyeri is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and resilience.
