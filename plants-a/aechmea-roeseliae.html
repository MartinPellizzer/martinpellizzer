Aechmea roeseliae

Aechmea roeseliae, commonly known as the Roesel's bromeliad, is a striking epiphytic plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

The plant's foliage is typically dark green with a metallic sheen, and it produces vibrant, long-lasting inflorescences in shades of red, orange, or yellow. Aechmea roeseliae thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its unique structure and ornamental value make it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.

Morphology

Aechmea roeseliae has broad, strap-like leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette formation, with a waxy surface and a bluish-green color.
