Anthemis bornmuelleri var. galilaea

Anthemis bornmuelleri var. galilaea, commonly known as the Galilaea anthemis, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry habitats, showcasing its adaptability to arid environments. Its flowers are yellow with a central disk, and it produces small, hairy seeds.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its hardy nature.
