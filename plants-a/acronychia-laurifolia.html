Acronychia laurifolia

Acronychia laurifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaved acronychia, is a small evergreen tree native to eastern Australia.

It is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves that are arranged alternately along the stems. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by woody, spherical fruits. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in rainforest or coastal habitats.

Acronychia laurifolia is valued for its ornamental leaves and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
