Alternanthera paronychioides var. aberrans

Alternanthera paronychioides var. aberrans, commonly known as the red-veined dwarf alternanthera, is a striking aquatic plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, contrasting foliage, with dark green leaves featuring bright red veins. The plant thrives in shallow, nutrient-rich waters and prefers moderate to high light conditions. It is often used in freshwater aquariums for its aesthetic appeal and ability to provide habitat for small aquatic life.

Its rapid growth rate makes it suitable for foreground planting in planted tanks.
