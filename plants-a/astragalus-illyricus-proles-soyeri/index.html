Astragalus Illyricus Proles Soyeri

Soyer's milkvetch, scientifically known as Astragalus illyricus proles soyeri, is a rare and lesser-known legume native to the Balkan Peninsula. This plant is part of the Fabaceae family, which includes many nitrogen-fixing species that play a vital role in soil health. Despite its ecological importance, Soyer's milkvetch remains understudied and faces threats from habitat loss and overgrazing. Its unique morphology and limited distribution make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists. Understanding and protecting this plant is essential for preserving biodiversity in its native region.
