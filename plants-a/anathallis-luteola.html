Anathallis luteola

Anathallis luteola, commonly known as the yellow orchid, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, yellowish-green flowers that bloom during the summer months. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its inflorescence consists of a single flower stem bearing several small, fragrant blooms.

Anathallis luteola is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
