Aucuba japonica var. himalaica

Aucuba japonica var. himalaica, commonly known as the Himalayan aucuba, is a compact evergreen shrub native to the Himalayas.

It features glossy, dark green, oval-shaped leaves that are often adorned with yellow or white variegation. The plant produces small, red berries that are an important food source for birds. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is well-suited for woodland gardens.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping in cooler climates.
