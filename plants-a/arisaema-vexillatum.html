Arisaema vexillatum

Arisaema vexillatum, commonly known as the Chinese Cucumber Plant, is a herbaceous perennial native to East Asia.

It features a distinctive, arrow-shaped leaf that is often deeply lobed, giving it a unique appearance. The plant produces an ornamental, spadix-like inflorescence surrounded by a pale green spathe. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its aesthetic value in gardens.

The plant is also valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
