Ascolepis protea var. stellata

Ascolepis protea var. stellata, commonly known as the star protea, is a striking South African plant belonging to the Proteaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, showy inflorescence composed of numerous small, brightly colored flowers arranged in a star-like pattern. The plant is native to the Western Cape region and thrives in well-drained, acidic soils. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and floral arrangements.

The flowers are pollinated primarily by birds, particularly sunbirds, making it an important species for local biodiversity.
