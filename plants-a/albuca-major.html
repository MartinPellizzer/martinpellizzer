Albuca major

Albuca major, commonly known as the Natal lily, is a perennial bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features strap-like, grassy leaves and produces clusters of white or pale yellow flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers. It is drought-tolerant and requires minimal care once established.

Albuca major is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in garden landscapes for its elegant appearance.
