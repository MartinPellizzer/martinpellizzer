Adesmia lotoides f. petiolulata

Adesmia lotoides f. petiolulata, commonly known as the slender-stemmed adesmia, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, trifoliate leaves and small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments, showcasing adaptations to drought conditions. Its stems are slender and often exhibit a trailing or prostrate growth habit.

This variant is noted for its ornamental value and is occasionally cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
