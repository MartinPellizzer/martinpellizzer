Anandria dimorpha var. autumnalis

Anandria dimorpha var. autumnalis, commonly known as the autumn anandria, is a unique variant of the Anandria dimorpha species.

It is characterized by its striking seasonal color changes, with leaves transitioning from vibrant green to deep red or orange in the fall. This plant exhibits a compact growth habit with dense foliage, making it suitable for small garden spaces. Its flowers are typically small and clustered, producing a subtle fragrance that attracts pollinators.

The plant thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, showcasing its adaptability to various environmental conditions.
