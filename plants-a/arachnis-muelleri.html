Arachnis muelleri

Arachnis muelleri, commonly known as the Mueller's arachnis, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, pendulous flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its flowers are usually white or pale yellow, with a fragrant scent that attracts pollinators.

Arachnis muelleri is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
