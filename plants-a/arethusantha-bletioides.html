Arethusantha bletioides

Arethusantha bletioides, commonly known as the Bletia orchid, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its compact, evergreen growth habit. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, with intricate patterns on the petals. Arethusantha bletioides is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
