Anguria glomerata

Anguria glomerata, commonly known as the clustered cucumber, is a member of the Cucurbitaceae family.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and hairy stems. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are unisexual, with separate male and female flowers on the same plant. Its fruit is a small, oval-shaped cucumber with a smooth skin and a mild, slightly sweet flavor.

It is often cultivated for both ornamental and edible purposes in warm climates.
