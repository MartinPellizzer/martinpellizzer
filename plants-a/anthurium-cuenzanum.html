Anthurium cuenzanum

Anthurium cuenzanum, commonly known as the Cuenza anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves and vibrant, spadix-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect sunlight and well-draining soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical landscapes.
