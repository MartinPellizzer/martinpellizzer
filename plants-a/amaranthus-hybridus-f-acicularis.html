Amaranthus hybridus f. acicularis

Amaranthus hybridus f. acicularis, commonly known as the needleleaf amaranth, is a variant of the common amaranth species.

It is characterized by its slender, needle-like leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant is an annual herb native to the Americas and is often found in disturbed soils. It produces small, clustered flowers that are typically green or red in color.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated as a decorative plant.
