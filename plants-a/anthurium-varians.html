Anthurium varians

Anthurium varians, commonly known as the Red-veined Anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped leaves with striking red veins against a deep green background. The plant produces spadices, which are spathes that enclose the true flowers, typically in shades of yellow or white. Anthurium varians thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and ability to purify indoor air.
