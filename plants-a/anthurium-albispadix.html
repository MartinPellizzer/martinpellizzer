Anthurium albispadix

Anthurium albispadix, commonly known as the white-spotted anthurium, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with distinctive white or pale markings. The plant produces vibrant red spathes that enclose its small, spadix-like inflorescence, which is typical of the Araceae family. Anthurium albispadix thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its unique foliage and ornamental value make it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical landscapes.
