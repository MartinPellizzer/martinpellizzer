Achyranthes minutiflora

Achyranthes minutiflora, commonly known as small-flowered cowhage, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas.

It typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, featuring upright stems that are often branched and covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in dense clusters at the ends of the branches. Its leaves are alternate, ovate to lanceolate in shape, and have a slightly rough texture.

The fruit is a small, dry capsule that contains several elliptical seeds.
