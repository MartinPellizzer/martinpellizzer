Artemisia fukudo

Artemisia fukudo, commonly known as Japanese mugwort, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, silver-green foliage. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are typically clustered in dense, spherical heads. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or rocky areas.

Artemisia fukudo is also used in traditional medicine and as a culinary herb in some cultures.
