Agropyron lolioides

Agropyron lolioides, commonly known as the slender wheatgrass, is a perennial grass native to Eurasia.

It is characterized by its fine, tufted growth habit and bluish-green foliage. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in meadows and grasslands.

Agropyron lolioides is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in erosion control.
