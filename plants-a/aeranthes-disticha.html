Aeranthes disticha

Aeranthes disticha, commonly known as the slender spider-orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest understories. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Aeranthes disticha is known for its unique floral structure and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
