Argentina glabriuscula var. oligandra

Argentina glabriuscula var. oligandra, commonly known as the Argentine spider flower, is a member of the Asteraceae family.

It is a herbaceous perennial native to South America, particularly Argentina. The plant features showy, daisy-like flowers with yellow centers and white to pale pink petals. It has hairy stems and leaves that are typically lance-shaped.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
