Acrostichum chartaceum

Acrostichum chartaceum, commonly known as the paper mulberry.

It is a member of the Moraceae family and is native to tropical and subtropical regions of the world. The plant is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are smooth and often have a waxy texture. It produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color.

The plant is often used in traditional medicine and has various ecological roles in its native habitats.
