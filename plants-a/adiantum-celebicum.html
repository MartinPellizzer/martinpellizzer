Adiantum celebicum

Adiantum celebicum, commonly known as the Celebes maidenhair fern, is a delicate fern native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Adiantaceae and is characterized by its finely divided, feather-like fronds that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires consistently moist soil to flourish. Its intricate leaf structure and elegant appearance make it a popular choice for indoor gardening and ornamental purposes.

Adiantum celebicum is also valued for its ability to purify the air and enhance the aesthetic appeal of its surroundings.
