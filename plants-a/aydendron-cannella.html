Aydendron cannella

Aydendron cannella, commonly known as the cinnamon tree, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its aromatic, cinnamon-scented bark and dark green, leathery leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are followed by elongated, woody fruits. Its bark is rich in essential oils and has been used in traditional medicine for centuries.

Aydendron cannella thrives in tropical and subtropical climates with well-drained soil.
