Aloe burgersfortensis

Aloe burgersfortensis, commonly known as the Burgersfort aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a waxy coating. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow flowers, which bloom in spring. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil.

Its leaves are used in traditional medicine and for their gel-like substance, which has soothing properties.
