Aralia maralia

Aralia maralia, commonly known as American aralia, is a deciduous shrub native to the eastern United States.

It features compound leaves that are palmately arranged, with each leaflet being oval and serrated. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers in umbels, which are followed by bright red berries. It thrives in partial to full shade and is often found in moist, wooded areas.

Its ornamental value comes from its foliage and berries, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.
