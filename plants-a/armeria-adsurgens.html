Armeria adsurgens

Armeria adsurgens, commonly known as the upright thrift, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of southern California and Baja California.

Armeria adsurgens is well adapted to sandy soils and can thrive in full sun, making it a hardy plant suitable for coastal gardens.
