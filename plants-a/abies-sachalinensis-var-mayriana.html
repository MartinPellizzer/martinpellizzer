Abies sachalinensis var. mayriana

Abies sachalinensis var. mayriana, commonly known as the Mayrian fir, is a rare variant of the sachalin fir native to the Korean Peninsula.

It is characterized by its narrow, conical shape and dark green, needle-like leaves. The tree produces small, cylindrical cones that mature to a reddish-brown color. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species.
