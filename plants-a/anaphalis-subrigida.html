Anaphalis subrigida

Anaphalis subrigida, commonly known as the silverleaf goatweed, is a flowering plant native to regions of Asia, including China, India, and Nepal.

The plant features silver-gray foliage due to dense covering of fine hairs, which helps reduce water loss in arid environments. Anaphalis subrigida is often found in rocky or dry habitats and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
