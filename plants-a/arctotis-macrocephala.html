Arctotis macrocephala

Arctotis macrocephala, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its bright, showy flowers that range in color from yellow to red, often with a distinctive central boss. The plant typically grows as a herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are upright and sturdy.

Arctotis macrocephala thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
