Angraecum nanum

Angraecum nanum, commonly known as the miniature orchid or the Madagascar star orchid, is a species of orchid native to Madagascar.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers that are usually white or pale yellow, with a distinctive star-shaped appearance. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture from the air, and it thrives in tropical climates with high humidity.

Angraecum nanum is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
