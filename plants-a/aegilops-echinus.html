Aegilops echinus, commonly known as the spiny goatgrass, is a perennial grass species native to the Mediterranean region.

Aegilops echinus is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in dry, rocky environments.

It has been studied for its potential role in agricultural systems due to its adaptability and resilience.
