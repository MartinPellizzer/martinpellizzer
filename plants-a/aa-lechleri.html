Aa lechleri

Aa lechleri, commonly known as the rubber tree, is a tropical tree native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is renowned for producing latex, which is used in the manufacture of natural rubber. The plant has thick, fibrous bark that contains high concentrations of rubber particles. Its leaves are large, elliptical, and have a glossy appearance.

Aa lechleri plays an important ecological role in its native habitat and is also valued for its medicinal properties.
