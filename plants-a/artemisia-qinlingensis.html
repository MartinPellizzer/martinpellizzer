Artemisia qinlingensis

Artemisia qinlingensis, commonly known as Qinling wormwood, is a perennial herb native to the Qinling Mountains in China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, silver-gray foliage. The plant produces small, yellowish flower heads that are clustered in composite inflorescences. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in rocky or forested areas.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
