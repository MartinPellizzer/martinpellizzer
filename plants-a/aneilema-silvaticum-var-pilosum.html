Aneilema silvaticum var. pilosum

Aneilema silvaticum var. pilosum, commonly known as the silky aneilema, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silky hair, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

This variant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
