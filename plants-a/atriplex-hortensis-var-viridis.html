Atriplex hortensis var. viridis

Atriplex hortensis var. viridis, commonly known as green spinach, is a variant of the species Atriplex hortensis.

It is a hardy, leafy plant that thrives in well-drained soils and full sun. The leaves are typically oval-shaped, with a slightly serrated edge and a mild, spinach-like flavor. This variety is often cultivated for its nutritional value and use in salads or as a vegetable.

It is also valued for its ability to tolerate saline conditions, making it suitable for certain arid or coastal environments.
