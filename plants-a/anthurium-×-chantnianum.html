Anthurium × chantnianum

Anthurium × chantnianum, commonly known as the flamingo flower, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Araceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful spathes that can range from red to pink, often resembling a flamingo's beak. The plant produces small, green berries that are edible but not commonly consumed by humans. Native to tropical regions of South America, it thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Its striking appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and indoor plant collections.
