Acianthera unguicallosa

Acianthera unguicallosa, commonly known as the clawed acianthera, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its compact, terrestrial growth habit and features showy, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. The plant has a short, stout stem with few leaves, and its roots are often clustered at the base. The flowers are borne on a single, erect scape and are notable for their intricate, spurred petals.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
