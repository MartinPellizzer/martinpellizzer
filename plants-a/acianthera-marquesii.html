Acianthera marquesii

Acianthera marquesii, commonly known as the Marquesan orchid, is a species of orchid native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, with intricate patterns that attract specific pollinators.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
