Argythamnia heteropetalodes

Argythamnia heteropetalodes, commonly known as the desert star.

It is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of the Middle East and Central Asia. The plant features small, star-shaped yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are narrow and covered in fine hairs, helping to reduce water loss in harsh environments.

It is adapted to survive in sandy soils and can tolerate extreme temperature fluctuations.
