Aloe hereroensis var. lutea

Aloe hereroensis var. lutea, commonly known as the Herero yellow aloe, is a succulent plant native to Namibia and Angola.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically yellow to green in color, with a distinctive waxy coating. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing yellow to orange flowers. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its leaves to survive drought conditions.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
