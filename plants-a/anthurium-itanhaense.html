Anthurium itanhaense

Anthurium itanhaense, commonly known as the Itanhaense arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy surface. The plant produces striking spadices, which are inflorescences that resemble a cluster of small flowers. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.

Anthurium itanhaense is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental foliage.
