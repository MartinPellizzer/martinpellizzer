Aidomene

Aidomene, commonly known as the sacred lotus, is a striking aquatic plant native to the tropical regions of Africa and Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can range in color from white to pink and deep red. The plant thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments, often found in ponds and wetlands. Its leaves are broad and floating, providing shade and habitat for various aquatic organisms.

The lotus is also notable for its ability to purify water and support biodiversity in its ecosystem.
