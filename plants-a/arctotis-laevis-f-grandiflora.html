Arctotis laevis f. grandiflora

Arctotis laevis f. grandiflora, commonly known as the grandiflora arctotis, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features showy, daisy-like blooms with yellow centers and white to pale pink petals. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and long blooming period.
