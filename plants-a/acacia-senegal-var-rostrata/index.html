Acacia Senegal Var. Rostrata

Acacia senegal var. rostrata is a resilient shrub native to the Sahel region of Africa. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, making it a key species in desert ecosystems. Known for its unique pod structure and ability to fix nitrogen, it plays a vital role in soil enrichment. The plant is also valued for its gum arabic production, which has economic and medicinal significance. Its adaptability and ecological importance make it a subject of ongoing scientific and agricultural interest.
