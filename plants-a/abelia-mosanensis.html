Abelia mosanensis

Abelia mosanensis, commonly known as the Mosanensis abelia, is a flowering plant native to eastern China.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink, depending on the variety. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a smooth texture with a slightly serrated edge.

Abelia mosanensis thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained, acidic soil.
