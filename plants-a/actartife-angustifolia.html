Actartife angustifolia

Actartife angustifolia, commonly known as the narrowleaf actartife, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features slender, upright stems covered in fine hairs, and its leaves are elongated, narrow, and slightly serrated. The plant produces small, pale blue to purple flowers arranged in dense clusters, which bloom in the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value and resilience, it is sometimes cultivated in rock gardens and alpine settings.
