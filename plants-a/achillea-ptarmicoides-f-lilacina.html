Achillea ptarmicoides f. lilacina

Achillea ptarmicoides f. lilacina, commonly known as the lilac flowered yarrow, is a cultivar of the yarrow plant.

It is characterized by its striking lilac-pink flowers that bloom in flat, composite flower heads. The plant is a perennial herb with finely divided, gray-green leaves that form a basal rosette. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its botanical features make it both ornamental and useful in traditional herbal medicine.
