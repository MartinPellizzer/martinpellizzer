Aspidoglossum glabrescens

Aspidoglossum glabrescens, commonly known as the silver sword, is a striking succulent native to southern Africa.

It features a rosette of fleshy, silver-green leaves that are thick and waxy, adapted for water storage. The plant produces tall, branching stems that bear clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for xeriscaping and rock gardens.
