Aegilops biuncialis var. tenacissima

Aegilops biuncialis var. tenacissima, commonly known as the slender wild rye, is a grass species native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear minute, wind-pollinated flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open, disturbed habitats.

This variety is valued for its hardiness and potential use in ecological restoration projects.
