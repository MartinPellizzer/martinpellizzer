Anguloa × rolfei

Anguloa × rolfei, commonly known as the hybrid Anguloa, is a hybrid plant resulting from the cross between Anguloa loddigesii and Anguloa aurantiaca.

It is a member of the Orchidaceae family, characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers. The plant typically produces vibrant, colorful blooms in shades of red, orange, and yellow, which are highly prized in horticulture. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Anguloa × rolfei is often cultivated as a decorative plant for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for with proper conditions.
