Aloe archeri subsp. archeri

Aloe archeri subsp.

archeri, commonly known as the Cape aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa. It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, with white to yellowish tubercles along the edges.

The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing orange to red flowers. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine and for their soothing properties.
