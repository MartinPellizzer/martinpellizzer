Argyranthemum gracile

Argyranthemum gracile, commonly known as the slender marguerite, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial shrub that typically reaches a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that are often arranged in clusters, giving it a daisy-like appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its compact size and ornamental flowers make it a valuable addition to both garden landscapes and floral arrangements.
