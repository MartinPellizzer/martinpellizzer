Astragalus Bigelovii Var. Matthewsii

Astragalus bigelovii var. matthewsii is a rare and fascinating plant native to the southwestern united states. it belongs to the legume family and is known for its unique floral structure and medicinal properties. this variety is distinguished by its small, pale yellow flowers and hairy stems. it thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky soils. due to its limited distribution and ecological significance, it is considered a species of conservation concern.
