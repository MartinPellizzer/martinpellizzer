Armeria Plantaginea Subsp. Lacaitae

Armeria plantaginea subsp. lacaitae is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Andes. It is known for its compact growth habit and clusters of white or pale pink flowers that bloom in late spring. This subspecies thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in rocky or alpine habitats. Due to its limited distribution and fragile nature, it is considered a vulnerable species. Its unique beauty and ecological significance make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists alike.
