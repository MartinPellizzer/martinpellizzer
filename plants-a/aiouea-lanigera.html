Aiouea lanigera

Aiouea lanigera, commonly known as the silky aiouea, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree or shrub characterized by its dense, dark green foliage. The leaves are typically elliptical or ovate, with a smooth, waxy texture. It produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually white or pale yellow, followed by spherical, fleshy fruits.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
