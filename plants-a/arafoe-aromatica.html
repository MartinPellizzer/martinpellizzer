Arafoe aromatica

Arafoe aromatica, commonly known as the fragrant leaf plant, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, aromatic stems covered in small, lance-shaped leaves that emit a subtle, spicy scent. The plant produces tiny white flowers in early summer, which are clustered in umbels. Its leaves are often used in traditional herbal remedies for their calming properties.

Arafoe aromatica thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
