Asplenium polyodon var. bipinnatum

Asplenium polyodon var. bipinnatum, commonly known as the bipinnate maidenhair fern, is a variant of the common maidenhair fern.

It is characterized by its finely divided, bi-pinnate fronds that give it a delicate, lacy appearance. This fern thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas. Its fronds are typically dark green and have a soft, velvety texture.

The plant is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
