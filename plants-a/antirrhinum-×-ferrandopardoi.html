Antirrhinum × ferrandopardoi

Antirrhinum × ferrandopardoi, commonly known as the hybrid snapdragon, is a cross between two species of the genus Antirrhinum.

It is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that come in a wide range of colors, including red, orange, yellow, and pink. The plant typically grows to a height of 30–60 cm and produces flowers in clusters along the stems. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming period, which extends from late spring through early autumn.
