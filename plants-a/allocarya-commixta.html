Allocarya commixta

Allocarya commixta, commonly known as the mixed-flowered rock cress, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. The species is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in racemes. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in alpine or subalpine habitats.
