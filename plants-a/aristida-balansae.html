Aristida balansae

Aristida balansae, commonly known as the Balansa grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, three-lobed seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and open woodlands.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes used in restoration projects and as a forage crop.
