Allium xinlongense

Allium xinlongense, commonly known as the Xinlong onion, is a perennial bulbous plant native to China.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, round bulbs and grass-like leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is gaining popularity among horticulturists and gardeners.
