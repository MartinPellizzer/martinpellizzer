Alsodeia laxiflora

Alsodeia laxiflora, commonly known as the slender-flowered alsodeia, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of southern Africa.

It is a small, herbaceous annual that typically grows to a height of about 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to withstand drought conditions.

Alsodeia laxiflora is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and sandy soils.
