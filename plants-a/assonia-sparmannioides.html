Assonia sparmannioides

Assonia sparmannioides, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, daisy-like flowers with yellow centers and white petals. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. It has a compact, bushy growth habit with hairy leaves that help reduce water loss.

Assonia sparmannioides is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and floral arrangements.
