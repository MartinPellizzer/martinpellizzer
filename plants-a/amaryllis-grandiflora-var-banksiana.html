Amaryllis grandiflora var. banksiana

Amaryllis grandiflora var. banksiana, commonly known as the Banksian amaryllis, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems that bear large, showy flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant produces strap-shaped, evergreen leaves that are broad and glossy. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and containers.

Its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements make it a favored ornamental plant.
