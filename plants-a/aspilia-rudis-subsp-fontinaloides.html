Aspilia rudis subsp. fontinaloides

Aspilia rudis subsp.

fontinaloides, commonly known as the spring beauty, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia. It is characterized by its delicate, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale pink.
