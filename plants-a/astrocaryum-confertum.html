Astrocaryum confertum

Astrocaryum confertum, commonly known as the Brazilian buriti palm, is a tall, slender palm native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its large, fan-shaped leaves and produces clusters of yellowish-white flowers. The plant is notable for its large, edible fruits that contain a nutritious, starchy pulp. It thrives in wet, tropical conditions and is often found near water bodies.

Its ecological importance includes providing habitat and food for various wildlife species.
