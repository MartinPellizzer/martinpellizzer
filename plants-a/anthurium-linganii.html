Anthurium linganii

Anthurium linganii, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped, red spathes that enclose a spadix, which is often yellow or orange in color. The plant belongs to the Araceae family, known for its unique floral structures and ornamental value. Anthurium linganii thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its foliage consists of large, glossy, dark green leaves that add to its aesthetic appeal.
