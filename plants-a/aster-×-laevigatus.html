Aster × laevigatus

Aster × laevigatus, commonly known as the smooth aster, is a hybrid perennial plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that bloom in late summer to early fall. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet and produces blue to purple flowers with white centers. Its foliage is usually lance-shaped and covered in fine, soft hairs.

Aster × laevigatus thrives in full sun to partial shade and is often found in meadows and woodland edges.
