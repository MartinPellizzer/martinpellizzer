Ardisia foliosa

Ardisia foliosa, commonly known as the broadleaf ardisia, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are typically broad and ovate in shape. The plant produces small, white flowers followed by bright red berries, which are attractive to birds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in warm climates.
