Aphelandra nitens var. sinitzini

Aphelandra nitens var. sinitzini, commonly known as the Chinese flame tree, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, pendant-like inflorescences that bear clusters of bright orange-red bracts, which are often mistaken for flowers. The plant has dark green, glossy, heart-shaped leaves that provide a lush backdrop to its vibrant blooms. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

This species is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to add a dramatic visual impact to gardens and indoor spaces.
