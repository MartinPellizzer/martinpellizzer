Anabasis juncea

Anabasis juncea, commonly known as Russian thistle or goatweed, is an annual plant native to Eurasia.

It is characterized by its prickly, spiny stems and leaves, which help it to deter herbivores. The plant produces small, greenish flowers that are typically clustered in a globular shape. It thrives in dry, disturbed soils and is often found in agricultural fields and along roadsides.

Anabasis juncea is a common weed that can spread rapidly due to its prolific seed production.
