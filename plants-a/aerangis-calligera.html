Aerangis calligera

Aerangis calligera, commonly known as the Calligera orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its tall, slender stems and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically blooms in the summer months, producing clusters of white or pale pink flowers with a distinctive, intricate pattern. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
