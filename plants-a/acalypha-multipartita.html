Acalypha multipartita

Acalypha multipartita, commonly known as the Chinese nettle or false nettle, is a herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, palmately lobed leaves that often display a vibrant green or reddish hue. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Acalypha multipartita is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full sun.
