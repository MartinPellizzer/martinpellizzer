Anthemis rigida subsp. rigida

Anthemis rigida subsp.

rigida, commonly known as rigid anthemis, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe and parts of western Asia. It typically grows in dry, rocky, or sandy soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and coastal areas.

The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, yellow to orange composite flowers that bloom in summer. It is characterized by its rigid, upright stems and is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value.

The species is also known for its aromatic properties and has been used in traditional medicine.
