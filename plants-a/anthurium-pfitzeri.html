Anthurium pfitzeri

Anthurium pfitzeri, commonly known as the "Pfitzer's arrowhead," is a striking tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often brightly colored, ranging from deep green to variegated patterns. The plant produces striking spadices, which are specialized inflorescences that resemble a spadix, typical of the Araceae family. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.

Anthurium pfitzeri is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
