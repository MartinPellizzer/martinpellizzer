Avena subspicata var. agrostidea

Avena subspicata var. agrostidea, commonly known as the slender oat, is a grass species native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences with pale green to brown flowers. It thrives in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands.

Its botanical features make it a valuable component of ecological studies and habitat restoration efforts.
