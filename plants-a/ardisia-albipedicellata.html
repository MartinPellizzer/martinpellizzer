Ardisia albipedicellata

Ardisia albipedicellata, commonly known as the white-fruited beautyberry, is a species of flowering plant in the family Ericaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of small, white flowers. Ardisia albipedicellata prefers moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade, making it suitable for shaded garden environments.
