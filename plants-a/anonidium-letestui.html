Anonidium letestui

Anonidium letestui, commonly known as the African snowball tree, is a flowering plant native to the rainforests of Central and Eastern Africa.

It belongs to the family Annonaceae and is characterized by its large, showy white flowers that bloom in clusters, resembling snowballs. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. It produces a small, fleshy fruit that contains several seeds, and it is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The species is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
