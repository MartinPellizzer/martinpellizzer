Anthurium constrictum

Anthurium constrictum, commonly known as the "Red Velvet Anthurium," is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves with a deep red or maroon coloration on the underside. The plant produces spadices, which are the inflorescences consisting of a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe. Anthurium constrictum thrives in humid, warm environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and ornamental purposes.
