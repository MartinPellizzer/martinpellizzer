Areca baueri

Areca baueri, commonly known as the areca palm, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Arecaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that develop into edible fruits containing black seeds. Areca baueri is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.

Its adaptability to various climates makes it a popular choice for landscaping in tropical and subtropical regions.
