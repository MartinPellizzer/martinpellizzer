Andropogon nigritanus

Andropogon nigritanus, commonly known as black bluestem, is a tall grass native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its dark, almost black-colored stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. This plant typically grows in moist to wet soils, often found in wetlands and along riverbanks. It plays an important role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various wildlife species.

Andropogon nigritanus is also valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
