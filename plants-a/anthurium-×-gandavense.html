Anthurium × gandavense

Anthurium × gandavense, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Araceae family.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces vibrant red spadices, which are the modified leaves that enclose the small, inconspicuous flowers. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.

Anthurium × gandavense is widely cultivated as a decorative plant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
