Anemonoides × korzhinskyi

Anemonoides × korzhinskyi, commonly known as the hybrid anemone, is a perennial herbaceous plant characterized by its showy, cup-shaped flowers.

It typically produces white or pale yellow blooms that appear in early spring. The plant features a fibrous root system and upright, branching stems covered in soft, hairy foliage. Native to regions of Central Asia, it thrives in well-drained soils and partial to full sun.

Its botanical structure makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and woodland borders.
