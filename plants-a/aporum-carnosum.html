Aporum carnosum

Aporum carnosum, commonly known as the beefsteak plant, is a member of the Araliaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of the Americas, including parts of Brazil and the Caribbean. The plant is characterized by its large, fleshy, heart-shaped leaves that are typically deep green with a slightly waxy texture. It produces small, clustered white flowers followed by red, berry-like fruits.

Aporum carnosum is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking foliage and unique appearance.
