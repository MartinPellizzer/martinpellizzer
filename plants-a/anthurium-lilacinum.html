Anthurium lilacinum

Anthurium lilacinum, commonly known as the lilac anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy surface. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-like inflorescences that range in color from white to pink, often surrounded by a colorful spathe. It thrives in humid, warm environments and requires indirect sunlight for optimal growth.

Anthurium lilacinum is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and unique floral structures.
