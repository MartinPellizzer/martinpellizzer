Alocasia gageana

Alocasia gageana, commonly known as the Black Velvet Alocasia, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are dark green with a velvety texture. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, nutrient-rich soil. Its growth is slow, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Alocasia gageana is known for its ornamental value and is often appreciated for its unique foliage.
