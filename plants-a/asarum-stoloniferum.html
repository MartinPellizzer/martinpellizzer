Asarum stoloniferum

Asarum stoloniferum, commonly known as wild ginger, is a perennial herb native to the forests of eastern North America.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a fragrant, aromatic quality. The plant produces small, reddish-brown flowers that are typically hidden beneath its foliage. Its rhizomatous root system allows it to spread via stolons, forming dense colonies.

Asarum stoloniferum is valued for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional herbal remedies.
