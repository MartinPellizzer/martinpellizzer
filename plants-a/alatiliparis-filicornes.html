Alatiliparis filicornes

Alatiliparis filicornes, commonly known as the feather-fruited alatiliparis, is a unique plant species characterized by its distinctive botanical features.

It belongs to the family Alatiliparaceae and is native to specific regions in Southeast Asia. The plant is notable for its elongated, feather-like structures that adorn its inflorescences, giving it a striking appearance. Its leaves are typically narrow and lance-shaped, with a smooth surface and a waxy texture.

Alatiliparis filicornes thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.
