Arctotis anthemoides

Arctotis anthemoides, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that range in color from yellow to red. The plant typically grows as a shrub or subshrub, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are borne in clusters at the ends of the stems.

Arctotis anthemoides is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
