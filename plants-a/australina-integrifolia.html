Australina integrifolia

Australina integrifolia, commonly known as the smooth-leaved heath, is a shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its simple, entire leaves that are typically arranged in a dense, compact form. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are usually pink or white, blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in heathlands and coastal areas.

Australina integrifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting native wildlife.
