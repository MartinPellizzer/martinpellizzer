Anthonotha elongata

Anthonotha elongata, commonly known as the slender sandverbena, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its erect, branching stems and small, opposite leaves. The plant produces clusters of tiny, white to pale blue flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats.
