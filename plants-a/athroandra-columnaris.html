Athroandra columnaris

Athroandra columnaris, commonly known as the slender flame tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, fiery orange-red flowers that bloom in clusters, often in spring and early summer. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are elliptical in shape and glossy in appearance. Its bark is smooth and reddish-brown, and it produces small, round fruits that contain seeds.

Athroandra columnaris is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and parks due to its vibrant floral display.
