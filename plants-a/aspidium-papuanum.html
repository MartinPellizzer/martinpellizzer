Aspidium papuanum

Aspidium papuanum, commonly known as the Papuan water fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of New Guinea and nearby islands. The plant is characterized by its delicate, feather-like fronds that are typically green and glossy. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found near streams or in dense forest undergrowth.

Aspidium papuanum plays a role in its ecosystem by providing habitat and contributing to soil stability.
