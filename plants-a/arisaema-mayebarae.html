Arisaema mayebarae

Arisaema mayebarae, commonly known as the Mayebarae arrowhead, is a rare and unique member of the Araceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia, particularly in parts of Thailand and Vietnam. The plant is characterized by its striking, arrow-shaped leaves and a distinctive, spadix-like inflorescence. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native range.
