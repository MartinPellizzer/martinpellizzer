Aster smallii

Aster smallii, commonly known as the Sierra aster, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its showy blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer and early fall. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is often found in mountainous regions. It has hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Aster smallii is an important nectar source for pollinators and is valued for its ornamental beauty in gardens.
