Anthurium mapastepecense

Anthurium mapastepecense, commonly known as the Mapastepec anthurium, is a species native to the humid lowland forests of southern Mexico.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a glossy texture. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-bearing inflorescences that are often surrounded by a colorful spathe, which can range from red to orange. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in tropical gardens.
