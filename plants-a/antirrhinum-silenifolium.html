Antirrhinum silenifolium

Antirrhinum silenifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaf snapdragon, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

Antirrhinum silenifolium is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil types.
