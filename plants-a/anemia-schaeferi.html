Anemia schaeferi

Anemia schaeferi, commonly known as the Schaefer's anemone, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central America.

It is characterized by its showy, white to pink petals and a compact, bushy growth habit. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are basal, forming a rosette and are covered in fine, silky hairs.

Anemia schaeferi is often found in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.
