Agave verschaffeltii

Agave verschaffeltii, commonly known as the Blue Agave, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a succulent plant characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are typically blue-gray in color. The plant produces a tall flowering stalk that can reach several meters in height. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Its leaves are thick and fleshy, storing water to help it survive in dry conditions.
