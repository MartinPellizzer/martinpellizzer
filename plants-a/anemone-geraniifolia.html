Anemone geraniifolia

Anemone geraniifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved anemone, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the eastern United States.

It features delicate, white to pale pink flowers with five petals, typically blooming in early spring. The plant has finely divided, fern-like leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.

Anemone geraniifolia is often found in woodland habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
