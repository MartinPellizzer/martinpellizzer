Acianthera saraca-taquerensis

Acianthera saraca-taquerensis, commonly known as the Taquerensis orchid, is a species of orchid native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, delicate flowers with a distinctive coloration. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often found in cloud forests. Its leaves are linear and arranged in a rosette pattern, aiding in moisture retention.

The orchid's pollination is primarily facilitated by specific insect species, contributing to its ecological niche.
