Anthurium superbum subsp. brentberlinii

Anthurium superbum subsp.

brentberlinii, commonly known as the Brent Berlin Anthurium, is a member of the Araceae family and is native to the cloud forests of Costa Rica. It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are often a deep green with a glossy texture.

The plant produces inflorescences with brightly colored spathes, typically in shades of red or orange, which are accompanied by small, white spadices. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in tropical gardens.
