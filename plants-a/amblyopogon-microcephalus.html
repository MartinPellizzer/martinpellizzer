Amblyopogon microcephalus

Amblyopogon microcephalus, commonly known as the small-headed grass, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa and Asia.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its compact, low-growing habit. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue.
