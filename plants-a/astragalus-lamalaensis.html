Astragalus lamalaensis

Astragalus lamalaensis, commonly known as the Lamala milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod-bearing fruits. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments. It produces small, yellow to green flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer.
