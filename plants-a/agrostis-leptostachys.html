Agrostis leptostachys

Agrostis leptostachys, commonly known as slender spikegrass, is a species of grass native to various regions of Europe, Asia, and Africa.

It is a perennial plant characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows, grasslands, and along riverbanks.

Agrostis leptostachys is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in ecological restoration projects.
