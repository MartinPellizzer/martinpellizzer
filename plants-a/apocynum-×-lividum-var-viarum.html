Apocynum × lividum var. viarum

Apocynum × lividum var. viarum, commonly known as the red spiderling, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Apocynaceae family.

It is characterized by its distinctive red, spiny stems and clusters of small, white to pink flowers. The leaves are typically narrow and slightly hairy, and the plant produces follicles that contain toxic, milky sap. It is native to the southeastern United States and thrives in moist, well-drained soils.

This species is often found in woodland edges and disturbed areas, showcasing its adaptability to various environmental conditions.
