Arachnorchis winfieldii

Arachnorchis winfieldii, commonly known as the Winfield's spider orchid, is an epiphytic orchid native to the tropical regions of Australia.

It is characterized by its unique inflorescence, which consists of a single, slender spike bearing several small, spider-like flowers. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, using its aerial roots to absorb moisture and nutrients from the environment. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish in color, with intricate patterns that mimic the appearance of spiders.

This orchid is relatively rare and is often found in shaded, humid habitats within rainforests.
