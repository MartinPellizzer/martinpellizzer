Anthurium nangaritense

Anthurium nangaritense, commonly known as the Nangaritense arrowhead, is a striking tropical plant native to Ecuador.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its unique inflorescence consisting of a spadix and a spathe. The plant features heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a velvety texture and a prominent midrib. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, surrounded by a showy, colorful spathe.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.
