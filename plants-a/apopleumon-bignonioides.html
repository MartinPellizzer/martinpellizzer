Apopleumon bignonioides

Apopleumon bignonioides, commonly known as the yellow trumpet vine, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking yellow, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters during the warm season. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for warm climates.

Its rapid growth habit and ability to climb make it a popular choice for trellises and vertical gardening.
