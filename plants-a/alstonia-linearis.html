Alstonia linearis

Alstonia linearis, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a tropical tree native to Africa and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from yellow to red, often appearing in clusters. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are typically elliptical or linear in shape. Its bark is smooth and light-colored, and it can grow up to 20 meters tall.

Alstonia linearis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
