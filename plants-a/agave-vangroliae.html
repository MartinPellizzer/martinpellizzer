Agave vangroliae

It belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are thick and fleshy, adapted for water storage. Its leaves have a bluish-gray color with sharp, spiny margins, and it is well-suited to rocky and dry environments.

Agave vangroliae is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and drought tolerance.
