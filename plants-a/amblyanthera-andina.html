Amblyanthera andina

Amblyanthera andina, commonly known as the Andean blushing bell, is a flowering plant native to the Andes Mountains.

It is characterized by its striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in high-altitude regions with cool, moist conditions. Its leaves are usually elliptical and covered in a fine, soft hair.

Amblyanthera andina is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens in cooler climates.
