Aster muliensis

Aster muliensis, commonly known as the Muli aster, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Yunnan Province in China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or open habitats at high elevations, showcasing adaptability to harsh environments. Its flowers are usually blue to violet in color, with ray florets surrounding a central disk.

Aster muliensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also studied for its potential medicinal properties.
