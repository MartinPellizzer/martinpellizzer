Allium rotundum subsp. scaberrimum

Allium rotundum subsp.

scaberrimum, commonly known as the rough globe onion, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region. It features bulbous stems that produce clusters of small, spherical, pale pink to white flowers.

The leaves are narrow, grass-like, and often covered in a rough, scabrous texture, giving the subspecies its name. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and mild, onion-like scent.
