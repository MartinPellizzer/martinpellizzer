Aloe petrophila

Aloe petrophila, commonly known as the rock aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its spiky, fleshy leaves that store water, typical of arid environments. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow to orange flowers. It thrives in rocky soils and is adapted to dry, sunny conditions.

Aloe petrophila is often used in traditional medicine and is valued for its hardy nature.
