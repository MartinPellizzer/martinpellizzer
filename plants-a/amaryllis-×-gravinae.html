Amaryllis × gravinae

Amaryllis × gravinae, commonly known as the hybrid amaryllis, is a striking bulbous plant native to South America.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in a variety of colors, including red, orange, and yellow. The plant produces large, strap-like leaves that are typically broad and grass-like in appearance. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

This hybrid is popular for its long-lasting blooms and ease of cultivation in both indoor and outdoor settings.
