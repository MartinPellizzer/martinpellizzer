Ajuga shikotanensis

Ajuga shikotanensis, commonly known as Shikotan ajuga, is a perennial herb native to Japan.

It belongs to the plantain family (Plantaginaceae) and is characterized by its dense, low-growing mats of foliage. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple in color, which bloom in early summer. Its leaves are oval-shaped with a slightly hairy surface, and it thrives in moist, shaded environments.

Ajuga shikotanensis is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive foliage and flowering habits.
