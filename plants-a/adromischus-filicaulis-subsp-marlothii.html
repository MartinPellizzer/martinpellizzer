Adromischus filicaulis subsp. marlothii

Adromischus filicaulis subsp.

marlothii, commonly known as the Marloth's fiddleleaf, is a succulent plant native to South Africa. It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a waxy coating, adapted for water storage.

The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal watering, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
