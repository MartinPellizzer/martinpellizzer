Aira wibeliana

Aira wibeliana, commonly known as the Wibel's air grass, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Arctic and subarctic regions.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its dense, tufted growth habit. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, adapted to withstand harsh cold and strong winds.

Aira wibeliana plays a role in stabilizing soil in its native tundra environments.
