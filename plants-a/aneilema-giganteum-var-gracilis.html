Aneilema giganteum var. gracilis

Aneilema giganteum var. gracilis, commonly known as the slender giant aneilema, is a striking tropical plant native to eastern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and large, elongated leaves that can reach up to 30 cm in length. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom throughout the year, attracting various pollinators. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and requires partial to full shade for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
