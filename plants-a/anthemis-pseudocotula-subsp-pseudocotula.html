Anthemis pseudocotula subsp. pseudocotula

Anthemis pseudocotula subsp.

pseudocotula, commonly known as the narrowleaf tansy, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region. It is characterized by its aromatic, yellow flowers and linear, grass-like leaves.

The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers. It typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is often found in open habitats such as fields and roadsides.

Its medicinal properties have been historically used in traditional remedies.
