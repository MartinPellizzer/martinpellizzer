Agapanthus umbellatus variegatus

Agapanthus umbellatus variegatus, commonly known as the variegated agapanthus, is a striking evergreen perennial native to South Africa.

It features showy, umbel-shaped clusters of blue or purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has strap-like, dark green leaves with distinctive white or cream variegation. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and container planting.
