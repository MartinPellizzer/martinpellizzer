Aloe pseudonigricans

Aloe pseudonigricans, commonly known as the black aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in a branched inflorescence. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil.

Aloe pseudonigricans is often cultivated for its ornamental value and potential medicinal properties.
