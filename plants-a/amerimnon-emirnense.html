Amerimnon emirnense

Amerimnon emirnense, commonly known as the Emerald Star Fern, is a rare tropical plant native to the humid rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It features delicate, feather-like fronds that are typically a vibrant green, with some varieties displaying subtle variegation. The plant thrives in shaded, moist environments and requires high humidity and well-draining soil to flourish. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for botanical gardens and specialized indoor collections.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered endangered and is protected in many regions.
