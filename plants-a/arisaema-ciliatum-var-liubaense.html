Arisaema ciliatum var. liubaense

Arisaema ciliatum var. liubaense, commonly known as the Liubaense Chinese Arum, is a herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its unique inflorescence consisting of a spadix surrounded by a spathe. The plant features a rhizomatous root system and produces arrow-shaped leaves with ciliate margins. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value makes it a sought-after species in horticulture.
