Arachnorchis attingens

Arachnorchis attingens, commonly known as the spider orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to southeastern Australia.

It typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in open woodland or heathland environments. The plant features a slender, hairy stem with a single or few flowers that are usually pale green to yellow in color. Its unique flower structure includes a pouch-like labellum that resembles a spider, giving it its common name.

Arachnorchis attingens is a short-lived perennial, with its flowering period occurring in spring or early summer.
