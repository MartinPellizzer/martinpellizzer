Acrostichum latifolium var. firmum

Acrostichum latifolium var. firmum, commonly known as the broadleaf fern, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, broad, leathery leaves that can reach up to 2 meters in length. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or near water bodies. Its fronds are pinnately compound, with a distinctive, upright growth habit.

It thrives in humid climates and requires consistent moisture and indirect sunlight for optimal growth.
