Artemisia zayuensis

Artemisia zayuensis, commonly known as Yunnan wormwood, is a perennial shrub native to the mountainous regions of southwestern China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, silver-gray foliage. The plant produces small, yellowish flower heads that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in high-altitude environments with cool, moist conditions.

Due to its unique chemical composition, it has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
