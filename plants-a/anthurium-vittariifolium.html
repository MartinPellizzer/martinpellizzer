Anthurium vittariifolium

Anthurium vittariifolium, commonly known as the vittaria-leaf anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are often adorned with intricate patterns of veins. The plant produces small, spadix-like inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, typical of the Araceae family. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical plant collections.
