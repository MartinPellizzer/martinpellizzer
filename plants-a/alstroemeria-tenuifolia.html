Alstroemeria tenuifolia

Alstroemeria tenuifolia, commonly known as the slender-leaved alstroemeria, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the family Alstroemeriaceae and is characterized by its narrow, strap-like leaves and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments and prefers well-drained soil. Its flowers come in a range of colors, including red, orange, yellow, and pink, making it a popular ornamental plant.

Alstroemeria tenuifolia is often used in garden landscapes and floral arrangements due to its attractive appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.
