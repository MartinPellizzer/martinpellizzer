Atractylis yemensis

Atractylis yemensis, commonly known as Yemen dandelion, is a flowering plant native to the Arabian Peninsula.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and medicinal properties. The plant features rough, hairy leaves and produces yellow flowers that bloom in the summer. It is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid conditions.

Atractylis yemensis has been traditionally used in folk medicine for its purported health benefits.
