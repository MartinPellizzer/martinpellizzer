Aloe laetepunctata

Aloe laetepunctata, commonly known as the spiny aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are often covered in small spines, providing protection against herbivores. The plant produces yellow flowers in clusters, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, requiring minimal water and well-draining soil.

Aloe laetepunctata is valued for its resilience and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
