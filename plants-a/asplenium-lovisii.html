Asplenium lovisii

Asplenium lovisii, commonly known as the Lofgren's spleenwort, is a small, terrestrial fern belonging to the family Aspleniaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, strap-like leaves that are typically less than 10 centimeters in length. This species is often found growing on rocky surfaces or in shaded, moist environments, such as along streams or in forest undergrowth. The fronds are usually unbranched and have a distinctive, narrow shape with a serrated or toothed margin.

Asplenium lovisii is a low-growing plant that plays a minor but ecologically significant role in its native habitats.
