Arnica ovata

Arnica ovata, commonly known as the oval arnica, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the genus Arnica in the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features upright, hairy stems and basal leaves that are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are yellow with a central disc, typical of the sunflower family.

Arnica ovata is often found in alpine meadows and rocky habitats at high elevations.
