Adesmia minor var. riojana

Adesmia minor var. riojana, commonly known as the Rioja dwarf burro bean, is a low-growing shrub native to South America.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant typically features trifoliate leaves with small, rounded leaflets. It produces small, yellow flowers that are clustered in racemes.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its compact size and foliage.
