Aloe tegetiformis

Aloe tegetiformis, commonly known as the creeping aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, sword-shaped leaves that are typically green with a pale blue or yellowish tinge. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique growth habit and ornamental appearance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
