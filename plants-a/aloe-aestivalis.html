Aloe aestivalis

Aloe aestivalis, commonly known as the summer aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a waxy coating, which helps it retain moisture in arid environments. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the summer months, adding a striking contrast to its foliage. Its root system is shallow but extensive, allowing it to absorb water efficiently from the soil.

Aloe aestivalis is well adapted to dry climates and requires minimal water once established.
