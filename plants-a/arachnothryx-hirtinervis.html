Arachnothryx hirtinervis

Arachnothryx hirtinervis, commonly known as the hairy-veined arachnothryx, is a tropical shrub native to South America.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves with hairy veins. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in racemes, followed by legume-like pods containing seeds. Its hairy foliage and robust growth make it adaptable to various soil types.

It is often found in moist, shaded environments within tropical rainforests.
