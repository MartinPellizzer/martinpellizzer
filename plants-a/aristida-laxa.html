Aristida laxa

Aristida laxa, commonly known as slender dropseed, is a perennial grass native to North America.

It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as prairies, meadows, and disturbed soils. The plant has slender, grass-like leaves and produces small, fluffy seed heads that are characteristic of the grass family. It is often used in native plant gardens for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.

Aristida laxa is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils.
