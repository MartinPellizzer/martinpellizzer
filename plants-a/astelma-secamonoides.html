Astelma secamonoides

Astelma secamonoides, commonly known as the red spider lily, is a striking plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant red flowers that resemble spiders, hence its common name. The plant has strap-like, dark green leaves that are narrow and elongated. It thrives in warm, humid climates and prefers well-drained soil.

Astelma secamonoides is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique appearance.
