Abutilon viscosum

Abutilon viscosum, commonly known as velvetleaf, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in a dense, velvety pubescence. The plant produces clusters of small, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release numerous seeds.

Velvetleaf is often found in disturbed soils and can become invasive in some regions.
