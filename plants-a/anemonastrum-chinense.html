Anemonastrum chinense

Anemonastrum chinense, commonly known as Chinese sandmat, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial herb characterized by its fleshy, succulent stems and leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is adapted to withstand drought conditions.

Anemonastrum chinense is often used in traditional medicine and has ornamental value in dry garden settings.
