Aloe × desmetiana

Aloe × desmetiana, commonly known as the hybrid aloe, is a succulent plant that results from the cross between Aloe vera and Aloe arborescens.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a waxy coating, which helps it retain moisture in arid environments. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers in clusters during the warmer months, adding ornamental value. It is native to regions with dry climates and is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal and potential medicinal properties.

Aloe × desmetiana is relatively low-maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardening.
