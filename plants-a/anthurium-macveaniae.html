Anthurium macveaniae

Anthurium macveaniae, commonly known as the MacVeana's anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are often a deep green with a metallic sheen. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-like inflorescences surrounded by colorful spathe flowers, typically in shades of red or orange. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium macveaniae is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
