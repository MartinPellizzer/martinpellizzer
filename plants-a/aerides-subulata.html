Aerides subulata

Aerides subulata, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid with delicate, strap-like leaves and small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its flowers are usually pale green or white, with a slight pinkish hue.

Aerides subulata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to cultivate in suitable conditions.
