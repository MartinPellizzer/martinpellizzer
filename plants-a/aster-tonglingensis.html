Aster tonglingensis

Aster tonglingensis, commonly known as the Tongling aster, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically features showy purple or blue flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
