Aeschynomene javanica

Aeschynomene javanica, commonly known as the Java bluestem, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, blue or purple flowers. The plant has small, trifoliate leaves and produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds.

It is often found in disturbed soils and is used in some regions for forage or soil stabilization.
