Asterochaete tenuis

Asterochaete tenuis, commonly known as the slender hairgrass, is a grass species native to parts of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, slender blades and small, inconspicuous flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as wetlands and grassy areas. It has a fibrous root system that helps stabilize soil and prevent erosion.

Asterochaete tenuis is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its adaptability and resilience.
