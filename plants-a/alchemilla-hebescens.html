Alchemilla hebescens

Alchemilla hebescens, commonly known as the pale alchemilla, is a low-growing perennial plant native to Europe.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its basal rosette of narrow, silver-green leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Its foliage is covered in fine hairs, giving it a soft, velvety texture.
