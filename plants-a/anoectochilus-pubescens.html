Anoectochilus pubescens

Anoectochilus pubescens, commonly known as the hairy toothed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its hairy, often reddish stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers with a distinctive toothed lip. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value and rarity, it is sometimes cultivated in horticulture.
