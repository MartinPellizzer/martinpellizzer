Arabis stellulata

Arabis stellulata, commonly known as the starwort, is a flowering plant in the Brassicaceae family.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, typically found in rocky or sandy soils. The plant produces small, white or pink flowers arranged in clusters, giving it a star-like appearance. It has a low-growing habit with basal leaves that are often lobed or toothed.

Arabis stellulata is often used in rock gardens due to its hardiness and ornamental value.
