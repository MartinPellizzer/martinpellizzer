Ageratina brevipes

Ageratina brevipes, commonly known as the short-stemmed blueweed, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, blue or purple flowers that bloom in clusters. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands.
