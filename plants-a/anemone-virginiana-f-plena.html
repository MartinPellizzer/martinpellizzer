Anemone virginiana f. plena

Anemone virginiana f. plena, commonly known as the White Wood Anemone, is a herbaceous perennial native to eastern North America.

It features showy, double white petals that resemble small flowers, giving it a delicate and ornamental appearance. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded woodlands and thrives in rich, well-drained soil. Its leaves are palmately lobed and provide a lush green backdrop to its spring blooms.

It is valued in gardens for its early-season flowering and ecological benefits to pollinators.
