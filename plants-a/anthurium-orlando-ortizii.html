Anthurium orlando-ortizii

Anthurium orlando-ortizii, commonly known as the Orange-Ortiz Anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped spathe that ranges from yellow to orange in color, often with intricate patterns. The plant produces small, greenish-white spadices that serve as the actual flowers, surrounded by the showy spathe. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium orlando-ortizii is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in greenhouse settings.
