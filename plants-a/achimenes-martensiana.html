Achimenes martensiana

Achimenes martensiana, commonly known as the red-flowered achimenes, is a tropical perennial plant native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that range in color from red to pink and orange. The plant produces striking, velvety bracts that often contrast with the flower color. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Achimenes martensiana is often cultivated as a garden ornamental due to its vibrant blooms and compact growth habit.
