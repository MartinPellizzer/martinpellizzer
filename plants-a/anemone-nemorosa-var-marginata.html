Anemone nemorosa var. marginata

Anemone nemorosa var. marginata, commonly known as the Margined Wood Anemone, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It features showy, white to pale pink flowers with distinctive red or purple markings at the base of the petals. The plant has trifoliate leaves that are palmately arranged and covered in fine hairs. It typically grows in moist, shaded woodlands and thrives in partial to full shade.

The flowering period occurs in early spring, making it a notable early bloom in woodland gardens.
