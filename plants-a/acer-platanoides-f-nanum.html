Acer platanoides f. nanum

Acer platanoides f. nanum, commonly known as the dwarf maple, is a compact variant of the Norway maple.

It features small, rounded leaves that are typically lobed and have a serrated edge. The plant produces delicate, yellowish-green flowers in early spring, followed by red or orange samaras in the fall. It is well-suited for smaller gardens or containers due to its compact size.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various soil types.
