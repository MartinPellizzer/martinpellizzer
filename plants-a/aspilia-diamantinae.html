Aspilia diamantinae

Aspilia diamantinae, commonly known as the diamond daisy, is a striking flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that are surrounded by a ring of white or pale pink bracts, creating a diamond-like appearance. The plant typically grows in open grasslands and rocky areas, preferring well-drained soils. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Aspilia diamantinae is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant display.
