Assonia stipulacea

Assonia stipulacea, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves and showy, violet-colored flowers that bloom in spring. The plant prefers well-drained soil and bright, indirect light to thrive. Its foliage is thick and glossy, often with a waxy texture.

Assonia stipulacea is a popular ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and ease of care.
