Aechmea corymbosa

Aechmea corymbosa, commonly known as the pineapple bromeliad, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

Its leaves are typically narrow and covered in a waxy coating, which helps reduce water loss in its natural habitat.

Aechmea corymbosa is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant appearance and ability to thrive in indoor environments.
