Arabidella Procumbens

Prostrate arabidella, scientifically known as Arabidella procumbens, is a low-growing flowering plant native to the western United States. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in sandy or gravelly soils. With its compact, sprawling form and small, delicate flowers, it plays a vital role in its ecosystem. Despite its modest appearance, it is an important species for pollinators and soil stabilization. Its resilience and adaptability make it a fascinating subject for ecological study.
