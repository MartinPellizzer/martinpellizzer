Aloe patersonii

Aloe patersonii, commonly known as the Paterson's aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, sword-shaped leaves that are typically green with white or yellowish markings. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with clusters of yellow or orange flowers. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with plenty of sunlight.

Aloe patersonii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ease of care.
