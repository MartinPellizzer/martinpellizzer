Anthurium × albanense

Anthurium × albanense, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a hybrid plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped spathes that range in color from deep red to orange, often with a contrasting green or white spathe. The plant belongs to the Araceae family and is prized for its striking foliage and long-lasting blooms. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Anthurium × albanense is popular as a decorative plant due to its ornamental value and ease of care.
