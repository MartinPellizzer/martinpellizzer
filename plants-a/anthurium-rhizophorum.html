Anthurium rhizophorum

Anthurium rhizophorum, commonly known as the red-veined anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves with deep red veins against a dark green background. The plant produces vibrant red spadices, which are the modified leaves that house the tiny, inconspicuous flowers. Anthurium rhizophorum thrives in humid, shaded environments with high air circulation.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical landscapes.
