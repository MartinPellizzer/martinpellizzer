Ajania gracilis

Ajania gracilis, commonly known as slender ajania, is a herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in sunny to partially shaded environments. Its foliage is often narrow and grass-like, with small, clustered flowers that bloom in spring.

Ajania gracilis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
