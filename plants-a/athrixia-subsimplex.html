Athrixia subsimplex

Athrixia subsimplex, commonly known as the narrow-leaved sandmat, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Amaranthaceae and is characterized by its low-growing, sprawling habit. Athrixia subsimplex thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid regions.
