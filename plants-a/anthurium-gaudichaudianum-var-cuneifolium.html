Anthurium gaudichaudianum var. cuneifolium

Anthurium gaudichaudianum var. cuneifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaf anthurium, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its elongated, lance-shaped leaves that are often a deep green with a glossy texture. The plant produces striking spadices, which are inflorescences that resemble bright red or orange spikes. It thrives in humid, warm environments with bright, indirect light.

Its unique foliage and vibrant flower structures make it a popular choice for indoor gardening and ornamental purposes.
