Aristida purpurascens var. minor

Aristida purpurascens var. minor, commonly known as the purple grass or lesser purple grass, is a perennial grass species native to the western United States.

It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. The plant features slender, tufted blades with a distinctive purple tinge along the midribs. Its inflorescence consists of dense, spike-like clusters of small, pale green to reddish flowers.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in native plant gardens.
