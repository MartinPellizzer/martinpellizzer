Agave langlassei

Agave langlassei, commonly known as the Langlasse's agave, is a species native to the arid regions of Mexico.

It is a succulent plant characterized by its rosette-shaped growth pattern and thick, fleshy leaves that store water. The leaves are typically blue-green in color with white marginal teeth, giving it a striking appearance. It produces a tall floral stalk that bears yellowish-green flowers, which bloom in the spring.

This plant is well adapted to dry environments and requires minimal water once established.
