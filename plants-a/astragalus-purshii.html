Astragalus purshii

Astragalus purshii, commonly known as Pursh's milkvetch, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to North America, particularly found in the western United States and parts of Canada. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats such as grasslands and sagebrush steppe. It features compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces small, yellow to greenish flowers in racemes.

Astragalus purshii is known for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, contributing to soil fertility and supporting ecological balance.
