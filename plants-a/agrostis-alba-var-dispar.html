Agrostis alba var. dispar

Agrostis alba var. dispar, commonly known as the slender meadowgrass, is a variant of the common bentgrass.

It is a perennial grass species native to temperate regions of Europe and Asia. This plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows, roadsides, and pastures. It has slender, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences.

Its botanical characteristics make it adaptable to a variety of soil types and environmental conditions.
