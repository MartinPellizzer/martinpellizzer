Alstroemeria umbrosa

Alstroemeria umbrosa, commonly known as the shadow alstroemeria, is a perennial flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped, dark green leaves and clusters of showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant thrives in partially shaded to shady environments, making it well-suited for woodland gardens. Its stems are often reddish-brown and slightly hairy, adding to its ornamental appeal.

Alstroemeria umbrosa is valued for its long-lasting blooms and adaptability to various soil types.
