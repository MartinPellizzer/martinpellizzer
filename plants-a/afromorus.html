Afromorus

Afromorus, commonly known as the African flame flower, is a striking plant native to parts of Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant red and orange flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as bees and birds. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 6 meters. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and often have a rough texture.

Afromorus thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens in tropical and subtropical regions.
