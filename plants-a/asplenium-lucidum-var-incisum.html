Asplenium lucidum var. incisum

Asplenium lucidum var. incisum, commonly known as the glossy spleenwort, is a fern species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Aspleniaceae and is characterized by its delicate, evergreen foliage. The leaves are typically lanceolate, with a glossy, dark green surface and serrated margins. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often growing on rocks or tree trunks.

This variety is distinguished by its finely incised leaflets, which contribute to its ornamental appeal.
