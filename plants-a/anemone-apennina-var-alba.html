Anemone apennina var. alba

Anemone apennina var. alba, commonly known as the white Italian anemone, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe.

It features showy, white, single or double flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has finely divided, fern-like leaves that are basal and often covered in a silvery down. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens and its role in supporting pollinators.
