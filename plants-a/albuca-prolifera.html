Albuca prolifera

Albuca prolifera, commonly known as the African lily, is a bulbous plant native to southern Africa.

It produces clusters of narrow, grass-like leaves and bears clusters of white or pale yellow flowers. The plant is adapted to arid environments and prefers well-drained soil. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ease of care.

Albuca prolifera is also known for its ability to propagate through offsets, making it a popular choice for gardeners.
