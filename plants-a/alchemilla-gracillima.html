Alchemilla gracillima

Alchemilla gracillima, commonly known as slender lady's mantle, is a low-growing perennial plant native to Europe and western Asia.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its compact, mat-forming growth habit. The plant features delicate, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of silky hairs. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers in early summer, which are clustered in tight, upright inflorescences.

Alchemilla gracillima is well adapted to moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas or along stream banks.
