Afzelia scabra

Afzelia scabra, commonly known as African shea or African mahogany, is a tropical tree native to Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its hard, durable wood. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces yellowish flowers. Afzelia scabra is often found in moist, fertile soils in lowland rainforests.

It is also valued for its seeds, which are used in traditional medicine and as a source of oil.
