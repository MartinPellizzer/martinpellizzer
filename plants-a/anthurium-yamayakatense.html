Anthurium yamayakatense

Anthurium yamayakatense, commonly known as the Yamayakatense Arrowhead, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive inflorescence consisting of a spadix and a spathe. The plant features broad, arrow-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. It thrives in humid, warm environments with bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Anthurium yamayakatense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.
