Ameroglossum intermedium

Ameroglossum intermedium, commonly known as the intermediate nightshade, is a herbaceous plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other nightshades like tomatoes and potatoes. The plant features showy, bell-shaped flowers that are typically purple or violet in color. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a rough texture.

The fruit is a small, fleshy berry that contains several seeds.
