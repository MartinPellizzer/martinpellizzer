Aneilema florentii

Aneilema florentii, commonly known as the African violet, is a tropical plant native to Central and Eastern Africa.

It is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves and clusters of showy, violet-colored flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters and prefers bright, indirect light. It thrives in humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Aneilema florentii is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
