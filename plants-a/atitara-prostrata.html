Atitara prostrata

Atitara prostrata, commonly known as the prostrate atitara, is a low-growing shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are typically covered in a dense layer of fine hairs. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in terminal inflorescences. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in humid forests.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens.
