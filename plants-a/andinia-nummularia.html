Andinia nummularia

Andinia nummularia, commonly known as the silver cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, rounded leaves that are typically gray-green in color, covered in a fine layer of white wax. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with plenty of sunlight.

Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
