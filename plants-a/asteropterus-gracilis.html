Asteropterus gracilis

Asteropterus gracilis, commonly known as the slender fern, is a small, delicate fern native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. The plant features finely divided, pinnately compound leaves that give it a feathery appearance. Its rhizomes are often covered in a dense, woolly coating, which helps retain moisture.

Asteropterus gracilis is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and ability to thrive in low-light conditions.
