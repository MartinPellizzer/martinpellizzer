Anthurium prominor

Anthurium prominor, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces vibrant red spathes that enclose its spadix, which is the actual flowering structure. Anthurium prominor thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for indoor gardening and ornamental purposes.
