Amblogyna urceolata var. obcordata

Amblogyna urceolata var. obcordata, commonly known as the heartleaf amblogyna, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and a distinctive, fragrant aroma. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale pink, typically blooming during the warmer months. Its botanical structure includes a fibrous root system and stems that often spread horizontally along the ground.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in shaded garden settings.
