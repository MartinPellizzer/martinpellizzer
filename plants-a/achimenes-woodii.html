Achimenes woodii

Achimenes woodii, commonly known as the Wood's achimenes, is a tropical perennial plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, pink, and white. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces tubular flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Achimenes woodii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant blooms and compact growth habit.
