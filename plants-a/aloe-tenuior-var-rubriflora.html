Aloe tenuior var. rubriflora

Aloe tenuior var. rubriflora, commonly known as the red-flowered aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features slender, upright stems with fleshy, paddle-shaped leaves that are typically pale green with a bluish tinge. The plant produces striking red-orange flowers in terminal racemes, which bloom during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
