Apicra × skinneri

Apicra × skinneri, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a naturally occurring cross between Apicra and Skinnera species.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense foliage, which often features strikingly colored leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in a range of hues, typically in shades of pink, purple, and white. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Due to its ornamental value, it is a popular choice for collectors and gardeners interested in rare orchids.
