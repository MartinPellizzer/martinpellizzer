Agrostis × castriferrei

Agrostis × castriferrei, commonly known as the hybrid bentgrass, is a perennial grass species that results from the cross between Agrostis capillaris and Agrostis stolonifera.

It is characterized by its fine, tufted growth habit and its ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions. The plant typically forms dense mats and is often found in moist, temperate environments. Its foliage is narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green coloration.

Agrostis × castriferrei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping and turfgrass applications.
