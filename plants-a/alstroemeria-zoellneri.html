Alstroemeria zoellneri

Alstroemeria zoellneri, commonly known as the Zoellner's alstroemeria, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Chile and Argentina.

It belongs to the family Alstroemeriaceae and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, orange, and yellow. The plant typically grows as a herbaceous perennial, with strap-like leaves and a compact growth habit.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
