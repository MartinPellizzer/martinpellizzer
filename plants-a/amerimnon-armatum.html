Amerimnon armatum

Amerimnon armatum, commonly known as the rock rose, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of fragrant, pink or white flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has woody stems and small, leathery leaves that are adapted to dry conditions. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Amerimnon armatum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
