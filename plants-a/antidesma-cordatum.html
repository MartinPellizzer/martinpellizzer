Antidesma cordatum

Antidesma cordatum, commonly known as the heartleaf antidesma, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to parts of Africa and Southeast Asia.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for horticultural cultivation in tropical regions.
