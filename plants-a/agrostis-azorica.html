Agrostis azorica

Agrostis azorica, commonly known as Azorean bentgrass, is a low-growing perennial grass native to the Azores.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in coastal areas and mountainous regions. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It is adapted to the humid and temperate climate of its native habitat.

Agrostis azorica is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in stabilizing soil in its natural environment.
