Armeria maritima subvar. monchiquensis

Armeria maritima subvar. monchiquensis, commonly known as the Canary Islands thrift, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Canary Islands.

It features dense clusters of white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and is adapted to coastal environments.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in harsh, maritime conditions.
