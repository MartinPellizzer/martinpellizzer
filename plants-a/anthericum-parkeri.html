Anthericum parkeri

Anthericum parkeri, commonly known as the African lily, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and large, showy white or pale blue flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that form a dense rosette at the base. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.

Anthericum parkeri is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
