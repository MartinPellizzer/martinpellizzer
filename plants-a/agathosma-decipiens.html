Agathosma decipiens

Agathosma decipiens, commonly known as buchu, is a small evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic leaves that emit a strong, spicy scent. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and has a compact, bushy growth habit. Its leaves are narrow, leathery, and often have a bluish-green hue.

Agathosma decipiens is valued for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally for its antispasmodic and diuretic effects.
