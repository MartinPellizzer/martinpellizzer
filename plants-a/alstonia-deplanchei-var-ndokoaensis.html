Alstonia deplanchei var. ndokoaensis

Alstonia deplanchei var. ndokoaensis, commonly known as the Ndokoa alstonia, is a deciduous tree native to the tropical rainforests of Central Africa.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation programs.
