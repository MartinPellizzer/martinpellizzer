Agrostis leonii

Agrostis leonii, commonly known as golden tuft grass, is a perennial grass species native to Europe, Asia, and Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, tufted growth habit and bright golden-yellow inflorescences. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in temperate climates. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green coloration.

Agrostis leonii is often found in meadows, pastures, and along stream banks, where it plays a role in soil stabilization and habitat provision.
