Agathosma formosissima

Agathosma formosissima, commonly known as sweet fennel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, finely divided leaves and yellowish flowers. The plant is often grown for its culinary and medicinal uses, particularly in traditional African medicine. It prefers well-drained soils and full sunlight, making it suitable for cultivation in Mediterranean climates.

Its essential oils are valued for their antimicrobial and anti-inflammatory properties.
