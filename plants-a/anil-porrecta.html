Anil porrecta

Anil porrecta, commonly known as the slender cowpea, is a climbing legume native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features compound leaves with three to five oval-shaped leaflets and produces small, yellowish-white flowers. The plant bears cylindrical, hairy pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in warm climates and is often cultivated for its edible seeds.

Anil porrecta is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, benefiting surrounding plants.
