Aster brickellioides

Aster brickellioides, commonly known as the brickell's aster, is a herbaceous perennial native to the southeastern United States.

It features showy, daisy-like flowers with purple to blue petals and a yellow center, blooming in late summer to early fall. The plant has alternate, lance-shaped leaves that are usually hairy, providing a textured appearance. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained, sandy soils.

Aster brickellioides is often used in native plant gardens for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
