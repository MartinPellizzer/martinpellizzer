Asplenium costale var. desvauxii

Asplenium costale var. desvauxii, commonly known as the Desvaux's spleenwort, is a fern species belonging to the Aspleniaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant prefers moist, shaded environments and is often found growing on rock surfaces or in crevices. Its leaves are usually dark green and have a leathery texture, which helps it retain moisture.

This variety is notable for its compact growth habit and is sometimes cultivated for its ornamental appeal.
