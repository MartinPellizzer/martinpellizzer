Aster rubicundus

Aster rubicundus, commonly known as the redstem aster, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its bright yellow to orange flowers that bloom in the fall. The plant typically grows in open habitats such as meadows and woodlands. It has slender, hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves.

Aster rubicundus is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits to pollinators.
