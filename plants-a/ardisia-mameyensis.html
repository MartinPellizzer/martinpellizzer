Ardisia mameyensis

Ardisia mameyensis, commonly known as the mamey sapote, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Central America.

Ardisia mameyensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and its fruit, which is used in both culinary and traditional medicinal applications.
