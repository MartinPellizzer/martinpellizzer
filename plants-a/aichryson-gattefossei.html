Aichryson gattefossei

Aichryson gattefossei, commonly known as the Desert Rose, is a succulent plant native to the Canary Islands.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with ample sunlight.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
