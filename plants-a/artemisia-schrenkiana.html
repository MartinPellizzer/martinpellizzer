Artemisia schrenkiana

Artemisia schrenkiana, commonly known as the Schrenk's wormwood, is a perennial shrub native to Central Asia.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage, which consists of aromatic, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flower heads that are clustered in dense, spherical clusters. It thrives in dry, well-drained soils and is often found in steppe and semi-desert environments.

Artemisia schrenkiana is valued for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
