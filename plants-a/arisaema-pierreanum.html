Arisaema pierreanum

Arisaema pierreanum, commonly known as the Pierre's arrowhead, is a striking member of the Araceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of China, where it thrives in moist, shaded environments. The plant features a distinctive arrowhead-shaped leaf and produces an umbrella-like inflorescence with a spadix and a spathe. Its botanical characteristics include a corm-like tuber that serves as its storage organ.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage and floral display.
