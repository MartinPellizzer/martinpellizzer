Arundo vulnerans

Arundo vulnerans, commonly known as the sensitive reed, is a tall, perennial grass native to southern Africa.

It features slender, hollow stems that are often used in traditional crafts and as a source of fiber. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are sensitive to touch, hence its common name. It thrives in moist environments and is often found near water sources.

Its ornamental value and adaptability make it a popular choice for landscaping in warm climates.
