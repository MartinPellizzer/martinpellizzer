Asterothrix

Asterothrix, commonly known as the star thorn, is a genus of flowering plants belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its spiny stems and small, daisy-like flowers that typically bloom in the summer months. The plant is native to regions of Africa and is often found in arid or semi-arid environments. Asterothrix species are known for their hardy nature and ability to thrive in poor soil conditions.

The leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, while the flowers come in shades of yellow and white.
