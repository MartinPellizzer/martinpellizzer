Asarum speciosum

Asarum speciosum, commonly known as the showy asarum, is a perennial herb native to the eastern United States.

It features heart-shaped, reddish-brown leaves with a distinctive mottled pattern. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers in early spring, which are clustered in a spathe-like structure. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland habitats.

Asarum speciosum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting pollinators.
