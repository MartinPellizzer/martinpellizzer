Anaxagorea rufa

Anaxagorea rufa, commonly known as the red-leafed evergreen, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green to reddish-brown leaves that change color with age. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers followed by woody capsules containing numerous seeds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forests.

Anaxagorea rufa is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in local cultures.
