Amellus scabridus

Amellus scabridus, commonly known as the rough amellus, is a flowering plant native to specific regions of South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Amellus scabridus is often found in grassy areas and rocky soils, where it thrives in full sun.
