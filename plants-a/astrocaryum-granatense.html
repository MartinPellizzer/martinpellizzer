Astrocaryum granatense

Astrocaryum granatense, commonly known as the red acuri palm, is a tall, slender palm native to the Amazon rainforest of South America.

It is characterized by its elongated, slender trunk and large, pinnate leaves that can reach several meters in length. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish flowers followed by bright red, fleshy fruits that are edible and have a sweet, tropical flavor. Its fruit is an important food source for various wildlife species.

The palm is also valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated for its unique appearance.
