Aloe elegans

Aloe elegans, commonly known as the slender aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features narrow, upright leaves that are typically blue-green in color, covered in a waxy cuticle to reduce water loss. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers on a tall stem, which bloom during the summer months. Aloe elegans is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal watering.

Its leaves contain a gel-like substance that is sometimes used for its potential medicinal properties.
