Anemone nemorosa var. semiplena

Anemone nemorosa var. semiplena, commonly known as the wood anemone, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features delicate, white or pale pink flowers with a distinctive, fluffy center, characteristic of its semiplena variety. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded woodlands and has palmately divided, trifoliate leaves. It blooms in early spring, often before the canopy of trees fully develops.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its graceful appearance.
