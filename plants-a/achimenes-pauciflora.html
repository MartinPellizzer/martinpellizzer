Achimenes pauciflora

Achimenes pauciflora, commonly known as the sparse-flowered achimenes, is a herbaceous perennial native to tropical regions of South America.

It features striking tubular flowers that range in color from red to pink, often with contrasting white or yellow markings. The plant has heart-shaped, glossy leaves and produces showy blooms throughout the growing season. It thrives in warm, humid conditions and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking vibrant, tropical flair.
