Athyrium rotundifolium

Athyrium rotundifolium, commonly known as the round-leaved Japanese shield fern, is a species of fern native to East Asia.

It features elegant, shield-shaped fronds with a distinctive rounded leaf blade. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream sides. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine in some cultures.

The plant's foliage is characterized by a smooth, glossy texture and a deep green color.
