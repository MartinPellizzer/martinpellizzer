Artanthe flexuosa

Artanthe flexuosa, commonly known as the silverleaf artanthe, is a herbaceous perennial native to the western United States.

It features basal leaves that are silvery-white with a fuzzy texture, contrasting with its upright, flowering stems. The plant produces small, pinkish to purple flowers in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its ornamental value lies in its striking foliage and delicate floral display.
