Actinostemon concolor var. gardneri

Actinostemon concolor var. gardneri, commonly known as the Gardner's concolor.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to specific regions of Mexico. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered flowers in shades of pink to purple. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Its botanical interest lies in its unique floral structure and adaptability to arid environments.
