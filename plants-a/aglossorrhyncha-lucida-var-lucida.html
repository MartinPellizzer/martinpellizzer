Aglossorrhyncha lucida var. lucida

Aglossorrhyncha lucida var. lucida, commonly known as the shining leafless orchid, is a rare and specialized orchid species native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its lack of leaves, with photosynthesis occurring through its modified stems. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are typically white or pale green, often clustered in loose inflorescences. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in forest understories.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species.
