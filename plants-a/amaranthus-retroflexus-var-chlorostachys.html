Amaranthus retroflexus var. chlorostachys

Amaranthus retroflexus var. chlorostachys, commonly known as the green-stemmed amaranth, is a species of flowering plant in the amaranth family.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and lance-shaped leaves that are typically green to reddish in color. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from green to red, depending on the stage of bloom. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in agricultural fields and along roadsides.

This variant is notable for its ornamental value and potential use in herbal medicine.
