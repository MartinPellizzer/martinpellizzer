Alyssum montanum var. atlanticum

Alyssum montanum var. atlanticum, commonly known as Atlantic alyssum, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of the southeastern United States.

It is a low-growing, mat-forming perennial with small, fragrant white or pale yellow flowers. The plant features silvery-gray foliage and is well adapted to sandy, well-drained soils. It thrives in full sun and is often found in dune environments and coastal grasslands.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscaping.
