Alsophila chnoodes

Alsophila chnoodes, commonly known as the narrow-leaf tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and feathery fronds that give it a distinctive, elegant appearance. The plant produces spores on the undersides of its fronds, which are essential for its reproductive cycle. Alsophila chnoodes thrives in humid, tropical environments with well-drained soil.

It is often used in ornamental horticulture due to its aesthetic value and ability to thrive in shaded conditions.
