Anthurium validinervium

Anthurium validinervium, commonly known as the red-veined anthurium, is a tropical flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves with bright red veins against a deep green background. The plant produces vibrant orange to red spadices, which are the modified leaves that enclose the actual flowers. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.

Anthurium validinervium is popular as a ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
