Aa brevis

Aa brevis, commonly known as the short-leafed fern, is a species of fern characterized by its compact, upright growth habit.

It features small, lance-shaped fronds that are typically less than 30 centimeters in length. The plant's rhizomes are short and thick, contributing to its ability to thrive in moist, shaded environments. Aa brevis is native to tropical and subtropical regions, where it often grows in dense, humid forests.

Its delicate, feathery appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in suitable climates.
