Acacia producta

Acacia producta, commonly known as the slender acacia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small, shrub-like species that typically grows in dry, sandy soils. The plant produces yellow flowers in clusters, which are often followed by long, narrow pods. Its leaves are bipinnate, consisting of numerous small leaflets.

Acacia producta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
