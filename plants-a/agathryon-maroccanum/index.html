Agathryon Maroccanum

Agathryon maroccanum is a rare and striking plant native to the arid regions of Morocco. It belongs to the family Acanthaceae and is known for its unique, spiky foliage and vibrant flower clusters. This plant thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and requires minimal water, making it well-suited for dry climates. Its ornamental value has made it a sought-after specimen among collectors and gardeners. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively obscure in the broader horticultural community.
