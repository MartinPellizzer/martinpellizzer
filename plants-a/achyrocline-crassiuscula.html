Achyrocline crassiuscula

Achyrocline crassiuscula, commonly known as the narrowleaf sandwort, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean islands.

It is a small, herbaceous shrub that typically grows in coastal areas and sandy soils. The plant produces small, white to pale pink flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the dry season. Its leaves are narrow and fleshy, adapted to conserve water in arid environments.

Achyrocline crassiuscula is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens in tropical regions.
