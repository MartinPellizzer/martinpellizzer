Atriplex patula var. gunnii

Atriplex patula var. gunnii, commonly known as Gunn's saltbush, is a shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves that are adapted to arid environments. The plant produces small, greenish flowers in clusters, which are typically wind-pollinated. It thrives in saline soils and is often found in coastal and inland regions with poor soil conditions.

This variety is valued for its ecological role in stabilizing soils and providing habitat for native wildlife.
