Allium splendens subsp. splendens

Allium splendens subsp.

splendens, commonly known as the purple onion, is a bulbous plant native to the Mediterranean region. It features striking violet to purple flowers that bloom in summer, adding ornamental value to gardens.

The plant has a robust root system and produces small, edible bulbs that are used in culinary applications. Its leaves are hollow and cylindrical, typical of the Allium genus.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a hardy and versatile garden plant.
