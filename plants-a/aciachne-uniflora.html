Aciachne uniflora

Aciachne uniflora, commonly known as the single-flower grass, is a species of grass native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, erect growth habit and produces a single, spike-like inflorescence at the top of each stem. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue.

Aciachne uniflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in native plant gardens.
