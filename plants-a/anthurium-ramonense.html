Anthurium ramonense

Anthurium ramonense, commonly known as the Ramonense arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to Central America.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive spathe and spadix inflorescence. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy green leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Anthurium ramonense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated as a houseplant.
