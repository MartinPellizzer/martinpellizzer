Anthospermum prostratum var. glabrum

Anthospermum prostratum var. glabrum, commonly known as the smooth anthospermum, is a low-growing, herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, arranged in dense inflorescences. The leaves are usually elliptical or ovate, with a smooth surface, contributing to its varietal name "glabrum.". It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The plant has a fibrous root system and is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential medicinal properties.
