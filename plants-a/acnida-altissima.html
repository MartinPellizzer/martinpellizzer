Acnida altissima

Acnida altissima, commonly known as the tall acacia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small shrub or tree that typically grows up to 3 meters in height. The plant is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in savanna and woodland habitats.

Acnida altissima is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
