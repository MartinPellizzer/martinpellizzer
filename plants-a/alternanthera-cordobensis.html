Alternanthera cordobensis

Alternanthera cordobensis, commonly known as the red-stemmed alternanthera, is a striking aquatic plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red stems and lance-shaped, bright green leaves that often have a reddish tinge. This plant thrives in tropical and subtropical freshwater environments, preferring moderate to high light conditions. Its foliage can vary in color depending on water parameters and light exposure, making it a popular choice for aquariums.

It is often used in the background of planted tanks due to its height and visual appeal.
