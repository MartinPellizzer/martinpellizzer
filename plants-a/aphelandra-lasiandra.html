Aphelandra lasiandra

Aphelandra lasiandra, commonly known as the golden torch plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the Caribbean and Central America.

It is characterized by its striking, pendulous inflorescences that resemble torches, with bright yellow bracts and small white flowers. The plant has lance-shaped, dark green leaves with a glossy texture and emits a mild fragrance. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

Aphelandra lasiandra is often cultivated for its ornamental value in both indoor and outdoor gardens.
