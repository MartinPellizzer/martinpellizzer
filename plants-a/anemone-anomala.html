Anemone anomala

Anemone anomala, commonly known as the lesser crown imperial, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, often with a distinctive purple or red base. The plant has a fibrous root system and basal leaves that are typically lobed or divided. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Anemone anomala is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
