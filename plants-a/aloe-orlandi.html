Aloe orlandi

Aloe orlandi, commonly known as the Cape aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are fleshy and covered in a white powdery coating. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with bright red or orange flowers. Aloe orlandi is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil.

Its leaves are used in traditional medicine and for their soothing properties.
