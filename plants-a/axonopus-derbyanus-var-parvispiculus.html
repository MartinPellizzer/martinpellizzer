Axonopus derbyanus var. parvispiculus

Axonopus derbyanus var. parvispiculus, commonly known as the narrow-leaved axonopus, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features slender, grass-like leaves with a fine texture and a compact growth habit. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spikelets, typical of the Poaceae family. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments.

Its botanical characteristics make it a useful plant in ecological restoration and ornamental landscaping.
