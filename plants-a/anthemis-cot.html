Anthemis cot

Anthemis cot, commonly known as common tansy, is a flowering plant in the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and aromatic foliage. The plant typically grows in moist, sunny environments and is native to Europe and western Asia. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their antiseptic properties.

The plant's flowers are visited by a variety of pollinators, including bees and butterflies.
