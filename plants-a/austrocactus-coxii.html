Austrocactus coxii

Austrocactus coxii, commonly known as the Cox's cactus, is a species native to Argentina.

It is a small, columnar cactus with a distinctive appearance, featuring a green, ribbed stem covered in white to yellow spines. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months, adding a striking visual element to its form. Austrocactus coxii is adapted to arid environments and requires well-draining soil and minimal water.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique appearance.
