Aspalathus mollis

Aspalathus mollis, commonly known as sweet clover, is a small, evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It features small, delicate leaves that are typically arranged in pairs along the stems. The plant produces fragrant, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in clusters, adding ornamental value to gardens. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its attractive appearance and pleasant scent, it is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
