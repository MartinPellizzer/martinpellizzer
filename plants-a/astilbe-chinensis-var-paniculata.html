Astilbe chinensis var. paniculata

Astilbe chinensis var. paniculata, commonly known as Chinese astilbe, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to East Asia.

It features upright, branched stems that bear dense clusters of small, fluffy flowers in shades of white, pink, or red. The foliage is lush and fern-like, providing ornamental value throughout the growing season. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens.

This variety is valued for its attractive blooms and ability to add texture and color to shaded landscapes.
