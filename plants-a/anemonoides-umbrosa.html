Anemonoides umbrosa

Anemonoides umbrosa, commonly known as the shadow anemone, is a flowering plant native to the temperate regions of the Pacific Northwest.

It is characterized by its delicate, pale yellow to white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are heart-shaped and have a soft, velvety texture.

Anemonoides umbrosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting local pollinators.
