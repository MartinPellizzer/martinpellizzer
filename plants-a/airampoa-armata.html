Airampoa armata

Airampoa armata, commonly known as the slender grass.

It is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered inflorescences. It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.

Airampoa armata is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in stabilizing soil in humid climates.
