Agathis ovata

Agathis ovata, commonly known as the narrow-leafed ironwood, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Araucariaceae and is characterized by its tall, straight trunk and dense, dark green foliage. Its wood is highly valued for its durability and strength, making it important in traditional craftsmanship and construction.

Agathis ovata also plays a significant role in local ecosystems, providing habitat and food for various wildlife species.
