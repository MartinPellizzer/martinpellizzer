Aglaonema novoguineense

Aglaonema novoguineense, commonly known as the Guinean evergreen.

This plant is a member of the Araceae family and is native to tropical regions of New Guinea. It is characterized by its striking, variegated foliage with shades of white, green, and pink. The leaves are typically broad and lance-shaped, with a glossy texture.

It thrives in humid, shady environments and is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant.
