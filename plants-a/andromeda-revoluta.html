Andromeda revoluta

Andromeda revoluta, commonly known as the snowflake plant, is a flowering shrub native to the Pacific Northwest of North America.

It features dense clusters of white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has dark green, glossy leaves and a compact, bushy growth habit. It thrives in moist, acidic soils and partial to full shade.

Andromeda revoluta is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to stabilize soil in wetland environments.
