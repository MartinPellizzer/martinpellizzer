Agrostis Vulgaris Var. Hornungiana

Agrostis vulgaris var. hornungiana is a lesser-known grass species that thrives in specific ecological niches. It is commonly found in moist, shaded environments such as woodland floors and stream banks. This variant is distinguished by its unique growth habits and subtle morphological features. Despite its modest appearance, it plays an important role in local ecosystems. Its study offers insights into plant adaptation and biodiversity in temperate regions.
