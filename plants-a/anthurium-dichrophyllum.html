Anthurium dichrophyllum

Anthurium dichrophyllum, commonly known as the red and green anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green on the upper surface and a lighter green or sometimes reddish on the underside. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-shaped inflorescences surrounded by colorful spatheae, which are often red or orange. It thrives in humid, warm environments with bright, indirect light.

Anthurium dichrophyllum is a popular ornamental plant due to its unique foliage and long-lasting flowers.
