Astilbe × amabilis

Astilbe × amabilis, commonly known as the Japanese feather reed, is a hybrid ornamental plant belonging to the Saxifragaceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, plume-like inflorescences that range in color from white to pink and red, depending on the cultivar. The plant typically grows to a height of 30 to 60 cm, with attractive, heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated. It thrives in partial shade and moist, well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens.

Astilbe × amabilis is valued for its ornamental foliage and delicate flowers, which bloom in late spring to early summer.
