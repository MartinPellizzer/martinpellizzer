Asplenium niamniamense

Asplenium niamniamense, commonly known as the Niamniam fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Aspleniaceae and is characterized by its compact, clustered growth habit. The plant features dark green, leathery fronds that are often divided into narrow, lance-shaped segments. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Asplenium niamniamense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant.
