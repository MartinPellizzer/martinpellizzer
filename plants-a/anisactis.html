Anisactis

Anisactis, commonly known as the sea holly, is a genus of flowering plants belonging to the family Asteraceae.

Anisactis plants are adapted to saline environments, featuring salt-excreting structures on their leaves.

Their reproductive structures are composite, consisting of both ray and disk flowers, which facilitate wind and insect pollination.
