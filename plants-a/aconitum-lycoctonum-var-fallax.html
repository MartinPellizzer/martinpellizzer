Aconitum lycoctonum var. fallax

Aconitum lycoctonum var. fallax, commonly known as the autumn aconite, is a herbaceous perennial native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It produces striking blue to purple flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. The plant has dark green, heart-shaped leaves that are deeply lobed and appear early in the growing season. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Due to its toxic alkaloids, it should be handled with care and kept away from children and pets.
