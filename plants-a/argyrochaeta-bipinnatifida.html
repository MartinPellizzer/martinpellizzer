Argyrochaeta bipinnatifida

Argyrochaeta bipinnatifida, commonly known as the silverleaf pincushion.

This plant is a member of the Asteraceae family and is native to South Africa. It is characterized by its silvery, woolly foliage and branched, cylindrical flower heads. The leaves are pinnatifid, meaning they are deeply divided into narrow segments, giving the plant a distinctive appearance.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
