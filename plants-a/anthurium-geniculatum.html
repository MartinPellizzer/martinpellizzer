Anthurium geniculatum

Anthurium geniculatum, commonly known as the curlleaf anthurium, is a tropical flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are often dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces vibrant red spathes that enclose its yellow spadix, creating a dramatic floral display. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.

Anthurium geniculatum is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
