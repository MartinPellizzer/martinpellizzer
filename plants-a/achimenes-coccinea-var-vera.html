Achimenes coccinea var. vera

Achimenes coccinea var. vera, commonly known as the red-flowered achimenes, is a tropical perennial plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular red flowers that bloom in summer, attracting pollinators such as hummingbirds and bees. The plant has heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are dark green and often have a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking vibrant, exotic blooms.
