Alsophila batesii

Alsophila batesii, commonly known as the bamboo fern, is a species of tree fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and a crown of feathery, compound fronds. The plant has a robust root system that helps it thrive in moist, shaded environments. Its fronds are typically dark green and can grow quite large, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Alsophila batesii is often cultivated for its aesthetic value in gardens and as a decorative plant.
