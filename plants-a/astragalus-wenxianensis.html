Astragalus wenxianensis

Astragalus wenxianensis, commonly known as the Wenxian milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in arid and semi-arid environments, making it a hardy species. It plays a role in soil stabilization and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.

Its ecological significance is notable in its native habitats.
