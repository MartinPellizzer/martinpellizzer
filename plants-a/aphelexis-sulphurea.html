Aphelexis sulphurea

Aphelexis sulphurea, commonly known as the sulphur leaf shrub, is a perennial shrub native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its yellow-green foliage and small, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant has a woody stem with a rough, sometimes hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, rocky habitats.

Aphelexis sulphurea is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
