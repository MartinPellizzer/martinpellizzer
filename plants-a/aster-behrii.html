Aster behrii

Aster behrii, commonly known as the Behri's aster, is a flowering plant native to the western United States and northern Mexico.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in dry, sunny environments. Its flowers are usually blue to purple in color, with ray florets surrounding a central disk of tubular flowers.

Aster behrii is often found in chaparral and scrub habitats, where it contributes to local biodiversity.
