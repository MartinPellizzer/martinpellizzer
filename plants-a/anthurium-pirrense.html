Anthurium pirrense

Anthurium pirrense, commonly known as the red-tipped anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Colombia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves with a deep red margin. The plant produces a spadix inflorescence surrounded by a colorful spathe, which is typical of the genus Anthurium. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Anthurium pirrense is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
