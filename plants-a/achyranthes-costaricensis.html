Achyranthes costaricensis

Achyranthes costaricensis, commonly known as the Costa Rican achyranthes, is a herbaceous plant native to Central America.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and lanceolate leaves. The plant produces small, greenish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its roots are often used in traditional medicine for their purported anti-inflammatory properties.

Achyranthes costaricensis thrives in moist, tropical environments and is often found in shaded undergrowth.
