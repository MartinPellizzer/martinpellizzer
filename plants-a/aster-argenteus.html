Aster argenteus

Aster argenteus, commonly known as the silver aster, is a flowering plant native to eastern North America.

It is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that range in color from white to pale blue. The plant has hairy, gray-green foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. It typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and woodlands.

Aster argenteus is a perennial that blooms in the summer months and attracts pollinators like bees and butterflies.
