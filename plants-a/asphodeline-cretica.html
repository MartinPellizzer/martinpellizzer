Asphodeline cretica

Asphodeline cretica, commonly known as the rock asphodel, is a perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and spikes of bright yellow flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas. It is drought-tolerant and has a compact growth habit.

Its botanical features make it an ornamental and hardy choice for xeriscaping.
