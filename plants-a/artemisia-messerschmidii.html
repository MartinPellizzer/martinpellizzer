Artemisia messerschmidii

Artemisia messerschmidii, commonly known as the alpine wormwood, is a flowering plant in the sunflower family.

It is native to the high elevations of the European Alps and is adapted to cold, dry environments. The plant has aromatic, silver-gray foliage with narrow, lance-shaped leaves. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in summer.

Artemisia messerschmidii is often used in traditional medicine and is valued for its resilience in harsh climates.
