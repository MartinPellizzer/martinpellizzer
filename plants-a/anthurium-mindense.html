Anthurium mindense

Anthurium mindense, commonly known as the Mindanao anthurium, is a striking tropical plant native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped leaves that can range from dark green to deep red. The plant produces clusters of spadices, which are modified leaves that enclose the true flowers. Anthurium mindense thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor and greenhouse cultivation.
