Anvillea platycarpa

Anvillea platycarpa, commonly known as the flat-fruited anvillea, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and thrives in full sun. Its fruit is a dry, elongated capsule that contains numerous small seeds.

Anvillea platycarpa is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens due to its vibrant blooms.
