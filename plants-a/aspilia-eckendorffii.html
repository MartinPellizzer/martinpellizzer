Aspilia eckendorffii

Aspilia eckendorffii, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, orange-red inflorescences that resemble flames, which bloom in late summer. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family and is known for its showy, daisy-like flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in savannas and open woodlands.

Aspilia eckendorffii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
