Asplenium × dolosum var. uginense

Asplenium × dolosum var. uginense, commonly known as the Ugin's shield fern, is a hybrid species within the Aspleniaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen foliage with dark green, leathery fronds that are typically shield-shaped. The plant produces small, spore-bearing sorus clusters on the undersides of its leaves, typical of ferns. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and horticultural collections.
