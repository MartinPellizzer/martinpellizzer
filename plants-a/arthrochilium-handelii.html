Arthrochilium handelii

Arthrochilium handelii, commonly known as the Cape orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, upright stems and lanceolate leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers with a distinctive lip that is often darker in color. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.

Arthrochilium handelii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to cultivate under suitable conditions.
