Aloe × heteracantha

Aloe × heteracantha, commonly known as the hybrid aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is a hybrid between Aloe vera and Aloe arborescens, resulting in a plant with unique characteristics. The leaves are thick, fleshy, and often have a serrated edge, with a pale green to bluish-green coloration. It produces yellow flowers in clusters during the summer months, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies.

This plant is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it suitable for xeriscaping and ornamental gardening.
