Angraecum inflatum

Angraecum inflatum, commonly known as the giant star orchid, is a striking epiphytic orchid native to Madagascar.

It features long, arching stems that can grow up to several meters in length, supporting clusters of large, star-shaped flowers. The flowers are typically white or pale pink, with a distinctive central column and elongated petals. This species thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil and consistent moisture.

Angraecum inflatum is noted for its slow growth and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
