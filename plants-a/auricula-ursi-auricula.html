Auricula-ursi auricula

Auricula-ursi auricula, commonly known as the bear's ear, is a low-growing alpine plant with distinctive, hairy, kidney-shaped leaves that resemble bear ears.

It belongs to the family Ericaceae and is native to the mountainous regions of Europe and Asia. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically pink or purple, blooming in late summer. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in alpine meadows and forest edges.
