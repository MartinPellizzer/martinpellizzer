Armeria alliacea var. sicorisiensis

Armeria alliacea var. sicorisiensis, commonly known as Sicilian thrift, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features dense clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has succulent, fleshy leaves that help it retain moisture in arid conditions. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its hardy nature and ornamental value make it a popular choice for landscaping in Mediterranean climates.
