Agoseris Glauca Var. Dasycephala

Agoseris glauca var. dasycephala is a lesser-known member of the sunflower family native to the western United States. This variety is distinguished by its dense, woolly foliage and striking blue-green flowers that bloom in late spring. It thrives in dry, rocky habitats and is often found in mountainous regions. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively obscure in horticultural circles. Its unique appearance and ecological role make it a fascinating subject for botanical study.
