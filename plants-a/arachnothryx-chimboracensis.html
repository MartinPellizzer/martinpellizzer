Arachnothryx chimboracensis

Arachnothryx chimboracensis, commonly known as the Andean spider tree, is a species of flowering plant native to the Andean regions of South America.

The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences, followed by pod-like fruits containing several seeds.
