Arnica sancti-laurentii

Arnica sancti-laurentii, commonly known as the Saint-Laurent's arnica, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to the alpine regions of Central Europe, including parts of Switzerland and Austria. The plant produces yellow-orange flowers with a distinctive central disk, typical of the genus Arnica. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils at high altitudes.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for ecological studies in mountainous environments.
