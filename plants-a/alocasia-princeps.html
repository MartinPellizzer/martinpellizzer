Alocasia princeps

Alocasia princeps, commonly known as the Chinese velvetleaf, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a dense, velvety texture. The plant thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light. Its foliage displays deep green to purple hues, depending on light exposure and care.

Alocasia princeps is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
