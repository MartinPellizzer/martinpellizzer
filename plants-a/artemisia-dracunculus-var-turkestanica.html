Artemisia dracunculus var. turkestanica

Artemisia dracunculus var. turkestanica, commonly known as Turkestan wormwood, is a perennial herb native to Central Asia.

It is characterized by its aromatic, silver-green foliage and small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant produces a strong, distinctive scent due to its high concentration of volatile oils. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in mountainous regions.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional uses in herbal medicine.
