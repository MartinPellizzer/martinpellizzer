Alyssum aizoides

Alyssum aizoides, commonly known as the lesser alyssum, is a low-growing perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale purple, blooming in early spring. The plant has hairy, linear leaves that are arranged in opposite pairs along the stems. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Alyssum aizoides is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
