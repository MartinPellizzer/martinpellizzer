Adenosma uliginosa

Adenosma uliginosa, commonly known as the swamp adenosma, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It typically grows in moist or wet environments such as marshes and swampy areas. The plant has slender, often hairy stems and small, clustered leaves. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, with five petals and a prominent central gland.

Adenosma uliginosa is often found in wetland ecosystems, contributing to the biodiversity of such habitats.
