Astracantha meraca

Astracantha meraca, commonly known as the Cape rose.

It is a flowering plant native to South Africa, characterized by its distinctive rose-like flowers and spiny stems. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family and is known for its upright growth habit. Its leaves are typically narrow and hairy, while its flowers are usually pink or purple in color.

Astracantha meraca is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
