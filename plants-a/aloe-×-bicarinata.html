Aloe × bicarinata

Aloe × bicarinata, commonly known as the hybrid aloe, is a succulent plant native to the Cape Region of South Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a waxy coating. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow to orange flowers, which bloom in late summer. Aloe × bicarinata is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively drought-tolerant.

It prefers well-draining soil and full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
