Aneilema gardneri var. glabrior

Aneilema gardneri var. glabrior, commonly known as the smooth aneilema, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and large, lance-shaped leaves that are typically smooth and glabrous. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are usually pale green to yellow in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments.

This variant is notable for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its foliage and subtle floral display.
