Astragalus kokandensis

Astragalus kokandensis, commonly known as the Kokand kummerowia, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its yellow flowers and compound leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its seeds are enclosed in a pod, a common feature among legumes, and it plays a role in soil stabilization in its native habitat.

Astragalus kokandensis is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphology and ecological significance.
