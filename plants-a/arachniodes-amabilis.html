Arachniodes amabilis

Arachniodes amabilis, commonly known as the silver lace fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, lacy fronds that give it a distinctive and ornamental appearance. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit, with arching stems that produce compound leaves. Its foliage is typically dark green with a silvery underside, creating a striking visual contrast.

Arachniodes amabilis prefers shaded, humid environments and is often used in ornamental horticulture for its aesthetic appeal.
