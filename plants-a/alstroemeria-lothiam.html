Alstroemeria lothiam

Alstroemeria lothiam, commonly known as the Peruvian lily, is a striking evergreen perennial native to South America.

It features vibrant, tubular flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, orange, yellow, and pink. The plant has strap-like, grassy foliage that adds a lush texture to garden landscapes. It thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

Alstroemeria lothiam is also valued for its long-lasting blooms, which make it a favorite in floral arrangements.
