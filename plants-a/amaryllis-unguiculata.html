Amaryllis unguiculata

Amaryllis unguiculata, commonly known as the red spider lily, is a striking bulbous plant native to southern Africa.

It features striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to deep red, often with intricate patterns resembling spider webs. The plant produces large, strap-like leaves that are broad and smooth, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Amaryllis unguiculata is often cultivated for its dramatic blooms and is relatively easy to care for in suitable climates.
