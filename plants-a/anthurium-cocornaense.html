Anthurium cocornaense

Anthurium cocornaense, commonly known as the Cocoraense Arrowhead, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its unique inflorescence consisting of a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy dark green leaves that are often adorned with distinctive veins. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Anthurium cocornaense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in botanical gardens.
