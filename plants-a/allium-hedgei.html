Allium hedgei

Allium hedgei, commonly known as the Sierra foothill garlic.

It is a perennial herb native to the western United States, particularly California. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale purple flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are grass-like and aromatic, with a strong garlic scent.

Allium hedgei is often found in rocky soils and open woodlands, thriving in full sun to partial shade.
