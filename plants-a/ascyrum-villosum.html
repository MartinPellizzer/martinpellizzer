Ascyrum villosum

Ascyrum villosum, commonly known as the hairy tansy, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves, which give it a distinctive, rough texture. The plant produces yellow-orange composite flowers that are arranged in clusters, typical of the daisy family. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and wastelands.
