Argyrolobium pumilum var. pilosum

Argyrolobium pumilum var. pilosum, commonly known as the silky burr veldt.

It is a low-growing perennial herb native to South Africa, characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit. The plant features hairy, dark green leaves and produces small, yellow flowers in summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grassland habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
