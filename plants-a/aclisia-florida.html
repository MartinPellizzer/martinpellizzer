Aclisia florida

Aclisia florida, commonly known as the Florida starfruit, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its small, star-shaped yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant produces bright orange, fleshy fruits that are edible and contain a single seed. Aclisia florida thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a suitable plant for warm climates.

Its ornamental value and unique fruit make it a notable species in botanical gardens and native plant collections.
