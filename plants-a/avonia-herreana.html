Avonia herreana

Avonia herreana, commonly known as the Herrean orchid, is a species native to the Andean region of South America.

It is a herbaceous plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family, characterized by its delicate, showy flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often found in cloud forests. Its flowers are usually small, with a unique coloration that ranges from white to pink and purple.

Avonia herreana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
