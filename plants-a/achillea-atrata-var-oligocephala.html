Achillea atrata var. oligocephala

Achillea atrata var. oligocephala, commonly known as the dark yarrow, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe.

It features dark green, deeply lobed leaves and produces small, dark purple to black flowers in flat-topped clusters. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and thrives in full sun. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used traditionally in herbal medicine.

Its botanical characteristics make it a hardy and drought-tolerant species suitable for xeriscaping.
