Anthemis leptophylla

Anthemis leptophylla, commonly known as the slenderleaf pyrethrum, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in summer. It is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid conditions.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural study.
