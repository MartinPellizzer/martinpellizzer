Artabotrys sericeus

Artabotrys sericeus, commonly known as the silky tamarind tree, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its smooth, pale bark and delicate, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. The tree produces small, round fruits that enclose a single seed, and its leaves are simple, alternate, and often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in tropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and medicinal properties.
