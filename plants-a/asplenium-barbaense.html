Asplenium barbaense

Asplenium barbaense, commonly known as the Barbaense spleenwort, is a small, terrestrial fern belonging to the family Aspleniaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, clustered growth habit and the presence of small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as on rocks or in forest undergrowth. Its spores are produced in clusters on the underside of the leaves, a feature common among ferns.

Asplenium barbaense is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, where it thrives in humid conditions.
