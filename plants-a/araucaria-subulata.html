Araucaria subulata

Araucaria subulata, commonly known as the silver fir, is a coniferous evergreen tree native to South America.

It is characterized by its slender, needle-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The tree produces small, spherical cones that mature to a reddish-brown hue. It prefers well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade, making it suitable for temperate climates.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping and garden planting.
