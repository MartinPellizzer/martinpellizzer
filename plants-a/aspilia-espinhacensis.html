Aspilia espinhacensis

Aspilia espinhacensis, commonly known as the Espinhacense flame flower, is a flowering plant native to specific regions of South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble a single bloom. Aspilia espinhacensis thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats like grasslands and rocky areas.
