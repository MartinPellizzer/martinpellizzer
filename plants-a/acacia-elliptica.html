Acacia elliptica

Acacia elliptica, commonly known as the narrow-leaf wattle, is a shrub or small tree native to Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, elliptical leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of whitish hairs. It is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping.
