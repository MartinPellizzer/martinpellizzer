Alternanthera sintenisii

Alternanthera sintenisii, commonly known as the red-stemmed Alternanthera, is a species of flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its striking red stems and small, inconspicuous flowers. The plant typically grows as an annual herb, reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters. Its foliage is often green with a slight reddish tinge, adding visual interest to gardens.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
