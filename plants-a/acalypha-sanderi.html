Acalypha sanderi

Acalypha sanderi, commonly known as the silverleaf acalypha, is a flowering plant native to Central America.

It is characterized by its striking silver-green foliage, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, pale green flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full sun.

Acalypha sanderi is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive leaves and ease of care.
