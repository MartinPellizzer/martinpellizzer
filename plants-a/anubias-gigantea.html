Anubias gigantea

Anubias gigantea, commonly known as giant anubias, is a hardy aquatic plant native to Africa.

It features broad, dark green leaves that can grow up to 30 cm in length, providing a lush, dense foliage. The plant thrives in low to moderate light conditions and is adaptable to a range of water temperatures. It is often used in aquariums for its aesthetic appeal and ability to help maintain water quality.

Anubias gigantea is also known for its slow growth rate and minimal maintenance requirements.
