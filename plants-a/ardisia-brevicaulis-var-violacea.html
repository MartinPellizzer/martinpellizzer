Ardisia brevicaulis var. violacea

Ardisia brevicaulis var. violacea, commonly known as the violetleaf ardisia, is a compact evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It features small, glossy, dark green leaves with a distinctive violet hue on the underside, giving it its common name. The plant produces clusters of small, white, star-shaped flowers followed by bright red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for shaded garden landscapes.

Its ornamental value comes from its vibrant foliage and seasonal flowering.
