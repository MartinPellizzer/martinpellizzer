Amomum trichanthera

Amomum trichanthera, commonly known as the nutmeg orchid, is a member of the ginger family (Zingiberaceae).

It is native to Southeast Asia, including countries like Indonesia, Malaysia, and the Philippines. Amomum trichanthera is often cultivated for its aesthetic value in gardens and as a decorative plant.
