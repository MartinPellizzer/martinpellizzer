Aloe bulbillifera var. bulbillifera

Aloe bulbillifera var. bulbillifera, commonly known as the giant aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, branching stems and fleshy, sword-shaped leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. The plant produces vibrant orange or yellow flowers in terminal panicles, which bloom during the warmer months. Its leaves contain a gel-like substance that is used in traditional medicine and skincare products.

This species is well adapted to arid environments, making it a hardy and drought-tolerant plant.
