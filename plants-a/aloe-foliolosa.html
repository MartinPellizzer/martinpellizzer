Aloe foliolosa

Aloe foliolosa, commonly known as the leaf aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a waxy coating, which helps it store water. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow-orange flowers, blooming in late summer. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their soothing properties.

Aloe foliolosa thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.
