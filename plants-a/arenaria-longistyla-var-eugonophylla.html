Arenaria longistyla var. eugonophylla

Arenaria longistyla var. eugonophylla, commonly known as the long-styled sandwort, is a flowering plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is characterized by its low-growing habit and small, white or pinkish flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in alpine or arctic regions. Its leaves are linear and often covered with fine hairs, adapting to harsh environmental conditions.

This variety is distinguished by its elongated stamens, which contribute to its unique botanical appearance.
