Allium schisticola

Allium schisticola, commonly known as the rock onion, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils, thriving in well-drained environments. The plant features basal leaves that are narrow and grass-like, with a strong onion-like odor. Its flowers are small, white or pale blue, and are clustered in umbels.

Allium schisticola is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in rock gardens.
