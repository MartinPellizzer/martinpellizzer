Alyssum Montenegrinum

Alyssum montenegrinum is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Montenegro. It is known for its small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters during the late spring and summer. This species thrives in rocky and well-drained soils, often found in alpine meadows and forest edges. Its unique appearance and limited distribution make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists. Protecting Alyssum montenegrinum is crucial for preserving biodiversity in its native habitat.
