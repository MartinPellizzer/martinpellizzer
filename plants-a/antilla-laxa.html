Antilla laxa

Antilla laxa, commonly known as the slender antillan.

It is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean, particularly found in parts of Cuba and the Bahamas. The plant features slender, climbing stems with opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy. Its flowers are small and clustered, typically in axillary inflorescences, and are usually white or pale green.

Antilla laxa is adapted to grow in moist, shaded environments, often along forest edges or in disturbed areas.
