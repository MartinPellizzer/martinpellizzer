Anerincleistus barnesii

Anerincleistus barnesii, commonly known as the Barnes' cycad, is a rare and ancient plant species belonging to the Cycadaceae family.

It is characterized by its stout, unbranched trunk and a crown of feathery, pinnate leaves that are typically dark green in color. The plant produces large, cone-like structures that contain both male and female reproductive organs, typical of cycads. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often in well-drained, sandy soils.
