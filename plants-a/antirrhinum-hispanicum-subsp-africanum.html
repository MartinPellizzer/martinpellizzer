Antirrhinum hispanicum subsp. africanum

Antirrhinum hispanicum subsp.

africanum, commonly known as the African snapdragon, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It belongs to the Plantaginaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, yellow, and orange.
