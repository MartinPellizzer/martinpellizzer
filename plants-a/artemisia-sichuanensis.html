Artemisia sichuanensis

Artemisia sichuanensis, commonly known as Sichuan wormwood, is a perennial herb native to China.

It is characterized by its aromatic, silver-gray foliage and small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant thrives in dry, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes many other medicinal and ornamental plants.

Artemisia sichuanensis is valued for its essential oils and has been used in traditional Chinese medicine.
