Ardisia nhatrangensis var. glaucescens

Ardisia nhatrangensis var. glaucescens, commonly known as the Nhatrang glossy ardisia, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are typically elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, white flowers followed by bright red berries, which are attractive to birds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in warm climates.
