Arundo cinnoides

Arundo cinnoides, commonly known as the Chinese arundo or giant reed, is a tall, perennial grass native to Asia.

It features long, narrow leaves that are often bluish-green in color and can grow up to several meters in height. The plant produces dense clusters of small, fluffy flowers that are typically pink or purple in hue. It thrives in moist environments and is often found near water bodies such as rivers and wetlands.

Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread rapidly, making it both a valuable ecological plant and a potential invasive species.
