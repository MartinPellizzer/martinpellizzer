Aconitum yamazakii

Aconitum yamazakii, commonly known as Yamazaki aconite, is a herbaceous perennial native to Japan.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to purple spires of flowers. The plant features large, palmately lobed leaves that are typically bright green and hairy. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Due to its toxic properties, it should be handled with care.
