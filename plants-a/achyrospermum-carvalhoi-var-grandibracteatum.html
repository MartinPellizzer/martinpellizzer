Achyrospermum carvalhoi var. grandibracteatum

Achyrospermum carvalhoi var. grandibracteatum, commonly known as the Brazilian blue mistflower, is a flowering plant native to Brazil.

The plant features showy blue to violet flowers that bloom in dense, rounded clusters, creating a striking visual effect. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its ornamental appeal.
