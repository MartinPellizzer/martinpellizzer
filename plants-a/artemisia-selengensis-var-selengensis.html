Artemisia selengensis var. selengensis

Artemisia selengensis var. selengensis, commonly known as Selengian wormwood, is a perennial herb native to the cold regions of Siberia and Central Asia.

It is characterized by its aromatic, silver-gray foliage and small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in summer. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often growing in alpine or rocky habitats. Its leaves contain volatile oils, which contribute to its medicinal and aromatic properties.

This variety is valued for its resilience to harsh climates and its potential uses in traditional medicine.
