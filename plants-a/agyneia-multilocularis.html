Agyneia multilocularis

Agyneia multilocularis, commonly known as the multi-chambered orchid, is a unique flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its intricate, multi-chambered flower structure. The plant features elongated, slender stems with clustered inflorescences that produce small, delicate flowers. Its botanical significance lies in its complex reproductive system and specialized pollination mechanisms.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered a rare and endangered species in its natural range.
