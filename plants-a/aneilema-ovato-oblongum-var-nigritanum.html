Aneilema ovato-oblongum var. nigritanum

Aneilema ovato-oblongum var. nigritanum, commonly known as the black-leaved aneilema, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its ovate-oblong leaves that are dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of white and pink, which bloom during the wet season. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for shade gardens and indoor cultivation.
