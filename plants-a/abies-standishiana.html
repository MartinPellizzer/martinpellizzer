Abies standishiana

Abies standishiana, commonly known as the Standish fir, is a species of conifer native to the mountainous regions of western China.

It is characterized by its slender, conical shape and its dark green, needle-like leaves that are arranged in spirals along the branches. The tree produces small, spherical cones that typically mature to a reddish-brown color. It thrives in cool, moist climates and is often found at high altitudes where temperatures are lower.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
