Astragalus nurensis var. melanotrichus

Astragalus nurensis var. melanotrichus, commonly known as the black-haired milkvetch, is a perennial legume native to Central Asia.

It is characterized by its erect stems and trifoliate leaves with dark purple to black hairy margins. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in racemes, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its root system is extensive and contributes to soil stabilization in arid environments.

It is often found in dry steppe and grassland habitats.
