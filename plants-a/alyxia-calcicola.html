Alyxia calcicola

Alyxia calcicola, commonly known as the limestone alyxia, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of Australia.

It is adapted to grow in limestone soils, which influence its growth habits and chemical composition. Alyxia calcicola is often found in rocky outcrops and is an important component of local flora in its native habitat.
