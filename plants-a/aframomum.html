Aframomum, commonly known as African cardamom, is a genus of flowering plants in the ginger family, Zingiberaceae.

These plants are native to tropical regions of Africa and are characterized by their aromatic, rhizomatous growth habit. Aframomum species are valued for their medicinal properties and are often used in herbal remedies.
