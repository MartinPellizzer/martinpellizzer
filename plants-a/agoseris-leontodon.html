Agoseris leontodon

Agoseris leontodon, commonly known as the golden crownbeard, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features a basal rosette of gray-green, hairy leaves and produces showy yellow ray florets surrounded by orange-brown disk florets. Agoseris leontodon thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in open habitats such as meadows and grasslands.
