Adiantum intermedium var. medioximum

Adiantum intermedium var. medioximum, commonly known as the intermediate maidenhair fern, is a delicate fern species characterized by its finely divided, bright green fronds.

It belongs to the family Adiantaceae and is native to parts of Asia, including China and Japan. The plant features sori that are linear and arranged along the margins of the fronds, typical of the genus Adiantum. It prefers moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas or along stream banks.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and conservation efforts.
