Arctium sakawense

Arctium sakawense, commonly known as the Sakawa burweed, is a flowering plant native to parts of East Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and thistles. The plant is characterized by its spiny, globe-shaped fruits that contain seeds, typical of the genus Arctium. Its leaves are deeply lobed and often covered in fine hairs.
