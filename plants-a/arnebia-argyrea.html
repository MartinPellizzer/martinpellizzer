Arnebia argyrea

Arnebia argyrea, commonly known as the silver root or silverweed, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of Asia.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its silver-green foliage and showy pink or red flowers. The plant has a deep taproot system that allows it to thrive in dry conditions. Its leaves are often covered in fine, white hairs, which help reduce water loss.
