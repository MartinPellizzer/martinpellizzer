Actinidia tetramera var. badongensis

Actinidia tetramera var. badongensis, commonly known as the Badong Actinidia, is a rare and endemic species native to China.

It belongs to the Actinidiaceae family and is characterized by its climbing habit and deciduous foliage. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are typically followed by edible berries. Its leaves are heart-shaped with serrated edges and have a distinctive aromatic scent.

Due to its limited distribution and unique botanical features, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
