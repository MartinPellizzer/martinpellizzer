Astilbe taquetii

Astilbe taquetii, commonly known as the Chinese astilbe, is a herbaceous perennial native to China.

It features upright, branched inflorescences that bear delicate, plume-like flowers in shades of pink, white, and red. The plant has lance-shaped, dark green foliage that remains attractive throughout the growing season. It thrives in partial to full shade and prefers moist, well-drained soil.

Astilbe taquetii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and woodland settings.
