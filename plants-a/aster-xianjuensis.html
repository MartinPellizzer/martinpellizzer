Aster xianjuensis

Aster xianjuensis, commonly known as the Xianju aster, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers. The plant is characterized by its showy, violet-blue flower heads that bloom in late summer. It typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in mountainous regions.

Due to its ornamental value and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
