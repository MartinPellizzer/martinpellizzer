Arisaema undulatifolium f. viridiflorum

Arisaema undulatifolium f. viridiflorum, commonly known as the green-flowered ripple-leaf arisaema, is a variant of the species Arisaema undulatifolium.

It is a herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in regions of China, Vietnam, and Thailand. The plant features distinctive, undulating leaves that give it its common name, and its flowers are characterized by a unique, spadix-like structure surrounded by a leaf-like bract. The inflorescence is typically greenish in color, which is a notable trait of this particular form.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
