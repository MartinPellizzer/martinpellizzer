Aeschynanthus nervosus

Aeschynanthus nervosus, commonly known as the veined ivy or the silver veined ivy, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and glossy, dark green leaves with prominent veins. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically white or pale pink, which bloom in clusters along the stems. It thrives in humid, warm environments and is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ornamental value.

Aeschynanthus nervosus requires bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to flourish.
