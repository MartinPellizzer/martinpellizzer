Alternanthera filifolia

Alternanthera filifolia, commonly known as the threadleaf Alternanthera, is a striking aquatic plant native to South America.

It features narrow, thread-like leaves that are often a vibrant red or pink, especially when exposed to bright light. The plant thrives in shallow, slow-moving waters and is popular in aquariums for its ornamental value. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or white.

Its rapid growth rate makes it an effective choice for pond and aquarium landscaping.
