Alternanthera amoena f. reinhardii

Alternanthera amoena f. reinhardii, commonly known as the Red Alternanthera, is a striking ornamental plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red to crimson foliage, which becomes more intense under strong sunlight. The plant typically grows as a herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 30 inches. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Its compact growth habit and colorful leaves make it a popular choice for water gardens and container planting.
