Asystasia gangetica var. mendeliana

Asystasia gangetica var. mendeliana, commonly known as the pink-flowered asystasia, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking violet to pink tubular flowers. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy surface, and it typically grows in moist, shaded environments. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant floral display.

The species is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
