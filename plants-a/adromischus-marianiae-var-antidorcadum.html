Adromischus marianiae var. antidorcadum

Adromischus marianiae var. antidorcadum, commonly known as the "Rainbow Plant," is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features compact, rosette-shaped leaves that are fleshy and covered in a fine, powdery coating. The leaves often display a range of colors, from green to pink and red, depending on light exposure. It produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months.

This plant is well-suited for rock gardens or container cultivation due to its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements.
