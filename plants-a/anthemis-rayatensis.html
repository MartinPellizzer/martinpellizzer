Anthemis rayatensis

Anthemis rayatensis, commonly known as the Rayatense ragwort, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of ray and disk flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in temperate climates. Its yellowish-orange flowers bloom in the summer months, attracting various pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species in its native habitat.
