Abarema moniliformis

Abarema moniliformis, commonly known as the chain flower or yellow ball tree, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its distinctive yellow, spherical flower heads. Its leaves are trifoliate, consisting of three oval-shaped leaflets, and it produces pods that contain several seeds.

Abarema moniliformis is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
