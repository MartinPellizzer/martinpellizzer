Aglaonema minus var. nanum

Aglaonema minus var. nanum, commonly known as the dwarf Chinese evergreen, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, dark green leaves with striking white and silver variegation, often marked with intricate patterns. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening. Its foliage remains dense and vibrant throughout the year, requiring minimal maintenance.

Aglaonema minus var. nanum is also appreciated for its air-purifying qualities and ability to tolerate dry air.
