Aglaonema marmoratum

Aglaonema marmoratum, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated foliage featuring shades of green, white, and pink, which are caused by the presence of anthocyanins and chlorophyll. The plant has arrow-shaped leaves that are often marked with intricate patterns, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening. It thrives in low-light conditions and requires moderate moisture, making it easy to care for.

Its ability to purify indoor air further enhances its appeal as a decorative and functional plant.
