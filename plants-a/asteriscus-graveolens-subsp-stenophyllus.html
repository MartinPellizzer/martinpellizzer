Asteriscus graveolens subsp. stenophyllus

Asteriscus graveolens subsp.

stenophyllus, commonly known as the narrow-leaf asteriscus, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region. It is characterized by its slender, aromatic leaves and small, clustered yellow flowers that bloom in late summer.

The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes. Its essential oils contain compounds like thymol and carvacrol, which contribute to its strong scent and potential medicinal uses.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in herbal remedies.
