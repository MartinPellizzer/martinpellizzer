Alyssum montanum f. simplex

Alyssum montanum f. simplex, commonly known as the simple alpine alyssum, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It features compact, mat-forming growth with small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in alpine and rocky habitats.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders in horticultural settings.
