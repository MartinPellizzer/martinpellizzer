Annona neosericea

Annona neosericea, commonly known as the velvet annona, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its smooth, dark green leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by yellow, berry-like fruits. Its fruit is edible, though typically consumed raw or used in local culinary traditions.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional medicinal uses in indigenous communities.
