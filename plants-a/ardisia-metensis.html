Ardisia metensis

Ardisia metensis, commonly known as the Brazilian pepper tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to South America.

It belongs to the family Ericaceae and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of small, white flowers. The plant produces bright red berries that are an important food source for various bird species. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its attractive foliage and seasonal blooms.
