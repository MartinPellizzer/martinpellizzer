Anthurium potarense

Anthurium potarense, commonly known as the Potareno arrowhead, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its unique inflorescence consisting of a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe. The plant features broad, arrow-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.

Anthurium potarense is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant foliage and exotic appearance.
