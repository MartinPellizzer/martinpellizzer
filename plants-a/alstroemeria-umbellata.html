Alstroemeria umbellata

Alstroemeria umbellata, commonly known as the umbrella lily, is a striking flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Alstroemeriaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that come in a range of colors including red, orange, and yellow. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet, featuring narrow, grass-like leaves and a dense cluster of blooms at the top of its stem.

Alstroemeria umbellata thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
