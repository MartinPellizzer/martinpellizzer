Agrostis sinocontracta

Agrostis sinocontracta, commonly known as the Chinese narrowleaf bentgrass, is a perennial grass species native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves that are typically less than 10 cm in length. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wet meadows and marshy areas.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and ecological restoration projects.
