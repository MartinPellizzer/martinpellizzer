Amaracus brevidens

Amaracus brevidens, commonly known as the shortleaf amaracus, is a species of flowering plant native to certain regions of South America.

It belongs to the family Amaranthaceae, which includes many species with showy flowers and often edible leaves. Amaracus brevidens is often found in moist, well-drained soils in woodland or shrubland habitats.
