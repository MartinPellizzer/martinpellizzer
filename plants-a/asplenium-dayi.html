Asplenium dayi

Asplenium dayi, commonly known as the Chinese shield fern, is a species of fern native to China.

It belongs to the family Aspleniaceae and is characterized by its compact, rosette-like growth habit. The fronds are typically leathery and have a distinctive, shield-shaped appearance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in rocky or forested areas.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in horticulture.
