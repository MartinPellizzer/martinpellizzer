Argylia trifoliata

Argylia trifoliata, commonly known as the three-leaved argylia, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of eastern China.

The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that bloom in late spring, typically in shades of white and pale pink. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in shaded forest understories.
