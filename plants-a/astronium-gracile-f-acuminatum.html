Astronium gracile f. acuminatum

Astronium gracile f. acuminatum, commonly known as the slender star sedge, is a flowering plant species belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and small, clustered flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests or wetlands. It has a compact growth habit and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.
