Anaphalis mariae f. alba

Anaphalis mariae f. alba, commonly known as the silverleaf mouseear, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a silvery appearance. The plant produces small, pale yellow to white composite flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is often found in alpine or subalpine environments.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological significance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens for ornamental purposes.
