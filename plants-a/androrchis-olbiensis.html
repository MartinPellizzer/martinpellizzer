Androrchis olbiensis

Androrchis olbiensis, commonly known as the Olbiense Orchid, is a rare and endemic species native to the mountainous regions of the Caucasus.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a short, hairy stem and a single, showy flower that typically blooms in late spring. The plant has lance-shaped leaves and produces a distinctive, fragrant orchid flower with a yellow lip. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is adapted to the cool, humid climates of its native habitat.
