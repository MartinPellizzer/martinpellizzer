Anthemis diffusa

Anthemis diffusa, commonly known as blueweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its aromatic blue or purple flowers and hairy, gray-green leaves. The plant is often found in dry, open habitats such as meadows and rocky areas. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Anthemis diffusa is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
