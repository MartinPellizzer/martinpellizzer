Alsophila acutula

Alsophila acutula, commonly known as the narrow-leaf tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

Its rhizome system supports the growth of its tall, fibrous trunk, while its fronds are typically arranged in a spiral pattern.
