Aster spathulifolius var. oharae

Aster spathulifolius var. oharae, commonly known as the Ohara's spatulate aster, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and showy blooms. The plant features narrow, spatulate-shaped leaves that are typically arranged in a basal rosette. Its flowers are usually purple to blue in color, with ray florets surrounding a central disk.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
