Aspalathus spinosissima

Aspalathus spinosissima, commonly known as the spiked bushveld pea, is a perennial shrub native to South Africa.

It features slender, spiny stems and small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant produces small, bean-like pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, sandy environments.

Its botanical characteristics make it an important species in the fynbos vegetation of the region.
