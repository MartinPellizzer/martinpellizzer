Arundarbor fortunei

Arundarbor fortunei, commonly known as the golden mist fern, is a rare and striking plant native to the misty highlands of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds that shimmer with a golden hue under sunlight. The plant thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil to flourish. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for botanical gardens and conservation efforts.

Due to habitat loss, it is classified as an endangered species, highlighting the need for its protection.
