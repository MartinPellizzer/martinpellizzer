Astragalus bingoellensis

Astragalus bingoellensis, commonly known as the Mongolian milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to the steppes of Central Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant is known for its deep root system, which allows it to thrive in arid and semi-arid environments. It plays an important role in soil stabilization and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered a vulnerable species in some regions.
