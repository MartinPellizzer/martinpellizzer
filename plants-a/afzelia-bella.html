Afzelia bella

Afzelia bella, commonly known as the African rosewood, is a tropical tree native to regions of Africa and Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark, dense wood and is often valued for its use in furniture and musical instruments. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are typically elliptical in shape. It produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually white or pale yellow.

Afzelia bella is also noted for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types, making it adaptable to different environments.
