Anthurium reticulatum var. truncatulum

Anthurium reticulatum var. truncatulum, commonly known as the truncated anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves with a deep green color and prominent veins. The plant produces vibrant red spathes that enclose bright yellow spadices, making it a popular choice for ornamental purposes. It thrives in humid, warm environments with indirect light and requires well-draining soil.

Anthurium reticulatum var. truncatulum is relatively low maintenance but benefits from regular watering and occasional fertilization.
