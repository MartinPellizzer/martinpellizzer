Anemone yezoensis

Anemone yezoensis, commonly known as the Japanese anemone, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to East Asia.

It features showy, cup-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in late summer. The plant has finely divided, feather-like leaves that are often bluish-green in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens.

Anemone yezoensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to naturalize in suitable climates.
