Anthemis boveana var. aguilarii

Anthemis boveana var. aguilarii, commonly known as the Alboraya daisy, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features upright stems covered in fine, white to pale yellow hairs, and its leaves are narrow, linear, and slightly hairy. The plant produces small, daisy-like yellow flowers with a central disk surrounded by white ray florets. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
