Aspidium reptans var. asplenioides

Aspidium reptans var. asplenioides, commonly known as the creeping spleenwort, is a fern species belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its small, delicate fronds that are typically less than 10 centimeters in length. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found growing on rocks or decaying wood. Its sporangia are clustered in sori on the underside of the fronds, a common feature in many ferns.

Asplenioides is known for its hardy nature and ability to tolerate a variety of soil conditions.
