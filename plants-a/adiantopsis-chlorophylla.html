Adiantopsis chlorophylla

Adiantopsis chlorophylla, commonly known as the green maidenhair fern, is a species of fern native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its delicate, lace-like fronds that are typically light green in color. The plant belongs to the family Adiantaceae and is closely related to the genus Adiantum. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and prefers well-drained soil.

Its unique botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and ecological restoration projects.
