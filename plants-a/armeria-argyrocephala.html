Armeria argyrocephala

Armeria argyrocephala, commonly known as silverhead thrift, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces dense clusters of white or pale yellow flowers. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is often found in rocky or sandy soils. It thrives in full sun and is drought-tolerant, making it suitable for rock gardens.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and ecological restoration projects.
