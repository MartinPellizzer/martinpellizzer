Agelanthus dodoneifolius

Agelanthus dodoneifolius, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, bright red flowers that bloom in clusters during the rainy season. The plant has compound leaves with elongated, serrated leaflets that give it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular ornamental species.

Its rapid growth and showy blooms make it a notable addition to gardens and landscapes.
