Aglaia conferta

Aglaia conferta, commonly known as the white aglaia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree or shrub that typically grows up to 10 meters in height. The plant features simple, alternate leaves that are usually ovate or elliptic in shape. Its flowers are small, fragrant, and clustered in inflorescences, often with a white or pale yellow color.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release the seeds, which are small and flat.
