Aneilema acuminatum var. marginatum

Aneilema acuminatum var. marginatum, commonly known as the margins leaf aneilema, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, lance-shaped leaves with a distinctive white or pale margin. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant, white or pale pink flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for gardens and indoor plant collections.
