Acianthus petiolatus

Acianthus petiolatus, commonly known as the spurred orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its unique spurred petals and a compact, rosette-like growth habit. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial shade.

Acianthus petiolatus is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and botanical collections.
