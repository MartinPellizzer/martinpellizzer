Adiantum deflectens var. subaristatum

Adiantum deflectens var. subaristatum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf maidenhair fern, is a delicate fern species native to Southeast Asia.

It features finely divided, lance-shaped fronds that are typically dark green and have a distinctive, elegant appearance. The plant is characterized by its small, bright green pinnae that are often slightly curled at the tips. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found growing on rocks or in forest understories.

Its botanical significance lies in its unique morphology and ecological role in tropical and subtropical habitats.
