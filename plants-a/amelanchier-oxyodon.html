Amelanchier oxyodon

Amelanchier oxyodon, commonly known as the red elderberry, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to North America.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and clusters of small, white flowers that appear in early spring.
