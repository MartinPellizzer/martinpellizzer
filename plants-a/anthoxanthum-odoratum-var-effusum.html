Anthoxanthum odoratum var. effusum

Anthoxanthum odoratum var. effusum, commonly known as sweet vernal grass, is a flowering plant belonging to the grass family Poaceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow to orange flowers, which bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows, roadsides, and sandy soils. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a slightly hairy texture.

It is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil and support pollinators.
