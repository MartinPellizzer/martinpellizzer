Agave rubrocincta

Agave rubrocincta, commonly known as the red-belt agave, is a succulent plant native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped growth pattern and thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The leaves are adorned with red or purple margins, which give the plant its distinctive appearance. It produces tall, branched inflorescences that bear yellowish-green flowers.

This plant is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
