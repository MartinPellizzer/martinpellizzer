Arisaema honbaense

Arisaema honbaense, commonly known as the Honbae arrowhead, is a flowering plant native to the Korean Peninsula.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its unique inflorescence composed of a spadix and a spathe. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its leaves are arrow-shaped, and it produces small, greenish-white flowers.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and has cultural significance in traditional Korean horticulture.
