Anthurium uleanum var. nanayense

Anthurium uleanum var. nanayense, commonly known as the Nanayense Philodendron, is a rare and striking plant native to the tropical rainforests of South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its unique inflorescence consisting of a spadix and a spathe. The plant features large, heart-shaped leaves with a deep green color and prominent veins, often adorned with a reddish or purplish tint. It thrives in high humidity and indirect light, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Due to its limited distribution and ornamental value, it is considered a sought-after specimen among plant enthusiasts.
