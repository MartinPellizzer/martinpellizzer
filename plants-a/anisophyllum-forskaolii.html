Anisophyllum forskaolii

Anisophyllum forskaolii, commonly known as the desert shield-plant, is a succulent shrub native to arid regions of the Arabian Peninsula.

It features fleshy, spiny leaves that help it conserve water in harsh environments. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers during the warmer months, typically in spring or early summer. Its root system is extensive, allowing it to anchor itself in sandy soils and access deep moisture sources.

Anisophyllum forskaolii is well adapted to extreme temperatures and low precipitation, making it a resilient species in desert ecosystems.
