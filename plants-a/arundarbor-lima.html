Arundarbor lima

Arundarbor lima, commonly known as the golden lima bean, is a tropical plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves and produces pods containing edible, yellowish-green beans. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth. Its flowers are typically white or pale purple, and it is cultivated for its nutritional seeds.

The botanical features of Arundarbor lima make it a valuable crop in regions with suitable growing conditions.
