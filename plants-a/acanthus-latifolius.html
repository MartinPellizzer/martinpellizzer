Acanthus latifolius

Acanthus latifolius, commonly known as the broad-leaved acanthus, is a striking ornamental plant native to the Mediterranean region.

Its architectural form and foliage make it a popular choice for garden design and historical architecture.

Acanthus latifolius is also valued for its role in traditional herbal medicine and its use in horticultural displays.
