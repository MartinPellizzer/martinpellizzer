Abacopteris × insularis

Abacopteris × insularis, commonly known as the hybrid paper mulberry, is a naturally occurring hybrid between *Abacopteris* and *Broussonetia* species.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to parts of Southeast Asia, including the Philippines and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its fibrous, pale bark and heart-shaped leaves with a slightly serrated edge. It produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in panicles, followed by small, round fruits.

Due to its rapid growth and ability to thrive in various soil types, it has been introduced to other regions for ornamental and industrial purposes.
