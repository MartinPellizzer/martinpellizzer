Anemone coronaria f. parviflora

Anemone coronaria f. parviflora, commonly known as the small-flowered crown anemone, is a compact, herbaceous perennial native to the Mediterranean region.

It features delicate, nodding flowers in shades of pink, white, or purple, often with a distinctive yellow center. The plant produces small, clustered flowers on short stems, and its leaves are basal, spoon-shaped, and covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

This cultivar is valued for its early spring bloom and hardy nature.
