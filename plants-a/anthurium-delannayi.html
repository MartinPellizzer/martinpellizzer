Anthurium delannayi

Anthurium delannayi, commonly known as the Delannay's arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive spathe and spadix inflorescence. The plant features broad, arrowhead-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy surface. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Anthurium delannayi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated as a houseplant.
