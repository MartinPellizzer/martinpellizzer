Agrostis kudoi

Agrostis kudoi, commonly known as Kudoi grass, is a perennial grass species native to East Asia.

It typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and wetlands. The plant has slender, upright stems and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a slightly hairy texture.

Agrostis kudoi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping and ecological restoration projects.
