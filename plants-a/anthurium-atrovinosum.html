Anthurium atrovinosum

Anthurium atrovinosum, commonly known as the black velvet anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its dark, velvety leaves that range from deep green to almost black in color. The plant produces striking, spadix-shaped inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, typically in shades of red or orange. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium atrovinosum is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its aesthetic appeal and air-purifying qualities.
