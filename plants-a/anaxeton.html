Anaxeton

Anaxeton, commonly known as the wild carrot or Queen Anne's lace, is a biennial herbaceous plant native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It belongs to the Apiaceae family, characterized by its distinctive umbellate inflorescence and aromatic foliage. Anaxeton is often found in disturbed soils and can be invasive in certain regions.
