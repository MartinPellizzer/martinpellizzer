Aloe gariusana

Aloe gariusana, commonly known as the Natal aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, upright leaves that are typically green with a waxy coating. The plant produces tall, branched flower stalks that bear yellowish-green flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Aloe gariusana is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional medicinal uses.
