Aspidium bulbiferum

Aspidium bulbiferum, commonly known as the bulbous water fern, is a species of fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds. The plant typically grows in wet, shaded environments such as marshes and along stream banks. Its rhizomes are often bulbous, giving it its common name.

Aspidium bulbiferum is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to thrive in humid conditions.
