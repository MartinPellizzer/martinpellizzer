Arisaema elephas f. jihaense

Arisaema elephas f. jihaense, commonly known as the Jihaen arrowhead, is a variant of the arrowhead plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, heart-shaped leaves and spadix inflorescence typical of the Araceae family. The plant produces a single, narrow spadix with small, inconspicuous flowers surrounded by a modified leaf called a spathe. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
