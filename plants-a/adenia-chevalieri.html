Adenia chevalieri

Adenia chevalieri, commonly known as the African star gooseberry, is a climbing shrub native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Passifloraceae and is characterized by its woody stems and opposite, ovate leaves. The plant produces showy, five-petaled flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. Its fruit is a fleshy, yellow berry that contains several seeds.
