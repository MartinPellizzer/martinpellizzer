Aspidostemon lacrimans

Aspidostemon lacrimans, commonly known as the weeping salsify, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces striking, yellow-orange flowers with a distinctive, inflated, seed-bearing structure. The plant has slender, upright stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine, white hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable candidate for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Its unique appearance and adaptability make it an interesting addition to both ornamental and ecological gardens.
