Amorphospermum balansae

Amorphospermum balansae, commonly known as the Balansae starling tree, is a tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree that typically grows in humid, lowland forest environments. The plant features simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical in shape and have a smooth, glossy surface. Its flowers are small and clustered in inflorescences, and it produces non-showy, fleshy fruits that contain a single seed.

Due to its unique botanical characteristics, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
