Aptosimum lineare var. ciliatum

Aptosimum lineare var. ciliatum, commonly known as the slender hairgrass, is a grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its fine, slender blades and is often found in moist, well-drained soils. The plant typically grows in open grasslands and along riverbanks, preferring full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are usually covered with short, ciliate hairs, giving it its scientific name.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping for its delicate, grass-like appearance.
