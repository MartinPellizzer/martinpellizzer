Anthurium rudgeanum

Anthurium rudgeanum, commonly known as the Rudge's anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length. The plant produces striking spadices, which are brightly colored, spadix-like structures that serve as its reproductive organs. Anthurium rudgeanum thrives in humid, shaded environments with high levels of atmospheric moisture.

Its unique foliage and ornamental spadices make it a popular choice for indoor gardening and botanical collections.
