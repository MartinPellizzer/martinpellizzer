Angraecum zigzag

Angraecum zigzag, commonly known as the zigzag orchid, is a striking epiphytic plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its unique, undulating leaf structure that gives it its common name. The plant produces fragrant, white or pale pink flowers that bloom at night, attracting specific moths for pollination. Angraecum zigzag thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Its intricate morphology and nocturnal blooming habits make it a fascinating subject in botanical study.
