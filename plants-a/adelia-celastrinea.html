Adelia celastrinea

Adelia celastrinea, commonly known as the silky nightshade, is a flowering plant belonging to the Solanaceae family.

It is native to parts of Africa and is characterized by its small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale purple. The plant produces small, round berries that are typically yellow or orange when mature. Its leaves are ovate with a slightly hairy texture, and it thrives in well-drained soils in full sun to partial shade.

Adelia celastrinea is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and unique floral display.
