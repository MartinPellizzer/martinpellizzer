Anthurium rivulorum

Anthurium rivulorum, commonly known as the river anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically deep green with a velvety texture. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-like inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, often in shades of red or orange. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium rivulorum is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
