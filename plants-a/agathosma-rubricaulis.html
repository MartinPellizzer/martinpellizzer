Agathosma rubricaulis

Agathosma rubricaulis, commonly known as the red-stemmed buchu, is a small, aromatic shrub native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family, which includes citrus plants, and is characterized by its fragrant, evergreen leaves. The plant has a compact growth habit with slender, reddish stems that give it its common name. Its leaves are narrow, leathery, and often have a distinctive citrus-like aroma.

Agathosma rubricaulis is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal medicine.
