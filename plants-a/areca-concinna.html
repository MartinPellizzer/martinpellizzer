Areca concinna

Areca concinna, commonly known as the dwarf areca palm, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized palm with a slender trunk and pinnate leaves that are typically dark green. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers followed by edible, black fruits. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil.

Areca concinna is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and adaptability.
