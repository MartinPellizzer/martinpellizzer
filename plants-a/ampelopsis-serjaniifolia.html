Ampelopsis serjaniifolia

Ampelopsis serjaniifolia, commonly known as the Chinese ivy, is a climbing vine native to eastern Asia.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a waxy surface and can grow up to several meters in length. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are followed by red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and is often used in ornamental landscaping.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for vertical gardening and erosion control.
