Armeria alpina var. microcephala

Armeria alpina var. microcephala, commonly known as the alpine thrift, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

It features dense clusters of small, white or pinkish flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is well adapted to rocky and well-drained soils. It is often found in alpine meadows and is tolerant of cold and harsh environmental conditions.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for rock gardens and alpine landscapes.
