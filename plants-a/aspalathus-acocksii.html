Aspalathus acocksii

Aspalathus acocksii, commonly known as the Acocksii bushveld clover, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that range in color from white to pink. Aspalathus acocksii is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil.
