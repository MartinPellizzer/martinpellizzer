Aeschynomene obovalis

Aeschynomene obovalis, commonly known as the broadleaf aeschynomene, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in arid environments due to its drought tolerance.
