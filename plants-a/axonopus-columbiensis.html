Axonopus columbiensis

Axonopus columbiensis, commonly known as the Columbia axonopus, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a member of the grass family (Poaceae) and typically grows in wetland or marshy environments. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences.
