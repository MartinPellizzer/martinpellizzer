Aster rivesii

Aster rivesii, commonly known as the Rives' aster, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its showy blue to purple flowers that bloom in the summer months. The plant typically grows in open habitats such as meadows and grasslands. It has hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often tinged with silver.

Aster rivesii is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological role in supporting pollinators.
