Agathosma gillivrayi

Agathosma gillivrayi, commonly known as Cape daisy, is a small shrub native to the Western Cape region of South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its narrow, aromatic leaves and small yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to the Mediterranean climate of the region. It is often found in fynbos vegetation, which is known for its biodiversity and unique plant species.

Agathosma gillivrayi is also valued for its essential oils, which are used in aromatherapy and traditional medicine.
