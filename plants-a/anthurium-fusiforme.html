Anthurium fusiforme

Anthurium fusiforme, commonly known as the arrowhead plant, is a tropical evergreen with striking, heart-shaped leaves that are often dark green with a glossy texture.

It produces vibrant red spathes that enclose its spadix, a characteristic feature of the Araceae family. The plant thrives in humid, warm environments and requires indirect light to flourish. Its foliage is dense and can grow quite large, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Anthurium fusiforme is also noted for its air-purifying qualities, contributing to its appeal as both an ornamental and beneficial houseplant.
