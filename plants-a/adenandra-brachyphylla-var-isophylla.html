Adenandra brachyphylla var. isophylla

Adenandra brachyphylla var. isophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leaved adenandra, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It features small, narrow leaves that are arranged alternately along the stems. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. It is typically found in rocky or sandy soils in coastal and mountainous regions.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its compact growth and seasonal flowering.
