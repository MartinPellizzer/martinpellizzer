Anthyllis vulneraria subsp. rifana

Anthyllis vulneraria subsp.

rifana, commonly known as the Rif viper's bugloss, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region. It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky and arid environments.

The plant produces small, blue or violet flowers arranged in dense clusters. Its leaves are narrow and often covered in fine hairs.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its hardy nature.
