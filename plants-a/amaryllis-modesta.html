Amaryllis modesta

Amaryllis modesta, commonly known as the modest amaryllis, is a species of flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant produces a rosette of broad, strap-like leaves that are evergreen and can grow up to 30 cm in length. It blooms in the spring and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.

Amaryllis modesta is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for both novice and experienced gardeners.
