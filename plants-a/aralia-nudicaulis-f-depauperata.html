Aralia nudicaulis f. depauperata

Aralia nudicaulis f. depauperata, commonly known as the slender aralia, is a low-growing perennial plant native to eastern North America.

It features small, clustered greenish-white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has delicate, fern-like leaves that are typically shorter and more refined compared to the typical aralia species. It thrives in shaded to partially shaded environments and prefers moist, well-drained soil.

This form is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique foliage structure.
