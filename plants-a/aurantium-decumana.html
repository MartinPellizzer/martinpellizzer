Aurantium decumana

Aurantium decumana, commonly known as the Decumanus orange, is a species of citrus plant native to southern Europe.

It is characterized by its evergreen shrub or small tree form, with dark green, glossy leaves and fragrant white flowers. The plant produces small, round fruits that are typically yellow or orange in color when mature. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and its potential use in horticulture and traditional medicine.

The fruit contains essential oils and is used in aromatherapy and culinary applications.
