Ardisia impressa var. impressa

Ardisia impressa var. impressa, commonly known as the glossy bucktooth plant, is a species native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are often marked with distinctive white or pale veins. The plant produces small, red berries that are attractive to birds and other wildlife. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
