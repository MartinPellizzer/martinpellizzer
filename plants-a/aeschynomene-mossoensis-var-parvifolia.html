Aeschynomene mossoensis var. parvifolia

Aeschynomene mossoensis var. parvifolia, commonly known as small-leaved Aeschynomene, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its small, narrow leaves and clusters of small, pale yellow flowers. The plant is typically found in moist, disturbed habitats such as grasslands and riverbanks.

It has a shallow root system and is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
