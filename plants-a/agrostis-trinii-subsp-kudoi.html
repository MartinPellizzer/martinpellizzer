Agrostis trinii subsp. kudoi

Agrostis trinii subsp.

kudoi, commonly known as Kudoi bentgrass, is a low-growing perennial grass native to East Asia. It typically forms dense, mat-like growth and thrives in moist, well-drained soils.

The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It is often found in wetland or riparian habitats.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable species for ecological restoration projects.
