Andrographis lobelioides var. composita

Andrographis lobelioides var. composita, commonly known as the composite andrographis, is a perennial herb native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in clusters. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its leaves contain andrographolide, a bioactive compound with antimicrobial and anti-inflammatory properties.

It is often used in traditional medicine for its therapeutic benefits.
