Aeonium davidbramwellii

Aeonium davidbramwellii, commonly known as the David Bramwell aeonium, is a succulent plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped growth pattern, with thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces striking orange to red flowers on tall, branched stalks during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright sunlight to maintain its vibrant coloration.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively low maintenance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
