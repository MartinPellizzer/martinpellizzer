Astrostemma spartioides

Astrostemma spartioides, commonly known as the mountain aster, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant typically grows in rocky or mountainous areas, thriving in well-drained soils. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are often erect and branched.

Astrostemma spartioides is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
