Arenaria setifera

Arenaria setifera, commonly known as the silky sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to arctic and alpine regions.

It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to cold climates. The plant features small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in early summer. Its leaves are typically covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Arenaria setifera plays a role in stabilizing soil in harsh environments and is often found in tundra and mountainous areas.
