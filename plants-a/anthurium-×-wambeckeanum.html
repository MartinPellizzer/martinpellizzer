Anthurium × wambeckeanum

Anthurium × wambeckeanum, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a hybrid plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are usually dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces vibrant red spathes that enclose small, spadix-like flowers, which are typical of the Araceae family. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect sunlight to flourish.

Anthurium × wambeckeanum is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
