Anneslia myriophylla

Anneslia myriophylla, commonly known as the mountain glory, is a striking perennial plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its vibrant yellow flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces numerous small, tubular flowers arranged in a compact cluster. Anneslia myriophylla thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and alpine settings.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long-lasting blooms and unique appearance.
