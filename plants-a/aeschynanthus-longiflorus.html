Aeschynanthus longiflorus

Aeschynanthus longiflorus, commonly known as the long-flowered ivy.

It is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly in countries like Kenya and Tanzania. The plant is characterized by its trailing growth habit and vibrant, tubular flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are typically oval-shaped and have a glossy appearance.

Aeschynanthus longiflorus is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and showy blooms.
