Aubrieta parviflora

Aubrieta parviflora, commonly known as the rockcress, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces clusters of small, bright yellow flowers. The plant typically blooms in early spring and is well-suited for rock gardens or borders due to its compact size. Its leaves are basal, glossy, and dark green, often covered in a fine layer of white hairs.

Aubrieta parviflora is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it an excellent choice for xeriscaping.
