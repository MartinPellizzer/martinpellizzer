Astrotricha pauciflora

Astrotricha pauciflora, commonly known as the few-flowered starwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Himalayas.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads and fibrous root system. The plant features small, daisy-like flowers with white petals and yellow centers, blooming in the summer months. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, adapting to the harsh alpine environment.

Astrotricha pauciflora is often found in rocky soils and is adapted to cold, arid conditions.
