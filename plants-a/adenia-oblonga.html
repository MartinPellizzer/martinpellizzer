Adenia oblonga

Adenia oblonga, commonly known as the African star apple, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height. The plant produces striking yellow or orange flowers that are clustered in umbels, followed by edible, star-shaped fruits. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a rough texture.

The plant is adapted to thrive in well-drained soils and requires a warm, humid climate for optimal growth.
