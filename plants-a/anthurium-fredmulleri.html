Anthurium fredmulleri

Anthurium fredmulleri, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Colombia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped, crimson-colored spathe that surrounds a spadix, which is typically yellow or green. The plant produces aerial roots and thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light. It prefers well-draining soil and consistent moisture, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Its striking appearance makes it a sought-after ornamental plant in horticulture.
