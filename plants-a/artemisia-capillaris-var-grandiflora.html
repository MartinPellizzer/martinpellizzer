Artemisia capillaris var. grandiflora

Artemisia capillaris var. grandiflora, commonly known as Chinese wormwood, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It features silver-gray foliage with a strong, aromatic scent due to its high concentration of volatile oils. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy ornamental plant.

This variety is often used in traditional medicine and as a garden plant for its ornamental and functional properties.
