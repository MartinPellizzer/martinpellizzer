Antidesma rugosum

Antidesma rugosum, commonly known as the rough antidesma, is a shrub or small tree native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its rough, often thorny bark and opposite, elliptical leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers followed by orange to red berries that are an important food source for various animals.
