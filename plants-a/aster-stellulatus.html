Aster stellulatus

Aster stellulatus, commonly known as the starflower, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant produces small, star-shaped blossoms that range in color from white to purple, depending on the variety. Aster stellulatus typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in woodland or prairie habitats.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, providing nectar for pollinators.
