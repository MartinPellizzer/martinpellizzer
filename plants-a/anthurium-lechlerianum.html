Anthurium lechlerianum

Anthurium lechlerianum, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a reddish tint. The plant produces vibrant red spadices, which are the showy parts that house the actual flowers. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Anthurium lechlerianum is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
