Astragalus rubromarginatus subsp. oeroilanicus

Astragalus rubromarginatus subsp.

oeroilanicus, commonly known as the red-margin milkvetch, is a flowering plant species belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae. It is characterized by its distinctive red-veined leaves and yellow pea-like flowers.

Native to specific regions in Central Asia, this subspecies thrives in arid and semi-arid environments. The plant has small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds, typical of the Fabaceae family.

It plays a role in soil stabilization and is of interest in ecological and botanical studies.
