Aquilegia canadensis var. aurea

Aquilegia canadensis var. aurea, commonly known as the golden bellflower, is a flowering plant native to eastern North America.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange.
