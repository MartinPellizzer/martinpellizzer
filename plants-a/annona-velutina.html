Annona velutina

Annona velutina, commonly known as velvet annona, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is a small to medium-sized tree or shrub that typically grows up to 5 meters in height. The plant features smooth, dark green leaves and produces large, yellowish-white flowers with a strong, unpleasant odor. Its fruit is a rough, spiny berry that ripens to a yellow or orange color.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in some indigenous cultures.
