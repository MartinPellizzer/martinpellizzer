Armeria alpina f. nana

Armeria alpina f. nana, commonly known as the alpine thrift, is a low-growing, mat-forming plant native to alpine regions of Europe.

It features compact, silver-green foliage with dense, upright clusters of white or pink flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant is characterized by its succulent leaves and ability to thrive in poor, rocky soils. It is well adapted to cold climates and is often found in mountainous areas.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and hardiness in garden settings.
