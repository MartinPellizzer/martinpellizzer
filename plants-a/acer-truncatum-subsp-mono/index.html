Acer Truncatum Subsp. Mono

Acer truncatum subsp. mono is a rare and unique maple species native to the mountainous regions of central and southern China. This subspecies is distinguished by its striking foliage and distinctive leaf shape, which sets it apart from other maple varieties. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in forested areas with well-drained soil. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species. Its beauty and ecological significance make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists alike.
