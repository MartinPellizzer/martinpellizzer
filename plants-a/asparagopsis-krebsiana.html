Asparagopsis krebsiana

Asparagopsis krebsiana, commonly known as the red dwarf lily, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its compact, evergreen growth habit. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Its foliage consists of narrow, strap-like leaves that are often used in horticultural arrangements.

Asparagopsis krebsiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to care for in garden settings.
