Alternanthera Pungens Var. Leiantha

Alternanthera pungens var. leiantha is a striking aquatic plant known for its vibrant red and pink foliage. It thrives in tropical and subtropical freshwater environments, often found in slow-moving waters and shaded areas. This variant is prized by aquascapers for its aesthetic appeal and ease of care. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, adding a dramatic touch to any planted tank. With proper lighting and nutrient balance, it can flourish and enhance the visual depth of an aquarium setting.
