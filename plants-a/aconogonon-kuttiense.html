Aconogonon kuttiense

Aconogonon kuttiense, commonly known as the Kuttanad morning glory, is a flowering plant native to the wetlands of Kerala, India.

It is a member of the Convolvulaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers that bloom in the early morning. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces tendrils that help it climb and spread across vegetation. It thrives in moist, tropical climates and is often found near water bodies.
