Asteriscus stenophyllus var. filifolius

Asteriscus stenophyllus var. filifolius, commonly known as the threadleaf asteriscus, is a perennial shrub native to the southwestern United States.

It features narrow, linear leaves that give it its common name, and it produces small, clustered flowers in shades of purple or pink. The plant is adapted to arid environments and thrives in well-drained soils. It is often used in xeriscaping due to its low water requirements and ornamental value.

The species is also valued for its role in supporting local pollinators.
