Anathallis duplooyi

Anathallis duplooyi, commonly known as the orchid mantis, is a unique epiphytic orchid native to the rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its striking resemblance to an orchid, with its green, leaf-like structures and delicate, flower-like appendages. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, typically growing on trees or rocks. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, often green or brown in color, which helps it blend in with its surroundings.

Anathallis duplooyi plays a role in its ecosystem by providing nectar to certain insect species, despite its deceptive appearance.
