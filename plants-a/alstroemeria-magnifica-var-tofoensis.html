Alstroemeria magnifica var. tofoensis

Alstroemeria magnifica var. tofoensis, commonly known as the Tofo alstroemeria, is a striking South American plant native to Bolivia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange, often with contrasting black veins. The plant produces sword-shaped leaves that are dark green and glossy, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

This variety is valued for its unique appearance and is often used in floral arrangements.
