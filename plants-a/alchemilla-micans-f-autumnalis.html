Alchemilla micans f. autumnalis

Alchemilla micans f. autumnalis, commonly known as autumnal ladies' mantle, is a low-growing perennial plant native to Europe.

It features broad, silver-green leaves that are often covered in a fine, fuzzy layer, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in late summer to early autumn, which are clustered in loose, upright inflorescences. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas or along stream banks.

Its foliage remains attractive throughout the growing season, making it a valuable addition to ornamental gardens.
