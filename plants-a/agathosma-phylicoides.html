Agathosma phylicoides

Agathosma phylicoides, commonly known as African geranium, is a small, evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It features narrow, aromatic leaves and produces small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in spring. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and is often used in rock gardens or as a border plant. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, requiring minimal water once established.

Agathosma phylicoides is also valued for its essential oils, which are used in aromatherapy and traditional medicine.
