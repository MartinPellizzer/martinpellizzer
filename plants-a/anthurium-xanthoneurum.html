Anthurium xanthoneurum

Anthurium xanthoneurum, commonly known as the yellow-veined anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves with prominent yellow veins, which give it its common name. The plant produces vibrant spathes that range from red to orange, often with a contrasting color on the inner surface. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.

Anthurium xanthoneurum is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and long-lasting blooms.
