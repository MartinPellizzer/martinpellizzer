Asplenium platyneuron f. multifidum

Asplenium platyneuron f. multifidum, commonly known as the multifidum spleenwort, is a fern species belonging to the family Aspleniaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds that give it a lacy appearance. This fern typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rock crevices. The fronds are usually evergreen, with a dark green color and a slightly glossy surface.

It is a relatively small plant, typically reaching heights of up to 15 centimeters.
