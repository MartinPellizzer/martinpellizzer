Anthurium tikalense

Anthurium tikalense, commonly known as the Tikalense arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to Central America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces a spadix inflorescence surrounded by a colorful spathe, which is typical of the genus Anthurium. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in botanical gardens.
