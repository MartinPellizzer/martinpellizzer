Adnaria resinosa

Adnaria resinosa, commonly known as the resinous adnaria, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the family Apocynaceae and is characterized by its woody stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces a milky latex sap, which is one of its distinguishing features. Adnaria resinosa typically grows in coastal areas and thrives in well-drained soils.
