Angelica cryptotaeniifolia

Angelica cryptotaeniifolia, commonly known as the alpine angelica, is a rare and slender perennial plant native to the high alpine regions of Europe.

It features finely divided, fern-like leaves that are adapted to withstand cold and harsh environmental conditions. The plant produces small, white to greenish-yellow flowers arranged in a dense umbel, which bloom in late summer. Its roots are thick and fleshy, often containing aromatic compounds.

Angelica cryptotaeniifolia is valued for its medicinal properties and is considered a symbol of resilience in alpine ecosystems.
