Anthurium albertiae

Anthurium albertiae, commonly known as the Albert's arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to Central America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are often brightly colored, ranging from deep green to red or purple. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-bearing inflorescences that are surrounded by a colorful spathe, typical of the Araceae family. It thrives in humid, warm environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium albertiae is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
