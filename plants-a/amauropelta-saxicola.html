Amauropelta saxicola

Amauropelta saxicola, commonly known as the saxicola shield fern, is a small, evergreen fern native to the southeastern United States.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically found in rocky or wooded areas. The plant has a compact, low-growing habit with a dense rosette of leathery, dark green leaves. Its fronds are divided into numerous narrow segments, giving it a distinctive, shield-like appearance.

Amauropelta saxicola is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens with suitable humidity and light conditions.
