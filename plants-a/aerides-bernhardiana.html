Aerides bernhardiana

Aerides bernhardiana, commonly known as the Bernhardiana orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its slender, upright stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers with prominent purple markings. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil.

Aerides bernhardiana is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
