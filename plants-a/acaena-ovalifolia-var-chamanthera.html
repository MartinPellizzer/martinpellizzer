Acaena ovalifolia var. chamanthera

Acaena ovalifolia var. chamanthera, commonly known as the Chamanthera burrplant, is a low-growing shrub native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its dense, spiny foliage and small, woolly leaves that are oval in shape. The plant produces bright yellow flowers in spring, followed by spherical, prickly fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its unique morphology and ecological role, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
