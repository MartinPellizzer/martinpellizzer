Aloe leachii

Aloe leachii, commonly known as the Cape aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, upright stems with a smooth, grayish-green bark and produces clusters of bright red flowers during the summer months. The plant has thick, fleshy leaves that store water, a characteristic adaptation to its arid habitat. Aloe leachii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.

It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.
