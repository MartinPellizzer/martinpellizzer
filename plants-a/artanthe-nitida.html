Artanthe nitida

Artanthe nitida, commonly known as the slender artanthe, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It features slender, upright stems with narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, silky hairs. The plant produces small, white to pale purple flowers arranged in dense, spike-like clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Artanthe nitida is often cultivated for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
