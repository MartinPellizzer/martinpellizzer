Arundinella mukurthiana

Arundinella mukurthiana, commonly known as the Mukurthi bamboo, is a flowering plant native to the Indian subcontinent.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and narrow leaves. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences that are often inconspicuous compared to its vegetative structure. It thrives in moist, tropical environments and is often found in wetlands and riverine areas.

Its growth habit makes it an important component of the local flora in its native habitat.
