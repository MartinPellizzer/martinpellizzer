Anthurium cerrocampanense

Anthurium cerrocampanense, commonly known as the Cerro Campanense arum, is a striking tropical plant native to the mountainous regions of Ecuador.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped leaves and distinctive spadix inflorescence. The plant typically thrives in humid, shaded environments with well-draining soil. Its foliage is often a deep green with a glossy texture, adding to its ornamental value.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and sought-after species among collectors.
