Acalypha macrostachya f. macrophylla

Acalypha macrostachya f. macrophylla, commonly known as the broad-leafed spurflower, is a striking tropical plant characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and vibrant, spiky inflorescences.

Native to Southeast Asia, it thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests. The plant's foliage is notable for its glossy texture and deep green color, while its flowers range in color from red to orange, adding visual interest. It is a popular ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and ability to thrive in indoor settings with proper care.

The species is also valued for its potential ecological benefits, including attracting pollinators and supporting local biodiversity.
