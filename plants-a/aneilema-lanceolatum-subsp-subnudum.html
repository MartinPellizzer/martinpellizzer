Aneilema lanceolatum subsp. subnudum

Aneilema lanceolatum subsp.

subnudum, commonly known as the naked-stemmed arrowroot, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical Africa. It belongs to the family Araceae and is characterized by its arrow-shaped leaves and distinctive, spadix-like inflorescence.
