Artemisia wudanica

Artemisia wudanica, commonly known as Wudan wormwood, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, silvery-gray foliage. The plant produces small, yellowish flower heads that are clustered in composite inflorescences. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and have a strong, camphor-like scent.

Artemisia wudanica is often used in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.
