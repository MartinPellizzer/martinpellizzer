Athyrium silvicola var. kinokuniense

Athyrium silvicola var. kinokuniense, commonly known as the Japanese shield fern, is a variant of the broadleaf fern native to Japan.

It is characterized by its distinctive, broadly ovate to elliptical fronds that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant features a short, creeping rhizome and produces spores in clusters on the underside of its fronds. It thrives in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky slopes.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for cultivation in shaded garden settings.
