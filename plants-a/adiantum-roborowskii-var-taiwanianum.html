Adiantum roborowskii var. taiwanianum

Adiantum roborowskii var. taiwanianum, commonly known as the Taiwan maidenhair fern, is a delicate fern species native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the family Adiantaceae and is characterized by its finely divided, feathery fronds that resemble lace. The plant prefers cool, moist environments and is often found in shaded, rocky habitats. Its leaves are typically bright green and have a slender, arching growth habit.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
