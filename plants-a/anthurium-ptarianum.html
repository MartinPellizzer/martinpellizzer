Anthurium ptarianum

Anthurium ptarianum, commonly known as the velvet leaf anthurium, is a striking tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a velvety texture. The plant produces vibrant red spathes that enclose bright yellow spadices, creating a dramatic floral display. Anthurium ptarianum thrives in humid, warm environments and requires indirect light to flourish.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical landscapes.
