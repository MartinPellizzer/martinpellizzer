Abelia spathulata f. micrantha

Abelia spathulata f. micrantha, commonly known as the narrow-leaved abelia, is a compact, deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically a dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white to pinkish flowers in the summer, which are attractive to pollinators. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained, moist soil.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
