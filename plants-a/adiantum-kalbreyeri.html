Adiantum kalbreyeri

Adiantum kalbreyeri, commonly known as the Kalbreyer's maidenhair fern, is a species of fern native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds that resemble those of other maidenhair ferns. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are usually bright green and have a leathery texture.

Adiantum kalbreyeri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens.
