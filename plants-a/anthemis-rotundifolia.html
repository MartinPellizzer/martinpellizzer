Anthemis rotundifolia

Anthemis rotundifolia, commonly known as roundleaf pyrethrum, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant features rounded, hairy leaves and produces small, spherical seed heads that are often used in ornamental gardening. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

The plant is also valued for its pyrethrin content, which has natural insect repellent properties.
