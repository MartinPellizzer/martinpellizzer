Agrostis bodaibensis

Agrostis bodaibensis, commonly known as the Bodaibens grass, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family Poaceae.

It is native to the Arabian Peninsula, particularly found in the United Arab Emirates. This perennial grass is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often growing in sandy or rocky soils.

The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny flowers, typical of many grass species.
