Androcymbium crenulatum

Androcymbium crenulatum, commonly known as the zigzag lily, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded habitats and has lance-shaped leaves with a distinctive zigzag pattern along the edges. Its blooming period occurs during the spring and early summer months.

Androcymbium crenulatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage and delicate flowers.
