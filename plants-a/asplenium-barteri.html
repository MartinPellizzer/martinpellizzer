Asplenium barteri

Asplenium barteri, commonly known as the Barter's spleenwort, is a small evergreen fern belonging to the family Aspleniaceae.

It is characterized by its compact, clustered growth habit and dark green, leathery fronds. The plant typically features two types of leaves: sterile, spore-bearing leaves and fertile, spore-producing leaves. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found on rocks or tree trunks in tropical and subtropical regions.

Asplenium barteri is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its low maintenance and ornamental appeal.
