Aloe capitata var. capitata

Aloe capitata var. capitata, commonly known as the cannonball aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense rosette of fleshy, gray-green leaves that are arranged in a compact, spherical shape. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing yellow flowers, which bloom in summer. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice for both ornamental and medicinal purposes.
