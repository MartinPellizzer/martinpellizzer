Alocasia spectabilis

Alocasia spectabilis, commonly known as the giant arrowroot, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, arrowhead-shaped leaves that can grow up to 3 feet in length. The plant thrives in humid, shady environments and requires well-draining, fertile soil. Its rhizomatous growth habit makes it an excellent choice for indoor cultivation or as a houseplant.

Alocasia spectabilis is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in tropical landscaping.
