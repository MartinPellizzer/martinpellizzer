Anthemis pallida

Anthemis pallida, commonly known as the pale everlasting, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant produces small, pale yellow to white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It is often found in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to Mediterranean climates.

Its foliage is aromatic and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
