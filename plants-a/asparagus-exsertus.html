Asparagus exsertus

Asparagus exsertus, commonly known as the slender asparagus, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant features thin, erect stems with small, scale-like leaves. Its flowers are usually yellow or greenish-yellow, arranged in clusters at the base of the stems.

Asparagus exsertus is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
