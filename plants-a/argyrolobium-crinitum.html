Argyrolobium crinitum

Argyrolobium crinitum, commonly known as the silver-haired burr.

It is a flowering plant native to southern Africa, characterized by its hairy, silver-gray foliage and unique seed pods. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
