Argyrocome cotula

Argyrocome cotula, commonly known as silverleaf groundsel, is a small herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage. The plant produces small, daisy-like flowers that are usually yellow or white in color. It has a compact growth habit and is often found in open grasslands and along rocky outcrops.

Argyrocome cotula is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
