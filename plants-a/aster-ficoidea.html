Aster ficoidea

Aster ficoidea, commonly known as the fuzzy aster, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the eastern United States.

It features showy, daisy-like flowers with purple to pink petals and a yellow center, blooming in late summer to early fall. The plant has hairy, gray-green foliage that gives it a distinctive, fuzzy appearance. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Aster ficoidea is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
