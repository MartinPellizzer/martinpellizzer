Aloe ngongensis

Aloe ngongensis, commonly known as the Ngong aloe, is a succulent plant native to Kenya.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a waxy coating. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers on tall, branching inflorescences. It is adapted to arid environments and stores water in its leaves for survival.

Aloe ngongensis is often used in traditional medicine and as an ornamental plant.
