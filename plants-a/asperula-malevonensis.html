Asperula malevonensis

Asperula malevonensis, commonly known as the malevonensis shrub, is a species of flowering plant in the Heath family (Ericaceae).

It is native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia, particularly in countries like Vietnam and Laos. The plant is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of small, bell-shaped white or pink flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and is often found in forest understories.

Asperula malevonensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its hardy nature and aesthetic qualities.
