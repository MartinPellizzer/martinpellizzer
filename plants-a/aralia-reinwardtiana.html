Aralia reinwardtiana

Aralia reinwardtiana, commonly known as the giant aralia, is a striking plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, palm-like leaves that can grow up to 3 meters in length, making it a dramatic addition to any landscape. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers that are followed by bright red berries, which are an important food source for various wildlife. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in rainforests.

Due to its size and ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
