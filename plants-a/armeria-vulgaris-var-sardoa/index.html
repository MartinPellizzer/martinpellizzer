Armeria Vulgaris Var. Sardoa

Armeria vulgaris var. sardoa is a striking flowering plant native to the coastal regions of southern Europe. Known for its dense clusters of pink or white flowers, it thrives in sandy or gravelly soils. This variety is particularly hardy, able to withstand harsh maritime conditions and salty winds. Often used in coastal gardens for its ornamental value and resilience. It is a favorite among gardeners seeking a low-maintenance yet visually appealing plant.
