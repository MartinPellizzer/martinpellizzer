Anemone patens subvar. taraoi

Anemone patens subvar. taraoi, commonly known as the Japanese anemone, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Japan.

It features showy, white to pink flower heads that bloom in late summer to early autumn. The plant has a basal rosette of heart-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are typically arranged in a single cluster at the end of each stem.

It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.
