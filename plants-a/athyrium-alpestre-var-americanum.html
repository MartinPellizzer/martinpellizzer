Athyrium alpestre var. americanum

Athyrium alpestre var. americanum, commonly known as the American lady fern, is a deciduous fern native to the eastern United States.

It features delicate, bipinnate fronds that are typically light green with a slightly curled appearance. The plant thrives in shady, moist environments and prefers well-drained, acidic soils. It is often found in woodland areas and along stream banks.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and conservation efforts.
