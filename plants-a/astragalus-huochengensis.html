Astragalus huochengensis

Astragalus huochengensis, commonly known as the Huocheng milkvetch, is a flowering plant species native to China.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its hairy stems and small, yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its root system is known for its medicinal properties, often used in traditional Chinese medicine.

This species plays a significant role in its native ecosystem, contributing to soil stabilization and supporting local biodiversity.
