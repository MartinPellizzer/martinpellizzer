Arachis vallsii

Arachis vallsii, commonly known as the Brazilian peanut or wild peanut, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family Fabaceae.

It is native to South America, particularly Brazil, and is characterized by its small, yellow flowers and underground pods containing edible seeds. Arachis vallsii is also noted for its ornamental value and potential agricultural significance.
