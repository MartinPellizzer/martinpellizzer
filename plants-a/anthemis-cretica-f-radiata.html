Anthemis cretica f. radiata

Anthemis cretica f. radiata, commonly known as the radiate anthemis, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers, which are typically arranged in a radiate pattern, giving it its common name. The plant is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in well-drained soils. It produces small, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional herbal medicine.
