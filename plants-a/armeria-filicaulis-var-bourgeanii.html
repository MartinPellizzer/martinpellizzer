Armeria filicaulis var. bourgeanii

Armeria filicaulis var. bourgeanii, commonly known as the silverleaf armeria, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features dense clusters of small, white to pinkish flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has basal rosettes of narrow, silvery-gray leaves that are covered in fine, soft hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its ornamental value comes from its compact form and delicate floral display.
