Athyrium galeottii

Athyrium galeottii, commonly known as the golden fern, is a species of flowering plant in the family Dryopteridaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, feather-like fronds that are typically bright green with a subtle golden hue. Native to the southeastern United States, this fern thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for shady garden landscapes.
