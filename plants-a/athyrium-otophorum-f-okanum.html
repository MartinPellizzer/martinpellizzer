Athyrium otophorum f. okanum

Athyrium otophorum f. okanum, commonly known as the Okanum Japanese Shield Fern, is a fern species native to Japan.

It belongs to the Dryopteridaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped fronds with a distinctive, shield-like appearance. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its rhizomes are creeping, allowing it to spread and form dense colonies.

The fronds are usually dark green and have a finely serrated margin, contributing to its ornamental appeal.
