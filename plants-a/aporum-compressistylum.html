Aporum compressistylum

Aporum compressistylum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved aporum, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family, which includes plants like philodendrons and calla lilies. The plant is characterized by its elongated, narrow leaves that are often a deep green with a glossy texture. Its inflorescence consists of a spadix surrounded by a modified leaf called a spathe, typical of the Araceae family.

Aporum compressistylum is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its striking foliage and unique floral structure.
