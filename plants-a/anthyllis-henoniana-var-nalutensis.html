Anthyllis henoniana var. nalutensis

Anthyllis henoniana var. nalutensis, commonly known as the Nalut anthyllis, is a flowering plant native to the Balkan region.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its upright growth habit and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in early summer, attracting pollinators such as bees. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or semi-arid environments.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and locally endemic species.
