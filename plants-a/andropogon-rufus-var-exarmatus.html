Andropogon rufus var. exarmatus

Andropogon rufus var. exarmatus, commonly known as the slender bluestem, is a grass species native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its narrow, blue-green leaves and compact, cylindrical inflorescence. The plant typically grows in open woodlands and prairies, preferring well-drained soils. It plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various wildlife species.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for native plant gardens.
