Anthurium andraeanum var. louisae

Anthurium andraeanum var. louisae, commonly known as the Red Star Anthurium, is a tropical flowering plant native to the Amazon basin.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped, glossy dark green leaves and its vibrant red spathes that enclose the spadix. The plant produces showy, long-lasting flowers that are typically red or orange in color. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

This variety is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in indoor settings.
