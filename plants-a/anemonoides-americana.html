Anemonoides americana

Anemonoides americana, commonly known as the American anemone, is a herbaceous perennial native to North America.

It features showy, single or double flowers with white to pink petals and yellow centers, typically blooming in early spring. The plant has a short, hairy stem that supports its clustered leaves, which are heart-shaped and serrated. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable addition to native plant landscapes.
