Aerides odorata var. pallida

Aerides odorata var. pallida, commonly known as the pale orchid, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, pale pink or white flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to tree bark in humid tropical environments. Its leaves are elongated and strap-like, and it produces fragrant blooms during the warmer months.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value in horticulture due to its elegant appearance and unique fragrance.
