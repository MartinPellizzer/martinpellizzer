Armeria alliacea var. javalambrica

Armeria alliacea var. javalambrica, commonly known as the Java silver bush, is a low-growing, evergreen shrub native to the mountainous regions of Java.

It features dense clusters of small, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in spring and summer. The plant has succulent, silver-gray foliage that is both ornamental and drought-tolerant. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or coastal landscapes.

Its compact growth habit and attractive appearance make it a popular choice for ornamental horticulture.
