Acacia swazica

Acacia swazica, commonly known as Swazi thornveld acacia, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky soils. It plays a role in soil stabilization and provides habitat for various insects and small animals.

Its thorny stems offer protection against herbivores.
