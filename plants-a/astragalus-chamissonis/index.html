Astragalus Chamissonis

Astragalus chamissonis is a delicate flowering plant native to the western united states. it belongs to the legume family and is known for its small, pea-like flowers. this species thrives in rocky and sandy soils, often found in coastal and mountainous regions. it plays an important role in its ecosystem by providing nectar for pollinators. due to habitat loss, it is considered a vulnerable species requiring conservation efforts.
