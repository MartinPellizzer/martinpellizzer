Adesmia punctata var. punctata

Adesmia punctata var. punctata, commonly known as the punctate burr legume, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in dry, arid environments. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters, followed by distinctive, spiny pods. Its leaves are compound, consisting of several small, oval-shaped leaflets.

The species is valued for its hardy nature and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
