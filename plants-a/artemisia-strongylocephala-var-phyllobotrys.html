Artemisia strongylocephala var. phyllobotrys

Artemisia strongylocephala var. phyllobotrys, commonly known as the narrow-leaf mugwort, is a perennial shrub native to southern Africa.

It features aromatic, gray-green leaves that are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in dense, spherical clusters called cymes. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

This variety is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
