Aristolochia kanukuensis

Aristolochia kanukuensis, commonly known as the Kanuku Dutchman's pipe, is a flowering plant native to the rainforests of Central Africa.

It is characterized by its unique, pipe-shaped flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color. The plant produces heart-shaped leaves with a rough texture and emits a mild, earthy scent. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in the understory of dense tropical forests.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal potential, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens.
