Aetheria

Aetheria, commonly known as the celestial orchid, is a rare and exotic plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Andes.

It is characterized by its striking, iridescent flowers that range in color from deep violet to shimmering blue. The plant has a delicate, fern-like foliage that thrives in cool, humid environments. Aetheria's unique ability to absorb and reflect light gives it its otherworldly appearance.
