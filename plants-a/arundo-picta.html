Arundo picta

Arundo picta, commonly known as the variegated reed, is a striking ornamental grass native to the eastern United States.

It features tall, slender stalks with striking variegated leaves that display shades of green, white, and yellow. The plant thrives in moist to wet soils and is often found near water bodies such as ponds and streams. Its foliage adds a dramatic visual element to landscapes, making it a popular choice for water gardens and wetland restoration projects.

Arundo picta is also valued for its ability to stabilize soil and provide habitat for wildlife.
