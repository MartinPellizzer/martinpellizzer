Anthurium carneospadix

Anthurium carneospadix, commonly known as the red spadix anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, spadix-shaped inflorescence, which is typically bright red or orange in color. The plant features large, heart-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and requires consistent moisture in its soil.

Anthurium carneospadix is often cultivated for its unique floral structure and is a popular choice among plant enthusiasts.
