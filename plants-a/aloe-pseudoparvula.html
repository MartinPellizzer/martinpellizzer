Aloe pseudoparvula

Aloe pseudoparvula, commonly known as the dwarf aloe or small aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features rosettes of narrow, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a slight bluish tint. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the warmer months, usually in summer. It is adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Aloe pseudoparvula is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its compact size and attractive appearance.
