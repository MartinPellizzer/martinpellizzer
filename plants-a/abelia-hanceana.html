Abelia hanceana

Abelia hanceana, commonly known as Chinese abelia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features showy, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink and red, blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has opposite, ovate to elliptic leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers moist, well-drained soil.

Abelia hanceana is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various garden conditions.
