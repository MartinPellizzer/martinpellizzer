Astragalus kubensis

Astragalus kubensis, commonly known as Kuben's milkvetch, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to the arid and semi-arid regions of Central Asia, particularly Kazakhstan. The plant is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellowish to reddish flowers that bloom in late spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to harsh environmental conditions.

Astragalus kubensis plays a role in stabilizing soil and supporting local biodiversity in its native habitat.
