Anisolepis pyrethrum

Anisolepis pyrethrum, commonly known as the fireweed, is a flowering plant native to the southern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant produces yellow to orange flowers that bloom in late summer and early fall. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in full sun to partial shade.

Anisolepis pyrethrum is known for its hardy nature and ability to colonize open areas quickly.
