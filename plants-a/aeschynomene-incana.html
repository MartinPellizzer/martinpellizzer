Aeschynomene incana

Aeschynomene incana, commonly known as silverleaf desmodium, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in dense clusters, followed by small, oblong seed pods. It is often found in disturbed soils and is sometimes used in erosion control due to its rapid growth.

Aeschynomene incana is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
