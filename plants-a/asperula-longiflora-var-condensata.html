Asperula longiflora var. condensata

Asperula longiflora var. condensata, commonly known as the dense-flowered woodruff, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe.

It features small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters during late spring to early summer. The plant has dark green, glossy leaves that are typically ovate to elliptic in shape. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

Its dense growth habit and ornamental flowers make it a valuable addition to shaded landscapes.
