Ambrosia elatior var. artemisiifolia

Ambrosia elatior var. artemisiifolia, commonly known as common ragweed, is an annual flowering plant native to North America.

It features finely divided, fern-like leaves and produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that appear in late summer. The plant is characterized by its extensive root system and its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types. It is often found in disturbed areas such as roadsides and fields.

Due to its pollen, it is a common allergen and can cause significant respiratory issues in sensitive individuals.
