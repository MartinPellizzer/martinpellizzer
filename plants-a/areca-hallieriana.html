Areca hallieriana

Areca hallieriana, commonly known as the Hallier's areca, is a species of palm native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and feather-like leaves that can reach several meters in length. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by clustered fruits containing seeds. It thrives in tropical climates with high humidity and well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
