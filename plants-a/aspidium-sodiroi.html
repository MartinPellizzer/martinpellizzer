Aspidium sodiroi

Aspidium sodiroi, commonly known as the Sodiro's water fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, pinnate fronds that are often covered in fine, silky hairs. Native to tropical and subtropical regions, this plant thrives in moist, shaded environments. Its rhizomes are typically creeping and produce numerous erect, branching fronds.

The plant is often found in wetlands, stream banks, and other humid habitats where it plays a role in stabilizing soil and supporting local biodiversity.
