Anthurium caperatum

Anthurium caperatum, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a tropical flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves and vibrant red spathes that enclose the small, spadix-like inflorescence. The plant thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect sunlight to flourish. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical landscapes.

Anthurium caperatum is also noted for its ability to purify air, adding to its appeal as a decorative and beneficial plant.
