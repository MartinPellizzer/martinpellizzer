Alsobia dianthiflora

Alsobia dianthiflora, commonly known as the silverleaf gesneria, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its striking silver-white foliage and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows as a compact shrub with opposite, ovate leaves that are covered in fine, silvery hairs. Its flowers, which bloom in shades of pink to purple, are adapted for pollination by hummingbirds and bees.

Alsobia dianthiflora is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its unique appearance and ability to thrive in shaded, humid environments.
