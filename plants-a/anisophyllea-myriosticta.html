Anisophyllea myriosticta

Anisophyllea myriosticta, commonly known as the broad-leafed anisophyllea, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, elliptical leaves that are usually smooth and glossy. The plant typically grows as an understory species in humid, shaded forest environments. Its leaves are often marked with distinct venation patterns, which contribute to its ornamental value.

Anisophyllea myriosticta is valued in horticulture for its aesthetic appeal and ability to thrive in partial shade.
