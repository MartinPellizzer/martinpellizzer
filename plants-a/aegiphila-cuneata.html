Aegiphila cuneata

Aegiphila cuneata, commonly known as the narrow-leafed aegiphila, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Brazil.

Aegiphila cuneata is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine by local communities.

Aegiphila cuneata has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem, with a dark green upper surface and a lighter underside.
