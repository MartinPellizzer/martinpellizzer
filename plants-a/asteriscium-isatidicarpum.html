Asteriscium isatidicarpum

Asteriscium isatidicarpum, commonly known as the Indian aster, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayas.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant produces small, pink to purple florets arranged in a compact, star-like cluster. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation in temperate climates.
