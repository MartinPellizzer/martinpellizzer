Alternanthera peruviana

Alternanthera peruviana, commonly known as the red flame plant, is a tropical aquatic plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red or pink leaves, which can change color based on light exposure and nutrient levels. The plant thrives in freshwater environments and is often used in aquariums for its ornamental value. It prefers moderate to high light conditions and can grow both submerged and as a floating plant.

Its rapid growth rate makes it a popular choice for pond and tank decoration.
