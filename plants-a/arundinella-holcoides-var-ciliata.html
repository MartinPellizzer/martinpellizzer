Arundinella holcoides var. ciliata

Arundinella holcoides var. ciliata, commonly known as the silky grass or silky bamboo, is a perennial grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, cylindrical stems that are often covered with fine, silky hairs. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spikelets, which are typical of the grass family. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a distinct sheath at the base.

This species is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its aesthetic appeal and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
