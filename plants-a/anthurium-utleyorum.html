Anthurium utleyorum

Anthurium utleyorum, commonly known as the Utleys' anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-like inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, typically in shades of red or orange. It thrives in humid, warm environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium utleyorum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant.
