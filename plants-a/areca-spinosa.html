Areca spinosa

Areca spinosa, commonly known as the areca palm, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and feathery, pinnate leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers followed by edible fruits that are often used in traditional medicine. Areca spinosa thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Its botanical features make it a popular ornamental plant in tropical gardens.
