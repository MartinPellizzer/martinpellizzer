Anthurium variable var. ottonianum

Anthurium variable var. ottonianum, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a reddish or purple tinge. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-shaped inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, which can range from white to deep red. It thrives in humid, warm environments with bright, indirect light.

This variety is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
