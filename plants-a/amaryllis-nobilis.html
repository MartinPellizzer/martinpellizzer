Amaryllis nobilis

Amaryllis nobilis, commonly known as the belladonna lily, is a bulbous plant native to South America.

It produces striking, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, typically blooming in late spring or early summer. The plant features broad, strap-like leaves that emerge from a central bulb and can grow up to several feet in height. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and indoor cultivation.

Amaryllis nobilis is also known for its ability to bloom reliably when cared for properly.
