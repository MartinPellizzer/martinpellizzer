Allosorus ciliatus var. laxus

Allosorus ciliatus var. laxus, commonly known as the slender hairroot, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia.

It features slender, hairy stems and small, lance-shaped leaves with ciliate margins. The plant produces clusters of tiny, pale yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Its fibrous roots are notable for their fine, thread-like structure.
