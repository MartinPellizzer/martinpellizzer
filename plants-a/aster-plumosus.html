Aster plumosus

Aster plumosus, commonly known as the silky aster, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its showy, purple to pink flower heads that bloom in late summer and early fall. The plant features finely divided, feathery leaves that give it a delicate, airy appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for native gardens.

Aster plumosus is also valued for its ecological benefits, providing nectar for pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
