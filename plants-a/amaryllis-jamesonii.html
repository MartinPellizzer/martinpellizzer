Amaryllis jamesonii

Amaryllis jamesonii, commonly known as the Jameson's amaryllis, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant produces large, strap-like leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Amaryllis jamesonii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ease of care.
