Ardisia carnosicaulis

Ardisia carnosicaulis, commonly known as the red-berried ardisia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 3 meters. The plant produces small, white or pinkish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its fruit is a bright red, fleshy berry that contains several seeds.

The leaves are glossy and oval-shaped, often with a slight waxy coating.
