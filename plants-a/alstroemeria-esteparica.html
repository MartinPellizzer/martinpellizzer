Alstroemeria esteparica

Alstroemeria esteparica, commonly known as the Estepa alstroemeria, is a flowering plant native to South America, specifically found in Argentina.

It belongs to the family Alstroemeriaceae and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that come in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry habitats, showcasing its adaptability to arid environments.

Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves, and it is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and floral arrangements.
