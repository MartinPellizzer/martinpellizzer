Acinodendron barbeyanum

Acinodendron barbeyanum, commonly known as the African velvet tree, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters. The tree produces woody capsules containing numerous small, black seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and inland forested areas.

Its unique appearance and ornamental value make it a popular plant in horticulture.
