Aster incertus

Aster incertus, commonly known as the uncertain aster, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and woodland edges. It produces showy purple to pink flowers that bloom in the summer months.

Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
