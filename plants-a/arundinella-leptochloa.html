Arundinella leptochloa

Arundinella leptochloa, commonly known as the slender grass.

It is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Pacific. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories or wetlands. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue, and it produces small, spike-like inflorescences.

Arundinella leptochloa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in horticulture for its aesthetic qualities.
