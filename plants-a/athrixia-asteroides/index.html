Athrixia Asteroides

African daisy, scientifically known as Athrixia asteroides, is a striking member of the Asteraceae family native to southern Africa. This resilient plant thrives in arid and semi-arid regions, showcasing its adaptability to harsh environments. Its vibrant yellow flowers, often with a red or orange center, make it a standout in gardens and natural landscapes. The plant's unique foliage, featuring silvery-white hairs, adds to its ornamental appeal. With its hardy nature and eye-catching blooms, the African daisy is both a valuable addition to native flora and a popular choice for landscaping.
