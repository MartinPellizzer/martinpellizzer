Anuragia stellata

Anuragia stellata, commonly known as the star-leaved daisy, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has finely divided, grass-like leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white pubescence. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Anuragia stellata is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and rockeries.
