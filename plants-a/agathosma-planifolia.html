Agathosma planifolia

Agathosma planifolia, commonly known as the Cape geranium, is a succulent shrub native to South Africa.

It features small, fleshy leaves that are typically gray-green in color, adapted to store water in arid environments. The plant produces clusters of tiny, pale pink to white flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Due to its hardy nature, it is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and low maintenance requirements.
