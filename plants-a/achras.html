Achras

Achras, commonly known as the breadfruit tree, is a tropical plant native to the Indo-Pacific region.

It belongs to the family Moraceae and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and smooth, pale-green bark. The plant is also valued for its ornamental qualities and ability to thrive in humid, tropical climates.
