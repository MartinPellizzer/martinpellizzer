Acer Longipes Var. Pubigerum

Acer longipes var. pubigerum is a rare and striking member of the maple family known for its unique foliage and ornamental appeal. Native to the mountainous regions of China, this variant thrives in cool, humid climates with well-drained soil. Its leaves are characterized by a distinctive pubescent texture, giving it a soft, fuzzy appearance that sets it apart from other maple species. The plant is valued for its aesthetic qualities and is often sought after by horticulturists and botanists alike. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively uncommon in cultivation, making it a prized addition to specialized gardens.
