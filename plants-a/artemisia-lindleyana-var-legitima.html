Artemisia lindleyana var. legitima

Artemisia lindleyana var. legitima, commonly known as blue mist shrub, is a perennial shrub native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its aromatic, silvery-blue foliage that resembles a misty cloud, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Its hardy nature and ornamental value make it a popular choice for drought-tolerant gardens.
