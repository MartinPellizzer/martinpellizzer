Aster bubonium

Aster bubonium, commonly known as the purple coneflower, is a perennial herb native to North America.

It features showy, daisy-like flowers with purple petals and a prominent yellow central disk. The plant has upright, branching stems covered in fine hairs, and its leaves are lance-shaped with serrated edges. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, often found in prairies and open woodlands.

Aster bubonium is valued for its ornamental beauty and its role as a nectar source for pollinators.
