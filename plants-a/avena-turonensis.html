Avena turonensis

Avena turonensis, commonly known as Turonian oat, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family Poaceae.

It is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in rocky, well-drained soils. The plant produces slender, upright stems with narrow, grass-like leaves, and it blooms with small, spike-like inflorescences. Its seeds are enclosed in bracts, and the plant is often found in coastal or mountainous areas.

Avena turonensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
