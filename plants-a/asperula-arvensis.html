Asperula arvensis

Asperula arvensis, commonly known as field asperula, is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in spring. The plant has leathery, oval-shaped leaves that are dark green and glossy. It thrives in well-drained soils and partial to full sun, making it a hardy ground cover.

Asperula arvensis is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to stabilize soil in rocky or disturbed areas.
