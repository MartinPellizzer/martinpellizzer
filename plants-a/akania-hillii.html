Akania hillii

Akania hillii, commonly known as the African snowbell, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa.

It is a small shrub that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant produces bell-shaped flowers that are usually white or pale blue, which bloom in spring. Its leaves are simple and opposite, often with a slightly hairy texture.

Akania hillii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
