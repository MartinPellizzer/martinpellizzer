Actinostemma lobatum f. semilobatum

Actinostemma lobatum f. semilobatum, commonly known as the semilobate actinostemma, is a variant of the Actinostemma lobatum species.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to certain regions of North America. The plant features lance-shaped leaves with a distinctive semilobed margin, which sets it apart from the typical form. Its flowers are small and clustered, typically appearing in shades of white or pale pink.

This subspecies is often found in moist, woodland environments and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role.
