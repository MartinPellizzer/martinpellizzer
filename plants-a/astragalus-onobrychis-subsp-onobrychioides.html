Astragalus onobrychis subsp. onobrychioides

Astragalus onobrychis subsp.

onobrychioides, commonly known as yellow bur pea, is a perennial legume native to southern Europe and western Asia. It is characterized by its yellow flowers and trifoliate leaves, which are typical of the Fabaceae family.

The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas.

This subspecies is noted for its ornamental value and its ability to fix nitrogen, making it beneficial for soil enrichment.
