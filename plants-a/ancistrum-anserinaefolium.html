Ancistrum anserinaefolium

Ancistrum anserinaefolium, commonly known as the silver feather plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Araceae.

Ancistrum anserinaefolium is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique foliage and ability to thrive in humid environments.
