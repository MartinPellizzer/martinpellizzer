Alsophila madagascarica

Alsophila madagascarica, commonly known as the Madagascar tree fern, is a species native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and a crown of feathery fronds. The plant has a robust rhizome that supports its growth and helps in nutrient absorption. Its fronds are typically dark green and can grow quite long, contributing to its ornamental value.

Alsophila madagascarica is well adapted to humid, tropical environments and thrives in shaded, moist conditions.
