Ardisia perreticulata

Ardisia perreticulata, commonly known as the red-berried ardisia, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are opposite in arrangement. The plant produces small, white flowers followed by bright red berries that are edible for humans and birds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Ardisia perreticulata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in gardens.
