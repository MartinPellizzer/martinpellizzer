Argyroderma delaetii

Argyroderma delaetii, commonly known as the silverleaf orchid, is a rare and striking member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the humid forests of Madagascar, where it thrives in shaded, moist environments. The plant is characterized by its unique silver-green leaves and delicate, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of white and pale yellow. It has a compact growth habit and is often found growing epiphytically on trees.
