Anotites debilis

Anotites debilis, commonly known as the slender waterwheel plant, is a floating aquatic plant native to the Americas.

It has long, narrow leaves that are arranged in whorls around its stem, giving it a distinctive wheel-like appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are typically borne on long, slender stalks. It thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments, often found in ponds and lakes.

Anotites debilis plays a role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat for small aquatic organisms.
