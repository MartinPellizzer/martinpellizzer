Aster molliusculus

Aster molliusculus, commonly known as the purple coneflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America.

It features showy, daisy-like flowers with purple petals surrounding a prominent yellow center. The plant has hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Aster molliusculus is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
