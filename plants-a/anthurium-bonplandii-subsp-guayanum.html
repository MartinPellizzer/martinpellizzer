Anthurium bonplandii subsp. guayanum

Anthurium bonplandii subsp.

guayanum, commonly known as the Guayana anthurium, is a tropical plant native to South America, particularly found in regions of Venezuela and Guyana. It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically deep green with a glossy texture.

The plant produces striking spadix inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, which can range from white to pink or red. It thrives in humid, warm environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and botanical collections.
