Argyrolobium prostratum

Argyrolobium prostratum, commonly known as the prostrate silverlobelia, is a low-growing perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features silver-gray foliage and produces small, showy blue or purple flowers during the spring and summer months. The plant has a sprawling growth habit, often forming dense mats on the ground. It is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, thriving in well-drained soils.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscape designs.
