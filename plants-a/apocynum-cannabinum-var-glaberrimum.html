Apocynum cannabinum var. glaberrimum

Apocynum cannabinum var. glaberrimum, commonly known as smooth dogbane, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is a member of the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its erect, branched stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, greenish-white flowers that are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It is typically found in moist soils along stream banks, wet meadows, and other disturbed sites.

Smooth dogbane is known for its milky sap, which contains toxic compounds and is used in traditional medicine.
