Aerides odorata subvar. immaculata

Aerides odorata subvar. immaculata, commonly known as the immaculate orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its upright growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale pink, often with a yellow lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil.

This subvariety is prized for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
