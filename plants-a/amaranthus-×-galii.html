Amaranthus × galii

Amaranthus × galii, commonly known as redroot amaranth, is a herbaceous annual plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and broad, lance-shaped leaves that are typically red-veined. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually green or reddish in color. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it adaptable to various environmental conditions.

Amaranthus × galii is often found in disturbed sites such as roadsides and fields.
