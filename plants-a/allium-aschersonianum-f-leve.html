Allium aschersonianum f. leve

Allium aschersonianum f. leve, commonly known as the purple stripe onion, is a botanical variant of the common onion.

It is characterized by its distinctive purple stripes on the bulb and leaves, which are more pronounced than in the typical variety. The plant is a member of the Amaryllidaceae family and is native to Central Asia. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it suitable for cultivation in temperate climates.
