Arum esculentum

Arum esculentum, commonly known as the arrowhead plant, is a herbaceous perennial native to wetlands and marshy areas in the Americas.

It features arrow-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a glossy texture. The plant produces striking spadices, which are violet or purple in color, surrounded by a showy white or green spathe. It thrives in moist, shady conditions and is often used in water gardens or as a decorative plant.

The plant is also valued for its ability to purify water and support aquatic ecosystems.
