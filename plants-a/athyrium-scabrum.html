Athyrium scabrum

Athyrium scabrum, commonly known as the rough shield fern, is a species of fern native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the family Athyriaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, shield-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky crevices. Its leaves are usually dark green and have a rough, textured surface, which gives it its common name.

Athyrium scabrum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and ability to thrive in partial shade.
