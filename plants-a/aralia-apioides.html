Aralia apioides

Aralia apioides, commonly known as the umbrella fern, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features a single, thick, unbranched stem that supports a cluster of pinnately compound leaves resembling an umbrella. The leaves are typically dark green and have a leathery texture. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers in umbrella-like clusters, followed by yellowish berries.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
