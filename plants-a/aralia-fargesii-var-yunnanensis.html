Aralia fargesii var. yunnanensis

Aralia fargesii var. yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan aralia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to the mountainous regions of southwestern China.

It is characterized by its compound leaves, which are often arranged in a feather-like pattern, and its clusters of small, white flowers that bloom in the spring. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in forested areas at elevations ranging from 1,500 to 3,000 meters.
