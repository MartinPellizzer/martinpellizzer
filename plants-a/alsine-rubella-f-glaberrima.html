Alsine rubella f. glaberrima

Alsine rubella f. glaberrima, commonly known as the glabrous alsine, is a flowering plant species belonging to the family Caryophyllaceae.

It is characterized by its low-growing, herbaceous habit and small, pink to white flowers. The plant typically thrives in moist, sandy soils and is often found in coastal or wetland habitats. Its leaves are usually smooth and glabrous, giving it its specific epithet.
