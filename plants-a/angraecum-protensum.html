Angraecum protensum

Angraecum protensum, commonly known as the Madagascar star orchid, is a unique epiphytic orchid native to Madagascar.

It features striking, star-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale pink, with a distinct central column. The plant has a long, slender stem and is known for its ability to thrive in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers are often fragrant and bloom at night, attracting specific pollinators like moths.

Angraecum protensum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and rarity.
