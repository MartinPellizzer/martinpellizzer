Acosta oviceps

Acosta oviceps, commonly known as the giant orchid, is a striking member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the Andean regions of South America, particularly in Ecuador and Peru. The plant is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can reach up to 30 centimeters in diameter. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, often with a waxy coating.

Acosta oviceps thrives in high-altitude environments with cool, moist conditions.
