Alchemilla hybrida var. intermedia

Alchemilla hybrida var. intermedia, commonly known as the velvetleaf lady's mantle, is a perennial plant belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, hairy foliage that gives it a soft, velvety appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

This cultivar is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive foliage and low maintenance requirements.
