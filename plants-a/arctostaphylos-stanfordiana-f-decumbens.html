Arctostaphylos stanfordiana f. decumbens

Arctostaphylos stanfordiana f. decumbens, commonly known as the decumbent manzanita, is a low-growing shrub native to California.

It features small, oval, glossy evergreen leaves that are typically reddish-brown in color. The plant produces clusters of white or pinkish flowers in the spring, which are followed by red berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to dry, sunny environments.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in local habitats.
