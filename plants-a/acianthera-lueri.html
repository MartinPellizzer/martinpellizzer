Acianthera lueri

Acianthera lueri, commonly known as the Luer's orchid, is a species of orchid native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces small, dark green leaves and blooms with pale pink to white flowers that have a distinctive, intricate structure. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and somewhat elusive species in cultivation.
