Arabis biennis

Arabis biennis, commonly known as the annual rockcress, is a flowering plant in the Brassicaceae family.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, typically found in rocky or sandy soils. The plant produces small, white or purple flowers in clusters, which bloom from spring to early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.

Arabis biennis is often used in rock gardens or as a wildflower due to its hardiness and aesthetic appeal.
