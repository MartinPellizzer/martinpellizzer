Amomum botryoideum

Amomum botryoideum, commonly known as the star anise plant, is a perennial herb native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its aromatic, star-shaped fruit, which is used in both culinary and medicinal applications. The plant belongs to the ginger family (Zingiberaceae) and features rhizomatous growth with lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are pale green and clustered in inflorescences, while the fruit develops in a unique, star-like shape.

The essential oils found in its fruit contribute to its distinctive flavor and therapeutic properties.
