Anthurium × rudolfianum

Anthurium × rudolfianum, commonly known as the Red-veined Anthurium, is a hybrid plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are deep green with prominent red veins. The plant produces vibrant spadices, which are its showy, spadix-shaped inflorescences, typically in shades of red or orange. It thrives in humid, warm environments with bright, indirect light.

Anthurium × rudolfianum is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
