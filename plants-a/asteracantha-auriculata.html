Asteracantha auriculata

Asteracantha auriculata, commonly known as the ear-shaped aster, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces bright yellow flowers with a distinctive, ear-like shape on the petals. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open grasslands and rocky areas.

Asteracantha auriculata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
