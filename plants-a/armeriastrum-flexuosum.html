Armeriastrum flexuosum

Armeriastrum flexuosum, commonly known as the curlleaf filaree, is a low-growing, herbaceous perennial native to the western United States.

It features small, white flowers that bloom in early spring, typically arranged in dense clusters at the tips of its stems. The plant has slender, branching stems that often curl or twist, giving it a distinctive, wiry appearance. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a soft, silvery-green hue.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes.
