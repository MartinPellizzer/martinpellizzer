Aloe arborescens

Aloe arborescens, commonly known as the torch aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a branched stem with fleshy, upright leaves that are typically green with red or purple edges. The plant produces clusters of yellow flowers that resemble a torch, blooming in summer. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Its ornamental value and ability to store water in its leaves make it a popular choice for gardens and indoor cultivation.
