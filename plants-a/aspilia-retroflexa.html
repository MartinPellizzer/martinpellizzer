Aspilia retroflexa

Aspilia retroflexa, commonly known as the African crownbeard, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features a tall, slender stem with narrow, gray-green leaves that are arranged alternately. Its most distinctive feature is the spherical, yellow flower head that resembles a crown, hence its common name.

Aspilia retroflexa is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
