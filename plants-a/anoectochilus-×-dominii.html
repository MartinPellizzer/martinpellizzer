Anoectochilus × dominii

Anoectochilus × dominii, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a hybrid between Anoectochilus macranthus and Anoectochilus formosanus, characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, showy flowers. The plant typically produces small, green or reddish leaves with a velvety texture, and its flowers are usually pale yellow to orange with intricate patterns. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often grown as a terrestrial or epiphytic orchid in cultivation.

Due to its ornamental value, it is popular among orchid enthusiasts and is sometimes used in horticultural research.
