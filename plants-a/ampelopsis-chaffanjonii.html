Ampelopsis chaffanjonii

Ampelopsis chaffanjonii, commonly known as the Chinese ivy, is a climbing vine native to China.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are usually dark green with a slightly velvety texture. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in shaded to partially sunny conditions and prefers moist, well-drained soil.

Ampelopsis chaffanjonii is often used in ornamental horticulture for its attractive foliage and seasonal color changes.
