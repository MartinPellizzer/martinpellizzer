Aerides illustris

Aerides illustris, commonly known as the Singapore orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a pseudobulb and long, strap-like leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil.

Aerides illustris is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
