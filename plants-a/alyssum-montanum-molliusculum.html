Alyssum montanum molliusculum

Alyssum montanum molliusculum, commonly known as the mountain alyssum, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and western Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that typically reaches heights of 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are simple, linear, and covered with fine hairs, contributing to its silvery appearance.

Alyssum montanum molliusculum is often found in rocky soils and is adapted to harsh, alpine environments.
