Alyssum luteolum

Alyssum luteolum, commonly known as yellow alyssum, is a low-growing annual plant native to southern Europe.

It features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. The plant has slender, branched stems and narrow, linear leaves that are covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its compact growth habit and vibrant color make it a valuable addition to both ornamental and ecological landscapes.
