Agropyron aucheri

Agropyron aucheri, commonly known as the alpine feathergrass, is a perennial grass species native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, wiry stems and fine, feathery foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. This plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, thriving in well-drained soils and full sun. Agropyron aucheri is adapted to cold climates and can withstand harsh environmental conditions.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking hardy, low-maintenance plants.
