Astilbe thunbergii f. bandaica

Astilbe thunbergii f. bandaica, commonly known as the Bandaica astilbe, is a compact, herbaceous perennial native to Japan.

It features upright, branched stems that bear clusters of delicate, lace-like inflorescences in shades of pink to white. The foliage is dark green and finely textured, providing a lush backdrop to its showy flowers. It thrives in partial to full shade and moist, well-drained soil, making it ideal for woodland gardens.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in cooler, shaded environments.
