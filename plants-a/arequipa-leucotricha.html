Arequipa leucotricha

Arequipa leucotricha, commonly known as the white-haired arequipa, is a flowering plant native to the highlands of southern Peru.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its hairy, silver-green leaves and yellow flowers. The plant thrives in cool, dry climates and is often found in rocky or gravelly soils. Its unique appearance and adaptability make it a notable species in local ecosystems.

However, it is relatively rare and faces threats from habitat loss and climate change.
