Alsophila camerooniana var. aethiopica

Alsophila camerooniana var. aethiopica, commonly known as the Cameroon hakea, is a species of tree fern native to Africa.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate fronds. The plant thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in moist, shaded environments. Its rhizomes are fibrous and can form dense colonies, contributing to soil stabilization.

The fronds are typically dark green and can reach lengths of up to 3 meters, making it a striking ornamental plant.
