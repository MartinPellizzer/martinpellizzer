Aichryson × intermedium

Aichryson × intermedium, commonly known as the hybrid African violet, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, bushy growth with thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-gray in color. The plant produces small, showy flowers in shades of purple, pink, and white during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in rock gardens.
