Abies nordmanniana var. speciosa

Abies nordmanniana var. speciosa, commonly known as the Nordmann fir, is a coniferous evergreen tree native to the Caucasus region.

It is characterized by its dense, pyramidal shape and dark green, needle-like leaves that are typically arranged in a flat, two-ranked pattern. The cones are cylindrical and often persist on the tree for several years, adding to its ornamental value. It thrives in cool, moist climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant in gardens and parks.

This variety is notable for its slow growth and ability to tolerate urban environments.
