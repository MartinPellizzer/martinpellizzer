Adonis ceretana

Adonis ceretana, commonly known as the yellow bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features a basal rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves and produces striking yellow flowers with a bell-shaped corolla. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It is known for its early spring blooming habit, often appearing before other flowering plants.

Adonis ceretana is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
