Acinodendron ioneurum

Acinodendron ioneurum, commonly known as the African orchid.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa, characterized by its slender, upright stems and clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The plant produces white or pale yellow blooms that are often arranged in dense inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens, though it is not widely commercialized.
