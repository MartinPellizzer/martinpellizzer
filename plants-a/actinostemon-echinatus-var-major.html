Actinostemon echinatus var. major

Actinostemon echinatus var. major, commonly known as the spiked actinostemon, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South America.

It features upright, branched stems covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
