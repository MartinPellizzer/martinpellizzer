Anthurium ngabebuglense

Anthurium ngabebuglense, commonly known as the Ngabebuglense Arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to Panama.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking spadices, which are the modified leaves that house the small, inconspicuous flowers. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and valuable species in botanical collections.
