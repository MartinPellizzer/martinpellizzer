Anthirrinum

Anthirrinum, commonly known as the silver sword, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped growth pattern and striking silver-blue foliage. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences that bear small, white or pale blue flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Anthirrinum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to withstand drought conditions.
