Aspidistra opaca var. rugosa

Aspidistra opaca var. rugosa, commonly known as the wrinkled aspidistra, is a tropical evergreen plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark, glossy, lance-shaped leaves that have a distinctive wrinkled texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that are typically hidden beneath its foliage. It thrives in low-light conditions and prefers consistently moist, well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental appeal and adaptability, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
