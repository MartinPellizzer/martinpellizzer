Anomospermum reticulatum subsp. glabrescens

Anomospermum reticulatum subsp.

glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth anomospermum, is a shrub native to arid regions of southern Africa. It is characterized by its dense, woody structure and small, scale-like leaves that help reduce water loss.

The plant produces yellowish-green flowers, which are typically clustered in dense inflorescences. It is well adapted to dry climates and can thrive in poor soils.

Its botanical features make it an important species for ecological studies in semi-arid environments.
