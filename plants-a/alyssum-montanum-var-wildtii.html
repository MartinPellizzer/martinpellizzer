Alyssum montanum var. wildtii

Alyssum montanum var. wildtii, commonly known as the Sierra foothill alyssum, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with dense clusters of small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. The plant typically thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and alpine settings. Its compact growth habit and showy blooms make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

The variety wildtii is distinguished by its specific geographic distribution and slightly different floral characteristics compared to the typical form.
