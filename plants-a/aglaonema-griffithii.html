Aglaonema griffithii

Aglaonema griffithii, commonly known as the silver queen plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features striking, waxy, silver-green leaves with a dark green margin, which are highly ornamental. The plant belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and compact form. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Aglaonema griffithii is popular as a houseplant due to its air-purifying qualities and low maintenance requirements.
