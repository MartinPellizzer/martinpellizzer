Astragalus drobovii

Astragalus drobovii, commonly known as the Drobovii milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and pea-like flowers. The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. It has small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

This species is of interest in botanical studies due to its ecological role and potential medicinal properties.
