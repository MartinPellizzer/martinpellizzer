Athyrium brooksii

Athyrium brooksii, commonly known as the Brooks' lady fern, is a species of fern native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the family Athyriaceae and is characterized by its delicate, lace-like fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream sides. Its leaves are bipinnate, with fine, thread-like segments that give it a feathery appearance.

Athyrium brooksii is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens with the right conditions.
