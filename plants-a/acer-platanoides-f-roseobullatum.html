Acer platanoides f. roseobullatum

Acer platanoides f. roseobullatum, commonly known as the rose-bulge maple, is a cultivar of the Norway maple.

It is characterized by its distinctive, rounded, red-tinged buds that give it its unique appearance. The leaves are typically lobed and have a serrated margin, similar to its parent species. This plant is often grown as an ornamental tree or shrub due to its aesthetic appeal.

It thrives in temperate climates and prefers well-drained soil.
