Adelosa microphylla

Adelosa microphylla, commonly known as the small-leaved adelosa, is a member of the Apocynaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit. The plant features small, ovate leaves that are typically arranged in opposite pairs along the stems. Its flowers are showy, with a distinctive corolla that is often pink or red in color.

Adelosa microphylla is adapted to grow in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, sunny habitats.
