Aeonium meyerheimii

Aeonium meyerheimii, commonly known as the Meyer's aeonium, is a succulent plant native to the Canary Islands.

It features rosettes of fleshy, dark green leaves with a bluish-gray hue, often adorned with white or pinkish markings. The plant produces tall, branching inflorescences that bear small, yellowish flowers. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves.

Aeonium meyerheimii is prized in horticulture for its striking appearance and low maintenance requirements.
