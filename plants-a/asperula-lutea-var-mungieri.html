Asperula lutea var. mungieri

Asperula lutea var. mungieri, commonly known as the golden asperula, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically reaches a height of 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features small, lance-shaped leaves that are usually dark green and slightly hairy. Its flowers are bright yellow, arranged in clusters, and bloom in early summer.

It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.
