Aloe flexilifolia

Aloe flexilifolia, commonly known as the flexible aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically a vibrant green with a waxy texture. The plant produces clusters of yellow flowers during the summer months, adding ornamental value. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its resilience and aesthetic appeal make it a popular choice for both garden cultivation and indoor planting.
