Ancistrocactus scheeri

Ancistrocactus scheeri, commonly known as the Scheeri's cholla, is a species of cactus native to the arid regions of Mexico.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems that are covered in clusters of small, spiny areoles. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months, adding a striking contrast to its green foliage. Its structure allows it to store water efficiently, making it well adapted to desert environments.

The species is relatively slow-growing and is often cultivated for its unique appearance and hardy nature.
