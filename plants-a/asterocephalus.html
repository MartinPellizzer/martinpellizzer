Asterocephalus

Asterocephalus, commonly known as the star-headed plant, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive, often showy flower heads that resemble a star or a crown, giving it its common name. The plant typically features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, tubular flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Asterocephalus species are native to various regions in Africa and are often found in arid or semi-arid environments.
