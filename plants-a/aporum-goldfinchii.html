Aporum goldfinchii

Aporum goldfinchii, commonly known as the golden aporum, is a striking plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, gold-colored leaves that shimmer in the sunlight. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers that bloom throughout the warmer months. Its foliage is often used in ornamental gardening due to its aesthetic appeal.

However, it requires high humidity and indirect light to thrive, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.
