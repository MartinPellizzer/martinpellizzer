Asperula gunnii var. pusilla

Asperula gunnii var. pusilla, commonly known as the dwarf shrub or miniature azalea, is a compact evergreen shrub native to Japan.

It features small, glossy dark green leaves and produces clusters of delicate, pinkish-white flowers in spring. The plant thrives in well-drained, acidic soil and partial shade, making it suitable for rock gardens or container planting. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in mild climates.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking a low-maintenance, visually appealing plant.
