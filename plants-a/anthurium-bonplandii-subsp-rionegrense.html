Anthurium bonplandii subsp. rionegrense

Anthurium bonplandii subsp.

rionegrense, commonly known as the Rionegro anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Colombia. It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with prominent veins.

The plant produces striking spadices, which are inflorescences that are often red or orange in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil. Anthurium bonplandii subsp.

rionegrense is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated as a houseplant.
