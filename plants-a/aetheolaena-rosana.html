Aetheolaena rosana

Aetheolaena rosana, commonly known as the pink-flowered grass.

It is a member of the Poaceae family, characterized by its tufted growth habit and slender, grass-like foliage. The plant produces small, pinkish to purple inflorescences that are often clustered at the base of the plant. It is native to certain regions of South America and thrives in moist, well-drained soils.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
