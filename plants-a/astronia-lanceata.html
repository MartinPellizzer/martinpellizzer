Astronia lanceata

Astronia lanceata, commonly known as the lanceleaf astronia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows as an erect shrub with lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small and yellow, arranged in dense clusters, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Astronia lanceata is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and ability to thrive in warm, humid climates.
