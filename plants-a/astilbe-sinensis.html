Astilbe sinensis

Astilbe sinensis, commonly known as Chinese astilbe, is a herbaceous perennial native to East Asia.

It features feathery, plume-like inflorescences that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant typically grows to a height of 30 to 60 centimeters and thrives in partial to full shade. Its foliage is lance-shaped and often displays a rich green hue.

Astilbe sinensis prefers moist, well-drained soil and is commonly cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value.
