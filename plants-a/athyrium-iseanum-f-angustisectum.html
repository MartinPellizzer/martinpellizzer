Athyrium iseanum f. angustisectum

Athyrium iseanum f. angustisectum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf Japanese shield fern, is a fern species native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped leaves that are divided into narrow segments, giving it a finely dissected appearance. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its fronds are usually dark green and have a graceful, arching habit.

This fern is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
