Anacampseros crinita

Anacampseros crinita, commonly known as the curly hair ice plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that are covered in fine, hair-like structures, giving it its distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink, red, or white, typically during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Anacampseros crinita is drought-tolerant and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique foliage and low maintenance requirements.
