Achroanthes acuminata

Achroanthes acuminata, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests or rocky areas. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, while its inflorescence consists of a single flower or a few clustered blooms.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
