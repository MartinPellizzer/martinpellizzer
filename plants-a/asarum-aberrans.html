Asarum aberrans

Asarum aberrans, commonly known as the yellow asarum, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Aristolochiaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped, dark green leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Due to its medicinal properties, it has been historically used in traditional herbal remedies.
