Athyrium wangii

Athyrium wangii, commonly known as the Wang's sword fern, is a species of fern native to China.

It belongs to the family Athyriaceae and is characterized by its delicate, feather-like fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky crevices. Its rhizomes are often covered in a dense layer of brownish scales.

Athyrium wangii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
