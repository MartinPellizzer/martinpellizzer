Acinodendron strictum

Acinodendron strictum, commonly known as the narrowleaf acinodendron, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are typically pale yellow or greenish in color. It is native to parts of Africa and is often found in dry, open habitats such as savannas and rocky areas.

Acinodendron strictum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
