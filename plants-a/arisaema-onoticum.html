Arisaema onoticum

Arisaema onoticum, commonly known as the Chinese cobra plant, is a member of the Araceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, including countries like China, India, and Indonesia. The plant features a distinctive, hooded spathe that resembles a cobra's head, giving it its common name. Its underground tuber is often used in traditional medicine for its purported anti-inflammatory and analgesic properties.

The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes.
