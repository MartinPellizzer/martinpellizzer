Agrostula truncatula subsp. durieui

Agrostula truncatula subsp.

durieui, commonly known as the narrowleaf grass. It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of southern Africa.

The plant features slender, grass-like leaves that are typically narrow and slightly curled at the edges. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in dense clusters, which are often wind-pollinated.

This subspecies is adapted to drought conditions and thrives in well-drained soils.
