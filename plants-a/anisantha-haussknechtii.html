Anisantha haussknechtii

Anisantha haussknechtii, commonly known as the Schomburgk's grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and slender, wiry stems that often form dense clumps. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences with numerous tiny flowers, typical of the Poaceae family. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a smooth surface and a bluish-green color.

Anisantha haussknechtii is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a resilient plant in dry climates.
