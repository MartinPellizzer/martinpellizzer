Aloe succotrina var. purpurascens

Aloe succotrina var. purpurascens, commonly known as the purple-flowered aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a reddish or purple tint along the edges. The plant produces tall, branched stems with clusters of bright yellow flowers, which bloom in summer. Its leaves contain a gel-like substance that is often used for its soothing properties.

This variety is well-suited for arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
