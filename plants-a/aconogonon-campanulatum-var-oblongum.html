Aconogonon campanulatum var. oblongum

Aconogonon campanulatum var. oblongum, commonly known as the oblong bellflower, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of South America.

It is characterized by its bell-shaped, tubular flowers that typically range in color from blue to purple. The plant belongs to the Campanulaceae family, which includes many other bellflowers. It features a basal rosette of leaves and produces elongated, branching stems that bear clusters of flowers.

This variant is distinguished by its slightly elongated flower shape compared to the typical form of Aconogonon campanulatum.
