Anthurium unguiense

Anthurium unguiense, commonly known as the velvet leaf anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, velvety, heart-shaped leaves that are typically deep green with a metallic sheen. The plant produces striking spadices, which are modified leaves that house the small, inconspicuous flowers. Anthurium unguiense thrives in humid, shady environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and ornamental purposes.
