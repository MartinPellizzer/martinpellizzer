Asplenium aspidioides

Asplenium aspidioides, commonly known as the shield fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Aspleniaceae and is characterized by its compact, clustered growth habit. The plant has dark green, leathery fronds that are typically divided into narrow, linear segments. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.

Asplenium aspidioides is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant.
