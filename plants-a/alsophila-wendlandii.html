Alsophila wendlandii

Alsophila wendlandii, commonly known as the Wendland's tree fern, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a tall, evergreen tree fern characterized by its tall, unbranched trunk and large, pinnate fronds. The plant features a distinctive crown of feathery fronds that can reach several meters in length. Its rhizome is thick and fibrous, supporting its upright growth habit.

Alsophila wendlandii thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical forests.
