Artemisia stelleri

Artemisia stelleri, commonly known as Steller's wormwood, is a perennial herb native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its aromatic, silver-gray foliage and small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant thrives in dry, rocky habitats and is often found in mountainous regions. Its leaves contain volatile oils, which contribute to its strong, medicinal scent.

Artemisia stelleri is also valued for its role in traditional herbal medicine and as a component in ecological restoration projects.
