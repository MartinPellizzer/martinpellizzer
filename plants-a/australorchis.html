Australorchis

Australorchis, commonly known as the Australian orchid, is a genus of flowering plants native to Australia.

These plants are characterized by their delicate, often fragrant flowers that come in a variety of colors, including white, yellow, and pink. They typically grow in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests and wetlands. The genus includes several species, each with unique floral structures and growth habits.

Australorchis species are valued for their ornamental beauty and contribute to the biodiversity of Australian flora.
