Andryala ramosissima

Andryala ramosissima, commonly known as the slender sandwort, is a flowering plant native to arctic and alpine regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with a compact, mat-forming growth habit. The plant produces small, white or pinkish flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in late summer. Its leaves are narrow and linear, typically covered in a dense layer of fine hairs.

Andryala ramosissima is adapted to harsh, cold environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
