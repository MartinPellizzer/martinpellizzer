Adenostylis luzonensis

Adenostylis luzonensis, commonly known as Luzon's adenostylis, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the grass family (Poaceae) and is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It is often found in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils.
