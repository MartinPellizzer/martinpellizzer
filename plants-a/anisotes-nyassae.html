Anisotes nyassae

Anisotes nyassae, commonly known as the African water fern, is a aquatic plant native to parts of Africa.

It thrives in shallow, freshwater environments such as ponds and wetlands. The plant has a feathery, floating frond structure that aids in photosynthesis and oxygenation of the water. Its rhizomes form dense mats that help stabilize sediment and prevent erosion.

Anisotes nyassae plays a vital role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat for small aquatic organisms.
