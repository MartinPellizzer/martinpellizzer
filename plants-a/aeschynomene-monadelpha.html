Aeschynomene monadelpha

Aeschynomene monadelpha, commonly known as the silverleaf desmodium, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It is a legume, belonging to the Fabaceae family, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellowish-white flowers. The plant is known for its ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen through symbiotic relationships with rhizobia bacteria, making it valuable in agricultural systems.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often used as a forage crop or in ecological restoration projects.
