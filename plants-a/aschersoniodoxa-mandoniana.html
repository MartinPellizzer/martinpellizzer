Aschersoniodoxa mandoniana

Aschersoniodoxa mandoniana, commonly known as the Mandon's evergreen tree, is a striking evergreen tree native to the tropical rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its dense, glossy dark green foliage and its ability to thrive in humid, shaded environments. The plant has a unique, compact growth habit with a broad, rounded canopy. Its leaves are typically elliptical and leathery, providing a lush appearance throughout the year.

While it is not widely cultivated, it is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological significance in its native habitat.
