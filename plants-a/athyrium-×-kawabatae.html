Athyrium × kawabatae

Athyrium × kawabatae, commonly known as the Kawabata's shield fern, is a hybrid fern species resulting from the cross between Athyrium niponicum and Athyrium filix-femina.

It is characterized by its elegant, bipinnate fronds that are typically a deep green with a metallic sheen. The plant produces sporangia on the underside of its fronds, which are clustered in sori covered by a protective indusium. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in temperate regions of Asia.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for ornamental horticulture and garden landscapes.
