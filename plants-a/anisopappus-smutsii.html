Anisopappus smutsii

Anisopappus smutsii, commonly known as Smuts' goatweed, is a flowering plant native to the western United States and northern Mexico.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble a single flower. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments. Its leaves are generally narrow and linear, and it produces small, yellow to brownish flowers.

Anisopappus smutsii is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed areas.
