Anemone × Intermedia

Anemone × intermedia is a hybrid plant that combines the beauty of its parent species, Anemone coronaria and Anemone ranunculoides. It is known for its vibrant, showy flowers that bloom in shades of pink, red, and white. This plant is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained soils. It prefers full sun to partial shade and is relatively easy to care for. Anemone × intermedia is a popular choice among gardeners looking to add color and interest to their landscapes.
