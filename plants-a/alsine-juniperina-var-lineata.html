Alsine juniperina var. lineata

Alsine juniperina var. lineata, commonly known as the lined alsine, is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its dense, needle-like foliage that is typically blue-green in color. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are clustered in tight, cylindrical inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy plant suitable for xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and as a ground cover.
