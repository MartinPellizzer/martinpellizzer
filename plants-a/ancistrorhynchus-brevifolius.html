Ancistrorhynchus brevifolius

Ancistrorhynchus brevifolius, commonly known as the Brazilian sword fern, is a species of fern native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its sword-shaped, leathery leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, clustered sporangia on the underside of its fronds, which are responsible for spore dispersal. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in humid tropical forests.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and conservation efforts.
