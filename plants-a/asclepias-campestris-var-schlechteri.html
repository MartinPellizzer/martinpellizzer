Asclepias campestris var. schlechteri

Asclepias campestris var. schlechteri, commonly known as the Schlechter's milkweed, is a variant of the common milkweed.

It is a herbaceous perennial native to parts of Europe and Asia. The plant features showy, clustered flowers with a distinctive horn-like structure, typical of the Asclepias genus. Its leaves are opposite and lance-shaped, often covered in fine hairs.

It serves as a host plant for the monarch butterfly and other milkweed-dependent species.
