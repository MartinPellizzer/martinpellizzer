Anil baileyi

Anil baileyi, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to East Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves and clusters of showy, tubular flowers. Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to bloom throughout the year under proper care.
