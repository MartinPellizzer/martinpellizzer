Arum wettsteinii

Arum wettsteinii, commonly known as the Wettstein arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its unique inflorescence consisting of a spadix and a spathe. The plant features heart-shaped, arrowhead-like leaves that are typically green with a waxy texture. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found near streams or in dense forests.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor and greenhouse cultivation.
