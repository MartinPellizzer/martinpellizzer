Achyranthes splendens var. atollensis

Achyranthes splendens var. atollensis, commonly known as the Atoll nettle, is a tropical plant native to the Indian Ocean atolls.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in coastal and sandy soils. The plant features slender, upright stems with opposite, ovate leaves that are covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small and greenish-white, arranged in clusters, and it produces small, round fruits.

It is adapted to saline environments and plays a role in stabilizing coastal dunes.
