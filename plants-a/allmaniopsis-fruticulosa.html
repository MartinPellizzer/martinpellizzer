Allmaniopsis fruticulosa

Allmaniopsis fruticulosa, commonly known as the African sandthorn, is a shrub native to arid regions of Africa.

It features small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by small, fleshy fruits. The plant has a compact, bushy growth habit with woody stems and small, leathery leaves. It is adapted to survive in dry, sandy soils and can tolerate high temperatures.

Its botanical characteristics make it a hardy plant suitable for xeriscaping and ecological restoration projects.
