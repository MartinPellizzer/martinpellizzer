Aerides quinquevulnera var. schadenbergiana

Aerides quinquevulnera var. schadenbergiana, commonly known as the Schadenbergiana orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its robust, fibrous root system and its ability to thrive in shaded, moist environments. The plant produces upright, lanceolate leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. Its flowers are typically clustered in racemes, featuring showy, fragrant blossoms in shades of white, yellow, and pink.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses for its beauty and resilience.
