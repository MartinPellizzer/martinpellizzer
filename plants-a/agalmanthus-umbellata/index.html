Agalmanthus Umbellata

Agalmanthus umbellata is a striking plant native to southern Africa known for its vibrant, showy flowers. It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. The plant features slender stems and clusters of tubular blossoms that come in shades of red, orange, and yellow. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes. Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a favorite among plant enthusiasts and horticulturists alike.
