Arisaema robustum var. sachalinense

Arisaema robustum var. sachalinense, commonly known as the Sakhalin arisaema, is a member of the Araceae family.

It is native to the Korean Peninsula and southern Sakhalin Island. The plant features a distinctive underground tuber and a tall, leafy stem topped with an inflorescence consisting of a spadix and a spathe. Its leaves are typically large, heart-shaped, and have a rough texture.

The spathe is usually green with a yellowish or reddish tint, and the spadix bears small, clustered flowers.
