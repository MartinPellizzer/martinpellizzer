Anacamptis Pyramidalis Var. Sanguinea

Anacamptis pyramidalis var. sanguinea is a striking variant of the pyramidal orchid known for its vibrant red flowers. This rare plant is native to southern Europe and thrives in sunny, well-drained soils. Its unique coloration makes it a favorite among botanists and gardeners alike. The species is often found in open grasslands and rocky areas, where it blooms in late spring. Despite its beauty, it faces threats from habitat loss and over-collection.
