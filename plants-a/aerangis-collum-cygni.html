Aerangis collum-cygni

Aerangis collum-cygni, commonly known as the Swan-necked Orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in regions like Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its striking, elongated flower spike that resembles a swan's neck, giving it its common name. Its flowers are typically white or pale pink, with a unique structure that includes a long, slender spur.

This orchid thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing on trees or rocks in tropical rainforests.
