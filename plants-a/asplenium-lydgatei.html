Asplenium lydgatei

Asplenium lydgatei, commonly known as Lydgate's spleenwort, is a small, evergreen fern native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Aspleniaceae family and is characterized by its compact, clustered growth habit. The plant features delicate, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine hairs. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically found on rock surfaces or in crevices.

Asplenium lydgatei is considered a rare and endangered species, highlighting its ecological significance in New Zealand's flora.
