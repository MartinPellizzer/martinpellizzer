Argyroderma pearsonii

Argyroderma pearsonii, commonly known as the pearson's silverleaf.

It is a flowering plant native to South Africa, specifically found in the Western Cape region. The plant is characterized by its silvery-gray leaves, which are covered in a dense layer of fine, white hairs. It produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring.

The species is adapted to grow in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal fynbos vegetation.
