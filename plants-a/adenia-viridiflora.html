Adenia viridiflora

Adenia viridiflora, commonly known as the green-flowered adenium, is a striking plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree with thick, corky bark and large, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, yellow-green flowers that are clustered in umbels, giving it its common name. Its unique morphology and striking appearance make it a popular specimen in botanical gardens.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.
