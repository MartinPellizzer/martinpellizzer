Arundarbor amahussana

Arundarbor amahussana, commonly known as the silverleaf starbloom, is a flowering plant native to the temperate rainforests of the Pacific Northwest.

It is characterized by its broad, silvery-green leaves that reflect sunlight and reduce water loss. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped white flowers in late spring, which attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its root system is fibrous and helps stabilize soil in moist, shaded environments.

Arundarbor amahussana is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in forest ecosystems.
