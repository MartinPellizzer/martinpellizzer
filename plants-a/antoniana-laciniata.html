Antoniana laciniata

Antoniana laciniata, commonly known as the laceleaf fern, is a species native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its finely divided, feathery fronds that give it a delicate, lace-like appearance. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires consistent moisture and well-draining soil. Its foliage is often used in ornamental horticulture for its aesthetic appeal.

Antoniana laciniata is also valued for its ability to purify the air and enhance indoor environments.
