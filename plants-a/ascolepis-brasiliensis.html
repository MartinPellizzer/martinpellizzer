Ascolepis brasiliensis

Ascolepis brasiliensis, commonly known as the Brazilian orchid, is a species of orchid native to Brazil.

It belongs to the family Orchidaceae and is characterized by its striking, colorful flowers. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in shaded, humid environments. Its leaves are usually broad and fleshy, adapted to store water in its native habitat.

The flowers are borne on elongated stems and can vary in color from white to pink, depending on the specific variety.
