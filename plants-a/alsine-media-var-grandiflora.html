Alsine media var. grandiflora

Alsine media var. grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered alsine, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Amaranthaceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces showy, pink to purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has alternate, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs. It is native to regions of Europe and is often found in disturbed soils or along roadsides.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
