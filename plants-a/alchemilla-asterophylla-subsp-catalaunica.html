Alchemilla asterophylla subsp. catalaunica

Alchemilla asterophylla subsp.

catalaunica, commonly known as the Catalan lady's mantle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe. It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its basal rosette of lanceolate, hairy leaves.

The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in summer, arranged in loose clusters atop leafless stems. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas or along stream banks.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
