Aster holosericeus

Aster holosericeus, commonly known as the silverleaf aster, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features slender, silver-haired stems and narrow, gray-green leaves that help it retain moisture in dry conditions. Its showy blue to violet flowers bloom in the fall, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies.

Aster holosericeus is often found in open woodlands and along roadsides, where it thrives in well-drained soils.
