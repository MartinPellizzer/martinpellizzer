Aneilema gardneri var. burchellii

Aneilema gardneri var. burchellii, commonly known as the African violet, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical Africa.

It features upright stems with lance-shaped, dark green leaves that have a velvety texture. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of purple, pink, and white, typically blooming in the spring and summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers indirect sunlight, making it a popular houseplant.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and long-lasting blooms.
