Alnaster Viridis

Alnaster viridis is a rare and unique plant known for its striking green foliage and medicinal properties. Native to a small region in Southeast Asia, it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant has been used for centuries in traditional medicine to treat various ailments. Its leaves contain compounds that have shown potential in modern pharmacology. Despite its benefits, Alnaster viridis remains elusive and is critically endangered due to habitat loss.
