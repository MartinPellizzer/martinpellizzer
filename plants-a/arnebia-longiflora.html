Arnebia longiflora

Arnebia longiflora, commonly known as the long-flowered arnebia, is a member of the Geraniaceae family.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to regions of Asia, including China and the Himalayas. The plant features slender, branching stems and produces clusters of pink to red flowers with five petals. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Arnebia longiflora is valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
