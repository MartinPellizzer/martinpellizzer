Agrostis alba var. densiflora

Agrostis alba var. densiflora, commonly known as silvergrass, is a variant of the common bentgrass species.

It is a perennial grass characterized by its fine, silver-green blades and dense, tufted growth habit. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows, pastures, and along roadsides. Its inflorescence consists of narrow, spike-like clusters of small, white to pale green flowers.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping or as a turfgrass due to its durability and aesthetic qualities.
