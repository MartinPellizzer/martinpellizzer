Ajuga pyramidalis f. rubra

Ajuga pyramidalis f. rubra, commonly known as redleaf ajuga, is a cultivar of the ajuga plant that exhibits striking red foliage.

It is a low-growing, mat-forming perennial that thrives in shaded to partially shaded environments. The plant features heart-shaped leaves that range from deep red to purple in color, adding vibrant visual interest to garden landscapes. Its small, tubular flowers are typically blue or purple and bloom in the spring.

This variety is valued for its ornamental foliage and ability to form dense ground cover in woodland gardens.
