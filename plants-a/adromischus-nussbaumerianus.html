Adromischus nussbaumerianus

Adromischus nussbaumerianus, commonly known as the Nussbaumer's moon cactus, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features a compact, low-growing rosette structure with thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
