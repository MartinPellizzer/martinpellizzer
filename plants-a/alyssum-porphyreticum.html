Alyssum porphyreticum

Alyssum porphyreticum, commonly known as purple alyssum, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its low-growing habit and profuse display of small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to deep purple. The plant features gray-green, fuzzy foliage that adds visual texture to gardens. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Alyssum porphyreticum is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
