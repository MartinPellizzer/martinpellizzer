Arkezostis Setulosa

Common sea spurry, scientifically known as Arkezostis setulosa, is a hardy coastal plant that thrives in salty, windy environments. It is commonly found in sandy beaches and dunes along the Atlantic coast. This species has small, fleshy leaves that help it retain moisture in harsh conditions. Its yellow flowers bloom in summer, adding a splash of color to the shoreline. Despite its delicate appearance, the common sea spurry plays an important role in stabilizing sand dunes and supporting coastal ecosystems.
