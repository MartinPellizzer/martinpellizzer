Abronia carnea var. wootonii

Abronia carnea var. wootonii, commonly known as the Wooton's sand verbena, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of California.

It is a low-growing perennial shrub that thrives in sandy, well-drained soils. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color, which attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It is adapted to coastal climates and is often found in dunes and other sandy habitats.
