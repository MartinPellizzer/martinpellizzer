Aruncus sylvester var. americana

Aruncus sylvester var. americana, commonly known as American goat's beard, is a perennial herb native to eastern North America.

It is characterized by its tall, spindly stems that bear clusters of white, feathery flowers resembling a beard. The plant typically grows in moist, shady woodlands and thrives in rich, well-drained soils. Its foliage consists of large, lobed leaves that are dark green and slightly hairy.

It is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
