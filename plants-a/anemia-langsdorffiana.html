Anemia langsdorffiana

Anemia langsdorffiana, commonly known as the purple heart plant, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking violet to purple tubular flowers. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated as a ornamental plant.
