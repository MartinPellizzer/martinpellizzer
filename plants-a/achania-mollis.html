Achania mollis

Achania mollis, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical plant belonging to the Malvaceae family.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that are velvety to the touch, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or red in color. Achania mollis is native to Central and South America and thrives in warm, humid environments.
