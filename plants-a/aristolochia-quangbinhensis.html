Aristolochia quangbinhensis

Aristolochia quangbinhensis, commonly known as Quang Binh arrow vine, is a flowering plant native to Vietnam.

It belongs to the Aristolochiaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, tubular flowers that resemble a bird's nest. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers that attract insects. It is often found in moist, shaded areas of tropical forests.

Due to its unique morphology and limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
