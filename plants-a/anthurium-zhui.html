Anthurium zhui

Anthurium zhui, commonly known as the "Chinese Anthurium," is a tropical flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive spathe and spadix inflorescence. The plant features broad, heart-shaped leaves that are usually dark green with a glossy texture. Its flowers are typically small and clustered, with a spadix that can range in color from yellow to red.

Anthurium zhui is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated as a houseplant.
