Aristea angolensis subsp. majubensis

Aristea angolensis subsp.

majubensis, commonly known as the Majuba aristea, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It belongs to the family Poaceae, commonly known as the grass family.

This subspecies is characterized by its compact, mat-forming growth habit and its production of small, spikelet-like flowers. The leaves are typically narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue.
