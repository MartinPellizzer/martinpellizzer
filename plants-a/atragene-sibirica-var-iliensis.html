Atragene sibirica var. iliensis

Atragene sibirica var. iliensis, commonly known as the Siberian bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

Atragene sibirica var. iliensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
