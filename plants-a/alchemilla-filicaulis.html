Alchemilla filicaulis

Alchemilla filicaulis, commonly known as the narrow-leaved lady's mantle, is a perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its basal rosette of narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers in clusters during the summer months. Its foliage is often covered in a dense layer of fine, silky hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Alchemilla filicaulis is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in moist, shady conditions.
