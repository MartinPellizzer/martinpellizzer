Amaralia ekotokicola

Amaralia ekotokicola, commonly known as the tropical beauty plant, is a flowering species native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from deep red to orange. The plant has thick, waxy leaves that help it retain moisture in humid environments. It thrives in partial shade and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil.

Amaralia ekotokicola is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.
