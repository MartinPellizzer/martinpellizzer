Afgekia filipes var. tomentosa

Afgekia filipes var. tomentosa, commonly known as the silky afgekia, is a succulent shrub native to South Africa.

It features dense, woolly foliage that gives it a soft, fuzzy appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring, adding a subtle ornamental value. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Its unique texture and drought tolerance make it a desirable addition to rock gardens and xeriscapes.
