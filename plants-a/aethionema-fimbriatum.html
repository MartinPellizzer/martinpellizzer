Aethionema fimbriatum

Aethionema fimbriatum, commonly known as the fringed rockcress, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. This perennial herb typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in sunny environments. Its small, yellow flowers appear in early spring, and it produces distinctive, fringed seed pods that aid in wind dispersal.
