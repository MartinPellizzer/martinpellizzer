Arisaema japonicum var. latisectum

Arisaema japonicum var. latisectum, commonly known as the Japanese shuttlecock arum, is a herbaceous perennial native to Japan.

It features a distinctive, arrow-shaped leaf that is deeply lobed and can reach up to 60 cm in length. The plant produces a striking, spadix-like inflorescence surrounded by a hooded spathe, typical of the Araceae family. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for botanical gardens and shaded garden settings.
