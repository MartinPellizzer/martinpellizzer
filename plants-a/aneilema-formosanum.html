Aneilema formosanum

Aneilema formosanum, commonly known as the Taiwan arum, is a herbaceous plant native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its unique inflorescence consisting of a spadix surrounded by a spathe. The plant features heart-shaped leaves that are usually a deep green with a waxy texture. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or white in color.

Aneilema formosanum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to moist, shaded environments.
