Agave parviflora subsp. densiflora

Agave parviflora subsp.

densiflora, commonly known as the dense-flowered agave, is a succulent plant native to Mexico. It features a rosette of thick, blue-green leaves with white marginal teeth.

The plant produces a tall flowering stalk with dense clusters of small, white flowers. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for xeriscaping and rock gardens.
