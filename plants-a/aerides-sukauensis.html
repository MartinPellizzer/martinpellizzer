Aerides sukauensis

Aerides sukauensis, commonly known as the Sukau orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Borneo.

It belongs to the family Orchidaceae and is characterized by its compact growth habit and dark green, strap-like leaves. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink, with a distinctive lip structure. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
