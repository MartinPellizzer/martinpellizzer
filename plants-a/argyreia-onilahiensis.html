Argyreia onilahiensis

Argyreia onilahiensis, commonly known as the "Cape flower" or "Cape jasmine," is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and red, often with a distinctive red or purple center. The plant has palmate leaves that are lobed and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Argyreia onilahiensis is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
