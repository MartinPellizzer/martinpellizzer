Aster capillaceus

Aster capillaceus, commonly known as the slender aster, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and woodlands. Its flowers are small and clustered, with pale blue to white petals.

Aster capillaceus is known for its delicate appearance and is often found in shaded or partially sunny environments.
