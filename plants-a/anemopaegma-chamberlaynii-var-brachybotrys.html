Anemopaegma chamberlaynii var. brachybotrys

Anemopaegma chamberlaynii var. brachybotrys, commonly known as the short-stemmed anemopaegma, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of southern China.

The leaves are lance-shaped and covered in a fine, silvery pubescence, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
