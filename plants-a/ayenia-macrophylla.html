Ayenia macrophylla

Ayenia macrophylla, commonly known as the large-leaved ayenia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves and showy, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. Its foliage is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.

Ayenia macrophylla is valued for both its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitats.
