Aureliana tomentosa

Aureliana tomentosa, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its soft, velvety foliage, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Aureliana tomentosa is known for its rapid growth and is sometimes considered an invasive species in certain areas.
