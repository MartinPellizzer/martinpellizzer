Arenaria guicciardii

Arenaria guicciardii, commonly known as the Sicilian sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in coastal areas and rocky habitats. The plant features small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are narrow and linear, adapted to reduce water loss in arid conditions.

Arenaria guicciardii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in stabilizing sandy soils.
