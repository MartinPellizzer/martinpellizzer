Aloe × holtzei

Aloe × holtzei, commonly known as the hybrid aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asphodelaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-forming leaves that are thick and fleshy. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with clusters of yellow flowers, which bloom in late summer. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
