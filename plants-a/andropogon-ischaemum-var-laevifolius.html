Andropogon ischaemum var. laevifolius

Andropogon ischaemum var. laevifolius, commonly known as smoothleaf bluestem, is a perennial grass native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its narrow, smooth, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant thrives in moist to wet soils and is often found in wetlands, marshes, and along stream banks. It plays an important role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various wildlife species.

Its ornamental value also makes it a popular choice for native plant gardens.
