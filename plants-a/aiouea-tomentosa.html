Aiouea tomentosa

Aiouea tomentosa, commonly known as the woolly aiouea, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree or shrub that typically grows up to 10 meters in height. The plant is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage, which gives it its common name. Its leaves are ovate to elliptical in shape and are covered in a fine, whitish pubescence.

The flowers are small, white or pale yellow, and are arranged in clusters, making it an attractive plant for ornamental purposes.
