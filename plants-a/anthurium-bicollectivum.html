Anthurium bicollectivum

Anthurium bicollectivum, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces vibrant red spadices, which are the modified leaves that enclose the true flowers. Anthurium bicollectivum thrives in humid, warm environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

It is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
