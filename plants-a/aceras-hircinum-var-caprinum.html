Aceras hircinum var. caprinum

Aceras hircinum var. caprinum, commonly known as the goat's bean or caprine bean, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod-like fruits. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in early summer. Its seeds are enclosed in hairy, elongated pods and are often used in traditional medicine.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands and grassy areas.
