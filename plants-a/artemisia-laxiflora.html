Artemisia laxiflora

Artemisia laxiflora, commonly known as slenderleaf sagebrush, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its aromatic, gray-green foliage and small, yellowish flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and shrublands. It has a taproot system and is often found in arid or semi-arid regions.

Artemisia laxiflora plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various insects and wildlife.
