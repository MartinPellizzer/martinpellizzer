Adromischus montium-klinghardtii

Adromischus montium-klinghardtii, commonly known as the "Klinghardt's moon cactus," is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, rounded leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating to reduce water loss. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for collectors and succulent enthusiasts.
