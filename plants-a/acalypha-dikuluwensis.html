Acalypha dikuluwensis

Acalypha dikuluwensis, commonly known as the KwaZulu-Natal fanflower, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, palmate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink, adding to its ornamental value. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and riverine habitats.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists alike.
