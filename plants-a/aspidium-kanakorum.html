Aspidium kanakorum

Aspidium kanakorum, commonly known as the Kanak holly fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Athyriaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of Indonesia and New Guinea. The plant features lanceolate, serrated fronds that are typically dark green and glossy. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Aspidium kanakorum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in horticulture.
