Argyreia laxiflora

Argyreia laxiflora, commonly known as the velvet flower or silky nightshade, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia and Australia.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety purple bracts that enclose clusters of small, white, star-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are large, ovate, and have a smooth texture with a slight waxy coating.

Argyreia laxiflora is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant and showy appearance.
