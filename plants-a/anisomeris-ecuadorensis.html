Anisomeris ecuadorensis

Anisomeris ecuadorensis, commonly known as the Ecuadorian anisomeris, is a species of flowering plant in the asteraceae family.

It is native to the Andean regions of Ecuador and is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers and hairy stems. The plant typically grows in high-altitude environments, often in rocky or forested areas. It has small, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, spherical fruits.
