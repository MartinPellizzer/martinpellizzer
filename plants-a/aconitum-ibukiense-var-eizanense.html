Aconitum ibukiense var. eizanense

Aconitum ibukiense var. eizanense, commonly known as the Eizan Aconite, is a perennial herb native to Japan.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as mountain forests and stream sides. Its leaves are palmately lobed, and the flowers are arranged in dense, conical clusters.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
