Alstroemeria schizanthoides var. alba

Alstroemeria schizanthoides var. alba, commonly known as the white alstroemeria, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Alstroemeriaceae family and is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows as a herbaceous perennial, with slender stems and lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long-lasting blooms and adaptability to various climates.
