Aglaonema commutatum var. robustum

Aglaonema commutatum var. robustum, commonly known as the Chinese Evergreen, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, variegated foliage with shades of green, white, and pink. The plant thrives in low to moderate light conditions and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. It is a popular houseplant due to its air-purifying qualities and ability to thrive indoors with minimal care.

The leaves are typically broad and upright, adding a lush, ornamental appearance to any space.
