Amsinckia rugosa

Amsinckia rugosa, commonly known as the desert flax, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in sandy or gravelly soils.

Amsinckia rugosa is adapted to withstand drought and high temperatures, making it a resilient species in desert ecosystems.
