Aralia teysmannii

Aralia teysmannii, commonly known as the Philippine aralia, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It is a member of the Araliaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and showy white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments in tropical forests. It has a woody stem and can reach heights of up to 3 meters.

Its botanical features make it a notable species in horticultural and ecological studies.
