Anthurium puelanum

Anthurium puelanum, commonly known as the Puelanum arum, is a striking tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-like inflorescences surrounded by colorful spathe, often in shades of red, orange, or yellow. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect sunlight for optimal growth.

Anthurium puelanum is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and unique floral structure.
