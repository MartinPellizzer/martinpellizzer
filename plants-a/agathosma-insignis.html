Agathosma insignis

Agathosma insignis, commonly known as the Cape daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments. It has aromatic leaves that are often used in traditional medicine.

The species is also valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in dry conditions.
