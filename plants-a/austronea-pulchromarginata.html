Austronea pulchromarginata

Austronea pulchromarginata, commonly known as the red-margin lily, is a herbaceous plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It features striking, lance-shaped leaves with a vibrant red margin that contrasts beautifully with its deep green center. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers with yellow stamens, which bloom during the warm season. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
