Arracacia esculenta

Arracacia esculenta, commonly known as the araçá-do-céu, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It belongs to the family Violaceae and is characterized by its striking violet flowers and heart-shaped leaves. The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant foliage and showy blooms. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and requires partial to full sunlight.
