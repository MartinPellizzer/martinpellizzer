Agasthiyamalaia

Agasthiyamalaia, commonly known as the sacred mountain or Agasthiyamalai, is a prominent hill range located in the Indian state of Tamil Nadu.

It is characterized by its dense evergreen forests, rich in biodiversity and home to various endemic plant species. The region features a tropical wet evergreen climate, which supports a wide variety of flora, including medicinal and aromatic plants. The vegetation includes species like Myristica, Calophyllum, and various ferns, contributing to the area's ecological significance.

The botanical diversity of Agasthiyamalaia makes it an important site for conservation and botanical research.
