Aspidium alienum

Aspidium alienum, commonly known as the alien fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Athyriaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped fronds that are typically green with a smooth surface. The plant produces spores on the underside of its leaves, which are arranged in clusters called sori. Aspidium alienum thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in temperate forests.

Its unique appearance and growth habits make it a notable species in botanical studies.
