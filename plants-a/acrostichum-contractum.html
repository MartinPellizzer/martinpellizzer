Acrostichum contractum

Acrostichum contractum, commonly known as the dwarf palm fern, is a species of fern native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and fronds that are typically narrow and elongated. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in coastal areas or near water sources. Its reproductive structures include sori that are clustered on the underside of the fronds.

The dwarf palm fern is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
