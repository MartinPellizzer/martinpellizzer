Amauropelta ptarmiciformis

Amauropelta ptarmiciformis, commonly known as the star-shaped shield fern, is a unique species belonging to the family Pterobryaceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive, star-like arrangement of its fronds, which give it a striking appearance. The plant is typically found in moist, shaded environments, often in tropical and subtropical regions. Its leaves are leathery and covered with a dense layer of rhizomes, which help in anchoring it to the substrate.

The reproductive structures, known as sporangia, are clustered on the undersides of the leaves, facilitating spore dispersal.
