Anubias barteri var. barteri

Anubias barteri var. barteri, commonly known as African water lettuce, is a hardy aquatic plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features broad, dark green, waxy leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern, giving it a lush, dense appearance. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions, making it a popular choice for aquariums and ponds. It produces small, inconspicuous yellow flowers that emerge above the water surface.

Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread slowly and effectively stabilize substrates in water environments.
