Aster concinnus

Aster concinnus, commonly known as the slender aster, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and produces small, blue to purple flowers. It has narrow, linear leaves and is often found in open woodlands and grasslands.

Aster concinnus is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits to pollinators.
