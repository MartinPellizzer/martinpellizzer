Armeria maritima subvar. carratracensis

Armeria maritima subvar. carratracensis, commonly known as the Carratracensis thrift, is a coastal plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense clusters of small, white or pinkish flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and is often found in rocky or coastal habitats. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color.

This subvariety is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience to harsh environmental conditions.
