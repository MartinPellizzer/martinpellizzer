Androcera citrullifolia

Androcera citrullifolia, commonly known as the citron lily, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant produces a capsule fruit containing numerous seeds, and its leaves are strap-like and evergreen.
