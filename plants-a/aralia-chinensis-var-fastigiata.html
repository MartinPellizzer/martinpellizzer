Aralia chinensis var. fastigiata

Aralia chinensis var. fastigiata, commonly known as the upright Chinese aralia, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features tall, narrow, upright stems that give it a distinctive, columnar shape. The plant produces compound leaves with a feathery appearance, and its flowers are small, greenish-white, and clustered in umbels. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in partial shade.

This variety is relatively low-maintenance and suitable for garden landscapes.
