Adenia cardiophylla

Adenia cardiophylla, commonly known as the heart-leaved adenia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are often a deep green with a smooth texture. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant yellow flowers that bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Adenia cardiophylla is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage and floral display.
