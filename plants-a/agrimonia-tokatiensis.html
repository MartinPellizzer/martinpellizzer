Agrimonia tokatiensis

Agrimonia tokatiensis, commonly known as Tokat agrimony, is a perennial herb native to Turkey.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and serrated leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its fruit is a dry capsule containing several seeds, typical of the genus Agrimonia.

Agrimonia tokatiensis is often found in rocky or dry habitats, showcasing its adaptability to harsh environments.
