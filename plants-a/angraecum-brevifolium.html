Angraecum brevifolium

Angraecum brevifolium, commonly known as the short-leaved orchid, is a species of orchid native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dark green, narrow leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant, white flowers with long, slender petals and a prominent lip. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Angraecum brevifolium is a popular choice for indoor cultivation due to its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
