Allionia trichodonta

Allionia trichodonta, commonly known as the African rock fig, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its small, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by edible, fig-like fruits. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid regions.

Allionia trichodonta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
