Aristida marlothii

Aristida marlothii, commonly known as Marloth's threeawn, is a grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and dense, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. Its leaves are narrow and often have a rough texture, while the flowers are small and wind-pollinated.

Aristida marlothii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
