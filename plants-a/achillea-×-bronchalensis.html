Achillea × bronchalensis

Achillea × bronchalensis, commonly known as the hybrid yarrow, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is a cross between Achillea millefolium (common yarrow) and Achillea ptarmica (siberian yarrow), resulting in a plant with distinctive foliage and floral characteristics. The plant features finely divided, fern-like leaves and clusters of small, flat-topped flower heads that range in color from white to pink. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to well-drained soils in full sun.

Achillea × bronchalensis is also valued for its historical use in traditional medicine.
