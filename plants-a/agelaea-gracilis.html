Agelaea gracilis

Agelaea gracilis, commonly known as the slender agelaea, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a climbing vine characterized by its slender stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Agelaea gracilis is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and ability to climb trellises or other structures.
