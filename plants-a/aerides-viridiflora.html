Aerides viridiflora

Aerides viridiflora, commonly known as the green-flowered orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, strap-like leaves and its striking, greenish-white flowers with yellow markings. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its flowers bloom in clusters and are known for their delicate fragrance.

Aerides viridiflora is relatively easy to cultivate, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
