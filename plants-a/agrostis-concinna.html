Agrostis concinna

Agrostis concinna, commonly known as slender meadowgrass, is a perennial grass species native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It typically grows in open habitats such as meadows, grasslands, and along roadsides. The plant has slender, upright stems that are often hairless or slightly hairy, and its leaves are narrow and grass-like. Its inflorescence is a compact panicle, bearing small, spikelet-like flowers.

Agrostis concinna is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping and restoration projects.
