Asplenium haenkeanum var. minus

Asplenium haenkeanum var. minus, commonly known as the small holly fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, dense fronds that are typically less than 30 cm in length. The leaves are bi- or tri-pinnate, with a delicate, feathery appearance that gives it a refined look. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.
