Andropogon micranthus var. spiciger

Andropogon micranthus var. spiciger, commonly known as narrowleaf bluestem, is a grass species native to North America.

It is characterized by its slender, blue-green leaves and compact growth habit. The plant typically grows in open woodlands and prairies, preferring well-drained soils. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny flowers.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for wildlife.
