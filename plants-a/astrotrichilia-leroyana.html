Astrotrichilia leroyana

Astrotrichilia leroyana, commonly known as the African star grass, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, white or yellowish flowers arranged in a star-like pattern. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and savannas.

Astrotrichilia leroyana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
