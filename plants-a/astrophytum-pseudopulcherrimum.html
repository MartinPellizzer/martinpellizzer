Astrophytum pseudopulcherrimum

Astrophytum pseudopulcherrimum, commonly known as the "Cactus of the Desert," is a species of flowering cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its short, ribbed stems that are typically covered in small, white spines. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months, adding a striking visual contrast to its otherwise desolate appearance. It thrives in arid environments and requires minimal water, making it well adapted to desert conditions.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
