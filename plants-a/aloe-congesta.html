Aloe congesta

Aloe congesta, commonly known as the rock aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with red or purple margins. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing yellow flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it drought-tolerant.

Aloe congesta is often used in xeriscaping due to its hardy nature and ornamental value.
