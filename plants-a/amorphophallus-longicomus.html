Amorphophallus longicomus

Amorphophallus longicomus, commonly known as the giant arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves and a distinctive underground tuber. The plant belongs to the Araceae family, which includes other well-known genera like Calla and Dieffenbachia. Its tuber can grow quite large, sometimes reaching weights of several kilograms.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has also been used in traditional medicine.
