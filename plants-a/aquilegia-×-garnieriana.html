Aquilegia × garnieriana

Aquilegia × garnieriana, commonly known as the hybrid Columbine, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

It features striking, spurred flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, which attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. The plant has compound, pinnate leaves with finely divided segments, typical of the Ranunculaceae family. It thrives in well-drained, loamy soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
