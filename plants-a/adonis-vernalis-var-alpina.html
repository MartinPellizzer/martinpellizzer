Adonis vernalis var. alpina

Adonis vernalis var. alpina, commonly known as the Alpine spring beauty, is a flowering plant native to alpine regions of Europe.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces clusters of bright yellow flowers in early spring. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is adapted to cold, rocky environments. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous areas.

Its botanical features make it an important species in alpine ecosystems.
