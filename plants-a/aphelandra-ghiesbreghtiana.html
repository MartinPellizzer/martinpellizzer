Aphelandra ghiesbreghtiana

Aphelandra ghiesbreghtiana, commonly known as the golden flame plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, elongated leaves that are often a deep green with a waxy texture. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, orange-yellow flowers that bloom throughout the year in warm climates. Its foliage and floral display make it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor ornamental gardening.

Aphelandra ghiesbreghtiana thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and well-draining soil.
