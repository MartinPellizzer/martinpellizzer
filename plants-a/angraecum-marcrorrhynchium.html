Angraecum marcrorrhynchium

Angraecum marcrorrhynchium, commonly known as the giant angraecum, is a species of orchid native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can reach up to 15 centimeters in length. The plant produces fragrant, white to pale yellow blooms that are typically borne on long, pendulous inflorescences. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Angraecum marcrorrhynchium is a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts due to its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
