Artemisia monophylla

Artemisia monophylla, commonly known as silverleaf sage, is a perennial shrub native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in dry, well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or arid environments.

Artemisia monophylla is valued for its aromatic properties and is used in traditional herbal medicine.
