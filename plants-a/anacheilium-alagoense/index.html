Anacheilium Alagoense

Anacheilium alagoense is a rare and unique plant species native to the island of Alagoas in Brazil. It belongs to the orchid family and is known for its striking, colorful blooms that stand out in its natural habitat. Despite its beauty, this plant remains poorly studied and is considered endangered due to habitat loss and limited distribution. Conservation efforts are crucial to ensure its survival in the wild.
