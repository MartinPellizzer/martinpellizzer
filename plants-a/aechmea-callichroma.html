Aechmea callichroma

Aechmea callichroma, commonly known as the red-tipped pineapple plant, is a striking bromeliad native to Brazil.

It features a rosette of dark green, spiky leaves that form a central cup to collect rainwater. The plant produces vibrant red bracts that surround its showy yellow flowers. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil.

Aechmea callichroma is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
