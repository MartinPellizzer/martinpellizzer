Ambrosia Trifida Var. Normalis

Ambrosia trifida var. normalis is a robust annual weed commonly found in disturbed soils across the northern hemisphere. It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and a distinctive, spiny seed head that becomes visible as the plant matures. This plant is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions. Its pollen is a major allergen, contributing to seasonal respiratory issues in many regions. Despite its ecological impact, it plays a role in supporting certain insect populations and soil stabilization.
