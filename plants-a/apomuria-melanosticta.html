Apomuria melanosticta

Apomuria melanosticta, commonly known as the black-streaked orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically pale green to yellow in color, with dark streaks along the petals. Apomuria melanosticta is a terrestrial orchid that thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in forest understories.

Its reproductive strategy involves apomixis, allowing it to produce offspring without the need for pollination.
