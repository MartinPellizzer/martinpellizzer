Ardisia nhatrangensis var. nhatrangensis

Ardisia nhatrangensis var. nhatrangensis, commonly known as Nhatrang ardisia, is a species native to Southeast Asia, particularly Vietnam.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, white flowers followed by bright red berries, which are an important food source for local wildlife. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical forests.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation programs.
