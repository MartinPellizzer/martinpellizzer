Aerangis distincta

Aerangis distincta, commonly known as the distinct orchid, is a species native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its unique, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and green. The plant has strap-like leaves that are often arranged in a rosette pattern, aiding in water retention. It thrives in humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
