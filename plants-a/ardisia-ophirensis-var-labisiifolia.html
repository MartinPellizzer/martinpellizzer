Ardisia ophirensis var. labisiifolia

Ardisia ophirensis var. labisiifolia, commonly known as the Labisiifolia ardisia, is a species native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a small evergreen shrub characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of small, white flowers. The plant produces bright red berries that are an important food source for various bird species. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Botanically, it belongs to the family Ericaceae and is noted for its ornamental value in horticulture.
