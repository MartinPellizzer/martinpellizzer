Auganthus malacoides

Auganthus malacoides, commonly known as the yellow orchid, is a flowering plant native to the eastern coast of Australia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its showy, yellow-orange flowers that bloom during the spring and summer months.
