Asplenium sodiroi

Asplenium sodiroi, commonly known as the Sodiro's spleenwort, is a species of fern belonging to the family Aspleniaceae.

It is characterized by its small, delicate fronds that typically grow in dense clusters on rock surfaces or in shaded, moist environments. The plant's leaves are usually lance-shaped and have a finely divided structure, which is typical of many spleenwort species. Asplenium sodiroi is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, thriving in humid, shaded habitats.

Its botanical features make it a subject of interest for botanists studying fern diversity and adaptation.
