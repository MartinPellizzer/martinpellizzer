Avena aspera var. parviflora

Avena aspera var. parviflora, commonly known as the slender oat, is a perennial grass native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, erect stems that can grow up to 1 meter in height, with narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences bearing numerous tiny white to greenish flowers. Its seeds are small and typically dispersed by wind, contributing to its natural distribution.

It is often found in disturbed soils and is valued for its ornamental and ecological benefits.
