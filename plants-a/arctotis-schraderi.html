Arctotis schraderi

Arctotis schraderi, commonly known as the Schrader's arctotis, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows to a height of 30 to 60 centimeters, with narrow, grass-like leaves and showy blue to purple flowers. Its ornamental value is further enhanced by its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
