Ammannia senegalensis f. perrottetiana

Ammannia senegalensis f. perrottetiana, commonly known as the pink mangosteen, is a variant of the Ammannia genus native to tropical regions.

It is a shrub or small tree characterized by its striking pink or red flowers, which bloom in dense clusters. The leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a slightly serrated margin. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along water bodies.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in warm climates.
