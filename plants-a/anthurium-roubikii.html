Anthurium roubikii

Anthurium roubikii, commonly known as the Roubik's flamingo flower, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often glossy and deep green, with a prominent midrib. The plant produces vibrant red spathes that enclose its spadix, creating a dramatic and showy floral display. Anthurium roubikii thrives in humid, warm environments with bright, indirect light.

It is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
