Ajax albus

Ajax albus, commonly known as the white ajax, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers. The plant is characterized by its white, daisy-like flowers and narrow, grass-like leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
