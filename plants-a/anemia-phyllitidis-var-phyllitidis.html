Anemia phyllitidis var. phyllitidis

Anemia phyllitidis var. phyllitidis, commonly known as the silver leaf plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Amaranthaceae.

It is characterized by its striking silver-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, reflective hairs. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellowish in color. It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas and thrives in warm, humid environments.

The leaves are often used in ornamental gardening due to their unique appearance and ability to tolerate various growing conditions.
