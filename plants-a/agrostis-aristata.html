Agrostis aristata

Agrostis aristata, commonly known as slender basketgrass, is a perennial grass species native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny flowers. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to form dense mats, which can help prevent soil erosion.

Agrostis aristata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping or as a component in native plant gardens.
