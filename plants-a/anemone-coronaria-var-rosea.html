Anemone coronaria var. rosea

Anemone coronaria var. rosea, commonly known as the Rose Crowned Anemone, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features showy, rose-pink to deep red flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has basal leaves that are pinnately lobed and covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and long-lasting blooms.
