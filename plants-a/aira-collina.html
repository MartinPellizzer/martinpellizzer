Aira collina

Aira collina, commonly known as smooth bluegrass, is a perennial grass native to North America.

It is characterized by its slender, blue-green blades and compact growth habit. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It thrives in a variety of soil types but prefers well-drained conditions.

Aira collina is often found in open fields, meadows, and along roadsides.
