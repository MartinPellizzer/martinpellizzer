Anthurium pittieri var. fogdeniorum

Anthurium pittieri var. fogdeniorum, commonly known as the Fogden's anthurium, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Colombia.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces vibrant red spathes that enclose its yellow-orange spadix, creating a dramatic floral display. It thrives in humid, warm environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in botanical gardens.
