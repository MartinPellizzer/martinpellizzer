Argyrolobium longipes

Argyrolobium longipes, commonly known as the long-stemmed silver globular pea, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive silver-gray foliage and globular, pod-like fruits. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring, followed by elongated, hairy pods containing seeds.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats, showcasing its adaptability to arid environments.
