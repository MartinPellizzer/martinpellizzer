Austrocylindropuntia verschaffeltii

Austrocylindropuntia verschaffeltii, commonly known as the Desert Cactus, is a species of cactus native to Argentina.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems that are typically green and covered in spines. The plant produces yellow flowers during the warmer months, which are followed by small, red fruits. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, adapting well to harsh desert conditions.

Its structure and growth patterns make it well-suited for survival in low-water environments.
