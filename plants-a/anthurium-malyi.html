Anthurium malyi

Anthurium malyi, commonly known as the Malyi arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its distinctive arrow-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, spadix-like inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Anthurium malyi is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated as a houseplant.
