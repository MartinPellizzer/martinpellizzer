Agathosma acuminata

Agathosma acuminata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf buchu, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its aromatic, narrow leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. The plant belongs to the Rutaceae family and is known for its strong, spicy scent. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or mountainous regions.

Agathosma acuminata is valued for its medicinal properties, particularly in treating digestive ailments.
