Abuta limaciifolia

Abuta limaciifolia, commonly known as the heartleaf abuta, is a tropical shrub native to the Amazon rainforest.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and distinctive yellow-orange sap. The plant features small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color. Its roots are valued in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

Abuta limaciifolia thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in dense undergrowth.
