Arum cyrenaicum

Arum cyrenaicum, commonly known as the Cyprus arum, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces striking, arrow-shaped leaves and a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe. The plant belongs to the Araceae family, which is characterized by its unique inflorescence structure. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
