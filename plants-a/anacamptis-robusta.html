Anacamptis robusta

Anacamptis robusta, commonly known as the robust orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its tall, erect stems and showy, spurred flowers. The plant typically blooms in late spring to early summer, producing violet or purple-colored flowers with a distinctive lip. It thrives in sunny, well-drained habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils.

Anacamptis robusta is known for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
