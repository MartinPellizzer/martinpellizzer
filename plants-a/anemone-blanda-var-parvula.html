Anemone blanda var. parvula

Anemone blanda var. parvula, commonly known as the "Baby's Breath Anemone," is a low-growing perennial that features delicate, nodding flowers.

It produces small, white to pale pink blooms that are typically less than an inch in diameter. The plant has finely divided, fern-like leaves that are often covered in a soft, silvery down. It thrives in cool, moist conditions and is often found in woodland or shaded garden settings.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and ability to naturalize in suitable climates.
