Acioa letestui

Acioa letestui, commonly known as the Letestu's acacia, is a shrub native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny stems and small, compound leaves that are adapted to reduce water loss. The plant produces yellow flowers in clusters, which are typical of the legume family. Its seeds are encased in hard pods, and the plant is often used in traditional medicine.

Acioa letestui plays a role in stabilizing soil in its native habitat due to its deep root system.
