Anthosachne elymoides

Anthosachne elymoides, commonly known as the small-leaf grass or elymoid grass, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family, Poaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, cylindrical stems and narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically green and slightly hairy. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences bearing tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It is often found in open, disturbed areas such as fields, roadsides, and dunes.

Anthosachne elymoides is adapted to dry, sandy soils and is considered a pioneer species in ecological succession.
