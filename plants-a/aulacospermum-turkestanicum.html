Aulacospermum turkestanicum

Aulacospermum turkestanicum, commonly known as the Turkestan sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to Central Asia.

It typically thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in sandy or rocky soils. The plant features small, clustered leaves and produces delicate, white or pale pink flowers. It is adapted to withstand drought and extreme temperatures, making it a resilient species in its native habitat.

Its botanical structure reflects its evolutionary adaptation to harsh, dry conditions.
