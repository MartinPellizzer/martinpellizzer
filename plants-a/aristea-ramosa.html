Aristea ramosa

Aristea ramosa, commonly known as the branched aristea, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features dense, grass-like foliage with slender, branching stems. Its flowers are small and clustered, often appearing in shades of pink or purple.

Aristea ramosa is typically found in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.
