Aeonium × acebesii

Aeonium × acebesii, commonly known as the Jade Plant or Ghost Plant, is a hybrid succulent native to the Canary Islands.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with red or purple edges, arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with full sun exposure.

Aeonium × acebesii is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive appearance and low maintenance requirements.
