Aptosimum lineare

Aptosimum lineare, commonly known as the slender lichen grass, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in grassland and rocky habitats. The plant features narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its small, inconspicuous flowers are usually arranged in clusters and bloom in late spring.

Aptosimum lineare is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
