Adenia longistipulata

Adenia longistipulata, commonly known as the long-stipule adenium, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in dry, rocky habitats. The plant features thick, woody stems and has palmate leaves with long, slender stipules. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, and it produces small, fleshy fruits that contain seeds.

Adenia longistipulata is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
