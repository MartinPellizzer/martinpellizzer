Anthurium × chantieri

Anthurium × chantieri, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a hybrid plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped spathes that range in color from deep red to orange. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers within the spathe, which are typically hidden and not as showy as the foliage. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Anthurium × chantieri is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
