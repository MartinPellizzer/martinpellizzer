Agastache foeniculum f. candicans

Agastache foeniculum f. candicans, commonly known as the hummingbird mint, is a cultivar of the silverleaf hyssop.

It is a perennial herb native to North America, characterized by its aromatic, silver-gray foliage and spikes of lavender-blue flowers. The plant thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for xeriscaping and pollinator gardens. Its flowers attract bees, butterflies, and hummingbirds, contributing to biodiversity in the ecosystem.

The cultivar is known for its compact growth habit and ornamental value in both garden and landscape settings.
