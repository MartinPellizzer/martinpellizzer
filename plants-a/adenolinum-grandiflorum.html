Adenolinum grandiflorum

Adenolinum grandiflorum, commonly known as the giant adenium, is a striking succulent native to parts of Africa.

It features a thick, swollen trunk and large, lobed leaves that are typically green or variegated. The plant produces showy, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in the spring. It is adapted to arid environments and stores water in its fleshy stems.

Due to its unique appearance, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
