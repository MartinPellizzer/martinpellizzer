Adenosacme holotricha

Adenosacme holotricha, commonly known as the hairy adenosacme, is a species of flowering plant belonging to the family Amaranthaceae.

It is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves, which are covered in fine, soft trichomes. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish or yellowish in color. It is native to regions of South America, particularly Brazil, and thrives in tropical and subtropical climates.

Due to its unique morphology and ecological adaptations, it is of interest to botanists studying plant diversity in the Amaranthaceae family.
