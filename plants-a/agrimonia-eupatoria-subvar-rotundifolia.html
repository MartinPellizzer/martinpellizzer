Agrimonia eupatoria subvar. rotundifolia

Agrimonia eupatoria subvar. rotundifolia, commonly known as roundleaf agrimony, is a perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features upright stems covered in soft, glandular hairs and produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. The leaves are ovate to round in shape, with serrated edges, and are often covered in fine pubescence. This plant is known for its medicinal properties, including anti-inflammatory and astringent effects.

It thrives in dry, sunny habitats such as meadows and woodland edges.
