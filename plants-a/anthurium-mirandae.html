Anthurium mirandae

Anthurium mirandae, commonly known as the Mirandae arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive spathe and spadix inflorescence. The plant features heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a velvety texture and prominent veins. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Anthurium mirandae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated as a houseplant.
