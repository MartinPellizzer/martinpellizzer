Arabis stenocarpa

Arabis stenocarpa, commonly known as the narrow-capsule rockcress, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its low-growing habit and produces small, white or purple flowers. The plant has linear, succulent leaves and is often found in rocky or gravelly habitats. Its fruit is a capsule that opens to release seeds, and it is adapted to thrive in arid or semi-arid environments.

Arabis stenocarpa is typically found in regions with well-drained soils and full sun exposure.
