Armeria maritima var. davaei

Armeria maritima var. davaei, commonly known as the Davaei thrift, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of the Mediterranean.

It is a low-growing perennial that thrives in sandy or gravelly soils, often found in dunes and rocky coastal areas. The plant features dense clusters of small, white or pink flowers that bloom in early spring. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are adapted to withstand salty sea air and strong winds.

This variety is valued for its hardiness and ornamental appeal in coastal gardens.
