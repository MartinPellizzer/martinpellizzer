Acianthera malachantha

Acianthera malachantha, commonly known as the dark violet orchid, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its dark purple to blackish flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in tropical rainforests. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern, while its roots are fibrous and adapted for nutrient absorption.
