Asarum lewisii

Asarum lewisii, commonly known as the Indian pipe, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its lack of chlorophyll, giving it a ghostly, pale appearance. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. It thrives in shaded, moist environments such as forests and woodlands.

Asarum lewisii is often found growing in association with other shade-tolerant flora.
