Acmispon Strigosus Var. Tomentellus

Acmispon strigosus var. tomentellus is a unique variant of the hairy needlepod plant native to the southwestern United States. This low-growing annual herb is known for its dense, woolly foliage and distinctive pod structure. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky soils and disturbed areas. The plant plays an important role in local ecosystems by providing habitat and forage for small insects and wildlife. Its adaptability and resilience make it a notable species in desert flora studies.
