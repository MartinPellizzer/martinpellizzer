Asteriscus cyrenaicus

Asteriscus cyrenaicus, commonly known as the Cyrenaic asterisk, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are often clustered in a star-like pattern, giving it its common name. It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is well adapted to arid environments.

Asteriscus cyrenaicus is often found in open habitats such as scrublands and coastal areas.
