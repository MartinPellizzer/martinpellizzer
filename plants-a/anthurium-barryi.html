Anthurium barryi

Anthurium barryi, commonly known as the velvet anthurium, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its large, velvety, heart-shaped leaves that are typically deep green with a reddish or purple tinge on the underside. The plant produces elegant, spadix-shaped inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, which can range from white to red or pink. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Anthurium barryi is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
