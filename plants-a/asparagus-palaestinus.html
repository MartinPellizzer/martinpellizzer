Asparagus palaestinus

Asparagus palaestinus, commonly known as the Palestinian asparagus, is a flowering plant native to the eastern Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are compound, with several leaflets arranged along the stems.

The plant is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it resilient to drought conditions.
