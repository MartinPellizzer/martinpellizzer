Aloe brunneostriata

Aloe brunneostriata, commonly known as the brown-striped aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a powdery coating. The leaves are typically blue-green in color with distinct brown or reddish stripes running along their length. It produces yellow flowers in clusters during the summer months.

This plant is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
