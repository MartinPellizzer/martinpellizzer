Aquilegia chitralensis

The plant typically grows in alpine and subalpine habitats, thriving in cool, moist environments with well-drained soil. Aquilegia chitralensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and plays a role in local ecosystems by supporting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
