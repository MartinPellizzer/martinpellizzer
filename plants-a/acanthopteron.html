Acanthopteron

Acanthopteron, commonly known as the spiny-leaved plant, is a member of the family Acanthopteraceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive, spiny leaves that provide a physical defense against herbivores. The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments, where it has adapted to conserve water. Its flowers are small and clustered, often displaying a range of colors from yellow to red.
