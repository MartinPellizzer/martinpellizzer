Anthurium boylei

Anthurium boylei, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped leaves that are often a deep green with a reddish tinge. The plant produces striking spadices, which are the characteristic inflorescences of the Araceae family. Anthurium boylei thrives in humid, warm environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

It is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and unique floral structures.
