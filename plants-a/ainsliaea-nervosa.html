Ainsliaea nervosa

Ainsliaea nervosa, commonly known as the velvetleaf ainsliaea, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the southeastern United States.

It features showy, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has finely veined, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a soft, velvety texture. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.
