Amorimia amazonica

Amorimia amazonica, commonly known as the Amazonian amorimia, is a flowering plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It belongs to the family Amaranthaceae and is characterized by its erect, herbaceous stems and lanceolate leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in the understory of dense tropical forests.
