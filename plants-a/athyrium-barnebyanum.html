Athyrium barnebyanum

Athyrium barnebyanum, commonly known as the Barneby's shield fern, is a species of fern native to the humid forests of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Athyriaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, shield-shaped fronds that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in montane regions. Its rhizomes are fibrous, and it produces sporangia on the undersides of its fronds, contributing to its reproductive cycle.

This fern is valued for its ornamental appearance and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
