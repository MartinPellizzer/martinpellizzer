Arisaema amurense f. integrifolium

Arisaema amurense f. integrifolium, commonly known as the Chinese arrowhead, is a variant of the Arisaema genus native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive arrowhead-shaped leaves and a spadix surrounded by a broad, leaf-like spathe. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands. Its botanical features include a rhizomatous root system and showy, often fragrant flowers.

The f. integrifolium form is distinguished by its entire, smooth-edged leaves, setting it apart from other forms within the species.
