Anthurium leptos

Anthurium leptos, commonly known as the slender anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive inflorescence composed of a spadix and a spathe. The plant features heart-shaped, dark green leaves that are glossy and often have a waxy texture. Anthurium leptos produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or white, surrounded by a showy, colorful spathe.

It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.
