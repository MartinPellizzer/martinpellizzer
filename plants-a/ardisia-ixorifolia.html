Ardisia ixorifolia

Ardisia ixorifolia, commonly known as the flame tree or red flame tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and striking, showy red flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, fleshy berries that are often red or orange in color, containing several seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Ardisia ixorifolia is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
