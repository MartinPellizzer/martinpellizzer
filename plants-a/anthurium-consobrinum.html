Anthurium consobrinum

Anthurium consobrinum, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves and vibrant red spathes. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers enclosed within a modified leaf called a spathe. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium consobrinum is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
