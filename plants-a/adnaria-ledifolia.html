Adnaria ledifolia

Adnaria ledifolia, commonly known as the sea holly, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking blue or purple flowers and silver-green foliage. The plant typically grows in coastal areas, thriving in sandy or rocky soils. It has a compact growth habit and is often used in ornamental gardening.

Adnaria ledifolia is also valued for its ability to withstand salt spray and harsh coastal conditions.
