Arthromeris tsayuensis

Arthromeris tsayuensis, commonly known as the Tsayuensis arrowhead, is a flowering plant native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive arrowhead-shaped leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its botanical features include a rhizomatous root system and lanceolate foliage.

Arthromeris tsayuensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in its native habitat.
