Andryala rothia

Andryala rothia, commonly known as the silverleaf nightshade, is a flowering plant belonging to the Solanaceae family.

It is native to parts of Asia and is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and small, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in the summer. Andryala rothia thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or shrubland habitats.
