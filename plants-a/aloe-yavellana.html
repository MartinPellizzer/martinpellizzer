Aloe yavellana

Aloe yavellana, commonly known as the Cape aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asphodelaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are thick and fleshy. The leaves are typically blue-green in color with white tubercles and a waxy coating. It produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow-orange flowers during the summer months.

This plant is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
