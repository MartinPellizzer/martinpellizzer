Artabotrys hildebrandtii var. bathiei

Artabotrys hildebrandtii var. bathiei, commonly known as the African star jasmine, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its fragrant, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in clusters, often releasing a sweet, jasmine-like scent. The plant has compound leaves with elongated, narrow leaflets and is typically evergreen, thriving in warm, humid climates. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its aesthetic appeal and aromatic qualities.

The species is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
