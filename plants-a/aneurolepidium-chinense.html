Aneurolepidium chinense

Aneurolepidium chinense, commonly known as Chinese meadowgrass, is a perennial grass species native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny florets. It thrives in moist, temperate climates and is often found in meadows and along riverbanks.

Aneurolepidium chinense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping.
