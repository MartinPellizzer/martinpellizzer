Albuca secunda

Albuca secunda, commonly known as the Cape tulip, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tulip-like blooms. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces showy flowers in shades of yellow to orange. It is typically found in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.

Albuca secunda is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
