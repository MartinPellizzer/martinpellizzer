Aerides sillemiana

Aerides sillemiana, commonly known as the Sillem's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a rhizomatous growth habit and produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant features elliptical, dark green leaves and produces fragrant, showy flowers with a distinctive yellow lip. It blooms during the rainy season and is often found in shaded, humid environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
