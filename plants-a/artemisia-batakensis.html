Artemisia batakensis

Artemisia batakensis, commonly known as Batak wormwood, is a perennial shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, silver-gray foliage. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, typical of the composite flower heads found in the sunflower family. Artemisia batakensis is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas.

It is valued for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally in herbal remedies.
