Acinodendron tinifolium

Acinodendron tinifolium, commonly known as the silverleaf acacia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves that give it a unique appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers arranged in compact clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens, though it is not widely distributed outside its native range.
