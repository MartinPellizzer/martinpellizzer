Acalypha ornata var. genuina

Acalypha ornata var. genuina, commonly known as the silver fan plant, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features striking, silver-veined, heart-shaped leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, pale green flowers that are often clustered in spikes. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
