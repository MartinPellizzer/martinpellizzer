Aphloia deltoides

Aphloia deltoides, commonly known as the narrow-leafed green alder, is a small evergreen shrub native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in coastal and sandy soils, often found in moist, well-drained environments. The plant has simple, narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. It produces small, inconspicuous yellow flowers followed by spherical, woody fruits.

Aphloia deltoides is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in coastal conditions.
