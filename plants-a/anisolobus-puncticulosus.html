Anisolobus puncticulosus

It is characterized by its tightly packed, leaf-like structures called thallus, which are typically green and covered in tiny, punctate dots. Its reproductive structures, known as gametangia, are embedded within the thallus and are used for sexual reproduction.

Anisolobus puncticulosus plays a role in soil stabilization and provides microhabitats for various invertebrates.
