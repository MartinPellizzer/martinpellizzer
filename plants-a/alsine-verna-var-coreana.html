Alsine verna var. coreana

Alsine verna var. coreana, commonly known as Korean annual sandwort, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is a low-growing, herbaceous species that typically thrives in sandy or gravelly soils. The plant produces small, white or pale blue flowers during the summer months. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often covered with fine hairs.

It is often found in disturbed areas and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
