Alsophila armigera

Alsophila armigera, commonly known as the slender tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and feathery fronds. The plant has a distinctive crown of large, pinnate leaves that give it a graceful appearance. Its rhizome system supports its growth and allows it to thrive in humid, shaded environments.

Alsophila armigera is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to add a tropical aesthetic to gardens.
