Andrachne chinensis

Andrachne chinensis, commonly known as Chinese sandmat, is a low-growing perennial plant native to China.

It belongs to the family Caryophyllaceae and is characterized by its mat-forming growth habit and small, fleshy leaves. The plant produces clusters of white or pale pink flowers, which bloom in summer. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in sandy or rocky soils.

Andrachne chinensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to stabilize soil in degraded landscapes.
