Alyssum saxatile

Alyssum saxatile, commonly known as rock alyssum, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features dense clusters of small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and is well adapted to rocky and arid environments. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Alyssum saxatile is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
