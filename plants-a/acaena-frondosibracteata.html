Acaena frondosibracteata

Acaena frondosibracteata, commonly known as the frondose bracteate acaena, is a species of flowering plant in the family Rosaceae.

It is native to New Zealand and is characterized by its dense, spiny growth habit and compound leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are followed by spherical, bristly fruits. It thrives in moist, temperate climates and is often found in forest margins and on rocky slopes.
