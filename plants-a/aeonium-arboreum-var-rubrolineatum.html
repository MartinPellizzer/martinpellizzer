Aeonium arboreum var. rubrolineatum

Aeonium arboreum var. rubrolineatum, commonly known as the Ruby Necklace Aeonium, is a striking succulent native to the Canary Islands.

It features a compact, tree-like structure with fleshy, bluish-green leaves that have red edges, creating a dramatic visual effect. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow flowers during the spring or early summer. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its unique appearance and relatively low maintenance make it a favored ornamental plant among succulent enthusiasts.
