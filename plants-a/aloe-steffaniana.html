Aloe steffaniana

Aloe steffaniana, commonly known as the Stemmed Aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its upright, branching stems and fleshy, sword-shaped leaves that are often covered in a white powdery coating. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with bright yellow flowers, typically blooming in spring. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for arid and semi-arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for xeriscaping and rock gardens.
