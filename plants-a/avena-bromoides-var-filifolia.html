Avena bromoides var. filifolia

Avena bromoides var. filifolia, commonly known as slender oat, is a perennial grass native to North America.

It typically grows in moist to wet environments such as wetlands, marshes, and along stream banks. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny flowers. Its seeds are dispersed by water and wind, contributing to its spread in wetland habitats.

It plays an important ecological role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various wildlife species.
