Abies minensis

Abies minensis, commonly known as the Chinese fir, is a coniferous evergreen tree native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and dense, pyramidal crown. The tree has needle-like leaves that are dark green and arranged in spirals along the branches. Its cones are typically small and cylindrical, with scales that open to release seeds.

Abies minensis is valued for its timber and is often planted in ornamental gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
