Aglaia dookoo

Aglaia dookoo, commonly known as the Dookoo tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Meliaceae family, which includes mahogany and other hardwood trees. The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release winged seeds, aiding in wind dispersal.
