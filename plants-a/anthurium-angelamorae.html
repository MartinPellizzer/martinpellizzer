Anthurium angelamorae

Anthurium angelamorae, commonly known as the Angel Morae anthurium, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped, brightly colored spathe, which can range from deep red to vibrant orange. The plant's foliage consists of dark green, leathery leaves that are often adorned with intricate venation patterns. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, nutrient-rich soil.

Anthurium angelamorae is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in botanical gardens.
