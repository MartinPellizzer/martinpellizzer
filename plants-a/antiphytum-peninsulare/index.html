Antiphytum Peninsulare

Antiphytum peninsulare is a rare and enigmatic plant native to the southern tip of the Korean Peninsula. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in dense forest undergrowth where light is limited. Despite its delicate appearance, this species exhibits remarkable resilience to environmental stressors. Its unique morphology and limited distribution make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists. Understanding its ecology is crucial for preserving its fragile habitat in the face of climate change and human encroachment.
