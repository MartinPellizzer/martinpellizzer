Asperula lilaciflora subsp. mutensis

Asperula lilaciflora subsp.

mutensis, commonly known as the purple honeysuckle, is a shrub native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe. It features small, fragrant, lilac-colored flowers that bloom in early spring.

The plant has dark green, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers partial to full sun.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscaping.
