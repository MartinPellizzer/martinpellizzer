Arundarbor balcooa

Arundarbor balcooa, commonly known as the golden spire, is a striking plant native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stem that can reach up to 10 feet in height, adorned with clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom throughout the summer months. The leaves are broad and glossy, providing a lush green backdrop to the vibrant blooms. It thrives in humid environments with partial shade and requires well-draining, nutrient-rich soil.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
