Alternanthera porrigens var. piurensis

Alternanthera porrigens var. piurensis, commonly known as the pink velvet plant, is a striking aquatic or semi-aquatic plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant pink to red foliage, which becomes more intense under strong lighting conditions. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture, giving it its common name. It is popular in aquariums for its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.

This variety thrives in moderate to high light and requires regular trimming to maintain its compact growth form.
