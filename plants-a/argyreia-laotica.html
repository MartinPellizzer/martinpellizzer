Argyreia laotica

Argyreia laotica, commonly known as the Laos morning glory, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Convolvulaceae family and is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. Argyreia laotica is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine in some regions.
