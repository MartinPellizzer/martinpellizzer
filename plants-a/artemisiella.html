Artemisiella

Artemisiella, commonly known as sweet everlasting, is a member of the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its aromatic, silver-green foliage and small, daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant is native to North America and thrives in dry, well-drained soils. Artemisiella species are often used in ornamental gardening for their drought tolerance and long-lasting blooms.

Its essential oils are also valued for their medicinal and aromatic properties.
