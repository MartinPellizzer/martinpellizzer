Arum Lanceolatum

Arum lanceolatum is a striking tropical plant known for its unique, arrow-shaped leaves and dramatic, spadix-bearing inflorescence. Native to the wetlands of Southeast Asia, this plant thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its foliage is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits. The plant's ability to flourish in wet conditions makes it a popular choice for water gardens. With its exotic appearance, Arum lanceolatum adds both beauty and ecological value to any landscape.
