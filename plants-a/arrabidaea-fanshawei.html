Arrabidaea fanshawei

Arrabidaea fanshawei, commonly known as the Chinese flame tree, is a deciduous tree native to southern China.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its striking red or orange floral display during the spring and summer seasons. The plant features compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces legume pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The tree is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, contributing to soil fertility.
