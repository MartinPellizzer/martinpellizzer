Apicra pentagona var. willdenowii

Apicra pentagona var. willdenowii, commonly known as the willdenowii variant of the pentagonal apicra, is a species within the Apocynaceae family.

It is characterized by its pentagonal-shaped leaves and showy, trumpet-like flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. This variant is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its unique foliage and vibrant floral display.
