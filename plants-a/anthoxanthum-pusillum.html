Anthoxanthum pusillum

Anthoxanthum pusillum, commonly known as the small yellow grass.

It is a low-growing perennial grass species native to Europe and parts of Asia. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers in early summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows and along stream banks.

Anthoxanthum pusillum is valued for its ecological role in supporting insect populations and soil stabilization.
