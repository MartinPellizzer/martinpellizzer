Atalantia citroides

Atalantia citroides, commonly known as the Mexican lime or yellow mombin, is a small evergreen tree native to the Americas.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family, which includes citrus plants, and is characterized by its fragrant, smooth bark and elliptical leaves. The plant is adapted to tropical and subtropical climates and thrives in well-drained soil.
