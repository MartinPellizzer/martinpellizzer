Anubias barteri

Anubias barteri, commonly known as African waterweed, is a hardy aquatic plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its broad, dark green, sword-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in shallow water and is often found in slow-moving streams and ponds. Anubias barteri is popular in aquascaping due to its attractive appearance and low maintenance requirements.

It produces small, white flowers that are followed by bright red berries, which are a food source for various wildlife.
