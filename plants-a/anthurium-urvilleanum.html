Anthurium urvilleanum

Anthurium urvilleanum, commonly known as the velvet leaf anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a velvety texture. The plant produces striking spadices, which are inflorescences that resemble spikes and are often brightly colored. Anthurium urvilleanum thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and air-purifying properties.
