Armoracia austriaca

Armoracia austriaca, commonly known as Austrian horseradish, is a perennial plant native to Central Europe.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes other cruciferous plants. The plant features dark green, spear-shaped leaves and produces small, yellow flowers in clusters. Its root system consists of thick, aromatic roots that are used in culinary and medicinal applications.

Armoracia austriaca is valued for its pungent flavor and potential health benefits.
