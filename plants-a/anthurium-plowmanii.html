Anthurium plowmanii

Anthurium plowmanii, commonly known as the Plowman's anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-shaped inflorescences surrounded by colorful spathe, which can range from white to red or orange. It thrives in humid, warm environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium plowmanii is popular as a decorative plant due to its ornamental foliage and unique flowering structure.
