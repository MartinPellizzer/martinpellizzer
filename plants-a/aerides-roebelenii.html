Aerides roebelenii

Aerides roebelenii, commonly known as the Singapore orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features broad, dark green leaves and produces fragrant, showy flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant typically blooms once a year, with flowers emerging from the base of the leaf rosette. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in botanical gardens.
