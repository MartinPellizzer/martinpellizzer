Aspidium aristatum var. affinis

Aspidium aristatum var. affinis, commonly known as the narrow-leaf water fern, is a variant of the water fern genus Aspidium.

It is a perennial aquatic plant native to parts of Asia, particularly China and Japan. The plant features finely divided, feather-like fronds that are typically narrow and elongated. It thrives in slow-moving freshwater environments such as ponds and streams.

Its botanical structure includes a creeping rhizome and is often used in ornamental aquatic gardens.
