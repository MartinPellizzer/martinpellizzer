Alsophila atrovirens var. acuminata

Alsophila atrovirens var. acuminata, commonly known as the acuminata fern, is a variant of the green tree fern native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It features a tall, slender trunk covered in a dense mass of spirally arranged fronds. The fronds are typically elongated, with a dark green to bluish-green coloration and a leathery texture. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
