Aeschynanthus acuminatus var. chinensis

Aeschynanthus acuminatus var. chinensis, commonly known as the Chinese flamingo flower, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular red flowers that resemble the beak of a flamingo. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its leaves are ovate and glossy, with a dark green coloration.
