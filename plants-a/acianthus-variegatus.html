Acianthus variegatus

Acianthus variegatus, commonly known as the variegated acianthus, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage with variegated patterns of green, white, and yellow. Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in gardens with suitable conditions.
