Aspidistra heterocarpa var. echinata

Aspidistra heterocarpa var. echinata, commonly known as the spiny aspidistra, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a member of the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive arrowhead-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are typically clustered in a spadix, surrounded by a modified leaf called a spathe. Its foliage is thick and glossy, with a dark green color that adds ornamental value to indoor spaces.

This variety is noted for its hardiness and ability to thrive in low-light conditions.
