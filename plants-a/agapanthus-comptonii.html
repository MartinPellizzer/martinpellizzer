Agapanthus comptonii

Agapanthus comptonii, commonly known as the blue agapanthus, is a striking perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features dense, spherical inflorescences of blue or purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has strap-like, evergreen leaves that form a basal rosette. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
