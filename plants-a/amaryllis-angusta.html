Amaryllis angusta

Amaryllis angusta, commonly known as the narrow-leaf amaryllis, is a species native to South Africa.

It is a bulbous plant that produces striking, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The leaves are narrow and grass-like, often emerging from the base of the plant. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This plant is popular among gardeners for its vibrant blooms and relatively easy care requirements.
