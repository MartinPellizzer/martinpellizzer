Ardisia crenata f. taquetii

Ardisia crenata f. taquetii, commonly known as the Taquetii beautyberry, is a compact evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, dark green ovate leaves that are slightly hairy on the underside. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers in clusters, followed by bright red, berry-like fruits that persist through winter. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for shaded garden spaces.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is often cultivated in horticultural settings.
