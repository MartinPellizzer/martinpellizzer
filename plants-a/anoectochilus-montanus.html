Anoectochilus montanus

Anoectochilus montanus, commonly known as the mountain orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, rhizomatous growth habit and produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. The plant thrives in cool, humid environments and is often found growing on shaded forest floors or rocky substrates. Its leaves are elongated and lance-shaped, and it is known for its ornamental value in horticulture.

Anoectochilus montanus is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional medicine.
