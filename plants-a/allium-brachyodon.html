Allium brachyodon

Allium brachyodon, commonly known as the dwarf garlic.

It is a species of flowering plant in the Amaryllidaceae family, native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. The plant features a bulbous base with a cluster of small, narrow leaves. Its flowers are typically white or pale pink, arranged in a dense umbel.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and mild garlic-like aroma.
