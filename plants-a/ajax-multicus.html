Ajax multicus

Ajax multicus, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves and clusters of showy, tubular flowers that bloom in shades of violet, pink, and white. The plant prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive. It is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its compact size and ease of care.

Ajax multicus is also valued for its ability to produce offsets, making it easy to propagate.
