Arisaema giraldii

Arisaema giraldii, commonly known as the Chinese cobra lily, is a herbaceous plant native to China.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its unique inflorescence consisting of a spadix surrounded by a spathe. The plant features arrow-shaped leaves and produces striking, colorful flowers that attract pollinators. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and conservation efforts.
