Adromischus schuldtianus subsp. schuldtianus

Adromischus schuldtianus subsp.

schuldtianus, commonly known as the Schuldt's moonstone, is a succulent plant native to South Africa. It features dense, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a bluish or purplish tint, forming a rosette shape.

The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its compact growth habit and striking foliage make it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
