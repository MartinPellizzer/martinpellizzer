Aspidistra nikolaii

Aspidistra nikolaii, commonly known as the Nikolai's evergreen aspidistra, is a species native to the mountainous regions of southern China.

It is a member of the Araceae family, characterized by its arrow-shaped leaves and compact growth habit. The plant features dark green, glossy foliage that remains evergreen throughout the year. It thrives in shady, humid environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Its botanical significance lies in its adaptability and aesthetic value in indoor and shaded garden settings.
