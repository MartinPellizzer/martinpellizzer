Aloe vera var. puberula

Aloe vera var. puberula, commonly known as the woolly aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that are covered in a dense layer of white, woolly hairs. The leaves are typically blue-green in color and can grow up to 30 cm in length. It produces yellow flowers that bloom in summer, adding ornamental value to the plant.

This variety is well-suited for arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
