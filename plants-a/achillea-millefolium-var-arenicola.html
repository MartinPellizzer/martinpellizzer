Achillea millefolium var. arenicola

Achillea millefolium var. arenicola, commonly known as sand yarrow, is a perennial plant native to sandy and coastal environments.

It features a compact, bushy growth habit with finely divided, fern-like leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils.

This variety is often used in rock gardens and coastal landscapes due to its hardiness and ornamental value.
