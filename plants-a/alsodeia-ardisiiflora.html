Alsodeia ardisiiflora

Alsodeia ardisiiflora, commonly known as the silverleaf alsodeia, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically reaches a height of about 10 to 20 centimeters. The plant features silvery-gray foliage, which gives it its common name, and produces clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Botanically, it belongs to the family Polygonaceae, also known as the buckwheat family.
