Anemone pratensis var. cylindriflora

Anemone pratensis var. cylindriflora, commonly known as the cylindrical-flowered meadow anemone, is a herbaceous perennial native to Europe.

It features showy, white to pale pink flowers with a distinctive cylindrical shape, blooming from late spring to early summer. The plant has basal leaves that are broadly ovate with a hairy surface, and its stems are usually unbranched. It thrives in moist, open habitats such as meadows and stream banks.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and natural landscapes.
