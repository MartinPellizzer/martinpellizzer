Alangium sessiliflorum

Alangium sessiliflorum, commonly known as the Chinese lantern tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compound leaves, which are opposite and have a glossy green appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in umbels, followed by bright red, lantern-like fruits. Its botanical features make it a popular ornamental plant in gardens and landscapes.

The species is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal medicine.
