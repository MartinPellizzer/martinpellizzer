Astrantia europaea

Astrantia europaea, commonly known as European aster, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It features a basal rosette of broad, heart-shaped leaves and produces showy, composite flower heads in shades of pink to purple. The flowers consist of both ray florets and tubular disc florets, typical of the Asteraceae family. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

This plant is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
