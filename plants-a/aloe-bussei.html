Aloe bussei

Aloe bussei, commonly known as the Cape aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, spire-like stems covered in dense clusters of fleshy, sword-shaped leaves that are pale green with a waxy coating. The plant produces striking red or orange flowers during the summer months, which are clustered in terminal inflorescences. Its leaves contain a gel-like substance that is often used for its soothing properties.

Aloe bussei is well adapted to arid environments, making it a hardy and low-maintenance plant for xeriscaping.
