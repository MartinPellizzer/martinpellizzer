Aster tenuis

Aster tenuis, commonly known as slender aster, is a herbaceous perennial native to North America.

It features slender, upright stems covered in fine, hairy hairs. The plant produces small, white to pale blue flowers arranged in clusters at the tips of the stems. Its leaves are lance-shaped, with a rough texture and a slightly hairy surface.

Aster tenuis thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows and along stream banks.
