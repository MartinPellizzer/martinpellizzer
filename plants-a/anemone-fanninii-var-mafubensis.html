Anemone fanninii var. mafubensis

Anemone fanninii var. mafubensis, commonly known as the Mafubense anemone, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, pink to red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant features basal leaves that are palmately lobed and often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it a suitable candidate for rock gardens or borders.

Its ornamental value is complemented by its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
