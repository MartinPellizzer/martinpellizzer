Astronia williamsii

Astronia williamsii, commonly known as the Williams' astronia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters. Astronia williamsii is often found in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests or along stream banks.

Its botanical significance lies in its role as a component of diverse ecosystems and its potential for ornamental use in horticulture.
