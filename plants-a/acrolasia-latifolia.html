Acrolasia latifolia

Acrolasia latifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaved acrolasia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, broad leaves that can reach up to 30 cm in length. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
