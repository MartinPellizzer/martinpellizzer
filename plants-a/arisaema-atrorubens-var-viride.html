Arisaema atrorubens var. viride

Arisaema atrorubens var. viride, commonly known as the green arrowhead, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive spathe and spadix inflorescence. The plant features heart-shaped, arrowhead-like leaves that are typically a vibrant green. Its flowers are usually yellow with a spadix that bears both male and female flowers.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often used in ornamental gardening due to its striking foliage.
