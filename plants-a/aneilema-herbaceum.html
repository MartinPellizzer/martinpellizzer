Aneilema herbaceum

Aneilema herbaceum, commonly known as the African violet, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical Africa.

It features upright, branching stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces showy, clustered flowers in shades of purple, pink, and white, which bloom throughout the year under suitable conditions. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers bright, indirect light.

Aneilema herbaceum is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
