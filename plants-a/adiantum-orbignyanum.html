Adiantum orbignyanum

Adiantum orbignyanum, commonly known as the Orbignya maidenhair fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Adiantaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds that resemble the shape of a feather, giving it its common name. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its leaves are usually bright green and possess a leathery texture, which helps in reducing water loss.

Adiantum orbignyanum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in indoor conditions with proper care.
