Aster tweediei

Aster tweediei, commonly known as the New Zealand aster, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and coastal areas. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered blue or purple flowers. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Aster tweediei is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native environment.
