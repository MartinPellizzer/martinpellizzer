Arisaema yamatense subsp. yamatense

Arisaema yamatense subsp.

yamatense, commonly known as the Yamato arrowhead, is a species native to Japan. It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its distinctive arrowhead-shaped leaves.

The plant produces a spadix surrounded by a spathe, a feature typical of the Araceae family. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it a notable subject in both horticulture and ecological studies.
