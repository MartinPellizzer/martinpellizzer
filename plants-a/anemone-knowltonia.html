Anemone knowltonia

Anemone knowltonia, commonly known as the showy anemone, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the eastern United States.

It features showy, white to pinkish flowers with a distinctive, frilly petal structure. The plant typically grows in moist, woodland habitats and has heart-shaped, palmately lobed leaves. Its flowering period occurs in early spring, and it is often found in shaded or partially shaded environments.

Anemone knowltonia is valued for its ornamental beauty and ecological role in supporting pollinators.
