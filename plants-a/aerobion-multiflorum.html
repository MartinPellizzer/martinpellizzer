Aerobion multiflorum

Aerobion multiflorum, commonly known as the multibloom aerobion, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense clusters of small, fragrant white and pink flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has lance-shaped, glossy leaves that are arranged alternately along its upright stem. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and requires partial to full sunlight for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
