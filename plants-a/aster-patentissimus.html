Aster patentissimus

Aster patentissimus, commonly known as the showy aster, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet and produces vibrant blue to purple flowers in the late summer and early fall. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Aster patentissimus is often found in prairies, meadows, and along roadsides, thriving in full sun and well-drained soil.
