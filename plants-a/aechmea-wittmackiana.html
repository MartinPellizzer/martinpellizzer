Aechmea wittmackiana

Aechmea wittmackiana, commonly known as the Wittmack's pineapple bromeliad, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that form a central cup to collect rainwater. The plant produces striking inflorescences with brightly colored bracts, often in shades of red, orange, or yellow. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often used in ornamental horticulture.

The species is known for its ability to adapt to various growing conditions, making it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
