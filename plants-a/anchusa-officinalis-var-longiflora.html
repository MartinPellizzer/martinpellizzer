Anchusa officinalis var. longiflora

Anchusa officinalis var. longiflora, commonly known as long-flowered velvet flower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe.

It features upright, branching stems covered in soft, velvety foliage. The plant produces clusters of blue or purple tubular flowers that bloom in summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a hairy texture.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or dry habitats.
