Adiantum kaulfussii

Adiantum kaulfussii, commonly known as the Kaulfuss maidenhair fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Adiantaceae and is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds that resemble the shape of a feather. The plant thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in mountainous regions. Its leaves are typically bright green and have a distinctive, elegant appearance.

Adiantum kaulfussii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded gardens.
