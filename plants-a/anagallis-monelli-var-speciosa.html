Anagallis monelli var. speciosa

Anagallis monelli var. speciosa, commonly known as the showy forget-me-not, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a low-growing annual with delicate, blue-violet flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has opposite, hairy leaves that are typically heart-shaped and covered in fine pubescence. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or disturbed areas.

Its striking appearance makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking ornamental value.
