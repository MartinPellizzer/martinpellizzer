Arisaema ruwenzoricum

Arisaema ruwenzoricum, commonly known as the Ruwenzori arrowhead, is a striking tropical plant native to the mountainous regions of East Africa.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its unique inflorescence composed of a spadix surrounded by a spathe. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and dark green coloration. Its flowering period occurs during the wet season, producing bright yellow spadices that attract pollinators.

Due to its rarity and habitat specificity, it is considered a valuable species for botanical research and conservation efforts.
