Acer monspessulanum var. athoum

Acer monspessulanum var. athoum, commonly known as the Athoum maple, is a rare variant of the maple tree native to southern France.

It is characterized by its distinctive, palmate leaves that turn vibrant shades of red and orange in the fall. The plant typically grows as a small to medium-sized tree, reaching heights of up to 10 meters. Its bark is smooth and greyish, becoming fissured with age.
