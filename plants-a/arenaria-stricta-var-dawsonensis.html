Arenaria stricta var. dawsonensis

Arenaria stricta var. dawsonensis, commonly known as Dawson's sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Arctic and subarctic regions.

It typically forms dense mats of narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, white or pinkish flowers in early summer. The plant thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in tundra and alpine habitats. It is adapted to cold climates and can tolerate extreme temperature fluctuations.

Its botanical features make it an important component of high-altitude and polar ecosystems.
