Asterocytisus radiatus

Asterocytisus radiatus, commonly known as the radiant cytisus, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white, typically in late spring. The plant has small, compound leaves and is native to the Mediterranean region. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often used in landscaping and as a cut flower.
