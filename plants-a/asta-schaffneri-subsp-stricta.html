Asta schaffneri subsp. stricta

Asta schaffneri subsp.

stricta, commonly known as the narrowleaf asta, is a flowering plant native to South Africa. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and showy yellow flowers.

The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens due to its compact growth habit and attractive blooms. Asta schaffneri subsp.

stricta is relatively drought-tolerant, making it suitable for xeriscaping in arid regions.
