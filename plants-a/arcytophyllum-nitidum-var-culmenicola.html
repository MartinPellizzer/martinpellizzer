Arcytophyllum nitidum var. culmenicola

Arcytophyllum nitidum var. culmenicola, commonly known as the alpine sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It thrives in rocky and arid environments, showcasing a compact growth habit with dense, mat-forming foliage. The plant features small, rounded leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white wax, aiding in water retention. Its flowers are typically pink to purple, appearing in clusters and blooming during the summer months.

This variety is well adapted to harsh alpine conditions, making it a resilient and hardy species.
