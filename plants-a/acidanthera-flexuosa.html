Acidanthera flexuosa

Acidanthera flexuosa, commonly known as the wandering bell orchid, is a species of flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces small, clustered inflorescences.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
