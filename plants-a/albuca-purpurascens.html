Albuca purpurascens

Albuca purpurascens, commonly known as the purple-flowered albuca, is a bulbous plant native to southern Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces striking, bell-shaped flowers in shades of purple and white. The plant is part of the Asparagaceae family and is often grown for its ornamental value. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Albuca purpurascens is relatively low maintenance and can thrive in a variety of garden settings.
