Agapetes arunachalensis

Agapetes arunachalensis, commonly known as the Arunachal blueberry, is a species of flowering plant in the Ericaceae family.

It is native to the eastern Himalayas, particularly in the Indian state of Arunachal Pradesh. This shrub typically grows at high altitudes, often in moist, shaded forest environments. Its leaves are elliptical and dark green, while its flowers are small and white or pink in color.

The plant produces edible berries that are rich in antioxidants and are valued in local cuisines.
