Arundinella fuscata var. celebica

Arundinella fuscata var. celebica, commonly known as the Celebes bamboo, is a variant of the bamboo species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender culms that can grow up to several meters in height. The plant has a robust root system that helps stabilize soil and prevent erosion. Its leaves are long and narrow, with a smooth texture and a slight sheen.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional crafts in its native regions.
