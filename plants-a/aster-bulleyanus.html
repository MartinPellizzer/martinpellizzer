Aster bulleyanus

Aster bulleyanus, commonly known as the Bulleyan's aster, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 feet. The plant features showy, blue to purple flower heads that bloom in late summer to early fall. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Aster bulleyanus thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands and along stream banks.
