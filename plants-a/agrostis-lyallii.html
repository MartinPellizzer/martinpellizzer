Agrostis lyallii

Agrostis lyallii, commonly known as Lyall's bentgrass, is a perennial grass species native to New Zealand.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and rocky areas. The plant has fine, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It is characterized by its ability to thrive in cool, temperate climates.

Its botanical features make it an important component of native ecosystems in its habitat.
