Actinostemon polymorphus var. longifolius

Actinostemon polymorphus var. longifolius, commonly known as the long-leaved actinostemon, is a species native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its slender, upright stems and elongated, lance-shaped leaves. The leaves are often covered in fine hairs, giving the plant a soft, textured appearance. It produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale green, which bloom during the wet season.

This plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
