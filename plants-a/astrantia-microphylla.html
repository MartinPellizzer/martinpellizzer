Astrantia microphylla

Astrantia microphylla, commonly known as the small-leaved aster, is a herbaceous perennial native to Europe.

It features showy, spherical flower heads that range in color from white to pink, often with a violet or purple center. The plant has finely divided, fern-like leaves that are typically bright green and slightly hairy. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Astrantia microphylla is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
