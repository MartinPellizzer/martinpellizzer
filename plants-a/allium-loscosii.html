Allium loscosii

Allium loscosii, commonly known as the Loscosii garlic, is a perennial bulbous plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Caucasus and Central Asia.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its dense, clustered bulbs and narrow, grass-like leaves. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and culinary potential.
