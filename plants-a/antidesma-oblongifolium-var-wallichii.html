Antidesma oblongifolium var. wallichii

Antidesma oblongifolium var. wallichii, commonly known as the Himalayan antidesma, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It is a small evergreen shrub with elliptical, leathery leaves that are typically dark green in color. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by small, fleshy berries. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural study.
