Aphelandra fulgens

Aphelandra fulgens, commonly known as the golden flame plant, is a striking tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is a member of the Arecaceae family, which includes palms, and is characterized by its large, glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow, candle-like inflorescences that bloom during the warmer months. Aphelandra fulgens is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its vibrant foliage and unique floral display.
