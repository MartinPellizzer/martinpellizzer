Abutilon regnellii

Abutilon regnellii, commonly known as the velvetleaf abutilon, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its fuzzy, velvety leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces showy, lantern-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. It is often found in moist, tropical or subtropical environments.

Abutilon regnellii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various growing conditions.
