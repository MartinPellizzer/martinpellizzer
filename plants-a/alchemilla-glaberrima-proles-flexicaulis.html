Alchemilla glaberrima proles flexicaulis

Alchemilla glaberrima proles flexicaulis, commonly known as the slender lady's mantle, is a low-growing perennial herb native to parts of Europe.

It features basal leaves that are broadly ovate to round, with a distinctive velvety underside. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in panicles during the summer months. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable addition to native plant gardens and ecological restoration projects.
