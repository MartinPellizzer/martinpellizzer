Asplenium polymorphum

Asplenium polymorphum, commonly known as the variable spleenwort, is a species of fern belonging to the family Aspleniaceae.

It is characterized by its small, delicate fronds that are typically less than 10 centimeters in length. The plant exhibits a wide range of morphological variation, which contributes to its common name. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often growing on rocks or tree trunks.

Asplenium polymorphum is widely distributed across temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.
