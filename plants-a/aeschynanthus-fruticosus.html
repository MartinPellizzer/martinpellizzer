Aeschynanthus fruticosus

Aeschynanthus fruticosus, commonly known as the coral vine, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant produces small, elliptical leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated as a decorative plant in hanging baskets or containers.

Its unique appearance and ability to bloom continuously make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
