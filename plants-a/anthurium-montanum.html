Anthurium montanum

Anthurium montanum, commonly known as the mountain anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves and vibrant spadix flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in mountainous regions. Its foliage is dark green with a glossy texture, and it produces showy inflorescences that are popular in horticulture.

Anthurium montanum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in botanical gardens.
