Aloe reinwardtii

Aloe reinwardtii, commonly known as the Reinwardt's aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, with a waxy coating to reduce water loss. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow flowers that bloom in summer. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Aloe reinwardtii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional medicinal uses.
