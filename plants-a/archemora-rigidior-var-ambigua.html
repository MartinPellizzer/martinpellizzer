Archemora rigidior var. ambigua

Archemora rigidior var. ambigua, commonly known as the ambiguous rigid archemora, is a lesser-known variant of the archemora genus.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and distinctive, narrow leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery pubescence.
