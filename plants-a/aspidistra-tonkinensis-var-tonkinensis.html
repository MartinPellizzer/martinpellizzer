Aspidistra tonkinensis var. tonkinensis

Aspidistra tonkinensis var. tonkinensis, commonly known as the Tonkin aspidistra, is a species of flowering plant in the family Araceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly Vietnam, and is characterized by its dark green, strap-like leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are borne on a spadix, typical of the Araceae family. It is a shade-tolerant plant that thrives in humid, cool environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental indoor plant.

Its foliage is highly valued for its aesthetic appeal and ability to thrive in low-light conditions.
