Athyrium distans

Athyrium distans, commonly known as the Japanese shield fern, is a species of fern native to East Asia.

It belongs to the family Athyriaceae and is characterized by its delicate, pinnately compound fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky slopes. Its rhizomes are creeping and produce numerous sporangia on the underside of the fronds.

Athyrium distans is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its elegant foliage.
