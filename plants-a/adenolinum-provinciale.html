Adenolinum provinciale

Adenolinum provinciale, commonly known as the Natal orchid, is a species of orchid native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and yellow. The plant has a compact growth habit with a pseudobulb structure typical of many orchid species. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often arranged in a rosette pattern.

Adenolinum provinciale thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
