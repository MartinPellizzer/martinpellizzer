Achyrocline tamariscina

Achyrocline tamariscina, commonly known as Tamarillo or Yellow Tamarillo, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its yellow-orange flowers and oval-shaped fruits. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, preferring well-drained soils and full sun exposure. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture.

The fruit, which is edible, has a sweet and tangy flavor, making it a popular crop in some parts of the world.
