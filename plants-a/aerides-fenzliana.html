Aerides fenzliana

Aerides fenzliana, commonly known as the Fenzli's orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its upright growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Aerides fenzliana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
