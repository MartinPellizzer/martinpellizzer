Aspalathoides genistae

Aspalathoides genistae, commonly known as the silver leaf bush, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its slender, silver-gray leaves and small yellow flowers. The plant is often found in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. It has a compact growth habit and is valued for its ornamental appeal.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
