Aster oculus-christi

Aster oculus-christi, commonly known as the Christ's eye aster, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads composed of ray and disk florets. The plant features showy, violet-blue flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Aster oculus-christi is often found in rocky or gravelly soils and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
