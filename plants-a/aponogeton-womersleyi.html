Aponogeton womersleyi

Aponogeton womersleyi, commonly known as the African water sword, is a flowering aquatic plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its long, narrow, sword-shaped leaves that float on the water's surface. The plant produces striking, showy flowers that are typically pink or red in color. It thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments and requires good light for optimal growth.

Aponogeton womersleyi is often cultivated in ponds and water gardens for its ornamental value.
