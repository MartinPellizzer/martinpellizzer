Anil candicans

Anil candicans, commonly known as the sacred lotus, is a tropical aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that bloom above the water surface, while its leaves are broad and float on the water. The plant has a complex root system that anchors it in muddy substrates and helps filter water. Its flowers typically range in color from white to pink and are known for their fragrance.

Anil candicans plays an important role in both ecological and cultural contexts in its native regions.
