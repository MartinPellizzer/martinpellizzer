Atriplex hortensis var. rubra

Atriplex hortensis var. rubra, commonly known as red oriole spinach, is a cultivar of the common spinach plant.

It is characterized by its vibrant red stems and leaves, which are typically harvested young for their tender texture and mild flavor. The plant is a biennial, though it is often grown as an annual in agricultural settings. It thrives in cool climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is valued for its nutritional content and adaptability to various growing conditions.
