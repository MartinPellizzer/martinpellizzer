Aloysia polygalifolia

Aloysia polygalifolia, commonly known as the silver leaf honeysuckle, is a shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray leaves and clusters of small, fragrant flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and its ability to attract pollinators. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Aloysia polygalifolia is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
