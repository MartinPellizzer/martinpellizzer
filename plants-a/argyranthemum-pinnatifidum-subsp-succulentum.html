Argyranthemum pinnatifidum subsp. succulentum

Argyranthemum pinnatifidum subsp.

succulentum, commonly known as the silverleaf marguerite, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its succulent, silver-green foliage and daisy-like yellow flowers.

The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers. Its compact growth habit and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for xeriscaping.

The subspecies is distinguished by its finely pinnatifid leaves and showy, long-lasting blooms.
