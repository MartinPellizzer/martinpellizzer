Aetheilema anisophyllum

Aetheilema anisophyllum, commonly known as the woolly flax lily, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage and tall, slender stems that produce small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

It is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
