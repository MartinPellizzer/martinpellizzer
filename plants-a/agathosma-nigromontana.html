Agathosma nigromontana

Agathosma nigromontana, commonly known as the black mountain agathosma, is a succulent shrub native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, glandular leaves that produce a strong, distinctive scent. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments. Its small, daisy-like flowers are usually yellow or orange and bloom during the warmer months.

Agathosma nigromontana is valued for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional herbal remedies.
