Agrostis foliata

Agrostis foliata, commonly known as the silvergrass, is a perennial grass species native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, silver-gray foliage and compact growth habit, making it a popular ornamental plant. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that are typically green or brown in color. Agrostis foliata thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Its low maintenance requirements and ability to form dense mats make it suitable for rock gardens and as a ground cover.
