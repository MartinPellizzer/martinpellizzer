Asparagus equisetoides

Asparagus equisetoides, commonly known as the Chinese asparagus fern, is a perennial plant native to China and other parts of East Asia.

It features a rhizomatous root system and produces slender, leafless stems that bear small, scale-like leaves. The plant is characterized by its ability to form dense mats, often used in landscaping for its ornamental value. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Asparagus equisetoides is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional Chinese medicine.
