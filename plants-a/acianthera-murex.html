Acianthera murex

Acianthera murex, commonly known as the spiked acianthera, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its compact, herbaceous growth habit and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale pink. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its leaves are ovate to lanceolate in shape, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

Acianthera murex is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
