Aeranthes monteverdi

Aeranthes monteverdi, commonly known as the Monteverde orchid, is a species native to the cloud forests of Costa Rica.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its slender, hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers with a white base and deep violet petals. It thrives in humid, shaded environments with well-drained soil.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
