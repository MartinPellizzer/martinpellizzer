Astragalus visibilis

Astragalus visibilis, commonly known as the visible milkvetch, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the western United States.

It typically grows in dry, rocky habitats such as sagebrush steppe and open woodlands. The plant features compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces yellow to orange flowers in late spring to early summer. Its fruit is a legume pod that contains several small, kidney-shaped seeds.

Astragalus visibilis is part of the legume family (Fabaceae) and is known for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil.
