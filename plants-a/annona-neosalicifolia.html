Annona neosalicifolia

Annona neosalicifolia, commonly known as the silky annona, is a tropical tree native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine, silky hair, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces fragrant, yellowish-green flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. Its fruit is a rough, greenish-yellow berry that matures into a yellow or orange color.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and has some traditional medicinal uses.
