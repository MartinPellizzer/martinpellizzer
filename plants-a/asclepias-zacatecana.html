Asclepias zacatecana

Asclepias zacatecana, commonly known as the Zacatecan milkweed, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its milky sap, which is typical of the genus Asclepias. The plant produces showy, clustered flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a hairy texture.
