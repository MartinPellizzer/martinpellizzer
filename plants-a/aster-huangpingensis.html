Aster huangpingensis

Aster huangpingensis, commonly known as the Huangping aster, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers. The plant is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that bloom in the summer months. It typically grows in mountainous regions and is valued for its ornamental appeal.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
