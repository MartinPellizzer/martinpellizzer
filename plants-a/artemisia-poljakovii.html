Artemisia poljakovii

Artemisia poljakovii, commonly known as the Poljakov's wormwood, is a perennial shrub native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, silver-gray foliage. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are typically clustered in dense, spherical heads. It thrives in dry, rocky environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Its essential oils contribute to its medicinal and aromatic properties.
