Alsophila lamoureuxii

Alsophila lamoureuxii, commonly known as the Lamoureux's tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds. The plant has a distinctive crown of fronds that can reach several meters in height, giving it a tropical appearance. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in dense forests.

The rhizome of Alsophila lamoureuxii is thick and fibrous, supporting its upright growth and contributing to its ecological role in the forest ecosystem.
