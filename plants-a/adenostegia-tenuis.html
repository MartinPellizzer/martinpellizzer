Adenostegia tenuis

Adenostegia tenuis, commonly known as the slender stemless dandelion, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its basal rosette of narrow, fleshy leaves. The plant produces small, yellow composite flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in coastal areas.

Adenostegia tenuis is adapted to arid conditions and has a low-growing habit to conserve moisture.
