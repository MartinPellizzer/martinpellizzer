Astragalus mahneshanensis

Astragalus mahneshanensis, commonly known as Mahneshan milkvetch, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, particularly found in Afghanistan and Pakistan. The plant is characterized by its hairy stems, compound leaves, and small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to pink. It produces small, lens-shaped seeds that are often dispersed by wind.

Astragalus mahneshanensis is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, showcasing its resilience to harsh climatic conditions.
