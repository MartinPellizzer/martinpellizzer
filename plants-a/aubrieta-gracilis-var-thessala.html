Aubrieta gracilis var. thessala

Aubrieta gracilis var. thessala, commonly known as the Thessalian aubrieta, is a low-growing, flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features compact, mat-forming growth with bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has small, rounded leaves and is well-suited for rock gardens or as a border plant. It prefers full sun and well-drained soil, making it a drought-tolerant choice for gardeners.

Its ornamental value lies in its vibrant color and hardy nature.
