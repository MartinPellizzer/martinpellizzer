Anogra xylocarpa

Anogra xylocarpa, commonly known as the African snowball tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its clusters of white, fluffy flowers that resemble snowballs, blooming in late winter to early spring. The plant has compound leaves with serrated edges and produces small, woody fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas and sandy soils.

The botanical features of Anogra xylocarpa make it a distinctive and ornamental plant in its native habitat.
