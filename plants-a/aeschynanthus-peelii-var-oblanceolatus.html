Aeschynanthus peelii var. oblanceolatus

Aeschynanthus peelii var. oblanceolatus, commonly known as the Peeli's ivy-leaved gloxinia, is a tropical flowering plant native to western Africa.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and striking, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. The leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a velvety texture, while the stems are flexible and often cascading. This species thrives in humid, warm environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

It is popular among gardeners for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation in suitable conditions.
