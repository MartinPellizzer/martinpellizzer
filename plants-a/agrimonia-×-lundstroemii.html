Agrimonia × lundstroemii

Agrimonia × lundstroemii, commonly known as the hybrid agrimony, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is a natural hybrid between Agrimonia eupatoria and Agrimonia pilosa, exhibiting characteristics of both parent species. The plant typically grows to a height of 30–60 cm, with upright, branching stems covered in soft, grayish hairs. Its leaves are pinnately lobed, and it produces small, yellowish flowers in clusters during the summer months.

Agrimonia × lundstroemii is often found in disturbed soils and open habitats, such as meadows and roadsides.
