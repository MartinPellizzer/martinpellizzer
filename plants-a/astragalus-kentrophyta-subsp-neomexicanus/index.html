Astragalus Kentrophyta Subsp. Neomexicanus

Astragalus kentrophyta subsp. neomexicanus is a rare flowering plant native to the southwestern united states. it belongs to the legume family and is known for its distinctive blue to violet flowers. this subspecies is found primarily in arid and semi-arid regions of new mexico. it plays an important role in local ecosystems by providing nectar for pollinators. due to habitat loss and limited distribution, it is considered a species of conservation concern.
