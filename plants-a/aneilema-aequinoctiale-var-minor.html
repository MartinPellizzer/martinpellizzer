Aneilema aequinoctiale var. minor

Aneilema aequinoctiale var. minor, commonly known as the small equinox lily, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive inflorescence composed of a spadix and a spathe. The plant produces showy, white to pale yellow flowers, often with a subtle fragrance, and thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are typically broad and heart-shaped, providing a lush, green backdrop to its delicate blooms.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
