Adonis magellanica

Adonis magellanica, commonly known as the Magellan lily, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its showy, cup-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to temperate climates. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs.

Adonis magellanica is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
