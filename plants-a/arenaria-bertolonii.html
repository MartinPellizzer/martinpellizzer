Arenaria bertolonii

Arenaria bertolonii, commonly known as the Bertoloni's sandwort, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically thrives in sandy or rocky soils. The plant produces small, white or pink flowers during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are usually succulent, adapted to conserve water in arid environments.

Arenaria bertolonii is often found in coastal areas and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
