Astragalus ningxiaensis

Astragalus ningxiaensis, commonly known as Ningxia milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of Ningxia, China.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its slender stems and small, yellowish flowers. The plant has small, compound leaves and produces pods that contain kidney-shaped seeds. It is adapted to survive in dry, rocky environments and is often found in steppe and desert ecosystems.

Due to its limited distribution and ecological significance, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
