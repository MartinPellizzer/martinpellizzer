Anthurium fasciale

Anthurium fasciale, commonly known as the velvet leaf anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive spathe and spadix inflorescence. The plant features dark green, velvety foliage with a prominent midrib and waxy texture. Its flowers are typically small and clustered, with a spadix that can range in color from white to yellow.

Anthurium fasciale is often cultivated for its ornamental value and air-purifying qualities.
