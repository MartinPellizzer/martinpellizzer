Aster florulentus

Aster florulentus, commonly known as the showy aster, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies, sunflowers, and other composite flowers. The plant is characterized by its vibrant blue to purple flower heads, which are composed of numerous small florets. It typically grows in moist, woodland habitats and thrives in partial to full sunlight.

Aster florulentus is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
