Anthurium amoenum

Anthurium amoenum, commonly known as the velvet leaf anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically deep green with a velvety texture. The plant produces bright red spadices, which are the spathes that enclose the true flowers. Anthurium amoenum thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

It is popular as a decorative plant due to its ornamental foliage and unique floral structure.
