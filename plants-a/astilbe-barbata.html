Astilbe barbata

Astilbe barbata, commonly known as the feathered astilbe, is a herbaceous perennial native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its upright, branching stems that bear clusters of small, delicate flowers in shades of pink, red, or white. The plant produces feathery, plume-like inflorescences that give it a soft, airy appearance. It thrives in partial to full shade and prefers moist, well-drained soil.

Astilbe barbata is valued in gardens for its ornamental foliage and prolonged blooming period.
