Asterocephalus columnae var. holosericea

Asterocephalus columnae var. holosericea, commonly known as the silky columnar cactus, is a unique succulent native to the arid regions of South America.

It is characterized by its tall, unbranched stems that can grow up to several meters in height. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale pink flowers during the warmer months. Its leaves are reduced to spines, and its stems are covered in a dense layer of fine, silky hairs.

This variant is particularly noted for its striking appearance and ability to thrive in harsh, dry environments.
