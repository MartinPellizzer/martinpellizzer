Asplenium cultrifolium var. moorianum

Asplenium cultrifolium var. moorianum, commonly known as the Moorman's sword fern, is a variant of the sword fern species native to New Zealand.

It is a fern characterized by its upright, sword-shaped fronds that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories. Its spores are produced on the undersides of the fronds, which are typically clustered in sori.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with appropriate humidity and light conditions.
