Anil spachii

Anil spachii, commonly known as the Indian nettle, is a herbaceous plant native to South Asia.

It belongs to the Urticaceae family, which includes other stinging plants. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a rough, hairy texture and produces small greenish-white flowers. It is often found in moist, shaded areas such as forests and wetlands.

Anil spachii is known for its stinging hairs that can cause skin irritation upon contact.
