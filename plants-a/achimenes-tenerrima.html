Achimenes tenerrima

Achimenes tenerrima, commonly known as the flame violet, is a tropical perennial plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange and yellow. The plant produces showy bracts that often resemble the flowers, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Achimenes tenerrima is often cultivated as a bedding plant due to its ability to bloom profusely during the summer months.
