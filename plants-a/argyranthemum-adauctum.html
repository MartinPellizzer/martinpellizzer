Argyranthemum adauctum

Argyranthemum adauctum, commonly known as the silver geranium, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silver-green foliage and showy, daisy-like flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant is a member of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is known for its compact growth habit. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

The flowers are often used in floral arrangements due to their delicate appearance and long-lasting blooms.
