Adenophora polyantha subsp. polyantha

Adenophora polyantha subsp.

polyantha, commonly known as the Chinese bellflower, is a herbaceous perennial native to China. It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, often with a contrasting throat.

The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in woodland or meadow habitats. Its leaves are opposite, lanceolate, and covered in fine hairs.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in partial shade.
