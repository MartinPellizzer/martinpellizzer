Anagallis arvensis subsp. latifolia

Anagallis arvensis subsp.

latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf mouse-ear, is a flowering plant belonging to the Primulaceae family. It is characterized by its small, spurred flowers that are typically pink or purple in color.
