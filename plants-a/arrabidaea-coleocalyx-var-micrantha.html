Arrabidaea coleocalyx var. micrantha

Arrabidaea coleocalyx var. micrantha, commonly known as the small-flowered arrabidaea, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, typically during the cooler months. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are often hairy and have a rough texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens, though it requires specific care to flourish.
