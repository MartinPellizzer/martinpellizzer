Agave dasylirioides

Agave dasylirioides, commonly known as the narrow-leaved agave, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a succulent plant characterized by its rosette-forming structure and thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces a tall, branched inflorescence that bears white to pale yellow flowers. It is adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Its striking appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
