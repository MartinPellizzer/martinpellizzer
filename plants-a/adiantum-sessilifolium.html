Adiantum sessilifolium

Adiantum sessilifolium, commonly known as the shield fern, is a species of fern characterized by its delicate, shield-shaped fronds.

It belongs to the family Adiantaceae and is native to various regions in the Americas. The plant features bipinnate leaves that are typically dark green and have a finely divided appearance. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Adiantum sessilifolium is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
