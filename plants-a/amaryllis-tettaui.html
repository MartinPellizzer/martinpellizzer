Amaryllis tettaui

Amaryllis tettaui, commonly known as the Tettaui amaryllis, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that come in a range of colors, including red, orange, and yellow. The plant typically blooms in late summer or early autumn, adding vibrant color to gardens. Its leaves are broad and strap-like, often emerging after the flowers have faded.

Amaryllis tettaui is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking a dramatic, seasonal display.
