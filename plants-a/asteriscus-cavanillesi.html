Asteriscus cavanillesi

Asteriscus cavanillesi, commonly known as the Cavanilles' asterisk, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white, typically in the summer months. The plant has slender, branching stems covered in fine, grayish hairs. Its leaves are narrow and opposite, with a slightly serrated edge.

Asteriscus cavanillesi is well adapted to dry, rocky soils and is often found in coastal areas.
