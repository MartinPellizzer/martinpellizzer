Aster limprichtii var. gracilior

Aster limprichtii var. gracilior, commonly known as the slender aster, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of central China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of ray and disc florets. The plant typically grows in rocky or well-drained soils and thrives in temperate climates. Its showy blue to purple flowers bloom in late summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

The variety gracilior is distinguished by its more delicate and slender growth habit compared to the typical form of Aster limprichtii.
