Anacamptis × gerakarionis

Anacamptis × gerakarionis, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a natural hybrid between Anacamptis morio and Anacamptis pyramidalis.

It is a terrestrial herbaceous plant with a rhizomatous root system and slender, upright stems. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing numerous fragrant, tubular flowers in shades of pink and white. It thrives in moist, calcareous soils and is typically found in wet meadows and marshy areas.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting pollinators.
