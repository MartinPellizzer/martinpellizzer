Aerides turma

Aerides turma, commonly known as the Turma orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its delicate, linear leaves and clustered, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its inflorescence consists of multiple small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom during the warmer months.

Aerides turma is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
