Acer campestre f. josifovicii

Acer campestre f. josifovicii, commonly known as the Josifovici's hedge maple, is a compact, slow-growing ornamental shrub or small tree native to Central Europe.

It features narrow, ovate to elliptical leaves that are typically dark green on top and slightly hairy underneath. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in early spring, followed by red or orange samaras that persist into winter. It thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it suitable for garden borders or hedges.

This cultivar is prized for its dense growth habit and seasonal color changes, adding visual interest to landscapes throughout the year.
