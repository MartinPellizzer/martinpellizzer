Ammannia Robinsoniana

Ammannia robinsoniana is a striking wetland plant known for its vibrant red flowers and unique ecological role. Native to the southeastern United States, it thrives in swampy and pond environments. This species is often overlooked despite its ecological importance in supporting local wildlife. Its ability to tolerate waterlogged soils makes it a key indicator of healthy wetland ecosystems. The plant's beauty and resilience make it a valuable subject for both conservation and botanical study.
