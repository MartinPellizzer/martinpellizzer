Astronia atroviridis

Astronia atroviridis, commonly known as the black-veined aster, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dark green, velvety foliage. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in composite flower heads. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy habitats.

Astronia atroviridis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
