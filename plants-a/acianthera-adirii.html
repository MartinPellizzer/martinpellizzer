Acianthera adirii

Acianthera adirii, commonly known as the Adirii's orchid, is a tropical flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant has strap-like leaves and produces small, clustered inflorescences. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and valuable species in botanical collections.
