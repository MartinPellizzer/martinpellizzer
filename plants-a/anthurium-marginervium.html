Anthurium marginervium

Anthurium marginervium, commonly known as the velvet leaf anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a dense, velvety texture. The plant produces striking spadices, which are the modified leaves that house the actual flowers. Anthurium marginervium prefers bright, indirect light and high humidity to thrive.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and ornamental purposes.
