Ardisia lindenii

Ardisia lindenii, commonly known as the Lindenberg's ardisia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and produces small, white flowers followed by red, fleshy berries. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Ardisia lindenii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
