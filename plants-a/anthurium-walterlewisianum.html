Anthurium walterlewisianum

Anthurium walterlewisianum, commonly known as the Walter Lewis anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are dark green with a velvety texture. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-like inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, typically in red, orange, or yellow. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Anthurium walterlewisianum is popular as a ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and air-purifying properties.
