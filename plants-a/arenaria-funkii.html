Arenaria funkii

Arenaria funkii, commonly known as the Funk's sandwort, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that typically forms dense mats of foliage. The plant produces small, white or pinkish flowers that bloom in early summer. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to cold, alpine environments.

Its botanical features include narrow leaves and a compact growth habit, making it well-suited for harsh mountain climates.
