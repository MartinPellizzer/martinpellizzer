Abies fortunei

Abies fortunei, commonly known as the Chinese fir, is a coniferous evergreen tree native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its conical shape, dark green foliage, and distinctive bark that becomes fissured with age. The tree produces small, spherical cones that contain winged seeds, typical of many fir species. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in mountainous regions.

Its wood is valued for its durability and resistance to decay, making it useful in construction and furniture-making.
