Aspidium lunense

Aspidium lunense, commonly known as the moon fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds that are typically light green in color. The plant produces spores in small, round structures called sori, which are located on the underside of the fronds. Aspidium lunense is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia and is often found growing in shaded, moist environments.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
