Aubletiana macrostachys

Aubletiana macrostachys, commonly known as the giant flame tree, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy orange-red flowers that bloom in clusters, creating a dramatic visual impact. The plant has compound leaves with elongated leaflets, and its trunk is often smooth and greyish in color. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Aubletiana macrostachys is also valued for its ability to provide shade and support biodiversity in its ecosystem.
