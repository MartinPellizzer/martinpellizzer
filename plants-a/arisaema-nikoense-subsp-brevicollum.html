Arisaema nikoense subsp. brevicollum

Arisaema nikoense subsp.

brevicollum, commonly known as the dwarf arrowhead plant, is a member of the Araceae family. It is characterized by its distinctive arrow-shaped leaves and a spadix surrounded by a spathe.

Native to the mountainous regions of Japan, this species thrives in cool, moist environments. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters, making it a compact and ornamental choice for gardens.

Its botanical features make it a popular subject for both horticultural and scientific study.
