Argyrolobium pseudotuberosum

Argyrolobium pseudotuberosum, commonly known as the silver globular pea, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive silver-gray foliage and spherical, tuberous root system. The plant produces small, yellow, pea-like flowers that bloom in late spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists.
