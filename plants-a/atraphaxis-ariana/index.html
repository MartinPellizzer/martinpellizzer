Atraphaxis Ariana

Atraphaxis ariana is a rare and resilient plant native to the arid regions of the Middle East. It thrives in extreme desert conditions, showcasing remarkable adaptations to survive with minimal water. This species is characterized by its compact growth form and tough, waxy leaves that reduce water loss. Its unique features make it an important subject for ecological and botanical study. Despite its hardiness, it remains threatened by habitat loss and climate change.
