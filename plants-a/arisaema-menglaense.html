Arisaema menglaense

Arisaema menglaense, commonly known as Mengla arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to southern China.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its unique inflorescence consisting of a spadix and a spathe. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its leaves are arrowhead-shaped, and it produces striking, colorful flowers that attract pollinators.

Arisaema menglaense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
