Anthemis caespitosa

Anthemis caespitosa, commonly known as the common yarrow, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in dry, sunny environments such as grasslands and rocky soils. The plant features a compact, bushy growth habit with numerous small, bright yellow flowers arranged in flat-topped clusters. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often covered in fine hairs, while its stems are sturdy and branching.

Anthemis caespitosa is known for its aromatic properties and has been used in traditional herbal medicine for various purposes.
