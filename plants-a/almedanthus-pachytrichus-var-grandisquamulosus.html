Almedanthus pachytrichus var. grandisquamulosus

It is characterized by its thick, scale-like leaves that provide a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in arid environments, showcasing adaptations to harsh, dry conditions. Its foliage is dense and often forms a low, mat-like structure.
