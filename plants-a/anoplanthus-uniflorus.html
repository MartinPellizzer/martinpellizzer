Anoplanthus uniflorus

Anoplanthus uniflorus, commonly known as the single-flower rush, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in wetland environments, such as marshes and along the edges of lakes and rivers. The plant has slender, grass-like leaves that are often submerged or partially submerged in water. It produces a single, erect flowering spike that bears small, inconspicuous flowers.

Anoplanthus uniflorus plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for aquatic organisms.
