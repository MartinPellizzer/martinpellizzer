Anthericum rossii

Anthericum rossii, commonly known as the Ross's lily, is a rare and endangered plant species native to the eastern United States.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces showy, white or pale pink flowers with a distinct, sweet fragrance. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.
