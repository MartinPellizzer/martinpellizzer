Aster lancifolius

Aster lancifolius, commonly known as the lanceleaf aster, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands. It features lance-shaped leaves and produces showy blue to purple flowers in the fall.

Aster lancifolius is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits to pollinators.
