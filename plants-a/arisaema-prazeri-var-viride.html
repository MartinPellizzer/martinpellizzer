Arisaema prazeri var. viride

Arisaema prazeri var. viride, commonly known as the green arrowhead, is a herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its distinctive arrow-shaped leaves. The plant produces a spadix inflorescence surrounded by a modified leaf called a spathe. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it a notable species in both horticultural and ecological contexts.
