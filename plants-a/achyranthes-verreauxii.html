Achyranthes verreauxii

Achyranthes verreauxii, commonly known as the African balsam, is a flowering plant native to Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features alternate, ovate leaves with a rough texture and produces clusters of small, pink or purple flowers. It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its fibrous root system.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional medicinal uses.
