Anthyllis vulneraria subsp. ajmasiana

Anthyllis vulneraria subsp.

ajmasiana, commonly known as the Ajman thornvetch, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae. It is characterized by its erect or semi-erect growth habit and produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in racemes.

The plant has trifoliate leaves with serrated margins, and its stems are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in sandy or rocky soils.

This subspecies is native to parts of the Middle East and is adapted to withstand drought conditions.
