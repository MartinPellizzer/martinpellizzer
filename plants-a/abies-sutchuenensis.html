Abies sutchuenensis

Abies sutchuenensis, commonly known as the Sichuan fir, is a species of fir native to the mountainous regions of Sichuan, China.

It is a coniferous evergreen tree that typically grows at high altitudes, often in moist, cool climates. The tree has a conical shape with closely packed, needle-like leaves that are dark green and slightly curved. Its cones are woody and usually hang downward, containing seeds that are dispersed by wind.

This species plays an important ecological role in its native habitat, providing habitat for various wildlife species.
