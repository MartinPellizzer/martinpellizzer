Ameletia tenella

Ameletia tenella, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture, and it produces small, greenish-white flowers. Its fruit is a capsule that contains numerous tiny seeds.

Ameletia tenella is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands.
