Arundinaria yonoskei

Arundinaria yonoskei, commonly known as the Yonosuke bamboo, is a species native to Japan.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and narrow leaves. This bamboo is typically found in moist, shaded environments such as mountain forests. Its growth habit is clump-forming, and it can reach heights of up to 10 meters.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
