Anthurium acutissimum

Anthurium acutissimum, commonly known as the sharp-leaved anthurium, is a tropical flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped spathes that can range in color from deep red to orange and yellow. The plant produces striking, spadix-like inflorescences that are often surrounded by the colorful spathe. Its leaves are elongated, leathery, and have a sharp, pointed tip, giving it its common name.

Anthurium acutissimum thrives in humid, warm environments and is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant.
