Aneilema ovalifolium

Aneilema ovalifolium, commonly known as the oval-leaf aneilema, is a tropical plant native to regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its broad, oval-shaped leaves that are typically arranged alternately along the stems. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually white or pale pink, which bloom during the rainy season. Aneilema ovalifolium thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for ecological studies and ornamental horticulture.
