Ascyrum multicaule

Ascyrum multicaule, commonly known as the many-stemmed orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands. The plant features a slender, branching stem system with numerous small, clustered leaves. Its flowers are small and greenish-white, often arranged in loose inflorescences.

Ascyrum multicaule is notable for its ability to thrive in humid conditions and its role in local ecosystems.
