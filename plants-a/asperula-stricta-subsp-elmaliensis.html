Asperula stricta subsp. elmaliensis

Asperula stricta subsp.

elmaliensis, commonly known as the Elmaliensis shrub, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the eastern Mediterranean region. It features small, glossy, dark green leaves that are elliptical in shape and slightly hairy on the underside.

The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale pink flowers in early spring, which are followed by small, red berries. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in mild climates.
