Abies excelsa var. remontii

Abies excelsa var. remontii, commonly known as the Himalayan fir, is a variant of the noble fir native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender form and dense, dark green foliage. The tree produces cones that are typically cylindrical and have a distinctive shape. Its bark is smooth and grayish, becoming fissured with age.

This variant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in horticulture for its aesthetic appeal.
