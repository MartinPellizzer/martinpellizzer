Aglaonema helferi

Aglaonema helferi, commonly known as the Helfer's arum.

This plant is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its striking, variegated foliage. Its leaves are typically broad and heart-shaped, with a glossy texture and vibrant color patterns. Aglaonema helferi thrives in low-light conditions and requires moderate humidity to maintain its ornamental appearance.

It is a popular houseplant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
