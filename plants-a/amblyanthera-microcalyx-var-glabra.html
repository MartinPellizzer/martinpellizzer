Amblyanthera microcalyx var. glabra

Amblyanthera microcalyx var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth amblyanthera, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. The plant has smooth, glabrous leaves and stems, which distinguish it from other varieties in the genus. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
