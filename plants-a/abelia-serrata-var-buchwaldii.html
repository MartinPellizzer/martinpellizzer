Abelia serrata var. buchwaldii

Abelia serrata var. buchwaldii, commonly known as the Buchwald abelia, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features showy, tubular pink to white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has glossy, ovate leaves with serrated edges, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and adaptability to various garden conditions.
