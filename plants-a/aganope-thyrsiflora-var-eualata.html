Aganope thyrsiflora var. eualata

Aganope thyrsiflora var. eualata, commonly known as the golden thorn.

This variant is a shrub native to southern Africa, characterized by its dense, spiny stems and clusters of small, yellowish flowers. The leaves are typically narrow and opposite, with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or coastal habitats.

The plant is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its unique appearance.
