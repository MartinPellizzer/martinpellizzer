Arabis amplexicaulis var. serrata

The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers in early spring, which are arranged in racemes. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is adapted to cold, alpine environments.

Due to its ornamental value and hardiness, it is occasionally cultivated in rock gardens and alpine settings.
