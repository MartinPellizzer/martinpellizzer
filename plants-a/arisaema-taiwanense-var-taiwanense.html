Arisaema taiwanense var. taiwanense

Arisaema taiwanense var. taiwanense, commonly known as the Taiwan arrowhead, is a herbaceous plant native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its distinctive arrow-shaped leaves and a spadix surrounded by a spathe. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as mountain forests. It produces small, greenish-white flowers that are followed by yellow berries.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
