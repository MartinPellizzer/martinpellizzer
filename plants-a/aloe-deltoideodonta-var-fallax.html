Aloe deltoideodonta var. fallax

Aloe deltoideodonta var. fallax, commonly known as the Cape aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, with a waxy coating to reduce water loss. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow-orange flowers, which bloom in summer. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal watering.

Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine and for their soothing properties.
