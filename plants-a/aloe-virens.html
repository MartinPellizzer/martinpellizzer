Aloe virens

Aloe virens, commonly known as the silver aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features tall, narrow, blue-green leaves that are covered in a fine, silvery down. The plant produces yellow flowers in clusters during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil.

Aloe virens is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to store water in its fleshy leaves.
