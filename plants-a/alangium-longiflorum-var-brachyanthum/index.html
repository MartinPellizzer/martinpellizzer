Alangium Longiflorum Var. Brachyanthum

Alangium longiflorum var. brachyanthum is a rare and unique variant of the alangium plant family. It is native to specific regions in Southeast Asia and is known for its striking floral characteristics. This plant features elongated, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, fragrant flowers. Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a delicate and endangered species. Its beauty and rarity make it a subject of interest for botanists and plant enthusiasts alike.
