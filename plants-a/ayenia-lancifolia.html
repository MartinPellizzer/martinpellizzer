Ayenia lancifolia

Ayenia lancifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved ayenia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.

Ayenia lancifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its distinctive foliage and blooms.
