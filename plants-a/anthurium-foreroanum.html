Anthurium foreroanum

Anthurium foreroanum, commonly known as the Forero anthurium, is a tropical plant native to South America, particularly Colombia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves and vibrant, spadix-like inflorescence. The plant thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light. Its foliage is often a deep green, while the spadix can range from red to yellow, depending on the variety.

Anthurium foreroanum is popular as a decorative plant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
