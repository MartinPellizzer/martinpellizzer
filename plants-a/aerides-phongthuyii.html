Aerides phongthuyii

Aerides phongthuyii, commonly known as the Phong Thuy orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its erect, hairy stems and large, showy flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its flowers are usually pale yellow to greenish-white with prominent purple or brown markings.

This species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
