Asparagus spinosissimus

Asparagus spinosissimus, commonly known as the spiny asparagus, is a perennial shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, spiny foliage and clusters of small, white flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces small, edible berries that are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rocky or arid landscapes.

Its ornamental value comes from its unique appearance and hardy nature.
