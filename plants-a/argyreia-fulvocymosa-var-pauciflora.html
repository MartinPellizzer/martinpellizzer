Argyreia fulvocymosa var. pauciflora

Argyreia fulvocymosa var. pauciflora, commonly known as the pink morning glory, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking pink to purple flowers that bloom in the early morning. The plant is a vigorous climber with heart-shaped leaves and a trailing growth habit. It belongs to the Convolvulaceae family, which includes other species of morning glories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
