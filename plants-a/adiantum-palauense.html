Adiantum palauense

Adiantum palauense, commonly known as the Palau fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Adiantaceae.

It is native to the island nation of Palau in the western Pacific Ocean. The plant is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds that resemble a feather, giving it a graceful appearance. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or along stream banks.

Adiantum palauense is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
