Agathosma schlechtendalii

Agathosma schlechtendalii, commonly known as African geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Geraniaceae and is characterized by its small, clustered flowers and aromatic leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
