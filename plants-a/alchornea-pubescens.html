Alchornea pubescens

Alchornea pubescens, commonly known as the hairy alchornea, is a tropical shrub belonging to the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is characterized by its hairy, ovate leaves that are often reddish in color. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is native to parts of Central and South America.

Alchornea pubescens is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
