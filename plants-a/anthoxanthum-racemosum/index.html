Anthoxanthum Racemosum

Anthoxanthum racemosum is a unique grass species known for its distinctive yellowish-green foliage and dense growth habit. It thrives in moist, acidic soils and is commonly found in wetlands and along stream banks. This plant plays an important role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various small organisms. Its ability to tolerate waterlogged conditions makes it a valuable species in ecological restoration projects. Despite its modest appearance, Anthoxanthum racemosum contributes significantly to the biodiversity of its native environments.
