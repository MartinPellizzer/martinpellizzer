Acer huangpingense

Acer huangpingense, commonly known as the Huangping maple, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It belongs to the genus Acer, which includes various species of maple trees. The plant is characterized by its distinctive palmate leaves that turn vibrant shades of red and yellow in autumn. It typically grows in mountainous regions with a temperate climate.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and for landscape purposes.
