Acaena cylindristachya var. nitidissima

Acaena cylindristachya var. nitidissima, commonly known as the silky burrplant, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is a low-growing shrub characterized by its dense, spiny foliage and small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant produces spherical, woolly fruits that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by animals. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The variety nitidissima is distinguished by its smoother, more glossy leaves compared to other varieties of Acaena cylindristachya.
