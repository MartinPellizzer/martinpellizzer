Anemone messarensis

Anemone messarensis, commonly known as the Sicilian anemone, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features showy, white to pinkish flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has finely divided, fern-like leaves that are typically bluish-green in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.

Anemone messarensis is often found in rocky or coastal areas, showcasing its adaptability to harsh environments.
