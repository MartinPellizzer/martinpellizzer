Aristida vulgaris var. senegalensis

Aristida vulgaris var. senegalensis, commonly known as the Senegalese dropseed, is a perennial grass native to Africa and parts of the Middle East.

It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. The plant has slender, erect stems and produces small, fluffy panicles of seeds. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green color.

It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
