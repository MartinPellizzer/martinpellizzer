Aristolochia egensis

Aristolochia egensis, commonly known as the Brazilian birthwort, is a climbing vine native to South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive, heart-shaped leaves and its unique, intricate flowers that resemble a pipe or a funnel. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are often covered in fine hairs. Its fruit is a smooth, elongated capsule that contains numerous tiny seeds.

Aristolochia egensis is valued in traditional medicine but contains toxic compounds that require careful handling.
