Aristida wachteri

Aristida wachteri, commonly known as Wachter's grass, is a perennial grass species native to arid and semi-arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, spike-like inflorescences that are typically pale green to golden in color. The plant has narrow, linear leaves with a hairy texture, which helps reduce water loss in dry environments. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open grasslands and rocky areas.

Aristida wachteri is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in stabilizing soil in degraded habitats.
