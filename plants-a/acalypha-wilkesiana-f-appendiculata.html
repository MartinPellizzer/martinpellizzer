Acalypha wilkesiana f. appendiculata

Acalypha wilkesiana f. appendiculata, commonly known as the silver flame plant, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety silver-green foliage with reddish veins, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color. It thrives in warm, humid environments and prefers well-drained soil.

This cultivar is often used in ornamental gardening for its aesthetic appeal and ability to thrive in indoor or outdoor settings.
