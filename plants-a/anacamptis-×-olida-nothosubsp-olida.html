Anacamptis × olida nothosubsp. olida

Anacamptis × olida nothosubsp.

olida, commonly known as the hybrid lady's tresses, is a member of the Orchidaceae family. It is a rhizomatous perennial herb with a distinctive inflorescence of dense, spiraled flowers.

The plant typically grows in moist, calcareous habitats such as meadows and wetlands. Its flowers are usually pale purple to white, with a spurred lip that aids in pollination by insects.

This hybrid species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting pollinator populations.
