Aeschynanthus speciosus

Aeschynanthus speciosus, commonly known as the jewel orchid, is a member of the Gesneriaceae family and is native to tropical regions of Africa and Southeast Asia.

It is an evergreen shrub characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that come in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant produces showy bracts that often resemble petals, enhancing its ornamental appeal. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Aeschynanthus speciosus is popular among horticulturists for its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
