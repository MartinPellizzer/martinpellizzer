Araujia Angustifolia

Araujia angustifolia is a unique flowering plant native to South America. It is known for its striking bioluminescent properties under certain conditions. The plant has been studied for its ecological and medicinal potential. Its seeds are dispersed by animals that eat its fruit. This plant remains a subject of scientific interest due to its unusual characteristics.
