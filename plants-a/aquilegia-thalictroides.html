Aquilegia thalictroides

Aquilegia thalictroides, commonly known as the yellow monkeyflower, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its showy, spurred petals that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant typically grows in moist, rocky habitats and has a short growing season. Its leaves are trifoliate, with three leaflets that are usually serrated.
