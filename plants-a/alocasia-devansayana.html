Alocasia devansayana

Alocasia devansayana, commonly known as the Devansayana arrowleaf, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often dark green with a glossy texture. The plant features a stout, rhizomatous stem that allows it to spread and form dense clumps. Alocasia devansayana thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires consistent moisture and well-draining soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical landscapes.
