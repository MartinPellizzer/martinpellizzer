Anguillaria triquetra

Anguillaria triquetra, commonly known as the three-angled arrowhead, is a tropical aquatic plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive three-lobed leaves that are often submerged in water. The plant thrives in shallow, slow-moving waters and is often found in marshy or swampy habitats. Its leaves are typically dark green and have a waxy texture, which helps in reducing water loss.

Anguillaria triquetra is popular in aquariums due to its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.
