Andreettaea cabellensis

Andreettaea cabellensis, commonly known as the Brazilian feather grass, is a flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Poaceae family, which includes grasses, and is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in warm climates.
