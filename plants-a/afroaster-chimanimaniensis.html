Afroaster chimanimaniensis

Afroaster chimanimaniensis, commonly known as the Chimanimani aster, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, with ray florets surrounding a central disk.

Afroaster chimanimaniensis is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
