Allium Paniculatum Var. Legitimum

Allium paniculatum var. legitimum is a striking member of the onion family known for its vibrant purple flowers and robust growth. Native to the mountainous regions of central Asia, this plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun. Its distinctive spherical inflorescence makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders. While it is hardy and low maintenance, it requires careful attention to soil conditions for optimal growth. This variety is also valued for its edible bulbs, which offer a mild garlic-like flavor.
