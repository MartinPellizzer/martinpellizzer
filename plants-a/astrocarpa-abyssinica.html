Astrocarpa abyssinica

Astrocarpa abyssinica, commonly known as the Abyssinian starflower, is a flowering plant native to Ethiopia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to the high-altitude environments of its native region.

Astrocarpa abyssinica is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphology and potential ecological significance in its habitat.
