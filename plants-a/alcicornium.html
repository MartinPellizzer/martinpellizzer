Alcicornium

Alcicornium, commonly known as the silverleaf orchid, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking silver-green leaves and clusters of delicate, fragrant flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it well-suited for drought-prone environments. Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice for xeriscaping and rock gardens.

Alcicornium is also valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal.
