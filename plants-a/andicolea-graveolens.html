Andicolea graveolens

Andicolea graveolens, commonly known as the stinking nightshade, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other nightshades such as tomatoes and potatoes. The plant is characterized by its small, bell-shaped flowers and hairy stems. Its leaves are typically ovate and have a rough texture.
