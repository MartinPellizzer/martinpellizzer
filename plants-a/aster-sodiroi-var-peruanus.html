Aster sodiroi var. peruanus

Aster sodiroi var. peruanus, commonly known as the Peruvian aster, is a flowering plant native to the Andean regions of South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically produces showy blue to purple flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in high-altitude grasslands.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens, though it is more commonly encountered in its natural habitat.
