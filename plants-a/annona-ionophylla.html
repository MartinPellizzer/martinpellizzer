Annona ionophylla

Annona ionophylla, commonly known as the yellow annona, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is a small to medium-sized tree that can grow up to 10 meters tall, with a broad, spreading canopy. The plant produces large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green color. Its flowers are yellow with a distinctive red or purple calyx, and it bears yellow or greenish-yellow fruits that are edible.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
