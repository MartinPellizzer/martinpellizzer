Aegiphila moldenkeana

Aegiphila moldenkeana, commonly known as the Brazilian mountain mint, is a flowering plant native to the highlands of Brazil.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its small, tubular flowers are typically purple or pink and are arranged in dense clusters.

Aegiphila moldenkeana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
