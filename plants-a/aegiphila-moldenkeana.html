Aegiphila moldenkeana

Aegiphila moldenkeana, commonly known as the Brazilian mountain mint, is a flowering plant native to the highlands of Brazil.

Aegiphila moldenkeana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
