Anoectochilus setaceus

Anoectochilus setaceus, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and delicate, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically pale yellow or greenish-white. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, acidic soil.

Anoectochilus setaceus is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in a tropical greenhouse setting.
