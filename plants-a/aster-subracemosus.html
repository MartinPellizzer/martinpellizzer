Aster subracemosus

Aster subracemosus, commonly known as the slender aster, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads and medicinal properties. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and woodlands. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are blue to purple in color.

Aster subracemosus is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
