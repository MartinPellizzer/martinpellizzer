Anemone nuttalliana

Anemone nuttalliana, commonly known as the Nuttall's anemone, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the western United States.

It features showy, white to pale pink flowers with a distinctive central tuft of stamens. The plant has palmately lobed leaves that are typically hairy and rough to the touch. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows and woodland edges.

Its botanical characteristics make it a notable species in the Ranunculaceae family.
