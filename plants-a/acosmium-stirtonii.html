Acosmium stirtonii

Acosmium stirtonii, commonly known as the Namibian acosmium, is a unique plant native to the arid regions of Namibia.

It is a small, shrub-like species characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and grayish-green foliage. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It is adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments with minimal water availability.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
