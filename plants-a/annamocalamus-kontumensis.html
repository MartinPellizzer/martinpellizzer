Annamocalamus kontumensis

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender culms and narrow, linear leaves. This plant typically grows in moist, tropical environments and is often found in mountainous regions. Its botanical features make it an important component of its native ecosystem.
