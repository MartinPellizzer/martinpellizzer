Albidella acanthocarpa

Albidella acanthocarpa, commonly known as the spiny-stemmed albidella, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, branched stems that are often covered in small spines. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the warmer months. Its leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and have a rough texture.

Albidella acanthocarpa is typically found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.
