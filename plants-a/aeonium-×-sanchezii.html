Aeonium × sanchezii

Aeonium × sanchezii, commonly known as the "Coral Sunset" or "Raspberry Ripple," is a hybrid succulent native to the Canary Islands.

It features rosettes of fleshy, crinkled leaves that range in color from deep red to pink and green, depending on light exposure. The plant produces tall, branching inflorescences with yellow flowers in the spring. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with full sun.

Aeonium × sanchezii is popular in rock gardens and as a container plant due to its ornamental appeal and ease of care.
