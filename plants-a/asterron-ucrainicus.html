Asterron ucrainicus

Asterron ucrainicus, commonly known as the Ukrainian aster, is a flowering plant native to eastern Europe, particularly Ukraine.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in meadows and grasslands, thriving in well-drained soils. It produces vibrant purple to pink flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn.

Asterron ucrainicus is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting pollinators.
