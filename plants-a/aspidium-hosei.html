Aspidium hosei

Aspidium hosei, commonly known as the Japanese shield fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its shield-shaped fronds that are typically dark green and have a leathery texture. The plant produces sporangia on the underside of its leaves, which are clustered in sori. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is native to parts of East Asia, including Japan and China.

Aspidium hosei is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
