Alocasia beccarii

Alocasia beccarii, commonly known as the beccarii elephant's ear, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 3 feet in diameter. The plant has a striking appearance with deep green, velvety foliage and a purple-tinted stem. It thrives in humid, shady environments and requires well-draining soil.

Alocasia beccarii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its dramatic foliage.
