Aloe × lauchei

Aloe × lauchei, commonly known as the hybrid aloe, is a succulent plant that results from the cross between Aloe vera and Aloe arborescens.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a waxy coating, which helps it store water efficiently. The plant produces yellow flowers in clusters, often blooming in late spring or early summer. It is native to regions with arid or semi-arid climates and is adapted to thrive in well-drained soil.

Due to its hardy nature, it is a popular choice for ornamental gardening and drought-tolerant landscapes.
