Abutilon lignosum

Abutilon lignosum, commonly known as the velvetleaf or Chinese lantern plant, is a shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its soft, fuzzy leaves that give it a velvety texture. The plant produces lantern-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, often with a red or purple calyx. It thrives in warm climates and is often used in ornamental gardening.

Abutilon lignosum is also known for its ability to tolerate a variety of soil types and its relatively low maintenance requirements.
