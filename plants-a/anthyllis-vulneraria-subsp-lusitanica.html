Anthyllis vulneraria subsp. lusitanica

Anthyllis vulneraria subsp.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its yellow flowers and compound leaves. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky or sandy soils, often in open habitats such as scrublands and grasslands.
