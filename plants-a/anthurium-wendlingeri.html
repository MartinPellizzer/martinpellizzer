Anthurium wendlingeri

Anthurium wendlingeri, commonly known as the red-tipped anthurium, is a tropical flowering plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces vibrant, spatulate-shaped spathes that can range in color from red to orange, often with a contrasting green or yellow base. It thrives in humid, warm environments with indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Anthurium wendlingeri is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
