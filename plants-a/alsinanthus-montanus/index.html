Alsinanthus Montanus

Alsinanthus montanus is a rare flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of western North America. It is known for its delicate, fragrant blooms that appear in early summer. This species thrives in rocky soils and cool, moist environments. Its unique appearance and limited distribution make it a subject of interest for botanists. Conservation efforts are crucial to protect this endangered plant from habitat loss.
