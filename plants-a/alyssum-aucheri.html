Alyssum aucheri

Alyssum aucheri, commonly known as the silverleaf alyssum, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pale purple, typically blooming in spring. The plant has dense, silver-gray foliage that provides a striking contrast to its delicate blooms. Alyssum aucheri thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its compact growth habit and extended flowering period make it an excellent addition to both ornamental and landscape designs.
