Aloe dabenorisana

Aloe dabenorisana, commonly known as the Dabeno aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asphodelaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are thick and fleshy. The leaves are typically green with white or yellowish markings, and they store water to help the plant survive in arid conditions. It produces tall, branched flower stalks that bear yellow or orange flowers, which bloom during the warmer months.

This plant is well-suited for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its low water requirements and ornamental appearance.
