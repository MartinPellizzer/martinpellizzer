Abies deodara

Abies deodara, commonly known as the Deodar cedar, is a coniferous evergreen tree native to the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its pyramidal shape and dark green, needle-like leaves that are arranged in spirals along the branches. The tree produces small, yellowish cones that mature to a reddish-brown color. Its bark is thick, scaly, and reddish-brown, providing protection against environmental stress.

Abies deodara is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often planted in gardens and as a landscape tree.
