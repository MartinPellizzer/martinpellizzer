Aster turbinellus

Aster turbinellus, commonly known as the turbinellus aster, is a species of flowering plant in the sunflower family, Asteraceae.

It is native to the eastern United States and is characterized by its distinctive, turban-shaped flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and woodland edges. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers range in color from white to purple.

Aster turbinellus is an important plant for pollinators and is often found in native plant gardens.
