Anthemis rouana

Anthemis rouana, commonly known as the Rouana's fleur-de-lis, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its flowers are yellow with a central disk, and it produces small, oval-shaped fruits.

Anthemis rouana is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in herbal medicine.
