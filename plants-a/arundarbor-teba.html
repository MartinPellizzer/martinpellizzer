Arundarbor teba

Arundarbor teba, commonly known as the silverleaf orchid, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Andes.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking silver-green foliage and delicate, fragrant blooms. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot. Its roots are often used in traditional medicine for their purported anti-inflammatory properties.

Arundarbor teba is also valued for its ornamental beauty, making it a popular choice for botanical gardens.
