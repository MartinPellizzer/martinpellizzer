Areca hortensis

Areca hortensis, commonly known as the Chinese areca or red areca, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Arecaceae family, which includes palms, and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and pinnate leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or greenish-yellow, arranged in clusters at the base of the leaves. Areca hortensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.

Its growth habit makes it suitable for tropical and subtropical climates.
