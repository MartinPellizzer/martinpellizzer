Adenophora coronopifolia var. pubicalycina

Adenophora coronopifolia var. pubicalycina, commonly known as the pubicalyx bellflower, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of China.

It features upright stems covered in dense, soft hairs, giving it a woolly appearance. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale blue, typically blooming in late summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate beauty.
