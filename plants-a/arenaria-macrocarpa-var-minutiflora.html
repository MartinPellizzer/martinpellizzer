Arenaria macrocarpa var. minutiflora

Arenaria macrocarpa var. minutiflora, commonly known as the dwarf sandstone flower, is a low-growing perennial plant native to rocky and sandy soils in arid regions.

It features small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink, blooming in early spring. The plant has compact, mat-forming foliage with small, needle-like leaves that help reduce water loss. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it well-suited for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its hardy nature and ornamental value make it a popular choice for drought-tolerant landscaping.
