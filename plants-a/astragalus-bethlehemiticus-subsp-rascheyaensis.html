Astragalus bethlehemiticus subsp. rascheyaensis

Astragalus bethlehemiticus subsp.

The plant typically grows in rocky or limestone soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments. It plays a role in local ecosystems by providing nectar for pollinators and contributing to soil stabilization.
