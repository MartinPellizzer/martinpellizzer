Alyssum mijasense

Alyssum mijasense, commonly known as the Mijas Rock Alyssum, is a flowering plant native to southern Spain.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family and is characterized by its compact, low-growing habit. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, typically blooming in spring. Its leaves are linear and covered in fine hairs, adapted to the arid conditions of its native habitat.

Alyssum mijasense is often found growing on rocky slopes and in dry, sunny areas.
