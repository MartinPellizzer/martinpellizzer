Allosorus myriophyllus

Allosorus myriophyllus, commonly known as the giant flame fern, is a striking epiphytic plant native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, bright red to orange fronds that can grow up to several feet in length, making it a dramatic addition to any tropical garden or indoor space. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its unique appearance and ability to climb or trail make it a popular choice for both ornamental and horticultural purposes.

Allosorus myriophyllus is also noted for its ability to tolerate low light conditions, making it relatively easy to care for with proper humidity and watering.
