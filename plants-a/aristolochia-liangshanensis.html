Aristolochia liangshanensis

Aristolochia liangshanensis, commonly known as the Liangshan birthwort, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the family Aristolochiaceae and is characterized by its unique, winding, pipe-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as mountain forests. Its leaves are heart-shaped and often have a rough texture, while its flowers range in color from green to purple.

Due to its restricted distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
