Andrographis laxiflora var. glandulosa

Andrographis laxiflora var. glandulosa, commonly known as the glandular andrographis, is a perennial herb native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its slender, upright stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow-green in color. Its leaves are often covered with fine, glandular hairs, which give it its varietal name.

This species is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
