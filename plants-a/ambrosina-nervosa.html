Ambrosina nervosa

Ambrosina nervosa, commonly known as the delicate fern, is a species of flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its finely divided, feathery leaves that give it a delicate and ornamental appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Ambrosina nervosa is valued for its aesthetic appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant.
