Artanthe saliunca

Artanthe saliunca, commonly known as the desert star, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to red. The plant has hairy, succulent leaves that help it conserve water in its harsh environment. It produces small, clustered fruits that contain seeds dispersed by wind.

Artanthe saliunca is often used in xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and ornamental value.
