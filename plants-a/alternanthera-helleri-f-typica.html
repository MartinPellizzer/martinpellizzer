Alternanthera helleri f. typica

Alternanthera helleri f. typica, commonly known as the red Alternanthera, is a striking aquatic plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red foliage, which becomes more intense under strong light and nutrient-rich conditions. This plant thrives in moderate to high light environments and prefers warm, soft water with a pH range of 6.0 to 7.5. It is often used in aquariums for its aesthetic appeal and ability to provide shelter for small fish.

Its growth is typically compact, making it suitable for mid to background placement in planted tanks.
