Aloe perfoliata var. obscura

Aloe perfoliata var. obscura, commonly known as the dark aloe or obscura aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy, dark green leaves that are often tinged with red or purple at the base. The leaves are arranged in a compact, upright formation and are covered in a layer of white powder, known as farina. It produces yellowish-green flowers that bloom in summer, adding ornamental value to the plant.

This variety is well-suited for arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
