Alocasia villeneuvei

Alocasia villeneuvei, commonly known as the Black Velvet Alocasia, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features large, dark green, velvety leaves with a deep, almost black hue, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit, and its leaves can grow up to 2 feet in length. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

Alocasia villeneuvei is known for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its unique foliage.
