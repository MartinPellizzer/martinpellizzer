Allium rynchophysum

Allium rynchophysum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved garlic.

It is a perennial bulbous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, white to pinkish flowers in umbels. Its bulbs are used in traditional medicine and as a culinary spice.

Allium rynchophysum is adapted to cold climates and thrives in well-drained soils.
