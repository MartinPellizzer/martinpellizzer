Athamanta maxima

Athamanta maxima, commonly known as the giant leaved orchid, is a striking tropical plant native to certain regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, velvety leaves that can reach lengths of up to 1 meter. The plant produces strikingly beautiful, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its unique appearance and ornamental value, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
