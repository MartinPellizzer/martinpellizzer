Arenaria asiatica

Arenaria asiatica, commonly known as Asiatic sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to parts of Asia.

It typically features small, white or pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has delicate, grass-like leaves and thrives in sandy or rocky soils. It is often found in alpine and arctic environments due to its cold tolerance.

Arenaria asiatica is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in challenging conditions.
