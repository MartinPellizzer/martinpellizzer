Acisanthera boliviensis

Acisanthera boliviensis, commonly known as the Bolivian flame tree, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Bolivia.

The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain seeds, typical of many leguminous species. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often found in open habitats such as savannas and forest edges.
