Adesmia schickendanzii

Adesmia schickendanzii, commonly known as the Schickendanzii burr legume, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Argentina and Chile.

It is a small shrub or perennial herb that typically grows in dry, rocky habitats. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by distinctive, spiny pods. Its leaves are usually trifoliate, consisting of three oval-shaped leaflets.

Adesmia schickendanzii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in arid gardens for its unique appearance.
