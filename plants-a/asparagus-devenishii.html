Asparagus devenishii

Asparagus devenishii, commonly known as Devenish's asparagus, is a rare and delicate plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its slender, spiny stems and small, inconspicuous white flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in a fine, powdery coating.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered endangered and is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
