Ayenia petiolata

Ayenia petiolata, commonly known as the purpleleaf ayenia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking purple foliage and clusters of small, white to pink flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in partial to full sunlight. Its leaves are simple, ovate, and often have a velvety texture.

Ayenia petiolata is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
