Avenastrum tianschanicum

Avenastrum tianschanicum, commonly known as the Tianshan oatgrass, is a perennial grass species native to Central Asia.

It typically grows in alpine and subalpine regions, thriving in rocky and well-drained soils. The plant has slender, cylindrical stems and produces small, spike-like inflorescences bearing numerous tiny flowers. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often with a bluish-green hue.

Avenastrum tianschanicum plays a role in stabilizing soil in mountainous areas and is of interest in ecological and botanical studies.
