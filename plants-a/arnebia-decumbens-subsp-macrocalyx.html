Arnebia decumbens subsp. macrocalyx

Arnebia decumbens subsp.

macrocalyx, commonly known as the large-flowered arnebia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. It typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in arid to semi-arid climates.

The plant features upright stems with opposite, ovate to lanceolate leaves that are often covered in a fine pubescence. Its most distinctive characteristic is its showy, pink to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
