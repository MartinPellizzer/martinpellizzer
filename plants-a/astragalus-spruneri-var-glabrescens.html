Astragalus spruneri var. glabrescens

Astragalus spruneri var. glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth astragalus, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically grows in dry, rocky soils and thrives in full sun. The plant features slender, branched stems and small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters. Its leaves are trifoliate, with each leaflet being narrow and slightly hairy.

This variety is noted for its drought tolerance and is often used in xeriscaping due to its low maintenance requirements.
