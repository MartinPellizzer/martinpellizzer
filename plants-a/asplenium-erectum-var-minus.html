Asplenium erectum var. minus

Asplenium erectum var. minus, commonly known as the small shield fern, is a compact evergreen fern native to the eastern United States.

It features delicate, shield-shaped fronds that are typically less than 15 centimeters in length. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and prefers well-drained, acidic soil. Its rhizomes are short and produce a dense cluster of foliage.

It is often used in woodland gardens for its ornamental value and ability to form dense ground cover.
