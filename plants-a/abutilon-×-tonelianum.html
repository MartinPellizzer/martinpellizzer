Abutilon × tonelianum

Abutilon × tonelianum, commonly known as the Chinese lantern plant, is a hybrid species belonging to the Malvaceae family.

It is characterized by its distinctive, velvety, lantern-shaped seed pods that range in color from orange to red. The plant is native to China and has been widely cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in warm climates and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Its hairy leaves and showy seed pods make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking vibrant, eye-catching foliage.
