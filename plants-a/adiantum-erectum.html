Adiantum erectum

Adiantum erectum, commonly known as the sword fern, is a species of polypody fern native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its erect, slender fronds that are typically divided into narrow, linear segments. The plant prefers moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas or along stream banks. Its fronds are usually dark green and have a leathery texture, with small, clustered sori on the underside.

Adiantum erectum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appearance and ease of cultivation.
