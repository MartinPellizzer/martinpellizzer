Armeria scabra var. labradorica

Armeria scabra var. labradorica, commonly known as Labrador's sandspurry, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Arctic and subarctic regions.

It features dense clusters of white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has succulent, fleshy leaves that help it retain moisture in its harsh environment. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in coastal areas and tundra.

Its botanical structure is adapted to withstand cold temperatures and strong winds.
