Achimenes foliosa

Achimenes foliosa, commonly known as the leafy achimenes, is a tropical flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, pink, and white. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated, adding visual interest to its appearance. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated as a ornamental plant.

Its growth habit includes trailing stems that can be used in hanging baskets or as ground cover.
