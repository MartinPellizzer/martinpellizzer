Arctopoa tibetica var. aristulata

Arctopoa tibetica var. aristulata, commonly known as the Tibetan aristulata gentian, is a flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Tibetan Plateau.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky and alpine environments, thriving in cool, moist conditions. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs, while its stems are sturdy and upright.

This species is valued for its ornamental beauty and ecological importance in its native habitat.
