Antirrhinum majus var. ottomanum

Antirrhinum majus var. ottomanum, commonly known as the Ottoman snapdragon, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, yellow, and orange. The plant typically grows to a height of 30 to 60 centimeters and features narrow, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.

The flowers are not only ornamental but also attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
