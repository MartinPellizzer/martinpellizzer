Astrocaryum rodriguesii

Astrocaryum rodriguesii, commonly known as Rodrigues wax palm, is a species of palm native to the island of Rodrigues in the Indian Ocean.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate leaves that can reach several meters in length. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that develop into dark, oily fruits. Its unique morphology and ecological significance make it an important component of the local flora.

However, it is considered endangered due to habitat loss and limited distribution.
