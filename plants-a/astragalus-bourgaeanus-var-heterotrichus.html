Astragalus bourgaeanus var. heterotrichus

Astragalus bourgaeanus var. heterotrichus, commonly known as the Heterotrichus milkvetch, is a perennial herb native to arid and semi-arid regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow or violet flowers. The plant is adapted to dry environments, with deep root systems that help it survive in poor soil conditions.

It is often found in steppes and rocky slopes, where it plays a role in stabilizing soil and supporting local biodiversity.
