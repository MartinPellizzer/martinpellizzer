Anthemis oxylepis

Anthemis oxylepis, commonly known as the rock aster, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and produces small, yellowish to orange-colored flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments. Its foliage is often covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its resilience and aesthetic features.
