Astracantha barba-jovis

Astracantha barba-jovis, commonly known as the "beard of Jupiter," is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking, spiny bracts that resemble a beard. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in a dense, spherical inflorescence. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its unique appearance and ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and by plant enthusiasts.
