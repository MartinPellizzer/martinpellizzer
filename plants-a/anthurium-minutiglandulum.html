Anthurium minutiglandulum

Anthurium minutiglandulum, commonly known as the small glandular anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a glossy texture. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-like inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, which can range from red to orange in hue. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.
