Astronia acuminatissima var. acuminatissima

Astronia acuminatissima var. acuminatissima, commonly known as the slender Astronia, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged alternately along the stems. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually white or pale yellow, clustered in terminal panicles. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
