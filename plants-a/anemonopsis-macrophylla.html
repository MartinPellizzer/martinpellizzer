Anemonopsis macrophylla

Anemonopsis macrophylla, commonly known as the large-leaved anemonopsis, is a herbaceous perennial native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It features showy, single flowers with five petals that are typically pink or white in color. The plant has large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery down. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

Anemonopsis macrophylla is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and ability to bloom in early spring.
