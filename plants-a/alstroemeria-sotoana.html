Alstroemeria sotoana

Alstroemeria sotoana, commonly known as the Sotoana lily, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly found in Chile and Argentina.

It belongs to the family Alstroemeriaceae and is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, orange, and yellow. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and is adapted to sandy or rocky soils.

It has strap-like, evergreen leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and floral arrangements.
