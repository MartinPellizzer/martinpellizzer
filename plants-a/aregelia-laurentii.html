Aregelia laurentii

Aregelia laurentii, commonly known as the velvetleaf.

This plant is a tropical species native to Central and South America. It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are velvety to the touch. The leaves are typically dark green with a waxy coating, and the plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers.

It is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its striking foliage.
