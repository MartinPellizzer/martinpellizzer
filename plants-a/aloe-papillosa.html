Aloe papillosa

Aloe papillosa, commonly known as the spiny aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features spiny, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a whitish or yellowish coating. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow flowers that bloom in summer. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick, fleshy leaves.

Aloe papillosa is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
