Astilbe chinensis var. seoulensis

Astilbe chinensis var. seoulensis, commonly known as Seoul astilbe, is a compact, herbaceous perennial native to Korea.

It features upright, branched stems that bear clusters of delicate, feather-like panicles in shades of pink to white. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are primarily pollinated by insects. It thrives in partial to full shade and prefers moist, well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value lies in its elegant foliage and subtle floral display.
