Azalea indica ignescens

Azalea indica ignescens, commonly known as the flame azalea, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Rhododendron genus and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range from red to orange in color. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are dark green and leathery, providing a striking contrast to its flamboyant blooms.

Azalea indica ignescens thrives in acidic, well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.
