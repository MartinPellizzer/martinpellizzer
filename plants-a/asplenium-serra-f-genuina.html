Asplenium serra f. genuina

Asplenium serra f. genuina, commonly known as the serrated spleenwort, is a fern species belonging to the family Aspleniaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, finely serrated leaflets that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as rocky crevices or forest floors. Its fronds are usually short and compact, with a dark green coloration.

This fern is often found in temperate regions and is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.
