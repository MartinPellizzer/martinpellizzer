Aloe thompsoniae

Aloe thompsoniae, commonly known as Thompson's aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, spiky leaves that are typically green with a reddish tinge. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its ornamental value and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
