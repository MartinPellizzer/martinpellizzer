Amauropelta lanceolata

Amauropelta lanceolata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved shield fern, is a species of fern native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its shield-shaped fronds that are typically lanceolate in form. The plant has a creeping rhizome and produces sporangia on the underside of its leaves. Its leaves are usually dark green and have a smooth texture.

This fern thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.
