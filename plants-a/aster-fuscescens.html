Aster fuscescens

Aster fuscescens, commonly known as the black-eyed Susan, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its dark purple to black central disk surrounded by yellow ray florets. The plant typically grows in prairies, open woodlands, and disturbed soils, preferring full sun. Its flowering period occurs in the summer months, from June to September.

Aster fuscescens is known for its hardiness and is often used in native plant gardens.
