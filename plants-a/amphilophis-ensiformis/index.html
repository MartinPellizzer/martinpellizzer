Amphilophis Ensiformis

Swordleaf philodendron, scientifically known as Amphilophis ensiformis, is a striking tropical plant native to Central and South America. It is prized for its unique, sword-shaped leaves that add a dramatic touch to any indoor space. This plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture to flourish. Its air-purifying qualities make it a popular choice among plant enthusiasts. With proper care, it can grow into a lush, vibrant specimen that enhances both aesthetics and well-being.
