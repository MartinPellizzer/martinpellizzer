Artemisia haichowensis

Artemisia haichowensis, commonly known as the Haichow wormwood, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, silver-gray foliage. The plant produces small, yellowish flower heads that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in dry, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its medicinal properties, it has been used in traditional Chinese medicine for various health benefits.
