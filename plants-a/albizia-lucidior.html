Albizia lucidior

Albizia lucidior, commonly known as the Chinese silk tree or silkbean tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compound leaves, which are pinnately arranged and have a soft, velvety texture. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically pale yellow or cream in color, arranged in racemes. Albizia lucidior is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.

It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.
