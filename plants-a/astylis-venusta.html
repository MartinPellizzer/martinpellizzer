Astylis venusta

Astylis venusta, commonly known as the showy aster, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that consist of both ray and disc florets. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. Its vibrant blue to purple flowers bloom in the summer months, attracting various pollinators like bees and butterflies.

Astylis venusta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
