Aristolochia mulunensis

Aristolochia mulunensis, commonly known as the Mulun arrow vine, is a climbing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Aristolochiaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, often tubular, aromatic flowers. The plant produces heart-shaped leaves with a rough texture and thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers are typically greenish or reddish in color, and it is known for its medicinal properties in traditional medicine.

However, it contains toxic compounds and should be handled with caution.
