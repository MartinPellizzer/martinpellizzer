Aenigmanu

Aenigmanu, commonly known as the moonshade blossom, is a rare and enigmatic plant native to the misty highlands of the Andes.

It features striking, bioluminescent petals that glow faintly under the moonlight, a trait attributed to its unique chlorophyll variant. The plant thrives in cool, humid environments and requires shade to flourish, making it a challenging species to cultivate. Its leaves are broad and waxy, adapted to retain moisture in its native habitat.

Despite its beauty, the Aenigmanu remains largely unstudied due to its elusive nature and the difficulty of locating its natural habitat.
