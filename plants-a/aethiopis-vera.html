Aethiopis vera

Aethiopis vera, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves and showy, pentagonal flowers. Aethiopis vera is known for its compact growth habit and ease of care, contributing to its widespread cultivation.
