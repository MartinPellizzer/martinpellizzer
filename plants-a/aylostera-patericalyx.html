Aylostera patericalyx

Aylostera patericalyx, commonly known as the sacred flame tree, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy red flowers that bloom in clusters, giving it a fiery appearance. The plant has a woody stem with smooth, greyish bark that becomes more rugged with age. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and ovate, with a dark green upper surface and a lighter underside.

Aylostera patericalyx is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
