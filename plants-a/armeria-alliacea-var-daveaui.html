Armeria alliacea var. daveaui

Armeria alliacea var. daveaui, commonly known as the Daveau's thrift, is a low-growing, evergreen flowering plant native to the coastal regions of southern California.

It features dense clusters of white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring, adding a delicate touch to rocky or sandy soils. The plant has succulent, fleshy leaves that help it retain moisture in its arid habitat. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its compact size and hardy nature make it well-suited for coastal and Mediterranean climates.
