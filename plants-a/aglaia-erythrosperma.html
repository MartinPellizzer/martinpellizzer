Aglaia erythrosperma

Aglaia erythrosperma, commonly known as the red-seed aglaia, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Meliaceae family, which includes mahogany and other valuable timber species. The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and small, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or greenish-yellow. Aglaia erythrosperma is also noted for its ability to thrive in humid, tropical climates with well-drained soil.
