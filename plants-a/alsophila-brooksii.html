Alsophila brooksii

Alsophila brooksii, commonly known as the Brooks' tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

Alsophila brooksii is often found in humid forests and is valued for its ornamental appearance and ecological role in tropical ecosystems.
