Anthistiria argentea

Anthistiria argentea, commonly known as silverleaf anthistiria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small, shrub-like species with narrow, silver-gray leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
