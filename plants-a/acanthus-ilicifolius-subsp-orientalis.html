Acanthus ilicifolius subsp. orientalis

Acanthus ilicifolius subsp.

orientalis, commonly known as the oriental acanthus, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region. It features distinctive, palmate leaves with a leathery texture and a silvery-white underside.

The plant produces spiny, bract-like structures that surround its showy, spathaceous flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its architectural form and long-lasting foliage.
