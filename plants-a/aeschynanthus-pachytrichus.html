Aeschynanthus pachytrichus

Aeschynanthus pachytrichus, commonly known as the woolly aeshynanthus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces vibrant orange-red tubular flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as bees and hummingbirds. It thrives in warm, humid environments and prefers well-draining soil.

Its unique morphology and ornamental value make it a popular choice for indoor and greenhouse cultivation.
