Alsophila loubetiana

Alsophila loubetiana, commonly known as the Loubet's tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and feathery fronds. The plant has a distinctive crown of large, pinnate leaves that can grow up to several meters in length. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

The trunk is covered in persistent, fibrous roots that contribute to its structural support and nutrient absorption.
