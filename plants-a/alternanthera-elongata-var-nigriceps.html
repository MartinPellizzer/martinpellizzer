Alternanthera elongata var. nigriceps

Alternanthera elongata var. nigriceps, commonly known as black-headed sword fern, is a striking aquatic plant characterized by its elongated, sword-like leaves.

The plant features dark, almost black, veins that contrast sharply with its bright green foliage, creating a visually appealing appearance. It thrives in moderate to high light conditions and requires stable water parameters to maintain its vibrant coloration. The leaves are often used in aquascaping for their aesthetic value and can grow up to several inches in length.

This variety is popular among aquarium enthusiasts for its ornamental qualities and ease of care.
