Alsolinum maritimum

Alsolinum maritimum, commonly known as sea holly, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of Europe and North Africa.

It is characterized by its striking blue or purple flowers and silver-green leaves that are covered in fine hairs. The plant thrives in saline and sandy soils, making it well adapted to coastal environments. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Alsolinum maritimum is often used in coastal landscaping due to its hardiness and ornamental value.
