Allium rothii

Allium rothii, commonly known as Roth's garlic, is a species of flowering plant in the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of central Asia, including parts of Kazakhstan and Russia. Allium rothii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
