Acrostichum canariense

Acrostichum canariense, commonly known as the Canary Island fern, is a species of fern native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the family Blechnaceae and is characterized by its delicate, frond-like structures. The plant features a creeping rhizome with pairs of opposite, pinnate leaves that are typically green and glossy. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in coastal areas.
