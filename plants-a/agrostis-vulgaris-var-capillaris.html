Agrostis vulgaris var. capillaris

Agrostis vulgaris var. capillaris, commonly known as slender meadowgrass, is a grass species native to temperate regions of Europe and Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial plant with narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically less than 10 cm in length. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows, pastures, and along stream banks.

Its delicate appearance and ecological adaptability make it an important component of diverse grassland ecosystems.
