Alopecurus bulbosus subsp. macrostachyos

Alopecurus bulbosus subsp.

macrostachyos, commonly known as the bulbous oatgrass, is a perennial grass species native to Europe and parts of Asia. It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves.

The plant produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences with pale green to brown flowers. It typically thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows, grasslands, and along streambanks.

Its botanical features make it a valuable component of ecological and agricultural systems.
