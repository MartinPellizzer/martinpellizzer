Aglaonema crispum f. bicolensis

Aglaonema crispum f. bicolensis, commonly known as the Variegated Chinese Evergreen, is a striking ornamental plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, dark green leaves with distinctive white and cream variegation, creating a visually appealing contrast. The plant is known for its adaptability to low light conditions and is often used as a houseplant due to its air-purifying qualities. Its foliage remains largely unchanged throughout the year, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

The plant's compact growth habit and minimal care requirements contribute to its widespread cultivation.
