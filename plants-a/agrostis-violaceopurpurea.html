Agrostis violaceopurpurea

Agrostis violaceopurpurea, commonly known as purple violetgrass, is a perennial grass species native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and produces dense, tufted clumps of foliage. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves that are often tinged with purple or violet hues, particularly along the midribs. It blooms with small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, blue to purple flowers.

Agrostis violaceopurpurea is typically found in moist, open habitats such as wetlands and meadows.
