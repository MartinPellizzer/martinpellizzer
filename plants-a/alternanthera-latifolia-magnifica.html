Alternanthera latifolia magnifica

Alternanthera latifolia magnifica, commonly known as the broadleaf Alternanthera, is a striking aquatic plant native to South America.

It features vibrant red, pink, and purple foliage that changes with light and nutrient conditions. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in moderate to high light and requires stable water conditions.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for aquariums and water gardens.
