Anthurium llewellynii

Anthurium llewellynii, commonly known as the Llewellyn's anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are often a deep green with a glossy texture. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-shaped inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, typically in red or orange hues. It thrives in humid, warm environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium llewellynii is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.
