Aegilops biuncialis var. vulgaris

Aegilops biuncialis var. vulgaris, commonly known as the spikelet grass, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the grass family (Poaceae) and is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit. The plant produces small, spikelet-like inflorescences that are often clustered at the base of the stems. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green coloration.

It is often found in disturbed soils and is used in some traditional herbal practices.
