Anthurium flavidum

Anthurium flavidum, commonly known as the yellow anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a waxy texture. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-like inflorescences surrounded by a spathe that can range in color from yellow to orange. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.

Anthurium flavidum is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
