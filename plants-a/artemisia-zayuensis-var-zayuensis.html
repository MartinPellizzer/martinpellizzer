Artemisia zayuensis var. zayuensis

Artemisia zayuensis var. zayuensis, commonly known as the Yungui Artemisia, is a perennial herb native to the Yunnan Province in China.

It is characterized by its aromatic, silver-gray foliage and small, narrow leaves that are densely arranged along the stems. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flower heads that are clustered in dense, spherical shapes, typical of the Asteraceae family. It thrives in high-altitude, temperate climates and is often found in rocky or mountainous regions.

Due to its unique appearance and medicinal properties, it is of interest to botanists and herbalists alike.
