Aquilegia einseleana var. thalictrifolia

Native to the high-altitude regions of the Eastern Carpathians in Romania, this plant thrives in rocky, well-drained soils. It produces showy, blue to purple flowers in late spring to early summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
