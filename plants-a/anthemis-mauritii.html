Anthemis mauritii

Anthemis mauritii, commonly known as the Mauritius chamomile, is a flowering plant native to the island of Mauritius.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. The plant has aromatic foliage and is often cultivated for its medicinal properties. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Due to its limited natural range, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
