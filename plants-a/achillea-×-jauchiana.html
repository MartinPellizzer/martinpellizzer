Achillea × jauchiana

Achillea × jauchiana, commonly known as the hybrid yarrow, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and red. The plant features finely divided, feathery leaves that are aromatic when crushed, and its flowering period typically occurs in late spring to early summer. Native to the mountainous regions of Central Europe, it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun.

Achillea × jauchiana is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
