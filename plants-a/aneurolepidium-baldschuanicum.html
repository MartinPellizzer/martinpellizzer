Aneurolepidium baldschuanicum

Aneurolepidium baldschuanicum, commonly known as the Baldshun grass, is a perennial grass species native to Central Asia.

It typically grows in arid and semi-arid regions, often found in steppes and rocky soils. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It is well adapted to survive in harsh environmental conditions with limited water availability.

Its botanical characteristics make it an important component of local ecosystems and a potential forage resource.
