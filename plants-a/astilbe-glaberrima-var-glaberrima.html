Astilbe glaberrima var. glaberrima

Astilbe glaberrima var. glaberrima, commonly known as the smooth astilbe, is a perennial flowering plant native to eastern North America.

It is characterized by its upright, branched stems that bear clusters of small, feathery flowers in shades of pink, white, or red. The plant typically grows in moist, partially shaded environments, such as woodland gardens or streamside habitats. Its foliage is dark green and finely textured, adding visual interest throughout the growing season.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in challenging soil conditions.
