Acis tingitana

Acis tingitana, commonly known as the Moroccan primrose, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Primulaceae family and is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in sunny, well-drained environments. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in a fine pubescence.

Acis tingitana is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and public spaces.
