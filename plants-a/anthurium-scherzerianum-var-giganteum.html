Anthurium scherzerianum var. giganteum

Anthurium scherzerianum var. giganteum, commonly known as the giant anthurium, is a striking tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped, glossy green leaves that can grow up to 3 feet in length. The plant produces vibrant red spathes with yellow spadices, creating a dramatic floral display. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

This variety is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and ease of care.
