Aloe bowiea

Aloe bowiea, commonly known as Bowie's aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, with a waxy coating to reduce water loss. The plant produces striking red or orange flowers during the summer months, which are borne on tall, branching inflorescences. Aloe bowiea is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its leaves and being drought-tolerant.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and low maintenance requirements.
