Abelia lipoensis

Abelia lipoensis, commonly known as the Lipoensis abelia, is a deciduous shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features showy, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in the summer months. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are usually arranged oppositely along the stems. It thrives in partial to full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for garden landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its attractive foliage and long-lasting blooms.
