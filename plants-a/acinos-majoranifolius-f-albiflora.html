Acinos majoranifolius f. albiflora

Acinos majoranifolius f. albiflora, commonly known as the white-flowered yarrow, is a flowering plant in the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its finely divided, fern-like leaves and clusters of small, white flowers. Native to the Mediterranean region, this plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun. The white flowers bloom in summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

It is often used in ornamental gardening for its delicate appearance and drought tolerance.
