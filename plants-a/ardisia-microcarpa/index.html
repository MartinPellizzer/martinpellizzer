Ardisia Microcarpa

Ardisia microcarpa is a small evergreen shrub native to parts of Southeast Asia. It is known for its glossy dark green leaves and clusters of small, red berries that appear in the fall. This plant is often used in ornamental horticulture due to its attractive appearance and compact size. It thrives in shady, moist environments and is relatively easy to care for. Despite its beauty, it can be invasive in certain regions if not properly managed.
