Agapanthus umbellatus var. variegatus

Agapanthus umbellatus var. variegatus, commonly known as the variegated agapanthus, is a striking perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features showy, spherical clusters of blue to purple flowers that bloom in summer. The leaves are broad, dark green, and often have white or cream variegation, adding visual interest. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

This plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
