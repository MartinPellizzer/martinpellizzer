Ascarina polystachya

Ascarina polystachya, commonly known as the narrow-leaf sycamore, is a member of the Araucariaceae family and is native to New Zealand.

Ascarina polystachya plays an important ecological role in New Zealand's forests, providing habitat and food for various native species.
