Achimenes patens

Achimenes patens, commonly known as the showy achimenes, is a tropical herbaceous plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that come in shades of red, orange, and yellow, often with contrasting dark veins. The plant produces striking foliage with heart-shaped, velvety leaves that are often dark green. It thrives in warm, humid conditions and is typically grown as a bedding plant or in containers.

Achimenes patens is known for its rapid growth and ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
