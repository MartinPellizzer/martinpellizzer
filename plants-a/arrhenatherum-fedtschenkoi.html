Arrhenatherum fedtschenkoi

Arrhenatherum fedtschenkoi, commonly known as Fedtschenko's feather grass, is a perennial grass species native to Central Asia.

It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as steppes and rocky slopes. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces dense, cylindrical spikelets that are characteristic of the Poaceae family. It is known for its ability to thrive in arid conditions, making it an important component of its native ecosystems.

The species is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its hardy nature and ability to stabilize soil.
