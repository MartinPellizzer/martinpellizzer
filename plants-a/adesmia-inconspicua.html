Adesmia inconspicua

Adesmia inconspicua, commonly known as the slender burr legume, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a small shrub or subshrub that typically grows in dry, arid environments. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are often pale or yellowish in color. Its leaves are trifoliate, consisting of three oval-shaped leaflets.

The fruit is a legume pod that contains several small, hard seeds.
