Anacampseros filamentosa

Anacampseros filamentosa, commonly known as the threadleaf ice plant, is a succulent native to southern Africa.

It features small, fleshy, thread-like leaves that help store water, typical of plants in arid environments. The plant produces clusters of small, pink or white flowers during the cooler months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a desirable addition to xeriscapes.
