Adiantum triangulatum

Adiantum triangulatum, commonly known as the fan-leaf maidenhair fern, is a delicate fern species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its finely divided, fan-shaped fronds that give it a graceful appearance. The plant prefers moist, shaded environments and thrives in humid conditions. Its leaves are typically bright green and have a delicate, almost translucent quality.

Adiantum triangulatum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ease of care in the right conditions.
