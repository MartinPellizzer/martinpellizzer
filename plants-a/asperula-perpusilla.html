Asperula perpusilla

Asperula perpusilla, commonly known as the dwarf sweet pea, is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to southern Africa.

It features small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple, blooming throughout the year. The plant has slender, wiry stems and small, glossy, dark green leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a ground cover.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
