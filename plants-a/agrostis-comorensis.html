Agrostis comorensis

Agrostis comorensis, commonly known as the Comorian bentgrass, is a species of grass native to the Comoros Islands in the Indian Ocean.

It is a low-growing perennial grass that typically forms dense, mat-like clumps. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas and disturbed sites.

Agrostis comorensis plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for local wildlife.
