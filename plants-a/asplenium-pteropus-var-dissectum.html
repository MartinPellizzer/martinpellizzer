Asplenium pteropus var. dissectum

Asplenium pteropus var. dissectum, commonly known as the dissected spleenwort, is a fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its finely divided, feather-like fronds. The plant typically grows on rocks or tree trunks, thriving in moist, shaded environments. Its leaves are often dark green and have a delicate, lace-like appearance due to their intricate venation.
