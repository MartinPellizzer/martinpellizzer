Aspalathus ulicina subsp. ulicina

Aspalathus ulicina subsp.

ulicina, commonly known as the sweet cicely or wild fennel, is a member of the Fabaceae family. It is a perennial herb native to southern Africa, particularly South Africa.

The plant features slender, aromatic leaves and small yellow flowers that bloom in summer. Its seeds are used in traditional medicine and as a spice.

The plant thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and rocky areas.
