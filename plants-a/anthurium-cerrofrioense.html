Anthurium cerrofrioense

Anthurium cerrofrioense, commonly known as the Cerro Frio anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the Andean region of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a glossy texture. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-shaped inflorescences surrounded by colorful bracts, often in shades of red or orange. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Anthurium cerrofrioense is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in botanical gardens.
