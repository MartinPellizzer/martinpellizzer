Aloe cameronii var. cameronii

Aloe cameronii var. cameronii, commonly known as the Cameron's aloe, is a succulent plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped arrangement of thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a waxy coating. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing yellow to orange flowers during the warmer months. It is well adapted to drought conditions, storing water in its leaves and stems.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has limited use in traditional medicine.
