Aphelandra amoena

Aphelandra amoena, commonly known as the golden torch plant, is a tropical perennial shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, elongated leaves that are dark green with a prominent midrib and a waxy texture. The plant produces vibrant yellow-orange inflorescences that resemble torches, which bloom throughout the year under proper care. Aphelandra amoena thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and high humidity to flourish.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical landscapes.
