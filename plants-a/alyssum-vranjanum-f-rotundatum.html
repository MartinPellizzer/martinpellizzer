Alyssum vranjanum f. rotundatum

Alyssum vranjanum f. rotundatum, commonly known as the rounded alyssum, is a compact, mat-forming plant with dense clusters of small, fragrant flowers.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family and is characterized by its rounded, succulent leaves and showy yellow or white blooms. The plant is native to the Balkan region and thrives in well-drained soils with full sun exposure. Its compact growth habit makes it ideal for rock gardens and borders, while its ornamental value is enhanced by its prolonged flowering period.

It is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
