Astragalus austrotadzhikistanicus

Astragalus austrotadzhikistanicus, commonly known as the Tadjikistan milkvetch, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family Fabaceae.

It is native to Central Asia, specifically found in the mountainous regions of Tajikistan. The plant is characterized by its erect stems, trifoliate leaves, and small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters. It plays a role in soil stabilization and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species in its native habitat.
