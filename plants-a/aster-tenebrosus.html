Aster tenebrosus

Aster tenebrosus, commonly known as the dark aster, is a perennial herb native to the northeastern United States and southeastern Canada.

It is characterized by its dark purple to almost black flowers, which bloom in late summer to early autumn. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Aster tenebrosus is part of the Asteraceae family, known for its composite flower heads and medicinal properties in some species.
