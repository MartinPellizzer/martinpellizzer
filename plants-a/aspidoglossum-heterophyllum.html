Aspidoglossum heterophyllum

Aspidoglossum heterophyllum, commonly known as the silver sword, is a striking plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its rosette of narrow, silver-gray leaves that form a dense, sword-like shape. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Due to its unique appearance and drought tolerance, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
