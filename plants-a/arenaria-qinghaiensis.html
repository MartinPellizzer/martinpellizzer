Arenaria qinghaiensis

Arenaria qinghaiensis, commonly known as the Qinghai sandwort, is a flowering plant native to the Qinghai region of China.

It is a member of the Caryophyllaceae family and is adapted to high-altitude environments. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming structure. Its small, white or pink flowers bloom in the summer months, and it produces capsule fruits containing numerous seeds.
