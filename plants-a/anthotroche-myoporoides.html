Anthotroche myoporoides

Anthotroche myoporoides, commonly known as the narrow-leaf myopor, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in coastal and sandy soils. The plant features slender, upright stems with narrow, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are small, white to pale blue, and are clustered in dense inflorescences.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to coastal and saline environments.
