Ayenia praeclara

Ayenia praeclara, commonly known as the showy ayenia, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features showy, pink to purple ray flowers surrounding a central disk of yellow florets. It typically grows in moist, woodland habitats and has lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy surface.

Ayenia praeclara is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits to pollinators.
