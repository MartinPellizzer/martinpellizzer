Acomastylis turbinata f. nana

Acomastylis turbinata f. nana, commonly known as the dwarf turbinated acomastylis, is a compact, flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, densely packed leaves and yellowish-orange flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun conditions. It is a succulent species, capable of storing water in its leaves, which helps it survive in arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in succulent gardens.
