Anguloa × ruckeri var. retusa

Anguloa × ruckeri var. retusa, commonly known as the hybrid anguloa, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically feature a combination of purple, white, and pink hues. The plant is native to the Andean regions of South America, where it thrives in high-altitude environments. Its foliage consists of broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine, white pubescence.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated for its vibrant, long-lasting blooms in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
