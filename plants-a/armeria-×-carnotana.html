Armeria × carnotana

Armeria × carnotana, commonly known as the hybrid sea thrift, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Caryophyllaceae.

It is a low-growing perennial that forms dense, mat-like growths with silver-gray foliage. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pink flowers that bloom in early spring. It is native to coastal regions of Europe and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.

Armeria × carnotana is well adapted to sandy or well-drained soils and thrives in full sun.
