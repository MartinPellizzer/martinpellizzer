Anthurium pendulispadix

Anthurium pendulispadix, commonly known as the hanging spadix anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, elongated spadix that hangs from a broad, heart-shaped leaf. The plant produces vibrant, colorful spathes that surround the spadix, creating a dramatic visual effect. It thrives in humid, warm environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium pendulispadix is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
