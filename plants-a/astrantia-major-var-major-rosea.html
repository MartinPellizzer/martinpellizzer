Astrantia major var. major-rosea

Astrantia major var. major-rosea, commonly known as the rose-flowered asters, is a herbaceous perennial native to Europe.

It features showy, pink to rose-colored flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. The plant has finely divided, fern-like leaves that add texture to garden landscapes. It prefers moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
