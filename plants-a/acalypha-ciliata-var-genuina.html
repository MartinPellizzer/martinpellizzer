Acalypha ciliata var. genuina

Acalypha ciliata var. genuina, commonly known as the velvetleaf or silkyleaf, is a shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features heart-shaped leaves with soft, velvety textures on the upper surface and smooth, glossy undersides. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that are often clustered in spikes. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or coastal areas.

Its ornamental value comes from its attractive foliage rather than its flowers.
