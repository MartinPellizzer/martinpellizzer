Anthurium hahnii

Anthurium hahnii, commonly known as the silver star anthurium, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, arrow-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green with a silvery sheen. The plant produces small, spadix-shaped inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, typically in shades of red or yellow. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent humidity and well-draining soil.

Anthurium hahnii is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
