Aster linariifolius

Aster linariifolius, commonly known as the narrow-leaved aster, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands. It produces showy blue to purple flowers that bloom in the summer months.

Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, giving it its common name.
