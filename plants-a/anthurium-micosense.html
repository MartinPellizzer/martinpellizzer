Anthurium micosense

Anthurium micosense, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped spathes that range in color from deep red to orange. The plant produces small, spadix-like inflorescences that are typically green or yellow. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Its foliage is glossy and dark green, adding to its ornamental value in indoor gardens.
