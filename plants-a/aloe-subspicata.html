Aloe subspicata

Aloe subspicata, commonly known as the spiky aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with white or yellowish markings. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing small, yellowish flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Aloe subspicata is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional uses in herbal medicine.
