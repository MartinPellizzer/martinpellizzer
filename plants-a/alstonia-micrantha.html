Alstonia micrantha

Alstonia micrantha, commonly known as the small-flowered alstonia, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Apocynaceae and is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and opposite, ovate leaves. The plant produces small, white or pinkish flowers arranged in clusters, followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has medicinal uses in traditional medicine.

Alstonia micrantha thrives in humid, shaded environments and is adapted to grow in tropical and subtropical regions.
