Arabis koehleri

Arabis koehleri, commonly known as the Sierra Nevada rockcress, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of western North America.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils at high elevations. The plant produces small, white or pale purple flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are basal, forming a rosette and are covered in a fine layer of hairs.

Arabis koehleri is adapted to cold climates and is often found in alpine environments.
