Aconitum napellus f. subfissum

Aconitum napellus f. subfissum, commonly known as the European white aconite, is a variant of the deadly beauty aconite.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe. The plant features striking blue to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are deeply lobed and have a distinctive, almost fern-like appearance.

Due to its toxic alkaloids, it is considered highly poisonous and should be handled with care.
