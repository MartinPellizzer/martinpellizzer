Artemisia altaiensis

Artemisia altaiensis, commonly known as Altai wormwood, is a perennial shrub native to Central Asia.

It is characterized by its aromatic, silver-gray foliage and small, yellowish flowers. The plant thrives in dry, rocky habitats and is often found in mountainous regions. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is known for its medicinal properties.

Artemisia altaiensis is also used in traditional medicine for its purported anti-inflammatory and antiseptic effects.
