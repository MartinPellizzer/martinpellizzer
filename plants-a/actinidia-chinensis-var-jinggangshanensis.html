Actinidia chinensis var. jinggangshanensis

Actinidia chinensis var. jinggangshanensis, commonly known as the Jinggangshan kiwifruit, is a variant of the Actinidia genus native to China.

It is characterized by its vigorous growth and the production of small, fuzzy fruits with a distinctive flavor. The plant is a deciduous vine that thrives in temperate climates and requires a chilling period for optimal fruit development. Its leaves are heart-shaped with a serrated margin, and it produces yellowish-green flowers in the spring.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and potential as a fruit crop.
