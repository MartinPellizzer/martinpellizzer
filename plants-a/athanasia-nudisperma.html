Athanasia nudisperma

Athanasia nudisperma, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies, sunflowers, and ragworts. The plant is characterized by its showy, daisy-like yellow flowers and linear, grass-like leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Athanasia nudisperma is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
