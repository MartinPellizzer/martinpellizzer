Aloe translucens

Aloe translucens, commonly known as the translucent aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features slender, upright stems that are covered in a thin, waxy cuticle, which helps reduce water loss. Its leaves are narrow and fleshy, with a bluish-green hue and a translucent, waxy coating that gives it its name. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in clusters during the summer months.

It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
