Atylus formosus

Atylus formosus, commonly known as the Chinese orchid, is a species of orchid native to Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that come in a variety of colors, including white, pink, and purple. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture from the soil.

Atylus formosus is valued not only for its ornamental beauty but also for its cultural significance in traditional medicine.
