Alstonia vitiensis f. glabra

Alstonia vitiensis f. glabra, commonly known as the smooth alstonia, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Apocynaceae.

It is characterized by its smooth, glabrous bark and opposite, elliptic leaves with a smooth margin. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers followed by elongated, woody fruits containing seeds. Native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and traditional medicine practices.
