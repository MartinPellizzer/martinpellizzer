Apium fractophyllum

Apium fractophyllum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved celery.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region. The plant features slender, aromatic stems and finely divided, feathery leaves. Its flowers are small and white, arranged in umbrella-like inflorescences.

Apium fractophyllum is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its delicate appearance and pleasant fragrance.
