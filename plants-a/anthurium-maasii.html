Anthurium maasii

Anthurium maasii, commonly known as the Maasii flamingo flower, is a tropical plant native to Colombia.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are dark green with a velvety texture. The plant produces vibrant red spathes that enclose a spadix, which is typical of the Araceae family. It thrives in humid, warm environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Anthurium maasii is popular as a decorative plant due to its ornamental foliage and unique floral structures.
