Aster alatipes

Aster alatipes, commonly known as the slender aster, is a flowering plant native to the western United States and parts of Canada.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies, sunflowers, and other composite flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and stream banks. It produces showy, purple or blue flowers that bloom in the summer months.

Its leaves are narrow and often have a slightly hairy texture, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
