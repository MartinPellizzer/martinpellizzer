Aloe officinalis var. angustifolia

Aloe officinalis var. angustifolia, commonly known as narrow-leaf aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features upright, narrow, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a reddish tinge along the edges. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing yellow to orange flowers. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its leaves and requiring minimal irrigation.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional medicinal uses.
