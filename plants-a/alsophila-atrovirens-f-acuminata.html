Alsophila atrovirens f. acuminata

Alsophila atrovirens f. acuminata, commonly known as the acuminata form of the black-green tree fern, is a variant of the Alsophila atrovirens species.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and a crown of feathery, dark green fronds. The fronds are typically long and narrow, with a slightly pointed tip, giving the plant its "acuminata" designation. This fern thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive and ornamental species in botanical gardens and natural habitats.
