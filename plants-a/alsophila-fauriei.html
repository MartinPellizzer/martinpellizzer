Alsophila fauriei

Alsophila fauriei, commonly known as the Faurie's tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia and the Pacific.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate fronds. The plant has a slow growth rate and can live for several decades, making it a long-lived species. Its rhizome system supports the growth of the trunk and helps anchor the plant in moist, shaded environments.

The fronds are typically dark green and can reach lengths of up to several meters, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
