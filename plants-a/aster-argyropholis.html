Aster argyropholis

Aster argyropholis, commonly known as the silverleaf aster, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which gives it its common name. The plant produces showy blue to purple flowers that bloom in the fall. It typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is often found in open woodlands or grasslands.

Aster argyropholis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits to pollinators.
