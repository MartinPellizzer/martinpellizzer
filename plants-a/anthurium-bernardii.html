Anthurium bernardii

Anthurium bernardii, commonly known as the velvet leaf anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its large, dark green, velvety leaves that are often heart-shaped or elliptical. The plant produces striking, spadix-shaped inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, typically in shades of red or orange. It thrives in humid, shaded environments with high levels of indirect light.

Anthurium bernardii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated as a houseplant.
