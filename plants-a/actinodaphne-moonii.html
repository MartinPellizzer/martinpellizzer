Actinodaphne moonii

Actinodaphne moonii, commonly known as the Moon's Daphne, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae, which includes laurels, cinnamon, and avocado. The plant is characterized by its smooth, dark green leaves and small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruits are small, round berries that turn from green to yellow as they mature.
