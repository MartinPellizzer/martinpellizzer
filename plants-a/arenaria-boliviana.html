Arenaria boliviana

Arenaria boliviana, commonly known as the Bolivian sandwort, is a flowering plant native to the Andean regions of Bolivia.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that thrives in rocky and arid environments. The plant produces small, white or pink flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. Its leaves are typically fleshy and succulent, adapted to conserve water in dry conditions.

Arenaria boliviana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in rock gardens.
