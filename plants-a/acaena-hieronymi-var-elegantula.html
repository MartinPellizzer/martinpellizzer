Acaena hieronymi var. elegantula

Acaena hieronymi var. elegantula, commonly known as the elegant bullock's wheat, is a low-growing perennial plant native to New Zealand.

It features dense clusters of small, spiny fruits that are typically red or orange in color. The plant has compound leaves with numerous stiff, bristle-like leaflets. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.

Its botanical characteristics make it an important component of New Zealand's native flora.
