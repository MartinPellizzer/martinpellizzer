Aoranthe cladantha

Aoranthe cladantha, commonly known as the clustered orchid, is a species of flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit, with dense clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces pale yellow to greenish-white blooms that are often adorned with subtle markings. Native to tropical regions, it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Its unique morphology and ornamental value make it a sought-after species in horticulture.
