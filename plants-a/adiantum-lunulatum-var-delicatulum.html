Adiantum lunulatum var. delicatulum

Adiantum lunulatum var. delicatulum, commonly known as the moon's tears fern, is a delicate, epiphytic fern native to the southeastern United States.

It features finely divided, bright green fronds with a distinctive, almost translucent appearance. The fronds are typically arched and have a soft, feathery texture, with small, spoon-shaped pinnae that give it a graceful look. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, acidic soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in hanging baskets or as a houseplant.
