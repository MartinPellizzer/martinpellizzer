Aspalathus rigida

Aspalathus rigida, commonly known as the rigid bushveld pea, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant produces small, round, yellow pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Aspalathus rigida is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional medicine.
