Agathaea tenera

Agathaea tenera, commonly known as the soft agathis, is a flowering plant native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is a member of the Lauraceae family, which includes laurels and avocado trees. The plant is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruit is a woody capsule containing several seeds, and it is often found in shaded, humid environments.
