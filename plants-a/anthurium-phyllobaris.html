Anthurium phyllobaris

Anthurium phyllobaris, commonly known as the leafy anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are often brightly colored, ranging from deep green to variegated patterns. The plant produces small, spadix-like inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, typical of the Araceae family. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium phyllobaris is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated as a houseplant.
