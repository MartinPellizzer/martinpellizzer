Actinodaphne macroptera

Actinodaphne macroptera, commonly known as the large-leaf sandalwood, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae and is characterized by its aromatic, dark green, leathery leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by elliptical, reddish-brown fruits containing a single seed. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland and hill forests.
