Aspalathus bungei

Aspalathus bungei, commonly known as the Bunge's tea plant, is a small, evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its narrow, needle-like leaves and small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant is often cultivated for its leaves, which are used to make a mild, caffeine-free herbal tea. It prefers well-drained, sandy soils and full sun to partial shade.

Aspalathus bungei is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in arid environments.
