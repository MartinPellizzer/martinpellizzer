Acer euchlorum

Acer euchlorum, commonly known as the paper birch, is a deciduous tree native to eastern North America.

It is characterized by its white, peeling bark that reveals layers of light gray beneath, giving it a distinctive appearance. The leaves are ovate to elliptic, with finely toothed margins and a bright green upper surface. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained, acidic soils.

This tree is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to provide habitat for various wildlife species.
