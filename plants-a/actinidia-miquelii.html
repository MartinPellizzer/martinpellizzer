Actinidia miquelii

Actinidia miquelii, commonly known as the Miquel's actinidia, is a deciduous climbing vine native to China.

It belongs to the Actinidiaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and small, greenish-white flowers. The plant produces edible berries that are similar in taste to kiwifruit but are less commonly consumed. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in moist, shaded environments.

Actinidia miquelii is valued for its ornamental qualities and potential horticultural uses.
