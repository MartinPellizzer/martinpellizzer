Aerobion implicatum

Aerobion implicatum, commonly known as the spiral moss, is a unique species of moss characterized by its intricate, curling growth pattern.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest floors or along stream banks. The plant's leaves are arranged in a spiral formation, giving it a distinctive appearance. It has a delicate, feathery texture and is known for its ability to retain moisture.

This moss plays an important role in soil stabilization and provides habitat for small invertebrates.
