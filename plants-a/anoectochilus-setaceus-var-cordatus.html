Anoectochilus setaceus var. cordatus

Anoectochilus setaceus var. cordatus, commonly known as the cordate anoectochilus, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its lanceolate, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine, white pubescence. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, typically arranged in a spike. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires consistent moisture and high air humidity.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in terrariums or as a houseplant.
