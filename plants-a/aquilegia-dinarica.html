Aquilegia dinarica

Aquilegia dinarica, commonly known as the Dinaric columbine, is a perennial flowering plant native to the Balkan Peninsula.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its striking, spurred flowers that come in shades of blue, purple, and white. Aquilegia dinarica is valued for its ornamental beauty and ecological importance in supporting pollinators.
