Alsodeia verticillata

Alsodeia verticillata, commonly known as the clustered alsike, is a herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in moist meadows and grasslands, preferring well-drained soils. The plant features a basal rosette of lance-shaped leaves and produces small, yellow flowers in clusters. Its flowering period occurs in late spring to early summer.

Alsodeia verticillata is often found in disturbed habitats and can thrive in partial shade.
