Alternanthera macbridei f. minor

Alternanthera macbridei f. minor, commonly known as dwarf red Alternanthera, is a compact, trailing plant native to South America.

It features small, ovate leaves that are typically bright red or pink, though they may vary in color depending on light conditions. The plant produces tiny, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or white, which are not particularly showy. It thrives in warm, humid environments and prefers bright, indirect light.

This variety is popular in aquascaping and as a houseplant due to its vibrant foliage and ease of care.
