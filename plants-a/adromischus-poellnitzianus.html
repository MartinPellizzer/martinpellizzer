Adromischus poellnitzianus

Adromischus poellnitzianus, commonly known as the "Coral Cactus," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features small, fleshy, rounded leaves that are typically red or pink in color, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces clusters of tiny, star-shaped flowers that are usually white or pale pink during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its leaves and requiring minimal watering.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
