Aconitum zigzag subsp. kishidae

Aconitum zigzag subsp.

kishidae, commonly known as the Kishida zigzag larkspur, is a perennial herb native to Japan. It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers.

The plant features a tall, unbranched stem with alternate, lance-shaped leaves that are often adorned with wavy edges. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and stream sides.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
