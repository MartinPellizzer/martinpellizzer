Achyronia stellaris

Achyronia stellaris, commonly known as the starry achyronia, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant produces showy, star-shaped yellow flowers that bloom in late summer to early fall. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Achyronia stellaris thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands and along stream banks.
