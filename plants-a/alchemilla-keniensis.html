Alchemilla keniensis

Alchemilla keniensis, commonly known as Kenyan lady's mantle, is a flowering plant native to the highlands of Kenya.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its basal rosette of hairy, silver-green leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in early spring. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in alpine or subalpine regions.

Alchemilla keniensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional medicinal uses in local communities.
