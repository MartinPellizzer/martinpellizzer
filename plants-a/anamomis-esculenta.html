Anamomis esculenta

Anamomis esculenta, commonly known as the African bitter melon, is a climbing vine native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Cucurbitaceae family, which includes cucumbers and pumpkins. The plant produces elongated, bitter-tasting fruits that are typically harvested when green. Its leaves are heart-shaped and often used in traditional medicine.

The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
