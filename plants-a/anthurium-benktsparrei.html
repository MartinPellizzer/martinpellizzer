Anthurium benktsparrei

Anthurium benktsparrei, commonly known as the velvet leaf anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are deep green with a velvety texture. The plant produces bright red spadices, which are the modified leaves that enclose the true flowers. Anthurium benktsparrei thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

It is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
