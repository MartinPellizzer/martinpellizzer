Anthurium erythrostachyum

Anthurium erythrostachyum, commonly known as the red-stemmed anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped leaves that are typically deep green with a glossy texture. The plant produces striking, spadix-shaped inflorescences that are often surrounded by a colorful spathe. It thrives in humid, warm environments with bright, indirect light.

Due to its ornamental value, it is popular as a houseplant and in botanical gardens.
