Anthurium lloense

Anthurium lloense, commonly known as the Lloense arrowhead, is a tropical flowering plant native to the Andean region of South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its unique inflorescence consisting of a spadix and a spathe. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy dark green leaves that are often variegated with white or cream markings. It produces vibrant, spadix-like flowers that are typically red or orange in color.

Anthurium lloense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.
