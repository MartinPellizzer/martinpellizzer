Anchonium billardierei

Anchonium billardierei, commonly known as the slender anchonium, is a rare and endangered plant species native to southern Australia.

It is a small, herbaceous perennial that typically grows in dry, sandy soils. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, yellowish-green flowers during the spring. It is adapted to arid environments and has a limited geographic distribution.

Due to habitat loss and environmental changes, conservation efforts are crucial for its survival.
