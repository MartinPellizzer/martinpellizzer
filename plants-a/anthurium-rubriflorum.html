Anthurium rubriflorum

Anthurium rubriflorum, commonly known as the red-flowered anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces vibrant red spathes that enclose its spadix, which is covered in tiny, inconspicuous flowers. Anthurium rubriflorum thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, fertile soil.

It is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its ornamental foliage and unique floral structure.
