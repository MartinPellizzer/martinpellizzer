Angorkis Rohrii

Angorkis rohrii is a rare and enigmatic plant discovered in the remote highlands of Rohria. Its unique appearance features a translucent, bioluminescent stem that emits a soft blue glow at night. This plant thrives in nutrient-poor soil and requires specific humidity levels to survive. Despite its beauty, little is known about its reproductive habits or ecological role. Researchers are currently studying its potential medicinal properties and conservation status.
