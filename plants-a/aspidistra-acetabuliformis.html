Aspidistra acetabuliformis

Aspidistra acetabuliformis, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen, is a member of the Araceae family and is native to southern China.

It is characterized by its dark green, lance-shaped leaves that are often glossy and arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are followed by bright red berries. It is a low-maintenance plant that thrives in shady, humid environments.

Aspidistra acetabuliformis is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its adaptability and ornamental appeal.
