Alyssum cuneipetalum

Alyssum cuneipetalum, commonly known as the narrow-petaled alyssum, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Brassicaceae, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. The plant features small, fragrant white or pale yellow flowers arranged in dense clusters. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often covered in fine hairs.
