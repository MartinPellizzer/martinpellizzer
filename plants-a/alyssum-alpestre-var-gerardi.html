Alyssum alpestre var. gerardi

Alyssum alpestre var. gerardi, commonly known as alpine alyssum, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It features dense clusters of small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink, blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has slender, hairy stems and needle-like leaves that are typically gray-green in hue. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and alpine landscapes.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental flowers make it a valuable addition to garden ecosystems.
