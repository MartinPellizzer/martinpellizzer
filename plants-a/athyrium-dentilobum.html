Athyrium dentilobum

Athyrium dentilobum, commonly known as the toothed shield fern, is a species of fern native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the family Athyriaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, finely divided fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky slopes. Its fronds are usually bi- or tri-pinnate, with a serrated or toothed margin.

Athyrium dentilobum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and is often cultivated in shady garden settings.
