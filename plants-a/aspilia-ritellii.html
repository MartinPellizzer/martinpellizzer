Aspilia ritellii

Aspilia ritellii, commonly known as the African torch lily, is a striking plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, narrow leaves and bright yellow flowers that resemble torches. The plant typically grows in well-drained soils and thrives in full sun, making it suitable for arid or semi-arid environments. Its flowering period usually occurs during the summer months, adding a vibrant touch to its natural habitat.

Aspilia ritellii is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
