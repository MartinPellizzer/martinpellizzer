Aliciella stenothyrsa

Aliciella stenothyrsa, commonly known as the narrow-thorned aliciella, is a member of the Acanthaceae family.

It is a shrub-like plant characterized by its slender, branched stems and small, opposite leaves. The plant produces clusters of tubular, two-lipped flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas.

Aliciella stenothyrsa is often found in moist, shaded habitats such as forest understories and along stream banks.
