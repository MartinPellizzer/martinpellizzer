Ascyrum stans var. obovatum

Ascyrum stans var. obovatum, commonly known as the broad-leafed ascyrum, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to certain regions of Europe.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream sides. The plant features obovate leaves that are broad at the base and tapering toward the tip, with a smooth, waxy surface. Its small, greenish-white flowers are clustered in umbels and bloom in early spring.

The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and is often used in ornamental gardening for its aesthetic appeal.
