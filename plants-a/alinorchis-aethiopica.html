Alinorchis aethiopica

Alinorchis aethiopica, commonly known as the Ethiopian orchid, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of Ethiopia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its small, intricate flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and green. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in mountainous areas with well-drained soil. Its unique morphology and limited distribution make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists.

Due to habitat loss and environmental changes, Alinorchis aethiopica is considered endangered and requires protection to ensure its survival.
