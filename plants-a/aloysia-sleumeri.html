Aloysia sleumeri

Aloysia sleumeri, commonly known as the velvetleaf sage, is a perennial shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its grayish-green, velvety leaves and clusters of fragrant, pale blue to purple flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for xeriscaping. Its aromatic foliage and showy blooms make it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Aloysia sleumeri is also valued for its use in traditional medicine and as a source of essential oils.
