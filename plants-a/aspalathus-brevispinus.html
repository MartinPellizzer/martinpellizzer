Aspalathus brevispinus

Aspalathus brevispinus, commonly known as the small-spined bushveld pea, is a small shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and small, serrated leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, pea-like flowers that are typically pale yellow to orange in color. It is adapted to thrive in well-drained, sandy soils and is often found in grassland habitats.

Aspalathus brevispinus is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
