Aspidoglossum restioides

Aspidoglossum restioides, commonly known as the silver spider plant, is a member of the Asparagaceae family.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its striking silver-green foliage with a velvety texture. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers in clusters, which are followed by blue-black berries. It is a compact, evergreen perennial that thrives in well-drained soil and bright, indirect light.

Aspidoglossum restioides is relatively low maintenance and suitable for indoor cultivation.
