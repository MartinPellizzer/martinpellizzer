Albuca bainesii

Albuca bainesii, commonly known as the Baines' albuca, is a bulbous plant native to southern Africa.

It features striking, sword-shaped leaves that are often bluish-green in color. The plant produces clusters of pale yellow to white, tubular flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Albuca bainesii is prized for its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
