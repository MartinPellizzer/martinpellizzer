Adenanthos forrestii

Adenanthos forrestii, commonly known as the Forrester's adenanthos, is a small shrub native to southeastern Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family, which includes eucalyptus and tea trees. The plant is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers. It is typically found in heathlands and rocky areas, thriving in well-drained soils.
