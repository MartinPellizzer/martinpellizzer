Aeonium × lambii

Aeonium × lambii, commonly known as the "Lambii Aeonium," is a hybrid succulent plant native to the Canary Islands.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy, blue-green leaves that are often covered in a powdery bloom. The plant produces striking yellow or orange flowers in spring, which are borne on tall, branching stems. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or container planting.

Aeonium × lambii is prized for its ornamental value and ease of care in arid climates.
