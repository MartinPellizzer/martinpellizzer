Asplenium paleaceum

Asplenium paleaceum, commonly known as the pale fern, is a species of evergreen fern belonging to the family Aspleniaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds that are typically light green in color. This plant is native to tropical and subtropical regions, where it thrives in shaded, moist environments. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread slowly and form dense mats.

Asplenium paleaceum is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to enhance the aesthetic of shaded garden spaces.
