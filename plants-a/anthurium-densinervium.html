Anthurium densinervium

Anthurium densinervium, commonly known as the dense-veined anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves with prominent, dense veins that give it its common name. The plant produces vibrant, spatulate-shaped spathes that can range in color from red to orange, often with a contrasting green or yellow spatula. It thrives in humid, warm environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Anthurium densinervium is popular as a ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.
