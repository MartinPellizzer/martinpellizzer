Artemisia campestris var. pyramidata

Artemisia campestris var. pyramidata, commonly known as the pyramidal tarragon, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features upright, narrow leaves that are gray-green in color, with a strong, aromatic scent. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that appear in summer, typically in clustered inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and drought tolerance.
