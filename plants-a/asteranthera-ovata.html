Asteranthera ovata

Asteranthera ovata, commonly known as the oval asteranthera, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive, ovate-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. The plant produces small, tubular florets arranged in a composite head, typical of the Asteraceae family. Native to certain regions of Africa, it thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight.

Its botanical features make it a notable species for both ornamental and ecological purposes.
