Aloe scaberrima

Aloe scaberrima, commonly known as the rock aloe or sour aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that are covered in a layer of white, powdery wax. The plant typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its leaves contain a gel that is used in traditional medicine for its soothing properties.

Aloe scaberrima is also valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
