Asparagus altissimus var. foeniculaceus

Asparagus altissimus var. foeniculaceus, commonly known as the foeniculaceus asparagus, is a perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems that can reach heights of up to 3 meters. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in dense, feather-like clusters. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often giving it a distinctive, elegant appearance.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its visual appeal.
