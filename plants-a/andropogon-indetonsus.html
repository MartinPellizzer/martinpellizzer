Andropogon indetonsus

Andropogon indetonsus, commonly known as the uncertain grass, is a member of the grass family Poaceae.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and narrow, linear leaves. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as prairies and disturbed soils. Its inflorescence consists of a panicle of spikelets, which are often loosely arranged.

Due to its indeterminate classification, its exact taxonomic placement remains uncertain, making it a subject of ongoing botanical study.
