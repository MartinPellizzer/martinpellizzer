Anthurium mikeneei

Anthurium mikeneei, commonly known as the Mikeneei arrowhead, is a member of the Araceae family and is native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

This plant is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are often a deep green with a glossy texture. The inflorescence consists of a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe, which can range from white to red or orange. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect sunlight for optimal growth.

Anthurium mikeneei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in botanical gardens.
