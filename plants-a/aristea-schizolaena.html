Aristea schizolaena

Aristea schizolaena, commonly known as the narrow-leaved aristea, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid regions. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces small, daisy-like flowers in shades of white and yellow.
