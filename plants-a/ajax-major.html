Ajax major

Ajax major, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies, sunflowers, and thistles. The plant is characterized by its bright yellow flowers with a central brown disk, surrounded by white or pale pink ray florets. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Ajax major is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
