Anserina opulifolia

Anserina opulifolia, commonly known as the rosebay willowherb, is a flowering plant native to Europe, Asia, and North America.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems that can grow up to 2 meters in height, often with a reddish-brown coloration. The plant produces clusters of small, pink or reddish flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a rough texture, often with a serrated margin.
