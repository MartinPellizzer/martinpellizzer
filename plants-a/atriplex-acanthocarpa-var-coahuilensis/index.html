Atriplex Acanthocarpa Var. Coahuilensis

Atriplex acanthocarpa var. coahuilensis is a hardy shrub native to the arid regions of northern Mexico and the southwestern United States. It is known for its ability to thrive in harsh, saline environments where few other plants can survive. The plant features gray-green leaves and spiny stems that help reduce water loss and deter herbivores. It plays an important role in stabilizing soil and supporting local wildlife in desert ecosystems. This resilient species is a valuable example of plant adaptation to extreme conditions.
