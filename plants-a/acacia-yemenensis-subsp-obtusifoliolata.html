Acacia yemenensis subsp. obtusifoliolata

Acacia yemenensis subsp.

obtusifoliolata, commonly known as the Yemen acacia, is a shrub or small tree native to Yemen and parts of the Arabian Peninsula. It is characterized by its bipinnate leaves, which are composed of small, rounded leaflets that give it a distinctive appearance.

The plant produces yellow globular flowers, which are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky or sandy soils. Acacia yemenensis subsp.

obtusifoliolata is well adapted to drought conditions and is an important component of the local flora in its native range.
