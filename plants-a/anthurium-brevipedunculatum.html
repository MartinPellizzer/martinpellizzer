Anthurium brevipedunculatum

Anthurium brevipedunculatum, commonly known as the red-tipped anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces clusters of bright red spathes that enclose small, yellowish spadices, which are the actual flowering structures. Anthurium brevipedunculatum thrives in humid, warm environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

It is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its vibrant foliage and unique floral display.
