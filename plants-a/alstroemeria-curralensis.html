Alstroemeria curralensis

Alstroemeria curralensis, commonly known as the Curralense Alstroemeria, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It belongs to the family Alstroemeriaceae and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that come in a range of colors including red, orange, and yellow. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and has strap-like leaves that are often variegated.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
