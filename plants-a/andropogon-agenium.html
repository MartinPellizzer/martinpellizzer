Andropogon agenium

Andropogon agenium, commonly known as slender bluestem, is a perennial grass native to the southeastern United States.

It typically grows in wetland habitats such as marshes and swampy areas. The plant has narrow, blue-green leaves that are often rolled at the edges, giving it a distinctive appearance. It produces small, fluffy seeds that are dispersed by wind, aiding in its propagation.

Andropogon agenium plays an important role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various wildlife species.
