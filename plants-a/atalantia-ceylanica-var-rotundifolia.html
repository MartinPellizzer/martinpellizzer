Atalantia ceylanica var. rotundifolia

Atalantia ceylanica var. rotundifolia, commonly known as the round-leaf atalantia, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are broadly ovate to round in shape. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers followed by orange-red, fleshy berries. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive addition to tropical and subtropical gardens.
