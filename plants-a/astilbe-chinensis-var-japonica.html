Astilbe chinensis var. japonica

Astilbe chinensis var. japonica, commonly known as Japanese feather reed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to East Asia.

It features delicate, plume-like panicles of pink or white flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has lance-shaped, dark green leaves that are arranged in a loose, feathery pattern. It thrives in shady, moist environments and prefers well-drained, humus-rich soil.

Astilbe chinensis var. japonica is often used in ornamental gardening for its elegant appearance and ability to add texture to woodland gardens.
