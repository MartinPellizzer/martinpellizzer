Alchemilla fissa var. faeroensis

Alchemilla fissa var. faeroensis, commonly known as the Faeroese lady's mantle, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Faroe Islands.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its dense, silver-green foliage that forms a mat-like structure. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in late spring, which are clustered in compact inflorescences. Its leaves are typically lobed and covered in fine, soft hairs, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal environments.
