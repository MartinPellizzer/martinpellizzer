Alsophila engelii

Alsophila engelii, commonly known as the Engel's tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and a crown of feather-like fronds that can reach several meters in length. The plant has a slow growth rate and thrives in humid, shaded environments with well-drained soil. Its fronds are typically dark green and have a leathery texture, contributing to its ornamental value.

Alsophila engelii plays a role in tropical ecosystems by providing habitat and shelter for various small organisms.
