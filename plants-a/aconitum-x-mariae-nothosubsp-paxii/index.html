Aconitum × Mariae Nothosubsp. Paxii

Aconitum × mariae nothosubsp. paxii is a rare and striking hybrid plant known for its vibrant blue to purple flowers. It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is closely related to other aconite species. This plant is native to the mountainous regions of Central Europe, where it thrives in moist, shaded environments. Its unique genetic makeup results from crosses between Aconitum mariae and Aconitum paxii. Due to its beauty and rarity, it is highly sought after by botanists and gardeners alike.
