Aira kawakamii

Aira kawakamii, commonly known as Kawakamii grass, is a perennial grass species native to Japan.

It belongs to the Poaceae family and is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green coloration.

Aira kawakamii is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks.
