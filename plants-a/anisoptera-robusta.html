Anisoptera robusta

Anisoptera robusta, commonly known as the African fan palm, is a tropical plant native to Central and Southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, fan-shaped leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that develop into red, fleshy fruits containing seeds. Its leaves are often used in traditional crafts and as thatching material.

Anisoptera robusta thrives in warm, humid climates and is adapted to grow in a variety of soil types.
