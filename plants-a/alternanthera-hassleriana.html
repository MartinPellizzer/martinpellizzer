Alternanthera hassleriana

Alternanthera hassleriana, commonly known as the red-stemmed alternanthera, is a herbaceous plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking red stems and lance-shaped leaves that often display a vibrant red or purple hue. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant in water gardens and bog environments.

Its rapid growth and colorful foliage make it a popular choice for aquatic and terrestrial landscapes.
