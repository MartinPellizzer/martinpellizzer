Aconitum napellus f. minimum

Aconitum napellus f. minimum, commonly known as the dwarf monkshood, is a compact variant of the common monkshood.

It is a herbaceous perennial with striking blue-violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant features large, pleated leaves that are characteristic of the Aconitum genus. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Due to its toxic nature, it should be handled with care.
