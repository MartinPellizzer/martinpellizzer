Alisma plantago-aquatica f. stenophyllum

Alisma plantago-aquatica f. stenophyllum, commonly known as narrow-leaved water plantain, is a variant of the water plantain species.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant native to wetland habitats in temperate regions. The plant features narrow, linear leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette at the base. Its flowers are small and clustered in spike-like inflorescences, often with three stamens.

This subspecies is adapted to aquatic or semi-aquatic environments, thriving in shallow water or moist soils.
