Acer dieckii f. monstrosum

Acer dieckii f. monstrosum, commonly known as the giant maple, is a unique variant of the Chinese maple.

It is characterized by its massive, palmately lobed leaves that can grow up to 30 centimeters in diameter. The plant is a cultivar of Acer dieckii, which is native to China and is known for its ornamental value. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Its striking foliage makes it a popular choice for shade gardens and as a specimen plant.
