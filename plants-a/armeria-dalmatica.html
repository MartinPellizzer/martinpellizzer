Armeria dalmatica

Armeria dalmatica, commonly known as the Dalmatian thrift, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features compact, silver-green foliage and produces clusters of pink or white flowers in early summer. The plant is characterized by its succulent leaves and showy, bell-shaped blooms that attract pollinators. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Armeria dalmatica is valued for its hardiness and ornamental appeal in horticultural settings.
