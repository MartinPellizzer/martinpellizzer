Aeschynanthus amoenus

Aeschynanthus amoenus, commonly known as the pink flamingo plant, is a tropical shrub native to western Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular, pink to red flowers that resemble the plumage of a flamingo. The plant produces small, oval-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Aeschynanthus amoenus is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its vibrant floral display.
