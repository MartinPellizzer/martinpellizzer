Aloe deserti

Aloe deserti, commonly known as the desert aloe, is a succulent plant native to arid regions of southern Africa.

It features a rosette of thick, fleshy leaves that store water, enabling it to survive in dry conditions. The leaves are typically blue-green in color with a waxy coating that reduces water loss. It produces yellow or orange flowers on tall, branched stems during the warmer months.

This plant is well adapted to harsh environments and is often used in traditional medicine.
