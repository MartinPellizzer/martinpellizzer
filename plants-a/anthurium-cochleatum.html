Anthurium cochleatum

Anthurium cochleatum, commonly known as the spiral anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are often brightly colored, ranging from deep green to red. The plant produces a unique spadix inflorescence surrounded by a colorful spathe, which is typical of the Araceae family. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Anthurium cochleatum is popular as a decorative plant due to its ornamental foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
