Asplenium unilaterale f. majus

Asplenium unilaterale f. majus, commonly known as the giant shield fern, is a variant of the shield fern species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its large, leathery, evergreen fronds that can grow up to several feet in length. The plant typically thrives in shaded, moist environments with high humidity, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation. Its fronds are divided into shield-shaped segments, giving it a distinctive appearance.

The plant is also known for its ability to tolerate low light conditions, making it adaptable to various indoor settings.
