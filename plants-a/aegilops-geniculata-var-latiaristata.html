Aegilops geniculata var. latiaristata

Aegilops geniculata var. latiaristata, commonly known as the slender wild oat, is a grass species native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences with two to three flowers per spikelet. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of habitats, including fields and along roadsides.

This species is notable for its potential agricultural and ecological significance.
