Aloe marlothii var. bicolor

Aloe marlothii var. bicolor, commonly known as the bicolor aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, fleshy leaves that are typically green with red or yellow markings. The plant produces tall, branched flower stalks that bear yellow to orange flowers, adding to its ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

This variety is often cultivated for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
