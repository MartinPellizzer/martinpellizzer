Astilbe leucantha

Astilbe leucantha, commonly known as the white feather astilbe, is a herbaceous perennial native to eastern Asia.

It features delicate, plume-like inflorescences that range in color from white to pale pink, giving it a soft and ethereal appearance. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery down. It is valued in gardens for its ornamental foliage and subtle floral display.

Astilbe leucantha prefers well-drained, loamy soils and can be grown in USDA hardiness zones 4 through 8.
