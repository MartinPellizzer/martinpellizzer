Aglaonema haenkei

Aglaonema haenkei, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen, is a tropical evergreen plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features striking, variegated foliage with shades of green, white, and pink, making it a popular ornamental plant. The leaves are typically lance-shaped and can grow up to 30 centimeters in length. It thrives in low light conditions and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This plant is known for its air-purifying qualities and is often used in interior landscaping.
