Acinodendron medium

Acinodendron medium, commonly known as the African milk tree, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small to medium-sized tree that can grow up to 10 meters in height. The plant is characterized by its smooth, pale bark and compound leaves with long, slender leaflets. Its flowers are small and clustered in spikes, typically appearing in yellow or greenish-white hues.

The plant is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it drought-tolerant and suitable for xeriscaping.
