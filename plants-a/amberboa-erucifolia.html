Amberboa erucifolia

Amberboa erucifolia, commonly known as the yellow rocket, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has slender, branching stems and lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Amberboa erucifolia is also known for its historical use in traditional medicine.
