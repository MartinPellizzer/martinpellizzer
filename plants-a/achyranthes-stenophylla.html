Achyranthes stenophylla

Achyranthes stenophylla, commonly known as narrow-leaf smartweed, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa and Asia.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish flowers arranged in dense clusters at the tips of the stems. It thrives in moist, disturbed soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Its rapid growth and ability to spread through rhizomes make it an invasive species in some regions.
