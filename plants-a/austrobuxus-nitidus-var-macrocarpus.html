Austrobuxus nitidus var. macrocarpus

Austrobuxus nitidus var. macrocarpus, commonly known as the glossy boxwood, is a shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage, which is typically glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by red, fleshy berries. Its leaves are simple, elliptical, and have a leathery texture.

This variety is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appearance and hardiness in various climates.
