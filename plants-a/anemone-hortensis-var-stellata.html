Anemone hortensis var. stellata

Anemone hortensis var. stellata, commonly known as the star anemone, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It features showy, white to pink, star-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has a short, upright stem with basal leaves that are heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking ornamental foliage and early-season blooms.
