Alvardia arborescens

Alvardia arborescens, commonly known as the golden flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features striking, bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer seasons. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually ovate or elliptic in shape. Its bark is smooth and often exhibits a reddish or brownish color.

Alvardia arborescens is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant floral display.
