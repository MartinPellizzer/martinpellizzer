Arachis stenosperma

Arachis stenosperma, commonly known as the narrow-seeded groundnut, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant produces small, yellow flowers and develops underground pods containing seeds. Its seeds are used as a food source and are rich in protein and oil.

Arachis stenosperma is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it beneficial for agricultural systems.
