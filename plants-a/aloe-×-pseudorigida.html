Aloe × pseudorigida

Aloe × pseudorigida, commonly known as the hybrid aloe, is a succulent plant native to the Cape region of South Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a white powdery coating called farina. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with clusters of yellow or orange flowers. Its leaves are used in traditional medicine for their soothing and healing properties.

Aloe × pseudorigida is valued both for its ornamental beauty and its practical applications in horticulture and herbal remedies.
