Amauropelta pachyrhachis

Amauropelta pachyrhachis, commonly known as the thick-stemmed shield fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its large, shield-shaped fronds that are typically broad and robust. The plant has a distinctive rhizome that is thick and fleshy, which helps it store water and nutrients. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its unique morphology makes it a notable species in botanical studies and horticulture.
