Aerangis floribunda

Aerangis floribunda, commonly known as the fragrant orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its elegant, pendulous flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces showy, fragrant blossoms in shades of white, pink, and purple, often with a subtle spicy scent. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture from the humid environment it thrives in.

Aerangis floribunda is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
