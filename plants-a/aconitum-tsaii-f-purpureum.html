Aconitum tsaii f. purpureum

Aconitum tsaii f. purpureum, commonly known as the purple-flowered aconite, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Ranunculaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking purple or violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant features palmately divided, deeply lobed leaves that are typically bluish-green in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Due to its toxic alkaloids, it is important to handle the plant with care.
