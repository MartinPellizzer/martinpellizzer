Asplenium erectum var. aequibasis

Asplenium erectum var. aequibasis, commonly known as the upright spleenwort, is a fern species native to North America.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and lance-shaped, dark green leaves. The fronds are typically short and compact, with a distinctive basal lobing that gives it its scientific name. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as rocky crevices and forest floors.

This variety is often found in the northeastern United States and parts of Canada.
