Asplenium callipteris var. pinnatisectum

Asplenium callipteris var. pinnatisectum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf maidenhair fern, is a variant of the common maidenhair fern.

It is a perennial fern native to parts of Asia, including China and Japan. The plant features delicate, finely divided, dark green fronds that are pinnatisect, meaning they are deeply divided into narrow segments. It prefers moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for humid indoor gardens and shaded outdoor landscapes.
