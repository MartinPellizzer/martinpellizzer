Aerides trigona

Aerides trigona, commonly known as the triangular orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, triangular-shaped pseudobulbs and elongated, lanceolate leaves. The plant produces inflorescences with numerous fragrant, showy flowers that are typically pink or white in color. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Aerides trigona is popular among orchid enthusiasts due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
