Arachnorchis viridescens

Arachnorchis viridescens, commonly known as the green spider orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to southeastern Australia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests and understoreys. The plant features a slender, leafless stem with a single, lance-shaped leaf. Its flowers are greenish-yellow with prominent dark veins, and they bloom from late winter to early spring.

Arachnorchis viridescens is a rare and delicate species, often found in specific microhabitats.
