Anthurium glanduligerum

Anthurium glanduligerum, commonly known as the glandular anthurium, is a tropical flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are often glossy and deep green. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-like inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, typically in shades of red or orange. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Anthurium glanduligerum is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
