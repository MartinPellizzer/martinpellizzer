Amyris guianensis

Amyris guianensis, commonly known as the Guianese amyris, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, resinous wood. The plant typically grows as a small tree or shrub, reaching heights of up to 10 meters. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a rough texture.

The flowers are small and clustered, with a strong, sometimes unpleasant odor.
