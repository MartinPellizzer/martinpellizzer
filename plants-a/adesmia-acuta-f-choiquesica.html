Adesmia acuta f. choiquesica

Adesmia acuta f. choiquesica, commonly known as the Choique's sharp adesmia, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, branching stems and small, trifoliate leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces yellowish-white flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences, typical of the Fabaceae family. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in sandy soils of South America.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and taxonomic studies.
