Achillea schreineri

Achillea schreineri, commonly known as Schreiner's yarrow, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, herbaceous growth habit. Its leaves are finely divided and gray-green in color, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Schreiner's yarrow is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to well-drained soils.
