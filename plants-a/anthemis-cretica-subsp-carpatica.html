Anthemis cretica subsp. carpatica

Anthemis cretica subsp.

carpatica, commonly known as the Carpathian ragwort, is a flowering plant native to the Carpathian region. It is a perennial herb with a compact, upright growth habit and hairy stems.

The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in dense, flat-topped clusters, typical of the Asteraceae family. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

It thrives in rocky or calcareous soils and is often found in mountainous areas.
