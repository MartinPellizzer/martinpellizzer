Anthurium elquincense

Anthurium elquincense, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves and vibrant red spathes that encase the spadix. The plant belongs to the Araceae family and is prized for its ornamental value in both indoor and outdoor gardens. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Anthurium elquincense requires well-draining soil and regular watering to maintain its health and aesthetic appeal.
