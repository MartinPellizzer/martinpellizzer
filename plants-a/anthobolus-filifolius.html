Anthobolus filifolius

Anthobolus filifolius, commonly known as the silky filifolius, is a species of flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, silvery-green leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a soft appearance. Its flowers are typically small and yellow, arranged in dense clusters.
