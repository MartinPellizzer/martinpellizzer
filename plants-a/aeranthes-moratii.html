Aeranthes moratii

Aeranthes moratii, commonly known as the Moratii's orchid, is a species of orchid native to South America, particularly found in Brazil.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant has a compact growth habit with a pseudobulb and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and somewhat challenging plant to grow in cultivation.
