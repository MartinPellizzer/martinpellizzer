Axonopus boliviensis

Axonopus boliviensis, commonly known as the Bolivian axonopus, is a perennial grass species native to South America.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories and wetlands. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, inconspicuous flowers. It is often found in regions with high humidity and well-drained soil.

Due to its adaptability, it is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
