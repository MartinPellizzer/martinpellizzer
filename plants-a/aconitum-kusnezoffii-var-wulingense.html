Aconitum kusnezoffii var. wulingense

Aconitum kusnezoffii var. wulingense, commonly known as the Wuling monkshood, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to China.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to purple hooded flowers. The plant features large, palmately compound leaves with a deeply lobed structure. It typically grows in moist, shady environments such as forests and stream sides.

Due to its toxic alkaloids, it should be handled with care.
