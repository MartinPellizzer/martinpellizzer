Aloe silicicola

Aloe silicicola, commonly known as the silica aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces yellow-orange flowers during the summer months, which are borne on tall, branching inflorescences. Its leaves contain a gel-like substance that is sometimes used for its purported medicinal properties.

Aloe silicicola is adapted to sandy soils and full sun exposure, making it a hardy, low-maintenance plant for xeriscaping.
