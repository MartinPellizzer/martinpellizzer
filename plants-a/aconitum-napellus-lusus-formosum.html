Aconitum napellus lusus formosum

Aconitum napellus lusus formosum, commonly known as the Chinese monkshood, is a variant of the common aconite.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of China. The plant features striking blue to purple flowers arranged in a spire-like inflorescence. Its leaves are palmately lobed with a distinctive hooded shape.

Due to its toxic alkaloids, it is considered highly poisonous and requires careful handling.
