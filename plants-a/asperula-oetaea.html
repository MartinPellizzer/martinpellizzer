Asperula oetaea

Asperula oetaea, commonly known as the Alpine shrub or Etna heath, is a small evergreen shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils, often in alpine or subalpine habitats. The plant features small, dark green leaves that are densely arranged along its stems. Its flowers are usually pink or white, appearing in clusters during the spring and early summer.

Asperula oetaea is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in harsh environmental conditions.
