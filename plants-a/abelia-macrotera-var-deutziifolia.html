Abelia macrotera var. deutziifolia

Abelia macrotera var. deutziifolia, commonly known as the dwarf abelia, is a compact shrub native to China.

It features small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green color. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale pink flowers during the summer months. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and hardy nature in garden settings.
