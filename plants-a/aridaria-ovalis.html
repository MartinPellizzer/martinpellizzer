Aridaria ovalis

Aridaria ovalis, commonly known as the oval leaf desert plant, is a succulent native to arid regions.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that store water, enabling it to survive in dry environments. The plant produces small, pale flowers during brief periods of rainfall. Its root system is extensive, allowing it to access deep underground moisture.

Aridaria ovalis plays a vital role in stabilizing sandy soils and providing habitat for desert wildlife.
