Adromischus schuldtianus

Adromischus schuldtianus, commonly known as the Schuldt's ice plant, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, thick leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the summer months. It is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its compact size and attractive foliage.

Adromischus schuldtianus prefers well-draining soil and full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
