Aloe schilliana

Aloe schilliana, commonly known as the Schill's aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, with a waxy coating to reduce water loss. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow flowers, which bloom in the spring. It is adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Aloe schilliana is valued both for its ornamental beauty and its potential medicinal properties.
