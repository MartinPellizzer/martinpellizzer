Alsophila fallacina

Alsophila fallacina, commonly known as the slender tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and feathery fronds. The plant has a slow growth rate and can reach heights of up to 10 meters in suitable conditions. Its rhizome is thick and woody, supporting the upright structure of the plant.

The fronds are bi-directionally arranged, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
