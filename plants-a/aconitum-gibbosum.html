Aconitum gibbosum

Aconitum gibbosum, commonly known as the humped aconite, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of central and southern Europe.

It features striking blue to purple flowers that bloom in late summer, typically from July to September. The plant has dark green, heart-shaped leaves that are deeply lobed and covered in a fine down. Aconitum gibbosum grows up to 1.5 meters tall, with a sturdy stem that supports its showy floral display.

It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and alpine settings.
