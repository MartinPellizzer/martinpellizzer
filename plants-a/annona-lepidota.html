Annona lepidota

Annona lepidota, commonly known as the rough custard apple, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is a small to medium-sized tree or shrub that can grow up to 5 meters in height. The plant features smooth, dark green leaves with a slightly hairy underside. Its flowers are typically white or pale green, with a strong, unpleasant odor.

The fruit is a rough, spiky berry that turns from green to yellow or red when mature.
