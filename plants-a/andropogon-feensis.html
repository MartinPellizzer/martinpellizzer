Andropogon feensis

Andropogon feensis, commonly known as the Feense grass, is a species of grass native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and narrow, linear leaves that are typically green in color. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences that are often wind-pollinated. It thrives in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and woodland edges.

Andropogon feensis is valued for its role in soil stabilization and as a forage plant for livestock.
