Aglaonema nebulosum f. nanum

Aglaonema nebulosum f. nanum, commonly known as the dwarf cloud plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves with distinctive, wavy margins and a subtle, variegated pattern. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers, though it is primarily grown for its ornamental foliage. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate humidity and well-draining soil.

This variety is prized for its compact size and ability to thrive indoors with minimal care.
