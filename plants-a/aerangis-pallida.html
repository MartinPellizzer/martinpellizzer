Aerangis pallida

Aerangis pallida, commonly known as the pale orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to parts of Southeast Asia, including Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant produces delicate, pale pink to white flowers that bloom during the wet season. Its leaves are elliptical and arranged in a basal rosette, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Aerangis pallida is often cultivated for its aesthetic value in tropical gardens and as a cut flower.
