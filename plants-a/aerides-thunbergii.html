Aerides thunbergii

Aerides thunbergii, commonly known as the Thunberg's orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant has pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, aiding in its survival in humid, shaded environments. Its roots are epiphytic, allowing it to grow on trees without harming them.

The flowers typically bloom in summer, adding ornamental value to both indoor and outdoor gardens.
