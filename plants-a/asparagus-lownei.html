Asparagus lownei

Asparagus lownei, commonly known as the Lowne's asparagus, is a perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its dense, tufted growth habit. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, inconspicuous white or greenish flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
