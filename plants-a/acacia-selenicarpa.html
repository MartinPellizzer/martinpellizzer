Acacia selenicarpa

Acacia selenicarpa, commonly known as the silver acacia, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, pale green foliage and yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has small, flattened pods that contain seeds, and it is adapted to arid environments. It is often used in traditional medicine and has ornamental value due to its attractive appearance.

Its botanical features make it well-suited for xeriscaping in dry climates.
