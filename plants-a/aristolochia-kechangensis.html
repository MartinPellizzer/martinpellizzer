Aristolochia kechangensis

Aristolochia kechangensis, commonly known as the Kechang birthwort, is a flowering plant belonging to the Aristolochiaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of southern China, particularly in Yunnan Province. The plant is characterized by its unique, tubular-shaped flowers that are often greenish or brownish in color. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a rough texture.

Due to its ornamental value and potential medicinal properties, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and research settings.
