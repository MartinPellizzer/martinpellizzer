Argyrolobium robustum

Argyrolobium robustum, commonly known as the silver globular pea, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its globular, silver-gray seed pods that resemble small balls, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Argyrolobium robustum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
