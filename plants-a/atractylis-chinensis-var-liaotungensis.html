Atractylis chinensis var. liaotungensis

Atractylis chinensis var. liaotungensis, commonly known as the Liaotung Atractylis, is a perennial herb native to eastern China.

It is characterized by its fibrous root system and upright, hairy stems that bear alternate, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in panicles, which bloom in the summer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and mountainous regions.

This variant is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional Chinese medicine.
