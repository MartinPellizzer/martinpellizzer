Acanthocladus dukei

Acanthocladus dukei, commonly known as the African snowflake, is a rare and unique plant species native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in late winter. The plant has a compact, shrub-like growth habit with dense foliage. Its leaves are small, narrow, and covered in a fine, silvery bloom.
