Arisaema elephas var. handelii

Arisaema elephas var. handelii, commonly known as the giant arrowhead, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in diameter, with a distinctive arrowhead-shaped spathe that encloses the spadix. The plant produces showy, spadix-shaped inflorescences that are typically yellow-green in color, surrounded by a hooded spathe. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

This variant is prized for its ornamental value and is cultivated for its dramatic foliage and unique floral structure.
