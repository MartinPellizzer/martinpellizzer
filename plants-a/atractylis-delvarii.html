Atractylis delvarii

Atractylis delvarii, commonly known as Delvar's wormwood, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features tall, hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often grayish-green in color. Its flowers are small and yellow, forming clusters that attract various pollinators.

Atractylis delvarii is typically found in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to thrive in arid environments.
