Abutilon fraseri

Abutilon fraseri, commonly known as the Fraser's abutilon, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments. The plant features soft, hairy stems and heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture. It produces showy, lantern-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange, which bloom in the spring.

Abutilon fraseri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
