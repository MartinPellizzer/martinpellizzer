Armeria maritima var. caballeroi

Armeria maritima var. caballeroi, commonly known as the Caballero's sea thrift, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of California and Baja California.

It is a low-growing perennial with dense clusters of white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has silver-gray foliage with a fuzzy texture, adapted to thrive in sandy, well-drained soils. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to stabilize sandy soils along shorelines.
