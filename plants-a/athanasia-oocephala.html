Athanasia oocephala

Athanasia oocephala, commonly known as the Cape goldcup, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features yellow ray florets surrounding a central disk of tubular flowers, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in coastal areas.

Athanasia oocephala is also known for its resilience to drought and salt spray, making it well adapted to harsh environments.
