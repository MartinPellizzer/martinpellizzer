Ardisia mystica

Ardisia mystica, commonly known as the purple-leafed ardisia, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that turn a striking purple or reddish hue during the cooler months. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers followed by dark red berries that are edible but often used as ornamental features. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is valued for its aesthetic appeal in landscaping.

Ardisia mystica is also noted for its ability to tolerate partial sunlight, making it adaptable to various garden settings.
