Astilbe longicarpa

Astilbe longicarpa, commonly known as the long-stemmed astilbe, is a herbaceous perennial native to eastern Asia.

It features upright, branched stems that bear clusters of fragrant, lace-like flowers in shades of pink, white, or red. The plant has dark green, pinnately compound leaves that are often used in ornamental gardening. It thrives in moist, shady environments and prefers well-drained, fertile soil.

Astilbe longicarpa is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to add color to woodland gardens.
