Arenaria querioides subsp. oscensis

Arenaria querioides subsp.

oscensis, commonly known as the Desert Sand Verbena, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico. It is a low-growing perennial shrub that thrives in arid and semi-arid environments.

The plant produces small, pink or purple flowers that bloom in the spring and summer months. Its leaves are typically narrow and gray-green, adapted to reduce water loss in hot, dry conditions.

It is often found in sandy soils and is an important component of desert ecosystems.
