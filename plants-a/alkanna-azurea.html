Alkanna azurea

Alkanna azurea, commonly known as blue alkanna, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking blue-violet flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits.

Alkanna azurea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant color.
