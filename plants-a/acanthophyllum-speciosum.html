Acanthophyllum speciosum

Acanthophyllum speciosum, commonly known as the beautiful acanthophyllum, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of Central Asia.

It is a shrub characterized by its dense, spiny foliage and compact growth form, which helps it conserve water in harsh environments. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its roots are deep and extensive, allowing it to access groundwater in dry soils.

Acanthophyllum speciosum plays an important ecological role in stabilizing desert soils and supporting local biodiversity.
