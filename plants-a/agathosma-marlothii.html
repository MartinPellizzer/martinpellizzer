Agathosma marlothii

Agathosma marlothii, commonly known as the Cape daisy, is a perennial shrub native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant has aromatic leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in well-drained soils and is drought-tolerant, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
