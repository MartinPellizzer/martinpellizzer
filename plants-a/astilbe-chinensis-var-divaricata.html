Astilbe chinensis var. divaricata

Astilbe chinensis var. divaricata, commonly known as the Chinese astilbe, is a herbaceous perennial native to China.

It features dense, feathery plumes of pink to white flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has lance-shaped, dark green leaves that are arranged in a basal rosette. It thrives in moist, shady environments and prefers well-drained, fertile soil.

Astilbe chinensis var. divaricata is valued for its ornamental foliage and delicate floral display in garden settings.
