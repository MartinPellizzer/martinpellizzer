Astrolepis cochisensis subsp. chihuahuensis

The plant thrives in shaded, moist environments such as rocky crevices and forest understories. Its reproductive structures, including sori, are located on the underside of the fronds, typical of many ferns.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a species of conservation concern.
