Acacia suaveolens subsp. prostrata

Acacia suaveolens subsp.

prostrata, commonly known as the prostrate sweet acacia, is a low-growing shrub native to southern Africa. It features slender, prostrate stems that often root at the nodes, forming dense mats.

The plant produces small, yellow, globular flowers arranged in clusters, which are typical of the Acacia genus. Its leaves are bipinnate, with numerous small, linear leaflets.

This subspecies is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it resilient to drought conditions.
