Acaena stellaris

Acaena stellaris, commonly known as the star burr, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its distinctive star-shaped flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces small, spiky fruits that contain seeds enclosed in a hairy, prickly husk. It thrives in a variety of soil types but prefers well-drained conditions.

Acaena stellaris is often found in coastal and subalpine regions, where it contributes to local biodiversity.
