Anoectochilus xanthophyllus

Anoectochilus xanthophyllus, commonly known as the golden orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, strap-like leaves and produces yellow-orange flowers with a distinctive red lip. The plant thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found in forest understories. It has a short rhizome and is known for its ornamental value due to its vibrant floral display.

Anoectochilus xanthophyllus is also valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
