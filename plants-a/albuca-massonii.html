Albuca massonii

Albuca massonii, commonly known as the Cape tulip, is a bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features striking, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink and yellow, often resembling tulips. The plant produces a tall, slender stem with narrow, grass-like leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Albuca massonii is also known for its ability to produce multiple offsets, allowing for easy propagation.
