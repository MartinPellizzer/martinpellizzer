Aeonium × castellodecorum

Aeonium × castellodecorum, commonly known as the Decorum Aeonium, is a hybrid succulent native to the Canary Islands.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy leaves that range in color from deep green to reddish hues, depending on light exposure. The plant produces tall, branching stems with clusters of yellow flowers in late spring or early summer. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with full sun.

Due to its striking appearance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and as a houseplant.
