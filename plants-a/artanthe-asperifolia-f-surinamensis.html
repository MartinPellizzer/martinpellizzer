Artanthe asperifolia f. surinamensis

Artanthe asperifolia f. surinamensis, commonly known as the Surinam artanthe, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South America.

It features upright, branching stems covered in rough, bristly hairs. The leaves are lance-shaped, with a prominently veined underside and a serrated margin. Its flowers are small, clustered in dense inflorescences, typically in shades of pink or purple.

This plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded, woodland environments.
