Amaranthus hybridus var. bouchonii

Amaranthus hybridus var. bouchonii, commonly known as the Bouchonii amaranth, is a species of flowering plant in the amaranth family.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and vibrant, feather-like bracts that display a range of colors, including red, orange, and purple. The plant is native to South America and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It prefers full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for gardens and landscapes.

Its seeds are also edible and can be used in various culinary applications.
