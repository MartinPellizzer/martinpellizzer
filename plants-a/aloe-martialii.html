Aloe martialii

Aloe martialii, commonly known as the Martial's aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy, gray-green leaves that are covered in a waxy cuticle to reduce water loss. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellowish-green flowers, typically blooming in spring. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it suitable for arid environments.

Aloe martialii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional medicinal uses.
