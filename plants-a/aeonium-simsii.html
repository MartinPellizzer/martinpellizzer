Aeonium simsii

Aeonium simsii, commonly known as the "Lion's Tail," is a succulent plant native to the Canary Islands.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with red or pink hues along the edges. The plant produces tall, branched flowering stalks that bear yellow flowers, which appear in the spring. Aeonium simsii is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a favored ornamental plant.
