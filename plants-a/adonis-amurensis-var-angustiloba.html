Adonis amurensis var. angustiloba

Adonis amurensis var. angustiloba, commonly known as the narrow-leaved adonis, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to eastern Asia.

It features basal rosettes of narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine pubescence. The plant produces yellow flowers in early spring, which are arranged in a single terminal raceme. It is adapted to grow in moist, well-drained soils in woodland or shrubland habitats.

Its botanical characteristics make it a distinctive species within the Ranunculaceae family.
