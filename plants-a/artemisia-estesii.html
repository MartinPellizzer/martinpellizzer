Artemisia estesii

Artemisia estesii, commonly known as Estes' sagebrush, is a perennial shrub native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its aromatic, silver-gray foliage and small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky soils and open habitats. Its leaves contain essential oils that contribute to its strong, minty scent.

Artemisia estesii plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various wildlife species.
