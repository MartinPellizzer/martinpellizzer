Aretia carnea var. elongata

Aretia carnea var. elongata, commonly known as the elongated aretia, is a tropical flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It features elongated, lance-shaped leaves that are typically bright green with a waxy texture. The plant produces striking clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of pink and red, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in humid, well-drained soil and requires indirect sunlight to flourish.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for tropical gardens and indoor collections.
