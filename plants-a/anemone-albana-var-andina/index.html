Anemone Albana Var. Andina

Andean white anemone, scientifically known as Anemone albana var. andina, is a rare and striking flower native to the high-altitude regions of the Andes. This delicate plant is characterized by its pure white petals and compact, daisy-like blooms that stand out against the rugged mountain landscape. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine meadows and rocky outcrops. Due to its limited distribution and fragile habitat, the Andean white anemone is considered a vulnerable species. Conservation efforts are crucial to protect this unique botanical wonder from the threats of climate change and habitat destruction.
