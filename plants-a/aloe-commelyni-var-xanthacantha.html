Aloe commelyni var. xanthacantha

Aloe commelyni var. xanthacantha, commonly known as the yellow aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a yellowish or reddish tinge along the edges. The plant produces upright, branched inflorescences with yellow flowers that bloom in summer. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Its unique coloration and ornamental value make it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor gardens.
