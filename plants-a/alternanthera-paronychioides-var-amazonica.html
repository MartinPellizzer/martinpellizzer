Alternanthera paronychioides var. amazonica

Alternanthera paronychioides var. amazonica, commonly known as the Amazonian Alternanthera, is a striking aquatic plant native to South America.

It features vibrant red and purple foliage that intensifies under strong lighting conditions. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often tinged with red, making it a popular choice for aquariums. It thrives in moderate to high light and requires stable water parameters.

Its unique coloration and ease of care make it a favored addition to freshwater planted tanks.
