Aralia chinensis var. pyramidalis

Aralia chinensis var. pyramidalis, commonly known as the Chinese angelica or pyramid aralia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to eastern Asia.

It features compound leaves that are typically arranged in a feathery, pyramidal shape, giving it its distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers in umbels, which are followed by bright red berries. It thrives in partial to full shade and is often used in ornamental gardening for its architectural form.

The species is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional Chinese medicine.
