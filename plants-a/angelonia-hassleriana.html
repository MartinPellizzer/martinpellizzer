Angelonia hassleriana

Angelonia hassleriana, commonly known as the winter angelonia, is a perennial flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Plantaginaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, tubular flowers. The plant produces clusters of bright pink to red flowers that bloom throughout the growing season, making it a popular choice for gardens. Its foliage is broad, lance-shaped, and covered in a fine, silvery down, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Angelonia hassleriana is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and containers.
