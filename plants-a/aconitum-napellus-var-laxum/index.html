Aconitum Napellus Var. Laxum

Aconitum napellus var. laxum is a striking member of the buttercup family known for its vibrant blue to purple flowers. This variant of the deadly beauty aconite is native to the mountainous regions of Europe and Asia. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade. The plant is toxic due to its high concentration of alkaloids, making it a dangerous species to handle. Despite its harmful properties, it is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.
