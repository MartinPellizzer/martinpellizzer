Aconitum carmichaelii var. carmichaelii

Aconitum carmichaelii var. carmichaelii, commonly known as Chinese aconite, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to East Asia.

It features striking blue to purple spires of flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has dark green, deeply lobed leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.

Due to its toxic alkaloids, it is important to handle the plant with care.
