Alstroemeria aurantiaca

Alstroemeria aurantiaca, commonly known as the orange alstroemeria, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the family Alstroemeriaceae and is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from orange to red. The plant typically grows as a herbaceous perennial, with strap-like leaves and a compact growth habit. Its flowers are often used in floral arrangements due to their long-lasting nature and striking appearance.

Alstroemeria aurantiaca prefers well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
