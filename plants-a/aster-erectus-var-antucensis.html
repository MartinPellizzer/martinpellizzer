Aster erectus var. antucensis

Aster erectus var. antucensis, commonly known as the Antuco aster, is a flowering plant native to the Andes Mountains of Chile.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy blue to purple ray florets. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils at high altitudes. It belongs to the sunflower family (Asteraceae) and is known for its compact, bushy form.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in alpine gardens.
