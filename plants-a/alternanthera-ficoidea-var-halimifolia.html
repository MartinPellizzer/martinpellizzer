Alternanthera ficoidea var. halimifolia

Alternanthera ficoidea var. halimifolia, commonly known as the red-leaf alternate or red velvet plant, is a striking aquatic plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red foliage, which becomes more intense under bright light and nutrient-rich conditions. The plant has slender, branching stems that can grow up to 30 cm in length, making it suitable for both foreground and midground in aquariums. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a velvety texture, enhancing its ornamental appeal.

It prefers moderate to high water temperatures and is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice among aquarium enthusiasts.
