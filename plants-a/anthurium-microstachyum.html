Anthurium microstachyum

Anthurium microstachyum, commonly known as the small-stalked anthurium, is a tropical flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-like inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, which can range from red to orange in hue. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.

Anthurium microstachyum is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its ornamental foliage and unique floral structure.
