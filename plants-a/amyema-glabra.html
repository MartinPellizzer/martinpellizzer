Amyema glabra

Amyema glabra, commonly known as the smooth amyema, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Myrtaceae, which includes eucalyptus and tea trees. The plant is characterized by its smooth, glabrous stems and opposite, ovate leaves. It produces small, white or pale pink flowers that are clustered in inflorescences.

Amyema glabra is often found in dry, sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments.
