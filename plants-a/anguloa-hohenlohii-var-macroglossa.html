Anguloa hohenlohii var. macroglossa

Anguloa hohenlohii var. macroglossa, commonly known as the giant spider lily, is a striking member of the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is native to the Andean regions of South America, where it thrives in cool, humid environments. The plant is characterized by its large, showy white flowers with a distinctive red or orange stripe down the center. Its leaves are broad and strap-like, often reaching several feet in length.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its dramatic floral display.
