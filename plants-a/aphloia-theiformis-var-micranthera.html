Aphloia theiformis var. micranthera

Aphloia theiformis var. micranthera, commonly known as the slender flame tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, flame-like inflorescences that appear in clusters along the stems. The leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a slightly waxy texture. The flowers are small, fragrant, and typically white or pale yellow, with showy bracts that enhance their visual appeal.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value in warm climates.
