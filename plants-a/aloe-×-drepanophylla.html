Aloe × drepanophylla

Aloe × drepanophylla, commonly known as the hybrid aloe or the hybrid tree aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is a hybrid between Aloe arborescens and Aloe ferox, resulting in a unique combination of traits from both parent species. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, featuring thick, fleshy leaves that are often bluish-green in color. It produces yellow flowers in clusters, which bloom in late spring or early summer.

Aloe × drepanophylla is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its drought tolerance and attractive foliage.
