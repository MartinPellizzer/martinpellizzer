Ardisia robusta

Ardisia robusta, commonly known as the glossy buckeye, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, dark green, elliptical leaves that are typically arranged in whorls at the nodes. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers that are followed by bright red, berry-like fruits. Ardisia robusta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade.
