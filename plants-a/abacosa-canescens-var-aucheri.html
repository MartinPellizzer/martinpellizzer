Abacosa canescens var. aucheri

Abacosa canescens var. aucheri, commonly known as the hairy abacosa, is a variant of the abacosa plant native to certain regions of South America.

It is characterized by its grayish-green, hairy leaves and compact growth habit. The plant typically produces small, white to pale yellow flowers during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
