Alpagrostis schleicheri

Alpagrostis schleicheri, commonly known as the Schleicher's bluegrass, is a perennial grass species native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its fine, bluish-green foliage and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in well-drained soils and is adapted to temperate climates. It is often found in open habitats such as meadows and rocky slopes.

Its botanical features make it a valuable component of native grassland ecosystems.
