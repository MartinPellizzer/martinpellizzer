Aloe disticha

Aloe disticha, commonly known as the zigzag aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features short, fleshy leaves that are arranged in a zigzag pattern, giving it its distinctive appearance. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow-orange flowers that bloom in spring. Its leaves contain a gel-like substance that is often used for its soothing properties.

Aloe disticha is well-suited for arid environments and requires minimal water to thrive.
