Aerides retusa

Aerides retusa, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers with a distinctive lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
