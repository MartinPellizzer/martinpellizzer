Astroloba turgida

Astroloba turgida, commonly known as the star-shaped bulbous plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features a swollen, rounded base with a cluster of narrow, grass-like leaves that radiate outward. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It is adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water, making it a hardy choice for rock gardens.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular ornamental plant.
