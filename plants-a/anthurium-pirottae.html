Anthurium pirottae

Anthurium pirottae, commonly known as the Pirotta's anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically deep green with a glossy texture. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-shaped inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, often in shades of red or orange. It thrives in humid, warm environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium pirottae is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
