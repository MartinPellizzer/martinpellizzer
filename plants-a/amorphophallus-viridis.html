Amorphophallus viridis

Amorphophallus viridis, commonly known as the green arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its arrowhead-shaped leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. The plant produces a striking, spadix-like inflorescence that can reach several feet in height. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is adapted to humid tropical climates.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for both horticultural and ecological studies.
