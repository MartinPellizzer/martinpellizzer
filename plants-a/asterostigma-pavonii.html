Asterostigma pavonii

Asterostigma pavonii, commonly known as the velvetleaf aster, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads and medicinal properties. The plant features broad, velvety leaves and produces small, clustered yellow flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland areas.

Asterostigma pavonii is valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional uses in herbal medicine.
