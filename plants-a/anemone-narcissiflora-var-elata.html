Anemone narcissiflora var. elata

Anemone narcissiflora var. elata, commonly known as the tall anemone, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the eastern United States.

It features showy, cup-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, with a yellow center. The plant typically grows to a height of 2 to 4 feet, with finely divided, feather-like leaves that are aromatic. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland gardens.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking both ornamental and ecological value.
