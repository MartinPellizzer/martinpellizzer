Aster zeyheri

Aster zeyheri, commonly known as the Zeyher's aster, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its showy purple or blue flowers bloom in spring, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies.

Aster zeyheri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant colors and hardy nature.
