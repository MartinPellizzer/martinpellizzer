Asteromyrtus

Asteromyrtus, commonly known as the silverleaf myrtle, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to Australia and is characterized by its aromatic, often silver-gray leaves. The plants typically have small, white or pinkish flowers that bloom in clusters. Asteromyrtus species are generally shrubs or small trees, often found in coastal or sandy soils.

They are valued for their ornamental appeal and are sometimes used in horticulture for their hardy nature and unique foliage.
