Airampoa orurensis

Airampoa orurensis, commonly known as the Brazilian feather grass, is a perennial grass species native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-gray foliage and delicate, feather-like inflorescences that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for ornamental landscaping. It is often used in garden settings for its ornamental value and ability to form dense, low-growing mats.

Airampoa orurensis is also valued for its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements.
