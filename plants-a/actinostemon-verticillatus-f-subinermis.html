Actinostemon verticillatus f. subinermis

Actinostemon verticillatus f. subinermis, commonly known as the slender actinostemon, is a compact, herbaceous perennial native to southern Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves arranged in a whorl at the base of the plant, giving it a distinctive, rosette-like appearance. The plant produces small, pale green flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences, typically blooming in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its foliage remains attractive throughout the year, offering ornamental value in both garden and horticultural settings.
