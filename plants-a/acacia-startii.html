Acacia startii

Acacia startii, commonly known as the Christmas tree acacia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, feathery leaves and clusters of small, yellowish flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for arid and semi-arid environments. Its unique appearance and seasonal blooming make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Acacia startii is also valued for its drought tolerance and ability to stabilize soil in sandy regions.
