Arum orientale subsp. orientale

Arum orientale subsp.

orientale, commonly known as the Chinese arrowhead, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to eastern Asia. It features arrow-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy, with a prominent midrib.

The plant produces striking, spatulate-shaped flowers that are usually greenish-white with a prominent spadix. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in wetlands or along riverbanks.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage and floral display.
