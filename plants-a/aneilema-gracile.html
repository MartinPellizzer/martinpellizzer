Aneilema gracile

Aneilema gracile, commonly known as the slender aneilema, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical Africa.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and swampy areas. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often arranged alternately along its stem. Its flowers are small and clustered, with a distinctive coloration that ranges from white to pale pink.

Aneilema gracile is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
