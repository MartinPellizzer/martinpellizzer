Athyrium henryi

Athyrium henryi, commonly known as the Henry's lady fern, is a species of fern native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds that are typically bi- to tri-pinnate. The plant prefers moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas. Its rhizomes are relatively short and produce a dense network of roots.

Athyrium henryi is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and ease of cultivation.
