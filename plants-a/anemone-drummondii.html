Anemone drummondii

Anemone drummondii, commonly known as Drummond's anemone, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It typically grows in dry, rocky habitats such as grasslands and open woodlands. The plant features showy, pink to red flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are palmately divided and covered in fine, soft hairs.

It is a perennial herb that produces a basal rosette of foliage and is often found in disturbed soils.
