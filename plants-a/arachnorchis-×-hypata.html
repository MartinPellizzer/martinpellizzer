Arachnorchis × hypata

Arachnorchis × hypata, commonly known as the hybrid spider orchid, is a captivating member of the Orchidaceae family.

This plant is native to southeastern Australia and thrives in rocky and sandy soils. It produces delicate, spider-like flowers with intricate patterns and a subtle fragrance. The hybrid is the result of crossbreeding between Arachnorchis flava and Arachnorchis hypatia, showcasing genetic diversity.

Its unique morphology and limited distribution make it a subject of interest for botanists and orchid enthusiasts alike.
