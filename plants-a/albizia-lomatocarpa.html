Albizia lomatocarpa

Albizia lomatocarpa, commonly known as the silky albizia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 6 meters tall. The plant is characterized by its compound leaves, which are pinnately arranged and have a soft, silky texture. Its small, fragrant flowers are typically pale yellow or white, appearing in clusters.

Albizia lomatocarpa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
