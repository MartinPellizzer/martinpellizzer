Adenocalymma densiflorum

Adenocalymma densiflorum, commonly known as the dense-flowered adenocalymma, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows in dry, rocky habitats, often found in the succulent Karoo region. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are usually pink or purple in color, arranged in dense clusters. Its leaves are narrow and succulent, adapted to conserve water in arid environments.

Adenocalymma densiflorum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with well-drained soil.
