Arthrostemma dicranantherum

Arthrostemma dicranantherum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved arthrostemma, is a herbaceous plant belonging to the family Acanthaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia, including parts of China, India, and Southeast Asia. The species is characterized by its unique floral structure and is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes.
