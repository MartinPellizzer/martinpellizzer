Argyrella incana

Argyrella incana, commonly known as the silver hair plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, daisy-like flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. Argyrella incana thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements.
