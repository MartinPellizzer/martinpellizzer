Ardisia sciophila

Ardisia sciophila, commonly known as the shade-loving ardisia, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It thrives in low-light conditions, making it ideal for shaded garden environments. The plant features glossy, dark green leaves that are typically oval in shape. Its small, white or pale pink flowers are followed by bright red berries that attract birds.

Ardisia sciophila is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in humid, shaded climates.
