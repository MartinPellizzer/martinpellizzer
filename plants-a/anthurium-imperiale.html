Anthurium imperiale

Anthurium imperiale, commonly known as the imperial anthurium, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often a deep green with a velvety texture. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-shaped inflorescences surrounded by a showy, brightly colored spathe, typically in shades of red or orange. It thrives in humid, warm environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium imperiale is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and air-purifying properties.
