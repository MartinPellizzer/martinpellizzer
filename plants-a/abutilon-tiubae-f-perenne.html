Abutilon tiubae f. perenne

Abutilon tiubae f. perenne, commonly known as the evergreen abutilon, is a variant of the abutilon species native to tropical regions.

It is a shrub characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that are typically hairy and have a velvety texture. The plant produces clusters of small, yellow flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. Its bark is fibrous and often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.

This plant thrives in well-drained soil and requires ample sunlight to flourish.
