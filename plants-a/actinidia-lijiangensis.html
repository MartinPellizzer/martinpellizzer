Actinidia lijiangensis

Actinidia lijiangensis, commonly known as the Liangjiang actinidia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Actinidiaceae.

It is native to China, specifically found in the Yunnan and Sichuan provinces. This vine-like plant is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and small, greenish-yellow flowers that appear in early summer. The plant produces small, edible berries that are typically harvested in the fall.

Due to its limited distribution and unique botanical features, it is considered a rare and valuable species in botanical research.
