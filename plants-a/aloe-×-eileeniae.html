Aloe × eileeniae

Aloe × eileeniae, commonly known as the hybrid aloe, is a naturally occurring hybrid between Aloe vera and Aloe ferox.

It is characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a slight reddish tinge. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers that bloom in summer, adding ornamental value. It is native to South Africa and thrives in well-drained soil with plenty of sunlight.

Due to its hybrid nature, it exhibits a combination of traits from both parent species, making it unique in the Aloe genus.
