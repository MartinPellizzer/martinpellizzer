Artemisia iberica

Artemisia iberica, commonly known as Spanish wormwood, is a perennial herb native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, silver-gray foliage. The plant produces small, yellowish flower heads that bloom in late summer. It thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in Mediterranean climates.

Artemisia iberica is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
