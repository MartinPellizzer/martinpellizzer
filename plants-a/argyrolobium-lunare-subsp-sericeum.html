Argyrolobium lunare subsp. sericeum

Argyrolobium lunare subsp.

sericeum, commonly known as the silver globular pea, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its distinctive silver-gray foliage and spherical, globular seed pods.

The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, thriving in well-drained soils.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
