Aspidopterys roxburghiana var. glabriuscula

Aspidopterys roxburghiana var. glabriuscula, commonly known as the smooth-leafed evergreen, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, leathery foliage with a smooth, glabrous texture. The leaves are typically elliptical or ovate, with a glossy dark green surface. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are often clustered in inflorescences.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.
