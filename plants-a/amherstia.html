Amherstia

Amherstia, commonly known as the Chinese bellflower, is a genus of flowering plants in the Campanulaceae family.

It is native to eastern Asia, particularly China, and includes several species such as Amherstia nobilis. The plant is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from blue to purple. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and arranged alternately along the stem.

Amherstia is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and botanical collections.
