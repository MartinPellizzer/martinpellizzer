Artemisia tainingensis

Artemisia tainingensis, commonly known as the Taining wormwood, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, gray-green foliage. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in composite heads, typical of the sunflower family. It thrives in dry, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its medicinal properties, it has been used in traditional Chinese medicine for various health purposes.
