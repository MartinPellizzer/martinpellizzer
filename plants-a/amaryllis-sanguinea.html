Amaryllis sanguinea

Amaryllis sanguinea, commonly known as the red amaryllis, is a striking bulbous plant native to South America.

It produces vibrant red flowers with a deep, velvety texture, often with a subtle fragrance. The plant features strap-like, narrow leaves that are typically a deep green color. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Amaryllis sanguinea is popular for its showy blooms and is often cultivated as a decorative plant.
