Adhatoda aurantiaca

Adhatoda aurantiaca, commonly known as the Indian Trumpet or Red Trumpet Plant, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular, red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It belongs to the family Acanthaceae and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Adhatoda aurantiaca is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
