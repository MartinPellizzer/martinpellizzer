Austroblechnum Banksii

Austroblechnum banksii is a rare and elegant fern native to the temperate rainforests of New Zealand. This plant belongs to the Blechnaceae family and is known for its delicate, lace-like fronds that shimmer in the shade. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found growing on rock faces or in shaded crevices. Due to its limited habitat and threats from habitat loss, it is classified as a vulnerable species. Conservation efforts are crucial to ensure its survival in the wild.
