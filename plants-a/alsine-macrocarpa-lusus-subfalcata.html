Alsine macrocarpa lusus subfalcata

Alsine macrocarpa lusus subfalcata, commonly known as the broadleaf sandmat, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a member of the Amaranthaceae family, characterized by its succulent leaves and showy, pink to purple flowers.
