Acidoton acidothamnus

Acidoton acidothamnus, commonly known as the sour thorn, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, spiny foliage and small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has adapted to arid environments with its succulent leaves, which store water efficiently. Its root system is deep, allowing it to access moisture from lower soil layers.

The plant is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
