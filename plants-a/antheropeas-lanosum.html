Antheropeas lanosum

Antheropeas lanosum, commonly known as the woolly antler plant, is a flowering plant native to parts of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, hairy stems and leaves that are covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Antheropeas lanosum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
