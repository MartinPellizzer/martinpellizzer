Acropogon sageniifolia

Acropogon sageniifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf acropogon, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its slender, lance-shaped leaves and showy, tubular flowers. Acropogon sageniifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
