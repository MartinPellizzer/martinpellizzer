Araucaria hunsteinii var. klinkii

Araucaria hunsteinii var. klinkii, commonly known as the Klinkii Norfolk Island pine, is a variant of the Norfolk Island pine native to Norfolk Island.

It is a coniferous evergreen tree characterized by its tall, slender trunk and feathery, needle-like leaves. The plant exhibits a distinctive branching pattern with horizontal branches that form a conical shape. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Its botanical features make it a unique addition to both natural and cultivated landscapes.
