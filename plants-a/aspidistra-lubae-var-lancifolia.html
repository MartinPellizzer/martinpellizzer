Aspidistra lubae var. lancifolia

Aspidistra lubae var. lancifolia, commonly known as the lance-leaf aspidistra, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and leathery in texture. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments and is well-suited for indoor cultivation. It has a compact growth habit with a rhizomatous root system that allows it to spread slowly over time.

The flowers, though rare, are small and inconspicuous, with spadices that are typically greenish-white in color.
