Arenaria angustisepala

Arenaria angustisepala, commonly known as narrow-lobed sandwort, is a low-growing flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of the Mediterranean.

It typically thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is adapted to withstand drought conditions. The plant features small, clustered flowers that are usually white or pale pink, blooming in early spring. Its leaves are narrow and fleshy, helping to conserve water in harsh environments.

It is often found in rocky crevices or along coastal dunes, showcasing its resilience to extreme climates.
