Aglaonema costatum var. brevispathum

Aglaonema costatum var. brevispathum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf Chinese evergreen, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated foliage with broad, dark green leaves featuring narrow white or cream-colored margins. The plant is known for its compact growth habit and ability to thrive in low-light conditions, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and have a smooth texture, with a glossy appearance that enhances its ornamental value.

This variety is relatively low-maintenance, requiring minimal watering and occasional fertilization to maintain its vibrant appearance.
