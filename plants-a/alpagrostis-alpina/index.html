Alpagrostis Alpina

Alpagrostis alpina is a hardy grass species native to high-altitude regions of the northern hemisphere. It thrives in cold, rocky environments and is commonly found in alpine meadows and mountainous areas. This plant is known for its resilience to harsh weather conditions and poor soil quality. Its slender, tufted growth habit makes it a distinctive feature in its natural habitat. Alpagrostis alpina plays an important ecological role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for small alpine organisms.
