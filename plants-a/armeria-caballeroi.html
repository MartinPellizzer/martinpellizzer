Armeria caballeroi

Armeria caballeroi, commonly known as the Mexican thrift, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of Baja California, Mexico.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces clusters of white or pink flowers in early spring. The plant has succulent, fleshy leaves that help it retain moisture in its arid environment. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its hardy nature and ornamental flowers make it a valued plant in horticulture.
