Aneilema umbrosum var. ovato-oblongum

Aneilema umbrosum var. ovato-oblongum, commonly known as the broad-leaved aneilema, is a tropical plant native to parts of Africa.

It is characterized by its large, ovate-oblong leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers with showy yellow stamens. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, fertile soil.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
