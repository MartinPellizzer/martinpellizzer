Achyranthes nodosa

Achyranthes nodosa, commonly known as the clustered spurge, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces small, greenish-yellow flowers in clusters. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually oval to lance-shaped. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed areas or along roadsides.

Achyranthes nodosa is known for its milky sap, which can be toxic if ingested.
