Alocasia reversa

Alocasia reversa, commonly known as the Black Velvet Plant, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features large, heart-shaped leaves with a deep, velvety black upper surface and a lighter green underside. The plant is characterized by its unique, waxy texture and dramatic foliage that makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening. Alocasia reversa thrives in high humidity and indirect light, requiring regular watering to maintain its lush appearance.

Its ornamental value lies in its bold, contrasting leaf colors and elegant, almost sculptural form.
