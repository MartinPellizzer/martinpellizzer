Adesmia corymbosa var. corymbosa

Adesmia corymbosa var. corymbosa, commonly known as the clustered adesmia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in dry, rocky habitats. The plant features small, clustered inflorescences with white or pale yellow flowers. Its leaves are usually opposite and ovate, with a rough texture.

The species is valued for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its compact growth and delicate blooms.
