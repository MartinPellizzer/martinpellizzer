Astrophytum quadricostatum

Astrophytum quadricostatum, commonly known as the four-spine cactus, is a compact, globular cactus native to the Sonoran Desert.

It features a ribbed, spiny stem with four prominent central spines and smaller radial spines. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun with well-draining soil.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
