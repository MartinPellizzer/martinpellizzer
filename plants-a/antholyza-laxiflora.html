Antholyza laxiflora

Antholyza laxiflora, commonly known as the slender-flowered antholyza, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, yellowish to brownish flowers arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence. It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Antholyza laxiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
