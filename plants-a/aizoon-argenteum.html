Aizoon argenteum

Aizoon argenteum, commonly known as silverleaf nightshade, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, featuring silver-gray foliage due to dense pubescence. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. Its fruit is a small, rounded berry that turns from green to red as it matures.

Aizoon argenteum is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage.
