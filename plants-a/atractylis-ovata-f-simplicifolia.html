Atractylis ovata f. simplicifolia

Atractylis ovata f. simplicifolia, commonly known as the smoothleaf atractylis, is a variant of the common atractylis species.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant native to southern Europe and parts of western Asia. The plant features broad, smooth, lance-shaped leaves that are typically hairy on the underside. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in a panicle inflorescence.

This plant thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.
