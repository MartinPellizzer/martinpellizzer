Arisaema takesimense

Arisaema takesimense, commonly known as the Takeyama arrowhead, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive inflorescence consisting of a spadix surrounded by a modified leaf called a spathe. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered around the spadix, which is typically yellow or orange in color. Arisaema takesimense is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its unique foliage and striking floral structure.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is relatively easy to grow in suitable conditions.
