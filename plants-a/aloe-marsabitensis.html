Aloe marsabitensis

Aloe marsabitensis, commonly known as the Marsabit aloe, is a succulent plant native to Kenya.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a reddish tinge. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow flowers, which bloom during the rainy season. Aloe marsabitensis is adapted to arid environments and stores water in its leaves for survival.

It is valued for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional remedies.
