Aristida thonningii

Aristida thonningii, commonly known as the Thonning's grass, is a perennial grass species native to Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically bluish-green in color. The plant produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences that are often clustered at the tips of the stems. It thrives in dry, open habitats and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.

Aristida thonningii is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
