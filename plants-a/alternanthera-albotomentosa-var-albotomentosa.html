Alternanthera albotomentosa var. albotomentosa

Alternanthera albotomentosa var. albotomentosa, commonly known as white-haired Alternanthera, is a species native to South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive silver-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 30 inches. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a velvety texture.

It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or yellow in color.
