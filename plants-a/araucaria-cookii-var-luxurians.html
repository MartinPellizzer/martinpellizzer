Araucaria cookii var. luxurians

Araucaria cookii var. luxurians, commonly known as the Cook's Araucaria, is a rare and ancient conifer native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and dense, symmetrical crown of needle-like leaves. The plant exhibits a unique morphology with closely spaced, scale-like leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in forested areas with well-drained soil.

Due to its limited distribution and conservation status, it is considered a valuable species in botanical studies and conservation efforts.
