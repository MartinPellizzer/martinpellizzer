Agrostophyllum libanoanum

Agrostophyllum libanoanum, commonly known as the Lebanon grass.

It is a flowering plant belonging to the family Poaceae, which includes grasses. This species is native to the Mediterranean region, particularly Lebanon, where it grows in rocky and arid environments. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, inconspicuous flowers.
