Aristotelia fruticosa

Aristotelia fruticosa, commonly known as the kiwifruit vine, is a climbing shrub native to South America.

It produces small, edible berries that are rich in vitamins and antioxidants. The plant has a woody stem with thorny branches and heart-shaped leaves. It thrives in temperate climates and requires well-drained soil.

The fruit is typically harvested when it turns from green to yellow or brown.
