Aristolochia luzmariana

Aristolochia luzmariana, commonly known as the Brazilian birthwort, is a climbing vine native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and distinctive, intricate flowers that resemble a pipe or a horn. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are often covered in a dense network of veins. Its fruit is a slender, elongated capsule that contains numerous tiny seeds.

This species is valued in traditional medicine for its purported medicinal properties.
