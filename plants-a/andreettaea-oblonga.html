Andreettaea oblonga

Andreettaea oblonga, commonly known as the Brazilian broom.

It is a flowering plant native to Brazil, belonging to the Fabaceae family. The plant is characterized by its slender, branching stems and small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to pink. Its leaves are trifoliate, consisting of three oval-shaped leaflets.
