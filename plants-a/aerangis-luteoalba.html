Aerangis luteoalba

Aerangis luteoalba, commonly known as the Yellow White Orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features slender, upright stems and produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, white pubescence. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
