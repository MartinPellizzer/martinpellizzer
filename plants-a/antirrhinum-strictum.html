Antirrhinum strictum

Antirrhinum strictum, commonly known as the upright snapdragon, is a herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It produces striking, tubular flowers that are typically red, orange, or yellow, which attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. The plant has linear, upright leaves and grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Antirrhinum strictum is often used in floral arrangements due to its vibrant colors and long-lasting blooms.
