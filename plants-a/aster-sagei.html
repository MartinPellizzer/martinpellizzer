Aster sagei

Aster sagei, commonly known as the alpine aster, is a perennial herb native to high-altitude regions of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and robust, hairy stems. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine meadows, thriving in cool, moist environments. Its flowers are usually blue to violet in color, with ray florets that resemble petals.

Aster sagei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional herbal medicine.
