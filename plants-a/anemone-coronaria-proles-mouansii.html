Anemone coronaria proles mouansii

Anemone coronaria proles mouansii, commonly known as the Mouansis Crown Anemone, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its showy, pink to red petals that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are basal, heart-shaped, and covered in a fine layer of white hairs.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a cut flower.
