Abelia serrata f. colorata

Abelia serrata f. colorata, commonly known as the Crimson Abelia, is a compact, deciduous shrub native to China.

It features showy, tubular flowers that range from white to deep pink, often with a red throat, blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has elliptical, serrated leaves that provide a textured appearance throughout the growing season. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and is drought-tolerant once established.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden borders and containers.
