Angraecoides nasuta

Angraecoides nasuta, commonly known as the slender star orchid, is a species of orchid native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its delicate, star-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale pink. The plant grows as an epiphyte, often found on trees in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers emit a sweet fragrance, attracting specific pollinators like moths.

Angraecoides nasuta is valued for its ornamental beauty and is a subject of interest in botanical research due to its unique adaptations.
