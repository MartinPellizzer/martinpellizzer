Arctotis bellidiastrum

Arctotis bellidiastrum, commonly known as the golden crownbeard, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features showy, yellow-orange ray florets surrounding a central disk of tubular flowers, creating a striking appearance. It typically grows in open, sunny habitats and is often found in grasslands and rocky areas.

Arctotis bellidiastrum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
