Argyrocome frenchii

Argyrocome frenchii, commonly known as the French's silverleaf.

It is a species of flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region, characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and compact growth habit. The leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine, white hairs, giving the plant its distinctive appearance. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that bloom in early spring, followed by small, round fruits.

This plant is often cultivated as an ornamental due to its aesthetic appeal and low maintenance requirements.
