Anthurium scherzerianum var. aurorae

Anthurium scherzerianum var. aurorae, commonly known as the Aurora Anthurium, is a tropical flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are often brightly colored, ranging from deep red to vibrant orange. The plant produces clusters of small, spadix-like flowers surrounded by a colorful spathe, which can be red, yellow, or orange. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.

This variety is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in indoor settings.
