Astracantha Crenophila

Astracantha crenophila is a rare and striking plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is known for its unique, spiky appearance. The plant features dense, woolly foliage that helps it retain moisture in harsh environments. Its small, yellow flowers bloom in clusters, adding a subtle yet eye-catching touch to its appearance. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, Astracantha crenophila is considered a vulnerable species.
