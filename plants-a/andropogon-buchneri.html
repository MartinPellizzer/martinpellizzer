Andropogon buchneri

Andropogon buchneri, commonly known as giant kinggrass, is a tall, perennial grass native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its coarse, fibrous leaves and dense, cylindrical inflorescences. This plant is known for its rapid growth rate and high biomass production, making it suitable for forage and bioenergy applications. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in disturbed soils.

Andropogon buchneri has a deep root system that helps prevent soil erosion and improves soil structure.
