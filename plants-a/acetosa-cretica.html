Acetosa cretica

Acetosa cretica, commonly known as Cretan sandwort, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically thrives in sandy or rocky soils. The plant features small, white or pale pink flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in early spring. Its leaves are usually narrow and linear, adapted to conserve water in arid conditions.

Acetosa cretica is often found in coastal areas and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological resilience.
