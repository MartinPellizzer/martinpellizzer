Asthenatherum fragile

Asthenatherum fragile, commonly known as the fragile feathergrass, is a low-growing grass species native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

It is characterized by its fine, slender leaves and delicate, feathery inflorescences that give it a soft, airy appearance.
