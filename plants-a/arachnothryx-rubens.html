Arachnothryx rubens

Arachnothryx rubens, commonly known as the red-stemmed arachnothryx, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the Amazon rainforest.

It belongs to the Rubiaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive red-brown stems and dark green, elliptical leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that are clustered in terminal panicles, followed by small, round berries. It is often found in moist, shaded understory habitats and is valued for its ornamental qualities.

The species is also noted for its potential medicinal uses in traditional herbal practices.
