Anthemis odontostephana var. tubicina

Anthemis odontostephana var. tubicina, commonly known as the tuberculate anthemis, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact, upright growth habit and dense clusters of small, yellowish-white flowers. The leaves are usually hairy and have a distinctive tuberculate (wart-like) texture, giving the plant its specific epithet. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
