Arisaema paichuanense

Arisaema paichuanense, commonly known as the Paichuan arrowhead, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its distinctive arrow-shaped leaves. The plant produces an inflorescence consisting of a spadix surrounded by a spathe, typical of the genus Arisaema. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
