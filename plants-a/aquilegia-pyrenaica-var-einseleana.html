Aquilegia pyrenaica var. einseleana

Aquilegia pyrenaica var. einseleana, commonly known as the Pyrenean columbine, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Pyrenees region.

It features showy, spurred flowers that are typically blue or purple, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. The plant has compound leaves with palmate lobes, and its stems are hairy and often branched. It thrives in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in mountainous areas.

The species is valued for its ornamental beauty and ecological importance in its native habitat.
