Athyrium nanchuanense

Athyrium nanchuanense, commonly known as the Nanchuan fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to China.

It belongs to the family Athyriaceae and is characterized by its delicate, pinnately compound leaves. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as forest understories. Its fronds are often adorned with small, spore-producing structures called sori.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
