Acalypha urophylla

Acalypha urophylla, commonly known as the red-leaf acalypha, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety red leaves that are often variegated with green and yellow tones. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically green or yellow in color. It is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant foliage.

Acalypha urophylla prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.
