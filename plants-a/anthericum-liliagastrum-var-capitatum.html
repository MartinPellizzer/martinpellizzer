Anthericum liliagastrum var. capitatum

Anthericum liliagastrum var. capitatum, commonly known as the clustered lily-of-the-valley, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It features slender, arching stems that bear small, fragrant, white flowers clustered in umbels. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged in a basal rosette. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodlands or along stream banks.

Its botanical characteristics make it a notable species in the Asparagaceae family.
