Agrostis canina var. formosana

Agrostis canina var. formosana, commonly known as the Formosan bentgrass, is a flowering plant belonging to the grass family Poaceae.

It is a perennial species native to Taiwan, where it thrives in moist, well-drained soils. The plant has slender, tufted growth and produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences. Its leaves are narrow and often have a bluish-green hue, and it is adapted to temperate climates.

This variety is of interest in botanical studies due to its unique morphology and ecological significance in its native habitat.
