Alocasia azlanii

Alocasia azlanii, commonly known as the Azlanii Elephant Ear, is a tropical plant native to Malaysia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 inches in length. The plant thrives in humid, warm environments with bright, indirect light. Its foliage is often a deep green with subtle variegation, making it a striking ornamental plant.

Alocasia azlanii requires well-draining soil and consistent moisture to flourish.
