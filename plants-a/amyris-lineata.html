Amyris lineata

Amyris lineata, commonly known as the narrow-leafed amyris, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its slender, linear leaves that are often arranged in whorls around the stems. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. Amyris lineata is often grown for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.

It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
