Acacia etbaica subsp. platycarpa

Acacia etbaica subsp.

platycarpa, commonly known as the Ethiopian wattle, is a shrub native to Ethiopia. It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow floral clusters.

The plant produces flat, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky soils.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional uses in local communities.
