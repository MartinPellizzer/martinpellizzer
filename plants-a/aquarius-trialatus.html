Aquarius trialatus

Aquarius trialatus, commonly known as the blue star, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features striking blue and purple star-shaped flowers that bloom in the summer months. The plant has a compact, low-growing habit with silver-gray foliage. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
