Astrocaryum humile

Astrocaryum humile, commonly known as the small buriti palm, is a species of palm native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, fan-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that develop into hard, woody fruits. The fruits contain edible seeds that are often used in local cuisines.

Astrocaryum humile plays an important ecological role in its habitat, providing food for various wildlife species.
