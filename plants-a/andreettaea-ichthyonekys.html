Andreettaea ichthyonekys

Andreettaea ichthyonekys, commonly known as the Ichthyonekys fern, is a rare and unique plant native to the Atlantic Forest of Brazil.

It is characterized by its delicate, feather-like fronds that are typically less than 10 centimeters in length. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing on rock surfaces or in crevices. Its reproductive structures are small and inconspicuous, with spores dispersed by wind.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered endangered and requires conservation efforts to ensure its survival.
