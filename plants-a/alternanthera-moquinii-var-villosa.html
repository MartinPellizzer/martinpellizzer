Alternanthera moquinii var. villosa

Alternanthera moquinii var. villosa, commonly known as velvet leaf alternanthera, is a striking aquatic plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its velvety, hairy foliage that gives it a unique texture and appearance. The plant typically features lance-shaped leaves that are a deep red or purple in color, especially when grown in low-light conditions. It thrives in freshwater environments and is often used in aquariums for its ornamental value.

This variety is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice among aquatic plant enthusiasts.
