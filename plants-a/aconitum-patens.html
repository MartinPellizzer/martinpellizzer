Aconitum patens

Aconitum patens, commonly known as the Himalayan monkshood, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has palmately divided leaves and produces showy, hood-shaped flowers that attract pollinators. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine or subalpine habitats.

Due to its toxic alkaloids, it should be handled with care.
