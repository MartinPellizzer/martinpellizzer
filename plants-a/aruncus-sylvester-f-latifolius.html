Aruncus sylvester f. latifolius

Aruncus sylvester f. latifolius, commonly known as the broadleaf goat's beard, is a herbaceous perennial native to eastern North America.

It features striking, feathery plumes of white flowers that appear in late spring to early summer. The plant has large, heart-shaped leaves with a rough texture and a dark green color. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Its botanical characteristics make it a distinctive and ornamental addition to gardens and natural landscapes.
