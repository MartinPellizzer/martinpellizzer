Athyrium tagawae

Athyrium tagawae, commonly known as the Tagawa's blade fern, is a species of fern native to Japan.

It belongs to the family Athyriaceae and is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as forests and rocky areas. Its leaves are bioluminescent, emitting a faint glow in the dark, which is a unique feature among ferns.

This rare and striking plant is of interest to botanists and conservationists due to its limited distribution and ecological significance.
