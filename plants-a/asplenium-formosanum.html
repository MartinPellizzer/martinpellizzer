Asplenium formosanum

Asplenium formosanum, commonly known as the Formosan spleenwort, is a species of fern belonging to the family Aspleniaceae.

It is characterized by its small, delicate fronds that are typically less than 10 centimeters in length. The plant has a creeping rhizome and produces spores in clusters on the underside of its leaves. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in rocky crevices or on the bases of trees.

Asplenium formosanum is native to Southeast Asia, including parts of China, Japan, and Taiwan.
