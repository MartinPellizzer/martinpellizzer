Androsace mollis

Androsace mollis, commonly known as the fairy sedge or moss campion, is a low-growing perennial plant belonging to the Primulaceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit and produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or white. Androsace mollis is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in rocky or gravelly soils.
