Acmella nuttaliana

Acmella nuttaliana, commonly known as the triggerplant, is a flowering plant native to South America.

The plant belongs to the family Asteraceae and is known for its vibrant yellow and orange hues. It thrives in moist, sunny environments and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.
