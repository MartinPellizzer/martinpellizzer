Aster pinnatifidus f. robustus

Aster pinnatifidus f. robustus, commonly known as the robust aster, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the southeastern United States.

It features a robust, upright growth habit with multiple stems that can reach heights of up to 2 feet. The plant produces showy, daisy-like flowers with white petals and a yellow center, blooming in late summer to early autumn. Its leaves are lance-shaped, serrated, and arranged alternately along the stems.

The robust aster is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
