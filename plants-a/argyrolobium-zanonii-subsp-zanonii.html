Argyrolobium zanonii subsp. zanonii

Argyrolobium zanonii subsp.

zanonii, commonly known as the Zanon's silver globular vetch, is a perennial legume native to South Africa. It is characterized by its spherical, silver-green seed pods that contain small, dark seeds.

The plant typically grows in well-drained soils and is adapted to Mediterranean-type climates. It produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring, attracting pollinators such as bees.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological benefits, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and restoration projects.
