Anthurium platyrhizum

Anthurium platyrhizum, commonly known as the broad-rooted anthurium, is a tropical flowering plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically deep green with a metallic sheen. The plant produces striking spadices, which are modified leaves that enclose the true flowers, often in shades of red or orange. Anthurium platyrhizum thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

It is a popular ornamental plant due to its vibrant foliage and unique floral structure.
