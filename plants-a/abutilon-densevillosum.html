Abutilon densevillosum

Abutilon densevillosum, commonly known as the woolly abutilon, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its densely hairy, woolly stems and leaves, which give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces showy, lantern-shaped yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Abutilon densevillosum is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique foliage and vibrant blooms.
