Anthurium elegans

Anthurium elegans, commonly known as the velvet leaf anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a velvety texture. The plant produces striking spadices, which are the brightly colored, spadix-like structures that serve as the flower. Anthurium elegans thrives in humid, warm environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and ornamental purposes.
