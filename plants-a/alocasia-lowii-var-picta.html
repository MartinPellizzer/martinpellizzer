Alocasia lowii var. picta

Alocasia lowii var. picta, commonly known as the Painted Alocasia, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features broad, heart-shaped leaves with intricate, variegated patterns of green, cream, and purple. The leaves are often adorned with dark veins that create a striking visual contrast. It thrives in humid, warm environments with bright, indirect light.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value in both indoor and outdoor gardens.
