Aira elegantissima

Aira elegantissima, commonly known as the elegant air plant, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the grass family (Poaceae) and is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit. The plant produces delicate, spike-like inflorescences that bear small, white to pale blue flowers. Its leaves are narrow, grass-like, and often have a slight bluish tint.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.
