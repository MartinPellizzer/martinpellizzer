Aulomyrcia aureolanata

Aulomyrcia aureolanata, commonly known as the golden-leaved myrcia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the rainforests of South America, particularly in Brazil and Peru. The plant is characterized by its glossy, golden-yellow leaves and small, fragrant white flowers. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in the understory of tropical forests.

The plant has been studied for its potential medicinal properties and ecological role in supporting local biodiversity.
