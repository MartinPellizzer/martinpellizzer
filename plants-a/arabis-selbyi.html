Arabis selbyi

Arabis selbyi, commonly known as Selby's rockcress, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other well-known flowering plants. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. It typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and is adapted to high-altitude environments.
