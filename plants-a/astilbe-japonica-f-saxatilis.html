Astilbe japonica f. saxatilis

Astilbe japonica f. saxatilis, commonly known as the rock astilbe, is a compact, herbaceous perennial that thrives in shady, moist environments.

It features delicate, plume-like inflorescences that range in color from white to pink, adding a soft, ethereal beauty to gardens. The plant has finely divided, fern-like foliage that enhances its ornamental appeal throughout the growing season. It is well-suited for rock gardens, woodland borders, and shaded garden beds due to its preference for well-drained, acidic soil.

This cultivar is particularly valued for its resilience and ability to thrive in challenging, moist conditions.
