Aniba sericea

Aniba sericea, commonly known as silky aniba, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurels and cinnamon trees. The plant is characterized by its aromatic leaves, which are often used in traditional medicine. It produces small, fragrant white flowers that are followed by spherical, blue-black berries.

Aniba sericea is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in herbal remedies for various ailments.
