Aloe acutissima var. isaloana

Aloe acutissima var. isaloana, commonly known as the Isalo aloe, is a succulent plant native to Madagascar.

It features narrow, spiny leaves that are typically a bluish-green color, often with a waxy coating. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing orange to red flowers. It is adapted to arid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Its unique morphology makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
