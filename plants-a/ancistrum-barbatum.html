Ancistrum barbatum

Ancistrum barbatum, commonly known as the hairy ancistrum, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its large, showy spathe and spadix inflorescence. The plant has thick, fleshy stems and heart-shaped leaves with a waxy texture. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires consistent moisture.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor and greenhouse cultivation.
