Anthurium veitcheum

Anthurium veitcheum, commonly known as the Veitcheum arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are often deep green with a glossy texture. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-shaped inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, typically in red, orange, or yellow hues. It thrives in humid, warm environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium veitcheum is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and long-lasting blooms.
