Anthurium dendrobates

Anthurium dendrobates, commonly known as the dart frog plant, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped leaves that are often brightly colored, ranging from red to orange. The plant produces striking spadix inflorescences, which are surrounded by a colorful spathe. It thrives in humid, warm environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium dendrobates is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
