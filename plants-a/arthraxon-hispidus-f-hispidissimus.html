Arthraxon hispidus f. hispidissimus

Arthraxon hispidus f. hispidissimus, commonly known as the hairy leaved arthraxon, is a variant of the species Arthraxon hispidus.

It is a perennial grass native to southern Africa, characterized by its dense, tufted growth habit. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves that are densely covered in fine, stiff hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. It typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in open grasslands or along water margins.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its visual appeal.
