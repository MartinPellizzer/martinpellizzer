Acalypha cuspidata var. genuina

Acalypha cuspidata var. genuina, commonly known as the silver leaf plant, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking silver-green foliage with a velvety texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that are clustered in spikes. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade.

Its ornamental value lies in its unique leaf color and texture, making it a popular choice for shaded garden areas.
