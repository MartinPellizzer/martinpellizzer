Alsophila hymenodes

Alsophila hymenodes, commonly known as the silver fern, is a species of tree fern native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feathery fronds that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant has a slow growth rate and can live for several decades, making it a long-lived species. Its fronds are typically light green to silver in color, providing a striking visual contrast.

Alsophila hymenodes plays an important ecological role in its native habitat, contributing to forest structure and providing habitat for various organisms.
