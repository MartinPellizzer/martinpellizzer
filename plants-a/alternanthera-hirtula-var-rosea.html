Alternanthera hirtula var. rosea

Alternanthera hirtula var. rosea, commonly known as the red-leaf Alternanthera, is a striking aquatic plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant pink to red foliage, which intensifies under strong light conditions. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a textured appearance. It thrives in moderate to high light and requires stable water temperatures.

This variety is popular in aquariums for its ornamental value and ease of care.
