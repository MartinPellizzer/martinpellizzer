Amaryllis fulgens

Amaryllis fulgens, commonly known as the red amaryllis, is a striking bulbous plant native to South America.

It produces large, showy flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, often with a distinctive red or yellow center. The plant features strap-like, narrow leaves that are typically broad at the base and taper toward the tip. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardens and containers.

Amaryllis fulgens is known for its ability to bloom reliably when cared for properly, often producing multiple flowers on a single stem.
