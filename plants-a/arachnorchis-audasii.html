Arachnorchis audasii

Arachnorchis audasii, commonly known as the bold orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Orchidaceae and is characterized by its unique, spider-like appearance due to its elongated, hairy stems and specialized flowers. The plant typically grows in heathlands and open forests, thriving in well-drained soils. Its flowers are usually pale green or yellowish with a distinctive, hooded structure.

Arachnorchis audasii is known for its resilience and ability to withstand harsh environmental conditions.
