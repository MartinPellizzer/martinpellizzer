Aechmea flemingii

Aechmea flemingii, commonly known as the red-tipped pineapple plant, is a striking bromeliad native to the rainforests of Brazil.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that form a central cup, which collects water and nutrients. The plant produces a tall, branched inflorescence with vibrant red bracts that contrast beautifully with its green foliage. Aechmea flemingii thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often used as a decorative plant in tropical gardens.

Its unique appearance and ability to store water make it well adapted to its natural habitat.
