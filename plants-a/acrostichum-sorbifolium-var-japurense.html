Acrostichum sorbifolium var. japurense

Acrostichum sorbifolium var. japurense, commonly known as the Japanese water fern, is a variant of the water fern genus Acrostichum.

It is a perennial fern that thrives in wet, marshy environments, often found in subtropical and tropical regions. The plant features large, lance-shaped fronds that can grow up to several feet in length. Its rhizomes are fibrous and help anchor it in waterlogged soils.

This variant is notable for its adaptability to aquatic conditions and its role in wetland ecosystems.
