Arctagrostis tilesii

Arctagrostis tilesii, commonly known as the tile grass, is a perennial grass species native to the northern United States and southern Canada.

It is characterized by its fine, tufted growth habit and narrow, bluish-green leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands, marshes, and along stream banks.

Arctagrostis tilesii plays an important ecological role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various wildlife species.
