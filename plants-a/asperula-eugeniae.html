Asperula eugeniae

Asperula eugeniae, commonly known as the Japanese evergreen azalea, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of showy, bell-shaped flowers. Asperula eugeniae is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in partial shade.
