Aldama retroflexa

Aldama retroflexa, commonly known as the Mexican bellflower, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped, blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its leaves are opposite and often covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.
