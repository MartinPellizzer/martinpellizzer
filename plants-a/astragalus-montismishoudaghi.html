Astragalus montismishoudaghi

Astragalus montismishoudaghi, commonly known as the Montis Mischoudaghi milkvetch, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its erect, branched stems and small, pea-like flowers that typically range in color from white to pale purple.
