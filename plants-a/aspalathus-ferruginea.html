Aspalathus ferruginea

Aspalathus ferruginea, commonly known as the red bush willow, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, woody stems and small, yellowish-green leaves. The plant produces small, pale green flowers that are clustered in loose inflorescences. It is adapted to grow in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in coastal regions.

Aspalathus ferruginea is also known for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
