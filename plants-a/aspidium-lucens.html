Aspidium lucens

Aspidium lucens, commonly known as the shining water fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped fronds that are often covered in a fine layer of silvery hairs, giving it a luminous appearance. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or along streams. Its rhizomes are wiry and help anchor it to the substrate while absorbing nutrients.

Aspidium lucens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
