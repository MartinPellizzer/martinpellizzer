Anthurium trinervium

Anthurium trinervium, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a reddish or maroon tint. The plant produces bright red spadices, which are the modified leaves that surround the tiny, inconspicuous flowers. Anthurium trinervium thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.

It is popular as a decorative plant due to its vibrant foliage and long-lasting blooms.
