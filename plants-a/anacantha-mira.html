Anacantha mira

Anacantha mira, commonly known as the beautiful spurge, is a flowering plant belonging to the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking, spiny leaves and vibrant yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. Native to regions of Africa and the Middle East, it thrives in arid and semi-arid environments. The plant produces a milky sap that is toxic and can cause skin irritation.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and resilience.
