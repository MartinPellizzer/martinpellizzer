Angraecoides erecta

Angraecoides erecta, commonly known as the erect angraecoid, is a species of orchid native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its erect growth habit and elongated, narrow leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale green, with a distinctive spathaceous structure. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Angraecoides erecta is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts due to its ornamental value and unique appearance.
