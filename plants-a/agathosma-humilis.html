Agathosma humilis

Agathosma humilis, commonly known as the Cape garlic.

It is a small, shrub-like plant native to the Western Cape region of South Africa. The plant features aromatic, narrow leaves and small, yellowish-white flowers. It is often found growing in rocky, well-drained soils.

Agathosma humilis is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
