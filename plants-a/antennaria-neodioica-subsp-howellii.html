Antennaria neodioica subsp. howellii

Antennaria neodioica subsp.

howellii, commonly known as Howell's mouse-ear, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the western United States. It belongs to the family Compositae (Asteraceae) and is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit.
