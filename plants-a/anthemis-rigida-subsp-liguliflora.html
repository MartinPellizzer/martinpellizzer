Anthemis rigida subsp. liguliflora

Anthemis rigida subsp.

liguliflora, commonly known as rigid fennel, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of western Asia. It is characterized by its aromatic, finely divided leaves and yellow composite flowers that bloom in late summer.

The plant typically grows in dry, sunny habitats such as rocky soils and open fields. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is known for its medicinal properties.

The subspecies liguliflora is distinguished by its more rigid stem and narrower leaves compared to other subspecies of Anthemis rigida.
