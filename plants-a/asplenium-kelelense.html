Asplenium kelelense

Asplenium kelelense, commonly known as the Kellelense spleenwort, is a species of fern belonging to the family Aspleniaceae.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant has delicate, lance-shaped leaves that are usually dark green and slightly curled. It produces spores in tiny, clustered structures called sorus, which are located on the underside of the fronds.

Asplenium kelelense is native to tropical regions of Africa and is often found in montane forests.
