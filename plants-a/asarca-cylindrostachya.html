Asarca cylindrostachya

Asarca cylindrostachya, commonly known as the narrow-stemmed asarum, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the eastern United States.

It typically grows in moist, shaded forest floors and has heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive veining pattern.
