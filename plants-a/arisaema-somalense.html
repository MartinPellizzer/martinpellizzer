Arisaema somalense

Arisaema somalense, commonly known as the Somali arrowhead, is a flowering plant native to Somalia.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its unique inflorescence consisting of a spadix surrounded by a spathe. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in rocky or mountainous regions. Its leaves are arrowhead-shaped, with a smooth texture and a deep green color.

The flowers are usually green or white, and the plant produces berries that contain toxic seeds.
