Anteriorchis Coriophora Subsp. Fragrans

Fragrant anteriorchis, scientifically known as Anteriorchis coriophora subsp. fragrans, is a rare and enchanting plant native to the misty highlands of Southeast Asia. This subshrub is distinguished by its delicate, aromatic foliage and small, clustered flowers that emit a subtle, sweet fragrance. Despite its beauty, it remains under-studied due to its limited distribution and elusive habitat. Its unique characteristics make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists alike. Protecting this species is crucial for preserving biodiversity in its fragile ecosystem.
