Aristea simplex

Aristea simplex, commonly known as the common arrowhead, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in wet or marshy environments. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves that are often arranged in a rosette pattern. Its small, white or pale blue flowers are clustered in dense inflorescences, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Aristea simplex is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often used in water gardens or bog gardens.
