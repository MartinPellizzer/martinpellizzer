Aspidium sumatranum

Aspidium sumatranum, commonly known as the Sumatran shield fern, is a species of polypodioid fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit and pinnate fronds. The fronds are typically leathery and have a dark green color with a glossy surface. It thrives in humid, shaded environments such as tropical rainforests.

The plant is often found growing on rocks or tree trunks, where it forms dense colonies.
