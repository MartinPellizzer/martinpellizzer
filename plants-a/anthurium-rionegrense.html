Anthurium rionegrense

Anthurium rionegrense, commonly known as the Rionegro anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Colombia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves and vibrant spadix. The plant produces bright red or orange spadices that are often surrounded by a white or green spathe. It thrives in humid, warm environments with bright, indirect light.

Anthurium rionegrense is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
