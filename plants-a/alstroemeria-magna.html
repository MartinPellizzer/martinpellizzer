Alstroemeria magna

Alstroemeria magna, commonly known as the giant alstroemeria, is a striking perennial plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Alstroemeriaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, orange, and yellow. The plant features strap-like, lance-shaped leaves that are often dark green and glossy. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

Alstroemeria magna is also noted for its long blooming period, typically from late spring to early autumn.
