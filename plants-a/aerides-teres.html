Aerides teres

Aerides teres, commonly known as the compact orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant has strap-like leaves that are dark green and velvety to the touch. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Aerides teres is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
