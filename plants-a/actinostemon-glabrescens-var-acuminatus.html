Actinostemon glabrescens var. acuminatus

Actinostemon glabrescens var. acuminatus, commonly known as the narrow-leaved actinostemon, is a species native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in grassland habitats. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silky texture. Its flowers are small and clustered in dense inflorescences, usually with a yellowish-green color.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
