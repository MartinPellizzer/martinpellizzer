Aglaonema chermsiriwattanae

Aglaonema chermsiriwattanae, commonly known as the Thai evergreen or Thai aglaonema, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves with striking white and silver veins, which give it a distinctive ornamental appearance. The plant is evergreen, meaning it retains its foliage year-round, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening. It thrives in low to moderate light conditions and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Aglaonema chermsiriwattanae is also known for its air-purifying properties, contributing to improved indoor air quality.
