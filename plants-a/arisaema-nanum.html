Arisaema nanum

Arisaema nanum, commonly known as the dwarf arrowroot, is a member of the Araceae family.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia. The plant features a short rhizome with a cluster of narrow, elongated leaves. Its striking inflorescence consists of a spadix surrounded by a modified leaf called a spathe.

Arisaema nanum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in shaded gardens.
