Amaryllis slateriana

Amaryllis slateriana, commonly known as the Slater's amaryllis, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It produces vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, often with a contrasting dark throat. The plant features strap-like, grassy leaves that emerge from a basal rosette of fleshy, white, and often hairy leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and container planting.

Amaryllis slateriana is known for its showy blooms and relatively easy care, making it a favorite among horticultural enthusiasts.
