Arctotheca nivea

Arctotheca nivea, commonly known as the snowflake flower, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its white, daisy-like flowers. The plant produces small, white ray florets surrounded by yellow disk florets, which are typical of the Asteraceae family. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Arctotheca nivea is also known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional medicine.
