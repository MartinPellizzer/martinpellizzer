Anemone villosissima

Anemone villosissima, commonly known as the hairy anemone, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the eastern United States.

It features showy, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has hairy, lobed leaves that are often covered in fine, soft pubescence. It typically grows in moist, woodland habitats and prefers partial to full shade.

Its botanical characteristics make it a distinctive and valuable addition to native plant gardens.
