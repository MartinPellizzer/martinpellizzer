Acaena splendens var. gracilis

Acaena splendens var. gracilis, commonly known as the slender mat grass, is a low-growing perennial plant native to New Zealand.

It features dense, mat-forming growth with slender, cylindrical stems that often lie close to the ground. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in tight inflorescences, typically blooming in spring. Its leaves are narrow, grass-like, and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

This variety is well adapted to rocky and coastal environments, thriving in well-drained soils.
