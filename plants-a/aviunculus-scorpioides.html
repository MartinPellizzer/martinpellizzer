Aviunculus scorpioides

Aviunculus scorpioides, commonly known as the scorpion weed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its small, yellowish flowers that are typically arranged in a dense, clustered inflorescence. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are usually arranged in a rosette pattern. It is native to arid and semi-arid regions of southern Africa.

Aviunculus scorpioides is often found growing in disturbed soils and can thrive in harsh environmental conditions.
