Asplenium alternans

Asplenium alternans, commonly known as the zigzag fern, is a species of fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its distinctive, zigzagging fronds that give it its common name. The plant has a compact growth habit and is often found in shaded, moist environments such as forests or rocky crevices. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and have a finely divided, delicate appearance.

Asplenium alternans is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in low-light conditions.
