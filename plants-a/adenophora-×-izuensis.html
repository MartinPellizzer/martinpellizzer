Adenophora × izuensis

Adenophora × izuensis, commonly known as the Izu bellflower, is a hybrid species native to Japan.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped, blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. It has lance-shaped leaves and produces showy, clustered inflorescences.
