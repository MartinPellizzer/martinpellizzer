Albizia barinensis

Albizia barinensis, commonly known as the Philippine albizia, is a deciduous leguminous tree native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its bipinnate leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The tree produces flat, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It is often found in lowland forests and is valued for its timber and ornamental qualities.

Albizia barinensis also has traditional uses in local medicine and is sometimes cultivated for its ecological benefits.
