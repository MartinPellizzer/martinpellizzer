Anisotome flexuosa

Anisotome flexuosa, commonly known as the zigzag grass, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive zigzag or undulating leaf blades, which give it its common name. The plant typically grows in grasslands and open woodland areas, preferring well-drained soils. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, often during the summer months.

Anisotome flexuosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage structure.
