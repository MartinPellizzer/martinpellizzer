Anemone hortensis subsp. hortensis

Anemone hortensis subsp.

hortensis, commonly known as the garden anemone, is a herbaceous perennial native to Europe. It features showy, cup-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, and lavender, which bloom in early spring.

The plant has finely divided, fern-like leaves that are alternate and often hairy. It thrives in cool, moist environments and prefers partial to full shade.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in temperate climates.
