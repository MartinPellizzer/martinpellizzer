Arundina kanehirae

Arundina kanehirae, commonly known as the Kanehira's bamboo, is a species of grass native to Japan.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and narrow, sword-shaped leaves. Arundina kanehirae is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in native habitats.
