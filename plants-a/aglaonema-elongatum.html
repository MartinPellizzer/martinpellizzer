Aglaonema elongatum

Aglaonema elongatum, commonly known as the Chinese Evergreen, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated foliage with shades of green, white, and pink. The plant has thick, leathery leaves that are elongated and pointed, giving it a distinctive appearance. It is a member of the Araceae family, which includes plants like philodendrons and peace lilies.

Aglaonema elongatum is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low light requirements and ability to thrive in humid environments.
