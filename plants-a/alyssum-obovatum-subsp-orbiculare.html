Alyssum obovatum subsp. orbiculare

Alyssum obovatum subsp.

orbiculare, commonly known as the round-leaf alyssum, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region. It features small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pale pink, typically blooming in spring.

The plant has rounded, succulent leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, contributing to its drought tolerance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and extended flowering period.
