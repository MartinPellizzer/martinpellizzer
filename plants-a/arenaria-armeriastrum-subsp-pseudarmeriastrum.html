Arenaria armeriastrum subsp. pseudarmeriastrum

Arenaria armeriastrum subsp.

pseudarmeriastrum, commonly known as the coastal sand-verbena, is a flowering plant native to the western coast of North America. It is a low-growing, mat-forming perennial that thrives in sandy or gravelly soils.
