Aciotis fragilis var. lancifolia

Aciotis fragilis var. lancifolia, commonly known as the slender aciotis, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically features slender, lance-shaped leaves and produces small yellow flowers arranged in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats.
