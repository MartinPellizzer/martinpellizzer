Aspidistra lutea var. luteorubra

Aspidistra lutea var. luteorubra, commonly known as the red-leaved aspidistra, is a variant of the aspidistra genus native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, dark green to reddish-brown foliage, which adds a dramatic visual element to indoor spaces. The plant typically features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and often have a slight sheen. It thrives in low-light conditions and requires moderate humidity, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique coloration and ease of care contribute to its appeal among plant enthusiasts.
