Acmispon americanus var. helleri

Acmispon americanus var. helleri, commonly known as Heller's burr medic, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

Its leaves are trifoliate, consisting of three oval-shaped leaflets, and it is adapted to thrive in arid and semi-arid environments.
