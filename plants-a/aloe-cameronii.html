Aloe cameronii

Aloe cameronii, commonly known as Cameron's aloe, is a succulent plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern, which helps it store water efficiently. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with small, tubular flowers that are often yellow or orange in color. Its leaves contain a gel-like substance that is used in traditional medicine and skincare products.

Aloe cameronii is adapted to thrive in hot, dry environments with minimal rainfall.
