Aerides odorata f. immaculata

Aerides odorata f. immaculata, commonly known as the immaculate orchid, is a hybrid orchid species prized for its elegant appearance.

It is characterized by its long, arching inflorescences that bear clusters of fragrant, white to pale pink flowers. The plant is native to Southeast Asia and thrives in warm, humid environments with good air circulation. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining potting media to prevent root rot.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
