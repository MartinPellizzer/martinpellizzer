Anthotium humile var. junciforme

Anthotium humile var. junciforme, commonly known as the narrow-leaved anthotium, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive inflorescence composed of a spadix and a spathe. The plant typically grows in wet, shaded environments such as marshes and swamps. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, while its flowers are small and clustered.
