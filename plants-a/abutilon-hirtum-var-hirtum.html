Abutilon hirtum var. hirtum

Abutilon hirtum var. hirtum, commonly known as velvetleaf, is a perennial shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are densely covered in soft, velvety hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces showy, lantern-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, typically blooming in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Its fibrous roots and hairy foliage make it adaptable to various environmental conditions.
