Agropyron rothii

Agropyron rothii, commonly known as the slender wheatgrass, is a perennial grass native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. This plant typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Agropyron rothii is often found in alpine meadows and has a fibrous root system that helps stabilize soil.

Its seeds are small and wind-dispersed, contributing to its natural spread across its habitat.
