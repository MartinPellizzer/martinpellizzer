Arrabidaea pruinosa

Arrabidaea pruinosa, commonly known as the purple sand verbena, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, branching stems and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to violet. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in coastal or arid regions.
