Avenella stricta subsp. mairei

Avenella stricta subsp.

mairei, commonly known as Maire's avenella, is a perennial shrub native to New Zealand. It typically grows in coastal and subalpine regions, thriving in well-drained soils.

The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small, greenish-white, and clustered in loose inflorescences.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in native habitats.
