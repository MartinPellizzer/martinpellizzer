Adnula petiolaris

Adnula petiolaris, commonly known as the slender leaf fern, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, elongated fronds that arise from a creeping rhizome. The leaves are typically narrow and have a soft, feathery texture, with a light green coloration. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires consistent moisture to flourish.

This plant is often cultivated as an ornamental due to its elegant appearance and air-purifying qualities.
