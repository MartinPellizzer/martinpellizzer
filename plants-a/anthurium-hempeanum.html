Anthurium hempeanum

Anthurium hempeanum, commonly known as the Emerald Queen, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a waxy texture. The plant produces vibrant spadices, which are the colorful, spathelike structures that enclose the true flowers. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Anthurium hempeanum is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its aesthetic appeal and air-purifying qualities.
