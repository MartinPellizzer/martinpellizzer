Anemone crinita var. alpina

Anemone crinita var. alpina, commonly known as the Alpine crown anemone, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the alpine regions of Europe.

It features showy, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has finely divided, fern-like leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine, white hairs. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is adapted to cold, high-altitude environments.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens with suitable conditions.
