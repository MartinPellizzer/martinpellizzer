Alternanthera brasiliana f. angustifolia

Alternanthera brasiliana f. angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf alternanthera, is a striking aquatic or semi-aquatic plant native to South America.

It features slender, elongated leaves that are typically a vibrant red or pink, especially when exposed to adequate light. The plant thrives in shallow water and is often used in aquariums for its ornamental value. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or yellow.

Its rapid growth rate makes it a popular choice for water gardeners seeking a low-maintenance, visually appealing plant.
