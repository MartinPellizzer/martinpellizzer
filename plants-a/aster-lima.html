Aster lima

Aster lima, commonly known as the lima bean vine, is a flowering plant belonging to the nightshade family.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and clusters of small, blue or purple flowers. The plant produces elongated, green pods that contain edible seeds, which are widely used in culinary applications. Native to South America, Aster lima thrives in warm climates and is often cultivated for both its ornamental value and nutritional benefits.

Its botanical features make it a versatile plant in both gardens and agricultural settings.
