Apios delavayi

Apios delavayi, commonly known as the Chinese groundnut or Delavay's groundnut, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its climbing habit and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers that are followed by pod-like fruits containing edible, tuberous roots. These roots are used in traditional Chinese cuisine and are valued for their nutty flavor and nutritional content.

Apios delavayi is also studied for its potential medicinal properties and ecological role in soil enrichment.
