Amaryllis granatiflora

Amaryllis granatiflora, commonly known as the sweet-scented amaryllis, is a bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It produces showy, bell-shaped flowers that are typically pink or red with a subtle, sweet fragrance. The plant features strap-like leaves that are broad and grass-like in appearance. It blooms in late spring or early summer, often producing multiple inflorescences.

Amaryllis granatiflora is relatively easy to cultivate and is popular in gardens for its ornamental value.
