Aulacospermum popovii

Aulacospermum popovii, commonly known as the Mongolian milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in arid and semi-arid environments. The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in dense spikes, and its leaves are compound with small, oval-shaped leaflets. It has a deep root system, allowing it to survive in poor soil conditions.
