Aerides cylindrica

Aerides cylindrica, commonly known as the narrow-leafed orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, greenish-white flowers with a faintly fragrant scent. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and high humidity.

Aerides cylindrica is often cultivated for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
