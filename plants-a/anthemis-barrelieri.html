Anthemis barrelieri

Anthemis barrelieri, commonly known as the Barrelier's fleabane, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant produces yellow flowers with a central disc, typical of the genus Anthemis. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky or sandy soils.

The species is noted for its aromatic properties and has been used in traditional medicine.
