Aloe cataractarum

Aloe cataractarum, commonly known as the mountain aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its upright, branching stems and narrow, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces striking, tubular orange or red flowers that bloom in summer. Aloe cataractarum is adapted to arid environments and requires well-draining soil and minimal water.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional medicinal uses.
