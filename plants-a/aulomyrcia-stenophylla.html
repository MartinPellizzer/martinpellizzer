Aulomyrcia stenophylla

Aulomyrcia stenophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leafed myrcia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and small, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in clusters. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The fruit is a small, fleshy berry that contains several seeds.
