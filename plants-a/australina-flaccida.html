Australina flaccida

Australina flaccida, commonly known as the slender lomatia, is a shrub native to southern Australia.

It is characterized by its narrow, willow-like leaves and clusters of small, white flowers. The plant is adapted to dry, sandy soils and is often found in coastal regions. It produces woody fruits that contain seeds dispersed by animals.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological significance, it is a notable species in its native habitat.
