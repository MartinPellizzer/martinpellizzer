Artemisia caerulescens subsp. cretacea

Artemisia caerulescens subsp.

cretacea, commonly known as the Cretan blue artemisia, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region. It features aromatic, silver-gray foliage with narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs.

The plant produces small, yellowish-green flower clusters during the summer months. It is well adapted to dry, rocky soils and full sun exposure.

Its aromatic properties make it a valued plant in traditional herbal medicine and horticulture.
