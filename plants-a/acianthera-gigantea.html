Acianthera gigantea

Acianthera gigantea, commonly known as the giant acianthera, is a striking orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can reach up to 10 centimeters in diameter. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments within the tropical rainforests. Its leaves are broad and dark green, providing a lush backdrop to its vibrant blooms.

Acianthera gigantea is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by enthusiasts of tropical flora.
