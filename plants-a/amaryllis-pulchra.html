Amaryllis pulchra

Amaryllis pulchra, commonly known as the beautiful amaryllis, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that come in a range of colors including pink, red, and white. The plant produces strap-like, grassy leaves that are evergreen and can grow up to several feet in length.

Amaryllis pulchra typically blooms in late spring or early summer, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking vibrant, seasonal color.
