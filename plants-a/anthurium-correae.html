Anthurium correae

Anthurium correae, commonly known as the Red-veined Anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Colombia.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves with deep red veins against a dark green background. The plant produces small, spadix-shaped flowers surrounded by a bright red spathe, which is typical of the Araceae family. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Anthurium correae is popular as a decorative plant due to its ornamental foliage and ease of care.
