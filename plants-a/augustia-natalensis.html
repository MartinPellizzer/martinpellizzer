Augustia natalensis

Augustia natalensis, commonly known as Natal grass, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in grasslands and along riverbanks, preferring well-drained soils. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping.

Augustia natalensis is also known for its resilience to drought and grazing.
