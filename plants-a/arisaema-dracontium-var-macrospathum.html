Arisaema dracontium var. macrospathum

Arisaema dracontium var. macrospathum, commonly known as the giant Chinese arrowhead, is a herbaceous perennial native to China.

It features a distinctive, arrow-shaped leaf that can grow up to 1 meter in length. The plant produces a spadix surrounded by a spathe, which is typically green with a yellow or orange hue. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in forest understories.

The species is valued for its ornamental appearance and has been cultivated for its unique foliage.
