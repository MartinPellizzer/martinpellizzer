Armeria maritima f. losae

Armeria maritima f. losae, commonly known as the Los Angeles form of sea thrift, is a low-growing, mat-forming plant native to coastal regions of California.

It features dense clusters of pink to white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are typically covered in a fine layer of white hairs, providing a silvery appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it well-suited for coastal and rocky garden environments.

This form is notable for its ornamental value and adaptability to harsh, maritime climates.
