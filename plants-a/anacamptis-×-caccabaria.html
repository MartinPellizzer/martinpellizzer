Anacamptis × caccabaria

Anacamptis × caccabaria, commonly known as the hybrid lady's tresses, is a perennial orchid native to southern Europe.

It is a hybrid between Anacamptis morio and Anacamptis pyramidalis, resulting in a plant with distinctive, fragrant, and spirally arranged flowers. The inflorescence typically consists of 10–20 violet or purple flowers that bloom in late summer. It prefers calcareous soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and woodland edges.

This species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
