Aspilia diffusiflora

Aspilia diffusiflora, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, daisy-like flowers with white petals and a yellow center, which bloom in clusters. The plant has hairy, gray-green foliage and is often found in grasslands and open habitats. It is a perennial herb that produces small, dry fruits containing numerous seeds.

Aspilia diffusiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
