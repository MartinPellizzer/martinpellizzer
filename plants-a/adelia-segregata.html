Adelia segregata

Adelia segregata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved adelia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
