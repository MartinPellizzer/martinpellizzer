Aucuba yunnanensis var. pubigera

Aucuba yunnanensis var. pubigera, commonly known as the Yunnan aucuba, is a rare evergreen shrub native to China.

It features glossy, dark green leaves with a velvety texture and prominent veins. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in umbels, followed by red, fleshy berries. It thrives in shady, moist environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Its ornamental value makes it a sought-after plant in horticulture, though it is not widely cultivated outside its native habitat.
