Anechites circinalis

Anechites circinalis, commonly known as the common sandmat, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is a low-growing herb with a mat-forming habit, typically found in sandy or gravelly soils. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are ovate to elliptic in shape and have a rough texture.

Anechites circinalis is adapted to arid environments and is often used in restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil.
