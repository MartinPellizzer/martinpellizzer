Anneslia Speciosa

Showy anneslia, scientifically known as Anneslia speciosa, is a striking member of the Campanulaceae family native to the mountainous regions of South America. This perennial herb is celebrated for its vibrant, showy flowers that bloom in late summer and early autumn. Its unique bell-shaped blossoms, often in shades of pink and white, attract a variety of pollinators including bees and butterflies. Despite its beauty, Showy anneslia is relatively rare and faces threats from habitat loss and over-collection. Conservation efforts are essential to preserve this enchanting plant for future generations.
