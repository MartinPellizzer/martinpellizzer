Aloe decaryi

Aloe decaryi, commonly known as the Decary aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a reddish tinge, arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow flowers, which bloom in late summer. It is adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water, making it drought-tolerant.

Aloe decaryi is valued for its ornamental beauty and has some traditional uses in herbal medicine.
