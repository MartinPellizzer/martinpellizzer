Acalypha psilostachya var. psilostachya

Acalypha psilostachya var. psilostachya, commonly known as the silverleaf acalypha, is a shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive silvery-white undersides on its leaves, which give it its common name. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in spike-like inflorescences. Its leaves are usually ovate to elliptic in shape and have a leathery texture.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular ornamental plant in gardens.
