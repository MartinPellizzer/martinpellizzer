Aloe albocincta

Aloe albocincta, commonly known as the white-banded aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features striking, narrow leaves that are typically green with white or pale yellow bands. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing yellow to orange flowers. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil.

Aloe albocincta is often cultivated for its ornamental value and resilience in arid environments.
