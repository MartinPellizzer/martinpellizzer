Aichryson dichotomum var. laxum

Aichryson dichotomum var. laxum, commonly known as the silver leafed jade plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically silver or gray-green in color, arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or white during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Its slow growth rate and ornamental appeal make it a popular choice for rock gardens and container cultivation.
