Acetosella haenkeana

Acetosella haenkeana, commonly known as the purple sorrel, is a small herbaceous plant belonging to the Primulaceae family.

It is characterized by its rosette of oval, dark green leaves with a reddish or purplish tinge on the underside. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pinkish flowers during the spring season. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Acetosella haenkeana is noted for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
