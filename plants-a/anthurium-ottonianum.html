Anthurium ottonianum

Anthurium ottonianum, commonly known as the Otto's arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves and vibrant spadix flowers. The plant thrives in humid, warm environments and requires indirect sunlight for optimal growth. Its foliage is often a deep green with a glossy texture, and it produces colorful spadices that range from red to yellow.

Anthurium ottonianum is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
