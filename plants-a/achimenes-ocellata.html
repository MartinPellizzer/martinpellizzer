Achimenes ocellata

Achimenes ocellata, commonly known as the spotted bellflower, is a tropical perennial herb native to Central and South America.

It features striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically pink or purple with a distinctive central spot. The plant has heart-shaped, glossy leaves and produces showy blooms throughout the growing season. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated as a decorative plant.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
