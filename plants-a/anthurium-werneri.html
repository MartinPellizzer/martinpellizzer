Anthurium werneri

Anthurium werneri, commonly known as the Werneri arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a glossy texture. The plant produces striking spadices, which are modified leaves that house the actual flowers, often in shades of red or orange. Anthurium werneri thrives in humid, warm environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical landscapes.
