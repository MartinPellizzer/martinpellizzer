Anneslia speciosa

Anneslia speciosa, commonly known as the showy anneslia, is a striking perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range from pink to red, with distinctive, spurred petals. The plant typically grows in rocky or well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its botanical features include narrow, lance-shaped leaves and a compact, bushy growth habit.

Anneslia speciosa is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and botanical collections.
