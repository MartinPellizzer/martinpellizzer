Aponogeton crinifolius

Aponogeton crinifolius, commonly known as the featherleaf arrowhead, is a perennial aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features elongated, ribbon-like leaves that are often submerged, giving it a delicate, flowing appearance. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom above the water surface, adding ornamental value to ponds and water gardens. It thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments with adequate sunlight.

Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense underwater vegetation.
