Anthurium gracilistipum

Anthurium gracilistipum, commonly known as the slender-leaf anthurium, is a tropical flowering plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its long, narrow, and often glossy green leaves that can grow up to several feet in length. The plant produces striking spadix flowers surrounded by a colorful spathe, typically in shades of red, orange, or yellow. It thrives in humid, warm environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium gracilistipum is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
