Alibrexia brevifolia

Alibrexia brevifolia, commonly known as the short-leaved alibrexia, is a unique plant species characterized by its compact growth habit and distinctive foliage.

The plant produces clusters of delicate, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from pale pink to white, adding to its ornamental value.
