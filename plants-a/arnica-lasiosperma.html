Arnica lasiosperma

Arnica lasiosperma, commonly known as the roughleaf arnica, is a perennial herb native to the western United States.

It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. The plant features erect stems that are covered in stiff, bristly hairs, giving it a rugged appearance. Its yellow flowers are clustered in dense, spherical heads, and the leaves are lance-shaped with a rough texture.

Arnica lasiosperma is valued for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional herbal remedies.
