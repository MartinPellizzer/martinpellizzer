Anthurium andraeanum album

Anthurium andraeanum album, commonly known as the white anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Colombia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its striking white spathe and spadix. The plant produces large, heart-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green. Its flowers are not showy but are important for its ornamental value.

It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.
