Artanthe patens

Artanthe patens, commonly known as the silverleaf artanthe, is a perennial herb native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces small, pink to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.

Artanthe patens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in native plant gardens.
