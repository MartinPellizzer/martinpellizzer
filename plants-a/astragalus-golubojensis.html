Astragalus golubojensis

Astragalus golubojensis, commonly known as the Golubojenskaya milkvetch, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, particularly in Kazakhstan and southern Russia. The plant is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, yellowish flowers that bloom in early summer. Its leaves are pinnately compound, and it produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds.

Astragalus golubojensis is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, often growing in rocky or sandy soils.
