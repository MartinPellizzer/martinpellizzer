Acinodendron biglomeratum

Acinodendron biglomeratum, commonly known as the clustered acacia, is a shrub or small tree native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, clustered inflorescences and small, yellowish flowers that attract various pollinators. The plant has pinnately compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in savanna and woodland habitats.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes used in ornamental gardening.
