Armeriastrum oliganthum

Armeriastrum oliganthum, commonly known as the narrowleaf sea oats, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of California and Baja California.

It is a member of the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and small, clustered white flowers. The plant thrives in sandy, well-drained soils and is often found in dune environments. It plays an important ecological role by stabilizing coastal dunes and providing habitat for various insects and small animals.

Armeriastrum oliganthum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
