Aspidistra elatior

Aspidistra elatior, commonly known as the cast iron plant, is a hardy evergreen perennial native to eastern Asia.

It features dark green, broad, strap-like leaves that are glossy and upright, giving it a lush, architectural appearance. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and requires minimal watering, making it ideal for indoor environments. It produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that emerge from the base of the leaves, though they are not showy.

Aspidistra elatior is valued for its durability and aesthetic appeal in both indoor and outdoor settings.
