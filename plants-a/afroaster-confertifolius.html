Afroaster confertifolius

Afroaster confertifolius, commonly known as the dense-leaved aster, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are densely arranged along the stem, giving it its common name. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, with a central disc surrounded by ray florets.

Afroaster confertifolius is often found in grasslands and rocky areas, thriving in well-drained soils.
