Aulomyrcia curatellifolia var. grandifolia

Aulomyrcia curatellifolia var. grandifolia, commonly known as the large-leaved aulomyrcia, is a tropical plant native to the Caribbean.

It is characterized by its large, leathery leaves that are often a deep green in color. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. It thrives in humid, warm climates and requires well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
