Acacia undulata var. elegans

Acacia undulata var. elegans, commonly known as the elegant wattle, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It features slender, zigzagging branches and produces small, yellow, fluffy flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has bipinnate leaves composed of numerous small leaflets. It is adapted to arid environments and thrives in well-drained soils.

The elegant wattle is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive appearance and hardy nature.
