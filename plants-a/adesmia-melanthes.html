Adesmia melanthes

Adesmia melanthes, commonly known as the blackstem burr.

It is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly found in Argentina and Chile. The plant is characterized by its woody stems and small, clustered flowers that bloom in spring. Its leaves are typically narrow and slightly hairy, and it produces burrs that aid in seed dispersal.

Adesmia melanthes is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and hardy nature.
