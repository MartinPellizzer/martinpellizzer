Aerides fieldingii var. williamsii

Aerides fieldingii var. williamsii, commonly known as the Williams' Aerides, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features slender, upright stems that bear clusters of fragrant, greenish-yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its leaves are elongated and strap-like, often arranged in a rosette pattern.

This orchid is prized for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to cultivate under the right conditions.
