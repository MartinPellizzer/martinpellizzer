Actinodaphne chinensis var. rotundifolia

Actinodaphne chinensis var. rotundifolia, commonly known as the round-leafed camphor tree, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, including parts of China, India, and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its glossy, elliptical to round leaves and small, clustered white flowers. Its fruits are drupes, typically containing a single seed.

This species is valued for its aromatic wood and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
