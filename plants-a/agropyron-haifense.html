Agropyron haifense

Agropyron haifense, commonly known as the Haifeng grass, is a perennial grass species native to China.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and slender, wiry stems. The plant produces spike-like inflorescences bearing small, spikelet-based flowers. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green coloration.

Agropyron haifense is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a resilient species in harsh climates.
