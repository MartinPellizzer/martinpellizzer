Athanasia capitata

Athanasia capitata, commonly known as the Crown of Thorns, is a succulent shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its spiny, fleshy stems and clusters of small, bright yellow flowers. The plant has thick, water-storing leaves that help it survive in arid environments. It produces small, red, berry-like fruits that are edible but have a bitter taste.

Athanasia capitata is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its hardiness and unique appearance.
