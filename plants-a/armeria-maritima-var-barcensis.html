Armeria maritima var. barcensis

Armeria maritima var. barcensis, commonly known as the Barcens sandverbena, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of South Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial that thrives in sandy, well-drained soils and full sun exposure. The plant features slender, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers during the spring and summer months. It is adapted to coastal environments, showing resistance to salt spray and strong winds.

This variety is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in coastal landscapes.
