Anil lespedezioides

Anil lespedezioides, commonly known as the silverleaf tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray, fuzzy foliage that gives it its common name. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by flat, bean-like pods containing seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy environments.

Anil lespedezioides is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
