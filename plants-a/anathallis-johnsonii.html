Anathallis johnsonii

Anathallis johnsonii, commonly known as Johnson's anathallis, is a species of orchid native to Brazil.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that grows on trees in tropical rainforests. The plant produces delicate, greenish-yellow flowers with a distinctive, elongated spur. Its botanical features include narrow, strap-like leaves and a compact inflorescence.

It is part of the Orchidaceae family and is noted for its unique floral morphology and ecological adaptation.
