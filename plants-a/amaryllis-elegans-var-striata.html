Amaryllis elegans var. striata

Amaryllis elegans var. striata, commonly known as the Zebra Amaryllis, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, strap-like leaves with a prominent white stripe running down the center, giving it its distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of vibrant red or orange flowers with yellow centers, typically blooming in late summer to early autumn. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and containers.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
