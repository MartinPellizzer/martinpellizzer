Anthemis schizostephana

Anthemis schizostephana, commonly known as the narrow-leaved everlasting, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are often arranged in dense clusters, giving it a fluffy appearance. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, adapted to reduce water loss in its arid environment.

Anthemis schizostephana is often used in floral arrangements due to its long-lasting blooms and ornamental value.
