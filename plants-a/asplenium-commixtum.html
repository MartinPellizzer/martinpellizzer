Asplenium commixtum

Asplenium commixtum, commonly known as the mixed spleenwort, is a fern species belonging to the family Aspleniaceae.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen foliage and is typically found growing on rocky substrates or in shaded, moist environments. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit, allowing it to spread and form dense mats. Its fronds are usually narrow and lanceolate, with a smooth or slightly serrated margin.

Asplenium commixtum is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in shaded garden settings.
