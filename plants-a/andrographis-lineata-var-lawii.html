Andrographis lineata var. lawii

Andrographis lineata var. lawii, commonly known as the narrow-leaf andrographis, is a variant of the Andrographis genus native to Southeast Asia.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its slender, elongated leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

The botanical features of this variant include its distinct leaf morphology and reproductive structures typical of the Andrographis species.
