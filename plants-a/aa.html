Aa

Aa, commonly known as the Hawaiian silversword, is a unique plant native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It belongs to the sunflower family (Asteraceae) and is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and striking, spiky flower heads. The plant thrives in the volcanic soils of Hawaii's high elevations, where it has adapted to harsh environmental conditions. It plays a significant role in the local ecosystem and is considered a symbol of the islands' natural heritage.

Due to habitat loss and invasive species, the silversword is also a conservation concern.
