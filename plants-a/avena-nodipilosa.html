Avena nodipilosa

Avena nodipilosa, commonly known as the nodding oat, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the grass family (Poaceae) and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and spikelet clusters. The plant produces small, two-lobed flowers that are typically greenish or brownish in color.
