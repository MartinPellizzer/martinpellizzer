Athyrium angustum f. confertum

Athyrium angustum f. confertum, commonly known as the narrow fern, is a fern species characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped fronds.

It belongs to the family Athyriaceae and is native to regions in Asia, including China and Japan. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky slopes. Its fronds are usually dark green and have a delicate, feathery appearance.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and ability to thrive in partial shade.
