Agathosma acuminata var. candollei

Agathosma acuminata var. candollei, commonly known as Cape citrus or wild citrus, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its aromatic, small, greenish-yellow flowers and oval-shaped, leathery leaves. The plant produces small, round fruits that are typically green when mature. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal regions.

Due to its unique scent and potential medicinal properties, it is of interest to botanists and herbalists alike.
