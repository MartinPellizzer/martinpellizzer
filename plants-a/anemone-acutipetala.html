Anemone acutipetala

Anemone acutipetala, commonly known as the sharp-petaled anemone, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features showy, white to pale pink flowers with deeply divided, sharply toothed petals. The plant has a short, stout rhizome and basal leaves that are palmately lobed with hairy surfaces. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is often found in alpine meadows.

Its flowering period typically occurs in late spring to early summer.
