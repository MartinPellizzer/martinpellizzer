Alstonia neriifolia var. glabra

Alstonia neriifolia var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth alstonia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its smooth, glabrous bark and opposite, ovate leaves that are typically dark green in color. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in moist, well-drained soils.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
