Acacia drummondii subsp. elegans

Acacia drummondii subsp.

elegans, commonly known as the elegant acacia, is a shrub native to the arid regions of Australia. It features slender, upright stems and small, feathery leaves that are adapted to reduce water loss in dry environments.

The plant produces yellow flowers in dense, globular clusters, which attract various pollinators such as bees and birds. It is often found in sandy soils and can thrive in full sun.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for drought-tolerant landscaping.
