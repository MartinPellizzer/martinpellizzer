Anthurium sukutense

Anthurium sukutense, commonly known as the red-veined anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves with striking red veins against a deep green background. The plant produces vibrant red spathes that enclose its yellowish spadix, making it a popular choice for ornamental purposes. It thrives in humid, warm environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium sukutense is relatively low maintenance, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.
