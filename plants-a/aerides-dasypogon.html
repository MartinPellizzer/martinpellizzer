Aerides dasypogon

Aerides dasypogon, commonly known as the hairy lip orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces showy, fragrant flowers with a distinctive hairy lip. The plant typically features broad, leathery leaves and thrives in humid, shaded environments. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.

Aerides dasypogon is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is known for its resilience in various growing conditions.
