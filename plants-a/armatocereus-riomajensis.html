Armatocereus riomajensis

Armatocereus riomajensis, commonly known as the Riomaja cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a columnar cactus characterized by its thick, ribbed stems and spiny areoles. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, requiring well-drained soil and full sun exposure.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
