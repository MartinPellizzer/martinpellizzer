Astelma

Astelma, commonly known as the starflower, is a genus of flowering plants in the sunflower family, Asteraceae.

It is native to southern Africa and consists of several species, many of which are shrubs or small trees. The plant is characterized by its showy, star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of yellow, orange, and red. Astelma species typically have narrow, leathery leaves and produce small, capsule-like fruits.

These plants are often cultivated for their ornamental value and are adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.
