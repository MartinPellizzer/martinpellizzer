Astragalus soxmaniorum

Astragalus soxmaniorum, commonly known as the Chinese milkvetch, is a perennial legume native to China.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellowish flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant produces cylindrical pods that contain several seeds, typical of the Fabaceae family. It thrives in dry, well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and rocky areas.

Astragalus soxmaniorum is valued for its medicinal properties in traditional Chinese medicine.
