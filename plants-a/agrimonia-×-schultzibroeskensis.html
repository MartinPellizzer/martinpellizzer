Agrimonia × schultzibroeskensis

Agrimonia × schultzibroeskensis, commonly known as the hybrid agrimony, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It belongs to the rose family (Rosaceae) and is characterized by its upright growth habit and serrated leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-red flowers that bloom in late summer. Agrimonia × schultzibroeskensis is often found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional herbal medicine.
