Astrantia caucasica

Astrantia caucasica, commonly known as Caucasian aster, is a perennial herb native to the Caucasus region.

It features basal leaves that are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pinkish flowers with prominent central disks. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight.

Astrantia caucasica is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
