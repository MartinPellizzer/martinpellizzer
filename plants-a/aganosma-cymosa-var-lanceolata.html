Aganosma cymosa var. lanceolata

Aganosma cymosa var. lanceolata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved aganosma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, lance-shaped leaves that are usually arranged alternately along the stem. The plant produces small, clustered yellow flowers that bloom in spring, adding a delicate touch to its appearance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for horticultural study and conservation efforts.
