Arenaria cucubaloides var. confertiflora

Arenaria cucubaloides var. confertiflora, commonly known as the denseflower sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to arctic and alpine regions.

It features a mat-forming growth habit with densely packed, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white wax. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink, blooming in early summer. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is adapted to cold, harsh environments.

This variety is notable for its compact structure and ability to withstand extreme weather conditions.
