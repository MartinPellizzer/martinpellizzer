Amomum raoi var. oblongum

Amomum raoi var. oblongum, commonly known as the broadleaf cardamom, is a tropical plant belonging to the ginger family (Zingiberaceae).

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its aromatic, elliptical leaves and fragrant, three-lobed flowers. The plant produces dark brown, oblong-shaped fruits that contain aromatic seeds used in traditional medicine and culinary applications. Its botanical features make it a valuable species in both horticulture and pharmacology.
