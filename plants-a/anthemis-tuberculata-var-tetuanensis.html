Anthemis tuberculata var. tetuanensis

Anthemis tuberculata var. tetuanensis, commonly known as the Tetuan carnation, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered inflorescences. It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes in gardens and landscapes.

This variety is well-suited to full sun and well-drained soils, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
