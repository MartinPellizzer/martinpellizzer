Agrostis sikkimensis

Agrostis sikkimensis, commonly known as Sikkim grass, is a species of grass native to the eastern Himalayas.

It is a perennial plant that typically grows in moist, temperate environments, often found in meadows and forest margins. The plant has slender, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in horticulture.

Agrostis sikkimensis plays a role in stabilizing soil and supporting local biodiversity in its native habitat.
