Anthemis kandaharica

Anthemis kandaharica, commonly known as Kandahar ragwort, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its flowers are yellow with a central disc, and it produces small, hairy seeds that are dispersed by the wind.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species in its native habitat.
