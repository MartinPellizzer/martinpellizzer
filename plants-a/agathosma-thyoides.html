Agathosma thyoides

Agathosma thyoides, commonly known as Cape daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers with yellow centers and white petals. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and has a succulent, low-growing habit. It produces small, oval-shaped leaves and is often used in traditional medicine.
