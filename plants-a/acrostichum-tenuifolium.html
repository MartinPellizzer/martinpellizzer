Acrostichum tenuifolium

Acrostichum tenuifolium, commonly known as the slender-leaf fern, is a species of fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Davalliaceae and is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, such as forests and stream banks. Its spores are produced on the underside of the fronds, which are arranged in a distinctive, fan-like pattern.
