Agave obscura

Agave obscura, commonly known as the Mexican cup plant, is a succulent plant native to Mexico.

It features a rosette of thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-gray in color. The plant produces a tall, branched inflorescence with small, greenish-white flowers. It is adapted to arid environments and stores water in its leaves and stem.

Agave obscura is often cultivated for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
