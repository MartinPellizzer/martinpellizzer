Androrchis × ligustica

Androrchis × ligustica, commonly known as the Ligurian orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces delicate, fragrant flowers with a distinctive coloration. The plant typically features long, narrow leaves and blooms in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained, calcareous soils and requires partial shade for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
