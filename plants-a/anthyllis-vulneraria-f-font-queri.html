Anthyllis vulneraria f. font-queri

Anthyllis vulneraria f. font-queri, commonly known as the Font-Queri's burnet, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

The plant produces trifoliate leaves with a hairy surface, and its root system is typically fibrous. It thrives in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils, often in temperate regions.
