Aster solandri

Aster solandri, commonly known as the New Zealand aster, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in coastal and subalpine regions, thriving in well-drained soils. Its flowers are usually blue to purple in color, with ray florets surrounding a central disk.

Aster solandri is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in native habitats.
