Adinobotrys atropurpureus

Adinobotrys atropurpureus, commonly known as the purple-leafed hibiscus, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep purple or bronze color. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of red, orange, or yellow, which bloom during the warm season. It thrives in humid, well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Adinobotrys atropurpureus is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a decorative plant in tropical landscapes.
