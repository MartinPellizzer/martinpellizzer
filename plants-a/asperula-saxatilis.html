Asperula saxatilis

Asperula saxatilis, commonly known as the rock asperula, is a small evergreen shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are typically arranged in whorls around the stems. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers during the spring and early summer. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous areas.

Asperula saxatilis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to tolerate harsh environmental conditions.
