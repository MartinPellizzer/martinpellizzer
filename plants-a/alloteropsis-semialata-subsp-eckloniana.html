Alloteropsis semialata subsp. eckloniana

Alloteropsis semialata subsp.

eckloniana, commonly known as the Ecklon's grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa. It typically grows in coastal and sandy soils, often found in dune environments.

The plant has slender, erect stems with narrow, linear leaves that are usually hairy. Its inflorescence consists of a panicle of small, spikelet-like flowers.

It is adapted to withstand salt spray and is often used in coastal restoration projects.
