Anthurium Variable Var. Ottonianum

Anthurium variable var. ottonianum is a striking tropical plant known for its vibrant, heart-shaped leaves and unique spadix inflorescence. Native to the humid lowlands of Central and South America, this species thrives in warm, moist environments with bright indirect light. Its foliage often displays a mix of green, red, and yellow tones, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening. The plant's ornamental value extends beyond its foliage, as its small, clustered flowers add an intriguing visual element. With proper care, Anthurium variable var. ottonianum can flourish as a long-lived and visually appealing houseplant.
