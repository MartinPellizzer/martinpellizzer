Alternanthera rufa var. glabrata

Alternanthera rufa var. glabrata, commonly known as red leaf alternanthera, is a striking aquatic plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red foliage, which intensifies under strong light conditions. The plant has slender, branching stems and small, lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged oppositely. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in shallow, slow-moving waters.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for aquariums and water gardens.
