Anthurium fogdeniorum

Anthurium fogdeniorum, commonly known as the Fogden's anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a waxy texture. The plant produces vibrant red spathes that enclose its spadix, which is often covered in tiny, clustered flowers. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Anthurium fogdeniorum is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and air-purifying properties.
