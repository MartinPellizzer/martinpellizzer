Astragalus aboriginorum var. glabriusculus, commonly known as the smooth-hairy milkvetch, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its slender stems and small, pea-like flowers that typically range in color from pale yellow to reddish-brown. Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and vulnerable species.
