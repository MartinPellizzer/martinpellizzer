Anemia hirsuta var. subfiliformis

Anemia hirsuta var. subfiliformis, commonly known as the silky anemia, is a rare variant of the Anemia hirsuta species.

It is characterized by its delicate, hair-like leaves that give it a soft, feathery appearance. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its leaves are usually light green and have a slightly waxy texture.

This variant is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and unique foliage structure.
