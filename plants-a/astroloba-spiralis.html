Astroloba spiralis

Astroloba spiralis, commonly known as the spiral moon cactus, is a species of flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asphodelaceae family and is characterized by its unique spiral-shaped leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers during the warmer months, adding to its ornamental value. It is a succulent, meaning it stores water in its leaves and is adapted to arid environments.

Due to its striking appearance and ease of care, it is popular among succulent enthusiasts.
