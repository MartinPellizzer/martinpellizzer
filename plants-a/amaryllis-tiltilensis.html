Amaryllis tiltilensis

Amaryllis tiltilensis, commonly known as the Tiltill's amaryllis, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers. The plant typically features strap-like, evergreen leaves that form a rosette at the base. It blooms in late spring or early summer, producing vibrant flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow.

Amaryllis tiltilensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements in suitable climates.
