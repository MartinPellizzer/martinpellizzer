Ardisia pubicalyx

Ardisia pubicalyx, commonly known as the velvet evergreen or broadleaf ardisia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrsinaceae.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Following flowering, it develops small, red, fleshy berries that are attractive to birds.

Native to Southeast Asia, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and adaptability to various growing conditions.
