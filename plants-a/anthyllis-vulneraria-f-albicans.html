Anthyllis vulneraria f. albicans

Anthyllis vulneraria f. albicans, commonly known as the white scurvygrass, is a variant of the common scurvygrass.

It is a low-growing, annual herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. The plant features small, bright white flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are pinnately divided, and it thrives in dry, disturbed soils.

It is often found in sandy or gravelly habitats, such as coastal dunes and road verges.
