Ayenia oblongata

Ayenia oblongata, commonly known as the narrowleaf ayenia, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Ayenia oblongata is often found in shaded areas and is valued for its ecological role in supporting pollinators.
