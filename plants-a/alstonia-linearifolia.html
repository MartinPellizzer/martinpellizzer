Alstonia linearifolia

Alstonia linearifolia, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, bright red flowers that appear in clusters during the rainy season. The plant has simple, alternate, lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and glossy. Its bark is smooth and often exhibits a reddish or brownish hue.

Alstonia linearifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and as a street tree due to its rapid growth and showy blooms.
