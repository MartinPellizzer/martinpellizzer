Agrostis brachystachys

Agrostis brachystachys, commonly known as the slender bentgrass, is a low-growing perennial grass species native to Europe, Asia, and Africa.

It features fine, narrow leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences that are typically less than 5 cm in length. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows, wetlands, and along stream banks. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to form dense mats, which can stabilize soil and prevent erosion.

Agrostis brachystachys is also valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to tolerate a variety of environmental conditions.
