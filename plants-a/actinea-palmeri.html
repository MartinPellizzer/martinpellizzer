Actinea palmeri

Actinea palmeri, commonly known as Palmer's actinia, is a species of sea anemone found in the Pacific Ocean.

It belongs to the family Actinidae and is characterized by its delicate, translucent tentacles. This marine organism is typically found in shallow, warm waters and is often associated with coral reefs. Actinea palmeri plays a role in the marine ecosystem by serving as prey for various fish and invertebrates.

Its vibrant colors and unique morphology make it a notable species in underwater biodiversity studies.
