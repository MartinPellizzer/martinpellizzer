Allium herderianum

Allium herderianum, commonly known as Herder's garlic, is a species of flowering plant in the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas, particularly in regions of India and Nepal. The plant produces small, white, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, growing from a bulbous base similar to other allium species.

The plant is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
