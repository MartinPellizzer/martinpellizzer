Ansellia africana var. nilotica

Ansellia africana var. nilotica, commonly known as the African peace lily, is a tropical plant native to eastern Africa.

It is characterized by its broad, dark green, heart-shaped leaves that are glossy and arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces striking white or pale green spathe flowers that bloom on tall, slender stems. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate moisture, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its ability to purify air and tolerate low light conditions adds to its appeal as a decorative and functional plant.
