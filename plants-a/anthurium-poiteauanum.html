Anthurium poiteauanum

Anthurium poiteauanum, commonly known as the Poiteau's anthurium, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a reddish underside. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-shaped inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, which can range from red to orange. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Anthurium poiteauanum is a popular ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
