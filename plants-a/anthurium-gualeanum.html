Anthurium gualeanum

Anthurium gualeanum, commonly known as the Gualeanum arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to Central America.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its unique inflorescence consisting of a spadix and spathe. The plant typically features heart-shaped leaves with a glossy surface and vibrant green coloration. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Anthurium gualeanum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in botanical gardens.
