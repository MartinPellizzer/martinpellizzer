Alyssum montanum var. brigantiacum

Alyssum montanum var. brigantiacum, commonly known as the Sierra Nevada alyssum, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces clusters of small, fragrant white or pale yellow flowers. The plant has linear to lance-shaped leaves and is often found in rocky or gravelly soils. It thrives in full sun and is adapted to dry, alpine environments.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for ecological restoration and ornamental gardening.
