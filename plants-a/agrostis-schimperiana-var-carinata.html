Agrostis schimperiana var. carinata

Agrostis schimperiana var. carinata, commonly known as the ridge grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves with a distinct midrib. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences bearing tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open grasslands and rocky habitats.

Its ecological importance includes providing forage for wildlife and stabilizing soil in arid environments.
