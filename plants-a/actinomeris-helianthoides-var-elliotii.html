Actinomeris helianthoides var. elliotii

Actinomeris helianthoides var. elliotii, commonly known as Elliot's sunflower plant, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and medicinal properties. The plant typically features yellow to orange ray flowers and a central disk of tubular flowers, resembling a sunflower. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in dry, open habitats.

Its botanical significance lies in its potential use in traditional medicine and its role in local ecosystems.
