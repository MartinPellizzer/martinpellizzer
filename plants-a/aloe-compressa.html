Aloe compressa

Aloe compressa, commonly known as the narrow-leaf aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features slender, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in summer, often from a tall stem. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves.

Aloe compressa is popular as a decorative plant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
