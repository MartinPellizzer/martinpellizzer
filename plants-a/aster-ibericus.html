Aster ibericus

Aster ibericus, commonly known as the Iberian aster, is a flowering plant native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is often found in Mediterranean climates. Its flowers are usually blue to violet in color, with ray florets surrounding a central disc.

Aster ibericus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
