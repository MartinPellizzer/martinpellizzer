Arisaema alpestre

Arisaema alpestre, commonly known as the alpine arum, is a herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It features a distinctive leaf structure with a broad, heart-shaped leaf blade and a short, petiole. The plant produces an inflorescence consisting of a spadix surrounded by a spathe, typical of the Araceae family. Its flowers are unisexual, with separate male and female flowers on the spadix.

Arisaema alpestre thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in rocky or forested habitats at high altitudes.
