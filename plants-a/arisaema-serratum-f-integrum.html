Arisaema serratum f. integrum

Arisaema serratum f. integrum, commonly known as the smooth-leaf arrowhead, is a variant of the arrowhead plant characterized by its smooth, undivided leaves.

It is native to the mountainous regions of eastern Asia, including China and Korea. The plant produces striking, spadix-bearing inflorescences surrounded by a distinctive, hood-like spathe. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and ecological restoration projects.
