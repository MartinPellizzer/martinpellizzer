Azalea fulgens

Azalea fulgens, commonly known as the flame azalea, is a deciduous shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from red to orange and yellow. The plant typically grows to a height of 3 to 6 feet and features dense, dark green foliage. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sun.

Azalea fulgens is a popular ornamental plant in gardens due to its vibrant blooms and hardy nature.
