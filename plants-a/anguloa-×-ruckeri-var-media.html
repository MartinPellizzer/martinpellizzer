Anguloa × ruckeri var. media

Anguloa × ruckeri var. media, commonly known as the hybrid anguloa, is a species of flowering plant in the Gesneriaceae family.

It is native to the Andean regions of South America, particularly Ecuador and Peru. Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by horticulturists.
