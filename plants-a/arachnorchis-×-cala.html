Arachnorchis × cala

Arachnorchis × cala, commonly known as the hybrid spider orchid, is a fascinating member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Arachnorchis phalaenopsis and Arachnorchis latifolia, showcasing a blend of characteristics from both parent plants. The plant is known for its intricate, spider-like flower structure, which is both ornamental and biologically distinctive. It typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often found in temperate regions of Australia.

Due to its rarity and unique morphology, it is a subject of interest for botanists and orchid enthusiasts alike.
