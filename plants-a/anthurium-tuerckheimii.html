Anthurium tuerckheimii

Anthurium tuerckheimii, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically deep green with a reddish or purple tint. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-shaped inflorescences that are often surrounded by a showy spathe. It thrives in humid, warm environments with bright, indirect light.

Anthurium tuerckheimii is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
