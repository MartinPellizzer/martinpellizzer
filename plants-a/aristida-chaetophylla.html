Aristida chaetophylla

Aristida chaetophylla, commonly known as silky hairgrass, is a perennial grass species native to Australia.

It is characterized by its fine, silky-textured foliage and produces small, pale green to brown spikelets. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. It has a compact growth habit and is often found in coastal and inland regions.

Its botanical features make it a valuable plant for ecological restoration and ornamental gardening.
