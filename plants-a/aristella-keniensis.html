Aristella keniensis

Aristella keniensis, commonly known as the Kenyan aristella, is a flowering plant native to East Africa.

It belongs to the family Orchidaceae, commonly known as the orchid family. The plant is characterized by its striking, spurred orchid flowers that range in color from white to purple. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and by plant enthusiasts.
