Aloe tartarensis

Aloe tartarensis, commonly known as the Cape aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a waxy coating. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow flowers during the summer months. Aloe tartarensis is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine and for ornamental purposes.
