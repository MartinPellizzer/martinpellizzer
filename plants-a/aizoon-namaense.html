Aizoon namaense

Aizoon namaense, commonly known as the Namib Desert dandelion, is a flowering plant native to the Namib Desert in southern Africa.

It is a small, perennial herb that thrives in arid and coastal environments. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in spring, followed by fluffy seeds dispersed by the wind. Its leaves are succulent, adapted to store water in a harsh climate.

Aizoon namaense plays a role in stabilizing sandy soils and providing food for local wildlife.
