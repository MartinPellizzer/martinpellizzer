Aerides orthocentra

Aerides orthocentra, commonly known as the orchid with striking red and yellow flowers, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its epiphytic growth habit, meaning it grows on trees without being parasitic. The plant produces showy, fragrant blooms that are typically clustered at the base of the inflorescence. Its leaves are usually dark green and arranged in a rosette pattern, providing a lush appearance.

Aerides orthocentra is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and adaptability to various growing conditions.
