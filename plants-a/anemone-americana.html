Anemone americana

Anemone americana, commonly known as the American anemone, is a herbaceous perennial native to North America.

It features showy, white to pale pink flowers with a central cluster of stamens, typically blooming in early spring. The plant has palmately divided, lobed leaves that are often hairy and serrated. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Anemone americana is often found in woodland areas and along stream banks, where it contributes to the ecological diversity of its habitat.
