Aster sanqingensis

Aster sanqingensis, commonly known as the Sanqing aster, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers. This species is characterized by its showy, violet-blue flower heads that bloom in the summer. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions and is adapted to cooler climates.

Its botanical features make it a subject of interest for both horticulture and ecological studies.
