Acetosella vulgaris f. integrifolia

Acetosella vulgaris f. integrifolia, commonly known as the smooth wood sorrel, is a low-growing perennial plant characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellow flowers.

The leaves are typically rounded with smooth edges, distinguishing it from other varieties of wood sorrel.
