Aloe inermis

Aloe inermis, commonly known as the smooth aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, narrow leaves that are pale green to grayish in color. The plant produces tall, branched stems with yellowish-green flowers during the summer months. Its leaves contain a gel-like substance that is sometimes used for its soothing properties.

Aloe inermis is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil and full sun.
