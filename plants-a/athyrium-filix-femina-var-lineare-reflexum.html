Athyrium filix-femina var. lineare-reflexum

Athyrium filix-femina var. lineare-reflexum, commonly known as the reflexed lady fern, is a variant of the common lady fern characterized by its distinctive, reflexed fronds.

It is a member of the Dryopteridaceae family and is native to the temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere. The plant features slender, upright fronds with a delicate, lacy appearance, and its leaves are typically bi- or tri-pinnate. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

The varietal form, lineare-reflexum, is distinguished by its elongated, reflexed leaflets that give it a unique, flowing appearance.
