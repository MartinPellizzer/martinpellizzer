Alocasia argyrea

Alocasia argyrea, commonly known as the silver queen alocasia, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features large, heart-shaped leaves that are often adorned with a silvery or white veining, giving it its distinctive appearance. The plant is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in humid, warm environments. It prefers well-draining soil and indirect sunlight to prevent leaf burn.

Alocasia argyrea is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
