Arivela Cleomoides

Arivela cleomoides is a rare and fascinating flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America. It belongs to the Cleomaceae family, known for its unique and often showy blooms. This plant is characterized by its delicate, clustered flowers and its ability to thrive in moist, shaded environments. Due to habitat loss, it faces significant threats to its survival. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this beautiful and ecologically important species.
