Arisaema flavum subsp. tibeticum

Arisaema flavum subsp.

tibeticum, commonly known as the Tibetan yellow arrowhead, is a herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Tibet. It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its distinctive arrow-shaped leaves and spadix inflorescence.

The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments at high altitudes. Its flowers are surrounded by a modified leaf called a spathe, which is usually yellow or green in color.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its unique foliage and floral structure.
