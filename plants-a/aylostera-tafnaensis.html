Aylostera tafnaensis

Aylostera tafnaensis, commonly known as the Tafna aster, is a flowering plant native to Algeria.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky and arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, and it produces small, white to pale yellow flowers.

Aylostera tafnaensis is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphology and ecological significance in its native habitat.
