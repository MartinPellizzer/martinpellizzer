Avena scabriuscula

Avena scabriuscula, commonly known as rough oat, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a member of the grass family (Poaceae) and is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences with numerous tiny, greenish-white flowers. Its leaves are narrow, hairy, and often have a rough texture, which gives it its common name.

Avena scabriuscula is typically found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes.
