Alocasia macrorrhizos var. rubra

Alocasia macrorrhizos var. rubra, commonly known as the Red-veined Alocasia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves with deep green veins and a striking red or purple underside. The plant thrives in humid, warm environments with bright, indirect light. Its foliage is often used in ornamental horticulture for its aesthetic appeal.

It requires well-draining soil and consistent moisture to flourish.
