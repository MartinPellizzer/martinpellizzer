Aspidistra nigra

Aspidistra nigra, commonly known as the black aspidistra, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, glossy leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a leathery texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are typically clustered in spikes. It is a shade-tolerant plant, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.

Aspidistra nigra is valued for its ornamental foliage and ability to thrive in low-light conditions.
