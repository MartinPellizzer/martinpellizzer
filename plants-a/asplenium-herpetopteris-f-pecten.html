Asplenium herpetopteris f. pecten

Asplenium herpetopteris f. pecten, commonly known as the feather-leaf spleenwort, is a fern species belonging to the family Aspleniaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, feather-like fronds that are typically narrow and elongated. The plant exhibits a rosette growth form, with the fronds emerging from a central point. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found growing on rock surfaces or in crevices.

The fronds are usually dark green and have a leathery texture, contributing to its adaptability in humid, woodland habitats.
