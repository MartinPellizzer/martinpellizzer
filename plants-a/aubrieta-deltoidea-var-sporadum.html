Aubrieta deltoidea var. sporadum

Aubrieta deltoidea var. sporadum, commonly known as the rock cress, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features dense clusters of small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has succulent, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-gray in color, adapted to retain moisture. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and full sun, making it ideal for rock gardens or borders.

Its compact size and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
