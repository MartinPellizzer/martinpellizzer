Astragalus taiyuanensis

Astragalus taiyuanensis, commonly known as the Taiyuan milkvetch, is a flowering plant species belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to China, specifically found in the Shanxi province, where it thrives in arid and semi-arid environments. The plant is characterized by its slender stems, compound leaves, and small, yellowish flowers that bloom in late spring. It plays a role in soil stabilization and is of interest for its potential medicinal properties.

Due to habitat loss and over-collection, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native range.
