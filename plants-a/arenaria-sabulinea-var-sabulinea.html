Arenaria sabulinea var. sabulinea

Arenaria sabulinea var. sabulinea, commonly known as sand sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to arctic and alpine regions.

It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is adapted to harsh environmental conditions. The plant features small, white or pinkish flowers that bloom in early summer. Its leaves are typically linear and covered in a dense layer of fine hairs.

It plays an important role in stabilizing soil in its native habitats.
