Artemisia turkestanica

Artemisia turkestanica, commonly known as the Turkestan wormwood, is a perennial herb native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, silver-gray foliage. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in composite heads, typical of the daisy family. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky or sandy soils.

Artemisia turkestanica is valued for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional medicine for various ailments.
