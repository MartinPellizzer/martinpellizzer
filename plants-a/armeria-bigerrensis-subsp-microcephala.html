Armeria bigerrensis subsp. microcephala

Armeria bigerrensis subsp.

microcephala, commonly known as the dwarf sea thrift, is a low-growing evergreen perennial native to coastal areas of southern China. It features dense clusters of small, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring.

The plant has succulent, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, adapted to withstand salty air and coastal conditions. It thrives in well-drained sandy or gravelly soils and is often used in rock gardens or coastal landscapes.

Its compact form and hardy nature make it a popular choice for gardeners in maritime climates.
