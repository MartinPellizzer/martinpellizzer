Allium minutum

Allium minutum, commonly known as the dwarf garlic or lesser garlic, is a small, flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and clusters of small, white or pale purple flowers. Its leaves are grass-like and narrow, often growing close to the ground.

Allium minutum is often found in rocky or gravelly soils and is adapted to dry, sunny environments.
