Alocasia reginula

Alocasia reginula, commonly known as the velvet leaf alocasia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, velvety foliage with a striking purple underside. The plant features a short, stocky stem and produces small, inconspicuous white flowers. It thrives in humid, warm environments with bright, indirect light.

Alocasia reginula is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its unique and elegant appearance.
