Agrostis clarkei

Agrostis clarkei, commonly known as Clarke's bentgrass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in coastal areas and is adapted to sandy or gravelly soils. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It is often found in dune environments and is resilient to salt spray and wind.

Its botanical features make it an important component of coastal vegetation in its native range.
