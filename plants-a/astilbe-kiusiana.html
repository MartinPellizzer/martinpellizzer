Astilbe kiusiana

Astilbe kiusiana, commonly known as the Kiusian false spirea, is a herbaceous perennial native to Japan.

It features delicate, fern-like foliage that turns bronze in the fall. The plant produces upright clusters of pink to lavender flowers in late summer. It thrives in partial shade and moist, well-drained soil.

Astilbe kiusiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to add color to shaded garden spaces.
