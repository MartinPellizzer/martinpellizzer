Alternanthera malmeana var. straminea

Alternanthera malmeana var. straminea, commonly known as the golden alternanthera, is a striking aquatic plant native to South America.

It features lance-shaped leaves that are bright golden in color, with a soft, velvety texture. The plant thrives in shallow, slow-moving waters and prefers moderate to high light conditions. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in aquariums and ponds.

Its foliage remains vibrant throughout the growing season, making it a popular choice for freshwater horticulture.
