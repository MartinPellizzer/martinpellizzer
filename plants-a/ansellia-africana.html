Ansellia africana

Ansellia africana, commonly known as the African arrowroot, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive inflorescence consisting of a spadix surrounded by a spathe. The plant features large, arrowhead-shaped leaves that are glossy and often have a waxy texture. Its flowers are typically greenish-white with a spadix that bears both male and female flowers.

Ansellia africana is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in some African cultures.
