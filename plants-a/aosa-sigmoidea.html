Aosa sigmoidea

Aosa sigmoidea, commonly known as the zigzag aosa, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive zigzagging stems, which give it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in humid, tropical environments and is often found in forest understories.

Aosa sigmoidea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
