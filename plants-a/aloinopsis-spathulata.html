Aloinopsis spathulata

Aloinopsis spathulata, commonly known as the broad-leaved aloinopsis, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, with a waxy coating to store water. The plant produces tall, branched stems with small, yellow flowers during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
