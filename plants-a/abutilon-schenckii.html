Abutilon schenckii

Abutilon schenckii, commonly known as the velvetleaf abutilon, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 3 meters in height, characterized by its hairy, velvety leaves. The plant produces showy, lantern-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a soft, fuzzy texture.

It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.
