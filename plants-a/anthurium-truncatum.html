Anthurium truncatum

Anthurium truncatum, commonly known as the velvet leaf anthurium, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are velvety and dark green with a deep red or purple underside. The plant produces striking spadices, which are modified leaves that house the small, inconspicuous flowers. Anthurium truncatum thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and ornamental purposes.
