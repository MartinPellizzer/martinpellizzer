Aster filipes

Aster filipes, commonly known as slenderleaf aster, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and woodland edges. Its leaves are narrow and linear, and it produces showy purple to pink flowers in the fall.

Aster filipes is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting pollinators.
