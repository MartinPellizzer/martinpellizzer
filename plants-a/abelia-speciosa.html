Abelia speciosa

Abelia speciosa, commonly known as the Chinese abelia, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features showy, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink, blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. It prefers partial to full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for a variety of garden settings.

Abelia speciosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
