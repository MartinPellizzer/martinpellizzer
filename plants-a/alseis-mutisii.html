Alseis mutisii

Alseis mutisii, commonly known as the Mexican alseis, is a flowering plant belonging to the Ericaceae family.

It is native to Mexico and is characterized by its evergreen shrub form, with small, leathery leaves. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers that are typically pink or white, which bloom in spring. Alseis mutisii thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and is often found in forested areas.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
