Ardisia japonica var. montana

Ardisia japonica var. montana, commonly known as the mountain ardisia, is a compact evergreen shrub native to Japan.

It features glossy, elliptical leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers in the spring, which are followed by bright red berries in the fall. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

Its ornamental value comes from its attractive foliage and seasonal blooms.
