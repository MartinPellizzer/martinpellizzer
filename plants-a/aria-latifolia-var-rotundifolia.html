Aria latifolia var. rotundifolia

Aria latifolia var. rotundifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaved aria, is a striking plant characterized by its large, round, and often waxy leaves.

It belongs to the Araliaceae family and is native to tropical regions of Asia. The plant typically features clusters of small, white flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its foliage is often used in ornamental horticulture due to its aesthetic appeal.

It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.
