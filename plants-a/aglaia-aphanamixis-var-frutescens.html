Aglaia aphanamixis var. frutescens

Aglaia aphanamixis var. frutescens, commonly known as the fragrant aglaia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized shrub that typically grows up to 3 meters in height. The plant features simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. Its flowers are small, fragrant, and usually yellow or greenish-yellow, arranged in clusters.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds.
