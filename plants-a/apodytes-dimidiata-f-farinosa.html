Apodytes dimidiata f. farinosa

Apodytes dimidiata f. farinosa, commonly known as the Farinosa karoo.

This succulent plant is native to South Africa and is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems and small, clustered leaves. The leaves are typically gray-green in color and have a powdery coating, which helps reduce water loss in arid conditions. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in spring, adding a subtle ornamental value.

The plant is well adapted to dry, rocky environments and is often used in xeriscaping due to its low water requirements.
