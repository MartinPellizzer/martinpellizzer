Aechmea × fulgofascicata var. tricolor

Aechmea × fulgofascicata var. tricolor, commonly known as the Trinidadian pineapple bromeliad, is a hybrid bromeliad native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful rosette of broad, waxy leaves that display a mix of red, orange, and yellow hues. The plant produces a tall, branched inflorescence that bears small, showy flowers, typically in shades of red and orange. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil with high humidity.

This ornamental plant is popular in tropical gardens and as a houseplant due to its vibrant appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
