Acer buergerianum var. jiujiangense

Acer buergerianum var. jiujiangense, commonly known as the Jiujiang maple, is a deciduous tree native to southern China.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves that turn vibrant red in the fall. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in early spring, followed by winged seeds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full sunlight.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscape gardening.
