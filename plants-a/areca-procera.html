Areca procera

Areca procera, commonly known as the tall areca palm, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like leaves that can reach several meters in length. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that develop into dark blue, round fruits. Areca procera is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.

Its growth habit makes it suitable for both indoor and outdoor cultivation in warm climates.
