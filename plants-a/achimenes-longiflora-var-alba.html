Achimenes longiflora var. alba

Achimenes longiflora var. alba, commonly known as the white achimenes, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its tubular, showy flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. The plant produces striking, spurred petals that attract pollinators such as hummingbirds and bees. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Its foliage consists of heart-shaped, glossy leaves that contribute to its aesthetic appeal.
