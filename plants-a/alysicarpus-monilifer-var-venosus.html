Alysicarpus monilifer var. venosus

Alysicarpus monilifer var. venosus, commonly known as the veiny cluster bean, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant features slender, climbing stems with hairy surfaces and produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters. Its fruit is a distinctive, elongated pod that contains several kidney-shaped seeds.

This variety is notable for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its unique foliage and floral display.
