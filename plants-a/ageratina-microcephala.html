Ageratina microcephala

Ageratina microcephala, commonly known as the small-headed blue mistflower, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

Ageratina microcephala is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive blooms and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
