Aethionema monospermum var. rosaceum

Aethionema monospermum var. rosaceum, commonly known as the Rosy Sea Rocket, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces small, bright yellow flowers in early spring. The plant has aromatic, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Its botanical features make it an attractive addition to horticultural collections.
