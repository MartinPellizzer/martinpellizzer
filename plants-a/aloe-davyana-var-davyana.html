Aloe davyana var. davyana

Aloe davyana var. davyana, commonly known as the narrow-leaved aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in arid or semi-arid environments. The plant features narrow, upright leaves that are often covered in a waxy cuticle to reduce water loss. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange and appear in clusters at the ends of the stems.

Aloe davyana var. davyana is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional medicinal uses.
