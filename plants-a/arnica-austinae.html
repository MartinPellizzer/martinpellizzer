Arnica austinae

Arnica austinae, commonly known as the Austina's arnica, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It belongs to the genus Arnica within the family Asteraceae, which includes many flowering plants with composite flower heads. The plant is characterized by its yellow-orange flowers that bloom in summer, and its hairy, lance-shaped leaves.
