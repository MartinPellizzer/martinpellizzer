Achillea tanacetifolia var. stricta

Achillea tanacetifolia var. stricta, commonly known as narrow-leaved yarrow, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, upright stems and finely divided, fern-like leaves that give it a delicate appearance. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in flat-topped clusters, typical of the Asteraceae family. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its aromatic foliage and extended blooming period contribute to its horticultural value.
