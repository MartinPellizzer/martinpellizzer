Acosta balearica

Acosta balearica, commonly known as the Balearic acosta, is a rare flowering plant native to the Balearic Islands.

It is a small herbaceous plant with slender stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, delicate white flowers with five petals, typically blooming in late spring. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to the Mediterranean climate.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species.
