Asplenium splendens subsp. drakensbergense

Asplenium splendens subsp.

drakensbergense, commonly known as the Drakensberg maidenhair fern, is a rare and specialized fern species native to the Drakensberg mountain range in southern Africa. It is characterized by its delicate, dark green, lance-shaped fronds that are often adorned with intricate sori on the underside.

The plant thrives in shaded, moist environments, typically in rocky crevices or on forest floors. Its unique morphology and limited distribution make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists.

Due to habitat destruction, it is considered vulnerable and requires careful preservation.
