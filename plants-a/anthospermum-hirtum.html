Anthospermum hirtum

Anthospermum hirtum, commonly known as the hairy antelope's foot, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its hairy, succulent leaves and showy, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to purple. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.

Its unique appearance and ability to thrive in harsh conditions make it a notable species in its native habitat.
