Aloe tsitongambarikana

Aloe tsitongambarikana, commonly known as the Natal aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asphodelaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are thick and fleshy. Aloe tsitongambarikana is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water to thrive.
