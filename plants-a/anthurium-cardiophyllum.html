Anthurium cardiophyllum

Anthurium cardiophyllum, commonly known as the heart-leaved anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking heart-shaped leaves, which are typically a deep green with a velvety texture. The plant produces vibrant red spathes that enclose its small, white spadix, creating a dramatic floral display. Anthurium cardiophyllum thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

It is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental value and ability to purify indoor air.
