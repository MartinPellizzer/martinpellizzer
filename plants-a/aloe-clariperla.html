Aloe clariperla

Aloe clariperla, commonly known as the pale leaf aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically pale green to bluish-gray in color. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow flowers during the summer months. Its leaves contain a gel-like substance that is used in traditional medicine and skincare products.

Aloe clariperla is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water to thrive.
