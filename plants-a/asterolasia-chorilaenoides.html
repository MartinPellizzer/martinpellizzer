Asterolasia chorilaenoides

Asterolasia chorilaenoides, commonly known as the narrow-leaf asterolasia, is a flowering plant native to the island of Socotra.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in arid environments and is adapted to the unique ecological conditions of Socotra.
