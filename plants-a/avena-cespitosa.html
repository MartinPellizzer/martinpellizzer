Avena cespitosa

Avena cespitosa, commonly known as slender oat, is a perennial grass native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It forms dense, clump-like growths with narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically bluish-green in color. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences bearing numerous tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. Its roots are fibrous and help stabilize soil, making it a valuable plant for erosion control.

Avena cespitosa is also sometimes cultivated as a forage crop for livestock.
