Astragalus maowensis

Astragalus maowensis, commonly known as the Maowen milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, pea-like flowers. The plant is typically found in mountainous regions and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments. Astragalus maowensis is known for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional Chinese medicine.

Its ecological significance lies in its role as a contributor to soil stabilization and biodiversity in its native habitat.
