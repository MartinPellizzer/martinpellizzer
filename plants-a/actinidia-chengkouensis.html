Actinidia chengkouensis

Actinidia chengkouensis, commonly known as the Chengkou actinidia, is a species of flowering plant in the genus Actinidia.

It is native to China and is characterized by its climbing habit and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are typically followed by edible berries. These berries are often used in traditional Chinese medicine and are valued for their nutritional content.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
