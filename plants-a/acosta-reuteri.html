Acosta reuteri

Acosta reuteri, commonly known as the Reuter's acosta, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the family Acanthaceae and is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that range in color from blue to violet. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its leaves are opposite and ovate, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

Acosta reuteri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its distinctive floral display.
