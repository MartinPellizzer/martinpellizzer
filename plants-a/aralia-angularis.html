Aralia angularis

Aralia angularis, commonly known as the zigzag aralia, is a herbaceous perennial native to the eastern United States.

It features distinctive, angular, zigzagging stems that give it its common name. The plant has compound leaves with serrated edges, and small, greenish-white flowers that appear in umbels. It thrives in partial to full shade and is often found in moist, wooded areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for shaded garden landscapes.
