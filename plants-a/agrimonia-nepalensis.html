Agrimonia nepalensis

Agrimonia nepalensis, commonly known as the Nepal agrimony, is a perennial herb native to the Himalayan region.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its upright stems and clusters of small, yellowish flowers. The plant produces distinctive seed pods that split open to release its seeds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous areas.

Agrimonia nepalensis is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
