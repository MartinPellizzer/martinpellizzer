Arenaria reptans

Arenaria reptans, commonly known as the creeping sandwort, is a low-growing flowering plant native to arctic and alpine regions.

It produces small, white or pink flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has a mat-forming habit with thin, succulent stems and small, fleshy leaves. It thrives in well-drained, sandy or rocky soils and is often found in tundra and mountainous environments.

Arenaria reptans is adapted to cold climates and is often used in ecological restoration projects.
