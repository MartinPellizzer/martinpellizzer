Astrotricha obovata

Astrotricha obovata, commonly known as the broad-leafed starwort, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of central and southern Europe.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads and often hairy foliage. The plant features a basal rosette of broad, ovate leaves with a rough, hairy texture. Its flowering heads consist of small yellow flowers arranged in a star-like pattern, giving it its common name.

Astrotricha obovata thrives in moist, rocky soils and is often found in alpine and subalpine habitats.
