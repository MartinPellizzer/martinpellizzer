Anthericum succulentum

Anthericum succulentum, commonly known as the succulent lily, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is characterized by its fleshy, thick leaves that store water, adapting it to arid environments. The plant produces striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, often appearing in late spring or early summer. Its growth habit is typically clump-forming, with a rosette of leaves surrounding the central stem.

Anthericum succulentum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
