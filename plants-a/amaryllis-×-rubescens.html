Amaryllis × rubescens

Amaryllis × rubescens, commonly known as the red amaryllis, is a hybrid plant that combines traits from various amaryllis species.

It is native to South America and is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from red to pink. The plant produces strap-like leaves that are typically broad and dark green. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Amaryllis × rubescens is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its vibrant blooms and ease of care.
