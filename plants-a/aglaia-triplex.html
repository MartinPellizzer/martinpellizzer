Aglaia triplex

Aglaia triplex, commonly known as the three-leaved aglaia, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Meliaceae, which includes mahogany and other valuable hardwoods. The plant is characterized by its trifoliate leaves, where each leaflet is elliptical and glossy. Its small, yellowish-green flowers are borne in clusters and are followed by orange-red, fleshy fruits.
