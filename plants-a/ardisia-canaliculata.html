Ardisia canaliculata

Ardisia canaliculata, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen or Christmas berry, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, dark green leaves and produces small, white flowers followed by red, berry-like fruits. The plant is known for its ornamental value, often cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and seasonal fruit display. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is relatively easy to care for.

The berries are toxic to humans and animals, so caution is advised when handling or consuming them.
