Alyssum murale var. squamatistellatum

Alyssum murale var. squamatistellatum, commonly known as the squamatistellatum alyssum, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial herb characterized by its dense clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers. The plant features hairy, silver-gray foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
