Ada lehmannii

Ada lehmannii, commonly known as the lehmann's ada, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and prefers full sun exposure. It has narrow, linear leaves and is often found in coastal or mountainous regions.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
