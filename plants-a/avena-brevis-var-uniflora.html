Avena brevis var. uniflora

Avena brevis var. uniflora, commonly known as the narrow oat with single flower, is a lesser-known variety of the oat genus.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and reduced number of inflorescences compared to typical oat species. The plant typically features slender, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like clusters of flowers. It is native to certain regions of Europe and has been studied for its potential in ecological and agricultural contexts.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and specialized plant.
