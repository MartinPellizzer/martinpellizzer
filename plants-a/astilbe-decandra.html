Astilbe decandra

Astilbe decandra, commonly known as the fragrant aster.

It is a herbaceous perennial native to eastern Asia, particularly China and Japan. The plant features upright, branching stems that bear clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers. Its foliage is dark green and finely textured, adding visual interest in the garden.

Astilbe decandra thrives in partial shade and moist, well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens.
