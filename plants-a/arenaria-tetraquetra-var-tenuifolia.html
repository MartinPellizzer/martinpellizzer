Arenaria tetraquetra var. tenuifolia

Arenaria tetraquetra var. tenuifolia, commonly known as slender sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to arctic and alpine regions.

It features small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink, blooming in late spring to early summer. The leaves are narrow and succulent, adapted to retain moisture in harsh, cold environments. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

This variety is valued for its hardiness and ornamental appeal in garden settings.
