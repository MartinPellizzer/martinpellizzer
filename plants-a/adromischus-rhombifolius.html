Adromischus rhombifolius

Adromischus rhombifolius, commonly known as the football plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy, rhombic-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, powdery coating. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the spring. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in bright, indirect light.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
