Alyssum lanuginosum

Alyssum lanuginosum, commonly known as fuzzy alyssum, is a low-growing annual plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink, often appearing in clusters. The plant has soft, hairy foliage that gives it a velvety texture, and its stems are usually prostrate or spreading. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Alyssum lanuginosum is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
