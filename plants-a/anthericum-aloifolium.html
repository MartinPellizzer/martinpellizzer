Anthericum aloifolium

Anthericum aloifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaved lily of the valley, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa.

It features lance-shaped, dark green leaves that are often arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces clusters of small, white, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodlands and along stream banks.

Its botanical characteristics make it a distinctive and ornamental addition to gardens and natural habitats.
