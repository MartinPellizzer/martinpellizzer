Aichryson villosum

Aichryson villosum, commonly known as the woolly aichryson, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features dense, woolly foliage that gives it a unique, fuzzy appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the warmer months, typically in spring or summer. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its compact growth habit makes it an attractive addition to rock gardens or as a potted plant.
