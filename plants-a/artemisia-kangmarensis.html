Artemisia kangmarensis

Artemisia kangmarensis, commonly known as the Kangmere Artemisia, is a perennial shrub native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, silver-gray foliage. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in late summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments.

Its essential oils have been studied for potential medicinal properties.
