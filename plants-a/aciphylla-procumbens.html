Aciphylla procumbens

The plant has succulent, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white wax, reducing water loss through transpiration. Its small, inconspicuous flowers are typically green or reddish and appear in clusters during the cooler months.

Aciphylla procumbens plays an important ecological role in stabilizing soils and providing habitat for small desert organisms.
