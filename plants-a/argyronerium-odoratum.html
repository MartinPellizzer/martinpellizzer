Argyronerium odoratum

Argyronerium odoratum, commonly known as the sweet flag, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to wetland areas in Asia and the Americas.

It features rhizomatous growth and narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. The plant produces small, greenish flowers that are clustered in dense, spike-like inflorescences. It is adapted to thrive in moist or aquatic environments, making it a common species in marshes and along the edges of ponds.

The plant is also known for its aromatic properties, with a mild, sweet scent that contributes to its traditional use in herbal medicine.
