Acrostichum plumbicaule

Acrostichum plumbicaule, commonly known as the slender tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to tropical regions of the Pacific.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feathery fronds that can reach several meters in length. The plant has a distinctive, hollow stem covered in a layer of persistent, brownish scales. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in coastal areas and lowland forests.

Its unique structure and growth habit make it an important component of tropical ecosystems.
