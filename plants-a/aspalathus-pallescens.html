Aspalathus pallescens

Aspalathus pallescens, commonly known as the white pea shrub, is a small, evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, white or pale yellow flowers. The plant is adapted to arid environments and has a deep root system that allows it to thrive in dry conditions.

It is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
