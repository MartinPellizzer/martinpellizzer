Artemisia kruhsiana subsp. multisecta

Artemisia kruhsiana subsp.

multisecta, commonly known as the multisection wormwood, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region. It features aromatic, silver-gray foliage with finely divided, linear leaves that give it a feathery appearance.

The plant produces small, yellowish-green flower heads that appear in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its essential oils have been used historically for their medicinal and aromatic properties.
