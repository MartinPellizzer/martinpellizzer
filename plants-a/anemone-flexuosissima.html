Anemone flexuosissima

Anemone flexuosissima, commonly known as the flexible anemone, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the eastern United States.

It features showy, white to pinkish flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has slender, branched stems that often arch or trail along the ground. Its leaves are trifoliate, with three deeply lobed leaflets that are hairy beneath.

Anemone flexuosissima thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland gardens.
