Arenaria modesta var. africana

Arenaria modesta var. africana, commonly known as African sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It typically thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in coastal or arid regions. The plant features small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink, blooming in late spring to early summer. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to withstand harsh environmental conditions.
