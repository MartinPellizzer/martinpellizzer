Arenaria tmolea var. stenophylla

Arenaria tmolea var. stenophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leaf sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to arid regions of North America.

It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is adapted to dry, sunny environments. The plant features small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in spring. Its leaves are narrow and succulent, helping to conserve moisture in harsh conditions.

This variety is often found in disturbed soils and is considered a hardy, drought-tolerant species.
