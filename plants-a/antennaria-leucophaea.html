Antennaria leucophaea

Antennaria leucophaea, commonly known as the white-leaved mouse-ear, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

The plant features small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring, often appearing in clusters. Its leaves are typically covered in fine, white hairs, giving it a distinctive, silvery appearance.
