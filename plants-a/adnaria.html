Adnaria

Adnaria, commonly known as the sea hibiscus, is a flowering plant belonging to the Malvaceae family.

It is native to coastal regions of the Mediterranean and the Caribbean, where it thrives in sandy or rocky soils. The plant features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from pink to red, often with a yellow center. Its leaves are alternate, heart-shaped, and covered in fine hairs.

Adnaria is often used in ornamental gardening due to its hardiness and attractive blooms.
