Astragalus kustanaicus

Astragalus kustanaicus, commonly known as Kustanai milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its small, yellow pea-like flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments. It has compound leaves and produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
