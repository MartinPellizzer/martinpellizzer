Alternanthera achyrantha var. parvifolia

Alternanthera achyrantha var. parvifolia, commonly known as the small-leaved Alternanthera, is a tropical perennial plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, colorful foliage that can range from red to purple, pink, and green. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow, which appear in clusters along the stems. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for water gardens and containers.

Its ornamental value lies primarily in its foliage, which can be used to create visually striking displays in aquatic and terrestrial settings.
