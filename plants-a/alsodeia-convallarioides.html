Alsodeia convallarioides

Alsodeia convallarioides, commonly known as the sweet pimpernel, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with small, heart-shaped leaves and clusters of white or pinkish flowers. The plant produces small, round berries that are edible but have a mildly tart flavor. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or disturbed habitats.

Alsodeia convallarioides is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
