Aristea anceps

Aristea anceps, commonly known as the silver carpet plant, is a perennial succulent native to South Africa.

It features dense, mat-forming growth with narrow, silvery-gray leaves that are covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink to purple during the spring and summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its compact size and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
