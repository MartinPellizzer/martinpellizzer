Agathosma marifolia

Agathosma marifolia, commonly known as Cape aster, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant is shrub-like, with grey-green, aromatic leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to dry, coastal environments.

Agathosma marifolia is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
