Arachnorchis tessellata

Arachnorchis tessellata, commonly known as the tessellated spider orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to southeastern Australia.

It typically grows in woodland and grassland habitats, thriving in well-drained soils. The plant features a slender, erect stem with a single or few flowers that are usually pale green to yellowish in color. Its unique floral structure includes a hairy, spidery labellum that gives it its common name.

Arachnorchis tessellata is a rare and protected species, often found in specific microhabitats with particular soil and light conditions.
