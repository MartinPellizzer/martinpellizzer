Arenaria kansuensis

Arenaria kansuensis, commonly known as the Kansu sandwort, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of China.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that thrives in sandy and rocky soils, often found in desert and semi-desert environments. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are typically narrow and succulent, adapted to conserve water in harsh conditions.

Arenaria kansuensis plays a role in stabilizing soil and preventing erosion in its native habitat.
