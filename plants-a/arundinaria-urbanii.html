Arundinaria urbanii

Arundinaria urbanii, commonly known as the Chinese bamboo or golden bamboo, is a species of grass native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems that can grow up to several meters in height. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically arranged in panicles. Its leaves are long and narrow, with a smooth texture and a slightly bluish-green color.

Arundinaria urbanii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in landscaping and traditional Chinese medicine.
