Azalea mollis var. glabrior

Azalea mollis var. glabrior, commonly known as the smooth azalea, is a deciduous shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It features dense, upright growth with showy, clustered flowers that range from white to pink and red. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are smooth and glossy, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

Azalea mollis var. glabrior is valued for its early spring blooming and is often used in landscaping for its vibrant floral display.
