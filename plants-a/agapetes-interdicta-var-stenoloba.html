Agapetes interdicta var. stenoloba

Agapetes interdicta var. stenoloba, commonly known as the narrow-leaf silvertonia, is a species of evergreen shrub native to the Andean regions of South America.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and blueberries, and is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit.
