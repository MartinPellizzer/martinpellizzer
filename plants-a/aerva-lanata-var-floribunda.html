Aerva lanata var. floribunda

Aerva lanata var. floribunda, commonly known as the clustered cowhage, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a herbaceous annual or perennial species characterized by its erect, branched stems and hairy foliage. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which are typically borne in the axils of the leaves. Its leaves are palmately lobed, and the plant is often found in disturbed soils or along roadsides.

Aerva lanata var. floribunda is known for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional medicine for various ailments.
