Aldama seemannii

Aldama seemannii, commonly known as the Mexican orchid, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in shaded, humid environments. Its roots are fibrous, and it thrives in well-drained soil.

Aldama seemannii is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
