Ardisia rubiginosa

Ardisia rubiginosa, commonly known as the red-rooted evergreen shrub, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of small, white, fragrant flowers. The plant produces bright red berries that are an important food source for various wildlife species. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in warm climates.
