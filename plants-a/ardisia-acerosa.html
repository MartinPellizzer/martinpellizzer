Ardisia acerosa

Ardisia acerosa, commonly known as the glossy buckeye, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It features elliptical, glossy dark green leaves that are opposite in arrangement. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers that are typically followed by red, berry-like fruits. Ardisia acerosa is known for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and seasonal blooms.

It prefers shaded to partially sunny conditions and well-drained soil.
