Alternanthera nodiflora var. linearifolia

Alternanthera nodiflora var. linearifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf Alternanthera, is a herbaceous plant native to South America.

It features slender, linear leaves that are often arranged alternately along the stem. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, typically in shades of pink or white. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight.

This variety is popular in aquatic and bog gardens due to its ornamental foliage and adaptability.
