Acaena pinnatifida subsp. aculeata

Acaena pinnatifida subsp.

aculeata, commonly known as the spiny acaena, is a shrub native to New Zealand. It is characterized by its dense, spiny growth habit and small, oval-shaped leaves.

The plant produces bright red berries that are an important food source for native birds. It thrives in a variety of soil types but prefers well-drained conditions. Acaena pinnatifida subsp.

aculeata is often found in forest margins, scrublands, and rocky areas.
