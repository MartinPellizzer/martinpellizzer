Aeginetia multiflora

Aeginetia multiflora, commonly known as the many-flowered aeginetia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in late winter to early spring. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Aeginetia multiflora is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.
