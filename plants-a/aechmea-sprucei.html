Aechmea sprucei

Aechmea sprucei, commonly known as the pink pineapple bromeliad, is a striking epiphytic plant native to Central and South America.

It features a rosette of broad, waxy, and often colorful leaves that form a central cup to collect rainwater. The plant's foliage is typically green with a pink or red hue, especially along the leaf margins. It produces a striking inflorescence with red bracts and small white flowers that bloom in the summer.

Aechmea sprucei is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its adaptability and ornamental appeal.
