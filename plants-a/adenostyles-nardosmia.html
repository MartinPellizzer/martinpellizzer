Adenostyles nardosmia

Adenostyles nardosmia, commonly known as the fragrant bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its bell-shaped, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of blue or purple. The plant has a compact growth habit with narrow, grass-like leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Adenostyles nardosmia is often cultivated for its ornamental value and pleasant scent.
