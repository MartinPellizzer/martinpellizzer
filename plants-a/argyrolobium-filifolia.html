Argyrolobium filifolia

Argyrolobium filifolia, commonly known as the silky burr vetch.

It is a low-growing annual legume native to southern Africa, characterized by its finely hairy stems and small, yellow flowers. The plant produces small, globular pods that contain several seeds, which are often dispersed by wind. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.

Its botanical features make it a valuable component of ecological studies in its native habitat.
