Asplenium ikenoi

Asplenium ikenoi, commonly known as the Ikenoi spleenwort, is a small, terrestrial fern belonging to the Aspleniaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped leaves that are typically less than 10 centimeters in length. The plant produces spores in clusters on the underside of its fronds, a feature typical of ferns. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices.

Asplenium ikenoi is often found in temperate regions and is noted for its ability to tolerate low light conditions.
