Aeonium lindleyi subsp. lindleyi

Aeonium lindleyi subsp.

lindleyi, commonly known as the silver aeonium, is a succulent plant native to the Canary Islands. It features rosettes of thick, fleshy, silver-green leaves that are covered in fine hairs.

The plant produces upright flowering stalks that bear yellow flowers in the spring. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with full sun exposure.

Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
