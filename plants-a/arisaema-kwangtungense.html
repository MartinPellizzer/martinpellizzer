Arisaema kwangtungense

Arisaema kwangtungense, commonly known as the Guangdong arrowhead, is a flowering plant native to southern China.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its distinctive arrow-shaped leaves and striking spadix inflorescence. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense colonies.

The flowers are usually greenish-yellow, with a small, fleshy spathe that encloses the spadix.
