Arundarbor

Arundarbor, commonly known as the silverleaf elder, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to temperate regions.

It features compound leaves that are typically gray-green in color, with a distinctive silvery underside. The plant produces small, clustered white flowers in the spring, followed by dark purple berries in the fall. Arundarbor thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

It is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
