Alyssum cuneifolium var. laxiusculum

Alyssum cuneifolium var. laxiusculum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved alyssum, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the western United States.

It features small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pale pink, typically blooming in spring. The foliage is characterized by narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. This variety is well-suited for rock gardens and as a ground cover due to its compact growth habit.

It prefers well-drained soils and full sun, making it a versatile choice for xeriscaping.
