Aloe bainesii

Aloe bainesii, commonly known as the Cape aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, with a waxy coating that helps it retain moisture. The plant produces tall, branched flower stalks that bear yellow-orange flowers, which bloom in the summer. Aloe bainesii is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine and as a source of gel for topical applications.
