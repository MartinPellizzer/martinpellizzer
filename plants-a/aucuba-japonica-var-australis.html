Aucuba japonica var. australis

Aucuba japonica var. australis, commonly known as the Australian aucuba, is a compact evergreen shrub native to Japan.

It features glossy, dark green, ovate leaves with prominent yellow veins, creating a striking visual contrast. The plant produces small, inconspicuous yellow-green flowers in the spring, followed by red berries that are attractive to birds. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for shaded garden areas.

Its dense foliage and year-round color make it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.
