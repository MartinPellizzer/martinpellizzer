Abutilon wissadifolium

Abutilon wissadifolium, commonly known as the "velvetleaf abutilon," is a flowering plant native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its fuzzy, velvety leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces showy, lantern-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Its unique foliage and vibrant blooms make it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
