Adonanthe tianschanica

Adonanthe tianschanica, commonly known as the Tianshan bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the Tianshan mountain range in Central Asia.

It is a perennial herb with slender stems and small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from blue to purple. The plant produces small, oval berries that are typically green when immature and turn red when mature. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to the cold, high-altitude environments of its native habitat.

Adonanthe tianschanica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens with suitable conditions.
