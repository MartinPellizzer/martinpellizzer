Antirrhinum parcinum

Antirrhinum parcinum, commonly known as the narrowleaf snapdragon, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and clusters of bright red or orange tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is often found in coastal areas. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Antirrhinum parcinum is a perennial species that blooms from late spring to early summer.
