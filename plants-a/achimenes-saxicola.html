Achimenes saxicola

Achimenes saxicola, commonly known as the rock achimenes, is a tropical flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that come in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in warm, humid environments. Its foliage consists of heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are often covered in a fine, white powdery coating.

Achimenes saxicola is a popular ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
