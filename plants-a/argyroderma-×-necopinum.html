Argyroderma × necopinum

Argyroderma × necopinum, commonly known as the hybrid argyroderma.

It is a cross between two species of the genus Argyroderma, which are native to South America. This plant typically exhibits characteristics of both parent species, including its silver-white leaves and showy yellow flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and by plant enthusiasts.
