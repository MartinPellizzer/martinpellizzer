Arthromeris notabilis

Arthromeris notabilis, commonly known as the showy glassworts, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Amaranthaceae.

It is characterized by its succulent, fleshy stems and leaves that store water, adapting it to arid environments. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish or reddish in color. It is native to regions of southern Africa, particularly in South Africa and Namibia.

Arthromeris notabilis is often found in coastal and sandy habitats, where it thrives in well-drained soils.
