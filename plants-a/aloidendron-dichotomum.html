Aloidendron dichotomum

Aloidendron dichotomum, commonly known as the mountain aloes, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are fleshy and covered in a waxy cuticle, which helps it retain water in arid environments.
