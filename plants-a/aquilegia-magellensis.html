Aquilegia magellensis

Aquilegia magellensis, commonly known as the Magellanic crownfern, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its striking, spurred flowers that come in shades of red, yellow, and orange. Aquilegia magellensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting pollinators.
