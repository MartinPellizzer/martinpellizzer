Alchemilla fissa var. argentea

Alchemilla fissa var. argentea, commonly known as silver-spleenwort, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It features basal rosettes of narrow, silver-gray leaves that are covered in fine, silky hairs. The plant produces upright flowering stems bearing small, greenish-yellow flowers in early summer. It thrives in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Its foliage is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and its ability to tolerate wet conditions.
