Atriplex parishii var. parishii

Atriplex parishii var. parishii, commonly known as saltbush, is a shrub native to arid and semi-arid regions of the southwestern United States.

It is characterized by its small, scale-like leaves that reduce water loss, making it well adapted to saline and drought conditions. The plant produces small, greenish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in desert scrub and salt marsh environments.

Atriplex parishii var. parishii plays an important role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various desert wildlife.
