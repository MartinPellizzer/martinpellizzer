Aloe glauca var. rhodacantha

Aloe glauca var. rhodacantha, commonly known as the red aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of fleshy, blue-green leaves with red or pinkish margins. The plant produces tall, branching inflorescences with yellow flowers during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with full sun exposure.

Its striking coloration makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
