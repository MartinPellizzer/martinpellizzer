Artemisia tianschanica

Artemisia tianschanica, commonly known as the Tian Shan wormwood, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its aromatic, silver-gray foliage and small, yellowish-green flowers that appear in late summer. The plant thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found at high elevations. Its leaves contain essential oils, which contribute to its strong, bitter scent.

Artemisia tianschanica is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
