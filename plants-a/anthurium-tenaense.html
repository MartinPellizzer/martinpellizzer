Anthurium tenaense

Anthurium tenaense, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red spadix and dark green, heart-shaped leaves. The plant belongs to the Araceae family, which is known for its unique inflorescence structure consisting of a spadix and spathe. Anthurium tenaense thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical landscapes.
