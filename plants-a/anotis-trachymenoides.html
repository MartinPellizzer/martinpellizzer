Anotis trachymenoides

Anotis trachymenoides, commonly known as the narrow-leaved everlasting, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and heathlands. Its small, daisy-like flowers are usually pale blue or white, and it produces fluffy, wind-dispersed seeds.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
