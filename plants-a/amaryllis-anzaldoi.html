Amaryllis anzaldoi

Amaryllis anzaldoi, commonly known as the Anzaldo's amaryllis, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers. The plant typically produces clusters of bright red or orange blooms during the summer months. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Amaryllis anzaldoi is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
