Aechmea × fulgoramosa

Aechmea × fulgoramosa, commonly known as the flame bromeliad, is a hybrid bromeliad native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, torch-like inflorescence that can range from red to orange, giving it its striking appearance. The plant features a rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves that collect water, forming a central pool that supports various microorganisms and small animals. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardens.

Its unique appearance and ability to thrive in a variety of conditions make it a desirable ornamental plant.
