Aspidium thelypteris var. squamigerum

Aspidium thelypteris var. squamigerum, commonly known as the silver fern, is a variant of the common water fern.

It is a fern belonging to the family Athyriaceae, characterized by its distinctive fronds with a silvery, scaly appearance on the underside. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky areas. This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
