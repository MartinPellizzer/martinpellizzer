Anguria kunthiana

Anguria kunthiana, commonly known as the Mexican watermelon or wild watermelon, is a species native to Central and South America.

It is a member of the Cucurbitaceae family, which includes other cucurbits like cucumbers and squash. The plant is characterized by its trailing growth habit, hairy stems, and large, lobed leaves.
