Aster potosinus

Aster potosinus, commonly known as the Potosi aster, is a flowering plant native to the highlands of South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant produces showy blue to purple flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and alpine settings.

Due to its ornamental value and unique appearance, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by plant enthusiasts.
