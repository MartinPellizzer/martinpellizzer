Anthurium barbanum

Anthurium barbanum, commonly known as the black anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its dark, velvety leaves and striking, spadix-shaped inflorescence. The plant belongs to the Araceae family, which is known for its unique floral structures. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical landscapes.
