Adenostyles alpina var. macrocephala

Adenostyles alpina var. macrocephala, commonly known as the Alpine Adenostyles, is a flowering plant native to the alpine regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces showy, pink to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils at high altitudes, where it thrives in cool, moist conditions. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it is often found in mountainous areas with limited soil cover.

This variety is noted for its resilience to harsh environmental conditions, making it a hardy species in alpine ecosystems.
