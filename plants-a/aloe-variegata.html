Aloe variegata

Aloe variegata, commonly known as the "Zebra Aloe," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features striking, narrow, light green leaves with distinct white or yellowish stripes, giving it its zebra-like appearance. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow flowers during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with plenty of sunlight.

Aloe variegata is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique foliage and low maintenance requirements.
