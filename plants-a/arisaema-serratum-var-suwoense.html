Arisaema serratum var. suwoense

Arisaema serratum var. suwoense, commonly known as the Suwo Mountain Arisaema, is a herbaceous plant native to China.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its distinctive spathe and spadix inflorescence. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as mountain forests. Its leaves are arrow-shaped and have serrated edges, giving it a unique appearance.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and has some traditional medicinal uses in local cultures.
