Apatostelis leinigii

Apatostelis leinigii, commonly known as the New Zealand flatworm, is a species of flatworm native to New Zealand.

It is a free-living, terrestrial flatworm that thrives in moist, forested environments. This species is notable for its role in controlling populations of invasive earthworms, which are considered a threat to native ecosystems. Apatostelis leinigii has a slender, translucent body and can grow up to 15 centimeters in length.
