Aloe perfoliata var. suberecta

Aloe perfoliata var. suberecta, commonly known as the upright aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, gray-green leaves that are arranged vertically, giving it a compact and upright growth habit. The leaves are lined with small teeth along the edges, and the plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow flowers. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright sunlight, making it suitable for arid or semi-arid environments.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
