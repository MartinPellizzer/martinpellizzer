Alsine tenuifolia var. genuina

Alsine tenuifolia var. genuina, commonly known as slender alsike, is a flowering plant in the family Caryophyllaceae.

It is native to the eastern United States and thrives in moist, sandy soils along streambanks and wetlands. The plant produces small, white or pink flowers that bloom in early summer. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often appearing in whorls around the stems.

It is an important species for pollinators and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
