Atylosia pubescens var. mollis

Atylosia pubescens var. mollis, commonly known as the soft-hairy goldenrod, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its yellow composite flowers and hairy, gray-green foliage. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and thrives in full sun. It produces small, wind-pollinated flowers that attract a variety of pollinators, including bees and butterflies.

The species is known for its drought tolerance and is often used in native plant landscaping.
