Aeschynomene micrantha

Aeschynomene micrantha, commonly known as small-flowered aeschynomene, is a herbaceous annual plant native to tropical regions of Africa and the Americas.

The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers that are typically arranged in racemes. Aeschynomene micrantha is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in poor nutrient conditions.
