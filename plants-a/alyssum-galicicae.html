Alyssum galicicae

Alyssum galicicae, commonly known as the Balkan alyssum, is a flowering plant native to the Balkan Peninsula.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. The plant produces small, spherical seed pods that contain numerous tiny seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.

Alyssum galicicae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
