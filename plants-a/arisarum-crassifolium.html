Arisarum crassifolium

Arisarum crassifolium, commonly known as the broadleaf arisarum, is a unique plant belonging to the Araceae family.

It is characterized by its large, broad leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces striking inflorescences with a spathe and spadix, typical of the arum family. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in temperate regions.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
