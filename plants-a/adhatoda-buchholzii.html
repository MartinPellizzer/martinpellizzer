Adhatoda buchholzii

Adhatoda buchholzii, commonly known as the African arrowroot or Indian Trumpet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa and southern Asia.

It is a perennial shrub that grows up to 2 meters tall, characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers. The plant produces small, round fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forest understories or along riverbanks.
