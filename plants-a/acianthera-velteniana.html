Acianthera velteniana

Acianthera velteniana, commonly known as the Velten's orchid, is a species of orchid native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It is a terrestrial herb with a short, hairy stem and a single, lance-shaped leaf. The plant produces a single, showy flower with a distinctive greenish-blue color and a yellow lip. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a delicate and endangered species.
