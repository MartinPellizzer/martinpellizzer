Aloe × platylepis

Aloe × platylepis, commonly known as the hybrid aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Aloe arborescens and Aloe africana. The plant features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with white or red markings. It produces yellow flowers in clusters during the summer months.

Aloe × platylepis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in well-draining soil.
