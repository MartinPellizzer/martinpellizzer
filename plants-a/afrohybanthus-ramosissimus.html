Afrohybanthus ramosissimus

Afrohybanthus ramosissimus, commonly known as the African hybanthus, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact shrub form, with dense, branched stems that give it a bushy appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant yellow flowers clustered in racemes, which bloom during the warmer months. Its leaves are typically ovate or elliptical, with a slightly hairy texture.

Afrohybanthus ramosissimus is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.
