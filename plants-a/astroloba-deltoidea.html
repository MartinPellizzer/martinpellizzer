Astroloba deltoidea

Astroloba deltoidea, commonly known as the delta-shaped astroloba, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family, which is known for its unique adaptations to arid environments. The plant features a basal rosette of fleshy, triangular leaves that are often tinged with purple or red, especially in full sun. It produces striking, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the summer months.

Due to its compact size and ornamental appeal, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and arid landscape designs.
