Abutilon rufinerve

Abutilon rufinerve, commonly known as the red-veined abutilon, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 3 meters in height, characterized by its hairy stems and broad, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, lantern-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, often with a red or purple throat. Its fibrous bark and soft, fuzzy texture make it a popular ornamental plant in gardens.

Abutilon rufinerve thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
