Aspidistra globulifera

Aspidistra globulifera, commonly known as the globe-leaf aspidistra, is a member of the Araceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including the Philippines and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its dark green, glossy, and often waxy leaves that are typically broad and elongated. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or white, followed by dark blue or black berries.

Aspidistra globulifera is valued for its ornamental foliage and is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant.
