Adesmia adrianii

Adesmia adrianii, commonly known as the Adrian's burr legume, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Argentina and Chile.

Its leaves are typically trifoliate, consisting of three oval-shaped leaflets, and the plant is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.

Adesmia adrianii is often found in dry grasslands and along riverbanks, where it plays a role in stabilizing soil and supporting local biodiversity.
