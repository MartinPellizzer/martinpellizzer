Anisostachya elytraria

Anisostachya elytraria, commonly known as the red grass or red oat grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, reddish stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences that are typically reddish in color, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and open woodlands.

Anisostachya elytraria is valued for its drought tolerance and ability to stabilize soil in arid environments.
