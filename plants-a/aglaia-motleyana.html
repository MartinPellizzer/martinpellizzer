Aglaia motleyana

Aglaia motleyana, commonly known as the yellow mangosteen, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Meliaceae and is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and fragrant, yellowish-green flowers. The fruit is a round, segmented berry that turns from green to yellow when mature. The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in local medicine.
