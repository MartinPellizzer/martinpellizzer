Arundarbor maxima

Arundarbor maxima, commonly known as the giant sunspire, is a striking tropical plant characterized by its towering, elongated stems that can reach heights of up to 30 feet.

Its leaves are large, waxy, and often display a gradient of colors from deep green to vibrant gold. The plant produces showy, clustered flowers in shades of yellow and orange, which bloom during the warm season. It thrives in humid, well-drained soils and requires ample sunlight to flourish.

Due to its size and ornamental value, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and tropical landscapes.
