Astyposanthes leiocarpa

Astyposanthes leiocarpa, commonly known as the silky tree or silky wood, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, smooth, and often waxy bark, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, arranged in clusters. Its leaves are usually alternate and have a velvety texture.
