Aleuritopteris stenochlamys

Aleuritopteris stenochlamys, commonly known as the narrow-chalmed water fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds that give it a lacy appearance. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or along stream banks. Its reproductive structures, including sori, are located on the underside of the fronds.

Aleuritopteris stenochlamys is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
