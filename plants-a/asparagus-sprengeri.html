Asparagus sprengeri

Asparagus sprengeri, commonly known as the silver asparagus or evergreen asparagus, is a perennial flowering plant native to South Africa.

It features delicate, fern-like foliage with a silvery-blue hue due to dense, fine hairs on its leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in early spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its drought tolerance and ornamental value make it a versatile addition to many garden settings.
