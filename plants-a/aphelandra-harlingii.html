Aphelandra harlingii

Aphelandra harlingii, commonly known as the Harling's torch plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the Caribbean.

It features striking, elongated leaves with a dark green upper surface and a lighter, often variegated underside. The plant produces vibrant, pendulous inflorescences with yellow-orange bracts that resemble torches, giving it its common name. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and high humidity.

Aphelandra harlingii is popular as a decorative plant due to its ornamental foliage and unique floral display.
