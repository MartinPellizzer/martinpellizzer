Ardisia fusca var. glabrata

Ardisia fusca var. glabrata, commonly known as the smooth ardisia, is a shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are typically smooth to the touch, giving it its varietal name. The plant produces small, white flowers followed by bright red berries, which are an important food source for birds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden landscapes.
