Adnaria Thymelaeoides

Adnaria thyme, scientifically known as Adnaria thymelaeoides, is a unique and lesser-known plant species native to specific regions of southern Europe. This aromatic herb is often mistaken for common thyme due to its similar appearance and fragrance. Despite its small size, Adnaria thyme plays an important ecological role in its native habitat. It is valued for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies for centuries. Due to habitat loss and overharvesting, Adnaria thyme is now considered a vulnerable species.
