Andropterum

Andropterum, commonly known as the common hop, is a climbing plant belonging to the Cannabaceae family.

It is characterized by its opposite, heart-shaped leaves and small, greenish-yellow flowers that appear in clusters. Andropterum is native to Europe and parts of Asia, and it thrives in moist, temperate environments.
