Aubrieta deltoidea var. intermedia

Aubrieta deltoidea var. intermedia, commonly known as the silverleaf aubrieta, is a low-growing, mat-forming perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, typically from March to May. The plant has delicate, fern-like foliage that is covered in a fine, silvery-white pubescence, giving it its common name. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

This variety is noted for its hardiness and ability to tolerate coastal conditions.
