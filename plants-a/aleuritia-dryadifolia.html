Aleuritia dryadifolia

Aleuritia dryadifolia, commonly known as the silverlace tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its delicate, lace-like white flowers that appear in early spring. The plant has compound leaves that are finely divided, giving it a feathery appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it suitable for ornamental gardens.

The fruit is a small, yellowish drupe that matures in late summer.
