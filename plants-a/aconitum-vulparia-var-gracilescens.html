Aconitum vulparia var. gracilescens

Aconitum vulparia var. gracilescens, commonly known as the slender monkshood, is a herbaceous perennial native to parts of Europe.

It features striking blue to purple flowers arranged in a helmet-like shape, typical of the genus Aconitum. The plant has dark green, heart-shaped leaves with a serrated edge, and its stems are usually hairy. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

However, it is highly toxic and should be handled with care.
