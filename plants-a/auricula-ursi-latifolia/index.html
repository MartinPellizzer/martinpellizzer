Auricula-Ursi Latifolia

Auricula-ursi latifolia is a rare and fascinating plant native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas. It is known for its striking, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. This species thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in rocky crevices. Its unique appearance and ecological significance make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists. Despite its beauty, it remains a poorly studied plant with limited cultivation outside its natural habitat.
