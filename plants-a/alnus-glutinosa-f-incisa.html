Alnus glutinosa f. incisa

Alnus glutinosa f. incisa, commonly known as the narrow-leaved alder, is a shrub or small tree native to parts of Europe and western Asia.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found near water sources such as streams and wetlands.
