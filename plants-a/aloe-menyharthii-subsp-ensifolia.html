Aloe menyharthii subsp. ensifolia

Aloe menyharthii subsp.

ensifolia, commonly known as the sword aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa. It is characterized by its tall, narrow, grass-like leaves that are often blue-green in color.

The plant produces tall inflorescences with yellow flowers, which bloom in summer. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil.

Due to its unique appearance, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
