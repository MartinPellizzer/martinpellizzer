Aerangis oligantha

Aerangis oligantha, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to parts of Africa, particularly South Africa, where it thrives in rocky and sandy soils. The plant features a compact, upright growth habit with narrow, grass-like leaves. Its flowers are small, white or pale green, and are arranged in a spike, giving it a delicate appearance.

Aerangis oligantha is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
