Agathosma decora var. pseudohybrida

Agathosma decora var. pseudohybrida, commonly known as the Cape geranium, is a succulent shrub native to South Africa.

It features small, aromatic leaves that are often used in traditional medicine and aromatherapy. The plant produces clusters of pink or purple flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Its unique appearance and medicinal properties make it a popular choice for both ornamental and herbal purposes.
