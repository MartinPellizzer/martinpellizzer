Acnistus breviflorus

Acnistus breviflorus, commonly known as the short-flowered acnistus, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows in coastal and sandy habitats. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the warmer months. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and often covered in fine hairs.

Acnistus breviflorus is noted for its adaptability to arid conditions and is sometimes used in ornamental gardening.
