Asteranthemum dahuricum

Asteranthemum dahuricum, commonly known as the Siberian aster, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the cold climates of Asia.

It features showy, daisy-like flowers with purple to blue petals and yellow centers, blooming in late summer to early autumn. The plant has a compact growth habit with upright stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or alpine settings.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long-lasting blooms and frost resistance.
