Arisaema lihengianum

Arisaema lihengianum, commonly known as the Liheng arrowhead, is a herbaceous plant native to China.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its distinctive arrow-shaped leaves. The plant produces an inflorescence consisting of a spadix surrounded by a modified leaf called a spathe. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its unique morphology and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and valuable species in botanical studies.
