Arisaema zhui

Arisaema zhui, commonly known as the Zhui arisaema, is a rare and striking plant native to China.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its unique inflorescence structure consisting of a spadix surrounded by a leaf-like spathe. The plant produces a single, large, yellowish-green spathe that encloses a spadix with both male and female flowers. Its botanical features include a tuberous rhizome and heart-shaped leaves with a waxy surface.

Arisaema zhui is often found in moist, shaded environments and is valued for its ornamental and medicinal properties.
