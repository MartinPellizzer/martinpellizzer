Alsine mesogitana

Alsine mesogitana, commonly known as the Mediterranean sandgrass, is a flowering plant belonging to the Amaranthaceae family.

It is a low-growing annual herb that thrives in sandy and coastal environments. The plant produces small, greenish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are usually linear and covered with fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.

Alsine mesogitana is adapted to arid conditions and is often found in dunes and other dry habitats.
