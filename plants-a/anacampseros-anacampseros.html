Anacampseros anacampseros

Anacampseros anacampseros, commonly known as the desert star or stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to arid regions of Africa and the Middle East.

It features a basal rosette of fleshy, blue-green leaves that store water, adapting to its harsh environment. The plant produces small, star-shaped yellow flowers during the warmer months, typically in spring or summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
