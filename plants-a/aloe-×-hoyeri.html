Aloe × hoyeri

Aloe × hoyeri, commonly known as the hybrid aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with red or orange tips. The plant produces tall, branching inflorescences with yellow flowers, which bloom in the spring. Aloe × hoyeri is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some medicinal uses.
