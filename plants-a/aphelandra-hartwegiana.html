Aphelandra hartwegiana

Aphelandra hartwegiana, commonly known as the golden torch plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Arecaceae family, which includes palms, and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnately compound leaves. Aphelandra hartwegiana is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
