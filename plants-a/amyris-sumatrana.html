Amyris sumatrana

Amyris sumatrana, commonly known as the Sumatran amyris, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia, particularly Indonesia.

Amyris sumatrana typically grows in tropical rainforests and thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Its botanical features make it an important species in both ecological and commercial contexts.
