Abronia maritima

Abronia maritima, commonly known as the sea blite, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of the Mediterranean and parts of Europe.

It is a low-growing perennial that thrives in sandy or gravelly soils, often found in dunes and coastal grasslands. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are succulent and fleshy, adapted to retain moisture in arid environments.

Abronia maritima is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in stabilizing coastal dunes.
