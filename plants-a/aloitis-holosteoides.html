Aloitis holosteoides

Aloitis holosteoides, commonly known as the Zebra Plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features striking, upright stems with distinctive zebra-like patterns of light and dark green. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
