Aporum sambasanum

Aporum sambasanum, commonly known as the Sambas orchid, is a rare and unique orchid species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically feature vibrant colors such as red, purple, and yellow. The plant has a complex root system that helps it thrive in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are usually broad and lance-shaped, adapted for efficient photosynthesis in low-light conditions.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, Aporum sambasanum is often found in protected areas and is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
