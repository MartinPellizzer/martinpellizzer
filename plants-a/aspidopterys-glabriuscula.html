Aspidopterys glabriuscula

Aspidopterys glabriuscula, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its smooth, dark green leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous yellow flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. Its fruit is a small, three-lobed capsule that contains several seeds.

Aspidopterys glabriuscula is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories and along streambanks.
