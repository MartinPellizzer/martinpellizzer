Adiantum aubertii

Adiantum aubertii, commonly known as the maidenhair fern, is a delicate and elegant fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features finely divided, feathery fronds that are typically dark green and have a glossy appearance. The plant thrives in shady, humid environments and requires well-draining, acidic soil. It is known for its ability to thrive indoors with proper care, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Adiantum aubertii is also notable for its ornamental value and its ability to purify the air.
