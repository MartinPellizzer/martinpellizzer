Anthurium kareniae

Anthurium kareniae, commonly known as the Karenia anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive spathe and spadix inflorescence. The plant features vibrant, heart-shaped leaves that are often green with red or purple veins. It thrives in humid, warm environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium kareniae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated as a houseplant.
