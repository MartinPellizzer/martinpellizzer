Astragalus multicaulis var. ajanensis

Astragalus multicaulis var. ajanensis, commonly known as the Ajanensis milkvetch, is a perennial herb native to arid and semi-arid regions of central Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant is adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments with deep root systems that help it access water from deeper soil layers.

It plays a role in soil stabilization and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
