Atriplex nummularia var. monumentalis

Atriplex nummularia var. monumentalis, commonly known as the silver dust plant, is a low-growing perennial succulent native to the western United States.

It features small, rounded, silver-gray leaves that are densely packed, giving it a distinctive, mat-like appearance. The plant produces yellow flowers in the spring, which are clustered in dense, spherical inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it well-suited for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its drought tolerance and ornamental value make it a popular choice for arid landscape designs.
