Actinomeris squarrosa var. flava

Actinomeris squarrosa var. flava, commonly known as the yellow star moss, is a species of liverwort belonging to the family Pottiaceae.

It is characterized by its dense, cushion-like growth form and bright yellowish-green coloration. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. This variety is notable for its vibrant color and is often used in horticultural displays due to its aesthetic appeal.
