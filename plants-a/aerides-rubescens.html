Aerides rubescens

Aerides rubescens, commonly known as the red-stemmed orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its erect, hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often reddish in color. The plant produces fragrant, showy flowers in shades of pink and white, typically in clusters at the base of the stem. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Aerides rubescens is a popular ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
