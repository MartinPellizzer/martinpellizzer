Argemone gracilenta

Argemone gracilenta, commonly known as slender pricklypoppy, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain seeds with a distinctive oil-rich coating. It is adapted to arid environments and is often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides.
