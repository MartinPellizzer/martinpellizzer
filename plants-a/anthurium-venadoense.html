Anthurium venadoense

Anthurium venadoense, commonly known as the Venadoense arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive spathe and spadix inflorescence. The plant features large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. Its flowers are small and clustered, often hidden within the spathe, which is usually brightly colored.

Anthurium venadoense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.
