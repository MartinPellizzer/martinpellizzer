Aspidistra elatior var. vietnamensis

Aspidistra elatior var. vietnamensis, commonly known as the Vietnamese evergreen aspidistra, is a hardy, evergreen perennial native to Southeast Asia.

It features dark green, broad, lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and upright, forming a dense, compact clump. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening. It has a slow growth rate and is relatively drought-tolerant once established.

The foliage remains evergreen throughout the year, adding a lush, tropical appearance to its surroundings.
