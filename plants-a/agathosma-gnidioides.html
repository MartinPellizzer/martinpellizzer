Agathosma gnidioides

Agathosma gnidioides, commonly known as African germander, is a small shrub native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, small, greenish-yellow flowers. The plant has small, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is drought-tolerant, making it suitable for rocky or dry landscapes.

Its medicinal properties have been traditionally used in African herbal medicine.
