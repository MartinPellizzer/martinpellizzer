Acianthera minima

Acianthera minima, commonly known as the miniature orchid or small acianthera, is a delicate flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its small, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. It has slender, upright stems and lance-shaped leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs.

Due to its small size and ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant or in specialized gardens.
