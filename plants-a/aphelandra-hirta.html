Aphelandra hirta

Aphelandra hirta, commonly known as the velvet leaf or silver vine, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, waxy, dark green leaves that have a velvety texture. The plant produces small, fragrant, white flowers that bloom in clusters, often with a subtle yellowish tint. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Aphelandra hirta is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and unique appearance.
