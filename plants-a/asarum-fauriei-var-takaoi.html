Asarum fauriei var. takaoi

Asarum fauriei var. takaoi, commonly known as Takao asarum, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Japan.

It belongs to the Aristolochiaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped, reddish-brown leaves and small, spathaceous flowers. The plant thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest undergrowth. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread slowly and form dense mats.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
