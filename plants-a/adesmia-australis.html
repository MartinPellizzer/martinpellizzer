Adesmia australis

Adesmia australis, commonly known as the Australian burr vetch, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a low-growing annual herb that typically reaches a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the warmer months. Its leaves are trifoliate, consisting of three oval-shaped leaflets.

Adesmia australis is known for its ability to thrive in arid and semi-arid environments, making it a hardy species adapted to dry conditions.
