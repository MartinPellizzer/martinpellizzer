Astrantia major var. illyrica

Astrantia major var. illyrica, commonly known as the Illyrian masterwort, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Balkan Peninsula.

It features a basal rosette of lance-shaped, dark green leaves with a hairy texture. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale pink flowers arranged in umbels, which bloom from late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in native gardens.
