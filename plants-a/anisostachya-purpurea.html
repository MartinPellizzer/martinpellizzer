Anisostachya purpurea

Anisostachya purpurea, commonly known as purplegrass, is a perennial grass native to southern Africa.

It features slender, upright stems that can grow up to 1.5 meters in height. The plant produces panicles of small, purple to pink flowers that bloom in late summer. Its foliage is narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue.

Anisostachya purpurea is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
