Agave schottii var. treleasei

Agave schottii var. treleasei, commonly known as the trelease's agave, is a succulent plant native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped growth pattern and thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with small, white flowers that bloom in the spring. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it suitable for arid and semi-arid environments.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice for xeriscaping and rock gardens.
