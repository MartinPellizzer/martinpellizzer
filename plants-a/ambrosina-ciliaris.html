Ambrosina ciliaris

Ambrosina ciliaris, commonly known as the silverleaf ambrosia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features silver-gray foliage with finely toothed margins, giving it a distinctive appearance. It produces small yellow flowers that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Ambrosina ciliaris is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
