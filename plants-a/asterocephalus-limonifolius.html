Asterocephalus limonifolius

Asterocephalus limonifolius, commonly known as the lemon-leaf aster, is a unique plant native to the arid regions of South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads and medicinal properties. The plant features narrow, lemon-scented leaves that are often used in traditional herbal medicine. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, arranged in a cluster at the top of the plant.

Asterocephalus limonifolius is well adapted to harsh environments, with a deep root system that helps it survive in drought conditions.
