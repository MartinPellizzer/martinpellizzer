Agathosma glabrata var. eckloniana

Agathosma glabrata var. eckloniana, commonly known as African sage, is a perennial shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its aromatic, gray-green leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in spring. The plant is well adapted to dry, rocky soils and is often found in fynbos vegetation. It is widely used in traditional medicine for its purported anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties.

Its essential oils are also valued in aromatherapy and perfumery.
