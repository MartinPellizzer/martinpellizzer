Asterocephalus arenarius

Asterocephalus arenarius, commonly known as the coastal aster.

It is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of southern Africa. The plant is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and small, star-shaped yellow flowers. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in dune environments.

Asterocephalus arenarius is adapted to withstand harsh coastal conditions, including salt spray and strong winds.
