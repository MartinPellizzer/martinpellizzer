Aloe antandroi

Aloe antandroi, commonly known as the Cape aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with white or yellowish markings. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with bright orange or yellow flowers. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its leaves.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional medicinal uses.
