Anthurium pichinchae var. rigescens

Anthurium pichinchae var. rigescens, commonly known as the Pichincha anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the Andes region of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a waxy texture. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-like inflorescences that range in color from red to orange, often surrounded by a colorful spathe. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

This variety is popular among plant enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
