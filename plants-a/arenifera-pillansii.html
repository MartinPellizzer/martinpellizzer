Arenifera pillansii

Arenifera pillansii, commonly known as the Karoo sandthorn, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows in arid and semi-arid regions, adapted to survive in sandy soils. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in spring, attracting pollinators such as bees. Its leaves are small and often covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.

The species is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
