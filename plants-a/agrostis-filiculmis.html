Agrostis filiculmis

Agrostis filiculmis, commonly known as the feathergrass, is a flowering plant belonging to the grass family Poaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and delicate, feathery inflorescences. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed areas such as roadsides and fields, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green coloration.

Agrostis filiculmis is often used in ornamental gardening for its aesthetic appeal and low maintenance requirements.
