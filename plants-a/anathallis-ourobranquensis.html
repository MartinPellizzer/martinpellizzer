Anathallis ourobranquensis

Anathallis ourobranquensis, commonly known as the golden branch anathallis, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking yellow and green floral structure. The plant typically grows on trees in humid, shaded forest environments. Its flowers are small but visually appealing, with a unique coloration that aids in attracting specific pollinators.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
