Astilbe japonica f. angustifoliolata

Astilbe japonica f. angustifoliolata, commonly known as narrow-leaf Japanese astilbe, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Japan.

It features delicate, plume-like panicles of pinkish-white flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are finely toothed, giving it a refined appearance. It thrives in partial shade and moist, well-drained soil, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

Its ornamental value lies in its soft, airy foliage and subtle floral display.
