Adiantum varians

Adiantum varians, commonly known as the maidenhair fern, is a delicate and elegant plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its finely divided, lace-like fronds that give it a feathery appearance. The plant prefers cool, moist environments and is often found in shaded, rocky habitats. Its leaves are typically dark green and have a distinctive, almost translucent quality.

Adiantum varians is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
