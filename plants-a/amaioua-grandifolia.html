Amaioua grandifolia

Amaioua grandifolia, commonly known as the large-leaved amaioua, is a tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, glossy, elliptical leaves that can grow up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, arranged in clusters. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-drained soil.

Amaioua grandifolia is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.
