Anacamptis Coriophora Subsp. Fragrans

Fragrant orchid, scientifically known as Anacamptis coriophora subsp. fragrans, is a captivating member of the orchid family that thrives in specific habitats across Europe. This delicate plant is distinguished by its subtle yet enchanting fragrance, which becomes more pronounced as it blooms. Often found in meadows and grasslands, it plays a vital role in supporting local pollinators and maintaining ecological balance. Its unique appearance and ecological significance make it a subject of both botanical interest and conservation concern. Despite its beauty, the Fragrant orchid faces threats from habitat loss and climate change, highlighting the need for continued protection and study.
