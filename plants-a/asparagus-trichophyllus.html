Asparagus trichophyllus

Asparagus trichophyllus, commonly known as the silky asparagus, is a perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its fine, feathery foliage and small, white flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces edible, tender shoots that are often harvested in early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Its unique appearance and culinary value make it a prized addition to gardens and cuisines.
