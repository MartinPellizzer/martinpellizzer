Artemisia borealis f. purshii

Artemisia borealis f. purshii, commonly known as the Arctic sandwort, is a low-growing perennial herb native to the Arctic and subarctic regions.

It features silver-gray foliage with narrow, linear leaves that are densely packed along its stems. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in clusters during the short growing season. It thrives in well-drained, sandy or gravelly soils and is adapted to cold climates.

Artemisia borealis f. purshii plays a role in stabilizing soil in harsh environments and is often found in tundra and alpine habitats.
