Aloe verrucula

Aloe verrucula, commonly known as the疣状芦荟, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a waxy coating. The leaves are often covered in small, white bumps or tubercles, which give it its common name. It produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months.

This plant is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water to thrive.
