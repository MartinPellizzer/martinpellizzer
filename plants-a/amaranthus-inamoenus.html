Amaranthus inamoenus

Amaranthus inamoenus, commonly known as the smooth amaranth, is an annual herb native to the southern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its erect, branching stems and lance-shaped leaves that are typically smooth and glabrous. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from green to red, depending on the species and environmental conditions. Amaranthus inamoenus is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of climates.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine by some indigenous communities.
