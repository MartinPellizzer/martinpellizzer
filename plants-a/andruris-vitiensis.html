Andruris vitiensis

Andruris vitiensis, commonly known as the Vitiensis orchid, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Vanuatu.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its elegant, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and purple. The plant features strap-like leaves and produces small, clustered inflorescences. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
