Aster nakaoi

Aster nakaoi, commonly known as the Japanese aster, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters and produces small, violet-blue flowers in the fall. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, giving them a slightly fuzzy texture.

Aster nakaoi is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional Japanese gardens.
