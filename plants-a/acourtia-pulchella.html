Acourtia pulchella

Acourtia pulchella, commonly known as the showy acourtia, is a perennial flowering plant native to California and Baja California.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its vibrant yellow to orange flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky soils and open habitats, often in coastal areas. It has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, daisy-like blooms.

Acourtia pulchella is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting local pollinators.
