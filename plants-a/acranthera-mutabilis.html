Acranthera mutabilis

Acranthera mutabilis, commonly known as the variable acranthera, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and is adapted to sandy soils and saline conditions. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds, aiding in its dispersal.
