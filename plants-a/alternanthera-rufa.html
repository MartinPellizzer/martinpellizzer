Alternanthera rufa

Alternanthera rufa, commonly known as red Alternanthera, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking red to purple foliage, which becomes more vibrant in full sun. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white or pink flowers that appear in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often used in aquatic gardens or as a decorative plant in water features.

Its rapid growth and ornamental leaves make it a popular choice for both gardeners and aquascapers.
