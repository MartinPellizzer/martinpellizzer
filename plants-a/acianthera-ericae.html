Acianthera ericae

Acianthera ericae, commonly known as the Cape orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in spring. The plant has a compact growth habit with a short, hairy stem and linear leaves. Its flowers are usually pale green or yellowish with a distinctive hooded labellum.

Acianthera ericae thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
