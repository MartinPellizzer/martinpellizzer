Alsophila trichiata

Alsophila trichiata, commonly known as the silky fern, is a species of tree fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and a crown of feathery, light green fronds. The fronds are bipinnate, meaning they have a complex, layered structure with small, delicate pinnae. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in forest understories.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
