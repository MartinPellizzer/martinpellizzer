Acalypha mollis

Acalypha mollis, commonly known as velvetleaf or woollyleaf, is a shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its soft, fuzzy leaves that give it a velvety texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellowish in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed habitats.

Acalypha mollis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping.
