Amelanchier chinensis

Amelanchier chinensis, commonly known as Chinese serviceberry, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the rose family (Rosaceae) and is characterized by its ornamental value, featuring showy white to pink flowers in early spring. The plant produces edible berries that ripen in late summer, which are appreciated for their sweet, tangy flavor. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Amelanchier chinensis is also valued for its ecological benefits, providing food for birds and other wildlife.
