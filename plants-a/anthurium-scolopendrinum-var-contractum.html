Anthurium scolopendrinum var. contractum

Anthurium scolopendrinum var. contractum, commonly known as the compact anthurium, is a member of the Araceae family and is native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces colorful spadices, which are the modified leaf-like structures that enclose the inflorescence. Its foliage is often adorned with intricate patterns and veins, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Due to its compact growth habit, it is well-suited for smaller spaces and requires moderate light and humidity to thrive.
