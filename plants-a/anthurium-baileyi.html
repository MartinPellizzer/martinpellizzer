Anthurium baileyi

Anthurium baileyi, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces vibrant red spathes that enclose its small, spadix-like inflorescence, which is a unique feature of the Araceae family. Anthurium baileyi thrives in humid, warm environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical landscapes.
