Asperula glomerata var. latibracteata

Asperula glomerata var. latibracteata, commonly known as the broad-bract asperula, is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to southern Europe.

It features small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, typically from late spring to early summer. The plant has leathery, elliptical leaves that are dark green and glossy. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a ground cover.

Its ornamental value comes from its compact growth habit and seasonal floral display.
