Ardisia sinensis

Ardisia sinensis, commonly known as Chinese beautyberry, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters, featuring glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, white to pinkish flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by bright red, berry-like fruits. These fruits are not only ornamental but also serve as a food source for birds and other wildlife.

Ardisia sinensis is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal in gardens and landscapes.
