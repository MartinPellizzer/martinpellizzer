Acalypha schlechtendaliana var. mollis

Acalypha schlechtendaliana var. mollis, commonly known as silky acalypha, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its soft, fuzzy leaves that give it a distinctive, velvety appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking texture and visual interest.
