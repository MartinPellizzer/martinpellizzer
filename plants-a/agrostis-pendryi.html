Agrostis pendryi

Agrostis pendryi, commonly known as the pendulous grass, is a species of grass native to the southern regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, arching stems that often hang down, giving it a unique appearance. The plant produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences that are typically green or brown in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open grasslands or along rocky outcrops.

Agrostis pendryi is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in landscaping due to its graceful form.
