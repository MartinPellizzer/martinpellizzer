Arachnorchis pumila

Arachnorchis pumila, commonly known as the dwarf spider orchid, is a small terrestrial orchid native to southern Australia.

It features a compact, low-growing habit with narrow, grass-like leaves and produces delicate, spider-like flowers in shades of white and green. The plant thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats. Its unique floral structure and subtle coloration make it a distinctive member of the orchid family.

Due to its specialized habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and protected species in its native range.
