Anthurium panduriforme var. sagittaria

Anthurium panduriforme var. sagittaria, commonly known as the arrow-shaped anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped or arrow-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a metallic sheen. The plant produces striking spadices, which are inflorescences that consist of a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe. It thrives in high humidity and indirect light, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique foliage and vibrant floral structures make it a visually appealing addition to any plant collection.
