Amaryllis aglaiae

Amaryllis aglaiae, commonly known as the Christmas bell, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It produces vibrant, bell-shaped flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, typically blooming during the winter months. The plant features strap-like, evergreen leaves that emerge from a basal rosette of bulbs. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Amaryllis aglaiae is popular as a decorative plant due to its bold, showy blooms and relatively easy care requirements.
