Aloe hazeliana

Aloe hazeliana, commonly known as the Cape aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with red or purple margins. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellowish-green flowers during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Aloe hazeliana is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional medicinal uses.
