Anchonium tournefortii var. canescens

Anchonium tournefortii var. canescens, commonly known as the silverleaf anchonium, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which results from dense covering of fine, white hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in spring, typically in clusters along the stems. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or arid environments.

Anchonium tournefortii var. canescens is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
