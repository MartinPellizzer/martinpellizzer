Aster Creticus

Aster creticus is a striking plant known for its vibrant blue and purple flowers. It thrives in rocky soils and is native to the Mediterranean region. This hardy perennial is popular among gardeners for its resilience and aesthetic appeal. Its unique appearance makes it a standout in any landscape. Careful cultivation ensures its beauty blooms year after year.
