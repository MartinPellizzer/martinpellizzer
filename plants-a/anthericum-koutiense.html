Anthericum koutiense

Anthericum koutiense, commonly known as the Koutiense arum, is a species of flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its unique inflorescence composed of a spadix and a spathe. The plant features large, heart-shaped leaves that are often brightly colored, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers clustered around the spadix. Anthericum koutiense is typically found in moist, shaded environments such as forests or wetlands.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
