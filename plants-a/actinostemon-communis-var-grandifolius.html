Actinostemon communis var. grandifolius

Actinostemon communis var. grandifolius, commonly known as the large-leaf actinostemon, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features lance-shaped, bright green leaves with a smooth texture and a slightly waxy surface. The plant produces clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white and pale yellow, typically blooming during the warmer months. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to retain moisture in well-drained soils.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in shaded environments.
