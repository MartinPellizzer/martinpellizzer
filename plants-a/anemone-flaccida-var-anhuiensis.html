Anemone flaccida var. anhuiensis

Anemone flaccida var. anhuiensis, commonly known as the Anhui anemone, is a herbaceous perennial native to China.

It features delicate, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has finely divided, fern-like leaves that are often covered in a soft, fuzzy texture. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and wetland habitats.
