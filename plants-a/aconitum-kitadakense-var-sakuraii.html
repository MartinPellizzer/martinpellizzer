Aconitum kitadakense var. sakuraii

Aconitum kitadakense var. sakuraii, commonly known as the Sakurai Aconite, is a perennial herb native to Japan.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its tall, sparsely branched stem and showy blue to purple flowers. The plant produces palmate leaves with deeply lobed margins, and its roots contain toxic alkaloids. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils in shaded to partially shaded environments.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitat.
