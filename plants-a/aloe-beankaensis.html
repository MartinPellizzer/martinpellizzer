Aloe beankaensis

Aloe beankaensis, commonly known as the Beanka aloe, is a succulent plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that store water, which is an adaptation to its dry habitat. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow flowers, typically blooming in late summer. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their soothing properties.

Aloe beankaensis is valued both for its ornamental beauty and its potential medicinal applications.
