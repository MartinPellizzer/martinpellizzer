Ancylostemon trichanthus

Ancylostemon trichanthus, commonly known as the three-lobed pandanus, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Pandanaceae and is characterized by its aromatic, sword-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or white in color. Its roots are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal or wetland areas.
