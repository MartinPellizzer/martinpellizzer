Aconitum × bujbense

Aconitum × bujbense, commonly known as the hybrid garden aconite, is a perennial plant belonging to the Ranunculaceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, sparsely branched stems that bear large, showy blue to purple flowers. The plant produces showy, spurred petals and has a distinctive, toxic alkaloid-rich root system. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Aconitum × bujbense is valued in gardens for its ornamental beauty but requires caution due to its poisonous nature.
