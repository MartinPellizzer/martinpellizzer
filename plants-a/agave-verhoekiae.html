Agave verhoekiae

Agave verhoekiae, commonly known as the Chihuahua agave, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a succulent plant characterized by its rosette-shaped growth pattern and thick, fleshy leaves. The leaves are typically blue-green in color with white to grayish markings, providing a striking visual appearance. It produces a tall flowering stalk that bears yellow flowers, which bloom in the spring.

This plant is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
