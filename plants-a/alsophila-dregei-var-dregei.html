Alsophila dregei var. dregei

Alsophila dregei var. dregei, commonly known as the Drege's tree fern, is a species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnately compound fronds that can reach several meters in length. The plant belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is typically found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky outcrops. Its rhizome is robust, allowing it to anchor firmly in soil and absorb nutrients efficiently.
