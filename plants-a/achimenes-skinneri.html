Achimenes skinneri

Achimenes skinneri, commonly known as the Skinner's flame flower, is a tropical perennial plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that come in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped blooms that are typically borne on upright stems. Its foliage consists of glossy, heart-shaped leaves that are often dark green in color.

Achimenes skinneri is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often grown as a container plant or in shaded garden beds.
