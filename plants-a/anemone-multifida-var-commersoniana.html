Anemone multifida var. commersoniana

Anemone multifida var. commersoniana, commonly known as the Commerson's anemone, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South America.

It features delicate, fern-like foliage with deeply lobed leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces showy, white to pale pink flowers with a central cluster of stamens, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. It prefers moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable addition to both ecological and ornamental landscapes.
