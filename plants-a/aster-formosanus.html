Aster formosanus

Aster formosanus, commonly known as the Formosan aster, is a flowering plant native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its flowers are usually blue to violet in color, with ray florets surrounding a central disk.

Aster formosanus is valued for its ornamental beauty and ecological importance in supporting local pollinators.
