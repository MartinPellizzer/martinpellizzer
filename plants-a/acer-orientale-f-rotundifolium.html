Acer orientale f. rotundifolium

Acer orientale f. rotundifolium, commonly known as the round-leaf maple, is a unique cultivar of the oriental maple.

It is characterized by its broad, rounded leaves that are distinctly different from the typical narrow leaves of the species. The plant is native to China and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, thriving in temperate climates.

Its foliage displays vibrant colors in the fall, making it a popular choice for landscape design.
