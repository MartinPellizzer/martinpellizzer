Acamptopappus sphaerocephalus

Acamptopappus sphaerocephalus, commonly known as the globe-headed acamptopappus, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant is distinguished by its spherical, dense flower heads that are typically pink to purple in color. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it thrives in arid and semi-arid environments.
