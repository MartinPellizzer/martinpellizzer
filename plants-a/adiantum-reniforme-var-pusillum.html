Adiantum reniforme var. pusillum

Adiantum reniforme var. pusillum, commonly known as the miniature maidenhair fern, is a delicate and elegant fern species native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its small, kidney-shaped fronds that are finely divided and possess a distinctive rachis with tiny, upward-facing spore clusters. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and prefers well-draining, humus-rich soil. It is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental value and ease of care.

Its unique appearance and ability to thrive in low-light conditions make it a favored addition to both indoor and outdoor gardens.
