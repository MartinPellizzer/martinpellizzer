Aloe schomeri

Aloe schomeri, commonly known as Schomer's aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically gray-green in color, with a waxy texture to store water. The plant produces upright, branched inflorescences that bear yellow or orange flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for arid or semi-arid climates.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
