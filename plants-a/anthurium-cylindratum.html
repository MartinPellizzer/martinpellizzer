Anthurium cylindratum

Anthurium cylindratum, commonly known as the cylindrical anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, elongated spathes that can range in color from deep red to maroon, often with a contrasting green or yellow midrib. The plant produces small, greenish-white spadices inside the spatulate leaf-like structure, which are the actual flowers. Anthurium cylindratum thrives in humid, warm environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.

Its unique foliage and vibrant colors make it a popular choice for indoor gardening and ornamental purposes.
