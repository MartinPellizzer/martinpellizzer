Alyssum montanum var. orbiculare

Alyssum montanum var. orbiculare, commonly known as the globe alyssum, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features compact, mat-forming growth with small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink. The leaves are typically narrow and gray-green, often covered in a fine layer of hair. This variety is characterized by its spherical flower heads, which give it its common name.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
