Aglaonema tricolor

Aglaonema tricolor, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen, is a tropical evergreen plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated foliage with shades of white, green, and red. The plant has thick, waxy leaves that are typically oval or elliptical in shape. It thrives in low-light conditions and requires moderate humidity and well-draining soil.

Aglaonema tricolor is popular as a houseplant due to its aesthetic appeal and air-purifying qualities.
