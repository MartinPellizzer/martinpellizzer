Adiantum aethiopicum var. pohlii

Adiantum aethiopicum var. pohlii, commonly known as the Pohlii maidenhair fern, is a striking variety of the maidenhair fern family.

It is characterized by its delicate, lace-like fronds that are typically dark green with a subtle metallic sheen. Native to tropical regions of Africa, this plant thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its fronds are bi-lobed and often display a distinctive, almost fern-like appearance.

It is a popular ornamental plant due to its elegance and ease of care in suitable conditions.
