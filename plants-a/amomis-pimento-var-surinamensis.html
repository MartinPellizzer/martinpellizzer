Amomis pimento var. surinamensis

Amomis pimento var. surinamensis, commonly known as the Surinamese pimento, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It belongs to the family Piperaceae and is characterized by its aromatic, peppery leaves and small, berry-like fruits. The plant is often cultivated for its culinary uses, particularly in regional cuisines. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
