Aphelandra sinitzini

Aphelandra sinitzini, commonly known as the Chinese flame tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, pendulous inflorescences that bear bright orange to red flowers, which are highly visible and attract pollinators. The plant has glossy, dark green, elongated leaves that provide a lush backdrop to its vibrant blooms. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Aphelandra sinitzini is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
