Aster martii

Aster martii, commonly known as the Sierra aster, is a flowering plant native to the western United States and parts of Mexico.

It is characterized by its showy blue to purple flowers and is typically found in rocky or open habitats. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is known for its composite flower heads. Aster martii thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in alpine or montane regions.

It plays an important role in supporting local pollinators and is a symbol of resilience in harsh environments.
