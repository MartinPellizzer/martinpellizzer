Arenaria martrinii

Arenaria martrinii, commonly known as the sandspurry, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, white or pink flowers that bloom in early spring, typically on stems that rise from a basal rosette of leaves. The plant has succulent, fleshy leaves that help it retain moisture in its arid habitat. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy areas.

Arenaria martrinii is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and ability to tolerate harsh environmental conditions.
