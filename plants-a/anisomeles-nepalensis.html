Anisomeles nepalensis

Anisomeles nepalensis, commonly known as the Himalayan flax, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Nepal and northern India.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in moist, shaded environments at high altitudes. The plant features lance-shaped leaves with a smooth texture and produces small, pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters. Its fibrous stems and leaves have been traditionally used for their medicinal properties.

Anisomeles nepalensis is valued for its adaptability to harsh alpine conditions and its role in local ecosystems.
