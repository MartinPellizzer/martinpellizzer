Androcymbium gramineum var. saharae

Androcymbium gramineum var. saharae, commonly known as the Saharan grass orchid, is a rare and specialized plant native to the arid regions of the Sahara Desert.

It is a member of the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and exhibits unique adaptations to survive in extreme desert conditions. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that bloom during brief periods of rainfall. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, helping to reduce water loss through transpiration.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native range.
