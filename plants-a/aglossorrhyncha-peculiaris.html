Aglossorrhyncha peculiaris

Aglossorrhyncha peculiaris, commonly known as the peculiar leafless orchid, is a rare and enigmatic plant species belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its lack of leaves, with photosynthesis occurring through its modified stems. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in late spring. Due to its unique morphology and limited distribution, it is considered a conservation concern.

Aglossorrhyncha peculiaris thrives in shaded, moist environments, typically found in tropical and subtropical regions.
