Agave asperrima subsp. asperrima

Agave asperrima subsp.

asperrima, commonly known as the rough agave, is a succulent plant native to Mexico. It is characterized by its spiny, fleshy leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern.

The plant produces a tall, branched inflorescence with yellowish-green flowers. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, requiring well-drained soil and full sun.

Its botanical features make it well adapted to withstand drought and high temperatures.
