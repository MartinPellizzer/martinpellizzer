Actinidia dielsii

Actinidia dielsii, commonly known as the Chinese mayapple, is a deciduous climbing vine native to China.

It belongs to the Actinidiaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and fragrant, yellowish-green flowers. The plant produces small, edible berries that are often used in traditional Chinese medicine. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is valued for its ornamental and medicinal properties.

Due to its rarity and habitat specificity, it is considered an endangered species in its native range.
