Asphodelus albus var. proterophyllus

Asphodelus albus var. proterophyllus, commonly known as the white asphodel, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and clusters of white or pale yellow flowers. This species is noted for its resilience and ability to thrive in arid conditions.
