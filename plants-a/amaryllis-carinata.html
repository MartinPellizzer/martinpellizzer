Amaryllis carinata

Amaryllis carinata, commonly known as the zigzag amaryllis, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from white to deep red. The plant features strap-like leaves that are often striped with contrasting colors. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Amaryllis carinata is popular as a ornamental plant due to its vibrant blooms and unique foliage.
