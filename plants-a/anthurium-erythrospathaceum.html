Anthurium erythrospathaceum

Anthurium erythrospathaceum, commonly known as the red-spathe anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped leaves and striking, spathaceous inflorescences. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers surrounded by a brightly colored spathe, which can range from red to orange. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.

Anthurium erythrospathaceum is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and air-purifying properties.
