Anguria spinulosa var. glabrata

Anguria spinulosa var. glabrata, commonly known as the smooth spiny cucumber, is a variant of the spiny cucumber species.

It is characterized by its smooth, hairless stems and leaves, distinguishing it from other varieties. The plant produces small, green, spiny fruits that are typically harvested when immature. It thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This variety is often cultivated for its unique appearance and edible qualities.
