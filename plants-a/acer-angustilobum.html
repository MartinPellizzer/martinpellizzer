Acer angustilobum

Acer angustilobum, commonly known as the red maple, is a deciduous tree native to eastern North America.

It is characterized by its distinctive palmate leaves that turn vibrant red in the fall. The tree produces small, red samaras that are dispersed by wind. It thrives in a variety of soil types but prefers moist, well-drained conditions.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping and urban environments.
