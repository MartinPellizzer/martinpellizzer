Aloe dominella

Aloe dominella, commonly known as the desert aloe or narrow-leaved aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features rosettes of narrow, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a slight bluish tint. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers on tall, branched inflorescences during the warmer months. Aloe dominella is adapted to arid environments, storing water in its leaves to survive drought conditions.

It is often used in traditional medicine and as an ornamental plant due to its hardy nature and aesthetic appeal.
