Aira cespitosa var. brevifolia

Aira cespitosa var. brevifolia, commonly known as slender false oat, is a perennial grass species native to North America.

It features tufted growth with slender, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed sites such as meadows and roadsides. It has a fibrous root system and is adapted to a variety of soil types.

Its botanical characteristics make it an important component of native plant communities.
