Agrostis capillaris var. aristata

Agrostis capillaris var. aristata, commonly known as slender meadowgrass, is a grass species native to temperate regions of Europe and Asia.

It typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows, dunes, and riverbanks. The plant has slender, wiry stems and small, spike-like inflorescences that are often adorned with bristles. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green color and a hairy texture.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping or as a component in ecological restoration projects.
