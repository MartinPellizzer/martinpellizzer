Aniba megaphylla

Aniba megaphylla, commonly known as the large-leafed aniba, is a shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurels and cinnamon trees. The plant is characterized by its large, elliptical leaves that are often glossy and dark green. It produces small, fragrant flowers followed by berries that are typically red when mature.

Aniba megaphylla is valued for its aromatic properties and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
