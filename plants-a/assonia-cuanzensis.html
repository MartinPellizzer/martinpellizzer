Assonia cuanzensis

Assonia cuanzensis, commonly known as the African milkweed, is a flowering plant native to Central Africa.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family, which includes many species with milky sap. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, oval-shaped, and often have a velvety texture.

The flowers are clustered in umbels and are usually yellow or greenish-yellow, attracting various pollinators.
