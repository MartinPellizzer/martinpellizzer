Aphora sericea

Aphora sericea, commonly known as silverleaf aphora, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, silky hairs. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Aphora sericea is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
