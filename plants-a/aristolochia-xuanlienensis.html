Aristolochia xuanlienensis

Aristolochia xuanlienensis, commonly known as the Xuanlien Dutchman's pipe, is a flowering plant native to Vietnam.

It belongs to the Aristolochiaceae family and is characterized by its unique, pipe-shaped flowers that are often purple or green in color. The plant produces heart-shaped leaves with a rough texture and is typically found in tropical and subtropical regions. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some cultures.

However, it contains toxic compounds and should be handled with care.
