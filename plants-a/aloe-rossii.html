Aloe rossii

Aloe rossii, commonly known as the Ross's aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern, which helps it store water efficiently. The leaves are usually green with a waxy coating, and they may have small, white spots or bumps. It produces yellow or orange flowers on tall, branched stems during the summer months.

This plant is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
