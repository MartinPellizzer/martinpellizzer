Arabis tuberosa

Arabis tuberosa, commonly known as the candytuft, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features rosettes of silvery, woolly leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, bright white or pink flowers in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long-lasting blooms and low maintenance requirements.
