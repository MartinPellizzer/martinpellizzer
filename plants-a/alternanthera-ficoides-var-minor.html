Alternanthera ficoides var. minor

Alternanthera ficoides var. minor, commonly known as dwarf red alternanthera, is a compact, trailing plant native to Brazil.

It features small, lance-shaped leaves that are typically bright red or purple, though they can vary in color depending on light conditions. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually white or pale pink, which appear in clusters along the stems. It thrives in moist, well-draining soil and prefers partial to full sun, making it a popular choice for water gardens and bog environments.

Its vibrant foliage makes it a striking addition to aquatic and terrestrial landscapes.
