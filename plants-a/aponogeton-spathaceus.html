Aponogeton spathaceus

Aponogeton spathaceus, commonly known as the broad-leaf arrowhead, is a tropical aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features long, ribbon-like leaves that float on the water surface, with a broad, arrow-shaped blade. The plant produces striking white or pale pink flowers that rise above the water, often in clusters. It thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments with ample sunlight.

Aponogeton spathaceus is popular in aquascaping for its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.
