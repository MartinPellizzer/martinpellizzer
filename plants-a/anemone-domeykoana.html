Anemone domeykoana

Anemone domeykoana, commonly known as the Domeyko anemone, is a flowering plant native to the Andes mountains of Chile.

It is characterized by its showy, pink to white petals that resemble those of other anemone species. The plant typically grows in rocky, alpine environments and prefers well-drained soil. Its leaves are usually basal, with a distinctive lobed or serrated edge.

Anemone domeykoana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens in cooler climates.
