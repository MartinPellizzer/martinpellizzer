Aporum hainanense

Aporum hainanense, commonly known as Hainan aporum, is a rare flowering plant native to the tropical rainforests of Hainan Island in China.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its large, showy inflorescence consisting of a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil. It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered endangered and is protected in its native region.
