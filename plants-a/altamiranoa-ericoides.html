Altamiranoa ericoides

Altamiranoa ericoides, commonly known as the Mexican heath, is a shrub native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and blueberries. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in acidic environments. It features small, leathery leaves and produces clusters of pink or white flowers.

Altamiranoa ericoides is well adapted to dry conditions and is often found in mountainous regions.
