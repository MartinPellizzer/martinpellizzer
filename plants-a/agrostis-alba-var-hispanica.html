Agrostis alba var. hispanica

Agrostis alba var. hispanica, commonly known as Spanish silvergrass, is a perennial grass native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-gray foliage and tufted inflorescence, which gives it a distinctive appearance. This plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in grasslands and disturbed sites. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscaping.

The species is also valued for its role in stabilizing soil and supporting local wildlife.
