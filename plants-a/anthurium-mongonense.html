Anthurium mongonense

Anthurium mongonense, commonly known as the Mongonense Arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive inflorescence consisting of a spadix and a spathe. The plant features broad, heart-shaped leaves with a waxy surface, often displaying vibrant green hues. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Anthurium mongonense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated as a houseplant.
