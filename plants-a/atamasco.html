Atamasco

Atamasco, commonly known as *Lobelia cardinalis*, is a flowering plant native to eastern North America.

It is characterized by its striking red-orange tubular flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in moist, woodland habitats and thrives in partial to full shade. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged opposite along the stem.
