Asplenium martianum var. muelleri

Asplenium martianum var. muelleri, commonly known as the Mueller's shield fern, is a rare and specialized fern species native to specific regions of Australia.

It belongs to the family Aspleniaceae and is characterized by its compact, evergreen foliage with a distinctive shield-shaped frond structure. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in rocky or forested areas. Its spores are produced on the underside of the fronds, and it reproduces through sexual reproduction via spore dispersal.
