Astragalus tatsienensis

Astragalus tatsienensis, commonly known as the Tatsienlung milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its small, yellow flowers and compound leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. It is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional Chinese medicine.

Its conservation status is of concern due to habitat loss and overharvesting.
