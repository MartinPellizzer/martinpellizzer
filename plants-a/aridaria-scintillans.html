Aridaria scintillans

Aridaria scintillans, commonly known as the shimmering desert orchid, is a rare flowering plant adapted to arid environments.

It possesses thick, waxy leaves that minimize water loss and thrive in sandy soils. The plant produces striking, bioluminescent flowers that glow faintly at night, attracting nocturnal pollinators. Its root system is extensive, allowing it to access deep groundwater sources.

Due to its unique adaptations, it is classified as a vulnerable species in its native habitat.
