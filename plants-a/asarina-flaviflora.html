Asarina flaviflora

Asarina flaviflora, commonly known as the yellow-flowered asarina, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Myrtaceae and is characterized by its small, yellowish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and often have a fragrant scent.
