Amaryllis lindleyana

Amaryllis lindleyana, commonly known as the Lindley's amaryllis, is a bulbous plant native to South America.

It produces striking, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, typically during the summer months. The plant features strap-like, sword-shaped leaves that are broad and smooth. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Amaryllis lindleyana is popular as a garden plant due to its vibrant blooms and relatively easy care requirements.
