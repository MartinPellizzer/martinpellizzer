Aeschynanthus magnificus

Aeschynanthus magnificus, commonly known as the magnificent wax plant, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

Aeschynanthus magnificus is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
