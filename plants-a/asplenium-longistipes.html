Asplenium longistipes

Asplenium longistipes, commonly known as the long-stemmed spleenwort, is a species of fern belonging to the family Aspleniaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, upright rhizomes and dark green, lanceolate leaves that are often deeply lobed. The fronds are typically short and compact, with fertile shoots emerging from the base of the plant. It prefers moist, shaded environments and is often found growing on rocks or tree trunks in tropical and subtropical regions.

This fern is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in shaded garden settings.
