Andropogon sericeus f. ciliatus

Andropogon sericeus f. ciliatus, commonly known as the silky grass or silky bluestem, is a flowering plant belonging to the grass family Poaceae.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-haired foliage and slender, upright growth habit. Native to the southeastern United States, it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun. The plant produces small, clustered spikelets that are typically green to brown in color.

It is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its attractive appearance and low maintenance requirements.
