Anomatheca xanthospila

Anomatheca xanthospila, commonly known as the yellow-spotted daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like inflorescence. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces yellow flowers with white centers. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and vibrant floral display.
