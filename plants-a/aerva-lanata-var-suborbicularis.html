Aerva lanata var. suborbicularis

Aerva lanata var. suborbicularis, commonly known as the orbicular aerva, is a variant of the aerva plant native to certain regions of Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, sunny environments. The leaves are distinctive, being orbicular in shape with a hairy texture. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters.

It is valued for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional herbal remedies.
