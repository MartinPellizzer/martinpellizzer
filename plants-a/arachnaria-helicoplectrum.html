Arachnaria helicoplectrum

Arachnaria helicoplectrum, commonly known as the spiral helix fern, is a unique species characterized by its intricate, spiraling fronds.

This plant belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia. Its leaves are arranged in a distinctive helical pattern, which aids in maximizing light absorption. The rhizome is thick and woody, supporting the plant's growth in shaded, moist environments.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its elegant, twisting appearance.
