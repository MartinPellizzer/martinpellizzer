Azalea nudiflora var. periclymenoides

Azalea nudiflora var. periclymenoides, commonly known as the Japanese azalea, is a deciduous shrub native to Japan.

It features showy, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink and red, typically blooming in late spring. The plant has elliptical, dark green leaves that are opposite in arrangement. It prefers acidic, well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
