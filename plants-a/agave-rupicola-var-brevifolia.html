Agave rupicola var. brevifolia

Agave rupicola var. brevifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved stonecress agave, is a succulent plant native to Mexico.

It features a short, thick stem with fleshy, blue-green leaves that are narrow and recurved. The plant produces a tall, branched inflorescence with white or pale yellow flowers. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Its striking appearance makes it a popular choice for xeriscaping and rock gardens.
