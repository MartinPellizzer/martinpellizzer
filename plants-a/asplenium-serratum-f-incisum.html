Asplenium serratum f. incisum

Asplenium serratum f. incisum, commonly known as the serrated spleenwort, is a fern species belonging to the family Aspleniaceae.

It is characterized by its finely serrated leaf margins, which give it its common name. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as rocky crevices or forest floors. Its fronds are usually narrow and lance-shaped, with a dark green coloration.

This fern is often found in temperate regions and is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.
