Anthurium rhodorhizum

Anthurium rhodorhizum, commonly known as the red-veined anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves with deep red veins against a dark green background. The plant produces vibrant red spathes that enclose its small, spadix-like inflorescence. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Anthurium rhodorhizum is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
