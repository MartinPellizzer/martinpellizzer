Anacamptis × bicknellii

Anacamptis × bicknellii, commonly known as the Bicknell's orchid, is a hybrid species native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its tall, erect stem and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically blooms in late spring to early summer, producing violet-blue to purple flowers with a distinctive lip. It thrives in sunny, well-drained habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils.

Anacamptis × bicknellii is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and fragrance.
