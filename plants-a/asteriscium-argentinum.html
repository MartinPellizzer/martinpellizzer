Asteriscium argentinum

Asteriscium argentinum, commonly known as the Argentine asteriscium, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or purple in color, arranged in a star-like pattern. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in horticulture for its delicate appearance.
