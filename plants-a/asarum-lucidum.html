Asarum lucidum

Asarum lucidum, commonly known as shining asarum, is a perennial herb native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, glossy leaves and clusters of small, reddish-brown flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine for their purported therapeutic properties.

The species is also valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.
