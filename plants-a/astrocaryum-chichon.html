Astrocaryum chichon

Astrocaryum chichon, commonly known as the Chichon palm, is a species of palm native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like leaves that can reach several meters in length. The plant produces small, clustered flowers followed by edible fruits that are often consumed by local communities. Its botanical features make it an important species in tropical ecosystems and traditional agriculture.

The Chichon palm is also valued for its ornamental appeal and sustainable use in landscaping.
