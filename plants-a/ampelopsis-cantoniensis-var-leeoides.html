Ampelopsis cantoniensis var. leeoides

Ampelopsis cantoniensis var. leeoides, commonly known as the Guangdong honeysuckle vine, is a climbing plant native to southern China.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are typically lobed and have a smooth texture. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. Its berries are orange-red when ripe and contain several seeds.

This species is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its attractive foliage and flowering habit.
