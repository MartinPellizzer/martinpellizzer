Agrostis delislei

Agrostis delislei, commonly known as Delisle's bentgrass, is a low-growing perennial grass native to southern Africa.

It features slender, tufted stems and small, spike-like inflorescences that produce numerous tiny seeds. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open grasslands and rocky areas. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue when mature.

Agrostis delislei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
