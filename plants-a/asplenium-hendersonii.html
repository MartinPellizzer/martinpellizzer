Asplenium hendersonii

Asplenium hendersonii, commonly known as Henderson's spleenwort, is a small, evergreen fern native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky crevices. The plant has a compact, rosette-like structure with finely divided, lance-shaped leaves. Its fronds are usually dark green and can reach up to 15 centimeters in length.

Asplenium hendersonii is notable for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and is often found epiphytically on trees or stones.
