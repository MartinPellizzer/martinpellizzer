Anticharis azurea

Anticharis azurea, commonly known as the Azure Anticharis, is a striking flowering plant native to certain regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant blue petals that contrast beautifully with its green foliage. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest understories or along stream banks. Its flowers are tubular and attract pollinators like hummingbirds and bees.
