Allium stocksianum

Allium stocksianum, commonly known as the purple onion, is a perennial bulbous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features upright, strap-like leaves that are typically bluish-green in color. The plant produces clusters of small, purple or pink flowers in late spring to early summer. Its bulbs are edible and have a mild onion flavor, often used in cooking.

Allium stocksianum is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and culinary versatility.
