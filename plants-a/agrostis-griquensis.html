Agrostis griquensis

Agrostis griquensis, commonly known as Grootfontein grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in open grasslands and disturbed soils, often found in regions with a temperate climate. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It is well adapted to dry conditions and can thrive in nutrient-poor soils.

Its botanical features make it a valuable component of local ecosystems and a potential forage crop.
