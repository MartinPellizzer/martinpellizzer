Ardisia crenata var. angusta

Ardisia crenata var. angusta, commonly known as narrowleaf evergreen ardisia, is a compact shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, dark green, narrow elliptical leaves that remain evergreen throughout the year. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers in the spring, followed by bright red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in partial to full shade and prefers moist, well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
