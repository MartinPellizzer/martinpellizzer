Atulandra aragonensis

Atulandra aragonensis, commonly known as the Aragon orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the Andean regions of South America, particularly Ecuador and Peru. This plant typically grows in high-altitude cloud forests, where it thrives in cool, humid conditions. Its flowers are usually pale green to white, with a distinctive lip that is often darker in color.

Atulandra aragonensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
