Anemone govaniana

Anemone govaniana, commonly known as the African anemone, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features showy, white to pinkish flowers that resemble a crown, typically blooming in spring. The plant has palmate leaves with deeply lobed edges, and it prefers well-drained soil and full sun. It is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid conditions.

Anemone govaniana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
