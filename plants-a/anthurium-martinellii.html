Anthurium martinellii

Anthurium martinellii, commonly known as the Martinellii Anthurium, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a metallic sheen. The plant produces striking spadices, which are the showy parts of the inflorescence, often in shades of red or orange. It thrives in humid, warm environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Anthurium martinellii is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
