Agapanthus minor

Agapanthus minor, commonly known as the small African lily, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It features dense clusters of blue or white, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has strap-like, evergreen leaves that form a rosette at the base. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for gardens and rockeries.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact size and long-lasting blooms.
