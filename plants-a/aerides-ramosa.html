Aerides ramosa

Aerides ramosa, commonly known as the ramose orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, clump-forming growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, yellow-green flowers. The plant has strap-like, dark green leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Aerides ramosa is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
