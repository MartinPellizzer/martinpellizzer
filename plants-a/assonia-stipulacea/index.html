Assonia Stipulacea

Assonia tree, scientifically known as Assonia stipulacea, is a rare and unique species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia. This evergreen tree is characterized by its dense foliage and distinctive, lance-shaped leaves that give it a striking appearance. While not widely cultivated, it holds cultural and ecological significance in its native habitat. Its slow growth and ornamental value make it a sought-after plant among botanists and collectors. Despite its beauty, Assonia stipulacea remains relatively unknown to the general public.
