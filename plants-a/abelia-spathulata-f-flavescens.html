Abelia spathulata f. flavescens

Abelia spathulata f. flavescens, commonly known as the golden abelia, is a compact, deciduous shrub native to China.

It features narrow, elliptical leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant, white flowers that transition to a soft yellow hue as they age. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained, moist soil.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various garden conditions.
