Antirrhinum × montserratii

Antirrhinum × montserratii, commonly known as the Montserrat snapdragon, is a hybrid plant native to the island of Montserrat in the Caribbean.

It is a herbaceous perennial characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, orange, and yellow. The plant typically grows to a height of 30–60 centimeters and features linear, grass-like leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Due to its unique appearance and limited natural range, it is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
