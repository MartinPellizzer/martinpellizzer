Alternanthera brasiliana var. glabriuscula

Alternanthera brasiliana var. glabriuscula, commonly known as the velvet leaf plant, is a herbaceous perennial native to South America.

It features striking, velvety leaves that range in color from deep red to purple, depending on light exposure. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full sun.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for water gardens and pond edges.
