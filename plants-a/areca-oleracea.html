Areca oleracea

Areca oleracea, commonly known as the horsehead plant, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, palm-like leaves that are often used in traditional medicine and as a decorative plant. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that eventually develop into edible, red berries. Its leaves are rich in nutrients and are used in various culinary and medicinal applications.

Areca oleracea thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
