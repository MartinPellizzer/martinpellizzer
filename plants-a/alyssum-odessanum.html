Alyssum odessanum

Alyssum odessanum, commonly known as the Odessa alyssum, is a flowering plant native to the Caucasus region.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in early summer. Alyssum odessanum is often found in rocky or dry habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal.

It is also used in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
