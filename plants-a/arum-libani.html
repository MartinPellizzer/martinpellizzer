Arum libani

Arum libani, commonly known as the Lebanon arrowhead, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features arrow-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces striking yellowish-white spadices surrounded by a colorful spathe. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Arum libani is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.
