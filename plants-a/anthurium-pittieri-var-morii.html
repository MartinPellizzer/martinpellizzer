Anthurium pittieri var. morii

Anthurium pittieri var. morii, commonly known as the Morii Anthurium, is a striking tropical plant native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a glossy texture. The plant produces clusters of spadices, which are inflorescences that resemble spikes, often in shades of red or orange. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Anthurium pittieri var. morii is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in botanical gardens.
