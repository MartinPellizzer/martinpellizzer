Achillea visianii

Achillea visianii, commonly known as the Visianian yarrow, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features gray-green, fern-like leaves and produces small, yellow to white composite flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or dry habitats.

Achillea visianii is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
