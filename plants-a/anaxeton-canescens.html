Anaxeton canescens

Anaxeton canescens, commonly known as the silverleaf thistle, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage, which is covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces spiny, cylindrical leaves and purple to pinkish flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky or sandy soils.

Anaxeton canescens is adapted to withstand drought conditions, making it a resilient species in its native habitat.
