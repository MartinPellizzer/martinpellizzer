Argyrolobium nanum

Argyrolobium nanum, commonly known as the silver globular pea, is a small, flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a member of the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in coastal areas.
