Arenaria verna var. brachypetala

Arenaria verna var. brachypetala, commonly known as the spring sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to temperate regions of Europe and Asia.

It typically features small, white or pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has compact, mat-forming foliage with narrow, grass-like leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Its botanical structure is adapted to withstand harsh environmental conditions, making it a resilient species in its native range.
