Acridocarpus excelsus var. lamii

Acridocarpus excelsus var. lamii, commonly known as the golden flame tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its bright yellow to orange flowers that bloom in dense, showy clusters, creating a striking visual display. The plant has compound leaves with elongated, lance-shaped leaflets that are often arranged in an alternate pattern. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The species is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
