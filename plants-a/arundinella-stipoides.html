Arundinella stipoides

Arundinella stipoides, commonly known as the slender bamboo or narrow-leaf bamboo, is a perennial grass native to parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems that can grow up to several meters in height. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in panicles, typical of the grass family. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a smooth texture and a bluish-green color.

Arundinella stipoides is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional landscaping in its native regions.
