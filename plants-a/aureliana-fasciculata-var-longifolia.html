Aureliana fasciculata var. longifolia

Aureliana fasciculata var. longifolia, commonly known as the long-leaved goldenbush, is a shrub native to the arid regions of South America.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The leaves are elongated and often covered in fine hairs, giving the plant a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to withstand harsh environmental conditions.
