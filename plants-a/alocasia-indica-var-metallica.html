Alocasia indica var. metallica

Alocasia indica var. metallica, commonly known as the metallic elephant ear, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, velvety, dark green leaves with a metallic sheen, which give it its common name. The plant belongs to the Araceae family and is known for its dramatic, arrow-shaped foliage. It thrives in humid, warm environments and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and ornamental purposes.
