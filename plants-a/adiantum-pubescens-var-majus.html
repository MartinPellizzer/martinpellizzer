Adiantum pubescens var. majus

Adiantum pubescens var. majus, commonly known as the Southern Maidenhair Fern, is a delicate and elegant fern native to the southeastern United States.

It features finely divided, arching fronds that are typically light green with a silvery underside. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and prefers well-draining, acidic soil. It is characterized by its unique, feathery appearance and its ability to form dense, lush colonies.

Adiantum pubescens var. majus is often used in ornamental horticulture for its aesthetic appeal and easy care requirements.
