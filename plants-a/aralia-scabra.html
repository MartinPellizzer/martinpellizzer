Aralia scabra

Aralia scabra, commonly known as the rough aralia, is a flowering plant native to eastern North America.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters. The plant features compound leaves with serrated edges and small, greenish-white flowers arranged in umbels. Its fruits are berries that turn red when mature, attracting various birds and small mammals.

Aralia scabra is often found in woodland areas and thrives in partial shade to full sun.
