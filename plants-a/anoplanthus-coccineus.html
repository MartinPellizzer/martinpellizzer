Anoplanthus coccineus

Anoplanthus coccineus, commonly known as the red-flowered grass, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like stems and bright red inflorescences that bloom in late summer. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a common sight in savanna habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
