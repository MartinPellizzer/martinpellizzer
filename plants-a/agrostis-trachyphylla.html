Agrostis trachyphylla

Agrostis trachyphylla, commonly known as silver hair grass, is a perennial grass species native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-gray foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in open, sunny areas such as meadows, pastures, and woodland edges. Its inflorescence consists of a dense cluster of spikelets, which are often covered in a fine layer of hair.

Agrostis trachyphylla is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in landscaping for its aesthetic appeal.
