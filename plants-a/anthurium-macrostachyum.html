Anthurium macrostachyum

Anthurium macrostachyum, commonly known as the giant anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length. The plant produces striking, spadix-shaped inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, which can range from white to deep red. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in greenhouse settings.
