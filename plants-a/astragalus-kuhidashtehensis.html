Astragalus kuhidashtehensis

Astragalus kuhidashtehensis, commonly known as Kuhidashtehen milkvetch, is a flowering plant species native to Iran.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its small, pea-like flowers and trifoliate leaves. The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions.

Its roots are known to have medicinal properties, making it of interest in traditional herbal medicine.
