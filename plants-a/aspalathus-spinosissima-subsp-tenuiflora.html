Aspalathus spinosissima subsp. tenuiflora

Aspalathus spinosissima subsp.

tenuiflora, commonly known as the slender-flowered mountain tea, is a flowering plant native to South Africa. It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that bloom in spring.

The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in areas with moderate rainfall. Its leaves are trifoliate, and the species is noted for its ornamental value in gardens.

It is also used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
