Adonis vernalis

Adonis vernalis, commonly known as the spring beauty, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, early spring blooms. The plant typically grows in woodland areas and has basal leaves that are heart-shaped and hairy. Its flowers are usually pale yellow or white, with five petals and numerous stamens.

Adonis vernalis is known for its short growing season and is often found in moist, shaded environments.
