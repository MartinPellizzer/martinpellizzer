Appendicula biumbonata var. exappendiculata

Appendicula biumbonata var. exappendiculata, commonly known as the dwarf orchid, is a small, epiphytic orchid native to the cloud forests of Southeast Asia.

It features delicate, lance-shaped leaves and produces tiny, clustered flowers in shades of white and pale yellow. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, organic-rich soil. It is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Its unique appearance and compact size make it a favored ornamental plant among orchid enthusiasts.
