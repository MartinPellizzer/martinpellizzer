Anguria polyphyllos

Anguria polyphyllos, commonly known as the multileaf cucumber, is a unique member of the Cucurbitaceae family.

It is characterized by its broad, lobed leaves that can vary in shape, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are typically unisexual, with separate male and female flowers on the same plant. Its fruit is a elongated, cucumber-like structure that can range in color from green to yellow.

Due to its ornamental value and edible fruit, it is cultivated in some regions for both aesthetic and culinary purposes.
