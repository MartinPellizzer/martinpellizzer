Actinostemon communis f. glabrescens

Actinostemon communis f. glabrescens, commonly known as the smoothleaf actinostemon, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Amaranthaceae and is characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves that are typically smooth and glabrous, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually pink or red in color, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its striking foliage and compact growth habit.

This variety is noted for its resilience and ability to adapt to various environmental conditions.
