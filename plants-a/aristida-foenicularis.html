Aristida foenicularis

Aristida foenicularis, commonly known as the desert dropseed, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its fine, needle-like leaves and small, spike-like inflorescences. The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in stabilizing soil in dry regions.
