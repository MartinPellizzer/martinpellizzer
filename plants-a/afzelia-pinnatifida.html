Afzelia pinnatifida

Afzelia pinnatifida, commonly known as the African satinwood, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and compound leaves with pinnate arrangement. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by woody pods containing flat, elliptical seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in savanna and woodland areas.

Due to its durable wood, it is valued for timber and traditional uses.
