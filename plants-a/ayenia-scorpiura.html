Ayenia scorpiura

Ayenia scorpiura, commonly known as the spiny hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its woody stems and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to red. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas or near riverbanks.

Ayenia scorpiura is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
