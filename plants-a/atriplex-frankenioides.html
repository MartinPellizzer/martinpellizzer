Atriplex frankenioides

Atriplex frankenioides, commonly known as Frankenioides saltbush, is a shrub native to the arid regions of western North America.

It is characterized by its small, scale-like leaves that reduce water loss in harsh environments. The plant produces small, greenish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in saline and alkaline soils, making it a common species in desert and coastal habitats.

Its adaptability to extreme conditions makes it an important component of desert ecosystems.
