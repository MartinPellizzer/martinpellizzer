Aloe frutescens

Aloe frutescens, commonly known as the Cape aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that store water, a characteristic typical of succulents. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow or orange flowers. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their healing properties.

Aloe frutescens thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for arid climates.
