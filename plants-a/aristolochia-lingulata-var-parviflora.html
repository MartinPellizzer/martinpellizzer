Aristolochia lingulata var. parviflora

Aristolochia lingulata var. parviflora, commonly known as the small-flowered Dutchman's pipe, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its unique, pipe-shaped flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and emits a mild, sweet fragrance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodlands and along stream banks.

The species is part of the Aristolochiaceae family and is known for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal medicine.
