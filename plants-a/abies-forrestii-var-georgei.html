Abies forrestii var. georgei

Abies forrestii var. georgei, commonly known as the George's fir, is a rare and endemic conifer native to the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its narrow, conical shape and dark green, needle-like leaves. The plant produces cone-like structures that contain seeds, typical of the Cupressaceae family. It thrives in high-altitude environments with cool, moist conditions.
