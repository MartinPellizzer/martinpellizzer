Anthurium ribeiroi

Anthurium ribeiroi, commonly known as the Ribeiro's flamingo flower, is a tropical plant native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its striking, bright red spadix and elongated, heart-shaped spatula. The plant belongs to the Araceae family, which is known for its unique inflorescence structure. Its foliage consists of large, glossy, dark green leaves that are often variegated.

Anthurium ribeiroi thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.
