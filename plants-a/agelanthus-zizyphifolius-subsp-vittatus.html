Agelanthus zizyphifolius subsp. vittatus

Agelanthus zizyphifolius subsp.

The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in shaded, moist environments. Its leaves are simple and alternate, with a rough texture and a slightly serrated margin.

The flowers are small and clustered, often hidden within cup-like structures called cyathia, which is typical of the Euphorbiaceae family.
