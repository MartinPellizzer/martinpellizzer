Alsophila baroumba

Alsophila baroumba, commonly known as the Baroumba tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to eastern Australia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and a crown of feathery fronds that can reach several meters in length. The plant has a thick, fibrous root system that helps it anchor in moist, shaded environments. Its fronds are typically bright green and have a smooth, leathery texture.

Alsophila baroumba plays an important ecological role in providing habitat and shelter for various forest organisms.
