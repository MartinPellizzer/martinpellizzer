Amaryllis fosteri

Amaryllis fosteri, commonly known as the Foster's amaryllis, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to deep red, often with a contrasting stripe. The plant produces large, strap-like leaves that are evergreen and can grow up to 3 feet in length. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Amaryllis fosteri is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
