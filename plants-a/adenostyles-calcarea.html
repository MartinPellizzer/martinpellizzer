Adenostyles calcarea

Adenostyles calcarea, commonly known as the limestone bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in rocky or calcareous soils. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers that are usually pink or purple in color. Its leaves are alternate and lanceolate, often with hairy surfaces.

Adenostyles calcarea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with well-drained soil.
