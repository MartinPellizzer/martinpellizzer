Aloe perryi

Aloe perryi, commonly known as the desert aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are thick and fleshy, designed to store water. The plant produces striking red, orange, or yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. Aloe perryi thrives in arid environments and requires minimal water, making it well-suited for xeriscaping.

Its leaves contain a gel-like substance that is sometimes used for its soothing properties.
