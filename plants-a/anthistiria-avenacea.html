Anthistiria avenacea

Anthistiria avenacea, commonly known as the slender antler orchid, is a flowering plant native to the Andes mountains of South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in high-altitude grasslands and rocky areas. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers with a distinctive, elongated shape that resembles an antler. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it has a short, rhizomatous root system.

Anthistiria avenacea is known for its unique morphology and is often found in moist, well-drained soils.
