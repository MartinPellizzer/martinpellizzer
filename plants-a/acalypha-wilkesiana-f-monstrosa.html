Acalypha wilkesiana f. monstrosa

Acalypha wilkesiana f. monstrosa, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen or croton, is a tropical shrub characterized by its vibrant, colorful foliage.

The plant features palmate leaves with striking variegation, often displaying shades of red, orange, and yellow. It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia. The f. monstrosa form is distinguished by its enlarged, waxy leaves and compact growth habit.

It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
