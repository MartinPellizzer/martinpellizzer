Adenophora stricta var. nanjingensis

Adenophora stricta var. nanjingensis, commonly known as the Nanjing bellflower, is a perennial herb native to eastern China.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale blue. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with a hairy texture and is often found in moist, shaded environments. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.

The species is also used in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.
