Anthurium gracilipedunculatum

Anthurium gracilipedunculatum, commonly known as the slender-stemmed anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are often a deep green with a glossy texture. The plant produces vivid, spadix-like inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, typically in shades of red, orange, or yellow. It thrives in humid, warm environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium gracilipedunculatum is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and unique floral structures.
