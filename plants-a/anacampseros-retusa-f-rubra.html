Anacampseros retusa f. rubra

Anacampseros retusa f. rubra, commonly known as the red-flowered stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, triangular leaves that are typically green with a reddish tinge, especially under sunny conditions. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink to red, which bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its compact growth habit and vibrant blooms make it an attractive addition to arid landscape designs.
