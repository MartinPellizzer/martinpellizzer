Agathophyllum marginatum

Agathophyllum marginatum, commonly known as the coastal daisy or sea daisy, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of southern Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow to orange flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is adapted to saline and coastal conditions. It has narrow, linear leaves and is often found in dunes and along the shoreline.

Agathophyllum marginatum is valued for its ornamental beauty and its ability to stabilize coastal dunes.
