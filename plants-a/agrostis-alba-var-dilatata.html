Agrostis alba var. dilatata

Agrostis alba var. dilatata, commonly known as silvergrass, is a grass species native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

It is characterized by its slender, silver-haired leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed areas such as meadows, roadsides, and fields. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny florets.

Agrostis alba var. dilatata is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its aesthetic appeal and low maintenance requirements.
