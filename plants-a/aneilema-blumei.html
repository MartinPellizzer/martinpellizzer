Aneilema blumei

Aneilema blumei, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces small, clustered yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. Aneilema blumei is typically found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for various ailments.
