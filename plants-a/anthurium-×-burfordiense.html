Anthurium × burfordiense

Anthurium × burfordiense, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a hybrid plant native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped spathe that ranges from deep red to maroon, with a spadix that is typically yellow or orange. The plant thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect light to prevent leaf burn. It has broad, dark green foliage that adds to its ornamental appeal.

Anthurium × burfordiense is popular as a houseplant due to its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
