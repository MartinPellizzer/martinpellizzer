Athyrium zhenfengense

Athyrium zhenfengense, commonly known as the Zhenfeng fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to China.

It belongs to the family Athyriaceae and is characterized by its delicate, bipinnate fronds that are typically bright green. The plant grows in shaded, moist environments, often found in forest undergrowth. Its spore-producing structures, called sori, are arranged in lines on the underside of the fronds.

Athyrium zhenfengense is valued for its ornamental foliage and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
