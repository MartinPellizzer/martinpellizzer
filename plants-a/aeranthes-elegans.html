Aeranthes elegans

Aeranthes elegans, commonly known as the elegant spider orchid, is a species native to South Africa.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in grasslands and open woodland habitats. The plant features a slender, upright stem with a single, showy flower that has a distinctive spider-like appearance. Its flowers are usually white or pale yellow with intricate patterns and a strong, pleasant fragrance.

Aeranthes elegans is known for its delicate beauty and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
