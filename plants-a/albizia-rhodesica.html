Albizia rhodesica

Albizia rhodesica, commonly known as the Rhodes thorn, is a leguminous tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and showy, fragrant flowers that range from white to pink. The plant has a woody, thorny stem and produces flat, pod-like fruits containing seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in savanna and woodland habitats.

Albizia rhodesica is valued for its ornamental qualities and has traditional uses in local medicine.
