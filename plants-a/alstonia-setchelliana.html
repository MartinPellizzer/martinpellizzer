Alstonia setchelliana

Alstonia setchelliana, commonly known as the giant's beard or the tree of heaven, is a striking tropical tree native to the island of Madagascar.

It is characterized by its towering height, often reaching up to 30 meters, and its distinctive, hanging flowers that resemble long, delicate beards. The plant features dark green, glossy leaves that are opposite and elliptical in shape. Its flowers are typically yellow with orange or red markings, and it produces woody capsules containing numerous seeds.

Alstonia setchelliana is also noted for its rapid growth rate and ability to thrive in a variety of soil types.
