Aporum vanhulstijnii

Aporum vanhulstijnii, commonly known as the Van Hulstijn's aporum, is a rare and striking plant native to the tropical rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in diameter, showcasing a deep green color with intricate veining. The plant produces striking, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange, which bloom during the rainy season. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires high levels of moisture and well-draining soil.

Due to its rarity and unique appearance, it is highly sought after by botanists and plant enthusiasts alike.
