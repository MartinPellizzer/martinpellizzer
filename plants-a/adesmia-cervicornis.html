Adesmia cervicornis

Adesmia cervicornis, commonly known as the horned adesmia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its distinctive, curved pods. The plant typically grows in dry, arid environments and has adapted to thrive in nutrient-poor soils. Its small, bright yellow flowers are followed by elongated, twisted fruits that contain the seeds.
