Athanasia glabrescens

Athanasia glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth everlasting, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant produces showy, daisy-like blooms that are typically white or pale yellow, with a central disc of small florets. It is a perennial herb that thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Athanasia glabrescens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in gardens for its long-lasting flowers.
