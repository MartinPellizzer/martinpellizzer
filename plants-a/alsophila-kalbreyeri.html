Alsophila kalbreyeri

Alsophila kalbreyeri, commonly known as the Kalbreyer's tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds that can reach several meters in length. The plant has a distinctive crown of spirally arranged fronds that give it a unique, elegant appearance. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical forests.

Alsophila kalbreyeri plays an important ecological role in its habitat, providing shelter and food for various wildlife species.
