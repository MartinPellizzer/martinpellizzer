Arrabidaea costaricensis

Arrabidaea costaricensis, commonly known as the Costa Rican arrabidaea, is a flowering plant native to Central America.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, also known as the legume or bean family. The plant is characterized by its compound leaves and small, pea-like flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in tropical and subtropical regions, often found in moist, well-drained soils.
