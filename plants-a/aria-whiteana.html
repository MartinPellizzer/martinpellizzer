Aria whiteana

Aria whiteana, commonly known as the white-flowered bell heath, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and related shrubs. The plant is characterized by its small, bell-shaped white flowers that bloom in spring. It typically grows in sandy or rocky soils in coastal regions.

Aria whiteana is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting local wildlife.
