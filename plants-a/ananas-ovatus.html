Ananas ovatus

Ananas ovatus, commonly known as the pineapple guava, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is a member of the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its rosette of fleshy, spathate leaves. The plant produces a unique, fragrant fruit that is both edible and highly aromatic. Its botanical features include a central stem that bears a cluster of flowers, which develop into the fruit.

Ananas ovatus is valued for its ornamental beauty and culinary use in various regions.
