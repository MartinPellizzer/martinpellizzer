Asparagus plumosus

Asparagus plumosus, commonly known as the silver asparagus or feather asparagus, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, fern-like, silver-green foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous yellow flowers that bloom in late spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Asparagus plumosus is also valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
