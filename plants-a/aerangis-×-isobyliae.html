Aerangis × isobyliae

Aerangis × isobyliae, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a cross between Aerangis and Isobryum species.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its elegant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern around the pseudobulbs.

This hybrid is prized in horticulture for its ornamental value and adaptability to various growing conditions.
