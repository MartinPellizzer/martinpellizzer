Agathosma tenuissima

Agathosma tenuissima, commonly known as the narrowleaf buchu, is a small, evergreen shrub native to the Western Cape of South Africa.

It is characterized by its aromatic, narrow leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. The plant belongs to the Rutaceae family, which includes citrus plants, and is known for its pungent scent. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.

Agathosma tenuissima is also valued for its essential oils, which are used in aromatherapy and as a natural remedy.
