Aloe kirkii

Aloe kirkii, commonly known as Kirk's aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are thick, fleshy, and covered in a white, powdery coating called bloom. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow flowers that bloom in spring. Its leaves contain a gel that is often used for its soothing properties.

Aloe kirkii is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water to thrive.
