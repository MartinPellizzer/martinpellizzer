Arisaema dahaiense

Arisaema dahaiense, commonly known as the Dahai arrowhead, is a member of the Araceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in China, Vietnam, and Indonesia. The plant features a distinctive arrow-shaped leaf and a spadix surrounded by a spathe, typical of the genus Arisaema. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The plant is noted for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
