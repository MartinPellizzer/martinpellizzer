Aulomyrcia detergens

Aulomyrcia detergens, commonly known as the soapberry plant, is a species native to Central and South America.

It is a small evergreen tree or shrub that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of small, yellowish-white flowers. Its fruit is a smooth, green, spherical berry that turns yellow when mature.

The plant is notable for its ability to produce a soapy substance when its leaves are crushed, which gives it its common name.
