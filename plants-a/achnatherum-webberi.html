Achnatherum webberi

Achnatherum webberi, commonly known as Webber's needlegrass, is a perennial grass species native to the western United States.

It typically grows in dry, rocky habitats such as slopes and open woodlands. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces spikelets that are clustered in dense panicles. It is adapted to arid environments and is often found in regions with low precipitation.
