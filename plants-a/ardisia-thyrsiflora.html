Ardisia thyrsiflora

Ardisia thyrsiflora, commonly known as the flame bush, is a shrub native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It features opposite, elliptic to ovate leaves that are glossy and dark green. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pinkish flowers followed by bright red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Ardisia thyrsiflora is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to attract wildlife.
