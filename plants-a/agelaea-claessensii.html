Agelaea claessensii

Agelaea claessensii, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves and showy, tubular flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant thrives in humid, well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light. It is a popular ornamental plant due to its compact size and long-lasting blooms.

Agelaea claessensii is often cultivated as a houseplant and is relatively easy to care for with proper watering and temperature conditions.
