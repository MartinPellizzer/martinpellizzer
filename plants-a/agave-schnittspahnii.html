Agave schnittspahnii

Agave schnittspahnii, commonly known as the blue agave or the Mexican blue agave, is a succulent plant native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped growth pattern and thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-gray in color. The plant produces a tall, branched inflorescence that bears small, greenish-white flowers. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Its striking appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in dry climates.
