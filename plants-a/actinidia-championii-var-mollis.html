Actinidia championii var. mollis

Actinidia championii var. mollis, commonly known as the soft-fleshed kiwifruit, is a deciduous vine native to China.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, fuzzy fruits that are typically yellow or brown in color. The plant thrives in temperate climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth. Its fruit is rich in vitamins and antioxidants, making it a nutritious and popular fruit.

The variety is valued for its unique flavor and is often cultivated for both commercial and home gardening purposes.
