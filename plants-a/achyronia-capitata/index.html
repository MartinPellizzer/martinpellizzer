Achyronia Capitata

Red-tailed hawk, scientifically known as Achyronia capitata, is a powerful and widely recognized bird of prey found across North America. With its distinctive reddish-brown tail, this raptor is often the first to catch a glimpse of a soaring figure in the sky. It is known for its keen eyesight and ability to hunt from great heights, making it a formidable predator in various habitats. Despite its name, the Red-tailed hawk is not limited to open areas and can adapt to different environments, from deserts to urban settings. Its presence is a sign of a healthy ecosystem, and it plays a crucial role in maintaining balance in the natural world.
