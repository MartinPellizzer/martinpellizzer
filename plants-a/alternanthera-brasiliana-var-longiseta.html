Alternanthera brasiliana var. longiseta

Alternanthera brasiliana var. longiseta, commonly known as the red flame plant, is a striking aquatic or semi-aquatic plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red or pink foliage, which becomes more intense under strong light conditions. The plant has slender, branching stems that can grow up to several feet in length, making it ideal for background planting in aquariums. Its leaves are elongated and often have a slightly serrated edge, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

It thrives in warm, tropical climates and requires moderate to high water flow for optimal growth.
