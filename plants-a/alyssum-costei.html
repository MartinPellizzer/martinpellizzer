Alyssum costei

Alyssum costei, commonly known as the Sierra foothill alyssum, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically reaches heights of 10 to 15 centimeters. The plant features small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pale pink, blooming in clusters. Its leaves are linear and covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss in its arid habitat.

Alyssum costei is often found in rocky soils and open areas, thriving in full sun.
