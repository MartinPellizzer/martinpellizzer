Arisaema microspadix

Arisaema microspadix, commonly known as the dwarf arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features a distinctive underground tuber and a cluster of narrow, arrow-shaped leaves. The plant produces a spadix, which is a dense, spiky cluster of tiny flowers surrounded by a modified leaf called a spathe. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The species is also valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
