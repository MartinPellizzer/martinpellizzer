Aloe kulalensis

Aloe kulalensis, commonly known as the Kulal aloe, is a succulent plant native to the Arabian Peninsula.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that store water, adapting to the arid environment. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in summer, adding ornamental value. Its leaves contain a gel-like substance used in traditional medicine.

Aloe kulalensis is relatively rare and requires specific care to thrive in cultivation.
