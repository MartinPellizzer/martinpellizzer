Acacia urophylla var. glaberrima

Acacia urophylla var. glaberrima, commonly known as the smooth acacia, is a shrub or small tree native to parts of Africa.

It is characterized by its smooth, often glabrous bark and bipinnate leaves that are typically narrow and linear. The plant produces yellow to golden flowers in dense, globular clusters, which are highly attractive to pollinators. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in savannas and open woodlands.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional uses in local communities.
