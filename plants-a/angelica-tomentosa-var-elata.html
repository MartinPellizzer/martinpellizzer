Angelica tomentosa var. elata

Angelica tomentosa var. elata, commonly known as the tall angelica, is a member of the Apiaceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems that can reach heights of up to 2 meters. The plant features large, compound leaves with a hairy surface, giving it a fuzzy appearance. Its flowers are small and clustered in umbels, typically white or greenish in color.

It thrives in moist, temperate climates and is often found in woodland or wetland habitats.
