Asplenium cocae

Asplenium cocae, commonly known as the cocoa spleen, is a species of fern belonging to the family Aspleniaceae.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that typically grows on tree trunks or branches in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant has delicate, lance-shaped fronds with a dark green color and a serrated margin. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil.

Asplenium cocae is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine in some cultures.
