Ardisia discolor

Ardisia discolor, commonly known as the variegated evergreen, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves with striking white and yellow variegation. The plant produces small, white flowers followed by bright red berries that are attractive to birds. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for shaded garden areas.

Ardisia discolor is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in indoor environments.
