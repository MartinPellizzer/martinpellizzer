Anthistiria gigantea

Anthistiria gigantea, commonly known as the giant anthistiria, is a striking plant native to specific regions in South America.

It is characterized by its large, waxy leaves that can grow up to several feet in length. The plant produces vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from deep red to orange, attracting various pollinators. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates with high humidity and well-drained soil.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological importance, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation programs.
