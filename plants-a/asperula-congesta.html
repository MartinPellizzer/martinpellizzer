Asperula congesta

It is native to Japan and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of showy, pink or white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, acidic soils and thrives in partial to full shade. It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often used in gardens and landscapes.

Asperula congesta is also noted for its hardiness and ability to tolerate a range of environmental conditions.
