Amaryllis barbata

Amaryllis barbata, commonly known as the naked lady, is a bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant produces strap-like leaves that emerge after the flowers have faded. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires a period of dormancy to bloom successfully.

Amaryllis barbata is popular as a ornamental plant due to its vibrant blooms and ease of cultivation.
