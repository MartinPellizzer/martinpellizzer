Arachnorchis thysanochila

Arachnorchis thysanochila, commonly known as the spider orchid, is a rare and striking orchid species native to Australia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, intricate floral structure that resembles a spider's web. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in rainforest or heathland areas. Its flowers are predominantly white or pale pink, with a distinctive, elongated labellum that aids in pollination.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a vulnerable species in some regions.
