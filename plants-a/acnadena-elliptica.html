Acnadena elliptica

Acnadena elliptica, commonly known as the narrow-leaf acnadena, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in grassland and savanna habitats. The plant features narrow, elliptical leaves and produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in clusters. It is adapted to arid conditions and has a deep root system for water absorption.

Acnadena elliptica is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
