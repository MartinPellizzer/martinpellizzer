Alsine verna var. alpestris

Alsine verna var. alpestris, commonly known as alpine sandwort, is a flowering plant native to alpine and arctic regions.

It is a member of the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit. The plant produces small, white or pink flowers that bloom in early summer. Its leaves are typically narrow and covered in fine hairs, adapted to withstand cold and windy conditions.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.
