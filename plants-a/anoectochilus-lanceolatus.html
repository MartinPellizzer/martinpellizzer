Anoectochilus lanceolatus

Anoectochilus lanceolatus, commonly known as the lanceolate slipper orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, lance-shaped leaves and distinctive, slipper-like flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers are usually pale green to white, with intricate patterns that aid in pollination.

Anoectochilus lanceolatus is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
