Austroblechnum raiateense

Austroblechnum raiateense, commonly known as the Raiatea maidenhair fern, is a species of fern native to the Pacific Islands, particularly Tahiti and Moorea.

Austroblechnum raiateense plays a significant role in the local ecosystem, providing habitat for various microorganisms and small invertebrates.
