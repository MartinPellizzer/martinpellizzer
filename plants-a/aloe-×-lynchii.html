Aloe × lynchii

Aloe × lynchii, commonly known as the hybrid aloe or the hybrid aloe plant, is a succulent species native to South Africa.

It is a hybrid between Aloe arborescens and Aloe ferox, resulting in a plant with distinctive orange-red bracts that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a waxy coating, and it is well adapted to arid environments.

Aloe × lynchii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in well-draining soil and full sun.
