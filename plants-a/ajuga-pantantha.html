Ajuga pantantha

Ajuga pantantha, commonly known as carpet bugleweed, is a perennial herb native to eastern North America.

It features low-growing, mat-forming stems that produce small, tubular flowers in shades of blue to purple. The plant has heart-shaped to ovate leaves that are typically hairy and arranged in opposite pairs. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable plant for erosion control and wildlife habitat.
