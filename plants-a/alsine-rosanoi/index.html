Alsine Rosanoi

Alsine rosanoi is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of the Caucasus. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is known for its small, pale blue flowers. This species is listed as endangered due to habitat loss and over-collection. Its unique appearance and limited distribution make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists. Protecting Alsine rosanoi is crucial for preserving biodiversity in its native ecosystem.
