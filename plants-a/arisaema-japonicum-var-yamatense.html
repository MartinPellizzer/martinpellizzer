Arisaema japonicum var. yamatense

Arisaema japonicum var. yamatense, commonly known as the Japanese cobra lily, is a herbaceous perennial native to Japan.

It features striking, arrow-shaped leaves that are often a vibrant green with a reddish tint. The plant produces unique, hooded spathes that enclose bright yellow to orange spadices, resembling a cobra's hood. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and has cultural significance in traditional Japanese gardens.
