Arisaema galeatum

Arisaema galeatum, commonly known as the spiny arrowhead, is a member of the Araceae family.

It is native to parts of East Asia, including China, Japan, and Korea. The plant features a distinctive, arrowhead-shaped leaf with a spiny margin. Its inflorescence consists of a spadix surrounded by a modified leaf called a spathe.

Arisaema galeatum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique foliage.
