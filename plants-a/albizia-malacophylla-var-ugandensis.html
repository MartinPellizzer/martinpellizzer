Albizia malacophylla var. ugandensis

Albizia malacophylla var. ugandensis, commonly known as the Uganda Albizia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to eastern Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant features bipinnate leaves with small, rounded leaflets, and its flowers are typically yellow or white, arranged in racemes. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in woodland and savanna habitats.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential ecological benefits in its native range.
