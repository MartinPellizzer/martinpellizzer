Aspilia floribunda

Aspilia floribunda, commonly known as the African flame flower, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant produces bright orange to red disk flowers surrounded by yellow ray florets, which are highly visible and attract pollinators. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, often growing in rocky or sandy habitats.

Its striking appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
