Atractylis chinensis var. simplicifolia

Atractylis chinensis var. simplicifolia, commonly known as Chinese Atractylis, is a perennial herb native to China.

It features a rosette of broad, hairy, lance-shaped leaves that are often grayish-green in color. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in a panicle during the summer months. It is often found in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to arid conditions.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional medicinal uses.
