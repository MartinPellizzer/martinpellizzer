Allium roseum var. requienii

Allium roseum var. requienii, commonly known as the pink garlic chive, is a striking ornamental plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves that are tinged with a pale pink hue, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, clustered purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and mild garlic-like fragrance.

This variety is relatively easy to grow in well-drained soil and full sun.
