Anthurium ottobuchtienii

Anthurium ottobuchtienii, commonly known as the Otto Buchtien Anthurium, is a striking tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped leaves that can range in color from deep green to variegated patterns. The plant produces striking spadices, which are its showy, often red or orange-colored inflorescences. It thrives in humid, warm environments with bright, indirect light.

Anthurium ottobuchtienii is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
