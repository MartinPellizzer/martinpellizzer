Alternanthera nana

Alternanthera nana, commonly known as dwarf Alternanthera, is a compact, herbaceous plant native to South America.

It features small, oval-shaped leaves that are often brightly colored, ranging from red to pink and purple. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full sunlight.

Due to its ornamental foliage, it is often used in water gardens and as a foreground plant in aquatic setups.
