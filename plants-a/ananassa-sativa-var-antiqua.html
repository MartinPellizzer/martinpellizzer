Ananassa sativa var. antiqua

Ananassa sativa var. antiqua, commonly known as the ancient pineapple, is a less common variety of the pineapple plant.

It is characterized by its smaller size and more aromatic fruit compared to modern cultivars. The plant has a robust stem with a fibrous core, and its leaves are broad and sword-shaped. It is native to South America and thrives in tropical climates with well-drained soil.

This variety is valued for its unique flavor and historical significance in botanical history.
