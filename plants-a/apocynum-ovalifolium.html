Apocynum ovalifolium

Apocynum ovalifolium, commonly known as the ovalleaf dogbane, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in moist, wooded areas. The plant features opposite, oval-shaped leaves and produces clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. Its milky sap contains toxic compounds, which can be harmful to humans and animals.

The plant is part of the Apocynaceae family, known for its showy flowers and medicinal properties.
