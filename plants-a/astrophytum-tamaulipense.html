Astrophytum tamaulipense

Astrophytum tamaulipense, commonly known as the Tamaulipan cactus, is a columnar cactus native to Mexico.

It features a ribbed, cylindrical stem that is typically greenish-gray in color. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It has a shallow root system and is adapted to arid environments.

Astrophytum tamaulipense is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
