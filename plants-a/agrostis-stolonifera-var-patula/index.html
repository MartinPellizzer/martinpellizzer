Agrostis Stolonifera Var. Patula

Annual bluegrass, scientifically known as Agrostis stolonifera var. patula, is a cool-season grass commonly found in temperate regions. It is characterized by its fine texture, low-growing habit, and ability to form dense mats. This plant is often used in turfgrass applications due to its rapid growth and ability to thrive in shaded conditions. However, it is also considered an invasive weed in many areas, outcompeting native vegetation. Its adaptability and persistence make it both a valuable and challenging plant to manage.
