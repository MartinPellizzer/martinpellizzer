Anthurium luzense

Anthurium luzense, commonly known as the Luzon anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves and vibrant, spathiflorous flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its foliage is usually a deep green with prominent veins, and the spathe can range in color from red to orange.

Anthurium luzense is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant.
