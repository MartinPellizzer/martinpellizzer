Anthyllis weldeniana var. tenuicaulis

Anthyllis weldeniana var. tenuicaulis, commonly known as the slender-stemmed anthyllis, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its narrow, cylindrical stems and small, pinkish-white flowers. The plant has trifoliate leaves composed of three oval-shaped leaflets, and it produces small, bean-like pods that contain several seeds.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or dry habitats.
