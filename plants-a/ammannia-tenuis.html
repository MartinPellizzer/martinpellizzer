Ammannia tenuis

Ammannia tenuis, commonly known as slender ammannia, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or red in color. The plant has slender, branching stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found near water bodies.

Ammannia tenuis is a perennial herb that is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens and wetland habitats.
