Alsophila goyazensis

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and feathery fronds. The plant exhibits a unique combination of primitive and advanced features in its reproductive structures. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, typically found in tropical rainforests.
