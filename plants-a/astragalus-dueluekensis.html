Astragalus dueluekensis

Astragalus dueluekensis, commonly known as the Dzüülek Astragalus, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its small, pea-like flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid conditions. Its leaves are compound, and it produces pods that contain several seeds.
