Aeranthes laxiflora

Aeranthes laxiflora, commonly known as the slender-flowered orchid, is a tropical orchid native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America.

It is characterized by its delicate, pendulous flowers that bloom in shades of white and pale pink. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rock crevices. Its leaves are elongated and lance-shaped, often with a waxy coating to reduce water loss.

Aeranthes laxiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
