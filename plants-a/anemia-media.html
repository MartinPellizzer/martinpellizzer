Anemia media

Anemia media, commonly known as the "middle anemic" plant, is a rare and lesser-known species with unique botanical characteristics.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often found in temperate regions. The plant features slender, elongated leaves that are dark green and slightly velvety in texture. Its flowers are small, white, and clustered in loose inflorescences, blooming in early spring.
