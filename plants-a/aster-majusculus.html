Aster majusculus

Aster majusculus, commonly known as the small aster, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands. Its small, clustered flowers bloom in shades of blue to purple, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies.

Aster majusculus is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil and support local wildlife.
