Anacamptis morio f. picta-rosea

Anacamptis morio f. picta-rosea, commonly known as the Roseate Greenhood, is a striking orchid species native to southern Europe.

It features a distinctive hood-shaped flower with a deep greenish-blue hue and a rose-pink lip, which sets it apart from other members of the genus. The plant typically grows in moist, calcareous habitats such as woodlands and grasslands. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, contributing to its overall ornamental appeal.

This orchid is noted for its rarity and is often found in specific microhabitats, making it a prized addition to botanical collections.
