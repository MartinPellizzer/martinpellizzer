Androcymbium exiguum subsp. vogelii

Androcymbium exiguum subsp.

vogelii, commonly known as the Vogel's orchid, is a rare and diminutive orchid species native to specific regions of southern Africa. It is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically display a combination of white and green hues, which help it blend into its natural habitat.

The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, often growing on rocky outcrops or in forest understories. Its botanical features include narrow, grass-like leaves and a short, erect stem.
