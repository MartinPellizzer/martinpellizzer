Aeginetia aeginetia

Aeginetia aeginetia, commonly known as the yellow nightshade, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30-60 cm. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in summer, followed by orange to red berries. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and have a rough texture.

Aeginetia aeginetia is often found in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to Mediterranean climates.
