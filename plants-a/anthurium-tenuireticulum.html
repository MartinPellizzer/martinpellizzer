Anthurium tenuireticulum

Anthurium tenuireticulum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its elongated, narrow leaves that are typically a deep green with a glossy texture. The plant produces striking spadices, which are inflorescences that resemble a spathe, often in shades of red or yellow. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Anthurium tenuireticulum is popular as a decorative plant due to its ornamental foliage and unique floral structures.
