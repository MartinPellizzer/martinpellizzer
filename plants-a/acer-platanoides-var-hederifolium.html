Acer platanoides var. hederifolium

Acer platanoides var. hederifolium, commonly known as the European maple or hedge maple, is a deciduous tree native to Europe.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves that are deeply lobed and have a distinctive wavy edge. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in early spring, followed by winged seeds called samaras. It prefers well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it suitable for temperate climates.

This variety is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and moderate growth habit.
