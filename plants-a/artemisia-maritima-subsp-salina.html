Artemisia maritima subsp. salina

Artemisia maritima subsp.

salina, commonly known as saltwort artemisia, is a perennial herb native to coastal regions of the northern hemisphere. It is characterized by its aromatic, silver-gray foliage and is adapted to saline and sandy environments.

The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dunes and salt marshes.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes used in landscaping for erosion control in coastal areas.
