Aster riddelii

Aster riddelii, commonly known as the Riddel's aster, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies, sunflowers, and other composite flowers. The plant is characterized by its showy, purple to blue flower heads that bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are typically narrow and grass-like, adapted to thrive in high-altitude environments.

Aster riddelii is often found in rocky soils and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
