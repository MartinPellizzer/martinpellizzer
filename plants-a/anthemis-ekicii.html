Anthemis ekicii

Anthemis ekicii, commonly known as the Ekici's fleabane, is a flowering plant native to Turkey.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky and arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its yellow flowers are composed of ray and disk florets, typical of the family.

It is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and slopes.
