Anisopappus schinzii

Anisopappus schinzii, commonly known as Schinz's dandelion, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and alternate leaves. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to arid environments. Its flowers are yellow and produce small, wind-dispersed seeds.

It is often found in desert scrub and grassland habitats.
