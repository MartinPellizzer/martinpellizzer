Aponogeton braunii

Aponogeton braunii, commonly known as the Braun's water arrowhead, is a tropical aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features long, narrow leaves that float on the water surface, with some leaves extending underwater. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom above the water, typically in the summer months. It thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments with moderate light exposure.

Aponogeton braunii is often cultivated in ponds and water gardens for its ornamental value.
