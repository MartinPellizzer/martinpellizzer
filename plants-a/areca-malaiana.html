Areca malaiana

Areca malaiana, commonly known as the Malayan areca palm, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and pinnately compound leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that develop into edible fruits, which are often consumed in the region. Areca malaiana thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in tropical gardens.
