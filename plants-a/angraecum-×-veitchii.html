Angraecum × veitchii

Angraecum × veitchii, commonly known as the Veitch's orchid, is a hybrid species native to Madagascar.

It is a climbing orchid with long, slender stems and large, showy flowers that typically bloom at night. The plant features dark green, strap-like leaves and produces fragrant, white or pale yellow blossoms. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light and requires well-draining soil.

Angraecum × veitchii is prized for its beauty and is often cultivated as a decorative plant in greenhouses and tropical gardens.
