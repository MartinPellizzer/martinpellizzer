Abies chensiensis var. salouenensis

Abies chensiensis var. salouenensis, commonly known as the Salouenensis fir, is a rare variant of the Chinese fir.

It is native to the mountainous regions of eastern China, particularly in Sichuan and Yunnan provinces. This evergreen conifer is characterized by its slender, conical shape and dark green, needle-like leaves. The cones are typically small and clustered, with reddish-brown scales.

It thrives in cool, moist climates and is often found in high-altitude forest ecosystems.
