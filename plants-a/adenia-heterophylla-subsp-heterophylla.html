Adenia heterophylla subsp. heterophylla

Adenia heterophylla subsp.

heterophylla, commonly known as the variable adenia, is a flowering plant native to eastern Australia. It is a shrub characterized by its woody stems and alternate, often variable in shape, leaves.

The plant produces yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, typically appearing in spring. Its fruit is a fleshy, orange-red berry that contains several seeds.

This species is often found in rocky or sandy soils in coastal and inland areas.
