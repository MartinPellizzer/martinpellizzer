Armeria maritima f. spectabilis

Armeria maritima f. spectabilis, commonly known as the showy thrift, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of the northern hemisphere.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and its profuse display of small, bright pink to white flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained, sandy or gravelly soils and is highly tolerant of salt spray and harsh coastal conditions. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are evergreen in milder climates.

This cultivar is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and hardiness in challenging environments.
