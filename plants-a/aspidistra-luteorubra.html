Aspidistra luteorubra

Aspidistra luteorubra, commonly known as the red-leaved aspidistra, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a evergreen perennial plant characterized by its dark green, broad, strap-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. Aspidistra luteorubra is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and its tolerance to indoor environments.

It is often cultivated as a decorative foliage plant due to its attractive, glossy leaves.
