Aristida plumosa var. sokotrana

Aristida plumosa var. sokotrana, commonly known as the Sokotra feathergrass, is a unique grass species native to the island of Socotra.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery appearance, with slender, branched inflorescences that give it a soft, plume-like look. The plant typically grows in arid and semi-arid environments, adapting to the harsh conditions of its native habitat. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a silvery-blue hue that helps reflect sunlight.

This variety is part of the Aristida genus, which includes many grasses adapted to dry climates across Africa and Asia.
