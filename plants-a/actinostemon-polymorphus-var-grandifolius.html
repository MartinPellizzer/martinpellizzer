Actinostemon polymorphus var. grandifolius

Actinostemon polymorphus var. grandifolius, commonly known as the grandifolius actinostemon, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to certain regions of South America.

It features a basal rosette of thick, fleshy, and often hairy leaves that are lanceolate to ovate in shape. The plant produces striking, showy inflorescences composed of numerous small, tubular flowers arranged in a dense spike. These flowers are typically pink to purple in color and bloom during the warmer months.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in well-drained soils with partial to full sun exposure.
