Arisaema averyanovii

Arisaema averyanovii, commonly known as the Yunnan arrowhead, is a rare and ornamental plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its unique inflorescence known as a spadix, which is enclosed by a modified leaf called a spathe. The plant produces striking, asymmetrical flowers with a greenish-yellow spathe and a spadix that bears both male and female flowers. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forested areas at high altitudes.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a valuable species for botanical study and conservation efforts.
