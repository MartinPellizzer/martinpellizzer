Asplenium klossii

Asplenium klossii, commonly known as the Kloss's spleenwort, is a rare and enigmatic fern belonging to the family Aspleniaceae.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen foliage, with dark green, leathery fronds that are typically short and dense. Native to the high-altitude regions of New Guinea, this plant thrives in cool, moist, and shaded environments. Its reproductive structures, including sporangia, are clustered on the undersides of its leaves, typical of many ferns.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, Asplenium klossii is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
