Allium cyathophorum

Allium cyathophorum, commonly known as the globe onion, is a bulbous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features a cluster of small, rounded bulbs that resemble a globe, giving it its common name. The plant produces tall, slender stems with narrow, grass-like leaves. Its flowers are typically white or pale pink, arranged in a spherical inflorescence.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and as a source of edible bulbs.
