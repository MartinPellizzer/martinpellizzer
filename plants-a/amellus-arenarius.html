Amellus arenarius

Amellus arenarius, commonly known as the sand lily, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are linear and grass-like, often growing close to the ground.

Amellus arenarius is adapted to arid environments and is often found in coastal or dune habitats.
