Achyranthes lanaiensis

Achyranthes lanaiensis, commonly known as the Lanai morning glory, is a flowering plant native to the Hawaiian island of Lanai.

It belongs to the family Amaranthaceae and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in the early morning. The plant typically grows as an annual herb with slender stems and heart-shaped leaves. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas and disturbed habitats.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species in Hawaii.
