Aglaia beccarii

Aglaia beccarii, commonly known as the Beccari's aglaia, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Meliaceae family, which includes mahogany and other hardwoods. The plant is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves and small, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or greenish-yellow. Its fruits are drupes, containing a single seed enclosed in a hard, woody pulp.
