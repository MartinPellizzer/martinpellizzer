Achyronia Ciliaris

Achyronia ciliaris is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to specific regions of South America. It is known for its striking, pale pink to white blooms that appear during the warmer months. This species thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories. Its unique appearance and limited distribution make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists. Due to habitat loss, Achyronia ciliaris faces threats to its survival and requires careful protection.
