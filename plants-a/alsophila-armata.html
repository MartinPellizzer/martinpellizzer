Alsophila armata

Alsophila armata, commonly known as the slender tree fern, is a species native to Southeast Asia and the Pacific Islands.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and a crown of feathery fronds that can reach several meters in length. The plant has a unique ability to form a trunk-like structure from its rootstock, which allows it to grow upright rather than spreading. Its fronds are typically bright green and have a delicate, lace-like appearance.

Alsophila armata thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.
