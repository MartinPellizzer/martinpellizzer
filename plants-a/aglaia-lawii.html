Aglaia lawii

Aglaia lawii, commonly known as the yellow angel's trumpets, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Meliaceae and is characterized by its smooth, gray bark and pinnate leaves. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, yellow-orange flowers that bloom throughout the year.
