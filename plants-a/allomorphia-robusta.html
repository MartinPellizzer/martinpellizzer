Allomorphia robusta

Allomorphia robusta, commonly known as the robust allomorph, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical regions.

It features a robust, upright stem with alternate, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale pink, typically blooming in the spring. Its root system is fibrous and extensive, allowing it to thrive in well-drained soils.

Allomorphia robusta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its resilience and aesthetic qualities.
