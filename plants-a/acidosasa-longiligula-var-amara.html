Acidosasa longiligula var. amara

Acidosasa longiligula var. amara, commonly known as the bitter leaf lettuce, is a variant of the longiligula species within the genus Acidosasa.

It is characterized by its elongated, narrow leaves that are typically dark green with a slightly bitter taste. This plant thrives in cool climates and prefers well-drained, loamy soil. It is often cultivated for its edible leaves, which are used in various culinary applications.

The plant's botanical structure includes a basal rosette of leaves with a short, upright stem.
