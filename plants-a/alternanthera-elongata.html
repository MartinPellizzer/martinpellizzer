Alternanthera elongata

Alternanthera elongata, commonly known as the red stem plant, is a striking aquatic or semi-aquatic plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its elongated, often reddish stems and small, lance-shaped leaves that can vary in color depending on light conditions. The plant thrives in warm, shallow waters and is popular in aquariums for its vibrant appearance. It prefers moderate to high light and can tolerate a range of water conditions, making it relatively easy to care for.

Its unique coloration and growth pattern make it a favorite among aquatic plant enthusiasts.
