Aristida spicata

Aristida spicata, commonly known as slender dropseed, is a perennial grass native to North America.

It is characterized by its fine, grass-like foliage and produces slender, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as prairies and sandy soils. It has narrow, linear leaves and is often used in native plant landscaping due to its ornamental value.

Aristida spicata is also valued for its role in soil stabilization and wildlife habitat.
