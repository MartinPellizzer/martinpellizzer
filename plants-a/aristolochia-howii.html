Aristolochia howii

Aristolochia howii, commonly known as the California birthwort, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its unique, pipe-shaped flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color. The plant produces heart-shaped leaves with a rough, hairy texture. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Aristolochia howii is also known for its medicinal properties, though its use is limited due to potential toxicity.
