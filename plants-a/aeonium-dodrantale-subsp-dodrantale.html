Aeonium dodrantale subsp. dodrantale

Aeonium dodrantale subsp.

dodrantale, commonly known as the Rockrose Aeonium, is a succulent plant native to the Canary Islands. It features rosettes of thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with red or purple margins.

The plant produces tall, branching stems with small yellow flowers during the spring or early summer. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Its striking appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
