Aralia henryi

Aralia henryi, commonly known as Chinese aralia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features compound leaves with a feathery appearance, typically arranged in a whorled pattern along the stems. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers in umbels, which are followed by berries that turn red when mature. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its attractive foliage and unique growth habit.
