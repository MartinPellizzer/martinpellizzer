Acinodendron martiusianum

Acinodendron martiusianum, commonly known as the Martiusian acacia, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant produces pods that contain seeds, which are often dispersed by wind. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in savannah and woodland habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens.
