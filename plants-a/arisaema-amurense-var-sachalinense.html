Arisaema amurense var. sachalinense

Arisaema amurense var. sachalinense, commonly known as the Japanese arrowhead, is a herbaceous perennial native to East Asia.

It features a distinctive arrow-shaped leaf that is often deeply lobed, giving it a unique appearance. The plant produces a spadix surrounded by a modified leaf called a spathe, which is typically green or white in color. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
