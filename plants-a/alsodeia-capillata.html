Alsodeia capillata

Alsodeia capillata, commonly known as the slender alsike, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is characterized by its leguminous traits and often nitrogen-fixing abilities. The plant features trifoliate leaves and produces small, pea-like flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows and woodland edges.

Alsodeia capillata is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting pollinators.
