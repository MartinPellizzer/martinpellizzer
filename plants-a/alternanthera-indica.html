Alternanthera indica

Alternanthera indica, commonly known as red leaf sorrel, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red, pink, or purple foliage, which becomes more intense under full sunlight. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its striking leaves.
