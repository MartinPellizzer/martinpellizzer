Astragalus supranubias var. brevidens

Astragalus supranubias var. brevidens, commonly known as the short-stemmed cloud alpine.

It is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compact growth habit and small, pea-like flowers. Native to the alpine regions of South America, it thrives in rocky and well-drained soils at high elevations. Its leaves are typically trifoliate, with small, hairy leaflets that help reduce water loss in its harsh environment.
