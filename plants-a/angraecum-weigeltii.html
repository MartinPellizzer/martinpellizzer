Angraecum weigeltii

Angraecum weigeltii, commonly known as the Wiegelt's star orchid, is a rare and striking epiphytic orchid native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its long, slender stems and showy, star-shaped flowers that typically bloom at night. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences with white to pale blue petals and a distinctive, elongated lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, porous media for optimal growth.

Angraecum weigeltii is valued for its unique appearance and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
