Alniphyllum fortunei f. hypoglaucum

Alniphyllum fortunei f. hypoglaucum, commonly known as the variegated alniphyllum, is a variant of the alniphyllum plant native to China.

It is a small evergreen shrub characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves with distinctive white or yellow variegation. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers in the spring, followed by red berries that are edible but not commonly consumed by humans. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its unique foliage makes it a desirable addition to shaded gardens and rockeries.
