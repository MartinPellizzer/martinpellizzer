Auxopus kamerunensis

Auxopus kamerunensis, commonly known as the Cameroon star grass, is a flowering plant native to Cameroon and neighboring regions of Central Africa.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
