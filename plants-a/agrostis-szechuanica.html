Agrostis szechuanica

Agrostis szechuanica, commonly known as the Sichuan bentgrass, is a perennial grass species native to China.

It is characterized by its fine, tufted growth habit and slender, wiry stems. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences with pale green to brown florets. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Agrostis szechuanica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is used in landscaping and as a ground cover.
