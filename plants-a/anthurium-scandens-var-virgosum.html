Anthurium scandens var. virgosum

Anthurium scandens var. virgosum, commonly known as the climbing anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped leaves that are typically bright green with a metallic sheen. The plant produces striking spadix inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, which can range from white to red. It thrives in humid, warm environments and requires indirect sunlight for optimal growth.

Anthurium scandens var. virgosum is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and unique flowering structure.
