Aulomyrcia obtusa var. grandifolia

Aulomyrcia obtusa var. grandifolia, commonly known as the large-flowered aulomyrcia, is a species native to specific regions of South America.

It is a shrub or small tree characterized by its broad, elliptical leaves that are usually leathery and glossy. Its fruit is a small, woody capsule that contains several seeds.
