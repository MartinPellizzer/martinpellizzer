Acrostichum aubertii var. mollissimum

Acrostichum aubertii var. mollissimum, commonly known as the silky fern, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, lance-shaped, and deeply pinnate fronds that are covered in a fine, silky texture. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found near water bodies. Its fronds can grow up to two meters in length, making it a striking addition to tropical gardens.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its unique foliage.
