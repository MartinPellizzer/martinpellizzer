Astrophytum myriostigma var. strongylogonum

Astrophytum myriostigma var. strongylogonum, commonly known as the strongylogonum variation of the star cactus, is a succulent plant native to the deserts of northern Mexico.

It features a short, cylindrical stem with a dense covering of spines and a star-shaped pattern of areoles. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months, adding a striking visual contrast to its spiky form. Its thick, fleshy stems store water, allowing it to survive in arid conditions.

This variety is prized for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, making it a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts.
