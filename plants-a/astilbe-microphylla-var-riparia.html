Astilbe microphylla var. riparia

Astilbe microphylla var. riparia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf astilbe, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to eastern North America.

It is characterized by its finely cut, dark green leaves that form a dense basal rosette. The plant produces upright spikes of small, fluffy, pinkish-white flowers in late summer. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found along stream banks and in woodland areas.

Astilbe microphylla var. riparia is valued in gardens for its ornamental foliage and delicate floral display.
