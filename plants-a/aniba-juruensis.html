Aniba juruensis

Aniba juruensis, commonly known as the Juruense myrtle, is a small evergreen shrub native to the Amazon rainforest.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurels and cinnamon trees. The plant is characterized by its aromatic, dark green leaves and small, white flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruits are small drupes that contain a single seed, typical of the Lauraceae family.

Aniba juruensis is valued for its potential medicinal properties and is often found in moist, shaded forest understories.
