Achyranthes bidentata var. villosa

Achyranthes bidentata var. villosa, commonly known as hairy cow knee, is a variant of the cow knee plant native to Asia.

It is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves, which give it its common name. The plant typically grows in moist environments such as riverbanks and wetlands. Its flowers are small and clustered, often with a reddish or purple hue.

It is valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
