Astronia scabrida

Astronia scabrida, commonly known as the rough astronia, is a shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its rough, bark-like texture and small, clustered leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom during the wet season. Astronia scabrida is often found in humid, lowland areas with well-drained soil.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
