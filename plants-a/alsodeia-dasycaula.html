Alsodeia dasycaula

Alsodeia dasycaula, commonly known as the woolly alsodeia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage and showy blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid environments. Its hairy leaves help reduce water loss, making it well adapted to drought conditions.

It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
