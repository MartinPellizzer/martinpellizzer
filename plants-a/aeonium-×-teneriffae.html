Aeonium × teneriffae

Aeonium × teneriffae, commonly known as the Tree Aeonium, is a hybrid succulent plant native to the Canary Islands.

It features a thick, tree-like stem with rosettes of fleshy, blue-green leaves that are often covered in a powdery waxy coating. The plant produces tall, branching inflorescences with yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with plenty of sunlight.

Aeonium × teneriffae is popular in rock gardens and as a decorative indoor plant due to its striking appearance and low maintenance requirements.
