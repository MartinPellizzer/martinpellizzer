Artemisia tongtchouanensis

Artemisia tongtchouanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan wormwood, is a perennial shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, silver-green foliage. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clustered inflorescences, typical of the genus Artemisia. It thrives in dry, rocky environments and is often found in alpine regions.

Due to its medicinal properties, it has been used in traditional Chinese medicine for various ailments.
