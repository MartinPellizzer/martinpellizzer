Ardisia alajuelae

Ardisia alajuelae, commonly known as the Costa Rican beautyberry, is a species of flowering plant in the family Ericaceae.

It is native to Central America, particularly found in the cloud forests of Costa Rica and Panama. Ardisia alajuelae is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its attractive foliage and unique fruit display.
