Aglaia nyaruensis

Aglaia nyaruensis, commonly known as the Java aglaia, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Meliaceae family, which includes mahogany and other valuable hardwoods. The plant is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers that appear in clusters. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds.
