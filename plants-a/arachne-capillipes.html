Arachne capillipes

Arachne capillipes, commonly known as the slender nettle, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Urticaceae.

It is characterized by its soft, hairy stems and heart-shaped leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It is native to various regions in Europe and Asia, often found in moist, shaded environments.

Arachne capillipes is known for its stinging hairs, which can cause skin irritation upon contact.
