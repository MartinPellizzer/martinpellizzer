Anemone multifida f. leucantha

Anemone multifida f. leucantha, commonly known as the white-rayed anemone, is a variant of the multifida anemone characterized by its striking white petals.

It is a perennial herb native to the eastern United States, typically found in moist woodlands and shaded areas. The plant features a short, upright stem with palmately divided, lobed leaves that are often hairy. Its flowers, which bloom in spring, consist of showy white petals surrounding a cluster of yellow stamens.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in partially shaded garden conditions.
