Aracium mauritianum

Aracium mauritianum, commonly known as the Mauritius starwood, is a rare endemic plant native to the island of Mauritius.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its distinctive star-shaped leaves and yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky and coastal areas, adapted to the island's unique climate and soil conditions. It plays an important role in the local ecosystem, providing habitat and food for native insect species.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered endangered and is the subject of conservation efforts.
