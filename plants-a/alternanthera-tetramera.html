Alternanthera tetramera

Alternanthera tetramera, commonly known as the red-stemmed Alternanthera, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking red stems and lance-shaped leaves that often display a variegated pattern of green and red. The plant produces small, inconspicuous yellow flowers arranged in spikes, which are typically borne on the upper stems. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This ornamental plant is popular in water gardens and as a foliage plant due to its vibrant colors and ease of care.
