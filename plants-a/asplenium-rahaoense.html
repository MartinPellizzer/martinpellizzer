Asplenium rahaoense

Asplenium rahaoense, commonly known as the Rahao spleenwort, is a rare fern species native to the island of Mauritius.

It belongs to the family Aspleniaceae and is characterized by its compact, evergreen foliage. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as rocky crevices or forest undergrowth. Its fronds are lanceolate, with a serrated margin and a dark green coloration.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species.
