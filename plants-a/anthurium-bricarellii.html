Anthurium bricarellii

Anthurium bricarellii, commonly known as the Bricarellii Philodendron, is a tropical plant native to Central America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces small, spadix-like inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, which can range from red to orange. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.

Anthurium bricarellii is popular as a houseplant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
