Actinidia jiankouensis

Actinidia jiankouensis, commonly known as the Jiankou actinidia, is a rare species native to China.

It belongs to the Actinidiaceae family and is characterized by its climbing habit and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. Its fruit is a small, yellow berry that ripens in late summer, though it is not widely cultivated.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered an endangered species.
