Achyranthes ciliata

Achyranthes ciliata, commonly known as the hairy smartweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Polygonaceae family.

It is characterized by its upright, branched stems and alternate, lance-shaped leaves with hairy margins. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It is native to tropical and subtropical regions and is often found in wetlands or disturbed soils.

Achyranthes ciliata is known for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
