Adiantum altissimum

Adiantum altissimum, commonly known as the royal fern, is a species of fern native to the southeastern United States.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in swampy areas or along stream banks.

The fronds are usually evergreen, providing a lush, tropical look throughout the year.
