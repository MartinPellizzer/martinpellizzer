Anthriscus keniensis

Anthriscus keniensis, commonly known as Kenyan cow parsley, is a flowering plant native to East Africa.

It belongs to the Apiaceae family, which includes parsley, carrots, and dill, and is characterized by its finely divided leaves and umbel-shaped flower clusters.
