Abutilon muticum var. parvifolium

Abutilon muticum var. parvifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaf abutilon, is a flowering plant native to parts of South America.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features heart-shaped leaves that are usually smaller and more narrow compared to the typical abutilon species. Its flowers are pendulous and often yellow or orange, adding ornamental value to gardens.

It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate to high levels of sunlight.
