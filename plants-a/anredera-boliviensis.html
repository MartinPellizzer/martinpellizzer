Anredera boliviensis

Anredera boliviensis, commonly known as the silver vine, is a climbing plant native to South America.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a silvery underside, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are typically clustered in panicles. It is often found growing in shaded, moist environments such as forests or rocky areas.

Anredera boliviensis is known for its rapid growth and is sometimes used in horticulture for its ornamental value.
