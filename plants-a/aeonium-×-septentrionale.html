Aeonium × septentrionale

Aeonium × septentrionale, commonly known as the Blue Cheddar or Blue Star, is a hybrid succulent plant native to the Canary Islands.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy, blue-gray leaves that are densely packed and covered in a fine, powdery coating. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow flowers in the spring. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit and striking color make it a visually appealing addition to both indoor and outdoor landscapes.
