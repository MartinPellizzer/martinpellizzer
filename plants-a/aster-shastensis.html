Aster shastensis

Aster shastensis, commonly known as Shasta aster, is a perennial herb native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that range in color from white to purple. The plant typically grows in open meadows and rocky areas at high elevations. It has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

Aster shastensis plays an important role in supporting local pollinators and is valued for its ornamental beauty.
