Anthurium dolichostachyum

Anthurium dolichostachyum, commonly known as the long-stalked anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are often a deep green with a reddish or purple underside. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-like inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, typically in shades of red, orange, or yellow. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect sunlight for optimal growth.

Anthurium dolichostachyum is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
