Anemone minuta

Anemone minuta, commonly known as the dwarf anemone, is a small flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It typically grows in moist, wooded areas and thrives in partial to full shade. The plant produces delicate, white to pale pink flowers with a distinctive, hairy center. Its leaves are heart-shaped and have a soft, velvety texture.

Anemone minuta is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and its ability to naturalize in suitable conditions.
