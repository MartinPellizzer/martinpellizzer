Anthemis macrotis

Anthemis macrotis, commonly known as the large-flowered everlasting, is a flowering plant native to the southern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant produces showy yellow flowers with a central disk, and its leaves are typically linear and aromatic. Anthemis macrotis thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed areas.
