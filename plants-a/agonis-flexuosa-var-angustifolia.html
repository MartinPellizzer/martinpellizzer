Agonis flexuosa var. angustifolia

Agonis flexuosa var. angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved m absolutely, is a small evergreen shrub native to southern Australia.

It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, white flowers in clusters during the spring, which are followed by woody, cone-like fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and heathland environments.

Due to its ornamental value and hardy nature, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
