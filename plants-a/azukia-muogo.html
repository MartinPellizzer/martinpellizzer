Azukia muogo

Azukia muogo, commonly known as the African bean, is a leguminous plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, pod-like fruits. The plant is valued for its edible seeds, which are rich in protein and used in various traditional dishes. Azukia muogo thrives in well-drained soils and requires a warm, humid climate for optimal growth.

It also plays a role in soil enrichment due to its nitrogen-fixing ability.
