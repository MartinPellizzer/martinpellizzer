Auxopus madagascariensis

Auxopus madagascariensis, commonly known as the Madagascar spider orchid, is a species of orchid native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its unique, spider-like appearance, with long, thin leaves and a distinctive flower structure. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments within the rainforests of Madagascar. Its flowers are usually green or yellowish-green, with intricate patterns that resemble spider webs.

Auxopus madagascariensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by enthusiasts of rare and exotic plants.
