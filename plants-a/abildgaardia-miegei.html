Abildgaardia miegei

Abildgaardia miegei, commonly known as the Cape orchid, is a species of orchid native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is known for its ornamental value in horticulture.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is often cultivated in controlled environments.
