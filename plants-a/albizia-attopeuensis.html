Albizia attopeuensis

Albizia attopeuensis, commonly known as the Attopeu Albizia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in Laos and Vietnam.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is characterized by its leguminous properties and nitrogen-fixing capabilities. The plant features compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces small, fragrant, pod-like fruits.

It is often found in moist, tropical forest environments and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
