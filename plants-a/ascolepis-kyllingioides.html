Ascolepis kyllingioides

Ascolepis kyllingioides, commonly known as the Cape daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and has a compact, shrub-like form. Its leaves are narrow and hairy, while the flowers bloom in clusters.

Ascolepis kyllingioides is well adapted to arid conditions and is often used in gardens for its ornamental value.
