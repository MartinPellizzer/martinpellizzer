Aulojusticia linifolia

Aulojusticia linifolia, commonly known as the silkyleaf trumpetvine, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

The plant produces vibrant yellow or orange tubular flowers that attract pollinators such as bees and hummingbirds.
