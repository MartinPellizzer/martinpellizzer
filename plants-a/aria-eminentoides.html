Aria eminentoides

Aria eminentoides, commonly known as the silverleaf arrowhead, is a herbaceous plant native to the wetlands of southern Africa.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a silvery underside, which give it its common name. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in clusters during the rainy season. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found near water bodies.

Its unique appearance and adaptability make it a notable species in its native habitat.
