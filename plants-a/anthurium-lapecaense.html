Anthurium lapecaense

Anthurium lapecaense, commonly known as the Lapecaense arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and striking spadix inflorescence. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its foliage is typically a deep green with a waxy texture, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers surrounded by a colorful spathe.

Anthurium lapecaense is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is appreciated in both indoor and outdoor gardening.
