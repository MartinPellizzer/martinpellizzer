Acer nigrum var. sinuosum

Acer nigrum var. sinuosum, commonly known as the sinuous maple, is a deciduous tree native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange foliage in autumn, which makes it a popular ornamental plant. The leaves are typically palmately lobed with a distinctive sinuous margin, contributing to its unique appearance. It prefers moist, well-drained soils and partial to full sunlight.

This variety is valued for its aesthetic appeal and adaptability to various garden settings.
