Aloe thorncroftii

Aloe thorncroftii, commonly known as the Cape Town aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a waxy texture, and it produces striking yellow-orange flowers during the summer months. The plant is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its leaves to survive dry conditions. It is often found growing in rocky or sandy soils, and it thrives in full sun.

Aloe thorncroftii is valued not only for its ornamental appearance but also for its potential medicinal properties.
