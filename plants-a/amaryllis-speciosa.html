Amaryllis speciosa

Amaryllis speciosa, commonly known as the showy amaryllis, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It produces tall, upright stems that bear large, showy flowers in shades of pink, red, and white. The leaves are broad, strap-like, and emerge from the base of the plant. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

This plant is popular for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
