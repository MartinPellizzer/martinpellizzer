Agrostis condensata

Agrostis condensata, commonly known as slender bentgrass, is a low-growing grass species native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

It typically forms dense, mat-like growth and has narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences, with each spikelet containing two to three florets. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows, lawns, and along stream banks.

Agrostis condensata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping and turfgrass management.
