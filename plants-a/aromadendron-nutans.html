Aromadendron nutans

Aromadendron nutans, commonly known as the hanging aromatic tree, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

The plant belongs to the Rutaceae family and is known for its ornamental value due to its unique appearance. Its leaves are simple and opposite, with a glossy surface, while its fruit is a small, round drupe.
