Achillea millefolium var. nigrescens

Achillea millefolium var. nigrescens, commonly known as dark purple yarrow, is a variant of the common yarrow plant.

It is a perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia, characterized by its dark purple to almost black flower heads. The plant features finely divided, fern-like leaves and is known for its aromatic, often slightly bitter scent. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in meadows and dry grasslands.

This variety is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
