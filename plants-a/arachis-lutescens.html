Arachis lutescens

Arachis lutescens, commonly known as the yellow groundnut, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family.

It is native to South America and is characterized by its yellow flowers and edible seeds. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, thriving in well-drained soils. It has compound leaves and produces pods containing small, yellowish seeds.

Due to its ornamental value and potential agricultural uses, it is sometimes cultivated for both decorative and nutritional purposes.
