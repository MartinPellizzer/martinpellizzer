Asplenium herpetopteris f. nigropilosum

Asplenium herpetopteris f. nigropilosum, commonly known as the black-hairy shield fern, is a fern species characterized by its distinctive foliage.

It features lance-shaped, dark green fronds with a dense covering of short, black hairs, giving it a unique appearance. The plant belongs to the family Aspleniaceae and is typically found in shaded, moist environments. Its rhizomes are short and creeping, supporting the growth of its delicate fronds.

This fern is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in shaded gardens.
