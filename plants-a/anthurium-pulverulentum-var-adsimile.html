Anthurium pulverulentum var. adsimile

Anthurium pulverulentum var. adsimile, commonly known as the velvet anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its dark green, velvety leaves that are often marked with a faint white or silver pattern. The plant produces striking spadices, which are large, colorful, and often red or orange in color. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical landscapes.
