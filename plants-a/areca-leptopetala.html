Areca leptopetala

Areca leptopetala, commonly known as the slender-leaf areca, is a species of palm native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, arching leaves that are typically light green in color. The plant produces small, white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by elongated, three-angled fruits. It thrives in tropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Areca leptopetala is relatively low-maintenance and suitable for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.
