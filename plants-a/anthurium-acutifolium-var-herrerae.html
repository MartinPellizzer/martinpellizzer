Anthurium acutifolium var. herrerae

Anthurium acutifolium var. herrerae, commonly known as the Herrera's spathiflorum, is a striking tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a metallic sheen. The plant produces vibrant, spathiflorum-like inflorescences, which are often red or orange in color, surrounded by a deep green spathe. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil.

This variety is popular among plant enthusiasts for its ornamental value and ease of care.
