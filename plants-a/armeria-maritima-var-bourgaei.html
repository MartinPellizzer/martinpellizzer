Armeria maritima var. bourgaei

Armeria maritima var. bourgaei, commonly known as the coastal thrift, is a low-growing, evergreen perennial native to coastal areas of southern California and Baja California.

It features dense clusters of bright white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has succulent, fleshy leaves that help it retain moisture in its arid, coastal habitat. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

This variety is known for its hardiness and ability to withstand salt spray and wind.
