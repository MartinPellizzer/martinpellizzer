Amaryllis reticulata var. striatifolia

Amaryllis reticulata var. striatifolia, commonly known as the Striate Amaryllis, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and red, often with intricate streaking or striping. The plant produces large, broad leaves that are typically striped or variegated, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

Its blooming period typically occurs in late spring to early summer, adding vibrant color to gardens during that time.
