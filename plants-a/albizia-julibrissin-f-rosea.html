Albizia julibrissin f. rosea

Albizia julibrissin f. rosea, commonly known as the roseum or pink silk tree, is a cultivar of the silk tree, native to China.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery foliage and showy, pink to lavender-pink flower clusters that bloom in late spring. The plant is a deciduous legume, belonging to the Fabaceae family, and is known for its rapid growth and adaptability to various soil types. Its flowers are often used in ornamental horticulture for their soft, fluffy appearance and fragrance.

The plant also has nitrogen-fixing abilities, making it beneficial for soil enrichment.
