Aralia franchetii

Aralia franchetii, commonly known as the Chinese angelica or Franchet's aralia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features compound leaves that are typically pinnately arranged, with a feathery appearance. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers in umbel-like clusters during the summer months. Its berries are red or black and contain several seeds.

Aralia franchetii is valued in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.
