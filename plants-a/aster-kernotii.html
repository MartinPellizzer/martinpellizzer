Aster kernotii

Aster kernotii, commonly known as the Kernot's aster, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils in arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its flowers are usually blue to purple in color, with ray florets surrounding a central disk.

Aster kernotii is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and sagebrush steppe.
