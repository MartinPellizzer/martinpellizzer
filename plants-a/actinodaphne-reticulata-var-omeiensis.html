Actinodaphne reticulata var. omeiensis

Actinodaphne reticulata var. omeiensis, commonly known as the Omei laurel, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is native to China, specifically the Sichuan province, and is characterized by its aromatic evergreen leaves and small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows as a small tree or shrub, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are leathery, dark green, and have a distinctive reticulate venation pattern.

The fruit is a small, dark berry that matures in the autumn, contributing to its ecological importance in local habitats.
