Alsinanthemum europaeum var. latifolium

Alsinanthemum europaeum var. latifolium, commonly known as the broadleaf alsinanthemum, is a flowering plant native to Europe.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and grasslands. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, with a slightly hairy surface.

It produces small yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months.
