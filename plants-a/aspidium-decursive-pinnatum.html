Aspidium decursive-pinnatum

Aspidium decursive-pinnatum, commonly known as the narrow-pinnate water fern, is a species of aquatic fern native to parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its finely divided, pinnate leaves that are often submerged in water. The plant typically grows in slow-moving or still freshwater environments such as ponds and streams. Its fronds are usually dark green and have a delicate, feathery appearance.

Aspidium decursive-pinnatum plays a role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat and oxygen for various aquatic organisms.
