Argyrolobium variopile

Argyrolobium variopile, commonly known as the variable silver globular.

It is a flowering plant native to southern Africa, characterized by its globular shape and silver-green foliage. The plant produces small, pink to purple flowers during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
