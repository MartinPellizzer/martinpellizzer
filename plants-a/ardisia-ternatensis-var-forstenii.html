Ardisia ternatensis var. forstenii

Ardisia ternatensis var. forstenii, commonly known as the Forsten's beautyberry, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Ericaceae and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers that are followed by bright purple berries. These berries are edible but often have a slight bitterness.
