Achatocarpus nigricans

Achatocarpus nigricans, commonly known as black thorn.

It is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia, characterized by its spiny stems and small, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, dark purple flowers that are followed by woody, three-lobed fruits. It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often found in coastal areas and savannas.

The plant has a slow growth rate and is adapted to withstand drought conditions.
