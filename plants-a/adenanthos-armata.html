Adenanthos armata

Adenanthos armata, commonly known as the narrow-leafed adenanthos, is a small shrub native to southern Australia.

It is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and clusters of small, yellowish flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is adapted to dry, coastal environments. It is often found in heathlands and coastal dunes, where it thrives in full sun.

Adenanthos armata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its hardy nature and unique foliage.
