Arisaema thunbergii subsp. autumnale

Arisaema thunbergii subsp.

autumnale, commonly known as the autumn arum, is a herbaceous perennial native to East Asia. It features a distinctive arrow-shaped leaf with a spadix and a spathe, typical of the Araceae family.

The plant produces striking, pale green to yellowish spadices that emerge in late summer to early autumn. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas.

The subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage and seasonal blooms.
