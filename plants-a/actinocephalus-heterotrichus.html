Actinocephalus heterotrichus

Actinocephalus heterotrichus, commonly known as the star-shaped liverwort, is a non-vascular plant belonging to the family Porellaceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive, star-like thallus structure composed of overlapping, leaf-like lobes.
