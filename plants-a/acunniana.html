Acunniana

Acunniana, commonly known as the rare desert orchid, is a unique plant species native to arid regions of Central Asia.

It features striking, pale pink flowers with a velvety texture, adapted to thrive in extreme heat and low moisture conditions. The plant has a compact growth habit with thick, fleshy leaves that store water efficiently. Its root system is shallow but extensive, allowing it to absorb nutrients from the dry soil.

Due to its limited habitat and environmental threats, Acunniana is considered endangered and requires conservation efforts to ensure its survival.
