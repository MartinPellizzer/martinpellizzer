Athyrium enorme

Athyrium enorme, commonly known as the giant lady fern, is a striking member of the Dryopteridaceae family.

It is native to the forests of eastern Asia, including China, Japan, and Korea. This plant is characterized by its large, feathery fronds that can reach lengths of up to 1.5 meters. Its leaves are bipinnate, with a distinctive, elegant appearance that makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Athyrium enorme thrives in shady, moist environments and requires well-drained, acidic soil for optimal growth.
