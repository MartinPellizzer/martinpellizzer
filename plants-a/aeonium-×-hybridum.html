Aeonium × hybridum

Aeonium × hybridum, commonly known as the Crown of Thorns, is a succulent plant native to the Canary Islands.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green or blue-gray in color. The plant produces striking clusters of yellow or orange flowers during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil with full sun exposure.

Aeonium × hybridum is popular in rock gardens and as a decorative plant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
