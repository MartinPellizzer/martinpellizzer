Apargia dubia

Apargia dubia, commonly known as the slender false dragonhead, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands. Its small, tubular flowers are usually pink or purple and bloom in late summer.

Apargia dubia is known for its delicate appearance and is often found in shaded or partially sunny environments.
