Antholyza aethiopica var. vittigera

Antholyza aethiopica var. vittigera, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of purple, pink, and white. The leaves are velvety and dark green, often with a slight waxy coating. It prefers well-draining soil and indirect sunlight, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

This variety is known for its ability to produce multiple blooms throughout the growing season.
