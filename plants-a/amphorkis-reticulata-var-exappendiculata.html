Amphorkis reticulata var. exappendiculata

Amphorkis reticulata var. exappendiculata, commonly known as the reticulated amphorkis, is a unique variant of the amphorkis genus.

It is characterized by its intricate, net-like leaf venation, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its leaves are usually elongated and have a slightly waxy texture, which helps in reducing water loss.

The flowers are small and clustered, often displaying a range of colors from white to pale yellow.
