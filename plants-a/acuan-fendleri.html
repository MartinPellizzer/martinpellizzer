Acuan fendleri

Acuan fendleri, commonly known as the Fendler's acacia, is a shrub native to the arid regions of South America.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant has slender, woody stems and bipinnate leaves that are adapted to conserve water. Its flowers are typically yellow or greenish-yellow, arranged in dense clusters.

Acuan fendleri is well-suited to harsh environments and is often found in rocky soils.
