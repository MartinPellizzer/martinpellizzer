Aalius

Aalius, commonly known as the silverleaf plant, is a member of the Acanthaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking silver-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Aalius is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
