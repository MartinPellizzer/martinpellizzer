Aloe bruynsii

Aloe bruynsii, commonly known as the Cape aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features striking, dark green, fleshy leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with orange to red flowers during the summer months. Aloe bruynsii is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional medicinal uses.
