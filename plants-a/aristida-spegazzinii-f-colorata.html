Aristida spegazzinii f. colorata

Aristida spegazzinii f. colorata, commonly known as the colorful threadgrass, is a flowering plant species native to South America.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit. The plant features fine, thread-like leaves and produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences. Its most distinguishing feature is the vibrant, colorful bracts that give it its common name.
