Arenaria modesta subsp. modesta

Arenaria modesta subsp.

modesta, commonly known as the modest sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. It typically forms dense mats of tiny, succulent leaves and produces small, white or pink flowers during the summer months.

The plant thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to harsh alpine environments. Its compact growth habit and ability to withstand cold temperatures make it a hardy species.

It is often found in alpine meadows and is valued for its ornamental appeal in rock gardens.
