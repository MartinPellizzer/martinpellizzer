Anthurium polancoi

Anthurium polancoi, commonly known as the Polanco's arrowhead, is a striking tropical plant native to the mountainous regions of Colombia.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive spathe and spadix inflorescence. The plant features broad, heart-shaped leaves that are typically bright green with a waxy texture. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Anthurium polancoi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in botanical gardens.
