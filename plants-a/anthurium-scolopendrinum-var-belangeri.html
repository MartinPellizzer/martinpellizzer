Anthurium scolopendrinum var. belangeri

Anthurium scolopendrinum var. belangeri, commonly known as the Belanger's anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-like inflorescences surrounded by colorful spatula-shaped bracts, often in shades of red or orange. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

This variety is popular among plant enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
