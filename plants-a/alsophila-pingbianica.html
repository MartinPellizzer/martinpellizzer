Alsophila pingbianica

Alsophila pingbianica, commonly known as the Pingbian huperzia, is a species of fern belonging to the family Huperziaceae.

It is native to China, specifically found in the Yunnan Province. The plant is characterized by its tall, slender stems and dense, glossy fronds that are typically green in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often growing in mountainous regions.

The species is notable for its ecological importance and is considered a rare and endangered plant in its natural habitat.
