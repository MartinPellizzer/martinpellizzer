Abelia serrata var. glaberrima

Abelia serrata var. glaberrima, commonly known as the smoothleaf abelia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, fragrant, white to pinkish flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The leaves are typically ovate to elliptical in shape and have a smooth, glabrous surface, giving the plant its varietal name. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained, moist soil.

This plant is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
