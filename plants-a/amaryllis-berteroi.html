Amaryllis berteroi

Amaryllis berteroi, commonly known as the red amaryllis, is a striking flowering plant native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its tall, sturdy stems and large, showy flowers. The plant produces clusters of vibrant red or orange flowers that bloom in late summer to early fall. Its foliage consists of broad, strap-like leaves that emerge from a bulbous base, typical of many amaryllis species.

This plant is popular in gardens and as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
