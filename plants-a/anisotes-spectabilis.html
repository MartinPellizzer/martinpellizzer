Anisotes spectabilis

Anisotes spectabilis, commonly known as the showy anisotes, is a herbaceous perennial native to the southeastern United States.

It features striking, showy white to pale pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has heart-shaped, lanceolate leaves that are often hairy and have a distinctive aromatic scent. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or swampy areas.

Anisotes spectabilis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, providing nectar for pollinators.
