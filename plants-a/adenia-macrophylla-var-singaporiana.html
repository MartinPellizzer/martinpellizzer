Adenia macrophylla var. singaporiana

Adenia macrophylla var. singaporiana, commonly known as the Singapore adenium, is a unique variant of the adenium genus native to Singapore.

It is a small, succulent shrub characterized by its thick, fleshy stems and striking red or pink flowers. The plant features glossy, dark green leaves that are often lobed or serrated. It thrives in warm, sunny environments with well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is often cultivated as a decorative plant in tropical and subtropical regions.
