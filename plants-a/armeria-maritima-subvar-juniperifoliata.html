Armeria maritima subvar. juniperifoliata

Armeria maritima subvar. juniperifoliata, commonly known as the Juniper-leaf Sea Thrift, is a low-growing perennial plant native to coastal regions of the northern hemisphere.

It features dense clusters of small, pink to white flowers that bloom in early summer. The leaves are narrow and slightly succulent, giving the plant a distinctive appearance. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is highly tolerant of salt spray and coastal conditions.

This variety is often used in coastal landscaping due to its hardiness and ornamental value.
