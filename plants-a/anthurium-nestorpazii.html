Anthurium nestorpazii

Anthurium nestorpazii, commonly known as the Red-veined Anthurium, is a tropical flowering plant native to Central America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves with deep red veins contrasting against its dark green background. The plant produces vibrant red spathes that enclose small, yellowish spadices, typical of the Araceae family. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium nestorpazii is popular as a decorative plant due to its ornamental foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
