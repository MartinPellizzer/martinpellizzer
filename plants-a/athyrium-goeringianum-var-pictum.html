Athyrium goeringianum var. pictum

Athyrium goeringianum var. pictum, commonly known as the painted shield fern, is a striking fern native to eastern Asia.

It features delicate, bipinnate fronds with a unique, variegated pattern of light and dark green, giving it its common name. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories. Its rhizomes are fibrous and help anchor it to rocky or wooded substrates.

The foliage is often described as ornamental, making it a popular choice for shady garden landscapes.
