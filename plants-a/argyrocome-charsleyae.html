Argyrocome charsleyae

Argyrocome charsleyae, commonly known as the silverleaf lily.

It is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of South America, particularly in Ecuador. The plant is characterized by its striking silver-green foliage and showy, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in spring. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
