Arisaema longilaminum

Arisaema longilaminum, commonly known as the long-leaf arisaema, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its unique inflorescence composed of a spadix and a spathe. The plant features large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine hairs. Its flowers are typically greenish-yellow, and it produces berries that contain several seeds.

Arisaema longilaminum is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its striking foliage and interesting floral structure.
