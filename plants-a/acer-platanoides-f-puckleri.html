Acer platanoides f. puckleri

Acer platanoides f. puckleri, commonly known as the Puckler's maple, is a unique cultivar of the Norway maple.

It is characterized by its striking, deeply lobed leaves that turn vibrant shades of red in the fall. The plant is a deciduous tree with a rounded, spreading habit and is often used in ornamental landscaping. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained, fertile soils.

This cultivar is prized for its ornamental value and adaptability to various garden conditions.
