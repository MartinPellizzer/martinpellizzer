Asplenium rhizophyllum var. biscaynianum

Asplenium rhizophyllum var. biscaynianum, commonly known as the Biscaynian rock spleenwort, is a variety of the rock spleenwort genus.

It is a small, epiphytic fern that typically grows on rock surfaces or in crevices. The plant has a compact rosette of lanceolate, dark green leaves with a leathery texture. It produces spore-bearing sorus clusters on the underside of its leaves.

This variety is adapted to moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.
