Aster puniceus f. brachypyllus

Aster puniceus f. brachypyllus, commonly known as the compact aster, is a cultivar of the New England aster.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and vibrant purple to pink flower heads. The plant typically blooms in late summer to early fall, producing showy composite flowers. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

Its foliage is usually gray-green, adding visual interest throughout the growing season.
