Armeria maritima subsp. bottendorfensis

Armeria maritima subsp.

bottendorfensis, commonly known as the Bottendorf's thrift, is a low-growing perennial plant native to coastal areas of Europe. It features dense clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.

The plant has succulent, silver-gray foliage that is adapted to withstand salty sea air and sandy soils. It thrives in full sun and is often found in dune environments.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in stabilizing coastal habitats.
