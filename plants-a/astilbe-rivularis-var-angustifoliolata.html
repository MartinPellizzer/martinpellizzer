Astilbe rivularis var. angustifoliolata

Astilbe rivularis var. angustifoliolata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved river astilbe, is a herbaceous perennial native to eastern Asia.

It features delicate, feathery plumes of pale pink to white flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has compound, lance-shaped leaves that are typically narrow and finely toothed. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found near streams or in woodland gardens.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in humid climates.
