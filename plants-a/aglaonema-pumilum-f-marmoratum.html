Aglaonema pumilum f. marmoratum

Aglaonema pumilum f. marmoratum, commonly known as the marbled aglaonema, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features striking, dark green leaves with distinctive white and cream marbled patterns, which add visual interest to any indoor space. The plant has a short stem and produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are rarely seen. It thrives in low to moderate light conditions and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental value and ease of care.
