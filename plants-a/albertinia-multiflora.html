Albertinia multiflora

Albertinia multiflora, commonly known as the many-flowered Albertinia, is a herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its clustered inflorescences of small, yellowish flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are linear and aromatic, often used in traditional medicine.

It is also valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens due to its compact growth habit and delicate flowers.
