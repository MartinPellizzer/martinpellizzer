Adesmia horridiuscula f. sericea

Adesmia horridiuscula f. sericea, commonly known as the silky adesmia, is a flowering plant species native to South America.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its small, pea-like flowers. The plant features finely hairy stems and leaves, which give it a distinctive silvery appearance. Its foliage is typically alternate and pinnately compound, with small, ovate leaflets.
