Alsophila longipes

Alsophila longipes, commonly known as the long-needle tree fern, is a tropical evergreen plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and feathery fronds. The plant has a distinctive appearance with its long, narrow leaves that can grow up to several meters in length. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Alsophila longipes is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
