Alternanthera polygonoides f. halimioides

Alternanthera polygonoides f. halimioides, commonly known as the Halimoides Alternanthera, is a striking aquatic plant characterized by its vibrant red to purple foliage.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is native to South America, particularly Brazil. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are often variegated with contrasting colors, making it a popular choice for aquariums and water gardens. It thrives in moderate to high light conditions and requires stable water parameters to maintain its coloration.

Its ornamental value lies in its intense coloration and ease of care, making it a favorite among aquatic plant enthusiasts.
