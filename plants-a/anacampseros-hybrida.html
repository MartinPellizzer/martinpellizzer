Anacampseros hybrida

Anacampseros hybrida, commonly known as the hybrid stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, gray-green leaves that store water, adapting to arid environments. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental appeal make it a desirable addition to drought-tolerant landscapes.
