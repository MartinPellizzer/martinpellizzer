Amaryllis doraniae

Amaryllis doraniae, commonly known as the Doran's amaryllis, is a species native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that come in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant produces large, strap-like leaves that are often spotted with white or green. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers bright, indirect light.

Amaryllis doraniae is a bulbous plant that can be grown both outdoors in mild climates and as a container plant indoors.
