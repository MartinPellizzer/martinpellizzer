Allium stenopetalum var. pumilum

Allium stenopetalum var. pumilum, commonly known as the dwarf bulbous onion, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features a compact growth habit with narrow, grass-like leaves and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. The plant produces small, ovoid bulbs that are often used in traditional herbal remedies. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable addition to both ornamental and ecological landscapes.
