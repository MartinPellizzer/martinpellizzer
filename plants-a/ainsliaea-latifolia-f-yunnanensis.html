Ainsliaea latifolia f. yunnanensis

Ainsliaea latifolia f. yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan Ainsliaea, is a variant of the Ainsliaea genus native to China.

It is a flowering plant characterized by its showy, pinkish-white bracts that resemble petals, though they are actually modified leaves.
