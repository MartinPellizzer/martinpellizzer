Aristolochia moupinensis

Aristolochia moupinensis, commonly known as the Yunnan pipevine, is a climbing vine native to China.

It is characterized by its distinctive, heart-shaped leaves and its complex, pipe-like flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. The plant produces small, dark blue to black berries that contain the seeds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forests and mountainous regions.

Due to its unique morphology and ecological role, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
