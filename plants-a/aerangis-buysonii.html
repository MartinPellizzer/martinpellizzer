Aerangis buysonii

Aerangis buysonii, commonly known as the Malagasy orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its slender, hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink with a yellow lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Aerangis buysonii is a rare and endangered species, highlighting the importance of conservation efforts in its native habitat.
