Azalea indica var. plena

Azalea indica var. plena, commonly known as the double-flowered azalea, is a compact evergreen shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its dense, showy clusters of double blooms, which are typically pink to red in color. The plant produces small, oval leaves that are dark green and glossy. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its prolonged blooming period in spring and early summer.
