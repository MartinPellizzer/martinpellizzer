Adenophora coelestis var. uehatae

Adenophora coelestis var. uehatae, commonly known as the sky blue aster, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, blue-violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves and is often found in moist, shady environments such as forests and stream sides. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.

The variety 'uehatae' is distinguished by its slightly different flower structure compared to the typical form of Adenophora coelestis.
