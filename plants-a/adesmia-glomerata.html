Adesmia glomerata

Adesmia glomerata, commonly known as the clustered legume, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows in dry, rocky habitats. The plant features small, clustered flowers that are often pink or purple in color. Its leaves are trifoliate, meaning they are composed of three leaflets.

Adesmia glomerata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
