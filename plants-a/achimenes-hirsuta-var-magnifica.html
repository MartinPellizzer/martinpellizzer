Achimenes hirsuta var. magnifica

Achimenes hirsuta var. magnifica, commonly known as the hairy achimenes, is a tropical perennial plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in a range of colors including red, pink, and white. The plant has hairy, ovate leaves and produces tubular flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

This variety is popular among gardeners for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
