Acacia nervosula

Acacia nervosula, commonly known as the slender wattle, is a shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its fine, feathery foliage and yellow flowers that appear in globular clusters. The plant has slender, wiry branches and small, compound leaves with a delicate, thread-like appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.

Acacia nervosula is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
