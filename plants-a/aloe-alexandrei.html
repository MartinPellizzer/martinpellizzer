Aloe alexandrei

Aloe alexandrei, commonly known as the Cape aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, spire-like growth habit and its striking, sword-shaped leaves that are fleshy and covered in a white powdery coating. The plant produces vibrant orange-red flowers in terminal spikes, which bloom during the summer months. Aloe alexandrei is adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves to survive in dry conditions.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
