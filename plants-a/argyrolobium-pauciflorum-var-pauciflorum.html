Argyrolobium pauciflorum var. pauciflorum

Argyrolobium pauciflorum var. pauciflorum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved silver globular vetch, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils. The plant features small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in globular clusters. Its leaves are narrow and often have a silvery or pale green appearance.

It is adapted to arid environments and is often found in open, disturbed habitats.
