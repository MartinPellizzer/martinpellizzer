Astragalus tunetanus

Astragalus tunetanus, commonly known as the Tunisian milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to North Africa, particularly Tunisia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its small, pea-like flowers that typically range in color from white to pink. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces pods that contain several seeds.

It is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a resilient species in harsh climates.
