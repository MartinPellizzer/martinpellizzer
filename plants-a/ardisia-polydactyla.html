Ardisia polydactyla

Ardisia polydactyla, commonly known as the many-fingered ardisia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. It is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and adaptability to various soil types.
