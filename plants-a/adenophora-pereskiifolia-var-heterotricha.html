Adenophora pereskiifolia var. heterotricha

Adenophora pereskiifolia var. heterotricha, commonly known as the heterotricha bellflower, is a perennial herb native to the eastern regions of China.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves and clusters of bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale blue. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
