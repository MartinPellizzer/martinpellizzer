Ampelopsis heterophylla var. kulingensis

Ampelopsis heterophylla var. kulingensis, commonly known as the Chinese honeysuckle vine, is a climbing plant native to eastern Asia.

It features heart-shaped to ovate leaves that vary in size and shape depending on the growth conditions. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that develop into red, fleshy berries. Its vines are often used in ornamental gardening due to their attractive foliage and fruit.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is valued for its ecological benefits in supporting wildlife.
