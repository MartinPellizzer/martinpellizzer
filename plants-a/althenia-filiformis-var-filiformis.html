Althenia filiformis var. filiformis

Althenia filiformis var. filiformis, commonly known as the slender althenia, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, thread-like stems that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale blue in color. Its leaves are narrow and lanceolate, arranged alternately along the stem.

Althenia filiformis var. filiformis is often found in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.
