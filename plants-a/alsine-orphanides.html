Alsine orphanides

Alsine orphanides, commonly known as the Mediterranean sandwort, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing, herbaceous species that typically thrives in sandy or rocky soils. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are linear and often covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.

Alsine orphanides is adapted to arid environments and is often found in coastal or inland dunes.
