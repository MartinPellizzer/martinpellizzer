Alsine schimperi var. graminifolia

Alsine schimperi var. graminifolia, commonly known as the slender alsike, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the family Amaranthaceae and is characterized by its low-growing, herbaceous habit. The plant features slender, grass-like leaves and small, pale green flowers that appear in clusters. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows and along stream banks.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and ability to stabilize soil in wetland environments.
