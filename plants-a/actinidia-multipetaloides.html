Actinidia multipetaloides

Actinidia multipetaloides, commonly known as the Chinese mayapple, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Actinidiaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and showy white flowers. The plant produces small, edible berries that ripen in the fall. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
