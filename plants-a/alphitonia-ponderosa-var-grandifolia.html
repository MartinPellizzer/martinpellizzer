Alphitonia ponderosa var. grandifolia

Alphitonia ponderosa var. grandifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaved alphitonia, is a flowering plant native to eastern Australia.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub or tree that typically grows in coastal and subtropical regions. The plant features elliptical, glossy, dark green leaves and produces small, fragrant white flowers in clusters. Its botanical characteristics include a woody stem, simple leaves, and a preference for well-drained soils.

It is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and adaptability to various garden conditions.
