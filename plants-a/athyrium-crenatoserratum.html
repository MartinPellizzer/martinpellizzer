Athyrium crenatoserratum

Athyrium crenatoserratum, commonly known as the serrated shield fern, is a species of fern native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the Dryopteridaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, bipinnate fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky slopes. Its leaves are covered with fine, hairy structures that aid in water retention.

Athyrium crenatoserratum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
