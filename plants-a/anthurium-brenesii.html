Anthurium brenesii

Anthurium brenesii, commonly known as the Red Anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped spathes that range from red to orange in color. The plant produces small, green berries that are edible but not commonly consumed by humans. It thrives in humid, warm environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.

Its striking appearance makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and ornamental purposes.
