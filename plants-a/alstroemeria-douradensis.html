Alstroemeria douradensis

Alstroemeria douradensis, commonly known as the golden alstroemeria, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It belongs to the family Alstroemeriaceae and is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, orange, and yellow. The plant typically grows in moist, shady environments and has narrow, strap-like leaves.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and floral arrangements.
