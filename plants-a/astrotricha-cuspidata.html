Astrotricha cuspidata

Astrotricha cuspidata, commonly known as the hooked aster, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in the summer. It thrives in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands.

Astrotricha cuspidata is often found in the eastern and central United States.
