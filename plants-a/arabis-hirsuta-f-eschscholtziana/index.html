Arabis Hirsuta F. Eschscholtziana

Arabis hirsuta f. eschscholtziana is a unique variant of the hairy rockcress known for its compact growth and delicate blooms. This plant thrives in rocky or gravelly soils and prefers sunny to partially shaded environments. Its small, white flowers appear in early spring, adding a touch of elegance to alpine gardens. The foliage is covered in fine hairs, giving it a soft, textured appearance. It is a valued addition to rock gardens and is often sought after by horticulturists for its ornamental appeal.
