Anthurium brownii

Anthurium brownii, commonly known as the brown anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are often a deep green with a reddish or brownish tint. The plant produces clusters of small, spadix-like inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, which can range from white to pink. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Anthurium brownii is popular as a ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
