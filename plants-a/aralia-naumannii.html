Aralia naumannii

Aralia naumannii, commonly known as the Japanese aralia, is a deciduous shrub native to East Asia.

It features compound leaves with multiple leaflets arranged in a feather-like pattern. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers in clusters, followed by red berries that are edible for birds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscaping.
