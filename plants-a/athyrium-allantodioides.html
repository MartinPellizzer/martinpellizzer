Athyrium allantodioides

Athyrium allantodioides, commonly known as the narrow-leaf Japanese shield fern, is a perennial fern native to eastern Asia.

It features delicate, lance-shaped leaves with a distinctive, shield-like appearance, typically arranged in a compact rosette. The fronds are usually dark green and have a finely divided, feathery texture. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

This species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and ease of cultivation.
