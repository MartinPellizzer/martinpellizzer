Ammannia mossambicensis

Ammannia mossambicensis, commonly known as the African enchanter's nightshade, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. The plant features small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in clusters, and its leaves are usually oval-shaped with a smooth texture. Ammannia mossambicensis has a fibrous root system and is known for its ability to thrive in humid conditions.

It is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its unique appearance and adaptability.
