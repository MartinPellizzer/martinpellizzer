Athyrium zayuense

Athyrium zayuense, commonly known as the Yunnan shield fern, is a species native to the mountainous regions of Yunnan, China.

It belongs to the family Athyriaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, shield-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as forest undergrowth, where it thrives in cool and humid conditions. Its fronds are usually bi- or tri-pinnate, with finely divided, serrated leaflets.

Athyrium zayuense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens for its unique foliage.
