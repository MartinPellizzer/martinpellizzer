Anemone tenuifolia

Anemone tenuifolia, commonly known as the slender anemone, is a perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features delicate, palmate leaves that are typically lobed and covered in fine hairs. The plant produces showy, white or pale pink flowers with numerous petals arranged in a star-like pattern. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodlands or along stream banks.

Its botanical name reflects its slender leaves, which are a distinguishing characteristic of the species.
