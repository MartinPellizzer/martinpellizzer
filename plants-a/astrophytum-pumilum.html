Astrophytum pumilum

Astrophytum pumilum, commonly known as the Christmas cactus, is a succulent plant native to the rocky soils of Mexico.

It features a short, thick stem covered in areoles, from which clusters of bright, funnel-shaped flowers emerge during the holiday season. The plant's leaves are modified into spines, and it has a shallow root system adapted to arid environments. It requires minimal water and thrives in bright, indirect sunlight.

Its unique blooming cycle makes it a popular ornamental plant in gardens and homes.
