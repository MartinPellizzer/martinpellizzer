Alexitoxicon inamoenum

Alexitoxicon inamoenum, commonly known as the golden flame orchid, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features vibrant orange and yellow flowers that resemble flames, making it a visually captivating species. The plant has strap-like leaves that are broad and waxy, adapted to thrive in humid environments. It prefers well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.

Its unique appearance and relatively easy care make it a popular choice among plant enthusiasts.
