Astrocaryum aureum

Astrocaryum aureum, commonly known as the golden palm, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is a member of the Arecaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnately compound leaves. The plant produces yellow-orange fruits that are edible and often used in local cuisines. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in warm climates.
