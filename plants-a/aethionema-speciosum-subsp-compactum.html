Aethionema speciosum subsp. compactum

Aethionema speciosum subsp.

compactum, commonly known as the compact stonecress, is a low-growing, mat-forming plant native to the Mediterranean region. It features small, clustered leaves and produces clusters of bright yellow flowers in the spring.

The plant is typically found in rocky or gravelly soils, thriving in sunny, well-drained environments. Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for rock gardens and alpine settings.

It is also valued for its ornamental appeal and hardy nature.
