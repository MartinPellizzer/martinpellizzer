Anemone transvaalensis var. filifolia

Anemone transvaalensis var. filifolia, commonly known as the Filifoliated Transvaal Anemone, is a striking perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features showy, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has finely divided, fern-like leaves that give it a delicate, lacey appearance. It prefers well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to thrive in a variety of garden conditions.
