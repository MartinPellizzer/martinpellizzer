Asplenium obtusatum var. lucidum

Asplenium obtusatum var. lucidum, commonly known as the glossy holly fern, is a variant of the broad-leaf holly fern.

It is characterized by its dark green, glossy, and leathery fronds that are typically broad and lance-shaped. The plant is native to tropical and subtropical regions, particularly in Southeast Asia. Its fronds are evergreen, and it thrives in moist, shaded environments.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
