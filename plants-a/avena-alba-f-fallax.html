Avena alba f. fallax

Avena alba f. fallax, commonly known as the white oat or silver oat, is a variant of the common oat species.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. The plant features slender, erect stems that can grow up to 1 meter in height. Its leaves are narrow and silvery-gray due to dense pubescence, giving it a distinctive appearance.

The inflorescence consists of a spike of small, white to pale green flowers, which are arranged in a loose panicle.
