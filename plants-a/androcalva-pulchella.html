Androcalva pulchella

Androcalva pulchella, commonly known as the beautiful leafy vine, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its climbing habit, with slender stems that twine around supports as it grows. Its leaves are heart-shaped and have a smooth, glossy surface, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Androcalva pulchella is valued in horticulture for its aesthetic qualities and ability to thrive in warm, humid climates.
