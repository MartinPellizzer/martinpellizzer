Anoectochilus rumphii

Anoectochilus rumphii, commonly known as the golden orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern, and it thrives in well-drained, nutrient-rich soil.
