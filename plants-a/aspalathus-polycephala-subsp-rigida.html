Aspalathus polycephala subsp. rigida

Aspalathus polycephala subsp.

rigida, commonly known as the rigid flatbush, is a shrub native to South Africa. It is characterized by its dense, leathery foliage and small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring.

The plant thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy environments. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to tolerate coastal conditions.

The subspecies is distinguished by its more rigid growth habit compared to other members of the species.
