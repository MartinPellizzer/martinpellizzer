Achillea tianschanica

Achillea tianschanica, commonly known as the Tian Shan yarrow, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or alpine environments. The plant features slender, upright stems and produces clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers. Its leaves are pinnately divided, with a feathery appearance, and are covered in fine hairs.

Achillea tianschanica is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
