Ada euodes

Ada euodes, commonly known as the white sand tree, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by small, round fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

The tree is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its foliage and floral display.
