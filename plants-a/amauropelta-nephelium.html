Amauropelta nephelium

It is characterized by its intricate, lace-like fronds that resemble clouds, giving it its common name. Its reproductive structures, known as sporangia, are clustered in sori on the underside of the fronds.

Amauropelta nephelium thrives in humid environments and requires high levels of light and moisture for optimal growth.
