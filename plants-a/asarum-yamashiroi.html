Asarum yamashiroi

Asarum yamashiroi, commonly known as Yamashiro asarum, is a perennial herb native to Japan.

It belongs to the Aristolochiaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and distinctive, hooded flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its flowers are usually greenish-yellow with a complex, winding structure that resembles a pipe.

Asarum yamashiroi is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in herbal medicine.
