Aster diplostephioides subsp. yunanensis

Aster diplostephioides subsp.

yunanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan aster, is a perennial herb native to China. It belongs to the sunflower family (Asteraceae) and is characterized by its showy, violet-blue flower heads.

The plant typically grows in mountainous regions and has lance-shaped leaves with rough textures. It produces small, tubular flowers that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
