Aechmea dichlamydea var. trinitensis

Aechmea dichlamydea var. trinitensis, commonly known as the Trinidad pineapple bromeliad, is a striking epiphytic plant native to the Caribbean.

It features a rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves that form a central rosette, which collects rainwater. The plant produces a tall, branched inflorescence with showy, violet to purple flowers. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Its unique appearance and adaptability make it a popular choice for ornamental cultivation.
