Astrocaryum burity

Astrocaryum burity, commonly known as buriti, is a palm tree native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate leaves. The plant produces yellow-orange fruits that contain edible seeds, which are rich in beta-carotene. The leaves are often used in traditional medicine and as a source of fiber.

Its ecological importance lies in its role as a food source for various wildlife species.
