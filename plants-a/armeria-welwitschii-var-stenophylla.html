Armeria welwitschii var. stenophylla

Armeria welwitschii var. stenophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leaved thrift, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense clusters of white or pink flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has linear, grass-like leaves and is often found in rocky or sandy soils. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in coastal and Mediterranean climates.
