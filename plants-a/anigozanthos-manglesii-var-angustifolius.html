Anigozanthos manglesii var. angustifolius

Anigozanthos manglesii var. angustifolius, commonly known as the narrow-leaf kangaroo paw, is a striking Australian plant with distinctive, paw-like flowers.

It features vibrant red and orange tubular flowers that bloom in spring, attracting nectar-feeding birds and insects. The plant has narrow, strap-like leaves that are typically green and covered in fine hairs. It is native to the south-western region of Western Australia and thrives in well-drained soils.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and floral arrangements.
