Astrocaryum minus var. terrae-firmae

Astrocaryum minus var. terrae-firmae, commonly known as the Brazilian buriti or small buriti, is a palm native to the Amazon Basin.

It is a medium-sized tree that typically grows up to 15 meters tall, with a slender trunk and pinnate leaves. The plant produces yellowish-white flowers followed by large, football-shaped fruits that contain edible seeds. Its botanical features include a fibrous, palmate leaf structure and a unique, segmented fruit with a hard, woody outer layer.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and traditional uses in local communities.
