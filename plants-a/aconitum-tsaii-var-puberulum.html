Aconitum tsaii var. puberulum

Aconitum tsaii var. puberulum, commonly known as the pubescent aconite, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to China.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its tall, sparsely branched stems that can reach up to 1.5 meters in height. The plant produces striking blue to purple flowers in late summer, arranged in dense racemes. Its leaves are deeply lobed and have a velvety texture due to the presence of fine hairs.

Aconitum tsaii var. puberulum is noted for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
