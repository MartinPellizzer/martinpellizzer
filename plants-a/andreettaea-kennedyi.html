Andreettaea kennedyi

Andreettaea kennedyi, commonly known as the Kennedy's Andreettaea, is a rare flowering plant native to the Atlantic Forest of Brazil.

It is a shrub with woody stems and small, clustered leaves. The plant produces delicate white or pale pink flowers that bloom during the wet season. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is adapted to the specific microclimates of its native habitat.
