Aridaria resurgens

Aridaria resurgens, commonly known as the desert reemergence, is a rare succulent native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy stems and leaves adapted to store water, allowing it to survive in harsh, dry environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the rainy season. Its root system is extensive, enabling it to anchor itself in loose, sandy soils.

Due to its limited distribution and sensitivity to environmental changes, it is considered a vulnerable species.
