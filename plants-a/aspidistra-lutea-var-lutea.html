Aspidistra lutea var. lutea

Aspidistra lutea var. lutea, commonly known as the yellow aspidistra, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, glossy leaves that are typically elongated and lance-shaped. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or yellow in color. It thrives in shady, humid environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Aspidistra lutea var. lutea is known for its adaptability and ability to tolerate low light conditions.
