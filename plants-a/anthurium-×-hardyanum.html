Anthurium × hardyanum

Anthurium × hardyanum, commonly known as the velvet leaf anthurium, is a hybrid plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep, velvety green. The plant produces striking spadices, which are the modified leaves that house the small, inconspicuous flowers. Anthurium × hardyanum thrives in humid, warm environments with bright, indirect light.

It is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
