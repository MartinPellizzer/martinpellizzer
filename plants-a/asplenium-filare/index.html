Asplenium Filare

Asplenium filare, also known as the basket fern, is a unique and resilient species of fern that thrives in humid environments. This plant is characterized by its dense, leafy fronds that resemble a basket, giving it its common name. Native to various parts of Asia and the Pacific, it is often found in shaded, moist areas near streams or in dense forests. Its ability to grow in low-light conditions makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening. With its elegant appearance and easy care requirements, Asplenium filare is both an ornamental and ecologically valuable plant.
