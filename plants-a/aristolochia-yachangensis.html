Aristolochia yachangensis

Aristolochia yachangensis, commonly known as the Yachang Dutchman's Pipe, is a flowering plant native to China.

It is characterized by its distinctive, pipe-shaped flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color. The plant belongs to the family Aristolochiaceae and is known for its complex, twisting floral structure. Its leaves are heart-shaped and have a rough texture, while its vines can grow quite long.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has traditional medicinal uses in some cultures.
