Arthraxon cryptatherus subsp. submuticus

Arthraxon cryptatherus subsp.

submuticus, commonly known as the narrowleaf grassvetch, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It is a perennial herb that typically grows in grassy or open habitats.

The plant has slender, erect stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. Its inflorescence consists of dense clusters of tiny, pale blue to purple flowers.

It plays a role in stabilizing soil in its native environment and is of interest to botanists studying grassland ecosystems.
