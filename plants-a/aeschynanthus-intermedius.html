Aeschynanthus intermedius

Aeschynanthus intermedius, commonly known as the hanging lantern plant, is a member of the Gesneriaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its trailing growth habit and showy, tubular flowers. The plant produces vibrant orange or red flowers that bloom in clusters, often resembling small lanterns. Its foliage is typically dark green with a velvety texture, and it thrives in bright, indirect light.

Aeschynanthus intermedius is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
