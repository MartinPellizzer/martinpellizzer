Aloe micracantha

Aloe micracantha, commonly known as the narrow-leaved aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features compact, upright growth with narrow, fleshy leaves that are often tinged with red or purple at the base. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers during the summer months. Its leaves contain a gel-like substance that is sometimes used for its medicinal properties.

Aloe micracantha is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water to thrive.
