Aspilia wedeliiformis

Aspilia wedeliiformis, commonly known as the Wedeli's flame flower, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking, brightly colored flower heads that resemble flames. The plant produces both ray and disk flowers, with the central disk flowers being tubular and often yellow or orange in color. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in a fine layer of white hairs, giving them a silvery appearance.

Aspilia wedeliiformis is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant blooms and unique foliage.
