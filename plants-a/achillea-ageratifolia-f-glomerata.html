Achillea ageratifolia f. glomerata

Achillea ageratifolia f. glomerata, commonly known as the clustered yarrow, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in temperate regions, characterized by its dense, clustered inflorescences. The plant features finely divided, gray-green leaves and produces small, white or pale yellow flowers. It is often found in meadows, woodland edges, and disturbed soils.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
