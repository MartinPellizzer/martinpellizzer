Asphodelus ramosus var. aestivus

Asphodelus ramosus var. aestivus, commonly known as the summer asphodel, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It features tall, narrow leaves and produces clusters of yellowish-green flowers in the summer months. The plant is characterized by its robust rhizomatous root system, which allows it to thrive in well-drained soils. It is often found in dry, sunny grasslands and rocky areas.

Its botanical structure makes it adaptable to Mediterranean climates and suitable for ornamental gardening.
