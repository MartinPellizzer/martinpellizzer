Anthurium lilafructum

Anthurium lilafructum, commonly known as the Lilafructum Philodendron, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped leaves that can range in color from deep green to dark red. The plant produces striking spadices, which are specialized inflorescences that house small, inconspicuous flowers. It thrives in humid, warm environments with bright, indirect light.

Anthurium lilafructum is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and ease of care.
