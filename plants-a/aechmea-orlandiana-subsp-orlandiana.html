Aechmea orlandiana subsp. orlandiana

Aechmea orlandiana subsp.

orlandiana, commonly known as the Orange Sword, is a striking bromeliad native to South America. It is characterized by its rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves that form a central cup, which collects rainwater.

The plant produces vibrant orange-red bracts that surround its inflorescence, giving it its common name. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

The species is popular as a decorative plant due to its unique appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.
