Aruana silvestris

Aruana silvestris, commonly known as the wild aruana, is a climbing plant native to the Amazon rainforest.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its woody stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, five-petaled flowers that are typically pink or red in color. Its fruits are capsule-like structures that release seeds when mature.
