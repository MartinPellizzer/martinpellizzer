Anelsonia

Anelsonia, commonly known as the silverleaf anelsonia, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. Anelsonia thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rocky or arid landscapes.

It is often used in xeriscaping due to its low water requirements and ornamental value.
