Asterostigma

Asterostigma, commonly known as the star-stigma plant, is a unique species characterized by its distinctive floral structure.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is known for its complex inflorescence. The plant features a central disk surrounded by ray florets, with the stigmas arranged in a star-like pattern, giving it its common name. Asterostigma is typically found in temperate regions and thrives in well-drained soils.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for both horticultural study and ecological research.
