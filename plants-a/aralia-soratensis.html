Aralia soratensis

Aralia soratensis, commonly known as the Japanese aralia, is a deciduous shrub native to Japan.

It features compound leaves that are typically pinnately arranged, with a feathery appearance. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers in clusters during the summer months. Its berries are red and edible, often attracting birds.

Aralia soratensis is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and adaptability to various soil conditions.
