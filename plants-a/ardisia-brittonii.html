Ardisia brittonii

Ardisia brittonii, commonly known as the Hawaiian beautyberry, is a flowering plant native to the Hawaiian Islands.

Ardisia brittonii is valued for its ornamental appeal and plays a role in the ecological diversity of Hawaii's native flora.
