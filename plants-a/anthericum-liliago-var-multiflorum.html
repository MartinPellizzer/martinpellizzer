Anthericum liliago var. multiflorum

Anthericum liliago var. multiflorum, commonly known as the clustered star-of-Bethlehem, is a herbaceous perennial native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features rhizomatous growth and produces dense clusters of white, star-shaped flowers in early summer. The plant has lance-shaped, grass-like leaves that are typically bluish-green in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in shady garden settings.
