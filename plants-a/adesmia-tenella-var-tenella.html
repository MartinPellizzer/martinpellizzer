Adesmia tenella var. tenella

Adesmia tenella var. tenella, commonly known as the slender legume, is a herbaceous perennial native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod-bearing fruits. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. Its small, clustered flowers are usually white or pale pink, and it produces slender, cylindrical pods that contain several seeds.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to fix nitrogen in the soil.
