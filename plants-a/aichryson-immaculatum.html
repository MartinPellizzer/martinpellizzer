Aichryson immaculatum

Aichryson immaculatum, commonly known as the silver leaf crassula, is a small, evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It features succulent, fleshy leaves that are typically gray-green in color, covered in a fine, white powder. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers during the summer months, adding a subtle ornamental touch. It is well-suited for rock gardens or as a container plant due to its compact size and low maintenance requirements.

Its ability to store water in its leaves makes it drought-tolerant and ideal for arid climates.
