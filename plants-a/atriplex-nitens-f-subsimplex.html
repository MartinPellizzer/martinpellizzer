Atriplex nitens f. subsimplex

Atriplex nitens f. subsimplex, commonly known as the narrow-leaved saltbush, is a shrub native to arid and semi-arid regions of North America.

It is characterized by its small, narrow, and often gray-green leaves that are adapted to reduce water loss in harsh environments. The plant produces small, greenish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences, typical of the Chenopodiaceae family. It thrives in saline soils and is often found in desert scrub and coastal dunes.
