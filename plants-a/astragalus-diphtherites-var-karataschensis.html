Astragalus diphtherites var. karataschensis

Astragalus diphtherites var. karataschensis, commonly known as Karataschen astragalus, is a perennial herb native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its slender stems and small, yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid environments. It has small, compound leaves and produces pods that contain several seeds.

This variety is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphology and ecological adaptations.
