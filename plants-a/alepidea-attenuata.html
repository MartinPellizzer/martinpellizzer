Alepidea attenuata

Alepidea attenuata, commonly known as the slender lily, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its strap-like, linear leaves and showy, bell-shaped flowers. Alepidea attenuata is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively drought-tolerant once established.
