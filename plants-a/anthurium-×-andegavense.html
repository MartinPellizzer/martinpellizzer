Anthurium × andegavense

Anthurium × andegavense, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a hybrid plant native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a glossy surface. The plant produces vibrant red spadices, which are modified leaves that enclose the inflorescence. Anthurium × andegavense thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

It is widely cultivated as a decorative plant due to its ornamental value and ability to thrive in indoor conditions.
