Acacia scalena

Acacia scalena, commonly known as the slender acacia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small shrub that typically grows in dry, rocky habitats. The plant features small, bipinnate leaves and produces yellow, spherical flower heads. Its woody pods contain seeds that are dispersed by wind.

Acacia scalena is valued for its hardy nature and is often used in landscaping in arid regions.
