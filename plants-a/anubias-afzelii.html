Anubias afzelii

Anubias afzelii, commonly known as African water spinach, is a hardy aquatic plant native to Africa.

It features broad, dark green, oval-shaped leaves that are often slightly wavy in appearance. The plant typically grows in shallow waters and thrives in a variety of water conditions. It is popular in aquariums due to its ease of care and ability to provide shelter for aquatic life.

Anubias afzelii is also valued for its ability to absorb nutrients from the water, helping to maintain water quality.
