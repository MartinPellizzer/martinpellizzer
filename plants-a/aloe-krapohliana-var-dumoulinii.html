Aloe krapohliana var. dumoulinii

Aloe krapohliana var. dumoulinii, commonly known as the Cape Town aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features upright, sparsely branched stems that are covered in a greyish-blue waxy coating. The plant produces dense clusters of tubular, yellowish-green flowers during the summer months. Its leaves are fleshy and ribbed, with a serrated margin and a distinctive blue-green coloration.

It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
