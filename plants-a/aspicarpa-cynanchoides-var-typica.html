Aspicarpa cynanchoides var. typica

Aspicarpa cynanchoides var. typica, commonly known as the typical aspicarpa, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to certain regions of Asia.

It belongs to the family Apocynaceae, which includes milkweeds and related species. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. Its flowers are typically small, clustered in umbels, and produce a distinctive, fleshy fruit known as a follicle.
