Ardisia diversifolia

Ardisia diversifolia, commonly known as the variegated evergreen ardisia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant features glossy, dark green leaves with white or yellow variegation, and it produces small, white flowers followed by red, fleshy berries. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and compact growth habit.

Ardisia diversifolia prefers shaded to partially shaded environments and well-drained soil.
