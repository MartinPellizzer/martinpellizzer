Argyrolobium baptisioides

Argyrolobium baptisioides, commonly known as the silver globular pea, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its globular, silver-green seed pods that resemble small spheres, which are a striking feature of the plant. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring, adding a delicate touch to its appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its unique appearance and ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
