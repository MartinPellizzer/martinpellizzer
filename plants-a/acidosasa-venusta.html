Acidosasa venusta

Acidosasa venusta, commonly known as the beautiful acid tree, is a species of flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, oval-shaped leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. The plant produces small, round fruits that contain a single seed. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for study in both horticulture and ecological research.
