Agave ferox

Agave ferox, commonly known as the Mexican fire agave, is a succulent plant native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its thick, spiky leaves that can grow up to 3 meters in length. The plant produces a tall, branched flower stalk that bears clusters of yellow flowers. It is adapted to arid environments and stores water in its fleshy leaves.

Its botanical features make it both ornamental and ecologically significant in its native habitat.
