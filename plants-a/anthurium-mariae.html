Anthurium mariae

Anthurium mariae, commonly known as the Marie's arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, glossy green leaves and striking, spadix-shaped inflorescences. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers surrounded by a colorful spathe, which can range from red to orange. It thrives in humid, warm environments with bright, indirect light.

Anthurium mariae is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
