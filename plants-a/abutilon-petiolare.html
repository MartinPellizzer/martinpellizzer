Abutilon petiolare

Abutilon petiolare, commonly known as the Chinese lantern plant, is a flowering shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its velvety, heart-shaped leaves and distinctive, inflated seed pods that resemble small lanterns. The plant produces clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements make it a favored plant in both indoor and outdoor settings.
