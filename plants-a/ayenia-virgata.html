Ayenia virgata

Ayenia virgata, commonly known as the silverleaf ayenia, is a perennial shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. Ayenia virgata thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.

It is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive appearance and drought tolerance.
