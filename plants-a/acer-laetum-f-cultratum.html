Acer laetum f. cultratum

Acer laetum f. cultratum, commonly known as the silver maple variant with a sword-shaped leaf, is a cultivated form of the silver maple tree.

It is characterized by its distinctive, elongated leaves that resemble a sword, giving it a unique appearance. The plant is native to North America and thrives in moist, well-drained soils. It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and rapid growth rate.

The leaves turn vibrant shades of yellow in the fall, adding seasonal interest to the landscape.
