Aeschynanthus macgregorii

Aeschynanthus macgregorii, commonly known as the Christmas ivy, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and vibrant, tubular flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant features glossy, dark green leaves that are often used in ornamental gardening. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Aeschynanthus macgregorii is particularly popular during the holiday season due to its seasonal flowering pattern.
