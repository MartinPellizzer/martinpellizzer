Asparagus sarmentosus var. kunthii

Asparagus sarmentosus var. kunthii, commonly known as the Kunth's asparagus, is a perennial shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its sprawling growth habit and produces small, white to greenish flowers. The plant has compound leaves with multiple leaflets and is often found in humid, shaded environments. Its berries are typically red or orange when mature and contain several seeds.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various soil types.
