Acer platanoides f. aureomarginatum

Acer platanoides f. aureomarginatum, commonly known as the golden margin maple, is a cultivar of the Norway maple.

It is characterized by its striking foliage, with leaves that have a yellow edge and a green or reddish-green center. The plant is typically deciduous, shedding its leaves in the fall. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

This ornamental tree is often used in landscaping for its aesthetic appeal and adaptability to various growing conditions.
