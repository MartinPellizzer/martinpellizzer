Anacampseros angustifolia

Anacampseros angustifolia, commonly known as narrowleaf stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, narrow leaves arranged in a rosette pattern, which help it store water in arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements make it ideal for xeriscaping.
