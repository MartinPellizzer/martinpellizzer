Aubrieta albanica

Aubrieta albanica, commonly known as the Albanian aubrieta, is a flowering plant native to the Balkan region.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces clusters of small, bright yellow flowers in early spring. The plant has compact, mat-forming foliage with dark green, wrinkled leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and alpine settings.

Aubrieta albanica is valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
