Acetosella corniculata var. subglabra

Acetosella corniculata var. subglabra, commonly known as the smooth wood sorrel, is a low-growing perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features trifoliate leaves with heart-shaped lobes and produces small, yellow flowers in early spring. The plant prefers moist, acidic soils and is often found in woodlands and shaded areas. Its leaves are covered in fine, smooth hairs, distinguishing it from other varieties of wood sorrel.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in native plant gardens.
