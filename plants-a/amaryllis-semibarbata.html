Amaryllis semibarbata

Amaryllis semibarbata, commonly known as the barbed amaryllis, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, strap-like leaves that are often tinged with red or purple at the base. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, or red, often with a distinctive red or orange stripe down the center. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and containers.

Its blooming period typically occurs in late spring to early summer, adding a dramatic floral display to the landscape.
