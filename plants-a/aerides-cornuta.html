Aerides cornuta

Aerides cornuta, commonly known as the horned orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a compact, sympodial growth habit with a pseudobulb and a single, upright leaf. The plant produces a single, elongated flower spike bearing a single, strikingly shaped flower with a distinctive horn-like spur. Its flowers are typically white or pale pink, with a strongly fragrant scent.

Aerides cornuta is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a potted plant.
