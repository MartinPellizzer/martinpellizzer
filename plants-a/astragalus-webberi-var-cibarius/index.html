Astragalus Webberi Var. Cibarius

Astragalus webberi var. cibarius is a rare and unique plant native to the arid regions of the southwestern united states. it belongs to the legume family and is known for its distinctive appearance and ecological significance. this variety is often found in rocky soils and dry habitats where it thrives with minimal water. its flowers are typically small and yellow or greenish, adding a subtle beauty to the landscape. due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a valuable subject for botanical study and conservation efforts.
