Acuan interior

Acuan interior, commonly known as the indoor peace lily, is a tropical plant native to the Americas.

It features dark green, glossy leaves that are typically broad and heart-shaped. The plant produces white, spatulate flowers that are often hidden within leaf-like bracts. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low light requirements and ability to thrive in humid environments.

Its ability to purify indoor air makes it a popular choice for home and office spaces.
