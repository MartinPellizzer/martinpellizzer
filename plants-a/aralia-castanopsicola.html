Aralia castanopsicola

Aralia castanopsicola, commonly known as the Chinese angelica tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It belongs to the Araliaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and clusters of small, white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and can reach heights of up to 5 meters. Its berries are red or black and are often consumed by birds and small mammals.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional Chinese medicine.
