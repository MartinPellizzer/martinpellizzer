Aloe perfectior

Aloe perfectior, commonly known as the Cape aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, pointed leaves that are typically green with a reddish tinge. The plant produces tall, branched stems with clusters of yellow flowers during the summer months. Its leaves contain a gel-like substance that is often used in topical treatments.

Aloe perfectior thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for xeriscaping.
