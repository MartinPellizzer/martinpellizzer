Alcantarea burle-marxii

Alcantarea burle-marxii, commonly known as the Burle Marx Alcantarea, is a striking bromeliad native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its large, vibrant, and often colorful rosette of broad, waxy leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in diameter. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences that bear showy, long-lasting flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Due to its ornamental value, it is also cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation programs.
