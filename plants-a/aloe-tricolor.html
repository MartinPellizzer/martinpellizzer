Aloe tricolor

Aloe tricolor, commonly known as the tri-color aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features strikingly colored leaves that display three distinct shades of green, yellow, and red, creating a visually appealing appearance. The plant produces tall, branched flower stalks that bear yellow flowers, adding to its ornamental value. Aloe tricolor thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its leaves contain a gel that is sometimes used for topical applications, although it is less commonly cultivated for medicinal purposes compared to other aloe species.
