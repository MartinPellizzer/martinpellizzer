Aster bellidoides

Aster bellidoides, commonly known as the yellow wood-sorrel, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, yellowish flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and thrives in moist, well-drained soils. It is often found in woodland areas and along stream banks.

Aster bellidoides is noted for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
