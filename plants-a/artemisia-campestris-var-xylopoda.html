Artemisia campestris var. xylopoda

Artemisia campestris var. xylopoda, commonly known as the wood sage, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features aromatic, silver-gray foliage with lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flower heads that appear in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and disturbed areas.

Its medicinal properties have been historically used in traditional remedies.
