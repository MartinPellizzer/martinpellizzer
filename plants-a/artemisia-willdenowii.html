Artemisia willdenowii

Artemisia willdenowii, commonly known as blue artemisia, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its aromatic, silver-gray foliage and small, yellowish-green flower heads. The plant is a member of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers. It typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and coastal areas.

Artemisia willdenowii is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
