Acrocomia hassleri

Acrocomia hassleri, commonly known as the Hassler's palm, is a species of flowering plant in the Arecaceae family.

It is native to southern Mexico and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate leaves. The plant produces yellowish-white inflorescences that bear small, clustered fruits. Acrocomia hassleri is adapted to tropical and subtropical climates and thrives in well-drained soils.
