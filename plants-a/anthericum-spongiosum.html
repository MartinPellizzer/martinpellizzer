Anthericum spongiosum

Anthericum spongiosum, commonly known as the sponge plant, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Araceae family.

It is characterized by its distinctive, spongy, and fibrous root system, which gives it its common name. The plant produces a tall, leafless stem that bears a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe. Native to the Mediterranean region, it thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial shade.

Its unique morphology and ornamental value make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and horticultural collections.
