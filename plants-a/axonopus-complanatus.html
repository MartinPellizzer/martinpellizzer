Axonopus complanatus

Axonopus complanatus, commonly known as flatleaf muhly, is a low-growing grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features narrow, flat leaves that are typically blue-green in color, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in lawns, gardens, and disturbed areas.

Axonopus complanatus is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to tolerate various environmental conditions.
