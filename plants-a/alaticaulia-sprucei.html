Alaticaulia sprucei

Alaticaulia sprucei, commonly known as the alpine spruce, is a rare and specialized evergreen conifer native to high-altitude regions of South America.

It is characterized by its slender, conical shape and dense foliage, adapted to thrive in cold, nutrient-poor environments.
