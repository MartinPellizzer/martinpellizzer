Astragalus koraiensis

Astragalus koraiensis, commonly known as Korean milkvetch, is a perennial herb native to the Korean Peninsula.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in forested areas and mountainous regions, preferring well-drained soils. It is known for its ability to fix nitrogen, contributing to soil fertility.

Astragalus koraiensis is also valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
