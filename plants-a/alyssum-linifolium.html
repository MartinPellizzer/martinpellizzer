Alyssum linifolium

Alyssum linifolium, commonly known as slenderleaf alyssum, is a low-growing, mat-forming perennial native to the western United States.

It features small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pale pink, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has linear, finely textured leaves that are covered in a soft, fuzzy coating, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Alyssum linifolium is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
