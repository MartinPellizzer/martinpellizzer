Aster kraussii

Aster kraussii, commonly known as the Cape aster, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features showy purple to pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Aster kraussii is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
