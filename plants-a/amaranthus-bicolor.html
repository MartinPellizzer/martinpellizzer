Amaranthus bicolor

Amaranthus bicolor, commonly known as the purple-leaf amaranth, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its vibrant, bi-colored leaves that range from green to deep purple. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow. Amaranthus bicolor is often cultivated for its ornamental value and edible leaves.

It thrives in warm climates and is relatively drought-tolerant.
