Ayenia byttnerioides

Ayenia byttnerioides, commonly known as the Christmas bell, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in winter. The plant typically grows as a shrub, reaching heights of up to 1 meter, with narrow, gray-green leaves. Its flowers are usually a combination of white and yellow, with a prominent central disk.

Ayenia byttnerioides is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
