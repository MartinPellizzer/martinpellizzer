Aphyllarum tuberosum

Aphyllarum tuberosum, commonly known as the tuberous aphyllarum, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of South America.

It is characterized by its underground tubers, which serve as storage organs for nutrients. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. Its leaves are usually reduced or absent, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique tuberous structure.
