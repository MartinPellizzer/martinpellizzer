Alchemilla pilosiplica

Alchemilla pilosiplica, commonly known as the silky alchemilla, is a low-growing perennial plant native to Europe.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a soft, silvery appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, greenish-yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas or along stream banks.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and ecological restoration projects.
