Anthurium impolitoellipticum

Anthurium impolitoellipticum, commonly known as the elliptic anthurium, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped leaves that are typically deep green with a waxy texture. The plant produces striking spathes that can range in color from red to orange, often with a contrasting midrib. It thrives in humid, shady environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Anthurium impolitoellipticum is a popular ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
