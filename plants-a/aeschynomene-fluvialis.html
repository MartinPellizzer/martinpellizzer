Aeschynomene fluvialis

Aeschynomene fluvialis, commonly known as riverbed aeschynomene, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a herbaceous annual that typically grows in wetland environments, such as riverbanks and floodplains. The plant has slender, branching stems and small, delicate leaves that are often opposite in arrangement. Its flowers are usually pale blue or white, arranged in clusters, and attract various pollinators.
