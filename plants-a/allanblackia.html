Allanblackia

Allanblackia, commonly known as African mango or allanblackia africana, is a tropical plant native to West Africa.

It is characterized by its large, glossy leaves and clusters of yellow or orange flowers. The plant produces unique, sticky fruits that contain small, hard seeds. Allanblackia is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.

It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
