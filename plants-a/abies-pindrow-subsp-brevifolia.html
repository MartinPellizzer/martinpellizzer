Abies pindrow subsp. brevifolia

Abies pindrow subsp.

brevifolia, commonly known as the Kashmir pine, is a coniferous tree native to the Himalayas. It is characterized by its narrow, conical shape and dark green, needle-like leaves.

The tree produces small, woody cones that contain seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in cool, moist climates at high altitudes.

This subspecies is valued for its timber and is also an important part of the mountain ecosystem.
