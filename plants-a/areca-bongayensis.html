Areca bongayensis

Areca bongayensis, commonly known as the Bongayen areca, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Arecaceae family, which includes palms, and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and clustered leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color. Its fruit is a spiny, fibrous structure that contains edible seeds.

Areca bongayensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine in some regions.
