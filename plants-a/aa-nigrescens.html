Aa nigrescens

Aa nigrescens, commonly known as the black hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its dark purple to black leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, often reaching heights of up to 2 meters. Its flowers bloom in late summer and early fall, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Aa nigrescens is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
