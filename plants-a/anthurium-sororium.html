Anthurium sororium

Anthurium sororium, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red spathes and elongated, glossy green leaves. The plant belongs to the Araceae family and is prized for its ornamental value in horticulture. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Its unique floral structure consists of a spadix surrounded by a colorful bract, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
