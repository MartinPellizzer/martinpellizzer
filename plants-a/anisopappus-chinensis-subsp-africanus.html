Anisopappus chinensis subsp. africanus

Anisopappus chinensis subsp.

africanus, commonly known as African goatweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family. It is characterized by its erect, branched stems and small, pale yellow to white composite flowers.

The plant produces small, oval-shaped seeds that are often dispersed by wind. It is native to Africa and thrives in arid and semi-arid environments.

Due to its adaptability, it is sometimes considered a weed in agricultural areas.
