Anthurium philodendroides

Anthurium philodendroides, commonly known as the red-veined anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves with deep red veins. The plant produces colorful spadices, which are the modified leaves that house the flowers. It thrives in humid, warm environments with bright, indirect light.

Anthurium philodendroides is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
