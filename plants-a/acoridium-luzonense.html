Acoridium luzonense

Acoridium luzonense, commonly known as the Luzon arrowhead, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its arrow-shaped leaves and striking inflorescence. The plant typically grows in wetlands and marshy areas, thriving in humid and shaded environments. It has a rhizomatous root system that helps it spread and establish in moist soils.

Due to its unique morphology and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
