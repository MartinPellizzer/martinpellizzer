Asterostemma repandum

Asterostemma repandum, commonly known as the wandering star, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant produces small, green, oval-shaped fruits that contain the seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, often growing in rocky or sandy habitats.

Asterostemma repandum is a popular choice for rock gardens and is valued for its ornamental beauty.
