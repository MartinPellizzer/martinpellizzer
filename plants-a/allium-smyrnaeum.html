Allium smyrnaeum

Allium smyrnaeum, commonly known as Smyrna garlic, is a bulbous plant native to the eastern Mediterranean region.

It produces clusters of small, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has a strong garlic odor and is often cultivated for its edible bulbs. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it thrives in well-drained soils.

Allium smyrnaeum is a hardy perennial that is well-suited for rock gardens and borders.
