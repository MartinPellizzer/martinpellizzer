Adesmia aueri

Adesmia aueri, commonly known as the yellow bellflower, is a flowering plant native to parts of South America.

The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. Its leaves are trifoliate, meaning they consist of three leaflets, and it produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds.
