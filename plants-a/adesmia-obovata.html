Adesmia obovata

Adesmia obovata, commonly known as the broad-ovate adesmia, is a flowering plant native to parts of South America.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows in dry, arid environments. The plant features small, clustered flowers that are usually pink or white in color. Its leaves are ovate and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Adesmia obovata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
