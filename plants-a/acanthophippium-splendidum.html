Acanthophippium splendidum

Acanthophippium splendidum, commonly known as the splendid starfish cactus, is a unique succulent native to the arid regions of Mexico.

It is characterized by its star-shaped, fleshy stems that are often covered in soft, spiny tubercles. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the spring and summer months, adding a striking visual element to its landscape. It is well adapted to drought conditions, storing water in its thick, fleshy stems.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and protected species in its native environment.
