Allium xiphopetalum

Allium xiphopetalum, commonly known as the Persian onion or spearleaf garlic.

This perennial plant belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is native to Central Asia. It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of pale pink to lilac flowers. The bulb is small and is often used in culinary applications.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
