Anonis rotundifolia

Anonis rotundifolia, commonly known as the round-leaf anonas, is a tropical plant native to the Caribbean and Central America.

It is characterized by its oval, waxy leaves and small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces edible fruits that are similar in appearance to cherries, with a sweet and tart flavor. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and fruit yield.

The species is also valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
