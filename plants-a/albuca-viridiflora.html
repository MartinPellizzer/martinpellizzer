Albuca viridiflora

Albuca viridiflora, commonly known as the green-flowered albuca, is a member of the Asparagaceae family and is native to southern Africa.

It is a bulbous plant that produces narrow, grass-like leaves and clusters of white or greenish-white flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade. It is often grown as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and unique floral display.

Albuca viridiflora is relatively low maintenance and suitable for rock gardens or containers.
