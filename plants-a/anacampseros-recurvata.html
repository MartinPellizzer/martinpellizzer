Anacampseros recurvata

Anacampseros recurvata, commonly known as the zigzag ice plant, is a succulent species native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, ribbed leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. Its unique, zigzagging leaf arrangement gives it a distinctive appearance.

It is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its hardiness and aesthetic appeal.
