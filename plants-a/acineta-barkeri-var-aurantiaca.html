Acineta barkeri var. aurantiaca

Acineta barkeri var. aurantiaca, commonly known as the orange acineta, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, cylindrical stems that are typically bright orange to reddish in color. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers that are usually yellow or orange, adding to its ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is often cultivated as a decorative plant in arid gardens.
