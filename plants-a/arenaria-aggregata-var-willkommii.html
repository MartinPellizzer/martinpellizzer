Arenaria aggregata var. willkommii

Arenaria aggregata var. willkommii, commonly known as the sandspurry, is a low-growing perennial plant native to arid and semi-arid regions.

It features small, fleshy leaves and produces clusters of white or pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant is adapted to sandy soils and can thrive in harsh, dry environments. It is often found in coastal dunes and disturbed soils, showcasing its resilience.

Its botanical characteristics make it an important species for ecological studies in sandy ecosystems.
