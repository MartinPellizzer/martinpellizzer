Astracantha pycnocephala

Astracantha pycnocephala, commonly known as the thick-headed aster, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense, spherical heads of small, yellowish flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, and the stems are often hairy.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
