Acianthera cryptoceras

Acianthera cryptoceras, commonly known as the cryptic orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to South America.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid with delicate, greenish-white flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky crevices. Its roots are specialized for absorbing nutrients from the soil, and it has a short, rhizomatous stem.
