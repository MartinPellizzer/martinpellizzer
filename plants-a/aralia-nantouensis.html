Aralia nantouensis

Aralia nantouensis, commonly known as Nantou aralia, is a flowering plant native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the Araliaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its fruit is a red, fleshy berry that contains several seeds.

Aralia nantouensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and plays a role in local ecosystems.
