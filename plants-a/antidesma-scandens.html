Antidesma scandens

Antidesma scandens, commonly known as the climbing antidesma, is a woody vine native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its climbing habit and opposite, elliptical leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in clusters, followed by capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds.
