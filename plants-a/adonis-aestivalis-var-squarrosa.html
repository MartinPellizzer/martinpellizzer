Adonis aestivalis var. squarrosa

Adonis aestivalis var. squarrosa, commonly known as the squarrose adonis, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of the Mediterranean.

It is a member of the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, yellowish-orange flowers. The plant produces small, rounded leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a fuzzy appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often growing in rocky or alpine environments.

The flowers typically bloom in early spring, adding a vibrant touch to its natural habitat.
