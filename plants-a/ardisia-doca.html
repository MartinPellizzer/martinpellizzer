Ardisia doca

Ardisia doca, commonly known as the glossy privet, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, glossy leaves and produces small, white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant's fruit is a bright red berry, which contains several seeds. Ardisia doca is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and seasonal blooms.

It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it adaptable to various garden settings.
