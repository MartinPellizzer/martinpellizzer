Adromischus fragilis var. numeensensis

Adromischus fragilis var. numeensensis, commonly known as the Numensense Adromischus, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features small, fleshy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically green with a slight bluish or purple tinge. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It is a low-maintenance plant that thrives in well-draining soil and full sun exposure.

Due to its compact size and unique appearance, it is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant or in rock gardens.
