Aster latahensis

Aster latahensis, commonly known as the Latah aster, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads and showy blooms. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in woodland or meadow habitats. Its flowers are usually blue to purple in color, with ray florets that bloom in the summer months.

Aster latahensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance as a nectar source for pollinators.
