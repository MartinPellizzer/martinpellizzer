Alternanthera asterotricha

Alternanthera asterotricha, commonly known as the star-tasselled woundwort, is a tropical flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, star-shaped inflorescences that give it its common name. The plant features alternate, lance-shaped leaves that are often reddish or purple in color. It produces small, white or pink flowers that are clustered in dense, tassel-like formations.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
