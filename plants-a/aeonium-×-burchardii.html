Aeonium × burchardii

Aeonium × burchardii, commonly known as the Burchard's Aeonium, is a hybrid succulent plant native to the Canary Islands.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with red or purple hues along the edges. The plant produces tall, branching inflorescences that bear yellow flowers, which bloom in spring or early summer. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with plenty of sunlight.

Aeonium × burchardii is popular in rock gardens and as a container plant due to its ornamental appearance and low maintenance requirements.
