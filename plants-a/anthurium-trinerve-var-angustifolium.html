Anthurium trinerve var. angustifolium

Anthurium trinerve var. angustifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaf anthurium, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its elongated, narrow leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy surface. The plant produces striking spadices, which are inflorescences that consist of a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe. It thrives in humid, warm environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in indoor gardening and tropical landscapes.
