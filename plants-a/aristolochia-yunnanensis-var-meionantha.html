Aristolochia yunnanensis var. meionantha

Aristolochia yunnanensis var. meionantha, commonly known as the Yunnan arrow vine, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Aristolochiaceae family and is characterized by its unique, tubular, and often fragrant flowers. The plant produces heart-shaped leaves and has a climbing growth habit. It is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional Chinese medicine.

However, it contains toxic compounds and should be handled with care.
