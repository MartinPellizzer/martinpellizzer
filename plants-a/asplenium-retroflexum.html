Asplenium retroflexum

Asplenium retroflexum, commonly known as the curled shield fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Aspleniaceae.

It is characterized by its compact, clump-forming growth habit and dark green, leathery fronds that are often curled or twisted. The fronds are typically divided into narrow, linear segments with a distinctive, almost shield-like appearance. This plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant.
