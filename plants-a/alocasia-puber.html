Alocasia puber

Alocasia puber, commonly known as the velvet leaf alocasia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant thrives in humid, warm environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its striking foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and ornamental purposes.

Alocasia puber is also known for its ability to purify the air, making it both aesthetically pleasing and beneficial for indoor spaces.
