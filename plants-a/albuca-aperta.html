Albuca aperta

Albuca aperta, commonly known as the Cape tulip, is a bulbous perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features narrow, strap-like leaves that are often blue-green in color. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of yellow to orange, typically in spring. Albuca aperta thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

It is also valued for its drought tolerance and ornamental appeal in gardens.
