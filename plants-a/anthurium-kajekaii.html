Anthurium kajekaii

Anthurium kajekaii, commonly known as the Kajekaii arrowhead plant, is a tropical species native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces vibrant red spadices, which are the spiky, clustered inflorescences typical of the Araceae family. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Anthurium kajekaii is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in botanical gardens.
