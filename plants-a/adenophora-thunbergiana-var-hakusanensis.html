Adenophora thunbergiana var. hakusanensis

Adenophora thunbergiana var. hakusanensis, commonly known as the Hakusan bellflower, is a flowering plant native to parts of East Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale blue. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or mountainous regions. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a hairy texture.
