Asparagopsis densiflora

Asparagopsis densiflora, commonly known as the dense-flowered asparagus, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its compact, evergreen growth habit. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered white or pale pink flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Asparagopsis densiflora is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive foliage and resilience in various climates.
