Aphelexis selaginifolia

Aphelexis selaginifolia, commonly known as the silver leaf honeysuckle, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has a trailing or climbing growth habit, making it suitable for hanging baskets or as a ground cover. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its unique leaf color and pleasant scent.
