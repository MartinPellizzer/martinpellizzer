Anthurium davidneillii

Anthurium davidneillii, commonly known as the Neill's Anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are often a deep green with a glossy texture. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-like inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, typically in shades of red or orange. It thrives in high humidity and indirect light, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Its unique foliage and showy flowers make it a standout ornamental plant in botanical collections.
