Amaryllis belladonna rosea-perfecta

Amaryllis belladonna rosea-perfecta, commonly known as the rose-perfect amaryllis, is a striking hybrid of the amaryllis genus.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from deep red to pale pink, often with a bicolor pattern. The plant produces large, strap-like leaves that are dark green and broad, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires ample sunlight to bloom fully.

This hybrid is prized for its vibrant blooms and is a popular choice for both garden cultivation and indoor planting.
