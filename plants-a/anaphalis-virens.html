Anaphalis virens

Anaphalis virens, commonly known as the silverleaf fleabane, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features silvery-white hairs on its leaves and stems, giving it a distinctive, frost-like appearance. It typically grows in open habitats such as meadows and grasslands.

Anaphalis virens is often found in moist to dry soils and can thrive in full sun.
