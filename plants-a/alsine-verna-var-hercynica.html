Alsine verna var. hercynica

Alsine verna var. hercynica, commonly known as the Hercynian yellowweed, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and small, yellowish flowers. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils, such as roadsides and fields, and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while the seeds are small and dispersed by wind.

It is often found in temperate climates and is adapted to withstand dry conditions.
