Anaphalis yangii

Anaphalis yangii, commonly known as the Yang's frost flower, is a species of flowering plant in the aster family.

It is native to China, specifically found in the mountainous regions of Sichuan and Yunnan provinces. The plant is characterized by its woolly, silver-green leaves and clusters of small, white, daisy-like flowers. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine meadows.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
