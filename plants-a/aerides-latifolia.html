Aerides latifolia

Aerides latifolia, commonly known as the broad-leafed orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, strap-like leaves and produces fragrant, long-lasting flowers in shades of white, pink, and lavender. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity and well-draining soil. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in both indoor and outdoor settings.

Aerides latifolia is a popular choice for gardeners seeking a resilient and visually appealing orchid.
