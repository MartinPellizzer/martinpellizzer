Asplenium erectopaleaceum

Asplenium erectopaleaceum, commonly known as the upright pale fern, is a species of evergreen fern characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped fronds.

It belongs to the family Aspleniaceae and is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as forests and rocky crevices. Its fronds are usually upright, with a pale green coloration that gives it its common name.

Asplenium erectopaleaceum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
