Argythamnia argentea

Argythamnia argentea, commonly known as silverleaf sandmat, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of southern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial shrub that typically reaches heights of 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features silvery-gray foliage, which helps reduce water loss in its harsh environment. Its small, yellow flowers are arranged in dense clusters and bloom during the warmer months.

Argythamnia argentea is adapted to sandy soils and is often found in coastal and inland desert areas.
