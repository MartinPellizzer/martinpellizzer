Afroligusticum volkensii

Afroligusticum volkensii, commonly known as the East African star grass, is a flowering plant native to eastern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its distinctive yellow-orange flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in open grasslands and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces achenes as its fruit type.

Afroligusticum volkensii is often used in traditional medicine and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
