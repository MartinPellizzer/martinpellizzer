Asarum sonunsanense f. viriluteolum

Asarum sonunsanense f. viriluteolum, commonly known as the hairy asarum, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of East Asia.

It belongs to the Aristolochiaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped, aromatic leaves. The plant produces unique, fragrant, and often dark purple or brownish flowers that are tubular in shape. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.

Due to its medicinal properties, it has been used in traditional herbal remedies for various ailments.
