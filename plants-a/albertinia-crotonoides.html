Albertinia crotonoides

Albertinia crotonoides, commonly known as the African croton, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its rough, often hairy leaves and bright yellow flowers that appear in clusters. The plant produces small, woody fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes used in ornamental gardening.
