Artabotrys dielsianus

Artabotrys dielsianus, commonly known as the golden torch tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers that resemble torches, which bloom in clusters during the rainy season. The plant has compound leaves with elongated leaflets and produces small, round fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive addition to tropical gardens and natural landscapes.
