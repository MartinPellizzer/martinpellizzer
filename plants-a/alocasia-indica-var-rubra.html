Alocasia indica var. rubra

Alocasia indica var. rubra, commonly known as the Red-veined Alocasia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves with striking red veins against a deep green background. The plant thrives in humid, warm environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and as a decorative plant.

Alocasia indica var. rubra is also valued for its air-purifying properties and ability to thrive in low-light conditions.
