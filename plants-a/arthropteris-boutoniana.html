Arthropteris boutoniana

Arthropteris boutoniana, commonly known as the tropical shield fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, shield-shaped fronds that are typically light green in color. The plant grows in moist, shaded environments, often in tropical rainforests. Its reproductive structures, including sori, are located on the underside of the fronds.

Arthropteris boutoniana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
