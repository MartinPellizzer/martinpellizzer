Anemone Hortensis Var. Fulgens

Anemone hortensis var. fulgens is a striking perennial known for its vibrant, showy flowers. This variety boasts large, double blooms that come in shades of red, pink, and white. It thrives in cool climates and prefers partial to full shade. The plant is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice among gardeners seeking colorful, elegant blooms.
