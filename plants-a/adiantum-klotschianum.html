Adiantum klotschianum

Adiantum klotschianum, commonly known as the Klotschian maidenhair fern, is a species of fern native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds that are typically bright green with a slightly glossy appearance. The plant prefers shady, moist environments and is often found in forest understories. Its reproductive structures, called sori, are located on the underside of the fronds and release spores for propagation.

Adiantum klotschianum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in humid conditions.
