Albuca jacquinii

Albuca jacquinii, commonly known as the Jacob's lily, is a bulbous perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of white, bell-shaped flowers during the summer months. The plant is adapted to arid environments and requires well-drained soil to thrive. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ease of care.
