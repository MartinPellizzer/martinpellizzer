Anthurium purpureospathum

Anthurium purpureospathum, commonly known as the purple-spathe anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, elongated spathes that are typically a deep purple or maroon color, which enclose the small, greenish-white spadix. The plant has heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are dark green and can grow up to 30 cm in length. It thrives in humid, warm environments with indirect light and requires well-draining soil.

Anthurium purpureospathum is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
