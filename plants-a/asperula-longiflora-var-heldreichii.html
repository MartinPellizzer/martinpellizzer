Asperula longiflora var. heldreichii

Asperula longiflora var. heldreichii, commonly known as the Heldreich's woodruff, is a perennial evergreen shrub native to the mountainous regions of the Balkans.

It features small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters during late spring to early summer. The plant has dark green, leathery leaves that are opposite in arrangement and often have a waxy texture. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and woodland plantings.
