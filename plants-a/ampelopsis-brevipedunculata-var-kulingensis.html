Ampelopsis brevipedunculata var. kulingensis

Ampelopsis brevipedunculata var. kulingensis, commonly known as the Kuling ivy, is a climbing vine native to China.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a slightly serrated edge and produces small, greenish-yellow flowers in clusters. The plant is characterized by its vigorous growth habit and ability to climb various structures. Its berries, which turn red when mature, are edible but not commonly consumed by humans.

It is often used in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.
