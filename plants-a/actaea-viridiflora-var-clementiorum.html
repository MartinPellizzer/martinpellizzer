Actaea viridiflora var. clementiorum

Actaea viridiflora var. clementiorum, commonly known as the Clement's green-flowered actaea, is a perennial herb native to eastern North America.

It features compound, pinnately arranged leaves that are typically dark green with a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces striking greenish-white flowers in early summer, which are clustered in upright, branched cymes. Its fruit is a red, fleshy berry that matures in late summer, containing several seeds.

This variety is often found in moist, shaded woodlands and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
