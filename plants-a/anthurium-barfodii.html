Anthurium barfodii

Anthurium barfodii, commonly known as the Barfod's anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-shaped flowers surrounded by a colorful spathe, which can range from red to orange. It thrives in humid, well-draining soil and requires indirect sunlight for optimal growth.

Anthurium barfodii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
