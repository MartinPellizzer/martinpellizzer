Adenophora xifengensis

Adenophora xifengensis, commonly known as the Xifeng bellflower, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped, violet-blue flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions and has lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture. It is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
