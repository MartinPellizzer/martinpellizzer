Acmella darwinii

Acmella darwinii, commonly known as the Darwin's button.

It is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Brazil. The plant features a compact, bushy growth habit with bright yellow flowers that resemble small buttons. Its leaves are ovate and covered in fine hairs, giving them a velvety texture.

The species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to grow in well-drained soil.
