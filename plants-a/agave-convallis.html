Agave convallis

Agave convallis, commonly known as the narrowleaf agave, is a species of flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a succulent plant characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are typically narrow and blue-green in color. The plant produces a tall, branched inflorescence with small, white or pale yellow flowers. It is adapted to arid environments and can thrive in well-drained soil with minimal water.

Agave convallis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and resilience in xeriscaping.
