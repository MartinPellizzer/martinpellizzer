Astrotricha brachyandra

Astrotricha brachyandra, commonly known as the narrow-leaved starwort, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features small, daisy-like flowers that are typically yellow or white in color. Its leaves are narrow and often covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Astrotricha brachyandra is often found in open grasslands and rocky areas, where it thrives in well-drained soils.
