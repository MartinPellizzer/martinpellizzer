Asterolasia rivularis

Asterolasia rivularis, commonly known as the river aster, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as riverbanks and forest floors. Its leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and have a rough texture.

The flowers are small and yellow, arranged in clusters, and bloom during the rainy season.
