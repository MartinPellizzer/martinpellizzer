Astrophytum myriostigma var. grandiflorum

Astrophytum myriostigma var. grandiflorum, commonly known as the giant dayflower cactus, is a succulent native to the Sonoran Desert.

It is characterized by its thick, ribbed stems that can grow up to 2 meters in height. The plant produces large, showy yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It has a shallow root system and is adapted to arid environments with minimal rainfall.

Due to its striking appearance, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
