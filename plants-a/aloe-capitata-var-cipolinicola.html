Aloe capitata var. cipolinicola

Aloe capitata var. cipolinicola, commonly known as the Cape aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of thick, fleshy, blue-green leaves that store water, typical of arid environments. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow to orange flowers, which bloom in spring. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their soothing properties.

It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for xeriscaping.
