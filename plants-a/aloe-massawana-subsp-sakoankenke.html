Aloe massawana subsp. sakoankenke

Aloe massawana subsp.

sakoankenke, commonly known as the KwaZulu-Natal aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa. It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a reddish tinge.

The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing yellow flowers, which bloom in summer. It is well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, storing water in its leaves for survival.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and traditional herbal practices.
