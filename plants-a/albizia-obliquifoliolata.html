Albizia obliquifoliolata

Albizia obliquifoliolata, commonly known as the slender coral tree, is a deciduous leguminous tree native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It features compound leaves with an unusual, zigzag arrangement of leaflets, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or white, arranged in dense, panicle-like clusters. Its seeds are enclosed in long, slender pods that split open when mature.

Albizia obliquifoliolata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
