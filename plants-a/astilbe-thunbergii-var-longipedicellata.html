Astilbe thunbergii var. longipedicellata

Astilbe thunbergii var. longipedicellata, commonly known as the long-peduncled thunberg astilbe, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to eastern Asia.

It features upright, slender stems that bear clusters of fragrant, pale pink to white flowers. The plant has finely divided, fern-like leaves that are glossy and provide ornamental value throughout the growing season. It thrives in moist, shady environments and prefers well-drained, fertile soil.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for woodland gardens and shaded landscapes.
