Aulomyrcia platyclada var. kaieteurensis

Aulomyrcia platyclada var. kaieteurensis, commonly known as the Kaieteurean myrtle, is a rare evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires a humid, subtropical climate.
