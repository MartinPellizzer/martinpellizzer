Aster ansif

Aster ansif, commonly known as the narrow-leaved aster, is a flowering plant native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically features slender, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of small, blue to purple flowers. Aster ansif thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it a hardy addition to gardens.

Its botanical features make it an attractive and ecologically valuable plant for pollinators.
