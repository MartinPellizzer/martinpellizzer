Aconiopteris gorgonea

Aconiopteris gorgonea, commonly known as the Gorgonea shield fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive, shield-shaped fronds that are typically leathery and covered in small, scale-like structures. The plant is native to tropical and subtropical regions, particularly in Southeast Asia and the Pacific Islands. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
