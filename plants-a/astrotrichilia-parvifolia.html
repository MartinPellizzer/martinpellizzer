Astrotrichilia parvifolia

Astrotrichilia parvifolia, commonly known as the small-leaved star-leaf, is a flowering plant native to the southern United States.

It is characterized by its small, narrow leaves and clusters of white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as prairies and woodland edges. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Astrotrichilia parvifolia is often found in well-drained soils and is adapted to dry, warm climates.
