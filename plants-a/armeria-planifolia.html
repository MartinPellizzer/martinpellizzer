Armeria planifolia

Armeria planifolia, commonly known as flatleaf thrift, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces clusters of small, white or pink flowers. The plant has linear, fleshy leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its hardy nature and ability to tolerate harsh conditions make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
