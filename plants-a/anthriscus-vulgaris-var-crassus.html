Anthriscus vulgaris var. crassus

Anthriscus vulgaris var. crassus, commonly known as broad-leaved cow parsley, is a flowering plant in the Apiaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, fern-like leaves and white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in umbels.
