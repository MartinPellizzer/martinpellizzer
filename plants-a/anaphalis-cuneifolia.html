Anaphalis cuneifolia

Anaphalis cuneifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leafed silverweed, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, typically covered in a dense layer of fine, white pubescence.

Anaphalis cuneifolia is often found in rocky or dry habitats, where it thrives in well-drained soils.
