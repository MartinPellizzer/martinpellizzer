Actaea erythrocarpa f. kamtschatika

Actaea erythrocarpa f. kamtschatika, commonly known as the Kamchatka redfruit.

It is a variant of the redfruit plant, characterized by its distinctive red berries that contrast with its dark green foliage. The plant is native to the mountainous regions of the Russian Far East, particularly Kamchatka. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in forested areas.

The species is noted for its ornamental value due to its striking flowers and berries.
