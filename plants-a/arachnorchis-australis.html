Arachnorchis australis

Arachnorchis australis, commonly known as the Australian spider orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to southeastern Australia.

It is characterized by its unique, spider-like appearance, with slender, hairy stems and small, intricate flowers. The plant typically blooms in spring, producing pale green or yellowish flowers that are highly specialized for pollination by specific insects. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests and understoreys.

Arachnorchis australis is a rare and protected species, highlighting its ecological significance in its native habitat.
