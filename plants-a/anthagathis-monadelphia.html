Anthagathis monadelphia

Anthagathis monadelphia, commonly known as the Cape jasmine, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces small, fragrant white or pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and have a slightly serrated margin.

The plant is characterized by its aromatic foliage and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
