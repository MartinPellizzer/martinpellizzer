Arenaria stygia f. olympica

It is a low-growing perennial that thrives in cold, moist, and shaded environments, often found in alpine meadows and rocky substrates. The plant features small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in early summer, adding a delicate touch to its harsh habitat. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are adapted to withstand strong winds and low temperatures.
