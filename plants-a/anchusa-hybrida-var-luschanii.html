Anchusa hybrida var. luschanii

Anchusa hybrida var. luschanii, commonly known as the Luschan's blueweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central Asia.

It features striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in summer, adding vibrant color to gardens. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy, contributing to its ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for drought-tolerant landscaping.
