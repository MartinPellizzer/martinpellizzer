Arenaria siyuanakouensis

Arenaria siyuanakouensis, commonly known as the Siyuanakou sandwort, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit. The plant features small, white or pinkish flowers that bloom in early summer. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid regions.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
