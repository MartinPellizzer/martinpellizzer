Athyrium klotzschii

Athyrium klotzschii, commonly known as the Japanese mottled wood fern, is a species of fern native to East Asia.

It belongs to the family Athyriaceae and is characterized by its elegant, feather-like fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors and rocky crevices. Its leaves are bi-colored, with a dark green upper surface and a lighter green underside, creating a striking visual contrast.

Athyrium klotzschii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in partial shade.
