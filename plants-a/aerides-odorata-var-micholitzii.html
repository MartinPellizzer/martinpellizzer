Aerides odorata var. micholitzii

Aerides odorata var. micholitzii, commonly known as the Micholitz's orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are strap-shaped and often covered in a dense layer of fine hairs.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
