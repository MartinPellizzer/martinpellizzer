Astragalus cysticalyx

Astragalus cysticalyx, commonly known as the Chinese milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to regions of Asia, including China, Mongolia, and Russia.

The plant is known for its ability to thrive in arid and semi-arid environments, making it a hardy species adapted to dry conditions. Astragalus cysticalyx is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
