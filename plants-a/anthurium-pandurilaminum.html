Anthurium pandurilaminum

Anthurium pandurilaminum, commonly known as the golden shield anthurium, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a metallic sheen. The plant produces vibrant red spadices surrounded by a showy, white to yellow spathe, which is a hallmark of the Araceae family. It thrives in high humidity and indirect light, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique foliage and colorful inflorescence make it a standout specimen in both ornamental and botanical collections.
