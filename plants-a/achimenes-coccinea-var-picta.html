Achimenes coccinea var. picta

Achimenes coccinea var. picta, commonly known as the painted flame bell, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red flowers with intricate, contrasting patterns that resemble flames. The plant features heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a velvety texture. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
