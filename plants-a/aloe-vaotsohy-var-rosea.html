Aloe vaotsohy var. rosea

Aloe vaotsohy var. rosea, commonly known as the pink aloe, is a succulent plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its fleshy, spiky leaves that are typically pink or reddish in color, especially when exposed to sunlight. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor gardens.
