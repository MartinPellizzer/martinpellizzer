Anemone lepida

Anemone lepida, commonly known as the showy anemone, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the northeastern United States.

It features showy, pink to purple flowers with a distinctive yellow center, blooming in early spring. The plant has palmately divided, lobed leaves that are typically bluish-green in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.

Anemone lepida is often found in woodland habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
