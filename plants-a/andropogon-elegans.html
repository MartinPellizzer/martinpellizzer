Andropogon elegans

Andropogon elegans, commonly known as silky grass or silky stem grass, is a tall perennial grass native to the southeastern United States.

It features slender, wiry stems that are often covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in dense, spike-like inflorescences. It thrives in moist, open habitats such as wetlands and prairies.

Its ecological importance includes providing habitat and food for various wildlife species.
