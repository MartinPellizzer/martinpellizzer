Armeria montana var. stenophylla

Armeria montana var. stenophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leaved thrift, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of western North America.

It is characterized by its compact, mat-forming growth habit and its clusters of small, bright pink or white flowers. The leaves are narrow and linear, often covered in a fine layer of white hairs, which helps reduce water loss in its arid habitat.
