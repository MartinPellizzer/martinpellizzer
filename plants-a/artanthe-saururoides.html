Artanthe saururoides

Artanthe saururoides, commonly known as the narrowleaf artanthe, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features slender, upright stems with narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy. Its small, daisy-like flowers are typically blue or purple, adding ornamental value to gardens.

Artanthe saururoides is adapted to dry conditions, making it a resilient choice for arid landscapes.
