Asterolasia sola

Asterolasia sola, commonly known as the coastal aster, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and thrives in sunny, coastal environments. Its showy yellow flowers bloom in spring and attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Asterolasia sola is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in coastal ecosystems.
