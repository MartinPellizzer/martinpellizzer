Alaticaulia receptrix

Alaticaulia receptrix, commonly known as the winged stem orchid, is a unique flowering plant native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

The plant produces small, delicate flowers in shades of white and pale yellow, often arranged in clusters. Its leaves are typically narrow and grass-like, growing close to the ground.

Alaticaulia receptrix thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical forest understories.
