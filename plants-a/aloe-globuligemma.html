Aloe globuligemma

Aloe globuligemma, commonly known as the "Globular Aloe," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of thick, fleshy, bluish-green leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces striking yellow to orange flowers on tall, branched stems during the summer months. Its compact size and unique appearance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.

Aloe globuligemma is also valued for its medicinal properties, with gel from its leaves used in traditional remedies.
