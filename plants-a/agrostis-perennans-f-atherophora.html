Agrostis perennans f. atherophora

Agrostis perennans f. atherophora, commonly known as the silver hair grass, is a variant of the perennial grass species Agrostis perennans.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-tipped leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. This form is typically found in moist, well-drained soils in temperate regions. The plant has a low-growing habit and forms dense mats, making it suitable for ground cover.

It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of conditions.
