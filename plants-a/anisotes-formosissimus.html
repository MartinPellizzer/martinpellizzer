Anisotes formosissimus

Anisotes formosissimus, commonly known as the showy anisote, is a striking plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that often display a vibrant mix of colors, including purple, pink, and white. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Anisotes formosissimus is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in moist, shaded environments.
