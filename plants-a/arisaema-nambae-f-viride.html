Arisaema nambae f. viride

Arisaema nambae f. viride, commonly known as the green-flowered arrowhead, is a variant of the Arisaema nambae species native to Southeast Asia.

This plant is characterized by its distinctive arrow-shaped leaves and a spadix surrounded by a spathe. The foliage is typically a vibrant green, which sets it apart from other varieties of the species. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens.
