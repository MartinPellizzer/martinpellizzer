Acanthopetalus mirabilis

Acanthopetalus mirabilis, commonly known as the marvel cactus, is a species of flowering plant native to the arid regions of South America.

It belongs to the family Cactaceae and is characterized by its spiny stems and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows as a small shrub, often forming dense clusters to conserve moisture. Its flowers, which bloom in shades of yellow and orange, attract pollinators such as bees and birds.

Acanthopetalus mirabilis is adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments through its succulent stems and reduced leaves.
