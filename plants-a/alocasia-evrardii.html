Alocasia evrardii

Alocasia evrardii, commonly known as the Black Velvet Alocasia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark, velvety foliage with intricate, almost spider-like leaf veins. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are broad and heart-shaped, with a deep green to almost black coloration.

Alocasia evrardii thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
