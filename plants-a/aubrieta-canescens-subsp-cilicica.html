Aubrieta canescens subsp. cilicica

Aubrieta canescens subsp.

cilicica, commonly known as the Cilician aubrieta, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the eastern Mediterranean region. It is characterized by its compact, mat-forming growth habit and produces clusters of small, bright yellow flowers in early spring.

The plant features silver-gray foliage with a woolly texture, which helps it retain moisture in arid conditions. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and alpine settings.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
