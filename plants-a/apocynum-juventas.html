Apocynum juventas

Apocynum juventas, commonly known as the young milkweed, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family, which includes many plants with milky sap. The plant produces showy, pink to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are ovate and slightly hairy, and the stems are covered in fine, white hairs.

Apocynum juventas is often found in moist, open habitats such as meadows and woodland edges.
