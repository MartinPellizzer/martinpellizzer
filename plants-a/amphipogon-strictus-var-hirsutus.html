Amphipogon strictus var. hirsutus

Amphipogon strictus var. hirsutus, commonly known as the hairy strict amphipogon, is a species of flowering plant native to certain regions of South America.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and hairy foliage, which gives it its varietal name "hirsutus.". The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
