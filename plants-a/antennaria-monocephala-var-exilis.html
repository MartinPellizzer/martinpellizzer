Antennaria monocephala var. exilis

Antennaria monocephala var. exilis, commonly known as the slender sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to arctic and alpine regions.

The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to cold, harsh environments.
