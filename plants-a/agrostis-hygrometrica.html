Agrostis hygrometrica

Agrostis hygrometrica, commonly known as the moisture grass, is a species of grass that thrives in moist environments.

It is characterized by its fine, tufted growth and dark green foliage. This plant is often found in wet meadows, along stream banks, and in other damp habitats. Its ability to tolerate high humidity makes it well-suited for areas with frequent rainfall.

Agrostis hygrometrica plays an important role in stabilizing soil and supporting local biodiversity.
