Alchemilla glomerulans f. glabrior

Alchemilla glomerulans f. glabrior, commonly known as the smooth alpine lady's mantle, is a low-growing perennial plant native to mountainous regions of Europe.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit. The plant features broad, silver-green leaves that are covered in a fine, waxy coating, giving it a distinctive appearance. Its small, yellowish-green flowers appear in late spring to early summer, forming compact clusters.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in rocky, well-drained soils.
