Apocynum venetum subsp. venetum

Apocynum venetum subsp.

venetum, commonly known as Chinese apocynum, is a flowering plant native to East Asia. It is a perennial shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters.

The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a rough texture and serrated edges. Its flowers are clustered in umbels and are typically white or pale pink in color.

The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small, elliptical seeds.
