Acaena glaberrima

Acaena glaberrima, commonly known as the silky burr, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the family Rosaceae and is characterized by its woody stems and persistent, spiny bracts. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are followed by spherical, hairy fruits. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and have a serrated margin.
