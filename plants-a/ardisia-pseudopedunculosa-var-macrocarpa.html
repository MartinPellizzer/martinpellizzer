Ardisia pseudopedunculosa var. macrocarpa

Ardisia pseudopedunculosa var. macrocarpa, commonly known as the large-fruited ardisia, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are elliptical in shape and arranged alternately along the stems. The plant produces small, white to pale pink flowers followed by dark red, berry-like fruits that are edible. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forests.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in tropical and subtropical regions.
