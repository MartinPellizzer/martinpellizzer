Arundinella hirta var. depauperata

Arundinella hirta var. depauperata, commonly known as the slender bamboo grass, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories and wetlands. The plant features slender, erect stems that are often branched and covered with fine hairs. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a soft texture and a mild aromatic scent.

It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually clustered in spikes, contributing to its ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
