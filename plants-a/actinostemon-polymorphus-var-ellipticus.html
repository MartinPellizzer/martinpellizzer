Actinostemon polymorphus var. ellipticus

Actinostemon polymorphus var. ellipticus, commonly known as the narrow-leaf actinostemon, is a species of flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, often found in arid or semi-arid regions. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters. Its botanical characteristics make it well adapted to drought conditions, with a fibrous root system that helps it survive in poor soil environments.

It is sometimes cultivated for its ornamental value due to its distinctive foliage and delicate blooms.
