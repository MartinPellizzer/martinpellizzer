Anthemis montana subsp. linnaeana

Anthemis montana subsp.

linnaeana, commonly known as the Linnaean anthemis, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Europe. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense foliage.

The plant produces yellow flowers with ray florets, typically blooming in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in alpine and rocky habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
