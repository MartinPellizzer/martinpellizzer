Arenaria aggregata subsp. fontiqueri

Arenaria aggregata subsp.

fontiqueri, commonly known as the Fontiquera sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region. It typically thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.

The plant features small, clustered flowers that are usually white or pale pink, blooming in early spring. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often covered in fine hairs to reduce water loss.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in stabilizing sandy soils.
