Aconitum nagarum f. dielsianum

Aconitum nagarum f. dielsianum, commonly known as the Dielsian monkshood, is a variant of the monkshood plant native to the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its striking blue-violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant features palmate leaves with deeply lobed segments, typical of the Ranunculaceae family. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in alpine meadows.

Due to its toxic alkaloids, it is important to handle the plant with care.
