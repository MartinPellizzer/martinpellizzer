Ardisia solanacea var. elata

Ardisia solanacea var. elata, commonly known as the tall ardisia, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and produces small, red berries that are edible for humans and birds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and gardens. It has a compact, upright growth habit with multiple stems and can reach heights of up to 3 meters.

The botanical features of this variety include its dense foliage and attractive, ornamental berries that contribute to its horticultural value.
