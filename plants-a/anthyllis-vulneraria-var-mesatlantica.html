Anthyllis vulneraria var. mesatlantica

Anthyllis vulneraria var. mesatlantica, commonly known as the Atlantic restharrow, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the coastal regions of the Atlantic.

It features slender, erect stems that bear small, yellowish-white flowers arranged in dense, spike-like clusters. The plant has bipinnate leaves that are finely divided, contributing to its delicate appearance. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is adapted to maritime climates with high salt exposure.

This variety is often found in dunes and coastal grasslands, playing a role in stabilizing sandy environments.
