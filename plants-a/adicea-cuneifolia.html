Adicea cuneifolia

Adicea cuneifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf adicea, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its slender, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or forested areas.
