Arnica unalaschcensis var. unalaschcensis

Arnica unalaschcensis var. unalaschcensis, commonly known as the Unalaska arnica, is a flowering plant native to the Aleutian Islands of Alaska.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in open, rocky habitats. The plant features hairy, basal leaves and produces yellow-orange flower heads with prominent central disks. It blooms in late summer and is adapted to the cool, maritime climate of its native range.

This variant is part of the genus Arnica, which is known for its medicinal properties in other species.
