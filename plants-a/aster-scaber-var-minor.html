Aster scaber var. minor

Aster scaber var. minor, commonly known as the rough-stemmed aster, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America.

It typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands. The plant features rough, hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves with a serrated edge. Its flowers are blue to purple and bloom in late summer to early fall.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, providing nectar for pollinators.
