Arenaria vitoriana

Arenaria vitoriana, commonly known as the Violet Sandwort, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically reaches heights of about 10 to 20 centimeters. The plant features small, clustered flowers that are usually pink or purple in color, blooming in early spring. Its leaves are succulent and fleshy, adapted to retain moisture in arid environments.

The species is often found in sandy or gravelly soils, thriving in well-drained conditions.
