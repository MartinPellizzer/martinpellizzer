Aeranthes filipes

Aeranthes filipes, commonly known as the slender-stemmed orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are typically white or pale green, often arranged in a spike. Aeranthes filipes thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
