Andropogon nardus subsp. grandis

Andropogon nardus subsp.

grandis, commonly known as tall bluestem, is a perennial grass native to North America. It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and narrow, bluish-green leaves.

This species thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in prairies and open woodlands. It plays an important role in ecological systems by providing habitat and forage for wildlife.

The plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is used in landscaping and restoration projects.
