Astragalus Exscapus Subsp. Pubiflorus

Astragalus exscapus subsp. pubiflorus is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to specific regions of western North America. It belongs to the legume family and is known for its distinctive appearance and ecological importance. This subspecies is characterized by its hairy flowers and slender stems, which help it thrive in its often harsh environment. Due to habitat loss and limited distribution, it is considered a vulnerable species. Conservation efforts are essential to protect this unique and ecologically valuable plant.
