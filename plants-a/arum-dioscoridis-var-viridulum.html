Arum dioscoridis var. viridulum

Arum dioscoridis var. viridulum, commonly known as the green arrowhead, is a herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia.

It features arrow-shaped leaves that are typically a vibrant green, with a slightly glossy texture. The plant produces striking, spadix-like inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, often in shades of white or yellow. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found near water bodies.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some cultures.
