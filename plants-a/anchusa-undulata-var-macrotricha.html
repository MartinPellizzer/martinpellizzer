Anchusa undulata var. macrotricha

Anchusa undulata var. macrotricha, commonly known as the large-fruited betony, is a perennial herb native to southern Europe.

It belongs to the Boraginaceae family and is characterized by its hairy, branched stems and blue to purple flowers. The plant produces distinctive, elongated, hairy fruits that are often mistaken for seeds. It thrives in sunny, well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or dry habitats.
