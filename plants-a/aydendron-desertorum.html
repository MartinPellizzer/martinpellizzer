Aydendron desertorum

Aydendron desertorum, commonly known as the desert flame vine, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of North America.

It is characterized by its vibrant orange to red flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has adapted to harsh desert conditions with deep roots and thick, waxy leaves to reduce water loss. Its flowers attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies, playing a role in local ecosystems.

Aydendron desertorum is often used in xeriscaping due to its low water requirements and ornamental value.
