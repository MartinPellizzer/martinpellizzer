Anathallis spiculifera

Anathallis spiculifera, commonly known as the spicate anathallis, is a species of flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its unique floral structure. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in tropical rainforests.

Anathallis spiculifera is known for its intricate pollination mechanisms, relying on specific insect species for reproduction.
