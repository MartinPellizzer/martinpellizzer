Anthurium igarapeum

Anthurium igarapeum, commonly known as the Igarapeum anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the Amazon rainforest.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves and vibrant spadix. The plant produces an inflorescence consisting of a spadix surrounded by a spathe, which is often brightly colored. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Anthurium igarapeum is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated as a houseplant.
