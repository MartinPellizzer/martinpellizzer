Aulomyrcia thyrsiflora var. petiolaris

Aulomyrcia thyrsiflora var. petiolaris, commonly known as the petioled aulomyrcia, is a rare shrub native to specific regions of South America.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and small, elliptical leaves with prominent petioles. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for horticultural study due to its unique morphology and limited distribution.
