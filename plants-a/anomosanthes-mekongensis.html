Anomosanthes mekongensis

Anomosanthes mekongensis, commonly known as the Mekong ghost orchid, is a rare and enigmatic orchid species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its elongated, ghost-like appearance and striking, pale pink to white flowers. This plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in the understory of dense forests. Its unique morphology and limited distribution make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists.

Due to habitat destruction and limited natural propagation, it is considered endangered and requires careful conservation efforts.
