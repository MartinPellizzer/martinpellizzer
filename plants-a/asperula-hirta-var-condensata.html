Asperula hirta var. condensata

Asperula hirta var. condensata, commonly known as the dense evergreen azalea, is a compact shrub native to Japan.

It features dense clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of pink and white, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has dark green, leathery leaves that provide a lush backdrop for its vibrant blooms. It prefers partial shade and well-drained, acidic soil, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in moist, shaded environments.
