Anthericum ugogoense

Anthericum ugogoense, commonly known as the Ugogo arrowhead, is a herbaceous plant native to East Africa.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive inflorescence consisting of a spadix surrounded by a spathe. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and swampy areas. Its leaves are arrow-shaped and often have a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

Anthericum ugogoense is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its aesthetic appeal.
