Acisanthera punctatissima var. gracillima

Acisanthera punctatissima var. gracillima, commonly known as the slender acisanthera, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features slender, branching stems that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the cooler months. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly serrated edge.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
