Aster regalis

Aster regalis, commonly known as the royal aster, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its showy composite flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and woodland edges. Its flowers bloom in late summer to early autumn, displaying a range of colors from white to purple.

Aster regalis is valued for its ornamental beauty and historical use in traditional medicine.
