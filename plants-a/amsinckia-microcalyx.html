Amsinckia microcalyx

Amsinckia microcalyx, commonly known as the narrowleaf ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features slender, hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine pubescence. Its small, yellow flowers appear in dense clusters, and it produces small, bead-like seeds.
