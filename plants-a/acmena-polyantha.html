Acmena polyantha

Acmena polyantha, commonly known as the Chinese hibiscus, is a deciduous shrub native to southern China and Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and red, typically blooming in summer. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are ovate to elliptical in shape, with a glossy upper surface and a lighter underside. Its fruit is a follicle that splits open to release small, winged seeds.

Acmena polyantha is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
