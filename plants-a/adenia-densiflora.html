Adenia densiflora

Adenia densiflora, commonly known as the dense-flowered adenia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant features opposite, elliptic leaves with a smooth upper surface and a hairy underside. Its showy, clustered flowers are usually pink or white, and they appear in spring.

Adenia densiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and blooms.
