Antrophyum formosanum

Antrophyum formosanum, commonly known as the Taiwan cycad, is a species of cycad native to Taiwan.

It is characterized by its tall, unbranched stem and large, pinnately compound leaves. The plant produces cones, with male and female cones occurring on separate plants. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates with well-drained soil.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered endangered and requires conservation efforts to ensure its survival.
