Auriculardisia brenesii

Auriculardisia brenesii, commonly known as the Guanacaste orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically bloom in the spring. The plant features a pseudobulb structure, which stores water and nutrients, aiding in its survival in dry environments. Its leaves are broad and strap-like, often arranged in a rosette pattern.

This orchid thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.
