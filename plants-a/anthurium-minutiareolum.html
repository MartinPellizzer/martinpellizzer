Anthurium minutiareolum

Anthurium minutiareolum, commonly known as the miniature anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a glossy texture. The plant produces small, spadix-like inflorescences surrounded by a vibrant spathe, often in shades of red or orange. It thrives in humid, warm environments with indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.
