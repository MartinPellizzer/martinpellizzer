Allocarya stricta

Allocarya stricta, commonly known as the narrowleaf tansy, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America.

It typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its small yellow flowers are clustered in dense, flat-topped inflorescences.

Allocarya stricta is known for its aromatic foliage and is often used in traditional herbal medicine.
