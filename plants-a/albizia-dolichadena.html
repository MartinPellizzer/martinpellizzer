Albizia dolichadena

Albizia dolichadena, commonly known as the longpod albizia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and pod-like fruits. The plant has a shallow root system and is often found in disturbed soils, making it adaptable to various environmental conditions. Its flowers are typically pale yellow or white, and it produces elongated pods that contain several seeds.

Albizia dolichadena is sometimes used in traditional medicine and for soil stabilization in degraded areas.
