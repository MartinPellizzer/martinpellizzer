Asplenium praemorsum var. laceratum

Asplenium praemorsum var. laceratum, commonly known as the shredded maidenhair fern, is a variant of the maidenhair fern family (Aspleniaceae).

It is characterized by its delicate, finely divided, and often frayed fronds that give it a distinctive, almost lace-like appearance. Native to tropical regions, this plant thrives in humid, shaded environments with high air moisture. Its leaves are typically dark green and have a leathery texture, which helps it retain moisture in its natural habitat.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value in indoor and outdoor gardens.
