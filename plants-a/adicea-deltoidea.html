Adicea deltoidea

Adicea deltoidea, commonly known as the delta leaf daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its deltoid-shaped leaves and showy yellow flowers with white ray florets. The plant typically grows in well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.

Adicea deltoidea is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
