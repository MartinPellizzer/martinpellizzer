Anacamptis × paparistoi

Anacamptis × paparistoi, commonly known as the hybrid green orchid, is a natural hybrid between Anacamptis morio and Anacamptis pyramidalis.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its tall, erect stems and clusters of greenish-yellow flowers. The plant typically blooms in late summer to early autumn, producing showy, lip-like petals that attract pollinators. It thrives in sunny, well-drained habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
