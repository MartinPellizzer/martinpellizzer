Arundarbor lumampao

Arundarbor lumampao, commonly known as the Moonlight Fern, is a rare tropical plant native to the dense rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, silver-green fronds that shimmer in the dappled light of the forest canopy. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires consistent moisture in its soil. Its roots are fibrous and help stabilize the soil in its natural habitat.

The plant is noted for its unique ability to absorb and reflect light, giving it a mystical appearance under moonlight.
