Arachnothryx glabrata

Arachnothryx glabrata, commonly known as the smoothleaf arachnothryx, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are usually smooth and elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by pod-like fruits containing several seeds.

It is often found in lowland rainforests and is valued for its hard, durable wood.
