Aloe pronkii

Aloe pronkii, commonly known as the Natal aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, erect stems covered in sharp, spiny leaves that are typically green with red or purple margins. The plant produces clusters of yellow flowers during the summer months, adding ornamental value. Aloe pronkii is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick, fleshy leaves.

It is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its striking appearance and resilience.
