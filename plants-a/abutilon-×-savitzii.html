Abutilon × savitzii

Abutilon × savitzii, commonly known as the Chinese lantern plant, is a hybrid species within the Malvaceae family.

It is characterized by its distinctive, papery, lantern-shaped bracts that range in color from red to orange. The plant is native to China and has been widely cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Abutilon × savitzii is often used in gardens and as a houseplant due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
