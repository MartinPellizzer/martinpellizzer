Acaena masafuerana

Acaena masafuerana, commonly known as the Chilean burr bush, is a shrub native to southern Chile and Argentina.

It belongs to the family Rosaceae and is characterized by its dense, thorny growth habit. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in spring, followed by distinctive spiny fruits. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a hairy texture.
