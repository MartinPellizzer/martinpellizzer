Anthurium balaoanum

Anthurium balaoanum, commonly known as the Balaoan anthurium, is a rare and endemic plant species native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive inflorescence consisting of a spadix and spathe. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often found in lowland rainforests. Its leaves are large, heart-shaped, and have a waxy texture, while its flowers are usually bright red or orange.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native range.
