Anemone chinensis

Anemone chinensis, commonly known as the Chinese anemone, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to East Asia.

It features a short, stout rhizome and produces clusters of showy, white or pinkish flowers with five to eight petals. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are palmately divided, with a rough, hairy texture.

Anemone chinensis is valued in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported medicinal properties.
