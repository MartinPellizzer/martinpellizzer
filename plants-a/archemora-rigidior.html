Archemora rigidior

Archemora rigidior, commonly known as the rigid orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the temperate regions of the northern hemisphere.

The plant produces striking, showy flowers that are typically white or pale pink, with a distinctive lip petal. Archemora rigidior thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade in woodland environments.
