Aloe doei var. lavranosii

Aloe doei var. lavranosii, commonly known as the Lavranos aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, upright stems that can grow up to 2 meters in height, adorned with narrow, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a reddish tinge. The plant produces striking orange-red flowers in raceme-like clusters during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, requiring minimal water once established.

Due to its ornamental value and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for xeriscaping and rock gardens.
