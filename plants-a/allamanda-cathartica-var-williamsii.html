Allamanda cathartica var. williamsii

Allamanda cathartica var. williamsii, commonly known as Williams' allamanda, is a tropical shrub native to South America.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, often with a contrasting red or purple throat. The plant has dark green, glossy leaves that are elliptical in shape and arranged oppositely on the stems. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

The flowers are not only visually striking but also attract pollinators such as bees and birds.
