Anthurium recusatum

Anthurium recusatum, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are usually dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces vibrant red spathes that enclose its small, yellowish spadices, which are the actual flowers. Anthurium recusatum thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.

It is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
