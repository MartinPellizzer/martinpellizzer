Aerangis flexuosa

Aerangis flexuosa, commonly known as the spiral orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique spiral arrangement of leaves, which gives it its common name. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in some regions for horticultural purposes.
