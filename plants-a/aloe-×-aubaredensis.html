Aloe × aubaredensis

Aloe × aubaredensis, commonly known as the hybrid aloe or the aloe vera hybrid, is a naturally occurring hybrid between *Aloe vera* and *Aloe barbadensis*.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a yellowish or reddish tinge. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with small, white or pale yellow flowers. It is native to the island of Socotra, located in the Arabian Sea, and is well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.

Aloe × aubaredensis is valued for its potential medicinal properties and is often cultivated for its gel-like substance used in skincare and health products.
