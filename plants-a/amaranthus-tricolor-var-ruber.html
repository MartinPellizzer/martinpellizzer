Amaranthus tricolor var. ruber

Amaranthus tricolor var. ruber, commonly known as red leaf amaranth, is a strikingly colorful plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its vibrant red, green, and orange foliage, which makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening. The plant produces small, greenish flowers that appear in dense clusters, adding to its visual appeal. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it relatively easy to cultivate.

This variety is also valued for its nutritional content, as its leaves are edible and rich in vitamins and minerals.
