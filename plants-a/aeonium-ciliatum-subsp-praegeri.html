Aeonium ciliatum subsp. praegeri

Aeonium ciliatum subsp.

praegeri, commonly known as the Praeger's Aeonium, is a succulent plant native to the Canary Islands. It features rosettes of thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with red or purple margins.

The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with small, star-shaped yellow flowers. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its leaves and stems.

This subspecies is popular among succulent enthusiasts for its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
