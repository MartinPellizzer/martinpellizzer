Astilboides tabularis

Astilboides tabularis, commonly known as the broadleaf astilbe, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to eastern Asia.

It features upright, slender stems that bear large, palmately compound leaves with a distinctive silvery-white underside. The plant produces showy, cone-shaped flower clusters in shades of pink, red, or white, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland settings.

Astilboides tabularis is valued in gardens for its ornamental foliage and delicate floral display.
