Agave wallisii

Agave wallisii, commonly known as the Mexican stonecress, is a succulent plant native to Mexico.

It features a rosette of thick, blue-gray leaves that are covered in a dense layer of fine white hairs. The plant produces a tall, branched inflorescence with small, greenish-white flowers. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it suitable for arid environments.

Agave wallisii is often used in xeriscaping due to its low maintenance and ornamental appeal.
