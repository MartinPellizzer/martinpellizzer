Abutilon cavaleriei

Abutilon cavaleriei, commonly known as the Chinese velvetleaf, is a perennial shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its soft, fuzzy leaves that give it a velvety texture. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, typically during the spring and summer seasons. It thrives in warm climates and prefers well-drained soil.

Abutilon cavaleriei is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable growing conditions.
