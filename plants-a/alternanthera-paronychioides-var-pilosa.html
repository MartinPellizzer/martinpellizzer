Alternanthera paronychioides var. pilosa

Alternanthera paronychioides var. pilosa, commonly known as the woolly alternate red, is a striking aquatic plant characterized by its vibrant red or pink foliage.

It features dense, velvety hairs on its leaves, which give it a soft, textured appearance. The plant typically grows in shallow, slow-moving waters and thrives in full to partial sunlight. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often display a gradient of colors from red to green.

It is popular in aquascaping for its aesthetic appeal and ease of maintenance.
