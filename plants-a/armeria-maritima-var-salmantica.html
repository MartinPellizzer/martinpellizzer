Armeria maritima var. salmantica

Armeria maritima var. salmantica, commonly known as Salmantica thrift, is a compact, low-growing evergreen perennial native to coastal regions of southern Europe.

It features dense clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has succulent, fleshy leaves that are adapted to tolerate salty air and sandy soils. It thrives in full sun and is often used in rock gardens or coastal landscapes.

Its hardy nature makes it a popular choice for seaside gardens and coastal restoration projects.
