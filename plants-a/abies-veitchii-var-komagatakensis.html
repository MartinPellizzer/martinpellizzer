Abies veitchii var. komagatakensis

Abies veitchii var. komagatakensis, commonly known as the Komagatake fir, is a rare variant of the Veitch fir native to Japan.

It is characterized by its slender, conical shape and dark green, needle-like foliage. The plant exhibits a slow growth rate and thrives in cool, moist environments with well-drained soil. Its cones are typically small and reddish-brown, containing winged seeds.

This variant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and specialized horticultural settings.
