Anthurium longifolium

Anthurium longifolium, commonly known as the long-leaved anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to several feet in length. The plant produces vibrant red spathes that enclose its small, spadix-like inflorescence. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium longifolium is often cultivated for its ornamental value in both indoor and outdoor gardens.
