Achyranthes leiantha

Achyranthes leiantha, commonly known as the slender cowpea, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its erect, branched stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces small, round fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed areas or along roadsides.

Its botanical features make it an important species for ecological studies and potential agricultural use.
