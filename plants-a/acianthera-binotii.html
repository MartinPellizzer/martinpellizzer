Acianthera binotii

Acianthera binotii, commonly known as the two-spotted orchid, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, aiding its survival in its natural habitat. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, contributing to its overall appearance.

Acianthera binotii is often found in humid, shaded environments such as forests and rocky areas.
