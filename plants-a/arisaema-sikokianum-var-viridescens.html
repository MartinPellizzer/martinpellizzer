Arisaema sikokianum var. viridescens

Arisaema sikokianum var. viridescens, commonly known as the greenish arrowhead plant, is a member of the Araceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of southern Japan, particularly the island of Shikoku. The plant features a distinctive arrowhead-shaped leaf that is typically a vibrant green, with a slightly waxy texture. Its inflorescence consists of a spadix surrounded by a spathe, which is usually a pale green or white color.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in shaded gardens or as a houseplant.
