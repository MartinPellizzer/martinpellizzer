Anthurium leptostachyum

Anthurium leptostachyum, commonly known as the narrow-stalked anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped leaves that are often a deep green with a waxy texture. The plant produces striking spadices, which are modified leaves that enclose the true flowers, typically in shades of red or orange. Anthurium leptostachyum thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical landscapes.
