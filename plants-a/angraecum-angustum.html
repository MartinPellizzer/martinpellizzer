Angraecum angustum

Angraecum angustum, commonly known as the narrow-leafed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and long, narrow leaves. The plant produces striking white or pale pink flowers with long, slender petals and a prominent lip. It is adapted to grow in rocky or limestone habitats and requires well-drained soil.
