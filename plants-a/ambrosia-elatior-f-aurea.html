Ambrosia elatior f. aurea

Ambrosia elatior f. aurea, commonly known as the golden ambrosia, is a variant of the common ambrosia plant.

It is a herbaceous perennial that features finely divided, feather-like leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for gardens in temperate regions.

Due to its allergenic properties, it is often considered a weed in agricultural settings.
