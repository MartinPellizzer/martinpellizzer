Albizia mainaea

Albizia mainaea, commonly known as the yellow albizia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compound leaves, which are typically pinnately arranged and have a soft, feathery appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually yellow or white, arranged in racemose inflorescences. Albizia mainaea is often found in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
