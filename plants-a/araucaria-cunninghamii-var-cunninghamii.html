Araucaria cunninghamii var. cunninghamii

Araucaria cunninghamii var. cunninghamii, commonly known as the Huon pine, is a coniferous evergreen tree native to Tasmania.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and dense, pyramidal crown. The tree has needle-like leaves that are dark green and arranged in spirals around the branches. Its cones are small and woody, containing seeds that are dispersed by wind.

This species is notable for its longevity and is considered a relic of ancient forests.
