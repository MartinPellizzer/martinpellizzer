Argyrolobium aberdaricum

Argyrolobium aberdaricum, commonly known as the silver globular pea, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive silver-gray foliage and spherical, clustered inflorescences. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
