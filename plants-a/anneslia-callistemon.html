Anneslia callistemon

Anneslia callistemon, commonly known as the pink bottlebrush, is a striking evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant pink to red cylindrical flower spikes, which resemble bottle brushes, and are highly attractive to birds and insects. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving them a soft, velvety texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for coastal gardens.

Anneslia callistemon is also valued for its ability to tolerate salt spray, making it suitable for seaside planting.
