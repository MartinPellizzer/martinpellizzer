Astragalus chinensis

Astragalus chinensis, commonly known as Chinese milkvetch, is a perennial legume native to China.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant produces pods that contain several small, kidney-shaped seeds. It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often found in arid regions.

Astragalus chinensis is valued for its medicinal properties in traditional Chinese medicine.
