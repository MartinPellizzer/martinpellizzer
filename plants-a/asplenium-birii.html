Asplenium birii

Asplenium birii, commonly known as the silver maidenhair fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Aspleniaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, finely divided, dark green fronds with a silvery underside. Native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia, it thrives in shaded, moist environments. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit, with creeping stems that produce new fronds from the base.

Its unique appearance and ornamental value make it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardening.
