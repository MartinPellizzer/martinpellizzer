Anacamptis × subpapilionacea

Anacamptis × subpapilionacea, commonly known as the hybrid butterfly orchid, is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Anacamptis morio and Anacamptis palustris.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a tall, erect stem that bears a single raceme of showy, butterfly-shaped flowers. The flowers are typically pink to purple in color, with a distinctive lip that is often darker and more contrasting. This plant thrives in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in meadows and woodland edges.

It is valued for its ornamental beauty and is a popular choice for garden cultivation.
