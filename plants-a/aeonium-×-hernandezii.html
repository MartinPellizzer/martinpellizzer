Aeonium × hernandezii

Aeonium × hernandezii, commonly known as the Rainbow Aeonium, is a hybrid succulent plant native to the Canary Islands.

It features rosettes of fleshy, thick leaves that range in color from green to red, pink, and purple, creating a vibrant, colorful display. The plant produces tall, branching inflorescences with yellow flowers in the spring. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with ample sunlight.

Aeonium × hernandezii is popular among succulent enthusiasts for its striking appearance and ease of care.
