Abelia hersii

Abelia hersii, commonly known as the dwarf abelia or mountain abelia, is a compact evergreen shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It features small, fragrant white to pinkish flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has glossy, dark green foliage that remains attractive throughout the growing season. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained, acidic soil.

Abelia hersii is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and adaptability to various landscape conditions.
