Actinidia megalocarpa

Actinidia megalocarpa, commonly known as the giant Chinese gooseberry, is a deciduous vine native to China.

It is characterized by its vigorous growth and can reach lengths of up to 30 meters. The plant produces heart-shaped leaves that are typically 5 to 10 centimeters in size. Its flowers are small and greenish-yellow, appearing in clusters, while the fruit is a large, fuzzy, yellowish berry.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated for its edible fruit, though it is less commonly consumed than its smaller relative, Actinidia deliciosa.
