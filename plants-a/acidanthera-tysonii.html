Acidanthera tysonii

Acidanthera tysonii, commonly known as the Tyson's orchid, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its inflorescence consists of a single, erect spike bearing several showy, violet or purple flowers.
