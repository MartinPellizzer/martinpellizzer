Acaena sanguisorbae subvar. majoriceps

Acaena sanguisorbae subvar. majoriceps, commonly known as the purple tufted grass.

This plant is characterized by its tufted growth habit and distinctive purple foliage. It produces small, spherical fruits that are often used in traditional medicine. The leaves are typically alternate and have a serrated edge.

It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in woodland or grassland habitats.
