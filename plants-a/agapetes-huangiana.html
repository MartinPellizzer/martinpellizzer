Agapetes huangiana

Agapetes huangiana, commonly known as the Huang's silverleaf, is a species of flowering plant in the Ericaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of China, particularly in Yunnan Province. This evergreen shrub typically grows at high elevations, often in moist, shaded environments. Its leaves are dark green and leathery, with a silvery underside, giving it its common name.

The plant produces small, bell-shaped white or pink flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer.
