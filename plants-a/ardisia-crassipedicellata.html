Ardisia crassipedicellata

Ardisia crassipedicellata, commonly known as the glossy privet, is a evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, dark green, elliptical leaves that are often arranged in opposite pairs. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers followed by bright red, fleshy berries that are attractive to birds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and landscapes around the world.
