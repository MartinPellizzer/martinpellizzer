Amaryllis belladonna var. barbata

Amaryllis belladonna var. barbata, commonly known as the Barbata Amaryllis, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It features large, showy flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, often with a distinctive orange or yellow throat. The plant produces strap-like leaves that are broad and waxy, typical of the Amaryllis genus. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is popular in horticulture for its vibrant blooms and relatively easy care requirements.
