Amomum pubescens

Amomum pubescens, commonly known as the Chinese ginger or wild ginger, is a perennial herb native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes other aromatic plants like ginger and turmeric. The plant features a rhizomatous root system with thick, fleshy stems and lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are small and clustered in inflorescences, typically with a yellow or greenish hue.

The plant is valued for its aromatic rhizomes, which are used in traditional medicine and as a spice.
