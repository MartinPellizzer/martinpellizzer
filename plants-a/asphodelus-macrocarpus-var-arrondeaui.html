Asphodelus macrocarpus var. arrondeaui

Asphodelus macrocarpus var. arrondeaui, commonly known as the Arrondeau's asphodel, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features strap-like, grass-like leaves that are typically bluish-green in color. The plant produces tall, slender flowering stems that bear clusters of white or pale yellow flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
