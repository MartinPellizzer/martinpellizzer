Aristolochia tonduzii

Aristolochia tonduzii, commonly known as the Tonduz's Dutchman's pipe, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa.

It is characterized by its unique, pipe-shaped flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color. The plant belongs to the Aristolochiaceae family and is known for its complex, often twisted floral structure. Its leaves are heart-shaped and have a rough texture, while the stems are usually hairy.

This species is often found in moist, shaded habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
