Arachnothryx galeottii

Arachnothryx galeottii, commonly known as the Brazilian white wood, is a tropical tree native to South America.

It belongs to the Leguminosae family and is characterized by its smooth, gray bark and compound leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by pod-like fruits containing several seeds. It is often found in humid lowland forests and plays a role in local ecosystems.
