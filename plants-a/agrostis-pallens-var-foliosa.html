Agrostis pallens var. foliosa

Agrostis pallens var. foliosa, commonly known as the silky bluegrass, is a cool-season grass native to North America.

It is characterized by its fine, bluish-green foliage and tufted inflorescences. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows and along stream banks. It has narrow, grass-like leaves with a distinctive silvery-blue sheen.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in native plant gardens.
