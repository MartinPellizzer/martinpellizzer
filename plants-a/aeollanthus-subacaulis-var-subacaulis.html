Aeollanthus subacaulis var. subacaulis

Aeollanthus subacaulis var. subacaulis, commonly known as the slender aeollanthus, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant has a low-growing habit with narrow, grass-like leaves. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments.
