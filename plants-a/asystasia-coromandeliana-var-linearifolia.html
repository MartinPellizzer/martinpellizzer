Asystasia coromandeliana var. linearifolia

Asystasia coromandeliana var. linearifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf asystasia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its slender, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically purple or violet in color. It is often cultivated as a ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and compact growth habit.

This variant is distinguished by its narrower leaves compared to the typical form of Asystasia coromandeliana.
