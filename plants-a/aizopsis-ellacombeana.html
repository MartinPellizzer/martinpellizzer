Aizopsis ellacombeana

Aizopsis ellacombeana, commonly known as the African umbrella plant, is a striking evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, upright stems and large, umbrella-like clusters of narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in summer, adding a delicate touch to its appearance. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is well-suited to warm, humid climates.

Aizopsis ellacombeana prefers partial shade to full sun and well-drained soil, making it a versatile choice for garden settings.
