Amaryllis platensis

Amaryllis platensis, commonly known as the giant amaryllis, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can reach up to 12 inches in diameter, typically in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant features broad, strap-like leaves that are often bluish-green in color. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Amaryllis platensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
