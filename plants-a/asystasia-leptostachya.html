Asystasia leptostachya

Asystasia leptostachya, commonly known as the slender-stemmed asystasia, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its upright, branching stems and small, lance-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are usually pale purple to white in color, blooming in the summer months. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Asystasia leptostachya is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate foliage and flowering habit.
