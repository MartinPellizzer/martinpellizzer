Artemisia Frigida Var. Argyrophylla

Argyrophylla artemisia, scientifically known as Artemisia frigida var. argyrophylla, is a hardy perennial shrub native to arid and semi-arid regions of North America. This plant is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which helps it retain moisture in harsh environments. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy landscapes. Due to its resilience, it is commonly used in xeriscaping and erosion control. Its distinctive appearance and low maintenance make it a valuable addition to native plant gardens.
