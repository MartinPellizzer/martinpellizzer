Actephila africana

Actephila africana, commonly known as the African arrowhead, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features a rhizomatous root system and produces clusters of small, white to pinkish flowers. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along riverbanks.

Actephila africana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional medicine.
