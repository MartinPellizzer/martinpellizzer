Aristolochia gehrtii

Aristolochia gehrtii, commonly known as the Mexican birthwort, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and parts of Central America.

It is characterized by its unique, heart-shaped leaves and its distinctive, pipe-shaped flowers that are often greenish or brownish in color. The plant belongs to the Aristolochiaceae family and is known for its complex chemical composition, including various alkaloids. It is often found in moist, shaded areas such as forests and along stream banks.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and traditional herbal practices.
