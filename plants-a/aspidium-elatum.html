Aspidium elatum

Aspidium elatum, commonly known as the broad fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its large, robust fronds that can reach lengths of up to 1 meter. The fronds are typically bipinnate, with a distinctive, almost fan-like appearance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in temperate regions.

The plant is known for its ability to spread through rhizomes, forming dense colonies in suitable habitats.
