Asterostigma lividum var. typicum

Asterostigma lividum var. typicum, commonly known as the purple star orchid, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking violet to purple petals that resemble small stars, giving it its common name. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in shaded, moist environments. Its leaves are lance-shaped and dark green, while the flowers bloom in clusters.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
