Aspilia prieureana

Aspilia prieureana, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies, sunflowers, and other composite flowers. The plant features showy, yellow ray florets surrounding a central disk of tubular flowers, creating a distinctive daisy-like appearance. It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky outcrops.

Aspilia prieureana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
