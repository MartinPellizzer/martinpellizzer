Athanasia hirsuta

Athanasia hirsuta, commonly known as the hairy immortality, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its hairy, glandular stems and leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in late summer. It is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments.

Athanasia hirsuta is known for its resilience and ability to survive in harsh conditions.
