Angraecum biteaui

Angraecum biteaui, commonly known as the Madagascar star orchid, is a rare and striking epiphytic orchid native to Madagascar.

It features elongated, pendant inflorescences with star-shaped flowers that are predominantly white with contrasting purple and green markings. The plant is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves and is adapted to thrive in humid, shaded forest environments. It blooms during the dry season, producing fragrant flowers that attract specific nocturnal pollinators.

Due to its rarity and unique appearance, it is highly valued in horticultural circles.
