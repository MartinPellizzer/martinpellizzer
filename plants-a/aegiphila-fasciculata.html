Aegiphila fasciculata

Aegiphila fasciculata, commonly known as the clustered aegiphila, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Brazil.

Aegiphila fasciculata is often found in dry, open habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential medicinal properties.
