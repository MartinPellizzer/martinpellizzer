Acer Campestre Subsp. Marsicum

Acer campestre subsp. marsicum is a rare and distinctive variant of the common hedge maple. It is native to specific regions in central and eastern europe. This subspecies is known for its unique leaf shape and coloration. It thrives in woodland and rocky habitats. Its conservation status highlights the need for environmental protection.
