Austronea densiflora

Austronea densiflora, commonly known as the dense-flowered austronea, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its leaves are usually opposite, ovate, and have a glossy surface.

Austronea densiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens.
