Alsophila srilankensis

Alsophila srilankensis, commonly known as the Srilankan tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to Sri Lanka.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnately compound fronds. The plant has a slow growth rate and can reach heights of up to 10 meters in optimal conditions. Its fronds are often used in traditional medicine and horticulture.

The species is valued for its ornamental appearance and ecological significance in its native habitat.
