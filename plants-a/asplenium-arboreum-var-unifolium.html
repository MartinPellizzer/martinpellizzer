Asplenium arboreum var. unifolium

Asplenium arboreum var. unifolium, commonly known as the single-leaved wood spleenwort, is a fern native to the tropical regions of the Americas.

It is a member of the Aspleniaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect growth habit. The plant typically forms dense clusters on rock surfaces or in shaded, moist environments. Its leaves are simple, with a lanceolate shape and a smooth, glossy surface.

It is often found in humid, tropical climates and is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.
