Avenastrum elatius

Avenastrum elatius, commonly known as the tall oatgrass, is a perennial grass species native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands, heaths, and rocky soils. The plant features slender, erect stems that can reach heights of up to 1 meter, with narrow, grass-like leaves. Its flowering spikes are usually pale green to brown, and it produces small, wind-pollinated flowers.

Avenastrum elatius is valued for its ecological role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various insects and small animals.
