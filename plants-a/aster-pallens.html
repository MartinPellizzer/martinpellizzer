Aster pallens

Aster pallens, commonly known as the pale aster, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in open habitats such as meadows and woodlands, thriving in well-drained soils. Its flowers are usually pink to purple in color, with white ray florets, and bloom in the late summer to early fall.

Aster pallens is also known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
