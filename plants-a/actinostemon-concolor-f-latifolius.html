Actinostemon concolor f. latifolius

Actinostemon concolor f. latifolius, commonly known as the broad-leaved actinostemon, is a horticultural form of a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is characterized by its large, ovate leaves that are typically a deep green with a smooth texture. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in subtropical and Mediterranean climates.
