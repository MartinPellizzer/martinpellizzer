Agave boldinghiana

Agave boldinghiana, commonly known as the Boldingh's agave, is a succulent plant native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped growth pattern and thick, fleshy leaves that are often blue-green in color. The plant produces a tall flowering stalk that bears white or pale purple flowers. It is adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Its striking appearance makes it a popular choice for xeriscaping and rock gardens.
