Aloe peacockii

Aloe peacockii, commonly known as the peacock aloe, is a striking succulent native to South Africa.

It features tall, branching stems that bear clusters of vibrant, peacock-blue flowers. The plant has thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with red or purple margins. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright sunlight to flourish.

Aloe peacockii is prized for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
