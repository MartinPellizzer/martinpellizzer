Aquilegia buergeriana f. ecalcarata

Aquilegia buergeriana f. ecalcarata, commonly known as the Buerger's columbine, is a flowering plant belonging to the Ranunculaceae family.

It is characterized by its showy, spurred flowers that typically range in color from blue to purple. The plant features compound leaves with palmate lobes, and its stems are usually hairy. Native to regions of East Asia, it thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial shade.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
