Anthurium rociorojasiae

Anthurium rociorojasiae, commonly known as the Red Vein Anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped leaves with striking red veins. The plant produces spadices, which are the inflorescences that consist of a spadix surrounded by a modified leaf called a spathe. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil.

Anthurium rociorojasiae is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in greenhouses.
