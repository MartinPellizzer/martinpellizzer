Anthurium prominens

Anthurium prominens, commonly known as the flame anthurium, is a striking tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped spathe that ranges from red to orange in color. The plant features a tall, leafy stem with waxy, dark green leaves that are elongated and leathery in texture. Its inflorescence consists of a spadix surrounded by the colorful spathe, which is the showy part of the flower.

Anthurium prominens thrives in humid, warm environments and is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant.
