Ardisia cambodiana

Ardisia cambodiana, commonly known as the Cambodian beautyberry, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Ericaceae and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, white flowers that are followed by bright red, berry-like fruits. These fruits are edible and often used in traditional medicine.

Ardisia cambodiana is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in shaded environments.
