Aeschynanthus moningerae

Aeschynanthus moningerae, commonly known as the morning glory orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Gesneriaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly Kenya and Tanzania. The plant is characterized by its vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers that open in the morning and close in the evening. It typically grows as an epiphyte or terrestrial plant, often found in shaded, humid environments.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and horticultural collections.
