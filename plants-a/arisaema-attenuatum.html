Arisaema attenuatum

Arisaema attenuatum, commonly known as the Chinese arrowroot or snake-root, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its unique inflorescence consisting of a spadix surrounded by a spathe. The plant features a rhizomatous root system and produces striking, ornamental foliage with heart-shaped leaves. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its decorative value.

However, it contains toxic compounds and should be handled with care.
