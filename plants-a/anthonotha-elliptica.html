Anthonotha elliptica

Anthonotha elliptica, commonly known as the elliptic anther orchid, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, evergreen growth habit. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are often clustered in inflorescences. Its leaves are elliptical in shape, hence the species name, and are arranged in a rosette pattern.

Anthonotha elliptica is typically found in shaded, humid environments such as forest understories.
