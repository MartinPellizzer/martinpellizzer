Ageratina purpusii

Ageratina purpusii, commonly known as the white mistflower, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and parts of Central America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Ageratina purpusii is often found in moist, shaded environments and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
