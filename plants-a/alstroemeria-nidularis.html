Alstroemeria nidularis

Alstroemeria nidularis, commonly known as the clustered alstroemeria, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Chile and Argentina.

It belongs to the family Alstroemeriaceae and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that come in a variety of colors including red, orange, and yellow. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and has a compact, bushy habit with narrow, strap-like leaves.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening due to its vibrant blooms and adaptability to different soil conditions.
