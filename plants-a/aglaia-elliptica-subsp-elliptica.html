Aglaia elliptica subsp. elliptica

Aglaia elliptica subsp.

elliptica, commonly known as the elliptic aglaia, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia. It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree or shrub that typically grows in tropical rainforests.

The plant is characterized by its elliptical, glossy leaves and produces small, fragrant yellow flowers. Its fruits are capsule-like structures that contain aromatic seeds.

It is valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
