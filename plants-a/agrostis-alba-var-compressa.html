Agrostis alba var. compressa

Agrostis alba var. compressa, commonly known as silvergrass or white grass, is a perennial grass species native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-gray foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in open, moist habitats such as meadows, lawns, and along stream banks. Its inflorescence consists of a dense, spike-like cluster of small, pale green to white flowers.

Agrostis alba var. compressa is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its aesthetic appeal and low maintenance requirements.
