Acaena magellanica var. laevigata

Acaena magellanica var. laevigata, commonly known as the smooth burr plant, is a shrub native to South America, particularly found in Patagonia.

It is characterized by its woody stems and spherical, spiny fruits that contain seeds. The leaves are alternate, simple, and often covered with fine hairs. The plant thrives in dry, sunny environments and is adapted to withstand harsh climatic conditions.

It plays a role in local ecosystems by providing food for various bird and small animal species.
