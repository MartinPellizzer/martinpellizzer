Achimenes longiflora var. margaritae

Achimenes longiflora var. margaritae, commonly known as the Margarita Achimenes, is a hybrid plant native to South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces striking, showy flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant features lance-shaped leaves and is known for its ability to thrive in warm, humid climates. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant in gardens and greenhouses.

Its flowers bloom in clusters, adding a vibrant touch to floral displays.
