Anemone californica

Anemone californica, commonly known as the California poppy, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It features showy, bright orange to red flowers that bloom in spring and early summer. The plant has basal leaves that are deeply lobed and covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in open fields and along roadsides.

The species is also known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
