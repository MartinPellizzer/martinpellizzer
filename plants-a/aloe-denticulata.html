Aloe denticulata

Aloe denticulata, commonly known as the toothed aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a white or blueish tint. The leaves are serrated or toothed, giving the plant its common name. It produces yellow flowers in clusters during the summer months.

This plant is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
