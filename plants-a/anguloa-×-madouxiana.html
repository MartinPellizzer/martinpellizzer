Anguloa × madouxiana

Anguloa × madouxiana, commonly known as the hybrid angel's trumpets, is a hybrid plant resulting from the cross between Anguloa and Brugmansia species.

It belongs to the family Solanaceae, which includes nightshade plants, and is native to South America. The plant is characterized by its large, showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that come in various colors, including white, yellow, and pink. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is sometimes grown as a houseplant or in gardens.

However, it contains toxic alkaloids and should be handled with care.
