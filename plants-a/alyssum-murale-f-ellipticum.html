Alyssum murale f. ellipticum

Alyssum murale f. ellipticum, commonly known as the narrow-leafed alyssum, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, clustered flowers that typically range in color from white to pale yellow. The plant has elliptical leaves that are usually hairy, giving it a distinct appearance. Native to southern Europe, it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun.

It is often used in rock gardens and as a ground cover due to its hardy nature and ornamental value.
