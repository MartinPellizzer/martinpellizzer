Aristida plumosa var. aethiopica

Aristida plumosa var. aethiopica, commonly known as the Ethiopian plumgrass, is a perennial grass species native to Africa.

It features slender, tufted stems with fine, silky leaves that give it a soft, feathery appearance. The plant produces small, pale green to brown spikelets that are typically arranged in loose, open clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and open woodlands.

Due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
