Argostemma vietnamicum

Argostemma vietnamicum, commonly known as Vietnamese argostemma, is a member of the Rubiaceae family, characterized by its ornamental and medicinal value.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Southeast Asia, particularly Vietnam, where it thrives in moist, shaded environments. Its root system is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native range.
