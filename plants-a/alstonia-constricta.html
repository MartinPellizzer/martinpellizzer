Alstonia constricta

Alstonia constricta, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a deciduous tree native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, bright red flowers that bloom in clusters, adding vibrant color to the landscape. The tree has compound leaves with long, slender leaflets, and its bark is smooth and often fissured. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular ornamental plant.

Alstonia constricta is also valued for its fast growth and ability to provide shade in urban settings.
