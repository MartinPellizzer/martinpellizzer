Agrostophyllum dischorense

Agrostophyllum dischorense, commonly known as the Brazilian star-of-Bahia, is a flowering plant native to the Atlantic Forest of Brazil.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy yellow-orange inflorescences. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and has lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture. It is often found in humid tropical regions and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
