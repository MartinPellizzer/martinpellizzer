Acorus calamus var. belangeri

Acorus calamus var. belangeri, commonly known as the Belanger's sweetflag, is a variant of the sweetflag plant native to parts of North America.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant characterized by its aromatic, sword-shaped leaves and a rhizomatous growth habit. The plant typically grows in wetland environments such as marshes and along the edges of ponds. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in dense, spike-like inflorescences.

This variety is valued for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally in herbal remedies.
