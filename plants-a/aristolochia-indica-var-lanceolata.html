Aristolochia indica var. lanceolata

Aristolochia indica var. lanceolata, commonly known as the Indian birthwort, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, aromatic leaves and distinctive, pipe-shaped flowers that are often greenish or brownish in color. The plant produces small, yellowish-green berries that contain the seeds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its unique morphology and medicinal properties, it is of interest in both horticulture and traditional medicine.
