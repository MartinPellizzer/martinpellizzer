Alhagi mannifera var. kardochorum

Alhagi mannifera var. kardochorum, commonly known as the Kardochorum alfalfa, is a perennial shrub native to Central Asia.

It is characterized by its small, compound leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, making it well adapted to harsh, dry climates. It has a deep root system that allows it to access water from deeper soil layers.

This variety is often used for forage and soil stabilization in degraded landscapes.
