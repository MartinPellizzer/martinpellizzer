Arctium congestum

Arctium congestum, commonly known as the clustered burdock, is a biennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features tall, hairy stems with large, heart-shaped leaves that are often lobed. Its most distinctive feature is the spiky, bur-like seed heads that aid in wind and animal dispersal.

Arctium congestum is known for its medicinal properties, particularly in traditional herbal remedies.
