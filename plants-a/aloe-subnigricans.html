Aloe subnigricans

Aloe subnigricans, commonly known as the black aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a reddish or purplish tinge. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow flowers, which bloom in the spring. It is drought-tolerant and prefers well-draining soil, making it suitable for arid environments.

Aloe subnigricans is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional medicinal uses.
