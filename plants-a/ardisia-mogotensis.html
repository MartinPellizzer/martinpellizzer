Ardisia mogotensis

Ardisia mogotensis, commonly known as the Malayan beautyberry, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a member of the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers that are followed by bright red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

The fruits are an important food source for various bird species.
