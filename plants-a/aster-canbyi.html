Aster canbyi

Aster canbyi, commonly known as Canby's aster, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in sunny, arid environments. Its flowers are usually purple or blue, with ray florets surrounding a central disk.

Aster canbyi is often found in open habitats such as meadows and grasslands.
