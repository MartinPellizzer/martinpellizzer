Asyneuma linifolium

Asyneuma linifolium, commonly known as the silky leaf sunflower, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and distinctive foliage. The plant features narrow, silvery-haired leaves that give it a soft, almost velvety appearance. Its flowers are typically yellow with a central disk, blooming in late summer to early fall.

Asyneuma linifolium is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
