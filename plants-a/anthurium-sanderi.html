Anthurium sanderi

Anthurium sanderi, commonly known as the Sander's flamingo flower, is a tropical plant native to Central America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are dark green with a metallic sheen. The plant produces vibrant red spathes that enclose small, spadix-like inflorescences. It thrives in humid, warm environments with bright, indirect light.

Anthurium sanderi is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and unique floral structure.
