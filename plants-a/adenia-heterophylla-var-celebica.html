Adenia heterophylla var. celebica

Adenia heterophylla var. celebica, commonly known as the Celebes Adenia, is a climbing shrub native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It features large, heart-shaped leaves that vary in size and texture depending on the environmental conditions. The plant produces striking, fleshy, red or orange berries that are an important food source for various bird species. Its flowers are typically greenish-white with a distinctive, fleshy calyx that becomes fleshy and colorful as the fruit develops.

This variant is notable for its ornamental value and ecological significance in its native habitat.
