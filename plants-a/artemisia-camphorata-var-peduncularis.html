Artemisia camphorata var. peduncularis

Artemisia camphorata var. peduncularis, commonly known as Chinese wormwood, is a perennial herb native to China.

It is characterized by its aromatic, silver-gray foliage and slender, branched stems. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in dry, well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or mountainous regions.

This variety is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional Chinese medicine.
