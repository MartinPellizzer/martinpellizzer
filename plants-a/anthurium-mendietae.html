Anthurium mendietae

Anthurium mendietae, commonly known as the Mendieta's arrowhead, is a rare species native to the tropical rainforests of Central America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves with a waxy texture and deep green coloration. The plant produces vibrant red spadices, which are modified leaves that house the tiny, pale yellow flowers. Anthurium mendietae thrives in high humidity and indirect light, making it a challenging but rewarding plant for experienced growers.

Its unique foliage and ornamental value make it a sought-after species in the horticultural trade.
