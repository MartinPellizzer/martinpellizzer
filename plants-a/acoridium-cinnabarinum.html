Acoridium cinnabarinum

Acoridium cinnabarinum, commonly known as the red-flowered acoridium, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Acoraceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, bright red flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has strap-like leaves that are usually green with a slight reddish tint. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in swampy or wetland areas.

The plant is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its striking floral display.
