Asplenium sandersonii var. vagans

Asplenium sandersonii var. vagans, commonly known as the wandering spleen fern, is a variegated fern species native to Southeast Asia.

It features delicate, lance-shaped fronds with a distinctive pattern of light and dark green markings. The plant produces small, spore-bearing structures called sori on the underside of its leaves. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardening.
