Aerobion gracile

Aerobion gracile, commonly known as the slender air plant, is a unique epiphytic species that thrives in humid, shaded environments.

It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that collect water and nutrients. It is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its low maintenance and aesthetic appeal.
