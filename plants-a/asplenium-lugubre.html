Asplenium lugubre

Asplenium lugubre, commonly known as the sorrowful spleenwort, is a species of fern belonging to the family Aspleniaceae.

It is characterized by its dark green, leathery fronds that are often tinged with purple, giving it a somber appearance. This plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as rock crevices or forest floors. Its sporangia are clustered in sori on the underside of the fronds, a common reproductive feature in ferns.

Asplenium lugubre is a small, evergreen plant that thrives in humid climates and is often found in temperate regions.
