Aerangis carnea

Aerangis carnea, commonly known as the pink orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking pink and white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has long, slender stems and lance-shaped leaves that are arranged in a spiral pattern. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Aerangis carnea is often cultivated for its ornamental value in both indoor and outdoor gardens.
