Aralia rigida

Aralia rigida, commonly known as steel fern, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern North America.

It features compound leaves with a distinctive fern-like appearance, and its stems are sturdy and somewhat woody. The plant produces small, white flowers in umbels, which are followed by dark blue berries. Aralia rigida is often found in moist, wooded areas and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.

It is also used in traditional herbal medicine for its purported health benefits.
