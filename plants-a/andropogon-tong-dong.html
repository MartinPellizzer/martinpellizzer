Andropogon tong-dong

Andropogon tong-dong, commonly known as the Chinese switchgrass, is a perennial grass native to China.

It belongs to the Poaceae family, which includes many other grass species. The plant is characterized by its tall, slender stems and narrow, linear leaves. It thrives in moist environments and is often found in wetlands or along riverbanks.

Its fibrous root system helps prevent soil erosion, making it ecologically significant in its habitat.
