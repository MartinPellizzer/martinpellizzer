Aulosepalum pulchrum

Aulosepalum pulchrum, commonly known as the beautiful flame orchid, is a rare and striking orchid species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant red and orange flowers, which are often adorned with intricate patterns resembling flames. The plant has a delicate, strap-like leaf structure and produces small, clustered inflorescences. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its rarity and aesthetic appeal, it is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts and conservationists alike.
