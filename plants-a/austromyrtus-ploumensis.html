Austromyrtus ploumensis

Austromyrtus ploumensis, commonly known as the Tasmanian myrtle, is a small evergreen shrub native to Tasmania.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family, which includes eucalyptus and tea trees. The plant has small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically glossy green and emit a mild aromatic scent. It produces small, white flowers followed by woody capsules containing numerous small, dark seeds.

Austromyrtus ploumensis is adapted to the cool, moist climate of Tasmania and is often found in swampy or wetland habitats.
