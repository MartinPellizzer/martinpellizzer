Arisaema addis-ababense

Arisaema addis-ababense, commonly known as Addis Ababa arisaema, is a unique member of the Araceae family native to Ethiopia.

It is characterized by its striking, arrow-shaped leaves and a spadix surrounded by a distinctive, hood-like spathe. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often found in forested areas or along stream banks. Its botanical features include a rhizomatous root system and a short, erect stem.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and potentially endangered species.
