Astrocarpa sesamoides var. erecta

Astrocarpa sesamoides var. erecta, commonly known as the upright astrocarpa, is a flowering plant native to specific regions of South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows as an erect shrub with narrow, linear leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers in clusters.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for horticultural study, though it is not widely cultivated.
