Ardisia amboinensis

Ardisia amboinensis, commonly known as the Java star or Amboina star, is a member of the Ericaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small evergreen shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features glossy, dark green elliptical leaves and produces clusters of small, white, star-shaped flowers. After flowering, it develops bright red, fleshy berries that are attractive to birds.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and seasonal display of flowers and fruits.
