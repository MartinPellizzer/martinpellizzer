Argyreia splendens

Argyreia splendens, commonly known as the red morning glory, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from bright red to deep purple. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind. Its leaves are heart-shaped and have a velvety texture.

Argyreia splendens is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a climbing plant.
