Arisaema serratum f. japonicum

Arisaema serratum f. japonicum, commonly known as the Japanese pinwheel plant, is a variant of the common arisaema.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant native to East Asia, particularly Japan. The plant features a distinctive, umbrella-like inflorescence with spadices and a central spadix, typical of the Araceae family. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and have serrated edges, giving it its scientific name.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
