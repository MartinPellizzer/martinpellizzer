Astrophytum pullispinum

Astrophytum pullispinum, commonly known as the Mexican star cactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its short, thick stems that are covered in white, woolly hairs. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is a slow-growing species that thrives in well-draining soil and full sun.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for desert gardens and rockeries.
