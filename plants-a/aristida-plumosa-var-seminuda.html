Aristida plumosa var. seminuda

Aristida plumosa var. seminuda, commonly known as the silky feather grass, is a perennial grass species native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery plumes that give it a soft, flowing appearance. The plant typically grows in open, sunny areas such as prairies and rocky soils. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-gray hue that helps it retain moisture.

It is often used in ornamental landscaping for its elegant, lightweight foliage.
