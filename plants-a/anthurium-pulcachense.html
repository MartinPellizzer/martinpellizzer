Anthurium pulcachense

Anthurium pulcachense, commonly known as the pink anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped spathe that ranges from pink to red, often with a contrasting green spadix. The plant thrives in humid, warm environments with indirect light and well-draining soil. Its foliage consists of large, glossy, dark green leaves that contribute to its ornamental appeal.

Anthurium pulcachense is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
