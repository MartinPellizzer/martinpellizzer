Alaternus balearica

Alaternus balearica, commonly known as the Balearic alaternus, is a rare and endemic plant species native to the Balearic Islands.

It is a small shrub characterized by its narrow, elongated leaves and clusters of small, yellowish flowers. The plant thrives in rocky, coastal habitats and is adapted to the arid conditions of its environment. It plays an important role in the local ecosystem, providing habitat and food for various insects and small animals.

Due to its limited distribution and threats from habitat loss, it is considered a vulnerable species.
