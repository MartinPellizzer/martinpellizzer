Arachnothryx guerrerensis

Arachnothryx guerrerensis, commonly known as the Guerrerensis Thornwood, is a tropical tree native to Central America.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and thorny stems. The plant produces small, yellow flowers followed by pod-like fruits containing seeds. It is often found in humid lowland forests and is valued for its hard, durable wood.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native range.
