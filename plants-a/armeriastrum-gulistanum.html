Armeriastrum gulistanum

Armeriastrum gulistanum, commonly known as the Gulistanum silverweed, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the arid regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the family Caryophyllaceae and is characterized by its silver-gray foliage due to dense pubescence. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers in clusters during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to harsh, dry environments.
