Angorkis gladiifolia

Angorkis gladiifolia, commonly known as the swordleaf orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It features tall, slender stems with broad, lance-shaped leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces striking, showy flowers in shades of white and pink, often with intricate patterns on the petals. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its unique appearance and limited habitat, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
