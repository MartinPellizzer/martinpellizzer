Aster stuebelii

Aster stuebelii, commonly known as the Stuebel's aster, is a perennial herb native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by composite flower heads made up of many small florets. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet and features showy blue to purple ray flowers surrounding a yellow disk. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, and it thrives in moist, well-drained soils.

Aster stuebelii is often found in woodland edges and wetlands, where it contributes to local biodiversity.
