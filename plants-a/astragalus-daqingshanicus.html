Astragalus daqingshanicus

Astragalus daqingshanicus, commonly known as the Daqingshan milkvetch, is a perennial herb native to China, specifically found in the Daqingshan region.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant is adapted to grow in rocky and arid environments, showcasing resilience to harsh conditions.

Its root system is known for its medicinal properties, often used in traditional Chinese medicine.
