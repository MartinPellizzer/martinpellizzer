Aerides houlletiana var. majus

Aerides houlletiana var. majus, commonly known as the large-leafed orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, leathery leaves and showy, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its flowers are usually pale yellow to orange with dark markings, and it prefers warm, humid conditions.

It is a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
