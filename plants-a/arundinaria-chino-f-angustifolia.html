Arundinaria chino f. angustifolia

Arundinaria chino f. angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved bamboo, is a variant of the Chinese bamboo species.

It is characterized by its slender, elongated culms and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. This plant is native to eastern Asia and thrives in moist, well-drained soils. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and hardy nature.

The variety exhibits a distinct genetic variation that sets it apart from other members of the Arundinaria genus.
