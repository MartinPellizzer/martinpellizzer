Anthurium navasii

Anthurium navasii, commonly known as the Navas' anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces vibrant spathes, which are modified leaves that enclose the small, spadix-like flowers. Anthurium navasii thrives in humid, warm environments and requires indirect sunlight for optimal growth.

Its unique foliage and ornamental value make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
