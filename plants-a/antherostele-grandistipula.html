Antherostele grandistipula

Antherostele grandistipula, commonly known as the giant-stippled antherostele, is a unique plant species characterized by its distinctive botanical features.

It possesses a robust rhizome system that supports its tall, erect growth habit. The leaves are broad and lance-shaped, with prominent venation patterns that contribute to its striking appearance. Its flowers are clustered in dense inflorescences, featuring showy, petal-like bracts that attract pollinators.

This plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically found in temperate rainforests.
