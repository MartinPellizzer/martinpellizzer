Anthurium nutibarense

Anthurium nutibarense, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically deep green with a metallic sheen. The plant produces vibrant red spathes that enclose its spadices, which are the actual flowering structures. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium nutibarense is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and unique floral display.
