Anthurium fontellanum

Anthurium fontellanum, commonly known as the Fontella anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-shaped flowers surrounded by a colorful spathe, often in shades of red, orange, or yellow. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Anthurium fontellanum is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
