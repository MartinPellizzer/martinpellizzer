Argyrolobium angustistipulatum

Argyrolobium angustistipulatum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved silver globular.

It is a flowering plant native to South Africa, characterized by its globular shape and silvery foliage. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in summer, adding ornamental value to its appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Its unique appearance and adaptability make it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
