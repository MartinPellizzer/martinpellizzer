Aspidistra caespitosa

Aspidistra caespitosa, commonly known as the clustered aspidistra, is a perennial evergreen plant native to China.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its dark green, glossy, and strap-like leaves that form dense clusters. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically hidden beneath its foliage. It thrives in shady, humid environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage.

Aspidistra caespitosa is also valued for its ability to tolerate low light and poor soil conditions.
