Aster yomena var. angustifolius

Aster yomena var. angustifolius, commonly known as narrow-leaved aster, is a perennial herb native to parts of East Asia.

It features showy blue to purple flowers that bloom in the late summer and early fall. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in well-drained soils and full to partial sunlight, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens or borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
