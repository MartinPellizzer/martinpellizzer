Alternanthera porrigens

Alternanthera porrigens, commonly known as the red flame plant, is a striking aquatic or semi-aquatic plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red or pink foliage, which becomes more intense under strong light conditions. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often fleshy and can vary in color depending on the environment. It thrives in warm, humid conditions and is popular in aquariums for its ornamental value.

Alternanthera porrigens is relatively easy to care for, making it a favorite among aquatic plant enthusiasts.
