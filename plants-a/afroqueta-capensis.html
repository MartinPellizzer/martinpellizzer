Afroqueta capensis

Afroqueta capensis, commonly known as the Cape afroqueta, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are opposite and lance-shaped, often with a slightly hairy texture.
