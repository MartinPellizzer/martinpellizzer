Alyxia royeniana

Alyxia royeniana, commonly known as the royal alyxia, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean and Central America.

It is a woody vine characterized by its small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant produces small, fleshy berries that are typically yellow or orange when mature. Alyxia royeniana thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often climbing trees or shrubs in its natural habitat.
