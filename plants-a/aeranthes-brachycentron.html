Aeranthes brachycentron

Aeranthes brachycentron, commonly known as the narrow-leafed sword fern, is a species of fern native to the Caribbean.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its slender, upright fronds that are often covered in fine hairs. The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens.
