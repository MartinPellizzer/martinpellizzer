Anogra longiflora

Anogra longiflora, commonly known as the long-flowered anogra, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features slender, upright stems that bear clusters of small, tubular flowers. The flowers are typically pale green to white in color and are arranged in spikes along the stem. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem.

It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.
