Ampelocissus dichrothrix

Ampelocissus dichrothrix, commonly known as the silver vine, is a climbing plant native to southern Africa.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery down, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are followed by bright red berries. It is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types and its tolerance to drought.

Ampelocissus dichrothrix is often used in ornamental gardening due to its aesthetic appeal and hardy nature.
