Agrostis tenuis f. vinealis

Agrostis tenuis f. vinealis, commonly known as slender bentgrass form vinealis, is a variant of the slender bentgrass species.

It is a low-growing grass with fine, narrow leaves that are typically green in color. This form is often found in moist, shaded environments such as woodland edges or along stream banks. Its inflorescence consists of dense, spike-like clusters of small, greenish flowers.

The plant has a fibrous root system and is adapted to thrive in nutrient-poor soils.
