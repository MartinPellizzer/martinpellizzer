Anthemis filicaulis

Anthemis filicaulis, commonly known as the slender leaf everlasting, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are surrounded by pale green bracts, giving it a delicate appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its long-lasting blooms.
