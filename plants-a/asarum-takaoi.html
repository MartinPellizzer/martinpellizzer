Asarum takaoi

Asarum takaoi, commonly known as Kii Asarum, is a perennial herb native to Japan.

It belongs to the Aristolochiaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and unique, maroon-colored flowers. The plant thrives in shaded, moist environments, typically found in forest understories. Its flowers are highly specialized, featuring a complex structure that attracts specific pollinators.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, Asarum takaoi is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
