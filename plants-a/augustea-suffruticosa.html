Augustea suffruticosa

Augustea suffruticosa, commonly known as the wild olive or desert olive, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its small, oval leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces small, edible fruits that are often used in traditional cuisine. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in sandy or rocky soils.

Augustea suffruticosa plays an important role in stabilizing arid ecosystems and providing habitat for wildlife.
