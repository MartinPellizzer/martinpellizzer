Agoseris laciniata

Agoseris laciniata, commonly known as the fringed goldenweed, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Its leaves are typically deeply lobed and fern-like, contributing to its ornamental value.

Agoseris laciniata thrives in dry, sunny environments and is often found in meadows and along rocky slopes.
