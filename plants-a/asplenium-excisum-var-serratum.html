Asplenium excisum var. serratum

Asplenium excisum var. serratum, commonly known as the serrated spleenwort, is a fern belonging to the family Aspleniaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped leaves that are finely serrated along the margins. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as rock crevices or forest floors. Its fronds are usually dark green and have a leathery texture.

This variety is often found in temperate regions and is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.
