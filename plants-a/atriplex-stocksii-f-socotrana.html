Atriplex stocksii f. socotrana

Atriplex stocksii f. socotrana, commonly known as Socotra saltbush, is a shrub native to the island of Socotra.

It is characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves that are typically gray-green in color. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, showcasing adaptations to saline and nutrient-poor soils.

This unique species is an important part of the local flora and ecosystem on Socotra.
