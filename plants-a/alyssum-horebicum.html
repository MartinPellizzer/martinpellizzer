Alyssum horebicum

Alyssum horebicum, commonly known as the Jordan Valley alyssum, is a flowering plant native to the Middle East.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces clusters of small, fragrant white or pale yellow flowers. The plant has hairy, linear leaves and thrives in well-drained soils. It is often found in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions.

Alyssum horebicum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in native plant gardens.
