Alternanthera baueri

Alternanthera baueri, commonly known as the red-leaf alternanthera, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, brightly colored foliage, often displaying shades of red, pink, and purple. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full sunlight.

Alternanthera baueri is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its vibrant leaves and ease of care.
