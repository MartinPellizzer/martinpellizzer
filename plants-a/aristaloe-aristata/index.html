Aristaloe Aristata

Aristaloe aristata is a striking succulent native to southern Africa. It is known for its tall, spire-like stems and clusters of vibrant, bell-shaped flowers. This plant thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers. With proper care, Aristaloe aristata can add a dramatic touch to any garden.
