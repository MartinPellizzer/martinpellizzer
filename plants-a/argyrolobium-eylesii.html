Argyrolobium eylesii

Argyrolobium eylesii, commonly known as the Eyles' silver globular pea, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive spherical shape and silvery foliage. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
