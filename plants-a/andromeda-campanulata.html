Andromeda campanulata

Andromeda campanulata, commonly known as the bell-shaped andromeda, is a flowering plant belonging to the Ericaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia, particularly in countries like China and Vietnam. The plant is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in spring. Its evergreen leaves are glossy and oval-shaped, providing a lush backdrop to the vibrant flowers.

Andromeda campanulata thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded forest environments.
