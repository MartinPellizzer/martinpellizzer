Anacampseros lubbersii

Anacampseros lubbersii, commonly known as the lubbersii stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It features fleshy, upright stems and small, waxy leaves that help it retain moisture in its harsh environment. The plant produces clusters of star-shaped, yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a desirable addition to xeriscaped landscapes.
