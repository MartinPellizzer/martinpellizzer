Andira retusa var. laurifolia

Andira retusa var. laurifolia, commonly known as the laurel andira, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Central and South America.

It features compound leaves that are alternate and often have a glossy, dark green appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by woody, cylindrical fruits. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for horticultural collections in warm climates.
