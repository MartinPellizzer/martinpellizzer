Abutilon × hybridum

Abutilon × hybridum, commonly known as the Chinese lantern plant, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 3 meters tall. The plant is characterized by its velvety, heart-shaped leaves and vibrant orange to red bracts that resemble lanterns. It produces small, yellow flowers that are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds.

The plant thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.
