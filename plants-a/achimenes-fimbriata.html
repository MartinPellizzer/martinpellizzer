Achimenes fimbriata

Achimenes fimbriata, commonly known as the fringed trumpet vine, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces vibrant, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves and is known for its rapid growth and ability to climb or trail. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil and full sun to partial shade.

Achimenes fimbriata is popular in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators like hummingbirds and bees.
