Anoectochilus sanguineus

Anoectochilus sanguineus, commonly known as the red-stemmed orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid that typically grows on trees in humid, shaded environments. The plant is characterized by its striking red or purple stem and lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are usually pale green or yellow with a distinctive red or purple lip.

Anoectochilus sanguineus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in terrariums or as a houseplant.
