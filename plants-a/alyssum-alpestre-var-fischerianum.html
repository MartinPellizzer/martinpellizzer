Alyssum alpestre var. fischerianum

Alyssum alpestre var. fischerianum, commonly known as the alpine alyssum, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and lavender. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and alpine landscapes. Its compact growth habit and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking hardy, decorative plants.

It is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
