Aiouea pachypoda

Aiouea pachypoda, commonly known as the broad-leaved aiouea, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, elliptical leaves that are often glossy and dark green in color. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, arranged in clusters. Its fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release seeds.

Aiouea pachypoda is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
