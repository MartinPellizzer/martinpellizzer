Abutilon flavum

Abutilon flavum, commonly known as the yellow abutilon, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 3 meters in height. The plant produces showy, lantern-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a rough texture.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant blooms and adaptability to various growing conditions.
