Anemone trullifolia var. souliei

Anemone trullifolia var. souliei, commonly known as the Soulie's anemone, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

It features delicate, palmate leaves that are deeply lobed and covered in fine hairs. The plant produces showy, white to pink flowers with a distinctive, narrow corona, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in cool, moist environments and prefers partial to full shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a sought-after addition to woodland gardens and shaded landscapes.
