Aerangis concavipetala

Aerangis concavipetala, commonly known as the concave-petaled orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant has strap-like leaves and produces showy, pendulous inflorescences. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and private collections.
