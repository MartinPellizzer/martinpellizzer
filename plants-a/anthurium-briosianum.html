Anthurium briosianum

Anthurium briosianum, commonly known as the Briosianum Anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces striking spadices, which are modified leaves that enclose the true flowers. It thrives in humid, warm environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium briosianum is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and ease of care.
