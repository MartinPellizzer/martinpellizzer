Abutilon impressum

Abutilon impressum, commonly known as the velvetleaf abutilon, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its fuzzy, velvety leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces showy, lantern-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in disturbed soils or along roadsides.

Abutilon impressum is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
