Athyrium hohuanshanense

Athyrium hohuanshanense, commonly known as the Hohuanshan Athyrium, is a fern native to China, specifically found in the Hohuanshan region.

It belongs to the Dryopteridaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and mountainous areas.
