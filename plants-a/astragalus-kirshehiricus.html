Astragalus kirshehiricus

Astragalus kirshehiricus, commonly known as Kirshehir milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to Turkey.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its small, yellow pea-like flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid environments. It has compound leaves and produces pods that contain small, round seeds.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
