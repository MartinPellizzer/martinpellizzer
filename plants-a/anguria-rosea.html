Anguria rosea

Anguria rosea, commonly known as the pink cucumber, is a unique hybrid plant that combines features of both cucumbers and ornamental gourds.

It produces elongated, pale green fruits with a distinctive pinkish hue, which develop as they mature. The plant is a vigorous vine that thrives in warm climates and requires full sun for optimal growth. Its flowers are typically yellow with a subtle pink tint, adding to its ornamental appeal.

Anguria rosea is often cultivated for its unusual appearance and can be grown as a decorative plant in gardens or containers.
