Aralia quinquefolia var. major

Aralia quinquefolia var. major, commonly known as American giant aralia, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern North America.

It is characterized by its large, palmately compound leaves that can reach up to 30 cm in diameter. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers in umbels, which are followed by bright red berries. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodlands and along streambanks.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, providing food for birds and other wildlife.
