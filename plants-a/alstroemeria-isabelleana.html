Alstroemeria isabelleana

Alstroemeria isabelleana, commonly known as the Isabelleana Alstroemeria, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the family Alstroemeriaceae and is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant typically grows as a herbaceous perennial, with strap-like leaves and a compact growth habit. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long-lasting blooms and ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
