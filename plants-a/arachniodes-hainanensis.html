Arachniodes hainanensis

Arachniodes hainanensis, commonly known as the Hainan spider fern, is a rare and unique species native to the tropical regions of China.

It belongs to the Dryopteridaceae family and is characterized by its intricate, lacy fronds that resemble spider webs, giving it its common name. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as mountain forests, where it thrives in the undergrowth.
