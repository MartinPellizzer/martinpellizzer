Aichryson × buchii

Aichryson × buchii, commonly known as the hybrid African violet, is a compact evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It features dense, fleshy, dark green leaves that are often covered in a fine, white powdery coating called bloom. The plant produces small, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, typically in spring and summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Due to its ornamental value, it is widely cultivated as a houseplant.
