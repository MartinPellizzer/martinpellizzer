Aporum capitellatum

Aporum capitellatum, commonly known as the golden star sedge, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a member of the Cyperaceae family, which includes grass-like plants known as sedges. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or brown in color. It thrives in wet or moist environments, such as marshes and swampy areas.
