Aloe aurelienii

Aloe aurelienii, commonly known as the Aurelien's Aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, with a waxy coating that helps it retain moisture. The plant produces striking yellow flowers during the summer months, which are borne on tall, branched stems. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their soothing properties.

Aloe aurelienii thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it well-suited for arid environments.
