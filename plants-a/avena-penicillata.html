Avena penicillata

Avena penicillata, commonly known as slender oat, is a perennial grass native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, blade-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny flowers. It thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in chaparral and woodland habitats.

Avena penicillata is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to stabilize soil in arid environments.
