Anthyllis vulneraria var. soloitama

Anthyllis vulneraria var. soloitama, commonly known as the solitary broom.

It is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and North Africa, characterized by its upright growth habit and yellow flowers. The plant belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and features trifoliate leaves with small, rounded lobes. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats such as coastal dunes and rocky slopes.

This variety is notable for its limited distribution and is considered a rare plant in some regions.
