Aster Menziesii Var. Bernardinus

Aster menziesii var. bernardinus is a rare and distinctive plant native to the western united states. it belongs to the aster family and is known for its showy blue to purple flowers. this variety is often found in rocky soils and mountainous regions. its unique characteristics make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists. due to its limited range, it is considered endangered and requires careful protection.
