Arisaema yoshinagae

Arisaema yoshinagae, commonly known as the Yoshinaga's arrowhead, is a rare and unique member of the Araceae family.

It is native to Japan and is characterized by its striking, arrow-shaped leaves and distinctive spadix inflorescence. The plant produces a single, narrow spadix surrounded by a hood-like spathe, which is often brightly colored. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is typically found in mountainous regions.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a vulnerable species.
