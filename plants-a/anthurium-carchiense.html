Anthurium carchiense

Anthurium carchiense, commonly known as the Carchi arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to the Andean region of South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a metallic sheen. The plant produces a unique inflorescence consisting of a spadix surrounded by a spathe, which can vary in color from white to pink. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Anthurium carchiense is popular among collectors for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
