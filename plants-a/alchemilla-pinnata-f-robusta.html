Alchemilla pinnata f. robusta

Alchemilla pinnata f. robusta, commonly known as the robust alpine lady's mantle, is a variant of the alpine lady's mantle species.

It is a perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe, particularly the Alps. The plant is characterized by its dense, silver-white foliage that forms a compact rosette. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers in early summer, which are clustered in spherical inflorescences.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to thrive in well-drained, rocky soils.
