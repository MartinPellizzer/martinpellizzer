Afrolimon capense

Afrolimon capense, commonly known as the Cape limon.

It is a small, evergreen shrub native to the coastal regions of South Africa. The plant features narrow, leathery leaves and produces small, fragrant yellow flowers. It is well adapted to sandy soils and coastal climates.

Afrolimon capense is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive appearance and hardy nature.
