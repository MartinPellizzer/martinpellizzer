Athyrium × reichsteinii subsp. praetermissum

Athyrium × reichsteinii subsp.

praetermissum, commonly known as the golden shield fern, is a hybrid species within the Athyriaceae family. It is characterized by its finely divided, bright green fronds that often display a golden hue on the underside.

The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and woodland areas. It produces small, clustered spore cases on the underside of its leaves, which are essential for reproduction.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens with high humidity and partial shade.
