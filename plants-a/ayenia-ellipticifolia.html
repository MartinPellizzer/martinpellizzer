Ayenia ellipticifolia

Ayenia ellipticifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf ayenia, is a species of flowering plant native to the south-western United States and northern Mexico.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads composed of both ray and disk flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments. Its leaves are elliptical in shape and have a distinctive, narrow appearance, while its flowers are small and yellowish-green in color.

Ayenia ellipticifolia is often found in scrublands and open woodlands, where it contributes to the local biodiversity.
