Aponogeton afroviolaceus var. angustifolius

Aponogeton afroviolaceus var. angustifolius, commonly known as the narrow-leaf African water plant, is a species of aquatic plant native to Africa.

It features long, narrow leaves that are often submerged, with a distinctive violet hue on the underside. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom above the water surface. It thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for aquariums and water gardens.
