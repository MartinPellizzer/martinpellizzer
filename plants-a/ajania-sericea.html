Ajania sericea

Ajania sericea, commonly known as silverleaf ajania, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces small, daisy-like flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Ajania sericea is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive appearance and drought tolerance.
