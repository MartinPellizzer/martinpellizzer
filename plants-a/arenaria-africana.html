Arenaria africana

Arenaria africana, commonly known as African sandwort, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically thrives in sandy or gravelly soils. The plant produces small, white or pink flowers during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are linear and often covered in a fine layer of white hairs.

It is well adapted to arid environments and is often found in coastal dunes or rocky habitats.
