Angraecum gladiifolium

Angraecum gladiifolium, commonly known as the sword-leafed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Madagascar.

It features linear, sword-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers that are usually white or pale yellow, with a distinctive spurred lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Angraecum gladiifolium is known for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its unique appearance.
