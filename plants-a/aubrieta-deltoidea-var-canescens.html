Aubrieta deltoidea var. canescens

Aubrieta deltoidea var. canescens, commonly known as the silver aubrieta, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features compact, mat-forming growth with dense clusters of small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has silvery-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, soft hairs, giving it a distinctive, frost-resistant appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it an ideal choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its compact size and long flowering period make it a popular ornamental plant in horticulture.
