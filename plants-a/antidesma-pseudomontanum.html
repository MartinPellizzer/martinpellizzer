Antidesma pseudomontanum

Antidesma pseudomontanum, commonly known as the mountain antidesma, is a small evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in humid, shaded environments within tropical and subtropical forests. The plant has opposite, elliptical leaves that are glossy and dark green in color. Its small, yellowish flowers are clustered in dense inflorescences, and it produces small, fleshy berries that are edible.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
