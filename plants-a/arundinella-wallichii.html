Arundinella wallichii

Arundinella wallichii, commonly known as the Chinese bamboo or bamboo grass, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Asia.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender stems that can reach heights of up to 3 meters. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. Its leaves are long, narrow, and often have a bluish-green hue with a rough texture.

Arundinella wallichii is often cultivated for ornamental purposes and is also used in traditional medicine in some regions.
