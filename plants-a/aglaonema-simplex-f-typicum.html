Aglaonema simplex f. typicum

Aglaonema simplex f. typicum, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen, is a tropical flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated foliage with shades of white, green, and pink. The plant thrives in low-light conditions and requires moderate humidity to flourish. Its leaves are typically broad and elliptical, often with a waxy texture.

While it produces small, inconspicuous flowers, the ornamental value of the plant lies primarily in its foliage.
