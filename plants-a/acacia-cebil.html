Acacia cebil

Acacia cebil, commonly known as the Cebil tree, is a species native to the Canary Islands.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree with a broad, spreading crown. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in dense, globular clusters, which are typical of the Acacia genus. Its leaves are bipinnate, consisting of numerous small, oval-shaped leaflets.

The Cebil tree is adapted to arid and coastal environments, making it resilient to drought and salt spray.
