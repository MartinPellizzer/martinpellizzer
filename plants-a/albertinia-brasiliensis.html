Albertinia brasiliensis

Albertinia brasiliensis, commonly known as the Brazilian star-of-Bethlehem, is a flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant features strap-like, grassy leaves that emerge from a bulbous stem. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated for decorative purposes.
