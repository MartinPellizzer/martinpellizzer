Anacampseros alta var. alta

Anacampseros alta var. alta, commonly known as the high mountain stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, rosette-forming structure with thick, fleshy leaves that store water, enabling it to survive in dry environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, or red, typically during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it both ornamental and ecologically valuable in its native habitat.
