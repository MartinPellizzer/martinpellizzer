Aloe fontainei

Aloe fontainei, commonly known as the Cape aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, spire-like stems that can grow up to 1.5 meters in height. Its leaves are thick, fleshy, and arranged in a rosette pattern, often with a reddish or purplish tinge. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers in summer, which are clustered in racemes.

It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
