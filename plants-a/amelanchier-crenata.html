Amelanchier crenata

Amelanchier crenata, commonly known as the smooth serviceberry, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to eastern North America.

It features oval to elliptical leaves that are typically dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces small, white to pale pink flowers in early spring, followed by edible, sweet berries in the summer. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained soil.

Amelanchier crenata is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, providing food for birds and pollinators.
