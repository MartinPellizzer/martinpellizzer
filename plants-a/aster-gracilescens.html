Aster gracilescens

Aster gracilescens, commonly known as the slender aster, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its delicate, blue to purple flowers that bloom in late summer and early fall. The plant typically grows in open habitats such as meadows and rocky slopes, preferring well-drained soils. Its slender stems and small, hairy leaves are adaptations to its arid environment.

Aster gracilescens is an important nectar source for pollinators and is often found in native plant gardens.
