Anthurium grossum

Anthurium grossum, commonly known as the giant anthurium, is a striking tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive spathe and spadix flower structure. The plant features large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. Its inflorescence consists of a colorful spadix surrounded by a showy, modified leaf called a spathe.

Anthurium grossum thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its vibrant foliage and unique floral display.
