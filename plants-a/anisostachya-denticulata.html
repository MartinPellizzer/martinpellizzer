Anisostachya denticulata

Anisostachya denticulata, commonly known as the serrated grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and narrow, linear leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in dense, spike-like inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and rocky areas.

Due to its hardy nature, it is occasionally cultivated for ornamental or ecological purposes.
