Areca macrocalyx

Areca macrocalyx, commonly known as the giant areca palm, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like leaves that can reach several meters in length. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that develop into clustered, edible fruits. Its botanical features make it a popular ornamental plant in warm climates.

It thrives in well-drained soil and requires ample sunlight for optimal growth.
