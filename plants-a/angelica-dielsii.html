Angelica dielsii

Angelica dielsii, commonly known as the Chinese angelica or Diels' angelica, is a perennial herb native to eastern Asia.

The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in umbels, typical of the family. Its botanical features make it a valuable species in both horticulture and phytotherapy.
