Aspidosperma gardneri var. ellipticum

Aspidosperma gardneri var. ellipticum, commonly known as the elliptic aspidosperma, is a species native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant has smooth, grayish-brown bark and elliptical-shaped leaves that are glossy on the upper surface. Its flowers are small and greenish-white, while the fruit is a capsule that splits open to release seeds.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and has some traditional uses in local medicine.
