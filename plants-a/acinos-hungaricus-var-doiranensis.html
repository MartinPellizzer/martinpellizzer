Acinos hungaricus var. doiranensis

Acinos hungaricus var. doiranensis, commonly known as the Doiran thistle, is a rare and endemic plant species native to the Balkans.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant with a rosette of silver-green, woolly leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers in summer, which are clustered in a single head. It thrives in rocky, limestone-rich soils and is adapted to arid conditions.
