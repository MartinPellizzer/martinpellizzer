Agropyron latronum var. microstachyum

Agropyron latronum var. microstachyum, commonly known as the slender wheatgrass, is a perennial grass species native to central Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and fine, blue-green foliage. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences with short, compact spikelets. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in steppe and grassland habitats.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping and erosion control.
