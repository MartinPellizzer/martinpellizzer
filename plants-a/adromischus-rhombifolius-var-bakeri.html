Adromischus rhombifolius var. bakeri

Adromischus rhombifolius var. bakeri, commonly known as the Baker's Jade, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are rhombic in shape, often tinged with red or pink at the edges. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Its compact growth habit and vibrant foliage make it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
