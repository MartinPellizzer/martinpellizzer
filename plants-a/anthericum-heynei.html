Anthericum heynei

Anthericum heynei, commonly known as the yellow lily of the valley, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its striking yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant features arrow-shaped leaves and produces small, clustered flowers that are often surrounded by a spathe. Anthericum heynei is typically found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and botanical collections.
