Artemisia × kobayashii

Artemisia × kobayashii, commonly known as Japanese wormwood, is a hybrid shrub native to Japan.

It features silvery-gray, aromatic foliage that is finely divided and covered in tiny, scale-like leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in panicles during the summer months. It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.

Artemisia × kobayashii thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
