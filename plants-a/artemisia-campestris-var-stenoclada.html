Artemisia campestris var. stenoclada

Artemisia campestris var. stenoclada, commonly known as narrow-leaved wormwood, is a perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features silver-gray foliage with narrow, linear leaves that are often aromatic and covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in panicles, which bloom in late summer. It thrives in dry, well-drained soils and is often found in open habitats such as meadows and roadsides.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
