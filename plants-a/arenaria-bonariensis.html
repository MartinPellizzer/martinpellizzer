Arenaria bonariensis

Arenaria bonariensis, commonly known as the Argentine sandwort, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically forms dense mats of foliage. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in coastal areas.

Its hardy nature makes it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
