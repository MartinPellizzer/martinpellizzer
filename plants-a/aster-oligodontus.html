Aster oligodontus

Aster oligodontus, commonly known as the narrow-leaved aster, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features slender, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, blue to purple flowers in the fall. It thrives in moist, woodland habitats and is often found in shaded environments.

Aster oligodontus is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits to pollinators.
