Atriplex assoi

Atriplex assoi, commonly known as the desert saltbush, is a shrub native to arid and semi-arid regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its small, spine-like leaves that help reduce water loss in harsh environments. The plant produces small green or yellowish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Atriplex assoi is adapted to saline soils and can thrive in areas with low rainfall.

It plays an important role in stabilizing soils and providing forage for wildlife.
