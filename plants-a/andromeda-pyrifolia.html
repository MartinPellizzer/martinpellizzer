Andromeda pyrifolia

Andromeda pyrifolia, commonly known as the Japanese andromeda, is a deciduous shrub native to East Asia.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring, typically in shades of white or pink. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth texture and a slightly glossy appearance. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Andromeda pyrifolia is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in woodland gardens.
