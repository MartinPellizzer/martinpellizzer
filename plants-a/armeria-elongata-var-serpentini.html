Armeria elongata var. serpentini

Armeria elongata var. serpentini, commonly known as the serpent's head.

This variant is characterized by its elongated, grass-like leaves and clusters of small, white flowers that bloom in late spring. It is a low-growing perennial that thrives in well-drained, sandy soils. The plant is native to the coastal regions of California and is adapted to tolerate salt spray and drought conditions.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
