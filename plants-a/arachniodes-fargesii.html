Arachniodes fargesii

Arachniodes fargesii, commonly known as the Farges' rock fern, is a perennial fern native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its delicate, lace-like fronds that resemble spider webs, giving it its common name. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional Chinese medicine for their purported health benefits.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in forest ecosystems.
