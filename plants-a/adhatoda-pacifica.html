Adhatoda pacifica

Adhatoda pacifica, commonly known as the Indian Trumpet or Blue Trumpet, is a flowering plant native to India and Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 3 meters in height, characterized by its lance-shaped leaves and striking blue or violet flowers. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in moist, well-drained soils.

Adhatoda pacifica is valued for its ornamental beauty and has traditional uses in herbal medicine.
