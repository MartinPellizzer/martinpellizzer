Artanthe flexicaulis

Artanthe flexicaulis, commonly known as the slender artanthe, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the family Amaranthaceae and is characterized by its upright growth habit and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or red, adding ornamental value to gardens. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Its botanical features make it an attractive and low-maintenance choice for native plant gardens.
