Arachnorchis douglasiorum

Arachnorchis douglasiorum, commonly known as the Douglas's spider orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Orchidaceae and is characterized by its unique, spider-like appearance due to its elongated, slender leaves and intricate floral structure. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as rainforests and understoreys. It produces small, greenish-white flowers that are often overlooked due to their subtle coloration.

Arachnorchis douglasiorum is a rare and protected species, highlighting its ecological significance and the need for conservation efforts.
