Asperula cynanchica var. tenuissima

Asperula cynanchica var. tenuissima, commonly known as slender woodruff, is a low-growing, evergreen shrub native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It features small, dark green, leathery leaves that are typically narrow and elongated. The plant produces clusters of delicate, white, bell-shaped flowers in early spring, which are often fragrant. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in woodlands or along stream banks.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is sometimes used in rock gardens or as ground cover.
