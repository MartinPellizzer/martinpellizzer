It is native to Europe, western Asia, and North Africa, and is characterized by its tall, erect stems and showy, velvety flowers that range in color from pink to red. Its leaves are heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers bloom in late spring to early summer.

Alcea tiliacea is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has been used historically in traditional medicine.
