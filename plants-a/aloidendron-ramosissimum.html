Aloidendron ramosissimum

Aloidendron ramosissimum, commonly known as the Natal aloes, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, spiny foliage and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. The plant produces yellow flowers in clusters, which are typically followed by red or orange gelatinous fruits. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Aloidendron ramosissimum is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional African medicine.
