Anacamptis × rayyana

Anacamptis × rayyana, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a natural hybrid between Anacamptis morio and Anacamptis pyramidalis.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant that produces tall, erect stems with multiple spike-like inflorescences. The flowers are typically pale pink to white, with a distinctive lip that is often marked with purple or brown. It thrives in sunny, well-drained habitats such as grasslands and open woodlands.

This plant is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
