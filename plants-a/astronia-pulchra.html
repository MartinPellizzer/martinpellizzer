Astronia pulchra

Astronia pulchra, commonly known as the velvetleaf or velvetleaf tree, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is a deciduous tree or shrub that can grow up to 10 meters tall, featuring smooth, greyish bark and opposite, ovate to elliptic leaves. The leaves are typically dark green on top and lighter underneath, with a velvety texture. It produces small, greenish-white flowers in clusters, followed by woody, capsule-like fruits containing numerous seeds.

This plant is often found in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests and is valued for its ornamental and medicinal properties.
