Archidendron yunnanense

Archidendron yunnanense, commonly known as the Yunnan legume, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to southwestern China.

It belongs to the Leguminosae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, pea-like flowers. The plant produces pod fruits that contain several seeds, and it is often found in moist, forested areas.

Its botanical features make it an important species in ecological studies and traditional medicine.
