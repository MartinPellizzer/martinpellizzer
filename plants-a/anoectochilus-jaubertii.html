Anoectochilus jaubertii

Anoectochilus jaubertii, commonly known as the orchid of the forest, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, strap-like leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as mountain forests. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is often found in epiphytic or lithophytic conditions.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is a subject of interest in both horticulture and botanical research.
