Arisaema lobatum

Arisaema lobatum, commonly known as the Chinese snake root, is a herbaceous plant native to eastern Asia.

It features a distinctive, spadix-shaped inflorescence surrounded by a large, hooded spathe. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. Its leaves are arrow-shaped with a pronounced lobed base, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Arisaema lobatum is valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
