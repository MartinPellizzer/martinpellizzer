Aspidium aculeatum var. batjanense

Aspidium aculeatum var. batjanense, commonly known as the Batjan's holly fern, is a variant of the holly fern genus Aspidium.

It is a perennial evergreen fern that typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often found in forest understories. The plant features lanceolate, serrated leaves that are dark green and leathery in texture. Its sporangia are clustered in sori on the underside of the fronds, a characteristic feature of ferns.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its attractive foliage.
