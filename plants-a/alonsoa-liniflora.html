Alonsoa liniflora

Alonsoa liniflora, commonly known as the silky alonsoa, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its slender, branched stems covered in fine, silky hairs. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded, woodland areas.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens, though it is not widely commercially grown.
