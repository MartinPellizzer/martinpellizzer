Alsophila villosa var. dusenii

Alsophila villosa var. dusenii, commonly known as the Dusen's tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and a crown of large, feather-like fronds that can reach several meters in length. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, typically found in tropical and subtropical rainforests. Its rhizome system supports its growth, allowing it to anchor firmly in moist, nutrient-rich soil.

The fronds are often covered in fine, silky hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
