Arundinella setosa var. esetosa

Arundinella setosa var. esetosa, commonly known as the slender bamboo reed, is a variant of the bamboo reed species native to southern China.

It is a perennial grass characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, linear leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in shaded to semi-shaded environments. Its inflorescence consists of a panicle of small, spikelet-like flowers, which are usually greenish or brownish in color.

This variant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
