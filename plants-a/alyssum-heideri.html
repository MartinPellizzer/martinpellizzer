Alyssum heideri

Alyssum heideri, commonly known as the German alyssum, is a low-growing, mat-forming perennial native to Europe.

It features small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in early summer. The plant has slender, branched stems and produces small, rounded leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Alyssum heideri is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
