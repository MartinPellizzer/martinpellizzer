Aron

Aron, commonly known as the aron plant or *Aronia* species, is a genus of flowering plants in the rose family.

These plants are native to North America and are characterized by their dark purple berries and compound leaves. Aron plants typically grow in moist, shady environments and are often found in woodlands or wetlands. They are valued for their ornamental appeal and their berries, which are rich in antioxidants.

The genus includes several species, such as *Aronia arbutifolia* and *Aronia melanocarpa*.
