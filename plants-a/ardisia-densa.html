Ardisia densa

Ardisia densa, commonly known as the glossy woodbine, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are typically elliptical in shape and have a leathery texture. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by bright red berries that are attractive to birds. Ardisia densa thrives in shady, moist environments and is often used in ornamental horticulture.

Its dense foliage and striking fruit make it a popular choice for garden landscapes.
