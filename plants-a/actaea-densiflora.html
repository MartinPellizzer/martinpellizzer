Actaea densiflora

Actaea densiflora, commonly known as the dense-flowered actaea, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to eastern North America.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and clusters of white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded woodlands and thrives in rich, organic soils. Its fruit is a follicle that contains numerous small, dark seeds.

Actaea densiflora is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
