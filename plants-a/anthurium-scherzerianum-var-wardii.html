Anthurium scherzerianum var. wardii

Anthurium scherzerianum var. wardii, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a tropical flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces vibrant red spathes that enclose its yellow spadix, creating a dramatic floral display. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

This variety is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and ease of care.
