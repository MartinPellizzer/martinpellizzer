Anacamptis × yvesii

Anacamptis × yvesii, commonly known as the Yves' orchid, is a hybrid orchid species belonging to the genus Anacamptis in the family Orchidaceae.

It is characterized by its tall, erect flowering stems that bear a cluster of fragrant, violet-blue flowers with yellow markings. The plant is native to southern Europe and thrives in calcareous soils, often found in meadows and grasslands. It is a perennial herb that produces pseudobulbs and has linear, grass-like leaves.

The species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
