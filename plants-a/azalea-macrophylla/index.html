Azalea Macrophylla

Azalea macrophylla is a striking evergreen shrub native to the mountainous regions of eastern Asia. Known for its large, showy flowers and lush green foliage, it is a popular choice among gardeners and horticulturists. This plant thrives in acidic, well-drained soil and requires partial to full sun for optimal growth. Its blooms, which come in shades of red, pink, and white, add a dramatic flair to any landscape. With proper care, Azalea macrophylla can flourish for many years, offering seasonal beauty and ecological benefits.
