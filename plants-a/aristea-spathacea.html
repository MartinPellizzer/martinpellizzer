Aristea spathacea

Aristea spathacea, commonly known as the Cape mountain grass, is a perennial plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Poaceae, which includes grasses, and is characterized by its tufted growth habit and dense, compact form. Aristea spathacea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly used in rock gardens and as a drought-tolerant landscape plant.
