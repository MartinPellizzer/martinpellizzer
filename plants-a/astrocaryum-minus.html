Astrocaryum minus

Astrocaryum minus, commonly known as the red palm or Brazilian red palm, is a species of palm native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its slender, unbranched trunk and large, pinnately compound leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by dark red, fleshy fruits. Its fruit is edible and has a sweet, tangy flavor, often consumed fresh or processed into beverages.

The plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its unique appearance.
