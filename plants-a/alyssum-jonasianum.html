Alyssum jonasianum

Alyssum jonasianum, commonly known as the Jonas's alyssum, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Alyssum jonasianum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
