Acacia haematostoma

Acacia haematostoma, commonly known as the red-stemmed acacia, is a species of flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in dry, rocky habitats. The plant is characterized by its slender, reddish stems and bipinnate leaves with small, needle-like leaflets. Its yellow flowers are clustered in cylindrical inflorescences and bloom during the warmer months.

Acacia haematostoma is valued for its hardiness and is often used in ornamental gardening.
