Aquilariella borneensis

Aquilariella borneensis, commonly known as the Bornean aquilaria, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to the tropical rainforests of Borneo, where it thrives in humid and shaded environments. The plant is characterized by its small, aromatic leaves and produces small white flowers. It is often found growing on tree trunks or as an epiphyte, showcasing its adaptability to various substrates.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered vulnerable and requires conservation efforts to ensure its survival.
