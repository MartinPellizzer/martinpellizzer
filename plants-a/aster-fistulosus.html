Aster fistulosus

Aster fistulosus, commonly known as the slender aster, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and clusters of small, usually blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as prairies and disturbed soils. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a hairy texture.

Aster fistulosus is a member of the Asteraceae family, known for its composite flower heads and ecological importance to pollinators.
