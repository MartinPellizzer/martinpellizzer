Agave ensifera

Agave ensifera, commonly known as the sword agave, is a striking plant native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its tall, narrow, and sword-shaped leaves that are often blue-green in color. The plant produces a tall flowering stalk that can reach up to 10 feet in height, bearing white or yellow flowers. It is a perennial succulent that thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.

Agave ensifera is also valued for its use in traditional medicine and as an ornamental plant.
