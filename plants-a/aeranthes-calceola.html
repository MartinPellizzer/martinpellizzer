Aeranthes calceola

Aeranthes calceola, commonly known as the miniature orchid or the little shoe orchid, is a small, terrestrial orchid native to tropical regions of South America.

It features a compact growth habit with a single, short stem and a cluster of small, greenish-white flowers. The plant has a rhizomatous root system and is often found growing in shaded, moist environments. Its flowers are typically short-lived, opening in the morning and closing by midday.

Aeranthes calceola is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouse settings due to its specific care requirements.
