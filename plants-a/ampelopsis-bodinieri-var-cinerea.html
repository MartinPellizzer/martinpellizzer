Ampelopsis bodinieri var. cinerea

Ampelopsis bodinieri var. cinerea, commonly known as silver vine, is a climbing vine native to East Asia.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive silvery-gray underside, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that develop into red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and is often used in ornamental landscaping.

Its foliage is popular as a cat toy due to its aromatic properties.
