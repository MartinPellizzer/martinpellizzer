Alyssum obovatum subsp. paradoxum

Alyssum obovatum subsp.

paradoxum, commonly known as the rock alissum, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region. It features small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in spring.

The plant has compact, mat-forming foliage with silvery-green leaves that are covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal environments.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscaping.
