Adenophora stenanthina subsp. stenanthina

Adenophora stenanthina subsp.

stenanthina, commonly known as the narrow-flowered bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to eastern Asia. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

The plant features lance-shaped leaves and bell-shaped flowers that are usually blue to purple in color. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting local wildlife.
