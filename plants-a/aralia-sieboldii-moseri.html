Aralia sieboldii moseri

Aralia sieboldii moseri, commonly known as the Japanese aralia, is a deciduous shrub native to Japan.

It features compound leaves that are typically palmately lobed, with a deep green color and a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers in umbels, which are followed by dark blue berries. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained, moist soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
