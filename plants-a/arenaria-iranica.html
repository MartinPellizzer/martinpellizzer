Arenaria iranica

Arenaria iranica, commonly known as Persian sandflower, is a flowering plant native to Iran.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming structure. The plant produces small, white or pinkish flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky soils and open fields.

Its adaptability to harsh conditions makes it an important species in ecological restoration projects.
