Ardisia caudatifolia

Ardisia caudatifolia, commonly known as the flame tree or red flame tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It features attractive, lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green, often with a slight reddish tinge. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences, followed by bright red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it a popular ornamental plant.

Its botanical characteristics make it well-suited for garden landscapes in warm climates.
