Aristolochia liukiuensis

Aristolochia liukiuensis, commonly known as the Ryukyu pipevine, is a flowering plant native to the Ryukyu Islands of Japan.

It is characterized by its distinctive, tubular, and often heart-shaped leaves, which are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are usually purple or greenish in color, with a complex, winding structure. It belongs to the Aristolochiaceae family and is known for its ornamental value in gardens.

However, it contains toxic compounds and should be handled with care.
