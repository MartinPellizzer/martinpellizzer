Albizia minyi

Albizia minyi, commonly known as the Chinese silk tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant is notable for its fragrant, fluffy flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Albizia minyi typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping and garden use.
