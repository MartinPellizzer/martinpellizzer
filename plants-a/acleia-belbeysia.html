Acleia belbeysia

Acleia belbeysia, commonly known as the Belbeys' blueweed, is a flowering plant native to the British Isles.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in damp, grassy areas and has hairy, lance-shaped leaves. It is a short-lived perennial that blooms from June to August.

Due to its limited distribution and rarity, it is considered a protected species in the UK.
