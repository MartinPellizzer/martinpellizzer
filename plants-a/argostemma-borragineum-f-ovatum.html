Argostemma borragineum f. ovatum

Argostemma borragineum f. ovatum, commonly known as the ovate leaf argostemma, is a variant of the argostemma genus native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its ovate-shaped leaves and clusters of small, blue-violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forests or along stream banks. Its flowers are often visited by pollinators like bees and butterflies.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
