Arthrocnemum belangerianum

Arthrocnemum belangerianum, commonly known as the desert sandmat, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of Africa and the Middle East.

It is a low-growing shrub with small, fleshy leaves adapted to conserve water in harsh environments. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in the spring, contributing to its ecological role in stabilizing sandy soils.
