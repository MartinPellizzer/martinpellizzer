Ajania hypoleuca

Ajania hypoleuca, commonly known as the white ajania, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in dense clusters, which bloom in late summer. Its leaves are narrow, grass-like, and covered in a fine layer of white hairs.

Ajania hypoleuca is often found in rocky or alpine environments, where it thrives in well-drained soils.
