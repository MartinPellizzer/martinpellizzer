Agathosma mollis

Agathosma mollis, commonly known as Cape garlic or wild thyme, is a small evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It features aromatic, needle-like leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Agathosma mollis is valued for its essential oils and has been used in aromatherapy and as a medicinal herb.
