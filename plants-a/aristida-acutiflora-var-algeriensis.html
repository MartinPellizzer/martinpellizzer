Aristida acutiflora var. algeriensis

Aristida acutiflora var. algeriensis, commonly known as Algerian needlegrass, is a flowering plant native to North Africa.

It is a perennial grass that typically grows in arid and semi-arid environments. The plant features slender, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. Its flowers are usually pale green to brown, and it is adapted to withstand drought conditions.

This variety is often found in sandy soils and is used in ecological restoration projects.
