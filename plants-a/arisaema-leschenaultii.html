Arisaema leschenaultii

Arisaema leschenaultii, commonly known as the Leschenault's arisaema, is a member of the Araceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Thailand, Vietnam, and Cambodia. The plant features a distinctive inflorescence consisting of a spadix surrounded by a modified leaf called a spathe. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and can grow up to 30 cm in diameter.

Arisaema leschenaultii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
