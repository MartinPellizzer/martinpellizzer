Areca dransfieldii

Areca dransfieldii, commonly known as the Areca palm, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like leaves that give it a distinctive, elegant appearance. The plant belongs to the Arecaceae family and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in both indoor and outdoor settings. Its leaves are typically a deep green, and it can grow up to 10 meters in height under optimal conditions.

Areca dransfieldii prefers well-draining soil and bright, indirect light to thrive.
