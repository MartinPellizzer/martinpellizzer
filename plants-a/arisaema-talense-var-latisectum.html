Arisaema talense var. latisectum

Arisaema talense var. latisectum, commonly known as the broad-lobed arrowhead, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its unique inflorescence consisting of a spadix surrounded by a spathe. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with broad, lobed margins, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardeners and horticulturists.
