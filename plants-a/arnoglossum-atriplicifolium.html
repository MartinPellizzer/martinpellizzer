Arnoglossum atriplicifolium

Arnoglossum atriplicifolium, commonly known as the purple coneflower, is a perennial herb native to North America.

It features showy, violet-purple flower heads that are surrounded by narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant has a robust root system and can grow up to 2 meters tall. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly rough texture.

It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for native gardens.
