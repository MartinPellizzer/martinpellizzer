Alsophila armata var. menziesii

Alsophila armata var. menziesii, commonly known as the Menzies' tree fern, is a species native to the Pacific Islands, particularly Hawaii.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and a crown of feathery, compound fronds that can reach several meters in length. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in coastal and lowland forests. Its rhizome is thick and woody, supporting the growth of the fronds above.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in its native habitat.
