Aporum undulatum

Aporum undulatum, commonly known as the wavy aporum, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, waxy, undulating leaves that can grow up to several feet in length. The leaves are typically a deep green with a slightly variegated pattern, adding visual interest to any setting. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture to maintain its lush appearance.

Due to its unique foliage, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and ornamental purposes.
