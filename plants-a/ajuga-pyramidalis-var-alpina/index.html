Ajuga Pyramidalis Var. Alpina

Ajuga pyramidalis var. alpina is a hardy perennial plant known for its vibrant blue to purple flowers and dense, mat-forming growth habit. It thrives in shaded to partially shaded environments and is commonly found in alpine and woodland settings. This variety is prized for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in rocky or poor soils. Its low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking a resilient ground cover. The plant's ability to spread and form a lush carpet also makes it ideal for erosion control in natural landscapes.
