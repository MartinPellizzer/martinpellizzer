Alstonia plumosa

Alstonia plumosa, commonly known as the flame tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, pendulous inflorescences that produce clusters of small, yellow-orange flowers. The tree has glossy, dark green leaves that are opposite and often have a waxy texture. Its bark is smooth and pale, with a distinctive flaky appearance.

Alstonia plumosa is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often planted in gardens and as a street tree in warm climates.
