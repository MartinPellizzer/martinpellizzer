Alafia intermedia

Alafia intermedia, commonly known as the spider plant, is a herbaceous perennial native to Africa.

It features long, arching stems with small, narrow leaves that are typically variegated with white and green. The plant produces clusters of white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in spring. It is often grown as a houseplant due to its adaptability and ease of care.

Alafia intermedia is also noted for its ability to thrive in low light conditions and its air-purifying properties.
