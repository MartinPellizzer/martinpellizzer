Anisacantha diacantha

Anisacantha diacantha, commonly known as the spiked nightshade, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in clusters, often in spring and summer. Its leaves are oval-shaped with a rough texture and a strong, unpleasant odor.

The fruit is a small, hard capsule that contains several seeds, which are dispersed by wind or animals.
