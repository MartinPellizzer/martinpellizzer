Areca tigillaria

Areca tigillaria, commonly known as the Chinese areca or the fan palm, is a species of flowering plant in the Arecaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that develop into dark blue, fleshy fruits. Areca tigillaria is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.

Its growth habit makes it a popular choice for tropical gardens and as a houseplant.
