Astrocaryum guianense

Astrocaryum guianense, commonly known as the Guianese palm, is a tropical plant native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is a tall, slender palm that can grow up to 20 meters in height, with pinnately arranged, feather-like leaves. The plant produces large, yellowish-orange fruits that are encased in a hard, spiky shell. Its trunk is typically straight and covered with a smooth, pale bark.

Astrocaryum guianense is valued for its ornamental appearance and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
