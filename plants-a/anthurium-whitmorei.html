Anthurium whitmorei

Anthurium whitmorei, commonly known as the White More's anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a glossy texture. The plant produces striking spadix flowers surrounded by a colorful spathe, often in shades of red or white. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium whitmorei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated as a houseplant.
