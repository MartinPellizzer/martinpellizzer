Artanthe Colubrina

Artanthe colubrina is a striking perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of South America. It is known for its vibrant, showy flowers that bloom in shades of purple and pink throughout the summer months. This plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders. Its unique foliage and long-lasting blooms add both visual interest and ecological value to the landscape. Artanthe colubrina is not only beautiful but also attracts a variety of pollinators, enhancing biodiversity in gardens.
