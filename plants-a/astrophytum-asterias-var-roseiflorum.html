Astrophytum asterias var. roseiflorum

Astrophytum asterias var. roseiflorum, commonly known as the rose-flowered star cactus, is a compact, globular cactus native to Mexico.

It features a short, thick stem with radial spines and produces vibrant pink or red flowers during the spring. The plant has a unique star-shaped pattern on its surface, giving it its common name. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with plenty of sunlight.

Its slow growth rate makes it an ideal choice for rock gardens or as a container plant.
