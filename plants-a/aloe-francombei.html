Aloe francombei

Aloe francombei, commonly known as the Cape aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, cylindrical stems that are segmented and covered in a layer of white, waxy bloom. The plant produces striking, showy inflorescences of yellow or orange flowers during the summer months. Its leaves are thick, fleshy, and often have a bluish-green hue with marginal teeth.

Aloe francombei is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
