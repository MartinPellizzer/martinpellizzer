Anthericum unyamweziense

Anthericum unyamweziense, commonly known as the Nyamwezi lily, is a rare and endemic plant species native to the Eastern African region, particularly Tanzania.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its tall, slender stems and large, showy white or pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories or along stream banks. Its leaves are broad, lance-shaped, and often have a waxy coating, which helps in water retention.
