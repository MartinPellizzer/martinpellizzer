Achimenes argyrostigma

Achimenes argyrostigma, commonly known as the silver-streaked achimenes, is a tropical perennial plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, pink, and white. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery down, giving it its common name. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as a ornamental plant.

Achimenes argyrostigma is known for its ability to produce multiple blooms throughout the growing season.
