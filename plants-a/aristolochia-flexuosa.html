Aristolochia flexuosa

Aristolochia flexuosa, commonly known as the flexible Dutchman's pipe, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its unique, winding, pipe-shaped flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color. The plant produces heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and emits a mild, aromatic scent. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found along stream banks and in woodland areas.

The species is also notable for its medicinal uses in traditional herbal practices.
