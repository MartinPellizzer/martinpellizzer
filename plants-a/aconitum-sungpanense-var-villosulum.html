Aconitum sungpanense var. villosulum

Aconitum sungpanense var. villosulum, commonly known as the hairy goat's beard, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its tall, sparsely branched stems that can reach up to 2 meters in height. The plant features large, showy blue to purple flowers arranged in a spire-like inflorescence, which bloom in late summer. Its leaves are palmately compound, with lobes that are often covered in fine, soft hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

This variant is noted for its hairy foliage and is often found in moist, shaded environments in its natural habitat.
