Aeschynomene fructipendula

Aeschynomene fructipendula, commonly known as the fruit-bearing aeshynomene, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

The plant produces small, clustered pods that contain numerous tiny seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It has trifoliate leaves and is often found in disturbed soils, making it a pioneer species in certain ecosystems.
