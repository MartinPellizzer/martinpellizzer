Alsophila mertensii

Alsophila mertensii, commonly known as the Mertens' tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feathery fronds that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant has a distinctive crown of sterile fronds that form a dense, umbrella-like shape. Its rhizome is thick and fibrous, supporting the growth of the trunk and roots.

Alsophila mertensii is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and along riverbanks.
