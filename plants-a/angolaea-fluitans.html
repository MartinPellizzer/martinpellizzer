Angolaea fluitans

Angolaea fluitans, commonly known as the floating angel's tears, is a rare aquatic plant native to the freshwater ecosystems of South America.

The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, often in clusters at the water's surface. Its roots are adapted to absorb nutrients from the water, allowing it to thrive in still or slow-moving waters.
