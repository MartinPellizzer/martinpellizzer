Angelonia coffreana

Angelonia coffreana, commonly known as the tropical angelonia, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is a member of the Plantaginaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in a fine layer of hair, while its flowers are borne in clusters at the ends of branches.

Angelonia coffreana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its long blooming period.
