Aneilema lanceolatum var. evolutius

Aneilema lanceolatum var. evolutius, commonly known as the broadleaf arrowroot, is a tropical plant native to Africa.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped, broad leaves that are often tinged with red or purple at the base. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or white in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Its rhizomes are used in traditional medicine and as a source of starch.
