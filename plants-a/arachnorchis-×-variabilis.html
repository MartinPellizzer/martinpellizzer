Arachnorchis × variabilis

Arachnorchis × variabilis, commonly known as the variable spider orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to Australia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its intricate, spider-like floral structure. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in rainforest or woodland areas. Its flowers are usually pale green or yellow with dark markings, and it produces a unique, fragrant scent to attract pollinators.

Arachnorchis × variabilis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens with the right conditions.
