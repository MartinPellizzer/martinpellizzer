Acalypha estrellana

Acalypha estrellana, commonly known as Estrellana or the Mexican ironwood, is a flowering plant native to Central America and parts of Mexico.

It is a small to medium-sized shrub or tree that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features simple, opposite leaves that are usually ovate to elliptical in shape. Its flowers are small and clustered in dense inflorescences, often with vibrant colors that attract pollinators.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
