Agrimonia pilosa var. glabrata

Agrimonia pilosa var. glabrata, commonly known as smooth agrimony, is a perennial herb native to North America.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and serrated leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. Its fruits are dry, capsule-like structures that release tiny seeds when mature.

Agrimonia pilosa var. glabrata is often found in open woods, meadows, and along roadsides, where it thrives in well-drained soils.
