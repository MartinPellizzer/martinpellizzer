Astoma seselifolium

Astoma seselifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaved astoma, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are narrow and often hairy, and its flowers are small, yellowish, and arranged in a flat-topped cluster.

Astoma seselifolium is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in rock gardens.
