Arenaria roylei

Arenaria roylei, commonly known as the Kashmir sandwort, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayan region.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that thrives in rocky and alpine environments. The plant features small, white or pinkish flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. Its leaves are typically succulent and adapted to conserve water in arid conditions.

Arenaria roylei plays a role in stabilizing soil in its native habitat and is of interest to botanists studying alpine flora.
