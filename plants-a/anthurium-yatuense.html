Anthurium yatuense

Anthurium yatuense, commonly known as the Yatuense flamingo flower, is a tropical plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves and vibrant, spadix-like inflorescence. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its foliage is glossy and can reach up to 30 centimeters in length, while the spathe is often a deep red or orange color.

Anthurium yatuense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for decorative purposes in indoor and greenhouse settings.
