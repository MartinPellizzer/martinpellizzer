Acer negundo f. densiflorum

Acer negundo f. densiflorum, commonly known as the Chinese silver maple, is a cultivar of the silver maple tree.

It is characterized by its dense, finely divided leaves that give it a delicate, feathery appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in early spring, which are clustered in drooping catkins. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in riparian zones.

This ornamental tree is valued for its aesthetic appeal and adaptability to various environmental conditions.
