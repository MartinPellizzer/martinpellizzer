Aframomum kayserianum

Aframomum kayserianum, commonly known as the African cardamom, is a perennial herb native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the ginger family (Zingiberaceae) and is characterized by its aromatic, branched rhizomes. The plant produces narrow, lance-shaped leaves and small, pale yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its rhizomes are valued for their medicinal properties and are used in traditional medicine.
