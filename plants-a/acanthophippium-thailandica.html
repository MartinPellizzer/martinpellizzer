Acanthophippium thailandica

Acanthophippium thailandica, commonly known as the Thai thorned milkweed, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia, particularly Thailand.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its woody stems and milky sap. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically pink or red in color. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in moist, well-drained soils.
