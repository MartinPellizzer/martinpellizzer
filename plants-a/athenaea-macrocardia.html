Athenaea macrocardia

Athenaea macrocardia, commonly known as the large-heart aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with showy, orange-red flowers that attract pollinators. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their soothing properties.

The species is valued in horticulture for its resilience and ornamental appeal.
