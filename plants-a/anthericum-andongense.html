Anthericum andongense

Anthericum andongense, commonly known as the Andong arum, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Korea.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive inflorescence consisting of a spadix surrounded by a spathe. The plant features large, heart-shaped leaves that are often prominently veined. Its flowers are unisexual, with separate male and female parts, typical of the arum family.

Anthericum andongense is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or wetlands.
