Astracantha kirshehirica

Astracantha kirshehirica, commonly known as the Kırşehir thornvetch, is a flowering plant native to Turkey.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its spiny stems and small, pea-like flowers. The plant typically grows in arid and semi-arid regions, adapting to harsh environmental conditions.

Its seeds are enclosed in pods and can remain viable in the soil for several years, contributing to its persistence in its native habitat.
