Agrostis Stolonifera Var. Scabriglumis

Scabrous bentgrass, scientifically known as Agrostis stolonifera var. scabriglumis, is a lesser-known grass species that thrives in cool, moist environments. This variant of bentgrass is distinguished by its rough, scabrous texture and distinctive leaf structure. It is commonly found in wet meadows, stream banks, and other damp habitats across temperate regions. Despite its modest appearance, it plays a vital role in stabilizing soil and supporting local biodiversity. Its resilience and adaptability make it an important subject for ecological studies and conservation efforts.
