Aulomyrcia bolivarensis

Aulomyrcia bolivarensis, commonly known as the Bolivian myrcia, is a species of flowering plant native to the Andean region of Bolivia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family, which includes eucalyptus and other aromatic plants. The plant is characterized by its small, white or pink flowers and glossy, elliptical leaves. It thrives in humid, shaded environments at high altitudes.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
