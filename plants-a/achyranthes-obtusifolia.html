Achyranthes obtusifolia

Achyranthes obtusifolia, commonly known as blunt-leaved smartweed, is a herbaceous plant native to the Americas.

It typically grows in moist soils along streams, marshes, and wetlands, preferring full sun to partial shade. The plant has alternate, ovate to lanceolate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its small, clustered flowers are usually pink to purple, appearing in late summer to early fall.

The plant is known for its fibrous roots and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
