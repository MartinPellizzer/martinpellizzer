Arisaema tengtsungense var. pentaphyllum

Arisaema tengtsungense var. pentaphyllum, commonly known as the five-lobed arisaema, is a species native to China.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its distinctive, palmate leaves and spadix inflorescence. The plant produces yellowish-green spadix with small, clustered flowers, typical of the Araceae family. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens.
