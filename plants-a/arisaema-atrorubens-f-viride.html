Arisaema atrorubens f. viride

Arisaema atrorubens f. viride, commonly known as the green-flowered arrowhead, is a variant of the arrowhead plant characterized by its distinct greenish flowers.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is native to parts of Southeast Asia. The plant features a short, stout rhizome with broad, arrow-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green. Its inflorescence consists of a spadix enclosed by a modified leaf called a spathe, which is often a lighter green than the rest of the plant.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often grown in shaded garden environments.
