Antholyza ringens

Antholyza ringens, commonly known as the silverleaf daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray leaves and bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in well-drained soils and prefers full sun exposure. Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Antholyza ringens is also valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
