Alyssum halimifolium

Alyssum halimifolium, commonly known as coastal alyssum, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in spring and summer. The plant has hairy, silver-green foliage that helps it retain moisture in arid environments. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal and rocky garden settings.

Alyssum halimifolium is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
