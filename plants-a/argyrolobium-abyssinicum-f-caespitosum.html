Argyrolobium abyssinicum f. caespitosum

Argyrolobium abyssinicum f. caespitosum, commonly known as the Abyssinian silver globular veldt, is a low-growing perennial plant native to Ethiopia.

It features dense, compact foliage with silver-gray leaves that form a mat-like structure. The plant produces small, yellow flowers in spring, which are clustered in spherical inflorescences. It is well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, thriving in well-drained soils.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance in landscaping.
