Adonis microcarpa var. discolor

Adonis microcarpa var. discolor, commonly known as the variegated dwarf adonis, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is a member of the Ranunculaceae family, characterized by its showy, yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens, though it is considered rare in the wild.
