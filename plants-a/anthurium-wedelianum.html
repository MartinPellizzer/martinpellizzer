Anthurium wedelianum

Anthurium wedelianum, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces vibrant red spadices, which are the inflorescences that resemble flowers. It thrives in humid, warm environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium wedelianum is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and air-purifying properties.
