Athrixia rosmarinifolia var. rosmarinifolia

Athrixia rosmarinifolia var. rosmarinifolia, commonly known as the Cape rosemary, is a low-growing, evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It features small, needle-like leaves that are dark green and covered in a fine, silvery pubescence. The plant produces small, pale blue to violet flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. Its aromatic foliage gives off a slight minty scent when crushed.

It is well adapted to rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun.
