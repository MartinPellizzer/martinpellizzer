Achyrocline alata var. vauthieriana

Achyrocline alata var. vauthieriana, commonly known as the Brazilian aster, is a flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and has a compact, bushy habit. Its leaves are narrow and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are erect and often branched.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
