Abutilon eufigarii

Abutilon eufigarii, commonly known as the silky abutilon, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its soft, fuzzy leaves and showy, lantern-shaped yellow flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for tropical gardens and indoor plant displays.
