Aralia shangiana

Aralia shangiana, commonly known as the Chinese aralia, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern China.

It features compound leaves that are pinnately arranged, with long, slender leaflets that give it a graceful appearance. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers in umbel-like clusters, which are followed by dark blue berries. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a suitable choice for woodland gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its attractive foliage and unique fruiting habit.
