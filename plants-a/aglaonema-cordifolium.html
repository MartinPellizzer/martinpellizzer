Aglaonema cordifolium

Aglaonema cordifolium, commonly known as the Chinese Evergreen, is a tropical evergreen plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features striking, heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with shades of white, green, and pink. The plant is well-known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening. Its foliage is not only aesthetically pleasing but also contributes to air purification.

Aglaonema cordifolium is relatively low maintenance, requiring minimal watering and occasional fertilization to maintain its vibrant appearance.
