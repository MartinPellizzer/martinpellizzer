Aralia lantsangensis

Aralia lantsangensis, commonly known as the Yunnan aralia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to the mountainous regions of southern China.

It belongs to the Araliaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves, which are often palmately divided with a prominent midrib. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in umbels, followed by edible, red berries that are an important food source for various wildlife.

It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in its native habitat.
