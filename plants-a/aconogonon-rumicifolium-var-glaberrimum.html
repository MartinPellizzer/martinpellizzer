Aconogonon rumicifolium var. glaberrimum

Aconogonon rumicifolium var. glaberrimum, commonly known as the smoothleaf aconogonon, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and is characterized by its basal rosette of smooth, glabrous leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in dense, spike-like inflorescences. It is often found in open, disturbed habitats such as grasslands and along roadsides.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens in regions with a warm climate.
