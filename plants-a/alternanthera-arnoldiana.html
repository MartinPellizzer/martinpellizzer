Alternanthera arnoldiana

Alternanthera arnoldiana, commonly known as the red-leaf alternanthera, is a striking aquatic plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red foliage, which becomes more intense under bright light and nutrient-rich conditions. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, waxy coating. It thrives in moderate to high light environments and prefers soft to moderately hard water with a slightly acidic to neutral pH.

Alternanthera arnoldiana is popular in freshwater aquariums for its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.
