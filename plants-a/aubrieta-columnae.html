Aubrieta columnae

Aubrieta columnae, commonly known as the alpine aubrieta, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces clusters of bright yellow flowers in early spring. The plant has compact, mat-forming growth habit with small, silver-gray foliage. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and alpine settings.

Aubrieta columnae is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in cooler climates.
