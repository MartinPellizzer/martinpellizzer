Argyrolobium goodioides

Argyrolobium goodioides, commonly known as the Good's silver globular pea, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a low-growing, perennial herb with a distinctive silver-gray foliage that reflects light. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.

Due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
