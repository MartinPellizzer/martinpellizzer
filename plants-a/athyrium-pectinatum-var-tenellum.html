Athyrium pectinatum var. tenellum

Athyrium pectinatum var. tenellum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf Japanese painted fern, is a variant of the Japanese painted fern characterized by its slender, gracefully arching fronds.

It features dark green, serrated leaves with a delicate, feathery appearance, often displaying a subtle variegation of lighter green along the margins. This fern thrives in shaded, moist environments and is prized for its ornamental value in both garden and indoor settings. It prefers well-drained, acidic soil and moderate temperatures, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens.

The plant is relatively low maintenance, requiring minimal pruning and regular watering to maintain its lush, vibrant foliage.
