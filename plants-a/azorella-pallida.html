Azorella pallida

Azorella pallida, commonly known as the pale azorella, is a member of the Apiaceae family and is native to the Andean regions of South America.

Azorella pallida is adapted to nutrient-poor soils and can endure harsh climatic conditions, making it a resilient species in its native habitat.
