Actinidia zhejiangensis

Actinidia zhejiangensis, commonly known as the Zhejiang actinidia, is a deciduous vine native to China.

It belongs to the Actinidiaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant produces edible berries that are often used in traditional Chinese medicine. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in shaded, moist environments.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is occasionally cultivated in horticulture.
