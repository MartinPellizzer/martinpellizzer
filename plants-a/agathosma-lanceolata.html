Agathosma lanceolata

Agathosma lanceolata, commonly known as African geranium, is a perennial shrub native to southern Africa.

It features small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in spring. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It is well adapted to arid environments and thrives in well-drained soils.

Agathosma lanceolata is also valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
