Aloe cylindracea var. rigida

Aloe cylindracea var. rigida, commonly known as the rigid aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features upright, cylindrical leaves that are typically green with a reddish tinge, especially when exposed to full sun. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow flowers during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental leaves make it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.
