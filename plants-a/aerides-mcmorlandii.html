Aerides mcmorlandii

Aerides mcmorlandii, commonly known as the McMorland's orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, moist environments. The plant features a sympodial growth habit with pseudobulbs and linear leaves. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish with a distinctive red or orange lip.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
