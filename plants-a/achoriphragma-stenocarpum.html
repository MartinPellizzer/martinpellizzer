Achoriphragma stenocarpum

Achoriphragma stenocarpum, commonly known as the narrow-seeded acorid, is a member of the Acoraceae family.

It is a small aquatic or semi-aquatic plant characterized by its long, ribbon-like leaves that float on the water surface. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or brown in color. It is native to parts of Southeast Asia and is often found in still or slow-moving freshwater habitats.
