Arthrosolen gnidioides

Arthrosolen gnidioides, commonly known as the Indian sandalwood.

It is a small evergreen tree native to southern India and Sri Lanka, characterized by its aromatic wood and distinctive foliage. The plant has small, elliptical leaves that are glossy and dark green, often with a slight waxy coating. Its flowers are pale yellow and appear in clusters, followed by small, woody fruits.

The tree is valued for its timber and has traditional uses in perfumery and medicine.
