Aristolochia binhthuanensis

Aristolochia binhthuanensis, commonly known as Binh Thuan arrowhead, is a flowering plant native to Vietnam.

It belongs to the Aristolochiaceae family and is characterized by its unique, tubular flowers that resemble a heart shape. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are often clustered in inflorescences. Its leaves are heart-shaped and have a rough texture, while the stems are woody and branched.

This species is of interest in botanical studies due to its medicinal potential and ecological significance.
