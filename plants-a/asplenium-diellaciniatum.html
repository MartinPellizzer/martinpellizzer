Asplenium diellaciniatum

Asplenium diellaciniatum, commonly known as the creeping spleenwort, is a small, evergreen fern belonging to the family Aspleniaceae.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense, dark green fronds that often form a mat-like structure. The plant typically features small, lance-shaped leaves with a smooth or slightly toothed margin. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often growing on rocks or soil in temperate regions.

Asplenium diellaciniatum is a hardy species that is well-suited for woodland gardens or as a ground cover in shaded areas.
