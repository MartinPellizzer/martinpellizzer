Anagyris sinensis

Anagyris sinensis, commonly known as the Chinese milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to China.

It is a perennial legume characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant produces small, elongated pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in dry, arid environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.

Anagyris sinensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it beneficial for ecological restoration projects.
