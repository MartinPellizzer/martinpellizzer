Adenia cardiocarpa

Adenia cardiocarpa, commonly known as the heart-shaped adenia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive heart-shaped leaves and clusters of yellowish-green flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. It thrives in tropical and subtropical regions with well-drained soil and partial shade.

The fruit is a fleshy, red berry that contains several seeds.
