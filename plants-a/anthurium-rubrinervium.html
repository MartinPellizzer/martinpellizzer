Anthurium rubrinervium

Anthurium rubrinervium, commonly known as the red-veined anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves with prominent red veins, which give it its common name. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-shaped inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, typically in shades of red or orange. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Anthurium rubrinervium is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
