Adenophora Polymorpha Var. Divaricata

Blue flax, scientifically known as Adenophora polymorpha var. divaricata, is a striking perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of East Asia. It is celebrated for its vivid blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer, adding a dramatic touch to gardens and natural landscapes. This plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a versatile choice for both wild and cultivated environments. Its unique appearance and ecological benefits have made it a favorite among botanists and gardeners alike. With its delicate yet resilient nature, Blue flax stands out as a remarkable addition to any plant collection.
