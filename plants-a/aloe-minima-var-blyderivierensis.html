Aloe minima var. blyderivierensis

Aloe minima var. blyderivierensis, commonly known as the Blyderivier aloe, is a small, compact succulent native to South Africa.

It features short, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a pale blue or grayish hue, often adorned with white or pale yellow marginal teeth. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid or semi-arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
