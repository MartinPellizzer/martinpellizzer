Artemisia remosa

Artemisia remosa, commonly known as the slender wormwood, is a perennial herb native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its aromatic, gray-green foliage and small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant thrives in dry, sunny environments and is often found in rocky soils. Its leaves contain volatile oils, which contribute to its strong, bitter scent.

Artemisia remosa is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
