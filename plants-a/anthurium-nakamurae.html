Anthurium nakamurae

Anthurium nakamurae, commonly known as the Nakamura's anthurium, is a species of flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Araceae family, which is characterized by its distinctive inflorescence consisting of a spadix and a spathe. The plant produces striking, colorful spathe that can range from red to orange, often with a contrasting green or yellow base. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Anthurium nakamurae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in tropical gardens.
