Allium Erdelii Var. Genuinum

Allium erdelii var. genuinum is a rare and striking plant native to the Carpathian Mountains. Known for its vibrant purple flowers and unique bulb structure, it stands out among other members of the Allium family. This variety is particularly valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological significance. Due to habitat loss and climate change, it faces increasing threats to its survival. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this beautiful and endangered species.
