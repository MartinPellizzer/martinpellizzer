Aubrieta deltoidea var. cithaeronea

Aubrieta deltoidea var. cithaeronea, commonly known as the Cretan aubrieta, is a compact, mat-forming perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, adding a vibrant splash of color to gardens. The plant has dark green, ovate leaves that are slightly hairy, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice for xeriscaping and coastal gardens.
