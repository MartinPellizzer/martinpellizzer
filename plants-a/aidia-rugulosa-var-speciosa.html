Aidia rugulosa var. speciosa

Aidia rugulosa var. speciosa, commonly known as the showy aidia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, showy inflorescences that consist of numerous small, tubular flowers clustered in a dense, rounded head. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly habitats and prefers full sun to partial shade. Its foliage is often covered in a fine, velvety pubescence, giving it a soft, textured appearance.

The species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in dry conditions.
