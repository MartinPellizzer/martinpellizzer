Alsine tenuifolia var. velenovskyi

Alsine tenuifolia var. velenovskyi, commonly known as the narrowleaf sandmat, is a flowering plant belonging to the Amaranthaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, delicate leaves and clusters of tiny, greenish flowers that bloom in summer. Native to arid and semi-arid regions, this plant is well adapted to dry conditions and poor soils. It typically grows close to the ground, forming dense mats that help prevent soil erosion.

The plant is often found in sandy or gravelly habitats across parts of Europe and Asia.
