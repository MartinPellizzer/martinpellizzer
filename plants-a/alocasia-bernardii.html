Alocasia bernardii

Alocasia bernardii, commonly known as the Black Velvet plant, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features large, dark green, velvety leaves with intricate patterns and a glossy texture. The plant has a short, stout stem and produces small, white flowers that are rarely seen. It thrives in humid environments with bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil.

Alocasia bernardii is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
