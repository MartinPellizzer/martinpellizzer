Anthurium caripense

Anthurium caripense, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves and vibrant red spadix surrounded by a spathe. The plant belongs to the Araceae family, which is known for its unique inflorescence structure. Anthurium caripense thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect sunlight for optimal growth.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and ornamental plant collections.
