Achilleanthus schmutzii

Achilleanthus schmutzii, commonly known as the Schmutzii achillea, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features showy yellow ray flowers surrounding a central disk of smaller florets. It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.

Its botanical structure includes narrow, linear leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
