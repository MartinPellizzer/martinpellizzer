Alchemilla ayazii

Alchemilla ayazii, commonly known as Ayaz's lady's mantle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Turkey.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its basal rosette of silver-green, hairy leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or rocky habitats.

Alchemilla ayazii is valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional medicinal uses.
