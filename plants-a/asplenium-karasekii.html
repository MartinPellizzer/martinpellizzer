Asplenium karasekii

Asplenium karasekii, commonly known as the Karasek's spleenwort, is a species of fern belonging to the family Aspleniaceae.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that typically grows on rocks or tree trunks in shaded, humid environments. The plant has delicate, lance-shaped leaves that are often clustered at the base, with a distinctive frond structure. It is characterized by its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and its preference for moist, well-drained substrates.

Asplenium karasekii is native to parts of Southeast Asia and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.
