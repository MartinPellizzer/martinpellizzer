Alsophila baronii

Alsophila baronii, commonly known as the Baron's tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds that can reach several meters in length. The plant has a distinctive crown of pinnate fronds that give it a graceful, tropical appearance. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Alsophila baronii plays an important ecological role in its habitat by providing shelter and food for various wildlife species.
