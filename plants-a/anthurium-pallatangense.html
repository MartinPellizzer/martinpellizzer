Anthurium pallatangense

Anthurium pallatangense, commonly known as the Pallatanga arrowroot, is a tropical plant native to Colombia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves with a vibrant green color and prominent veins. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically hidden beneath a colorful spathe. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Anthurium pallatangense is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is appreciated in horticultural collections for its unique foliage.
