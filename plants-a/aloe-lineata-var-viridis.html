Aloe lineata var. viridis

Aloe lineata var. viridis, commonly known as the Green Aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features striking, narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically a vibrant green color, often with a slight blue or silver hue. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
