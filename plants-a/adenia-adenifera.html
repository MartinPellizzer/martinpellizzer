Adenia adenifera

Adenia adenifera, commonly known as the smooth-stemmed adenium, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, often woody stems and large, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late summer. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release seeds.

Adenia adenifera is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a resilient species in harsh conditions.
