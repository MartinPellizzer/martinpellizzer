Aloe spicata

Aloe spicata, commonly known as the spiny aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color and adorned with sharp, spiny projections. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in spring. Aloe spicata is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice for rock gardens and arid landscapes.
