Argyrolobium pedunculare

Argyrolobium pedunculare, commonly known as the silver globular pea, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has a compact, bushy growth habit with small, hairy leaves. Its seed pods are spherical and contain several seeds, which are often dispersed by wind.

It is a member of the Fabaceae family and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
