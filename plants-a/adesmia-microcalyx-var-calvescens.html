Adesmia microcalyx var. calvescens

Adesmia microcalyx var. calvescens, commonly known as the narrow-leaved burr legume, is a flowering plant native to parts of South America.

It is a small shrub characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, bead-like fruits that contain seeds, typical of the Fabaceae family. It thrives in dry, arid environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes used in restoration projects in degraded landscapes.
