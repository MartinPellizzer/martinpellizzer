Alysicarpus glumaceus subsp. glumaceus

Alysicarpus glumaceus subsp.

glumaceus, commonly known as the hairy sida, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions. It is a herbaceous annual or perennial species belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.
