Aridaria congesta

Aridaria congesta, commonly known as the clustered arid flora, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and small, clustered leaves that help reduce water loss. The plant produces small, pale yellow or greenish flowers that bloom in spring. It has a deep root system that allows it to access groundwater in dry conditions.

Aridaria congesta is adapted to survive in harsh environments with minimal rainfall and high temperatures.
