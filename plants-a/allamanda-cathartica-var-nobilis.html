Allamanda cathartica var. nobilis

Allamanda cathartica var. nobilis, commonly known as the noble allamanda, is a tropical shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant produces large, waxy leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
