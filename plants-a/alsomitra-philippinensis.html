Alsomitra philippinensis

Alsomitra philippinensis, commonly known as the Philippine alsomitra, is a climbing vine native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Leguminosae family and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant produces pod-like fruits that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in forested areas.

Its botanical features make it an important species in ecological studies of Philippine flora.
