Adenia pyromorpha

Adenia pyromorpha, commonly known as the flame tree or fire tree, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters, often resembling flames. The plant has a woody stem with a rough, fissured bark, and its leaves are typically alternate and ovate. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in full sun.

Adenia pyromorpha is valued both for its ornamental beauty and its ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
