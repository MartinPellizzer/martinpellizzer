Asplenium horridum var. feani

Asplenium horridum var. feani, commonly known as the Feani's spleenwort, is a fern species belonging to the Aspleniaceae family.

It is a small, epiphytic or lithophytic plant, typically found growing on rocks or tree trunks in humid, shaded environments. The plant has finely divided, lanceolate leaves with a distinctive, serrated margin. Its spore-producing structures, called sori, are located on the underside of the fronds.

This variety is notable for its compact growth habit and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.
