Albuca schinzii

Albuca schinzii, commonly known as the Cape tulip, is a perennial bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of bell-shaped, white or yellow flowers. The plant is adapted to arid environments and thrives in well-drained soil. It is often grown as an ornamental plant for its elegant appearance.

Albuca schinzii is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.
