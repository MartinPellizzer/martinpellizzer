Alternanthera amoena f. rosea

Alternanthera amoena f. rosea, commonly known as the pink flame plant, is a striking ornamental plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant pink to red foliage, which becomes more intense under full sun exposure. The plant belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in water gardens and containers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full sunlight.

Its foliage is typically lance-shaped and displays a striking coloration that changes with environmental conditions.
