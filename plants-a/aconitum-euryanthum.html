Aconitum euryanthum

Aconitum euryanthum, commonly known as the Indian aconite, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Himalayas.

It features striking blue to purple flowers that bloom in late summer, typically from July to September. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with a waxy texture and a distinctive, hairy stem. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Due to its toxic alkaloids, it is important to handle the plant with care.
