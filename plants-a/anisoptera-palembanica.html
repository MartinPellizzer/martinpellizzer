Anisoptera palembanica

Anisoptera palembanica, commonly known as the Palembang ironwood, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, hard wood that is highly valued for construction and furniture. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are typically elliptical in shape. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by woody capsules containing seeds.

Anisoptera palembanica is often found in lowland and hill forest ecosystems.
