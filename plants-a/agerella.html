Agerella

Agerella, commonly known as the silverleaf nightshade, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other nightshades like tomatoes and potatoes. The plant features hairy, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale purple. Its fruit is a small, fleshy berry that turns red when mature.

Agerella is known for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
