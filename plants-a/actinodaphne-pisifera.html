Actinodaphne pisifera

Actinodaphne pisifera, commonly known as the broadleaf laurel, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae, which includes many aromatic and economically important plants. The plant is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves that are typically arranged in whorls around the stem.
