Albuca virens subsp. robusta

Albuca virens subsp.

robusta, commonly known as the robust albuca, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa. It features narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color, with a waxy coating that helps reduce water loss.

The plant produces clusters of white or pale yellow flowers during the summer months. It is adapted to arid environments and requires well-draining soil to thrive.

Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and container cultivation.
