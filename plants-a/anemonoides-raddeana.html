Anemonoides raddeana

Anemonoides raddeana, commonly known as the Himalayan anemone, is a flowering plant native to the alpine regions of the Himalayas.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its showy, white to pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky and well-drained soils at high elevations. Its leaves are palmately divided, and the flowers bloom in early spring.
