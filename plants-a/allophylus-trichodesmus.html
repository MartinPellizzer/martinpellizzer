Allophylus trichodesmus

Allophylus trichodesmus, commonly known as the African snowberry, is a deciduous shrub native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in clusters, followed by smooth, spherical berries that turn red when mature. The species is notable for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its attractive foliage and fruit.
