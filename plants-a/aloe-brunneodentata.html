Aloe brunneodentata

Aloe brunneodentata, commonly known as the brown-toothed aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically gray-green in color, with a distinctive reddish or brownish margin. The plant produces upright, branched inflorescences bearing yellow to orange flowers. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its leaves and requiring minimal irrigation.

Aloe brunneodentata is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
