Aloinopsis cibdela

Aloinopsis cibdela, commonly known as the Cape tiger's claw, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color with distinctive red or purple markings. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the warmer months, adding visual interest to its appearance. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
