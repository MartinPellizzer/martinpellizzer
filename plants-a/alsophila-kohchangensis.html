Alsophila kohchangensis

Alsophila kohchangensis, commonly known as the Kohchang hakea, is a species of tree fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnately compound fronds. The plant has a slow growth rate and can reach heights of up to 10 meters in optimal conditions. Its fronds are typically dark green and have a leathery texture, contributing to its ornamental value.

Alsophila kohchangensis is often found in humid, shaded environments such as tropical rainforests.
