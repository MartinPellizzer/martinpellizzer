Abutilon patens

Abutilon patens, commonly known as the velvetleaf abutilon, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its soft, fuzzy leaves and showy, lantern-shaped flowers that typically range in color from yellow to orange. The plant is a shrub or small tree, often reaching heights of up to 3 meters. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and waste areas.

Its rapid growth and adaptability make it both a useful ornamental plant and a potential weed in some regions.
