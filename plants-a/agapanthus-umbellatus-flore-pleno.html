Agapanthus umbellatus flore-pleno

Agapanthus umbellatus flore-pleno, commonly known as the full-flowered African lily, is a striking perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features dense, spherical clusters of white or blue flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has strap-like, evergreen leaves that form a rosette at the base. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
