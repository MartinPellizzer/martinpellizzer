Artemisia simplex

Artemisia simplex, commonly known as silverleaf sage, is a perennial herb native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, dense hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in late summer. Artemisia simplex is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid conditions.

It is valued for its aromatic properties and has been used in traditional medicine.
