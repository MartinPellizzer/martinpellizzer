Aleuritia reticulata

Aleuritia reticulata, commonly known as the Chinese snowberry, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features small, white flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring. The plant produces bright red berries that are edible for birds but toxic to humans. Its leaves are ovate with a serrated margin and a hairy texture.

Aleuritia reticulata is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
