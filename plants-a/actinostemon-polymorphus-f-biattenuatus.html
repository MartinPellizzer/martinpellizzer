Actinostemon polymorphus f. biattenuatus

Actinostemon polymorphus f. biattenuatus, commonly known as the broad-leaved actinostemon, is a rare and distinctive plant species characterized by its unique leaf morphology.

It features broad, ovate leaves with a flattened, almost spoon-shaped base, giving it a striking appearance. The plant is native to specific regions in Australia, where it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun exposure. Its foliage is often adorned with subtle variegation, adding to its ornamental value.
