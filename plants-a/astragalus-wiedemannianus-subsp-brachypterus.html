Astragalus wiedemannianus subsp. brachypterus

Astragalus wiedemannianus subsp.

brachypterus, commonly known as the short-stalked milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to China. It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellowish flowers.
