Acaena krausei var. meionandra

Acaena krausei var. meionandra, commonly known as the small-flowered wood rose, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the family Rosaceae and is characterized by its dense, spiny growth habit. The plant produces small, pink or white flowers that bloom in spring, followed by distinctive burr-like fruits. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs.
