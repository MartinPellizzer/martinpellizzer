Arundo contracta

Arundo contracta, commonly known as the narrowleaf reed, is a perennial grass native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and can grow up to several meters in height. The plant thrives in wet or moist environments, often found near water sources such as rivers and marshes. Its flowering stems produce small, clustered spikes of pale green to yellowish flowers.

Arundo contracta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping and wetland restoration projects.
