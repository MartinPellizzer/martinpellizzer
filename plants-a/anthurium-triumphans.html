Anthurium triumphans

Anthurium triumphans, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces vibrant red spathes that enclose its spadix, which is the actual flower structure. Anthurium triumphans thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

It is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and ability to purify indoor air.
