Adiantum taiwanianum

Adiantum taiwanianum, commonly known as the Taiwan maidenhair fern, is a member of the Adiantaceae family.

It is a delicate, evergreen fern characterized by its finely divided, bright green fronds that resemble a feather. Native to Taiwan, it thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories. The plant's fronds are bi-lobed and have a distinctive, elegant appearance.

It is prized in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in humid, indoor conditions.
