Astrophytum columnare

Astrophytum columnare, commonly known as the columnar cactus or moon cactus, is a unique desert plant native to the arid regions of Mexico.

It has a distinctive appearance with its tall, cylindrical stem that resembles a column, often covered in small, spiny tubercles. The plant produces vibrant yellow or orange flowers during the spring, which attract pollinators like bees and butterflies. Its thick, fleshy stems store water, allowing it to survive in harsh, dry environments.

Astrophytum columnare is popular among cactus enthusiasts due to its striking appearance and low maintenance requirements.
