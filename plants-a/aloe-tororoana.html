Aloe tororoana

Aloe tororoana, commonly known as the Tororo aloe, is a succulent plant native to East Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that store water, typical of arid environments. The plant produces yellow flowers in clusters during the dry season. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their soothing properties.

Aloe tororoana is well adapted to drought conditions, making it a resilient species in its native habitat.
