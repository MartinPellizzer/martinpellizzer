Amerimnon ferrugineum

Amerimnon ferrugineum, commonly known as the rusty ironbark, is a shrub or small tree native to South America.

It is characterized by its rough, fissured bark that often appears rusty or reddish in color. The plant has small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. It produces yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by small, round fruits.

The species is adapted to dry and rocky environments, making it a hardy plant in arid regions.
