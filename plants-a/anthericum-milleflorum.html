Anthericum milleflorum

Anthericum milleflorum, commonly known as the star of Bethlehem, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features a rosette of narrow, gray-green leaves that form a dense basal cluster. The plant produces striking, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in the spring. It is characterized by its fibrous root system and its ability to thrive in well-drained soils.

Anthericum milleflorum is often used in ornamental gardening for its elegant appearance and historical significance.
