Aloe gossweileri

Aloe gossweileri, commonly known as the desert aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that store water, enabling it to survive in arid environments. The plant produces yellow flowers during the rainy season, typically in summer. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their soothing properties.

Aloe gossweileri thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for xeriscaping.
