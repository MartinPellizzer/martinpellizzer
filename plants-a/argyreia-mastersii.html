Argyreia mastersii

Argyreia mastersii, commonly known as the red devil flower, is a climbing vine native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety red bracts that surround small, white flowers. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and is known for its rapid growth habit. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
