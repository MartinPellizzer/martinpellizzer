Aridaria barkerae

Aridaria barkerae, commonly known as the desert spire, is a rare shrub adapted to arid environments.

It possesses thick, waxy leaves that reduce water loss and enhance survival in dry conditions. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or pale in color, which helps it blend with its surroundings. Its bark is rugged and fissured, providing structural support and protection against harsh weather.

Aridaria barkerae is native to specific regions of southern Africa, where it thrives in sandy or rocky soils.
