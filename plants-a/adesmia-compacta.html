Adesmia compacta

Adesmia compacta, commonly known as the compact burr legume, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a small, shrub-like species characterized by its dense, rounded growth habit and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in compact inflorescences. Its fruit is a legume pod that contains several small, rounded seeds.

Adesmia compacta is often found in dry, disturbed habitats and is adapted to arid environments.
