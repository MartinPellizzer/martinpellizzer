Arisaema formosanum

Arisaema formosanum, commonly known as the Formosan arrowroot, is a herbaceous plant native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its unique, spadix-like inflorescence surrounded by a colorful spathe. The plant features arrow-shaped leaves that are typically bright green and have a smooth texture. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
