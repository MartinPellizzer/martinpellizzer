Arundarbor aureostriata

Arundarbor aureostriata, commonly known as the golden stripe orchid, is a striking tropical plant characterized by its vibrant yellow and green striped leaves.

Native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia, it thrives in humid, shaded environments with high atmospheric moisture. The plant features delicate, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters, often displaying shades of white and pale yellow. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb nutrients from the rich, organic soil of its natural habitat.

Arundarbor aureostriata is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and greenhouses.
