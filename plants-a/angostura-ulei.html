Angostura ulei

Angostura ulei, commonly known as the Brazilian bitter orange, is a citrus species native to South America.

It is characterized by its thick, pebbled rind and small, sour fruits. The plant has dark green, glossy leaves and produces fragrant white flowers. Its essential oils are extracted from the peel and are widely used in perfumery and medicine.

The botanical features of Angostura ulei make it a valuable plant in both horticulture and commercial applications.
