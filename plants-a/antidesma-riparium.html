Antidesma riparium

Antidesma riparium, commonly known as river antidesma, is a shrub or small tree native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves that are often elliptical in shape. Antidesma riparium is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
