Alternanthera junciflora

Alternanthera junciflora, commonly known as the red flame plant, is a species of flowering plant in the Amaranthaceae family.

It is native to South America and is characterized by its striking red foliage and small, clustered pink to purple flowers. The plant is typically herbaceous, with succulent stems and leaves that are often variegated in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Alternanthera junciflora is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its vibrant color and ease of care.
