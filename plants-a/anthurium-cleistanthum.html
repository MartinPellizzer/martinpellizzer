Anthurium cleistanthum

Anthurium cleistanthum, commonly known as the velvet anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a velvety texture. The plant produces vibrant red spadices, which are the spiky, club-shaped structures that house the tiny, inconspicuous flowers. Anthurium cleistanthum thrives in high humidity and indirect light, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Its unique foliage and ornamental value make it a sought-after plant among horticultural enthusiasts.
