Armeria arenaria subsp. arenaria

Armeria arenaria subsp.

arenaria, commonly known as sandstem, is a low-growing evergreen perennial native to coastal regions of Europe and western Asia. It features dense clusters of small, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring.

The plant has succulent, blue-green leaves that are adapted to withstand salty air and sandy soils. It thrives in full sun and is often found in dunes, cliffs, and coastal grasslands.

Its hardy nature makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscaping.
