Artemisia deliliana

Artemisia deliliana, commonly known as Delil's wormwood, is a perennial shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, silver-gray foliage. The plant produces small, yellowish flower heads that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in alpine and steppe habitats.

Due to its medicinal properties, it has been used traditionally in herbal remedies.
