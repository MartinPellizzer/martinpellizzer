Anthereon aryter

Anthereon aryter, commonly known as the flameleaf orchid, is a striking plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant orange and red petals, which are adorned with intricate patterns resembling flames. The plant features a slender, erect stem with broad, lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green. Its flowers bloom in clusters, producing a sweet, spicy fragrance that attracts pollinators like bees and butterflies.

Botanically, it belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is known for its unique floral structure and adaptability to humid environments.
