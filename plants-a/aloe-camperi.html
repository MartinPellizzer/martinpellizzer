Aloe camperi

Aloe camperi, commonly known as the Cape aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a white or yellowish margin. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow to orange flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun exposure.

Aloe camperi is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional medicinal uses.
