Andrachne Nana

Andrachne dwarf, scientifically known as Andrachne nana, is a small, herbaceous plant native to the western United States. It thrives in rocky and arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. This species is characterized by its compact growth habit and delicate, clustered foliage. Despite its diminutive size, it plays a vital role in its ecosystem by supporting local pollinators and soil stability. Its unique appearance and resilience make it a fascinating subject for botanical study and conservation efforts.
