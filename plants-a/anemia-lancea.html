Anemia lancea

Anemia lancea, commonly known as the lanceleaf anemone, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of central and southern Europe.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves and showy, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade. Anemia lancea is a herbaceous perennial that can reach heights of up to 30 centimeters.

Its botanical features make it a valuable addition to woodland gardens and naturalized landscapes.
