Areca cincta

Areca cincta, commonly known as the lace areca or the banded areca, is a tropical palm native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, upright trunk and feathery, compound leaves that give it a graceful appearance. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences with pale yellow flowers, which are followed by elongated, three-lobed fruits. Areca cincta thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and indoor cultivation.
