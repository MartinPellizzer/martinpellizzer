Aspidium helveolum

It is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds that are typically submerged or floating in freshwater habitats. Aspidium helveolum is often found in still or slow-moving freshwater environments, where it contributes to aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat for small organisms.
