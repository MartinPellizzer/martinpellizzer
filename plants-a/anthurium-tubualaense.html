Anthurium tubualaense

Anthurium tubualaense, commonly known as the Tubualense Arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to the Amazon basin.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a glossy texture. The plant produces striking spadices, which are the characteristic inflorescences of the Araceae family. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, rich soil.

Anthurium tubualaense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated as a houseplant in warm climates.
