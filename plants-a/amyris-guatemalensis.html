Amyris guatemalensis

Amyris guatemalensis, commonly known as the Guatemalan sandarac tree, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to Central America, particularly Guatemala, and is characterized by its aromatic, resinous wood. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a fragrant scent.

Amyris guatemalensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
