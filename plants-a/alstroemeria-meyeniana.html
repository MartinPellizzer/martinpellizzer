Alstroemeria meyeniana

Alstroemeria meyeniana, commonly known as the Chilean flame lily, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Chile and Argentina.

It belongs to the Alstroemeriaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that come in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant typically grows as a herbaceous perennial, with strap-like leaves and a compact growth habit. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.

Its striking appearance and long-lasting blooms make it a valued ornamental plant in horticulture.
