Adromischus liebenbergii subsp. orientalis

Adromischus liebenbergii subsp.

orientalis, commonly known as the "Lion's Tail," is a succulent plant native to South Africa. It features rosettes of fleshy, dark green leaves with a distinctive white or silver underside.

The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect sunlight.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
