Anthemiopsis

Anthemiopsis, commonly known as the golden groundsel, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and is native to parts of Europe and Asia. The plant typically grows in dry, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. Its leaves are usually narrow and gray-green, with a slightly rough texture.
