Asplenium adiantum-nigrum var. yuanum

Asplenium adiantum-nigrum var. yuanum, commonly known as the Yunnan maidenhair fern, is a variant of the common maidenhair fern native to China.

It is a small, evergreen fern characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds that are typically dark green in color. The plant thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories. Its rhizomes are fibrous and help anchor it to rocky or soil substrates.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
