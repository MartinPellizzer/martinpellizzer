Arabis brownii

Arabis brownii, commonly known as the Brown's rockcress, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically thrives in rocky or gravelly soils. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers during the spring and early summer. Its leaves are basal, linear, and often covered in fine hairs.

Arabis brownii is adapted to arid environments and is often found in mountainous regions.
