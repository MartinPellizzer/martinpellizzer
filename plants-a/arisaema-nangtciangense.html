Arisaema nangtciangense

Arisaema nangtciangense, commonly known as the Nangtciang arrowhead, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Yunnan Province in China.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive spathe and spadix inflorescence. The plant features a rhizomatous root system and produces heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a waxy cuticle. Its flowers are typically greenish-white with a central spadix that bears numerous small, inconspicuous flowers.

Arisaema nangtciangense is often found in moist, shaded habitats and is valued for its ornamental qualities and traditional medicinal uses.
