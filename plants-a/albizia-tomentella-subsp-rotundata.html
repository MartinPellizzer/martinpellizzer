Albizia tomentella subsp. rotundata

Albizia tomentella subsp.

rotundata, commonly known as the silky albizia, is a shrub or small tree native to tropical regions of Africa. It is characterized by its soft, hairy foliage and distinctive, flat, bean-like pods.

The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or white, arranged in dense clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed or open habitats. Albizia tomentella subsp.

rotundata is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in some cultures.
