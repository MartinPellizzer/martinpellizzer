Achillea × correvoniana

Achillea × correvoniana, commonly known as the hybrid yarrow, is a perennial plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is a naturally occurring hybrid between Achillea millefolium (common yarrow) and Achillea ptarmica (wood yarrow). The plant features finely divided, feathery leaves and produces clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers. It is often found in disturbed soils and open habitats, thriving in full sun.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice in gardens and landscaping.
