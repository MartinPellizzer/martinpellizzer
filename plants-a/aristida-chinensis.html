Aristida chinensis

Aristida chinensis, commonly known as Chinese dropseed, is a perennial grass species native to East Asia.

It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. The plant has slender, erect stems and produces small, fluffy seeds that are dispersed by wind. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green color.

Aristida chinensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping and ecological restoration projects.
