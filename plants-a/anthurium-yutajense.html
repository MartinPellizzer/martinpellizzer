Anthurium yutajense

Anthurium yutajense, commonly known as the Yutajense arrowhead, is a member of the Araceae family native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive arrow-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy surface. The plant produces striking spadices, which are inflorescences that consist of a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Anthurium yutajense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in tropical gardens.
