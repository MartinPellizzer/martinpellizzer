Achyronia acanthes

Achyronia acanthes, commonly known as the purple coneflower, is a flowering plant native to the United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, violet-purple flowers that bloom in the summer. Achyronia acanthes is also known for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
