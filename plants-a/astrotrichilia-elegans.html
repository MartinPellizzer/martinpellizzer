Astrotrichilia elegans

Astrotrichilia elegans, commonly known as the elegant star-hair plant, is a unique member of the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its striking, star-shaped hairs that cover its stems and leaves, giving it a distinctive appearance. Native to specific regions in South America, this plant thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its flowering period typically occurs during the warmer months, producing small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters.

Due to its rarity and ornamental value, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by plant enthusiasts.
