Armeria barcensis

Armeria barcensis, commonly known as the Barcens sandspurry, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of southern Europe and North Africa.

It is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit and produces clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers. The plant has fleshy, succulent leaves that help it retain moisture in dry conditions. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Armeria barcensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
