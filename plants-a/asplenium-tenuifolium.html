Asplenium tenuifolium

Asplenium tenuifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaved spleenwort, is a small, evergreen fern belonging to the family Aspleniaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped leaves that are typically less than 10 centimeters in length. This plant prefers moist, shaded environments and is often found growing on rocks or in crevices. Its rhizomes are fibrous and help anchor it to rocky substrates.

Asplenium tenuifolium is a hardy species that is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation.
