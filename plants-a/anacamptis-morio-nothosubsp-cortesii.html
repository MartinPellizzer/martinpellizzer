Anacamptis morio nothosubsp. cortesii

Anacamptis morio nothosubsp.

cortesii, commonly known as the Cortesii Orchid, is a rare orchid species native to the Iberian Peninsula. It is a terrestrial herb with a sympodial growth habit, characterized by a short rhizome and a rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves.

The plant produces a single spike bearing several pale yellow to greenish-white flowers with prominent purple markings. It thrives in calcareous soils and prefers sunny to partially shaded habitats.
