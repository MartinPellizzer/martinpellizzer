Argyrolobium linnaeanum var. majus

Argyrolobium linnaeanum var. majus, commonly known as the large-flowered silver globular vetch, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its spherical, silver-gray seed pods. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in summer, adding ornamental value to gardens. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open grasslands and rocky areas.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological role, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists alike.
