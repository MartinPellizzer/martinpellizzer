Anisochilus carnosus var. eriocephalus

Anisochilus carnosus var. eriocephalus, commonly known as the woolly anisochilus, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, clustered white flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

This species is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique texture and visual appeal.
