Artemisia alba var. biasolettiana

Artemisia alba var. biasolettiana, commonly known as white artemisia, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which consists of narrow, lance-shaped leaves covered in fine, white hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flower heads that appear in late summer, adding a subtle ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Due to its aromatic properties, it is also used in traditional herbal medicine.
