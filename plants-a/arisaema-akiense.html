Arisaema akiense

Arisaema akiense, commonly known as the Japanese arrowhead, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive inflorescence composed of a spadix and a spathe. The plant features arrow-shaped leaves and produces small, greenish-white flowers. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Arisaema akiense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
