Avenastrum caffrum var. natalense

Avenastrum caffrum var. natalense, commonly known as Natal grass, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in composite heads, typical of the daisy family. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and rocky areas.

Natal grass is valued for its ornamental appeal and is used in some traditional medicinal practices.
