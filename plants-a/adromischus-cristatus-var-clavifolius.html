Adromischus cristatus var. clavifolius

Adromischus cristatus var. clavifolius, commonly known as the rosette-shaped stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features dense, fleshy leaves arranged in a compact rosette, often with a bluish-green hue and a waxy texture. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or purple during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
