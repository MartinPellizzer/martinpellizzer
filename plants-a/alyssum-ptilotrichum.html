Alyssum ptilotrichum

Alyssum ptilotrichum, commonly known as the woolly alyssum, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing herbaceous plant with small, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white, pink, or purple. The plant features dense, silvery-gray foliage covered in fine, soft hairs, which give it a distinctive, woolly appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Alyssum ptilotrichum is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
