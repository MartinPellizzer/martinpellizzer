Amauropelta euthythrix

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, shield-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and rocky crevices. Its leaves are usually bright yellow to golden in color, which gives it its common name.
