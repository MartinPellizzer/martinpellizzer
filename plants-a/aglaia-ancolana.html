Aglaia ancolana

Aglaia ancolana, commonly known as the white aglaia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Meliaceae family, which includes mahogany and other valuable hardwoods. The plant is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves and small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. Its fruit is a woody capsule that contains several seeds.
