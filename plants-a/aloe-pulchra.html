Aloe pulchra

Aloe pulchra, commonly known as the "beautiful aloe," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, with white or pale blue markings. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing yellow flowers during the summer months. Aloe pulchra is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its leaves and requiring minimal irrigation.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and low maintenance requirements.
