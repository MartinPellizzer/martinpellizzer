Anemonospermos leucanthemodes

Anemonospermos leucanthemodes, commonly known as the white anemone, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has a compact, bushy growth habit with hairy stems and deeply lobed leaves. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens, though it is more commonly found in its natural habitat.
