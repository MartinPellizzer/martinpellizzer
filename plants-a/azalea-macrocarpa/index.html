Azalea Macrocarpa

Azalea macrocarpa is a striking evergreen shrub native to the coastal regions of southern California and Baja California. Known for its vibrant clusters of showy flowers, it blooms in spring with shades of pink, red, and white. This plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for coastal gardens. Its hardy nature and ornamental value have made it a favorite among horticulturists and garden enthusiasts. Despite its beauty, it requires careful attention to avoid overwatering and root rot.
