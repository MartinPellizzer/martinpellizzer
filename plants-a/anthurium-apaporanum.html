Anthurium apaporanum

Anthurium apaporanum, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped spathes that can range in color from deep red to orange, often with contrasting green veins. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers within the spathe, which are typically green or yellow. Its leaves are large, glossy, and leathery, often with a dark green hue and prominent veins.

Anthurium apaporanum thrives in humid, warm environments and is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant.
