Achyranthes reineckii

Achyranthes reineckii, commonly known as the red root or red brome, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in grassy areas and along roadsides. The plant has erect, branched stems and produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from pink to red. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a rough texture.

Achyranthes reineckii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
