Albuca Knersvlaktensis

Albuca knersvlaktensis is a rare and striking plant native to the mountainous regions of South Africa. It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is known for its unique, star-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring. This species thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and requires minimal water, making it an ideal choice for rock gardens. Its foliage consists of long, strap-like leaves that add visual interest throughout the growing season. Despite its beauty, Albuca knersvlaktensis is threatened by habitat loss and over-collection, highlighting the need for conservation efforts.
