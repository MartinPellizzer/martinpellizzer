Anil Pungens

Anil pungens is a unique plant known for its strong, pungent odor. It thrives in humid environments and is often found in tropical regions. This plant has been used in traditional medicine for centuries. Its distinctive scent makes it both fascinating and controversial. Despite its reputation, it remains a subject of scientific interest.
