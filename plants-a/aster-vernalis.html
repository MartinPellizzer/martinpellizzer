Aster vernalis

Aster vernalis, commonly known as the spring aster, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and woodland edges. The plant features upright stems covered in narrow, lance-shaped leaves. Its showy, daisy-like flowers bloom in shades of purple and white from late spring to early summer.

Aster vernalis is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting pollinators.
