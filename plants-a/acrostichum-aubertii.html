Acrostichum aubertii

Acrostichum aubertii, commonly known as the giant reed or giant bamboo reed, is a tall, herbaceous plant native to the wetlands of Southeast Asia.

The plant thrives in moist or aquatic environments, often forming dense stands in marshes and along waterways. Acrostichum aubertii is also valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
