Aphelandra pepe-parodii

Aphelandra pepe-parodii, commonly known as the Pepe Parodii torch plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the Caribbean.

It features striking, elongated leaves with a deep green color and a prominent midrib, giving it a dramatic, architectural appearance. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, tubular flowers in shades of yellow to orange, which bloom during the summer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and high humidity to flourish.

Aphelandra pepe-parodii is well-suited for indoor cultivation and adds a lush, tropical aesthetic to any space.
