Allium schoenoprasum subsp. lusitanicum

Allium schoenoprasum subsp.

lusitanicum, commonly known as the Iberian chive, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe. It features slender, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered purple or white flowers.

The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows. It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is closely related to other Allium species.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable plant for both ecological and ornamental purposes.
