Alyssum foliosum var. foliosum

Alyssum foliosum var. foliosum, commonly known as the common alyssum, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense clusters of small, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant features narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Alyssum foliosum var. foliosum is also valued for its ability to attract beneficial insects such as bees and butterflies.
