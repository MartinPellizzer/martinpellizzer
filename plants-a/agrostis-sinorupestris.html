Agrostis sinorupestris

Agrostis sinorupestris, commonly known as the mountain grass or alpine grass, is a perennial grass species native to high-altitude regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its fine, tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and is adapted to cold, arid environments. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-dispersed seeds.

Due to its hardy nature, it is often used in ecological restoration projects in alpine and subalpine habitats.
