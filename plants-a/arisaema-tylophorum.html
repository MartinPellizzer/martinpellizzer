Arisaema tylophorum

Arisaema tylophorum, commonly known as the Chinese arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features a distinctive, arrow-shaped leaf that is often used in traditional medicine. The plant produces a unique, spadix-like inflorescence surrounded by a modified leaf called a spathe. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
