Astilbe × lemoinei

Astilbe × lemoinei, commonly known as the Chinese astilbe, is a hybrid shrub native to East Asia.

It features upright, branching stems that bear dense clusters of small, fluffy flowers in shades of pink, red, or white. The plant is valued for its ornamental foliage, which ranges from dark green to bronze, adding visual interest throughout the growing season. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens.

Astilbe × lemoinei is also noted for its ability to attract pollinators such as butterflies and bees.
