Arnica glabrata

Arnica glabrata, commonly known as the smooth arnica, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the sunflower family (Asteraceae) and is characterized by its yellow-orange flowers and hairy leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine habitats, thriving in well-drained soils. Arnica glabrata is known for its medicinal properties, particularly in traditional herbal medicine.

Its flowers contain compounds that have been used for their anti-inflammatory and analgesic effects.
