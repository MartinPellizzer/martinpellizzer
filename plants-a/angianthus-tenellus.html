Angianthus tenellus

Angianthus tenellus, commonly known as the slender angianthus, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Acanthaceae and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its flowers are often pink or purple and bloom during the summer months.
