Asplenium bradei

Asplenium bradei, commonly known as the Brade's spleenwort, is a small, evergreen fern belonging to the family Aspleniaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, linear leaves that are typically clustered around the base of the plant. The plant produces spore-bearing structures called sori, which are usually found on the underside of the fronds. Asplenium bradei is often found growing on rock surfaces or in shaded, moist environments.

It is a relatively small fern, typically reaching only a few centimeters in height.
