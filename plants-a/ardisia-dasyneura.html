Ardisia dasyneura

Ardisia dasyneura, commonly known as the glossy buckeye, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are typically ovate to elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers followed by bright red, fleshy berries. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forests.

The berries are an important food source for various birds and mammals.
