Anthurium olfersianum

Anthurium olfersianum, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a striking tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped spathes that range from red to orange in color. The plant produces small, green berries that mature to a dark red, containing seeds for propagation. Its foliage is broad and glossy, adding to its ornamental value in both indoor and outdoor settings.

Anthurium olfersianum thrives in humid, warm environments with bright, indirect light.
