Argyranthemum Adauctum Subsp. Jacobaeifolium

Argyranthemum adauctum subsp. jacobaeifolium is a striking member of the Asteraceae family known for its vibrant, daisy-like flowers. Native to southern Africa, this plant thrives in warm, sunny climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. Its unique foliage, featuring deeply lobed leaves, adds an additional layer of visual interest to gardens and landscapes. The plant is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Despite its beauty, it requires well-drained soil and moderate watering to flourish successfully.
