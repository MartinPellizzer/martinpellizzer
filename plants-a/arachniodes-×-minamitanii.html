Arachniodes × minamitanii

Arachniodes × minamitanii, commonly known as the Japanese cloud fern, is a hybrid fern species native to Japan.

It belongs to the Dryopteridaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, lacy fronds that resemble spider webs, hence its common name. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky crevices. Its rhizomes are fibrous, and it produces small, clustered spore sacs on the undersides of its leaves.

Arachniodes × minamitanii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and is often cultivated in shady gardens.
