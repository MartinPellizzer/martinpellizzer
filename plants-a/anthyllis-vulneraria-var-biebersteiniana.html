Anthyllis vulneraria var. biebersteiniana

Anthyllis vulneraria var. biebersteiniana, commonly known as the lesser burnet, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its dense clusters of tiny, pink or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. Its leaves are trifoliate, consisting of three small, oval-shaped leaflets.
