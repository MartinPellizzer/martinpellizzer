Achyranthes aspera f. annulosa

Achyranthes aspera f. annulosa, commonly known as the ringed cowherb, is a variant of the common cowherb.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant native to tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features upright stems with a distinctive ringed appearance, which is a key botanical characteristic. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have serrated margins.

The flowers are small, greenish-yellow, and clustered in spikes, typical of the Amaranthaceae family.
