Astridia velutina var. lutata

Astridia velutina var. lutata, commonly known as the velvetleaf astridia, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of South America.

It is characterized by its soft, hairy foliage and clusters of small, pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests or along stream banks. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a velvety texture, contributing to its common name.
