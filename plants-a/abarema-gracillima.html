Abarema gracillima

Abarema gracillima, commonly known as the slender spiderflower, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Leguminosae family, which is characterized by its characteristic pod fruits and nitrogen-fixing abilities. The plant features delicate, fern-like leaves and produces small, clustered white or pale pink flowers. It is often found in moist, shaded habitats such as forest understories.
