Aeranthes fragrans

Aeranthes fragrans, commonly known as the fragrant orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of white and pale pink. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are broad and strap-like, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Aeranthes fragrans is valued in horticulture for its unique aroma and aesthetic beauty.
