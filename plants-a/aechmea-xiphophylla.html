Aechmea xiphophylla, commonly known as the pineapple bromeliad, is a striking epiphytic plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

Aechmea xiphophylla thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its unique appearance and ability to grow without soil make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
