Anomospermum chloranthum subsp. confusum

Anomospermum chloranthum subsp.

confusum, commonly known as the desert hibiscus, is a shrub native to arid regions of Africa. It features woody stems and produces showy, yellow flowers with five petals.

The plant has small, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It is adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments with minimal water.

Its flowers attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies, contributing to local biodiversity.
