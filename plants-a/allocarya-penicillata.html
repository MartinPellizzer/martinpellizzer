Allocarya penicillata

Allocarya penicillata, commonly known as the slender thistle, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, spiny leaves and produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It is often found in rocky or sandy soils, thriving in arid environments.

Its botanical structure supports its adaptation to dry climates and limited nutrient availability.
