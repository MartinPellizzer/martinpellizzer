Acrostichum klotzschii

Acrostichum klotzschii, commonly known as the giant chain fern, is a large, tropical fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, arching fronds that can reach lengths of up to 3 meters. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit, with a thick, horizontal underground stem that supports its massive foliage. Its leaves are compound, consisting of multiple pinnae arranged in a feather-like pattern.

This fern thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.
