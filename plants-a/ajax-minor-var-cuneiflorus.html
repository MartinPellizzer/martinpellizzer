Ajax minor var. cuneiflorus

Ajax minor var. cuneiflorus, commonly known as the narrow-flowered ajax, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, narrow leaves and daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its compact size and attractive blooms.

The varietal name 'cuneiflorus' refers to its lance-shaped flowers, distinguishing it from other varieties of Ajax.
