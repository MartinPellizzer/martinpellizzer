Anthemis calcarea

Anthemis calcarea, commonly known as the limestone groundsel, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact, herbaceous growth habit and small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant thrives in calcareous soils, often found on rocky or limestone-rich habitats. Its leaves are typically covered in fine hairs, giving them a silvery appearance.

Anthemis calcarea is often used in rock gardens due to its hardy nature and ornamental value.
