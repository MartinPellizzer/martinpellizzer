Aeranthes filiformis

Aeranthes filiformis, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a species native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid that typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant features delicate, slender stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. Its flowers are small and greenish-white, often clustered in a raceme, and bloom during the rainy season.

Aeranthes filiformis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
