Ageratum tomentosum f. bracteatum

Ageratum tomentosum f. bracteatum, commonly known as the bracteate ageratum, is a compact, bushy plant native to Mexico.

It features small, daisy-like flowers in shades of purple, pink, and white, often with a white or pale pink center. The plant has hairy, grayish foliage that helps it retain moisture in dry conditions. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is often used in ornamental gardening for its vibrant blooms.

Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for borders, containers, and hanging baskets.
