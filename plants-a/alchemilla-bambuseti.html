Alchemilla bambuseti

Alchemilla bambuseti, commonly known as the bamboo alchemilla, is a member of the Rosaceae family.

It is a low-growing perennial plant characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit. The plant features finely divided, bright green leaves that are often covered in a silvery-white powdery coating. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers in spring, which are clustered in umbels.

Alchemilla bambuseti is well adapted to rocky or gravelly soils and is often found in alpine or subalpine regions.
