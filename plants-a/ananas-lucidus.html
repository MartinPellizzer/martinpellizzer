Ananas lucidus

Ananas lucidus, commonly known as the silver pineapple, is a unique bromeliad native to the tropical rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its striking silver-blue foliage, which is covered in a waxy coating that reflects light. The plant produces a small, edible fruit that is similar in appearance to a pineapple but has a more delicate flavor. Its leaves are arranged in a rosette pattern, forming a central cup that collects rainwater, providing a microhabitat for small organisms.

Ananas lucidus is valued both for its ornamental beauty and its ecological role in its native environment.
