Arisaema intermedium

Arisaema intermedium, commonly known as the Chinese arrowroot, is a herbaceous plant native to East Asia.

It features a short, rhizomatous stem and large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically green with a distinctive white or pale-colored margin. The plant produces a spadix surrounded by a spathe, which is characteristic of the Araceae family. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in forest understories.

The plant is cultivated for its edible tubers, which are used in traditional Chinese cuisine.
