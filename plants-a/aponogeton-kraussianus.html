Aponogeton kraussianus

Aponogeton kraussianus, commonly known as the African water starwort, is a submerged aquatic plant native to southern Africa.

It features long, ribbon-like leaves that are typically green with a waxy coating, which helps reduce water loss. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom above the water surface, making it visually distinctive. It thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments and is often found in ponds and lakes.

Aponogeton kraussianus is valued in aquascaping for its ornamental appearance and ecological benefits.
