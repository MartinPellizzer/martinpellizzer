Aeranthes gladiifolia

Aeranthes gladiifolia, commonly known as the swordleaf orchid, is a tropical plant native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America.

It is characterized by its striking, sword-shaped leaves and clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forests or near streams. Its leaves are long and narrow, often reaching up to 30 centimeters in length, and are arranged in a rosette pattern.

The flowers bloom in late spring or early summer, adding ornamental value to the plant.
