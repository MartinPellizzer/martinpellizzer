Arisaema kunstleri

Arisaema kunstleri, commonly known as the Kunst's arisaema, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive inflorescence composed of a spadix and a spathe. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are arrow-shaped and can reach lengths of up to 30 centimeters.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
