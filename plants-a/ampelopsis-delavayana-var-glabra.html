Ampelopsis delavayana var. glabra

Ampelopsis delavayana var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth ampelopsis, is a climbing vine native to the mountainous regions of China.

It features compound leaves with three to five leaflets, which are typically smooth and glabrous. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in racemes, followed by bright red berries that are edible. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value is due to its attractive foliage and vibrant fruiting display.
