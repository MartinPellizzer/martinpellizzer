Anthurium julospadix

Anthurium julospadix, commonly known as the red-tipped anthurium, is a striking tropical plant native to Colombia.

It features vibrant, heart-shaped leaves with a deep red margin, contrasting beautifully with its glossy green center. The plant produces striking spadices, which are bright red, spadix-like structures that serve as the flowering part. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium julospadix is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
