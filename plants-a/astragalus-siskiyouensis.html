Astragalus siskiyouensis

Astragalus siskiyouensis, commonly known as Siskiyou milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellowish flowers. This species is typically found in rocky, dry habitats such as slopes and meadows. It plays a role in soil stabilization and provides nectar for pollinators.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and sensitive species.
