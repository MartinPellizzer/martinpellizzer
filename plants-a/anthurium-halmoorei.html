Anthurium halmoorei

Anthurium halmoorei, commonly known as the Halmoore's anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are deep green with a waxy texture. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-shaped inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, typically in shades of red or orange. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Anthurium halmoorei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in botanical gardens.
