Alternanthera regelii

Alternanthera regelii, commonly known as red Alternanthera, is a striking aquatic plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red or pink foliage, which becomes more intense under strong light conditions. The plant typically grows in shallow water and thrives in a variety of water conditions, making it a popular choice for aquariums. Its leaves are opposite and lance-shaped, with a soft texture that adds visual appeal to planted tanks.

Alternanthera regelii is often used as a foreground or midground plant due to its compact growth habit and aesthetic value.
