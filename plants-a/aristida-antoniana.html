Aristida antoniana

Aristida antoniana, commonly known as the silvergrass or antoniana grass, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, tufted growth habit and slender, silver-gray leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that are often pink to reddish in color, adding ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for xeriscaping and low-maintenance gardens.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for both ecological and aesthetic purposes in landscaping.
