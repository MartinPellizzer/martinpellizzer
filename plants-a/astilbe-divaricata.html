Astilbe divaricata

Astilbe divaricata, commonly known as the variable astilbe, is a herbaceous perennial native to eastern Asia.

It features upright, branched stems that bear clusters of small, lace-like, pink to white flowers. The plant has trifoliate leaves that are dark green and slightly hairy. It thrives in partial to full shade and moist, well-drained soil.

Astilbe divaricata is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
