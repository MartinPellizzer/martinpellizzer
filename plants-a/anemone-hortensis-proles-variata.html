Anemone hortensis proles variata

Anemone hortensis proles variata, commonly known as the garden anemone, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe.

It features showy, cup-shaped flowers that come in a range of colors, including white, pink, and red, depending on the variety. The plant has finely divided, fern-like leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value.

The flowers typically bloom in early spring, adding a vibrant touch to gardens during the cooler months.
