Aspidium christii

Aspidium christii, commonly known as the African shield fern, is a species of fern native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its shield-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its fronds are usually dark green and have a leathery texture.

Aspidium christii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
