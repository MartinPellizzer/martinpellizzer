Atriplex yeelirrie

Atriplex yeelirrie, commonly known as the Yeelirrie saltbush, is a shrub native to the arid and semi-arid regions of Western Australia.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage, which helps reduce water loss in its harsh environment. The plant produces small, greenish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences, typical of the Chenopodiaceae family. Its succulent leaves are adapted for water storage, and it has a deep root system that allows it to access groundwater.
