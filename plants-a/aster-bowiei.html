Aster bowiei

Aster bowiei, commonly known as the Bowie aster, is a perennial herb native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces showy, daisy-like flowers in shades of purple and blue. The plant features narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Aster bowiei is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
