Anthurium angustilaminatum var. albidum

Anthurium angustilaminatum var. albidum, commonly known as the white-leaf anthurium, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that are typically white or pale green in color. The plant produces striking spadix inflorescences surrounded by a spathe, which can range in color from yellow to red. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

This variety is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive indoors with proper care.
