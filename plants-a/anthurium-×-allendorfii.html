Anthurium × allendorfii

Anthurium × allendorfii, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a hybrid plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped leaves that are typically deep green with a red or maroon underside. The plant produces striking, spadix-shaped inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, often in shades of red, orange, or yellow. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Anthurium × allendorfii is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and long-lasting blooms.
