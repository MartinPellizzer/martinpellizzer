Amaryllis hugoi

Amaryllis hugoi, commonly known as the Hugoi amaryllis, is a bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features striking, funnel-shaped flowers that can range in color from white to pink and red. The plant produces a tall, erect stem with several leaves that are broad and strap-like. It blooms in late winter to early spring, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its foliage and flowers are both ornamental, contributing to its appeal in horticultural settings.
