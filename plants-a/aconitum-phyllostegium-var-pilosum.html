Aconitum phyllostegium var. pilosum

Aconitum phyllostegium var. pilosum, commonly known as the silky leaf aconite, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of western China.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its tall, sparsely branched stems that can reach up to 2 meters in height. The plant produces showy blue to purple flowers arranged in a raceme, which bloom in late summer. Its leaves are palmately compound, with a distinctive silvery pubescence that gives it its common name.

This species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and striking floral display.
