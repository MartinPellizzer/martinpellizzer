Abies spectabilis var. densa

Abies spectabilis var. densa, commonly known as the dense fir, is a variant of the noble fir native to the Pacific Northwest of North America.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and dark green, needle-like foliage. The plant has short, stout cones that are typically borne on the upper branches. Its needles are stiff and have a slightly resinous texture, contributing to its evergreen nature.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a Christmas tree due to its attractive appearance and hardy nature.
