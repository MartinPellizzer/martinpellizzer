Alsine attica

Alsine attica, commonly known as Mediterranean sandwort, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing annual herb with small, white or pink flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has succulent leaves and stems, adapted to survive in arid and sandy environments. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas and dry grasslands.

Alsine attica plays a role in stabilizing soils and providing habitat for small invertebrates.
