Anemia

Anemia, commonly known as a deficiency of red blood cells or hemoglobin, is not a plant but a medical condition.

However, certain plants are known for their ability to help manage anemia due to their high iron, vitamin B12, and folate content. Plants like spinach, kale, and beetroot are rich in nutrients that support red blood cell production. Some herbal remedies, such as nettle and parsley, are also used traditionally to address anemia.

These plants play a significant role in dietary interventions for individuals with anemia.
