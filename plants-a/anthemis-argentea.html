Anthemis argentea

Anthemis argentea, commonly known as silver fleabane, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and yellow daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Anthemis argentea is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and can also serve as a nectar source for pollinators.
