Artocarpus gongshanensis

Artocarpus gongshanensis, commonly known as the Gongshan breadfruit, is a tropical tree native to China, specifically found in Yunnan Province.

It belongs to the Moraceae family, which includes figs and mulberries, and is characterized by its broad leaves and distinctive fruit. The fruit, which resembles a small breadfruit, develops from an inflorescence called a syconium and is typically green when mature. Its botanical features make it an important species for ecological and horticultural study in its native habitat.
