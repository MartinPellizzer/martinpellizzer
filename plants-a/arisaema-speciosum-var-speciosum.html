Arisaema speciosum var. speciosum

Arisaema speciosum var. speciosum, commonly known as the Chinese cobra plant, is a member of the Araceae family.

It is native to China and is characterized by its striking, hooded spathe and a spadix that bears numerous tiny, white to yellowish flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense colonies.

The leaves are large, heart-shaped, and often have a rough texture, contributing to its ornamental value in gardens.
