Arisaema inclusum

Arisaema inclusum, commonly known as the Chinese arrowhead, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to East Asia.

It features a distinctive arrow-shaped leaf that is often covered in a dense layer of fine hairs. The plant produces a spadix surrounded by a modified leaf called a spathe, which is typically green or yellowish in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Arisaema inclusum is valued for its ornamental appearance and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
