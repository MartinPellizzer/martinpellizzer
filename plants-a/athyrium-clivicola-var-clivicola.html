Athyrium clivicola var. clivicola

Athyrium clivicola var. clivicola, commonly known as the cliff fern, is a variant of the broad-leafed wood fern.

It is native to the mountainous regions of East Asia, including China, Japan, and Korea. This fern typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as rocky crevices and forest floors. Its fronds are bipinnate, with a distinctive dark green color and a slightly glossy texture.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in shady gardens.
