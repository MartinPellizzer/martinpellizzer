Aphelandra rosulata var. albinotata

Aphelandra rosulata var. albinotata, commonly known as the white-veined flame tree, is a striking tropical plant characterized by its large, glossy, heart-shaped leaves with prominent white veins.

Native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia, it thrives in humid, warm climates with high light exposure. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, orange-red flowers that bloom throughout the year, adding vibrant color to its surroundings. Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and ornamental landscaping.

Due to its specific care requirements, it is often cultivated in controlled environments to ensure optimal growth.
