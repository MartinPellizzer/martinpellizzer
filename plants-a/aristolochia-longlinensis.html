Aristolochia longlinensis

Aristolochia longlinensis, commonly known as the Longlin pipevine, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the family Aristolochiaceae and is characterized by its unique, tubular flowers that resemble pipes. The plant produces heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive waxy texture. It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream sides.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is sometimes cultivated in horticulture.
