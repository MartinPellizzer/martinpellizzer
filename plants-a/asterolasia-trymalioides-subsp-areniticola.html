Asterolasia trymalioides subsp. areniticola

Asterolasia trymalioides subsp.

areniticola, commonly known as the coastal asterolasia, is a succulent shrub native to arid and semi-arid regions of southern Africa. It is characterized by its fleshy, ribbed stems and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale pink.

The plant thrives in well-drained sandy or gravelly soils, often found in coastal or inland dune environments. Its leaves are typically narrow and covered in a waxy coating to reduce water loss.

This subspecies is well adapted to harsh, dry conditions and plays a role in stabilizing sandy soils in its native habitat.
