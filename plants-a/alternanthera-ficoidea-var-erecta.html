Alternanthera ficoidea var. erecta

Alternanthera ficoidea var. erecta, commonly known as red flame alternanthera, is a striking aquatic plant native to South America.

It features vibrant red to pink foliage that becomes more intense under strong lighting conditions. The plant typically grows in a compact, upright form, making it ideal for foreground or midground in aquariums. It prefers moderate to high water temperatures and moderate to high light levels.

Its leaves are often used to create a visually appealing contrast in planted tank setups.
