Ageratina Adenophora

Ageratina adenophora is an invasive plant species native to tropical America. It is known for its fluffy white flowers and rapid growth rate. This plant spreads quickly and can outcompete native vegetation. It is often found in disturbed areas such as roadsides and pastures. Controlling its spread is essential to protect local ecosystems.
