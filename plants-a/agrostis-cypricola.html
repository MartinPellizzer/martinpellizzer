Agrostis cypricola

Agrostis cypricola, commonly known as the red hair grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky outcrops. The plant has slender, tufted culms with dense clusters of fine, reddish-brown inflorescences. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green coloration.

Agrostis cypricola is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping and restoration projects.
