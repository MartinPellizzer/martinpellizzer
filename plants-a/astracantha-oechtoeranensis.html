Astracantha oechtoeranensis

Astracantha oechtoeranensis, commonly known as the Echtoeran Astracantha, is a perennial herb native to specific regions of South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and fibrous roots. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest understories or along stream banks. Its leaves are lanceolate and covered with fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow and arranged in dense clusters.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
