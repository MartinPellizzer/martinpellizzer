Alyssum dasycarpum var. pterospermum

Alyssum dasycarpum var. pterospermum, commonly known as the woolly alyssum, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage and small, clustered flowers that bloom in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It is often used in garden settings for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.

Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for rock gardens and borders.
