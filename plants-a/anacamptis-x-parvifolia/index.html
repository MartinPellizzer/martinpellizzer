Anacamptis × Parvifolia

Anacamptis × parvifolia is a hybrid orchid that combines the beauty of its parent species. It is known for its delicate, fragrant flowers that bloom in late spring. This plant thrives in sunny, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows and grasslands. Its unique appearance makes it a favorite among orchid enthusiasts. Despite its elegance, it requires careful cultivation to flourish.
