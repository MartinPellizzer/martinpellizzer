Arisaema sinii

Arisaema sinii, commonly known as the Chinese cobra plant, is a herbaceous perennial native to China.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, hooded spathe and spadix inflorescence. The plant features a rhizomatous root system that allows it to spread and form dense clumps. Its leaves are typically arrow-shaped and can grow up to 30 centimeters in length.

Arisaema sinii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has traditional uses in herbal medicine.
