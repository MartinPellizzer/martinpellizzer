Anaphalis javanica

Anaphalis javanica, commonly known as the silverweed or frost flower, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

Anaphalis javanica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique texture and visual interest.
