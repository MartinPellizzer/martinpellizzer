Aneilema floribundum

Aneilema floribundum, commonly known as the African bellflower, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that typically bloom in clusters. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forest understories.

Aneilema floribundum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
