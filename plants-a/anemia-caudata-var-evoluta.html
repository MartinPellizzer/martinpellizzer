Anemia caudata var. evoluta

Anemia caudata var. evoluta, commonly known as the curled anemone, is a herbaceous perennial native to the eastern United States.

It features showy, pink to purple flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has finely divided, fern-like leaves that are often covered in a soft, woolly texture. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for woodland gardens and native plant landscapes.
