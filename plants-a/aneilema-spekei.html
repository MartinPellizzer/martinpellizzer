Aneilema spekei

Aneilema spekei, commonly known as the Speke's candleplant, is a striking tropical plant native to East Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, candle-like stems that can grow up to several meters in height. The plant features large, waxy, dark green leaves that are often ribbed and have a distinctive appearance. It produces clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers, which bloom in the evening.

Aneilema spekei is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to full sun and well-drained soil.
