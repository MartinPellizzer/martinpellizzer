Albuca annulata

Albuca annulata, commonly known as the ringed bulbous plant, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, ringed, bulbous stem that stores water and nutrients. The plant produces clusters of narrow, grass-like leaves and small, white or pale yellow flowers during the summer months. It prefers well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Albuca annulata is also valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
