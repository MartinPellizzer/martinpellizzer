Atropis angustata

Atropis angustata, commonly known as the slender leaf arrowhead, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive inflorescence consisting of a spadix and a spathe. The plant typically features broad, arrowhead-shaped leaves that are often glossy and deep green. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Its botanical features make it an important species in ecological studies and horticultural cultivation.
