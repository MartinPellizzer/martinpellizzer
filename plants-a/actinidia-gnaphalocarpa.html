Actinidia gnaphalocarpa

Actinidia gnaphalocarpa, commonly known as the Chinese mayapple, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Actinidiaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, greenish-white flowers. The plant produces edible berries that are often used in traditional Chinese medicine. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is valued for its ornamental and medicinal properties.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for both horticultural and pharmacological study.
