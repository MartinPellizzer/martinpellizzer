Acaena macrostemon subsp. longiplumosa

Acaena macrostemon subsp.

The plant produces small, red or orange fruits that are often covered in hooked bracts, aiding in seed dispersal by attaching to animals. Its leaves are typically alternate and have a rough, hairy texture.
