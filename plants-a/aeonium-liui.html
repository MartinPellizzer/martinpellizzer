Aeonium liui

Aeonium liui, commonly known as the Lithuanian Aeonium, is a succulent plant native to the Canary Islands.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, with a waxy texture. The plant produces tall, branching inflorescences that bear yellow flowers, which bloom in late spring or early summer. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a striking addition to any succulent collection.
