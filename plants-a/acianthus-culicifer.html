Acianthus culicifer

Acianthus culicifer, commonly known as the mosquito plant, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, violet-colored flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has a compact, bushy growth habit with narrow, grass-like leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Its unique name comes from the appearance of its flowers, which resemble mosquitoes.
