Aliella helichrysoides subsp. nitidum

Aliella helichrysoides subsp.

nitidum, commonly known as the glossy goldenrod, is a flowering plant native to South Africa. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow, daisy-like flowers.

The subspecies is distinguished by its glossy, narrow leaves and compact growth habit. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
