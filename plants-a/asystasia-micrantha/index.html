Asystasia Micrantha

Common asystasia, scientifically known as Asystasia micrantha, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia. It belongs to the family Acanthaceae and is characterized by its striking violet or purple flowers. This plant is often found in moist, shaded areas and thrives in humid environments. Despite its ornamental appeal, it is also known for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal practices. Its unique appearance and ecological significance make it a notable species in botanical studies.
