Aloe vera var. lanzae

Aloe vera var. lanzae, commonly known as the sword aloe, is a succulent plant native to the Arabian Peninsula.

It is characterized by its tall, narrow, sword-like leaves that are covered in a blue-grayish wax. The leaves are fleshy and store water, allowing the plant to thrive in arid environments. It produces yellow flowers that bloom in summer, adding ornamental value.

This variety is often cultivated for its decorative appeal and has some traditional uses in herbal medicine.
