Achillea kotschyi subsp. kotschyi

Achillea kotschyi subsp.

kotschyi, commonly known as Kotschyi's yarrow, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region. It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils.

The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in flat-topped clusters. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, giving them a silvery appearance.

It is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and coastal areas.
