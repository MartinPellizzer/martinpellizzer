Asplenium crenatoserratum

Asplenium crenatoserratum, commonly known as the notched-serrated spleenwort, is a small, epiphytic fern belonging to the family Aspleniaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped leaves that are often notched and serrated along the margins. The plant typically grows on rocks or tree trunks in moist, shaded environments. Its fronds are usually dark green and have a leathery texture.

Asplenium crenatoserratum is widely distributed in tropical and subtropical regions, where it thrives in humid conditions.
