Atragene × triternata

Atragene × triternata, commonly known as the hybrid spurge, is a flowering plant belonging to the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is characterized by its erect, branching stems and opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy texture. Atragene × triternata is known for its adaptability and is sometimes considered a weed in agricultural settings.
