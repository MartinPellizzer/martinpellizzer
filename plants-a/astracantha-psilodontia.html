Astracantha psilodontia

Astracantha psilodontia, commonly known as the narrow-leafed aster, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and perennial growth habit. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, daisy-like flowers in shades of white and yellow. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and rocky areas.

Astracantha psilodontia is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local pollinators.
