Arenaria jussiaei

Arenaria jussiaei, commonly known as the rock sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to rocky and coastal regions.

It typically features small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink, blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has a compact rosette of leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, helping to retain moisture in arid environments. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in maritime climates.

Its hardy nature makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscaping.
