Acacia brachybotrya f. glabra

Acacia brachybotrya f. glabra, commonly known as the smooth acacia, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, often multi-branched stems and small, clustered leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers in dense spikes, which are typical of the Acacia genus. Its leaves are reduced to small, scale-like structures, giving it a distinctive appearance.

This species is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a hardy plant in dry conditions.
