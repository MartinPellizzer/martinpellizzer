Artemisia Ludoviciana Var. Redolens

artemisia ludoviciana var. redolens is a fragrant perennial shrub known for its distinctive silver-gray foliage and strong aromatic scent. native to the north american plains, it thrives in dry, well-drained soils and full sun. this plant is often used in xeriscaping due to its low water requirements and hardy nature. its leaves emit a fresh, herbal aroma that can be both invigorating and calming. while it is a popular choice for ornamental gardens, it also has historical uses in herbal medicine and as a natural insect repellent.
