Aralia quinquefolia var. notoginseng

Aralia quinquefolia var. notoginseng, commonly known as Chinese ginseng or notoginseng, is a herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

It is characterized by its compound leaves, which typically consist of five leaflets, giving it the species name *quinquefolia*. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in umbels, followed by dark blue berries. Its roots are the primary part used in traditional medicine, valued for their purported health benefits.

The botanical classification places it within the Araliaceae family, which includes other plants with similar medicinal uses.
