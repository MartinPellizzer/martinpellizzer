Amethystanthus excisus var. coreanus

Amethystanthus excisus var. coreanus, commonly known as the Korean amethyst flower, is a flowering plant native to Korea.

It is characterized by its striking violet to purple flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery pubescence. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a suitable candidate for rock gardens or containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long-lasting blooms and unique coloration.
