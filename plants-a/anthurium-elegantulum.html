Anthurium elegantulum

Anthurium elegantulum, commonly known as the elegant arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are often brightly colored with a deep green to reddish hue. The plant produces vibrant spadices, which are the modified leaves that enclose the true flowers. It thrives in humid, warm environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Anthurium elegantulum is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
