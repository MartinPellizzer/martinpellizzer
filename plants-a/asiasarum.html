Asiasarum

Asiasarum, commonly known as the Asian ginseng or Chinese ginseng, is a herbaceous plant native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the Araliaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, fleshy roots that are highly valued in traditional medicine. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments and has compound leaves with serrated edges. Its flowers are small and greenish-white, often clustered in umbels, and it produces berries that contain the seeds.

Asiasarum is known for its adaptogenic properties and has been used for centuries to enhance vitality and reduce stress.
