Allium rubens

Allium rubens, commonly known as the red onion, is a bulbous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features a spherical, fleshy bulb that is typically red or purple in color. The plant produces tall, hollow stems with narrow, grass-like leaves. Its flowers are clustered in umbels, consisting of small, white or pale pink petals.

Allium rubens is valued for its culinary use and also for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
