Alsophila heteromorpha

Alsophila heteromorpha, commonly known as the giant chain fern, is a species of tree fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate fronds that can reach several meters in length. The plant has a robust rhizome system that allows it to thrive in humid, shaded environments. Its fronds are typically dark green and have a leathery texture, contributing to its ornamental value.

Alsophila heteromorpha plays an important ecological role in tropical forests by providing habitat and shelter for various organisms.
