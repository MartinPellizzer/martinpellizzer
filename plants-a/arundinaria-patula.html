Arundinaria patula

Arundinaria patula, commonly known as the Chinese bamboo or golden bamboo, is a species of grass native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems that can grow up to 6 meters in height. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are often a striking golden or yellowish-green color. It is a clump-forming plant that spreads slowly through rhizomes, making it suitable for ornamental landscaping.

Its rapid growth and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions make it a popular choice for gardeners.
