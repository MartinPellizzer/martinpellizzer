Anthurium brevipes

Anthurium brevipes, commonly known as the short-stemmed anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped leaves that are often a deep green with a glossy texture. The plant produces striking spadices, which are modified leaves that enclose the small, inconspicuous flowers. Anthurium brevipes thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical landscapes.
