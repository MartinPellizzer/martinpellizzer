Adelobotrys fuscescens

Adelobotrys fuscescens, commonly known as the black thorn.

It is a flowering plant native to southern Africa, characterized by its woody, thorny stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces red, fleshy fruits that are edible for certain animals but toxic to humans. It thrives in dry, rocky environments and is often found in shrublands and woodlands.

Its botanical features make it an important component of its native ecosystem.
