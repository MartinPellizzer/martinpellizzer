Aster Rigidus

Aster rigidus is a hardy perennial plant known for its striking blue to purple flowers. It thrives in dry, rocky soils and is commonly found in arid regions across North America. This plant is a member of the Asteraceae family, which includes many familiar wildflowers. Its rigid stems and compact growth make it well adapted to harsh environments. Aster rigidus is both an ornamental and ecological valuable species in native landscapes.
