Aglaia roxburghiana var. courtallensis

Aglaia roxburghiana var. courtallensis, commonly known as the Courtallense Aglaia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a member of the Meliaceae family, characterized by its ornamental value and aromatic properties. The plant typically features compound leaves with opposite arrangement and produces small, yellowish-green flowers. Its fruit is a woody capsule that contains several seeds.
