Aeschynomene chimanimaniensis

Aeschynomene chimanimaniensis, commonly known as the Chimanimani sicklegrass, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its slender, creeping stems and small, trifoliate leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in racemes, followed by pods that contain several seeds.

It is often found in disturbed soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.
