Alismorkis clavata

Alismorkis clavata, commonly known as the clustered water plantain, is a herbaceous aquatic plant native to wetlands and marshy areas.

It features narrow, linear leaves that are often submerged or floating, with a distinctive clustering growth habit. The plant produces small, white or greenish flowers that are typically clustered at the base of the leaves. It thrives in shallow water and is often found in temperate regions.
