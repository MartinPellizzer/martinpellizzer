Aglaonema ovatum

Aglaonema ovatum, commonly known as the silver queen plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features striking, waxy leaves with a distinctive silver and green coloration, often with a pale yellow or white margin. The plant is known for its compact growth habit and ability to thrive in low-light conditions, making it a popular houseplant. Its foliage is adorned with intricate patterns that add visual interest to any indoor space.

Aglaonema ovatum is also noted for its air-purifying qualities, contributing to improved indoor air quality.
