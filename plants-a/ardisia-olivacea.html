Ardisia olivacea

Ardisia olivacea, commonly known as the oliveleaf ardisia, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, dark green elliptical leaves that are arranged alternately along its stems. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that are clustered in umbels, followed by bright red, fleshy berries. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for horticultural use in shaded gardens.
