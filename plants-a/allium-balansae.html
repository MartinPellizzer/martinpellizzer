Allium balansae

Allium balansae, commonly known as the Balansae onion, is a species of flowering plant in the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of central and eastern Turkey. The plant produces small, white or pinkish flowers arranged in umbels, and its bulbs are often used in traditional herbal medicine. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers a sunny to partially shaded environment.

Allium balansae is valued for its ornamental beauty and potential medicinal properties.
