Arausiaca

Arausiaca, commonly known as the flame flower, is a striking plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, fiery red flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green, providing a striking contrast to its colorful blooms. Arausiaca thrives in well-drained soil and requires plenty of sunlight to flourish.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and tropical landscapes.
