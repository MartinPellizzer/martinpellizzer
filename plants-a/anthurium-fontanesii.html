Anthurium fontanesii

Anthurium fontanesii, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a striking tropical plant native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped spathes that range in color from deep red to orange, often with a contrasting green or white inner surface. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers within the spathe, which are typically surrounded by a modified leaf called a spadix. Anthurium fontanesii thrives in high humidity and indirect light, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique foliage and long-lasting blooms make it a desirable ornamental plant.
