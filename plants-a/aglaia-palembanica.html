Aglaia palembanica

Aglaia palembanica, commonly known as the Palembang aglaia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Meliaceae family, which includes mahogany and other hardwood trees. The plant has some traditional uses in local medicine and is valued for its ornamental qualities.
