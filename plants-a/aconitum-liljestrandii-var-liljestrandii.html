Aconitum liljestrandii var. liljestrandii

Aconitum liljestrandii var. liljestrandii, commonly known as the Liljestrand's aconite, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features striking blue to purple flowers that bloom in late summer, typically from July to August. The plant has palmately compound leaves with deeply lobed segments, and its stems are typically tall and erect. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade, often found in alpine or subalpine environments.

Due to its toxic alkaloids, it is important to handle the plant with care and avoid ingestion.
