Ardisia purpurascens var. genuina

Ardisia purpurascens var. genuina, commonly known as the purple-leaf ardisia, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are typically a deep purple to dark green in color. The plant produces small, white flowers followed by bright red berries, which are an attractive feature. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
