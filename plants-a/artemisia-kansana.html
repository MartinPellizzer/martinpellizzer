Artemisia kansana

Artemisia kansana, commonly known as western sagebrush, is a shrub native to the western United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its aromatic, silver-gray foliage and small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, often growing in sandy or rocky soils. It plays an important role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various wildlife species.

Its essential oils have been used traditionally for their medicinal and aromatic properties.
