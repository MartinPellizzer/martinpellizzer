Ardisia clavelligera

Ardisia clavelligera, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen or star anise tree, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub or tree with glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers that are followed by bright red berries. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade.

Ardisia clavelligera is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
