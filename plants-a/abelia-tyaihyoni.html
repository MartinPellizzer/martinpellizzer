Abelia tyaihyoni

Abelia tyaihyoni, commonly known as the Chinese abelia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, fragrant, white to pinkish flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that turn red in the fall. It prefers partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

Its ornamental value comes from its showy blooms and seasonal color changes.
