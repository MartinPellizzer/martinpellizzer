Anthurium popayanense

Anthurium popayanense, commonly known as the Popayan anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Colombia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves and vibrant spadix. The plant produces a spathe, which is often brightly colored, typically in shades of red or orange. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Anthurium popayanense is valued in horticulture for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated as a houseplant.
