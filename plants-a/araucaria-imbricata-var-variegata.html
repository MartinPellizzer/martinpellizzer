Araucaria imbricata var. variegata

Araucaria imbricata var. variegata, commonly known as the variegated monkey puzzle tree, is a striking evergreen conifer native to Chile.

It is characterized by its distinctive, scale-like leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. The tree has a tall, narrow, conical shape with a straight trunk and dense, tiered branches. Its foliage is typically a mix of dark green and lighter colored patterns, adding visual interest.

This variant is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
