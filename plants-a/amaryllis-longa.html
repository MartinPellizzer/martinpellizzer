Amaryllis longa

Amaryllis longa, commonly known as the long-stemmed amaryllis, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its tall, sturdy stems that can reach up to 3 meters in height. The plant produces large, showy flowers in shades of red, white, and pink, often with a strong, spicy fragrance. Its leaves are broad and grass-like, and it thrives in warm, humid environments.

Amaryllis longa is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
