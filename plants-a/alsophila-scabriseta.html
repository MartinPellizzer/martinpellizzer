Alsophila scabriseta

Alsophila scabriseta, commonly known as the scaly tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feathery fronds that give it a distinctive appearance. The fronds are typically dark green and have a rough, scaly texture on their undersides, contributing to its common name. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical forests.

This plant plays an important role in its ecosystem by providing habitat and contributing to forest biodiversity.
