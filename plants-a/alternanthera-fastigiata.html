Alternanthera fastigiata

Alternanthera fastigiata, commonly known as the upright Alternanthera, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, feather-like leaves that can display a range of colors, including red, pink, and purple, depending on light conditions. The plant typically grows as a compact shrub with upright stems and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in water gardens and containers. It prefers full sun to partial shade and thrives in warm, humid environments.

Its foliage changes color with the seasons, making it a visually dynamic addition to aquatic and terrestrial landscapes.
