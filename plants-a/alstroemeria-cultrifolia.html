Alstroemeria cultrifolia

Alstroemeria cultrifolia, commonly known as the swordleaf alstroemeria, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its narrow, sword-shaped leaves and clusters of colorful, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shady environments and is often found in temperate regions. Its flowers come in a variety of colors, including red, orange, yellow, and pink, making it a popular ornamental plant.

Alstroemeria cultrifolia is also valued for its ability to thrive in partial shade and its relatively low maintenance requirements.
