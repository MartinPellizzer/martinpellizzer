Anguloa × media

Anguloa × media, commonly known as the hybrid anguloa, is a hybrid plant resulting from crosses between species of the genus Anguloa.

It is native to the Andes region of South America, where it thrives in cool, humid climates. The plant is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that come in a variety of colors, including pink, red, and white. It is a perennial herb with a fibrous root system and broad, lance-shaped leaves.

Anguloa × media is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.
