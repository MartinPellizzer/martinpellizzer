Armeria maritima subsp. itala

Armeria maritima subsp.

itala, commonly known as the Italian sea thrift, is a low-growing, evergreen flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region. It produces dense clusters of white, pink, or purple flowers that bloom in early spring.

The plant thrives in well-drained, sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in coastal areas. It has narrow, needle-like leaves that are gray-green in color.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to stabilize coastal dunes.
