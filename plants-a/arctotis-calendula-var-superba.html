Arctotis calendula var. superba

Arctotis calendula var. superba, commonly known as the African daisy, is a striking flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, daisy-like flowers that range in color from yellow to orange and red. The plant typically grows to a height of 30 to 60 centimeters and has narrow, grass-like leaves. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.

Its botanical features make it both ornamental and ecologically valuable in its native habitat.
