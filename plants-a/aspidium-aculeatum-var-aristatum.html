Aspidium aculeatum var. aristatum

Aspidium aculeatum var. aristatum, commonly known as the aristate water fern, is a variant of the common water fern.

It is a fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae, characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as stream sides and wetlands. Its fronds are usually dark green and have a distinctive, serrated edge.

It is known for its ability to thrive in aquatic or semi-aquatic conditions.
