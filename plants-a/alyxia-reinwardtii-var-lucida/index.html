Alyxia Reinwardtii Var. Lucida

Alyxia reinwardtii var. lucida is a rare and striking flowering plant native to southern Africa. It is known for its vibrant, tubular flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for xeriscaping. Its unique appearance and adaptability have made it a subject of interest among botanists and gardeners alike. Despite its beauty, it remains underappreciated and often overlooked in the plant world.
