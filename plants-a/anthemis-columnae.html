Anthemis columnae

Anthemis columnae, commonly known as the columned fleur-de-lis, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb with a compact, bushy growth habit and produces yellow-orange flowers that bloom in summer. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and has a strong, aromatic scent. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its historical use in herbal medicine is noted for its potential anti-inflammatory properties.
