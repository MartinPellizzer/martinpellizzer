Agonandra excelsa

Agonandra excelsa, commonly known as the giant flame tree, is a striking tropical plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its large, showy red flowers that bloom in clusters, creating a dramatic visual impact. The plant has a tall, straight trunk with a smooth, greyish-brown bark. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and ovate, with a dark green upper surface and a lighter underside.

Agonandra excelsa thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
