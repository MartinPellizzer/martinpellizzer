Ambrosia Chamissonis Var. Bipinnatisecta

Ambrosia chamissonis var. bipinnatisecta is a striking plant known for its delicate, fern-like foliage. Native to the western united states, it thrives in coastal and sandy environments. Its leaves are finely divided, giving it a feathery appearance that catches the light beautifully. This variety is often found in disturbed soils and is adapted to harsh, maritime conditions. It plays an important role in local ecosystems and is a popular choice for native gardens.
