Arisaema thunbergii f. geomundoense

Arisaema thunbergii f. geomundoense, commonly known as the Geomundo Arisaema, is a unique variant of the common arisaema species.

It is characterized by its striking, often colorful spathe that encloses the spadix, a feature typical of the Araceae family. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in mountainous regions of Southeast Asia. Its botanical structure includes a rhizomatous root system and palmate leaves with a distinct, waxy texture.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
