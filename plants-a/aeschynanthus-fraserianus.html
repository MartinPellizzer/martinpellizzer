Aeschynanthus fraserianus

Aeschynanthus fraserianus, commonly known as the coral vine, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant produces small, fleshy fruits that contain seeds covered in a sticky, resinous coating. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its striking appearance.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for hanging baskets and greenhouses.
