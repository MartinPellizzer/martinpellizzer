Athyrium sandwichianum

Athyrium sandwichianum, commonly known as the Sandwich Island fern, is a species native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It is a member of the Dryopteridaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, feather-like fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest understories. Its rhizomes are creeping and often form dense colonies.

The fronds are bipinnate, with a distinctive appearance that makes it easily recognizable in its native habitat.
