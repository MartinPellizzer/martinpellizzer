Actinostemon concolor var. ellipticus

Actinostemon concolor var. ellipticus, commonly known as the smoothleaf actinostemon, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the southern United States.

It features lance-shaped, smooth, dark green leaves that are typically arranged alternately along the stems. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of pink to purple, which bloom in the spring. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full sun, making it suitable for wetland or garden environments.

Its botanical structure supports its role in both ornamental gardening and ecological habitats.
