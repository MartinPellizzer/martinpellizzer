Aerides picotiana

Aerides picotiana, commonly known as the Picotiana orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a rhizomatous growth habit and produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest undergrowth. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink with a distinctive red or purple lip.

Aerides picotiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
