Aspidium fasciculatum

Aspidium fasciculatum, commonly known as the clustered water fern, is a species of fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests or along stream banks. Its rhizomes are often clustered, giving it the common name "clustered water fern.".
