Alsophila decrescens

Alsophila decrescens, commonly known as the slender tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate fronds that can reach several meters in length. The plant has a distinctive crown of fertile fronds that produce spores, contributing to its reproductive cycle. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forests.

Alsophila decrescens plays an important ecological role in its habitat by providing shelter and food for various organisms.
