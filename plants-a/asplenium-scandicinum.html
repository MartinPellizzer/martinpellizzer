Asplenium scandicinum

Asplenium scandicinum, commonly known as the climbing spleenwort, is a fern belonging to the family Aspleniaceae.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that typically grows on trees and rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant has slender, wiry stems that allow it to climb and spread across surfaces. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and have a delicate, feathery appearance.

Asplenium scandicinum is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, where it thrives in moist, nutrient-rich conditions.
