Arisaema hastatum

Arisaema hastatum, commonly known as the Chinese arrowhead, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to East Asia.

It features a distinctive arrow-shaped leaf that is often deeply lobed, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces a spadix surrounded by a spathe, a characteristic feature of the Araceae family. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical structure supports its role as a tropical and subtropical ornamental plant.
