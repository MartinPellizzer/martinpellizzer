Avena planiculmis

Avena planiculmis, commonly known as the flat-leaf oat, is a perennial grass native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, flat leaves and compact, spike-like inflorescence. The plant produces small, two-lobed flowers that are typically greenish or brownish in color. Avena planiculmis thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows and along stream banks.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
