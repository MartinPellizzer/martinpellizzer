Alchemilla geranioides var. pentagona

Alchemilla geranioides var. pentagona, commonly known as the pentagonal lady's mantle, is a low-growing perennial plant native to Europe.

It features dense clusters of silver-white, shield-shaped leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, pinkish-white flowers in late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and woodland settings.
