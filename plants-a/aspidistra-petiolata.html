Aspidistra petiolata

Aspidistra petiolata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf aspidistra, is a species of flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the family Araceae, which includes plants like peace lilies and calla lilies. The plant features dark green, narrow, and elongated leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. It produces small, inconspicuous white flowers enclosed within a spadix, which is typical of the Araceae family.

Aspidistra petiolata is often cultivated for its attractive foliage and ability to thrive in low-light conditions.
