Alternanthera porrigens var. mearsii

Alternanthera porrigens var. mearsii, commonly known as the red-leaf alternanthera, is a strikingly ornamental plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red foliage, which becomes more intense under bright light conditions. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often adorned with a pinkish or reddish hue. It thrives in moist, well-draining soil and prefers partial to full sunlight.

This variety is popular in aquariums and water gardens due to its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.
