Alsophila aculeata

Alsophila aculeata, commonly known as the hawksbeard tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk covered in persistent, fibrous roots. The plant features large, feather-like fronds that grow in a dense, bushy crown. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in coastal and mountainous regions.

Alsophila aculeata plays an important ecological role in its native habitat, providing shelter and food for various wildlife species.
