Arisaema filiforme var. sumatranum

Arisaema filiforme var. sumatranum, commonly known as the slender arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its distinctive arrow-shaped leaves and a spadix inflorescence. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or wetlands. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

The flowers are usually greenish-white, with a spathe that encloses the spadix.
