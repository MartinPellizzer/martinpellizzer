Andromeda pulverulenta

Andromeda pulverulenta, commonly known as the glossy green andromeda, is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to the eastern United States.

It features dense clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, blooming in early spring. The plant has dark green, leathery leaves that provide year-round foliage. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is often used in woodland gardens and as a ground cover.
