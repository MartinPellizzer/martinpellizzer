Araucaria muelleri

Araucaria muelleri, commonly known as the Mueller's Araucaria, is a coniferous tree native to southeastern Australia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and a crown of dense, pyramidal branches. The tree produces small, cone-like structures that contain both male and female reproductive organs. Its foliage consists of needle-like leaves that are typically dark green and arranged in spirals.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and parks for its unique appearance.
