Aneilema dregeanum subsp. mossambicense

Aneilema dregeanum subsp.

mossambicense, commonly known as the Mozambique arrowhead, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to eastern Africa. It features striking, arrow-shaped leaves that are typically bright green with a waxy texture.

The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers during the rainy season. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in marshy or swampy areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for wetland gardens and water features.
