Asplenium salicifolium var. austrobrasiliense

Asplenium salicifolium var. austrobrasiliense, commonly known as the Brazilian wall fern, is a fern species native to South America.

It belongs to the family Aspleniaceae and is characterized by its delicate, leathery fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest undergrowth or rocky crevices. Its leaves are pinnately divided, with a lanceolate shape and a serrated margin.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
