Acer platanoides var. reitenbachii

Acer platanoides var. reitenbachii, commonly known as the Reitenbachii Norway maple, is a variant of the Norway maple characterized by its distinctive foliage.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a serrated margin and a broad, lobed structure. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in early spring, followed by winged samaras that aid in seed dispersal. It is a deciduous tree that thrives in temperate climates, preferring well-drained soils and full to partial sunlight.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping and urban gardens.
