Anthurium bayae

Anthurium bayae, commonly known as the Bayae arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its unique inflorescence consisting of a spadix and a spatula-shaped bract. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy, dark green leaves that are often variegated with white or silver markings. Its striking, colorful spadix can range from red to yellow, depending on the variety.

Anthurium bayae thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its aesthetic appeal.
