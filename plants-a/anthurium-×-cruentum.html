Anthurium × cruentum

Anthurium × cruentum, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Araceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Central and South America. The plant is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves and vibrant red spadix, which is surrounded by a colorful spathe. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and requires well-draining soil.

Anthurium × cruentum is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its long-lasting blooms and ornamental foliage.
