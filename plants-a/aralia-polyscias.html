Aralia polyscias

Aralia polyscias, commonly known as the evergreen aralia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compound leaves, which are often divided into numerous narrow, lance-shaped segments. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in umbels, followed by dark blue berries. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often used as an ornamental plant in gardens.

Its foliage provides a lush, dense appearance, making it popular in indoor and tropical landscapes.
