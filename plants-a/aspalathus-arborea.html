Aspalathus arborea

Aspalathus arborea, commonly known as the silver leaf bush, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-green leaves and small, yellowish flowers that bloom in spring. The plant is often found in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in well-drained environments. It has a compact growth habit and is commonly used in landscaping for its ornamental value.

Aspalathus arborea is also known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
