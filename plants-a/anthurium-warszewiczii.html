Anthurium warszewiczii

Anthurium warszewiczii, commonly known as the Warszewicz's anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are often bright green with a metallic sheen. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-like inflorescences surrounded by colorful spathe, typically in shades of red, orange, or yellow. It thrives in humid, warm environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium warszewiczii is popular as a ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
