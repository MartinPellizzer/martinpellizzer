Adesmia horrida var. minor

Adesmia horrida var. minor, commonly known as the lesser horrid adesmia, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, clustered flowers that are typically pale yellow to cream in color. The plant produces small, elliptical leaves that are usually hairy, giving it a rough texture. It is native to arid and semi-arid regions of Australia, where it thrives in well-drained soils.

This species is often found in open woodlands and along rocky outcrops, showcasing its adaptability to harsh environmental conditions.
