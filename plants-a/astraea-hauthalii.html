Astraea hauthalii

Astraea hauthalii, commonly known as the star orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale blue in color. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, aiding in its survival in arid environments. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Due to its unique appearance and rarity, Astraea hauthalii is a prized species among orchid enthusiasts.
