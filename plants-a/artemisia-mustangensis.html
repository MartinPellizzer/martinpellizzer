Artemisia mustangensis

Artemisia mustangensis, commonly known as the mustang sage, is a perennial shrub native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its aromatic, silver-gray foliage and small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in arid and semi-arid environments. It belongs to the sunflower family (Asteraceae) and is known for its medicinal and aromatic properties.

Artemisia mustangensis is also valued for its role in traditional herbal practices and ecological stability in its native habitats.
