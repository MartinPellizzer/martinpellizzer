Alsophila rigens

Alsophila rigens, commonly known as the rigid tree fern, is a species native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and a crown of feather-like fronds that can grow quite large. The plant belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is known for its ability to thrive in moist, shaded environments. Its rhizome system supports its growth, and it can reach heights of up to 10 meters in optimal conditions.

Alsophila rigens is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to add a lush, tropical aesthetic to gardens.
