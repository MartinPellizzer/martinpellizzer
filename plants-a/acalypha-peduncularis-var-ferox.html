Acalypha peduncularis var. ferox

Acalypha peduncularis var. ferox, commonly known as the ferox spurflower, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, spiky inflorescences that are often red or orange in color. The plant has palmate leaves with five to seven lobes, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or swampy areas.
