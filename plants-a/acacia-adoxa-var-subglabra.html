Acacia adoxa var. subglabra

Acacia adoxa var. subglabra, commonly known as the smooth acacia, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It features small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in dense, spherical inflorescences. The leaves are simple, alternate, and have a smooth texture, which distinguishes it from other acacia varieties. The plant is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky soils.

Its botanical characteristics make it an important species in local ecosystems and traditional medicine.
