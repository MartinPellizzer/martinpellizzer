Amomyrtella

Amomyrtella, commonly known as the Christmas bell, is a genus of flowering plants native to South America.

These plants are shrubs or small trees, typically found in tropical and subtropical regions. They are characterized by their showy, bell-shaped flowers that come in various colors, including white, yellow, and red. The leaves are usually simple and alternate, with a glossy surface.

Amomyrtella species are valued for their ornamental appeal and are often cultivated in gardens and as houseplants.
