Aster mackaui

Aster mackaui, commonly known as the Mackaui aster, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. It produces showy purple to blue flowers that bloom in the summer months.

Its botanical features make it an important component of native plant communities and a subject of ecological study.
