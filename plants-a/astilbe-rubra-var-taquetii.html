Astilbe rubra var. taquetii

Astilbe rubra var. taquetii, commonly known as the Taquet's red astilbe, is a herbaceous perennial native to China.

It features upright, branching stems that bear dense clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of pink to red. The plant has feather-like, bipinnate leaves that are dark green and finely textured. It thrives in partial to full shade and moist, well-drained soil.

It is valued in gardens for its ornamental foliage and delicate floral display.
