Aster schlechteri

Aster schlechteri, commonly known as the Schlechter's aster, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically features narrow, linear leaves and produces showy blue to purple flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in alpine or rocky habitats.

Due to its limited distribution and specific ecological requirements, it is considered a rare and somewhat elusive species.
