Astragalus onobrychis f. microphyllus

Astragalus onobrychis f. microphyllus, commonly known as the narrow-leaved bur pea, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its small, compound leaves and yellow pea-like flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is native to regions of Europe and western Asia. It is often found in grasslands, wastelands, and along roadsides.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
