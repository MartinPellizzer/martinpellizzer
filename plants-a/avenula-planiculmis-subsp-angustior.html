Avenula planiculmis subsp. angustior

Avenula planiculmis subsp.

angustior, commonly known as the narrow-leaved meadow grass, is a flowering plant belonging to the grass family Poaceae. It is characterized by its slender, grass-like appearance with narrow, linear leaves and small, spike-like inflorescences.

This subspecies is often found in temperate regions and is valued for its ecological role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for invertebrates.
