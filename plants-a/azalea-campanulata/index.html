Azalea Campanulata

Azalea campanulata is a striking evergreen shrub native to the eastern coast of asia. it is known for its bell-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white during spring. this plant thrives in acidic soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for gardens. its elegant appearance and hardy nature make it a favorite among botanists and gardeners alike. with proper care, azalea campanulata can provide a beautiful display for many seasons.
