Aloe pluridens var. beckeri

Aloe pluridens var. beckeri, commonly known as the Becker's aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a waxy coating, which helps it retain moisture in arid environments. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow flowers, blooming in the spring. It is well adapted to full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental value make it a desirable plant for both horticultural and botanical collections.
