Aloe wrefordii

Aloe wrefordii, commonly known as the narrow-leaf aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features narrow, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow flowers during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it suitable for arid climates.

Aloe wrefordii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional medicinal uses.
