Antirrhinum meonanthum subsp. salcedoi

Antirrhinum meonanthum subsp.

salcedoi, commonly known as the Salcedo's snapdragon, is a flowering plant native to the Iberian Peninsula. It belongs to the genus Antirrhinum, which includes various species of snapdragons, and is characterized by its distinctive, bilaterally symmetrical flowers.
