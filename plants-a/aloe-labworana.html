Aloe labworana

Aloe labworana, commonly known as the Natal aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a reddish tinge, often adorned with white or yellow tubercles. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing yellow or orange flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires ample sunlight, making it suitable for arid and semi-arid environments.

Aloe labworana is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used traditionally for its medicinal properties.
