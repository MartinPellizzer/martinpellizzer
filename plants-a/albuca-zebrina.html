Albuca zebrina

Albuca zebrina, commonly known as the zebra plant, is a bulbous perennial native to southern Africa.

It features striking, grass-like leaves with distinctive white and green stripes, giving it its common name. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a desirable addition to both indoor and outdoor collections.
