Asplundia lutea

Asplundia lutea, commonly known as the yellow asplundia, is a flowering plant belonging to the Araceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant features heart-shaped leaves and produces striking yellow flowers with a spathe and spadix structure. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Its botanical characteristics make it a unique addition to tropical gardens and greenhouses.
