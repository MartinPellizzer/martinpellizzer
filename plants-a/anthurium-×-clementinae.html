Anthurium × clementinae

Anthurium × clementinae, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Araceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped spathe that ranges from red to orange in color. Its inflorescence consists of a spadix surrounded by the colorful spathe, which is actually a modified leaf.

Anthurium × clementinae is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in greenhouse settings.
