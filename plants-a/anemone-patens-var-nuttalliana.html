Anemone patens var. nuttalliana

Anemone patens var. nuttalliana, commonly known as the Nuttall's anemone, is a herbaceous perennial native to the western United States.

It features showy, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has basal leaves that are lobed and hairy, with a rosette growth habit. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.

Its botanical characteristics make it a desirable addition to native plant gardens and wildflower habitats.
