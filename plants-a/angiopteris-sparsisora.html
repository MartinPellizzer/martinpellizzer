Angiopteris sparsisora

Angiopteris sparsisora, commonly known as the sparse-veined fern, is a tropical fern species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, pinnately compound fronds that can reach up to 2 meters in length. The leaves are typically dark green with a sparse venation pattern, giving it its common name. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant.
