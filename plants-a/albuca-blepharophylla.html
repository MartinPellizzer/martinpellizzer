Albuca blepharophylla

Albuca blepharophylla, commonly known as the starry albuca, is a bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features striking, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Albuca blepharophylla is often cultivated for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
