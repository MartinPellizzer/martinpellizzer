Alsinanthus balearicus

Alsinanthus balearicus, commonly known as the Balearic sandwort, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that typically reaches heights of about 10 to 20 centimeters. The plant features small, white or pale pink flowers arranged in dense clusters, which bloom in the spring. Its leaves are linear and succulent, adapted to conserve water in arid environments.

Alsinanthus balearicus is often found in coastal dunes and rocky soils, showcasing its resilience to harsh conditions.
