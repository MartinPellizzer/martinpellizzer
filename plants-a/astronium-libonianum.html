Astronium libonianum

Astronium libonianum, commonly known as the Brazilian star apple, is a tropical evergreen tree native to South America.

It belongs to the family Anacardiaceae and is characterized by its smooth, light gray bark and large, elliptical leaves. The tree produces yellowish-green flowers that are followed by round, star-shaped fruits with a sweet, aromatic pulp.

The fruit is typically eaten fresh or used in jams and beverages, and the plant is valued for its ornamental and culinary uses.
