Ambrosia cumanensis

Ambrosia cumanensis, commonly known as the Cumanese ambrosia, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant produces small, yellow to greenish flowers that are typically clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, and it often grows in disturbed or open habitats.

Ambrosia cumanensis is known for its allergenic pollen, which can contribute to seasonal allergies in some individuals.
