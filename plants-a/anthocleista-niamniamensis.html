Anthocleista niamniamensis

Anthocleista niamniamensis, commonly known as the African moonseed, is a flowering plant native to central and western Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height, characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and opposite, simple leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in clusters, followed by capsule fruits containing several seeds.

It thrives in tropical and subtropical regions, often found in moist forests and along riverbanks.
