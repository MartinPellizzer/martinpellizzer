Amorphophallus yaoi

Amorphophallus yaoi, commonly known as the Yaoi arrowhead, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its unique inflorescence consisting of a spadix and a spathe. The plant produces large, showy flowers that are often white or cream in color, with a strong, unpleasant odor to attract pollinators. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The tuberous roots of Amorphophallus yaoi can be used in traditional medicine and cooking in some cultures.
