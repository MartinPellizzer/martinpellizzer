Antinoria agrostidea subsp. agrostidea

Antinoria agrostidea subsp.

agrostidea, commonly known as the narrowleaf antinoria, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa. It typically grows in open grasslands and rocky soils, thriving in full sun to partial shade.

The plant features slender, grass-like leaves that are narrow and linear, often with a bluish-green hue. Its flowering stems produce small, clustered inflorescences with tiny, white to pale yellow flowers.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively low maintenance in suitable climates.
