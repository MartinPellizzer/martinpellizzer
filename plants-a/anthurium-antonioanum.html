Anthurium antonioanum

Anthurium antonioanum, commonly known as the Antonio's Anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are deep green with a glossy texture. The plant produces vibrant, spathiflorous flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, which are actually modified leaves called spathes. Anthurium antonioanum thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

It is a popular ornamental plant due to its unique foliage and long-lasting blooms.
