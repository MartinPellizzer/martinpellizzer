Aeschynomene lyonnetii

Aeschynomene lyonnetii, commonly known as Lyonnet's sicklepod, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, yellow flowers. The plant produces distinctive, curved seed pods that contain small, black seeds. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in arid or semi-arid environments.

Due to its adaptability, it is sometimes considered a weed in agricultural settings.
