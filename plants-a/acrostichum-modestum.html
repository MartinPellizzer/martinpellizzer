Acrostichum modestum

Acrostichum modestum, commonly known as the slender fern, is a species of fern native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, feather-like fronds that are typically green and glossy. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories. Its reproductive structures, including sori, are located on the underside of the fronds.

Acrostichum modestum plays an important role in its ecosystem by providing habitat and contributing to soil stability.
