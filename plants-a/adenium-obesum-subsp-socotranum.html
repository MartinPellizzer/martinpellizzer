Adenium obesum subsp. socotranum

Adenium obesum subsp.

socotranum, commonly known as the Socotra candelabra, is a unique succulent native to the Socotra archipelago. It is characterized by its swollen, bottle-shaped trunk and branched, often thornless stems.

The plant has thick, waxy leaves that are adapted to store water, typical of arid environments. It produces striking yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months.

Its distinctive morphology and ecological adaptations make it a notable species in its native habitat.
