Agatea vieillardii

Agatea vieillardii, commonly known as the Vieillard's agate tree, is a unique member of the Malvaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its striking agate-like bark, which gives it its common name. It produces small, white flowers that bloom in clusters, adding to its ornamental value.
