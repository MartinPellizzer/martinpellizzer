Aster douglasii

Aster douglasii, commonly known as the Douglas aster, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its showy purple or blue flowers that bloom in late summer and early fall. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and grasslands. It has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, tubular flowers arranged in a composite head.

Aster douglasii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in native plant gardens.
