Adenanthos strictus

Adenanthos strictus, commonly known as the narrow-leaved adenanthos, is a low-growing shrub native to southeastern Australia.

It is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a soft texture. The plant produces small, clustered yellow flowers that bloom in spring, adding a delicate floral display. It is typically found in dry, sandy soils and thrives in sunny, well-drained environments.

Adenanthos strictus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in native gardens.
