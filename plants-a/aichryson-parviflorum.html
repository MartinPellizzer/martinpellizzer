Aichryson parviflorum

Aichryson parviflorum, commonly known as the silverleaf aichryson, is a small, evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It features dense, silver-gray foliage with a compact, rounded growth habit. The plant produces small, yellow flowers in spring, which are clustered in inflorescences. It is well adapted to dry, rocky environments and requires minimal water once established.

Aichryson parviflorum is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive appearance and drought tolerance.
