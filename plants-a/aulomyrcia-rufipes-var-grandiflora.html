Aulomyrcia rufipes var. grandiflora

Aulomyrcia rufipes var. grandiflora, commonly known as the grandiflora variant of the red-footed aulomyrcia, is a tropical plant native to Central America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red and orange. The plant has compound leaves with serrated margins and thrives in warm, humid environments. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant floral display.

However, it requires well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight for optimal growth.
