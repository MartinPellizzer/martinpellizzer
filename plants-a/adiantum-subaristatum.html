Adiantum subaristatum

Adiantum subaristatum, commonly known as the rock fern, is a species of fern characterized by its delicate, lace-like fronds.

It belongs to the family Adiantaceae and is native to regions of South America, particularly in Brazil. The plant typically grows on rocky outcrops and in moist, shaded environments. Its fronds are bipinnate, with a distinctive, arching appearance that gives it an elegant look.

Adiantum subaristatum is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
