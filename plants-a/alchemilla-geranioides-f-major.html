Alchemilla geranioides f. major

Alchemilla geranioides f. major, commonly known as the large-leaved lady's mantle, is a perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features broad, hairy leaves that are often arranged in a rosette pattern, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Its foliage is used in traditional herbal medicine for its purported soothing properties.
