Adiantum fragile

Adiantum fragile, commonly known as the brittle fern, is a delicate and elegant plant belonging to the family Adiantaceae.

It is characterized by its finely divided, feathery fronds that give it a lacy appearance. Native to the humid forests of Southeast Asia, it thrives in moist, shaded environments. The plant's leaves are typically light green and have a fragile texture, which is reflected in its common name.

Adiantum fragile is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its aesthetic appeal and air-purifying qualities.
