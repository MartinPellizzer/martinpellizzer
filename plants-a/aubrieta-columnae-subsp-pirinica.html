Aubrieta columnae subsp. pirinica

Aubrieta columnae subsp.

pirinica, commonly known as the Pirin Aubrieta, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of the Balkans. It features compact, mat-forming growth with dense clusters of small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring.

The plant has hairy, gray-green leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is often found in alpine or subalpine habitats.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in cold climates.
