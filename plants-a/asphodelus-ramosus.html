Asphodelus ramosus

Asphodelus ramosus, commonly known as the tufted asphodel, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa.

It is a member of the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its upright, grass-like foliage and spikes of yellow or orange flowers.
