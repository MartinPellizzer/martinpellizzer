Arisaema iyoanum var. nakaianum

Arisaema iyoanum var. nakaianum, commonly known as the Nakaianum arisaema, is a species of flowering plant in the Araceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Japan, particularly in the southern part of Honshu. The plant is characterized by its unique, hooded inflorescence, which consists of a spadix surrounded by a modified leaf called a spathe. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and can reach up to 30 centimeters in length.

This variant is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its striking foliage and unusual floral structure.
