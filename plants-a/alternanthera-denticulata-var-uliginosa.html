Alternanthera denticulata var. uliginosa

Alternanthera denticulata var. uliginosa, commonly known as swamp beauty, is a striking aquatic plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red and pink inflorescences that stand out against its slender, grass-like leaves. The plant thrives in shallow, slow-moving waters and is often found in wetlands and marshy areas. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread easily along the water's edge.

It is popular in aquariums for its ornamental value and ability to provide habitat for small aquatic life.
