Adenophora bulleyana var. alba

Adenophora bulleyana var. alba, commonly known as the white adenophora, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to eastern Asia.

It features showy, white to pale blue flowers that bloom in late summer, typically from July to September. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy and arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or streamside habitats.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation in suitable climates.
