Afroqueta

Afroqueta, commonly known as the African violet, is a popular ornamental plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the genus Saintpaulia and is characterized by its velvety foliage and vibrant, showy flowers. The plant typically has upright stems with heart-shaped leaves that are a deep green color. Its flowers come in a variety of colors, including purple, blue, white, and pink, and bloom throughout the year under proper care.

Afroqueta thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
