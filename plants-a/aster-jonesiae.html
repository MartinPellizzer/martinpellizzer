Aster jonesiae

Aster jonesiae, commonly known as the Jonesia aster, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central America.

It is characterized by its showy, purple-blue composite flowers that bloom in the summer months. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss in its arid environment.

Aster jonesiae is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
