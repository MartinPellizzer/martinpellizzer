Asterostigma zebrina

Asterostigma zebrina, commonly known as the zebra aster, is a unique member of the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated foliage with alternating green and white stripes, which give it its common name. The plant produces small, daisy-like flowers in shades of yellow and white, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. Native to South Africa, it thrives in well-drained soil and full sun conditions.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.
