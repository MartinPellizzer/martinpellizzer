Anacamptis × larzacensis

Anacamptis × larzacensis, commonly known as the larzac orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to southern Europe.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its tall, erect stems and showy, violet-blue flowers. The plant typically grows in grasslands and open woodlands, preferring well-drained soils. Its flowers are arranged in a raceme and bloom in late spring to early summer.

Anacamptis × larzacensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
