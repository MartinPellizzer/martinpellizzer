Arabis Japonica Var. Macrocarpa

Arabis japonica var. macrocarpa is a striking flowering plant known for its vibrant yellow blooms and compact growth habit. Native to Japan, this variety thrives in cool climates and well-drained soil conditions. Its large, showy flowers make it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders. The plant is also valued for its low maintenance requirements and ability to attract pollinators. With its elegant appearance and easy care, it's a favored addition to many garden landscapes.
