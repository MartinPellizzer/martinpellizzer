Arundo purpurascens

Arundo purpurascens, commonly known as purple reed, is a tall, perennial grass native to southern Africa.

It features tall, slender stalks that can grow up to 3 meters in height, with narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, clustered spikes of pale green to reddish flowers during the summer months. It thrives in wet or marshy environments and is often found near water bodies.

Its striking purple hue makes it a popular ornamental plant in gardens and landscapes.
