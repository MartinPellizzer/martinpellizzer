Alsophila xantholepia

Alsophila xantholepia, commonly known as the yellow-leaf tree fern, is a species native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and a crown of feathery fronds that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant has a robust root system that helps it thrive in moist, shaded environments. Its fronds are typically bright green, with some varieties displaying a yellowish tinge on the underside.

Alsophila xantholepia is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its elegant and exotic look.
