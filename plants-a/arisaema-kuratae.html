Arisaema kuratae

Arisaema kuratae, commonly known as the Kurata's arrowhead, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its unique inflorescence consisting of a spadix surrounded by a spathe. The plant produces a single, showy flower with a spathe that is typically green or yellow in color. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense colonies in moist, shaded environments.

Arisaema kuratae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens with appropriate humidity and light conditions.
