Achyranthes mollis

Achyranthes mollis, commonly known as smallflower smartweed, is a herbaceous annual plant native to the eastern United States.

It typically grows in moist soils along stream banks, wetlands, and disturbed areas. The plant has slender, upright stems and small, clustered flowers that range in color from pink to red. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Achyranthes mollis is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil and provide habitat for wildlife.
