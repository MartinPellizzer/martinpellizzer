Alyxia magnifolia

Alyxia magnifolia, commonly known as the showy alyxia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that are typically pink or red in color. The plant produces small, elliptical leaves that are often arranged in pairs along the stems. Alyxia magnifolia thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in coastal areas or near freshwater sources.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscaping in suitable climates.
