Agapanthus tuberosus

Agapanthus tuberosus, commonly known as the African lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features striking, umbrella-shaped inflorescences composed of numerous blue or white flowers. The plant has strap-like, dark green leaves that are aromatic and often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to bloom reliably in summer.
