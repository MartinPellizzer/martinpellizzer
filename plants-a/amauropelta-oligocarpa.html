Amauropelta oligocarpa

Amauropelta oligocarpa, commonly known as the narrow-leafed cycad, is a member of the Cycadaceae family and is native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is a small, evergreen tree with a short trunk and a crown of pinnately compound leaves. The leaves are typically narrow and dark green, with a leathery texture. The plant produces cones, with the female cones being more prominent and containing the seeds.

It is adapted to tropical and subtropical climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
