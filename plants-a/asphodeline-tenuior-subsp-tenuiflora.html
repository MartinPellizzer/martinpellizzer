Asphodeline tenuior subsp. tenuiflora

Asphodeline tenuior subsp.

tenuiflora, commonly known as the narrow-leaved asphodel, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa. It features narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically bluish-green in color.

The plant produces upright spikes of small, white or pale yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Its botanical characteristics make it an attractive and low-maintenance addition to Mediterranean-style gardens.
