Anthurium bredemeyeri var. lanceolatum

Anthurium bredemeyeri var. lanceolatum, commonly known as the lanceolate anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped spathes that can range in color from deep red to orange-red, often with a contrasting green or yellow midrib. The plant produces small, inconspicuous spadices that are typically yellow or white, housed within the spathe. It thrives in high humidity and indirect light, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its foliage is broad and lance-shaped, contributing to its ornamental appeal.
