Aster yomena var. dentata

Aster yomena var. dentata, commonly known as the toothed aster, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Japan.

It features showy, daisy-like flowers with purple to blue petals and a yellow center, blooming in late summer to early autumn. The leaves are lance-shaped and have distinct toothed margins, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

This variety is valued in gardens for its vibrant blooms and ability to attract pollinators.
