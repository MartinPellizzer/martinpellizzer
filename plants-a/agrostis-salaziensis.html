Agrostis salaziensis

Agrostis salaziensis, commonly known as Salazian bentgrass, is a species of grass native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fine, tufted growth habit and slender, dark green leaves. The plant typically thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or grassland habitats. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny flowers.

Agrostis salaziensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in horticulture.
