Aletris yaanica

Aletris yaanica, commonly known as the mountain lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of East Asia.

It typically grows in shaded, moist woodland habitats, preferring well-drained soils. The plant features a rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves and produces delicate, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white or pale pink. Aletris yaanica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its elegant appearance.

It plays a role in local ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various insects and small animals.
