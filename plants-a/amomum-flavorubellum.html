Amomum flavorubellum

Amomum flavorubellum, commonly known as the red-flowered cardamom, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the ginger family (Zingiberaceae) and is characterized by its aromatic, rhizomatous growth habit. The plant produces striking red flowers that bloom in clusters, which are followed by elongated, three-lobed fruits. Its leaves are broad, lance-shaped, and have a smooth, waxy texture.
