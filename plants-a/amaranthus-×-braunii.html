Amaranthus × braunii

Amaranthus × braunii, commonly known as Chinese amaranth, is a species of flowering plant in the amaranth family.

It is native to China and is characterized by its tall, erect growth habit and large, showy inflorescences. The plant produces bright red, orange, or yellow flowers that are often used in ornamental gardening. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, with a slightly hairy texture.

Amaranthus × braunii is also valued for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions and its resilience to drought.
