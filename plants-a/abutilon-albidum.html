Abutilon albidum

Abutilon albidum, commonly known as the white abutilon, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 3 meters in height. The plant has soft, hairy leaves that are typically heart-shaped or ovate. Its flowers are pale yellow to white, with five petals and stamens that are often longer than the petals.

It produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind.
