Anthurium stenoglossum

Anthurium stenoglossum, commonly known as the narrow-lipped anthurium, is a tropical flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped leaves and its distinctive, spathaceous inflorescence that typically bears bright red or orange spathes. The plant thrives in humid, warm environments with bright, indirect light. Its foliage is often variegated, featuring a mix of green, white, and red hues.

Anthurium stenoglossum is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
