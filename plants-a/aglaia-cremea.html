Aglaia cremea

Aglaia cremea, commonly known as the cream flame tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a member of the Meliaceae family, which includes mahogany and other valuable hardwoods. The plant is characterized by its striking, flame-like yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a rough texture.
