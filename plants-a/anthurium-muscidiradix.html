Anthurium muscidiradix

Anthurium muscidiradix, commonly known as the muscidiradix anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive spadix and spatula-shaped leaf. The plant features large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a velvety texture. Its inflorescence consists of a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe, which can range from white to pink or red.

Anthurium muscidiradix is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in warm, humid environments.
