Acnistus pringlei

Acnistus pringlei, commonly known as the Pringle's acnistus, is a shrub native to the southern United States.

It features small, opposite leaves that are typically ovate to elliptic in shape. The plant produces clusters of white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. Its fruit is a small, dry capsule that contains several seeds.

Acnistus pringlei is often found in sandy or rocky soils in open woodlands and along streambanks.
