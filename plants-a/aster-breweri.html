Aster breweri

Aster breweri, commonly known as the Whorled Aster, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its showy purple to blue flowers that bloom in late summer and early fall. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and has a compact, bushy growth habit. Its leaves are narrow and arranged in whorls around the stem, giving it its common name.

Aster breweri is a member of the sunflower family (Asteraceae) and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance.
