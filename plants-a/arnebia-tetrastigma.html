Arnebia tetrastigma

Arnebia tetrastigma, commonly known as the Himalayan everlasting, is a flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky and arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh climates. The plant produces small, pink or purple flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are alternate and oval-shaped, often covered in a fine layer of hairs.

Arnebia tetrastigma is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
