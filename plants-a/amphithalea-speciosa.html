Amphithalea speciosa

Amphithalea speciosa, commonly known as the showy amphithalea, is a striking member of the Rubiaceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in spring. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its inflorescence consists of clusters of small, tubular flowers arranged in cymes.
