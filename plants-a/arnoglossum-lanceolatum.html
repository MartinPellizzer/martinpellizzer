Arnoglossum lanceolatum

Arnoglossum lanceolatum, commonly known as the lanceleaf herb.

It is a perennial herb native to North America, typically found in moist, shaded woodlands. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy and have a strong, somewhat unpleasant odor. Its flowers are yellow and occur in clusters, attracting various pollinators.

The plant is known for its unique morphology and ecological importance in its native habitat.
