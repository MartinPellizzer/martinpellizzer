Acianthus reniformis var. oblonga

Acianthus reniformis var. oblonga, commonly known as the broad-leafed acianthus, is a striking bromeliad native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its broad, elliptical leaves that form a rosette, often with a waxy, blue-green hue. The plant produces striking inflorescences with bright red bracts that enclose clusters of small, white flowers. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in indoor settings with proper care.
