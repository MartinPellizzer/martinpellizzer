Anthospermum aethiopicum var. reflexifolium

Anthospermum aethiopicum var. reflexifolium, commonly known as the reflexed-leaved anthospermum, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa and the Arabian Peninsula.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, clustered white or pale yellow flowers. The leaves are typically narrow and reflexed, meaning they curve backward at the tips, giving the plant a distinctive appearance. It thrives in arid or semi-arid environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.

The plant is sometimes used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
