Acanthophyllum fontanesii subsp. schugnanicum

Acanthophyllum fontanesii subsp.

schugnanicum, commonly known as the Schugnan sandthorn, is a shrub native to the arid regions of Central Asia. It is characterized by its dense, spiny foliage and small, often inconspicuous flowers.

The plant thrives in sandy and rocky soils, demonstrating high adaptability to harsh environmental conditions. Its structure helps it retain moisture and protect against wind erosion.

It plays a role in stabilizing desert ecosystems and is of interest for its ecological and potential medicinal values.
