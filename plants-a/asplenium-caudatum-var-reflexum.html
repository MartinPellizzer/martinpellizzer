Asplenium caudatum var. reflexum

Asplenium caudatum var. reflexum, commonly known as the hooked spleenwort, is a fern that belongs to the family Aspleniaceae.

It is characterized by its dark green, lance-shaped leaves that are often curled or twisted at the tips. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as rock crevices or woodland areas. Its spores are produced on the underside of the fronds, which are arranged in a compact rosette.

This variety is often found in temperate regions and is valued for its ornamental appearance and ecological role in forest ecosystems.
