Astracantha rascheyaensis

Astracantha rascheyaensis, commonly known as the Raschey's thornveld, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features spiny stems and leaves, with yellow-orange flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often found in grassland ecosystems.

Due to its unique morphology and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
