Asteranthopsis asterias

Asteranthopsis asterias, commonly known as the starflower, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers with white petals and a yellow central disk. Native to the mountainous regions of Central and South America, it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun. The plant produces small, oval-shaped fruits called cypsela, which are dispersed by wind.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
