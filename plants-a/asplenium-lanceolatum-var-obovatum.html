Asplenium lanceolatum var. obovatum

Asplenium lanceolatum var. obovatum, commonly known as the broad-leaf maidenhair fern, is a variant of the maidenhair fern family.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped, obovate leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The fronds are usually compound, with a delicate, feathery appearance. This plant prefers shady, moist environments and is often found in woodland areas.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to care for in a humid indoor environment.
