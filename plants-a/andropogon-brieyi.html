Andropogon brieyi

Andropogon brieyi, commonly known as the slender bluestem, is a grass species native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its fine, blue-green foliage and narrow, cylindrical seed heads. The plant typically grows in moist to wet soils, often found in wetlands and along stream banks. It plays a vital role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various wildlife species.

Andropogon brieyi is an important component of wetland ecosystems, contributing to biodiversity and ecological balance.
