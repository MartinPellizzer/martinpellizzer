Anthurium triangulopetiolum

Anthurium triangulopetiolum, commonly known as the triangular anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are often a deep green with a glossy texture. The plant produces small, spadix-like inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, typically in shades of red or orange. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil.

Anthurium triangulopetiolum is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and relatively low maintenance requirements.
