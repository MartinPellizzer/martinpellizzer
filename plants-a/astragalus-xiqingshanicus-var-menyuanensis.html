Astragalus xiqingshanicus var. menyuanensis

Astragalus xiqingshanicus var. menyuanensis, commonly known as the Menyuan milkvetch, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky or sandy soils.

It plays a role in soil stabilization and is of interest for its potential medicinal properties.
