Aristida purpurascens var. glaucissima

Aristida purpurascens var. glaucissima, commonly known as the glaucous dropseed, is a perennial grass native to the southern United States.

It features narrow, silver-green blades that give it a distinctive appearance, often with a bluish-gray hue. The plant produces small, fluffy seed heads that are visually striking and attract various pollinators. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for native plant gardens.

Its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements make it a valuable addition to ecological restoration projects.
