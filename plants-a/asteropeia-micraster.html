Asteropeia micraster

Asteropeia micraster, commonly known as the small aster.

It is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically features small, daisy-like flowers with a yellow central disc surrounded by white or pale pink petals. It is native to regions of southern Africa and thrives in well-drained, sandy soils.
