Aretia cylindrica

Aretia cylindrica, commonly known as the cylindrical aretia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its slender, cylindrical stems and clusters of small, tubular flowers. Aretia cylindrica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
