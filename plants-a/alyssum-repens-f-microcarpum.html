Alyssum repens f. microcarpum

Alyssum repens f. microcarpum, commonly known as dwarf alyssum, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pale pink, typically blooming in spring. The plant has compact foliage with silvery-gray leaves, and its stems are often covered in fine hairs. It is well-suited for rock gardens and borders due to its ornamental value and hardiness.

The subspecies f. microcarpum is distinguished by its smaller, more numerous flowers compared to the typical form.
