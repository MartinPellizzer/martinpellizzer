Asteridea athrixioides f. athrixioides

Asteridea athrixioides f. athrixioides, commonly known as the common aster, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that typically bloom in shades of purple, pink, and white. The plant has alternate, lance-shaped leaves that are usually hairy, and it produces small, cylindrical flower heads. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
