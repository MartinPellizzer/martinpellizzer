Anthurium leptocaule

Anthurium leptocaule, commonly known as the slender-stemmed anthurium, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its elongated, slender stem and large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a glossy texture. The plant produces striking spadices, which are the characteristic inflorescences of the Araceae family, often in shades of red or white. Anthurium leptocaule thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor cultivation and botanical gardens.
