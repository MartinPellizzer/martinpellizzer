Aeranthes bathieana

Aeranthes bathieana, commonly known as the Bathieana orchid, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its elegant, pendulous inflorescences. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in coastal regions. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, with a distinctive lip that is often adorned with intricate patterns.

Aeranthes bathieana is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
