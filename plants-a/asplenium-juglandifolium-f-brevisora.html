Asplenium juglandifolium f. brevisora

Asplenium juglandifolium f. brevisora, commonly known as the short-leaf water fern, is a fern species characterized by its compact, densely packed fronds.

It belongs to the family Aspleniaceae and is native to regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of China and India. The fronds are typically short and broad, with a dark green, leathery texture and a distinctive, serrated margin. This fern thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in rocky or forested areas.

Its unique morphology makes it a valuable species for both horticultural and ecological studies.
