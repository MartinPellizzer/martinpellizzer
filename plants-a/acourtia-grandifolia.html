Acourtia grandifolia

Acourtia grandifolia, commonly known as the showy acourtia, is a flowering plant native to California and Baja California.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant produces showy, violet to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Acourtia grandifolia typically grows in rocky soils and is often found in coastal scrub habitats.

It is a perennial herb that thrives in mild, Mediterranean climates.
