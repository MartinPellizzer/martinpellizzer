Armeria Arenaria Subsp. Anomala

Armeria arenaria subsp. anomala is a low-growing plant native to coastal regions of western europe. it thrives in sandy and gravelly soils, often found in maritime climates. its small, white flowers bloom in clusters during the summer months. the subspecies is known for its hardy nature and ability to withstand salt spray and strong winds. it is a popular choice for coastal gardens and erosion control projects.
