Alliaria miqueliana

Alliaria miqueliana, commonly known as the Chinese garlic mustard, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes other mustard plants. The plant features alternate, heart-shaped leaves and produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in racemes. Its roots contain sulfur compounds that give it a distinctive garlic-like odor.

It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and streambanks.
