Ajax festalis

Ajax festalis, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that bloom in shades of yellow and orange. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and has a compact, bushy habit with narrow, grass-like leaves. It is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and can thrive in well-drained soil with full sun exposure.

Ajax festalis is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
