Anthurium macdougallii

Anthurium macdougallii, commonly known as the Macdougall's anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the Caribbean.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-shaped inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, often in red or orange hues. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Anthurium macdougallii is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and unique floral structure.
