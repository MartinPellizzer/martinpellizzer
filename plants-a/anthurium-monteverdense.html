Anthurium monteverdense

Anthurium monteverdense, commonly known as the Monteverde anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the cloud forests of Costa Rica.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped leaves and striking spadix inflorescence. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its botanical features make it a popular choice for indoor gardening and botanical collections.

Anthurium monteverdense is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological significance in its native habitat.
