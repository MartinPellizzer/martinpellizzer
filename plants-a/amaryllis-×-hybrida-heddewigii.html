Amaryllis × hybrida heddewigii

Amaryllis × hybrida heddewigii, commonly known as the Heddewigii amaryllis, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, orange, and yellow. The plant produces strap-like leaves that are typically dark green and broad. It is native to South America and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

This species is known for its ability to thrive in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade conditions.
