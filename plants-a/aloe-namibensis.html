Aloe namibensis

Aloe namibensis, commonly known as the Namib Desert Aloe, is a succulent plant native to the Namib Desert in Namibia.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that store water, enabling it to survive in arid conditions. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with bright yellow flowers, which attract pollinators such as bees and moths. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their healing properties.

Aloe namibensis is adapted to extreme environmental conditions, making it a unique example of desert flora.
