Alternanthera philoxeroides f. angustifolia

Alternanthera philoxeroides f. angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf red root grass, is a variant of the species native to South America.

It is a perennial herb that thrives in moist, disturbed soils and is often found in wetlands or along riverbanks.
