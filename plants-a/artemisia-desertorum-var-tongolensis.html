Artemisia desertorum var. tongolensis

Artemisia desertorum var. tongolensis, commonly known as the Tongolensis sagebrush, is a perennial shrub native to the arid regions of the western United States.

It is characterized by its aromatic, silver-gray foliage and small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant thrives in dry, sandy soils and is well adapted to harsh desert conditions. It plays an important role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for desert wildlife.

Its essential oils have been studied for potential medicinal properties.
