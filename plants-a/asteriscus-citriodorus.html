Asteriscus citriodorus

Asteriscus citriodorus, commonly known as lemon verbena, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its fragrant, lemon-scented leaves and small yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is often cultivated for its aromatic properties and ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for gardens and containers.

Its essential oils are used in aromatherapy and as a natural insect repellent.
