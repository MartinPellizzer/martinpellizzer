Alsine rostrata proles densiflora

Alsine rostrata proles densiflora, commonly known as the dense-flowered alsine, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Amaranthaceae.

It is characterized by its prostrate growth habit and small, clustered inflorescences. The plant produces tiny, greenish-white flowers that are often densely packed, giving it its common name. It typically thrives in moist, sandy soils and is often found in coastal or wetland habitats.

The species is known for its rapid growth and ability to colonize disturbed areas.
