Amaranthus dubius var. flexuosus

Amaranthus dubius var. flexuosus, commonly known as the flexible amaranth, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from green to red, depending on the variety. It is a member of the Amaranthaceae family, known for its nutritional value and ornamental use.

This species is often cultivated for its edible seeds and as a decorative plant in gardens.
