Ayenia boliviana

Ayenia boliviana, commonly known as the Bolivian ayenia, is a species of flowering plant native to the Andean regions of South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in high-altitude environments, often in rocky or arid soils, and is adapted to the harsh conditions of the Andes. Its foliage is usually narrow and grass-like, while its flowers are small and clustered in a disc-like structure.

Ayenia boliviana is valued for its ornamental potential and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
