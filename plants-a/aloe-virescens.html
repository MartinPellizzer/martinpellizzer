Aloe virescens

Aloe virescens, commonly known as the green aloe or the narrow-leaf aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features tall, erect stems with narrow, fleshy leaves that are typically a vibrant green color. The plant produces yellow flowers in clusters during the summer months, which are followed by small, red berries. Aloe virescens is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine and for their soothing properties.
