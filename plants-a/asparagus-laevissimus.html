Asparagus laevissimus

Asparagus laevissimus, commonly known as the smooth asparagus, is a perennial herb native to the western United States.

It typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. The plant has slender, erect stems and small, fern-like leaves that are smooth to the touch. Its flowers are usually pale green or yellow, and it produces small, edible berries.

Asparagus laevissimus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
