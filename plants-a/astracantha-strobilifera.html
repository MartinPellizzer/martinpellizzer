Astracantha strobilifera

Astracantha strobilifera, commonly known as the African milkweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of small, pale yellow flowers. The plant has hairy stems and leaves that are often covered in fine, white pubescence. Its fruit is a dry, woody capsule that contains several seeds.

It is adapted to arid environments and is often found in grasslands and rocky areas.
