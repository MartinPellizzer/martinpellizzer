Alternanthera helleri f. obtusior

Alternanthera helleri f. obtusior, commonly known as the broadleaf Alternanthera, is a striking aquatic plant characterized by its broad, ovate leaves that are often bright red or pink in color.

It is a cultivar of the species Alternanthera helleri, which is native to South America. The plant thrives in shallow, slow-moving freshwater environments and is popular in aquariums for its vibrant foliage. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow, and it spreads through both vegetative growth and seed dispersal.

Its ornamental value makes it a favorite among aquatic plant enthusiasts.
