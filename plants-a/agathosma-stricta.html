Agathosma stricta

Agathosma stricta, commonly known as Cape garlic.

It is a small shrub native to South Africa, characterized by its aromatic, narrow leaves and small white to pale yellow flowers. The plant is part of the Rutaceae family and is known for its pungent scent, similar to that of garlic. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Agathosma stricta is used in traditional medicine and is also cultivated for its ornamental value.
