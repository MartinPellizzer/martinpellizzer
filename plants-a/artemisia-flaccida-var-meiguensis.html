Artemisia flaccida var. meiguensis

Artemisia flaccida var. meiguensis, commonly known as Chinese wormwood, is a perennial herb native to China.

It is characterized by its aromatic, silver-gray foliage and small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in mountainous regions. It has been used traditionally in Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.

Its essential oils contain compounds like thujone and camphor, which contribute to its strong scent and medicinal properties.
