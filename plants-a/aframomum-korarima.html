Aframomum korarima

Aframomum korarima, commonly known as the African grain of paradise, is a flowering plant native to western Africa.

It is a member of the ginger family (Zingiberaceae) and is characterized by its tall, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are followed by red, fleshy fruits containing numerous seeds.
