Alliaria balansana

Alliaria balansana, commonly known as winter cress, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters. The plant is characterized by its pinnately divided leaves and is often found in moist, disturbed soils. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia but has been introduced to other regions.

Alliaria balansana is known for its peppery taste and has been used in traditional medicine and cuisine.
