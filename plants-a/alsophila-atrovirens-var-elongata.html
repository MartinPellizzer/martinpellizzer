Alsophila atrovirens var. elongata

Alsophila atrovirens var. elongata, commonly known as the long-stemmed tree fern, is a variant of the silver tree fern native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate fronds that can reach several meters in length. The plant exhibits a distinctive dark green to blackish-green coloration on its fronds, which contributes to its striking appearance. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Botanically, it is a member of the Polypodiaceae family and is valued for its ornamental qualities in horticulture.
