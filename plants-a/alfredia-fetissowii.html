Alfredia fetissowii

Alfredia fetissowii, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit. The plant features velvety, dark green leaves and produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of purple, pink, and white. Alfredia fetissowii is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
