Anthurium bradeanum

Anthurium bradeanum, commonly known as the Brade's anthurium, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped leaves that can range in color from deep green to reddish hues. The plant produces striking spadices, which are the modified leaves that enclose the true flowers, often in shades of red or orange. Anthurium bradeanum thrives in humid, warm environments with bright, indirect light.

Its unique foliage and ornamental value make it a popular choice for indoor gardening and botanical collections.
