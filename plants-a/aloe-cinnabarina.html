Aloe cinnabarina

Aloe cinnabarina, commonly known as the red aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features striking red and orange bracts that surround its yellow flowers, which bloom in late summer. The plant has fleshy, spine-tipped leaves that store water, typical of many succulents. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Aloe cinnabarina is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
