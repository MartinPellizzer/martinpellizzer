Artabotrys arachnoides

Artabotrys arachnoides, commonly known as the spider flower or spider orchid, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant, white, spider-like flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has a climbing habit and possesses a woody stem covered with a dense layer of fine, hair-like structures. Its leaves are elliptical, glossy, and arranged oppositely along the stem.

The botanical features of Artabotrys arachnoides make it a popular ornamental plant in horticulture.
