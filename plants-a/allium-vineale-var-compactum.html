Allium vineale var. compactum

Allium vineale var. compactum, commonly known as compact chive, is a compact, herbaceous perennial plant native to temperate regions of Europe and Asia.

It features slender, grass-like leaves that are aromatic and often used in culinary applications. The plant produces small, white or pale purple flowers in clusters, which appear in early summer. It prefers well-drained soil and full to partial sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or container planting.

Its dense growth habit makes it an effective ground cover and a valuable addition to ornamental landscapes.
