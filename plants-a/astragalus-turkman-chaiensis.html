Astragalus turkman-chaiensis

Astragalus turkman-chaiensis, commonly known as Turkmen Chaiensis milkvetch, is a perennial herb native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant is known for its deep root system, which helps it survive in arid and semi-arid environments.

It is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional medicine.
