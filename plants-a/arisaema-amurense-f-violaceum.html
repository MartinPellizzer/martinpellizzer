Arisaema amurense f. violaceum

Arisaema amurense f. violaceum, commonly known as the purple-flowered arrowhead, is a variant of the arrowhead plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive arrow-shaped leaves and striking violet to purple spadices. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. Its inflorescence consists of a spadix surrounded by a modified leaf called a spathe.

This ornamental plant is valued for its unique foliage and vibrant floral display.
