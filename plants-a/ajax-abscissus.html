Ajax abscissus

Ajax abscissus, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that range in color from white to yellow. The plant typically grows as a herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers bloom in clusters.

Ajax abscissus is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
