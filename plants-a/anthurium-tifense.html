Anthurium tifense

Anthurium tifense, commonly known as the Tifense arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to the Caribbean islands.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces striking spadices, which are the modified leaves that enclose the small, inconspicuous flowers. Anthurium tifense thrives in humid, warm environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and air-purifying qualities.
