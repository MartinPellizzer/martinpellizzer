Aloe acuminata var. major

Aloe acuminata var. major, commonly known as the Cape aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, upright stems that can grow up to 3 meters in height, adorned with narrow, fleshy, green leaves that have a spiky margin. The plant produces striking red or orange flowers in terminal inflorescences, typically during the summer months. Its leaves contain a gel-like substance that is used in traditional medicine and skincare products.

It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
