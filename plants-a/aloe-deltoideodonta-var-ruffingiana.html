Aloe deltoideodonta var. ruffingiana

Aloe deltoideodonta var. ruffingiana, commonly known as the Ruffin's Aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, with a waxy coating to reduce water loss. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing yellowish-orange flowers. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
