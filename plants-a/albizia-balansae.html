Albizia balansae

Albizia balansae, commonly known as the Balansae Albizia, is a species of legume native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its compound leaves, which are typically pinnate and have a soft, feathery appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually pale yellow or white, arranged in racemose inflorescences. Albizia balansae is often found in open woodlands and along riverbanks, where it thrives in well-drained soils.

Its seeds are enclosed in elongated pods, and the plant is known for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it ecologically significant in certain habitats.
