Azalea macrostemon

Azalea macrostemon, commonly known as the large-stamen azalea, is a species native to eastern Asia, particularly China.

It is a deciduous shrub that produces showy, funnel-shaped flowers in shades of pink, red, and white. The plant has opposite, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. Its flowers are characterized by their prominent stamens, which are often more visible than the petals.

Azalea macrostemon thrives in acidic, well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.
