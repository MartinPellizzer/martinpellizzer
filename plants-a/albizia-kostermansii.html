Albizia kostermansii

Albizia kostermansii, commonly known as the Koster's Albizia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

Albizia kostermansii is often found in open woodland and along riverbanks, where it thrives in well-drained soils.

Its ornamental value and potential ecological benefits make it a notable species in its native habitat.
