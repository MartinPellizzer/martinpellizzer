Aegiphila hoehnei var. spectabilis

Aegiphila hoehnei var. spectabilis, commonly known as the showy aegiphila, is a flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to violet. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine, hairy texture.

This species is often found in moist, shaded environments within tropical forests.
