Aloysia dusenii

Aloysia dusenii, commonly known as the Dusen's verbena, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It belongs to the Verbenaceae family and is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit. The plant produces showy clusters of blue or purple flowers during the summer months. Its leaves are opposite, narrow, and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Aloysia dusenii is often cultivated for its vibrant blooms and is used in both landscaping and traditional medicine.
