Alsophila plagiopteris

Alsophila plagiopteris, commonly known as the narrow-leaf tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and feathery fronds that grow in a dense, upright arrangement. The plant has a distinctive crown of bright green, lance-shaped leaves that give it a tropical appearance. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Alsophila plagiopteris is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
