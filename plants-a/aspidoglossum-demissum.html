Aspidoglossum demissum

Aspidoglossum demissum, commonly known as the silver sword, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its rosette of narrow, silver-green leaves that form a compact, sword-like shape. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink. It is a succulent, meaning it stores water in its leaves, making it drought-tolerant.

The botanical features of Aspidoglossum demissum make it a popular choice for rock gardens and arid environments.
