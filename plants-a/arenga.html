Arenga

Arenga, commonly known as sago palm, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Arecaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like leaves. The plant produces a starchy pith inside its stems, which is harvested for food and industrial use. Arenga palms can grow up to 20 meters tall and are often cultivated for their ornamental value.

The plant also plays a significant role in traditional medicine and local economies.
