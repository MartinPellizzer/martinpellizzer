Aster heynei

Aster heynei, commonly known as the yellow aster, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its showy yellow to orange flowers that bloom in the fall. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as wetlands and along stream banks. Aster heynei has lance-shaped leaves and produces small, tubular flowers that attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, which includes many other familiar flowering plants.
