Anthurium botijaense

Anthurium botijaense, commonly known as the Botijaense Arrowhead, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its unique inflorescence consisting of a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe. The plant features large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Anthurium botijaense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in botanical gardens.
