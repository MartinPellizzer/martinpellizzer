Arabis hirsuta f. platystigma

Arabis hirsuta f. platystigma, commonly known as the broad-stemmed hairy rockcress, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many flowering plants with small, cross-shaped flowers. The plant is characterized by its hairy stems and basal leaves, which are often covered in fine pubescence. It produces clusters of small, yellow flowers that bloom in early spring.

This variety is distinguished by its broader leaves and more robust growth compared to other forms of Arabis hirsuta.
