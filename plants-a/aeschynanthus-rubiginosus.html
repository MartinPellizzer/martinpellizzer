Aeschynanthus rubiginosus

Aeschynanthus rubiginosus, commonly known as the red-tipped gloxinia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant red bracts that enclose small, tubular flowers, which are typically white or pale pink. The plant produces aerial stems that can root at the nodes, allowing it to climb or trail. Its leaves are usually heart-shaped and have a glossy texture.

It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity to flourish.
