Aloe deltoideodonta var. contigua

Aloe deltoideodonta var. contigua, commonly known as the Cape aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern, with a bluish-green coloration. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing yellowish-green flowers. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its leaves and stems.

Its botanical structure makes it suitable for xerophytic gardens and rockeries.
