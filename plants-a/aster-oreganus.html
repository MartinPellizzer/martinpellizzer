Aster oreganus

Aster oreganus, commonly known as the mountain aster, is a flowering plant native to the western United States and parts of Canada.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky, arid environments, such as mountain slopes and open woods. It produces showy blue to purple flowers that bloom in the summer months.

Aster oreganus is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
