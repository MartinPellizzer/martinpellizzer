Asplenium striatum var. expansum

Asplenium striatum var. expansum, commonly known as the broad-leaf maidenhair fern, is a variegated fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features dark green, lance-shaped leaves with a distinctive white or silver midrib, giving it a striking appearance. The fronds are typically broad and upright, with a delicate, feathery texture. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, moist soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value in both indoor and outdoor gardens.
