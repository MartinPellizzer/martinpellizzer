Arisaema serratum var. latisectum

Arisaema serratum var. latisectum, commonly known as the broad-lobed arisaema, is a herbaceous plant native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, arrow-shaped leaves with broad, serrated lobes. The plant produces a spadix surrounded by a spathe, a feature common in many arum species. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
