Acuan glandulosum

Acuan glandulosum, commonly known as the sticky acuan, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glandular hairs that give its stems and leaves a sticky texture. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters. Its leaves are typically opposite and have a lanceolate shape.

Acuan glandulosum is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest undergrowth.
