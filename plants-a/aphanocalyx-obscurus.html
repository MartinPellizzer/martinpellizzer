Aphanocalyx obscurus

Aphanocalyx obscurus, commonly known as the dark aphanocalyx, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its small, inconspicuous flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or a small tree, with compound leaves and pod-like fruits.
