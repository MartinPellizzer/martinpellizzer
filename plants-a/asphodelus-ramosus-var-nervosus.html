Asphodelus ramosus var. nervosus

Asphodelus ramosus var. nervosus, commonly known as the nervous asphodel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

The variety is distinguished by its nervously veined leaves, which give it its common name.
