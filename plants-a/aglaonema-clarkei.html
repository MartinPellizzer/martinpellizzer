Aglaonema clarkei

Aglaonema clarkei, commonly known as the Chinese Evergreen, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves with striking white and silver variegation. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or white, followed by berries that are edible but not commonly consumed by humans.

Aglaonema clarkei is also valued for its air-purifying qualities and its ability to tolerate a range of environmental conditions.
