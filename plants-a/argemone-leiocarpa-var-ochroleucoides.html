Argemone leiocarpa var. ochroleucoides

Argemone leiocarpa var. ochroleucoides, commonly known as the yellow argemone, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its yellow to orange flowers and hairy, glandular leaves. The plant produces capsule fruits containing numerous small, black seeds. It thrives in dry, sunny environments and is often found in disturbed soils.

Its botanical features make it a notable species in ecological and horticultural contexts.
