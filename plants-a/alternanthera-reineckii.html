Alternanthera reineckii

Alternanthera reineckii, commonly known as Red Alternanthera, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its striking red, pink, or purple foliage. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and water features.
