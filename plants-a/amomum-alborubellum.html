Amomum alborubellum

Amomum alborubellum, commonly known as the white-red cardamom, is a member of the Zingiberaceae family, native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

Amomum alborubellum is valued for its medicinal properties and is often cultivated for its essential oils and flavoring compounds.
