Aristolochia amesiana

Aristolochia amesiana, commonly known as the American Dutchman's pipe, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its unique, pipe-shaped flowers that are typically greenish-yellow with a strong, musty odor. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and is often found in moist, wooded areas. It belongs to the family Aristolochiaceae and is known for its ornamental value.

However, it contains toxic compounds and should be handled with care.
