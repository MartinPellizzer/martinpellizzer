Aster sorrentinoi

Aster sorrentinoi, commonly known as the Sorrentino's aster, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry soils and prefers full sun exposure. Its flowers are usually blue to purple in color, with ray florets surrounding a central disk.

Aster sorrentinoi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its visual interest.
