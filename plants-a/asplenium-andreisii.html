Asplenium andreisii

Asplenium andreisii, commonly known as the rock fern, is a species of fern native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact, dense growth habit and is often found growing on rock surfaces or in crevices. The plant has narrow, dark green, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery pubescence. It is a perennial plant that thrives in well-drained, calcareous soils.

Asplenium andreisii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in rock gardens and as a houseplant.
