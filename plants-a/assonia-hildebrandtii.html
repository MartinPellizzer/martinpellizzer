Assonia hildebrandtii

Assonia hildebrandtii, commonly known as the Cape marigold, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and has a compact, bushy appearance. Its leaves are linear and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers bloom in clusters.

Assonia hildebrandtii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in coastal conditions.
