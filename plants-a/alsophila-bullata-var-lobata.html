Alsophila bullata var. lobata

Alsophila bullata var. lobata, commonly known as the lobate tree fern, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and a crown of feathery, pinnate fronds. The plant belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is typically found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its rhizome is robust, allowing it to anchor firmly in moist, shaded environments.

The fronds are evergreen and can grow quite large, contributing to its ornamental value in gardens and natural habitats.
