Anemone × seemenii

Anemone × seemenii, commonly known as the Japanese anemone, is a hybrid perennial plant native to East Asia.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple, typically blooming in late summer to early autumn. The plant has finely divided, feather-like leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it a popular choice for garden borders and woodland gardens.

Its botanical characteristics make it both ornamental and ecologically valuable in diverse landscapes.
