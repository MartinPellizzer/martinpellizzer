Asplenium balliviani

Asplenium balliviani, commonly known as the Balliviani's spleenwort, is a small, terrestrial fern belonging to the Aspleniaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped leaves that are typically less than 10 centimeters in length. This plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found growing on rocks or soil in temperate regions. Its fronds are usually dark green and have a serrated margin, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Asplenium balliviani is noted for its ability to tolerate low light conditions, making it a suitable choice for indoor gardening.
