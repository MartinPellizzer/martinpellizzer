Agave patonii

Agave patonii, commonly known as the Paton's agave, is a striking succulent native to Mexico.

It features a rosette of thick, blue-gray leaves that are adorned with white tubercles and spines. The plant produces a tall, branched inflorescence with showy yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice for rock gardens and arid landscapes.
