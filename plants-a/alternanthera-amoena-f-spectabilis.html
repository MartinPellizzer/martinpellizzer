Alternanthera amoena f. spectabilis

Alternanthera amoena f. spectabilis, commonly known as the red-leaf alternanthera, is a strikingly ornamental plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, bi-colored foliage, featuring deep red veins on a green or bronze background. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade. It is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal in water gardens or as a container plant.

Its rapid growth and ability to spread make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking visual interest.
