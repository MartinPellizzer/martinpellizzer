Apocynum venetum var. anomalum

Apocynum venetum var. anomalum, commonly known as the Chinese apocynum or strange apocynum, is a variant of the apocynum genus native to China.

It is a shrub characterized by its woody stems, opposite leaves, and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers. The plant produces a milky latex sap, which is a common feature among members of the Apocynaceae family. Its flowers are typically pink or white, and it blooms in the summer months.

The plant is also known for its medicinal properties, traditionally used in Chinese herbal medicine.
