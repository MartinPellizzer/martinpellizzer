Anthurium riofrioi

Anthurium riofrioi, commonly known as the Rio Frioi Anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its unique inflorescence known as a spadix, surrounded by a colorful spathe. The plant features large, heart-shaped leaves with a glossy surface, often displaying vibrant green hues. It thrives in humid, warm environments with bright, indirect light.

Anthurium riofrioi is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant.
