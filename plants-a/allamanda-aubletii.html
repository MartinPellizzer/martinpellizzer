Allamanda aubletii

Allamanda aubletii, commonly known as the golden trumpet vine, is a tropical shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically bright yellow or orange in color. The plant features glossy, dark green leaves that are opposite and ovate in shape. It produces fleshy, berry-like fruits that are edible but often have a bland taste.

Allamanda aubletii thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
