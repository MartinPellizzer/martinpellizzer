Ardisia doma

Ardisia doma, commonly known as the Christmas berry, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, dark green leaves and produces small, red berries that are edible when ripe. The plant belongs to the family Ericaceae and is characterized by its compact growth habit and attractive foliage. It thrives in shady, moist environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
