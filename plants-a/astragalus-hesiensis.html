Astragalus hesiensis

Astragalus hesiensis, commonly known as the Hubei milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its erect stems and small, pea-like flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid conditions. Its root system is extensive, allowing it to thrive in nutrient-poor soils.

Astragalus hesiensis is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional Chinese medicine.
