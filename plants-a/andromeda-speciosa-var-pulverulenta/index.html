Andromeda Speciosa Var. Pulverulenta

Pulverulent andromeda, scientifically known as Andromeda speciosa var. pulverulenta, is a striking evergreen shrub native to the coastal regions of southern California. Its unique foliage features a powdery, chalky white coating that gives it its distinctive name. This plant thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth. With its delicate pink and white flowers, it adds a subtle elegance to gardens and natural landscapes. Despite its beauty, it is relatively rare and often found in specific microhabitats along the coastline.
