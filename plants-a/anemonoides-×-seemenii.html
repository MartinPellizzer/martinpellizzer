Anemonoides × seemenii

Anemonoides × seemenii, commonly known as the Chinese anemone, is a hybrid perennial plant native to East Asia.

It features showy, single or double flowers in shades of white, pink, and red, typically blooming in spring. The plant has finely divided, fern-like leaves that are often hairy beneath, and it thrives in moist, well-drained soil. It is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to naturalize in suitable conditions.

Anemonoides × seemenii is relatively hardy and can tolerate light shade to full sun.
