Agrostemma × baldense

Agrostemma × baldense, commonly known as the hybrid meadow cranesbill, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the family Caryophyllaceae and is characterized by its upright stems and clusters of pink to purple flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of 30–60 cm and produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in meadows and along roadsides.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
