Anotis thymifolia var. thesioides

Anotis thymifolia var. thesioides, commonly known as the Tasmanian thyme, is a flowering plant native to Tasmania.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with aromatic leaves that resemble those of thyme. The plant produces small, white to pale blue flowers in spikes during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and heathland environments.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for ecological studies and horticultural use.
