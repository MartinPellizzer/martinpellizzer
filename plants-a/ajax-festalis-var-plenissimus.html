Ajax festalis var. plenissimus

Ajax festalis var. plenissimus, commonly known as the Double Ajax, is a cultivar of the Ajax plant, which is native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, double-layered flowers that are typically pink or white in color. The plant has a compact growth habit with broad, waxy leaves that are often variegated. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and as a cut flower due to its long-lasting blooms.
