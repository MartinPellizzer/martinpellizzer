Alsophila proceroides

Alsophila proceroides, commonly known as the slender tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds that can reach several meters in length. The plant has a robust root system that helps it thrive in moist, shaded environments. Its fronds are typically bright green and have a smooth, glossy texture.

Alsophila proceroides is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to add a tropical aesthetic to gardens.
