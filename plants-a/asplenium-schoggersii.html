Asplenium schoggersii

Asplenium schoggersii, commonly known as the Schoggers' spleenwort, is a small, terrestrial fern belonging to the family Aspleniaceae.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dark green, leathery fronds that are often covered in a fine layer of trichomes. The plant typically produces spore-bearing sporangia on the undersides of its leaves, which are clustered in sori surrounded by a protective indusium. Native to tropical and subtropical regions, it thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rock crevices.

Its unique morphology and ecological adaptations make it an interesting subject for botanical study.
