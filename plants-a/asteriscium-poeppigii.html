Asteriscium poeppigii

Asteriscium poeppigii, commonly known as the Pueblan aster, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or purple in color, arranged in a star-like pattern. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Asteriscium poeppigii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
