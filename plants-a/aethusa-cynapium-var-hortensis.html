Aethusa cynapium var. hortensis

Aethusa cynapium var. hortensis, commonly known as garden hemlock, is a biennial or short-lived perennial plant native to Europe.

It belongs to the Apiaceae family, characterized by its umbellate inflorescence and aromatic foliage. The plant features finely divided, fern-like leaves and produces small, greenish-white flowers in umbrella-shaped clusters. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and historical use in herbal medicine.
