Anarthria canaliculata

Anarthria canaliculata, commonly known as the narrow-veined orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and delicate, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in clusters. The plant prefers moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests. Its flowers are usually white or pale yellow with intricate patterns.

Anarthria canaliculata is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
