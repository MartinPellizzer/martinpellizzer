Agelaea nitida

Agelaea nitida, commonly known as the velvetleaf or silverleaf, is a tropical plant belonging to the Malvaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, silky hairs, giving them a velvety texture. It is sometimes cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage.
