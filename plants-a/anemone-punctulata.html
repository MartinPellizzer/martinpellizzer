Anemone punctulata

Anemone punctulata, commonly known as the dotted anemone, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It features showy, white to pale pink flowers with a distinctive dotted pattern on the petals. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are palmately lobed and often covered in fine hairs.

Anemone punctulata is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits to pollinators.
