Ancistrum argenteum

Ancistrum argenteum, commonly known as the silver anthurium, is a striking tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its unique inflorescence consisting of a spadix and a spathe. The plant features broad, waxy leaves that are typically a deep green with a silvery underside, giving it its common name. It thrives in humid, warm environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical landscapes.
