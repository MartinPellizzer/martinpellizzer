Abrodictyum strictum

Abrodictyum strictum, commonly known as the rigid reed.

It is a herbaceous plant native to southern Africa, characterized by its tall, upright stems and narrow leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color. It thrives in wetland environments and is often found near water bodies.

Its botanical features make it an important species in ecological studies of aquatic vegetation.
