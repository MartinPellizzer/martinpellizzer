Arenaria rossii var. elegans

Arenaria rossii var. elegans, commonly known as the elegant sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features dense clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has succulent, fleshy leaves that are adapted to withstand arid and rocky environments. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and alpine settings.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and delicate floral display.
