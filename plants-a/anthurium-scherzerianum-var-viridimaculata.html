Anthurium scherzerianum var. viridimaculata

Anthurium scherzerianum var. viridimaculata, commonly known as the green-spotted anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are often speckled with green or yellow spots. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-shaped inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, typically in shades of red or orange. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

This variety is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
