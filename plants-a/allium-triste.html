Allium triste

Allium triste, commonly known as the sorrowful onion, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and small, white to pale purple flowers. The plant produces bulbous structures that are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits. Allium triste thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for both horticultural study and medicinal research.
