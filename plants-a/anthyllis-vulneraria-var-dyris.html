Anthyllis vulneraria var. dyris

Anthyllis vulneraria var. dyris, commonly known as the Dyrian hedge-pink, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial shrub characterized by its slender, branched stems and small, pink or violet flowers. The plant produces pods that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal habitats.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscaping in suitable climates.
