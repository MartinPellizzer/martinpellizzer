Aerides turma f. anniversarius

Aerides turma f. anniversarius, commonly known as the Anniversarius Vanda, is a hybrid orchid belonging to the Vanda genus.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink, white, and purple. The plant is epiphytic, meaning it grows on other plants without being parasitic, and thrives in warm, humid environments. Its leaves are broad and fleshy, adapted to store water in its native habitat.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its striking floral display.
