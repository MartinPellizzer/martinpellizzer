Achimenes rusbyi

Achimenes rusbyi, commonly known as the Rusby's gloxinia, is a tropical shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, pink, and purple. The plant produces fleshy, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, white pubescence. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Achimenes rusbyi is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its vibrant blooms and unique foliage.
