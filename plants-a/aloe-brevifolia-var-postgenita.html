Aloe brevifolia var. postgenita

Aloe brevifolia var. postgenita, commonly known as the narrow-leaf aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact rosettes of fleshy, narrow, gray-green leaves that are covered in fine white hairs. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow flowers during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Its unique morphology and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for xeriscaping and rock gardens.
