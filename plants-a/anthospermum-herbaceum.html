Anthospermum herbaceum

Anthospermum herbaceum, commonly known as the small-flowered anthospermum, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. The plant features small, clustered white or pale yellow flowers that bloom during the summer months. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

The species is notable for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and its role in supporting local insect populations.
