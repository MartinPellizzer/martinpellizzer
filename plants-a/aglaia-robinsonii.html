Aglaia robinsonii

Aglaia robinsonii, commonly known as the golden apple or yellow mangosteen, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Meliaceae and is characterized by its smooth, dark brown bark and glossy, elliptical leaves. The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in local medicine.
