Arum italicum var. cylindraceum

Arum italicum var. cylindraceum, commonly known as the Italian arum or the white arum, is a striking tropical plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features a large, heart-shaped leaf with a distinctive, spadix-like inflorescence that is often white or pale green. The plant is noted for its ornamental value, particularly in shaded garden settings. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and requires partial to full shade.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.
