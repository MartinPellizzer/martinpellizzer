Aristolochia oblongata

Aristolochia oblongata, commonly known as the Chinese birthwort, is a climbing herbaceous plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, hooded flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces fruits that are elongated and contain seeds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodlands and along stream banks.

Due to its unique morphology and medicinal properties, it is of interest to botanists and herbalists alike.
