Anachortus oranensis

Anachortus oranensis, commonly known as the Oranese thistle, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its spiny leaves and purple, thistle-like flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to arid environments. It has a compact growth habit and is often found in coastal areas and mountainous regions.

Its botanical features make it a notable species in local flora and an important plant for ecological studies.
