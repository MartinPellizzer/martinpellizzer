Athanasia flexuosa

Athanasia flexuosa, commonly known as the wandering flame, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking, bright orange to red inflorescences. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically clustered in a spherical shape, giving it a fiery appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Athanasia flexuosa is also noted for its drought tolerance and ability to bloom throughout the year under favorable conditions.
