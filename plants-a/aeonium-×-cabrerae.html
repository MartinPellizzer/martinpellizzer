Aeonium × cabrerae

Aeonium × cabrerae, commonly known as the Ghost Plant, is a hybrid succulent native to the Canary Islands.

It features rosettes of fleshy, blue-green leaves that are often tinged with red or purple at the edges. The plant produces striking red or orange flowers in late spring or early summer. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with full sun exposure.

Its unique coloration and compact growth make it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
