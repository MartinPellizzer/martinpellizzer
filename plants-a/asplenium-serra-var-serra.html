Asplenium serra var. serra

Asplenium serra var. serra, commonly known as the serrate shield fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to parts of Asia, including China, Japan, and Korea.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its compact, dense foliage with finely serrated leaf margins. The fronds are typically dark green and glossy, with a shield-shaped appearance, and they grow in a rosette pattern. This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.
