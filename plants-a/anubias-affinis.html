Anubias affinis

Anubias affinis, commonly known as the narrow-leaf anubias, is a hardy aquatic plant native to Africa.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its narrow, elongated leaves that are typically dark green in color. The plant thrives in low to moderate light conditions and is often used in aquariums due to its adaptability. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by bright red berries.

Anubias affinis is also valued for its ability to help stabilize substrates in aquatic environments.
