Abildgaardia setifolia

Abildgaardia setifolia, commonly known as the slender leaf bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces delicate, bell-shaped flowers that are usually blue or violet in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Abildgaardia setifolia is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and rockeries.
