Asarum tubulosum

Asarum tubulosum, commonly known as the slender asarum, is a herbaceous perennial native to the eastern United States.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a smooth texture and a distinctive, aromatic scent. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in a spathaceous structure, typically blooming in late spring. Its roots are often used in traditional medicine for their purported medicinal properties.

Asarum tubulosum thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.
