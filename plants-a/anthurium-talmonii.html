Anthurium talmonii

Anthurium talmonii, commonly known as the Talmon's arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves and vibrant spadix. The plant produces a unique inflorescence consisting of a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Anthurium talmonii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated as a houseplant.
