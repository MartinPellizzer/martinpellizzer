Acacia sinuata

Acacia sinuata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf wattle, is a shrub native to Australia.

Acacia sinuata is adapted to dry, sandy soils and is drought-tolerant, making it suitable for arid environments.

It plays an important role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for native wildlife.
