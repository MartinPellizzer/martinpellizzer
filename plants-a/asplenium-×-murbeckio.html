Asplenium × murbeckio

Asplenium × murbeckio, commonly known as the hybrid maidenhair fern, is a naturally occurring hybrid between Asplenium trichomanes and Asplenium bulbiferum.

It is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped fronds that are typically dark green with a slightly glossy appearance. The plant produces small, spore-bearing structures called sporangia on the underside of its leaves. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in temperate regions.

Asplenium × murbeckio is valued for its ornamental qualities and is commonly cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
