Aridarum hansenii

Aridarum hansenii, commonly known as the Hansen's desert orchid, is a rare flowering plant native to arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, succulent-like leaves and small, delicate flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and requires minimal water, making it well-adapted to drought conditions. Its root system is shallow, allowing it to absorb moisture from the surface layer of the soil.

Due to habitat loss and over-collection, Aridarum hansenii is considered vulnerable and is protected in several conservation programs.
