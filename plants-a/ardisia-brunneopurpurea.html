Ardisia brunneopurpurea

Ardisia brunneopurpurea, commonly known as the brown-purple ardisia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrsinaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves with a reddish-purple underside. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
