Anthurium flexile subsp. flexile

Anthurium flexile subsp.

flexile, commonly known as the flexible anthurium, is a species native to the tropical regions of Central and South America. It is characterized by its glossy, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 centimeters in length.

The plant produces striking spadices, which are typically red or orange with a white or yellow spathe. It thrives in humid, warm environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth. Anthurium flexile subsp.

flexile is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its ornamental foliage and unique flowering structure.
