Aralia leroana

Aralia leroana, commonly known as the American aralia, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern North America.

It features compound leaves that are typically palmately arranged, with a feathery appearance. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers in umbel-like clusters, which are followed by red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in partial to full shade and is often found in moist woodland habitats.

Aralia leroana is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, supporting various wildlife species.
