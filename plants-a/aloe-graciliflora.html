Aloe graciliflora

Aloe graciliflora, commonly known as the slender-flowered aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, branched stems covered in narrow, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in clusters during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Aloe graciliflora is often cultivated for its ornamental value and resilience in harsh conditions.
