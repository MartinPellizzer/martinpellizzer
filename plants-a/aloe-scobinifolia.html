Aloe scobinifolia

Aloe scobinifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, spindly stems with narrow, fleshy leaves that are typically gray-green in color. The plant produces clusters of yellowish-green flowers during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Aloe scobinifolia is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional medicinal uses.
