Anthurium lucens

Anthurium lucens, commonly known as the shining anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a glossy texture. The plant produces striking spathes that can range in color from white to red, often with a yellow or green spatula. Anthurium lucens thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

It is a popular ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and air-purifying qualities.
