Apocynum viminale

Apocynum viminale, commonly known as green milkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family, which includes many milkweed species known for their milky sap. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet, featuring slender, branching stems covered in fine hairs. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often have a slightly serrated edge.

The flowers are small, clustered in umbels, and range in color from white to pale yellow, attracting various pollinators.
