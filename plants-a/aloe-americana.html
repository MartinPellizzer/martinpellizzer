Aloe americana

Aloe americana, commonly known as the American aloe, is a succulent plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with white or yellowish markings. The plant produces a tall, branched inflorescence with yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. Aloe americana is adapted to arid environments, storing water in its leaves to survive drought conditions.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional uses in herbal medicine.
