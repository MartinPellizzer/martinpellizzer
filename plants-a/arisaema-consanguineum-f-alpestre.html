Arisaema consanguineum f. alpestre

Arisaema consanguineum f. alpestre, commonly known as the alpine arrowhead, is a rare and striking plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

The plant features broad, arrowhead-shaped leaves that are often hairy and adapted to cold, high-altitude environments. Its flowers are typically greenish-yellow with a distinctive, fleshy spadix that bears tiny, inconspicuous flowers.
