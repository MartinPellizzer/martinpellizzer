Astilbe thunbergii var. kiusiana

Astilbe thunbergii var. kiusiana, commonly known as the Japanese feather reed, is a deciduous perennial plant native to Japan.

It is characterized by its upright, branched stems that bear clusters of small, feathery flowers in shades of pink, red, or white. The plant produces lance-shaped, dark green leaves that are serrated along the edges. It thrives in shady, moist environments and is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value.

Astilbe thunbergii var. kiusiana is well-suited for woodland gardens and can tolerate partial to full shade.
