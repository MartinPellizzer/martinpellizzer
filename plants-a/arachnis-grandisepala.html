Arachnis grandisepala

Arachnis grandisepala, commonly known as the broad-sepal orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in clusters. The plant features long, slender leaves and has a pseudobulb structure typical of many orchid species. Its sepals are notably broad and often display vibrant colors, contributing to its ornamental value.

Arachnis grandisepala thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
