Anoda hirta

Anoda hirta, commonly known as purpletop, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its erect, hairy stems and clusters of showy purple or pink flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Anoda hirta is often used in native plant gardens for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
