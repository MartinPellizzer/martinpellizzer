Atriplex littoralis var. marina

Atriplex littoralis var. marina, commonly known as saltbush, is a halophytic shrub adapted to coastal environments.

It thrives in saline soils and is often found in dunes and estuarine areas. The plant has succulent leaves that help reduce water loss in arid conditions. Its small, greenish flowers are clustered in panicles, and it produces small, hard seeds.

This species plays a vital role in stabilizing coastal soils and providing habitat for various wildlife.
