Ascyrum plumieri

Ascyrum plumieri, commonly known as the golden star fern, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds that bear small, yellowish flowers arranged in star-like clusters. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest undergrowth or near streams. Its leaves are usually bright green and have a soft, velvety texture.

Ascyrum plumieri is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its attractive appearance and ease of care.
