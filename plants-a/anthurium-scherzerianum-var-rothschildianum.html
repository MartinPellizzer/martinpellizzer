Anthurium scherzerianum var. rothschildianum

Anthurium scherzerianum var. rothschildianum, commonly known as the Red Anthurium, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped spathe that ranges from deep red to orange, often with a contrasting green spadix. The plant belongs to the Araceae family and is prized for its ornamental value in both indoor and outdoor settings. It thrives in warm, humid conditions with bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its unique foliage and long-lasting blooms make it a popular choice for horticultural enthusiasts.
