Adiantum politum

Adiantum politum, commonly known as the silver lace fern, is a delicate, herbaceous plant native to the southeastern United States.

It features finely divided, lacy fronds that give it a distinctive, ethereal appearance. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit, with creeping stems that can form dense colonies. Its leaves are typically bright green with a silvery sheen, especially on the underside.

Adiantum politum thrives in shady, moist environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.
