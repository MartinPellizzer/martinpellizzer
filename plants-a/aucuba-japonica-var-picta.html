Aucuba japonica var. picta

Aucuba japonica var. picta, commonly known as the variegated Japanese aucuba, is a striking evergreen shrub native to Japan.

It features broad, waxy leaves with a distinctive variegation pattern of creamy white, yellow, and green. The plant typically grows to a height of 3 to 6 feet, making it suitable for both indoor and outdoor cultivation. It thrives in partial to full shade and prefers moist, well-drained soil.

Its ornamental foliage makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and landscape design.
