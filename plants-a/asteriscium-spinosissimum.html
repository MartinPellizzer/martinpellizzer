Asteriscium spinosissimum

Asteriscium spinosissimum, commonly known as the spiny asterisk, is a rare and distinctive plant characterized by its unique floral structure.

It features small, star-shaped flowers that are typically blue or purple in color, arranged in clusters along the stems. The plant is native to specific regions in South America, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its foliage consists of narrow, spiny leaves that provide a striking contrast to its delicate blossoms.

Due to its rarity and ornamental value, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation programs.
