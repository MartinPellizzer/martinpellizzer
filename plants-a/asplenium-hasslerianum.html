Asplenium hasslerianum

Asplenium hasslerianum, commonly known as the Hessler's spleenwort, is a small, evergreen fern belonging to the family Aspleniaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped leaves that are typically less than 10 centimeters in length. This fern thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found growing on rock surfaces or in crevices. Its fronds are usually dark green and have a finely toothed margin.

Asplenium hasslerianum is notable for its ability to tolerate low light conditions and is often used in horticultural settings for its ornamental value.
