Aerides diurna

Aerides diurna, commonly known as the dawn orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features striking, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple, often with a distinctive golden or yellow lip. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are elongated and strap-like, typically arranged in a fan shape.

Aerides diurna thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity and well-draining potting media.
