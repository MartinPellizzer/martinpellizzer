Anemone seemenii subsp. lipsiensis

Anemone seemenii subsp.

lipsiensis, commonly known as the Lipsian anemone, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Balkan Peninsula. It typically grows in moist, calcareous soils in mountainous regions.

The plant features showy, white to pale pink flowers with a distinctive central yellow marking. Its leaves are palmately lobed and often have a hairy texture.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
