Anthurium cordiforme var. divergens

Anthurium cordiforme var. divergens, commonly known as the heart-shaped anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are often bright green with a glossy texture. The plant produces vibrant spadices, which are the modified leaves that enclose the small, inconspicuous flowers. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Anthurium cordiforme var. divergens is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
