Anthurium leonii

Anthurium leonii, commonly known as the Leonii Philodendron, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically deep green with a waxy texture. The plant produces spadices, which are specialized inflorescences that resemble flowers but are actually modified leaves. Anthurium leonii thrives in humid, warm environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical landscapes.
