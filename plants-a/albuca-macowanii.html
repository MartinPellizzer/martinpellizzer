Albuca macowanii

Albuca macowanii, commonly known as the Macowan's albuca, is a bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features strap-like, grass-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces tall, slender stems bearing clusters of white or pale yellow flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Albuca macowanii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and adaptability to dry conditions.
