Aichryson punctatum var. subvillosum

Aichryson punctatum var. subvillosum, commonly known as the velvet aichryson, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves covered in a fine, velvety pubescence, which helps reduce water loss in arid conditions. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal watering, making it suitable for rock gardens or container cultivation.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardening.
