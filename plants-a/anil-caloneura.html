Anil caloneura

Anil caloneura, commonly known as the silky leaf tree, is a shrub or small tree native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its elongated, narrow leaves that are often covered in a fine, silky hair, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are typically clustered in dense inflorescences. Its fruit is a three-lobed capsule that contains several seeds.

Anil caloneura is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its unique foliage and adaptability to various soil types.
