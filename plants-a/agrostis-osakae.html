Agrostis osakae

Agrostis osakae, commonly known as the Osaka grass, is a perennial grass species native to Japan.

It typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and stream banks. The plant has slender, erect stems that can reach heights of up to 60 centimeters. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green color and a hairy texture.

The inflorescence consists of a single spikelet, which is a characteristic feature of the Poaceae family.
