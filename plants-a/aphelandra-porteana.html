Aphelandra porteana

Aphelandra porteana, commonly known as the golden torch plant, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Aroviraceae family and is characterized by its long, pendant inflorescences that bear bright yellow bracts. The plant features broad, lance-shaped leaves with a waxy texture and a deep green color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate watering to maintain its vibrant appearance.

Aphelandra porteana is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
