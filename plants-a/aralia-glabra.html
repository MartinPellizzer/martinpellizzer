Aralia glabra

Aralia glabra, commonly known as American angel's trumpet, is a deciduous shrub native to the eastern United States.

It features compound leaves with a feathery appearance, and it produces small, greenish-white flowers in umbels. The plant is characterized by its milky sap and its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types. It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and hardy nature.

Aralia glabra is also known for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal remedies.
