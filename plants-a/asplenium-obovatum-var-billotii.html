Asplenium obovatum var. billotii

Asplenium obovatum var. billotii, commonly known as the Billot's shield fern, is a variant of the broad-leafed spleenwort.

It is a small, epiphytic fern that typically grows on tree trunks or rocks in moist, shaded environments. The leaves are obovate, with a distinctive, shield-like shape and a dense, compact structure. It has a fibrous root system and produces spores in clusters on the underside of its fronds.

This variety is native to regions of Southeast Asia and is valued for its ornamental appearance in botanical gardens.
