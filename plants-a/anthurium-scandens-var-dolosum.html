Anthurium scandens var. dolosum

Anthurium scandens var. dolosum, commonly known as the climbing anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 cm in length. The plant produces striking spadices, which are modified leaves that enclose the true flowers. It thrives in humid, warm environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

This variety is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its ornamental foliage and unique floral structure.
