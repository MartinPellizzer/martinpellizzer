Arctotis petiolata

Arctotis petiolata, commonly known as the blue daisy or African daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a perennial herb with upright stems and bright blue to purple ray flowers surrounding a yellow center. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves that are often hairy, and it thrives in well-drained soil and full sun. It is popular in gardens for its ornamental value and is often used in floral arrangements.

Arctotis petiolata is drought-tolerant and suitable for xeriscaping.
