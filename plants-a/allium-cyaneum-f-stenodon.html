Allium cyaneum f. stenodon

Allium cyaneum f. stenodon, commonly known as the narrow-leaf blue onion, is a botanical variant of the common blue onion.

It is a bulbous plant native to the Mediterranean region, characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and striking blue-violet flowers. The plant belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is valued for its ornamental appeal. Its foliage and flowers are often used in gardens for their aesthetic and aromatic qualities.

This form is distinguished by its narrower leaves compared to the typical form of Allium cyaneum.
