Allium heteronema

Allium heteronema, commonly known as the narrowleaf garlic.

It is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region, characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and small, clustered white or pinkish flowers. The plant produces bulbs that are similar to garlic but are less commonly cultivated. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Allium heteronema is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
