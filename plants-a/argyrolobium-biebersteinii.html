Argyrolobium biebersteinii

Argyrolobium biebersteinii, commonly known as the silver globular pea, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive silver-gray foliage and spherical, clustered flowers that bloom in shades of pink and purple. The plant belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and it has a creeping growth habit with hairy stems. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens, though it is more commonly seen in its natural environment.
