Astragalus podlechii

Astragalus podlechii, commonly known as the Podlech's milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its small, yellow pea-shaped flowers. The plant produces narrow, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments, often in rocky or sandy soils.

Astragalus podlechii is known for its ability to fix nitrogen, contributing to soil fertility in its native habitats.
