Aspilia martii

Aspilia martii, commonly known as the African firethorn, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking, spiky, red-orange flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is often found in rocky or sandy soils. It is adapted to arid conditions and thrives in full sun.
