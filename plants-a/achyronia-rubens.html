Achyronia rubens

Achyronia rubens, commonly known as the red spider lily, is a striking plant native to the eastern United States.

It features tall, slender stems that bear clusters of bright red flowers with white markings, resembling spiders, hence its common name. The plant is a herbaceous perennial that typically blooms in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and emerge from a basal rosette, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Achyronia rubens thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens.
