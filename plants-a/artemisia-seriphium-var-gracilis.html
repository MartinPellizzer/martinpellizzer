Artemisia seriphium var. gracilis

Artemisia seriphium var. gracilis, commonly known as slender leaf artemisia, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically grows in rocky or dry soils, preferring full sun exposure. The plant features aromatic, silver-gray leaves with a feathery texture, and small yellow flowers that bloom in summer. It is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.

This variety is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
