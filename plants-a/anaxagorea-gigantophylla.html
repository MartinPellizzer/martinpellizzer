Anaxagorea gigantophylla

Anaxagorea gigantophylla, commonly known as the giant-leaf sterculia, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, glossy, ovate leaves that can reach up to 50 cm in length. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers arranged in clusters, followed by woody pods containing flat, elliptical seeds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

The species is valued for its ornamental leaves and has some traditional uses in local cultures.
