Astrocaryum carnosum

Astrocaryum carnosum, commonly known as the Brazilian wax palm, is a tall, slender palm native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its smooth, grayish trunk and large, feather-like leaves that can reach up to 6 meters in length. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by large, round fruits that are orange-red in color. The fruit contains a single, hard seed surrounded by a fleshy, edible pulp.

It is valued for its ornamental beauty and has been used by indigenous communities for its fruit and fibers.
