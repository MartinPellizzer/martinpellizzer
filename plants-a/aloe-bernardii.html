Aloe bernardii

Aloe bernardii, commonly known as the giant aloe, is a striking succulent native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, upright stems and large, fleshy leaves that can grow up to 3 meters in height. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers in terminal panicles, which bloom during the summer months. Aloe bernardii is adapted to survive in dry conditions, storing water in its thick, fleshy leaves.

It is also valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine.
