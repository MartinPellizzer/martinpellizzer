Anthurium harlingianum

Anthurium harlingianum, commonly known as the Harling's anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces striking spadices, which are specialized inflorescences that resemble flowers but are actually modified leaves. Anthurium harlingianum thrives in humid, warm environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

It is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its ornamental foliage and unique floral structures.
