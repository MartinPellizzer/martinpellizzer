Artemisia pallida

Artemisia pallida, commonly known as pale sage, is a perennial herb native to North America.

It is characterized by its aromatic, silver-gray foliage and small, yellowish-green flower heads. The plant thrives in dry, well-drained soils and is often found in prairies and open woodlands. Its leaves contain essential oils, which contribute to its strong, minty scent.

Artemisia pallida is also valued for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
