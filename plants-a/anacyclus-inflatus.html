Anacyclus inflatus

Anacyclus inflatus, commonly known as the inflated goat's beard, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features a thick, fleshy stem with large, showy yellow ray florets surrounded by pale green disk florets. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and covered in fine hairs.

Anacyclus inflatus is often found in grasslands and open areas, thriving in well-drained soils.
