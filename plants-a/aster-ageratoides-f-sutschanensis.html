Aster ageratoides f. sutschanensis

Aster ageratoides f. sutschanensis, commonly known as the Sutschan's blueweed, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and medicinal properties. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces small blue to purple flowers arranged in clusters. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in forested areas at higher elevations.

Due to its ornamental value and potential pharmacological uses, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists.
