Acriopsis javanica var. auriculata

Acriopsis javanica var. auriculata, commonly known as the auriculate acris, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Apiaceae family, characterized by its aromatic leaves and umbellate inflorescences.
