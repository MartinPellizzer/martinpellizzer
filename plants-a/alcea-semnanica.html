Alcea semnanica

Alcea semnanica, commonly known as the Persian hollyhock, is a species of flowering plant in the Malvaceae family.

It is native to regions of Iran and is characterized by its tall, upright growth habit and showy, rose-like flowers. Alcea semnanica is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its visual appeal.
