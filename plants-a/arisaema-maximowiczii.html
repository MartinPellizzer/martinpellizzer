Arisaema maximowiczii

Arisaema maximowiczii, commonly known as the Chinese cobra lily, is a striking herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its unique inflorescence consisting of a spadix surrounded by a spathe. The plant features a stout, rhizomatous stem with large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and veined. Its flowers are unisexual, with separate male and female parts, and are often concealed within the spathe.

Arisaema maximowiczii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its exotic appearance.
