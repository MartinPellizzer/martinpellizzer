Alstroemeria cabralensis

Alstroemeria cabralensis, commonly known as the Cabral's lily, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly found in regions of Brazil and Paraguay.

It belongs to the family Alstroemeriaceae and is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that come in a range of colors including red, orange, and yellow. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and has strap-like, grassy leaves.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and horticultural collections.
