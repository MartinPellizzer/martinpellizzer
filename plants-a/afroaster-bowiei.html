Afroaster bowiei

Afroaster bowiei, commonly known as the African aster, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features showy, daisy-like blooms with white petals and a yellow center, typically appearing in spring. Afroaster bowiei thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its ornamental value and adaptability contribute to its cultivation in both gardens and botanical collections.
