Anthurium longiusculum

Anthurium longiusculum, commonly known as the long-spurred anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves and vibrant, spathaceous flowers that come in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect light to flourish. Its unique inflorescence consists of a brightly colored spathe and a spadix, which are typical features of the Araceae family.

Anthurium longiusculum is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental value and ability to purify indoor air.
