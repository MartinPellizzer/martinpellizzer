Argentipallium spiceri

Argentipallium spiceri, commonly known as the silver-pale orchid, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Andes.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique combination of silver and pale pink hues in its petals. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in cool, moist environments. Its flowers are small and clustered, often appearing in late spring or early summer.

Due to its restricted habitat and vulnerability to environmental changes, it is considered a species of conservation concern.
