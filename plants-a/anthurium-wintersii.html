Anthurium wintersii

Anthurium wintersii, commonly known as the Winters' anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a glossy texture. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-shaped inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, often in shades of red, orange, or yellow. Anthurium wintersii thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its unique foliage and exotic flowers make it a popular choice for indoor gardening and ornamental purposes.
