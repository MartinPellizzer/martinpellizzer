Acrostichum niveum

Acrostichum niveum, commonly known as the silver fern, is a tropical plant native to the Pacific Islands.

It is a member of the fern family and is characterized by its distinctive silver-colored fronds. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit, allowing it to spread and form dense colonies. Its fronds are typically pinnately divided with a soft, velvety texture.

Acrostichum niveum is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and is also significant in traditional culture.
