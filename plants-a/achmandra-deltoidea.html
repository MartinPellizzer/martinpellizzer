Achmandra deltoidea

Achmandra deltoidea, commonly known as the delta leaf achmandra, is a flowering plant native to the southern United States.

It is characterized by its deltoid-shaped leaves and clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in wetland habitats, such as marshes and swampy areas. It is a member of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.
