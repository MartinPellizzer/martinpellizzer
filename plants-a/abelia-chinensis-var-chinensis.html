Abelia chinensis var. chinensis

Abelia chinensis var. chinensis, commonly known as Chinese abelia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features fragrant, tubular flowers that bloom in clusters, typically in shades of pink and white. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are usually glossy and dark green. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained soil.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and adaptability to various garden conditions.
