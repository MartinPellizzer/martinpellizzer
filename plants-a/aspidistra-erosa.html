Aspidistra erosa

Aspidistra erosa, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen, is a perennial plant native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and often have a slightly wavy margin. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are typically hidden among the foliage. It thrives in low-light conditions and is well-suited for indoor environments.

Aspidistra erosa is known for its hardiness and ability to tolerate a range of soil types.
