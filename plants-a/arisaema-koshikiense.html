Arisaema koshikiense

Arisaema koshikiense, commonly known as the Koshiki arisaema, is a herbaceous plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its striking, arrow-shaped leaves and unique, spadix-bearing inflorescence. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and stream banks. Its foliage is often a deep green, while the inflorescence can range in color from yellow to orange.

Arisaema koshikiense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its visual interest.
