Artemisia globularia subsp. lutea

Artemisia globularia subsp.

lutea, commonly known as yellow globe-agaric, is a shrub-like plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of Central Asia. It is characterized by its dense, spherical growth habit and yellowish-gray foliage.

The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are typically clustered in spherical or globular inflorescences. It has a strong aromatic scent, which is typical of the Artemisia genus.

This subspecies is adapted to dry conditions and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
