Aster glaber

Aster glaber, commonly known as the rough aster, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves, which give it a rough texture. The plant produces showy blue to purple flowers that bloom in the fall. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, often found in meadows and open woodlands.

Aster glaber is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, providing nectar for pollinators.
