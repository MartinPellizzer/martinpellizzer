Asparagus sarmentosus var. densiflorus

Asparagus sarmentosus var. densiflorus, commonly known as the dense-flowered asparagus, is a perennial shrub native to southern Africa.

It features wiry, creeping stems that often root at the nodes, forming dense mats. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in spring. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and covered in fine hairs.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often used in ornamental gardening for its hardy and attractive appearance.
