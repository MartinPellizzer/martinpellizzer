Aerides longicornu

Aerides longicornu, commonly known as the long-horned orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid tropical environments. Its flowers are often pink or purple with a distinctive, elongated spur.

Aerides longicornu is prized in horticulture for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
