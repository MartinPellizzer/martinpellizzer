Anthistiria Prostrata

Anthistiria prostrata is a low-growing perennial plant native to southern Africa. It features small, pale green leaves that form a dense mat on the ground. This species is known for its ability to thrive in dry, sandy soils and harsh climates. Its flowers, though inconspicuous, are essential for its reproductive cycle. Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes used in landscaping for erosion control.
