Aloe veseyi

Aloe veseyi, commonly known as the Desert Aloe, is a succulent plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, with a waxy coating to reduce water loss. The plant produces tall, branching inflorescences with yellow or orange flowers, which bloom in the spring. Its leaves contain a gel-like substance that is used in traditional medicine and skincare products.

Aloe veseyi is well adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments due to its efficient water storage and reduced leaf surface area.
