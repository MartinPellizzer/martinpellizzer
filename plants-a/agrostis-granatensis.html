Agrostis granatensis

Agrostis granatensis, commonly known as the granate bentgrass, is a perennial grass species native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its fine, tufted growth habit and dense, dark green foliage. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows, wetlands, and along stream banks. Its small, spike-like inflorescence bears tiny, wind-pollinated flowers.

Agrostis granatensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, supporting a variety of insect life.
