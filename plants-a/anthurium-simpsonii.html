Anthurium simpsonii

Anthurium simpsonii, commonly known as the Simpson's anthurium, is a member of the Araceae family and is native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a glossy texture. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-shaped inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, often in shades of red, orange, or yellow. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Anthurium simpsonii is a popular ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
