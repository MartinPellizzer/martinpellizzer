Althenia orientalis subsp. orientalis

Althenia orientalis subsp.

orientalis, commonly known as the oriental althenia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region. It features upright, branching stems covered in fine hairs, and its leaves are lance-shaped with a rough texture.

The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in early summer. Its flowers are followed by capsule fruits containing several elliptical seeds. Althenia orientalis subsp.

orientalis is often found in dry, rocky soils and is valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional medicinal uses.
