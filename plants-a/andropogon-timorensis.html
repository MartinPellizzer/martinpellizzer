Andropogon timorensis

Andropogon timorensis, commonly known as Timor bluestem, is a grass species native to the island of Timor.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and narrow, blue-green leaves that give it its common name. The plant typically grows in open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny florets.

Andropogon timorensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
