Alyssum trochocarpum

Alyssum trochocarpum, commonly known as the rock alyssum, is a low-growing, mat-forming perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in spring. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Alyssum trochocarpum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to stabilize soils in rocky or sandy environments.
