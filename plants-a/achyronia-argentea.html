Achyronia argentea

Achyronia argentea, commonly known as the silverleaf nightshade, is a herbaceous plant native to parts of southern Africa.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other nightshades such as tomatoes and potatoes. Achyronia argentea is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid environments.
