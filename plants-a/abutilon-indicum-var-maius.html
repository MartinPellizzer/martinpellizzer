Abutilon indicum var. maius

Abutilon indicum var. maius, commonly known as the Indian velvet tree, is a species native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 6 meters in height, characterized by its hairy, velvety leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers that are often overlooked due to their modest appearance. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, flat seeds.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has traditional uses in herbal medicine.
