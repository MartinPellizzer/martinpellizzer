Ardisia pellucida subsp. lancetillensis

Ardisia pellucida subsp.

lancetillensis, commonly known as the narrow-leaved pellucida, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Central and South America. It features glossy, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green and slightly pointed.

The plant produces small, white flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences, followed by red, fleshy berries. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for horticultural use in tropical gardens.
