Ageratum conyzoides var. mexicanum

Ageratum conyzoides var. mexicanum, commonly known as Mexican blueweed, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and clusters of small, blue to purple flowers. The plant produces fluffy, white to grayish seeds that are easily dispersed by wind. It thrives in warm, sunny environments and is often found in disturbed soils.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
