Anthyllis vulneraria f. aequilobq

Anthyllis vulneraria f. aequilobq, commonly known as the lesser bird's-foot trefoil, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is a low-growing perennial herb characterized by its distinctive trifoliate leaves and yellow-orange flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in dry, rocky, or sandy soils. It has small, stipitate leaves and produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds.

This species is known for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it valuable in ecological restoration projects.
