Aciotis laxa var. robusta

Aciotis laxa var. robusta, commonly known as the robust dandelion, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of broad, fleshy leaves and yellow composite flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and fields. It has a taproot system that allows it to survive in various environmental conditions.

The species is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal practices.
