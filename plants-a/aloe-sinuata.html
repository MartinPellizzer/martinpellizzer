Aloe sinuata

Aloe sinuata, commonly known as the zigzag aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, narrow leaves that are arranged in a zigzag pattern, giving it its common name. The leaves are typically blue-green in color and have a waxy coating to reduce water loss. It produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow flowers that bloom in summer.

Aloe sinuata is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
