Arctotis stoechadifolia var. discolor

Arctotis stoechadifolia var. discolor, commonly known as the pink arctotis, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces vibrant pink to purple flowers in late summer and autumn. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming period and drought tolerance.
