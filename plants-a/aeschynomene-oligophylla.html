Aeschynomene oligophylla

Aeschynomene oligophylla, commonly known as partridge pea, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers. The plant is known for its ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, making it valuable in soil enrichment. It is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and fields.

Its seeds are edible and have been used by indigenous peoples for food and traditional medicine.
