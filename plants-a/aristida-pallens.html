Aristida pallens

Aristida pallens, commonly known as silvergrass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, silver-gray blades and panicle-like inflorescence that gives it a delicate, feathery appearance. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. Its leaves are narrow and often have a hairy surface, contributing to its distinctive look.

Aristida pallens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in native plant gardens.
