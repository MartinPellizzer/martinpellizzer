Arachnorchis cruscula

Arachnorchis cruscula, commonly known as the spider orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the eastern regions of Australia.

It typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as rainforests and understoreys. The plant features a slender, hairy stem with a single or few flowers that are usually pale green to yellowish in color. Its flowers have a distinctive, spider-like appearance due to their elongated, narrow petals and sepals.

Arachnorchis cruscula is known for its delicate beauty and is often found growing in association with other epiphytic orchids.
