Aponogeton satarensis

Aponogeton satarensis, commonly known as the Satarensis arrowhead, is a aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are typically submerged, with some floating foliage. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom above the water surface. It thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments.

Aponogeton satarensis is valued in aquariums for its ornamental appearance and ease of care.
