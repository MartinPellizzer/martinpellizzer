Aloysia Macrostachya

Aloysia macrostachya is a striking shrub native to the southern regions of South America. Known for its vibrant, fragrant flowers and silvery-green foliage, it is a popular choice for ornamental gardening. This plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it adaptable to various climates. Its unique appearance and pleasant aroma make it a favorite among gardeners and botanists alike. Aloysia macrostachya also holds cultural significance in its native regions and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
