Aloe × spinosissima

Aloe × spinosissima, commonly known as the spiny aloe, is a hybrid succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a waxy coating, and it produces striking red or orange flowers during the summer months. The plant is characterized by its spiny margins and is well adapted to arid environments. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.

Aloe × spinosissima is also valued for its potential medicinal properties, though it is not as widely used as other aloe species.
