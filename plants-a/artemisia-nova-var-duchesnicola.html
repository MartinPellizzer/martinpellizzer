Artemisia nova var. duchesnicola

Artemisia nova var. duchesnicola, commonly known as the New Zealand silver fern, is a perennial herb native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in dense, rounded heads. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and subalpine regions.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
