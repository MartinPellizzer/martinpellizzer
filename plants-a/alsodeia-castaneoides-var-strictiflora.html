Alsodeia castaneoides var. strictiflora

Alsodeia castaneoides var. strictiflora, commonly known as the narrow-flowered alsodeia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in coastal areas and is characterized by its upright habit and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in dense spikes, which bloom in spring. It is a member of the Asteraceae family, known for its composite flower heads.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
