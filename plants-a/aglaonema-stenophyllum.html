Aglaonema stenophyllum

Aglaonema stenophyllum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf Chinese evergreen, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, narrow leaves with striking white or cream veins. The plant is evergreen and thrives in low-light conditions, making it a popular houseplant. It has a compact growth habit and produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are rarely seen.

Its foliage makes it an excellent choice for indoor gardening due to its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.
