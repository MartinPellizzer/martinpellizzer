Aubrieta ekimii

Aubrieta ekimii, commonly known as the Turkish rockcress, is a low-growing perennial plant native to Turkey.

It produces clusters of small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has a mat-forming habit with dense, silver-gray foliage that is highly ornamental. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and alpine settings.

Aubrieta ekimii is valued for its hardiness and ability to thrive in challenging garden conditions.
