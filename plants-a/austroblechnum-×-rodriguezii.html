Austroblechnum × rodriguezii

Austroblechnum × rodriguezii, commonly known as the hybrid fern, is a member of the Dryopteridaceae family.

It is a sterile hybrid resulting from the cross between Austroblechnum pachyrhizum and Austroblechnum robustum. The plant is characterized by its delicate, pinnate fronds with a dark green color and a finely serrated margin. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
