Aloe nigra

Aloe nigra, commonly known as black aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dark green, fleshy leaves that are often tinged with red or purple at the edges. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with bright yellow flowers, which bloom in late summer. Aloe nigra is adapted to arid environments and stores water in its thick leaves.

It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes and has some traditional medicinal uses.
