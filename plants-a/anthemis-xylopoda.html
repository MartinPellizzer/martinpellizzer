Anthemis xylopoda

Anthemis xylopoda, commonly known as the xylopod daisy, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of ray and disk flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. Its foliage is covered in fine, hairy structures that help reduce water loss in arid environments.

The flowers are yellow with a central disc, and the plant is known for its aromatic properties.
