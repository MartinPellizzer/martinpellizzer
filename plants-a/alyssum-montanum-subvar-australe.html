Alyssum montanum subvar. australe

Alyssum montanum subvar. australe, commonly known as the Australian mountain alyssum, is a flowering plant native to southern Australia.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with compact, mat-forming growth habit. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, typically blooming in spring and summer. Its foliage consists of narrow, linear leaves that are gray-green in color.

Alyssum montanum subvar. australe is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its attractive appearance and drought tolerance.
