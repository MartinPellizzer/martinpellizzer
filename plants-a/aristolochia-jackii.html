Aristolochia jackii

Aristolochia jackii, commonly known as the Chinese birthwort, is a climbing vine native to Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive, intricate venation pattern. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically purple or greenish in color. Its unique floral structure is adapted for pollination by specific insects.

The plant contains various bioactive compounds, making it of interest in traditional medicine.
