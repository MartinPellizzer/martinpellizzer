Aloe kamnelii

Aloe kamnelii, commonly known as the Kamnelii aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, with a waxy coating to reduce water loss. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow flowers, which bloom in the summer months. It is adapted to arid environments and requires well-draining soil to thrive.

Aloe kamnelii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional medicinal uses.
