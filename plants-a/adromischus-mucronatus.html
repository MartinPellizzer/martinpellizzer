Adromischus mucronatus

Adromischus mucronatus, commonly known as the sharp-tipped ice plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that are covered in a fine, waxy coating to reduce water loss. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or purple during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental appearance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
