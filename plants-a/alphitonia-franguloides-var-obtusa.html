Alphitonia franguloides var. obtusa

Alphitonia franguloides var. obtusa, commonly known as the broad-leaved alphitonia, is a flowering plant native to eastern Australia.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree or shrub that typically grows in coastal and subtropical regions. The plant features glossy, ovate to elliptical leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. Its small, white or pale yellow flowers are clustered in racemes and bloom in spring.

The fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds.
