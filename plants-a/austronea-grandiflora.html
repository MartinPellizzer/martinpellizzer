Austronea grandiflora

Austronea grandiflora, commonly known as the giant flamebush, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy orange-red flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as birds and insects. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually elliptical in shape and have a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Austronea grandiflora is often used in ornamental gardening due to its vibrant floral display.
