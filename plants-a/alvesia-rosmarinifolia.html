Alvesia rosmarinifolia

Alvesia rosmarinifolia, commonly known as the rosemary-like plant, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its aromatic, needle-like leaves and small, daisy-like flowers that typically bloom in shades of yellow or white. Native to South America, particularly Brazil, it thrives in tropical and subtropical climates.
