Alepidea serrata var. serrata

Alepidea serrata var. serrata, commonly known as the serrated alepidea, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its narrow, strap-shaped leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces clusters of white to pale blue flowers, which bloom in spring and summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
