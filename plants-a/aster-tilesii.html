Aster tilesii

Aster tilesii, commonly known as the Turkish aster, is a flowering plant native to Turkey and parts of the Middle East.

It is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that bloom in shades of purple and white. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry habitats, showcasing its adaptability to harsh environments. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Aster tilesii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental qualities and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
