Aniseia stenantha var. macrostephana

Aniseia stenantha var. macrostephana, commonly known as the large-stemmed aniseia, is a variant of the aniseia plant native to certain regions of South America.

It is characterized by its robust, upright growth habit and large, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The flowers are small and clustered in terminal inflorescences, typically displaying shades of white or pale yellow. This variant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic foliage.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a suitable plant for temperate climates.
