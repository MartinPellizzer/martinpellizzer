Adelodypsis gracilis

Adelodypsis gracilis, commonly known as the slender ice plant, is a succulent species native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, cylindrical stems that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces small, white, star-shaped flowers during the cooler months. Its leaves are typically reduced to small scales, a characteristic adaptation to minimize water loss.

Adelodypsis gracilis is well-suited for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its low maintenance and drought tolerance.
