Arenaria litwinowii

Arenaria litwinowii, commonly known as the Litwinow's sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to Central Asia.

It typically thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky or sandy soils. The plant features small, clustered flowers that are usually white or pale pink, blooming in early summer. Its leaves are linear and succulent, adapted to conserve water in dry conditions.

Arenaria litwinowii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in stabilizing sandy soils.
