Andropogon demissus

Andropogon demissus, commonly known as slender bluestem, is a perennial grass native to the eastern and central United States.

It is characterized by its fine, blue-green foliage and slender, upright growth habit. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny flowers. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, often found in open woodlands and prairies.

Andropogon demissus is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, providing food and habitat for various wildlife species.
