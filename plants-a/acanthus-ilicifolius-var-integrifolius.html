Acanthus ilicifolius var. integrifolius

Acanthus ilicifolius var. integrifolius, commonly known as the broad-leaved acanthus, is a striking ornamental plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features large, palmate leaves with a velvety texture and a distinctive, spiny margin. The plant produces showy, spire-like inflorescences with purple to pink flowers that bloom in summer. Its foliage is evergreen, making it a valuable addition to gardens for year-round interest.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, often used in landscaping for its architectural form and ornamental value.
