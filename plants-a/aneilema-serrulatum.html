Aneilema serrulatum

Aneilema serrulatum, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves and vibrant, showy flowers that bloom in shades of purple, pink, and white. The plant thrives in humid, well-drained soil and requires indirect sunlight to flourish. Its botanical features include opposite, lance-shaped leaves and a short, fibrous root system.

Aneilema serrulatum is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
