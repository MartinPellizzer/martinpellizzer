Amaryllis × quadricolor

Amaryllis × quadricolor, commonly known as the rainbow amaryllis, is a hybrid plant resulting from crosses between different amaryllis species.

It is characterized by its vibrant, multicolored flowers that often display combinations of red, orange, yellow, and white. The plant typically produces large, showy blooms in late spring to early summer. Its foliage consists of broad, strap-like leaves that are usually dark green.

Amaryllis × quadricolor is a popular choice for gardeners due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
