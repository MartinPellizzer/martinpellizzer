Allionia cordifolia

Allionia cordifolia, commonly known as the heartleaf allionia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves, which are typically glossy and dark green. The plant produces clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, depending on the variety. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Allionia cordifolia is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
