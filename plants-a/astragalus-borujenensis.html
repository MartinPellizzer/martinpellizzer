Astragalus borujenensis

Astragalus borujenensis, commonly known as Borujen milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to Iran.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, pea-like flowers. The plant is typically found in arid and semi-arid regions, adapting to harsh environmental conditions. It plays a role in soil stabilization and may have potential medicinal uses, though further research is needed.

Its ecological significance contributes to the biodiversity of its native habitat.
