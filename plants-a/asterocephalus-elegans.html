Asterocephalus elegans

Asterocephalus elegans, commonly known as the elegant starhead, is a flowering plant native to the southern regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, star-shaped yellow flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and prefers full sun exposure. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in a fine layer of white hairs, which help reduce water loss.

Asterocephalus elegans is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
