Aster × sekimotoi

Aster × sekimotoi, commonly known as the Sekimoto's aster, is a hybrid perennial plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that bloom in the summer months. The plant typically grows to a height of 30 to 60 centimeters, with hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers come in shades of white, pink, and purple, making it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, requiring moderate water and care.
