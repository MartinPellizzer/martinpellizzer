Asplenium trichomanes nothosubsp. lusaticum

Asplenium trichomanes nothosubsp.

lusaticum, commonly known as the Lusatian shield fern, is a rare hybrid fern native to Central Europe. It is characterized by its compact, evergreen foliage with a distinctive, shield-shaped appearance.

The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and rocky crevices. Its fronds are dark green and have a leathery texture, with a unique arrangement of pinnae.

Due to its limited distribution and hybrid origin, it is considered a rare and often overlooked species in botanical studies.
