Anthericum mattiazzii

Anthericum mattiazzii, commonly known as the Mattiazzii's lily, is a rare and endangered plant species native to the alpine regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of pale green to white, bell-shaped flowers. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery bloom. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is typically found in mountainous areas with well-drained soils.

Due to habitat loss and climate change, conservation efforts are crucial for its survival.
