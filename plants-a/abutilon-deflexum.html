Abutilon deflexum

Abutilon deflexum, commonly known as the woolly abutilon, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features soft, hairy leaves that are often grayish-green in color. Its flowers are yellow and borne in clusters, adding ornamental value to the plant.

It thrives in warm, sunny conditions and is often cultivated as a decorative plant in gardens.
