Allium bassitense

Allium bassitense, commonly known as the narrow-leaved garlic.

It is a perennial bulbous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. The plant features grass-like leaves and produces clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers. Its bulbs are often used in traditional medicine and culinary practices.

Allium bassitense is adapted to cold climates and thrives in well-drained soils.
