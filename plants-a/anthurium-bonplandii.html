Anthurium bonplandii

Anthurium bonplandii, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a reddish or maroon tint. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-shaped inflorescences surrounded by a showy, colorful spathe. It thrives in humid, warm environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium bonplandii is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
