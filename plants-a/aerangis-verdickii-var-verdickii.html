Aerangis verdickii var. verdickii

Aerangis verdickii var. verdickii, commonly known as the Cape orchid, is a rare and striking orchid native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of fragrant, white or pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. It is known for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its elegant appearance.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native range.
