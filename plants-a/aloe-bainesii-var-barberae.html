Aloe bainesii var. barberae

Aloe bainesii var. barberae, commonly known as the Barber's aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a distinctive red or purple tint along the edges. The plant produces tall, branched flower stalks that bear yellow flowers, which bloom in the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires moderate sunlight, making it suitable for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.

Due to its ornamental value and resilience, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
