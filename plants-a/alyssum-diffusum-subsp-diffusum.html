Alyssum diffusum subsp. diffusum

Alyssum diffusum subsp.

diffusum, commonly known as common alyssum, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region. It features dense clusters of small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pale pink, typically blooming in spring.

The plant has finely divided, gray-green foliage that is often covered in a fuzzy coating. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its compact growth habit and long flowering period contribute to its ornamental value in gardens.
