Aloysia

Aloysia, commonly known as the silverleaf plant, is a genus of flowering plants in the verbena family.

It is characterized by its fragrant, tubular flowers that range in color from white to purple. The leaves are typically silver or gray-green, giving the plant its common name. Aloysia species are often used in ornamental gardening for their aesthetic appeal.

They are native to South America and thrive in warm, sunny climates.
