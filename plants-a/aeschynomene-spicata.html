Aeschynomene spicata

Aeschynomene spicata, commonly known as slender aeschynomene, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of soil types. It has a deep root system that helps in soil stabilization and nitrogen fixation.

Aeschynomene spicata is also used in some traditional medicinal practices.
