Arabidopsis tuemurnica

Arabidopsis tuemurnica, commonly known as the Tuemurnica thale, is a species of flowering plant in the Brassicaceae family.

It is a small, annual plant typically found in arid and semi-arid regions. The plant has small, green leaves and produces small white or yellow flowers. It is often used in botanical studies due to its genetic simplicity and rapid life cycle.

Its adaptability to harsh environments makes it an important subject in research on plant resilience.
