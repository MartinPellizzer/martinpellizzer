Arctotis acaulis var. undulata

Arctotis acaulis var. undulata, commonly known as the undulating arctotis, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that features compact, upright growth with bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, giving them a slightly fuzzy texture. This variety is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and its ability to thrive in full sun.

It prefers well-drained soil and is relatively drought-tolerant once established.
