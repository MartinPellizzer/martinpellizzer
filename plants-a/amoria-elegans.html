Amoria elegans

Amoria elegans, commonly known as the elegant orchid, is a species of flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant blooms that typically range in color from white to pale pink. The plant has strap-like leaves that are dark green and glossy, often arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Amoria elegans is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
