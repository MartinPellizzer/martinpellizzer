Aristida sahelica

Aristida sahelica, commonly known as Sahelian grass, is a perennial grass species native to the Sahel region of Africa.

It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, demonstrating remarkable drought tolerance. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It plays an important role in stabilizing soil and supporting local ecosystems.

Its adaptability makes it a valuable species for ecological restoration projects.
