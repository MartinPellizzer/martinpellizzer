Aloe eremophila

Aloe eremophila, commonly known as the desert aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are thick and fleshy, adapted to store water in arid environments. The leaves are typically blue-green in color with a waxy coating that reduces water loss. It produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow flowers, which bloom in late summer.

This plant is well-suited for xeriscaping and is relatively low maintenance once established.
