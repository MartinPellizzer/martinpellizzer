Astracantha brachycalyx

Astracantha brachycalyx, commonly known as the short calyx aster, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features small, daisy-like flowers that are typically blue to purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its delicate blooms.
