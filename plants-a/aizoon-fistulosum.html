Aizoon fistulosum

Aizoon fistulosum, commonly known as the slender milkweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It features slender, branching stems that are covered in fine, white hairs, and it produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters. The plant has opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are also hairy, contributing to its distinctive appearance. Aizoon fistulosum is known for its milky sap, which is a characteristic feature of the milkweed genus.

It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky soils and along dry streambeds.
