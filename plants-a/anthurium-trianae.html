Anthurium trianae

Anthurium trianae, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red spathe, which is actually a modified leaf that encloses the small, green spadix. The plant thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth. Its leaves are broad and glossy, often displaying a deep green color with a slight sheen.

Anthurium trianae is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
