Anthurium × hastiferum

Anthurium × hastiferum, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Araceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped spathe that can range in color from red to orange, pink, or white. The plant produces small, green spathes that enclose the spadix, which bears tiny, inconspicuous flowers. Native to the tropical regions of Central and South America, it thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Its striking appearance makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and as a decorative plant.
