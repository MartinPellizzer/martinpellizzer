Anothea scandens

Anothea scandens, commonly known as the climbing arrowroot, is a tropical perennial vine native to the Americas.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white or silver markings. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. Its roots are used in traditional medicine and as a starch source.

Anothea scandens thrives in humid, shady environments and is often found in rainforests.
