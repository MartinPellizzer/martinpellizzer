Aeschynanthus pulchellus

Aeschynanthus pulchellus, commonly known as the pink flame plant, is a member of the Gesneriaceae family and is native to tropical regions of Africa.

Aeschynanthus pulchellus is also noted for its ability to produce numerous flower clusters, making it a popular choice for hanging baskets and container gardens.
