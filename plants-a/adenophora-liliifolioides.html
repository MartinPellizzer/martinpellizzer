Adenophora liliifolioides

Adenophora liliifolioides, commonly known as the narrow-leaved bellflower, is a perennial herb native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped, blue to purple flowers. The plant has lanceolate leaves that are typically arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or rocky habitats.
