Albuca aspera

Albuca aspera, commonly known as the rough albuca, is a perennial bulbous plant native to southern Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of white or pale yellow flowers. The plant is characterized by its rough, textured surface on its leaves and stems. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Albuca aspera is often cultivated for its ornamental value and easy care requirements.
