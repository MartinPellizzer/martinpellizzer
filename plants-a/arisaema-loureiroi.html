Arisaema loureiroi

Arisaema loureiroi, commonly known as the Chinese arrowhead, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its distinctive arrow-shaped leaves. The plant produces an inflorescence consisting of a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.

The plant has traditional medicinal uses in some Asian cultures.
