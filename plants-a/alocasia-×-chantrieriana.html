Alocasia × chantrieriana

Alocasia × chantrieriana, commonly known as the Chinese Alocasia, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Araceae family.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length, often with a deep green color and prominent veins. The plant produces striking spatulate inflorescences with small, white flowers surrounded by a colorful spathe. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Alocasia × chantrieriana is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and ease of care.
