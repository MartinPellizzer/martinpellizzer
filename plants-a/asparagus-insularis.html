Asparagus insularis

Asparagus insularis, commonly known as the island asparagus, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and fleshy, gray-green foliage. The plant produces small, white to yellowish flowers during the spring and summer months. Its stems are often branched and can form a bushy appearance.

Asparagus insularis is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
