Alsophila lurida

Alsophila lurida, commonly known as the slender tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and feathery fronds. The plant has a distinctive crown of long, pinnate fronds that can reach several meters in length. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

The trunk is covered in a fibrous, spongy material that helps it retain moisture.
