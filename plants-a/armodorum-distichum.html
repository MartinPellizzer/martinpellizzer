Armodorum distichum

Armodorum distichum, commonly known as the narrow-leafed yarrow, is a perennial herb native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its slender, upright stems and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, pale yellow to white flowers arranged in dense, cylindrical inflorescences. It typically grows in dry, sandy or rocky soils and is found in various habitats across southern Australia.

Armodorum distichum is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its hardy nature and ability to stabilize soil.
