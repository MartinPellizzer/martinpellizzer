Alvordia glomerata var. insularis

Alvordia glomerata var. insularis, commonly known as the insular alvordia, is a flowering plant native to specific island regions.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The leaves are typically narrow and lance-shaped, with a smooth, glossy surface. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to coastal environments.
