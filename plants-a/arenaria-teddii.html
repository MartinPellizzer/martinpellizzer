Arenaria teddii

Arenaria teddii, commonly known as the Teddii sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the arid regions of western North America.

It is characterized by its dense clusters of small, white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has succulent, fleshy leaves that help it conserve water in its harsh environment. It typically grows in rocky soils and is often found in desert or semi-desert habitats.

Arenaria teddii is well adapted to survive in extreme conditions with minimal water availability.
