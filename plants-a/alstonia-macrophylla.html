Alstonia macrophylla

Alstonia macrophylla, commonly known as the giant alstonia or giant flame tree, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, glossy, elliptical leaves that can grow up to 30 cm in length. The plant produces showy, clustered yellow flowers that are followed by woody, capsule-like fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Its rapid growth and ornamental value make it a popular choice for landscaping in tropical regions.
