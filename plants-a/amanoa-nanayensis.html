Amanoa nanayensis

Amanoa nanayensis, commonly known as the Nanay Island amanoa, is a rare flowering plant native to the island of Nanay in the Philippines.

It belongs to the orchid family (Orchidaceae) and is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant grows in humid, shaded environments, often in lowland rainforests or along stream banks. Its conservation status is critically endangered due to habitat loss and limited distribution.

Amanoa nanayensis plays a significant role in the local ecosystem and is of great interest to botanists and conservationists.
