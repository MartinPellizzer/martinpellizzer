Antheroceras ornithogaloides

Antheroceras ornithogaloides, commonly known as the bird's nest orchid, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

The plant produces a single, showy bloom with a white or pale yellow color, surrounded by a hood-like structure. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.
