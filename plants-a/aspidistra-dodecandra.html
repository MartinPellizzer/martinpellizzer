Aspidistra dodecandra

Aspidistra dodecandra, commonly known as the African aspidistra, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical Africa.

It thrives in shady, moist environments and is often used in horticulture for its ornamental foliage.

Aspidistra dodecandra is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
