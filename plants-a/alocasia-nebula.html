Alocasia nebula

Alocasia nebula, commonly known as the Nebula Alocasia, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 3 feet in length, often with intricate patterns resembling the colors of a nebula. The plant belongs to the Araceae family, which is known for its unique inflorescence structure consisting of a spadix and spathe. Its foliage is typically a deep green with a silvery or variegated underside, adding to its ornamental appeal.

Alocasia nebula thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
