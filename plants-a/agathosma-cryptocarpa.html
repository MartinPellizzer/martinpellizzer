Agathosma cryptocarpa

Agathosma cryptocarpa, commonly known as the Cape citrus or wild bergamot, is a perennial shrub native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, narrow leaves and yellow flowers. The plant produces small, oval-shaped fruits that are typically green when mature. It thrives in dry, well-drained soils and is often found in fynbos vegetation.

Its essential oils are valued for their medicinal and aromatic properties.
