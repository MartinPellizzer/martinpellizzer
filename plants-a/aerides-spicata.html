Aerides spicata

Aerides spicata, commonly known as the spike orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its upright, branching stems and clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and shrublands. Its leaves are linear and arranged in a rosette pattern, which helps in water retention.

Aerides spicata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
