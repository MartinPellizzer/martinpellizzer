Andropogon ternatus subsp. macrothrix

Andropogon ternatus subsp.

macrothrix, commonly known as the tall bluestem, is a grass species native to the southeastern United States. It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and narrow, bluish-green leaves.

The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny flowers. It thrives in full sun and is often found in dry, sandy soils.

Its botanical features make it an important component of native grassland ecosystems.
