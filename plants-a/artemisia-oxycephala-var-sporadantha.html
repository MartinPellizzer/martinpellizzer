Artemisia oxycephala var. sporadantha

Artemisia oxycephala var. sporadantha, commonly known as the Sporadic Artemisia, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its aromatic, silver-gray foliage and small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is known for its medicinal properties, including antiseptic and anti-inflammatory effects.

Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for use in xeriscaping and ornamental gardening.
