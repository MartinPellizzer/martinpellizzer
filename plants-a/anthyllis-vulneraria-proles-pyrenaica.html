Anthyllis vulneraria proles pyrenaica

Anthyllis vulneraria proles pyrenaica, commonly known as the Pyrenean restharrow, is a flowering plant native to the Pyrenees mountain range.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its upright growth habit and small, pea-like flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or limestone soils and thrives in alpine or subalpine environments. This species is of interest to botanists due to its limited distribution and ecological adaptations to high-altitude conditions.
