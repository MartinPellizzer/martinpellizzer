Asplenium odontites

Asplenium odontites, commonly known as the toothed maidenhair fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Aspleniaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, spore-bearing sorus clusters on the underside of its leaves. Native to tropical and subtropical regions, it thrives in moist, shaded environments.

Its unique morphology and ornamental appearance make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
