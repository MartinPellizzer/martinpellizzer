Anthurium mayoanum

Anthurium mayoanum, commonly known as the Mayan anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central America.

It features vibrant, heart-shaped leaves that are dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces striking spadices, which are modified leaves that enclose the actual flowers. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Anthurium mayoanum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated as a houseplant.
