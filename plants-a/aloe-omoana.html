Aloe omoana

Aloe omoana, commonly known as the Red Aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically red or pink in color, which help it store water and withstand arid conditions. The plant produces tall, branched flower stalks that bear yellow or orange flowers, adding ornamental value. Its leaves contain a gel-like substance that has been used for its soothing properties.

Aloe omoana thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for xeriscaping.
