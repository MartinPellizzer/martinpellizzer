Asphodeliris gracilis

Asphodeliris gracilis, commonly known as the slender asphodel, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces spikes of small, white or pale blue flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is drought-tolerant. It is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and coastal areas.

Its botanical characteristics make it an attractive and resilient addition to gardens in suitable climates.
