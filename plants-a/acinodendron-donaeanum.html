Acinodendron donaeanum

Acinodendron donaeanum, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a deciduous tree that can grow up to 15 meters tall, featuring a broad, spreading canopy. The plant produces large, showy yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the dry season. Its leaves are pinnately compound, with small, oval-shaped leaflets.

The tree is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
