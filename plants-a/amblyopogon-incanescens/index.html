Amblyopogon Incanescens

Tropical milkweed, scientifically known as Amblyopogon incanescens, is a strikingly beautiful plant native to the warm climates of Central and South America. With its vibrant orange to red foliage and showy, clustered flowers, it adds a dramatic touch to any garden or landscape. This plant thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for butterfly gardens and xeriscaping. Its nectar-rich blooms attract a wide variety of pollinators, including monarch butterflies, bees, and hummingbirds. Despite its beauty, it requires careful management to prevent it from becoming invasive in certain regions.
