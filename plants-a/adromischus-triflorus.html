Adromischus triflorus

Adromischus triflorus, commonly known as the Three-flowered Stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, rosette-shaped growth pattern and fleshy, smooth leaves that are often bluish-green in color. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow to orange during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Adromischus triflorus is prized for its ornamental value and ease of care in both indoor and outdoor settings.
