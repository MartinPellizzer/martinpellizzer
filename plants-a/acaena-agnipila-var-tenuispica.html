Acaena agnipila var. tenuispica

Acaena agnipila var. tenuispica, commonly known as the slender-stemmed spinster, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its finely hairy stems and small, spiny leaves. The plant produces spherical, red-brown burrs that contain seeds, which are dispersed by animals. It thrives in coastal and subalpine environments, often growing in sandy or gravelly soils.

Due to its unique morphology and ecological role, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
