Anthurium kruseanum

Anthurium kruseanum, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped, red spathes that enclose the spadix, which produces small, inconspicuous flowers. The plant has dark green, leathery leaves that are often glossy and arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium kruseanum is popular as a houseplant due to its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
