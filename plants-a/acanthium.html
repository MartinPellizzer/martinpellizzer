Acanthium

Acanthium, commonly known as the spiny amaranth, is a genus of flowering plants in the amaranth family.

These plants are characterized by their spiny leaves and vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from red to orange. They are native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas. Acanthium species are often cultivated for their ornamental value and are also used in traditional medicine.

The plant's robust growth and adaptability make it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
