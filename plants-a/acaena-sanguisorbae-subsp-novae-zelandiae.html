Acaena sanguisorbae subsp. novae-zelandiae

Acaena sanguisorbae subsp.

novae-zelandiae, commonly known as New Zealand bramble, is a shrub native to New Zealand. It belongs to the family Rosaceae and is characterized by its prickly stems and small, spiny leaves.

The plant produces bright red, berry-like fruits that are an important food source for native birds. It thrives in a variety of soil types but prefers well-drained conditions.
