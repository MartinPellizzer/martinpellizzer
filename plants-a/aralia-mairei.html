Aralia mairei

Aralia mairei, commonly known as the Maire's aralia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It features compound leaves that are palmately arranged, with a distinctive silvery-white underside. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers in clustered inflorescences, followed by spiny, berry-like fruits. It thrives in temperate climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The bark and leaves have been used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.
