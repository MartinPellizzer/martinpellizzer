Alsodeia liberica

Alsodeia liberica, commonly known as the African nightshade, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is an annual herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features simple, alternate leaves that are usually ovate or elliptical in shape. Its small, white or pale purple flowers bloom in clusters during the cooler months.

The fruit is a capsule that contains several small, hard seeds.
