Agrostis × amurensis

Agrostis × amurensis, commonly known as the Amur ryegrass, is a hybrid grass species native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its fine, tufted leaves and dense, mat-forming growth habit. This plant is often found in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in temperate climates. Agrostis × amurensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is used in landscaping and turfgrass applications.

It also plays a role in stabilizing soil and preventing erosion in natural habitats.
