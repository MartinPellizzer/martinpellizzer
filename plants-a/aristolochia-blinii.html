Aristolochia blinii

Aristolochia blinii, commonly known as the Chinese birthwort, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Aristolochiaceae family and is characterized by its unique, twisting, pipe-shaped flowers. The plant produces heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and a distinct, often aromatic scent. Its flowers are typically greenish-yellow with a prominent, hood-like structure.

Aristolochia blinii is known for its medicinal uses in traditional Chinese medicine.
