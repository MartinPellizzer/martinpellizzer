Arundarbor pungens

Arundarbor pungens, commonly known as the sharp-leaved evergreen, is a perennial plant native to the temperate regions of the northern hemisphere.

It features lance-shaped, serrated leaves that are typically dark green on top and lighter in color underneath. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers in the spring, which are followed by blue-black berries. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its botanical characteristics make it both visually appealing and ecologically beneficial to local wildlife.
