Alocasia brancifolia

Alocasia brancifolia, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen or black magic plant, is a tropical flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, dark green, arrowhead-shaped leaves with a glossy texture. The plant produces clusters of small, white, spadix-like flowers surrounded by a purple spathe. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

Alocasia brancifolia is popular as a houseplant due to its striking appearance and air-purifying properties.
