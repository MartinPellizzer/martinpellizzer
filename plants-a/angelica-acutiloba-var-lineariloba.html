Angelica acutiloba var. lineariloba

Angelica acutiloba var. lineariloba, commonly known as the narrow-leaved angelica, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It features large, compound leaves that are elongated and narrow, with a distinctive serrated margin. The plant produces showy, umbel-shaped flower clusters that range in color from white to pale yellow. It thrives in moist, fertile soils and is often found in woodland or streamside habitats.

Its aromatic foliage and medicinal properties have made it a valued plant in traditional herbal medicine.
