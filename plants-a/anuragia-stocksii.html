Anuragia stocksii

Anuragia stocksii, commonly known as the Indian sandwort, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayan region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky and arid environments. The plant features small, white or pink flowers arranged in clusters at the ends of its stems. Its leaves are fleshy and succulent, adapted to conserve water in its native habitat.

Anuragia stocksii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in rock gardens.
