Agrimonia aitchisonii var. pedicellata

Agrimonia aitchisonii var. pedicellata, commonly known as the Kashmir agrimony, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of small, yellowish flowers. The plant has serrated leaves and is often found in moist, shaded environments. It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is valued for its medicinal properties in traditional medicine.

Its botanical features make it an important species in alpine ecosystems.
