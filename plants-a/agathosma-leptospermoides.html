Agathosma leptospermoides

Agathosma leptospermoides, commonly known as African sage, is a succulent shrub native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, narrow leaves and small yellow flowers. The plant is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its fleshy stems and leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, making it a hardy choice for xeriscaping.

Its essential oils are often used in aromatherapy and traditional medicine.
