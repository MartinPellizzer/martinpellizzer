Allium euosmum

Allium euosmum, commonly known as the purple garlic.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the western United States, particularly California. The plant features strap-like leaves and produces clusters of purple or pink flowers in the spring. It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is valued for its ornamental appeal.

Allium euosmum is also noted for its mild garlic scent, which is less pungent than that of common garlic.
