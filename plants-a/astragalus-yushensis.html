Astragalus yushensis

Astragalus yushensis, commonly known as Yushan milkvetch, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family Fabaceae.

It is native to the mountainous regions of China, particularly in the Yushan area of Yunnan Province. The plant is characterized by its compound leaves and small, pea-like flowers that typically bloom in shades of purple or pink. It thrives in rocky and well-drained soils, often growing at elevations between 2,000 and 4,000 meters.

Astragalus yushensis is valued for its ecological role in stabilizing soil and supporting local biodiversity.
