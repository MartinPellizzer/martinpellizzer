Adiantopsis senae

Adiantopsis senae, commonly known as the Sena's maidenhair fern, is a rare and delicate fern species native to the mountainous regions of Central America.

It belongs to the family Adiantaceae, characterized by its finely divided, feather-like fronds. The plant thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in cloud forests. Its leaves are typically bright green and possess a distinctive, elegant appearance.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, Adiantopsis senae is considered a vulnerable species.
