Actinophloeus bleeseri

Actinophloeus bleeseri, commonly known as the African starfish plant, is a unique succulent native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of fleshy, waxy leaves that are typically green with a bluish or purplish tint. The plant produces striking star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, which bloom in late winter to early spring. Its leaves are adapted to store water, allowing it to thrive in arid conditions.

The plant is prized for its ornamental value and ability to add a dramatic visual impact to rock gardens or container displays.
