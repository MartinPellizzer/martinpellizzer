Anthericum molle

Anthericum molle, commonly known as the soft anthericum, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in a fine, silky texture. The plant produces small, clustered white or pale green flowers that bloom in early summer. Anthericum molle is typically found in moist, shaded environments such as woodland floors or stream banks.

Its botanical characteristics make it a distinctive and often sought-after plant in horticultural collections.
