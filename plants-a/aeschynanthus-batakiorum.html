Aeschynanthus batakiorum

Aeschynanthus batakiorum, commonly known as the Batak orchid vine, is a tropical flowering plant native to the rainforests of Indonesia.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped blooms that are often visited by hummingbirds and other pollinators. Its leaves are typically opposite, ovate, and have a glossy surface.

Aeschynanthus batakiorum thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
