Aconitum × paniculatum var. flexicaule

Aconitum × paniculatum var. flexicaule, commonly known as the flexible monkshood, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to purple spires of flowers. The plant features large, palmately lobed leaves that are deeply divided, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Aconitum × paniculatum var. flexicaule is valued in gardens for its ornamental value and historical use in traditional medicine.
