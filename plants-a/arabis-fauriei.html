Arabis fauriei

Arabis fauriei, commonly known as the alpine rockcress, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and western Asia.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other cruciferous plants. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils at high altitudes, where it thrives in cool, moist conditions. Its flowers are small, white or pink, and arranged in a raceme, adding a delicate touch to its alpine habitat.

Arabis fauriei is often used in rock gardens due to its hardy nature and attractive blooms.
