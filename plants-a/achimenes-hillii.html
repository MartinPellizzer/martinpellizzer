Achimenes hillii

Achimenes hillii, commonly known as the purple-flowered achimenes, is a tropical perennial plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking violet or purple tubular flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. The plant has heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are typically dark green in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Achimenes hillii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant blooms and compact growth habit.
