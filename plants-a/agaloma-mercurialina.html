Agaloma mercurialina

Agaloma mercurialina, commonly known as the mercury orchid, is a rare and striking plant native to the mountainous regions of South America.

The plant thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot. Its blooming period typically occurs during the late spring and early summer months.
