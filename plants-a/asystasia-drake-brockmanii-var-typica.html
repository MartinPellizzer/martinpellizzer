Asystasia drake-brockmanii var. typica

Asystasia drake-brockmanii var. typica, commonly known as the narrow-leaved asystasia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and lance-shaped leaves.
