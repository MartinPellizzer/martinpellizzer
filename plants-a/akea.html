Akea

Akea, commonly known as the Hawaiian sea hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from red to yellow. Akea plays an important ecological role in supporting local wildlife and is also valued for its ornamental beauty.
