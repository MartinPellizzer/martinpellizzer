Aloe khamiesensis

Aloe khamiesensis, commonly known as the Khamies aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a waxy coating, which helps it retain moisture in arid conditions. The plant produces tall, branched flower stalks that bear yellowish-orange flowers, adding ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Aloe khamiesensis is also valued for its potential medicinal properties, similar to other aloe species.
