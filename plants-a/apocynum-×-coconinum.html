Apocynum × coconinum

Apocynum × coconinum, commonly known as the hybrid dogbane, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant produces a milky sap that contains toxic alkaloids, making it potentially harmful to humans and animals. Its leaves are opposite and oval-shaped, often with a slightly hairy texture.

Apocynum × coconinum is typically found in moist, disturbed soils along roadsides and in woodland edges.
