Anthurium peltigerum

Anthurium peltigerum, commonly known as the velvet leaf anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a velvety texture. The plant produces striking spadix flowers surrounded by a colorful spathe, which can range from red to orange. It thrives in humid, warm environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium peltigerum is popular as a decorative plant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
