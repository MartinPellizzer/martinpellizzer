Alstonia macrophylla var. glabra

Alstonia macrophylla var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth alstonia, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, smooth, glabrous leaves that are typically elliptical in shape. The tree produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers followed by woody capsules containing numerous seeds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forests.

Its bark is thick and fissured, providing a distinctive appearance and contributing to its ornamental value.
