Acaena ovalifolia var. chubutensis

Acaena ovalifolia var. chubutensis, commonly known as the Chubut burr bush, is a shrub native to the Patagonian region of Argentina.

It is characterized by its dense, spiny growth and small, oval-shaped leaves. The plant produces bright red, spherical fruits that are adapted for dispersal by animals. It thrives in dry, rocky habitats and is often found in steppe and grassland environments.

Its botanical features make it well-suited for erosion control in arid landscapes.
