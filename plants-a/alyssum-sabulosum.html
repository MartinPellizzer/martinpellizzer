Alyssum sabulosum

Alyssum sabulosum, commonly known as sand alyssum, is a flowering plant native to the western United States and parts of Mexico.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically reaches heights of 10 to 20 centimeters. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers throughout the summer months. Its leaves are narrow and gray-green, adapted to thrive in well-drained, sandy soils.

Alyssum sabulosum is often found in coastal dunes and other arid environments, showcasing its resilience to harsh conditions.
