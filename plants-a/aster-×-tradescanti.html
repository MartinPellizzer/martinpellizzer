Aster × tradescanti

Aster × tradescanti, commonly known as the Chinese aster, is a hybrid perennial plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that come in a variety of colors, including pink, red, and white. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet and features narrow, grass-like leaves. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

The flowers bloom in the summer months, making it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
