Achimenes multiflora

Achimenes multiflora, commonly known as the tropical crown flower, is a herbaceous perennial native to Central and South America.

It features striking, tubular, brightly colored flowers that bloom in clusters, typically in shades of red, pink, and orange. The plant has heart-shaped, glossy leaves and a bushy growth habit, making it suitable for container gardening or as a bedding plant. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming period, which can extend from summer into fall.
