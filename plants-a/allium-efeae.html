Allium efeae

Allium efeae, commonly known as the Efe's onion, is a species of flowering plant in the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, particularly in Kazakhstan and Uzbekistan. The plant produces narrow, grass-like leaves and small, clustered white or pale pink flowers. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.

Allium efeae is generally hardy and adaptable to a variety of soil conditions, making it a versatile choice for landscaping.
