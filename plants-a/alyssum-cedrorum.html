Alyssum cedrorum

Alyssum cedrorum, commonly known as mountain alyssum, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features dense clusters of small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has basal leaves that are narrow and linear, often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and alpine settings.

Alyssum cedrorum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
