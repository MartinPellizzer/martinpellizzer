Asplenium paucivenosum

Asplenium paucivenosum, commonly known as the slender spleenwort, is a small, evergreen fern belonging to the Aspleniaceae family.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette formation. The plant has a simple, non-branching rhizome and produces small, inconspicuous sporangia on the underside of its fronds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found growing on rocks or tree trunks in temperate regions.

Asplenium paucivenosum is a hardy species that is relatively easy to cultivate in controlled garden settings.
