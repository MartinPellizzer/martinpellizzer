Astragalus cymbaecarpos

Astragalus cymbaecarpos, commonly known as the broadleaf milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to the western United States and parts of Canada.

It is a member of the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow or pink flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid conditions. It has a deep taproot system that allows it to access water from deeper soil layers.
