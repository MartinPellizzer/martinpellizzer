Arctiodracon camtschatcensis

Arctiodracon camtschatcensis, commonly known as the Kamchatka dragon's blood tree, is a rare and ancient plant native to the Russian Far East.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and dense crown of long, narrow leaves. The plant produces a dark red resin, which has been historically used for medicinal and dyeing purposes. It thrives in humid, temperate climates and is often found in forested areas.
