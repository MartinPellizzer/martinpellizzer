Aloe umbellata

Aloe umbellata, commonly known as the umbrella aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, narrow leaves that are typically green with a slight blue tint. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing yellow flowers, which bloom in summer. Its leaves contain a gel-like substance that is sometimes used for its purported medicinal properties.

Aloe umbellata is relatively low maintenance and suitable for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.
