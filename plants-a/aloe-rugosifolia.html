Aloe rugosifolia

Aloe rugosifolia, commonly known as the rough-leaved aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are ribbed and often have a textured surface. The leaves are typically green with a bluish tinge and bear small, white to yellowish teeth along the edges. It produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellowish-green flowers.

This plant is adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
