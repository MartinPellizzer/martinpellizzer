Anthyllis vulneraria var. mixta

Anthyllis vulneraria var. mixta, commonly known as the mixed bitter vetch, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is a low-growing perennial herb characterized by its yellow flowers and trifoliate leaves. The plant is native to Europe and parts of western Asia, often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. It has small, hairy stems and produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds.

The species is known for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it beneficial for soil fertility.
