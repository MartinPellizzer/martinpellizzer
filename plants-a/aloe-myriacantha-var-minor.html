Aloe myriacantha var. minor

Aloe myriacantha var. minor, commonly known as the dwarf aloe or miniature aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features compact, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a reddish tinge, and it produces small, yellow flowers on slender stems. The plant is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and low maintenance requirements.

This variety is a smaller, more compact form of the larger Aloe myriacantha species.
