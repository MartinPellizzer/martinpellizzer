Amorphophallus calabaricus subsp. mayoi

Amorphophallus calabaricus subsp.

mayoi, commonly known as the giant arrowhead, is a species of flowering plant native to tropical Africa. It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves and striking inflorescence.

The plant produces a unique, spadix-like structure that is often covered in a fleshy, colorful spathe. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in swampy or marshy areas.

This subspecies is notable for its rapid growth and potential use in traditional medicine.
