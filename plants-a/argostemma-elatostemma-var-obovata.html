Argostemma elatostemma var. obovata

Argostemma elatostemma var. obovata, commonly known as the broad-leafed argostemma, is a herbaceous perennial native to Southeast Asia.

It features showy, pink to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has opposite, ovate to obovate leaves that are often hairy on the underside. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical characteristics make it a distinctive addition to botanical gardens and wetland habitats.
