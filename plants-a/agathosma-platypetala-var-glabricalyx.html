Agathosma platypetala var. glabricalyx

Agathosma platypetala var. glabricalyx, commonly known as African geranium, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It features small, clustered flowers that range in color from pink to purple, typically blooming in spring and summer. The plant has aromatic, lance-shaped leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental value make it a popular choice for both ornamental and medicinal purposes.
