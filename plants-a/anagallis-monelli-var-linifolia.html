Anagallis monelli var. linifolia

Anagallis monelli var. linifolia, commonly known as the strawberry violet, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a low-growing perennial with heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas or along stream banks. Its flowers bloom in spring, and it produces small, edible berries that are sometimes used in culinary applications.

The species is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting pollinators.
