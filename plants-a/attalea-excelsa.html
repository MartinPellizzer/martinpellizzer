Attalea excelsa

Attalea excelsa, commonly known as the royal palm, is a tall, slender palm native to South America.

It is characterized by its tall, unbranched stem and large, pinnately compound leaves that can reach several meters in length. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by dark blue, fleshy fruits that contain a single seed. It thrives in tropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The species is also valued for its hard, durable wood and has cultural significance in some regions.
