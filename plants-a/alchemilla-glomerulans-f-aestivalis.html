Alchemilla glomerulans f. aestivalis

Alchemilla glomerulans f. aestivalis, commonly known as the early alchemilla, is a low-growing perennial plant native to Central and Eastern Europe.

It features dense clusters of small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early summer, typically from June to July. The plant has dark green, velvety leaves that are often used in traditional herbal medicine. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas or along stream banks.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable addition to native plant gardens and ecological restoration projects.
