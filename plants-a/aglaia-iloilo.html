Aglaia iloilo

Aglaia iloilo, commonly known as Iloilo aglaia, is a species of flowering plant belonging to the Meliaceae family.

It is native to the Philippines, particularly the island of Iloilo, where it thrives in tropical and subtropical climates. Aglaia iloilo is often found in lowland forests and is valued for its ornamental and potential medicinal properties.
