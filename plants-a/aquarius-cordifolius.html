Aquarius cordifolius

Aquarius cordifolius, commonly known as the heartleaf aquarius, is a unique plant species characterized by its heart-shaped leaves.

It belongs to the family Acanthaceae and is native to tropical regions of South America. The plant features vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to purple, attracting pollinators like hummingbirds and bees. Its foliage is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is increasingly cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation programs.
