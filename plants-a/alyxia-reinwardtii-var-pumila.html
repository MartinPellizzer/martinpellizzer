Alyxia reinwardtii var. pumila

Alyxia reinwardtii var. pumila, commonly known as the dwarf alyxia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub characterized by its small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest undergrowth. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and often have a glossy texture.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate blooms.
