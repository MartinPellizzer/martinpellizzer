Amelancus × spicata

Amelancus × spicata, commonly known as the spicate amelancus, is a hybrid plant that exhibits characteristics of both its parent species.

It is typically found in moist, well-drained soils in temperate regions. The plant features elongated, spiculate inflorescences that are often used in ornamental horticulture. Its foliage is lance-shaped with a smooth texture, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers.

Amelancus × spicata is valued for its adaptability and aesthetic appeal in garden settings.
