Athanasia graminifolia

Athanasia graminifolia, commonly known as the grass-like everlasting.

This plant is characterized by its slender, grass-like foliage and compact growth habit. It produces small, daisy-like flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. The species is native to southern Africa and thrives in well-drained soils.

It is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
