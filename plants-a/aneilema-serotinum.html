Aneilema serotinum

Aneilema serotinum, commonly known as the late-flowering arrowroot, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its arrow-shaped leaves and striking inflorescence. The plant produces a cluster of small, white flowers surrounded by a colorful spathe, which is typical of the Araceae family. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in swampy or wetland areas.

Aneilema serotinum is valued for its ornamental qualities and has traditional uses in some African cultures.
