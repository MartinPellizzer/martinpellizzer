Acuan jamesii

Acuan jamesii, commonly known as the James' acuan, is a perennial herb native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of tiny, yellowish flowers during the spring and early summer. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to arid environments.

Acuan jamesii is often found in open woodlands and mountainous regions.
