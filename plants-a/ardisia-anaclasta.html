Ardisia anaclasta

Ardisia anaclasta, commonly known as the black ardisia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 3 meters. The plant produces small, dark red berries that are an important food source for various bird species. Its leaves are glossy and elliptical, with a dark green color on the upper surface and a lighter green underside.

Ardisia anaclasta is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in shaded environments.
