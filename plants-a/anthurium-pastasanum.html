Anthurium pastasanum

Anthurium pastasanum, commonly known as the pastasanum arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are deep green with a glossy texture. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-like inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, typically in shades of red or orange. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium pastasanum is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its aesthetic appeal and air-purifying qualities.
