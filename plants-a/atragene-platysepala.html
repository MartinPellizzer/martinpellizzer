Atragene platysepala

Atragene platysepala, commonly known as the flat-sepal atragene, is a member of the Acanthaceae family.

It is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly South Africa. The plant is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves and showy, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Its sepals are broad and flat, giving it its specific epithet "platysepala.".

The species is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its attractive floral display.
