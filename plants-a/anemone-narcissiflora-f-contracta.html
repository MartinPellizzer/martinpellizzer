Anemone narcissiflora f. contracta

Anemone narcissiflora f. contracta, commonly known as the dwarf anemone, is a compact, low-growing perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features showy, cup-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, often with a yellow center. The foliage consists of dark green, palmately lobed leaves that are finely textured. This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in well-drained soil.

It is often used in rock gardens or as a border plant due to its compact size and long blooming period.
