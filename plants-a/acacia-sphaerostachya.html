Acacia sphaerostachya

Acacia sphaerostachya, commonly known as the globe acacia, is a shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its spherical, yellowish flower heads that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant has compound leaves with small, paired leaflets and produces woody pods that contain edible seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.

Acacia sphaerostachya is also valued for its ornamental appeal and use in traditional medicine.
