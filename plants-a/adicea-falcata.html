Adicea falcata

Adicea falcata, commonly known as the sickleleaf daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, sickle-shaped leaves and produces yellow flowers with a white center. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Adicea falcata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
