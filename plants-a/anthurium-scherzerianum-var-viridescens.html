Anthurium scherzerianum var. viridescens

Anthurium scherzerianum var. viridescens, commonly known as the Emerald Arrowhead, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a vibrant green with a glossy texture. The plant produces striking spadices, which are elongated, spathaceous flowers that are usually bright yellow or orange in color. It thrives in humid, warm environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ability to thrive indoors under proper care.
