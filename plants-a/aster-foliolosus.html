Aster foliolosus

Aster foliolosus, commonly known as the leafy aster, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of small, blue to purple flowers. It typically grows in moist, woodland habitats and is often found in shaded areas.

Aster foliolosus is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, supporting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
