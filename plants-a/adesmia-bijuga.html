Adesmia bijuga

Adesmia bijuga, commonly known as the small-leaved burr legume, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its small, compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant produces pod-like fruits that contain several seeds, which are often dispersed by wind. It thrives in dry, arid environments and is adapted to poor soil conditions.

Adesmia bijuga is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
