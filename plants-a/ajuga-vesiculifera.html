Ajuga vesiculifera

Ajuga vesiculifera, commonly known as lesser bugleweed, is a perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded areas such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features oval, dark green leaves that are opposite on the stems. Its small, tubular flowers are usually pink or purple and appear in clusters.

Ajuga vesiculifera is known for its ability to spread via rhizomes, forming dense mats that can help prevent soil erosion.
