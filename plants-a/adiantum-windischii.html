Adiantum windischii

Adiantum windischii, commonly known as the Windisch's maidenhair fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Adiantaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds that are typically bi- or tri-pinnate. The plant is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Thailand, Vietnam, and Malaysia. It prefers shady, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and horticultural collections.
