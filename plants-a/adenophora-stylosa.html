Adenophora stylosa

Adenophora stylosa, commonly known as the slender bellflower, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It features upright stems that bear opposite, lance-shaped leaves with hairy surfaces. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers that are typically blue to purple in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or streamside habitats.

Adenophora stylosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
