Aeschynanthus cambodiensis

Aeschynanthus cambodiensis, commonly known as the Cambodian flame flower, is a member of the Gesneriaceae family.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Southeast Asia, particularly Cambodia and Vietnam. The plant is characterized by its vibrant red-orange tubular flowers that bloom in clusters, often resembling small flames. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical landscapes.
