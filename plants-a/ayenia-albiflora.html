Ayenia albiflora

Ayenia albiflora, commonly known as the white-flowered ayenia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features showy, white ray florets surrounding a central disk of yellow tubular flowers, which attract pollinators like bees and butterflies. Ayenia albiflora typically grows in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Its foliage consists of opposite, lance-shaped leaves with a hairy texture.
