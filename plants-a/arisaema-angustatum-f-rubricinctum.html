Arisaema angustatum f. rubricinctum

Arisaema angustatum f. rubricinctum, commonly known as the red-banded arisaema, is a variant of the narrow-leaf arisaema native to Southeast Asia.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its distinctive, arrow-shaped leaves and striking, spathaceous inflorescence. The plant features a unique floral structure with a spadix surrounded by a brightly colored spathe, often with a red or orange band. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The botanical interest of this plant lies in its ornamental value and its role in the ecological dynamics of its native habitat.
