Alocasia simonsiana

Alocasia simonsiana, commonly known as the Black Velvet Alocasia, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark, velvety leaves that have a unique, almost metallic sheen. The plant belongs to the Araceae family, which includes other ornamental plants like peace lilies and calla lilies. Alocasia simonsiana thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and as a statement plant in tropical gardens.
