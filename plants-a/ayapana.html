Ayapana

Ayapana, commonly known as *Piper auritum*, is a tropical flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other species like black pepper. The plant is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. Ayapana is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its attractive foliage.

It prefers moist, well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for shaded garden areas.
