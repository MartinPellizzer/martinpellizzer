Aster setosus

Aster setosus, commonly known as the rough aster, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves, which give it its common name. The plant produces clusters of small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in the late summer and early fall. Aster setosus is often found in dry, open habitats such as meadows and rocky areas.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, which includes many other familiar flowering plants.
