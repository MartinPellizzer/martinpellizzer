Allium schoenoprasum var. foliosum

Allium schoenoprasum var. foliosum, commonly known as garden chives, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves that are aromatic and often used in culinary applications. The plant produces small, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters at the top of the stem. Its rhizomatous root system allows it to spread and form dense patches in suitable conditions.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for herb gardens.
