Allantodia metteniana var. isobasis

Allantodia metteniana var. isobasis, commonly known as the yellow star-of-Bethlehem, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features striking yellow flowers with a star-shaped appearance, typically blooming in late winter to early spring. The plant has linear, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long-lasting flowers and drought tolerance.
