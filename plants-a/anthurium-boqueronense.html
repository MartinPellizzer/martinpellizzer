Anthurium boqueronense

Anthurium boqueronense, commonly known as the Boqueronense arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its unique inflorescence consisting of a spadix and a spathe. The plant features large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are often surrounded by a brightly colored spathe.

Anthurium boqueronense is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ability to thrive in humid environments.
