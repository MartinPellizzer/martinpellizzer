Ancistrocactus pinkavanus

Ancistrocactus pinkavanus, commonly known as the pink spiny cactus, is a flowering cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems covered in sharp, reddish-brown spines. The plant produces clusters of bright pink flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires full sun exposure.

Due to its unique appearance and ornamental value, it is often cultivated in arid gardens and collections.
