Anelytrum avenaceum

Anelytrum avenaceum, commonly known as the slender panic grass, is a perennial grass species native to South America.

It typically grows in grassland and open habitats, often in regions with temperate to subtropical climates. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences. Its botanical features include a fibrous root system and a culm that is often slender and erect.

It is sometimes used in horticulture for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil types.
