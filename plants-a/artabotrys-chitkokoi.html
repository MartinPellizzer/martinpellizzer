Artabotrys chitkokoi

Artabotrys chitkokoi, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its clusters of fragrant, yellow flowers that bloom in pendulous racemes, resembling a shower of golden droplets. The plant has simple, elliptical leaves that are glossy and dark green in color. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The fruit is a small, woody capsule that contains several seeds.
