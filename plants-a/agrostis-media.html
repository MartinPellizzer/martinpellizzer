Agrostis media

Agrostis media, commonly known as slender wheatgrass, is a perennial grass native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows, pastures, and along stream banks. The plant has fine, grass-like leaves and produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in erosion control.

Its ability to thrive in a variety of conditions makes it a versatile plant in both natural and cultivated settings.
