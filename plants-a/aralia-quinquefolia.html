Aralia quinquefolia

Aralia quinquefolia, commonly known as American aralia, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern North America.

It features compound leaves with five leaflets, giving it the species name "quinquefolia.". The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers in umbel-like clusters, which are followed by red, berry-like fruits. Its roots are often used in traditional medicine, and it is valued for its ornamental appearance.

Aralia quinquefolia thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.
