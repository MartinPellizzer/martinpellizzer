Aster lividus

Aster lividus, commonly known as the pale aster, is a flowering plant native to the northern regions of North America.

It is characterized by its blue to purple flowers and gray-green foliage, which help it thrive in harsh, dry environments. The plant typically grows in open woodlands, meadows, and rocky areas, preferring well-drained soils. Its flowers bloom in the fall, adding a striking visual element to the landscape during late summer and autumn.

Aster lividus is also notable for its medicinal properties, having been used by indigenous peoples for various therapeutic purposes.
