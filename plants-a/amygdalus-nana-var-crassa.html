Amygdalus nana var. crassa

Amygdalus nana var. crassa, commonly known as the dwarf peach or miniature peach, is a compact variety of the peach tree.

It is characterized by its small, rounded leaves and the production of small, sweet fruits. The plant thrives in temperate climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and manageable size.

The flowers are typically white or pale pink, adding to its aesthetic value in gardens.
