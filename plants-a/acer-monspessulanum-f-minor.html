Acer monspessulanum f. minor

Acer monspessulanum f. minor, commonly known as the miniature maple, is a compact variant of the Spanish maple.

It features small, rounded leaves that are typically a vibrant green during the growing season and turn red or orange in autumn. The plant has a slow growth rate, making it ideal for small spaces or container gardening. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Its ornamental value lies in its unique foliage and compact form, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.
