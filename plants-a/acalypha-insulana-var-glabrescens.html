Acalypha insulana var. glabrescens

Acalypha insulana var. glabrescens, commonly known as the smoothleaf copperleaf, is a shrub native to the Pacific Islands.

It is characterized by its smooth, glabrous leaves that are typically ovate to elliptic in shape. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in coastal areas and disturbed soils.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in warm regions.
