Astragalus kongrensis

Astragalus kongrensis, commonly known as the Chinese milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and pea-like flowers. The plant is known for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it beneficial for soil enrichment. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky or sandy soils.

Astragalus kongrensis is also valued in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.
