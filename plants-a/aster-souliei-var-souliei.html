Aster souliei var. souliei

Aster souliei var. souliei, commonly known as the Chinese aster, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant features showy, ray florets that are typically purple or pink, surrounding a central disk of tubular flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its ornamental value and adaptability contribute to its widespread cultivation in horticulture.
