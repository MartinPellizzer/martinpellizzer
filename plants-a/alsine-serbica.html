Alsine serbica

Alsine serbica, commonly known as Serbische Alse, is a species of flowering plant in the family Amaranthaceae.

It is a low-growing annual herb that typically reaches a height of about 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant has alternate, ovate to lanceolate leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs. Its small, greenish-white flowers are clustered in dense inflorescences, and it produces small, dry fruits called utricles.

Alsine serbica is native to Europe and parts of Asia, and it often grows in disturbed soils and along roadsides.
