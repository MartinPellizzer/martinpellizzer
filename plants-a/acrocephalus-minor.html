Acrocephalus minor

Acrocephalus minor, commonly known as the lesser reedling, is a small grass-like plant belonging to the Poaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It typically grows in moist, open habitats such as wet meadows and marshes.
