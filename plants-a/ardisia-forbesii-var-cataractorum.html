Ardisia forbesii var. cataractorum

Ardisia forbesii var. cataractorum, commonly known as the rainforest ardisia, is a species native to the humid tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a small evergreen shrub that typically grows in shaded, moist forest understories. The plant features elliptical, glossy dark green leaves and produces small, white to pale pink flowers. Its fruit is a bright red berry, which contains several seeds and is an important food source for birds and small mammals.

The plant's botanical characteristics make it well adapted to low-light conditions and high humidity environments.
