Adiantum rondonii

Adiantum rondonii, commonly known as the Rondon fern, is a species of fern native to South America.

It belongs to the family Adiantaceae and is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its fronds are usually bi- or tri-pinnate, with a distinctive veining pattern.

Adiantum rondonii is valued for its ornamental appearance and is often cultivated in humid, indoor environments.
