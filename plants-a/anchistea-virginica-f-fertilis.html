Anchistea virginica f. fertilis

Anchistea virginica f. fertilis, commonly known as the fertile form of the Virginia anchistea, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit.
