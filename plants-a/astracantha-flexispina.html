Astracantha flexispina

Astracantha flexispina, commonly known as the flexible spine cactus, is a succulent plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its spiny, cylindrical stems that store water. The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments, adapting to harsh conditions with its thick, fleshy stems. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, appearing at the base of the spines, and it produces small, round fruits.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptations, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
