Aulomyrcia lucida var. grandifolia

Aulomyrcia lucida var. grandifolia, commonly known as the grandiflora variant of the shining aulomyrcia, is a member of the Myrtaceae family.

It is characterized by its glossy, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green in color. The plant produces showy, white to pinkish flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soils and partial to full sunlight.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in warm regions.
