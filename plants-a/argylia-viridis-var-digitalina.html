Argylia viridis var. digitalina

Argylia viridis var. digitalina, commonly known as the green argyle.

It is a variant of the argyle plant, characterized by its vibrant green foliage. This plant typically features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires ample sunlight to flourish.

The botanical interest of this variety lies in its unique coloration and structural form.
