Aspidistra tenuifolia

Aspidistra tenuifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved aspidistra, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its dark green, strap-like leaves that are narrow and elongated. It is a hardy evergreen that thrives in low-light conditions and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Aspidistra tenuifolia is valued for its ability to tolerate shade and its elegant, foliage-driven appearance.
