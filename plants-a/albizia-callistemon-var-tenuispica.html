Albizia callistemon var. tenuispica

Albizia callistemon var. tenuispica, commonly known as the narrow-leaved bottlebrush, is a shrub native to Australia.

It features bright red or orange floral spikes that emerge from the ends of its branches, resembling bottlebrushes. The leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often with a slight waxy coating. This plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

Its ornamental value comes from its striking flowers and compact growth habit.
