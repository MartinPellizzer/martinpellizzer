Aeonium gomeraeum

Aeonium gomeraeum, commonly known as the Gomera's aeonium, is a succulent plant native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the Crassulaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-shaped growth pattern. The plant features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a bluish or purple tint. It produces vibrant yellow or orange flowers during the spring or early summer.

Aeonium gomeraeum is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
