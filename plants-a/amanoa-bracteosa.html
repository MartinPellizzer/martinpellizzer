Amanoa bracteosa

Amanoa bracteosa, commonly known as the bracteosa piper.

It is a flowering plant native to the Pacific Islands, particularly Hawaii, and belongs to the Piperaceae family. The plant is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often have a rough texture.

Amanoa bracteosa is adapted to grow in humid, shaded environments and is often found in coastal and lowland areas.
