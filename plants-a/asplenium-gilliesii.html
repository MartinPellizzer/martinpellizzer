Asplenium gilliesii

Asplenium gilliesii, commonly known as the narrow-leaf rock fern, is a species of fern native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen growth habit and its ability to thrive in rocky, shaded environments. The plant has dark green, lance-shaped fronds that are often covered in a dense layer of fine, silky hairs. It belongs to the family Aspleniaceae and is typically found in coastal and inland areas with moist, well-drained soils.

Asplenium gilliesii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
