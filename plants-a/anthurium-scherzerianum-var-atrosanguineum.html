Anthurium scherzerianum var. atrosanguineum

Anthurium scherzerianum var. atrosanguineum, commonly known as the Black Velvet Anthurium, is a striking tropical plant characterized by its deep, velvety purple foliage.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is native to the rainforests of South America. The plant features a prominent, spadix-shaped inflorescence surrounded by a colorful spathe, typically in shades of red or orange. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity and well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical landscapes.
