Anthurium alatipetiolum

Anthurium alatipetiolum, commonly known as the red-veined anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves with deep red veins against a glossy green background. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-shaped inflorescences that are often surrounded by a colorful spathe. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Anthurium alatipetiolum is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
