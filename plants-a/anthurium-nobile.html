Anthurium nobile

Anthurium nobile, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a tropical flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces vibrant red spathes that enclose a spadix, which is the actual flowering structure. Anthurium nobile thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.

Its unique foliage and colorful inflorescence make it a popular choice for indoor gardening and ornamental purposes.
