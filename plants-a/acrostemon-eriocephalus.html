Acrostemon eriocephalus

Acrostemon eriocephalus, commonly known as the velvetleaf arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that features heart-shaped, waxy leaves with a distinctive velvety texture. The plant produces small, white, clustered flowers that bloom in summer, adding ornamental value to gardens. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found near water bodies.

Its foliage and flowering habit make it a popular choice for tropical landscapes and wetland restoration projects.
