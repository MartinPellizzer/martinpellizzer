Alchornea multispicata

Alchornea multispicata, commonly known as the African coffee tree, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a small to medium-sized shrub or tree that typically grows up to 5 meters in height. The plant features simple, opposite leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. Its flowers are small and clustered in panicles, often with yellowish or greenish petals.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds.
