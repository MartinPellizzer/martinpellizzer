Acer cappadocicum var. glabrescens

Acer cappadocicum var. glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth maple, is a variant of the Turkish maple.

It is a deciduous tree native to the Cappadocia region of Turkey. The plant features palmate leaves with five lobes, which are typically smooth and glossy. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers in early spring, followed by orange-red samaras.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various soil types.
