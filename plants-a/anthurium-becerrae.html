Anthurium becerrae

Anthurium becerrae, commonly known as the Berreras Anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Colombia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves and vibrant, spadix-like inflorescence. The plant thrives in humid, warm environments with indirect light and well-draining soil. Its foliage is typically dark green with a glossy texture, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers surrounded by a colorful spathe.

Anthurium becerrae is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.
