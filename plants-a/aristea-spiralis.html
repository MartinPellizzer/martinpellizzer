Aristea spiralis

Aristea spiralis, commonly known as the spiral arnoldia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, mat-forming growth habit. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple in color, arranged in dense clusters. Its leaves are narrow, grass-like, and often covered in a fine layer of white hairs.

Aristea spiralis is well adapted to arid environments and thrives in full sun with well-drained soil.
