Amoria

Amoria, commonly known as the golden groundsel, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in dry, sunny environments. The plant features small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters, surrounded by pale green bracts. Its leaves are typically gray-green and covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.

Amoria is often used in rock gardens and as a decorative plant due to its compact growth and attractive appearance.
