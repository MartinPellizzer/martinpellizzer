Artemisia turcomanica

Artemisia turcomanica, commonly known as the Turkestan wormwood, is a perennial shrub native to Central Asia.

It is characterized by its aromatic, silver-gray foliage and small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant thrives in dry, arid environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes many other aromatic herbs and flowering plants.

Artemisia turcomanica is valued for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional medicine for various ailments.
