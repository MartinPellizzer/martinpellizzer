Acer platanoides f. crispum

Acer platanoides f. crispum, commonly known as the curly-leaf maple, is a cultivar of the Norway maple.

It is characterized by its distinctive, wavy, and often curling leaves that give it a unique appearance. The plant is a deciduous tree with a rounded crown and smooth, gray bark. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained, fertile soil.

This cultivar is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its aesthetic appeal and adaptability.
