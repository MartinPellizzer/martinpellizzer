Anthemis kitenensis

Anthemis kitenensis, commonly known as the Kiten tansy, is a flowering plant native to the Balkan Peninsula.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry habitats, showcasing its adaptability to harsh environments. Its yellow-orange flowers bloom in late summer, attracting various pollinators.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species within its range.
