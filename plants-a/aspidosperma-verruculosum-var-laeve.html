Aspidosperma verruculosum var. laeve

Aspidosperma verruculosum var. laeve, commonly known as the smooth aspidosperma, is a species of flowering plant in the Apocynaceae family.

It is native to Brazil and is characterized by its smooth, grayish bark and elliptical leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. Its fruits are follicles that split open to release seeds.
