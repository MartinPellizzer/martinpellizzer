Abolboda grandis var. grandis

Abolboda grandis var. grandis, commonly known as the giant abolboda, is a species of flowering plant native to specific regions in Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stem and large, heart-shaped leaves that can reach up to 30 cm in length. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom during the wet season. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Due to its unique morphology and limited distribution, it is considered a valuable subject for botanical research.
