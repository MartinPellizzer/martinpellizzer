Aronia Melanocarpa Var. Subpubescens

Chokeberry, scientifically known as Aronia melanocarpa var. subpubescens, is a hardy shrub native to eastern North America. This plant is prized for its dark, nutrient-rich berries that are both edible and highly antioxidant. Its adaptability to various soil types makes it a popular choice for landscaping and ecological restoration. The chokeberry's resilience and ornamental value contribute to its growing popularity among gardeners and conservationists. With its striking fall foliage and medicinal potential, it stands out as a versatile and valuable plant species.
