Armeria Adsurgens

Sea thrift, scientifically known as Armeria adsurgens, is a hardy coastal plant that thrives in harsh, saline environments. It is native to the rocky shores and dunes of the British Isles and northern Europe. This low-growing evergreen features dense clusters of pink or white flowers that bloom in spring and early summer. Its ability to withstand strong winds and salt spray makes it a valuable species for coastal restoration projects. Despite its tough conditions, sea thrift adds a delicate beauty to the rugged landscapes it calls home.
