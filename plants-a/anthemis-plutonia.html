Anthemis plutonia

Anthemis plutonia, commonly known as the purple tansy, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of ray and disc florets. The plant features upright, branched stems covered in fine, grayish hairs. Its leaves are narrow, lance-shaped, and covered in a dense layer of fine, white pubescence.

The flowers are yellow with a central dark purple disk, giving it a distinctive appearance.
