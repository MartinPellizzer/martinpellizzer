Asystasia plumbaginea

Asystasia plumbaginea, commonly known as the blue asystasia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows as a herbaceous perennial, often reaching heights of up to 60 centimeters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered with a fine pubescence.

The flowers are clustered in whorled inflorescences, adding to its ornamental value in gardens.
