Abacosa Micrantha

Abacosa micrantha is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of South America. It is known for its small, clustered flowers that bloom in shades of white and pale pink. This species thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in cloud forests. Due to habitat loss, it is considered endangered and requires careful conservation efforts. Its unique appearance and limited distribution make it a subject of interest for botanists and plant enthusiasts alike.
