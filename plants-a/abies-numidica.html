Abies numidica

Abies numidica, commonly known as the Atlas cedar, is a coniferous tree native to the Atlas Mountains of North Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, pyramidal shape and dark green, needle-like leaves. The tree produces small, spherical cones that contain winged seeds, typical of the Cupressaceae family. Its bark is smooth and grayish-brown, becoming fissured with age.

Abies numidica is well adapted to rocky, arid environments and is often found at high altitudes.
