Ayenia nummularia

Ayenia nummularia, commonly known as the silverleaf ayenia, is a low-growing perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features dense, rounded foliage that is covered in a fine layer of white, waxy hairs, giving it a silvery appearance. The plant produces small, showy flowers in shades of pink and white, which bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Ayenia nummularia is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
