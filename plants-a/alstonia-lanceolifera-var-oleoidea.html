Alstonia lanceolifera var. oleoidea

Alstonia lanceolifera var. oleoidea, commonly known as the African angel's trumpets, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It features large, showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, blooming in clusters. The plant has opposite, lanceolate leaves that are often covered in a fine, white pubescence. It produces elliptical, woody capsules containing numerous seeds, and the bark is thick and fissured.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
