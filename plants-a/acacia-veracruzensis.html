Acacia veracruzensis

Acacia veracruzensis, commonly known as the Veracruz acacia, is a shrub or small tree native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its bipinnate leaves, which are divided into numerous small leaflets. The plant produces yellow to golden-green flowers arranged in dense, cylindrical clusters. It thrives in dry to semi-arid environments and is often found in limestone soils.

Acacia veracruzensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
