Arthrocereus campos-portoi

Arthrocereus campos-portoi, commonly known as the Campos-Portoi cactus, is a species of cactus native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its columnar stem and spiny structure. The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments, adapting to harsh conditions with its thick, water-storing stems. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, appearing in clusters at the top of the stems.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and resilience in dry climates.
