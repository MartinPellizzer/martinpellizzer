Adiantum patens

Adiantum patens, commonly known as the maidenhair fern, is a delicate and ornamental fern native to the eastern United States.

It features finely divided, lace-like fronds that are typically bright green and turn bronze in the winter. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and prefers well-draining, acidic soil. It reproduces via spores and is often grown as a houseplant or in shaded garden beds.

Its unique appearance and easy care make it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor gardening.
