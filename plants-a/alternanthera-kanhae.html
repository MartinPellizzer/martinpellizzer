Alternanthera kanhae

Alternanthera kanhae, commonly known as the red-stemmed Alternanthera, is a species of flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its striking red stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant is typically herbaceous, with small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or white in color. It thrives in moist, sunny environments and is often used in aquatic gardens or as a decorative plant in water features.

Its ornamental value is largely due to its vivid stem color and compact growth habit.
