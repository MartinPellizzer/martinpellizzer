Asplenium trichomanes subsp. inexpectata

Asplenium trichomanes subsp.

inexpectata, commonly known as the slender shield fern, is a rare and delicate fern species native to specific regions of Europe. It is characterized by its small, compact fronds that are typically covered in fine, soft hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in rocky or limestone-rich soils. Its reproductive structures, including sporangia, are clustered on the underside of its fronds, typical of the Aspleniaceae family.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species in many areas.
