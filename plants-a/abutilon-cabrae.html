Abutilon cabrae

Abutilon cabrae, commonly known as the silky abutilon, is a shrub native to the Canary Islands.

It is characterized by its soft, fuzzy leaves and showy, lantern-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for coastal and rocky environments. Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Abutilon cabrae is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
