Amaranthus taishanensis

Amaranthus taishanensis, commonly known as the Taishan amaranth, is a species of flowering plant in the amaranth family.

It is native to China and is characterized by its upright growth habit and vibrant red or orange bracts. The plant produces small, green flowers that develop into three-winged seeds. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional Chinese medicine.

Amaranthus taishanensis is also valued for its nutritional content, particularly its high protein and mineral content.
