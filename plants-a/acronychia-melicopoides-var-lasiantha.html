Acronychia melicopoides var. lasiantha

Acronychia melicopoides var. lasiantha, commonly known as the broad-leaved silky oak, is a tropical rainforest tree native to northern Australia.

It is characterized by its large, glossy, elliptical leaves that are often covered in a fine, silky hair. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by woody, capsule-like fruits. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded, humid environments.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitat.
