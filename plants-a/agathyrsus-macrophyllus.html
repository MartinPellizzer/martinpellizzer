Agathyrsus macrophyllus

Agathyrsus macrophyllus, commonly known as the large-leafed agathyrsus, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, elliptical leaves that can grow up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by distinctive, winged fruits. Its foliage is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

Agathyrsus macrophyllus thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.
