Alocasia acuta var. angustipartita

Alocasia acuta var. angustipartita, commonly known as the narrow-leaf elephant ear, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features large, arrow-shaped leaves that are dark green with a distinctive light green midrib. The plant has a short, stout rhizome and produces small, inconspicuous white flowers. Its foliage is often used in ornamental gardening for its dramatic appearance.

It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.
