Alseuosmia macrophylla

Alseuosmia macrophylla, commonly known as the broad-leafed stinkwood, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, glossy, dark green leaves that can grow up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, arranged in clusters. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.
