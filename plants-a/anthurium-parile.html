Anthurium parile

Anthurium parile, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive inflorescence composed of a spadix and a spathe. The plant features broad, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. Its striking red spathe is often used in ornamental horticulture.

Anthurium parile thrives in humid, warm environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.
