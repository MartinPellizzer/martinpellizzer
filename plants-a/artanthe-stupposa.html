Artanthe stupposa

Artanthe stupposa, commonly known as the desert bell, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

The plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of red, pink, and purple, which bloom in late spring to early summer.
