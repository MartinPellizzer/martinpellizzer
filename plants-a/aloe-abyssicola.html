Aloe abyssicola

Aloe abyssicola, commonly known as the abyssinian aloe, is a succulent plant native to the highlands of Ethiopia.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in summer, adding ornamental value. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their healing properties.

Aloe abyssicola is well adapted to rocky soils and can withstand periods of drought.
