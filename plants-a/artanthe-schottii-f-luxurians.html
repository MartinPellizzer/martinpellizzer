Artanthe schottii f. luxurians

Artanthe schottii f. luxurians, commonly known as the luxuriant artanthe, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features upright, slender stems with narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are typically blue to violet in color, arranged in dense clusters, and bloom in the spring.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
