Argentina anserina subsp. egedii

Argentina anserina subsp.

egedii, commonly known as the Egedii water shield, is a aquatic plant native to South America. It is characterized by its floating leaves that are broadly ovate with a waxy surface.

The plant produces small, white flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater habitats.

Its botanical features make it an important species for wetland ecosystems.
