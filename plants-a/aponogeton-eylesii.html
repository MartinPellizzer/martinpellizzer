Aponogeton eylesii

Aponogeton eylesii, commonly known as the Eyles' water arrowhead, is a perennial aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features long, slender leaves that are typically submerged or floating, with a distinctive arrowhead shape. The plant produces small, white flowers that are often borne on short stalks above the water surface. It thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments and is often found in marshes and ponds.

Aponogeton eylesii is valued in aquascaping for its ornamental appeal and ability to provide habitat for aquatic life.
