Alternanthera martii f. bastosiana

Alternanthera martii f. bastosiana, commonly known as the red-leaf alternanthera, is a strikingly ornamental plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, variegated foliage, which often displays shades of red, pink, and green. The plant belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is valued for its compact growth habit and ability to thrive in a variety of light conditions. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and have a velvety texture, enhancing its visual appeal.

This cultivar is popular in aquatic and terrestrial gardening for its aesthetic value and ease of care.
