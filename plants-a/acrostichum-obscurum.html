Acrostichum obscurum

Acrostichum obscurum, commonly known as the dark fern, is a species of fern native to the tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its dark green, leathery fronds that are often densely packed and have a unique, compact growth habit. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, such as forest understories, where it thrives in high moisture and indirect light. Its reproductive structures, including sporangia, are located on the underside of the fronds, a common feature among ferns.

Acrostichum obscurum is valued for its ornamental appeal and plays a role in maintaining the ecological balance of its native habitats.
