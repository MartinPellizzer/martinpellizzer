Adenia tuberifera

Adenia tuberifera, commonly known as the African butter tree, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Passifloraceae and is characterized by its woody structure and tuberous roots. The plant produces large, showy flowers with five petals and a distinctive crown of filaments. Its fruit is a fleshy, edible berry that contains numerous seeds.
