Athamanta gigantea

Athamanta gigantea, commonly known as the giant athamantha, is a striking member of the Acanthaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. This plant is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from violet to deep purple. The leaves are typically large, lance-shaped, and have a velvety texture.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments in its natural habitat.
