Agrostis × ussuriensis

Agrostis × ussuriensis, commonly known as the Ussuri reedgrass, is a hybrid grass species native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and narrow, grass-like leaves, typically growing in wetland or marshy environments. The plant produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences that contain both sterile and fertile florets. It is often found in regions with high humidity and well-drained soils.

Its botanical features make it an important component of wetland ecosystems and a subject of study in plant taxonomy.
