Anthurium × macrolobum

Anthurium × macrolobum, commonly known as the giant anthurium, is a hybrid plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length, with a deep green color and a prominent midrib. The plant produces striking spadices, which are bright red or orange in color, surrounded by a showy spathe. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and requires well-draining soil.

Anthurium × macrolobum is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
