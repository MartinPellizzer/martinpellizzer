Anil foliosa

Anil foliosa, commonly known as the leafy anil, is a tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by round, fleshy fruits. Its botanical classification places it in the family Rubiaceae, which includes over 10,000 species.

Anil foliosa is valued for its aromatic leaves and has been used in herbal remedies for centuries.
