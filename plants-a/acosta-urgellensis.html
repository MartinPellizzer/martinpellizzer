Acosta urgellensis

Acosta urgellensis, commonly known as the Urgellensis acosta, is a flowering plant native to the Andean region of South America.

It belongs to the family Cactaceae and is characterized by its spiny stems and clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or arid environments, adapting to the harsh conditions of its native habitat. Its flowers are often yellow or orange, attracting pollinators such as bees and birds.
