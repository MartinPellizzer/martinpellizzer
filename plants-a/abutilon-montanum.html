Abutilon montanum

Abutilon montanum, commonly known as the mountain abutilon, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a shrub that typically grows in dry, rocky habitats. The plant produces showy, lantern-shaped flowers that are usually yellow or orange in color. Its leaves are heart-shaped and often covered in fine hairs.

Abutilon montanum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
