Attalea plowmanii

Attalea plowmanii, commonly known as the Brazil nut tree, is a large evergreen tree native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its tall, straight trunk and broad, feather-like leaves. The plant produces large, woody pods that contain the edible Brazil nuts. Its flowers are small and pale yellow, arranged in dense inflorescences.

The tree plays a vital role in the ecosystem, providing habitat and food for various wildlife species.
