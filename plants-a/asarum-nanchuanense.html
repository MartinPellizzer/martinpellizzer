Asarum nanchuanense

Asarum nanchuanense, commonly known as Nanchuan wild ginger, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Aristolochiaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped, aromatic leaves. The plant produces a unique, tubular flower that is often covered in a dense, hairy coating. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is typically found in mountainous regions.

Due to its medicinal properties and ornamental value, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists alike.
