Anthurium polynervium

Anthurium polynervium, commonly known as the Polynesian anthurium, is a striking tropical plant native to the Pacific Islands.

It features large, heart-shaped leaves with a glossy, dark green surface and prominent veins, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-shaped inflorescences surrounded by colorful spathe petals, typically in shades of red, orange, or yellow. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect sunlight for optimal growth.

Anthurium polynervium is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and air-purifying qualities.
