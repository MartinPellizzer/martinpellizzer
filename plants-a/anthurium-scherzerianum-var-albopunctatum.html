Anthurium scherzerianum var. albopunctatum

Anthurium scherzerianum var. albopunctatum, commonly known as the White Spotted Anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are often adorned with white or cream-colored spots. The plant produces vibrant red spadices, which are the showy, spadix-like flowers that are actually inflorescences. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

This variety is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
