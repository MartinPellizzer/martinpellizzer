Antirrhinum orontium var. leiocarpum

Antirrhinum orontium var. leiocarpum, commonly known as the smooth-capsule antirrhinum, is a flowering plant belonging to the Plantaginaceae family.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces showy, tubular flowers that are typically pink or red in color. The plant features lance-shaped leaves with a hairy texture, and its flowers are borne in racemes along the stem. The fruit is a capsule that remains closed until maturity, releasing seeds through a small opening.

It is native to regions of Europe and thrives in well-drained soils in full sun.
