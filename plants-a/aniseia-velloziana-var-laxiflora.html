Aniseia velloziana var. laxiflora

Aniseia velloziana var. laxiflora, commonly known as the slender-flowered aniseia, is a species of flowering plant native to the Andes region of South America.

The plant typically grows in high-altitude grasslands and rocky slopes, thriving in cool and temperate climates.
