Ardisia clarkeana

Ardisia clarkeana, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen or red-rooted ardisia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters, featuring glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, white flowers that are clustered in umbels, followed by bright red berries. Its roots are thick and fleshy, often used in traditional medicine.

Ardisia clarkeana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
