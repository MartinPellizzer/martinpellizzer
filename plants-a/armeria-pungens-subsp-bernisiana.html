Armeria pungens subsp. bernisiana

Armeria pungens subsp.

bernisiana, commonly known as the pink sand verbena, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of southern Africa. It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of small, pink to purple flowers that bloom in spring.

The plant thrives in sandy, well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy choice for coastal gardens. Its compact growth habit and ornamental flowers make it a popular rock garden and coastal landscape plant.

It is also valued for its ability to withstand salt spray and moderate drought conditions.
