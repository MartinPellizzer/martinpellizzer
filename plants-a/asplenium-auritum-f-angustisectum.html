Asplenium auritum f. angustisectum

Asplenium auritum f. angustisectum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf maidenhair fern, is a fern species characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped fronds.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is native to tropical and subtropical regions. The fronds are typically dark green and have a finely divided, feather-like structure. It prefers moist, shaded environments and is often found in rocky or woodland areas.

This fern is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
