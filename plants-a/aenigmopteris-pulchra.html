Aenigmopteris pulchra

Aenigmopteris pulchra, commonly known as the beautiful enigma fern, is a unique species of fern native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

The plant belongs to the family Athyriaceae and is known for its distinctive, often curling leaflets that give it an enigmatic appearance. Its reproductive structures, including spore-bearing sporangia, are typically found on the underside of its fronds.
