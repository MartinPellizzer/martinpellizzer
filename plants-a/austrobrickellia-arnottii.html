Austrobrickellia arnottii

Austrobrickellia arnottii, commonly known as the coastal brickellia, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of southeastern Australia.

It is a small shrub that typically grows in sandy or rocky soils along the shorelines. The plant produces showy, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in spring, attracting various pollinators such as bees and birds. Austrobrickellia arnottii is adapted to withstand salt spray and strong coastal winds, making it a resilient species in its native environment.
