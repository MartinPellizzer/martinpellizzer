Aristida sorzogonensis

Aristida sorzogonensis, commonly known as the Philippine dropseed, is a grass species native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its slender, erect growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny, wind-dispersed seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed areas.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological role, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
