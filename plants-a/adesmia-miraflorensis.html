Adesmia miraflorensis

Adesmia miraflorensis, commonly known as the Miraflores legume, is a flowering plant species native to the Andean regions of South America.

Adesmia miraflorensis is adapted to grow in rocky and arid environments, making it a resilient species in its native habitat.
