Acacia armata f. hybrida

Acacia armata f. hybrida, commonly known as the hybrid acacia, is a shrub or small tree native to Australia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, flat, feathery leaves that are often used in floral arrangements. The plant produces small, yellow, globular flowers that bloom in spring, attracting various pollinators. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal and Mediterranean climates.

Due to its ornamental value and hardiness, it is a popular choice for landscaping in arid regions.
