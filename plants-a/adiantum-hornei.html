Adiantum hornei

Adiantum hornei, commonly known as the royal fern, is a species of fern native to tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, lace-like fronds that are often covered in fine, silky hairs. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and stream banks. Its leaves are pinnately compound, with each pinna divided into smaller, linear segments.

Adiantum hornei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
