Anthurium poloense

Anthurium poloense, commonly known as the Polynesian anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the Pacific Islands.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are often brightly colored, ranging from deep green to variegated patterns. The plant produces vibrant spadices, which are modified leaves that enclose the true flowers. It thrives in humid, warm environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium poloense is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in botanical gardens.
