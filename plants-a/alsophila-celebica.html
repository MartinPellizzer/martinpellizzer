Alsophila celebica

Alsophila celebica, commonly known as the Celebes tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to the Indonesian archipelago.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate fronds. The plant has a robust rhizome system that allows it to thrive in humid, tropical environments. Its fronds can grow up to several meters in length, making it a striking feature in its natural habitat.

Alsophila celebica is often found in lowland forests and is valued for its ornamental and ecological significance.
