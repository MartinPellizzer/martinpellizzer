Albizia Clypearia

Albizia clypearia is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia and Africa. It is known for its distinctive yellow or white flowers that bloom in clusters. The tree has a slender trunk and drooping branches that give it a unique appearance. It is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits. This plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in various soil conditions.
