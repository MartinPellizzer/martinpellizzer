Artemisia forrestii

Artemisia forrestii, commonly known as Chinese wormwood, is a perennial shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, silver-gray foliage. The plant produces small, yellowish flower heads that are typically clustered in dense, spherical shapes. Artemisia forrestii thrives in dry, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

It is valued for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional Chinese medicine.
