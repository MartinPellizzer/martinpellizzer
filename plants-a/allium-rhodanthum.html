Allium rhodanthum

Allium rhodanthum, commonly known as the purple globemallow, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its showy, violet-purple flowers that bloom in late summer. Allium rhodanthum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
