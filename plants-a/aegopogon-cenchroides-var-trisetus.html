Aegopogon cenchroides var. trisetus

Aegopogon cenchroides var. trisetus, commonly known as the hairy goat's beard, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features a basal rosette of narrow, hairy leaves and produces upright flowering stems with clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers. The plant is characterized by its distinctive bracts and is often found in rocky or sandy soils. It is a member of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and gardeners alike.
