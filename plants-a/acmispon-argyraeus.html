Acmispon argyraeus

Acmispon argyraeus, commonly known as silverleaf blueweed, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a low-growing annual herb that typically reaches heights of 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant produces small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in clusters, adding a delicate aesthetic to its appearance. Its leaves are silver-gray in color, covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss in arid environments.

The species is often found in sandy or gravelly soils, thriving in full sun and drought conditions.
