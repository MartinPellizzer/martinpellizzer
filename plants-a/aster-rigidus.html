Aster rigidus

Aster rigidus, commonly known as stiff aster, is a perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its rigid, upright stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, blue to purple flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in meadows, grasslands, and rocky areas.

Aster rigidus is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
