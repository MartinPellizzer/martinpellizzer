Agrostis Angrenica

Angren grass, scientifically known as Agrostis angrenica, is a rare and unique grass species native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. This plant is characterized by its slender stalks and fine, delicate leaves that thrive in cool, high-altitude environments. Despite its modest appearance, Angren grass plays a vital role in stabilizing soil and supporting local ecosystems. Its discovery and study have contributed to our understanding of plant adaptation to extreme climates. Due to habitat loss and climate change, conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this distinctive species.
