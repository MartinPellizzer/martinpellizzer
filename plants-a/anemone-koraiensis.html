Anemone koraiensis

Anemone koraiensis, commonly known as the Korean anemone, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Korea.

It features delicate, white or pale pink flowers with a single layer of petals, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has a short, hairy stem that supports its clustered foliage of heart-shaped, palmate leaves. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in woodland or shaded areas.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in temperate climates.
