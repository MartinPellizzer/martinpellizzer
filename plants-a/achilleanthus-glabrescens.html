Achilleanthus glabrescens

Achilleanthus glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth achilleanthus, is a flowering plant native to the Andean region of South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry habitats, showcasing its adaptability to harsh environments. Its leaves are narrow and hairy, while its flowers are yellow with a distinctive central disk.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local pollinators.
