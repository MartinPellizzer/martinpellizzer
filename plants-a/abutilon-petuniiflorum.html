Abutilon petuniiflorum

Abutilon petuniiflorum, commonly known as the velvetleaf abutilon, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and showy, spade-shaped flowers that come in a variety of colors, including pink, red, and white. The plant is often cultivated as an ornamental due to its attractive foliage and blooms. It prefers well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, thriving in warm climates.

Its fibrous roots and hairy leaves contribute to its unique botanical appearance.
