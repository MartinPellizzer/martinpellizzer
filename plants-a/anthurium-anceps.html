Anthurium anceps

Anthurium anceps, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a tropical flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically deep green with a reddish hue on the underside. The plant produces bright red spadices, which are the modified leaves that enclose the tiny, yellowish flowers. Anthurium anceps thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect light to flourish.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and as a decorative plant in tropical landscapes.
