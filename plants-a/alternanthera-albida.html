Alternanthera albida

Alternanthera albida, commonly known as white Alternanthera, is a striking aquatic plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its bright white to pale pink foliage, which becomes more pronounced under bright light conditions. This species thrives in shallow, slow-moving waters and prefers warm temperatures. It is often cultivated in ponds and water gardens for its ornamental value.

The plant is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice among aquatic plant enthusiasts.
