Acrostichum simplex var. martinicense

Acrostichum simplex var. martinicense, commonly known as the Martinique fern, is a variant of the common brake fern.

It is a tropical plant native to the Caribbean, particularly found in the island of Martinique. This fern has feathery, compound leaves that are typically green and slightly glossy. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in humid forests.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in local ecosystems.
