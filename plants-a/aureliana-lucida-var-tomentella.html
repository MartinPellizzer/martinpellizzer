Aureliana lucida var. tomentella

Aureliana lucida var. tomentella, commonly known as the silver-leaved aureliana, is a distinctive plant characterized by its silvery, hairy foliage.

Its leaves are typically broad and ovate, covered in a dense layer of fine, white hairs that give it a frosted appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually a pale yellow or white in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in temperate forest regions.

Due to its unique appearance, it is a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
