Anacamptis × genevensis

Anacamptis × genevensis, commonly known as the Geneva orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to Europe.

It belongs to the genus Anacamptis, which includes several species of terrestrial orchids. The plant typically grows in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in meadows and woodland edges. It produces tall, erect stems with showy, fragrant flowers in shades of purple and white.

The hybrid nature of this plant contributes to its adaptability and ornamental value in horticulture.
