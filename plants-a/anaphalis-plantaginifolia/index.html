Anaphalis Plantaginifolia

Anaphalis plantaginifolia is a delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of central and southern China. It is known for its soft, woolly leaves and clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers that resemble miniature daisies. This species thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine meadows and rocky slopes. Its unique appearance and ecological importance make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively obscure and is threatened by habitat loss and climate change.
