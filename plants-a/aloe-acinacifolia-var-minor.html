Aloe acinacifolia var. minor

Aloe acinacifolia var. minor, commonly known as the narrow-leaf aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features slender, upright stems that produce thick, fleshy leaves with a bluish-green hue. The leaves are typically narrow and sword-shaped, giving the plant its common name. It produces small, yellow flowers during the summer months, which are clustered in inflorescences.

This plant is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
