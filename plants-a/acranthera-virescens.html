Acranthera virescens

Acranthera virescens, commonly known as the green-flowered acranthera, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in dense, spike-like clusters. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and rarity, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
