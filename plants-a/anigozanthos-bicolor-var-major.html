Anigozanthos bicolor var. major

Anigozanthos bicolor var. major, commonly known as the Christmas bush, is a striking perennial plant native to Western Australia.

It is characterized by its vibrant red and yellow flowers that bloom during the winter months, typically from June to August. The plant features dense, evergreen foliage composed of narrow, leathery leaves that provide a lush backdrop to its colorful blooms. It is a member of the Myrtaceae family and is well adapted to the arid and coastal environments of its native habitat.
