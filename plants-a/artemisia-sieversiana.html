Artemisia sieversiana

Artemisia sieversiana, commonly known as silver wormwood, is a perennial shrub native to temperate regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which consists of finely divided, aromatic leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or dry habitats.

Artemisia sieversiana is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
