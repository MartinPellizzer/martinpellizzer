Albizia retusa subsp. retusa

Albizia retusa subsp.

retusa, commonly known as the silky albizia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia. It is characterized by its compound leaves, which are pinnate and covered with fine, silky hairs.

The plant produces small, fragrant yellow flowers arranged in racemose inflorescences, typically blooming in summer. Its seeds are enclosed in flat, pod-like fruits that split open when mature. Albizia retusa subsp.

retusa is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.
