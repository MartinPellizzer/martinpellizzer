Aspidium reinwardtianum

Aspidium reinwardtianum, commonly known as the reinwardt's water fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, trifoliate fronds that are typically light green and have a feathery appearance. This plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its rhizomes are fibrous and help it anchor to substrates such as soil or rocks.

The spores are produced on the underside of the fronds, contributing to its reproductive strategy in humid climates.
