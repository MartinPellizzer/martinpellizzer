Aristida affinis

Aristida affinis, commonly known as slender false lovegrass, is a perennial grass native to North America.

It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. The plant has slender, erect stems and small, spike-like inflorescences. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a rough texture.

It is often used in restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil and tolerate drought conditions.
