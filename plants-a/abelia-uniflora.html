Abelia uniflora

Abelia uniflora, commonly known as the single-flower abelia, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern North America.

It features fragrant, tubular white to pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers moist, well-drained soil.

Abelia uniflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
