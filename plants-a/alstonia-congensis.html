Alstonia congensis

Alstonia congensis, commonly known as the African angel's trumpet, is a tropical evergreen tree native to western Africa.

It belongs to the family Apocynaceae and is characterized by its ornamental, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant has smooth, dark green leaves and can grow up to 20 meters tall in its natural habitat. It is often cultivated for its showy blooms and is also used in traditional medicine.

However, it contains toxic alkaloids and should be handled with care.
