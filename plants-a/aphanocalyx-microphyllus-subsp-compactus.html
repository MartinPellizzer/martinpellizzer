Aphanocalyx microphyllus subsp. compactus

Aphanocalyx microphyllus subsp.

compactus, commonly known as the compact umbrella tree, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia. It is characterized by its dense, rounded foliage and small, clustered white flowers that bloom in spring.

The plant has glossy, dark green leaves that are typically less than 5 cm in length. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated as a decorative indoor plant.

Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for small spaces and container gardening.
