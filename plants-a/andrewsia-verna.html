Andrewsia verna

Andrewsia verna, commonly known as the spring beauty, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It typically grows in moist, rocky habitats and is often found in woodlands and along stream banks. The plant produces small, showy flowers that are usually pink or white, blooming in early spring. Its leaves are lance-shaped and appear before the flowers, contributing to its ornamental value.

Andrewsia verna is valued for its ecological role in supporting pollinators and its aesthetic appeal in natural landscapes.
