Artemisiastrum palmeri

Artemisiastrum palmeri, commonly known as Palmer's sagebrush, is a shrub native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its aromatic, gray-green foliage and small, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, often found in sagebrush steppe ecosystems. Its leaves contain essential oils that contribute to its strong scent and medicinal properties.

Artemisiastrum palmeri plays a vital role in providing habitat and forage for various wildlife species.
