Anisantha rubens var. glabriglumis

Anisantha rubens var. glabriglumis, commonly known as the glabrous-winged grass, is a perennial grass species native to North America.

It typically grows in moist to wet habitats such as marshes, swales, and ditches. The plant features slender, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. Its flowers are usually greenish or pale yellow, and the plant is characterized by its smooth, glabrous surfaces.

It plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various invertebrates.
