Arum orientale var. gratum

Arum orientale var. gratum, commonly known as the Chinese arrowroot or the arrowhead plant, is a member of the Araceae family.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are often a deep green with a glossy texture. The plant produces a spadix, a dense cluster of tiny flowers surrounded by a modified leaf called a spathe. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

This variety is known for its ability to tolerate a range of soil types and is relatively easy to care for in a home setting.
