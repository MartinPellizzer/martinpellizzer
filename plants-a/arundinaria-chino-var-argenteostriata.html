Arundinaria chino var. argenteostriata

Arundinaria chino var. argenteostriata, commonly known as the silver-striped bamboo, is a variety of the Chinese bamboo species.

It is characterized by its gracefully arching culms that display a striking silver stripe along the nodes. The plant exhibits a clumping growth habit, making it suitable for small garden spaces. Its foliage is typically a lush green, with the silver stripe adding a subtle metallic sheen.

This ornamental bamboo is valued for its aesthetic appeal and adaptability to various soil conditions.
