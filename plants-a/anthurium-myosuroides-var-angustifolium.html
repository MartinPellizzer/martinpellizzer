Anthurium myosuroides var. angustifolium

Anthurium myosuroides var. angustifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaf anthurium, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces vibrant red spathes that enclose its small, spadix-like inflorescence. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

This variety is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and ease of care.
