Aerangis rostellaris

Aerangis rostellaris, commonly known as the star-shaped orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, star-like flowers that are typically white or pale pink with a yellowish center. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, forming compact clumps with several pseudobulbs. Its leaves are strap-shaped and arranged in a fan-like pattern, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Aerangis rostellaris thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and well-draining soil.
