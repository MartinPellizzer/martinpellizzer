Anthericum pugioniforme

Anthericum pugioniforme, commonly known as the sword fern, is a species of fern native to parts of Asia and Australia.

It is characterized by its long, narrow, and sword-shaped fronds that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forests or along stream banks. Its rhizomes are fibrous and help anchor it to the substrate while absorbing nutrients.

Anthericum pugioniforme is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
