Ardisia gracilenta

Ardisia gracilenta, commonly known as the slender beautyberry, is a deciduous shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, dark green leaves and produces small, clustered white flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant is notable for its bright red, berry-like fruits that appear in the fall, attracting birds and other wildlife. It prefers partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

Ardisia gracilenta is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
