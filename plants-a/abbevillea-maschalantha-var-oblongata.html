Abbevillea maschalantha var. oblongata

Abbevillea maschalantha var. oblongata, commonly known as the oblongleaf abbevillea, is a flowering plant native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy inflorescences that consist of numerous small, fragrant flowers clustered in panicles. The leaves are typically oblong in shape, with a glossy surface and a prominent midrib. This species thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
