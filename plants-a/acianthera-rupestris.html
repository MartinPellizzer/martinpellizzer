Acianthera rupestris

Acianthera rupestris, commonly known as the rock orchid, is a species of orchid native to southern Africa.

The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, typically during the summer months. Acianthera rupestris is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens with well-draining soil and bright, indirect light.
