Aster flexicaulis

Aster flexicaulis, commonly known as the slender aster, is a herbaceous perennial native to the United States.

It features upright stems with alternate, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces daisy-like flowers with blue to purple petals and yellow centers, blooming from late summer to early fall. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soils.

Aster flexicaulis is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
