Aplotaxis denticulata var. glabrata

Aplotaxis denticulata var. glabrata, commonly known as the smooth African butterbean, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant produces pods containing edible seeds that are used in local cuisines. It thrives in dry, well-drained soils and is adapted to arid environments.

Its botanical features make it an important species for both ecological and agricultural purposes in its native range.
