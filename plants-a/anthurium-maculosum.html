Anthurium maculosum

Anthurium maculosum, commonly known as the spotted anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are often adorned with distinctive red or brown spots. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-shaped inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, typically in shades of red or orange. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium maculosum is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
