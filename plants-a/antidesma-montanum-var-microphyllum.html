Antidesma montanum var. microphyllum

Antidesma montanum var. microphyllum, commonly known as the small-leaved antidesma, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. The plant features small, elliptical leaves that are usually glossy and dark green. Its flowers are small and yellowish, arranged in clusters, and it produces small, spherical berries.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and has some traditional uses in local medicine.
