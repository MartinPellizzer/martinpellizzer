Arenaria tchichatcheffii

Arenaria tchichatcheffii, commonly known as the Tchichatcheff's sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It thrives in rocky and arid environments, often found in alpine and subalpine zones. The plant has small, fleshy leaves that help it retain moisture in its dry habitat. Its flowers are typically white or pale pink, appearing in clusters during the summer months.

It plays a role in stabilizing soil in its native environment and is of interest to botanists studying alpine flora.
