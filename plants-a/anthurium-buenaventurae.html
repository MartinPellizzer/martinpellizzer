Anthurium buenaventurae

Anthurium buenaventurae, commonly known as the Buenaventura arrowroot, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central America.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive spathe and spadix inflorescence. The plant features broad, heart-shaped leaves with a deep green color and a velvety texture. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Anthurium buenaventurae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated as a houseplant.
