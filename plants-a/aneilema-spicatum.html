Aneilema spicatum

Aneilema spicatum, commonly known as the spike lily, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features striking, spike-like inflorescences that bear small, tubular flowers in shades of white, yellow, and red. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, with lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments.

Aneilema spicatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
