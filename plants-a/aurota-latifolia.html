Aurota latifolia

Aurota latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf aurota, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the temperate regions of the Americas.

It features lance-shaped leaves with a waxy texture, often displaying a bluish-green hue. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of yellow and green, which bloom in early summer. It prefers well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Aurota latifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in a variety of garden settings.
