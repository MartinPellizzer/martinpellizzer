Adenoncos suborbicularis

Adenoncos suborbicularis, commonly known as the orbicular adenoncos, is a flowering plant native to specific regions of South America.

It is characterized by its orbicular, or nearly round, leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine hairs. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories.
