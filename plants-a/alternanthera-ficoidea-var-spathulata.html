Alternanthera ficoidea var. spathulata

Alternanthera ficoidea var. spathulata, commonly known as the broad-leaf Alternanthera, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage that ranges from red to pink and purple, depending on light exposure. The leaves are broad and ovate, with a velvety texture that enhances their visual appeal. This plant thrives in warm, humid conditions and requires bright, indirect light to maintain its vibrant colors.

It is often cultivated as a decorative plant in water gardens or as a houseplant.
