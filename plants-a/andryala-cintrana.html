Andryala cintrana

Andryala cintrana, commonly known as the Canary Island rose, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces fragrant, pink or white flowers during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and covered in fine hairs.

Andryala cintrana is often found in coastal areas and rocky soils, thriving in the mild, humid climate of its native habitat.
