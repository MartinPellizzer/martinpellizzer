Asystasia dalzelliana

Asystasia dalzelliana, commonly known as the velvetleaf or jungle geranium, is a tropical flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its attractive, velvety leaves that are typically dark green with a soft texture. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink, often blooming in late spring to early summer. Asystasia dalzelliana is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and ability to thrive in shaded environments.

It is also used in traditional medicine in some regions for its purported health benefits.
