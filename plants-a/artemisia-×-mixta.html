Artemisia × mixta

Artemisia × mixta, commonly known as mugwort or sweet wormwood, is a hybrid shrub in the sunflower family.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and aromatic, often pungent scent. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are typically clustered in panicles. Native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere, it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun.

Artemisia × mixta is often used in traditional medicine and as a companion plant in gardens.
