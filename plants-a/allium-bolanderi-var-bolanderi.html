Allium bolanderi var. bolanderi

Allium bolanderi var. bolanderi, commonly known as Bolander's garlic, is a perennial herb native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its bulbous structure and grass-like leaves.
