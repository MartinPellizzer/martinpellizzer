Austrocylindropuntia schickendantzii

Austrocylindropuntia schickendantzii, commonly known as the Schickendantzii barrel cactus, is a species native to the arid regions of northern Argentina.

It is characterized by its cylindrical, ribbed stems that can grow up to 1.5 meters in height. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the spring and summer months, which are typically borne on the upper part of the stem. Its spines are relatively short and dense, providing protection from herbivores.

This cactus is well adapted to harsh desert conditions, storing water in its thick, fleshy stems.
