Annona chrysopetala

Annona chrysopetala, commonly known as the golden petal annona, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is a small to medium-sized tree or shrub that typically grows up to 5 meters in height. The plant features broad, heart-shaped leaves and produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are often clustered in inflorescences. Its fruit is a smooth, yellow or green berry that ripens to a golden color, containing a single large seed.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and has some traditional uses in local medicine.
