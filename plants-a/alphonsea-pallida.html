Alphonsea pallida

Alphonsea pallida, commonly known as the pale alphonsea, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features simple, alternate leaves that are usually ovate to elliptic in shape. Its flowers are small and usually white or pale yellow, arranged in clusters.

The fruit is a small, globose drupe that contains a single seed.
