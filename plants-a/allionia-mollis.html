Allionia mollis

Allionia mollis, commonly known as the golden star, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features attractive, star-shaped yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in a fine, silvery down.

Allionia mollis is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in partial shade.
