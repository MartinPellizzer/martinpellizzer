Artabotrys malayanus

Artabotrys malayanus, commonly known as the Malayan lantern tree, is a tropical flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, lantern-shaped inflorescences that are often brightly colored, typically in shades of red, orange, or yellow. Artabotrys malayanus is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its unique and showy flowers.
