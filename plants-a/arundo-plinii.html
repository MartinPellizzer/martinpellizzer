Arundo plinii

Arundo plinii, commonly known as the narrowleaf giant reed, is a tall, perennial grass native to the Mediterranean region.

It features long, narrow leaves that are often bluish-green in color, and it can grow up to 6 meters in height. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences that are typically greenish or brownish in color. It thrives in wet or moist environments, making it a common sight near water bodies.

Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread rapidly, often forming dense stands.
