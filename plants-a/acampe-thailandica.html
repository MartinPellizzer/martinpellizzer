Acampe thailandica

Acampe thailandica, commonly known as Thai acampe, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia, particularly Thailand.

It belongs to the family Acanthaceae and is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink. Acampe thailandica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
