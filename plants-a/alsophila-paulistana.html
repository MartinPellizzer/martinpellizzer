Alsophila paulistana

Alsophila paulistana, commonly known as the Paulista tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to Brazil.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds. The plant has a distinctive crown of fronds that can reach several meters in height, giving it a tropical appearance. Its rhizomes are thick and fibrous, allowing it to anchor firmly in moist, shaded environments.

Alsophila paulistana thrives in humid climates and is often found in forested areas with high humidity and partial shade.
