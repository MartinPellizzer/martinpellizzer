Apodynomene grandiflora

Apodynomene grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered apodynomene, is a striking tropical plant native to regions of Africa and Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and sometimes red. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and often have a slightly serrated edge.

Apodynomene grandiflora is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in warm climates for its vibrant blooms.
