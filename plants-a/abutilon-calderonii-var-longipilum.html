Abutilon calderonii var. longipilum

Abutilon calderonii var. longipilum, commonly known as the long-hair abutilon, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features soft, fuzzy leaves that are ovate to elliptic in shape. Its flowers are yellow and occur in clusters, adding ornamental value to the plant.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for cultivation in warm climates.
