Anasser

Anasser, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves and showy, tubular flowers. The plant thrives in humid, well-drained soil and requires indirect sunlight to flourish. Anasser is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.

Its flowers come in a variety of colors, including purple, pink, white, and blue.
