Alsinanthus montanus

Alsinanthus montanus, commonly known as the mountain alsine, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a member of the Amaranthaceae family and typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It is often found in alpine and subalpine regions, thriving in cool, moist environments.
