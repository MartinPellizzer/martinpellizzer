Acrostichum lastii

Acrostichum lastii, commonly known as the New Zealand tree fern, is a member of the Cyatheaceae family.

It is native to New Zealand and is characterized by its large, feather-like fronds that can grow up to 3 meters in length. The plant has a tall, unbranched trunk with a fibrous, spongy cortex. Its reproductive structures include both strobili and spores, typical of ferns.

Acrostichum lastii thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in native forests.
