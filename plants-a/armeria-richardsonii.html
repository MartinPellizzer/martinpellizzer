Armeria richardsonii

Armeria richardsonii, commonly known as the Sierra sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the western United States.

It typically thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in alpine and subalpine environments. The plant features dense clusters of small, white or pinkish flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white, waxy bloom.

Armeria richardsonii is well adapted to harsh mountain conditions, making it a resilient and hardy species.
