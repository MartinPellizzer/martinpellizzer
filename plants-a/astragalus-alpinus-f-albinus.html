Astragalus alpinus f. albinus

Astragalus alpinus f. albinus, commonly known as the alpine milkvetch, is a rare and striking plant characterized by its white or pale yellow flowers.

It is a member of the legume family, Fabaceae, and is native to high-altitude regions of the Alps and other mountainous areas. The plant features small, compound leaves and produces pods that contain small, round seeds.
