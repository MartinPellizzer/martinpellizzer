Aneilema paludosum

Aneilema paludosum, commonly known as the swamp hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves and showy, pink or red flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant thrives in moist, swampy environments and is often found near water bodies. It belongs to the Malvaceae family, which includes hibiscus and cotton.
