Aspidistra obliqua

Aspidistra obliqua, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen, is a perennial evergreen plant native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the family Araceae and is characterized by its dark green, lance-shaped leaves that are often marbled with silver or white veins. It is known for its adaptability to low light conditions and is popular as a houseplant due to its ease of care.

Aspidistra obliqua is also valued for its ability to thrive in a variety of indoor environments with minimal maintenance.
