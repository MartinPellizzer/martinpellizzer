Amaryllis × wellsiana

Amaryllis × wellsiana, commonly known as the hybrid amaryllis, is a striking bulbous plant native to South America.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, which bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant produces large, strap-like leaves that are typically a deep green color. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.

Amaryllis × wellsiana requires well-draining soil and bright, indirect light to thrive.
