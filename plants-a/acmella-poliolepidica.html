Acmella poliolepidica

Acmella poliolepidica, commonly known as the narrow-leafed pattypan, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is often found in moist, sunny habitats. It is sometimes cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its delicate appearance.
