Acantholimon spinicalyx

Acantholimon spinicalyx, commonly known as the spinyleaf flax, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features narrow, linear leaves that are covered in fine, bristly hairs, giving them a rough texture. The plant produces small, blue to purple flowers arranged in dense, spike-like clusters during the spring and early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Acantholimon spinicalyx is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
