Anotis urophylla

Anotis urophylla, commonly known as the white-flowered anotis, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in compact clusters. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and has a low, spreading habit with hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves. Anotis urophylla is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
