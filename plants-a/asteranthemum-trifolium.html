Asteranthemum trifolium

Asteranthemum trifolium, commonly known as the three-leaf asteranthemum, is a flowering plant characterized by its trifoliate leaves and showy, daisy-like flowers.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes many familiar garden plants such as daisies and sunflowers. The plant typically grows in well-drained soils and thrives in full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders. Its foliage is often aromatic, and the flowers bloom in a range of colors, from white to pink and purple.

The plant is known for its resilience and ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
