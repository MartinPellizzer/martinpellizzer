Anthurium ornatum

Anthurium ornatum, commonly known as the red-veined anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves with deep red veins against a dark green background. The plant produces small, spadix-like flowers enclosed within a colorful spathe, typically in shades of white, pink, or red. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium ornatum is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
