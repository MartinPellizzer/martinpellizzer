Ajax

Ajax, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies, sunflowers, and chrysanthemums. The plant features showy, daisy-like flowers with yellow centers and white or pink petals. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

The botanical name of Ajax is Gorteria diffusa, and it is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
