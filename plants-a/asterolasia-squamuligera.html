Asterolasia squamuligera

Asterolasia squamuligera, commonly known as the woolly asterolasia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub characterized by its small, narrow leaves that are covered in a dense layer of fine, white hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers that are typically pale blue to purple in color. It thrives in dry, rocky habitats and is often found in fynbos vegetation.

Asterolasia squamuligera is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
