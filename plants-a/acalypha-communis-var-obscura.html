Acalypha communis var. obscura

Acalypha communis var. obscura, commonly known as the dark-leaved spurge, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features dark green, ovate leaves with a velvety texture and prominent veins. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which are often overlooked due to their modest appearance. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded areas.

Its foliage is valued in ornamental horticulture for its striking coloration and texture.
