Anubias gracilis

Anubias gracilis, commonly known as slender anubias, is a hardy aquatic plant native to Africa.

It features narrow, elongated leaves that are typically dark green and slightly glossy. The plant grows in dense clusters, often forming thick mats in still or slow-moving water. It is well adapted to low-light conditions and can thrive in a variety of water temperatures.

Anubias gracilis is popular in aquariums for its aesthetic appeal and ability to provide shelter for aquatic life.
