Artemisia discolor

Artemisia discolor, commonly known as Chinese wormwood, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its gray-green, aromatic foliage and small yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant is part of the sunflower family (Asteraceae) and is often found in dry, rocky habitats. Its leaves contain volatile oils, which contribute to its strong, distinctive scent.

Artemisia discolor is sometimes used in traditional medicine and as a ornamental plant.
