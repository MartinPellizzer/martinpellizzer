Adenocarpus foliolosus var. villosus

Adenocarpus foliolosus var. villosus, commonly known as the hairy leaf sicklepod, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa and the Middle East.

It is characterized by its hairy, ovate leaves and small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces distinctive, curved seed pods that mature into dry, dehiscent fruits. It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid environments.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and taxonomic studies.
