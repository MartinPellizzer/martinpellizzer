Albizia minahassae var. umbellata

Albizia minahassae var. umbellata, commonly known as the Java Albizia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to Indonesia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant features fragrant, pale yellow or white flowers arranged in umbellate clusters, which attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in coastal areas or disturbed soils.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in warm regions.
