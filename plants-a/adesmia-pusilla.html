Adesmia pusilla

Adesmia pusilla, commonly known as the small adesmia, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is a small shrub or perennial herb native to South America, particularly found in regions of Argentina and Bolivia. The plant features trifoliate leaves and produces small, pea-like flowers that are typically pale yellow or greenish in color. It has small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds.

Adesmia pusilla is adapted to dry and semi-arid environments, showcasing its resilience to harsh conditions.
