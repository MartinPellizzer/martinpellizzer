Anthurium mourae

Anthurium mourae, commonly known as the Moura's arrowhead, is a striking tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its unique inflorescence consisting of a spadix and a spathe. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy dark green leaves that are often variegated with white or silver markings. Its flowers are typically small and inconspicuous, with the showy spathe serving as the primary ornamental feature.

Anthurium mourae thrives in humid, shaded environments and is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its vibrant foliage.
