Astragalus wulingensis

Astragalus wulingensis, commonly known as the Wuling milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its small, yellowish flowers and compound leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its root system is known for its medicinal properties, often used in traditional Chinese medicine.

Astragalus wulingensis plays a role in soil stabilization and is of interest for ecological restoration projects.
