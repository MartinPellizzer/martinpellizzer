Andromeda japonica var. albomarginata

Andromeda japonica var. albomarginata, commonly known as the white-edged Japanese andromeda, is a flowering shrub native to Japan.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers. The leaves are typically dark green with a distinctive white or pale margin, adding to its ornamental value. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

This plant is often cultivated for its attractive appearance and ability to bloom in early spring.
