Anapausia heudelotii

Anapausia heudelotii, commonly known as the African bellflower, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows in grasslands and rocky areas, preferring well-drained soils. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Anapausia heudelotii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and botanical collections.
