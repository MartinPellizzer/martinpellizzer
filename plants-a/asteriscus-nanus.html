Asteriscus nanus

Asteriscus nanus, commonly known as the dwarf asteriscus, is a small, flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically grows to a height of less than 10 centimeters, featuring compact, upright stems covered in fine, grayish hairs. The plant produces clusters of tiny, star-shaped flowers in shades of white to pale purple, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are narrow, linear, and arranged oppositely along the stems.

Asteriscus nanus is often cultivated as an ornamental plant in rock gardens and containers due to its delicate appearance and low maintenance requirements.
