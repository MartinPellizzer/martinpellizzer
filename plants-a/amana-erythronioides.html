Amana erythronioides

Amana erythronioides, commonly known as the red-flowered amana, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a herbaceous species characterized by its lance-shaped leaves and striking red or pink flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or wetlands. Its flowers are clustered in dense inflorescences and attract various pollinators, including bees and butterflies.

Amana erythronioides is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
