Anacampseros rotundifolia

Anacampseros rotundifolia, commonly known as the round-leaved ice plant, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a succulent species characterized by its fleshy, rounded leaves and vibrant yellow flowers. The plant is adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick, fleshy leaves. It produces small, delicate flowers that bloom in clusters, often in shades of yellow or orange.

This species is popular in rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and ornamental value.
