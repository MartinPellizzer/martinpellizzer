Anthemis arenicola var. tenuisecta

Anthemis arenicola var. tenuisecta, commonly known as the narrow-leaved sandwort, is a flowering plant native to arid and coastal regions of the Mediterranean.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers with prominent ray florets. It is adapted to sandy soils and can tolerate salt spray, making it a common species in coastal dunes.

Its botanical structure reflects its evolutionary adaptation to harsh, dry environments.
