Aphelandra variegata

Aphelandra variegata, commonly known as the variegated flame plant, is a striking tropical plant native to the Caribbean and Central America.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves with striking white and yellow variegation. The plant produces long, pendant-like inflorescences that bear clusters of small, fragrant, white flowers. Aphelandra variegata thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and high humidity to flourish.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical landscapes.
