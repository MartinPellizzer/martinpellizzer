Arctotis nudicaulis

Arctotis nudicaulis, commonly known as the narrow-leaved arctotis, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a herbaceous annual with slender, branching stems and small, daisy-like flowers that range in color from white to yellow. The plant features narrow, linear leaves that are usually hairless and arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and containers.

Its delicate appearance and extended blooming period make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
