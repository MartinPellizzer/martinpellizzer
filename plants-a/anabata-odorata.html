Anabata odorata

Anabata odorata, commonly known as the sweet pepper vine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and heart-shaped, glossy leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. Its fruit is a fleshy, red or yellow berry that contains numerous seeds.

Anabata odorata is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
