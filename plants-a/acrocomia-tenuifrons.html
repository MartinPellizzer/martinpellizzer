Acrocomia tenuifrons

Acrocomia tenuifrons, commonly known as the narrow-leaved palm, is a species of flowering plant in the Arecaceae family.

It is native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America, where it thrives in tropical and subtropical climates. Acrocomia tenuifrons is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional practices in its native regions.
