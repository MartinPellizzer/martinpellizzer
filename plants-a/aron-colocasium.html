Aron colocasium

Aron colocasium, commonly known as the golden star ginger, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features striking, bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, creating a dramatic visual display. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and often have a reddish tint along the edges. It thrives in humid, warm environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Aron colocasium is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
