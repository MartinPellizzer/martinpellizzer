Aeonium canariense var. virgineum

Aeonium canariense var. virgineum, commonly known as the Canary Island Aeonium, is a succulent plant native to the Canary Islands.

It features rosettes of fleshy, blue-green leaves that are densely packed and often covered in a powdery coating. The plant produces striking clusters of yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a favored ornamental plant in arid climates.
