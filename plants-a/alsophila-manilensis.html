Alsophila manilensis

Alsophila manilensis, commonly known as the Manila tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and a crown of feathery fronds that can reach several meters in length. The plant belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is known for its ability to thrive in humid, tropical environments. Its fronds are typically dark green and have a leathery texture, contributing to its ornamental value.
