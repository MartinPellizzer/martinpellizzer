Alsophila loheri

Alsophila loheri, commonly known as the Loher's tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds. The plant has a distinctive crown of pinnate leaves that can reach several meters in length. Its rhizome is thick and fibrous, supporting the upright growth of the trunk.

Alsophila loheri thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.
