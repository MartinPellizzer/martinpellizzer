Aichryson × praegeri

Aichryson × praegeri, commonly known as the hybrid jade plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy stems and leaves that are often variegated with shades of green, yellow, and white. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It is a popular ornamental plant due to its low maintenance and ability to thrive in a variety of indoor conditions.

Its botanical characteristics make it well-suited for xeriscaping and container gardening.
