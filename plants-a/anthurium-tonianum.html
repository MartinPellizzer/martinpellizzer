Anthurium tonianum

Anthurium tonianum, commonly known as the velvet leaf anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are velvety to the touch and exhibit a rich green to dark green coloration. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-shaped inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, often in red, orange, or yellow hues. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity and well-draining soil to flourish.

Anthurium tonianum is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
