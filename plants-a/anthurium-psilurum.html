Anthurium psilurum

Anthurium psilurum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its elongated, narrow leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces striking spadices, which are inflorescences that resemble a cluster of small flowers, often in shades of red or orange. Anthurium psilurum thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.

Its unique foliage and vibrant floral structures make it a popular choice for indoor gardening and ornamental purposes.
