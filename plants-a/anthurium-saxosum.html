Anthurium saxosum

Anthurium saxosum, commonly known as the rock anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces bright red spathes that enclose its spadix, which is covered in tiny, clustered flowers. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Anthurium saxosum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in the right conditions.
