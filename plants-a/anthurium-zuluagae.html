Anthurium zuluagae

Anthurium zuluagae, commonly known as the Zuluaga anthurium, is a striking tropical plant native to Colombia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped spathe that can range in color from deep red to orange, often with a contrasting green spadix. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its foliage consists of large, glossy, dark green leaves that are also ornamental.

Anthurium zuluagae is popular in horticulture for its unique appearance and ability to thrive indoors with proper care.
