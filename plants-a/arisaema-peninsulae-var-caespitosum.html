Arisaema peninsulae var. caespitosum

Arisaema peninsulae var. caespitosum, commonly known as the Peninsular Arisaema, is a flowering plant native to the Korean Peninsula.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its spatheous inflorescence. The plant produces a single, showy spathe that encloses a spadix bearing both male and female flowers. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks.

The leaves are usually heart-shaped and have a rough texture, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
