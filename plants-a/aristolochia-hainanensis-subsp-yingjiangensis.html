Aristolochia hainanensis subsp. yingjiangensis

Aristolochia hainanensis subsp.

yingjiangensis, commonly known as the Yingjiang arrowhead vine, is a flowering plant native to southern China. It belongs to the Aristolochiaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, pipe-shaped flowers that are often pale green or white in color. The plant produces heart-shaped leaves with a rough texture and is typically found in moist, shaded environments.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
