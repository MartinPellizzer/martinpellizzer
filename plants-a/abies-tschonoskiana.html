Abies tschonoskiana

Abies tschonoskiana, commonly known as the Tschonoskian fir, is a coniferous evergreen tree native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, conical shape and dense, dark green foliage. The tree produces cone-like structures that contain winged seeds, typical of the Pinaceae family. Its needles are flat and have a slightly resinous scent, and the bark is smooth with a reddish-brown color.

This species is adapted to high-altitude environments and is often found in alpine forests.
