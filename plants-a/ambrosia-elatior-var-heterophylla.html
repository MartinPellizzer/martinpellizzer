Ambrosia elatior var. heterophylla

Ambrosia elatior var. heterophylla, commonly known as giant ragweed, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and yellow flowers that appear in late summer. The plant is an annual herb that can grow up to 6 feet tall, with a taproot system that allows it to thrive in various soil types. It is known for producing a significant amount of pollen, which can contribute to allergic reactions in humans.

The species is often found in disturbed areas such as roadsides and agricultural fields.
