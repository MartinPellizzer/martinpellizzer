Asplenium obovatum subsp. obovatum

Asplenium obovatum subsp.

obovatum, commonly known as the broad-ovate spleenwort, is a fern native to Europe and parts of Asia. It is a small, evergreen fern that typically grows on rocky surfaces or in shaded, moist environments.

The plant has lanceolate to obovate-shaped leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. Its fronds are usually unbranched and can reach up to 30 centimeters in length.

The spores are produced in clusters on the underside of the fronds, typical of the Asplenium genus.
