Anguria pallida

Anguria pallida, commonly known as the pale cucumber, is a herbaceous plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Cucurbitaceae family, which includes other cucurbits like cucumbers and squash. The plant features slender, trailing stems with heart-shaped, pale green leaves. Its flowers are typically yellow and are followed by small, pale green fruits that resemble cucumbers.

Anguria pallida is often grown for its ornamental value and has mild edible qualities.
