Agapanthus pondoensis

Agapanthus pondoensis, commonly known as the Pondo lily, is a striking perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features large, showy blue or purple flower clusters that emerge on tall, sturdy stems. The plant has strap-like, dark green leaves that are arranged in a rosette at the base. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its ornamental value is complemented by its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and birds.
