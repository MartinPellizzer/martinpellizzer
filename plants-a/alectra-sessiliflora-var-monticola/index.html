Alectra Sessiliflora Var. Monticola

Alectra sessiliflora var. monticola is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of the southwestern United States. It is known for its small, pale yellow blooms that appear in late summer and early fall. This variant of Alectra sessiliflora thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this unique botanical treasure.
