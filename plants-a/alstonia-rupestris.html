Alstonia rupestris

Alstonia rupestris, commonly known as the rock alstonia, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and elongated, lance-shaped leaves that are opposite in arrangement. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers followed by woody, capsule-like fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and parks.
