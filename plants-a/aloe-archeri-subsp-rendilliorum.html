Aloe archeri subsp. rendilliorum

Aloe archeri subsp.

rendilliorum, commonly known as the Red Mountain Aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa. It is characterized by its upright, branching stems and fleshy, dark green leaves with a reddish tinge.

The plant produces yellow flowers in terminal clusters, which bloom during the summer months. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or arid landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for xeriscaping.
