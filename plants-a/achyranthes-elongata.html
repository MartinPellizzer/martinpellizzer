Achyranthes elongata

Achyranthes elongata, commonly known as the slender knotweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features erect, branched stems that are often hairy, with alternate leaves that are ovate to lanceolate in shape. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and riverbanks.

Its botanical characteristics make it a common weed in agricultural and urban environments.
