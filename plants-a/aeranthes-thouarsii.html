Aeranthes thouarsii

Aeranthes thouarsii, commonly known as the Madagascar orchid, is a species native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its unique and striking floral morphology. The plant typically features tall, erect stems with broad, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are usually brightly colored, with a complex structure that includes a prominent lip or labellum.

Aeranthes thouarsii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is appreciated for its rarity and beauty in botanical gardens.
