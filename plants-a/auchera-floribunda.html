Auchera floribunda

Auchera floribunda, commonly known as the silverleaf bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South America.

It features silver-gray foliage that is covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of blue or purple, which bloom in early spring. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and delicate blooms.
