Asplenium Polyodon Var. Bipinnatum

Cinnamon fern, scientifically known as Asplenium polyodon var. bipinnatum, is a striking evergreen fern native to the eastern United States. Its unique appearance, with dark green fronds that resemble cinnamon sticks, gives it its common name. This plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodlands and along stream banks. Despite its delicate look, it is quite hardy and adaptable to various soil types. It is a popular choice for gardeners seeking a low-maintenance yet visually appealing plant.
