Areca churchii

Areca churchii, commonly known as the red areca palm, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like leaves that give it a dramatic appearance. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences that eventually develop into red, fleshy fruits. Areca churchii thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping in tropical and subtropical regions.
