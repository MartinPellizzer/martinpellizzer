Adromischus blosianus

Adromischus blosianus, commonly known as the Blosian's stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a waxy coating to reduce water loss. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or red during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a container plant.

Its low maintenance and ornamental appeal make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
