Aloe × ucriae

Aloe × ucriae, commonly known as the hybrid aloe, is a succulent plant that results from the cross between Aloe vera and Aloe arborescens.

It features rosettes of fleshy, sword-shaped leaves that are typically green with a white powdery coating called farina. The plant produces clusters of yellow flowers during the spring or early summer, adding ornamental value. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires moderate sunlight, making it suitable for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.

Aloe × ucriae is valued for its hardiness and ability to adapt to various environmental conditions.
