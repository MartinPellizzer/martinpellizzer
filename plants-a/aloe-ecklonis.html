Aloe ecklonis

Aloe ecklonis, commonly known as the Cape aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces tall, branched flower stalks that bear yellowish-green flowers. It is adapted to arid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Aloe ecklonis is often used in ornamental gardening due to its striking appearance and resilience.
