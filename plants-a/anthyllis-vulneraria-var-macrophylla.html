Anthyllis vulneraria var. macrophylla

Anthyllis vulneraria var. macrophylla, commonly known as the large-leaved bitter vetch, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in dry, rocky, or gravelly soils. The plant features compound leaves with numerous small leaflets and produces yellow or reddish-purple pea-like flowers. Its seeds are enclosed in pods and are adapted for dispersal by wind or animals.

It is often found in open habitats such as grasslands, wastelands, and along roadsides.
