Anteriorchis fragrans f. apricorum

Anteriorchis fragrans f. apricorum, commonly known as the fragrant apricot variety, is a rare and unique botanical form.

It is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant exhibits a compact growth habit with smooth, dark green leaves. Its fruit is small, yellowish, and has a distinct sweet aroma.

This form is primarily found in specific microclimates with high humidity and well-drained soil.
