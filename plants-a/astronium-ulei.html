Astronium ulei

Astronium ulei, commonly known as the Brazilian star-apple tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to South America.

It belongs to the family Anacardiaceae and is characterized by its smooth, grey bark and large, elliptical leaves. The tree produces clusters of yellowish-green flowers followed by edible, star-shaped fruits that are sweet and juicy.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for both ornamental and agricultural purposes.
