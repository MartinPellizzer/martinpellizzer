Artabotrys insignis var. concolor

Artabotrys insignis var. concolor, commonly known as the white artabotrys, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white, bottle-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a smooth, glossy surface.

The fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release small, black seeds.
