Avena patens

Avena patens, commonly known as slender oat, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a member of the grass family (Poaceae) and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant produces both male and female flowers, with the female flowers typically found at the base of the spike. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows and along riverbanks.

Avena patens is also known for its ability to tolerate a variety of environmental conditions, making it a common weed in agricultural areas.
