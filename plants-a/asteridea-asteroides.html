Asteridea asteroides

Asteridea asteroides, commonly known as the starflower, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features showy, star-shaped blooms that typically range in color from white to purple, depending on the species. Its leaves are usually narrow and lance-shaped, arranged alternately along the stem.

Asteridea asteroides is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
