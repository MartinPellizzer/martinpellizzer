Anaxagorea pachypetala

Anaxagorea pachypetala, commonly known as the thick-leaved evergreen, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, thick, leathery leaves that are typically dark green in color. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its fruits are capsule-like structures that contain multiple seeds.

Anaxagorea pachypetala is often found in lowland rainforests and is valued for its ornamental foliage.
