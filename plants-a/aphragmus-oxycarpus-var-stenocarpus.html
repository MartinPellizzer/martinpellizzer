Aphragmus oxycarpus var. stenocarpus

Aphragmus oxycarpus var. stenocarpus, commonly known as the narrow-carpel aphragmus, is a member of the Malvaceae family.

It is a shrub characterized by its hairy stems and elliptical leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in loose inflorescences. Its fruits are capsule-like structures that split open to release seeds.
