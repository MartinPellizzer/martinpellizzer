Aster prescottii

Aster prescottii, commonly known as the Prescott aster, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and distinctive foliage. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. Its flowers are usually blue to purple in color, with ray florets surrounding a central disk.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance as a habitat for pollinators.
