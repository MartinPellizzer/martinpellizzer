Alsophila fujiiana

Alsophila fujiiana, commonly known as the Fuji Island tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to Japan.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds. The plant has a slow growth rate and can reach heights of up to 10 meters in optimal conditions. Its fronds are typically bright green and have a distinctive, elegant appearance.

Alsophila fujiiana is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its aesthetic value and ability to thrive in shaded, humid environments.
