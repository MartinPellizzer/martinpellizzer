Artanthe melanosticta

Artanthe melanosticta, commonly known as the black-stained artanthe, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the family Amaranthaceae and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, clustered flowers. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow blooms that are typically arranged in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often with a slightly hairy texture.

Artanthe melanosticta is adapted to arid environments and is often found in rocky soils and open habitats.
