Aconitum Cymbulatum Subsp. Fastigiatum

Aconitum cymbulatum subsp. fastigiatum is a striking member of the buttercup family known for its elegant, spire-like flowers. Native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, this plant thrives in cool, moist environments with well-drained soil. Its dark blue to purple petals contrast beautifully with its green, hairy foliage, making it a standout in gardens and wild landscapes. The plant contains potent alkaloids, which make it toxic and require careful handling. Despite its dangers, it is highly valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological significance.
