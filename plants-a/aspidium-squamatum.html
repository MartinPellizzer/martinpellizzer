Aspidium squamatum

Aspidium squamatum, commonly known as the scaly wood fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Athyriaceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive, scaly fronds that give it its common name. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its leaves are usually divided into multiple pairs of pinnae, with a leathery texture.
