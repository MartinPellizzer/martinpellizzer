Ammolirion comosum

Ammolirion comosum, commonly known as the woolly ammolirion, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that covers its stems and leaves, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
