Alchemilla commutata f. robusta

Alchemilla commutata f. robusta, commonly known as the robust lady's mantle, is a perennial plant belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that resembles a mantle, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and ecological restoration projects.
