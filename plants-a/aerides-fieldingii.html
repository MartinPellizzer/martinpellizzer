Aerides fieldingii

Aerides fieldingii, commonly known as the Fielding's orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features showy, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink with a distinctive yellow lip. The plant has strap-like leaves that are dark green and arranged in a rosette pattern. It blooms during the rainy season and requires high humidity and bright, indirect light to thrive.

Aerides fieldingii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
