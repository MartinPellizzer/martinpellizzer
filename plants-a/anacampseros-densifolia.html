Anacampseros densifolia

Anacampseros densifolia, commonly known as the dense-leaved stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features compact, rosette-shaped growth with thick, fleshy leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid conditions. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It prefers well-draining soil and full sun exposure, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
