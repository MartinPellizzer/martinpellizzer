Alyssum pichleri subsp. stojanovii

Alyssum pichleri subsp.

stojanovii, commonly known as the Stojanovii alissum, is a flowering plant native to the Balkan Peninsula. It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, characterized by its small, fragrant flowers and capsule fruits.
