Arctotis fastuosa var. spinulosa

Arctotis fastuosa var. spinulosa, commonly known as the spiked arctotis, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, spiny leaves and produces vibrant, daisy-like flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value and drought tolerance contribute to its appeal in horticultural settings.
