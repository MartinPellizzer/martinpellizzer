Agrostis syreistschikowii

Agrostis syreistschikowii, commonly known as the Siberian bentgrass, is a perennial grass species native to cold climates of Asia.

It typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in alpine or subarctic regions. The plant has fine, narrow leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It is adapted to withstand harsh environmental conditions, including low temperatures and limited sunlight.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable species for ecological studies and potential use in restoration projects.
