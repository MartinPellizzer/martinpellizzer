Anil disticha

Anil disticha, commonly known as the two-branched morning glory, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers that bloom in the early morning. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and a climbing habit, often seen in gardens and natural habitats. Its flowers are typically two-branched, giving it its scientific name.

Anil disticha is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
