Argyrolobium armindae

Argyrolobium armindae, commonly known as the Armindae's silver globular pea, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its legume-like pods and nitrogen-fixing abilities. The plant features small, white to pale pink flowers and is adapted to arid environments. It has a compact, globular growth habit with silvery foliage, giving it its common name.
