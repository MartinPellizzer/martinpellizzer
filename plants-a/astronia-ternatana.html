Astronia ternatana

Astronia ternatana, commonly known as the Ternate starling tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree that typically grows up to 10 meters in height. The plant has simple, opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. Its flowers are small and clustered in inflorescences, often with a yellowish or greenish color.

The fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release its seeds.
