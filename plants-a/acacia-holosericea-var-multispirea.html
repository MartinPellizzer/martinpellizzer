Acacia holosericea var. multispirea

Acacia holosericea var. multispirea, commonly known as the spiral acacia, is a shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive spirally arranged phyllodes, which are narrow, linear, and often have a slight zigzag appearance. The plant produces yellow flowers in compact globular inflorescences, typically during the warmer months. Its leaves are modified into phyllodes, reducing water loss in arid conditions.

It is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to withstand drought and high temperatures.
