Asteriscus maritimus proles littoralis

Asteriscus maritimus proles littoralis, commonly known as the coastal asteriscus, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions.

It is characterized by its small, star-shaped flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is adapted to salty, windy environments. It has small, narrow leaves that help reduce water loss in arid conditions.

This species plays an important role in stabilizing coastal dunes and supporting local wildlife.
