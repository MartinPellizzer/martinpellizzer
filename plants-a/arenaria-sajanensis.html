Arenaria sajanensis

Arenaria sajanensis, commonly known as the Sajan sandwort, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit. The plant produces small, white or pinkish flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are typically narrow and covered in fine hairs, adapted to the harsh, alpine environment.

Arenaria sajanensis thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.
