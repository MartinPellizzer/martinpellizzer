Achyranthes atra

Achyranthes atra, commonly known as black nightshade, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Amaranthaceae and is characterized by its erect, branched stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its fruits are small, globose berries that turn from green to black as they mature.

Achyranthes atra is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
