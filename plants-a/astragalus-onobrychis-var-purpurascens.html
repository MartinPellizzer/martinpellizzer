Astragalus onobrychis var. purpurascens

Astragalus onobrychis var. purpurascens, commonly known as the purple-hairy milkvetch, is a perennial legume native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and trifoliate leaves with hairy surfaces. The plant produces small, yellow flowers clustered in racemes, which bloom in early spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or dry habitats.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in supporting pollinators.
