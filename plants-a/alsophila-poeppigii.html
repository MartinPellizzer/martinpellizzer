Alsophila poeppigii

Alsophila poeppigii, commonly known as the Poeppig's tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and a crown of large, feather-like fronds. The plant has a robust root system that helps it thrive in moist, shaded environments. Its fronds are typically dark green and can grow quite long, contributing to its ornamental value.

Alsophila poeppigii plays an important role in tropical ecosystems, providing habitat and shelter for various organisms.
