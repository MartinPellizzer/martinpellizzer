Aristolochia austroszechuanica

Aristolochia austroszechuanica, commonly known as the Yunnan Dutchman's pipe, is a climbing vine native to China.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive, intricate venation pattern. The plant produces unique, tubular flowers that are often purple or greenish in color. Its flowers have a strong, aromatic scent that attracts specific pollinators.

The genus Aristolochia is known for its complex chemical composition, including various alkaloids.
