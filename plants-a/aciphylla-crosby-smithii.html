Aciphylla crosby-smithii

Aciphylla crosby-smithii, commonly known as the Crosby-Smith's silvergrass, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the grass family (Poaceae) and is characterized by its dense, silver-gray foliage that reflects sunlight. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences bearing numerous tiny white flowers. It thrives in dry, sunny environments and is often found in coastal and alpine regions.
