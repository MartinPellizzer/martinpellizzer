Amaryllis Gayana

Amaryllis gayana is a rare and striking plant native to the humid forests of South America. It is known for its large, showy flowers that bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow. This species belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family, which includes many popular ornamental plants. Its unique appearance and limited distribution make it a subject of interest for botanists and horticulturists. Due to habitat loss, Amaryllis gayana faces threats to its survival in the wild.
