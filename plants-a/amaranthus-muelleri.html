Amaranthus muelleri

Amaranthus muelleri, commonly known as Mueller's amaranth, is a species of flowering plant in the amaranth family.

It is native to Australia and is characterized by its upright growth habit and vibrant red or purple foliage. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically green or reddish in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.

Mueller's amaranth is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a garden plant.
