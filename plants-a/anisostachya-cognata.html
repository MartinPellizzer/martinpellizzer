Anisostachya cognata

Anisostachya cognata, commonly known as the Natal grass, is a tall, perennial grass native to southern Africa.

It features slender, erect stems that can grow up to 2 to 3 meters in height. The plant has long, narrow leaves with a distinctive blue-green color and a hairy texture. Its inflorescence consists of a dense panicle with small, spikelet-like flowers.

This grass is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
