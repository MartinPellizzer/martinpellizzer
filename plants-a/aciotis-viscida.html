Aciotis viscida

Aciotis viscida, commonly known as sticky aciotis, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Its small yellow flowers bloom in clusters, attracting various pollinators like bees and butterflies.
