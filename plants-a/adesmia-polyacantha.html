Adesmia polyacantha

Adesmia polyacantha, commonly known as the hairy legume, is a shrub native to South America.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant features dense, thorny stems and small, often hairy leaves that help reduce water loss. Its flowers are typically clustered in racemes and range in color from white to pale pink.

Adesmia polyacantha is adapted to arid environments, making it a resilient plant in dry regions.
