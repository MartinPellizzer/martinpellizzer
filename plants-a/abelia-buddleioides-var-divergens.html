Abelia buddleioides var. divergens

Abelia buddleioides var. divergens, commonly known as the Chinese abelia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features fragrant, tubular flowers that bloom in clusters, typically in shades of pink and white. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges, and it produces small, red berries in the fall. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and is relatively drought-tolerant once established.

Its ornamental value comes from its showy flowers and attractive foliage.
