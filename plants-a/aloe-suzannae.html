Aloe suzannae

Aloe suzannae, commonly known as the Suza aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, upright stems covered in sharp, spiny leaves that are typically green with a reddish tinge. The plant produces yellow-orange flowers during the summer months, which are clustered in racemes. It is adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Aloe suzannae is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional medicinal uses.
