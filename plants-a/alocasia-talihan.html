Alocasia talihan

Alocasia talihan, commonly known as the Talihan arrowleaf, is a striking tropical plant native to the Philippines.

It features large, heart-shaped leaves that are often adorned with intricate patterns and a deep green color. The plant belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its unique inflorescence composed of a spadix and spathe. Alocasia talihan thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
