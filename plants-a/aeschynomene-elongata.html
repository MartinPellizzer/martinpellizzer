Aeschynomene elongata

Aeschynomene elongata, commonly known as slender goffer, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed areas. Aeschynomene elongata has small, opposite leaves that are usually lance-shaped.

It is sometimes used in traditional medicine and as a forage plant for livestock.
