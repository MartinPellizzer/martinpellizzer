Arctotis leptorhiza var. breviscapa

Arctotis leptorhiza var. breviscapa, commonly known as the narrow-cupped arctotis, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers with bright yellow centers and white to pale blue petals. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. It has narrow, linear leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.

The variety breviscapa is distinguished by its shorter flower stems compared to the typical form.
