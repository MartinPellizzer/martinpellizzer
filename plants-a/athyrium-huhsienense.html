Athyrium huhsienense

Athyrium huhsienense, commonly known as the Huhsienshane fern, is a species of flowering plant in the family Athyriaceae.

It is native to China and is characterized by its delicate, feather-like fronds that are typically bioluminescent under certain conditions. The plant thrives in shaded, moist environments, often found in mountainous regions with high humidity. Its unique reproductive structures include spore-bearing sorus clusters on the underside of its leaves.

This fern is of interest to botanists due to its ecological significance and potential medicinal properties.
