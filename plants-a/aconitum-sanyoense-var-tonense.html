Aconitum sanyoense var. tonense

Aconitum sanyoense var. tonense, commonly known as the Sanyoense Monkshood, is a herbaceous perennial native to Japan.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to purple hood-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as mountain forests. Its leaves are palmately compound, with deeply lobed foliage that adds to its ornamental appeal.

It is valued in horticulture for its unique appearance and ability to thrive in specific ecological conditions.
