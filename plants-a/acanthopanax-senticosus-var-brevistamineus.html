Acanthopanax senticosus var. brevistamineus

Acanthopanax senticosus var. brevistamineus, commonly known as the short-staminate angel's trumpet, is a climbing shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its compound leaves with serrated margins and small, greenish-white flowers that appear in clusters. The plant produces small, fleshy berries that are typically red when mature. It is often found in forested areas at moderate elevations.

Due to its medicinal properties, it is valued in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.
