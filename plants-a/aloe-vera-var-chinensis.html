Aloe vera var. chinensis

Aloe vera var. chinensis, commonly known as Chinese aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern China and parts of Southeast Asia.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern, with a bluish-green coloration. The leaves contain a gel-like substance that is widely used for its soothing and moisturizing properties. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires moderate sunlight, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.

This variety is often cultivated for both ornamental purposes and its medicinal benefits.
