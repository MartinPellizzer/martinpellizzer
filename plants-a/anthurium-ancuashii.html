Anthurium ancuashii

Anthurium ancuashii, commonly known as the Andean anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the Andes region of South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves and vibrant, spadix-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in the understory of forests. Its foliage is usually dark green with a glossy texture, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers surrounded by a colorful spathe.

Anthurium ancuashii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in botanical gardens.
