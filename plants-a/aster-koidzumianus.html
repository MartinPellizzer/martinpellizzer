Aster koidzumianus

Aster koidzumianus, commonly known as the Koidzumian aster, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or alpine habitats. The plant features a slender stem with lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of small, blue to violet flowers. It blooms during the summer months and is valued for its ornamental qualities in horticulture.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species in its native range.
