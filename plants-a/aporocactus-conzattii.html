Aporocactus conzattii

Aporocactus conzattii, commonly known as the Conzatti's cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a small, compact cactus with a globular shape and dense, ribbed stems. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its unique appearance and slow growth, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and container cultivation.
