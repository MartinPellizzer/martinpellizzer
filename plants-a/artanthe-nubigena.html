Artanthe nubigena

Artanthe nubigena, commonly known as the cloudland artanthe, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy inflorescences. The plant typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered in a fine layer of hairs, which helps reduce water loss.

Artanthe nubigena is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
