Aneilema nyasense var. nyasense

Aneilema nyasense var. nyasense, commonly known as the Nyasense arrowroot, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to eastern Africa.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories and along stream banks. The plant features arrowhead-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. Its flowering stems bear small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters.

The plant is valued for its edible tubers, which are used in traditional cuisine and have potential for cultivation as a starch source.
