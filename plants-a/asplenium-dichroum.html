Asplenium dichroum

Asplenium dichroum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf spleenwort, is a small evergreen fern belonging to the Aspleniaceae family.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and slightly glossy. The plant produces spores in small, clustered sporangia located on the underside of the fronds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found growing on rocks or tree trunks.

Asplenium dichroum is a hardy plant that is well adapted to humid, temperate climates.
