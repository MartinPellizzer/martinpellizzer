Acacia olsenii

Acacia olsenii, commonly known as the desert acacia, is a shrub native to the arid regions of the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its slender, multi-branched stems and small, bean-like pods that contain its seeds. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, adding a striking visual element to its desert habitat. Acacia olsenii is well adapted to harsh conditions, with deep root systems and waxy leaves that reduce water loss.

It plays an important ecological role by providing food and shelter for various desert wildlife.
