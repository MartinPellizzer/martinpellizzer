Asiasarum dimidiatum

Asiasarum dimidiatum, commonly known as the Chinese bellflower, is a herbaceous perennial native to eastern Asia.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, often with a distinctive red or purple throat. The plant has hairy stems and heart-shaped leaves that are typically arranged in a basal rosette. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

Asiasarum dimidiatum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental flowers and adaptability to garden settings.
