Anochilus flanaganii

Anochilus flanaganii, commonly known as the Flanagan's anochilus, is a rare orchid species native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green in color. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers with a unique coloration, often featuring a combination of white and purple hues. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires specific soil conditions to flourish.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native range.
