Albuca abyssinica

Albuca abyssinica, commonly known as the Abyssinian albuca, is a bulbous plant native to Ethiopia.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of white or pale yellow flowers. The plant is adapted to arid and rocky environments, showcasing drought tolerance. Its bulbs are often used in traditional medicine in its native region.

It is a popular choice for rock gardens due to its ornamental value and low maintenance requirements.
