Anacamptis × feinbruniae

Anacamptis × feinbruniae, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a naturally occurring hybrid between Anacamptis morio and Anacamptis paludicola.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant that typically grows in wet meadows and marshy areas across Europe. The plant features a tall, erect stem with several whorls of showy, violet-blue flowers. Its leaves are linear and grass-like, and it blooms from late spring to early summer.

This hybrid is notable for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
