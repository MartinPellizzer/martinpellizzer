Aptenia haeckeliana

Aptenia haeckeliana, commonly known as the silver cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features small, fleshy, silver-green leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or purple during the warmer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its compact size and ornamental appearance make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
