Arachnangraecum ramosum

Arachnangraecum ramosum, commonly known as the branched spider plant, is a member of the Asparagaceae family.

Arachnangraecum ramosum is often grown as a decorative plant in hanging baskets or containers due to its elegant appearance and ease of care.
