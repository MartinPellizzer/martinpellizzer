Aloe wickensii

Aloe wickensii, commonly known as the Cape Town aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, often with a waxy coating. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow flowers during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its leaves to survive droughts.

Aloe wickensii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
