Agave fenzliana

Agave fenzliana, commonly known as the Fenzli's agave, is a species native to the arid regions of Mexico.

It is a succulent plant characterized by its rosette-shaped arrangement of thick, fleshy leaves. The leaves are typically blue-green in color with white marginal teeth, giving it a striking appearance. It produces a tall, branched flower stalk that bears yellow flowers, which bloom in the spring.

This plant is well adapted to drought conditions and requires minimal water once established.
