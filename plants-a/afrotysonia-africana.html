Afrotysonia africana

Afrotysonia africana, commonly known as the African star grass, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features a fibrous root system and produces upright, grass-like stems that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are typically blue-green in color, and it produces small, white or pale yellow flowers in clusters. Afrotysonia africana is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a resilient plant in dry climates.

It is often used in traditional medicine and as a source of fiber for local communities.
