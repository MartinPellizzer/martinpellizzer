Aloe tristicha

Aloe tristicha, commonly known as the zigzag aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, narrow, and somewhat zigzagging leaves that are covered in a white powdery coating. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters at the ends of its stems. Aloe tristicha is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
