Aspidistra bicolor

Aspidistra bicolor, commonly known as the variegated aspidistra, is a striking evergreen perennial plant native to Japan.

It features glossy, dark green leaves with distinctive white and yellow variegation, creating a visually appealing contrast. The plant thrives in low-light conditions and prefers consistently moist, well-draining soil. It is known for its hardiness and ability to tolerate indoor environments with minimal care.

Aspidistra bicolor is a popular choice for shaded indoor spaces due to its elegant appearance and ease of maintenance.
