Anisifolium schweinfurthii

Anisifolium schweinfurthii, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, daisy-like flowers that range in color from white to yellow and orange. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are often arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Anisifolium schweinfurthii is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
