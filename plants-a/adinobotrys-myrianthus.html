Adinobotrys myrianthus

Adinobotrys myrianthus, commonly known as the African starfruit tree, is a tropical plant native to East Africa.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and produces yellow-orange fruits with a star-like appearance. The plant is often cultivated for its edible fruit, which has a sweet and tangy flavor. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

The tree is also valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
