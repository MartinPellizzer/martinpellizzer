Avenastrum rigidulum

Avenastrum rigidulum, commonly known as rigid hairgrass, is a perennial grass species native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and dense clusters of spikelets that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to withstand harsh environmental conditions. Its leaves are narrow and rigid, contributing to its common name.

Avenastrum rigidulum is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in poor soil conditions.
