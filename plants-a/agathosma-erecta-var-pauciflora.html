Agathosma erecta var. pauciflora

Agathosma erecta var. pauciflora, commonly known as the Cape garlic.

It is a succulent shrub native to South Africa, characterized by its small, narrow leaves and compact growth habit. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers during the warmer months, which are typically clustered in dense inflorescences. It is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, storing water in its fleshy leaves.

Its aromatic foliage has made it a popular choice for ornamental and medicinal purposes.
