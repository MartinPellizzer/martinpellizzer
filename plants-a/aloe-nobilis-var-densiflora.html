Aloe nobilis var. densiflora

Aloe nobilis var. densiflora, commonly known as the dense-flowered aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy, and often blue-green leaves that are covered in a powdery coating. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with clusters of tubular, yellowish-orange flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
