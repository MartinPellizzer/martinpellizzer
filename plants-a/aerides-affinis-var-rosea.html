Aerides affinis var. rosea

Aerides affinis var. rosea, commonly known as the rose-colored orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, velvety pink to rose-colored flowers. The plant typically blooms in late spring to early summer, producing fragrant, showy blooms that last several weeks. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate to high humidity for optimal growth.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
