Anastrophus nematanthus

Anastrophus nematanthus, commonly known as the star-shaped nightshade, is a member of the Solanaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking, star-like flowers that are typically white or pale blue in color. The plant has hairy stems and leaves that are ovate in shape with serrated edges. It produces small, berry-like fruits that contain toxic seeds.

Anastrophus nematanthus is native to parts of Central and South America and is often found in moist, shaded environments.
