Androsace Vitaliana Var. Flosjugorum

Androsace vitaliana var. flosjugorum is a rare and delicate alpine plant known for its compact growth and showy white flowers. It thrives in high-altitude environments where few species can survive, often found in rocky meadows and mountainous regions. This variant is particularly prized by botanists and horticulturists for its unique floral characteristics and limited distribution. Its beauty makes it a sought-after specimen in specialized gardens and botanical collections. Despite its appeal, it remains a challenging plant to cultivate due to its specific environmental needs.
