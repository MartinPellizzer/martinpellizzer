Aria × robertsonii

Aria × robertsonii, commonly known as the hybrid lily, is a botanical hybrid resulting from the cross between Aria alpina and Aria tenuifolia.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. The plant features tall, slender stems that bear clusters of fragrant, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white to pink. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a basal rosette, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Aria × robertsonii is valued in horticulture for its elegant blooms and adaptability to various growing conditions.
