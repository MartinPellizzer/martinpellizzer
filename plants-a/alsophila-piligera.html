Alsophila piligera

Alsophila piligera, commonly known as the slender tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnately compound fronds that can reach several meters in length. The plant has a robust rhizome system that allows it to thrive in moist, shaded environments. Its fronds are typically a deep green and exhibit a distinctive, elegant appearance.

Alsophila piligera plays an important ecological role in tropical forests, providing habitat and contributing to biodiversity.
