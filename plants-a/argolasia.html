Argolasia

Argolasia, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a tropical plant native to parts of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, yellow flowers that bloom in long, hanging clusters, attracting pollinators like bees and birds. The plant has simple, opposite leaves and produces small, woody pods that contain seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Argolasia is often cultivated for its ornamental value and can be used in landscaping or as a shade tree.
