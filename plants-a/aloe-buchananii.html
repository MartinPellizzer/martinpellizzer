Aloe buchananii

Aloe buchananii, commonly known as the Cape aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features striking, spiky, grass-like leaves that are typically green with red or purple margins. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellowish-green flowers during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its fleshy leaves.

Aloe buchananii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some medicinal uses in traditional practices.
