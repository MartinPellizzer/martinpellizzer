Astronium graveolens var. dugandii

Astronium graveolens var. dugandii, commonly known as the yellow starwood, is a tropical tree native to South America, particularly found in countries like Colombia and Venezuela.

It belongs to the family Anacardiaceae and is characterized by its smooth, pale bark and dense, evergreen foliage. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are followed by spherical, red fruits containing several seeds.
