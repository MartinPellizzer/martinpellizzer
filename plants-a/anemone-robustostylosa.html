Anemone robustostylosa

Anemone robustostylosa, commonly known as the robust stem anemone, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features a robust, upright stem that can grow up to 1 meter in height, supporting large, showy white or pale pink flowers. The leaves are deeply lobed and arranged alternately along the stem, contributing to its distinctive appearance. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or alpine habitats.

Its flowering period typically occurs in late spring to early summer, making it a notable wildflower in its native environment.
