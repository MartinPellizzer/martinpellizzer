Ardisia cymosa

Ardisia cymosa, commonly known as the Christmas berry, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, dark green leaves and produces clusters of small, white flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant is notable for its bright red, berry-like fruits that ripen in the winter, making it a popular ornamental plant. Ardisia cymosa thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.

Its botanical characteristics make it well-suited for tropical and subtropical climates.
