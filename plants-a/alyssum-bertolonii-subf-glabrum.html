Alyssum bertolonii subf. glabrum

Alyssum bertolonii subf.

glabrum, commonly known as the smooth alyssum, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region. It is a low-growing perennial that produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow.

The plant has linear, hairy leaves that are typically arranged in a basal rosette. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming period and ability to attract pollinators.
