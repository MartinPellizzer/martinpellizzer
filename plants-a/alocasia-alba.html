Alocasia alba

Alocasia alba, commonly known as the White Alocasia, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a vibrant green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that bloom in clusters, adding a subtle touch of elegance. Alocasia alba thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and ornamental purposes.
