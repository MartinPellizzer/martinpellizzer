Aeschynomene javanica var. luxurians

Aeschynomene javanica var. luxurians, commonly known as the Java aeschynomene, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its slender, trailing stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, tropical environments and is often found in disturbed soils. It has small, pinnately compound leaves and produces pod-like fruits that contain several seeds.
