Achillea ligustica var. pinnatisecta

Achillea ligustica var. pinnatisecta, commonly known as the narrow-leaved yarrow, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features finely divided, fern-like leaves that give it a delicate, lacey appearance. The plant produces dense clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its aromatic foliage and long flowering period make it both ornamental and useful in traditional herbal medicine.
