Adenocheton

Adenocheton, commonly known as the African violet, is a genus of flowering plants native to Africa.

These plants are characterized by their dense, velvety foliage and showy, clustered flowers that come in a variety of colors. Adenocheton species typically grow as compact, bushy plants with a short, thick stem. They prefer bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.

The genus is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and ease of care.
