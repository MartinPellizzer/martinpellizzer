Anemone tenuiloba

Anemone tenuiloba, commonly known as the slender anemone, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the western United States.

It features delicate, white or pink flowers with a distinctive, narrow petal structure. The plant has a short, thick rhizome and basal leaves that are lance-shaped and hairy. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows and along stream banks.

Its botanical characteristics make it a notable species in the Ranunculaceae family.
