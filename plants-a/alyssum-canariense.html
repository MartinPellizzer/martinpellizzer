Alyssum canariense

Alyssum canariense, commonly known as Canary Island Alyssum, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically reaches heights of about 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features dense clusters of small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink. Its leaves are narrow and often covered in a fine layer of white hairs.

Alyssum canariense thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
