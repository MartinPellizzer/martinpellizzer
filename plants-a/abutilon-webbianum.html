Abutilon webbianum

Abutilon webbianum, commonly known as the velvetleaf abutilon, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 6 meters in height, characterized by its fuzzy, velvety leaves. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, hairy seeds.

Abutilon webbianum is often found in disturbed areas, such as roadsides and clearings, and is adapted to a variety of soil types.
