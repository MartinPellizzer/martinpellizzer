Afrothismia zambesiaca

Afrothismia zambesiaca, commonly known as the Zambesi lily, is a striking perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and large, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its foliage consists of broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating.

Afrothismia zambesiaca is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
