Anagyris chinensis

Anagyris chinensis, commonly known as the Chinese burrbean, is a perennial shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its pod-like fruits and nitrogen-fixing ability. The plant features compound leaves with stipules and produces yellow flowers that attract pollinators. Its fruits are woody pods containing several seeds, which are dispersed by wind or animals.

Anagyris chinensis is often used in traditional medicine and as a forage plant for livestock.
