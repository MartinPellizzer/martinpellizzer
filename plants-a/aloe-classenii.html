Aloe classenii

Aloe classenii, commonly known as the Desert Aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a waxy coating, which helps it store water. The plant produces tall, branched flower stalks that bear yellow or orange flowers, adding visual interest. It thrives in arid environments and requires minimal water, making it ideal for xeriscaping.

Its resilience and aesthetic appeal make it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor gardens.
