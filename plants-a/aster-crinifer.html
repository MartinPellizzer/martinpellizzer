Aster crinifer

Aster crinifer, commonly known as the sea aster, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of Europe and North America.

It is characterized by its blue to purple flower heads and feathery, grass-like leaves. The plant thrives in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in maritime environments. Its flowers bloom in late summer and early autumn, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Aster crinifer is valued for its ornamental qualities and its ability to stabilize coastal dunes.
