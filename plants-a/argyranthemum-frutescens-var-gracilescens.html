Argyranthemum frutescens var. gracilescens

Argyranthemum frutescens var. gracilescens, commonly known as the silverleaf marguerite, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and produces small, daisy-like flowers in shades of white and pale yellow. The leaves are silvery-gray, covered in fine hairs, which give the plant its common name. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
