Arawakia glossophylla

Arawakia glossophylla, commonly known as the golden leaf tree, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its broad, glossy, dark green leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or white in color. Its fruit is a capsule that contains several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

Arawakia glossophylla is valued for its ornamental beauty and has traditional uses in some indigenous communities.
