Anthurium pergamentaceum

Anthurium pergamentaceum, commonly known as the parchment anthurium, is a member of the Araceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often a deep green with a waxy texture. Its striking spadix and spathe make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Anthurium pergamentaceum thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and high humidity to flourish.
