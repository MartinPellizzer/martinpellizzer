Angiopteris boivinii

Angiopteris boivinii, commonly known as the Boivin's water fern, is a species of aquatic fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds that float on the water's surface. The plant has a complex life cycle involving both aquatic and terrestrial phases. Its rhizomes form dense mats that help stabilize water ecosystems.

Angiopteris boivinii plays a vital role in aquatic habitats by providing shelter for small aquatic organisms.
