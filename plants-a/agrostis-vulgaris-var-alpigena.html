Agrostis vulgaris var. alpigena

Agrostis vulgaris var. alpigena, commonly known as alpine bentgrass, is a flowering plant belonging to the grass family Poaceae.

It is a low-growing perennial grass that typically thrives in alpine and subalpine environments. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spikelet-based flowers. It is often found in rocky or gravelly soils at high elevations.

Its ability to tolerate cold and poor soil conditions makes it an important component of alpine ecosystems.
