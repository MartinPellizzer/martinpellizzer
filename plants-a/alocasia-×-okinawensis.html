Alocasia × okinawensis

Alocasia × okinawensis, commonly known as the Okinawan Alocasia, is a hybrid plant native to the Ryukyu Islands of Japan.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a metallic sheen. The plant belongs to the Araceae family, which includes many ornamental and tropical plants. Its growth habit is generally upright, with a rhizomatous root system that allows it to spread slowly in suitable conditions.

Alocasia × okinawensis prefers warm, humid environments and is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant.
