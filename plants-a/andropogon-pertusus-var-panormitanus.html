Andropogon pertusus var. panormitanus

Andropogon pertusus var. panormitanus, commonly known as the Panormitanus bluestem, is a grass species native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its fine, blue-green foliage and tufted growth habit, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as prairies and sandy soils. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny florets.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and its role in ecological restoration projects.
