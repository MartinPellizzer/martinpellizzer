Anthurium × carneum

Anthurium × carneum, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a hybrid plant native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped, crimson-colored spathe that surrounds a spadix, which is typically yellow or orange. The plant belongs to the Araceae family and is prized for its striking foliage and long-lasting blooms. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Anthurium × carneum is a popular ornamental plant in indoor gardening due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
