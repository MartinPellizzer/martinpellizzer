Anthurium scherzerianum var. peetersianum

Anthurium scherzerianum var. peetersianum, commonly known as the Peeters' Anthurium, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically deep green with a metallic sheen. The plant produces vibrant red spathes that enclose the spadix, creating an eye-catching floral display. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

This variety is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
