Alternanthera megaphylla

Alternanthera megaphylla, commonly known as the giant leaf Alternanthera, is a striking aquatic or semi-aquatic plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, vibrant green leaves that can grow up to 10 centimeters in length. The plant thrives in warm, humid environments and prefers bright, indirect light. It is often cultivated in aquariums or as a decorative plant in water gardens.

Its foliage is known for its ability to change color, especially in response to light and nutrient levels.
