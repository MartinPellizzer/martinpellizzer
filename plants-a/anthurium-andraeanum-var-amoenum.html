Anthurium andraeanum var. amoenum

Anthurium andraeanum var. amoenum, commonly known as the Red Velvet Anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are deep green with a velvety texture. The plant produces vibrant, spathiflorous inflorescences that range in color from red to pink, depending on the variety. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.

This ornamental plant is widely cultivated for its aesthetic appeal and is often used in floral arrangements and indoor gardening.
