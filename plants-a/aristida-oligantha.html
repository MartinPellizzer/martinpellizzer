Aristida oligantha

Aristida oligantha, commonly known as slender threeawn, is a grass species native to the western United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and small, spikelet-based flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. It has a compact growth habit and is often found in disturbed soils.

Aristida oligantha is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in stabilizing soil in arid environments.
