Amaryllis clusiana

Amaryllis clusiana, commonly known as the Clusian amaryllis, is a bulbous plant native to South America.

It produces striking, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant features strap-like, grassy leaves that emerge from a central bulb. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers bright, indirect light.

Amaryllis clusiana is often cultivated for its vibrant blooms and ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
