Astragalus khunsarensis

Astragalus khunsarensis, commonly known as the Kunshan milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its erect stems and small, pea-like flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid conditions. Its seeds are enclosed in pods and are often dispersed by wind.

Astragalus khunsarensis is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphology and ecological adaptations.
