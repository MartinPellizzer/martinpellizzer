Aster yukonensis

Aster yukonensis, commonly known as the Yukon aster, is a perennial herb native to the Yukon and Alaska regions of North America.

It typically grows in moist, open habitats such as tundra and meadows. The plant features upright stems that bear opposite, lance-shaped leaves with hairy surfaces. Its showy flowers, which range from white to purple, bloom in late summer and early fall.

Aster yukonensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting local pollinators.
