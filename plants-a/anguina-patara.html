Anguina patara

Anguina patara, commonly known as the patara thistle, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its spiny leaves and purple or pink flowers that bloom in the summer months. Native to the Mediterranean region, this plant thrives in dry, rocky soils and full sunlight. The plant's structure includes a robust stem with alternate, lance-shaped leaves.

It plays a role in supporting local pollinators and is sometimes used in traditional herbal medicine.
