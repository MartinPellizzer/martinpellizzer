Artemisia edgeworthii var. diffusa

Artemisia edgeworthii var. diffusa, commonly known as blue mist shrub, is a perennial shrub native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its silvery-blue foliage, which is covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flower heads that appear in late summer to early fall. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Its aromatic leaves and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
