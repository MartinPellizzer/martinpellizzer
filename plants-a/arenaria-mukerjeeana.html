Arenaria mukerjeeana

Arenaria mukerjeeana, commonly known as the Mukerjee's sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Himalayan regions of India.

It thrives in rocky and alpine environments, typically found at elevations between 2,000 and 5,000 meters. The plant features small, clustered flowers that are usually white or pale pink, blooming in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are linear and succulent, adapted to retain moisture in arid conditions.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in rock gardens and alpine collections.
