Anthurium subhastatum

Anthurium subhastatum, commonly known as the arrowhead plant, is a tropical species native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces striking spadix flowers enclosed within a colorful spathe, often in shades of red, orange, or yellow. It thrives in humid, warm environments and requires indirect sunlight for optimal growth.

Anthurium subhastatum is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and relatively low maintenance requirements.
