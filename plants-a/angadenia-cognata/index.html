Angadenia Cognata

Angadenia cognata is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the southeastern United States. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as rich hardwood forests and swampy areas. This species is known for its small, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring. Due to habitat loss and environmental changes, it is now considered endangered. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this unique and beautiful plant.
