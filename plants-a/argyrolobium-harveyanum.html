Argyrolobium harveyanum

Argyrolobium harveyanum, commonly known as the Harvey's silverlobed burr.

It is a flowering plant native to South Africa, characterized by its distinctive silver-green foliage and showy yellow flowers. The plant belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is known for its ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, enhancing soil fertility. It thrives in well-drained sandy soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.

The seed pods are woody and contain several small, hard seeds that are dispersed by wind or animals.
