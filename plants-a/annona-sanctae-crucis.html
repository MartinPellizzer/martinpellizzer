Annona sanctae-crucis

Annona sanctae-crucis, commonly known as the sacred cross annona, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is a small evergreen tree or shrub that can grow up to 5 meters in height. The plant produces large, heart-shaped leaves and yellowish-white flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruit, similar to a pear, is edible and has a sweet, aromatic flavor.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and has traditional uses in local medicine.
