Aconitum × stoerkianum

Aconitum × stoerkianum, commonly known as the hybrid monkshood, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Ranunculaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking, helmet-shaped blue or purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant features palmate leaves with deep lobes, and its stems are typically sparsely hairy. Native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, it thrives in moist, well-drained soils.

Due to its toxic alkaloids, it is important to handle the plant with care.
