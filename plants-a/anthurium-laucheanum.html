Anthurium laucheanum

Anthurium laucheanum, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a tropical flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped spathe that ranges from red to orange in color. The plant features a long, slender spadix that is often covered in small, inconspicuous flowers. Its leaves are typically deep green and glossy, with a waxy texture.

Anthurium laucheanum thrives in humid, warm environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.
