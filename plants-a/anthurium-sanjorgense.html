Anthurium sanjorgense

Anthurium sanjorgense, commonly known as the Sanjorge anthurium, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped leaves that are often adorned with intricate patterns and a deep, glossy green color. The plant produces striking spadices, which are specialized inflorescences that give it its dramatic appearance. It thrives in high humidity and indirect light, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated for its foliage rather than its flowers.
