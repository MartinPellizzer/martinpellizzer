Aridarum burttii

Aridarum burttii, commonly known as the Burtt's desert lily, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of Australia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stem and large, showy white or pale pink flowers that bloom in late winter or early spring. The plant has thick, fleshy leaves that help it retain moisture in its dry habitat. It is adapted to survive in harsh conditions with minimal rainfall.

Aridarum burttii plays an important role in pollination and provides nectar for native wildlife.
