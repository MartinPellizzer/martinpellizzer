Alocasia argyroneura

Alocasia argyroneura, commonly known as the silver veins alocasia, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that feature striking silver veins against a deep green background. The plant belongs to the Araceae family and is prized for its ornamental value in both indoor and outdoor settings. It thrives in humid, warm environments with bright, indirect light.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for collectors and garden enthusiasts.
