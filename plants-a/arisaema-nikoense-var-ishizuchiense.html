Arisaema nikoense var. ishizuchiense

Arisaema nikoense var. ishizuchiense, commonly known as the Ishizuchi arisaema, is a rare plant species native to Japan.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, leaf-like bracts that enclose its spadix. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky slopes. Its flowers are unisexual, with separate male and female flowers on the spadix.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its unique foliage and floral structure.
