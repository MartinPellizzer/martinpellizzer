Aloysia decipiens

Aloysia decipiens, commonly known as the red sage or scarlet verbena, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and opposite, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of vibrant red, tubular flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Aloysia decipiens is also valued for its ornamental appeal and use in traditional medicine.
