Arundo formosana subsp. robusta

Arundo formosana subsp.

robusta, commonly known as the robust reed, is a tall, perennial grass native to eastern Asia. It is characterized by its sturdy, hollow stems that can grow up to 3 meters in height.

The plant produces dense clusters of small, spike-like inflorescences that are typically green to brown in color. Its leaves are long, narrow, and have a fibrous texture, making it suitable for various practical uses.

It thrives in wet or moist environments, often found near rivers, lakes, and wetlands.
