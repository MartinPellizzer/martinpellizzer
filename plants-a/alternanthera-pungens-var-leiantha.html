Alternanthera pungens var. leiantha

Alternanthera pungens var. leiantha, commonly known as the red-leaf alternanthera, is a strikingly ornamental plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful foliage that ranges from deep red to purple and pink hues, depending on light exposure and care. The plant has slender, upright stems and small, inconspicuous flowers that appear in clusters. It thrives in moist, well-draining soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Its unique leaf coloration makes it a popular choice for aquatic and terrestrial gardens.
