Argyroxiphium virescens

Argyroxiphium virescens, commonly known as the green mountain mint, is a member of the Asteraceae family and is native to Hawaii.

It is a shrub that typically grows in moist, shaded environments on the island of Maui. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, white hairs, giving it a silvery appearance. Its small, tubular flowers are usually greenish-yellow and bloom in clusters.

Argyroxiphium virescens is adapted to the unique ecological conditions of Hawaii and is considered an endemic species.
