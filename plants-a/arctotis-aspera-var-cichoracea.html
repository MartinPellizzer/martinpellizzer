Arctotis aspera var. cichoracea

Arctotis aspera var. cichoracea, commonly known as the African daisy, is a perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, daisy-like flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, often with a dark central disk. The plant features rough, hairy leaves that are typically gray-green in appearance. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its drought tolerance and ability to attract pollinators make it a valuable addition to many gardens.
