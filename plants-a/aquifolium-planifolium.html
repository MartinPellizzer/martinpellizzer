Aquifolium planifolium

Aquifolium planifolium, commonly known as the smooth-leaf holly, is a species of evergreen shrub or small tree native to parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, glossy leaves that are typically smooth and oval in shape. The plant produces small, red berries that are an important food source for birds and other wildlife. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers partial to full sunlight.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
