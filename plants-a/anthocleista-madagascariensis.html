Anthocleista madagascariensis

Anthocleista madagascariensis, commonly known as the Madagascar moonseed, is a climbing shrub native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Loganiaceae family and is characterized by its woody stems and palmate leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are followed by woody, capsule-like fruits containing several seeds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.
