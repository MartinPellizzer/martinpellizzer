Alsodeia bourdaisii

Alsodeia bourdaisii, commonly known as the desert sand-verbena, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of the Middle East and North Africa.

It is a low-growing annual herb with small, pink or purple flowers that bloom during the cooler months. The plant has narrow, fleshy leaves adapted to conserve water in dry environments. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in disturbed or coastal areas.

Its ability to survive in harsh conditions makes it an important species in ecological restoration projects.
