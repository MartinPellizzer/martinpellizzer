Anthurium conspicuum

Anthurium conspicuum, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its glossy, heart-shaped leaves and vibrant, spadix-like inflorescence that ranges from red to orange in color. The plant thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil. Its unique floral structure consists of a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe, which is actually a modified leaf.

Anthurium conspicuum is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental value and ability to purify indoor air.
