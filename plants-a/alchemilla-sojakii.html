Alchemilla sojakii

Alchemilla sojakii, commonly known as the narrow-leaved lady's mantle, is a low-growing perennial plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit. The plant features elliptical, dark green leaves with a waxy coating, and it produces small, yellowish-green flowers in early summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or alpine environments.

Alchemilla sojakii is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to stabilize soil in challenging landscapes.
