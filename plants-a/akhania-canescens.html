Akhania canescens

Akhania canescens, commonly known as the silverleaf daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and showy yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is well adapted to arid conditions. Its leaves are covered in fine, white hairs, which help reduce water loss.

Akhania canescens is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and drought tolerance.
