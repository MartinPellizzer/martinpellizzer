Acalypha medibracteata var. calcicola

Acalypha medibracteata var. calcicola, commonly known as the limestone acalypha, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions.

It is characterized by its distinctive, palmate leaves that are often covered in fine, silky hairs. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. It is adapted to grow in limestone soils and can thrive in environments with limited water availability.
