Alsophila extensa

Alsophila extensa, commonly known as the broad-leaf tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate fronds that can reach several meters in length. The plant has a distinctive crown of feathery fronds that give it a graceful appearance. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Alsophila extensa plays an important ecological role by providing habitat and shelter for various forest organisms.
