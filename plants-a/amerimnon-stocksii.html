Amerimnon stocksii

Amerimnon stocksii, commonly known as the golden star orchid, is a flowering plant native to the rainforests of Queensland, Australia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking yellow and green foliage. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters, often resembling stars. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, acidic soil.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a conservation concern.
