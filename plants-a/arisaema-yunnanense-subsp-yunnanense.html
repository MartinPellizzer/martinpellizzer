Arisaema yunnanense subsp. yunnanense

Arisaema yunnanense subsp.

yunnanense, commonly known as the Yunnan arrowhead, is a member of the Araceae family. It is native to China, particularly in Yunnan Province, where it thrives in moist, shaded forest environments.

The plant is characterized by its distinctive arrow-shaped leaves and a spadix surrounded by a modified leaf called a spathe. It produces small, greenish-white flowers that are typically clustered in a single inflorescence.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appearance and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
