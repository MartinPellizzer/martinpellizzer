Achimenes longiflora var. major

Achimenes longiflora var. major, commonly known as the long-flowered achimenes, is a tropical flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The leaves are heart-shaped and glossy, often with a waxy texture. This variety is known for its ability to thrive in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant in gardens and greenhouses due to its vibrant blooms and relatively easy care requirements.
