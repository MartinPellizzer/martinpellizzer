Alternanthera hirtula subvar. cinabarina

Alternanthera hirtula subvar. cinabarina, commonly known as the red-stemmed Alternanthera, is a striking aquatic or semi-aquatic plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red stems and lance-shaped leaves that often display a reddish or purple hue. This variety thrives in shallow water with moderate to high light exposure and requires stable water conditions. Its foliage tends to change color based on light intensity and nutrient levels, making it a popular choice for aquariums.

The plant is relatively easy to care for and can be propagated through stem cuttings.
