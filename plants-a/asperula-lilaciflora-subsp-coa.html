Asperula lilaciflora subsp. coa

Asperula lilaciflora subsp.

coa, commonly known as the purple honeysuckle, is a flowering plant native to parts of East Asia. It is characterized by its small, fragrant, lilac-colored flowers that bloom in clusters.

The plant has slender, ascending stems and heart-shaped leaves with serrated edges. It thrives in shaded to partially shaded environments and prefers moist, well-drained soil.

This subspecies is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
