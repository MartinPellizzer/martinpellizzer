Acacia compta

Acacia compta, commonly known as the slender acacia, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, multi-branched stems and small, feathery leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers in compact spikes, which are followed by flat, triangular seed pods. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Acacia compta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.
