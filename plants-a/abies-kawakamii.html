Abies kawakamii

Abies kawakamii, commonly known as the Kawakamii fir, is a rare and endemic species native to Taiwan.

It is a coniferous evergreen tree that typically grows in high-altitude forests, reaching heights of up to 20 meters. The tree has needle-like leaves that are dark green on top and whitish underneath, with a distinctive aromatic scent. Its cones are small and cylindrical, maturing to release seeds that are dispersed by wind.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native range.
