Acianthera serrulata

Acianthera serrulata, commonly known as the serrated acianthera, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, linear leaves. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or white in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in cloud forests.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
