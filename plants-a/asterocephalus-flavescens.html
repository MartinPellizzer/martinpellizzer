Asterocephalus flavescens

Asterocephalus flavescens, commonly known as the yellow-headed aster.

It is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Brazil, and belongs to the Asteraceae family. The plant is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in open woodland or forest understory.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens, though it is not widely known outside its native range.
