Aeschynanthus gesneriflorus

Aeschynanthus gesneriflorus, commonly known as the flame violet, is a tropical shrub native to western Africa.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers that range from red to orange. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that bloom in clusters, often with contrasting yellow or green markings. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and glossy, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Aeschynanthus gesneriflorus thrives in warm, humid conditions and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in greenhouses.
