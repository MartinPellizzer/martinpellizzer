Alsine media

Alsine media, commonly known as common alsike grass, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Caryophyllaceae.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in moist, well-drained soils, often found in meadows and along stream banks. Its leaves are linear and slightly hairy, and it is known for its ability to thrive in temperate climates.

Alsine media is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects due to its adaptability and soil-stabilizing properties.
