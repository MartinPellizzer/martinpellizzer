Apama sumatrana

Apama sumatrana, commonly known as the Sumatran apama, is a species of orchid native to the Indonesian island of Sumatra.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and yellow. The plant has a complex root system and prefers moist, well-drained soil in shaded environments. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, often with a waxy texture.

Apama sumatrana is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
