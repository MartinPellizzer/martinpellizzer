Anemone patens var. pinnatifolia

Anemone patens var. pinnatifolia, commonly known as the showy anemone, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the eastern United States.

It features basal leaves that are pinnately lobed, giving it its scientific name, and produces showy, pink to white flowers in early spring. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade. It is often found in woodland areas and along stream banks.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for native plant gardens and ecological restoration projects.
