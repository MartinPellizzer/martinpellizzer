Anemone × girodii

Anemone × girodii, commonly known as the Japanese anemone, is a hybrid perennial plant native to East Asia.

It features showy, single or double flowers in shades of white, pink, or red, typically blooming in late summer to early autumn. The plant has finely divided, fern-like foliage that adds texture to garden landscapes. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers moist, well-drained soil.

Anemone × girodii is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
