Aloe arachnoidea

Aloe arachnoidea, commonly known as the spider aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, slender stems covered in dense, woolly white hair, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. Its leaves are narrow and fleshy, adapted to store water in arid environments.

Aloe arachnoidea is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
