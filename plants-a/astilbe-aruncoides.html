Astilbe aruncoides

Astilbe aruncoides, commonly known as the mountain astilbe, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of eastern Asia.

It features delicate, plume-like inflorescences that range in color from white to pink and purple, typically appearing in late spring to early summer. The plant has lance-shaped, dark green leaves that are arranged in a basal rosette, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in partial to full shade and moist, well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens.

Astilbe aruncoides is valued for its ability to add texture and color to shady garden spaces.
