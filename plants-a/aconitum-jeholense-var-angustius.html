Aconitum jeholense var. angustius

Aconitum jeholense var. angustius, commonly known as the narrow-leaved Chinese monkshood, is a perennial herb native to northern China.

It features striking violet-blue flowers arranged in a spire, typical of the Aconitum genus. The plant has palmately lobed leaves that are narrow and deeply divided, giving it a delicate appearance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its toxic properties, it should be handled with care.
