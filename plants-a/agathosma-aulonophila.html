Agathosma aulonophila

Agathosma aulonophila, commonly known as the narrow-leaved agathosma, is a succulent shrub native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, narrow leaves and small yellow flowers. The plant is adapted to arid environments, storing water in its fleshy stems and leaves. It is often found in rocky soils and has a creeping growth habit.

Due to its medicinal properties, it is also used in traditional herbal remedies.
