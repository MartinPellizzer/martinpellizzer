Aerangis caulescens

Aerangis caulescens, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, elongated stems and clustered, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its flowers are usually pale green or white, with intricate patterns that aid in pollination by insects.

Aerangis caulescens is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
