Athyrium × multifidum var. latisectum

Athyrium × multifidum var. latisectum, commonly known as the broad-leaf fern, is a hybrid fern species characterized by its large, deeply divided fronds.

The plant features lanceolate to ovate leaflets that are broad and often have a distinctive, feathery appearance. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream sides. The fronds are usually a vibrant green and can reach lengths of up to 1 meter.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage.
