Anthurium atropurpureum var. arenicola

Anthurium atropurpureum var. arenicola, commonly known as the red-tipped anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its dark purple, velvety leaves and striking red spathes that enclose the bright yellow spadix. The plant thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil. Its foliage is often used in ornamental horticulture for its aesthetic appeal.

Anthurium atropurpureum var. arenicola is also valued for its air-purifying properties and ability to thrive in indoor settings.
