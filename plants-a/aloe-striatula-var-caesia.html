Aloe striatula var. caesia

Aloe striatula var. caesia, commonly known as the blue aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features striking blue-green leaves that are thick and fleshy, adapted to store water in arid environments. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with small, tubular flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice for succulent gardens.
