Anthurium huamaliesense

Anthurium huamaliesense, commonly known as the Huamaliesense arrowhead, is a species of flowering plant native to the Andean region of South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive spathe and spadix inflorescence. The plant features broad, arrow-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a glossy texture. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This ornamental plant is valued for its aesthetic appeal and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in tropical gardens.
