Aceratium dolichostylum

Aceratium dolichostylum, commonly known as the slender-leafed acanthus, is a flowering plant belonging to the Acanthaceae family.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine, silky hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and greenhouses around the world.
