Antheroporum puudjaae

Antheroporum puudjaae, commonly known as the Puudjaae grass, is a flowering plant belonging to the Poaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It is typically found in moist, temperate environments, such as meadows and stream banks.
