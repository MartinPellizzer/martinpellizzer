Ayenia donatica

Ayenia donatica, commonly known as the showy goldenstar, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features bright yellow ray flowers surrounding a central disk of smaller florets, making it visually striking. It typically grows in moist, open habitats such as woodlands and wetlands.

Ayenia donatica is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, providing nectar for pollinators.
