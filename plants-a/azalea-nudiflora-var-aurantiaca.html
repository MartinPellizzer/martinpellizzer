Azalea nudiflora var. aurantiaca

Azalea nudiflora var. aurantiaca, commonly known as the orange-flowered azalea, is a variant of the evergreen azalea native to China.

It is characterized by its showy, orange-red blooms that appear before the leaves in early spring. The plant has small, elliptical leaves that are glossy and dark green, providing a striking contrast to its vibrant flowers. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners in temperate climates.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping for its seasonal color display.
