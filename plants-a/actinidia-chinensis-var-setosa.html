Actinidia chinensis var. setosa

Actinidia chinensis var. setosa, commonly known as the fuzzy actinidia, is a deciduous vine native to China.

It is characterized by its finely hairy, ovate leaves and small, fragrant greenish-white flowers. The plant produces edible berries that mature to a bright orange-red color, typically in late summer. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental and edible landscaping.

The variety is valued for its hardiness and ability to climb structures or trellises.
