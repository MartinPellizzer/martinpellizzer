Aclisia subumbellata

Aclisia subumbellata, commonly known as the yellow star-of-Bethlehem, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of small, star-like blooms. It is often found in grasslands and along roadsides.

Aclisia subumbellata is a perennial herb that thrives in well-drained soils.
