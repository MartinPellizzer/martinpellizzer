Amauropelta furva

Amauropelta furva, commonly known as the golden shield fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive, shield-shaped fronds that are typically bright yellow to orange in color. The fronds are usually pinnately divided, with small, ovate leaflets that contribute to its ornamental appeal.
