Artemisia strongylocephala f. virgata

Artemisia strongylocephala f. virgata, commonly known as the silverleaf artemisia, is a perennial shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, dense hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flower heads that are typically clustered in dense, rounded clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

This subspecies is valued for its aromatic properties and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
