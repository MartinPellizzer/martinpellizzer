Aloe barteri

Aloe barteri, commonly known as the silver aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically gray-green in color, covered in a layer of white powder called farina. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow-orange flowers that bloom in spring. It is drought-tolerant and prefers well-draining soil, making it suitable for arid environments.

Aloe barteri is often cultivated for ornamental purposes and has some medicinal uses.
