Ayenia bernieri

Ayenia bernieri, commonly known as the red-stemmed ayenia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, daisy-like flowers. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, yellow disc flowers surrounded by pink to red ray florets. Ayenia bernieri is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens due to its vibrant floral display.

It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it adaptable to various garden conditions.
