Amaryllis Angustifolia

Amaryllis angustifolia is a striking member of the Amaryllidaceae family known for its elegant, narrow leaves and showy flower spikes. Native to South Africa, this plant thrives in warm, sunny climates and is prized for its vibrant blooms. Its slender foliage and clusters of trumpet-shaped flowers make it a popular choice for gardens and indoor spaces. Easy to care for, it requires well-draining soil and moderate watering to flourish. This plant adds both beauty and biodiversity to any landscape.
