Alchemilla plicatula subsp. catalaunica

Alchemilla plicatula subsp.

catalaunica, commonly known as the Catalaunica lady's mantle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe. It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its basal rosette of rounded, velvety leaves.

The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in dense clusters during the summer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or meadow habitats.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
