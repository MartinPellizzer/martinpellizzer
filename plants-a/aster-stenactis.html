Aster stenactis

Aster stenactis, commonly known as the narrowleaf aster, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands. It produces showy purple to blue flowers that bloom in the late summer and early fall.

Its narrow, linear leaves and sturdy stems make it a distinctive feature in its natural environment.
