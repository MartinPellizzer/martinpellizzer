Arisaema planilaminum

Arisaema planilaminum, commonly known as the flat-leaf arrowhead, is a herbaceous plant native to parts of East Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive inflorescence composed of a spadix surrounded by a modified leaf called a spathe. The plant features broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often smooth and glossy, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands.

Its unique morphology and ecological adaptations make it a notable species in botanical studies.
