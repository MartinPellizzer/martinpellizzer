Astroloma lasionemum

Astroloma lasionemum, commonly known as the woolly astroloma, is a flowering plant native to Western Australia.

It is a low-growing shrub with dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, clustered white or pale pink flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.

Astroloma lasionemum is often found in coastal and sandy habitats, where it contributes to local biodiversity.
