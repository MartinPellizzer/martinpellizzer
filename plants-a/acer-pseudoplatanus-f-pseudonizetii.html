Acer pseudoplatanus f. pseudonizetii

Acer pseudoplatanus f. pseudonizetii, commonly known as the Nizetii maple, is a unique form of the European maple.

It is characterized by its distinctive foliage, featuring leaves with a serrated margin and a pale green underside. The plant exhibits a compact growth habit, making it suitable for smaller garden spaces. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in horticultural collections.
