Aster reevesii

Aster reevesii, commonly known as the Reeves' aster, is a herbaceous perennial native to East Asia.

It features slender, branching stems and produces small, daisy-like flowers in shades of white to purple. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sun.

Aster reevesii is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits to pollinators.
