Arenaria Cavanillesiana

Arenaria cavanillesiana is a small, flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region. It thrives in rocky and coastal environments, often growing in well-drained soils. This species is known for its delicate blue or purple flowers that bloom in early spring. Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes. Despite its modest size, Arenaria cavanillesiana plays an important role in supporting local pollinators and maintaining ecological balance.
