Aframomum submontanum

Aframomum submontanum, commonly known as the mountain cardamom, is a tropical plant native to eastern Africa.

It belongs to the ginger family (Zingiberaceae) and is characterized by its aromatic, clustered inflorescences. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are followed by dark, elongated fruits containing aromatic seeds. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.
