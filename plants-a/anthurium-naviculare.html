Anthurium naviculare

Anthurium naviculare, commonly known as the boat-shaped anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, heart-shaped leaves that are often bright green with a waxy texture. The plant produces striking spadices, which are modified leaves that enclose the true flowers, typically in shades of red or yellow. Anthurium naviculare thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and ornamental purposes.
