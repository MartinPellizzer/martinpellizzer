Aizoanthemum mossamedense

Aizoanthemum mossamedense, commonly known as the Cape Town star, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Aizoaceae, which is characterized by its fleshy leaves and adaptations to arid environments. The plant features a low-growing, mat-forming structure with small, star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of yellow and orange. Its leaves are thick and succulent, storing water to help it survive in dry conditions.

Aizoanthemum mossamedense is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and resilience.
