Arenaria villarii

Arenaria villarii, commonly known as the hairy sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in coastal areas and rocky habitats. The plant features small, white or pinkish flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are typically covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive, fuzzy appearance.

Arenaria villarii is well adapted to harsh environmental conditions, making it a resilient species in arid and coastal environments.
