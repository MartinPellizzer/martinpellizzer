Aerangis flabellifolia

Aerangis flabellifolia, commonly known as the fan-leaf orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, strap-like leaves that radiate outward, forming a fan-like shape. The plant produces fragrant, showy flowers in clusters, typically in shades of white and pink. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Aerangis flabellifolia is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
