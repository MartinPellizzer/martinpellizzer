Agathosma stadensis

Agathosma stadensis, commonly known as Cape aster, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and thrives in full sun. It has a compact, bushy growth habit with silvery-green leaves that are often aromatic.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
