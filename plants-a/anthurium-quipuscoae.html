Anthurium quipuscoae

Anthurium quipuscoae, commonly known as the Quipuscoa arrowhead, is a striking tropical plant native to Ecuador.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped leaves that often display a mix of green and red hues. The plant produces unique spadices, which are inflorescences consisting of a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe. Anthurium quipuscoae thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and botanical collections.
