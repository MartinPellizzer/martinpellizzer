Aponogeton flavidum

Aponogeton flavidum, commonly known as the yellow-flowered arrowhead, is a perennial aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features arrowhead-shaped leaves that float on the water surface, with roots extending into the substrate. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom above the water, adding a delicate touch to its aquatic habitat. It thrives in slow-moving freshwater environments and is often found in ponds and marshes.

Aponogeton flavidum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in water gardens.
