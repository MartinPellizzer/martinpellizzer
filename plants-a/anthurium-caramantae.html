Anthurium caramantae

Anthurium caramantae, commonly known as the Caramantae arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to Colombia.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive inflorescence consisting of a spadix and a spathe. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy, dark green leaves that are typically broad and arrowhead-shaped. It produces striking, colorful spathes that can range from white to red, depending on the variety.

Anthurium caramantae is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable growing conditions.
