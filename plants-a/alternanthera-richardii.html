Alternanthera richardii

Alternanthera richardii, commonly known as red flame or red beauty, is a striking aquatic plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red or pink foliage, which becomes more intense under bright light and proper nutrients. The plant thrives in moderate to high light conditions and requires a nutrient-rich substrate for optimal growth. It is often used in aquariums for its ornamental value and ability to create a lush, colorful backdrop.

Alternanthera richardii is a fast-growing plant that can spread quickly if not pruned regularly.
