Amaranthus zanensis

Amaranthus zanensis, commonly known as the Zambian amaranth, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and vibrant, showy inflorescences that range in color from red to orange. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters and thrives in full sun with well-drained soil. Its leaves are broad and oval, often with a slightly hairy texture.

Amaranthus zanensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its visual impact.
