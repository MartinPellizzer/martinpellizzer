Anemonoides altaica

Anemonoides altaica, commonly known as the Altaian anemone, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features a short, creeping rhizome and produces showy, white or pale pink flowers with numerous petals. The plant has finely divided, fern-like leaves that are typically arranged in a basal rosette. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine meadows and rocky slopes.

Anemonoides altaica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with suitable conditions.
