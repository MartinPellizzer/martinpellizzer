Anemone Albana Var. Violacea

Anemone albana var. violacea is a striking variant of the common anemone known for its vibrant violet flowers. This plant thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas. Its delicate petals contrast beautifully with its sturdy green foliage. The species is popular among gardeners for its ornamental value and ease of care. It also plays an important role in supporting local pollinators and wildlife.
