Aria tiliifolia

Aria tiliifolia, commonly known as the common tiliifolia, is a flowering plant belonging to the Malvaceae family.

It is native to parts of Asia, including China, Japan, and Korea, and thrives in moist, shady environments. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a smooth texture and produces small, pale yellow flowers in clusters. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release tiny, wind-dispersed seeds.

Aria tiliifolia is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
