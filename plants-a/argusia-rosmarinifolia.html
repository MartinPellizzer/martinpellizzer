Argusia rosmarinifolia

Argusia rosmarinifolia, commonly known as the Mediterranean rosemary.

It is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region, characterized by its aromatic evergreen leaves and small, pale blue to white flowers. The plant has needle-like leaves that are fragrant, often used in traditional herbal medicine. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and aromatic plant collections.
