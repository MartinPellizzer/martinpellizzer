Aizoon burchellii

Aizoon burchellii, commonly known as the Burchell's silverleaf, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters, typically during the warmer months. Its leaves are often covered in a fine layer of white hairs, contributing to its unique visual texture.

Aizoon burchellii is adapted to arid environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
