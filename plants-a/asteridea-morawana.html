Asteridea morawana

Asteridea morawana, commonly known as the Morawana aster, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that typically bloom in shades of purple and white. The plant features a central disc of tubular flowers surrounded by ray florets, typical of the Asteraceae family. Native to specific regions in Europe, it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun exposure.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
