Ania malayana

Ania malayana, commonly known as the Malay anise tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree that can grow up to 10 meters in height. The plant is characterized by its aromatic leaves, which are often used in traditional medicine and culinary applications. Its flowers are small and clustered, typically white or pale yellow, and it produces small, round fruits.

The plant thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in lowland forests.
