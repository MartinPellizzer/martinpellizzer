Asagraea officinalis

Asagraea officinalis, commonly known as the medicinal orchid, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, often fragrant blooms. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forests or along riverbanks. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, while its flowers come in a variety of colors, including white, yellow, and pink.

The botanical interest in Asagraea officinalis lies in its potential medicinal properties, which have been explored in traditional herbal practices.
