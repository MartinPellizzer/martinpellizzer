Aloe arabica

Aloe arabica, commonly known as the desert aloe, is a succulent plant native to the arid regions of North Africa and the Middle East.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that store water, allowing it to survive in dry environments. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in the spring, adding a striking visual element to its appearance. Its gel-like substance has been used for centuries for its soothing and healing properties.

Aloe arabica is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
