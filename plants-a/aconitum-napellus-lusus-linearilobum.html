Aconitum napellus lusus linearilobum

Aconitum napellus lusus linearilobum, commonly known as the linearilobed monkshood, is a variant of the European aconite.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Central and Eastern Europe. The plant features striking blue to purple hooded flowers that bloom in summer. Its leaves are palmately lobed, with a distinctive appearance that resembles a hood.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.
