Agathosma sladeniana

Agathosma sladeniana, commonly known as Cape daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers with yellow centers and white petals. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and has a low, spreading habit. Its leaves are aromatic and often used in traditional medicine.

It is also valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
