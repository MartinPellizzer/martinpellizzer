Aconitum senanense var. yuparense

Aconitum senanense var. yuparense, commonly known as the Yupari Aconite, is a member of the Ranunculaceae family and is native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant features striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in summer, attracting various pollinators. Its leaves are palmately compound, with finely divided lobes, and the stems are often hairy.

Due to its toxic alkaloids, it is considered a dangerous plant, requiring caution during handling.
