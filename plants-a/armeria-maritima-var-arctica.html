Armeria maritima var. arctica

Armeria maritima var. arctica, commonly known as Arctic sandpink, is a low-growing perennial plant native to Arctic and subarctic regions.

It features dense clusters of pink or white flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is adapted to withstand harsh, cold environments. It thrives in well-drained, sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in coastal areas.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in challenging climates.
