Alocasia putzeysii

Alocasia putzeysii, commonly known as the Black Velvet Alocasia, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, dark green, velvety leaves that have a deep, almost black appearance. The plant features a thick, rhizomatous stem that allows it to spread and form dense foliage. It thrives in humid, warm environments with bright, indirect light.

Due to its unique appearance, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and ornamental purposes.
