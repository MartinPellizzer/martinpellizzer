Auganthus malvaceus

Auganthus malvaceus, commonly known as the velvetleaf.

It is a member of the Malvaceae family, characterized by its showy, pale yellow to orange flowers. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are velvety to the touch, giving it its common name. It is native to tropical and subtropical regions and thrives in warm, humid climates.

Auganthus malvaceus is often found in disturbed soils and can become invasive in certain areas.
