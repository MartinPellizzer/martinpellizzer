Ambrosia linearis

Ambrosia linearis, commonly known as common ragweed, is an annual herbaceous plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its slender, linear leaves and produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that appear in the late summer. The plant is known for its allergenic pollen, which can cause significant respiratory issues in sensitive individuals. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found along roadsides and in agricultural fields.

Ambrosia linearis is a member of the Asteraceae family, which includes many other flowering plants with composite flower heads.
