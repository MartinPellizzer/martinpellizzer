Aristolochia palmeri

Aristolochia palmeri, commonly known as Palmer's Dutchman's pipe, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its unique, pipe-shaped flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with a rough texture and is often found in moist, wooded areas. It belongs to the Aristolochiaceae family and is known for its complex floral structure.

However, it contains toxic compounds and is not suitable for consumption.
