Alternanthera floridana

Alternanthera floridana, commonly known as the Florida beauty or redstem, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its vibrant red or purple stems and leaves that can range from green to red, depending on the variety. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white or yellow flowers that appear in clusters. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and is often found in wetlands or along the edges of water bodies.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and wetland restoration projects.
