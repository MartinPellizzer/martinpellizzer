Amylotheca rubra

Amylotheca rubra, commonly known as the red velvet orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking red and purple flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest understories. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, with a velvety texture that gives it its common name.

Amylotheca rubra is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
