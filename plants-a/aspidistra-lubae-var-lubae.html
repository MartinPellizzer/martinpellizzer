Aspidistra lubae var. lubae

Aspidistra lubae var. lubae, commonly known as the Lubae Aspidistra, is a species of evergreen perennial shrub native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, broad, and lance-shaped leaves that are typically glossy and arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are clustered in spikes, though they are not particularly showy. It thrives in low-light conditions and prefers moist, well-drained soil.

This variety is valued for its ornamental foliage and is often used in indoor gardening due to its adaptability and ease of care.
