Alternanthera herniarioides

Alternanthera herniarioides, commonly known as the red-stemmed alternanthera, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking red stems and lance-shaped leaves that often have a reddish tinge. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This species is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its vibrant foliage and ease of care.
