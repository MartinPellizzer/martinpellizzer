Andinia cardiocheila

Andinia cardiocheila, commonly known as the heartleaf orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its unique, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in cloud forests.

Due to its ornamental value and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
