Alstonia plumosa var. communis

Alstonia plumosa var. communis, commonly known as the white flame tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, feathery, light green foliage that gives it a delicate, airy appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, often followed by ornamental, winged fruits. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a street tree in tropical regions.
