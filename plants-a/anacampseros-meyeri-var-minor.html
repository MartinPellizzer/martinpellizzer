Anacampseros meyeri var. minor

Anacampseros meyeri var. minor, commonly known as the dwarf anacampseros, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features small, rosette-shaped leaves that are typically blue-gray in color, covered in a fine layer of white powder. The plant produces delicate, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or purple during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and container cultivation.
