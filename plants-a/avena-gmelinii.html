Avena gmelinii

Avena gmelinii, commonly known as slender oat, is a herbaceous annual plant native to Eurasia.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences bearing numerous tiny florets. Avena gmelinii is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.

It is sometimes cultivated as a forage crop or used in ecological restoration projects.
