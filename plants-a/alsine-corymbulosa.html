Alsine corymbulosa

Alsine corymbulosa, commonly known as the clustered alsike, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the family Amaranthaceae and is characterized by its small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in moist soils along stream banks and wet meadows. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs.
