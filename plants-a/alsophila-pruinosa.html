Alsophila pruinosa

Alsophila pruinosa, commonly known as the silver tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feathery fronds that can reach several meters in length. The fronds are typically covered in a fine, silvery down, giving the plant its common name. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical forests.

Alsophila pruinosa plays an important ecological role by providing habitat and shelter for various small animals and insects.
