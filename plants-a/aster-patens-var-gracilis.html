Aster patens var. gracilis

Aster patens var. gracilis, commonly known as the slender leaf aster, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and distinctive foliage. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and woodland edges. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, while its flowers are small, daisy-like blooms that range in color from white to pale purple.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, providing nectar for pollinators.
