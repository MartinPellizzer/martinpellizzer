Anthurium alexespinosae

Anthurium alexespinosae, commonly known as the Spiny Anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are often adorned with spines along the margins. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-shaped inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, typical of the Araceae family. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique appearance and ornamental value, it is cultivated as a decorative plant in botanical gardens and private collections.
