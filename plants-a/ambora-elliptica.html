Ambora elliptica

Ambora elliptica, commonly known as the oval ambora, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions.

It features elliptical, leathery leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of white and pink, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Ambora elliptica is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and delicate floral display.
