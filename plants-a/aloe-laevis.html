Aloe laevis

Aloe laevis, commonly known as the smooth aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are smooth and lack the spines found in many other aloe species. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow flowers, which bloom in the spring. Aloe laevis is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its leaves to survive drought conditions.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and low maintenance requirements.
