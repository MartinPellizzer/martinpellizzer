Abutilon flueckigerianum

Abutilon flueckigerianum, commonly known as the African velvetleaf, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its fuzzy, velvety leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or greenish-yellow in color. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in moist woodland or riverine habitats.

Abutilon flueckigerianum is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated as a decorative plant.
