Apicra arachnoidea

Apicra arachnoidea, commonly known as the spider-like orchid, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, spider-shaped inflorescence. The plant features slender, wiry stems and small, delicate flowers with intricate patterns. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a delicately endangered species.
