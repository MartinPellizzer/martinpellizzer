Avena pratensis var. festucoides

Avena pratensis var. festucoides, commonly known as slender oat, is a perennial grass species native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features slender, hairless stems that grow up to 60 cm tall, with narrow, grass-like leaves that are often slightly folded. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences bearing numerous tiny, two-lobed spikelets. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows, pastures, and along stream banks.

Its ecological value includes providing habitat and forage for various insects and livestock.
