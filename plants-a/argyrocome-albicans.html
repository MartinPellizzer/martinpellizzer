Argyrocome albicans

Argyrocome albicans, commonly known as the silver-white mistflower, is a striking plant characterized by its silvery-white foliage and delicate, feathery inflorescences.

The plant's leaves are covered in fine, reflective hairs that give it a shimmering appearance, especially in sunlight. Due to its ornamental value and unique appearance, it is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
