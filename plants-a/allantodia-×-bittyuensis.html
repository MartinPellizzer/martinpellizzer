Allantodia × bittyuensis

Allantodia × bittyuensis, commonly known as the hybrid Allantodia, is a flowering plant belonging to the Acanthaceae family.

It is a natural hybrid between Allantodia corymbosa and Allantodia glabra, exhibiting characteristics of both parent species. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that are typically violet or purple in color, arranged in dense clusters. It is native to regions of Southeast Asia and thrives in tropical and subtropical climates.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
