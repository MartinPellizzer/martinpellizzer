Amethystanthus inflexus var. canescens

Amethystanthus inflexus var. canescens, commonly known as the silverleaf amethyst flower, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, tubular, violet-pink flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has narrow, silver-gray leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a silvery appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

This variant is noted for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
