Adromischus cristatus var. cristatus

Adromischus cristatus var. cristatus, commonly known as the cristate adromischus, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, fleshy leaves that form a rosette shape, often with a distinctive crinkled or crested appearance. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
