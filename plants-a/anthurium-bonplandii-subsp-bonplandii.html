Anthurium bonplandii subsp. bonplandii

Anthurium bonplandii subsp.

bonplandii, commonly known as the Bonpland's anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America. It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a reddish or maroon underside.

The plant produces vibrant, spadix-shaped inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, often in shades of white, yellow, or red. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This species is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation under the right conditions.
