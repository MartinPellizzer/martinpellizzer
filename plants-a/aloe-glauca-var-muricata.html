Aloe glauca var. muricata

Aloe glauca var. muricata, commonly known as the Spiny Aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color with a waxy coating. The leaves are covered in small, spiny tubercles, giving the plant its distinctive appearance. It produces yellow-orange flowers that bloom in summer, adding ornamental value to the plant.

This species is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
