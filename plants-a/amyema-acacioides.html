Amyema acacioides

Amyema acacioides, commonly known as the slender acacia.

It is a shrub native to Australia, characterized by its slender, wiry stems and small, clustered leaves. The plant produces yellowish flowers that are typically arranged in dense, spike-like inflorescences. It is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, showing drought tolerance and efficient water use.

Amyema acacioides is often found in coastal and inland regions, contributing to local biodiversity and ecological stability.
