Areca latisecta

Areca latisecta, commonly known as the broad-leaf areca, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, fan-shaped leaves that can grow up to several feet in length. The plant belongs to the Arecaceae family, which includes palms, and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. Its leaves are segmented and have a distinctive, feathery appearance.

Areca latisecta thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
