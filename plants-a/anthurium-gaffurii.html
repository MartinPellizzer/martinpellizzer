Anthurium gaffurii

Anthurium gaffurii, commonly known as the red-tipped anthurium, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped leaves with a deep red margin. The plant produces striking spadices, which are the characteristic inflorescences of the genus Anthurium. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical landscapes.
