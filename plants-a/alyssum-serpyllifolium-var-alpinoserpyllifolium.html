Alyssum serpyllifolium var. alpinoserpyllifolium

Alyssum serpyllifolium var. alpinoserpyllifolium, commonly known as alpine winter creeper, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It features dense clusters of small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has succulent, fleshy leaves that are typically gray-green in color, adapted to retain moisture in arid environments. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and alpine settings.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to withstand cold temperatures.
