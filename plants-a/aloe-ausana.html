Aloe ausana

Aloe ausana, commonly known as the Cape aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, spiky stems and fleshy, sword-shaped leaves that store water, adapting to arid environments. The plant produces vibrant orange or red flowers in clusters at the top of the stem, typically during the summer months. Aloe ausana is a member of the Asphodelaceae family and is known for its resilience to drought and poor soil conditions.

Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine and for their soothing properties.
