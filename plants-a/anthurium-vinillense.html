Anthurium vinillense

Anthurium vinillense, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a reddish or maroon hue on the underside. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-shaped inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, often in shades of red or orange. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.

Anthurium vinillense is popular as a decorative plant due to its ornamental foliage and long-lasting blooms.
