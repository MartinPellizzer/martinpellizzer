Alsophila mesosora

Alsophila mesosora, commonly known as the narrow-leaf tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and feathery fronds. The plant has a robust root system that helps it thrive in moist, shaded environments. Its fronds are usually dark green and can grow quite long, contributing to its ornamental value.
