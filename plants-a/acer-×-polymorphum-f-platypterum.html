Acer × polymorphum f. platypterum

Acer × polymorphum f. platypterum, commonly known as the Japanese maple with flat leaves, is a unique cultivar of the maple family.

It is characterized by its distinctive, broad, flat leaf shape, which sets it apart from other maple varieties. The plant typically features vibrant red and orange foliage in the fall, making it a popular ornamental choice. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained, acidic soil.

This cultivar is valued for its aesthetic appeal and adaptability to garden settings.
