Adiantum thongthamii

Adiantum thongthamii, commonly known as the Thai maidenhair fern, is a species of fern native to Thailand.

It belongs to the family Adiantaceae and is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds that resemble feathers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its leaves are bioluminescent, a unique trait that helps it attract certain insects.

Adiantum thongthamii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
