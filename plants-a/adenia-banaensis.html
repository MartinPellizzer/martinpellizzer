Adenia banaensis

Adenia banaensis, commonly known as the Banaensis Adenia, is a climbing shrub native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Passifloraceae and is characterized by its woody stems and compound leaves. The plant produces striking flowers with five petals and a distinctive crown of stamens. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Adenia banaensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in some regions.
