Asarum dilatatum

Asarum dilatatum, commonly known as Chinese wild ginger, is a perennial herb native to eastern Asia.

It features a tuberous root system and heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are aromatic. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers in clusters, which are often hidden beneath its foliage. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in forests and wetlands.

Asarum dilatatum is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional Chinese medicine.
