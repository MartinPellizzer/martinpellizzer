Anthurium canasas

Anthurium canasas, commonly known as the velvet leaf anthurium, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are velvety to the touch and come in shades of deep green to dark red. The plant produces vibrant spathes, which are modified leaves that enclose the true flowers, typically in shades of red, orange, or yellow. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium canasas is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in the right conditions.
