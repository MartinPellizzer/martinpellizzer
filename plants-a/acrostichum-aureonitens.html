Acrostichum aureonitens

Acrostichum aureonitens, commonly known as the golden fern, is a species of fern native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, glossy, golden-yellow fronds that are highly ornamental. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories. Its fronds are pinnately compound, with numerous pinnae that contribute to its lush appearance.

Acrostichum aureonitens is valued in horticulture for its striking color and ability to add a tropical flair to gardens.
