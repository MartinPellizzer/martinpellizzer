Ascocentrum miniatum var. garayi

Ascocentrum miniatum var. garayi, commonly known as the miniature arrowroot, is a compact, evergreen herbaceous plant native to the Philippines.

It features a short rhizome with narrow, strap-like leaves that are typically bright green and have a waxy texture. The plant produces small, white or pale green flowers arranged in a spike, followed by berries that mature to a dark purple color. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low light requirements and minimal care needs.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and compact growth habit.
