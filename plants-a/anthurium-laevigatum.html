Anthurium laevigatum

Anthurium laevigatum, commonly known as the velvet leaf anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its broad, dark green, velvety leaves that are often adorned with prominent veins. The plant produces striking spathes that can range in color from white to red, depending on the variety. It thrives in humid, warm environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.

Anthurium laevigatum is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and air-purifying properties.
