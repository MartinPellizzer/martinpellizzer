Alocasia variegata

Alocasia variegata, commonly known as the Variegated Elephant Ear, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white, yellow, and green patterns. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soil and requires high humidity and indirect light to flourish. Its foliage adds dramatic visual interest to both indoor and outdoor gardens.

Alocasia variegata is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for plant enthusiasts.
