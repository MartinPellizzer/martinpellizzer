Ammocharis nerinoides

Ammocharis nerinoides, commonly known as the pink-flowered clivias, is a bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features striking pink or red flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. The plant has strap-like, evergreen leaves that are often used in ornamental gardening. It prefers well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.
