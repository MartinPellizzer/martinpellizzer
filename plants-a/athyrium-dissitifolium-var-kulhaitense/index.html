Athyrium Dissitifolium Var. Kulhaitense

Kulhait fern, scientifically known as Athyrium dissitifolium var. kulhaitense, is a rare and captivating species native to the lush, humid forests of northeastern India. This striking fern is characterized by its delicate fronds that unfurl in a spiral pattern, creating a visually mesmerizing display. It thrives in shaded, moist environments, often found growing along forest floors or on rocky outcrops. Due to its limited distribution and fragile habitat, the Kulhait fern is considered a vulnerable species facing threats from deforestation and habitat degradation. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this unique plant for future generations.
