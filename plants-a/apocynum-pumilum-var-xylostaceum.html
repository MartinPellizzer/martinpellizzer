Apocynum pumilum var. xylostaceum

Apocynum pumilum var. xylostaceum, commonly known as the small milkweed or dwarf milkweed, is a perennial herb native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit with oval, slightly hairy leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in the summer, followed by capsule fruits containing seeds with silky hairs. It is part of the Apocynaceae family, known for its milky sap and showy flowers.

This variety is often found in disturbed soils and is valued for its role in supporting pollinators.
