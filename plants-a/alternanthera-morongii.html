Alternanthera morongii

Alternanthera morongii, commonly known as the red-stemmed Alternanthera, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red stems and lance-shaped leaves that can vary in color from green to red or purple. This plant thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its striking foliage and colorful stems.

Its growth habit is typically compact, making it suitable for container gardening or as a border plant.
