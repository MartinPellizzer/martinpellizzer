Aloe spirella

Aloe spirella, commonly known as the spiral aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking spiral arrangement of fleshy, bluish-green leaves that form a dense rosette. The plant produces tall, branched flower stalks that bear yellowish-green flowers, which bloom in late summer. Aloe spirella is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil with plenty of sunlight.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and as a decorative houseplant.
