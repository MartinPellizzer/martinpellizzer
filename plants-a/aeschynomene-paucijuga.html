Aeschynomene paucijuga

Aeschynomene paucijuga, commonly known as smallleaf burr sage, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its slender, often hairy stems and small, opposite leaves. The plant produces yellowish to greenish flowers that are clustered in racemes, followed by small, woody pods containing several seeds.
