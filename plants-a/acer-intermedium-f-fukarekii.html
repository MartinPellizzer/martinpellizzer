Acer intermedium f. fukarekii

Acer intermedium f. fukarekii, commonly known as the Fukarekii maple, is a unique variant of the intermediate maple.

It is characterized by its distinctive foliage, featuring leaves with a vibrant green upper surface and a striking red or purple underside. The plant exhibits a moderate growth rate and typically reaches a height of around 6 to 10 meters. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained, fertile soil.

This ornamental tree is valued for its aesthetic appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and parks for its seasonal color changes.
