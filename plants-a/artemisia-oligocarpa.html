Artemisia oligocarpa

Artemisia oligocarpa, commonly known as silverleaf sage, is a perennial shrub native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, dense hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flower heads that appear in late summer to early fall. Artemisia oligocarpa is often found in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to arid environments.

It is valued for its aromatic properties and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
