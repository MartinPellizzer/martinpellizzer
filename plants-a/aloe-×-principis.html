Aloe × principis

Aloe × principis, commonly known as the hybrid aloe or hybrid aloe, is a naturally occurring hybrid between Aloe vera and Aloe arborescens.

It is a succulent plant native to South Africa, characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are thick and fleshy. The plant produces yellow-orange flowers in clusters during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for both ornamental and medicinal purposes.
