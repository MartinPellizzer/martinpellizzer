Anthyllis vulneraria f. petraea

Anthyllis vulneraria f. petraea, commonly known as the rock everlasting, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to arid and rocky environments.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its small, yellow flowers and narrow, evergreen leaves. The plant is adapted to harsh conditions, with deep root systems that help it survive in poor soils. It thrives in sunny, well-drained habitats such as limestone cliffs and rocky slopes.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to stabilize soil in difficult environments.
