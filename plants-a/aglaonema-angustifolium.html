Aglaonema angustifolium

Aglaonema angustifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaf Chinese evergreen, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated foliage with deep green and silver-white patterns. The leaves are typically narrow and elongated, giving the plant a sleek, elegant appearance. It thrives in low-light conditions and requires moderate humidity to flourish.

This plant is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and air-purifying properties.
