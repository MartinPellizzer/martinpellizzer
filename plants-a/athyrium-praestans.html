Athyrium praestans

Athyrium praestans, commonly known as the shining male fern, is a species of fern native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, arching fronds that are covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and stream sides. Its sporangia are clustered in sori on the underside of the fronds, a common reproductive feature in ferns.

Athyrium praestans is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its elegant foliage.
