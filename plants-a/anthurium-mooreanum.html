Anthurium mooreanum

Anthurium mooreanum, commonly known as the Moorean anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the island of Moorea in French Polynesia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a glossy texture. The plant produces striking spadix flowers surrounded by a colorful spathe, often in shades of red or orange. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium mooreanum is popular as a decorative plant due to its ornamental foliage and unique floral structure.
