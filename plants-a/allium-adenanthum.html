Allium adenanthum

Allium adenanthum, commonly known as the rock onion, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a bulbous perennial that produces clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is adapted to grow in rocky or sandy soils. It is often found in dry, sunny habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal.

Allium adenanthum is also used in traditional herbal medicine for its purported health benefits.
