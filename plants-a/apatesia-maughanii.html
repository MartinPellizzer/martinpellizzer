Apatesia maughanii

Apatesia maughanii, commonly known as the Maughan's ghost orchid, is a rare and enigmatic flowering plant native to the tropical rainforests of Queensland, Australia.

It belongs to the orchid family (Orchidaceae) and is characterized by its delicate, translucent leaves and striking, ghostly white flowers. The plant is epiphytic, meaning it grows on the surface of trees or rocks without deriving nutrients from them.
