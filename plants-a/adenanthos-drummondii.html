Adenanthos drummondii

Adenanthos drummondii, commonly known as the Drummond's adenanthos, is a flowering plant native to Western Australia.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 meter, featuring narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are usually pink or red in color, blooming from late autumn to early spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy environments.

Adenanthos drummondii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
