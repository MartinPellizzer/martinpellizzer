Ardisia dissitiflora

Ardisia dissitiflora, commonly known as the hairy-flowered evergreen, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are opposite and often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its fruit is a bright red, fleshy berry that contains several seeds.

Ardisia dissitiflora is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to shaded environments.
