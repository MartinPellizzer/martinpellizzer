Arum orientale var. stevenii

Arum orientale var. stevenii, commonly known as the Steven's arrowhead, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features large, arrow-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a metallic sheen. The plant produces striking, spadix-like inflorescences surrounded by a showy, purple spathe. It thrives in humid, warm environments and requires well-draining, rich soil.

This variant is prized for its ornamental value in both indoor and outdoor gardens.
