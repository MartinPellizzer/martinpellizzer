Atacamallium minutiflorum

Atacamallium minutiflorum, commonly known as the Atacama lily, is a rare flowering plant native to the arid regions of Chile.

The plant has adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments with minimal water, possessing a deep root system to access underground moisture.
