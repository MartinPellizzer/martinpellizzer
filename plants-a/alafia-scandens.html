Alafia scandens

Alafia scandens, commonly known as the climbing alafia, is a tropical vine native to regions of South America.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in clusters, often followed by yellowish berries. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Alafia scandens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
