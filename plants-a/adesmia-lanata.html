Adesmia lanata

Adesmia lanata, commonly known as the hairy adesmia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 3 meters in height. The plant features simple, opposite leaves that are often covered in fine, white hairs, giving it a fuzzy appearance. Its flowers are small and typically white or pale pink, arranged in clusters.

Adesmia lanata is adapted to dry and arid environments, making it a resilient species in its native habitat.
