Aglaonema schottianum

Aglaonema schottianum, commonly known as the silver queen plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated foliage with white and green patterns on its lance-shaped leaves. The plant belongs to the Araceae family, which includes other ornamental plants like peace lilies and calla lilies. It thrives in low-light conditions and requires moderate humidity and well-draining soil.

Aglaonema schottianum is popular as a houseplant due to its aesthetic appeal and air-purifying properties.
