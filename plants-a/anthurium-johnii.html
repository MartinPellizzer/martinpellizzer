Anthurium johnii

Anthurium johnii, commonly known as the "Cobra Lily," is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length, which are typically a deep green with a velvety texture. The plant produces vibrant red spathes that enclose its spadix, a unique inflorescence structure typical of the Araceae family. Anthurium johnii thrives in high humidity and indirect light, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its botanical features make it both an ornamental and ecologically significant species in its native habitat.
