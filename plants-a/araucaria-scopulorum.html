Araucaria scopulorum

Araucaria scopulorum, commonly known as the mountain araucaria, is a coniferous tree native to southern South America.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and dense, pyramidal crown. The plant has needle-like leaves that are typically arranged in whorls around the stem. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Its cones are small and woody, containing seeds that are dispersed by wind.
