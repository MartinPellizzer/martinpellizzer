Astilbe rivularis var. myriantha

Astilbe rivularis var. myriantha, commonly known as the river astilbe, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to eastern Asia.

It features upright, branched stems that bear clusters of small, fragrant, white to pink flowers. The plant has finely divided, dark green, fern-like leaves that are glossy and provide a striking contrast to the flowers. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found near streams or in wetlands.

Astilbe rivularis var. myriantha is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and ability to add texture and color to woodland settings.
