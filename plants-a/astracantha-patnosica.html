Astracantha patnosica

Astracantha patnosica, commonly known as the Patnosic thornveld, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid regions. Its leaves are usually narrow and covered in a fine layer of white hairs, which help reduce water loss.

The flowers, which bloom in spring, are small and yellow, arranged in a cluster that resembles a daisy.
