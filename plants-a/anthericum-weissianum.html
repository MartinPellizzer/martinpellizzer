Anthericum weissianum

Anthericum weissianum, commonly known as the Weissianum arum, is a herbaceous plant native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive inflorescence consisting of a spadix surrounded by a modified leaf called a spathe. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are typically a deep green color. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, with the showy spathe serving as an attractant for pollinators.

Anthericum weissianum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in shaded garden settings.
