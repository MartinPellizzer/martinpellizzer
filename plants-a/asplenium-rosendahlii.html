Asplenium rosendahlii

Asplenium rosendahlii, commonly known as the Rosendahl's spleenwort, is a small, epiphytic fern native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It typically grows on tree trunks or rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant has delicate, lance-shaped leaves that are often clustered closely together. Its fronds are usually dark green and have a soft, velvety texture.

Asplenium rosendahlii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or terrariums.
