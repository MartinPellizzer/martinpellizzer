Aloe ellenbergeri

Aloe ellenbergeri, commonly known as the Desert Aloe, is a succulent plant native to arid regions of Africa.

It features fleshy, spiky leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in dry conditions. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers during the warmer months, typically in spring or summer. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their soothing properties.

Aloe ellenbergeri is well-suited for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its low water requirements.
