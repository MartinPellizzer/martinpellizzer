Alstroemeria inodora

Alstroemeria inodora, commonly known as the sweet pepper plant, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the family Alstroemeriaceae and is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant typically grows as a herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are lance-shaped and slightly hairy, while its stems are upright and branching.

Alstroemeria inodora is valued in horticulture for its ornamental flowers and ability to thrive in a range of soil conditions.
