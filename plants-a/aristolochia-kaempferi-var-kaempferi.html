Aristolochia kaempferi var. kaempferi

Aristolochia kaempferi var. kaempferi, commonly known as the Japanese arrowhead vine, is a climbing plant native to Japan.

It features heart-shaped, glossy leaves and produces striking, pipe-shaped flowers that are typically purple or greenish-white. The plant belongs to the Aristolochiaceae family and is characterized by its unique floral morphology. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often used in ornamental horticulture.

However, it contains toxic compounds and should be handled with care.
