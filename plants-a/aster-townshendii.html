Aster townshendii

Aster townshendii, commonly known as the Townsend's aster, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads composed of numerous small florets. This perennial herb typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. Its blue to purple flowers bloom in the summer, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies.

The plant has narrow, linear leaves and is often found in disturbed or arid environments.
