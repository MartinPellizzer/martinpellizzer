Anthyllis vulneraria var. langei

Anthyllis vulneraria var. langei, commonly known as the Lange's burnet, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its compact, mat-forming growth habit and is native to specific regions of southern Africa. The plant produces small, pink to violet flowers arranged in dense clusters, which bloom during the summer months. Its leaves are trifoliate, consisting of three small, hairy leaflets, and the plant is often found in rocky or gravelly soils.
