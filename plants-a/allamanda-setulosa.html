Allamanda setulosa

Allamanda setulosa, commonly known as the hairy allamanda, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 6 meters in height, characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces large, showy yellow flowers with a prominent, prominent orange corona. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
