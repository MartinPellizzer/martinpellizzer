Aethionema annuum

Aethionema annuum, commonly known as the winter cress, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces small, yellow flowers in clusters during the spring and summer. The plant has alternate, pinnately divided leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.
