Angelonia spectabilis

Angelonia spectabilis, commonly known as the summer snapdragon, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its spikes of tubular, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, pink, and white. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves and is often grown as an ornamental in gardens and landscapes. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for borders and containers.

Angelonia spectabilis is known for its long blooming period, typically from late spring through early fall.
