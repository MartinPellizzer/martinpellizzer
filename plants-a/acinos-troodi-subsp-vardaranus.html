Acinos troodi subsp. vardaranus

Acinos troodi subsp.

vardaranus, commonly known as the Vardaran alpine bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Caucasus. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy yellow flowers.

The plant typically grows in rocky and alpine meadows, thriving in cold, dry environments. Its leaves are narrow and hairy, adapted to reduce water loss in harsh conditions.
