Armeria berlengensis var. gracilis

Armeria berlengensis var. gracilis, commonly known as the slender sea thrift, is a low-growing evergreen plant native to the coastal regions of the Azores.

It features dense clusters of small, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has fleshy, succulent leaves that are typically blue-gray in color, adapted to withstand salty sea air and coastal conditions. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy ornamental plant for coastal gardens.

Its compact growth habit and ability to tolerate harsh maritime climates make it a valuable addition to rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
