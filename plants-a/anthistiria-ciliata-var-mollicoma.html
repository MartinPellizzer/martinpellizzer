Anthistiria ciliata var. mollicoma

Anthistiria ciliata var. mollicoma, commonly known as the soft-hairy anthistiria, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its hairy, glandular leaves and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its unique morphology and ornamental value make it a sought-after species in horticulture.

It is also noted for its ability to withstand dry conditions, making it relatively drought-tolerant.
