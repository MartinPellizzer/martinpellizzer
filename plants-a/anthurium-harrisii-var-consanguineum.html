Anthurium harrisii var. consanguineum

Anthurium harrisii var. consanguineum, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces vibrant red spathes that enclose small, spadix-like inflorescences, which are typical of the Araceae family. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and ornamental horticulture.
