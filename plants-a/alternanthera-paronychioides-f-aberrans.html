Alternanthera paronychioides f. aberrans

Alternanthera paronychioides f. aberrans, commonly known as the red-veined wonder, is a striking ornamental plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, red-veined leaves that contrast beautifully with its deep green foliage. The plant belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is valued for its ornamental appeal in aquatic and terrestrial gardens. It typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 30 cm.

Its unique leaf patterns make it a popular choice for water gardens and container planting.
