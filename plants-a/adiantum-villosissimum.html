Adiantum villosissimum

Adiantum villosissimum, commonly known as the velvet fern, is a delicate fern species native to South America.

It features intricate, lace-like fronds that are covered in fine, soft hairs, giving it a velvety texture. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in rocky or forested areas. Its rhizomes are creeping and produce clusters of small, greenish-brown spore-bearing structures.

Adiantum villosissimum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
