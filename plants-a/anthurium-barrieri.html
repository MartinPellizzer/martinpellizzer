Anthurium barrieri

Anthurium barrieri, commonly known as the red-tipped anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are dark green with a reddish-brown margin. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-shaped inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, typically in shades of white, red, or yellow. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium barrieri is a popular ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
