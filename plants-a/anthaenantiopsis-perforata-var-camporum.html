Anthaenantiopsis perforata var. camporum

Anthaenantiopsis perforata var. camporum, commonly known as the Camp's perforate antelope plant, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

Its unique floral structure and ornamental value make it a popular choice for rock gardens and arid landscapes.
