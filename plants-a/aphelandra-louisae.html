Aphelandra louisae

Aphelandra louisae, commonly known as the golden torch plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the Caribbean.

It is characterized by its striking, elongated leaves that are dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, yellow-orange flowers that bloom throughout the year. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

Aphelandra louisae is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its vibrant foliage and showy blooms.
