Abutilon englerianum

Abutilon englerianum, commonly known as the Engler's abutilon, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 3 meters in height. The plant produces showy, lantern-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. Its leaves are heart-shaped and have a velvety texture, while its bark is smooth and grayish.

It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.
