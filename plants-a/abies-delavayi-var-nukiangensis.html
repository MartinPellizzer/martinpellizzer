Abies delavayi var. nukiangensis

Abies delavayi var. nukiangensis, commonly known as the Nukiang fir, is a variant of the Delavay fir native to the eastern Himalayas.

It is a coniferous evergreen tree characterized by its pyramidal shape and needle-like leaves. The foliage is typically dark green, with a slightly bluish tinge, and the cones are cylindrical and reddish-brown when mature. This species thrives in cool, moist climates at high altitudes, often found in mountainous regions of China.

Its botanical significance lies in its adaptation to alpine environments and its role in local ecosystems.
