Asplenium warmingii

Asplenium warmingii, commonly known as the Warming's spleenwort, is a small, epiphytic fern belonging to the family Aspleniaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, strap-like leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant is native to tropical and subtropical regions, often growing on rocks or tree bark in shaded environments. Its fronds are usually dark green and have a leathery texture, with spores produced on the undersides of the leaves.

Asplenium warmingii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various growing conditions.
