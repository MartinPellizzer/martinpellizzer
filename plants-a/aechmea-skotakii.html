Aechmea skotakii

Aechmea skotakii, commonly known as the Skotak's pineapple bromeliad, is a striking epiphytic plant native to the cloud forests of Costa Rica.

The plant produces a tall flower stalk with vibrant red bracts, which are often mistaken for flowers. Its leaves are typically dark green with white or silver markings, enhancing its ornamental appeal.

Aechmea skotakii thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
