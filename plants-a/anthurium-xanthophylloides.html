Anthurium xanthophylloides

Anthurium xanthophylloides, commonly known as the golden leaf anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, waxy, golden-yellow spathe that contrasts beautifully with its dark green spadix. The plant thrives in high humidity and indirect light, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening. Its foliage is broad and leathery, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Anthurium xanthophylloides is known for its ability to purify the air, adding to its value as a houseplant.
