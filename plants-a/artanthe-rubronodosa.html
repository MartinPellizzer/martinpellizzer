Artanthe rubronodosa

Artanthe rubronodosa, commonly known as the red-node artanthe, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It belongs to the family Amaranthaceae and is characterized by its striking, showy inflorescences that range from pink to red in color. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in full sun conditions. Its foliage is often covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive textured appearance.

Artanthe rubronodosa is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
