Alseodaphne nigrescens

Alseodaphne nigrescens, commonly known as the black alseodaphne, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, including parts of Indonesia, Malaysia, and the Philippines. This evergreen tree can grow up to 20 meters tall and is characterized by its dark, glossy leaves and aromatic bark. The plant produces small, white flowers followed by dark, berry-like fruits.

It thrives in tropical rainforests and is often found in secondary growth areas.
