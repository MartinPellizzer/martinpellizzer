Acourtia Dugesii Var. Queretarana

Acourtia dugesii var. queretarana is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the arid regions of central Mexico. It is known for its small, pale yellow blooms that appear during the spring season. This variety of Acourtia thrives in rocky soils and requires minimal water to survive. Due to its limited distribution and habitat loss, it is considered endangered by conservationists. Protecting this unique species is crucial for preserving biodiversity in its native environment.
