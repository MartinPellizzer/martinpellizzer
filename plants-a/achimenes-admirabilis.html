Achimenes admirabilis

Achimenes admirabilis, commonly known as the flame violet, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant features heart-shaped, velvety leaves and thrives in warm, humid environments. It is often cultivated as a decorative plant for its vibrant blooms and foliage.

Achimenes admirabilis prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
