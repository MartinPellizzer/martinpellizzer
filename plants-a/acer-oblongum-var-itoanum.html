Acer oblongum var. itoanum

Acer oblongum var. itoanum, commonly known as the Itoanum maple, is a deciduous tree native to Japan.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves that turn vibrant red and orange in the fall. The plant has a compact growth habit with a rounded canopy, making it suitable for smaller gardens. Its flowers are small and green, appearing in early spring before the leaves emerge.

The bark is smooth and gray, with a tendency to flake as the tree matures.
