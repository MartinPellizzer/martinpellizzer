Astragalus Afyonicus

Astragalus afyonicus is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Turkey. It belongs to the legume family, known for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil. This species is characterized by its small, pale purple flowers and finely divided leaves. Due to habitat loss and limited distribution, it is considered endangered. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this unique botanical gem.
