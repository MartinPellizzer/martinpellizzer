Anthurium curvatum

Anthurium curvatum, commonly known as the curled anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves and spadix inflorescence. The plant thrives in humid, warm environments with indirect light, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation. Its foliage is often a deep green with a waxy texture, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers.

Anthurium curvatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to care for with proper watering and soil conditions.
