Agalmyla stellifera

Agalmyla stellifera, commonly known as the star-flowered agalmyla, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces clusters of small, star-shaped white or pale blue flowers. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Agalmyla stellifera is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
