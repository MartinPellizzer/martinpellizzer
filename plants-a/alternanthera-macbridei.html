Alternanthera macbridei

Alternanthera macbridei, commonly known as the red-stemmed Alternanthera, is a striking aquatic plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red stems and lance-shaped leaves that can vary in color depending on light exposure. This plant thrives in moderate to high light conditions and is often cultivated in freshwater aquariums. It prefers warm water temperatures and moderate to high water flow.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for both hobbyist aquarists and botanical gardens.
