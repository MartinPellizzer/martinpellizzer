Ardisia henryi var. dielsii

Ardisia henryi var. dielsii, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen or red-rooted ardisia, is a species native to eastern Asia, particularly China.

It is a evergreen shrub characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of small, white flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant produces bright red berries that are an important food source for various bird species. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
