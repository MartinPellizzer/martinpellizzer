Acetosella Bakeriana

Acetosella bakeriana is a rare and delicate plant native to the high elevations of the Pacific Northwest. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in shaded forest floors. This small flowering plant is known for its unique violet-colored flowers and its role in local ecosystems. Due to habitat loss, it is considered endangered and requires conservation efforts. Its beauty and ecological importance make it a subject of both scientific study and botanical admiration.
