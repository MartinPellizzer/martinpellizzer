Agathosma cedrimontana

Agathosma cedrimontana, commonly known as the Cape snowbush, is a perennial shrub native to the Western Cape region of South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and small, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in spring. The plant thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and is adapted to dry, sunny conditions. It is often used in xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements.

The leaves are aromatic, containing essential oils that contribute to its use in traditional medicine and aromatherapy.
