Asplenium fujianense

Asplenium fujianense, commonly known as the Fujian spleenwort, is a small, evergreen fern native to China.

It belongs to the family Aspleniaceae and is characterized by its compact, dense foliage. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in rocky or forested areas. Its fronds are usually narrow and elongated, with a dark green color and a leathery texture.

Asplenium fujianense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
