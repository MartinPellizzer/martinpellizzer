Abronia latiuscula

Abronia latiuscula, commonly known as the narrow-leaved sandverbena, is a low-growing annual herb native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It typically thrives in sandy or gravelly soils, often found in arid or semi-arid environments. The plant has small, narrow leaves and produces clusters of tiny, white or pale blue flowers. It is adapted to withstand drought conditions and is often found in disturbed or open habitats.

Its botanical features make it a characteristic species of desert and coastal scrub ecosystems.
