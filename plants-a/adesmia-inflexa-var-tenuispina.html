Adesmia inflexa var. tenuispina

Adesmia inflexa var. tenuispina, commonly known as the slender spined adesmia, is a flowering plant native to parts of South America.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its small, delicate leaves and distinctive spiny stems. The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid environments.
