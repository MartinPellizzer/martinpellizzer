Agrostophyllum loheri, commonly known as the narrow-leaved rockheath.

The plant is characterized by its small, narrow leaves and clusters of white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It is often found in coastal and mountainous regions, adapting well to harsh environmental conditions.
