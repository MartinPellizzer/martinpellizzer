Aneilema scapiflorum var. minus

Aneilema scapiflorum var. minus, commonly known as the small-flowered aneilema, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical Africa.

It features erect, branching stems and lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged alternately along the stem. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its botanical characteristics include a fibrous root system and a preference for moist, well-drained soils.

It is often found in shaded or semi-shaded areas within humid forest environments.
