Asplenium gedeanum

Asplenium gedeanum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf rock fern, is a species of fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Aspleniaceae and is characterized by its compact, evergreen foliage. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as rock crevices or forest floors. Its fronds are usually narrow and elongated, with a leathery texture.

Asplenium gedeanum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in horticultural settings.
