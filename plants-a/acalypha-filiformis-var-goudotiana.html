Acalypha filiformis var. goudotiana

Acalypha filiformis var. goudotiana, commonly known as the silky filaments or silky acalypha, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, thread-like leaves that give it a feathery appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are often overlooked, but it is the unique foliage that makes it stand out. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Its ornamental value lies primarily in its soft, silky leaves, which can be used in ornamental gardening or as a houseplant.
