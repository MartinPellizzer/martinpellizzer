Azorella Hydrocotyloides

Azorella hydrocotyloides is a unique and resilient plant native to the high-altitude regions of South America. It thrives in harsh, cold environments where few other species can survive. This plant is known for its distinctive rosette shape and ability to store water in its thick, fleshy leaves. It plays an important role in its ecosystem by stabilizing soil and providing habitat for small organisms. Its adaptability makes it a fascinating subject for botanical study.
