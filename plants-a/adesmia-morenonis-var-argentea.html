Adesmia morenonis var. argentea

Adesmia morenonis var. argentea, commonly known as silverleaf adesmia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, clustered white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a landscape plant.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its unique leaf color and compact growth habit.
