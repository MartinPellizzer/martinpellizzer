Almutaster

Almutaster, commonly known as the desert star, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

The plant has a low, spreading growth habit with silvery-gray foliage that helps reduce water loss in hot, dry environments. Almutaster thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
