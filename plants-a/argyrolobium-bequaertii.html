Argyrolobium bequaertii

Argyrolobium bequaertii, commonly known as the silver globular pea, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive spherical pods that contain several seeds. The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in coastal areas. It produces small, white or pale yellow flowers during the winter months.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological significance, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
