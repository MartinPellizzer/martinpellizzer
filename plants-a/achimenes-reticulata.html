Achimenes reticulata

Achimenes reticulata, commonly known as the flame violet, is a tropical perennial plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, orange, and yellow. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with a hairy texture and thrives in warm, humid environments. It is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its vibrant blooms and ability to produce multiple flowering cycles.

Achimenes reticulata prefers well-draining soil and partial to full sunlight for optimal growth.
