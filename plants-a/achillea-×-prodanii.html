Achillea × prodanii

Achillea × prodanii, commonly known as the hybrid yarrow, is a perennial flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is a naturally occurring hybrid between Achillea millefolium (common yarrow) and Achillea ptarmica (sneezeweed). The plant is characterized by its finely divided, feathery leaves and clusters of small, pale yellow to white flowers. It typically grows in dry, sunny habitats such as meadows and rocky slopes.

Achillea × prodanii is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in herbal medicine.
