Asterostigma dracontium

Asterostigma dracontium, commonly known as the dragon's shadow aster, is a rare and enigmatic plant native to the dense, humid forests of Southeast Asia.

The plant has a unique growth habit, with its leaves arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem, enhancing its aesthetic appeal. Its scientific name reflects both its star-like flower clusters and its association with dragons in local folklore.
