Asplenium nitens

Asplenium nitens, commonly known as the silver maidenhair fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Aspleniaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, slender fronds that are typically covered in a silvery or pale green hue. Native to tropical and subtropical regions, this plant thrives in humid, shaded environments. The fronds are pinnately compound, with small, ovate pinnae that are often adorned with fine, silky hairs.

Asplenium nitens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in botanical gardens.
