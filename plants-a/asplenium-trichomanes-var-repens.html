Asplenium trichomanes var. repens

Asplenium trichomanes var. repens, commonly known as the creeping spleenwort, is a fern that belongs to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is a small, mat-forming plant that typically grows on rocks or soil in moist, shaded environments. This variety is notable for its ability to thrive in rocky or crevice habitats, making it a common sight in forested areas.
