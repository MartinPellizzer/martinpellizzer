Aristea cyanea

Aristea cyanea, commonly known as the blue aristea, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit. The plant produces showy blue to violet flower heads that bloom in spring and summer. Its leaves are narrow, grass-like, and arranged in a rosette pattern.

Aristea cyanea is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
