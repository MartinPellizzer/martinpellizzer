Aloe striata subsp. komaggasensis

Aloe striata subsp.

komaggasensis, commonly known as the Komaggasensis Aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa. It features rosettes of fleshy, gray-green leaves that are often covered in fine white hairs.

The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow to orange flowers during the summer months. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Its compact size and ornamental appearance make it a popular choice for both garden cultivation and horticultural collections.
