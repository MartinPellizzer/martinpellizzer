Aechmea bromeliifolia var. angustispica

Aechmea bromeliifolia var. angustispica, commonly known as the narrow-spurred aechmea, is a striking bromeliad native to South America.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that form a central cup, which collects rainwater for the plant's use. The leaves are typically green with a waxy coating, and they often have a reddish or pinkish hue along their edges. This variety produces a tall flower spike with vibrant red or orange flowers that bloom in the summer months.

It is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.
