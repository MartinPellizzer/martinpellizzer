Astragalus androssowii

Astragalus androssowii, commonly known as the Androssow's milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow or orange flowers. The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments, adapting to harsh climatic conditions. It has small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind or animals.
