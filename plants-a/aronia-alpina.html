Aronia alpina

Aronia alpina, commonly known as the alpine chokeberry, is a deciduous shrub native to the mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

It typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, thriving in cool, moist conditions. The plant produces small, dark red berries that are rich in antioxidants and nutrients. Its leaves are elliptical with serrated edges, and it has a dense, bushy growth habit.

Aronia alpina is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential medicinal properties.
