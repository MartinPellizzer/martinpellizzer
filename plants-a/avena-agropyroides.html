Avena agropyroides

Avena agropyroides, commonly known as slender oat, is a flowering plant belonging to the grass family Poaceae.

It is native to South America and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny, two-lobed flowers. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in open fields or along roadsides.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
