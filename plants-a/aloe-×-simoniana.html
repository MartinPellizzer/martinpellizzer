Aloe × simoniana

Aloe × simoniana, commonly known as the hybrid aloe or the hybrid aloe plant, is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Aloe vera and Aloe arborescens.

It is a succulent plant characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are fleshy and covered in a whitish, powdery coating called bloom. The plant produces bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. It is native to South Africa and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and medicinal properties.

Aloe × simoniana is relatively low maintenance and thrives in well-draining soil and full sun.
