Aglaonema nanum

Aglaonema nanum, commonly known as the dwarf Chinese evergreen, is a compact, evergreen perennial plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features striking, variegated foliage with shades of green, white, and pink, making it a popular ornamental plant. The leaves are typically elliptical or ovate, with a waxy texture and a smooth, glossy surface. It prefers indirect light and well-draining soil, making it ideal for indoor cultivation.

Aglaonema nanum is relatively low-maintenance and resistant to common pests, contributing to its widespread use in interior landscaping.
