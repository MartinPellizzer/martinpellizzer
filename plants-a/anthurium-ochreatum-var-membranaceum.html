Anthurium ochreatum var. membranaceum

Anthurium ochreatum var. membranaceum, commonly known as the velvet leaf anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are velvety to the touch and often display a deep green to reddish-brown coloration. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-shaped inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, typically in shades of red, orange, or yellow. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is popular among indoor gardeners for its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
