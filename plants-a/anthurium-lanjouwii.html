Anthurium lanjouwii

Anthurium lanjouwii, commonly known as the Lanjouw anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Indonesia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped leaves and striking spadix inflorescence. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often found in forest understories. Its foliage is dark green with a glossy texture, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers surrounded by a colorful spathe.

Anthurium lanjouwii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in botanical gardens.
