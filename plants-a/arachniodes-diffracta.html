Arachniodes diffracta

Arachniodes diffracta, commonly known as the spider fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to Southeast Asia.

It features delicate, lacy fronds that resemble spider webs, giving it its common name. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit, allowing it to spread and form dense clumps. Its leaves are bipinnate, with intricate divisions that contribute to its ornamental appeal.

Arachniodes diffracta thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often cultivated as a decorative plant in tropical gardens.
