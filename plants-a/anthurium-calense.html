Anthurium calense

Anthurium calense, commonly known as the velvet leaf anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically deep green with a velvety texture. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-shaped inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, often in shades of red, orange, or yellow. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.

Anthurium calense is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
