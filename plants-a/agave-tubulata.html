Agave tubulata

Agave tubulata, commonly known as the tubular agave, is a striking succulent native to Mexico.

It features a rosette of thick, blue-green leaves with white tubercles that give it its name. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with tubular flowers that range from yellow to red. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice for rock gardens and arid landscapes.
