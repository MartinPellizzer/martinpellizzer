Anthurium anchicayense

Anthurium anchicayense, commonly known as the Anchicayense arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to Ecuador.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are often bright green with a waxy texture. The plant produces an inflorescence consisting of a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe, which can range from white to red or yellow. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Anthurium anchicayense is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated as a houseplant.
