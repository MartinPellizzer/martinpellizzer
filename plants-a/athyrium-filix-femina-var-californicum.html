Athyrium filix-femina var. californicum

Athyrium filix-femina var. californicum, commonly known as the California lady fern, is a variant of the lady fern native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its delicate, bipinnate fronds that are often arching and gracefully drooping. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are typically bright green and have a fine, feathery texture.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appearance and ability to thrive in partial shade.
