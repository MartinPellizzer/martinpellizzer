Astragalus polybotrys

Astragalus polybotrys, commonly known as the Chinese milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to China and parts of Asia.

It is a member of the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and showy, yellowish-white flowers. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Astragalus polybotrys is also valued in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.
