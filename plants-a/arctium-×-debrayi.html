Arctium × debrayi

Arctium × debrayi, commonly known as the hairy burdock, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, heart-shaped leaves with a rough, hairy texture. The plant produces fluffy, spiky seed heads that are adapted for wind dispersal. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in meadows, roadsides, and waste areas.

Due to its hardy nature, it is considered an invasive species in some regions.
