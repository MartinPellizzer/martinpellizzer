Aloe mitriformis var. spinosior

Aloe mitriformis var. spinosior, commonly known as the spiny aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, with a spiny margin that gives it its common name. The plant produces clusters of yellow flowers during the summer months, which are borne on tall, branched stalks. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it suitable for arid or semi-arid environments.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
