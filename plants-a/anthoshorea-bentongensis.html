Anthoshorea bentongensis

Anthoshorea bentongensis, commonly known as the Bentong orchid, is a rare and endemic flowering plant found exclusively in the highland areas of Malaysia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and is typically found in montane forests. Its unique morphology and limited distribution make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered vulnerable and requires protection to ensure its survival.
