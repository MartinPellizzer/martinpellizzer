Aeschynanthus chorisepalus

Aeschynanthus chorisepalus, commonly known as the rainbow windowsill plant, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and vibrant, tubular flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped blooms that are often used in ornamental gardening. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Aeschynanthus chorisepalus is popular for its ability to bloom continuously throughout the year under proper care.
