Alpinia stachyodes var. yangchunensis

Alpinia stachyodes var. yangchunensis, commonly known as the Yangchun ginger or Chinese ginger, is a perennial herb native to southern China.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes other economically important plants like ginger and turmeric. The plant is characterized by its aromatic, rhizomatous growth and lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.
