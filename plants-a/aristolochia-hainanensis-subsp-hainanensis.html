Aristolochia hainanensis subsp. hainanensis

Aristolochia hainanensis subsp.

hainanensis, commonly known as Hainan arrowroot, is a flowering plant native to China. It belongs to the Aristolochiaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, tubular, and often fragrant flowers.

The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces fruits that are typically green and turn yellow when mature. It is primarily found in the mountainous regions of Hainan Island, where it thrives in humid, tropical climates.

Due to its unique morphology and limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
