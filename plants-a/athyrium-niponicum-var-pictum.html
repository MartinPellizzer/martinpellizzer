Athyrium niponicum var. pictum

Athyrium niponicum var. pictum, commonly known as the painted fern, is a striking ornamental fern native to East Asia.

It features delicate, lace-like fronds with a distinctive pattern of light and dark green hues, giving it its common name. The plant typically grows to a height of 12 to 24 inches and thrives in moist, shady environments. Its foliage is evergreen, providing year-round visual interest in garden settings.

It is well-suited for woodland gardens and requires well-drained, acidic soil for optimal growth.
