Aristea thyrsiflora

Aristea thyrsiflora, commonly known as the tufted arrowhead, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, tufted growth habit and its clusters of small, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its flowers are usually pink or purple, and it is known for its ornamental value in gardens.

Aristea thyrsiflora is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
