Agropyron dasyanthum f. glabrum

Agropyron dasyanthum f. glabrum, commonly known as the smooth wheatgrass, is a perennial grass species characterized by its glabrous (smooth) leaves and stems.

It is native to the temperate regions of Asia, particularly China and the Russian Far East. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. It has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences.

This variety is valued for its hardiness and is often used in ecological restoration projects.
