Argyrolobium linnaeanum

Argyrolobium linnaeanum, commonly known as the yellow burr vetch.

It is a flowering plant native to southern Africa, characterized by its yellow flowers and hairy stems. The plant belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and it has a typical trifoliate leaf structure. Its small, pod-like fruits contain seeds that are dispersed by wind.

Argyrolobium linnaeanum is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in arid conditions.
