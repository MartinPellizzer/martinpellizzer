Abutilon rehmannii

Abutilon rehmannii, commonly known as velvetleaf or Chinese lantern plant, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 3 meters tall, featuring heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers that are followed by spiny, lantern-shaped fruits. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its ornamental value comes from its unique foliage and distinctive fruiting structures.
