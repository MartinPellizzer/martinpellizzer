Anacamptis papilionacea subsp. heroica

Anacamptis papilionacea subsp.

heroica, commonly known as the Hero's Orchid, is a striking member of the Orchidaceae family. It is native to southern Europe and thrives in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes.

The plant produces tall, branched stems that bear numerous violet-blue flowers with a distinctive papilionaceous (butterfly-like) shape. Its inflorescence is characterized by a dense cluster of flowers, often with a contrasting yellow or white base.

The species is notable for its ornamental value and is occasionally cultivated in gardens for its beauty and resilience.
