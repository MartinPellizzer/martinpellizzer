Astilbe tsushimensis

Astilbe tsushimensis, commonly known as the Japanese astilbe, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Japan.

It features upright, branching stems that bear clusters of small, fern-like leaves. The plant produces delicate, lace-like panicles of white or pink flowers in late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, shady environments and prefers well-drained, humus-rich soil.

Astilbe tsushimensis is valued in gardens for its ornamental foliage and subtle floral display.
