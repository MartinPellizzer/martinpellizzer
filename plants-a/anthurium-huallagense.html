Anthurium huallagense

Anthurium huallagense, commonly known as the Huallagense arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to the Amazon rainforest of Peru.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant produces a spadix inflorescence surrounded by a colorful spathe, which is often used in floral arrangements. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and botanical collections.
