Artemisia tukuchaensis

Artemisia tukuchaensis, commonly known as the Tukucha wormwood, is a perennial shrub native to the high-altitude regions of the Tibetan Plateau.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, gray-green foliage with finely divided leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flower heads that are typically clustered in dense, spherical inflorescences. It thrives in cold, arid environments and is adapted to high-altitude conditions with low oxygen levels.

Due to its medicinal properties, it has been traditionally used in local herbal remedies.
