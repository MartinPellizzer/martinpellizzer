Acroptilon australe

Acroptilon australe, commonly known as the Australian crownvetch, is a perennial legume native to Australia.

It features trifoliate leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has a fibrous root system and is often found in disturbed soils. It is valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, improving soil fertility.

However, it can become invasive in certain regions if not managed properly.
