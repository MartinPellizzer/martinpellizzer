Anemonidium demissum

Anemonidium demissum, commonly known as the slender anemone, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant features showy, pink to purple flowers that bloom in spring. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often covered in fine hairs.

Anemonidium demissum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens.
