Agrostis sinaica

Agrostis sinaica, commonly known as the Sinai grass, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family Poaceae.

It is native to the arid regions of the Middle East and North Africa, particularly the Sinai Peninsula. This plant is characterized by its fine, tufted growth habit and small, spike-like inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to withstand drought conditions.

Agrostis sinaica plays a role in stabilizing soil in its native habitat and is sometimes used in restoration projects.
