Actinostemon polymorphus var. bicolor

Actinostemon polymorphus var. bicolor, commonly known as the two-colored actinostemon, is a strikingly ornamental plant native to South Africa.

It features vibrant, bi-colored flowers that typically display a mix of yellow and orange hues, arranged in dense, showy inflorescences. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery down, adding to its visual appeal. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained, sandy soils.

This variety is prized for its long blooming season and low maintenance requirements, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
