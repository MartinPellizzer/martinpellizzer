Achnatherum nakaii

Achnatherum nakaii, commonly known as the Nakai's needlegrass, is a perennial grass species native to East Asia.

It typically grows in moist meadows and mountainous regions, preferring temperate climates. The plant features slender, upright stems with narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its inflorescence consists of a panicle made up of small, spikelet-like flowers.

Achnatherum nakaii plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various small organisms.
