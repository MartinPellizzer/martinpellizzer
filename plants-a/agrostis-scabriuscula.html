Agrostis scabriuscula

Agrostis scabriuscula, commonly known as rough bentgrass, is a low-growing perennial grass species native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

It typically forms dense, mat-like growth and has narrow, grass-like leaves with a rough texture. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows, lawns, and along stream banks.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes considered a weed in agricultural settings.
