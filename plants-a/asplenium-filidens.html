Asplenium filidens

Asplenium filidens, commonly known as the narrow-leaf spleenwort, is a small, evergreen fern belonging to the family Aspleniaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped leaves that are typically less than 10 centimeters in length. This plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often growing on rocks or tree trunks in temperate regions. Its rhizomes are short and produce dense clusters of foliage.

Asplenium filidens is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
