Aegilops cylindrica var. fuliginosa

Aegilops cylindrica var. fuliginosa, commonly known as the dark red wheatgrass, is a perennial grass native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its dense, tufted growth habit and dark reddish-brown stems, which are often used in ornamental landscaping. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences with pale green to brown florets. It is adapted to thrive in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for dry garden environments.

This species is also valued for its potential in forage and as a source of genetic material for wheat improvement.
