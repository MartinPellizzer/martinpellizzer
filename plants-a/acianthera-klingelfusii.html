Acianthera klingelfusii

Acianthera klingelfusii, commonly known as the golden orchid, is a species of orchid native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its striking golden-yellow flowers and is typically found in tropical rainforests. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are usually elliptical and covered in a waxy cuticle to reduce water loss.

Acianthera klingelfusii is a popular choice for horticultural collections due to its ornamental value and unique appearance.
