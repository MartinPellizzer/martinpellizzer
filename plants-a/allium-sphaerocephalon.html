Allium sphaerocephalon

Allium sphaerocephalon, commonly known as the globe onion, is a bulbous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features a rounded, spherical bulb that is typically white or pale yellow in color. The plant produces tall, slender stems with narrow, grass-like leaves. Its flowers are small, clustered in a spherical shape, giving it its common name.

It is often cultivated for its edible bulbs and ornamental value in gardens.
