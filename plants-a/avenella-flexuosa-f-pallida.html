Avenella flexuosa f. pallida

Avenella flexuosa f. pallida, commonly known as the pale form of the flexible avena, is a low-growing perennial plant characterized by its soft, silvery foliage.

It belongs to the grass family (Poaceae) and is native to the northern regions of North America. The plant features slender, arching stems that produce small, pale green to whitish leaves. Its flowers are typically inconspicuous, forming in clusters at the base of the plant.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in moist, shaded environments.
