Aster brachycomoides

Aster brachycomoides, commonly known as the narrowleaf aster, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the western United States.

It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. The plant features basal and stem leaves that are linear to lanceolate, often with a hairy surface. Its flowers are small, clustered in dense heads, and are typically blue to purple in color.

Aster brachycomoides is part of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and medicinal potential.
