Anthurium podophyllum

Anthurium podophyllum, commonly known as the velvet leaf anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, velvety, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a reddish underside. The plant produces striking spadices, which are modified leaves that house the actual flowers. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Anthurium podophyllum is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
