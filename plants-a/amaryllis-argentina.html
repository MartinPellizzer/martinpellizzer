Amaryllis argentina

Amaryllis argentina, commonly known as the Argentine amaryllis, is a bulbous plant native to South America.

It produces striking, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of red, white, and pink, often with a contrasting stripe. The plant features broad, strap-like leaves that are typically dark green and sword-shaped. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Amaryllis argentina is popular for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
