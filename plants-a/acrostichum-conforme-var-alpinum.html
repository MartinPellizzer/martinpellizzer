Acrostichum conforme var. alpinum

Acrostichum conforme var. alpinum, commonly known as the alpine fern, is a variant of the common brake fern.

It is a perennial plant native to high-altitude regions of the Himalayas and other mountainous areas. The plant has slender, wiry fronds that are typically less than 30 cm in length. It thrives in cool, moist environments with well-drained soil.

Its botanical significance lies in its adaptability to alpine conditions and its role in mountain ecosystems.
