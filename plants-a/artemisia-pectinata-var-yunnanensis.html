Artemisia pectinata var. yunnanensis

Artemisia pectinata var. yunnanensis, commonly known as Yunnan wormwood, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of southwestern China.

It is characterized by its aromatic, silver-gray foliage and small, yellowish-green flower clusters. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in alpine or subalpine habitats. Its leaves contain volatile oils, which contribute to its strong, camphor-like scent.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and traditional medicinal uses in local herbal practices.
