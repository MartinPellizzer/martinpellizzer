Anthurium sagrilloanum

Anthurium sagrilloanum, commonly known as the Sagrillo anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a glossy texture. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-shaped inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, often in shades of red, orange, or yellow. It thrives in humid, warm environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium sagrilloanum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in botanical gardens.
