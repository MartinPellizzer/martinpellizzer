Aconitum napellus var. lilacinus

Aconitum napellus var. lilacinus, commonly known as the meadow aconite, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It features striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer, typically from July to September. The plant has dark green, palmately lobed leaves that are deeply divided, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Due to its toxic properties, it should be handled with care.
