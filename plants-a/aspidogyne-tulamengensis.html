Aspidogyne tulamengensis

Aspidogyne tulamengensis, commonly known as the Tulameng tamarind tree, is a member of the Rhamnaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree that can grow up to 15 meters in height. The plant is characterized by its pinnate leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruit is a drupe, containing a single seed surrounded by a fleshy pulp.

The tree is valued for its timber and has traditional uses in local medicine and as a source of food.
