Aloe perfoliata var. africana

Aloe perfoliata var. africana, commonly known as the African Aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, narrow leaves that are typically blue-green in color with white teeth along the edges. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow-orange flowers that bloom in summer. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice for both ornamental and medicinal purposes.
