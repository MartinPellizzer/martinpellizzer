Ayenia compacta

Ayenia compacta, commonly known as the compact ayenia, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Ayenia compacta is often found in wetlands and along the edges of swamps, where it plays a role in stabilizing soil and supporting local biodiversity.
