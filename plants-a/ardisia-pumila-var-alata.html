Ardisia pumila var. alata

Ardisia pumila var. alata, commonly known as the winged ardisia, is a compact evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features elliptical leaves that are glossy and slightly winged along the edges, giving it its distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers followed by red, berry-like fruits that are edible for some bird species. It thrives in shaded to partially sunny conditions and prefers moist, well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
