Actinidia eriantha var. brunnea

Actinidia eriantha var. brunnea, commonly known as the brown-flowered actinidia, is a deciduous vine native to China.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant produces small, edible berries that are typically brown when mature. It thrives in temperate climates and prefers partial to full shade.

Due to its ornamental value and unique floral characteristics, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
