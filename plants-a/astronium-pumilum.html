Astronium pumilum

Astronium pumilum, commonly known as the dwarf sycamore, is a small evergreen tree native to the Caribbean.

It typically grows to a height of about 5 to 8 meters, featuring a dense, rounded crown. The leaves are simple, alternate, and elliptical, with a glossy dark green upper surface and a lighter underside. Its flowers are small, greenish-yellow, and appear in clusters, followed by orange to red fruits that split open to release seeds.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical and subtropical gardens.
