Achillea millefolium var. aspleniifolia

Achillea millefolium var. aspleniifolia, commonly known as the fern-leaved yarrow, is a variant of the common yarrow species.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. The plant features finely divided, fern-like leaves that give it its distinctive appearance. It produces clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in summer.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to well-drained soils in full sun.
