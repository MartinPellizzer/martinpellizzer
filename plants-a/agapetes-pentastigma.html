Agapetes pentastigma

Agapetes pentastigma, commonly known as the five-stigma heather, is a species of flowering plant in the Ericaceae family.

It is native to the Andean regions of South America, particularly in Ecuador and Peru. The plant is a compact shrub with small, leathery leaves and produces clusters of bell-shaped flowers in shades of white and pink. It thrives in cool, humid environments and is often found in cloud forests.
