Amesium

Amesium, commonly known as the silverleaf nightshade, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

The plant is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and small, star-shaped flowers that bloom in the summer. Amesium is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its striking appearance.
