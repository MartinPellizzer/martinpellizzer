Anthemis debilifolia

Anthemis debilifolia, commonly known as the slender anthemis, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in sunny, well-drained environments. Its flowers are small and yellow, often with a subtle aromatic scent.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine for its mild sedative properties.
