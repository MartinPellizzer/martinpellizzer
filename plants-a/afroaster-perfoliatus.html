Afroaster perfoliatus

Afroaster perfoliatus, commonly known as the African leafy aster, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features dark green, ovate leaves with a hairy texture, and its flowers are typically pink to purple in color. Afroaster perfoliatus is often found in grasslands and rocky areas, thriving in well-drained soils.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
