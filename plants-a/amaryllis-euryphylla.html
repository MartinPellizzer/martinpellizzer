Amaryllis euryphylla

Amaryllis euryphylla, commonly known as the broadleaf amaryllis, is a striking bulbous plant native to South America.

It features large, showy flowers that can range in color from white to deep red, often with intricate patterns. The plant produces strap-like leaves that are broad and fleshy, adapted to store water in its native arid environment. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and containers.

Amaryllis euryphylla is known for its ability to bloom multiple times a year under optimal conditions.
