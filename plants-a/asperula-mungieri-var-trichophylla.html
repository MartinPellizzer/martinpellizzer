Asperula mungieri var. trichophylla

Asperula mungieri var. trichophylla, commonly known as the hairy asperula, is a flowering plant in the Heath family (Ericaceae).

It is native to Japan and is characterized by its small, upright growth habit and dense clusters of white or pink flowers. The variety trichophylla is distinguished by its hairy foliage, which gives it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

This plant is often cultivated as an ornamental for its attractive blooms and foliage.
