Acaena magellanica subsp. laevigata

Acaena magellanica subsp.

laevigata, commonly known as the smooth burr clover, is a low-growing shrub native to South America. It features small, yellow flowers that bloom in late summer, followed by spiny, spherical fruits.

The plant has hairy stems and leaves that are typically oval in shape. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in grassy areas or disturbed soils.

Its distinctive appearance and hardy nature make it a notable species in its native habitat.
