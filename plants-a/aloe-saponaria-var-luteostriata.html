Aloe saponaria var. luteostriata

Aloe saponaria var. luteostriata, commonly known as the yellow-striped aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features striking, lance-shaped leaves that are often tinged with yellow or orange along the edges, giving it its distinctive appearance. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
