Abolboda schultesii

Abolboda schultesii, commonly known as the Schultesi's lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Andes in South America.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in rocky or mountainous terrain.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists alike.
