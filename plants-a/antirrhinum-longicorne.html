Antirrhinum longicorne

Antirrhinum longicorne, commonly known as the long-horned snapdragon, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, tubular flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, yellow, and orange. The plant typically grows to a height of 30 to 60 centimeters and prefers well-drained soil in full sun. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while the stems are upright and branching.

Antirrhinum longicorne is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ease of care.
