Anthurium remotum

Anthurium remotum, commonly known as the remote anthurium, is a tropical flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its unique inflorescence consisting of a spadix and a spathe. The plant produces striking, colorful spathe that can range from red to green, depending on the variety. Its leaves are typically large, heart-shaped, and glossy, contributing to its ornamental value.

Anthurium remotum thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its aesthetic appeal.
