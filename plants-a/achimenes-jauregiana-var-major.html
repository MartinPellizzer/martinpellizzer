Achimenes jauregiana var. major

Achimenes jauregiana var. major, commonly known as the giant flame violet, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from red to orange, often with contrasting purple veins. The plant features heart-shaped, velvety leaves that are dark green and glossy. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is popular in gardens and as a houseplant due to its vibrant blooms and relatively easy care requirements.
