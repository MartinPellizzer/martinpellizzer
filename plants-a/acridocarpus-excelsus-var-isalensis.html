Acridocarpus excelsus var. isalensis

Acridocarpus excelsus var. isalensis, commonly known as the Isalensis fan palm, is a tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is a member of the Arecaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, fan-shaped leaves. The plant thrives in humid, warm climates and is often found in lowland forests or near water sources. Its botanical features include a smooth, pale trunk and leaves that can reach several meters in diameter.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its aesthetic qualities in tropical gardens.
