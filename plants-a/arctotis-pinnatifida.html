Arctotis pinnatifida

Arctotis pinnatifida, commonly known as the narrow-leaved arctotis, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features narrow, linear leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, with a distinctive central disk surrounded by ray florets.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
