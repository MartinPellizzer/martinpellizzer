Aglaonema philippinense

Aglaonema philippinense, commonly known as the Philippine evergreen or Philippine arum, is a tropical plant native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves with striking white and silver variegation. The plant belongs to the Araceae family, which includes other ornamental plants like peace lilies and calla lilies. It thrives in humid, shady environments and is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its aesthetic appeal and air-purifying properties.

Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense foliage, making it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardening.
