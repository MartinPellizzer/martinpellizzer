Asplenium × trojanii

Asplenium × trojanii, commonly known as the Trojan spleenwort, is a hybrid fern native to the western United States.

It belongs to the family Aspleniaceae and is characterized by its small, dark green, strap-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its reproductive structures, including sori, are located on the underside of the fronds, a common feature in many fern species.

Asplenium × trojanii is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to thrive in low-light conditions.
