Acalypha stachyura

Acalypha stachyura, commonly known as the silverleaf acalypha, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its striking silver-gray foliage, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically green or reddish in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate to high light exposure.

Acalypha stachyura is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its aesthetic appeal and adaptability to various growing conditions.
