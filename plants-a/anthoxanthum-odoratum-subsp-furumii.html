Anthoxanthum odoratum subsp. furumii

Anthoxanthum odoratum subsp.

furumii, commonly known as sweet vernal grass, is a flowering plant in the grass family Poaceae. It is characterized by its bright yellow inflorescence, which gives it its common name.

This subspecies is typically found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, wind-pollinated flowers.

It is often used as a forage plant for livestock due to its nutritional value.
