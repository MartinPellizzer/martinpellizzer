Anredera weberbaueri

Anredera weberbaueri, commonly known as the silver vine, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is a perennial herb with trailing stems that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant features heart-shaped, velvety leaves that are covered in fine, silvery hairs. Its small, tubular flowers are typically greenish-white and bloom in clusters during the summer months.

Anredera weberbaueri is often cultivated as a ground cover or ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and hardy nature.
