Asplenium semihastatum var. fadyeni

Asplenium semihastatum var. fadyeni, commonly known as the Fadyen's shield fern, is a rare and unique variant of the shield fern genus Asplenium.

It is characterized by its distinctive, elliptical-shaped fronds that are often covered in fine, silky hairs. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as rocky crevices or forest floors, where it can thrive in low light conditions. Its leaves are usually dark green and have a leathery texture, which helps it retain moisture in its natural habitat.

This variant is of particular interest to botanists due to its limited distribution and unique morphological features.
