Acetosa limoniastrum

Acetosa limoniastrum, commonly known as the lemon thyme.

It is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region, characterized by its aromatic, lemon-scented foliage. The plant produces small, pink to white flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. Its leaves are often used in culinary applications due to their distinctive citrus flavor.

Acetosa limoniastrum is also valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
