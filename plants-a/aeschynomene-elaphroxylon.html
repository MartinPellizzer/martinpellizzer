Aeschynomene elaphroxylon

Aeschynomene elaphroxylon, commonly known as velvetleaf, is a tropical annual herb native to Africa.

It is characterized by its hairy, heart-shaped leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant produces small, elliptical pods that contain several seeds. It is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and agricultural fields.

Its rapid growth and adaptability make it a common weed in many regions.
