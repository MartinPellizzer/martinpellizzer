Anthurium deminutum

Anthurium deminutum, commonly known as the miniature anthurium, is a tropical flowering plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its glossy, heart-shaped leaves and striking, spadix-like inflorescence that ranges in color from red to orange. The plant thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil. Its unique foliage and vibrant flowers make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Anthurium deminutum is also valued for its air-purifying properties and ability to thrive in low-light conditions.
