Astracantha eschkerensis

Astracantha eschkerensis, commonly known as the Eritrean milkweed, is a flowering plant native to the highlands of Eritrea.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in rocky or arid environments. The plant features showy yellow flowers that bloom in summer, attracting various pollinators. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.

The plant is known for its milky sap, which is characteristic of the milkweed genus.
