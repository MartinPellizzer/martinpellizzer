Aeonium percarneum

Aeonium percarneum, commonly known as the Orange Centipede, is a succulent plant native to the Canary Islands.

It features rosettes of fleshy, thick leaves that are typically green with a reddish or orange hue, especially under bright light. The plant produces tall, branching stems that bear clusters of yellow flowers in the spring. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with plenty of sunlight.

Aeonium percarneum is popular among succulent enthusiasts for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
