Anthurium macrourum

Anthurium macrourum, commonly known as the long-tongued anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often a deep green with a glossy texture. The plant produces striking spadices, which are modified leaves that enclose the true flowers, typically in shades of red or orange. Anthurium macrourum thrives in humid, warm environments with bright, indirect light.

It is a popular ornamental plant due to its vibrant foliage and unique floral structure.
