Arachnothryx scabra

Arachnothryx scabra, commonly known as the rough arachnothryx, is a shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces woody pods that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind or animals. Its rough, textured bark and hairy foliage are notable botanical features.

Arachnothryx scabra is often found in moist, shaded environments within tropical forests.
