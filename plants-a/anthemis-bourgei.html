Anthemis bourgei

Anthemis bourgei, commonly known as the Cape Verde daisy, is a flowering plant native to the Cape Verde Islands.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies, sunflowers, and other composite flowers. The plant produces small, yellow flowers with white centers, resembling a daisy. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to the arid climate of its native habitat.
