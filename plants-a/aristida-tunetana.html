Aristida tunetana

Aristida tunetana, commonly known as the Tunisian threeawn, is a perennial grass native to North Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences that are typically found in clusters. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often growing on rocky soils.

Its botanical features make it an important component of Mediterranean and desert ecosystems.
