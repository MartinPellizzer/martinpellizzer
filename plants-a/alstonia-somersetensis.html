Alstonia somersetensis

Alstonia somersetensis, commonly known as the Somerset alstonia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Africa, particularly South Africa.

It is a deciduous tree that can grow up to 15 meters tall, characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and opposite, ovate leaves. The plant produces fragrant, white flowers in clusters, which are followed by elongated, woody pods containing seeds.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous areas.
