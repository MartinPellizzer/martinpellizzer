Adenanthos cygnorum

Adenanthos cygnorum, commonly known as the Grey Kangaroo Paw, is a striking evergreen shrub native to south-western Australia.

It is characterized by its unique, paw-like flowers that are typically greyish or pink in color, with a prominent central spur. The plant produces small, oval-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine, white pubescence. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Adenanthos cygnorum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various environmental conditions.
