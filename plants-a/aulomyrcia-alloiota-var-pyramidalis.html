Aulomyrcia alloiota var. pyramidalis

Aulomyrcia alloiota var. pyramidalis, commonly known as the pyramidal pittosporum, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its dense, pyramidal growth habit and dark green, leathery foliage. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that are followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in horticultural settings.
