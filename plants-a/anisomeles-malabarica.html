Anisomeles malabarica

Anisomeles malabarica, commonly known as the Malabar starling plant, is a flowering plant native to the Western Ghats of India.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its striking, yellow-orange composite flowers that resemble a star. The plant typically grows as an erect herb with slender, hairy stems and alternate leaves that are ovate to lanceolate in shape. Anisomeles malabarica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
