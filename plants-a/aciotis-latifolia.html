Aciotis latifolia

Aciotis latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf aciotis, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in grasslands and open habitats, thriving in well-drained soils. It features narrow, linear leaves and produces yellow flowers that bloom in summer.

Aciotis latifolia is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
