Acacia heteroneura var. heteroneura

Acacia heteroneura var. heteroneura, commonly known as the narrow-leaf wattle, is a shrub or small tree native to Australia.

It features bipinnate leaves that are narrow and linear, with each leaflet being very small and delicate. The plant produces yellow flowers in globular or cylindrical clusters, which are typical of the Acacia genus. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping due to its hardy nature.
