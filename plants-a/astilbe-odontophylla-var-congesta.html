Astilbe odontophylla var. congesta

Astilbe odontophylla var. congesta, commonly known as the clustered astilbe, is a herbaceous perennial native to eastern Asia.

It features finely divided, feathery leaves that create a lush, green backdrop for its showy flower clusters. The plant produces panicles of pink to white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, shady conditions and is well-suited for woodland gardens.

Its ornamental value lies in its delicate foliage and long-lasting blooms.
