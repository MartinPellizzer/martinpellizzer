Artemisia handel-mazzettii

Artemisia handel-mazzettii, commonly known as the Tibetan wormwood, is a flowering plant native to high-altitude regions of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, silver-gray foliage. The plant produces small, yellowish flower heads that are clustered in dense, spherical shapes. It thrives in arid and rocky environments, often found in mountainous areas.

Its essential oils contain compounds with potential medicinal properties.
