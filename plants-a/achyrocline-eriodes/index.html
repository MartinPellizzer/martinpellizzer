Achyrocline Eriodes

Achyrocline eriodes is a unique flowering plant native to the coastal regions of Brazil. It is known for its striking violet-blue flowers that bloom during the summer months. This species thrives in sandy soils and is often found in dune environments. Its medicinal properties have been studied for their potential health benefits. Despite its beauty, it remains a relatively obscure plant in the botanical world.
