Anthurium vanderknaapii

Anthurium vanderknaapii, commonly known as the "Red Star" anthurium, is a tropical flowering plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are deep green with a metallic sheen. The plant produces vibrant red spathes that enclose a spadix, which is a dense cluster of tiny flowers. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium vanderknaapii is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and long-lasting blooms.
