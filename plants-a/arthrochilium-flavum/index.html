Arthrochilium Flavum

Flower orchid, scientifically known as Arthrochilium flavum, is a rare and delicate species found in specific tropical regions. This orchid is distinguished by its vibrant yellow petals and unique floral structure that sets it apart from other orchid varieties. Despite its beauty, it faces significant threats due to habitat loss and over-collection. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this species for future generations. Its ecological role and aesthetic value make it a noteworthy subject of botanical study.
