Arundarbor latifolia

Arundarbor latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf arundarbor, is a flowering plant native to temperate regions.

It is characterized by its wide, serrated leaves that can grow up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers in the spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Arundarbor latifolia is often used in ornamental gardening for its aesthetic appeal and resilience.
