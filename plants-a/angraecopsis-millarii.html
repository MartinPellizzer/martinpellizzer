Angraecopsis millarii

Angraecopsis millarii, commonly known as the millarii star orchid, is a striking epiphytic orchid native to Madagascar.

It features long, arching inflorescences with numerous pale yellow to white flowers that have a distinctive star-like shape. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, acidic soil. It is known for its fragrant blooms, which attract nocturnal pollinators such as moths.

Due to its unique appearance and specific care requirements, it is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
