Arundinaria argenteostriata

Arundinaria argenteostriata, commonly known as the silver-stripped bamboo, is a striking ornamental grass native to China.

It features tall, slender stalks that are often adorned with distinctive silver-white stripes along the nodes. The plant produces feathery, light green foliage that adds a refined texture to gardens. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Due to its elegant appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for water gardens and shaded landscapes.
