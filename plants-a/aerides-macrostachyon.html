Aerides macrostachyon

Aerides macrostachyon, commonly known as the giant orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a large epiphytic orchid that can grow up to several meters in height. The plant features long, arching inflorescences with numerous fragrant, showy flowers. Its leaves are broad, leathery, and arranged in a rosette pattern.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media for optimal growth.
