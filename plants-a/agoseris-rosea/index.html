Agoseris Rosea

Agoseris rosea is a striking wildflower native to the western United States, known for its vibrant pink and white blooms. This member of the Asteraceae family thrives in dry, open habitats such as meadows and rocky slopes. Its delicate yet resilient nature makes it a favorite among botanists and nature enthusiasts. The plant's unique appearance and ecological importance have made it a symbol of mountainous landscapes. Despite its beauty, Agoseris rosea faces threats from habitat loss and invasive species.
