Ardisia crispa var. dielsii

Ardisia crispa var. dielsii, commonly known as the glossy evergreen shrub, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, glossy leaves and small, red berries that appear in clusters. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in tropical and subtropical regions. Its foliage is dense, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

The berries are not only attractive but also serve as a food source for various birds and small mammals.
