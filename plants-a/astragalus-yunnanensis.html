Astragalus yunnanensis

Astragalus yunnanensis, commonly known as Yunnan milkvetch, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Yunnan, China.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky or sandy soils. Its root system is deep and extensive, allowing it to access water from deeper soil layers.

Astragalus yunnanensis is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional Chinese medicine.
