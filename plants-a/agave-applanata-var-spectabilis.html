Agave applanata var. spectabilis

Agave applanata var. spectabilis, commonly known as the showy agave, is a striking plant native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its large, fleshy, blue-green leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it well-suited for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice for xeriscaping and ornamental gardening.
