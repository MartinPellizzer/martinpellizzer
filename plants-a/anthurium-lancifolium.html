Anthurium lancifolium

Anthurium lancifolium, commonly known as the sharp-leafed anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically deep green with a glossy texture. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-like inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, which can range from red to yellow. It thrives in humid, warm environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium lancifolium is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
