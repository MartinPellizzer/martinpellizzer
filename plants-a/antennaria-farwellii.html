Antennaria farwellii

Antennaria farwellii, commonly known as the Farwell's mouse-eared moss, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the family Caryophyllaceae and is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit. The plant features tiny, hairy leaves that are typically less than 1 centimeter in length. Its flowers are small, pinkish-white, and clustered in racemes, blooming in early spring.
