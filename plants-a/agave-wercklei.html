Agave wercklei

Agave wercklei, commonly known as the Blue Agave, is a succulent plant native to the arid regions of Mexico.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that are blue-green in color, often with white or grayish variegation. The plant produces an inflorescence that can reach several meters in height, bearing small, white or yellowish flowers. It is well adapted to hot, dry climates and requires minimal water once established.

The plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping due to its striking appearance.
