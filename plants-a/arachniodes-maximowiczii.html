Arachniodes maximowiczii

Arachniodes maximowiczii, commonly known as the giant bamboo fern, is a striking evergreen fern native to East Asia.

It features large, feathery fronds that can grow up to several meters in length, giving it a dramatic appearance. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit, allowing it to spread and form dense colonies. Its leaves are typically bipinnate, with a distinctive, almost spider-like appearance that contributes to its common name.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often used in ornamental horticulture for its lush, tropical foliage.
