Anisostachya humblotii

Anisostachya humblotii, commonly known as the dwarf reed, is a grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and slender, erect culms that are often hairy. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences with pale green to yellowish flowers. Its leaves are narrow and slightly folded, with a rough texture.

Anisostachya humblotii is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a resilient plant in harsh conditions.
