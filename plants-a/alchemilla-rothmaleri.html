Alchemilla rothmaleri

Alchemilla rothmaleri, commonly known as the Rothmaler's lady's mantle, is a perennial plant belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of velvety, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers during the summer months, which are borne on slender stalks. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas or along stream banks.

Alchemilla rothmaleri is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to help prevent soil erosion.
