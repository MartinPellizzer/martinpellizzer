Artanthe crocata

Artanthe crocata, commonly known as the Crocus Artanthe, is a striking plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of yellow and orange. The plant features slender, grass-like leaves and a short, sturdy stem. Its flowers are typically borne in clusters at the top of the stem, adding a dramatic visual impact to gardens.

Artanthe crocata is valued for its ornamental qualities and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
