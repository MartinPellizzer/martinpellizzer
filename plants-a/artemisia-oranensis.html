Artemisia oranensis

Artemisia oranensis, commonly known as Moroccan wormwood, is a perennial shrub native to North Africa.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and aromatic, slightly bitter scent. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in dry, well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or arid environments.

Artemisia oranensis is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional remedies.
