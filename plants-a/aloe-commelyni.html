Aloe commelyni

Aloe commelyni, commonly known as the narrow-leaf aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features narrow, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, with a waxy coating to reduce water loss. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing yellow flowers, which bloom in spring. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and low maintenance requirements.

Aloe commelyni is adapted to arid environments, making it drought-tolerant and suitable for xeriscaping.
