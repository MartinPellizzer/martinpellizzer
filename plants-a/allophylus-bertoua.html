Allophylus bertoua

Allophylus bertoua, commonly known as the African hibiscus, is a deciduous shrub native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are often hairy and have a rough texture. Allophylus bertoua is often used in traditional medicine and is valued for its ornamental qualities in gardens.
