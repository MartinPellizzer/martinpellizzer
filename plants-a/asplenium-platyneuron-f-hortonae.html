Asplenium platyneuron f. hortonae

Asplenium platyneuron f. hortonae, commonly known as the Horton's spleenwort, is a fern belonging to the Aspleniaceae family.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that typically grows on tree trunks or rocks in shaded, moist environments. The fronds are typically narrowly lanceolate, with a dark green color and a serrated margin. It produces sporangia on the underside of its leaves, which are clustered in strobila.

This fern is often found in temperate regions and is valued for its ornamental appeal in shaded garden settings.
