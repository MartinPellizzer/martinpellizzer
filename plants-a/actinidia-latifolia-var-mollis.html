Actinidia latifolia var. mollis

Actinidia latifolia var. mollis, commonly known as the soft-leaf kiwifruit, is a deciduous vine native to China.

It produces small, fuzzy, green fruits that are typically eaten raw or used in jams. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive silvery underside. It thrives in temperate climates and requires a cold winter dormancy period.

The variety is valued for its edible fruit and ornamental value due to its attractive foliage.
