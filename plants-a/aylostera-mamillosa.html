Aylostera mamillosa

Aylostera mamillosa, commonly known as the woolly aylostera, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often found in forest understories.

Aylostera mamillosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
