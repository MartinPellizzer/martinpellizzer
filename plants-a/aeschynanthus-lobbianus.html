Aeschynanthus lobbianus

Aeschynanthus lobbianus, commonly known as the red-veined ivy or flame ivy, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a trailing evergreen shrub characterized by its dark green, leathery leaves with prominent red veins. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically red or orange in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for hanging baskets or as a trailing plant in greenhouses.

Aeschynanthus lobbianus is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to bloom throughout the year under suitable conditions.
