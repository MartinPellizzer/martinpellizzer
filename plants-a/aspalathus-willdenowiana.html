Aspalathus willdenowiana

Aspalathus willdenowiana, commonly known as the Cape chestnut, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its small, dark green leaves and produces yellow flowers in clusters. The plant is adapted to arid environments and has a deep root system for water absorption. It is also known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional African medicine.

The plant's resilience makes it suitable for cultivation in dry climates.
