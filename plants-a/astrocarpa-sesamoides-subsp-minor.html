Astrocarpa sesamoides subsp. minor

Astrocarpa sesamoides subsp.

minor, commonly known as the small-seeded sesamoid daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads.

The plant produces small, daisy-like flowers with yellow ray florets and dark brown disk florets. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it typically grows in dry, sandy soils.

It is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and rocky outcrops.
