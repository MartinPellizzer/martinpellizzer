Anthurium eminens subsp. eminens

Anthurium eminens subsp.

eminens, commonly known as the eminens anthurium, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of South America. It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length, often with a deep green color and prominent veins.

The plant produces vibrant red spadices, which are the spathes that enclose the true flowers. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This species is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to adapt to indoor growing conditions.
