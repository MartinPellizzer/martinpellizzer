Assonia parviflora

Assonia parviflora, commonly known as the small-flowered assonia, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, upright stems covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers arranged in clusters at the ends of the stems. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often with a slightly hairy texture.

Assonia parviflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
