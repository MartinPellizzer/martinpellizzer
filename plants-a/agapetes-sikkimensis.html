Agapetes sikkimensis

Agapetes sikkimensis, commonly known as the Sikkim silverleaf, is a species of flowering plant in the Ericaceae family.

It is native to the Himalayas, particularly found in the eastern regions of Nepal and Sikkim. Agapetes sikkimensis produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are usually pink or white, blooming in the spring.
