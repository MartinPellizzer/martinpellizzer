Aspidium germanii

Aspidium germanii, commonly known as the common water fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Athyriaceae.

It is characterized by its finely divided, pinnate fronds that are typically light green and have a delicate appearance. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forests and along stream banks. Its reproductive structures, including sporangia, are clustered in sori on the underside of the fronds.

Aspidium germanii plays a role in maintaining soil stability and providing habitat for various microorganisms and small invertebrates.
