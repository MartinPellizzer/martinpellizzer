Ajuga × knafii

Ajuga × knafii, commonly known as carpet bugleweed, is a low-growing perennial plant native to Europe.

It belongs to the Valerianaceae family and is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit. The plant features dark green, oval-shaped leaves and produces clusters of small, violet-blue flowers in late spring. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often used in ornamental landscaping for its attractive foliage and early blooming period.

Ajuga × knafii is also valued for its ability to suppress weeds and improve soil stability in woodland gardens.
