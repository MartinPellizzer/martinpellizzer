Aloe baumii

Aloe baumii, commonly known as the Christmas cactus, is a succulent plant native to South America.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid conditions. The plant produces vibrant, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow during the winter months. Its stems are segmented and can be branched, adding to its ornamental appeal.

Aloe baumii prefers indirect sunlight and well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
