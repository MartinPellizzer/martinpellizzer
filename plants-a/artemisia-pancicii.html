Artemisia pancicii

Artemisia pancicii, commonly known as Pancici's wormwood, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, silver-gray foliage. The plant produces small, yellowish flower heads that are typically clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or dry habitats.

Due to its strong scent, it is sometimes used in traditional herbal remedies.
