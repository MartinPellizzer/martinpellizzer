Anchusa hybrida var. proceroides

Anchusa hybrida var. proceroides, commonly known as the tall blueweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to southern Europe.

It features upright, branched stems that can grow up to 1.5 meters in height, covered with dense, blue-gray foliage. The plant produces showy, spike-like inflorescences of violet-blue flowers from late spring to early summer. Its leaves are lanceolate and slightly hairy, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it a popular choice for garden borders and wildflower meadows.
