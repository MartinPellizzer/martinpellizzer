Allamanda williamsii

Allamanda williamsii, commonly known as the golden trumpet vine, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy yellow flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. The plant features waxy, dark green leaves and a rapid growth rate, making it an excellent choice for privacy screens or ornamental purposes. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, requiring regular watering and pruning to maintain its shape.

Allamanda williamsii is also noted for its ability to climb or trail, adapting well to various garden settings.
