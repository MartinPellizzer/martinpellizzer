Anthospermum aethiopicum var. ciliare

Anthospermum aethiopicum var. ciliare, commonly known as the hairy anthospermum, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

The species is also valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various soil types.
