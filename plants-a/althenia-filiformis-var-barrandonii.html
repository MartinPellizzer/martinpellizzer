Althenia filiformis var. barrandonii

Althenia filiformis var. barrandonii, commonly known as the Barrandon's threadleaf althenia, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to specific regions of southern Africa.

The plant thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and requires a dry, Mediterranean climate to flourish.
