Aeschynanthus lancilimbus

Aeschynanthus lancilimbus, commonly known as the long-stemmed flame vine, is a tropical shrub native to western Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange, often resembling flames. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green. It thrives in warm, humid conditions and requires well-draining soil.

Aeschynanthus lancilimbus is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
