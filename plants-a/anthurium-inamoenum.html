Anthurium inamoenum

Anthurium inamoenum, commonly known as the "Velvet Heart" plant, is a tropical species native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped spathes that range in color from deep red to orange, often with a velvety texture. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers inside the spathe, which are typically hidden from view. Its foliage consists of broad, glossy, dark green leaves that are often veined with white or silver patterns.

Anthurium inamoenum thrives in high humidity and indirect light, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.
