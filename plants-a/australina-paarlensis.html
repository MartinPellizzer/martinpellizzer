Australina paarlensis

Australina paarlensis, commonly known as the Karoo sandthorn, is a low-growing shrub native to the arid regions of South Africa.

It belongs to the family Rhamnaceae and is characterized by its small, spiny leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant is adapted to survive in harsh, dry conditions with its deep root system and waxy leaf surface. It plays a role in stabilizing sandy soils and provides habitat for small animals.

Due to its resilience, it is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects in degraded landscapes.
