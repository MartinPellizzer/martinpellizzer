Anthurium sabanitense

Anthurium sabanitense, commonly known as the Sabanitense Arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to Central America.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its unique inflorescence consisting of a spadix and a spathe. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are typically deep green with a waxy texture. It thrives in humid, warm environments and requires indirect sunlight for optimal growth.

Anthurium sabanitense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated as a houseplant.
