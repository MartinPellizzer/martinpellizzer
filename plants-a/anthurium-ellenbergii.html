Anthurium ellenbergii

Anthurium ellenbergii, commonly known as the elliptic anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a glossy texture. The plant produces striking spadices, which are brightly colored, spadix-like inflorescences that are often red or orange. Anthurium ellenbergii thrives in humid, warm environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

It is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
