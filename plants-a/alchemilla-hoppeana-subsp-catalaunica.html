Alchemilla hoppeana subsp. catalaunica

Alchemilla hoppeana subsp.

catalaunica, commonly known as the Catalaunian lady's mantle, is a low-growing perennial plant native to Central Europe. It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its basal rosette of silver-green, hairy leaves.

The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in spring, which are clustered in dense, upright inflorescences. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland or streamside habitats.

Its ornamental value comes from its attractive foliage and ability to thrive in partial shade.
