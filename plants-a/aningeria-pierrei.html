Aningeria pierrei

Aningeria pierrei, commonly known as the Madagascar star orchid, is a rare and striking orchid native to the island of Madagascar.

It is characterized by its unique, star-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale blue with prominent yellow markings. The plant has a compact growth habit, with short stems and dense clusters of blooms. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often forming a basal rosette.
