Aspilia africana var. guineensis

Aspilia africana var. guineensis, commonly known as African daisy or Guinean African daisy, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, yellow flowers that resemble daisies. The plant typically grows as a herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in some African cultures.
