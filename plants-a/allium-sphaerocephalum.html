Allium sphaerocephalum

Allium sphaerocephalum, commonly known as the globe garlic or round-headed garlic, is a bulbous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features a cluster of small, rounded bulbs that are often white or pale pink in color. The plant produces narrow, grass-like leaves and small, dainty flowers that bloom in shades of purple or white. It is typically grown as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and mild garlic scent.

Allium sphaerocephalum is relatively low maintenance and thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.
