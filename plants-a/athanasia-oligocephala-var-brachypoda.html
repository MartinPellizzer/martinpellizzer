Athanasia oligocephala var. brachypoda

Athanasia oligocephala var. brachypoda, commonly known as the short-headed everlasting, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit. The plant produces small, daisy-like flowers in shades of yellow and white, which bloom in spring and early summer. Its leaves are narrow, linear, and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its drought-resistant nature.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
