Acourtia platyphylla

Acourtia platyphylla, commonly known as the flatleaf acourtia, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the sunflower family (Asteraceae) and is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit. The plant features small, daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are typically broad and flat, which gives it its common name.
