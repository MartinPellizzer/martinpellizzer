Asplenium haenkeanum

Asplenium haenkeanum, commonly known as the rock fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Aspleniaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds that are typically dark green and leathery in texture. This plant is native to regions of Southeast Asia, including the Philippines and Indonesia, where it thrives in shaded, moist environments. Asplenium haenkeanum prefers rocky or calcareous substrates and is often found in forest understories.

Its unique morphology and habitat make it a notable species in botanical studies and horticulture.
