Asterochiton pygmaeus

Asterochiton pygmaeus, commonly known as the dwarf starburster, is a small flowering plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant produces small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, giving it a star-like appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it well adapted to its harsh environment.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological significance, it is a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists.
