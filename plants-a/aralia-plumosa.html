Aralia plumosa

Aralia plumosa, commonly known as the feathery aralia, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern North America.

It features compound leaves that are pinnately arranged, giving it a feathery appearance. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers in umbel-like clusters during late spring. Its berries are typically red or black and serve as a food source for birds.

Aralia plumosa is valued in gardens for its ornamental foliage and adaptability to various soil types.
