Asplenium phyllitidis

Asplenium phyllitidis, commonly known as the rock spleenwort, is a small, evergreen fern that thrives in moist, shaded environments.

It is characterized by its compact, clustered growth habit and dark green, lance-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows on rocks, walls, or in crevices, where it forms dense mats. Its fronds are usually short and narrow, with a distinctive midrib that is prominent on the underside.

Asplenium phyllitidis is a hardy species that is often found in temperate regions and is relatively easy to cultivate in gardens with the right conditions.
