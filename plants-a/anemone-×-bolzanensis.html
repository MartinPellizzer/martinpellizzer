Anemone × bolzanensis

Anemone × bolzanensis, commonly known as the Bolzanian anemone, is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Anemone coronaria and Anemone hupehensis.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of 10–30 cm, featuring showy, cup-shaped flowers in shades of pink, red, or white. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders. Its foliage consists of dark green, palmate leaves that are often hairy and aromatic.

The flowers bloom in early summer, adding a vibrant touch to gardens with their delicate, lace-like petals.
