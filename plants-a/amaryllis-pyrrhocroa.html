Amaryllis pyrrhocroa

Amaryllis pyrrhocroa, commonly known as the red-tipped amaryllis, is a striking bulbous plant native to South America.

It produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, often with a distinctive red or orange tip. The plant features strap-like, dark green leaves that are broad and somewhat fleshy. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Amaryllis pyrrhocroa is a popular choice for container gardening due to its vibrant blooms and relatively easy care requirements.
