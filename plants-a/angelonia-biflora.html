Angelonia biflora

Angelonia biflora, commonly known as the winter angel, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that come in shades of red, pink, and purple. The plant has opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and containers.

Angelonia biflora is known for its long blooming season, often lasting from spring through fall.
