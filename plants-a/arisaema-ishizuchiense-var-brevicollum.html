Arisaema ishizuchiense var. brevicollum

Arisaema ishizuchiense var. brevicollum, commonly known as the Ishizuchi arrowhead, is a rare and ornamental plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its unique inflorescence consisting of a spadix surrounded by a spathe. The plant features heart-shaped leaves and produces striking, often colorful flower clusters. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is typically found in mountainous regions.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a vulnerable species.
