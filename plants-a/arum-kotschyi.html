Arum kotschyi

Arum kotschyi, commonly known as the Ethiopian arrowleaf, is a striking ornamental plant native to the highlands of Ethiopia.

It features a striking, arrow-shaped leaf that is often a deep green with a metallic sheen. The plant produces a spadix, a type of inflorescence, surrounded by a colorful spathe, which can range from white to purple. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in montane forests.

Due to its unique appearance, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and by plant enthusiasts worldwide.
