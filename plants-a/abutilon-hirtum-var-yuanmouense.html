Abutilon hirtum var. yuanmouense

Abutilon hirtum var. yuanmouense, commonly known as Yuanmou velvetleaf, is a variant of the velvetleaf plant native to China.

It is characterized by its hairy, ovate leaves and showy, lantern-shaped flowers that range from yellow to orange in color. The plant typically grows as an annual herb, often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides. Its fibrous stems and broad leaves contribute to its adaptability in various environmental conditions.

This variant is of interest in botanical studies due to its unique morphological traits and ecological significance.
