Aloe zeylanica

Aloe zeylanica, commonly known as the Ceylon aloe, is a succulent plant native to Sri Lanka.

It belongs to the Asphodelaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are fleshy and covered in a white powdery substance. The plant produces tall, branched flower stalks that bear yellowish-green flowers. Its leaves contain a gel-like substance that has been used for its medicinal properties.

Aloe zeylanica is often cultivated for ornamental purposes and traditional herbal use.
