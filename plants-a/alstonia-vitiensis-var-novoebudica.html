Alstonia vitiensis var. novoebudica

Alstonia vitiensis var. novoebudica, commonly known as the New Ebudica alstonia, is a tropical tree native to the Pacific Islands.

It belongs to the family Apocynaceae and is characterized by its smooth, gray bark and opposite, ovate leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in humid, coastal environments and is often found in lowland forests.
