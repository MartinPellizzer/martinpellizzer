Arachnanthe sulingi

Arachnanthe sulingi, commonly known as the Suling's spider orchid, is a rare and unique flowering plant native to the tropical rainforests of Borneo.

It belongs to the orchid family (Orchidaceae) and is characterized by its intricate, spider-like floral structure, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, delicate flowers in shades of white and pink, often with a subtle fragrance. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires specific soil conditions to flourish.
