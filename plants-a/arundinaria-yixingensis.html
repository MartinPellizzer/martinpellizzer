Arundinaria yixingensis

Arundinaria yixingensis, commonly known as the Xixing bamboo, is a species of grass native to China.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and narrow leaves. This plant is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and along riverbanks. Its growth habit resembles that of other bamboo species, though it is botanically classified as a grass rather than a true bamboo.
