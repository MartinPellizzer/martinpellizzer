Aeonium × velutinum

Aeonium × velutinum, commonly known as the velvet aeonium, is a hybrid succulent plant native to the Canary Islands.

It features rosettes of fleshy, dark green leaves covered in a fine, velvety coating. The plant produces striking, branched inflorescences of yellow flowers in the spring. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil with ample sunlight.

Due to its ornamental value, it is popular among gardeners and collectors.
