Abutilon straminicarpum

Abutilon straminicarpum, commonly known as the Indian mallow or velvetleaf, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters. The plant features palmate leaves with five to seven lobes, and its flowers are usually yellow or orange with five petals. The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, hairy seeds.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed areas or along roadsides.
