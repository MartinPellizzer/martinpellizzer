Arundinaria spanostachya

Arundinaria spanostachya, commonly known as the Chinese bamboo or giant bamboo, is a species of grass native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender culms that can reach heights of up to 10 meters. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences with pale green to yellowish flowers. Its leaves are long and narrow, with a smooth texture and a waxy coating.

Arundinaria spanostachya is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its elegant appearance and resilience in various climates.
