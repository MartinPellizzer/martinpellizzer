Asystasia neesiana

Asystasia neesiana, commonly known as the silverleaf asystasia, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking silver-gray foliage. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically violet or purple in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Asystasia neesiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
