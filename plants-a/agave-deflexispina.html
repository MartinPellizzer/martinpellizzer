Agave deflexispina

Agave deflexispina, commonly known as the narrow-spined agave, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a succulent plant characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that are often covered in sharp spines. The plant produces a tall flowering stalk that bears clusters of white or yellow flowers. Its leaves are typically blue-green in color and can grow up to 1 meter in length.

Agave deflexispina is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
