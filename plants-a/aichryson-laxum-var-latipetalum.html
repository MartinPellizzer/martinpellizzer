Aichryson laxum var. latipetalum

Aichryson laxum var. latipetalum, commonly known as the broad-petaled aichryson, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It features dense clusters of small, fleshy, yellow-green leaves that are often used in rock gardens or as container plants. The plant produces showy, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, requiring minimal water once established.

Its ornamental value lies in its compact growth habit and vibrant floral display.
