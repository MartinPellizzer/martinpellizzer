Astephanus carassensis

Astephanus carassensis, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies, sunflowers, and other similar plants. The plant is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that range in color from white to pink and yellow. It typically grows in open, sunny areas and is often found in grasslands and along roadsides.

Astephanus carassensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
