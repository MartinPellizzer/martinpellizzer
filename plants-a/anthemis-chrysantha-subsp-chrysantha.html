Anthemis chrysantha subsp. chrysantha

Anthemis chrysantha subsp.

chrysantha, commonly known as the golden everlasting, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region. It is a herbaceous perennial that produces bright yellow flowers with a distinctive, aromatic scent.

The plant features narrow, linear leaves and is characterized by its compact growth habit. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its flowers are often used in floral arrangements due to their long-lasting nature and vibrant color.
