Athanasia juncea

Athanasia juncea, commonly known as the common everlasting, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are usually pink or purple in color. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it is often found in open, sunny habitats.

Athanasia juncea is known for its ability to thrive in arid conditions, making it a resilient species in its native environment.
