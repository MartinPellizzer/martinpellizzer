Aconitum rockii var. fengii

Aconitum rockii var. fengii, commonly known as the Fengii Larkspur, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to China.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its tall, sparsely branched stems that can reach up to 2 meters in height. The plant produces showy blue to purple flowers arranged in dense spikes, which bloom in late summer. Its leaves are palmately compound, with finely divided lobes, and it thrives in moist, well-drained soils.

Due to its toxic alkaloids, it is important to handle the plant with care.
