Alchemilla rutenbergii f. robusta

Alchemilla rutenbergii f. robusta, commonly known as the robust alchemilla, is a variant of the alchemilla species native to Central Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial plant characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit and silver-gray foliage. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in early summer, which are clustered in branched inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained, sandy or rocky soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Its hardy nature makes it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping in arid climates.
