Aspilia eenii

Aspilia eenii, commonly known as the African flame flower, is a striking plant native to southern Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small flames. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces vibrant red to orange inflorescences that bloom in summer. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated for its bold, showy appearance.
