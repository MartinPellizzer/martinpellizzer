Anthurium darcyi

Anthurium darcyi, commonly known as the Darcy's flamingo flower, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are often deep green with a glossy texture. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-shaped inflorescences surrounded by colorful, spatulate bracts that can range from red to orange. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium darcyi is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and unique floral display.
