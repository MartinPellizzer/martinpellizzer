Agrostis sericea

Agrostis sericea, commonly known as silky bentgrass, is a perennial grass native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-gray leaves that are covered with a dense layer of silky hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. This plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows, pastures, and along stream banks. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers.

Agrostis sericea is valued for its ornamental qualities and is also used in turfgrass mixtures for its fine texture and durability.
