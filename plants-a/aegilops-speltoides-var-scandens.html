Aegilops speltoides var. scandens

Aegilops speltoides var. scandens, commonly known as goatgrass, is a wild grass species native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant produces awned lemmas and is often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides. It has been studied for its potential as a source of genetic diversity for wheat improvement.

Its botanical features make it an important forage plant and a candidate for breeding programs.
