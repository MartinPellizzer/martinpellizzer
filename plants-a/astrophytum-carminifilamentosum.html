Astrophytum carminifilamentosum

Astrophytum carminifilamentosum, commonly known as the crimson filaments cactus, is a species native to the arid regions of Mexico.

It is a small, globular cactus with a distinctive rib structure that gives it a spiny, segmented appearance. The plant produces vibrant red flowers during the spring, which contrast beautifully with its green foliage. Its stems are covered in fine, hair-like structures called filaments, which give it its common name.

Astrophytum carminifilamentosum is well adapted to drought conditions, making it a hardy choice for xeriscaping.
