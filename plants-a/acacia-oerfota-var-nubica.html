Acacia oerfota var. nubica

Acacia oerfota var. nubica, commonly known as the Nubian acacia, is a shrub or small tree native to arid and semi-arid regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant has phyllodes, which are modified leaf stalks that function as leaves, reducing water loss in dry environments. It produces yellow to orange flowers, which are clustered in spherical inflorescences.

Acacia oerfota var. nubica is adapted to survive in nutrient-poor soils and can thrive with minimal water.
