Acampe dentata

Acampe dentata, commonly known as the toothed acampe, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features slender, upright stems with alternate, toothed leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small and clustered in dense spikes, usually appearing in shades of pink or purple.

Acampe dentata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
