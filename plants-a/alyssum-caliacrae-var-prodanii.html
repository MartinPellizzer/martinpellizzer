Alyssum caliacrae var. prodanii

Alyssum caliacrae var. prodanii, commonly known as the Balkan alyssum, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of the Balkans.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces clusters of small, fragrant white or pale yellow flowers. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and is adapted to rocky and well-drained soils. It thrives in full sun and is often found in alpine and subalpine habitats.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders in horticulture.
