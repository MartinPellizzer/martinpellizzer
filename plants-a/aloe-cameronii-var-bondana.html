Aloe cameronii var. bondana

Aloe cameronii var. bondana, commonly known as the Cameron's Aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a waxy coating, which helps it retain moisture in arid environments. The plant produces striking, tall flower stalks that bear yellow to orange flowers, often blooming in late winter or early spring. Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for rock gardens or as a container plant.

It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for xeriscaping.
