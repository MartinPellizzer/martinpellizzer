Asparagus plumosus var. tenuissimus

Asparagus plumosus var. tenuissimus, commonly known as the slender asparagus fern, is a low-growing, evergreen plant native to southern Africa.

It features delicate, feathery foliage composed of thin, thread-like leaves that give it a soft, airy appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers followed by red berries that are edible but not commonly consumed by humans. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for indoor and garden settings.

Its ornamental value comes from its unique foliage rather than its flowers or fruits.
