Artemisia thunbergiana

Artemisia thunbergiana, commonly known as Chinese wormwood, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its aromatic, gray-green foliage and slender, branched stems. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in spherical inflorescences. It thrives in dry, well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or mountainous regions.

Artemisia thunbergiana is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional Chinese medicine.
