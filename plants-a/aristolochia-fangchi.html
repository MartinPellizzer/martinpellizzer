Aristolochia fangchi

Aristolochia fangchi, commonly known as the Chinese birthwort, is a flowering plant native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, heart-shaped leaves and unique, winding flowers that resemble a pipe. The plant belongs to the Aristolochiaceae family and is known for its complex chemical composition, including various alkaloids. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal uses, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
