Aristida pallens var. tenuifolia

Aristida pallens var. tenuifolia, commonly known as slenderleaf threeawn, is a grass species native to arid and semi-arid regions of North America.

It is characterized by its fine, thread-like leaves and slender, bristled inflorescences that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed soils and is often found in grasslands and along roadsides. Its small, wind-dispersed seeds contribute to its ability to colonize new areas quickly.

The botanical features of this plant make it well-adapted to harsh environmental conditions.
