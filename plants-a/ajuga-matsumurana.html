Ajuga matsumurana

Ajuga matsumurana, commonly known as Japanese bugleweed, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the family Plantaginaceae and is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit. Ajuga matsumurana is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to stabilize soil in wetland habitats.
