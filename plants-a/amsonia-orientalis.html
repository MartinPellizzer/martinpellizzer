Amsonia orientalis

Amsonia orientalis, commonly known as blue mistflower, is a perennial herb native to North America.

It features aromatic, blue-green foliage and produces delicate, blue-violet flower clusters in late summer. The plant thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil. Its showy flowers attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.

Amsonia orientalis is often used in native plant gardens for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
