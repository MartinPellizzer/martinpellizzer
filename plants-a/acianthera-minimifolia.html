Acianthera minimifolia

Acianthera minimifolia, commonly known as the miniature orchid, is a small, terrestrial orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features delicate, slender stems and produces tiny, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale pink. The plant has a compact growth habit and is often found in shaded, humid environments. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it blooms during the warmer months.

Due to its small size and ornamental appeal, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
