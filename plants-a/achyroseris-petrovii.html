Achyroseris petrovii

Achyroseris petrovii, commonly known as the desert sunflower, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has a low, spreading growth habit with hairy stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. It is adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments through deep root systems and water-storing tissues.

Its flowers attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies, contributing to its ecological importance in its native habitat.
