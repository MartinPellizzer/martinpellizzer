Achillea × lundstroemii

Achillea × lundstroemii, commonly known as the hybrid yarrow, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Achillea millefolium (common yarrow) and Achillea ptarmica (wooly yarrow). The plant features finely divided, feathery leaves and produces clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers. It is native to Europe and thrives in well-drained soils in full sun.

Achillea × lundstroemii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in gardens for its ability to attract pollinators.
