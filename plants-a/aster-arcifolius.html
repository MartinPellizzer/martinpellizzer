Aster arcifolius

Aster arcifolius, commonly known as the narrowleaf aster, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and medicinal properties. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with hairy surfaces, and its flowers are blue to purple in color.

Aster arcifolius is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
