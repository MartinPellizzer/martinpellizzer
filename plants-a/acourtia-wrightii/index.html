Acourtia Wrightii

Acourtia wrightii is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the coastal scrub habitats of southern California. It thrives in sandy soils and is often found in areas with mild, coastal climates. This species is known for its small, showy white flowers that bloom in early spring. Due to habitat loss and environmental changes, Acourtia wrightii is classified as endangered. Conservation efforts are underway to protect and preserve this unique botanical species.
