Aloe × inermis

Aloe × inermis, commonly known as the hybrid aloe or hybrid aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is a cross between Aloe arborescens and Aloe ferox, resulting in a plant with unique characteristics. The leaves are thick, fleshy, and often have a reddish or pinkish hue, especially when exposed to sunlight. It produces yellow flowers in clusters during the summer months, adding ornamental value to gardens.

This plant is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it suitable for xeriscaping and rock gardens.
