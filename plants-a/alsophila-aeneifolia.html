Alsophila aeneifolia

Alsophila aeneifolia, commonly known as the silver fern, is a species of tree fern native to Southeast Asia.

It features a tall, slender trunk covered in smooth, silver-gray bark. The plant produces large, frond-like leaves that are deeply divided and resemble the shape of a fern. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

The silver fern is valued for its ornamental appearance and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
