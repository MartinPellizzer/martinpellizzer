Astrocaryum jauari

Astrocaryum jauari, commonly known as the jauari palm, is a tropical plant native to the Amazon Basin.

It is a member of the Arecaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate leaves. The plant produces yellowish-orange fruits that contain a single seed, which is often used in traditional practices. Its botanical structure supports its role in both ecological and cultural contexts within its native habitat.

The species is also valued for its ornamental appeal and potential economic uses.
