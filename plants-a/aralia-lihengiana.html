Aralia lihengiana

Aralia lihengiana, commonly known as the Chinese aralia, is a perennial plant native to China.

It belongs to the Araliaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and umbel-like inflorescence. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments and can reach heights of up to 2 meters. Its foliage is often used in traditional medicine, and it is valued for its ornamental qualities in gardens.

The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are followed by dark blue berries.
