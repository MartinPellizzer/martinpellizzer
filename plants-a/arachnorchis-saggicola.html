Arachnorchis saggicola

Arachnorchis saggicola, commonly known as the Saggicola orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to southern Australia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in spring. The plant has a fibrous root system and produces a single, narrow leaf. Its flowers are usually greenish-yellow with a distinctive, hairy labellum.

Arachnorchis saggicola is often found in open habitats such as heathlands and grassy areas.
