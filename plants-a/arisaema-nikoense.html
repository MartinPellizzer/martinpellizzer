Arisaema nikoense

Arisaema nikoense, commonly known as the Niko's arisaema, is a member of the Araceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Japan, specifically Honshu and Shikoku. The plant features a striking, spadix-shaped inflorescence surrounded by a hooded spathe, typical of the genus Arisaema. Its foliage consists of heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white and green patterns.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
