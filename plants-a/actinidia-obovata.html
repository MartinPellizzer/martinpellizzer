Actinidia obovata

Actinidia obovata, commonly known as the Chinese gooseberry, is a deciduous vine native to China.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and small, greenish-yellow flowers that appear in early summer. The plant produces edible berries that are rich in vitamin C and have a tart flavor. Actinidia obovata thrives in temperate climates and prefers well-drained soil.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and as a source of nutrition.
