Andryala integrifolia subsp. perennans

Andryala integrifolia subsp.

perennans, commonly known as the perennial sandverbena, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States. It is a low-growing perennial with opposite, ovate to lanceolate leaves that are typically hairy.
