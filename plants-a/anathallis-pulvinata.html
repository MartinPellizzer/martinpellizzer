Anathallis pulvinata

Anathallis pulvinata, commonly known as the pulvinate anathallis, is a species of orchid native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its delicate, pendulous inflorescence and small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments within the Atlantic Forest biome. Its leaves are ovate and slightly hairy, while its roots are adapted for epiphytic growth on trees.

Anathallis pulvinata is notable for its unique floral structure and is of interest to botanists and orchid enthusiasts alike.
