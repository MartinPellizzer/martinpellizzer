Austronea fimbrimarginata

Austronea fimbrimarginata, commonly known as the coastal morning glory, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Indo-Pacific.

It is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows along coastal areas and in wetlands, thriving in saline and humid conditions. Its leaves are heart-shaped and often have a waxy texture, while its stems are slender and climbing.

Austronea fimbrimarginata is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in stabilizing coastal soils.
