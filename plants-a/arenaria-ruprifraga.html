Arenaria ruprifraga

Arenaria ruprifraga, commonly known as the rock sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to rocky and arid environments.

It typically thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in alpine or mountainous regions. The plant features small, fleshy leaves that help it retain moisture in harsh conditions. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, appearing in clusters at the ends of stems.

Arenaria ruprifraga is valued for its resilience and ability to stabilize soil in rocky habitats.
