Achimenes bodneri-hirsuta

Achimenes bodneri-hirsuta, commonly known as the hairy achimenes, is a tropical perennial plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers that come in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant features hairy, glandular leaves that are often covered in fine, soft hairs. It thrives in warm, humid environments and prefers partial to full sunlight.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardeners and horticulturists seeking colorful, exotic plants.
