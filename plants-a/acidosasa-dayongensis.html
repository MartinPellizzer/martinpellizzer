Acidosasa dayongensis

Acidosasa dayongensis, commonly known as the Yunnan acacia, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its pod fruits and nitrogen-fixing ability. The plant features compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces yellow or pale green flowers. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in forested areas.
