Alsophila ramosii

Alsophila ramosii, commonly known as the slender tree fern, is a species native to Southeast Asia, including regions such as Malaysia and Indonesia.

It is a member of the Cyatheaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and a crown of feathery fronds. The plant has a robust rhizome that supports its growth and helps anchor it in moist, shaded environments. Its fronds are typically dark green and can reach lengths of up to 2 meters, contributing to its ornamental value.

Alsophila ramosii thrives in humid climates and is often found in tropical rainforests.
