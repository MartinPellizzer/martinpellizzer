Atriplex candicans

Atriplex candicans, commonly known as white gooseweed, is a flowering plant native to the arid and semi-arid regions of North America.

It is a member of the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves that help it conserve water in harsh environments.
