Athyrium mehrae

Athyrium mehrae, commonly known as the Mehra's lady fern, is a species of fern native to the Himalayas.

It belongs to the family Athyriaceae and is characterized by its finely divided, delicate fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often found in mountainous regions. Its spores are produced on the underside of the fronds, which are arranged in a distinctive, feather-like pattern.

The botanical features of Athyrium mehrae make it a valuable species for ecological studies and horticultural cultivation.
