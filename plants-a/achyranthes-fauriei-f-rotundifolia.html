Achyranthes fauriei f. rotundifolia

Achyranthes fauriei f. rotundifolia, commonly known as the roundedleaf betony, is a variant of the Achyranthes genus native to parts of Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant features broad, rounded leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its small, clustered flowers are usually pink or purple, blooming in late spring to early summer.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in woodland gardens.
