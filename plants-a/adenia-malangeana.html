Adenia malangeana

Adenia malangeana, commonly known as the Malangea adenium, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features thick, fleshy stems and leaves that are often lobed or deeply divided. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, with a distinctive structure that attracts pollinators like birds and insects.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
