Arenaria leptoclados var. minutiflora

Arenaria leptoclados var. minutiflora, commonly known as the dwarf sand-flower, is a low-growing perennial plant native to rocky and sandy soils in temperate regions.

It features small, often blue or white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has compact, mat-forming foliage with narrow, grass-like leaves. It is well adapted to harsh environments, tolerating drought and poor soil conditions.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for rock gardens and alpine landscapes.
