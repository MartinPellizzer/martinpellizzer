Achimenes calderonii

Achimenes calderonii, commonly known as the Mexican flamebush, is a tropical shrub native to Central America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange, often with a contrasting throat. The plant produces showy bracts that resemble petals, enhancing its ornamental appeal. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Achimenes calderonii is often cultivated for its striking floral display and is a popular choice in tropical gardens.
