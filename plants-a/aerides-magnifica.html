Aerides magnifica

Aerides magnifica, commonly known as the magnificent orchid, is a striking tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can reach up to 12 inches in diameter, with vibrant colors ranging from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its inflorescence is a raceme of fragrant, spurred flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer.

Aerides magnifica requires bright, indirect light and consistent humidity to thrive in cultivation.
