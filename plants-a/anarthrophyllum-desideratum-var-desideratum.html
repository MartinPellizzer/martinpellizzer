Anarthrophyllum desideratum var. desideratum

Anarthrophyllum desideratum var. desideratum, commonly known as the rare desert flax, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of central and southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, branched stems and small, tubular flowers that range in color from pale yellow to orange. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to withstand extreme temperature fluctuations and limited water availability. Its leaves are typically reduced to small scales, minimizing water loss through transpiration.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native range.
