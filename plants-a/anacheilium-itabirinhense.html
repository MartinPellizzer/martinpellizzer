Anacheilium itabirinhense

Anacheilium itabirinhense, commonly known as the Itabirinhense orchid, is a rare and endemic species native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique floral structure and vibrant colors. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments within the Atlantic Forest biome. It features a sympodial growth habit with pseudobulbs and linear leaves.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered critically endangered and requires conservation efforts to ensure its survival.
