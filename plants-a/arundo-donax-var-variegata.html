Arundo donax var. variegata

Arundo donax var. variegata, commonly known as the variegated giant reed, is a striking ornamental grass native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It features tall, slender stems that can reach up to 6 meters in height, with distinctive variegated leaves that display shades of white, green, and yellow. The plant is highly adaptable, thriving in wet or moist soils and full sun, making it suitable for water gardens or pond edges. Its rapid growth and ability to form dense clumps make it an effective erosion control plant.

However, it can become invasive in certain regions if not properly managed.
