Alstroemeria simsii f. roseopurpurea

Alstroemeria simsii f. roseopurpurea, commonly known as the purple-pink alstroemeria, is a hybrid cultivar prized for its vibrant, showy flowers.

It features elongated, tubular petals that range in color from deep purples to soft pinks, often with a contrasting throat. The plant has narrow, strap-like leaves that are arranged in a fan-like pattern, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for gardens and floral arrangements.

This cultivar is valued for its long-lasting blooms and adaptability to various growing conditions.
