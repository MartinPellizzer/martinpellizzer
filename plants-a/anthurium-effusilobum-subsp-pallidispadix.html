Anthurium effusilobum subsp. pallidispadix

Anthurium effusilobum subsp.

pallidispadix, commonly known as the pale-spadix anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central America. It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a waxy texture.

The plant produces striking, spadix-like inflorescences that are often pale green or white, contrasting with the colorful spathe. It thrives in humid, warm environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.

This species is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
