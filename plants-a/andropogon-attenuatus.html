Andropogon attenuatus

Andropogon attenuatus, commonly known as slender bluestem, is a tall grass native to North America.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, blue-green leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. This plant typically grows in open woodlands, prairies, and disturbed soils, thriving in full sun. Its inflorescence consists of a dense panicle of small, spikelet-bearing branches, which produce light seeds dispersed by wind.

Andropogon attenuatus plays an important role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various wildlife species.
