Asplenium siobarense

Asplenium siobarense, commonly known as the Sierra Nevada spleenwort, is a rare and endemic fern species native to the Sierra Nevada mountain range in California.

It is a small, epiphytic fern that typically grows on the trunks of trees or on rocky surfaces in moist, shaded environments. The plant has delicate, lance-shaped leaves that are often clustered closely together, and it produces spores in small, clustered clusters on the underside of its fronds. Asplenium siobarense is known for its limited distribution and is considered a vulnerable species due to habitat loss and environmental changes.
