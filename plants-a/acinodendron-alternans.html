Acinodendron alternans

Acinodendron alternans, commonly known as the zigzag acacia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its unique, zigzagging branches and bipinnate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in dense, globular inflorescences. Its seeds are encased in hard, woody pods that split open when mature.

Acinodendron alternans is adapted to arid environments and is often found in dry savannas and rocky areas.
