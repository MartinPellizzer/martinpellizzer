Adenaria parvifolia

Adenaria parvifolia, commonly known as the small-flowered adenaria, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its grass-like foliage and small, tubular flowers. Adenaria parvifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
