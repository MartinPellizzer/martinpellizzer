Abutilon ochsenii var. stellaris

Abutilon ochsenii var. stellaris, commonly known as the star abutilon, is a perennial shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its hairy, glandular leaves and showy, lantern-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant produces small, spherical fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its unique appearance and adaptability make it a distinctive addition to native plant collections.
