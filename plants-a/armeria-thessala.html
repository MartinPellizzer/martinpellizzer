Armeria thessala

Armeria thessala, commonly known as the Thessalian thrift, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the eastern Mediterranean region.

It features dense clusters of small, white or pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has linear, silver-gray leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Armeria thessala is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to tolerate harsh environmental conditions.
