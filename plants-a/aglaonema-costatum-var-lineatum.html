Aglaonema costatum var. lineatum

Aglaonema costatum var. lineatum, commonly known as the silver queen plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features striking, variegated foliage with broad, lance-shaped leaves that have a distinctive silver-white margin and green center. The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in low-light conditions and requires moderate humidity and well-draining soil.

Its foliage remains vibrant throughout the year, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
