Aristea homblei

Aristea homblei, commonly known as the silver carpet plant, is a low-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features dense clusters of narrow, silver-gray leaves that form a compact, mat-like appearance. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink or purple during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique foliage and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
