Arundo calamagrostis

Arundo calamagrostis, commonly known as green reed or common reed, is a tall, grass-like plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, cylindrical stems that are often green or bluish-green in color. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny flowers, typically blooming in late summer. Arundo calamagrostis is often found in wetlands, marshes, and along the edges of water bodies.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to stabilize soil in aquatic environments.
