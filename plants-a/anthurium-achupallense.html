Anthurium achupallense

Anthurium achupallense, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green. The plant produces vibrant red spathes that enclose its spadix, which is typically yellow or orange. Anthurium achupallense thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and air-purifying properties.
