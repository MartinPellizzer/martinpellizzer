Aloe × puberula

Aloe × puberula, commonly known as the hybrid aloe, is a succulent plant resulting from the cross between Aloe vera and Aloe arborescens.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a reddish tinge, especially under bright sunlight. The plant produces yellow, tubular flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Aloe × puberula is valued for its ornamental appeal and mild medicinal properties similar to its parent species.
