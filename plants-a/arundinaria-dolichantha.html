Arundinaria dolichantha

Arundinaria dolichantha, commonly known as the Chinese bamboo or giant bamboo, is a species of grass native to China.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks that can grow up to several meters in height. The plant has long, narrow leaves that are typically green and arranged in a spiral pattern. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are clustered in panicles.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
