Avena sativa subsp. nodipilosa

Avena sativa subsp.

nodipilosa, commonly known as the nodular oat, is a lesser-known subspecies of the oat plant. It is characterized by its compact growth habit and shorter, more robust stems compared to typical oat varieties.

The leaves are generally narrower and more upright, contributing to its distinct appearance. This subspecies is adapted to specific environmental conditions, often found in regions with cooler climates.

Its botanical features make it of interest for ecological studies and potential agricultural use.
