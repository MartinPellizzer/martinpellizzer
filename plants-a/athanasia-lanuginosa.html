Athanasia lanuginosa

Athanasia lanuginosa, commonly known as the woolly everlasting, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its soft, hairy leaves and showy yellow flowers. The plant is evergreen and thrives in well-drained, sandy soils. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.

Athanasia lanuginosa is also used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
