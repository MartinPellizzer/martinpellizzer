Artemisia globularia f. lutea

Artemisia globularia f. lutea, commonly known as the yellow globular tarragon, is a compact, low-growing shrub native to southern Europe.

It features small, gray-green, aromatic leaves that are densely packed and often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in late summer, which are borne in clusters atop the foliage. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a border plant.

Its aromatic properties and ornamental value make it a popular choice in horticulture.
