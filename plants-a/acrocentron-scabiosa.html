Acrocentron scabiosa

Acrocentron scabiosa, commonly known as the scarlet pimpernel, is a flowering plant native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It is characterized by its small, bright yellow flowers with five petals, which often change color in response to light and temperature. The plant has hairy, lance-shaped leaves and thrives in sunny, well-drained soils. It is often found in meadows, along roadsides, and in disturbed areas.

Acrocentron scabiosa is a short-lived perennial that blooms from spring to early summer.
