Aloe trichosantha subsp. trichosantha

Aloe trichosantha subsp.

trichosantha, commonly known as the Cape aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa. It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically gray-green in color, often with a waxy coating to reduce water loss.

The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing yellow-orange flowers, which attract pollinators like bees and birds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for arid and semi-arid climates.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional uses in herbal medicine.
