Arenaria verna f. epilis

Arenaria verna f. epilis, commonly known as the mossy sandwort, is a horticultural form of the sandwort plant.

It is a low-growing, mat-forming perennial that thrives in moist, well-drained soils. The plant features dense clusters of small, white flowers that bloom in early spring. Its foliage is characterized by a dense, moss-like appearance, giving it a unique ornamental quality.

It is often cultivated in rock gardens or as a ground cover due to its compact growth habit and ability to tolerate partial shade.
