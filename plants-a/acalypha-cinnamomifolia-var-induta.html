Acalypha cinnamomifolia var. induta

Acalypha cinnamomifolia var. induta, commonly known as the cinnamon leaf acalypha, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its aromatic, cinnamon-scented leaves that are often used in traditional medicine.
