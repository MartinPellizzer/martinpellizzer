Arisaema cangshanense

Arisaema cangshanense, commonly known as the Cangshan arrowhead, is a herbaceous plant native to China.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive inflorescence consisting of a spadix and a spathe. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in mountainous regions. Its leaves are arrow-shaped, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in Chinese medicine.
