Aloe schinzii

Aloe schinzii, commonly known as the Schinz's aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, blue-green leaves that are covered in a layer of white powder, known as farina. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow flowers during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Aloe schinzii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional medicinal uses.
