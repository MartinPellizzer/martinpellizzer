Alternanthera pilosa var. microphylla

Alternanthera pilosa var. microphylla, commonly known as the small-leaf Alternanthera, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, colorful foliage that can range from red to purple. The leaves are small, lance-shaped, and often display striking variegation. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or white.

This plant is popular in aquatic and terrestrial gardens for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation.
