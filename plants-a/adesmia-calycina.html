Adesmia calycina

Adesmia calycina, commonly known as the velvetleaf tree, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is a small to medium-sized tree that typically grows up to 10 meters in height. The plant has palmate leaves with five to seven lobes, and its flowers are small, clustered in inflorescences. It produces small, yellowish fruits that contain seeds enclosed in a fleshy, orange-red aril.
