Anthurium taylorianum

Anthurium taylorianum, commonly known as the Taylor's anthurium, is a tropical flowering plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped spathe that ranges in color from deep red to orange. The plant produces clusters of small, spadix-like flowers that are typically green or yellow. It thrives in humid, warm environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Its striking appearance makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and botanical collections.
