Aegilops ventricosa var. subulata

Aegilops ventricosa var. subulata, commonly known as the slender spikegrass, is a perennial grass species native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its slender, spike-like inflorescence and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments. It has small, wind-pollinated flowers and produces distinctive, three-angled seeds.

This variety is often found in open habitats such as coastal dunes and limestone slopes.
