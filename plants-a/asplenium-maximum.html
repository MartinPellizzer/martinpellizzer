Asplenium maximum

Asplenium maximum, commonly known as the giant spleenwort, is a fern that belongs to the family Aspleniaceae.

It is characterized by its tall, erect fronds that can reach up to 1.5 meters in height, making it one of the tallest ferns in the genus Asplenium. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as rocky crevices or forest floors. Its leaves are pinnately compound, with lance-shaped pinnae that are often covered in a dense layer of fine hairs.

Asplenium maximum is primarily found in temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere, where it thrives in humid and cool climates.
