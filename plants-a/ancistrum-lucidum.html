Ancistrum lucidum

Ancistrum lucidum, commonly known as the bright ancistrum, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family, which includes plants such as philodendrons and peace lilies. The plant is characterized by its striking, waxy leaves that are often a deep green with a glossy appearance. Its inflorescence consists of a spadix surrounded by a spathe, typical of the Araceae family.

Ancistrum lucidum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in both indoor and outdoor gardens.
