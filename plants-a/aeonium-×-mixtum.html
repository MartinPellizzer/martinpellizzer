Aeonium × mixtum

Aeonium × mixtum, commonly known as the Rainbow Aeonium, is a hybrid succulent plant belonging to the Crassulaceae family.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped growth pattern and vibrant, colorful foliage that can range from green to red, pink, and purple. The plant is native to the Canary Islands and thrives in well-drained soil with ample sunlight. Its leaves are thick and fleshy, adapted for storing water, making it highly drought-tolerant.

Aeonium × mixtum is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and ease of care.
