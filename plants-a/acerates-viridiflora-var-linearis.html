Acerates viridiflora var. linearis

Acerates viridiflora var. linearis, commonly known as the Green-flowered Maple, is a variant of the Viridiflora Maple characterized by its narrow, linear leaves.

This plant exhibits a distinct botanical feature with its foliage displaying a vibrant green hue, often with subtle variegation. The flowers are small and greenish-white, typically appearing in clusters, adding to its ornamental value. It thrives in temperate climates and prefers well-drained soil.

Its unique leaf morphology and coloration make it a notable species in horticultural collections.
