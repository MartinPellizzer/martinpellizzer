Aristolochia lauterbachiana

Aristolochia lauterbachiana, commonly known as the Lauterbach's Dutchman's pipe, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its unique, pipe-shaped flowers that are often green or reddish in color. The plant produces heart-shaped leaves with a rough texture and is typically found in moist, wooded areas. It belongs to the Aristolochiaceae family and is known for its ornamental value.

However, it contains toxic compounds and should be handled with care.
