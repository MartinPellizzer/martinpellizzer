Aster souliei

Aster souliei, commonly known as the Chinese aster, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows to a height of 30 to 60 centimeters, featuring narrow, lance-shaped leaves and vibrant purple or pink flowers. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and is also used in traditional Chinese medicine.

Aster souliei thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking colorful, hardy plants.
