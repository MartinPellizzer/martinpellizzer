Antiosorus

Antiosorus, commonly known as the purple false nettle, is a perennial herb native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the family Urticaceae, which includes other nettle relatives. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive purple underside, and it produces small, greenish-white flowers in clusters. Antiosorus is often found in moist, shaded areas such as woodlands and stream banks.
