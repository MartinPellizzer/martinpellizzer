Anthurium stockwellii

Anthurium stockwellii, commonly known as the Stockwell's anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped leaves that are typically deep green with a glossy surface. The plant produces striking spadices, which are modified leaves that enclose the true flowers, often in shades of red or orange. It thrives in humid, warm environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium stockwellii is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
