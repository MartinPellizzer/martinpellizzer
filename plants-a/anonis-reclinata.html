Anonis reclinata

Anonis reclinata, commonly known as the hanging anona, is a tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is a climbing shrub that typically grows along forest edges or in shaded understories. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its leaves are elliptical with a smooth texture and a slightly waxy surface.

The fruit is a small, round berry that turns from green to yellow or red when mature.
