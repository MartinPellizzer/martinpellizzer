Anthurium andraeanum var. atropurpureum

Anthurium andraeanum var. atropurpureum, commonly known as the Black Velvet Anthurium, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its dark purple to black, velvety leaves and vibrant red spathes that enclose the spadix. The plant belongs to the Araceae family and is valued for its ornamental qualities in both indoor and outdoor settings. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Regular watering and well-draining soil are essential for its healthy growth.
