Arisaema liemiana

Arisaema liemiana, commonly known as the Liem's arisaema, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its unique inflorescence consisting of a spadix surrounded by a spathe. The plant features a rhizomatous underground stem and produces striking, often colorful, leaf structures. Its leaves are typically large, heart-shaped, and have a velvety texture.

Arisaema liemiana is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine in some regions.
