Attalea dubia

Attalea dubia, commonly known as the Amazonian palm or yellow palm, is a tall, slender palm native to the Amazon rainforest.

It features a smooth, grayish trunk that can grow up to 15 meters in height. The plant produces large, yellow-orange inflorescences that are highly visible and attract various pollinators. Its leaves are pinnately compound, with long, narrow leaflets that contribute to its distinctive appearance.

The plant plays an important ecological role in the rainforest by providing habitat and food for numerous species.
