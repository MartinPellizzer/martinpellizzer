Anthurium nymphaeifolium var. roezlii

Anthurium nymphaeifolium var. roezlii, commonly known as the Water Lily Leaf Anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that resemble water lily leaves, hence its common name. The plant produces striking spadices with vibrant red or orange hues, surrounded by white or green spatulate leaves. It thrives in high humidity and indirect light, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique foliage and exotic flowers make it a valuable addition to botanical collections and ornamental plantings.
