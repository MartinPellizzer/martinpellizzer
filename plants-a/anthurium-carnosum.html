Anthurium carnosum

Anthurium carnosum, commonly known as the velvet leaf anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in a dense, velvety texture. The plant produces striking spadices, which are the modified leaves that surround the true flowers. Its foliage is often a deep green with a reddish or purple tint on the underside.

Anthurium carnosum thrives in humid, shaded environments and is popular as a decorative houseplant.
