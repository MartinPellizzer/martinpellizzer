Anthemis montana var. subcinerea

Anthemis montana var. subcinerea, commonly known as the gray anthemis, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and small, yellowish flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in full sun. Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and alpine landscapes.

The subspecies subcinerea is distinguished by its more densely hairy leaves compared to the typical form.
