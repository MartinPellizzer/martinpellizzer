Armeria Maritima Var. Pubigera

Armeria maritima var. pubigera is a hardy coastal plant known for its vibrant pink flowers and salt-tolerant nature. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils along shorelines and cliffs. This variety is prized for its ornamental value and ability to stabilize dunes. Its compact growth makes it ideal for rock gardens and seaside landscapes. It remains a resilient symbol of nature's adaptation to harsh environments.
