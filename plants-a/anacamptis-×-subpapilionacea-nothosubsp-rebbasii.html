Anacamptis × subpapilionacea nothosubsp.

rebbasii, commonly known as the Rebbas' lady's mantle, is a hybrid orchid species native to southern Europe. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, spurred orchid flowers that are typically pink or purple in color.

The plant features a basal rosette of lance-shaped leaves and produces showy, butterfly-like petals. It thrives in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in woodland or meadow habitats.

This nothosubspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
