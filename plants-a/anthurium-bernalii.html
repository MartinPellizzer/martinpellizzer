Anthurium bernalii

Anthurium bernalii, commonly known as the Bernal's arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves and vibrant, spadix-like inflorescence. The plant thrives in humid, warm environments with indirect light and well-draining soil. Its foliage is often a deep green with a glossy texture, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers surrounded by a colorful spathe.

Anthurium bernalii is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
