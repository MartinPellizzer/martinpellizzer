Abarema racemiflora

Abarema racemiflora, commonly known as the pink flame tree, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its striking pink or red flowers that bloom in racemes. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces pods that contain seeds. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant floral display.

Abarema racemiflora thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
