Alsobia punctata

Alsobia punctata, commonly known as the velvet tunic, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, velvety purple bracts that resemble a tunic. The plant produces small, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, which are borne on whorled inflorescences. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered in a fine, velvety texture.

Alsobia punctata is often grown as an ornamental plant for its vibrant and long-lasting foliage and flowers.
