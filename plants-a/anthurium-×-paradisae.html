Anthurium × paradisae

Anthurium × paradisae, commonly known as the paradise anthurium, is a hybrid plant native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-shaped inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, often in shades of red, orange, or yellow. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium × paradisae is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
