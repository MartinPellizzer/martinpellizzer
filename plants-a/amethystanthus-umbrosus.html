Amethystanthus umbrosus

Amethystanthus umbrosus, commonly known as the shadow orchid, is a rare and striking plant native to the mountainous regions of South Africa.

It is characterized by its unique, violet-colored flowers that resemble tiny amethyst crystals, hence its common name. The plant is a terrestrial orchid that thrives in shaded, moist environments, often growing in rocky soils. Its blooming period typically occurs during the winter months, adding a rare beauty to the landscape.

Due to its limited habitat and specific ecological requirements, it is considered a vulnerable species.
