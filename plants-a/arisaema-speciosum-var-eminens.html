Arisaema speciosum var. eminens

Arisaema speciosum var. eminens, commonly known as the Giant Chinese Arum, is a striking ornamental plant native to the mountainous regions of southern China.

It features a large, heart-shaped leaf that can grow up to 1 meter in diameter, with a distinctive spathe that is typically green or white with a red or purple tint. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green spadices, which are the actual flowering structures. It thrives in shady, moist environments and is often cultivated for its dramatic foliage and unique inflorescence.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes grown in gardens and botanical collections.
