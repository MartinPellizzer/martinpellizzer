Aster tubulosus

Aster tubulosus, commonly known as the slender aster, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that consist of both ray and disk flowers. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and woodlands. It produces small, tubular flowers that are usually blue to purple in color.

Aster tubulosus is often used in native plant gardens due to its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
