Anemone latifolia

Anemone latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf anemone, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the eastern United States.

It features showy, white to pale pink flowers with a distinctive central cluster of yellow stamens. The plant has palmately lobed leaves that are typically broad and heart-shaped. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Anemone latifolia is often found in woodland habitats and is valued for its ornamental beauty and ecological benefits.
