Astrotricha floccosa var. subpeltata

Astrotricha floccosa var. subpeltata, commonly known as the fluffcap or woolly star, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the western United States.

It features a basal rosette of soft, hairy, spoon-shaped leaves that are covered in white, woolly hairs. The plant produces small, star-like yellow flowers in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are characterized by their distinctive, narrow, and peltate (shield-shaped) structure.

It thrives in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes.
