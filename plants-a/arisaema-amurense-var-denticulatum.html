Arisaema amurense var. denticulatum

Arisaema amurense var. denticulatum, commonly known as the toothed arrowhead, is a herbaceous plant native to parts of East Asia.

It features a distinctive arrowhead-shaped leaf that is often deeply lobed and has serrated edges. The plant produces a spadix surrounded by a spathe, typical of the Araceae family. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forested areas.

Its botanical characteristics make it a notable species in both horticulture and ecological studies.
