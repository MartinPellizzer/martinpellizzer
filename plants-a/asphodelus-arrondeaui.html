Asphodelus arrondeaui

Asphodelus arrondeaui, commonly known as the Spanish asphodel, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in dry, rocky habitats. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of yellow or orange flowers. It belongs to the family Asphodelaceae, which includes many species adapted to arid environments.

Asphodelus arrondeaui is often used in traditional medicine and has cultural significance in various regions.
