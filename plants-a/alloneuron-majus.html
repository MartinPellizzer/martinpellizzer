Alloneuron majus

Alloneuron majus, commonly known as the giant flame tree, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy red flowers that bloom in clusters, creating a dramatic visual impact. The plant has a straight, unbranched trunk and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in warm climates. Its leaves are bipinnate, meaning they are divided into small, feather-like segments.

Alloneuron majus thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for tropical gardens.
