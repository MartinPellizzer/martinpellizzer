Anerincleistus dajakorum

Anerincleistus dajakorum, commonly known as the Dajakorum orchid, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, nutrient-rich soil to flourish. Its unique morphology includes elongated leaves and a compact growth habit, making it a sought-after species among botanical enthusiasts.
