Asplenium kauaiense

Asplenium kauaiense, commonly known as Kauai spleenwort, is a rare fern native to the Hawaiian island of Kauai.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that grows on the bark of trees, typically in humid, shaded environments. The plant has slender, dark green fronds with a distinctive, narrow shape and a serrated margin. It belongs to the family Aspleniaceae and is characterized by its unique reproductive structures.

Due to habitat loss and limited distribution, it is considered endangered and is of significant interest to botanists and conservationists.
