Anthurium guadalupeae

Anthurium guadalupeae, commonly known as Guadalupe arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces a spadix, which is surrounded by a colorful spathe, typical of the genus Anthurium. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Anthurium guadalupeae is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is appreciated for its unique foliage.
