Aralia chinensis var. glabrescens

Aralia chinensis var. glabrescens, commonly known as the Chinese aralia, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features compound leaves that are typically palmately lobed, with a smooth, glabrous surface. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers in compound umbels, followed by red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
