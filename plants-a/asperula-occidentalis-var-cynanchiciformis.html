Asperula occidentalis var. cynanchiciformis

Asperula occidentalis var. cynanchiciformis, commonly known as the western asperula, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the western United States.

It features small, dark green leaves that are glossy and arranged in a dense, bushy habit. The plant produces clusters of delicate, white to pale pink flowers in late spring to early summer. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained, acidic soil.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to tolerate urban conditions.
