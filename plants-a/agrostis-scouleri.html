Agrostis scouleri

Agrostis scouleri, commonly known as Scouler's bentgrass, is a perennial grass species native to western North America.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and stream banks. The plant has slender, tufted stems and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green color.

Agrostis scouleri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in native plant gardens.
