Allium aladaghense

Allium aladaghense, commonly known as the Aladagh garlic, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Allium genus, which includes various species of onions and garlic. Its leaves are grass-like and narrow, while its bulbous stem is used for culinary and medicinal purposes.
