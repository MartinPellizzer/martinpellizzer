Agrostemma × walkeri

Agrostemma × walkeri, commonly known as the hybrid wallflower, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Brassicaceae.

It is a naturally occurring hybrid between Agrostemma githago and other related species, often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides. Agrostemma × walkeri is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
