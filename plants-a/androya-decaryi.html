Androya decaryi

Androya decaryi, commonly known as the Madagascar lily, is a striking plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its large, showy, white flowers with a distinctive yellow center. The plant has strap-like, dark green leaves that grow from a bulbous base. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Androya decaryi is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
