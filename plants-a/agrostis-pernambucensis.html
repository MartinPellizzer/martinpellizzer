Agrostis pernambucensis

Agrostis pernambucensis, commonly known as the Pernambuco grass, is a perennial grass species native to South America.

It is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and fine, silver-green foliage. The plant typically thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and wetlands. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny flowers.

Agrostis pernambucensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping and erosion control.
