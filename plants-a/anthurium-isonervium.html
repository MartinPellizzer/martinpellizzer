Anthurium isonervium

Anthurium isonervium, commonly known as the velvet leaf anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a velvety texture. The plant produces striking spadices, which are the inflorescences that resemble flowers but are actually modified leaves. Its foliage is often variegated with shades of red, pink, and white, adding to its ornamental value.

Anthurium isonervium thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
