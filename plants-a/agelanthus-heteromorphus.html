Agelanthus heteromorphus

Agelanthus heteromorphus, commonly known as the variable agelanthus, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the family Acanthaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, often asymmetrical leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically arranged in clusters, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight.
