Artemisia fruticosa

Artemisia fruticosa, commonly known as silver sagebrush, is a shrub native to North America.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which consists of small, aromatic leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in open plains and rocky areas.

Artemisia fruticosa is also known for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally by Indigenous peoples.
