Anthyllis Terniflora

Anthyllis terniflora is a flowering plant known for its delicate, fern-like leaves and clusters of small, pea-like flowers. Native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa, it thrives in rocky and arid environments. This perennial species is often found in open woodlands and along coastal cliffs. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and drought-tolerant landscaping. Despite its beauty, it remains a relatively obscure plant in the broader gardening community.
