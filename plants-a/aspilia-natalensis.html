Aspilia natalensis

Aspilia natalensis, commonly known as Natal torch or Natal flame, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking, bright orange to red flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spiny fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and open woodlands.

Aspilia natalensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
