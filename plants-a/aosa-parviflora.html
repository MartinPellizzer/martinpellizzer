Aosa parviflora

Aosa parviflora, commonly known as the small-flowered aosa, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, small white or pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Aosa parviflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its elegant appearance.
