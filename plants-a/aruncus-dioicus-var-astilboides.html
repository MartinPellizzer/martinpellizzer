Aruncus dioicus var. astilboides

Aruncus dioicus var. astilboides, commonly known as the Japanese fern-ferns, is a herbaceous perennial native to eastern Asia.

It features delicate, plume-like inflorescences that are white to pink in color, resembling the flowers of the astilbe plant. The plant has compound, feather-like leaves that are dark green and glossy, providing an ornamental texture. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often used in woodland gardens.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for adding visual interest to shaded landscapes.
