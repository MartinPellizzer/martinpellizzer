Aleuritia souliei

Aleuritia souliei, commonly known as the Chinese snowbell, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its clusters of small, white, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that turn yellow in autumn. It prefers moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade.

Aleuritia souliei is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and adaptability to various garden conditions.
