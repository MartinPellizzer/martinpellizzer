Allionia cristata

Allionia cristata, commonly known as the crested allionia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, upward-facing flowers that are typically pink or white. Allionia cristata is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
