Asplenium pseudonitidum var. schmalzii

Asplenium pseudonitidum var. schmalzii, commonly known as the Schmalz's spleenwort, is a fern species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Aspleniaceae and is characterized by its small, clustered growth habit. The plant features lanceolate, serrated leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found on rocks or tree trunks.

Its reproductive structures, including sporangia, are located on the underside of the fronds, typical of ferns.
