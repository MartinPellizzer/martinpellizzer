Aristolochia polyrrhiza

Aristolochia polyrrhiza, commonly known as the broad-leaved Dutchman's pipe, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its distinctive, pipe-shaped flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with a rough texture and is often found in moist, shaded environments. It belongs to the Aristolochiaceae family and is known for its complex floral structure.

However, it contains toxic compounds and should be handled with care.
