Amaryllis vaginata

Amaryllis vaginata, commonly known as the pink amaryllis, is a striking bulbous plant native to South America.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically pink or red, with a distinctive greenish throat. The plant produces large, strap-like leaves that are often spotted or streaked with green. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Amaryllis vaginata is popular as a decorative plant due to its vibrant blooms and relatively easy care requirements.
