Alsophila saparuensis

Alsophila saparuensis, commonly known as the Saparuena tree fern, is a species native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feathery fronds that can reach several meters in length. The plant belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is known for its ability to thrive in humid, shaded environments. Its rhizome system supports the growth of new shoots, contributing to its dense canopy structure.
