Amaryllis kermesina

Amaryllis kermesina, commonly known as the red amaryllis, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, sturdy stems that bear clusters of vibrant red flowers with prominent yellow stamens. The plant produces large, heart-shaped leaves that are broad and glossy, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and containers.

Amaryllis kermesina is known for its long blooming period, often lasting several weeks during the summer months.
