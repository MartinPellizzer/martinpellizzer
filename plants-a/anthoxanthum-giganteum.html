Anthoxanthum giganteum

Anthoxanthum giganteum, commonly known as giant feather grass, is a perennial grass species native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and long, feathery plumes that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, inconspicuous flowers in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping and erosion control.
