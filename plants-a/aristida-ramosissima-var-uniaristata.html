Aristida ramosissima var. uniaristata

Aristida ramosissima var. uniaristata, commonly known as the slender threeawn, is a grass species native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its slender, wiry stems and small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. Its leaves are narrow and often have a rough texture, and it produces wind-dispersed seeds.

This variety is notable for its ecological role in stabilizing soil and providing forage for wildlife.
