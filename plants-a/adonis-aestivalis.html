Adonis aestivalis

Adonis aestivalis, commonly known as the summer adonis, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It features bright yellow flowers that bloom in early summer, typically from June to July. The plant has a basal rosette of leaves and produces elongated, branching stems that bear the showy flowers. Its foliage consists of lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture, and it is often found in rocky or sandy soils.

Adonis aestivalis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
