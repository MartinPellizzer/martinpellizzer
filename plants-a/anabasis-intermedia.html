Anabasis intermedia

Anabasis intermedia, commonly known as the desert sandmat, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of Asia and the Middle East.

It is a low-growing shrub with a deep root system that allows it to survive in harsh, dry environments. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are typically small and fleshy, adapted to reduce water loss.

Anabasis intermedia is often used in traditional medicine and is also valued for its ability to stabilize sandy soils.
