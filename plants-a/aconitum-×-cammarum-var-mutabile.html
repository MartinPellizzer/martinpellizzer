Aconitum × cammarum var. mutabile

Aconitum × cammarum var. mutabile, commonly known as the variable monkshood, is a hybrid perennial plant belonging to the Ranunculaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking, hood-shaped flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. The plant features palmate leaves with a distinctive, waxy texture and a strong, toxic scent. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade in temperate climates.

Due to its toxicity, it should be handled with care and is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value.
