Aulomyrcia ottonis

Aulomyrcia ottonis, commonly known as the Mexican yellowwood, is a small evergreen tree native to Mexico.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae and is characterized by its smooth, dark bark and glossy, elliptical leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by dark blue berries. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often found in moist, well-drained soils.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and as a landscape plant.
