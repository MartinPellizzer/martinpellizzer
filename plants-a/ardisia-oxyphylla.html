Ardisia oxyphylla

Ardisia oxyphylla, commonly known as the flame bush, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Asia and the Pacific.

It features small, glossy, dark green leaves that are opposite in arrangement. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pinkish flowers, followed by bright red berries that are attractive to birds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.

Ardisia oxyphylla is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in shaded garden environments.
