Axyris sphaerosperma var. caucasica

Axyris sphaerosperma var. caucasica, commonly known as the Caucasian axyris, is a perennial herb native to the Caucasus region.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters. The plant is characterized by its distinctive, spherical seed pods that mature to a dark brown color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or mountainous habitats.

Due to its unique morphology and limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
