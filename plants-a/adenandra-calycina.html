Adenandra calycina

Adenandra calycina, commonly known as the Natal hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 3 to 6 meters. The plant features showy, five-petaled flowers that are usually bright yellow or orange in color. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a rough texture.

Adenandra calycina is known for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its attractive blooms.
