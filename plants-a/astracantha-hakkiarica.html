Astracantha hakkiarica

Astracantha hakkiarica, commonly known as the Hakkari thornveld, is a flowering plant native to the Hakkari region of Turkey.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its spiny stems and small, yellowish flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its leaves are narrow and covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.

Astracantha hakkiarica is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and shrublands.
