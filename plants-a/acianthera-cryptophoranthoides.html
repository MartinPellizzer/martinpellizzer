Acianthera cryptophoranthoides

Acianthera cryptophoranthoides, commonly known as the cryptophoranthoides orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in a spike, often with a faintly fragrant scent. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a delicate species with limited distribution.
