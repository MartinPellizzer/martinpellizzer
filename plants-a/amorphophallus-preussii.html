Amorphophallus preussii

Amorphophallus preussii, commonly known as the giant arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves that can grow up to 3 meters in length. The plant produces a striking, spadix inflorescence that is often covered in a red or purple spathe. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Due to its unique appearance and rapid growth, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
