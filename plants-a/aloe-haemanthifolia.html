Aloe haemanthifolia

Aloe haemanthifolia, commonly known as the Cape aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a bluish tint and have a spiny margin. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow-orange flowers during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its leaves to survive drought conditions.

Aloe haemanthifolia is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional medicinal uses.
