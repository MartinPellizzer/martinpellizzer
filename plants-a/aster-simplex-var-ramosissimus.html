Aster simplex var. ramosissimus

Aster simplex var. ramosissimus, commonly known as the showy aster, is a herbaceous perennial native to North America.

It is characterized by its clusters of small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in the fall. The plant has a bushy growth habit with numerous branches, giving it a compact and dense appearance. Its leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and covered in fine hairs.

It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is often used in gardens for its ornamental value.
