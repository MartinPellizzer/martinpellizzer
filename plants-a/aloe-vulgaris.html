Aloe vulgaris

Aloe vulgaris, commonly known as the common aloe, is a succulent plant native to Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces tall, branching flower stalks that bear yellowish or greenish flowers. Its gel-like pulp is widely used for its soothing and healing properties.

Aloe vulgaris is valued in both horticulture and traditional medicine for its resilience and versatility.
