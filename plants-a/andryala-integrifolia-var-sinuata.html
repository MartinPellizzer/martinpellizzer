Andryala integrifolia var. sinuata

Andryala integrifolia var. sinuata, commonly known as the smoothleaf andryala, is a herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia.

It features compound leaves with entire margins and a distinctive, aromatic scent. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical characteristics make it a subject of interest in ecological and medicinal studies.
