Acianthera obscura

Acianthera obscura, commonly known as the dark acianthera, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its small, dark green leaves and compact growth habit. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers that are typically violet or purple in color. It thrives in shaded, moist environments, often found in forest understories.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a conservation concern in many regions.
