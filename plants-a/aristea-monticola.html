Aristea monticola

Aristea monticola, commonly known as the mountain aristea, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, mat-forming growth habit. The plant produces small, daisy-like flowers that are typically white or pale blue, blooming in spring and summer. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often covered in fine hairs.

Aristea monticola is well adapted to rocky and arid environments, making it a resilient choice for rock gardens.
