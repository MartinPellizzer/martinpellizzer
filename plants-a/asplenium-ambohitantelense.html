Asplenium ambohitantelense

Asplenium ambohitantelense, commonly known as the Madagascar shield fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the family Aspleniaceae and is characterized by its compact, rosette-like growth habit. The plant features dark green, leathery fronds with a distinctive shield-shaped appearance, which gives it its common name. It thrives in shaded, humid environments, typically found in forest undergrowth.

Asplenium ambohitantelense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant.
