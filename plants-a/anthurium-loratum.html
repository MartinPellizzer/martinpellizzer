Anthurium loratum

Anthurium loratum, commonly known as the red-tailed anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are often a deep green with a reddish tinge on the underside. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-shaped inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, typically in shades of red or orange. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect light to flourish.

Anthurium loratum is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
