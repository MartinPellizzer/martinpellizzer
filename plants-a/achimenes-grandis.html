Achimenes grandis

Achimenes grandis, commonly known as the showy achimenes, is a tropical perennial plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that come in a variety of vibrant colors such as red, orange, and yellow. The plant has heart-shaped, dark green leaves that are glossy and arranged oppositely along the stems. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Achimenes grandis is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its showy blooms and is popular in gardens and floral arrangements.
