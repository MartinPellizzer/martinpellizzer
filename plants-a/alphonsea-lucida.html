Alphonsea lucida

Alphonsea lucida, commonly known as the shining alphonsea, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by orange-red berries. Its bark is smooth and grayish, with a distinctive scent when crushed.

Alphonsea lucida is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
