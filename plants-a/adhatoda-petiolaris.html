Adhatoda petiolaris

Adhatoda petiolaris, commonly known as the Indian Trumpet or Kuth, is a flowering plant native to India and parts of Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 3 meters tall, characterized by its opposite, elliptical leaves and fragrant, trumpet-shaped flowers. The plant produces small, oval fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often found in moist, well-drained soils.

Botanically, it belongs to the family Acanthaceae and is valued for its ornamental and medicinal properties.
