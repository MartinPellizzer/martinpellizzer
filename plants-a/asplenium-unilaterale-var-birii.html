Asplenium unilaterale var. birii

Asplenium unilaterale var. birii, commonly known as the birii spleenwort, is a fern belonging to the Aspleniaceae family.

It is a small, epiphytic or lithophytic plant that typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves with a distinctive asymmetrical base, giving it its common name. Its fronds are usually dark green and have a leathery texture.

It is found primarily in tropical and subtropical regions of Asia and is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.
