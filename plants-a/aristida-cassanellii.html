Aristida cassanellii

Aristida cassanellii, commonly known as the Cassanelli's threeawn, is a perennial grass native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in open grasslands and rocky areas, preferring well-drained soils. The plant produces slender, spike-like inflorescences composed of small, bristly spikelets. Its foliage is narrow and grass-like, with a distinctive silver-blue hue due to the fine hairs on the leaves.

It is adapted to arid conditions and is often found in regions with low rainfall.
