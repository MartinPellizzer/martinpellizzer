Asyneuma lanceolatum f. stenophyllum

Asyneuma lanceolatum f. stenophyllum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved asyneuma, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and small, daisy-like yellow flowers. This variant is typically found in arid or semi-arid regions, where it adapts to dry conditions with reduced leaf surface area. The plant exhibits a compact growth habit and is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value.

Its botanical features make it well-suited for xeriscaping and coastal gardens.
