Agrostophyllum reichenbachii

Agrostophyllum reichenbachii, commonly known as the narrow-leafed silver grass, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, silver-gray leaves and small, inconspicuous flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and thrives in full sun. It is often used in ornamental gardening for its fine texture and drought tolerance.

Its botanical features make it well-suited for xeriscaping and coastal landscapes.
