Anthurium nanum

Anthurium nanum, commonly known as the dwarf anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact size, typically reaching only a few inches in height, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening. The plant features heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. Its striking spadix, surrounded by a colorful spathe, adds to its ornamental value.

Anthurium nanum thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and high humidity to flourish.
