Alternanthera argentata

Alternanthera argentata, commonly known as silver velvet plant, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking silver-gray foliage, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or white in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This ornamental plant is often used in water gardens and as a decorative foliage plant in containers.
