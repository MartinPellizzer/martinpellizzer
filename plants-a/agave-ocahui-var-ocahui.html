Agave ocahui var. ocahui

Agave ocahui var. ocahui, commonly known as the Ocahui agave, is a succulent plant native to the arid regions of Mexico.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped growth pattern and thick, fleshy leaves that are often blue-green in color. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with numerous white to pale purple flowers. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it well-suited for xeriscaping.

The species is also valued for its ornamental appeal and potential use in traditional practices.
