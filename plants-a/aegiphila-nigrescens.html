Aegiphila nigrescens

Aegiphila nigrescens, commonly known as blackleaf aegiphila, is a flowering plant native to the Andean regions of South America.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its dark green, leathery leaves that turn reddish-brown with age. It produces small, tubular flowers that are typically purple or pink in color.

Aegiphila nigrescens is often found in rocky or dry habitats and is adapted to high-altitude environments.
