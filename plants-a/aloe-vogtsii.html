Aloe vogtsii

Aloe vogtsii, commonly known as the Vogts aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern, storing water to help it survive in arid environments. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow or orange flowers, which bloom in late summer. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their healing properties.

Aloe vogtsii is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
