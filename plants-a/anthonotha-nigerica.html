Anthonotha nigerica

Anthonotha nigerica, commonly known as the black nightshade, is a herbaceous plant belonging to the Solanaceae family.

It is characterized by its hairy stems and alternate leaves that are ovate to lanceolate in shape. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale purple, followed by berries that mature to a dark purple or black color. It is native to various regions in Africa and is often found in disturbed soils and open habitats.

Due to its toxic properties, it is important to handle the plant with care.
