Actinostemon polymorphus

Actinostemon polymorphus, commonly known as the variable actinostemon, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features upright, branching stems that can reach heights of up to 1.5 meters. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in dense, cylindrical clusters. Its leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and often have a hairy texture.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.
