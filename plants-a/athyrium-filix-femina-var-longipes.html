Athyrium filix-femina var. longipes

Athyrium filix-femina var. longipes, commonly known as the long-stemmed lady fern, is a variant of the lady fern characterized by its elongated fronds.

It is a deciduous fern native to eastern Asia, including regions of China, Japan, and Korea. The plant features bipinnate leaves that are often dark green and have a delicate, feathery appearance. It thrives in shady, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
