Asplenium kaulfussii

Asplenium kaulfussii, commonly known as the Kaulfuss's spleenwort, is a small, evergreen fern belonging to the Aspleniaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact, rosette-like growth habit and delicate, lance-shaped fronds. Native to Southeast Asia, this plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found growing on rocks or tree trunks. Its leaves are typically dark green and have a smooth, glossy surface.

Asplenium kaulfussii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in low-light conditions.
