Aloe granata

Aloe granata, commonly known as the granate aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color with a waxy texture. The plant produces striking red or orange flowers during the summer months, which attract pollinators like bees and birds. Its leaves contain a gel-like substance that is often used for its soothing and moisturizing properties.

Aloe granata is well-suited for arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
