Aegiphila minasensis

Aegiphila minasensis, commonly known as the Minasensis Aegiphila, is a flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its small, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of purple or pink flowers. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in moist, shaded environments.
