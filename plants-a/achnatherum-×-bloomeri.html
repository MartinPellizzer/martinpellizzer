Achnatherum × bloomeri

Achnatherum × bloomeri, commonly known as the hybrid blue grama, is a grass species native to the central United States.

It is a sterile hybrid between Achnatherum album and Achnatherum pungens, resulting in a plant with unique genetic characteristics. The grass has fine, blue-green blades and produces small, spike-like inflorescences that are often tinged with purple. It thrives in dry, open habitats such as prairies and grasslands, and is adapted to withstand drought and grazing.

Its botanical features make it an important component of native plant communities and a subject of study in plant genetics.
