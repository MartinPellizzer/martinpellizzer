Aethionema × warleyense

Aethionema × warleyense, commonly known as the Warley carnation, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and profuse display of semi-double, pink to red flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of 20–30 cm and features dark green, slightly hairy foliage. It is native to the British Isles and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.

Aethionema × warleyense prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it a hardy and low-maintenance plant for temperate climates.
