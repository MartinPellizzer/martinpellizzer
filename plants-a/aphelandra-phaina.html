Aphelandra phaina

Aphelandra phaina, commonly known as the golden flame plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features striking, pendulous inflorescences with bright yellow bracts that resemble flames, making it a visually captivating ornamental plant. The leaves are dark green, glossy, and lance-shaped, contributing to its lush appearance. It thrives in warm, humid conditions with bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil.

Aphelandra phaina is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and indoor spaces.
