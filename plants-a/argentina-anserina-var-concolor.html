Argentina anserina var. concolor

Argentina anserina var. concolor, commonly known as the smooth argentine anserina, is a variant of the argentine anserina, a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its smooth, glabrous stems and leaves, which distinguish it from other varieties. The plant typically grows in moist, temperate environments and is often found in wetlands or along riverbanks. Its flowers are small and clustered, usually displaying a range of colors from white to pale pink.

This variant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
