Adelia barbata

Adelia barbata, commonly known as the barbed adelia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features slender, upright stems covered in fine, hair-like structures that give it its common name. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale pink, typically blooming in late spring. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a slightly hairy texture.

Adelia barbata is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments.
