Ageratina stricta

Ageratina stricta, commonly known as the slender ageratum, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its small, white or pink flowers arranged in clusters. Ageratina stricta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
