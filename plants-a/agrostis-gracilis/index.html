Agrostis Gracilis

Agrostis gracilis is a delicate grass species commonly found in temperate regions. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often seen in meadows and along stream banks. This plant is known for its slender leaves and compact growth habit. Its small, inconspicuous flowers bloom in late summer, adding subtle beauty to the landscape. Despite its modest appearance, Agrostis gracilis plays an important role in supporting local ecosystems.
