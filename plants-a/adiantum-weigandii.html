Adiantum weigandii

Adiantum weigandii, commonly known as the royal fern, is a delicate and elegant fern native to South America.

It features finely divided, bi-lobed fronds that are typically bright green and often have a slightly curled appearance. The plant produces small, spore-bearing structures called sori on the underside of its leaves. It thrives in moist, shady environments and requires consistent humidity to flourish.

Adiantum weigandii is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
