Aloe melanacantha

Aloe melanacantha, commonly known as the black aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically dark green with a bluish tint. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow flowers during the summer months. Its leaves contain a gel that is used for its healing properties, though it is less common than other aloe species.

Aloe melanacantha is adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water to thrive.
