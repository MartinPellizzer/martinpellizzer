Alyssum maritimum f. densiflorum

Alyssum maritimum f. densiflorum, commonly known as the denseflower sea thrift, is a compact, mat-forming perennial plant native to coastal regions of Europe.

It features small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink, blooming in spring and early summer. The plant has silvery-gray foliage with a fuzzy texture, adapted to tolerate salty air and harsh coastal conditions. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its compact growth habit and long-lasting blooms make it an attractive and low-maintenance ornamental plant.
