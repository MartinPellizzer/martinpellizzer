Arachnorchis diversiflora

Arachnorchis diversiflora, commonly known as the variable spider orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Australia.

It typically grows in heathlands and open forests, thriving in well-drained soils. The plant features a slender, upright stem with a single or few flowers at the top. Its flowers are usually greenish-yellow with a distinctive, intricate pattern resembling a spider's web.

Arachnorchis diversiflora is known for its unique morphology and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.
