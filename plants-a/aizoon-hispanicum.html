Aizoon hispanicum

Aizoon hispanicum, commonly known as the Spanish thyme, is a small evergreen shrub native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry soils and is often found in Mediterranean climates. Its leaves are fragrant and used in culinary and medicinal applications.

Aizoon hispanicum produces small, pale purple flowers that bloom in the summer months.
