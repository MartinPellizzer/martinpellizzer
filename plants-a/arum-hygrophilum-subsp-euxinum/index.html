Arum Hygrophilum Subsp. Euxinum

Arum hygrophilum subsp. euxinum is a rare and intriguing member of the Araceae family. Native to the wetlands of southeastern Europe, it thrives in moist, shaded environments. Its striking appearance features large, heart-shaped leaves and a unique inflorescence. Despite its beauty, it is often overlooked due to its limited distribution. This plant offers valuable insights into wetland ecosystems and botanical diversity.
