Asterochlaena cuspidata

Asterochlaena cuspidata, commonly known as the spiked aster.

It is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers. Native to South America, it thrives in tropical and subtropical climates. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces yellow to orange flowers that bloom in clusters.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
