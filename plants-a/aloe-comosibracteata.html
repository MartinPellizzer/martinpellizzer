Aloe comosibracteata

Aloe comosibracteata, commonly known as the Cape aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, bluish-green leaves that are covered in a powdery white coating. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow to orange flowers during the summer months. Its leaves contain a gel-like substance that is used in traditional medicine and skincare products.

Aloe comosibracteata is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
