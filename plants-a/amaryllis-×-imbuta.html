Amaryllis × imbuta

Amaryllis × imbuta, commonly known as the hybrid amaryllis, is a striking bulbous plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its large, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, orange, and yellow. The plant produces a tall, sturdy stem that can reach up to 2 meters in height, bearing clusters of trumpet-shaped blooms. Its bulbs are fleshy and store water, allowing it to thrive in well-drained soil and full sun.

Amaryllis × imbuta is popular as a ornamental plant due to its vibrant flowers and relatively easy care requirements.
