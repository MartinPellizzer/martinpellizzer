Aconitum × paniculatum f. flexicaule

Aconitum × paniculatum f. flexicaule, commonly known as the flexicaule monkshood, is a hybrid perennial plant belonging to the Ranunculaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking, hood-shaped blue or purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant features palmately lobed leaves with a distinctive waxy coating, and it thrives in moist, shady environments. It is native to the mountainous regions of Europe and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Due to its toxic alkaloids, it should be handled with care.
