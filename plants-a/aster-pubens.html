Aster pubens

Aster pubens, commonly known as the hairy aster, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the sunflower family (Asteraceae) and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that bloom in the fall. The plant features hairy stems and leaves, which give it its common name. It thrives in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands.

Aster pubens is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting pollinators.
