Alnus × fiekii var. dressleri

Alnus × fiekii var. dressleri, commonly known as the dwarf mountain alder, is a hybrid shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Betulaceae family and is characterized by its small, oval leaves and catkins. The plant typically grows in well-drained soils and thrives in temperate to cold climates. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to stabilize soil in rocky or alpine environments.
