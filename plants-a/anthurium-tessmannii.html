Anthurium tessmannii

Anthurium tessmannii, commonly known as the velvet leaf anthurium, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a dense, velvety texture. The plant produces striking spadices, which are its inflorescences, typically in shades of red, orange, or yellow. Anthurium tessmannii thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for both ornamental gardening and botanical collections.
