Asplenium spectabile

Asplenium spectabile, commonly known as the spectable spleenwort, is a member of the fern family (Polypodiaceae).

It is a small, epiphytic fern that typically grows on rocks or trees in humid, shaded environments. The plant has delicate, lance-shaped leaves that are often clustered in a rosette formation. Its fronds are usually dark green and possess a soft, velvety texture.

Asplenium spectabile is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in shaded garden settings.
