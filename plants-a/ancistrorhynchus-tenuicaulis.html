Ancistrorhynchus tenuicaulis

Ancistrorhynchus tenuicaulis, commonly known as the slender-scaled armoured catfish, is a species of freshwater catfish native to South America.

It belongs to the Loricariidae family, characterized by its armored body and specialized mouth structure for grazing on algae. The plant-like appearance of its body is due to the presence of bony plates that cover its skin. It typically inhabits slow-moving or still waters with abundant vegetation.

This species is often found in the Amazon River basin and is valued in the aquarium trade for its unique appearance and behavior.
