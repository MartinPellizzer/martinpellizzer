Anthurium xanthum

Anthurium xanthum, commonly known as the yellow anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces vibrant orange to yellow spathes that enclose the spadix, which is a unique floral structure typical of the Araceae family. Anthurium xanthum thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and as a decorative plant in tropical landscapes.
