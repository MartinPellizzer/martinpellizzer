Aloe borziana

Aloe borziana, commonly known as the desert aloe, is a succulent plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in dry environments. The plant produces striking, tall flower stalks with yellow-orange flowers that bloom in the summer. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their soothing properties.

Aloe borziana is well adapted to withstand high temperatures and low rainfall.
