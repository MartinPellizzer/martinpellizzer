Aira spectabilis

Aira spectabilis, commonly known as the showy air plant, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of spike-like inflorescences. The plant produces small, white or pale blue flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its ornamental value comes from its elegant appearance and ability to attract pollinators.
