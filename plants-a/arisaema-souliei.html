Arisaema souliei

Arisaema souliei, commonly known as the Sichuan arrowhead, is a herbaceous plant native to China.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its distinctive arrow-shaped leaves and unique inflorescence. The plant produces a spadix surrounded by a spathe, which is typical of the arum family. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for both horticultural and ecological studies.
