Aizoon camforosma

Aizoon camforosma, commonly known as the camphor tree, is a deciduous tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its smooth, gray bark and opposite, ovate leaves with a fragrant, aromatic scent. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that develop into woody capsules containing numerous seeds. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often cultivated for its medicinal and aromatic properties.

The tree is also valued for its hard, durable wood used in construction and craftsmanship.
