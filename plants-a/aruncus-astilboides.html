Aruncus astilboides

Aruncus astilboides, commonly known as the Japanese feather reed, is a herbaceous perennial native to eastern Asia.

It features tall, slender stems that bear feathery, plume-like panicles of white to pale pink flowers. The plant thrives in moist, shady environments and has lance-shaped, dark green leaves that are slightly hairy. It is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to add texture and contrast.

The species is closely related to other members of the Smilacaceae family.
