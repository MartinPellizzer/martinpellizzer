Ayenia beyrichiana

Ayenia beyrichiana, commonly known as the Beaufort daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that bloom in shades of yellow and white. The plant typically grows in coastal and sandy soils, thriving in well-drained environments. It features a low-growing habit with narrow, linear leaves and is often used in rock gardens or as a border plant.

Ayenia beyrichiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
