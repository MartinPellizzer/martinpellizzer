Ardisia reflexiflora

Ardisia reflexiflora, commonly known as the reflexed evergreen.

It is a shrub native to Southeast Asia, characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of small, white flowers. The plant produces bright red berries that are edible for humans but toxic to some animals. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical forests.

Ardisia reflexiflora is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to tolerate partial shade.
