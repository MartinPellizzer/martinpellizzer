Astragalus baisensis

Astragalus baisensis, commonly known as the Bais milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Astragalus baisensis is known for its deep root system, which helps it survive in nutrient-poor soils.

It also plays a role in soil stabilization and is of interest for its potential medicinal properties.
