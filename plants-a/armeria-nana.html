Armeria nana

Armeria nana, commonly known as dwarf thrift, is a low-growing flowering plant native to the Arctic and alpine regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

It features dense clusters of small, bright white or pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has succulent, silver-gray leaves that help it retain moisture in harsh environments. It is often found growing in rocky soils and is adapted to cold climates.

Armeria nana is a hardy perennial that is popular in rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
