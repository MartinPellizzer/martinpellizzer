Aglaonema cochinchinense

Aglaonema cochinchinense, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated foliage with shades of white, green, and pink. Its leaves are typically broad and lance-shaped, with a glossy surface that enhances its visual appeal.

Aglaonema cochinchinense is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and ability to thrive in low-light conditions.
