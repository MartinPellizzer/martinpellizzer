Adesmia kieslingii

Adesmia kieslingii, commonly known as the Kiesling's milkweed, is a species of flowering plant in the family Fabaceae.

It is native to South America, particularly Argentina and Chile, and thrives in arid and semi-arid environments. The plant is characterized by its small, compound leaves and clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It produces pods that contain seeds with tufts of hair, aiding in wind dispersal.

Adesmia kieslingii is known for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it beneficial for soil enrichment in degraded landscapes.
