Anthurium josei

Anthurium josei, commonly known as the Joseph's anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped spathe that comes in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers inside a spadix, which is typical of the Araceae family. Its foliage is broad and glossy, adding to its ornamental appeal.

Anthurium josei thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect sunlight for optimal growth.
