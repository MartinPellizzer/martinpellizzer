Artabotrys nitidus

Artabotrys nitidus, commonly known as the white star jasmine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are glossy and dark green in color. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and pleasant aroma.
