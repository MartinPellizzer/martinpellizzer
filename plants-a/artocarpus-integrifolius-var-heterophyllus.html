Artocarpus integrifolius var. heterophyllus

Artocarpus integrifolius var. heterophyllus, commonly known as the breadfruit tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, lobed leaves and produces a fruit that is widely used for food. The plant is a member of the Moraceae family and is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in warm climates.
