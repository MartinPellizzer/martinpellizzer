Anthostyrax tonkinensis

Anthostyrax tonkinensis, commonly known as Vietnamese storax, is a small evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae and is characterized by its aromatic leaves and bark. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by dark purple berries. Its essential oils, extracted from the bark and leaves, are used in traditional medicine and perfumery.

Anthostyrax tonkinensis is valued for its unique fragrance and medicinal properties.
