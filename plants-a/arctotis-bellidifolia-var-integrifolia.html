Arctotis bellidifolia var. integrifolia

Arctotis bellidifolia var. integrifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaved arctotis, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, daisy-like flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The leaves are typically broad and entire, giving the plant its specific variety name. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

This species is also valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
