Agelanthus combreticola

Agelanthus combreticola, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters during the dry season. The plant has a woody stem and produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in savanna and woodland habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and parks in warm climates.
