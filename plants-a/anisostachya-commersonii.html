Anisostachya commersonii

Anisostachya commersonii, commonly known as Commerson's reed, is a tall, perennial grass native to South America.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences with pale green to yellowish flowers. It thrives in moist environments such as wetlands and riverbanks.

Its fibrous roots and robust growth make it an important species in ecological restoration projects.
