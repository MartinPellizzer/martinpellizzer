Azalea occidentalis

Azalea occidentalis, commonly known as the western azalea, is a deciduous shrub native to the western United States.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to deep pink, blooming in spring. The plant has elliptical leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Azalea occidentalis is known for its ornamental value and adaptability to various climates.
