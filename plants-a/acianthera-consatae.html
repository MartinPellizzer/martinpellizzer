Acianthera consatae

Acianthera consatae, commonly known as the Brazilian violet.

It is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Brazil. This terrestrial orchid typically grows in shaded, humid environments within the Atlantic Forest. It features small, violet-colored flowers with a distinctive lip that is often marked with contrasting patterns.

The plant has a fibrous root system and thrives in well-drained, acidic soils.
