Athanasia cuneiformis

Athanasia cuneiformis, commonly known as the narrow-leaf everlasting, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink to purple in color, arranged in dense clusters. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Athanasia cuneiformis is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
