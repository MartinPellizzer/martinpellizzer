Asplenium iskardense

Asplenium iskardense, commonly known as the Iskardense spleenwort, is a small, terrestrial fern belonging to the family Aspleniaceae.

It is characterized by its compact, dense growth habit and narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green in color. The plant produces sporangia in clusters on the underside of its fronds, a feature typical of many ferns. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found on rocks or soil in temperate regions.

Asplenium iskardense is relatively uncommon and is often associated with specific microhabitats, making it a subject of interest in botanical studies.
