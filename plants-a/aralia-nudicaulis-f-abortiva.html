Aralia nudicaulis f. abortiva

Aralia nudicaulis f. abortiva, commonly known as the abortive leaf aralia, is a variant of the American aralia characterized by its distinctive foliage.

This plant typically features reduced or absent leaflets, giving it a unique appearance compared to the typical form. It is native to the eastern United States and thrives in moist, shaded environments. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in umbels.

Its botanical interest lies in its morphological variation and ecological adaptability.
