Alchemilla pastoralis f. breviloba-glabriflora

Alchemilla pastoralis f. breviloba-glabriflora, commonly known as the short-stalked lady's mantle, is a low-growing perennial plant native to Europe.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its dense, silver-green foliage that forms a compact mat. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers clustered in rounded inflorescences, which bloom in late spring to early summer.
