Anaphalis sericeoalbida

Anaphalis sericeoalbida, commonly known as the silver hair grass, is a flowering plant native to the mountains of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, white, woolly inflorescences that resemble clouds, giving it a unique and ethereal appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are often hidden within the fluffy bracts. It thrives in alpine and subalpine environments, preferring well-drained soils and cool climates.

Anaphalis sericeoalbida is often used in ornamental horticulture for its striking visual appeal.
