Anisifolium spectabile

Anisifolium spectabile, commonly known as the showy anisifolium, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant produces vibrant, showy blooms that range from yellow to red, depending on the species. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and have a rough texture, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Anisifolium spectabile is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and natural landscapes.
