Aa aurantiaca

Aa aurantiaca, commonly known as the orange tree, is a species of citrus plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its evergreen leaves, which are ovate and dark green with a leathery texture. The plant produces fragrant, yellow-orange flowers that give way to edible fruits containing segmented, juicy pulp. Aa aurantiica thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Its botanical features make it a popular ornamental and agricultural plant worldwide.
