Achyranthes urbani

Achyranthes urbani, commonly known as the urban smartweed, is a herbaceous annual plant native to parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its erect, branched stems and lanceolate leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in urban environments such as roadsides and vacant lots.

Due to its adaptability, it is considered a common weed in many regions.
