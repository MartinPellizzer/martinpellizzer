Aregelia concentrica

Aregelia concentrica, commonly known as the concentric begonia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, concentrically patterned leaves that resemble target or wheel shapes. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, often arranged in clusters. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

Aregelia concentrica is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to add vibrant color to shaded garden spaces.
