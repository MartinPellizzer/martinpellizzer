Aidia pallens

Aidia pallens, commonly known as the silverleaf daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-white leaves and daisy-like yellow flowers with a central disc. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family and is known for its compact growth habit. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
