Atriplex tauschii

Atriplex tauschii, commonly known as saltbush, is a shrub native to arid and semi-arid regions of Eurasia.

It is characterized by its succulent, often hairy leaves that help reduce water loss in dry environments. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It is adapted to saline soils and can thrive in areas with high salt content.

Atriplex tauschii is also valued for its ability to stabilize soil and prevent erosion in degraded landscapes.
