Acianthera verecunda

Acianthera verecunda, commonly known as the purple-stemmed orchid, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking purple stems and clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments such as rainforests. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is known for its delicate, ephemeral blooms.

Botanically, it has a sympodial growth habit and produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients.
