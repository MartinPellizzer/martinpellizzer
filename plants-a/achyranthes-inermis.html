Achyranthes inermis

Achyranthes inermis, commonly known as the slender cowplant, is a herbaceous annual plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its erect, branched stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and fields.

Its rapid growth and adaptability make it a common weed in agricultural areas.
