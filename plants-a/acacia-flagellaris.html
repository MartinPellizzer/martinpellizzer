Acacia flagellaris

Acacia flagellaris, commonly known as the slender acacia, is a shrub or small tree native to the arid regions of Australia.

The plant has phyllodes rather than true leaves, which are narrow and strap-like, adapted to reduce water loss in its harsh environment.
