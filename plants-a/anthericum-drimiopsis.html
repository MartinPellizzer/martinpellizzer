Anthericum drimiopsis

Anthericum drimiopsis, commonly known as the drimiopsis lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves that form a dense rosette near the ground. The plant produces striking, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of yellow to orange, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Due to its ornamental value and ease of care, it is a popular choice among gardeners.
