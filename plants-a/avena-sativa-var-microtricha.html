Avena sativa var. microtricha

Avena sativa var. microtricha, commonly known as the slender oat, is a variety of the oat plant characterized by its shorter stature and fine, delicate leaves.

It is native to the temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere and thrives in well-drained soils. The plant produces compact spikes of small, white to pale blue flowers, which are followed by awned grains. It is often cultivated for forage and has potential uses in herbal medicine due to its nutritional profile.

Its unique morphology makes it distinct from other oat varieties and suitable for specific agricultural applications.
