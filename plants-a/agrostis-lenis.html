Agrostis lenis

Agrostis lenis, commonly known as slender bentgrass, is a perennial grass species native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows, pastures, and along stream banks. The plant has slender, creeping stems that form dense mats, and its narrow, grass-like leaves are typically dark green in color. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers.

Agrostis lenis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping or as a component in native plant gardens.
