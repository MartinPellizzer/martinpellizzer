Aster tenuiculus

Aster tenuiculus, commonly known as the narrowleaf aster, is a species of flowering plant in the sunflower family.

It is native to North America and thrives in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands. The plant features slender, linear leaves and produces small, clustered blue or purple flowers. It is often found in the eastern United States and parts of Canada.

Aster tenuiculus plays a role in supporting pollinators and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
