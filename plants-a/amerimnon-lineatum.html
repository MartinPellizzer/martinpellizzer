Amerimnon lineatum

Amerimnon lineatum, commonly known as the zigzag fern, is a member of the Polypodiaceae family.

It is characterized by its distinctive, undulating fronds that give it its common name. The plant has a creeping rhizome with dark brown, scaly roots. Its leaves are pinnately divided and have a leathery texture.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.
