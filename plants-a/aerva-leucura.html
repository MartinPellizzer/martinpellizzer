Aerva leucura

Aerva leucura, commonly known as white aerva, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, sandy soils and thrives in warm climates. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs.

Aerva leucura is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its delicate appearance.
