Asarca tenuiflora

Asarca tenuiflora, commonly known as slender-flowered asarum, is a perennial herb native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and small, hooded flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its rhizomatous root system allows it to spread and form dense colonies.

Asarca tenuiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
