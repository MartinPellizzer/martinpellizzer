Argyrolobium muddii

Argyrolobium muddii, commonly known as Mudd's silver globular pea, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit and produces clusters of small, spherical pods. The plant features hairy stems and leaves with a distinctive trifoliate structure. Its flowers are typically pale yellow to cream in color, and it blooms during the cooler months.

Argyrolobium muddii is adapted to arid environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
