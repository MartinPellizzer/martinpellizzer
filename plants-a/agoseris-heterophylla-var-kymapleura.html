Agoseris heterophylla var. kymapleura

Agoseris heterophylla var. kymapleura, commonly known as the Kymapleura lomatoid, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features basal leaves that are lanceolate to oblanceolate, with serrated margins. Its flowering stems are often branched and bear yellow to orange composite flowers.

It thrives in dry, open habitats such as meadows and rocky slopes.
