Artabotrys costatus

Artabotrys costatus, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its pendulous clusters of yellow, fragrant flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has simple, opposite, and ovate leaves that are glossy and dark green. Its fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release seeds.

Artabotrys costatus is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
