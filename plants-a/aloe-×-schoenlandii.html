Aloe × schoenlandii

Aloe × schoenlandii, commonly known as the hybrid aloe or the hybrid aloe plant, is a succulent species native to South Africa.

It is a hybrid between Aloe barberae and Aloe ferox, resulting in a plant with unique characteristics. The plant features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with red or purple markings. It produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow flowers, which bloom in summer.

Aloe × schoenlandii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable growing conditions.
