Aeonium spathulatum var. cruentum

Aeonium spathulatum var. cruentum, commonly known as the Crimson Crust or Crimson Aeonium, is a succulent plant native to the Canary Islands.

It features rosettes of fleshy, spoon-shaped leaves that are typically green with red or pink hues along the edges. The plant produces striking red or orange flowers on tall, branching stems during the spring or summer. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
