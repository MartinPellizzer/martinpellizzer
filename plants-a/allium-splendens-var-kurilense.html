Allium splendens var. kurilense

Allium splendens var. kurilense, commonly known as the Kuril garlic chive, is a perennial herb native to the Kuril Islands.

It features slender, grass-like leaves that are typically bluish-green in color. The plant produces small, white to pale pink flowers arranged in dense umbels, which bloom in early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and mild garlic-like fragrance.
