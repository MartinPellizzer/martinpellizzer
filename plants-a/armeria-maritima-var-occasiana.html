Armeria maritima var. occasiana

Armeria maritima var. occasiana, commonly known as the coastal thrift, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of the United States and Canada.

It is a low-growing, mat-forming plant that thrives in sandy, well-drained soils. The plant produces small, pink to white flowers in dense clusters during the spring and early summer. It is well adapted to salt spray and harsh coastal conditions, making it a hardy ornamental plant.

Its foliage is evergreen, with narrow, grass-like leaves that help reduce water loss in arid environments.
