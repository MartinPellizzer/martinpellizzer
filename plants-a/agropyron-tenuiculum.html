Agropyron tenuiculum

Agropyron tenuiculum, commonly known as slender wheatgrass, is a perennial grass native to Eurasia.

It is characterized by its fine, tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny florets. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid environments.

Agropyron tenuiculum is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to stabilize soil in erosion-prone areas.
