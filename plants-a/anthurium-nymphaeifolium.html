Anthurium nymphaeifolium

Anthurium nymphaeifolium, commonly known as the water lily anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the humid lowland forests of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are often a deep green with a waxy texture. The plant produces vibrant, spathaceous flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, which are borne on a showy, leaf-like bract. Its botanical features make it a popular choice for indoor cultivation due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.

It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and humidity to flourish.
