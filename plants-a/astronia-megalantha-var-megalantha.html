Astronia megalantha var. megalantha

Astronia megalantha var. megalantha, commonly known as the large-leaved astronia, is a species of flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its large, broad leaves that can reach up to 30 cm in length, often with a glossy surface. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom during the rainy season. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
