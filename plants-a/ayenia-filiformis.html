Ayenia filiformis

Ayenia filiformis, commonly known as the slender ayenia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant produces small, yellow to orange flowers that are arranged in a dense, compact cluster. Ayenia filiformis typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices.

Its slender, creeping stems and delicate foliage make it a unique addition to botanical gardens and conservation efforts.
