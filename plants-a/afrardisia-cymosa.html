Afrardisia cymosa

Afrardisia cymosa, commonly known as the African flame flower, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking red-orange flowers that bloom in cymes, giving it its common name. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
