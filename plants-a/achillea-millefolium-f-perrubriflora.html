Achillea millefolium f. perrubriflora

Achillea millefolium f. perrubriflora, commonly known as the red-flowered yarrow, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is a horticultural form of the common yarrow, characterized by its striking red-orange flowers that bloom in flat-topped clusters. The plant features finely divided, feather-like leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental qualities and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
