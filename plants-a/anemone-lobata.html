Anemone lobata

Anemone lobata, commonly known as the Pacific anemone or the sea anemone, is a marine plant found in coastal areas of the Pacific Northwest.

It has a distinctive, flower-like appearance with fleshy, petal-like structures that are actually modified leaves. The plant thrives in shallow, rocky intertidal zones and is adapted to withstand strong wave action. Its reproductive system involves both sexual and asexual methods, with some individuals producing offspring through vegetative propagation.

Anemone lobata plays an important ecological role by providing habitat and food for various marine organisms.
