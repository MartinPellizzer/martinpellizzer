Asparagus conglomeratus

Asparagus conglomeratus, commonly known as the clustered asparagus, is a perennial shrub native to southern Africa.

It features dense, spiny foliage and produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in spring. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and ability to thrive in arid conditions. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to retain moisture in dry environments.

Asparagus conglomeratus is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
