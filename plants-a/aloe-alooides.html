Aloe alooides

Aloe alooides, commonly known as the narrow-leaved aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features tall, sparsely branched stems that bear narrow, fleshy, gray-green leaves with white to yellowish margins. The plant produces yellow, tubular flowers that are clustered in racemes, typically blooming in spring. Aloe alooides is adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves and stems.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional medicinal uses.
