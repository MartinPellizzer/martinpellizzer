Alsophila lasiosora

Alsophila lasiosora, commonly known as the slender tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Davalliaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feathery fronds. The plant has a distinctive root system that helps anchor it in moist, shaded environments. Its fronds are typically bright green and can grow quite long, contributing to its ornamental value.
