Arctotis ramosissima

Arctotis ramosissima, commonly known as the silverleaf arctotis, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage, which helps reduce water loss in arid environments. The plant produces showy, daisy-like flowers in shades of yellow and orange, typically blooming in spring and summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Arctotis ramosissima is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
