Alchemilla nebulosa f. autumnalis

Alchemilla nebulosa f. autumnalis, commonly known as autumn alchemilla, is a flowering plant belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit and produces clusters of small, yellowish-white flowers. The plant features broad, lobed leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of silky hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is typically found in woodland or meadow habitats.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
