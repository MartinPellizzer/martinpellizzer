Agave kellermaniana

Agave kellermaniana, commonly known as the Mexican hat agave, is a succulent plant native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped growth pattern and thick, fleshy leaves that are often blue-green in color. The plant produces an impressive inflorescence that resembles a Mexican hat, which can reach several meters in height. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, requiring well-drained soil and full sun exposure.

Agave kellermaniana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
