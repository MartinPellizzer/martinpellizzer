Andryala gracilis

Andryala gracilis, commonly known as the slender sandwort, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Montiaceae.

It is a low-growing perennial herb characterized by its thin, succulent stems and small, clustered leaves. Due to its drought tolerance and ornamental appeal, it is sometimes cultivated in rock gardens and xeriscapes.
