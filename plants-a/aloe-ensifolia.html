Aloe ensifolia

Aloe ensifolia, commonly known as the sword aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features tall, narrow, sword-shaped leaves that are covered in a thick layer of white, powdery wax. The plant produces vibrant orange or yellow flowers on tall, branching stalks during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Aloe ensifolia is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional medicinal uses.
