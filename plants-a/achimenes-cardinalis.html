Achimenes cardinalis

Achimenes cardinalis, commonly known as the cardinal balsam, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy texture. Its flowers are borne on long, slender stems and are often used in ornamental gardening.

Achimenes cardinalis thrives in warm, humid conditions and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
