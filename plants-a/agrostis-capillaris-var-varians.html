Agrostis capillaris var. varians

Agrostis capillaris var. varians, commonly known as the variable bentgrass, is a low-growing grass species native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically forms dense, mat-like growth and is often found in moist, well-drained soils. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spikelet-based flowers. It is known for its adaptability to various environmental conditions.

This variety is often used in turfgrass applications due to its fine texture and durability.
