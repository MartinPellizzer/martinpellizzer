Acer Pictum Subsp. Pubigerum

Acer pictum subsp. pubigerum is a rare and beautiful species of maple native to the mountainous regions of eastern Asia. It is known for its striking red and yellow foliage that transforms the landscape during autumn. This subspecies thrives in cool, humid climates and is often found in shaded forest environments. Its unique leaf patterns and ornamental value make it a sought-after plant among horticulturists. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively obscure in the broader horticultural community.
