Anisopogon avenaceus

Anisopogon avenaceus, commonly known as the slender grassvetch, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as roadsides and fields. The plant has slender, erect stems and small, pea-like flowers that are usually pale green to yellow in color. Its leaves are alternate and pinnately compound, with small, oval-shaped leaflets.

Anisopogon avenaceus is characterized by its ability to fix nitrogen through symbiotic relationships with rhizobia bacteria in its root nodules.
