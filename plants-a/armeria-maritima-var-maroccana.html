Armeria maritima var. maroccana

Armeria maritima var. maroccana, commonly known as the Moroccan thrift, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of North Africa.

It is a low-growing, mat-forming perennial that thrives in sandy or rocky soils. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pinkish flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to harsh coastal environments, tolerating salt spray and strong winds.

Its compact growth habit and attractive flowers make it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
