Anisophyllea boehmii

Anisophyllea boehmii, commonly known as the Bohm's anisophyllea, is a tropical shrub native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves that are usually glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release tiny seeds.

Anisophyllea boehmii is often found in lowland rainforests and is valued for its ornamental qualities.
