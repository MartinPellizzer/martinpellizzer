Aechmea virens

Aechmea virens, commonly known as the green pineapple plant, is a bromeliad native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that form a central cup, which collects water and nutrients. The plant produces a striking inflorescence with colorful bracts that range from red to orange. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often used in ornamental horticulture.

Aechmea virens is known for its ability to adapt to various growing conditions, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
