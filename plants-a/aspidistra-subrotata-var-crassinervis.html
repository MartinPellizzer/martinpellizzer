Aspidistra subrotata var. crassinervis

Aspidistra subrotata var. crassinervis, commonly known as the broad-veined aspidistra, is a striking evergreen plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It features dark, glossy, strap-like leaves that are often marked with prominent, parallel veins, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant is valued for its ornamental foliage and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings. It prefers cool, humid conditions and well-draining soil, making it relatively easy to care for.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for those seeking a lush, low-maintenance indoor plant.
