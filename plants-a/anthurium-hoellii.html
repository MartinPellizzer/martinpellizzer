Anthurium hoellii

Anthurium hoellii, commonly known as the Crimson Velvet Anthurium, is a striking tropical plant native to Colombia.

It is characterized by its large, velvety, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep red or maroon color. The plant produces spadices, which are spathes that enclose the true flowers, often in a contrasting color. It thrives in humid, warm environments with bright, indirect light.

Anthurium hoellii is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
