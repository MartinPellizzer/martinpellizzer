Alternanthera pubiflora f. purpurea

Alternanthera pubiflora f. purpurea, commonly known as the purple foliage alternanthera, is a striking ornamental plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant purple to reddish leaves, which contrast beautifully with its small, inconspicuous white flowers. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full sun exposure. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in water gardens and as a bog plant.

Its foliage changes color with seasonal variations, adding dynamic visual interest to aquatic or humid environments.
