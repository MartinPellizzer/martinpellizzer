Abronia maritima subsp. maritima

Abronia maritima subsp.

maritima, commonly known as the sea blite, is a halophytic plant adapted to coastal environments. It thrives in saline soils and is often found in dune ecosystems along the Atlantic coast.

The plant has succulent, fleshy leaves that store water, aiding in its survival in arid conditions. Its small, white flowers are typically clustered in dense inflorescences.

It plays an important ecological role in stabilizing dunes and providing habitat for coastal wildlife.
