Armeria alpina var. canescens

Armeria alpina var. canescens, commonly known as the Alpine thrift, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It features dense clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has linear, silver-gray leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive, frost-resistant appearance. It thrives in well-drained, sandy or rocky soils and is often found in alpine and subalpine environments.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in harsh climates.
