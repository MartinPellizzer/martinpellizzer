Achimenes longiflora var. warszewiczii

Achimenes longiflora var. warszewiczii, commonly known as the Warszewicz's achimenes, is a tropical flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to red, often with contrasting yellow or white throats. The plant produces showy bracts that enhance its ornamental appeal, and it thrives in warm, humid environments. It is typically grown as an annual in temperate regions, requiring well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Its foliage is heart-shaped and glossy, adding to its aesthetic value in garden settings.
