Aerides leeana

Aerides leeana, commonly known as the Leeana orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale pink, typically blooming in the spring. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil.

Aerides leeana is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ease of care in a tropical garden setting.
