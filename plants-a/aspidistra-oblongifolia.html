Aspidistra oblongifolia

Aspidistra oblongifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaf aspidistra, is a perennial evergreen plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It features dark green, glossy, and elongated leaves that are typically broad and lance-shaped. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are followed by dark blue berries. It is well-suited for shady environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Its foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and landscape design.
