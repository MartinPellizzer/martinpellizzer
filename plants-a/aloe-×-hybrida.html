Aloe × hybrida

Aloe × hybrida, commonly known as the hybrid aloe, is a succulent plant native to Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that are often arranged in a rosette pattern. The leaves are typically green with a waxy coating, which helps the plant retain moisture in arid environments. It produces yellow or orange flowers during the growing season, usually in summer.

This plant is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and ability to thrive in a variety of conditions.
