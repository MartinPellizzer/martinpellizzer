Aster zuluensis

Aster zuluensis, commonly known as the Zulu aster, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It produces small, blue or violet flowers that bloom in the spring and summer months.

Aster zuluensis is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and drought-resistant qualities.
