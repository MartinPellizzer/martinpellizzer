Allium cyaneum var. stenodon

Allium cyaneum var. stenodon, commonly known as the narrow-leaved blue onion, is a perennial bulbous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of small, blue to purple flowers. The plant is characterized by its distinctive blue hue, which is due to the presence of anthocyanin pigments. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its unique color and hardy nature.
