Aster siemssenii

Aster siemssenii, commonly known as the Sierra aster, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of ray and disk florets. The plant typically grows in rocky or open habitats, thriving in full sun and well-drained soils. Its showy purple to blue flowers bloom in the summer months, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Aster siemssenii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in native plant gardens.
