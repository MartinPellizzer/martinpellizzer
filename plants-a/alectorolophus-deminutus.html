Alectorolophus deminutus

It is characterized by its delicate, blue to purple flowers and hairy stems. Native to alpine regions of Europe and Asia, it thrives in rocky, well-drained soils. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, showy blooms in early summer.

Due to its habitat preferences, it is often found in mountainous environments where it plays a role in local ecosystems.
