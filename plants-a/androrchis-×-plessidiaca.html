Androrchis × plessidiaca

Androrchis × plessidiaca, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a cross between Androrchis lutescens and Plessisidia orchidoides.

It is a terrestrial orchid native to the eastern United States, particularly in the Appalachian region. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded woodlands and has a fibrous root system. Its flowers are usually greenish-yellow with a pale lip, and it blooms in late spring to early summer.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
