Achyronia sarcantha

Achyronia sarcantha, commonly known as the pink sand verbena, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its delicate, pink to purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has narrow, gray-green leaves and thrives in sandy, well-drained soils. It is often found in coastal dunes and other arid environments.
