Acalypha ovata

Acalypha ovata, commonly known as the velvetleaf or redleaf, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Pacific.

The plant has palmate leaves with lobed edges, and its small, inconspicuous flowers are borne in clusters.
