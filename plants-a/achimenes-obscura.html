Achimenes obscura

Achimenes obscura, commonly known as the dark achimenes, is a tropical perennial herb native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its erect, hairy stems and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant produces fleshy, tuberous roots that help it survive periods of drought. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often covered with fine hairs.

Achimenes obscura is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
