Abies chinensis

Abies chinensis, commonly known as the Chinese fir, is a coniferous evergreen tree native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and dense, pyramidal crown. The tree has needle-like leaves that are dark green and arranged in spirals along the branches. Its cones are typically reddish-brown and contain winged seeds.

This species is valued for its timber and is often planted in ornamental landscapes due to its aesthetic appeal.
