Artabotrys aurantiacus var. multiflorus

Artabotrys aurantiacus var. multiflorus, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its pendulous clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. The plant has simple, ovate leaves with a glossy surface and emits a fragrant scent. Its fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release smooth, black seeds.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
