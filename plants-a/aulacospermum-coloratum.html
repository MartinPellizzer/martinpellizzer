Aulacospermum coloratum

Aulacospermum coloratum, commonly known as the rainbow spurge, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features striking, colorful bracts that range from yellow to red, giving it its common name. The plant has a compact, bushy growth habit with fleshy, dark green leaves. It produces small, inconspicuous yellow flowers that bloom in spring.

Aulacospermum coloratum is well-suited for rock gardens and requires well-draining soil and full sun.
