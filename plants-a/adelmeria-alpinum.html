Adelmeria alpinum

Adelmeria alpinum, commonly known as the alpine adelmeria, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of California.

Adelmeria alpinum thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and is often found in moist, shaded habitats along the coast.
