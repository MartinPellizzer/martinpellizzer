Alternanthera denticulata var. bifaria

Alternanthera denticulata var. bifaria, commonly known as the red-leaf alternanthera, is a striking aquatic plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its bright red to pink foliage, which becomes more vivid under strong light conditions. The plant has elongated, narrow leaves that are often tinged with red or purple hues, making it a popular choice for aquariums. It prefers moderate to high lighting and can thrive in a variety of water conditions.

This variety is known for its ease of care and ability to add vibrant color to planted tanks.
