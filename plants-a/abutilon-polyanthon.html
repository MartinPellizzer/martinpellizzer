Abutilon polyanthon

Abutilon polyanthon, commonly known as the velvetleaf abutilon, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 3 meters in height. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and produces clusters of yellow flowers. Its flowers are typically borne on peduncles and bloom in the summer months.

The plant is often found in moist, well-drained soils in woodland or disturbed areas.
