Anoectochilus bullenii

Anoectochilus bullenii, commonly known as the Bulle's slipper orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, dark green leaves and a unique, pouch-like flower that resembles a slipper. The plant produces flowers in shades of white and pale yellow, often with intricate patterns on the lip. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil.

Anoectochilus bullenii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
