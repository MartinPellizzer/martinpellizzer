Aizoon karooicum

Aizoon karooicum, commonly known as the Karoo aizoon, is a succulent plant native to the arid regions of South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family, also known as the ice plant family, and is characterized by its fleshy, water-storing leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to survive in dry, hot climates. It produces small, star-shaped flowers that are often pink or purple, blooming during the cooler months.

Aizoon karooicum is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to thrive in low-water environments.
