Arenaria megalantha var. ensifolia

Arenaria megalantha var. ensifolia, commonly known as the swordleaf sandwort, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features basal rosettes of narrow, sword-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, white or pinkish flowers in clusters during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained, sandy or rocky soils and is adapted to full sun.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance in garden settings.
