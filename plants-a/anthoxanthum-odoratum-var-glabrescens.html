Anthoxanthum odoratum var. glabrescens

Anthoxanthum odoratum var. glabrescens, commonly known as sweet vernal grass, is a flowering plant in the grass family Poaceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow to orange inflorescence, which gives it its common name. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and pastures. It has slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves.

This variety is distinguished by its smooth, hairless appearance compared to other forms of sweet vernal grass.
