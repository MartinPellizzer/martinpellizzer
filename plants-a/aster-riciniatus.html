Aster riciniatus

Aster riciniatus, commonly known as the velvety aster, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its showy composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a soft, velvety texture. Its flowers are typically blue to purple in color, with ray florets surrounding a central disk of tubular flowers.

Aster riciniatus is often grown as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and blooms.
