Anemone filisecta

Anemone filisecta, commonly known as the Chinese anemone, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to East Asia.

It features showy, white to pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has finely divided, feather-like leaves that are typically arranged in a basal rosette. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Anemone filisecta is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and easy care requirements.
