Abryanthemum acinaciforme

Abryanthemum acinaciforme, commonly known as the swordleaf ice plant, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, sword-shaped leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red during the summer months. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and low maintenance requirements.

Abryanthemum acinaciforme is well adapted to full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
