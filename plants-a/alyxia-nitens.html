Alyxia nitens

Alyxia nitens, commonly known as the silverleaf alyxia, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a woody vine characterized by its slender stems and opposite, elliptic leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery pubescence. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that are typically clustered in terminal panicles. Alyxia nitens thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or swampy habitats.

Its ornamental value comes from its attractive foliage and delicate floral display.
