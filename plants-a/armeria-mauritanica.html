Armeria mauritanica

Armeria mauritanica, commonly known as the upright sandverbena, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region and parts of North Africa.

It is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit and features small, white to pinkish flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and is often found in sandy or rocky soils. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Armeria mauritanica is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
