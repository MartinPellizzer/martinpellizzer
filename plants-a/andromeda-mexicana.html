Andromeda mexicana

Andromeda mexicana, commonly known as the Mexican wintergreen, is a species of flowering plant in the Ericaceae family.

It is native to Mexico and is characterized by its evergreen foliage and clusters of small, bell-shaped white or pink flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, acidic soils and is often found in mountainous regions. It has a compact growth habit and is valued for its ornamental appeal.

The leaves are leathery and dark green, providing year-round visual interest.
