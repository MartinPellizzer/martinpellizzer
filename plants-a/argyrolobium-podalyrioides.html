Argyrolobium podalyrioides

Argyrolobium podalyrioides, commonly known as the silver globemallow, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and showy, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is drought-tolerant, making it well adapted to arid environments. Its unique appearance and ability to thrive in harsh conditions make it a popular choice for xeriscaping.

The species is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits to local wildlife.
