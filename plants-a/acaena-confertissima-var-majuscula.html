Acaena confertissima var. majuscula

Acaena confertissima var. majuscula, commonly known as the large burr plant, is a perennial shrub native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its dense, spiny seed heads that form large, spherical clusters, which are a key feature of its botanical appearance. The plant has compound leaves with serrated margins and produces small, yellowish-green flowers during the spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and subalpine regions.

The species is notable for its ability to form extensive stands and its role in stabilizing soils in its native habitat.
