Astragalus Sphaerophysa Var. Lehmannianus

Astragalus sphaerophysa var. lehmannianus is a rare and unique plant species found in specific regions of western North America. It belongs to the legume family and is known for its distinctive spherical seed pods. This variant is closely related to other Astragalus species but has distinct morphological features. Due to its limited distribution and habitat requirements, it is considered endangered. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this botanical gem.
