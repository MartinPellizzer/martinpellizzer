Armeria maritima var. neglecta

Armeria maritima var. neglecta, commonly known as sea thrift, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of Europe and North America.

It is a low-growing perennial that thrives in sandy, well-drained soils and full sun. The plant produces dense clusters of white, star-shaped flowers in early spring. It is characterized by its rosette-forming foliage and salt-tolerant nature, making it well-suited for coastal environments.

Its hardy and ornamental qualities make it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscaping.
