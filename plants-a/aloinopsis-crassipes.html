Aloinopsis crassipes

Aloinopsis crassipes, commonly known as the broad-leaved aloinopsis, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces yellow flowers during the summer months, adding ornamental value. It belongs to the Asphodelaceae family, which includes other succulent species.

Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for rock gardens and container planting.
