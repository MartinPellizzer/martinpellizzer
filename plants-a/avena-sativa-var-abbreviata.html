Avena sativa var. abbreviata

Avena sativa var. abbreviata, commonly known as the dwarf oat, is a variety of the common oat plant.

It is characterized by its shorter stature and compact growth habit compared to typical oat varieties. This plant produces small, oval-shaped grains that are often used in specialty food products. Botanically, it belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

Its reduced height makes it suitable for ornamental gardening and specialized agricultural applications.
