Aquilegia vulgaris var. grandiflora

Aquilegia vulgaris var. grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered columbine, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features showy, spurred flowers that typically bloom in spring, with vivid colors ranging from blue to purple and red. The plant has compound, pinnately arranged leaves with finely toothed margins. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, often found in woodland or meadow environments.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and natural landscapes.
