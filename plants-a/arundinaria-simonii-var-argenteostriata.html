Arundinaria simonii var. argenteostriata

Arundinaria simonii var. argenteostriata, commonly known as the silver-striped bamboo, is a variety of the Chinese bamboo species.

It is characterized by its striking silver-white stripes on the culms, which contrast beautifully with its dark green foliage. This plant is native to China and thrives in moist, well-drained soils. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

The plant exhibits a clumping growth habit and is relatively hardy, making it suitable for a variety of climates.
