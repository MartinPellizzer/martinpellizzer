Avena alba f. triflora

Avena alba f. triflora, commonly known as white oat grass, is a flowering plant belonging to the grass family Poaceae.

It is a variant of the common oat, characterized by its pale blue or white inflorescence. This plant typically grows in temperate regions and is often found in disturbed soils or along roadsides. The inflorescence of Avena alba f. triflora consists of three to six spikelets, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in landscaping or as a decorative plant.
