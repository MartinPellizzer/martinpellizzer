Aspidostemon litoralis

Aspidostemon litoralis, commonly known as the coastal aspidostemon, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils along the shoreline. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, pale blue or white flowers in clusters. It is adapted to salt spray and sandy environments, making it a characteristic component of coastal vegetation.

Its ecological importance includes providing habitat and nectar for coastal pollinators.
