Amerimnon purpureum

Amerimnon purpureum, commonly known as purple amaryllis, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant purple and red flowers that bloom in late summer or early autumn. The plant features broad, strap-like leaves that are typically green with a reddish tinge. It thrives in warm, humid conditions and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This ornamental plant is often cultivated for its showy blossoms and ease of care in suitable environments.
