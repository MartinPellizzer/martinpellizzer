Arthropteris heteromorpha

Arthropteris heteromorpha, commonly known as the variable sword fern, is a species of fern native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped fronds that are often divided into multiple segments. The plant produces spores on the underside of its leaves, which are typically clustered in sori. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest undergrowth.

Its unique morphology and adaptability make it a notable species in botanical studies.
