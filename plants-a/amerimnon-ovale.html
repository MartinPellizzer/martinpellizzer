Amerimnon ovale

Amerimnon ovale, commonly known as the oval amaryllis, is a striking bulbous plant native to South America.

It features large, showy flowers that are typically pink or white, with six petals arranged in a star-like pattern. The plant produces a tall, straight stem that can reach up to 3 feet in height. Its foliage consists of strap-like leaves that are broad and dark green.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
