Alatiliparis

Alatiliparis, commonly known as the silverleaf daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and bright yellow flower heads. The plant typically grows in well-drained soils and thrives in full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping. Its compact growth habit and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for low-maintenance landscaping.

The flowers bloom in spring and early summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
