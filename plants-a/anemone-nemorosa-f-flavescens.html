Anemone nemorosa f. flavescens

Anemone nemorosa f. flavescens, commonly known as the yellow wood anemone, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant that typically grows in woodland areas and shaded gardens. The plant features delicate, white or pale yellow flowers with five petals, often surrounded by a cluster of yellow stamens. Its leaves are palmately divided, with a lobed appearance that adds to its ornamental value.

The species is known for its early spring bloom, often appearing before the canopy of trees has fully developed.
