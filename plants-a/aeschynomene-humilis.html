Aeschynomene humilis

Aeschynomene humilis, commonly known as smallleaf aeschynomene, is a low-growing annual herb native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant has a fibrous root system and is often found in disturbed soils, making it a common weed in agricultural fields.

It is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, contributing to soil fertility.
