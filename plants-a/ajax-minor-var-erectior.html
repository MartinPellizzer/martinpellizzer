Ajax minor var. erectior

Ajax minor var. erectior, commonly known as the upright dwarf dandelion, is a variant of the common dandelion native to Europe.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant characterized by its basal rosette of narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellow composite flowers that develop into fluffy white seed heads. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in meadows, roadsides, and gardens.

Its botanical features make it easily identifiable and ecologically significant in various habitats.
