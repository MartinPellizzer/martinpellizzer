Asyneuma limonifolium subsp. kellererianum

Asyneuma limonifolium subsp.

kellererianum, commonly known as the Kellerer's lemonleaf, is a perennial shrub native to South America. It features aromatic, lanceolate leaves with a distinct yellowish-green coloration.

The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in dense inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Its botanical characteristics make it a unique subject for horticultural and ecological studies.
