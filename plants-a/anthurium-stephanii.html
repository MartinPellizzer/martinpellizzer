Anthurium stephanii

Anthurium stephanii, commonly known as the red-veined anthurium, is a striking tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped leaves with deep red veins. The plant produces spadices, which are modified leaves that enclose the actual flowers, typically in a bright red or orange color. Anthurium stephanii thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical landscapes.
