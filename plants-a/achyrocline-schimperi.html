Achyrocline schimperi

Achyrocline schimperi, commonly known as the sacred lotus of the Caribbean, is a flowering plant native to the island of Saint Lucia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and is adapted to saline and sandy soils. Its foliage consists of narrow, lance-shaped leaves, and it is often found in maritime habitats.
