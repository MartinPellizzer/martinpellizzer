Amanoa robusta

Amanoa robusta, commonly known as the robust amanoa, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of the Pacific Islands.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow. Amanoa robusta is valued not only for its ornamental beauty but also for its ecological role in supporting local biodiversity.
