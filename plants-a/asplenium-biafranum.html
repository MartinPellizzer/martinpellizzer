Asplenium biafranum

Asplenium biafranum, commonly known as the African shield fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Aspleniaceae and is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and dark green, leathery fronds. The plant typically forms a rosette of shield-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white spore-producing structures. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.

Asplenium biafranum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is occasionally cultivated as a houseplant.
