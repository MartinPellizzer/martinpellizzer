Abelia spathulata var. elliptica

Abelia spathulata var. elliptica, commonly known as the elliptic abelia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features narrow, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white to pink flowers in late spring to early summer. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained soil.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various garden conditions.
