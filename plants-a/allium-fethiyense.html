Allium fethiyense

Allium fethiyense, commonly known as the Fethiyen garlic, is a species of flowering plant in the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is native to the island of Socotra, which is located in the Arabian Sea. The plant is characterized by its striking, umbrella-shaped inflorescence composed of white to pale blue flowers. Its leaves are long and narrow, often with a bluish-green hue.

Allium fethiyense is adapted to the arid and rocky environment of Socotra, showcasing unique botanical features that aid in its survival.
