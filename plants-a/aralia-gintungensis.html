Aralia gintungensis

Aralia gintungensis, commonly known as the Philippine aralia, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Araliaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, clustered white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded areas of tropical forests. It has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for horticultural and ecological research.
