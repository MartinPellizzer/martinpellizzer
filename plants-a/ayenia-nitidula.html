Ayenia nitidula

Ayenia nitidula, commonly known as the smooth ayenia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Ayenia nitidula is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
