Alternanthera pungens

Alternanthera pungens, commonly known as red flame or red velvet, is a striking aquatic plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red to orange foliage, which intensifies under bright light conditions. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. It thrives in shallow waters with moderate to high light and is popular in aquariums for its ornamental value.

Alternanthera pungens is a fast-growing plant that can be propagated through stem cuttings.
