Argyrolobium virgatum

Argyrolobium virgatum, commonly known as the silver globular pea, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its spherical, silver-gray foliage. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring, followed by pod-like fruits containing seeds. It is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a distinctive addition to native plant collections.
