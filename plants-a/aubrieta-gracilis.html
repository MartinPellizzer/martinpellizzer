Aubrieta gracilis

Aubrieta gracilis, commonly known as slender aubrieta, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features compact, mat-forming growth with clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has hairy, lance-shaped leaves that are typically covered in a fine layer of down. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Aubrieta gracilis is known for its hardiness and ability to withstand coastal conditions.
