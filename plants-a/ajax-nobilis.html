Ajax nobilis

Ajax nobilis, commonly known as the noble ajax, is a flowering plant belonging to the Acanthaceae family.

It is native to parts of Africa and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from red to purple. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub, reaching heights of up to 1.5 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens in tropical and subtropical regions.
