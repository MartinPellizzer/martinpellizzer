Artemisia hallaisanensis f. swatowiana

Artemisia hallaisanensis f. swatowiana, commonly known as the Swatow wormwood, is a perennial shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, gray-green foliage. The plant produces small, yellowish flower heads that are typically clustered in compact inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its medicinal properties, it has been used in traditional Chinese medicine for various ailments.
