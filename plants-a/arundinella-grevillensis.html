Arundinella grevillensis

Arundinella grevillensis, commonly known as the Greville's reed, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the grass family, Poaceae, and is characterized by its tall, cylindrical stems and narrow leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, greenish flowers. It thrives in wetland environments and is often found in swampy areas along rivers.
