Acetosella debilis

Acetosella debilis, commonly known as the weak sorrel, is a small, low-growing perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a hardy species that can tolerate partial shade and is sometimes used in ornamental gardening for its delicate appearance.
