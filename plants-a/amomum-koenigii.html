Amomum koenigii

Amomum koenigii, commonly known as black pepper.

It is a flowering plant native to India and belongs to the ginger family, Zingiberaceae. The plant produces small, dark purple berries that are dried and used as a spice. The fruit contains a high concentration of piperine, which contributes to its pungent flavor.

It is cultivated for both its ornamental value and its aromatic properties.
