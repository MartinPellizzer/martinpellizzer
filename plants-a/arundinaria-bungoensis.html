Arundinaria bungoensis

Arundinaria bungoensis, commonly known as the Bungo bamboo, is a species of grass native to Japan.

It is characterized by its tall, slender culms that can grow up to several meters in height. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are typically green in color and arranged in a dense, clumping growth habit. It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as along stream banks and in forest understories.

The Bungo bamboo is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been cultivated for traditional purposes in Japanese horticulture.
