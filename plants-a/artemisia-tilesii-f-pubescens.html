Artemisia tilesii f. pubescens

Artemisia tilesii f. pubescens, commonly known as the pubescent mugwort, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its gray-green, fuzzy foliage and aromatic, pungent scent. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and woodland edges. Its small, yellowish-green flowers are clustered in panicles and bloom in late summer.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine for various purposes.
