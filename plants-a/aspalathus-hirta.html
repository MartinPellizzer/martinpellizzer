Aspalathus hirta

Aspalathus hirta, commonly known as the silver leaf bush, is a small shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which gives it its common name, and small, pale green flowers that bloom in spring. The plant is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it suitable for xeriscaping. It is also known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional African medicine.

Aspalathus hirta is an important species in its native habitat, contributing to local biodiversity and ecological stability.
