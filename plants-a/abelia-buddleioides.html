Abelia buddleioides

Abelia buddleioides, commonly known as the Chinese abelia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features showy, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink and are clustered in terminal panicles. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are usually medium green and turn reddish in the fall. It thrives in partial to full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for garden landscapes.

Abelia buddleioides is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
