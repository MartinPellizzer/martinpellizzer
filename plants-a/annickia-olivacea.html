Annickia olivacea

Annickia olivacea, commonly known as the olive tree of Madagascar, is a flowering plant native to the island nation.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree with smooth, dark green, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that are clustered in terminal panicles. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds.

Annickia olivacea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical and subtropical gardens.
