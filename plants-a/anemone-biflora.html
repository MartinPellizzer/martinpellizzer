Anemone biflora

Anemone biflora, commonly known as the two-flowered anemone, is a herbaceous perennial native to the eastern United States.

It features showy, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has basal leaves that are heart-shaped and covered in fine, soft hairs. It typically grows in moist, woodland habitats and prefers partial to full shade.

Anemone biflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting local pollinators.
