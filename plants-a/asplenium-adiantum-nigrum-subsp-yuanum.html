Asplenium adiantum-nigrum subsp. yuanum

Asplenium adiantum-nigrum subsp.

yuanum, commonly known as the Yunnan maidenhair fern, is a species of fern native to China. It belongs to the family Aspleniaceae and is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds.

The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in mountainous regions. Its leaves are usually dark green and have a feathery appearance, with a distinctive blackish hue on the underside.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
