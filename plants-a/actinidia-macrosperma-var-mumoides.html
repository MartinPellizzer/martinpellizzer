Actinidia macrosperma var. mumoides

Actinidia macrosperma var. mumoides, commonly known as the Chinese Actinidia, is a deciduous climbing vine native to China.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and small, fragrant flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant produces edible berries that are typically yellow or orange in color and are often used in traditional Chinese medicine. It thrives in temperate climates and requires well-drained soil and partial shade for optimal growth.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and medicinal properties.
