Anthemis × stipulacea

Anthemis × stipulacea, commonly known as the hybrid blueweed, is a cross between Anthemis cotula and Anthemis tinctoria.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant characterized by its aromatic, finely divided leaves and small blue to purple flower heads. The plant produces ray florets that are typically blue or violet in color, surrounded by a series of yellow disk florets. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and fields.

The species is noted for its medicinal properties and has been used historically in traditional herbal remedies.
