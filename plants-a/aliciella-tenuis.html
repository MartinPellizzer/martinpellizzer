Aliciella tenuis

Aliciella tenuis, commonly known as the slender aliciella, is a small, herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. The plant has slender, upright stems with small, narrow leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small and clustered, usually white or pale green, and bloom in the spring.

Aliciella tenuis is known for its resilience and is often found in coastal and dry environments.
