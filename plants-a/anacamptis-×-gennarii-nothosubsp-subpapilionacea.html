Anacamptis × gennarii nothosubsp.

subpapilionacea, commonly known as the Papilionaceous Orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to southern Europe. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, butterfly-shaped flowers.

The plant typically grows in calcareous soils and thrives in sunny, open habitats. Its inflorescence features a single spike with numerous showy, violet to purple flowers.

This nothosubspecies exhibits a combination of traits from its parent species, Anacamptis morio and Anacamptis pyramidalis.
