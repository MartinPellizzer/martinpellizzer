Andropogon ischyranthus

Andropogon ischyranthus, commonly known as little bluestem, is a perennial grass native to North America.

It is characterized by its fine, blue-green blades and compact growth habit, often forming dense clumps. The plant is adapted to a variety of soil types but thrives in well-drained, sandy or loamy soils. It is a key component of native grasslands and prairies, providing habitat and forage for wildlife.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for native plant gardens and restoration projects.
