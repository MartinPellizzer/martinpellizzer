Aspidium capitainei

It is a small, terrestrial fern that typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often found in rocky crevices or on tree trunks. Its fronds are usually dark green and leathery, with a distinctive, narrow shape.

Aspidium capitainei is native to the Cape Verde Islands and is adapted to the humid, tropical climate of its native habitat.
