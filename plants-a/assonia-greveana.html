Assonia greveana

Assonia greveana, commonly known as the Cape honeysuckle, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Acanthaceae and is characterized by its vibrant orange to red tubular flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and often covered in fine hairs.
