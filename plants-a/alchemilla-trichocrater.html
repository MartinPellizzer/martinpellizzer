Alchemilla trichocrater

Alchemilla trichocrater, commonly known as the hairy alchemilla, is a perennial plant native to parts of Europe and western Asia.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit. The plant features small, greenish-white flowers that appear in clusters during the summer months. Its leaves are covered in fine hairs, giving it a velvety texture.

Alchemilla trichocrater is often found in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and streambanks.
