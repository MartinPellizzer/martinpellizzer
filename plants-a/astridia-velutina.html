Astridia velutina

Astridia velutina, commonly known as the velvet fern, is a species of flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its soft, velvety leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer seasons. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Astridia velutina is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with the right conditions.
