Aloe commelyni var. flavispina

Aloe commelyni var. flavispina, commonly known as the yellow-spined aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, upright stems that are often tinged with red or purple, and its leaves are thick, spiny, and covered in a waxy cuticle. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellowish-green flowers, which bloom in summer. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its leaves and tolerating drought conditions.

Its unique spiny structure and striking appearance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
