Aerides spuria

Aerides spuria, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a herbaceous plant with a rhizomatous growth habit, often found in moist, shaded forest environments. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, contributing to its overall elegant appearance.

Aerides spuria is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
