Alyssum ramosissimum

Alyssum ramosissimum, commonly known as the clustered alyssum, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a low-growing herb with dense clusters of small, fragrant white or yellow flowers. The plant features wiry stems that branch profusely, giving it a bushy appearance. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often covered in fine hairs.

Alyssum ramosissimum is well adapted to arid environments and is often used in rock gardens or as a ground cover.
