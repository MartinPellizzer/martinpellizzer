Asystasia gangetica var. gangetica

Asystasia gangetica var. gangetica, commonly known as Indian nettle or velvetleaf, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Asia.

It belongs to the family Acanthaceae and is characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in wetlands and along riverbanks.
