Athyrium × ikutae

Athyrium × ikutae, commonly known as the hybrid Japanese shield fern, is a fern native to Japan and Korea.

It is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Athyrium nipponicum and Athyrium hirtum. The plant features fronds that are typically bipinnate, with a distinctive shield-shaped base. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
