Acrocentron boissieri

Acrocentron boissieri, commonly known as the Boissier's starwort, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and scrublands. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, while its flowers are small and clustered in a distinctive star-like arrangement.

Acrocentron boissieri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
