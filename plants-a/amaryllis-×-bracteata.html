Amaryllis × bracteata

Amaryllis × bracteata, commonly known as the hybrid amaryllis, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy blooms that come in a variety of colors. The plant typically produces large, trumpet-shaped flowers on tall, sturdy stems. Its foliage consists of broad, strap-like leaves that are often evergreen.

Amaryllis × bracteata is popular as a decorative plant due to its vibrant flowers and relatively easy care requirements.
