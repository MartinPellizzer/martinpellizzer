Ayenia blanchetiana

Ayenia blanchetiana, commonly known as the white sand verbena, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a member of the Verbenaceae family and typically grows in dry, sandy soils. The plant produces small, white or pale purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its foliage consists of narrow, gray-green leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

Ayenia blanchetiana is adapted to arid environments and is often found in desert scrub and grassland habitats.
