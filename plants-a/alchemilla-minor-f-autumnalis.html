Alchemilla minor f. autumnalis

Alchemilla minor f. autumnalis, commonly known as autumnal lady's mantle, is a low-growing perennial plant native to Europe.

It features dense, silver-gray foliage that turns reddish in the autumn, adding ornamental value to gardens. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in late summer, which are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

Its hardy nature and attractive foliage make it a popular choice for ground cover and border plantings.
