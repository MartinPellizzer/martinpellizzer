Aeonium × tamaimense

Aeonium × tamaimense, commonly known as the "Coral Cactus," is a hybrid succulent native to the Canary Islands.

It features rosettes of fleshy, green to reddish leaves that often have a waxy texture. The plant produces vibrant orange to red flowers on tall, branching stems during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
