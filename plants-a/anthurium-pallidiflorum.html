Anthurium pallidiflorum

Anthurium pallidiflorum, commonly known as the pale-flowered anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a glossy texture. The plant produces striking spadices, which are inflorescences that are usually pale green or white in color. Anthurium pallidiflorum thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

It is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its ornamental foliage and unique floral structures.
