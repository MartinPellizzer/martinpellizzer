Anthurium heraclioanum

Anthurium heraclioanum, commonly known as the Heraclioanum Arrowhead, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-like inflorescences surrounded by colorful spathe petals, often in shades of red, orange, or yellow. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Anthurium heraclioanum is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its unique foliage and long-lasting blooms.
