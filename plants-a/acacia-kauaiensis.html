Acacia kauaiensis

Acacia kauaiensis, commonly known as Kauai acacia, is a flowering plant native to the Hawaiian island of Kauai.

It is a small tree or shrub that typically grows in wet, forested areas. The plant produces bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting various pollinators. Its leaves are compound, consisting of multiple small leaflets arranged along a central stem.

Acacia kauaiensis plays an important ecological role in its native habitat, contributing to the biodiversity of the region.
