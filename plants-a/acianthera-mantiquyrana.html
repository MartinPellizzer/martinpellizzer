Acianthera mantiquyrana

Acianthera mantiquyrana, commonly known as the Mantiqueira orchid, is a species of orchid native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique floral structure and vibrant coloration. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments within the Atlantic Forest biome. Its flowers are often fragrant and attract specific pollinators, contributing to its ecological significance.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
