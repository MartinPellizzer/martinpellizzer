Aethusa cynapium var. macrocarpa

Aethusa cynapium var. macrocarpa, commonly known as the giant cowbane, is a variant of the cowbane plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a biennial or short-lived perennial herb that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters. The plant features large, heart-shaped leaves that are often lobed and covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small, greenish-white, and clustered in umbels, typical of the Apiaceae family.

The fruit is a dry, ribbed schizocarp that splits into two one-seeded parts when mature.
