Aeschynomene tenuis

Aeschynomene tenuis, commonly known as slender leaf aeschynomene, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its small, trifoliate leaves and slender stems. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are typically clustered in racemes, followed by elongated, cylindrical pods containing several seeds.
