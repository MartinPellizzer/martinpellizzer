Abies nebrodensis

Abies nebrodensis, commonly known as the Nebroden fir, is a species of evergreen conifer native to the Alps.

It is characterized by its slender, pyramidal shape and needle-like leaves that are dark green and arranged in spirals. The cones of this plant are typically reddish-brown and hang downward, containing winged seeds. It thrives in cool, moist environments at high altitudes.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and its role in alpine ecosystems.
