Artemisia maritima var. szowitziana

Artemisia maritima var. szowitziana, commonly known as the coastal tarragon, is a perennial herb native to coastal regions of the northern hemisphere.

It is characterized by its aromatic, silver-gray foliage and small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in maritime environments. It is valued for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally in herbal remedies.

Its adaptability to salt spray and strong winds makes it a hardy and resilient species.
