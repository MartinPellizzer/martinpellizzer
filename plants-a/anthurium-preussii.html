Anthurium preussii

Anthurium preussii, commonly known as the Preuss's anthurium, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-shaped inflorescences surrounded by a showy, often brightly colored spathe. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium preussii is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and unique flowering structure.
