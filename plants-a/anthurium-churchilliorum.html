Anthurium churchilliorum

Anthurium churchilliorum, commonly known as the Churchill's anthurium, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped spathe that comes in shades of red, orange, and yellow, often with a contrasting green spadix. The plant thrives in humid, warm environments with indirect light and well-draining soil. Its foliage consists of large, glossy, dark green leaves that add to its ornamental appeal.

Anthurium churchilliorum is popular as a houseplant due to its aesthetic value and relatively easy care requirements.
