Alstonia muelleriana

Alstonia muelleriana, commonly known as the Mueller's alstonia, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Apocynaceae and is characterized by its smooth, grey bark and opposite, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers followed by woody capsules containing numerous seeds.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
