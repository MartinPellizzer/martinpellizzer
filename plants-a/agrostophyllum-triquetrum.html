Agrostophyllum triquetrum

Agrostophyllum triquetrum, commonly known as the three-cornered grass.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South America, particularly found in the Andean regions. The plant is characterized by its triangular stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.

Agrostophyllum triquetrum is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
