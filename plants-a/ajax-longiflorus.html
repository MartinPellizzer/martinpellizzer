Ajax longiflorus

Ajax longiflorus, commonly known as the red-hot poker, is a striking plant native to southern Africa.

It features tall, spiky flower clusters that bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow, creating a dramatic visual display. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, tubular flowers that attract pollinators like bees and birds. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its architectural form and vibrant blooms make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
