Amaryllis cylindracea

Amaryllis cylindracea, commonly known as the slender amaryllis, is a bulbous plant native to South America.

It produces striking, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves that grow from a basal rosette of bulbs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

Its blooming period typically occurs in late spring to early summer.
