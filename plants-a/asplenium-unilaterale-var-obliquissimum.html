Asplenium unilaterale var. obliquissimum

Asplenium unilaterale var. obliquissimum, commonly known as the oblique maidenhair fern, is a variant of the maidenhair fern genus Asplenium.

It is characterized by its unique, asymmetrical fronds that curve to one side, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant has slender, dark green leaves with a delicate, lace-like texture. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and botanical collections.
