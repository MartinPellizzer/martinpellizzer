Aloe andersonii

Aloe andersonii, commonly known as Anderson's aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, dark green leaves that are covered in a waxy cuticle, which helps it retain moisture in arid environments. The plant produces tall, branching stems with yellow flowers during the warmer months. It is well adapted to rocky soils and full sun exposure.

Due to its unique appearance and resilience, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
