Abies fabri subsp. yuanbaoshanensis

Abies fabri subsp.

yuanbaoshanensis, commonly known as Yuanbaoshan fir, is a rare and endemic coniferous species native to China. It is characterized by its slender, conical shape and dark green, needle-like leaves.

The tree produces small, cylindrical cones that mature to a reddish-brown color. It thrives in cool, moist climates at high altitudes, typically in mountainous regions.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a valuable subject for botanical research and conservation efforts.
