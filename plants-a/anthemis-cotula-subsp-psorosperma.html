Anthemis cotula subsp. psorosperma

Anthemis cotula subsp.

psorosperma, commonly known as lesser tansy, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads.

The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. It produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in late summer.

Its aromatic leaves and flowers have been used traditionally in herbal remedies.
