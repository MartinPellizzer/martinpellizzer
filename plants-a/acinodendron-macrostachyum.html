Acinodendron macrostachyum

Acinodendron macrostachyum, commonly known as the giant flame tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy red flowers that bloom in clusters, creating a dramatic visual impact. The plant has compound leaves with long, slender leaflets and produces woody pods that contain seeds. It thrives in humid climates and is often found in lowland forests.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in warm regions.
