Alyssum tenuifolium

Alyssum tenuifolium, commonly known as slenderleaf alyssum, is a low-growing annual plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It features small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in spring. The plant has delicate, linear leaves that are typically less than an inch in length. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Alyssum tenuifolium is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
