Anisotome flabellata

Anisotome flabellata, commonly known as the fanleaf grass, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its flat, fan-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in dense, spike-like inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and along riverbanks.

Anisotome flabellata is valued for its ornamental appearance and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
