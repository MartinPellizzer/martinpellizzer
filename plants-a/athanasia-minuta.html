Athanasia minuta

Athanasia minuta, commonly known as the dwarf immortality, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, delicate yellow flowers. The plant produces small, hairy leaves that are typically less than 2 inches in length. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a hardy plant suitable for rock gardens.

Athanasia minuta is also known for its ability to survive in arid conditions, showcasing its resilience.
