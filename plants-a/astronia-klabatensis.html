Astronia klabatensis

Astronia klabatensis, commonly known as the Klabat aster, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of New Guinea.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant produces showy, violet-blue to purple flower heads that bloom in late winter to early spring. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments at high altitudes.

Astronia klabatensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
