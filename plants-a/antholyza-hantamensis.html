Antholyza hantamensis

Antholyza hantamensis, commonly known as the Hantam starwort, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is found in the Western Cape region. It has narrow, linear leaves and produces small yellow flowers arranged in a cluster.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
