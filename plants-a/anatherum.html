Anatherum

Anatherum, commonly known as the false panicgrass, is a genus of grasses belonging to the family Poaceae.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and slender, cylindrical spikelets. The plants typically have narrow, grass-like leaves and are often found in dry, open habitats. Anatherum species are known for their ability to thrive in arid and semi-arid environments.
