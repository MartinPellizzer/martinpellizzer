Aster hoorebekia

Aster hoorebekia, commonly known as the Hoorebeke's aster, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that typically bloom in shades of purple and white. Native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, this plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun. Its foliage consists of narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

Aster hoorebekia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
