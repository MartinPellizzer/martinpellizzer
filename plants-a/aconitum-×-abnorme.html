Aconitum × abnorme

Aconitum × abnorme, commonly known as the hybrid aconite, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Ranunculaceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, sparsely branched stems that can reach heights of up to 2 meters. The plant produces large, showy flowers in shades of blue, purple, or white, which are typically arranged in a raceme. Its leaves are palmately compound, with deeply lobed divisions, and its roots are tuberous.

Aconitum × abnorme is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.
