Arachnothryx flocculosa

Arachnothryx flocculosa, commonly known as the silky arachnothryx, is a tropical shrub native to South America.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and showy, pea-like flowers. The plant produces small, fluffy pods that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

Its growth habit and floral structure make it an interesting subject for ecological and botanical studies.
