Achras zapota var. sphaerica

Achras zapota var. sphaerica, commonly known as chicozapote, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Central America and the Caribbean.

It is characterized by its smooth, dark green leaves and produces small, yellowish-green flowers that develop into woody, spherical fruits. The fruit, known as a chicozapote, has a thick, leathery outer shell and a sweet, edible pulp. The plant is valued for its ornamental appearance and its use in traditional medicine.

It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its fruit and timber.
