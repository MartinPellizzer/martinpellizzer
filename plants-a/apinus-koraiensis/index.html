Apinus Koraiensis

Apinus koraiensis is a rare and fascinating plant native to the mountainous regions of Korea. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in shaded forest areas. This species is known for its unique leaf structure and delicate flowers that bloom in the spring. Due to habitat loss and climate change, it faces increasing threats to its survival. Conservation efforts are crucial to ensure its continued existence in the wild.
