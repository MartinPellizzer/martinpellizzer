Actinodaphne ramosii

Actinodaphne ramosii, commonly known as the hairy-leaf actinodaphne, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of India, Indonesia, and the Philippines. The fruit, which is small and berry-like, matures to a dark color and is typically consumed by birds and other wildlife.
