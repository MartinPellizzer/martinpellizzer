Aster interior

Aster interior, commonly known as the interior aster, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that bloom in late summer and early fall. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid environments. Its foliage consists of narrow, linear leaves that help reduce water loss.

Aster interior is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting pollinators.
