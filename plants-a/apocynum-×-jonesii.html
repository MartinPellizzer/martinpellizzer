Apocynum × jonesii

Apocynum × jonesii, commonly known as the hybrid dogbane, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America.

It belongs to the family Apocynaceae and is characterized by its milky sap and opposite leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically white or pink in color. It is often found in moist, disturbed soils along roadsides and in open woods.

Apocynum × jonesii is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some historical use in traditional medicine.
