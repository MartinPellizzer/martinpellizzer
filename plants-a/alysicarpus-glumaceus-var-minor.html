Alysicarpus glumaceus var. minor

Alysicarpus glumaceus var. minor, commonly known as small-headed burr grass, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is characterized by its leguminous properties and nitrogen-fixing capabilities. The plant produces small, hairy pods that contain seeds with a hard, burred surface. It typically grows in disturbed soils and can be found in grasslands and along roadsides.
