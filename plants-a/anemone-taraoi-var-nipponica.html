Anemone taraoi var. nipponica

Anemone taraoi var. nipponica, commonly known as the Japanese anemone, is a flowering plant native to Japan and parts of eastern Asia.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its showy, single or double pink to white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are palmately divided, with a delicate, fern-like appearance.
