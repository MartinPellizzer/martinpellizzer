Anil prieureana

Anil prieureana, commonly known as the French anemone, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe.

It features showy, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has finely divided, fern-like leaves that are often covered in a powdery coating. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Anil prieureana is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to naturalize in suitable conditions.
