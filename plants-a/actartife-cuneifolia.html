Actartife cuneifolia

Actartife cuneifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf actartife, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It features lance-shaped, dark green leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white hairs, giving it a silvery appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of pink and white, which bloom in the spring. Its fibrous roots are valued in traditional medicine for their purported anti-inflammatory properties.

Actartife cuneifolia thrives in well-drained soils and requires partial to full sunlight for optimal growth.
