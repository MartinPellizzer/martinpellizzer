Aneilema clarkei

Aneilema clarkei, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its upright, herbaceous stems and large, velvety leaves that are typically green with a waxy texture. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of purple, pink, and white, often with a contrasting central stripe. It thrives in humid, well-drained soil and requires indirect sunlight for optimal growth.

Aneilema clarkei is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
