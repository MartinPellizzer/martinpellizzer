Anthurium panamense

Anthurium panamense, commonly known as the Panama arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to Central America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are often a deep green with a reddish or purple tint. The plant produces an inflorescence consisting of a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe, which can range from white to red or orange. It thrives in humid, warm environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Anthurium panamense is popular as a decorative plant due to its ornamental foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
