Astilbe biternata var. crenatiloba

Astilbe biternata var. crenatiloba, commonly known as the serrated leaf astilbe, is a herbaceous perennial native to eastern Asia.

It features upright, branched stems that bear clusters of fragrant, lace-like flowers in shades of pink, white, or red. The leaves are deeply lobed and have a serrated edge, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in partial to full shade and moist, well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens.

This variety is valued for its delicate foliage and long-lasting blooms that add color to shaded landscapes.
