Acanthophippium sylhetense var. pictum

Acanthophippium sylhetense var. pictum, commonly known as the Sylhet pictorial orchid, is a rare and endemic species native to the northeastern region of Bangladesh.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, colorful floral patterns that resemble intricate paintings. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist habitats such as forest understories and along stream banks. Its leaves are lanceolate and covered with a dense layer of fine, white pubescence.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a conservation concern.
