Anthurium salvinii

Anthurium salvinii, commonly known as the velvet anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a velvety texture. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-shaped inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, often in shades of red, orange, or yellow. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect sunlight for optimal growth.

Anthurium salvinii is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
