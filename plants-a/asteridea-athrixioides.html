Asteridea athrixioides

Asteridea athrixioides, commonly known as the silver aster, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and clusters of small, star-shaped flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is known for its medicinal properties. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its ornamental value is complemented by its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
