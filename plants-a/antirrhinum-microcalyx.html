Antirrhinum microcalyx

Antirrhinum microcalyx, commonly known as the dwarf snapdragon, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, tubular flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant produces small, rounded seed pods that contain numerous tiny seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming period, which extends from spring to early autumn.
