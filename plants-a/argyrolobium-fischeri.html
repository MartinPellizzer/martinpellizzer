Argyrolobium fischeri

Argyrolobium fischeri, commonly known as the Fischer's silver globular pea, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its spherical, silver-gray seed pods. The plant produces small, white or pink flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
