Agathaea microphylla

Agathaea microphylla, commonly known as the small-leaved agathis, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Araucariaceae and is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and tall, straight trunk. The leaves are small, ovate, and arranged spirally along the stems. It produces coniferous-type cones and is valued for its durable wood.

This species plays an important ecological role in its native habitats.
