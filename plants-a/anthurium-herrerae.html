Anthurium herrerae

Anthurium herrerae, commonly known as the Herrera's anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are deep green with a metallic sheen. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-shaped inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, typically in shades of red or orange. It thrives in humid, warm environments with bright, indirect light.

Anthurium herrerae is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
