Asplenium obtusatum var. obliquum

Asplenium obtusatum var. obliquum, commonly known as the oblique shield fern, is a variant of the broad-leaved shield fern.

It is a perennial fern native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in regions like Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant features lanceolate, leathery leaves that are typically dark green and have a slightly wavy margin. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.

Its unique leaf shape and growth habit make it a distinctive species within the Asplenium genus.
