Angraecum longicalcar

Angraecum longicalcar, commonly known as the long spur angraecum, is a species of orchid native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its striking, elongated floral spurs that can reach lengths of up to 15 centimeters. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences with showy, white to pale yellow flowers. Its leaves are typically elliptical and arranged in a rosette pattern.

Angraecum longicalcar thrives in humid, shaded environments and is adapted to grow on trees or rocks in its native habitat.
