Asarum cavaleriei

Asarum cavaleriei, commonly known as Chinese wild betony, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the Aristolochiaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped, aromatic leaves. The plant produces small, dark purple flowers that are often hidden beneath its foliage. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its medicinal properties, it has been used in traditional Chinese medicine for various ailments.
