Arnica silvatica

Arnica silvatica, commonly known as mountain arnica, is a perennial herb native to the alpine regions of Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or grassy habitats at high altitudes. Its leaves are basal, lance-shaped, and covered in fine hairs.

Arnica silvatica is known for its medicinal properties, particularly in traditional herbal remedies.
