Alyssum calycinum var. genuinum

Alyssum calycinum var. genuinum, commonly known as the Narrow-leaf Alyssum, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pale pink, typically blooming in spring. The plant has slender, branching stems and narrow, linear leaves that are covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its compact size and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for ornamental horticulture.
