Ascolepis setigera

Ascolepis setigera, commonly known as the slender antler orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly in countries like Kenya and Tanzania. The plant is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in shaded, moist environments, often found in forest understories.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a threatened species in its native range.
