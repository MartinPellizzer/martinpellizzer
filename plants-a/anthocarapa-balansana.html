Anthocarapa balansana

Anthocarapa balansana, commonly known as the Balansan starfruit, is a tropical plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the family Anacardiaceae, which includes other notable species like the cashew and mango. The plant is characterized by its compound leaves and small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruit, resembling a starfruit in appearance, is edible and has a sweet, aromatic flavor.
