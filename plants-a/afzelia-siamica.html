Afzelia siamica

Afzelia siamica, commonly known as Siam rosewood, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark, dense wood that is highly valued for its durability and aesthetic qualities. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually elliptical in shape. It produces small, white flowers that are followed by elongated, woody fruits.

Afzelia siamica is often found in lowland forests and is known for its slow growth rate.
