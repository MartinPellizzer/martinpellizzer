Aloe spiralis

Aloe spiralis, commonly known as the spiral aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its unique spiral arrangement of fleshy, blue-green leaves that form a compact rosette. The plant produces small, yellow flowers on tall, branched stems during the summer months. Aloe spiralis is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with plenty of sunlight.

Its compact size and striking appearance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation.
