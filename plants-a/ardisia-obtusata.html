Ardisia obtusata

Ardisia obtusata, commonly known as the blunt-leaf ardisia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a member of the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and blueberries. The plant features glossy, dark green leaves that are typically ovate to elliptical in shape. Its small, white flowers are clustered in umbels and are followed by bright red berries that are edible for some bird species.
