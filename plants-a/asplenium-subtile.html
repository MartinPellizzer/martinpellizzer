Asplenium subtile

Asplenium subtile, commonly known as the delicate spleenwort, is a small, evergreen fern belonging to the family Aspleniaceae.

It is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves that are typically less than 10 centimeters in length. This plant prefers moist, shaded environments and is often found growing on rocks or in crevices. Its fronds are usually pale green and have a delicate, lace-like appearance.

Asplenium subtile is a low-maintenance plant that is well-suited for indoor cultivation.
