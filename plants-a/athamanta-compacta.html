Athamanta compacta

Athamanta compacta, commonly known as the dwarf daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces small, daisy-like flowers in shades of white and yellow. The plant has a compact, bushy habit with narrow, linear leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
