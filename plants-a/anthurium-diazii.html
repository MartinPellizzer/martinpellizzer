Anthurium diazii

Anthurium diazii, commonly known as the Red Flame Anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped spathes that range in color from deep red to orange. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically surrounded by a showy, leaf-like bract. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Anthurium diazii is popular as a houseplant due to its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
