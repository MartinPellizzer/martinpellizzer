Alsophila nigropaleata

Alsophila nigropaleata, commonly known as the black-stemmed tree fern, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds that grow in a spiral pattern. The plant has a robust root system that helps it thrive in moist, shaded environments. Its fronds are typically dark green and can reach lengths of up to two meters.

Alsophila nigropaleata plays an important ecological role in tropical forests, providing habitat and contributing to forest structure.
