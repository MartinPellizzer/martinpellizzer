Aloe menyharthii subsp. menyharthii

Aloe menyharthii subsp.

menyharthii, commonly known as the Natal aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa. It features tall, branched stems with narrow, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a reddish tinge.

The plant produces striking orange-red flowers in clusters during the summer months. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or arid landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance, it is a popular choice for xeriscaping.
