Agrostis stolonifera f. amethystina

Agrostis stolonifera f. amethystina, commonly known as purple meadowgrass, is a flowering plant belonging to the grass family Poaceae.

It is a variant of Agrostis stolonifera, characterized by its distinctive purple to violet-colored inflorescences. This cultivar is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its attractive coloration and fine-textured foliage. It thrives in well-drained soils and full to partial sunlight, making it suitable for a variety of garden environments.

The plant is known for its low maintenance and ability to form dense, attractive lawns or ground covers.
