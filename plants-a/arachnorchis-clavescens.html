Arachnorchis clavescens

Arachnorchis clavescens, commonly known as the clubbed spider-orchid, is an epiphytic orchid native to Australia.

It is characterized by its unique, club-shaped pseudobulbs and long, narrow leaves. The plant produces striking, spider-like flowers with a distinctive purple and yellow coloration. It typically blooms in spring and is found in subtropical and tropical regions.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
