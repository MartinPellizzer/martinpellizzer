Adonis vernalis f. adulta

Adonis vernalis f. adulta, commonly known as the spring adonis, is a flowering plant belonging to the Ranunculaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in woodland or meadow habitats. Its leaves are trifoliate, with three deeply lobed leaflets.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its early blooming period.
