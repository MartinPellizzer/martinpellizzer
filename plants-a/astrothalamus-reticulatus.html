Astrothalamus reticulatus

Astrothalamus reticulatus, commonly known as the net-veined star thorn, is a member of the family Anacardiaceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive star-shaped thorns and compound leaves with a reticulated venation pattern. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by red, fleshy fruits. It is native to parts of Africa and thrives in tropical and subtropical climates.

Due to its unique morphology and ecological significance, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists alike.
