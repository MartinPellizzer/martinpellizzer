Aster proteus

Aster proteus, commonly known as the purple coneflower, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its showy purple to blue flowers that bloom in the summer months. The plant has a robust root system and thrives in well-drained soils. It is often found in prairies and open woodlands, where it contributes to the local biodiversity.

Aster proteus is also valued for its ornamental beauty and is a popular choice for gardens and restoration projects.
