Angraecum cultriforme

Angraecum cultriforme, commonly known as the sword orchid, is a rare and striking epiphytic orchid native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its long, slender, sword-like leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers with long, spiral-shaped sepals and petals. It blooms during the dry season, typically in late summer or early autumn.

Angraecum cultriforme is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts due to its unique appearance and rarity.
