Anthurium macrocephalum

Anthurium macrocephalum, commonly known as the large-headed anthurium, is a tropical flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-shaped inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, typically in shades of red, orange, or yellow. It thrives in humid, warm environments with bright, indirect light.

Anthurium macrocephalum is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and air-purifying properties.
