Aethionema banaticum

Aethionema banaticum, commonly known as the Banat carnation, is a flowering plant native to the Carpathian region.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces vibrant pink or white flowers in early summer. The plant has small, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or alpine settings.

Its botanical features make it a valuable addition to botanical collections and gardens.
