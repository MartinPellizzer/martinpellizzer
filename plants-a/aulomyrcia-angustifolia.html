Aulomyrcia angustifolia

Aulomyrcia angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leafed myrcia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and small, clustered white or pinkish flowers. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments within humid forests.

The fruit is a small, fleshy berry that contains several seeds.
