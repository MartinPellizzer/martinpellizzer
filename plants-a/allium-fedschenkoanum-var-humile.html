Allium fedschenkoanum var. humile

Allium fedschenkoanum var. humile, commonly known as the dwarf garlic or small garlic, is a herbaceous plant belonging to the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, clustered bulbs and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers in umbels, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Native to Central Asia, it thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.

Due to its ornamental value and mild garlic scent, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
