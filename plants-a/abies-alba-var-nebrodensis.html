Abies alba var. nebrodensis

Abies alba var. nebrodensis, commonly known as the Nebroden fir, is a rare and endemic subspecies of the European fir.

It is characterized by its slender, conical shape and dark green, needle-like leaves. The plant is native to the Alps, specifically in regions of Italy and Austria. It thrives in high-altitude environments with cool, moist climates.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species.
