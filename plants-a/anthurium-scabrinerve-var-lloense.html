Anthurium scabrinerve var. lloense

Anthurium scabrinerve var. lloense, commonly known as the Lloense anthurium, is a striking tropical plant native to the Andean region of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped leaves that are often a deep green with prominent veins. The plant produces striking spadices, which are typically red or orange, surrounded by a showy, white or cream-colored spathe. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.
