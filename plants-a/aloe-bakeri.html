Aloe bakeri

Aloe bakeri, commonly known as the Baker's aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, spiky leaves that store water, typical of arid environments. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow flowers, which bloom in summer. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their soothing properties.

Aloe bakeri thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for xeriscaping.
