Agrostis schischkinii

Agrostis schischkinii, commonly known as Schischkin's bentgrass, is a low-growing perennial grass species native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It typically forms dense, mat-like growth and is characterized by its fine, wiry leaves and compact inflorescence. This plant thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is often found in alpine and subalpine habitats. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping.

Agrostis schischkinii is also of interest to botanists due to its adaptability to harsh environmental conditions.
