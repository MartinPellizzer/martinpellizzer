Achimenes × georgeana var. discolor

Achimenes × georgeana var. discolor, commonly known as the variegated achimenes, is a hybrid plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, pink, and white. The leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a smooth, glossy texture. This plant thrives in warm, humid conditions and prefers well-draining soil.

It is often cultivated as a decorative plant for gardens and indoor spaces due to its ornamental value.
