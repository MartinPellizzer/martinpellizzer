Anoectochilus densiflorus

Anoectochilus densiflorus, commonly known as the dense-flowered orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

Anoectochilus densiflorus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
