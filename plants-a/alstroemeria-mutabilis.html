Alstroemeria mutabilis

Alstroemeria mutabilis, commonly known as the variable alstroemeria, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the family Alstroemeriaceae and is characterized by its striking, often colorful flowers that can change hue as they mature. The plant typically grows as a herbaceous perennial, with strap-like leaves and tall, slender stems. Its flowers are usually borne in clusters and can range in color from red to orange to yellow.

Alstroemeria mutabilis is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
