Aster bezanillana

Aster bezanillana, commonly known as the Canary Island aster, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or coastal areas, showing adaptability to harsh environments. Its flowers are usually blue to violet in color, with ray florets surrounding a central disk.

Aster bezanillana is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting pollinators.
