Anemone jiachaensis

Anemone jiachaensis, commonly known as the Jiacha anemone, is a perennial flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its showy, single or double flowers that typically bloom in spring. The plant features basal leaves that are palmately lobed and covered in soft, hairy texture. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
