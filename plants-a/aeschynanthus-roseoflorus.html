Aeschynanthus roseoflorus

Aeschynanthus roseoflorus, commonly known as the rose-flowered ivy.

It is a flowering plant native to the rainforests of western Africa. The plant produces clusters of small, showy flowers that are typically pink or red in color. It has trailing stems and is often grown as a houseplant due to its ornamental value.

The leaves are usually elliptical and have a waxy texture, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
