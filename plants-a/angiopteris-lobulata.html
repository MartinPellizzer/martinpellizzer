Angiopteris lobulata

Angiopteris lobulata, commonly known as the lobed fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Athyriaceae.

It is characterized by its large, lobed fronds that can reach lengths of up to 2 meters. The plant is native to Southeast Asia and thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its reproductive structures include sori that are clustered on the underside of the fronds.

Angiopteris lobulata is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
