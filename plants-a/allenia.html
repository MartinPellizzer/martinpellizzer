Allenia

Allenia, commonly known as the silverleaf nightshade, is a flowering plant belonging to the Solanaceae family.

It is native to regions of Africa and is characterized by its small, white or pale blue flowers. The plant has hairy, oval-shaped leaves that give it its common name. Allenia typically grows as an annual herb, reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters.

It is often found in disturbed soils and is sometimes considered a weed in agricultural settings.
