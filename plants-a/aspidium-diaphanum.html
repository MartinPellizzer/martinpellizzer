Aspidium diaphanum

Aspidium diaphanum, commonly known as the translucent fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Athyriaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, translucent fronds that give it a unique visual appearance. Native to tropical and subtropical regions, this plant thrives in moist, shaded environments. The fronds are typically bipinnate, with small, closely spaced pinnae that contribute to its intricate structure.

Aspidium diaphanum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
