Andersonia ferricola

Andersonia ferricola, commonly known as the ironstone lily, is a flowering plant native to the western regions of South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking violet to purple flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils, often found in quartzite formations. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.
