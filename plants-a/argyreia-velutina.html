Argyreia velutina

Argyreia velutina, commonly known as the velvet flower or velvet leaf, is a climbing vine native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its velvety, dark green foliage and clusters of small, pale yellow flowers. The plant produces striking, lantern-shaped fruits that turn bright red when mature. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in moist, shaded environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and greenhouses worldwide.
