Anthurium funiferum

Anthurium funiferum, commonly known as the red-tendril anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are dark green with prominent veins. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-shaped inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, typically in shades of red or orange. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Anthurium funiferum is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
