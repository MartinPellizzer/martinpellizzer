Anemia ahenobarba

Anemia ahenobarba, commonly known as the desert lily, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are adapted to reduce water loss in hot, dry environments. It produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

Anemia ahenobarba is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
