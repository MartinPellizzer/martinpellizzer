Asterocarpus arboreus

Asterocarpus arboreus, commonly known as the tree asterocarpus, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its woody stem and compound leaves, which are often arranged in a feather-like pattern. Its flowers are small and clustered in dense heads, typically displaying yellow or orange hues.

Asterocarpus arboreus is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.
