Aloe eru var. hookeri

Aloe eru var. hookeri, commonly known as the Cape aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, spire-like stems covered in thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces striking red or orange flowers during the summer months, which are borne on tall, branched inflorescences. Its leaves contain a gel-like substance that is often used for its medicinal properties.

Aloe eru var. hookeri is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
