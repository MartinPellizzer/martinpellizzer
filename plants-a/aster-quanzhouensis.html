Aster quanzhouensis

Aster quanzhouensis, commonly known as the Quanzhou aster, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers. The plant is characterized by its blue to violet flowers and narrow, grass-like leaves. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forest margins or along stream banks.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
