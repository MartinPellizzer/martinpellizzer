Amaryllis illustris

Amaryllis illustris, commonly known as the pink amaryllis, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant produces strap-shaped leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Amaryllis illustris is often cultivated for its ornamental value and can be grown both indoors and outdoors.
