Achillea × intermedia

Achillea × intermedia, commonly known as the yarrow hybrid, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, flat-topped clusters of small, usually white or pale yellow flowers. The plant has finely divided, gray-green leaves that are aromatic and often used in herbal remedies. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a common garden plant.

Achillea × intermedia is known for its hardiness and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
