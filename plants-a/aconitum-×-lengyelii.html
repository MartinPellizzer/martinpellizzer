Aconitum × lengyelii

Aconitum × lengyelii, commonly known as the Lengyel hybrid bugbane, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Europe.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its tall, sparsely leafed stems that can reach heights of up to 2 meters. The plant produces striking blue to purple spires of tubular flowers, which bloom in late summer. Its foliage consists of palmately compound leaves with serrated edges, typical of the genus Aconitum.

Aconitum × lengyelii is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and prefers moist, well-drained soils in partial shade.
