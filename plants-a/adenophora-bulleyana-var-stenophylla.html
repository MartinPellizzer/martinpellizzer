Adenophora bulleyana var. stenophylla

Adenophora bulleyana var. stenophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leaf bellflower, is a perennial herb native to eastern China.

It features slender, upright stems that bear opposite, lanceolate leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers that are typically blue to violet in color, arranged in clusters at the tips of the stems. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
