Albuca polyphylla

Albuca polyphylla, commonly known as the many-leafed albuca, is a bulbous plant native to southern Africa.

It features a cluster of narrow, grass-like leaves that form a dense rosette. The plant produces tall, slender stems topped with clusters of white or pale yellow flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Albuca polyphylla is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
