Arundarbor refracta

Arundarbor refracta, commonly known as the crystalline willow, is a unique plant species characterized by its translucent leaves that refract light in a prismatic manner.

Native to the misty highlands of the northern regions, it thrives in cool, moist environments with partial shade. The plant's stems are flexible and adorned with small, velvety leaves that shimmer under sunlight. Its flowers, which bloom in early spring, are small and clustered, producing a subtle, sweet fragrance.

Due to its rare and delicate nature, it is highly valued in botanical gardens and conservation efforts.
