Angiopteris laciniata

Angiopteris laciniata, commonly known as the lace fern, is a species of fern characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds.

It belongs to the family Athyriaceae and is native to Southeast Asia, including regions such as India, China, and Indonesia. Angiopteris laciniata is often cultivated in shaded gardens or as a houseplant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
