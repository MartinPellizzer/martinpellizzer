Ayenia herbacea

Ayenia herbacea, commonly known as the common ayenia, is a flowering plant native to the temperate regions of South America.

It is a low-growing, herbaceous perennial that typically reaches heights of about 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant produces small, showy flowers that are usually pink or white, arranged in clusters along the stems. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Ayenia herbacea thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or shaded areas.
