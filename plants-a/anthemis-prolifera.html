Anthemis prolifera

Anthemis prolifera, commonly known as the spreading sunflower, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features slender, branching stems and small yellow flowers that bloom in summer. Its leaves are typically linear and covered in fine hairs.

Anthemis prolifera is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments.
