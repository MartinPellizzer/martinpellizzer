Aeranthes pachyrrhyza

Aeranthes pachyrrhyza, commonly known as the broad-ribbed orchid, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy rhizomes and striking, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of yellow and orange. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories. Its leaves are broad and strap-like, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Aeranthes pachyrrhyza is valued in horticulture for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated for its unique floral display.
