Arisaema xuanweiense

Arisaema xuanweiense, commonly known as the Xuanwei arrowhead, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its distinctive arrow-shaped leaves and spadix inflorescence. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as mountain forests. Its flowers are unisexual, with separate male and female flowers on the spadix.

Arisaema xuanweiense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
