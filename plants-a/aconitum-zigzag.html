Aconitum zigzag

Aconitum zigzag, commonly known as the zigzag monkshood, is a member of the Ranunculaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking, hood-shaped flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. The plant has a tall, erect stem with palmate leaves that are deeply lobed. Native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, it thrives in moist, well-drained soils.

Due to its toxic alkaloids, it is important to handle the plant with care.
