Anemone alba

Anemone alba, commonly known as the white anemone, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features showy, white petals that surround a yellow center, creating a striking floral display. The plant typically grows in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are palmately lobed, with a rough, hairy texture.

Anemone alba is known for its early spring blooming habit, often appearing among the first wildflowers to emerge in temperate regions.
