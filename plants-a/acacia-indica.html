Acacia indica

Acacia indica, commonly known as the Indian acacia, is a flowering plant native to the Indian subcontinent.

It is a medium-sized shrub or tree that can grow up to 10 meters in height. The plant is characterized by its bipinnate leaves and yellow, spherical flower heads. Its bark and leaves have been used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

Acacia indica thrives in arid and semi-arid climates, making it well-adapted to dry environments.
