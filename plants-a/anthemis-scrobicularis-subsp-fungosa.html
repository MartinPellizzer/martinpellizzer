Anthemis scrobicularis subsp. fungosa

Anthemis scrobicularis subsp.

fungosa, commonly known as the fungous anthemis, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, mat-forming growth habit.

The plant produces small, yellow to orange composite flowers that bloom in summer. Its leaves are typically gray-green with a fuzzy texture, and it thrives in well-drained, sandy soils.

It is often used in rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and ornamental value.
