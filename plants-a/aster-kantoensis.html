Aster kantoensis

Aster kantoensis, commonly known as the Kanto aster, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers bloom in shades of purple and white.

Aster kantoensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting pollinators.
