Aerangis verdickii var. rusituensis

Aerangis verdickii var. rusituensis, commonly known as the Rusituensis Aerangis, is a rare and striking orchid species native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its elegant, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of fragrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in tropical rainforests. Its botanical significance lies in its unique floral structure and limited distribution, making it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered vulnerable, highlighting the need for protective measures.
