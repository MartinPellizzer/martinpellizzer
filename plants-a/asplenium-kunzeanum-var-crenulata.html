Asplenium kunzeanum var. crenulata

Asplenium kunzeanum var. crenulata, commonly known as the serrated shield fern, is a terrestrial fern belonging to the Aspleniaceae family.

It is characterized by its distinctive, lanceolate to ovate leaves with serrated margins, which give it its common name. The fronds are typically dark green and have a leathery texture, often with a slightly curled appearance. This variety is native to tropical and subtropical regions, where it thrives in moist, shaded environments.

It is often found growing in forest undergrowth or on rocky surfaces, showcasing its adaptability to various microhabitats.
