Acer palmatum f. brevilobum

Acer palmatum f. brevilobum, commonly known as the compact Japanese maple, is a cultivar of the Japanese maple tree known for its smaller, more rounded leaves.

It exhibits a compact growth habit, making it suitable for smaller gardens or containers. The foliage is typically a vibrant green in summer, turning shades of red and orange in the fall. It prefers partial shade and well-drained soil, and it is relatively resistant to pests and diseases.

This ornamental plant is prized for its aesthetic appeal and adaptability to various garden settings.
