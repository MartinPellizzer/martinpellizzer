Anchusa × aetheria

Anchusa × aetheria, commonly known as the hybrid forget-me-not, is a flowering plant belonging to the Boraginaceae family.

It is a hybrid between Anchusa officinalis and Anchusa tetrasepala, resulting in a plant with distinctive blue to violet flowers. The species is characterized by its hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves, which are often covered in fine pubescence. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Anchusa × aetheria is also noted for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
