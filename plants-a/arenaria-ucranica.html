Arenaria ucranica

Arenaria ucranica, commonly known as the Ukrainian sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the steppes of Ukraine.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its compact, mat-forming growth habit. The plant produces small, white or pinkish flowers in early summer, which are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are typically narrow and fleshy, adapted to survive in dry, sandy environments.

Arenaria ucranica is often found in open, disturbed soils and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
