Anthurium cupreum

Anthurium cupreum, commonly known as the copper anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a metallic sheen. The plant produces vibrant red spathes that enclose its spadix, which is a typical feature of the Araceae family. Anthurium cupreum thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, nutrient-rich soil.

Its unique foliage and ornamental value make it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical landscapes.
