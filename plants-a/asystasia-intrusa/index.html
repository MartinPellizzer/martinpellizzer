Asystasia Intrusa

Asystasia intrusa is a strikingly beautiful flowering plant native to Southeast Asia. It is known for its vibrant purple and blue flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. This species thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its unique foliage and colorful blooms make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening. Despite its beauty, it can be invasive in certain areas, requiring careful management to prevent ecological disruption.
