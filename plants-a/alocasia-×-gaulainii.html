Alocasia × gaulainii

Alocasia × gaulainii, commonly known as the Black Velvet Plant, is a hybrid cultivar of the Alocasia genus, characterized by its dark, velvety foliage.

It is native to Southeast Asia and thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light. The plant features large, heart-shaped leaves with a glossy, almost metallic sheen that enhances its ornamental appeal. It requires well-draining soil and consistent moisture to maintain its vibrant appearance.

Alocasia × gaulainii is popular as a houseplant due to its striking foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
