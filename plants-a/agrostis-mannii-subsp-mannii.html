Agrostis mannii subsp. mannii

Agrostis mannii subsp.

mannii, commonly known as the dwarf bentgrass, is a low-growing perennial grass species native to parts of Africa. It typically forms dense, mat-like growth and is often found in moist, well-drained soils.

The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping.

Botanically, it is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit and its ability to thrive in temperate climates.
