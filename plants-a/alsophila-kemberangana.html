Alsophila kemberangana

Alsophila kemberangana, commonly known as the Kembera tree fern, is a species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in rainforests and along riverbanks. The plant plays a significant ecological role in its native habitat by providing shelter and food for various wildlife species.
