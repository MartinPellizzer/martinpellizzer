Aglaonema malaccense

Aglaonema malaccense, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated foliage with shades of green, white, and pink. The plant belongs to the Araceae family and is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and can grow up to 30 cm in length.

Aglaonema malaccense is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and air-purifying properties.
