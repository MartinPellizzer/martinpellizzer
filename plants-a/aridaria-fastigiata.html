Aridaria fastigiata

Aridaria fastigiata, commonly known as the upright aridaria, is a succulent plant native to arid regions.

It features a tall, columnar stem with sparse, spiky leaves that reduce water loss. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It is adapted to survive in low-water environments with well-draining soil.

Its unique structure makes it a striking addition to xeriscaped gardens.
