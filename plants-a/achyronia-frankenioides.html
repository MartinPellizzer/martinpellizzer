Achyronia frankenioides

Achyronia frankenioides, commonly known as the Frankenioides sunflower, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its yellow-orange ray flowers and greenish-brown disk flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and thrives in full sun. It is often found in fynbos vegetation, which is a unique type of shrubland ecosystem.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
