Artemisia hanseniana

Artemisia hanseniana, commonly known as the Hansen's wormwood, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the sunflower family (Asteraceae) and is characterized by its aromatic, silver-gray foliage. The plant produces small, yellowish flower heads that are clustered in dense, spherical shapes. It thrives in dry, well-drained soils and is often found in alpine or arid environments.

Due to its strong scent and medicinal properties, it has been used traditionally in herbal remedies.
