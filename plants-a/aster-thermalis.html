Aster thermalis

Aster thermalis, commonly known as the thermal aster, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that bloom in shades of purple, pink, and white. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders. Its foliage is typically gray-green, with hairy leaves that help reduce water loss in dry conditions.

Aster thermalis is known for its resilience and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
