Astragalus patrius

Astragalus patrius, commonly known as milkvetch, is a perennial herb native to Europe, North Africa, and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant produces cylindrical seed pods that contain numerous tiny, kidney-shaped seeds. Astragalus patrius is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils.

It has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
