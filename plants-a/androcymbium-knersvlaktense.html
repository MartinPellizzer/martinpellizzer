Androcymbium knersvlaktense

It is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, intricately patterned flowers that resemble a crown. The plant thrives in sandy, well-drained soils and requires specific microclimatic conditions to flourish. Its small stature and delicate appearance make it a distinctive component of the region's botanical diversity.
