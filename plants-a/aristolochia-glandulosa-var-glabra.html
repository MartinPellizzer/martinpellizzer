Aristolochia glandulosa var. glabra

Aristolochia glandulosa var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth glandular arrowhead, is a member of the Aristolochiaceae family.

It is a climbing vine characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and showy, hooded flowers. The plant is native to tropical regions of Central and South America. Its flowers are typically greenish-white with a distinct, intricate pattern.

The species is known for its medicinal properties, though it contains toxic compounds that require careful handling.
