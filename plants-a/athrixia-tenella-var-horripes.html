Athrixia tenella var. horripes

Athrixia tenella var. horripes, commonly known as the velvet tassel plant, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a velvety appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers that are clustered in tassels, which are often mistaken for leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its unique texture and ornamental value make it a distinctive addition to botanical collections.
