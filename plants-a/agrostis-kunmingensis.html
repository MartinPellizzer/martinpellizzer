Agrostis kunmingensis

Agrostis kunmingensis, commonly known as the Kunming bentgrass, is a perennial grass species native to China.

It typically grows in moist, temperate climates and is often found in meadows and along stream banks. The plant has slender, wiry stems and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green coloration.

Agrostis kunmingensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens and landscaping.
