Adipera × arsenei

Adipera × arsenei, commonly known as the hybrid snowflake, is a perennial flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows to a height of 10–30 cm and features basal leaves that are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine meadows.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens, though it is not widely available.
