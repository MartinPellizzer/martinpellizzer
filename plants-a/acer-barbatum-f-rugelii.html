Acer barbatum f. rugelii

Acer barbatum f. rugelii, commonly known as the rugelii maple, is a rare and unique variant of the Japanese maple.

It is characterized by its finely serrated, ovate leaves that display a striking range of colors from bright red to deep crimson during the autumn season. The plant is native to Japan and thrives in temperate climates with well-drained soil. Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for smaller gardens or as a container plant.

Due to its ornamental value and limited distribution, it is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal in horticulture.
