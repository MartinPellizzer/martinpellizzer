Arisaema arunachalensis

Arisaema arunachalensis, commonly known as the Arunachal arrowhead, is a member of the Araceae family and is native to the eastern Himalayas.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its distinctive arrowhead-shaped leaves and a spadix surrounded by a spathe. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments at high altitudes. Its botanical features include a rhizomatous root system and showy, often colorful inflorescences.

Arisaema arunachalensis is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphology and ecological significance in its native habitat.
