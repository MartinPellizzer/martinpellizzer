Alocasia macrorrhizos var. brisbanensis

Alocasia macrorrhizos var. brisbanensis, commonly known as the Brisbane velvet leaf, is a striking tropical plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are dark green with a velvety texture and prominent veins. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, fertile soil. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique foliage.

Alocasia macrorrhizos var. brisbanensis is also known for its ability to purify indoor air, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
