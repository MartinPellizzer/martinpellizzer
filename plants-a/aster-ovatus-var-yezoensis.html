Aster ovatus var. yezoensis

Aster ovatus var. yezoensis, commonly known as the Yezo Aster, is a perennial herb native to Japan.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and clusters of small, blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30 cm and features narrow, grass-like leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a suitable candidate for rock gardens or borders.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its delicate floral display.
