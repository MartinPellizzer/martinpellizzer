Ammannia santoi

Ammannia santoi, commonly known as the redroot ammannia, is a flowering plant native to the wetlands of South America.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and vibrant red or purple stems, which are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in late summer. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a slightly serrated edge.

Ammannia santoi thrives in moist, nutrient-rich soils and is often found in marshy or swampy environments.
