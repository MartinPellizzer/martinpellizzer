Asemeia hebeclada var. hebeclada

Asemeia hebeclada var. hebeclada, commonly known as the silver leaf hebe, is a perennial shrub native to South America.

It features narrow, silver-gray leaves that are densely covered with fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, tubular flowers in shades of blue or purple, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and drought tolerance.
