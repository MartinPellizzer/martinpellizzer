Aloiampelos tenuior var. tenuior

Aloiampelos tenuior var. tenuior, commonly known as the narrow-leaved silver vine, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and small, fragrant white or pale yellow flowers. The plant produces slender, cylindrical fruits that contain numerous small, hard seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or container cultivation.

Its ornamental value lies in its delicate foliage and prolonged flowering period.
