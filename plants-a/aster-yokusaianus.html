Aster yokusaianus

Aster yokusaianus, commonly known as the Japanese aster, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its flowers bloom in the summer months, producing showy blue to purple ray florets.

Aster yokusaianus is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
