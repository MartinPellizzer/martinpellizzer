Ackama nymanii

Ackama nymanii, commonly known as the African orchid, is a species of orchid native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and showy, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in late summer. The plant produces large, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, white pubescence. Ackama nymanii thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Its unique floral structure and ornamental value make it a popular choice for botanical gardens and horticultural collections.
