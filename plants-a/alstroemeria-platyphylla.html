Alstroemeria platyphylla

Alstroemeria platyphylla, commonly known as the flat-leaved lily, is a South American plant belonging to the Alstroemeriaceae family.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that come in a range of colors including red, orange, and yellow. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and is often found in the Andes region. It produces small, clustered berries that contain the seeds.

Alstroemeria platyphylla is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various garden conditions.
