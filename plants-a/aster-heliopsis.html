Aster heliopsis

Aster heliopsis, commonly known as sunflower aster, is a herbaceous perennial native to eastern North America.

It features upright stems with opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, hairy textures. The plant produces showy, daisy-like flowers with yellow centers and purple-blue ray florets, blooming from late summer to early fall. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and natural landscapes.

Aster heliopsis is also valued for its ecological benefits, providing nectar for pollinators like bees and butterflies.
