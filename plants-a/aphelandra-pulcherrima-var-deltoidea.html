Aphelandra pulcherrima var. deltoidea

Aphelandra pulcherrima var. deltoidea, commonly known as the Delta Shieldvine, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Central and South America.

It features elongated, lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and often variegated with shades of green and white. The plant produces striking, pendant-like flowers that are typically yellow with red or orange markings, attracting pollinators such as hummingbirds and bees. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and well-draining soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical landscapes.
