Astilbe austrosinensis

Astilbe austrosinensis, commonly known as the Chinese astilbe, is a herbaceous perennial native to China.

It features delicate, plume-like inflorescences that range in color from white to pink and purple, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has lance-shaped, dark green leaves that are arranged in a basal rosette. It thrives in moist, shady environments and prefers well-drained, fertile soils.

Astilbe austrosinensis is valued in gardens for its ornamental foliage and subtle floral display.
