Astrocaryum perangustatum

Astrocaryum perangustatum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved buriti, is a species of palm native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its slender, elongated leaves that are typically arranged in a fan shape. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by edible fruits that are dark purple when mature. Its trunk is often segmented and can reach significant heights, making it a notable component of the forest canopy.

The species is valued for its ecological role and has been used by local communities for various traditional purposes.
