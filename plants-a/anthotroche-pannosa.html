Anthotroche pannosa

Anthotroche pannosa, commonly known as the velvetleaf.

It is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas. The plant is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine, velvety texture. It produces small, yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences.

The species is often found in disturbed habitats and is known for its rapid growth and adaptability.
