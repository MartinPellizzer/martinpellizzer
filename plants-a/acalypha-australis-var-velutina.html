Acalypha australis var. velutina

Acalypha australis var. velutina, commonly known as velvetleaf acalypha, is a shrub native to Australia.

It features palmate leaves with a velvety texture, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
