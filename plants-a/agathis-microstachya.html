Agathis microstachya

Agathis microstachya, commonly known as the narrow-stalked agar.

It is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia, characterized by its tall, straight trunk and large, glossy, elliptical leaves. The plant produces small, clustered yellow flowers followed by woody, cone-like fruits containing aromatic seeds. It is valued for its durable, heavy timber used in construction and furniture.

The species is also noted for its ecological importance in providing habitat for various wildlife species.
