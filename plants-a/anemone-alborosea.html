Anemone alborosea

Anemone alborosea, commonly known as the white anemone, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of central Asia.

It features showy, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring, typically from April to May. The plant has basal leaves that are heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs, and its stems are usually unbranched and somewhat hairy. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Anemone alborosea is often found in alpine meadows and is valued for its ornamental beauty in garden settings.
