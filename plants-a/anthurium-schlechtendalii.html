Anthurium schlechtendalii

Anthurium schlechtendalii, commonly known as the Schlechtendal's anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are often a deep green with prominent veins. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-shaped inflorescences surrounded by colorful, waxy spatula-like bracts. It thrives in humid, warm environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium schlechtendalii is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
