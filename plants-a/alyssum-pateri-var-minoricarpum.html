Alyssum pateri var. minoricarpum

Alyssum pateri var. minoricarpum, commonly known as the dwarf alyssum, is a low-growing perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features dense clusters of small, fragrant white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has finely divided, needle-like leaves that give it a delicate appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Its compact size and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
