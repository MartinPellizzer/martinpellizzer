Abelia dielsii

Abelia dielsii, commonly known as the Chinese abelia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features attractive, fragrant, tubular flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are usually dark green and have a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained soil.

Abelia dielsii is valued in gardens for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
