Alchemilla rutenbergii var. iratsyensis

Alchemilla rutenbergii var. iratsyensis, commonly known as the Madagascar rockfoil, is a low-growing perennial plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit. The plant features small, bright green leaves that are covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. It produces yellowish-white flowers in late spring, which are clustered in umbels.

This species is well adapted to rocky and arid environments, making it a resilient and hardy plant.
