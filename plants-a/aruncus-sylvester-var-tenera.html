Aruncus sylvester var. tenera

Aruncus sylvester var. tenera, commonly known as the soft-stemmed goat's beard, is a herbaceous perennial native to eastern North America.

It features tall, arching clusters of fluffy, white to pale pink bracts that resemble a soft, feathery plume. The plant has smooth, hollow stems and lance-shaped, serrated leaves that are often bluish-green in color. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodlands and stream banks.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and is cultivated for its unique, showy inflorescences.
