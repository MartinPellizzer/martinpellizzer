Athyrium kanghsienense

Athyrium kanghsienense, commonly known as the Kanghsi fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to China.

It belongs to the family Athyriaceae and is characterized by its delicate, lace-like fronds that are typically bi- or tri-pinnate. The plant thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories. Its rhizomes are creeping and produce numerous spore-bearing leaves.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens.
