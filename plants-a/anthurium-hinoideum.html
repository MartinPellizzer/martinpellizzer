Anthurium hinoideum

Anthurium hinoideum, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a striking tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces vibrant red spathes that enclose the spadix, a central inflorescence that bears small, inconspicuous flowers. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium hinoideum is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and long-lasting blooms.
