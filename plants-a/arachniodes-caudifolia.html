Arachniodes caudifolia

Arachniodes caudifolia, commonly known as the long-tailed arachniodes, is a striking evergreen fern native to Southeast Asia.

It features elegant, arching fronds that can grow up to 2 meters in length, with a distinctive silvery-white underside. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and prefers well-draining, acidic soil. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

It is also valued for its air-purifying qualities and ability to thrive in indoor conditions.
