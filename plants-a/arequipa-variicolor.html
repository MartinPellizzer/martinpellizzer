Arequipa variicolor

Arequipa variicolor, commonly known as the Andean rainbow orchid, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Andes.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, colorful blooms that display a variety of hues. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, where it thrives in cool, moist conditions. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, while its flowers are often clustered in inflorescences.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists.
