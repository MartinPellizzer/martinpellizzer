Armeria pseudarmeria subsp. villosa

Armeria pseudarmeria subsp.

villosa, commonly known as the hairy thrift, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region. It features dense clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in early summer.

The plant has silver-gray foliage covered in fine, soft hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in coastal and rocky habitats.

Its hardy nature makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscaping.
