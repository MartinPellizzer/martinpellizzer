Ajax grandiflorus

Ajax grandiflorus, commonly known as the large-flowered ajax, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that bloom in shades of yellow and orange. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, bract-like structures around its flower heads. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
