Amesia setschuanica

Amesia setschuanica, commonly known as Chinese daisy, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, daisy-like flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight. Its foliage is usually gray-green, with hairy leaves that help reduce water loss.

Amesia setschuanica is often used in ornamental gardening for its delicate appearance and ability to thrive in a variety of conditions.
