Asplenium erectum var. acuminatum

Asplenium erectum var. acuminatum, commonly known as the acuminated spleenwort, is a fern species belonging to the family Aspleniaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, upright fronds that are typically dark green and covered in small, rounded teeth along the margins. The plant produces sporangia on the underside of its leaves, which are clustered in groups called sori. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found growing on rocks or tree trunks in temperate regions.

This variety is notable for its compact growth habit and is often used in horticultural settings for its ornamental appeal.
