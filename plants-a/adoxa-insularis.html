Adoxa insularis

Adoxa insularis, commonly known as the island adoxa, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually pink or white in color. Its leaves are heart-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.

Adoxa insularis thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.
