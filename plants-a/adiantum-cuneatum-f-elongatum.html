Adiantum cuneatum f. elongatum

Adiantum cuneatum f. elongatum, commonly known as the elongated maidenhair fern, is a varietal form of the maidenhair fern genus.

It is characterized by its slender, lance-shaped fronds that are longer than those of the typical species. The plant features delicate, finely divided leaves that are typically dark green and have a glossy appearance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in temperate regions.

This fern is prized for its ornamental value and is commonly cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
