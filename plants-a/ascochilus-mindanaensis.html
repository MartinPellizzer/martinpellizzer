Ascochilus mindanaensis

Ascochilus mindanaensis, commonly known as the Mindanao ascochilus, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its intricate, showy flowers. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its roots are adapted to absorb nutrients from the acidic soil of its native habitat.

Ascochilus mindanaensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
