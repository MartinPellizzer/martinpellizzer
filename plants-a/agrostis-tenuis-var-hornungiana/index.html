Agrostis Tenuis Var. Hornungiana

Agrostis tenuis var. hornungiana is a rare and delicate grass species known for its fine, slender blades and pale green color. Native to specific regions in Europe, it thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. This variant is often overlooked due to its subtle appearance but holds ecological significance for local wildlife. Its limited distribution makes it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists. Protecting its habitat is crucial for preserving this unique plant.
