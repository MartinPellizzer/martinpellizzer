Arundinella smaragdina

Arundinella smaragdina, commonly known as the emerald bamboo, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family Poaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Malaysia and Indonesia. Its leaves are long and narrow, with a vibrant green color that contributes to its common name.

Arundinella smaragdina thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forests.
