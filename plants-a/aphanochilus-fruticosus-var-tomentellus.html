Aphanochilus fruticosus var. tomentellus

It is a terrestrial orchid that lacks chlorophyll and relies entirely on mycorrhizal fungi for nutrient uptake. The plant produces small, pale green or brownish flowers that are often overlooked due to their inconspicuous appearance. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is typically found in cloud forests or high-altitude grasslands.
