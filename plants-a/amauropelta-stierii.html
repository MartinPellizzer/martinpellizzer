Amauropelta stierii

Amauropelta stierii, commonly known as the Stier's shield fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Dryopteridaceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive, shield-shaped fronds that are typically divided into a series of pinnae. Native to tropical and subtropical regions, this plant thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky outcrops. The fronds are usually evergreen, with a leathery texture and a dark green coloration.

Its reproductive structures, including sori, are located on the underside of the fronds, typical of many fern species.
