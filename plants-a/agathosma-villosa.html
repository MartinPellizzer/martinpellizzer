Agathosma villosa

Agathosma villosa, commonly known as the Cape star or African star, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, star-shaped yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has hairy, grey-green leaves and thrives in well-drained, sandy soils. It is often used in rock gardens and as a ground cover due to its compact growth habit.

Agathosma villosa is also valued for its aromatic properties and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
