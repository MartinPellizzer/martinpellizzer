Athyrium vidalii var. amabile

Athyrium vidalii var. amabile, commonly known as the pink fern, is a striking ornamental plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Athyriaceae and is characterized by its delicate, bipinnate fronds that display a pale pink hue on the underside. The plant thrives in shaded, moist environments and prefers well-drained, acidic soil. Its unique coloration makes it a popular choice for shaded garden areas and indoor cultivation.

It is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of care in suitable conditions.
