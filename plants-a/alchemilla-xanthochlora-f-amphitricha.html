Alchemilla xanthochlora f. amphitricha

Alchemilla xanthochlora f. amphitricha, commonly known as the yellow tansy, is a flowering plant belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its low-growing habit and dense clusters of yellowish-green flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant features finely divided, feather-like leaves that are covered in a soft, whitish down. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland edges or shaded gardens.

This ornamental plant is valued for its attractive foliage and ability to attract pollinators.
