Anthurium binotii

Anthurium binotii, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with prominent veins. The plant produces vibrant red spathes that enclose a spadix, which is the actual flowering structure. Anthurium binotii thrives in humid, warm environments with indirect light.

Its unique foliage and colorful inflorescence make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
