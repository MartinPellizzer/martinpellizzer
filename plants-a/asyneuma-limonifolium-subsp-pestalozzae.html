Asyneuma limonifolium subsp. pestalozzae

Asyneuma limonifolium subsp.

pestalozzae, commonly known as the lemon-leaf asyneuma, is a flowering plant native to South America. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads.
