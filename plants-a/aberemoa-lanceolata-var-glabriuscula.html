Aberemoa lanceolata var. glabriuscula

Aberemoa lanceolata var. glabriuscula, commonly known as the smoothleaf aberemoa, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves that are smooth and often glossy in appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
