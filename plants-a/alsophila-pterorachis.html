Alsophila pterorachis

Alsophila pterorachis, commonly known as the slender tree fern, is a species native to tropical and subtropical regions of the world.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and feathery fronds. The plant has a distinctive, unbranched stem that is covered with persistent, fibrous roots. Its fronds are typically bipinnate, with a light green color and a delicate, airy appearance.

Alsophila pterorachis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and can thrive in shaded, moist environments.
