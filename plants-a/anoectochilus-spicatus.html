Anoectochilus spicatus

Anoectochilus spicatus, commonly known as the spicate helmet orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, hairy rhizomes and its distinctive, spurred flowers that are typically violet or purple in color. The plant thrives in shaded, humid environments, often growing in forest understories. Its leaves are elongated and strap-like, and it produces small, clustered inflorescences.

Anoectochilus spicatus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
