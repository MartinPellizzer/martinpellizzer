Ayenia riedeliana

Ayenia riedeliana, commonly known as the Riedel's ayenia, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly found in Argentina and Chile.

Ayenia riedeliana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate blooms.
