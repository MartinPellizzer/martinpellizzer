Albizia julibrissin f. tianshuiensis

Albizia julibrissin f. tianshuiensis, commonly known as the Tianshui silk tree, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its feathery, bipinnate leaves and showy, fluffy pink or white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant is native to China and is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its delicate appearance.
