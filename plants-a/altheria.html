Altheria

Altheria, commonly known as the silverleaf tree, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its striking silver-gray foliage, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. Altheria is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.

Its leaves are sometimes used as a source of natural dye.
