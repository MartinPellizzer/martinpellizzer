Anisomeris estrellana

Anisomeris estrellana, commonly known as the Estrella daisy, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of ray and disk florets. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun, making it well adapted to arid environments. Its foliage is gray-green with a fuzzy texture, and it produces showy yellow to orange flowers.

Anisomeris estrellana is often used in xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and ornamental value.
