Asyneuma rigidum subsp. rigidum

Asyneuma rigidum subsp.

rigidum, commonly known as rigid false dandelion, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in open, disturbed areas such as roadsides and grasslands.

The plant features upright, hairy stems and basal leaves with a rough, textured surface. Its yellow flowers are borne in clusters and bloom in the spring.

It has a fibrous root system and is adapted to thrive in well-drained soils.
