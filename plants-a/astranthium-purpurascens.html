Astranthium purpurascens

Astranthium purpurascens, commonly known as the red-flowered starwort, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features bright red or purple flowers that are often arranged in a star-like pattern, giving it its common name. Its leaves are typically narrow and slightly hairy, with a basal rosette growth habit.

Astranthium purpurascens is often found in dry, sunny habitats such as rocky soils and coastal areas.
