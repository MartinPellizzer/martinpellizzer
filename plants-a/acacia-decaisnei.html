Acacia decaisnei

Acacia decaisnei, commonly known as the Decaisne's acacia, is a shrub native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, often spiny stems and bipinnate leaves composed of numerous small, oval leaflets. The plant produces yellow, spherical flowers that are clustered in dense, globular inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in savanna and woodland habitats.

Acacia decaisnei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
