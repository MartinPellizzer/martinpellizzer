Alocasia acuminata

Alocasia acuminata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved arrowroot, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length. The plant produces striking, spadix-like flowers that are typically green or yellow in color. Alocasia acuminata thrives in humid, shady environments and requires well-draining, fertile soil.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its dramatic foliage.
