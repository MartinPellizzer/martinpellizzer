Alsine tetraquetra

Alsine tetraquetra, commonly known as the four-angled alsike, is a flowering plant native to temperate regions of Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Amaranthaceae and is characterized by its upright, branched stems and small, inconspicuous flowers.
