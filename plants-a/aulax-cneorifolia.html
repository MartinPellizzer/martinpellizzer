Aulax cneorifolia

Aulax cneorifolia, commonly known as the Kashmir rose or Indian rose, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayas.

It is a shrub that typically grows in mountainous regions with cool climates. The plant produces small, pink to white flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a slightly serrated edge.

The plant is valued for its ornamental beauty and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
