Achimenes × rinzii

Achimenes × rinzii, commonly known as the hybrid begonia, is a flowering plant belonging to the Begoniaceae family.

It is a hybrid between Achimenes and Rynia, resulting in a plant with unique ornamental features. The species is characterized by its vibrant, showy bracts that come in a variety of colors, often resembling flowers. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated as a decorative plant in gardens and greenhouses.

Its foliage is typically glossy and variegated, adding to its aesthetic appeal.
