Aralia dumetorum

Aralia dumetorum, commonly known as the shrub aralia, is a herbaceous plant native to eastern North America.

It typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, featuring compound leaves with a feathery appearance. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers in umbel-like clusters, which are followed by red, berry-like fruits. Its root system is fibrous, and it is often found in moist, wooded areas.

The plant has been used in traditional herbal medicine for its purported medicinal properties.
