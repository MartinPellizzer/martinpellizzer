Aeonium korneliuslemsii

Aeonium korneliuslemsii, commonly known as the Kornelius' Aeonium, is a succulent plant native to the Canary Islands.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with red or purple edges, especially when exposed to sunlight. The plant produces striking clusters of yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a container plant.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
