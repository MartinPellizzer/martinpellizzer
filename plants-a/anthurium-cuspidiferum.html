Anthurium cuspidiferum

Anthurium cuspidiferum, commonly known as the spathiflorum anthurium, is a member of the Araceae family and is native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped spathe that surrounds a spadix, which is typically green or yellow in color. The plant produces aerial roots and thrives in humid, warm environments with bright, indirect light. Its foliage consists of large, glossy, dark green leaves with prominent veins.

Anthurium cuspidiferum is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
