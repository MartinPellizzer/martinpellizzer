Artanthe augusta

Artanthe augusta, commonly known as the silverleaf artanthe, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and upright growth habit. The plant produces small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Artanthe augusta is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
