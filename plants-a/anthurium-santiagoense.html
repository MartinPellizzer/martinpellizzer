Anthurium santiagoense

Anthurium santiagoense, commonly known as the Santiago anthurium, is a member of the Araceae family.

It is native to the humid lowland forests of central Mexico. The plant is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 centimeters in length. Its striking spadix and spathe make it a popular ornamental plant.

It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and high humidity for optimal growth.
