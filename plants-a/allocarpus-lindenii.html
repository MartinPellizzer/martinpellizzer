Allocarpus lindenii

Allocarpus lindenii, commonly known as the African sandthorn, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its small, yellow flowers and distinctive, spine-tipped leaves. The plant produces small, round fruits that are typically green when mature. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in sandy or rocky soils.

The botanical features of Allocarpus lindenii make it well adapted to survive in harsh, dry conditions.
