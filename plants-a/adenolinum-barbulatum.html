Adenolinum barbulatum

Adenolinum barbulatum, commonly known as the feather-tailed cycad, is a unique plant belonging to the Cycadaceae family.

It is native to parts of Southeast Asia, particularly Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant features a short, stout trunk and a crown of large, feather-like leaves that give it its common name. Its reproductive structures include a large, cone-like strobilus that produces both male and female spores.

Adenolinum barbulatum is characterized by its slow growth and ability to thrive in well-drained, sandy soils.
