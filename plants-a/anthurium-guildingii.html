Anthurium guildingii

Anthurium guildingii, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically deep green with a reddish tint. The plant produces vibrant red spathes that enclose its yellow spadix, creating a visually appealing flower structure. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Anthurium guildingii is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
