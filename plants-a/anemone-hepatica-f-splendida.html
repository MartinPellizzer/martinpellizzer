Anemone hepatica f. splendida

Anemone hepatica f. splendida, commonly known as the splendour hepatica, is a striking variant of the hepatica plant.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range from white to pale pink, often with a yellow center. The plant features heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and slightly hairy. It thrives in woodland environments with moist, well-drained soil.

This cultivar is valued for its early spring bloom and is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental appeal.
