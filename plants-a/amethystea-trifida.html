Amethystea trifida

Amethystea trifida, commonly known as the purple flame plant, is a striking member of the Acanthaceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant purple and pink inflorescences, which resemble flames, and its trifoliate leaves that are often variegated with white or cream. Native to tropical regions, it thrives in warm, humid climates with well-draining soil. The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for those seeking a bold and colorful addition to their botanical collection.
