Athyrium niponicum var. pachyphlebium

Athyrium niponicum var. pachyphlebium, commonly known as the Japanese painted fern, is a striking fern native to Japan and Korea.

It features elegant, bipinnate fronds with a distinctive dark green to black central rib and lighter green segments, giving it a painted appearance. The plant thrives in moist, shady environments and prefers well-drained, acidic soil. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for adding texture and visual interest to shaded landscapes.
