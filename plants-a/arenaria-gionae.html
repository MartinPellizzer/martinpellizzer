Arenaria gionae

Arenaria gionae, commonly known as the Gionae sandwort, is a flowering plant native to the rocky and alpine regions of southern Europe.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit. The plant produces small, white or pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are typically linear and covered in fine hairs, adapted to withstand harsh environmental conditions.

Arenaria gionae is often found in dry, sunny habitats such as rocky slopes and gravelly soils.
