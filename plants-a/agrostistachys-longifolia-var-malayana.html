Agrostistachys longifolia var. malayana

Agrostistachys longifolia var. malayana, commonly known as the Malay arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in wet, shaded environments such as swamps and marshes. The plant features slender, arrow-shaped leaves that are often submerged or floating, adapted for aquatic conditions. Its flowers are small and clustered, usually greenish-white in color, and it produces small, oval fruits.

This variant is distinguished by its elongated leaves and specific habitat preferences compared to other members of the genus.
