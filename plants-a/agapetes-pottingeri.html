Agapetes pottingeri

Agapetes pottingeri, commonly known as the silverleaf agapete, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit. Agapetes pottingeri is adapted to cool, moist environments and is often found in cloud forests.
