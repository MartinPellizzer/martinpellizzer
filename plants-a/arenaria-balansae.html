Arenaria balansae

Arenaria balansae, commonly known as the Balansae sandwort, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial that thrives in rocky and arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. The plant produces small, white or pink flowers during the spring and summer months, adding a delicate touch to its natural habitat. Its leaves are typically small and fleshy, which helps it conserve water in its dry surroundings.

Arenaria balansae is often found in alpine meadows and scree slopes, where it plays a role in stabilizing soil and supporting local biodiversity.
