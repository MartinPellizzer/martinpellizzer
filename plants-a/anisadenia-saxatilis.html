Anisadenia saxatilis

Anisadenia saxatilis, commonly known as the rock anisadenia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen shrub form with small, glossy leaves. The plant produces clusters of pale yellow to white, tubular flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Anisadenia saxatilis is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and delicate flowers.
