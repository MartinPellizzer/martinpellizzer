Artemisia herba-alba var. densiflora

Artemisia herba-alba var. densiflora, commonly known as white wormwood, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its gray-green foliage and compact, dense growth habit. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flower clusters that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for arid and semi-arid environments.

This species is often used in herbal medicine and as a ornamental plant in xeriscaping.
