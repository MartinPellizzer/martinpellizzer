Anotis

Anotis, commonly known as the velvet leaf or African daisy, is a genus of flowering plants in the Asteraceae family.

It is native to southern Africa and features showy, daisy-like flowers with yellow centers and white or pink petals. The leaves are typically velvety and hairy, giving the plant its common name. Anotis species are often found in rocky or sandy soils and thrive in full sun.

They are popular in gardens for their ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
