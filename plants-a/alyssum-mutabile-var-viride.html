Alyssum mutabile var. viride

Alyssum mutabile var. viride, commonly known as green alpine alyssum, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It features dense clusters of small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has silver-gray foliage with a slightly fuzzy texture, and its stems are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and alpine settings.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract beneficial insects.
