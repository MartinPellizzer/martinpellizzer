Aloe albostriata

Aloe albostriata, commonly known as the white-striped aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color with distinct white or pale green stripes. The plant produces tall, branching inflorescences with yellow-orange flowers that bloom in the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Aloe albostriata is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable growing conditions.
