Arisaema jinshajiangense

Arisaema jinshajiangense, commonly known as the Jinshajiang arrowhead, is a rare and endangered plant species native to China.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its distinctive arrowhead-shaped leaves and spadix inflorescence. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as mountainous regions and forest understories. Its botanical features include a short rhizome, heart-shaped leaves, and a unique floral structure with a spathe.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is of significant interest in botanical research and conservation efforts.
