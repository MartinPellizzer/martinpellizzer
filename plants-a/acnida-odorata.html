Acnida odorata

Acnida odorata, commonly known as the fragrant acacia, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa and the Arabian Peninsula.

It is characterized by its small, clustered yellow flowers and slender, woody stems. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. Acnida odorata is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid conditions.

Its aromatic leaves and flowers make it a notable species in its native ecosystems.
