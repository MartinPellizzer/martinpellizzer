Anthurium carrionii

Anthurium carrionii, commonly known as the Carrion's arrowhead, is a striking tropical plant native to Colombia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces vibrant spadices, which are the modified leaves that house the actual flowers. Anthurium carrionii thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Its unique appearance and adaptability make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
