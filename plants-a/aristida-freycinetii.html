Aristida freycinetii

Aristida freycinetii, commonly known as the Freycinet's grass, is a perennial grass species native to Australia.

It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and woodlands. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It is characterized by its ability to thrive in arid conditions and its resilience to drought.

This grass is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its hardy nature and low maintenance requirements.
