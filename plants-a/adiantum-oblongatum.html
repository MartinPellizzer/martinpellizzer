Adiantum oblongatum

Adiantum oblongatum, commonly known as the maidenhair fern, is a delicate and elegant fern species native to parts of Asia, including China and Japan.

It is characterized by its finely divided, arching fronds that give it a graceful appearance. The plant features small, bright green pinnae with a distinctive, narrow shape. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and prefers well-drained, humus-rich soil.

Adiantum oblongatum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in a home setting.
