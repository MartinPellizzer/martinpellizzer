Anemone narcissiflora f. prostrata

Anemone narcissiflora f. prostrata, commonly known as the prostrate narcissus anemone, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features broad, waxy, dark green leaves that form a dense mat on the ground. The plant produces showy, cup-shaped flowers in shades of white to pale yellow, resembling daisies. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a ground cover.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to tolerate drought conditions.
