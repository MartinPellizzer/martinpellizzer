Allium canadense subsp. ecristatum

Allium canadense subsp.

ecristatum, commonly known as the Canada bunchgrass or prairie onion, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to North America. It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its dense, grass-like foliage and small, clustered flowers.
