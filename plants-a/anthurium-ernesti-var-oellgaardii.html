Anthurium ernesti var. oellgaardii

Anthurium ernesti var. oellgaardii, commonly known as the Oellgaard's anthurium, is a member of the Araceae family and is native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are often dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-shaped inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, typically in shades of red, orange, or yellow. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is popular among collectors for its ornamental value and unique foliage.
