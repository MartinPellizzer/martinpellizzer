Astrantia major subsp. minor

Astrantia major subsp.

minor, commonly known as small meadow astrantia, is a herbaceous perennial native to Europe. It features upright, branched stems that bear clusters of small, white to pinkish flowers.

The plant has palmate, lobed leaves that are typically hairy and have a rough texture. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

This species is valued in gardens for its ornamental foliage and delicate floral display.
