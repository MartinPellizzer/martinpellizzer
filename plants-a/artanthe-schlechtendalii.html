Artanthe schlechtendalii

Artanthe schlechtendalii, commonly known as the Schlechtendal's artanthe, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features striking, violet-blue to purple flowers that bloom in dense, showy clusters during the summer months. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with a silvery underside, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long flowering period and drought tolerance.
