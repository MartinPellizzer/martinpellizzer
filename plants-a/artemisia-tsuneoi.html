Artemisia tsuneoi

Artemisia tsuneoi, commonly known as Japanese wormwood, is a perennial shrub native to Japan.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, silver-gray foliage. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in late summer, attracting various pollinators. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or mountainous regions.

Artemisia tsuneoi is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
