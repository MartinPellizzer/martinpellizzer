Aspidistra Luteorubra

Aspidistra luteorubra is a striking plant known for its vibrant orange and red foliage. Native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia, it thrives in humid, shaded environments. This evergreen perennial is prized for its ornamental value and ease of care. Its unique leaf patterns make it a popular choice for indoor gardening. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively rare in cultivation.
