Astartea montana

Astartea montana, commonly known as the mountain aster, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads and medicinal properties. The plant typically grows in rocky or open habitats, thriving in well-drained soils. Its showy purple or white flowers bloom in the summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Astartea montana is valued for its ornamental beauty and has been used in traditional herbal medicine for its purported health benefits.
