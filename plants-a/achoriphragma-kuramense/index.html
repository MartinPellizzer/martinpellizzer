Achoriphragma Kuramense

Achoriphragma kuramense is a rare and enigmatic plant species native to the highlands of Ethiopia. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, known for its diverse and ecologically significant members. This plant is characterized by its unique morphology and adaptation to its harsh, high-altitude environment. Due to its limited distribution and fragile habitat, it faces significant threats from climate change and human encroachment. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this rare botanical treasure for future generations.
