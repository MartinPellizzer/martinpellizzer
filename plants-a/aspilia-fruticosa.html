Aspilia fruticosa

Aspilia fruticosa, commonly known as the red torch or red torch lily, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces striking red, torch-like inflorescences that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a rosette at the base.

Aspilia fruticosa is known for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant flowers.
