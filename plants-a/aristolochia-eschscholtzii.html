Aristolochia eschscholtzii

Aristolochia eschscholtzii, commonly known as the California pipevine, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a perennial vine that produces distinctive, tubular flowers with a deep purple or maroon color. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and is known for its strong, aromatic scent. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or streamside habitats.

The plant is also notable for its role in supporting the life cycle of the pipevine swallowtail butterfly.
