Asplenium dianae

Asplenium dianae, commonly known as the Dianae's spleenwort, is a small, epiphytic fern belonging to the family Aspleniaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly found in the Philippines and Indonesia. The plant has a compact rosette of lance-shaped leaves that are usually dark green and have a leathery texture. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing on tree trunks or rocks.

Asplenium dianae is notable for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant.
