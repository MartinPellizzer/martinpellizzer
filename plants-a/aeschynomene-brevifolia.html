Aeschynomene brevifolia

Aeschynomene brevifolia, commonly known as smallleaf aeschynomene, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas.

The plant features trifoliate leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces small, yellowish-white flowers arranged in clusters. Its seeds are encased in pods that split open when mature, dispersing the seeds by wind.
