Aster pubentior

Aster pubentior, commonly known as the pubescent aster, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands. Its leaves are covered in fine hairs, giving it a fuzzy appearance, and its flowers bloom in shades of purple and blue.

Aster pubentior is often found in the eastern United States and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
