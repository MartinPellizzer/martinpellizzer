Astragalus sogdianus

Astragalus sogdianus, commonly known as the Sogdian milkvetch, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to Central Asia, particularly found in regions such as Afghanistan, Tajikistan, and Pakistan. The plant typically grows in arid and semi-arid environments, often in rocky or sandy soils. It produces small, yellow or pale green flowers arranged in dense clusters, and its leaves are compound with small leaflets.

Astragalus sogdianus is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional medicine for various health benefits.
