Anil tenuifolia

Anil tenuifolia, commonly known as slender anil, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It features slender, linear leaves that are often arranged alternately along its stem. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish or white in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or grassland habitats.

Anil tenuifolia is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
