Arctostaphylos pechoensis var. cruzensis

Arctostaphylos pechoensis var. cruzensis, commonly known as the Cruzensis Manzanita, is a shrub native to the western United States, specifically found in California.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes many evergreen shrubs and small trees. The plant is characterized by its dense, gnarled growth habit and small, leathery leaves that are typically evergreen. This species is adapted to rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in chaparral and coastal scrub habitats.
