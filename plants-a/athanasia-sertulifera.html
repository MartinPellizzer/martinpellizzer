Athanasia sertulifera

Athanasia sertulifera, commonly known as the golden crownbeard, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features showy yellow flowers arranged in dense, circular inflorescences, giving it its common name. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Athanasia sertulifera is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
