Artemisia elongata

Artemisia elongata, commonly known as slender wormwood, is a perennial herb native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its aromatic, silver-gray foliage and slender, branched stems. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in sagebrush steppe ecosystems.

Artemisia elongata is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
