Ardisia perpendicularis var. balansana

Ardisia perpendicularis var. balansana, commonly known as the Balansan ardisia, is a species native to the Philippines.

It is a small evergreen shrub characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of small, white flowers. The plant produces bright red berries that are an important food source for local wildlife. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forest understories.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for horticultural cultivation in tropical regions.
