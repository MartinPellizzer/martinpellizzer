Alsodeia echinocarpa var. nervosa

Alsodeia echinocarpa var. nervosa, commonly known as the hairy alsodeia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves with prominent venation. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters. It thrives in dry, rocky environments and is often found in coastal regions.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural study.
