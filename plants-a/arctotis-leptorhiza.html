Arctotis leptorhiza

Arctotis leptorhiza, commonly known as the narrowleaf arctotis, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces bright yellow to orange flowers in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant blooms and compact growth habit.
