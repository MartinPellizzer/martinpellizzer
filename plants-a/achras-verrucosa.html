Achras verrucosa

Achras verrucosa, commonly known as the cannonball tree, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, round, yellowish fruit that resembles a cannonball, which is covered in a thick, spiny husk. The plant has palm-like leaves and produces showy, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruit is typically harvested when it is fully mature and has developed a hard, woody texture.

The plant is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its striking appearance and cultural significance in various regions.
