Arisaema listeri

Arisaema listeri, commonly known as the Lister's arisaema, is a member of the Araceae family.

It is native to parts of East Asia, including China, Korea, and Japan. The plant produces a striking inflorescence consisting of a spadix surrounded by a modified leaf called a spathe. Its foliage consists of large, heart-shaped leaves that are often deeply lobed.

Arisaema listeri is typically found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks.
