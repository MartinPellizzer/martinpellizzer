Arctotis namaquana

Arctotis namaquana, commonly known as the Namibian arctotis, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows in arid and semi-arid regions. The plant features hairy, lance-shaped leaves and produces showy, daisy-like yellow flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun exposure.

Arctotis namaquana is often used in xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and ornamental value.
