Acaena elongata subvar. compacta

Acaena elongata subvar. compacta, commonly known as the dense mat-forming acaena, is a low-growing shrub native to New Zealand.

It features dense clusters of small, spiny leaves and produces yellowish-white flowers that bloom in spring. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit, which forms a mat-like structure on the ground. Its fruits are small, hairy achenes that are dispersed by wind, aiding in its propagation.

This species is often found in coastal and subalpine regions, where it thrives in well-drained soils.
