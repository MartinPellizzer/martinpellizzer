Albizia gummifera var. gummifera

Albizia gummifera var. gummifera, commonly known as the gum tree or silky gum, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and Australia. The plant is characterized by its smooth, pale bark and trifoliate leaves with a silvery underside. It produces yellowish-white flowers in dense spikes, which are followed by elongated, woody pods.

The tree is valued for its hard, durable wood and its ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
