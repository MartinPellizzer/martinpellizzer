Ascolepis protea var. floribunda

Ascolepis protea var. floribunda, commonly known as the yellow star orchid, is a South African plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers with a star-shaped pattern, which bloom in late summer to early autumn. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun exposure. Its inflorescence consists of a raceme of fragrant, tubular flowers that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
