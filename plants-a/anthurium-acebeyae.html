Anthurium acebeyae

Anthurium acebeyae, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a reddish tinge. The plant produces vibrant red spadices, which are the showy parts that enclose the actual flowers. Anthurium acebeyae thrives in high humidity and indirect light, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its ornamental value is further enhanced by its ability to bloom throughout the year under proper care.
