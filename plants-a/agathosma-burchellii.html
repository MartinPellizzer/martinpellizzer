Agathosma burchellii

Agathosma burchellii, commonly known as buchu, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its aromatic, pungent leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and has a creeping or bushy growth habit. Its leaves are pinnately lobed and emit a strong, distinctive odor when crushed.

Agathosma burchellii is valued for its medicinal properties and is also used as a spice in certain cuisines.
