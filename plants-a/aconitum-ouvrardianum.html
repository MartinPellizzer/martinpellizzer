Aconitum ouvrardianum

Aconitum ouvrardianum, commonly known as the Chinese aconite, is a member of the Ranunculaceae family and is native to China.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces striking blue to purple spathaceous flowers. The plant has dark green, palmately lobed leaves that are deeply divided. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Due to its toxic alkaloids, it should be handled with care.
