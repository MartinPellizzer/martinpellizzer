Astronidium samoense

Astronidium samoense, commonly known as the Samoan starfruit tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to the Pacific Islands.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae, which includes laurels and cinnamon trees. The plant is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruit is a yellow, star-shaped drupe that is edible and has a sweet, tangy flavor.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and for its unique fruit.
