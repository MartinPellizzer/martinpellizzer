Aglaia cuspidata

Aglaia cuspidata, commonly known as the crowned beauty or yellow bird of paradise, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Meliaceae and is characterized by its striking, bright yellow flowers that resemble a bird in flight. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are elliptical in shape and often have a slightly serrated edge. Its fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release its seeds.
