Anthurium bullosum

Anthurium bullosum, commonly known as the bubble anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, waxy, heart-shaped leaves that are often brightly colored, ranging from deep green to red or purple. The plant produces small, spadix-shaped flowers surrounded by a colorful spathe, which is a modified leaf. It thrives in humid, warm environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.

Anthurium bullosum is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
