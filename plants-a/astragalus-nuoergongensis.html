Astragalus nuoergongensis

Astragalus nuoergongensis, commonly known as the Nuoergong milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its erect stems and compound leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, which are typical of the genus Astragalus. It is often found in arid and semi-arid regions, adapting to harsh environmental conditions.

Due to its limited distribution and ecological significance, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
