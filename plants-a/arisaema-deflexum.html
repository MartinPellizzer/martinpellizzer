Arisaema deflexum

Arisaema deflexum, commonly known as the flexible arisaema, is a herbaceous plant native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive inflorescence consisting of a spadix surrounded by a spathe. The plant typically grows in moist, wooded areas and has a rhizomatous root system that allows it to spread vegetatively. Its leaves are arrow-shaped and often have a rough texture, while the flowers are small and inconspicuous.

Arisaema deflexum is known for its ability to thrive in shaded environments and is sometimes cultivated for its ornamental value.
