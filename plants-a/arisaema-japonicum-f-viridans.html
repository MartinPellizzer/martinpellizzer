Arisaema japonicum f. viridans

Arisaema japonicum f. viridans, commonly known as the green-flowered arisaema, is a variant of the Japanese arisaema species.

It is characterized by its distinctive, umbrella-shaped inflorescence with green spathes and yellow spadices. The plant is native to eastern Asia, including Japan, Korea, and parts of China. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky slopes.

The botanical features of this plant make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening due to its unique appearance and adaptability.
