Acanthus dioscoridis var. perringii

Acanthus dioscoridis var. perringii, commonly known as the Perring's spiny acanthus, is a rare and ornamental plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, spiny leaves with a distinctive silvery-blue underside. The plant produces showy, spire-like inflorescences that bear small, pale blue to purple flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
