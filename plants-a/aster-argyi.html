Aster argyi

Aster argyi, commonly known as the Chinese aster or frost flower, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its stiff, branched stems and opposite, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, daisy-like flowers that range in color from white to purple, depending on the variety. Aster argyi is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional Chinese medicine.

It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.
