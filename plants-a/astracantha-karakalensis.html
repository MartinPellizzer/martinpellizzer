Astracantha karakalensis

Astracantha karakalensis, commonly known as the Karakalinskaya Astracantha, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant produces small, yellow to orange flowers that bloom in the summer months. It typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions.

Its structure includes narrow, linear leaves and a compact, upright growth habit.
