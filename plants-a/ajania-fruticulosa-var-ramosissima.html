Ajania fruticulosa var. ramosissima

Ajania fruticulosa var. ramosissima, commonly known as the silverleaf ajania, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, silver-gray foliage that provides a striking visual contrast. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental appeal make it a popular choice for coastal and Mediterranean climates.
