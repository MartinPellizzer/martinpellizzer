Amaryllis blossfeldiae

Amaryllis blossfeldiae, commonly known as the Blossfeld's amaryllis, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, sturdy stems that bear clusters of large, showy flowers in shades of red, pink, and white. The foliage consists of broad, strap-like leaves that are typically green with a reddish tinge. This species is prized for its vibrant blooms and is often cultivated as a decorative plant.

It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.
