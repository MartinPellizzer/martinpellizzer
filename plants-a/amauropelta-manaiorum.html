Amauropelta manaiorum

Amauropelta manaiorum, commonly known as the Manaiorua fern, is a species of tree fern native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds. The plant has a distinctive crown of fronds that can reach up to 3 meters in height, giving it a unique and elegant appearance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The fronds are compound, with pinnae that are narrowly lanceolate and have a leathery texture.
