Ardisia cestrifolia

Ardisia cestrifolia, commonly known as the evergreen strawberry tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and small, red berries that resemble strawberries. The plant produces white to pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Ardisia cestrifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a decorative shrub.
