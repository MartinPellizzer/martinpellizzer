Allium rotundum subsp. multiflorum

Allium rotundum subsp.

multiflorum, commonly known as Chinese leek or Chinese chives, is a perennial herb native to China. It is characterized by its dense, grass-like foliage and clusters of small, white to pale purple flowers.

The plant produces bulbous structures that are often used in culinary applications. It thrives in temperate climates and requires well-drained soil.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for both ornamental and edible gardening.
