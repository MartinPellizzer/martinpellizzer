Anacampseros borderei

Anacampseros borderei, commonly known as the Cape Town stone plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, gray-green leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern, adapted for water storage. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the summer months, adding a delicate touch to its appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
