Aa lozanoi

Aa lozanoi, commonly known as the Zanzibar parasol tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the coastal regions of East Africa.

It belongs to the family Arecaceae, commonly known as the palm family, and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and a broad, umbrella-like crown of fronds. The plant produces large, showy inflorescences that bear small, fragrant flowers, which are followed by edible fruits. Its leaves are long, pinnately compound, and often used in traditional medicine and crafts.

Aa lozanoi thrives in humid, coastal climates and is often found growing near the shoreline.
