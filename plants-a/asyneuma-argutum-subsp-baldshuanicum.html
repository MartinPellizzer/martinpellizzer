Asyneuma argutum subsp. baldshuanicum

Asyneuma argutum subsp.

baldshuanicum, commonly known as the Baldshuan narrowleaf sunflower, is a flowering plant native to parts of China. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit.

The plant produces daisy-like yellow flowers with a central disk surrounded by ray florets. It typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid environments.

Its botanical features make it a notable species in the study of desert flora.
