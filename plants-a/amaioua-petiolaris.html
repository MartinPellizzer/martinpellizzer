Amaioua petiolaris

Amaioua petiolaris, commonly known as the leafy vine, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its climbing habit and elongated, heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white and green patterns. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by orange-red berries containing a single seed. Amaioua petiolaris thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in tropical climates.
