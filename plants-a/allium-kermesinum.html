Allium kermesinum

Allium kermesinum, commonly known as the Kermes onion, is a perennial bulbous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered white or pale pink flowers. The plant has a strong onion-like aroma and is often grown for its ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, preferring temperate climates.

Allium kermesinum is also used in some traditional culinary applications.
