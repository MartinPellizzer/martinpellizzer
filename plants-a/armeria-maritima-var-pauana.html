Armeria maritima var. pauana

Armeria maritima var. pauana, commonly known as the coastal thrift, is a flowering plant native to the Atlantic coastal regions of North America.

It is a low-growing, evergreen perennial that thrives in sandy, well-drained soils. The plant produces dense clusters of white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring. It is well adapted to maritime climates, tolerating salt spray and strong winds.

Its hardy nature makes it a popular choice for coastal gardens and erosion control.
