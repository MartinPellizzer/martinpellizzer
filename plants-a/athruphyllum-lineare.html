Athruphyllum lineare

Athruphyllum lineare, commonly known as the linear fern, is a species of fern native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its slender, linear fronds that grow in a rosette pattern. The plant typically thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories. Its leaves are simple and have a delicate, feathery appearance.

Athruphyllum lineare plays an important role in the ecological balance of its native habitat.
