Arracacia luxeana

Arracacia luxeana, commonly known as the Brazilian star apple, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Anacardiaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant produces a fruit that resembles a star in shape, hence its common name. The fruit has a sweet, aromatic pulp with a texture similar to a lychee.

It is valued not only for its edible fruit but also for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
