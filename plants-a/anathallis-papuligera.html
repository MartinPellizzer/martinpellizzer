Anathallis papuligera

Anathallis papuligera, commonly known as the papular anathallia, is a species of orchid native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its unique, hairy stems and leaves that are often covered in small, raised bumps. The plant produces striking, white to pale yellow flowers with a distinct, intricate structure. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, typically found in the understory of tropical forests.

Anathallis papuligera is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is known for its ornamental value and ecological significance in its native habitat.
