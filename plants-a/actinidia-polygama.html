Actinidia polygama

Actinidia polygama, commonly known as the Chinese mayapple, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the Actinidiaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and showy white flowers. The plant produces small, green berries that ripen to a bright orange-red color. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has historical uses in traditional medicine.

Its growth habit and foliage make it a popular choice for woodland gardens.
