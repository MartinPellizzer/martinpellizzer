Aphelandra porteana var. clava

Aphelandra porteana var. clava, commonly known as the golden torch plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to South America.

It features striking, elongated leaves that are glossy and dark green with a prominent midrib. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant, white flowers that resemble torches, giving it its common name. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and high humidity.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical landscapes.
