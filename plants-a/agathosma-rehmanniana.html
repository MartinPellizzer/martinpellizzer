Agathosma rehmanniana

Agathosma rehmanniana, commonly known as the Cape aster, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers and aromatic leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their antiseptic and anti-inflammatory properties.

Agathosma rehmanniana is also valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
