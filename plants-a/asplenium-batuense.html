Asplenium batuense

Asplenium batuense, commonly known as the Batu Rock Fern, is a terrestrial fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Aspleniaceae and is characterized by its compact, rhizomatous growth habit. The plant typically features lanceolate, serrated leaves that are dark green and often have a leathery texture. It thrives in shaded, moist environments, often found on rock surfaces or in dense forest understories.

Asplenium batuense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in shady garden settings.
