Alsophila conantiana

Alsophila conantiana, commonly known as the Conant's tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to Mexico.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds. The plant has a robust root system that helps it anchor in rocky or clay soils. Its spores are produced on the undersides of the fronds, contributing to its reproductive cycle.
