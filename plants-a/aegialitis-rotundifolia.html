Aegialitis rotundifolia

Aegialitis rotundifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf seagrass, is a marine flowering plant found in coastal and estuarine environments.

Its reproductive structures include small, inconspicuous flowers that are pollinated by water currents.

Aegialitis rotundifolia plays a vital role in stabilizing sediments and improving water quality in coastal ecosystems.
