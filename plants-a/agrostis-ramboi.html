Agrostis ramboi

Agrostis ramboi, commonly known as the Rambo's bentgrass, is a perennial grass species native to parts of Asia.

It typically grows in moist, temperate climates and is often found in meadows and along stream banks. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping.

Botanically, it belongs to the Poaceae family, which includes many other grasses.
