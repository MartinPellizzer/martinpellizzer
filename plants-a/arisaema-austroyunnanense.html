Arisaema austroyunnanense

Arisaema austroyunnanense, commonly known as the Yunnan arrowhead, is a member of the Araceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Yunnan, China, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant features a distinctive arrow-shaped leaf and a spadix surrounded by a spathe, typical of the genus Arisaema. Its botanical structure includes a rhizomatous root system and produces small, greenish-yellow flowers.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has traditional uses in local medicine.
