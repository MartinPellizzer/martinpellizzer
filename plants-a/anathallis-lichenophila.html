Anathallis lichenophila

Anathallis lichenophila, commonly known as the lichen-loving orchid, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its unique association with lichens, often growing in close proximity to them. The plant typically features slender, erect stems and small, greenish-white flowers. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in cloud forests.

Due to its specialized habitat and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
