Argyrolobium keniense

Argyrolobium keniense, commonly known as the Kenyan silver globular pea, is a flowering plant native to Kenya.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is characterized by its legume fruits and nitrogen-fixing abilities. The plant is notable for its spherical, silver-gray seed pods that contain several small, dark seeds. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky soils.

The species is of interest for its potential in ecological restoration and as a source of forage for livestock.
