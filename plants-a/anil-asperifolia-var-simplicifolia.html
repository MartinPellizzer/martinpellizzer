Anil asperifolia var. simplicifolia

Anil asperifolia var. simplicifolia, commonly known as the smoothleaf anil, is a species native to certain regions of South America.

It is a shrub characterized by its simple, lance-shaped leaves that are usually smooth on the upper surface. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
