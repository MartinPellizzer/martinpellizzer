Ascolepis anthemiflora

Ascolepis anthemiflora, commonly known as the Cape daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and has a compact, low-growing habit. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, while its flowers are bright yellow with a central disk.

It is well adapted to sandy soils and is often used in coastal landscaping due to its hardiness.
