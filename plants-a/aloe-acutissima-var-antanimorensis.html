Aloe acutissima var. antanimorensis

Aloe acutissima var. antanimorensis, commonly known as the Antanimoro aloe, is a succulent plant native to Madagascar.

It features spiky, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a bluish tint, and it produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow flowers. The plant is adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves to survive drought conditions.

It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its unique appearance and hardy nature.
