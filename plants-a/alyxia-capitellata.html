Alyxia capitellata

Alyxia capitellata, commonly known as the Florida holly, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a small shrub that typically grows in wetlands and swampy areas. The plant features glossy, dark green leaves and produces clusters of small, white flowers. Alyxia capitellata is part of the Solanaceae family, which includes nightshades.
