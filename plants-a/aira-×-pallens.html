Aira × pallens

Aira × pallens, commonly known as the Japanese grass or silver grass, is a hybrid plant belonging to the grass family Poaceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive silver-gray foliage and slender, upright growth habit. The plant produces small, pale green flowers in panicles during the summer months. Native to East Asia, it is often cultivated for ornamental purposes in gardens and landscapes.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for adding texture and visual interest to garden designs.
