Agathosma collina

Agathosma collina, commonly known as the mountain agathosma, is a small evergreen shrub native to the Western Cape of South Africa.

It is characterized by its aromatic, needle-like leaves that are typically gray-green in color. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in spring, followed by small, fleshy fruits. It thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its unique appearance and fragrance, it is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes.
