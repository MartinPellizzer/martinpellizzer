Alocasia atrorubens

Alocasia atrorubens, commonly known as the red-topped alocasia, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are dark green with a reddish tinge on the underside. The plant thrives in humid, shady environments and requires well-draining, nutrient-rich soil. Its foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and ornamental purposes.

Alocasia atrorubens is also known for its ability to purify the air, making it both aesthetically pleasing and beneficial for indoor spaces.
