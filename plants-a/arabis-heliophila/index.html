Arabis Heliophila

Arabis heliophila is a small, flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of western Asia. It thrives in sunny, well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or gravelly habitats. Known for its delicate white or pink flowers, it blooms in late spring to early summer. This plant is also called the sun-loving rockcress due to its preference for bright light. It is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often used in rock gardens and alpine arrangements.
