Axonopus fissifolius var. polystachyus

Axonopus fissifolius var. polystachyus, commonly known as the narrow-leaf axonopus, is a grass species native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves that are often narrow and slightly folded. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories or along stream banks. Its inflorescence consists of clustered spikelets, which are small and dense.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in low-light conditions.
