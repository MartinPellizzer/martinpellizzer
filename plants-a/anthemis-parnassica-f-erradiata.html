Anthemis parnassica f. erradiata

Anthemis parnassica f. erradiata, commonly known as the radiate anthemis, is a flowering plant characterized by its distinctive radiate inflorescence.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces yellow flowers with a central disk surrounded by ray florets. It thrives in rocky and alpine environments, often growing at high altitudes.

Its botanical structure reflects adaptations to harsh, arid conditions, making it a resilient species in its native habitat.
