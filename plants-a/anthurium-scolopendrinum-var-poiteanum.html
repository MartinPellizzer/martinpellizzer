Anthurium scolopendrinum var. poiteanum

Anthurium scolopendrinum var. poiteanum, commonly known as the Poiteanum Anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces vibrant red spadices, which are the spathes that enclose the true flowers. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

This variety is popular among plant enthusiasts for its ornamental value and ease of care.
