Aster standleyi

Aster standleyi, commonly known as the Standley's aster, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of Central America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or open habitats, thriving in cool, moist environments. Its flowers are usually blue or violet, with narrow, linear leaves that help reduce water loss.

Aster standleyi is valued for its ornamental appeal and plays a role in supporting local pollinator populations.
