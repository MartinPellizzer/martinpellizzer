Astrophytum myriostigma subsp. coahuilense

Astrophytum myriostigma subsp.

coahuilense, commonly known as the Coahuila star cactus, is a small, spineless cactus native to northern Mexico. It features a globular stem covered in white, woolly hairs that help reduce water loss.

The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is adapted to arid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is often cultivated as a decorative plant.
