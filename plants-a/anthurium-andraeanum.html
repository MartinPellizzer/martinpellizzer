Anthurium andraeanum

Anthurium andraeanum, commonly known as the flamingo flower, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped spathes that come in a variety of colors, including red, pink, and white. The plant belongs to the Araceae family and is known for its unique inflorescence, which consists of a spadix surrounded by a colorful bract. Its foliage consists of large, glossy, arrow-shaped leaves that are also ornamental.

Anthurium andraeanum is popular as a houseplant due to its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
