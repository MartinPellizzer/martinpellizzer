Aster shortii

Aster shortii, commonly known as the Short's aster, is a flowering plant native to the northeastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and stream banks. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are blue to purple in color.

Aster shortii is known for its ornamental value and is often found in native plant gardens.
