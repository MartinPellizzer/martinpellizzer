Armeria vulgaris f. arctica

Armeria vulgaris f. arctica, commonly known as Arctic thrift, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Arctic and subarctic regions.

It features dense clusters of small, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has silver-gray foliage with a woolly texture, adapted to withstand harsh, cold climates. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in tundra and rocky habitats.

Its hardy nature makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and alpine landscapes.
