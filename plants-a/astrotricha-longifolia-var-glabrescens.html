Astrotricha longifolia var. glabrescens

Astrotricha longifolia var. glabrescens, commonly known as the smoothleaf starwort, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and typical herbaceous growth habit. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine, smooth layer of hair, giving it its varietal name. Its flowers are small and yellow, arranged in a dense cluster at the top of the stem.

It typically grows in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in meadows and woodland edges.
