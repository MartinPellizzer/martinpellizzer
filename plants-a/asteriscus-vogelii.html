Asteriscus vogelii

Asteriscus vogelii, commonly known as Vogel's asterisk, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers that are typically pale blue to purple in color. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats.

Asteriscus vogelii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
