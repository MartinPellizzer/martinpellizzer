Anthurium holtonianum var. cohaerens

Anthurium holtonianum var. cohaerens, commonly known as the Holton's anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped leaves that can range in color from deep green to dark red. The plant produces striking spadices, which are the colorful, spathaceous structures that house the tiny, inconspicuous flowers. It thrives in humid, warm environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.
