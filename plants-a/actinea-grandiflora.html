Actinea grandiflora

Actinea grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered actinia, is a striking member of the Araceae family.

It is characterized by its large, showy spatheal flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. The plant features a short, thick rhizome and produces numerous small, green, spadix-like structures within the spathe. Native to tropical regions, it thrives in humid, warm environments with partial shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical landscapes.
