Anthurium grandifolium

Anthurium grandifolium, commonly known as the giant anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length. The plant produces striking spadices, which are modified leaves that enclose the true flowers. It thrives in humid, warm environments with bright, indirect light.

Anthurium grandifolium is popular as a decorative plant due to its lush foliage and unique inflorescences.
