Aeonium × orbelindense

Aeonium × orbelindense, commonly known as the Sunset Aeonium, is a hybrid succulent plant native to the Canary Islands.

It features rosettes of fleshy, triangular leaves that range in color from green to red and pink, depending on light exposure. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with small, yellowish flowers during the spring. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with full sun.

Aeonium × orbelindense is popular in rock gardens and as a decorative houseplant due to its striking appearance and low maintenance requirements.
