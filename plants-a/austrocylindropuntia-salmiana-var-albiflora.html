Austrocylindropuntia salmiana var. albiflora

Austrocylindropuntia salmiana var. albiflora, commonly known as the white-flowered columnar cactus, is a species native to southern South America.

It is characterized by its tall, cylindrical stem that can grow up to several meters in height. The plant produces large, showy white flowers that bloom during the spring and summer months. It has spiny areoles and is adapted to arid environments with minimal water requirements.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique appearance.
