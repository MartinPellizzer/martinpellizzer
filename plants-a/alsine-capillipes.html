Alsine capillipes

Alsine capillipes, commonly known as the slender alsike, is a low-growing annual plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Caryophyllaceae, which includes many flowering plants with showy blooms. The plant features small, delicate flowers that are typically pink or white, arranged in clusters at the ends of its stems. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it thrives in disturbed soils such as roadsides and fields.

Alsine capillipes is often found in moist environments and is a common weed in agricultural areas.
