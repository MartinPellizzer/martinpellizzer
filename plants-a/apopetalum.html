Apopetalum

Apopetalum, commonly known as the false hellebore, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Melanthiaceae.

It is characterized by its basal leaves that are typically lobed or divided, and its flowers are usually clustered in umbels. It is native to parts of Asia and is often found in moist, shaded environments.
