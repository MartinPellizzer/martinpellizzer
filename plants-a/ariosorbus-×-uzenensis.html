Ariosorbus × uzenensis

Ariosorbus × uzenensis, commonly known as the Uzen hybrid sorrel, is a hybrid plant resulting from the cross between Ariosorbus kiusiuensis and Ariosorbus hondoensis.

It is a low-growing, herbaceous perennial native to Japan, specifically found in the Uzen region of Hokkaido. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive reddish or purple underside, and it produces small, yellow flowers in early spring. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Ariosorbus × uzenensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is occasionally cultivated in specialty gardens.
