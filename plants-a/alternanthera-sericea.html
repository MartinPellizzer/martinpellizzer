Alternanthera sericea

Alternanthera sericea, commonly known as silverleaf Alternanthera, is a species of flowering plant in the Amaranthaceae family.

It is native to South America and is characterized by its striking silver-gray foliage. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or red, depending on the variety. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant in gardens and water features.

Its foliage is particularly notable for its reflective, metallic appearance, which gives it its common name.
