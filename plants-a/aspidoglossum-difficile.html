Aspidoglossum difficile

Aspidoglossum difficile, commonly known as the silver sword, is a striking succulent native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of fleshy, silver-green leaves that are covered in fine, white hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences that bear small, yellow flowers, typically blooming in spring. It is adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick, fleshy leaves.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for succulent gardens and rockeries.
