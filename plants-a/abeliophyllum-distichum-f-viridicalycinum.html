Abeliophyllum distichum f. viridicalycinum

Abeliophyllum distichum f. viridicalycinum, commonly known as the emerald leaf abeliophyllum, is a compact shrub native to Japan.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage that exhibits a striking emerald green color, which remains consistent throughout the growing season. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers in early spring, which are borne on short pedicels. Its foliage is particularly notable for its glossy texture and vibrant coloration, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

This cultivar is well-suited for container growing and can thrive in partial shade to full sun conditions.
