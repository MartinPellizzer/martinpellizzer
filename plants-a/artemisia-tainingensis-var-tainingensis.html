Artemisia tainingensis var. tainingensis

Artemisia tainingensis var. tainingensis, commonly known as Taining wormwood, is a perennial herb native to China.

It is characterized by its aromatic, silver-gray foliage and slender, erect stems. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flower heads that appear in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its medicinal properties, it is valued in traditional Chinese medicine.
