Aciotis paludosa var. latifolia

Aciotis paludosa var. latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf marsh fleabane, is a herbaceous plant native to wetland habitats in North America.

The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in a composite head, typical of the Asteraceae family. It thrives in moist soils and is often found in marshes, swamps, and along the edges of ponds.
