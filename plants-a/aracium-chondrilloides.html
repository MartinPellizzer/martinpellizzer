Aracium chondrilloides

Aracium chondrilloides, commonly known as the rock cress, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is native to southern Europe and western Asia, typically growing in rocky or limestone soils. The plant features small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, adapted to withstand arid conditions.
