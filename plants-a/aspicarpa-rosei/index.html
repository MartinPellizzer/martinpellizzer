Aspicarpa Rosei

Aspicarpa rosei is a rare and fascinating plant native to the high altitudes of southern Africa. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, known for its diverse and often showy flowers. This species is characterized by its striking pinkish-red petals and compact, bushy growth habit. Due to its limited distribution and unique appearance, it has become a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists. Despite its beauty, Aspicarpa rosei faces threats from habitat loss and climate change.
