Amomum sceletescens

Amomum sceletescens, commonly known as the slender cardamom, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the ginger family (Zingiberaceae) and is characterized by its aromatic, finger-like rhizomes. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers followed by oval, dark brown fruits containing aromatic seeds. Its botanical features include a tall, slender stem and lance-shaped leaves with a hairy underside.

The rhizomes are valued for their medicinal and culinary properties due to their distinctive spicy aroma.
