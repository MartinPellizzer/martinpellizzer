Allocasuarina muelleriana subsp. alticola

Allocasuarina muelleriana subsp.

alticola, commonly known as the mountain sheoak, is a shrub or small tree native to Australia. It is characterized by its distinctive cylindrical, scale-like leaves that are adapted to reduce water loss in arid environments.

The plant produces small, woody cones that contain seeds, typical of the Casuarinaceae family. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, rocky habitats.
