Agave × chisosensis

Agave × chisosensis, commonly known as the Chisos agave, is a hybrid plant native to the Chisos Mountains in Texas.

It is a succulent with thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces an upright flowering stalk that bears clusters of white flowers. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Its unique appearance and hardiness make it a popular choice for xeriscaping and rock gardens.
