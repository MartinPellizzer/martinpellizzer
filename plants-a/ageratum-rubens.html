Ageratum rubens

Ageratum rubens, commonly known as the red ageratum, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its fluffy, showy flower heads that range in color from white to deep red. The plant typically grows to a height of 30 to 60 centimeters and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its foliage is dark green and slightly hairy, providing a striking contrast to its vibrant blooms.

Ageratum rubens is often cultivated as an ornamental plant in gardens and for its use in floral arrangements.
