Alchemilla ellenbeckii subsp. ellenbeckii

Alchemilla ellenbeckii subsp.

ellenbeckii, commonly known as Ellenbeck's lady's mantle, is a perennial herb native to Europe. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodland floors and stream banks.

The plant features basal rosettes of heart-shaped, silver-green leaves that are covered in fine, soft hairs. Its small, yellowish-green flowers appear in summer and are clustered in loose, branched inflorescences.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in native plant gardens.
