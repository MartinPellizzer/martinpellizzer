Astronia alata

Astronia alata, commonly known as the winged tamarind, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compound leaves with distinctive wing-like structures on the leaflets. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in panicles. Its fruit is a flat, winged pod that splits open to release seeds.

Astronia alata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional medicine.
