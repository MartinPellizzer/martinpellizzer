Aquilegia turczaninowii

Aquilegia turczaninowii, commonly known as the Turczaninow's columbine, is a species of flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive spurred petals and vibrant blue or purple flowers. This species is valued for its ornamental beauty and ecological importance in its native habitats.
