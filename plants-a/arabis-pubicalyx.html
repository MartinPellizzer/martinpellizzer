Arabis pubicalyx

Arabis pubicalyx, commonly known as the pubicalyx rockcress, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other cruciferous plants. Arabis pubicalyx is well adapted to rocky and arid environments, making it a hardy species suitable for rock gardens and alpine settings.
