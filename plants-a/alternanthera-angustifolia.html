Alternanthera angustifolia

Alternanthera angustifolia, commonly known as narrowleaf Alternanthera, is a herbaceous plant native to South America.

It features slender, lance-shaped leaves that are often reddish or purple in color, especially under bright light. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It is commonly cultivated as a decorative plant in aquariums and water gardens due to its vibrant foliage.

Alternanthera angustifolia thrives in moist, nutrient-rich soil and prefers full to partial sunlight.
