Arenaria ludens

Arenaria ludens, commonly known as the dancing sandplant, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It thrives in sandy and rocky soils, often found in coastal areas and dry landscapes. The plant produces small, white or pink flowers that bloom in clusters, adding a delicate touch to its habitat. Its leaves are typically succulent, adapted to store water in arid environments.

Arenaria ludens is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to stabilize soil in harsh conditions.
