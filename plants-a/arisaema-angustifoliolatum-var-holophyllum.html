Arisaema angustifoliolatum var. holophyllum

Arisaema angustifoliolatum var. holophyllum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf arrowhead, is a member of the Araceae family.

It is characterized by its distinctive arrow-shaped leaves and a spadix surrounded by a spathe. Native to Southeast Asia, this plant thrives in moist, shaded environments. The plant produces small, white flowers that are followed by berries.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has traditional uses in herbal medicine.
