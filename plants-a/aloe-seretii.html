Aloe seretii

Aloe seretii, commonly known as the White Aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically pale green to white in color, covered in a waxy coating. The plant produces tall, branching inflorescences with yellow flowers during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its leaves to survive drought conditions.

Aloe seretii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional medicinal uses.
