Agrimonia japonica var. granulosa

Agrimonia japonica var. granulosa, commonly known as the granulated agrimony, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It features upright, branched stems covered in fine, glandular hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in dense, rounded clusters. Its leaves are alternate, serrated, and covered with small, raised bumps, giving them a granular texture.

It is often found in moist meadows and woodland edges, and is valued for its traditional medicinal uses.
