Alstonia lanceolifera

Alstonia lanceolifera, commonly known as the narrow-leaved alstonia, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its elongated, lance-shaped leaves that are opposite in arrangement. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers followed by woody, capsule-like fruits containing seeds. It is often found in lowland forests and is valued for its ornamental appearance.

The tree has a slow growth rate and is relatively hardy in humid climates.
