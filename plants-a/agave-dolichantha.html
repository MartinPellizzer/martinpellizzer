Agave dolichantha

Agave dolichantha, commonly known as the narrow-leaved agave, is a succulent plant native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and long, narrow leaves that can grow up to 3 meters in length. The plant produces a striking inflorescence of yellow flowers that bloom once in its lifetime. It is adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Its rosette growth pattern and ability to store water in its leaves make it well-suited for desert conditions.
