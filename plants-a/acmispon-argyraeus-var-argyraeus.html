Acmispon argyraeus var. argyraeus

Acmispon argyraeus var. argyraeus, commonly known as silverleaf cosmos, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a herbaceous annual or perennial that features silvery, fuzzy foliage and produces showy, daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant has compound leaves with serrated edges and thrives in full sun to partial shade. It is drought-tolerant and well-suited for xeriscaping due to its low water requirements.

The flowers attract pollinators such as butterflies and bees, making it a valuable addition to native gardens.
