Arisaema talense

Arisaema talense, commonly known as the Talanese arrowhead, is a member of the Araceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Thailand and Malaysia. The plant features a distinctive arrowhead-shaped leaf that is often deeply lobed. Its flowering structure consists of a spadix surrounded by a modified leaf called a spathe.

Arisaema talense is typically grown as an ornamental plant due to its unique foliage and striking floral display.
