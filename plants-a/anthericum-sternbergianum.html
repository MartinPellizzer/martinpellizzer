Anthericum sternbergianum

Anthericum sternbergianum, commonly known as the Sternberg's lily, is a herbaceous plant native to the wetlands of North America.

It features tall, narrow leaves and produces striking white or pale pink flowers with a distinctive, recurved shape. The plant is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit, allowing it to spread and form dense colonies. It thrives in moist, acidic soils and is often found in swampy or marshy habitats.

Anthericum sternbergianum plays an important ecological role by providing habitat and nectar for various pollinators.
