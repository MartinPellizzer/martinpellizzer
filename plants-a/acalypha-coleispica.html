Acalypha coleispica

Acalypha coleispica, commonly known as the silverleaf acalypha, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking silver-gray foliage, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, clustered greenish-white flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Acalypha coleispica is relatively low maintenance and can be grown in containers or in gardens with well-draining soil.
