Anemone viticella

Anemone viticella, commonly known as the wood anemone, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features delicate, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring, often before the leaves emerge. The plant has a short, rhizomatous root system and produces trifoliate leaves with a heart-shaped base. It thrives in moist, shaded woodland areas and is often found in forest understories.

Anemone viticella is valued for its ornamental beauty and is commonly cultivated in gardens and naturalized landscapes.
