Aloe hereroensis var. orpeniae

Aloe hereroensis var. orpeniae, commonly known as the Herero aloe, is a succulent plant native to Namibia and South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a waxy coating, adapted to store water in arid environments. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow to orange flowers, which bloom during the warmer months. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine and for their soothing properties.

It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for xeriscaping.
