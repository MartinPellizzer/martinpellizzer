Asteracantha macracantha

Asteracantha macracantha, commonly known as the giant asteracantha, is a striking plant native to parts of Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from purple to pink, often with a distinctive central disk. The plant has hairy, lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged in a basal rosette. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a notable addition to botanical collections.
