Aulomyrcia thyrsiflora var. obtusifolia

Aulomyrcia thyrsiflora var. obtusifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America.

The leaves are broad and ovate, often with a waxy texture, and the plant can grow up to several meters in height.
