Anteremanthus hatschbachii

Anteremanthus hatschbachii, commonly known as the yellow bellflower, is a striking perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features showy, tubular yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has lance-shaped, dark green leaves that are opposite in arrangement. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a valuable addition to xeriscapes.
