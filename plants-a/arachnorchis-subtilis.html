Arachnorchis subtilis

Arachnorchis subtilis, commonly known as the delicate spider orchid, is an epiphytic orchid native to Australia.

It features a slender, wiry stem with a single, narrow leaf and a small, pale green flower spike. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers with white petals and a distinctive, hooded labellum. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, acidic soil.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a vulnerable species.
