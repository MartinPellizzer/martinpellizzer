Aegiphila triantha

Aegiphila triantha, commonly known as the three-flowered aegiphila, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in tropical and subtropical environments.

Aegiphila triantha is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
