Asystasia quarterna

Asystasia quarterna, commonly known as the silver leaf plant, is a herbaceous perennial native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking silver-gray leaves and clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to violet. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its leaves are covered in fine, reflective hairs that give it its distinctive appearance.

Asystasia quarterna is valued in horticulture for its ornamental qualities and ability to thrive in various soil types.
