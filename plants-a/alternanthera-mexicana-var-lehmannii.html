Alternanthera mexicana var. lehmannii

Alternanthera mexicana var. lehmannii, commonly known as the Mexican Alternanthera, is a flowering plant native to Central America.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage. The leaves are often red, pink, or purple, which are particularly vibrant in full sun. It produces small, inconspicuous green flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
