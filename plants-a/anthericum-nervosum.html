Anthericum nervosum

Anthericum nervosum, commonly known as the slender rush, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Cyperaceae, which includes sedges and rushes, and is characterized by its grass-like appearance. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Anthericum nervosum is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in stabilizing soil in wetland ecosystems.
