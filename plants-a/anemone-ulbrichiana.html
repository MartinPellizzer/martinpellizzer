Anemone ulbrichiana

Anemone ulbrichiana, commonly known as the Ulbrich's anemone, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features showy, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has basal leaves that are deeply lobed and hairy, with a rosette growth habit. Its flowers are typically arranged in a single cluster at the top of the stem.

Anemone ulbrichiana thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a suitable candidate for rock gardens and alpine settings.
