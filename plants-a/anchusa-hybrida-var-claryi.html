Anchusa hybrida var. claryi

Anchusa hybrida var. claryi, commonly known as Clary Anchusa, is a perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of blue to purple flowers. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are hairy and covered in fine glands. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its showy blooms and ability to attract pollinators.
