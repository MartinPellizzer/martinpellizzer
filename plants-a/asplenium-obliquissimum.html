Asplenium obliquissimum

Asplenium obliquissimum, commonly known as the zigzag spleenwort, is a species of fern belonging to the family Aspleniaceae.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves that often exhibit a zigzag or undulating pattern, which gives it its common name. This plant is typically found growing on rocks or in shaded, moist environments, often in temperate regions. Its fronds are usually dark green and can reach lengths of up to 30 centimeters.

Asplenium obliquissimum is a perennial plant that reproduces through spores, and it plays a role in stabilizing soil in rocky habitats.
