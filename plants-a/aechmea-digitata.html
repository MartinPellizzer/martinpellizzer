Aechmea digitata

Aechmea digitata, commonly known as the pink pineapple bromeliad, is a striking epiphytic plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

Its foliage is typically dark green with a metallic sheen, and the leaves are stiff and sword-like in appearance.

Aechmea digitata thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
