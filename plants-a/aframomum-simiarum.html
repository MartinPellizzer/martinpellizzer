Aframomum simiarum

Aframomum simiarum, commonly known as African cardamom, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the ginger family (Zingiberaceae) and is characterized by its aromatic, branched rhizomes. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in spike-like inflorescences. Its leaves are long, narrow, and have a rough, textured surface.
