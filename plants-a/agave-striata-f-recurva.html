Agave striata f. recurva

Agave striata f. recurva, commonly known as the narrow-leaved agave, is a succulent plant native to Mexico.

It features narrow, gray-green leaves that are recurved, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with showy yellow flowers. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
