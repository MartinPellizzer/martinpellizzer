Aloe musapana

Aloe musapana, commonly known as the red aloe or red-tipped aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with red or purple tips. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing yellow flowers, which attract pollinators like birds and insects. Its gel-like pulp is used in traditional medicine and skincare products.

Aloe musapana is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with ample sunlight.
