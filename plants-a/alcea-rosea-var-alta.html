Alcea rosea var. alta

Alcea rosea var. alta, commonly known as the tall touch-me-not, is a variant of the common hollyhock.

It is characterized by its tall, upright growth habit and large, showy flowers that range from pink to red. The plant is native to Europe and has been cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden borders.

Its foliage is broad and heart-shaped, adding a lush texture to the landscape.
