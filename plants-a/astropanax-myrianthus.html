Astropanax myrianthus

Astropanax myrianthus, commonly known as the mountain ginseng, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araliaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, compound leaves with multiple leaflets.
