Aizopsis middendorffianum

Aizopsis middendorffianum, commonly known as the African umbrella plant, is a species of flowering plant in the Aroid family.

It is native to southern Africa and is characterized by its striking, umbrella-like inflorescence. The plant features large, waxy, green leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found near water bodies.
