Acanthopsis tuba

Acanthopsis tuba, commonly known as the tuberous acanthus, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking tuberous roots and showy, bract-like flowers. The plant produces clusters of vibrant blue to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs.

Acanthopsis tuba is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to well-drained soils.
