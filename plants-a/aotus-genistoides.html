Aotus genistoides

Aotus genistoides, commonly known as the yellow flame tree, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its striking yellow-orange flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, flat pods that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind. Its leaves are compound, consisting of several oval-shaped leaflets, and it thrives in full sun with well-drained soil.

The yellow flame tree is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant floral display.
