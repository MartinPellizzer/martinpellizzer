Acalypha leptostachya var. genuina

Acalypha leptostachya var. genuina, commonly known as the velvetleaf or silkyleaf, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its soft, velvety leaves that range in color from green to reddish-brown. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically green or yellowish in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
