Athyrium fangii

Athyrium fangii, commonly known as the Fang's lady fern, is a species of fern native to eastern Asia, including China, Japan, and Korea.

It belongs to the family Athyriaceae and is characterized by its delicate, bipinnate fronds that are often adorned with fine, hair-like structures. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky slopes. Its rhizomes are often covered in a dense layer of brownish or reddish scales.

Athyrium fangii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its elegant foliage.
