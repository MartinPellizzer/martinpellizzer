Agathosma brevifolia

Agathosma brevifolia, commonly known as buchu, is a small evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its aromatic, glandular leaves that emit a strong, distinctive scent. The plant belongs to the family Rutaceae and is known for its medicinal properties. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.
