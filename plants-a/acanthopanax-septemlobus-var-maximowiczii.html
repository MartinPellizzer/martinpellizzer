Acanthopanax septemlobus var. maximowiczii

Acanthopanax septemlobus var. maximowiczii, commonly known as the Chinese arrowroot or giant devil's ivy, is a climbing shrub native to China.

It features compound leaves with seven lobes, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are followed by red berries. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and traditional medicine.
