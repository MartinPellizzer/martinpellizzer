Aloe × panormitana

Aloe × panormitana, commonly known as the hybrid aloe or aloe vera hybrid, is a succulent plant native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the Asphodelaceae family and is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that store water, making it well adapted to arid environments. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellowish-green flowers, typically blooming in late spring or early summer. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine and skincare products due to their soothing and healing properties.

Aloe × panormitana is relatively easy to care for, requiring minimal water and full sun to thrive.
