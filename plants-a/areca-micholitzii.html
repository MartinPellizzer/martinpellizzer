Areca micholitzii

Areca micholitzii, commonly known as the Micholitz's areca, is a flowering plant belonging to the Arecaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of India and Indonesia. The plant features a slender, unbranched trunk and pinnate leaves that are dark green and glossy. It produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or white in color.

The fruit is a three-lobed, fibrous structure that contains several seeds.
