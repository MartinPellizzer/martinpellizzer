Aloe ferox

Aloe ferox, commonly known as Cape aloe, is a succulent plant native to the arid regions of South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that store water, enabling it to thrive in dry environments. The plant produces a tall, branched stem with clusters of yellow flowers, typically blooming in summer. Its gel-like sap contains compounds with medicinal properties, often used for skin treatments.

Aloe ferox is valued for its resilience and has been utilized by indigenous communities for centuries.
