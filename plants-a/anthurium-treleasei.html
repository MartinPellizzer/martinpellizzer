Anthurium treleasei

Anthurium treleasei, commonly known as the Trelease's anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically deep green with a glossy texture. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-shaped inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, often in shades of red or orange. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Anthurium treleasei is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and unique floral structure.
