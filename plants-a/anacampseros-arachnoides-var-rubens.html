Anacampseros arachnoides var. rubens

Anacampseros arachnoides var. rubens, commonly known as the red sand mat, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features a low-growing, mat-forming structure with fleshy, blue-green leaves covered in a fine, whitish coating. The leaves are often tinged with red or purple, especially when exposed to sunlight. It produces small, yellow flowers during the warmer months, which are borne on slender stems.

This species is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
