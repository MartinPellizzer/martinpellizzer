Afrormosia bequaertii

Afrormosia bequaertii, commonly known as the African shea tree, is a tropical tree native to western and central Africa.

It belongs to the family Meliaceae and is characterized by its smooth, dark bark and large, simple leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers followed by round, woody fruits containing a single seed. The tree is also valued for its timber, which is durable and resistant to decay.
