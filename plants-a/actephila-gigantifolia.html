Actephila gigantifolia

Actephila gigantifolia, commonly known as the giant leaf actephila, is a striking plant native to parts of East Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to several feet in diameter. The plant produces small, white flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in the summer months. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or wetlands.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and botanical collections.
