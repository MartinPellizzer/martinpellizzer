Aspalathus stricta

Aspalathus stricta, commonly known as African red bush, is a small evergreen shrub native to the Western Cape of South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and bright red flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has small, leathery leaves that are arranged in pairs along the stems. It thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and requires full sun exposure.

Aspalathus stricta is also known for its use in traditional medicine and as a source of Rooibos tea.
