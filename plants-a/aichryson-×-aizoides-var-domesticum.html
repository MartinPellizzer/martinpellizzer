Aichryson × aizoides var. domesticum

Aichryson × aizoides var. domesticum, commonly known as the hybrid African violet, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, thick leaves that are typically green with a waxy coating, adapted to store water. The plant produces small, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, often arranged in a compact inflorescence. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Due to its ornamental value, it is popular as a houseplant and is relatively easy to care for.
