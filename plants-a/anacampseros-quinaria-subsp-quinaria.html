Anacampseros quinaria subsp. quinaria

Anacampseros quinaria subsp.

quinaria, commonly known as the quinaria ice plant, is a succulent species native to southern Africa. It features a low-growing, mat-forming structure with fleshy, blue-green leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white powder.

The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or purple during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its leaves and stems.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
