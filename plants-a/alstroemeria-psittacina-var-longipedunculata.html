Alstroemeria psittacina var. longipedunculata

Alstroemeria psittacina var. longipedunculata, commonly known as the long pedunculate alstroemeria, is a striking perennial plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Alstroemeriaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, orange, and yellow. The plant features long, slender flower stalks that elevate its blooms, making it a visually appealing ornamental species. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are often used in floral arrangements.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and adaptability to various growing conditions.
