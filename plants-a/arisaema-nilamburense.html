Arisaema nilamburense

Arisaema nilamburense, commonly known as the Nilambur arrowroot, is a tropical plant native to the Western Ghats of India.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its striking, umbrella-shaped inflorescence. The plant produces a starchy, edible rhizome that is used in local cuisines. Its leaves are broad and arrowhead-shaped, contributing to its common name.

Arisaema nilamburense thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forested areas.
