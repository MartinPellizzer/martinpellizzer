Astilbe rivularis

Astilbe rivularis, commonly known as the water astilbe, is a herbaceous perennial native to eastern Asia.

It features upright, branching stems that bear clusters of small, fragrant, white to pinkish flowers. The plant has narrow, dark green, lance-shaped leaves that are often glossy. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is well-suited for wetland gardens.

Its botanical characteristics make it an attractive addition to water gardens and bog landscapes.
