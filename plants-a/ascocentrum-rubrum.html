Ascocentrum rubrum

Ascocentrum rubrum, commonly known as the red-stemmed arrowroot, is a tropical plant belonging to the Araceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its striking red stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in an inflorescence called a spadix, surrounded by a colorful spathe. Ascocentrum rubrum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.

Its growth habit makes it suitable for indoor cultivation in warm, humid environments.
