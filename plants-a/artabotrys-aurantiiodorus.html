Artabotrys aurantiiodorus

Artabotrys aurantiiodorus, commonly known as the golden flame tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant, orange-yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, giving it a vivid and showy appearance. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are elliptical in shape and often have a waxy texture. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, hairy seeds.

Artabotrys aurantiiodorus is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
