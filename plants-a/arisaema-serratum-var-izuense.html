Arisaema serratum var. izuense

Arisaema serratum var. izuense, commonly known as the Izu mountain arrowhead, is a herbaceous plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its distinctive arrow-shaped leaves and spadix inflorescence. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its botanical features include a rhizomatous root system and a short, erect stem.

The spathe is usually green with a yellowish or white inner surface, and it blooms from late spring to early summer.
