Aneilema nyasense var. brevisepala

Aneilema nyasense var. brevisepala, commonly known as the short-peduncled aneilema, is a herbaceous plant native to eastern Africa.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its striking, spatulate leaves and showy, white to cream-colored spathe. The plant produces small, clustered berries that are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for cultivation in tropical gardens.
