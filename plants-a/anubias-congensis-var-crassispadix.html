Anubias congensis var. crassispadix

Anubias congensis var. crassispadix, commonly known as the broad-stemmed African water plant, is a hardy aquatic plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features broad, thick, dark green leaves that are often used in aquariums for their aesthetic appeal and ability to provide shelter for aquatic life. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit, allowing it to spread slowly across the substrate. It prefers low to moderate light conditions and can thrive in a variety of water parameters.

Its adaptability makes it a popular choice for both beginners and experienced aquarists.
