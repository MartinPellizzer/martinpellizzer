Aeonium × afurense

Aeonium × afurense, commonly known as the Crown of Thorns, is a hybrid succulent plant native to the Canary Islands.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy, green or blue-gray leaves that are often covered in a powdery coating. The plant produces tall, branched stems with clusters of yellow flowers during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a striking addition to both indoor and outdoor landscapes.
