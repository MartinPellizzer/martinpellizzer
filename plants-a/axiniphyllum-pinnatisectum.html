Axiniphyllum pinnatisectum

Axiniphyllum pinnatisectum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hakea, is a shrub native to Australia.

It features compound leaves that are pinnatisect, meaning they are deeply divided into narrow, leaflets. The plant produces small, spherical flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.

The botanical characteristics of Axiniphyllum pinnatisectum make it an interesting subject for study in Australian flora.
