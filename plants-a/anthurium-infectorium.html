Anthurium infectorium

Anthurium infectorium, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces vibrant red spathes that enclose its yellow spadix, creating a dramatic floral display. Anthurium infectorium thrives in humid, warm environments with bright, indirect light.

It is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its ornamental foliage and unique flowering structure.
