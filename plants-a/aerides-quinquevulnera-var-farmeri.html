Aerides quinquevulnera var. farmeri

Aerides quinquevulnera var. farmeri, commonly known as the Farmer's Vase Orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white, pink, and red. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are elliptical and dark green, often with a waxy texture.

This orchid thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate humidity and well-draining soil for optimal growth.
