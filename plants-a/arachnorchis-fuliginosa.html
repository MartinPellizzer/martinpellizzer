Arachnorchis fuliginosa

Arachnorchis fuliginosa, commonly known as the smoky orchid, is an epiphytic or lithophytic orchid native to Australia.

It typically grows on rocks or trees, thriving in humid and shaded environments. The plant produces striking, dark purple to black flowers with a distinctive, fragrant scent. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture and nutrients from its substrate.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in specialized horticultural settings.
