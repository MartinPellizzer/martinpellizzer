Alstonia lenormandii var. coriacea

Alstonia lenormandii var. coriacea, commonly known as the rough-barked alstonia, is a rare and distinctive plant native to Madagascar.

The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by woody capsules containing its seeds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
