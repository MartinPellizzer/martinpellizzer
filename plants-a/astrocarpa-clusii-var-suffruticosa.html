Astrocarpa clusii var. suffruticosa

Astrocarpa clusii var. suffruticosa, commonly known as the yellow star vine, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and produces clusters of small, star-shaped yellow flowers. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth texture and a slightly succulent appearance. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Its botanical features make it suitable for hanging baskets or as a ground cover in shaded areas.
