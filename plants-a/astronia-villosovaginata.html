Astronia villosovaginata

Astronia villosovaginata, commonly known as the silky-leaved astronia, is a shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its dense, hairy foliage and small, clustered flowers that bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in some botanical gardens and private collections.
