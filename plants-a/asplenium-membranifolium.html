Asplenium membranifolium

Asplenium membranifolium, commonly known as the membranous fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features delicate, lance-shaped leaves that are typically pale green with a thin, membranous texture. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in rocky or forested areas. Its fronds are usually bi- or tri-pinnate, contributing to its intricate and elegant appearance.

Asplenium membranifolium is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and ability to thrive in humid conditions.
