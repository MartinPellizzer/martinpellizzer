Astartea schaueri

Astartea schaueri, commonly known as the silverleaf aster, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the southeastern United States.

It features narrow, silver-green leaves that are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, clustered flower heads that range in color from white to purple, typically blooming in the summer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Astartea schaueri is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, providing nectar for pollinators.
