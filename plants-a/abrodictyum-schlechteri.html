Abrodictyum schlechteri

Abrodictyum schlechteri, commonly known as the Schlechter's orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to South America.

It is a terrestrial herb with a sympodial growth habit, characterized by its pseudobulbs and linear leaves. The plant produces strikingly colored flowers that are typically yellow or orange with intricate patterns. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Due to its unique appearance and specific habitat requirements, it is a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
