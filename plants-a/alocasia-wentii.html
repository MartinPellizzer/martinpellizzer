Alocasia wentii

Alocasia wentii, commonly known as the velvet leaf alocasia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in a dense, velvety texture. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its foliage is often a deep green with a striking metallic sheen.

Alocasia wentii thrives in moist, shaded environments and is popular as a decorative houseplant.
