Aeschynomene gilletii

Aeschynomene gilletii, commonly known as partridge pea, is a leguminous plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is known for its ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, making it valuable in soil enrichment. It thrives in tropical and subtropical regions and is often found in disturbed soils.

Its seeds are edible and have been used in traditional cuisines.
