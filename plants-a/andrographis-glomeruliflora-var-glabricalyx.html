Andrographis glomeruliflora var. glabricalyx

Andrographis glomeruliflora var. glabricalyx, commonly known as the smooth-leafedandrographis, is a perennial herb native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its lanceolate, glabrous leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow-green in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its medicinal properties have been studied for their potential anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial effects.
