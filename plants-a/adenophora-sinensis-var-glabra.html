Adenophora sinensis var. glabra

Adenophora sinensis var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth adenophora, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its slender stems and bell-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as mountain forests. Its leaves are lanceolate and often covered with fine hairs, while the flowers are pale blue to white in color.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.
