Atriplex patens

Atriplex patens, commonly known as saltbush, is a shrub native to arid and semi-arid regions of North America.

It is characterized by its succulent leaves that are adapted to retain moisture in dry environments. The plant has small, greenish-white flowers that are typically clustered in dense inflorescences. Its salt-tolerant nature makes it a valuable plant for stabilizing soils in saline areas.

Atriplex patens plays an important role in ecological systems by providing habitat and food for various wildlife species.
