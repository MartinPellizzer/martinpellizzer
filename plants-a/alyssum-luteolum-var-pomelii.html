Alyssum luteolum var. pomelii

Alyssum luteolum var. pomelii, commonly known as the yellow alpine alyssum, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe.

It is a low-growing perennial with dense clusters of small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has narrow, silver-green leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a fuzzy appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and alpine settings.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking a hardy, decorative plant.
