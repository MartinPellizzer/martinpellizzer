Aizoanthemum rehmannii

Aizoanthemum rehmannii, commonly known as the ice plant, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, blue-green leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, typically blooming in spring. It is well adapted to sandy soils and full sun exposure.

Due to its drought tolerance and ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a decorative plant in gardens.
