Astragalus baotouensis

Astragalus baotouensis, commonly known as the Baotou milkvetch, is a perennial herb native to the arid regions of northern China.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant is adapted to harsh, semi-desert environments, with deep roots that help it survive in dry conditions.

It plays a role in stabilizing soil and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
