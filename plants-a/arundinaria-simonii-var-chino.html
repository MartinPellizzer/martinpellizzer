Arundinaria simonii var. chino

Arundinaria simonii var. chino, commonly known as Chinese bamboo or Chino bamboo, is a variety of bamboo characterized by its slender, erect culms.

It is native to eastern China and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and hardiness. The plant features narrow, linear leaves that are typically a deep green color. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

This variety is valued for its ability to form dense clumps and its use in landscaping and traditional Chinese medicine.
