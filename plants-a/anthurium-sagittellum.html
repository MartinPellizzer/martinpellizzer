Anthurium sagittellum

Anthurium sagittellum, commonly known as the arrowhead plant, is a tropical species native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, glossy, dark green leaves that are often arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces striking spadices, which are modified leaves that enclose the actual flowers, giving it a unique appearance. It thrives in humid, warm environments with bright, indirect light.

Anthurium sagittellum is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
