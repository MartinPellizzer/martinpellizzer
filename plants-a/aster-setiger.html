Aster setiger

Aster setiger, commonly known as the rough aster, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist meadows and woodlands, reaching heights of up to 2 feet. Its leaves are rough to the touch, with hairy surfaces, and its flowers consist of both ray and disk florets.

Aster setiger is known for its showy purple to blue flowers that bloom in late summer and early fall.
