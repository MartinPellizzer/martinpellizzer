Aplotaxis leontodontoides

Aplotaxis leontodontoides, commonly known as the African dandelion, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

The plant has hairy, narrow leaves and produces a capsule-like fruit that contains numerous small seeds. It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often found in grasslands and along roadsides.
