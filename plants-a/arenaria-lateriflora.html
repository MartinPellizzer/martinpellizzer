Arenaria lateriflora

Arenaria lateriflora, commonly known as the sandspurry, is a low-growing perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in coastal areas and dry meadows. The plant produces small, white or pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are typically linear and arranged along the stems, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental qualities and ability to stabilize sandy soils.
