Anthurium longistamineum

Anthurium longistamineum, commonly known as the long-stamen anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive inflorescence consisting of a spadix and spathe. The plant features broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often brightly colored, with a waxy texture. Its flowers are typically small and clustered around the spadix, which is usually yellow or green in color.

Anthurium longistamineum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and is often cultivated as a houseplant.
