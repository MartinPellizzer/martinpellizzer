Anacamptis morio f. viridiflora

Anacamptis morio f. viridiflora, commonly known as the Green-flowered Orchid, is a fascinating orchid species native to southern Europe.

It is a terrestrial plant that typically grows in open grasslands and woodland edges. The plant features a single, slender stem that bears a cluster of elongated, lance-shaped leaves. Its most distinctive characteristic is the vibrant greenish-yellow flowers, which contrast with its dark green foliage.

This orchid is part of the Orchidaceae family and is known for its delicate beauty and ecological significance.
