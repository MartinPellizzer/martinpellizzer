Amaryllis psittacina

Amaryllis psittacina, commonly known as the yellow amaryllis, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features vibrant yellow flowers with a distinctive orange throat, blooming in summer. The plant has strap-like, evergreen leaves that are broad and lance-shaped. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long-lasting blooms and ease of cultivation.
