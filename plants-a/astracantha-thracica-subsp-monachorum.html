Astracantha thracica subsp. monachorum

Astracantha thracica subsp.

monachorum, commonly known as the Thrace Astracantha, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe. It is characterized by its slender, branching stems and small, tubular flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink to purple.

The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds. It thrives in dry, open habitats such as rocky slopes and grasslands.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive member of the Acanthaceae family.
