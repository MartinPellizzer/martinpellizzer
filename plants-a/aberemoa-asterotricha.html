Aberemoa asterotricha

Aberemoa asterotricha, commonly known as the velvetleaf tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a velvety texture. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are clustered in panicles, and it is often found in moist, lowland areas. Its bark is thick and fibrous, and it has a moderate growth rate.

The plant is sometimes used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
