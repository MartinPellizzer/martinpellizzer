Anthurium adsimile

Anthurium adsimile, commonly known as the velvet leaf anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a velvety texture. The plant produces bright red spadices, which are the spathes that enclose the true flowers. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.

Anthurium adsimile is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
