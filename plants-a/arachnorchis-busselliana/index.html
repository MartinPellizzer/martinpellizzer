Arachnorchis Busselliana

Arachnorchis busselliana is a rare and striking orchid native to the rainforests of eastern Australia. Known for its unique appearance, it features intricate, spider-like structures on its flowers, giving it its intriguing common name. This plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing on the trunks of trees. Its delicate blooms, which range in color from white to pale pink, make it a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts. Due to its limited habitat and threats from deforestation, conservation efforts are crucial for its survival.
