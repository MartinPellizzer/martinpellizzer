Athanasia cinerea

Athanasia cinerea, commonly known as the silverleaf lily, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and produces clusters of small, white, bell-shaped flowers. The plant is often grown as an ornamental due to its elegant appearance and hardy nature. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Athanasia cinerea is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
