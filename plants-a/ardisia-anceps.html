Ardisia anceps

Ardisia anceps, commonly known as the red-flowered ardisia, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are elliptical in shape and often have a slight waxy coating. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers that are followed by bright red, fleshy berries. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Ardisia anceps is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and colorful fruit.
