Arabis commersonii

Arabis commersonii, commonly known as Commerson's rockcress, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or coastal areas. The plant features basal leaves that are pinnately lobed and small, white flowers that bloom in early spring. It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants.

Arabis commersonii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in harsh environments.
