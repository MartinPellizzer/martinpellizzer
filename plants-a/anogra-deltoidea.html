Anogra deltoidea

Anogra deltoidea, commonly known as the delta leaf anogra, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive deltoid-shaped leaves, which are often deeply lobed and arranged alternately along the stem. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color, emerging in the late spring or early summer. Anogra deltoidea thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded forest understories.

Its botanical features make it a subject of interest for botanists and horticulturists alike.
