Asplenium tenuicaule var. subvarians

Asplenium tenuicaule var. subvarians, commonly known as the slender fern, is a small evergreen fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features delicate, lance-shaped fronds that are typically less than 30 cm in length. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and prefers well-drained, acidic soil. Its rhizomes are fibrous and help anchor it to rocky or moist forest floors.

Botanically, it is characterized by its reduced, sub-terete (narrow and slightly flattened) stipes and small, closely spaced pinnae.
