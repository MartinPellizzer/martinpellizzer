Ampelopsis chinensis

Ampelopsis chinensis, commonly known as Chinese honeysuckle, is a climbing vine native to East Asia.

It features heart-shaped leaves and produces clusters of small, fragrant, yellowish-green flowers. The plant is characterized by its woody stems and is often found in moist, shaded environments. It is a member of the Vitaceae family, which includes other well-known vines.

Its botanical features make it a notable species in both horticulture and ecological studies.
