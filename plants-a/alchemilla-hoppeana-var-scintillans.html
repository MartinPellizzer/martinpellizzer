Alchemilla hoppeana var. scintillans

Alchemilla hoppeana var. scintillans, commonly known as the glittering lady's mantle, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features dense, silver-white to pale green foliage that shimmers in the sunlight, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous yellow flowers in early summer, which are clustered in umbels. It thrives in well-drained, moist soils and prefers partial to full shade.

This variety is valued in gardens for its ornamental foliage and adaptability to a range of environmental conditions.
