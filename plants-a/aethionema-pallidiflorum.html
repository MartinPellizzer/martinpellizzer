Aethionema pallidiflorum

Aethionema pallidiflorum, commonly known as the paleflower rockcress, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Turkey and the Caucasus.

It is a low-growing perennial herb characterized by its compact, mat-forming growth habit. The plant features small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, typically on short pedicels. Its leaves are alternate, ovate to elliptic, and covered with a dense layer of fine hairs.

Aethionema pallidiflorum thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in alpine or subalpine environments.
