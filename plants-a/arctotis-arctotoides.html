Arctotis arctotoides

Arctotis arctotoides, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features bright yellow to orange flowers with white to pink centers, which bloom in clusters during the summer months. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, giving them a slightly fuzzy texture.

Arctotis arctotoides thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
