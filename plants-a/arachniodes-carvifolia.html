Arachniodes carvifolia

Arachniodes carvifolia, commonly known as the sword fern, is a member of the Dryopteridaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, lance-shaped fronds that are often covered in a fine, silky hair. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and stream banks. Its rhizomatous structure allows it to spread and form dense colonies.

Arachniodes carvifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in horticultural settings.
