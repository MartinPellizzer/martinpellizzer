Auricula-Ursi Apennina

Auricula-ursi apennina is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the alpine regions of southern Europe. It is known for its striking violet or pink blooms that appear in late spring. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in rocky soils at high altitudes. Its scientific name reflects its close relation to the auricula family and its geographical origin. Due to habitat loss and climate change, this species is becoming increasingly endangered.
