Aster jiangkouensis

Aster jiangkouensis, commonly known as Jiangkou aster, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers. The plant is characterized by its showy purple-blue flowers that bloom in the summer months. It typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in mountainous regions.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
