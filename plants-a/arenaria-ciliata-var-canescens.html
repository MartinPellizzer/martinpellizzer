Arenaria ciliata var. canescens

Arenaria ciliata var. canescens, commonly known as the silky sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to arctic and alpine regions.

It features small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has a mat-forming growth habit with many slender, branching stems. Its leaves are typically covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a soft, fuzzy appearance.

This variety is well adapted to cold, dry environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
