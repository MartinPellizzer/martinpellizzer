Asparagus yunnanensis

Asparagus yunnanensis, commonly known as Yunnan asparagus, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its slender, spiny stems and small, inconspicuous white flowers. The plant produces edible young shoots, which are often cultivated for culinary purposes. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in forested areas with well-drained soil.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive member of the asparagus genus.
