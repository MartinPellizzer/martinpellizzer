Aster mirabilis

Aster mirabilis, commonly known as the Marvel of Peru, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its showy, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters, with lance-shaped leaves and vibrant, tubular flowers that come in shades of red, pink, and white. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
