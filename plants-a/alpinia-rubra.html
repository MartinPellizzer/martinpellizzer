Alpinia rubra

Alpinia rubra, commonly known as the red ginger, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

The plant is characterized by its tall, erect stems and broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often a deep green. Its most striking feature is the vibrant red or orange flowers that appear at the top of the stems.
