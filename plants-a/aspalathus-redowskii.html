Aspalathus redowskii

Aspalathus redowskii, commonly known as the red owl's eye, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its small, yellow, pea-like flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are trifoliate, consisting of three oval-shaped leaflets.

Aspalathus redowskii is often found in fynbos vegetation and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
