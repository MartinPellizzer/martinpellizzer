Aristea pusilla

Aristea pusilla, commonly known as the dwarf aristea, is a small, mat-forming succulent native to southern Africa.

It features dense clusters of tiny, pale green leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink or purple during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Due to its compact size and ornamental appeal, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
