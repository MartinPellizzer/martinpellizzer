Anemone deltoidea

Anemone deltoidea, commonly known as the deltoid anemone, is a herbaceous perennial native to North America.

It features showy, pink to white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has palmate, deltoid-shaped leaves that are deeply lobed and hairy. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable plant for ecological restoration and ornamental gardening.
