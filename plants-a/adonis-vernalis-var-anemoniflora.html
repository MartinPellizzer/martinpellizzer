Adonis vernalis var. anemoniflora

Adonis vernalis var. anemoniflora, commonly known as the spring adonis, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its early blooming habit, often appearing in late winter or early spring. The plant features basal leaves that are palmately lobed and bright yellow flowers with numerous stamens. It thrives in well-drained, alkaline soils and is often found in rocky or open woodland habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
