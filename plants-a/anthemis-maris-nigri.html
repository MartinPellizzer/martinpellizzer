Anthemis maris-nigri

Anthemis maris-nigri, commonly known as the Black Sea Anthemis, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of the Black Sea.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils along the shoreline, where it thrives in saline conditions. Its flowers are small and yellow, often arranged in a flat-topped cluster.

It plays a role in stabilizing coastal dunes and providing habitat for small invertebrates.
