Aconitum Napellus Var. Splendens

Aconitum napellus var. splendens is a striking member of the buttercup family known for its vibrant blue to purple flowers. This variant of the deadly beauty aconite is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes. Native to the mountainous regions of Europe, it thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial shade. Its toxic properties make it a plant to be handled with care and respect. Despite its danger, it remains a captivating and dramatic addition to any botanical collection.
