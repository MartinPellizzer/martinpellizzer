Anchusa Obtusifolia

Blueweed, scientifically known as Anchusa obtusifolia, is a strikingly beautiful flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region. It is characterized by its vibrant blue to purple blooms and its ability to thrive in well-drained soils. This plant is often found in rocky or gravelly areas, where it adds a splash of color to the landscape. Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice for gardens and wildflower displays. Despite its name, Blueweed is not related to true weeds and is valued for its ornamental qualities.
