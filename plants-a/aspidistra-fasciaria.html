Aspidistra fasciaria

Aspidistra fasciaria, commonly known as the zebra plant, is a striking evergreen perennial native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, lance-shaped leaves with distinctive white or silver longitudinal stripes. The plant thrives in low-light conditions and is highly tolerant of shade, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening. It produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are followed by dark blue berries.

The foliage remains attractive throughout the year, adding a tropical aesthetic to any space.
