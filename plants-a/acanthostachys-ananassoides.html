Acanthostachys ananassoides

Acanthostachys ananassoides, commonly known as the pineapple lily, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces striking, spiky inflorescences resembling a pineapple. The plant features sword-shaped leaves with a waxy, glossy surface. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Acanthostachys ananassoides is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique foliage.
