Aporum kjellbergii

Aporum kjellbergii, commonly known as the Kjellberg's giant water plant, is a striking aquatic plant native to tropical regions.

It features large, broad leaves that can grow up to several feet in length, often floating on the water's surface. The plant has long, flexible stems that help it spread across water bodies. Its flowers are typically small and clustered, producing a mild fragrance that attracts pollinators.

Aporum kjellbergii plays a vital role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat and oxygen to water life.
