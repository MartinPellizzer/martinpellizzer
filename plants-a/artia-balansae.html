Artia balansae

Artia balansae, commonly known as the Balansae orchid, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, grass-like foliage and small, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of white and pale pink. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and requires specific conditions to flourish, making it a challenging species for cultivation.

Due to habitat loss and over-collection, Artia balansae is considered endangered and is protected in several conservation programs.
