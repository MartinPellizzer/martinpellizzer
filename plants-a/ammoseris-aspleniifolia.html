Ammoseris aspleniifolia

Ammoseris aspleniifolia, commonly known as the slender swamp fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped leaves that are typically less than 30 centimeters in length. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in wetlands and along stream banks. Its fronds are often described as resembling those of the genus Asplenium, hence its specific epithet.

Ammoseris aspleniifolia is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in humid conditions.
