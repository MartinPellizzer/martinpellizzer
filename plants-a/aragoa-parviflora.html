Aragoa parviflora

Aragoa parviflora, commonly known as the small-flowered aragoa, is a herbaceous perennial native to the southeastern United States.

It features slender, upright stems covered in soft, hairy foliage. The plant produces clusters of tiny, white to pale pink flowers during the summer months. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly rough texture.

Aragoa parviflora thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full sun.
