Alocasia portei

Alocasia portei, commonly known as the Black Velvet Plant, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features large, heart-shaped leaves with a deep, velvety black color that contrasts beautifully with its green veins. The plant is a member of the Araceae family, known for its unique inflorescence consisting of a spadix and spathe. Alocasia portei thrives in humid, shady environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical landscapes.
