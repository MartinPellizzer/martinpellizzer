Amaranthus clementii

Amaranthus clementii, commonly known as the Clementii amaranth, is a species of flowering plant in the amaranth family.

It is native to the southern United States and northern Mexico, where it thrives in dry, rocky soils. The plant is characterized by its vibrant red and purple foliage, which becomes more intense as the season progresses. It produces small, inconspicuous green flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences.

Amaranthus clementii is often used in ornamental gardening due to its striking appearance and drought tolerance.
