Alternanthera ficoides var. versicolor

Alternanthera ficoides var. versicolor, commonly known as the Rainbow Plant, is a striking aquatic plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful foliage that displays shades of red, pink, and orange, depending on light exposure and nutrient levels. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often variegated, adding visual interest to freshwater aquariums. It thrives in moderate to high light conditions and requires regular fertilization to maintain its brilliant coloration.

Its rapid growth rate makes it a popular choice for both beginner and experienced aquarists.
