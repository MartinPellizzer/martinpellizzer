Aloe perfoliata var. barbadensis

Aloe perfoliata var. barbadensis, commonly known as the Barbados aloe, is a succulent plant native to the Caribbean.

It features rosettes of fleshy, dark green leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow flowers that bloom in summer. Its leaves are used for their gel, which has medicinal properties.

It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.
