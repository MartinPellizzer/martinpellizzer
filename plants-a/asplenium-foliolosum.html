Asplenium foliolosum

Asplenium foliolosum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf rock fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped leaves that are typically less than 10 centimeters in length. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often growing on rock surfaces or in crevices. Its fronds are usually dark green and have a finely serrated edge.

Asplenium foliolosum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in humid indoor settings.
