Agrostis Canina Var. Mertensii

Agrostis canina var. mertensii is a lesser-known grass species that thrives in specific ecological niches. It is commonly found in temperate regions, often in moist, well-drained soils. This variety is distinguished by its fine, tufted growth and slender leaves. Despite its modest appearance, it plays an important role in local ecosystems. Its study offers insights into grassland biodiversity and adaptation strategies.
