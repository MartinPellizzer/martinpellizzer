Anthurium multisulcatum

Anthurium multisulcatum, commonly known as the ridged anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves with prominent longitudinal veins. The plant produces bright red spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe, which is typical of the Araceae family. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
