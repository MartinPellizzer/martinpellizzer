Alsodeia calophylla

Alsodeia calophylla, commonly known as the silverleaf alsodeia, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with narrow, silver-gray leaves that reflect sunlight, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in early spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.

Alsodeia calophylla is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
