Astragaloides syphilitica

Astragaloides syphilitica, commonly known as the syphilitic milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of the southwestern United States and northwestern Mexico.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its small, yellowish flowers and compound leaves. The plant has a deep root system that allows it to survive in harsh, dry environments.
