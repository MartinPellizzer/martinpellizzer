Acioa somnolens

Acioa somnolens, commonly known as the sleepy acioa, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety purple flowers that bloom during the early morning hours. The plant has heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are often used in traditional medicine for their purported calming effects. Acioa somnolens thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its unique blooming pattern and medicinal properties make it a subject of interest in both botanical research and herbal practices.
