Anthurium suramaense

Anthurium suramaense, commonly known as the Surama arrowroot, is a tropical flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves and vibrant spadix inflorescence. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical landscapes.

Anthurium suramaense is also noted for its ability to purify air, enhancing its appeal as a decorative and beneficial plant.
