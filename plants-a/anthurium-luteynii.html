Anthurium luteynii

Anthurium luteynii, commonly known as the Luteyn's arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically bright green with a waxy texture. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-shaped inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, often in shades of red, orange, or yellow. Anthurium luteynii thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its unique foliage and floral structures make it a popular choice for indoor gardening and botanical collections.
