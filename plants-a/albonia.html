Albonia

Albonia, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Africa and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its dense clusters of bright yellow, pendulous flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually ovate or elliptical in shape. Albonia is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its showy flowers and fragrant scent.

It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for tropical and subtropical climates.
