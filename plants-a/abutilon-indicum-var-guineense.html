Abutilon indicum var. guineense

Abutilon indicum var. guineense, commonly known as the velvetleaf or Indian abutilon, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and showy, lantern-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of light conditions. It has a fibrous root system and produces capsules that contain numerous small seeds.

Due to its rapid growth and adaptability, it is sometimes considered an invasive species in certain regions.
