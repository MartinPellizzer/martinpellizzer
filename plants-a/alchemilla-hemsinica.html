Alchemilla hemsinica

Alchemilla hemsinica, commonly known as the Hemsin alkanet, is a perennial herb native to Turkey.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its basal rosette of heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Its common name reflects its historical use in alchemical practices and traditional medicine.
