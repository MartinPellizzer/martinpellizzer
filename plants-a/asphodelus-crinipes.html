Asphodelus crinipes

Asphodelus crinipes, commonly known as the crown vetch, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It features upright, slender stems that produce clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is adapted to thrive in well-drained soils. It is often found in rocky or arid environments due to its drought tolerance.

Asphodelus crinipes is also known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
