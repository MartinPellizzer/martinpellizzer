Aloe × tomlinsonii

Aloe × tomlinsonii, commonly known as the hybrid aloe or the African aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with white or yellowish markings. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow flowers, which bloom in late winter or early spring. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation.
