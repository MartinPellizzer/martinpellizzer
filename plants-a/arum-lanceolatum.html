Arum lanceolatum

Arum lanceolatum, commonly known as the arrowhead plant, is a tropical herbaceous plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped, arrowhead-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a glossy texture. The plant produces striking, spadix-like inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, often in shades of white, yellow, or red. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found near water bodies.

Arum lanceolatum is popular as a decorative plant due to its unique foliage and ornamental flowers.
