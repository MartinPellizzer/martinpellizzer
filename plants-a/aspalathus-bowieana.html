Aspalathus bowieana

Aspalathus bowieana, commonly known as the Bowie's bushveld pea, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its small, pea-like flowers that range in color from pink to purple. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is adapted to the arid conditions of its native habitat. Its leaves are trifoliate, consisting of three small, oval-shaped leaflets.

Aspalathus bowieana is often found in open grasslands and along coastal areas, where it thrives in full sun.
