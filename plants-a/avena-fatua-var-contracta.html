Avena fatua var. contracta

Avena fatua var. contracta, commonly known as slender oat, is a variant of the common oat that exhibits distinct botanical characteristics.

It is a herbaceous annual plant with a compact growth habit, typically reaching heights of 30 to 60 centimeters. The leaves are narrow and linear, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface, and the inflorescence consists of a spikelet arrangement. Its seeds are small, oval, and often have a distinct hilum, which is a characteristic feature of the Avena genus.

This plant is often found in disturbed soils and is considered a weed in agricultural settings.
