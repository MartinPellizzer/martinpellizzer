Alopecurus aristatus var. monspeliensis

Alopecurus aristatus var. monspeliensis, commonly known as the spiked grass or velvet grass, is a flowering plant species native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a member of the Poaceae family, which includes grasses, and is characterized by its fine, hairy leaves and distinctive spike-like inflorescence. The plant typically grows in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in meadows, pastures, and along stream banks.
