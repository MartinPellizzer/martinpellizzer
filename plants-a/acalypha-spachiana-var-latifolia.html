Acalypha spachiana var. latifolia

Acalypha spachiana var. latifolia, commonly known as broadleaf acalypha, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white or cream markings. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically green or red in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping and as a houseplant.
