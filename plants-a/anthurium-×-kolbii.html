Anthurium × kolbii

Anthurium × kolbii, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a hybrid plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped spathes that range in color from red to orange, often with a contrasting green or yellow midrib. The plant produces small, spadix-like inflorescences that are typically green or yellow, and it thrives in warm, humid environments. Anthurium × kolbii prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

It is a popular ornamental plant due to its striking foliage and long-lasting blooms.
