Aster brevis

Aster brevis, commonly known as the short aster, is a species of flowering plant native to the northeastern United States.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 10 to 20 inches. The plant produces small, clustered blue to purple flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, giving them a soft texture.

Aster brevis thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland edges and meadows.
