Aloe brevioribus

Aloe brevioribus, commonly known as the narrow-leaved aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, upright rosettes of fleshy, narrow leaves that are typically gray-green in color. The leaves are covered in a white powdery coating, known as farina, which helps reduce water loss. It produces clusters of yellow flowers during the summer months, which are followed by red, fleshy fruits.

This plant is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
