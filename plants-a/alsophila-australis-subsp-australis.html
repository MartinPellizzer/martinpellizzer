Alsophila australis subsp. australis

Alsophila australis subsp.

australis, commonly known as the Australian tree fern, is a tall, slender fern native to southeastern Australia. It features a tall, unbranched trunk covered in persistent, fibrous roots and a crown of large, feathery fronds.

The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in rainforests or along stream banks. Its fronds are typically dark green and can grow up to 2 meters in length.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitat.
