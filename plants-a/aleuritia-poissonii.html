Aleuritia poissonii

Aleuritia poissonii, commonly known as the Chinese snowbell, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features slender, arching branches and produces clusters of small, white, bell-shaped flowers in early spring. The plant has simple, ovate leaves that turn yellow in autumn. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Aleuritia poissonii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscape design.
