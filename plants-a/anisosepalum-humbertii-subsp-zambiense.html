Anisosepalum humbertii subsp. zambiense

Anisosepalum humbertii subsp.

zambiense, commonly known as the Zambian star orchid, is a rare and striking orchid species native to the Democratic Republic of the Congo. It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive, star-shaped flowers with vibrant colors.

The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are broad and glossy, and it produces small, clustered inflorescences.
