Avena sativa subsp. diffusa

Avena sativa subsp.

diffusa, commonly known as slender oat, is a lesser-known subspecies of the common oat. It is characterized by its shorter stature and more delicate foliage compared to typical oat varieties.

This plant typically grows in temperate regions and thrives in well-drained soils. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences with numerous florets, which are adapted for wind pollination.

While not as widely cultivated as other oat subspecies, it is valued for its potential in forage and ecological restoration.
