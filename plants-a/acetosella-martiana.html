Acetosella martiana

Acetosella martiana, commonly known as the purple sorrel, is a low-growing perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

Acetosella martiana is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
