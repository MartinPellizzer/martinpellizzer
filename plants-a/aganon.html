Aganon

Aganon, commonly known as the silverleaf aganon, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Araliaceae and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves with a silvery underside. The plant produces small, white flowers that are followed by orange-red berries. Aganon thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical forests.
