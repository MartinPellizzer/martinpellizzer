Acoridium stachyodes

Acoridium stachyodes, commonly known as the Florida leatherleaf, is a member of the Acoraceae family.

It is a small, evergreen shrub native to the southeastern United States, particularly Florida and Georgia. The plant features narrow, strap-like leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-yellow in color.

The plant is adapted to wetland environments and is often found in swamps and along the edges of water bodies.
