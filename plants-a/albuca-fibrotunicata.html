Albuca fibrotunicata

Albuca fibrotunicata, commonly known as the fibrous tunica albuca, is a bulbous plant native to southern Africa.

It features striking, strap-like leaves that are often bluish-green in color. The plant produces tall, branched stems with clusters of white or pale yellow flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Albuca fibrotunicata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
