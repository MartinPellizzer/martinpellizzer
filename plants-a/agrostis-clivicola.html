Agrostis clivicola

Agrostis clivicola, commonly known as the rock grass, is a perennial grass species native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It thrives in rocky and well-drained soils, often found in alpine and subalpine habitats. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences. It is adapted to cold climates and is often used in ecological restoration projects.

Its botanical features make it an important component of high-altitude ecosystems.
