Ambaiba nivea

Ambaiba nivea, commonly known as the white ambaiba, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are usually arranged alternately along the stems. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and requires a warm, humid climate.

Ambaiba nivea is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and botanical collections.
