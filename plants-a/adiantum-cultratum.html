Adiantum cultratum

Adiantum cultratum, commonly known as the scythe fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Adiantaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds that resemble a scythe, giving it its common name. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often found in rocky crevices or along stream banks. Its leaves are usually bright green and have a distinctive, almost metallic sheen.

Adiantum cultratum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its elegant appearance.
