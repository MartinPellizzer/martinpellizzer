Aster asteroides

Aster asteroides, commonly known as the starwort, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its clustered, star-shaped flowers that typically bloom in shades of white, pink, or purple. The plant has slender, branching stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, it thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows and woodland edges.

Aster asteroides is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine for its purported health benefits.
