Arenaria cretica var. stygia

Arenaria cretica var. stygia, commonly known as the Stygian sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically thrives in sandy or rocky soils and is adapted to coastal and arid environments. The plant features small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are linear and succulent, helping it retain moisture in dry conditions.

It is often found in dunes and along the shores of the Mediterranean Sea.
