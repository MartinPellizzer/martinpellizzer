Areca coronata

Areca coronata, commonly known as the crown areca palm, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and feathery, pinnate leaves that give it a graceful appearance. The plant produces small, white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by bright red berries. Areca coronata thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant in gardens and landscapes.
