Alternanthera ficoidea var. flavogrisea

Alternanthera ficoidea var. flavogrisea, commonly known as the golden gray Alternanthera, is a striking aquatic plant native to South America.

It features vibrant, lance-shaped leaves that display a unique combination of golden yellow and gray-green hues. The plant thrives in moderate to high light conditions and prefers warm, soft water with a pH range of 6.0 to 7.5. It is often used in aquariums for its aesthetic appeal and ability to provide shelter for small fish.

Its rapid growth rate makes it suitable for both foreground and midground planting in freshwater setups.
