Attalea nucifera

Attalea nucifera, commonly known as the palm tree, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, fan-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of yellow flowers that develop into fruits containing edible seeds. Its leaves are used in traditional crafts, while its fruit is a source of palm oil.

The plant plays a significant role in both ecological and economic systems in its native regions.
