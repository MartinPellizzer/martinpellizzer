Acacia tenuifolia var. producta

Acacia tenuifolia var. producta, commonly known as the narrow-leaf wattle, is a shrub native to Australia.

It features slender, feathery leaves that are typically green with a slight blue or greyish tinge. The plant produces yellow flowers in cylindrical clusters, which bloom during the spring and early summer. It is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it drought-tolerant.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens and landscaping.
