Aconitum lycoctonum var. altissimus

Aconitum lycoctonum var. altissimus, commonly known as the tall monkshood, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its striking blue-purple flowers arranged in a spire-like cluster, which bloom in late summer. The plant has large, pleated, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a dark green color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Due to its toxic properties, it should be handled with care.
