Actinodaphne salicina

Actinodaphne salicina, commonly known as the Salina laurel, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae and is characterized by its aromatic, dark green, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. Its fruit is a small, woody drupe containing a single seed.
