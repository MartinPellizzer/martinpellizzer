Artanthe maximiliana

Artanthe maximiliana, commonly known as the purple artanthe, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its showy, violet to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its foliage is lance-shaped and often covered in a fine, fuzzy texture.

Artanthe maximiliana is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
