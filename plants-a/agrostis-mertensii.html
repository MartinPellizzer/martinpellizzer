Agrostis mertensii

Agrostis mertensii, commonly known as Mertens' bentgrass, is a perennial grass species native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its fine, tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows, grasslands, and along stream banks. Its flowering stems bear small, spike-like inflorescences with numerous tiny flowers.

Agrostis mertensii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping and turfgrass applications.
