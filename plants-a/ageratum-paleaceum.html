Ageratum paleaceum

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its showy, clustered flowers. Ageratum paleaceum is often cultivated as an ornamental plant in gardens and floral arrangements due to its vibrant appearance and ability to attract pollinators.
