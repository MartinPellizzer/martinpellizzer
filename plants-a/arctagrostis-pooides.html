Arctagrostis pooides

Arctagrostis pooides, commonly known as the slender bluegrass, is a perennial grass native to North America.

It typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in prairies, wetlands, and along stream banks. The plant has fine, blue-green blades that are often used in ornamental landscaping due to their aesthetic appeal. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny seeds.

Arctagrostis pooides is also valued for its ecological role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various wildlife species.
