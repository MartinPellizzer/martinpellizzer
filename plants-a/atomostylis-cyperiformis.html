Atomostylis cyperiformis

Atomostylis cyperiformis, commonly known as the narrowleaf flat sedge, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Cyperaceae.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in wet or seasonally flooded habitats, such as marshes and wet meadows. The plant has slender, grass-like stems and produces small, clustered inflorescences that are often subtended by leaf-like bracts. Its leaves are linear and usually have a smooth or slightly rough surface.

Atomostylis cyperiformis plays an important role in stabilizing soil in wetland ecosystems and provides habitat for various invertebrates.
