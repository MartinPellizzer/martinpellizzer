Asplenium mildbraedii

Asplenium mildbraedii, commonly known as the mildbraed's spleenwort, is a species of fern belonging to the family Aspleniaceae.

It is a small, epiphytic or lithophytic plant, typically found growing on rocks or tree trunks in humid environments. The plant has slender, creeping rhizomes and produces small, ovate to lanceolate leaves that are usually dark green in color. Its spore-producing structures, called sori, are located on the underside of the leaves, arranged in distinct lines.

Asplenium mildbraedii is native to regions of southern Africa and is often found in shaded, moist habitats.
