Armeria leucocephala var. soleirolii

Armeria leucocephala var. soleirolii, commonly known as the white-flowered thrift, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features dense clusters of white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has basal rosettes of narrow, silver-gray leaves that are covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in coastal and maritime climates.
