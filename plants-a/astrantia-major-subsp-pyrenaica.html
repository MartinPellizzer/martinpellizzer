Astrantia major subsp. pyrenaica

Astrantia major subsp.

pyrenaica, commonly known as the Pyrenean astrantia, is a perennial herb native to the Pyrenees region. It features upright, branching stems that bear clusters of white to pinkish flowers.

The plant has palmate, heart-shaped leaves that are often hairy and covered in fine pubescence. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodlands and mountainous areas.

Its botanical characteristics make it a distinctive and ornamental addition to gardens and natural habitats.
