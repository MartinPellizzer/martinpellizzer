Anthyllis vulneraria f. montenegrina

Anthyllis vulneraria f. montenegrina, commonly known as the Montenegrin scarlet pimpernel, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is a low-growing perennial herb characterized by its small, bright yellow flowers and trifoliate leaves. This plant is native to the Balkan Peninsula, particularly found in rocky and calcareous soils of Montenegro. It thrives in sunny, well-drained environments and is often seen in alpine and subalpine regions.

The species is notable for its medicinal properties, historically used for its healing capabilities.
