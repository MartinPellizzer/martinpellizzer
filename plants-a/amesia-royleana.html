Amesia royleana

Amesia royleana, commonly known as the alpine sandwort, is a flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with small, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has a mat-forming growth habit, with narrow, linear leaves that are densely packed. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is adapted to cold, arid environments.

Amesia royleana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in rock gardens.
