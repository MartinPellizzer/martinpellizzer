Alsophila hyacinthei var. humblotii

Alsophila hyacinthei var. humblotii, commonly known as the Humblot's tree fern, is a species native to Madagascar.

It is a member of the Cyatheaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate fronds. The plant exhibits a distinctive spiral arrangement of its fronds, which gives it a graceful, ornamental appearance. Due to its unique morphology and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists alike.
