Antimima Compressa

Antimima compressa is a rare and enigmatic plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa. It thrives in harsh, dry environments where few other species can survive. Its unique appearance, featuring dense, compressed foliage, makes it easily identifiable. Despite its resilience, it remains under threat due to habitat loss and over-collection. This plant offers valuable insights into adaptation strategies in extreme climates.
