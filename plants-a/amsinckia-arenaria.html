Amsinckia arenaria

Amsinckia arenaria, commonly known as the desert smokeplant, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of North America.

It is a low-growing annual herb with hairy stems and small, clustered leaves. The plant produces delicate, pink to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in sandy or rocky soils and is adapted to withstand drought conditions.

Amsinckia arenaria is often found in desert scrub and grassland habitats.
