Acalypha commersoniana var. discolor

Acalypha commersoniana var. discolor, commonly known as the variegated ironweed, is a strikingly ornamental plant native to South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that features strikingly colored leaves with variegation of white, yellow, and green. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in dense, spike-like clusters. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and landscape design.
