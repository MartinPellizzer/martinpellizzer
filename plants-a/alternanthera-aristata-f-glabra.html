Alternanthera aristata f. glabra

Alternanthera aristata f. glabra, commonly known as the smooth alternanthera, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety foliage that often displays vibrant red, purple, or pink hues. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves with a smooth texture, distinguishing it from other varieties. It thrives in warm, humid conditions and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

This ornamental plant is popular in water gardens and as a houseplant due to its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.
