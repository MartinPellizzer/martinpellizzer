Agrostophyllum spinuliferum subsp. misoense

Agrostophyllum spinuliferum subsp.

misoense, commonly known as the Misoense grass. It is a perennial herb native to southern Africa, particularly South Africa.

The plant is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences with pale yellow to greenish flowers.

This subspecies is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, often found in sandy soils.
