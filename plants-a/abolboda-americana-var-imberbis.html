Abolboda americana var. imberbis

Abolboda americana var. imberbis, commonly known as the smoothleaf abolboda, is a herbaceous perennial native to the southeastern United States.

It features lance-shaped leaves that are typically smooth along the margins, distinguishing it from other varieties. The plant produces small, white to pale pink flowers in clusters during the spring and summer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full sunlight.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable addition to native plant gardens and ecological restoration projects.
