Alsophila glaziovii

Alsophila glaziovii, commonly known as the Glaziov's tree fern, is a species native to tropical and subtropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its rhizome is thick and fibrous, supporting its upright growth habit.
