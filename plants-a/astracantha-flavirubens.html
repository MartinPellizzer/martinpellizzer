Astracantha flavirubens

Astracantha flavirubens, commonly known as the red-flowered burweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Amaranthaceae.

It is native to southern Africa and is characterized by its distinctive, spiny bracts and showy, yellow-orange flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. Its seeds are enclosed in a hairy, three-lobed capsule, which aids in seed dispersal by wind.

Astracantha flavirubens is often used in ornamental gardening due to its vibrant color and unique appearance.
