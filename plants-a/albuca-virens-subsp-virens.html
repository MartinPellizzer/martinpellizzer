Albuca virens subsp. virens

Albuca virens subsp.

virens, commonly known as the greenbulb, is a perennial bulbous plant native to southern Africa. It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of white to pale yellow flowers.

The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and is often grown as an ornamental in gardens. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade. Albuca virens subsp.

virens is valued for its attractive foliage and ability to thrive in moderate climates.
