Aerides crispa var. lindleyana

Aerides crispa var. lindleyana, commonly known as the Lindley's crested vanda, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, pendant inflorescences and showy, fragrant flowers that come in a variety of colors including white, yellow, and pink. The plant has strap-like leaves that are often covered in a dense, velvety coating. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in greenhouses.
