Aglaonema flemingianum

Aglaonema flemingianum, commonly known as the Fleming's Chinese evergreen, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated foliage that features shades of green, white, and pink. The plant thrives in low-light conditions and requires moderate humidity to flourish. Its leaves are typically broad and lance-shaped, with a waxy texture that helps it retain moisture.

Aglaonema flemingianum is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and air-purifying properties.
