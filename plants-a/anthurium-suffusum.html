Anthurium suffusum

Anthurium suffusum, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a reddish tinge. The plant produces striking spathes that are often bright red or orange, enclosing a spadix that bears small, inconspicuous flowers. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Anthurium suffusum is a popular ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
