Aptosimum lineare var. acaule

Aptosimum lineare var. acaule, commonly known as the slender leaf tansy, is a variant of the tansy plant characterized by its lack of a distinct stem.

It is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family, known for its aromatic foliage and yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in temperate regions. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often with a distinct white vein, and it produces small, clustered flowers in the summer.

This variety is sometimes used in herbal medicine for its purported digestive and antiseptic properties.
