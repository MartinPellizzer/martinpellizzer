Actinodaphne shendurunii

Actinodaphne shendurunii, commonly known as the Shendurunii camphor tree, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is native to the Western Ghats of India, where it thrives in moist, tropical forest environments. The plant is characterized by its evergreen foliage and aromatic leaves, which contain essential oils. Its flowers are small and clustered, typically appearing in the axils of the leaves.

This species is valued for its timber and is also used in traditional medicine due to its medicinal properties.
