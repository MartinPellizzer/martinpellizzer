Atylus heterophyllus

Atylus heterophyllus, commonly known as the variable leaf fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive variability in leaf shape and size, which can change depending on environmental conditions. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its fronds are often bipinnate, with a delicate, feathery appearance.

Atylus heterophyllus is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
