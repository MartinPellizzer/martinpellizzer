Anthurium georgetatei

Anthurium georgetatei, commonly known as the Georgeta's anthurium, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped spathe that ranges in color from deep red to orange, often with contrasting green veins. The plant produces clusters of small, spadix-like flowers that are typically yellow or green, surrounded by the showy spathe. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium georgetatei is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in greenhouse settings.
