Alyssum bilimekii

Alyssum bilimekii, commonly known as the Turkish alyssum, is a flowering plant native to Turkey.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. This species is characterized by its small, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. Alyssum bilimekii is often found in rocky and arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental value make it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
