Anthurium rioacimense

Anthurium rioacimense, commonly known as the Rioacimense arrowhead, is a species of flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its unique inflorescence consisting of a spadix surrounded by a spathe. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. It produces striking, colorful spathes that can range from red to orange, often with contrasting veining.

Anthurium rioacimense is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.
