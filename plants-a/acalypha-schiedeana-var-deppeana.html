Acalypha schiedeana var. deppeana

Acalypha schiedeana var. deppeana, commonly known as the silky acalypha, is a shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its soft, woolly leaves that are often covered in fine, silky hairs. The plant produces small, clustered greenish-white flowers that are typically borne in dense inflorescences. Its foliage is highly ornamental, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
