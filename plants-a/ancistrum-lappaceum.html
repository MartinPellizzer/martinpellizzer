Ancistrum lappaceum

Ancistrum lappaceum, commonly known as the Lappaceum arrowroot, is a species of flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its striking, colorful spathes and clustered inflorescences. The plant features arrow-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white and green patterns. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for tropical gardens and indoor collections.
