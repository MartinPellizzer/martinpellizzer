Acaena adscendens subsp. cardiostemon

Acaena adscendens subsp.

cardiostemon, commonly known as the heart-stamen acaena, is a shrub native to New Zealand. It belongs to the family Rosaceae and is characterized by its dense, spiny growth habit.

The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are clustered in heads, followed by distinctive, three-lobed bracts. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a hairy texture.

This subspecies is adapted to a variety of soil types and is often found in coastal and inland areas of New Zealand.
