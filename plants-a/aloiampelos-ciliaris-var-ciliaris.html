Aloiampelos ciliaris var. ciliaris

Aloiampelos ciliaris var. ciliaris, commonly known as the silver dollar vine, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a climbing plant with trailing stems that produce clusters of small, white, star-shaped flowers. The leaves are ovate, with a silvery-white margin that gives the plant its common name. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This species is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and delicate blooms.
