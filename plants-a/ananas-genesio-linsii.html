Ananas genesio-linsii

Ananas genesio-linsii, commonly known as the pineapple lily, is a striking tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the genus Ananas, which is also the source of the fruit pineapple. The plant features tall, strap-like leaves that are often brightly colored, ranging from green to red or purple. Its flowers are small and clustered, typically appearing in shades of yellow or orange.

Ananas genesio-linsii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
