Acianthera longicaulis

Acianthera longicaulis, commonly known as the long-stemmed acianthera, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its slender, elongated stems and clusters of small, pale green to white flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are narrow and arranged along the stem, contributing to its elegant appearance.

Acianthera longicaulis is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
