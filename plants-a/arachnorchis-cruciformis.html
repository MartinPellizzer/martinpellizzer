Arachnorchis cruciformis

Arachnorchis cruciformis, commonly known as the cruciform spider-orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Australia.

It is characterized by its unique, spider-like appearance with long, slender petals and sepals. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and thrives in sunny to partially shaded environments. Its flowers, which bloom in spring, are usually white or pale yellow and have a distinctive cruciform shape.

Arachnorchis cruciformis is a rare and specialized species, often found in specific microhabitats across the Australian landscape.
