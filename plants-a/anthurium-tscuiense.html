Anthurium tscuiense

Anthurium tscuiense, commonly known as the Tscuiense Arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to the lowland rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a glossy texture. The plant produces striking spadix inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, which can range from red to orange. It prefers bright, indirect light and consistently moist, well-draining soil.

Anthurium tscuiense is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
