Amaryllis × hoodsii

Amaryllis × hoodsii, commonly known as the hybrid amaryllis, is a striking bulbous plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its tall, erect stems that bear large, showy flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant produces large, strap-like leaves that are often used for ornamental purposes in gardens and as houseplants. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Amaryllis × hoodsii is prized for its vibrant blooms and ease of cultivation, making it a popular choice for gardeners and florists alike.
