Artemisia filatovae

Artemisia filatovae, commonly known as Filatov's wormwood, is a shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, gray-green foliage. The plant produces small, yellowish flower heads that are typically clustered in compact inflorescences. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often growing at high elevations.

Artemisia filatovae is valued for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
