Adenostyles hybrida

Adenostyles hybrida, commonly known as the hybrid adenostyle, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features basal leaves that are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are often hairy as well. Its flowers are small and clustered in dense, spike-like inflorescences, often with pink or purple hues.

Adenostyles hybrida is adapted to well-drained soils and full sun, making it a common sight in rocky or coastal habitats.
