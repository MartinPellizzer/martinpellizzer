Aster miyagii

Aster miyagii, commonly known as the Miyagi aster, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It is characterized by its daisy-like flowers with white petals and a yellow center. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes many other flowering plants.

Aster miyagii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
