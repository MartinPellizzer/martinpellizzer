Aubrieta deltoidea var. microphylla

Aubrieta deltoidea var. microphylla, commonly known as the dwarf aubrieta, is a low-growing, mat-forming perennial native to the Mediterranean region.

It features compact, rounded foliage with small, dark green leaves that are densely packed. The plant produces vibrant clusters of four-petaled flowers in shades of yellow, orange, or red, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Due to its hardiness and ornamental value, it is a popular choice for gardeners seeking a resilient, colorful ground cover.
