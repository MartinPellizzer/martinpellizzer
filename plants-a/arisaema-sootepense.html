Arisaema sootepense

Arisaema sootepense, commonly known as the Sootep arrowhead, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its unique inflorescence consisting of a spadix surrounded by a spathe. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands. Its leaves are arrowhead-shaped, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers.

Arisaema sootepense is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine in some regions.
