Alchemilla strigosula var. carelica

Alchemilla strigosula var. carelica, commonly known as the Carelian lady's mantle, is a low-growing perennial plant native to northern Europe.

It features dense, silver-gray foliage that is covered in soft, woolly hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous yellow flowers in early summer, which are clustered in loose panicles. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas or along streambanks.

Its medicinal properties have been historically used in traditional herbal remedies.
