Aster tenellus

Aster tenellus, commonly known as the slender aster, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that consist of both ray and disk flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and woodlands. It produces small, white to pale blue flowers that bloom in the late summer and early fall.

Aster tenellus is often used in native plant gardens due to its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
