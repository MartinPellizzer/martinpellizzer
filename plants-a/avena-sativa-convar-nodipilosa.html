Avena sativa convar. nodipilosa

Avena sativa convar. nodipilosa, commonly known as slender oat, is a botanical variant of the common oat species.

It is characterized by its shorter, more compact growth habit compared to typical oats. The plant features slender stems that are often hairy, with narrow, grass-like leaves. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences bearing numerous florets.

This variant is adapted to cooler climates and is often used in ecological restoration and as a forage crop.
