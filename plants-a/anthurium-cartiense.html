Anthurium cartiense

Anthurium cartiense, commonly known as the Cartesian arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to Central America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped leaves and striking spadix inflorescence. The plant produces small, greenish flowers surrounded by a colorful spathe, which is often the most noticeable part of the plant. It thrives in humid, warm environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Anthurium cartiense is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.
