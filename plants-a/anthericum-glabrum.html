Anthericum glabrum

Anthericum glabrum, commonly known as the smooth anthericum, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and western Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its spatulate leaves and distinctive inflorescence. The plant features a short rhizome with erect, lanceolate leaves that are smooth and glossy. Its flowers are borne on a spadix surrounded by a modified leaf called a spathe, typical of the Araceae family.

Anthericum glabrum is often found in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.
