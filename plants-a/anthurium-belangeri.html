Anthurium belangeri

Anthurium belangeri, commonly known as the Belanger's anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped spathe that ranges in color from deep red to orange, often with a contrasting green spadix. The plant thrives in high humidity and indirect light, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation. Its foliage consists of large, glossy, dark green leaves that contribute to its ornamental appeal.

Anthurium belangeri is valued not only for its aesthetic qualities but also for its ability to purify the air in enclosed spaces.
