Alchornea costaricensis

Alchornea costaricensis, commonly known as the Costa Rican beauty leaf, is a shrub native to Central America.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its palmate leaves with five to seven lobes. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellowish in color. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in moist, shaded areas.
