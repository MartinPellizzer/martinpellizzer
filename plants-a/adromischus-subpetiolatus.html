Adromischus subpetiolatus

Adromischus subpetiolatus, commonly known as the "Coral Cactus," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features small, fleshy, spiny leaves that are typically red or pink in color, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, often blooming in summer. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves.

Adromischus subpetiolatus is a low-maintenance plant that thrives in bright, indirect sunlight and well-draining soil.
