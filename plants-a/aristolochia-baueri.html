Aristolochia baueri

Aristolochia baueri, commonly known as the Bauer's Dutchman's pipe, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its unique, pipe-shaped flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color. The plant belongs to the Aristolochiaceae family and is known for its complex, twisted floral structure. Its leaves are heart-shaped and often have a rough texture.

The plant contains various chemical compounds, some of which have been studied for their potential medicinal properties.
