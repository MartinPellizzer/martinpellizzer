Apluda blatteri

Apluda blatteri, commonly known as the slender grassvetch, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, trifoliate leaves. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are typically pale green to yellow in color. It is native to regions of southern Africa and thrives in dry, open habitats.

Apluda blatteri is often found in grasslands and along rocky outcrops, where it plays a minor role in local ecosystems.
