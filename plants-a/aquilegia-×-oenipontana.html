Aquilegia × oenipontana

Aquilegia × oenipontana, commonly known as the hybrid Columbine, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Ranunculaceae family.

It is characterized by its showy, spurred flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, yellow, and orange. The plant typically grows to a height of 30–60 cm and features palmate leaves with three to five leaflets. Native to the mountainous regions of Europe, it thrives in well-drained soils and partial to full sunlight.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and wildflower habitats.
