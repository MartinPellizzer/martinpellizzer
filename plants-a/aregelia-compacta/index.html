Aregelia Compacta

Compact aregelia, scientifically known as Aregelia compacta, is a unique and resilient plant species native to arid regions. It thrives in harsh environments where water is scarce and temperatures fluctuate widely. The plant's compact growth habit allows it to conserve moisture and withstand extreme conditions. Its small, dense foliage makes it an attractive addition to rock gardens and xeriscaping projects. Despite its tough exterior, the Compact aregelia offers a striking visual presence and is valued for its adaptability and low maintenance requirements.
