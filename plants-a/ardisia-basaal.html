Ardisia basaal

Ardisia basaal, commonly known as the red-berried evergreen, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, dark green leaves and produces small, white flowers followed by bright red berries. The plant is evergreen, meaning it retains its foliage year-round. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade.

Ardisia basaal is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.
