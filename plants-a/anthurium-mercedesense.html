Anthurium mercedesense

Anthurium mercedesense, commonly known as the Mercedes' anthurium, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Colombia.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a velvety texture. The plant produces vibrant red spathes that enclose a spadix, a feature typical of many anthurium species. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This ornamental plant is popular in horticulture for its aesthetic appeal and air-purifying qualities.
