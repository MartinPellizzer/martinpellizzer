Ayenia benensis

Ayenia benensis, commonly known as the Benensian thistle, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, silver-green leaves and produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in dry, open habitats and is often found in grasslands and disturbed soils.

Ayenia benensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been studied for its potential ecological benefits.
