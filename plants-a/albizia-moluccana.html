Albizia moluccana

Albizia moluccana, commonly known as the Moluccan Albizia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant has a fibrous root system that helps in soil stabilization and nitrogen fixation. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in coastal areas and disturbed soils.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in warm regions.
