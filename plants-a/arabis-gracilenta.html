Arabis gracilenta

Arabis gracilenta, commonly known as slender rockcress, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other flowering plants with distinctive four-petaled flowers. Arabis gracilenta is often cultivated in rock gardens due to its ornamental value and hardy nature.
