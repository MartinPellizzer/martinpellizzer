Astracantha roseocalycina

Astracantha roseocalycina, commonly known as the rose calyx star-thorn, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features spiny, woody stems and produces showy, pink to red flowers that bloom in spring. Its leaves are typically narrow and alternate, with a rough, hairy texture.

Astracantha roseocalycina is often found in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.
