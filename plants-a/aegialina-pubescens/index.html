Aegialina Pubescens

Aegialina pubescens is a unique flowering plant known for its distinctive appearance and ecological significance. It thrives in coastal environments, often found along sandy shores and tidal zones. The plant features hairy leaves and produces small, delicate flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its ability to withstand salt spray and harsh coastal conditions makes it an important part of beach ecosystems. This species plays a vital role in stabilizing dunes and supporting local biodiversity.
