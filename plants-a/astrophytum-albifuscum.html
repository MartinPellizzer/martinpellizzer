Astrophytum albifuscum

Astrophytum albifuscum, commonly known as the white-furred barrel cactus, is a compact, globular cactus native to the arid regions of Mexico.

It features a short, ribbed stem covered in dense, white to pale gray spines that provide protection from herbivores and intense sunlight. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months, which bloom at the top of the stem. Its succulent nature allows it to store water efficiently, making it well adapted to desert environments.

Astrophytum albifuscum is popular among cactus enthusiasts for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
