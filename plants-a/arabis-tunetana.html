Arabis tunetana

Arabis tunetana, commonly known as the Tunisian rockcress, is a flowering plant native to North Africa.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments. It produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in early spring.

Its leaves are usually basal, lobed, and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
