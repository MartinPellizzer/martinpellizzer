Arabis stenoloba

Arabis stenoloba, commonly known as the narrow-leaved rockcress, is a perennial flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other flowering plants with distinctive four-petaled flowers. Arabis stenoloba is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in rock gardens or alpine settings.
