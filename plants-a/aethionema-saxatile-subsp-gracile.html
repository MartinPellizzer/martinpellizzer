Aethionema saxatile subsp. gracile

Aethionema saxatile subsp.

gracile, commonly known as the slender rockcress, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region. It is a low-growing perennial with small, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of yellow flowers.

The plant thrives in rocky or gravelly soils and is adapted to dry, sunny environments. Its compact form and ornamental flowers make it suitable for rock gardens and alpine settings.

It is also valued for its ability to tolerate poor soil conditions and drought.
