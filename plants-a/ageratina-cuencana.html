Ageratina cuencana

Ageratina cuencana, commonly known as the Cuencan blueweed, is a flowering plant native to the Andean region of South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Ageratina cuencana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
