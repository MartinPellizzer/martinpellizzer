Aechmea × fulgolamarchei

Aechmea × fulgolamarchei, commonly known as the flaming bromeliad, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Bromeliaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking, brightly colored inflorescence that can range from red to orange, often resembling a flame. The plant features a rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves that form a central rosette, capable of collecting water. Native to South America, it thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light.

Its unique appearance and relatively low maintenance make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
