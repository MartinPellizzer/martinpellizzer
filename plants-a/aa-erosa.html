Aa erosa

Aa erosa, commonly known as the slender antelope's horn, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its delicate, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, while its stems are often branched and somewhat woody.

Aa erosa is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens due to its attractive floral display.
