Artemisia obtusiloba var. tuvinica

Artemisia obtusiloba var. tuvinica, commonly known as Tuvinian wormwood, is a perennial herb native to Central Asia.

It is characterized by its gray-green foliage and aromatic, finely divided leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flower heads that are typical of the Asteraceae family. It thrives in dry, rocky habitats and is often found in alpine or steppe regions.

Due to its medicinal properties, it has been used in traditional medicine for various ailments.
