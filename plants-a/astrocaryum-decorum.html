Astrocaryum decorum

Astrocaryum decorum, commonly known as the golden palm, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnately compound leaves that give it a lush, feathery appearance. The plant produces yellowish-orange fruits that are edible and often consumed by local communities. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in warm climates.

Astrocaryum decorum also plays a role in supporting local wildlife, including birds and insects.
