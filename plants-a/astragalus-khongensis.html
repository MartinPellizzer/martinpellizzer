Astragalus khongensis

Astragalus khongensis, commonly known as the Khongensis milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, pea-like flowers. The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. It has a taproot system that allows it to access deep water sources, and its flowers are often pale blue or violet in color.

Astragalus khongensis is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal remedies.
