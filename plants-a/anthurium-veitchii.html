Anthurium veitchii

Anthurium veitchii, commonly known as the Veitch's anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a metallic sheen. The plant produces striking spadices, which are the inflorescences that consist of a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe. It thrives in humid, warm environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium veitchii is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
