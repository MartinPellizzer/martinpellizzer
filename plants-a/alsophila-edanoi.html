Alsophila edanoi

Alsophila edanoi, commonly known as the Edanoi tree fern, is a species native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds that can reach several meters in length. The plant has a robust rhizome system that allows it to thrive in moist, shaded environments. Its spores are produced on the undersides of its fertile fronds, contributing to its reproductive strategy.

Alsophila edanoi plays an important ecological role in its native habitats, providing shelter and food for various wildlife species.
