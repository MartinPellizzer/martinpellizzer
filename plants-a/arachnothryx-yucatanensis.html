Arachnothryx yucatanensis

Arachnothryx yucatanensis, commonly known as the Yucatan spider tree, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Mexico and Central America.

It is a small to medium-sized tree that typically grows in humid lowland areas. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers, which are followed by distinctive, spiny fruits. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a rough texture.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens.
