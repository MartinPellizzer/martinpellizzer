Acalypha commersoniana var. gracilipes

Acalypha commersoniana var. gracilipes, commonly known as the slender croton, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, palmate leaves that are often tinged with red or purple hues. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value is largely due to its unique foliage, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
