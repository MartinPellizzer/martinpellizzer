Astragalus wulumuquanus

Astragalus wulumuquanus, commonly known as the Wulumuqi milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its upright growth habit and compound leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in racemes, which are typical of the genus Astragalus. It is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid conditions.
