Artemisia liaotungensis

Artemisia liaotungensis, commonly known as the Liaotung wormwood, is a perennial herb native to eastern China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, gray-green foliage. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in composite heads, typical of the family. It thrives in dry, well-drained soils and is often found in temperate regions.

Due to its medicinal properties, it has been used in traditional Chinese medicine for various ailments.
