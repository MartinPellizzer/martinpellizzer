Arenaria nuttallii var. gracilipes

Arenaria nuttallii var. gracilipes, commonly known as slender sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the western United States.

It typically thrives in sandy or gravelly soils, often found in arid or semi-arid environments. The plant produces small, white or pink flowers during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it has a compact growth habit.

It is often used in xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and ornamental value.
