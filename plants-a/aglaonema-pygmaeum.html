Aglaonema pygmaeum

Aglaonema pygmaeum, commonly known as the Chinese Evergreen or dwarf aglaonema, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features dark green, waxy leaves with striking, variegated patterns of white and cream, adding visual interest to indoor spaces. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions, making it an excellent choice for indoor gardening. It prefers well-draining soil and moderate humidity, with regular watering to maintain its health.

Aglaonema pygmaeum is also noted for its air-purifying qualities, contributing to improved indoor air quality.
