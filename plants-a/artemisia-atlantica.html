Artemisia atlantica

Artemisia atlantica, commonly known as Atlantic wormwood, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically grows in rocky, dry, and sunny environments, often found in coastal areas. The plant has gray-green, aromatic leaves and produces small yellow flowers in the summer. It is valued for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional remedies.

Its essential oils contain compounds with antimicrobial and anti-inflammatory effects.
