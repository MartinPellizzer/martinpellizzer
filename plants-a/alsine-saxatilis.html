Alsine saxatilis

Alsine saxatilis, commonly known as rock sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to rocky and coastal areas.

It typically has small, fleshy leaves that help it retain moisture in arid environments. The plant produces clusters of tiny white flowers, which bloom in early spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in crevices or on slopes.

Alsine saxatilis is a hardy species adapted to harsh, sunny conditions.
