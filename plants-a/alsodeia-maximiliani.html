Alsodeia maximiliani

Alsodeia maximiliani, commonly known as the narrow-leaved sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to dry, sunny environments. The plant features small, narrow leaves and produces clusters of tiny, white or pale pink flowers. It is often found in coastal areas and is valued for its ornamental appeal in garden settings.
