Amyema maytenifolia

Amyema maytenifolia, commonly known as the Australian flame tree, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, bright red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. The plant has compound leaves that are opposite and often have a leathery texture. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular ornamental species.

Amyema maytenifolia is also valued for its ability to provide habitat and food for native wildlife.
