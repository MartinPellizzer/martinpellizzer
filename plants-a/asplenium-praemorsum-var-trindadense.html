Asplenium praemorsum var. trindadense

Asplenium praemorsum var. trindadense, commonly known as the Trinidad spleenwort, is a fern belonging to the family Aspleniaceae.

It is a small, epiphytic fern that typically grows on tree trunks or rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant has finely divided, lance-shaped fronds that are usually dark green and glossy. It produces sporangia on the underside of its leaves, which are clustered in sori.

This variety is native to the Caribbean, particularly Trinidad and Tobago, and is often found in tropical rainforests.
