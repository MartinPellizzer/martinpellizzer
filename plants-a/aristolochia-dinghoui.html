Aristolochia dinghoui

Aristolochia dinghoui, commonly known as the Guangxi arrowhead vine, is a flowering plant native to southern China.

It belongs to the Aristolochiaceae family and is characterized by its unique, tubular flowers that resemble a heart shape. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It is often found growing in moist, shaded environments in mountainous regions.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
