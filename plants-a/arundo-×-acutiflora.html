Arundo × acutiflora

Arundo × acutiflora, commonly known as the hybrid reed, is a tall, grass-like plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features narrow, sword-shaped leaves that are often bluish-green in color, and it produces tall, branched inflorescences with small, greenish-white flowers. The plant is highly adaptable, thriving in a variety of soil types and climates, making it a common ornamental and landscaping plant. It is also used in phytoremediation due to its ability to absorb heavy metals from the soil.

However, it can become invasive in certain environments if not properly managed.
