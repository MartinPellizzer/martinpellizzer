Asplenium lunulatum var. setosum

Asplenium lunulatum var. setosum, commonly known as the hairy shield fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen fronds that are typically dark green with a velvety texture. The plant features small, rounded pinnae that are often covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated as a houseplant.
