Antschar

Antschar, commonly known as the African violet, is a tropical plant native to East Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves and showy, tubular flowers. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its foliage is often velvety and comes in various shades of green, while the flowers range from purple to white.

Antschar is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
