Aphelandra roezlii

Aphelandra roezlii, commonly known as the golden flame plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, elongated leaves that are often variegated with yellow and green patterns. The plant produces fragrant, lantern-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange, which bloom during the summer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

Aphelandra roezlii is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
