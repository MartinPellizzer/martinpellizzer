Aloe brownii

Aloe brownii, commonly known as the Brown's aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy, pale green leaves that are covered in a white powdery coating called farina. The plant produces tall, branched stems with yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Aloe brownii is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to store water in its leaves.
