Anthurium luteospathum

Anthurium luteospathum, commonly known as the yellow-veined anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves with prominent yellow veins against a dark green background. The plant produces bright red spadices, which are the modified leaves that enclose the actual flowers. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.

Anthurium luteospathum is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
