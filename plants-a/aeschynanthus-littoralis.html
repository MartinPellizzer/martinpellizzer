Aeschynanthus littoralis, commonly known as the coastal twinflower, is a member of the Gesneriaceae family and is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

Aeschynanthus littoralis is often found growing near coastal areas, which suggests it has some degree of salt tolerance.
