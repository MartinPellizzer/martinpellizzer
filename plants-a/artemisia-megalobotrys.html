Artemisia megalobotrys

Artemisia megalobotrys, commonly known as the giant sagebrush, is a shrub native to arid regions of North America.

It is characterized by its aromatic, silver-gray foliage and small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in desert and semi-desert environments. Its leaves contain volatile oils that contribute to its strong, minty scent.

Artemisia megalobotrys plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various desert wildlife.
