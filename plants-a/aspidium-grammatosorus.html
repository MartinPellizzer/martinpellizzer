Aspidium grammatosorus

Aspidium grammatosorus, commonly known as the common holly fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped fronds that are typically bright green and have a finely serrated margin. The plant produces small, round sporangia on the underside of its leaves, which are clustered in strobila. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Aspidium grammatosorus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
