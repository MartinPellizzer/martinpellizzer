Aneilema hockii var. rhizomatosum

Aneilema hockii var. rhizomatosum, commonly known as the rhizomatous aneilema, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and large, glossy, dark green leaves that often have a waxy coating. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread readily, making it both ornamental and potentially invasive in certain regions.
