Anathallis candida

Anathallis candida, commonly known as the white spider orchid, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, spider-like appearance with elongated, white flowers. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in tropical rainforests. Its flowers are pollinated by specific species of flies, which are attracted to its scent and color.

Anathallis candida is valued for its unique morphology and ecological significance in its native habitat.
