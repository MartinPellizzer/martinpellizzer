Alstroemeria chillanensis

Alstroemeria chillanensis, commonly known as the Chillan lily, is a flowering plant native to Chile.

It belongs to the family Alstroemeriaceae and is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant typically grows in rocky or coastal areas, thriving in well-drained soils. It has strap-like leaves and produces clusters of showy blooms that attract pollinators.

Alstroemeria chillanensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
