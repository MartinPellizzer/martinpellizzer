Achyranthes gilsonii

Achyranthes gilsonii, commonly known as the African knotweed, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves with serrated edges and produces small, greenish-yellow flowers in clusters. Its root system is fibrous and can become invasive in certain environments.

The plant is often found in moist, disturbed soils along riverbanks and in grasslands.
