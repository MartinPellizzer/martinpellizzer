Alysicarpus pokleanus

Alysicarpus pokleanus, commonly known as the slender cowpea, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

Its fruit is a legume, containing several seeds, and it is adapted to grow in tropical and subtropical regions.

Alysicarpus pokleanus is often found in disturbed soils and is used in some traditional agricultural systems.
