Adonis eriocalycina

Adonis eriocalycina, commonly known as the narrow-leaved adonis, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils at high altitudes. The plant produces showy, yellowish-orange flowers with a distinctive, narrow calyx. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often with a slight hairy texture.

Adonis eriocalycina is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its early spring blooms.
