Arundinaria simonii f. laydekeri

Arundinaria simonii f. laydekeri, commonly known as the Laydeker's bamboo, is a variant of the Chinese bamboo species.

It is characterized by its slender, gracefully arching stems that form dense clumps. The plant produces small, pale green leaves that are arranged in a fan-like pattern. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade.

This ornamental bamboo is valued for its elegant appearance and adaptability to various garden conditions.
