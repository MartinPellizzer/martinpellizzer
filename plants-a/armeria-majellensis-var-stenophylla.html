Armeria majellensis var. stenophylla

Armeria majellensis var. stenophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leaved thrift, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and produces clusters of pink or white flowers in spring. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and hardiness in temperate climates.
