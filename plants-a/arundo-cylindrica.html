Arundo cylindrica

Arundo cylindrica, commonly known as the narrowleaf reed, is a tall, perennial grass native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, cylindrical leaves that are typically blue-green in color, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, fluffy seeds that are dispersed by wind, aiding in its spread. It thrives in moist environments such as wetlands and marshes, where it can form dense stands.

Arundo cylindrica is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its graceful form and drought tolerance.
