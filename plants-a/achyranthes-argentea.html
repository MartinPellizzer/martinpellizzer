Achyranthes argentea

Achyranthes argentea, commonly known as silverleaf smartweed, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its silver-gray leaves, which are covered in fine, silky hairs. The plant produces small, clustered pink to reddish flowers that bloom in summer. It typically grows in moist soils along streambanks and in wetlands.

Achyranthes argentea is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
