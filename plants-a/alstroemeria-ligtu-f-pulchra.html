Alstroemeria ligtu f. pulchra

Alstroemeria ligtu f. pulchra, commonly known as the red alstroemeria, is a striking flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the family Alstroemeriaceae and is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that come in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant features strap-like leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and floral arrangements. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for coastal and temperate climates.

Its botanical beauty and adaptability contribute to its popularity in both horticulture and botanical study.
