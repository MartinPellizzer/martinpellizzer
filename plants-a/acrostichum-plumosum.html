Acrostichum plumosum

Acrostichum plumosum, commonly known as the silky fern, is a tropical fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, feathery fronds that are covered in soft, silky hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands. Its reproductive structures, including spore-bearing clusters, are located on the underside of its fronds.

Acrostichum plumosum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
