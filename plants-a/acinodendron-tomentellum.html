Acinodendron tomentellum

Acinodendron tomentellum, commonly known as the woolly acinodendron, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a shrub characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for study in plant morphology and ecology.
