Avena Spica-Venti

Avena spica-venti is a unique and resilient plant known for its ability to thrive in harsh environments. It is commonly found in arid and semi-arid regions where other plants struggle to survive. The plant's name, which translates to "wind-stalk," reflects its characteristic response to wind forces. Its structure is adapted to withstand strong gusts, with a flexible stem and reduced leaves. This species plays an important role in stabilizing soil and supporting local ecosystems.
