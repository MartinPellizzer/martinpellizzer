Acnistus parviflorus var. arboreus

Acnistus parviflorus var. arboreus, commonly known as the silky tree or silky acnistus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a small, deciduous tree or shrub that typically grows to a height of about 3 to 5 meters. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves that are covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. Its small, white flowers are clustered in terminal panicles and bloom during the wet season.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine in some regions.
