Astronia columnaris

Astronia columnaris, commonly known as the columnar astronia, is a unique plant native to the Andes region of South America.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems that can grow up to several meters in height. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale green, which bloom during the warmer months. Its leaves are narrow and linear, arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem.

Astronia columnaris is often cultivated for its ornamental value and adaptability to various growing conditions.
