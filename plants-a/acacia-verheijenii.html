Acacia verheijenii

Acacia verheijenii, commonly known as the Namib Desert acacia, is a shrub native to the arid regions of Namibia and Angola.

It is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has a deep root system that allows it to survive in harsh, dry environments. It plays an important role in stabilizing sandy soils and providing habitat for local wildlife.

Acacia verheijenii is also valued for its hardy nature and ability to thrive with minimal water.
