Anneslia mollicula

Anneslia mollicula, commonly known as the velvetleaf anneslia, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It features hairy, fleshy leaves that give it a distinctive, velvety appearance. The plant produces small, showy flowers in shades of pink and white, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Anneslia mollicula is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and botanical collections.
