Aloe bolusii

Aloe bolusii, commonly known as the Bolus aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are thick, fleshy, and covered in a layer of white powder. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow flowers during the summer months. Its leaves contain a gel-like substance that is used in traditional medicine and skincare products.

Aloe bolusii is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water to thrive.
