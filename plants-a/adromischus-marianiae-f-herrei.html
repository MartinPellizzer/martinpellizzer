Adromischus marianiae f. herrei

Adromischus marianiae f. herrei, commonly known as the Herre's moonstone, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, rounded rosettes of fleshy, blue-green leaves that resemble small stones. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink and white during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
