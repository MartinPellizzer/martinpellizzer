Albuca subspicata

Albuca subspicata, commonly known as the Natal grass or Natal lily, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb with linear, grass-like leaves that grow in dense clumps. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale green flowers on tall, slender stems. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Albuca subspicata is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
