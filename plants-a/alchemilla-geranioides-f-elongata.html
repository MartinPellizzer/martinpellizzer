Alchemilla geranioides f. elongata

Alchemilla geranioides f. elongata, commonly known as the elongated lady's mantle, is a variant of the lady's mantle plant characterized by its elongated leaves.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is native to Europe, particularly in regions of southern England and southern France. The plant features a low-growing habit with dense, silver-gray foliage that is often covered in a fine layer of silky hairs. Its flowers are small, clustered in umbels, and typically appear in shades of white or pale yellow.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in moist, shaded environments.
