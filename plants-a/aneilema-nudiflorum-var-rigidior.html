Aneilema nudiflorum var. rigidior

Aneilema nudiflorum var. rigidior, commonly known as the rigid aneilema, is a striking tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, upright growth habit and large, heart-shaped leaves that are often a deep green with a glossy texture. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in clusters, typically in shades of white and pink, which bloom during the warmer months. Its rigid, fibrous stems make it suitable for use in ornamental landscaping or as a potted plant.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively low maintenance, it is gaining popularity among horticultural enthusiasts.
