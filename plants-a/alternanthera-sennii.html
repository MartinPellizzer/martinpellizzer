Alternanthera sennii

Alternanthera sennii, commonly known as the red-veined sorrel, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves that display a deep red vein against a dark green background. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Alternanthera sennii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique foliage.
