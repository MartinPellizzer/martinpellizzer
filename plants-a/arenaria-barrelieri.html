Arenaria barrelieri

Arenaria barrelieri, commonly known as the Barrelier's sandwort, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial that thrives in sandy or rocky soils, often found in coastal areas. The plant produces small, white or pinkish flowers during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are typically narrow and fleshy, adapted to conserve water in arid environments.

Arenaria barrelieri is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in stabilizing coastal dunes.
