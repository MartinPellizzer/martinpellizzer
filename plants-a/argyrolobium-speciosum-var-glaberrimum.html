Argyrolobium speciosum var. glaberrimum

Argyrolobium speciosum var. glaberrimum, commonly known as the smooth argyrolobium, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its slender, upright stems and clusters of small, pale yellow flowers. The plant has palmate leaves with three to five leaflets, and its roots form symbiotic relationships with nitrogen-fixing bacteria.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.
