Anthurium giganteum

Anthurium giganteum, commonly known as the giant anthurium, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 2 feet in length, often with a deep green color and a waxy texture. The plant produces vibrant red spathes that enclose its small, yellowish spadices, which are the actual flowers. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium giganteum is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
