Aira capillaris var. tenorei

Aira capillaris var. tenorei, commonly known as the Tuscan grass, is a variant of the common hairgrass species.

It is a perennial grass native to the Mediterranean region, particularly Italy. The plant features slender, tufted leaves and produces delicate, spike-like inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valued ornamental plant in gardens and landscapes.
