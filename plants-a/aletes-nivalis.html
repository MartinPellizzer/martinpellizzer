Aletes nivalis

Aletes nivalis, commonly known as the snow alpine, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to high-altitude regions of the Himalayas.

It thrives in cold, rocky environments and is adapted to withstand harsh winter conditions. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are typically narrow and covered in a fine, silvery hairs that help reduce heat loss.

Due to its limited habitat and sensitivity to climate change, Aletes nivalis is considered a vulnerable species.
