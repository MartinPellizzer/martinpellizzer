Agathosma obtusa

Agathosma obtusa, commonly known as sweet fennel, is a small shrub native to South Africa.

The plant is often cultivated for its essential oils, which are used in aromatherapy and perfumery. Its medicinal properties have been traditionally used to treat respiratory and digestive ailments.
