Aglaonema oblongifolium

Aglaonema oblongifolium, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, dark green leaves with distinctive white and silver markings, which provide an ornamental appearance. The plant has a short, stout stem and produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that are rarely seen. Its leaves are oblong in shape, hence the species name, and are arranged in a rosette pattern.

Aglaonema oblongifolium is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and is popular as a houseplant due to its air-purifying qualities.
