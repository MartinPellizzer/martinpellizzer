Aeschynanthus jouyi

Aeschynanthus jouyi, commonly known as the golden flame ivy, is a flowering plant native to the rainforests of western Africa.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and produces clusters of bright orange-red tubular flowers. The leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly waxy texture. It thrives in humid, warm environments with bright, indirect light.

This species is popular in horticulture for its vibrant blooms and cascading appearance.
