Aerangis calantha

Aerangis calantha, commonly known as the Calantha Orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, colorful flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its leaves are broad and fleshy, adapted to store water in the humid, tropical environments it thrives in.

The flowers often display vibrant shades of red, orange, and yellow, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
