Athyrium baishanzuense

Athyrium baishanzuense, commonly known as the Baishanzu fern, is a species native to China, specifically found in the Baishanzu mountain range.

It belongs to the Dryopteridaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, feather-like fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as forests and rocky slopes. Its leaves are bipinnate, with a graceful, arching appearance that contributes to its ornamental value.

Athyrium baishanzuense is valued for its aesthetic appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
