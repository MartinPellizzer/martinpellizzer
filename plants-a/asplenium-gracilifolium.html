Asplenium gracilifolium

Asplenium gracilifolium, commonly known as the slender-leaved spleenwort, is a small, evergreen fern belonging to the family Aspleniaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, linear leaves that are typically less than 10 centimeters in length. This species thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found growing on rocks or tree trunks in temperate regions. Its rhizomes are fibrous and help anchor it to its substrate while absorbing nutrients.

Asplenium gracilifolium is a relatively uncommon plant, often requiring specific microclimatic conditions to flourish.
