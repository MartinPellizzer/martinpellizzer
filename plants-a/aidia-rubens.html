Aidia rubens

Aidia rubens, commonly known as the red-flowered aidia, is a species of flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Rubiaceae family, which includes coffee and gardenias. The plant is characterized by its vibrant red flowers that bloom in clusters, adding a striking visual appeal. Its leaves are typically oval-shaped with a glossy surface, and it thrives in tropical and subtropical climates.

Aidia rubens is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
