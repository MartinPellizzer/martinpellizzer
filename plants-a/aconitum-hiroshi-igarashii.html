Aconitum hiroshi-igarashii

Aconitum hiroshi-igarashii, commonly known as Hiroshi Igarashii monkshood, is a perennial herb native to Japan.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and mountainous regions. Its leaves are palmately compound, with deeply lobed segments, and its stems are erect and somewhat hairy.

Aconitum hiroshi-igarashii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
