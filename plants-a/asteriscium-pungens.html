Asteriscium pungens

Asteriscium pungens, commonly known as the spiked aster, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spiky flowers that bloom in shades of purple and white. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Due to its unique appearance and ornamental value, it is often cultivated in horticultural settings.
