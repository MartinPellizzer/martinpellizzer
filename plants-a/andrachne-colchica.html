Andrachne colchica

Andrachne colchica, commonly known as the Turk's cap lily, is a flowering plant native to the eastern Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange flowers that resemble small caps, which bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces small, dark berries that contain the seeds.

Andrachne colchica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
