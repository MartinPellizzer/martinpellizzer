Agathis macrophylla var. vitiensis

Agathis macrophylla var. vitiensis, commonly known as the Vitiensis Agathis, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the Pacific Islands, particularly Vanuatu and Fiji.

It is characterized by its large, elliptical leaves that are glossy and dark green, often with a waxy coating. The tree produces large, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color, followed by woody, cone-like fruits containing oily seeds. It thrives in humid, well-drained soils and is often found in lowland rainforests.

The plant is valued for its durable wood and has cultural significance in the regions where it is found.
