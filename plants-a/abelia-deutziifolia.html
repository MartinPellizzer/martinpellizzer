Abelia deutziifolia

Abelia deutziifolia, commonly known as the Chinese abelia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features slender, arching stems and opposite, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, tubular flowers in shades of white, pink, and red, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained, moist soil.

Abelia deutziifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various garden settings.
