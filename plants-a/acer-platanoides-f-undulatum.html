Acer platanoides f. undulatum

Acer platanoides f. undulatum, commonly known as the undulating Norway maple, is a cultivar of the Norway maple (Acer platanoides).

It is characterized by its uniquely undulating, wavy-edged leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant is a deciduous tree with a broad, spreading crown and smooth, gray bark. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained, fertile soils.

This cultivar is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its aesthetic appeal and adaptability.
