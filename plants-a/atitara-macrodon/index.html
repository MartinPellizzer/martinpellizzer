Atitara Macrodon

Atitara macrodon tree, scientifically known as Atitara macrodon, is a rare and ancient species native to the dense rainforests of Central America. This unique tree is characterized by its massive, buttressed roots and towering, umbrella-like canopy that can span over 30 meters in height. Despite its ecological significance, Atitara macrodon remains poorly studied due to its remote habitat and limited distribution. Conservation efforts are urgently needed to protect this endangered species from deforestation and climate change. Its survival is crucial for maintaining the biodiversity of its native ecosystem.
