Amaryllis umabisana

Amaryllis umabisana, commonly known as the Natal amaryllis, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that come in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant produces a tall, erect stem with narrow, strap-like leaves that are often bluish-green in color. It blooms in late summer to early autumn, making it a popular choice for gardeners in warmer climates.

Amaryllis umabisana is relatively easy to care for, requiring well-draining soil and moderate watering.
