Aloe ferox var. subferox

Aloe ferox var. subferox, commonly known as the Cape aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a reddish tinge, arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces tall, branched flower stalks that bear yellow or orange flowers, which bloom in summer. Its leaves contain a gel-like substance used in traditional medicine and skincare products.

It is adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
