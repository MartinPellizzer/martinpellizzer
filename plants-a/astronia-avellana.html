Astronia avellana

Astronia avellana, commonly known as the Spanish thorn or Astronia, is a shrub native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its spiny stems and small, yellowish flowers. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to the arid climate of its native habitat.

Astronia avellana is often found in coastal areas and is valued for its hardiness and ornamental appeal.
