Anemonastrum narcissiflorum subsp. zephyrum

Anemonastrum narcissiflorum subsp.

zephyrum, commonly known as the Zephyr bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region. It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow, typically blooming in late spring to early summer.

The plant has a compact, upright growth habit with hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its delicate appearance and long-lasting blooms.
