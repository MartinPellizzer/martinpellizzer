Aporosa illustris

Aporosa illustris, commonly known as the white-flowered hibiscus, is a species of flowering plant in the Malvaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of Indonesia and the Philippines. Aporosa illustris is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant due to its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
