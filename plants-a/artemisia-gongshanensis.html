Artemisia gongshanensis

Artemisia gongshanensis, commonly known as Gongshan wormwood, is a species of flowering plant in the sunflower family.

It is native to China, specifically the Yunnan Province, and thrives in high-altitude environments. The plant is characterized by its aromatic, silver-green foliage and small, yellowish flowers. It is often found in alpine meadows and rocky areas, where it contributes to the local ecosystem.

Due to its unique habitat and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
