Achyranthes rubrolutea

Achyranthes rubrolutea, commonly known as red-and-yellow senna, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters. The plant features palmately lobed leaves and produces yellow-orange flowers in clusters. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, round seeds.

The plant is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its striking floral display.
