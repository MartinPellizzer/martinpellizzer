Aloe mcloughlinii

Aloe mcloughlinii, commonly known as the Cape aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, narrow leaves that are typically blue-green in color, often with a waxy coating. The plant produces striking orange-red flowers in clusters during the summer months. It is adapted to arid environments and requires well-draining soil to thrive.

Aloe mcloughlinii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
