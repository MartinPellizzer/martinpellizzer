Andryala Pontana

Andryala pontana is a rare and lesser-known plant species native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in shaded forest understories. This plant is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped leaves and small, clustered white flowers. Due to habitat loss and limited distribution, it is considered vulnerable to extinction. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this unique botanical treasure.
