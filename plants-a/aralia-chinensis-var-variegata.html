Aralia chinensis var. variegata

Aralia chinensis var. variegata, commonly known as the Variegated Chinese Aralia, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features compound leaves with a distinctive variegated pattern of white, yellow, and green on a dark green background. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers in umbels, followed by spiny, berry-like fruits. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular ornamental plant in gardens.

Its foliage provides visual interest throughout the growing season.
