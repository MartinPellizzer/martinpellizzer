Anthemis hydruntina var. silensis

Anthemis hydruntina var. silensis, commonly known as the Silene Anthemis, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit. The plant produces small, yellowish to brownish composite flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are typically narrow and slightly hairy, with a strong, aromatic scent.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or dry habitats.
