Anil hebepetala

Anil hebepetala, commonly known as the blue bell vine, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and the Americas.

It is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped blue or violet flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Anil hebepetala is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
