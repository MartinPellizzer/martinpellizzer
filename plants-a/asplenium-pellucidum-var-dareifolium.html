Asplenium pellucidum var. dareifolium

Asplenium pellucidum var. dareifolium, commonly known as the transparent spleenwort, is a fern species belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, translucent leaves that give it a unique appearance. This variety is typically found in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. The fronds are usually narrow and have a leathery texture, with a distinct midrib.

It plays a role in nutrient cycling in its ecosystem and is often used as an indicator of environmental conditions.
