Amygdalus × persicoamygdala

Amygdalus × persicoamygdala, commonly known as the almond plum.

This hybrid plant is a cross between the sweet almond (Amygdalus dulcis) and the Chinese plum (Prunus mume). It exhibits characteristics of both parent species, including a rounded shape and small, sweet-tart fruits. The leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a slightly serrated edge.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and edible fruit, which can be used in cooking and baking.
