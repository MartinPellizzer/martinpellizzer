Avenastrum besseri

Avenastrum besseri, commonly known as the Bessera grass, is a perennial grass native to Central Asia.

It typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid conditions. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It is often found in mountainous regions and is valued for its resilience in harsh environments.

Avenastrum besseri plays a role in stabilizing soil and supporting local biodiversity.
