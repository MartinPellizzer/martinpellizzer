Achyrocoma tomentosa

Achyrocoma tomentosa, commonly known as the silver leaf daisy, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its fuzzy, silver-gray foliage and daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers. Its compact growth habit and ornamental appearance make it a popular choice for gardeners.

It is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
