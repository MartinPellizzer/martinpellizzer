Aster lowieanus

Aster lowieanus, commonly known as the lowie's aster, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and woodland edges. It produces small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in the summer months.

Aster lowieanus is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting local pollinators.
