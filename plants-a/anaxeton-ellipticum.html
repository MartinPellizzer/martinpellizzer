Anaxeton ellipticum

Anaxeton ellipticum, commonly known as the narrowleaf sunhemp, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its narrow, elliptical leaves and produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is an annual herb that thrives in warm, sunny environments. It has a fibrous root system and is often found in disturbed soils.

Anaxeton ellipticum is also valued for its use in soil preparation and as a forage crop.
