Anotis carnosa

Anotis carnosa, commonly known as the woolly anotis, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant features hairy, gray-green leaves that help reduce water loss in arid environments. It produces small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring.

Anotis carnosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
