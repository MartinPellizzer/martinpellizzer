Aglaonema elegans

Aglaonema elegans, commonly known as the Variegated Evergreen, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated foliage with white and green patterns on its broad, lance-shaped leaves. The plant belongs to the Araceae family, which includes other ornamental plants like peace lilies. It thrives in low-light conditions and requires moderate humidity to maintain its vibrant leaf colors.

Aglaonema elegans is popular as a houseplant due to its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.
