Arenaria cerastioides var. oranensis

Arenaria cerastioides var. oranensis, commonly known as the Oranensis sandwort, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically forms dense mats of foliage. The plant produces small, white or pink flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
