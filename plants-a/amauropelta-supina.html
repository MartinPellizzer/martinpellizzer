Amauropelta supina

Amauropelta supina, commonly known as the flat shield fern, is a unique plant belonging to the family Pteridaceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive, flat, shield-shaped fronds that lie close to the ground. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its reproductive structures, including sori, are located on the underside of the fronds.

The botanical features of Amauropelta supina make it an interesting subject for study in both horticulture and botany.
