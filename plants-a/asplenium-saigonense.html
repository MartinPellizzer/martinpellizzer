Asplenium saigonense

Asplenium saigonense, commonly known as the Saigon spleenwort, is a fern belonging to the family Aspleniaceae.

It is a terrestrial fern that typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. The plant has a slender, creeping rhizome with small, lance-shaped fronds that are usually less than 10 cm in length. Its leaves are usually dark green and have a delicate, fern-like appearance.

Asplenium saigonense is native to Southeast Asia and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.
