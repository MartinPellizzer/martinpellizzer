Anthurium × bicolor

Anthurium × bicolor, commonly known as the red and white anthurium, is a hybrid plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves and vibrant spadix. The plant produces colorful spathes that range from red to white, creating a dramatic visual display. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium × bicolor is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its long-lasting blooms and ornamental foliage.
