Azorella Polaris

Azorella polaris is a hardy alpine plant found in the extreme environments of Antarctica and the southern tip of South America. It thrives in conditions where most other plants cannot survive, often growing in rocky soils and under harsh climatic conditions. This plant is notable for its ability to survive freezing temperatures and long periods of darkness. Its unique structure allows it to store water and nutrients efficiently. Despite its tough environment, Azorella polaris plays an important role in the local ecosystems of the polar regions.
