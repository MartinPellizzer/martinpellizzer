Anthemis pedunculata subsp. clausonis

Anthemis pedunculata subsp.

clausonis, commonly known as the narrow-leaved tansy, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region. It is a perennial herb characterized by its aromatic, yellowish flowers and narrow, linear leaves.

The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and thrives in full sun. Its flowers are arranged in dense, branched clusters, and it produces small, oval-shaped fruits.

The species is noted for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally in herbal remedies.
