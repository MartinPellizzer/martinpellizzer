Athrixia tenella

Athrixia tenella, commonly known as the silver leaf broom.

It is a small, evergreen shrub native to South Africa, characterized by its dense, silver-gray foliage. The plant features narrow, leathery leaves that are arranged in a compact, bushy form. It produces small, yellowish flowers in clusters during the summer months.

Athrixia tenella is well adapted to dry, sandy soils and is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive appearance and drought tolerance.
