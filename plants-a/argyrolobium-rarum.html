Argyrolobium rarum

Argyrolobium rarum, commonly known as the rare silver globular pea, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its small, spherical, silver-gray pods that contain several seeds. The plant produces delicate, pale yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens for its unique appearance and ecological significance.
