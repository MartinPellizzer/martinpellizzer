Anagyris inodora

Anagyris inodora, commonly known as the sweet acacia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height. The plant produces small, yellow, pea-like flowers that bloom in spring. Its leaves are bipinnate, consisting of numerous small, oval-shaped leaflets.

Anagyris inodora is adapted to arid environments and is often found in rocky soils.
