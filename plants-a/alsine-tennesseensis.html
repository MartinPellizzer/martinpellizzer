Alsine tennesseensis

Alsine tennesseensis, commonly known as Tennessee alsine, is a species of flowering plant in the family Mallow.

It is a low-growing annual herb that typically reaches a height of about 10 to 20 centimeters. The plant features small, pale green to yellowish flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered with fine, silky hairs.

Native to the southeastern United States, it thrives in moist, sandy soils along stream banks and in open woodlands.
