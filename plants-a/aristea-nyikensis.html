Aristea nyikensis

Aristea nyikensis, commonly known as the Nyikeni silver grass, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features a compact rosette of narrow, silver-gray leaves that are covered in fine, silky hairs. The plant produces small, pale blue or purple flowers arranged in a dense inflorescence, which bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a desirable ornamental plant.
