Arisaema limbatum var. ionostemma

Arisaema limbatum var. ionostemma, commonly known as the narrow-leaf arisaema, is a flowering plant native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, umbrella-like inflorescence composed of a spadix surrounded by a modified leaf called a spathe. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. Its leaves are elongated and have a narrow, heart-shaped base, giving it its common name.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage and flowering structure.
