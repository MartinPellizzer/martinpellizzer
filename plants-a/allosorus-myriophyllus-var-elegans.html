Allosorus myriophyllus var. elegans

Allosorus myriophyllus var. elegans, commonly known as the elegant feather fern, is a rare and delicate fern species native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its finely divided, lacy fronds that give it a distinctive, ethereal appearance. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its reproductive structures, including sori, are located on the underside of the fronds, a common feature in many fern species.
