Actinospermum multiflorum

Actinospermum multiflorum, commonly known as the silverleaf honeysuckle, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
