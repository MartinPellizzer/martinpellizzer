Astrophytum perbellum

Astrophytum perbellum, commonly known as the beautiful star cactus, is a species native to the arid regions of Mexico.

It is a small, globular cactus with a ribbed structure and a dense covering of white to yellow spines. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months, which are often followed by red, fleshy fruits. Its thick, fleshy stems store water, allowing it to survive in harsh, dry environments.

Astrophytum perbellum is popular among cactus enthusiasts for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
