Austrostipa anaiwaniorum

Austrostipa anaiwaniorum, commonly known as the Anaiwana grass, is a perennial grass species native to Australia.

It belongs to the Poaceae family and is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit. The plant typically forms dense clumps with narrow, blade-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its inflorescence consists of a panicle made up of small, spikelet-like flowers.

Austrostipa anaiwaniorum is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a resilient plant in harsh conditions.
