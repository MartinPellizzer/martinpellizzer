Anthurium llanense

Anthurium llanense, commonly known as the Llanense arrowhead, is a striking tropical plant native to the Andean region of South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its unique inflorescence consisting of a spadix and a spathe. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy dark green leaves that are often veined with white or silver markings. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, typically emerging from the base of the spathe.

Anthurium llanense is valued for its ornamental foliage and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.
