Agrostis billardierei

Agrostis billardierei, commonly known as the slender meadowgrass, is a perennial grass species native to Australia.

It typically grows in coastal and sandy environments, thriving in well-drained soils. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It is often found in dune systems and salt marshes, contributing to stabilizing these dynamic habitats.

Its adaptability to harsh conditions makes it an important component of coastal ecosystems.
