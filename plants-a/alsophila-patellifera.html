Alsophila patellifera

Alsophila patellifera, commonly known as the giant tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and a crown of large, feathery fronds that can reach several meters in length. The plant has a slow growth rate and can live for many decades, making it a long-lived species. Its fronds are typically dark green and have a distinctive, elongated shape.

Alsophila patellifera plays an important role in tropical ecosystems, providing habitat and contributing to forest structure.
