Adenophora likiangensis

Adenophora likiangensis, commonly known as the Yunnan bellflower, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped, pale blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in alpine meadows and rocky areas at high elevations. Its leaves are lanceolate and covered with fine hairs, while its stems are often hairy as well.

Adenophora likiangensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is also used in traditional medicine.
