Anemia anthriscifolia var. rotundata

Anemia anthriscifolia var. rotundata, commonly known as the round-leaf anemone, is a herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features basal leaves that are rounded and often have a hairy texture, while its flowering stems bear small, white to pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in partial to full shade. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.

The species is also known for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal remedies.
