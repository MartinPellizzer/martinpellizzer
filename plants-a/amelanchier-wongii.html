Amelanchier wongii

Amelanchier wongii, commonly known as the Wong serviceberry, is a species of shrub or small tree native to China.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and showy white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant produces edible berries that ripen in the summer, which are often used in culinary applications.
