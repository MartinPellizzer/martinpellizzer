Artabotrys uniflorus

Artabotrys uniflorus, commonly known as the single-flowered beautyberry, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking violet to purple bracts that enclose its small, white flowers. The plant produces clusters of small, dark blue berries that are edible for humans and animals. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for horticultural use in tropical gardens.
