Abies hanburyana

Abies hanburyana, commonly known as the Hanbury fir, is a species of evergreen conifer native to the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and dense, pyramidal crown. The needles are dark green and have a waxy coating, while the cones are typically pendulous and have a distinctive shape. It thrives in cool, moist environments at high altitudes.

Due to its rarity and habitat specificity, it is considered an endangered species.
