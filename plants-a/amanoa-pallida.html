Amanoa pallida

Amanoa pallida, commonly known as the pale amanoa, is a species of flowering plant native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It belongs to the genus Amanoa, which is part of the orchid family, Orchidaceae. The plant is characterized by its delicate, pale green leaves and small, fragrant white flowers. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest understories.

Due to habitat loss and invasive species, Amanoa pallida is considered endangered and is protected under conservation efforts.
