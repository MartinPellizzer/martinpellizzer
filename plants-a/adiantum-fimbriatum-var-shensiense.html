Adiantum fimbriatum var. shensiense

Adiantum fimbriatum var. shensiense, commonly known as the Sichuan maidenhair fern, is a delicate fern native to eastern Asia.

It features finely divided, bi- or tri-pinnate fronds that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions. Its fronds are adorned with small, tooth-like projections along the edges.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is commonly cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
