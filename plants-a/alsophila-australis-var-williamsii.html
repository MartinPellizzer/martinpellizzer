Alsophila australis var. williamsii

Alsophila australis var. williamsii, commonly known as the Williams' tree fern, is a tall, elegant fern native to New Zealand.

It features a slender, unbranched trunk that is covered with a fibrous, brownish bark. The plant produces large, shield-shaped fronds that are dark green and have a leathery texture. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in coastal and lowland forests.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens with suitable conditions.
