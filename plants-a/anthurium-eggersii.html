Anthurium eggersii

Anthurium eggersii, commonly known as the Eggers' flamingo flower, is a tropical plant native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped spathes that range in color from deep red to orange. The plant produces small, greenish-white spadices that are typically hidden within the spathe. It thrives in humid, warm environments with bright, indirect light.

Anthurium eggersii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated as a houseplant.
