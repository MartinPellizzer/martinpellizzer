Arnica chamissonis var. longinodosa

Arnica chamissonis var. longinodosa, commonly known as long-stemmed arnica, is a perennial herb native to North America.

It features upright, branching stems that can grow up to 1 meter in height. The plant produces yellow to orange composite flowers, which are composed of many small tubular florets surrounded by yellow ray florets. Its leaves are lance-shaped, hairy, and often have a strong, bitter aroma.

This variant is often found in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and rocky slopes.
