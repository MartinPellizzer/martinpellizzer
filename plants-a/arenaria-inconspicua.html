Arenaria inconspicua

Arenaria inconspicua, commonly known as the inconspicuous sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the western United States.

It typically thrives in dry, sandy or gravelly soils, often found in open habitats such as rocky slopes and coastal dunes.
