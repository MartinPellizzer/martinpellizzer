Anthyllis vulneraria var. pseudovulneraria

Anthyllis vulneraria var. pseudovulneraria, commonly known as the false woundwort, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is a low-growing perennial shrub characterized by its small, yellow pea-like flowers and trifoliate leaves. Native to the Mediterranean region, it thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in coastal areas and steppes. The plant has small, rounded leaves and produces pods that contain several seeds.

It is adapted to harsh environments and plays a role in stabilizing soils in arid regions.
