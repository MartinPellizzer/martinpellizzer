Anisomeles dallachyi

Anisomeles dallachyi, commonly known as the Mexican morning glory, is a climbing vine native to Mexico and parts of Central America.

Anisomeles dallachyi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
