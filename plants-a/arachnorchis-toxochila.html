Arachnorchis toxochila

Arachnorchis toxochila, commonly known as the spider orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Australia.

It is a terrestrial herb with a slender, leafless stem that bears a single, showy flower. The plant typically grows in heathland and open forest habitats, preferring well-drained soils. Its flowers are usually pale green to brownish in color, with intricate patterns that mimic the appearance of spider webs.

Arachnorchis toxochila is characterized by its unique morphology and specialized pollination mechanisms.
