Austroblechnum banksii

Austroblechnum banksii, commonly known as the New Zealand water fern, is a species of fern native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its delicate, floating fronds that are typically found in freshwater environments. The plant has a distinctive appearance with its finely divided, light green leaves that can form dense mats on the water surface. It plays an important ecological role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat for small organisms.

Austroblechnum banksii is also valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in water gardens.
