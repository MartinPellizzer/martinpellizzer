Argyranthemum frutescens var. canariense

Argyranthemum frutescens var. canariense, commonly known as Canary daisy, is a compact, herbaceous perennial native to the Canary Islands.

It features showy, daisy-like flowers with white petals and a yellow center, blooming throughout the summer months. The plant has a bushy growth habit and is well-suited for rock gardens or as a container plant. It prefers full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a versatile addition to Mediterranean-style gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
