Aloe schweinfurthii var. labworana

Aloe schweinfurthii var. labworana, commonly known as the White Aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features a rosette of thick, fleshy, pale green leaves with a white or pale blue hue. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing yellow flowers. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with full sun exposure.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
