Agathis veitchii

Agathis veitchii, commonly known as the Veitch's ironwood, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the island of Madagascar.

It belongs to the family Araucariaceae and is characterized by its large, glossy, lance-shaped leaves. The tree produces conifer-like cones and is known for its dense, heavy wood that is highly valued for its durability. Due to habitat loss and overharvesting, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native range.
