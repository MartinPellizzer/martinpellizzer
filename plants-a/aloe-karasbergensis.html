Aloe karasbergensis

Aloe karasbergensis, commonly known as the Karasberg aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its upright, branched stems and thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a slight blue tint. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing yellow or orange flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Aloe karasbergensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively low maintenance.
