Aslia bulbipes

Aslia bulbipes, commonly known as the bulbous aslia, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact, bulb-like structure that stores nutrients and water, enabling it to survive in arid environments. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are typically white or pale pink, often with a subtle fragrance. Native to regions with dry climates, it thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water.

Its unique morphology and ornamental flowers make it a sought-after species among succulent and orchid enthusiasts.
