Aspalathus venosa

Aspalathus venosa, commonly known as the silverleaf bush, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silver-gray leaves and small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils. It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.

The leaves are sometimes used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.
