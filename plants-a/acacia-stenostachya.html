Acacia stenostachya

Acacia stenostachya, commonly known as the narrow-stemmed acacia, is a shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, often multi-branched stems and feathery, bipinnate leaves. The plant produces yellow to golden flowers in compact, globular inflorescences, which bloom during the warmer months. Its seeds are encased in hard, woody pods that split open to release the seeds.

Acacia stenostachya is often found in dry, sandy soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.
