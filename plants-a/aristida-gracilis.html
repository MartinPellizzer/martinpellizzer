Aristida gracilis

Aristida gracilis, commonly known as slender dropseed, is a grass species native to North America.

It is characterized by its fine, wiry leaves and slender, branching inflorescences. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as prairies and disturbed soils. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils.

Its delicate appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.
