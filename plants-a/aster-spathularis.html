Aster spathularis

Aster spathularis, commonly known as the narrowleaf aster, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and small, clustered blue to purple flowers that bloom in the fall. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands. It is often found in the eastern United States and parts of Canada.

Aster spathularis plays a role in supporting pollinators and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
