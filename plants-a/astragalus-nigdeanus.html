Astragalus nigdeanus

Astragalus nigdeanus, commonly known as the Nigde milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to Turkey.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its small, yellowish flowers and compound leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. It is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.

Its conservation status is of concern due to habitat loss and over-collection.
