Aster onustus

Aster onustus, commonly known as the purple coneflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads composed of numerous small florets. The plant features a tall, upright stem with narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs. Its most distinctive feature is the showy purple or pink flower head, which consists of a central disk surrounded by petal-like ray florets.

Aster onustus is valued for its ornamental beauty and is also used in traditional herbal medicine.
