Aria greenii

Aria greenii, commonly known as the green spurge, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring, adding a subtle ornamental value. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

However, it contains toxic sap and should be handled with care.
