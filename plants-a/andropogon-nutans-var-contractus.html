Andropogon nutans var. contractus

Andropogon nutans var. contractus, commonly known as narrowleaf bluestem, is a perennial grass native to North America.

It typically grows in open woodlands and prairie habitats, preferring well-drained soils. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences. It is characterized by its erect growth habit and is often found in disturbed or transitional habitats.

This variety is valued for its ecological role in stabilizing soil and providing forage for wildlife.
