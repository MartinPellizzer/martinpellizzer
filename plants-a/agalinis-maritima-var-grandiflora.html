Agalinis maritima var. grandiflora

Agalinis maritima var. grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered beach evening-primrose, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of the southeastern United States.

It is a herbaceous annual that typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils along dunes and shorelines. The plant features showy yellow flowers with four petals, which bloom in late summer to early fall. Its leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and often covered in fine hairs.

It plays an important ecological role in stabilizing coastal dunes and providing nectar for pollinators.
