Arachniodes chinensis var. cornopteris

Arachniodes chinensis var. cornopteris, commonly known as the golden shield fern, is a deciduous fern native to eastern Asia.

It features large, pinnately compound leaves with a distinctive golden hue on the underside, giving it its common name. The plant thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in mountainous regions. Its fronds are typically broad and can grow up to several feet in length.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appearance and ability to add texture to shaded garden spaces.
