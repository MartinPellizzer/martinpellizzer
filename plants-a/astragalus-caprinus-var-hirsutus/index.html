Astragalus Caprinus Var. Hirsutus

Astragalus caprinus var. hirsutus is a unique and resilient plant species native to specific regions of western North America. Known for its hairy leaves and distinctive floral structure, this variant of Astragalus stands out among its relatives. It thrives in rocky and arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. The plant plays an important role in local ecosystems, supporting pollinators and contributing to biodiversity. Its study offers insights into plant evolution and ecological resilience.
