Afroaster

Afroaster, commonly known as the African aster, is a genus of flowering plants native to Africa.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically features showy, brightly colored florets arranged in a composite head, often with a central disk surrounded by ray flowers. Afroaster species are often found in grasslands and rocky areas, thriving in well-drained soils.

They are valued for their ornamental appeal and are sometimes used in traditional medicine.
