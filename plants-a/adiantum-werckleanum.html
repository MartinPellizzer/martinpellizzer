Adiantum werckleanum

Adiantum werckleanum, commonly known as the water fern, is a delicate member of the Adiantaceae family.

It is characterized by its finely divided, lace-like fronds that give it a unique and elegant appearance. This plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often near streams or in damp woodlands. Its fronds are bi-lobed and have a distinctive ruffled margin, which helps in identifying the species.

Adiantum werckleanum is prized in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in humid conditions.
