Aneilema siamense

Aneilema siamense, commonly known as the Siam Anemone, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

Its leaves are heart-shaped and glossy, while the flowers consist of a white spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe.

Aneilema siamense thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
