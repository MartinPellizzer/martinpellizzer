Anthurium nonoense

Anthurium nonoense, commonly known as the Nonoense Philodendron, is a tropical plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive spathe and spadix inflorescence. The plant features broad, heart-shaped leaves with a waxy texture and deep green coloration. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Anthurium nonoense is often cultivated for its ornamental value and air-purifying properties.
