Adiantum capillus-veneris f. dissectum

Adiantum capillus-veneris f. dissectum, commonly known as the "Maiden's Tears" or "VeilTress," is a fern characterized by its finely dissected, lace-like fronds.

The plant features delicate, linear pinnae that create a cascading effect, giving it an elegant appearance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions. The fronds are typically bright green and remain fresh and vibrant throughout the growing season.

This fern is prized for its ornamental value and is commonly cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
