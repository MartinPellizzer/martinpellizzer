Alternanthera echinata

Alternanthera echinata, commonly known as the spikedAlternanthera, is a striking aquatic plant native to South America.

It features vibrant red or pink bracts that surround its small, inconspicuous flowers, making it a popular choice for aquariums and water gardens. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are typically green, though they can vary in color depending on light conditions. It thrives in warm, tropical climates and requires moderate to high light for optimal growth.

Its unique appearance and ease of care make it a favorite among aquatic plant enthusiasts.
