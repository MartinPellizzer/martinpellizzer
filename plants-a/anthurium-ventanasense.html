Anthurium ventanasense

Anthurium ventanasense, commonly known as the Ventana anthurium, is a striking tropical plant native to the Caribbean islands.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its unique inflorescence consisting of a spadix and a spathe. The plant produces vibrant, heart-shaped leaves that are often dark green with a glossy texture. Its flowers, while not showy, are essential for its reproductive process and attract specific pollinators.

Anthurium ventanasense is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and ability to thrive in humid, shaded environments.
