Arnica menziesii

Arnica menziesii, commonly known as mountain arnica, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of western North America.

It features a robust rhizome and produces showy, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and rocky slopes. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine, soft hairs.

Arnica menziesii is valued for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional herbal remedies.
