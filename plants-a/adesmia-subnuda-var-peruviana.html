Adesmia subnuda var. peruviana

Adesmia subnuda var. peruviana, commonly known as the Peruvian velvet bean, is a shrub native to South America.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are often clustered in inflorescences. Its leaves are trifoliate, with three oval-shaped leaflets, and the stems are covered in fine, soft hairs.

The seeds are enclosed in hairy, elongated pods, which contribute to its common name.
