Astragalus akkensis var. pinoyi

Astragalus akkensis var. pinoyi, commonly known as the Akkensian milkvetch, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow to orange flowers, which bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments, often in rocky or sandy soils. It is native to regions of Central Asia, including parts of Turkey and the Caucasus.

This variant is noted for its hardy nature and ability to thrive in harsh, dry conditions.
