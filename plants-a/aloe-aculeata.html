Aloe aculeata

Aloe aculeata, commonly known as the spiny aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are covered in sharp, spiny teeth along their edges. The plant produces yellow flowers during the summer months, which are typically borne on tall, branching stems. Its leaves store water, allowing it to survive in arid environments.

Aloe aculeata is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and low maintenance requirements.
