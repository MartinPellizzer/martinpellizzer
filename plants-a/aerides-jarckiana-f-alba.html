Aerides jarckiana f. alba

Aerides jarckiana f. alba, commonly known as the white form of the jarckiana orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking white flowers with a subtle pinkish hue, which stand out against its dark green foliage. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. It prefers bright, indirect light and moderate humidity, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Its elegant appearance and relatively easy care requirements make it a favored ornamental plant among orchid enthusiasts.
