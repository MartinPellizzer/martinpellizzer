Achimenes longiflora var. tugwelliana

Achimenes longiflora var. tugwelliana, commonly known as the Tugwell's velvetleaf, is a tropical perennial herb native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to purple. The plant produces showy bracts that are often mistaken for petals, enhancing its ornamental appeal. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.

Its foliage is typically heart-shaped with a velvety texture, adding to its visual interest.
