Aloe xanthacantha

Aloe xanthacantha, commonly known as the yellow aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color with a yellowish tinge. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow to orange flowers, which attract pollinators like bees and birds. Its leaves contain a gel-like substance that is sometimes used in traditional medicine.

Aloe xanthacantha is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
