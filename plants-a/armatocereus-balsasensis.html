Armatocereus balsasensis

Armatocereus balsasensis, commonly known as the Balsas hedge cactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems covered in white, woolly spines. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, preferring well-drained soils.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
