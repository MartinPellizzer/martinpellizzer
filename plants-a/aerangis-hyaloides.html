Aerangis hyaloides

Aerangis hyaloides, commonly known as the glassy orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, translucent or glass-like petals that give it its common name. The plant typically grows as a terrestrial orchid with slender, upright stems and small, clustered flowers. It prefers shady, humid environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for botanical gardens and orchid enthusiasts.
