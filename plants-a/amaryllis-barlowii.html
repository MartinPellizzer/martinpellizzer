Amaryllis barlowii

Amaryllis barlowii, commonly known as the red amaryllis, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, sturdy stems that can grow up to 3 feet in height, adorned with large, showy flowers in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant produces clusters of trumpet-shaped blooms that open sequentially, creating a dramatic display. Its foliage consists of broad, strap-like leaves that are dark green and often persist through the blooming period.

Amaryllis barlowii is prized for its vibrant flowers and is a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
