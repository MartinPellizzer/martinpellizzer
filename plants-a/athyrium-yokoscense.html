Athyrium yokoscense

Athyrium yokoscense, commonly known as the Japanese shield fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to Japan.

It is characterized by its distinctive, shield-shaped fronds that are typically dark green with a silvery underside. The plant grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest understories. Its rhizomes are creeping and produce dense clusters of foliage.

Athyrium yokoscense is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appearance and ability to thrive in partial shade.
