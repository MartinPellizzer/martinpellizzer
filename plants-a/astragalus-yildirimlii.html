Astragalus yildirimlii

Astragalus yildirimlii, commonly known as the Turkish milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to Turkey.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow or white flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. It produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind or animals.

Astragalus yildirimlii is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal medicine.
