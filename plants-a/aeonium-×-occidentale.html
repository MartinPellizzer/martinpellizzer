Aeonium × occidentale

Aeonium × occidentale, commonly known as the "Tree Aeonium," is a hybrid succulent plant native to the Canary Islands.

It features a rosette of thick, fleshy, blue-green leaves that form a compact, tree-like structure. The plant produces tall, branched flower stalks with yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with ample sunlight.

Aeonium × occidentale is popular in rock gardens and as a decorative indoor plant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
