Agapetes megacarpa

Agapetes megacarpa, commonly known as the giant fruit agapete, is a species of flowering plant in the Ericaceae family.

It is native to the cloud forests of South America, particularly in regions of Ecuador and Peru. The plant is characterized by its small, evergreen leaves and produces large, fleshy berries that are an important food source for various wildlife. The botanical features of Agapetes megacarpa make it a notable species in the study of Andean flora.
