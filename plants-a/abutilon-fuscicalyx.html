Abutilon fuscicalyx

Abutilon fuscicalyx, commonly known as the woolly abutilon, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its fuzzy, hairy foliage and showy, lantern-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are ovate to lanceolate in shape and often have a velvety texture.
