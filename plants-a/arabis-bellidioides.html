Arabis bellidioides

Arabis bellidioides, commonly known as the rock cress, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils. The plant features basal leaves that are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are white or pale yellow, arranged in racemes, and bloom in early spring.

Arabis bellidioides is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in harsh environmental conditions.
