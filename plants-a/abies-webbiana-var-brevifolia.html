Abies webbiana var. brevifolia

Abies webbiana var. brevifolia, commonly known as the short-leaf fir, is a variant of the silver fir native to the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its compact, pyramidal growth habit and short, stiff needles that are bluish-green in color. The cones are typically slender and upright, maturing to a reddish-brown hue. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found at high altitudes.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
