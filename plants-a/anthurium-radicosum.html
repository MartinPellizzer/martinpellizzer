Anthurium radicosum

Anthurium radicosum, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped spathes that range in color from deep red to orange. The plant produces small, green, spadix-like flowers that are typically hidden within the spathe. It thrives in humid, warm environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium radicosum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in a home setting.
