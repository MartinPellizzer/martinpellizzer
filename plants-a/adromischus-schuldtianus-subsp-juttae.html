Adromischus schuldtianus subsp. juttae

Adromischus schuldtianus subsp.

juttae, commonly known as the Jutta's rose-window plant, is a succulent native to South Africa. It features compact, rosette-shaped stems covered in dense, velvety white to grayish-blue hairs.

The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or purple during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
