Agrostis natalensis

Agrostis natalensis, commonly known as Natal grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in open grasslands and along riverbanks, thriving in well-drained soils. The plant has slender, tufted stems and produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny grains. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping.

Additionally, it plays a role in stabilizing soil and supporting local wildlife.
