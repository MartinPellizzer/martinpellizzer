Aster bojeri

Aster bojeri, commonly known as the Bojer's aster, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically produces showy blue to violet ray florets surrounded by yellow disk florets. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its ornamental value and adaptability have made it a favored plant in horticulture.
