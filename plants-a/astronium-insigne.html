Astronium insigne

Astronium insigne, commonly known as the golden star tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are followed by spherical, woody fruits containing several seeds. Its botanical significance lies in its unique leaf morphology and its role in local ecosystems.

It is also valued for its ornamental appeal and has been studied for potential medicinal properties.
