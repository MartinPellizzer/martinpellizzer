Asystasia mysorensis

Asystasia mysorensis, commonly known as the Indian nettle or Mysore nettle, is a herbaceous plant belonging to the Acanthaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Asia, including India, Sri Lanka, and Indonesia. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges, often covered in fine hairs. It produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or purple in color.

Asystasia mysorensis is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and ease of cultivation.
