Astrocaryum ulei

Astrocaryum ulei, commonly known as the yellow buriti, is a palm native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, fan-shaped leaves. The plant produces bright yellow fruits that are highly valued for their nutritional content and traditional uses. Its fruit pulp is rich in beta-carotene and is often consumed fresh or processed into beverages.

The tree also plays an important ecological role in supporting local wildlife and maintaining forest biodiversity.
