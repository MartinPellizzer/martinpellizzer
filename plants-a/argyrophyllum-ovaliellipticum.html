Argyrophyllum ovaliellipticum

Argyrophyllum ovaliellipticum, commonly known as the narrow-elliptic silverleaf.

This plant is a member of the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its silver-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, dense hairs. The leaves are typically oval to elliptic in shape, with a narrow base, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in composite heads, typical of the daisy family.

Argyrophyllum ovaliellipticum is often found in rocky or arid environments, where it thrives in well-drained soils.
