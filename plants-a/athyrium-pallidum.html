Athyrium pallidum

Athyrium pallidum, commonly known as the pale fern, is a species of fern native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the family Athyriaceae and is characterized by its delicate, lace-like fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its leaves are bi-lobed and have a pale green to silvery appearance.

Athyrium pallidum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
