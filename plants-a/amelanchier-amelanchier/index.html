Amelanchier Amelanchier

Amelanchier amelanchier, also known as serviceberry, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to North America. It is celebrated for its early spring blooms and edible berries that ripen in late summer. The plant is valued for its ornamental beauty and ecological benefits, providing food for birds and wildlife. Its hardy nature makes it adaptable to a variety of soil types and climates. This versatile plant is a popular choice for gardens and natural landscapes alike.
