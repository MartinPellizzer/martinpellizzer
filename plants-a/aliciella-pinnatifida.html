Aliciella pinnatifida

Aliciella pinnatifida, commonly known as the broad-leaved aliciella, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features alternate, pinnately compound leaves that are often hairy and have a distinctive, fern-like appearance. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in early summer. Its roots are fibrous and can be used in traditional herbal medicine.

Aliciella pinnatifida thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal habitats.
