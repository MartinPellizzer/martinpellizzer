Astrophytum myriostigma var. columnare

Astrophytum myriostigma var. columnare, commonly known as the columnar astrophytum, is a succulent cactus native to the deserts of Mexico.

It is characterized by its tall, cylindrical stem that can grow up to several feet in height. The plant produces clusters of yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are modified into spines, and it has a dense covering of fine hairs.

It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
