Anotis thymifolia var. hyssopifolia

Anotis thymifolia var. hyssopifolia, commonly known as hyssop-leaved anotis, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, narrow leaves that resemble those of hyssop. The plant produces small, clustered yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and long blooming period.
