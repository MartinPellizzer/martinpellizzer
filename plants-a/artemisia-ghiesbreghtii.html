Artemisia ghiesbreghtii

Artemisia ghiesbreghtii, commonly known as the Ghiesbreght's wormwood, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features gray-green, aromatic foliage with narrow, lance-shaped leaves. Its small yellow flowers appear in clustered inflorescences during the summer months.

Artemisia ghiesbreghtii is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
