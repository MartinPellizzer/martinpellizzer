Allium jodanthum

Allium jodanthum, commonly known as the Jodan's onion, is a species of flowering plant in the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, particularly in countries like Kazakhstan and Uzbekistan. The plant produces small, white or pale purple flowers arranged in a spherical cluster, and its leaves are narrow and grass-like. Allium jodanthum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its unique floral display.
