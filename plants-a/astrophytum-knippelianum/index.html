Astrophytum Knippelianum

Astrophytum knippelianum, also known as the knippel's moon cactus, is a unique and striking plant native to the arid regions of the southwestern United States. With its rounded, segmented shape and vibrant green or red hues, it resembles a miniature planet or a glowing orb. This plant is a type of cactus known as a "moon cactus" due to its striking coloration and compact form. It thrives in dry, sunny environments and requires minimal water and care. Despite its delicate appearance, it is remarkably resilient, making it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor gardening.
