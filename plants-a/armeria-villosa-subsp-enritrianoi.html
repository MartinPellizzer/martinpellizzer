Armeria villosa subsp. enritrianoi

Armeria villosa subsp.

enritrianoi, commonly known as the Eritrean thrift, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the highlands of Eritrea. It features dense clusters of small, pink to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.

The plant has silver-gray, woolly leaves that help it retain moisture in its arid environment. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and alpine settings.

This subspecies is notable for its ornamental value and its ability to tolerate harsh climatic conditions.
