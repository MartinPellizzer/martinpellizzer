Acidocroton pilosulus

Acidocroton pilosulus, commonly known as the hairy croton, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family, which is known for its diverse and often toxic species. The plant is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves with a distinctive, often lobed shape. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, typically clustered in inflorescences.
