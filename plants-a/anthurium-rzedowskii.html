Anthurium rzedowskii

Anthurium rzedowskii, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a striking tropical plant native to the forests of Mexico.

It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped spathe that ranges in color from deep red to orange, often with a contrasting green spadix. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its foliage consists of broad, glossy green leaves that add to its ornamental appeal.

Anthurium rzedowskii is popular in horticulture for its unique and colorful blooms.
