Aloysia Salsoloides

Aloysia salsoloides is a hardy shrub native to arid and semi-arid regions of the Americas. It is known for its silver-green foliage and clusters of small, pale blue flowers. This plant thrives in poor soils and is often found in desert scrub and coastal dunes. Its ability to withstand extreme temperatures makes it a valuable species for ecological restoration. Aloysia salsoloides plays an important role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for desert wildlife.
