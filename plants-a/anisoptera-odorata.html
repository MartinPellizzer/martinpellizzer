Anisoptera odorata

Anisoptera odorata, commonly known as the fragrant hibiscus, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It produces a distinctive, sweet fragrance, especially during the evening.

Anisoptera odorata is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
