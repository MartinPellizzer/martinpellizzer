Anthericum triquetrum var. trinervis

Anthericum triquetrum var. trinervis, commonly known as the three-angled lily-of-the-valley, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, white, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring. It prefers moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas.
