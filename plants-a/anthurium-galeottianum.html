Anthurium galeottianum

Anthurium galeottianum, commonly known as the Galeotti's anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-like inflorescences that are often surrounded by colorful, modified leaves called spatulae. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Anthurium galeottianum is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
