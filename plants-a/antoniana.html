Antoniana

Antoniana, commonly known as the African violet, is a popular ornamental plant native to East Africa.

It belongs to the genus Saintpaulia and is characterized by its vibrant, velvety flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant typically features rosettes of dark green, hairy leaves and produces flowers on tall, erect stems. Antoniana thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

It is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its adaptability and aesthetic appeal.
