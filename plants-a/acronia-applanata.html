Acronia applanata

Acronia applanata, commonly known as the flatleaf acronia, is a species of flowering plant belonging to the family Apocynaceae.

It is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves that are typically elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually white or pale yellow in color. Acronia applanata is native to certain regions of South America, particularly in Brazil.
