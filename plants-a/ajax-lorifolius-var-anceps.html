Ajax lorifolius var. anceps

Ajax lorifolius var. anceps, commonly known as the broad-leaf ajax, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often dark green and glossy. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires a humid, warm climate to flourish.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
