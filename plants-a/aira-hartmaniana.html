Aira hartmaniana

Aira hartmaniana, commonly known as Hartman's air grass, is a flowering plant belonging to the Poaceae family.

It is native to the western United States, particularly found in arid and semi-arid regions. The plant is characterized by its tufted growth habit and small, spike-like inflorescences. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-gray hue due to a dense covering of fine hairs.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
