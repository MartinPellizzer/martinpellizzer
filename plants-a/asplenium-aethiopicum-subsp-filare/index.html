Asplenium Aethiopicum Subsp. Filare

Asplenium aethiopicum subsp. filare is a lesser-known fern species native to parts of Africa and the Middle East. It is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds that give it a lacy appearance. This plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in rocky or forested areas. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively obscure in horticultural circles. Its unique structure and subtle elegance make it a fascinating subject for botanical study and cultivation.
