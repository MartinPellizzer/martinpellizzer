Asteriscus daltonii

Asteriscus daltonii, commonly known as Dalton's asterisk, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in arid environments. It produces small, yellowish to reddish flowers that bloom in the spring.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
