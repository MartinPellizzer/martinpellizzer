Afromendoncia phytocrenoides

Afromendoncia phytocrenoides, commonly known as the African water fern, is a unique aquatic plant native to the wetlands of sub-Saharan Africa.

The plant thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments, often forming dense mats that provide habitat for small aquatic organisms. Its reproductive structures are borne on specialized stalks, and it reproduces both sexually and asexually.
