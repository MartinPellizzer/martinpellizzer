Abies olcayana

Abies olcayana, commonly known as the Turkish fir, is a coniferous tree native to Turkey.

It belongs to the genus Abies, which includes various species of fir trees. The plant is characterized by its conical shape, dark green needles, and distinctive cones. It typically grows in mountainous regions with cool, moist climates.

The botanical features of Abies olcayana make it an important species in its native ecosystem.
