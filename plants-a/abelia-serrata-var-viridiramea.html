Abelia serrata var. viridiramea

Abelia serrata var. viridiramea, commonly known as the green-branch abelia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to China.

It features narrow, dark green leaves that remain foliage throughout the year, contributing to its ornamental value. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale pink flowers in late spring to early summer. Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for small gardens or container planting.

It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it adaptable to various garden conditions.
