Adenolinum perenne

Adenolinum perenne, commonly known as the evergreen lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of fragrant, white or pale pink flowers. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and ability to thrive in well-drained soils. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

Adenolinum perenne is also valued for its resilience to drought and mild frosts.
