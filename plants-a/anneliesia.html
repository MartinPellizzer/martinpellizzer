Anneliesia

Anneliesia, commonly known as the silverleaf orchid, is a rare and exotic plant native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking silver-green foliage and delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Anneliesia's unique botanical features make it a sought-after specimen in botanical gardens and among plant enthusiasts.

Its cultivation is challenging due to its specific environmental needs and limited natural habitat.
