Asterochaete nitens

Asterochaete nitens, commonly known as the slender aster.

It is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa, particularly South Africa. The species belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is known for its composite flower heads. Asterochaete nitens typically grows in grassland and rocky habitats, and it produces small, daisy-like flowers.
