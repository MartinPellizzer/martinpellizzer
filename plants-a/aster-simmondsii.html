Aster simmondsii

Aster simmondsii, commonly known as the desert aster, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its hairy stems and clustered blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments, such as deserts and rocky soils. Its leaves are narrow and often covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.

Aster simmondsii plays a role in supporting local pollinators and is adapted to thrive in harsh, dry conditions.
