Aerangis × primulina

Aerangis × primulina, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a cross between Aerangis and Primulina species.

It is a hybrid orchid that exhibits a combination of traits from its parent plants, including vibrant flower colors and a compact growth habit. The plant typically produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Due to its ornamental value, it is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
