Alternanthera nodiflora var. lanceolata

Alternanthera nodiflora var. lanceolata, commonly known as the red-veined joyweed, is a striking aquatic plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves that often display vibrant red veins against a deep green background. This variety thrives in shallow, slow-moving waters and is popular in aquariums for its ornamental value. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or white.

It is known for its ability to tolerate a range of water conditions, making it a versatile choice for both beginner and experienced aquarists.
