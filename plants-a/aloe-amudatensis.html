Aloe amudatensis

Aloe amudatensis, commonly known as the Amudatensis Aloe, is a species native to South Africa.

It is a succulent plant characterized by its fleshy, upright stems and rosettes of thick, spiky leaves. The leaves are typically green with a bluish tinge and have a waxy coating that helps reduce water loss. It produces yellow or orange flowers during the summer months, which are clustered in inflorescences.

This plant is adapted to arid environments and is often used in traditional medicine for its soothing properties.
