Alsophila verruculospinula

Alsophila verruculospinula, commonly known as the hairy spinous tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and a crown of feathery, pinnate fronds that can reach several meters in length. The fronds are covered with small, spiny structures, giving the plant its distinctive appearance. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical rainforests.

Its unique morphology and ecological role make it an important species in its native habitat.
