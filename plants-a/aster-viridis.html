Aster viridis

Aster viridis, commonly known as green starflower, is a herbaceous perennial native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features slender, upright stems that bear opposite, lance-shaped leaves with hairy surfaces. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white to pale blue, typically blooming in late summer to early autumn. Aster viridis thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sun.

It is often found in meadows, woodland edges, and along stream banks.
