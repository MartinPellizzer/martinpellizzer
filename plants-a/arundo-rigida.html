Arundo rigida

Arundo rigida, commonly known as giant reed, is a tall, perennial grass native to the Mediterranean region.

It features narrow, sword-shaped leaves and can grow up to 6 meters in height. The plant is characterized by its robust, fibrous stalks and is often found near water sources. It is highly adaptable, thriving in a variety of soil types and climates.

Due to its rapid growth and invasive tendencies, it is sometimes considered a weed in certain regions.
