Asphodelus tenuifolius f. micranthus

Asphodelus tenuifolius f. micranthus, commonly known as the narrow-leaved asphodel, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, grass-like leaves that are typically dark green and narrow in appearance. The plant produces spikes of small, pale yellow to white flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its botanical characteristics make it a distinctive and ornamental addition to Mediterranean-style gardens.
