Astragalus minshanensis

Astragalus minshanensis, commonly known as the Minshan milkvetch, is a flowering plant species native to China.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers and compound leaves. The plant is typically found in rocky and mountainous regions, thriving in arid and semi-arid environments. Astragalus minshanensis plays a role in soil stabilization and is of interest for its potential medicinal properties.

Its ecological significance highlights its importance in local biodiversity and ecosystem health.
