Alsomitra capricornica

Alsomitra capricornica, commonly known as the horned bean, is a tropical climbing plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive, horn-like seed pods. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that are often clustered in racemes. Its leaves are trifoliate, consisting of three oval-shaped leaflets.

The seeds are enclosed in elongated, woody pods that split open when mature, dispersing the seeds by wind.
