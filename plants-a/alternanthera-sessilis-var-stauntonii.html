Alternanthera sessilis var. stauntonii

Alternanthera sessilis var. stauntonii, commonly known as the red-leaf alternanthera, is a striking aquatic plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red or pink foliage, which becomes more intense under strong light and nutrient-rich conditions. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often arranged oppositely along its stems. It thrives in tropical and subtropical freshwater environments, typically in slow to moderate water currents.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for aquariums and water gardens.
