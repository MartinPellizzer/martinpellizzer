Azadehdelia

Azadehdelia, commonly known as the Persian violet, is a flowering plant native to the Middle East and parts of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Violaceae family and is characterized by its attractive, violet-blue flowers that bloom in late spring. Its ornamental value is enhanced by its fragrant blossoms and ability to thrive in temperate climates.
