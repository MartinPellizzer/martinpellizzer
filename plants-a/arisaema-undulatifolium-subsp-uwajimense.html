Arisaema undulatifolium subsp. uwajimense

Arisaema undulatifolium subsp.

uwajimense, commonly known as the Uwajima zigzag arum, is a member of the Araceae family. It is native to Japan and is characterized by its distinctive, undulating, heart-shaped leaves.

The plant produces a spadix surrounded by a spathe, typical of the arum family. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forested areas.

Its botanical features make it a unique and visually striking plant in horticultural collections.
