Ancistrocactus scheeri f. brevihamatus

Ancistrocactus scheeri f. brevihamatus, commonly known as the short-stemmed scheeri cactus, is a compact, spineless cactus native to Mexico.

It features a globular or cylindrical stem that is typically green to bluish-green in color. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and succulent collections.
