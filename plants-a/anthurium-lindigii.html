Anthurium lindigii

Anthurium lindigii, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-like inflorescences that range in color from red to orange, which are surrounded by a showy spathe. Anthurium lindigii thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.

It is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and long-lasting blooms.
