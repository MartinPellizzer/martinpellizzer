Asterolasia correifolia var. muelleri

Asterolasia correifolia var. muelleri, commonly known as the Mueller's asterolasia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered yellow flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to arid environments.
