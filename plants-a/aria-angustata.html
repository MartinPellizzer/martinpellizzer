Aria angustata

Aria angustata, commonly known as the narrow-leafed arrowhead, is a species of flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the family Araceae, which includes plants characterized by their unique inflorescence called a spadix. The plant features heart-shaped leaves that are typically narrow and pointed, giving it its common name. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers surrounded by a spathe, which is a modified leaf.

Aria angustata is often found in wetlands and marshy areas, where it thrives in moist, nutrient-rich soils.
