Amaryllis × bellabister

Amaryllis × bellabister, commonly known as the hybrid amaryllis, is a flowering plant resulting from the crossbreeding of different amaryllis species.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors such as red, pink, white, and yellow. The plant typically produces large, trumpet-shaped blooms that are often displayed in gardens or as potted plants. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Amaryllis × bellabister is popular for its ability to bloom reliably during the holiday season, making it a favored ornamental plant.
