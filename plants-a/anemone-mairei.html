Anemone mairei

Anemone mairei, commonly known as the Maire's anemone, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its showy, white to pinkish flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has finely divided, fern-like leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine hairs. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically found in forest understories.

Anemone mairei is valued for its ornamental beauty and is also an important part of New Zealand's native flora.
