Arisaema bathycoleum

Arisaema bathycoleum, commonly known as the deep-rooted arrowhead, is a unique member of the Araceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia, particularly in countries like Vietnam and Thailand. The plant features a striking, heart-shaped leaf that can grow up to 30 centimeters in diameter. Its inflorescence consists of a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe, which adds to its ornamental appeal.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species in the wild.
