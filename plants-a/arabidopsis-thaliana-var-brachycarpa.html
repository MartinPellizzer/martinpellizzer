Arabidopsis thaliana var. brachycarpa

Arabidopsis thaliana var. brachycarpa, commonly known as the short-seed Arabidopsis, is a variant of the thale cress plant.

It is a small, annual herb native to Europe and parts of Asia. This plant has simple, alternate leaves and produces small, yellow flowers. Its fruit is a short, cylindrical silique, distinguishing it from other Arabidopsis varieties.

It is often used in botanical studies due to its genetic simplicity and rapid life cycle.
