Aloe dolomitica

Aloe dolomitica, commonly known as the dolomite aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that store water, allowing it to survive in arid environments. The plant typically grows in rocky or well-drained soils, often in coastal regions. Its leaves are usually blue-green in color and bear white tubercles with spines.

Aloe dolomitica is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional medicinal uses.
