Arabis tenuis

Arabis tenuis, commonly known as slender rockcress, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like cabbage and mustard. This low-growing plant typically reaches a height of about 10 to 30 centimeters and has basal leaves that are narrow and linear. Its flowers are small, white or pale yellow, and arranged in clusters, blooming in early spring.

Arabis tenuis thrives in rocky or gravelly soils and is often found in alpine or coastal environments.
