Alstroemeria × orpetiae

Alstroemeria × orpetiae, commonly known as the hybrid alstroemeria, is a perennial plant belonging to the family Alstroemeriaceae.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, orange, yellow, and pink. The plant typically grows to a height of 30 to 60 centimeters, with strap-like leaves that are dark green and glossy. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

The hybrid nature of Alstroemeria × orpetiae results from crosses between different alstroemeria species, enhancing its ornamental value and adaptability.
