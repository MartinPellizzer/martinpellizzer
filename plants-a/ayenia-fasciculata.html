Ayenia fasciculata

Ayenia fasciculata, commonly known as the clustered ayenia, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant produces small, yellow to orange flowers that bloom in late summer and early fall. Ayenia fasciculata typically grows in moist, sandy soils along stream banks and in open woodlands.

Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that form dense clusters, giving it its common name.
