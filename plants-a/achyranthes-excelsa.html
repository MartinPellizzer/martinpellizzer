Achyranthes excelsa

Achyranthes excelsa, commonly known as the tall smartweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem that can grow up to 2 meters in height. The plant has alternate, lance-shaped leaves that are typically hairy and have a reddish or purplish hue. Its small, greenish flowers are arranged in dense clusters at the top of the stem.

Achyranthes excelsa is often found in moist soils along streambanks and in disturbed areas.
