Alsine × alpestris

Alsine × alpestris, commonly known as the alpine sandwort, is a hybrid plant belonging to the family Montiaceae.

It is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit and features small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or white. The plant thrives in dry, rocky, or sandy soils, often found in alpine or arctic regions. Its leaves are usually narrow and fleshy, adapted to conserve water in harsh environments.

Alsine × alpestris is known for its resilience and is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and hardiness.
