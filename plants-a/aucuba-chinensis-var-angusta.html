Aucuba chinensis var. angusta

Aucuba chinensis var. angusta, commonly known as the narrow-leaf Japanese aucuba, is a shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features dark green, oval-shaped leaves that are narrower than those of the standard Aucuba chinensis. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by red berries that are attractive to birds. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in shaded environments.

The leaves are usually evergreen, providing year-round interest in gardens.
