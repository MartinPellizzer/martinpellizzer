Alsophila latebrosa var. ornata

Alsophila latebrosa var. ornata, commonly known as the ornate tree fern, is a species native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and a crown of feathery fronds that can reach several meters in length. The plant belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is known for its slow growth and longevity. Its fronds are typically dark green and have a glossy appearance, making it a popular ornamental plant.
