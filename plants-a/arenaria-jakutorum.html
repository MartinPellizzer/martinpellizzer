Arenaria jakutorum

Arenaria jakutorum, commonly known as the Japanese sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to Japan.

It typically thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in coastal or mountainous regions. The plant features small, white or pink flowers that bloom in early spring, adding a delicate touch to its habitat. Its leaves are linear and closely spaced, forming a dense mat that helps stabilize the soil.

Arenaria jakutorum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in preventing erosion in harsh environments.
