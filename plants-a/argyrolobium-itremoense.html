Argyrolobium itremoense

Argyrolobium itremoense, commonly known as the Itremo silverlobium, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Ethiopia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is characterized by its legume fruits and often nitrogen-fixing abilities. The plant features small, pea-like flowers that are typically pale blue or white in color. It grows in rocky and well-drained soils, often in high-altitude environments.

Its botanical structure includes compound leaves and a taproot system adapted to its arid habitat.
