Alternanthera polygonoides

Alternanthera polygonoides, commonly known as the red-veined joyweed, is a striking aquatic or semi-aquatic plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red veins contrasting with its bright green leaves, making it a popular choice for aquariums and water gardens. The plant thrives in warm, humid environments with moderate to high light exposure. It prefers soft to moderately hard water and can grow both submerged and emersed, depending on the conditions.

Its rapid growth rate and aesthetic appeal make it a versatile and attractive addition to aquatic landscapes.
