Alloplectus doratostylus

Alloplectus doratostylus, commonly known as the narrow-leaved cress, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, erect growth habit and pinnately divided leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in racemes, which bloom in early spring. Its flowers are typically self-pollinated, and the plant is often found in moist, calcareous soils.

Alloplectus doratostylus is native to Europe and parts of western Asia.
