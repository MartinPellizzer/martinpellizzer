Astronidium bracteatum

Astronidium bracteatum, commonly known as the velvetleaf sterculia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree that typically grows up to 10 meters in height. The plant features dark green, glossy leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a leathery texture. It produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds.

The plant is often found in lowland rainforests and is valued for its ornamental and medicinal properties.
