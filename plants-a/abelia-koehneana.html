Abelia koehneana

Abelia koehneana, commonly known as the Chinese abelia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features attractive, fragrant, tubular flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained soil.

Abelia koehneana is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various garden conditions.
