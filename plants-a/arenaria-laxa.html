Arenaria laxa

Arenaria laxa, commonly known as the sandspurry, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically thrives in sandy or gravelly soils. The plant produces small, white or pink flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are succulent and fleshy, adapted to conserve water in arid conditions.

Arenaria laxa is often found in coastal dunes and other dry, open habitats.
