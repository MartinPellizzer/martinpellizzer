Arachnis labrosa f. labrosa

Arachnis labrosa f. labrosa, commonly known as the velvet tangerine, is a hybrid citrus plant characterized by its small, round, and fragrant fruits.

The plant is a member of the Rutaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia, particularly Thailand. It is distinguished by its thick, velvety rind and a sweet, tangy flavor in its pulp. The leaves are dark green, glossy, and have a slightly serrated edge.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value as well as its edible fruit.
