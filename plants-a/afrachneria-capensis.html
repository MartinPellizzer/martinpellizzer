Afrachneria capensis

Afrachneria capensis, commonly known as the Cape honeysuckle, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 2 to 5 meters. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular, yellow-orange flowers that bloom throughout the year. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and have a rough texture with serrated edges.

The plant is adapted to thrive in full sun and well-drained soils, making it suitable for coastal and Mediterranean climates.
