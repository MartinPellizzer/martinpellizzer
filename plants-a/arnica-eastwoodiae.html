Arnica eastwoodiae

Arnica eastwoodiae, commonly known as Eastwood's arnica, is a rare flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the genus Arnica, which is part of the sunflower family, Asteraceae. The plant produces yellow-orange flowers with prominent central disk florets, typical of the genus. It thrives in alpine environments, where it adapts to cold, high-altitude conditions.
