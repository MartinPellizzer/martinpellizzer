Asyneuma anhuiense

Asyneuma anhuiense, commonly known as the Anhui tansy, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its composite flower heads and aromatic foliage. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while the flowers consist of yellow disk florets surrounded by white to pale yellow ray florets.

Asyneuma anhuiense is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
