Arisaema serratum var. blumei

Arisaema serratum var. blumei, commonly known as the Blume's spiny arrowhead, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, spiky leaves and a spadix inflorescence surrounded by a spathe. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and is known for its striking, arrowhead-shaped foliage. It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a decorative plant.

The species is also noted for its medicinal properties in traditional Chinese medicine.
