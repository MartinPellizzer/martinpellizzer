Alstroemeria aurea var. valparadisiaca

Alstroemeria aurea var. valparadisiaca, commonly known as the golden alstroemeria, is a striking perennial plant native to South America.

It features vibrant, tubular flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, often with contrasting green markings. The plant has strap-like, grassy leaves that are glossy and arranged in a fan shape. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.

Its botanical beauty and long-lasting blooms contribute to its popularity in horticulture.
