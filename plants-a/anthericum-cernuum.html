Anthericum cernuum

Anthericum cernuum, commonly known as the nodding lady's tresses, is a flowering plant belonging to the orchid family (Orchidaceae).

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and its distinctive, fragrant, spiral arrangement of flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and wet meadows. Its flowers are usually pink to purple in color, and it produces small, round, brownish seeds.

Anthericum cernuum is often found in temperate regions of Europe and parts of Asia.
