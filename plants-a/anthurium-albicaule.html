Anthurium albicaule

Anthurium albicaule, commonly known as the white-stemmed anthurium, is a tropical flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces vibrant red spadices, which are specialized inflorescences that serve as the actual flowers. Anthurium albicaule thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and ornamental purposes.
