Anthurium lancea var. canarense

Anthurium lancea var. canarense, commonly known as the Canary Island anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is characterized by its long, narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a metallic sheen. The plant produces striking spathes that range in color from deep red to orange, often with a contrasting yellow or white interior. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

This variety is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
