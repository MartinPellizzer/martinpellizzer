Acer amplum var. jianshuiense

Acer amplum var. jianshuiense, commonly known as the Jianshui maple, is a variant of the common maple native to China.

It is characterized by its distinctive palmate leaves that turn vibrant red in the fall. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions with well-drained soil and moderate climates. Its bark is smooth and grayish-brown, becoming fissured with age.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and parks.
