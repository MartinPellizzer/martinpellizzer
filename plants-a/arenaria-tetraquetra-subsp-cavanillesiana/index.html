Arenaria Tetraquetra Subsp. Cavanillesiana

Arenaria tetraquetra subsp. cavanillesiana is a lesser-known member of the Caryophyllaceae family known for its delicate beauty and resilience. This subspecies thrives in rocky and coastal environments, often found in the Mediterranean region. Its small, white flowers bloom in clusters, adding a subtle charm to its rugged habitat. Despite its modest appearance, it plays an important role in supporting local ecosystems. This plant is a testament to nature's ability to adapt and flourish in challenging conditions.
