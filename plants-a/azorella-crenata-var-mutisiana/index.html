Azorella Crenata Var. Mutisiana

Azorella crenata var. mutisiana is a unique and resilient plant native to the high Andes of South America. It thrives in extreme conditions, often growing at elevations above 4,000 meters where temperatures are cold and conditions are harsh. This variety is known for its dense, mat-like growth and ability to survive in nutrient-poor soils. Its slow growth rate and long lifespan make it a rare and valuable species in alpine ecosystems. Despite its tough environment, it plays an important role in stabilizing soil and supporting local biodiversity.
