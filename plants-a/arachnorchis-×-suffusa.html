Arachnorchis × suffusa

Arachnorchis × suffusa, commonly known as the hybrid spider orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Australia.

It is a terrestrial herb with a short, stocky stem and a single, showy flower that typically blooms in spring. The plant features a complex root system and possesses specialized structures called pseudobulbs, which store water and nutrients. Its flowers are characterized by their intricate, spider-like appearance, with long, slender sepals and petals.

Arachnorchis × suffusa is often found in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests and undergrowth.
