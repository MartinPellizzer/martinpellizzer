Ardisia imperialis

Ardisia imperialis, commonly known as the imperial ardisia, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are typically elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, white flowers that are clustered in umbels, followed by bright red berries that are attractive to birds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant in many regions.
