Aloe atrovirens

Aloe atrovirens, commonly known as the dark green aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically dark green with a bluish tint, often marked by white or yellowish teeth along the edges. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow flowers during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it suitable for arid climates.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
