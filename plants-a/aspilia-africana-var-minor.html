Aspilia africana var. minor

Aspilia africana var. minor, commonly known as the lesser flame plant, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking, bright yellow to orange ray florets. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub with narrow, lance-shaped leaves. It produces small, tubular disk flowers surrounded by the showy outer florets.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
