Armeria soleirolii

Armeria soleirolii, commonly known as the Soleiroli's thrift, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces dense clusters of small, white or pinkish flowers. The plant has narrow, gray-green leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Armeria soleirolii is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
