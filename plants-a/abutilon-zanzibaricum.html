Abutilon zanzibaricum

Abutilon zanzibaricum, commonly known as the Zanzibar hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of East Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, often with a red throat. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are typically hairy, and it produces capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Abutilon zanzibaricum is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
