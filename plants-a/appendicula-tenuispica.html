Appendicula tenuispica

Appendicula tenuispica, commonly known as the slender-spurred appendicula, is a member of the Gesneriaceae family.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered with a fine, soft pubescence.

Appendicula tenuispica thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found in forest understories.
