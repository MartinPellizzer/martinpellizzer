Asterothamnus poliifolius

Asterothamnus poliifolius, commonly known as the silky star-thorn, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and robust, often hairy stems. The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its leaves are narrow and often covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

The flowers, which appear in spring, are small and yellow, arranged in clusters that resemble a star.
