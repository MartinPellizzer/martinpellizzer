Achyrocline candicans

Achyrocline candicans, commonly known as the Hawaiian sandverbena, is a flowering plant native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It is a small shrub that typically grows in coastal and lowland areas, characterized by its succulent, hairy leaves. The plant produces small, white to pale blue flowers that bloom in clusters, often in the spring and summer months. It is adapted to the dry, sunny conditions of its native habitat and is considered an endemic species.
