Astrotricha ledifolia var. linearis

Astrotricha ledifolia var. linearis, commonly known as the narrow-leaf starwort, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which is characterized by its composite flower heads and ray florets. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, where it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun exposure. Its leaves are linear and often covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are small and clustered in a composite head.

This variant is distinguished by its slender foliage compared to other varieties of Astrotricha.
