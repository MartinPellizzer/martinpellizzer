Argostemma condensum

Argostemma condensum, commonly known as the denseleaf arrowhead, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It belongs to the family Araceae, which includes many aquatic and semi-aquatic species. The plant is characterized by its arrowhead-shaped leaves and produces small, white flowers arranged in a spadix, typical of the Araceae family. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as marshes and along stream banks.

Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense colonies in suitable habitats.
