Asplenium auritum var. diversifolium

Asplenium auritum var. diversifolium, commonly known as the Variegated Ear Lobes Fern, is a strikingly ornamental fern native to the southeastern United States.

It features delicate, arching fronds with a distinctive ear-like shape at the tips, which are often adorned with variegated, silver-white markings. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and prefers well-draining, acidic soil. Its foliage is evergreen, providing year-round visual interest in both garden and indoor settings.

This variety is prized for its unique appearance and adaptability to humid, tropical climates.
