Anathallis breviscapa

Anathallis breviscapa, commonly known as the short-stemmed spider orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to South America.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid characterized by its delicate, spider-like flowers and short, hairy stems. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its flowers are usually greenish-yellow with a distinctive, intricate lip that aids in pollination by specific insect species.

Anathallis breviscapa exhibits typical orchid morphological features, including a pseudobulb and fibrous root system.
