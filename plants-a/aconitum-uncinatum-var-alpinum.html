Aconitum uncinatum var. alpinum

Aconitum uncinatum var. alpinum, commonly known as the Alpine monkshood, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the alpine regions of Europe.

It features striking, hood-shaped blue to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has dark green, heart-shaped leaves with a hairy texture. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Due to its toxic alkaloids, it should be handled with care.
