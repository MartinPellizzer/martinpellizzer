Anthurium karstenianum

Anthurium karstenianum, commonly known as the velvet leaf anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a velvety texture. The plant produces striking spadices, which are typically yellow or orange, surrounded by a colorful spathe. It thrives in humid, warm environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.

Anthurium karstenianum is popular as a decorative plant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
