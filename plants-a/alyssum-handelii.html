Alyssum handelii

Alyssum handelii, commonly known as the rock alyssum, is a low-growing perennial plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It features dense clusters of small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink, blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has silvery-gray foliage with small, rounded leaves that are covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it an excellent choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Alyssum handelii is also valued for its ability to attract beneficial insects such as bees and butterflies.
