Ardisia dressleri

Ardisia dressleri, commonly known as the Hawaiian Christmasberry, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are oval in shape and have a slight waxy texture. The plant produces small, white flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences, followed by bright red berries that resemble Christmas ornaments. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Ardisia dressleri is an important component of the native Hawaiian flora and is often found in coastal and lowland forests.
