Aster neoelegans

Aster neoelegans, commonly known as the New Zealand aster, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and along stream banks. Its flowers are usually blue to purple in color, with ray florets surrounding a central disk of tubular flowers.

Aster neoelegans is valued for its ornamental appeal and plays a role in local ecosystems by supporting pollinators.
