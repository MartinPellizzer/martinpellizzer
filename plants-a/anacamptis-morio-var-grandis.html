Anacamptis morio var. grandis

Anacamptis morio var. grandis, commonly known as the Large Green Orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to southern Europe.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem and large, lance-shaped leaves. The flowers are typically greenish-yellow with a distinctive purple or brown lip. It blooms from late spring to early summer, producing clusters of fragrant, showy flowers.

This variety is often found in open woodlands and grassy areas, thriving in well-drained soils.
