Adiantum hispidulum var. minus

Adiantum hispidulum var. minus, commonly known as the lesser holly fern, is a delicate fern native to the southeastern United States.

It features finely divided, feather-like fronds that are typically bright green with a silvery underside. The plant thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in woodland areas. Its small, clustered sporangia are located on the undersides of the fronds, typical of the Adiantum genus.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of care in humid, indoor settings.
