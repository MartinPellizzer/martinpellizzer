Acanthoglossum nervosum

Acanthoglossum nervosum, commonly known as the narrow-veined acanthoglossum, is a flowering plant species belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and small, daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. Due to its unique morphology and ecological niche, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
