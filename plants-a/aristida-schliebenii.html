Aristida schliebenii

Aristida schliebenii, commonly known as Schlieben's threeawn, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences composed of numerous bract-like structures. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in native plant gardens.
