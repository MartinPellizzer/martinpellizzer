Asplenium badense

Asplenium badense, commonly known as the Baden spleenwort, is a species of fern belonging to the family Aspleniaceae.

It is a small, terrestrial fern typically found in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. The plant has a distinctive appearance, with narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often divided into smaller segments. Its fronds are usually dark green and can reach lengths of up to 30 centimeters.

Asplenium badense is known for its ability to thrive in humid climates and is often used in horticultural settings for its ornamental value.
