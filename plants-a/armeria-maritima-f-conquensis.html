Armeria maritima f. conquensis

Armeria maritima f. conquensis, commonly known as the Conquense thrift, is a coastal plant native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It is a low-growing, evergreen perennial that thrives in sandy or gravelly soils. The plant features dense clusters of white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in spring. Its leaves are linear and silver-gray, providing a striking contrast to the white blooms.

It is well adapted to salt spray and harsh maritime conditions, making it a hardy ornamental plant for coastal gardens.
