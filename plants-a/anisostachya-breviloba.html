Anisostachya breviloba

Anisostachya breviloba, commonly known as the narrow-leaved panic grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and produces small, spikelet-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. Its botanical features include a fibrous root system and a clumping growth habit.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
