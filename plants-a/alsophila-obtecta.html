Alsophila obtecta

Alsophila obtecta, commonly known as the shield fern, is a species of tree fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnately compound fronds that can reach several meters in length. The fronds are typically dark green and have a leathery texture, with a distinctive shield-shaped base. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

The plant plays an important ecological role by providing habitat and shelter for various forest organisms.
