Asteromyrtus tranganensis

Asteromyrtus tranganensis, commonly known as the Tranganese asteromyrtus, is a species of flowering plant native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family, which includes eucalyptus and other aromatic plants. The plant is characterized by its small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. Its leaves are typically narrow and lance-shaped, with a fragrant scent when crushed.

This species is often found in moist, shaded environments within its native habitat.
