Aerangis Gravenreuthii

Aerangis gravenreuthii is a rare and exotic orchid native to southern Africa. It is known for its striking, fragrant blooms that appear in late winter or early spring. This plant thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires careful cultivation. Its unique appearance and limited distribution make it a prized specimen among orchid enthusiasts. Due to habitat loss, it is also considered vulnerable in the wild.
