Aristolochia setchuenensis var. holotricha

Aristolochia setchuenensis var. holotricha, commonly known as the Chinese birthwort, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Aristolochiaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, tubular, and often fragrant flowers. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface, and its stems are typically slender and climbing. It is known for its medicinal properties in traditional Chinese medicine, though it contains toxic compounds that require careful handling.

The species is also valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
