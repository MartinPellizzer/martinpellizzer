Anthemis oretana

Anthemis oretana, commonly known as the Greek tansy, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and wastelands. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are erect and somewhat woody.

Anthemis oretana is valued for its aromatic properties and has been used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
