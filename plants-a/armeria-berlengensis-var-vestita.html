Armeria berlengensis var. vestita

Armeria berlengensis var. vestita, commonly known as the Berling Strait thrift, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Azores.

It features dense clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has linear, grass-like leaves that are typically covered in a fine, white pubescence. It thrives in well-drained, sandy or rocky soils and is often found in coastal or maritime environments.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in challenging climates.
