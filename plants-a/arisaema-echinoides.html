Arisaema echinoides

Arisaema echinoides, commonly known as the spiny arrowhead, is a member of the Araceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its unique, spiky spathes that enclose the inflorescence. The plant produces a single, upright, spadix that bears both male and female flowers. Its foliage consists of heart-shaped leaves with a rough, textured surface.

Arisaema echinoides is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine in some regions.
