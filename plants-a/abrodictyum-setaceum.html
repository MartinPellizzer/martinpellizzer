Abrodictyum setaceum

Abrodictyum setaceum, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often near streams or in humid forests. Its leaves are elongated and grass-like, contributing to its overall delicate appearance.

Abrodictyum setaceum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
