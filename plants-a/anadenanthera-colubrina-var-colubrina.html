Anadenanthera colubrina var. colubrina

Anadenanthera colubrina var. colubrina, commonly known as the jurema or copal tree, is a leguminous plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its compound leaves, which are often used in traditional medicine. The tree produces small, green pods containing seeds that are rich in nutrients. Its root system has been historically used by indigenous peoples for various purposes, including as a source of protein.

The plant is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it beneficial in ecological restoration efforts.
