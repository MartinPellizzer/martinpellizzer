Alsophila sarawakensis

Alsophila sarawakensis, commonly known as the Sarawak tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to Borneo.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate fronds. The plant has a slow growth rate and can reach heights of up to 10 meters in optimal conditions. Its rhizomes are fibrous and contribute to its structural support, while its fronds provide shade and habitat for various organisms.

Alsophila sarawakensis plays a significant role in the ecological balance of its native rainforests.
