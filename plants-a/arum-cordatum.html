Arum cordatum

Arum cordatum, commonly known as the arrowhead plant, is a tropical flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are glossy and often have a distinctive white vein pattern. The plant produces striking, spatulate-shaped inflorescences with a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often used in water gardens.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.
