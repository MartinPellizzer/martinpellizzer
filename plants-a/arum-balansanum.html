Arum balansanum

Arum balansanum, commonly known as the Balansan arrowleaf, is a striking tropical plant native to the Philippines.

It features large, arrow-shaped leaves that are often a deep green with a glossy sheen. The plant produces striking, spatulate-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale pink, surrounded by a showy, leaf-like bract. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
