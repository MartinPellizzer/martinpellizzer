Artemisia dracunculus var. dracunculus

Artemisia dracunculus var. dracunculus, commonly known as tarragon, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features aromatic, linear leaves with a distinctive camphor-like scent. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in summer. Its roots are fibrous, and the stems are woody at the base.

Tarragon is often cultivated for its culinary uses and medicinal properties.
