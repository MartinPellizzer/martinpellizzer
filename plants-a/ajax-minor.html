Ajax minor

Ajax minor, commonly known as the lesser ajax, is a small, herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Apiaceae family, which includes carrots and parsley, and is characterized by its finely divided, fern-like leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in umbels, typical of the family. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
