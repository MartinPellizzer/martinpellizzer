Anoectochilus striatus

Anoectochilus striatus, commonly known as the streaked orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial herb with a short, creeping rhizome and erect, linear leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in a spike, typically blooming during the rainy season. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
