Achimenes × guentheri

Achimenes × guentheri, commonly known as the hybrid angel's trumpets, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Achimenes hirta and Achimenes tubiflora. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, which bloom throughout the growing season. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking vibrant, tropical blooms.
