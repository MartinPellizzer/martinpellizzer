Alsophila fragilis

Alsophila fragilis, commonly known as the slender tree fern, is a species native to the tropical and subtropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and a crown of feathery, pinnate fronds that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant grows in moist, shaded environments, typically along stream banks or in forest understories. Its fronds are typically bright green and can reach lengths of up to 2 meters.

Alsophila fragilis is a slow-growing plant that can live for several decades, making it a valuable component of its native ecosystems.
