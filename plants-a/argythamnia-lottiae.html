Argythamnia lottiae

Argythamnia lottiae, commonly known as the silverleaf thistle, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or arid environments.

Argythamnia lottiae is a perennial herb that can reach heights of up to 1 meter.
