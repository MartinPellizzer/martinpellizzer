Ambelania ternstroemiacea

Ambelania ternstroemiacea, commonly known as the Ternstroemiaceae plant, is a species belonging to the family Ternstroemiaceae.

It is a small, evergreen shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia. The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical in shape. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, typically greenish or yellowish in color, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits.

Ambelania ternstroemiacea is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest undergrowth.
