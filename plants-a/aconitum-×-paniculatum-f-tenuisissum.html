Aconitum × paniculatum f. tenuisissum

Aconitum × paniculatum f. tenuisissum, commonly known as the slender-flowered aconitum, is a hybrid perennial plant in the Ranunculaceae family.

It is characterized by its finely divided, fern-like leaves and clusters of small, blue to purple flowers. The species is native to the mountainous regions of Europe and is often found in moist, shaded habitats. Its flowers typically bloom in late summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

The plant is known for its ornamental value and is cultivated in gardens for its striking foliage and delicate blooms.
