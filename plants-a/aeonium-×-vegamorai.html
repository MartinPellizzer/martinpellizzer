Aeonium × vegamorai

Aeonium × vegamorai, commonly known as the Hybrid Aeonium, is a succulent plant native to the Canary Islands.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, with a waxy texture. The plant produces tall flower stalks bearing yellow or orange flowers, which appear in the spring. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Aeonium × vegamorai is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
