Acianthera eximia

Acianthera eximia, commonly known as the golden orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its showy, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in clusters, making it a striking ornamental plant. Acianthera eximia is valued for its beauty and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
