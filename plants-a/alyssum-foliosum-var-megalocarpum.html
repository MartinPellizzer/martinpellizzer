Alyssum foliosum var. megalocarpum

Alyssum foliosum var. megalocarpum, commonly known as the giant seed alyssum, is a perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features compact, mat-forming growth with small, dark green leaves and clusters of fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers. The plant produces large, showy seed pods that remain on the plant well into winter, adding ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking hardy, decorative plants.
