Astrotricha intermedia

Astrotricha intermedia, commonly known as the intermediate astertricha, is a perennial herb native to parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and often have a slightly hairy surface.

The flowers are usually yellow or orange, with ray florets that give it a daisy-like appearance.
