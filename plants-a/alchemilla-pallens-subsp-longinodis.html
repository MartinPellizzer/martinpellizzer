Alchemilla pallens subsp. longinodis

Alchemilla pallens subsp.

longinodis, commonly known as the long-stemmed lady's mantle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It features basal rosettes of narrow, silvery-green leaves with a distinctive waxy coating.

The plant produces upright flowering stems that bear clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers in late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.

Its ornamental value comes from its attractive foliage and delicate flowers, making it a popular choice for shady garden landscapes.
