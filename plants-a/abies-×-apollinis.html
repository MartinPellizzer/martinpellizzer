Abies × apollinis

It is characterized by its dense, pyramidal shape and dark green, needle-like foliage. The tree produces cone-like structures that mature to a reddish-brown color, typical of many fir species. It thrives in temperate climates with well-drained soil and moderate moisture.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes for its aesthetic appeal.
