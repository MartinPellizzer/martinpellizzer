Abolboda grandis

Abolboda grandis, commonly known as the grand magnolia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy white or pink flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant has broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often glossy in appearance. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Abolboda grandis is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and parks.
