Anthurium paraguayense var. coroicoanum

Anthurium paraguayense var. coroicoanum, commonly known as the Coroico anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Bolivia and Paraguay.

It features vibrant, heart-shaped leaves with a deep green color and a striking, spadix-like inflorescence. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers surrounded by a colorful spathe, which is a characteristic feature of the Araceae family. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.

The plant is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
