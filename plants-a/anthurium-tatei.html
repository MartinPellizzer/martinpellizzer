Anthurium tatei

Anthurium tatei, commonly known as the velvet leaf anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are velvety to the touch and come in shades of green, red, and purple. The plant produces small, spadix-shaped inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, which adds to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in humid, warm environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium tatei is popular as a houseplant due to its aesthetic value and relatively easy care requirements.
