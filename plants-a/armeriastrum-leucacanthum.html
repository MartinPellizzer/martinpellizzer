Armeriastrum leucacanthum

Armeriastrum leucacanthum, commonly known as silverleaf armeria, is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to the western United States.

It features dense clusters of small, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has silvery-gray foliage that provides a striking contrast to its delicate blooms. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its compact size and ornamental appeal make it a popular choice for coastal and arid landscapes.
