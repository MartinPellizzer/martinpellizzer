Aloe rakotonasoloi

Aloe rakotonasoloi, commonly known as the Madagascar aloe, is a succulent plant native to the arid regions of Madagascar.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that store water, allowing it to survive in dry conditions. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellowish-green flowers, which bloom during the rainy season. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their soothing properties.
