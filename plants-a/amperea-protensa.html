Amperea protensa

Amperea protensa, commonly known as the broadleaf arrowhead, is a tropical aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features elongated, heart-shaped leaves that are typically green with a waxy surface. The plant thrives in shallow freshwater environments, often found in ponds and slow-moving streams. Its roots are adapted for anchoring in soft substrates, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or white.

Amperea protensa is valued in aquatic gardens for its ornamental foliage and ability to provide habitat for aquatic life.
