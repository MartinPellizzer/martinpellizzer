Aster kyobuntensis

Aster kyobuntensis, commonly known as the Japanese aster, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its flowers are usually pink or purple, with ray florets surrounding a central disk of tubular flowers.

Aster kyobuntensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
