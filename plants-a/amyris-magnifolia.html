Amyris magnifolia

Amyris magnifolia, commonly known as the flame tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, bright red or orange flowers that bloom in dense clusters during the rainy season. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green, providing a striking contrast to its vibrant floral display. Amyris magnifolia is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and public spaces.

It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for warm, humid climates.
