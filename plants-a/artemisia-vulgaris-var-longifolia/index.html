Artemisia Vulgaris Var. Longifolia

Artemisia vulgaris var. longifolia is a hardy perennial plant commonly found in temperate regions around the world. Known for its distinctive silver-gray foliage and aromatic scent, it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun. This variant of the common wormwood is often used in herbal medicine and traditional remedies due to its medicinal properties. Its feathery leaves and small yellow flowers make it a popular choice for ornamental gardens. Despite its beauty, it can be invasive in some areas, requiring careful management.
