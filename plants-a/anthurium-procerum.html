Anthurium procerum

Anthurium procerum, commonly known as the slender anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped spathes that can range in color from deep red to orange. The plant produces small, greenish-white spadices inside the spatulate leaves, which are typical of the Araceae family. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.

Anthurium procerum is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its striking foliage and unique flowering structure.
