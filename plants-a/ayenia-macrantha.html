Ayenia macrantha

Ayenia macrantha, commonly known as the large-flowered ayenia, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, clustered flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant features narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are typically pink to purple in color, with five petals that are slightly notched at the edges.

Ayenia macrantha is well adapted to dry, sandy soils and is often found in fynbos vegetation.
