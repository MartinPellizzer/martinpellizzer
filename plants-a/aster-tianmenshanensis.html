Aster tianmenshanensis

Aster tianmenshanensis, commonly known as the Tianmen Mountain aster, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant produces showy, violet-blue or purple flower heads that bloom in late summer to early autumn. It thrives in high-altitude environments and is adapted to the cool, moist conditions of its native habitat.

Due to its limited distribution and unique appearance, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
