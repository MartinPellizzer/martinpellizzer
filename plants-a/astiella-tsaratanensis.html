Astiella tsaratanensis

Astiella tsaratanensis, commonly known as the Madagascar starwort, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and has small, narrow leaves. Its flowers are usually yellow or white, and it produces small, achene-like seeds.

Astiella tsaratanensis is of interest to botanists due to its unique adaptations and limited distribution.
