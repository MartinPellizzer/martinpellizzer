Aristolochia tequilana

Aristolochia tequilana, commonly known as Mexican birthwort, is a climbing vine native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and unique, fragrant flowers that resemble a pipe, hence its common name. The plant belongs to the Aristolochiaceae family and is known for its complex chemical composition. Its flowers are typically greenish-yellow with a strong, musky scent.

Due to its toxic properties, it is not recommended for use in traditional medicine without proper guidance.
