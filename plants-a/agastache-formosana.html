Agastache formosana

Agastache formosana, commonly known as the Taiwan hyssop, is a perennial herb native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant features showy, tubular flowers that range in color from purple to blue, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs, contributing to its ornamental value.

Agastache formosana thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.
