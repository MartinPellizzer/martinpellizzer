Ayenia laevigata

Ayenia laevigata, commonly known as the silky ayenia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are usually smooth and have a silvery or grayish underside, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Ayenia laevigata is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and coastal areas.
