Asplenium hookerianum var. baileyanum

Asplenium hookerianum var. baileyanum, commonly known as the Baileyanum spleenwort, is a fern species native to the eastern Himalayas.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that typically grows on tree trunks or rocks in moist, shaded environments. The plant has finely divided, needle-like leaves that are arranged in a compact rosette pattern. It belongs to the family Aspleniaceae and is characterized by its delicate, lace-like foliage.

Due to its restricted habitat, it is considered a rare and endemic species with limited distribution.
