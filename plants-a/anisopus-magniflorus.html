Anisopus magniflorus

Anisopus magniflorus, commonly known as the showy anisopus, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its striking, large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of yellow to orange. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, green, capsule-like fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or arid environments.

Anisopus magniflorus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
