Adodendron

Adodendron, commonly known as the rosebay willowherb, is a flowering plant native to Europe, Asia, and North America.

It belongs to the family Onagraceae and is characterized by its upright, branched stems and showy, pink or purple flowers. Adodendron is known for its adaptability and rapid growth, making it a common sight in various habitats.
