Adromischus cuneatus

Adromischus cuneatus, commonly known as the football plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, triangular leaves that are typically green with a slight reddish tinge under sunlight. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Its compact growth habit and unique leaf shape make it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
