Andropogon yunnanensis

Andropogon yunnanensis, commonly known as Yunnan switchgrass, is a perennial grass native to China.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and narrow leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forest margins or grasslands. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny florets.

Andropogon yunnanensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential use in ecological restoration projects.
