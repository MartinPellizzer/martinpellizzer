Aconitum × paniculatum f. perkalabense

Aconitum × paniculatum f. perkalabense, commonly known as the Perkalabense Aconite, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Ranunculaceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, upright growth habit and striking blue to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant features compound leaves with serrated edges, typical of the Aconitum genus. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade, making it a suitable addition to woodland gardens.

Due to its toxicity, it should be handled with care and kept away from children and pets.
