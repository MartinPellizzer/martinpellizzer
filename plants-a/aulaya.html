Aulaya

Aulaya, commonly known as the Indian bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayan region.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped, blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, rocky habitats and thrives in cool climates. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged oppositely along the stem.

Aulaya is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine for its purported health benefits.
