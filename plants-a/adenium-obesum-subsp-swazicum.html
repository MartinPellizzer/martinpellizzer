Adenium obesum subsp. swazicum

Adenium obesum subsp.

swazicum, commonly known as the Swazi bull's horn, is a succulent shrub native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its swollen, bottle-shaped trunk and thick, fleshy leaves.

The plant produces striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to red. It is adapted to arid environments and stores water in its stem for survival.

Its unique morphology and resilience make it a notable species in botanical studies and horticulture.
