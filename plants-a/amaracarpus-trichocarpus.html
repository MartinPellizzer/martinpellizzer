Amaracarpus trichocarpus

Amaracarpus trichocarpus, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical plant belonging to the Malvaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, soft hairs, giving it a velvety texture.
