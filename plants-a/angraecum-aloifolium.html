Angraecum aloifolium

Angraecum aloifolium, commonly known as the Madagascar orchid, is a species of orchid native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem and large, showy flowers that typically bloom at night. The plant produces striking white or pale pink flowers with long, slender petals and a prominent lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Angraecum aloifolium is a popular choice for collectors due to its unique appearance and rarity.
