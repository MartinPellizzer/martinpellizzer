Acacia sphaerogemma

Acacia sphaerogemma, commonly known as the globe acacia, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, spherical growth habit and small, fern-like leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in spherical clusters, which are typical of the Acacia genus. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy environments.

Due to its compact form and ornamental value, it is popular in horticulture for coastal gardens.
