Alyssum giosnanum subsp. explanatum

Alyssum giosnanum subsp.

explanatum, commonly known as the broadleaf alyssum, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region. It features small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in dense clusters, which bloom in early spring.

The plant has succulent, fleshy leaves that are typically oval or lance-shaped. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and long-lasting blooms.
