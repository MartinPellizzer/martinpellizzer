Amphiasma gracilicaule

Amphiasma gracilicaule, commonly known as the slender-stemmed orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, grass-like foliage and slender, erect stems that bear clusters of small, fragrant flowers. Native to tropical and subtropical regions, this plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial shade. Its unique morphology includes whorled leaves and spurred flowers, typical of many orchid species.

Amphiasma gracilicaule is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ecological importance in supporting pollinators.
