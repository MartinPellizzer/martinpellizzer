Ardisia × walkeri

Ardisia × walkeri, commonly known as the hybrid ardisia, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Myrsinaceae.

It is a naturally occurring hybrid between Ardisia crenata and Ardisia elliptica, characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers followed by bright red berries, which are attractive to birds. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and greenhouses worldwide.
