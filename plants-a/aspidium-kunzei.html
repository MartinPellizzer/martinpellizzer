Aspidium kunzei

Aspidium kunzei, commonly known as the broad-leafed water fern, is a species of fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its large, glossy, pinnate leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. Its fronds are biennial, with the fertile fronds appearing in the second year.

Aspidium kunzei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
