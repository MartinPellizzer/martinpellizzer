Amaryllis principis

Amaryllis principis, commonly known as the Cape Lily, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, erect stems that bear showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, and red. The plant produces large, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and containers.

Amaryllis principis is known for its ability to bloom reliably when provided with proper care and winter dormancy.
