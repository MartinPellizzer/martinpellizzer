Anemone vitifolia var. takasagomontana

Anemone vitifolia var. takasagomontana, commonly known as the Takasagomontana anemone, is a rare variant of the vitifolia anemone.

It is a herbaceous perennial native to Japan, characterized by its delicate, lace-like foliage and showy, white to pink flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest floors or stream banks. Its leaves are deeply lobed and often have a silvery-blue hue due to fine hair covering the surface.

The flowers bloom in early spring, adding a subtle yet elegant presence to its natural habitat.
