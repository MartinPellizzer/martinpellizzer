Aster chuanshanensis

Aster chuanshanensis, commonly known as the Chuanshan aster, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers. The plant is characterized by its blue to violet flowers and is often found in rocky or mountainous regions. It has narrow, lance-shaped leaves and is adapted to grow in high-altitude environments.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
