Aglaia calelanensis

Aglaia calelanensis, commonly known as the Philippine aglaia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Meliaceae.

It is native to the Philippines and is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers. Due to habitat loss, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native range.
