Asterochlaena orbicularis

Asterochlaena orbicularis, commonly known as the orbicular leaf spurge, is a perennial plant native to parts of Asia, particularly China.

It is characterized by its distinctive orbicular (rounded) leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the summer, attracting various pollinators. It is often found in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions.

Due to its ornamental value and hardy nature, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
