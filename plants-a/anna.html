Anna

Anna, commonly known as the common anna, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves, which consist of three oval-shaped leaflets. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are typically purple or pink in color. Anna is often found in temperate regions and thrives in well-drained soil.

It is also valued for its nitrogen-fixing ability, which enriches the soil.
