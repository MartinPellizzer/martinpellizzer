Austrocactus colloncurensis

Austrocactus colloncurensis, commonly known as the Colloncuri cactus, is a species native to Argentina.

It is a small, globular cactus with a ribbed stem and spines that provide protection against herbivores. The plant produces yellow flowers during the summer months, adding a vibrant touch to its arid habitat. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it well adapted to its desert environment.

Austrocactus colloncurensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in harsh conditions.
