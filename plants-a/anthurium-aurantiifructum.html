Anthurium aurantiifructum

Anthurium aurantiifructum, commonly known as the orange fruit anthurium, is a striking tropical plant native to Colombia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a glossy texture. The plant produces unusual, fleshy, orange-red berries that are both ornamental and edible. These berries are covered in a waxy coating and contain a single seed.

Anthurium aurantiifructum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in warm, humid environments.
