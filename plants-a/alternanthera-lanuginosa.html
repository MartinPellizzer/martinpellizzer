Alternanthera lanuginosa

Alternanthera lanuginosa, commonly known as the fuzzy Alternanthera, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its soft, hairy leaves that give it a distinctive, velvety appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or white in color. It thrives in moist, tropical environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Its rapid growth and attractive foliage make it a popular choice for aquatic and terrestrial gardens.
