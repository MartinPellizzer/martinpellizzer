Ateleia

Ateleia, commonly known as the "tropical milkweed," is a genus of flowering plants in the Apocynaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of the Americas and is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that range in color from orange to red. Ateleia species are known for their ability to thrive in sunny, well-drained soils and are often used in ornamental gardening.
