Aira aetnensis

Aira aetnensis, commonly known as the Aeolian grass, is a flowering plant native to the volcanic soils of Sicily.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It thrives in sunny, well-drained environments and is adapted to the harsh conditions of volcanic terrain.

Aira aetnensis plays a role in stabilizing soil and supporting local biodiversity in its native habitat.
