Aloe butiabana

Aloe butiabana, commonly known as the desert aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, adding visual interest to its appearance. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their soothing properties.

Aloe butiabana is well-suited for cultivation in dry climates and requires minimal watering.
