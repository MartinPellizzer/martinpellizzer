Aeranthes adenopoda

Aeranthes adenopoda, commonly known as the woolly broom.

It is a flowering plant native to South Africa, characterized by its dense, woolly foliage and clusters of yellow flowers. The plant belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is often found in rocky or sandy soils. It thrives in full sun and is relatively drought-tolerant, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
