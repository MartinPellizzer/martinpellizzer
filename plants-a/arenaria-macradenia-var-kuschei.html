Arenaria macradenia var. kuschei

Arenaria macradenia var. kuschei, commonly known as the Kusche's sandwort, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit. The plant produces small, white or pink flowers that bloom in early summer. Its foliage consists of narrow, linear leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs.

It thrives in well-drained, sandy or rocky soils and is adapted to cold, alpine environments.
