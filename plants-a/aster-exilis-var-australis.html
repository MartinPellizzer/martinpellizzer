Aster exilis var. australis

Aster exilis var. australis, commonly known as the Australian slender aster, is a flowering plant native to southern Australia.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with narrow, lance-shaped leaves and small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in spring. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in coastal and sandy habitats. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local pollinators.

Its compact size makes it suitable for rock gardens and native plant collections.
