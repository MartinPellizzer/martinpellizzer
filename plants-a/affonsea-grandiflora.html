Affonsea grandiflora

Affonsea grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered affonsea, is a tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. The plant has broad, lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Affonsea grandiflora is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.
