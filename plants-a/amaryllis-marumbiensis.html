Amaryllis marumbiensis

Amaryllis marumbiensis, commonly known as the Marumbi amaryllis, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers. The plant typically produces vibrant red, orange, or yellow blooms during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Amaryllis marumbiensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
