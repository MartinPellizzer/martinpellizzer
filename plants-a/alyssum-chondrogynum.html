Alyssum chondrogynum

Alyssum chondrogynum, commonly known as the rock Alyssum, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale pink, typically blooming in spring. The plant has compact, mat-forming foliage with hairy, gray-green leaves that help it retain moisture. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Alyssum chondrogynum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract beneficial insects.
