Arisaema praecox

Arisaema praecox, commonly known as the Chinese arrowroot or Japanese arrowroot, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to East Asia.

It features a stout, rhizomatous rootstock that produces a single, showy spathaceous inflorescence. The plant's unique floral structure consists of a green spathe enclosing a spadix with both male and female flowers. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in herbal medicine.
