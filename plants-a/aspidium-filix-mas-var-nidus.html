Aspidium filix-mas var. nidus

Aspidium filix-mas var. nidus, commonly known as the basket fern, is a variant of the common water fern.

It is characterized by its distinctive fronds that form a dense, bushy structure resembling a basket. The plant belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is native to tropical and subtropical regions. Its fronds are typically long and arching, with a leathery texture and a deep green color.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value.
