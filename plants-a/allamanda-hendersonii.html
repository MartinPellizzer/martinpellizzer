Allamanda hendersonii

Allamanda hendersonii, commonly known as the Henderson's allamanda, is a tropical shrub native to South America.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically bright yellow or orange in color. The plant has thick, waxy leaves that are glossy and arranged in a spiral pattern along the stems. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Allamanda hendersonii is also valued for its ability to produce fragrant blooms throughout the growing season.
