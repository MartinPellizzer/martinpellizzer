Austrocylindropuntia boliviana

Austrocylindropuntia boliviana, commonly known as the Bolivian columnar cactus, is a species native to the Andean regions of Bolivia.

It is characterized by its tall, cylindrical stems that can grow up to several meters in height. The plant has spiny areoles and produces yellow flowers during the warmer months. It is adapted to arid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Austrocylindropuntia boliviana is often cultivated for its ornamental value and resilience.
