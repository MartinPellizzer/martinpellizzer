Andropogon elegantissimus

Andropogon elegantissimus, commonly known as the slender bluestem, is a tall perennial grass native to the southeastern United States.

It features slender, blue-green leaves and produces panicles of small, fluffy flowers that appear in late summer. The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit and is often found in wetland environments such as marshes and swamps. It plays an important role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various wildlife species.

Its ornamental value also makes it a popular choice for ecological landscaping.
