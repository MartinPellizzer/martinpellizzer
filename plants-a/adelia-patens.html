Adelia patens

Adelia patens, commonly known as the coastal morning glory, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking violet to blue flowers that bloom in the early morning. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and thrives in sandy, well-drained soils. It is often found growing along shorelines and in dune environments.

Adelia patens plays an important ecological role by stabilizing sand dunes and providing nectar for pollinators.
