Alternanthera prostrata

Alternanthera prostrata, commonly known as red thread or redstem, is a low-growing herbaceous plant native to South America.

It features vibrant red or pink stems and leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant is characterized by its prostrate growth habit, spreading along the ground with slender, branching stems. Its small, inconspicuous flowers are typically green or yellow and appear in clusters.

It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often used in water gardens or as a ground cover.
