Actinidia remoganensis

Actinidia remoganensis, commonly known as the Chinese gooseberry, is a deciduous vine native to China.

It belongs to the Actinidiaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, greenish-white flowers. The plant produces berries that are typically yellow or red when mature, which are rich in vitamin C. It thrives in temperate climates and is often cultivated for its nutritional value and ornamental appeal.

However, it is less commonly found in cultivation compared to other Actinidia species.
