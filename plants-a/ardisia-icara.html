Ardisia icara

Ardisia icara, commonly known as the glossy privet, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its evergreen foliage, which is dark green and glossy in appearance. The plant produces small, white flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences, followed by red berries that are attractive to birds. Ardisia icara thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in warm climates.
