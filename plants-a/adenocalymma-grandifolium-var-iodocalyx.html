Adenocalymma grandifolium var. iodocalyx

Adenocalymma grandifolium var. iodocalyx, commonly known as the coastal wattle, is a flowering plant native to southern Australia.

It is a shrub characterized by its yellow flowers and feathery leaves, which are typically found in coastal regions. The plant thrives in sandy soils and is often found in dune environments, where it plays a role in stabilizing the soil.
