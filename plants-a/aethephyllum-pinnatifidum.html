Aethephyllum pinnatifidum

Aethephyllum pinnatifidum, commonly known as the fan-leaf fern, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, pinnately divided leaves that give it a distinctive, feathery appearance. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests. Its fronds are typically green and can grow up to several feet in length.

Aethephyllum pinnatifidum is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
