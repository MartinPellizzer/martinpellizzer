Aniseia medium

Aniseia medium, commonly known as the common anise plant, is a herbaceous perennial native to the Mediterranean region.

It features aromatic, lance-shaped leaves with a distinctive licorice-like scent. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring. Its seeds are often used in culinary and medicinal applications due to their volatile oils.

Aniseia medium thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and herbal collections.
