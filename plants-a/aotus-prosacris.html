Aotus prosacris

Aotus prosacris, commonly known as the São João's tree.

It is a species of flowering plant native to the Atlantic Forest of Brazil. The plant is characterized by its woody stem and dense foliage, with leaves that are elliptical and have a smooth surface. Its flowers are small and clustered, typically white or pale yellow in color.

Aotus prosacris is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
