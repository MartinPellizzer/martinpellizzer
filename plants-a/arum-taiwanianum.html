Arum taiwanianum

Arum taiwanianum, commonly known as the Taiwan arrowhead, is a flowering plant native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its unique inflorescence composed of a spadix and a spathe. The plant features heart-shaped, arrowhead-like leaves that are often glossy and green. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers enclosed within the spathe, which is typically white or pale yellow.

Arum taiwanianum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to moist, shaded environments.
