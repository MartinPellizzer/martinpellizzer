Aster tongolensis

Aster tongolensis, commonly known as the Tongol aster, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and features narrow, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are usually blue to violet in color, with ray florets surrounding a central disk of tubular flowers.

Aster tongolensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
