Anisostachya

Anisostachya, commonly known as the tallgrass bluestem, is a genus of grasses native to Africa and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The flowers of Anisostachya are typically arranged in dense, spike-like inflorescences, which are often blue or purple in color.
