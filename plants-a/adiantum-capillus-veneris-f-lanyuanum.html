Adiantum capillus-veneris f. lanyuanum

Adiantum capillus-veneris f. lanyuanum, commonly known as the Venus's hair, is a fern species characterized by its delicate, lace-like fronds.

It belongs to the family Adiantaceae and is native to various regions in Asia, including China. The plant features bipinnate leaves that are finely divided, giving it a feathery appearance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in rocky or forested areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for horticultural cultivation.
