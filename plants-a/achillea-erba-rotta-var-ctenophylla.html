Achillea erba-rotta var. ctenophylla

Achillea erba-rotta var. ctenophylla, commonly known as the silky yarrow, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its finely divided, silver-gray foliage and clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, sunny environments such as rocky soils and grasslands. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
