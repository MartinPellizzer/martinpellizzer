Alchemilla nafarroana

Alchemilla nafarroana, commonly known as the Navarre lady's mantle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its basal rosette of broad, hairy leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers during the summer months. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Alchemilla nafarroana is valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional medicinal uses.
