Achimenes patens var. unicolor

Achimenes patens var. unicolor, commonly known as the single-colored achimenes, is a tropical flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, pink, or white, with the varietal name "unicolor" referring to its uniform coloration. The plant features heart-shaped, dark green leaves and thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and showy blooms.

Achimenes patens var. unicolor is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for gardeners and indoor plant enthusiasts.
