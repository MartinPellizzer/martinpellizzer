Agrostophyllum hasseltii

Agrostophyllum hasseltii, commonly known as the African fireplant, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that store water, enabling it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces striking, red or orange flowers that bloom in late summer. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and drought tolerance.

Agrostophyllum hasseltii is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional African medicine.
