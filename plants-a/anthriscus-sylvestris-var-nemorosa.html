Anthriscus sylvestris var. nemorosa

Anthriscus sylvestris var. nemorosa, commonly known as wood anemone, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to temperate regions of Europe.

It features a rosette of broadly ovate, bluish-green leaves that are hairy beneath. The plant produces white, star-shaped flowers with five petals, typically blooming in early spring. It thrives in woodland habitats, preferring moist, well-drained soil and partial shade.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable component of native plant gardens and ecological restoration projects.
