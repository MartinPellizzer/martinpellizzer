Acacia elachantha

Acacia elachantha, commonly known as the slender acacia, is a small shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its narrow, trifoliate leaves and produces yellow flowers in spring. The plant is adapted to arid environments and is often found in rocky soils. Its seed pods are elongated and contain small, hard seeds.

Acacia elachantha is valued for its hardy nature and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
