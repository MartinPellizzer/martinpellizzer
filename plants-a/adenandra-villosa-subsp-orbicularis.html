Adenandra villosa subsp. orbicularis

Adenandra villosa subsp.

orbicularis, commonly known as the woolly adenandra, is a shrub native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its dense, woolly grey foliage and small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in summer.

The plant typically grows in coastal and mountainous regions, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are orbicular in shape, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

It is often used in ornamental gardening due to its unique texture and adaptability to various climates.
