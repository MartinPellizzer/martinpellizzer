Adenia epigea var. stylosa

Adenia epigea var. stylosa, commonly known as the rock fig or mountain fig, is a shrub native to Australia, particularly in the southern regions.

It is characterized by its woody stems and dense, glossy green foliage. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by edible, fleshy berries. It thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in coastal or mountainous areas.

Its unique morphology and adaptability make it a notable species in its native habitat.
