Artemisia heyneana

Artemisia heyneana, commonly known as blue artemisia, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its aromatic, silver-gray foliage and small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant is often found in dry, rocky soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves contain volatile oils, which contribute to its strong, distinctive scent.

Artemisia heyneana is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and its ability to attract beneficial insects.
