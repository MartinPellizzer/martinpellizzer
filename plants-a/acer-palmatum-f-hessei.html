Acer palmatum f. hessei

Acer palmatum f. hessei, commonly known as the Hessei maple, is a compact, slow-growing variant of the Japanese maple.

It features delicate, finely cut leaves that turn vibrant shades of red and orange in the fall. The plant typically reaches a height of about 2 to 3 meters, making it suitable for smaller gardens or containers. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, preferring cooler climates.

Its ornamental value lies in its striking foliage and graceful form.
