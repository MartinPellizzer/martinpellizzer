Aloe graminifolia

Aloe graminifolia, commonly known as the grass aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in a white powdery coating, which helps reduce water loss. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers on tall, branched stems during the summer months. It is adapted to arid environments and can thrive in well-draining soil with minimal water.

Aloe graminifolia is often used in ornamental gardening for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
