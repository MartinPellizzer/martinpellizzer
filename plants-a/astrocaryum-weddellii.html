Astrocaryum weddellii

Astrocaryum weddellii, commonly known as the Brazil nut tree, is a tall, evergreen tree native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its large, compound leaves and produces clusters of small, yellowish flowers. The tree's most notable feature is its hard, woody pod, which contains the edible Brazil nuts. It plays a crucial role in the ecosystem by providing food for various wildlife species.

The plant is also economically significant due to the commercial value of its seeds.
