Agathosma acutissima

Agathosma acutissima, commonly known as the Cape mountain tea, is a small shrub native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, small, and narrow leaves. The plant produces small yellowish-green flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or mountainous regions.

The leaves are widely used in traditional medicine and as a source of essential oils.
