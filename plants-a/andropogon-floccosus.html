Andropogon floccosus

Andropogon floccosus, commonly known as silky grass or silky beard grass, is a perennial grass species native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its fine, fluffy panicles that give it a soft, cloud-like appearance. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as prairies, roadsides, and disturbed soils. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue.

Andropogon floccosus plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for small insects and wildlife.
