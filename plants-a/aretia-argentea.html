Aretia argentea

Aretia argentea, commonly known as silver aretia, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking silver-gray foliage. The plant produces vibrant blue or purple flowers that bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
