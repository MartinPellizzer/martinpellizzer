Athyrium deltoidofrons f. reflexipinnulum

Athyrium deltoidofrons f. reflexipinnulum, commonly known as the reflexed pinnule fern, is a fern species characterized by its distinctive frond morphology.

The fronds are bipinnate, with the pinnules arranged in a reflexed manner, giving the plant its unique appearance. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories. Its leaves are dark green and have a leathery texture, which helps it retain moisture.

This fern is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in shaded garden settings.
