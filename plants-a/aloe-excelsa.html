Aloe excelsa

Aloe excelsa, commonly known as the tall aloe or the tree aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a tall, branching stem with thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces yellow flowers in clusters at the tips of its branches, often in late spring or early summer. Aloe excelsa is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its leaves and stems.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some medicinal uses.
