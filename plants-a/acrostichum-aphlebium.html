Acrostichum aphlebium

Acrostichum aphlebium, commonly known as the silver fern, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a member of the fern family and is characterized by its distinctive, silvery-green fronds. The plant features a creeping rhizome system that allows it to spread and form dense mats. Its fronds are pinnately compound, with a soft, velvety texture and a unique silver sheen.

Acrostichum aphlebium is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its attractive appearance and adaptability to various growing conditions.
