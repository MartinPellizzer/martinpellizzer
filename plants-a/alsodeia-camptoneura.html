Alsodeia camptoneura

Alsodeia camptoneura, commonly known as the zigzag alsodeia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in grasslands and rocky areas. The plant features a slender, upright stem with alternate, narrow leaves. Its small, clustered flowers are usually white or pale pink, appearing in spring.

Alsodeia camptoneura is known for its distinctive zigzagging stem, which gives it its common name.
