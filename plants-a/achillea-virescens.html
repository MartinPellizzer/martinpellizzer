Achillea virescens

Achillea virescens, commonly known as green yarrow, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features upright, branching stems covered with fine, hairy foliage. The plant produces small, clustered white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and aromatic, often used in traditional medicine.

Achillea virescens is valued for its ornamental appeal and medicinal properties.
