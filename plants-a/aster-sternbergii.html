Aster sternbergii

Aster sternbergii, commonly known as the narrowleaf aster, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and robust root systems. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. Its narrow, linear leaves are arranged alternately along the stem, and it produces small, purple to pink flowers in the fall.

Aster sternbergii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting pollinators.
