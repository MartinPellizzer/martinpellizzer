Anemone souliei

Anemone souliei, commonly known as the Chinese anemone, is a perennial flowering plant native to China.

It produces delicate, showy flowers that range from white to pink, often with a yellow center. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as woodlands or stream banks. Its foliage consists of palmate leaves with lobed edges, and it blooms in early to mid-summer.

Anemone souliei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens and floral arrangements.
