Agrostis aucklandica

Agrostis aucklandica, commonly known as Auckland grass, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its tufted growth habit with slender, dark green blades. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and subalpine regions.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is cultivated in gardens and restoration projects.
