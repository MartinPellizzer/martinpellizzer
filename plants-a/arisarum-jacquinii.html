Arisarum jacquinii

Arisarum jacquinii, commonly known as the Jacquin's arum, is a herbaceous perennial native to the Caribbean.

It features striking, arrow-shaped leaves that are dark green and glossy. The plant produces unique inflorescences with a spathe and spadix, typical of the Araceae family. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for botanical gardens and shaded landscapes.
