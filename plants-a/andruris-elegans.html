Andruris elegans

Andruris elegans, commonly known as the elegant orchid, is a tropical plant native to certain regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically features slender, upright stems and lance-shaped leaves. Its blooms are usually pale yellow to white, with intricate patterns that enhance its ornamental value.

Andruris elegans thrives in humid, well-drained environments and requires indirect sunlight for optimal growth.
