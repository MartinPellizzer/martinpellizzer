Artemisia capillaris var. arbuscula

Artemisia capillaris var. arbuscula, commonly known as Chinese artemisia or yin chen hao, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its aromatic, silver-gray foliage and small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in moist or wetland environments. Its leaves contain volatile oils, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

It is widely used in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported detoxifying and liver-supporting effects.
