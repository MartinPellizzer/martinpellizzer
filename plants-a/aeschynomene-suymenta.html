Aeschynomene suymenta

Aeschynomene suymenta, commonly known as Suymenta grass, is a perennial grass species native to South America.

It is characterized by its erect growth habit and slender, branching stems. The leaves are narrow and often have a hairy texture, while the inflorescence consists of spikelets arranged in dense clusters. It is adapted to thrive in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in disturbed soils and open areas.

This plant is valued for its potential in forage and erosion control due to its hardy nature and rapid growth.
