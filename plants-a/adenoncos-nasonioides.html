Adenoncos nasonioides

Adenoncos nasonioides, commonly known as the Cape mountain heath, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and small, leathery leaves. The plant produces clusters of pale yellow to white flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
