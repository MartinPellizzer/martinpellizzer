Aechmea longicuspis var. longicuspis

Aechmea longicuspis var. longicuspis, commonly known as the long-spined aecharia, is a bromeliad native to South America.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that form a central cup to collect rainwater. The plant produces striking red or orange flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor gardening.
