Aloe erecta

Aloe erecta, commonly known as the upright aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features tall, slender stems that can grow up to 3 meters in height, with lance-shaped leaves that are fleshy and covered in a waxy cuticle. The plant produces striking yellow flowers in clusters at the top of the stems, which bloom in late summer. Aloe erecta is adapted to arid environments, storing water in its leaves and stems to survive drought conditions.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and hardiness.
