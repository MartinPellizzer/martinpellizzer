Asplenium soboliferum

Asplenium soboliferum, commonly known as the rock fern, is a perennial fern native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically grows on rocky substrates and is characterized by its compact, tufted growth habit. The plant features finely divided, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine hairs. It thrives in dry, sunny conditions and is well adapted to rocky or gravelly soils.

Asplenium soboliferum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in rock gardens or as a container plant.
