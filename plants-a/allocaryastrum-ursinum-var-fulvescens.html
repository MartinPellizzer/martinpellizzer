Allocaryastrum ursinum var. fulvescens

Allocaryastrum ursinum var. fulvescens, commonly known as the golden-flowered thornbush, is a shrub native to South America.

It features woody stems covered in sharp thorns and has small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters. The leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a slightly hairy texture. This plant thrives in dry, sunny environments and is adapted to arid conditions.

It plays a role in local ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various insects and small animals.
