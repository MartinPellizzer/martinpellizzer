Argyrolobium tysonii

Argyrolobium tysonii, commonly known as the Tyson's silver globular pea, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit and produces small, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant has hairy stems and leaves with a distinctive, globular shape. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
