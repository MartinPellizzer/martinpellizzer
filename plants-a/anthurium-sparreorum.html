Anthurium sparreorum

Anthurium sparreorum, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a striking tropical plant native to Colombia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped spathe that ranges from red to orange in color. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers within a spadix, which is typical of the Araceae family. Its foliage consists of large, glossy, dark green leaves that are often veined with white or silver patterns.

Anthurium sparreorum is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
