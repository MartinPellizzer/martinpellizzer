Agave sisalana f. armata

Agave sisalana f. armata, commonly known as the armed sisal agave, is a variant of the sisal agave plant native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its robust, spiny leaves that provide natural protection against herbivores. The plant produces tall, cylindrical flower stalks that bear small, greenish-white flowers. It is widely cultivated for its fibrous leaves, which are used in the production of rope, textiles, and paper.

Its botanical features make it an important species in both agricultural and ecological contexts.
