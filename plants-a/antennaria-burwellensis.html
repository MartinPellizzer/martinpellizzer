Antennaria burwellensis

Antennaria burwellensis, commonly known as Burwell's mouse-eared moss.

It is a small, mat-forming perennial plant native to the high elevations of the Appalachian Mountains. The plant has hairy, grass-like leaves that are typically less than 1 inch long. It produces small, yellowish flowers in early spring, which are clustered in dense inflorescences.

This species is adapted to rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats.
