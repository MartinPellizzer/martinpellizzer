Aloe downsiana

Aloe downsiana, commonly known as the Down's aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a powdery blue or white coating. The plant produces clusters of yellow flowers during the summer months, which attract pollinators like bees and butterflies. Aloe downsiana is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its leaves to survive droughts.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and low maintenance requirements.
