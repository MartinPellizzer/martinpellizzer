Anthurium miaziense

Anthurium miaziense, commonly known as the Chinese flamingo flower, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves and vibrant, spadix-like inflorescence. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers surrounded by a colorful spathe, which is often the primary ornamental feature. It thrives in humid, warm environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.

Anthurium miaziense is popular as a houseplant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
