Aymoreana nitida

Aymoreana nitida, commonly known as the velvetleaf or velvetleaf tree, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, hard seeds.

Aymoreana nitida is often found in moist, shaded environments and is sometimes cultivated for its ornamental value.
