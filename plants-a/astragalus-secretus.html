Astragalus secretus

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its small, yellow pea-like flowers. The plant has compound leaves with small leaflets and produces pods that contain several seeds. It is adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments with minimal water availability.
