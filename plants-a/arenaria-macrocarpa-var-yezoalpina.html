Arenaria macrocarpa var. yezoalpina

Arenaria macrocarpa var. yezoalpina, commonly known as the Yezo alpine sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Japan.

It typically features dense clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has succulent, fleshy leaves that help it retain moisture in its alpine habitat. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is often found in high-altitude meadows.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to withstand harsh environmental conditions.
