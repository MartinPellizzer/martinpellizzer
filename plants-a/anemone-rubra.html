Anemone rubra

Anemone rubra, commonly known as the red anemone, is a herbaceous perennial native to the eastern United States.

It features showy, pink to red flowers that bloom in early spring, typically from March to May. The plant has basal leaves that are heart-shaped and covered in a fine, white down. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.

Anemone rubra is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting pollinators in woodland habitats.
