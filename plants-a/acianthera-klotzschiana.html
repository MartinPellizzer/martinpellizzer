Acianthera klotzschiana

Acianthera klotzschiana, commonly known as the Klotzschian orchid, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is known for its epiphytic growth habit, often found growing on trees in tropical rainforests. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture and nutrients from the air and rain.

The plant is valued for its ornamental beauty and is cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
