Abies tchugatskoi

Abies tchugatskoi, commonly known as the Tchugatsk fir, is a coniferous tree native to the mountainous regions of eastern Siberia.

It is characterized by its slender, conical shape and dark green, needle-like foliage. The tree produces cones that are typically cylindrical and contain winged seeds, aiding in wind dispersal. Its bark is thick and scaly, providing protection against harsh environmental conditions.

Abies tchugatskoi plays a significant role in the local ecosystem, offering habitat and food for various wildlife species.
