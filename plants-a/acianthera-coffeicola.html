Acianthera coffeicola

Acianthera coffeicola, commonly known as the coffee-colored orchid, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are elliptical and covered in a dense layer of fine hairs.
