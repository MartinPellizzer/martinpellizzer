Anemone tenuicaulis

Anemone tenuicaulis, commonly known as the slender-stemmed anemone, is a herbaceous perennial native to the western United States.

It features delicate, showy flowers with white petals and a yellow center, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has thin, upright stems and basal leaves that are finely divided. It thrives in well-drained soils and partial to full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and native plant landscapes.

Its botanical characteristics make it well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.
