Attalea × voeksii

Attalea × voeksii, commonly known as the hybrid palm, is a natural hybrid between Attalea maripa and Attalea panamensis.

It is a tall, slender palm with a smooth, grayish trunk and large, pinnate leaves that can reach up to 5 meters in length. The plant produces large, yellowish-pink flowers and is often found in wetland or swampy areas in Central and South America.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and has some traditional uses in local communities.
