Aa fiebrigii

Aa fiebrigii, commonly known as the giant lily, is a striking plant native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its massive, white, trumpet-shaped flowers that can reach up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot. Its leaves are large, broad, and often covered in a waxy coating to reduce water loss.

Aa fiebrigii is a rare and endangered species, making it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists alike.
