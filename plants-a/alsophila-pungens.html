Alsophila pungens

Alsophila pungens, commonly known as the slender tree fern, is a species native to tropical and subtropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a member of the Cyatheaceae family and is characterized by its tall, unbranched trunk and large, feather-like fronds. The plant has a distinctive crown of pinnate leaves that can reach several meters in length. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Alsophila pungens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a decorative plant in gardens.
