Aspidium scalare

Aspidium scalare, commonly known as the ladder brake, is a fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, fan-like fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forests and rocky areas. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

The fronds are bi-directionally divided, giving it a unique and elegant appearance.
