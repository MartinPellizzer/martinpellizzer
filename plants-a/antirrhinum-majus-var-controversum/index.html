Antirrhinum Majus Var. Controversum

Antirrhinum majus var. controversum is a rare and striking variant of the common snapdragon known for its unique floral characteristics. This plant is native to specific regions in southern Europe and is often found in rocky or disturbed habitats. Its flowers display a range of colors, from deep purples to bright reds, making it a popular choice among horticultural enthusiasts. Despite its beauty, it remains underdocumented in botanical studies due to its limited distribution. Cultivating this variety requires careful attention to soil and climate conditions to ensure its survival.
