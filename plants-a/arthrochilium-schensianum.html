Arthrochilium schensianum

Arthrochilium schensianum, commonly known as the Schensianum orchid, is a terrestrial orchid belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its erect, leafy stems and produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are often clustered in racemes. Native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia, this plant thrives in humid, shaded environments. The species is noted for its unique floral structure, with lip petals that are distinctly shaped and often adorned with intricate patterns.

It is primarily found in countries such as China, Vietnam, and Thailand, where it grows in forested areas.
