Artemisia seriphium var. simplex

Artemisia seriphium var. simplex, commonly known as the simple leaf wormwood, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, gray-green foliage. The plant produces small, yellow flower heads that are clustered in flat-topped inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or dry habitats.

Due to its medicinal properties, it has been historically used in traditional herbal remedies.
