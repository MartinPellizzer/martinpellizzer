Aspidistra jiangjinensis

Aspidistra jiangjinensis, commonly known as the Jiangjinensis Snake's Head Plant, is a species native to China.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its unique inflorescence structure. The plant features dark green, lance-shaped leaves with a glossy texture. It is evergreen and thrives in low-light conditions, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its striking, spadix-like flower cluster.
