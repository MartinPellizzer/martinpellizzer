Alaticaulia calagrasalis

Alaticaulia calagrasalis, commonly known as the narrow-leaved alaticaulia, is a perennial shrub native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It features small, narrow leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white wax, which helps reduce water loss in its harsh environment. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pale green or yellow, adapted for pollination by insects. Its root system is extensive and deep, allowing it to access water from deeper soil layers.

Alaticaulia calagrasalis is valued for its resilience and is often used in local landscaping and traditional medicine.
