Acacia delavayi var. kunmingensis

Acacia delavayi var. kunmingensis, commonly known as the Yunnan acacia, is a shrub native to southwestern China.

It is characterized by its bipinnate leaves, which are divided into small, feather-like segments. The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in cylindrical spikes, which bloom in the spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in its native habitat.
