Austrocylindropuntia yanganucensis

Austrocylindropuntia yanganucensis, commonly known as the Yanganucensis barrel cactus, is a species of cactus native to northern Argentina.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems that are ribbed and covered in spines. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Its structure and growth habits make it a unique example of succulent flora in the region.
