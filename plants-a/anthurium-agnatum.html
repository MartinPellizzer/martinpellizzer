Anthurium agnatum

Anthurium agnatum, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces vibrant red spathes that enclose a spadix, which is the actual flowering structure. Anthurium agnatum thrives in humid, warm environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

It is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and long-lasting blooms.
