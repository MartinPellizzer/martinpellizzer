Achyranthes heudelotii

Achyranthes heudelotii, commonly known as the African spurge, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features upright, branched stems covered in fine, soft hairs. Its leaves are alternate, ovate to lanceolate, and often have a hairy underside.

The flowers are small, yellowish-green, and clustered in dense, spike-like inflorescences.
