Acalypha deamii

Acalypha deamii, commonly known as the golden goosefoot, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

The plant typically grows as an annual herb with a fibrous root system and smooth, lance-shaped leaves. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are clustered in cyathia, typical of the Euphorbiaceae family.
