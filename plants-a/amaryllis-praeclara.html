Amaryllis praeclara

Amaryllis praeclara, commonly known as the showy amaryllis, is a striking bulbous plant native to South America.

It features large, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, pink, and white, often with a distinctive yellow or green stripe. The plant produces strap-like, dark green leaves that are broad and fleshy, adapted for storing water. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Amaryllis praeclara is popular as a ornamental plant due to its vibrant blooms and relatively easy care requirements.
