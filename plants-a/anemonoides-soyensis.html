Anemonoides soyensis

Anemonoides soyensis, commonly known as the Korean anemone, is a herbaceous perennial native to Korea.

It features showy, white or pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has a fibrous root system and lance-shaped leaves that are typically hairy. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.

Anemonoides soyensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in native gardens.
