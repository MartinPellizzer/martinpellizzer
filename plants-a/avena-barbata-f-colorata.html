Avena barbata f. colorata

Avena barbata f. colorata, commonly known as the colored slender oat, is a variant of the slender oat species native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its strikingly colored inflorescences, which often display shades of red, purple, and pink, making it a popular ornamental plant. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in full sun. Its foliage is narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue that adds to its visual appeal.

This cultivar is valued for its unique coloration and drought tolerance, making it suitable for xeriscaping and rock gardens.
