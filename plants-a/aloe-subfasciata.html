Aloe subfasciata

Aloe subfasciata, commonly known as the Zebra Aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features striking, narrow, bluish-green leaves with distinct white or yellowish stripes, giving it its zebra-like appearance. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with small, yellowish-green flowers. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Aloe subfasciata is valued for its ornamental beauty and ability to store water in its fleshy leaves.
