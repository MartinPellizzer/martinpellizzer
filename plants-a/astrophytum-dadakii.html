Astrophytum dadakii

Astrophytum dadakii, commonly known as the Dadakii barrel cactus, is a species native to the Sonoran Desert in North America.

It is a small, globular cactus with a short, thick stem and a crown of spines. The plant features areoles, which are small, raised areas on the stem where spines, flowers, and branches emerge. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, appearing in late spring to early summer.

Astrophytum dadakii is adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick, fleshy stem for survival during droughts.
