Acanthus ilicifolius var. ebracteatus

Acanthus ilicifolius var. ebracteatus, commonly known as the narrow-leaved acanthus, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
