Artemisia × wilczekiana

Artemisia × wilczekiana, commonly known as the gray sagebrush, is a hybrid shrub native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, dense hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish flower heads that are clustered in rounded or flat-topped inflorescences. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in disturbed soils and open habitats.

Its aromatic leaves contain volatile oils, making it a plant of interest in both ecological and medicinal contexts.
