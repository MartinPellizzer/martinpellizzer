Asplundia spectabilis

Asplundia spectabilis, commonly known as the showy asplundia, is a member of the Araceae family and is native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

This herbaceous plant typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, featuring a rhizomatous underground stem. Its foliage consists of large, heart-shaped leaves that are often a deep green with a waxy texture. The plant produces striking, spatulate-shaped flowers that are usually greenish-white with a prominent yellow spathe.

Asplundia spectabilis is often cultivated for its ornamental value in shaded garden environments.
