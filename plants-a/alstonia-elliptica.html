Alstonia elliptica

Alstonia elliptica, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a tropical tree native to Africa and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its large, elliptical leaves that are often a deep green with a waxy texture. The plant produces showy, clustered yellow flowers that bloom in summer, followed by woody pods containing seeds. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in warm, humid climates.

Alstonia elliptica is also valued for its fast growth and adaptability to various soil types.
