Achillea × obristii

Achillea × obristii, commonly known as the hybrid yarrow, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is a hybrid between Achillea millefolium (common yarrow) and Achillea ptarmica (tall yarrow), resulting in a plant with distinctive floral and foliage characteristics. The plant produces flat-topped clusters of small, usually white or pale yellow flowers, which are arranged in a composite inflorescence. Its leaves are finely divided and feathery, giving it a delicate appearance.

Achillea × obristii is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
