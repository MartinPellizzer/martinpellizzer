Anthurium pedunculare var. intermedium

Anthurium pedunculare var. intermedium, commonly known as the velvet leaf anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a velvety texture. The plant produces striking spadices, which are the characteristic inflorescences of the Araceae family. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium pedunculare var. intermedium is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
