Aster lucens

Aster lucens, commonly known as the luminous aster, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that range in color from white to violet, often with a yellow center. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy perennial in temperate climates. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Aster lucens is valued both for its ornamental beauty and its ecological role in supporting pollinators.
