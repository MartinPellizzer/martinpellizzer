Acianthus apprimus

Acianthus apprimus, commonly known as the slender marsh orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Africa and Asia.

It typically grows in wet or marshy environments, thriving in humid conditions with rich, organic soil. The plant features a slender, upright stem with a few lance-shaped leaves and produces small, delicate flowers in shades of white and pink. Its flowers are arranged in a loose spike and bloom during the spring and early summer.

Acianthus apprimus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens with moist, well-drained soil.
