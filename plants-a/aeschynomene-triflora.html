Aeschynomene triflora

Aeschynomene triflora, commonly known as smallleaf aeschynomene, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant is typically found in open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils, where it can thrive in full sun.
