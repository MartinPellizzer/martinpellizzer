Anthemis bornmuelleri var. bornmuelleri

Anthemis bornmuelleri var. bornmuelleri, commonly known as the Bornmueller's anthemis, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to southern Africa, particularly found in regions of Namibia and South Africa. The plant produces small, yellow to orange composite flowers that bloom in summer. Its leaves are typically narrow and hairy, adapted to arid environments.

It is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its striking floral display and resilience in dry conditions.
