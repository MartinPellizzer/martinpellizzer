Aloe descoingsii subsp. augustina

Aloe descoingsii subsp.

augustina, commonly known as the Augustina aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa. It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, with a waxy coating to reduce water loss.

The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with bright yellow flowers, which bloom in summer. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal watering.

Its foliage is often used in traditional medicine and as a source of gel for topical applications.
