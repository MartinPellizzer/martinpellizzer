Ayenia indica

Ayenia indica, commonly known as the Indian ayenia, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows as an annual or perennial herb, reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow with a central disk.

Ayenia indica is often found in disturbed soils and is used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
