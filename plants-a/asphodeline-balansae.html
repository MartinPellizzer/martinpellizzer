Asphodeline balansae

Asphodeline balansae, commonly known as the Balansae asphodel, is a perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and tall, branched flower spikes. The plant produces small, white to pale purple flowers that bloom in early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
