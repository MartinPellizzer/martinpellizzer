Arctotis acaulis f. undulata

Arctotis acaulis f. undulata, commonly known as the undulating arctotis, is a compact, herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It features narrow, undulating leaves that are typically grey-green in color, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces showy, daisy-like flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, which bloom throughout the growing season. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its unique foliage and vibrant blooms make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
