Athanasia cuneifolia

Athanasia cuneifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf everlasting.

It is a flowering plant native to South Africa, characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and clusters of small, pale yellow flowers. The plant is known for its drought tolerance and ability to thrive in well-drained soils. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Athanasia cuneifolia is often used in rock gardens and as a low-maintenance ornamental plant.
