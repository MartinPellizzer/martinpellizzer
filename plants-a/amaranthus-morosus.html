Amaranthus morosus

Amaranthus morosus, commonly known as black amaranth, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its dark purple to black bracts that surround the small, green flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters and thrives in warm climates. Its leaves are broad and heart-shaped, often with a slightly hairy texture.

The species is known for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional cuisines.
