Aster autranii

Aster autranii, commonly known as the Autran's aster, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically produces showy blue to purple flowers that bloom in the summer months. It prefers well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade, making it suitable for rock gardens or alpine settings.

Its ornamental value and adaptability make it a desirable addition to native plant collections.
