Asarca speciosa

Asarca speciosa, commonly known as the showy goldenroot, is a perennial herb native to the Pacific Northwest of North America.

It belongs to the Apiaceae family, which includes plants like carrots and parsley, and is characterized by its finely divided, fern-like leaves. Asarca speciosa thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.
