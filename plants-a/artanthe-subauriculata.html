Artanthe subauriculata

Artanthe subauriculata, commonly known as the silverleaf artanthe, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces showy, pink to purple flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements.
