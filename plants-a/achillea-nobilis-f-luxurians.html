Achillea nobilis f. luxurians

Achillea nobilis f. luxurians, commonly known as the showy yarrow, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and large, showy flower heads that range in color from white to pink and purple. Native to the Mediterranean region, this plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun. Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

The plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in herbal medicine.
