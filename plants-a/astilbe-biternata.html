Astilbe biternata

Astilbe biternata, commonly known as the fernleaf astilbe, is a perennial flowering plant native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its feathery, plume-like inflorescences that bloom in shades of pink, red, and white. The plant features finely divided, fern-like leaves that provide a delicate texture to the garden. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often used in woodland gardens.

Astilbe biternata is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to add color to shaded landscapes.
