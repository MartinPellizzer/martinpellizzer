Adesmia microcalyx var. microcalyx

Adesmia microcalyx var. microcalyx, commonly known as the small-calyx adesmia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a shrub characterized by its compact growth habit and small, clustered leaves. The plant produces small, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters, typically during the spring and summer seasons. Its calyx is notably small and persistent, giving it its scientific name.

It is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
