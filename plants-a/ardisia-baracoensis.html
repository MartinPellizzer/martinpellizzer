Ardisia baracoensis

Ardisia baracoensis, commonly known as the Philippine beautyberry, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Ericaceae and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of small, white to pinkish flowers. Ardisia baracoensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitat.
