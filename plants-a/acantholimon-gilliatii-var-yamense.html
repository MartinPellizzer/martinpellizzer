Acantholimon gilliatii var. yamense

Acantholimon gilliatii var. yamense, commonly known as the Yamenskaya stonecrop, is a low-growing perennial plant native to Central Asia.

It features succulent, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-gray in color, adapted to store water in arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or purple during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental foliage make it a desirable addition to drought-tolerant landscapes.
