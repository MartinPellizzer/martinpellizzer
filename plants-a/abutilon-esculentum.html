Abutilon esculentum

Abutilon esculentum, commonly known as velvetleaf, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and fluffy, pale green to yellowish flowers. The plant is an annual or perennial herb that thrives in warm climates with ample sunlight. Its stems are often covered in a soft, velvety pubescence, giving it its common name.

Velvetleaf is known for its rapid growth and is often considered a weed in agricultural settings.
