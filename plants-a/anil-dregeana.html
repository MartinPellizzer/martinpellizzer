Anil dregeana

Anil dregeana, commonly known as the blue hyacinth, is a striking plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of blue to violet flowers. The plant features strap-like, grassy leaves that are often covered in a fine, powdery coating. Anil dregeana thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming period, typically from late winter to early spring.
