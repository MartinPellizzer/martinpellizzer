Aristolochia nipponica

Aristolochia nipponica, commonly known as Japanese birthwort, is a climbing herbaceous plant native to East Asia.

It features heart-shaped, aromatic leaves and produces distinctive, pipe-shaped flowers that are often green or purple in color. The plant is characterized by its complex, winding stems and is often found in moist, shaded environments. It belongs to the Aristolochiaceae family and is known for its medicinal uses in traditional herbal medicine.

However, it contains toxic compounds and should be handled with care.
