Adenophora wulingshanica var. alterna

Adenophora wulingshanica var. alterna, commonly known as the Wuling Mountain adenophora, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its slender stems and bell-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions with moist, well-drained soils. Its leaves are lanceolate and covered with fine hairs, while its flowers are usually pale blue or white.

This variant is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
