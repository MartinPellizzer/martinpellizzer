Asplenium truncatilobum

Asplenium truncatilobum, commonly known as the cut-lobed spleenwort, is a species of fern belonging to the family Aspleniaceae.

It is characterized by its small, delicate fronds that are typically less than 10 centimeters in length. The leaves are usually bi- or tri-pinnate, with a distinctive truncate or cut-off lobed appearance at the tips. This plant prefers moist, shaded environments and is often found growing on rocks or tree trunks in temperate regions.

Its unique morphology and ecological preferences make it a notable species within its native range.
