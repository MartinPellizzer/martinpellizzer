Alliaria Rufa

Alliaria rufa is a small, flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia. It is commonly known as the red garlic or winter garlic. This plant belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other mustard family species. Alliaria rufa is often found in moist, shaded areas such as woodlands and stream banks. Its distinctive reddish flowers and strong garlic-like scent make it easily recognizable.
