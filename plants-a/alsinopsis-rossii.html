Alsinopsis rossii

Alsinopsis rossii, commonly known as the rock daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit. The plant produces showy yellow flowers that bloom in spring, often in clusters at the ends of its stems. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.

Alsinopsis rossii is often used in rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and ornamental value.
