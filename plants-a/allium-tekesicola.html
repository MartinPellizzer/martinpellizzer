Allium tekesicola

Allium tekesicola, commonly known as the Teksicola garlic, is a perennial herb native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its bulbous structure and grass-like foliage. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.
