Asystasia nemorum

Asystasia nemorum, commonly known as the ground orchid, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its slender, creeping stems and clusters of small, orchid-like flowers. Asystasia nemorum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
