Abutilon erosum

Abutilon erosum, commonly known as the woolly abutilon, is a flowering plant native to parts of the Americas.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments. The plant is characterized by its soft, hairy leaves and showy, lantern-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. It produces small, round fruits that contain seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

The botanical features of Abutilon erosum make it well adapted to dry conditions and suitable for use in xeriscaping.
