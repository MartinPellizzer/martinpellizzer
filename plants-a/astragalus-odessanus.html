Astragalus odessanus

Astragalus odessanus, commonly known as the Odessa milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to southern Russia and parts of Ukraine.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its erect, branched stems and small, yellowish flowers. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds, typical of the Fabaceae family. It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often found in steppe and semi-arid environments.

Astragalus odessanus is known for its hardy nature and ability to withstand harsh climatic conditions.
