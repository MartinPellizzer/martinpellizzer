Aspidium umbrosum

Aspidium umbrosum, commonly known as the shadow fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Athyriaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped fronds that are typically green with a slightly hairy texture. The plant prefers shady, moist environments and is often found in forest understories. Its spores are produced in clusters on the undersides of its leaves, contributing to its reproductive strategy.

Aspidium umbrosum plays a role in maintaining soil stability and providing habitat for small forest organisms.
