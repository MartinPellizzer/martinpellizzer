Anthurium caulorrhizum

Anthurium caulorrhizum, commonly known as the velvet leaf anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a dense, velvety texture. The plant produces vivid, spadix-like inflorescences surrounded by colorful spathe, which can range from red to yellow. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Its unique foliage and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical landscapes.
