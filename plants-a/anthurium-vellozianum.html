Anthurium vellozianum

Anthurium vellozianum, commonly known as the velvet anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a velvety texture. The plant produces striking spadices, which are modified leaves that enclose the actual flowers. Anthurium vellozianum thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor cultivation and botanical gardens.
