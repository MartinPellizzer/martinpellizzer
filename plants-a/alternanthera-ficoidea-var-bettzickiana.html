Alternanthera ficoidea var. bettzickiana

Alternanthera ficoidea var. bettzickiana, commonly known as the red-veined bettzickiana, is a striking aquatic plant characterized by its vibrant red veins contrasting against its dark green foliage.

It is native to South America and thrives in tropical and subtropical freshwater environments. The plant typically grows in shallow water, often forming dense mats that provide habitat for small aquatic life. Its leaves are lance-shaped and can vary in size depending on water conditions and nutrient availability.

It is popular in aquariums for its ornamental value and ability to enhance the aesthetic appeal of planted tanks.
