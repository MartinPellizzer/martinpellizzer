Anthurium amoenum var. amoenum

Anthurium amoenum var. amoenum, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped spathe that ranges from red to orange in color. The plant produces small, spadix-like inflorescences that are typically yellow or green. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Its striking foliage and long-lasting blooms make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
