Agrostis dyeri var. delicatior

Agrostis dyeri var. delicatior, commonly known as the slender meadowgrass, is a perennial grass species native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

It typically grows in open, moist habitats such as meadows, grasslands, and along streambanks. The plant has slender, erect stems with narrow, grass-like leaves that are often hairy along the edges. Its inflorescence consists of a spike-like cluster of small, two-lobed spikelets.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping or as a component in ecological restoration projects.
