Amygdalopsis furcata

Amygdalopsis furcata, commonly known as the forked almond, is a species of flowering plant in the family Rosaceae.

It is native to southern Europe and parts of western Asia, often found in rocky or limestone soils. Amygdalopsis furcata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
