Anemone patens f. stevensonis

Anemone patens f. stevensonis, commonly known as the Stevens' anemone, is a flowering plant native to the Pacific Northwest of the United States.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky stream banks. The plant produces showy, white to pink flowers with a distinctive, broad, spurred petal at the base. Its leaves are palmately lobed and often have a hairy texture.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in woodland gardens.
