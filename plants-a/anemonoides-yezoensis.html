Anemonoides yezoensis

Anemonoides yezoensis, commonly known as the Yezo anemone, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Japan.

It features showy, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has palmately divided, deeply lobed leaves that are typically hairy and covered in fine pubescence. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or streamside habitats.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking a hardy, ornamental plant.
