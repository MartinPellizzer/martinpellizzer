Aster gracilicaulis

Aster gracilicaulis, commonly known as slenderstem aster, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands. It produces small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in the summer months.

Its slender stems and delicate foliage make it a distinctive member of the aster genus.
