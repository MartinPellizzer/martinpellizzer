Afrolicania elaeosperma

Afrolicania elaeosperma, commonly known as the African moonveld grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky outcrops. The plant has slender, erect stems and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue.

Afrolicania elaeosperma is adapted to arid conditions and is often used in restoration projects due to its resilience.
