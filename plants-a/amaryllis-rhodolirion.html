Amaryllis rhodolirion

Amaryllis rhodolirion, commonly known as the red belladonna lily, is a striking plant native to South Africa.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to deep red, often with a contrasting purple or pink throat. The plant produces large, strap-like leaves that are broad and glossy, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its blooming period typically occurs in late spring to early summer, adding a dramatic flair to the landscape.
