Acianthera simpliciflora

Acianthera simpliciflora, commonly known as the simple-flowered acianthera, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and arranged in a basal rosette.

Acianthera simpliciflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
