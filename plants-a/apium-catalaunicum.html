Apium catalaunicum

Apium catalaunicum, commonly known as the Catalan cow parsley, is a flowering plant native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It belongs to the Apiaceae family, which includes carrots, parsley, and celery. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and has slender, branched stems with finely divided leaves. Its flowers are small and white, arranged in umbels, and it produces small, oval fruits.
