Aquilegia canadensis var. latiuscula

Aquilegia canadensis var. latiuscula, commonly known as the broad-leaved Canadian columbine, is a perennial herb native to eastern North America.

It features striking blue to purple spurred flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has palmately divided leaves with a distinctive basal rosette structure. Its flowers attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils in partial to full shade.
