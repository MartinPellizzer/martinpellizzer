Adenium swazicum

Adenium swazicum, commonly known as the Swazi adenium, is a striking succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its swollen, bottle-shaped trunk and smooth, often green or greyish bark. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers that bloom at the top of the stem, followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. Its leaves are typically narrow and arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem.

Adenium swazicum is adapted to arid environments and is known for its ability to store water in its thick trunk.
