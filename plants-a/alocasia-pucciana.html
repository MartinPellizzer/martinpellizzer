Alocasia pucciana

Alocasia pucciana, commonly known as the Chinese Evergreen, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, arrow-shaped leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length. The plant is a member of the Araceae family, which includes other ornamental plants like peace lilies and calla lilies. Alocasia pucciana prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.

Its foliage is often variegated with shades of green, white, and pink, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
