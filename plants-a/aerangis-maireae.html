Aerangis maireae

The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers with a unique coloration, typically featuring white or pale pink petals and a contrasting lip. Its botanical features make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists due to its limited distribution and vulnerability to habitat loss.
