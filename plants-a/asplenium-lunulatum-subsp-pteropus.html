Asplenium lunulatum subsp. pteropus

Asplenium lunulatum subsp.

pteropus, commonly known as the moon's fern, is a small evergreen fern native to Southeast Asia. It features delicate, lance-shaped fronds that are typically dark green with a slightly curled appearance.

The plant has a compact growth habit and often forms dense clusters. Its spore-producing structures, called sori, are located on the underside of the fronds.

It thrives in shady, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.
