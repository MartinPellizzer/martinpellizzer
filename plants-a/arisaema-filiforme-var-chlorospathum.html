Arisaema filiforme var. chlorospathum

Arisaema filiforme var. chlorospathum, commonly known as the green-spadix arisaema, is a striking ornamental plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features a tall, slender stem that bears a single, elongated spadix surrounded by a broad, leaf-like bract. The plant's foliage consists of a single, elongated, lance-shaped leaf that is often variegated with green and white patterns. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is valued for its unique and exotic appearance.

This variant is particularly notable for its vibrant green spadix and contrasting leaf coloration.
