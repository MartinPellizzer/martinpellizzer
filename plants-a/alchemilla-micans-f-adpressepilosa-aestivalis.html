Alchemilla micans f. adpressepilosa-aestivalis

Alchemilla micans f. adpressepilosa-aestivalis, commonly known as the compact alchemilla, is a low-growing perennial plant belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It features dense, mat-forming foliage with silver-green leaves that are softly hairy, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to stabilize soil in garden settings.
