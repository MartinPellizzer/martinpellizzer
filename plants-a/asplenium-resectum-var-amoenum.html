Asplenium resectum var. amoenum

Asplenium resectum var. amoenum, commonly known as the Amoenum Shield Fern, is a compact, evergreen fern native to Southeast Asia.

It features delicate, lance-shaped fronds that are typically dark green with a slightly curled appearance. The plant has a creeping rhizome that allows it to spread slowly and form dense mats. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and shaded garden settings.
