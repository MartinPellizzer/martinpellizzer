Anthospermum streyi

Anthospermum streyi, commonly known as the African snowball.

It is a shrub native to southern Africa, characterized by its dense, white flower clusters that resemble snowballs. The plant has simple, opposite leaves and produces small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

The plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
