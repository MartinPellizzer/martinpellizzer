Astridia alba

Astridia alba, commonly known as the white aster, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in the spring and summer. Its leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and covered in fine hairs.

Astridia alba is often found in moist, shaded areas and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
