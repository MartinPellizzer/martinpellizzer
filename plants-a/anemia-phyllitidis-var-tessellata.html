Anemia phyllitidis var. tessellata

Anemia phyllitidis var. tessellata, commonly known as the tessellated liverwort, is a species of thalloid liverwort belonging to the family Porellaceae.

It is characterized by its intricate, mosaic-like leaf patterns, which give it its common name. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as on rocks or in damp soil. Its thallus is leafy and consists of a central stem with closely arranged, flattened lobes.

The species is often found in temperate regions and is known for its delicate, greenish-yellow appearance.
