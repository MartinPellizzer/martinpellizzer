Apocynum alterniflorum

Apocynum alterniflorum, commonly known as the alternateflower dogbane, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is a member of the Apocynaceae family, which includes many species with milky sap. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as wetlands and meadows. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often have a rough texture.

The flowers are small, clustered in umbels, and are usually white or pale pink in color.
