Anthurium propinquum var. albispadix

Anthurium propinquum var. albispadix, commonly known as the white-spadix anthurium, is a striking tropical plant native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a velvety texture. The plant produces a spadix, which is a dense cluster of small, inconspicuous flowers surrounded by a showy spathe. The spathe is usually white or pale in color, giving the plant its distinguishing name.

It thrives in humid, warm environments with bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
