Agrostis solandri

Agrostis solandri, commonly known as New Zealand bentgrass, is a low-growing perennial grass native to New Zealand.

It features slender, tufted stems and narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically less than 5 cm in length. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences containing numerous tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It thrives in moist, temperate climates and is often found in wetlands and along streambanks.

Agrostis solandri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is used in ecological restoration projects.
