Artemisia dimoana

Artemisia dimoana, commonly known as the red sage, is a perennial shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its aromatic, silver-gray foliage and clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and medicinal properties. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it suitable for dry garden environments.

Its essential oils contain compounds with potential anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial benefits.
