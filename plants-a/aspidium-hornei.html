Aspidium hornei

Aspidium hornei, commonly known as the Horne's water fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia, including India, China, and Southeast Asia. The plant is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds that are typically bi- or tri-pinnate, with small, ovate to elliptical pinnae. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often growing on rocks or in forest understories.

Aspidium hornei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
