Anthemis handel-mazzettii

Anthemis handel-mazzettii, commonly known as the Ethiopian chamomile, is a flowering plant native to Ethiopia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers with white petals and a yellow center. The plant typically grows in high-altitude regions and has aromatic leaves that emit a subtle, sweet scent. It is valued for its potential medicinal properties and is often used in traditional herbal remedies.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for both horticultural and pharmacological study.
