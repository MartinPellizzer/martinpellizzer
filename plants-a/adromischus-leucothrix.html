Adromischus leucothrix

Adromischus leucothrix, commonly known as the silver leaf ice plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, rounded leaves that are covered in a fine, silvery coating, which helps reduce water loss. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white and pink during the growing season. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Due to its compact size and ornamental appearance, it is often cultivated as a decorative plant in arid environments.
