Avenella flexuosa var. elatior

Avenella flexuosa var. elatior, commonly known as the greater avenelle, is a flowering plant native to eastern North America.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in moist, open habitats such as woodlands and wetlands. The plant features slender, ascending stems with alternate, lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy. Its small, yellowish-white flowers appear in clusters and bloom in early summer.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting pollinators.
