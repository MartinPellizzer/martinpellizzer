Anotis leschenaultiana var. deltoidea

Anotis leschenaultiana var. deltoidea, commonly known as the broad-leafed anotis, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, deltoid-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, yellow-orange composite flowers that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It typically grows in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
