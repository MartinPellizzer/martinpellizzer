Anthemis leucanthemifolia subsp. philistea

Anthemis leucanthemifolia subsp.

philistea, commonly known as the pale anthemis, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and western Asia. It is characterized by its finely divided, gray-green leaves and small, yellow-orange composite flowers.

The plant typically grows in dry, sunny habitats such as rocky soils and grasslands. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes many other flowering plants with similar composite flower structures.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and has been cultivated for its delicate appearance.
