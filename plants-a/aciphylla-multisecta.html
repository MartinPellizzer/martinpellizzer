Aciphylla multisecta

Aciphylla multisecta, commonly known as the multi-sected silver grass, is a perennial shrub native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its silver-gray, woolly foliage that helps it retain moisture in arid environments. Aciphylla multisecta is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
