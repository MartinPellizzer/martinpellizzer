Actinidia fanjingshanensis

Actinidia fanjingshanensis, commonly known as the Fanjingshan actinidia, is a rare species of flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Actinidiaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. The plant produces edible berries that are similar in appearance to kiwifruit but are less commonly cultivated.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a valuable species for botanical research and conservation efforts.
