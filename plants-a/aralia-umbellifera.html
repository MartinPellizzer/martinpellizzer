Aralia umbellifera

Aralia umbellifera, commonly known as the umbrella aralia, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compound leaves, which are often palmately lobed and can grow quite large. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in an umbel, giving it its common name. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Aralia umbellifera is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
