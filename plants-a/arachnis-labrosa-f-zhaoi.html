Arachnis labrosa f. zhaoi

Arachnis labrosa f. zhaoi, commonly known as the Zhao's arachnis, is a rare and unique botanical variant within the Araceae family.

It is characterized by its striking, often variegated leaves that display a combination of green, white, and pink hues. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically borne on a spadix, a common feature in many arum species. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, making it a sought-after addition to tropical and subtropical greenhouses.

Due to its limited distribution and ornamental value, it is often cultivated by enthusiasts and botanists alike.
