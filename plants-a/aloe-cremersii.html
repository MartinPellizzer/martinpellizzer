Aloe cremersii

Aloe cremersii, commonly known as the Cape tulip, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features striking, tubular, red or orange flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has thick, fleshy leaves that store water, typical of succulents. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for arid environments.

Aloe cremersii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
