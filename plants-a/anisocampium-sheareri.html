Anisocampium sheareri

Anisocampium sheareri, commonly known as the narrowleaf false nettle, is a perennial herb native to the southeastern United States.

The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, and its stems are often covered with fine hairs. Anisocampium sheareri typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.
