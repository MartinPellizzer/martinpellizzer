Aralia deleauana

Aralia deleauana, commonly known as the giant aralia, is a striking plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, compound leaves that can reach several feet in length, often with a feathery appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, greenish-white flowers that are followed by bright red berries, which are an important food source for local wildlife. Aralia deleauana thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for botanical gardens and conservatories.
