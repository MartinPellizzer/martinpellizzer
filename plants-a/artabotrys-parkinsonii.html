Artabotrys parkinsonii

Artabotrys parkinsonii, commonly known as the pink shower tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, fragrant, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in clusters, resembling a shower of blossoms. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a smooth texture. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The tree is also valued for its aromatic wood, which is used in traditional crafts and furniture-making.
