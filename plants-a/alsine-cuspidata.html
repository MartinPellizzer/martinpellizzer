Alsine cuspidata

Alsine cuspidata, commonly known as the sharp-leafed sandwort, is a low-growing annual plant native to North America.

It typically thrives in sandy or gravelly soils, often found in open, disturbed areas such as roadsides and dunes. The plant has small, alternate leaves that are usually lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are clustered in loose inflorescences and are typically pink to purple in color.

Alsine cuspidata is adapted to arid environments and plays a role in stabilizing sandy soils.
