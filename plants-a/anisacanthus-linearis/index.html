Anisacanthus Linearis

Anisacanthus linearis is a striking evergreen shrub native to the southwestern united states. it is known for its long narrow leaves and vibrant red flowers that bloom in summer. this plant is highly drought tolerant and thrives in full sun. it is often used in xeriscaping due to its low maintenance requirements. its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.
