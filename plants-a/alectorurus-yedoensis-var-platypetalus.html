Alectorurus yedoensis var. platypetalus

Alectorurus yedoensis var. platypetalus, commonly known as the Japanese orchid or the Yedo orchid, is a species of orchid native to Japan.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically feature a combination of white, pink, and purple hues. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with rhizomes that produce multiple shoots. Its leaves are broad and ovate, often with a waxy texture.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is cultivated in gardens for its beauty and fragrance.
