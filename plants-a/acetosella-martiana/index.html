Acetosella Martiana

Acetosella martiana is a small, delicate plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe. It is commonly known as the dwarf sorrel and belongs to the buckwheat family. This species is often found growing in rocky or sandy soils at high altitudes. Its leaves are heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs, giving it a unique appearance. Despite its modest size, Acetosella martiana plays an important role in its ecosystem.
