Adenanthos eyrei

Adenanthos eyrei, commonly known as the coastal adenanthos, is a flowering plant native to southern Australia.

It is a low-growing shrub with narrow, needle-like leaves that are covered in a fine, white wax. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that appear in late winter to early spring. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in coastal regions.

Adenanthos eyrei is well adapted to dry, sunny conditions and is an important component of coastal vegetation.
