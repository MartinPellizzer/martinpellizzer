Apterigia caespitosa

Apterigia caespitosa, commonly known as the mountain everlasting, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or alpine environments. The plant features small, clustered flowers that bloom in shades of purple and white. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it often forms dense, low-growing mats.

Apterigia caespitosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its hardy nature.
