Alophia geniculata

Alophia geniculata, commonly known as the zigzag lily, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive zigzag-shaped stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, often with a greenish tint. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Alophia geniculata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
