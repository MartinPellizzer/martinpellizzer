Astronia loheri

Astronia loheri, commonly known as the Loher's astronia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous shrub, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are small and yellow, arranged in dense clusters.

Astronia loheri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its visual interest.
