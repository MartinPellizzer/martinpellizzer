Ardisia Cornudentata Var. Morrisonensis

Ardisia cornudentata var. morrisonensis is a rare and striking plant native to the cloud forests of Southeast Asia. It is known for its glossy, dark green leaves and vibrant red berries that attract a variety of wildlife. This variant of Ardisia is particularly valued for its ornamental qualities and unique foliage. Due to habitat loss and over-collection, it is now considered endangered in its natural range. Conservation efforts are underway to protect and propagate this beautiful species.
