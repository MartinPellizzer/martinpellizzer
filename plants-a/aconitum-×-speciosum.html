Aconitum × speciosum

Aconitum × speciosum, commonly known as the hybrid monkshood, is a striking perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its tall, spire-like stems that bear clusters of dark purple, hood-shaped flowers. The plant's leaves are deeply lobed and have a distinctive, almost poisonous appearance. It thrives in well-drained, fertile soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Aconitum × speciosum is valued in gardens for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for its dramatic floral display.
