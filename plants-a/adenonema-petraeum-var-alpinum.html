Adenonema petraeum var. alpinum

Adenonema petraeum var. alpinum, commonly known as the alpine adenonema, is a rare alpine plant native to high-altitude regions of the Himalayas.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its compact growth habit and small, clustered leaves. The plant produces delicate, yellowish flowers during the short growing season of its mountainous habitat. It thrives in rocky soils and requires full sun exposure.
