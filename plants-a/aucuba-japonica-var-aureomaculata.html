Aucuba japonica var. aureomaculata

Aucuba japonica var. aureomaculata, commonly known as the Japanese aucuba with yellow spots, is a compact evergreen shrub native to Japan.

Its foliage features dark green leaves with striking yellow or gold spots, creating a vibrant visual contrast. The plant has a dense, bushy growth habit and produces small, inconspicuous white flowers in the spring. It is well-suited for shaded garden environments and is often used as a decorative groundcover or border plant.

The leaves remain attractive throughout the year, making it a popular choice for landscaping in cooler climates.
