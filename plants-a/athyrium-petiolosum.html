Athyrium petiolosum

Athyrium petiolosum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf millefleur, is a fern native to eastern Asia.

It features delicate, feather-like fronds that are typically bright green with a silvery underside. The plant grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in forest ecosystems.
