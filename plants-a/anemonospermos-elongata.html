Anemonospermos elongata

Anemonospermos elongata, commonly known as the elongated anemonosperma, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, elongated stems and clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories or along stream banks. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture.
