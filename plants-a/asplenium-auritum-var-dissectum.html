Asplenium auritum var. dissectum

Asplenium auritum var. dissectum, commonly known as the dissected maidenhair fern, is a striking evergreen fern native to tropical regions of Asia.

It features delicate, finely divided fronds that give it a lacy, almost ethereal appearance. The plant's fronds are typically dark green and have a distinctive, feathery texture. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often used in ornamental horticulture.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
