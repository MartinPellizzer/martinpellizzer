Armeria Maritima Var. Sardoa

Armeria maritima var. sardoa is a striking coastal plant known for its vibrant pink flowers and hardy nature. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils along the shores of the Mediterranean and other temperate regions. This variety is prized for its ornamental value and ability to withstand harsh maritime conditions. Its compact growth habit makes it an ideal choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes. With its delicate yet resilient appearance, it adds a touch of elegance to any garden setting.
