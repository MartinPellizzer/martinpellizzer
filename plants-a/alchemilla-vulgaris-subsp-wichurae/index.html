Alchemilla Vulgaris Subsp. Wichurae

Lady's mantle, scientifically known as Alchemilla vulgaris subsp. wichurae, is a hardy perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It is recognized for its attractive, velvety foliage that ranges from silver-green to deep green. This plant thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas or along stream banks. Its common name is derived from its historical use in herbal medicine and its ability to supposedly turn lead into gold. Lady's mantle is a popular choice for gardeners seeking a low-maintenance, ornamental plant with ecological benefits.
