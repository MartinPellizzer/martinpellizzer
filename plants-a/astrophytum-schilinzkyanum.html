Astrophytum schilinzkyanum

Astrophytum schilinzkyanum, commonly known as the Schiller's star cactus, is a species native to the arid regions of Mexico.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems that are often covered in a layer of white, waxy bloom. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is a slow-growing cactus that thrives in well-draining soil and full sun.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
