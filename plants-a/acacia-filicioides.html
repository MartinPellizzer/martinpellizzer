Acacia filicioides

Acacia filicioides, commonly known as the fern-like acacia, is a shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its feathery, fern-like foliage, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are followed by flat, pod-like fruits containing seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Acacia filicioides is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive foliage and adaptability to various climates.
