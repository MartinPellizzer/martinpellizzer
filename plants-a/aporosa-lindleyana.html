Aporosa lindleyana

Aporosa lindleyana, commonly known as the velvetleaf.

It is a tropical shrub belonging to the Rubiaceae family, characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture. Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is cultivated in some regions for horticultural purposes.
