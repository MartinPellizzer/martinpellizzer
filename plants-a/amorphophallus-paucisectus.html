Amorphophallus paucisectus

Amorphophallus paucisectus, commonly known as the narrow-leaved arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive leaf morphology and inflorescence structure. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a narrow, pointed tip and produces a spadix surrounded by a spathe. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in swampy or marshy areas.

Its botanical features make it a notable species in both ecological and horticultural contexts.
