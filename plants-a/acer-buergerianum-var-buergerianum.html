Acer buergerianum var. buergerianum

Acer buergerianum var. buergerianum, commonly known as the Japanese maple, is a deciduous tree native to Japan.

It is characterized by its ornamental foliage, which displays vibrant colors during the growing season and in autumn. The leaves are typically palmately lobed, with a smooth margin and a glossy green surface. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for gardens and landscapes.

This variety is valued for its aesthetic appeal and is often cultivated for its seasonal beauty.
