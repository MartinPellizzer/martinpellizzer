Arisaema japonicum var. akitense

Arisaema japonicum var. akitense, commonly known as the Akitan Japanese Arrowhead, is a herbaceous perennial native to Japan.

It features a distinctive, arrowhead-shaped leaf that is typically green with a silvery underside. The plant produces an unusual, spadix-like inflorescence surrounded by a pale green spathe. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

The plant has ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its unique foliage and flowering structure.
