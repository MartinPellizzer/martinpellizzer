Alternanthera martii f. pinheirensis

Alternanthera martii f. pinheirensis, commonly known as the Pink Beauty or Pink Alternanthera, is a striking aquatic plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant pink to red foliage, which becomes more intense under strong light conditions. This cultivar is a popular choice for aquariums due to its ornamental value and ease of care. It thrives in warm, moderately lit environments with soft to moderately hard water.

Its leaves are typically narrow and lance-shaped, adding a unique visual appeal to planted tanks.
