Asplenium divisissimum

Asplenium divisissimum, commonly known as the finely divided spleenwort, is a member of the Aspleniaceae family.

It is a small, epiphytic fern that typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant is characterized by its finely divided, feathery fronds that give it a delicate appearance. Its rhizomes are short and often form dense clusters, which help anchor it to its substrate.

Asplenium divisissimum is often found in temperate regions and is valued for its ornamental qualities in horticulture.
