Artanthe spectabilis

Artanthe spectabilis, commonly known as the showy artanthe, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its striking, pink to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant features basal leaves that are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long-lasting blooms and drought tolerance.
