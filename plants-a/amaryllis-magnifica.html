Amaryllis magnifica

Amaryllis magnifica, commonly known as the giant amaryllis, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can reach up to 12 inches in diameter, typically in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant produces strap-like leaves that are broad and dark green, often emerging after the flowering period. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, requiring a period of dormancy to bloom successfully.

Amaryllis magnifica is a popular choice for gardeners seeking bold, dramatic floral displays.
