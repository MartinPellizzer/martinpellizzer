Anemone oenanthe

Anemone oenanthe, commonly known as the meadow anemone, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features a rosette of basal leaves that are typically heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs. The plant produces showy, white or pale pink flowers with a distinctive central cluster of stamens. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows, woodlands, and along stream banks.

Anemone oenanthe is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting pollinators.
