Artemisia nujianensis

Artemisia nujianensis, commonly known as the Chinese wormwood, is a perennial shrub native to the arid regions of China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, silver-gray foliage. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is drought-tolerant, making it well adapted to its native environment.

Artemisia nujianensis is often used in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.
