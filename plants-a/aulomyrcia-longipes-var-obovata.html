Aulomyrcia longipes var. obovata

Aulomyrcia longipes var. obovata, commonly known as the broad-leafed aulomyrcia, is a variant of the aulomyrcia plant native to specific regions of South America.

It is characterized by its broad, obovate leaves that are typically arranged oppositely along the stems. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually white or pale pink, which bloom during the warmer months.
