Anil zeyheri

Anil zeyheri, commonly known as Turkish nightshade, is a herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other nightshades like tomatoes and potatoes. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30-60 cm, featuring simple, alternate leaves that are ovate to lanceolate in shape. Its flowers are usually white or pale purple, with five petals, and it produces small, round berries that turn from green to red as they mature.

Anil zeyheri is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
