Aloe montemartinii

Aloe montemartinii, commonly known as the Montemartin aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a reddish tinge, arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces tall, branched stems with yellow flowers during the summer months. It is adapted to arid environments and requires well-draining soil to thrive.

Aloe montemartinii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
