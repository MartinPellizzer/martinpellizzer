Anaphalis sinica var. densata

Anaphalis sinica var. densata, commonly known as the denseflower mouse-ear, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its woolly, gray-green leaves and small, clustered yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as mountainous regions. Its flowers are often visited by pollinators like bees and butterflies.
