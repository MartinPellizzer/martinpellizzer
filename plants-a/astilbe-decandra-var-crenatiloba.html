Astilbe decandra var. crenatiloba

Astilbe decandra var. crenatiloba, commonly known as the serrated leaf astilbe, is a herbaceous perennial native to eastern Asia.

It features upright, branching stems that bear panicles of fragrant, pinkish-white flowers. The leaves are palmately divided with serrated edges, giving the plant a delicate, lace-like appearance. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and ability to add texture and color to shaded landscapes.
