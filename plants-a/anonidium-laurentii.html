Anonidium laurentii

Anonidium laurentii, commonly known as the African snowball tree, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the family Anonaceae and is characterized by its showy, clustered white flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces small, round fruits that are typically green when immature and turn yellow or red when mature. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a waxy texture, while its bark is smooth and grayish in color.

Anonidium laurentii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine in some African countries.
