Abutilon badium

Abutilon badium, commonly known as the velvetleaf abutilon, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and showy, lantern-like flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant typically grows as an annual or perennial herb, reaching heights of up to 2 meters. Its hairy stems and foliage give it a distinctive, fuzzy appearance.

It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in disturbed soils or along roadsides.
