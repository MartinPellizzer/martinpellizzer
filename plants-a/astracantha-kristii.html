Astracantha kristii

Astracantha kristii, commonly known as the Kristii's thornveld, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features spiny stems and narrow, grass-like leaves, adapted to its arid environment. It produces small yellow flowers that bloom in spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological role, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
