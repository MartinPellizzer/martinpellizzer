Anthurium discolor

Anthurium discolor, commonly known as the White Spathe Anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking white spathe, which contrasts with its deep green, velvety foliage. The plant produces small, greenish-white spadices that are typically hidden within the spathe. It thrives in humid, warm environments with bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium discolor is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
