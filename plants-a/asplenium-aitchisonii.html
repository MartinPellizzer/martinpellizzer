Asplenium aitchisonii

Asplenium aitchisonii, commonly known as the Aitchison's spleenwort, is a small, evergreen fern native to the mountainous regions of northern India and southern China.

It belongs to the family Aspleniaceae and is characterized by its compact, rosette-like growth habit. The plant features delicate, lance-shaped fronds that are typically green with a slightly serrated margin. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found growing on rock surfaces or in crevices.

Asplenium aitchisonii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
