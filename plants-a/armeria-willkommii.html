Armeria willkommii

Armeria willkommii, commonly known as the sea thrift, is a low-growing evergreen perennial native to coastal regions of southern Africa.

It features dense clusters of bright white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has succulent, fleshy leaves that help it retain moisture in its arid habitat. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its hardy nature and attractive appearance make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
