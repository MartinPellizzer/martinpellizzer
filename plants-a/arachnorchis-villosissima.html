Arachnorchis villosissima

Arachnorchis villosissima, commonly known as the hairy spider orchid, is an epiphytic orchid native to Australia.

It is characterized by its hairy, yellow-green pseudobulbs and linear leaves. The plant produces striking, spider-like flowers with long, slender petals and a prominent, club-shaped lip. It typically grows in shaded, humid environments such as rainforests.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
