Aristolochia cubensis

Aristolochia cubensis, commonly known as the Cuban birthwort, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean, particularly Cuba.

It is a member of the Aristolochiaceae family and is characterized by its unique, vine-like growth habit. The plant produces striking, hooded flowers that are often purple or greenish in color. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a rough texture.

However, it contains toxic compounds and is not recommended for cultivation or consumption.
