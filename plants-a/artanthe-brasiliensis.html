Artanthe brasiliensis

Artanthe brasiliensis, commonly known as the Brazilian artanthe, is a flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the family Amaranthaceae and is characterized by its striking, showy inflorescences. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in shaded or semi-shaded environments. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered in a fine, powdery coating.

Artanthe brasiliensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
