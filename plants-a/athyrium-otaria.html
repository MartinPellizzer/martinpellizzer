Athyrium otaria

Athyrium otaria, commonly known as the Japanese shield fern, is a species of fern native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the family Athyriaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, shield-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its leaves are bi-lobed and have a velvety texture, with a deep green coloration.

Athyrium otaria is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic foliage.
