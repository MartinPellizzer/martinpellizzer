Asplenium barkamense

Asplenium barkamense, commonly known as the barkamense spleenwort, is a species of fern native to the mountainous regions of Central America.

It belongs to the family Aspleniaceae and is characterized by its compact, evergreen foliage. The plant typically grows on rocky surfaces or in shaded, moist environments, showcasing its epiphytic or lithophytic growth habits. Its leaves are lanceolate and often have a serrated margin, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Asplenium barkamense is valued for its ornamental qualities and is occasionally cultivated in tropical gardens.
