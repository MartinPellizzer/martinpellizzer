Alpinia gracillima

Alpinia gracillima, commonly known as the slender ginger, is a perennial herb native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes other gingers and related plants. The plant features slender, grass-like leaves and produces fragrant, pale yellow flowers. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and aromatic properties.
