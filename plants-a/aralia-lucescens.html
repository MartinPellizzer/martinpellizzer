Aralia lucescens

Aralia lucescens, commonly known as the shining aralia, is a deciduous shrub native to the eastern United States.

It features compound leaves that are typically palmately arranged, with each leaflet being elongated and pointed. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers in umbel-like clusters, which are followed by bright red berries. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in shaded garden landscapes.
