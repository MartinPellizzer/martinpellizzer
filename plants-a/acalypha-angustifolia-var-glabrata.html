Acalypha angustifolia var. glabrata

Acalypha angustifolia var. glabrata, commonly known as the smoothleaf ironweed, is a herbaceous plant characterized by its slender, lance-shaped leaves that are smooth and glabrous.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often found in woodlands and stream banks. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually greenish-white in color, which bloom in the summer months.
