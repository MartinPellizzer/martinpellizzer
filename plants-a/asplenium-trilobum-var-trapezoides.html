Asplenium trilobum var. trapezoides

Asplenium trilobum var. trapezoides, commonly known as the trapezoid shield fern, is a variety of the common shield fern.

It is a small, evergreen fern that typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped fronds with a distinctive trapezoidal outline on the underside. Its spore-producing structures, called sori, are located on the undersides of the fronds.

This variety is often found in rocky or woodland soils and is valued for its ornamental appearance in horticulture.
