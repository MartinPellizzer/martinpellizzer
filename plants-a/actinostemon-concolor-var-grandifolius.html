Actinostemon concolor var. grandifolius

Actinostemon concolor var. grandifolius, commonly known as the large-leaved actinostemon, is a variant of the actinostemon concolor species.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a smooth surface. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually pale pink to white in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

This variant is often found in woodland habitats and is valued for its ornamental foliage.
