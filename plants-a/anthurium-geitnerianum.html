Anthurium geitnerianum

Anthurium geitnerianum, commonly known as the velvet leaf anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its dark green, velvety leaves that are often heart-shaped or arrowhead-shaped. The plant produces striking spadices, which are brightly colored, spadix-like structures that serve as the flower. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium geitnerianum is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
