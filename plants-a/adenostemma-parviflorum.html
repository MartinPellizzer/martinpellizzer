Adenostemma parviflorum

Adenostemma parviflorum, commonly known as the small-flowered adenostemma, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa and Asia.

Adenostemma parviflorum is known for its adaptability to various soil types and its ability to thrive in semi-arid conditions.
