Athysanus unilateralis

Athysanus unilateralis, commonly known as the single-sided orchid, is a rare and unusual species belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its unique floral structure, where only one side of the flower develops fully, giving it a striking asymmetrical appearance. Native to specific regions in South America, this plant thrives in humid, shaded environments within cloud forests. Its flowers are typically pale green or white, with intricate patterns that aid in pollination by specific insect species.

Due to its rarity and ecological significance, Athysanus unilateralis is considered a valuable subject for botanical research.
