Amaryllis × stenantha

Amaryllis × stenantha, commonly known as the hybrid amaryllis, is a striking bulbous plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, pink, white, and yellow. The plant produces strap-like leaves that are broad and lance-shaped, often emerging from the base of the bulb. Its flowers typically bloom in the spring or early summer, adding a dramatic touch to gardens.

This hybrid is prized for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation in well-drained soil.
