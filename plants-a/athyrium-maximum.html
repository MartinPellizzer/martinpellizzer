Athyrium maximum

Athyrium maximum, commonly known as the giant lady fern, is a striking fern species native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, robust fronds that can reach up to 2 meters in height, making it one of the largest ferns in its native habitat. The plant has a delicate, feathery appearance with finely divided, bright green leaves that are often covered in a layer of silky, white hairs. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and conservation efforts.
