Amphitecna megalophylla

Amphitecna megalophylla, commonly known as the large-leafed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, elongated leaves that can reach several inches in length. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers with a distinctive coloration, often featuring a mix of white, purple, and green hues. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, typically found in tropical forests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
