Aeranthes neoperrieri

Aeranthes neoperrieri, commonly known as the Perrier's orchid, is a species native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its unique floral structure. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are typically greenish-yellow with intricate patterns. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in forest understories.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
