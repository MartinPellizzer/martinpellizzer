Acrostichum rigens

Acrostichum rigens, commonly known as the stiff sword fern, is a perennial evergreen fern native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It features large, lance-shaped fronds that are rigid and upright, giving it its common name. The plant has a creeping rhizome that allows it to spread and form dense mats. Its fronds are typically dark green and glossy, with a leathery texture.

Acrostichum rigens thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found near water sources.
