Achillea ptarmica var. yezoensis

Achillea ptarmica var. yezoensis, commonly known as Yezo Yarrow, is a perennial herb native to Japan.

It features slender, upright stems covered in fine, grayish-white hairs. The plant produces small, fluffy white or pale yellow composite flowers arranged in flat-topped clusters. Its leaves are narrow, serrated, and aromatic, with a slightly bitter scent.

Yezo Yarrow is often found in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and rocky slopes.
