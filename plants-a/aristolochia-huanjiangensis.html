Aristolochia huanjiangensis

Aristolochia huanjiangensis, commonly known as the Huanjiang birthvane, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Aristolochiaceae family and is characterized by its unique, tubular flowers that resemble a heart shape. The plant produces dark purple to brown fruits that contain seeds enclosed in a fleshy, sticky coating. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal uses, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
