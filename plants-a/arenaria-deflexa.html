Arenaria deflexa

Arenaria deflexa, commonly known as the bent sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Arctic and subarctic regions.

It thrives in harsh environments such as tundra and rocky slopes, showcasing its adaptability to extreme climates. The plant has small, fleshy leaves that help reduce water loss, and its flowers are typically white or pink, appearing in clusters. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds, aiding in its propagation.

Arenaria deflexa plays a vital role in stabilizing soil and supporting local ecosystems in its native habitats.
