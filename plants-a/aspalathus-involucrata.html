Aspalathus involucrata

Aspalathus involucrata, commonly known as African red bush, is a small evergreen shrub native to the Western Cape region of South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, rounded growth habit and bright red flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and requires full sunlight.

Aspalathus involucrata is also the source of rooibos tea, which is renowned for its unique flavor and health benefits.
