Anemone multifida var. nowasadii

Anemone multifida var. nowasadii, commonly known as the Nowasadii anemone, is a herbaceous perennial native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features showy, white to pale pink flowers with deeply divided, lace-like petals that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has finely dissected, fern-like leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained, loamy soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in rock gardens or as a border plant.
