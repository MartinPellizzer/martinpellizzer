Arctotis australiensis

Arctotis australiensis, commonly known as the Australian arctotis, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, daisy-like flowers that range in color from yellow to red. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and woodlands. It has hairy, narrow leaves and produces numerous small seeds that are dispersed by the wind.

Arctotis australiensis is well adapted to arid conditions and is often used in native gardens for its ornamental value.
