Atractylis chinensis

Atractylis chinensis, commonly known as Chinese Atractylis, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in temperate regions and thrives in well-drained soils. Its leaves are often hairy and have a distinct aromatic scent.
