Anthurium argyrostachyum

Anthurium argyrostachyum, commonly known as the silver-stem anthurium, is a striking tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its glossy, heart-shaped leaves that are often a deep green with a metallic sheen. The plant produces striking spadix flowers surrounded by a broad, colorful spathe, typically in shades of red or white. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

Anthurium argyrostachyum is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and ability to purify indoor air.
