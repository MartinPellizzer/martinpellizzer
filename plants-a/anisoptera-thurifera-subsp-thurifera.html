Anisoptera thurifera subsp. thurifera

Anisoptera thurifera subsp.

thurifera, commonly known as the giant senna, is a large shrub or small tree native to tropical regions of South America. It is characterized by its compound leaves with numerous small leaflets and yellow flowers arranged in racemes.

The plant produces long, slender pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires a warm, humid climate.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its foliage.
