Adonis dentata var. subinermis

Adonis dentata var. subinermis, commonly known as the slender adonis, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe.

It features a basal rosette of narrow, toothed leaves and produces striking yellow flowers in early spring. The plant is characterized by its slender stems and is often found in rocky or gravelly soils. It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family, which includes buttercups and other similar flowering plants.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species in many areas.
