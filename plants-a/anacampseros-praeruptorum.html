Anacampseros praeruptorum

Anacampseros praeruptorum, commonly known as the red-flowered stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, paddle-shaped leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces vibrant red flowers that bloom in summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
