Andropogon vulgaris var. major

Andropogon vulgaris var. major, commonly known as switchgrass, is a perennial grass native to North America.

It is characterized by its tall, sturdy stalks and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant is well adapted to a variety of soil types and is often found in prairie and wetland habitats. Switchgrass is known for its ability to thrive in both full sun and partial shade.

It plays an important role in ecological restoration and is also used as a bioenergy crop.
