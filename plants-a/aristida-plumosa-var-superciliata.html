Aristida plumosa var. superciliata

Aristida plumosa var. superciliata, commonly known as the silky hairgrass, is a grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-gray foliage and fluffy, feathery inflorescences that give it a soft, ornamental appearance. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky outcrops. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a slight curl at the tips, contributing to its distinctive look.

The species is often used in landscaping for its visual appeal and low maintenance requirements.
