Acaena laxa

Acaena laxa, commonly known as the slender weed, is a species of flowering plant in the family Rosaceae.

It is a low-growing perennial shrub characterized by its soft, hairy stems and small, yellowish flowers. The plant produces small, spiny fruits that contain seeds, which are dispersed by animals. Native to South America, it is often found in disturbed soils and open habitats.
