Agrostis Canina F. Varians

Agrostis canina f. varians is a rare variant of the common bentgrass known for its unique growth characteristics. This plant thrives in specific environmental conditions, often found in moist, nutrient-rich soils. Its foliage displays subtle variations in color and texture compared to the standard form. Despite its limited distribution, it holds significant ecological value in certain habitats. Studying this variant offers insights into plant adaptation and genetic diversity.
