Athanasia villosa

Athanasia villosa, commonly known as the silky everlasting, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features showy, daisy-like blooms with yellow centers and white to pale blue petals. Its leaves are covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Athanasia villosa is often cultivated for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
