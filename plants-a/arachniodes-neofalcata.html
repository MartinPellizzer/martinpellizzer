Arachniodes neofalcata

Arachniodes neofalcata, commonly known as the silver lace fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, lacy fronds that give it a distinctive, almost spider-web-like appearance. The plant features bipinnate leaves with closely spaced, toothed segments that create a finely divided look. It thrives in shady, moist environments and is often used in ornamental gardening for its aesthetic appeal.

Arachniodes neofalcata is valued for its elegant foliage and ability to add a touch of subtlety to indoor or outdoor spaces.
