Astronidium circumscissum

Astronidium circumscissum, commonly known as the Shining Star Plant, is a member of the Rubiaceae family and is native to tropical regions of South America.

It is a shrub characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine, white hairs. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, arranged in clusters. Its fruit is a small, round capsule that splits open to release seeds.

Due to its ornamental value and unique appearance, it is cultivated in some botanical gardens and private collections.
