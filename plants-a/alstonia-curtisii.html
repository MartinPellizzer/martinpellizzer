Alstonia curtisii

Alstonia curtisii, commonly known as the Curtis's alstonia, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Apocynaceae and is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and opposite, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers followed by orange-red berries. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional medicine.
