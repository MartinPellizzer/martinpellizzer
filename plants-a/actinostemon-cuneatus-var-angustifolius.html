Actinostemon cuneatus var. angustifolius

Actinostemon cuneatus var. angustifolius, commonly known as the narrow-leaved actinostemon, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features upright, branching stems that are often covered in fine hairs. The leaves are lance-shaped, narrow, and have a slightly serrated margin. It produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white in color.

This plant is adapted to grow in well-drained soils and is often found in grassland or rocky habitats.
