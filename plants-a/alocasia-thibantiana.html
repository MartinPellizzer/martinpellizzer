Alocasia thibantiana

Alocasia thibantiana, commonly known as the Thibantian arrowleaf, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its unique inflorescence consisting of a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe. The plant features large, heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and deep green coloration. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Alocasia thibantiana is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and ability to purify indoor air.
