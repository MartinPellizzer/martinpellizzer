Aloe rigens

Aloe rigens, commonly known as the cannonball aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, rosette-like arrangement of fleshy, blue-green leaves that are covered in a layer of white powder. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow-orange flowers that bloom in summer. Aloe rigens is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it suitable for arid climates.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental appearance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
