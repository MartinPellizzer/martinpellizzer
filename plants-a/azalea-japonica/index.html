Azalea Japonica

Azalea japonica is a striking flowering plant native to eastern Asia, known for its vibrant blooms and lush foliage. It belongs to the Rhododendron family and is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes. This species typically features clusters of showy, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to red and white. Azalea japonica thrives in acidic soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for horticultural enthusiasts. Its beauty and resilience make it a beloved addition to both natural and cultivated environments.
