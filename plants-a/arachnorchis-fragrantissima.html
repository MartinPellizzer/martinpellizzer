Arachnorchis fragrantissima

Arachnorchis fragrantissima, commonly known as the fragrant spider-orchid, is an exquisite member of the Orchidaceae family native to Australia.

It is characterized by its delicate, spider-like appearance with elongated, thread-like petals and a distinctive, fragrant flower. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in heathlands or forests. Its flowers bloom in spring, displaying a range of colors from white to pale yellow.

This rare and ornamental species is highly sought after by horticulturists and conservationists alike.
