Acoridium convallariiforme

Acoridium convallariiforme, commonly known as the red spider lily, is a member of the Acoraceae family.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its strap-like, dark green leaves and striking red flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or wetlands. Its flowers are usually clustered in a spiral at the tip of the leafy stem.

It is known for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
