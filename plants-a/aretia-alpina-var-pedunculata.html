Aretia alpina var. pedunculata

Aretia alpina var. pedunculata, commonly known as the Alpine aretia, is a flowering plant native to the alpine regions of Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, low-growing habit. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in dense clusters, which bloom in late summer. Its leaves are narrow, grass-like, and covered in a fine layer of white hairs.

It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is often found in mountainous environments.
