Argyrolobium roseum var. ornithopodioides

Argyrolobium roseum var. ornithopodioides, commonly known as the silverleaf flax or ornithopodioides flax, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-blue foliage and clusters of small, pink to purple flowers. The plant belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and has a creeping growth habit with hairy stems. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens for its attractive appearance and drought tolerance.
