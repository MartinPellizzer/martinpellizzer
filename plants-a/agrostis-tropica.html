Agrostis tropica

Agrostis tropica, commonly known as tropical bentgrass, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands, marshes, and along stream banks. The plant has fine, tufted leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny flowers. It is adapted to high humidity and can tolerate periodic flooding.

Agrostis tropica plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various wildlife species.
