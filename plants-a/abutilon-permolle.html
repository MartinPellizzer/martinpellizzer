Abutilon permolle

Abutilon permolle, commonly known as the velvetleaf abutilon, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its soft, fuzzy leaves and showy, lantern-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil and ample sunlight. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and blooms.

Abutilon permolle is also known for its ability to tolerate some degree of drought once established.
