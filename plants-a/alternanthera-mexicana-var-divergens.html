Alternanthera mexicana var. divergens

Alternanthera mexicana var. divergens, commonly known as the Mexican Alternanthera, is a herbaceous perennial native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage that often displays shades of red, purple, and green. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are opposite and often have a velvety texture, while the small, inconspicuous flowers appear in clusters.

This plant is valued in horticulture for its ornamental leaves and ability to add vibrant color to gardens and landscapes.
