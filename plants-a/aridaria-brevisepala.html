Aridaria brevisepala

Aridaria brevisepala, commonly known as the short-leafed desert plant, is a shrub adapted to arid environments.

It features small, narrow leaves that reduce water loss through transpiration. The plant produces clusters of pale yellow flowers during the warm season. Its root system is deep and extensive, allowing it to access underground water sources.

It plays a vital role in stabilizing sandy soils and providing habitat for desert wildlife.
