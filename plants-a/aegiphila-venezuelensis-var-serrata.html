Aegiphila venezuelensis var. serrata

Aegiphila venezuelensis var. serrata, commonly known as the Venezuelan serrated aegiphila, is a shrub native to the Andean regions of Venezuela.

The plant is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges, and it produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color.
