Adromischus cristatus

Adromischus cristatus, commonly known as the cristate jade plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy, ribbed leaves that are typically green with a bluish tint. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or white during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
