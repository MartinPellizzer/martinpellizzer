Asplenium sessilifolium var. occidentale

Asplenium sessilifolium var. occidentale, commonly known as the Western maidenhair fern, is a variant of the broad-leafed maidenhair fern.

It is a perennial fern native to North America, particularly found in the western United States. The plant features delicate, bright green, lance-shaped leaves that are typically borne on short, upright stalks. Its fronds are pinnately divided, with a distinctive, finely toothed margin.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories or along stream banks.
