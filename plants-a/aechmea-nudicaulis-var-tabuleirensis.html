Aechmea nudicaulis var. tabuleirensis

Aechmea nudicaulis var. tabuleirensis, commonly known as the Tabuleirensis air plant, is a bromeliad native to the subtropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that form a central cup to collect rainwater. The plant produces striking inflorescences with vibrant flowers that bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow. It thrives in humid environments with bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil.

This variety is popular among collectors for its ornamental value and unique growth habit.
