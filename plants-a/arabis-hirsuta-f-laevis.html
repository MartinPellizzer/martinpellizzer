Arabis hirsuta f. laevis

Arabis hirsuta f. laevis, commonly known as the smooth hairy rockcress, is a variant of the hairy rockcress species.

It is a low-growing perennial plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family. The plant features small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are generally smooth and have a slightly hairy texture.

It typically thrives in rocky or gravelly soils in temperate regions.
