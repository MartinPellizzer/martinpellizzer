Agrostophyllum dicksoniae

Agrostophyllum dicksoniae, commonly known as the Dickson's grass tree, is a unique and striking plant native to the tropical rainforests of Queensland, Australia.

It belongs to the Poaceae family, which includes grasses, and is characterized by its tall, slender, grass-like appearance. The plant features a single, unbranched stem that can grow up to 5 meters in height, topped with a dense cluster of small, needle-like leaves. Its flowering spikes, which appear at the top of the stem, produce tiny white or pale blue flowers that bloom during the wet season.

This plant is not only ornamental but also plays a role in the ecological balance of its native habitat.
