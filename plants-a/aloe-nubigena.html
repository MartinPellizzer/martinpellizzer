Aloe nubigena

Aloe nubigena, commonly known as the cloud aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its upright, branching stems and fleshy, sword-shaped leaves that are often covered in a whitish powdery coating. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late summer. Aloe nubigena is adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves to survive drought conditions.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and resilience.
