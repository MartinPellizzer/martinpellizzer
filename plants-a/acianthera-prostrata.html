Acianthera prostrata

Acianthera prostrata, commonly known as the prostrate acianthera, is a terrestrial orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its low-growing habit, with slender stems and linear leaves. The plant produces small, pale green to white flowers that are typically clustered in racemes. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in humid forests.

Due to its delicate structure and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
