Anthurium hutchisonii

Anthurium hutchisonii, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are dark green with a velvety texture. The plant produces vibrant red spathes that enclose bright yellow spadices, making it a visually appealing ornamental species. It thrives in humid, warm environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium hutchisonii is popular in horticulture for its aesthetic value and relatively easy care requirements.
