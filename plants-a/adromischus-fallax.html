Adromischus fallax

Adromischus fallax, commonly known as the silver leaf plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, silver-gray leaves that are covered in fine, white hairs. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the growing season. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Its compact growth habit and striking appearance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation.
