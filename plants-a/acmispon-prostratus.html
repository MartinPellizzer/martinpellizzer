Acmispon prostratus

Acmispon prostratus, commonly known as the prostrate milkvetch, is a low-growing annual legume native to the western United States and parts of Canada.

It has slender, creeping stems that often lie close to the ground, forming dense mats. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are typically clustered in dense spikes. Its trifoliate leaves are compound, with three oval-shaped leaflets.

Acmispon prostratus is adapted to arid environments and is often found in disturbed soils, such as along roadsides or in sandy areas.
