Aranella

Aranella, commonly known as the white aralia, is a member of the Araliaceae family.

It is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America. The plant features large, compound leaves that can reach several feet in length. It produces small, white flowers arranged in umbels, which are followed by dark, berry-like fruits.

Aranella is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in warm, humid climates.
