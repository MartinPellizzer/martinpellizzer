Adenopus abyssinicus var. somaliensis

Adenopus abyssinicus var. somaliensis, commonly known as the Somali adenopus, is a shrub native to the Horn of Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, spiny foliage and small, pale green leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, adding visual interest to its appearance. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, making it well adapted to dry climates.

Its unique morphology and hardy nature make it a notable species in its native habitat.
