Anagallis linifolia var. eulinifolia

Anagallis linifolia var. eulinifolia, commonly known as the slender strawberry gentian, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of western Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, trailing stems and small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

The species is notable for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
