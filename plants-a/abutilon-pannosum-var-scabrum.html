Abutilon pannosum var. scabrum

Abutilon pannosum var. scabrum, commonly known as the rough abutilon, is a flowering plant native to parts of South America.

It is characterized by its hairy, glandular stems and leaves that are often rough to the touch. The plant produces showy, lantern-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in disturbed soils or along riverbanks.
