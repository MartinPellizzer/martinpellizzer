Artemisia junnanica

Artemisia junnanica, commonly known as Junnan wormwood, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, silver-gray foliage. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are typically clustered in composite heads. It thrives in dry, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Artemisia junnanica is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional Chinese medicine.
