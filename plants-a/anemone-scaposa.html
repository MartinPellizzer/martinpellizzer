Anemone scaposa

Anemone scaposa, commonly known as the slender anemone, is a herbaceous perennial native to the northern regions of North America.

It features upright, leafless stems that bear showy, white or pink flowers with numerous petal-like sepals. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and stream banks. Its leaves are basal, simple, and often covered in fine hairs.

Anemone scaposa is valued for its early spring blooms and is often used in native plant gardens.
