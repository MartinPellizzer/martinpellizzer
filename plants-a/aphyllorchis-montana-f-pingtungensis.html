Aphyllorchis montana f. pingtungensis

Aphyllorchis montana f. pingtungensis, commonly known as the Pingtung orchid, is a rare orchid species native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique floral structure and compact growth habit. The plant typically features small, showy flowers with intricate petal arrangements, often in shades of white and yellow. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in montane forests.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a conservation concern.
