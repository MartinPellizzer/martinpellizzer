Atractylis Persica

Persian atractylis, scientifically known as Atractylis persica, is a striking perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Iran. With its distinctive silver-green foliage and showy yellow flowers, it adds a unique touch to any garden or landscape. This plant is prized for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained soils. It is also valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits. Despite its beauty, it requires careful cultivation to ensure its survival in various climates.
