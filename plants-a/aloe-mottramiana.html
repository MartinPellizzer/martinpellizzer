Aloe mottramiana

Aloe mottramiana, commonly known as the Mottram's aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped arrangement of thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a reddish or purple hue. The leaves are covered in a layer of wax, which helps reduce water loss in arid environments. It produces yellow or orange flowers on tall, branched stems during the warmer months.

This species is well adapted to dry conditions and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
