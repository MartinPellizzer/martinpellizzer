Arachnorchis concinna

Arachnorchis concinna, commonly known as the slender spider-orchid, is an epiphytic orchid native to southeastern Australia.

It produces delicate, spider-like flowers that are typically pale green or yellow with brown markings. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, often growing on tree trunks or branches. Its root system is adapted for absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air and rain.

Arachnorchis concinna is known for its unique morphology and is a subject of interest in botanical studies.
