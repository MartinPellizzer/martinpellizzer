Anulocaulis leiosolenus var. leiosolenus

Anulocaulis leiosolenus var. leiosolenus, commonly known as the smooth-stemmed anulocaulis, is a flowering plant native to the Andes Mountains of South America.

It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-forming leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of trichomes. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences that bear white or pale pink flowers. This variant is distinguished by its smooth, glabrous stem and is often found in cloud forest environments.
