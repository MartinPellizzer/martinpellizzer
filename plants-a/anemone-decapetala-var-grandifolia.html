Anemone decapetala var. grandifolia

Anemone decapetala var. grandifolia, commonly known as the large-flowered anemone, is a herbaceous perennial native to the northeastern United States.

It features showy, white to pale pink flowers with numerous petals that bloom in early spring. The plant has a basal rosette of hairy, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodlands and along stream banks.

Its botanical characteristics make it a desirable addition to native plant gardens and ecological restoration projects.
