Aloe berevoana

Aloe berevoana, commonly known as the Zulu aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, with a waxy coating that helps it retain moisture. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow flowers, which bloom in the spring. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Aloe berevoana is often used in traditional medicine and as an ornamental plant due to its hardy nature and aesthetic appeal.
