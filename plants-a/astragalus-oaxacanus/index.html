Astragalus Oaxacanus

Astragalus oaxacanus is a rare flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of central Mexico. It belongs to the legume family and is known for its delicate, pea-like flowers that bloom in the spring. This species is highly sought after by botanists due to its limited distribution and unique ecological role. Despite its beauty, it faces threats from habitat loss and climate change. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this remarkable species for future generations.
