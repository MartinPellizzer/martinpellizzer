Aspilia baoulensis

Aspilia baoulensis, commonly known as the African flame flower, is a flowering plant native to West Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking, bright orange to red flower heads. The plant typically grows as an annual herb, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and covered in fine hairs.

The flowers are composed of numerous small tubular florets surrounded by bracts, giving it a fiery appearance.
