Adhatoda neesiana

Adhatoda neesiana, commonly known as the Indian Trumpet or Blue Trumpet, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces striking blue or purple trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Adhatoda neesiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
