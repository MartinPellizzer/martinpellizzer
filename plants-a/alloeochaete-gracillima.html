Alloeochaete gracillima

Alloeochaete gracillima, commonly known as the slender feather grass, is a delicate annual grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its fine, hair-like leaves and slender, branching stems that grow close to the ground. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences bearing numerous tiny flowers, which are typically wind-pollinated. Alloeochaete gracillima thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed areas.

Its botanical features make it a valuable component of local ecosystems, supporting various insect and bird species.
