Adesmia glomerula var. glomerula

Adesmia glomerula var. glomerula, commonly known as the clustered adesmia, is a flowering plant native to parts of South America.

It is a low-growing shrub characterized by its dense clusters of small, pea-like flowers that typically bloom in spring. The plant features trifoliate leaves composed of three oval-shaped leaflets, and its stems are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its compact growth and seasonal blooms.
