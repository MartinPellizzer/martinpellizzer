Astropanax volkensii

Astropanax volkensii, commonly known as the African ginseng, is a member of the Araliaceae family and is native to tropical Africa.

It is a shrub that can grow up to 2 meters tall, featuring palmate leaves with five to seven lobes. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in umbels, followed by dark blue berries. Its roots are rich in bioactive compounds, making it valuable in traditional medicine.

The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
