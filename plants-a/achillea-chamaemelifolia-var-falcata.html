Achillea chamaemelifolia var. falcata

Achillea chamaemelifolia var. falcata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved yarrow, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, upright stems that bear narrow, lance-shaped leaves with a silvery-gray underside. The plant produces clusters of small, flat-topped flower heads composed of numerous yellow to white florets. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its aromatic foliage and long-lasting flowers make it both ornamental and useful in traditional herbal medicine.
