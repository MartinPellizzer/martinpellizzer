Aidia micrantha var. msonju

Aidia micrantha var. msonju, commonly known as the Msonju lily, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in partial to full shade. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slight waxy coating, which helps reduce water loss.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and fragrance.
