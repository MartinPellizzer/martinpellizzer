Antirrhinum segurae

Antirrhinum segurae, commonly known as the Segura snapdragon, is a flowering plant native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It belongs to the Plantaginaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant typically grows in rocky or limestone soils and thrives in full sun. It has narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces seed pods that split open to release its seeds.
