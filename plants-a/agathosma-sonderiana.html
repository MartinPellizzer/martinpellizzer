Agathosma sonderiana

Agathosma sonderiana, commonly known as Cape tea or Agathosma, is a small evergreen shrub native to the Western Cape region of South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, narrow leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. The plant produces small yellow flowers that bloom in spring, attracting various pollinators. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Due to its medicinal properties and ornamental value, it is also cultivated in horticultural settings.
