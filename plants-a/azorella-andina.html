Azorella andina

Azorella andina, commonly known as the Andean azorella, is a member of the Apiaceae family and is native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

Azorella andina is often found in alpine meadows and rocky slopes, where it plays a role in stabilizing soil and supporting local biodiversity.
