Artemisia oelandica

Artemisia oelandica, commonly known as Oeland wormwood, is a perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as meadows and rocky soils. The plant features gray-green, aromatic foliage with narrow, lance-shaped leaves. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers that appear in clusters during the summer months.

Artemisia oelandica is valued for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally in herbal remedies.
