Anemone esquirolii

Anemone esquirolii, commonly known as the Esquiroli's anemone, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe.

The plant has palmate, lobed leaves that are often hairy and adapted to its alpine habitat.
