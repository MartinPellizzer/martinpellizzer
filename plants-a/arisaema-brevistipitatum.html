Arisaema brevistipitatum

Arisaema brevistipitatum, commonly known as the short-stemmed arrowhead, is a herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive arrow-shaped leaves and a spadix surrounded by a spathe. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands. Its underground tubers are sometimes used in traditional medicine.

Arisaema brevistipitatum is a member of the Araceae family, which includes other ornamental and economically important plants.
