Arundinella hirta var. ciliatiglumis

Arundinella hirta var. ciliatiglumis, commonly known as the hairy bamboo or slender bamboo, is a perennial grass native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It features slender, cylindrical stems that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it its common name. The leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a slightly hairy texture along the edges. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers in panicles, which are typically wind-pollinated.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adaptable to a variety of soil types.
