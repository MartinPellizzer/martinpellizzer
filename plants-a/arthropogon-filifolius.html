Arthropogon filifolius

Arthropogon filifolius, commonly known as the slender grass or filifolius grass, is a member of the Poaceae family.

It is a perennial grass that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It is often found in open woodlands and grasslands.

Its botanical features make it suitable for use in erosion control and as a forage plant.
