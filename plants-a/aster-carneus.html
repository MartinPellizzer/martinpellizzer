Aster carneus

Aster carneus, commonly known as the woolly aster, is a perennial flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its fuzzy, grayish-green leaves and showy blue to purple flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant belongs to the sunflower family (Asteraceae) and is often found in rocky or sandy soils. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Aster carneus is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in poor soil conditions.
