Aeschynomene bracteosa f. glabra

Aeschynomene bracteosa f. glabra, commonly known as the smooth bracteosa morning glory, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and heart-shaped leaves with a smooth texture. The plant produces small, pale blue or purple flowers that bloom in clusters. It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is often found in disturbed soils and open habitats.
