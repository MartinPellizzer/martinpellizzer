Aria richii

Aria richii, commonly known as the richii arrowhead, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features heart-shaped, arrowhead-like leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy surface. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Aria richii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable climates.
