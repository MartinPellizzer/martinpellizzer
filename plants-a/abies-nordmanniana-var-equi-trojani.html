Abies nordmanniana var. equi-trojani

Abies nordmanniana var. equi-trojani, commonly known as the Trojani fir, is a rare and distinctive variety of the Nordmann fir.

It is characterized by its dense, conical shape and dark green needles that are shorter and stiffer than those of the typical Nordmann fir. The tree produces small, cylindrical cones that are typically blue-green in color. It is native to the Caucasus region and is adapted to high-altitude environments with cold climates.

Due to its limited natural range and slow growth, it is considered a valuable and often rare ornamental tree in horticulture.
