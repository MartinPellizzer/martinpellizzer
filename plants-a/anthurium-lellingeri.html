Anthurium lellingeri

Anthurium lellingeri, commonly known as the Lellinger's arrowhead, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped leaves that often display a rich green color with a metallic sheen. The plant produces striking spadices, which are the modified leaves that enclose the small, inconspicuous flowers. Anthurium lellingeri thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and greenhouse cultivation.
