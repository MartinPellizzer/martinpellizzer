Amoora maingayi

Amoora maingayi, commonly known as the white soursop, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its large, glossy, heart-shaped leaves. The tree produces yellowish-green fruits with a rough, spiny exterior, containing a sweet, aromatic pulp. Its flowers are small, clustered, and typically white or pale green.
