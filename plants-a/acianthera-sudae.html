Acianthera sudae

Acianthera sudae, commonly known as the Southern Sudan orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a terrestrial herbaceous plant characterized by its slender, upright stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. It is native to the grasslands and rocky areas of southern Sudan and northern Uganda.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
