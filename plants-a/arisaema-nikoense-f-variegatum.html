Arisaema nikoense f. variegatum

Arisaema nikoense f. variegatum, commonly known as the Variegated Japanese Taro, is a unique cultivar of the Arisaema genus native to Japan.

It is characterized by its striking variegated leaves that display a mix of green, white, and yellow hues. The plant produces a spadix surrounded by a spathe, which is typical of the Arisaema family. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Due to its distinct foliage, it is a popular choice for gardeners seeking visual interest in shaded landscapes.
