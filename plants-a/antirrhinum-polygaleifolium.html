Antirrhinum polygaleifolium

Antirrhinum polygaleifolium, commonly known as the broadleaf toadflax, is a perennial flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

Antirrhinum polygaleifolium is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive foliage and floral display.
