Alocasia illustris

Alocasia illustris, commonly known as the black magic plant, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark, glossy leaves that have a striking, almost metallic appearance. The plant belongs to the Araceae family, which includes other ornamental plants like peace lilies and calla lilies. Alocasia illustris thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and as a statement plant in tropical landscapes.
