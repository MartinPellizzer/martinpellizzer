Anthurium cowanii

Anthurium cowanii, commonly known as the Cowan's anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a metallic sheen. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-shaped inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, often in shades of red, orange, or yellow. It thrives in humid, warm environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium cowanii is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and long-lasting blooms.
