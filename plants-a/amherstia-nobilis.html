Amherstia nobilis

Amherstia nobilis, commonly known as the noble amherstia, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the nightshade family (Solanaceae) and is characterized by its striking violet-blue flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant produces small, oval-shaped berries that are typically green when mature and turn red when ripe. Amherstia nobilis is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its attractive foliage and showy blossoms.

It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.
