Aloe fulleri

Aloe fulleri, commonly known as the Natal aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, sword-shaped leaves that are typically green with a reddish tinge along the edges. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing yellowish-orange flowers. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves.

Aloe fulleri is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional medicinal uses.
