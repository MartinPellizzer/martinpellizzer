Arikuryroba ruschiana

Arikuryroba ruschiana, commonly known as the Brazilian velvetleaf, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are velvety to the touch. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in disturbed soils.

Arikuryroba ruschiana is known for its rapid growth and is sometimes considered a weed in agricultural settings.
