Allamanda polyantha

Allamanda polyantha, commonly known as the golden trumpet vine, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant produces clusters of these vibrant blooms, which are often used in ornamental gardening. Its leaves are glossy and dark green, providing a striking contrast to its colorful flowers.

Allamanda polyantha is typically grown as a climbing plant, requiring support for its vigorous growth.
