Alsophila mollicula

Alsophila mollicula, commonly known as the velvet tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnately divided fronds that can reach several meters in length. The plant has a distinctive velvety texture on its fronds, which gives it its common name. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Alsophila mollicula plays an important ecological role by providing habitat and food for various forest organisms.
