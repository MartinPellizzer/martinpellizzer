Aloe verrucosospinosa

Aloe verrucosospinosa, commonly known as the spiny aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that are covered in short, spiny projections. The leaves are typically blue-green in color and can grow up to 1 meter in height. It produces yellow-orange flowers during the summer months, which are clustered in terminal inflorescences.

This species is adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
