Aneilema cymosum

Aneilema cymosum, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems and clustered leaves. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of purple, pink, and white, often with a contrasting throat. It thrives in humid, well-drained soil and requires indirect light for optimal growth.

Aneilema cymosum is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and ease of care.
