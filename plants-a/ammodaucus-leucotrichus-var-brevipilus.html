Ammodaucus leucotrichus var. brevipilus

Ammodaucus leucotrichus var. brevipilus, commonly known as the narrow-hair fennel, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, aromatic stems covered in fine, white hairs, which give it its common name. The plant produces yellow flowers in umbels, typical of the Apiaceae family, and has feathery, finely divided leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often growing in dry, rocky environments.
