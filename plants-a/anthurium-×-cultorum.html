Anthurium × cultorum

Anthurium × cultorum, commonly known as the flamingo flower, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Araceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful spathe that resembles a flower, though it is actually a modified leaf. The plant produces a spadix, which is the true flowering structure, covered in tiny, inconspicuous flowers. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.

Its striking appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental purposes in both indoor and outdoor settings.
