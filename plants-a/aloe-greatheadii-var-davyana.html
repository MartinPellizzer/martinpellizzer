Aloe greatheadii var. davyana

Aloe greatheadii var. davyana, commonly known as the Greathead's aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a waxy coating, adapted to store water in arid environments. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellowish-green flowers, which bloom in summer. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their soothing properties.

It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for xeriscaping.
