Aster chusanensis

Aster chusanensis, commonly known as the Chinese aster, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically produces showy blue to purple flowers with yellow centers, and it thrives in well-drained soils. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

Aster chusanensis is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
