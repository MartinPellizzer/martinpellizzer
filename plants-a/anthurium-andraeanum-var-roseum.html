Anthurium andraeanum var. roseum

Anthurium andraeanum var. roseum, commonly known as the pink anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are deep green with a velvety texture. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-shaped inflorescences that are typically pink or red, surrounded by a colorful spathe. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

This variety is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and long-lasting blooms.
