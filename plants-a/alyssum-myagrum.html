Alyssum myagrum

Alyssum myagrum, commonly known as sweet Alyssum, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple, blooming in clusters. The plant has silvery-green, fuzzy leaves and a compact, mounding growth habit. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Alyssum myagrum is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
