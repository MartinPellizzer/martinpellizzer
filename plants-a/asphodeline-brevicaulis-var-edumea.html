Asphodeline brevicaulis var. edumea

Asphodeline brevicaulis var. edumea, commonly known as the Edumea asphodel, is a compact, bulbous plant native to southern Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces tall, slender inflorescences with small, pale yellow to greenish-white flowers. The plant is adapted to arid and semi-arid conditions, making it drought-tolerant and suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes. Its foliage remains evergreen, providing visual interest throughout the year.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and resilience in low-maintenance garden settings.
