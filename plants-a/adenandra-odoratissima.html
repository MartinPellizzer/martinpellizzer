Adenandra odoratissima

Adenandra odoratissima, commonly known as the sweet olive, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a small evergreen shrub or tree that typically grows to a height of 2 to 6 meters. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters during the winter months. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and have a glossy dark green appearance.

The plant is valued for its ornamental value and is often used in landscaping due to its pleasant aroma and hardy nature.
