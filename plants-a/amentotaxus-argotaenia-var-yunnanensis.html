Amentotaxus argotaenia var. yunnanensis

Amentotaxus argotaenia var. yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan yew, is a rare and endangered conifer native to China.

It belongs to the Taxaceae family and is characterized by its scale-like leaves and pendulous cones. The plant is known for its slow growth and can reach heights of up to 20 meters in its natural habitat. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat loss, it is critically endangered and requires conservation efforts to survive.
