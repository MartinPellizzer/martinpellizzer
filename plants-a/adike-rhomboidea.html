Adike rhomboidea

Adike rhomboidea, commonly known as the diamond-shaped leaf plant, is a unique species characterized by its rhombus-shaped leaves.

Native to tropical regions, it thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect sunlight. The plant's foliage is often variegated, featuring shades of green, yellow, and white, which enhance its ornamental appeal. It has a compact growth habit, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.

The leaves are glossy and emit a mild, pleasant aroma when crushed.
