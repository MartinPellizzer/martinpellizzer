Argyreia rufohirsuta

Argyreia rufohirsuta, commonly known as the red-hair devil's ivy, is a climbing vine native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves that are velvety and covered in fine, reddish hairs. The plant produces small, pale green flowers that are followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its rapid growth and attractive foliage, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
