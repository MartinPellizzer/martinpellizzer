Alyssum trichostachyum var. oschtenicum

Alyssum trichostachyum var. oschtenicum, commonly known as the silverleaf alyssum, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features dense clusters of small, white flowers that bloom in spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. The plant has silver-gray foliage due to a layer of fine, white hairs covering its leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental value make it a versatile addition to various garden settings.
