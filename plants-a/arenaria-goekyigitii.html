Arenaria goekyigitii

Arenaria goekyigitii, commonly known as the Gökçiyiğit sandwort, is a flowering plant native to Turkey.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit. The plant features small, white or pink flowers that bloom in early summer. Its leaves are typically narrow and covered in fine hairs, adapting to arid environments.

It thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in alpine or mountainous regions.
