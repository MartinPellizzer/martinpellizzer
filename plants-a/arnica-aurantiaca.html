Arnica aurantiaca

Arnica aurantiaca, commonly known as the orange mountain hay.

It is a flowering plant native to the alpine regions of Europe and parts of Asia. The plant produces showy yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in a dense layer of fine hairs.

Arnica aurantiaca is often found growing in rocky soils and is adapted to cold, high-altitude environments.
