Albizia paivana

Albizia paivana, commonly known as the pink albizia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its compound leaves, which are typically pinnate and have a feathery appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually pink or white, arranged in racemes. Albizia paivana thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in warm climates.
