Aloe monteiroi

Aloe monteiroi, commonly known as Monteiro's aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asphodelaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are thick and fleshy. The leaves are typically gray-green in color with a textured surface and may have small teeth along the edges. It produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing yellowish-green flowers, which bloom in summer.

This plant is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
