Anthurium teribense

Anthurium teribense, commonly known as the Tiberense arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive inflorescence consisting of a spadix and a spathe. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and deep green coloration. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Anthurium teribense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated as a houseplant.
