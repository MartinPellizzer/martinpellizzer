Aloe umfoloziensis

Aloe umfoloziensis, commonly known as the Umfolozi aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a bluish tint. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing yellow flowers, which bloom during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun exposure.

Due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance, it is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
