Adonis baetica

Adonis baetica, commonly known as the Spanish globeflower, is a perennial plant native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It is characterized by its striking, globe-shaped yellow flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is often found in rocky or sandy soils. It is adapted to thrive in dry, sunny environments.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
