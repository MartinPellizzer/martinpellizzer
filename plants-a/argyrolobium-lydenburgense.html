Argyrolobium lydenburgense

Argyrolobium lydenburgense, commonly known as the Lydenburg silverlobed burr.

It is a perennial herb native to South Africa, characterized by its silver-white leaves and yellow flowers. The plant belongs to the Fabaceae family and is known for its unique, spiny pods that contain seeds. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky soils.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
