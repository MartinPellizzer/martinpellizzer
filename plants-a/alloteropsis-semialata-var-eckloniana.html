Alloteropsis semialata var. eckloniana

Alloteropsis semialata var. eckloniana, commonly known as the Ecklon's lovegrass, is a perennial grass native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, pale green to yellowish flowers in dense, spike-like inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and along coastal areas.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to stabilize soil in arid environments.
