Anthurium × greigii

Anthurium × greigii, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a hybrid plant native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped, red spathes that surround a spadix, which is typically yellow or orange. The plant thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth. Its foliage consists of large, glossy, dark green leaves that add to its ornamental appeal.

Anthurium × greigii is widely cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
