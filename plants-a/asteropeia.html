Asteropeia

Asteropeia, commonly known as the starflower, is a genus of flowering plants belonging to the family Asteraceae.

These plants are characterized by their showy, daisy-like flowers that typically feature a central disk surrounded by ray florets. Asteropeia species are native to various regions of the world, including parts of Asia and the Americas. They thrive in a variety of habitats, from moist woodlands to rocky slopes.

The genus is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting pollinators.
