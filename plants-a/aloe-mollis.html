Aloe mollis

Aloe mollis, commonly known as the silver aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that are covered in a powdery coating, which helps reduce water loss. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellowish-orange flowers during the summer months. It is adapted to arid environments and requires well-draining soil and minimal water.

Aloe mollis is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and low maintenance requirements.
