Acacia brevispica var. schweinfurthii

Acacia brevispica var. schweinfurthii, commonly known as the Schweinfurth acacia, is a shrub or small tree native to arid and semi-arid regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny foliage and yellow flowers that bloom in winter. The plant has compound leaves with small, paired leaflets and produces pods that contain seeds. It is adapted to survive in harsh environments with low rainfall and high temperatures.

Its botanical features make it an important component of local ecosystems and a source of traditional resources.
