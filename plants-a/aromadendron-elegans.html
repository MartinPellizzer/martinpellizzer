Aromadendron elegans

Aromadendron elegans, commonly known as the fragrant beauty, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its elegant, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
