Angraecum microphyton

Angraecum microphyton, commonly known as the tiny-leafed orchid, is a rare and fascinating species native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its small, narrow leaves and delicate, fragrant flowers that bloom at night. The plant belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is adapted to thrive in humid, shaded environments. Its unique morphology and nocturnal blooming habit make it a subject of interest for botanists and orchid enthusiasts.
