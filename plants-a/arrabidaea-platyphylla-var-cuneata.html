Arrabidaea platyphylla var. cuneata

Arrabidaea platyphylla var. cuneata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf arrabidaea, is a perennial shrub native to South Africa.

It features small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink, with five petals and a distinctive spurred structure. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy and arranged alternately along the stems. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to Mediterranean climates with dry summers.

Its botanical characteristics make it a unique and visually appealing addition to native gardens.
