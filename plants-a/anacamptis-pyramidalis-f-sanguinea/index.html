Anacamptis Pyramidalis F. Sanguinea

Anacamptis pyramidalis f. sanguinea is a striking variant of the pyramidal orchid known for its vibrant red petals. This rare and beautiful plant is part of the Orchidaceae family and is native to southern Europe. Its unique coloration makes it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts and botanists alike. The plant thrives in sunny, well-drained habitats and requires specific care to flourish. Its beauty and rarity make it a subject of both scientific study and horticultural admiration.
