Arenaria arctica var. grandiflora

Arenaria arctica var. grandiflora, commonly known as the Arctic sandwort, is a flowering plant native to cold, arctic and alpine regions.

It is a low-growing perennial that thrives in well-drained, sandy or rocky soils. The plant produces small, white or pink flowers in clusters during the short growing season of polar climates. Its leaves are typically fleshy and adapted to conserve moisture in harsh environments.

It plays a vital role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for Arctic wildlife.
