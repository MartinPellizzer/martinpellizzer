Aristolochia wenshanensis

Aristolochia wenshanensis, commonly known as the Wenshan pipevine, is a climbing vine native to China.

It is characterized by its distinctive, intricate, and often heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically greenish-white with a complex, winding structure. It belongs to the Aristolochiaceae family, known for its unique floral morphology.

However, it contains toxic compounds and is not recommended for cultivation due to its potential hazards.
