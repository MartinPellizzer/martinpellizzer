Alstroemeria albiflora

Alstroemeria albiflora, commonly known as the white-flowered alstroemeria, is a perennial plant native to South America.

It belongs to the family Alstroemeriaceae and is characterized by its striking white petals and vibrant green foliage. The plant produces tall, slender stems that bear clusters of showy, tubular flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.

Its ornamental value has led to its cultivation in various horticultural settings worldwide.
