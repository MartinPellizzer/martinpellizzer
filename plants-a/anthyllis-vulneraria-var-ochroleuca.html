Anthyllis vulneraria var. ochroleuca

Anthyllis vulneraria var. ochroleuca, commonly known as the yellow globemallow, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial herb characterized by its yellow flowers and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces small, spherical seed pods that contain numerous tiny seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
