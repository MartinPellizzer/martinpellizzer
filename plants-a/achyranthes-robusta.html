Achyranthes robusta

Achyranthes robusta, commonly known as the Indian knotgrass, is a robust annual herb native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features a stout, branched stem that is often covered with short, stiff hairs. The plant is notable for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional medicine for various health purposes.
