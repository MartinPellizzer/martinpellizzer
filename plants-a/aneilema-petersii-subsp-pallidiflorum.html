Aneilema petersii subsp. pallidiflorum

Aneilema petersii subsp.

pallidiflorum, commonly known as the pale-flowered aneilema, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to tropical Africa. It is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy.

The plant produces clusters of small, pale yellow to white flowers, which are arranged in inflorescences. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with suitable conditions.
