Aloe aaata

Aloe aaata, commonly known as the Mauna Loa aloe, is a succulent plant native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a waxy coating. The plant produces yellowish-orange flowers that bloom in summer, adding ornamental value. Aloe aaata is adapted to grow in volcanic soil and can thrive in full sun.

It is often used in traditional Hawaiian medicine and has cultural significance in the region.
