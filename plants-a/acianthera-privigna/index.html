Acianthera Privigna

Acianthera privigna is a rare and delicate orchid native to the tropical regions of South America. It is known for its striking violet-blue flowers and unique growth habits. This plant thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires careful cultivation. Its beauty makes it a prized specimen among orchid enthusiasts. Due to habitat loss, it is also considered a threatened species.
