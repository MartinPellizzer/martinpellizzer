Aneilema pusillum subsp. variabile

Aneilema pusillum subsp.

variabile, commonly known as the variable aneilema, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical Africa. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.

The plant features lance-shaped leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. Its flowers are small and clustered in inflorescences, often with a yellowish or greenish hue.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its foliage.
