Aerangis boutonii

Aerangis boutonii, commonly known as the Cape orchid, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers. The plant produces showy, violet-colored blooms with intricate patterns that attract pollinators. It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and prefers a sunny to partially shaded environment.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
