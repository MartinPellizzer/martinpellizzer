Albizia melanesica

Albizia melanesica, commonly known as the Melanesian siris, is a flowering plant native to the Pacific Islands.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is characterized by its leguminous properties and nitrogen-fixing capabilities. Albizia melanesica is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in some indigenous communities.
