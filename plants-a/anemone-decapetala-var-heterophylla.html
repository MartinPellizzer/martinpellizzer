Anemone decapetala var. heterophylla

Anemone decapetala var. heterophylla, commonly known as the heterophyllous anemone, is a variant of the common anemone with distinct leaf morphology.

It typically features basal leaves that are palmately lobed, while the upper leaves are more elongated and narrow. The plant produces showy white to pink flowers with a prominent central pistil and numerous stamens. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

This variety is often found in woodland areas and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
