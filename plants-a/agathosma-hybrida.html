Agathosma hybrida

Agathosma hybrida, commonly known as buchu, is a hardy, aromatic shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its small, fragrant leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant is well adapted to dry, rocky soils and can thrive in full sun. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine and as a culinary herb.

Agathosma hybrida is also valued for its essential oils, which are used in aromatherapy and natural remedies.
