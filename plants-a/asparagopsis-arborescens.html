Asparagopsis arborescens

Asparagopsis arborescens, commonly known as the tree asparagus, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asparagaceae family.

It is native to southern Africa and is characterized by its woody stems and evergreen foliage. The plant produces small, white or greenish flowers that are clustered in panicles, followed by small, berry-like fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.

Asparagopsis arborescens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its foliage and flowering display.
