Aloe amanensis

Aloe amanensis, commonly known as the African aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, spiky leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers on tall, branched stems during the warmer months. Its leaves contain a gel-like substance that has been used for its soothing properties.

Aloe amanensis is well-suited for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its low water requirements.
