Amaryllis × patentissima

Amaryllis × patentissima, commonly known as the patent amaryllis, is a hybrid plant resulting from crossbreeding between *Amaryllis belloni* and *Amaryllis papilio*.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, pink, and white. The plant produces trumpet-shaped blooms that are typically borne on a single stem, making it a striking addition to gardens or indoor spaces. It prefers well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, thriving in warm, humid environments.

This hybrid is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its vibrant, long-lasting flowers.
