Asterolasia buxifolia

Asterolasia buxifolia, commonly known as the box-leaf asterolasia, is a flowering plant native to the island of Socotra.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its dense, bushy growth habit. The plant features small, rounded leaves that resemble those of boxwood, giving it its common name. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, arranged in clusters, and bloom during the warmer months.

Asterolasia buxifolia is adapted to the arid and semi-arid environments of its native habitat.
