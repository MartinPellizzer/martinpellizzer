Anplectrum stellulatum

Anplectrum stellulatum, commonly known as the star-stemmed sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and grass-like plants. The plant has triangular stems and produces small, three-ranked flowers in spikelets. Its leaves are typically narrow and grass-like, with a distinct midrib.
