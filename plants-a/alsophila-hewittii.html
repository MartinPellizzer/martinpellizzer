Alsophila hewittii

Alsophila hewittii, commonly known as the Hewitt's tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds. Alsophila hewittii plays an important ecological role in its native habitat, providing shelter and food for various wildlife species.
