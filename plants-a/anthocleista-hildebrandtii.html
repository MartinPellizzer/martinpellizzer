Anthocleista hildebrandtii

Anthocleista hildebrandtii, commonly known as the African moonseed, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in umbels, and its leaves are typically elliptic or ovate in shape. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.

The fruit is a capsule that contains several small, smooth seeds.
