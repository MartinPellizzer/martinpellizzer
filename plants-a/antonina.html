Antonina

Antonina, commonly known as the red ant plant, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant red flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth texture and a deep green color. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Antonina is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
