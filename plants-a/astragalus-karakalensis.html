Astragalus karakalensis

Astragalus karakalensis, commonly known as Karakalinskaya milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its yellow flowers and compound leaves. The plant typically grows in arid and semi-arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. It has small, oval-shaped pods that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

This species is of interest in botanical studies due to its ecological and potential medicinal properties.
