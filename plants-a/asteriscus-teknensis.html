Asteriscus teknensis

Asteriscus teknensis, commonly known as the Teknese aster, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of numerous small florets. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun, exhibiting a compact, bushy growth habit. Its flowers are usually purple or blue, with ray florets that give it a daisy-like appearance.

Asteriscus teknensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is occasionally cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic and ecological benefits.
