Astrolepis cochisensis subsp. arizonica

Astrolepis cochisensis subsp.

arizonica, commonly known as Arizona star-scale. It is a perennial herb native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

The plant features a rosette of fleshy, strap-shaped leaves that are covered in a dense layer of white, star-shaped hairs. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers in late spring to early summer.

The species is adapted to arid environments and is often found in rocky soils and desert scrub habitats.
