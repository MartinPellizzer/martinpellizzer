Actephila gitingensis

Actephila gitingensis, commonly known as the Gitingensis ironwood, is a shrub native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Rubiaceae family and is characterized by its dense, woody structure and dark green, leathery leaves. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its fruit is a capsule containing numerous tiny seeds.
