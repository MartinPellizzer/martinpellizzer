Anthurium langsdorffii

Anthurium langsdorffii, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped spathe that ranges from red to orange in color. The plant features a striking, spadix that protrudes from the spathe, which is a common feature in the Araceae family. Its leaves are typically dark green and glossy, with a slightly wavy margin.

Anthurium langsdorffii thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect sunlight for optimal growth.
