Anthurium ovandense

Anthurium ovandense, commonly known as the Ovandense Arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to Central America.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive inflorescence consisting of a spadix and a spathe. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are typically deep green with a prominent venation pattern. It thrives in humid, warm environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Anthurium ovandense is often cultivated for its ornamental value and air-purifying properties.
