Araucaria rulei var. compacta

Araucaria rulei var. compacta, commonly known as the compact form of the Rule's Araucaria, is a unique variant of the Araucaria genus native to South America.

It is characterized by its dense, pyramidal shape and smaller, more closely clustered foliage compared to its standard counterpart. This variety exhibits needle-like leaves that are short and glossy, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It is typically evergreen and thrives in well-drained soil with moderate sunlight.

Due to its compact growth habit, it is often cultivated as a decorative plant in gardens and indoor spaces.
