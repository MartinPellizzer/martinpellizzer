Acaena ovalifolia var. elegans

Acaena ovalifolia var. elegans, commonly known as the elegant burr grass, is a low-growing perennial plant native to New Zealand.

It features small, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving them a soft texture. The plant produces bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring, followed by spiny, berry-like fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and subalpine regions.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological role, it is valued in both horticulture and conservation efforts.
