Alocasia erythraea

Alocasia erythraea, commonly known as the red-tailed arrowleaf, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features large, heart-shaped leaves with a vibrant red underside, which becomes visible when the foliage is turned over. The plant belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its unique inflorescence known as a spadix, surrounded by a colorful spathe. Alocasia erythraea thrives in humid, warm environments with bright, indirect light.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor and greenhouse cultivation.
