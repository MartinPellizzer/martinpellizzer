Armeriastrum latifolium

Armeriastrum latifolium, commonly known as broadleaf sandverbena, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a low-growing, mat-forming perennial that thrives in arid and semi-arid environments. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, white or pale pink flowers in summer. It is often found in rocky soils and open habitats such as grasslands and scrublands.

Its botanical characteristics make it well adapted to survive in harsh, dry conditions.
