Amaranthus × moquinii

Amaranthus × moquinii, commonly known as garden amaranth, is an annual plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its tall, branching stems and vibrant, showy flowers that range from red to orange in color. The plant produces small, three-lobed seeds that are often used as a food source. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental and edible gardens.

Its foliage is also edible and can be used in salads or cooked dishes.
