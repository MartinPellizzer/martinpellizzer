Acantholimon avenaceum var. avenaceum

Acantholimon avenaceum var. avenaceum, commonly known as the slender leafed stonecrop, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features succulent, fleshy leaves that are typically narrow and grass-like, adapted to retain moisture in arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white, which bloom in early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its hardy nature and ornamental value make it a popular choice for drought-tolerant landscaping.
