Aloe saudiarabica

Aloe saudiarabica, commonly known as the Saudi aloe, is a succulent plant native to the Arabian Peninsula.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that store water, adapting to the arid climate of its native habitat. The plant produces a gel-like substance within its leaves, which is used for medicinal and skincare purposes. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Its resilience and low maintenance make it a popular choice for desert gardens.
