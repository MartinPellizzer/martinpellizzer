Aphelandra lurida

Aphelandra lurida, commonly known as the dark-leaved angel's trumpet, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its dark green, glossy leaves and its showy, fragrant, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.
