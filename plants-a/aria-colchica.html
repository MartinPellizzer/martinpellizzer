Aria colchica

Aria colchica, commonly known as the Colchic Lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Caucasus region.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems that can reach up to 2 meters in height. The plant produces large, showy flowers with white petals and a prominent yellow stamen, typically blooming in late summer. Aria colchica thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a striking addition to gardens.

It is also notable for its medicinal properties, historically used in traditional medicine.
