Aspidistra zongbayi

Aspidistra zongbayi, commonly known as the Zongbai aspidistra, is a species native to China, specifically found in Yunnan Province.

It is a member of the Araceae family, characterized by its broad, dark green, strap-like leaves that are often variegated with silver or white markings. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically hidden beneath its foliage.

It is well adapted to shaded environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage.
