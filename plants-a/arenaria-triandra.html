Arenaria triandra

Arenaria triandra, commonly known as the sandstone flower, is a low-growing perennial plant native to rocky and arid environments.

It typically features small, white or pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has a mat-forming habit with hairy stems and narrow, linear leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Its botanical characteristics make it well adapted to harsh, dry conditions.
