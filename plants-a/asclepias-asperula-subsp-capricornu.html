Asclepias asperula subsp. capricornu

Asclepias asperula subsp.

capricornu, commonly known as the capricorn milkweed, is a species of flowering plant in the Apocynaceae family. It is native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico, where it thrives in arid and semi-arid environments.
