Aloe dyeri

Aloe dyeri, commonly known as the Cape aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features striking, fleshy, sword-shaped leaves that are typically blue-green in color with a waxy coating. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow or orange flowers, which bloom in the spring. Aloe dyeri is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional medicinal uses.
