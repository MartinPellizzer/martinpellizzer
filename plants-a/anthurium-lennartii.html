Anthurium lennartii

Anthurium lennartii, commonly known as the Lennart's anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces vibrant spadices, which are the characteristic inflorescences of the Anthurium genus, often in shades of red or orange. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Anthurium lennartii is popular as a decorative plant due to its ornamental foliage and unique floral structures.
