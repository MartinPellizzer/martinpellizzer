Aster tripolium subsp. longicaulis

Aster tripolium subsp.

longicaulis, commonly known as the longstem sand aster, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of Europe and North Africa. It is a low-growing herb with narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered blue or purple flowers.

The species thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in dunes or coastal habitats. It is adapted to harsh, salty environments and plays a role in stabilizing dune ecosystems.

The plant is typically found in open, sunny areas and is an important part of coastal flora.
