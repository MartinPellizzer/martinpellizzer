Argostemma pictum var. tetraphyllum

Argostemma pictum var. tetraphyllum, commonly known as the four-leaf arrowhead, is a herbaceous perennial native to Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive arrowhead shape, often with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, typically blooming in the summer months. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and ecological restoration projects.
