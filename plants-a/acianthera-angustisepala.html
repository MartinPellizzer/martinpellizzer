Acianthera angustisepala

Acianthera angustisepala, commonly known as the narrow-lobed acianthera, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers that are typically pale green to yellow in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species in its native range.
