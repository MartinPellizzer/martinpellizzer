Aspidium zollingerianum

Aspidium zollingerianum, commonly known as the Zollinger's water fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, feather-like fronds that are typically bioluminescent under certain conditions. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its spores are dispersed through the wind, contributing to its reproductive strategy.

Aspidium zollingerianum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in forest ecosystems.
