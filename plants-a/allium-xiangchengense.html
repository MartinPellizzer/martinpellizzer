Allium xiangchengense

Allium xiangchengense, commonly known as the Xiangcheng onion, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its bulbous structure and grass-like foliage. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale purple flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its ornamental value and potential medicinal properties, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists.
