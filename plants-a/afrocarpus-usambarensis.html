Afrocarpus usambarensis

Afrocarpus usambarensis, commonly known as the Usambara yellowwood, is a tall evergreen tree native to the mountainous regions of eastern Tanzania.

It belongs to the Podocarpaceae family and is characterized by its straight, cylindrical trunk and dark, aromatic wood. The tree has narrow, needle-like leaves that are dark green and glossy, and it produces small, inconspicuous yellow flowers.

Afrocarpus usambarensis is valued for its durable timber and plays an important ecological role in its native habitat.
