Aristolochia mindanaensis

Aristolochia mindanaensis, commonly known as the Mindanao birthwort, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its unique, heart-shaped leaves and its distinctive, pipe-like flowers that are often greenish-yellow in color. The plant is part of the Aristolochiaceae family, known for its complex chemical compounds. It thrives in tropical rainforests and is often found in moist, shaded environments.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
