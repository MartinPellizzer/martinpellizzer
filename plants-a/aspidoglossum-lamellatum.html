Aspidoglossum lamellatum

Aspidoglossum lamellatum, commonly known as the silver sword, is a striking plant native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of broad, silver-green leaves that are covered in a fine, powdery coating, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with small, tubular flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. It is a succulent, meaning it stores water in its leaves and is drought-tolerant.

Due to its unique look and low maintenance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and arid landscapes.
