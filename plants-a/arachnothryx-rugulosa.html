Arachnothryx rugulosa

Arachnothryx rugulosa, commonly known as the rough-leaved arachnothryx, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark, glossy leaves that are often covered in a fine, rough texture. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by woody pods containing flat, seed-like structures. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Its botanical features make it an important species in ecological studies and traditional medicine.
