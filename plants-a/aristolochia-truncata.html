Aristolochia truncata

Aristolochia truncata, commonly known as the Chinese birthwort, is a climbing vine native to East Asia.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a distinctive waxy texture. The plant produces intricate, pipe-shaped flowers that are typically greenish-yellow with a strong, unpleasant odor. Its roots and stems contain various alkaloids, some of which have been studied for potential medicinal properties.

However, the plant is toxic and should not be ingested without proper guidance.
