Aeranthes porrecta

Aeranthes porrecta, commonly known as the straight-flowered orchid, is a species native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, delicate flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its flowers are usually white or pale green, with a lip that is distinctly shaped and colored.

Aeranthes porrecta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
