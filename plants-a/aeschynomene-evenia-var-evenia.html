Aeschynomene evenia var. evenia

Aeschynomene evenia var. evenia, commonly known as partridge pea, is a leguminous plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is often found in dry, disturbed soils and is adapted to warm climates. It has a deep root system that helps it survive in arid conditions.

This species is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it useful in agricultural settings.
