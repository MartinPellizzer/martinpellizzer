Alternanthera ficoides

Alternanthera ficoides, commonly known as the red-stemmed alternanthera, is a tropical aquatic plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red stems and lance-shaped leaves that can vary in color from green to red depending on light exposure. This plant thrives in shallow, slow-moving waters and is often used in aquariums for its ornamental value. It prefers moderate to high light conditions and can tolerate a range of water temperatures.

Alternanthera ficoides is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for both beginner and experienced aquarists.
