Aulosepalum ramentaceum

Aulosepalum ramentaceum, commonly known as the silky leaf orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to certain regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, silky-textured leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces striking, often fragrant flowers that are typically pale yellow to greenish in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical forests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
