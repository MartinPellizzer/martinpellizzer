Aloe hoffmannii

Aloe hoffmannii, commonly known as Hoffmann's aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, with a waxy coating to reduce water loss. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow to orange flowers, which bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for arid environments.

Aloe hoffmannii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional medicinal uses.
