Adenophora jasionifolia

Adenophora jasionifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved bellflower, is a herbaceous perennial native to East Asia.

It features upright stems covered in fine, hairy hairs, and produces bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale blue. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often slightly hairy on the upper surface. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sun.

Adenophora jasionifolia is valued in horticulture for its ornamental flowers and ease of cultivation.
