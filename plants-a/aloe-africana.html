Aloe africana

Aloe africana, commonly known as the African aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features a rosette of fleshy, gray-green leaves that store water, adapting it to arid environments. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow or orange flowers, which attract pollinators like bees and birds. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their soothing properties.

Aloe africana is valued both for its ornamental beauty and its practical applications in horticulture and herbal remedies.
