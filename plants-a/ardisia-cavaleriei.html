Ardisia cavaleriei

Ardisia cavaleriei, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen or red-berried evergreen, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are elliptical in shape and often have a waxy texture. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers followed by bright red, berry-like fruits that are edible for some bird species. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Ardisia cavaleriei is valued for its attractive foliage and seasonal fruit display.
