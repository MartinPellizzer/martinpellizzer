Aster preissii

Aster preissii, commonly known as the Alpine aster, is a flowering plant native to the high altitudes of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically features bright yellow or white ray florets surrounding a central disk of smaller, tubular flowers. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to cold, arid environments.

Aster preissii is often found in alpine meadows and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance.
