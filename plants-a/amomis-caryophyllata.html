Amomis caryophyllata

Amomis caryophyllata, commonly known as the pink ursula, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family, which includes many species with showy, often pink or purple flowers. The plant features upright stems covered in fine hairs and produces clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or rocky areas.
