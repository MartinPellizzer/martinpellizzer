Anthurium nymphaeifolium var. ovatum

Anthurium nymphaeifolium var. ovatum, commonly known as the water lily anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often a deep green with a waxy texture. The plant produces striking spathes that range in color from white to pink, with a prominent yellow spadix inside. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, nutrient-rich soil.

This variety is popular in indoor gardening due to its ornamental foliage and unique floral structure.
