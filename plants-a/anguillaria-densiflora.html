Anguillaria densiflora

Anguillaria densiflora, commonly known as the dense-flowered anguillaria, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family, which includes hibiscus and cotton. The plant is characterized by its robust stems, large heart-shaped leaves, and clusters of showy, pale yellow to white flowers. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
