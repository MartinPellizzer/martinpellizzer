Antholyza vittigera

Antholyza vittigera, commonly known as the violet lily or blue violet lily, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia, particularly China and Japan.

It is a member of the Amaryllis family (Amaryllidaceae) and is characterized by its showy, violet-blue flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant has strap-like, grassy leaves that form a rosette at the base, and it produces a tall, slender stem with a single inflorescence. Antholyza vittigera is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its striking floral display and adaptability to various soil conditions.
