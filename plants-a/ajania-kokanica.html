Ajania kokanica

Ajania kokanica, commonly known as the Kokanica ajania, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky and alpine environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its foliage is often basal, with narrow, grass-like leaves, and it produces small, daisy-like flowers in the summer months.

Ajania kokanica is valued for its ornamental potential and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
