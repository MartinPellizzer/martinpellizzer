Acalypha unibracteata var. heterantha

Acalypha unibracteata var. heterantha, commonly known as the flame bush or red flame plant, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful bracts that range from red to orange, which are often mistaken for flowers. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually ovate to elliptic in shape. It produces small, inconspicuous yellow flowers clustered in dense inflorescences.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
