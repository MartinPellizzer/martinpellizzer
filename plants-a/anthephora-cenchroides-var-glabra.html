Anthephora cenchroides var. glabra

Anthephora cenchroides var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth anthephora, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features slender, erect stems with narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are smooth to the touch. Its flowers are typically yellow, arranged in clusters, and bloom during the warmer months.

It is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas.
