Aspidium unitum

Aspidium unitum, commonly known as the common water fern, is a species of fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its delicate, feather-like fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

Aspidium unitum plays an important ecological role in maintaining soil stability and providing habitat for small organisms.
