Adicea reticulata

Adicea reticulata, commonly known as the net-veined orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, moist environments. The plant features intricate, net-like venation on its leaves, which is a distinctive botanical characteristic. Its flowers are usually pale green or yellow, with a complex structure typical of orchids.

Adicea reticulata is often found in forest understories and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in its habitat.
