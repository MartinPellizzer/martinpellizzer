Aponogeton leptostachyus var. minor

Aponogeton leptostachyus var. minor, commonly known as the dwarf aquatic arrowhead, is a herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features narrow, arrow-shaped leaves that are typically submerged or floating, with a dark green coloration. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom above the water surface, adding ornamental value to aquatic gardens. It thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments with moderate light exposure.

This variety is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal and ease of care in pond settings.
