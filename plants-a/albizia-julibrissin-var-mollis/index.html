Albizia Julibrissin Var. Mollis

The Albizia julibrissin var. mollis, also known as the Chinese silk tree, is a striking ornamental plant native to eastern Asia. It is celebrated for its delicate, feathery leaves and showy, pinkish-red flowers that resemble clouds or silk ribbons. This variety is a popular choice for gardens due to its adaptability and ability to thrive in various soil conditions. Its blooming season typically occurs in late spring to early summer, adding a vibrant touch to landscapes. Despite its beauty, it requires proper care to maintain its health and prevent potential invasiveness in certain regions.
