Astragalus englerianus var. gingdongensis

Astragalus englerianus var. gingdongensis, commonly known as the Gingdong milkvetch, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to China.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. It is valued for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional Chinese medicine.

Its root system is known to have potential health benefits, though further research is needed to confirm its efficacy.
