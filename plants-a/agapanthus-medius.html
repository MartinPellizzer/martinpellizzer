Agapanthus medius

Agapanthus medius, commonly known as the African lily, is a striking perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features tall, upright stems that bear clusters of large, showy blue or purple flowers. The plant has broad, strap-like leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Agapanthus medius is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
