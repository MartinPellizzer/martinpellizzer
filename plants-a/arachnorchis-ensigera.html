Arachnorchis ensigera

Arachnorchis ensigera, commonly known as the spider orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to Australia.

It is characterized by its unique, spider-like appearance, with long, slender sepals and petals that resemble the legs of a spider. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as rainforests or under dense vegetation. Its flowers are usually greenish-yellow, with a distinctive hooded labellum that helps in attracting specific pollinators.

Arachnorchis ensigera is a rare and endangered species, requiring conservation efforts to ensure its survival.
