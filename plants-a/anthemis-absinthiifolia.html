Anthemis absinthiifolia

Anthemis absinthiifolia, commonly known as narrowleaf wormwood, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to southern Europe and western Asia, often found in rocky or sandy soils. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a distinctive aromatic scent.

It is sometimes used in traditional herbal remedies due to its medicinal properties.
