Arthrocnemum ciliolatum

Arthrocnemum ciliolatum, commonly known as the desert sandmat, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of Africa, the Middle East, and parts of Asia.

The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in summer, and its leaves are typically small, hairy, and adapted to reduce water loss.
