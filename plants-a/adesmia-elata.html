Adesmia elata

Adesmia elata, commonly known as the slender legume, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is characterized by its distinctive pod fruits and nitrogen-fixing abilities. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are compound, with small leaflets arranged in pairs, and it produces small, yellowish-white flowers.

The seeds are enclosed in slender, elongated pods that split open when mature to release the seeds.
