Anneslea fragrans var. ternstroemerioides

Anneslea fragrans var. ternstroemerioides, commonly known as the fragrant anneslea, is a rare and ornamental plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its evergreen foliage and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in the summer months. The plant has a compact growth habit and is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal and unique scent. It prefers well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for shaded garden environments.
