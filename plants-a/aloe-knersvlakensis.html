Aloe knersvlakensis

Aloe knersvlakensis, commonly known as the Knysna aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aloe genus and is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are thick and fleshy. The leaves are typically green with a reddish or purplish tinge, especially when exposed to full sun. It produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow flowers that bloom in the spring.

This plant is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
