Argyreia lawii

Argyreia lawii, commonly known as the "Blue Beauty" or "Blue Flowering Creeper," is a flowering vine native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking deep blue to violet flowers that bloom in clusters along its trailing stems. The plant has heart-shaped, glossy green leaves that are opposite in arrangement. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Argyreia lawii is a fast-growing plant that is well-suited for hanging baskets or trellises.
