Agathosma fastigiata

Agathosma fastigiata, commonly known as the Cape geranium, is a small, evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It features compact, rounded growth with narrow, dark green leaves that are densely packed along its stems. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple, blooming in spring and summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Agathosma fastigiata is also valued for its aromatic foliage, which is often used in traditional medicine.
