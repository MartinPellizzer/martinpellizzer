Amaryllis forgetii

Amaryllis forgetii, commonly known as the African lily, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, strap-like leaves and produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant is known for its ability to bloom profusely during the summer months. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Amaryllis forgetii is a popular choice for gardens and containers due to its vibrant blooms and relatively easy care requirements.
