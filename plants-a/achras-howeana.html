Achras howeana

Achras howeana, commonly known as the Natal date palm, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and the Indian Ocean.

It is a tall, slender tree with a straight trunk and a crown of pinnate leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that develop into edible fruits resembling dates. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to thrive in well-drained soils.

The Natal date palm is valued for its ornamental beauty and its ability to provide shade in warm climates.
