Agrostis cryptandra

Agrostis cryptandra, commonly known as the cryptandra grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its fine, wiry leaves and compact growth habit, often forming dense mats on rocky or sandy soils. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, showing drought tolerance and resilience to poor soil conditions.

Agrostis cryptandra is often found in fynbos vegetation, contributing to the ecological diversity of its habitat.
