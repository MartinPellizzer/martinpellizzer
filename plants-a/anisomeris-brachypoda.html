Anisomeris brachypoda

Anisomeris brachypoda, commonly known as the narrowleaf dandelion, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

Anisomeris brachypoda is adapted to arid conditions, with a deep root system that helps it conserve water.
