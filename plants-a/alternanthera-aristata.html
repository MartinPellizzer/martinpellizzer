Alternanthera aristata

Alternanthera aristata, commonly known as the velvet leaf.

It is a herbaceous plant native to South America, particularly Brazil, and is characterized by its striking, velvety foliage. The leaves are typically ovate to lanceolate, with a deep green color that often has a reddish or purple tinge on the underside. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full sun exposure.

This plant is often cultivated as an ornamental due to its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.
