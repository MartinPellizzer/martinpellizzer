Anthurium asplundii

Anthurium asplundii, commonly known as the asplundii arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to South America, specifically Brazil.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are often a deep green with a metallic sheen. The plant produces spadices, which are the spadix and spathe structures typical of the Araceae family, with the spathe being brightly colored, usually in shades of red or orange. Anthurium asplundii thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect light and well-draining soil.

It is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
