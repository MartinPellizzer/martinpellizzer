Aster menelii

Aster menelii, commonly known as the Menelii's aster, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that consist of both ray and disk flowers. The plant typically grows in arid and semi-arid environments, such as rocky soils and open plains. It produces small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in the summer months.

Aster menelii is often found in habitats with low moisture availability, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions.
