Anthurium bucayanum

Anthurium bucayanum, commonly known as the Bucayanum arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a glossy texture. The plant produces striking spadices, which are modified leaves that house the actual flowers, often in shades of red or yellow. It thrives in humid, warm environments and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Anthurium bucayanum is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
