Acer sunyiense

Acer sunyiense, commonly known as the Sunyi maple, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It belongs to the Sapindaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive palmate leaves with five to seven lobes. The tree produces small, yellowish-green flowers in early spring, followed by red to orange samaras in the fall. Its bark is smooth and grayish-brown, becoming more fissured with age.

Acer sunyiense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant autumn foliage.
