Anemone coronaria var. intermedia

Anemone coronaria var. intermedia, commonly known as the Intermediate Crown Flower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features showy, pink to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has hairy, lobed leaves that are often bluish-green in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its botanical characteristics make it both ornamental and ecologically valuable in its native habitat.
