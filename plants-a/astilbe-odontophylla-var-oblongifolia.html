Astilbe odontophylla var. oblongifolia

Astilbe odontophylla var. oblongifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf astilbe, is a herbaceous perennial native to eastern Asia.

It features upright, branching stems that bear clusters of fragrant, lace-like panicles of white or pink flowers. The plant has heart-shaped, dark green leaves that are broad and often slightly toothed. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is well-suited for woodland gardens.

Its ornamental value comes from its delicate foliage and showy floral displays.
