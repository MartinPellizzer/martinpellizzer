Aster rotundifolius

Aster rotundifolius, commonly known as the round-leaved aster, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of rounded, velvety leaves and produces clusters of small, blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes many other flowering plants with composite flower heads.

Aster rotundifolius is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive foliage and blooms.
