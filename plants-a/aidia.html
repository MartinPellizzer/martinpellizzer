Aidia

Aidia, commonly known as the bluebell or bellflower, is a genus of flowering plants belonging to the Campanulaceae family.

These plants are characterized by their bell-shaped flowers, which can range in color from blue to purple and white. Aidia species are typically herbaceous perennials, often found in woodland or rocky habitats. They produce small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.
