Arisaema wallichianum var. sikkimense

Arisaema wallichianum var. sikkimense, commonly known as the Sikkim arrowhead, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayan regions of India and Nepal.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its distinctive arrow-shaped leaves and a spadix inflorescence. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments at high altitudes. Its underground tubers are sometimes used in traditional medicine.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in its native habitat.
