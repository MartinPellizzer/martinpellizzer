Arthrophyllum lucens

Arthrophyllum lucens, commonly known as the luminous arthrophyllum, is a rare and striking plant native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, fern-like foliage and its unique ability to emit a faint bioluminescent glow during twilight hours. The plant's leaves are covered in fine, reflective hairs that enhance its luminous appearance. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in cloud forests.

Due to its rarity and aesthetic appeal, it is highly sought after by botanists and plant enthusiasts alike.
