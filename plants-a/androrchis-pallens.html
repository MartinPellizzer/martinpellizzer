Androrchis pallens

Androrchis pallens, commonly known as the pale orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Europe and western Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows. The plant features a slender, erect stem with a single, pale green or white flower that has a distinctive, elongated shape. Its leaves are linear and often covered in fine, soft hairs.

The species is noted for its delicate appearance and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
