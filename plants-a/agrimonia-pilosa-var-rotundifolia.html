Agrimonia pilosa var. rotundifolia

Agrimonia pilosa var. rotundifolia, commonly known as roundleaf agrimony, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features upright, branched stems covered in soft, grayish-green hairs. The leaves are oval to round in shape, with serrated edges, and are arranged alternately along the stem. Its small yellow flowers appear in late spring to early summer, forming dense clusters at the tips of the branches.

The plant is known for its medicinal properties, traditionally used to treat digestive issues and skin conditions.
