Arctotis tomentosa

Arctotis tomentosa, commonly known as the woolly arctotis, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features fuzzy, woolly leaves and produces vibrant, daisy-like flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its botanical characteristics make it both ornamental and suitable for attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies.
