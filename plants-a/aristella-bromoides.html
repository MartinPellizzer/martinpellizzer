Aristella bromoides

Aristella bromoides, commonly known as the silverleaf dandelion, is a herbaceous plant native to South America.

It features a basal rosette of silver-gray, fuzzy leaves that help reflect sunlight and reduce water loss. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that develop into white, puffball-like seed heads. It thrives in dry, sunny environments and is often found in disturbed soils.

Its unique appearance and adaptability make it a notable species in its native habitat.
