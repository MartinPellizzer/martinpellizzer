Aloe grata

Aloe grata, commonly known as the silver aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy, silver-gray leaves that store water, aiding in its survival in arid environments. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellowish-green flowers during the summer months. Its leaves contain a gel-like substance that has been used for its soothing and healing properties.

Aloe grata thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it an ideal choice for xeriscaping and rock gardens.
