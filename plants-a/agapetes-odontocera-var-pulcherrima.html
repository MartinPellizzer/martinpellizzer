Agapetes odontocera var. pulcherrima

Agapetes odontocera var. pulcherrima, commonly known as the purple bellflower, is a species of flowering plant in the Ericaceae family.

It is native to the high-altitude regions of South America, particularly in the Andes. The plant is characterized by its striking purple or violet flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its foliage consists of small, leathery leaves that are densely packed on the stems.
