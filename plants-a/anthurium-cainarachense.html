Anthurium cainarachense

Anthurium cainarachense, commonly known as the Cauca anthurium, is a species native to the mountainous regions of Colombia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves and vibrant spadix. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments at elevations between 1,000 and 2,500 meters. Its foliage is often dark green with a waxy texture, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers surrounded by a colorful spathe.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and valuable ornamental plant.
