Alopecurus alpinus

Alopecurus alpinus, commonly known as alpine silky grass, is a perennial flowering plant native to mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, tufted stems and small, pale blue to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has fine, silky hairs on its leaves and stems, giving it a soft, fuzzy appearance. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is often found in alpine meadows and grasslands.

Alopecurus alpinus is adapted to cold climates and can tolerate harsh environmental conditions.
