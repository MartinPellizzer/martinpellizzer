Alchemilla bungei

Alchemilla bungei, commonly known as the Bunge's lady's mantle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its basal rosette of heart-shaped, silver-green leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in summer, which are clustered in loose inflorescences. Alchemilla bungei thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas or along stream banks.

Its foliage is known for its ornamental value and has been used traditionally in herbal medicine.
