Acer willkommii

Acer willkommii, commonly known as the Willkomm maple, is a deciduous tree native to the mountainous regions of China.

It is characterized by its vibrant autumn foliage, which displays shades of red, orange, and yellow. The tree has palmately lobed leaves that are typically 5 to 10 centimeters in length. It produces small, red samaras that are dispersed by wind.

Acer willkommii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental qualities and adaptability to various soil conditions.
