Anthurium cascajalense

Anthurium cascajalense, commonly known as the Cascajal anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves and vibrant spadix inflorescence. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often found in the understory of tropical forests. Its foliage is thick and glossy, with a deep green color that enhances its ornamental value.

Anthurium cascajalense is appreciated for its unique appearance and is cultivated as a decorative plant in warm climates.
