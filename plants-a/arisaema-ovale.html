Arisaema ovale

Arisaema ovale, commonly known as the Chinese arrowhead, is a flowering plant native to Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its distinctive arrow-shaped leaves. The plant produces an inflorescence consisting of a spadix surrounded by a modified leaf called a spathe. Arisaema ovale is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has traditional uses in herbal medicine.

Its growth habit includes a tuberous root system that helps it survive in various soil conditions.
