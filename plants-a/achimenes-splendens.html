Achimenes splendens

Achimenes splendens, commonly known as the flame flower, is a tropical perennial plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular, brightly colored flowers that come in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant has heart-shaped, glossy leaves and produces showy blooms throughout the growing season. It thrives in warm, humid conditions and is often cultivated as a decorative plant in gardens and greenhouses.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for floral arrangements and ornamental horticulture.
