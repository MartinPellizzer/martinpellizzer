Aloe jucunda

Aloe jucunda, commonly known as the narrow-leaf aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, linear leaves that are typically green with a slight bluish tint. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers in clusters during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves.

Aloe jucunda is often used in traditional medicine and as an ornamental plant due to its hardy nature.
