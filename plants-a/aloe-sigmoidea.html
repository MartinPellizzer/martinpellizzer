Aloe sigmoidea

Aloe sigmoidea, commonly known as the zigzag aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a distinctive, branching stem with a zigzag pattern, giving it its common name. The plant produces narrow, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a blueish tint. It blooms with yellowish-green flowers that appear at the tips of the stems.

Aloe sigmoidea is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
