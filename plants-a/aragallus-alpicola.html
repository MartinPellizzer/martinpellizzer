Aragallus alpicola

Aragallus alpicola, commonly known as the alpine aragallus, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

The plant thrives in cold, moist environments and is often found in alpine meadows and rocky slopes. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to anchor itself in nutrient-poor soils, and it has adapted to withstand harsh climatic conditions.
