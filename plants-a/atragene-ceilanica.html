Atragene ceilanica

Atragene ceilanica, commonly known as the Sri Lankan star jasmine, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Sri Lanka.

It belongs to the Oleaceae family and is characterized by its fragrant, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in clusters. Atragene ceilanica is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its pleasant fragrance and visual appeal.
