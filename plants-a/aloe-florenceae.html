Aloe florenceae

Aloe florenceae, commonly known as the Florence aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a yellowish or reddish tinge. The plant produces tall, branching stems with clusters of yellow flowers during the summer months. Its leaves contain a gel-like substance that has been used for its soothing properties.

Aloe florenceae is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
