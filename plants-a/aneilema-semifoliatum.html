Aneilema semifoliatum

Aneilema semifoliatum, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves and showy, tubular flowers. Aneilema semifoliatum is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
