Acinodendron phaetrichum

Acinodendron phaetrichum, commonly known as the golden mist orchid, is a rare and exotic flowering plant native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, golden-yellow inflorescences that resemble delicate mist. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters, often in the early morning. Its leaves are lance-shaped and glossy, adapted to thrive in the humid, shaded environments of its natural habitat.

Due to its rarity and unique appearance, it is highly sought after by botanists and orchid enthusiasts.
