Alstroemeria hookeri

Alstroemeria hookeri, commonly known as the Hooker's alstroemeria, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the family Alstroemeriaceae and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant typically grows in a compact, bushy form with narrow, strap-like leaves. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and floral arrangements.

Alstroemeria hookeri prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a versatile choice for temperate climates.
