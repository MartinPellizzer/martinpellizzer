Achillea ageratifolia f. misicii

Achillea ageratifolia f. misicii, commonly known as the Misicii yarrow, is a horticultural form of the yarrow plant.

It is characterized by its finely divided, feathery leaves and clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers. This cultivar is prized for its ornamental value and is often used in garden landscapes for its delicate appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, exhibiting drought tolerance once established.

The plant is also noted for its aromatic foliage, which can be used in herbal remedies.
