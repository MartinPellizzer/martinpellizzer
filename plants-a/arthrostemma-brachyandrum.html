Arthrostemma brachyandrum

Arthrostemma brachyandrum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved arthrostemma, is a herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Arthrostemma brachyandrum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
