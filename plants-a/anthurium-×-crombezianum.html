Anthurium × crombezianum

Anthurium × crombezianum, commonly known as the Crimson Velvet Anthurium, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Araceae family.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety red spathes that enclose a bright yellow spadix, creating a dramatic floral display. The plant originates from hybridization between Anthurium andraeanum and Anthurium scherzerianum, resulting in a unique combination of traits. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and floral arrangements.
