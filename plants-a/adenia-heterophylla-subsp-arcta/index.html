Adenia Heterophylla Subsp. Arcta

Adenia heterophylla subsp. arcta is a striking member of the passionflower family known for its unique foliage and vibrant flowers. This plant is native to the tropical regions of Africa, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are large, heart-shaped, and often display a deep green with contrasting veins. The flowers, which bloom in spring, feature intricate patterns and a strong, sweet fragrance. Due to its ornamental value and rarity, it is highly sought after by plant enthusiasts and conservationists alike.
