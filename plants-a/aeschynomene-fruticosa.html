Aeschynomene fruticosa

Aeschynomene fruticosa, commonly known as partridge pea, is a shrub native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is often found in disturbed soils and is adapted to dry, sandy environments. It has small, oblong seeds that are dispersed by wind and animals.

Aeschynomene fruticosa is also known for its nitrogen-fixing ability, making it valuable in ecological restoration projects.
