Agathisanthemum bojeri var. linearifolia

Agathisanthemum bojeri var. linearifolia, commonly known as the linear-leaf agathisanthemum, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its ornamental value lies in its attractive foliage and delicate floral display.
