Artemisia occidentalisichuanensis

Artemisia occidentalisichuanensis, commonly known as Chinese wormwood, is a perennial shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its aromatic, silver-gray foliage and small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant thrives in dry, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes many flowering plants with composite flower heads.

Artemisia occidentalisichuanensis is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional Chinese medicine.
