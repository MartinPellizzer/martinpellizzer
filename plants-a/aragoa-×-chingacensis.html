Aragoa × chingacensis

Aragoa × chingacensis, commonly known as the Chinese aragoa, is a hybrid plant resulting from the cross between Aragoa sinensis and Aragoa chingacensis.

It is a shrub native to China, characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions. Its leaves are leathery and dark green, while its flowers range in color from white to pale pink.

Aragoa × chingacensis is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various soil types.
