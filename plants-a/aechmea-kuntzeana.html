Aechmea kuntzeana

Aechmea kuntzeana, commonly known as the Kuntze's bromeliad, is a species of flowering plant in the Bromeliaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil and Peru. The plant features a rosette of grass-like leaves that form a central cup, which collects water and nutrients. Its flowers are typically pink or red, emerging from the center of the leaf rosette.

Aechmea kuntzeana is appreciated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in shaded environments.
