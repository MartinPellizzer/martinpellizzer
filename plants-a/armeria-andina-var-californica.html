Armeria andina var. californica

Armeria andina var. californica, commonly known as the California sand verbena, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in coastal and inland sandy soils. The plant features slender, upright stems and produces clusters of small, pink to purple flowers. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often covered in fine hairs.

It is well adapted to arid conditions and is often found in coastal dunes and rocky habitats.
