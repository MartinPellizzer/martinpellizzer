Agave americana striata

Agave americana striata, commonly known as the Zebra Agave, is a striking succulent native to Mexico.

It features a rosette of thick, fleshy leaves with distinctive white to yellow stripes along the edges. The leaves are blue-green in color and can grow up to 2 meters in height. It produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow flowers that bloom in the spring.

This plant is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it a popular choice for xeriscaping.
