Asparagus recurvispinus

Asparagus recurvispinus, commonly known as the curly spined asparagus, is a shrub-like plant native to South Africa.

It features wiry, spiny stems that are often covered in a fine, white, powdery coating. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in branched inflorescences. Its leaves are typically narrow and slightly curled, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Asparagus recurvispinus is adapted to arid environments and thrives in well-drained soils.
