Astracantha hilaris

Astracantha hilaris, commonly known as the African starflower, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Acanthaceae and is characterized by its showy, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to purple. Astracantha hilaris is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
