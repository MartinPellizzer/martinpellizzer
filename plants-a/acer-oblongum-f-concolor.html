Acer oblongum f. concolor

Acer oblongum f. concolor, commonly known as the white-flowered maple, is a unique variant of the oblong maple.

It is characterized by its striking white flowers that contrast with its dark green foliage. The plant is native to the mountainous regions of eastern Asia, particularly China. Its leaves are typically oblong and have a smooth margin, while the bark is smooth and light grey.

This ornamental tree is valued for its aesthetic appeal and is often planted in gardens for its seasonal display.
