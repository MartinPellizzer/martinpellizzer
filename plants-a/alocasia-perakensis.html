Alocasia perakensis

Alocasia perakensis, commonly known as the Perak arrowleaf, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 inches in length. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit and produces striking, spadix-like inflorescences. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Alocasia perakensis is popular as a ornamental plant due to its unique foliage and aesthetic appeal.
