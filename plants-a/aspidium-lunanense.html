Aspidium lunanense

Aspidium lunanense, commonly known as the Lunan water fern, is a species of fern native to China.

It belongs to the family Athyriaceae and is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. Its reproductive structures, including sori, are located on the underside of its leaves.

Aspidium lunanense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
