Aloe torrei

Aloe torrei, commonly known as the Cape aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow flowers during the summer months. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their healing properties.

Aloe torrei is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in dry climates.
