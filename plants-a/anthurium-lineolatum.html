Anthurium lineolatum

Anthurium lineolatum, commonly known as the red-tendril anthurium, is a tropical flowering plant native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves with a deep green color and red veins, which are often adorned with intricate patterns. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-shaped inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, typically in shades of red or orange. It thrives in humid, warm environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium lineolatum is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and ability to purify indoor air.
