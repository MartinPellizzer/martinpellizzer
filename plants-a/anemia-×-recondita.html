Anemia × recondita

Anemia × recondita, commonly known as the hybrid anemia plant, is a botanical hybrid resulting from the cross between two species of the Anemia genus.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety foliage with deep green to purple hues, often featuring intricate patterns and variegation. The plant typically grows as a compact shrub, with dense, bushy growth that makes it suitable for container gardening or as a decorative indoor plant. Its leaves are aromatic, emitting a subtle, spicy scent when crushed, and it thrives in well-drained soil with indirect sunlight.

Due to its ornamental value and unique appearance, it is popular among plant enthusiasts and collectors.
