Angiopteris omeiensis

Angiopteris omeiensis, commonly known as the Omei fern, is a species of tree fern native to China.

It belongs to the family Angiopteridaceae and is characterized by its large, leathery fronds that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant has a robust rhizome and produces sporangia on the underside of its fronds, typical of ferns. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

The Omei fern is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its aesthetic appeal.
