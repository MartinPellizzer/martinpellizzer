Astragalus daenensis

Astragalus daenensis, commonly known as the Daen milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to Iran.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments, adapting to harsh conditions with deep root systems. It plays a role in soil stabilization and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.

Its ecological significance is notable in its native habitat.
