Aeschynomene herbacea

Aeschynomene herbacea, commonly known as partridge pea, is an annual leguminous plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of climates. It has a deep root system that helps in soil stabilization and nitrogen fixation.

Aeschynomene herbacea is also valued for its use in forage and as a cover crop in agricultural settings.
