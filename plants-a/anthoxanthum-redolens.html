Anthoxanthum redolens

Anthoxanthum redolens, commonly known as the sweet vernal grass, is a perennial grass species native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its bright yellow inflorescence, which gives it a striking appearance during the flowering season. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and grasslands. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a soft, slightly hairy texture.

Anthoxanthum redolens is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting pollinators.
