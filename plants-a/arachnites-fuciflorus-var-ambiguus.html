Arachnites fuciflorus var. ambiguus

Arachnites fuciflorus var. ambiguus, commonly known as the ambiguous mossy orchid, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, typically found in forest undergrowth. Its leaves are elongated and covered in a fine, moss-like coating, contributing to its unique appearance.
