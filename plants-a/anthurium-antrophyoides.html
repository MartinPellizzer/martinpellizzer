Anthurium antrophyoides

Anthurium antrophyoides, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a striking tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a reddish or purple tint along the veins. The plant produces clusters of bright red spadices, which are actually inflorescences rather than true flowers. Anthurium antrophyoides thrives in humid, warm environments and requires indirect light to flourish.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and ornamental purposes.
