Acacia salazarii

Acacia salazarii, commonly known as the salazarii acacia, is a shrub or small tree native to the arid regions of Africa and the Middle East.

It is characterized by its dense, thorned foliage and yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments with minimal water availability. Its leaves are small and often have a waxy coating to reduce water loss.

Acacia salazarii is also valued for its hard, durable wood and has been used in traditional medicine.
