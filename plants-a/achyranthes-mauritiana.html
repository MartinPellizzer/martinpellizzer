Achyranthes mauritiana

Achyranthes mauritiana, commonly known as the Mauritius bedstraw, is a flowering plant native to the island of Mauritius.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the wet season. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often hairy, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Achyranthes mauritiana plays a role in local ecosystems and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
