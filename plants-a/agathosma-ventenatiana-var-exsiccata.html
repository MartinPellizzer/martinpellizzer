Agathosma ventenatiana var. exsiccata

Agathosma ventenatiana var. exsiccata, commonly known as Cape garlic.

It is a perennial shrub native to South Africa, characterized by its aromatic, narrow leaves and small yellow flowers. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments. It produces small, oval-shaped fruits that contain seeds.

This variety is often used in traditional medicine and is valued for its essential oils.
