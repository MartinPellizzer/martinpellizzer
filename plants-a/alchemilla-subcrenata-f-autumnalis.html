Alchemilla subcrenata f. autumnalis

Alchemilla subcrenata f. autumnalis, commonly known as autumnal lady's mantle, is a perennial plant native to Europe.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its basal rosette of lanceolate, hairy leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers in late summer to early autumn. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Its ornamental value comes from its attractive foliage and prolonged flowering period.
