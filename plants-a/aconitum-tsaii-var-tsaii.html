Aconitum tsaii var. tsaii

Aconitum tsaii var. tsaii, commonly known as the Tsai's monkshood, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to China.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to purple spurred flowers. The plant features palmate leaves with deeply lobed divisions, and its stems are typically upright and hairy. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Due to its toxic alkaloids, it should be handled with care.
