Anthurium boudetii

Anthurium boudetii, commonly known as the Boudet's anthurium, is a striking tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are dark green with prominent veins, and its vibrant red spadix surrounded by a showy, spatulate spathe. The plant thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect light to flourish. It is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and unique inflorescence.

Anthurium boudetii is also valued in horticulture for its ability to purify indoor air.
