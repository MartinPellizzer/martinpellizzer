Amaryllis josephiniae var. griffithiana

Amaryllis josephiniae var. griffithiana, commonly known as the Griffith's amaryllis, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, which bloom in late summer to early autumn. The plant has strap-like, grassy leaves that emerge from a bulb and are often used for propagation. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

This variety is prized for its vibrant blooms and is a popular choice for gardeners seeking colorful, seasonal ornamental plants.
