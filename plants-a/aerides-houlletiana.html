Aerides houlletiana

Aerides houlletiana, commonly known as the Houllet's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers with a distinctive lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil.

Aerides houlletiana is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
