Albizia kalkora var. coreana

Albizia kalkora var. coreana, commonly known as the Korean albizia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to Korea.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant features pinnately compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets. It produces small, fragrant white or pale yellow flowers in clusters, typically blooming in spring.

Albizia kalkora var. coreana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in horticultural landscapes.
