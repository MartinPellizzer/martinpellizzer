Aerides radicosa

Aerides radicosa, commonly known as the root orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit and produces delicate, greenish-yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and along stream banks. Its roots are fibrous and play a crucial role in anchoring the plant and absorbing nutrients.

Aerides radicosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
