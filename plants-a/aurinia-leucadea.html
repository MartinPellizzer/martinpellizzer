Aurinia leucadea

Aurinia leucadea, commonly known as white aurinia, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the family Brassicaceae, which includes many flowering plants with small, four-petaled flowers. The plant features dense, woolly foliage that helps it retain moisture in its arid habitat. Its flowers are typically white or pale yellow, appearing in clusters during the summer months.
