Anthurium vomeriforme

Anthurium vomeriforme, commonly known as the vomeriform anthurium, is a tropical flowering plant native to Central America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are often brightly colored with a glossy texture. The plant produces vibrant spadices, which are specialized inflorescences that resemble a spadix, typical of the Araceae family. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.

Anthurium vomeriforme is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
