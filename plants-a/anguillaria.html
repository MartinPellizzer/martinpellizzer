Anguillaria

Anguillaria, commonly known as the angel's trumpet, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Apocynaceae.

It is characterized by its large, showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. The plant is native to South America and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. Anguillaria has opposite, simple, and usually hairy leaves that are broad and ovate.

It produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind.
