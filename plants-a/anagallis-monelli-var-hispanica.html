Anagallis monelli var. hispanica

Anagallis monelli var. hispanica, commonly known as Spanish honesty, is a flowering plant native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It produces delicate, white flowers with a subtle pinkish hue, often appearing in clusters along the stems. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders. Its flowers open in the morning and close by midday, a behavior known as nyctinasty.

The species is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its unique appearance.
