Alifana

Alifana, commonly known as the mountain holly, is a shrub native to the Canary Islands.

It features small, glossy, dark green leaves that are oval in shape. The plant produces white flowers with five petals, which bloom in the spring. Its fruit is a small, round berry that turns red when mature.

Alifana is adapted to grow in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas.
