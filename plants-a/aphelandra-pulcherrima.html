Aphelandra pulcherrima

Aphelandra pulcherrima, commonly known as the golden flame plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Central and South America.

Aphelandra pulcherrima is popular as a ornamental plant due to its vibrant appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
