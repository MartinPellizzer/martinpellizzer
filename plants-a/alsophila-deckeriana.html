Alsophila deckeriana

Alsophila deckeriana, commonly known as the Dekerian hakea, is a species of tree fern native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the family Hymenophyllaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feathery fronds. The plant has a distinctive crown of sterile fronds that form a dense, umbrella-like shape. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in coastal and forested areas.

The fronds are typically dark green and have a leathery texture, contributing to its ornamental appeal.
