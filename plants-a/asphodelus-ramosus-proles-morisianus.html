Asphodelus ramosus proles morisianus

Asphodelus ramosus proles morisianus, commonly known as the Morisian asphodel, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its grass-like foliage and tall, spike-like inflorescences. The Morisian asphodel is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
