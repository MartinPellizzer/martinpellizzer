Amaranthus spathulatus

Amaranthus spathulatus, commonly known as broadleaf amaranth, is a flowering plant in the amaranth family, Amaranthaceae.

It is characterized by its broad, arrowhead-shaped leaves and vibrant, showy inflorescences that range from red to orange. Native to the Americas, it is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of climates. The plant is known for its rapid growth and is sometimes cultivated as a ornamental or for its potential as a leaf vegetable.

Its seeds are also used in some traditional cuisines.
