Antirrhinum filiforme

Antirrhinum filiforme, commonly known as the slender snapdragon, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It produces vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are narrow and linear, contributing to its overall slender appearance.

Antirrhinum filiforme is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and relatively low maintenance requirements.
