Alocasia celebica

Alocasia celebica, commonly known as the Celebes arrowroot, is a striking tropical plant native to Indonesia.

It features large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length, with a dark green to almost black coloration. The plant produces small, white flowers that are typically hidden beneath its broad foliage. Alocasia celebica is a member of the Araceae family, characterized by its unique inflorescence structure.

It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its dramatic foliage.
