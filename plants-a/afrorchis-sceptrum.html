Afrorchis sceptrum

Afrorchis sceptrum, commonly known as the African orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically feature a deep purple or violet coloration. The plant produces a single, erect stem that bears a cluster of flowers, each with a distinctive lip that is often adorned with intricate patterns. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade in its natural habitat.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts for its unique and exotic appearance.
