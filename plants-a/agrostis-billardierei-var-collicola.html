Agrostis billardierei var. collicola

Agrostis billardierei var. collicola, commonly known as the mountain browntop, is a grass species native to Australia.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically bluish-green in color. The plant produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences that are often clustered at the base of the stems. It thrives in well-drained soils and is commonly found in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky outcrops.

This variety is notable for its resilience to environmental stress and is often used in ecological restoration projects.
