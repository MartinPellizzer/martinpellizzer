Abacopteris lineata

Abacopteris lineata, commonly known as the lined fern, is a species of fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its delicate, feather-like fronds that are typically divided into linear segments. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories. Its reproductive structures, including sporangia, are located on the underside of the fronds.

The lined fern is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
