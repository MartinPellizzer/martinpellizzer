Aeschynanthus bracteatus var. peelii

Aeschynanthus bracteatus var. peelii, commonly known as the Peeli's ivy leaf.

This evergreen shrub is native to tropical regions of Africa and thrives in humid, shaded environments. It is characterized by its trailing stems and small, glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces clusters of tubular, fragrant flowers in shades of red and orange, which bloom throughout the year.

It is often cultivated as a decorative plant for hanging baskets or as a ground cover in shaded gardens.
