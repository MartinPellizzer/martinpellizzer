Aira vallesiana

Aira vallesiana, commonly known as the valley aira, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the grass family, Poaceae, and is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny, white to pale blue flowers. It typically thrives in moist, well-drained soils in temperate climates.
