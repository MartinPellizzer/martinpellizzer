Arisaema thunbergii var. monostachyum

Arisaema thunbergii var. monostachyum, commonly known as the Japanese threadleaf arrowhead, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to East Asia.

It features a rhizomatous root system and produces distinctive, palmately lobed leaves that are often heart-shaped. The plant is characterized by its unique inflorescence, which consists of a spadix enclosed by a modified leaf called a spathe. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation in suitable climates.
