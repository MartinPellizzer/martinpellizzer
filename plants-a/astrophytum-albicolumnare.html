Astrophytum albicolumnare

Astrophytum albicolumnare, commonly known as the White Column Cactus, is a species native to the Chihuahuan Desert in Mexico.

It is a low-growing, spineless cactus with a distinctive, columnar shape and a grayish-green stem that often develops a whitish coating. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months, which bloom at the top of the stems. It is adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water, making it a popular choice for desert gardens.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a striking addition to xeriscapes.
