Anthurium friedrichii

Anthurium friedrichii, commonly known as the Friedrich's anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Colombia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves and vibrant spadix. The plant produces a unique inflorescence consisting of a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium friedrichii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated as a houseplant.
