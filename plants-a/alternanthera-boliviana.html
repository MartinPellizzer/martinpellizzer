Alternanthera boliviana

Alternanthera boliviana, commonly known as the Bolivian Alternanthera, is a striking aquatic plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red and orange foliage, which becomes more intense under strong light conditions. This species thrives in shallow, slow-moving waters and prefers nutrient-rich substrates. It is often used in aquariums for its ornamental value and ability to provide habitat for small aquatic life.

The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged oppositely along the stems.
