Arenaria smithiana

Arenaria smithiana, commonly known as Smith's sandwort, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically forms dense mats of foliage. The plant produces small, white or pinkish flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained, rocky or sandy soils and is often found in alpine or subalpine habitats.

Its botanical features include narrow, linear leaves and a compact growth habit adapted to harsh environmental conditions.
