Aechmea paniculata

Aechmea paniculata, commonly known as the pineapple bromeliad, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that form a central cup to collect water. The plant produces striking inflorescences with vibrant red or orange flowers, which are followed by small, edible berries. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Its unique structure and aesthetic appeal make it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
