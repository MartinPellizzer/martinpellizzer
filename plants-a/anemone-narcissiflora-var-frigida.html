Anemone narcissiflora var. frigida

Anemone narcissiflora var. frigida, commonly known as the Arctic anemone, is a perennial plant native to the Arctic and subarctic regions.

It features showy, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has a compact, low-growing habit with hairy, dark green foliage. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.

Its botanical characteristics make it a hardy and attractive addition to alpine gardens.
