Alsophila nigrescens

Alsophila nigrescens, commonly known as the black-stemmed tree fern, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a member of the Cyatheaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate fronds. The plant has a distinctive dark brown or black stem that becomes more pronounced with age. Its fronds are typically bright green and can grow up to several meters in length.

Alsophila nigrescens is often found in humid, shaded environments such as tropical rainforests.
