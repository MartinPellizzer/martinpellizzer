Anerincleistus collinus

Anerincleistus collinus, commonly known as the golden cup fern, is a tropical plant belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its compact, rosette-like growth habit and is native to regions of Southeast Asia. The plant features delicate, bright yellow to golden-green fronds that are often covered in fine, silky hairs. Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated as a decorative plant in tropical gardens and greenhouses.
