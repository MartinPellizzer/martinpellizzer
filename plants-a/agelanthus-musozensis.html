Agelanthus musozensis

Agelanthus musozensis, commonly known as the Musozense cycad, is a rare and ancient plant species native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Cycadaceae family and is characterized by its stout, unbranched trunk and large, pinnate leaves. The plant produces cones rather than flowers, a feature typical of cycads, and it has a slow growth rate.

Due to habitat loss and limited distribution, Agelanthus musozensis is considered endangered, highlighting the need for conservation efforts to protect this unique botanical species.
