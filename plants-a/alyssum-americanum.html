Alyssum americanum

Alyssum americanum, commonly known as American alyssum, is a low-growing annual plant native to North America.

It features small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in the spring and early summer. The plant has delicate, branching stems and narrow, linear leaves that are covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, often found in disturbed areas such as roadsides and fields.

Alyssum americanum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in native plant gardens.
