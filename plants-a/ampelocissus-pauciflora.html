Ampelocissus pauciflora

Ampelocissus pauciflora, commonly known as the slender cat's claw, is a climbing plant native to southern Africa.

It features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are often tinged with red or purple on the underside. The plant produces small, white to pale green flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its fruits are spherical, fleshy berries that turn yellow when mature.

Ampelocissus pauciflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in horticulture for its climbing habit and foliage.
