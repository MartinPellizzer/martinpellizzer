Aloe diolii

Aloe diolii, commonly known as the narrow-leaved aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically narrow and upright, with a bluish-green coloration. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers on branched inflorescences during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its leaves to survive dry conditions.

Aloe diolii is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and low maintenance requirements.
