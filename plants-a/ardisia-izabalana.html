Ardisia izabalana

Ardisia izabalana, commonly known as the Izabal beautyberry, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Central America.

It belongs to the family Ericaceae and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of small, white flowers. The plant produces bright red, berry-like fruits that are an important food source for various bird species. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in tropical regions.
