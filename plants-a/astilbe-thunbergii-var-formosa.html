Astilbe thunbergii var. formosa

Astilbe thunbergii var. formosa, commonly known as the Japanese feather fern, is a deciduous perennial shrub native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its upright, branching stems that bear clusters of small, fluffy, pinkish-white flowers. The plant produces delicate, fern-like leaves that are often variegated with shades of green and cream. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to add texture and color to woodland gardens.
