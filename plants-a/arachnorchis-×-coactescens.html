Arachnorchis × coactescens

Arachnorchis × coactescens, commonly known as the hybrid spider orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to Australia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, spider-like floral structure. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as rainforests or under dense vegetation. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish in color, with intricate patterns that aid in pollination by specific insect species.

Arachnorchis × coactescens is valued for its ornamental appeal and plays a role in the ecological diversity of its native habitats.
