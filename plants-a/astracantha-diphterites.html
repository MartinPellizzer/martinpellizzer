Astracantha diphterites

Astracantha diphterites, commonly known as the horned milkweed, is a species of flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, spiny seed pods that resemble horns, giving it its common name. The plant produces showy, yellow flowers that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its leaves are opposite and oval-shaped, with a slightly hairy texture.

Astracantha diphterites is adapted to arid environments and has a deep root system to access water from deeper soil layers.
