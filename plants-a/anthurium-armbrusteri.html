Anthurium armbrusteri

Anthurium armbrusteri, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a tropical flowering plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves and vibrant red spadix. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers surrounded by a colorful spathe, which is often the feature that attracts attention. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium armbrusteri is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
