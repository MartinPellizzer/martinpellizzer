Aster ruber

Aster ruber, commonly known as the red aster, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the sunflower family (Asteraceae) and is characterized by its vibrant red to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in open habitats such as meadows and woodlands, thriving in full sun. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are erect and branched.

Aster ruber is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens for its showy blooms.
