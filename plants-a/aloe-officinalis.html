Aloe officinalis

Aloe officinalis, commonly known as the medicinal aloe, is a succulent plant native to the Arabian Peninsula.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces a gel-like substance in its leaves, which is widely used for its soothing and healing properties. It belongs to the Asphodelaceae family and is closely related to other aloe species.

Aloe officinalis is valued both for its ornamental beauty and its medicinal applications.
