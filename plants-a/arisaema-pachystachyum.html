Arisaema pachystachyum

Arisaema pachystachyum, commonly known as the giant arrowhead, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length. The plant produces a unique spadix inflorescence surrounded by a broad, waxy spathe, which is a common feature in the Araceae family. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in humid tropical forests.

The botanical features of Arisaema pachystachyum make it a visually impressive and ecologically significant species in its native habitat.
