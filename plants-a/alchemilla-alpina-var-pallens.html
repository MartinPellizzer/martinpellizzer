Alchemilla alpina var. pallens

Alchemilla alpina var. pallens, commonly known as the alpine lady's mantle, is a perennial plant native to alpine regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its dense, silver-green foliage that forms a low-growing mat. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in early summer, which are clustered in rounded inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and full sun to partial shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to tolerate harsh environmental conditions.
