Anthurium aristatum

Anthurium aristatum, commonly known as the spathiflorum anthurium, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a glossy texture. The plant produces a unique inflorescence consisting of a spadix surrounded by a showy, colorful spathe. Its flowers are primarily pollinated by insects, and it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Anthurium aristatum is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
