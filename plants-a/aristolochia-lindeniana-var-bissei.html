Aristolochia lindeniana var. bissei

Aristolochia lindeniana var. bissei, commonly known as the slender Dutchman's pipe, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive, pipe-shaped flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color. The plant produces heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and a strong, sometimes unpleasant odor. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in the understory of tropical forests.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
