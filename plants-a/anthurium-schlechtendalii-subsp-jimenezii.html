Anthurium schlechtendalii subsp. jimenezii

Anthurium schlechtendalii subsp.

jimenezii, commonly known as the Jimenez's anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central America. It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped foliage with a glossy texture.

The plant produces striking spathes that range in color from deep red to orange, often with a contrasting veining. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect sunlight for optimal growth.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical landscapes.
