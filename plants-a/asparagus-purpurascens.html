Asparagus purpurascens

Asparagus purpurascens, commonly known as the purple asparagus, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its striking purple foliage and white flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. Its ornamental value comes from its vibrant coloration and unique leaf structure.

It is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal and drought tolerance.
