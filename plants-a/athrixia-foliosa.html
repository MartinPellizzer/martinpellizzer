Athrixia foliosa

Athrixia foliosa, commonly known as the velvetleaf or velvet fern, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit and its distinctive, velvety, dark green foliage. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Athrixia foliosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
