Anthurium nanegalense

Anthurium nanegalense, commonly known as the Nanegalense Arrowhead, is a species of flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family, which is characterized by its unique inflorescence known as a spadix, surrounded by a colorful spathe. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a glossy texture and vibrant green coloration. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Anthurium nanegalense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in tropical gardens.
