Agathosma adenandriflora

Agathosma adenandriflora, commonly known as the African geranium, is a small evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It features narrow, leathery leaves that are often aromatic and covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, pink to purple flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is also cultivated for use in aromatherapy and traditional medicine.
