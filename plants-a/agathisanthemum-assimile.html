Agathisanthemum assimile

Agathisanthemum assimile, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies, sunflowers, and other composite flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Agathisanthemum assimile is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
