Achimenes × naegelioides

Achimenes × naegelioides, commonly known as the hybrid begonia, is a tropical perennial plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the family Begoniaceae and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, pink, and white. The plant features heart-shaped, velvety leaves and produces tubular flowers that attract pollinators such as hummingbirds and bees.

It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
