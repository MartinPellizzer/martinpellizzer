Astragalus biserrula

Astragalus biserrula, commonly known as goat's horn vetch, is a flowering plant in the legume family Fabaceae.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant is known for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it beneficial for soil enrichment. It thrives in dry, rocky habitats and is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value.

The seeds of Astragalus biserrula are also used in traditional medicine in some regions.
