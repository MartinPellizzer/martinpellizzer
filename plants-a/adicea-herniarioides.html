Adicea herniarioides

Adicea herniarioides, commonly known as the sticky weed or sand mat, is a low-growing perennial plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of southern Africa.

It has a mat-forming habit with small, fleshy, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine, sticky hairs. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color. Its root system is extensive and helps it thrive in sandy or gravelly soils.

Adicea herniarioides is well adapted to drought conditions and can often be found in disturbed or dry habitats.
