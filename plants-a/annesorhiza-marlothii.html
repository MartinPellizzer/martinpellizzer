Annesorhiza marlothii

Annesorhiza marlothii, commonly known as the Marloth's milkbush, is a perennial shrub native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its succulent, branched stems and small, clustered leaves. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers that are typically arranged in dense, spike-like inflorescences. It is well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, often growing in sandy or rocky soils.

Annesorhiza marlothii is valued for its drought tolerance and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
