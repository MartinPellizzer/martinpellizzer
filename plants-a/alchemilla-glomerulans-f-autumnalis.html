Alchemilla glomerulans f. autumnalis

Alchemilla glomerulans f. autumnalis, commonly known as autumn alchemilla, is a compact, low-growing perennial plant native to Europe.

It features dense clusters of small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. The plant has silvery-gray, hairy leaves that are slightly succulent, contributing to its drought tolerance. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
