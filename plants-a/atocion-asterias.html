Atocion asterias

Atocion asterias, commonly known as the star aster, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its daisy-like appearance, featuring small white petals surrounding a yellow central disk. Native to South America, this plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade. Its flowers bloom in the spring and summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

The plant is often used in ornamental gardening for its aesthetic appeal and hardy nature.
