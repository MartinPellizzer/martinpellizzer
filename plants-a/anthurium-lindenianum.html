Anthurium lindenianum

Anthurium lindenianum, commonly known as the red-tailed anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically deep green with a reddish or purple tinge on the underside. The plant produces vibrant spadices, which are the showy, leaf-like structures that enclose the true flowers. It thrives in humid, warm environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Anthurium lindenianum is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
