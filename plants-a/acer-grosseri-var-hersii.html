Acer grosseri var. hersii

Acer grosseri var. hersii, commonly known as the Himalayan maple, is a deciduous tree native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its distinctive palmate leaves that turn vibrant shades of red and yellow in the fall. The plant thrives in cool, moist climates and is often found in mountainous regions with well-drained soils. Its small, yellowish-green flowers appear in early spring before the leaves emerge.

The tree is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its striking seasonal display.
