Alsodeia dasyprysis

Alsodeia dasyprysis, commonly known as the woolly alsodeia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils. The plant features hairy, branched stems and small, pale blue or purple flowers. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine, white hairs.

It is often found in open, sunny habitats and is adapted to dry conditions.
