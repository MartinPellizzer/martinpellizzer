Avena sterilis var. biaristata

Avena sterilis var. biaristata, commonly known as the slender oat, is a perennial grass species native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny florets. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed or open habitats.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
