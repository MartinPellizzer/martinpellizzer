Alnaster firmus var. hirtellus

Alnaster firmus var. hirtellus, commonly known as the hairy alnaster, is a shrub characterized by its dense, upright growth habit and hairy foliage.

The hairy alnaster is often used in ornamental gardening for its compact form and attractive floral display.
