Astracantha argyrothamnos

Astracantha argyrothamnos, commonly known as the silver-leafed astracantha, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and small, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

It is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
