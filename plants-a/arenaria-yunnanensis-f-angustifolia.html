Arenaria yunnanensis f. angustifolia

Arenaria yunnanensis f. angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved sandwort, is a rare alpine plant native to the mountainous regions of Yunnan Province in China.

It is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and small, white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to cold, high-altitude environments. Its foliage is often covered in a dense layer of fine hairs, which helps to reduce water loss in harsh conditions.

This fern-like species is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphology and limited distribution.
