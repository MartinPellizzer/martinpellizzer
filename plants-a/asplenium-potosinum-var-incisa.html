Asplenium potosinum var. incisa

Asplenium potosinum var. incisa, commonly known as the Potosi spleenwort, is a fern native to Mexico.

It belongs to the family Aspleniaceae and is characterized by its small, dark green, lance-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as rock crevices or forest floors. Its fronds are usually short and clustered, with a distinctive notched or incised margin.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
