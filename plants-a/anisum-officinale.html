Anisum officinale

Anisum officinale, commonly known as star anise, is a flowering plant native to eastern China, Vietnam, and Indonesia.

It is characterized by its distinctive star-shaped fruit, which contains aromatic seeds used in both culinary and medicinal applications. The plant belongs to the family Schisandraceae and is known for its essential oil, which contains compounds like anethol and shikimic acid. Its leaves are trifoliate, with serrated edges, and it produces small, greenish-yellow flowers.

Star anise is often cultivated for its medicinal properties and is also used in traditional Chinese medicine.
