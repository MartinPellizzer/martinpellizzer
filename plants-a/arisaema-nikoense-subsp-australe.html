Arisaema nikoense subsp. australe

Arisaema nikoense subsp.

australe, commonly known as the Australian arrowhead, is a herbaceous plant native to southeastern Australia. It features a distinctive arrowhead-shaped leaf that is typically bright green with a waxy texture.

The plant produces a spadix surrounded by a spathe, a characteristic feature of the Araceae family. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
