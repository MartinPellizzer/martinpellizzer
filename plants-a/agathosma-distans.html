Agathosma distans

Agathosma distans, commonly known as the Cape geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, daisy-like flowers and hairy, aromatic leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its compact growth habit and ornamental appearance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Agathosma distans is also valued for its use in traditional medicine and as a source of essential oils.
