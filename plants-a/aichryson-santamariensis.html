Aichryson santamariensis

Aichryson santamariensis, commonly known as the Santa Maria ice plant, is a succulent native to South America, specifically Argentina.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that are covered in a fine, white powdery coating, which helps reduce water loss. The plant produces small, star-shaped yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental appearance make it a popular choice for drought-tolerant landscaping.
