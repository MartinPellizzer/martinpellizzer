Avena capillacea

Avena capillacea, commonly known as slender oat, is a flowering plant species native to South America.

It is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny florets. Avena capillacea is often found in moist, temperate environments such as meadows and wetlands.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in horticulture for its delicate appearance.
