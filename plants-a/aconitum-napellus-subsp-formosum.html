Aconitum napellus subsp. formosum

Aconitum napellus subsp.

formosum, commonly known as the Japanese aconite, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to East Asia. It features showy, blue to purple spires of flowers that bloom in late summer.

The plant has dark green, heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture. It prefers moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade.

Due to its toxic alkaloids, it should be handled with care.
