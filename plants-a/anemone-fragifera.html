Anemone fragifera

Anemone fragifera, commonly known as the fragrant anemone, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is characterized by its showy, pink to purple flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has basal leaves that are heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice among gardeners seeking colorful, low-maintenance plants.
