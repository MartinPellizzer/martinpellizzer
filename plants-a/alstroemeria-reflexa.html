Alstroemeria reflexa

Alstroemeria reflexa, commonly known as the reflexed alstroemeria, is a species of flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the family Alstroemeriaceae and is characterized by its striking, tubular-shaped flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant typically grows as a herbaceous perennial, with long, strap-like leaves that are often variegated. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.

Its unique floral structure and vibrant hues make it a valuable addition to both ornamental and ecological landscapes.
