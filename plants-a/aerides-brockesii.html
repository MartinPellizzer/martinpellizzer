Aerides brockesii

Aerides brockesii, commonly known as the Brookes' orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces clusters of pale yellow to greenish-white blooms with intricate patterns. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Aerides brockesii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
