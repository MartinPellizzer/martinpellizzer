Acianthera langeana

Acianthera langeana, commonly known as the Lange's acianthera, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy flowers. The plant typically produces small, white to pale yellow blossoms that are clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated by enthusiasts, though it remains relatively rare in horticultural trade.
