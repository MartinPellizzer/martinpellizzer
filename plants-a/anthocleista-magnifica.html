Anthocleista magnifica

Anthocleista magnifica, commonly known as the African star apple, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 6 meters in height. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its fruits are fleshy, yellow or orange berries that contain a single seed.

The leaves are large, heart-shaped, and have a smooth, glossy surface.
