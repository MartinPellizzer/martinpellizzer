Arisaema sprengerianum var. dentatum

Arisaema sprengerianum var. dentatum, commonly known as the toothed arisaema, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features a distinctive, umbrella-shaped inflorescence with a spadix surrounded by a showy, leaf-like bract. The plant's foliage consists of large, heart-shaped leaves with serrated edges, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, moist soil.

This variant is prized in horticulture for its unique appearance and ability to add visual interest to shaded gardens.
