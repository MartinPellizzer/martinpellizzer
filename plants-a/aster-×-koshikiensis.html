Aster × koshikiensis

Aster × koshikiensis, commonly known as the Japanese aster, is a hybrid perennial plant native to Japan.

It is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows to a height of 30–60 cm and produces numerous flower heads on sturdy stems. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

The botanical name reflects its hybrid origin, combining species from the Aster genus.
