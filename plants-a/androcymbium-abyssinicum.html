Androcymbium abyssinicum

Androcymbium abyssinicum, commonly known as the Abyssinian orchid, is a species native to Ethiopia.

It is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique floral structure. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. It thrives in high-altitude environments and requires specific moisture and temperature conditions.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
