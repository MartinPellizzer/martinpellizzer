Artanthe chelseana

Artanthe chelseana, commonly known as Chelsea Flower Show plant, is a striking perennial belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant, daisy-like flowers that range in color from white to deep purple, depending on the cultivar. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically bluish-green in hue. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained, loamy soil.

Artanthe chelseana is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and public landscapes.
