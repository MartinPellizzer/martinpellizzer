Achyronia Ericifolia

Achyronia ericifolia is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of South America. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in rocky or mountainous terrain. This plant is known for its small, clustered flowers that bloom in shades of white and pale pink. Its leaves are covered in fine hairs, giving it a soft, woolly appearance. Due to its limited habitat and fragile nature, Achyronia ericifolia is considered a species of conservation concern.
