Armeriastrum truncatum

Armeriastrum truncatum, commonly known as the narrowleaf sea holly, is a flowering plant native to the coastal areas of California and Baja California.

It is a low-growing, mat-forming perennial that thrives in sandy or gravelly soils. The plant features small, white to pale blue flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are narrow and often have a slightly wavy margin.

It is well adapted to coastal conditions, tolerating salt spray and strong winds.
