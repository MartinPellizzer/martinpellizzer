Astranthium condimentum

Astranthium condimentum, commonly known as the spice thistle, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant produces small, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are spiny and often used as a decorative element in gardens.

While not widely cultivated for food, it has historical uses in herbal medicine and as a spice in certain traditional cuisines.
