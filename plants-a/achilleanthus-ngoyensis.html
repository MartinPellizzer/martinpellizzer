Achilleanthus ngoyensis

Achilleanthus ngoyensis, commonly known as the Ngoyensis Achilleanthus, is a flowering plant native to Central Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, daisy-like flowers in shades of yellow and white. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded forest understories.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and potentially endangered species.
