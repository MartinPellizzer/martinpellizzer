Argyrolobium trigonelloides

Argyrolobium trigonelloides, commonly known as the yellow burr vetch, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is characterized by its legume fruits and nitrogen-fixing abilities. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring, followed by distinctive triangular pods. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky soils and along coastal areas.

Its seeds are adapted for dispersal by wind, contributing to its widespread distribution.
