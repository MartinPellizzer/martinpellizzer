Argyroderma × octophyllum

Argyroderma × octophyllum, commonly known as the silverleaf tree.

It is a hybrid plant resulting from the cross between Argyroderma macrophyllum and Argyroderma trichophyllum. This species is characterized by its large, silvery-green leaves that are covered in a fine, powdery coating. It is native to South America, particularly Brazil, and thrives in tropical and subtropical climates.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its striking foliage and adaptability to various soil types.
