Alaticaulia odontopetala

Alaticaulia odontopetala, commonly known as the toothed petal tree, is a tropical plant native to Central America.

It is characterized by its distinctive, tooth-like petals that give it its scientific name. The plant typically grows as a small to medium-sized tree with a dense canopy. Its leaves are simple and alternate, often with a rough texture.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is valued for its ornamental features.
