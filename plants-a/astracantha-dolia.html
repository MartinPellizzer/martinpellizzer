Astracantha dolia

Astracantha dolia, commonly known as the woolly milkweed, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its hairy, gray-green stems and opposite, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late summer. Its latex contains milky sap, which is a common feature among the milkweed family.

Astracantha dolia is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments.
