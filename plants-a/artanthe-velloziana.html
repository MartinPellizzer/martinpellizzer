Artanthe velloziana

Artanthe velloziana, commonly known as the silverleaf artanthe, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are often covered in a fine, white powdery coating, which helps reduce water loss in arid conditions.

Artanthe velloziana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
