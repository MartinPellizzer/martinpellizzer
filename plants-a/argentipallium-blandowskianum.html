Argentipallium blandowskianum

Argentipallium blandowskianum, commonly known as the silver-pale orchid, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of southern Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stem and small, pale green to silver-colored flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in cool, moist environments. Its unique coloration and habitat make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists.

Due to its limited distribution and threats from habitat loss, it is considered endangered in its native range.
