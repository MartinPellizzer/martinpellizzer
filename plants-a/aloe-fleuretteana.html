Aloe fleuretteana

Aloe fleuretteana, commonly known as the Cape aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asphodelaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are thick and fleshy. The plant produces tall, branched flower stalks that bear small, yellowish-green flowers. Its leaves contain a gel-like substance that is often used for its medicinal properties.

Aloe fleuretteana is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water to survive.
