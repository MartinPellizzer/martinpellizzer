Anil thibaudiana

Anil thibaudiana, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to eastern Africa.

It is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves and clusters of showy, violet-colored flowers. The plant typically grows in a compact rosette form, with stems that remain close to the ground. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Anil thibaudiana is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
