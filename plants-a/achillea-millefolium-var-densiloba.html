Achillea millefolium var. densiloba

Achillea millefolium var. densiloba, commonly known as the denseleaf yarrow, is a variant of the common yarrow.

It is a perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia, characterized by its dense, dark green foliage. The plant produces flat, composite flower heads in shades of white to pale pink, typically blooming from late spring to early autumn. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy addition to gardens.

Its aromatic leaves and medicinal properties have made it historically valued in herbal remedies.
