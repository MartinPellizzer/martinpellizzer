Anisomeles moschata

Anisomeles moschata, commonly known as the musk melon or honeydew melon, is a member of the Cucurbitaceae family.

It is a trailing vine that produces large, pale green to yellowish fruits with a sweet, aromatic flesh. The plant features palmate leaves with lobed margins and tendrils for climbing. Its flowers are unisexual, with separate male and female blooms on the same plant.

Anisomeles moschata thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
