Artemisia strongylocephala var. sinensis

Artemisia strongylocephala var. sinensis, commonly known as Chinese wormwood, is a perennial herb native to China.

It is characterized by its aromatic, silver-gray foliage and small, yellowish-green flowers that appear in late summer. The plant contains volatile oils and sesquiterpene lactones, which contribute to its medicinal properties. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for cultivation in temperate climates.

Due to its chemical composition, it has been used in traditional Chinese medicine for various therapeutic purposes.
