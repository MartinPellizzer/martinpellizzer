Arctotis Aspera Var. Cuprea

Aspera cuprea, scientifically known as Arctotis aspera var. cuprea, is a striking member of the Asteraceae family native to southern Africa. This plant is characterized by its vibrant red and orange flowers that bloom in late summer and early autumn. Its fuzzy, gray-green foliage adds a unique texture to any garden or landscape. Aspera cuprea is prized for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained soils. It is a hardy plant that can tolerate drought and full sun, making it a popular choice for coastal and Mediterranean climates.
