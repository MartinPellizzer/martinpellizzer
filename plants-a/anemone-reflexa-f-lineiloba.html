Anemone reflexa f. lineiloba

Anemone reflexa f. lineiloba, commonly known as the narrow-leaved anemone, is a botanical variant of the common anemone.

It is characterized by its slender, elongated leaves and delicate, white to pale pink flowers. This plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream sides. It belongs to the family Ranunculaceae, which includes many showy flowering plants.

Its foliage and floral structure are adapted to thrive in cool, humid climates.
