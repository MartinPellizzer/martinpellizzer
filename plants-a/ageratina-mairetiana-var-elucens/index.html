Ageratina Mairetiana Var. Elucens

Ageratina mairetiana var. elucens is a rare and striking plant native to specific regions in South America. It is known for its delicate, fluffy white flowers that bloom in clusters, creating a soft and ethereal appearance. This variant of Ageratina mairetiana is particularly noted for its unique coloration and floral structure. Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a species of conservation concern. Its beauty and rarity make it a subject of interest for botanists and plant enthusiasts alike.
