Arum maculatum f. roseum

Arum maculatum f. roseum, commonly known as the pink spotted arum.

This variant is a cultivar of the common arum, characterized by its striking pinkish-red markings on the spathe. The plant features a showy, umbrella-like inflorescence with a spadix surrounded by a modified leaf called a spathe. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

The botanical interest of this plant lies in its unique coloration and its role in the Araceae family's diverse floral structures.
