Anthurium jicoteense

Anthurium jicoteense, commonly known as the Jicotea anthurium, is a species native to the tropical regions of Central America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped spathe that can range in color from deep red to orange. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its striking foliage and unique inflorescence make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Anthurium jicoteense thrives in humid, warm environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.
