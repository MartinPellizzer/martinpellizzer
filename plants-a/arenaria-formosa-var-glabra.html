Arenaria formosa var. glabra

Arenaria formosa var. glabra, commonly known as smooth sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to arctic and alpine regions.

It features dense clusters of small, white, four-petaled flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has slender, erect stems covered with smooth, glabrous leaves. It thrives in well-drained, sandy or gravelly soils and is adapted to cold climates.

This variety is often found in tundra and rocky habitats, where it plays a role in stabilizing soil and supporting local ecosystems.
