Artemisia xylorhiza

Artemisia xylorhiza, commonly known as desert sage, is a perennial shrub native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its aromatic, silver-gray foliage and small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, demonstrating remarkable drought tolerance. Its leaves contain essential oils that contribute to its strong, pungent scent.

Artemisia xylorhiza is often used in traditional medicine and as a ornamental plant in xeriscaped gardens.
