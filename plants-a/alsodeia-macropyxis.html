Alsodeia macropyxis

Alsodeia macropyxis, commonly known as the large-lobed alsodeia, is a herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, upright stems that bear alternate, pinnately compound leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable candidate for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance, it is occasionally cultivated in horticulture.
