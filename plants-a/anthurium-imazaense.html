Anthurium imazaense

Anthurium imazaense, commonly known as the Imazaense arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to the humid forests of Central America.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive spathe and spadix inflorescence. The plant features broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often dark green with a glossy texture. It thrives in warm, moist environments with indirect light, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant, spadix that can range from yellow to red in color.
