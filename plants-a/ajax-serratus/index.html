Ajax Serratus

Serrated ajax, scientifically known as Ajax serratus, is a striking member of the Asteraceae family native to South Africa. This plant is recognized for its unique, serrated leaf margins and vibrant, daisy-like flowers that bloom in late spring. Thriving in well-drained soils and full sun, it is a popular choice for xeriscaping and rock gardens. Its resilience and ornamental value make it a favorite among horticulturists and garden enthusiasts. With its adaptability and aesthetic appeal, the serrated ajax stands out as a remarkable addition to any garden landscape.
