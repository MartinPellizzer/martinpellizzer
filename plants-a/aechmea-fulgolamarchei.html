Aechmea fulgolamarchei

Aechmea fulgolamarchei, commonly known as the Flame Bromeliad, is a striking epiphytic plant native to the tropical rainforests of Central and South America.

The plant's vibrant red and orange bracts are highly ornamental, often mistaken for flowers, though they are actually modified leaves. Aechmea fulgolamarchei thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity and well-draining soil.

Its unique appearance and ease of care make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Morphology
