Anthurium scherzerianum purpureum

Anthurium scherzerianum purpureum, commonly known as the Purple Velvet Anthurium, is a striking tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped, velvety leaves that are typically deep green with a purple tinge. The plant produces showy, spadix-shaped inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, often in shades of red or orange. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil.

This plant is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
