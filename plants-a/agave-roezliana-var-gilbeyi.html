Agave roezliana var. gilbeyi

Agave roezliana var. gilbeyi, commonly known as Gilbey's agave, is a succulent plant native to Mexico.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-gray in color, arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces a tall, branched inflorescence with small, white flowers that bloom in the spring. It is adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
