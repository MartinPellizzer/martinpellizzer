Arabidopsis campestris

Arabidopsis campestris, commonly known as wall radish, is a member of the Brassicaceae family.

It is a small, herbaceous plant that typically grows in temperate regions. The plant has simple, alternate leaves and produces small, white or purple flowers. Its fruit is a silique, which contains several seeds.

Arabidopsis campestris is also used in botanical research due to its genetic simplicity and rapid life cycle.
