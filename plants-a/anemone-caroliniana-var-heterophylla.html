Anemone caroliniana var. heterophylla

Anemone caroliniana var. heterophylla, commonly known as the Carolina anemone, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodland floors. The plant produces showy, white to pinkish flowers with a distinctive central cluster of stamens. Its leaves are usually basal, forming a rosette, and are often lobed or deeply divided.

The species is noted for its adaptability and is sometimes used in native plant gardens for its ornamental value.
