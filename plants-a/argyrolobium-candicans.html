Argyrolobium candicans

Argyrolobium candicans, commonly known as the silver globular bush, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its legume-like pods and nitrogen-fixing ability. Its unique appearance and adaptability to arid conditions make it a notable species in its native habitat.

It is often used in ornamental gardening due to its striking visual appeal.
