Anthemis pedunculata subsp. turolensis

Anthemis pedunculata subsp.

turolensis, commonly known as the Turolensis anthemis, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and yellow-orange composite flowers.

The plant typically features linear, aromatic leaves and produces small, tubular florets arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and naturalized areas.
