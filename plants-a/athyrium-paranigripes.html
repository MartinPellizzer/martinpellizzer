Athyrium paranigripes

Athyrium paranigripes, commonly known as the black-stemmed lady fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Athyriaceae and is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and stream sides. Its leaves are bipinnate, with a distinctive dark green color and finely divided segments.

Athyrium paranigripes is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
