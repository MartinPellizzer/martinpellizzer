Acinodendron obscurum

Acinodendron obscurum, commonly known as the dark acacia, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellow flowers. The plant produces pod-like fruits that contain seeds, typical of the legume family. It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often found in savanna and woodland habitats.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
