Asplenium normale f. scythiforme

Asplenium normale f. scythiforme, commonly known as the Scythian maidenhair fern, is a variant of the common maidenhair fern.

It is a small, delicate fern with finely divided, lance-shaped fronds that are typically dark green and slightly curled. The plant thrives in shady, moist environments and is often found in woodland areas. Its fronds are characterized by their narrow, elongated shape and a distinctive, feathery appearance.

This fern is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
