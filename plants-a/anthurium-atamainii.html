Anthurium atamainii

Anthurium atamainii, commonly known as the Atamainii arrowhead, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its unique inflorescence consisting of a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe. The plant features broad, heart-shaped leaves with a glossy surface, often displaying vibrant green or variegated patterns. It thrives in high humidity and indirect light, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to produce clusters of small, inconspicuous flowers that emerge from the spathe.
