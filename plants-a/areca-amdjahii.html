Areca amdjahii

Areca amdjahii, commonly known as the Java areca, is a tropical palm native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and pinnate leaves that can reach several meters in length. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, which eventually develop into red, fleshy fruits. Areca amdjahii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.

Its growth habit makes it suitable for landscaping in warm, humid climates.
