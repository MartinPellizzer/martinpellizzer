Aristolochia macedonica

Aristolochia macedonica, commonly known as the Macedonian birthwort, is a flowering plant native to the Balkan region.

It is characterized by its unique, winding, pipe-shaped flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color. The plant produces heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and a strong, often unpleasant odor. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its medicinal properties and ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
