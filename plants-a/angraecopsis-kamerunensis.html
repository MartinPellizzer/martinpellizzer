Angraecopsis kamerunensis

Angraecopsis kamerunensis, commonly known as the Cameroon star orchid, is a flowering plant native to Cameroon.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive star-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments within tropical forests. Its leaves are elongated and lanceolate, while its roots are fibrous and adapted to nutrient-poor soils.

Angraecopsis kamerunensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
