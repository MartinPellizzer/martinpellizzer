Ajax gayi

Ajax gayi, commonly known as the slender everlasting, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its delicate, daisy-like flowers that bloom in shades of white and yellow. The plant produces small, cylindrical seed heads that are adapted for wind dispersal. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Ajax gayi is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
