Acer Angustilobum Var. Kwangtungense

Kwangtung maple, scientifically known as Acer angustilobum var. kwangtungense, is a unique and striking deciduous tree native to southern China. This variant of the narrow-leaf maple is celebrated for its vibrant autumn foliage, which displays shades of red, orange, and yellow. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in mountainous regions with well-drained soil. The tree's distinctive heart-shaped leaves and graceful form make it a popular choice for ornamental planting. Its ecological and aesthetic value has made it a sought-after species in horticulture and landscape design.
