Aristida steudeliana

Aristida steudeliana, commonly known as the slender dropseed, is a perennial grass native to North America.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-green blades and produces small, fluffy seed heads that give it a delicate appearance. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in prairies and open woodlands. It is drought-tolerant and has a clumping growth habit.

Aristida steudeliana is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, providing habitat for pollinators and small wildlife.
