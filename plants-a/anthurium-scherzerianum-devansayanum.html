Anthurium scherzerianum devansayanum

Anthurium scherzerianum devansayanum, commonly known as the Devansayanum Anthurium, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped spathe that comes in shades of red, orange, and yellow, often with a contrasting green spadix. The plant thrives in humid, warm environments with bright, indirect light and well-draining soil. Its foliage is dark green and glossy, adding to its ornamental appeal.

Anthurium scherzerianum devansayanum is popular as a houseplant due to its air-purifying qualities and long-lasting blooms.
