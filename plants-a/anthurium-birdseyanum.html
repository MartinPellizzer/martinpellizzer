Anthurium birdseyanum

Anthurium birdseyanum, commonly known as the Bird's Eye Anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the forests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-like inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, typically in shades of red or orange. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium birdseyanum is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its ornamental foliage and unique flowering structure.
