Amaryllis intiflora

Amaryllis intiflora, commonly known as the pink amaryllis, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, erect stems that bear large, showy flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant produces strap-like leaves that are broad and fleshy, adapted to store water in its native arid environment. It blooms in late summer to early autumn, adding vibrant color to gardens.

Its unique appearance and seasonal blooming make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
