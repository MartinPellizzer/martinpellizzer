Actinidia hemsleyana

Actinidia hemsleyana, commonly known as the Hemsley kiwifruit, is a deciduous vine native to China.

It belongs to the Actinidiaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and small, pale green fruits. The plant thrives in temperate climates and requires a cool, moist environment for optimal growth. Its fruit, though not widely cultivated, is edible and has a unique flavor profile.

The plant is also valued for its ornamental value due to its attractive foliage and flowering habits.
