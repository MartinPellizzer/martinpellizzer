Abies nordmanniana subsp. bornmuelleriana

Abies nordmanniana subsp.

bornmuelleriana, commonly known as the Nordmann fir, is a coniferous tree native to the Caucasus region. It is characterized by its dense, pyramidal shape and needle-like leaves that are blue-green in color.

The cones are typically pendulous and measure up to 10 centimeters in length. It thrives in cool, moist climates and is often found in mountainous areas.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in horticulture and reforestation efforts.
