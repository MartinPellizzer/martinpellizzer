Arctotis arborescens

Arctotis arborescens, commonly known as the tree arctotis, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 3 meters tall, featuring a woody stem and dense foliage. The plant produces showy, daisy-like flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, which bloom throughout the warmer months. Its leaves are typically narrow and hairy, and it thrives in well-drained soils with full sun exposure.

Arctotis arborescens is often cultivated for ornamental purposes in gardens and landscapes.
