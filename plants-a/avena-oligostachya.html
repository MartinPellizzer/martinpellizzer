Avena oligostachya

Avena oligostachya, commonly known as the slender oat, is a species of grass native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences with numerous tiny flowers, typical of the Poaceae family. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine or subalpine habitats.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes cultivated as an ornamental plant or for erosion control.
