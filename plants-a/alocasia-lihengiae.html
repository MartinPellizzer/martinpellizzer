Alocasia lihengiae

Alocasia lihengiae, commonly known as the Liheng's alocasia, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length, often with a deep green color and intricate veining. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, nutrient-rich soil. It is a member of the Araceae family, known for its unique inflorescence consisting of a spadix and spathe.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated as a decorative plant in tropical gardens and indoor spaces.
