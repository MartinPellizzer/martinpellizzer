Anodendron parviflorum

Anodendron parviflorum, commonly known as the small-flowered anodendron, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in coastal and sandy habitats, thriving in well-drained soils. The plant features small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often covered in fine hairs.

Anodendron parviflorum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
