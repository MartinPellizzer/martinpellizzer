Ardisia complanata

Ardisia complanata, commonly known as the flat-leaf evergreen or Chinese evergreen, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, dark green, elliptical leaves that are typically arranged in a flat, compact pattern. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers followed by bright red berries, which are attractive to birds. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often used as an ornamental plant in gardens.

Ardisia complanata is valued for its attractive foliage and ability to tolerate indoor conditions.
