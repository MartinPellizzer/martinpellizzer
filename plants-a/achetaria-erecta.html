Achetaria erecta

Achetaria erecta, commonly known as the upright achetaria, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Acanthaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and thrives in humid, well-drained soils. The plant features slender, upright stems with opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy. Its showy, tubular flowers are typically pink or purple and bloom in clusters.

Achetaria erecta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
