Aulomyrcia longipes var. spathulata

Aulomyrcia longipes var. spathulata, commonly known as the broad-leaved aulomyrcia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and by plant enthusiasts.
