Aloe karasbergensis subsp. hunsbergensis

Aloe karasbergensis subsp.

hunsbergensis, commonly known as the Hunsberg aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa. It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a waxy coating.

The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing yellowish-green flowers. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, requiring well-drained soil and minimal water.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
