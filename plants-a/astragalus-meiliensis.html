Astragalus meiliensis

Astragalus meiliensis, commonly known as Meilian milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to the Tibetan Plateau.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its small, yellow pea-like flowers. The plant is known for its deep root system, which helps it survive in high-altitude, arid environments. It produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds.

Astragalus meiliensis is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional Chinese medicine.
