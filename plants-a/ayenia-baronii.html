Ayenia baronii

Ayenia baronii, commonly known as the Baron's ayenia, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant produces showy, daisy-like flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. Ayenia baronii is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.

Its foliage consists of narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem.
