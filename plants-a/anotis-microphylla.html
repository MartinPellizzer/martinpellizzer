Anotis microphylla

Anotis microphylla, commonly known as the small-leaved anotis, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 meter. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in spring. Its leaves are narrow and opposite, with a rough texture.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
