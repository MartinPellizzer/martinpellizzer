Ainsliaea brandisiana

Ainsliaea brandisiana, commonly known as the golden flame or flame flower, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from orange to red, often resembling flames. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, which are typically borne on long, slender stalks. Its leaves are usually opposite, simple, and have a glossy, dark green appearance.

Ainsliaea brandisiana is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant due to its vibrant floral display.
