Athyrium filix-femina f. elatius

Athyrium filix-femina f. elatius, commonly known as the silver lace fern, is a variant of the common lady fern.

It is characterized by its delicate, lacy fronds that are typically a pale green with a silvery sheen. This fern thrives in shady, moist environments and is often found in woodland areas. The fronds are biennial, with the fertile fronds appearing in the second year.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appearance and ability to thrive in humid, shaded conditions.
