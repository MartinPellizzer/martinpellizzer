Areca nagensis

Areca nagensis, commonly known as the Nagara areca palm, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, fan-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that develop into edible fruits, which are often consumed by local populations. Areca nagensis thrives in humid climates with well-drained soil and requires partial to full sunlight.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in tropical regions.
