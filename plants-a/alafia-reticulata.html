Alafia reticulata

Alafia reticulata, commonly known as the net-veined fern, is a tropical plant belonging to the family Davalliaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds that exhibit a distinctive network of veins, giving it its common name. Alafia reticulata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
