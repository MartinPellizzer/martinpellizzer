Aconitum toxicum nothosubsp.

nyaradyanum, commonly known as the Nyaradyanum aconite, is a hybrid plant within the Ranunculaceae family. It is characterized by its tall, sparsely branched stem and dark green, palmately lobed leaves.

The plant produces showy, blue to purple flowers in late summer, which are arranged in racemose inflorescences. It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Europe and is adapted to cool, moist environments.
