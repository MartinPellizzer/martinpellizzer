Artemisia japonica var. hainanensis

Artemisia japonica var. hainanensis, commonly known as Hainan wormwood, is a perennial herb native to southern China.

It is characterized by its aromatic, silver-gray foliage and small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in mountainous regions. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is valued for its medicinal properties.

Its essential oils contain compounds with potential anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial effects.
