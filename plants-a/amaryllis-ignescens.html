Amaryllis ignescens

Amaryllis ignescens, commonly known as the fire lily, is a striking bulbous plant native to southern Africa.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to deep red, often with a fiery orange or yellow throat. The plant produces a tall, upright stem with several blooms that open sequentially, creating a dramatic display. Its leaves are broad, strap-like, and often have a waxy texture.

Amaryllis ignescens thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and containers.
