Antidesma japonicum var. uncinulatum

Antidesma japonicum var. uncinulatum, commonly known as the hooked antidesma, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features small, elliptical leaves with a smooth upper surface and a slightly hairy underside. Its flowers are clustered in small inflorescences and are usually white or pale yellow in color.

The fruit is a small, fleshy drupe that contains a single seed.
