Anoectochilus xingrenensis

Anoectochilus xingrenensis, commonly known as the Xingren Orchid, is a rare and endemic orchid species native to China.

The plant typically produces small, fragrant flowers with a unique coloration pattern, often featuring a combination of white, yellow, and green hues.
