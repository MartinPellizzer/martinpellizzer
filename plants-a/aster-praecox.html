Aster praecox

Aster praecox, commonly known as early aster, is a herbaceous perennial native to North America.

It produces showy, daisy-like flowers with purple to blue petals and yellow centers, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has alternate, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Aster praecox is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting pollinators.
