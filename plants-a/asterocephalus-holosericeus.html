Asterocephalus holosericeus

Asterocephalus holosericeus, commonly known as the silky star-hair, is a unique plant species characterized by its intricate floral structure.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is known for its composite flower heads. The plant features showy, daisy-like blooms with a central disc surrounded by ray florets, typically in shades of white or pale yellow. Its leaves are usually narrow and covered in fine, silky hairs, contributing to its common name.

This species is often found in arid or semi-arid environments, where it thrives in well-drained soils.
