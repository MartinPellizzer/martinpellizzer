Asplenium erectum var. mutilatum

Asplenium erectum var. mutilatum, commonly known as the mutilated shield fern, is a rare and lesser-known variant of the shield fern.

It is a small, evergreen fern native to specific regions of Southeast Asia, particularly in parts of Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant features delicate, lance-shaped fronds that are often partially or fully cut, giving it its distinctive appearance. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is typically found in tropical rainforests.

Its unique morphology makes it a subject of interest for botanists and fern enthusiasts alike.
