Athyrium silvicola f. kinokuniense

Athyrium silvicola f. kinokuniense, commonly known as the Kinokuni fern, is a fern species native to Japan.

It belongs to the family Athyriaceae and is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and stream sides. Its spores are borne on the undersides of the fronds in clusters.

The varietal form 'kinokuniense' is distinguished by its unique leaf morphology and habitat preferences.
