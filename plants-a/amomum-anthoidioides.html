Amomum anthoidioides

Amomum anthoidioides, commonly known as the white cardamom, is a member of the ginger family (Zingiberaceae) and is native to Southeast Asia.

Its rhizomes contain essential oils and compounds responsible for its distinctive fragrance and medicinal properties.

Amomum anthoidioides is valued for its culinary and therapeutic applications in various cultures.
