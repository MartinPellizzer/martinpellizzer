Abies pattonii

Abies pattonii, commonly known as the Chinese fir, is a coniferous evergreen tree native to China.

It belongs to the genus Abies in the family Pinaceae, characterized by its needle-like leaves and cone-bearing structure. The tree typically grows in mountainous regions, preferring cool and moist environments. Its foliage is dense and dark green, with a fragrant scent, and it produces small, cylindrical cones.

The species is valued for its timber and is also cultivated for ornamental purposes in horticulture.
