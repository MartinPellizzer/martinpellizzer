Arundinella avenacea

Arundinella avenacea, commonly known as the narrowleaf bamboo or slender bamboo, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

It belongs to the grass family (Poaceae) and is characterized by its slender, hollow stems that grow in dense clumps. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in panicles, which are typically wind-pollinated. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a smooth texture and a tendency to droop under heavy rain.

Arundinella avenacea is often used in ornamental gardening for its aesthetic appeal and resilience in various soil conditions.
