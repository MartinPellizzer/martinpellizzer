Anthemis montana var. calabrica

Anthemis montana var. calabrica, commonly known as the Calabrian anthemis, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features basal rosettes of narrow, gray-green leaves and produces yellow flowers with a distinctive central disk. The plant is often found in rocky or limestone soils, thriving in well-drained conditions. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used traditionally in herbal medicine.

Its flowering period typically occurs in late spring to early summer.
