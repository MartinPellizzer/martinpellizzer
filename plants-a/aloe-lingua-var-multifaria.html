Aloe lingua var. multifaria

Aloe lingua var. multifaria, commonly known as the multibract aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, with a waxy coating to reduce water loss. The plant produces upright, branched inflorescences with small, tubular flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their soothing properties.

It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.
