Alkanna orientalis subsp. orientalis

Alkanna orientalis subsp.

orientalis, commonly known as the oriental alkanna, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region. It is characterized by its upright, branched stems and clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers.

The plant produces distinctive orange-red rootstocks that are rich in alkaloids and have been used in traditional medicine. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or arid environments.

Its unique coloration and medicinal properties make it a notable species in botanical studies.
