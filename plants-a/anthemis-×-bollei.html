Anthemis × bollei

Anthemis × bollei, commonly known as the Bolle's tansy, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its aromatic, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in dense, flat-topped clusters. The plant typically grows to a height of 30–60 cm and features finely divided, fern-like leaves. Native to the Mediterranean region, it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is often cultivated in gardens and herbal practices.
