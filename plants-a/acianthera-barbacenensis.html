Acianthera barbacenensis

Acianthera barbacenensis, commonly known as the Brazilian orchid, is a species of orchid native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers. The plant typically grows in tropical rainforests, where it thrives in humid and shaded environments. Its roots are often epiphytic, meaning they grow on the surface of trees rather than in soil.

The flowers usually display a range of colors, including white, yellow, and green, making it a popular ornamental plant.
