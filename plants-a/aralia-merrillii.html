Aralia merrillii

Aralia merrillii, commonly known as the Chinese angelica tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It features compound leaves with long, slender leaflets that are often arranged in a feather-like pattern. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers in clustered umbels, followed by spiny, berry-like fruits. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Aralia merrillii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
