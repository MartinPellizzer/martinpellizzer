Acrostichum lindenii var. minus

Acrostichum lindenii var. minus, commonly known as the lesser lace fern, is a variant of the lace fern species native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds that give it a lacy appearance. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical rainforests. Its leaves are usually smaller and more refined compared to the standard variety, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

The botanical features of this fern make it well-suited for humid climates and indoor cultivation.
