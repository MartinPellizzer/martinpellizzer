Aloe subalbicans var. laevior

Aloe subalbicans var. laevior, commonly known as the smooth aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a waxy coating, which helps it retain water in arid environments. The plant produces tall, branched flower stalks that bear yellowish-green flowers, adding ornamental value. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for low-maintenance landscaping.
