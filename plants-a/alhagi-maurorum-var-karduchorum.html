Alhagi maurorum var. karduchorum

Alhagi maurorum var. karduchorum, commonly known as the Karduchian goat's beard, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive, feathery foliage and yellowish-white flowers. The plant is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, often found in steppes and rocky soils. It has a deep root system that allows it to survive in harsh, drought-prone conditions.

This variety is sometimes used in traditional medicine and as forage for livestock.
