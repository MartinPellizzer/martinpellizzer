Ardisia tenera

Ardisia tenera, commonly known as the glossy evergreen shrub, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features small, dark green, glossy leaves that are typically arranged in opposite pairs along its stems. The plant produces clusters of white, star-shaped flowers followed by bright red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade.

Ardisia tenera is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in shaded garden environments.
