Alternanthera sissoo

Alternanthera sissoo, commonly known as red-stemmed alternanthera, is a species of flowering plant in the Amaranthaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America and is characterized by its vibrant red stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and is often used in aquatic gardens or as a decorative plant in water features.

Its ornamental value comes from its striking foliage and adaptability to various growing conditions.
