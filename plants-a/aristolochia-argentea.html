Aristolochia argentea

Aristolochia argentea, commonly known as the silver vine, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, silver-veined leaves and distinctive, winding, pipe-shaped flowers. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow fruits that contain numerous seeds. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.

However, it contains toxic compounds and should be handled with care.
