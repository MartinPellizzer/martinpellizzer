Abronia × Alba Var. Variabilis

Abronia × alba var. variabilis is a unique hybrid plant that showcases the natural blending of genetic traits from its parent species. This variation is often observed in regions where the parent plants grow in close proximity, leading to a range of morphological differences. Its flowers exhibit a spectrum of colors, making it a striking addition to any garden or natural habitat. Despite its beauty, this plant is relatively rare and requires specific growing conditions to thrive. Understanding and conserving such hybrids is crucial for preserving biodiversity in plant ecosystems.
