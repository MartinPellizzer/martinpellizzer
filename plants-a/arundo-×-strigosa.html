Arundo × strigosa

Arundo × strigosa, commonly known as giant reed, is a hybrid grass species native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems that can grow up to 6 meters in height, with narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces dense clusters of small, spike-like flowers that are typically greenish or pale brown in color. It thrives in wet or moist environments, often found near water bodies such as rivers and wetlands.

Botanically, it is valued for its ability to stabilize soil and provide habitat for various wildlife species.
