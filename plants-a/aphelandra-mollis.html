Aphelandra mollis

Aphelandra mollis, commonly known as the silver velvet plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green, velvety leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant, white flowers with a yellow center, which bloom throughout the year in suitable conditions. Aphelandra mollis thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

It is often cultivated as a decorative indoor plant due to its attractive foliage and ornamental flowers.
