Anthurium acuminatum

Anthurium acuminatum, commonly known as the velvet leaf anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a velvety texture. The plant produces striking spadices, which are modified leaves that enclose the true flowers. Anthurium acuminatum thrives in humid, warm environments with indirect light.

Its unique foliage and vibrant spadices make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
