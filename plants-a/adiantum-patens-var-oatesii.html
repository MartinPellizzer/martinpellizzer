Adiantum patens var. oatesii

Adiantum patens var. oatesii, commonly known as the Oates' maidenhair fern, is a delicate and elegant fern native to Southeast Asia.

It features finely divided, glossy, and arching fronds that give it a graceful appearance. The plant is characterized by its small, bright green pinnae with a slightly curled margin. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires consistent moisture and well-draining soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in indoor gardens or shaded outdoor spaces.
