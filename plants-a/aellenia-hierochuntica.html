Aellenia hierochuntica

Aellenia hierochuntica, commonly known as the Cyprus starflower, is a rare and endangered plant native to Cyprus.

It is a small herbaceous species characterized by its star-shaped flowers and narrow, lanceolate leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky and arid environments, showcasing adaptations to survive in harsh conditions. It blooms in the spring, producing bright yellow flowers that attract pollinators.

Due to habitat loss and limited distribution, conservation efforts are crucial for its survival.
