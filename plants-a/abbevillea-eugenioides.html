Abbevillea eugenioides

Abbevillea eugenioides, commonly known as the Brazilian flame tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its striking, fiery orange to red flowers that bloom in dense clusters during the summer months. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are often hairy and have a rough texture. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Abbevillea eugenioides is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
