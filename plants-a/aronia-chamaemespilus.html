Aronia chamaemespilus

Aronia chamaemespilus, commonly known as the European cranberrybush, is a deciduous shrub native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features dark green, oval-shaped leaves that turn bright red in the autumn. The plant produces small, red berries that are edible and often used in culinary and medicinal applications. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight.

Aronia chamaemespilus is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, providing food for birds and other wildlife.
