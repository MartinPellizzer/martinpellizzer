Adenocarpus complicatus var. eglandulosa

Adenocarpus complicatus var. eglandulosa, commonly known as the glandular legume, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant produces small, round pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and rocky areas.

Due to its unique morphology and ecological role, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
