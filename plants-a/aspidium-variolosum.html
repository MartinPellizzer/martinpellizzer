Aspidium variolosum

Aspidium variolosum, commonly known as the variable water fern, is a fern species belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds that are typically bioluminescent under certain conditions. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its spores are dispersed through the air, contributing to its reproductive strategy.

The plant's structure includes a rhizome that supports its growth and helps in nutrient absorption from the soil.
