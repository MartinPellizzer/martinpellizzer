Acinodendron spennerostachyum

Acinodendron spennerostachyum, commonly known as the African ironwood, is a tropical tree native to regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, hard wood that is highly valued for its durability and resistance to decay. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical in shape. Its flowers are small and clustered, often producing a distinctive fragrance.

The fruit is a woody capsule that contains several seeds, which are dispersed by wind or animals.
