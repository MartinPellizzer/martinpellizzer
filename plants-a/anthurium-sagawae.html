Anthurium sagawae

Anthurium sagawae, commonly known as the Sagawa anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are often dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-like inflorescences surrounded by colorful, spatulate bracts. It thrives in humid, warm environments with bright, indirect light.

Anthurium sagawae is popular as a decorative plant due to its ornamental foliage and unique flowering structure.
