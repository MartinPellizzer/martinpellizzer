Arundinella chenii

Arundinella chenii, commonly known as Chinese bamboo or bamboo grass, is a perennial grass species native to China.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems that can grow up to several meters in height. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spikelets, typical of the grass family. Its leaves are long and narrow, with a smooth texture and a slight sheen.

Arundinella chenii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional Chinese medicine.
