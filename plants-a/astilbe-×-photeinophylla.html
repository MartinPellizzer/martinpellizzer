Astilbe × photeinophylla

Astilbe × photeinophylla, commonly known as the golden lace astilbe, is a hybrid perennial plant native to China.

It features delicate, fern-like foliage that turns a striking golden hue in the fall. The plant produces upright plumes of tiny, showy flowers in shades of pink, white, or red, depending on the cultivar. It thrives in partial to full shade and prefers moist, well-drained soil.

Astilbe × photeinophylla is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to add color to shady garden spaces.
