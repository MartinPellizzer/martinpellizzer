Aspidistra atrata

Aspidistra atrata, commonly known as the black aspidistra, is a perennial evergreen plant native to southern China.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its dark green, lance-shaped leaves with a glossy surface. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are surrounded by a spadix and a hood-like spathe. It thrives in low-light conditions and prefers moist, well-draining soil.

Aspidistra atrata is valued for its ornamental foliage and its ability to adapt to indoor environments.
