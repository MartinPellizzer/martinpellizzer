Aulomyrcia rufa

Aulomyrcia rufa, commonly known as the red-leaved hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red foliage and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Aulomyrcia rufa is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal remedies.
