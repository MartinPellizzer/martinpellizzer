Amellus asper var. glabriusculus

Amellus asper var. glabriusculus, commonly known as the smooth-leaved amellus, is a rare and lesser-known plant species within the Amellus genus.

Native to specific regions in southern Africa, it thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and full sun exposure.
