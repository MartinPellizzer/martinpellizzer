Anil digitata

Anil digitata, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves and clusters of showy, tubular flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant typically grows as a low-growing shrub or perennial herb, often reaching heights of up to 30 cm. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Anil digitata is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and ease of care.
