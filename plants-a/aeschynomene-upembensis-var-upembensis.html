Aeschynomene upembensis var. upembensis

Aeschynomene upembensis var. upembensis, commonly known as African leadplant, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its upright growth habit and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale pink, followed by small, pod-like fruits.
