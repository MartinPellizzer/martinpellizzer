Arnica Chamissonis Var. Andina

arnica chamissonis var. andina is a rare and unique plant species native to the high altitudes of the andes mountains. it belongs to the asteraceae family, known for its diverse and ecologically important members. this variant is distinguished by its striking yellow flowers and hairy leaves adapted to cold and harsh environments. it thrives in rocky soils and is often found in alpine meadows at elevations above 3000 meters. due to its limited distribution and ecological significance, it is considered a valuable subject for botanical research and conservation efforts.
