Alsophila acutidens

Alsophila acutidens, commonly known as the narrow-leaf tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and feathery fronds that give it a distinctive, elegant appearance. The plant has a robust root system that helps it thrive in moist, shaded environments. Its fronds are typically lance-shaped and can reach lengths of up to several meters.

Alsophila acutidens is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its aesthetic appeal and adaptability to tropical climates.
