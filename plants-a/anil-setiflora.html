Anil setiflora

Anil setiflora, commonly known as the narrow-leaved anil, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Anil setiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its resilience and aesthetic qualities.
