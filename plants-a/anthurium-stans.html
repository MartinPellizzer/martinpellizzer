Anthurium stans

Anthurium stans, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a striking tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped spathe that ranges from red to orange in color. The plant features a short, thick stem with waxy, dark green leaves that are typically glossy and lance-shaped. Anthurium stans thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Its unique floral structure consists of a spadix surrounded by the colorful spathe, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
