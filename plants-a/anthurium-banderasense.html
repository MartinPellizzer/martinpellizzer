Anthurium banderasense

Anthurium banderasense, commonly known as the Bandera arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to Central America.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive inflorescence consisting of a spadix and a spathe. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy, dark green leaves that are often used in ornamental horticulture. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Anthurium banderasense is valued for its aesthetic appeal and is relatively easy to care for in the right conditions.
