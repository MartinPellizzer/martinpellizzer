Acianthera wawraeana

Acianthera wawraeana, commonly known as the Wawra's acianthera, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant features a compact growth habit with a single stem and lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
