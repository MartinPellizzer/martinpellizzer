Astracantha gudrathii

Astracantha gudrathii, commonly known as the Gudrathii thornveld, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its spiny, woody stems and produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has a compact growth habit and is often found in rocky or sandy soils. It is adapted to arid conditions and requires minimal water once established.

The species is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphology and ecological significance in its native habitat.
