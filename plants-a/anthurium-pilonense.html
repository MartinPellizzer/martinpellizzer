Anthurium pilonense

Anthurium pilonense, commonly known as the Pilonense Arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a glossy texture. The plant produces striking spadices, which are elongated, spadix-like structures surrounded by a colorful spathe. Anthurium pilonense thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its unique foliage and exotic appearance make it a popular choice for indoor gardening and botanical collections.
