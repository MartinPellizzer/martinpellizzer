Acalypha patens

Acalypha patens, commonly known as the silverleaf acalypha, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, silver-gray foliage that contrasts with its green stems. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments.

Acalypha patens is valued in horticulture for its ornamental leaves and ability to tolerate a range of growing conditions.
