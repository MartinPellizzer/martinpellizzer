Arisaema thunbergii var. urashima

Arisaema thunbergii var. urashima, commonly known as the Urashima arisaema, is a variant of the Japanese arrowroot plant.

It is a herbaceous perennial native to Japan, characterized by its distinctive arrow-shaped leaves and spadix inflorescence. The plant produces striking, hooded spatulate flowers that are typically green or white in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage and floral structure.
