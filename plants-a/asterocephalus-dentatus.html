Asterocephalus dentatus

Asterocephalus dentatus, commonly known as the toothed starhead, is a perennial herb native to the southern regions of South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive star-shaped inflorescence and serrated leaf margins. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. Its flowers are usually white or pale blue, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits.

Asterocephalus dentatus is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
