Astragalus hotianensis

Astragalus hotianensis, commonly known as the Chinese milkvetch, is a perennial legume native to China.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has a deep root system, which helps it thrive in arid and semi-arid environments. It is often found in grasslands and rocky areas, contributing to soil stabilization.

Astragalus hotianensis is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional Chinese medicine.
