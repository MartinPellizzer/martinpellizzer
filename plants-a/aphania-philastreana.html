Aphania philastreana

Aphania philastreana, commonly known as the Cape tulip, is a striking South African flowering plant belonging to the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, showy, funnel-shaped flowers that come in a range of colors including yellow, orange, and red. The plant typically grows as a herbaceous perennial, often forming clumps with strap-like leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Aphania philastreana is notable for its ability to produce multiple flower stalks, enhancing its ornamental value in gardens.
