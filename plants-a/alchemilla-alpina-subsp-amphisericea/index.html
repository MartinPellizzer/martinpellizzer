Alchemilla Alpina Subsp. Amphisericea

Alchemilla alpina subsp. amphisericea is a rare and delicate plant native to the alpine regions of Europe. It thrives in rocky and moist environments, often found in high mountain areas where few plants can survive. This subspecies is known for its small, bright green leaves and subtle yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. Its scientific name reflects its historical association with alchemy and its medicinal properties. Despite its beauty, it remains a challenging plant to cultivate outside its natural habitat.
