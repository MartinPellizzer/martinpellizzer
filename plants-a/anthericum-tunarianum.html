Anthericum tunarianum

Anthericum tunarianum, commonly known as the Tunarian lily, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and western Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems that bear clusters of white or pale pink flowers. The plant has broad, strap-like leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern at the base. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Anthericum tunarianum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its elegant appearance.
