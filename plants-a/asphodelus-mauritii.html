Asphodelus mauritii

Asphodelus mauritii, commonly known as the Cape asphodel, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features strap-like, grassy leaves and produces tall, spike-like inflorescences with small, yellowish flowers. The plant is adapted to arid and rocky environments, making it drought-tolerant. It belongs to the family Asphodelaceae, which includes other succulent plants.

Its structure and growth habits make it a hardy ornamental plant suitable for xeriscaping.
