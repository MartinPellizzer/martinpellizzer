Armeria majellensis

Armeria majellensis, commonly known as the rock sandwort, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically forms dense mats of foliage. The plant produces clusters of small, white, star-shaped flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained, rocky or gravelly soils and is often found in coastal areas.

Armeria majellensis is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to tolerate harsh environmental conditions.
