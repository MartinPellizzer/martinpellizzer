Alyssum balkanicum f. depressum

Alyssum balkanicum f. depressum, commonly known as the Balkan Alyssum, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Balkan Peninsula.

It features compact, mat-forming growth with small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. The foliage consists of narrow, gray-green leaves that are densely packed, giving the plant a soft, almost silvery appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract beneficial insects like ladybugs and hoverflies.
