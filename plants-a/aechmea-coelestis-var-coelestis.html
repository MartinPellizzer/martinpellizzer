Aechmea coelestis var. coelestis

Aechmea coelestis var. coelestis, commonly known as the sky blue aechmea, is a striking bromeliad native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant blue-green rosette of broad, fleshy leaves that form a central cup, which collects rainwater. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing clusters of white to pale blue flowers. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique appearance and relatively low maintenance make it a favorite among plant enthusiasts.
