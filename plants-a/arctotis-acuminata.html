Arctotis acuminata

Arctotis acuminata, commonly known as the sharpleaf arctotis, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces vibrant, daisy-like flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Arctotis acuminata is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
