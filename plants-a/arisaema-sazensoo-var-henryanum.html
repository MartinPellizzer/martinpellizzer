Arisaema sazensoo var. henryanum

Arisaema sazensoo var. henryanum, commonly known as the Japanese cobra lily, is a herbaceous plant native to Japan.

It features a distinctive, hooded spathe that resembles a cobra's head, giving it its common name. The plant produces a cluster of small, white to yellowish flowers within the spathe. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and can grow up to 30 cm in length.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
