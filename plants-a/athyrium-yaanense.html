Athyrium yaanense

Athyrium yaanense, commonly known as the Yaan fern, is a species of fern native to China.

It belongs to the family Athyriaceae and is characterized by its delicate, bipinnate fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest undergrowth. Its spores are produced on the underside of the fronds in clusters called sori.

Athyrium yaanense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens with suitable humidity and light conditions.
