Amaranthus alopecurus

Amaranthus alopecurus, commonly known as smooth amaranth, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its upright stems and lance-shaped leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from green to red, depending on the species. It thrives in a variety of soil types but prefers well-drained conditions.

Amaranthus alopecurus is often found in disturbed areas such as roadsides and fields.
