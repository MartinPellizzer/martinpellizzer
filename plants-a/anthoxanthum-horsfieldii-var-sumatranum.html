Anthoxanthum horsfieldii var. sumatranum

Anthoxanthum horsfieldii var. sumatranum, commonly known as the Sumatran oatgrass, is a perennial grass species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically bluish-green in color. The plant produces small, spikelet-bearing inflorescences, which are typical of the Poaceae family. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments.

This variety is notable for its ornamental value and adaptability to various ecological conditions.
