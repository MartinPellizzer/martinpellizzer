Astragalus cavaleriei

Astragalus cavaleriei, commonly known as the Chinese milkvetch, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant is known for its deep root system, which allows it to thrive in arid and rocky environments. It is also valued in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.

The species is often found in grasslands and mountainous regions, where it plays a role in soil stabilization.
