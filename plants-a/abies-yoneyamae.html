Abies yoneyamae

Abies yoneyamae, commonly known as Yoneyama's fir, is a rare and endemic species native to Japan.

It belongs to the genus Abies, which includes various species of fir trees, and is characterized by its conical shape and needle-like leaves. The plant typically grows in high-altitude regions, thriving in cool, moist environments with well-drained soil. Its cones are small and often hang in clusters, contributing to its unique appearance.
