Anthemis lonadioides

Anthemis lonadioides, commonly known as the slender everlasting.

It is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly found in regions of Argentina and Chile. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. It produces small, yellow-to-orange ray flowers surrounded by a disk of tubular flowers.

The plant is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
