Amorphophallus chlorospathus

Amorphophallus chlorospathus, commonly known as the green-spathe amorphophallus, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its unique inflorescence consisting of a spadix and a spathe. The plant produces large, showy flowers that are typically white or green in color. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The foliage is broad and arrow-shaped, contributing to its striking appearance.
