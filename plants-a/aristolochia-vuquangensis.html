Aristolochia vuquangensis

Aristolochia vuquangensis, commonly known as the Vu Quang arrow vine, is a flowering plant native to Vietnam.

It belongs to the Aristolochiaceae family and is characterized by its unique, tubular flowers that resemble a horn or pipe. The plant produces dark purple to brownish flowers that are often covered in a dense, hairy texture. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped with a rough texture and are arranged alternately along the stem.

This species is of interest to botanists due to its distinctive morphology and potential medicinal properties.
