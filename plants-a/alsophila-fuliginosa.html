Alsophila fuliginosa

Alsophila fuliginosa, commonly known as the black-stemmed tree fern, is a species native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk covered with dark, leathery scales. The plant produces large, feather-like fronds that can reach several meters in length. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Alsophila fuliginosa plays an important role in its ecosystem by providing habitat and contributing to forest biodiversity.
