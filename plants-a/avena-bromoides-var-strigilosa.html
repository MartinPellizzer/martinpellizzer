Avena bromoides var. strigilosa

Avena bromoides var. strigilosa, commonly known as slender oat, is a perennial grass native to North America.

It typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands. The plant has slender, cylindrical stems and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a hairy texture.

It is often used in restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil and provide habitat for wildlife.
