Acanthocalycium brevispinum

Acanthocalycium brevispinum, commonly known as the short-spined cactus, is a species native to the arid regions of South America.

It is a low-growing, spineless cactus with a compact, globular shape. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. Its thick, fleshy stems store water, allowing it to survive in harsh, dry environments.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and resilience.
