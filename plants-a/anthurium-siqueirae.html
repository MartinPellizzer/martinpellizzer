Anthurium siqueirae

Anthurium siqueirae, commonly known as the Siqueira's anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Brazil.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped spathe that can range in color from green to red. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers within a spadix, which is typical of the Araceae family. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its unique foliage and ornamental value make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
