Aleuritia caveana

Aleuritia caveana, commonly known as the cave alder, is a shrub native to the mountainous regions of China.

It is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, white flowers in clusters, which bloom in early spring. Its fruit is a small, red drupe that contains a single seed.

Aleuritia caveana is often found in rocky or forested areas and is adapted to cool, humid climates.
