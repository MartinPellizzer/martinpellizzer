Asplenium erectum f. usambarense

Asplenium erectum f. usambarense, commonly known as the Usambarensis spleenwort, is a fern native to East Africa.

It is a small, terrestrial fern with delicate, lanceolate leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in rocky or forested areas. Its fronds are usually dark green and have a finely toothed margin.

This fern is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphology and geographic distribution.
