Anthurium chorense

Anthurium chorense, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped spathe that ranges in color from deep red to orange. The plant produces small, green berries that are edible but not commonly consumed. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect sunlight.

Its foliage is dark green and glossy, adding to its ornamental value.
