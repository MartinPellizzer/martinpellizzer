Asplenium colaniae

Asplenium colaniae, commonly known as the African shield fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and its distinctive, shield-shaped fronds that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in moist forest understories. Its fronds are usually divided into smaller, lance-shaped segments, and it produces sporangia on the undersides of its leaves.

Asplenium colaniae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant.
