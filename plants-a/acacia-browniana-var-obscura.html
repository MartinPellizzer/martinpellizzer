Acacia browniana var. obscura

Acacia browniana var. obscura, commonly known as the dark obscura acacia, is a shrub or small tree native to parts of Africa.

It is characterized by its dark green, narrow leaves and yellow floral spikes that bloom during the dry season. The plant produces small, woody pods that contain flat, rectangular seeds. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky soils.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes used in reforestation efforts in degraded landscapes.
