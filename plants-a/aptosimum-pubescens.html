Aptosimum pubescens

Aptosimum pubescens, commonly known as the hairy aptosimum, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Poaceae family, commonly referred to as the grass family. This species is characterized by its dense, hairy foliage and compact growth habit. It typically grows in moist, temperate climates and is often found in grasslands or along riverbanks.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
