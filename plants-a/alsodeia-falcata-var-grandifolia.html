Alsodeia falcata var. grandifolia

Alsodeia falcata var. grandifolia, commonly known as the large-leaved alsodeia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features elongated, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving them a soft texture. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its root system is fibrous and adapted to thrive in well-drained, sandy soils.

It is often found in open grasslands and along rocky outcrops, where it contributes to local biodiversity.
