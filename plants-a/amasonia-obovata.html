Amasonia obovata

Amasonia obovata, commonly known as the broadleaf sunflower, is a flowering plant native to the United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy yellow flowers and broad, heart-shaped leaves. Amasonia obovata is often used in native plant gardens for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
