Asparagus kiusianus

Asparagus kiusianus, commonly known as the Kiusianus asparagus, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features slender, spiny stems that grow from a fibrous root system. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters. Its foliage consists of narrow, pointed leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

It is typically found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments.
