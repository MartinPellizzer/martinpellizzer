Astilbe sikokumontana

Astilbe sikokumontana, commonly known as the Japanese feather fern, is a herbaceous perennial native to Japan.

It features delicate, plume-like inflorescences that bloom in shades of pink, red, and white, typically in late spring to early summer. The plant has fern-like, bipinnate leaves that are finely divided and provide a lush, textural contrast to its flowering display. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens.

Astilbe sikokumontana is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to add color and interest to shaded landscapes.
