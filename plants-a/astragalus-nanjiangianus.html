Astragalus nanjiangianus

Astragalus nanjiangianus, commonly known as the Sichuan milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its upright growth habit and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It is typically found in mountainous regions and thrives in well-drained soils.

Astragalus nanjiangianus is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential medicinal properties.
