Adenophyllum wrightii var. pulcherrimum

Adenophyllum wrightii var. pulcherrimum, commonly known as the showy adenophyllum, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

It is characterized by its striking, showy inflorescences that consist of clusters of small, pink to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a slightly hairy texture.
