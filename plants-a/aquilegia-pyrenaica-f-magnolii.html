Aquilegia pyrenaica f. magnolii

Aquilegia pyrenaica f. magnolii, commonly known as the Pyrenean columbine, is a hybrid form of the Pyrenean columbine, characterized by its striking, showy flowers.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is native to the Pyrenees region of Europe. The plant features compound, palmate leaves and blooms with spurred, pentagonal flowers in shades of pink to red. It thrives in woodland and alpine environments, preferring well-drained, loamy soils.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and botanical collections.
