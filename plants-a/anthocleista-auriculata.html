Anthocleista auriculata

Anthocleista auriculata, commonly known as the African bellflower, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, bell-shaped yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a waxy texture.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
