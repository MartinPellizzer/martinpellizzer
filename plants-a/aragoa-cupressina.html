Aragoa cupressina

Aragoa cupressina, commonly known as the Mexican cypress, is a species of coniferous evergreen tree native to Mexico.

It belongs to the family Cupressaceae and is characterized by its slender, conical shape and scale-like leaves. The plant produces small, spherical cones that contain seeds, typical of many conifer species. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for cultivation in arid and semi-arid regions.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often used in landscaping and horticulture.
