Aphelandra darienensis

Aphelandra darienensis, commonly known as the Darien torch plant, is a striking tropical species native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its long, pendant inflorescences that bear vibrant, often yellow or orange flowers. The plant features dark green, elliptical leaves with a smooth texture and a glossy appearance. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.

Aphelandra darienensis is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.
