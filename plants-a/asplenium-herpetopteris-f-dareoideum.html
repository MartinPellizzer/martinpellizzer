Asplenium herpetopteris f. dareoideum

Asplenium herpetopteris f. dareoideum, commonly known as the Dareoideum spleenwort, is a fern belonging to the family Aspleniaceae.

It is characterized by its small, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and have a leathery texture. The plant exhibits a unique morphology with fertile fronds that produce spores on the underside, distinguishing it from sterile fronds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in rock crevices or on tree trunks in tropical and subtropical regions.

This fern is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
