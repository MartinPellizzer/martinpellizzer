Athanasia leucoclada

Athanasia leucoclada, commonly known as the white-flowered everlasting.

It is a flowering plant native to South Africa, characterized by its delicate, white petals and compact, evergreen foliage. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is known for its long-lasting blooms. Athanasia leucoclada thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its resilience and ornamental value have contributed to its cultivation in various garden settings.
