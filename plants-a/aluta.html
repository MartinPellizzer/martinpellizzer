Aluta

Aluta, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers and velvety foliage. Aluta thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
