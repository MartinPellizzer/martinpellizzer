Anonidium brieyi

Anonidium brieyi, commonly known as the African snowball tree, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the family Anonaceae, which includes species known for their fragrant flowers and medicinal properties. The tree can grow up to 10 meters tall, featuring a broad, rounded crown and smooth, grayish bark. Its flowers are white and clustered in dense, spherical inflorescences, giving it a distinctive appearance.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional medicine for various ailments.
