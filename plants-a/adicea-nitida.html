Adicea nitida

Adicea nitida, commonly known as the silverleaf daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and showy white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. It produces small, cylindrical fruits that contain several seeds.

Adicea nitida is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
