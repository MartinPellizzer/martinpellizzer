Aloe macracantha

Aloe macracantha, commonly known as the giant aloe or Cape aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, spiny stems that can grow up to 3 meters in height, with thick, fleshy leaves that are green with white spots. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with small, yellowish flowers. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its leaves and stems.

Aloe macracantha is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional medicinal uses.
