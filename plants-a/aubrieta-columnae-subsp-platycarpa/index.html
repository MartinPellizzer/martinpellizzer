Aubrieta Columnae Subsp. Platycarpa

Aubrieta columnae subsp. platycarpa is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Tibetan Plateau. It belongs to the mustard family, known for its small, bright flowers that bloom in spring. This subspecies is distinguished by its compact growth habit and unique floral structure. Due to its limited distribution and fragile habitat, it is considered a conservation concern. Its beauty and ecological significance make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists alike.
