Aloe multifaria

Aloe multifaria, commonly known as the narrow-leaf aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features slender, upright stems with fleshy, narrow leaves that are typically green with a slight blue tint. The plant produces small, yellow flowers on spikes during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Aloe multifaria is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional medicinal uses.
