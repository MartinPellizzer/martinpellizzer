Ayenia scalpellata

Ayenia scalpellata, commonly known as the silverleaf ayenia, is a perennial shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces small, white to pale pink flowers in clusters during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are alternate and elliptical, often with a slightly serrated edge.

Ayenia scalpellata thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in woodland edges and rocky habitats.
