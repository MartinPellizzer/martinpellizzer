Anatherum Ligulatum

Anatherum ligulatum is a lesser-known grass species native to parts of Asia and Africa. It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and small, inconspicuous flowers. Despite its modest appearance, it plays a vital role in its native ecosystems. The plant is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. Its resilience and adaptability make it an interesting subject for botanical study.
