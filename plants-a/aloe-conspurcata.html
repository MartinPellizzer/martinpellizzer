Aloe conspurcata

Aloe conspurcata, commonly known as the spotted aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with distinctive white or yellow spots. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in summer, adding ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Aloe conspurcata is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal and drought tolerance.
