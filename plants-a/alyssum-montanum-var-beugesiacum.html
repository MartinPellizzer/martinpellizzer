Alyssum montanum var. beugesiacum

Alyssum montanum var. beugesiacum, commonly known as the Beuge's alpine alyssum, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically forms dense mats of foliage. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white to pale pink, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It prefers well-drained, rocky soils and is often found in alpine and subalpine habitats.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to thrive in harsh, high-altitude environments.
