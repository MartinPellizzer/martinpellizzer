Agrostis clavata f. flaccida

Agrostis clavata f. flaccida, commonly known as the flaccid bentgrass, is a variant of the bentgrass species native to temperate regions.

It is a low-growing, mat-forming grass with narrow, grass-like leaves that are often bluish-green in color. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences containing numerous tiny flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows, lawns, and along stream banks.

Its botanical features make it a valuable plant for ecological studies and ornamental gardening.
