Amaralia millenii

Amaralia millenii, commonly known as the Millennial amaryllis, is a striking flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its tall, sturdy stems and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers. The plant produces clusters of vibrant pink or white blooms during the summer months. Its foliage consists of broad, strap-like leaves that are evergreen and persist through much of the year.

Amaralia millenii is prized for its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements in suitable climates.
