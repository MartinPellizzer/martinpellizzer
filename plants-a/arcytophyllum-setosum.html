Arcytophyllum setosum

Arcytophyllum setosum, commonly known as the Sierra Nevada sage.

It is a perennial shrub native to the western United States, particularly found in high elevation areas of California and Nevada. The plant is characterized by its aromatic, silver-gray foliage and small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in late spring. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to harsh, alpine conditions.

Arcytophyllum setosum plays an important role in its ecosystem by providing habitat and food for various insects and small animals.
