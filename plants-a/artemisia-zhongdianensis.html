Artemisia zhongdianensis

Artemisia zhongdianensis, commonly known as the Zhongdian wormwood, is a flowering plant species native to the high-altitude regions of China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, silver-green foliage. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in compact clusters, typical of the genus Artemisia. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in alpine meadows and rocky slopes.

Due to its unique habitat and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
