Alniphyllum fortunei var. megaphyllum

Alniphyllum fortunei var. megaphyllum, commonly known as the giant alniphyllum, is a rare and distinctive plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

It is characterized by its large, leathery leaves that can grow up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant produces small, white flowers arranged in clusters, which are typically followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in shaded forest understories.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a valuable species for botanical study and conservation efforts.
