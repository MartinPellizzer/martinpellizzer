Acaena tehuelcha

Acaena tehuelcha, commonly known as the Chilean burr bush, is a shrub native to the Andes Mountains of southern South America.

It belongs to the family Rosaceae and is characterized by its woody stems and dense, spiny growth. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are followed by spherical, prickly fruits containing seeds. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a rough texture.
