Aleuritia imperialis

Aleuritia imperialis, commonly known as the imperial aleurita, is a rare flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, clustered white flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant has oval-shaped leaves with a smooth texture and a slightly waxy surface. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in shaded forest understories.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species.
