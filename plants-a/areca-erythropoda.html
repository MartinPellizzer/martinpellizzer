Areca erythropoda

Areca erythropoda, commonly known as the red areca palm, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and broad, feather-like leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that eventually develop into edible fruits. Its botanical features make it well-suited for warm, humid climates and well-drained soils.

It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes and in some regions for its fruit.
