Anthurium frontinoense

Anthurium frontinoense, commonly known as the Frontinoense Arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its unique inflorescence consisting of a spadix and a spathe. The plant features large, heart-shaped leaves with a glossy green surface and prominent veins. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Anthurium frontinoense is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated as a houseplant.
