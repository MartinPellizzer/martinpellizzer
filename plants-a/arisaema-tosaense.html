Arisaema tosaense

Arisaema tosaense, commonly known as the Tosa arrowhead, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its distinctive arrowhead-shaped leaves. The plant produces a spadix surrounded by a spathe, typical of many arum species. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Arisaema tosaense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
