Artemisia ghoratensis

Artemisia ghoratensis, commonly known as the Ghorat wormwood, is a perennial shrub native to the arid regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, silver-gray foliage. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are typically clustered in dense, spherical heads. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to dry, harsh environments.

Artemisia ghoratensis is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
