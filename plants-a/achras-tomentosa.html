Achras tomentosa

Achras tomentosa, commonly known as the cannonball tree, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, spherical yellow flowers that resemble cannonballs, which bloom in clusters. The plant produces heavy, woody fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The leaves are broad, palmate, and covered in a dense, woolly layer of fine hairs.
