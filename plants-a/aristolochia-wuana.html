Aristolochia wuana

Aristolochia wuana, commonly known as the Chinese birthwort, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Aristolochiaceae family and is characterized by its unique, heart-shaped leaves and striking, pipe-shaped flowers. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. Its vines can grow up to several meters in length, often climbing or trailing along supports.

The plant contains various bioactive compounds, making it of interest in traditional medicine.
