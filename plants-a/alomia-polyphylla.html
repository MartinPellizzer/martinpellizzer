Alomia polyphylla

Alomia polyphylla, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its velvety, heart-shaped leaves and clusters of showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows as a perennial herb, reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters. It prefers well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular houseplant.

Alomia polyphylla is valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of care in indoor environments.
