Arabis perelegans

Arabis perelegans, commonly known as the alpine rockcress, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other mustard family plants. Its small, white or yellowish flowers bloom in early spring, and it produces small, rounded capsules that contain numerous seeds.

Arabis perelegans is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in rock gardens or alpine settings.
