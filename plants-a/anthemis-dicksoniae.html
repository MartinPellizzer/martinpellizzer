Anthemis dicksoniae

Anthemis dicksoniae, commonly known as the New Zealand everlasting, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit. The plant produces small, yellowish-orange flowers that are often arranged in dense clusters. Its leaves are typically narrow and covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss in its native environment.

Anthemis dicksoniae is adapted to grow in rocky or coastal areas, showcasing its resilience to harsh conditions.
