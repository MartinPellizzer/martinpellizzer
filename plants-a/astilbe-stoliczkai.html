Astilbe stoliczkai

Astilbe stoliczkai, commonly known as the Chinese astilbe, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It features upright, branched stems that bear clusters of small, lace-like flowers in shades of pink, red, or white. The plant has lance-shaped, dark green leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in partial to full shade and moist, well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens.

Astilbe stoliczkai is valued for its ornamental foliage and delicate floral display.
