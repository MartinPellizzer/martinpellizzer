Abelia brachystemon

Abelia brachystemon, commonly known as Chinese abelia, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale pink flowers in the summer months. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained soil.

Abelia brachystemon is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various garden conditions.
