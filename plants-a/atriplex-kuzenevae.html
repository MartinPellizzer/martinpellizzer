Atriplex kuzenevae

Atriplex kuzenevae, commonly known as Kuzenev's saltbush, is a shrub native to the arid regions of central Asia.

It is characterized by its succulent, lobed leaves that are adapted to reduce water loss in harsh environments. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in saline and alkaline soils, making it a valuable species for reclaiming degraded lands.

Its ability to tolerate extreme drought and high salinity makes it an important plant in ecological restoration projects.
