Anthyllis vulneraria subvar. albiflora

Anthyllis vulneraria subvar. albiflora, commonly known as the white-flowered heal-all, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and western Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its small, white or pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. It has trifoliate leaves and produces pods that contain several seeds.

This subvariety is distinguished by its more prominent white flowers compared to other varieties of Anthyllis vulneraria.
