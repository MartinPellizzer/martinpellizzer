Alchornea brevistyla

Alchornea brevistyla, commonly known as the short-styled alchornea, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa and the Americas.

Its small, yellowish flowers are clustered in cup-like structures called cyathia, a characteristic feature of the Euphorbiaceae family.
