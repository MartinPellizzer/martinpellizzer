Athyrium xiangxiense

Athyrium xiangxiense, commonly known as the Xiangxi fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to China.

It belongs to the family Athyriaceae and is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped fronds with a distinctive, finely divided structure. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as mountain forests and rocky slopes. Its rhizomes are often covered in a dense, velvety layer of brownish hairs.

Athyrium xiangxiense is valued for its ornamental foliage and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
