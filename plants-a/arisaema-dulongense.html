Arisaema dulongense

Arisaema dulongense, commonly known as the Dulong's arisaema, is a herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Yunnan, China.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its unique inflorescence consisting of a spadix surrounded by a spathe. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are large, heart-shaped, and have a rough texture, while the spadix bears small, inconspicuous flowers.

Arisaema dulongense is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
