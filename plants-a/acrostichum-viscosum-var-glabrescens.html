Acrostichum viscosum var. glabrescens

Acrostichum viscosum var. glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth leather fern, is a variant of the common leather fern.

It is a terrestrial fern that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands. The plant features a creeping rhizome with dark green, leathery fronds that are usually unbranched. Its leaves are characterized by their smooth texture and lack of hairy structures, which distinguishes it from other varieties.

This fern is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in horticultural settings.
