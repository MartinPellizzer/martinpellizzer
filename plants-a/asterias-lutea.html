Asterias lutea

Asterias lutea, commonly known as the yellow starwort, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its bright yellow ray florets and a central disk of smaller flowers, typical of the daisy family. The plant typically grows in moist, often calcareous soils and is found in meadows and along stream banks. It produces small, oval-shaped fruits called cypselae, which are dispersed by wind.

Asterias lutea is a herbaceous perennial that blooms from late spring to early autumn.
