Aconitum hemsleyanum var. hemsleyanum

Aconitum hemsleyanum var. hemsleyanum, commonly known as the Hemsley's monkshood, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Himalayas.

It features striking purple to blue, hood-shaped flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has compound leaves with finely divided lobes, giving it a fern-like appearance. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Due to its toxicity, it should be handled with care.
