Asplenium pycnocarpon

Asplenium pycnocarpon, commonly known as the densefruit spleenwort, is a species of fern belonging to the family Aspleniaceae.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that typically grows on rocks or trees in humid, shaded environments. The plant has lance-shaped, serrated leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. Its reproductive structures, called sporangia, are clustered in dense, rounded formations on the underside of the leaves.

Asplenium pycnocarpon is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, where it thrives in high humidity and moderate temperatures.
