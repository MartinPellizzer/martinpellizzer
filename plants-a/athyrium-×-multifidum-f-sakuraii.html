Athyrium × multifidum f. sakuraii

Athyrium × multifidum f. sakuraii, commonly known as the Sakurai's Japanese shield fern, is a hybrid fern species native to Japan.

It is characterized by its finely divided, feather-like fronds that give it a delicate and ornamental appearance. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its foliage is often a vibrant green, with some cultivars displaying variegated patterns.

This fern is prized in horticulture for its aesthetic appeal and ability to thrive in partial shade.
