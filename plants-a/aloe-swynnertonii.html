Aloe swynnertonii

Aloe swynnertonii, commonly known as the Swynnerton's aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, spindly stems and clusters of narrow, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces yellow flowers during the summer months, which are arranged in a raceme. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Aloe swynnertonii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
