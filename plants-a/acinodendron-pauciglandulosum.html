Acinodendron pauciglandulosum

Acinodendron pauciglandulosum, commonly known as the sparse-glandular acinodendron, is a small evergreen shrub native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions. The plant features small, elliptical leaves with a glossy green surface and sparse glandular dots on the underside. Its flowers are small, white or pale yellow, and are arranged in clusters.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its compact form and attractive foliage.
