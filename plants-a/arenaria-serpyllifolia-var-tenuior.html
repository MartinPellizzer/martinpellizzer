Arenaria serpyllifolia var. tenuior

Arenaria serpyllifolia var. tenuior, commonly known as narrowleaf sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

It typically features slender, grass-like leaves and produces small, white or pink flowers in clusters. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to stabilize soil in marginal environments.

Its botanical characteristics make it a hardy and adaptable species suitable for rock gardens and alpine settings.
