Actinidia henanensis

Actinidia henanensis, commonly known as Henan kiwifruit, is a deciduous vine native to China.

It belongs to the Actinidiaceae family and is closely related to the kiwifruit species. The plant produces small, hairy, green berries that are typically harvested in late summer. Its leaves are heart-shaped with a serrated margin and are often covered in fine hairs.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and its fruit, which is used in traditional Chinese medicine.
