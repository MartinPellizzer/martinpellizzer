Aristolochia schubertioides

Aristolochia schubertioides, commonly known as the Schubert's Dutchman's pipe, is a climbing vine native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive, pipe-shaped flowers that are often greenish or reddish in color. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with a rough texture and is known for its strong, aromatic scent. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

The genus Aristolochia includes many species with similar morphological features and ecological roles.
