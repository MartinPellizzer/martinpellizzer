Argyrolobium pauciflorum

Argyrolobium pauciflorum, commonly known as the silver globular pea, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its globular, silver-green foliage and small, pale yellow flowers. The plant belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and it has a low-growing, mat-forming habit. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy environments.

Due to its ornamental appeal, it is cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
