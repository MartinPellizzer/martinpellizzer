Alstroemeria stenopetala

Alstroemeria stenopetala, commonly known as the narrow-petaled alstroemeria, is a species native to South America, particularly Chile and Argentina.

It is a flowering plant belonging to the family Alstroemeriaceae, characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that come in a variety of colors such as red, orange, and yellow. The plant typically grows as a herbaceous perennial, with strap-like leaves and a compact growth habit.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and floral arrangements.
