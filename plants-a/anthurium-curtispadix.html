Anthurium curtispadix

Anthurium curtispadix, commonly known as the narrow-leaf anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its elongated, narrow leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces striking spadices, which are the inflorescences that consist of a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe. Anthurium curtispadix thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

It is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
