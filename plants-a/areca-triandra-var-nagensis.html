Areca triandra var. nagensis

Areca triandra var. nagensis, commonly known as the Nagi areca, is a tropical palm native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, upright trunk and pinnately compound leaves that are typically bright green. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by elliptical, blackish fruits containing a single seed. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The variety is noted for its resilience and adaptability to various soil types.
