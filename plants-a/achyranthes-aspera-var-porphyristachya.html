Achyranthes aspera var. porphyristachya

Achyranthes aspera var. porphyristachya, commonly known as red sandalwood, is a variant of the species Achyranthes aspera, which belongs to the Amaranthaceae family.

It is characterized by its upright, branched stems that are typically reddish in color, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, greenish flowers arranged in dense, spike-like inflorescences, which are often surrounded by red bracts. Its leaves are lanceolate, with a rough texture due to the presence of fine hairs.

This variant is native to regions of India and is often found in dry, rocky habitats.
