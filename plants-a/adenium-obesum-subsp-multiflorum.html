Adenium obesum subsp. multiflorum

Adenium obesum subsp.

multiflorum, commonly known as the Desert Trumpet, is a succulent shrub native to arid regions of Africa and the Arabian Peninsula. It is characterized by its thick, swollen trunk and thick, waxy leaves that help it store water.

The plant produces striking red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as bees and birds. Its root system is deep and robust, allowing it to survive in nutrient-poor soils.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
