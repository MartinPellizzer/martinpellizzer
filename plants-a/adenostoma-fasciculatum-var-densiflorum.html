Adenostoma fasciculatum var. densiflorum

Adenostoma fasciculatum var. densiflorum, commonly known as the denseflower woolly bush, is a shrub native to the coastal regions of California.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that provides insulation against the harsh coastal climate. The plant produces small, pink to reddish flowers in clusters, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to full sun exposure.

This variety is often found in coastal scrub habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
