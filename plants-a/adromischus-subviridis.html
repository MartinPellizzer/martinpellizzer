Adromischus subviridis

Adromischus subviridis, commonly known as the Jade Necklace, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, light green, oval-shaped leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with plenty of sunlight.

Its compact size and attractive foliage make it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
