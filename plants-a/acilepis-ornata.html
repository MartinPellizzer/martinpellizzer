Acilepis ornata

Acilepis ornata, commonly known as the ornate acilepis, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, yellow, and orange. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy surface, and it thrives in well-drained, sandy soils. Acilepis ornata is often found in rocky habitats and is adapted to dry, sunny conditions.
