Adenodolichos rhomboideus var. kundelungensis

Adenodolichos rhomboideus var. kundelungensis, commonly known as the Kundelungensis Dolichos, is a climbing legume native to Central Africa.

It features trifoliate leaves with rhomboidal leaflets and produces small, yellow, pea-like flowers. The plant bears pods that contain several elliptical, flat seeds. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in moist, shaded environments.

Its botanical characteristics make it a subject of interest in both ecological and agricultural studies.
