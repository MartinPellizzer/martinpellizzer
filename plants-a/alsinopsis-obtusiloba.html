Alsinopsis obtusiloba

Alsinopsis obtusiloba, commonly known as the narrow-leaved alsin, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid environments.
