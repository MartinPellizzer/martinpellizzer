Anaphalis tenuissima

Anaphalis tenuissima, commonly known as the slender woollyweed, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central and South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Anaphalis tenuissima thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in cloud forests.
