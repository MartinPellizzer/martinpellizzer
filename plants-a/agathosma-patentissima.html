Agathosma patentissima

Agathosma patentissima, commonly known as the Cape geranium, is a small, evergreen shrub native to the Western Cape region of South Africa.

It features compact, aromatic foliage with small, rounded leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of tiny, white to pale pink flowers during the summer months. It is well adapted to dry, sandy soils and is drought-tolerant, making it suitable for rocky or well-drained gardens.

Agathosma patentissima is also valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
