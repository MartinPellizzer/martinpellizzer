Actinostemon glabrescens var. angustifolius

Actinostemon glabrescens var. angustifolius, commonly known as the narrow-leaf actinostemon, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features upright, branched stems covered in soft, hairy tissue that become smoother with age. The leaves are lance-shaped, with a narrow, pointed tip and a smooth margin. It produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pale green to yellow in color.

This plant is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.
