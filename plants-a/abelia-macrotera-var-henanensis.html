Abelia macrotera var. henanensis

Abelia macrotera var. henanensis, commonly known as the Henan abelia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features showy, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in late summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green, providing a striking contrast to its floral display. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a hardy addition to gardens.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various growing conditions.
