Androsace sarmentosa var. stenophylla

Androsace sarmentosa var. stenophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leaved sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to alpine and subalpine regions of Europe.

It features a mat-forming growth habit with small, fleshy leaves that are typically lance-shaped and densely packed. The plant produces clusters of white or pink flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is often found in mountainous environments.

Its botanical characteristics make it well adapted to harsh, cold climates.
