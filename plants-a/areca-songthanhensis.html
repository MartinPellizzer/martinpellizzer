Areca songthanhensis

Areca songthanhensis, commonly known as the Vietnamese areca palm, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Arecaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and feathery fronds. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellowish-green in color. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Areca songthanhensis is also valued for its potential medicinal properties in traditional medicine.
