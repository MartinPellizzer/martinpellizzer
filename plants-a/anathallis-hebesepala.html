Anathallis hebesepala

Anathallis hebesepala, commonly known as the pink spider orchid, is a species of orchid native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It is characterized by its unique floral structure, featuring a slender, spider-like flower with a distinctive pinkish hue. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments within tropical rainforests. Its flowers are pollinated by specific species of flies, showcasing a specialized mutualistic relationship.

Anathallis hebesepala is valued for its ornamental appeal and contributes to the biodiversity of its native ecosystems.
