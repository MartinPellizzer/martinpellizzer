Albuca pentheri

Albuca pentheri, commonly known as the Natal grass or Natal lily, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces tall, slender flower stalks that bear clusters of white or pale yellow flowers. The plant is characterized by its ornamental value, with its elegant appearance making it a popular choice for gardens. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Albuca pentheri is also noted for its ability to tolerate drought conditions once established.
