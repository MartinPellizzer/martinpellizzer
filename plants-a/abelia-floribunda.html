Abelia floribunda

Abelia floribunda, commonly known as the Chinese abelia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy on top, with a lighter underside. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, tubular flowers in shades of white, pink, and red, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Abelia floribunda thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained soil.

It is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
