Armeria campestris var. hoffmannii

Armeria campestris var. hoffmannii, commonly known as Hoffmann's thrift, is a low-growing, evergreen perennial that produces dense clusters of white or pink flowers.

It belongs to the carnation family (Caryophyllaceae) and is native to the Mediterranean region. The plant features wiry, grass-like leaves and is often used in rock gardens or as a border plant due to its hardiness and ornamental value.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for coastal and arid climates.
