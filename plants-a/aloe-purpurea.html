Aloe purpurea

Aloe purpurea, commonly known as the purple aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color with a distinctive purple hue along the edges. The plant produces striking, orange-red flowers that bloom in summer, attracting pollinators like bees and birds. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its leaves and requiring minimal irrigation.

Aloe purpurea is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional medicinal uses.
