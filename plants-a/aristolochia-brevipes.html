Aristolochia brevipes

Aristolochia brevipes, commonly known as the short-stemmed Dutchman's pipe, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive, heart-shaped leaves and its unique, pipe-like flowers that are often greenish with a purple or brownish hue. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is cultivated in some botanical gardens and herbal practices.
