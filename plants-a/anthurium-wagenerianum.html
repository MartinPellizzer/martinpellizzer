Anthurium wagenerianum

Anthurium wagenerianum, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces vibrant red spathes that enclose its yellowish spadix, which is the actual flowering structure. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium wagenerianum is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
