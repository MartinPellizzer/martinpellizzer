Achyranthes decumbens

Achyranthes decumbens, commonly known as the prostrate smartweed, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It typically grows in moist soils and can be found in wetlands, marshes, and along stream banks. The plant has a sprawling growth habit with prostrate stems that root at the nodes. Its leaves are ovate to lanceolate, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

The flowers are small, greenish-white, and clustered in dense, terminal spikes.
