Astragalus konyaensis

Astragalus konyaensis, commonly known as Konya milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to Turkey.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its erect, branched stems and small, pea-like flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid conditions. Its root system is deep, allowing it to access moisture from deeper soil layers.

Astragalus konyaensis is also known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
