Arenaria nivalomontana

Arenaria nivalomontana, commonly known as the alpine sandwort, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

It thrives in high-altitude environments, often found in alpine meadows and rocky slopes. The plant produces small, white or pink flowers that bloom in early summer. Its leaves are typically succulent and adapted to retain moisture in cold, arid conditions.

Arenaria nivalomontana is valued for its resilience and is often used in rock gardens and alpine plantings.
