Acer platanoides var. heterophyllum-variegatum

Acer platanoides var. heterophyllum-variegatum, commonly known as the variegated Norway maple, is a variant of the Norway maple with distinctive foliage.

It features leaves that are typically lobed and have a striking variegation pattern of green and white or yellow. The plant is native to Europe and has been introduced to other parts of the world. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Its ornamental value comes from its unique leaf coloration and its ability to grow in a variety of environmental conditions.
