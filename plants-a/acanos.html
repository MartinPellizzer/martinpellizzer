Acanos

Acanos, commonly known as the silverleaf tree, is a tropical plant native to parts of Africa and the Caribbean.

It is characterized by its dense, silvery-white foliage that gives it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting various pollinators. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

The acanos thrives in humid environments and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its striking appearance.
