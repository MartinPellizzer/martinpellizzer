Arum detruncatum var. caudatum

Arum detruncatum var. caudatum, commonly known as the caudate arum, is a striking tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It features large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with prominent veins. The plant produces a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe, which is characteristic of the Araceae family. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

This variant is prized for its ornamental value in both indoor and outdoor gardens.
