Ancylostemon humilis

Ancylostemon humilis, commonly known as the dwarf banyan, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, rounded leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in panicles. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in humid, tropical climates and is often found in lowland areas. Its roots can form aerial roots that help support the tree as it grows.

The plant has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
