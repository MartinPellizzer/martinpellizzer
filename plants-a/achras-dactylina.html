Achras dactylina

Achras dactylina, commonly known as the Natal date palm, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a tall, slender tree that can grow up to 20 meters in height, with a straight trunk and a crown of pinnate leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that develop into edible, sweet fruits resembling dates. Its leaves are long and feathery, providing a distinctive appearance.

The Natal date palm is adapted to tropical and subtropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and fruit production.
