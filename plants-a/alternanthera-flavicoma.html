Alternanthera flavicoma

Alternanthera flavicoma, commonly known as golden flame or red flame, is a tropical perennial plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful foliage that ranges from red to orange and yellow, making it a striking ornamental plant. The leaves are typically opposite, ovate, and have a smooth texture with a waxy coating. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light to maintain its vivid coloration.

This plant is often used in water gardens and as a houseplant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
