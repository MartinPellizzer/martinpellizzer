Alstroemeria oculata

Alstroemeria oculata, commonly known as the yellow alstroemeria, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Alstroemeriaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant typically grows in a clump-forming habit, with grass-like foliage and showy blooms that attract pollinators. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Alstroemeria oculata is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and floral arrangements.
