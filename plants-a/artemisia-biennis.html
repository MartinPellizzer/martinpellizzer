Artemisia biennis

Artemisia biennis, commonly known as annual wormwood, is a flowering plant in the sunflower family.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia and is characterized by its aromatic, silver-gray foliage. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in late summer. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in dry, sunny conditions.

Artemisia biennis is used in traditional medicine and has aromatic properties that make it useful in herbal remedies.
