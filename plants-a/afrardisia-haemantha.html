Afrardisia haemantha

Afrardisia haemantha, commonly known as the red-flowered African bell, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features striking red, tubular flowers that bloom in late summer, attracting pollinators like birds and insects. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant color and unique floral structure.
