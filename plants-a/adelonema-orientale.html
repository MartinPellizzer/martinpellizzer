Adelonema orientale

Adelonema orientale, commonly known as the Chinese bellflower, is a herbaceous perennial native to East Asia.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has slender, hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full sunlight.

Adelonema orientale is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens and landscapes.
