Anthocercis anisantha subsp. anisantha

Anthocercis anisantha subsp.

anisantha, commonly known as the purple tansy, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region. It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that are clustered in dense, spherical inflorescences.

The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and thrives in well-drained soils. It is often found in dry, open habitats such as rocky slopes and roadsides.

The species is known for its aromatic properties and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
