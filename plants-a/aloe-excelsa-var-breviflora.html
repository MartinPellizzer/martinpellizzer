Aloe excelsa var. breviflora

Aloe excelsa var. breviflora, commonly known as the dwarf African aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, upright leaves that are typically green with white or yellowish markings. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing yellowish-green flowers. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil with full sun exposure.

Its compact size and ornamental appearance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
