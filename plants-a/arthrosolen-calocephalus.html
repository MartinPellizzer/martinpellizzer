Arthrosolen calocephalus

Arthrosolen calocephalus, commonly known as the rock orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It features a compact, cushion-like growth habit with dense clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. The plant has a fibrous root system and produces narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.

The flowers are typically short-lived, blooming in late spring or early summer.
