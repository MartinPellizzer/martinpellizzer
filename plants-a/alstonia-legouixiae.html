Alstonia legouixiae

Alstonia legouixiae, commonly known as the Madagascar tree or the African star tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the family Apocynaceae and is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and compound leaves with three to five leaflets. The plant produces clusters of small, white, fragrant flowers followed by orange-red berries containing seeds.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional medicine for its potential medicinal properties.
