Asplenium radicans var. partita

Asplenium radicans var. partita, commonly known as the Split-leaf Nephrolepis, is a variant of the common spider plant.

It is a fern characterized by its distinctive, divided fronds that give it a unique, ornamental appearance. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments and is native to parts of Asia. Its leaves are often variegated with shades of green and silver, adding visual interest to gardens or indoor spaces.

It is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for both novice and experienced plant enthusiasts.
