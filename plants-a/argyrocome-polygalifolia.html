Argyrocome polygalifolia

Argyrocome polygalifolia, commonly known as the silverleaf mistflower, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is a member of the Polygalaceae family and is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are opposite and lance-shaped, while its flowers are small and clustered in panicles.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
