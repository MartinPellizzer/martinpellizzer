Abutilon bakeri

Abutilon bakeri, commonly known as the velvetleaf abutilon, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa and the Middle East.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 2 to 5 meters. The plant features soft, hairy leaves that are usually heart-shaped or ovate in form. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, and they bloom in clusters during the summer months.
