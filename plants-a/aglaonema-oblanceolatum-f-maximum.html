Aglaonema oblanceolatum f. maximum

Aglaonema oblanceolatum f. maximum, commonly known as the Chinese Evergreen, is a popular ornamental plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated foliage with deep green and cream or white markings. The plant thrives in low-light conditions and prefers indirect sunlight, making it ideal for indoor cultivation. Its leaves are typically ovate to elliptical in shape, with a smooth texture and a glossy appearance.

Aglaonema oblanceolatum f. maximum is known for its air-purifying qualities and ability to thrive with minimal care.
