Anthurium rothschildianum

Anthurium rothschildianum, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped spathe that ranges from deep red to orange in color, often with a contrasting green spadix. The plant thrives in humid, warm environments with bright, indirect light and well-draining soil. Its foliage consists of large, glossy, dark green leaves that add to its ornamental appeal.

Anthurium rothschildianum is popular as a houseplant due to its aesthetic value and relatively easy care requirements.
