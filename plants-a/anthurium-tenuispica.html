Anthurium tenuispica

Anthurium tenuispica, commonly known as the narrow-leaved anthurium, is a striking tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its unique inflorescence consisting of a spadix and a spathe. The plant features elongated, narrow leaves that are typically bright green with a glossy texture. Its flowers, though small, are often surrounded by a colorful spathe that can range from white to red.

Anthurium tenuispica is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in humid, well-lit environments.
