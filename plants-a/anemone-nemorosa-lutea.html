Anemone nemorosa-lutea

Anemone nemorosa-lutea, commonly known as the yellow wood anemone, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in woodland areas and thrives in partial shade with moist, well-drained soil. The plant features showy, cup-shaped flowers that are usually bright yellow or white, blooming in early spring. Its leaves are palmately divided, with a delicate, fern-like appearance.

The plant is known for its early blooming habit and is often found in forested regions across its native range.
