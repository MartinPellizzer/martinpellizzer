Angiopteris pallescens

Angiopteris pallescens, commonly known as the pale fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, lance-shaped fronds that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant has a distinctive, delicate appearance with a light green color and a feathery texture. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Angiopteris pallescens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
