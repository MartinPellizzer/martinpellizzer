Anthurium kunthianum

Anthurium kunthianum, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves and vibrant red spadix. The plant produces clusters of small, inconspicuous flowers surrounded by a colorful spathe. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium kunthianum is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
