Aesculus × Neglecta F. Erythroclados

Red-leaf horse chestnut, scientifically known as Aesculus × neglecta f. erythroclados, is a striking ornamental tree prized for its vibrant crimson foliage. This unique cultivar of the horse chestnut tree showcases deep red leaves that transform the landscape during the growing season. Native to the United States, it thrives in temperate climates and is often planted in gardens and public spaces. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscape designers and homeowners alike. Despite its beauty, it requires well-drained soil and partial shade to flourish.
