Anthurium ixtenamense

Anthurium ixtenamense, commonly known as the Guatemalan anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves and vibrant, spadix-like inflorescence. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in the understory of tropical forests. Its foliage is dark green with a glossy texture, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers surrounded by a colorful spathe.

Anthurium ixtenamense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant.
