Adenophora xiaoxiensis

Adenophora xiaoxiensis, commonly known as the small-flowered bellflower, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped, pale blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are opposite, lanceolate, and covered with fine hairs.

Adenophora xiaoxiensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
