Antimima pusilla

Antimima pusilla, commonly known as the small antimima, is a small flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and prefers full sun exposure. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are usually white or pale pink in color. It has a compact growth habit with short, hairy stems and small, narrow leaves.

Antimima pusilla is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in dry conditions.
