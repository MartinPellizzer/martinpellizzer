Arachnorchis splendens

Arachnorchis splendens, commonly known as the Splendid Spider Orchid, is an epiphytic orchid native to Australia.

It is characterized by its striking, spider-like appearance with long, slender petals and a distinctive, elongated spur. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers are usually a mix of white, green, and yellow, with a strong, sweet fragrance.

Arachnorchis splendens is a rare and protected species, requiring specific conditions to thrive.
