Amphineuron tonkinense

Amphineuron tonkinense, commonly known as the Tonkin sandmat, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its prostrate growth habit and hairy stems. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are ovate to lanceolate in shape and often covered with fine pubescence.

It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.
