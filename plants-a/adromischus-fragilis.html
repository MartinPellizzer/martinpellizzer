Adromischus fragilis

Adromischus fragilis, commonly known as the brittle ice plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, bluish-green leaves that are often tinged with red, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves.

Due to its delicate structure, it requires careful handling to prevent damage.
