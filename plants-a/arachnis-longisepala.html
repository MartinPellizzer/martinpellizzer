Arachnis longisepala

Arachnis longisepala, commonly known as the long-sepaled arachnid, is a species of flowering plant in the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking, elongated sepals that give it its common name. Native to tropical regions of South America, this plant thrives in humid, shaded environments. The flowers are typically white or pale pink, with a distinctive, intricate structure.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and unique floral morphology.
