Anthocleista laurentii

Anthocleista laurentii, commonly known as the African moonseed, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 6 meters in height. The plant produces clusters of small, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are typically alternate, simple, and ovate with a smooth surface.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release seeds, which are dispersed by wind.
