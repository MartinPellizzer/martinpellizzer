Anthemis cretica subsp. albida

Anthemis cretica subsp.

albida, commonly known as white anthemis, is a perennial herb native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads.

The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in dry, sunny habitats such as rocky soils and open fields.

Its botanical structure supports its role in pollination and ecological interactions within its native environment.
