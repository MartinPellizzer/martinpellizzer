Asarum grandiflorum var. colocasiifolium

Asarum grandiflorum var. colocasiifolium, commonly known as the broad-leaf asarum, is a perennial herb native to the eastern United States.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a velvety texture and a distinctive aromatic scent. The plant produces small, reddish-purple flowers in early spring, which are clustered in a spathaceous sheath. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in woodlands and along stream banks.

Its botanical characteristics make it a unique addition to both natural and cultivated landscapes.
