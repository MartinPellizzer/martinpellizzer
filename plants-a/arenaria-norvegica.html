Arenaria norvegica

Arenaria norvegica, commonly known as Norwegian sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to northern Europe.

It typically thrives in sandy or gravelly soils, often found in coastal areas and alpine environments. The plant features small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in early summer. Its leaves are succulent and arranged in a rosette pattern, aiding in water retention.

Arenaria norvegica is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to stabilize soil in harsh conditions.
