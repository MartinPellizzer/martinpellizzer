Argyreia nitida

Argyreia nitida, commonly known as the silver vine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and striking silver-green foliage. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, often followed by violet-colored berries. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as a decorative plant.

Its unique appearance and adaptability make it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor gardens.
