Adesmia pyramidata

Adesmia pyramidata, commonly known as the pyramid tree, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its small, clustered flowers and trifoliate leaves. The plant is typically shrub-like or small tree-like, with a dense, pyramidal shape that gives it its common name. It thrives in tropical and subtropical regions, often found in dry or semi-arid environments.

Adesmia pyramidata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
