Aphelandra dusenii

Aphelandra dusenii, commonly known as the Dusen's flameleaf, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, elongated leaves that often display a mix of green, yellow, and red hues, creating a dramatic visual effect. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, lantern-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in humid, shady environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Aphelandra dusenii is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
