Aechmea coelestis var. albomarginata

Aechmea coelestis var. albomarginata, commonly known as the Sky Plant or White-margin Sky Plant, is a bromeliad native to South America.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that form a central cup to collect rainwater. The leaves are typically blue-green with white margins, giving it a striking appearance. It produces vibrant red or orange flowers that bloom from the center of the rosette.

This plant is well-suited for indoor cultivation and thrives in bright, indirect light.
