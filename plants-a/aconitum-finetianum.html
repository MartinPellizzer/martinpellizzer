Aconitum finetianum

Aconitum finetianum, commonly known as the Swiss Aconite, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the alpine regions of Europe.

It features striking blue to purple flowers that bloom in late summer, typically from July to September. The plant has a tall, erect stem with palmate leaves that are deeply lobed and covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained, moist soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its toxic properties, it should be handled with care.
