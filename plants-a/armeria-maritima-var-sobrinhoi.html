Armeria maritima var. sobrinhoi

Armeria maritima var. sobrinhoi, commonly known as the silverleaf sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to coastal regions of southern California.

It features dense clusters of white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has succulent, silver-gray foliage that is adapted to withstand salty sea air and sandy soils. It thrives in well-drained, sunny conditions and is often found in dune environments.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in stabilizing coastal dunes.
