Acnistus bornmuelleri

Acnistus bornmuelleri, commonly known as the African milkweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, featuring woody stems and alternate, elliptical leaves.
