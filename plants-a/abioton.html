Abioton

Abioton, commonly known as the ghost flower, is a rare and enigmatic plant species native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that bloom only under specific environmental conditions, such as extreme drought or high temperatures. Its leaves are thick and waxy, adapted to minimize water loss in its harsh habitat.
