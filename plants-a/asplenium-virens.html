Asplenium virens

Asplenium virens, commonly known as the green shield fern, is a species of fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Aspleniaceae and is characterized by its compact, evergreen foliage. The fronds are typically leathery and have a glossy appearance, with a dense arrangement of small, elongated pinnae. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The plant's rhizomes are fibrous and help it spread through vegetative propagation.
