Aloe sobolifera

Aloe sobolifera, commonly known as the desert aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow flowers, which attract pollinators like bees and butterflies. Its rhizomatous root system helps it spread and colonize rocky soils.

Aloe sobolifera is valued for its hardy nature and is often used in xeriscaping and traditional medicine.
