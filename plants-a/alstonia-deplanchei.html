Alstonia deplanchei

Alstonia deplanchei, commonly known as the Madagascar tree or Malagasy tree, is a species of flowering plant in the family Apocynaceae.

It is native to Madagascar and is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and compound leaves with opposite arrangement. Alstonia deplanchei is a slow-growing tree that thrives in tropical climates with high humidity.
