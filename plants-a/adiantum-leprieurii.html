Adiantum leprieurii

Adiantum leprieurii, commonly known as the maidenhair fern, is a delicate and elegant fern species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features finely divided, arching fronds that give it a graceful appearance, with each leaflet resembling a slender feather. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, moist soil to flourish. Its rhizomes are often used in horticulture for their ornamental value and ease of propagation.

Adiantum leprieurii is prized for its air-purifying qualities and ability to thrive in indoor settings with proper care.
