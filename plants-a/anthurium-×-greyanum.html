Anthurium × greyanum

Anthurium × greyanum, commonly known as the velvet leaf anthurium, is a hybrid plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically deep green with a velvety texture. The plant produces striking spadices, which are the inflorescences that resemble a cluster of small flowers. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Anthurium × greyanum is popular as a ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
