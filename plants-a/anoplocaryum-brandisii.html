Anoplocaryum brandisii

Anoplocaryum brandisii, commonly known as the African sandmat, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its succulent, fleshy stems and leaves. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is adapted to arid environments. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or yellowish in color.
