Agrostis canina var. alba

Agrostis canina var. alba, commonly known as silver hairgrass, is a perennial grass species native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-gray foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows, pastures, and along stream banks. Its inflorescence consists of slender, spike-like clusters of small, pale green to silver flowers.

Agrostis canina var. alba is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in stabilizing soil in wetland environments.
