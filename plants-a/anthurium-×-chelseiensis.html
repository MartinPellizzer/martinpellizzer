Anthurium × chelseiensis

Anthurium × chelseiensis, commonly known as the pink anthurium, is a hybrid plant resulting from the cross between Anthurium andraeanum and Anthurium scherzerianum.

It is native to Colombia and is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped, velvety leaves that are typically a deep green with a reddish tint. The plant produces showy spathes that range in color from pink to red, making it a popular ornamental species. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Anthurium × chelseiensis is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
