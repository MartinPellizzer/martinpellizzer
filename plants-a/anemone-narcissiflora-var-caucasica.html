Anemone narcissiflora var. caucasica

Anemone narcissiflora var. caucasica, commonly known as the Caucasian anemone, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Caucasus region.

It features showy, cup-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale pink, often with a yellow center. The plant has finely divided, fern-like leaves that are typically bluish-green in appearance. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate blooms.
