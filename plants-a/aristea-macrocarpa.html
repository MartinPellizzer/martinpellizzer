Aristea macrocarpa

Aristea macrocarpa, commonly known as the large-fruited aristea, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit. The plant produces showy, daisy-like flowers with yellow petals and a central disk of tubular florets. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often covered in a fine layer of white hairs.

Aristea macrocarpa is adapted to arid environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
