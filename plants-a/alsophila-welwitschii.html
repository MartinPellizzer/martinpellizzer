Alsophila welwitschii

Alsophila welwitschii, commonly known as the Welwitsch's tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds that can reach several meters in length. The plant has a distinctive crown of fronds that gives it a unique, elegant appearance. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forested areas.

Its slow growth rate and ornamental value make it a popular choice for botanical gardens and conservation efforts.
