Aloe arneodoi

Aloe arneodoi, commonly known as the Cape aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asphodelaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-forming leaves that are fleshy and covered in a white powdery substance. The plant produces tall, branched flower stalks that bear yellowish-green flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Aloe arneodoi is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional uses in herbal medicine.
