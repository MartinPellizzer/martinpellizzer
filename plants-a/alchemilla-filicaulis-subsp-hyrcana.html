Alchemilla filicaulis subsp. hyrcana

Alchemilla filicaulis subsp.

hyrcana, commonly known as the Hyrcan alchemilla, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central Asia. It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit.

The plant features basal leaves that are typically lanceolate to ovate in shape, with a soft, velvety texture. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers in early summer, which are clustered in branched inflorescences.

This subspecies is adapted to rocky and dry habitats, showcasing a high degree of drought tolerance.
