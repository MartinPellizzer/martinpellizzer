Aplotaxis deltoidea

Aplotaxis deltoidea, commonly known as the delta milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows in arid and semi-arid environments. The plant features compound leaves and produces small, yellowish-green flowers in clusters. It is adapted to survive in dry conditions with deep roots and waxy foliage.

The species is sometimes used in traditional medicine and is of interest for its ecological role in its native habitat.
