Aspidistra lurida variegata

Aspidistra lurida variegata, commonly known as the variegated aspidistra, is a striking evergreen perennial native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It features dark green, broad, lance-shaped leaves with distinctive white and yellow variegation patterns. The plant is well-suited for shady environments and thrives in moist, well-drained soil. It is noted for its low maintenance requirements and ability to tolerate a range of indoor conditions.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor landscaping.
