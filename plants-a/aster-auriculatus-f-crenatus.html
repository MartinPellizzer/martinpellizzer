Aster auriculatus f. crenatus

Aster auriculatus f. crenatus, commonly known as the curly leaf aster, is a compact perennial herb native to eastern North America.

It features small, daisy-like flowers with white petals and a yellow center, blooming in late summer to early fall. The plant has a mat-forming growth habit with hairy, lance-shaped leaves that are often curled or crinkled. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
