Aloysia ternifolia

Aloysia ternifolia, commonly known as silver leaf honeysuckle, is a shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and fragrant, tubular flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and its ability to attract pollinators. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Aloysia ternifolia is also used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
