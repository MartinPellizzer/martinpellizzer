Aster juchaihu

Aster juchaihu, commonly known as the Chinese aster, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows to a height of 30 to 60 centimeters, featuring narrow, lance-shaped leaves and vibrant purple or blue flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Aster juchaihu is also valued for its ornamental beauty and use in traditional Chinese medicine.
