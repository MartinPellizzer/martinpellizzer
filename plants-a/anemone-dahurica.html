Anemone dahurica

Anemone dahurica, commonly known as the Siberian anemone, is a herbaceous perennial native to the colder regions of Asia.

It features delicate, white to pinkish flowers that bloom in early spring, often before the leaves emerge. The plant has a short, thick rhizome and lance-shaped, hairy leaves that are typically basal. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

Anemone dahurica is valued in gardens for its early blooming habit and elegant appearance.
