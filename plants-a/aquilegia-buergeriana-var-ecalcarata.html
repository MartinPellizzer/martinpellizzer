Aquilegia buergeriana var. ecalcarata

Aquilegia buergeriana var. ecalcarata, commonly known as the Buerger's columbine, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its striking, spurred petals that range in color from blue to purple. The plant produces showy, clustered flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are palmately compound, with finely divided leaflets, and it thrives in well-drained, rocky soils.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and naturalized areas.
