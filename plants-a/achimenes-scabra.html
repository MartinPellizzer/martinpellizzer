Achimenes scabra

Achimenes scabra, commonly known as the rough achimenes, is a species of flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant has hairy, often rough-textured leaves that are opposite in arrangement. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated as a garden ornamental.

Its flowers bloom throughout the growing season, making it a popular choice for tropical gardens and greenhouses.
