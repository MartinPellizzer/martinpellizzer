Alsine arvernensis

Alsine arvernensis, commonly known as the Arverne sandwort, is a small annual herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils, often in open habitats such as roadsides and disturbed areas. The plant has slender, branched stems and small, pale green to blue-gray leaves. Its flowers are tiny, white or pale blue, and appear in clusters at the tips of the stems.

It is characterized by its ability to thrive in nutrient-poor environments and is often found in areas with low moisture.
