Astragalus yunnanensis var. tatsienensis

Astragalus yunnanensis var. tatsienensis, commonly known as the Yunnan milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant is typically found in alpine meadows and rocky habitats at high altitudes. It plays a role in soil stabilization and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.

Its botanical features make it an important species for ecological studies in its native range.
