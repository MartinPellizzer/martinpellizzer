Alsophila balanocarpa

Alsophila balanocarpa, commonly known as the Balanocarpus hakea, is a species of tree belonging to the family Cyperaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of India, Malaysia, and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and dense, grass-like foliage. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by distinctive, spiky fruits.

Alsophila balanocarpa is often found in moist, well-drained soils in lowland and hill forest areas.
