Avena sterilis subvar. biaristata

Avena sterilis subvar. biaristata, commonly known as the slender oat, is a perennial grass species native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its slender, erect growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open habitats such as rocky slopes and fields.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
