Adromischus cristatus var. schonlandii

Adromischus cristatus var. schonlandii, commonly known as the Schonland's crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, rounded rosettes of fleshy, bluish-green leaves that are covered in a fine layer of white hair. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or red during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to maintain its compact growth.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
