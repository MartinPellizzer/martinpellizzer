Arachnothryx ovata

Arachnothryx ovata, commonly known as the oval-leaf arachnothryx, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the Amazon Basin.

It is characterized by its elongated, ovate leaves that are typically arranged oppositely along the stems. The plant produces small, clustered white flowers that are followed by woody, capsule-like fruits containing several seeds. Arachnothryx ovata is known for its ability to thrive in humid, shaded environments within dense rainforest canopies.

Its botanical features make it an important component of the ecological diversity in its native habitat.
