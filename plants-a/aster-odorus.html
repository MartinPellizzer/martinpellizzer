Aster odorus

Aster odorus, commonly known as sweet everlasting, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its small, daisy-like flowers that bloom in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant is native to North America and thrives in well-drained soils in full sun. Its foliage is aromatic, often described as having a subtle, sweet scent.

Aster odorus is known for its long-lasting flowers, which remain intact even when dried, making it a popular choice for floral arrangements.
