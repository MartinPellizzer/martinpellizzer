Ascyrum linifolium

Ascyrum linifolium, commonly known as the slenderleaf flax, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a member of the Linaceae family, which includes other flax species. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and has narrow, linear leaves that give it its common name. Its small, blue or purple flowers bloom in summer, and it produces capsule fruits containing numerous seeds.

Ascyrum linifolium is valued for its ecological role in supporting pollinators and stabilizing soil in arid environments.
