Aloe formosa

Aloe formosa, commonly known as the "Cape aloe," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features striking, fleshy, sword-shaped leaves that are typically blue-green in color with white tubercles. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with clusters of yellow to orange flowers. Aloe formosa is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional medicinal uses.
