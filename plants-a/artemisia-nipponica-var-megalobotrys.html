Artemisia nipponica var. megalobotrys

Artemisia nipponica var. megalobotrys, commonly known as the Japanese giant wormwood, is a perennial shrub native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its aromatic, silver-gray foliage and slender, branching stems. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in panicles during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a landscape accent.

Its botanical features make it a valuable plant for both ornamental and ecological purposes.
