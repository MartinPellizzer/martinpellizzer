Astridia blanda

Astridia blanda, commonly known as the slender aster, is a herbaceous perennial native to South America.

It features small, daisy-like flowers with white petals and a yellow center, blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has a low-growing habit with hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full sunlight.

Astridia blanda is often used in native gardens for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
