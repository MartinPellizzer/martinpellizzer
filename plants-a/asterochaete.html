Asterochaete

Asterochaete, commonly known as the star-shaped flower plant, is a member of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

It is characterized by its distinctive inflorescence, which consists of a central disk surrounded by ray florets that resemble star points. The plant typically has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, tubular flowers that bloom in clusters. It is native to regions of Africa and is often found in open, sunny habitats.

Asterochaete is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
