Anisocentra

Anisocentra, commonly known as the desert bell, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Apiaceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive umbellate inflorescences and aromatic foliage. The plants are native to arid and semi-arid regions of Africa, the Middle East, and southern Europe. Anisocentra species typically have finely divided leaves and produce small, yellow or white flowers.
