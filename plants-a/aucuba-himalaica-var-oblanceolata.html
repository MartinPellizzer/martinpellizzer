Aucuba himalaica var. oblanceolata

Aucuba himalaica var. oblanceolata, commonly known as the Himalayan aucuba, is a shrub native to the Himalayan region.

It features broad, dark green, elliptical leaves with a glossy texture and prominent veins. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are followed by red, fleshy berries. It thrives in partial shade and is often cultivated for its ornamental foliage.

This variety is valued for its hardiness and ability to tolerate a range of soil conditions.
