Aspidistra coccigera

Aspidistra coccigera, commonly known as the red-veined aspidistra, is a tropical evergreen plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features dark green, broad, lance-shaped leaves with distinctive red veins that add visual interest. The plant has a short, stout rhizome and produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are followed by dark blue berries. It thrives in shady, humid conditions and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Its foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its hardiness and aesthetic appeal.
