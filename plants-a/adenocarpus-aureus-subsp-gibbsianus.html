Adenocarpus aureus subsp. gibbsianus

Adenocarpus aureus subsp.

gibbsianus, commonly known as the golden burr, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It is a member of the Leguminosae family and is characterized by its yellow flowers and distinctive seed pods.
