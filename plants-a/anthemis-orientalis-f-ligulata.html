Anthemis orientalis f. ligulata

Anthemis orientalis f. ligulata, commonly known as the Ligulate Anthemis, is a variant of the oriental anthemis.

It is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its daisy-like appearance. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces yellow flowers with a central disk. It is native to regions of southern Europe and western Asia.

This form is noted for its compact growth habit and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
