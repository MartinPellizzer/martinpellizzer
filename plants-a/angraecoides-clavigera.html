Angraecoides clavigera

Angraecoides clavigera, commonly known as the Madagascar star orchid, is a species of orchid native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its distinctive star-shaped flowers, which are typically white or pale blue with a yellowish center. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with a rhizome that produces multiple shoots. Its leaves are elongated and grass-like, often covered in a fine, hairy texture.

Angraecoides clavigera is adapted to the humid, shaded environments of Madagascar's rainforests.
