Achyranthes nasikensis

Achyranthes nasikensis, commonly known as the Nasik Achyranthes, is a perennial shrub native to the Indian subcontinent.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in dry and semi-arid conditions, often found in rocky soils and open habitats.

Its botanical features make it an important species in traditional medicine and ecological studies.
