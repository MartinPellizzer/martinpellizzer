Aphelandra formosa

Aphelandra formosa, commonly known as the golden flame plant, is a tropical shrub native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its striking, elongated leaves that are dark green with a metallic sheen. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow, spathaceous flowers that bloom throughout the year. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect sunlight for optimal growth.

Aphelandra formosa is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
