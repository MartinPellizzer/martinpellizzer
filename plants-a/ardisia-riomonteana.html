Ardisia riomonteana

Ardisia riomonteana, commonly known as the Rio Monte Beauty, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to South America.

It features glossy, dark green elliptical leaves and produces small, white, fragrant flowers during the summer months. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for shaded garden areas.

Ardisia riomonteana is also noted for its ability to produce attractive red berries that persist into the winter.
