Aspidium leptophyllum

Aspidium leptophyllum, commonly known as the slender shield fern, is a species of fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its reproductive structures, including sori, are located on the underside of the fronds.

Aspidium leptophyllum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
