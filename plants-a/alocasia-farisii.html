Alocasia farisii

Alocasia farisii, commonly known as the Farisii arrowleaf, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features large, heart-shaped leaves with a deep green color and prominent veins, often adorned with a waxy coating. The plant belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its unique inflorescence consisting of a spadix and spathe. It thrives in humid, shady environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Alocasia farisii is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
