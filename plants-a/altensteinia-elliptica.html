Altensteinia elliptica

Altensteinia elliptica, commonly known as the narrow-leaf altensteinia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the temperate regions of Europe.

It features slender, upright stems that bear alternate, elliptic-shaped leaves with a smooth, glossy surface. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that are clustered in loose spikes, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. Altensteinia elliptica thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland edges or shaded wetlands.
