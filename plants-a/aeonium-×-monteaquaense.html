Aeonium × monteaquaense

Aeonium × monteaquaense, commonly known as the Monteaqua Aeonium, is a hybrid succulent plant native to the Canary Islands.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, often with a powdery coating. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow flowers during the spring or early summer. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with ample sunlight.

Aeonium × monteaquaense is popular among succulent enthusiasts for its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
