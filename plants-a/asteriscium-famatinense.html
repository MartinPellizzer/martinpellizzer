Asteriscium famatinense

Asteriscium famatinense, commonly known as the Famatinian aster, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of northern Argentina.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which are typical of the family. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, rocky habitats.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
