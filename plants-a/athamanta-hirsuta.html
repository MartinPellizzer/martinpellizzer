Athamanta hirsuta

Athamanta hirsuta, commonly known as the hairy athamanta, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture. Its flowers are small, clustered in dense inflorescences, and are usually pink or reddish in color.

Athamanta hirsuta is often found in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in full sun.
