Alsophila trichodesma

Alsophila trichodesma, commonly known as the slender tree fern, is a species native to Southeast Asia and the Pacific islands.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and a crown of feathery fronds that give it a distinctive, elegant appearance. The plant belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is typically found in humid, tropical environments. Its rhizome is robust, allowing it to anchor firmly in moist, shaded soils.
