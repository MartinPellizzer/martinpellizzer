Alpinia pinnanensis

Alpinia pinnanensis, commonly known as the Pinnate Alpinia, is a herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

Its flowers are typically clustered in inflorescences and are known for their vibrant colors and fragrant scent.

Alpinia pinnanensis is valued both for its ornamental beauty and potential medicinal properties.
