Alsine tenuifolia var. densiflora

Alsine tenuifolia var. densiflora, commonly known as narrowleaf sandmat, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and is adapted to thrive in dry conditions. Its flowers are usually pink or red, and it produces small, dry fruits that contain seeds.

It is often used in xeriscaping due to its low water requirements and ornamental value.
