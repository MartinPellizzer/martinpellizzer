Armeria pseudarmeria subsp. longifolia

Armeria pseudarmeria subsp.

longifolia, commonly known as the longleaf armeria, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia. It is a low-growing perennial that typically forms dense mats of foliage.

The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of white or pink flowers in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and prolonged blooming period.
