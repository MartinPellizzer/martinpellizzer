Arachnothryx secunda

Arachnothryx secunda, commonly known as the velvetleaf or velvet tree, is a tropical plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its compound leaves, which are often covered in a dense, velvety pubescence. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pale yellow or greenish in color. Its fruit is a legume pod that splits open to release its seeds.

Arachnothryx secunda is native to regions of South America and is often found in humid, lowland forests.
