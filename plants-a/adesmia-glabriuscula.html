Adesmia glabriuscula

Adesmia glabriuscula, commonly known as the silky adesmia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a shrub that typically grows in dry, rocky habitats. The plant features small, clustered flowers that are usually white or pale pink in color. Its leaves are simple, elliptical, and covered in a fine, silky hair, giving it a distinctive appearance.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
