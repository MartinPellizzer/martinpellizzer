Allium yueanum

Allium yueanum, commonly known as the Yuean garlic chive, is a perennial plant native to China.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its grass-like leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces bulbils instead of traditional bulbs, which are used for propagation. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for gardens and landscapes.

Its ornamental value and culinary potential make it a versatile addition to horticultural collections.
