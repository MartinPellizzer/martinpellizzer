Albuca dilucula

Albuca dilucula, commonly known as the dawn lily, is a bulbous plant native to southern Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant is adapted to arid conditions, making it drought-tolerant and suitable for rock gardens. Its bulbs are perennial, allowing it to regrow each year after flowering.

Albuca dilucula is valued in horticulture for its elegant appearance and low maintenance requirements.
