Abutilon tridens

Abutilon tridens, commonly known as the velvetleaf or Indian velvet, is a tropical shrub native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its soft, velvety foliage and clusters of small, yellow flowers. The plant is often found in moist, disturbed habitats such as roadsides and wetlands. Its leaves are palmately lobed and can reach up to 20 centimeters in length.

Abutilon tridens is also known for its ability to produce a sticky, milky sap when damaged.
