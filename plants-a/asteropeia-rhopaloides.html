Asteropeia rhopaloides

Asteropeia rhopaloides, commonly known as the narrow-leaf aster.

It is a perennial herb native to arid regions of South Africa. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, clustered flowers in shades of white and purple. It is adapted to survive in dry conditions with deep roots and minimal water requirements.

Asteropeia rhopaloides is often used in xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and ornamental value.
