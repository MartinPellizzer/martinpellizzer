Antidesma jucundum

Antidesma jucundum, commonly known as the sweet antidesma, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized tree or shrub that typically grows up to 5 meters in height. The plant features simple, opposite leaves that are often elliptical or ovate in shape. Its small, fragrant flowers are usually white or pale yellow and are clustered in inflorescences.

The fruit is a small, fleshy drupe that contains a single seed, and it is often used in traditional medicine.
