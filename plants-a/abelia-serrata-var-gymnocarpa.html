Abelia serrata var. gymnocarpa

Abelia serrata var. gymnocarpa, commonly known as the smooth-seeded abelia, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features small, showy white to pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges, and its stems are often reddish in color. It prefers partial shade to full sun and well-drained soil, making it adaptable to various garden conditions.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in temperate climates.
