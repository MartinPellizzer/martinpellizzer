Acer lauyuense

Acer lauyuense, commonly known as the Yunnan maple, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It belongs to the Sapindaceae family and is characterized by its palmate leaves with five to seven lobes. The tree produces small, yellowish-green flowers in early spring, followed by winged seeds. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in mountainous regions.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and parks.
