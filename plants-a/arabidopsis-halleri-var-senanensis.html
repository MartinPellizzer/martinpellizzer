Arabidopsis halleri var. senanensis

Arabidopsis halleri var. senanensis, commonly known as the Senanense rockcress, is a member of the Brassicaceae family and is native to parts of East Asia.

It is a small, herbaceous plant that typically grows in rocky or calcareous soils. The plant features basal leaves that are pinnately lobed and small, erect flowering stems with clusters of white or pale yellow flowers. It produces small, rounded pods that contain several seeds.

This species is of interest in botanical studies due to its ecological adaptations and potential genetic traits.
