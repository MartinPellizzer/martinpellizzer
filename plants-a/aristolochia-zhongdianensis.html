Aristolochia zhongdianensis

Aristolochia zhongdianensis, commonly known as the Zhongdian arrowhead vine, is a flowering plant native to the Yunnan Province in China.

It belongs to the Aristolochiaceae family and is characterized by its unique, tubular flowers that resemble a heart shape. The plant produces dark purple to brownish flowers, which are often covered in a dense layer of fine hairs. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and can vary in size, while the stems are woody and often climb or trail along the ground.

This species is of interest to botanists due to its morphological features and potential medicinal properties.
