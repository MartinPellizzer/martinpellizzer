Anthurium chromostachyum

Anthurium chromostachyum, commonly known as the rainbow anthurium, is a striking tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful spathes that range from red to orange, yellow, and green, creating a visually stunning display. The plant features a thick, segmented rhizome and produces small, inconspicuous flowers inside the spathe. Its foliage consists of large, heart-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green.

Anthurium chromostachyum thrives in humid, warm environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its unique appearance.
