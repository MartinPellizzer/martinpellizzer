Arctotis aspera f. undulata

Arctotis aspera f. undulata, commonly known as the undulating arctotis, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, undulating leaves and produces showy yellow flowers with a distinctive black center. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming period and drought tolerance.
