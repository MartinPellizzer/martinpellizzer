Alcea pontica

Alcea pontica, commonly known as the Pontic hollyhock, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters. The plant features large, showy flowers that range in color from pink to red, with a distinctive velvety texture. Its leaves are broad, heart-shaped, and covered in fine hairs.

Alcea pontica thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
