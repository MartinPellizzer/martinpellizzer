Adenanthera gersenii

Adenanthera gersenii, commonly known as the Gersen's ticktree, is a small shrub native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is characterized by its legume pods and nitrogen-fixing abilities. The plant features trifoliate leaves and produces yellow flowers that bloom in summer. Its fruit is a pod that splits open to release seeds, contributing to its dispersal.

Adenanthera gersenii is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments.
