Aster annectens

Aster annectens, commonly known as the showy aster, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet, with lance-shaped leaves and vibrant blue to purple ray flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and natural landscapes.

Its blooms attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies, contributing to ecological biodiversity.
