Aniseia cernua

Aniseia cernua, commonly known as the nodding anise tree, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family, which includes citrus plants, and is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The fruit is a small, round drupe that develops after the flowers, contributing to its ornamental value in gardens.
