Amaranthus melancholicus var. tricolor

Amaranthus melancholicus var. tricolor, commonly known as the tri-color amaranth, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its strikingly colorful foliage, which typically displays shades of red, green, and yellow. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or red in color. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

This variety is also valued for its resilience and ability to attract pollinators.
