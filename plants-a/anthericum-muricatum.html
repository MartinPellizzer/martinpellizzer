Anthericum muricatum

Anthericum muricatum, commonly known as the purple fern, is a striking plant native to South America.

It features feathery, dark green leaves with a distinctive purple or reddish tinge on the underside. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in rocky or woodland areas.

Its unique appearance and adaptability make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
