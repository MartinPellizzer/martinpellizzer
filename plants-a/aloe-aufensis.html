Aloe aufensis

Aloe aufensis, commonly known as the Cape aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, branching stems and fleshy, sword-shaped leaves that are often covered in a powdery white coating. The plant produces striking red, orange, or yellow flowers in clusters at the tips of its stems. Aloe aufensis is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves and being drought-tolerant.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional medicinal uses.
