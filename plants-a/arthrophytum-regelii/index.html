Arthrophytum Regelii

Regel's saxifrage, scientifically known as Arthrophytum regelii, is a hardy plant native to the arid regions of Central Asia. It thrives in rocky and sandy soils, often growing in high-altitude environments where few other plants can survive. With its compact, cushion-like growth form, it is well adapted to withstand extreme temperatures and limited water availability. This resilient species is a symbol of endurance in harsh landscapes, making it a subject of both ecological and botanical interest. Its unique appearance and survival strategies make it a fascinating example of plant adaptation in extreme conditions.
