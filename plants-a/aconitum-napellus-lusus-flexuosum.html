Aconitum napellus lusus flexuosum

Aconitum napellus lusus flexuosum, commonly known as the flexible monkshood, is a variant of the common aconite.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe, characterized by its striking blue or purple flowers. The plant features palmate leaves with a distinctive hooded bloom structure. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Due to its toxic properties, it should be handled with care.
