Anil macrostachya

Anil macrostachya, commonly known as the giant marsh orchid, is a striking plant native to the wetlands of South America.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and large, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant thrives in moist, acidic soils and is often found in marshy or swampy areas. Its flowering period typically occurs during the late spring and early summer months.

Anil macrostachya plays an important ecological role by providing habitat and nectar for various pollinators.
