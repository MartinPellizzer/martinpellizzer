Anthurium mancuniense

Anthurium mancuniense, commonly known as the Mancuniense arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces vibrant spadices, which are modified leaves that enclose the true flowers. It thrives in humid, warm environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Anthurium mancuniense is often cultivated for its ornamental value in both indoor and outdoor settings.
