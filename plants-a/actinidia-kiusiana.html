Actinidia kiusiana

Actinidia kiusiana, commonly known as the Chinese mayapple, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Actinidiaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and fragrant white flowers. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow berries that are edible but often have a bitter taste. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is valued for its ornamental qualities and potential medicinal uses.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for both horticultural and ecological studies.
