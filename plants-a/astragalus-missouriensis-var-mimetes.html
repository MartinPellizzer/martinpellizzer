Astragalus missouriensis var. mimetes

Astragalus missouriensis var. mimetes, commonly known as the Missouri milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to the central United States.

Its leaves are compound, consisting of several oval-shaped leaflets, and it has a deep taproot system that helps it survive in arid conditions.
