Ardisia angustifolia

Ardisia angustifolia, commonly known as narrow-leaved evergreen ardisia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Ericaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are typically narrow and elongated. Ardisia angustifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and fruit.
