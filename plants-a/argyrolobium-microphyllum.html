Argyrolobium microphyllum

Argyrolobium microphyllum, commonly known as the silver globular pea, is a low-growing perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features small, rounded leaves and produces yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it drought-tolerant. Its scientific name reflects its silver-appearing leaves and tiny foliage.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and resilience in challenging climates.
