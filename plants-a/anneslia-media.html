Anneslia media

Anneslia media, commonly known as the common anneslia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features lance-shaped, hairy leaves and produces striking, pinkish-purple flowers with five petals. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30-60 cm and blooms from late spring to early summer. Anneslia media prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
