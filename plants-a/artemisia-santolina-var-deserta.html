Artemisia santolina var. deserta

Artemisia santolina var. deserta, commonly known as desert santolina, is a low-growing shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features silver-gray foliage with a compact, rounded form, making it an attractive ornamental plant. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in summer, which are often hidden among the foliage. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it suitable for rocky or arid gardens.

This variety is valued for its aromatic properties and ability to attract beneficial insects.
