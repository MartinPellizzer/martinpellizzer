Anthurium molaui

Anthurium molaui, commonly known as the Molaui arrowroot, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive inflorescence consisting of a spadix and a spathe. The plant produces large, heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and deep green coloration. Its flowers are typically yellow or white, with a spadix that bears small, inconspicuous flowers.

Anthurium molaui is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
