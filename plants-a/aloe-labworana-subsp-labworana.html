Aloe labworana subsp. labworana

Aloe labworana subsp.

labworana, commonly known as the Cape aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa. It features tall, spiky inflorescences that bear orange to red flowers, which attract pollinators like birds and insects.

The plant has fleshy, sword-shaped leaves that store water, typical of arid environments. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Its ornamental value and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for gardens in Mediterranean climates.
