Anthemis antitaurica

Anthemis antitaurica, commonly known as the Taurus anthemis, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant produces small, yellow-orange flowers that are typically arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or arid environments.

Anthemis antitaurica is also known for its aromatic properties and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
