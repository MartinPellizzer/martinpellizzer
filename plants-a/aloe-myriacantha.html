Aloe myriacantha

Aloe myriacantha, commonly known as the many-thorned aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, with spines along the edges. The plant produces yellow flowers in clusters during the summer months. It is adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Its leaves are used in traditional medicine and for their gel-like substance.
