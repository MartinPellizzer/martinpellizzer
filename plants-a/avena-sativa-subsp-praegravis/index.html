Avena Sativa Subsp. Praegravis

Avena sativa subsp. praegravis is a lesser-known variant of the common oat plant. It thrives in cooler climates and is often found in mountainous regions. This subspecies is valued for its hardy nature and adaptability to poor soils. It has been used traditionally in herbal medicine and as a forage crop. Its unique characteristics make it an interesting subject for botanical study.
