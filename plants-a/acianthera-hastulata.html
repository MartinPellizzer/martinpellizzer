Acianthera hastulata

Acianthera hastulata, commonly known as the sword orchid, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its upright, sword-shaped leaves and striking, showy flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its flowers are usually pink or purple and bloom during the warmer months.

Acianthera hastulata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
