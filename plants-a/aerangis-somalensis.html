Aerangis somalensis

Aerangis somalensis, commonly known as the Somali orchid, is a rare and endemic species native to the Horn of Africa.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its striking, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing adaptations to its harsh native habitat. Its flowers are usually small to medium in size, with intricate patterns and vibrant colors that attract pollinators.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat vulnerability, it is considered a conservation concern.
