Aconitum koelleanum var. firmum

Aconitum koelleanum var. firmum, commonly known as the firmum variety of the koelleanum aconite, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features tall, sparsely branched stems that can reach heights of up to 2 meters, adorned with large, showy blue to purple flowers. The plant has palmately divided leaves that are deeply cut, giving it a distinctive, fern-like appearance. It thrives in cool, moist environments and prefers partial to full shade.

Its flowers bloom in late summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
