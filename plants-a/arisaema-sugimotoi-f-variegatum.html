Arisaema sugimotoi f. variegatum

Arisaema sugimotoi f. variegatum, commonly known as the Variegated Japanese Taro, is a striking ornamental plant native to Japan.

It features a robust, tuberous rhizome and heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive variegated pattern of green and white. The plant produces an erect spadix surrounded by a spathe, which is typically a deep green with a yellowish tint. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

Its unique foliage and dramatic inflorescence make it a popular choice for specialty plant collections.
