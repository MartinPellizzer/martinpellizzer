Alocasia atropurpurea

Alocasia atropurpurea, commonly known as the Purple Velvet Alocasia, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are dark green with a deep purple underside. The plant thrives in humid, shady environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and ornamental landscaping.

Alocasia atropurpurea is also valued for its air-purifying qualities and ability to thrive in low-light conditions.
