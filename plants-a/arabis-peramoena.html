Arabis peramoena

Arabis peramoena, commonly known as the rock cress, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is native to Europe and western Asia, often found growing in rocky or gravelly soils. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are basal, rosette-like, and covered in fine hairs.

Arabis peramoena is typically short-lived, reaching heights of up to 20 centimeters.
