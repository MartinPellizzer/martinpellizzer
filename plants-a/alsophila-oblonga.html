Alsophila oblonga

Alsophila oblonga, commonly known as the narrow-leaf tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and fronds that are typically dark green and leathery. The plant has a slow growth rate and can reach heights of up to 10 meters in ideal conditions. Its rhizome is robust, allowing it to anchor firmly in moist, shaded environments.

Alsophila oblonga is often found in tropical rainforests and is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.
