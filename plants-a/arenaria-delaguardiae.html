Arenaria delaguardiae

Arenaria delaguardiae, commonly known as the Delaguardia sandwort, is a flowering plant native to the high Andes of South America.

It is a low-growing perennial that thrives in rocky, alpine environments. The plant produces small, white or pink flowers that bloom in early summer. Its leaves are typically succulent and adapted to conserve water in arid conditions.

Arenaria delaguardiae is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitat.
