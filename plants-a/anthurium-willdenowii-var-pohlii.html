Anthurium willdenowii var. pohlii

Anthurium willdenowii var. pohlii, commonly known as the Pohlii Anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped leaves that are typically deep green with a glossy texture. The plant produces striking spadices, which are the modified leaves that enclose the true flowers. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in tropical gardens.
