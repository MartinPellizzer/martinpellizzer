Abelia graebneriana var. deutziifolia

Abelia graebneriana var. deutziifolia, commonly known as the dwarf abelia or compact abelia, is a compact, shrub-like plant native to China.

It features small, fragrant, white to pink flowers that bloom in late summer to early fall. The leaves are ovate to elliptic, with a glossy green surface and serrated edges. It prefers partial to full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

This variety is prized for its hardiness and ornamental value in garden settings.
