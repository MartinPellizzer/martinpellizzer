Agrostis chionogeiton

Agrostis chionogeiton, commonly known as snowgrass, is a perennial grass species native to the northern regions of North America.

It thrives in cold climates and is often found in alpine and subalpine environments. The plant has slender, tufted stems and produces small, white to pale blue flowers in the summer. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green coloration.

Agrostis chionogeiton plays an important ecological role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for small invertebrates.
