Aconogonon tripterocarpum

Aconogonon tripterocarpum, commonly known as the three-winged senna, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces elongated pods that contain several seeds, which are often dispersed by wind. Native to tropical regions of Africa, it thrives in moist, well-drained soils.
