Adenia ellenbeckii

Adenia ellenbeckii, commonly known as the mountain adenia, is a shrub native to the eastern regions of Australia.

It belongs to the family Passifloraceae and is characterized by its woody stems and compound leaves. The plant produces striking, showy flowers with five petals and a distinctive crown of stamens. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.
