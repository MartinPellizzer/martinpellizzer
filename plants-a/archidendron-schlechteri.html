Archidendron schlechteri

Archidendron schlechteri, commonly known as the African ebony tree, is a leguminous plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its dark, dense wood, which is highly valued for its durability and rich color. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in racemes. Its fruit is a pod that contains several flat, seed-like structures.

The tree is often found in moist, well-drained soils and can grow in both forest and savanna habitats.
