Adenia heterophylla

Adenia heterophylla, commonly known as the variable leaf adenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, often lobed leaves that vary in shape and size across different parts of the plant. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are typically clustered in racemes. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in rainforest or woodland habitats.

Adenia heterophylla is valued for its ornamental foliage and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
