Adicea Subserrata

Adicea subserrata is a unique flowering plant native to specific regions in Southeast Asia. It is known for its striking, delicate flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. This species thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories. Its leaves are lance-shaped with finely serrated edges, giving it its scientific name. Despite its beauty, Adicea subserrata remains a lesser-known gem in the world of horticulture.
