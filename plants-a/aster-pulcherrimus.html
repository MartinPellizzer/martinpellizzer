Aster pulcherrimus

Aster pulcherrimus, commonly known as the showy aster, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant produces bright purple to blue flowers that bloom in late summer to early fall. It has slender, hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture.

Aster pulcherrimus is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and meadows.
