Alyssum oxycarpum var. kurdicum

Alyssum oxycarpum var. kurdicum, commonly known as the Kurdish alpine alyssum, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of the Middle East.

The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine habitats, thriving in well-drained soils and full sun.
