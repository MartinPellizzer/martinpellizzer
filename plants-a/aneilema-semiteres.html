Aneilema semiteres

Aneilema semiteres, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows as a herbaceous perennial, often reaching heights of up to 30 cm. Its ornamental value is enhanced by the vibrant colors of its flowers, which range from purple to pink and white.
