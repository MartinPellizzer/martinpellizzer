Arrabidaea ateramnantha

Arrabidaea ateramnantha, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a deciduous tree native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant is known for its striking red and orange flowers that bloom in summer, attracting various pollinators. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular ornamental species.

Its bark and leaves have been used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.
