Amaryllis ciliaris

Amaryllis ciliaris, commonly known as the Cape Lily, is a bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It produces striking, bell-shaped flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, often with a distinctive white stripe down the center. The plant features strap-like leaves that are typically dark green and slightly hairy. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and containers.

Amaryllis ciliaris is known for its long blooming period, often lasting several weeks during the warmer months.
