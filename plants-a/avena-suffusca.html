Avena suffusca

Avena suffusca, commonly known as the slender oat, is a perennial grass species native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences bearing numerous florets, which are typically green to pale blue in color. Avena suffusca thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open habitats such as meadows and rocky areas.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine for its potential health benefits.
