Astragalus ramianensis

Astragalus ramianensis, commonly known as the Chinese milkvetch, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family Fabaceae.

It is native to China and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in arid and semi-arid environments, making it an important species in ecological restoration projects. Astragalus ramianensis also has medicinal properties and is used in traditional Chinese medicine.

Its seeds are often used as a food source for various wildlife species.
