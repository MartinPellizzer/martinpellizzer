Alsine unilateralis

Alsine unilateralis, commonly known as the one-sided sandmat, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of North America.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its succulent, prostrate growth habit. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are typically narrow and fleshy, adapted to conserve water in dry environments.

Alsine unilateralis is often found in sandy soils and is considered a hardy, low-maintenance plant for xeriscaping.
