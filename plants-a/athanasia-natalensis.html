Athanasia natalensis

Athanasia natalensis, commonly known as Natal's immortality, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, evergreen shrub form. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in winter, adding ornamental value to its appearance. It is adapted to dry conditions and thrives in well-drained soils.

Due to its resilience and unique appearance, it is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
