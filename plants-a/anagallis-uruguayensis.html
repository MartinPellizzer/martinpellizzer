Anagallis uruguayensis

Anagallis uruguayensis, commonly known as the Uruguay morning glory, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a herbaceous annual that typically grows in moist, open habitats. The plant features trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, blooming in early spring. Its leaves are alternate, heart-shaped, and covered in fine hairs.

Anagallis uruguayensis is often found in disturbed soils and is sometimes cultivated as an ornamental plant.
