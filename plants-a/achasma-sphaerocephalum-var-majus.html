Achasma sphaerocephalum var. majus

Achasma sphaerocephalum var. majus, commonly known as the giant globe lichen, is a unique lichen species characterized by its distinctive spherical or globular thallus.

It belongs to the family Verrucariaceae and is known for its dense, crust-like growth form that adheres tightly to rock surfaces. Its morphology and growth pattern make it an important indicator species for certain ecological conditions.
