Anthurium talamancae

Anthurium talamancae, commonly known as the Talamanca arrowroot or red anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped leaves that are typically deep green with a red or purple underside. The plant produces striking spadices, which are brightly colored inflorescences that serve as the flower structure. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium talamancae is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and ease of care.
