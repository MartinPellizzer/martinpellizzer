Acanthus dioscoridis var. grandiflorus

Acanthus dioscoridis var. grandiflorus, commonly known as the large-flowered spiny acanthus, is a striking ornamental plant native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and used in horticultural displays.
