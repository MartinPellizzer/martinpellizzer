Asystasia natalensis

Asystasia natalensis, commonly known as Natal asystasia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its erect, branched stems and opposite, ovate leaves. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that are typically purple or blue in color, arranged in whorls along the stems. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or grassland habitats.

Natal asystasia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
