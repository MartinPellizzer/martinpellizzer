Amasonia taligalea

Amasonia taligalea, commonly known as the Taligalea lily, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves that form a dense rosette near the ground. Its flowers are borne on tall, slender stalks, making it a visually appealing addition to gardens.

Amasonia taligalea is adapted to humid, warm climates and thrives in well-drained soil.
