Asplenium trichomanes var. viridissimum

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its compact, evergreen foliage. The fronds are typically leathery and have a dark green color, with a distinctive, curled appearance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.
