Afrocarum imbricatum

Afrocarum imbricatum, commonly known as the African shield fern, is a tropical plant native to parts of Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, overlapping foliage that forms a shield-like appearance, giving it its common name. The plant has broad, dark green leaves that are often used in ornamental gardening due to their attractive appearance. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Afrocarum imbricatum is also valued for its ability to purify the air and add a lush, green aesthetic to indoor spaces.
