Anisantha fasciculata

Anisantha fasciculata, commonly known as the clustered grassvetch, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is a low-growing perennial herb characterized by its dense, tufted growth habit and small, trifoliate leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in racemes, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its seeds are enclosed in small, hairy pods that mature in the autumn.

Anisantha fasciculata is typically found in dry, open habitats such as meadows and disturbed soils.
