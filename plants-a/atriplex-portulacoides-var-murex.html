Atriplex portulacoides var. murex

Atriplex portulacoides var. murex, commonly known as sandmat, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of the western United States.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves and small, greenish-yellow flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to withstand drought and high temperatures. Its leaves are often covered in a powdery coating, which helps reduce water loss.
