Aglaonema latifolium

Aglaonema latifolium, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, broad leaves that come in various shades of green, often with striking white or cream-colored veins. The plant thrives in low-light conditions and is popular as a houseplant due to its air-purifying qualities. It prefers well-draining soil and consistent moisture without overwatering.

Aglaonema latifolium is also valued for its ability to tolerate indoor environments with minimal care.
