Asplenium lanceum

Asplenium lanceum, commonly known as the lance fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Aspleniaceae.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped fronds that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, spore-bearing structures called sori on the underside of its leaves. It is often found growing in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky crevices.

Asplenium lanceum is valued for its ornamental appearance and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
