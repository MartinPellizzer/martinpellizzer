Argyrocome floribunda

Argyrocome floribunda, commonly known as the silverleaf daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and clusters of small, daisy-like white flowers. The plant typically grows in well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. It is a perennial shrub that can reach heights of up to 30 centimeters.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
