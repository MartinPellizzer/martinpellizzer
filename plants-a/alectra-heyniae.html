Alectra heyniae

Alectra heyniae, commonly known as the Heyn's snakeweed, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble a single flower. The plant typically grows as a low, spreading shrub with small, narrow leaves and yellow-orange flowers. It is adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments with minimal water availability.
