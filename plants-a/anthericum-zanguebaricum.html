Anthericum zanguebaricum

Anthericum zanguebaricum, commonly known as the Zanzibar arum, is a perennial herb native to the coastal regions of East Africa.

It features a striking, arrow-shaped leaf that can grow up to 1.5 meters in length. The plant produces a spadix surrounded by a spathe, typical of the Araceae family. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found near water bodies.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for tropical gardens.
