Aphelandra Ignea

Fire plant, scientifically known as Aphelandra ignea, is a striking tropical species native to the rainforests of South America. This plant is renowned for its vibrant, flame-like foliage that ranges from deep red to orange and yellow hues. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice among collectors and indoor gardeners alike. Despite its eye-catching look, the Fire plant is relatively easy to care for with proper light and watering. It thrives in bright, indirect sunlight and requires consistent moisture to maintain its lush, colorful leaves.
