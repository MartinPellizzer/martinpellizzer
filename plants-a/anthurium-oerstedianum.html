Anthurium oerstedianum

Anthurium oerstedianum, commonly known as the Oersted's anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves and vibrant, spadix-like inflorescence. The plant thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth. Its foliage is typically dark green with a glossy texture, and it produces showy, colorful flowers that attract pollinators.

Anthurium oerstedianum is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
