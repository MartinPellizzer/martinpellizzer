Aster rubicaulis

Aster rubicaulis, commonly known as the redstem aster, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its slender, reddish stems and small, clustered blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands. It is a perennial species that blooms from late summer into early autumn.

Its foliage consists of narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.
