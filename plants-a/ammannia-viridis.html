Ammannia viridis

Ammannia viridis, commonly known as green ammannia, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its smooth, dark green leaves that are opposite in arrangement. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or red in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along riverbanks.

Ammannia viridis is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some cultures.
