Arisaema longipedunculatum var. yakumontanum

Arisaema longipedunculatum var. yakumontanum, commonly known as the Yakumontan arisaema, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves and distinctive spadix surrounded by a showy spathe. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its botanical features include a rhizomatous root system and a short, stout stem.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated as a decorative plant.
