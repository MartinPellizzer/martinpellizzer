Avena anisopogon

Avena anisopogon, commonly known as the slender oat, is a species of grass native to South America.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny flowers. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in open, disturbed habitats.

Avena anisopogon is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping.
