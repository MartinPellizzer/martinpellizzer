Aria phitosiana

Aria phitosiana, commonly known as the silver leaf plant, is a species native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking silver-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, powdery hairs. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Aria phitosiana is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and air-purifying properties.
