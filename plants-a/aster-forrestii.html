Aster forrestii

Aster forrestii, commonly known as the Chinese aster, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant produces showy purple or blue flowers that bloom in the summer, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies. Aster forrestii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens due to its vibrant colors and resilience.

It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it a versatile choice for landscaping.
