Agave univittata var. subcanescens

Agave univittata var. subcanescens, commonly known as the narrow-leaved agave, is a succulent plant native to Mexico.

It features a rosette of thick, gray-green leaves with a narrow, waxy texture. The leaves have a distinct whitish margin, giving it a subtle, silvery appearance. It produces an inflorescence of yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer.

This plant is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
