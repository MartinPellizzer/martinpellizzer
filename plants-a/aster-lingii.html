Aster lingii

Aster lingii, commonly known as the Sierra aster, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that consist of both ray and disk florets. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. It produces vibrant purple to pink flowers that bloom in late summer to early fall.

Aster lingii is valued for its ornamental beauty and plays a role in supporting local pollinators.
