Anthurium hannoniae

Anthurium hannoniae, commonly known as the Hannon's peace lily, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, glossy, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 3 feet in length. The plant produces spadices, which are inflorescences consisting of a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe, typically in shades of white, red, or yellow. It thrives in humid, warm environments with indirect light and requires well-draining soil.

Anthurium hannoniae is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and air-purifying qualities.
