Azanza thespesioides var. flaviflora

Azanza thespesioides var. flaviflora, commonly known as the yellow-flowered azanza, is a shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged alternately along its stems. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, often blooming in clusters. Its bark is thick and fibrous, providing some resistance to pests and diseases.

Azanza thespesioides var. flaviflora is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in partial shade.
