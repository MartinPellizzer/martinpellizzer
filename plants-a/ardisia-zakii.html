Ardisia zakii

Ardisia zakii, commonly known as the Malabar starling.

This evergreen shrub is native to the Western Ghats of India and belongs to the family Myrsinaceae. It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and produces small, white flowers that are followed by bright red berries. The plant is often found in moist, shaded forest areas and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
