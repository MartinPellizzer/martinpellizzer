Anemone prattii

Anemone prattii, commonly known as the California anemone, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the western United States.

It features showy, white to pink flowers that bloom in early spring, typically from March to May. The plant has a short, thick rhizome and basal leaves that are heart-shaped with a hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in moist meadows and forested areas.

Anemone prattii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local pollinators.
