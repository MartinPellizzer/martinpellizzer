Adromischus kleinioides

Adromischus kleinioides, commonly known as the dwarf ice plant, is a succulent species native to South Africa.

It features small, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a bluish tint, arranged in a dense rosette formation. The plant produces delicate, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the warmer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its compact size and ornamental appeal make it a popular choice for xeriscaping and succulent collections.
