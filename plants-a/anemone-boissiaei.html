Anemone boissiaei

Anemone boissiaei, commonly known as the Boissiaei anemone, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It typically grows in rocky or alpine habitats, where it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun exposure. The plant features showy, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are basal, forming a rosette and are usually hairy or pubescent.

Anemone boissiaei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens with suitable conditions.
