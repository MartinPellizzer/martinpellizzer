Areca verschaffeltii

Areca verschaffeltii, commonly known as the velvetleaf areca, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Arecaceae family, which includes palms, and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or greenish-yellow in color. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine and as a source of fiber.

Areca verschaffeltii thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
