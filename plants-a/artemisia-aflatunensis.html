Artemisia aflatunensis

Artemisia aflatunensis, commonly known as the Aflatun wormwood, is a perennial herb native to the high-altitude regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, silver-green foliage. The plant produces small, yellowish flower heads that are typically clustered in composite inflorescences. Artemisia aflatunensis is adapted to harsh, arid environments and is often found in rocky or mountainous terrain.

It is valued for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
