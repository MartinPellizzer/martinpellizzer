Anubias nana

Anubias nana, commonly known as dwarf anubias, is a hardy aquatic plant native to Africa.

It features small, dark green, oval-shaped leaves that grow closely together, giving it a compact appearance. The plant has rhizomes that anchor it to substrates and produce new shoots along their length. It is often used in aquariums due to its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and its resistance to herbivorous fish.

Anubias nana is also valued for its ability to improve water quality by absorbing nitrates and other nutrients.
