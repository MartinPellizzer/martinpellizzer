Anthurium diversurense

Anthurium diversurense, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Colombia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped spatula and striking red spadix. The plant produces large, glossy leaves that can grow up to 30 cm in length. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and requires well-draining soil.

Anthurium diversurense is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
