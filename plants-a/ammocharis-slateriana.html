Ammocharis slateriana

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. Ammocharis slateriana is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to grow in suitable conditions.
