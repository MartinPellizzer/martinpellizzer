Arenaria pulvinata

Arenaria pulvinata, commonly known as the cushion sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to arctic and alpine regions.

It forms dense, mat-like growths that help stabilize soil in harsh environments. The plant has small, rounded leaves and produces clusters of white or pink flowers. It is adapted to cold climates and can tolerate poor soil conditions.

Its botanical features make it a hardy species often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
