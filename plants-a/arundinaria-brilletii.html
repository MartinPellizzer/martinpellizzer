Arundinaria brilletii

Arundinaria brilletii, commonly known as the Chinese bamboo or golden bamboo, is a species of grass native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and dense, feathery foliage. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that are often yellow or golden in color. Arundinaria brilletii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.

It prefers moist, well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.
