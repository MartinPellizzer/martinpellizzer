Aechmea nudicaulis var. plurifolia

Aechmea nudicaulis var. plurifolia, commonly known as the "Pineapple Plant" or "Air Plant," is a bromeliad native to South America.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that form a central cup to collect water and nutrients. The plant produces striking inflorescences with vibrant red, orange, or yellow flowers. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
