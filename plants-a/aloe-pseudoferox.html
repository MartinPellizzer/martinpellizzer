Aloe pseudoferox

Aloe pseudoferox, commonly known as the Cape aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are thick and fleshy, adapted for storing water in arid environments. The leaves are typically green with a reddish tinge and have a waxy coating to reduce water loss. It produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow flowers that bloom in spring.

This plant is well-suited for xeriscaping and is relatively low-maintenance once established.
