Alyssum skopjense

Alyssum skopjense, commonly known as the Skopje alyssum, is a low-growing annual plant native to the Balkan region.

It features small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pale pink, typically blooming in early summer. The plant has a compact, mat-like growth habit with hairy stems and leaves that are narrow and linear. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Alyssum skopjense is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract beneficial insects.
