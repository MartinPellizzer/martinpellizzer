Asplenium vargasii

Asplenium vargasii, commonly known as the Vargas holly fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Aspleniaceae and is characterized by its compact, dense foliage and leathery, dark green fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its rhizomes are often covered in a thick layer of moss, contributing to its unique appearance.

Asplenium vargasii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental qualities and ability to thrive in low-light conditions.
