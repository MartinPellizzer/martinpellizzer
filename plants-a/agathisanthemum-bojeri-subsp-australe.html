Agathisanthemum bojeri subsp. australe

Agathisanthemum bojeri subsp.

australe, commonly known as the Southern Agathisanthemum, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its compact, evergreen shrub form with small, dark green leaves.

The plant produces showy, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Due to its ornamental value and hardiness, it is a popular choice for gardeners in warmer climates.
