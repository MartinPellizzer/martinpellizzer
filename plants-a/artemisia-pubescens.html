Artemisia pubescens

Artemisia pubescens, commonly known as silverleaf sage, is a perennial herb native to North America.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flower clusters that bloom in late summer. It thrives in dry, well-drained soils and is often found in prairies and open woodlands.

Artemisia pubescens is valued for its aromatic properties and has been used in traditional medicine.
