Aloe barbertoniae

Aloe barbertoniae, commonly known as the silver aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of narrow, silver-gray leaves that are covered in a powdery coating, which helps reduce water loss. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with small, tubular flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It is adapted to arid environments and requires well-draining soil and minimal water.

Aloe barbertoniae is often cultivated for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
