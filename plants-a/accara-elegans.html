Accara elegans

Accara elegans, commonly known as the elegant spurge, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features upright stems covered in small, sticky glands, and its leaves are lance-shaped with a slightly hairy surface. It produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in the summer months.

Accara elegans is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in full sun to partial shade conditions.
