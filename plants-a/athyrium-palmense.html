Athyrium palmense

Athyrium palmense, commonly known as the palmate lady fern, is a species of fern native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, palmately divided fronds that give it a distinctive, feathery appearance. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine, and it is valued for its ornamental beauty in horticulture.

Athyrium palmense plays an important ecological role by providing habitat and food for various insects and small animals.
