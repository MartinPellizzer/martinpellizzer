Alsodeia dewevrei

Alsodeia dewevrei, commonly known as the Cape sugarbush, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its small, fragrant white flowers. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and has a dense, bushy growth habit. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs.

Alsodeia dewevrei is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
