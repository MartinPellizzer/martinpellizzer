Asplenium macraei var. strictum

Asplenium macraei var. strictum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf maidenhair fern, is a species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, linear fronds that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant has a compact growth habit, making it suitable for small spaces such as terrariums or shaded garden areas. It prefers moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, thriving in humid environments.

Its ornamental value lies in its intricate foliage, which adds a refined touch to both indoor and outdoor landscapes.
