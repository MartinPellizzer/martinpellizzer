Aster tuganianus

Aster tuganianus, commonly known as the Tugan aster, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads and medicinal properties. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in dry, sunny environments. Its flowers are usually blue to purple in color, with a central disc surrounded by ray florets.

Aster tuganianus is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
