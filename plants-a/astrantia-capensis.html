Astrantia capensis

Astrantia capensis, commonly known as the African aster, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features upright, branching stems that bear clusters of small, pink to purple flowers. The plant has palmate leaves with five to seven lobes, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Its flowering period typically occurs in late spring to early summer.
