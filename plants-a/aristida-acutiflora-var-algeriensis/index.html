Aristida Acutiflora Var. Algeriensis

Aristida acutiflora var. algeriensis is a rare and hardy grass species native to northern Africa. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, showcasing remarkable drought tolerance. This plant is known for its fine, silver-tipped foliage and compact growth habit. It is often used in xeriscaping due to its low maintenance requirements. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental and ecological landscaping.
