Asplenium angustatum var. langsdorffii

Asplenium angustatum var. langsdorffii, commonly known as the narrow fern, is a species of evergreen fern characterized by its slender, linear leaves.

It belongs to the family Aspleniaceae and is native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its fronds are usually dark green and have a delicate, feathery appearance.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
