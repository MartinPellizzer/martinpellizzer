Aristea abyssinica

Aristea abyssinica, commonly known as the Abyssinian carpet grass, is a flowering plant native to Ethiopia.

It belongs to the family Poaceae, which includes grasses, and is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered inflorescences with pale blue to purple flowers. Due to its resilience, it is sometimes used in restoration projects in degraded landscapes.
