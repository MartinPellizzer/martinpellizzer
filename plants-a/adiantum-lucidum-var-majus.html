Adiantum lucidum var. majus

Adiantum lucidum var. majus, commonly known as the shield fern, is a delicate and elegant fern native to the eastern United States.

It features intricate, lace-like fronds that are typically light green with a glossy sheen. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and prefers well-draining, acidic soil. Its rhizomes spread horizontally, allowing it to form dense, carpet-like colonies.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in woodland gardens.
