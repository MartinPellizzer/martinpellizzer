Aeschynanthus perrottetii var. planiculmis

Aeschynanthus perrottetii var. planiculmis, commonly known as the flat-leafed ivy vine, is a tropical flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and clusters of small, tubular, brightly colored flowers that typically range from red to orange. The leaves are ovate to elliptical in shape, with a smooth surface and a slightly waxy texture. This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value in hanging baskets or as a trailing plant in greenhouses.

It thrives in warm, humid conditions and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.
