Artemisia roxburghiana var. acutiloba

Artemisia roxburghiana var. acutiloba, commonly known as narrow-leaved wormwood, is a perennial shrub native to the Himalayas.

It features silvery-gray foliage with narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are aromatic and covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flower clusters that bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in alpine and rocky habitats.

Due to its medicinal properties, it has been used traditionally in herbal remedies.
