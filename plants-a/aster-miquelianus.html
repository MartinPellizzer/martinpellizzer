Aster miquelianus

Aster miquelianus, commonly known as the Korean aster, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and is known for its showy, violet-blue flowers that bloom in the summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture, while its stems are upright and branching.

Aster miquelianus is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in diverse soil conditions.
