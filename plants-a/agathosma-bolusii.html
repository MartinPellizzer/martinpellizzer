Agathosma bolusii

Agathosma bolusii, commonly known as Bolus's sage, is a perennial shrub native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, narrow leaves and small yellow flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or sandy habitats. It is valued for its essential oils, which are used in perfumery and traditional medicine.

Agathosma bolusii is also an important plant for pollinators and contributes to biodiversity in its native environment.
