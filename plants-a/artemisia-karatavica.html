Artemisia karatavica

Artemisia karatavica, commonly known as Karatavica wormwood, is a perennial herb native to Central Asia.

It is characterized by its aromatic, silver-gray foliage and small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant thrives in dry, rocky habitats and is often found in steppe and semi-desert regions. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes many other aromatic herbs.

Artemisia karatavica is valued for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally in various herbal remedies.
