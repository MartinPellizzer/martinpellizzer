Amphibolia succulenta

Amphibolia succulenta, commonly known as the desert rose, is a succulent plant native to arid regions of Africa.

It features fleshy, water-storing leaves that help it survive in dry conditions. The plant produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of pink, red, and white, often blooming during the rainy season. Its root system is adapted to absorb moisture efficiently from the soil.

Amphibolia succulenta is well-suited for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its low water requirements.
