Anemone × emiliana

Anemone × emiliana, commonly known as the hybrid anemone, is a perennial plant belonging to the Ranunculaceae family.

It is characterized by its showy, single or double flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows to a height of 12 to 24 inches and produces attractive, palmate leaves with a hairy texture. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

Anemone × emiliana is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to bloom in early spring.
