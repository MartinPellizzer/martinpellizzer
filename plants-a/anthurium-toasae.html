Anthurium toasae

Anthurium toasae, commonly known as the Toasae arrowhead, is a species of flowering plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its unique inflorescence consisting of a spadix and a spathe. The plant typically features heart-shaped, glossy dark green leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. Its striking, colorful spathes can range from red to orange, enhancing its ornamental value.

Anthurium toasae thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
