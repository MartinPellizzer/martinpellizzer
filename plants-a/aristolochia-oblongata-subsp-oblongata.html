Aristolochia oblongata subsp. oblongata

Aristolochia oblongata subsp.

oblongata, commonly known as the Chinese birthwort, is a perennial herb native to eastern Asia. It features heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive, intricate veining pattern.

The plant produces a unique, horseshoe-shaped flower that is often greenish-brown with a strong, unpleasant odor. Its tuberous roots are used in traditional Chinese medicine.

The species is characterized by its complex morphology and is part of the Aristolochiaceae family.
