Asterandra cornifolia

Asterandra cornifolia, commonly known as the horned aster, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, clustered flowers in shades of white to pale yellow. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks.

Asterandra cornifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
