Ascolepis vatkeana

Ascolepis vatkeana, commonly known as the Vatke's orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the eastern Himalayas.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy flowers. The plant typically produces small, clustered inflorescences with pale yellow to greenish-white petals. Its leaves are lanceolate and arranged in a rosette, often covered with a fine layer of white pubescence.

Ascolepis vatkeana is adapted to high-altitude environments and is often found in rocky or alpine habitats.
