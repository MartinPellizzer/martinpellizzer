Anemone okennonii

Anemone okennonii, commonly known as the Okinawa anemone, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It is characterized by its showy, white to pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has basal leaves that are heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it a desirable addition to native plant gardens and ecological restoration projects.
