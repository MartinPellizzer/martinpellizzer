Anthyllis vulneraria f. hirsuta

Anthyllis vulneraria f. hirsuta, commonly known as the hairy bitterling, is a variant of the common bitterling.

It is a low-growing perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia. The plant features small, pea-like flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, which gives it the 'hirsuta' designation.

This subspecies is often found in dry, calcareous soils and is adapted to thrive in sunny, open habitats.
