Aeschynomene paucijuga var. subscabra

Aeschynomene paucijuga var. subscabra, commonly known as the slender sesban, is a perennial legume native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features slender, erect stems and trifoliate leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are typically pale green to yellow in color. It is adapted to grow in dry, disturbed soils and is often found in savannas and grasslands.

This species is valued for its nitrogen-fixing ability and potential use in forage and soil improvement.
