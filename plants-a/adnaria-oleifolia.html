Adnaria oleifolia

Adnaria oleifolia, commonly known as the Mediterranean senna, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in late spring, followed by small, bean-like pods containing seeds. Its leaves are pinnately compound, with small, oval-shaped leaflets.

Adnaria oleifolia is often found in coastal areas and is adapted to sandy or rocky soils.
