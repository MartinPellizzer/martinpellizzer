Acer campestre var. hebecarpum

Acer campestre var. hebecarpum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf maple, is a variant of the common maple tree.

It is characterized by its slender, elongated leaves that are typically narrower than those of the typical Acer campestre. The plant produces small, winged seeds that are dispersed by wind, a common trait among maple species. It thrives in a variety of soil types and is adaptable to different light conditions.

This variety is often used in landscaping due to its ornamental value and hardiness.
