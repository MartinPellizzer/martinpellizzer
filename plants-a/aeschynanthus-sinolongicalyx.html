Aeschynanthus sinolongicalyx

Aeschynanthus sinolongicalyx, commonly known as the long-leaf gloxinia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant features elliptical, glossy leaves that are arranged oppositely along the stems. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Aeschynanthus sinolongicalyx is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its ornamental flowers and compact size.
