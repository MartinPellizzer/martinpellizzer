Adenostyles stenotricha

Adenostyles stenotricha, commonly known as the hairy adenostyle, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in grasslands and rocky areas, thriving in well-drained soils. Its leaves are hairy and linear, while its flowers are small and yellow, arranged in clusters.

Adenostyles stenotricha is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
