Alloplectus cristatus var. brevicalyx

Alloplectus cristatus var. brevicalyx, commonly known as the small-lobed spurred orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a small herbaceous plant characterized by its delicate, spurred orchid flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. The plant features a short, erect stem with several narrow, linear leaves arranged in a rosette pattern. Its flowers are borne on short pedicels and are often clustered at the apex of the stem.

This variety is notable for its unique floral structure and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in botanical gardens.
