Aloe capitata var. quartziticola

Aloe capitata var. quartziticola, commonly known as the quartz aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of thick, fleshy, blue-green leaves that are covered in a powdery white coating, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow flowers during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water, making it a hardy choice for xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular ornamental plant.
