Arrabidaea elliptica

Arrabidaea elliptica, commonly known as the African bellflower, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in grassland and savanna habitats. The plant features showy, bell-shaped flowers that are often pink or purple in color. Its leaves are elliptical and arranged alternately along the stem.

Arrabidaea elliptica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
