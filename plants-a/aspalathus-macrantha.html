Aspalathus macrantha

Aspalathus macrantha, commonly known as the silver needle bush, is a South African plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, delicate leaves and clusters of white or pale yellow flowers. The plant is native to the Western and Eastern Cape provinces of South Africa. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.

Aspalathus macrantha is also valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
