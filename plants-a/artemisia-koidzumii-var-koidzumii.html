Artemisia koidzumii var. koidzumii

Artemisia koidzumii var. koidzumii, commonly known as Japanese wormwood, is a perennial herb native to Japan.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage, which consists of finely divided, aromatic leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flower clusters that appear in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Its essential oils have been used in traditional medicine for their antiseptic and anti-inflammatory properties.
