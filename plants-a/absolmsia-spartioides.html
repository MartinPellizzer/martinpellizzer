Absolmsia spartioides

Absolmsia spartioides, commonly known as the silver leaf tree, is a member of the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is native to the arid regions of southern Africa, particularly South Africa. The plant is characterized by its silvery-white, velvety leaves that help reduce water loss in hot, dry climates. It produces small, inconspicuous greenish-yellow flowers arranged in clusters.

The tree is often used in xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and ornamental foliage.
