Aneilema scaberrimum var. fruticosum

Aneilema scaberrimum var. fruticosum, commonly known as the rough aneilema, is a shrub native to tropical Africa.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and swampy areas. The plant features rough, sometimes hairy stems and broad, ovate leaves with a waxy texture. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, often in green or reddish bracts.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in local medicine.
