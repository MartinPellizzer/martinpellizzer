Asystasia comorensis var. humilis

Asystasia comorensis var. humilis, commonly known as the dwarf asystasia, is a flowering plant native to the Comoros Islands.

It is a low-growing herbaceous perennial with tuberous roots that help it survive in its tropical environment. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves that are usually covered in a fine pubescence. Its flowers are small, tubular, and typically violet or purple in color, arranged in dense spikes.

It thrives in moist, shaded conditions and is often found in forest understories.
