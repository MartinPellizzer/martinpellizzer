Athyrium uniforme

Athyrium uniforme, commonly known as the uniform lady fern, is a species of fern native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds that are typically bright green and have a graceful, arching appearance. The plant thrives in shady, moist environments and is often found in forest understories. Its rhizomes are creeping and help it spread vegetatively.

Athyrium uniforme is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and adaptability to various soil types.
