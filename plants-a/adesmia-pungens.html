Adesmia pungens

Adesmia pungens, commonly known as the spiny legume, is a shrub native to arid and semi-arid regions of South America.

It is characterized by its woody stems and small, often thorny leaves. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are typically white or pale pink. Its fruit is a legume pod containing several seeds.

Adesmia pungens is adapted to survive in harsh environments, making it a resilient plant in dry climates.
