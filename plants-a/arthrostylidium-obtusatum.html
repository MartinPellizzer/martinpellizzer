Arthrostylidium obtusatum

Arthrostylidium obtusatum, commonly known as the broad-leaved rock heath, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, often in coastal or inland areas. The plant features erect, branching stems and produces small, white to pale blue flowers in clusters. Its leaves are broad and fleshy, adapted to retain moisture in arid environments.

Arthrostylidium obtusatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage and delicate blooms.
