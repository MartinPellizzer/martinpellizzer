Argentina festiva

Argentina festiva, commonly known as the Argentine festival flower, is a striking plant native to the grasslands of Argentina.

It features vibrant, showy blooms that range in color from red to purple, often resembling those of the peony. The plant typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, with broad, lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and glossy. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens in temperate climates.

Its ornamental value has led to its cultivation beyond its native habitat.
