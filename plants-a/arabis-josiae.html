Arabis josiae

Arabis josiae, commonly known as the snowdrop, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, bell-shaped flowers that typically bloom in early spring. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, white or pale yellow flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or shaded areas.

Arabis josiae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
