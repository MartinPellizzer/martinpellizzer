Adenia hastata var. glandulifera

Adenia hastata var. glandulifera, commonly known as the glandular adenia, is a climbing shrub native to eastern Australia.

It features heart-shaped, glossy leaves with prominent veins and glandular dots on the underside. The plant produces clusters of small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in spring. Its fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release seeds.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.
