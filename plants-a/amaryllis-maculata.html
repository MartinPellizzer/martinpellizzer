Amaryllis maculata

Amaryllis maculata, commonly known as the spotted amaryllis, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It produces large, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and red, often with intricate patterns and spots. The plant features strap-like, narrow leaves that are typically green with a reddish tint. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers bright, indirect light.

Amaryllis maculata is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its vibrant blooms and relatively easy care requirements.
