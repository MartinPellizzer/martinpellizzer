Alternanthera flavogrisea

Alternanthera flavogrisea, commonly known as the golden touch plant, is a member of the Amaranthaceae family and is native to South America.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its striking, colorful foliage that ranges from green to shades of red, orange, and yellow. The leaves are typically opposite, ovate, and covered with a waxy cuticle that enhances their vibrant appearance. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value in water gardens and as a decorative houseplant.
