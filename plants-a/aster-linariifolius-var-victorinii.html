Aster linariifolius var. victorinii

Aster linariifolius var. victorinii, commonly known as the Victorinii aster, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It features finely divided, fern-like leaves that give it a delicate, lacey appearance. The plant produces small, daisy-like flowers in shades of white to pale blue, typically blooming in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is often cultivated in gardens and natural habitats.
