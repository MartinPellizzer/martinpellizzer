Abolboda sceptrum

Abolboda sceptrum, commonly known as the sacred staff plant, is a striking member of the Araceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem adorned with large, heart-shaped leaves that can reach up to 30 cm in length. The plant produces a unique inflorescence consisting of a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe, often in shades of red and yellow. Native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Due to its ornamental value and cultural significance, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
