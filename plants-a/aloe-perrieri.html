Aloe perrieri

Aloe perrieri, commonly known as the Cape aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asphodelaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are fleshy and covered in a white powdery coating. The plant produces tall, branched flower stalks that bear yellowish-green flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Aloe perrieri is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional medicinal uses.
