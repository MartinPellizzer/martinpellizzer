Anathallis lobiserrata

Anathallis lobiserrata, commonly known as the serrated lip orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to South America, particularly Brazil, and is found in tropical rainforest environments. The plant is characterized by its unique floral structure, featuring a prominently lobed lip that distinguishes it from other orchid species. Its flowers are typically yellow or greenish-yellow, with a distinct three-lobed structure.

Anathallis lobiserrata is often epiphytic, growing on trees in the canopy layer of the forest, where it absorbs moisture and nutrients from the air and rain.
