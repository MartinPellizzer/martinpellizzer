Areca alba

Areca alba, commonly known as the white areca palm, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a member of the Arecaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and feathery, compound leaves. The plant produces small, white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by edible, round fruits containing a single seed. Areca alba is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.

Its growth habits make it suitable for warm, humid climates with well-drained soil.
