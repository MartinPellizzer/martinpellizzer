Agave univittata var. brevifolia

Agave univittata var. brevifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved agave, is a succulent plant native to Mexico.

It features short, narrow, blue-green leaves that are often lined with white or yellowish margins. The plant produces a tall, branched inflorescence with showy yellow to orange flowers. It is adapted to arid environments and requires well-draining soil.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and resilience in dry climates.
