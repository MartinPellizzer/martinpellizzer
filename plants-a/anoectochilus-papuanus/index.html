Anoectochilus Papuanus

Anoectochilus papuanus is a rare and exquisite orchid native to the highlands of Papua, Indonesia. It is known for its striking, velvety green leaves and delicate, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of white and pink. This plant thrives in cool, humid environments and is often found growing on tree trunks or rocks in mountainous regions. Due to habitat loss and over-collection, it is considered endangered and is protected in many areas. Its unique beauty and ecological significance make it a subject of both scientific interest and conservation concern.
