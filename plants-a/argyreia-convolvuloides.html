Argyreia convolvuloides

Argyreia convolvuloides, commonly known as the sacred lotus or velvet flower, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is a climbing vine with heart-shaped leaves and produces striking, showy flowers that range in color from white to deep red. The plant is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in warm, humid climates. Its flowers are often used in traditional ceremonies and have cultural significance in various parts of Southeast Asia.

The plant's botanical features make it both ornamental and ecologically valuable in its native habitats.
