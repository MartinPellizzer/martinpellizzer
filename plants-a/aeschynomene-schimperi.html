Aeschynomene schimperi

Aeschynomene schimperi, commonly known as Schimper's goatherb, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in disturbed or open habitats. Its small, yellowish flowers are arranged in clusters and are followed by cylindrical, dehiscent pods containing seeds.

Aeschynomene schimperi is sometimes used in traditional medicine and can also serve as forage for livestock.
