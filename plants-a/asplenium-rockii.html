Asplenium rockii

Asplenium rockii, commonly known as the Rock Fern, is a species of fern native to China.

It belongs to the family Aspleniaceae and is characterized by its compact, evergreen foliage. The plant typically grows in rocky or crevice habitats, showcasing its adaptability to harsh environments. Its fronds are dark green and often have a leathery texture, which helps in reducing water loss.

Asplenium rockii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in shaded, moist conditions.
