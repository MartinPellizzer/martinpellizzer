Asphodelus bornmuelleri

Asphodelus bornmuelleri, commonly known as the Mediterranean asphodel, is a perennial herb native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It typically grows in rocky or dry soils, preferring sunny to partially shaded conditions. The plant produces tall, slender stems with narrow, grass-like leaves and clusters of yellow or greenish-yellow flowers. Its inflorescence consists of dense, spike-like arrangements of small, tubular flowers.

Asphodelus bornmuelleri is often used in xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and ornamental value.
