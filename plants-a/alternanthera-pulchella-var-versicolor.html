Alternanthera pulchella var. versicolor

Alternanthera pulchella var. versicolor, commonly known as the Rainbow Plant, is a strikingly colorful species native to South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that features vibrant, variegated leaves with shades of red, pink, and green. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade. Its foliage changes color with the seasons, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

It is often used in water gardens or as a container plant due to its aesthetic appeal and adaptability.
