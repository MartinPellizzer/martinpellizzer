Athroisma pinnatifidum

Athroisma pinnatifidum, commonly known as the narrow-leafed pittosporum, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in coastal and lowland forest environments. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves with a distinctive pinnatifid (bifurcated) margin. Its flowers are small and fragrant, often appearing in clusters, and it produces dark blue or black berries.

Athroisma pinnatifidum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting native wildlife.
