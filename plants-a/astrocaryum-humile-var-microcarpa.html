Astrocaryum humile var. microcarpa

Astrocaryum humile var. microcarpa, commonly known as the dwarf palm or microcarpa palm, is a small, slow-growing palm native to the Amazon rainforest.

It typically reaches a height of less than 2 meters, with a short, unbranched trunk and a crown of pinnate leaves. The plant produces small, clustered yellow flowers followed by dark blue to black berries. Its leaves are long and narrow, with a smooth, waxy surface, and the plant is often found in shaded, moist environments.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens.
