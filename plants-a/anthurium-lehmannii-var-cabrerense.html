Anthurium lehmannii var. cabrerense

Anthurium lehmannii var. cabrerense, commonly known as the Cabrerense Anthurium, is a tropical plant native to parts of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with prominent veins. The plant produces colorful spadices, which are the modified leaves that enclose the true flowers. It thrives in humid, warm environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated as a houseplant.
