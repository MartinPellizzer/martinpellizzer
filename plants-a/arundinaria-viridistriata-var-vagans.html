Arundinaria viridistriata var. vagans

Arundinaria viridistriata var. vagans, commonly known as the green-striated bamboo, is a variant of the bamboo genus Arundinaria.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems that can grow up to several meters in height. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically a vibrant green, often with subtle striations. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded to partially shaded environments.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
