Astragalus neilreichianus

Astragalus neilreichianus, commonly known as Neilreich's milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to the arid and semi-arid regions of the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

The plant typically grows in rocky soils and is adapted to drought conditions, showcasing a deep root system for water absorption. Astragalus neilreichianus is also valued for its ecological role in stabilizing soil and supporting local biodiversity.
