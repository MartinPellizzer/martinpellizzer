Anthurium chiapasense

Anthurium chiapasense, commonly known as the Chiapas anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves and vibrant spathe flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its foliage is often a deep green, while the spathes can range in color from red to orange, depending on the variety.

Anthurium chiapasense is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal in both indoor and outdoor settings.
