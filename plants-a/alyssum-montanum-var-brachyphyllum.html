Alyssum montanum var. brachyphyllum

Alyssum montanum var. brachyphyllum, commonly known as the mountain alyssum, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a low-growing perennial with dense clusters of small, fragrant white or pale yellow flowers. The plant features hairy stems and leaves that are typically narrow and linear. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or alpine habitats.

Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and border plantings.
