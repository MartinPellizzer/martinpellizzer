Achimenes longiflora var. vera

Achimenes longiflora var. vera, commonly known as the long-flowered achimenes, is a tropical perennial plant native to South America.

It features striking, tubular flowers that range in color from red to pink and violet, often with a contrasting throat. The plant produces showy, spurred flowers that attract hummingbirds and other pollinators. Its foliage consists of heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem.

It thrives in warm, humid conditions and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant in gardens and greenhouses.
