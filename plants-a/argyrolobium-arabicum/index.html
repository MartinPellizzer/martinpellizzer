Argyrolobium Arabicum

Argyrolobium arabicum is a flowering plant native to arid regions of the Middle East and North Africa. It belongs to the legume family and is known for its delicate, white or pale pink blooms. This species thrives in sandy and rocky soils, often found in coastal and desert environments. Its ability to survive in harsh conditions makes it an important part of local ecosystems. The plant is also valued for its potential in ecological restoration and sustainable agriculture.
