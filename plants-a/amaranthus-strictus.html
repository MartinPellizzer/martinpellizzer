Amaranthus strictus

Amaranthus strictus, commonly known as the upright amaranth, is a species of flowering plant in the amaranth family.

It is native to the Americas and is characterized by its upright growth habit and dense, feathery foliage. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from green to red, depending on the variety. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.

Amaranthus strictus is also valued for its potential use in traditional medicine and as a source of edible seeds.
