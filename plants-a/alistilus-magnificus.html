Alistilus magnificus

Alistilus magnificus, commonly known as the grandiose beauty, is a striking flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy inflorescences that display vibrant colors ranging from deep red to bright yellow. The plant features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating, aiding in moisture retention. It thrives in humid, well-drained soils and requires partial to full sun exposure for optimal growth.

Alistilus magnificus is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a decorative plant in indoor settings.
