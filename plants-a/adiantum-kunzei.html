Adiantum kunzei

Adiantum kunzei, commonly known as the royal fern, is a species of fern native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the family Adiantaceae and is characterized by its delicate, lace-like fronds that are typically bright green. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories. Its rhizomes are fibrous, and it produces small, clustered spore cases on the undersides of its fronds.

Adiantum kunzei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens with high humidity and indirect light.
