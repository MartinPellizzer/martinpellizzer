Aquilegia vulgaris mixta

Aquilegia vulgaris mixta, commonly known as the garden columbine, is a hybrid perennial plant native to Europe.

It features showy, spurred flowers that come in a variety of colors including red, yellow, and purple. The plant has palmately divided, compound leaves that are typically dark green and slightly hairy. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for garden borders.

Its botanical characteristics make it both ornamental and ecologically valuable, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies.
