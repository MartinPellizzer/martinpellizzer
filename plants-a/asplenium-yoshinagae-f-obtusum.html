Asplenium yoshinagae f. obtusum

Asplenium yoshinagae f. obtusum, commonly known as the Yoshinaga's spleenwort, is a fern species characterized by its distinctive morphology and ecological preferences.

It belongs to the family Aspleniaceae and is noted for its small, compact growth habit. The fronds are typically short and upright, with a leathery texture and a dark green color. This fern thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in rocky or forested areas.

Its unique features make it a subject of interest for botanists and horticulturists alike.
