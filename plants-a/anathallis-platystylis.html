Anathallis platystylis

Anathallis platystylis, commonly known as the broad-styled anathallis, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, pendulous inflorescence and small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments within the understory of tropical forests. Its floral structure is specialized for pollination by specific insect species, particularly moths.

Anathallis platystylis is noted for its unique morphology and ecological significance in its native habitat.
