Athrixia asteroides

Athrixia asteroides, commonly known as the starry sandbur, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, spiny seed heads that resemble small stars, which give it its common name. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, followed by the development of its characteristic seed pods. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and coastal dunes.

Athrixia asteroides is an annual plant, completing its life cycle within a single growing season.
