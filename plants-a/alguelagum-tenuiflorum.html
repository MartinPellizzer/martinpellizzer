Alguelagum tenuiflorum

Alguelagum tenuiflorum, commonly known as the slender-flower desert plant, is a succulent native to arid regions of North Africa and the Middle East.

It features a short, thick stem with small, narrow leaves that help reduce water loss in its harsh environment. The plant produces tiny, pale yellow flowers that bloom in the early morning, adapting to the extreme temperatures of its habitat. Its root system is deep and extensive, allowing it to access underground water sources.

Due to its resilience, it is often used in xeriscaping and as a symbol of survival in desert ecosystems.
