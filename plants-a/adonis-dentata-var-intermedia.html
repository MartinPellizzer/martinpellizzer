Adonis dentata var. intermedia

Adonis dentata var. intermedia, commonly known as the intermediate spearthistle, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically grows in dry, rocky soils and thrives in sunny environments. The plant features basal leaves that are lance-shaped with serrated edges, and its flowers are yellow, arranged in a dense, terminal cluster. It produces small, oval seeds that are dispersed by wind, aiding in its spread.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes considered a weed in agricultural areas.
