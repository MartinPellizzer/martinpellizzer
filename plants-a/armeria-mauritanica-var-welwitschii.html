Armeria mauritanica var. welwitschii

Armeria mauritanica var. welwitschii, commonly known as the Cape Diamond, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of South Africa.

It is a low-growing, mat-forming perennial that produces dense clusters of white or pink flowers. The plant has linear, silver-blue leaves that are adapted to withstand coastal conditions such as salt spray and strong winds. It thrives in well-drained sandy soils and is often found in dune environments.

The flowers bloom in spring and are attractive to pollinators like bees and butterflies.
