Arnica petiolaris

Arnica petiolaris, commonly known as the slender arnica, is a flowering plant native to the Arctic and subarctic regions.

It belongs to the sunflower family (Asteraceae) and is characterized by its yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and grows in rocky or sandy soils. It is often found in alpine tundra and is adapted to cold, harsh environments.

Arnica petiolaris is also known for its medicinal properties, though it is less commonly used than other arnica species.
