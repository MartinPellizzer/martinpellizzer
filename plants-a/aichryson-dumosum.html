Aichryson dumosum

Aichryson dumosum, commonly known as the dense cushion plant, is a succulent shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, low-growing form with dense clusters of fleshy, spiny leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers in the spring, which are clustered in inflorescences at the ends of the stems. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Aichryson dumosum is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and hardy nature.
