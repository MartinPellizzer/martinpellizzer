Acianthera jordanensis

Acianthera jordanensis, commonly known as Jordan's orchid, is a rare and endemic orchid species native to the Middle East, specifically found in Jordan.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, showy flowers that typically bloom in spring. The plant has a compact growth habit with fleshy, strap-like leaves that help it conserve water in its arid environment. Its flowers are usually pale pink to white, with intricate patterns that aid in pollination by specific insect species.
