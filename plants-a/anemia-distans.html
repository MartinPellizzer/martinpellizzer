Anemia distans

Anemia distans, commonly known as the distant anemia, is a rare flowering plant native to certain regions of South America.

It is characterized by its unique, elongated leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in humid tropical forests.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a valuable species in botanical research.
