Aloe metallica

Aloe metallica, commonly known as the silver aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are covered in a distinctive silvery-blue waxy coating, which helps reduce water loss in arid environments. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring, adding ornamental value. Its leaves contain a gel-like substance that is sometimes used for its soothing properties.

Aloe metallica is well-suited for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements.
