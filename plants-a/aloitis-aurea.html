Aloitis aurea

Aloitis aurea, commonly known as the Orange Carpet Plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features compact, mat-forming growth with fleshy, blue-green leaves that are covered in a fine, white powdery coating. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the summer months, adding a delicate touch to its appearance. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Aloitis aurea is valued for its ornamental appeal and low maintenance requirements.
