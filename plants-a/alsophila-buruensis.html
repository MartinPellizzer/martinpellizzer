Alsophila buruensis

Alsophila buruensis, commonly known as the Buru tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to Indonesia.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds. The plant has a robust root system that helps it thrive in moist, shaded environments. Its fronds can reach lengths of up to 3 meters, making it a striking feature in its natural habitat.

Alsophila buruensis plays an important ecological role in tropical rainforests by providing shelter and habitat for various organisms.
