Acianthera cogniauxiana

Acianthera cogniauxiana, commonly known as the Cogniaux's acianthera, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are usually elongated and arranged in a rosette pattern.

The flowers, which are usually pink or white, bloom during the warmer months and attract various pollinators.
