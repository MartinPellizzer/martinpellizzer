Alocasia hollrungii

Alocasia hollrungii, commonly known as the Hollrung's elephant ear, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length. The plant thrives in humid, warm environments with bright, indirect light. Its foliage is often a deep green with a glossy texture, making it a striking ornamental plant.

Alocasia hollrungii requires well-draining soil and regular watering to maintain its health and vigor.
