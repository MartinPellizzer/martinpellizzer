Adenaria floribunda var. parvifolia

Adenaria floribunda var. parvifolia, commonly known as the small-flowered adenaria, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit. The plant produces clusters of small, pale green to white flowers that bloom in summer. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often arranged in a rosette pattern.

It is typically found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories or rocky outcrops.
