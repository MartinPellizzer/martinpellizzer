Acronema minus

Acronema minus, commonly known as the lesser acronema, is a small herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. The plant has slender, branching stems and small, opposite leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are tiny and clustered in dense inflorescences, usually greenish or white in color.

Acronema minus is valued in traditional medicine for its potential anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties.
