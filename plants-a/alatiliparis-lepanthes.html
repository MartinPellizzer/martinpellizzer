Alatiliparis lepanthes

Alatiliparis lepanthes, commonly known as the Lepanthes orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a small, epiphytic plant native to the tropical regions of Central and South America. The plant features slender, wiry stems and produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. Its leaves are typically narrow and arranged in a rosette pattern, aiding in water retention.

Alatiliparis lepanthes thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
