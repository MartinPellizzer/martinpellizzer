Arenaria arvatica

Arenaria arvatica, commonly known as mountain sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of North America.

It typically thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in alpine or subalpine environments. The plant produces small, white or pink flowers during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are usually small, fleshy, and arranged in a rosette pattern.

Arenaria arvatica is adapted to harsh conditions, making it a resilient species in high-altitude ecosystems.
