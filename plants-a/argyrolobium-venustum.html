Argyrolobium venustum

Argyrolobium venustum, commonly known as the silver globular vetch, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive silver-gray foliage and spherical, globular seed pods. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats.

Argyrolobium venustum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
