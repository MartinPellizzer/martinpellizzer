Aster bidwillii

Aster bidwillii, commonly known as the Bidwill's aster, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. Its flowers are usually blue to purple in color, with ray florets surrounding a central disk.

Aster bidwillii is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive blooms.
