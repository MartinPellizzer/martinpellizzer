Alternanthera reinhardii

Alternanthera reinhardii, commonly known as the red-stemmed Alternanthera, is a striking aquatic or semi-aquatic plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red or purple stems and variegated, lance-shaped leaves that often display shades of green, white, and pink. This plant thrives in warm, shallow waters and is popular in aquariums for its ornamental value. It prefers moderate to high light conditions and can grow both submerged and emersed.

Its rapid growth rate makes it an excellent choice for background planting in planted tanks.
