Amomum ranongense

Amomum ranongense, commonly known as Ranong cardamom, is a tropical perennial plant belonging to the ginger family, Zingiberaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly Thailand, Malaysia, and Indonesia, and thrives in humid, shaded environments. Amomum ranongense is valued for its unique flavor and medicinal properties, making it an important species in regional agriculture and pharmacology.
