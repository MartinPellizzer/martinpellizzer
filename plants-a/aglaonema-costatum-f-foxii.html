Aglaonema costatum f. foxii

Aglaonema costatum f. foxii, commonly known as the Foxii Variegated Aglaonema, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated foliage with shades of white, cream, and green. The leaves are typically broad, lance-shaped, and have a waxy texture. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate humidity and well-draining soil.

This plant is popular as a houseplant due to its aesthetic appeal and low maintenance requirements.
