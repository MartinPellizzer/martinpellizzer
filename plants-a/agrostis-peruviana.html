Agrostis peruviana

Agrostis peruviana, commonly known as the Peruvian bentgrass, is a species of grass native to South America.

It is characterized by its fine, tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, temperate environments, often found in meadows and along stream banks. Its flowering stems bear small, spike-like inflorescences with numerous tiny flowers.

Agrostis peruviana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
