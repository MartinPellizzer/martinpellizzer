Albizia longepedata

Albizia longepedata, commonly known as the longpod albizia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant features bipinnate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, and its flowers are typically pale yellow to cream in color.

Albizia longepedata produces long, slender pods that contain several seeds, and it is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
