Arundarbor tagoara

Arundarbor tagoara, commonly known as the silverleaf vine, is a deciduous climbing plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are typically covered in a fine silvery down, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow, often followed by small, round berries. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and requires partial to full sunlight.

Its rapid growth makes it suitable for trellises or as a ground cover in shaded areas.
