Asplenium usambarense

Asplenium usambarense, commonly known as the Usambara spleenwort, is a fern native to the mountainous regions of East Africa.

It belongs to the family Aspleniaceae and is characterized by its small, delicate fronds. The plant typically grows on rocky substrates or in shaded, moist environments. Its spore-producing structures, called sorus, are located on the underside of the fronds.

Asplenium usambarense is often found in forested areas and is valued for its ornamental appearance and ecological significance.
