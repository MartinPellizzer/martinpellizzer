Aningeria altissima

Aningeria altissima, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a slightly fuzzy appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Aningeria altissima is also valued for its ornamental beauty and ability to attract pollinators.
