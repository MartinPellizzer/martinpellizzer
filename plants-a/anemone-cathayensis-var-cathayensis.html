Anemone cathayensis var. cathayensis

Anemone cathayensis var. cathayensis, commonly known as the Chinese anemone, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to eastern Asia.

It features showy, white to pink flowers that bloom in early spring, typically from March to May. The plant has basal leaves that are heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are hairy and often reddish in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable addition to both ornamental and ecological landscapes.
