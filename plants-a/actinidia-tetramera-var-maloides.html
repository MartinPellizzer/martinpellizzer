Actinidia tetramera var. maloides

Actinidia tetramera var. maloides, commonly known as the Chinese may apple, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features compound leaves with a distinctive heart-shaped base and serrated edges. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. Its fruit is a yellowish-green berry that ripens in late summer.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its foliage and seasonal interest.
