Aeschynanthus × splendidus

Aeschynanthus × splendidus, commonly known as the splendid waxplant, is a hybrid shrub native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its pendulous, tubular flowers that are typically bright red or orange, often with contrasting yellow throats. The plant produces small, fleshy berries that contain the seeds. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Aeschynanthus × splendidus is often cultivated for its ornamental value in hanging baskets and as a decorative plant in greenhouses.
