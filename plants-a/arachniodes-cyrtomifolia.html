Arachniodes cyrtomifolia

Arachniodes cyrtomifolia, commonly known as the spider fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to eastern Asia.

It features delicate, arching fronds with a distinctive, lace-like appearance, giving it its common name. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit, allowing it to spread slowly and form dense clumps. Its leaves are typically dark green and have a finely divided, feathery structure.

It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often used in ornamental gardening for its aesthetic appeal.
