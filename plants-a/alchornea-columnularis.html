Alchornea columnularis

Alchornea columnularis, commonly known as the columnar alchornea, is a shrub or small tree native to tropical regions of Africa and Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its upright, columnar growth habit, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. Its small, greenish-white flowers are clustered in dense inflorescences, and it produces small, fleshy fruits that contain several seeds.

Alchornea columnularis is often used in traditional medicine and is valued for its ornamental qualities.
