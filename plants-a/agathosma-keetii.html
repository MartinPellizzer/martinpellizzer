Agathosma keetii

Agathosma keetii, commonly known as Keet's buchu, is a small shrub native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, pinnately compound leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are typically clustered in umbels. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine and as a flavoring agent.

Agathosma keetii thrives in well-drained soils and prefers a sunny to partially shaded environment.
