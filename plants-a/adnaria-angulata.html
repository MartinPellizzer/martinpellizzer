Adnaria angulata

Adnaria angulata, commonly known as the angular adnara, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its angular, often thorny stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal and rocky garden environments.

Adnaria angulata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional herbal medicine.
