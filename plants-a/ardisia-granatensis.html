Ardisia granatensis

Ardisia granatensis, commonly known as the red-berried evergreen, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, dark green leaves and produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is notable for its bright red berries, which are ornamental and attract birds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.

Ardisia granatensis is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.
