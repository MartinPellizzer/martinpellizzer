Anthemis rhodocentra

Anthemis rhodocentra, commonly known as the red centaury, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads.
