Ardisia mjoebergii

Ardisia mjoebergii, commonly known as the African star jasmine, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to southern Africa.

It features glossy, dark green leaves and produces small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in shaded environments, making it a popular choice for indoor and shaded outdoor gardens. Its ornamental value is enhanced by the appearance of red, berry-like fruits that follow the flowers.

Ardisia mjoebergii is also valued for its air-purifying qualities and is often used in horticultural settings for its aesthetic and ecological benefits.
