Astrocaryum manaoense

Astrocaryum manaoense, commonly known as the manao palm, is a species of palm native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate leaves that can reach several meters in length. The plant produces yellowish-white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by elongated, woody fruits containing a single seed. Its leaves are often used in traditional crafts, and the palm is valued for its ornamental qualities.

Astrocaryum manaoense plays an important ecological role in its native habitat, providing food and shelter for various wildlife species.
