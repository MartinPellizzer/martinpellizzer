Aster carnerosanus

Aster carnerosanus, commonly known as the Carnerosan aster, is a herbaceous perennial native to the western United States.

It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. The plant produces showy blue to purple flowers that bloom in the summer months. It has alternate, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

Aster carnerosanus is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting local pollinators.
