Anthericum sofiense

Anthericum sofiense, commonly known as the Sofia arum, is a flowering plant native to the Balkans.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its striking, spatulate leaves and unique inflorescence. The plant produces a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe, which is typical of many arum species. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Anthericum sofiense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
