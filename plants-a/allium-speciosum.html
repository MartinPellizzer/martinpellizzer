Allium speciosum

Allium speciosum, commonly known as the showy onion, is a bulbous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features striking purple to pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, edible bulbs underground. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Allium speciosum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
