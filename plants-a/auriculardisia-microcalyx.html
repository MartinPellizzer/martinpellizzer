Auriculardisia microcalyx

Auriculardisia microcalyx, commonly known as the small-hooded orchid, is a species of flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and small, hooded flowers that are typically pale green to yellowish in color. Its leaves are ovate and arranged in a basal rosette, often covered with a fine layer of white pubescence.

This orchid thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.
