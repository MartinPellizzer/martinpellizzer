Abutilon tubulosum var. tubulosum

Abutilon tubulosum var. tubulosum, commonly known as velvetleaf, is a tropical shrub native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves that are covered in a dense, velvety pubescence. The plant produces yellow flowers that are clustered in terminal panicles, followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in disturbed soils.

Due to its rapid growth and invasive potential, it is considered a weed in many regions.
