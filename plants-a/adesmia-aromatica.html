Adesmia aromatica

Adesmia aromatica, commonly known as the fragrant adesmia, is a small shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, showy flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has a compact growth habit with dense foliage, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a decorative plant. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

The species is relatively easy to care for, thriving in well-drained soil and full sun.
