Antidesma leucopodum

Antidesma leucopodum, commonly known as the whitefoot antidesma, is a shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its opposite, simple leaves that are usually ovate to elliptic in shape. The species is valued for its ornamental potential and has some traditional uses in local medicine.
