Anthurium ensifolium

Anthurium ensifolium, commonly known as the sword leaf anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its long, narrow, sword-shaped leaves that can grow up to several feet in length. The plant produces striking, colorful spathes that range from red to orange, often with a contrasting green or yellow spathe. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Anthurium ensifolium is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
