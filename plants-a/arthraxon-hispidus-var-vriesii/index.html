Arthraxon Hispidus Var. Vriesii

Arthraxon hispidus var. vriesii is a unique grass species native to parts of Southeast Asia. It is known for its distinctive hairy leaves and robust growth habit. This variety is often found in moist, fertile soils along riverbanks and wetlands. Its ability to thrive in wet conditions makes it an important part of local ecosystems. Due to its ornamental value and ecological role, it is gaining attention among botanists and gardeners alike.
