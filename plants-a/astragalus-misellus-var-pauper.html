Astragalus misellus var. pauper

Astragalus misellus var. pauper, commonly known as the poor astragalus, is a member of the legume family Fabaceae.

It is a small, perennial herb characterized by its slender stems and small, yellowish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is native to regions of western North America. Its leaves are compound, with small, oval leaflets, and it produces pod-like fruits that contain several seeds.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
