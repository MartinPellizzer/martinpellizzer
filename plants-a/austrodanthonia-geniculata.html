Austrodanthonia geniculata

Austrodanthonia geniculata, commonly known as the narrow leaf kangaroo grass, is a perennial grass species native to southeastern Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands and grasslands.

Austrodanthonia geniculata is valued for its ecological role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for native wildlife.
