Acacia zygioides

Acacia zygioides, commonly known as the narrow-leaf wattle, is a shrub native to Australia.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is drought-tolerant, making it suitable for arid and semi-arid regions.

Acacia zygioides is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, improving soil fertility in degraded landscapes.
