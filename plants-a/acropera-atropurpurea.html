Acropera atropurpurea

Acropera atropurpurea, commonly known as the purple nightshade, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes nightshades, tomatoes, and peppers. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically purple or violet in color. Its fruit is a fleshy berry that contains several seeds.
