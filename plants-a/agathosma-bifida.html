Agathosma bifida

Agathosma bifida, commonly known as African geranium, is a small, perennial shrub native to South Africa.

It features aromatic, narrow leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. The plant produces small, pink or white flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens.

Its essential oils are valued for their use in aromatherapy and natural remedies.
