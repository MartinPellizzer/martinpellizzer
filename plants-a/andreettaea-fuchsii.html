Andreettaea fuchsii

Andreettaea fuchsii, commonly known as the Andreetta's cactus, is a species of flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its cylindrical stem and clusters of yellow flowers. The plant is adapted to arid environments, featuring spines for protection and water storage in its stems. It typically grows in rocky soils and is found in the Brazilian state of Minas Gerais.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat threats, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
