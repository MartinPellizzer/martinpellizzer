Athyrium baldwinii

Athyrium baldwinii, commonly known as Baldwin's sword fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its graceful, arching fronds that are typically dark green with a silvery underside. The plant prefers moist, shaded environments and thrives in rich, well-drained soil. It produces sporangia on the underside of its fronds, which are clustered in sori.

This fern is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive foliage and adaptability to various garden conditions.
