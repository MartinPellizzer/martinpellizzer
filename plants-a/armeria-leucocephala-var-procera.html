Armeria leucocephala var. procera

Armeria leucocephala var. procera, commonly known as the tall white-flowered thrift, is a perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features dense clusters of white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has succulent, fleshy leaves that are typically gray-green in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its compact growth habit and long flowering period make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
