Arachnis calcarata subsp. longisepala

Arachnis calcarata subsp.

longisepala, commonly known as the long-sepaled arachnis, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia. It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers.

The subspecies is distinguished by its elongated sepals, which give it its common name. It typically grows in shaded, moist habitats such as forest understories.

The plant produces showy, white to pale yellow blooms that attract various pollinators.
