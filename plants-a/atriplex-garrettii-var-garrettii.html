Atriplex garrettii var. garrettii

Atriplex garrettii var. garrettii, commonly known as silverleaf chenopod, is a perennial shrub native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, reflective hairs. The plant produces small, greenish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in sandy or rocky soils.

Its botanical features make it well adapted to drought conditions and poor soil nutrients.
