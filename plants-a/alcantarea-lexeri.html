Alcantarea lexeri

Alcantarea lexeri, commonly known as the São Miguel pineapple.

This plant is a member of the Bromeliaceae family and is native to the Azores, specifically the island of São Miguel. It is a striking plant with large, colorful, and fleshy leaves that form a rosette shape. The leaves are often covered in a waxy cuticle, which helps the plant retain moisture in its native habitat.

Alcantarea lexeri produces showy, yellowish flowers that emerge from the center of the leaf rosette.
