Asparagus nodosus

Asparagus nodosus, commonly known as the knotty asparagus, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features slender, branched stems that are often covered in small, spiny projections. The plant produces small, white to greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in spring. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Asparagus nodosus is typically found in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.
