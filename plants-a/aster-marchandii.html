Aster marchandii

Aster marchandii, commonly known as the Sierra Nevada aster, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of California and Nevada.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils at high elevations. The plant features a basal rosette of narrow, hairy leaves and produces small, clustered flowers in shades of blue to purple. It blooms in the summer months and is adapted to the harsh, alpine environment.

Aster marchandii is an important component of the local flora and is often found in meadows and open slopes.
