Aria pallescens

Aria pallescens, commonly known as the pale arrowleaf, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean islands.

It is characterized by its arrowhead-shaped leaves that are typically pale green in color. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters. It thrives in moist, tropical environments and is often found near water sources.

Aria pallescens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
