Amaioua africana

Amaioua africana, commonly known as the African velvet tamarind, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and showy yellow flowers. The tree produces flat, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It is often found in savanna and woodland habitats, thriving in well-drained soils.

Its botanical features make it an important species for both ecological and potential medicinal uses.
