Arabidopsis Halleri F. Alpicola

Arabidopsis halleri f. alpicola is a rare and specialized plant species found in alpine environments. It belongs to the Brassicaceae family and is closely related to the more common Arabidopsis thaliana. This subspecies is adapted to cold and nutrient-poor soils, thriving in high-altitude regions. Its small size and unique ecological niche make it an important subject for botanical research. Studying Arabidopsis halleri f. alpicola provides insights into plant adaptation and survival in extreme conditions.
