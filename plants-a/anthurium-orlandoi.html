Anthurium orlandoi

Anthurium orlandoi, commonly known as the Orange-Red Anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped leaves that are typically deep green with a reddish tinge. The plant produces striking spadices, which are the modified leaves that enclose the true flowers. Anthurium orlandoi thrives in humid, warm environments with bright, indirect light.

It is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
