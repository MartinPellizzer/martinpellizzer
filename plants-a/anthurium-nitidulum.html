Anthurium nitidulum

Anthurium nitidulum, commonly known as the velvet leaf anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its dark green, velvety leaves that are glossy and lanceolate in shape. The plant produces striking spadices, which are modified leaves that enclose the true flowers. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Anthurium nitidulum is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its aesthetic appeal and air-purifying qualities.
