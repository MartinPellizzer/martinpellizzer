Alstroemeria versicolor f. cummingiana

Alstroemeria versicolor f. cummingiana, commonly known as the Cumming's alstroemeria, is a striking hybrid cultivar of the alstroemeria genus.

It is characterized by its vibrant, multicolored flowers that display a mix of red, orange, and yellow hues. The plant is native to South America and is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and floral arrangements. It typically grows as a herbaceous perennial with strap-like leaves and showy, clustered blooms.

This variety is well-suited for temperate climates and requires well-drained soil and full sun to thrive.
