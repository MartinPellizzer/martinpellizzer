Aegiphila novogranatensis

Aegiphila novogranatensis, commonly known as the Colombian shrub or mountain sage, is a flowering plant native to the Andean regions of South America.

The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit, hairy stems, and small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to purple. It thrives in high-altitude environments and is often found in rocky or mountainous terrain.

Aegiphila novogranatensis is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
