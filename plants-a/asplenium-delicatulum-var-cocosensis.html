Asplenium delicatulum var. cocosensis

Asplenium delicatulum var. cocosensis, commonly known as the coconut spleenwort, is a species of fern belonging to the family Aspleniaceae.

It is a small, epiphytic or lithophytic fern that typically grows on trees or rocks in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant has delicate, feather-like fronds that are usually dark green and finely divided. It produces spores in spore capsules that are clustered on the underside of the fronds.

This variety is notable for its unique morphology and adaptability to humid, shaded environments.
