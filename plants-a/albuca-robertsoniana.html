Albuca robertsoniana

Albuca robertsoniana, commonly known as the Robertson's albuca, is a bulbous plant native to southern Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of white or pale yellow flowers. The plant is adapted to arid environments and requires well-draining soil. It blooms in late summer or early autumn, adding ornamental value to gardens.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is often cultivated in controlled settings.
