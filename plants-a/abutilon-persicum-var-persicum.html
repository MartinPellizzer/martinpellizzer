Abutilon persicum var. persicum

Abutilon persicum var. persicum, commonly known as the Persian velvetleaf, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its hairy, ovate leaves that are often tinged with red or purple on the underside. The plant produces clusters of small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its fruit is a spiny capsule that contains several seeds.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular ornamental plant in gardens.
