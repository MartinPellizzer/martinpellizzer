Anthurium cutucuense

Anthurium cutucuense, commonly known as the Cutucu anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the Amazon rainforest.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and striking spadix inflorescence. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its vibrant colors and unique foliage make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Anthurium cutucuense is also valued for its ability to purify the air and enhance indoor environments.
