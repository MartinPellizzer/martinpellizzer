Amaranthus ataco

Amaranthus ataco, commonly known as red amaranth, is a species of flowering plant in the amaranth family.

It is native to the tropical regions of the Americas and is characterized by its vibrant red or purple foliage. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its striking leaf color.

Amaranthus ataco is also valued in some traditional cuisines for its edible leaves.
