Aconitum lycoctonum var. cynoctonum

Aconitum lycoctonum var. cynoctonum, commonly known as the European aconite, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features striking blue to purple flowers that bloom in late summer, typically from July to September. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are deeply divided, giving it a fern-like appearance. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

However, it is highly toxic, containing potent alkaloids that can be fatal if ingested.
