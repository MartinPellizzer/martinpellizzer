Aa argyrolepis

Aa argyrolepis, commonly known as the silverleaf pittosporum, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the family Pittosporaceae and is characterized by its dark green, glossy leaves that have a silvery underside. The plant produces small, fragrant white or yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It is often found in coastal and lowland areas, thriving in well-drained soils.

Aa argyrolepis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is used in landscaping due to its hardiness and adaptability.
