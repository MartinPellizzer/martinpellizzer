Adromischus fusiformis

Adromischus fusiformis, commonly known as the football plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, cylindrical leaves that are typically green with a bluish tinge, arranged in a rosette formation. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Its compact growth habit and unique appearance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and container plantings.
