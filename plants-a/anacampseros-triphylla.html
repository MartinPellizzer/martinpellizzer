Anacampseros triphylla

Anacampseros triphylla, commonly known as the three-leaf ice plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features a low-growing, mat-forming structure with fleshy, blue-green leaves that store water. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for desert gardens.
