Aster pickeringii

Aster pickeringii, commonly known as Pickering's aster, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in dry, rocky habitats. The plant features showy blue to purple flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.

Aster pickeringii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance to local pollinators.
