Acanthopanax esquirolii

Acanthopanax esquirolii, commonly known as the Chinese skullcap, is a climbing shrub native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant produces red berries that are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forested areas.

Its botanical features make it an important species in both ecological and medicinal contexts.
