Argyreia fulgens

Argyreia fulgens, commonly known as the flame vine, is a climbing plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant orange to red flowers that bloom in clusters along its stems. The plant has heart-shaped, velvety leaves that are often tinged with purple or reddish hues. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Argyreia fulgens is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its striking floral display.
