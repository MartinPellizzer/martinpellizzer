Asterocephalus rutifolius

Asterocephalus rutifolius, commonly known as the silverleaf daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies, sunflowers, and ragworts. The plant is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and showy yellow flowers that bloom in summer. It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in coastal regions.
