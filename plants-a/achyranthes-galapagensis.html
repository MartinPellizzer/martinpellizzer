Achyranthes galapagensis

Achyranthes galapagensis, commonly known as the Galápagos knotweed, is a flowering plant native to the Galápagos Islands.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows in moist, rocky soils along coastal areas. The plant features upright stems with opposite, ovate leaves that are covered in fine hairs. It produces small, clustered flowers that are usually pink or red in color.

The species is adapted to the unique ecological conditions of its island habitat.
