Actinea heterophylla var. linearifolia

Actinea heterophylla var. linearifolia, commonly known as the slenderleaf actinia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Araceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive, arrow-shaped leaves that are elongated and narrow, giving it a unique appearance. The plant produces small, clustered greenish-white flowers that are typically surrounded by a modified leaf called a spathe. It is native to tropical regions of the Americas and thrives in moist, shaded environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
