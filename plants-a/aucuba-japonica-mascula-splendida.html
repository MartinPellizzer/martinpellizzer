Aucuba japonica mascula-splendida

Aucuba japonica mascula-splendida, commonly known as the Japanese aucuba or variegated aucuba, is a compact evergreen shrub native to Japan.

It features glossy, dark green leaves with striking white and yellow variegation, which add visual interest to gardens. The plant produces small, inconspicuous yellowish-green flowers in the spring, followed by red, berry-like fruits that attract birds. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for woodland gardens or as a foundation plant.

Its ornamental foliage and hardy nature make it a popular choice for landscapers and gardeners.
