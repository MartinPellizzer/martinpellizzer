Acosta pallidior

Acosta pallidior, commonly known as the pale acosta, is a flowering plant species native to specific regions of South America.

It belongs to the family Acanthaceae and is characterized by its slender stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, pale yellow to greenish flowers that are often clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical forests.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
