Adelostigma senegalensis

Adelostigma senegalensis, commonly known as the Senegalese starling plant, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and trifoliate leaves composed of three oval-shaped leaflets. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pale yellow to white in color. It is native to the savannas and grasslands of West Africa, where it thrives in well-drained soils.

Adelostigma senegalensis is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
