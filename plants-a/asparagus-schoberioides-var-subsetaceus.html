Asparagus schoberioides var. subsetaceus

Asparagus schoberioides var. subsetaceus, commonly known as the silver asparagus or silver feather asparagus, is a perennial flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, silver-gray foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant yellow flowers in spring, which are followed by edible, needle-like berries. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements.
