Agrostis hygrometrica var. tandilensis

Agrostis hygrometrica var. tandilensis, commonly known as the Tandil bentgrass, is a perennial grass species native to South America.

It is characterized by its fine, tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. This variety is adapted to temperate climates and thrives in well-drained soils. It is often found in grasslands and open areas, contributing to the ecological diversity of its habitat.

Its botanical features make it an important plant for both ecological studies and horticultural applications.
