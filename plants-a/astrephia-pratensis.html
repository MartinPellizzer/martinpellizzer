Astrephia pratensis

Astrephia pratensis, commonly known as the marsh aster.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant native to wetland habitats in southern Africa. The plant features slender, upright stems and small, clustered flowers that bloom in shades of white and pink. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, adapted to thrive in moist, nutrient-rich soils.

Astrephia pratensis plays an important ecological role in its native environment by providing food and habitat for various insects and small animals.
