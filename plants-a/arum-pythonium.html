Arum pythonium

Arum pythonium, commonly known as the giant arrowhead, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features large, arrow-shaped leaves that can grow up to several feet in length, giving it a dramatic appearance. The plant produces a spadix, a dense cluster of small flowers surrounded by a showy, petal-like spathe. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in wetlands or along riverbanks.

Due to its unique foliage and ornamental value, it is cultivated in some tropical gardens.
