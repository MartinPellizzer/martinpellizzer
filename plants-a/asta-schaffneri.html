Asta schaffneri

Asta schaffneri, commonly known as the Schaffner's asta, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that range in color from white to yellow. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, cylindrical flower heads.

Asta schaffneri is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and rockeries.
