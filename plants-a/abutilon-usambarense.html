Abutilon usambarense

Abutilon usambarense, commonly known as the velvetleaf or African velvetleaf, is a shrub native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, soft hairs, giving it a velvety texture. The plant produces clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its fruits are capsules that contain numerous small, brown seeds.

Abutilon usambarense is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
