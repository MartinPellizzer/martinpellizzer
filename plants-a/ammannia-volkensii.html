Ammannia volkensii

Ammannia volkensii, commonly known as the red-flowered ammannia, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking red or pink flowers that bloom in clusters, adding vibrant color to its environment. The plant has alternate, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along riverbanks.

Ammannia volkensii is valued both for its ornamental appeal and its ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
