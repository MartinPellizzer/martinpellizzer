Aloe rigida

Aloe rigida, commonly known as the rigid aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically gray-green in color and have a spiny texture. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellowish-orange flowers during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Aloe rigida is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional medicinal uses.
