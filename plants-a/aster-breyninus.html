Aster breyninus

Aster breyninus, commonly known as the Breyning's aster, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and grasslands. It produces showy blue to purple flowers that bloom in the summer months.

Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
