Anthurium ernesti var. ernesti

Anthurium ernesti var. ernesti, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically deep green with a reddish or purplish tint. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-like inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, often in shades of red, orange, or yellow. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium ernesti var. ernesti is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and long-lasting blooms.
