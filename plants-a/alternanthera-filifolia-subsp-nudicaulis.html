Alternanthera filifolia subsp. nudicaulis

Alternanthera filifolia subsp.

nudicaulis, commonly known as the threadleaf Alternanthera, is a herbaceous perennial native to South America. It features slender, thread-like leaves that are often reddish or purplish in color, especially under bright light.

The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellowish. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full sun.

This species is popular in aquatic and bog gardens due to its ornamental foliage and ability to tolerate wet conditions.
