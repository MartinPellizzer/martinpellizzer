Anthurium cachoeirense

Anthurium cachoeirense, commonly known as the Cachoeira anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves and vibrant, spathaceous flowers. The plant thrives in humid, warm environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its foliage is often a deep green with a waxy texture, and the inflorescence consists of a colorful spathe and a spadix.

Anthurium cachoeirense is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.
