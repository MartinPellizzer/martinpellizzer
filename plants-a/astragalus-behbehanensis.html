Astragalus behbehanensis

Astragalus behbehanensis, commonly known as the Behbahan milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to Iran.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, pea-like flowers. The plant typically grows in arid and semi-arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to dry conditions.

It plays a role in soil stabilization and may have potential medicinal uses, though further research is needed.
