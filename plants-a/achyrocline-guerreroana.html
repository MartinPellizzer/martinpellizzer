Achyrocline guerreroana

Achyrocline guerreroana, commonly known as the Guerrero thyme, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments. Its leaves are aromatic and often used in traditional medicine.
