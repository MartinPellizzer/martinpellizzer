Ayenia reinwardtii

Ayenia reinwardtii, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that range in color from white to yellow. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and has a shrub-like structure with narrow, lance-shaped leaves. It produces small, tubular flowers that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Ayenia reinwardtii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
