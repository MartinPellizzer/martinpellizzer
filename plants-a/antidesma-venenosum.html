Antidesma venenosum

Antidesma venenosum, commonly known as the poison antidesma, is a tropical plant belonging to the family Euphorbiaceae.

It is native to regions of Africa, Asia, and the Pacific Islands, often growing in coastal or lowland areas. Due to its poisonous nature, it is often avoided in horticultural practices.
