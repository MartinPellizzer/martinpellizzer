Avena preslii

Avena preslii, commonly known as Presli's oat, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. Avena preslii is typically found in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.
