Alchemilla glaberrima var. firma

Alchemilla glaberrima var. firma, commonly known as the firm alkanet, is a perennial herb native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its low-growing habit and clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant has soft, hairy leaves that are often used in traditional herbal medicine. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland edges or rocky areas.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and ecological restoration projects.
