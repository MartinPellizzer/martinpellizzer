Astronium fraxinifolium f. mollissimum

Astronium fraxinifolium f. mollissimum, commonly known as the silky sycamore, is a variant of the sycamore tree characterized by its distinctive foliage.

The leaves are large, palmately lobed, and have a smooth, waxy texture that gives them a silky appearance. This form is typically found in tropical and subtropical regions, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant is part of the Euphorbiaceae family and is valued for its ornamental qualities.

Its bark and leaves have been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
