Acanthella conferta

Acanthella conferta, commonly known as the woolly acanthella, is a species of flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Acanthaceae and is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pale blue or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its unique appearance and ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
