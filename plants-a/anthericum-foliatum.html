Anthericum foliatum

Anthericum foliatum, commonly known as the broadleaf arum, is a herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It features a distinctive inflorescence consisting of a spadix surrounded by a showy spathe. The plant has broad, lance-shaped leaves that are typically green with a slightly waxy texture. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Anthericum foliatum is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
