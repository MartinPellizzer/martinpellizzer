Amaranthus chihuahuensis

Amaranthus chihuahuensis, commonly known as the Chihuahua amaranth, is a species of flowering plant native to the Chihuahua region of Mexico.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and lance-shaped leaves that are typically green to reddish in color. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range from green to red, depending on the stage of development. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, making it well adapted to drought conditions.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential use in traditional agriculture.
