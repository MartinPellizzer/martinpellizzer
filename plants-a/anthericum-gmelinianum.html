Anthericum gmelinianum

Anthericum gmelinianum, commonly known as the Gmelin’s lily, is a species of flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30–60 cm. The plant features strap-shaped, dark green leaves and produces showy, white or pale pink flowers in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Anthericum gmelinianum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its elegant appearance.
