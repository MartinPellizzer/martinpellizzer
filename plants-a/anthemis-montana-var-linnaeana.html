Anthemis montana var. linnaeana

Anthemis montana var. linnaeana, commonly known as the Linnaean anthemis, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its finely divided, feathery leaves and small, yellowish-white composite flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun conditions. It is known for its aromatic properties and has been used historically in traditional medicine.

The variety linnaeana is distinguished by its specific floral morphology and habitat preferences compared to other varieties of Anthemis montana.
