Agrostis shensiana

Agrostis shensiana, commonly known as the Chinese meadowgrass, is a perennial grass species native to Asia.

It typically grows in temperate climates and is often found in meadows, grasslands, and along riverbanks. The plant has slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves that are usually green and smooth. Its inflorescence consists of a spike-like cluster of small, spikelet-based flowers.

Agrostis shensiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping and ecological restoration projects.
