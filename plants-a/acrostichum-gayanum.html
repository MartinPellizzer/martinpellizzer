Acrostichum gayanum

Acrostichum gayanum, commonly known as the Sri Lankan fern, is a species of tree fern native to Sri Lanka.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, unbranched trunk covered in persistent, fibrous roots. The plant features large, pinnately compound leaves that can reach several meters in length. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

The fronds are typically bright green and contribute to the plant's ornamental value.
