Agathosma patula

Agathosma patula, commonly known as the Cape geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, daisy-like flowers and aromatic foliage. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

Agathosma patula is also cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and hardy nature.
