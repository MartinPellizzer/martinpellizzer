Achimenes longiflora var. latifolia

Achimenes longiflora var. latifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaved achimenes, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from red to pink and orange. The plant features broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, fuzzy texture. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is typically grown as a decorative plant in gardens or as a houseplant.

Its rapid growth and ability to produce numerous blooms make it a popular choice for ornamental purposes.
