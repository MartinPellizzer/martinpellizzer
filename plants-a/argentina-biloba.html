Argentina biloba

Argentina biloba, commonly known as the Argentine fan palm, is a striking plant native to the subtropical regions of Argentina.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and a crown of wide, fan-shaped leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and requires ample sunlight to flourish. Its leaves are often used in traditional crafts and as ornamental elements in gardens.

Argentina biloba is also valued for its ability to provide shade and its resilience to certain environmental conditions.
