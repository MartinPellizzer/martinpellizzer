Anemone narcissiflora var. protracta

Anemone narcissiflora var. protracta, commonly known as the protracta anemone, is a variant of the narcissus-flowered anemone.

It is a herbaceous perennial native to the eastern United States, particularly in the southeastern regions. The plant features showy, cup-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, often with a yellow center. Its foliage consists of heart-shaped, dark green leaves that are arranged in a basal rosette.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its early spring blooms.
