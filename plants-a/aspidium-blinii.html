Aspidium blinii

Aspidium blinii, commonly known as the Blin's water fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as along stream banks or in dense forests. Its rhizomes are often covered in a dense mat of roots, which help anchor it to substrates.
