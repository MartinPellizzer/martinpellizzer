Allium cyathophorum var. farreri

Allium cyathophorum var. farreri, commonly known as the purple bulbous onion, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It features spherical, purple-tinted bulbs that are often used in ornamental gardening due to their striking appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pinkish flowers during the spring and summer seasons. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically bluish-green in color.

This variety is valued for its hardiness and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
