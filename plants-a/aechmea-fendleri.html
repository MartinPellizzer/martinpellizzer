Aechmea fendleri

Aechmea fendleri, commonly known as the Mexican pineapple or the red-tipped pineapple, is a striking bromeliad native to Mexico and Central America.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that form a central cup, which collects rainwater for the plant's use. The leaves are typically dark green with red or purple tips, providing a visually appealing contrast. It produces a striking inflorescence with bright red flowers that emerge from the center of the rosette.

This plant thrives in bright, indirect light and is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
