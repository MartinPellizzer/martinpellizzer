Abutilon grewiifolium

Abutilon grewiifolium, commonly known as the woolly abutilon, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its soft, fuzzy leaves and showy, lantern-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in coastal areas.

Its unique appearance and ornamental value make it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
