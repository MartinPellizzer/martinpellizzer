Anthurium kayapii

Anthurium kayapii, commonly known as the Kayapi arrowhead, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of Brazil.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its unique inflorescence consisting of a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe. The plant features large, heart-shaped leaves that are often bright green with a glossy texture. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, with a spadix that can be either male or female.

Anthurium kayapii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.
