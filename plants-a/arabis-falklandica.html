Arabis falklandica

Arabis falklandica, commonly known as Falkland's rockcress, is a flowering plant native to the Falkland Islands.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically reaches heights of up to 10 centimeters. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are basal, rosette-forming, and covered in a fine layer of white hairs.

Arabis falklandica thrives in rocky, coastal environments and is adapted to the cool, windy climate of its native habitat.
