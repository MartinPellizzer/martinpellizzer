Aglaonema hospitum

Aglaonema hospitum, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated foliage that features shades of green, white, and yellow. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers, though it is more prized for its ornamental leaves than its blooms. It thrives in low-light conditions and requires moderate humidity and well-draining soil.

Aglaonema hospitum is a popular houseplant due to its air-purifying qualities and ability to thrive indoors.
