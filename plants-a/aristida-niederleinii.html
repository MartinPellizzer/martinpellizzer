Aristida niederleinii

Aristida niederleinii, commonly known as the Niederlein's threeawn, is a grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, erect growth habit and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky outcrops. Its leaves are narrow and hairy, and the flowers are usually pale green to brown in color.

Aristida niederleinii is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil in arid environments.
