Arthrophytum persicum

Arthrophytum persicum, commonly known as the Persian stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to the arid regions of Persia.

It is characterized by its fleshy, thick leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in dry environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and white, typically during the spring and summer months. Its compact growth habit and ability to survive with minimal water make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Arthrophytum persicum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and low maintenance requirements.
