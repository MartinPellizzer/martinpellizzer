Actinostemon polymorphus f. angustifolius

Actinostemon polymorphus f. angustifolius, commonly known as the narrow-leaved actinostemon, is a succulent plant characterized by its thick, fleshy stems and elongated, narrow leaves.

It belongs to the Asclepiadaceae family, which includes milkweeds, and is native to arid regions of southern Africa. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in umbels, adding a delicate floral display.

Its ability to store water in its leaves makes it well adapted to drought conditions, and it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant in dry gardens.
