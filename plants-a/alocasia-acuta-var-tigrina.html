Alocasia acuta var. tigrina

Alocasia acuta var. tigrina, commonly known as the Tiger's Tail Alocasia, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features large, heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive dark green color and prominent, contrasting white or silver veins. The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit and can reach heights of up to 6 feet. Its foliage is often used in ornamental horticulture for its dramatic visual appeal.

It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.
