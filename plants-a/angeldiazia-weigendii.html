Angeldiazia weigendii

Angeldiazia weigendii, commonly known as the angel's tears.

This plant is a member of the Cactaceae family and is native to the arid regions of South America. It is characterized by its tall, columnar stems and clusters of small, white flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant has a slow growth rate and requires well-draining soil to thrive.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and unique foliage.
