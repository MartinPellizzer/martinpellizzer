Aglaonema tenuipes

Aglaonema tenuipes, commonly known as the narrow-leaved Chinese evergreen, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, narrow leaves with distinctive white or cream-colored veins. The plant is evergreen, meaning it retains its foliage year-round, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening. It thrives in low-light conditions and requires moderate humidity and well-draining soil.

Aglaonema tenuipes is also known for its air-purifying qualities, contributing to its popularity as a houseplant.
