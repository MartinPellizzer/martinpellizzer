Argyrolobium harmsianum

Argyrolobium harmsianum, commonly known as the silver globular pea, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its spherical, silver-gray foliage and clusters of small, violet flowers. The plant belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is known for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil. It thrives in well-drained sandy soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological benefits, it is cultivated in gardens and restoration projects.
