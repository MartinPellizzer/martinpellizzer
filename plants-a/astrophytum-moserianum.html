Astrophytum moserianum

Astrophytum moserianum, commonly known as the "moon cactus," is a species of cactus native to the arid regions of Mexico.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stem that often appears segmented, giving it a unique appearance. The plant produces vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, typically blooming in the spring. Its fleshy, spiny stems store water, allowing it to survive in dry environments.

Astrophytum moserianum is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its low maintenance and striking visual appeal.
