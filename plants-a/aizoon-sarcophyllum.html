Aizoon sarcophyllum

Aizoon sarcophyllum, commonly known as the stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its fleshy, thick leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, typically during the summer months. It grows in rocky or gravelly soils and is often found in coastal areas.

Aizoon sarcophyllum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly used in rock gardens and xeriscaping.
