Aspidium aculeatum subsp. lobatum

Aspidium aculeatum subsp.

lobatum, commonly known as the lobate water fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae. It is characterized by its delicate, lanceolate fronds that are typically divided into lobes, giving it a distinctive appearance.

The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in temperate regions with high humidity. Its spores are produced on the underside of the fronds, and it reproduces through sexual reproduction via spore dispersal.

This fern is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
