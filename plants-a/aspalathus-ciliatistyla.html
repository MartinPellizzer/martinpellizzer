Aspalathus ciliatistyla

Aspalathus ciliatistyla, commonly known as the silver bush, is a small, evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its narrow, silvery-gray leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring, followed by small, round, brown fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Aspalathus ciliatistyla is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
