Adelia membranifolia f. hirsuta

Adelia membranifolia f. hirsuta, commonly known as the hairy membrane leaf.

It is a rare and unique variant of the Adelia membranifolia species, characterized by its hairy foliage and delicate, translucent leaves. The plant exhibits a compact growth habit with small, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine, soft hairs. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is typically found in tropical or subtropical regions.

Its botanical features make it a subject of interest for botanists and plant enthusiasts alike.
