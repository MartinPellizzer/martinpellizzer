Astragalus penduliflorus var. minhensis

Astragalus penduliflorus var. minhensis, commonly known as the Vietnamese milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow or pale green flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. It has small, hairy stems and produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds.

This variety is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphology and ecological adaptations.
