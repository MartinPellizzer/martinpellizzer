Anthurium blanchetianum

Anthurium blanchetianum, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves and vibrant red spathes that enclose the spadix. The plant thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil. Its flowers are typically short-lived but attract pollinators such as bees and hummingbirds.

Anthurium blanchetianum is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental value and ease of care.
