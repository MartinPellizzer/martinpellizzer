Anthericum liliago var. fontqueri

Anthericum liliago var. fontqueri, commonly known as the Fontqueri's lily of the valley, is a variant of the lily of the valley plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its clusters of small, bell-shaped white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has heart-shaped, glossy green leaves that form a dense rosette at the base. It produces a fragrant, tubular flower that attracts pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

The botanical features of this variety make it a popular choice for woodland gardens and naturalized landscapes.
