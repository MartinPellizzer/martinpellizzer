Azukia nakashimae

Azukia nakashimae, commonly known as the Japanese bean plant, is a species native to Japan.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes and beans. The plant is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, pea-like flowers. It produces pods that contain edible seeds, similar to other leguminous plants.

Azukia nakashimae is often cultivated for its nutritional value and is used in traditional Japanese cuisine.
