Aloe carowii

Aloe carowii, commonly known as the Cape aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, branching stem and fleshy, sword-shaped leaves that are covered in a layer of white powder. The plant produces yellow-orange flowers in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Aloe carowii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional medicinal uses.
