Anemopaegma robustum

Anemopaegma robustum, commonly known as the robust anemopaegma, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features upright, hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves with serrated edges. Its showy yellow flowers bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable addition to gardens in temperate climates.
