Anthurium lentii

Anthurium lentii, commonly known as the velvet leaf anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a velvety texture. The plant produces striking spadices, which are often red or orange, surrounded by a showy spathe. Anthurium lentii thrives in high humidity and indirect light, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique foliage and ornamental value make it a sought-after plant among horticulturists and plant enthusiasts.
