Alsophila sechellarum

Alsophila sechellarum, commonly known as the Seychelles tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to the Seychelles.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds. The plant has a slow growth rate and can live for several decades, making it a long-lived species. Its unique appearance and ecological significance make it a notable component of the Seychelles' flora.

The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and plays a role in the local ecosystem by providing habitat for various organisms.
