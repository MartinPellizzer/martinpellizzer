Anoectochilus acalcaratus

Anoectochilus acalcaratus, commonly known as the rock orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and is often found growing on rocky substrates. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are typically clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are usually fleshy and succulent, adapted to store water in its native arid environments.

Anoectochilus acalcaratus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
