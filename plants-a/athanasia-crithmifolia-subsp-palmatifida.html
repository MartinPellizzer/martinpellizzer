Athanasia crithmifolia subsp. palmatifida

Athanasia crithmifolia subsp.

palmatifida, commonly known as the palmate leaf everlasting, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense clusters of small, yellowish flowers.

The subspecies is distinguished by its palmately divided leaves, which give it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or coastal habitats.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its hardy nature and long-lasting blooms.
