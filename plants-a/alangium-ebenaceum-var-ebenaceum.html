It is native to Southeast Asia, including countries like India, Indonesia, and Malaysia. The plant is characterized by its evergreen leaves, which are typically elliptical and have a smooth texture. The wood of this plant is highly valued for its aromatic properties and has been traditionally used in incense, furniture, and medicinal applications.
