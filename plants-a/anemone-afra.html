Anemone afra

Anemone afra, commonly known as the African anemone, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its showy, pink to white flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has basal leaves that are palmately lobed and hairy, and its stems are usually erect. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Anemone afra is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
