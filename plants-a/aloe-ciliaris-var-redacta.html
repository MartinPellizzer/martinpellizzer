Aloe ciliaris var. redacta

Aloe ciliaris var. redacta, commonly known as the red-tipped aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, narrow leaves that are typically green with red or pinkish tips. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing yellow flowers, which attract pollinators like bees and butterflies. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
