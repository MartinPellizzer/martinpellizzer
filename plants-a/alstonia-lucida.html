Alstonia lucida

Alstonia lucida, commonly known as the white flame tree, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, giving it a luminous appearance. The plant has compound leaves with elongated, lance-shaped leaflets. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing as a deciduous tree or shrub.

Alstonia lucida is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
