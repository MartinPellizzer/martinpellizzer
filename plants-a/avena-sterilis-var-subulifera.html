Avena sterilis var. subulifera

Avena sterilis var. subulifera, commonly known as the slender oat, is a perennial grass species native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its narrow, needle-like leaves and compact growth habit. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny florets. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal habitats.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes used in landscaping or as a ornamental plant.
