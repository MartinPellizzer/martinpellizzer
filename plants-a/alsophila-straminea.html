Alsophila straminea

Alsophila straminea, commonly known as the slender tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and a crown of feathery fronds that can reach several meters in length. The plant has a fibrous root system and a rhizome that supports its growth. Its fronds are typically bright green and have a smooth, leathery texture.

Alsophila straminea thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical forests.
