Asplenium dielmannii

Asplenium dielmannii, commonly known as the African shield fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, rosette-like growth habit and distinctive, shield-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest undergrowth or rocky crevices. Its fronds are usually dark green with a leathery texture and often have a waxy coating.

Asplenium dielmannii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant.
