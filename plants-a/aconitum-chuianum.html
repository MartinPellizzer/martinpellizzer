Aconitum chuianum

Aconitum chuianum, commonly known as the Chinese monkshood, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue or purple spurred flowers. The plant features palmately compound leaves with a distinctive heart-shaped base. Aconitum chuianum thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and has traditional medicinal uses in Chinese herbal medicine.
