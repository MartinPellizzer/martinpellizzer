Anemia speciosa

Anemia speciosa, commonly known as the showy anemone, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It is characterized by its showy, pink to purple flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant has palmate, deeply lobed leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight.

Anemia speciosa is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens.
