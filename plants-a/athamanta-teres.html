Athamanta teres

Athamanta teres, commonly known as the narrow-leafed thyme.

It is a small, mat-forming perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region. The plant features slender, aromatic leaves that are often used in culinary applications. Its flowers are typically pink or purple, appearing in clusters during the summer months.

Athamanta teres thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy plant suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.
