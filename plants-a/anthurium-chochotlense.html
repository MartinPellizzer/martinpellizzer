Anthurium chochotlense

Anthurium chochotlense, commonly known as the Mexican flame anthurium, is a striking tropical plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped leaves and striking spadix inflorescence. The plant thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil. Its foliage is often a deep green with a reddish or maroon tint, adding visual interest to any indoor space.

Anthurium chochotlense is relatively low-maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
