Anthurium bajobonitense

Anthurium bajobonitense, commonly known as the Bajobonitense Arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped leaves that are often a deep green with a glossy texture. The plant produces striking spadices, which are modified leaves that enclose the true flowers, typically in shades of red or orange. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in botanical gardens.
