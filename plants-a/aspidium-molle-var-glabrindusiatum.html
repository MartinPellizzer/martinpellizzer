Aspidium molle var. glabrindusiatum

Aspidium molle var. glabrindusiatum, commonly known as the smooth shield fern, is a variant of the common shield fern, characterized by its smooth, glabrous fronds.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is native to tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features a rhizomatous growth habit with lanceolate, bipinnate leaves that are often dark green and leathery. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical significance lies in its morphological variation and ecological adaptability.
