Athyrium nyalamense

Athyrium nyalamense, commonly known as the Nyalam fern, is a species native to the Himalayan regions of Nepal and India.

It is a member of the Dryopteridaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, feather-like fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and mountainous regions. Its rhizomes are often covered in a dense layer of brown, hairy structures.

The spores are produced on the underside of the fronds, which are arranged in a distinctive, fan-like pattern.
