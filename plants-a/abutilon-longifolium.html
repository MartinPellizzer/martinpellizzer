Abutilon longifolium

Abutilon longifolium, commonly known as the woolly abutilon, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its fuzzy, gray-green leaves and showy, lantern-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its unique appearance and adaptability contribute to its appeal in both natural and cultivated settings.
