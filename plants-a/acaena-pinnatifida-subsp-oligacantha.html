Acaena pinnatifida subsp. oligacantha

Acaena pinnatifida subsp.

oligacantha, commonly known as the slender burr bush, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand. It belongs to the family Rosaceae and is characterized by its woody stems and dense, spiny bracts.

The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are followed by spherical, prickly fruits. It thrives in coastal and lowland environments, often forming dense shrubs.
