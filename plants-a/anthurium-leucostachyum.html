Anthurium leucostachyum

Anthurium leucostachyum, commonly known as the white spathified anthurium, is a member of the Araceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a metallic sheen. Its inflorescence consists of a spadix surrounded by a white, spatulate bract.

This species is valued in horticulture for its striking foliage and is often grown as a decorative plant.
