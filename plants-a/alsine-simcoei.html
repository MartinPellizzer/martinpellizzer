Alsine simcoei

Alsine simcoei, commonly known as the coastal alsike, is a low-growing perennial herb native to the coastal regions of the western United States.

It typically thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is adapted to salt spray and coastal winds. The plant features small, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of pale blue to violet flowers. It is often found in dunes and other coastal habitats where it plays a role in stabilizing sand.

Alsine simcoei is an important species for coastal ecosystems due to its resilience and ecological value.
