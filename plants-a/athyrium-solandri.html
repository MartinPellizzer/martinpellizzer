Athyrium solandri

Athyrium solandri, commonly known as the Solander's sword fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Athyriaceae and is characterized by its delicate, bipinnate fronds that are often arching or drooping. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its rhizomes are creeping and produce numerous fertile and sterile fronds.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
