Anthurium andraeanum var. divergens

Anthurium andraeanum var. divergens, commonly known as the Red-veined Anthurium, is a tropical flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves with deep red veins contrasting against dark green foliage. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-shaped inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, typically in shades of red or orange. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.

This variety is popular as a decorative plant due to its ornamental leaves and long-lasting blooms.
