Anemia × semihirsuta

Anemia × semihirsuta, commonly known as the hybrid hirsute anemia, is a botanical hybrid resulting from the cross between Anemia and Semihirsuta species.

It is characterized by its unique foliage, featuring a combination of hairy and smooth textures on its leaves. The plant typically exhibits a compact growth habit with broad, lance-shaped leaves that vary in color from dark green to reddish tones. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often grown as a decorative plant in gardens and as a houseplant.
