Anguria elliptica

Anguria elliptica, commonly known as the elliptic cucumber, is a species of flowering plant in the Cucurbitaceae family.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas and is characterized by its elliptical-shaped fruits. The plant produces yellow flowers and has trailing vines that can grow up to several meters in length. Its leaves are heart-shaped with a slightly hairy surface.

The fruit is typically green, turning yellow or orange when mature, and is edible, though less commonly cultivated than other cucumber varieties.
