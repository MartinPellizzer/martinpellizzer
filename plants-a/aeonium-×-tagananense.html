Aeonium × tagananense

Aeonium × tagananense, commonly known as the Tree Aeonium, is a hybrid succulent plant native to the Canary Islands.

It features a rosette of thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, with a distinctive central stem that can grow quite tall. The plant is known for its striking appearance and ability to form a tree-like structure as it matures. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Aeonium × tagananense is popular among succulent enthusiasts for its ornamental value and ease of care.
