Abutilon nobile

Abutilon nobile, commonly known as velvetleaf or Chinese lantern plant, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its velvety, hairy leaves that give it a soft, fuzzy appearance. The plant produces showy, lantern-shaped flowers that range in color from red to orange, often blooming in late summer. Its ornamental value comes from both its foliage and floral display.

It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is relatively drought-tolerant once established.
