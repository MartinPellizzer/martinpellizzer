Artemisia vulgaris var. americana

Artemisia vulgaris var. americana, commonly known as common mugwort, is a perennial herb native to North America.

It features silvery-green, aromatic leaves with a strong, camphor-like scent. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in dry, disturbed soils and is often found in fields, roadsides, and waste areas.

Its medicinal properties have been historically used in traditional herbal remedies.
