Ascaricida kotschyana

Ascaricida kotschyana, commonly known as the Kashmir Himalayan bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its bell-shaped, violet-blue flowers that bloom in the summer months. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture, and it thrives in cool, moist environments. Ascaricida kotschyana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in alpine gardens.
