Athanasia scabra

Athanasia scabra, commonly known as the rough everlasting, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense, hairy foliage and small, yellowish flowers. The plant is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it suitable for arid environments. Its common name reflects its ability to retain its appearance for long periods, resembling an everlasting flower.

Athanasia scabra is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and resilience.
