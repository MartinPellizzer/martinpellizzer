Argyreia nuda

Argyreia nuda, commonly known as the naked lady or sacred lotus, is a climbing vine native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant produces a distinctive, spiny capsule that contains numerous seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
