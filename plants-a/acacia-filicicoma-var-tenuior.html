Acacia filicicoma var. tenuior

Acacia filicicoma var. tenuior, commonly known as the slender-leaved acacia, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its narrow, feathery leaves that give it a delicate appearance. The plant produces yellow flowers in spikes, which are often visited by bees and other pollinators. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.

Its unique foliage and ornamental value make it a popular choice for drought-tolerant landscaping.
