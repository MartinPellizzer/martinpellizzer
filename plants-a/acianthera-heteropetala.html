Acianthera heteropetala, commonly known as the variable violet orchid, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, violet-colored flowers that bloom during the warmer months. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, such as forest understories. Its leaves are elongated and often have a waxy texture, which helps in retaining moisture.
