Arundinella laxiflora

Arundinella laxiflora, commonly known as the slender reed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences with white or pale pink flowers. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Arundinella laxiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
