Achillea × jaborneggii

Achillea × jaborneggii, commonly known as the hybrid yarrow, is a perennial plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is a hybrid between Achillea millefolium (common yarrow) and Achillea ptarmica (tansy). The plant features finely divided, fern-like leaves and produces clusters of small, white to pinkish flowers. It is often found in meadows and open woodland areas, thriving in full sun.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice in garden landscapes.
