Asplenium × baldense

Asplenium × baldense, commonly known as the hybrid shield fern, is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between *Asplenium trichomanes* and *Asplenium bulbiferum*.

It is characterized by its dense, leathery fronds that are typically dark green with a metallic sheen. The plant produces small, spore-bearing structures called sori on the underside of its leaves. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Its unique appearance and adaptability make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
