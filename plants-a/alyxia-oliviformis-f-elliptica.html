Alyxia oliviformis f. elliptica

Alyxia oliviformis f. elliptica, commonly known as the elliptic flame vine, is a flowering plant belonging to the Apocynaceae family.

It is a compact, evergreen shrub characterized by its elliptical, dark green leaves that are glossy and arranged oppositely along the stems. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant, orange-yellow flowers that bloom throughout the warmer months. It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Its foliage and flowers make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking a vibrant, low-maintenance plant.
