Aria evansii

Aria evansii, commonly known as the Evans' arrowhead, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the wetlands of eastern North America.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy, with a distinctive arrowhead shape. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in moist, rich soils and is often found in marshes and along stream banks.

Aria evansii is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting local wildlife.
