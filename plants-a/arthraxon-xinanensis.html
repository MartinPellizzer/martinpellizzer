Arthraxon xinanensis

Arthraxon xinanensis, commonly known as the Chinese arrowhead, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands. The plant features narrow, arrow-shaped leaves that are often arranged in a rosette pattern. Its flowering stems produce small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in spike-like inflorescences.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
