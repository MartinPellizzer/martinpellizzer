Ayenia laevigata var. acuminata

Ayenia laevigata var. acuminata, commonly known as the slender ayenia, is a shrub native to South America, particularly found in regions of Argentina and Chile.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, arranged in clusters, and bloom during the warmer months.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its visual interest.
