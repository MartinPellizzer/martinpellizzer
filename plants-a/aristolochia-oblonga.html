Aristolochia oblonga

Aristolochia oblonga, commonly known as the Chinese birthwort, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to East Asia.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves and produces unique, maroon-colored flowers that resemble a horn or pipe. The plant is characterized by its aromatic, complex scent, which is often described as sweet and musky. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is commonly found in woodland areas.

The plant contains various bioactive compounds, making it both medicinally significant and potentially toxic.
