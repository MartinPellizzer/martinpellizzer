Alsophila clementis

Alsophila clementis, commonly known as the Clement's tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds. The plant has a distinctive crown of pinnate leaves that can reach several meters in length. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in coastal and montane forests.

Alsophila clementis plays an important ecological role in its native habitat, providing shelter and food for various wildlife species.
