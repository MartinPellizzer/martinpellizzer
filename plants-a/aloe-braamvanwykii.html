Aloe braamvanwykii

Aloe braamvanwykii, commonly known as the Braamvlei aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that store water, a typical adaptation to arid environments. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow flowers, which bloom in summer. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their healing properties.

Aloe braamvanwykii is also valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and resilience.
