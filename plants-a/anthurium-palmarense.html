Anthurium palmarense

Anthurium palmarense, commonly known as the Palmarense Arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to the Caribbean.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a glossy texture. The plant produces striking spadix flowers surrounded by a colorful spathe, often in shades of red or orange. It thrives in humid, warm environments and requires indirect sunlight for optimal growth.

Anthurium palmarense is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its aesthetic appeal and air-purifying qualities.
