Argyrolobium sankeyi

Argyrolobium sankeyi, commonly known as the Sankey's silver globular pea, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, globular growth habit and silver-gray foliage. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and valuable species in botanical collections.
