Artemisiopsis villosa

Artemisiopsis villosa, commonly known as the silky tansy, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features wiry, branching stems covered in dense, silky white hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flower clusters that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and also covered in fine, soft hairs.

Artemisiopsis villosa is often found in dry, disturbed soils and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
