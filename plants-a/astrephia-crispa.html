Astrephia crispa

Astrephia crispa, commonly known as the silverleaf orchid, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its unique, silver-green foliage and delicate, orchid-like flowers that bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its leaves are often crinkled or wavy, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Astrephia crispa is popular among collectors for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
