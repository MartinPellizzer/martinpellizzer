Astragalus elwendicus

Astragalus elwendicus, commonly known as the Elwendic milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, pea-like flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments. It produces pods that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.
