Ascolepis capensis var. pleiostachya

Ascolepis capensis var. pleiostachya, commonly known as the Cape star, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, star-like inflorescence composed of numerous small, white to pale blue flowers. The plant typically grows in coastal and sandy soils, thriving in well-drained conditions. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it is often found in coastal fynbos vegetation.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
