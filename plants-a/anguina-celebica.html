Anguina celebica

Anguina celebica, commonly known as the Celebes root or Celebes arrowhead, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Araceae and is characterized by its arrow-shaped leaves and striking inflorescence. The plant produces a tuberous root that is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits. Its growth habit includes a rhizomatous system that allows it to spread and form dense colonies in moist, shaded environments.

Anguina celebica is also valued for its ornamental appeal due to its unique foliage and floral display.
