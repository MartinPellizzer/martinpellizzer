Arachnis longicaulis f. flavescens

Arachnis longicaulis f. flavescens, commonly known as the yellow-stemmed arachnis, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its elongated, slender stems that often display a distinctive yellowish or reddish hue. The plant produces small, delicate orchid-like flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It is native to tropical regions of South America and thrives in humid, shaded environments.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
