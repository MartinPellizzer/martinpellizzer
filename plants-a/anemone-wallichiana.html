Anemone wallichiana

Anemone wallichiana, commonly known as the Himalayan anemone, is a perennial flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its showy, pink to white flowers that bloom in early spring. Anemone wallichiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and plays a role in local ecosystems by providing nectar for pollinators.
