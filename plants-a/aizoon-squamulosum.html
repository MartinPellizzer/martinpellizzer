Aizoon squamulosum

Aizoon squamulosum, commonly known as the squamulate aizoon, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Aizoaceae, commonly referred to as the ice plant family. The plant is characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves that are covered in a fine layer of white, powdery wax. It produces small, star-shaped flowers that range in color from pink to red, typically blooming in spring.

Aizoon squamulosum is well adapted to arid environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
