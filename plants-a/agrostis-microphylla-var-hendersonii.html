Agrostis microphylla var. hendersonii

Agrostis microphylla var. hendersonii, commonly known as Henderson's fineleaf grass, is a low-growing perennial grass species native to New Zealand.

It features slender, tufted stems with narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically less than 1 cm in width. The plant produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences that are often hidden among the foliage. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and montane regions.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is used in native landscaping and restoration projects.
