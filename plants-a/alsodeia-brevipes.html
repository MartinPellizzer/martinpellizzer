Alsodeia brevipes

Alsodeia brevipes, commonly known as the short-footed alsodeia, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing annual herb with narrow, grass-like leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically blooms in spring, producing pale blue or purple flowers that attract pollinators. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed or open habitats.

Due to its delicate appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and specialized species.
