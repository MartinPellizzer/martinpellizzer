Aralia gintungensis var. gintungensis

Aralia gintungensis var. gintungensis, commonly known as the Philippine aralia, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Araliaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and clusters of small, white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments in tropical forests. It has a tall, slender stem with a smooth, green bark.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for horticultural cultivation in warm climates.
