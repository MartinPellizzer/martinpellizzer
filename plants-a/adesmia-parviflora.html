Adesmia parviflora

Adesmia parviflora, commonly known as the small-flowered legume, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is native to regions of South America, particularly in Argentina and Chile. The plant is characterized by its delicate, small flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. Its leaves are trifoliate, consisting of three small oval-shaped leaflets.
