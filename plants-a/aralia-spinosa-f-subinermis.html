Aralia spinosa f. subinermis

Aralia spinosa f. subinermis, commonly known as the smooth aralia, is a variant of the common aralia.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to the eastern United States. The plant features compound leaves with a smooth, almost velvety texture, distinguishing it from its more spiny relatives. Its flowers are small and greenish-white, typically appearing in clusters.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its foliage and adaptability to various soil conditions.
