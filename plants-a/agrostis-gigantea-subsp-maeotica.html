Agrostis gigantea subsp. maeotica

Agrostis gigantea subsp.

maeotica, commonly known as the Maeotica grass, is a perennial grass species native to the steppes of Eastern Europe and parts of Central Asia. It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands, dunes, and disturbed soils.

The plant has slender, erect stems that can reach up to 1 meter in height, with narrow, grass-like leaves. Its inflorescence consists of a loose panicle bearing small, spikelet-like flowers.

This subspecies is characterized by its adaptability to various soil types and its ability to thrive in semi-arid conditions.
