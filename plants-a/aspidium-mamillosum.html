Aspidium mamillosum

Aspidium mamillosum, commonly known as the hairy shield fern, is a species of fern native to parts of Asia and Africa.

It belongs to the family Athyriaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, shield-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its fronds are covered with fine, hairy structures that give it its common name.
