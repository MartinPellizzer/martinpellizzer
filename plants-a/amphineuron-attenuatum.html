Amphineuron attenuatum

Amphineuron attenuatum, commonly known as the slender swamp grass, is a herbaceous plant native to wetland environments in North America.

It typically grows in shallow, marshy areas where it thrives in moist, nutrient-rich soils. The plant features slender, cylindrical stems that are often reddish in color and covered in fine hairs. Its small, inconspicuous flowers are arranged in spike-like clusters and are pollinated by wind.
