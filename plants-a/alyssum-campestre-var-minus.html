Alyssum campestre var. minus

Alyssum campestre var. minus, commonly known as lesser field alyssum, is a low-growing, herbaceous perennial native to Europe.

It features small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink, blooming in clusters. The plant has linear to lance-shaped leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in meadows and along roadsides.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
