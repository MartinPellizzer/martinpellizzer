Aconitum × paniculatum var. penninum

Aconitum × paniculatum var. penninum, commonly known as the alpine aconite, is a hybrid plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its tall, sparsely branched stems that can reach heights of up to 2 meters. The plant produces striking blue to purple flowers arranged in a panicle inflorescence, which blooms in late summer. Its leaves are deeply lobed and have a distinctive, almost fern-like appearance.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and alpine settings.
