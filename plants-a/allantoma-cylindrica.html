Allantoma cylindrica

Allantoma cylindrica, commonly known as the cylindrical allantoma, is a flowering plant native to specific regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, cylindrical stem and small, clustered leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in arid or semi-arid environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.

Due to its unique morphology and limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
