Amphinomia rabenaviana

Amphinomia rabenaviana, commonly known as the Madagascar orchid, is a unique flowering plant native to the island of Madagascar.

The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, thriving in the dense rainforests of its native habitat. Its flowers are notable for their intricate structure and vibrant hues, which attract specific pollinators.
