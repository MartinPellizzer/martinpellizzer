Aster sutschanensis

Aster sutschanensis, commonly known as the Chinese aster, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces showy, violet-blue to purple flowers that bloom in the summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its ornamental value and adaptability contribute to its widespread cultivation.
