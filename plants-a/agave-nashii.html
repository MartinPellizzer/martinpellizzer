Agave nashii

Agave nashii, commonly known as the Mexican blue agave, is a species of flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are typically blue-green in color. It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its striking appearance and hardy nature.
