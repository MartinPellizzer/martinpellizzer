Agrostis oligoclada

Agrostis oligoclada, commonly known as slender meadowgrass, is a grass species native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial plant with narrow, grass-like leaves and small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows, grasslands, and along riverbanks. Its botanical features include rhizomes and culms that are often reddish in color, and it produces tiny, wind-pollinated flowers.

Agrostis oligoclada is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in stabilizing soil in wetland environments.
