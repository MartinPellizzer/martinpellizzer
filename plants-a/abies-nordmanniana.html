Abies nordmanniana

Abies nordmanniana, commonly known as the Nordmann fir, is a coniferous evergreen tree native to the Caucasus region.

It is characterized by its dense, pyramidal shape and dark green, needle-like leaves that are arranged in spirals along the branches. The tree produces small, round cones that mature to a reddish-brown color, containing winged seeds. It thrives in temperate climates with well-drained soil and moderate rainfall.

The Nordmann fir is often cultivated as an ornamental tree due to its aesthetic appeal and hardiness.
