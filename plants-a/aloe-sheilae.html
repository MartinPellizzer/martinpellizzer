Aloe sheilae

Aloe sheilae, commonly known as the Sheilae Aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a waxy coating, which helps it retain moisture in arid environments. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow flowers, blooming in late summer. Its leaves contain a gel-like substance that is sometimes used for its soothing properties.

Aloe sheilae is well-suited for cultivation in dry climates and requires minimal watering.
