Anthurium regale

Anthurium regale, commonly known as the royal anthurium, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length, with a deep green color and prominent veins. The plant produces vibrant red spathes that enclose its spadix, which is adorned with tiny, yellowish flowers. Anthurium regale thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor cultivation and botanical gardens.
