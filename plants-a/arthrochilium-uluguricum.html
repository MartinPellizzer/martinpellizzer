Arthrochilium uluguricum

Arthrochilium uluguricum, commonly known as the Uluguru orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Uluguru Mountains in Tanzania.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stem and a single, showy flower with a distinctive lip. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish in color, with intricate patterns on the petals and sepals.

Arthrochilium uluguricum is a rare and endemic species, making it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists.
