Alsine verna proles gerardi

Alsine verna proles gerardi, commonly known as Gerardi's common sandwort, is a low-growing perennial herb native to North America.

It typically thrives in moist, sandy or gravelly soils, often found in open habitats such as meadows and wetlands. The plant features small, white or pinkish flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in early spring. Its leaves are alternate, linear, and often covered with fine hairs.

It plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for small invertebrates.
