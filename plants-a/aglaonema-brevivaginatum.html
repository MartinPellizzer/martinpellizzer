Aglaonema brevivaginatum

Aglaonema brevivaginatum, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen or silver queen, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated leaves that display shades of white, green, and silver. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub with thick, fleshy stems and lance-shaped foliage. It thrives in low-light conditions and requires moderate humidity and well-draining soil.

Aglaonema brevivaginatum is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and air-purifying properties.
