Aloe × caesia var. disticha

Aloe × caesia var. disticha, commonly known as the narrow-leaf aloe, is a hybrid plant that exhibits characteristics of both its parent species, Aloe caesia and Aloe disticha.

It is native to southern Africa, particularly South Africa, where it thrives in arid and semi-arid environments. The plant features tall, slender stems with narrow, fleshy leaves that are often tinged with red or purple at the base. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange and are arranged in dense, upright inflorescences.

Aloe × caesia var. disticha is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its drought tolerance and unique appearance.
