Ageratina Articulata

Ageratina articulata is a strikingly beautiful flowering plant known for its fluffy, white to pale blue blooms. Native to the tropical regions of the Americas, it thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in moist, shaded areas. Its unique, branched inflorescence gives it a distinctive appearance, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens. The plant is also valued for its medicinal properties, with traditional uses in various cultures. Despite its beauty, it can be invasive in some regions, requiring careful management to prevent ecological disruption.
