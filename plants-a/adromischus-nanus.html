Adromischus nanus

Adromischus nanus, commonly known as the dwarf ice plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features small, fleshy, rounded leaves that are typically a vibrant green or bluish-gray in color. The plant produces delicate, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves.

Due to its compact size and attractive appearance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
