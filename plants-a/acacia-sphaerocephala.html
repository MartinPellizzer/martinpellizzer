Acacia sphaerocephala

Acacia sphaerocephala, commonly known as the globe acacia, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, spherical crown and small, feathery leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers in winter, which are followed by flat, bean-like pods. It is well adapted to arid environments and can tolerate drought conditions.

The species is often used in landscaping due to its hardy nature and attractive appearance.
