Asparagopsis decaisnei

Asparagopsis decaisnei, commonly known as the yellow asparagus fern, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Asparagaceae and is characterized by its dense, grass-like foliage and small, inconspicuous flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments and is often found in coastal areas. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to form dense mats, which can be both ornamental and invasive in certain regions.

The leaves are usually bright green and have a slightly fibrous texture, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
