Arenaria kashmirica

Arenaria kashmirica, commonly known as Kashmir sandwort, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Kashmir.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit. The plant produces small, white or pink flowers that bloom in early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in alpine and subalpine environments.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
