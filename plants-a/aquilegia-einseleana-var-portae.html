Aquilegia einseleana var. portae

Aquilegia einseleana var. portae, commonly known as the Alpine columbine, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the alpine regions of Europe.

It features striking blue to purple spurred flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has compound leaves with serrated edges, and its stems are often hairy. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting pollinators.
