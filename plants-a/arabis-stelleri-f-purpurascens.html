Arabis stelleri f. purpurascens

Arabis stelleri f. purpurascens, commonly known as the purple-stemmed rockcress, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of central Asia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other cruciferous plants. The plant is characterized by its low-growing habit, with basal leaves and small, white or pale purple flowers that bloom in early spring.

Its stems are distinctly purple, which is a distinguishing feature of this cultivar. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or alpine environments.
