Aster invenustus

Aster invenustus, commonly known as the showy aster, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in open woodlands and prairies, thriving in well-drained soils. Its showy, violet-blue flowers bloom in the fall, attracting pollinators such as butterflies and bees.

Aster invenustus is valued for its ornamental beauty and ecological benefits in native plant gardens.
