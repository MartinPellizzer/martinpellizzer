Armeriastrum kurdicum

Armeriastrum kurdicum, commonly known as Kurdish sandwort, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Turkey and northern Iraq.

It typically grows in rocky and arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. The plant features small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring, adding a delicate touch to its habitat. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often forming dense mats along the ground.

Armeriastrum kurdicum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in rock gardens or as a ground cover in dry landscapes.
