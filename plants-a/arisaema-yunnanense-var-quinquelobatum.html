Arisaema yunnanense var. quinquelobatum

Arisaema yunnanense var. quinquelobatum, commonly known as the Yunnan arrowhead, is a herbaceous plant native to China.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its distinctive arrow-shaped leaves. The plant produces a spadix surrounded by a spathe, typical of the arum family. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for both horticultural and ecological study.
