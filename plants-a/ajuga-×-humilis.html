Ajuga × humilis

Ajuga × humilis, commonly known as carpet bugle or narrowleaf ajuga, is a low-growing perennial groundcover plant native to Europe.

It features dark green, elongated leaves that are typically narrower than those of other ajuga species. The plant produces clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of blue to purple, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Ajuga × humilis thrives in partial to full shade and is often used in woodland gardens for its ornamental value and ability to suppress weeds.

It prefers moist, well-drained soil and is relatively easy to maintain once established.
