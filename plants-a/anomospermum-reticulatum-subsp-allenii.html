Anomospermum reticulatum subsp. allenii

Anomospermum reticulatum subsp.

allenii, commonly known as the Allen's spider bean, is a shrub native to arid regions of Africa. It is characterized by its woody stems and compound leaves with serrated edges.

The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that are followed by bean-like pods containing edible seeds. It is adapted to survive in dry climates with low rainfall.

Its botanical features make it an important species in ecological and agricultural studies.
