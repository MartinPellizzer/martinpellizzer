Aloe leandrii

Aloe leandrii, commonly known as the Leandrii aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a slight blue or purple tint. The plant produces striking red or orange flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Aloe leandrii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional medicinal uses.
