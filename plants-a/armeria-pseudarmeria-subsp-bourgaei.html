Armeria pseudarmeria subsp. bourgaei

Armeria pseudarmeria subsp.

bourgaei, commonly known as the Bourgai's thrift, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the coastal regions of southern California. It features dense clusters of small, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in the spring.

The plant has succulent, fleshy leaves that are typically gray-green in color, adapted to the arid coastal environment. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

This subspecies is notable for its hardiness and ability to withstand salt spray and strong winds.
