Agathosma barnesiae

Agathosma barnesiae, commonly known as Cape star, is a succulent shrub native to the Western Cape of South Africa.

It features small, fleshy leaves that are typically gray-green in color, adapted to store water in arid environments. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in spring, adding a delicate touch to its appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Due to its unique appearance and hardiness, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant in gardens.
