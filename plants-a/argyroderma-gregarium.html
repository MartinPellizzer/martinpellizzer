Argyroderma gregarium

Argyroderma gregarium, commonly known as the silverleaf groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small, herbaceous annual that typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments. The plant features silver-gray foliage due to a dense covering of fine hairs, which helps reduce water loss. Its flowers are yellow and clustered in small heads, blooming during the cooler months.

It is often found in disturbed soils and is adapted to thrive in harsh, dry conditions.
