Anthurium oxyphyllum

Anthurium oxyphyllum, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces vibrant red spathes that enclose bright yellow spadices, which are the actual flower structures. Anthurium oxyphyllum thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.

Its unique foliage and showy inflorescence make it a popular choice for indoor gardening and ornamental purposes.
