Allium callimischon subsp. callimischon

Allium callimischon subsp.

callimischon, commonly known as the purple onion, is a perennial bulbous plant native to the Mediterranean region. It features tall, slender stems that bear lance-shaped, pale green leaves.

The plant produces umbels of small, pale purple to white flowers, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its bulbs are edible and have a mild, sweet flavor, often used in cooking.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and culinary versatility in Mediterranean cuisine.
