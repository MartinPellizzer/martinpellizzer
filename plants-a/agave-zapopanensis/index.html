Agave Zapopanensis

Agave zapopanensis is a unique species native to the central highlands of Mexico. It is known for its striking appearance and ability to thrive in arid environments. This plant is often mistaken for other agave varieties due to its similar structure. It has been used traditionally by local communities for various purposes. Its conservation status highlights the importance of protecting native flora.
