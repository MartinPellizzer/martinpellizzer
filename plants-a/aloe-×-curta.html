Aloe × curta

Aloe × curta, commonly known as the compact aloe, is a hybrid succulent plant resulting from the cross between Aloe vera and Aloe barbadensis.

It is characterized by its short, dense rosette of fleshy, pointed leaves that are typically green with a reddish tinge at the tips. The plant produces tall, branching inflorescences with yellow flowers, which appear in late spring or early summer. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves and requiring minimal watering.

Aloe × curta is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its compact size and attractive appearance.
