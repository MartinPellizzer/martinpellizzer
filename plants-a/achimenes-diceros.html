Achimenes diceros

Achimenes diceros, commonly known as the flame flower, is a tropical perennial plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular, showy flowers that range in color from red to orange and yellow. The plant has heart-shaped, velvety leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Achimenes diceros is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant blooms and compact growth habit.
