Aloe rhodesiana

Aloe rhodesiana, commonly known as the Rhodes aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically gray-green in color, with a waxy coating to reduce water loss. The plant produces tall, branched flower stalks that bear yellow flowers, which bloom in the spring. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Aloe rhodesiana is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and hardy nature.
