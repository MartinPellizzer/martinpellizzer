Aspilia smithiana

Aspilia smithiana, commonly known as the African flame flower, is a striking ornamental plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy yellow flowers that are surrounded by long, narrow bracts resembling flames. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 1.5 meters. Its foliage consists of lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green.

Aspilia smithiana thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
