Agathosma acerosa

Agathosma acerosa, commonly known as Cape spearmint, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, narrow leaves and small yellow flowers. The plant is often found in rocky or sandy soils, thriving in dry and sunny conditions. Its essential oils are valued for their medicinal and aromatic properties.

Agathosma acerosa is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
