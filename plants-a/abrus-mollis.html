Abrus mollis

Abrus mollis, commonly known as the velvet tare, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, showy flowers that range from pink to purple. The plant produces flat, pod-like fruits that contain small, dark seeds. Its leaves are palmately lobed, and the stems are often covered in a fine, velvety texture.

Abrus mollis is primarily cultivated for its ornamental value and has also been used in traditional medicine.
