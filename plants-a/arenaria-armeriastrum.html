Arenaria armeriastrum

Arenaria armeriastrum, commonly known as the sandspurry, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features succulent, fleshy leaves that help it retain moisture in arid environments. The plant produces small, white or pinkish flowers that bloom in early spring. Its root system is shallow, allowing it to thrive in sandy or rocky soils.

It is often found in coastal dunes and other dry, open habitats.
