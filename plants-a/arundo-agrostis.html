Arundo agrostis

Arundo agrostis, commonly known as the slender reed, is a tall, perennial grass native to Europe, western Asia, and North Africa.

It typically grows in wet or marshy areas, often forming dense stands along water bodies. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bloom in late summer. It is often used in landscaping for erosion control due to its robust root system.

However, it can become invasive in certain environments if not managed properly.
