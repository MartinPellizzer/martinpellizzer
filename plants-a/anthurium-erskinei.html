Anthurium erskinei

Anthurium erskinei, commonly known as the Erskine's flamingo flower, is a tropical plant native to Colombia.

It is characterized by its striking, elongated spadix and large, heart-shaped spatula. The plant produces vibrant red spadix with a unique, almost flame-like appearance. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light.

Its foliage is dark green and glossy, adding to its ornamental appeal.
