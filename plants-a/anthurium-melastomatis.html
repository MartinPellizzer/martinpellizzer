Anthurium melastomatis

Anthurium melastomatis, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a striking tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped spathes that range in color from red to orange. The plant produces small, green berries that are edible but not commonly consumed by humans. Its foliage is broad and glossy, contributing to its ornamental value in both indoor and outdoor settings.

Anthurium melastomatis thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
