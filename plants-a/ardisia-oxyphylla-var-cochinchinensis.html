Ardisia oxyphylla var. cochinchinensis

Ardisia oxyphylla var. cochinchinensis, commonly known as the Vietnamese beautyberry, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small evergreen shrub characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of small, white flowers. The plant produces bright red, fleshy berries that are a key feature in its ornamental value. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

The berries are not only visually striking but also attract birds and other wildlife.
