Arctotis diffusa

Arctotis diffusa, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, daisy-like flowers that range in color from yellow to orange and red. The plant typically grows to a height of 30 to 60 centimeters and produces numerous small, showy blooms throughout the growing season. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Arctotis diffusa is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
