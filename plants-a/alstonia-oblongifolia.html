Alstonia oblongifolia

Alstonia oblongifolia, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a deciduous tree native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features large, glossy, ovate leaves that are typically arranged oppositely along the stems. The plant produces showy, clustered flowers that are usually pink or red, followed by woody, capsule-like fruits containing seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Alstonia oblongifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often planted in gardens and as a street tree.
