Aglaonema oblongifolium var. curtisii

Aglaonema oblongifolium var. curtisii, commonly known as the Curtis's Chinese evergreen, is a tropical evergreen plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are typically variegated with shades of green, white, and pink, creating an attractive visual display. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening. Its foliage is dense and compact, with a somewhat upright growth habit.

Aglaonema oblongifolium var. curtisii is also valued for its air-purifying qualities and ease of care.
