Angelica reflexa

Angelica reflexa, commonly known as the reflexed angelica, is a biennial or perennial herb native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems that can reach up to 2 meters in height, often with a distinct reddish or purple hue. The plant features large, compound leaves with serrated edges and small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in umbrella-like clusters. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Angelica reflexa is known for its aromatic properties and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
