Aloe decurvidens

Aloe decurvidens, commonly known as the Cape aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, ribbed leaves that are typically green with a bluish tint and often have a waxy coating. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellowish-orange flowers that bloom in spring. Its leaves contain a gel-like substance that is used in traditional medicine and skincare products.

Aloe decurvidens is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
