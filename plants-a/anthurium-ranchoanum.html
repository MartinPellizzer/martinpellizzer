Anthurium ranchoanum

Anthurium ranchoanum, commonly known as the Rancho Anubis, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are often dark green with a metallic sheen. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-shaped inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, which can range from red to orange. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Anthurium ranchoanum is popular as a decorative plant due to its ornamental foliage and unique floral structure.
