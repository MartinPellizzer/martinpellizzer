Aspidium martinicense

Aspidium martinicense, commonly known as the Martinique water fern, is a member of the family Polypodiaceae.

It is a terrestrial fern that typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions. It is characterized by its ability to thrive in humid conditions and its preference for well-drained soil.

The botanical features of Aspidium martinicense make it an important species in ecological studies of fern diversity.
