Aralia chabrieri

Aralia chabrieri, commonly known as the white aralia, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features compound leaves that are pinnately divided, giving it a delicate, fern-like appearance. The plant produces small, white flowers in clusters, which are followed by bright red berries. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Aralia chabrieri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens for its unique foliage and seasonal interest.
