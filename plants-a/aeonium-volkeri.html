Aeonium volkeri

Aeonium volkeri, commonly known as the Volker's Aeonium, is a succulent plant native to the Canary Islands.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with red or purple hues at the edges. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences that bear yellow flowers, which bloom in the spring. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its compact size and striking appearance make it a desirable addition to both indoor and outdoor collections.
