Arundastrum cuspidatum

Arundastrum cuspidatum, commonly known as purple top grass, is a tall, rhizomatous grass native to North America.

It features distinctive, purple-tinged inflorescences that are often used in floral arrangements. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spikelet-based flowers. It thrives in moist to wet soils and is often found in wetlands and along stream banks.

Arundastrum cuspidatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting wildlife.
