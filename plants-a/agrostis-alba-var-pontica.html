Agrostis alba var. pontica

Agrostis alba var. pontica, commonly known as the Pontic silvergrass, is a grass species native to the Black Sea region.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-tomentose leaves and tufted growth habit. This variety thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wet meadows and marshy areas. Its flowering stems bear small, spike-like inflorescences with pale green to brown florets.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting local wildlife.
