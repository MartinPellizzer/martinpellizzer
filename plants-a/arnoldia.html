Arnoldia

Arnoldia, commonly known as the golden trumpet or yellow trumpet, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Acanthaceae.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. The plants are native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Americas. Arnoldia species often grow as shrubs or small trees, and they are valued for their ornamental appeal.

They prefer well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
