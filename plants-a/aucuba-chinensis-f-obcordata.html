Aucuba chinensis f. obcordata

Aucuba chinensis f. obcordata, commonly known as the obcordate aucuba, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features broad, glossy, dark green leaves that are elliptical with a distinctive obcordate (heart-shaped) base. The plant produces small, inconspicuous yellowish-green flowers in the spring, followed by red, fleshy berries that are attractive to birds. It is highly valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and ability to thrive in shaded environments.

This cultivar is often used in landscaping for its dense growth habit and year-round visual interest.
