Acalypha schinzii

Acalypha schinzii, commonly known as the red-leaf acalypha, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety red leaves that contrast with its green stems. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically green or yellowish in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight.

Acalypha schinzii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its vibrant foliage and ease of care.
