Arisaema amurense var. inaense

Arisaema amurense var. inaense, commonly known as the Inaense Arrowhead, is a herbaceous plant native to eastern Asia.

It features a distinctive, arrow-shaped leaf that is often broad and heart-shaped at the base. The plant produces a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe, which is typical of the Araceae family. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and ecological restoration projects.
