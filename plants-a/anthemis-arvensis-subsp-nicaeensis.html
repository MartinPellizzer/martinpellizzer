Anthemis arvensis subsp. nicaeensis

Anthemis arvensis subsp.

nicaeensis, commonly known as the narrow-leaved ragwort, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family. It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves.

The plant produces small, yellowish to brownish composite flowers that bloom in summer. It is typically found in disturbed soils and open habitats across Europe.

This subspecies is notable for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
