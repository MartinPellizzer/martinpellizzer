Arundinaria variabilis var. disticha

Arundinaria variabilis var. disticha, commonly known as the bamboo fern or common bamboo, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and feather-like leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. Native to parts of Asia, this plant thrives in moist, shaded environments. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to form dense clumps.

The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically clustered in spike-like inflorescences.
