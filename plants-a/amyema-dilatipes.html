Amyema dilatipes

Amyema dilatipes, commonly known as the broad-leaved ghost orchid, is an epiphytic plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, pale green to white flowers. The plant has strap-shaped leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine, white hairs. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, typically growing on the trunks of trees in rainforest areas.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a vulnerable species.
