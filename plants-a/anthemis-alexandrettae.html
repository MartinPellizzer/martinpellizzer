Anthemis alexandrettae

Anthemis alexandrettae, commonly known as the Alexandretta ragged robin, is a flowering plant native to the eastern Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features slender, hairy stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are typically pink to reddish in color, with ray florets surrounding a central disk.

It thrives in dry, rocky habitats and is often found in coastal areas and arid regions.
