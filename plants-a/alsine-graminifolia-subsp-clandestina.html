Alsine graminifolia subsp. clandestina

Alsine graminifolia subsp.

clandestina, commonly known as the hidden grass fleabane, is a low-growing annual plant native to the Mediterranean region. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and small, yellowish flowers.

The subspecies is distinguished by its compact growth habit and subtle floral features compared to other members of the species. It typically thrives in disturbed soils and open habitats, such as roadsides and fields.
