Albizia purpurascens

Albizia purpurascens, commonly known as the purple wisteria, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 6 meters in height. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant, violet-colored flowers that bloom in the spring. Its leaves are bipinnate, consisting of numerous small leaflets, and it has a fibrous root system.

Albizia purpurascens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
