Avenula hackelii subsp. stenophylla

Avenula hackelii subsp.

stenophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leaf avenula, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region. It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade.

The plant features slender stems and narrow, grass-like leaves that are often grayish-green in color. Its small, inconspicuous flowers are usually white or pale green and appear in early summer.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance in landscaping.
