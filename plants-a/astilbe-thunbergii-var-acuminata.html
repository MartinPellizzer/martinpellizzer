Astilbe thunbergii var. acuminata

Astilbe thunbergii var. acuminata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved thunberg astilbe, is a herbaceous perennial native to eastern Asia.

It features delicate, feathery plumes of pink to white flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often variegated with silver or white markings. It prefers partial to full shade and moist, well-drained soil, making it ideal for woodland gardens.

Its ornamental value lies in its soft, flowing foliage and subtle floral display.
