Aloe multicolor

Aloe multicolor, commonly known as the rainbow aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features striking, colorful bracts that range from red, orange, to yellow, giving it its common name. The plant has fleshy, ribbed leaves that store water, typical of succulents. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright sunlight.

Aloe multicolor is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for.
