Arundinaria paniculata f. chimakisasa

Arundinaria paniculata f. chimakisasa, commonly known as the Japanese bamboo or Chimakiasa bamboo, is a unique variant of the common bamboo species.

It is characterized by its slender, green culms that often exhibit a striking reddish hue when exposed to sunlight. The plant produces small, clustered panicles of white flowers, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its foliage is dense and lush, making it an attractive ornamental plant in gardens.

This variety is native to Japan and is prized for its elegant appearance and hardy nature.
