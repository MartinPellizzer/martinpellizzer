Autogenes × tenuior

Autogenes × tenuior, commonly known as the hybrid spider orchid, is a fascinating plant with unique botanical characteristics.

It is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Autogenes and tenuior, two distinct orchid genera. The plant typically features slender, wiry stems and produces small, delicate flowers with intricate patterns. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, adapted to thrive in shaded, moist environments.

This orchid is often found in temperate regions and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological significance.
