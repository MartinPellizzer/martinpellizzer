Angelica lignescens

Angelica lignescens, commonly known as the woody angelica, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

Angelica lignescens is often found in moist meadows and woodlands, and it has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
