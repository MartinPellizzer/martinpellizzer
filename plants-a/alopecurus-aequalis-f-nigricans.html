Alopecurus aequalis f. nigricans

Alopecurus aequalis f. nigricans, commonly known as black-haired mouse-ear, is a flowering plant in the grass family Poaceae.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its dark green, hairy leaves and small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.
