Ardisia humilis f. parvifolia

Ardisia humilis f. parvifolia, commonly known as the small-leaved evergreen shrub, is a compact plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are smaller and more densely packed compared to the typical species. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers followed by bright red berries, which are attractive to birds. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often used in ornamental horticulture.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking a low-maintenance, decorative plant.
