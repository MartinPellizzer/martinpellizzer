Adesmia viciifolia

Adesmia viciifolia, commonly known as the sicklepod or velvetleaf, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is a member of the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod-like fruits. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and can be found in various habitats such as fields and roadsides. Its small, yellow flowers are followed by elongated, hairy pods that contain the seeds.
