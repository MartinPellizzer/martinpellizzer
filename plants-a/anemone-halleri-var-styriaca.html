Anemone halleri var. styriaca

Anemone halleri var. styriaca, commonly known as the Styrian anemone, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central Europe.

It features showy, white to pale pink flowers with a distinctive, elongated center, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has palmately divided, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of silky hairs. It thrives in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking native, ornamental plants.
