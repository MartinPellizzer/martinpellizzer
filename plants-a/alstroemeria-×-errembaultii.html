Alstroemeria × errembaultii

Alstroemeria × errembaultii, commonly known as the hybrid alstroemeria, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the family Alstroemeriaceae and is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers in a variety of colors. The plant typically grows as a perennial shrub with grass-like leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

The hybrid nature of this species contributes to its diverse range of flower forms and colors.
