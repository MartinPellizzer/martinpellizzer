Aristida filipendula

Aristida filipendula, commonly known as silverhair grass, is a perennial grass native to North America.

It is characterized by its slender, silver-gray foliage and delicate, panicle-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed sites such as roadsides and prairies. It is often found in the eastern and central United States, thriving in full sun.

Its fine texture and ornamental appearance make it a popular choice for native plant gardens.
