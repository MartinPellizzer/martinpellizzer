Aeschynomene egena

Aeschynomene egena, commonly known as silverleaf desmodium, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a legume belonging to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant is known for its ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, making it beneficial in soil enrichment. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often used in forage and cover cropping.

Its rapid growth and adaptability make it a valuable species in ecological restoration projects.
