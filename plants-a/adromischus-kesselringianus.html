Adromischus kesselringianus

Adromischus kesselringianus, commonly known as the "Coral Cactus," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features small, fleshy, rounded leaves that are typically red or pink in color, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces clusters of tiny, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or red during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
