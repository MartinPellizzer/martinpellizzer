Acanthus barteri

Acanthus barteri, commonly known as the Barter's spiny acanthus, is a herbaceous perennial native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, spiny leaves with a deep green color and intricate, palmate veins. The plant produces showy, sparsely branched inflorescences with blue to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Due to its ornamental value and unique appearance, it is often cultivated in gardens for its visual appeal.
