Adiantum platyphyllum var. obliquum

Adiantum platyphyllum var. obliquum, commonly known as the oblique maidenhair fern, is a delicate fern species native to Southeast Asia.

It features finely divided, arching fronds with a distinctive, lance-shaped appearance. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit, allowing it to spread slowly through underground stems. Its leaves are typically dark green and glossy, with a unique, slightly curled margin.

It thrives in shady, humid environments and requires well-drained, moist soil for optimal growth.
