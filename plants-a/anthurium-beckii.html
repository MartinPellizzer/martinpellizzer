Anthurium beckii

Anthurium beckii, commonly known as the velvet leaf anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a velvety texture. The plant produces striking, spadix-like inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, often in shades of red or orange. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium beckii is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and air-purifying properties.
