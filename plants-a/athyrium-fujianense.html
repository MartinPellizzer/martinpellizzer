Athyrium fujianense

Athyrium fujianense, commonly known as the Fujian lady fern, is a species of fern native to China.

It belongs to the family Athyriaceae and is characterized by its delicate, bipinnate fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as forests and rocky slopes. Its rhizomes are often covered in a dense mat of roots, which help anchor it to the substrate.

The leaves are usually dark green and have a fine, feathery appearance, making it a popular ornamental plant in gardens.
