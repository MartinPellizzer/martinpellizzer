Anthurium dussii

Anthurium dussii, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces vibrant red spathes that enclose its spadix, which is a characteristic feature of the Araceae family. Anthurium dussii thrives in humid, warm environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

It is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and unique inflorescence.
