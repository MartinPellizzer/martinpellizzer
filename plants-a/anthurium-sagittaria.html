Anthurium sagittaria

Anthurium sagittaria, commonly known as the arrowhead plant, is a tropical flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and striking spadix inflorescence. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage.

Anthurium sagittaria is also valued for its air-purifying properties and ability to thrive in indoor conditions.
