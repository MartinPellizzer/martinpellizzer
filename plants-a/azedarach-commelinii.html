Azedarach commelinii

Azedarach commelinii, commonly known as the fragrant flame tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, palmate leaves and clusters of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant produces small, spherical fruits that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Azedarach commelinii is also valued for its ability to tolerate poor soil conditions and its use in traditional medicine.
