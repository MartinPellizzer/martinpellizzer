Alchemilla gottsteiniana

Alchemilla gottsteiniana, commonly known as the Gottstein's lady's mantle, is a low-growing perennial plant native to Central Europe.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit. The plant features basal leaves that are shield-shaped and covered in a fine layer of white hairs, giving it a silvery appearance. Its small, yellowish-green flowers appear in summer and are clustered in compact inflorescences.

Alchemilla gottsteiniana is often found in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and rocky slopes.
