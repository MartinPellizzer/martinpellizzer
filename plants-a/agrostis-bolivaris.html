Agrostis bolivaris

Agrostis bolivaris, commonly known as Bolivian bentgrass, is a perennial grass species native to South America.

It typically grows in moist, temperate environments such as meadows and grasslands. The plant has slender, tufted stems and small, spike-like inflorescences. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green coloration.

Agrostis bolivaris is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in horticultural settings.
