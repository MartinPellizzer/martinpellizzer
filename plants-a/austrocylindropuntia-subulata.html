Austrocylindropuntia subulata

Austrocylindropuntia subulata, commonly known as the slender columnar cactus, is a species native to the arid regions of South America.

It is characterized by its tall, cylindrical stems that can grow up to several meters in height. The plant has small, spineless areoles and produces yellow flowers during the warmer months. Its leaves are reduced to spines, and it stores water in its thick, fleshy stems.

This cactus is well adapted to harsh desert environments and is often found in rocky soils.
