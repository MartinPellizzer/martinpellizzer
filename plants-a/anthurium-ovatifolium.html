Anthurium ovatifolium

Anthurium ovatifolium, commonly known as the broad-leaf anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, ovate-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a glossy texture. The plant produces striking spadices, which are inflorescences that resemble a spathe and are often brightly colored. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Anthurium ovatifolium is popular as a ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and air-purifying qualities.
