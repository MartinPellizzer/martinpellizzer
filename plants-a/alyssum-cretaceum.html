Alyssum cretaceum

Alyssum cretaceum, commonly known as the Cretan alyssum, is a low-growing annual plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has hairy, succulent leaves that help it retain moisture in arid environments. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Alyssum cretaceum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract beneficial insects.
