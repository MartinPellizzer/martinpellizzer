Ancistrorhynchus brunneomaculatus

Ancistrorhynchus brunneomaculatus, commonly known as the brown-spotted corydoras, is a species of catfish native to the freshwater rivers of South America.

It is a bottom-dwelling species that feeds primarily on algae, detritus, and small invertebrates.
