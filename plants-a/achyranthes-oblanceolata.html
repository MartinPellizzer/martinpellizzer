Achyranthes oblanceolata

Achyranthes oblanceolata, commonly known as the broadleaf smartweed, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters. The plant features alternate, ovate to elliptic leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. Its small, clustered flowers are usually pink or red, appearing in late summer to early autumn.

The plant is known for its fibrous roots and is often found in moist soils along stream banks or in wetlands.
