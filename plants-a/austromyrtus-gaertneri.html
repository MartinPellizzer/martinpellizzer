Austromyrtus gaertneri

Austromyrtus gaertneri, commonly known as the Christmas bush, is a small evergreen shrub native to southeastern Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family, which includes eucalyptus and tea tree species. The plant is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and small, fragrant white flowers that bloom during the winter months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and heathland environments.

Austromyrtus gaertneri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its seasonal flowering.
