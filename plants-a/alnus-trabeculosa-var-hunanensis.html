Alnus trabeculosa var. hunanensis

Alnus trabeculosa var. hunanensis, commonly known as the Hunan alder, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its serrated, ovate leaves and catkin-like inflorescences. The plant produces small, winged seeds that are dispersed by wind, aiding in its spread. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forest edges or stream banks.

This variety is valued for its ecological role in soil stabilization and as a habitat for various wildlife species.
