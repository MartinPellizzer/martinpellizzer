Achras vitellina

Achras vitellina, commonly known as the yellow star tree, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, palm-like leaves and distinctive yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces a heavy, waxy fruit that contains a single, large seed. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The tree is also valued for its hard, durable wood in some regions.
