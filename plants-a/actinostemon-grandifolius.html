Actinostemon grandifolius

Actinostemon grandifolius, commonly known as the large-leaf actinostemon, is a flowering plant native to parts of Central America and the Caribbean.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves that can reach up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in the summer months. It typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in shaded or semi-shaded environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in tropical and subtropical gardens.
