Armouria

Armouria, commonly known as the silver leaf plant, is a member of the Acanthaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking silver-gray foliage and clusters of tubular, pinkish-white flowers. The plant is native to tropical regions of Africa and thrives in warm, humid climates. It has a woody stem and produces small, elliptical leaves with a velvety texture.

Armoria is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable growing conditions.
