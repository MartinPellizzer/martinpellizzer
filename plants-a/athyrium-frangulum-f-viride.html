Athyrium frangulum f. viride

Athyrium frangulum f. viride, commonly known as the green lace fern, is a fern species characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds.

It belongs to the family Athyriaceae and is native to eastern Asia, including regions of China, Japan, and Korea. The plant features a rhizomatous growth habit, with arching, feather-like leaves that are typically bright green. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.

The foliage exhibits a subtle variegation in some cultivars, adding visual interest to its appearance.
