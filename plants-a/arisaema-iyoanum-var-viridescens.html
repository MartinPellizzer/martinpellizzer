Arisaema iyoanum var. viridescens

Arisaema iyoanum var. viridescens, commonly known as the Green Veined Cnidium, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its distinctive leaf structure with a long, narrow petiole and a palmately lobed blade. The plant produces a spadix surrounded by a spathe, which is typically green with a white or yellowish base. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The species is valued for its ornamental appearance and has traditional uses in some regions.
