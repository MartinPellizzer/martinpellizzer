Agrostemma bungeanum

Agrostemma bungeanum, commonly known as the yellow weed or Bunge's grass-pink, is an annual flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

Agrostemma bungeanum is known for its ability to thrive in poor soil conditions and is considered a weed in agricultural settings.
