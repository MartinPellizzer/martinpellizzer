Alternanthera hirtula var. nitens

Alternanthera hirtula var. nitens, commonly known as the red-stemmed Alternanthera, is a vibrant aquatic plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking red or pink stems and dark green, lance-shaped leaves. This variety thrives in moderate to high light conditions and is often used in freshwater aquariums for its ornamental value. It prefers soft to moderately hard water with a pH range of 6.0 to 7.5.

The plant is relatively easy to care for and can be propagated through stem cuttings.
