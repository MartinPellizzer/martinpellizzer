Aquifolium ilex

Aquifolium ilex, commonly known as the Holly.

It is a evergreen shrub or small tree native to the Mediterranean region. The plant features glossy, dark green leaves with spiny margins and red berries that persist through winter. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular ornamental plant.

Its botanical characteristics include opposite leaf arrangement and small, fragrant flowers that bloom in spring.
