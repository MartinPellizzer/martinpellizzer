Alstroemeria pallida

Alstroemeria pallida, commonly known as the white alstroemeria, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the family Alstroemeriaceae and is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that typically range in color from white to pink. The plant features strap-like leaves that are often bright green and arranged in a fan shape. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Alstroemeria pallida is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and floral arrangements.
