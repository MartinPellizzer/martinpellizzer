Adhatoda nilgherrensis

Adhatoda nilgherrensis, commonly known as the Nilgiri adhatoda, is a flowering plant native to the Western Ghats of India.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant produces small, pale green to white flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the rainy season. Its leaves are elliptical, slightly hairy, and have a distinctive aromatic scent.

Adhatoda nilgherrensis is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional Ayurvedic medicine.
