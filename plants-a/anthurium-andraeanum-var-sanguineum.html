Anthurium andraeanum var. sanguineum

Anthurium andraeanum var. sanguineum, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a tropical flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped spathes that range from deep red to crimson in color. The plant produces striking spadices, which are the actual flowers, often contrasting in color with the spathes. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.

The foliage is typically dark green and glossy, adding to its ornamental appeal.
