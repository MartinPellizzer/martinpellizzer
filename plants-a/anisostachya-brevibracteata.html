Anisostachya brevibracteata

Anisostachya brevibracteata, commonly known as the narrow-bracted grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences with pale green to brownish flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grassland and savanna habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and restoration projects.
