Adromischus cooperi

Adromischus cooperi, commonly known as the cooper's adenophora, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, dark green leaves that are often tinged with red or purple under direct sunlight. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Adromischus cooperi is prized for its compact growth habit and ornamental appeal in rock gardens and containers.
