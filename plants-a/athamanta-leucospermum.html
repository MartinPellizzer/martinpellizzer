Athamanta leucospermum

Athamanta leucospermum, commonly known as the white-flowered snowball tree, is a species native to South Africa.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that typically grows in coastal and mountainous regions. The plant is characterized by its dense clusters of white, fluffy flowers that bloom in spring. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often covered in fine hairs.

Athamanta leucospermum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its showy display.
