Anapausia nicotianifolium

Anapausia nicotianifolium, commonly known as the tobacco-leaf anapausia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Acanthaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil, and thrives in humid, shaded environments. It is often found growing in forest understories and along stream banks.
