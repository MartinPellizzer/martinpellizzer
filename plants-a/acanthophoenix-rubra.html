Acanthophoenix rubra

Acanthophoenix rubra, commonly known as the red palm, is a species of palm native to the island of Socotra.

It is characterized by its distinctive red or orange-colored leaves and a slender, unbranched trunk. The plant has a unique morphology, with long, feather-like leaves that are often covered in a waxy cuticle. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, adapting to the harsh climate of its native habitat.

Due to its limited distribution and ecological significance, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
