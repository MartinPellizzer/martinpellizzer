Alchemilla chlorosericea

Alchemilla chlorosericea, commonly known as the silver-haired lady's mantle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It features basal leaves that are broadly ovate with a silvery-white underside, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in panicle-like clusters during the summer months. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas or along stream banks.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens with wet or damp soil conditions.
