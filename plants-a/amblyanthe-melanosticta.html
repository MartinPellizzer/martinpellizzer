Amblyanthe melanosticta

Amblyanthe melanosticta, commonly known as the black-streaked orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Asia, particularly found in countries like India and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, dark green leaves. Its flowers are typically pale yellow or greenish-white with dark brown or black markings.

Amblyanthe melanosticta is often grown as a decorative plant due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
