Aphelandra dodsonii

Aphelandra dodsonii, commonly known as the Dodson's flameleaf.

It is a member of the Aroid family, characterized by its striking, colorful bracts that resemble flowers. The plant features a slender, upright stem with broad, glossy, dark green leaves that have a velvety texture. Native to the tropical regions of South America, it thrives in humid, warm environments.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and botanical collections.
