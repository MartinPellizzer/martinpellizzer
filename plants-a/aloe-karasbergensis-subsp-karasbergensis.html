Aloe karasbergensis subsp. karasbergensis

Aloe karasbergensis subsp.

karasbergensis, commonly known as the Karasberg aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa. It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a reddish tinge, often bearing white tubercles.

The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow to orange flowers. It is adapted to arid environments and stores water in its leaves.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional uses in herbal medicine.
