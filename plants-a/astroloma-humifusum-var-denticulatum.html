Astroloma humifusum var. denticulatum

Astroloma humifusum var. denticulatum, commonly known as the serrated astroloma, is a low-growing shrub native to southern Australia.

It features small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink, often with a distinctive, star-like appearance. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to dry, sunny conditions.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in native gardens.
