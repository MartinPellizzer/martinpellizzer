Arracacia decumbens

Arracacia decumbens, commonly known as jaborandi, is a flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the family Apocynaceae and is characterized by its trailing growth habit and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces a milky sap that contains various alkaloids, some of which have medicinal properties. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

However, the plant is toxic and should be handled with care.
