Aster foliaceus

Aster foliaceus, commonly known as the leafy aster, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the eastern United States.

It features delicate, daisy-like flowers that bloom in shades of blue and purple from late summer to early fall. The plant has finely divided, fern-like foliage that adds a soft texture to gardens. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained, moist soil.

Aster foliaceus is often used in native plant gardens for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
