Ardisia excelsa

Ardisia excelsa, commonly known as the glossy buckeye, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, glossy leaves that are typically elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers followed by bright red, fleshy berries that are attractive to birds. Ardisia excelsa thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forests.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for horticultural use in tropical and subtropical gardens.
