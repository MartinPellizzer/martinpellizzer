Aspalathus incana

Aspalathus incana, commonly known as the silver bush, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal regions.

Aspalathus incana is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is used in traditional medicine.
