Aster delectus

Aster delectus, commonly known as the delightful aster, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that come in a variety of colors, including purple, blue, and white. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are upright and sturdy.

Aster delectus is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
