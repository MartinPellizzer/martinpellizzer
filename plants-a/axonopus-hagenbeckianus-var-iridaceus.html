Axonopus hagenbeckianus var. iridaceus

Axonopus hagenbeckianus var. iridaceus, commonly known as the iridescent grass, is a variant of the axonopus genus characterized by its striking, colorful foliage.

This plant typically exhibits a clump-forming growth habit with narrow, grass-like leaves that display a range of hues, including shades of green, blue, and purple. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments. The plant is noted for its ornamental value, particularly due to the iridescent sheen on its leaves.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and landscape design.
