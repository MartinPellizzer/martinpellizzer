Alternanthera scandens

Alternanthera scandens, commonly known as redstem sorrel, is a tropical flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its bright red stems and heart-shaped leaves that often display a striking red or purple hue. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white or pink flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Alternanthera scandens is often used in ornamental gardening for its vibrant foliage.
