Anemone regeliana

Anemone regeliana, commonly known as the Regel's anemone, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features showy, single or double flowers with white petals and a yellow center, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has finely divided, feather-like leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine hairs. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and alpine settings.

Its botanical characteristics make it a striking addition to botanical gardens and native plant collections.
