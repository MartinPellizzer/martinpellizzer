Amaryllis mexicana

Amaryllis mexicana, commonly known as the Mexican bellflower, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters and features broad, strap-like leaves. It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The flowers bloom in late summer to early autumn, adding a vibrant touch to gardens and landscapes.
