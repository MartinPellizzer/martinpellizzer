Anoectochilus dewildeorum

Anoectochilus dewildeorum, commonly known as the Dewilde's slipper orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the high-altitude regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, slipper-shaped lip that is often adorned with intricate patterns and a rich, velvety texture. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often on forest floors or among leaf litter. Its flowers are usually small, with a striking combination of colors ranging from white to pale pink and purple.

Anoectochilus dewildeorum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts for its delicate beauty.
