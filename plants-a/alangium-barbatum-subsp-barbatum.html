Alangium barbatum subsp. barbatum

Alangium barbatum subsp.

barbatum, commonly known as the hairy alangium, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia. It belongs to the family Berberidaceae and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are often hairy on the underside.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional uses in local medicine.
