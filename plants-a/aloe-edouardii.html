Aloe edouardii

Aloe edouardii, commonly known as the Edward's aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, gray-green leaves that store water, typical of desert plants. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, requiring minimal water.

Due to its ornamental value and hardiness, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
