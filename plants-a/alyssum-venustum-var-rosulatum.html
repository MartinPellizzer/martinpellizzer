Alyssum venustum var. rosulatum

Alyssum venustum var. rosulatum, commonly known as the Rosulate Alyssum, is a low-growing, mat-forming perennial native to southern Europe.

It features dense clusters of small, fragrant white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring and early summer. The plant has silvery-gray, woolly foliage that is both ornamental and drought-tolerant. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a border plant.

Its compact growth habit and long blooming period make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
