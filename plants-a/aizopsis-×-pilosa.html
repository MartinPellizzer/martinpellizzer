Aizopsis × pilosa

Aizopsis × pilosa, commonly known as the African fern palm, is a hybrid plant resulting from the cross between Aizopsis lehmannii and Aizopsis indica.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate leaves that resemble those of a palm tree. The plant is native to southern Africa and thrives in humid, tropical climates. Its foliage makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and landscape design.

It is also valued for its ability to tolerate a range of soil types and its relatively low maintenance requirements.
