Anthurium contemtum

Anthurium contemtum, commonly known as the velvet leaf anthurium, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are velvety to the touch and often display a deep green or reddish hue. The plant produces small, spadix-like inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, which can range from white to pink or red. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Anthurium contemtum is a popular ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
