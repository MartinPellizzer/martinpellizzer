Asplundia aulacostigma

Asplundia aulacostigma, commonly known as the narrow-ribbed arrowroot, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

Asplundia aulacostigma is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has also been used in traditional medicine.
