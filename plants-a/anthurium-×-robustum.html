Anthurium × robustum

Anthurium × robustum, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a hybrid plant native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces striking spadices, which are bright red or orange in color, often surrounded by a white or green spathe. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Anthurium × robustum is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
