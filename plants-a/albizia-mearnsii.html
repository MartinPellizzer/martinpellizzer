Albizia mearnsii

Albizia mearnsii, commonly known as the silk thorn acacia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its feathery, bipinnate leaves and clusters of small, yellowish-white flowers. The plant produces long, slender pods that contain several seeds. It is often used in reforestation due to its rapid growth and ability to fix nitrogen in the soil.

Albizia mearnsii is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a decorative plant.
