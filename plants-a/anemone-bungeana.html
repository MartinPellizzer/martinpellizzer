Anemone bungeana

Anemone bungeana, commonly known as the Bunge's anemone, is a herbaceous perennial native to the mountainous regions of central Asia.

It features showy, white to pink flowers that bloom in early spring, often appearing before the leaves fully develop. The plant has a short, rhizomatous root system and hairy stems that are often reddish in color. Its basal leaves are palmately lobed and have a velvety texture.

Anemone bungeana thrives in well-drained soils and partial to full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and alpine displays.
