Asplenium dulongjiangense

Asplenium dulongjiangense, commonly known as the Dulongjiang spleenwort, is a rare fern species native to China.

It belongs to the family Aspleniaceae and is characterized by its small, intricate leaves that are typically arranged in a compact rosette. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in rocky or forested areas. Its fronds are usually dark green and have a leathery texture, with a distinctive venation pattern.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a botanically significant and ecologically valuable species.
