Aspidistra partita

Aspidistra partita, commonly known as the silver-leaf aspidistra, is a striking evergreen perennial native to Japan.

It features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are dark green with a silvery-white midrib, creating a striking visual contrast. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in spring, followed by dark blue berries. It thrives in low-light conditions and prefers moist, well-drained soil.

Aspidistra partita is valued for its elegant foliage and adaptability to indoor environments.
