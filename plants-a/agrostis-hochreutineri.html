Agrostis hochreutineri

Agrostis hochreutineri, commonly known as Hochreuter's bentgrass, is a perennial grass species native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its fine, tufted growth habit and slender, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows, pastures, and along stream banks. Its inflorescence consists of a dense cluster of spikelets, which are often tinged with red or purple.

Agrostis hochreutineri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping and turfgrass management.
