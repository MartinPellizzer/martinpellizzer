Anthurium herthae

Anthurium herthae, commonly known as the Herth's arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces vibrant red spadices, which are the modified leaves that enclose the true flowers. Anthurium herthae thrives in high humidity and indirect light, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to adapt well to various indoor environments when provided with proper care.
