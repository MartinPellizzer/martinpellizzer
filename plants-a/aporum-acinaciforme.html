Aporum acinaciforme

Aporum acinaciforme, commonly known as the sword fern, is a species of fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its sword-shaped, elongated fronds that can grow up to several feet in length. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and prefers well-draining soil. Its rhizomes spread horizontally, allowing it to form dense colonies.

Aporum acinaciforme is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to create a lush, green ground cover.
