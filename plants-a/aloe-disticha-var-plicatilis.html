Aloe disticha var. plicatilis

Aloe disticha var. plicatilis, commonly known as the pleated aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, dark green leaves that are pleated or folded, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow flowers during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with plenty of sunlight.

Its compact size and ornamental leaves make it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
