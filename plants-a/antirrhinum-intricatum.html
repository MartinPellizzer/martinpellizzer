Antirrhinum intricatum

Antirrhinum intricatum, commonly known as the intricate snapdragon, is a flowering plant belonging to the Plantaginaceae family.

It is native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa, where it thrives in rocky and coastal environments. Antirrhinum intricatum is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
