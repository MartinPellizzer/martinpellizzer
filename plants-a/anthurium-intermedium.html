Anthurium intermedium

Anthurium intermedium, commonly known as the velvet leaf anthurium, is a tropical flowering plant native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a velvety texture. The plant produces striking spadices, which are brightly colored, spadix-like structures that serve as the flower. Anthurium intermedium thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires high levels of moisture and indirect light.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for indoor gardening and botanical collections.
