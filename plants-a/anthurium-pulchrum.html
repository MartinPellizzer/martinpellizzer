Anthurium pulchrum

Anthurium pulchrum, commonly known as the "Beautiful Arrowhead," is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces striking spadices, which are brightly colored, spadix-like flowers that are often red or orange in hue. Anthurium pulchrum thrives in humid, warm environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

It is a popular ornamental plant due to its vibrant foliage and unique floral structures.
