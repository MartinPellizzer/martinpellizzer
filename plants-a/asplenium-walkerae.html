Asplenium walkerae

Asplenium walkerae, commonly known as the Walkera's spleenwort, is a small, epiphytic fern belonging to the family Aspleniaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in the mountainous regions of countries like Thailand and Vietnam. This plant typically grows on tree trunks or rocks, thriving in humid, shaded environments. Its fronds are narrow and closely spaced, with a delicate, lace-like appearance.

Asplenium walkerae is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in tropical gardens and greenhouses.
