Aponogeton taiwanensis

Aponogeton taiwanensis, commonly known as the Taiwan arrowhead, is a tropical aquatic plant native to Taiwan.

It is characterized by its arrowhead-shaped leaves that float on the water surface, with long, slender roots that anchor it in the substrate. The plant produces striking white or pink flowers that rise above the water, adding ornamental value to ponds and water gardens. It thrives in warm, shallow waters with good sunlight exposure.

Aponogeton taiwanensis is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal and ease of care in aquatic environments.
