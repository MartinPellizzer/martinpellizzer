Asarum hexalobum var. hexalobum

Asarum hexalobum var. hexalobum, commonly known as Indian snakeroot, is a perennial herb native to the eastern United States.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a velvety texture and a distinctive, mottled appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in a spike, blooming in late spring. Its rhizomatous root system is thick and fleshy, often used in traditional medicine.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.
