Achyranthes argentea var. villosior

Achyranthes argentea var. villosior, commonly known as silverleaf smartweed, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features slender, upright stems that are often covered in fine, silvery hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and have a slightly hairy surface. It produces small, greenish flowers in clusters, followed by three-lobed, reddish-brown seeds.

This plant thrives in moist soils and is often found in wetlands, marshes, and along stream banks.
