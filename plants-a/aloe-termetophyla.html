Aloe termetophyla

Aloe termetophyla, commonly known as the termite aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are thick and fleshy, adapted for water storage. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow flowers, which bloom in the summer months. Its leaves contain a gel-like substance that is used in traditional medicine and skincare products.

Aloe termetophyla is relatively drought-tolerant, making it suitable for arid and semi-arid environments.
