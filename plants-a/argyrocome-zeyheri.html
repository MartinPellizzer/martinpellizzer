Argyrocome zeyheri

Argyrocome zeyheri, commonly known as the silverleaf tree, is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub or tree native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-white undersides on its leaves, which give it its common name. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are typically followed by spherical, woody fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.

Argyrocome zeyheri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
