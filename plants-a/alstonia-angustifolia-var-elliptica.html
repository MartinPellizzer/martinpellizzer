Alstonia angustifolia var. elliptica

Alstonia angustifolia var. elliptica, commonly known as the narrow-leaf alstonia, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It features lanceolate to elliptic-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy on top, with a lighter underside. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers arranged in clusters, followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for horticultural collections in warm climates.
