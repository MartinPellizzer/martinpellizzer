Adromischus subdistichus

Adromischus subdistichus, commonly known as the silver leaf or ghost plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, silver-green leaves that are covered in fine, white hairs. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
