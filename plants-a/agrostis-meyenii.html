Agrostis meyenii

Agrostis meyenii, commonly known as the Meyen's bentgrass, is a perennial grass species native to South America.

It is characterized by its fine, tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, temperate environments, often found in meadows and along stream banks. Its inflorescence consists of a dense cluster of spikelets, which are small, compact flower clusters.

Agrostis meyenii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping and turfgrass applications.
