Anthurium cardenasii

Anthurium cardenasii, commonly known as the Cadenas anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Colombia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped spathes that can range in color from deep red to orange. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers within a spadix, which is typical of the Araceae family. Its foliage is dark green and glossy, with prominent veins that add to its ornamental appeal.

Anthurium cardenasii thrives in humid, warm environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.
