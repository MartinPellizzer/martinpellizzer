Astilbe longipedicellata

Astilbe longipedicellata, commonly known as the long-stemmed astilbe, is a herbaceous perennial native to China.

It features upright, branched stems that bear clusters of small, fragrant, pinkish-white flowers. The plant has lance-shaped, dark green leaves that are densely arranged along the stem. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in forest understories.

Astilbe longipedicellata is valued in gardens for its ornamental foliage and delicate floral display.
