Aleuritia Sikkimensis

Aleuritia sikkimensis is a rare and unique plant native to the eastern Himalayas. It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is known for its striking floral display. This evergreen shrub thrives in cool, moist environments at high altitudes. Its leaves are dark green and glossy, while its flowers are white and fragrant. Due to its limited range and habitat specificity, it is considered a conservation concern.
