Allium nuttallii

Allium nuttallii, commonly known as Nuttall's onion, is a species of flowering plant in the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is native to the western United States, particularly found in arid and semi-arid regions. The plant produces small, spherical bulbs and has narrow, grass-like leaves. Its flowers are typically white or pale purple, arranged in a loose cluster.

It is often used in traditional herbal medicine and is valued for its aromatic properties.
