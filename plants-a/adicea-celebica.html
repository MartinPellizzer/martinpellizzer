Adicea celebica

Adicea celebica, commonly known as the Celebes sandalwood, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Indonesia.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae and is characterized by its aromatic, oily wood that is highly valued for its fragrance and durability. The plant typically grows in lowland and hill forest areas, reaching heights of up to 20 meters. Its leaves are alternate, elliptical, and have a smooth texture with a glossy appearance.

The flowers are small and white, arranged in clusters, and the fruit is a small, round drupe.
