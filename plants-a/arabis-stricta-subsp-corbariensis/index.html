Arabis Stricta Subsp. Corbariensis

Arabis stricta subsp. corbariensis is a rare and lesser-known member of the Brassicaceae family. Native to a specific region in France, this subspecies thrives in rocky and coastal environments. Its compact growth habit and distinctive white flowers make it a striking addition to botanical gardens. Due to its limited distribution, it is considered endangered and requires careful conservation efforts. Studying this plant offers insights into the adaptability of flora in extreme conditions.
