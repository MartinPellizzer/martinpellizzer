Aneilema nummularia

Aneilema nummularia, commonly known as the shield leaf plant, is a species native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that are round or oval in shape, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

Aneilema nummularia thrives in humid, well-drained soils and requires partial to full sunlight for optimal growth.
