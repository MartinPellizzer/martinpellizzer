Abelia aschersoniana

Abelia aschersoniana, commonly known as the Chinese abelia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features opposite, elliptical leaves that turn yellow in the fall. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white to pink flowers in the summer. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained soil.

Abelia aschersoniana is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various garden conditions.
