Anthurium elatum

Anthurium elatum, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces vibrant red spathes that enclose a spadix, which is the actual flowering structure. Anthurium elatum thrives in humid, warm environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical landscapes.
