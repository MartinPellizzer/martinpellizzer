Aulomyrcia insularis

Aulomyrcia insularis, commonly known as the island hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean islands.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in coastal areas and sandy soils. The plant produces showy, five-petaled flowers that are usually pink or red in color. Its leaves are broad and elliptical, often with a waxy texture.
