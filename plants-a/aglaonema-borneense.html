Aglaonema borneense

Aglaonema borneense, commonly known as the Bornean prayer plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated foliage with shades of white, green, and pink. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are followed by red berries. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value.

Aglaonema borneense is also valued for its air-purifying properties and is often used in traditional medicine.
