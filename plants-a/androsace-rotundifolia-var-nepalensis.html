Androsace rotundifolia var. nepalensis

Androsace rotundifolia var. nepalensis, commonly known as the Nepal cushion plant, is a low-growing perennial herb native to the Himalayan region.

It features dense, mat-forming growth with small, rounded leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of delicate, pinkish-white flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine meadows and rocky habitats.

Its compact form and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for rock gardens and alpine collections.
