Atractylodes chinensis var. liaotungensis

Atractylodes chinensis var. liaotungensis, commonly known as the Liaotung Atractylodes, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its rhizomatous root system and erect, hairy stems. The plant produces yellowish to brownish flowers arranged in a composite head, typical of the Asteraceae family. Its rhizomes are widely used in traditional Chinese medicine for their purported digestive and anti-inflammatory properties.

The species is often cultivated for its medicinal value and is found in moist, shaded environments in mountainous regions.
