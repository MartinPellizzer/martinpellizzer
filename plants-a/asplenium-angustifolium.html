Asplenium angustifolium

Asplenium angustifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaved spleenwort, is a small evergreen fern belonging to the Aspleniaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, lance-shaped leaves that are typically less than 10 centimeters in length. This plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found growing on rock surfaces or in crevices. Its fronds are usually clustered at the base, with a distinctive, compact growth habit.

Asplenium angustifolium is primarily found in temperate regions and is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value.
