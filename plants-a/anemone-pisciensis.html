Anemone pisciensis

Anemone pisciensis, commonly known as the water anemone, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in wet or marshy habitats, thriving in moist, well-drained soils. Its leaves are usually heart-shaped and have a velvety texture.

Anemone pisciensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting pollinators.
