Arisaema urashima var. pictum

Arisaema urashima var. pictum, commonly known as the painted urashima arisaema, is a striking ornamental plant native to Japan.

It is characterized by its unique, palmate leaves with a vibrant green color and intricate patterns that resemble brushwork. The plant produces a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe, often in shades of white, pink, or red, which adds to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic value.

The species is also valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
