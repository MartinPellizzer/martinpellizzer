Anychia filiformis

Anychia filiformis, commonly known as the slender anychia, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant features slender, cylindrical stems and small, linear leaves that are arranged in a spiral pattern. Its flowers are borne in clusters and are usually pale blue or violet in color.

Anychia filiformis is adapted to dry, rocky soils and is often found in coastal areas.
