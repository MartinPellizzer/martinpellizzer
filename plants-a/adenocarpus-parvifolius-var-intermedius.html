Adenocarpus parvifolius var. intermedius

Adenocarpus parvifolius var. intermedius, commonly known as the small-leaved grassvetch, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, pinnately compound leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in racemes, which are followed by flat, dehiscent pods containing several seeds. It is typically found in grasslands and disturbed soils, thriving in temperate climates.

Its botanical features make it an important species for ecological studies and soil stabilization.
