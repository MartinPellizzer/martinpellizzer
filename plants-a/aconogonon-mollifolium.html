Aconogonon mollifolium

Aconogonon mollifolium, commonly known as the velvetleaf aconogonon, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its soft, velvety leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in disturbed soils or along roadsides.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens, though it can become invasive in certain environments.
