Aeonium × marreroi

Aeonium × marreroi, commonly known as the "Coral Cactus," is a hybrid succulent native to the Canary Islands.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy leaves that range in color from blue-green to pinkish-red, depending on light exposure. The plant produces vibrant orange to red flowers on tall, branching stems during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with full sun.

Aeonium × marreroi is popular in rock gardens and as a container plant due to its striking appearance and low maintenance requirements.
