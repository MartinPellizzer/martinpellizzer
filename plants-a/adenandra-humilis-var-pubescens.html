Adenandra humilis var. pubescens

Adenandra humilis var. pubescens, commonly known as the silky adenandra, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, hairy foliage that gives it a soft, velvety appearance. The plant produces small, white flowers arranged in clusters, which are often fragrant. Its leaves are typically oval-shaped with a waxy texture.

This species is well adapted to dry conditions and thrives in well-drained soils.
