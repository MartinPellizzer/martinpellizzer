Alguelagum lamiifolium

Alguelagum lamiifolium, commonly known as the silverleaf plant, is a species native to arid and semi-arid regions.

It is characterized by its silvery, fuzzy leaves that help reduce water loss through transpiration. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. Its root system is deep and extensive, allowing it to access groundwater in dry environments.

Alguelagum lamiifolium is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
