Achillea capillaris

Achillea capillaris, commonly known as slender yarrow, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to Europe and western Asia and is characterized by its slender stems and small, white or pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as meadows and rocky areas. It has feathery, aromatic leaves and is often used in traditional medicine.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for ecological and horticultural purposes.
