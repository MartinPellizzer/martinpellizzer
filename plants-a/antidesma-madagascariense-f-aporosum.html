Antidesma madagascariense f. aporosum

Antidesma madagascariense f. aporosum, commonly known as the Madagascar antidesma, is a shrub or small tree native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves that are typically elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, followed by capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds. Due to its unique morphology and limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
