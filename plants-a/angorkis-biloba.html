Angorkis biloba

Angorkis biloba, commonly known as the split-leaf fern, is a unique plant characterized by its distinctive bifurcated leaves.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia. The plant features fronds that split into two lobes, giving it its common name. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires consistent moisture.

Its intricate leaf structure makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
