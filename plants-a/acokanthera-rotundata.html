Acokanthera rotundata

Acokanthera rotundata, commonly known as the African arrow poison.

It is a flowering plant native to southern Africa, characterized by its dark green, glossy leaves and showy yellow flowers. The plant contains toxic alkaloids, making it a source of traditional poison for arrow tips. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland and savanna habitats.

Due to its toxicity, it is handled with caution and is not widely cultivated.
