Aerides suaveolens

Aerides suaveolens, commonly known as the sweet-scented orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, showy flowers that bloom in clusters during the warm season. The plant has strap-like, dark green leaves that are arranged in a basal rosette. It thrives in humid, well-drained environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Aerides suaveolens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in greenhouse settings.
