Aglaia cucullata

Aglaia cucullata, commonly known as the helmet tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Meliaceae and is characterized by its distinctive helmet-shaped fruit. The plant has dark green, glossy leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. Its bark is thick and often has a rough texture, while its wood is valued for its durability and aromatic properties.

Aglaia cucullata is also used in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic benefits.
