Aeranthes rutilus

Aeranthes rutilus, commonly known as the pink orchid, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, pinkish-white flowers that bloom during the rainy season. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, such as forest understories. Its leaves are elongated and lance-shaped, with a smooth texture.

Aeranthes rutilus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
