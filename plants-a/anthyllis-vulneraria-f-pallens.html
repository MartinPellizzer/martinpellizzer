Anthyllis vulneraria f. pallens

Anthyllis vulneraria f. pallens, commonly known as the pale woundwort, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit and is native to arid and semi-arid regions of southern Europe. The plant features pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer, and its leaves are trifoliate, consisting of three small, oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

This variety is distinguished by its lighter coloration compared to the typical form of Anthyllis vulneraria.
