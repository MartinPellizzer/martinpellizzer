Alectorolophus antiquus

Alectorolophus antiquus, commonly known as the ancient larkspur, is a member of the Ranunculaceae family.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its basal rosette of lanceolate leaves and striking blue or purple flowers. Alectorolophus antiquus is known for its ornamental value and has been cultivated for centuries in gardens and botanical collections.
