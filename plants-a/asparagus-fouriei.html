Asparagus fouriei

Asparagus fouriei, commonly known as the Chinese asparagus fern, is a perennial flowering plant native to China and Southeast Asia.

It features arching, feather-like leaves that give it a lush, fern-like appearance. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers in clusters, which are followed by red berries. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for shaded garden areas.

Asparagus fouriei is also valued for its ornamental qualities and is often used in landscaping and indoor gardening.
