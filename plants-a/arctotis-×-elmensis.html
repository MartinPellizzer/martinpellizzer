Arctotis × elmensis

Arctotis × elmensis, commonly known as the hybrid arctotis or sometimes the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, daisy-like flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub with narrow, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscaping.

Arctotis × elmensis is known for its drought tolerance and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
