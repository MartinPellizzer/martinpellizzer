Amomum subulatum

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its narrow, pointed leaves and aromatic, spicy fruits. Native to Southeast Asia, it thrives in tropical and subtropical climates with high humidity. The plant's rhizomes and fruits are used in traditional medicine and as a spice.
