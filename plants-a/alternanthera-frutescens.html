Alternanthera frutescens

Alternanthera frutescens, commonly known as red flame or red shrimp plant, is a tropical flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, vibrant red or pink bracts that surround small, inconspicuous flowers. The plant typically grows as an annual or perennial herb, reaching heights of up to 3 feet. Its foliage is often variegated with shades of green and red, adding visual interest to gardens.

It thrives in warm, humid conditions and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
