Alsophila firma

Alsophila firma, commonly known as the hardy tree fern, is a species native to Southeast Asia and the Pacific Islands.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and a crown of feathery fronds that give it a distinctive, elegant appearance. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical forests. Its rhizome system allows it to anchor firmly in the soil while supporting its tall, upright structure.

The fronds are typically evergreen, providing a lush green canopy throughout the year.
