Agropyron ponticum

Agropyron ponticum, commonly known as the tall wheatgrass, is a perennial grass native to Eurasia.

It is characterized by its robust growth, with tall, slender stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces spike-like inflorescences that contain small, two-lobed flowers. It thrives in a variety of soil types and is often found in open, disturbed areas.

Agropyron ponticum is valued for its ability to stabilize soil and is used in ecological restoration projects.
