Ascyrum nummulariifolium

Ascyrum nummulariifolium, commonly known as the silver leaf plant, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, round, silver-gray leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, white flowers in clusters during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Due to its drought tolerance and ornamental value, it is often cultivated in arid climates.
