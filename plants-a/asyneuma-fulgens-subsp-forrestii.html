Asyneuma fulgens subsp. forrestii

Asyneuma fulgens subsp.

forrestii, commonly known as the Forest's blazing aster, is a flowering plant native to specific regions of Southeast Asia. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, brightly colored inflorescences.

The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as mountain forests. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers range from yellow to orange in color.

It plays a role in local ecosystems by providing nectar for pollinators and habitat for small organisms.
