Anotis filiformis

Anotis filiformis, commonly known as the slender anotis, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and has a low, spreading habit. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often covered in fine hairs.

Anotis filiformis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
