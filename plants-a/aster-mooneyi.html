Aster mooneyi

Aster mooneyi, commonly known as the Mooney's aster, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is often found in sagebrush habitats. Its flowers are usually purple or blue, with ray florets surrounding a central disk of tubular flowers.

Aster mooneyi is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting local pollinators.
