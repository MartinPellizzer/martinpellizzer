Aerides rosea

Aerides rosea, commonly known as the rose orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking pink and white flowers with a distinctive red lip. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, forming pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are broad and elliptical, often with a waxy texture.

Aerides rosea thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity and well-draining potting media.
