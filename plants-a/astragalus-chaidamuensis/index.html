Astragalus Chaidamuensis

Astragalus chaidamuensis is a rare and unique plant species native to the Qinghai-Tibet Plateau. It belongs to the legume family and is known for its distinctive floral structure and ecological significance. This plant thrives in high-altitude environments, showcasing remarkable adaptability to extreme conditions. Its discovery and study contribute to our understanding of biodiversity in arid and cold regions. Conservation efforts are crucial to protect this species from habitat loss and climate change.
