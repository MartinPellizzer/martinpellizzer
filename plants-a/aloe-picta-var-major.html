Aloe picta var. major

Aloe picta var. major, commonly known as the giant aloe, is a striking succulent native to South Africa.

It features tall, spiky leaves that can grow up to 3 meters in height, forming a rosette structure. The leaves are thick and fleshy, with a bluish-green color and distinctive white spots or markings. It produces tall flower stalks with yellow flowers, which bloom in summer.

This species is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
