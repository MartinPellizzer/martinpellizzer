Artemisia sogdiana

Artemisia sogdiana, commonly known as the Sogdian wormwood, is a perennial shrub native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, silver-gray foliage. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flower heads that are typically borne in clusters. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky or sandy soils.

Artemisia sogdiana is valued for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
