Astragalus sakaryaensis

Astragalus sakaryaensis, commonly known as Sakarya milkvetch, is a flowering plant species native to Turkey.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, pea-like flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions.

It plays a role in soil stabilization and is of interest for its potential ecological and medicinal properties.
