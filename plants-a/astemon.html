Astemon

Astemon, commonly known as the sea holly, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive blue or purple flower heads surrounded by spiny, leaf-like bracts. Native to the Mediterranean region, Astemon thrives in coastal areas and well-drained soils. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
