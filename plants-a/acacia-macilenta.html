Acacia macilenta

Acacia macilenta, commonly known as the slender acacia, is a shrub native to the arid regions of South Africa.

It features delicate, feathery leaves that are often clustered at the ends of its branches. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and birds. Its seed pods are elongated and contain hard, angular seeds.

Acacia macilenta is well adapted to drought conditions, making it a resilient plant in harsh environments.
