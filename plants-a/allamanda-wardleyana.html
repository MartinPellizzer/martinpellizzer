Allamanda wardleyana

Allamanda wardleyana, commonly known as the yellow allamanda, is a striking tropical shrub native to South America.

It features large, showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically bright yellow, though they can vary in hue. The plant has thick, waxy leaves that are dark green and often have a slightly serrated edge. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Allamanda wardleyana is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for gardeners in tropical and subtropical regions.
