Asterohyptis

Asterohyptis, commonly known as the silverleaf hyptis, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pale purple. Asterohyptis typically grows as a shrub or subshrub, often found in moist, shaded environments.

Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.
