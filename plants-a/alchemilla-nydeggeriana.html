Alchemilla nydeggeriana

Alchemilla nydeggeriana, commonly known as the Nydegger's lady's mantle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its basal rosette of broad, hairy leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Alchemilla nydeggeriana is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and its ability to attract pollinators.
