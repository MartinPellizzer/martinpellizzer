Achoriphragma botschantzevii

Achoriphragma botschantzevii, commonly known as the Botswana thornveld grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and slender, wiry stems that often bear sharp spines. The plant typically thrives in dry, sandy soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a hairy texture that helps reduce water loss.

Achoriphragma botschantzevii plays a role in stabilizing soils and providing forage for wildlife in its native habitat.
