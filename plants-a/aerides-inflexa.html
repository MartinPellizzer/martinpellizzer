Aerides inflexa

Aerides inflexa, commonly known as the flexed orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its pendant, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters along the stems. The plant has strap-like, dark green leaves that are often spotted with silver or white markings. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining potting media.

Aerides inflexa is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
