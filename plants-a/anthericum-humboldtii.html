Anthericum humboldtii

Anthericum humboldtii, commonly known as the Humboldt lily, is a striking plant native to the Andes Mountains of South America.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stem that can reach up to 2 meters in height. The plant produces large, showy white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are long, narrow, and grass-like, forming a dense rosette at the base.

Anthericum humboldtii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its elegant appearance.
