Aloe bicolor

Aloe bicolor, commonly known as the two-colored aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, thick leaves that are typically green with a reddish or pinkish tinge along the edges. The plant produces yellow flowers in clusters during the summer months. Its leaves contain a gel-like substance that is often used for its soothing properties.

Aloe bicolor thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.
