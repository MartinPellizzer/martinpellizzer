Arisaema commutatum

Arisaema commutatum, commonly known as the Chinese threadleaf arrowhead, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to East Asia.

It features a distinctive arrowhead-shaped leaf with a long, narrow petiole. The plant produces a spadix surrounded by a modified leaf called a spathe, which is typically green or reddish in color. Its flowers are unisexual, with male and female flowers located on separate parts of the spadix.

Arisaema commutatum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has traditional uses in herbal medicine.
