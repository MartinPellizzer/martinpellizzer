Arachnis siamensis

Arachnis siamensis, commonly known as the Siam arachnid, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in tropical rainforests, thriving in humid and shaded environments. Its roots are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

The botanical study of Arachnis siamensis contributes to the understanding of orchid diversity and ecological adaptations in tropical ecosystems.
