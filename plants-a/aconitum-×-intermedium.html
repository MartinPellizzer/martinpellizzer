Aconitum × intermedium

Aconitum × intermedium, commonly known as the hybrid leopard's bane, is a member of the Ranunculaceae family.

It is a tall perennial herb that typically grows to heights of 1 to 2 meters. The plant features showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from blue to purple, often with a contrasting yellow or orange center. Its leaves are deeply lobed and have a rough, hairy texture.

Aconitum × intermedium is native to Europe and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.
