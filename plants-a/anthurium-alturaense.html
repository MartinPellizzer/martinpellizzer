Anthurium alturaense

Anthurium alturaense, commonly known as the Alturaense Arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central America.

It is characterized by its glossy, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a striking metallic sheen. The plant produces striking spadices, which are large, colorful inflorescences that are often red or orange in color. It thrives in humid, warm environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium alturaense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in botanical gardens.
