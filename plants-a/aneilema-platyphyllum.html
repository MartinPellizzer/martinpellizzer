Aneilema platyphyllum

Aneilema platyphyllum, commonly known as the broadleaf aneilema, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features lance-shaped leaves that are typically bright green and glossy, with a smooth texture. The plant produces small, clustered white flowers that bloom throughout the year, adding ornamental value. It thrives in humid, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.

Aneilema platyphyllum is often cultivated for its attractive foliage and ease of care in indoor and tropical garden settings.
