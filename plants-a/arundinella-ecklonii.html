Arundinella ecklonii

Arundinella ecklonii, commonly known as the yellow flag iris, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features tall, slender stems that can grow up to 2 meters in height, often with a reddish or purple hue. The plant produces striking yellow to orange flowers in clusters, typically blooming in late summer. Its leaves are long, narrow, and grass-like, often forming a dense basal rosette.

Arundinella ecklonii is adapted to wetland environments and is often found in marshes and along riverbanks.
