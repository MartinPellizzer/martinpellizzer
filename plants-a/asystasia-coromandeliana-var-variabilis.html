Asystasia coromandeliana var. variabilis

Asystasia coromandeliana var. variabilis, commonly known as the variable asystasia, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features lance-shaped leaves that are typically pubescent and arranged alternately along the stem. The plant produces small, tubular flowers in clusters, often with a purple or violet hue, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking foliage with unique texture and color.
