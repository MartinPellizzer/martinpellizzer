Armeriastrum peroninii

Armeriastrum peroninii, commonly known as the Peron's silverbush, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically reaches heights of 10 to 20 centimeters. The plant features small, clustered white or pale pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its foliage consists of narrow, silver-gray leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Armeriastrum peroninii thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
