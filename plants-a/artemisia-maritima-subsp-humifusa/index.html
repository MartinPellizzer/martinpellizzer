Artemisia Maritima Subsp. Humifusa

Artemisia maritima subsp. humifusa is a hardy shrub native to coastal regions of northwestern europe and north america. it thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in salt marshes and dunes. this subspecies is characterized by its low-growing habit and silvery-gray foliage. it is known for its aromatic leaves and its ability to tolerate harsh maritime conditions. due to its resilience and ecological importance, it plays a vital role in stabilizing coastal ecosystems.
