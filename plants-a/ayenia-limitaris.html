Ayenia limitaris

Ayenia limitaris, commonly known as the rock lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South America.

It typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing adaptations to harsh conditions. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered flowers in shades of yellow or orange. It is often found in coastal areas and can thrive in poor soil conditions.

Ayenia limitaris is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in challenging climates.
