Avellinia festucoides

Avellinia festucoides, commonly known as the silverleaf buckthorn, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray leaves and small, dark blue berries. The plant has a woody stem and is typically found in sandy or rocky soils. It is adapted to arid environments and can thrive in full sun.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive component of local flora.
