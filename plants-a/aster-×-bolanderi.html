Aster × bolanderi

Aster × bolanderi, commonly known as the Bolander's aster, is a hybrid perennial plant native to the western United States.

It typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and woodlands. The plant features showy, daisy-like flowers with blue to purple petals that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Aster × bolanderi is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological importance in supporting pollinators.
