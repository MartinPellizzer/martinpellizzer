Angraecum cowanii

Angraecum cowanii, commonly known as the Cowan's star orchid, is a rare and exquisite epiphytic orchid native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and large, showy white flowers with intricate patterns. The plant typically blooms at night, producing fragrant, star-shaped blossoms that attract specific nocturnal pollinators. Its foliage consists of long, narrow leaves that help it thrive in the humid, shaded environments of its natural habitat.

Due to its rarity and specific ecological requirements, it is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts and conservationists alike.
