Aloe fievetii

Aloe fievetii, commonly known as the Cape aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, erect stems with narrow, fleshy leaves that are typically bluish-green in color. The plant produces striking red or orange flowers during the summer months, which are clustered in umbels. Aloe fievetii is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
