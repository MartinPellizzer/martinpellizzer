Acetosella calligera

Acetosella calligera, commonly known as the silverweed, is a low-growing perennial plant belonging to the Primulaceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit and small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring. Acetosella calligera is often found in temperate regions and is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological benefits.
