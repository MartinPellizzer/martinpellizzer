Aronia arbutifolia f. glabra

Aronia arbutifolia f. glabra, commonly known as smooth flame bush, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern North America.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, glabrous stems and clustered, red-orange berries. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in partial to full sun. Its foliage is alternate and ovate, with a dark green upper surface and a lighter underside.

The smooth flame bush is valued for its ornamental appeal and wildlife benefits, providing food for birds and other animals.
