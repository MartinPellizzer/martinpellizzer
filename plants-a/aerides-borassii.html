Aerides borassii

Aerides borassii, commonly known as the giant orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy inflorescences that can reach up to 2 meters in height. The plant produces numerous fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in a raceme. Its leaves are broad and sword-shaped, often reaching lengths of up to 1 meter.

Aerides borassii thrives in humid, warm climates and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
