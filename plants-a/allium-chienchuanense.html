Allium chienchuanense

Allium chienchuanense, commonly known as the Chienchuan garlic, is a species of flowering plant in the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is native to China, specifically found in the Yunnan Province, where it grows in mountainous regions. The plant produces spherical bulbs that are often used in traditional Chinese medicine. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, while its flowers are typically white or pale pink.

Allium chienchuanense is valued for its potential medicinal properties and is also appreciated for its ornamental appeal.
