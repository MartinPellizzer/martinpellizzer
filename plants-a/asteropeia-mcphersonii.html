Asteropeia mcphersonii

Asteropeia mcphersonii, commonly known as the McPherson's aster, is a flowering plant native to specific regions of South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features slender, branching stems and small, clustered flowers that bloom in shades of white and purple. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, often found in dry, open habitats.
