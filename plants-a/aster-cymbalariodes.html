Aster cymbalariodes

Aster cymbalariodes, commonly known as the purple coneflower, is a herbaceous perennial native to North America.

It features showy, daisy-like flowers with purple petals and a yellow central disk, which attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. The plant typically grows to a height of 2 to 4 feet, with lance-shaped leaves that are hairy and gray-green in color. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Aster cymbalariodes is valued for its ornamental beauty and ecological benefits in native plant gardens.
