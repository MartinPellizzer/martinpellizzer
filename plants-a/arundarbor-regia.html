Arundarbor regia

Arundarbor regia, commonly known as the royal flame tree, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy red flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. The plant has compound leaves with long, slender leaflets that give it a feathery appearance. Its bark is smooth and dark brown, often developing fissures with age.

Arundarbor regia is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and public spaces.
