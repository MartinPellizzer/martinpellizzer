Astronia williamsii var. ramosii

Astronia williamsii var. ramosii, commonly known as the ramose astronia, is a shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its dense, branching structure and small, elliptical leaves that are typically arranged in opposite pairs. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers during the warmer months. Its foliage is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
