Achras nitida

Achras nitida, commonly known as the silver tree, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It is a small, evergreen tree characterized by its silvery, powdery coating on its leaves and bark. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters during the rainy season. Its unique appearance makes it a popular ornamental plant in tropical gardens.

Achras nitida is also valued for its hardy nature and ability to thrive in poor soil conditions.
