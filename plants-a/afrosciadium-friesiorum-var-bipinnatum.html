Afrosciadium friesiorum var. bipinnatum

Afrosciadium friesiorum var. bipinnatum, commonly known as the African fern, is a striking ornamental plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds that give it a lush, fern-like appearance. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, fertile soil to flourish. Its leaves are typically bipinnate, meaning they are divided into smaller, secondary pinnae, creating a finely textured foliage.

This variety is valued for its aesthetic appeal and is often used in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
