Anthurium vaupesianum

Anthurium vaupesianum, commonly known as the velvet anthurium, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a velvety texture. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-shaped inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, often in shades of red, orange, or yellow. It thrives in humid, warm environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium vaupesianum is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
