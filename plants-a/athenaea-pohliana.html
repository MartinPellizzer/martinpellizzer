Athenaea pohliana

Athenaea pohliana, commonly known as the Pohliana tree, is a tropical evergreen species native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, glossy dark green leaves and produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in warm climates for its aesthetic qualities. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and requires partial to full sunlight.

The tree is also notable for its ability to adapt to various environmental conditions, making it a resilient choice for landscaping.
