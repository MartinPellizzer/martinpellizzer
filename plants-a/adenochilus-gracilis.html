Adenochilus gracilis

Adenochilus gracilis, commonly known as the slender spike orchid, is a flowering plant native to the eastern regions of Australia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, slender spikes that bear small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests or under dense vegetation. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often forming a rosette at the base.

Adenochilus gracilis is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
