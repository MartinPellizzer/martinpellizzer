Aloe amicorum

Aloe amicorum, commonly known as the friendship aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically gray-green in color, with a waxy coating to reduce water loss. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters during the cooler months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal watering.

Aloe amicorum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional medicinal uses.
