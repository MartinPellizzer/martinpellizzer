Arachniodes × kenzo-satakei

Arachniodes × kenzo-satakei, commonly known as the Kenzo Satake hybrid, is a hybrid fern that exhibits a unique combination of traits from its parent species.

It typically features lance-shaped, leathery fronds with a dark green color and a textured surface. The plant produces spore-bearing structures called sori, which are clustered on the underside of the fronds. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Its foliage provides a striking contrast to its surroundings, making it a popular choice in botanical gardens and shaded landscapes.
