Adenostegia canescens

Adenostegia canescens, commonly known as the hairy stemless sunflower, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

Adenostegia canescens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant yellow blooms.
