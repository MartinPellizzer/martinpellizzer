Aerides maculosa

Aerides maculosa, commonly known as the spotted orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its upright, branching stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces fragrant, showy flowers with a distinctive spotted pattern on the petals and sepals. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Aerides maculosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is popular among orchid enthusiasts.
