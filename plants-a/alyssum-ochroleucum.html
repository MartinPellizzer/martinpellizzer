Alyssum ochroleucum

Alyssum ochroleucum, commonly known as yellow whitlow grass, is a low-growing perennial plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It features small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to yellow, blooming in early spring. The plant has hairy, linear leaves and is often found in rocky or gravelly soils. It is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in poor soil conditions.

Alyssum ochroleucum is also known for its medicinal uses in traditional herbal remedies.
