Abutilon bedfordianum

Abutilon bedfordianum, commonly known as the velvetleaf or Chinese lantern plant, is a flowering plant in the mallow family.

It is characterized by its fuzzy, velvety leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces lantern-shaped, papery bracts that enclose its small, inconspicuous flowers. It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Abutilon bedfordianum thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
