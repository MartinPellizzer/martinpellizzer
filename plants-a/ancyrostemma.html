Ancyrostemma

Ancyrostemma, commonly known as the yellow nightshade, is a flowering plant belonging to the Solanaceae family.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and Australia. The plant features showy yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, often with a distinctive red or purple calyx. Its leaves are typically ovate or lance-shaped, with a rough texture.

Ancyrostemma is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of light conditions.
