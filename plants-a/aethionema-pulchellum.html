Aethionema pulchellum

Aethionema pulchellum, commonly known as the beautiful rockcress, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically reaches heights of about 10 to 20 centimeters. The plant features basal leaves that are pinnately lobed and are covered in a dense layer of white, woolly hairs. Its flowers are small, yellowish-green, and borne in clusters at the ends of the stems.

Aethionema pulchellum thrives in rocky or gravelly soils and is often found in dry, sunny habitats.
