Anthurium scherzerianum var. dixonii

Anthurium scherzerianum var. dixonii, commonly known as the Red Velvet Anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety red bracts that surround the spadix, giving it a dramatic appearance. The plant has dark green, glossy, heart-shaped leaves that contribute to its ornamental value. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

This variety is popular in horticulture for its vibrant color and ease of care.
