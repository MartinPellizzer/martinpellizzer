Alternanthera bettzickiana var. spathulata

Alternanthera bettzickiana var. spathulata, commonly known as the broadleaf Alternanthera, is a striking aquatic or semi-aquatic plant native to South America.

It features vibrant red, pink, or orange foliage that changes with light exposure and nutrient levels. The plant has lance-shaped to ovate leaves that are often variegated with contrasting colors. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is popular in both aquariums and garden landscapes.

Its ornamental value makes it a favored choice for water feature design and ornamental horticulture.
