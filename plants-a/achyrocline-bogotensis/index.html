Achyrocline Bogotensis

Achyrocline bogotensis is a rare and enigmatic flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of Colombia. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, known for its diverse and often showy blooms. This species is characterized by its delicate, small white flowers and hairy, narrow leaves. Due to its limited habitat and fragile ecosystem, it is considered endangered and faces significant conservation challenges. Studying Achyrocline bogotensis offers valuable insights into the biodiversity of Andean flora and the threats it faces in the wild.
