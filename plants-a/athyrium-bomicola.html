Athyrium bomicola

Athyrium bomicola, commonly known as the golden shield fern, is a species of fern native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the family Athyriaceae and is characterized by its delicate, feather-like fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. Its leaves are usually dark green with a slight golden hue on the underside, giving it its common name.

Athyrium bomicola is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appearance and ability to thrive in partial shade.
