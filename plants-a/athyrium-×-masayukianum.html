Athyrium × masayukianum

Athyrium × masayukianum, commonly known as the Japanese painted fern, is a hybrid fern species that exhibits a striking combination of foliage colors.

It features delicate, lace-like fronds with a mix of green, silver, and gold hues, creating a visually appealing appearance. The plant is native to Japan and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes. It thrives in shady, moist environments and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for adding texture and color to shaded garden areas.
