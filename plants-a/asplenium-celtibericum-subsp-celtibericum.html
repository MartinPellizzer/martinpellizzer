Asplenium celtibericum subsp. celtibericum

Asplenium celtibericum subsp.

celtibericum, commonly known as the Celtiberian spleenwort, is a small, terrestrial fern native to southern Europe. It typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky areas.

The plant has delicate, lance-shaped leaves that are usually less than 10 centimeters in length. It belongs to the family Aspleniaceae and is characterized by its compact growth habit and intricate frond structure.

This subspecies is of particular interest to botanists due to its restricted distribution and unique ecological adaptations.
