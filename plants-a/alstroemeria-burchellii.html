Alstroemeria burchellii

Alstroemeria burchellii, commonly known as the Burchell's flame lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South America.

It belongs to the family Alstroemeriaceae and is characterized by its striking, tubular, brightly colored flowers that range from red to orange and yellow. The plant features strap-like leaves and produces showy inflorescences that attract pollinators such as bees and birds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.

Alstroemeria burchellii is also valued for its ornamental beauty and relatively low maintenance requirements.
