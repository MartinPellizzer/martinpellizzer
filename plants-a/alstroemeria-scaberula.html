Alstroemeria scaberula

Alstroemeria scaberula, commonly known as the rough alstroemeria, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the family Alstroemeriaceae and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in temperate climates. Its leaves are narrow and slightly rough to the touch, giving it its common name.

Alstroemeria scaberula is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its vibrant blooms and long-lasting flowers.
