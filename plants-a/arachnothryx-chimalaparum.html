Arachnothryx chimalaparum

Arachnothryx chimalaparum, commonly known as the Chimalapa tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Central America.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, clustered flowers. The tree produces pods that contain edible seeds, which are often used in local cuisines. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Its ecological importance includes providing habitat and food for various wildlife species.
