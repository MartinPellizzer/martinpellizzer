Aerides multiflora var. dactyloides

Aerides multiflora var. dactyloides, commonly known as the Dactyloides orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, exhibiting an epiphytic lifestyle. Its leaves are dark green, leathery, and arranged in a rosette pattern.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in shaded, humid environments.
