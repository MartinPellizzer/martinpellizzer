Astragalus fontianus

Astragalus fontianus, commonly known as the Chinese milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow or white flowers. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in arid and semi-arid environments, making it a hardy species.

It is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional Chinese medicine.
