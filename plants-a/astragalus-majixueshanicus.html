Astragalus majixueshanicus

Astragalus majixueshanicus, commonly known as the Majixueshan milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to the Qinghai-Tibet Plateau in China.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its small, yellow pea-like flowers and compound leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky and alpine environments, adapting to the harsh conditions of high altitudes.

It plays a role in soil stabilization and is of interest for its potential medicinal properties.
