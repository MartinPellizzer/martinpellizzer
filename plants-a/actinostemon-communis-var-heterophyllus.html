Actinostemon communis var. heterophyllus

Actinostemon communis var. heterophyllus, commonly known as the variable leaf actinostemon, is a herbaceous plant native to certain regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive variegated foliage, which varies in shape and color depending on environmental conditions. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. Its leaves are often palmately lobed, with a soft, velvety texture.

This species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to different soil types.
