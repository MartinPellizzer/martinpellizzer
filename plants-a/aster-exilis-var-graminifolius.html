Aster exilis var. graminifolius

Aster exilis var. graminifolius, commonly known as the slender aster, is a low-growing perennial herb native to the western United States.

It features slender, grass-like leaves that are typically basal and linear in shape. The plant produces small, daisy-like flowers with white petals and a yellow central disk, blooming in late summer to early fall. It thrives in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes.

Its botanical characteristics make it well adapted to arid environments and suitable for xeriscaping.
