Arachnites luteus var. minus

Arachnites luteus var. minus, commonly known as the lesser yellow orchid, is a rare orchid species native to specific regions of Australia.

It is characterized by its delicate, yellowish flowers and compact growth habit. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as rainforests or under dense vegetation. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often arranged in a rosette pattern.

Botanically, it belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is noted for its unique floral structure and limited geographic distribution.
