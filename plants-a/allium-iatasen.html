Allium iatasen

Allium iatasen, commonly known as the purple onion, is a bulbous plant native to Central Asia.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of pale purple to pink flowers. The plant is characterized by its small, spherical bulbs that are often used in culinary applications. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Allium iatasen is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract beneficial insects.
