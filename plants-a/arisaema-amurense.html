Arisaema amurense

Arisaema amurense, commonly known as the Chinese arrowroot, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It features a short rhizome with a cluster of dark green, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces a spadix surrounded by a spathe, which is characteristic of the Araceae family. Its flowers are typically greenish-yellow, and it blooms in the summer months.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has traditional medicinal uses in some cultures.
