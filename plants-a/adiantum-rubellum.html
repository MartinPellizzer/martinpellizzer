Adiantum rubellum

Adiantum rubellum, commonly known as the red fern, is a species of fern that belongs to the family Adiantaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds that are typically reddish-brown in color, especially when young. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas. Its rhizomes are short and produce a dense network of roots that help anchor it to the substrate.

Adiantum rubellum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
