Actephila megistophylla

Actephila megistophylla, commonly known as the giant leaved actephila, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, palmate leaves that can reach up to 30 cm in diameter. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in dense clusters. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Due to its unique morphology, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists alike.
