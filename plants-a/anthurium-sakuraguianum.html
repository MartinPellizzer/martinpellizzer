Anthurium sakuraguianum

Anthurium sakuraguianum, commonly known as the Sakuraguian anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces striking spathes that can range in color from red to orange, often resembling a flower. It thrives in humid, warm environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium sakuraguianum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated as a houseplant.
