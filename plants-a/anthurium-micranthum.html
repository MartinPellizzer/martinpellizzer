Anthurium micranthum

Anthurium micranthum, commonly known as the small-flowered anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped leaves and unique spadix inflorescence. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers surrounded by a colorful spathe, which is often the primary ornamental feature. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium micranthum is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
