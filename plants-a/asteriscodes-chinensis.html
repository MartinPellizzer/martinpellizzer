Asteriscodes chinensis

Asteriscodes chinensis, commonly known as Chinese asterisk, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features small, star-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, arranged in a cluster. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for gardens and landscapes.

Asteriscodes chinensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional Chinese medicine.
