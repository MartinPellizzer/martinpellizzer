Arundo coleotricha var. versicolor

Arundo coleotricha var. versicolor, commonly known as the variable rush, is a perennial grass-like plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It typically grows in wet or moist environments such as marshes, stream banks, and damp woodlands. The plant has long, narrow leaves that are often variegated, giving it its common name. Its flowering stems produce small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in dense spikes.

This species is noted for its adaptability and can thrive in a variety of soil types as long as there is adequate moisture.
