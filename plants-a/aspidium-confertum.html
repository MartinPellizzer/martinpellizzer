Aspidium confertum

Aspidium confertum, commonly known as the dense shield fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its dense, overlapping fronds that form a shield-like structure, providing protection to the fertile spore-bearing structures. Native to tropical and subtropical regions, this plant thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine in some cultures.

The plant's morphology is adapted for efficient spore dispersal and survival in its natural habitat.
