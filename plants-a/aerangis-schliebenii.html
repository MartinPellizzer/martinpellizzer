Aerangis schliebenii

Aerangis schliebenii, commonly known as the Schlieben's orchid, is a rare and striking orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its elegant, pendulous flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant has a compact growth habit with thick, fleshy roots adapted to store moisture in its natural habitat. Its leaves are broad and strap-like, often arranged in a rosette pattern to maximize light absorption.

This orchid thrives in humid, shaded environments and is highly valued for its ornamental beauty in horticultural collections.
