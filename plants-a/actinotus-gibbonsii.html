Actinotus gibbonsii

Actinotus gibbonsii, commonly known as the rock lily, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is a member of the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue or purple flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in rocky or coastal areas, thriving in well-drained soils. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often covered in a fine, silvery hair.
