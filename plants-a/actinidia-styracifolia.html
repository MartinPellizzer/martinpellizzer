Actinidia styracifolia

Actinidia styracifolia, commonly known as the Chinese gooseberry, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Actinidiaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant produces edible berries that are rich in vitamin C and are widely cultivated for their nutritional value.

It thrives in temperate climates and is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and fruit-bearing potential.
