Athyrium musashiense

Athyrium musashiense, commonly known as the Musashino fern, is a species of polypody fern native to Japan.

It is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped fronds that are typically light green with a slightly hairy texture. The plant grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in forest ecosystems.
