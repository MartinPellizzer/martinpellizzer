Astroloba egregia

Astroloba egregia, commonly known as the star-shaped bulbous plant, is a species native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its unique, star-like inflorescence composed of numerous small, tubular flowers. The plant features a bulbous base that stores water, allowing it to survive in arid conditions. Its leaves are typically narrow and fleshy, adapted for reducing water loss.

Astroloba egregia is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is well-suited to rock gardens and xeriscaping.
