Aloe vacillans

Aloe vacillans, commonly known as the wavering aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, pointed leaves that are typically green with a reddish tinge. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow-orange flowers during the summer months. It is adapted to arid environments and requires well-draining soil to thrive.

Aloe vacillans is often cultivated for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
