Anagallis orientalis

Anagallis orientalis, commonly known as the oriental pink, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing annual herb with slender stems and small, fleshy leaves. The plant produces clusters of delicate, five-petaled flowers that range in color from pink to white. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Anagallis orientalis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its charming blooms.
