Arisaema stenospathum

Arisaema stenospathum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf arisaema, is a herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its unique inflorescence consisting of a spadix surrounded by a spathe. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. Its leaves are elongated and narrow, often with a distinct midrib, and its flowers are small and clustered.

Arisaema stenospathum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its striking foliage.
