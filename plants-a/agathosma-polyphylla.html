Agathosma polyphylla

Agathosma polyphylla, commonly known as the Many-leaved Cape Geranium, is a perennial shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its aromatic, glandular leaves and small, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to purple. The plant thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions. It is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal medicine.

Agathosma polyphylla is also an important plant in the fynbos biome, contributing to local biodiversity.
