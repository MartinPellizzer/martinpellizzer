Agathosma juniperifolia

Agathosma juniperifolia, commonly known as the Cape rosemary, is a small evergreen shrub native to the Western Cape region of South Africa.

It is characterized by its aromatic, needle-like leaves that are typically gray-green in color. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring, followed by small, fleshy berries. It is well adapted to dry, sandy soils and is often found in fynbos vegetation.

Agathosma juniperifolia is valued for its essential oils and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
