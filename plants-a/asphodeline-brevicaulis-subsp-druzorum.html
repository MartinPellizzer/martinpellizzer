Asphodeline brevicaulis subsp. druzorum

Asphodeline brevicaulis subsp.

druzorum, commonly known as the Druze asphodel, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region. It is a perennial herb characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and tall, erect flowering stems.

The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in a spike-like inflorescence. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive and ornamental addition to Mediterranean-style gardens.
