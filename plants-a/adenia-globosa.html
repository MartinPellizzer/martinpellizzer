Adenia globosa

Adenia globosa, commonly known as the globular adenium, is a striking succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its swollen, bottle-shaped stem that stores water, allowing it to survive in arid environments. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the warmer months, adding a dramatic visual element to its appearance. Its leaves are thick and fleshy, adapted to reduce water loss through transpiration.

Overall, Adenia globosa is a unique and resilient plant with distinctive botanical features.
