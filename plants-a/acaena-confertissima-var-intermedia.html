Acaena confertissima var. intermedia

Acaena confertissima var. intermedia, commonly known as the New Zealand thistle, is a perennial shrub native to New Zealand.

It features dense clusters of spiny, woody stems covered in soft, woolly hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often have a slightly serrated edge.

It is adapted to grow in a variety of soil types and is commonly found in coastal and subalpine regions.
