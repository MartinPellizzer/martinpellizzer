Anthurium jamesonii

Anthurium jamesonii, commonly known as the Jameson's anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are often bright green with a waxy texture. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-like inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, typically in shades of red, orange, or yellow. It thrives in humid, warm environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium jamesonii is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
