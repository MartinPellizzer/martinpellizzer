Alguelagum speciosum

Alguelagum speciosum, commonly known as the golden flame cactus, is a striking succulent native to the arid regions of northern Africa.

It features a tall, cylindrical stem covered in vibrant yellow spines that give it its distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of bright orange flowers during the warm season, adding to its ornamental value. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it a popular choice for xeriscaping.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
