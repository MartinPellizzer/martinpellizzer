Ajania leontopodioides

Ajania leontopodioides, commonly known as the mountain everlasting, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The flowers, which bloom in spring, are usually pale blue or white, adding a delicate beauty to its natural habitat.
