Aethionema erinaceum

Aethionema erinaceum, commonly known as the hairy rockcress, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically forms dense mats of foliage. The plant features small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow, blooming in early spring. Its leaves are usually covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive textured appearance.

Aethionema erinaceum thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in dry, sunny environments.
