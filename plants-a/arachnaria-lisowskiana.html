Arachnaria lisowskiana

Arachnaria lisowskiana, commonly known as the Lisowskian spider plant, is a rare and unique species native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its intricate, spider-like inflorescence, which gives it its common name. The plant features small, delicate flowers arranged in clusters, often with a pale green or white hue. Its leaves are typically narrow and grass-like, contributing to its overall appearance.

Arachnaria lisowskiana is adapted to cold, arid environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
