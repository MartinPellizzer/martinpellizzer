Aloe imalotensis var. imalotensis

Aloe imalotensis var. imalotensis, commonly known as the Imalotensis Aloe, is a succulent plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a bluish tint, arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing yellow to orange flowers, which attract pollinators such as bees and birds. It is well adapted to hot, dry climates and requires minimal water once established.

The species is valued for its ornamental beauty and has some traditional medicinal uses in its native habitat.
