Asteranthe asterias

Asteranthe asterias, commonly known as the starflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small stars. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces showy yellow to orange flowers with a distinctive star-shaped pattern. Asteranthe asterias thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming period, typically from late spring to early autumn.
