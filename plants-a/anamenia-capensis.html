Anamenia capensis

Anamenia capensis, commonly known as the Cape anemone, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces showy yellow to orange flower heads that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Anamenia capensis is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
