Avena sativa convar. patula

Avena sativa convar. patula, commonly known as slender oat, is a variant of the common oat characterized by its slender, elongated stems and reduced leaf size.

It is a grass species native to temperate regions and is often found in disturbed soils or as a weed in agricultural fields. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences with numerous florets, which are typically wind-pollinated. Its seeds are enclosed in a hull and are used in various food and industrial applications.

The botanical features of this variant make it adaptable to a range of environmental conditions.
