Alsophila cubensis

Alsophila cubensis, commonly known as the Cuban tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to Cuba.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds. The plant has a distinctive crown of compound leaves that can reach several meters in height. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Its slow growth rate and unique appearance make it a striking ornamental plant.
