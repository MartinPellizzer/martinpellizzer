Actinodaphne montana

Actinodaphne montana, commonly known as the mountain actinodaphne, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in regions of Myanmar, Thailand, and Vietnam. Its fruit is a drupe, typically containing a single seed, and it thrives in humid, tropical climates.

Due to its ornamental value and potential medicinal properties, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation programs.
