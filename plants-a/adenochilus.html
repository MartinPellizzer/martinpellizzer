Adenochilus

Adenochilus, commonly known as the star orchid, is a genus of flowering plants belonging to the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

These plants are characterized by their distinctive, often star-shaped flowers that are typically brightly colored. Adenochilus species are native to various regions in Southeast Asia, including the Philippines, Indonesia, and New Guinea. They thrive in humid, shaded environments and are often found in tropical rainforests.
