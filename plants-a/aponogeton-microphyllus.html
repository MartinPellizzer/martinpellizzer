Aponogeton microphyllus

Aponogeton microphyllus, commonly known as the Chinese water fern, is a tropical aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its finely divided, feather-like leaves that float on the water's surface. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that bloom above the water, followed by spore-bearing structures. It thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments with ample sunlight.

Its unique morphology and ornamental value make it a popular choice for water gardens and pond ecosystems.
