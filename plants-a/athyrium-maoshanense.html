Athyrium maoshanense

Athyrium maoshanense, commonly known as the Maoshan fern, is a species of fern native to China.

It belongs to the family Athyriaceae and is characterized by its delicate, feather-like fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its spores are produced on the underside of its fronds, which are arranged in a bipinnate pattern.

Athyrium maoshanense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
