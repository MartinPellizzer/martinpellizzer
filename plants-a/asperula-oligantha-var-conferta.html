Asperula oligantha var. conferta

Asperula oligantha var. conferta, commonly known as the dense asperula, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the western United States.

It features small, dark green leaves that are densely arranged along the stems. The plant produces clusters of white, bell-shaped flowers in the spring, which are fragrant and attractive to pollinators. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a ground cover.

Its hardy nature and ornamental value make it a popular choice in horticulture.
