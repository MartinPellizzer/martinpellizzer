Anemone yulongshanica var. glabrescens

Anemone yulongshanica var. glabrescens, commonly known as the Yulongshan anemone, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to China.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its showy, palmate flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as mountain forests and rocky slopes. Its leaves are palmately divided with hairy surfaces that become glabrous as the plant matures.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
