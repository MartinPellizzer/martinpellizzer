Aponogeton distachyos var. lagrangei

Aponogeton distachyos var. lagrangei, commonly known as the Lagrange's arrowhead, is a species of aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features long, narrow leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom above the water surface, adding ornamental value. It thrives in shallow, still waters with moderate light and nutrient levels.

Its rhizomes are fibrous and help anchor it in the substrate, making it a popular choice for pond and aquarium settings.
