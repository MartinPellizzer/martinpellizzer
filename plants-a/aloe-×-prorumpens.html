Aloe × prorumpens

Aloe × prorumpens, commonly known as the hybrid aloe or the hybrid aloe plant, is a succulent species native to South Africa.

It is a naturally occurring hybrid between Aloe ferox and Aloe arborescens, characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are thick and fleshy. The plant produces bright yellow flowers in clusters during the summer months, which attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water, making it a popular choice for xeriscaping and indoor gardening.
