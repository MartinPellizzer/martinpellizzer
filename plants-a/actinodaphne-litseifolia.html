Actinodaphne litseifolia

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical in shape and have a smooth texture. It produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically greenish-white in color.
