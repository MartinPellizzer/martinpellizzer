Avena splendens

Avena splendens, commonly known as the shining oat, is a herbaceous plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and delicate, feathery leaves. The plant produces small, white to pale blue flowers that bloom in summer. Avena splendens is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.

It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.
