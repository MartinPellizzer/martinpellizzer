Achillea × morisiana

Achillea × morisiana, commonly known as the hybrid yarrow, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Achillea millefolium (common yarrow) and Achillea ptarmica (sneezeweed). The plant features finely divided, feathery leaves and produces clusters of small, fluffy yellow to white flower heads. It is often found in disturbed soils and open habitats, thriving in full sun.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice in gardens and landscapes.
