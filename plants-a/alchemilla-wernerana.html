Alchemilla wernerana

Alchemilla wernerana, commonly known as the yellow alkanet, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its basal rosette of heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers in early summer, which attract pollinators such as bees. Its foliage is often used in traditional herbal medicine for its purported healing properties.

Alchemilla wernerana thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas.
