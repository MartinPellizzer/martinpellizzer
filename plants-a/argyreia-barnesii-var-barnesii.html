Argyreia barnesii var. barnesii

Argyreia barnesii var. barnesii, commonly known as the rainbow vine, is a climbing plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful flowers that range from violet to deep red, often with a metallic sheen. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and a strong, trailing growth habit. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
