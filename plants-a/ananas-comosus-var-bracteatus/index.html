Ananas Comosus Var. Bracteatus

Ananas comosus var. bracteatus is a unique and lesser-known variant of the pineapple plant. It is characterized by its distinctive bracteate structure, which gives it a more ornamental appearance than the typical pineapple. This variety is often grown for its aesthetic value rather than its fruit. While it shares some genetic traits with the common pineapple, it lacks the typical fruiting process. Its cultivation is gaining interest among horticulturists and plant enthusiasts alike.
