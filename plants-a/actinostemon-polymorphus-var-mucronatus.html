Actinostemon polymorphus var. mucronatus

Actinostemon polymorphus var. mucronatus, commonly known as the narrow-leaved actinostemon, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features upright, branched stems covered in soft, hairy foliage. The leaves are narrow and linear, often with a reddish tinge, and the plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers in terminal clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

This variant is noted for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
