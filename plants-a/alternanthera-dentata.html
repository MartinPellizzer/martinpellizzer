Alternanthera dentata

Alternanthera dentata, commonly known as the toothed Alternanthera, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage that often displays shades of red, pink, and purple. The plant features small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pink in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sun.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for water gardens and pond margins.
