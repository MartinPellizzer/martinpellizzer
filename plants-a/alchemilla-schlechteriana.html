Alchemilla schlechteriana

Alchemilla schlechteriana, commonly known as the rock alchemilla, is a perennial plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit. The plant features basal leaves that are typically gray-green in color with a fuzzy texture. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers in spring, which are clustered in dense, rounded inflorescences.

Alchemilla schlechteriana is adapted to rocky and arid environments, making it a hardy and drought-tolerant species.
