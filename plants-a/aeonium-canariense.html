Aeonium canariense

Aeonium canariense, commonly known as the "Tree Aeonium," is a succulent plant native to the Canary Islands.

It features a thick, upright stem covered in fleshy, waxy leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces striking clusters of yellow flowers during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Aeonium canariense is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its unique appearance and resilience.
