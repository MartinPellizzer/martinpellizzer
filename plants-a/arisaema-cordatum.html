Arisaema cordatum

Arisaema cordatum, commonly known as the heartleaf arisaema, is a herbaceous plant native to eastern North America.

It features a distinctive, heart-shaped leaf that is often deeply lobed, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces a spadix surrounded by a modified leaf called a spathe, which is typically green or purple in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodlands and stream banks.

Arisaema cordatum is valued for its ornamental foliage and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique aesthetic.
