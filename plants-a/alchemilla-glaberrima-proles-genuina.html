Alchemilla glaberrima proles genuina

Alchemilla glaberrima proles genuina, commonly known as the smooth lady's mantle, is a perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its basal rosette of heart-shaped, smooth, and silver-green leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in panicles during the summer months. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas or along stream banks.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and traditional herbal medicine.
