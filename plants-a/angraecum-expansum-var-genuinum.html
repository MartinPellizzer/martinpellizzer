Angraecum expansum var. genuinum

Angraecum expansum var. genuinum, commonly known as the Madagascar star orchid, is a rare and striking epiphytic orchid native to Madagascar.

It features long, arching inflorescences with numerous white, star-shaped flowers that emit a sweet fragrance, especially at night. The plant thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light and well-draining potting mix. Its unique morphology and nocturnal blooming habit make it a prized specimen among orchid enthusiasts.

Due to its habitat specificity and conservation status, it is often cultivated in specialized greenhouses.
