Anisophyllea fruticulosa

Anisophyllea fruticulosa, commonly known as the tropical shrub, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive leaf morphology, with leaves that are typically opposite and have a leathery texture. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release tiny seeds.

Anisophyllea fruticulosa is often found in moist, shaded environments within tropical forests.
