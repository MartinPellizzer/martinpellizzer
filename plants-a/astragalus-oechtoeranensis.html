Astragalus oechtoeranensis

Astragalus oechtoeranensis, commonly known as the Oechtoeran milkvetch, is a flowering plant species native to specific regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its small, pea-like flowers and compound leaves. The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments, thriving in well-drained soils.

Astragalus oechtoeranensis is known for its medicinal properties, often used in traditional herbal remedies for its purported health benefits.
