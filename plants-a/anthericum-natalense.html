Anthericum natalense

Anthericum natalense, commonly known as Natal grass or Natal buttercup, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features a basal rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, yellowish-green flowers in spike-like clusters. The plant is characterized by its fibrous root system and is often found in moist, well-drained soils. It is adapted to thrive in coastal and sandy environments.

Anthericum natalense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
