Ampliglossum batemanianum

Ampliglossum batemanianum, commonly known as the Bateman's ampliglossum, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features showy, yellow-orange ray florets surrounding a central disk of tubular flowers. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs.

Ampliglossum batemanianum is often found in moist, open habitats such as grasslands and stream banks.
