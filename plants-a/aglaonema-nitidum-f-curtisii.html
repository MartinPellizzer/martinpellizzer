Aglaonema nitidum f. curtisii

Aglaonema nitidum f. curtisii, commonly known as the Curtis's shining aglaonema, is a varietal form of the aglaonema plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated foliage featuring bright white and green patterns on its dark green leaves. The plant is evergreen and thrives in low-light conditions, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening. Its leaves are thick and waxy, which helps it retain moisture and resist pests.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and air-purifying qualities.
