Aster macounii

Aster macounii, commonly known as the Macoun's aster, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads and medicinal properties. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and woodlands. It produces showy blue to purple flowers that bloom in the summer months.

Its leaves are generally lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
