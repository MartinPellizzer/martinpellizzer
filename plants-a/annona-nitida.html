Annona nitida

Annona nitida, commonly known as the white annona, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is a small, evergreen tree or shrub that typically grows to a height of about 5 meters. The plant produces large, heart-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green in color. Its flowers are white and fragrant, often appearing in clusters, and it bears edible, yellow or green fruits that are similar in appearance to a pear.

The plant is valued for its ornamental beauty and is also used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
