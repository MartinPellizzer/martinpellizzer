Aegiphila venezuelensis var. venezuelensis

Aegiphila venezuelensis var. venezuelensis, commonly known as the Venezuelan aegiphila, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Venezuela.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its small, fragrant flowers and hairy, lance-shaped leaves. It typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid environments.
