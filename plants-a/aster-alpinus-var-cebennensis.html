Aster alpinus var. cebennensis

Aster alpinus var. cebennensis, commonly known as the Alpine aster, is a perennial herb native to the alpine regions of Europe.

It features a basal rosette of narrow, silver-green leaves and produces striking blue to violet flowers in the summer. The plant is adapted to cold, rocky environments and thrives in full sun. It is often found in mountain meadows and alpine tundra.

Its flowers attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies, contributing to the ecological diversity of its habitat.
