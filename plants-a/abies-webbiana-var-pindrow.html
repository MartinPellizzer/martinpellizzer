Abies webbiana var. pindrow

Abies webbiana var. pindrow, commonly known as the Pindrow fir, is a rare and endemic conifer native to the western Himalayas.

It is characterized by its slender, pyramidal shape and dark green, needle-like leaves. The cones are typically pendulous and can reach lengths of up to 15 centimeters. This species thrives in cool, moist environments at high altitudes.

Its limited distribution and unique morphology make it a subject of interest in botanical studies.
