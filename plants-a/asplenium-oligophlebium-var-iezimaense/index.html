Asplenium Oligophlebium Var. Iezimaense

Asplenium oligophlebium var. iezimaense is a rare fern species native to specific regions of Southeast Asia. It belongs to the family Aspleniaceae and is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped fronds. This variant is distinguished by its unique leaf structure and subtle coloration. Due to habitat loss and limited distribution, it is considered endangered. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this rare botanical treasure.
