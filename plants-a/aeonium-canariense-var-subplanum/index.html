Aeonium Canariense Var. Subplanum

Coral aeonium, scientifically known as Aeonium canariense var. subplanum, is a striking succulent native to the Canary Islands. With its compact rosettes and vibrant blue-green foliage, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts. This plant thrives in well-draining soil and full sun, making it ideal for rock gardens or container planting. Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements have made it a favorite in modern horticulture. Despite its beauty, it requires careful attention to avoid overwatering and root rot.
