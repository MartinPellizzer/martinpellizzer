Agrostis muelleriana

Agrostis muelleriana, commonly known as Mueller's bentgrass, is a species of grass native to eastern Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial plant that typically forms dense, mat-like growth. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows and along stream banks.

Botanically, it is characterized by its fine texture and ability to form dense vegetation, making it a common component of wetland ecosystems.
