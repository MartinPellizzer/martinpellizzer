Arum orientale subsp. amoenum

Arum orientale subsp.

amoenum, commonly known as the Chinese arrowroot or arrowhead plant, is a herbaceous perennial native to southern China and Southeast Asia. It features striking, arrow-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a metallic sheen.

The plant produces clusters of spadices, which are inflorescences that resemble a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The plant is also valued for its edible tubers, which are used in traditional Chinese cuisine.
