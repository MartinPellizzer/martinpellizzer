Acianthera tikalensis

Acianthera tikalensis, commonly known as the Tikal orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces showy, fragrant flowers with a distinctive coloration. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are broad and dark green, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Acianthera tikalensis is valued for its beauty and is sometimes cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
