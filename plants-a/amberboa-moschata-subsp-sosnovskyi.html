Amberboa moschata subsp. sosnovskyi

Amberboa moschata subsp.

sosnovskyi, commonly known as the Sosnovsky's amberboa, is a subspecies native to Central Asia. It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its aromatic, glandular leaves and stems.

The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in early summer. Its essential oils, derived from the leaves and stems, are valued for their musky and spicy fragrance.

This subspecies is often cultivated for its ornamental and aromatic qualities in horticulture.
