Adesmia laxa

Adesmia laxa, commonly known as the slender burr legume, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its small, compound leaves and clusters of pea-like flowers that range in color from white to purple. The plant produces small, woody pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in dry, arid environments and is often found in coastal regions.

Adesmia laxa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage and floral display.
