Antilla

Antilla, commonly known as the Mexican flame plant, is a tropical shrub native to Central America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red and orange flowers, which bloom in clusters during the warmer months. The plant has elliptical, dark green leaves that are opposite in arrangement. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Antilla is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
