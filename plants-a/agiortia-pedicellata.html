Agiortia pedicellata

Agiortia pedicellata, commonly known as the Christmas star, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive star-shaped flowers that bloom in winter, typically from June to August. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Agiortia pedicellata is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
