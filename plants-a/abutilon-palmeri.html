Abutilon palmeri

Abutilon palmeri, commonly known as Palmer's abutilon, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in arid and semi-arid environments. The plant has palmately lobed leaves and produces showy, lantern-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to red. It is adapted to drought conditions and thrives in well-drained soils.

The species is often found in rocky habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance.
