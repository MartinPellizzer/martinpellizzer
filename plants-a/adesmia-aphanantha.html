Adesmia aphanantha

Adesmia aphanantha, commonly known as the slender leaf burr legume, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, delicate leaves and distinctive, clustered pods that contain several seeds. Native to tropical regions of South America, it thrives in moist, shaded environments. The plant has a compact growth habit and is often found in undergrowth or along stream banks.
