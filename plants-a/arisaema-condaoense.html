Arisaema condaoense

Arisaema condaoense, commonly known as the Condao arrowhead, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its unique inflorescence composed of a spadix and a spathe. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest floors or stream banks. Its leaves are arrowhead-shaped, with a prominent midrib, and its flowers are usually green or white.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appearance and has some traditional uses in local cultures.
