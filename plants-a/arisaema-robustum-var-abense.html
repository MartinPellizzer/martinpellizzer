Arisaema robustum var. abense

Arisaema robustum var. abense, commonly known as the Giant Arrowhead, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in diameter, giving it a dramatic appearance. The plant produces clusters of spadices, which are spadix-like structures surrounded by a hooded spathe, typical of the Araceae family. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for tropical gardens and greenhouse cultivation.
