Arabis stelleri var. japonica

Arabis stelleri var. japonica, commonly known as Japanese rockcress, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically forms dense mats of foliage. The plant features basal leaves that are oval to lance-shaped with a waxy coating. It produces clusters of small, white, four-petaled flowers in early spring.

This species is well adapted to rocky and coastal environments, thriving in full sun and well-drained soil.
