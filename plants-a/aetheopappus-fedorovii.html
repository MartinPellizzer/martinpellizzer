Aetheopappus fedorovii

Aetheopappus fedorovii, commonly known as the Fedorov's goldenweed, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in arid and semi-arid regions, showcasing adaptations to harsh environmental conditions. Its yellow flowers are arranged in dense clusters, and it has hairy leaves that help reduce water loss.

It is often found in rocky soils and is valued for its ornamental and potential medicinal uses.
