Aloe viguieri

Aloe viguieri, commonly known as the Viguier's aloe, is a succulent plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern, storing water to help it survive in dry conditions. The leaves are often bluish-green in color and have a waxy coating to reduce water loss. It produces yellow flowers in summer, which are borne on tall, branched stems.

This plant is valued for its ornamental beauty and is also used in traditional medicine for its soothing properties.
