Arrabidaea salzmannii

Arrabidaea salzmannii, commonly known as the Cape fennel, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It features aromatic, lance-shaped leaves and produces yellow flowers in clusters. The plant is characterized by its strong scent and is often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun.

Its botanical significance lies in its unique chemical composition and ecological role in its native habitat.
