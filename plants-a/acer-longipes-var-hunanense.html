Acer longipes var. hunanense

Acer longipes var. hunanense, commonly known as the Hunan winged maple, is a deciduous tree native to southern China.

It is characterized by its distinctive winged seeds, which are typical of the Acer genus. The tree produces palmate leaves that are usually 5-7 lobes, with a serrated margin. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

The species is valued for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
