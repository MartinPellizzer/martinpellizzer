Astracantha multifoliolata

Astracantha multifoliolata, commonly known as the many-leaved thornveld, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its dense, thorny stems and opposite, lobed leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the warmer months. Its botanical features make it well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.

It is often found in rocky soils and is valued for its ornamental and medicinal properties.
