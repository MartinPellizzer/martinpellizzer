Antirrhinum graniticum subsp. onubensis

Antirrhinum graniticum subsp.

onubensis, commonly known as the Granitic Snapdragon, is a flowering plant native to southern Spain. It is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that typically range in color from red to orange.

The plant thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid environments. Its leaves are narrow and linear, adapted to reduce water loss in its native habitat.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting pollinators.
