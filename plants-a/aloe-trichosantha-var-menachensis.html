Aloe trichosantha var. menachensis

Aloe trichosantha var. menachensis, commonly known as the Menachensis Aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, with a waxy coating to reduce water loss. The plant produces striking orange to red flowers on tall, erect stems, which bloom during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Its leaves contain a gel-like substance that has been used for various medicinal and cosmetic purposes.
