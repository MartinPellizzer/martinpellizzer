Alocasia × splendens

Alocasia × splendens, commonly known as the African Mask Alocasia, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Araceae family.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often adorned with striking patterns and a deep green color. The plant originates from a cross between Alocasia macrorrhiza and Alocasia odora, resulting in its unique foliage. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is popular as a houseplant and in tropical gardens.
