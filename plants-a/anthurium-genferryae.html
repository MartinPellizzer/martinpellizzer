Anthurium genferryae

Anthurium genferryae, commonly known as the Genferry's arrowhead, is a tropical flowering plant native to the rainforests of Colombia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves and vibrant, spadix-like inflorescence. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its foliage is often a deep green with a glossy texture, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers surrounded by a colorful spathe.

Anthurium genferryae is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in botanical gardens.
