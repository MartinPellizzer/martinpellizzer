Adenostyles albida subsp. kerneri

Adenostyles albida subsp.

kerneri, commonly known as Kern's whitehead, is a flowering plant native to the western United States. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in late summer.

The plant typically grows in open, rocky habitats, such as slopes and grasslands. It has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, clustered inflorescences.

This subspecies is often found in California and is adapted to dry, sunny environments.
