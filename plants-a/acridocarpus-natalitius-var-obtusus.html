Acridocarpus natalitius var. obtusus

Acridocarpus natalitius var. obtusus, commonly known as Natal flame tree, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, bright red flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. The plant has compound leaves that are pinnate and often have a leathery texture. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it suitable for cultivation in warm climates.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens and landscapes.
