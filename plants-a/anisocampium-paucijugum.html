Anisocampium paucijugum

Anisocampium paucijugum, commonly known as the few-lobed campion, is a flowering plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is characterized by its erect, branching stems and opposite, ovate to lanceolate leaves. The flowers are typically pink or white, arranged in clusters at the ends of the stems. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds.

It is often found in dry, open habitats such as meadows and rocky soils.
