Anthurium nitidum var. obtegens

Anthurium nitidum var. obtegens, commonly known as the velvet anthurium, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its glossy, velvety leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces spadices, which are modified leaves that enclose the true flowers, often in shades of red or orange. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Anthurium nitidum var. obtegens is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and relatively low maintenance requirements.
