Anemarrhena cavaleriei

Anemarrhena cavaleriei, commonly known as the Chinese sandwort, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the family Liliaceae and is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit. The plant features slender, grass-like leaves and produces small, greenish-white flowers in clusters. It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often found in mountainous regions.
