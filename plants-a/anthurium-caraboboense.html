Anthurium caraboboense

Anthurium caraboboense, commonly known as the Carabobo anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Venezuela.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves and vibrant spadix. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often found in the understory of tropical forests. Its flowers are concealed within a spathe, a leaf-like structure that surrounds the spadix.

Anthurium caraboboense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated for its unique foliage and foliage coloration.
