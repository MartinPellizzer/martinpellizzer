Aconitum volubile var. oligotrichum

Aconitum volubile var. oligotrichum, commonly known as the slender monkshood, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Ranunculaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are deeply lobed and have a distinctive, somewhat poisonous appearance.

This variety is noted for its shorter, more compact growth habit compared to other Aconitum species.
