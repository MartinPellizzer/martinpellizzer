Aspalathus juniperina subsp. gracilifolia

Aspalathus juniperina subsp.

gracilifolia, commonly known as the slender-leaved bushveld tea, is a shrub native to South Africa. It features small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically gray-green in color and covered in fine hairs.

The plant produces yellow flowers in clusters, which are followed by small, bean-like pods. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to semi-arid environments.

Due to its unique characteristics, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists alike.
