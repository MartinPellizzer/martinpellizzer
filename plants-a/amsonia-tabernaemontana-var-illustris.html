Amsonia tabernaemontana var. illustris

Amsonia tabernaemontana var. illustris, commonly known as the Bluestem Blazingstar, is a striking perennial plant native to the southeastern United States.

It features showy, cobalt-blue flower clusters that appear in late summer, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies. The plant has a distinctive bluish-green foliage that turns coppery in the fall, adding seasonal interest. Its root system is fibrous and well-suited for stabilizing soil in moist to wet conditions.

It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is often found in prairies and woodland edges.
