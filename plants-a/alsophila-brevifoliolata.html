Alsophila brevifoliolata

Alsophila brevifoliolata, commonly known as the short-leaved tree fern, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and a crown of feathery, compound fronds. The plant has a slow growth rate and can live for several decades, making it a long-lived species. Its fronds are typically dark green and have a distinctive, narrow shape.

Alsophila brevifoliolata is often found in humid, shaded environments such as forests and along stream banks.
