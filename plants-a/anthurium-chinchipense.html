Anthurium chinchipense

Anthurium chinchipense, commonly known as the velvet leaf anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a velvety texture. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-like inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, often in shades of red, orange, or yellow. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Anthurium chinchipense is popular as a decorative plant due to its unique foliage and ornamental flowers.
