Alocasia inornata

Alocasia inornata, commonly known as the white-veined arrow leaf, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves with striking white veins against a deep green background. The plant belongs to the Araceae family, which includes other ornamental plants like peace lilies and calla lilies. Alocasia inornata typically grows as an understory plant in humid, shaded environments.

Its foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its aesthetic appeal and air-purifying qualities.
