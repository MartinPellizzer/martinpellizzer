Alocasia nobilis

Alocasia nobilis, commonly known as the Chinese Evergreen, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 3 feet in length. The plant is a member of the Araceae family, which includes other ornamental plants like peace lilies and calla lilies. Its leaves are typically a deep green with a glossy texture, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are surrounded by a spadix.

Alocasia nobilis is popular as a houseplant due to its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and its striking foliage.
