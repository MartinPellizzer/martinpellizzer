Anthurium austin-smithii

Anthurium austin-smithii, commonly known as the Austin-Smith anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-shaped inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, often in shades of red, orange, or yellow. It thrives in humid, warm environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium austin-smithii is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
