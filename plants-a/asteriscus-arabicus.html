Asteriscus arabicus

Asteriscus arabicus, commonly known as the Arabian aster, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of the Middle East and North Africa.

It is a low-growing shrub with small, clustered blue or purple flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant has silver-gray leaves that help reduce water loss in its harsh environment. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it well adapted to desert conditions.

Asteriscus arabicus is often used in xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and ornamental value.
