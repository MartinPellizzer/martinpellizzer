Allium schoenoprasum var. albidum

Allium schoenoprasum var. albidum, commonly known as white chives, is a variant of the common chive plant.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. The plant produces slender, hollow stems that are typically white or pale blue in color. Its leaves are grass-like and have a mild, onion-like aroma.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and mild flavor in culinary applications.
