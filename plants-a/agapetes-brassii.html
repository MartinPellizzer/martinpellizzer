Agapetes brassii

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and blueberries, and is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit. The plant features small, elliptical leaves that are typically covered in a fine layer of silvery-white hairs, giving it its distinctive appearance. Agapetes brassii is often found in moist, shaded habitats at high altitudes, where it thrives in cool and humid conditions.
