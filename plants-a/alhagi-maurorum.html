Alhagi maurorum

Alhagi maurorum, commonly known as the desert bean or goat's bean, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of the Middle East, North Africa, and parts of Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 3 meters tall, characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellowish-white flowers. The plant produces pods that contain edible seeds, which are often used in traditional cuisines.

Alhagi maurorum is adapted to survive in harsh environments, with deep roots and a capacity to fix nitrogen, making it an important plant in desert ecosystems.
