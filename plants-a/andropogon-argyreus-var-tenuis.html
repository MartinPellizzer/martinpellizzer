Andropogon argyreus var. tenuis

Andropogon argyreus var. tenuis, commonly known as silver bluestem, is a grass species native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and narrow, upright growth habit. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, white to pale blue flowers. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, often found in open woodlands and prairies.

Its ornamental value and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for native plant gardens.
