Abutilon planiflorum

Abutilon planiflorum, commonly known as the velvetleaf abutilon, is a shrub native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its soft, fuzzy leaves and clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant has a hairy stem and heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine, velvety hairs. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in disturbed soils.

Abutilon planiflorum is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
