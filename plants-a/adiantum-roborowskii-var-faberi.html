Adiantum roborowskii var. faberi

Adiantum roborowskii var. faberi, commonly known as the Chinese maidenhair fern, is a delicate and elegant fern species native to China.

It features finely divided, feathery fronds that are typically dark green and often have a silvery underside. The plant thrives in shady, moist environments and prefers well-draining, acidic soil. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.

Its unique morphology and graceful appearance make it a popular choice among plant enthusiasts.
