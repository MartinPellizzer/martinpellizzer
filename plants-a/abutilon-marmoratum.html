Abutilon marmoratum

Abutilon marmoratum, commonly known as the marble abutilon, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves that display a marbled pattern of green and silver. The plant produces clusters of small, yellow flowers that bloom in the spring and summer. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking visual interest in shaded areas.
