Aspalathus pilantha

Aspalathus pilantha, commonly known as the silver leaf bush, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its small, needle-like leaves that give it a silvery appearance. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in spring, adding visual interest to its appearance. It is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to dry climates.

Aspalathus pilantha is valued for its ornamental qualities and ability to thrive in challenging environments.
