Alfonsia

Alfonsia, commonly known as the flame tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy red or orange flowers that resemble flames, which bloom in clusters. The plant has a straight, slender trunk and can grow up to 20 meters in height. Its leaves are compound, consisting of several oval-shaped leaflets.

Alfonsia is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
