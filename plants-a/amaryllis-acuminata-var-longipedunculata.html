Amaryllis acuminata var. longipedunculata

Amaryllis acuminata var. longipedunculata, commonly known as the long-pedunculate amaryllis, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, erect stems with large, showy flowers that bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant produces multiple flowers on long, slender peduncles, giving it a dramatic appearance. Its foliage consists of broad, strap-like leaves that are evergreen throughout the growing season.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
