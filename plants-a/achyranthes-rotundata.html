Achyranthes rotundata

Achyranthes rotundata, commonly known as roundleaf smartweed, is a herbaceous annual plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features upright, branched stems that are often hairy, and its leaves are ovate to round in shape with toothed margins. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or red, followed by elliptical, three-lobed seed pods. It thrives in moist, disturbed soils and is often found in wetlands, ditches, and along riverbanks.

Its botanical characteristics make it a common weed in agricultural and urban settings.
