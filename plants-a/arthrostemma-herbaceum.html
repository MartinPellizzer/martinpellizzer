Arthrostemma herbaceum

Arthrostemma herbaceum, commonly known as the slender arrowhead, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Pacific.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in wet or marshy habitats. The plant features heart-shaped leaves and produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters. It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in water gardens and wetland restoration projects.
