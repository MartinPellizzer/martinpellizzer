Acer nepalense

Acer nepalense, commonly known as the Nepal maple, is a deciduous tree native to the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its palmate leaves that turn vibrant shades of red and yellow in autumn. The tree produces small, yellowish-green flowers in early spring, followed by winged seeds. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in moist, well-drained soils.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in regions with suitable growing conditions.
