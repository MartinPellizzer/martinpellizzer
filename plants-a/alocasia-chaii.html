Alocasia chaii

Alocasia chaii, commonly known as the Chaii Elephant Ear, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length, featuring a deep green color with a distinctive veining pattern. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soil and requires high humidity and indirect sunlight to flourish. Its foliage makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening, though it is toxic if ingested.

Alocasia chaii is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal and air-purifying qualities.
