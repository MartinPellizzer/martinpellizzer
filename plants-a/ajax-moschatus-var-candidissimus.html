Ajax moschatus var. candidissimus

Ajax moschatus var. candidissimus, commonly known as the white-flowered musk lily, is a rare variant of the musk lily species.

It is characterized by its pale, almost white petals that contrast with its typical yellow or orange hues. This plant is native to specific regions in North America, particularly in the eastern United States. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodland floors.

The plant's botanical features include a distinctive musk-like scent and a tuberous root system.
