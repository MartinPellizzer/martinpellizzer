Aralia pseudoginseng

Aralia pseudoginseng, commonly known as Japanese angelica root, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Araliaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and small greenish-white flowers. The plant produces a fleshy, aromatic root that is often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for both ornamental and medicinal purposes.
