Asparagus homblei

Asparagus homblei, commonly known as the Hombli asparagus, is a member of the Asparagaceae family.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows in rocky or arid environments. The plant features slender, succulent stems and small, narrow leaves. Its flowers are usually white or pale green and are borne in clusters.

Asparagus homblei is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in challenging conditions.
