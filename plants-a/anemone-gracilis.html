Anemone gracilis

Anemone gracilis, commonly known as the slender anemone, is a herbaceous perennial native to the eastern United States.

It features delicate, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has finely divided, fern-like leaves that are usually basal and clustered at the base. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Anemone gracilis is valued for its ornamental beauty and ecological role in supporting pollinators.
