Asteranthera

Asteranthera, commonly known as the starflower, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that typically feature a central disc surrounded by ray florets. The plant is native to various regions of the Americas, particularly in the United States and Mexico. Asteranthera species are often found in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and woodland edges.

Botanically, they are known for their intricate inflorescence structure and their ability to produce numerous small, herbaceous flowers.
