Artabotrys aurantiacus

Artabotrys aurantiacus, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 10 meters tall. The plant is characterized by its clusters of bright yellow, pendulous flowers that resemble small showers, hence its common name. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and ovate, with a glossy green upper surface.

The plant produces small, spherical fruits that contain a single seed, and it thrives in warm, humid climates with well-drained soil.
