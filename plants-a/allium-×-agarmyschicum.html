Allium × agarmyschicum

Allium × agarmyschicum, commonly known as the giant garlic.

It is a hybrid plant belonging to the Amaryllidaceae family, characterized by its large, bulbous structure and strong garlic scent. The plant typically grows in temperate climates and is often cultivated for its culinary and medicinal uses. Its leaves are broad and grass-like, while its flowers form a dense cluster of white or pinkish blossoms.

This hybrid is valued for its robust growth and versatility in both garden and agricultural settings.
