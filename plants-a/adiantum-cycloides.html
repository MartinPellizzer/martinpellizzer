Adiantum cycloides

Adiantum cycloides, commonly known as the shield fern, is a species of fern native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its delicate, lace-like fronds that are typically light green with a slightly curled appearance. The plant features a creeping rhizome that produces upright, spore-bearing fronds. Its leaves are pinnately compound, with small, rounded pinnae that give it a unique, shield-like appearance.

Adiantum cycloides thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas.
