Avena barbata subsp. wiestii

Avena barbata subsp.

wiestii, commonly known as the Wiest's slender oat, is a perennial grass species native to the western United States. It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves.

The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences bearing numerous florets, typical of the Poaceae family. It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid environments.
