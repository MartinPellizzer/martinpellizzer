Antrophyum d'urvillaei

Antrophyum d'urvillaei, commonly known as the giant cycad, is a striking plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Cycadaceae family and is characterized by its large, feather-like leaves that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant has a thick, trunk-like stem that is often covered in a fibrous, spongy material. It produces small, cone-like structures that contain its seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
