Actinostachys inopinata

Actinostachys inopinata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf sword fern, is a species of fern native to the wet tropical forests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its slender, arching fronds that are typically less than 1 meter in length. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit, with dark brown, wiry rhizomes that help it spread through the forest understory. Its leaves are pinnately compound, with closely spaced, linear pinnae that give it a delicate, grass-like appearance.

This fern thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found growing along stream banks or in humid, low-light habitats.
