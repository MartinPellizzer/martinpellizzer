Abelia × grandiflora

Abelia × grandiflora, commonly known as the Chinese abelia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features showy, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink and red, blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has opposite, ovate to lance-shaped leaves that are medium green and turn reddish in the fall. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained, moist soil.

Abelia × grandiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various garden conditions.
