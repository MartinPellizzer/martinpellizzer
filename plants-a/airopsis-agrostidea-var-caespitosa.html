Airopsis agrostidea var. caespitosa

Airopsis agrostidea var. caespitosa, commonly known as the tufted sea lettuce, is a marine green alga that thrives in shallow coastal waters.

It forms dense, mat-like growths that are often found on rocky substrates. The plant has a filamentous structure with a branching pattern that allows it to spread across surfaces. It is characterized by its bright green color and ability to withstand tidal fluctuations.

This species plays an important role in coastal ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various marine organisms.
