Adeia discoidea

Adeia discoidea, commonly known as the spiral orchid, is a unique member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in regions like Borneo and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its striking spiral arrangement of leaves, which gives it its common name. Its flowers are small and white, often clustered in a loose inflorescence.

Adeia discoidea is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its distinctive foliage.
