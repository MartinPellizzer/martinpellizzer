Anthurium pariense

Anthurium pariense, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive spathe and spadix inflorescence. The plant features broad, heart-shaped leaves with a deep green color and a velvety texture. Its striking red spathe is often used in floral arrangements due to its vibrant color.

Anthurium pariense thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect sunlight for optimal growth.
