Aster erigeron

Aster erigeron, commonly known as the common fleabane, is a flowering plant in the sunflower family.

It is characterized by its daisy-like flowers, which typically feature white petals and a yellow central disk. Native to North America, it thrives in a variety of habitats, including meadows and disturbed soils. The plant produces small, fluffy seeds that are easily dispersed by the wind.

Its botanical features make it a common sight in wildflower gardens and natural landscapes.
