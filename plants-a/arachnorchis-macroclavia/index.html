Arachnorchis Macroclavia

The arachnorchis macroclavia is a rare and striking orchid native to the wet tropical forests of Australia. Known for its unique appearance, it features intricate patterns and vibrant colors that mimic the look of spider webs. This plant thrives in humid environments with rich, well-draining soil and partial shade. Its delicate flowers bloom in late winter or early spring, adding a touch of beauty to the undergrowth. Due to its limited habitat and threats from deforestation, conservation efforts are crucial to ensure its survival.
