Anthurium bueyense

Anthurium bueyense, commonly known as the Bueyense arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to the Andean regions of South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its unique inflorescence consisting of a spadix and a spathe. The plant features waxy, heart-shaped leaves that are typically green with prominent veins. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Anthurium bueyense is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated as a houseplant.
