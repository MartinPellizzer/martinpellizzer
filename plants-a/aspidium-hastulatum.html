Aspidium hastulatum

Aspidium hastulatum, commonly known as the sword fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Athyriaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, sword-shaped fronds that are typically 30 to 60 cm in length. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit, with a creeping stem that produces new shoots along its length. Its leaves are bipinnate, with small, oblong pinnae that are arranged alternately along the rachis.

Aspidium hastulatum is native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere and thrives in moist, shaded environments.
