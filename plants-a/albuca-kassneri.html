Albuca kassneri

Albuca kassneri, commonly known as the Zulu lily, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a bulbous perennial that produces striking white or pale yellow flowers during the summer months. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white wax. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Albuca kassneri is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
