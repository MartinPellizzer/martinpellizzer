Aberemoa hadrantha

Aberemoa hadrantha, commonly known as the golden flame tree, is a striking tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy yellow-orange flowers that bloom in clusters, creating a dramatic visual display. The plant has compound leaves with long, slender leaflets and produces small, woody pods that contain seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Its rapid growth and ability to tolerate various soil types make it a popular choice for landscaping in tropical regions.
