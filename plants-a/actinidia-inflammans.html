Actinidia inflammans

Actinidia inflammans, commonly known as the red-flowered actinidia, is a deciduous vine native to China.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and striking red flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant produces small, edible berries that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers moist, well-drained soil.

Actinidia inflammans is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional Chinese medicine.
