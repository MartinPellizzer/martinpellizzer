Agonis flexuosa var. flexuosa

Agonis flexuosa var. flexuosa, commonly known as the narrow-leafed mallee, is a shrub or small tree native to southern Australia.

It is characterized by its narrow, needle-like leaves and a multi-stemmed, gnarled appearance. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers in winter, which are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to dry, inland climates.

Agonis flexuosa var. flexuosa is often used in landscaping for its hardiness and ornamental value.
