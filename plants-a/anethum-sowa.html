Anethum sowa

Anethum sowa, commonly known as Sowa fenugreek, is a herbaceous plant native to the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its slender, branched stems and small, trifoliate leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in umbels, typical of the Apiaceae family. Its seeds are used in traditional medicine and as a spice, valued for their aromatic properties.

Anethum sowa thrives in cool, high-altitude environments and is often found in alpine meadows.
