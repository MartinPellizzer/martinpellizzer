Anthurium gregneversii

Anthurium gregneversii, commonly known as the Gregnevers' anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its unique inflorescence consisting of a spadix and a spathe. The plant produces striking, colorful spathe that can range from red to yellow, often with a contrasting green or white base. Its leaves are typically large, heart-shaped, and glossy, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Anthurium gregneversii thrives in humid, warm environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
