Abutilon nigricans

Abutilon nigricans, commonly known as the black velvet abutilon, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its hairy, dark green leaves and striking, showy flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small, flat seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Its unique appearance and adaptability make it a popular choice for gardens and botanical collections.
