Actinidia kolomikta var. shihmienensis

Actinidia kolomikta var. shihmienensis, commonly known as the Shihmien barberry, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its distinctive variegated leaves that display a mix of white, green, and pink hues, especially during the growing season. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers in early spring, followed by edible berries that ripen to a dark red or black color. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained, moist soil.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to a range of garden conditions.
