Astracantha granatensis

Astracantha granatensis, commonly known as the Granada thistle, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its spiny, upright stems and large, purple to violet flower heads. The plant produces small, achene-like seeds that are dispersed by wind. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or arid environments.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological role, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
