Armeria duriensis subsp. oretana

Armeria duriensis subsp.

oretana, commonly known as the Oretan thrift, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region. It features dense clusters of white or pink flowers that bloom in early spring.

The plant has basal leaves that are linear and often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and long-lasting flowers.
