Arenaria yunnanensis var. linearifolia

Arenaria yunnanensis var. linearifolia, commonly known as the Yunnan sandwort, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Yunnan, China.

It features small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters, typically during the spring and early summer. The plant has linear, grass-like leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white hairs, aiding in water retention. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is adapted to high-altitude environments.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in stabilizing soil in its native habitat.
