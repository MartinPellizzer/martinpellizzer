Aloe armatissima

Aloe armatissima, commonly known as the armed aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are covered in sharp, spine-like projections, which provide protection against herbivores. The plant produces yellow to orange flowers during the summer months, which are borne on tall, branched stems. Its leaves store water, allowing it to survive in arid environments.

Aloe armatissima is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
