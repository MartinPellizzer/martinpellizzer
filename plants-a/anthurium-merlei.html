Anthurium merlei

Anthurium merlei, commonly known as the red-veined anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are deep green with prominent red veins. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-shaped inflorescences that are often surrounded by a showy spathe. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium merlei is popular as a decorative plant due to its ornamental foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
