Agrimonia eupatoria var. mollis

Agrimonia eupatoria var. mollis, commonly known as soft agrimony, is a perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features upright, branched stems that are covered in soft, hairy foliage. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in clusters, which bloom in late summer. Its leaves are heart-shaped and have a distinctive serrated edge.

It is often found in woodland edges, meadows, and disturbed soils, and is valued for its historical use in herbal medicine.
