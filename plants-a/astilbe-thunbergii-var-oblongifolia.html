Astilbe thunbergii var. oblongifolia

Astilbe thunbergii var. oblongifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf Japanese astilbe, is a herbaceous perennial native to Japan.

It features upright, branched inflorescences that bear clusters of pale pink to white, feathery flowers. The plant has lance-shaped, dark green leaves that are narrow and slightly serrated. It thrives in moist, shady environments and prefers well-drained, humus-rich soil.

This variety is valued for its elegant appearance and suitability in woodland gardens.
