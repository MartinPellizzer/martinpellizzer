Anisopappus lastii

Anisopappus lastii, commonly known as the lastii sunflower, is a member of the Asteraceae family and is native to the southwestern United States.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, often found in arid or semi-arid environments. The plant features bright yellow flower heads that are surrounded by pale green bracts, giving it a distinctive appearance. Its leaves are usually narrow and covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss in its harsh habitat.

Anisopappus lastii is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its hardy nature and visual appeal.
