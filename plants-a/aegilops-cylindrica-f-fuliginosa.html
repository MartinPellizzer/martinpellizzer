Aegilops cylindrica f. fuliginosa

Aegilops cylindrica f. fuliginosa, commonly known as the dark wheatgrass, is a grass species native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its dark green, cylindrical leaves and compact growth habit. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences with numerous tiny florets. It is often used in ornamental gardening and as a cover crop due to its hardiness and adaptability.

The species is also of interest in agricultural research for its potential genetic contributions to wheat breeding programs.
