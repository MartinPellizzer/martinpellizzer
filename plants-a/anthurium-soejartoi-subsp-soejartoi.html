Anthurium soejartoi subsp. soejartoi

Anthurium soejartoi subsp.

soejartoi, commonly known as the Soejart's anthurium, is a species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia. It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a glossy texture.

The plant produces vibrant red spadices, which are the modified leaves that enclose the true flowers. It thrives in humid, warm environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in botanical gardens.
