Aster lucidulus f. firmus

Aster lucidulus f. firmus, commonly known as the firm-leaf aster, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the northeastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and opposite leaf arrangement. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically hairy and have a bluish-green hue. Its flowers are small, clustered in composite inflorescences, with white to pale blue petals.

It thrives in moist, open habitats such as meadows and woodland edges.
