Alsophila cunninghamii

Alsophila cunninghamii, commonly known as the Cunningham's tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to Australia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant has a distinctive crown of fronds that gives it a graceful, upright appearance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The fronds are typically bright green and can be used for ornamental purposes in gardens.
