Angraecum modestum

Angraecum modestum, commonly known as the modest orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of white or pale yellow flowers that bloom during the rainy season. The plant has a fibrous root system and thrives in moist, well-drained soil. It is often found in shaded, humid environments such as forest understories.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is a subject of interest for botanists and orchid enthusiasts.
