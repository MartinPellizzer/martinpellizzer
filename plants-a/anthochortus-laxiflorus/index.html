Anthochortus Laxiflorus

Anthochortus laxiflorus is a striking member of the aster family known for its vibrant purple and pink flowers. Native to the coastal regions of southern Africa, this plant thrives in sandy soils and salty air. Its unique, loosely arranged blooms make it a popular choice for coastal gardens and rockeries. With its hardy nature, it is well-suited for harsh environments. This plant not only adds beauty to landscapes but also supports local wildlife.
