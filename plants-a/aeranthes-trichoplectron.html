Aeranthes trichoplectron

Aeranthes trichoplectron, commonly known as the velvetleaf orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its dark green, velvety leaves and compact growth habit. It produces striking, fragrant flowers that are typically purple or violet in color.

Aeranthes trichoplectron thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found in forest understories.
