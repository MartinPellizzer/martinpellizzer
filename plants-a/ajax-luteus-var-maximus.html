Ajax luteus var. maximus

Ajax luteus var. maximus, commonly known as the golden glory, is a striking ornamental plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features showy, bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically bright green, providing a striking contrast to its vibrant blooms. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a border plant.

Its compact growth habit and long flowering period make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking a bold, colorful addition to their landscape.
