Alsophila dilatata

Alsophila dilatata, commonly known as the giant tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate fronds that can reach several meters in length. The plant has a robust rhizome system that allows it to thrive in humid, shaded environments. Its fronds are typically light green and have a leathery texture.

Alsophila dilatata plays an important role in tropical ecosystems, providing habitat and contributing to forest biodiversity.
