Armeria filicaulis var. minor

Armeria filicaulis var. minor, commonly known as the narrow-leaved thrift, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features dense clusters of small, white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has slender, grass-like leaves that are typically narrow and linear in shape. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its hardy nature and ornamental value make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking drought-resistant plants.
