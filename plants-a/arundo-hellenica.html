Arundo hellenica

Arundo hellenica, commonly known as Greek reed, is a tall, perennial grass native to southern Europe.

It features long, narrow leaves and can grow up to 4 meters in height. The plant is characterized by its robust rhizomes and is often found in wetland environments. It has a high water content and is sometimes used in ornamental landscaping.

Botanically, it belongs to the Poaceae family and is closely related to other reed species.
