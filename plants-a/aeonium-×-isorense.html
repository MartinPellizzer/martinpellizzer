Aeonium × isorense

Aeonium × isorense, commonly known as the "Sunburst Aeonium," is a hybrid succulent plant native to the Canary Islands.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy leaves that range from green to blue-gray in color, often with red or pink edges. The plant produces striking inflorescences of yellow flowers in the spring, which are followed by small, fleshy fruits. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements contribute to its appeal among succulent enthusiasts.
