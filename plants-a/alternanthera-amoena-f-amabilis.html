Alternanthera amoena f. amabilis

Alternanthera amoena f. amabilis, commonly known as the red-leaf alternanthera, is a striking ornamental plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red foliage, which becomes more intense under full sun exposure. The plant features small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full sunlight.

This cultivar is often used in water gardens and as a border plant due to its aesthetic appeal and easy maintenance.
