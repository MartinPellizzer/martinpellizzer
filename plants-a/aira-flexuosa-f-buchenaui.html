Aira flexuosa f. buchenaui

Aira flexuosa f. buchenaui, commonly known as the Buchenau's tufted grass, is a flowering plant belonging to the grass family Poaceae.

It is a perennial species characterized by its tufted growth habit and slender, wiry stems. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny flowers, which are typically wind-pollinated. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue and a rough texture.

This variety is often found in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands.
