Anthurium albopunctatum

Anthurium albopunctatum, commonly known as the white-spotted anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves with white spots. The plant produces a spadix inflorescence surrounded by a colorful spathe, which is typical of its genus. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Anthurium albopunctatum is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
