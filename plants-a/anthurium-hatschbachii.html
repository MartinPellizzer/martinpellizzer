Anthurium hatschbachii

Anthurium hatschbachii, commonly known as the Hatshbach anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the humid lowland forests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-like inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, often in shades of red, orange, or yellow. It thrives in warm, moist environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium hatschbachii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in botanical gardens.
