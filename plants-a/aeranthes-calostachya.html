Aeranthes calostachya

Aeranthes calostachya, commonly known as the purple orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Caribbean.

It is characterized by its striking violet to purple flowers, which bloom in clusters along the stem. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with each stem producing a new growth from a node. Its leaves are typically narrow and grass-like, arranged along the stem.

Aeranthes calostachya thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in coastal regions.
