Aster bellus

Aster bellus, commonly known as the beautiful aster, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that typically bloom in shades of purple, blue, and white. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. Native to North America, it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun.

Aster bellus is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
