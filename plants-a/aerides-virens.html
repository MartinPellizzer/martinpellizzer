Aerides virens

Aerides virens, commonly known as the green orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant features lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of greenish-white flowers. It is valued for its unique coloration and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.

Aerides virens is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
