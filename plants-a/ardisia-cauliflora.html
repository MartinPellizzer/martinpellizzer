Ardisia cauliflora

Ardisia cauliflora, commonly known as the tropical beautyberry, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and produces clusters of small, white flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant bears red, fleshy berries that are visually striking and attract various birds and small mammals. Ardisia cauliflora thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in warm climates.
