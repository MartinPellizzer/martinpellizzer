Angraecum christyanum

Angraecum christyanum, commonly known as the Christyan's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stem and large, showy flowers that typically bloom at night. The plant produces pendulous, white or pale pink blossoms with a strong, sweet fragrance, which attracts specific pollinators like moths. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Angraecum christyanum is a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts due to its striking appearance and unique blooming habits.
