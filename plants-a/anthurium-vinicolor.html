Anthurium vinicolor

Anthurium vinicolor, commonly known as the violet anthurium, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped leaves that range in color from deep green to dark purple. The plant produces showy, spadix-shaped inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, often in shades of pink, red, or violet. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium vinicolor is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and long-lasting blooms.
