Aichryson tortuosum subsp. tortuosum

Aichryson tortuosum subsp.

tortuosum, commonly known as the tortuous aichryson, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit with dense clusters of fleshy, cylindrical stems.

The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. Its leaves are typically green and somewhat fleshy, adapted to store water in arid environments.

It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.
