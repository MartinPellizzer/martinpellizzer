Asplenium hebeiense

Asplenium hebeiense, commonly known as the Hebei shield fern, is a species of fern native to China.

It belongs to the family Aspleniaceae and is characterized by its compact, shield-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often found in forest understories. Its leaves are leathery and have a distinctive, intricate pattern of veins.

Asplenium hebeiense is valued for its ornamental appearance and is sometimes cultivated in horticulture.
