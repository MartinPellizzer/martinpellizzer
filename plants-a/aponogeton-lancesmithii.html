Aponogeton lancesmithii

Aponogeton lancesmithii, commonly known as the Lanceleaf Aponogeton, is a species of aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, narrow leaves that float on the water surface, with a waxy texture that aids in buoyancy. The plant produces strikingly ornate flowers that are typically pink or red, which bloom above the water. It thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments and requires good lighting for optimal growth.

Aponogeton lancesmithii is popular in aquariums due to its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.
