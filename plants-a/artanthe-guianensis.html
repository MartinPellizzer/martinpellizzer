Artanthe guianensis

Artanthe guianensis, commonly known as the Guianan artanthe, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the family Amaranthaceae and is characterized by its striking, showy inflorescences. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest understories. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs.

Artanthe guianensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
