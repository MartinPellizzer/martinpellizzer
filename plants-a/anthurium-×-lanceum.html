Anthurium × lanceum

Anthurium × lanceum, commonly known as the velvet leaf anthurium, is a hybrid plant native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a velvety texture. The plant produces striking, spadix-shaped inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, often in shades of red, orange, or yellow. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Anthurium × lanceum is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and unique floral structures.
