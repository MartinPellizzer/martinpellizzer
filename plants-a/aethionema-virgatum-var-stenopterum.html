Aethionema virgatum var. stenopterum

Aethionema virgatum var. stenopterum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved rockcress, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically thrives in rocky or gravelly soils. The plant features small, basal leaves that are linear to lanceolate in shape. Its flowers are usually yellow or white, clustered in racemes, and bloom in early spring.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in poor, well-drained conditions.
