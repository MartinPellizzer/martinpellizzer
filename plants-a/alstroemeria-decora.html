Alstroemeria decora

Alstroemeria decora, commonly known as the Decorative Alstroemeria, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the family Alstroemeriaceae and is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, orange, yellow, and pink. The plant typically grows as a herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 1 meter, with long, narrow leaves. Its flowers are often used in floral arrangements due to their vibrant colors and long-lasting blooms.

Alstroemeria decora prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
