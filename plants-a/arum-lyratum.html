Arum lyratum

Arum lyratum, commonly known as the Chinese arrowhead, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to East Asia.

It features arrow-shaped leaves that are glossy and often have a distinctive white veins. The plant produces striking, spatulate-shaped flowers that are typically green or purple in color. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread easily in moist, shady environments.

Arum lyratum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and wetlands.
