Arisaema thunbergii

Arisaema thunbergii, commonly known as the Japanese arrowroot or threadleaf arisaema, is a herbaceous plant native to East Asia.

It features a distinctive underground tuber and a cluster of narrow, arrow-shaped leaves. The plant produces a spadix surrounded by a showy, hood-like spathe, typical of the Araceae family. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
