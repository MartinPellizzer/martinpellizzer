Amaryllis linearifolia

Amaryllis linearifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved amaryllis, is a striking plant native to South Africa.

It features long, strap-like leaves that can grow up to several feet in length, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, often in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Amaryllis linearifolia is a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor gardens due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
