Abies nordmanniana var. bornmuelleriana

Abies nordmanniana var. bornmuelleriana, commonly known as the Nordmann fir variety Bornmuelleriana, is a variant of the Nordmann fir, native to the Caucasus region.

It is a coniferous evergreen tree characterized by its conical shape and dense, dark green foliage. The leaves are typically needle-like, arranged in spirals along the branches, and have a slightly curved tip. It produces cones that are often reddish-brown and contain winged seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a landscape tree.
