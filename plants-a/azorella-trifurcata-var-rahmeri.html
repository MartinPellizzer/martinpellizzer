Azorella trifurcata var. rahmeri

Azorella trifurcata var. rahmeri, commonly known as the cushion plant, is a member of the Apiaceae family and is native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

It thrives in cold, arid environments and is adapted to withstand harsh conditions such as strong winds and intense sunlight.
