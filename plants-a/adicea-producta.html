Adicea producta

Adicea producta, commonly known as the common tamarisk, is a shrub or small tree native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its dense, aromatic foliage and small, scale-like leaves. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers in clusters, which are typically pollinated by insects. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it adaptable to various environments.

Adicea producta is often used in landscaping due to its ornamental value and hardy nature.
