Adromischus festivus

Adromischus festivus, commonly known as the Festive Stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, rounded rosettes of fleshy, gray-green leaves that are covered in a powdery coating, aiding in water retention. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, requiring minimal water once established.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
