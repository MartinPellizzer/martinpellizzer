Aira notarisiana

Aira notarisiana, commonly known as the Philippine grass, is a perennial grass species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny flowers. It is often found in moist, open habitats such as forests and grasslands.

Aira notarisiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is used in horticulture and ecological restoration projects.
