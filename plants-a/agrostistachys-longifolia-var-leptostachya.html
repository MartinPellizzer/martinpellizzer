Agrostistachys longifolia var. leptostachya

Agrostistachys longifolia var. leptostachya, commonly known as the slender-stalked grass.

It is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun exposure.

Its botanical characteristics make it suitable for ornamental use in gardens and as a ground cover.
