Asplenium drepanopteron

Asplenium drepanopteron, commonly known as the fan-fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Aspleniaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds that resemble a fan or a feather, giving it its common name. Native to tropical and subtropical regions, this plant thrives in moist, shaded environments. The fronds are typically evergreen and can reach lengths of up to 30 centimeters.

It is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in the right conditions.
