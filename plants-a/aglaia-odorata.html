Aglaia odorata

Aglaia odorata, commonly known as the fragrant aglaia, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Meliaceae and is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces yellow or orange fruits that contain flat, winged seeds. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has traditional medicinal uses in some cultures.
