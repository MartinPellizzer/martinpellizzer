Aloinopsis acuta

Aloinopsis acuta, commonly known as the sharp-tipped aloes, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of narrow, pointed leaves that are typically green with a bluish tinge. The plant produces tall, branched flowering stems with yellow flowers that bloom in summer. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.

Its compact size and ornamental leaves make it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
