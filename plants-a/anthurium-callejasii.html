Anthurium callejasii

Anthurium callejasii, commonly known as the Callejasii Philodendron, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are often deep green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, spadix-like flowers surrounded by a colorful spathe, which can range from red to orange. It thrives in high humidity and indirect light, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements have made it a sought-after ornamental plant.
