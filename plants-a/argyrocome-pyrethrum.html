Argyrocome pyrethrum

Argyrocome pyrethrum, commonly known as the silverleaf pyrethrum, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has silvery-gray foliage, which gives it its common name, and it thrives in well-drained, sunny conditions. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.

The plant also produces pyrethrin, a natural insecticide used in organic pest control.
