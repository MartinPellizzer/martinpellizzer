Alpinia kiushiana

Alpinia kiushiana, commonly known as the Japanese ginger or mountain ginger, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes other economically important plants like ginger and turmeric. Alpinia kiushiana is valued both for its ornamental appeal and for its potential medicinal properties.
