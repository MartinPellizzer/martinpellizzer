Areca rubra

Areca rubra, commonly known as the red areca palm, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that develop into red, fleshy fruits. Areca rubra thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in tropical and subtropical regions.
