Aspilia cachimboensis

Aspilia cachimboensis, commonly known as the Cachimbo everlet, is a flowering plant native to the tropical rainforests of South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads.
