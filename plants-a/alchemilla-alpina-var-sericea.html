Alchemilla alpina var. sericea

Alchemilla alpina var. sericea, commonly known as silver alpine lady, is a low-growing perennial plant native to alpine regions of Europe.

It features dense clusters of small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has silvery-gray foliage due to a dense covering of fine, silky hairs. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is often found in mountainous habitats.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to tolerate harsh environmental conditions.
