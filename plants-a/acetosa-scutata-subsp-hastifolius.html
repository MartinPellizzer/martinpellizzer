Acetosa scutata subsp. hastifolius

Acetosa scutata subsp.

hastifolius, commonly known as the narrow-leaved scutary, is a perennial herb native to parts of Europe and western Asia. It typically grows in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in meadows and grasslands.

The plant features slender stems and lanceolate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small and white, arranged in dense, spike-like clusters.

It is valued in traditional herbal medicine for its potential diuretic and anti-inflammatory properties.
