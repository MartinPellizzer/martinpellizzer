Ajax × odorus

Ajax × odorus, commonly known as the hybrid sweet potato, is a cross between *Ipomoea batatas* and *Ipomoea trifida*.

It is characterized by its vigorous growth and the production of tuberous roots that are often sweet and edible. The plant typically has heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and can grow up to several meters in height. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

The hybrid is valued for its high yield and adaptability to various growing conditions.
