Amyema plicatula

Amyema plicatula, commonly known as the narrow-leafed mistletoe, is a hemiparasitic plant that grows on various host trees, primarily in the southern regions of Australia.

Amyema plicatula is adapted to thrive in dry, sunny environments and is often found in woodlands and forests.
