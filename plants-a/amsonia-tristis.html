Amsonia tristis

Amsonia tristis, commonly known as blue mistflower, is a perennial herb native to North America.

It features showy, blue to lavender flower clusters that bloom in late summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Amsonia tristis is often used in native plant gardens for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
