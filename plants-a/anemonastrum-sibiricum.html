Anemonastrum sibiricum

Anemonastrum sibiricum, commonly known as the Siberian anemone, is a flowering plant native to the cold regions of Siberia and Central Asia.

It is a perennial herb with showy, white to pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has basal leaves that are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, and its stems are usually upright and branched. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to harsh, arctic or alpine environments.

Anemonastrum sibiricum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens in colder climates.
