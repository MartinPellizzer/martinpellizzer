Albizia tenuispica

Albizia tenuispica, commonly known as the slender-leaf Albizia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its bipinnate leaves and showy, fragrant flowers that range from white to pink. The plant has a shallow root system and thrives in well-drained soils, making it adaptable to various environmental conditions.

It is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and can also serve as a source of fodder for livestock.
