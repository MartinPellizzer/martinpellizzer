Androrchis × aurunca

Androrchis × aurunca, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a cross between Androrchis flaccida and Orchis morio.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a slender, leafy stem that bears a single, showy spike of flowers. The flowers are typically pale yellow to orange, with a distinctive hooded lip. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodlands or along stream banks.

This hybrid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
