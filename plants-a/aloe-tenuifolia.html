Aloe tenuifolia

Aloe tenuifolia, commonly known as narrow-leaved aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features slender, upright stems with narrow, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. Aloe tenuifolia is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its leaves to survive drought conditions.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
