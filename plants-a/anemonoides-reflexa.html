Anemonoides reflexa

Anemonoides reflexa, commonly known as the reflexed anemone, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, white to pinkish flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has basal leaves that are heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers sunny to partially shaded environments.

Anemonoides reflexa is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and its ability to add color to early spring landscapes.
