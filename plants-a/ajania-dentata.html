Ajania dentata

Ajania dentata, commonly known as the mountain ajania, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Ajania dentata is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitat.
