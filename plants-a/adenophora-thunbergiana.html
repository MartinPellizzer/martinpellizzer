Adenophora thunbergiana

Adenophora thunbergiana, commonly known as the Chinese bellflower, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, often with a purple or violet throat. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged in a basal rosette. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to bloom throughout the summer months.
