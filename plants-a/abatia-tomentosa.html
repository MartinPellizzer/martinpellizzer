Abatia tomentosa

Abatia tomentosa, commonly known as the silky abatia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its soft, hairy leaves and clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its bark is often used in traditional medicine for its potential anti-inflammatory properties.

The fruit is a capsule that contains several small, hard seeds.
