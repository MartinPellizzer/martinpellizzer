Angelica nuristanica

Angelica nuristanica, commonly known as the Nuristan angelica, is a tall biennial or perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Afghanistan and Pakistan.

It belongs to the Apiaceae family, characterized by its aromatic leaves and flowers. The plant features large, compound leaves with a pungent odor and yellowish-green umbels of small flowers. Its roots are often harvested for their medicinal properties, used in traditional remedies.

Angelica nuristanica thrives in cool, moist environments at high altitudes.
