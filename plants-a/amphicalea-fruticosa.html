Amphicalea fruticosa

Amphicalea fruticosa, commonly known as the coral tree, is a flowering plant native to the Cape region of South Africa.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 2 to 5 meters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a rough, hairy texture.
