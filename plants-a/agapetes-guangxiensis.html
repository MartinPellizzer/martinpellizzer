Agapetes guangxiensis

Agapetes guangxiensis, commonly known as the Guangxi stone pine, is a species of evergreen shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit. The plant features small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. It produces bell-shaped flowers in shades of white or pink, which bloom in the spring.

Agapetes guangxiensis is often found in shaded, moist forest environments and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
