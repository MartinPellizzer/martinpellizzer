Agrostis boeckeleri

Agrostis boeckeleri, commonly known as the Boeckeler's bentgrass, is a perennial grass species native to South America.

It typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its inflorescence consists of a dense cluster of spikelets, which are characteristic of the Poaceae family.

Agrostis boeckeleri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in horticultural settings.
