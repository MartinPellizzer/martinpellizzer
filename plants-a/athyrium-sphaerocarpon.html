Athyrium sphaerocarpon

Athyrium sphaerocarpon, commonly known as the globe-ferns, is a species of fern native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its compact, clustered growth habit and dark green, leathery fronds. The plant produces sporangia in spherical clusters, which is a distinctive feature of its reproductive structure. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in woodland areas.

Its unique morphology and habitat preferences make it a notable species within the Athyriaceae family.
