Arenaria zhenkangensis

Arenaria zhenkangensis, commonly known as the Zhenkang sandwort, is a flowering plant species native to China.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit. The plant produces small, white or pink flowers that bloom in early summer. It thrives in sandy or rocky soils and is adapted to arid environments.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
