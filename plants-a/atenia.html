Atenia

Atenia, commonly known as the common tamarisk, is a shrub or small tree native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the family Tamaricaceae and is characterized by its small, scale-like leaves that are often mistaken for bark. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that appear in clusters, typically in the spring. Atenia is known for its ability to thrive in arid and saline soils, making it a hardy species in harsh environments.

Its root system is extensive, which helps it survive in dry conditions and prevents soil erosion.
