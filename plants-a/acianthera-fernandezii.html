Acianthera fernandezii

Acianthera fernandezii, commonly known as the Fernandez's acianthera, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in cloud forests.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
