Anthurium panduriforme

Anthurium panduriforme, commonly known as the paddle anthurium, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy surface. The plant produces striking spadices, which are brightly colored inflorescences that serve as the flower structure. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.

Anthurium panduriforme is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and ease of care.
