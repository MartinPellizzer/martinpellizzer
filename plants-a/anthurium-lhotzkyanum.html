Anthurium lhotzkyanum

Anthurium lhotzkyanum, commonly known as the Lhotzkyana arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, glossy, dark green leaves that are often variegated with white or cream markings. The plant produces striking spadices, which are modified leaves that enclose the true flowers, typically in shades of red or orange. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium lhotzkyanum is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
