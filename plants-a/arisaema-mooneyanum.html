Arisaema mooneyanum

Arisaema mooneyanum, commonly known as the Mooney's arrowhead, is a herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its unique inflorescence consisting of a spadix surrounded by a modified leaf called a spathe. The plant produces striking, arrow-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white and green patterns. Its flowers are typically green or yellow, and it thrives in moist, shaded environments.

Arisaema mooneyanum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its distinctive foliage.
