Anthurium pyrifolium

Anthurium pyrifolium, commonly known as the pineapple leaf anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, glossy, heart-shaped leaves that resemble pineapple leaves, hence its common name. The plant produces striking, spadix-like inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, which can range from red to yellow or orange. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium pyrifolium is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
