Astartea laricifolia

Astartea laricifolia, commonly known as the silverleaf aster, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and woodlands. Its leaves are silver-gray due to a dense covering of fine hairs, and its flowers bloom in late summer to early fall.

Astartea laricifolia is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and flowers.
