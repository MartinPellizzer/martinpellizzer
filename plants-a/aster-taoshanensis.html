Aster taoshanensis

Aster taoshanensis, commonly known as the Taoshan aster, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers. The plant is characterized by its showy purple or blue flowers that bloom in the summer months. It typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in forested areas.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for both ecological studies and horticultural interest.
