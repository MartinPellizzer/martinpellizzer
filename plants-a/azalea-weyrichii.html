Azalea weyrichii

Azalea weyrichii, commonly known as the Weyrich azalea, is a species native to China.

It belongs to the Rhododendron genus and is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to deep pink. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are glossy and elliptical, providing a striking contrast to its vibrant blooms.

Azalea weyrichii prefers acidic, well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
