Aster polius

Aster polius, commonly known as the purple coneflower, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features showy, violet to purple petals surrounding a central cone-like structure composed of tiny florets. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and natural landscapes.

Aster polius is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
