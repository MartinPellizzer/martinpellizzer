Alternanthera helleri

Alternanthera helleri, commonly known as the red-leaf alternanthera, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage, which can range from deep red to orange and pink, depending on light exposure. The plant has opposite, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, inconspicuous flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to maintain its vibrant colors.

Alternanthera helleri is often grown as an ornamental plant in water gardens or as a houseplant.
