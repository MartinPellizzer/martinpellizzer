Amaryllis toxicaria

Amaryllis toxicaria, commonly known as the red spider lily, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, strap-like leaves and produces clusters of vibrant red flowers with a distinctive spider-like appearance. The plant is part of the Amaryllidaceae family, which includes other ornamental and toxic plants. Its bulbs contain toxic alkaloids, making it potentially harmful if ingested.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and containers.
