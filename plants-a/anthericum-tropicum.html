Anthericum tropicum

Anthericum tropicum, commonly known as the tropical arum, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It features a distinctive inflorescence composed of a spadix surrounded by a showy, modified leaf called a spathe. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged along the spadix, which are typically enclosed within the spathe. Its leaves are broad, arrow-shaped, and often have a waxy texture.

Anthericum tropicum is adapted to humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.
