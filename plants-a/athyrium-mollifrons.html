Athyrium mollifrons

Athyrium mollifrons, commonly known as the silky fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Athyriaceae and is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and stream sides. Its leaves are soft and hairy, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Athyrium mollifrons is valued for its ornamental qualities in horticulture.
