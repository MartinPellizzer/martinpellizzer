Anemone nemorosa var. monoica

Anemone nemorosa var. monoica, commonly known as the single-flowered wood anemone, is a herbaceous perennial native to Europe.

It features delicate, white or pale pink flowers with a single layer of petals, typically blooming in early spring. The plant has basal leaves that are heart-shaped and covered in a fine, white down. It thrives in woodland environments, preferring moist, well-drained soil.

Its reproductive strategy involves wind pollination, and it is often found in shaded, forested areas.
