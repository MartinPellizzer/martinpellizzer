Anthoxanthum odoratum var. puelii

Anthoxanthum odoratum var. puelii, commonly known as sweet vernal grass, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the grass family (Poaceae) and is characterized by its bright yellow to golden-green inflorescences. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows, prairies, and disturbed soils. It is a perennial species that produces small, wind-pollinated flowers.

Its aromatic foliage and vibrant color make it a notable species in ecological and botanical studies.
