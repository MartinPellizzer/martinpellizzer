Ayenia velutina

Ayenia velutina, commonly known as the velvet ayenia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features soft, velvety leaves that give it its common name, and it produces small, daisy-like flowers in shades of yellow and white. Ayenia velutina thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical structure supports its role in local ecosystems, providing habitat and nectar for pollinators.
