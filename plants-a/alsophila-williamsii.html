Alsophila williamsii

Alsophila williamsii, commonly known as the Williams' tree fern, is a species native to New Zealand.

It is a member of the Cyatheaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feathery fronds. The plant has a distinctive crown of pinnate leaves that can grow up to several meters in length. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Alsophila williamsii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological significance in its native habitat.
