Aerides leoniae

Aerides leoniae, commonly known as the Leonia orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant features a slender, upright stem with a single, lance-shaped leaf. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish-white, with a distinctive lip that is often darker in color.

Aerides leoniae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
