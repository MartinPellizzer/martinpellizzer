Ardisia eglandulosa

Ardisia eglandulosa, commonly known as the smooth ardisia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a member of the Ericaceae family and typically grows as an evergreen shrub. The plant features glossy, dark green leaves and produces small, white, fragrant flowers. Its fruit is a bright red, fleshy berry that contains several seeds.
