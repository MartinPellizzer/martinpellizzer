Acalypha senilis

Acalypha senilis, commonly known as silverleaf ironweed, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking silver-gray foliage, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically green or reddish in color. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in forest understories.

Acalypha senilis is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique leaf coloration.
