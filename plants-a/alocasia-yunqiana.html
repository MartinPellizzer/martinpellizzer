Alocasia yunqiana

Alocasia yunqiana, commonly known as the Yunqian Alocasia, is a striking tropical plant native to China.

It features large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a waxy texture. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its foliage is often adorned with subtle variegation, adding visual interest to its appearance.

Alocasia yunqiana is prized for its ornamental value and is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
