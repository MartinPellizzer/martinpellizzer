Asplenium formosum

Asplenium formosum, commonly known as the Taiwan spleenwort, is a small, evergreen fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Aspleniaceae and is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped leaves that are often clustered at the base. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky crevices. Its fronds are usually short and compact, with a dark green color and a slightly wavy appearance.

Asplenium formosum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in shaded garden settings.
