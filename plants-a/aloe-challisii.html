Aloe challisii

Aloe challisii, commonly known as the Orange Tree Aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that are typically a bright green color with a slight orange tint. The plant produces yellow flowers in clusters during the summer months, adding ornamental value. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Aloe challisii is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal and potential medicinal properties.
