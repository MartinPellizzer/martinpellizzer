Anthyllis vulneraria var. dichroma

Anthyllis vulneraria var. dichroma, commonly known as the two-colored heal-all, is a perennial flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive two-colored flowers, typically pink and white. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Its ability to fix nitrogen makes it an important plant in soil enrichment and ecological restoration.
