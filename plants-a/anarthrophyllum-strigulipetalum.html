Anarthrophyllum strigulipetalum

Anarthrophyllum strigulipetalum, commonly known as the velvetleaf sandwort, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically forms dense mats of foliage. The plant features small, lance-shaped leaves covered in fine, velvety hairs, which give it its common name. Its flowers are small and clustered, often appearing in shades of white or pale yellow.

It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.
