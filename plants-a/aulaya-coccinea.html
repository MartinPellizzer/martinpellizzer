Aulaya coccinea

Aulaya coccinea, commonly known as the red bellflower, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange-pink flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sun. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a hairy texture.

Aulaya coccinea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
