Aerides japonica

Aerides japonica, commonly known as the Japanese orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, often hairy stems and clusters of fragrant, showy flowers. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences with numerous white to pinkish flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Aerides japonica is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for both novice and experienced orchid growers.
