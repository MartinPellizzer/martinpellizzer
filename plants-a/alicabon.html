Alicabon

Alicabon, commonly known as the golden spurge, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa and Asia.

Its small, inconspicuous flowers are typically green or yellow and are clustered in cyathia, a unique structure characteristic of the Euphorbiaceae family.
