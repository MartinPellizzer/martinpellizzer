Arachnorchis radiata

Arachnorchis radiata, commonly known as the radiant spider-orchid, is an epiphytic orchid native to Australia.

It is characterized by its delicate, spider-like appearance with slender, wiry stems and small, pale green leaves. The plant produces striking, showy flowers in shades of yellow and orange, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, loose soil.

Arachnorchis radiata is a rare and protected species, often found in rainforest habitats.
