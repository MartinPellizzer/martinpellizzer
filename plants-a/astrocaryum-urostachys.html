Astrocaryum urostachys

Astrocaryum urostachys, commonly known as the Brazilian wax palm, is a tall, slender palm native to the Amazon rainforest.

It features a tall, unbranched trunk with a smooth, grayish-brown bark. The plant produces large, fan-shaped leaves that are often used in traditional crafts. Its fruit is a small, spiny drupe that contains a single seed.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in its native habitat.
