Avenastrum planiculme

Avenastrum planiculme, commonly known as the flat-leaf wormwood, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features slender, upright stems with narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small and yellow, arranged in clustered inflorescences, and it produces small, oval-shaped seeds.

Avenastrum planiculme is typically found in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to thrive in arid environments.
