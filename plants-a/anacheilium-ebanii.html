Anacheilium ebanii

Anacheilium ebanii, commonly known as the golden bell orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as forest understories, where it thrives in partial shade and high humidity.
