Anthurium acutangulum

Anthurium acutangulum, commonly known as the spiny anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves with spiky edges. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-like inflorescences that are often surrounded by a colorful spathe. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect sunlight for optimal growth.

Anthurium acutangulum is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its unique foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
