Arctium cylindraceum

Arctium cylindraceum, commonly known as the cylindrical burdock, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features tall, slender stems covered in spiny bracts that form distinctive, cylinder-shaped seed pods. Its leaves are heart-shaped with a rough, hairy texture, and it produces small yellow flowers in the summer.

The plant is often found in disturbed soils and is known for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal medicine.
