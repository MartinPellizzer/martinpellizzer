Arisaema lobatum var. crenatum

Arisaema lobatum var. crenatum, commonly known as the crested arrowhead, is a herbaceous plant native to the eastern United States.

It features a distinctive, arrowhead-shaped leaf with a prominent crest, and its inflorescence consists of a spadix enclosed by a spathe. The plant is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit and produces small, greenish-white flowers. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

The botanical features of this plant make it a notable species in the Araceae family.
