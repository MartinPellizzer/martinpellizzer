Angianthus tomentosus

Angianthus tomentosus, commonly known as the woolly angianthus, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun exposure.

Angianthus tomentosus is often cultivated for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
