Astrantia major var. carniolica

Astrantia major var. carniolica, commonly known as the Carniolica masterwort, is a perennial herb native to Central Europe.

It features basal leaves that are heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs, with a distinctive flowering head of pink to purple flowers. The plant produces small, fluffy seeds that are dispersed by the wind. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
