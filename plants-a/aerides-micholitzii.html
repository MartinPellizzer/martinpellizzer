Aerides micholitzii

Aerides micholitzii, commonly known as the Micholitz's orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a sympodial orchid with a short rhizome and several pseudobulbs, each bearing a single leaf. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences with showy, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate humidity and well-draining soil.

Aerides micholitzii is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
