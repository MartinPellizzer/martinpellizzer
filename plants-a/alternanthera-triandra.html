Alternanthera triandra

Alternanthera triandra, commonly known as the red-stemmed Alternanthera, is a striking ornamental plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red stems and lance-shaped leaves that often display a mix of green and red hues. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade. It is typically grown as an annual in temperate climates but can be perennial in tropical regions.

Its unique coloration makes it a popular choice for water gardens and containers.
