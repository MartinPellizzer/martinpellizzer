Aster novae-scotiae

Aster novae-scotiae, commonly known as the Nova Scotia aster, is a perennial herb native to the coastal regions of Nova Scotia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and medicinal properties. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils along the shores, thriving in maritime climates. Its flowers are usually blue to purple in color, with ray florets surrounding a central disk.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
