Agrimonia eupatoria var. nepalensis

Agrimonia eupatoria var. nepalensis, commonly known as Nepal agrimony, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayan regions of Nepal and northern India.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late summer. Its fruits are dry, capsule-like structures that release tiny seeds when mature.

Agrimonia eupatoria var. nepalensis is valued for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal medicine for various ailments.
