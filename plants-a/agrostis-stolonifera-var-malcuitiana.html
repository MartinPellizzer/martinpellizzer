Agrostis stolonifera var. malcuitiana

Agrostis stolonifera var. malcuitiana, commonly known as the slender meadowgrass, is a cool-season grass species native to temperate regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its fine, tufted blades and creeping growth habit, forming dense, low-growing mats. The plant typically thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in meadows, pastures, and disturbed sites. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, with a bluish-green coloration.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping for erosion control.
