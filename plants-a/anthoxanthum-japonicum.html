Anthoxanthum japonicum

Anthoxanthum japonicum, commonly known as Japanese meadowgrass, is a perennial grass species native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Poaceae family and is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and marshes. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green coloration.

Anthoxanthum japonicum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping.
