Amaranthus catechu

Amaranthus catechu, commonly known as Chinese spinach or red amaranth, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its erect, branched stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often reddish or purple in color. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically green or yellowish in hue. Its leaves are edible and are used in various cuisines, particularly in Asian countries.

Amaranthus catechu is also valued for its nutritional content, including high levels of protein and iron.
