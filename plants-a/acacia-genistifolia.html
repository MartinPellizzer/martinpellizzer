Acacia genistifolia, commonly known as the silky acacia, is a shrub or small tree native to parts of Africa and the Arabian Peninsula.

The plant produces yellow flowers in clusters, which are followed by long, narrow pods containing seeds. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, showing adaptability to poor soil conditions.
