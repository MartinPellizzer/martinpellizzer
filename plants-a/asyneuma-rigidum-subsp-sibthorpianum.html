Asyneuma rigidum subsp. sibthorpianum

Asyneuma rigidum subsp.

sibthorpianum, commonly known as the stiff asyneuma, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads.

The plant typically grows as a perennial herb with rigid, upright stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. Its small yellow flowers appear in clusters, contributing to its ornamental value.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grassland habitats.
