Anthurium mapiriense

Anthurium mapiriense, commonly known as the Mapiriense flamingo flower, is a tropical plant native to Colombia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its striking, vibrant red spadix and heart-shaped, waxy leaves. The plant thrives in humid, warm environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and ornamental purposes.

Anthurium mapiriense is also valued for its air-purifying properties and ability to thrive in low-light conditions.
