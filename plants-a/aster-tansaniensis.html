Aster tansaniensis

Aster tansaniensis, commonly known as the Tansanian aster, is a flowering plant native to East Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in grasslands and rocky areas, thriving in temperate climates. Its flowers are usually blue to purple in color, with ray florets surrounding a central disc.

Aster tansaniensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting pollinators.
