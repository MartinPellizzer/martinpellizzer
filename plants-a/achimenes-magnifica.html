Achimenes magnifica

Achimenes magnifica, commonly known as the showy achimenes, is a tropical perennial plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, pink, and white. The plant features heart-shaped leaves and produces showy inflorescences that bloom throughout the growing season. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Achimenes magnifica is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a cut flower.
