Aglaonema pictum var. scortechinii

Aglaonema pictum var. scortechinii, commonly known as the Silver Queen Chinese Evergreen, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated foliage with silver and green patterns on its broad, lance-shaped leaves. The plant is evergreen, meaning it retains its leaves year-round, and thrives in low-light conditions. It is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

The leaves are typically smooth and glossy, adding a decorative touch to any room.
