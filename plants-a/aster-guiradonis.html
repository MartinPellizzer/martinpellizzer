Aster guiradonis

Aster guiradonis, commonly known as the Guiradonis aster, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forests and stream sides. Its flowers are usually purple or blue, with ray florets surrounding a central disk of tubular flowers.

Aster guiradonis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting pollinators.
