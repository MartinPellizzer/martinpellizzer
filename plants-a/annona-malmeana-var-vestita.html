Annona malmeana var. vestita

Annona malmeana var. vestita, commonly known as the velvet annona, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its smooth, velvety leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces large, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruit is a yellow, spiky berry that ripens in late summer.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional medicinal uses.
