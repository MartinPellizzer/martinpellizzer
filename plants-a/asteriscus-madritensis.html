Asteriscus madritensis

Asteriscus madritensis, commonly known as the Spanish aster, is a flowering plant native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry habitats, showcasing its adaptability to arid environments. It produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in the summer months.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and drought-tolerant landscaping.
