Alocasia flemingiana

Alocasia flemingiana, commonly known as the Fleming's elephant ear, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 3 feet in length. The plant belongs to the Araceae family, which includes other ornamental plants like calla lilies. Its leaves are usually a deep green with a velvety texture and often have a reddish tint on the underside.

Alocasia flemingiana thrives in humid, shady environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
