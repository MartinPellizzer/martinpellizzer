Aeonium haworthii subsp. volkeri

Aeonium haworthii subsp.

volkeri, commonly known as the Volker's Aeonium, is a succulent plant native to the Canary Islands. It features rosettes of thick, fleshy, blue-green leaves that are covered in a powdery bloom, giving it a distinctive appearance.

The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow flowers in the spring. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its compact size and striking foliage make it a popular choice for both ornamental gardens and indoor cultivation.
