Aspidogyne bicolor

Aspidogyne bicolor, commonly known as the two-colored aspidogyne, is a member of the Rubiaceae family.

It is a small shrub characterized by its opposite, elliptical leaves that are typically green on the upper surface and pale or white on the underside. Aspidogyne bicolor is native to tropical regions of Africa and is often found in moist, shaded environments.
