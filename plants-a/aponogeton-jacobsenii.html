Aponogeton jacobsenii

Aponogeton jacobsenii, commonly known as the African water lettuce, is a aquatic plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its floating leaves that are broad and rounded, often with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, white flowers that emerge from the water surface. It thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments.

Aponogeton jacobsenii is often used in ornamental ponds and water gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
