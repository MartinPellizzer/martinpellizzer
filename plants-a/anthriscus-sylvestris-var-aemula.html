Anthriscus sylvestris var. aemula

Anthriscus sylvestris var. aemula, commonly known as the slender cow parsley, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream sides. It is often mistaken for other members of its genus due to its similar appearance.
