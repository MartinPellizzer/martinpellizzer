Alsophila zenkeri

Alsophila zenkeri, commonly known as the Zenker's tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to tropical regions of Africa and Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds. The plant has a distinctive crown of fronds that can reach several meters in height, giving it a lush, tropical appearance. Its rhizome system supports its growth and helps in nutrient absorption from the surrounding soil.

Alsophila zenkeri is often found in humid, shaded environments such as rainforests and is valued for its ornamental qualities in horticulture.
