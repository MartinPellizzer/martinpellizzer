Aronia × willdenowii

Aronia × willdenowii, commonly known as the purple beauty berry, is a hybrid shrub native to eastern North America.

It is characterized by its dark purple berries that persist through the winter, providing food for birds and other wildlife. The plant typically grows to a height of 3 to 6 feet and features compound leaves with serrated edges. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is adaptable to a variety of soil types.

The botanical features of Aronia × willdenowii make it a valuable addition to ornamental gardens and ecological landscapes.
