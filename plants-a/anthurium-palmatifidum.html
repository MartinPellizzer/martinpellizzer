Anthurium palmatifidum

Anthurium palmatifidum, commonly known as the broad-leaved anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, glossy, heart-shaped leaves that are deeply divided, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces striking spadices, which are modified leaves that house the actual flowers. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium palmatifidum is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
