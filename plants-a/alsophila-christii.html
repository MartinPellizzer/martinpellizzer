Alsophila christii

Alsophila christii, commonly known as the Christ's tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds that can reach several meters in length. The plant has a distinctive crown of fronds that gives it a unique appearance, often resembling a tree with a crown of greenery. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forested areas.

The fronds are typically evergreen, contributing to its ornamental value in horticulture.
