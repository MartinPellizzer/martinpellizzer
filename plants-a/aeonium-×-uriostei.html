Aeonium × uriostei

Aeonium × uriostei, commonly known as the Ghost Plant, is a hybrid succulent native to the Canary Islands.

It features rosettes of fleshy, triangular leaves that are typically pale green to bluish-gray, giving it a ghostly appearance. The plant produces tall, branching inflorescences with yellow flowers in the spring. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with full sun.

Aeonium × uriostei is popular among succulent enthusiasts for its unique aesthetics and low maintenance requirements.
