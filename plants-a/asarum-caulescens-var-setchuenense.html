Asarum caulescens var. setchuenense

Asarum caulescens var. setchuenense, commonly known as the Sichuan asarum, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Sichuan, China.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, reddish-brown leaves that are often covered in a dense, waxy coating. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in a spathaceous inflorescence, typical of the Aristolochiaceae family. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

The plant contains various bioactive compounds, making it of interest in traditional medicine and phytochemical research.
