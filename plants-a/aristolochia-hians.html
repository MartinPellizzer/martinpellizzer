Aristolochia hians

Aristolochia hians, commonly known as the slender Dutchman's pipe, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean and northern South America.

It is characterized by its unique, pipe-shaped flowers that are typically greenish-yellow with a distinctive hooded structure. The plant produces small, elliptical leaves that are often heart-shaped at the base. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical forests.

The genus Aristolochia is known for its complex chemical compounds, some of which have medicinal uses.
