Abutilon Asiaticum

Abutilon asiaticum is a unique flowering plant native to parts of Asia. It is known for its distinctive lantern-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of yellow and orange. This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade. Its adaptability makes it a popular choice for gardens and green spaces.
