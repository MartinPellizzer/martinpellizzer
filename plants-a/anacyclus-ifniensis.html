Anacyclus ifniensis

Anacyclus ifniensis, commonly known as the Ifni thistle, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of North Africa.

It is characterized by its spiny, lobed leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers. It thrives in sandy or rocky soils and is adapted to withstand drought conditions.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
