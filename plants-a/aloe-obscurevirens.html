Aloe obscurevirens

Aloe obscurevirens, commonly known as the dark red aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a reddish tinge. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with small, yellow flowers. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for xeriscaping and indoor gardening.
