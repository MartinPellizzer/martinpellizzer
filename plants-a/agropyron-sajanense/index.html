Agropyron Sajanense

Agropyron sajanense is a resilient grass species native to the cold climates of Siberia and northern Asia. It thrives in harsh environments with low temperatures and poor soil conditions. This plant is known for its hardy nature and ability to stabilize soil in arid and tundra regions. Its deep root system helps prevent erosion and supports biodiversity in its ecosystem. Despite its tough growing conditions, Agropyron sajanense plays a vital role in the survival of local wildlife and plant life.
