Argyrolobium dimidiatum

Argyrolobium dimidiatum, commonly known as the yellow bursaria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in sandy or rocky soils. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are alternate and pinnately divided, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and along coastal areas.
