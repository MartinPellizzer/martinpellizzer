Achillea millefolium var. megacephala

Achillea millefolium var. megacephala, commonly known as the giant yarrow, is a variant of the common yarrow plant.

It is characterized by its large, showy flower heads that are typically white or pale pink. The plant has finely divided, feathery leaves that give it a delicate appearance. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia and thrives in well-drained soils.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
