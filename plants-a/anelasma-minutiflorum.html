Anelasma minutiflorum

Anelasma minutiflorum, commonly known as the small-flowered anelasma, is a species of flowering plant native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a herbaceous plant with small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in the spring. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. Its botanical features include a fibrous root system and a compact growth habit.

Anelasma minutiflorum is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.
