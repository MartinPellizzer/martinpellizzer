Aristotelia × colensoi

Aristotelia × colensoi, commonly known as the kawakawa tree, is a hybrid species native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Elaeocarpaceae family and is characterized by its evergreen foliage and showy white flowers. The plant typically grows as a small to medium-sized tree or shrub, reaching heights of up to 10 meters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a distinctive heart-shaped base.

The fruit is a small, fleshy drupe that contains a single seed, and the plant is valued for its ecological and cultural significance in New Zealand.
