Aiolon canadense

Aiolon canadense, commonly known as the Canadian spider orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its unique, spider-like appearance due to its elongated, narrow leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands.

Aiolon canadense is often found in the eastern United States and parts of Canada.
