Arenaria davisii

Arenaria davisii, commonly known as Davis's sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Sierra Nevada in California.

It is characterized by its dense clusters of tiny, white flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant has small, succulent leaves that help it conserve water in its arid environment. It thrives in rocky soils and is often found in alpine meadows.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species within its range.
