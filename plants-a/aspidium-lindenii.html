Aspidium lindenii

Aspidium lindenii, commonly known as the Lindenberg's water fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, feather-like fronds that are typically bi-lobed and covered in small, rounded sori. This plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found near streams or in dense forest understories. Its rhizomes are creeping and help it spread along the forest floor.

Aspidium lindenii is valued for its ornamental appeal and plays a role in maintaining ecological balance in its native habitat.
