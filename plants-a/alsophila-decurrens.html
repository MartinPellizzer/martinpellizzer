Alsophila decurrens

Alsophila decurrens, commonly known as the narrow-leaf tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and a crown of feathery, lance-shaped fronds. The plant belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is known for its ability to thrive in humid, tropical environments. Its fronds are typically dark green and can grow up to several meters in length.
