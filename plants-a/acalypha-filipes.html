Acalypha filipes

Acalypha filipes, commonly known as silky ironwood, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height. The plant is characterized by its dark green, glossy leaves and small, inconspicuous flowers. Its bark is thick and fibrous, which contributes to its common name.

It thrives in tropical climates and is often found in dry forests and scrublands.
