Aeranthes tenella var. borbonica

Aeranthes tenella var. borbonica, commonly known as the Bourbon miniature orchid, is a compact, epiphytic orchid native to Madagascar.

It features small, delicate leaves and produces fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers in a dense, spike-like cluster. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media. It is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated as a houseplant.

Due to its specific care requirements, it is considered a challenging but rewarding species for experienced growers.
