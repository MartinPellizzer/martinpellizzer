Aeonium × lidii

Aeonium × lidii, commonly known as the "Lidii's Aeonium," is a hybrid succulent plant belonging to the Crassulaceae family.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped growth pattern, with thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green or variegated with white or pink markings. Native to the Canary Islands, this plant is well adapted to arid and rocky environments. It produces striking inflorescences of yellow or orange flowers during the spring or summer.

Aeonium × lidii is popular among gardeners for its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
