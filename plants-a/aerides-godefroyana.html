Aerides godefroyana

Aerides godefroyana, commonly known as the Godefroy's orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that support its foliage and flowering. Its flowers are usually pink or purple, with a distinctive spurred lip.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
