Anthericum comosum

Anthericum comosum, commonly known as the clustered lily, is a herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers. The plant is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit, which allows it to spread and form dense colonies. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or meadow environments.

Anthericum comosum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
