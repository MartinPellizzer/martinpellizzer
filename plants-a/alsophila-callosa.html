Alsophila callosa

Alsophila callosa, commonly known as the hairy tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnately compound fronds that give it a distinctive, graceful appearance. The plant has a slow growth rate and can live for many decades, making it a long-lived species. Its fronds are typically bright green and can reach lengths of up to 3 meters.

Alsophila callosa thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical forests.
