Allionia polytricha

Allionia polytricha, commonly known as the common rock rose, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant features small, clustered white or pink flowers that bloom in the spring. Its leaves are narrow and gray-green, often covered in a fine layer of hair.

Allionia polytricha is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in poor soil conditions.
