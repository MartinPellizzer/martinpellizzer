Argyrolobium zanonii subsp. majus

Argyrolobium zanonii subsp.

majus, commonly known as the large-flowered silver-lobed burr. It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa, characterized by its silver-green foliage and showy yellow flowers.

The plant produces distinctive, spiny pods that contain seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.
