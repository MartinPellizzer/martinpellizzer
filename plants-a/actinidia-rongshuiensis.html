Actinidia rongshuiensis

Actinidia rongshuiensis, commonly known as the Rongshui actinidia, is a rare species of flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Actinidiaceae family and is characterized by its deciduous vines with compound leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in the spring, followed by edible berries that are similar to kiwifruit in appearance. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in moist, shaded forest environments.
