Argyrocome tietkensii

Argyrocome tietkensii, commonly known as the Tietkens' silverleaf.

It is a rare flowering plant native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia. The plant is characterized by its silvery-gray leaves, which are covered in fine, reflective hairs. It produces small, clustered white flowers that bloom during the rainy season.
