Aloe × rigida

Aloe × rigida, commonly known as the hybrid aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, sword-shaped leaves that are typically green with a bluish tint and may have white spots. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow to orange flowers, which bloom in late summer. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Aloe × rigida is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has mild medicinal properties.
