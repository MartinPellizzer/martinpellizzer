Asplenium × mustangense

Asplenium × mustangense, commonly known as the Mustang spleenwort, is a hybrid fern species that exhibits unique botanical characteristics.

It is a member of the Aspleniaceae family and is native to the southwestern United States, particularly in Arizona and Nevada. The plant is characterized by its small, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. It thrives in rocky, arid environments and is often found growing on limestone substrates.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
