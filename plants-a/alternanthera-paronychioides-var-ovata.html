Alternanthera paronychioides var. ovata

Alternanthera paronychioides var. ovata, commonly known as the velvetleaf alternanthera, is a tropical plant characterized by its striking, velvety foliage.

It features lance-shaped leaves that are often a deep red or purple, with a soft, textured surface. The plant is native to South America and thrives in warm, humid environments. It is typically grown as a decorative plant in gardens or as a houseplant due to its ornamental value.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for water gardens and bog habitats.
