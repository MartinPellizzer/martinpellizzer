Actinocephalus verae

Actinocephalus verae, commonly known as the velvet leaf.

It is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves that are covered in fine hairs. It produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color.

Actinocephalus verae is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and compact growth habit.
