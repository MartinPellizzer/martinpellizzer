Adonis vernalis var. serotina

Adonis vernalis var. serotina, commonly known as the late spring adonis, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its striking yellow flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in sunny to partially shaded environments. Its leaves are palmately lobed, and the flowers consist of numerous stamens surrounding a central pistil.

Adonis vernalis var. serotina is often found in alpine and subalpine habitats, where it plays a role in local ecosystems.
