Acianthera rostellata

Acianthera rostellata, commonly known as the rostellate orchid, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its flowers are usually pale pink to white, with a distinct rostellum, a specialized structure at the base of the lip.

Acianthera rostellata is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
