Acer × durettii f. aureomarmoratum

Acer × durettii f. aureomarmoratum, commonly known as the golden marbled maple, is a unique hybrid species within the maple genus.

It is characterized by its striking foliage, featuring a marbled pattern of yellow and green on its leaves. The plant is native to the mountainous regions of China, where it thrives in temperate climates. Its leaves are typically ovate to elliptical in shape, with a smooth margin and a serrated edge.

This ornamental tree is valued for its aesthetic appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant seasonal color changes.
