Aracium succisifolium

Aracium succisifolium, commonly known as the rock rose, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves that help it conserve water in arid environments. The plant produces vibrant pink or white flowers during the spring and summer seasons. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Aracium succisifolium is also valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
