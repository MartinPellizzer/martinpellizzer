Amaryllis caiaponica

Amaryllis caiaponica, commonly known as the Mexican bell amaryllis, is a striking bulbous plant native to Central America.

It features large, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and red, often with a bell-shaped form. The plant produces strap-like, narrow leaves that are typically a deep green. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Amaryllis caiaponica is a popular choice for gardens and container planting due to its vibrant blooms and relatively easy care requirements.
