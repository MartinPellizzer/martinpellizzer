Athanasia flexuosa var. oligocephala

Athanasia flexuosa var. oligocephala, commonly known as the narrow-headed everlasting pea, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, branching stems and small, pea-like flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant produces small, round, woody pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and rocky outcrops.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
