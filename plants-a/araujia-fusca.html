Araujia fusca

Araujia fusca, commonly known as the nightshade vine, is a member of the Solanaceae family.

It is native to South America and is characterized by its climbing habit and showy, nocturnal flowers. The plant produces small, round berries that are typically green when mature. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Araujia fusca is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, thriving in warm, humid environments.
