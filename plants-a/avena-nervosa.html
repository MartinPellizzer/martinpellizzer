Avena nervosa

Avena nervosa, commonly known as slender oat, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Avena nervosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in ecological restoration projects.
