Anoectochilus pingbianensis

Anoectochilus pingbianensis, commonly known as the Pingbian orchid, is a rare and endemic orchid species native to China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its small, delicate flowers and epiphytic growth habit. The plant typically grows on tree bark in humid, shaded forest environments. Its botanical features include lance-shaped leaves and a compact inflorescence with pale yellow to white flowers.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species in conservation efforts.
