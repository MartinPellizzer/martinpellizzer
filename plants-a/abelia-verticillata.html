Abelia verticillata

Abelia verticillata, commonly known as the Chinese abelia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features opposite, ovate leaves that are typically dark green and have a smooth texture. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, tubular flowers in shades of pink and white during the summer months. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained soil.

Abelia verticillata is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various garden conditions.
