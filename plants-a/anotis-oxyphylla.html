Anotis oxyphylla

Anotis oxyphylla, commonly known as the mountain daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, often in mountainous regions. The plant features showy yellow flowers with a distinctive central disc, and it has narrow, linear leaves. It is adapted to dry conditions and can thrive in full sun.

Anotis oxyphylla is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
