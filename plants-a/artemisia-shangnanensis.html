Artemisia shangnanensis

Artemisia shangnanensis, commonly known as the Shangnan wormwood, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, silver-gray foliage. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are typically clustered in composite heads. It thrives in dry, rocky habitats and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its medicinal properties, it has been used in traditional Chinese medicine for various health purposes.
