Arenaria aggregata var. obtusifolia

Arenaria aggregata var. obtusifolia, commonly known as the bluntleaf sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to arid and semi-arid regions.

It typically features dense clusters of small, white flowers that bloom in early spring. The leaves are generally rounded and fleshy, adapted to conserve water in dry environments. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance in landscaping.
