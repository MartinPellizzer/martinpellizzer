Acacia blommaertii

Acacia blommaertii, commonly known as the Blommaert's acacia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in coastal and sandy soils. The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in cylindrical spikes, which are often clustered at the ends of branches. Its leaves are bipinnate, consisting of multiple small leaflets, and it is well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.
