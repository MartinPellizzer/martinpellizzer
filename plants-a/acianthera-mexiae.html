Acianthera mexiae

Acianthera mexiae, commonly known as the Mexican pink orchid, is a species of orchid native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, pinkish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern.
