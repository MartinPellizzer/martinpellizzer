Amphicalea gentianoides

Amphicalea gentianoides, commonly known as the mountain gentian, is a flowering plant belonging to the Gentianaceae family.

The plant is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers, which are typically tubular in shape and attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
