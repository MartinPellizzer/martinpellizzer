Axonopus elongatus

Axonopus elongatus, commonly known as slender axonopus, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically less than 10 cm in length. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually clustered in spikelets. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in lawns and disturbed areas.

Its rapid growth and dense foliage make it both a useful ground cover and a potential weed in cultivated landscapes.
