Adesmia glomerula var. australior

Adesmia glomerula var. australior, commonly known as the Australian clustered legume, is a perennial shrub native to southeastern Australia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces pods that contain lens-shaped seeds, which are often used in traditional Indigenous practices. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.

Due to its ecological importance and ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and restoration projects.
