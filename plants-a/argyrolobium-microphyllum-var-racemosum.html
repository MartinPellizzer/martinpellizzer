Argyrolobium microphyllum var. racemosum

Argyrolobium microphyllum var. racemosum, commonly known as the slenderleaf silverlobed burr.

It is a flowering plant native to South Africa, characterized by its narrow, silver-haired leaves and small, yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is adapted to arid conditions. It produces cylindrical pods that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
