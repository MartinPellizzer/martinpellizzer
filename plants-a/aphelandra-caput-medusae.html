Aphelandra caput-medusae

Aphelandra caput-medusae, commonly known as the Medusa's Head, is a striking tropical plant native to the Caribbean.

It is characterized by its long, arching stems and clusters of bright yellow, spathiflorous flowers that resemble a crown. The plant features large, heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and a deep green color. It thrives in humid, warm environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Aphelandra caput-medusae is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its unique and eye-catching appearance.
