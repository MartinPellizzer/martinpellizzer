Armeria maritima var. pujolii

Armeria maritima var. pujolii, commonly known as the Pujol's sea thrift, is a succulent flowering plant native to coastal regions of southern Europe.

It features dense, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-gray in color, adapted to retain moisture in arid environments. The plant produces clusters of white or pink flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for horticultural use.
