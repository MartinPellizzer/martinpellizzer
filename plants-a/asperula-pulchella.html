Asperula pulchella

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and rhododendrons. The plant is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. Asperula pulchella is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to grow in challenging environmental conditions.
