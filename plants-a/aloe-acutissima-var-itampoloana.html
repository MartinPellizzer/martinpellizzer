Aloe acutissima var. itampoloana

Aloe acutissima var. itampoloana, commonly known as the Itampoloana aloe, is a succulent plant native to Madagascar.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, with a waxy coating to reduce water loss. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow flowers, which bloom during the dry season. It is adapted to arid environments and thrives in full sun with well-draining soil.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
