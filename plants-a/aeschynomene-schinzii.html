Aeschynomene schinzii

Aeschynomene schinzii, commonly known as Schinz's goatherd's weed, is a perennial shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod-bearing fruit. The plant is notable for its ability to thrive in poor soil conditions, making it a hardy species. Its small, yellow flowers are followed by elongated, hairy pods containing several seeds.

Aeschynomene schinzii is often used in traditional medicine and as a forage crop for livestock.
