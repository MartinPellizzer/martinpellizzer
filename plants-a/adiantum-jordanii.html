Adiantum jordanii

Adiantum jordanii, commonly known as the Jordan fern, is a delicate, evergreen fern native to the southeastern United States.

It features finely divided, lace-like fronds that are typically light green with a silvery underside. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in rocky or wooded areas. Its rhizomes are short and produce a dense carpet of foliage.

Adiantum jordanii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
