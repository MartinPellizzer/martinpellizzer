Aucuba chinensis

Aucuba chinensis, commonly known as Japanese laurel, is a evergreen shrub or small tree native to eastern Asia.

It features glossy, dark green, oval-shaped leaves that are often variegated with yellow or white margins. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by red, fleshy berries that are attractive to birds. It thrives in partial to full shade and is adaptable to a variety of soil types.

Aucuba chinensis is valued in landscaping for its ornamental foliage and hardiness in temperate climates.
