Anthericum usseramense

Anthericum usseramense, commonly known as the Usseramen lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its strap-shaped, grass-like leaves. The plant produces clusters of white or pale pink flowers during the spring and summer months. Its bulbs are underground storage organs that help it survive in dry conditions.
