Arachnothryx armentalis

Arachnothryx armentalis, commonly known as the velvetleaf or Brazilian velvetleaf, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 10 meters in height. The plant features palmate leaves with three to five lobes, and its flowers are small, greenish-yellow, and clustered in panicles. It produces small, red fruits that contain several seeds.

The plant is often found in moist, shaded areas of tropical forests and is known for its ornamental value.
