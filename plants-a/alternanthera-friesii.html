Alternanthera friesii

Alternanthera friesii, commonly known as the red-stemmed Alternanthera, is a herbaceous plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking red stems and lance-shaped leaves that often display vibrant pink or red hues. The plant typically grows in wetlands and marshy areas, thriving in moist to aquatic conditions. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or yellow.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in water gardens and pond landscapes.
