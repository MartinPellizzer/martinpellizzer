Apicra Viscosa

Apicra viscosa is a rare and intriguing plant known for its unique appearance and ecological significance. Found primarily in humid, shaded environments, it thrives in regions with high moisture levels. Its sticky, resinous leaves have earned it the name "viscosa," meaning sticky in Latin. Despite its small size, it plays a vital role in supporting local biodiversity. This plant remains a subject of fascination for botanists and nature enthusiasts alike.
