Achimenes × baumannii

Achimenes × baumannii, commonly known as the hybrid water primrose, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, pink, and white. The plant typically grows in wet or marshy environments, thriving in moist soil conditions. Its leaves are heart-shaped and often have a waxy texture, while its stems are usually erect and branching.

This species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
