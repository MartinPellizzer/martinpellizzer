Anthurium lapathifolium

Anthurium lapathifolium, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a striking tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant crimson spathes that enclose a spadix, which is often adorned with small, yellowish flowers. The plant's foliage consists of large, heart-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in humid, warm environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.

Anthurium lapathifolium is popular as a houseplant due to its long-lasting blooms and ease of care.
