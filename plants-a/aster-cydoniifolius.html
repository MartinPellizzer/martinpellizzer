Aster cydoniifolius

Aster cydoniifolius, commonly known as the Cydonia-leaved aster, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of ray and disk florets. The plant features dark green, lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy surface, and its flowers bloom in shades of purple and white. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun, making it a hardy perennial in suitable climates.

Its ornamental value has led to its cultivation in gardens for its attractive foliage and blooms.
