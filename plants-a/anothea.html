Anothea

Anothea, commonly known as the blue mistflower, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Euphorbiaceae.

It is characterized by its showy, clustered inflorescences that resemble mist or clouds, giving it its common name. They are often cultivated for their ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
