Aspidistra paucitepala

Aspidistra paucitepala, commonly known as the narrow-leaved aspidistra, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a perennial evergreen plant characterized by its dark green, narrow, and leathery leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in shady, humid environments and is often used as an ornamental plant in shaded garden settings.

Aspidistra paucitepala is known for its ability to tolerate low light conditions and is relatively easy to care for.
