Alangium borneense

Alangium borneense, commonly known as the Bornean alangium, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that typically grows in lowland rainforests. The plant features simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. Its flowers are small and clustered in inflorescences, and it produces berries that contain a single seed.

Alangium borneense is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional medicinal uses.
