Anemone Fulgens

Anemone fulgens is a striking perennial plant known for its vibrant, showy flowers. Native to the eastern United States, it thrives in moist, shady environments along stream banks and woodland edges. Its large, colorful blooms come in shades of pink, red, and white, making it a popular choice for gardens and natural landscapes. This plant is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies. With its elegant appearance and ecological benefits, Anemone fulgens is a fascinating subject for both botanists and gardening enthusiasts.
