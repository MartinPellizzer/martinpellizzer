Aphelandra clava

Aphelandra clava, commonly known as the golden torch plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features striking, elongated leaves with a glossy dark green surface and prominent veins. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers that resemble torches, blooming during the warmer months. Aphelandra clava thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and high humidity to flourish.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardens and shaded outdoor spaces.
