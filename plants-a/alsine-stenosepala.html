Alsine stenosepala

Alsine stenosepala, commonly known as the narrow-sepaled sandmat, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of southern Africa.

The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and is adapted to withstand drought conditions. Its short stature and compact growth habit make it well-suited for xerophytic environments.

It is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and rocky outcrops.
