Aspalathus aemula

Aspalathus aemula, commonly known as the silverleaf bush, is a small shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its narrow, silvery-green leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in spring. It is often found in rocky soils and is adapted to dry, sunny environments.

Aspalathus aemula is also used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
