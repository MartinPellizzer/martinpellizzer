Amorphophallus bulbifer var. marmoratus

Amorphophallus bulbifer var. marmoratus, commonly known as the marble-stemmed arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, arrow-shaped leaves with a distinctive mottled appearance. The plant produces large, showy flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It is a member of the Araceae family, known for its unique inflorescence structure.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in warm, humid environments.
