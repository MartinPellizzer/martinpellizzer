Aeschynanthus roseus

Aeschynanthus roseus, commonly known as the pink flamingo plant, is a member of the Gesneriaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its striking, tubular, pink to red flowers. The plant produces showy, pendulous blooms that are often covered in fine, velvety hairs. Its leaves are typically elliptical and dark green, with a slightly waxy texture.

Aeschynanthus roseus is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its vibrant flowers and compact growth habit.
