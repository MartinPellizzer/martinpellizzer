Alchemilla Alpina Var. Transiens

Alpine lady's mantle, scientifically known as Alchemilla alpina var. transiens, is a resilient perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in alpine meadows and high-altitude environments. With its distinctive silver-green foliage and clusters of tiny, bright yellow flowers, it adds a touch of elegance to its natural habitat. The plant is named for its historical association with alchemists, who believed it held mystical properties. Its hardy nature makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and low-maintenance landscaping.
