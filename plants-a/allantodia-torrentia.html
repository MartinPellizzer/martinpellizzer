Allantodia torrentia

Allantodia torrentia, commonly known as the torrent alpine bellflower, is a perennial flowering plant native to high-altitude regions of the Andes.

It is characterized by its compact, cushion-like growth form, which helps it withstand harsh mountain conditions.
