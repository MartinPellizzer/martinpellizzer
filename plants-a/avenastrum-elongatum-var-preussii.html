Avenastrum elongatum var. preussii

Avenastrum elongatum var. preussii, commonly known as the slender wheatgrass, is a perennial grass species native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, elongated leaves and dense, grass-like growth habit. The plant typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
