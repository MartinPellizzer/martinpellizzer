Anthurium queirozianum

Anthurium queirozianum, commonly known as the Queirozianum arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to Colombia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves with a vibrant green color and prominent veins. The plant produces an inflorescence known as a spadix, which is surrounded by a colorful spathe. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect sunlight for optimal growth.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and ornamental purposes.
