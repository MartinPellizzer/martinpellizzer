Artemisia yunnanensis var. nujianensis

Artemisia yunnanensis var. nujianensis, commonly known as the Nujian Artemisia, is a perennial shrub native to the mountainous regions of Yunnan Province in China.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, dense hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flower heads that are typically clustered in panicles, blooming in the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in alpine or subalpine habitats.

This variety is valued for its aromatic properties and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
