Argyrolobium sericosemium

Argyrolobium sericosemium, commonly known as the silky burr vetch.

It is a flowering plant native to southern Africa, characterized by its hairy stems and small, yellow pea-like flowers. The plant produces small, spiny pods that contain seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky or sandy soils.

The species is adapted to drought conditions and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
