Anthurium guayaquilense

Anthurium guayaquilense, commonly known as the Guayaquil anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the coastal regions of Ecuador.

It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces striking spadices, which are the modified leaves that enclose the true flowers. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect sunlight for optimal growth.

Anthurium guayaquilense is often cultivated for its ornamental value in indoor and tropical gardens.
