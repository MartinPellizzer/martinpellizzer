Anthurium crassitepalum

Anthurium crassitepalum, commonly known as the giant anthurium, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 2 feet in length, often with a deep green color and prominent veins. The plant produces vibrant red spathes that enclose its yellow spadix, creating a dramatic floral display. It thrives in humid, warm environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium crassitepalum is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
