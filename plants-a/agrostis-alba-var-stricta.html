Agrostis alba var. stricta

Agrostis alba var. stricta, commonly known as slender bentgrass, is a grass species native to temperate regions of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its fine, tufted growth habit and narrow, bluish-green leaves. This plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows, lawns, and along stream banks. Its flowering stems produce small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny, wind-pollinated flowers.

Agrostis alba var. stricta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in horticultural settings.
