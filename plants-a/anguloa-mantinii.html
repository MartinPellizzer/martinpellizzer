Anguloa mantinii

Anguloa mantinii, commonly known as the Mantinii Anguloa, is a striking member of the Orchidaceae family native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically feature a vibrant pink or purple hue with intricate patterns. The plant thrives in high-altitude environments, often growing in moist, shaded areas with well-drained soil. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, enabling it to survive in its native habitat's variable climate.

Anguloa mantinii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated by enthusiasts for its unique floral display.
