Ajania elegantula

Ajania elegantula, commonly known as the elegant ajania, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, blue or purple flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens or alpine settings.

Due to its ornamental value and limited distribution, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
