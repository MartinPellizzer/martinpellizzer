Ajuga lycia

Ajuga lycia, commonly known as carpet bugle or silverweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the plantain family (Plantaginaceae) and is characterized by its low-growing, spreading habit. The plant features dark green, ovate leaves with a silvery underside, and it produces small, tubular flowers in shades of blue or purple. Ajuga lycia thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas or along stream banks.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to form dense ground cover.
