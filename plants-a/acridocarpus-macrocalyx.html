Acridocarpus macrocalyx

Acridocarpus macrocalyx, commonly known as the giant flame tree, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy orange-red flowers that bloom in clusters, creating a striking visual display. The plant has compound leaves composed of several oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The tree is also valued for its hard, durable wood used in construction and furniture-making.
