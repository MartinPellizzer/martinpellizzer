Agrostis Canina Var. Varians

Agrostis canina var. varians is a lesser-known grass species that thrives in moist, nutrient-rich soils. It is often found in meadows and along stream banks, where it plays a vital role in stabilizing the ecosystem. This variant of Agrostis canina is notable for its subtle variations in leaf structure and growth patterns. Despite its modest appearance, it supports a diverse range of insect and bird life. Understanding this plant helps in appreciating the complexity of natural habitats.
