Aretiastrum sedifolium

Aretiastrum sedifolium, commonly known as the woolly leaf aster, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of South America.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that helps it retain moisture in harsh environments. The plant produces small, daisy-like flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is a popular choice for xeriscaping and native plant gardens.
