Amaryllis nervosa

Amaryllis nervosa, commonly known as the veined amaryllis, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to deep red, often with intricate veining. The plant produces a tall, sturdy stem with several leaves that are broad and strap-like. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Amaryllis nervosa is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
