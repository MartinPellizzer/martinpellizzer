Austrobuxus montis-do

Austrobuxus montis-do, commonly known as the Mountain Bush, is a shrub native to the temperate regions of southern South America.

It features dense, leathery leaves that are typically dark green and have a glossy appearance. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers in clusters during the spring season. Its berries are small, red, and edible, often consumed by birds and other wildlife.

Austrobuxus montis-do is well adapted to rocky and well-drained soils, making it a hardy plant in mountainous environments.
