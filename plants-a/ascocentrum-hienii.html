Ascocentrum hienii

Ascocentrum hienii, commonly known as the Hien's arrowroot.

It is a species of flowering plant in the Araceae family, native to the Philippines. The plant features a short, stocky rhizome with broad, arrow-shaped leaves that are dark green and glossy. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers in a spadix, surrounded by a modified leaf called a spathe.

Ascocentrum hienii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
