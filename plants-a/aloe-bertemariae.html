Aloe bertemariae

Aloe bertemariae, commonly known as the Berta's aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a waxy coating. The plant produces tall, branching inflorescences with yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Aloe bertemariae is valued both for its ornamental appeal and its potential medicinal properties.
