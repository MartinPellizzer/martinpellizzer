Achimenes hintoniana

Achimenes hintoniana, commonly known as the Hinton's achimenes, is a herbaceous perennial native to South America.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that come in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and produces showy bracts that enhance its ornamental appeal. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in greenhouse settings.

Due to its delicate nature, it requires careful attention to light and watering conditions for optimal growth.
