Ageratum mexicanum rubrum

Ageratum mexicanum rubrum, commonly known as red ageratum, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, clustered flowers. The plant features velvety, ovate leaves that are typically gray-green in color. Its flowers are usually pink to red in hue, with a central disc surrounded by ray florets.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its vibrant colors and ability to thrive in various garden conditions.
