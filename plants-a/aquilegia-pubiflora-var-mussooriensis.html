Aquilegia pubiflora var. mussooriensis

Aquilegia pubiflora var. mussooriensis, commonly known as the Mussoorie Himalayan Columbine, is a flowering plant native to the alpine regions of the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its distinctive spurred petals and vibrant, often purple or crimson blooms. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments at high altitudes. Its leaves are basal, compound, and covered in fine, soft hairs.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in mountain ecosystems.
