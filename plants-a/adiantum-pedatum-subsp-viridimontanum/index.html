Adiantum Pedatum Subsp. Viridimontanum

Adiantum pedatum subsp. viridimontanum is a rare and striking fern native to the eastern united states. it is known for its delicate, lace-like fronds that shimmer in the sunlight. this subspecies thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for botanical gardens and conservation efforts. due to habitat loss, it faces threats to its survival and requires careful protection.
