Anthurium unense

Anthurium unense, commonly known as the "Red Anthurium," is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically deep green with a reddish or purplish tint. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-like inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, which can range from red to orange. It thrives in humid, warm environments with bright, indirect light.

Anthurium unense is popular as a decorative plant due to its aesthetic appeal and air-purifying qualities.
