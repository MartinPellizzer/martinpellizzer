Aponogeton bogneri

Aponogeton bogneri, commonly known as the Bogner's arrowhead, is a aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its arrowhead-shaped leaves that are typically green with a waxy surface. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom above the water surface. It thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments.

Aponogeton bogneri is often cultivated as an ornamental plant in ponds and water gardens.
