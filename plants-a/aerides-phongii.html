Aerides phongii

Aerides phongii, commonly known as the Phongii orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, fibrous root system and its ability to thrive in moist, shaded environments. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, pale yellow to greenish-white flowers during the rainy season. Its leaves are typically broad and strap-like, arranged in a rosette pattern.

Aerides phongii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
