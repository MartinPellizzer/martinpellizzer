Aloe vera

Aloe vera, commonly known as the African Lily, is a succulent plant native to the arid regions of Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in dry environments. The gel inside the leaves contains various nutrients and has been used for centuries for its medicinal properties. It is widely cultivated for both ornamental and therapeutic purposes.

Its ability to regenerate and its resilience make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
