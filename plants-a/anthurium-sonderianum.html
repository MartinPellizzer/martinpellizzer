Anthurium sonderianum

Anthurium sonderianum, commonly known as the Sonder's anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-like inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, often in shades of red or orange. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.

Anthurium sonderianum is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
