Aloe clarkei

Aloe clarkei, commonly known as the Clarke's aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, with a waxy coating to reduce water loss. The plant produces tall, branched flowering stems with yellow-orange flowers, which bloom in summer. It is adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water, making it drought-tolerant.

Aloe clarkei is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and resilience.
