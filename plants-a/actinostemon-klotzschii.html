Actinostemon klotzschii

Actinostemon klotzschii, commonly known as the mountain bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the high elevations of the Andes in South America.

The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, where it thrives in cool, moist conditions. Its flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
