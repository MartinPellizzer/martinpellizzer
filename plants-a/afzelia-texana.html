Afzelia texana

Afzelia texana, commonly known as the Texas afzelia, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to the southeastern United States.

It typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in areas with partial to full sun. The plant has simple, alternate, elliptic to ovate leaves that are usually glossy and dark green. Its small, fragrant flowers are white or pale pink and appear in clusters, followed by woody, elongated pods containing several seeds.

Afzelia texana is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to thrive in a variety of garden settings.
