Aloe lineata var. lineata

Aloe lineata var. lineata, commonly known as the Zebra Aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features striking, linear leaves that are typically blue-green in color with distinct white or yellowish markings. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with small, tubular flowers that range from yellow to orange in color. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
