Alsophila australis var. pallida

Alsophila australis var. pallida, commonly known as the pale tree fern, is a variant of the tree fern native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate fronds that can reach several meters in length. The plant exhibits a slow growth rate and thrives in moist, shaded environments with well-drained soil. Its fronds are typically a pale green, distinguishing it from other varieties of Alsophila.

This species plays an important ecological role in its native habitat, providing shelter and food for various wildlife species.
