Adesmia philippiana

Adesmia philippiana, commonly known as the Philippine legume, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, with a distinctive yellow or white coloration.

Adesmia philippiana is adapted to tropical and subtropical climates, often found in moist, well-drained soils in forested areas.
