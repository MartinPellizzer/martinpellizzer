Anthurium concolor

Anthurium concolor, commonly known as the velvet leaf anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are dark green with a velvety texture. The plant produces bright red spadices, which are the modified leaves that house the tiny, inconspicuous flowers. Anthurium concolor thrives in humid, warm environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

It is popular as a decorative plant due to its ornamental foliage and ability to purify indoor air.
