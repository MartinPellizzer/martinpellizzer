Ailanthus fauveliana

Ailanthus fauveliana, commonly known as the Fauvel's tree of heaven, is a species of flowering plant in the Simaroubaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its compound leaves with long, slender leaflets. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are followed by distinctive, three-lobed fruits. It is a fast-growing tree that can reach heights of up to 20 meters.
