Acacia buxifolia subsp. pubiflora

Acacia buxifolia subsp.

pubiflora, commonly known as the silky acacia, is a shrub native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its dense, leathery foliage and small, yellow flowers that appear in globular clusters.

The plant produces flat, woody pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
