Anthemis cretica subsp. saportana

Anthemis cretica subsp.

saportana, commonly known as the Narrow-leaved Pyrethrum, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region. It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, yellow-orange flowers.

The plant is part of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is known for its aromatic properties. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in herbal medicine.
