Astragalus neofedtschenkoanus

Astragalus neofedtschenkoanus, commonly known as the Neofedtschenkoan milkvetch, is a flowering plant species native to central Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its small, yellow pea-like flowers. The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments, showcasing adaptations to harsh, dry conditions. Its leaves are compound, with small, oval-shaped leaflets, and it produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds.

This species is of interest to botanists due to its ecological significance and potential medicinal properties.
