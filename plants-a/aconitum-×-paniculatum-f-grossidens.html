Aconitum × paniculatum f. grossidens

Aconitum × paniculatum f. grossidens, commonly known as the Giant Monkshood, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Ranunculaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, striking purple-black flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant is native to the mountainous regions of central and eastern Europe. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Its foliage consists of palmately compound leaves with serrated edges, adding to its ornamental value in gardens.
