Anthemis gaudium-solis

Anthemis gaudium-solis, commonly known as the sun's joy, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in sunny, well-drained soils. The plant produces bright yellow flowers with a distinctive central disk, which attract various pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in coastal areas.

The plant is also used in traditional herbal medicine for its purported calming properties.
