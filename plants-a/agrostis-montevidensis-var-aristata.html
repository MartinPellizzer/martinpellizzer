Agrostis montevidensis var. aristata

Agrostis montevidensis var. aristata, commonly known as the Argentine bentgrass, is a perennial grass species native to South America.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and slender, wiry stems. The leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green coloration. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in grassy areas and along streambanks.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping.
