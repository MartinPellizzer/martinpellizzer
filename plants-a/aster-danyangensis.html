Aster danyangensis

Aster danyangensis, commonly known as the Danyang aster, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of small, blue to purple flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
