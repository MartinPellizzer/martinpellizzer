Aeschynanthus lasiocalyx

Aeschynanthus lasiocalyx, commonly known as the velvet tangerine.

This evergreen shrub is native to western Africa, particularly Nigeria and Cameroon. It is characterized by its pendulous, tubular flowers that are typically orange or red in color. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is well-suited for hanging baskets or as a container plant.
