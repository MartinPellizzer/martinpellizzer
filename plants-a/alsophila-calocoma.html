Alsophila calocoma

Alsophila calocoma, commonly known as the golden tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds that can reach several meters in length. The plant has a distinctive yellowish-brown coloration, which gives it its common name. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical forests.

Alsophila calocoma plays an important ecological role by providing habitat and shelter for various small animals and insects.
