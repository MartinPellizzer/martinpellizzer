Anthurium laingi

Anthurium laingi, commonly known as the Laing's anthurium, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its unique inflorescence consisting of a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe. The plant features waxy, heart-shaped leaves that are typically deep green with a metallic sheen. Its flowers are usually bright red or orange, with a spadix that can grow up to 30 centimeters in length.

Anthurium laingi thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
