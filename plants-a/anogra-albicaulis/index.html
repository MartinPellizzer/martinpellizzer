Anogra Albicaulis

White-stemmed anogra, scientifically known as Anogra albicaulis, is a rare and striking plant native to the high-altitude regions of South America. Its most distinguishing feature is its pale, almost white stem, which contrasts sharply with its vibrant green leaves. This unique appearance makes it a subject of fascination for botanists and plant enthusiasts alike. Despite its beauty, the species faces threats from habitat loss and climate change. Conservation efforts are underway to protect this delicate and enigmatic flora.
