Aster puniceus var. oligocephalus

Aster puniceus var. oligocephalus, commonly known as the redhead aster, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the northeastern United States.

It features showy, conical flower heads that are typically pink to red in color, with narrow, linear leaves. The plant thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained, sandy or loamy soils. It is known for its ornamental value and is often used in native plant gardens.

The species is part of the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads and robust root systems.
