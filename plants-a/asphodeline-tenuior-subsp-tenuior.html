Asphodeline tenuior subsp. tenuior

Asphodeline tenuior subsp.

tenuior, commonly known as the slender asphodel, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa. It features narrow, grass-like leaves that are often bluish-green in color.

The plant produces tall, slender flowering stems with small, white or pale pink flowers arranged in a panicle. It is adapted to arid and rocky environments, showing drought tolerance.

Its botanical structure reflects its evolutionary adaptation to survive in harsh, sun-exposed conditions.
