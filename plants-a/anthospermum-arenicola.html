Anthospermum arenicola

Anthospermum arenicola, commonly known as the sand dune star.

It is a flowering plant native to arid and coastal regions, particularly in southern Africa. The plant is adapted to sandy soils and can thrive in harsh, saline environments. Its small, star-shaped flowers are typically white or pale blue, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits.

It plays an important ecological role in stabilizing sand dunes and preventing erosion.
