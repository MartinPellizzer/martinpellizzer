Artemisia kawakamii

Artemisia kawakamii, commonly known as Kawakami's wormwood, is a perennial shrub native to Japan.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, silver-gray foliage. The plant produces small, yellowish flower heads that are clustered in flat-topped inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.

Artemisia kawakamii is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
