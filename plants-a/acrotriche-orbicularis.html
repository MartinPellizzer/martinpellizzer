Acrotriche orbicularis

Acrotriche orbicularis, commonly known as the orbicular acrotriche, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, thick leaves that are often arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. It thrives in arid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
