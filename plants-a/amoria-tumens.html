Amoria tumens

Amoria tumens, commonly known as the swollen amoria, is a perennial herb native to the temperate regions of Europe.

It features erect, hairy stems that can grow up to 60 centimeters in height. The plant produces clusters of small, pale greenish-yellow flowers during the summer months. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and covered in fine pubescence.

Amoria tumens is often found in moist meadows and along stream banks, where it thrives in partial shade to full sun.
