Aconitum liljestrandii var. falcatum

Aconitum liljestrandii var. falcatum, commonly known as the slender aconite, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features striking blue to purple flowers that bloom in late summer, typically from July to September. The plant has palmately compound leaves that are deeply lobed and often have a feathery appearance. It thrives in well-drained, moist soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Due to its toxicity, it should be handled with care and is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value.
