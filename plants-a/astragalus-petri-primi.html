Astragalus petri-primi

Astragalus petri-primi, commonly known as the first-year petri bean, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its small, pea-like flowers that typically bloom in early spring. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia and thrives in rocky, well-drained soils.
