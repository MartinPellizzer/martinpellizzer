Aristida tenuissima

Aristida tenuissima, commonly known as slender threeawn, is a grass species native to arid and semi-arid regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its fine, wiry blades and small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is drought-tolerant. It has a low-growing habit and often forms dense mats.

Its botanical features make it an important component of native plant communities in its habitat.
