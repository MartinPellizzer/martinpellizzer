Anthurium surinamense

Anthurium surinamense, commonly known as the Surinam anthurium, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-like inflorescences that are often surrounded by colorful bracts. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium surinamense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated as a houseplant.
