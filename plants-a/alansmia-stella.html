Alansmia stella

Alansmia stella, commonly known as the star alansmia, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

It is a member of the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are usually narrow and covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.

Alansmia stella is known for its vibrant pink to purple blooms that appear in late spring to early summer.
