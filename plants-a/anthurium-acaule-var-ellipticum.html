Anthurium acaule var. ellipticum

Anthurium acaule var. ellipticum, commonly known as the velvet leaf anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a velvety texture. The plant produces striking spadices, which are the modified leaves that enclose the inflorescence. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Anthurium acaule var. ellipticum is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
