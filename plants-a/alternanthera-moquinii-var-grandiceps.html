Alternanthera moquinii var. grandiceps

Alternanthera moquinii var. grandiceps, commonly known as the redstem alternanthera, is a striking aquatic plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red stems and lance-shaped leaves that often display a striking contrast of colors. This species thrives in shallow, slow-moving freshwater environments and prefers moderate to high light conditions. Its rapid growth rate makes it a popular choice for aquascaping, where it can be used to create dynamic visual effects.

The plant is also known for its ability to produce small, inconspicuous flowers that bloom in late summer.
