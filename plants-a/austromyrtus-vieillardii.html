Austromyrtus vieillardii

Austromyrtus vieillardii, commonly known as the Tasmanian myrtle, is a small evergreen shrub native to Tasmania.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family, which includes eucalypts and other aromatic plants. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and slightly hairy on the underside. Its flowers are small, white or pale pink, and are clustered in dense inflorescences.

Austromyrtus vieillardii is known for its fragrant foliage and is often found in moist, shaded environments.
