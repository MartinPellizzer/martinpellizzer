Aridaria elongata

Aridaria elongata, commonly known as the elongated desert grass, is a resilient plant adapted to arid environments.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves that minimize water loss through transpiration. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or pale in color. Its root system is extensive, allowing it to access deep groundwater.

Aridaria elongata plays a crucial role in stabilizing sandy soils and providing minimal forage for desert wildlife.
