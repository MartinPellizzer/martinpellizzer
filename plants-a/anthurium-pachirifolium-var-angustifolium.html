Anthurium pachirifolium var. angustifolium

Anthurium pachirifolium var. angustifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaf anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a velvety texture. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-shaped inflorescences surrounded by a showy spathe, which can range in color from white to red. It thrives in humid, warm environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.

This variety is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
