Abutilon behrianum

Abutilon behrianum, commonly known as the velvetleaf abutilon, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 3 meters in height. The plant is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that are velvety to the touch, giving it its common name. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, with five petals and a distinctive central column.

The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind.
