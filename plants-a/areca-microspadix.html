Areca microspadix

Areca microspadix, commonly known as the small-spadix areca, is a species of palm native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, unbranched stem and a single, compact inflorescence that bears both male and female flowers. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in a spadix, typical of the Araceae family. Its leaves are long and pinnately compound, providing a feathery appearance.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable tropical environments.
