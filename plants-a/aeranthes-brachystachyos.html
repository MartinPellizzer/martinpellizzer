Aeranthes brachystachyos

Aeranthes brachystachyos, commonly known as the short-stalked spider orchid, is a species of orchid native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and distinctive, spider-like flower structure. The plant typically features a single, upright stem with a few leaves and a cluster of small, greenish-white flowers. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and prefers a sunny to partially shaded environment.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
