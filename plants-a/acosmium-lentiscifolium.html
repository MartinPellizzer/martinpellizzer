Acosmium lentiscifolium

Acosmium lentiscifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaved acosmium, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, lance-shaped leaves that are typically covered in a fine, white pubescence. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by spherical, woody fruits. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and scrubland habitats.

Due to its hardy nature, it is occasionally cultivated for ornamental purposes in gardens.
