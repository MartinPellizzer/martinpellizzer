Allium inops

Allium inops, commonly known as the slender leek, is a perennial herb native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Allium genus, which includes onions, garlic, and chives. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, white or pale purple flowers in umbels. It is typically found in rocky or arid environments, thriving in well-drained soils.

Allium inops is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
