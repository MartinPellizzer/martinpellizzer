Afzelia parviflora

Afzelia parviflora, commonly known as the small-flowered afzelia, is a tropical tree belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is native to regions of Africa, particularly in countries like Nigeria and Ghana. The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and small, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in clusters. Afzelia parviflora produces hard, woody seeds enclosed in pods, which contribute to its ecological role in forest ecosystems.

It is valued for its durable timber and is often found in mixed woodland and secondary forest habitats.
