Anthurium hayanum

Anthurium hayanum, commonly known as the Hainan anthurium, is a tropical plant native to China's Hainan Island.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves and vibrant, spadix-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its foliage is often a deep green, while the spathe can range from red to yellow, depending on the variety.

Anthurium hayanum is appreciated for its ornamental value and is cultivated for its unique and colorful floral displays.
