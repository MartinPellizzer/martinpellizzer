Aria umbellata

Aria umbellata, commonly known as the umbrella plant, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, umbrella-like inflorescence composed of numerous white or pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and often have a waxy texture.

Aria umbellata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
