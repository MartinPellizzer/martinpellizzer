Anisum

Anisum, commonly known as star anise, is a flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is native to regions of China, Vietnam, and other parts of Southeast Asia. The plant produces small, star-shaped fruits that are used as a spice and in traditional medicine. The seeds inside the fruit contain essential oils that contribute to its aromatic properties.

Anisum is valued for its distinctive licorice-like flavor and its role in both culinary and medicinal applications.
