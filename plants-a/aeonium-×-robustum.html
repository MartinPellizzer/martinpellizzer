Aeonium × robustum

Aeonium × robustum, commonly known as the Chocolate Soldier, is a hybrid succulent plant native to the Canary Islands.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically dark green with a reddish or purplish hue, especially under bright light. The plant produces tall, branching stems with rosettes of leaves that can grow up to 3 feet in height. It is known for its striking appearance and ability to thrive in well-draining soil with minimal water.

Aeonium × robustum is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its unique foliage and resilience in arid conditions.
