Armeria alliacea var. bilbilitana

Armeria alliacea var. bilbilitana, commonly known as the Bilbilitan thrift, is a flowering plant native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces dense clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers. The plant has succulent, linear leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white wax. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming period, typically from spring to early summer.
