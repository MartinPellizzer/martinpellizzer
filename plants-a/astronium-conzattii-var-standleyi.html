Astronium conzattii var. standleyi

Astronium conzattii var. standleyi, commonly known as the red cedar or Brazilian red cedar, is a member of the family Burseraceae.

It is native to South America, particularly Brazil, Colombia, and Venezuela, and thrives in tropical rainforests. The fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release seeds, contributing to its role in forest ecosystems.
