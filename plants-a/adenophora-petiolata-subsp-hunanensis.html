Adenophora petiolata subsp. hunanensis

Adenophora petiolata subsp.

hunanensis, commonly known as the Hunan bellflower, is a perennial herb native to China. It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale blue.

The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

The subspecies is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for garden use.
