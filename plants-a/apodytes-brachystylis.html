Apodytes brachystylis

Apodytes brachystylis, commonly known as the narrow-stemmed sand mat, is a low-growing shrub native to arid and semi-arid regions of southern Africa.

It has small, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white hairs, which help reduce water loss in hot, dry conditions. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color, followed by small, spherical fruits. It is well adapted to sandy soils and can survive in environments with limited rainfall.

Due to its hardy nature, it is often used in xeriscaping and for stabilizing dunes.
