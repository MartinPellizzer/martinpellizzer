Artemisia yunnanensis

Artemisia yunnanensis, commonly known as Yunnan wormwood, is a perennial shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, silver-gray foliage. The plant produces small, yellowish to brownish flower heads that are typically clustered in dense, spherical shapes. It thrives in dry, well-drained soils and is often found in alpine or rocky habitats.

Due to its medicinal properties, it has been used in traditional Chinese medicine for various health purposes.
