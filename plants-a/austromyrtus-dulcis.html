Austromyrtus dulcis

Austromyrtus dulcis, commonly known as the Tasmanian pepper tree, is a small evergreen tree native to Tasmania.

It features smooth, grey-barked trunks and elliptical, glossy green leaves. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in spring, followed by red, fleshy fruits. It is adapted to temperate climates and is often found in coastal and moist forest areas.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
