Arenaria linearifolia

Arenaria linearifolia, commonly known as the linear-leaved sandwort, is a flowering plant native to arctic and alpine regions.

It is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit and small, clustered flowers. The plant has linear, grass-like leaves that are adapted to withstand cold and windy conditions. It thrives in well-drained, rocky or sandy soils and is often found in tundra and mountainous areas.

Arenaria linearifolia plays a role in stabilizing soil in harsh environments and is an important part of the ecological community in its habitat.
