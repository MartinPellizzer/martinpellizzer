Asplenium lankongense

Asplenium lankongense, commonly known as the Lankong fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to eastern Africa.

It belongs to the family Aspleniaceae and is characterized by its compact, rosette-like growth habit. The plant features lance-shaped, leathery fronds that are typically dark green with a glossy appearance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories or on rocky outcrops.

Asplenium lankongense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens.
