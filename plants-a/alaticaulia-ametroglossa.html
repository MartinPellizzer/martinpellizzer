Alaticaulia ametroglossa

Alaticaulia ametroglossa, commonly known as the slender-stemmed alaticaulia, is a rare shrub native to specific regions of South America.

It is characterized by its slender, woody stems and small, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, white pubescence. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, which are typically borne on short pedicels. Its botanical features make it well adapted to dry, rocky habitats where it can thrive with minimal water.
