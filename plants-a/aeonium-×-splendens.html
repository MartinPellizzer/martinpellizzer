Aeonium × splendens

Aeonium × splendens, commonly known as the Crown of Thorns, is a hybrid succulent plant native to the Canary Islands.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with red or purple hues along the edges. The plant produces tall, branching inflorescences that bear yellow flowers in the spring. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with plenty of sunlight.

Its striking appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
