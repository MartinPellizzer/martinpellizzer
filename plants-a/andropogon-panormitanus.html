Andropogon panormitanus

Andropogon panormitanus, commonly known as the Panormitanus grass, is a perennial grass species native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to temperate climates. The plant features narrow, blade-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed areas.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes used in landscaping for erosion control.
