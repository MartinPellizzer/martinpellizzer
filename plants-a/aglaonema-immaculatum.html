Aglaonema immaculatum

Aglaonema immaculatum, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, waxy leaves with striking white and silver variegation. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening. Its foliage remains attractive throughout the year, requiring minimal maintenance.

Aglaonema immaculatum is also noted for its air-purifying qualities, contributing to improved indoor air quality.
