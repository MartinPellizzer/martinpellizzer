Arisaema angustifoliolatum var. serratifolium

Arisaema angustifoliolatum var. serratifolium, commonly known as the serrated leaf arisaema, is a member of the Araceae family.

It is native to southern China and is characterized by its distinctive, palmate leaves with serrated margins. The plant produces a spadix surrounded by a spathe, a typical feature of the arum family. It thrives in shady, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value is due to its unique foliage and dramatic inflorescence.
