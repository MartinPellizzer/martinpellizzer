Asterocephalus luteus

Asterocephalus luteus, commonly known as the yellow starhead, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features a basal rosette of narrow, gray-green leaves and produces bright yellow ray florets surrounded by a central disk of smaller flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Asterocephalus luteus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its striking appearance.
