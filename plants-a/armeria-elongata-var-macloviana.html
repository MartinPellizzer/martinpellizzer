Armeria elongata var. macloviana

Armeria elongata var. macloviana, commonly known as the silver carpet plant, is a low-growing, mat-forming perennial native to southern Europe.

It features dense clusters of small, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has succulent, silver-gray foliage that is covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and alpine settings.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking a drought-tolerant, decorative plant.
