Alchemilla orbiculata f. villifera

Alchemilla orbiculata f. villifera, commonly known as the hairy lady's mantle, is a variant of the lady's mantle plant characterized by its densely hairy foliage.

This perennial plant belongs to the Rosaceae family and is native to temperate regions of Europe. Its leaves are typically rounded and covered in fine, soft hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in early summer, which are clustered in loose inflorescences.

It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive foliage and ease of cultivation.
