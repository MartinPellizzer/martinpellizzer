Anotis capitata

Anotis capitata, commonly known as the mountain daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, spherical inflorescence. The plant produces small, daisy-like flowers that range in color from white to pink, depending on the variety. It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to dry, sunny environments.

Anotis capitata is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to grow in gardens with well-draining soil.
