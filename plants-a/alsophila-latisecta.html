Alsophila latisecta

Alsophila latisecta, commonly known as the broad-leaf tree fern, is a species native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feathery fronds that can reach several meters in length. The plant has a distinctive crown of pinnate leaves, giving it a graceful, tree-like appearance. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Its slow growth rate and unique morphology make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
