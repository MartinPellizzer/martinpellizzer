Artemisia hyrcana

Artemisia hyrcana, commonly known as the Hyrcanian wormwood, is a perennial herb native to temperate regions of Asia and Europe.

It is characterized by its aromatic, silver-gray foliage composed of finely divided, needle-like leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flower heads that are clustered in dense, spherical inflorescences. Artemisia hyrcana thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas.

It is valued for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally in herbal remedies.
