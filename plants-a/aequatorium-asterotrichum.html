Aequatorium asterotrichum

Aequatorium asterotrichum, commonly known as the star-haired aequatorium, is a flowering plant native to specific regions of South America.

It belongs to the family Aequatoriaceae and is characterized by its unique, star-like hairs on its stems and leaves. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, arranged in clusters. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
