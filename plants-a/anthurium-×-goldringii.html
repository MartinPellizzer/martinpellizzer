Anthurium × goldringii

Anthurium × goldringii, commonly known as the Gold Ring Anthurium, is a hybrid plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are often a deep green with a metallic sheen. The plant produces vibrant, spatulate-shaped spathes that can range in color from red to orange, depending on the variety. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium × goldringii is popular as a decorative plant due to its ornamental foliage and long-lasting blooms.
