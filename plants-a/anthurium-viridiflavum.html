Anthurium viridiflavum

Anthurium viridiflavum, commonly known as the Green-Yellow Anthurium, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped leaves that range from deep green to yellowish-green in color. The plant produces spadices, which are specialized inflorescences that consist of a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe. Anthurium viridiflavum thrives in high humidity and indirect light, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique foliage and relatively easy care requirements contribute to its appeal among plant enthusiasts.
