Arisaema jingdongense

Arisaema jingdongense, commonly known as the Jingdong arrowhead, is a rare flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its distinctive arrow-shaped leaves and spadix inflorescence. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as mountain forests. Its flowers are unisexual, with separate male and female parts, and it produces berries as its fruit.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species.
