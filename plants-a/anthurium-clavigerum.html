Anthurium clavigerum

Anthurium clavigerum, commonly known as the sword anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, elongated spathes that can range in color from deep red to orange, often with a contrasting green or yellow midrib. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers inside the spathe, which are followed by bright red berries. It thrives in humid, warm environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and ornamental purposes.
