Anthurium roraimense

Anthurium roraimense, commonly known as the Roraima arrowhead, is a striking tropical plant native to the dense rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often brightly colored, ranging from deep green to vibrant red. The plant produces striking spadices, which are specialized inflorescences that serve as the flower cluster. Anthurium roraimense thrives in high humidity and indirect light, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Its unique appearance and ornamental value make it a sought-after plant among horticulture enthusiasts.
