Ardisia lindleyana

Ardisia lindleyana, commonly known as the Chinese beautyberry, is a deciduous shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces small, white flowers in clusters during the spring, followed by vibrant purple berries in the fall. These berries are a key feature, attracting birds and other wildlife.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
