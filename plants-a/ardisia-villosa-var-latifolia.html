Ardisia villosa var. latifolia

Ardisia villosa var. latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf ardisia, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, ovate to elliptical leaves that are typically dark green and have a velvety texture. The plant produces small, white to pinkish flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by bright red berries in the fall. It thrives in shady, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation in tropical and subtropical regions.
