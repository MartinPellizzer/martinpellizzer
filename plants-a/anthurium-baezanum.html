Anthurium baezanum

Anthurium baezanum, commonly known as the Baezana Anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central America.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its unique inflorescence consisting of a spadix and a spathe. The plant features heart-shaped leaves that are typically deep green with a glossy texture. Its flowers are usually pale green or white, with a prominent spadix that can vary in color depending on the variety.

Anthurium baezanum is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and relatively low maintenance requirements.
