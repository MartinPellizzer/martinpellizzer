Argyrolobium abyssinicum var. garamantum

Argyrolobium abyssinicum var. garamantum, commonly known as the Garamantian silverlobed burr.

It is a perennial legume native to the Horn of Africa, characterized by its silver-gray foliage and distinctive, spiny pods. The plant thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky soils. Its flowers are typically pink or violet, and it plays a role in soil stabilization due to its deep root system.

It is also valued for its ornamental appeal and potential medicinal uses.
