Amaryllis zeylanica

Amaryllis zeylanica, commonly known as the Ceylon amaryllis, is a bulbous plant native to Sri Lanka.

It features strap-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and narrow. The plant produces clusters of white or pale pink flowers with a waxy texture. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Amaryllis zeylanica is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
