Areca monostachya

Areca monostachya, commonly known as the narrow-leaf areca, is a tropical palm native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, unbranched trunk and pinnate leaves that are narrow and elongated. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers arranged in a single, slender inflorescence, which is a distinguishing feature. Its fruit is a drupe that matures to a reddish color, containing a single seed.

Areca monostachya is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its elegant appearance and adaptability to various growing conditions.
