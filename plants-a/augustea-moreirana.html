Augustea moreirana

Augustea moreirana, commonly known as the Cape Town daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy yellow flowers surrounded by white ray florets. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and is well adapted to sandy soils and coastal climates. It produces small, oval-shaped fruits called cypsela, which are dispersed by wind.

Augustea moreirana is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and public spaces.
