Adiantum edentulum

Adiantum edentulum, commonly known as the fan-leaf maidenhair fern, is a delicate and elegant fern native to the southeastern United States.

Its leaves are characterized by their lack of teeth or serrations, giving it its scientific name "edentulum," meaning "toothless.".
