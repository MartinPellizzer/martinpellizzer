Arisaema lichiangense

Arisaema lichiangense, commonly known as the Lijiang arrowhead, is a species of flowering plant native to the Yunnan Province in China.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive inflorescence consisting of a spadix surrounded by a spathe. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as mountain forests and rocky areas. Its leaves are arrow-shaped, hence the common name, and the plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers.

Arisaema lichiangense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional Chinese medicine.
