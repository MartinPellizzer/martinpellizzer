Alchemilla ophioreina

Alchemilla ophioreina, commonly known as the silverweed, is a low-growing perennial plant belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, it thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.

Alchemilla ophioreina is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive appearance and easy maintenance.
