Arisaema grapsospadix

Arisaema grapsospadix, commonly known as the narrow-leaf arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, arrow-shaped leaves that are usually narrow and pointed. The plant produces a spadix, a type of inflorescence, which is surrounded by a large, showy spathe. Its botanical features include a rhizomatous underground stem and a preference for moist, shaded environments.

Arisaema grapsospadix is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique foliage structure.
