Anacamptis pyramidalis var. condensata

Anacamptis pyramidalis var. condensata, commonly known as the condensed pyramidal orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its compact, pyramidal inflorescence. The plant produces showy, spurred orchid flowers in shades of violet and white, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in sunny, well-drained habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas.

The variety condensata is distinguished by its more compact growth habit compared to the typical form of Anacamptis pyramidalis.
