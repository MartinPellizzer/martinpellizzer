Alstroemeria apertiflora f. lucidior

Alstroemeria apertiflora f. lucidior, commonly known as the brightleaf alstroemeria, is a hybrid form of the alstroemeria plant characterized by its glossy, waxy leaves.

This cultivar is prized for its vibrant, long-lasting flowers that come in a range of colors, including red, orange, and yellow. The plant is native to South America and thrives in temperate climates with well-drained soil. It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes in gardens and floral arrangements.

Its foliage adds a striking visual element, complementing its showy blooms.
