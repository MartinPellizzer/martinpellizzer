Anthurium krukovii

Anthurium krukovii, commonly known as the Krukovii arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive inflorescence consisting of a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that can grow up to 30 centimeters in length. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium krukovii is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated as a houseplant.
