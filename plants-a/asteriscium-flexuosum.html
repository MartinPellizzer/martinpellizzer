Asteriscium flexuosum

Asteriscium flexuosum, commonly known as the flexible asterisk, is a flowering plant native to the southern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant produces small, yellow to orange flowers arranged in a star-like pattern, giving it its common name. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands and along stream banks.

Asteriscium flexuosum is an important plant for pollinators and contributes to the ecological diversity of its habitat.
