Ariosorbus hybrida

Ariosorbus hybrida, commonly known as the hybrid snowbell, is a deciduous shrub native to the mountainous regions of central and eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its ornamental white bell-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring, often before the leaves emerge. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and slightly hairy on the underside. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a border plant.

Ariosorbus hybrida is valued for its early blooming habit and cold hardiness, though it may require protection in colder climates.
