Acer kungshanense

Acer kungshanense, commonly known as the Kungshan maple, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It is characterized by its palmately lobed leaves that turn vibrant shades of red and yellow in autumn. The tree produces small, winged samaras that aid in seed dispersal. It typically grows in mountainous regions with cool, moist climates.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
