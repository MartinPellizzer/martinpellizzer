Anonis spinosa

Anonis spinosa, commonly known as the spiny anona, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its woody stems covered in sharp, spiny projections. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that develop into round, yellow or orange fruits. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a rough, textured surface.

Anonis spinosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
