Agapanthus umbellatus var. multiflorus

Agapanthus umbellatus var. multiflorus, commonly known as the African lily or blue agapanthus, is a striking perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features dense, spherical inflorescences composed of numerous blue or purple flowers, which bloom in summer. The plant has strap-like, evergreen leaves that form a rosette at the base. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for gardens and landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long-lasting blooms and ability to naturalize in suitable climates.
