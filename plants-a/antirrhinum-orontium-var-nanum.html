Antirrhinum orontium var. nanum

Antirrhinum orontium var. nanum, commonly known as the dwarf snapdragon, is a compact, low-growing plant native to southern Europe.

It features small, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to red, often with contrasting markings. The plant has slender, branching stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its delicate appearance and long blooming period add ornamental value to garden spaces.
