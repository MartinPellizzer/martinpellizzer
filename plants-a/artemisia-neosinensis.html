Artemisia neosinensis

Artemisia neosinensis, commonly known as Chinese wormwood, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, silver-gray foliage. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in dense clusters called heads. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid regions.

Artemisia neosinensis is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional Chinese medicine.
