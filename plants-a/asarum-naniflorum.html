Asarum naniflorum

Asarum naniflorum, commonly known as small-flowered wild betony, is a perennial herb native to the eastern United States.

It typically grows in moist, wooded areas and has heart-shaped, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, reddish-purple flowers that bloom in early spring. Its rhizomatous root system is fibrous and often used in traditional herbal medicine.

Asarum naniflorum is a member of the Aristolochiaceae family, known for its unique floral structures and medicinal properties.
