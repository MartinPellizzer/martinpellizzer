Aristida vilfifolia

Aristida vilfifolia, commonly known as the slender wiregrass, is a grass species native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its fine, wiry leaves and small, spike-like inflorescences. This plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as prairies and sandy soils. It is often found in disturbed areas and can form dense mats in dry environments.

The botanical features of Aristida vilfifolia make it an important component of native grassland ecosystems.
