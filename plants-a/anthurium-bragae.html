Anthurium bragae

Anthurium bragae, commonly known as the Braga's arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive spathe and spadix inflorescence. The plant features heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a waxy texture and a vibrant red spathe that surrounds the yellow spadix. Anthurium bragae thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical landscapes.
