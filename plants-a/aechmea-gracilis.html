Aechmea gracilis

Aechmea gracilis, commonly known as the slender pineapple plant, is a striking bromeliad native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that form a central cup, which collects water and nutrients. The plant produces vibrant, showy inflorescences with colorful bracts that range from red to orange. Aechmea gracilis thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical landscapes.
