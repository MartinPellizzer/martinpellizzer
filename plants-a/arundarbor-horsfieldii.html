Arundarbor horsfieldii

Arundarbor horsfieldii, commonly known as the Horsfield's arundinaria, is a rare and endangered grass species native to the mountainous regions of southern China.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and long, narrow leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is highly sensitive to environmental changes.

Conservation efforts are ongoing to protect this unique botanical species from further decline.
