Ammannia luederitzii var. hereroeensis

Ammannia luederitzii var. hereroeensis, commonly known as the Herero ammannia, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its small, clustered leaves and bright red or orange flowers that bloom in summer. The plant thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky soils. It is a member of the Amaranthaceae family, which includes many species with similar adaptive features.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for drought-tolerant landscaping.
