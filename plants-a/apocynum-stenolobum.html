Apocynum stenolobum

Apocynum stenolobum, commonly known as narrowleaf dogbane, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the family Apocynaceae, which includes many plants with milky sap and showy flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters, featuring opposite, narrow, and often hairy leaves. Its flowers are clustered in umbels and are usually pink or white, with five petals.

The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small, toxic seeds.
