Afzelia zenkeri

Afzelia zenkeri, commonly known as African rosewood, is a tropical tree native to western and central Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its dense, dark-colored wood, which is highly valued for its durability and aesthetic appeal. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in racemes.

Afzelia zenkeri is also known for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types and its role in local ecosystems as a source of timber and traditional medicine.
