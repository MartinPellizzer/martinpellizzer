Anemone jenisseensis

Anemone jenisseensis, commonly known as the Jenise River anemone, is a flowering plant native to the Siberian regions of Russia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, open habitats such as riverbanks and wet meadows. The plant features showy, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are palmately divided, with a velvety texture and a bluish-green hue.

Anemone jenisseensis is adapted to cold climates and is often found in areas with well-drained, loamy soils.
