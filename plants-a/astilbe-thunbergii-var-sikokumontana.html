Astilbe thunbergii var. sikokumontana

Astilbe thunbergii var. sikokumontana, commonly known as the Japanese astilbe, is a herbaceous perennial native to Japan.

It features upright, branched stems that bear clusters of small, fragrant, pinkish-white flowers. The plant has dark green, lance-shaped leaves that are finely toothed and provide attractive foliage throughout the growing season. It thrives in partial shade and moist, well-drained soil, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

Its ornamental value lies in both its delicate flowers and its lush, textured foliage.
