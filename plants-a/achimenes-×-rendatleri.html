Achimenes × rendatleri

Achimenes × rendatleri, commonly known as the hybrid achimenes, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Achimenes tetragona and Achimenes pavonii. The plant is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in a range of colors including red, pink, and white. It typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters.

The leaves are heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while the stems are hairy and often branching.
