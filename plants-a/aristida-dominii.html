Aristida dominii

Aristida dominii, commonly known as the Dominique's threeawn, is a grass species native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its slender, tufted growth and produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences. The plant typically thrives in sandy or rocky soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments. Its leaves are narrow and often have a rough texture, while the flowering structures are usually pale green to brown in color.

Aristida dominii is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed areas.
