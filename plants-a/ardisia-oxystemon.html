Ardisia oxystemon

Ardisia oxystemon, commonly known as the red-berried evergreen, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, white flowers followed by bright red berries that are visually striking. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
