Anthurium penningtonii

Anthurium penningtonii, commonly known as the Pennington's anthurium, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often brightly colored, ranging from deep green to reddish hues. The plant produces showy, spadix-like inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, which is typical of the Araceae family. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Anthurium penningtonii is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and unique floral structure.
