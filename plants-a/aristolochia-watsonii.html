Aristolochia watsonii

Aristolochia watsonii, commonly known as the Chinese birthwort, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, heart-shaped leaves and its unique, vine-like growth habit. The plant produces striking, tubular flowers that are often purple or greenish in color. It belongs to the Aristolochiaceae family and is known for its complex chemical composition.

However, it contains toxic compounds and should be handled with care.
