Actinocephalus falcifolius

Actinocephalus falcifolius, commonly known as the narrow-leaved actinocephalus, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows in dry, rocky habitats. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small and yellow, arranged in dense clusters.

It is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage.
