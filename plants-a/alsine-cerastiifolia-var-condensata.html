Alsine cerastiifolia var. condensata

Alsine cerastiifolia var. condensata, commonly known as the dense alsike.

This perennial herb is native to parts of Europe and Asia, characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit. It features small, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in early summer, typically arranged in dense clusters. The plant has hairy, narrow leaves that are often covered in fine pubescence.

It thrives in moist, sandy or calcareous soils and is often found in meadows and along riverbanks.
