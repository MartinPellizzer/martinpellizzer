Aeranthes rigidula

Aeranthes rigidula, commonly known as the rigid flame flower, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its striking, flame-like inflorescences. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically bright red or orange in color. It thrives in warm, sunny environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.

Aeranthes rigidula is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in botanical gardens and by plant enthusiasts.
