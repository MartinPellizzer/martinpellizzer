Aydendron desertorum var. glabratum

Aydendron desertorum var. glabratum, commonly known as the smooth desert aydendron, is a succulent plant native to arid regions.

It features thick, fleshy stems that store water, enabling it to survive in dry environments. The leaves are typically small and reduced, minimizing water loss through transpiration. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish or yellowish in color.

This variety is characterized by its lack of pubescence, giving it a smooth, polished appearance.
