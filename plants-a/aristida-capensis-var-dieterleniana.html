Aristida capensis var. dieterleniana

Aristida capensis var. dieterleniana, commonly known as the Cape grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky outcrops. The plant has slender, cylindrical stems and small, spike-like inflorescences that are characteristic of the Aristida genus. Its leaves are narrow and hairy, with a bluish-green coloration.

It is well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a resilient and drought-tolerant species.
