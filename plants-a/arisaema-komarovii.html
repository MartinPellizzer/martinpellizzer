Arisaema komarovii

Arisaema komarovii, commonly known as the Komarov's arisaema, is a species of flowering plant in the Araceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of eastern China, particularly in Sichuan and Yunnan provinces. The plant is characterized by its distinctive, spadix-shaped inflorescence surrounded by a large, hood-like spathe. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its unique morphology and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
