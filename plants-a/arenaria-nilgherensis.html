Arenaria nilgherensis

Arenaria nilgherensis, commonly known as the Nilgiri sandwort, is a flowering plant native to the Western Ghats of India.

It is a low-growing herbaceous plant characterized by its dense clusters of small, white flowers. The plant typically thrives in rocky and sandy soils, often found in alpine and subalpine regions. It has small, linear leaves that are adapted to withstand harsh climatic conditions.

Arenaria nilgherensis plays a role in stabilizing soil in its native habitat and is of interest to botanists due to its unique ecological adaptations.
