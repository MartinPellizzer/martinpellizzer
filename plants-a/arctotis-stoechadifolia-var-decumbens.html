Arctotis stoechadifolia var. decumbens

Arctotis stoechadifolia var. decumbens, commonly known as the prostrate arctotis, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its low-growing, spreading habit with hairy, gray-green leaves. The plant produces showy, daisy-like flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, which bloom throughout the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
