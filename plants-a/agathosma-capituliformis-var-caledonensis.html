Agathosma capituliformis var. caledonensis

Agathosma capituliformis var. caledonensis, commonly known as Cape aster, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit. The plant produces small, daisy-like flowers in shades of yellow and white, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Its aromatic leaves and ornamental flowers make it a valuable addition to both gardens and horticultural collections.
