Achyranthes leptostachya

Achyranthes leptostachya, commonly known as slender stamen knotweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Asia.

It belongs to the Polygonaceae family and is characterized by its upright stems and clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture and is often found in moist, shady environments. It produces small, dry fruits that contain several seeds, facilitating its spread through wind dispersal.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
