Amerosedum stenopetalum

Amerosedum stenopetalum, commonly known as the narrow-petaled stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern, allowing it to store water efficiently. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and arid landscapes.

Its compact size and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for low-maintenance landscaping.
