Arundina densa

Arundina densa, commonly known as the bamboo orchid, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its dense, grass-like foliage and striking inflorescences. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, violet or white flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Arundina densa is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
