Aloe lavranosii

Aloe lavranosii, commonly known as the Cape Town aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically bluish-green in color, with a waxy coating to retain moisture. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing yellow flowers, which attract pollinators like bees and birds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for arid and semi-arid environments.

Aloe lavranosii is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used traditionally for its medicinal properties.
