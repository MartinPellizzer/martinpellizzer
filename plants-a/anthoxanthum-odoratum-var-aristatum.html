Anthoxanthum odoratum var. aristatum

Anthoxanthum odoratum var. aristatum, commonly known as sweet vernal grass, is a flowering plant in the grass family Poaceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow to orange inflorescence, which gives it its common name. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and sandy soils. It is a perennial species that thrives in temperate climates.

Its unique coloration makes it a valuable addition to ecological and ornamental landscapes.
