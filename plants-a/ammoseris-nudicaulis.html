Ammoseris nudicaulis

Ammoseris nudicaulis, commonly known as the slender bulbous orchid, is a species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its small, tubular bulbs and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces delicate, white or pale pink flowers with a distinctive, spurred lip. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts of rare orchids.
