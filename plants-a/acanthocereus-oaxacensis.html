Acanthocereus oaxacensis

Acanthocereus oaxacensis, commonly known as the Oaxaca cactus, is a columnar cactus native to Mexico.

It features spiny, ribbed stems that are typically green and covered with white, woolly hairs. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers that bloom at night, attracting nocturnal pollinators. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Its structure and growth habits make it a striking addition to desert gardens.
