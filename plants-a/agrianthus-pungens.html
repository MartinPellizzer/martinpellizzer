Agrianthus pungens

Agrianthus pungens, commonly known as the spiny agave, is a succulent plant native to the arid regions of Mexico.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped growth pattern and thick, spiny leaves that are adapted to store water. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing small, greenish-white flowers. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Due to its striking appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
