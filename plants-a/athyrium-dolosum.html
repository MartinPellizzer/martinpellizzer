Athyrium dolosum

Athyrium dolosum, commonly known as the false holly fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Athyriaceae and is characterized by its delicate, feather-like fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest understories. Its spores are released from fertile sorus clusters located on the underside of its leaves.

Athyrium dolosum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its elegant foliage.
