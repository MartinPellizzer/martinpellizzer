Atriplex Halimus F. Ramosissima

Atriplex halimus f. ramosissima is a hardy shrub known for its salt tolerance and adaptability to arid environments. This plant thrives in coastal areas and saline soils where few others can survive. Its dense, branched structure makes it an effective windbreak and soil stabilizer. Often used in ecological restoration projects, it plays a vital role in preventing erosion. With its unique ability to thrive in harsh conditions, it is a valuable species in sustainable landscaping and land management.
