Atractylodes chinensis f. quinqueloba

Atractylodes chinensis f. quinqueloba, commonly known as five-lobed atractylodes, is a variant of the common atractylodes plant native to China.

It is characterized by its distinctive five-lobed leaves, which give it its common name. The plant belongs to the family Asteraceae and is often found in moist, shaded environments. Its rhizomes are used in traditional Chinese medicine for their purported health benefits.

The botanical features of this plant make it a subject of interest in both horticulture and pharmacology.
