Abutilon molle

Abutilon molle, commonly known as velvetleaf, is a herbaceous plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its soft, velvety leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that are often clustered in racemes. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in disturbed soils.

Abutilon molle is also known for its fibrous stems, which have been used in traditional crafts and textiles.
