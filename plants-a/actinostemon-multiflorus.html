Actinostemon multiflorus

Actinostemon multiflorus, commonly known as the many-flowered actinostemon, is a herbaceous perennial native to parts of Africa.

It features upright, branched stems that bear clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Its ornamental value comes from its delicate flowers and compact growth habit.
