Alyxia linearis

Alyxia linearis, commonly known as the slender alyxia, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a woody vine that typically grows in moist, wooded areas. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in the spring. Its leaves are opposite, elliptical, and have a smooth texture.

Alyxia linearis is often found in swampy or wetland habitats, where it thrives in humid conditions.
