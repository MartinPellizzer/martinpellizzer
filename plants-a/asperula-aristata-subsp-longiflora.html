Asperula aristata subsp. longiflora

Asperula aristata subsp.

longiflora, commonly known as the long-flowered sweetbox, is a shrub native to southern Europe and western Asia. It features small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer months.

The plant has dark green, glossy leaves that are opposite in arrangement. It thrives in well-drained soils and partial to full shade, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

Its ornamental value comes from its delicate flowers and evergreen foliage.
