Agrostis thunbergii

Agrostis thunbergii, commonly known as slender meadowgrass, is a perennial grass species native to Asia, including regions of China, Japan, and Korea.

It typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows, grasslands, and along stream banks. The plant has fine, tufted blades with a blue-green color and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping.

Agrostis thunbergii is also known for its ability to thrive in shaded conditions, making it a versatile choice for garden settings.
