Acianthera Morenoi

Acianthera morenoi is a rare and fascinating orchid species native to the cloud forests of South America. Known for its striking violet and white flowers, it captivates botanists and plant enthusiasts alike. This plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing on tree trunks or in rocky crevices. Due to habitat loss, it faces significant threats to its survival. Conservation efforts are crucial to ensure its continued existence in the wild.
