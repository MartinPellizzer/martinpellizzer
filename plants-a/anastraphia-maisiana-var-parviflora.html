Anastraphia maisiana var. parviflora

Anastraphia maisiana var. parviflora, commonly known as the small-flowered anastraphia, is a species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its slender stems and lance-shaped leaves. The flowers are small, white or pale pink, and clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
