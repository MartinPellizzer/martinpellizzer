Afrachneria ampla

Afrachneria ampla, commonly known as the giant African lily, is a striking plant native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy, white to pale blue flowers that bloom in clusters, often reaching several feet in height. The plant has strap-like, elongated leaves that are typically green with a waxy texture. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
