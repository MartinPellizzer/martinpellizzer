Adolia capensis

Adolia capensis, commonly known as the Cape adolia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Acanthaceae and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from red to purple. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun conditions. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs.

Adolia capensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
