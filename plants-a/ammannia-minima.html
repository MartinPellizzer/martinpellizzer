Ammannia minima

Ammannia minima, commonly known as the small ammannia, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa, Asia, and Australia.

It is a herbaceous annual that typically grows to a height of about 30 cm. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually pink or red in color, which bloom throughout the growing season. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Ammannia minima is often found in moist or wet environments, such as marshes and along stream banks.
