Asplenium viride var. oblongum

Asplenium viride var. oblongum, commonly known as the green maidenhair fern, is a variant of the maidenhair fern species characterized by its elongated, lance-shaped fronds.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions, where it thrives in moist, shaded environments. The plant features a delicate, feathery appearance with finely divided leaves that are typically a vibrant green. It prefers well-draining soil and consistent humidity, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its ornamental value lies in its elegant foliage, which adds a touch of refinement to both natural and cultivated landscapes.
