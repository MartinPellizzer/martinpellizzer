Anthurium ellipsoideum

Anthurium ellipsoideum, commonly known as the elliptic anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-shaped inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, which can range from red to orange. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity and well-draining soil.

Anthurium ellipsoideum is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
