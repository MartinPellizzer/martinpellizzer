Alantsilodendron decaryanum

Alantsilodendron decaryanum, commonly known as the Decary's alant tree, is a rare and ancient plant native to the rainforests of Madagascar.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and broad, palm-like leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting various pollinators. Its unique botanical features include a fibrous root system and a woody structure that allows it to thrive in humid, nutrient-rich environments.
