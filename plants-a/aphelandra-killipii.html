Aphelandra killipii

Aphelandra killipii, commonly known as the Killip's flame tree, is a tropical plant native to the Caribbean.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, pendant-like flowers that range in color from yellow to red. Aphelandra killipii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.
