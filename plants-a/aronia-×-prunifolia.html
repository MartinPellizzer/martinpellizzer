Aronia × prunifolia

Aronia × prunifolia, commonly known as the purple beauty chokeberry, is a hybrid shrub native to North America.

It is characterized by its dark purple to black berries, which are rich in antioxidants and anthocyanins. The plant typically grows to a height of 1–3 meters and features compound leaves with serrated edges. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and health benefits from its edible fruits.
