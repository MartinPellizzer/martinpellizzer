Aerides suavissima var. ballantiniana

Aerides suavissima var. ballantiniana, commonly known as the Ballantini's orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant typically blooms during the summer months, with pendulous inflorescences bearing multiple blooms. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its delicate appearance and long-lasting blooms.
