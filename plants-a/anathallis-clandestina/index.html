Anathallis Clandestina

Ghost orchid, scientifically known as Anathallis clandestina, is a rare and elusive flowering plant native to the rainforests of South America. It is famous for its ghostly appearance, with pale, translucent petals that give it its otherworldly reputation. Despite its beauty, the ghost orchid is extremely difficult to find, growing on tree branches in humid, shaded environments. Its unique biology and habitat make it a subject of fascination for botanists and conservationists alike. Protecting this mysterious plant is crucial for preserving biodiversity in its fragile ecosystem.
