Axonopus radiatus

Axonopus radiatus, commonly known as the narrowleaf axonopus, is a species of grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its fine, grass-like leaves and compact growth habit. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments. Axonopus radiatus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is used in landscaping and ground cover applications.

It is also known for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types and its tolerance to moderate foot traffic.
