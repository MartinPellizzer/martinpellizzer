Alsophila fenicis

Alsophila fenicis, commonly known as the flame tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds that can reach several meters in length. The plant has a robust root system that helps stabilize soil and prevent erosion. Its fronds are typically bright green, with a slightly serrated edge.

Alsophila fenicis thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in forest understories.
