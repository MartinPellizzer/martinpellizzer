Aneilema macrorrhizum

Aneilema macrorrhizum, commonly known as the broad-rooted aneilema, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories and wetlands. The plant features large, rhizomatous roots that help it anchor and absorb nutrients efficiently. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, often with a glossy surface.

The flowers are small and clustered, usually in green or red bracts, contributing to its ornamental value.
