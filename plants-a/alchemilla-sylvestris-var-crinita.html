Alchemilla sylvestris var. crinita

Alchemilla sylvestris var. crinita, commonly known as the silky lady's mantle, is a perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its soft, hairy leaves that give it a velvety appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in early summer, which are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas or along stream banks.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking a low-maintenance, decorative plant.
