Anoectochilus luteus

Anoectochilus luteus, commonly known as the golden orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers and is often found growing on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant has lance-shaped leaves and produces small, clustered blooms during the rainy season. It is a popular ornamental plant due to its vibrant color and unique appearance.

Botanically, it belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is known for its epiphytic growth habit.
