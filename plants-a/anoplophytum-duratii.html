Anoplophytum duratii

Anoplophytum duratii, commonly known as the Duratii's spurge, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, ribbed stems and leaves that are typically green with a waxy coating. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. It is adapted to arid environments, storing water in its leaves and stems to survive drought conditions.

Anoplophytum duratii is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
