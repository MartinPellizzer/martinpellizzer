Anathallis Iguapensis

Anathallis iguapensis is a rare and fascinating orchid native to the rainforests of Ecuador. This delicate plant is part of the orchid family, known for its intricate and often striking floral structures. It thrives in humid, shaded environments where it can anchor itself to trees or rocky surfaces. Due to its limited distribution and habitat destruction, it is considered endangered. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this unique species for future generations.
