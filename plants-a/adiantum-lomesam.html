Adiantum lomesam

Adiantum lomesam, commonly known as the Lomesam maidenhair fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Adiantaceae and is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds that resemble a feather. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as forest understories or rocky crevices. Its leaves are bioluminescent, emitting a faint glow in the dark, which is a unique adaptation for attracting nocturnal pollinators.

This rare and striking plant is valued for its ornamental beauty and ecological significance in its native habitat.
