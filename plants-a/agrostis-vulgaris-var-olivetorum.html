Agrostis vulgaris var. olivetorum

Agrostis vulgaris var. olivetorum, commonly known as slender meadowgrass, is a perennial grass species native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in moist, nutrient-rich soils in meadows, grasslands, and along stream banks. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny florets. It is often found in open habitats and is adapted to temperate climates.

Its botanical features make it a common component of natural and managed grassland ecosystems.
