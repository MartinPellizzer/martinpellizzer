Astragalus lhorongensis

Astragalus lhorongensis, commonly known as the Tibetan milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Tibetan Plateau.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its small, yellow pea-like flowers. The plant has compound leaves with numerous leaflets and produces small, hairy pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often growing in rocky or sandy soils.

Due to its limited distribution and ecological significance, it is considered a valuable species for botanical and ecological studies.
