Acacia dewevrei

Acacia dewevrei, commonly known as the dwarf silver acacia, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its small, narrow leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in winter. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and is drought-tolerant, making it suitable for arid environments. Its foliage has a silvery sheen, which helps reflect sunlight and reduce water loss.

Acacia dewevrei is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
