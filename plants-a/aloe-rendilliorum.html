Aloe rendilliorum

Aloe rendilliorum, commonly known as the Red Mountain Aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with red or purple margins. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow flowers, which bloom in late summer. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil.

Aloe rendilliorum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ease of care.
