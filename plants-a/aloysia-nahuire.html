Aloysia nahuire

Aloysia nahuire, commonly known as the Nahuire verbena, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly found in regions of Argentina and Chile.

It belongs to the Verbenaceae family and is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to purple. Aloysia nahuire is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant display.
