Aloe deflexidens

Aloe deflexidens, commonly known as the flexible aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, with a waxy coating to reduce water loss. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow-orange flowers, which bloom in the spring. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Aloe deflexidens is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional medicinal uses.
