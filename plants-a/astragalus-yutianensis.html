Astragalus yutianensis

Astragalus yutianensis, commonly known as the Yutian milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky or sandy soils. It plays a role in soil stabilization and is of interest for its potential medicinal properties.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
