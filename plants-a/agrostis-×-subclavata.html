Agrostis × subclavata

Agrostis × subclavata, commonly known as the slender meadowgrass, is a hybrid grass species belonging to the Poaceae family.

It is characterized by its fine, tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. This plant typically thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows and along stream banks. Agrostis × subclavata is known for its ability to form dense vegetation, which can help prevent soil erosion.

Its flowering stems produce small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny florets.
