Arenaria macrosepala var. minor

Arenaria macrosepala var. minor, commonly known as the dwarf sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to arctic and alpine regions.

It typically features small, clustered leaves and produces delicate, white or pink flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is often found in tundra and mountainous habitats. Its compact growth habit and frost-resistant nature make it well adapted to harsh environmental conditions.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in challenging garden settings.
