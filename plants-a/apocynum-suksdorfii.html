Apocynum suksdorfii

Apocynum suksdorfii, commonly known as Suksdorf's milkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the United States.

It belongs to the family Apocynaceae, which includes many species with milky sap. The plant typically grows in moist to wet habitats such as marshes and wetlands. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often have a hairy texture.

The flowers are small, clustered in umbels, and usually have a pinkish or white color.
