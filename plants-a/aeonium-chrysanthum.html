Aeonium chrysanthum

Aeonium chrysanthum, commonly known as the "Lion's Tail," is a succulent plant native to the Canary Islands.

It features striking, fleshy, rosette-shaped leaves that are typically green with red or purple edges. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers on tall, branching stems during the spring or early summer. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a desirable addition to both indoor and outdoor landscapes.
