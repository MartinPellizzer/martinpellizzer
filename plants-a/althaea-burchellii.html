Althaea burchellii

Althaea burchellii, commonly known as Burchell's mallard, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces showy, cup-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, which bloom in late summer. Its leaves are heart-shaped and covered in a fine layer of soft, fuzzy hair.

Althaea burchellii is often found in grasslands and along riverbanks, where it thrives in well-drained soils.
