Agave gentryi

Agave gentryi, commonly known as the Gentry's agave, is a species of flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its tall, spire-like inflorescence that can reach heights of up to 10 feet. The plant features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color with a waxy coating. It produces white to pale yellow flowers that bloom once in its lifetime.

Agave gentryi is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
