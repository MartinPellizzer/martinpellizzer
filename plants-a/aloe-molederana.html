Aloe molederana

Aloe molederana, commonly known as the Cape aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aloe genus and is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are thick and fleshy, adapted for storing water. The leaves are typically green with a reddish tinge and have a waxy coating to reduce water loss. It produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow-orange flowers, which attract pollinators like bees and birds.

This plant is well-suited to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
