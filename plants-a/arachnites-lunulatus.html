Arachnites lunulatus

Arachnites lunulatus, commonly known as the moonflower spider plant, is a unique member of the Asparagaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking, spider-like flowers that open at night, emitting a faint fragrance. The plant produces small, white, tubular blooms that are often covered in a fine, silvery down. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a slight bluish tint.

Arachnites lunulatus thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate watering to prevent root rot.
