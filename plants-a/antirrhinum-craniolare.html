Antirrhinum craniolare

Antirrhinum craniolare, commonly known as the skullcap antirrhinum, is a flowering plant belonging to the Plantaginaceae family.

It is characterized by its erect, branched stems and clusters of showy, two-lipped flowers that range in color from pink to purple. Antirrhinum craniolare is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
