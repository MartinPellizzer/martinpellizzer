Ardisia hatoana

Ardisia hatoana, commonly known as the Hatoana beautyberry, is a shrub native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Ericaceae and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. Ardisia hatoana is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal and ability to thrive in shaded environments.
