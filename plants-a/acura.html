Acura

Acura, commonly known as the Acurina, is a rare flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Andes.

It features striking violet-blue petals and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in a fine, silvery down.

Acura is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in specialized botanical gardens.
