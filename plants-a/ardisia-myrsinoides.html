Ardisia myrsinoides

Ardisia myrsinoides, commonly known as the glossy leaf ardisia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrsinaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Asia, including India, Malaysia, and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters. After flowering, it produces bright red berries that are edible for some bird species.

Ardisia myrsinoides is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and ability to thrive in shaded environments.
