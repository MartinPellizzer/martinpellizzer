Achyrocline tomentosa

Achyrocline tomentosa, commonly known as the woolly achyrocline, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean and parts of South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant is known for its dense, woolly foliage, which gives it a distinctive appearance. It typically grows in coastal areas and is often found in sandy or limestone soils.
