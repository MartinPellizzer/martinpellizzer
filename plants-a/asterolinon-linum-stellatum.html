Asterolinon linum-stellatum

Asterolinon linum-stellatum, commonly known as the starflower, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking star-shaped yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30-60 centimeters and has slender, branching stems covered in fine hairs. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often with a slightly hairy texture.

The starflower is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional herbal medicine.
