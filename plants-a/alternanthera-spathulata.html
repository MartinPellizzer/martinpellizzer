Alternanthera spathulata

Alternanthera spathulata, commonly known as the broad-leaf alternanthera, is a species of flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, brightly colored leaves that range from red to pink and purple, often displaying a gradient of hues. The plant typically grows as an annual or perennial herb, reaching heights of up to 3 feet. Its small, inconspicuous flowers are usually green or yellow and appear in clusters.

It prefers full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
