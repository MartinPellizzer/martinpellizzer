Aphora reticulata

Aphora reticulata, commonly known as the net-veined orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant has a complex root system and thrives in moist, shaded environments. Its leaves are narrow and elongated, with a distinct network of veins.

Aphora reticulata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
