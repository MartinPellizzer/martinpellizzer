Andromeda empetrifolia

Andromeda empetrifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf andromeda, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and blueberries, and is characterized by its evergreen foliage and showy, bell-shaped flowers. Andromeda empetrifolia is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to tolerate shade.
