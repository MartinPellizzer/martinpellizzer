Anemia flexuosa f. genuina

Anemia flexuosa f. genuina, commonly known as the wavy anemia, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to specific regions of South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive wavy, undulating leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. It prefers moist, shaded environments and is often found in humid, tropical forests.
