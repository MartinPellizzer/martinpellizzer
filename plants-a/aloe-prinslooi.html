Aloe prinslooi

Aloe prinslooi, commonly known as the Prinsloo aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asphodelaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are thick and fleshy. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow flowers that bloom in summer. Its leaves contain a gel-like substance that is used for medicinal and skincare purposes.

Aloe prinslooi is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil and full sun.
