Arabis nuristanica

Arabis nuristanica, commonly known as the Nuristan rockcress, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, particularly in Afghanistan and Pakistan.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other cruciferous plants. The plant typically grows in rocky and alpine environments, thriving at high altitudes. It produces small, white or yellowish flowers that bloom in early spring.

Its leaves are usually basal, rosette-like, and covered in fine hairs, adapting to the harsh climate of its habitat.
