Androrchis × petterssonii

Androrchis × petterssonii, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a naturally occurring hybrid between Androrchis alpina and Androrchis flaccida.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a slender, erect stem bearing a single, showy flower. The plant typically grows in alpine or subalpine regions, preferring well-drained, rocky soils. Its flowers are usually pale yellow to greenish-yellow, with a distinctive lip that is often darker in color.

This hybrid is relatively rare and is of interest to orchid enthusiasts and botanists due to its unique genetic makeup and floral characteristics.
