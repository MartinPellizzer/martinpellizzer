Aster acaulis

Aster acaulis, commonly known as the rock aster, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and is often found growing on rocky slopes and cliffs. The plant produces showy blue to purple flower heads that bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are narrow and linear, adapted to withstand harsh environmental conditions.

Aster acaulis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with well-drained soil.
