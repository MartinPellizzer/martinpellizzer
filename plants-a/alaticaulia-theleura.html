Alaticaulia theleura

Alaticaulia theleura, commonly known as the silverleaf orchid, is a striking member of the orchid family found in specific regions of Southeast Asia.

This plant is characterized by its slender, hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves that give it its common name. It produces delicate, pale yellow flowers with a distinct scent, often blooming during the cooler months. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its unique morphology and limited distribution make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists.
