Angelonia leandrii

Angelonia leandrii, commonly known as the tropical angelonia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to purple. The plant produces showy, long-lasting blooms that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its foliage is typically lance-shaped and covered in a fine, velvety texture.

Angelonia leandrii thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
