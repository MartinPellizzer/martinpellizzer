Ancylanthos rubiginosus

Ancylanthos rubiginosus, commonly known as the rusty ancylanthus, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from red to orange, often with a contrasting yellow or white center. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a rough or hairy texture.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
