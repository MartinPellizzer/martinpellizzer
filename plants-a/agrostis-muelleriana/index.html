Agrostis Muelleriana

Agrostis muelleriana is a low-growing grass species native to parts of Asia and Australia. It is commonly found in grassy areas, meadows, and along roadsides. This plant is known for its fine, wiry leaves and small, inconspicuous flowers. It plays an important role in stabilizing soil and supporting local wildlife. Due to its hardiness, it is sometimes used in landscaping and restoration projects.
