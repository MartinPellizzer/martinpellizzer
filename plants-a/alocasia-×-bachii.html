Alocasia × bachii

Alocasia × bachii, commonly known as the Black Velvet Alocasia, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Araceae family.

It is characterized by its dark, velvety foliage that ranges from deep purple to black, with intricate patterns of lighter green. The plant produces striking, spadix-shaped inflorescences surrounded by a white or green spathe. Native to Southeast Asia, it thrives in humid, shaded environments with high levels of indirect light.

Due to its ornamental value, it is widely cultivated as a houseplant and in tropical gardens.
