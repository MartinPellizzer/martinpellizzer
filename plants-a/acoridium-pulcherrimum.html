Acoridium pulcherrimum

Acoridium pulcherrimum, commonly known as the golden star ginger, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Zingiberaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its striking, yellow-orange inflorescences that resemble stars. Acoridium pulcherrimum is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
