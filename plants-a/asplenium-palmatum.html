Asplenium palmatum

Asplenium palmatum, commonly known as the Japanese spleenwort, is a fern belonging to the family Aspleniaceae.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen foliage with a distinctive, palmate shape. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often found on rocks or tree trunks in temperate regions. Its fronds are leathery and have a dark green color with a glossy appearance.

Asplenium palmatum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in low-light conditions.
