Appendicula tenuifolia var. filiformis

Appendicula tenuifolia var. filiformis, commonly known as the slender leaf appendicula, is a unique species within the Gesneriaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, thread-like leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows as a low-growing herb, often found in shaded, moist environments. Its flowers are small, tubular, and usually display a range of colors from pink to white.

This variant is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
