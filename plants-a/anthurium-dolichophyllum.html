Anthurium dolichophyllum

Anthurium dolichophyllum, commonly known as the long-leaf anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its striking, elongated leaves that can grow up to several feet in length. The plant produces a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe, which is typical of the genus Anthurium. It thrives in humid, warm environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical landscapes.
