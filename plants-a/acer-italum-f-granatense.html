Acer italum f. granatense

Acer italum f. granatense, commonly known as the Granatense maple, is a rare and ornamental variant of the Italian maple.

It is characterized by its striking red foliage, which becomes more intense as the growing season progresses. The plant typically grows as a small tree or shrub, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are palmately lobed, with a smooth margin and a vibrant green color that turns crimson in autumn.

It thrives in well-drained soils and partial to full sunlight, making it a desirable addition to gardens and landscapes.
