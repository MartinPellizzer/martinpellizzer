Alsophila andersonii var. miaoensis

Alsophila andersonii var. miaoensis, commonly known as the Miao bamboo, is a variant of the genus Alsophila, which belongs to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is a type of fern that is native to China, specifically found in the Yunnan and Guizhou provinces. This plant is characterized by its slender, bamboo-like stems and feathery fronds, which give it a distinctive appearance. The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its aesthetic appeal.
