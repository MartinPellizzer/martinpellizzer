Alsodeia minutiflora

Alsodeia minutiflora, commonly known as the small-flowered alsodea, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with narrow, grass-like leaves that form a dense mat. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white or pale blue flowers arranged in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal or sandy habitats.

Due to its delicate appearance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value.
