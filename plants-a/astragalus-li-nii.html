Astragalus li-nii

Astragalus li-nii, commonly known as the Chinese milkvetch, is a perennial legume native to China.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant produces pods that contain small, edible seeds, which are often used in traditional Chinese medicine. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, preferring well-drained soils.

Astragalus li-nii is valued for its medicinal properties, particularly its potential immune-boosting effects.
