Arundo alpestris

Arundo alpestris, commonly known as the alpine reed, is a tall, perennial grass native to mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

It typically grows in wet or moist habitats such as meadows, stream banks, and damp woodlands. The plant has long, narrow leaves and produces dense clusters of small, spike-like flowers. It is characterized by its robust growth and ability to thrive in cold, high-altitude environments.

Arundo alpestris is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and erosion control capabilities.
