Aster ciliosus

Aster ciliosus, commonly known as the silky aster, is a herbaceous perennial native to the eastern United States.

It features showy, daisy-like flowers with purple to pink petals and yellow centers that bloom in late summer to early fall. The plant has slender, upright stems covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it its common name. Its leaves are lance-shaped and hairy, often with a bluish-green hue.

Aster ciliosus thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland edges and wetlands.
