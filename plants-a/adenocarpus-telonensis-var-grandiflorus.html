Adenocarpus telonensis var. grandiflorus

Adenocarpus telonensis var. grandiflorus, commonly known as the large-flowered burr legume, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its pod fruits and nitrogen-fixing abilities. The plant features compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces showy yellow to orange flowers. Its fruit is a hairy, three-lobed pod that contains several seeds.

This species is often found in grasslands and disturbed soils, thriving in full sun conditions.
