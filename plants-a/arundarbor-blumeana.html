Arundarbor blumeana

Arundarbor blumeana, commonly known as the Blume's arundinaria, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the grass family, Poaceae, and is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and narrow leaves. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences that are often green or reddish in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
