Actinodaphne sesquipedalis var. glabra

Actinodaphne sesquipedalis var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth actinodaphne, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its smooth, glabrous bark and aromatic leaves, which emit a distinctive scent. Its flowers are small and clustered, typically appearing in axillary inflorescences.
