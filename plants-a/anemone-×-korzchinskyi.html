Anemone × korzchinskyi

Anemone × korzchinskyi, commonly known as the hybrid anemone, is a perennial herbaceous plant that belongs to the Ranunculaceae family.

It is characterized by its showy, cup-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and are typically borne on stems that rise above the foliage. The plant features palmately divided, lobed leaves that are often hairy and have a distinctive texture. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens.

Anemone × korzchinskyi is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to naturalize in suitable climates.
