Astragalus topalanense

Astragalus topalanense, commonly known as Topal's milkvetch, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family Fabaceae.

It is native to Turkey and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid conditions. It produces pods that contain small, round seeds, which are dispersed by wind.
