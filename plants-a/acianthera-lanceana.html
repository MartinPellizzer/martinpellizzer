Acianthera lanceana

Acianthera lanceana, commonly known as the lance-leaved orchid, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its slender, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, delicate flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. The plant is epiphytic, meaning it grows on the surface of trees or other plants without deriving nutrients from them. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and by plant enthusiasts.
