Alyssum loiseleurii

Alyssum loiseleurii, commonly known as the rock allyssum, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features dense clusters of small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink, blooming in early spring. The plant has compact, silver-gray foliage with a mat-forming habit, making it ideal for rock gardens or as a ground cover. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, requiring minimal water once established.

Alyssum loiseleurii is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
