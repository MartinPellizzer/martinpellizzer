Anathallis Spannageliana

Anathallis spannageliana is a rare and fascinating orchid species native to the cloud forests of South America. This delicate plant is known for its unique morphology and specialized pollination mechanisms. It thrives in humid, shaded environments where it forms a symbiotic relationship with specific fungi. Its striking flowers are often camouflaged to blend with the surrounding foliage. Due to habitat loss, this species is now considered endangered, making it a subject of conservation interest.
