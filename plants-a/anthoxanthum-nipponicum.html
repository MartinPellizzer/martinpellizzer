Anthoxanthum nipponicum

Anthoxanthum nipponicum, commonly known as Japanese oatgrass, is a perennial grass species native to East Asia.

It typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and woodland edges. The plant has slender, erect stems that bear small, spike-like inflorescences. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue.

Anthoxanthum nipponicum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in native plant gardens.
