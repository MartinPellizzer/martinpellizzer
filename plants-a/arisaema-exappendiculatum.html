Arisaema exappendiculatum

Arisaema exappendiculatum, commonly known as the Chinese thread-leaf arisaema, is a herbaceous plant native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, palmate leaves with elongated lobes and a unique, spadix-like inflorescence. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are enclosed within a leaf-like spathe. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Arisaema exappendiculatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
