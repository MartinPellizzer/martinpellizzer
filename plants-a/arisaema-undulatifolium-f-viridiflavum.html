Arisaema undulatifolium f. viridiflavum

Arisaema undulatifolium f. viridiflavum, commonly known as the Green-Yellow Arisaema, is a variant of the undulating-leaf arisaema, characterized by its unique leaf morphology.

The plant features a striking, undulating leaf blade that is typically green with a yellowish-green hue, giving it its common name. It produces a spadix surrounded by a spathe, which is usually a deep green color with a subtle yellowish tinge. This fern-like plant is native to Southeast Asia and thrives in moist, shaded environments.

Its botanical features make it a notable ornamental plant in horticulture.
