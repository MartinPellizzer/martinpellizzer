Amsonia tabernaemontana var. tabernaemontana

Amsonia tabernaemontana var. tabernaemontana, commonly known as blue mistflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the southeastern United States.

It features showy, blue-violet flower clusters that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are dark green and slightly hairy. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Its ornamental value comes from its long-lasting flowers and attractive foliage throughout the growing season.
