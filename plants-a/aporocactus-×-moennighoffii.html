Aporocactus × moennighoffii

Aporocactus × moennighoffii, commonly known as the Moennighoff's prickly pear, is a hybrid cactus species native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its flattened, ribbed stems that store water. The plant produces vibrant yellow or orange flowers during the spring and summer months. Its spiny stems and ability to thrive in arid conditions make it well-suited for xeriscaping.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in harsh environments.
