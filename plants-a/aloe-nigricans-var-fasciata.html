Aloe nigricans var. fasciata

Aloe nigricans var. fasciata, commonly known as the zebra aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features striking, narrow, dark green leaves with prominent white or pale green stripes, giving it its distinctive appearance. The leaves are thick and fleshy, adapted to store water in arid environments. It produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellowish-green flowers during the warmer months.

This variety is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for succulent gardens and indoor collections.
