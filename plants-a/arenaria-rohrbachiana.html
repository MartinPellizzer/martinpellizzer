Arenaria rohrbachiana

Arenaria rohrbachiana, commonly known as the Rohrbach sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the alpine regions of Europe.

It thrives in rocky and sandy soils, often found in mountainous and arctic environments. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in the summer months, typically from June to August. Its leaves are succulent and fleshy, adapted to retain moisture in harsh conditions.

Arenaria rohrbachiana is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in difficult soil conditions.
