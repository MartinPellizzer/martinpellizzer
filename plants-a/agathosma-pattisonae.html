Agathosma pattisonae

Agathosma pattisonae, commonly known as the Cape snowbush, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, white to pale blue flowers that bloom in dense clusters. The plant thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is typically found in coastal and mountainous regions. It has aromatic leaves that are often used in traditional medicine.

Agathosma pattisonae is also valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
