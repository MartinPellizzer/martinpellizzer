Adiantum capillus-veneris var. daphnites

Adiantum capillus-veneris var. daphnites, commonly known as the Venus's hair, is a delicate fern species native to parts of Asia and Africa.

It features finely divided, feathery fronds that are typically bright green and have a silky texture. The plant thrives in shady, moist environments and is often found near streams or in forest understories. Its small, spore-bearing structures are located on the underside of the fronds, typical of ferns.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its graceful appearance.
