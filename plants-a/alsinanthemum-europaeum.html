Alsinanthemum europaeum

Alsinanthemum europaeum, commonly known as European alsinathemum, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Amaranthaceae.

It is characterized by its erect, branched stems and small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. The plant produces small, oval-shaped seeds that are dispersed by wind, aiding in its spread across various habitats. It is often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides, thriving in temperate climates.

Alsinanthemum europaeum is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to stabilize soil in certain environments.
