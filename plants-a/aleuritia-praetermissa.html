Aleuritia praetermissa

Aleuritia praetermissa, commonly known as the Chinese snowberry, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features small, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant produces bright red berries that are ornamental but not typically edible. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a suitable choice for woodland gardens.

Its foliage turns vibrant shades of red and orange in the fall, adding seasonal interest to the landscape.
