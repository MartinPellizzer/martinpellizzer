Anthurium jenmanii

Anthurium jenmanii, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a tropical flowering plant native to Central America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces vibrant red spathes that enclose bright yellow spadices, creating a dramatic floral display. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium jenmanii is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and long-lasting blooms.
