Arnebia decumbens subsp. decumbens

Arnebia decumbens subsp.

decumbens, commonly known as the Himalayan bistort, is a perennial herb native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas. It features upright, slender stems that bear alternate, lance-shaped leaves with a hairy texture.

The plant produces striking clusters of pink or purple flowers during the summer months. Its root system is fibrous and often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.

It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
