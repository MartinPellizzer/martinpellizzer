Anemone japonica var. hybrida

Anemone japonica var. hybrida, commonly known as Japanese anemone hybrid, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to East Asia.

It features showy, cup-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, and red, which bloom from late summer to early autumn. The plant has finely divided, fern-like foliage that adds texture to garden landscapes. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it suitable for a variety of garden settings.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long-lasting blooms and ability to attract pollinators.
