Anthurium floribundum

Anthurium floribundum, commonly known as the laceleaf anthurium, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped, glossy green leaves with a velvety texture and deep green veins. The plant produces striking spadices, which are modified leaves that enclose the actual flowers, often in shades of red or orange. Anthurium floribundum thrives in high humidity and indirect light, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique foliage and vibrant inflorescences make it a desirable ornamental plant.
