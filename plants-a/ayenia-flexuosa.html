Ayenia flexuosa

Ayenia flexuosa, commonly known as the flexible ayenia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a hairy texture.

The flowers bloom in the spring, producing small, yellowish to white florets that attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
