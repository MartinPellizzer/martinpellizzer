Aegiphila pacifica

Aegiphila pacifica, commonly known as the Pacific aegiphila, is a flowering plant native to the Pacific Islands.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its small, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of tiny, tubular flowers that range in color from white to purple.
