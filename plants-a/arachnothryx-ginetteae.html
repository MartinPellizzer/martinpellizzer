Arachnothryx ginetteae

Arachnothryx ginetteae, commonly known as the Ginette's arachnothryx, is a member of the Fabaceae family, characterized by its leguminous properties.

It is a climbing shrub native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant features compound leaves with stipules and produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Its fruit is a legume pod, containing several elliptical seeds.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
