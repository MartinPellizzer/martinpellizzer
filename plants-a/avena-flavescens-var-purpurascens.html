Avena flavescens var. purpurascens

Avena flavescens var. purpurascens, commonly known as the purple oatgrass, is a perennial grass native to North America.

It features slender, erect stems that often turn a striking reddish-purple color, especially under stress or during late summer. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences with pale green to yellowish flowers. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue.

This variety is often found in disturbed soils and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
