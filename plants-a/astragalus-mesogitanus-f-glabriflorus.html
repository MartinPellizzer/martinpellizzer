Astragalus mesogitanus f. glabriflorus

Astragalus mesogitanus f. glabriflorus, commonly known as the smooth-flowered mesogitan milkvetch, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, pea-like flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or arid soils and is adapted to drought conditions. Its flowers are usually pale yellow to white, and it produces small, lens-shaped pods containing seeds.

This subspecies is of interest in botanical studies due to its unique floral morphology and ecological adaptations.
