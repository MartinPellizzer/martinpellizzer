Anthurium longissimum

Anthurium longissimum, commonly known as the long-spurred anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves and vibrant, spathaceous flowers that come in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant produces a long, slender spadix that is often more prominent than the colorful spathe. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil.

Anthurium longissimum is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
