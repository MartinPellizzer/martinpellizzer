Adiantum tinctum var. wagneri

Adiantum tinctum var. wagneri, commonly known as the Wagner's Maidenhair Fern, is a variant of the common maidenhair fern.

It is a delicate, evergreen fern characterized by its finely divided, dark green fronds that resemble lace. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its rhizomes are creeping and produce clusters of small, spore-bearing leaves.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value in horticulture due to its elegant appearance and ability to thrive in controlled indoor conditions.
