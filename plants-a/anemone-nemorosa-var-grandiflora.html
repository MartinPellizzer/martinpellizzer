Anemone nemorosa var. grandiflora

Anemone nemorosa var. grandiflora, commonly known as the wood anemone, is a herbaceous perennial native to Europe.

It features delicate, white or pale pink flowers with a distinctive, showy center. The plant has finely divided, trifoliate leaves that are often covered in a fine, silky hair. It thrives in moist, shaded woodlands and prefers well-drained, loamy soil.

Its blooming period typically occurs in early spring, making it a valuable early-season nectar source for pollinators.
