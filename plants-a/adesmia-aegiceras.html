Adesmia aegiceras

Adesmia aegiceras, commonly known as the coastal legume, is a shrub native to coastal regions of Australia and Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, trifoliate leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is adapted to saline and sandy soils, making it a hardy species found in coastal dunes and tidal zones. It plays a role in stabilizing coastal ecosystems and preventing erosion.

Adesmia aegiceras is also valued for its potential in ecological restoration projects due to its resilience to harsh environmental conditions.
