Astilbe myriantha

Astilbe myriantha, commonly known as the Japanese aster or mountain astilbe, is a herbaceous perennial native to eastern Asia.

It features upright, branched stems that bear panicles of small, fragrant, pinkish-white flowers. The plant has heart-shaped, dark green leaves that are often adorned with silver-white markings. It thrives in partial to full shade and moist, well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens.

Astilbe myriantha is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to add texture and color to shaded landscapes.
