Anthocercis anisantha

Anthocercis anisantha, commonly known as the red-flowered orchid, is a member of the orchid family (Orchidaceae).

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its striking, vibrant red flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are elongated and lance-shaped, typically arranged in a rosette pattern.

Anthocercis anisantha is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and greenhouses.
