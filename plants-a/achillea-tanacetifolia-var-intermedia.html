Achillea tanacetifolia var. intermedia

Achillea tanacetifolia var. intermedia, commonly known as the intermediate yarrow, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features finely divided, fern-like leaves that are gray-green in color, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces flat-topped clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy and low-maintenance garden plant.

Its aromatic foliage and long-lasting flowers make it a valuable addition to both ornamental and ecological landscapes.
