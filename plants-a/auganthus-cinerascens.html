Auganthus cinerascens

Auganthus cinerascens, commonly known as the silverleaf daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and bright yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes. Its compact growth habit and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for low-maintenance gardens.

Auganthus cinerascens is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
