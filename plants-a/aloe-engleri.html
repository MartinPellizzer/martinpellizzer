Aloe engleri

Aloe engleri, commonly known as the Cape aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with red or purple margins. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing yellow or orange flowers. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its leaves.

Aloe engleri is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional medicinal uses.
