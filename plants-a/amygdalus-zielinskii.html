Amygdalus zielinskii

Amygdalus zielinskii, commonly known as the Chinese bitter almond.

It is a species of the Prunus genus, native to China, and is characterized by its small, oval leaves and white flowers. The plant produces small, bitter almonds that are not typically used for culinary purposes. It is often found in rocky or mountainous regions, thriving in temperate climates.

Due to its toxicity, it is important to handle the plant with care.
