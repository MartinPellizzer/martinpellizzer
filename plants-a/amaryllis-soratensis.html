Amaryllis soratensis

Amaryllis soratensis, commonly known as the Soratensis amaryllis, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that come in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant produces large, strap-like leaves that are typically green and glossy. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and can thrive in well-drained soil with ample sunlight.

Amaryllis soratensis is known for its ability to bloom multiple times a year under optimal growing conditions.
