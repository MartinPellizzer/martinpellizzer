Arisaema kerrii

Arisaema kerrii, commonly known as the Kerri's arrowhead, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive inflorescence consisting of a spadix surrounded by a spathe. The plant features heart-shaped leaves and produces clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Arisaema kerrii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
