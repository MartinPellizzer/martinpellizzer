Achimenes tubiflora

Achimenes tubiflora, commonly known as the trumpet vine or red achimenes, is a tropical flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular, red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters throughout the growing season. The plant has heart-shaped, glossy leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant. It thrives in warm, humid conditions and prefers well-draining soil.

Achimenes tubiflora is a member of the Acanthaceae family and is known for its ability to attract hummingbirds and other pollinators.
