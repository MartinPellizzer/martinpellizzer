Acnistus parviflorus

Acnistus parviflorus, commonly known as the smallflower acnistus, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features small, white flowers that are clustered in umbels, which bloom in spring. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

It is adapted to thrive in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.
