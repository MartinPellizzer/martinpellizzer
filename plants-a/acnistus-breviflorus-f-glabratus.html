Acnistus breviflorus f. glabratus

Acnistus breviflorus f. glabratus, commonly known as the smoothflower acnistus, is a rare and less commonly cultivated variant of the acnistus genus.

It is characterized by its reduced flowering and smoother foliage compared to the typical form. This plant is native to specific regions in southern Africa, where it thrives in rocky, well-drained soils. Its botanical features include small, clustered flowers and a low-growing, mat-forming habit.

Due to its rarity and unique morphology, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists alike.
