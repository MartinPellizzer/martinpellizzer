Acalypha reticulata var. ovalifolia

Acalypha reticulata var. ovalifolia, commonly known as the oval-leaved spurflower, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features striking, colorful bracts that often resemble flowers, which are actually modified leaves. The plant has palmate leaves with a reticulate venation pattern, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded to partially shaded environments.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and ecological restoration projects.
