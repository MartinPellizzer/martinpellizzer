Anthurium pentaphyllum var. pentaphyllum

Anthurium pentaphyllum var. pentaphyllum, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are dark green with a metallic sheen. The plant produces vibrant red spadices, which are the showy inflorescences that house the tiny, white flowers. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Its foliage and floral structure make it a popular choice for indoor gardening and ornamental purposes.
