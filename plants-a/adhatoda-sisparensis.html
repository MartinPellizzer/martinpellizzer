Adhatoda sisparensis

Adhatoda sisparensis, commonly known as the Indian Trumpet or Malabar Trumpet, is a flowering plant native to the Indian subcontinent.

Adhatoda sisparensis is also valued for its medicinal properties, particularly in traditional Ayurvedic medicine.
