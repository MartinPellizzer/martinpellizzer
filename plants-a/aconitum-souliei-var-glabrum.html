Aconitum souliei var. glabrum

Aconitum souliei var. glabrum, commonly known as the smooth aconite, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its tall, sparsely branched stems that can reach up to 2 meters in height. The plant produces showy blue to purple flowers in late summer, arranged in dense clusters at the top of the stem. Its leaves are deeply lobed and have a distinctive, finely divided appearance.

Aconitum souliei var. glabrum is noted for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
