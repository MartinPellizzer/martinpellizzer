Amelanchier amabilis

Amelanchier amabilis, commonly known as the Saskatoon serviceberry, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to North America.

It produces edible berries that are rich in nutrients and often used in culinary applications. The plant has alternate, simple leaves that are ovate to elliptic in shape. It flowers with white to pinkish clusters before the leaves emerge, and the berries ripen in late summer.

Amelanchier amabilis is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological benefits, providing food for birds and wildlife.
