Acianthus tenuilabris

Acianthus tenuilabris, commonly known as the narrow-lipped acianthus, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and small, delicate flowers. Native to South Africa, this plant thrives in moist, shaded environments. The flowers typically bloom in late summer, displaying a range of colors from white to pale pink.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
