Anthurium donovaniae

Anthurium donovaniae, commonly known as the red-tipped anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Colombia.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are dark green with a reddish tint along the edges. The plant produces vibrant orange spathes that enclose its spadices, which are the actual flowers. It thrives in humid, warm environments with bright, indirect light.

Anthurium donovaniae is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
