Aegiphila skutchii

Aegiphila skutchii, commonly known as the Skutch's aegiphila, is a shrub native to the Andean regions of South America.

Aegiphila skutchii is adapted to grow in rocky or dry habitats, showcasing its resilience to harsh environmental conditions.
