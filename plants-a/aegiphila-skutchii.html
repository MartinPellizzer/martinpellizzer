Aegiphila skutchii

Aegiphila skutchii, commonly known as the Skutch's aegiphila, is a shrub native to the Andean regions of South America.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants. The plant features small, opposite leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are typically pink or purple, arranged in whorls along the stems.

Aegiphila skutchii is adapted to grow in rocky or dry habitats, showcasing its resilience to harsh environmental conditions.
