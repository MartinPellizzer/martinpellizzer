Amaryllis sweetii

Amaryllis sweetii, commonly known as the sweetii amaryllis, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that come in shades of red, pink, and white, often with a distinctive yellow or orange stripe. The plant produces large, strap-like leaves that are broad and waxy, typical of the Amaryllis genus. It thrives in well-draining soil and prefers bright, indirect light.

Amaryllis sweetii is prized for its vibrant blooms and is a popular choice for both garden cultivation and indoor planting.
