Andropogon sincoranus

Andropogon sincoranus, commonly known as tall bluestem, is a tall grass native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its slender, blue-green blades and can grow up to 6 feet in height. The plant has a fibrous root system that helps stabilize soil and prevent erosion. It thrives in full sun and is often found in prairies, woodland edges, and disturbed sites.

Its seeds are dispersed by wind, contributing to its spread and ecological importance in grassland ecosystems.
