Aster haastii

Aster haastii, commonly known as the Haast's aster, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forests and wetlands, and can reach heights of up to 1.5 meters. Its bright yellow flowers are surrounded by pale green bracts, creating a striking visual contrast.

Aster haastii is also valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
