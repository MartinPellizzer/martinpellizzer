Acanthobotrya cinerea

Acanthobotrya cinerea, commonly known as the silverleaf acanthobotrya, is a species of flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Acanthaceae and is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are covered in fine, white hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.
