Anonidium floribundum

Anonidium floribundum, commonly known as the tropical evergreen tree, is a member of the Anonaceae family.

It is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its fruit is a spherical, yellowish drupe that contains a single seed.

Anonidium floribundum is native to tropical regions of Africa and thrives in humid, warm climates.
