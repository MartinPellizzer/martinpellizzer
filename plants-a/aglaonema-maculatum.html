Aglaonema maculatum

Aglaonema maculatum, commonly known as the Chinese Evergreen, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated foliage with shades of green, white, and red, which makes it a popular ornamental plant. The plant has dark green, lance-shaped leaves that are often marked with intricate patterns of light and dark hues. It thrives in low-light conditions and requires moderate humidity and well-draining soil.

Aglaonema maculatum is also known for its air-purifying qualities, making it a favored choice for indoor gardening.
