Allomorphia multinervia

Allomorphia multinervia, commonly known as the multi-nerved allomorph, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its complex venation pattern in its leaves, which are typically large and broad. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually white or pale yellow. Its root system is fibrous, allowing it to thrive in moist, well-drained soils.

Allomorphia multinervia is often found in rainforests and is valued for its ornamental qualities.
