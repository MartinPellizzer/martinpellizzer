Avena spicata

Avena spicata, commonly known as slender oat, is a grass species native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its narrow, upright stems and small, spike-like clusters of flowers. The plant produces slender, awned spikelets that are typically green to reddish in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and disturbed areas.

Avena spicata is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
