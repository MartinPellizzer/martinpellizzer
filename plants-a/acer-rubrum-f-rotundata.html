Acer rubrum f. rotundata

Acer rubrum f. rotundata, commonly known as the red maple with rounded leaves, is a distinctive cultivar of the red maple tree.

It is characterized by its broad, rounded leaves that are typically a deep red or orange-red in the fall, offering striking visual appeal. The plant retains its foliage longer into the winter compared to other red maple varieties, making it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping. It thrives in a variety of soil types but prefers moist, well-drained conditions.

This cultivar is often used in urban settings due to its adaptability and vibrant seasonal color.
