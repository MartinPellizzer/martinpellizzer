Amaryllis traubii f. doraniana

Amaryllis traubii f. doraniana, commonly known as the Doraniana amaryllis, is a striking hybrid plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, orange, and yellow. The plant typically grows as a bulbous perennial, with strap-like leaves that are broad and fleshy. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires a period of dormancy to produce blooms.

This cultivar is popular among gardeners for its vibrant flowers and relatively easy care requirements.
