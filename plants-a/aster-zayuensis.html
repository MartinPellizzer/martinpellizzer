Aster zayuensis

Aster zayuensis, commonly known as the Yarlung aster, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of Tibet and Sichuan in China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies, sunflowers, and other composite flowers. This species is characterized by its striking purple to blue flowers and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine meadows and rocky habitats.

Due to its unique beauty and ecological significance, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
