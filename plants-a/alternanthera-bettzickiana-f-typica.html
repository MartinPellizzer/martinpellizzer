Alternanthera bettzickiana f. typica

Alternanthera bettzickiana f. typica, commonly known as the Red Alternanthera, is a striking aquatic plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red to pink foliage, which becomes more intense under strong light conditions. The plant thrives in warm freshwater environments and is often used in aquascaping for its aesthetic appeal. It prefers moderate to high water flow and can be propagated through stem cuttings.

Its foliage is often used in both freshwater and saltwater aquariums due to its hardy nature and visual impact.
