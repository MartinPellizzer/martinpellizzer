Adenophora hunanensis

Adenophora hunanensis, commonly known as the Hunan bellflower, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped, blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or mountainous regions. Its leaves are lanceolate and hairy, while its stems are erect and branched.

Adenophora hunanensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and traditional medicinal uses.
