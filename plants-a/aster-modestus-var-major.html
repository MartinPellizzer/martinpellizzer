Aster modestus var. major

Aster modestus var. major, commonly known as the showy aster, is a perennial herb native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and vibrant blue to purple flower heads that bloom in the fall. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet and features narrow, linear leaves. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for gardens and natural habitats.
