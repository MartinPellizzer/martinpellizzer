Anemone somaliensis

Anemone somaliensis, commonly known as the Somali anemone, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of Somalia and adjacent areas of Ethiopia and Kenya.

It is characterized by its delicate, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pale pink, often with a distinctive central disk. The plant has basal leaves that are lobed or heart-shaped, and it thrives in dry, well-drained soils. It is adapted to survive in harsh, semi-desert environments, often flowering after rainfall.
