Anthocephalus Cadamba

Anthocephalus cadamba is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia known for its striking ornamental value. It features large, glossy leaves and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of red and orange. This plant is often cultivated in gardens and public spaces for its aesthetic appeal and ability to thrive in warm, humid climates. Its wood is also valued for its durability and is used in traditional crafts and construction. Despite its beauty, it requires careful attention to soil and water conditions to flourish.
