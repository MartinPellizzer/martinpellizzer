Anneslea donnaiensis

Anneslea donnaiensis, commonly known as the Java orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial herb with a short, rhizomatous stem and lanceolate, glossy leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink, with a distinctive lip petal. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Anneslea donnaiensis is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
