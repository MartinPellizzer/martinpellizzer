Allium rubellum

Allium rubellum, commonly known as the red onion, is a perennial bulbous plant belonging to the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in temperate climates. The plant produces spherical bulbs that are rich in sulfur compounds, contributing to its distinct pungent aroma. Its leaves are grass-like, narrow, and often bluish-green in color.

Allium rubellum is widely cultivated for its edible bulbs, which are used in various culinary applications.
