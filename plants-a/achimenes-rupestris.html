Achimenes rupestris

Achimenes rupestris, commonly known as the rock achimenes, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in rocky or arid environments. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, which bloom in summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various growing conditions.
