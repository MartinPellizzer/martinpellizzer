Astronium fraxinifolium f. glaberrimum

Astronium fraxinifolium f. glaberrimum, commonly known as the glabrous starfruit tree, is a variant of the starfruit tree characterized by its smooth, glabrous bark and leaves.

The plant is notable for its distinctive, star-shaped fruits that are typically yellow or orange when mature.
