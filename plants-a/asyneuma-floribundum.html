Asyneuma floribundum

Asyneuma floribundum, commonly known as the flowering asyneuma, is a herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant produces showy, daisy-like flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grassland and rocky habitats.

Asyneuma floribundum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
