Amelanchier humilis var. compacta

Amelanchier humilis var. compacta, commonly known as the dwarf serviceberry, is a deciduous shrub native to North America.

It features small, white flowers that bloom in early spring, followed by edible berries in the summer. The plant has a compact growth habit, making it suitable for small gardens or as a hedgerow. Its leaves turn a striking red or orange in the fall, adding ornamental value.

It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is relatively low maintenance.
