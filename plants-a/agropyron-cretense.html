Agropyron cretense

Agropyron cretense, commonly known as the Cretaceous needlegrass, is a grass species native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in dry, sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny seeds.

Agropyron cretense is of interest to botanists due to its ancient lineage and its role in understanding grass evolution.
