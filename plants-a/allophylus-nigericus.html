Allophylus nigericus

It is native to parts of Southeast Asia, including Indonesia, Malaysia, and the Philippines. The plant is a small to medium-sized tree or shrub, typically reaching heights of up to 10 meters. The plant produces small, clustered flowers followed by red or black berries that are edible for some bird species.
