Aporum helvolum

Aporum helvolum, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a velvety texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or yellow in color. It is often found in wetlands and along the edges of ponds and streams.

Due to its rapid growth, it can become invasive in certain regions.
