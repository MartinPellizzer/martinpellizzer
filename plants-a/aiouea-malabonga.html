Aiouea malabonga

Aiouea malabonga, commonly known as the Malabar ironwood, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and smooth, dark bark. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by elliptical, leathery fruits. It is valued for its hard, heavy wood, which is used in construction and furniture.

The species is also appreciated in horticulture for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil types.
