Alsophila muelleri

Alsophila muelleri, commonly known as the Mueller's tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to Australia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and a crown of large, feather-like fronds. The plant belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is known for its ability to thrive in moist, shaded environments. Its fronds can grow up to several meters in length, contributing to its ornamental value.
