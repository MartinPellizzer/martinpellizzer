Arenaria assadii

Arenaria assadii, commonly known as the Assad's sandwort, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of the Middle East.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit. The plant features small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or white, adapted to thrive in sandy and rocky soils. It has narrow, linear leaves that help reduce water loss in its harsh environment.

Arenaria assadii is often found in coastal dunes and dry steppe habitats, showcasing its resilience to extreme conditions.
