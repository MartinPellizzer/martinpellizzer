Abolboda rigida

Abolboda rigida, commonly known as the rigid lichen, is a species of lichen that belongs to the family Parmeliaceae.

It is characterized by its dense, crust-like growth form and typically grows on rock surfaces in arid or semi-arid environments. The thallus is usually pale to dark gray, with a firm and leathery texture. It lacks distinct lobes and has a uniform appearance, making it easily identifiable in its habitat.

This lichen is often found in regions with low humidity and high sunlight exposure.
