Adriana tomentosa var. tomentosa

Adriana tomentosa var. tomentosa, commonly known as the woolly adriana, is a species native to certain regions of South America.

It is a shrub characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The leaves are typically gray-green in color and covered in fine, soft hairs. It produces small, fragrant flowers that are often white or pale pink in color.

This plant is well adapted to dry, rocky environments and is often found in coastal areas.
