Anthurium gaudichaudianum var. chamissonis

Anthurium gaudichaudianum var. chamissonis, commonly known as the Chamisson's anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically deep green with a metallic sheen. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-shaped inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, often in red or orange hues. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

This variety is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and unique floral structure.
