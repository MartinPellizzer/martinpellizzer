Antennaria muscoides

The plant features small, green to reddish-brown leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, and it produces tiny, white or pale pink flowers in clusters. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to cold, harsh environments.

Antennaria muscoides plays a role in stabilizing soil in its native habitats and is often found in tundra and alpine meadows.
