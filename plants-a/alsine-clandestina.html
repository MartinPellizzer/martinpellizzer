Alsine clandestina

Alsine clandestina, commonly known as the secret alsine, is a small annual herbaceous plant belonging to the Amaranthaceae family.

It typically grows in disturbed soils and is found in arid and semi-arid regions. The plant has slender, branching stems and small, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are clustered in dense inflorescences and are usually pink or purple in color.

Alsine clandestina is known for its ability to thrive in harsh environments and is often used in traditional medicine.
