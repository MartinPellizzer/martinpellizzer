Achillea longirama

Achillea longirama, commonly known as the slender yarrow, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its slender, upright stems and clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are linear and aromatic, often used in traditional herbal medicine.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits to pollinators.
