Achillea millefolium var. sudetica

Achillea millefolium var. sudetica, commonly known as the Sudetic yarrow, is a perennial herb native to Central Europe.

It features finely divided, feathery leaves that give it a distinctive, lace-like appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy addition to gardens.

This variety is closely related to other yarrow species and is valued for its ornamental and medicinal properties.
