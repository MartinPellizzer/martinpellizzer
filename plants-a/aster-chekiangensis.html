Aster chekiangensis

Aster chekiangensis, commonly known as the Chekiang aster, is a flowering plant native to eastern China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically features showy purple or blue flowers that bloom in the autumn season. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in woodland areas or along forest edges.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
