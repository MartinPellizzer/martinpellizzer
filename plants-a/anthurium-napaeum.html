Anthurium napaeum

Anthurium napaeum, commonly known as the Napaeum anthurium, is a striking tropical plant native to the mountainous regions of Central America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped spathe that comes in shades of red, orange, and yellow, often with a contrasting green spadix. The plant thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect light to flourish. Its foliage consists of large, glossy, dark green leaves that add to its ornamental appeal.

Anthurium napaeum is popular as a houseplant due to its ability to purify the air and its long-lasting, showy blooms.
