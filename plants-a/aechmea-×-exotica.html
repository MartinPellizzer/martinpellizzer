Aechmea × exotica

Aechmea × exotica, commonly known as the pineapple bromeliad, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Bromeliaceae family.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that form a central cup, which collects water and nutrients. The plant produces striking, spadix-like inflorescences that are often brightly colored. Native to South America, it thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardening.
