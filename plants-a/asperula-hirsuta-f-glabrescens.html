Asperula hirsuta f. glabrescens

Asperula hirsuta f. glabrescens, commonly known as the silky woodrose, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, dark green, oval leaves that are covered with fine, silky hairs, giving the plant its characteristic texture. The flowers are typically pink to purple, appearing in clusters during late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardy nature.
