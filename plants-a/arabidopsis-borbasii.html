Arabidopsis borbasii

Arabidopsis borbasii, commonly known as the Borbasii thale cress, is a species of flowering plant in the Brassicaceae family.

It is a small annual herb with simple, alternate leaves and small white or yellow flowers. Native to southern Africa, it thrives in arid and semi-arid environments. The plant has a short life cycle and is often used in ecological studies due to its adaptability.

Its genome has been studied for its potential in understanding plant stress responses.
