Angiopteris leschenaultiana

Angiopteris leschenaultiana, commonly known as the giant shield fern, is a large, tropical fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its massive, shield-shaped fronds that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant has a robust rhizome system that supports its extensive foliage. Its leaves are typically bipinnate, with a distinctive, almost metallic sheen on the underside.

This species thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.
