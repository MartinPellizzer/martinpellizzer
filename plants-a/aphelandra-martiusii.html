Aphelandra martiusii

Aphelandra martiusii, commonly known as the Martius's flame plant, is a tropical shrub native to the Caribbean and northern South America.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, pendulous inflorescences that resemble flames. The plant features lance-shaped, dark green leaves with a glossy surface and produces vibrant yellow-orange bracts that enclose small, tubular flowers. It thrives in humid, warm environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Aphelandra martiusii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its visually appealing foliage and unique floral display.
