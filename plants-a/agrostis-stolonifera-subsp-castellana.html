Agrostis stolonifera subsp. castellana

Agrostis stolonifera subsp.

castellana, commonly known as Castellan grass, is a low-growing perennial grass native to southern Europe. It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences.

The plant is characterized by its stoloniferous growth habit, which allows it to spread and form dense mats. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and rocky areas.

Its botanical structure makes it adaptable to various environmental conditions.
