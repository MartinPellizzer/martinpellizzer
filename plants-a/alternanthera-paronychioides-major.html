Alternanthera paronychioides major

Alternanthera paronychioides major, commonly known as the red-stemmed Alternanthera, is a striking aquatic plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red or purple stems and lance-shaped leaves that often display a gradient of colors. This species thrives in shallow, slow-moving waters and prefers full sun to partial shade. Its foliage is often used in aquariums for its aesthetic appeal and ability to provide shelter for small aquatic life.

The plant is known for its rapid growth and ease of propagation through stem cuttings.
