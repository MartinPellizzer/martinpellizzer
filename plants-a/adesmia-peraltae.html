Adesmia peraltae

Adesmia peraltae, commonly known as the Peralta's legume, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly found in regions of Argentina and Bolivia.

The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are compound, consisting of several oval-shaped leaflets, and it produces small, pea-like flowers in shades of white or pale pink.

Adesmia peraltae is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, showcasing resilience to drought conditions.
