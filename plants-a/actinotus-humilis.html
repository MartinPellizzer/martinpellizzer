Actinotus humilis

Actinotus humilis, commonly known as the mountain mouse-ear, is a low-growing, herbaceous plant native to southern Australia.

It features small, white to pale pink flowers that are clustered in dense, spherical inflorescences. The leaves are typically heart-shaped and covered in fine, soft hairs. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in shaded, woodland areas.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting native insect populations.
