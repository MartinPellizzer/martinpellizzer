Astragalus dscheratuensis

Astragalus dscheratuensis, commonly known as the Djerat Mountain milkvetch, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, particularly in the Tien Shan and Pamir ranges. The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit, trifoliate leaves, and small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to pale purple. It thrives in rocky and arid environments, often growing at high altitudes.

Astragalus dscheratuensis plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for local wildlife.
