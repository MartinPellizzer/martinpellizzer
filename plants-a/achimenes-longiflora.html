Achimenes longiflora

Achimenes longiflora, commonly known as the long-flowered achimenes, is a tropical flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from red to pink. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 1 meter, with lance-shaped leaves and showy bracts. Its blooming period extends from spring to autumn, making it a popular choice for gardeners in tropical and subtropical regions.
