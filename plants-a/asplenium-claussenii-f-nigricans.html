Asplenium claussenii f. nigricans

Asplenium claussenii f. nigricans, commonly known as the black fern, is a variant of the broadleaf fern characterized by its dark, almost black fronds.

It is a member of the Aspleniaceae family and is native to tropical regions of South America. The plant features lance-shaped leaves with a glossy surface and is typically evergreen. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
