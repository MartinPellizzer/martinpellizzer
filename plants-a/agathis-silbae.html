Agathis silbae

Agathis silbae, commonly known as the Silba's Agathis, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It belongs to the family Araucariaceae and is characterized by its tall, straight trunk and broad, feather-like leaves. The tree produces large, woody cones that contain edible seeds, which are a food source for various wildlife species.
