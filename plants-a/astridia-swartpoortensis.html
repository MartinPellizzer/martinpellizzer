Astridia swartpoortensis

Astridia swartpoortensis, commonly known as the Swartpoort aster, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in grassland habitats. It produces small, white to pinkish flowers that bloom in the spring.

Astridia swartpoortensis is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for garden use.
