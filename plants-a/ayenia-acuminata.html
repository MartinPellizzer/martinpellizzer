Ayenia acuminata

Ayenia acuminata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf ayenia, is a species of flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Ayenia acuminata is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
