Abroma Augustum

Abroma augustum is a striking flowering plant known for its vibrant, trumpet-shaped blooms. Native to tropical regions of Asia, it thrives in warm and humid climates. This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators. Its leaves are large and glossy, adding to its visual appeal. Despite its beauty, it requires careful attention to soil and water conditions for optimal growth.
