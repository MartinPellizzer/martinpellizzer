Ajax hexangularis

Ajax hexangularis, commonly known as the six-angle plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Aizoaceae.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its unique, hexagonal-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Due to its striking appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
