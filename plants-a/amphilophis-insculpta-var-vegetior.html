Amphilophis insculpta var. vegetior

Amphilophis insculpta var. vegetior, commonly known as the vibrant leaf fern, is a species of fern characterized by its intricate, finely serrated fronds.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, often growing on tree trunks or rocks. Its leaves are typically a deep green, with a velvety texture that enhances its ornamental appeal.
