Annona nana var. oblonga

Annona nana var. oblonga, commonly known as the dwarf soursop or miniature soursop, is a compact, tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It produces small, round to oblong fruits with a thick, spiny rind and a soft, white, aromatic flesh. The plant has smooth, dark green leaves that are heart-shaped and emit a mild fragrance. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and edible fruits, which are rich in nutrients.
