Anachortus divaricatus subsp. oranensis

Anachortus divaricatus subsp.

oranensis, commonly known as the Oranensis spurge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa. It features erect, branching stems that are often covered in fine, white hairs.

The leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and usually have a slightly hairy surface. Its small, yellowish-green flowers are arranged in clusters and bloom during the warmer months.

This species is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a resilient plant in harsh conditions.
