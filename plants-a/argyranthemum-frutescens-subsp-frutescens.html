Argyranthemum frutescens subsp. frutescens

Argyranthemum frutescens subsp.

frutescens, commonly known as the shrub argyranthemum, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands. It is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit and produces clusters of white to pale pink flowers.

The plant has hairy, grayish-green foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
