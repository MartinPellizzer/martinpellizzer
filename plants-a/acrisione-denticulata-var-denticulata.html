Acrisione denticulata var. denticulata

Acrisione denticulata var. denticulata, commonly known as the denticulate acrisione, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically grows in dry, rocky soils and thrives in full sun. The plant features slender, upright stems with lance-shaped leaves that have finely toothed margins. Its small, pale yellow flowers appear in clusters during the summer months.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to arid environments.
