Aristolochia huebneriana

Aristolochia huebneriana, commonly known as the Brazilian birthwort, is a climbing vine native to South America.

It features heart-shaped, aromatic leaves and produces distinctive, pipe-shaped flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color. The plant is part of the Aristolochiaceae family and is known for its complex chemical composition, including various alkaloids. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is sometimes cultivated, though caution is advised due to its potential toxicity.
