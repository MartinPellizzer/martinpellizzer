Andropogon nutans var. avenaceus

Andropogon nutans var. avenaceus, commonly known as the slender bluestem, is a grass species native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and small, spike-like inflorescences that produce light blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in open woodlands and disturbed sites, preferring well-drained soils. Its foliage is narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green coloration that becomes more prominent during the growing season.

This variant is often used in native plant landscaping due to its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
