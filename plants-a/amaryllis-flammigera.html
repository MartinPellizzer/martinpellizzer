Amaryllis flammigera

Amaryllis flammigera, commonly known as the flame amaryllis, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from red to orange, often with a yellow throat. The plant typically blooms in spring or early summer, producing clusters of flowers on sturdy stems. Its foliage consists of strap-like leaves that are dark green and somewhat fleshy.

Amaryllis flammigera is well-suited for both indoor and outdoor cultivation, thriving in well-drained soil and full sun.
