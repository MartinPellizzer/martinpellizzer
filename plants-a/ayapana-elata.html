Ayapana elata

Ayapana elata, commonly known as the Brazilian moonseed, is a climbing plant native to South America.

It belongs to the family Menispermaceae and is characterized by its slender, twining stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by elongated, berry-like fruits. Ayapana elata is often found in moist, tropical forests and is known for its medicinal properties.
