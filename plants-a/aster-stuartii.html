Aster stuartii

Aster stuartii, commonly known as Stuart's aster, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in dry, open habitats. Its flowers are usually blue to purple in color and bloom in the summer months.

Stuart's aster is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting local pollinators.
