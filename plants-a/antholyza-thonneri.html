Antholyza thonneri

Antholyza thonneri, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its attractive, velvety foliage and showy flowers. The plant typically grows as a compact shrub with upright stems and clustered leaves. Antholyza thonneri is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
