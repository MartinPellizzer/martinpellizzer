Arundo nitida

Arundo nitida, commonly known as the narrowleaf reed, is a tall, perennial grass native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, stiff leaves that are often blue-green in color, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in panicles during the summer months. It is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its elegant form and drought tolerance.

Arundo nitida is also valued for its ability to stabilize soil and provide habitat for wildlife.
