Arenaria rubella f. plena

Arenaria rubella f. plena, commonly known as the full-flowered sandspurry, is a flowering plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is a low-growing perennial herb characterized by its dense clusters of small, pink or white flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant features hairy stems and leaves that are typically opposite and oval in shape. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in disturbed or open habitats.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and alpine displays.
