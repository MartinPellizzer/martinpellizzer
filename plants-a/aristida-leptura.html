Aristida leptura

Aristida leptura, commonly known as slender dropseed, is a perennial grass native to the United States.

It is characterized by its fine, wiry blades and compact, cylindrical seed heads. This plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as prairies and sandy soils. It is often used in native plant gardens for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.

Aristida leptura is also valued for its ability to stabilize soil and provide habitat for pollinators.
