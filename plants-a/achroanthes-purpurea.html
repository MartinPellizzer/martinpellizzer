Achroanthes purpurea

Achroanthes purpurea, commonly known as the purple sandwort, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically thrives in rocky or sandy soils. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually purple or pink in color. Its leaves are linear and often covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.
