Anthurium lamanense

Anthurium lamanense, commonly known as the Lamanese arrowhead, is a tropical flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive spathe and spadix inflorescence. The plant features broad, arrow-shaped leaves that are often bright green with a waxy texture. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires consistent moisture and warmth.

Anthurium lamanense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant.
