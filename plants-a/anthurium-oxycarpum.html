Anthurium oxycarpum

Anthurium oxycarpum, commonly known as the spiny anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive, waxy, spiky spathe that can range in color from deep red to orange. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers inside the spathe, which are followed by bright red berries. Its leaves are typically dark green and heart-shaped, with a glossy surface.

Anthurium oxycarpum thrives in humid, warm environments and is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant.
