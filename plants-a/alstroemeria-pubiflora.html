Alstroemeria pubiflora

Alstroemeria pubiflora, commonly known as the velvet tassel lily, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the family Alstroemeriaceae and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant typically grows as a herbaceous perennial, featuring strap-like leaves and long, slender stems. Its flowers are often used in floral arrangements due to their vibrant hues and long-lasting nature.

Alstroemeria pubiflora thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.
