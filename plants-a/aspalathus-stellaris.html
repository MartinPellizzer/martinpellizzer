Aspalathus stellaris

Aspalathus stellaris, commonly known as the star bush, is a small evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, star-shaped yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has narrow, leathery leaves that are arranged in a spiral pattern along the stems. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

Due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance, it is often cultivated in Mediterranean climates.
