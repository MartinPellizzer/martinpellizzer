Anthurium rhodostachyum

Anthurium rhodostachyum, commonly known as the red-tendril anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are deep green with a velvety texture. The plant produces vibrant red spathes that enclose its spadix, which is often covered in tiny, yellowish flowers. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Anthurium rhodostachyum is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and unique inflorescence.
