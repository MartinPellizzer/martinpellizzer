Aspidoglossum eylesii

Aspidoglossum eylesii, commonly known as the Eyles' snakeplant, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features upright, sword-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a slight purplish tinge. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers on slender stalks during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and prefers well-draining soil and bright, indirect light.

Aspidoglossum eylesii is a low-maintenance plant suitable for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.
