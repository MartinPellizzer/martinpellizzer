Acrostichum rufum

Acrostichum rufum, commonly known as the red fern, is a species of fern native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its feathery fronds that are typically reddish in color, especially when young. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in humid forests. Its spores are released from sori located on the underside of the fronds.

Acrostichum rufum plays a role in soil stabilization and provides habitat for various small organisms.
