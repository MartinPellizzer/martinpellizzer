Aglaonema ridleyanum

Aglaonema ridleyanum, commonly known as the Ridly's Chinese evergreen, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated foliage with shades of green, white, and yellow. The leaves are typically elliptical and have a smooth, waxy texture. This plant is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and is popular as a houseplant.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is valued for its ornamental beauty and air-purifying qualities.
