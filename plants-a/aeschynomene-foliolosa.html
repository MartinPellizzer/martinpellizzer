Aeschynomene foliolosa

Aeschynomene foliolosa, commonly known as smallleaf aeschynomene, is a perennial shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant is known for its ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, making it valuable in soil enrichment and sustainable agriculture.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often used in erosion control due to its extensive root system.
