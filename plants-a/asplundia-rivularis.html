Asplundia rivularis

Asplundia rivularis, commonly known as the river asparagus, is a member of the Araceae family.

It is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Thailand and Indonesia. The plant features a short rhizome with dense, fleshy leaves that form a rosette. Its inflorescence consists of a spadix surrounded by a modified leaf called a spathe.

Asplundia rivularis is often found growing in wet, shaded environments such as along riverbanks and in marshy areas.
