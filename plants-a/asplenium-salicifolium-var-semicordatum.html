Asplenium salicifolium var. semicordatum

Asplenium salicifolium var. semicordatum, commonly known as the heart-shaped spleenwort, is a fern belonging to the family Aspleniaceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive, semicircular fronds that are typically dark green and leathery in texture. This variety is native to regions of southern Africa, particularly South Africa, where it thrives in moist, shaded environments. The plant's reproductive structures, including sori, are located on the underside of the fronds, typical of many fern species.

It is a low-maintenance plant often cultivated for its ornamental value and ease of care.
