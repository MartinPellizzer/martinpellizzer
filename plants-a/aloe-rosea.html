Aloe rosea

Aloe rosea, commonly known as the rose aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, upright leaves that are typically tinged with red or pink hues, especially at the tips. The plant produces striking, showy flowers in shades of pink, red, or orange, which bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires plenty of sunlight, making it suitable for arid climates.

Aloe rosea is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively low maintenance once established.
