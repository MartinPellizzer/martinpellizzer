Adiantum bonatianum var. subaristatum

Adiantum bonatianum var. subaristatum, commonly known as the slender maidenhair fern, is a delicate fern species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its finely divided, feathery fronds that are typically bright green and slightly curled. The plant prefers shady, humid environments and thrives in well-drained, moist soil. It produces small, inconspicuous spore-bearing structures on the underside of its fronds.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.
