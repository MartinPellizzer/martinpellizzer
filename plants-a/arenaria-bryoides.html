Arenaria bryoides

Arenaria bryoides, commonly known as the mossy sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to arctic and alpine regions.

It belongs to the family Caryophyllaceae and is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit. The plant produces small, white or pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are typically narrow and grass-like, adapted to withstand harsh environmental conditions.
