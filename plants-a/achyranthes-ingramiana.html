Achyranthes ingramiana

Achyranthes ingramiana, commonly known as Ingram's smartweed, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments. The plant features upright, branched stems covered in fine hairs, and its leaves are lance-shaped with a rough texture. It produces small, clustered flowers that are usually pink to red in color, which bloom in late summer.

The plant is adapted to dry conditions and has a fibrous root system that helps it retain moisture.
