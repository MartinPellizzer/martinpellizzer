Adromischus grasbergensis

Adromischus grasbergensis, commonly known as the Grasberg stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a bluish or purplish tinge. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and succulent collections.
