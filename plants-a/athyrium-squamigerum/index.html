Athyrium Squamigerum

Athyrium squamigerum is a lesser-known fern species that thrives in shaded, moist environments. Its delicate fronds are covered in tiny, scale-like structures that give it a unique appearance. Native to parts of Asia, this plant is often found in forest understories where it plays a vital role in the ecosystem. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively obscure in horticultural circles. Understanding its growth habits can help in its conservation and cultivation.
