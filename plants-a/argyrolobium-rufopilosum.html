Argyrolobium rufopilosum

Argyrolobium rufopilosum, commonly known as the red-hair vetch, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trailing growth habit and hairy stems. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are followed by narrow, pod-like fruits. It has adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a resilient species in harsh conditions.

Its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil makes it ecologically significant in certain ecosystems.
