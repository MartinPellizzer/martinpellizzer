Aloe perfoliata var. brevifolia

Aloe perfoliata var. brevifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, rosette-forming leaves that are typically narrow and pointed, with a pale green to blue-gray coloration. The plant produces small, white to yellowish flowers on branched inflorescences during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its fleshy leaves.

Due to its hardy nature, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant in dry gardens and rockeries.
