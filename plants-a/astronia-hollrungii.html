Astronia hollrungii

Astronia hollrungii, commonly known as the Hooker's astronia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads and showy inflorescences. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are opposite, elliptical, and often covered with fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow and arranged in clusters.

Astronia hollrungii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
