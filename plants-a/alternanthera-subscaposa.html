Alternanthera subscaposa

Alternanthera subscaposa, commonly known as the red-stemmed Alternanthera, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking red stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often a vibrant green. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters during the growing season. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for water gardens and aquatic plant setups.
