Anotis nummularia

Anotis nummularia, commonly known as silver dollar plant, is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to Australia.

It features dense, rounded leaves that are typically silver-white to gray-green in color, resembling small coins. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.
