Arenaria alpicola

Arenaria alpicola, commonly known as alpine sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

It thrives in rocky, alpine environments and is adapted to cold, harsh climates. The plant features small, white or pink flowers that bloom in early summer. Its leaves are typically fleshy and succulent, helping it retain moisture in arid conditions.

Arenaria alpicola plays an important ecological role in stabilizing soil in high-altitude ecosystems.
