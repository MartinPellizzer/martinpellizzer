Acarna chinensis

Acarna chinensis, commonly known as Chinese lantern or red root, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the family Amaranthaceae and is characterized by its vibrant red, papery bracts that resemble small lanterns. The plant has upright, branched stems and produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters. Its roots are often used in traditional Chinese medicine for their purported health benefits.

Acarna chinensis is cultivated both for ornamental purposes and for its medicinal properties.
