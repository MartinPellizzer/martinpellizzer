Aichryson × aizoides

Aichryson × aizoides, commonly known as the hybrid African violet, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, thick leaves that are typically green with a waxy texture, adapted to store water. The plant produces small, showy flowers in shades of purple, pink, and white, often during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Due to its hybrid nature, it is often cultivated for ornamental purposes in gardens and indoor spaces.
