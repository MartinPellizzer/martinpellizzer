Arenaria Macrocarpa Var. Koreana

Arenaria macrocarpa var. koreana is a unique and resilient plant native to the mountainous regions of Korea. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in rocky or shaded areas. This variant of Arenaria is known for its delicate white flowers that bloom in early spring. Its compact growth habit makes it an attractive choice for rock gardens and woodland settings. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively unknown outside of horticultural circles.
