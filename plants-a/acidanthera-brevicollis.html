Acidanthera brevicollis

Acidanthera brevicollis, commonly known as the short-necked orchid, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its compact, erect growth habit. The plant produces small, showy flowers that are typically pink or purple in color, with a distinctive, short floral tube. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grassland or rocky habitats.
