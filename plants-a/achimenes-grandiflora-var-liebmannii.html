Achimenes grandiflora var. liebmannii

Achimenes grandiflora var. liebmannii, commonly known as the Liebmann's achimenes, is a tropical herbaceous perennial native to South America.

It features showy, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to red, often with a contrasting throat. The plant has heart-shaped, hairy leaves and produces striking, spurred blooms that attract pollinators. It thrives in warm, humid conditions and requires well-drained soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and as a cut flower.
