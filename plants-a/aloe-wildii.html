Aloe wildii

Aloe wildii, commonly known as the Cape aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with white or yellow markings. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow-orange flowers during the summer months. Its leaves contain a gel-like substance that is often used for its soothing properties.

Aloe wildii is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water to thrive.
