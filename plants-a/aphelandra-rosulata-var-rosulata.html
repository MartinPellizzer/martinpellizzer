Aphelandra rosulata var. rosulata

Aphelandra rosulata var. rosulata, commonly known as the Rosy-leafed Flame Tree, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces vibrant, pendulous inflorescences with bright yellow bracts that resemble flames, making it a popular ornamental species. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Its unique foliage and showy flowers make it a favored choice for indoor cultivation and garden landscapes.
