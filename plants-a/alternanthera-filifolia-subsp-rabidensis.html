Alternanthera filifolia subsp. rabidensis

Alternanthera filifolia subsp.

rabidensis, commonly known as the red-stemmed Alternanthera, is a striking aquatic plant native to South America. It is characterized by its vibrant red stems and lance-shaped, dark green leaves that often have a slight reddish tint.

This subspecies thrives in shallow, slow-moving freshwater environments and is often used in aquariums for its ornamental value. Its rapid growth rate makes it a popular choice for gardeners and aquarists alike.

The plant's botanical features make it both visually appealing and ecologically beneficial in its native habitats.
