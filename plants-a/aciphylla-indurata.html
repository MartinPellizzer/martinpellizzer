Aciphylla indurata

Aciphylla indurata, commonly known as the hard-leaved spinifex, is a perennial shrub native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its dense, spiny foliage that forms a low, mat-like growth. Aciphylla indurata is well adapted to rocky and sandy soils, making it a hardy species in coastal and inland regions.
