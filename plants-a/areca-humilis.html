Areca humilis

Areca humilis, commonly known as the dwarf areca palm, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized palm with a slender trunk and feather-like leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that eventually develop into edible fruits. Areca humilis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to warm, humid climates.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for landscaping in tropical and subtropical regions.
