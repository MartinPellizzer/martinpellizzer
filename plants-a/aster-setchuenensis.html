Aster setchuenensis

Aster setchuenensis, commonly known as the Sichuan aster, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions and has lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture. Its flowers are usually blue to purple in color, with ray florets surrounding a central disk.

Aster setchuenensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
