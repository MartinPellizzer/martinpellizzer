Andromeda paniculata var. foliosiflora

Andromeda paniculata var. foliosiflora, commonly known as the flowering andromeda, is a species of flowering plant in the Ericaceae family.

It is native to parts of East Asia, including China, Japan, and Korea. The plant is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of small, bell-shaped white or pink flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.
