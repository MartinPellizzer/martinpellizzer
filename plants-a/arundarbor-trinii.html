Arundarbor trinii

Arundarbor trinii, commonly known as the silverleaf orchid, is a flowering plant native to the humid forests of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking silver-green foliage and delicate, fragrant blooms. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to tree trunks without deriving nutrients from them. Its roots form a symbiotic relationship with mycorrhizal fungi, aiding in nutrient absorption.

Arundarbor trinii prefers shaded environments and thrives in high humidity, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.
