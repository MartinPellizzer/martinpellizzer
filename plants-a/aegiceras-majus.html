Aegiceras majus

Aegiceras majus, commonly known as the mangrove apple or sea apple, is a small evergreen tree native to the coastal regions of Southeast Asia and the Indian subcontinent.

Aegiceras majus plays an important ecological role in stabilizing coastal soils and providing habitat for various marine and terrestrial species.
