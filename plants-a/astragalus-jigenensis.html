Astragalus jigenensis

Astragalus jigenensis, commonly known as the Chinese milkvetch, is a perennial legume native to China.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant produces pods that contain several seeds, and it thrives in dry, rocky environments. It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes many nitrogen-fixing plants.

Astragalus jigenensis is often used in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.
