Aster mangtaoensis

Aster mangtaoensis, commonly known as the Mangtao aster, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically features showy purple or blue flowers that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists.
