Asplenium forisiense var. majus

Asplenium forisiense var. majus, commonly known as the giant shield fern, is a variant of the shield fern genus Asplenium.

It is characterized by its large, broad, and leathery fronds that can grow up to several feet in length. The plant typically thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its fronds are usually dark green with a glossy surface and may have a distinctive, segmented appearance.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
