Agave univittata var. ensifera

Agave univittata var. ensifera, commonly known as the sword agave, is a succulent plant native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its tall, narrow, grass-like leaves that are often blue-green in color. The plant produces a tall flowering stalk that bears clusters of white or pale yellow flowers. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it suitable for arid and semi-arid climates.

Its striking appearance makes it a popular choice for xeriscaping and rock gardens.
