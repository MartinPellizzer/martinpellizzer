Anemone globosa var. lithophila

Anemone globosa var. lithophila, commonly known as the rock anemone, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to rocky and alpine environments.

It features showy, white to pink flowers with a distinctive central pistil and prominent stamens. The plant typically grows in crevices or on rock surfaces, adapting to poor soil conditions. Its leaves are basal, spoon-shaped, and often covered in a fine, silvery down.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in challenging, rocky habitats.
