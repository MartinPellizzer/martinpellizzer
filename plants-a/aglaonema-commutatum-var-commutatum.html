Aglaonema commutatum var. commutatum

Aglaonema commutatum var. commutatum, commonly known as the Chinese Evergreen, is a tropical evergreen plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features striking, variegated leaves with shades of white, green, and pink, which are often adorned with red or pink veins. The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit and can reach heights of up to 3 meters in its natural habitat. It thrives in low-light conditions and requires moderate humidity and well-draining soil.

Its foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental value and low maintenance requirements.
