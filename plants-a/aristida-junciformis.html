Aristida junciformis

Aristida junciformis, commonly known as slender dropseed, is a perennial grass native to the United States.

It typically grows in open, dry habitats such as prairies, woodlands, and disturbed soils. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bloom in late summer. Its fine, silver-green foliage makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardens and erosion control.

It is also valued for its ability to thrive in poor soils and its low maintenance requirements.
