Anthurium yankuamense

Anthurium yankuamense, commonly known as the Yankuamense arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to parts of Central America.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive inflorescence consisting of a spadix and spathe. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are typically deep green with a waxy texture. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Anthurium yankuamense is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in the right conditions.
