Aloe rubrolutea

Aloe rubrolutea, commonly known as the red-and-yellow aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically red or yellow on the edges and green on the inside. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with tubular, orange to red flowers. It is adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Aloe rubrolutea is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional medicinal uses.
