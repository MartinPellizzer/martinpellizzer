Artemisia heribaudii

Artemisia heribaudii, commonly known as wormwood, is a flowering plant in the sunflower family.

It is native to Europe, western Asia, and North Africa, and it thrives in dry, sunny environments. The plant is characterized by its aromatic, silver-gray foliage and small yellow flowers. It contains volatile oils and is often used in traditional medicine.

However, it is toxic in large quantities and should be handled with care.
