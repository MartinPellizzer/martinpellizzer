Agretta

Agretta, commonly known as sea holly, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its spiny, leaf-like bracts that surround its small, yellow flower heads. Native to coastal regions of the Mediterranean, it thrives in sandy or rocky soils. The plant is often found in maritime environments, where it withstands salt spray and strong winds.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
