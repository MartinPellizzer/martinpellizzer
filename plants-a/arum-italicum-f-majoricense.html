Arum italicum f. majoricense

Arum italicum f. majoricense, commonly known as the Majorican arrowhead, is a striking tropical plant native to the island of Majorca.

It features heart-shaped, arrowhead-like leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces striking red spadices surrounded by white or pale yellow spatulate bracts, creating a dramatic visual display. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

This variety is a popular choice for tropical gardens and indoor plant collections due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
