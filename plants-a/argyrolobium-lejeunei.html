Argyrolobium lejeunei

Argyrolobium lejeunei, commonly known as the silver globular pea, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its distinctive spherical seed pods. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its unique appearance and ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
