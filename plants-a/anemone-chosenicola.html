Anemone chosenicola

Anemone chosenicola, commonly known as the African anemone, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features basal leaves that are typically heart-shaped and covered in a dense layer of fine, white hairs. The plant produces showy, pink to purple flowers with five petals, often surrounded by a ring of white ray florets. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.

Anemone chosenicola is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
