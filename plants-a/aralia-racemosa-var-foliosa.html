Aralia racemosa var. foliosa

Aralia racemosa var. foliosa, commonly known as the American aralia, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern North America.

It features compound leaves that are typically palmately divided, giving it a distinctive, fern-like appearance. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers in upright clusters, which are followed by spiny, berry-like fruits. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden landscapes.
