Agoseris pumila

Agoseris pumila, commonly known as the dwarf agoseris, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit. The plant features small, yellow to pale green composite flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are typically gray-green with a fuzzy texture, and it thrives in dry, rocky soils.
