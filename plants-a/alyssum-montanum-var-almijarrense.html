Alyssum montanum var. almijarrense

Alyssum montanum var. almijarrense, commonly known as the Almijarrense alyssum, is a flowering plant native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It is a low-growing perennial herb characterized by its dense clusters of small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and is typically found in rocky or gravelly habitats. It is well adapted to dry conditions and thrives in full sun.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in rock gardens and borders.
