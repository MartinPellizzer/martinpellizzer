Arisaema speciosum var. mirabile

Arisaema speciosum var. mirabile, commonly known as the Chinese dragon's tongue flower, is a striking ornamental plant native to China.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its unique inflorescence consisting of a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe. The plant features arrow-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white and green patterns, adding visual interest. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.

Its growth habit and foliage make it a popular choice for both horticultural and botanical collections.
