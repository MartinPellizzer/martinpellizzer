Avena requienii

Avena requienii, commonly known as the Canary Island oat, is a perennial grass species native to the Canary Islands.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences with numerous tiny florets. Avena requienii is adapted to the arid and coastal climates of its native habitat.

It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
