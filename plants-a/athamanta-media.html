Athamanta media

Athamanta media, commonly known as the common rock rose, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features compact, upright growth with small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant has leathery, evergreen leaves that are typically gray-green in hue. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Athamanta media is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
