Amaranthus × aellenii

Amaranthus × aellenii, commonly known as the red amaranth, is a hybrid plant species within the Amaranthaceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant red to purple foliage and branched inflorescences that produce small, clustered flowers. The plant is native to the southern United States and is often found in disturbed soils, such as roadsides and fields. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a hardy and adaptable species.

Amaranthus × aellenii is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine in some regions.
