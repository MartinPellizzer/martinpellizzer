Anthemis deserti

Anthemis deserti, commonly known as the desert ragwort, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that consist of both ray and disk flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, sandy soils and is well adapted to withstand high temperatures and limited water availability. Its yellowish-green flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, and the plant is often found in desert scrub and grassland habitats.

Anthemis deserti is also known for its medicinal properties, with some traditional uses in herbal remedies.
