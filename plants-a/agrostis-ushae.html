Agrostis ushae

Agrostis ushae, commonly known as the Usha grass, is a species of grass native to the high-altitude regions of the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its slender, tufted growth and fine, needle-like leaves. This plant typically thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine meadows and rocky slopes. Agrostis ushae has a low-growing habit and is adapted to withstand harsh climatic conditions.

Its botanical features make it an important component of the alpine flora in its native habitat.
