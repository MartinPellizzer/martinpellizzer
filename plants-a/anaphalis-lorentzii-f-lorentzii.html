Anaphalis lorentzii f. lorentzii

Anaphalis lorentzii f. lorentzii, commonly known as the silverleaf goatweed, is a flowering plant in the aster family (Asteraceae).

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, dense hairs. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow composite flowers that bloom in late summer. It is native to regions of Central and South America, where it typically grows in dry, open habitats.

Anaphalis lorentzii f. lorentzii is often used in ornamental gardening for its distinctive appearance and drought tolerance.
