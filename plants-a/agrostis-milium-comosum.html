Agrostis milium-comosum

Agrostis milium-comosum, commonly known as the slender meadowgrass, is a perennial grass species native to various parts of Europe and Asia.

It typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows, marshes, and along stream banks. The plant has slender, erect stems that can reach heights of up to 60 centimeters. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green color and a hairy texture.

The inflorescence consists of a single spikelet that is often surrounded by a dense cluster of bristles, giving it a distinctive appearance.
