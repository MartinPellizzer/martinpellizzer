Alocasia porphyroneura

Alocasia porphyroneura, commonly known as the red-veined alocasia, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that display vibrant, deep green with striking red or purple veins. The plant belongs to the Araceae family, which includes other ornamental plants like philodendrons and calla lilies. Alocasia porphyroneura thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical landscapes.
