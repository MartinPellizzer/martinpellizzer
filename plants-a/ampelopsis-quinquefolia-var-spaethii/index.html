Ampelopsis Quinquefolia Var. Spaethii

Ampelopsis quinquefolia var. spaethii is a striking variant of the common bittersweet vine known for its vibrant autumn foliage. This plant is native to eastern North America and thrives in moist, wooded areas. Its heart-shaped leaves and clusters of orange-red berries make it a popular choice for ornamental gardens. The varietal form spaethii is distinguished by its unique leaf structure and enhanced fall color. It is both a beautiful addition to landscapes and an important food source for wildlife.
