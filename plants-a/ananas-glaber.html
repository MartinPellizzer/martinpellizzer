Ananas glaber

Ananas glaber, commonly known as the pineapple, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial with a rosette of sword-shaped leaves that form a dense, spiky structure. The plant produces a distinctive, fleshy fruit that develops from a central flower cluster. Its leaves are covered in a waxy cuticle and have sharp, pointed tips.

The plant is known for its high water content and is widely cultivated for its edible fruit.
