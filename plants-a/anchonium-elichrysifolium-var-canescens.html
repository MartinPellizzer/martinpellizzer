Anchonium elichrysifolium var. canescens

Anchonium elichrysifolium var. canescens, commonly known as the silverleaf anchonium, is a perennial herb native to arid regions of southern Africa.

It features narrow, linear leaves that are covered in a dense layer of fine, silvery hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the warmer months. Its root system is deep and fibrous, allowing it to thrive in poor, sandy soils.

This species is well adapted to drought conditions, making it a hardy plant in semi-desert environments.
