Annona hystricoides

Annona hystricoides, commonly known as the spiny tropical apple, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its large, spiny fruit that resembles a prickly pear. The plant produces white or pale yellow flowers and has a dense, evergreen canopy. Its fruit is edible, though the skin is thick and often covered in sharp spines.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and has traditional uses in some cultures.
