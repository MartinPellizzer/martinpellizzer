Asplenium drueryii

Asplenium drueryii, commonly known as the Druery's spleenwort, is a small, epiphytic fern native to tropical regions of Africa.

It typically grows on tree trunks or rocks, thriving in humid, shaded environments. The plant has a compact rosette of lance-shaped, dark green leaves that are usually densely packed. Its reproductive structures, known as sori, are located on the undersides of the fronds.

Asplenium drueryii is characterized by its delicate appearance and is often found in montane or lowland rainforests.
