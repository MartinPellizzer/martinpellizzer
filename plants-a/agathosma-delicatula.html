Agathosma delicatula

Agathosma delicatula, commonly known as the sweet geranium, is a small, evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It features narrow, aromatic leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. The plant produces small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in spring and summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Its essential oils are valued for their aromatic properties and are used in perfumes and aromatherapy.
