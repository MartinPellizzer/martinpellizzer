Aphyllorchis maliauensis

Aphyllorchis maliauensis, commonly known as the Maliauensis orchid, is a rare and unique orchid species native to Borneo.

It is characterized by its small, compact growth habit and distinctive floral structure. The plant typically produces a single, showy flower with a yellow lip and greenish sepals. It thrives in the shaded, humid environments of lowland rainforests.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species.
