Anemonoides uralensis

Anemonoides uralensis, commonly known as the Ural anemone, is a herbaceous perennial native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features showy, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring, often before the leaves fully emerge. The plant has a short, thick rhizome and lance-shaped leaves that are deeply lobed. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.

Anemonoides uralensis is valued in gardens for its early blooming habit and ornamental appeal.
