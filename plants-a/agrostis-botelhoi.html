Agrostis botelhoi

Agrostis botelhoi, commonly known as the Botelho's bentgrass, is a species of grass native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial plant with narrow, grass-like leaves and small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and along riverbanks. Its botanical features include a fibrous root system and small, wind-pollinated flowers.

Agrostis botelhoi is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in stabilizing soil in its native habitat.
