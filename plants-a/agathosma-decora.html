Agathosma decora

Agathosma decora, commonly known as Cape aster, is a perennial shrub native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, small, greenish-yellow flowers. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to coastal and mountainous regions.

Agathosma decora is also valued for its essential oils, which are used in aromatherapy and traditional medicine.
