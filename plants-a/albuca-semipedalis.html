Albuca semipedalis

Albuca semipedalis, commonly known as the narrow-leaved albuca, is a bulbous plant native to southern Africa.

It features grass-like, narrow leaves that are often tinged with reddish or purple hues. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and seasonal blooms.
