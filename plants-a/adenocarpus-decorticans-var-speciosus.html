Adenocarpus decorticans var. speciosus

Adenocarpus decorticans var. speciosus, commonly known as the ornamental burr plant, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, spiny, spherical fruits that enclose the seeds. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters, adding visual interest to its appearance. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, contributing to its overall herbaceous structure.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to dry, well-drained soils.
