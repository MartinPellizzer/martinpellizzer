Anthurium gaudichaudianum var. libonianum

Anthurium gaudichaudianum var. libonianum, commonly known as the Libonian anthurium, is a member of the Araceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. Its striking spadix, which is often red or orange, is surrounded by a colorful spathe.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ability to thrive in indirect light and humid conditions.
