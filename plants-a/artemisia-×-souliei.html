Artemisia × souliei

Artemisia × souliei, commonly known as Chinese tarragon, is a hybrid shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its aromatic, silver-gray foliage and small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and medicinal properties. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its essential oils contain compounds like camphor and thujone, which contribute to its distinctive scent and potential therapeutic uses.
