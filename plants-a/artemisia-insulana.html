Artemisia insulana

Artemisia insulana, commonly known as silverleaf sage, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which results from dense covering of fine hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in compact clusters, typically blooming in the summer months. Artemisia insulana is often found in rocky or well-drained soils, thriving in full sun.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and aromatic properties.
