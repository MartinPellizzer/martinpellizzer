Aponogeton bernierianus

Aponogeton bernierianus, commonly known as the Bernier's water arrowhead, is a aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features long, narrow leaves that are typically submerged or floating, with a distinctive arrowhead shape. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom above the water surface. It is often cultivated in ponds and water gardens for its ornamental value.

Its rhizomes are also used in traditional medicine in some regions.
