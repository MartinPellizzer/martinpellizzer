Aria thaiszii

Aria thaiszii, commonly known as the Thai arrowroot, is a herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family, which includes many tropical plants characterized by their distinctive inflorescences. The plant features broad, arrow-shaped leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. Its rhizomes are edible and have a starchy texture similar to taro.

Aria thaiszii is valued for both its nutritional properties and its role in local ecosystems.
