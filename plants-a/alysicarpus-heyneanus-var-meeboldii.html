Alysicarpus heyneanus var. meeboldii

Alysicarpus heyneanus var. meeboldii, commonly known as the African burr bean, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

The plant produces distinctive, spiny pods that contain small, kidney-shaped seeds. Its leaves are trifoliate, with three oval-shaped leaflets, and it typically grows as an annual herb.
