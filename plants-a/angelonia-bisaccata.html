Angelonia bisaccata

Angelonia bisaccata, commonly known as the summer snapdragon, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It is a perennial shrub that produces vibrant, tubular flowers in shades of red, pink, and purple. The plant has lance-shaped leaves and thrives in full sun to partial shade. It is valued for its long blooming period, often lasting from summer through fall.

Angelonia bisaccata is commonly used in gardens and landscapes for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
