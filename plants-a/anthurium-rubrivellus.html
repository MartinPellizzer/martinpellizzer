Anthurium rubrivellus

Anthurium rubrivellus, commonly known as the red-veined anthurium, is a tropical flowering plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves with vibrant red veins against a deep green background. The plant produces clusters of bright red spathes that enclose small, inconspicuous flowers. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium rubrivellus is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
