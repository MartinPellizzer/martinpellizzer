Androrchis × serraniana

Androrchis × serraniana, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a naturally occurring hybrid between Androrchis apus and Androrchis serratus.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its delicate, greenish-yellow flowers with a distinctive red or purple lip. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb nutrients from the soil.

Botanically, it is notable for its complex hybrid origin and its role in orchid hybridization studies.
