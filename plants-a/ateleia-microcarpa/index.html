Ateleia Microcarpa

Ateleia microcarpa is a small, evergreen tree native to the tropical regions of South America. It is known for its delicate, fragrant flowers and its ability to thrive in humid, shaded environments. This plant is often found in the understory of dense rainforests, where it plays a vital role in the ecosystem. Its unique appearance and ecological importance make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists. Despite its beauty, Ateleia microcarpa faces threats from habitat loss and deforestation.
