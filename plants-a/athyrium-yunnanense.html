Athyrium yunnanense

Athyrium yunnanense, commonly known as Yunnan shield fern, is a species of fern native to China.

It belongs to the family Athyriaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, shield-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forests and rocky areas. Its leaves are bi- or tri-pinnate, with a delicate, feathery appearance.

Athyrium yunnanense is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
