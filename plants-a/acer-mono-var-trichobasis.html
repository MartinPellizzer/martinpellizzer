Acer mono var. trichobasis

Acer mono var. trichobasis, commonly known as the trichobasis maple, is a variant of the single-leaf maple.

It is characterized by its unique, elongated leaves that are typically trifoliate, with each leaflet having a hairy underside. The plant is native to the mountainous regions of southeastern China. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in forested areas.

Its ornamental value is due to its striking foliage and distinctive leaf structure.
