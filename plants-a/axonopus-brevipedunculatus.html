Axonopus brevipedunculatus

Axonopus brevipedunculatus, commonly known as the small-bracted axonopus, is a flowering plant belonging to the grass family (Poaceae).

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in wetland or marshy habitats, often forming dense mats near water bodies. Axonopus brevipedunculatus is adapted to aquatic or semi-aquatic environments, making it an important component of wetland ecosystems.
