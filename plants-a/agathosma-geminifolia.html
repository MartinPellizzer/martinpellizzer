Agathosma geminifolia

Agathosma geminifolia, commonly known as African germander, is a small, evergreen shrub native to the Western Cape of South Africa.

It features aromatic, needle-like leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring, attracting pollinators such as bees. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Its essential oils are valued for their antiseptic and anti-inflammatory properties.
