Alismorkis reflexa

Alismorkis reflexa, commonly known as the narrow-leaf water plantain, is a semi-aquatic plant native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

The plant has slender, ribbon-like leaves that are often submerged or floating, with a waxy surface that helps reduce water loss. Its small, greenish-white flowers are arranged in dense spikes and bloom in early summer.
