Artemisia biebersteinii

Artemisia biebersteinii, commonly known as the desert sage, is a perennial shrub native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its aromatic, gray-green foliage and small yellow flower clusters. The plant is adapted to arid environments, featuring silvery leaves that reflect sunlight and reduce water loss. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes many other aromatic herbs and flowering plants.

Artemisia biebersteinii is often used in traditional medicine and landscaping due to its hardiness and ornamental value.
