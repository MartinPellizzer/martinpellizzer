Allium habibii

Allium habibii, commonly known as the Habibii onion, is a bulbous plant native to the arid regions of Iran.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its tall, cylindrical leaves and umbel-like flower clusters. Due to its limited distribution, Allium habibii is considered a rare and lesser-known species within the Allium genus.
