Afrothismia foertheriana

Afrothismia foertheriana, commonly known as the African orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Orchidaceae and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and green, typical of many orchid species. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Due to its ornamental value and limited distribution, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation programs.
