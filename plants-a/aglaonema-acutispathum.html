Aglaonema acutispathum

Aglaonema acutispathum, commonly known as the Shiny Shamrock, is a tropical evergreen plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are typically oval or heart-shaped. The plant features striking white and cream-colored veins that contrast beautifully with its deep foliage. It thrives in low-light conditions and requires moderate watering to maintain its vibrant appearance.

Aglaonema acutispathum is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and air-purifying properties.
