Achimenes candida

Achimenes candida, commonly known as the white trumpet vine, is a tropical herbaceous plant native to Central and South America.

It features showy, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink, often with a contrasting throat. The plant produces oval, hairy leaves that are arranged alternately along the stems. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Achimenes candida is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
