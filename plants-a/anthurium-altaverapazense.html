Anthurium altaverapazense

Anthurium altaverapazense, commonly known as the Altaverapazense arrowhead, is a species of flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Central America.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive inflorescence consisting of a spadix and a spathe. The plant features broad, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green color with a glossy texture. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Anthurium altaverapazense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in botanical gardens.
