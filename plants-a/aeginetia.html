Aeginetia

Aeginetia, commonly known as the sandmat, is a genus of flowering plants belonging to the family Amaranthaceae.

These plants are typically found in arid and semi-arid regions, where they thrive in sandy or gravelly soils. Aeginetia species are characterized by their small, clustered flowers and hairy, often succulent leaves.
