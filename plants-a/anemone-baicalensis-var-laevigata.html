Anemone baicalensis var. laevigata

Anemone baicalensis var. laevigata, commonly known as the smooth anemone, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of East Asia.

It features showy, white to pinkish flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has finely divided, fern-like leaves that are typically smooth or slightly hairy. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
