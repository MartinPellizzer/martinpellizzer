Anemopaegma nigrescens

Anemopaegma nigrescens, commonly known as the black anemopaegma, is a flowering plant native to the Andes Mountains of South America.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in high-altitude environments, often in rocky or alpine soils. Anemopaegma nigrescens is adapted to cold climates and is known for its resilience in harsh, mountainous conditions.
