Aristolochia delavayi var. micrantha

Aristolochia delavayi var. micrantha, commonly known as the small-flowered Dutchman's pipe, is a climbing vine native to China.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive waxy texture and emits a strong, often unpleasant odor. The plant produces intricate, pipe-shaped flowers that are typically greenish-yellow with a prominent hood. It belongs to the Aristolochiaceae family and is known for its medicinal properties in traditional Chinese medicine.

However, it contains toxic compounds and should be handled with care.
