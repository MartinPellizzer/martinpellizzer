Argyrocome condensata

Argyrocome condensata, commonly known as the silverleaf lily.

It is a flowering plant native to South Africa, characterized by its striking silver-green foliage and showy white to pink flowers. The plant belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.
