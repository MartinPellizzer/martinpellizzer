Actinidia eriantha f. alba

Actinidia eriantha f. alba, commonly known as the white-flowered actinidia, is a variant of the actinidia genus native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its white flowers that bloom in early summer, contrasting with the typical green or yellow flowers of the species. The plant produces small, edible fruits that are often used in traditional Chinese medicine. Its leaves are heart-shaped and have a slightly serrated edge, contributing to its ornamental value.

This species is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal and potential medicinal properties.
