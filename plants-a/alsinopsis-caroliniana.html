Alsinopsis caroliniana

It is a member of the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, sandy soils along stream banks and in wetlands. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds.
