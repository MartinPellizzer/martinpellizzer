Andinia Auriculata

Andinia auriculata is a rare and striking plant native to the cloud forests of South America. Its most distinctive feature is its large, ear-shaped leaves that catch the morning light. This species thrives in cool, humid environments with high levels of shade. Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is highly sought after by plant enthusiasts. Conservation efforts are crucial to protect this delicate species from habitat loss.
