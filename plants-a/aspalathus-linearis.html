Aspalathus linearis

Aspalathus linearis, commonly known as the rooibos plant, is a shrub native to the Western Cape region of South Africa.

It is characterized by its small, needle-like leaves and is closely related to the legume family. The plant is unique in that it does not have true leaves, and its flowers are small and inconspicuous. Rooibos is renowned for its rich content of antioxidants and flavonoids, which contribute to its health benefits.

It is also notable for its ability to thrive in arid conditions, making it an important crop in its native habitat.
