Arctotis staticifolia

Arctotis staticifolia, commonly known as the sticky arctotis, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, daisy-like flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant produces sticky secretions on its stems and leaves, which can deter herbivores. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Arctotis staticifolia is often cultivated for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
