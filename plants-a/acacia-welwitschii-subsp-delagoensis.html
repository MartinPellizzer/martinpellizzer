Acacia welwitschii subsp. delagoensis

Acacia welwitschii subsp.

delagoensis, commonly known as the Delagoa acacia, is a shrub or small tree native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its slender, often multi-branched stems and bipinnate leaves that are typically reduced to small, scale-like structures.

The plant produces yellow to orange spherical pods that contain several seeds. It is well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, showing drought tolerance and deep root systems.

This subspecies is also valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
