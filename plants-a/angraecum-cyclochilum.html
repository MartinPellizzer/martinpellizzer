Angraecum cyclochilum

Angraecum cyclochilum, commonly known as the cyclochilum orchid, is a species native to Madagascar.

It is a epiphytic orchid that grows on trees and rocks, thriving in humid, tropical environments. The plant features long, narrow leaves and produces striking, fragrant flowers with a unique spiral-shaped lip. Its flowering period typically occurs during the rainy season, and it is known for its ability to bloom at night.

Angraecum cyclochilum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
