Arisaema rubrirhizomatum

Arisaema rubrirhizomatum, commonly known as the red-rooted arisaema, is a herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its unique inflorescence consisting of a spadix surrounded by a spathe. The plant features a rhizomatous root system, which is typically reddish in color, giving it its common name. Its leaves are arrow-shaped and often have a velvety texture, while the flowers are small and clustered.

Arisaema rubrirhizomatum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine in some regions.
