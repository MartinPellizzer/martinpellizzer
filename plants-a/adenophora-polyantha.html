Adenophora polyantha

Adenophora polyantha, commonly known as the Chinese bellflower, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in late summer. The plant has hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves, and it thrives in moist, well-drained soils. It is often found in woodland areas and along streambanks.

Adenophora polyantha is valued for its ornamental beauty and is used in traditional Chinese medicine.
