Artemisia steveniana

Artemisia steveniana, commonly known as silverleaf sage, is a perennial shrub native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, dense hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, rocky habitats.

Artemisia steveniana has aromatic leaves that emit a strong, pungent scent when crushed.
