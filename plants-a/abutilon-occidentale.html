Abutilon occidentale

Abutilon occidentale, commonly known as the velvetleaf or Chinese lantern plant, is a flowering shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are typically velvety and green, with a distinctive waxy texture. The plant produces lantern-shaped seed pods that turn red when mature, adding ornamental value. It thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Abutilon occidentale is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal and is also used in traditional medicine.
