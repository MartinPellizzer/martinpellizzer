Asperula cynanchica var. scabrida

Asperula cynanchica var. scabrida, commonly known as the rough asperula, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and western Asia.

It is a low-growing evergreen shrub with small, leathery leaves that have a rough, textured surface. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white or pale pink flowers in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and partial shade, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a ground cover.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental foliage make it a valued addition to gardens.
