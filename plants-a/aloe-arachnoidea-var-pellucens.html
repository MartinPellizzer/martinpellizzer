Aloe arachnoidea var. pellucens

Aloe arachnoidea var. pellucens, commonly known as the translucent aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, cylindrical stems covered in a thin, waxy cuticle. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers during the summer months. Its leaves are typically narrow and somewhat translucent, giving it a unique appearance.

It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
