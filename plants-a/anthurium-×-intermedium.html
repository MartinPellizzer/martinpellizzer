Anthurium × intermedium

Anthurium × intermedium, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a hybrid plant native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a reddish or purple tint. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-shaped inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, which can range from red to orange or yellow. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium × intermedium is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and long-lasting blooms.
