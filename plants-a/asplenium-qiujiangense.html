Asplenium qiujiangense

Asplenium qiujiangense, commonly known as the Qiujiang shield fern, is a rare and endemic species native to China.

It belongs to the family Aspleniaceae and is characterized by its compact, evergreen foliage. The plant features triangular, serrated fronds that are typically dark green and glossy. It thrives in shaded, moist environments, often found in rocky or forested areas.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a valuable subject for botanical research and conservation efforts.
