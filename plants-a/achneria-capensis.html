Achneria capensis

Achneria capensis, commonly known as the Cape honeysuckle, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, featuring opposite, simple, and often hairy leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular, fragrant flowers that are usually yellow or orange in color. It blooms throughout the year, making it a valuable plant for attracting pollinators.

The species is well adapted to various soil types and can thrive in full sun to partial shade.
