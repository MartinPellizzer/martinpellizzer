Agation comptonii

Agation comptonii, commonly known as the Compton's agate plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, cylindrical stems that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid conditions. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. Its leaves are typically green and covered in a fine, powdery coating that helps reduce water loss.

Agation comptonii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and resilience.
