Angraecum boutonii

Angraecum boutonii, commonly known as the Christmas star, is a species of orchid native to the mountainous regions of South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, star-like white flowers that bloom during the winter months. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences with numerous small, fragrant flowers. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Angraecum boutonii is also noted for its ability to survive in arid conditions, making it a resilient choice for collectors and gardeners.
