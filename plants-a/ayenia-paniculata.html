Ayenia paniculata

Ayenia paniculata, commonly known as the clustered ayenia, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant produces showy, yellow to orange composite flowers that bloom in late summer to early fall. Its leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and often covered in fine hairs.

Ayenia paniculata is typically found in moist, wooded areas and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
