Anthotium rubriflorum

Anthotium rubriflorum, commonly known as the red-flowered anthotium, is a species of flowering plant native to the Caribbean.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its unique inflorescence consisting of a spadix surrounded by a spathe. The plant features heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a smooth texture and prominent veins. Its striking red flowers are borne in clusters, making it a visually appealing ornamental plant.

Anthotium rubriflorum thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.
