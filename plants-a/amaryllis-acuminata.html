Amaryllis acuminata

Amaryllis acuminata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved amaryllis, is a bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features strap-shaped, narrow leaves that are typically a deep green color. The plant produces striking flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, often with a contrasting white or yellow center. It blooms in late summer to early autumn, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Amaryllis acuminata prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
