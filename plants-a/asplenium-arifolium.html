Asplenium arifolium

Asplenium arifolium, commonly known as the fan-leaf maidenhair fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Aspleniaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, finely divided, and often arching fronds that give it a graceful appearance. Native to tropical and subtropical regions, this plant thrives in moist, shaded environments. Its leaves are typically dark green and have a leathery texture, which helps it retain moisture.

Asplenium arifolium is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and ability to thrive in indoor conditions.
