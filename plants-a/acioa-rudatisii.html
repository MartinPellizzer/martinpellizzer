Acioa rudatisii

Acioa rudatisii, commonly known as the rough acacia, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is characterized by its leguminous properties and nitrogen-fixing capabilities. The plant features small, compound leaves and produces yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its seeds are encased in pods, and the plant is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.

Acioa rudatisii is often used in traditional medicine and for soil stabilization in degraded landscapes.
