Avena aristidoides

Avena aristidoides, commonly known as the slender oat, is a grass species native to the western United States and parts of Mexico.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny florets. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed areas.

Avena aristidoides is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to stabilize soil in arid environments.
