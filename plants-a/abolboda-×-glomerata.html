Abolboda × glomerata

Abolboda × glomerata, commonly known as the hybrid sweet potato.

It is a cross between Abolboda and Glomerata species, resulting in a plant with unique tuberous roots that are rich in starch. The leaves are typically heart-shaped with a velvety texture, and the plant thrives in warm, humid climates. It is cultivated for its edible roots, which have a sweet, nutty flavor.

This hybrid is valued for its adaptability and high yield in agricultural settings.
