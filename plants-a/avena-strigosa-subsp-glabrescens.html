Avena strigosa subsp. glabrescens

Avena strigosa subsp.

glabrescens, commonly known as slender oat, is a perennial grass native to North America. It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves.

The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous florets. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Botanically, it is valued for its ecological role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various wildlife species.
