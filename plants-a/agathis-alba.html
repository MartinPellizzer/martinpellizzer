Agathis alba

Agathis alba, commonly known as the white cedar, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the family Araucariaceae and is characterized by its tall, straight trunk and dense, dark green foliage. Agathis alba also plays a significant ecological role in its native habitats, providing shelter and food for various wildlife species.
