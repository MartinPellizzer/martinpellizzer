Adromischus marianiae

Adromischus marianiae, commonly known as the Cape snowflake, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, rounded leaves that are typically tinged with red or purple, especially when exposed to sunlight. The plant produces small, star-shaped white or pink flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Adromischus marianiae is valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
