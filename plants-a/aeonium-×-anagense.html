Aeonium × anagense

Aeonium × anagense, commonly known as the "Tree Aeonium," is a hybrid succulent plant native to the Canary Islands.

It features a compact, tree-like structure with a dense rosette of thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with red or pink edges. The leaves are arranged in a spiral pattern around a short, sturdy stem, giving it a unique and striking appearance. It produces vibrant yellow or orange flowers in late spring or early summer, which are borne on tall, branching inflorescences.

This plant is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
