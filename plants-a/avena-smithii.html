Avena smithii

Avena smithii, commonly known as the slender oat, is a perennial grass species native to the western United States.

It typically grows in rocky or open habitats, often in mountainous regions. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. Its flowers are wind-pollinated, and it is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.

Avena smithii is valued for its ecological role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for native wildlife.
