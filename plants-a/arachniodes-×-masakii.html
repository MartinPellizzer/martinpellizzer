Arachniodes × masakii

Arachniodes × masakii, commonly known as the laceleaf fern, is a hybrid plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, finely divided, and often lace-like fronds that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte or terrestrial fern, thriving in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are usually dark green and can reach lengths of up to 1 meter.

Arachniodes × masakii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and is often cultivated in shaded garden settings.
