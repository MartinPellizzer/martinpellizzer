Aloe delicatifolia

Aloe delicatifolia, commonly known as the delicate leaf aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of narrow, fleshy, and often bluish-green leaves that are covered in a fine layer of white powder. The plant produces tall, branching inflorescences with yellowish-orange flowers during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Aloe delicatifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
