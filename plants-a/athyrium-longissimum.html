Athyrium longissimum

Athyrium longissimum, commonly known as the long-stalked fern, is a species of fern native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender rhizomes and its delicate, bipinnate fronds that can reach lengths of up to 1.5 meters. The fronds are typically bright green and have a graceful, arching appearance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forests and along stream banks.

This plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
