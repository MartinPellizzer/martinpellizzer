Aloe tewoldei

Aloe tewoldei, commonly known as the Tewolde's aloe, is a species of flowering plant native to Ethiopia.

It belongs to the Asphodelaceae family and is characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves that store water, adapting it to arid environments. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and potentially endangered species.
