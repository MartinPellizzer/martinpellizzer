Anthurium versicolor var. versicolor

Anthurium versicolor var. versicolor, commonly known as the variegated anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage, with leaves that display a mix of green, white, and red hues. The plant produces bright red spathes that enclose its small, spadix-like inflorescence. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium versicolor var. versicolor is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
