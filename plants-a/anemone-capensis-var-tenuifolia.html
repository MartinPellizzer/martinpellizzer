Anemone capensis var. tenuifolia

Anemone capensis var. tenuifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved anemone, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features basal leaves that are narrow and linear, often with a hairy texture, and produces showy, white to pink flowers in early spring. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders. Its delicate appearance and seasonal bloom make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

It is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
