Arundina meyenii

Arundina meyenii, commonly known as the bamboo orchid, is a species of flowering plant in the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its tall, bamboo-like stems that can reach heights of up to 3 meters. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale green flowers that bloom in summer, adding ornamental value to gardens. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for tropical and subtropical climates.

Arundina meyenii is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal and ease of care in suitable environments.
