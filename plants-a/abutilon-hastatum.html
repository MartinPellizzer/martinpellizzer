Abutilon hastatum

Abutilon hastatum, commonly known as the velvetleaf abutilon, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its fuzzy, velvety leaves and showy, lantern-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant typically grows as an annual or perennial shrub, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
