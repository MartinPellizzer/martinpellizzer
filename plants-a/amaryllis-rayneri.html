Amaryllis rayneri

Amaryllis rayneri, commonly known as the red-tipped amaryllis, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers. The plant features strap-like leaves that are often a deep green color. Its flowers are typically pink or red with a white throat and are borne on tall, sturdy stalks.

Amaryllis rayneri is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
