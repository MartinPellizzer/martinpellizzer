Aglaonema simplex f. cordifolium

Aglaonema simplex f. cordifolium, commonly known as the heart-leaf aglaonema, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It features dark green, heart-shaped leaves with striking white and cream variegation, which add visual interest to any indoor space. The plant is highly tolerant of low light conditions and requires moderate watering to thrive. Its foliage remains vibrant throughout the year, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

It is also known for its air-purifying qualities, enhancing its appeal as a decorative and functional plant.
