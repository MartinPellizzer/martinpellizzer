Ayenia minytricha

Ayenia minytricha, commonly known as the narrow-leaved ayenia, is a flowering plant native to parts of South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in shrubland and grassland habitats, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are narrow and often covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are small and clustered in dense inflorescences.

Ayenia minytricha is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
