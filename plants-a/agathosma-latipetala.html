Agathosma latipetala

Agathosma latipetala, commonly known as the narrow-leaved bushveld sage, is a perennial shrub native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its aromatic, narrow leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant is well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, often growing in rocky soils. It is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional African medicine.

Its essential oils are also utilized in aromatherapy and as a natural remedy for various ailments.
