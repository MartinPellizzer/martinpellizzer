Agathisanthemum bojeri var. glabriflorum

Agathisanthemum bojeri var. glabriflorum, commonly known as the smoothflower agathisanthemum, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy yellow-orange composite flowers. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
