Alstroemeria × davisiae

Alstroemeria × davisiae, commonly known as the Davis hybrid alstroemeria, is a hybrid plant resulting from the cross between Alstroemeria aurea and Alstroemeria lutea.

It is native to South America and is characterized by its vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant typically grows as a herbaceous perennial with strap-like, grassy foliage. It prefers well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice in floral arrangements and ornamental horticulture.
