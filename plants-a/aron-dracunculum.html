Aron dracunculum

Aron dracunculum, commonly known as the dragon's blood tree, is a unique plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is characterized by its slow growth and distinctive umbrella-like canopy of thick, leathery leaves. The tree produces a red, resinous sap that has been historically used for medicinal and artistic purposes. Its botanical features include a thick, flaky bark and small, inconspicuous white flowers.

The plant is adapted to arid environments and is often found in rocky soils.
