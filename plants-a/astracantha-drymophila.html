Astracantha drymophila

Astracantha drymophila, commonly known as the narrowleaf starburst, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant produces small, bright yellow flowers that are surrounded by narrow, leaf-like bracts, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Astracantha drymophila is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
