Anthurium hickenii var. ovaliellipticum

Anthurium hickenii var. ovaliellipticum, commonly known as the oval-elliptic anthurium, is a striking tropical plant native to Central America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a glossy texture. The plant produces vibrant red spathes that enclose bright yellow spadices, creating a dramatic floral display. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

This variety is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
