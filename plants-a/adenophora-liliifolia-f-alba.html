Adenophora liliifolia f. alba

Adenophora liliifolia f. alba, commonly known as the white-flowered adenophora, is a cultivar of the adenophora species native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its pale to white flowers, which contrast with its grass-like, linear leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as woodland areas or along stream banks. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in garden settings for its delicate appearance.
