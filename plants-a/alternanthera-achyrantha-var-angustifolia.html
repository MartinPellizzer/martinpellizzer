Alternanthera achyrantha var. angustifolia

Alternanthera achyrantha var. angustifolia, commonly known as narrow-leaf velvetleaf, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to South America.

It features lance-shaped, dark green leaves with a velvety texture and prominent veins. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellowish in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental foliage rather than its flowers.
