Astrocalyx pleiosandra

Astrocalyx pleiosandra, commonly known as the star calyx pleiosandra, is a member of the Malvaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking, star-shaped calyces that encase the flowers, giving it a unique appearance. The plant typically features palmate leaves with a velvety texture and a variety of flower colors, including pink, red, and white. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in moist, shaded environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
