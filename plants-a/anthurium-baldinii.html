Anthurium baldinii

Anthurium baldinii, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are glossy and deep green. The plant produces vibrant red spathes that enclose its yellow spadix, creating a dramatic floral display. Anthurium baldinii thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

It is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental value and ability to purify indoor air.
