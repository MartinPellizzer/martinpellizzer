Actinodaphne philippinensis

Actinodaphne philippinensis, commonly known as the Philippine laurel, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae and is characterized by its aromatic, glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by spherical, fleshy fruits. Its bark and leaves are used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitat.
