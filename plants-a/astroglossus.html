Astroglossus

Astroglossus, commonly known as the star-shaped tongue plant, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Acanthaceae.

These plants are native to tropical regions of Africa and are characterized by their distinctive, star-like flowers. The leaves are typically opposite and lance-shaped, often with a velvety texture.
