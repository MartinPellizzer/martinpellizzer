Armeria Bigerrensis Subsp. Microcephala

Armeria bigerrensis subsp. microcephala is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Andes. It is characterized by its small, compact growth habit and striking clusters of pink or white flowers. This subspecies thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in rocky or alpine habitats. Due to its limited range and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species. Its beauty and ecological significance make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists alike.
