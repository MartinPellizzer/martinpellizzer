Abies nordmanniana f. tortifolia

Abies nordmanniana f. tortifolia, commonly known as the twisted foliage fir, is a rare and distinctive variant of the Nordmann fir.

It is characterized by its unique, twisted foliage that sets it apart from the typical straight needles of its parent species. This form is native to the Caucasus region and thrives in cool, moist climates with well-drained soil. The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for study in plant morphology and horticulture.
