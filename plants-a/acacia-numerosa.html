Acacia numerosa

Acacia numerosa, commonly known as the many-flowered acacia, is a shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compound leaves, which are often feathery and arranged in pairs. The plant produces yellow flowers in dense, spherical clusters, which are typical of the Acacia genus. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, showing adaptability to poor soils.

The species is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
