Astilbe rubra

Astilbe rubra, commonly known as red flame astilbe, is a herbaceous perennial native to eastern North America.

It features upright, branched inflorescences that produce showy, plume-like flowers in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant has finely divided, feather-like leaves that are often a deep green, providing a lush backdrop to its colorful blooms. It thrives in partial to full shade and moist, well-drained soil, making it ideal for woodland gardens.

Astilbe rubra is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to add texture and color to shaded landscapes.
