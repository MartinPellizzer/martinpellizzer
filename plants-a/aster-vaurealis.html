Aster vaurealis

Aster vaurealis, commonly known as white wood aster, is a herbaceous perennial native to North America.

It features upright stems covered in soft, grayish-green foliage. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale lavender flowers in the late summer and early fall. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Aster vaurealis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
