Achillea × serbani

Achillea × serbani, commonly known as the hybrid yarrow, is a perennial herb native to the Balkan Peninsula.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its flat, composite flower heads that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant typically grows to a height of 30–60 cm, with finely divided, gray-green leaves and a strong, aromatic scent. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy addition to gardens.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is often cultivated for both aesthetic and therapeutic purposes.
