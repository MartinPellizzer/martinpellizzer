Armeria maritima var. goodalliana

Armeria maritima var. goodalliana, commonly known as Goodall's thrift, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense clusters of small, pink or white flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and thrives in full sun. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are evergreen throughout the year.

It is often used in rock gardens and coastal landscapes due to its hardy nature and ornamental value.
