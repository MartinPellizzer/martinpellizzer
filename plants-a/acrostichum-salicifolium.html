Acrostichum salicifolium

Acrostichum salicifolium, commonly known as the silky fern, is a species of fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds that are often covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a soft appearance. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands. Its leaves are usually bipinnate, with small, lance-shaped pinnae that contribute to its ornamental value.

Acrostichum salicifolium plays a role in maintaining soil stability and providing habitat for various small organisms.
