Acianthera dasychila

Acianthera dasychila, commonly known as the woolly acianthera, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and hairy, or pubescent, foliage. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically pale green or yellowish in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and specialized orchid species.
