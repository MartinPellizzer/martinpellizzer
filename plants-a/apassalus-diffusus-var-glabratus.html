Apassalus diffusus var. glabratus

Apassalus diffusus var. glabratus, commonly known as the smooth apassalus, is a rare and lesser-known plant species characterized by its unique botanical features.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often found in temperate forest regions. The plant has elongated, lance-shaped leaves that are smooth and glabrous, giving it its varietal name. Its flowers are small, clustered in dense inflorescences, and are usually pale green to white in color.
