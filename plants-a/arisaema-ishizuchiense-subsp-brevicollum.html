Arisaema ishizuchiense subsp. brevicollum

Arisaema ishizuchiense subsp.

brevicollum, commonly known as the Ishizuchi arrowhead, is a rare and distinctive member of the Araceae family. It is characterized by its unique, arrowhead-shaped leaves and a striking, spadix-like inflorescence.

Native to Japan, this plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in mountainous regions. Its botanical features include a short, stout rhizome and a single, showy spathe that encloses the spadix.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a valuable species for botanical study and conservation efforts.
