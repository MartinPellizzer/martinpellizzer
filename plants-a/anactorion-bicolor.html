Anactorion bicolor

Anactorion bicolor, commonly known as the two-colored orchid, is a striking member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant features showy, bilaterally symmetrical flowers with two distinct color zones, typically white and purple. Its roots are adapted to absorb nutrients from the acidic soils of its natural habitat.

Anactorion bicolor thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
