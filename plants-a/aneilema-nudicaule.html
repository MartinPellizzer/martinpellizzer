Aneilema nudicaule

Aneilema nudicaule, commonly known as the slender aneilema, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. Its flowers are small and clustered in dense inflorescences, often with white or pale yellow petals.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
