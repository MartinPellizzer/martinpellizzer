Allium globosum var. stevenii

Allium globosum var. stevenii, commonly known as Steven's onion, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features a bulbous base with narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of small, white to pale purple flowers. The plant is characterized by its dense, spherical inflorescence, which gives it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or alpine settings.

This variety is valued for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its unique floral display.
