Aronia × heterophylla

Aronia × heterophylla, commonly known as the purple chokeberry, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its dark purple to black berries, which are rich in antioxidants and anthocyanins. The plant typically grows as a shrub, reaching heights of 1 to 3 meters, with alternate, ovate leaves that have serrated margins. Its flowers are small, white to pink, and clustered in corymbs, blooming in early summer.

The species is native to North America and is cultivated for both ornamental and edible purposes.
