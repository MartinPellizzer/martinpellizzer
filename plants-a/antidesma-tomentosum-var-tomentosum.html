Antidesma tomentosum var. tomentosum

Antidesma tomentosum var. tomentosum, commonly known as the hairy antidesma, is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub or tree native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, hairy foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The leaves are typically opposite, ovate, and covered with a fine pubescence. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by edible, fleshy fruits that are often consumed by local communities.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and has traditional uses in medicine and food.
