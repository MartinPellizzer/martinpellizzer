Asplenium laurentii

Asplenium laurentii, commonly known as the laurentii spleenwort, is a small evergreen fern native to tropical regions of Africa, Asia, and Australia.

It belongs to the Aspleniaceae family and is characterized by its compact, clustered growth habit. The plant features delicate, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found growing on rocks or tree trunks.

Asplenium laurentii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to care for in a humid indoor setting.
