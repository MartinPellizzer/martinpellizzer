Alysicarpus longifolius var. major

Alysicarpus longifolius var. major, commonly known as the large-leaved alysicarpus, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod-like fruits. The plant features trifoliate leaves with elongated, lance-shaped leaflets. Its flowers are typically small and clustered, often in racemes, and are usually pale yellow to greenish in color.
