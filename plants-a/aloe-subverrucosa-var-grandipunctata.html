Aloe subverrucosa var. grandipunctata

Aloe subverrucosa var. grandipunctata, commonly known as the giant-spined aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that are covered in prominent, sharp spines. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow flowers, typically blooming in summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for arid and semi-arid environments.

Due to its striking appearance, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
