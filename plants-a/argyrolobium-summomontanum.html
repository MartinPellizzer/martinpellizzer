Argyrolobium summomontanum

Argyrolobium summomontanum, commonly known as the mountain everlasting, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing, mat-forming perennial that produces small, pale yellow to white flowers. The plant has hairy stems and leaves, which help it retain moisture in its arid habitat. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy areas.

Its flowers bloom in late summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
