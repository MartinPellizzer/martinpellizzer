Allionia gigantea

Allionia gigantea, commonly known as the giant allionia, is a striking plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from pink to white, often with a contrasting red or purple throat. The plant features a robust, upright stem with lance-shaped leaves that are typically covered in a fine, white powder. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Allionia gigantea is known for its long blooming period, which can last several months during the warmer seasons.
