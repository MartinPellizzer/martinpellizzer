Arabis Sagittata Var. Lusitanica

Arabis sagittata var. lusitanica is a lesser-known yet strikingly beautiful plant native to southern Europe. This variant of Arabis sagittata is distinguished by its unique leaf shape and delicate flowers. It thrives in rocky and limestone soils, often found in alpine and coastal regions. Its foliage resembles arrows, giving it a distinctive and elegant appearance. Despite its beauty, it remains a rare and underappreciated member of the Brassicaceae family.
