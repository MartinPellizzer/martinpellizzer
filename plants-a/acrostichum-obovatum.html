Acrostichum obovatum

Acrostichum obovatum, commonly known as the broad-leaf hibiscus, is a member of the Malvaceae family.

It is a perennial plant characterized by its large, heart-shaped, obovate leaves that are often covered in a fine, white pubescence. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.
