Aspilia camporum

Aspilia camporum, commonly known as the mountain daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies, sunflowers, and other similar plants. The plant features showy yellow flowers surrounded by white ray florets, giving it a daisy-like appearance. It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in mountainous regions.
