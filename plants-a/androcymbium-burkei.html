Androcymbium burkei

Androcymbium burkei, commonly known as the Burke's orchid, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, intricate floral structure. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically pale yellow or greenish-white in color. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires specific soil conditions to flourish.
