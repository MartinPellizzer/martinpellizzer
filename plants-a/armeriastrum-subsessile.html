Armeriastrum subsessile

Armeriastrum subsessile, commonly known as the slender sea holly, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of California and Baja California.

It is a low-growing shrub with narrow, grass-like leaves and small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is adapted to coastal conditions, including salt spray and strong winds. It is often found in maritime chaparral and coastal dunes habitats.

Armeriastrum subsessile is an important component of the local flora, contributing to the biodiversity of its ecosystem.
