Arthrosolen fulgens

Arthrosolen fulgens, commonly known as the golden flame orchid, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant orange and yellow flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are long and strap-like, often covered in a waxy coating to retain moisture.

The flowers bloom in clusters, producing a dazzling display that attracts various pollinators.
