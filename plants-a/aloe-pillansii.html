Aloe pillansii

Aloe pillansii, commonly known as the Kleinman's aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, often with a waxy coating. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow flowers during the summer months. Aloe pillansii is adapted to arid environments, storing water in its leaves to survive droughts.

Its unique appearance and resilience make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
