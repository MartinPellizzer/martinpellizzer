Anthurium purpureum

Anthurium purpureum, commonly known as the velvet leaf anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a velvety texture. The plant produces striking spadices, which are inflorescences that resemble flowers but are actually modified leaves. Anthurium purpureum thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical landscapes.
