Agrostis turkestanica

Agrostis turkestanica, commonly known as the Turkestan meadowgrass, is a perennial grass species native to Central Asia.

It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spikelet-based flowers. It is characterized by its ability to thrive in arid conditions and poor soil.

Agrostis turkestanica plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing forage for livestock in its native regions.
