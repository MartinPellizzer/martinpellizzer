Amorphophallus niahensis

Amorphophallus niahensis, commonly known as the Niah arrowleaf, is a flowering plant native to Borneo.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves. The plant produces an inflorescence consisting of a spadix surrounded by a spathe, typical of the genus Amorphophallus. It thrives in tropical rainforest environments, often growing in shaded, moist habitats.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
