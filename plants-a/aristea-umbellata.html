Aristea umbellata

Aristea umbellata, commonly known as the white star grass, is a perennial flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, tufted growth habit and produces clusters of small, star-shaped white flowers. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Aristea umbellata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
