Aster subulatus var. australis

Aster subulatus var. australis, commonly known as the Australian starwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to southern Australia.

It features narrow, needle-like leaves that are typically covered in fine, silky hairs. The plant produces small, daisy-like flowers in shades of white and purple, which bloom from late winter to early spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy environments.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to tolerate harsh, dry conditions.
