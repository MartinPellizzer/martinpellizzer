Anemone × clarae

Anemone × clarae, commonly known as the hybrid anemone, is a perennial flowering plant that results from the cross between Anemone coronaria and Anemone hupehensis.

It features showy, cup-shaped blooms that range in color from white to pink and red, often with a distinctive central dark spot. The plant typically grows to a height of 12 to 24 inches and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are basal, forming a rosette, and are typically lance-shaped with a hairy texture.

Anemone × clarae is well-suited for rock gardens and borders, adding ornamental value with its vibrant flowers.
