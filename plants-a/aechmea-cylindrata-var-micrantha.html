Aechmea cylindrata var. micrantha

Aechmea cylindrata var. micrantha, commonly known as the dwarf pineapple bromeliad, is a striking epiphytic plant native to the tropical rainforests of South America.

It features a compact rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves that form a central cup to collect rainwater. The plant produces inflorescences with vibrant, showy bracts that range in color from red to orange, attracting pollinators. Its foliage is often adorned with white or yellowish markings, adding to its ornamental appeal.

This variety is appreciated for its low maintenance and ability to thrive in indirect light, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
