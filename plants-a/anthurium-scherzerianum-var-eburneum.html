Anthurium scherzerianum var. eburneum

Anthurium scherzerianum var. eburneum, commonly known as the white anthurium, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped, white spathes that contrast with its deep green, velvety foliage. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow, which are followed by bright red berries. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated as a houseplant.
