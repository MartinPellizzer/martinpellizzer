Astrocaryum chonta

Astrocaryum chonta, commonly known as the chonta palm, is a species of palm native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate leaves that can reach several meters in length. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by clustered, edible fruits that are consumed by various wildlife. Its fruit is also used by indigenous communities for food and traditional medicine.

The chonta palm plays a vital role in the ecological balance of the rainforest due to its ability to provide habitat and food resources.
