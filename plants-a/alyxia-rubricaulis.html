Alyxia rubricaulis

Alyxia rubricaulis, commonly known as the red-stemmed alyxia, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a woody vine that typically grows in moist, wooded areas. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a glossy green surface and prominent veins. Its showy, fragrant flowers are usually pink to red, blooming in summer.

Alyxia rubricaulis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits to pollinators.
