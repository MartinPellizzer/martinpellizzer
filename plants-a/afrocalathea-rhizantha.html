Afrocalathea rhizantha

Afrocalathea rhizantha, commonly known as the African calathea, is a tropical plant native to Central Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, arrow-shaped leaves that are often variegated with shades of green, white, and pink. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread slowly, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Afrocalathea rhizantha is also valued for its air-purifying qualities and ability to enhance the aesthetic of indoor spaces.
