Aster silenifolius

Aster silenifolius, commonly known as the narrow-leaved aster, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the sunflower family (Asteraceae) and is characterized by its small, clustered blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as woodlands and meadows. Its leaves are narrow and silvery-green, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It is often used in native plant gardens for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
