Achnophora tatei

Achnophora tatei, commonly known as the Cape York thistle, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its spiny leaves and purple flowers, which bloom in late spring. The plant typically grows in dry, sandy soils and is found in coastal areas of Queensland. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.
