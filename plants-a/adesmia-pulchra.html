Adesmia pulchra

Adesmia pulchra, commonly known as the beautiful adesmia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forests or riverbanks. Its leaves are trifoliate, consisting of three oval-shaped leaflets.

Adesmia pulchra is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic value.
