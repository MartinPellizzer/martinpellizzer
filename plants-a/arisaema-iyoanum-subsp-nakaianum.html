Arisaema iyoanum subsp. nakaianum

Arisaema iyoanum subsp.

nakaianum, commonly known as the Nakaianum arrowhead, is a member of the Araceae family. It is native to Japan and is characterized by its distinctive arrowhead-shaped leaves.

The plant produces a spadix surrounded by a modified leaf called a spathe, which is typically green or purple in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
