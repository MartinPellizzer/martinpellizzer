Aloysia beckii

Aloysia beckii, commonly known as the silverleaf honeysuckle, is a shrub native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It is characterized by its silvery-white undersides on its leaves, which give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters, typically in shades of white or pale yellow. It is often found in moist, shaded areas and is valued for its ornamental qualities.

Aloysia beckii is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
