Anthericum matogrossense

Anthericum matogrossense, commonly known as the Matogrossense arum, is a flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive inflorescence consisting of a spadix surrounded by a modified leaf called a spathe. The plant features a tuberous rhizome and produces showy, often colorful bracts that attract pollinators.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.
