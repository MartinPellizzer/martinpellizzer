Agrostis floresii

Agrostis floresii, commonly known as the Flores grass, is a species of flowering plant native to the island of Flores in Indonesia.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit. The plant produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences with both male and female flowers, typical of many grass species. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green coloration.

Agrostis floresii is adapted to grow in well-drained soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats.
