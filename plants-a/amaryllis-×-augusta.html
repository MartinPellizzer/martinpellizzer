Amaryllis × augusta

Amaryllis × augusta, commonly known as the hybrid amaryllis, is a striking hybrid plant resulting from crosses between various amaryllis species.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that come in a range of colors including red, pink, white, and orange. The plant produces strap-shaped, grass-like leaves that are typically broad and fleshy. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers bright, indirect light.

Amaryllis × augusta is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is popular as a houseplant or in garden settings.
