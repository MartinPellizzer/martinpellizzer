Asplenium xinyiense

Asplenium xinyiense, commonly known as the Xinyi shield fern, is a species of fern native to China.

It belongs to the family Aspleniaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, shield-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories. Its leaves are leathery and have a compact structure, which helps it retain moisture.

Asplenium xinyiense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in horticulture.
