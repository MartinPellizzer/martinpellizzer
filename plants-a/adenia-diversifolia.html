Adenia diversifolia

Adenia diversifolia, commonly known as the African bellflower, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant has a climbing habit, often using its tendrils to support itself on other vegetation. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a rough texture.

Adenia diversifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
