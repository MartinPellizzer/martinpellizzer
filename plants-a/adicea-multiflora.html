Adicea multiflora

Adicea multiflora, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves and clusters of showy, violet-colored flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of about 10-20 cm and thrives in bright, indirect light. It prefers well-draining soil and moderate humidity, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Adicea multiflora is known for its ability to bloom continuously throughout the year under proper care.
