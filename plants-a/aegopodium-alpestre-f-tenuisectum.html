Aegopodium alpestre f. tenuisectum

Aegopodium alpestre f. tenuisectum, commonly known as the slenderleaf lady's mantle, is a botanical variant of the lady's mantle plant.

It is a herbaceous perennial that belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features finely divided, fern-like leaves that give it a delicate appearance. It typically grows in alpine or rocky habitats, thriving in well-drained soils.

Its flowers, which appear in the summer, are yellow and clustered in a spherical shape, contributing to its ornamental value.
