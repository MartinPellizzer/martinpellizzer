Acioa dawei

Acioa dawei, commonly known as the Chinese acacia, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes and is characterized by its pod-like fruits. The plant is known for its feathery leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. It is often found in mountainous regions and is adapted to temperate climates.

Acioa dawei is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional medicinal uses.
