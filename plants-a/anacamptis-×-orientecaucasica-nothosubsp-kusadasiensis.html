Anacamptis × orientecaucasica nothosubsp. kusadasiensis

Anacamptis × orientecaucasica nothosubsp.

kusadasiensis, commonly known as the Kusadasi orchid, is a hybrid orchid native to Turkey. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking violet-blue flowers with yellow markings.

The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and rocky areas. It has a pseudobulbous structure with strap-like leaves and produces a branched inflorescence.

This nothosubspecies is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens.
