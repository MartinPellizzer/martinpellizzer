Asterochlaena morongii f. discolor

Asterochlaena morongii f. discolor, commonly known as the variegated starburry.

This plant is a succulent species native to South Africa, characterized by its fleshy, upright stems and clusters of small, star-shaped flowers. The variegated leaves display a striking mix of green, white, and sometimes pink hues, making it an ornamental favorite. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively low maintenance, it is popular among succulent enthusiasts.
