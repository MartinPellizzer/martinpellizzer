Alepidea ciliaris

Alepidea ciliaris, commonly known as the silver leaf everlasting, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and small, daisy-like flowers. The plant is perennial and thrives in well-drained sandy or rocky soils. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its drought tolerance and attractive appearance.

Alepidea ciliaris is also used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
