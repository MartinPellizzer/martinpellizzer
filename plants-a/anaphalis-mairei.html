Anaphalis mairei

Anaphalis mairei, commonly known as the silver hairgrass, is a flowering plant native to East Asia, particularly China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Anaphalis mairei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
