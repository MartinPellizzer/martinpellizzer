Adenia wightiana subsp. africana

Adenia wightiana subsp.

africana, commonly known as African star gooseberry, is a shrub native to tropical Africa. It belongs to the family Passifloraceae and is characterized by its woody stems and compound leaves.

The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are followed by fleshy, berry-like fruits. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.
