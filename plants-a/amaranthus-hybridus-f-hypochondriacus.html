Amaranthus hybridus f. hypochondriacus

Amaranthus hybridus f. hypochondriacus, commonly known as red leaf amaranth, is a cultivar of the amaranth plant known for its vibrant red foliage and edible leaves.

It is a member of the Amaranthaceae family and is native to tropical and subtropical regions. The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit and dense, colorful leaves that can range from deep red to maroon. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental and culinary purposes.

The leaves are rich in nutrients and can be used in salads, soups, and as a garnish.
