Ardisia punctipetala

Ardisia punctipetala, commonly known as the spotted flame tree, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 6 meters tall. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves that are often marked with red or purple spots. Its showy, fragrant flowers are typically white or pale pink and appear in clusters.

The fruit is a bright red, fleshy berry that contains several seeds.
