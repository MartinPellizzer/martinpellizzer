Allium khozratense

Allium khozratense, commonly known as the Khozrat onion, is a bulbous plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and small, clustered white or pink flowers. The plant produces edible bulbs that are used in local cuisines for their mild, sweet flavor. It thrives in temperate climates and is often cultivated for both ornamental and culinary purposes.

Its botanical structure is similar to other Allium species, with a complex root system and a short stem.
