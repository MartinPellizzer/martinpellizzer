Abelia macrotera var. engleriana

Abelia macrotera var. engleriana, commonly known as Engler's abelia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features clusters of fragrant, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink, blooming in late summer. The leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a smooth or slightly hairy surface. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various garden conditions.
