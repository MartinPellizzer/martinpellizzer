Anthurium theresiopolitanum

Anthurium theresiopolitanum, commonly known as the Red Velvet Anthurium, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety red spathes that enclose the inflorescence. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers clustered around a spadix, typical of the Araceae family. Its foliage consists of large, heart-shaped leaves with a glossy, dark green surface.

This ornamental plant thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.
