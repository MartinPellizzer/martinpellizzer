Aloe schliebenii

Aloe schliebenii, commonly known as the Schlieben's aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, with a waxy coating to reduce water loss. The plant produces tall, branched flower stalks that bear yellow flowers, which bloom in late summer. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Aloe schliebenii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and low maintenance requirements.
