Anthurium mateoi

Anthurium mateoi, commonly known as the Mateo's arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are often a vibrant green with a waxy texture. The plant produces spadices, which are specialized inflorescences that consist of a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe. Anthurium mateoi thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and ornamental purposes.
