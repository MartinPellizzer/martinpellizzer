Arabis ionocalyx

Arabis ionocalyx, commonly known as the narrow-leaved rockcress, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is native to regions of the Mediterranean, including parts of southern Europe and western Asia. It thrives in rocky or gravelly soils and is often found in coastal or alpine environments.
