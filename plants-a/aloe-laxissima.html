Aloe laxissima

Aloe laxissima, commonly known as the slender aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features long, narrow, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a waxy coating. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellowish-green flowers, which bloom in summer. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Aloe laxissima is often cultivated for its ornamental value and resilience in dry climates.
