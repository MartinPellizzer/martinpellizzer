Aeschynomene moluccana

Aeschynomene moluccana, commonly known as Moluccan velvetleaf, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Asia and Africa.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is often found in disturbed areas, such as roadsides and fields, due to its adaptability to various soil types. It has a fibrous root system and can grow up to 1.5 meters in height.

Aeschynomene moluccana is also known for its potential use in forage and as a cover crop in agricultural systems.
