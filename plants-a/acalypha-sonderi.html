Acalypha sonderi

Acalypha sonderi, commonly known as the silver leaf tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking silver-gray foliage, which results from dense pubescent (hairy) leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically green or yellowish in color. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Acalypha sonderi is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
