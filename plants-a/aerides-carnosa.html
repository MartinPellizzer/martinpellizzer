Aerides carnosa

Aerides carnosa, commonly known as the orchid with the waxy, velvety leaves, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and attractive, fragrant flowers. The plant produces showy, long-lasting blooms in shades of white, pink, and red, often with a subtle fragrance. Its leaves are thick and fleshy, adapted to store water in its native humid environments.

Aerides carnosa is a popular choice for indoor cultivation due to its resilience and ornamental value.
