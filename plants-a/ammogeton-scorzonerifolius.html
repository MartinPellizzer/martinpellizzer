Ammogeton scorzonerifolius

Ammogeton scorzonerifolius, commonly known as the scarlet pimpernel, is a flowering plant native to Europe, Asia, and Africa.

It is a low-growing annual herb that typically reaches a height of about 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features slender stems and small, clustered leaves that are often hairy. Its flowers are bright yellow and appear in clusters, blooming from late spring to early autumn.

The species is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in a variety of soil types.
