Ardisia myriosticta

Ardisia myriosticta, commonly known as the clustered beautyberry, is a species of flowering plant in the family Ericaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of India, Malaysia, and Indonesia. The berries are edible and have a sweet, slightly tart flavor, making the plant both ornamental and potentially useful in local cuisines.
