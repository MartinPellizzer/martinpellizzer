Alstonia oleandrifolia

Alstonia oleandrifolia, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its large, palmate leaves and showy, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces woody, capsule-like fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for tropical and subtropical climates.

Alstonia oleandrifolia is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
