Ardisia coclensis

Ardisia coclensis, commonly known as the Christmas berry, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, dark green leaves and produces small, red berries that are edible for humans and animals. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
