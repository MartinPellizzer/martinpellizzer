Atractylis ovata var. simplicifolia

Atractylis ovata var. simplicifolia, commonly known as narrow-leaved sandwort, is a low-growing perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, green, ovate leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces yellow flowers in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to tolerate harsh environmental conditions.
