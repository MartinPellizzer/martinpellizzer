Adenostyles × canescens

Adenostyles × canescens, commonly known as the hybrid silverleaf lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the family Liliaceae and is characterized by its slender, often hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are typically covered in a fine layer of white hairs, giving it a silvery appearance.

Adenostyles × canescens is often found in moist, open habitats such as meadows and streambanks.
