Ardisia Squamata

Ardisia squamata is a striking evergreen shrub native to eastern asia known for its glossy dark green leaves and vibrant red berries. it thrives in shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions. the plant's unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening. its berries are not only visually appealing but also attract birds and other wildlife. ardisia squamata is a versatile plant that can be grown in both containers and in the ground.
