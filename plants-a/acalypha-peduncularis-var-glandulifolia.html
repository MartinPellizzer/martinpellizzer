Acalypha peduncularis var. glandulifolia

Acalypha peduncularis var. glandulifolia, commonly known as the glandular acalypha, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, palmate leaves with a reddish or purple underside, which are often covered in fine, glandular hairs. Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in some horticultural settings for its unique foliage.
