Amorphophallus elegans

Amorphophallus elegans, commonly known as the elegant arrowhead, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family, which includes plants like calla lilies and peace lilies. The plant produces striking, arrow-shaped leaves that can grow up to 2 meters in length. Its underground corm is used in traditional medicine and as a food source in some regions.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological significance in its native habitats.
