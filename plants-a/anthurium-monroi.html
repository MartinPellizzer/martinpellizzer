Anthurium monroi

Anthurium monroi, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces striking red spathes that enclose its bright yellow spadix, which is the actual flowering structure. Anthurium monroi thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and ornamental plant collections.
