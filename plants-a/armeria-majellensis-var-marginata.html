Armeria majellensis var. marginata

Armeria majellensis var. marginata, commonly known as the margined thrift, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its dense, low-growing clusters of white or pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant features narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its hardy nature and ornamental value make it a popular choice among gardeners.
