Asplenium anjozorobense

Asplenium anjozorobense, commonly known as the Madagascar shield fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the family Aspleniaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, shield-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories. Its fronds are usually dark green and possess a leathery texture, which helps it retain moisture.

Asplenium anjozorobense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant.
