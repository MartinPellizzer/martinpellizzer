Anemia × zanonii

Anemia × zanonii, commonly known as the hybrid anemone, is a botanical hybrid resulting from the cross between Anemone coronaria and Anemone zanonii.

It is characterized by its showy, cup-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in partial to full sun. Its foliage consists of basal leaves that are lobed and often covered in a fine, silvery down.

Anemia × zanonii is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for.
