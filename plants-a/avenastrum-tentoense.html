Avenastrum tentoense

Avenastrum tentoense, commonly known as the Tentoense grass, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as rocky slopes and sandy soils. The plant features slender, tufted stems with narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowering period occurs in late spring to early summer, producing small, spike-like inflorescences.

Avenastrum tentoense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
