Astrantia minor

Astrantia minor, commonly known as lesser dropwort, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe.

It features delicate, umbel-like clusters of small, white or pinkish flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has basal leaves that are broadly ovate with toothed margins, and its stems are slender and branched. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Astrantia minor is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits to pollinators.
