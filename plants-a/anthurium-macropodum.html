Anthurium macropodum

Anthurium macropodum, commonly known as the giant arrowhead plant, is a tropical species native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 3 feet in length. The plant produces striking spadices, which are bright red or orange in color, surrounded by a white spathe. It thrives in humid, warm environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium macropodum is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and air-purifying qualities.
