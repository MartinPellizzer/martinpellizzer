Agelaea densiflora

Agelaea densiflora, commonly known as the dense-flowered agelaea, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is a member of the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of small, showy flowers. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
