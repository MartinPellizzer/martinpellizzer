Alchemilla spectabilior

Alchemilla spectabilior, commonly known as the showy lady's mantle, is a perennial plant belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is native to Europe and is characterized by its basal rosette of heart-shaped, velvety leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its foliage is often used in traditional herbal medicine for its purported soothing properties.

Alchemilla spectabilior thrives in moist, shaded environments and is valued in gardens for its ornamental appearance.
