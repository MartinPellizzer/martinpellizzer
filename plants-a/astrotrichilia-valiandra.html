Astrotrichilia valiandra

Astrotrichilia valiandra, commonly known as the Natal star tree, is a distinctive evergreen tree native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its unique, star-shaped leaves that are arranged in a whorl pattern around the stem. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by winged fruits that aid in seed dispersal. Its bark is smooth and often exhibits a reddish or brownish color.

Astrotrichilia valiandra is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking foliage.
