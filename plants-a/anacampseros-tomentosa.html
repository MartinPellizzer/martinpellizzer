Anacampseros tomentosa

Anacampseros tomentosa, commonly known as the woolly anacampseros, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, gray-green leaves covered in a dense layer of white, woolly hair. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and succulent collections.
