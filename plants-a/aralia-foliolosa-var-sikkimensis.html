Aralia foliolosa var. sikkimensis

Aralia foliolosa var. sikkimensis, commonly known as the Sikkim aralia, is a shrub native to the Himalayan regions of India and Nepal.

It features compound leaves with elongated, serrated leaflets that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in umbels, followed by dark blue berries that are edible. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value and adaptability make it a popular choice for horticultural cultivation.
