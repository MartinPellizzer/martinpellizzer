Angelonia acuminatissima

Angelonia acuminatissima, commonly known as the tropical angelonia, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and Central America.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of tubular, showy flowers that range in color from pink to red. The leaves are opposite, ovate, and slightly hairy, providing a textured contrast to the vibrant blooms. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This plant is often used in gardens for its long blooming season and ornamental value.
