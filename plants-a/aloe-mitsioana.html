Aloe mitsioana

Aloe mitsioana, commonly known as the Cape aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, segmented stems covered in a layer of white, powdery wax. The plant produces striking, tall inflorescences with bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer. Its leaves are fleshy and succulent, adapted to store water in arid environments.

Aloe mitsioana is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional medicinal uses.
