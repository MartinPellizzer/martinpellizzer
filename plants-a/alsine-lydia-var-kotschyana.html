Alsine lydia var. kotschyana

Alsine lydia var. kotschyana, commonly known as Kotschyan's alsine, is a flowering plant belonging to the Amaranthaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, greenish-white flowers and succulent, fleshy leaves. This variant is native to regions of Central Asia and the Middle East, often found in rocky or arid environments. The plant exhibits a low-growing habit, typically forming dense mats or cushions.

Its botanical features make it well adapted to survive in harsh, dry conditions.
