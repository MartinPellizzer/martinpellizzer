Acinodendron poeppigii

Acinodendron poeppigii, commonly known as the white-flowered acinodendron, is a small evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in humid, shaded environments.
