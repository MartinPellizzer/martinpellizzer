Armeria mauritanica var. minor

Armeria mauritanica var. minor, commonly known as the dwarf sea thrift, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features dense, compact clusters of white or pale pink flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has needle-like leaves that are gray-green in color, providing a striking contrast to its showy flowers. It thrives in well-drained sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in coastal areas.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to tolerate coastal conditions.
