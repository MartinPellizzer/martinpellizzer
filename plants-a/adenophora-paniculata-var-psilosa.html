Adenophora paniculata var. psilosa

Adenophora paniculata var. psilosa, commonly known as the slender bellflower, is a perennial herb native to eastern Asia.

It features slender, upright stems with opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy. The plant produces clusters of bell-shaped flowers, typically blue to purple in color, which bloom in summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full sunlight.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
