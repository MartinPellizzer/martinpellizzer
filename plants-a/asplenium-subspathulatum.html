Asplenium subspathulatum

The plant has linear, lanceolate leaves that are often clustered closely together, with a distinctive, narrow sheath at the base of each leaf. Asplenium subspathulatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often found in botanical gardens and conservation areas.
