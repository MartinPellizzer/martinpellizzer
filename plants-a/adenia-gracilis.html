Adenia gracilis

Adenia gracilis, commonly known as the slender adenia, is a flowering plant native to eastern Australia.

It is a shrub that typically grows in coastal and dry forest habitats. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in clusters. Its fruit is a fleshy, yellow berry that contains several seeds.

Adenia gracilis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage and floral display.
