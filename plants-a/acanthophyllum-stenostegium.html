Acanthophyllum stenostegium

Acanthophyllum stenostegium, commonly known as the sand spurge, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of Central Asia.

It is a low-growing shrub characterized by its dense, spiny foliage and small, yellowish flowers. The plant has adapted to harsh environments through its succulent stems and reduced leaves, which help minimize water loss. It plays a role in stabilizing sandy soils and providing habitat for small desert animals.

Acanthophyllum stenostegium is often found in dunes and other dry, wind-swept habitats.
