Actinostemon polymorphus f. microphyllus

Actinostemon polymorphus f. microphyllus, commonly known as the dwarf actinostemon, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are densely arranged along its stems, giving it a bushy appearance. The plant produces clusters of white, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in spring, adding ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens or container planting.

Its unique foliage and delicate flowers make it a prized addition to coastal gardens.
