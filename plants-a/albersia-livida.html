Albersia livida

Albersia livida, commonly known as the silverleaf albersia, is a rare and striking plant native to the mountainous regions of South Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive silver-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, dense hairs that give it a unique appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, adding a delicate touch to its overall form. Albersia livida thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
