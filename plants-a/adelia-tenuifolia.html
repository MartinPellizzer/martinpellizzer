Adelia tenuifolia

Adelia tenuifolia, commonly known as the slender leaf adelia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its flowers, which range in color from pink to red, bloom during the summer months.

Adelia tenuifolia is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
