Anthurium tetragonum

Anthurium tetragonum, commonly known as the velvet leaf anthurium, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a velvety texture. The plant produces striking, spadix-shaped inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, often in shades of red, orange, or yellow. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.

Anthurium tetragonum is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
