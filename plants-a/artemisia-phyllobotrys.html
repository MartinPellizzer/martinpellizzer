Artemisia phyllobotrys

Artemisia phyllobotrys, commonly known as the Chinese wormwood, is a perennial shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its aromatic, silver-gray foliage and small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family and is known for its medicinal properties. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in mountainous regions.

Its leaves contain volatile oils, which contribute to its distinctive scent and therapeutic uses.
