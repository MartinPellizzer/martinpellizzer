Alocasia megawatiae

Alocasia megawatiae, commonly known as the Black Velvet Alocasia, is a striking ornamental plant native to the rainforests of Borneo.

It is characterized by its dark, velvety leaves that are typically heart-shaped with a deep green to almost black coloration. The plant thrives in high humidity and partial shade, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation. Its foliage is often adorned with subtle veins that add to its visual appeal.

Alocasia megawatiae requires well-draining soil and consistent moisture to flourish.
