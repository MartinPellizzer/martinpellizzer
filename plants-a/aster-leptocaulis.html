Aster leptocaulis

Aster leptocaulis, commonly known as the slender-stemmed aster, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands. Its slender stems and small, blue to purple flowers bloom in the summer months.

Aster leptocaulis is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil and provide nectar for pollinators.
