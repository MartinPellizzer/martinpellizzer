Asplenium serra var. remotum

Asplenium serra var. remotum, commonly known as the remote serrated spleenwort, is a fern species native to parts of Asia and the Pacific.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its delicate, strap-like leaves that are often serrated along the edges. The plant typically grows on rocks or tree trunks in moist, shaded environments. Its fronds are usually dark green and can reach lengths of up to 30 centimeters.

Asplenium serra var. remotum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in humid, indoor settings.
