Amaryllis platypetala

Amaryllis platypetala, commonly known as the flat-petaled amaryllis, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant features strap-like, evergreen leaves that can grow up to several feet in length. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Amaryllis platypetala is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
