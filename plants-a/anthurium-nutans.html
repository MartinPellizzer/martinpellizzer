Anthurium nutans

Anthurium nutans, commonly known as the hanging anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-shaped inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, typically in red or orange hues. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Anthurium nutans is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its ornamental foliage and unique flowering structure.
