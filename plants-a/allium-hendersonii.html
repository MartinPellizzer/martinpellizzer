Allium hendersonii

Allium hendersonii, commonly known as Henderson's onion, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of central Asia.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its bulbous root system and grass-like leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a hardy perennial in suitable climates.
