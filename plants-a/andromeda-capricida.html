Andromeda capricida

Andromeda capricida, commonly known as the Cape rose, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and blueberries. The plant produces showy, pink or white flowers that bloom in spring. Its leaves are evergreen, glossy, and oval-shaped, providing year-round interest.

Andromeda capricida thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and partial shade, making it a popular choice for gardeners in temperate climates.
