Acinodendron quadrifolium

Acinodendron quadrifolium, commonly known as the four-leaved acinodendron, is a tropical plant belonging to the Rubiaceae family.

It is characterized by its distinctive four-leaf clover-like foliage, which gives it its common name. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, such as rainforests in Southeast Asia. Acinodendron quadrifolium is valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
