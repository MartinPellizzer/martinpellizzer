Aphelandra × dubia

Aphelandra × dubia, commonly known as the wax plant or candle plant, is a hybrid species within the Aroid family (Araceae).

It is characterized by its striking, waxy, dark green leaves that are often variegated with yellow or white markings. The plant produces inflorescences consisting of a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe, which can range from red to orange in hue. Native to tropical regions of the Americas, it thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Aphelandra × dubia is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
