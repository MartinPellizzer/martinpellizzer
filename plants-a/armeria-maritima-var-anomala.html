Armeria maritima var. anomala

Armeria maritima var. anomala, commonly known as the coastal thrift or sea thrift, is a low-growing, evergreen perennial native to coastal regions of the northern hemisphere.

It features dense clusters of small, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has a mat-forming growth habit with silver-gray foliage that is adapted to withstand salty air and wind. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy choice for coastal gardens.

Its ornamental value and ecological importance in stabilizing dune environments are notable.
