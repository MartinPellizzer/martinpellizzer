Angraecum bidens

Angraecum bidens, commonly known as the Madagascar star orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale pink with intricate patterns. The plant has long, narrow leaves and produces fragrant blooms that attract nocturnal pollinators such as moths. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Angraecum bidens is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
