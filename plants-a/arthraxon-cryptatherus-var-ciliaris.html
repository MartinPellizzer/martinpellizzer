Arthraxon cryptatherus var. ciliaris

Arthraxon cryptatherus var. ciliaris, commonly known as the narrow-leafed grass or silky grass, is a perennial grass species native to Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, hair-like leaves that are often covered with fine, silky hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in a clump form and produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and open woodlands.

This grass is valued for its ornamental qualities and is also used in ecological restoration projects.
