Asplenium confusum

Asplenium confusum, commonly known as the confused maidenhair fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Aspleniaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds that are typically dark green and glossy. Native to tropical and subtropical regions, this plant thrives in moist, shaded environments. The fronds are usually bi- or tri-pinnate, with a distinctive zigzag appearance along their veins.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
