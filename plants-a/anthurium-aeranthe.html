Anthurium aeranthe

Anthurium aeranthe, commonly known as the velvet leaf anthurium, is a tropical flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its dark green, velvety leaves that are often adorned with intricate patterns of veins. The plant produces striking, spadix-shaped inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, typically in shades of red, orange, or yellow. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.

Anthurium aeranthe is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
