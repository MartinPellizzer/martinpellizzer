Aerides lawrenceae var. sandersiana

Aerides lawrenceae var. sandersiana, commonly known as the Singapore orchid, is a rare and elegant orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant typically blooms during the summer months, adding a striking visual element to its environment. It thrives in humid, shaded conditions and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its rarity and beauty, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
