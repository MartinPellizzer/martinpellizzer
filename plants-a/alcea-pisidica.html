Alcea pisidica

Alcea pisidica, commonly known as the Turkish hollyhock, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the eastern Mediterranean region.

Alcea pisidica is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its vibrant flowers and adaptability to various soil types.
