Aralia laetevirens

Aralia laetevirens, commonly known as the happy green aralia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compound, feather-like leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in umbels, which are typically borne on slender peduncles. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated as a houseplant in many parts of the world.
