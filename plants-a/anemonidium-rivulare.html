Anemonidium rivulare

Anemonidium rivulare, commonly known as the river anemonidium, is a flowering plant native to regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, clustered flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found near streams or in forest understories. Its leaves are heart-shaped and have a velvety texture, while its stems are slender and often covered in fine hairs.

Anemonidium rivulare is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to add a touch of elegance to shady garden spaces.
