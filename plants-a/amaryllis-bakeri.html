Amaryllis bakeri

Amaryllis bakeri, commonly known as the Baker's amaryllis, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It produces tall, upright stems bearing clusters of large, showy flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant is characterized by its strap-like, evergreen leaves that persist through the growing season. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Amaryllis bakeri is a popular choice for gardens and containers due to its vibrant blooms and relatively easy care requirements.
