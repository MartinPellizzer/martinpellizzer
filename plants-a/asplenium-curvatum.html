Asplenium curvatum

Asplenium curvatum, commonly known as the curled spleenwort, is a small, evergreen fern belonging to the family Aspleniaceae.

It is characterized by its compact, dark green, leathery fronds that are typically curled or twisted, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces spores on the underside of its fronds, which are arranged in clusters called sori. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found growing on rocks or tree trunks in temperate regions.

Asplenium curvatum is a hardy plant that is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
