Amauropelta

Amauropelta, commonly known as the golden shield fern, is a unique member of the Polypodiaceae family.

It is characterized by its distinctive, shield-shaped fronds that are typically golden or yellowish in color. The plant has a creeping rhizome that produces small, clustered sporangia on the underside of its leaves. Amauropelta thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for both horticultural and scientific study.
