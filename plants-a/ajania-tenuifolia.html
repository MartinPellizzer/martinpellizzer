Ajania tenuifolia

Ajania tenuifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved ajania, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features slender, grass-like leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. Its small, daisy-like flowers are usually blue to purple in color and bloom in the summer months.

Ajania tenuifolia is often found in rocky or alpine habitats, where it thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight.
