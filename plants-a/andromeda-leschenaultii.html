Andromeda leschenaultii

Andromeda leschenaultii, commonly known as the narrowleaf wintergreen, is a deciduous shrub native to the western United States and parts of Mexico.

The plant produces bright red berries that are edible for birds and small mammals, aiding in seed dispersal. Andromeda leschenaultii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in native plant communities.
