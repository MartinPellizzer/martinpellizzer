Athamanta condensata

Athamanta condensata, commonly known as the clustered leaved orchid, is a flowering plant native to the Andes Mountains of South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its compact growth habit and clustered inflorescence. The plant produces small, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, often with intricate patterns on the petals. It thrives in cool, humid environments and is often found in high-altitude grasslands.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and valuable species in botanical collections.
