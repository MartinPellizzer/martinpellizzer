Aglaonema nitidum f. cinereum

Aglaonema nitidum f. cinereum, commonly known as the Black Velvet Aglaonema, is a striking ornamental plant characterized by its dark, velvety foliage.

The leaves are typically a deep, glossy black with prominent white veins, creating a dramatic visual contrast. It is native to Southeast Asia and thrives in low-light conditions, making it an ideal indoor plant. The plant is known for its ability to tolerate shade and requires moderate humidity and well-draining soil.

Its foliage remains evergreen throughout the year, adding a lush, tropical aesthetic to any setting.
