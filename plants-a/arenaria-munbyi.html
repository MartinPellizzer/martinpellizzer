Arenaria munbyi

Arenaria munbyi, commonly known as the Munby's sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically thrives in sandy or rocky soils, often found in coastal areas and dry habitats. The plant features small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, arranged in a rosette pattern to conserve moisture.

Arenaria munbyi is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to stabilize coastal dunes.
