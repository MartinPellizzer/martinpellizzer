Anemone falconeri

Anemone falconeri, commonly known as the cliff anemone, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region and parts of southern Europe.

It thrives in rocky, coastal environments and is adapted to withstand strong winds and saline conditions. The plant produces showy, white or pink flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are deeply lobed and often covered in a fine, fuzzy layer to reduce water loss.

Anemone falconeri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with suitable conditions.
