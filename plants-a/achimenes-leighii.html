Achimenes leighii

Achimenes leighii, commonly known as the purple-flowered achimenes, is a tropical perennial plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from pink to deep purple, often with a contrasting throat. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves and produces tubular flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in warm, humid conditions and is often cultivated as a decorative plant in gardens and greenhouses.

Achimenes leighii is known for its ability to attract pollinators such as hummingbirds and butterflies.
