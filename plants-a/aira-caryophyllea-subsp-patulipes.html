Aira caryophyllea subsp. patulipes

Aira caryophyllea subsp.

patulipes, commonly known as the purple false oat, is a perennial grass species native to the western United States. It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of small, violet-colored flowers that bloom in late summer.
