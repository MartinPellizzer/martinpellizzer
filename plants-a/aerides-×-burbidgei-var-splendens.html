Aerides × burbidgei var. splendens

Aerides × burbidgei var. splendens, commonly known as the splendens orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and often feature a contrasting lip. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its foliage consists of long, narrow leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is cultivated for its beauty in both tropical and controlled environments.
