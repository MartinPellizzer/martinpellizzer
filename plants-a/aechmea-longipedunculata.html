Aechmea longipedunculata, commonly known as the long-stemmed bromeliad, is a striking epiphytic plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

Aechmea longipedunculata is appreciated for its ornamental value and is commonly cultivated as a houseplant or in tropical gardens.
