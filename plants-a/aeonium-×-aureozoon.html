Aeonium × aureozoon

Aeonium × aureozoon, commonly known as the Orange Aeonium, is a succulent plant native to the Canary Islands.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with prominent orange or red markings. The plant produces tall, branching inflorescences with small yellow flowers during the growing season. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its leaves and stems.

Aeonium × aureozoon is popular in rock gardens and as a decorative houseplant due to its striking appearance and low maintenance requirements.
