Androsace rotundifolia var. elegans

Androsace rotundifolia var. elegans, commonly known as the elegant rock jasmine, is a low-growing perennial plant native to alpine and subalpine regions of Europe.

It features dense clusters of small, white to pinkish flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has rounded, fleshy leaves that are typically covered in a fine, woolly layer of hair. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is often found in mountainous habitats.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for rock gardens and alpine displays.
