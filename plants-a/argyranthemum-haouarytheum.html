Argyranthemum haouarytheum

Argyranthemum haouarytheum, commonly known as the Madagascar marguerite, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers with white petals and a yellow center. The plant typically grows as a herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters. Its foliage is covered in fine, silvery hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
