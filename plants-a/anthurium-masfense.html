Anthurium masfense

Anthurium masfense, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped spathes that range in color from deep red to orange, often with contrasting green veins. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are concealed within the spathe, which is actually a modified leaf. Anthurium masfense thrives in high humidity and indirect light, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements have made it a favored ornamental plant among horticulturists and plant enthusiasts.
