Arum orientale subsp. besserianum

Arum orientale subsp.

besserianum, commonly known as the Besserianum arrowhead, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia. It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often a vibrant green with a reddish tint.

The plant produces a spadix surrounded by a spathe, which is typically a deep red or orange color. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value in tropical gardens and as a houseplant.
