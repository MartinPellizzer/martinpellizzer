Anthemis maritima

Anthemis maritima, commonly known as sea holly, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of Europe and North Africa.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and striking blue or purple flower heads. The plant thrives in salty, well-drained soils and is often found in dunes and rocky shores. Its flowers are composed of ray florets surrounding a central disk, typical of the Asteraceae family.

Anthemis maritima is also known for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally in herbal remedies.
