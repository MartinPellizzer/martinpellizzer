Astragalus ghanbarianii

Astragalus ghanbarianii, commonly known as Ghanbarian's milkvetch, is a flowering plant species native to Iran.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellowish flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions.

It plays a role in soil stabilization and is of interest for its potential medicinal properties.
