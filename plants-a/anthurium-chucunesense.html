Anthurium chucunesense

Anthurium chucunesense, commonly known as the Chucunesense arrowhead, is a tropical flowering plant native to Ecuador.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, heart-shaped leaves and striking spadix inflorescence. The plant produces bright red spadix surrounded by a white spathe, making it visually appealing. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Anthurium chucunesense is often cultivated for its ornamental value in greenhouses and botanical gardens.
