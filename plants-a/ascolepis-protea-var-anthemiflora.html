Ascolepis protea var. anthemiflora

Ascolepis protea var. anthemiflora, commonly known as the yellow starfire, is a striking South African plant belonging to the Proteaceae family.

It is characterized by its bright yellow, star-shaped flowers that bloom in late summer and early autumn. The plant has a woody stem with dense, hairy foliage that helps it retain moisture in its arid native habitat. Its flowers are typically arranged in compact, spherical inflorescences, giving it a distinctive appearance.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is well-suited to rock gardens and coastal areas.
