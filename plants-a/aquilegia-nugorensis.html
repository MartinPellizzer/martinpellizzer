Aquilegia nugorensis

Aquilegia nugorensis, commonly known as the Nugorensis Columbine, is a rare and endangered flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of central Europe.

The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in woodland or alpine habitats. Its leaves are deeply lobed and form a basal rosette, while its stems are upright and bear the showy flowers.
