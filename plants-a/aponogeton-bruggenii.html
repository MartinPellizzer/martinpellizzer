Aponogeton bruggenii

Aponogeton bruggenii, commonly known as the Burmese arrowhead, is a herbaceous aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features arrowhead-shaped leaves that are typically bright green and have a waxy texture. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom above the water surface, followed by distinctive, elongated seed pods. It thrives in shallow, still waters and is often found in marshy areas or along the edges of ponds.

Aponogeton bruggenii is valued in aquatic gardening for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
