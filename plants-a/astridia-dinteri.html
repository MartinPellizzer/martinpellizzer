Astridia dinteri

Astridia dinteri, commonly known as the Dinter's aster, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. It typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to arid environments.

Astridia dinteri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens with suitable conditions.
