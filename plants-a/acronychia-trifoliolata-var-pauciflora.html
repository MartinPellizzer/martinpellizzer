Acronychia trifoliolata var. pauciflora

Acronychia trifoliolata var. pauciflora, commonly known as the slender-flowered blackwood, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to northern Australia.

It is characterized by its dark, glossy bark and trifoliate leaves that are often elliptic to ovate in shape. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in clusters, followed by woody, edible fruits. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in coastal and lowland rainforest areas.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation programs.
