Azureocereus viridis

Azureocereus viridis, commonly known as the green cactus or green moon cactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its tall, columnar stems that are typically green or bluish-green in color. The plant produces white or pale pink flowers during the spring and summer months. It is a slow-growing plant that prefers well-draining soil and full sun exposure.

Azureocereus viridis is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.
