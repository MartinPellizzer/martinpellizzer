Achnatherum turcomanicum

Achnatherum turcomanicum, commonly known as the Turkestan needlegrass, is a perennial grass species native to Central Asia.

It typically grows in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in steppes and rocky soils. The plant has slender, cylindrical stems that are usually less than 1 meter in height. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green color and a prominent midrib.

The inflorescence is a panicle composed of small, spikelet-like flowers, which are wind-pollinated.
