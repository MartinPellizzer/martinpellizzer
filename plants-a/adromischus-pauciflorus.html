Adromischus pauciflorus

Adromischus pauciflorus, commonly known as the rock rose or small-flowered adromischus, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features dense, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a waxy coating, which helps it retain moisture in arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or red during the spring and summer months. It grows in a compact, bushy form and is well-suited for rock gardens or container cultivation.

Due to its low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
