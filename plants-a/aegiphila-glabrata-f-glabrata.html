Aegiphila glabrata f. glabrata

Aegiphila glabrata f. glabrata, commonly known as the smooth aegiphila, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine, silky hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance, it is often cultivated in Mediterranean climates.
