Alchemilla acutiloba subsp. catillaris

Alchemilla acutiloba subsp.

catillaris, commonly known as cat's tail lady's mantle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and upright flowering stems.

The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in cylindrical inflorescences resembling a cat's tail. Its leaves are covered in a dense layer of fine hairs, giving them a velvety texture.

It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland or streamside habitats.
