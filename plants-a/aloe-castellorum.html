Aloe castellorum

Aloe castellorum, commonly known as the Castle Aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with red or purple margins. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow flowers, which bloom in the spring. Its leaves contain a gel-like substance that is often used in skincare products.

Aloe castellorum is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water to thrive.
