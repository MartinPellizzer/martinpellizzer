Alsophila rebeccae var. normalis

Alsophila rebeccae var. normalis, commonly known as the Rebeccae's tree fern, is a species native to the tropical and subtropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and a crown of feathery fronds that give it a distinctive, elegant appearance. The plant has a slow growth rate and can reach heights of up to 10 meters in optimal conditions. Its fronds are typically bright green and remain evergreen throughout the year.

Alsophila rebeccae var. normalis thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests or along stream banks.
