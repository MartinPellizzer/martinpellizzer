Arisaema galeiforme

Arisaema galeiforme, commonly known as the horned arisaema, is a member of the Araceae family.

It is native to China and is characterized by its unique, spathaceous inflorescence. The plant features a tall, leafless stem topped with a striking, club-shaped spathe. Its foliage consists of large, heart-shaped leaves with a rough, hairy texture.

Arisaema galeiforme is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
