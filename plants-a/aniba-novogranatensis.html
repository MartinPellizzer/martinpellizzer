Aniba novogranatensis

Aniba novogranatensis, commonly known as Brazilian camphor tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the Amazon rainforest.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic leaves and bark. The plant produces small, white flowers followed by dark purple berries. Its leaves are opposite, elliptical, and have a distinct camphor-like scent.

Aniba novogranatensis is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional medicine for its anti-inflammatory and antiseptic effects.
