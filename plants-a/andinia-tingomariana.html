Andinia tingomariana

Andinia tingomariana, commonly known as the Tingomara lily, is a flowering plant native to the Andes Mountains of South America.

It belongs to the family Liliaceae and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in high-altitude regions with cool, moist conditions. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a waxy coating, while its flowers can range in color from white to pink.
