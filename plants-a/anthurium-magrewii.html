Anthurium magrewii

Anthurium magrewii, commonly known as the Magrew's arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are often a deep green with a glossy texture. The plant produces clusters of vibrant red spadices, which are the modified leaves that enclose the true flowers. Anthurium magrewii thrives in humid, warm environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.

Its unique foliage and ornamental value make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
