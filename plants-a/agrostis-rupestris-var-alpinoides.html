Agrostis rupestris var. alpinoides

Agrostis rupestris var. alpinoides, commonly known as the alpine bentgrass, is a low-growing grass species native to mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in alpine meadows and high-altitude habitats. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It is adapted to cold climates and can withstand harsh environmental conditions.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in stabilizing soil in rugged terrain.
