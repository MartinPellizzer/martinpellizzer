Astracantha delbesii

Astracantha delbesii, commonly known as the Cape Flats daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers with yellow centers and white petals. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and is adapted to sandy or gravelly soils. It produces small, spiny fruits that contain seeds dispersed by wind.

Astracantha delbesii is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
