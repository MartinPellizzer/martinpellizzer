Anthephora elegans var. acuminata

Anthephora elegans var. acuminata, commonly known as the slender rock orchid, is a rare and delicate orchid species native to specific regions of South Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, white pubescence. The plant produces fragrant, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in a loose raceme, typically blooming during the late winter to early spring. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal or mountainous habitats.
