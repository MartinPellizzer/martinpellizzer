Acanthotheca pinnatisecta

Acanthotheca pinnatisecta, commonly known as the narrow-leaved acanthotheca, is a species of flowering plant in the family Acanthaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, finely dissected leaves that give it a feathery appearance. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically arranged in clusters, often in shades of pink or purple. Native to tropical regions of Africa, it thrives in moist, shaded environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
