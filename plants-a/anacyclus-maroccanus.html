Anacyclus maroccanus

Anacyclus maroccanus, commonly known as Moroccan crown flower, is a flowering plant native to North Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in arid and semi-arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its leaves are usually hairy and alternate, while the stem is erect and branching.

Anacyclus maroccanus is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
