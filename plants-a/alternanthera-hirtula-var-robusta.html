Alternanthera hirtula var. robusta

Alternanthera hirtula var. robusta, commonly known as the red-stemmedAlternanthera, is a strikingly ornamental plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red or purple stems, which contrast beautifully with its lance-shaped, dark green leaves. This variety thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade. It is often cultivated in water gardens or as a bog plant due to its affinity for wet conditions.

The plant is known for its rapid growth and ability to produce small, inconspicuous white flowers in the summer months.
