Aphanopleura leptoclada

Aphanopleura leptoclada, commonly known as the slender-leafed fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, linear fronds that are typically less than 30 centimeters in length. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Aphanopleura leptoclada is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
