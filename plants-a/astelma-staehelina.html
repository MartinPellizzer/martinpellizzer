Astelma staehelina

Astelma staehelina, commonly known as the desert star.

It is a succulent plant native to arid regions of southern Africa. The plant features rosettes of fleshy, blue-green leaves that store water, aiding in its survival in dry environments. It produces showy, star-shaped yellow flowers during the wet season.

Astelma staehelina is well adapted to harsh conditions, making it a resilient and visually striking addition to rock gardens or xeriscapes.
