Aloe bicomitum

Aloe bicomitum, commonly known as the two-lobed aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a waxy coating, adapted to store water in arid environments. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers on tall, branched stems during the growing season. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their soothing properties.

Aloe bicomitum thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for xeriscaping.
