Aster takasago-montanus

Aster takasago-montanus, commonly known as the Takasago mountain aster, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and showy blooms. This perennial species typically grows in mountainous regions, preferring well-drained soils and full sun exposure. Its flowers, which bloom in the fall, range in color from white to purple, adding ornamental value to its natural habitat.

The plant is also valued for its ecological role in supporting pollinators and maintaining biodiversity in alpine ecosystems.
