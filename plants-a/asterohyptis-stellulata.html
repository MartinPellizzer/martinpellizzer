Asterohyptis stellulata

Asterohyptis stellulata, commonly known as the star hyptis, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its small, star-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters, often in shades of white, purple, or pink. Its leaves are typically narrow and aromatic, with a slightly hairy texture.

Asterohyptis stellulata is often grown as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and delicate floral display.
