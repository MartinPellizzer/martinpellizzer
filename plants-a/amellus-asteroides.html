Amellus asteroides

Amellus asteroides, commonly known as the starflower, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the Arctic and alpine regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

It is characterized by its striking star-shaped flowers, which typically bloom in shades of white, yellow, or orange. The plant has a low-growing habit with clustered stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is adapted to cold climates.

Amellus asteroides plays a role in supporting local pollinators and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value.
