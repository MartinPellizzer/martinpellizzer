Astragalus Tatsienensis Var. Kangrenbuchiensis

Astragalus tatsienensis var. kangrenbuchiensis is a rare and distinctive plant species native to the mountainous regions of China. It belongs to the legume family and is known for its unique floral structure and ecological significance. This variant is particularly noted for its adaptations to high-altitude environments, making it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it faces threats from environmental changes and human activity. Studying this plant provides valuable insights into the biodiversity and resilience of alpine ecosystems.
