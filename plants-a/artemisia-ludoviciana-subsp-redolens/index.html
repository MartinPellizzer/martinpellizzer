Artemisia Ludoviciana Subsp. Redolens

Artemisia ludoviciana subsp. redolens is a hardy perennial shrub native to the central and northern united states. it is known for its aromatic foliage and distinctive silver-gray leaves that shimmer in the sun. this plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun conditions, making it a popular choice for xeriscaping. its strong, earthy scent is often described as fresh and invigorating, reminiscent of fall air. with its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements, it is an excellent addition to native gardens and erosion control projects.
