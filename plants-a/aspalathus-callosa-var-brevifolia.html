Aspalathus callosa var. brevifolia

Aspalathus callosa var. brevifolia, commonly known as the small-leaved bushveld pea, is a perennial shrub native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant features small, narrow leaves and produces yellow flowers that bloom in late spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grassland and rocky habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and hardy nature, it is cultivated in gardens and used in ecological restoration projects.
