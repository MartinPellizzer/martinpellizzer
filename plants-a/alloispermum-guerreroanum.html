Alloispermum guerreroanum

Alloispermum guerreroanum, commonly known as Guerrero's alpine gentian, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of Mexico.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky soils and is adapted to cold, mountainous environments. It has lance-shaped leaves and produces small, tubular flowers that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
