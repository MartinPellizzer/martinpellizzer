Armeniaca vulgaris var. xiongyueensis

Armeniaca vulgaris var. xiongyueensis, commonly known as the Xiongyue peach, is a variant of the peach tree native to China.

It is characterized by its large, sweet, and aromatic fruits with a distinctive yellowish-orange skin. The plant exhibits typical characteristics of the Prunus genus, including simple leaves and showy pink or white flowers. It thrives in temperate climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This variety is valued for its culinary and ornamental uses in horticulture.
