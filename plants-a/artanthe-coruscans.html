Artanthe coruscans

Artanthe coruscans, commonly known as the shimmering artanthe, is a striking plant native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, jewel-toned foliage that ranges from deep purples to bright reds and blues. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of pink and white, which bloom during the cooler months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full to partial sunlight.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
