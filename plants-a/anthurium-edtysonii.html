Anthurium edtysonii

Anthurium edtysonii, commonly known as the Ed Tyson's anthurium, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped leaves that can range in color from deep green to dark red, depending on the variety. The plant produces striking spadices, which are the characteristic inflorescences of the Araceae family, often surrounded by colorful bracts. It thrives in humid, shady environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in botanical gardens.
