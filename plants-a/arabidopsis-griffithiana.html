Arabidopsis griffithiana

Arabidopsis griffithiana, commonly known as the Griffith's thale cress, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayas.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes other well-known species like Arabidopsis thaliana. This plant is characterized by its small, compact growth habit and produces small white or pink flowers. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine regions.

Due to its genetic similarity to Arabidopsis thaliana, it is of interest in botanical and genetic research.
