Artemisia semsek

Artemisia semsek, commonly known as Semsek wormwood, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its aromatic, gray-green foliage and small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in mountainous areas. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes many other aromatic herbs.

Artemisia semsek is valued for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional remedies for various ailments.
