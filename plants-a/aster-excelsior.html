Aster excelsior

Aster excelsior, commonly known as the tall blue aster, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems that can reach heights of up to 2 meters. The plant produces showy blue to purple flowers in the late summer and early fall. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Aster excelsior is also valued for its ecological benefits, providing nectar for pollinators.
