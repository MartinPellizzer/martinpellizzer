Ardisia crenata subsp. obtusifolia

Ardisia crenata subsp.

obtusifolia, commonly known as the blunt-leaf evergreen, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtle family (Myrtaceae). It is native to Southeast Asia, including countries such as Malaysia and Indonesia.

The plant is characterized by its glossy, ovate to elliptical leaves that are typically dark green in color. It produces small, white or pale pink flowers followed by bright red berries, which are an important food source for various bird species.

This subspecies is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and compact growth habit.
