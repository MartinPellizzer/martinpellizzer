Astroloba egregia var. fardeniana

Astroloba egregia var. fardeniana, commonly known as the Farden's astroloba, is a striking succulent native to South Africa.

It features dense, cylindrical rosettes of fleshy, blue-green leaves that are often tinged with red or purple at the tips. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, requiring minimal water once established.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and arid landscapes.
