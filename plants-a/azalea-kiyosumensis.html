Azalea kiyosumensis

Azalea kiyosumensis, commonly known as the Kiyosumi azalea, is a species native to Japan.

It belongs to the Rhododendron genus and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage. The plant produces clusters of showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink and white during the spring. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soil and partial to full shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
