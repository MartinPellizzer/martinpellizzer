Amorphophallus ranchanensis

Amorphophallus ranchanensis, commonly known as the Ranchan arrowhead, is a flowering plant native to the Western Ghats of India.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its distinctive arrowhead-shaped leaves. The plant produces a striking inflorescence with a spadix and a spathe, typical of its botanical group. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
