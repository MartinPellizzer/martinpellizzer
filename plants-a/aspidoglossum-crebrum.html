Aspidoglossum crebrum

Aspidoglossum crebrum, commonly known as the silver sword, is a striking succulent native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of narrow, silver-tipped leaves that form a dense, sword-like shape. The plant produces tall, branched stems that bear clusters of small, white to pale purple flowers. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a desirable addition to both indoor and outdoor collections.
