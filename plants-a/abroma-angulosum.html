Abroma angulosum

Abroma angulosum, commonly known as the angular abroma, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows in dry, rocky habitats, showcasing its adaptability to arid environments. The plant produces small, clustered yellow flowers that bloom in summer, adding a vibrant touch to its surroundings. Its leaves are narrow and angular, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Abroma angulosum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage and seasonal blooms.
