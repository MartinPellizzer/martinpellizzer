Anthurium chiriacoense

Anthurium chiriacoense, commonly known as the Chiriacoense arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are often brightly colored. The plant produces an inflorescence known as a spadix, which is surrounded by a colorful spathe. It thrives in humid, warm environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Anthurium chiriacoense is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in botanical gardens.
