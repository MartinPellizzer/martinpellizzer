Achyranthes porphyristachya

Achyranthes porphyristachya, commonly known as redroot smartweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Polygonaceae and is characterized by its upright, branched stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white in color, followed by reddish-brown seeds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands, marshes, and along streambanks.

Its reddish roots give it its common name and contribute to its ecological importance in supporting local wildlife.
