Abies pseudochensiensis

Abies pseudochensiensis, commonly known as the Chinese fir, is a coniferous evergreen tree native to China.

It belongs to the family Pinaceae and is characterized by its slender, conical shape and dark green, needle-like leaves. The tree produces small, spherical cones that mature to a reddish-brown color. It thrives in temperate climates with well-drained soil and is often found in mountainous regions.

This species is valued for its timber and is also cultivated for ornamental purposes.
