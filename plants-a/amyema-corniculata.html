Amyema corniculata

Amyema corniculata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hakea, is a shrub native to Australia.

It belongs to the Proteaceae family and is characterized by its narrow, needle-like leaves. The plant produces bright red or orange flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It is adapted to dry, sandy soils and is often found in heathlands and coastal areas.

Amyema corniculata is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
