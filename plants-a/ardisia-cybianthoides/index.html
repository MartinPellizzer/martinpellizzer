Ardisia Cybianthoides

Coral beauty, scientifically known as Ardisia cybianthoides, is a striking evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia. With its glossy dark green leaves and clusters of bright red berries, it stands out in any landscape. This plant is prized for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of conditions. It is commonly used in gardens and as a houseplant due to its adaptability and visual appeal. Its unique appearance and easy care make it a popular choice among plant enthusiasts.
