Acnistus hauthalii

Acnistus hauthalii, commonly known as the Hauthal's acnistus, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its branched stems and small, clustered leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in late spring, attracting various pollinators. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens, though it is not widely distributed outside its native range.
