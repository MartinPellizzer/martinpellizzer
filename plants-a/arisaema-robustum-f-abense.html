Arisaema robustum f. abense

Arisaema robustum f. abense, commonly known as the giant arrowhead, is a striking member of the Araceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of southern China and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and showy, spadix-bearing inflorescence. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories. Its botanical features include a tuberous rhizome and a distinctive, palmately lobed foliage.

The spadix is typically yellowish with a contrasting red or purple base, making it visually striking.
