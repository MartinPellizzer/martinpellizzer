Adhatoda capensis

Adhatoda capensis, commonly known as the African Trumpet or Blue Trumpet, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 3 meters tall, characterized by its lance-shaped leaves and striking blue or violet trumpet-shaped flowers. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Adhatoda capensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and has traditional medicinal uses in some African cultures.
