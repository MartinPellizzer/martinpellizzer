Aylostera mixticolor

Aylostera mixticolor, commonly known as the mixed-color aylostera, is a member of the Acanthaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its striking, variegated foliage with shades of green, yellow, and white. It produces clusters of tubular, violet-blue flowers that bloom in summer.

Aylostera mixticolor is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
