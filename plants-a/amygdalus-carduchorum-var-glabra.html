Amygdalus carduchorum var. glabra

Amygdalus carduchorum var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth almond.

This variety is a shrub or small tree native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. It produces small, edible fruits that resemble almonds in appearance. The plant is characterized by its smooth, glabrous bark and lance-shaped leaves.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and historical use in traditional medicine.
