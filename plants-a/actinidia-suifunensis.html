Actinidia suifunensis

Actinidia suifunensis, commonly known as the Chinese mayapple, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the Actinidiaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and showy, white, bell-shaped flowers. The plant produces small, green berries that ripen to a yellow-orange color, which are edible but often have a bitter taste.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
