Abies Forrestii Var. Ferreana

Abies forrestii var. ferreana is a rare and striking conifer native to the high altitudes of China. This variant of the firs is known for its unique reddish-brown bark and dense, pyramidal growth habit. It thrives in cool, moist climates and is often found in mountainous regions with rich soil. The plant's ornamental value has made it a sought-after species in horticulture. Despite its beauty, it remains elusive and difficult to cultivate outside its native habitat.
