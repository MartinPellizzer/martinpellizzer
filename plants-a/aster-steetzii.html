Aster steetzii

Aster steetzii, commonly known as Steetz's aster, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that bloom in late summer and early fall. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and grasslands. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a hairy texture that helps it retain moisture.

Aster steetzii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting pollinators.
