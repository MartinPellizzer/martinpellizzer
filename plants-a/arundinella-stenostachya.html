Arundinella stenostachya

Arundinella stenostachya, commonly known as the narrow-staminal reed, is a perennial grass native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in wetland habitats such as marshes and along riverbanks. The plant features slender, cylindrical stems that can reach heights of up to 2 meters. Its inflorescence consists of a loose cluster of spikelets, each bearing small, two-lobed flowers.

The leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a prominent midrib and a fibrous texture.
