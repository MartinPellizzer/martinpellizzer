Alternanthera menziesii

Alternanthera menziesii, commonly known as red-veined joyweed, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking foliage, featuring bright red veins against deep green leaves. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade. It is often grown as an ornamental plant for its vibrant leaf coloration.

Its rapid growth makes it suitable for use in water gardens or as a ground cover.
