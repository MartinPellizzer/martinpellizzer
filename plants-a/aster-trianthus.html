Aster trianthus

Aster trianthus, commonly known as the purple coneflower, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features showy purple or pink petals surrounding a central cone-like structure composed of tiny florets. Aster trianthus typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, providing nectar for pollinators.
