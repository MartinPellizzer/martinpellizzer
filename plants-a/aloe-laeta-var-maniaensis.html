Aloe laeta var. maniaensis

Aloe laeta var. maniaensis, commonly known as the Maniaensis Aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, with a waxy coating that helps reduce water loss. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing yellow flowers, which bloom in late winter to early spring. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Due to its ornamental value and ease of care, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
