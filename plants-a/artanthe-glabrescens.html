Artanthe glabrescens

Artanthe glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth artanthe, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is often found in open woodlands and grasslands. It has narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, pale blue to purple flowers.

Artanthe glabrescens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
