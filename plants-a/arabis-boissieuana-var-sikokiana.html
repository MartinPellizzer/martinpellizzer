Arabis boissieuana var. sikokiana

Arabis boissieuana var. sikokiana, commonly known as the Sikokian rockcress, is a rare and endemic plant species native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, characterized by its small, cruciform flowers and pinnately divided leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils at high altitudes, demonstrating a preference for well-drained and sunny environments. Its flowers, which range in color from white to pale yellow, bloom in early spring, adding a delicate touch to its alpine habitat.
