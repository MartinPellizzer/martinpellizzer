Artabotrys macrophyllus

Artabotrys macrophyllus, commonly known as the white sand orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, glossy, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers. The plant produces showy, pendulous inflorescences that bloom during the wet season. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
