Azarolus intermedia

Azarolus intermedia, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking clusters of yellow flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has compound leaves with lance-shaped leaflets and produces small, winged fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Azarolus intermedia is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.
