Aloe tauri

Aloe tauri, commonly known as the Taurine aloe, is a succulent plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It features fleshy, upright stems that are covered in a layer of white, powdery wax, which helps reduce water loss. The plant produces narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color, with a waxy coating. Aloe tauri blooms with yellow flowers that emerge from the base of the plant, often in late spring or early summer.

It is well adapted to hot, dry climates and requires minimal water once established.
