Arenaria yulongshanensis

Arenaria yulongshanensis, commonly known as the Yulongshan sandwort, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit. The plant features small, white or pink flowers that bloom in early summer, typically arranged in clusters. Its leaves are narrow and succulent, adapted to survive in arid and rocky environments.

It is often found in alpine meadows and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in stabilizing soil in harsh climates.
