Alsine picta var. albiflora

Alsine picta var. albiflora, commonly known as the white-flowered sand-verbena, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

Its leaves are narrow and linear, often covered in fine hairs, and it has a compact growth habit.
