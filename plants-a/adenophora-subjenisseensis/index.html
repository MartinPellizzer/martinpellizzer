Adenophora Subjenisseensis

Adenophora subjenisseensis is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of China. It belongs to the Campanulaceae family, known for its bell-shaped flowers. This species is characterized by its slender stems and pale blue to purple blooms. Due to its limited habitat and environmental threats, it is considered endangered. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this unique botanical treasure.
