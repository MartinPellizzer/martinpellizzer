Azalea Barbata

Azalea barbata is a striking evergreen shrub native to the mountainous regions of China. Known for its vibrant clusters of pink and white flowers, it adds a splash of color to any garden. This plant thrives in acidic soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice among horticulturists. Its foliage is dense and glossy, providing year-round visual appeal. With proper care, Azalea barbata can bloom repeatedly throughout the growing season.
