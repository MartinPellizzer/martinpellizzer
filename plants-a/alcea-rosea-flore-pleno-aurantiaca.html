Alcea rosea flore-pleno-aurantiaca

Alcea rosea flore-pleno-aurantiaca, commonly known as the golden touch sunflower, is a hybrid variety of the common sunflower.

It is characterized by its large, showy, double-flowered blooms that are predominantly yellow with a golden-orange center. The plant is a herbaceous annual that can grow up to 3 meters tall, featuring sturdy, hairy stems and large, heart-shaped leaves. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.

Its vibrant color and ornamental value make it a standout in both cultivated and natural settings.
