Anthurium spectabile

Anthurium spectabile, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are often deep green with a reddish or maroon tint. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-shaped inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, typically in shades of red, pink, or white. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.

Anthurium spectabile is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and long-lasting blooms.
