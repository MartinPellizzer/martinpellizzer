Arisaema glaucescens var. viridiflorum

Arisaema glaucescens var. viridiflorum, commonly known as the green-flowered arrowhead, is a member of the Araceae family.

It is characterized by its distinctive arrowhead-shaped leaves and a spadix surrounded by a spathe. The plant produces bright green flowers, which are typically clustered in a spadix, and it thrives in moist, shaded environments. It is native to regions of East Asia, including China and Japan.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique foliage structure.
