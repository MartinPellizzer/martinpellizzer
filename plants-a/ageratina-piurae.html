Ageratina piurae

Ageratina piurae, commonly known as the Piura blueweed, is a flowering plant native to the Andean regions of South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Ageratina piurae is often used in traditional medicine and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
