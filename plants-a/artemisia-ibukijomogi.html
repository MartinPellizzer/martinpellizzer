Artemisia ibukijomogi

Artemisia ibukijomogi, commonly known as the Japanese wormwood, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, silver-gray foliage. The plant produces small, yellowish flower heads that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats.

Artemisia ibukijomogi is valued for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
