Aragallus nothoxys

Aragallus nothoxys, commonly known as the narrow-leaved aragallus, is a flowering plant species belonging to the family Polygonaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and lance-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs.
