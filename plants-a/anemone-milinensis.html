Anemone milinensis

Anemone milinensis, commonly known as the Milinensis anemone, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or dry soils, often found in coastal areas. The plant produces showy, white to pinkish flowers with five petals, which bloom in early spring. Its leaves are basal, palmate, and have a distinctive lobed shape.

Anemone milinensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
