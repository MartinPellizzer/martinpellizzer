Anthemis leucantha

Anthemis leucantha, commonly known as white ragwort, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and thrives in temperate climates. Its flowers are yellow with a white central disk, and it produces small, oval-shaped seeds.

It is often found in open habitats such as fields and coastal areas.
