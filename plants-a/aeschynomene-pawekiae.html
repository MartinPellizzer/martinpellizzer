Aeschynomene pawekiae

Aeschynomene pawekiae, commonly known as the Cape Verde morning glory, is a flowering plant native to the Cape Verde Islands.

It is a herbaceous annual that produces vibrant blue to violet flowers, typically blooming in the morning. The plant has slender, branching stems and heart-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy surface. It thrives in arid and coastal environments, showing adaptability to poor soil conditions.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens in tropical and subtropical regions.
