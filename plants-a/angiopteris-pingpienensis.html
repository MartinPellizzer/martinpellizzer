Angiopteris pingpienensis

Angiopteris pingpienensis, commonly known as the Pingpien fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Angiopteridaceae.

It is characterized by its large, compound fronds that can reach lengths of up to 2 meters. The plant exhibits a distinctive morphology with a creeping rhizome and large, bipinnate leaves. It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia, particularly in China and Southeast Asia.

Angiopteris pingpienensis is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky areas.
