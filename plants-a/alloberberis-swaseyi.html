Alloberberis swaseyi

Alloberberis swaseyi, commonly known as the Swasey's barberry, is a shrub native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

The plant produces yellow flowers in the spring, which are followed by red, berry-like fruits that are edible for some wildlife species. Alloberberis swaseyi thrives in rocky soils and is often found in desert or semi-arid environments.
