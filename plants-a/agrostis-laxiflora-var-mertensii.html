Agrostis laxiflora var. mertensii

Agrostis laxiflora var. mertensii, commonly known as the Mertens' slender grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in grasslands and open woodlands, preferring well-drained soils. The plant has slender, upright stems with narrow, grass-like leaves that are often hairy. Its inflorescence consists of a spike-like cluster of small, spikelet-bearing branches.

It plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for small invertebrates.
