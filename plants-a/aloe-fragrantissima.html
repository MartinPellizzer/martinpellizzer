Aloe fragrantissima

Aloe fragrantissima, commonly known as the sweet aloe or the fragrant aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, with a waxy coating that helps it retain moisture. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. Its leaves contain a gel-like substance that is often used for its soothing properties.

Aloe fragrantissima is well-suited for dry climates and requires minimal water to thrive.
