Anthelis arenaria

Anthelis arenaria, commonly known as the sand sandwort, is a low-growing flowering plant native to arid and coastal regions.

It is characterized by its compact, cushion-like growth habit and is often found in sandy or gravelly soils. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are typically succulent, adapted to conserve water in harsh environments.
