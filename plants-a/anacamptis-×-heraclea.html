Anacamptis × heraclea

Anacamptis × heraclea, commonly known as the Narrow-leaved Orchid, is a hybrid species within the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and clusters of pale yellow to greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and grasslands. It is native to southern Europe and is often found in calcareous soils.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
