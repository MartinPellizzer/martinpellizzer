Astronia meyeri

Astronia meyeri, commonly known as the Meyer's astronia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant produces showy, daisy-like flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. Astronia meyeri is often found in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
