Albikia

Albikia, commonly known as the silverleaf tree, is a tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, glossy leaves that have a silvery underside, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters, often attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies. Its wood is valued for its durability and is used in traditional construction and furniture-making.

Albikia also has medicinal properties, with various parts of the plant used in herbal remedies for their anti-inflammatory and antioxidant effects.
