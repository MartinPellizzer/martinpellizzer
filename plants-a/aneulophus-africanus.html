Aneulophus africanus

Aneulophus africanus, commonly known as the African leaf roller, is a species of moth belonging to the family Tortricidae.

It is characterized by its small size and the distinctive patterns on its wings, which often resemble leaves, aiding in camouflage. The larvae of this species feed on the leaves of various plants, primarily in the regions of Africa.
