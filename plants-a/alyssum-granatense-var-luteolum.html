Alyssum granatense var. luteolum

Alyssum granatense var. luteolum, commonly known as the orange alyssum, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense clusters of small, bright yellow flowers. The plant produces tiny, oval-shaped seeds that are dispersed by wind, contributing to its natural spread. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant color and low maintenance requirements.
