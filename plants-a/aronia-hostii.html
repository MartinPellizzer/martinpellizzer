Aronia hostii

Aronia hostii, commonly known as the purple beauty chokeberry, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern North America.

It is characterized by its dark purple to black berries, which are rich in antioxidants and anthocyanins. The plant produces showy white to pale pink flowers in early spring, followed by vibrant red foliage in the fall. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers moist, well-drained soil.

Aronia hostii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping and conservation efforts.
