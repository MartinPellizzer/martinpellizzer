Aloe arborescens var. viridifolia

Aloe arborescens var. viridifolia, commonly known as the Green Aloe or Tree Aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a branching structure with fleshy, thick leaves that are typically green with a slight blue or purple tinge. The plant produces bright yellow flowers in clusters during the summer months. Its leaves contain a gel-like substance that is often used for its soothing and healing properties.

It thrives in well-draining soil and requires moderate sunlight to flourish.
