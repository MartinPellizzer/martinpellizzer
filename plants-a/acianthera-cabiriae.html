Acianthera cabiriae

Acianthera cabiriae, commonly known as the Cabiria orchid, is a terrestrial orchid belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to South America, particularly Brazil, and thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant features a sympodial growth habit with a rhizome and produces flowering stems with clustered, showy flowers. Its flowers are typically pink or purple, with a distinctive lip that attracts pollinators.

Acianthera cabiriae is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
