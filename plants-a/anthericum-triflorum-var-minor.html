Anthericum triflorum var. minor

Anthericum triflorum var. minor, commonly known as the lesser three-flowered lily, is a herbaceous perennial native to Europe.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, white or pale pink flowers in clusters of three. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream sides. Its flowers bloom in early spring, and it is often found in moist soils with good drainage.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in natural habitats.
