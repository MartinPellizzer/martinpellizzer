Aster ferdinandi

Aster ferdinandi, commonly known as the Ferdinand's aster, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet. The plant features showy, daisy-like flowers with purple to blue petals and yellow centers. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, giving them a slightly fuzzy texture.

Aster ferdinandi thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and naturalized areas.
