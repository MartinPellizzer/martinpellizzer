Agathosma adnata

Agathosma adnata, commonly known as African geranium, is a perennial shrub native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, small, greenish-yellow flowers. The plant has dark green, lance-shaped leaves with a distinctive silvery underside. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rocky or sandy gardens.

Agathosma adnata is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
