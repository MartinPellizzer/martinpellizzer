Anthurium acaimense

Anthurium acaimense, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a tropical flowering plant native to Central America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves and vibrant red spathes. The plant produces small, greenish-white spadices inside the spatulate structure, which are the actual flowers. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium acaimense is popular as a decorative plant due to its ornamental foliage and unique floral display.
