Anthurium × desmetianum

Anthurium × desmetianum, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a hybrid plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically deep green with a reddish or maroon tint. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-shaped inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, often in shades of red, orange, or yellow. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.

Anthurium × desmetianum is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and long-lasting blooms.
