Asplundia ahlneri

Asplundia ahlneri, commonly known as the Ahlner's arrowroot, is a tropical plant belonging to the Araceae family.

It is native to the Philippines and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and striking inflorescence. The plant produces a starchy root that is used as a food source in some regions. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Asplundia ahlneri is valued both for its ornamental beauty and its edible properties.
