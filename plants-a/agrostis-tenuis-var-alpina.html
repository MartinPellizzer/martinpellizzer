Agrostis tenuis var. alpina

Agrostis tenuis var. alpina, commonly known as alpine slender grass, is a low-growing perennial grass species native to mountainous regions.

It typically thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine meadows and rocky slopes. The plant has fine, slender leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It is adapted to withstand harsh climatic conditions, including strong winds and low temperatures.

Its botanical features make it an important component of high-altitude ecosystems.
