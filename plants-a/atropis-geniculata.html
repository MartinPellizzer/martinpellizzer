Atropis geniculata

Atropis geniculata, commonly known as the zigzag orchid, is a unique flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, often colorful flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has a distinctive zigzagging stem, which gives it its common name and aids in its identification. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and arranged alternately along the stem.

Atropis geniculata thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
