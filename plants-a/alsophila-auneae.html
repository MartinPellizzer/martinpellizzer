Alsophila auneae

Alsophila auneae, commonly known as the Aune's tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feathery fronds. The plant has a robust root system that helps it anchor in moist, shaded environments. Its fronds are typically bipinnate, with a distinctive, elegant appearance that makes it popular in horticulture.

Alsophila auneae plays an important ecological role in its native habitat by providing shelter and food for various wildlife species.
