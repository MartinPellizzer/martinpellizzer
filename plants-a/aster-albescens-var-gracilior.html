Aster albescens var. gracilior

Aster albescens var. gracilior, commonly known as the slender white aster, is a herbaceous perennial native to the northeastern United States.

It features finely divided, gray-green leaves and produces clusters of small, white flowers with yellow centers. The plant typically grows in moist, woodland habitats and blooms in late summer to early fall. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, providing nectar for pollinators.

Its botanical characteristics make it a distinctive member of the Asteraceae family.
