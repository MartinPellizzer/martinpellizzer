Azalea yunnanensis

Azalea yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan azalea, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Rhododendron genus and is characterized by its vibrant, showy blooms that come in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions with cool, humid climates. Its leaves are broad and leathery, providing a striking contrast to its colorful flowers.

Azalea yunnanensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
