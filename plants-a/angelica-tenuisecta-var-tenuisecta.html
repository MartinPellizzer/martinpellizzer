Angelica tenuisecta var. tenuisecta

Angelica tenuisecta var. tenuisecta, commonly known as the narrow-leaved angelica, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to parts of Asia.

It features tall, slender stems that can reach up to 2 meters in height, topped with small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in compound umbels. The plant has finely divided, feathery leaves that give it a delicate appearance. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in forested areas.

Its botanical characteristics make it a distinctive member of the Apiaceae family.
