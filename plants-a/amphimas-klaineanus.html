Amphimas klaineanus

Amphimas klaineanus, commonly known as the Klaineanus orchid, is a flowering plant native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and clustered, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, with intricate patterns on the petals. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.
