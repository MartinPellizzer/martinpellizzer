Argyrolobium andrewsianum

Argyrolobium andrewsianum, commonly known as the silver globular pea, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its globular, silver-gray seed pods. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
