Adiantum senae

Adiantum senae, commonly known as the Sena's maidenhair fern, is a species of fern belonging to the Adiantum genus.

It is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds that resemble the shape of a feather. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often found in rocky or forested areas. Its leaves are usually dark green and have a distinctive, elegant appearance.

Adiantum senae is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
