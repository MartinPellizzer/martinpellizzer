Anvillea faurei

Anvillea faurei, commonly known as the yellow bell vine, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant yellow, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and can grow as a vine or a shrub. It thrives in warm, sunny climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Anvillea faurei is also valued for its fragrant blossoms and adaptability to various soil types.
