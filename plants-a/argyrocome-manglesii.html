Argyrocome manglesii

Argyrocome manglesii, commonly known as the silverleaf manglesia, is a flowering plant native to specific regions of South America.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.
