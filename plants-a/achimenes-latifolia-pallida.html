Achimenes latifolia-pallida

Achimenes latifolia-pallida, commonly known as the pale broadleaf achimenes, is a tropical flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from pink to white. The plant typically grows as a herbaceous perennial, often reaching heights of up to 1 meter. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking vibrant, colorful blooms.
