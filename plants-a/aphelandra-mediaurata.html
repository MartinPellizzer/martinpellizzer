Aphelandra mediaurata

Aphelandra mediaurata, commonly known as the golden flame plant, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, pendulous inflorescences that bear bright yellow bracts, which are often mistaken for flowers. The plant's leaves are elliptical, glossy, and dark green, providing a lush backdrop to its vibrant blooms. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Aphelandra mediaurata is well-suited for indoor cultivation and adds a dramatic visual impact to any space.
