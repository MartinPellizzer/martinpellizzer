Arisaema lobatum var. latisectum

Arisaema lobatum var. latisectum, commonly known as the broad-lobed arisaema, is a member of the Araceae family.

It is native to eastern Asia, particularly China, and is characterized by its distinctive, arrow-shaped leaves with broad, deeply lobed segments. The plant produces an attractive, spadix-like inflorescence surrounded by a broad, green spathe. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for shade gardens and botanical collections.
