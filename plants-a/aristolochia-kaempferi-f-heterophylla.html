Aristolochia kaempferi f. heterophylla

Aristolochia kaempferi f. heterophylla, commonly known as the Japanese arrowhead vine, is a flowering plant belonging to the Aristolochiaceae family.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and distinctive, pipe-shaped flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. The plant is native to Southeast Asia and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. Its leaves are usually larger and more elongated compared to the typical form of Aristolochia kaempferi.

The species is also noted for its rapid growth habit and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
