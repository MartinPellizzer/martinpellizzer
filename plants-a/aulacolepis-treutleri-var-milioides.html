Aulacolepis treutleri var. milioides

Aulacolepis treutleri var. milioides, commonly known as the slender spikegrass, is a grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along water bodies.

Its botanical features make it an important component of wetland ecosystems.
