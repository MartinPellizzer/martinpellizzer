Auxopus macranthus

Auxopus macranthus, commonly known as the giant flame vine, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from red to orange, often with a distinctive flame-like appearance. The plant features heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green, providing a lush backdrop to its vibrant blooms. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Auxopus macranthus is also known for its climbing habit, making it a popular choice for trellises and arbors.
