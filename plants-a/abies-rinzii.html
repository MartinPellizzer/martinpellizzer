Abies rinzii

Abies rinzii, commonly known as the Rinzii fir, is a species of conifer native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, pyramidal shape and dark green, needle-like leaves. The tree produces cones that are typically reddish-brown and contain winged seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in cool, moist climates and is often found in high-altitude forests.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
