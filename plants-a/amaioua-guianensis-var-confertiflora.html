Amaioua guianensis var. confertiflora

Amaioua guianensis var. confertiflora, commonly known as the dense-flowered amaioua, is a species native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is a woody plant characterized by its thick, leathery leaves and clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The plant produces yellowish-white flowers that are often arranged in dense inflorescences, contributing to its common name. Its botanical features include a smooth, bark-like stem and elliptical leaves with a glossy surface.
