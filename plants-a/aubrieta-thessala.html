Aubrieta thessala

Aubrieta thessala, commonly known as the Balkan rockcress, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Balkan Peninsula.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many flowering plants with characteristic cruciform flowers. The plant produces small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, typically from March to May. Aubrieta thessala thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and alpine settings.

Its hardy nature and ornamental value make it a popular choice among gardeners seeking drought-tolerant plants.
