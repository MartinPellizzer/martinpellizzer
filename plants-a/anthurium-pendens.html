Anthurium pendens

Anthurium pendens, commonly known as the hanging anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves and vibrant, spathiflorous inflorescence. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers surrounded by a colorful spathe, which is often the main ornamental feature. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium pendens is popular as a houseplant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
