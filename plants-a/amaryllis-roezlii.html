Amaryllis roezlii

Amaryllis roezlii, commonly known as the Zambian lily, is a striking bulbous plant native to southern Africa.

It features tall, strap-like leaves and produces clusters of large, showy flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and containers. Its blooming period typically occurs in late winter to early spring, adding color during the colder months.

Amaryllis roezlii is relatively easy to care for, requiring minimal water and regular feeding during its active growing season.
