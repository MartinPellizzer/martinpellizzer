Arduina inermis

Arduina inermis, commonly known as the smooth arduina, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and have a smooth texture, contributing to its common name.

The fruit is a small, dry capsule that contains several seeds.
