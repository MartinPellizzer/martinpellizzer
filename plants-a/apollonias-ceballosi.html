Apollonias ceballosi

Apollonias ceballosi, commonly known as the Ceballos' apollonias, is a rare and unique plant species native to specific regions in South America.

It is characterized by its striking, often colorful flowers that bloom during the warmer months. The plant has a compact growth habit, with narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. Its botanical significance lies in its limited distribution and potential role in local ecosystems.
