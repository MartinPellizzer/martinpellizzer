Agrostis scabra var. tenuis

Agrostis scabra var. tenuis, commonly known as slender rough fescue, is a grass species native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

It is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves with a rough texture. The plant typically forms dense, low-growing mats and is often found in disturbed soils or open habitats. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences bearing numerous tiny flowers.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
