Anthurium kunthii var. cylindricum

Anthurium kunthii var. cylindricum, commonly known as the cylindrical anthurium, is a tropical flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are often dark green with a velvety texture. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-shaped inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, typically in shades of red or orange. It thrives in humid, warm environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.

Its unique foliage and showy flowers make it a popular choice for indoor gardening and ornamental purposes.
