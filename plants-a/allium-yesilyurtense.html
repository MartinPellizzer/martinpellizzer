Allium yesilyurtense

Allium yesilyurtense, commonly known as the Yesilyurt onion, is a species of flowering plant in the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is native to Turkey and is characterized by its bulbous base and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale purple flowers that bloom in the summer months. It is adapted to grow in dry, rocky soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its ornamental value and potential culinary uses, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
