Arundinaria hsienchuensis

Arundinaria hsienchuensis, commonly known as the Hsienchu bamboo, is a species of grass native to Taiwan.

It is characterized by its tall, slender culms that can reach heights of up to 5 meters. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are typically green and smooth in texture. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Arundinaria hsienchuensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated for its ecological benefits.
