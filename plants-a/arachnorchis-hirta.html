Arachnorchis hirta

Arachnorchis hirta, commonly known as the hairy spider orchid, is an epiphytic plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, spider-like appearance due to its elongated, hairy pseudobulbs. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are often clustered in a spike. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, organic-rich substrates.

Arachnorchis hirta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
