Acnistus insularis

Acnistus insularis, commonly known as the island acnistus, is a flowering plant native to the Pacific islands.

It is a shrub that typically grows in coastal areas and is adapted to saline and sandy soils. The plant produces small, white flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a rough texture.

Acnistus insularis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens in tropical regions.
