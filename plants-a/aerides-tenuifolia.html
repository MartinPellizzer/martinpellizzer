Aerides tenuifolia

Aerides tenuifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leafed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features slender, grass-like leaves that are typically green with a waxy texture. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow, often with a slight pink tinge. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Aerides tenuifolia is appreciated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to cultivate in suitable conditions.
