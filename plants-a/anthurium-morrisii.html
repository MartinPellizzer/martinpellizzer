Anthurium morrisii

Anthurium morrisii, commonly known as the Morris' anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped leaves that are typically deep green with a glossy texture. The plant produces striking spadices, which are modified leaves that enclose the actual flowers, often in shades of red, orange, or yellow. Anthurium morrisii thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

It is popular as a decorative plant due to its unique foliage and long-lasting floral display.
