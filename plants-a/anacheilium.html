Anacheilium

Anacheilium, commonly known as the ghost orchid, is a rare and elusive orchid species native to the southeastern United States.

The plant thrives in specific wetland habitats, often growing on the bark of trees or in swampy areas. Anacheilium has a symbiotic relationship with mycorrhizal fungi, which is essential for its nutrient absorption.
