Arenaria tenuifolia var. vaillantiana

Arenaria tenuifolia var. vaillantiana, commonly known as the slender sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to arid and rocky environments.

It features small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are adapted to reduce water loss in dry conditions. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal dunes or inland gravelly areas.

This variant is noted for its resilience and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value.
