Anthemis cretica var. liguliflora

Anthemis cretica var. liguliflora, commonly known as narrowleaf tansy, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features wiry, branched stems and linear, aromatic leaves with a strong camphor-like scent. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flower heads that are composed of numerous tiny florets. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.

Its medicinal properties have been historically used for digestive and antiseptic purposes.
