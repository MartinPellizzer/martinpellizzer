Anthurium marense

Anthurium marense, commonly known as the Marense arrowhead, is a striking tropical plant native to the Caribbean.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically bright green with a waxy texture. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-like inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, often in shades of red or orange. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Anthurium marense is popular as a decorative plant due to its ornamental foliage and unique flowering structure.
