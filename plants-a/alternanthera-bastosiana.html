Alternanthera bastosiana

Alternanthera bastosiana, commonly known as the red-stemmed Alternanthera, is a species of flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking red or purple stems and leaves that often display a vibrant red or pink coloration. The plant belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is known for its ornamental value in gardens and water features. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Its growth habit is typically herbaceous, with some varieties exhibiting a creeping or upright form.
