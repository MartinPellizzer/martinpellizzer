Astracantha transoxana

Astracantha transoxana, commonly known as the Transoxiana thornveld, is a perennial shrub native to Central Asia.

It is characterized by its spiny stems and small, yellowish flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant has thick, fleshy leaves adapted to arid environments, allowing it to conserve water efficiently. It thrives in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in steppe and desert regions.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes cultivated as an ornamental plant in dry gardens.
