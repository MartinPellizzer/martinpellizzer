Adenia stenodactyla

Adenia stenodactyla, commonly known as the narrow-fingered adenia, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa and the Arabian Peninsula.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments. The plant features compound leaves with elongated, narrow leaflets and produces striking yellow or orange flowers. It is often found in rocky or coastal areas, where it adapts well to poor soil conditions.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional uses in local cultures.
