Aquilegia × emodi

Aquilegia × emodi, commonly known as the Japanese columbine, is a hybrid species native to Japan.

It is characterized by its striking, spurred flowers that come in a range of colors including red, orange, and yellow. The plant typically grows to a height of 30–60 cm and prefers well-drained, loamy soil. Its leaves are palmately compound, with five to seven leaflets that are deeply lobed.

Aquilegia × emodi is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to grow in temperate climates.
