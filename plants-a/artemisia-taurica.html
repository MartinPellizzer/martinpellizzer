Artemisia taurica

Artemisia taurica, commonly known as Tauric wormwood, is a perennial herb native to southern Europe and parts of Asia.

It grows in dry, rocky, and sunny habitats, often found in steppe and arid regions. The plant has aromatic, silver-gray foliage with small, narrow leaves that are covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small and yellow, appearing in clusters, and it produces a strong, bitter scent.

Artemisia taurica is valued for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally in herbal remedies.
