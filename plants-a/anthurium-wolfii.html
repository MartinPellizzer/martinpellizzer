Anthurium wolfii

Anthurium wolfii, commonly known as the Wolf's anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-shaped inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, often in shades of red or orange. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.

Anthurium wolfii is popular as a decorative plant due to its ornamental foliage and unique floral structure.
