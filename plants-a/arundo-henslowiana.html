Arundo henslowiana

Arundo henslowiana, commonly known as Henslow's reed, is a tall, perennial grass native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically grows to heights of 2 to 4 meters, featuring narrow, grass-like leaves with a bluish-green hue. The plant produces dense, cylindrical inflorescences that bloom in late summer, adding ornamental value to gardens. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Its fibrous roots help prevent soil erosion, contributing to its ecological benefits.
