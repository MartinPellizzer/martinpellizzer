Argyreia Maingayi

Argyreia maingayi is a striking climbing vine native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia. Known for its vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers that range from white to deep pink, it adds a dramatic flair to gardens and landscapes. This plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires ample sunlight to flourish. Its rapid growth makes it an excellent choice for trellises, fences, or pergolas. Despite its beauty, it can become invasive if not properly managed.
