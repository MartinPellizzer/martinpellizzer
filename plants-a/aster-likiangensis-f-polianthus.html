Aster likiangensis f. polianthus

Aster likiangensis f. polianthus, commonly known as the Chinese aster or garden aster, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its showy, composite flower heads. The plant typically features vibrant purple, pink, or white flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its striking appearance and ability to thrive in various garden conditions.

The foliage is usually dark green and slightly hairy, adding texture to its overall appearance.
