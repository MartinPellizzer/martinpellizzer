Acianthera serratifolia

Acianthera serratifolia, commonly known as the serrated leaf orchid, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, spurred flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are elongated and have a serrated edge, contributing to its common name.

Acianthera serratifolia is a herbaceous perennial that thrives in well-drained soil and requires indirect sunlight for optimal growth.
