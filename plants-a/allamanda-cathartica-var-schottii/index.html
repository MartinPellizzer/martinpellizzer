Allamanda Cathartica Var. Schottii

Allamanda cathartica var. schottii is a striking tropical plant known for its vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers. Native to South America, this variety thrives in warm, humid climates and requires ample sunlight. Its foliage is glossy and dark green, adding a lush aesthetic to any garden or indoor space. The plant is popular among horticulturists for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements. With proper attention, it can bloom spectacularly throughout the growing season.
