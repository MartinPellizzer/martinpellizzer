Aloe buchlohii

Aloe buchlohii, commonly known as the Cape Town aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, rosette-shaped growth habit and thick, fleshy leaves that store water. The leaves are typically gray-green with a waxy coating, and they have a serrated edge. It produces yellow flowers on tall, branched inflorescences during the summer months.

This plant is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
