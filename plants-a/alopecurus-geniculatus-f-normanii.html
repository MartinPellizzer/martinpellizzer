Alopecurus geniculatus f. normanii

Alopecurus geniculatus f. normanii, commonly known as Norman's bentgrass, is a rare and distinctive grass species native to specific regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and slender, often curved leaves. The plant typically forms dense mats, which help stabilize soil and prevent erosion. It prefers moist, nutrient-rich soils and is often found in wet meadows or along stream banks.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a botanically significant and conservation-worthy species.
