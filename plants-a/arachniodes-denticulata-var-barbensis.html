Arachniodes denticulata var. barbensis

Arachniodes denticulata var. barbensis, commonly known as the Barben's Japanese spleenwort, is a fern species native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped fronds that are typically green with a slightly serrated edge. The plant thrives in shaded, moist environments, often found in forest understories. Its rhizomes are fibrous and help anchor it to rocky or soil surfaces.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and is cultivated in gardens for its elegant foliage.
