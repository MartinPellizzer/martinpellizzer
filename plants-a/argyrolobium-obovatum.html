Argyrolobium obovatum

Argyrolobium obovatum, commonly known as the silverleaf burrpea, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant produces small, spherical pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in shrubland and rocky areas.

Due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
