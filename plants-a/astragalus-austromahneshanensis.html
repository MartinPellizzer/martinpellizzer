Astragalus austromahneshanensis

Astragalus austromahneshanensis, commonly known as the southern Tibetan milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of southwestern China.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine habitats, showcasing its adaptability to harsh environments.

Its seeds are encased in a hard pod, a common feature among members of the genus Astragalus.
