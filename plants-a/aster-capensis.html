Aster capensis

Aster capensis, commonly known as the Cape aster, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in coastal and mountainous regions, thriving in well-drained soils. Its flowers are usually blue to purple in color, with ray florets surrounding a central disk.

Aster capensis is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
