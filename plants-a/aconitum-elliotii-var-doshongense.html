Aconitum elliotii var. doshongense

Aconitum elliotii var. doshongense, commonly known as the Doshong Blue Aconite, is a perennial herb native to the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has large, palmately lobed leaves that are typically dark green and hairy. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Due to its ornamental value and toxicity, it is often cultivated in gardens but requires caution during handling.
