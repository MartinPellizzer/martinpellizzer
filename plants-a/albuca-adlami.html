Albuca adlami

Albuca adlami, commonly known as the Cape tulip, is a bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features striking, showy flowers that resemble tulips, typically in shades of yellow and orange. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, white flowers in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Albuca adlami is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to grow in temperate climates.
