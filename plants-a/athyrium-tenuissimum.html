Athyrium tenuissimum

Athyrium tenuissimum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf fern, is a species of fern native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds that often have a soft, feathery appearance. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. Its rhizomes are usually short and produce a cluster of leaves.

Athyrium tenuissimum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and ability to thrive in woodland gardens.
