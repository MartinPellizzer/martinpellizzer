Arachnothryx peruviana

Arachnothryx peruviana, commonly known as the Peruvian spider tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to South America.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive, spiny thorns and compound leaves. The tree produces small, yellowish flowers that are followed by elongated, pod-like fruits containing several seeds. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological importance, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
