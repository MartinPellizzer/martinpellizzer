Adiantum hosei

Adiantum hosei, commonly known as the lace fern, is a delicate and elegant fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Adiantaceae and is characterized by its finely divided, feathery fronds that give it a lacy appearance. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in mountainous regions. Its leaves are usually bi- or tri-pinnate, with a distinctive, almost translucent quality.

Adiantum hosei is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in humid, indoor settings.
