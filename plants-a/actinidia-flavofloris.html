Actinidia flavofloris

Actinidia flavofloris, commonly known as the golden-flowered actinidia, is a deciduous vine native to China.

It is characterized by its delicate, yellowish flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant produces small, edible berries that are typically yellow or orange in color. Its leaves are heart-shaped and have a slightly serrated edge.

Actinidia flavofloris is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also appreciated for its medicinal properties in traditional Chinese medicine.
