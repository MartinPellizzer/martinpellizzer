Asplenium serra var. natalense

Asplenium serra var. natalense, commonly known as Natal's serrated spleenwort, is a fern belonging to the family Aspleniaceae.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often serrated along the edges. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its rhizomes are often creeping and produce clusters of small, dark green fronds.

This variety is native to southern Africa and is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.
