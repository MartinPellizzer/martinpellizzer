Achyronia costulata

Achyronia costulata, commonly known as the narrow-leafed milkweed, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa and the Middle East.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its slender, often hairy stems and narrow, elongated leaves.
