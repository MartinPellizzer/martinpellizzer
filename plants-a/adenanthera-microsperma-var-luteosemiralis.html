Adenanthera microsperma var. luteosemiralis

Adenanthera microsperma var. luteosemiralis, commonly known as the yellow-blossom burr legume, is a variant of the genus Adenanthera native to Australia.

It is a small, shrub-like plant characterized by its woody stems and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces distinctive, spiny pods that contain small, hard seeds. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in sandy or rocky soils.

Its botanical features make it an important species for ecological studies and potential agricultural use.
