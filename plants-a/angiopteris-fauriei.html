Angiopteris fauriei

Angiopteris fauriei, commonly known as the Faurie's water fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, delicate fronds that can reach lengths of up to 2 meters. The plant has a distinctive morphology with a creeping rhizome and a bipinnate leaf structure. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its reproductive structures include sori that are clustered on the underside of the fronds.
