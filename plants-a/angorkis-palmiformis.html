Angorkis palmiformis

Angorkis palmiformis, commonly known as the spiny leaf palm, is a unique plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its palm-like appearance, with a stout, unbranched stem and large, leathery leaves that have a distinctive spiny margin. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically greenish-white in color, followed by clustered fruit that turns from green to reddish as it matures. Angorkis palmiformis thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing in dense forest understories.

Its ornamental value has made it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical landscapes.
