Argyrolobium abyssinicum

Argyrolobium abyssinicum, commonly known as the Abyssinian silverlobed cowpea, is a flowering plant native to Ethiopia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and small, pea-like flowers. The plant is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it resilient to drought conditions. It produces edible seeds that are used as a food source in some regions.

Its botanical features make it an important species for both ecological and agricultural studies in its native habitat.
