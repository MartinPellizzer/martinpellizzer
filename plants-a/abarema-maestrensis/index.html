Abarema Maestrensis

Maestrense everlasting, scientifically known as Abarema maestrensis, is a rare and striking plant native to the high-altitude regions of South America. With its delicate, ever-lasting flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white, it captivates botanists and nature enthusiasts alike. This species thrives in cool, moist environments, often found in cloud forests and mountainous terrains. Its unique appearance and limited distribution make it a subject of scientific interest and conservation concern. Despite its beauty, the plant remains elusive, growing in remote locations that are difficult to access.
