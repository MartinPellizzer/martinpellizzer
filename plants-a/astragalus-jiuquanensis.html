Astragalus jiuquanensis

Astragalus jiuquanensis, commonly known as the Jiuquan milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of China.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its small, yellowish flowers and compound leaves. This species is adapted to harsh, dry environments, showcasing deep root systems and drought-resistant features. It plays a role in stabilizing soils and supporting local biodiversity in its native habitat.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
