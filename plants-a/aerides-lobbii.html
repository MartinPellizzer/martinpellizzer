Aerides lobbii

Aerides lobbii, commonly known as the Lobb's orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows on trees in humid, shaded environments, with its roots anchored in the bark. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are often pink or white, with a distinctive lip that is usually darker in color. It prefers well-drained, loose substrates and requires moderate to high humidity for optimal growth.

Aerides lobbii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
