Anagallis linifolia var. monelli

Anagallis linifolia var. monelli, commonly known as the Mexican strawberry flower, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and parts of Central America.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces clusters of small, pink to red flowers with five petals. The plant has slender, hairy stems and heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or open habitats.

The flowers bloom in spring and early summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
