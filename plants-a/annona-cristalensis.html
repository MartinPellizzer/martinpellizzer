Annona cristalensis

Annona cristalensis, commonly known as the crystalline annona, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and fragrant white flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant produces a yellow, fleshy fruit with a sweet and acidic flavor, similar to a cherimoya. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Annona cristalensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and edible fruit.
