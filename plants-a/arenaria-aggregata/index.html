Arenaria Aggregata

Arenaria aggregata is a resilient flowering plant commonly found in rocky and sandy environments. It thrives in harsh conditions, showcasing its adaptability to arid and coastal regions. The plant produces small, delicate blooms that add a touch of beauty to its rugged habitat. With its compact growth habit, it often forms dense mats that help prevent soil erosion. This species is valued for its ecological role and its ability to survive in challenging climates.
