Ampelopsis brevipedunculata var. heterophylla

Ampelopsis brevipedunculata var. heterophylla, commonly known as the variable grape ivy, is a climbing vine native to eastern Asia.

It features heart-shaped leaves that vary in size and shape, giving it its varietal name. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by blue-black berries. It thrives in partial shade and moist, well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for trellises and vertical gardens.
