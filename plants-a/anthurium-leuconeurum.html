Anthurium leuconeurum

Anthurium leuconeurum, commonly known as the red-veined anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped leaves with striking red veins against a deep green background. The plant produces clusters of small, spadix-like flowers surrounded by a colorful spathe. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium leuconeurum is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
