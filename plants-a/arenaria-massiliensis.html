Arenaria massiliensis

Arenaria massiliensis, commonly known as the Massilian sandwort, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically thrives in sandy or rocky soils. The plant features small, white or pinkish flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, adapted to withstand arid conditions.

Arenaria massiliensis is often found in coastal areas and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
