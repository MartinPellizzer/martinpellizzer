Aristolochia kunmingensis

Aristolochia kunmingensis, commonly known as the Yunnan pipevine, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Aristolochiaceae family and is characterized by its unique, pipe-shaped flowers that are often green or purple in color. The plant produces heart-shaped leaves and is known for its aromatic, complex scent. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and research settings.
