Agation lenormandii

Agation lenormandii, commonly known as the Lenormand's agate.

This plant is a member of the Apiaceae family, characterized by its distinctive umbellate inflorescence. It features finely divided, fern-like leaves that contribute to its ornamental appeal. The flowers are typically small and white, arranged in clusters that give the plant a delicate appearance.

Native to southern Africa, it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun exposure.
