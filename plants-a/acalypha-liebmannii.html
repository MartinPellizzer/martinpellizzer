Acalypha liebmannii

Acalypha liebmannii, commonly known as the silverleaf acalypha, is a shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking silver-green foliage, which gives it a unique appearance in gardens. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically green or reddish in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a versatile choice for landscaping.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to tolerate drought and coastal conditions.
