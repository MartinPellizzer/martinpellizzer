Alangium Villosum

Alangium villosum is a lesser-known yet fascinating plant native to parts of Asia. It belongs to the family Berberidaceae and is known for its unique appearance and ecological significance. This plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forests and mountainous regions. Its leaves are soft and hairy, giving it a distinctive texture. Despite its beauty, Alangium villosum remains underappreciated in both horticulture and scientific research.
