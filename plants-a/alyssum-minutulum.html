Alyssum minutulum

Alyssum minutulum, commonly known as dwarf alyssum, is a low-growing annual plant native to southern Europe.

It features dense clusters of small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink, blooming from spring to early summer. The plant has slender, branched stems and small, ovate leaves that are covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Alyssum minutulum is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
