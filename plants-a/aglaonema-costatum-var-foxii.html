Aglaonema costatum var. foxii

Aglaonema costatum var. foxii, commonly known as the Foxii Chinese Evergreen, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features striking, lance-shaped leaves with a deep green background and bright red or orange veins, creating a bold, eye-catching appearance. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening. Its foliage remains vibrant throughout the year, offering aesthetic value without requiring frequent pruning.

Aglaonema costatum var. foxii is also noted for its air-purifying qualities, contributing to improved indoor air quality.
