Artanthe glabella

Artanthe glabella, commonly known as the smooth artanthe, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the family Amaranthaceae and is characterized by its upright growth habit and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. Artanthe glabella is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to thrive in arid conditions.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive member of the genus Artanthe.
