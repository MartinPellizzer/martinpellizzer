Acetosella Zuccarinii

Zuccarini's lesser catchfly, scientifically known as Acetosella zuccarinii, is a small flowering plant native to the alpine regions of Europe. This species belongs to the family Caryophyllaceae, commonly known as the carnation family. It is characterized by its delicate pinkish flowers and low-growing habit, often found in rocky or gravelly soils. Despite its modest appearance, it plays an important ecological role in its habitat. Due to its limited distribution and specific environmental requirements, it is considered a rare and vulnerable species.
