Alchemilla australiana

Alchemilla australiana, commonly known as the Australian lady's mantle, is a perennial herb native to Australia.

It features basal leaves that are deeply lobed and covered in a dense layer of fine, silky hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in clusters during the spring and summer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or swampy areas.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable plant for both ecological and ornamental purposes.
