Actinostemon polymorphus var. angustatus

Actinostemon polymorphus var. angustatus, commonly known as the narrow-leaved actinostemon, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves that are typically narrow and linear. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually white or pale pink, emerging from the leaf axils. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
