Astragalus chengkangensis

Astragalus chengkangensis, commonly known as the Chengkang milkvetch, is a flowering plant species native to China.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions.

It plays a role in soil stabilization and is of interest for its potential medicinal properties.
