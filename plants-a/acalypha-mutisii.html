Acalypha mutisii

Acalypha mutisii, commonly known as the velvetleaf or silverleaf acalypha, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia and the Pacific.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety foliage that ranges from silver to deep green. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Acalypha mutisii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique leaf texture.
