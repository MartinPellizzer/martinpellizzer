Apatanthus crinitus

Apatanthus crinitus, commonly known as the silky lily, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, pale pink to white flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces long, slender seed pods. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its delicate, feathery appearance and prolonged blooming period.
