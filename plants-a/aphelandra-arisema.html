Aphelandra arisema

Aphelandra arisema, commonly known as the flameleaf croton, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, elongated leaves that are typically green with red or orange veins. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually yellow or orange in color. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Aphelandra arisema is often cultivated as a ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and unique appearance.
