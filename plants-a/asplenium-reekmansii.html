Asplenium reekmansii

Asplenium reekmansii, commonly known as the New Zealand maidenhair fern, is a small, delicate fern native to New Zealand.

It features intricate, finely divided fronds that are typically dark green and have a feathery appearance. The plant thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories. It has a unique ability to survive in rocky or crevice habitats due to its rhizomatous growth habit.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant.
