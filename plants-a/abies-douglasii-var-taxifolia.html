Abies douglasii var. taxifolia

Abies douglasii var. taxifolia, commonly known as the Douglas fir var. taxifolia, is a variant of the Douglas fir native to the western United States.

This variety thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often growing in mountainous regions.
