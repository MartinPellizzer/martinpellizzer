Achillea micrantha f. tenuisecta

Achillea micrantha f. tenuisecta, commonly known as narrow-leaved yarrow, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its finely divided, gray-green foliage. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow composite flowers arranged in flat-topped clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in dry, rocky habitats.

Its botanical features make it a valuable addition to native plant gardens and ecological restoration projects.
