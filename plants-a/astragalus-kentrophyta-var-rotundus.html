Astragalus kentrophyta var. rotundus

Astragalus kentrophyta var. rotundus, commonly known as the round-leaved milkvetch, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its rounded, heart-shaped leaves and produces small, yellowish to brownish flowers. The plant is native to arid and semi-arid regions of Central Asia, including parts of Kazakhstan and Uzbekistan. It thrives in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in steppe and desert environments.

The species is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
