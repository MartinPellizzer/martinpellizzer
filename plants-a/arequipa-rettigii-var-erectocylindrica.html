Arequipa rettigii var. erectocylindrica

Arequipa rettigii var. erectocylindrica, commonly known as the Arequipa lily, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Andes.

It is characterized by its upright, cylindrical stems and large, showy flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in rocky or alpine habitats. Its leaves are typically narrow and grass-like, while its flowers are clustered in terminal inflorescences.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens for its unique appearance.
