Austrobuxus horneanus

Austrobuxus horneanus, commonly known as the New Zealand holly, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the family Aquifoliaceae and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage. The leaves are typically oval-shaped with serrated edges and a leathery texture. It produces small, red berries that are an important food source for native birds.
