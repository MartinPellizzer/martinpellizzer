Agrostis Punicea Var. Filifolia

Redtop grass, scientifically known as Agrostis punicea var. filifolia, is a striking ornamental grass that thrives in moist to wet environments. Its vibrant red plumes and tufted foliage make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking visual interest. This variety is particularly noted for its ability to form dense, colorful clumps that add texture and color to landscapes. While it prefers partial shade, it can also tolerate full sun with adequate water. Its unique appearance and adaptability make it a valuable addition to both natural and cultivated settings.
