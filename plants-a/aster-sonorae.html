Aster sonorae

Aster sonorae, commonly known as the Sonoran aster, is a flowering plant native to the Sonoran Desert.

It is characterized by its showy, purple to blue flowers that bloom in the fall. The plant typically grows in rocky soils and has a compact, bushy form. Its leaves are narrow and grayish-green, adapted to reduce water loss in arid conditions.

Aster sonorae plays an important role in supporting local pollinators and wildlife in its native habitat.
