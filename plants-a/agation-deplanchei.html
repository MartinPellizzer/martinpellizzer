Agation deplanchei

Agation deplanchei, commonly known as the Deplanche's agate plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green or blue-green in color. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the growing season. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
