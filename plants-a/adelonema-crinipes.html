Adelonema crinipes

Adelonema crinipes, commonly known as the silky tree or silky lily, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant has a slender, woody stem and produces clusters of small, fragrant blooms. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and have a soft, velvety texture.

Adelonema crinipes is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and is relatively easy to care for in warm climates.
