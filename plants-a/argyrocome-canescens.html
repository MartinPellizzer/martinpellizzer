Argyrocome canescens

Argyrocome canescens, commonly known as the silver-haired dandelion, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its hairy, silver-gray stems and leaves, which give it its common name. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the spring and early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and rocky areas.

Argyrocome canescens is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional medicinal uses.
