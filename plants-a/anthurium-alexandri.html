Anthurium alexandri

Anthurium alexandri, commonly known as the Alexander's anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Colombia.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a metallic sheen. The plant produces vibrant red spadices, which are the modified leaves that enclose the actual flowers. Anthurium alexandri thrives in humid, warm environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.

Its unique foliage and colorful inflorescences make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
