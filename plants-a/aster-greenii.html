Aster greenii

Aster greenii, commonly known as the green aster, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its bright green foliage and clusters of small, daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and woodland edges. It is a perennial species that thrives in full sun to partial shade.

The green aster plays an important role in supporting pollinators like bees and butterflies.
