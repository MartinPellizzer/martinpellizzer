Anemone taraoi

Anemone taraoi, commonly known as the Taroan anemone, is a herbaceous perennial native to the mountainous regions of Japan.

It features showy, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring, typically from March to April. The plant has dark green, palmately lobed leaves that are often covered in a fine, silky hair. Its root system consists of thick, fleshy rhizomes that help it survive in its native habitat.

Anemone taraoi is often found in moist, shaded forest floors and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
