Areca vestiaria

Areca vestiaria, commonly known as the velvet areca, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a member of the Arecaceae family, which includes palms, and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnately compound leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that are borne in clusters at the base of the leaves. Areca vestiaria is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its attractive foliage and ability to thrive in tropical and subtropical climates.

It is also valued for its potential medicinal properties in traditional herbal practices.
