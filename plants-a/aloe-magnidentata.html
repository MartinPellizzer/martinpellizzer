Aloe magnidentata

Aloe magnidentata, commonly known as the giant aloe or the Cape aloe, is a striking succulent native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, upright stem and large, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a white powdery coating. The plant produces striking yellow-orange flowers that bloom in clusters at the top of the stem. Aloe magnidentata is adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves to survive droughts.

It is a popular ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and resilience.
