Aloe kniphofioides

Aloe kniphofioides, commonly known as the Cape aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, branching stems with narrow, fleshy leaves that are typically green with red or purple margins. The plant produces vibrant orange or yellow flowers in terminal clusters during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves.

Aloe kniphofioides is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
