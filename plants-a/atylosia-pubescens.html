Atylosia pubescens

Atylosia pubescens, commonly known as the hairy goldenweed, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It produces yellow flowers that bloom in the summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Atylosia pubescens is often found in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to thrive in harsh, semi-desert conditions.
