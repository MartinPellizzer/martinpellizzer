Anisanthus abyssinicus

Anisanthus abyssinicus, commonly known as the Abyssinian sand verbena, is a flowering plant native to Ethiopia.

It is a low-growing annual herb with slender, branching stems and small, pale yellow flowers. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky soils.

Its botanical features make it an important component of the local flora in its native habitat.
