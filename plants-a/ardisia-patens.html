Ardisia patens

Ardisia patens, commonly known as the evergreen beauty or small-leaved flame tree, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a member of the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of small, white flowers. The plant produces bright red berries that are an important food source for various bird species. Ardisia patens thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in warm climates.
