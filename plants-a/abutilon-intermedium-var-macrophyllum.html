Abutilon intermedium var. macrophyllum

Abutilon intermedium var. macrophyllum, commonly known as the Chinese lantern plant, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically velvety in texture. The plant produces showy, papery bracts that resemble lanterns, which are often more prominent than the small, inconspicuous flowers. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
