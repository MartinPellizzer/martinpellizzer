Ajuga grosseserrata

Ajuga grosseserrata, commonly known as greater bluebug.

It is a low-growing perennial herb native to the eastern United States, characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit. The plant features dark green, serrated leaves and produces clusters of small, blue to purple flowers in the spring. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodlands and along stream banks.

Ajuga grosseserrata is valued in landscaping for its ornamental appeal and ability to suppress weeds in shaded areas.
