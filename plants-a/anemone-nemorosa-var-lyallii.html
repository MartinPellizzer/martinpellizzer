Anemone nemorosa var. lyallii

Anemone nemorosa var. lyallii, commonly known as the Lyall's wood anemone, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of New Zealand.

It features delicate, white or pale pink flowers with five to seven petals, typically blooming in early spring. The plant has a short, stout rhizome and basal leaves that are heart-shaped with a hairy texture. It thrives in moist, shaded woodland environments and is often found in forest floors.

Its botanical characteristics make it a distinctive and ecologically important species in its native habitat.
