Alstroemeria atrorubra

Alstroemeria atrorubra, commonly known as the dark red alstroemeria, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the family Alstroemeriaceae and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from deep red to orange. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and is adapted to sandy, well-drained soils. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves, and it produces showy blooms during the spring and summer months.

Alstroemeria atrorubra is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in a variety of garden settings.
