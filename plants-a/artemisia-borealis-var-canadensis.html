Artemisia borealis var. canadensis

Artemisia borealis var. canadensis, commonly known as Canadian tarragon, is a perennial herb native to North America.

It is characterized by its aromatic, gray-green foliage and small, yellowish flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as meadows and rocky slopes. It has a woody stem that becomes more prominent with age, and its leaves are lance-shaped with a distinct, sharp aroma.

This variety is often used in herbal remedies and as a culinary herb due to its strong, distinctive scent.
