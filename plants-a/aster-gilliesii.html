Aster gilliesii

Aster gilliesii, commonly known as the Gillies' aster, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and produces showy blue to purple flowers. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it blooms in the summer months.

Aster gilliesii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting pollinators.
