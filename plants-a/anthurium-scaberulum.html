Anthurium scaberulum

Anthurium scaberulum, commonly known as the rough-leaved anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its dark green, velvety leaves that are covered in a fine, rough texture. The plant produces striking, heart-shaped spathes that can range in color from deep red to orange. It thrives in humid, warm environments with indirect light.

Anthurium scaberulum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in the right conditions.
