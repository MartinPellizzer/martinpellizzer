Anoectochilus vitiensis

Anoectochilus vitiensis, commonly known as the Vitiensis Orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant has thick, fleshy leaves that store moisture, adapting to its humid, shaded forest habitat. It thrives in high humidity and requires indirect light for optimal growth.

Anoectochilus vitiensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in the right conditions.
