Anthurium cerropelonense

Anthurium cerropelonense, commonly known as the Cerro Pelonense anthurium, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often a deep green with a glossy texture. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-shaped inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, typically in shades of red or orange. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Anthurium cerropelonense is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its aesthetic appeal.
