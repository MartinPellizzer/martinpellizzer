Anagallis arvensis f. carnea

Anagallis arvensis f. carnea, commonly known as the red strawberry blossom, is a flowering plant belonging to the Primulaceae family.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in disturbed soils and open habitats. The plant features showy, pink to red flowers with five petals that are often tinged with white, resembling strawberries. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and hairy, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
