Anemonospermos campanulata

Anemonospermos campanulata, commonly known as the bell-shaped anemonospermos, is a flowering plant belonging to the Campanulaceae family.

It is characterized by its bell-shaped, tubular flowers that typically range in color from white to pale blue. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture, and it thrives in moist, well-drained soils. It is often found in woodland areas and along stream banks, where it contributes to the ecological diversity of its habitat.
