Agrostis rigidula var. fukuyamae

Agrostis rigidula var. fukuyamae, commonly known as the Fukuyama bentgrass, is a low-growing perennial grass native to Japan.

It typically forms dense, compact tufts and has narrow, grass-like leaves with a bluish-green color. The plant produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences, and its flowers are wind-pollinated. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded, woodland environments.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is used in specialty lawn and garden settings.
