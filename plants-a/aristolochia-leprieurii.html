Aristolochia leprieurii

Aristolochia leprieurii, commonly known as the Leprieur's Dutchman's pipe, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its unique, pipe-shaped flowers that are often greenish or brownish in color. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and is typically found in moist, shaded environments. It belongs to the Aristolochiaceae family and is known for its complex floral structure.
