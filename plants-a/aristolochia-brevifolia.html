Aristolochia brevifolia

Aristolochia brevifolia, commonly known as the short-leaved Dutchman's pipe, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive, tubular, hooded flowers that are typically purple or greenish in color. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are usually smooth and glossy. It belongs to the Aristolochiaceae family and is known for its aromatic foliage.

However, it contains toxic compounds and should be handled with care.
