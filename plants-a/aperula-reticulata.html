Aperula reticulata

Aperula reticulata, commonly known as the net-veined orchid, is a species of flowering plant in the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pale pink. The plant features a slender, erect stem with lance-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine, reticulate pattern. Native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
