Anthemis prodanii

Anthemis prodanii, commonly known as the Balkan tansy, is a flowering plant native to the Balkan Peninsula.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces yellowish to brownish flower heads composed of numerous small florets. It thrives in rocky and arid environments, often found in mountainous regions.

Its botanical structure supports its role in ecological systems, providing habitat and nectar for pollinators.
