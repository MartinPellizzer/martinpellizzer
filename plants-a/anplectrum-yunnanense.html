Anplectrum yunnanense

Anplectrum yunnanense, commonly known as the Yunnan arrowhead, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its arrow-shaped leaves and distinctive inflorescence. Anplectrum yunnanense is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional medicine.
