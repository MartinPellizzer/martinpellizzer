Aspidium simplicissimum

Aspidium simplicissimum, commonly known as the simple fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its simple, bipinnate fronds that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its rhizomes are thick and creeping, allowing it to spread vegetatively.

Aspidium simplicissimum is valued for its ornamental appearance and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
