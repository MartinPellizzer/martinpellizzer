Adonis × hybrida var. caludiopolitana

Adonis × hybrida var. caludiopolitana, commonly known as the Caucasian Adonis, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Ranunculaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking, early spring blooms of bright yellow flowers, which appear before the leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or mountainous regions, preferring well-drained soils and full sun exposure. Its foliage consists of palmately divided, hairy leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is cultivated in gardens and conservation efforts aim to protect its natural habitats.
