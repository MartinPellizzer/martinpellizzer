Anthurium scherzerianum var. rodigasianum

Anthurium scherzerianum var. rodigasianum, commonly known as the Rodigasianum anthurium, is a tropical evergreen plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are dark green with a velvety texture. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-shaped inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, typically in shades of red or orange. It thrives in humid, warm environments with bright, indirect light.

This variety is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and ability to purify indoor air.
