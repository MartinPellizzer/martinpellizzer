Anil Stricta

Anil stricta is a hardy flowering plant known for its vibrant blooms and resilience in various climates. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardeners. The plant's striking flowers come in shades of red, pink, and white, adding color to any landscape. Its compact growth habit makes it ideal for borders, containers, and rock gardens. With minimal care requirements, Anil stricta is both beautiful and easy to maintain.
