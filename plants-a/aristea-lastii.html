Aristea lastii

Aristea lastii, commonly known as the silver chalice, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its rosette-forming growth habit. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically white or pale blue in color. It is well adapted to arid environments and thrives in full sun.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
