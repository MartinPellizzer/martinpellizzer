Acalypha dictyoneura f. dictyoneura

Acalypha dictyoneura f. dictyoneura, commonly known as the velvetleaf or silkyleaf, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its soft, velvety leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish or reddish in color. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and landscapes for its aesthetic appeal.
