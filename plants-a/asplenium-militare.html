Asplenium militare

Asplenium militare, commonly known as the sword fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to various regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the family Aspleniaceae and is characterized by its slender, sword-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces spores in small, clustered structures called sori, which are located on the underside of the fronds. Asplenium militare thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found growing on rocks or in forest undergrowth.

Its unique morphology and adaptability make it a notable species in both horticultural and ecological contexts.
