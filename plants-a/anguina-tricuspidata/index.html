Anguina Tricuspidata

Anguina tricuspidata is a small, soil-dwelling nematode known for its unique life cycle and ecological role. This microscopic worm is commonly found in agricultural fields and gardens where it feeds on plant roots. Its presence can significantly impact crop health and yield. Despite its tiny size, it plays a crucial part in soil ecosystems. Understanding Anguina tricuspidata is key to managing plant diseases and promoting sustainable farming.
