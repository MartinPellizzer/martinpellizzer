Aeschynomene fluminensis var. tuberculata

Aeschynomene fluminensis var. tuberculata, commonly known as the tuberculate sesban, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It is a legume characterized by its erect or semi-erect growth habit and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in racemes, followed by elongated, cylindrical pods. It has a deep root system that helps in soil stabilization and nitrogen fixation.

This variety is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its adaptability and rapid growth.
