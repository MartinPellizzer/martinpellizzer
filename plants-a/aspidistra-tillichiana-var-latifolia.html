Aspidistra tillichiana var. latifolia

Aspidistra tillichiana var. latifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaf aspidistra, is a species native to eastern Asia, particularly China.

It is a member of the Araceae family, characterized by its arrow-shaped leaves and spadix inflorescence. The plant is known for its dark, glossy foliage and ability to thrive in low-light conditions, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening. It prefers moist, well-drained soil and is relatively drought-tolerant once established.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of care in both indoor and outdoor settings.
