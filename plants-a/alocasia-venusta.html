Alocasia venusta

Alocasia venusta, commonly known as the velvet leaf alocasia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are deep green with a velvety texture. The plant produces striking, spadix-like inflorescences surrounded by a showy purple spathe. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Alocasia venusta is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and air-purifying qualities.
