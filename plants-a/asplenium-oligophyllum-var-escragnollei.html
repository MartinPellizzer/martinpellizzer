Asplenium oligophyllum var. escragnollei

Asplenium oligophyllum var. escragnollei, commonly known as the Escragnolle's spleenwort, is a fern belonging to the family Aspleniaceae.

It is characterized by its small, delicate fronds that are typically less than 10 centimeters in length. The plant exhibits a unique morphology with a creeping rhizome and linear, often dimorphic leaves. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found growing on rocks or in crevices.

This variant is notable for its limited distribution and is considered a rare species in its native habitat.
