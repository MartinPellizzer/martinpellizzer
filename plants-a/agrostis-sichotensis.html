Agrostis sichotensis

Agrostis sichotensis, commonly known as the Siberian grass, is a flowering plant belonging to the Poaceae family.

It is a perennial grass that typically grows in temperate regions, particularly in the northern hemisphere. The plant has slender, tufted shoots and produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green coloration.

Agrostis sichotensis is adapted to cold climates and is often found in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows and roadsides.
