Arisaema aequinoctiale

Arisaema aequinoctiale, commonly known as the equinox arisaema, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, spadix-like inflorescence surrounded by a colorful spathe. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a rough, textured surface and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

The species is also noted for its potential medicinal properties in traditional herbal practices.
