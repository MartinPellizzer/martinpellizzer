Anemone hepatica var. asiatica

Anemone hepatica var. asiatica, commonly known as the Asian liverwort anemone, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia.

It features basal leaves that are typically heart-shaped with a distinctive wavy margin. The plant produces delicate, showy flowers in shades of white and yellow, often appearing in early spring. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Its foliage and flowers contribute to its ornamental value in gardens and natural habitats.
