Astragalus sanbilingensis

Astragalus sanbilingensis, commonly known as the Sanbiling milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its roots are known to have medicinal properties, often used in traditional Chinese medicine.

Astragalus sanbilingensis plays a role in soil stabilization and supports local biodiversity in its native habitat.
