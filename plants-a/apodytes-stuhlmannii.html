Apodytes stuhlmannii

Apodytes stuhlmannii, commonly known as the silver flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking silver-white to pale yellow flowers that appear in late winter or early spring. The plant has pinnate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a velvety texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to coastal and sandy environments.

Apodytes stuhlmannii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens for its unique foliage and flowering display.
