Aubrieta deltoidea var. degeniana

Aubrieta deltoidea var. degeniana, commonly known as the Dalmatian aubrieta, is a low-growing, flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features dense clusters of small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has a mat-forming habit with silver-green, ovate leaves that are covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its compact size and long blooming period make it an attractive addition to alpine gardens.
