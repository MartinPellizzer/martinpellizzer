Avenastrum aetolicum

Avenastrum aetolicum, commonly known as the Aeolian grass, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in rocky or coastal areas, thriving in well-drained soils. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue.

Avenastrum aetolicum is often used in ornamental gardening due to its hardy nature and aesthetic appeal.
