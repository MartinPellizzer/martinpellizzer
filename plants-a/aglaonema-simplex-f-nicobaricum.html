Aglaonema simplex f. nicobaricum

Aglaonema simplex f. nicobaricum, commonly known as the Nicobar arrowroot or Nicobar arum, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated foliage with white and green patterns on its broad, heart-shaped leaves. The plant belongs to the Araceae family, which includes many ornamental and sometimes toxic plants. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant.

Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense clumps in suitable conditions.
