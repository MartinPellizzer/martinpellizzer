Acalypha wilkesiana f. illustris

Acalypha wilkesiana f. illustris, commonly known as the velvetleaf or red-leaf hibiscus, is a cultivar of the Acalypha genus native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety foliage that ranges from deep red to bronze in color. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite and have a unique, textured surface that gives it a distinctive appearance.

It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are often overshadowed by its ornamental leaves.
