Anemone hortensis subsp. heldreichii

Anemone hortensis subsp.

heldreichii, commonly known as the Heldreich's anemone, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Balkan Peninsula. It features showy, cup-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in early spring.

The plant has basal leaves that are heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stem is usually unbranched and somewhat hairy. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade, making it a suitable choice for woodland gardens.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable addition to native plant collections and ecological restoration projects.
