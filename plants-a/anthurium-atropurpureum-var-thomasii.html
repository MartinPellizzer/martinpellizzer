Anthurium atropurpureum var. thomasii

Anthurium atropurpureum var. thomasii, commonly known as the Thomasii Anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically deep green with reddish veins. The plant produces vibrant red spathes that enclose its spadix, which is often covered in tiny, white to yellowish flowers. It thrives in humid, warm environments with bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil.

This variety is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
