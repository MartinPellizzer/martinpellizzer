Asteriscus schultzii

Asteriscus schultzii, commonly known as the Schultze's asteriscus, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically features small, yellow to orange flowers arranged in a cluster, giving it a star-like appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Asteriscus schultzii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate blooms.
