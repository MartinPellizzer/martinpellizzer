Anthurium limonense

Anthurium limonense, commonly known as the yellow anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, waxy, yellow spathe that surrounds a spadix, which is typically green or reddish in color. The plant produces striking, heart-shaped leaves that are dark green and often have a glossy texture. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.

Anthurium limonense is popular as a decorative plant due to its unique and eye-catching floral display.
