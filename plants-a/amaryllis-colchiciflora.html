Amaryllis colchiciflora

Amaryllis colchiciflora, commonly known as the Colchiciflora Amaryllis, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, strap-like leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length, forming a dense rosette. The plant produces large, showy flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, typically blooming in summer. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long-lasting blooms and ability to thrive in a variety of climates.
