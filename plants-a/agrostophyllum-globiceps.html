Agrostophyllum globiceps

Agrostophyllum globiceps, commonly known as the globe-headed agrostophyllum, is a flowering plant native to the Cape region of South Africa.

It is a small, evergreen shrub that typically grows in coastal fynbos vegetation. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense, spherical inflorescences that give it its common name. Its flowers are usually white or pale yellow, and they bloom in spring.

Agrostophyllum globiceps is adapted to sandy, well-drained soils and is often found in sunny, coastal environments.
