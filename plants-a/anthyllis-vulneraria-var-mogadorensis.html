Anthyllis vulneraria var. mogadorensis

Anthyllis vulneraria var. mogadorensis, commonly known as the Mogador thornvetch, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with woody stems and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers arranged in dense clusters. It thrives in rocky and sandy soils, often found in coastal and arid environments.

Its botanical features make it well adapted to the harsh conditions of its native habitat.
