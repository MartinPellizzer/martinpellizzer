Artabotrys oliviformis

Artabotrys oliviformis, commonly known as the olive-shaped starfruit, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compound leaves, which are opposite and often palmately lobed. The plant produces fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that are clustered in panicles, followed by small, round fruits resembling starfruits. The fruit turns from green to yellow or orange when mature and has a mild, sweet flavor.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
