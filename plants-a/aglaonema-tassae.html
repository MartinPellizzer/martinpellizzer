Aglaonema tassae

Aglaonema tassae, commonly known as the Tassae arrowroot or Tassae plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features striking, variegated foliage with dark green leaves marked by creamy white and pinkish veins. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and ability to thrive in low-light conditions. Its leaves are typically elliptical or ovate in shape, with a leathery texture.

Aglaonema tassae is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and air-purifying properties.
