Arisaema barnesii subsp. barnesii

Arisaema barnesii subsp.

barnesii, commonly known as the Barnes' arrowhead, is a member of the Araceae family. It is native to the mountainous regions of southern China, particularly in Yunnan Province.

The plant is characterized by its distinctive arrow-shaped leaves and a spadix surrounded by a spathe. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The subspecies is noted for its ornamental value and is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens.
