Alocasia cordifolia

Alocasia cordifolia, commonly known as the heart-leaf alocasia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 inches in length. The plant belongs to the Araceae family, which includes other ornamental plants like peace lilies and calla lilies. Alocasia cordifolia thrives in humid, shady environments and requires well-draining soil.

Its striking foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical landscapes.
