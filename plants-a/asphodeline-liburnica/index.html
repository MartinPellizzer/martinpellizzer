Asphodeline Liburnica

Asphodeline liburnica is a striking plant native to the Mediterranean region, known for its tall, narrow leaves and vibrant yellow flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes. This perennial plant is valued not only for its ornamental appeal but also for its historical and cultural significance. Its resilience and adaptability have made it a favored species among gardeners and botanists alike. Asphodeline liburnica continues to captivate with its beauty and ecological importance in its native habitats.
