Aloe chabaudii var. chabaudii

Aloe chabaudii var. chabaudii, commonly known as the Chabaud's aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with white or yellowish markings. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing yellow to orange flowers. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil.

Its striking appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
