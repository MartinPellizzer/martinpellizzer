Avonia mallei

Avonia mallei, commonly known as the velvetleaf or velvetleaf shrub, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Malvaceae, which includes hibiscus and cotton plants. The plant is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, velvety hairs. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, with five petals and a prominent central stamen.

Avonia mallei is often used in ornamental gardening due to its striking foliage and showy blooms.
