Anetanthus gracilis subsp. munchiquensis

Anetanthus gracilis subsp.

munchiquensis, commonly known as the Munchique slender bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the Andean region of Colombia. It is a herbaceous perennial with slender, upright stems and small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale pink.

The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous habitats. Its leaves are lanceolate and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

This subspecies is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphology and restricted distribution.
