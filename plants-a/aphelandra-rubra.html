Aphelandra rubra

Aphelandra rubra, commonly known as the red-veined begonia, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves with deep red veins against a dark green background. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. Aphelandra rubra thrives in humid, warm environments and requires bright, indirect light.

It is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its ornamental foliage.
