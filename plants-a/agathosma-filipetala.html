Agathosma filipetala

Agathosma filipetala, commonly known as the Cape aster, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers with yellow centers and white to pink petals. The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in fynbos vegetation. It has aromatic leaves that are used in traditional medicine and as a source of essential oils.

Agathosma filipetala is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitat.
