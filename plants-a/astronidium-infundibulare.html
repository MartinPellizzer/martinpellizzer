Astronidium infundibulare

Astronidium infundibulare, commonly known as the hooded starling tree, is a small evergreen tree native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves that are dark green on top and lighter beneath. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, arranged in clusters. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds.

This species is notable for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
