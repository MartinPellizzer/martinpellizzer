Asplenium × diasii

Asplenium × diasii, commonly known as the hybrid shield fern, is a sterile hybrid species resulting from the cross between Asplenium trichomanes and Asplenium bulbiferum.

It is characterized by its dense, overlapping fronds that form a compact rosette, typically with a dark green to blackish coloration. The plant lacks true roots and relies on its rhizomes for nutrient absorption, which is a common trait among ferns. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in temperate regions.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in horticultural settings.
