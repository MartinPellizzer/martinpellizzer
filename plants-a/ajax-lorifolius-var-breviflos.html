Ajax lorifolius var. breviflos

Ajax lorifolius var. breviflos, commonly known as the narrow-leaved ajax, is a variant of the ajax plant characterized by its slender, lance-shaped leaves.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, where it thrives in warm, humid climates. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in clusters, adding ornamental value to gardens. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight for optimal growth.

Due to its compact size and attractive foliage, it is often cultivated as a decorative plant in tropical landscapes.
