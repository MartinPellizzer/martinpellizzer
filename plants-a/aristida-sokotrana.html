Aristida sokotrana

Aristida sokotrana, commonly known as the Sokotra grass, is a unique plant native to the island of Socotra.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its slender, wiry stems and small, spikelet-like inflorescences. The plant is adapted to the arid and rocky environment of Socotra, exhibiting a high degree of drought tolerance. Its leaves are often covered in a fine layer of hairs, which helps reduce water loss.

This species is of particular interest to botanists due to its endemic status and distinctive morphology.
