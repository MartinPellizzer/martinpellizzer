Angraecum palmiforme

Angraecum palmiforme, commonly known as the palmate angraecum, is a species of orchid native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its broad, palm-like leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces striking white or pale green flowers with long, slender petals. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Angraecum palmiforme is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique appearance.
