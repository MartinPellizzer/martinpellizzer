Astragalus paposanus

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly habitats, often in arid or semi-arid regions. Astragalus paposanus is also valued for its potential medicinal properties in traditional herbal practices.
