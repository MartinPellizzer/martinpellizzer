Amaryllis cernua

Amaryllis cernua, commonly known as the nodding amaryllis, is a bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features striking, downward-facing flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant produces strap-like leaves that are broad and grassy in appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Amaryllis cernua is often cultivated for its ornamental value and can be grown in gardens or as a potted plant.
