Ardisia humilis var. arborescens

Ardisia humilis var. arborescens, commonly known as the shrub ardisia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a low-growing evergreen shrub that typically reaches a height of about 1 meter. The plant features glossy, dark green leaves and produces small, white flowers that are clustered in umbels. Following flowering, it develops bright red berries that are attractive to birds.

It is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in shaded conditions.
