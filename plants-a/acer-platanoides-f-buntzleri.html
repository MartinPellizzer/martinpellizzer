Acer platanoides f. buntzleri

Acer platanoides f. buntzleri, commonly known as the Buntzler Norway maple, is a cultivar of the Norway maple known for its distinctive foliage.

It features large, palmate leaves with a broad, rounded shape and a serrated margin. The leaves are typically a vibrant green in summer, turning yellow in the fall. This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping.

It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it adaptable to a range of garden conditions.
