Aspilia chariensis

Aspilia chariensis, commonly known as the African crownbeard, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

Its striking yellow flower heads are surrounded by white ray florets, creating a distinctive crown-like appearance.

Aspilia chariensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its visual appeal.
