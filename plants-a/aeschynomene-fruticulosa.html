Aeschynomene fruticulosa

Aeschynomene fruticulosa, commonly known as the slender aeshynomene, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, branching stems and small, opposite leaves that are usually ovate to elliptic in shape. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pale green to yellowish in color, followed by small, round pods containing several seeds. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.

Aeschynomene fruticulosa is sometimes used in traditional medicine and as a forage plant for livestock.
