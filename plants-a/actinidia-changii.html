Actinidia changii

Actinidia changii, commonly known as Chinese gooseberry, is a deciduous vine native to China.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant produces small, edible berries that are rich in vitamin C and have a tart flavor. It thrives in temperate climates and requires well-drained soil and partial shade.

Actinidia changii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and nutritional benefits.
