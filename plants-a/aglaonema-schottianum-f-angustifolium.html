Aglaonema schottianum f. angustifolium

Aglaonema schottianum f. angustifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaved Chinese evergreen, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features striking, variegated leaves with a mix of green, white, and pink hues, which are often adorned with dark veins. The plant has glossy, elongated foliage that can grow up to several feet in height, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation. It thrives in low-light conditions and requires moderate humidity and well-draining soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a favored ornamental plant in both residential and commercial settings.
