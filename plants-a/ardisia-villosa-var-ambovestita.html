Ardisia villosa var. ambovestita

Ardisia villosa var. ambovestita, commonly known as the hairy ardisia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are often covered in fine, white hairs. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its fruit is a bright red, fleshy berry that contains several seeds.

This species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various soil conditions.
