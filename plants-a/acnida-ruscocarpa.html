Acnida ruscocarpa

Acnida ruscocarpa, commonly known as the red-flowered acnida, is a tropical shrub native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are usually ovate to elliptic in shape. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink and red, depending on the species. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, dry seeds.

Acnida ruscocarpa is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest undergrowth.
