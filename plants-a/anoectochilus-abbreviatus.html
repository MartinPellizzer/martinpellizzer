Anoectochilus abbreviatus

Anoectochilus abbreviatus, commonly known as the short-stemmed helmet orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, compact growth habit and is often found in shaded, humid forest environments. The plant has fleshy, dark green leaves that are often covered in a waxy cuticle, which helps it retain moisture. Its flowers are typically pale green or white, with a distinctive helmet-shaped lip.

Anoectochilus abbreviatus is a popular species among orchid enthusiasts due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
