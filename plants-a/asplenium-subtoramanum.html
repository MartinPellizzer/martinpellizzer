Asplenium subtoramanum

Asplenium subtoramanum, commonly known as the Toraman spleenwort, is a small, evergreen fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Aspleniaceae and is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped leaves that are typically less than 10 centimeters in length. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often growing on rocks or tree trunks. Its fronds are usually dark green and have a slightly serrated edge, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Asplenium subtoramanum is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
