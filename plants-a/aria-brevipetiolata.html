Aria brevipetiolata

Aria brevipetiolata, commonly known as the short-stemmed arrowleaf, is a tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive arrow-shaped leaves, which are usually bright green and have a smooth texture. The plant typically grows as an understory species in humid, shaded environments. It produces small, fragrant flowers that are often white or pale yellow.

Aria brevipetiolata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
