Aechmea bauxilumii

Aechmea bauxilumii, commonly known as the Bauxilumii Bromeliad, is a striking epiphytic plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features a rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves that form a central cup, which collects water and nutrients from the environment. The plant produces vibrant, long-lasting inflorescences with colorful bracts that can range from red to orange. Its foliage is often adorned with silver or white markings, adding to its ornamental appeal.

Aechmea bauxilumii thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
