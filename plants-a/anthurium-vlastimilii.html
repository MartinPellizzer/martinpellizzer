Anthurium vlastimilii

Anthurium vlastimilii, commonly known as the Vlastimil's arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are often a vibrant green with a glossy texture. The plant produces spadices, which are specialized inflorescences that resemble a cluster of small, spadix-like flowers. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.

Anthurium vlastimilii is popular as a decorative plant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
