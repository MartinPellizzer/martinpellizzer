Aralia lessonii

Aralia lessonii, commonly known as the Japanese aralia, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features compound, pinnately arranged leaves that are often ornamental due to their attractive foliage. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers in umbels, followed by berries that mature to a dark purple color. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.
