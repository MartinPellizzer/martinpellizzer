Arctotis odorata

Arctotis odorata, commonly known as the sweet violet, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its fragrant, violet-colored flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and thrives in well-drained soil with full sun exposure. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and pleasant scent.
