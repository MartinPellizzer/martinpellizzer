Aspalathus scholliana

Aspalathus scholliana, commonly known as Scholliana Bushveld Thunbergia, is a species of legume native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, trifoliate leaves and clusters of small, pale yellow to white flowers. The plant typically grows in open grasslands and rocky areas, preferring well-drained soils. It is a low-growing shrub that can reach up to 1 meter in height.

Aspalathus scholliana is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to thrive in semi-arid conditions.
