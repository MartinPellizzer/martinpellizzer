Amaranthus hybridus var. chlorostachys

Amaranthus hybridus var. chlorostachys, commonly known as purple amaranth, is a striking ornamental plant characterized by its vibrant purple foliage and feather-like, silver-green bracts.

It is a member of the Amaranthaceae family and is native to tropical and subtropical regions. The plant thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, exhibiting rapid growth during the warm seasons. Its leaves are edible and have been used in traditional cuisines, while its ornamental qualities make it popular in gardens and landscapes.

The species is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators and its resilience to various environmental conditions.
