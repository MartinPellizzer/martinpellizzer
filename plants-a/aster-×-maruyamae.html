Aster × maruyamae

Aster × maruyamae, commonly known as the Maruyama aster, is a hybrid perennial plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that bloom in shades of pink, purple, and white. The plant typically grows to a height of 30–60 cm and prefers well-drained soil in full sun to partial shade. Its foliage consists of narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, adding to its ornamental appeal.

This plant is valued in gardens for its vibrant blooms and adaptability to various growing conditions.
