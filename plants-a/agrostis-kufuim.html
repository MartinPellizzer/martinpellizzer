Agrostis kufuim

Agrostis kufuim, commonly known as the Kufuim grass, is a perennial grass species native to certain regions of Africa.

It typically grows in open grasslands and along the edges of forests, preferring well-drained soils. The plant has slender, erect stems that can reach heights of up to 60 centimeters. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green coloration.

Agrostis kufuim is often used for forage and in erosion control due to its hardy nature.
