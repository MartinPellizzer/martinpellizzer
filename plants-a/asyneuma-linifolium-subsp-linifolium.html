Asyneuma linifolium subsp. linifolium

Asyneuma linifolium subsp.

linifolium, commonly known as the linear-leaved asyneuma, is a herbaceous perennial native to South Africa. It features slender, upright stems with narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in dense clusters, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance, it is occasionally cultivated in horticultural settings.
