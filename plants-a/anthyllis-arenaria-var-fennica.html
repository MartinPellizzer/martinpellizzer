Anthyllis arenaria var. fennica

Anthyllis arenaria var. fennica, commonly known as the Fennoscandian sand legume, is a perennial herb native to northern Europe.

It thrives in sandy and calcareous soils, often found in coastal and alpine regions. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are typically pink or violet in color. Its leaves are trifoliate, consisting of three oval-shaped leaflets.

This variety is adapted to harsh environments, making it a resilient and ecologically significant species in its habitat.
