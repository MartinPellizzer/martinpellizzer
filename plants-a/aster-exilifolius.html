Aster exilifolius

Aster exilifolius, commonly known as the slender-leaved aster, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the United States.

It typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of small, blue to purple flowers in the fall. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by composite flower heads and a wide range of ecological adaptations.

Aster exilifolius plays a role in supporting pollinators and maintaining biodiversity in its native ecosystems.
