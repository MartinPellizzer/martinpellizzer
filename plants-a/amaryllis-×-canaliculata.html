Amaryllis × canaliculata

Amaryllis × canaliculata, commonly known as the hybrid amaryllis, is a striking bulbous plant native to South America.

It produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, often with contrasting markings. The plant features broad, strap-like leaves that are typically dark green and arranged in a fan shape. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

This hybrid is popular for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation in both indoor and outdoor settings.
