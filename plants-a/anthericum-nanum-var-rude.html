Anthericum nanum var. rude

Anthericum nanum var. rude, commonly known as the small anthericum or dwarf lily of the valley, is a low-growing perennial plant native to parts of Europe.

It features slender, grass-like leaves and produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale green. The plant thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.

Its botanical characteristics make it a unique addition to the Araceae family.
