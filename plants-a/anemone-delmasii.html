Anemone delmasii

Anemone delmasii, commonly known as the Delmas' anemone, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features showy, pink to purple flowers that bloom in early spring, typically from March to May. The plant has basal leaves that are palmately lobed, with a hairy texture, and upright, branching stems. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its early blooming habit and delicate floral display.
