Agrostis conferta var. austropatagonica

Agrostis conferta var. austropatagonica, commonly known as the Patagonian bentgrass, is a perennial grass species native to South America.

It is characterized by its fine, tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. This plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in temperate regions. It has small, spike-like inflorescences that produce tiny seeds.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping and turfgrass applications.
