Acetosa Ellenbeckii

Acetosa ellenbeckii is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Andes. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is known for its small, pale blue flowers that bloom in the early spring. This species is closely related to other members of the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage. Due to its limited habitat and vulnerability to environmental changes, it is considered endangered. Conservation efforts are crucial to ensure its survival in the wild.
