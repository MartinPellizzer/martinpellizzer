Alchemilla viguieri

Alchemilla viguieri, commonly known as the golden groundsel, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit. The plant features basal leaves that are broadly ovate to round, with a soft, velvety texture. Its flowers are small, yellow, and borne in clusters, typically appearing in early summer.

Alchemilla viguieri thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or rocky habitats.
