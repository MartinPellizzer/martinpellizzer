Anthurium fraseri

Anthurium fraseri, commonly known as the Fraser's arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically bright green with a metallic sheen. The plant produces spadices, which are specialized inflorescences that are often brightly colored, typically in shades of red or orange. Anthurium fraseri thrives in humid, warm environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.

It is a popular ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
