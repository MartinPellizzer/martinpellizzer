Aristolochia vitiensis

Aristolochia vitiensis, commonly known as the Vitiensis Dutchman's pipe, is a climbing vine native to tropical regions of the Pacific.

It is characterized by its unique, tubular flowers that are often purple or greenish in color. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces fruit that resembles a small, elongated capsule. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

The species is notable for its complex floral structure and its role in certain ecological interactions.
