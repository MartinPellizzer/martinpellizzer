Arenaria elongata

Arenaria elongata, commonly known as the sandspurry, is a low-growing perennial plant native to temperate regions of Europe and Asia.

It typically features wiry stems and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in disturbed or open habitats. Its leaves are narrow and linear, and the flowers are often borne on elongated pedicels.

Arenaria elongata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its hardy nature and delicate blooms.
