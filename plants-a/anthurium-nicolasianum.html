Anthurium nicolasianum

Anthurium nicolasianum, commonly known as the Nicolasianum arum, is a tropical plant native to the Caribbean region.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its unique inflorescence consisting of a spadix and a spathe. The plant features large, heart-shaped leaves with a vibrant green color and a glossy texture. Its flowers are typically small and inconspicuous, with the showy spathe serving as an attractant for pollinators.

Anthurium nicolasianum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in shaded gardens and indoor spaces.
