Astrophytum myriostigma subsp. myriostigma

Astrophytum myriostigma subsp.

myriostigma, commonly known as the "Star Cactus," is a member of the Cactaceae family native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico. It is a small, globular cactus with a ribbed stem that often develops a star-shaped pattern of tubercles.

The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months, which are typically borne at the top of the stem. It is adapted to arid environments and requires well-draining soil and minimal water.

Its unique appearance and slow growth make it a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts.
