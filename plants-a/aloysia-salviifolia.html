Aloysia salviifolia

Aloysia salviifolia, commonly known as the silverleaf sage, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its silver-gray, velvety leaves and spikes of fragrant, tubular flowers that range in color from white to purple. The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and aromatic properties. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for Mediterranean climates.

Aloysia salviifolia is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
