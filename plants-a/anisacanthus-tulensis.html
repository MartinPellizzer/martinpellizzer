Anisacanthus tulensis

Anisacanthus tulensis, commonly known as the turk's cap lily, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its showy, orange-red bracts that surround its small, yellow flowers. The plant is a shrub with rough, hairy stems and dark green, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for xeriscaping.

Its striking appearance and drought tolerance make it a valuable addition to native gardens.
