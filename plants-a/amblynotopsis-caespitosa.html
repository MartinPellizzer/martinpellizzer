Amblynotopsis caespitosa

Amblynotopsis caespitosa, commonly known as the tufted moss gentian, is a flowering plant belonging to the Gentianaceae family.

It is a small, herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and rocky stream banks. The plant features delicate, blue to purple flowers that bloom in late summer, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies.
