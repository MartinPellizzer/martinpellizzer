Andersonia hammersleyana

Andersonia hammersleyana, commonly known as the Hammersley's bellflower, is a flowering plant native to Western Australia.

It is a member of the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in rocky soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments. It produces small, oval-shaped leaves and has a compact, bushy growth habit.
