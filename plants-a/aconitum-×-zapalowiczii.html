Aconitum × zapalowiczii

Aconitum × zapalowiczii, commonly known as the hybrid aconite, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Ranunculaceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, sparsely branched stems that can reach heights of up to 1.5 meters. The plant produces showy blue to purple flowers in late summer, arranged in dense, spikelike clusters. Its leaves are palmately compound, with deeply lobed segments that are often hairy.

Aconitum × zapalowiczii is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
