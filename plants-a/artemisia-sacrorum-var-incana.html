Artemisia sacrorum var. incana

Artemisia sacrorum var. incana, commonly known as sacred thyme or holy thyme, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features aromatic, silvery-gray foliage with lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flower heads that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Its essential oils have been used historically for their medicinal and aromatic properties.
