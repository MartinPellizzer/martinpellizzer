Arisaema sukotaiense

Arisaema sukotaiense, commonly known as the Sukotai arrowhead, is a flowering plant native to Thailand.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its unique inflorescence consisting of a spadix surrounded by a spathe. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories. Its leaves are arrowhead-shaped, with a smooth texture and a vibrant green color.

The flowers are usually white or pale yellow, and the plant is noted for its ornamental value and traditional use in local medicine.
