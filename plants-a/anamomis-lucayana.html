Anamomis lucayana

Anamomis lucayana, commonly known as the Lucayan anemone, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean islands.

It is characterized by its showy, pink or purple flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and sandy soils, thriving in warm, humid climates. Its foliage consists of broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, white powder.

Anamomis lucayana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
