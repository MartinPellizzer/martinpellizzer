Asplenium protensum

Asplenium protensum, commonly known as the narrow fern, is a species of evergreen fern belonging to the family Aspleniaceae.

It is characterized by its narrow, elongated fronds that are typically divided into small, lance-shaped segments. This plant is native to tropical and subtropical regions, including parts of Africa, Asia, and the Pacific Islands. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories or on rocky outcrops.

The fronds are usually evergreen, with a leathery texture and a distinctive, intricate pattern of veins.
