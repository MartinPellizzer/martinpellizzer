Alopecurus pratensis var. altissimus

Alopecurus pratensis var. altissimus, commonly known as tall meadow grass, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems that can reach heights of up to 1.5 meters. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences with numerous tiny greenish-white flowers. It thrives in moist, nutrient-rich soils and is often found in meadows, pastures, and wetlands.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting pollinators.
