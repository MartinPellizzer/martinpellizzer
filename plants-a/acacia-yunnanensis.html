Acacia yunnanensis

Acacia yunnanensis, commonly known as Yunnan acacia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its bipinnate leaves and yellow flowers. The plant is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it resilient to drought conditions. It plays a role in preventing soil erosion and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.

Its ecological importance makes it a valuable species in its native habitat.
