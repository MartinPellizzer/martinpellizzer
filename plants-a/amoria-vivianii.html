Amoria vivianii

Amoria vivianii, commonly known as the Vivanii's orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically feature vibrant colors such as red, orange, and yellow. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its roots are fibrous and often grow in dense clusters, adapting to its natural habitat in humid, shaded environments.

Amoria vivianii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in greenhouses or warm climates.
