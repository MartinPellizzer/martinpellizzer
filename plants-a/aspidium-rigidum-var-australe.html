Aspidium rigidum var. australe

Aspidium rigidum var. australe, commonly known as the Australian holly fern, is a species of fern native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its delicate, feather-like fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its spores are produced on the underside of the fronds, and it reproduces through sexual reproduction.
