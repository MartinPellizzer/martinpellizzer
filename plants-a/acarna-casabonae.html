Acarna casabonae

Acarna casabonae, commonly known as the Casablanca lily, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and violet. The plant has strap-like, dark green leaves that are glossy and lance-shaped. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Acarna casabonae is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
