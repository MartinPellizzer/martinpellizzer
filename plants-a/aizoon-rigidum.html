Aizoon rigidum

Aizoon rigidum, commonly known as the rigid aizoon, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features upright, branching stems that are typically covered in stiff, rigid hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in early spring, followed by narrow, linear leaves. Aizoon rigidum is often found in rocky or sandy soils, thriving in well-drained environments.

Its botanical characteristics make it a distinctive and resilient species within its native habitat.
