Anthurium williamsii

Anthurium williamsii, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are dark green with a velvety texture. The plant produces showy, spadix-shaped inflorescences surrounded by a vibrant, often red or orange spathe. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium williamsii is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
