Alsophila gammiei

Alsophila gammiei, commonly known as the Gammie's tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

The plant belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is known for its ability to thrive in humid, shaded environments. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine, while its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping.
