Astrocaryum princeps var. vitellinum

Astrocaryum princeps var. vitellinum, commonly known as the yellow palm or golden palm, is a species of palm native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate leaves that can reach several meters in length. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by elongated, fibrous fruits that contain a single seed. Its leaves are often used in traditional crafts and for thatching.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in tropical ecosystems.
