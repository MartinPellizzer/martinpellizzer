Agauria nummulariifolia

Agauria nummulariifolia, commonly known as the silver dollar plant, is a succulent species native to South Africa.

It features small, round, silver-green leaves that are densely packed along its short, fleshy stems. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and ability to store water in its thick, fleshy leaves. It produces yellow flowers in summer, adding a subtle ornamental touch to its appearance.

Agauria nummulariifolia thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid or semi-arid environments.
