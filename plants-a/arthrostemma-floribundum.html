Arthrostemma floribundum

Arthrostemma floribundum, commonly known as the cluster arrowhead, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Americas.

Arthrostemma floribundum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
