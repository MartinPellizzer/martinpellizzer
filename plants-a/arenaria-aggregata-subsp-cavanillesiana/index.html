Arenaria Aggregata Subsp. Cavanillesiana

Arenaria aggregata subsp. cavanillesiana is a low-growing flowering plant native to the rocky slopes of southern Europe. It is characterized by its dense clusters of small white flowers that bloom in late spring. This subspecies thrives in well-drained soils and full sun exposure. Its compact growth habit makes it ideal for rock gardens and alpine landscapes. Due to its hardiness and beauty, it is a popular choice among gardeners and botanists alike.
