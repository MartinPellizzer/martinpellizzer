Aralia obtusiloba

Aralia obtusiloba, commonly known as the American aralia, is a deciduous shrub native to the eastern United States.

It features compound leaves that are typically lobed and have a rough, hairy texture. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers in umbel-like clusters, which are followed by dark purple berries. Its roots are fibrous and can be used in traditional herbal medicine.

Aralia obtusiloba is often found in moist woodlands and thrives in partial shade.
