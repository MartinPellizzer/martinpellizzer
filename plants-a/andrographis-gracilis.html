Andrographis gracilis

Andrographis gracilis, commonly known as slender andrographis, is a herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its slender, upright stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow-green in color. It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and along stream banks.

Andrographis gracilis is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal medicine.
