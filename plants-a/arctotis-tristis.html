Arctotis tristis

Arctotis tristis, commonly known as the silverleaf daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and bright yellow flowers with white centers. The plant typically grows in dry, sandy soils and is well adapted to arid environments. It produces small, daisy-like blooms that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Arctotis tristis is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its aesthetic appeal and drought tolerance.
