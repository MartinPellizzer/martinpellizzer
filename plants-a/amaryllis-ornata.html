Amaryllis ornata

Amaryllis ornata, commonly known as the golden amaryllis, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features large, showy flowers in shades of yellow to orange, often with red or purple streaks. The plant produces strap-like leaves that are broad and pointed, growing from a bulbous base. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Amaryllis ornata is popular for its vibrant blooms and is often cultivated as a decorative plant.
