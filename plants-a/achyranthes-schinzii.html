Achyranthes schinzii

Achyranthes schinzii, commonly known as Schinz's smartweed, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa and the Middle East.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its erect, branched stems and alternate leaves. The plant produces small, greenish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in moist, disturbed soils and is often found in wetlands or along riverbanks.

Achyranthes schinzii is known for its ability to tolerate both wet and dry conditions, making it a versatile species in its native habitats.
