Alchemilla hayirliogluorum

Alchemilla hayirliogluorum, commonly known as the Turkish stonecrop, is a perennial plant native to Turkey.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit. The plant features silver-gray leaves with a woolly texture and produces small, yellowish-green flowers in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or limestone-rich areas.

Alchemilla hayirliogluorum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to tolerate harsh environmental conditions.
