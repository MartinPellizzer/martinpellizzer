Athyrium brevifrons var. angustifrons

Athyrium brevifrons var. angustifrons, commonly known as the narrow-leaf Japanese shield fern, is a variant of the Japanese shield fern native to eastern Asia.

It is a perennial fern characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and dark green, glossy fronds. The plant features triangular, shield-shaped pinnae that are narrow and closely spaced, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and is cultivated in gardens for its elegant foliage.
