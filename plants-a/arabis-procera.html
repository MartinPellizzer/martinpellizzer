Arabis procera

Arabis procera, commonly known as the tall honesty, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of white or pink flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds. Arabis procera thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its delicate appearance and ease of cultivation contribute to its popularity among gardeners.
