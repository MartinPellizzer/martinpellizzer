Aloe longiaristata

Aloe longiaristata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, cylindrical stems covered in dense, blue-green leaves that are narrow and pointed. The leaves are lined with small, white tubercles and produce a gel-like substance that is used in traditional medicine. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

This species is valued for its ornamental beauty and medicinal properties.
