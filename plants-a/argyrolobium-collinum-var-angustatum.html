Argyrolobium collinum var. angustatum

Argyrolobium collinum var. angustatum, commonly known as the narrowleaf silverlobed crotalaria, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features slender, branching stems with opposite, narrow, lance-shaped leaves that have a silvery underside. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in racemes, which bloom from late winter to early spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open grasslands and rocky areas.

Its unique foliage and flowering pattern make it a distinctive addition to botanical collections.
