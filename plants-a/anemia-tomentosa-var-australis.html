Anemia tomentosa var. australis

Anemia tomentosa var. australis, commonly known as the Australian woolly anemia, is a perennial shrub native to Australia.

It features dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive, soft appearance. The plant has small, white or pale green flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy environments.

Its unique morphology and adaptability make it a notable species in its native habitat.
