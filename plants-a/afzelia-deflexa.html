Afzelia deflexa

Afzelia deflexa, commonly known as the African ironwood, is a tropical tree species native to Africa.

It is characterized by its hard, dense wood, which is highly valued for its strength and durability. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually ovate or elliptic in shape. It produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by woody pods containing flat, lens-shaped seeds.

Afzelia deflexa is often found in dry tropical forests and is known for its slow growth and long lifespan.
