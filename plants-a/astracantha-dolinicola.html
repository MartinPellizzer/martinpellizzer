Astracantha dolinicola

Astracantha dolinicola, commonly known as the Madagascar starburst, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its showy, composite flower heads. The plant features a short, woody stem with narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, arranged in a cluster that resembles a star, hence its common name.

Astracantha dolinicola is adapted to the dry, seasonal climate of its native habitat and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
