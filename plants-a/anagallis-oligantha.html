Anagallis oligantha

Anagallis oligantha, commonly known as the slender pink, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a small, herbaceous perennial that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant produces delicate, five-petaled flowers in shades of pink or white, which bloom from late spring to early autumn. It has a compact growth habit with hairy stems and leaves that are often covered in fine pubescence.

Anagallis oligantha is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and easy care requirements.
