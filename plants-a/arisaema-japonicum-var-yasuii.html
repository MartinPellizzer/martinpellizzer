Arisaema japonicum var. yasuii

Arisaema japonicum var. yasuii, commonly known as the Yasui's Japanese Cuckoo Plant, is a herbaceous perennial native to Japan.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its unique inflorescence consisting of a spadix surrounded by a spathe. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors and rocky areas. Its leaves are arrow-shaped and have a glossy, dark green appearance.

The flowering period occurs in early summer, producing a striking white spadix with a yellowish base.
