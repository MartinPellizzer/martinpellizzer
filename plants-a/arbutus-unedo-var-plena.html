Arbutus unedo var. plena

Arbutus unedo var. plena, commonly known as the variegated strawberry tree, is a compact evergreen shrub native to the western Mediterranean.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves with white or cream variegation, which add visual interest throughout the year. The plant produces small, white, bell-shaped flowers in late winter or early spring, followed by red, berry-like fruits that mature in the fall. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.

Its unique foliage and seasonal blooms make it a distinctive addition to gardens and natural habitats.
