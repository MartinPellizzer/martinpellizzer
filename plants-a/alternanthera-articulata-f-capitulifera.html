Alternanthera articulata f. capitulifera

Alternanthera articulata f. capitulifera, commonly known as the compact alternanthera, is a compact, bushy perennial plant native to South America.

It features small, lance-shaped leaves that are typically bright red or pink, especially when exposed to full sun. The plant produces dense clusters of tiny, greenish-white flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full sunlight.

This ornamental plant is often used in water gardens and as a ground cover due to its attractive foliage and hardy nature.
