Astilbe thunbergii var. aethusifolia

Astilbe thunbergii var. aethusifolia, commonly known as the silver feather astilbe, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its finely divided, silvery-white to pale green foliage that gives it a distinctive, feathery appearance. The plant produces panicles of small, pale pink to white flowers in late summer, which add ornamental value to gardens. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often used in woodland gardens for its elegant foliage.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in partial to full shade.
