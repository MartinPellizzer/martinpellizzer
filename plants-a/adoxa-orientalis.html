Adoxa orientalis

Adoxa orientalis, commonly known as the oriental false pepper.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant native to eastern Europe and western Asia. The plant features delicate, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters, often resembling small bells. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Adoxa orientalis is often found in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and streambanks.
