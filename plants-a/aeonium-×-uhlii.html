Aeonium × uhlii

Aeonium × uhlii, commonly known as the "Crimson Beauty," is a hybrid succulent plant belonging to the Crassulaceae family.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped growth pattern, with thick, fleshy leaves that are typically red or pink in color. Native to the Canary Islands, this plant thrives in well-draining soil and full sun exposure. Its foliage often features a striking gradient of colors, transitioning from deep red at the base to a lighter green at the tips.

Aeonium × uhlii is relatively low-maintenance, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
