Achyranthes cylindrica

Achyranthes cylindrica, commonly known as the round-stemmed cowherb, is a herbaceous annual plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, with slender, cylindrical stems that are often reddish in color. The leaves are alternate, simple, and ovate with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers in clusters, followed by capsule fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds.

It is often found in moist soils along stream banks and in disturbed areas.
