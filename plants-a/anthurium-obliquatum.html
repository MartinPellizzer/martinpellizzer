Anthurium obliquatum

Anthurium obliquatum, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces vibrant red spadices, which are the showy inflorescences that house the small, white flowers. Anthurium obliquatum thrives in humid, warm environments with bright, indirect light.

It is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
