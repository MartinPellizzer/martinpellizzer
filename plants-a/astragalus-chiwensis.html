Astragalus chiwensis

Astragalus chiwensis, commonly known as the Chiwan milkvetch, is a perennial legume native to China.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in arid and semi-arid environments, making it an important species in ecological restoration projects.

Astragalus chiwensis also has medicinal properties and is used in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.
