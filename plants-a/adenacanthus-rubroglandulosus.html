Adenacanthus rubroglandulosus

Adenacanthus rubroglandulosus, commonly known as the red glandular adenacanthus, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its hairy, glandular leaves and bright yellow to orange composite flowers. Native to southern Africa, this plant thrives in arid and semi-arid environments. Its structure includes a stout, branching stem with opposite leaves that are often covered in fine, reddish glands.

The plant is adapted to withstand drought conditions, making it a resilient species in its native habitat.
