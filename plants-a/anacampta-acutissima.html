Anacampta acutissima

Anacampta acutissima, commonly known as the narrow-leaved lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of southern Africa.

It features slender, grass-like leaves that are typically narrow and pointed, with a bluish-green hue. The plant produces tall, slender flowering stems that bear clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Anacampta acutissima is often used in rock gardens and as a ornamental plant due to its delicate appearance.
