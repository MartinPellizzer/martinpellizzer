Angelica refracta

Angelica refracta, commonly known as the narrow-leaved angelica, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Apiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and vegetables such as carrots and parsley. Angelica refracta is valued for its medicinal properties, particularly for its potential anti-inflammatory and digestive benefits.
