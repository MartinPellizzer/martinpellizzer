Arrhenachne juncea

Arrhenachne juncea, commonly known as slender marshgrass, is a grass species native to wetland environments in North America.

It is characterized by its slender, cylindrical stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences, typical of the Poaceae family. It thrives in moist soils, often found in marshes, swales, and along the edges of ponds.

Its ecological role includes providing habitat and food for various wetland organisms.
