Anoectochilus nicobaricus

Anoectochilus nicobaricus, commonly known as the Nicobar orchid, is a terrestrial orchid belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the Nicobar Islands and parts of Southeast Asia, where it thrives in humid, shaded forest environments. The plant features a sympodial growth habit with a rhizome and produces lanceolate, dark green leaves. Its flowers are typically small, with a distinctive labellum, and are often pale yellow or greenish-white.

Anoectochilus nicobaricus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
