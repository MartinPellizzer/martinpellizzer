Agathosma stokoei

Agathosma stokoei, commonly known as Stokoe's sage, is a small shrub native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its aromatic, needle-like leaves and small yellow flowers. The plant is often found in rocky, well-drained soils in mountainous regions. It is valued for its essential oils, which are used in aromatherapy and traditional medicine.
