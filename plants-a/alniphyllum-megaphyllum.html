Alniphyllum megaphyllum

Alniphyllum megaphyllum, commonly known as the large-leaf alniphyllum, is a deciduous shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, ovate leaves that can grow up to 15 centimeters in length. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers followed by dark blue berries. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Its foliage is valued for its ornamental qualities and its ability to support local wildlife.
