Arundinaria forrestii

Arundinaria forrestii, commonly known as the Chinese bamboo or forrest bamboo, is a species of grass native to eastern China.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems that can reach heights of up to 10 meters. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in panicles, which are typically green or brown in color. Its rhizomes are robust and enable it to spread rapidly through clonal growth.

Arundinaria forrestii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and its ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
