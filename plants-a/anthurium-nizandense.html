Anthurium nizandense

Anthurium nizandense, commonly known as the Nizan's arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to Central America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces vibrant spadices, which are the modified leaves that enclose the small, inconspicuous flowers. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.
