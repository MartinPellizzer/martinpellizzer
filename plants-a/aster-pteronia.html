Aster pteronia

Aster pteronia, commonly known as the Cape aster, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features showy, daisy-like blooms with purple or white petals, often surrounded by a ring of smaller, yellowish florets. It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.

Aster pteronia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant, long-lasting flowers.
