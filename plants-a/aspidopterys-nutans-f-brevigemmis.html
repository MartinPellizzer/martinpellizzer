Aspidopterys nutans f. brevigemmis

Aspidopterys nutans f. brevigemmis, commonly known as the dwarf weeping fig, is a compact, shrub-like form of the weeping fig species.

It features small, ovate leaves that are glossy and dark green, with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by round, green fruits that turn yellow when mature. It is native to tropical regions of the Americas and thrives in warm, humid climates.

This cultivar is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and manageable size.
