Anthurium lynniae

Anthurium lynniae, commonly known as the Lynniae anthurium, is a species of flowering plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its unique inflorescence consisting of a spadix and a spathe. The plant produces striking, heart-shaped leaves with a glossy surface and vibrant colors ranging from green to deep red. Its flowers are typically small and clustered around the spadix, often with a distinctive color contrast between the spathe and the spadix.

Anthurium lynniae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in botanical gardens.
