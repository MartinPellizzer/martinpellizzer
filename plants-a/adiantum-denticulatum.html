Adiantum denticulatum

Adiantum denticulatum, commonly known as the maidenhair fern, is a delicate and elegant fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features finely divided, feathery fronds that are typically bright green and have a distinctive, lacy appearance. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and prefers well-draining, acidic soil. Its rhizomes are slender and often covered in a fine, white coating.

Adiantum denticulatum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often used in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
