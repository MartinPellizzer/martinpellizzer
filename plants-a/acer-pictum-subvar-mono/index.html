Acer Pictum Subvar. Mono

Acer pictum subvar. mono is a strikingly beautiful maple tree known for its vibrant fall foliage. Native to the eastern United States, it thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial shade. This variety is prized for its unique leaf patterns and adaptability to various garden conditions. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice among landscapers and garden enthusiasts. Despite its beauty, it requires careful attention to soil and water needs for optimal growth.
