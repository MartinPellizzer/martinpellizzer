Anthurium perviride

Anthurium perviride, commonly known as the Emerald Heart Anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are a vibrant green with a glossy texture. The plant produces small, spadix-like inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, typically in shades of white or pink. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium perviride is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
