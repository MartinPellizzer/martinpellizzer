Actinostemon glabrescens var. macrophyllus

Actinostemon glabrescens var. macrophyllus, commonly known as the large-leaf actinostemon, is a species of flowering plant in the family Euphorbiaceae.

It is characterized by its broad, glossy leaves that can reach up to 30 cm in length. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in a cluster, which are typically greenish-yellow in color. It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa and thrives in moist, well-drained soils.

This variant is often cultivated for its ornamental foliage rather than its flowers.
