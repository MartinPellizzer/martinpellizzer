Alocasia scalprum

Alocasia scalprum, commonly known as the scarlet prayer plant, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are often deep green with a reddish or purple hue on the underside. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, rich soil to flourish. Its foliage is highly ornamental, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Alocasia scalprum is known for its ability to purify the air and enhance the aesthetic appeal of any space.
