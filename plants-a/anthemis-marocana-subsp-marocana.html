Anthemis marocana subsp. marocana

Anthemis marocana subsp.

marocana, commonly known as Moroccan tansy, is a flowering plant native to North Africa. It is a herbaceous perennial with a compact growth habit and hairy, grey-green foliage.

The plant produces small, yellowish-white composite flowers arranged in flat-topped clusters, typical of the Asteraceae family. It thrives in dry, sunny environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.

Its aromatic properties and ornamental value make it a desirable plant in arid gardens.
