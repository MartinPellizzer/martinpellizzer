Anthemis nigricans

Anthemis nigricans, commonly known as black ragwort, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant features upright, branching stems covered in fine, hairy foliage. Its flowers are typically dark yellow to brownish in color, with a distinctive, slightly aromatic odor.

Black ragwort is known for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally in herbal remedies.
