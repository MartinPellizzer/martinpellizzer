Anthurium valerii

Anthurium valerii, commonly known as the velvet leaf anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a velvety texture. The plant produces striking spadices, which are specialized inflorescences that resemble a spike of flowers. Anthurium valerii thrives in humid, warm environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and ornamental purposes.
