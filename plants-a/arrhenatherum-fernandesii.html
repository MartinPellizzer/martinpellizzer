Arrhenatherum fernandesii

Arrhenatherum fernandesii, commonly known as the Portuguese feathergrass, is a grass species native to southern Europe and North Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like foliage and produces delicate, feathery inflorescences. This plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. It is often found in coastal and mountainous regions, where it thrives in well-drained conditions.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
