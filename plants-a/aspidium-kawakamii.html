Aspidium kawakamii

Aspidium kawakamii, commonly known as Kawakami's water fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, fronds that are typically bipinnate, with a distinctive appearance that sets it apart from other ferns in its genus. Native to Japan, this plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in mountainous regions. Its reproductive structures, including sori and indusia, are well-developed and typical of the family.

Aspidium kawakamii is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
