Ambrosia longistylis

Ambrosia longistylis, commonly known as the long-styled ambrosia, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and ragweeds. The plant is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and small yellow flowers that bloom in the summer. It is often found in disturbed soils and open habitats, such as roadsides and fields.

Ambrosia longistylis is known for its allergenic pollen, which can contribute to seasonal allergies in humans.
