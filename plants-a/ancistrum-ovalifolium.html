Ancistrum ovalifolium

Ancistrum ovalifolium, commonly known as the oval-leafed arrowroot, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. The plant produces striking inflorescences consisting of a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Ancistrum ovalifolium is also valued for its edible tubers, which are similar in texture to traditional arrowroot.
