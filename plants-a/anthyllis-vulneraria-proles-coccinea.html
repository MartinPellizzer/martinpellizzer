Anthyllis vulneraria proles coccinea

Anthyllis vulneraria proles coccinea, commonly known as the red-stemmed bitter vetch, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its erect, branched stems that are often tinged with red, and its small, pea-like flowers that range in color from pink to deep red. The plant produces small, leathery pods that contain several seeds, typical of many legumes. It is native to regions of Europe and western Asia, often found in dry, calcareous soils.

This species is known for its ability to fix nitrogen, contributing to soil fertility in its native habitats.
