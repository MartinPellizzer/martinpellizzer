Aster kootenayi

Aster kootenayi, commonly known as the Kootenay aster, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the western United States and southern Canada.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and opposite leaf arrangement. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and stream banks. It features showy purple to pink flowers that bloom in the summer, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies.

Its foliage consists of narrow, lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture.
