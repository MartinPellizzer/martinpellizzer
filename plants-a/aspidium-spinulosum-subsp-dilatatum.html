Aspidium spinulosum subsp. dilatatum

Aspidium spinulosum subsp.

dilatatum, commonly known as the broad-leaf water fern, is a fern species native to parts of Asia and Africa. It belongs to the family Athyriaceae and is characterized by its large, pinnately divided fronds.

The fronds are typically bright green and have a delicate, feathery appearance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found near streams or in humid forests.
