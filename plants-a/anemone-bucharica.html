Anemone bucharica

Anemone bucharica, commonly known as the Bukharan anemone, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central Asia.

It typically grows in rocky and arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. The plant features basal leaves that are lobed and hairy, with a compact growth habit. Its flowers are showy, with white petals and a yellow center, appearing in spring.

Anemone bucharica is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
