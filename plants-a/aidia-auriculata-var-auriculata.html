Aidia auriculata var. auriculata

Aidia auriculata var. auriculata, commonly known as the auriculate aidia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, yellowish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its botanical name reflects the auriculate (ear-shaped) base of its leaves.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.
