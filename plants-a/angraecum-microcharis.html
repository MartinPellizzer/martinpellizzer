Angraecum microcharis

Angraecum microcharis, commonly known as the slender star orchid, is a rare and delicate epiphytic orchid native to Madagascar.

It features slender, wiry stems and produces small, white, star-shaped flowers with a distinctive fragrance. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media. Its flowers open at night, attracting nocturnal pollinators such as moths.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts and conservationists.
