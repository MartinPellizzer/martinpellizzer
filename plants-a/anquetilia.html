Anquetilia

Anquetilia, commonly known as the "cotton tree," is a tropical plant native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its large, fibrous seed pods that resemble cotton balls, which are used in traditional medicine. The plant thrives in humid, warm climates and requires well-drained soil to flourish. Its leaves are broad and heart-shaped, providing shade and contributing to the ecosystem.

Anquetilia is also valued for its ornamental appeal due to its striking appearance.
