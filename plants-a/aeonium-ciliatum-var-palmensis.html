Aeonium ciliatum var. palmensis

Aeonium ciliatum var. palmensis, commonly known as the Palmense Aeonium, is a succulent plant native to the Canary Islands.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy, blue-green leaves that are covered in fine, white hairs. The plant produces striking red or pink flowers on tall, branched inflorescences during the spring or summer. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its leaves and stems.

This variety is prized in horticulture for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation.
