Anthurium apiaense

Anthurium apiaense, commonly known as the Aipa Anthurium, is a striking tropical plant native to the lowland rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped spathes that can range in color from deep red to bright orange, often with intricate patterns. The plant produces small, spadix-like inflorescences that are typically green or yellow, surrounded by the showy spathe. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Anthurium apiaense is valued both for its ornamental beauty and its role in traditional medicine in its native regions.
