Asterolasia nivea

Asterolasia nivea, commonly known as the snow aster, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, white to pale blue flowers that bloom in late summer and early autumn. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and rocky slopes. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are arranged in a basal rosette.

Asterolasia nivea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic beauty.
