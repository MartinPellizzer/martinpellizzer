Aster leiospermus

Aster leiospermus, commonly known as the smoothleaf aster, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and medicinal properties. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and woodland edges. Its leaves are smooth and lance-shaped, and it produces showy blue to purple flowers in the fall.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting pollinators.
