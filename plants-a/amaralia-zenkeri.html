Amaralia zenkeri

Amaralia zenkeri, commonly known as the Zenker's amaryllis, is a striking flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its tall, upright stems and large, showy flowers. The plant produces clusters of trumpet-shaped blooms in shades of white, pink, and red, often with a contrasting purple or red throat. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and containers.

Due to its ornamental value, it is widely cultivated for its vibrant and long-lasting flowers.
