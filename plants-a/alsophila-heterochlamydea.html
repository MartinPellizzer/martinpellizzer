Alsophila heterochlamydea

Alsophila heterochlamydea, commonly known as the slender tree fern, is a species native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and a crown of feathery fronds that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is known for its ability to thrive in humid, shaded environments. Its fronds are typically bioluminescent, adding an otherworldly glow to its foliage.
