Aeschynomene dewevrei

Aeschynomene dewevrei, commonly known as the dwarf silverleaf aeschynomene, is a small, herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, pea-like flowers. The plant is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in arid environments due to its drought tolerance.
