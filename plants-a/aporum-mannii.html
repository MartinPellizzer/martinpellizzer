Aporum mannii

Aporum mannii, commonly known as the African orchid or the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces small, clustered blooms in shades of white, pink, and purple, often with a velvety texture. Aporum mannii prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements contribute to its popularity among plant enthusiasts.
