Arthrostemma heterostemon

Arthrostemma heterostemon, commonly known as the narrow-leaved arrowhead, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Southeast Asia.

The species is also valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to stabilize soil in wet environments.
