Acosta cinerea

Acosta cinerea, commonly known as the silverleaf acosta, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the family Acanthaceae and is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
