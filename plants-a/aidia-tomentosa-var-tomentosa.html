Aidia tomentosa var. tomentosa

Aidia tomentosa var. tomentosa, commonly known as the woolly aidia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, white, woolly foliage that covers its stems and leaves, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique texture.
