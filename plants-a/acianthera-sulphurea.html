Acianthera sulphurea

Acianthera sulphurea, commonly known as the sulphur orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil, and is characterized by its striking yellow-orange floral coloration. The plant produces small, intricate flowers that are often clustered in inflorescences, and its leaves are typically broad and lance-shaped. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
