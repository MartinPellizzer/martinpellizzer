Aeridostachya robusta

Aeridostachya robusta, commonly known as the robust stamen orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant features elongated, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine hairs. Its flowers are clustered in racemes, with each bloom displaying a unique combination of colors and intricate patterns.

Aeridostachya robusta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and greenhouse settings.
