Antimima gracillima

Antimima gracillima, commonly known as the slender antimima, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its small, delicate leaves and clusters of pale blue or purple flowers that bloom during the cooler months. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and requires minimal water, making it well adapted to its harsh environment. Its growth habit is typically low and spreading, with a compact form that helps it conserve moisture.

Antimima gracillima is often found in rocky outcrops and sandy soils, contributing to the biodiversity of its native habitat.
