Antiaris Challa

Antiaris challa is a striking plant known for its unique appearance and hardiness. It features thick, waxy leaves that can grow up to several feet in length. Native to parts of Africa, it thrives in warm and humid environments. This plant is often used as a decorative indoor plant due to its dramatic look. Its ability to grow quickly makes it a popular choice for those seeking a bold green statement.
