Anthopogon brevifolius

Anthopogon brevifolius, commonly known as the short-leaved anthopogon, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and small, narrow leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in composite heads, typical of the daisy family. It is native to certain regions of North America and thrives in dry, open habitats.
