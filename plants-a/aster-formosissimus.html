Aster formosissimus

Aster formosissimus, commonly known as the Chinese aster, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant typically grows to a height of 30 to 60 centimeters and features narrow, grass-like leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

The flowers bloom in the summer and are often used in ornamental horticulture.
