Adromischus hemisphaericus

Adromischus hemisphaericus, commonly known as the football plant or marble plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, rounded leaves that are typically green with a bluish or purplish hue. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
