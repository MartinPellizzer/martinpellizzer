Amphidasya spathulata

Amphidasya spathulata, commonly known as the broad-leaved amphidasya, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Araceae.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and striking, spathaceous inflorescence. Native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers within a spathe, typical of the Araceae family.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its unique foliage and exotic appearance.
