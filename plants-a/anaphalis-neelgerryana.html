Anaphalis neelgerryana

Anaphalis neelgerryana, commonly known as the Neelgerry silverweed, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Indian state of Tamil Nadu.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant produces small, fluffy white flowers that resemble clouds, giving it its common name. It thrives in rocky and well-drained soils, often found in alpine meadows.

Anaphalis neelgerryana is adapted to cold climates and is typically found at elevations above 2,000 meters.
