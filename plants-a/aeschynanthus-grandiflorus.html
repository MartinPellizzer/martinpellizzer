Aeschynanthus grandiflorus

Aeschynanthus grandiflorus, commonly known as the Christmas ivy, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ornamental value. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters, typically in shades of red, orange, or yellow. Its leaves are glossy and dark green, and it thrives in bright, indirect light.

Aeschynanthus grandiflorus is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
