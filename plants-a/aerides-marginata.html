Aerides marginata

Aerides marginata, commonly known as the Orchid with White Margins, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces fragrant, showy flowers with white petals and a yellow lip. The plant typically features long, narrow leaves and thrives in warm, humid environments. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.

Aerides marginata is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is prized for its elegance and adaptability.
