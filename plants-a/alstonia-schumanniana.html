Alstonia schumanniana

Alstonia schumanniana, commonly known as the flame tree, is a deciduous tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, bright red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters during the dry season. The plant has opposite, simple, and often hairy leaves that turn yellow before falling off. Its bark is smooth and greyish, becoming fissured with age.

The tree is widely cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
