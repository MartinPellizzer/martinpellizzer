Agave polyacantha var. xalapensis

Agave polyacantha var. xalapensis, commonly known as the Mexican horseweed or blue agave, is a succulent plant native to Mexico.

It features a rosette of thick, blue-green leaves with sharp, spiny margins. The plant produces a tall, branched inflorescence with small, greenish-white flowers. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils.

Its hardy nature makes it a popular choice for xeriscaping and ornamental gardening.
