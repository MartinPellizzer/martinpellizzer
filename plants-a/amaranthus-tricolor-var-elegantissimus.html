Amaranthus tricolor var. elegantissimus

Amaranthus tricolor var. elegantissimus, commonly known as the purple velvet amaranth, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its vibrant, tri-colored leaves that range from green to red and purple. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its feathery seed heads add visual interest and are often used in floral arrangements.
