Aristida holathera var. holathera

Aristida holathera var. holathera, commonly known as the silvergrass, is a perennial grass native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-gray foliage and slender, upright growth habit. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that are often tinged with pink or red, adding visual interest in the fall. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and native plant landscapes.

Its ornamental value and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice for ecological restoration projects.
