Angraecum × primulinum

Angraecum × primulinum, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a rare and captivating epiphytic plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that bloom at night. The plant typically produces long, pendulous inflorescences with white or pale-colored blossoms, which are adapted for pollination by moths. Angraecum × primulinum thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil and consistent moisture.

Due to its rarity and unique beauty, it is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts and conservationists alike.
