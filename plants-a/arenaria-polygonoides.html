Arenaria polygonoides

Arenaria polygonoides, commonly known as sand pink, is a low-growing perennial plant native to arctic and alpine regions.

It has small, pink or white flowers that bloom in early summer, typically from June to July. The plant features a compact growth habit with many stems arising from a central base. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, adapted to withstand harsh, cold environments.

It thrives in well-drained, sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in tundra and rocky habitats.
