Aloe corbisieri

Aloe corbisieri, commonly known as the White Aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically pale green with a white or grayish hue. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with small, tubular flowers that are usually yellow or orange in color. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it suitable for arid environments.

Aloe corbisieri is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional medicinal uses.
