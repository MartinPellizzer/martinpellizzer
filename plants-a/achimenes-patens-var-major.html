Achimenes patens var. major

Achimenes patens var. major, commonly known as the giant flame violet, is a tropical perennial herb native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that come in shades of red, pink, and white, often with a distinctive flame-like appearance. The plant features heart-shaped, velvety leaves that are typically dark green and covered in fine hairs. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is popular among gardeners for its ornamental value and ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
