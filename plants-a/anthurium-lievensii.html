Anthurium lievensii

Anthurium lievensii, commonly known as the "Red Velvet Anthurium," is a tropical plant native to Colombia.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are deep green with a velvety texture. The plant produces vibrant red spathes that enclose its yellowish spadix, creating a dramatic floral display. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium lievensii is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated as a houseplant.
