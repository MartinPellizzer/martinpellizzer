Arenaria Lanuginosa

Arenaria lanuginosa is a small, flowering plant native to arctic and alpine regions. It is known for its soft, hairy leaves and delicate white or pink flowers. This species thrives in harsh environments with low temperatures and limited nutrients. It plays an important role in stabilizing soil and supporting local wildlife. Its unique adaptations make it a fascinating subject for ecological study.
