Aloe berhana

Aloe berhana, commonly known as the red aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, sword-shaped leaves that are typically green with red or purple margins. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow flowers that bloom in summer. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.

Aloe berhana is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional medicinal uses.
