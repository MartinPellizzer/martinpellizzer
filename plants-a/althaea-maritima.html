Althaea maritima

Althaea maritima, commonly known as seamallow, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of Europe and western Asia.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in sandy or saline soils near the shore. The plant produces showy, pink or purple flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are heart-shaped and have a soft, velvety texture.

Althaea maritima is well adapted to salt spray and can thrive in harsh maritime environments.
