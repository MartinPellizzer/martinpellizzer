Allium stellerianum var. acutisepalum

Allium stellerianum var. acutisepalum, commonly known as the acute-sepal onion, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered white or purple flowers in the summer. The plant belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its bulbous underground stem. It is often found in moist, open habitats such as meadows and forest edges.
