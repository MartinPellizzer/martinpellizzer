Archanthemis fruticulosa

Archanthemis fruticulosa, commonly known as the shrub daisy, is a perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features upright, branching stems covered in fine, hairy foliage. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish flowers with white centers, resembling daisies. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and long blooming period.
