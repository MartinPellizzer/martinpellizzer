Aloe perfoliata var. mitriformis

Aloe perfoliata var. mitriformis, commonly known as the helmet aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, blue-green leaves that are often covered in a white powdery coating. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow flowers that bloom in summer. Its leaves are used for their gel, which has medicinal properties.

It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.
