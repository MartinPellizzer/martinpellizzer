Anisolobus amazonicus var. latifolius

Anisolobus amazonicus var. latifolius, commonly known as the broadleaf arrowleaf, is a species native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can reach up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, often found in moist, shaded environments. Its flowers are small and clustered, producing a distinctive yellowish-green color.

This variant is notable for its adaptability to humid tropical climates and its potential use in traditional medicine.
