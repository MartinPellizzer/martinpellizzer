Asphodeline baytopiae

Asphodeline baytopiae, commonly known as the Baytop's asphodel, is a striking plant native to the mountainous regions of Turkey.

It belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its tall, upright stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
