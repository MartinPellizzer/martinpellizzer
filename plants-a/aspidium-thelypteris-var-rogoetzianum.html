Aspidium thelypteris var. rogoetzianum

Aspidium thelypteris var. rogoetzianum, commonly known as the Rogozian shield fern, is a variant of the common shield fern, characterized by its distinctive fronds.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the temperate regions of Asia, particularly in China and Japan. The plant features bipinnate fronds with a shield-shaped base, which gives it its common name. Its leaves are typically dark green and glossy, with a leathery texture.

This fern thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
