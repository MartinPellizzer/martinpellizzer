Aloe obscura var. magnidens

Aloe obscura var. magnidens, commonly known as the giant desert aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, with a waxy coating to reduce water loss. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellowish-orange flowers that bloom in late summer. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its leaves and stems.

Due to its hardy nature, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant in dry climates.
