Arculus

Arculus, commonly known as the common arculus, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Scrophulariaceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces showy, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. The plant is native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere and thrives in well-drained soils. Arculus species often have opposite leaves that are ovate to lanceolate in shape.

It is primarily valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
