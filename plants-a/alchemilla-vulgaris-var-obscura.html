Alchemilla vulgaris var. obscura

Alchemilla vulgaris var. obscura, commonly known as the dark alkanet, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features dense, low-growing clusters of basal leaves that are dark green and velvety in texture. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in early summer, which are clustered in upright spikes. Its leaves are rich in mucilage, making it useful in traditional herbal remedies.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodlands and along stream banks.
