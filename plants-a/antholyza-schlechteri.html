Antholyza schlechteri

Antholyza schlechteri, commonly known as the Schlechter's antholyza, is a rare flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble a single flower. The plant produces small, daisy-like blooms with yellow disk flowers surrounded by white to pale blue ray florets. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in cloud forests.
