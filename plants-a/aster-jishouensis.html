Aster jishouensis

Aster jishouensis, commonly known as the Jishou aster, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers. The plant is characterized by its showy purple to blue flowers that bloom in the summer. It typically grows in mountainous regions and is adapted to cooler climates.

Its botanical features make it a subject of interest for both horticultural and ecological studies.
