Achetaria floribunda

Achetaria floribunda, commonly known as the yellow bellflower, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a smooth texture, while the stems are erect and often branched.

Achetaria floribunda is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
