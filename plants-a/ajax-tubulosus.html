Ajax tubulosus

Ajax tubulosus, commonly known as the slender ajax, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its narrow, tubular leaves and small, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance, it is often cultivated in gardens and rockeries.
