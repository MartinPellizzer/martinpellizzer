Areca ahmadii

Areca ahmadii, commonly known as the Ahmadi areca palm, is a species of palm native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feathery fronds that give it a distinctive, elegant appearance. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its leaves are often used in traditional crafts and as ornamental foliage in gardens.

Areca ahmadii is also valued for its ability to tolerate partial shade, making it a versatile choice for landscaping.
