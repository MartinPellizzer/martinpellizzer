Acacia socotrana

Acacia socotrana, commonly known as Socotra thorn tree, is a unique plant native to the Socotra archipelago.

It is characterized by its distinctive, thorn-covered stems and compound leaves that are adapted to the arid climate. The plant produces yellow flowers followed by flat, pod-like fruits containing seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is drought-tolerant, making it well-suited to the harsh environment of its native habitat.

Acacia socotrana plays an important ecological role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for local wildlife.
