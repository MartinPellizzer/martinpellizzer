Aspalathus polyacanthus

Aspalathus polyacanthus, commonly known as the silver leaf bush, is a small evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its narrow, silvery-gray leaves and small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant is well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, thriving in well-drained soils. It is often used in ornamental gardening for its drought tolerance and attractive foliage.

The species is also valued for its role in traditional medicine and ecological restoration projects.
