Aerides farmeri

Aerides farmeri, commonly known as the Farmer's orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and strikingly colorful flowers that range from white to pink and purple. The plant typically produces multiple flower spikes with fragrant blossoms, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation. Its pseudobulbs store water, allowing it to thrive in low-light conditions with minimal watering.

Aerides farmeri is well-suited for beginners due to its relatively easy care requirements.
