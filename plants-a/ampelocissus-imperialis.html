Ampelocissus imperialis

Ampelocissus imperialis, commonly known as the imperial leaf vine, is a climbing plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 centimeters in diameter. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers followed by dark blue, berry-like fruits. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a decorative plant in gardens and greenhouses.
