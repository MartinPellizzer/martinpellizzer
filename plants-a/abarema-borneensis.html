Abarema borneensis

Abarema borneensis, commonly known as the Bornean flame tree, is a species of flowering plant native to Borneo.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its striking red and orange flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has bipinnate leaves, which are divided into small, feather-like leaflets. It thrives in tropical rainforest environments and is often found growing in secondary forests or along riverbanks.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for horticultural cultivation in warm climates.
