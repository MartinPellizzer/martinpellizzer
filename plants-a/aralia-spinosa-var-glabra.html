Aralia spinosa var. glabra

Aralia spinosa var. glabra, commonly known as smooth aralia, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern North America.

It features compound leaves that are typically pinnately arranged, with smooth, glossy foliage. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers in umbels, which are followed by orange to red berries. It thrives in partial to full shade and is often found in moist, wooded areas.

Its ornamental value comes from its attractive foliage and berries, making it a popular choice for native plant gardens.
