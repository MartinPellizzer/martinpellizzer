Anemone multilobulata

Anemone multilobulata, commonly known as the multilobed anemone, is a herbaceous perennial native to eastern North America.

It features showy, white to pinkish flowers with five to seven petals that bloom in early spring. The plant has deeply lobed, palmately arranged leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, rich soils and is often found in woodlands and stream banks.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable addition to native plant gardens and ecological restoration projects.
