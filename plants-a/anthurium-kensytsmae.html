Anthurium kensytsmae

Anthurium kensytsmae, commonly known as the Ken's tsmae anthurium, is a rare tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a velvety texture. The plant produces vibrant red spadices, which are the modified leaves that enclose the small, inconspicuous flowers. Anthurium kensytsmae thrives in high humidity and indirect light, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Due to its limited distribution and unique appearance, it is highly sought after by plant enthusiasts and conservationists alike.
