Aechmea regelii, commonly known as the velvet plant, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that form a central cup to collect rainwater. Aechmea regelii is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and ornamental value.
