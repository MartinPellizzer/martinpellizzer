Acaena juvenca

Acaena juvenca, commonly known as the New Zealand burr bush, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the family Rosaceae and is characterized by its dense, thorny growth habit. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring, followed by bright red berries in autumn. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and covered in fine hairs.
