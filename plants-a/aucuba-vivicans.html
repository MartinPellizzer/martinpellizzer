Aucuba vivicans

Aucuba vivicans, commonly known as the Japanese aucuba, is a flowering plant native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green, oval-shaped leaves that are often variegated with yellow or white margins. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers in clusters, which are followed by red, berry-like fruits. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and ability to thrive in shaded environments.
