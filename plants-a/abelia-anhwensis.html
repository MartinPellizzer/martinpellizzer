Abelia anhwensis

Abelia anhwensis, commonly known as the Chinese abelia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features showy, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in late summer. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are glossy and dark green, providing a striking contrast to its floral display. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained soil.

Abelia anhwensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens for its attractive foliage and long-lasting blooms.
