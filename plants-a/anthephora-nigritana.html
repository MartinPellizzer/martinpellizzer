Anthephora nigritana

Anthephora nigritana, commonly known as the black-tailed orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, dark green leaves. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers that are typically violet or purple in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil.

Anthephora nigritana is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
