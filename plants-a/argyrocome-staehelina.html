Argyrocome staehelina

Argyrocome staehelina, commonly known as the silverleaf dandelion, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its hairy, silver-gray foliage and bright yellow composite flowers. The plant typically grows in grassy areas and along roadsides, preferring well-drained soils. It produces small, wind-dispersed seeds that contribute to its spread.

Argyrocome staehelina is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
