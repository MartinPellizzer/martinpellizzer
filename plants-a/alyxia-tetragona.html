Alyxia tetragona

Alyxia tetragona, commonly known as the four-angle alyxia, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is a woody vine that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features small, fragrant flowers that are usually white or pale yellow. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and often have a slightly rough texture.

Alyxia tetragona is often found in disturbed habitats and can be a pioneer species in certain ecosystems.
