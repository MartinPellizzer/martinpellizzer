Arenaria achalensis

Arenaria achalensis, commonly known as the Achal mountain sandwort, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of Pakistan.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit. The plant produces small, white or pink flowers during the spring and summer seasons. It thrives in rocky and sandy soils, often found in mountainous and semi-arid environments.

Its ability to survive in harsh conditions makes it an important component of local ecosystems.
