Anthurium friedrichsthalii

Anthurium friedrichsthalii, commonly known as the Friedrichsthal's anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are often a deep green with a waxy texture. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-shaped inflorescences surrounded by colorful spathe petals, typically in shades of red, orange, or yellow. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Anthurium friedrichsthalii is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated as a houseplant.
