Alyssum debarense

Alyssum debarense, commonly known as the Balkan alyssum, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Balkan Peninsula.

It features dense clusters of small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pale pink, blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has basal leaves that are linear to lanceolate, often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens and borders.

Alyssum debarense is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
