Anthurium cataniapoense

Anthurium cataniapoense, commonly known as the Cataniapoense Arrowhead, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped leaves that can vary in color from deep green to reddish hues. The plant produces striking spadices, which are the modified leaves that enclose the small, inconspicuous flowers. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in botanical gardens.
