Adenia populifolia var. pentamera

Adenia populifolia var. pentamera, commonly known as the velvet leaf or velvet vine, is a climbing shrub native to parts of Africa.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in a dense, velvety pubescence. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its unique foliage and ornamental value make it a popular choice for horticultural use.
