Aegilops ovata var. eigiana

Aegilops ovata var. eigiana, commonly known as the slender wheatgrass, is a perennial grass species native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences bearing numerous tiny florets. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed or cultivated areas.

Due to its hardiness and adaptability, it is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
