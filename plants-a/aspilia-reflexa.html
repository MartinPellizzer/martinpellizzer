Aspilia reflexa

Aspilia reflexa, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that range in color from white to yellow. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spiky bracts beneath its flower heads. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

Aspilia reflexa thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for drought-tolerant gardens.
