Athamanta arachnoidea

Athamanta arachnoidea, commonly known as the spider orchid, is a unique flowering plant native to the arid regions of South Africa.

It is characterized by its intricate, spider-like appearance, with long, slender petals and a distinctive central column. The plant typically grows from a corm and produces its showy flowers during the cooler months. Its flowers are pollinated by specific insects, showcasing a specialized ecological relationship.

Due to its rarity and habitat specificity, it is considered a conservation concern in its native range.
