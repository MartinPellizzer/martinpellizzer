Alocasia dussii

Alocasia dussii, commonly known as the Dussis arrowleaf, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves that are often a deep green with a metallic sheen. The plant belongs to the Araceae family, which includes many ornamental plants known for their dramatic foliage. Alocasia dussii thrives in humid, shady environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical landscapes.
