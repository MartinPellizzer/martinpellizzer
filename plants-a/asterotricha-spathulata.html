Asterotricha spathulata

Asterotricha spathulata, commonly known as the broad-leaved asterotricha, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its broad, spatulate leaves and clusters of small, daisy-like flowers. Native to certain regions of South America, this species thrives in moist, temperate climates. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Its botanical features make it a valuable addition to ecological studies and horticultural gardens.
