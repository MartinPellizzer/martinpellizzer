Amaranthus tricolor var. mangostanus

Amaranthus tricolor var. mangostanus, commonly known as red leaf amaranth, is a vibrant annual plant native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its strikingly colored foliage, which can range from deep red to purple and green. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow. Its leaves are often used in culinary applications, adding both color and nutritional value to dishes.

It thrives in warm climates and is cultivated for ornamental and edible purposes.
