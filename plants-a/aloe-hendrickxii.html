Aloe hendrickxii

Aloe hendrickxii, commonly known as Hendrickson's aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asphodelaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-shaped growth pattern. The plant features thick, fleshy leaves covered in a layer of white powdery substance called farina. It produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow flowers during the summer months.

Aloe hendrickxii is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water to thrive.
