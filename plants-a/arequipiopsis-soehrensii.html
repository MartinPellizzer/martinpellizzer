Arequipiopsis soehrensii

Arequipiopsis soehrensii, commonly known as the Arequipa orchid, is a flowering plant native to the Andean highlands of Peru.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy inflorescences. The plant typically produces clusters of small, brightly colored flowers in shades of yellow and orange, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in cool, humid environments and is often found in rocky or mountainous regions.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
