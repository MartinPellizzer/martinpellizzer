Acalypha buchenavii

Acalypha buchenavii, commonly known as the silverleaf acalypha, is a shrub native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its striking silver-green foliage, which is covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its unique leaf color and compact growth habit make it a desirable addition to tropical and subtropical landscapes.
