Argomuellera macrophylla f. laurentii

Argomuellera macrophylla f. laurentii, commonly known as the Laurentii form of the large-leafed argomuellera, is a variant of a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, lance-shaped leaves that are often a vibrant green with a slightly glossy surface. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale pink, typically blooming in the spring. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for cultivation in temperate climates.

This form is prized for its ornamental value and is often grown in gardens and as a houseplant.
