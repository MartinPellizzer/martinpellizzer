Austrobuxus nitidus

Austrobuxus nitidus, commonly known as the glossy boxwood, is a shrub native to southern Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage that is typically dark green and glossy. The plant has small, elliptical leaves that are arranged in a compact, bushy form. It produces small, white flowers that are clustered in terminal spikes, followed by red, fleshy fruits.

Austrobuxus nitidus is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil types.
