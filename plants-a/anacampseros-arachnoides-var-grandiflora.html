Anacampseros arachnoides var. grandiflora

Anacampseros arachnoides var. grandiflora, commonly known as the giant sandmat, is a succulent plant native to arid regions of southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are covered in a fine, white powdery coating, which helps reduce water loss. The plant produces large, showy yellow flowers that bloom in late winter or early spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for xeriscaping.
