Anthurium allenii

Anthurium allenii, commonly known as the Allen's arrowhead, is a striking tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its unique inflorescence consisting of a spadix and a spathe. The plant features large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. Its flowers are small and clustered on the spadix, surrounded by a showy, often colorful spathe.

Anthurium allenii thrives in humid, warm environments and is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant.
