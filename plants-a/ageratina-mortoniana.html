Ageratina mortoniana

Ageratina mortoniana, commonly known as the Morton's blue mistflower, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

Ageratina mortoniana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
