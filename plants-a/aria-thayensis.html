Aria thayensis

Aria thayensis, commonly known as the Thai arrowroot, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family, which includes plants like taro and calla lilies. The plant produces a starchy root that is often used as a food source. Its leaves are large and heart-shaped, with a smooth texture.

Aria thayensis thrives in moist, well-drained soil and requires a warm, humid climate to grow optimally.
