Aloe acutissima var. fiherenensis

Aloe acutissima var. fiherenensis, commonly known as the Fiherenensis Aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact rosette of fleshy, pointed leaves that are typically green with a reddish tinge. The leaves are covered in a layer of white, powdery wax, which helps to reduce water loss in arid conditions. It produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellowish-orange flowers during the summer months.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in well-drained soil with minimal water.
