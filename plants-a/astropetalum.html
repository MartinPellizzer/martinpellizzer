Astropetalum

Astropetalum, commonly known as the star petal cactus, is a genus of flowering plants native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its spiny stems and showy, star-shaped flowers. The plants typically grow in arid or semi-arid environments, adapting to harsh conditions with their succulent structure. Astropetalum species often display vibrant colors, with flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red.

These cacti are prized for their ornamental value and are often cultivated in gardens for their unique appearance.
