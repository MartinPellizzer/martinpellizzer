Arenaria takasagomontana

Arenaria takasagomontana, commonly known as the Takasago sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to Japan.

It features small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has a compact rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves that help it survive in rocky or sandy soils. It is often found in alpine or subalpine regions, where it thrives in cool, moist conditions.

Its botanical characteristics make it a hardy and resilient species well adapted to its specific ecological niche.
