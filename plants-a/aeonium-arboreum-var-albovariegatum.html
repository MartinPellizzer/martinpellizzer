Aeonium arboreum var. albovariegatum

Aeonium arboreum var. albovariegatum, commonly known as the 'Sunset' or 'Variegated Tree Aeonium', is a succulent plant native to the Canary Islands.

It features a tree-like structure with thick, fleshy stems that branch out into rosettes of fleshy, waxy leaves. The leaves are typically green with striking white or yellow variegation, creating a visually appealing contrast. It thrives in well-draining soil and full sun, requiring minimal water once established.

This plant is popular in rock gardens and as a decorative indoor plant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
