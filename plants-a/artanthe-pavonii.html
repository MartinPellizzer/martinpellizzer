Artanthe pavonii

Artanthe pavonii, commonly known as the rainbow artanthe, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features upright stems with lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are showy, with petals that range in color from pink to violet, creating a striking visual display.

Artanthe pavonii thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
