Anchonium elichrysifolium var. brachycarpum

Anchonium elichrysifolium var. brachycarpum, commonly known as the narrow-leafed anchonium, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Amaranthaceae.

It is characterized by its small, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of tiny, greenish flowers. The plant is native to arid and semi-arid regions of southern Africa, where it thrives in sandy or gravelly soils. It is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and fields.

Due to its low water requirements, it is considered a hardy and resilient species adapted to harsh environmental conditions.
