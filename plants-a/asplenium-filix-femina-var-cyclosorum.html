Asplenium filix-femina var. cyclosorum

Asplenium filix-femina var. cyclosorum, commonly known as the cyclosorum maidenhair fern, is a variant of the common maidenhair fern.

It is a compact, evergreen fern that typically grows in shaded, moist environments. The plant features delicate, lance-shaped fronds with a distinctive, dark green color and a finely toothed margin. Its rhizomes are short and produce clusters of small, spore-bearing sorus.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
