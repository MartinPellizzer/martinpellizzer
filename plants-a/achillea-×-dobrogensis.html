Achillea × dobrogensis

Achillea × dobrogensis, commonly known as the Dobrogian chrysanthemum, is a hybrid perennial plant native to the Carpathian region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and showy yellow to orange composite flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of 30–60 cm and thrives in well-drained soil and full sun. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its attractive blooms and hardiness.

The species is also noted for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
