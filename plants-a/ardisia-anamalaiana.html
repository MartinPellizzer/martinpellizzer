Ardisia anamalaiana

It is native to the Western Ghats of India, specifically found in the Anamala Hills region. The plant is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and produces small, red berries that are an attractive feature. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.
