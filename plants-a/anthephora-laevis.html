Anthephora laevis

Anthephora laevis, commonly known as the smooth anthephora, is a flowering plant native to parts of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are usually smooth and glossy. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens, though it is not widely commercially available.
