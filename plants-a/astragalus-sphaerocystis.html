Astragalus sphaerocystis

Astragalus sphaerocystis, commonly known as the globe milkvetch, is a perennial herb native to arid and semi-arid regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant produces small, spherical seed pods that contain several seeds. It is adapted to survive in dry environments with deep root systems and reduced leaf surface.

Astragalus sphaerocystis is often found in sandy soils and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.
