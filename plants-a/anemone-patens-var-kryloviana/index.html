Anemone Patens Var. Kryloviana

Anemone patens var. kryloviana is a striking perennial plant known for its delicate, showy flowers. Native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, it thrives in cool, moist environments. This variant of the common anemone is prized for its vibrant colors and unique floral structure. Its foliage is finely divided, adding to its ornamental appeal. Gardeners often cultivate it for its ability to add beauty and interest to shaded landscapes.
