Adiantum urbanianum

Adiantum urbanianum, commonly known as the eastern maidenhair fern, is a species of fern characterized by its delicate, lace-like fronds.

It belongs to the family Adiantaceae and is native to eastern North America, particularly in forested areas. The plant features bipinnate leaves that are finely divided, giving it a feathery appearance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and prefers well-drained, acidic soils.

Adiantum urbanianum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
