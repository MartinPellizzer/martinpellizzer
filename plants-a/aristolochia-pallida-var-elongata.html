Aristolochia pallida var. elongata

Aristolochia pallida var. elongata, commonly known as the slender Dutchman's pipe, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its distinctive, hollow, pipe-shaped flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with a rough texture and emits a mild, sweet fragrance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local pollinators.
