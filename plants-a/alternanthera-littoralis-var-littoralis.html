Alternanthera littoralis var. littoralis

Alternanthera littoralis var. littoralis, commonly known as the marsh alternanthera, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking red or pink flowers that bloom in dense clusters. The plant typically grows in wetland environments, such as marshes and along the edges of ponds. Its leaves are often lance-shaped and can vary in color from green to red, depending on environmental conditions.

It is a herbaceous perennial that thrives in moist, sunny locations.
