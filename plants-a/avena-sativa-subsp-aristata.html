Avena sativa subsp. aristata

Avena sativa subsp.

aristata, commonly known as the slender oat, is a perennial grass species native to parts of Europe and Asia. It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves.

The plant produces panicles of small, white to greenish flowers, which are typically wind-pollinated. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in meadows and grasslands.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for ecological studies and potential agricultural use.
