Aralia ferox f. nana

Aralia ferox f. nana, commonly known as the dwarf giant aralia, is a compact variant of the giant aralia species.

It is characterized by its small, rounded leaves that are typically less than 10 cm in diameter. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers that bloom in late summer. It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Due to its slow growth and compact form, it is well-suited for smaller garden spaces or indoor cultivation.
