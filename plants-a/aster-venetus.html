Aster venetus

Aster venetus, commonly known as the silverleaf aster, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features silver-gray foliage with a fuzzy texture, and its flowers bloom in shades of purple and blue. Aster venetus typically grows in moist, woodland environments and is often found in shaded or partially shaded areas.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, providing nectar for pollinators.
