Agrostis prominens

Agrostis prominens, commonly known as the tall bentgrass, is a perennial grass species native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in moist, temperate climates and is often found in meadows, grasslands, and along stream banks. The plant has slender, erect stems that can reach heights of up to 60 centimeters, with narrow, grass-like leaves. Its inflorescence consists of spikelets that are arranged in a dense, cylindrical cluster.

Agrostis prominens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping or as a ground cover.
