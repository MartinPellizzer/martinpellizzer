Adonis walziana

Adonis walziana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with suitable conditions.

Morphology

Adonis walziana has leaves that are basal, oblanceolate in shape, with a dark green color and a slightly hairy texture, arranged in a rosette formation.

The stem is upright, slender, and smooth with a pale greenish hue, growing from the base of the plant. Its flowers are showy, with bright yellow petals arranged in a raceme, blooming in early summer and pollinated primarily by insects.
