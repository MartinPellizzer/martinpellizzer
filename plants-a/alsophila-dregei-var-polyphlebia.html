Alsophila dregei var. polyphlebia

Alsophila dregei var. polyphlebia, commonly known as the giant tree fern, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, unbranched trunk covered in persistent, fibrous roots. The plant produces large, feather-like fronds that can grow up to several meters in length. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, typically found in tropical and subtropical forests.

Its unique morphology and ecological role make it an important component of its native ecosystems.
