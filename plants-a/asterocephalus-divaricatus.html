Asterocephalus divaricatus

Asterocephalus divaricatus, commonly known as the variable asterhead, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, showy inflorescence that resembles a cluster of small daisies. The plant typically grows in grassland habitats and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, while its flowers are often blue or purple in color.
