Aerangis erythrura

Aerangis erythrura, commonly known as the red-flowered orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking red petals and vibrant floral display. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid forest environments. Its flowers bloom in clusters, often with a sweet fragrance that attracts pollinators like bees and butterflies.

Aerangis erythrura is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and adaptability to various growing conditions.
