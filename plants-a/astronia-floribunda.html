Astronia floribunda

Astronia floribunda, commonly known as the flame tree, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, fiery orange to red flowers that bloom in clusters during the dry season. The plant has compound leaves composed of several oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular ornamental species.

Astronia floribunda is also valued for its rapid growth and ability to provide shade in warm climates.
