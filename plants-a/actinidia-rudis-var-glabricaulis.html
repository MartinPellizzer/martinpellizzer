Actinidia rudis var. glabricaulis

Actinidia rudis var. glabricaulis, commonly known as the smooth-stemmed kiwi, is a variant of the Actinidia genus native to China.

It is a deciduous vine characterized by its smooth, slender stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that give way to edible, fuzzy fruits similar to the common kiwi. It thrives in temperate climates and requires well-drained soil and partial shade for optimal growth.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential culinary use.
