Asplenium filix-femina var. purpureum

Asplenium filix-femina var. purpureum, commonly known as the Purple Maidenhair Fern, is a variant of the common maidenhair fern characterized by its striking purple foliage.

It is a terrestrial fern that thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically found in temperate regions.
