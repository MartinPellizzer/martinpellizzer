Amaryllis belladonna baptisa-multiflora

Amaryllis belladonna baptisa-multiflora, commonly known as the sweet-scented amaryllis, is a striking bulbous plant native to South America.

It features large, showy flowers that come in shades of red, pink, and white, often with a subtle fragrance. The plant produces strap-like, dark green leaves that emerge from a basal rosette. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

This species is known for its ability to bloom reliably when cared for properly, adding a dramatic touch to any garden setting.
