Aloe rebmannii

Aloe rebmannii, commonly known as the Rebmanni Aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, with a waxy coating to reduce water loss. The plant produces tall, branching inflorescences with bright yellow or orange flowers. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Aloe rebmannii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and medicinal properties.
