Albizia chinensis var. smithiana

Albizia chinensis var. smithiana, commonly known as the Chinese silk tree or Smith's albizia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its compound leaves, which are typically pinnately arranged with small, oval-shaped leaflets. The plant produces fluffy, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in summer, adding ornamental value to gardens. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for cultivation in temperate climates.

The plant is also valued for its nitrogen-fixing ability, which benefits soil fertility.
