Astragalus vesicarius subsp. carniolicus

Astragalus vesicarius subsp.

carniolicus, commonly known as the Carpathian milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to the Carpathian Mountains. It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its small, pea-shaped flowers.

The plant typically grows in rocky or mountainous habitats, preferring well-drained soils. It has compound leaves with small leaflets and produces pods that contain several seeds.
