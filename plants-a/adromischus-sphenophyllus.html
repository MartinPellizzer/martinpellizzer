Adromischus sphenophyllus

Adromischus sphenophyllus, commonly known as the "Cushion Stonecrop," is a compact, succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features dense, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a bluish or purplish tinge, arranged in a rosette formation. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or container cultivation.

Due to its slow growth and ornamental appeal, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
