Atalantia roxburghiana var. roxburghiana

Atalantia roxburghiana var. roxburghiana, commonly known as the Indian mangosteen, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Clusiaceae and is characterized by its dark green, elliptical leaves and fragrant, yellowish-white flowers. The plant produces edible, pinkish-purple fruits that are rich in antioxidants and have a sweet, tangy flavor.
