Aniba gardneri

Aniba gardneri, commonly known as Gardner's aniba, is a small evergreen tree or shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurels, and is characterized by its aromatic, leathery leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are typically clustered in terminal inflorescences. Its fruit is a small, woody capsule that contains several seeds.

Aniba gardneri is often found in moist, shaded habitats within tropical rainforests.
