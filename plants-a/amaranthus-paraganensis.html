Amaranthus paraganensis

Amaranthus paraganensis, commonly known as the Paraganensis amaranth, is a flowering plant species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and striking, vibrant flowers that range in color from red to yellow. The plant produces small, triangular seeds that are often used in traditional cuisines. Amaranthus paraganensis thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a suitable candidate for ornamental gardening.

Its foliage is also valued for its nutritional content, containing high levels of protein and minerals.
