Anemone bogenhardiana

Anemone bogenhardiana, commonly known as the Bogenhard's anemone, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features showy, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has finely divided, fern-like leaves that are typically basal and covered in a dense layer of soft, white hairs. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine meadows and rocky slopes.

The species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in garden settings.
