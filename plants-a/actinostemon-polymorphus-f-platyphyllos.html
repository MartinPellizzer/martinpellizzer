Actinostemon polymorphus f. platyphyllos

Actinostemon polymorphus f. platyphyllos, commonly known as the broad-leaf actinostemon, is a variant of the Actinostemon genus native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette formation. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually pale green to white in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

This succulent is often cultivated for its ornamental value and adaptability to arid conditions.
