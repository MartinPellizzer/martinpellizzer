Andropogon kwiluensis

Andropogon kwiluensis, commonly known as the Kwiluensis grass, is a species of grass native to Central Africa.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and narrow leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in savanna and woodland habitats. It plays a role in local ecosystems by providing habitat and forage for various animals.

Due to its limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
