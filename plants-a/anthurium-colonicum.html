Anthurium colonicum

Anthurium colonicum, commonly known as the red-veined anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with prominent red veins. The plant produces striking spadix flowers surrounded by a colorful spathe, which can range from red to orange. It thrives in humid, warm environments and requires indirect sunlight for optimal growth.

Anthurium colonicum is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and air-purifying properties.
