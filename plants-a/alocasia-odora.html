Alocasia odora

Alocasia odora, commonly known as the fragrant alocasia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with shades of green, white, and yellow. The plant produces striking purple spathes that enclose its small, white flowers, which emit a sweet fragrance. Alocasia odora thrives in humid, warm environments with bright, indirect light.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical landscapes.
