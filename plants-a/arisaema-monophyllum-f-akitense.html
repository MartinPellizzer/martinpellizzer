Arisaema monophyllum f. akitense

Arisaema monophyllum f. akitense, commonly known as the Akitan arrowhead, is a unique variant of the arrowhead plant native to Japan.

It is characterized by its distinctive, heart-shaped leaves and a spadix that produces a cluster of small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or along streambanks. Its botanical features include a rhizomatous root system and a short, stout stem.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens with suitable humidity and light conditions.
