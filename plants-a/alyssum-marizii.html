Alyssum marizii

Alyssum marizii, commonly known as the alpine alyssum, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and alpine settings. Its compact growth habit and extended blooming period make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Alyssum marizii is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
