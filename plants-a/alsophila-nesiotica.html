Alsophila nesiotica

Alsophila nesiotica, commonly known as the New Zealand tree fern, is a species native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate fronds. The plant is evergreen and can grow up to 10 meters in height, with a slow growth rate. Its fronds are typically dark green and have a leathery texture, contributing to its ornamental value.

Alsophila nesiotica plays an important ecological role in New Zealand's native forests.
