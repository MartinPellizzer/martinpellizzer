Argyrocome forrestii

Argyrocome forrestii, commonly known as the silverleaf forest fern, is a rare and delicate fern species native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking silvery-white undersides on its leaves, which give it its common name. The plant has a compact, rosette-like growth habit with finely divided, lance-shaped fronds. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in shaded, forested areas.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native range.
