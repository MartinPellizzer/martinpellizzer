Aponogeton stuhlmannii

Aponogeton stuhlmannii, commonly known as the aquatic arrowhead, is a tropical aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features long, ribbon-like leaves that float on the water surface, with roots extending into the substrate. The plant produces striking white to pink flowers that bloom above the water, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in shallow, still waters with soft, acidic soils.

Aponogeton stuhlmannii is often cultivated in ponds and water gardens for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
