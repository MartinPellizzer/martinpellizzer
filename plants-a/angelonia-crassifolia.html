Angelonia crassifolia

Angelonia crassifolia, commonly known as the rough-leaved angelonia, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of showy, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to red. The leaves are thick and leathery, giving the plant a distinctive appearance. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

This plant is also valued for its long blooming season, which can last from summer through fall.
