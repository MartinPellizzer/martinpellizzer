Asterolasia rupestris subsp. recurva

Asterolasia rupestris subsp.

recurva, commonly known as the narrow-leaf rock aster, is a succulent shrub native to South Africa. It features small, narrow leaves that are typically blue-gray in color, adapted to reduce water loss in arid environments.

The plant produces showy, daisy-like flowers in shades of purple or blue, which bloom during the cooler months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy choice for rock gardens.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental flowers make it a popular plant among succulent enthusiasts.
