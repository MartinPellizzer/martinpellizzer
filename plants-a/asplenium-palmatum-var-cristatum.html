Asplenium palmatum var. cristatum

Asplenium palmatum var. cristatum, commonly known as the cristate spleenwort, is a fern species native to Japan.

It is characterized by its distinctive, fan-shaped fronds that are often adorned with a unique crest-like structure along the central vein. The plant typically grows on rock surfaces or in shaded, humid environments. Its leaves are dark green and have a leathery texture, with small, rounded pinnae.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
