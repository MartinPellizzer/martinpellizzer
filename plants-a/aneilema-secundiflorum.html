Aneilema secundiflorum

Aneilema secundiflorum, commonly known as the red-flowered spider plant, is a tropical perennial herb native to Africa.

It features upright, branching stems with lance-shaped, dark green leaves that have a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces clusters of small, bright red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its foliage and flowers make it an attractive addition to shaded gardens or indoor spaces.

It prefers moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade for optimal growth.
