Argyrolobium monticola

Argyrolobium monticola, commonly known as the mountain silverhead, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small, annual herb that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are narrow and hairy, and it is often found in mountainous regions.
