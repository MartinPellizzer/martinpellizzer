Artemisia sacrorum var. latiloba

Artemisia sacrorum var. latiloba, commonly known as sacred wormwood, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features aromatic, gray-green foliage with lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, white hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flower clusters during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rocky or dry garden environments.

Due to its medicinal properties, it has been used historically in traditional herbal remedies.
