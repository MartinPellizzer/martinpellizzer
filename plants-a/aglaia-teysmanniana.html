Aglaia teysmanniana

Aglaia teysmanniana, commonly known as the yellow angel's trumpets, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Magnoliaceae family and is characterized by its large, showy yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has smooth, dark green leaves and produces small, fleshy fruits that contain toxic seeds. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has traditional uses in herbal medicine.
