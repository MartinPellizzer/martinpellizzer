Antirrhinum ramosissimum var. flavum

Antirrhinum ramosissimum var. flavum, commonly known as the yellow antirrhinum, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

It is characterized by its branched stems and clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.
