Agrostis Vulgaris Var. Varians

Agrostis vulgaris var. varians is a lesser-known grass species that thrives in moist, temperate environments. This variant of the common bentgrass is often found in meadows, wetlands, and along the edges of ponds. Its slender stems and small, dark green leaves make it distinct from other grasses in the Agrostis genus. Despite its modest appearance, it plays an important role in stabilizing soil and supporting local wildlife. Understanding this plant offers insight into the biodiversity of grassland ecosystems.
