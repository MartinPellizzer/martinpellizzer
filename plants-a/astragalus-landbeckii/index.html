Astragalus Landbeckii

Astragalus landbeckii is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of western North America. It belongs to the legume family, known for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil. This species is often found in rocky or gravelly habitats, thriving in dry and cool environments. Its small, yellowish flowers bloom in early summer, adding a subtle beauty to its rugged surroundings. Due to habitat loss and limited distribution, Astragalus landbeckii is considered a vulnerable species requiring conservation efforts.
