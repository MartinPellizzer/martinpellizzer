Aeschynanthus aucklandiae

Aeschynanthus aucklandiae, commonly known as the Auckland's slipper orchid, is a tropical climbing plant native to New Zealand.

It features striking, brightly colored flowers that resemble small slippers, typically in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are usually glossy and dark green. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Aeschynanthus aucklandiae is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
