Aeschynanthus curtisii

Aeschynanthus curtisii, commonly known as the coral vine, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant produces small, elliptical leaves that are opposite and covered in fine hairs. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Aeschynanthus curtisii is known for its ability to bloom prolifically during the warmer months.
