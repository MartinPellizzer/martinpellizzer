Acleisanthes purpusiana var. purpusiana

Acleisanthes purpusiana var. purpusiana, commonly known as the desert four o'clock, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

The plant produces clusters of showy, fragrant flowers that open in the late afternoon and close by morning, a characteristic known as nyctinasty.
