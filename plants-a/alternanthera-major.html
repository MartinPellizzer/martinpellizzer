Alternanthera major

Alternanthera major, commonly known as red flame or red alternanthera, is a striking aquatic or semi-aquatic plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red or pink foliage, which becomes more intense under strong light conditions. The plant has slender, branching stems and small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often used in water gardens and aquariums.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for both decorative and ecological applications.
