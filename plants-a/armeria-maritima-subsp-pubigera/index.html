Armeria Maritima Subsp. Pubigera

Armeria maritima subsp. pubigera is a coastal plant known for its striking pink flowers and salt-tolerant nature. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils along the shores of the Atlantic and Pacific coasts. This subspecies is often found in dune environments where it plays a vital role in stabilizing sand and preventing erosion. Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for coastal gardens and restoration projects. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively underappreciated compared to other flowering plants.
