Axonopus reederi

Axonopus reederi, commonly known as the Reed's axonopus, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. The plant has slender, upright stems and produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are often clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a smooth texture and a tendency to form dense mats.

Axonopus reederi is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its low maintenance and ability to tolerate various environmental conditions.
