Aspidistra paucitepala subsp. paucitepala

Aspidistra paucitepala subsp.

paucitepala, commonly known as the narrow-leaved aspidistra, is a species native to Southeast Asia. It is characterized by its dark green, elongated leaves that are typically narrow and have a smooth texture.

The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish in color. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical forests.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to indoor cultivation.
