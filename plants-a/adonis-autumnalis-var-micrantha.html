Adonis autumnalis var. micrantha

Adonis autumnalis var. micrantha, commonly known as the autumn adonis, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features basal rosettes of lance-shaped, dark green leaves that are covered in fine hairs. The plant produces striking yellow flowers in late summer to early autumn, which are arranged in a dense, terminal cluster. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is often cultivated in gardens and natural habitats.
