Actinidia diversicolora

Actinidia diversicolora, commonly known as the Chinese mayapple, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant produces edible berries that ripen to a bright red color in late summer. It thrives in partial shade and moist, well-drained soils.

Actinidia diversicolora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its foliage and fruit.
