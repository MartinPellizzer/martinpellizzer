Adenia borneensis var. microcarpa

Adenia borneensis var. microcarpa, commonly known as the small-fruited Adenia, is a climbing shrub native to Borneo.

It belongs to the family Passifloraceae and is characterized by its woody stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by distinctive, round, red berries. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Due to its unique morphology and limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
