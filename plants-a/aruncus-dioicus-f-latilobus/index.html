Aruncus Dioicus F. Latilobus

Aruncus dioicus f. latilobus is a striking variant of the common goat's beard plant known for its lush, feathery foliage. This fern-like perennial thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland gardens. Its leaves are broader and more lance-shaped than the typical form, giving it a unique visual appeal. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers in spring, adding a delicate touch to the landscape. It is a valued addition to native plant collections due to its ornamental beauty and ecological benefits.
