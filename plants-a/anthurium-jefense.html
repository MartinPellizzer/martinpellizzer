Anthurium jefense

Anthurium jefense, commonly known as the Jefense anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive inflorescence consisting of a spadix and a spathe. The plant produces bright, colorful spathes that range from red to orange, often with a contrasting green or white base. Its leaves are typically large, heart-shaped, and have a glossy texture.

Anthurium jefense thrives in humid, warm environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.
