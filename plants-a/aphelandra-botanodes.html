Aphelandra botanodes

Aphelandra botanodes, commonly known as the silver-veined zebra plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the Caribbean and parts of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, elongated, lance-shaped leaves that have a distinctive silver-white midrib and green margins. The plant produces fragrant, tubular flowers in shades of yellow and orange, which bloom in clusters during the warmer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

Aphelandra botanodes is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its ornamental foliage and unique floral display.
