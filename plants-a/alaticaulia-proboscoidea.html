Alaticaulia proboscoidea

Alaticaulia proboscoidea, commonly known as the winged-stemmed orchid, is a unique flowering plant native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, elongated stems that are covered in soft, wing-like structures, which aid in water retention and structural support. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are typically greenish-white in color, arranged in clusters along the stem. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, often growing in tropical rainforests.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
