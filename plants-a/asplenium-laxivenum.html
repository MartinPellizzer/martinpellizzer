Asplenium laxivenum

Asplenium laxivenum, commonly known as the slender shield fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Aspleniaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped fronds that are often covered in fine, hair-like structures. Native to tropical and subtropical regions, this plant thrives in moist, shaded environments. Its spores are produced in small, clustered clusters on the underside of its fronds.

Asplenium laxivenum is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in controlled indoor conditions.
