Amorphophallus cicatricifer

Amorphophallus cicatricifer, commonly known as the giant tiger beetle plant, is a member of the Araceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its large, showy inflorescence. The plant produces a single, spadix surrounded by a large, colorful spathe. Its botanical features include a tuberous root system that stores nutrients.

The plant is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in humid, shaded environments.
