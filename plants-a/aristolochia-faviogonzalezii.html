Aristolochia faviogonzalezii

Aristolochia faviogonzalezii, commonly known as the Faviogonzalez's Dutchman's pipe, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Aristolochiaceae family and is characterized by its unique, pipe-shaped flowers that are often greenish or reddish in color. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with a rough texture and is typically found in tropical and subtropical regions. It is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
