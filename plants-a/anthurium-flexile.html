Anthurium flexile

Anthurium flexile, commonly known as the flexible anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its unique inflorescence consisting of a spadix and spathe. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that can grow up to 30 centimeters in length. Its flowers are typically small and clustered, with the spathe often displaying vibrant colors such as red or orange.

Anthurium flexile is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ability to thrive in shaded environments.
