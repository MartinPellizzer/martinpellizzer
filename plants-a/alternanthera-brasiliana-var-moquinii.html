Alternanthera brasiliana var. moquinii

Alternanthera brasiliana var. moquinii, commonly known as Red Alternanthera, is a striking aquatic or semi-aquatic plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red or pink foliage, which becomes more intense under strong lighting and nutrient-rich conditions. The plant has slender, branching stems and small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters. It thrives in warm, tropical climates and is often cultivated in water gardens or as a decorative plant in ponds.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for both freshwater and marine aquariums.
