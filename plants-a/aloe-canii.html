Aloe canii

Aloe canii, commonly known as the Cape aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, often with a waxy coating. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow to orange flowers, which bloom in spring. Aloe canii is adapted to arid environments and stores water in its leaves for survival.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional medicinal uses.
