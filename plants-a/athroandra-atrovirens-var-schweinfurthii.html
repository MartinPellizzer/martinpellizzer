Athroandra atrovirens var. schweinfurthii

Athroandra atrovirens var. schweinfurthii, commonly known as the Schweinfurth's black-leafed hibiscus, is a tropical shrub native to Africa.

It features dark green, ovate leaves with a glossy texture and prominent veins. The plant produces showy, five-petaled flowers in shades of yellow to orange, typically blooming during the wet season. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forest understories or along riverbanks.
