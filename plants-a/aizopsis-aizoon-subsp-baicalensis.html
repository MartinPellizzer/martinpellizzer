Aizopsis aizoon subsp. baicalensis

Aizopsis aizoon subsp.

baicalensis, commonly known as the Baicalensis Aizoon, is a perennial herb native to the temperate regions of East Asia. It features slender, upright stems with lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged alternately along the stem.

The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers in spike-like clusters during the summer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate foliage and flowering habit.
