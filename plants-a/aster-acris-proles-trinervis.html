Aster acris proles trinervis

Aster acris proles trinervis, commonly known as the slender blue aster, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of ray and disk florets. The plant typically grows in open habitats such as meadows and grasslands, thriving in full sun. Its blue to purple flowers bloom in late summer, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies.

The leaves are usually narrow and have a feathery appearance, contributing to its ornamental value in gardens.
