Argostemma borragineum var. corymbosum

Argostemma borragineum var. corymbosum, commonly known as the clustered argostemma, is a perennial herb native to parts of Asia.

It features erect, branching stems covered with soft, hairy foliage. The plant produces clusters of small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are typically ovate to lanceolate in shape, with a slightly hairy surface.

This species is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories or along stream banks.
