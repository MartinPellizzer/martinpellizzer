Anthemis melanoloma

Anthemis melanoloma, commonly known as black anthemis, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features small, dark purple to black flowers that bloom in clusters, giving it its distinctive appearance. Its leaves are typically narrow and slightly hairy, with a strong, often aromatic scent.

Anthemis melanoloma is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential medicinal properties.
