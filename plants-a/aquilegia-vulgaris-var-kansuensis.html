Aquilegia vulgaris var. kansuensis

Aquilegia vulgaris var. kansuensis, commonly known as the Kansu columbine, is a perennial herb native to China.

It features showy, spurred flowers in shades of blue, purple, and white, typically blooming in early summer. The plant has compound, pinnately arranged leaves with finely toothed margins. It thrives in well-drained soils and partial to full shade, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its delicate foliage and long-lasting blooms.
