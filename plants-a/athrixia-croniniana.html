Athrixia croniniana

Athrixia croniniana, commonly known as the Cape snowflake, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its delicate, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in winter. The plant has a low-growing habit with slender, hairy stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. It thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.

Athrixia croniniana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its frost-resistant blooms.
