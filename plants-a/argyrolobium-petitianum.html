Argyrolobium petitianum

Argyrolobium petitianum, commonly known as the small-flowered silver-lobed burr.

It is a flowering plant native to South Africa, characterized by its hairy, yellow flowers and distinctive, spiny pods. The plant belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and has a sprawling growth habit. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.

Its ecological importance includes providing habitat and food for various insects and small animals.
