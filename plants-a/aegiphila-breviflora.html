Aegiphila breviflora

Aegiphila breviflora, commonly known as the short-flowered aegiphila, is a shrub native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its small, tubular flowers are typically pink or purple and bloom in clusters.

Aegiphila breviflora is adapted to high-altitude environments and thrives in well-drained, rocky soils.
