Abarema kinabaluensis

Abarema kinabaluensis, commonly known as the Kinabalu tree, is a species of flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Borneo.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, pod-like fruits. The plant is adapted to high-altitude environments, exhibiting features that help it thrive in cool, moist conditions.

It plays a significant role in the local ecosystem by providing habitat and food for various wildlife species.
