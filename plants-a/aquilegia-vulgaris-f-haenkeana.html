Aquilegia vulgaris f. haenkeana

Aquilegia vulgaris f. haenkeana, commonly known as the Haenke's columbine, is a cultivar of the European columbine.

It is characterized by its striking, spurred flowers that come in shades of red, purple, and blue. The plant is a herbaceous perennial with fern-like, bipinnate leaves. It thrives in cool, moist environments and prefers partial to full shade.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and wildflower gardens.
