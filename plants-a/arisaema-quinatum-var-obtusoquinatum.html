Arisaema quinatum var. obtusoquinatum

Arisaema quinatum var. obtusoquinatum, commonly known as the obtuse-leaf arrowhead, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its unique inflorescence consisting of a spadix surrounded by a spathe. The plant produces striking, umbrella-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy appearance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

The species is valued for its ornamental foliage and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
