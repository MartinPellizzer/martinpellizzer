Astrantia × rissensis

Astrantia × rissensis, commonly known as the Rissens hybrid astilbe, is a perennial plant native to Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, spires of pink to purple flowers. The plant produces small, oval-shaped seeds that are dispersed by wind, aiding in its propagation. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens.

Its ornamental value lies in its delicate foliage and long-lasting blooms that attract pollinators.
