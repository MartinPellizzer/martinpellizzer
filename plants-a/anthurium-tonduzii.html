Anthurium tonduzii

Anthurium tonduzii, commonly known as the Tonduzii arrowhead plant, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its unique inflorescence consisting of a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe. The plant features large, heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and deep green coloration. Its flowers are typically green or white, with a distinctive, spiky central spadix.

Anthurium tonduzii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ability to thrive in low-light conditions.
