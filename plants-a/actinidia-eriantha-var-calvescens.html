Actinidia eriantha var. calvescens

Actinidia eriantha var. calvescens, commonly known as the Chinese mayapple, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features compound leaves with a distinctive heart-shaped base and serrated margins. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in early spring. Its fruit is a yellowish-green berry that ripens in late summer.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its foliage and ecological benefits.
