Aspidistra neglecta

Aspidistra neglecta, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen, is a shade-tolerant perennial plant native to eastern Asia.

It features dark green, leathery leaves that are elliptical in shape and arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that emerge from the base of the leaves. It is well adapted to low-light conditions and requires moderate humidity and well-draining soil.

Aspidistra neglecta is popular as a houseplant due to its hardiness and aesthetic appeal.
