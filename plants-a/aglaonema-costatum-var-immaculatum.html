Aglaonema costatum var. immaculatum

Aglaonema costatum var. immaculatum, commonly known as the White Butterfly Plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, waxy, dark green leaves with distinctive white and cream-colored veins that resemble butterfly wings. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening. Its foliage remains vibrant throughout the year, requiring minimal maintenance.

The plant is also noted for its air-purifying qualities, contributing to improved indoor air quality.
