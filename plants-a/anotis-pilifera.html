Anotis pilifera

Anotis pilifera, commonly known as the hairy anotis, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features hairy stems and leaves, giving it its common name. It produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
