Aster sericeus var. microphyllus

Aster sericeus var. microphyllus, commonly known as the silky aster, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its finely hairy, silver-gray foliage and small, daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and prairies. It has a compact growth habit and is often found in dry, well-drained soils.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits to pollinators.
