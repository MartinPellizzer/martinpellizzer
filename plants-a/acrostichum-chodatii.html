Acrostichum chodatii

Acrostichum chodatii, commonly known as the golden fern, is a species of fern native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its large, feathery fronds that can grow up to several feet in length. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in humid forests or along stream banks. Its leaves are typically bright green, with a slightly serrated edge.

Acrostichum chodatii is valued for its ornamental appearance and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with the right conditions.
