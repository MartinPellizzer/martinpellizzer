Anthurium reflexinervium

Anthurium reflexinervium, commonly known as the reflexed nerve anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves with a prominent midrib and intricate vein patterns. The plant produces bright red spadices surrounded by a colorful spathe, which are its showy floral structures. Anthurium reflexinervium thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, nutrient-rich soil.

It is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and unique flowering habit.
