Alchemilla haumannii

Alchemilla haumannii, commonly known as the Haumann's lady's mantle, is a perennial plant belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is native to Europe and is characterized by its basal rosette of heart-shaped, silver-green leaves. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers in early summer, which are clustered in small, rounded inflorescences. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or streamside habitats.

Alchemilla haumannii is valued in gardens for its ornamental foliage and ability to attract pollinators.
