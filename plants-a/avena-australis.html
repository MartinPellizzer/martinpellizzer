Avena australis

Avena australis, commonly known as slender oat, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a perennial grass species that typically grows in moist, temperate climates. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences with numerous tiny flowers. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue.

Avena australis is often cultivated for its nutritional value, particularly in animal feed.
