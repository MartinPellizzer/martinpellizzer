Aconitum × paniculatum var. czeremossicum

Aconitum × paniculatum var. czeremossicum, commonly known as the Czeremossiczic Monkshood, is a hybrid perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, hood-shaped flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. The plant produces tall, branched stems that bear clusters of showy, spurred flowers. Its leaves are deeply lobed and have a distinctive, somewhat poisonous appearance.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
