Alocasia alboviolacea

Alocasia alboviolacea, commonly known as the Purple Velvet Alocasia, is a striking ornamental plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a purplish tinge on the underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that emerge from the base of the plant. Alocasia alboviolacea thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Its foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical landscapes.
