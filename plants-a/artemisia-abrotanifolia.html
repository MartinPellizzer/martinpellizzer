Artemisia abrotanifolia

Artemisia abrotanifolia, commonly known as Spanish sage, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its aromatic, silver-gray foliage and small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant contains essential oils and compounds such as thujone, which contribute to its strong scent and medicinal properties. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often growing in rocky or dry environments.

Due to its aromatic nature, it is sometimes used in herbal remedies and traditional medicine.
