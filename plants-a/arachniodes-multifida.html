Arachniodes multifida

Arachniodes multifida, commonly known as the forked fern, is a species of flowering plant in the family Polypodiaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds that give it a lacy appearance. The leaves are bi- or tri-pinnate, with a distinctive forked shape that contributes to its ornamental value.
