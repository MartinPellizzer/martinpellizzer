Anacamptis brancifortii

Anacamptis brancifortii, commonly known as the Branciforti's orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its upright, leafy stems and showy, violet-blue flowers. The plant typically grows in calcareous soils and thrives in sunny to partially shaded environments. Its flowers are borne in racemes and are known for their intricate, spurred structure typical of orchids.

Anacamptis brancifortii is often found in open woodlands and grasslands, where it contributes to the floral diversity of its habitat.
