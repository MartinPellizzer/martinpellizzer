Anotis foetida

Anotis foetida, commonly known as the stink bush, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, often strongly scented leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in heads, typical of the daisy family. Its foliage is usually dark green and has a rough, hairy texture.

Anotis foetida is often used in traditional medicine and is also valued as an ornamental plant.
