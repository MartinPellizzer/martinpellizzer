Apocynum × ciliolatum

Apocynum × ciliolatum, commonly known as the silky dogbane, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Apocynaceae family.

It is characterized by its hairy, slender stems and opposite, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in late summer. Its fruits are follicles that split open to release seeds covered in fine, silky hairs.

Silky dogbane is native to the eastern United States and is often found in moist, open habitats.
