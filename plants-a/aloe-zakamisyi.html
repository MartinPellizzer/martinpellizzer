Aloe zakamisyi

Aloe zakamisyi, commonly known as the Natal aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, branching stems and fleshy, narrow leaves that are typically green with red or purple margins. The plant produces yellow flowers in clusters at the tips of its stems, which bloom during the summer months. Aloe zakamisyi is adapted to arid environments and stores water in its leaves to survive drought conditions.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional medicinal uses.
