Arisaema akitense

Arisaema akitense, commonly known as the Akitan arrowhead, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its unique inflorescence consisting of a spadix surrounded by a spathe. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are typically clustered together. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

The foliage consists of heart-shaped leaves that are broad and glossy, adding to its ornamental appeal.
