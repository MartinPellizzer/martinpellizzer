Artemisia hancei

Artemisia hancei, commonly known as Chinese wormwood, is a perennial herb native to China.

It is characterized by its aromatic, silvery-gray foliage and small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family and is known for its medicinal properties. Artemisia hancei typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is often used in traditional Chinese medicine.

Its essential oils contain compounds with potential anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial effects.
