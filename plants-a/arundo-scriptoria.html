Arundo scriptoria

Arundo scriptoria, commonly known as the script grass, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features tall, slender stems that can reach up to 2 meters in height, with narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in panicles, which are typically greenish or brownish in color. It is often found growing in disturbed soils and along roadsides.

Arundo scriptoria is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in ancient writing practices.
