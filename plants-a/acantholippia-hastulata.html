Acantholippia hastulata

Acantholippia hastulata, commonly known as the slenderleaf acantholippia, is a shrub native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, yellowish flowers. Acantholippia hastulata is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
