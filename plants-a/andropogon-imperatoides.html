Andropogon imperatoides

Andropogon imperatoides, commonly known as big bluestem, is a tallgrass prairie plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its deep root system, which allows it to thrive in dry, sandy soils. The plant produces large, blue-green flowers in late summer, which are typical of the grass family Poaceae. It plays a vital role in stabilizing soil and supporting biodiversity in grassland ecosystems.

Big bluestem is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping and restoration projects.
