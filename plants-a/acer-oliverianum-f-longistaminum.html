Acer oliverianum f. longistaminum

Acer oliverianum f. longistaminum, commonly known as the long-stamen maple, is a rare and distinctive variant of the Oliver's maple.

It is characterized by its elongated stamens, which give it a unique floral appearance. The plant is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to the mountainous regions of China. Its leaves are typically palmately lobed, with a green upper surface and a lighter underside.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or forested habitats at higher elevations.
