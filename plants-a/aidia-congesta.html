Aidia congesta

Aidia congesta, commonly known as the clustered aidia, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and small, clustered inflorescences. The plant produces showy, blue to violet flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are typically narrow and grass-like, and it thrives in well-drained soils.

Aidia congesta is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
