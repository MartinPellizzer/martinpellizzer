Anemia buniifolia

Anemia buniifolia, commonly known as the arrow leaf tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, palmate leaves that resemble the shape of an arrowhead. The plant thrives in humid, warm climates and is often found in lowland forests. Its leaves are typically a deep green, with a smooth texture and prominent veins.

Anemia buniifolia is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated for its medicinal properties.
