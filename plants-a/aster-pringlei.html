Aster pringlei

Aster pringlei, commonly known as Pringle's aster, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically features showy purple to blue flowers that bloom in the summer months. Aster pringlei thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or alpine environments.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
