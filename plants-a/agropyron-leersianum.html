Agropyron leersianum

Agropyron leersianum, commonly known as the slender wheatgrass, is a perennial grass native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It is characterized by its fine, tufted growth habit and narrow, bluish-green leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed areas.

Agropyron leersianum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping and erosion control.
