Abutilon pannosum var. pannosum

Abutilon pannosum var. pannosum, commonly known as the woolly abutilon, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its soft, hairy foliage and showy, lantern-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are alternate, heart-shaped, and covered in fine, white pubescence.

It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive appearance.
