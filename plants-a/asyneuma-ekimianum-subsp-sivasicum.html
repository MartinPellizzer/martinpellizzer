Asyneuma ekimianum subsp. sivasicum

Asyneuma ekimianum subsp.

sivasicum, commonly known as the Sivas mountain daisy, is a flowering plant native to Turkey. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its yellow ray florets and greenish disc florets.

The plant typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. It has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, daisy-like flowers that bloom in the summer months.

This subspecies is of interest to botanists due to its unique ecological niche and limited distribution.
