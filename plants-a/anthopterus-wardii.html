Anthopterus wardii

Anthopterus wardii, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, flame-like red and orange flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces woody pods that contain seeds. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular ornamental plant.

Its rapid growth and showy blooms make it a valuable addition to gardens and landscapes.
