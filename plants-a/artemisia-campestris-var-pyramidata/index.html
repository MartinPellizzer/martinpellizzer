Artemisia Campestris Var. Pyramidata

Artemisia campestris var. pyramidata is a hardy perennial known for its compact, mound-like growth habit. This variety of artemisia features silvery-gray foliage that adds a striking visual contrast to any garden. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a versatile choice for rock gardens or borders. Its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements make it ideal for xeriscaping. This plant is not only ornamental but also attracts beneficial insects and supports local wildlife.
