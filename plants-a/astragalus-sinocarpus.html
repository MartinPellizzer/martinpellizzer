Astragalus sinocarpus

Astragalus sinocarpus, commonly known as Chinese milkvetch, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant produces pods that contain several seeds, which are often used in traditional Chinese medicine. It thrives in dry, temperate climates and is often found in grasslands and rocky soils.

Its ability to fix nitrogen makes it an important plant for soil enrichment.
