Acnistus sellowii

Acnistus sellowii, commonly known as the Brazilian spurge, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy surface. Its small, yellowish-green flowers are clustered in umbels and bloom in the spring.

Acnistus sellowii is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in partial shade.
