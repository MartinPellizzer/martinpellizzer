Aloitis foliosa

Aloitis foliosa, commonly known as the silver moon cactus, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, cylindrical stems that are covered in a powdery white coating, giving it a distinctive silvery appearance. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the spring and summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with plenty of sunlight.

Aloitis foliosa is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
