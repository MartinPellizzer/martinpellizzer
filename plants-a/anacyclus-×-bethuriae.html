Anacyclus × bethuriae

Anacyclus × bethuriae, commonly known as the hybrid sunflower, is a cross between Anacyclus and Helianthus species.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its hairy stems and yellow composite flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. Its foliage consists of alternate, lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture.

Anacyclus × bethuriae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
