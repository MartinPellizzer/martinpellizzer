Aerides odorata

Aerides odorata, commonly known as the fragrant orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features broad, leathery leaves and produces fragrant, pendulous flowers in shades of white, pink, and yellow. The plant thrives in humid, warm environments with bright, indirect light. It is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.

Aerides odorata requires well-draining potting media and regular watering to maintain its health and bloom.
