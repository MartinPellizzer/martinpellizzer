Aster adnatus

Aster adnatus, commonly known as the showy aster, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist meadows and woodlands, preferring well-drained soil. It produces showy purple to blue flowers that bloom in the late summer and early fall.

Aster adnatus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens for its vibrant color and ecological benefits.
