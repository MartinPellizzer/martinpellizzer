Aglaonema integrifolium

Aglaonema integrifolium, commonly known as the Chinese Evergreen, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves with intricate, often variegated patterns. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening. Its foliage remains evergreen throughout the year, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Aglaonema integrifolium is also valued for its air-purifying qualities and low maintenance requirements.
