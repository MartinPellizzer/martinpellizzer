Anthemis coarctata

Anthemis coarctata, commonly known as the narrowleaf tansy, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small yellow flowers arranged in dense clusters. It thrives in dry, open habitats and is often found in grasslands and rocky areas.

Anthemis coarctata is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its aromatic properties.
