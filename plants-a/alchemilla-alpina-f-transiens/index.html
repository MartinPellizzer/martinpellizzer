Alchemilla Alpina F. Transiens

Alpine lady's mantle, scientifically known as Alchemilla alpina f. transiens, is a striking perennial plant native to the high altitudes of the Alps. With its lush, velvety foliage and delicate white flowers, it is a favorite among botanists and gardeners alike. This unique variety of lady's mantle is prized for its hardiness and ability to thrive in rocky, well-drained soils. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for alpine gardens and rockeries. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively rare in cultivation, making it a sought-after addition to specialized plant collections.
