Anacamptis × menosii

Anacamptis × menosii, commonly known as the lesser marsh orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to southern Europe.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of pale pink to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in wet meadows, marshes, and along the edges of ponds, preferring moist and nutrient-rich soils. Its leaves are linear and grass-like, while its roots are fibrous and adapted to waterlogged conditions.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and plays a role in supporting local biodiversity.
