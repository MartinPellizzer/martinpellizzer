Artemisia pallens

Artemisia pallens, commonly known as gray sagebrush, is a perennial shrub native to North America.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which consists of narrow, aromatic leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Artemisia pallens is well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, thriving in well-drained soils.

It plays an important role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various wildlife species.
