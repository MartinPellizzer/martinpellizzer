Astragalus gongshanensis

Astragalus gongshanensis, commonly known as Gongshan milkvetch, is a flowering plant species native to China.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its compound leaves and pea-like flowers. The plant is typically found in alpine meadows and rocky areas at high elevations. It plays a role in soil stabilization and is of interest for its potential medicinal properties.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
