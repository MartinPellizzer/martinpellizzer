Amygdalus persica plena

Amygdalus persica plena, commonly known as the double peach.

This cultivar is a variant of the peach tree characterized by its double-flowered buds, which produce two or more layers of petals. The plant is a deciduous tree with a rounded crown and smooth, gray bark. It thrives in temperate climates and requires full sun for optimal fruit production.

The flowers are typically pink or white, adding ornamental value to the garden before the fruit develops.
