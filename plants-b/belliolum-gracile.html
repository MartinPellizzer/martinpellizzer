Belliolum gracile

Belliolum gracile, commonly known as the slender bellflower, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, upright stems that bear small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of blue and violet. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often covered in fine hairs.

Belliolum gracile is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
