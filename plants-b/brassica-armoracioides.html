Brassica armoracioides

Brassica armoracioides, commonly known as the yellow rocket, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many edible and ornamental plants. The plant features basal leaves that are elongated and hairy, with bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.

Brassica armoracioides is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in gardens for its vibrant color.
