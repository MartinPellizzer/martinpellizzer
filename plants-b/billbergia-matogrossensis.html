Billbergia matogrossensis

Billbergia matogrossensis, commonly known as the Matogrossense Billbergia, is a striking bromeliad native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its vibrant, long-lasting inflorescences that display a range of colors, including red, orange, and yellow. The plant features a rosette of sword-shaped, evergreen leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Billbergia matogrossensis is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
