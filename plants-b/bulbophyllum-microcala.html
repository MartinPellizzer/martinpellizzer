Bulbophyllum microcala

Bulbophyllum microcala, commonly known as the miniature caladium, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its compact growth habit and small, fleshy pseudobulbs. The plant produces intricate, colorful flowers that are typically pink or purple, with a distinctive spurred lip. Its leaves are usually heart-shaped and can be green or variegated, adding to its ornamental appeal.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements, it is a popular choice for indoor cultivation.
