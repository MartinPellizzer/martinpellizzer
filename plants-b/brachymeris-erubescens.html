Brachymeris erubescens

Brachymeris erubescens, commonly known as the red-tailed brachymeris, is a species of moth belonging to the family Brachymerididae.

It is characterized by its distinctive red-tailed wings, which are often contrasting with its darker body coloration. The larvae of this species feed on a variety of plants, primarily within the families Asteraceae and Fabaceae. Its life cycle includes a pupal stage that is typically found in leaf litter or soil.

This moth is of interest to entomologists due to its unique morphological features and ecological role in its native habitat.
