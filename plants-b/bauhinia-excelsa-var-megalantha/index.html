Bauhinia Excelsa Var. Megalantha

Bauhinia excelsa var. megalantha is a striking tropical plant known for its large, showy flowers. Native to parts of Southeast Asia, it thrives in warm, humid climates with ample sunlight. The plant features broad, heart-shaped leaves and clusters of vibrant pink or purple blooms. It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its aesthetic appeal. This variety is a popular choice for gardens and landscapes seeking a dramatic floral display.
