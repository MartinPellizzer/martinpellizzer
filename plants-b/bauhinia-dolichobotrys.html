Bauhinia dolichobotrys

Bauhinia dolichobotrys, commonly known as the long-stemmed bauhinia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical regions of Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive bipinnate leaves and showy, butterfly-like flowers. The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant pink or white blooms. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Additionally, it has a symbiotic relationship with nitrogen-fixing bacteria, enhancing soil fertility.
