Basistemon spinosus

Basistemon spinosus, commonly known as the spiny basistemon, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant features upright, branching stems that are covered in sharp, spiny projections. Its flowers are small, clustered in dense inflorescences, and are usually pink or purple in color.

Basistemon spinosus thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable plant for rock gardens and dry landscapes.
