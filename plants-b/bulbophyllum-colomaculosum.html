Bulbophyllum colomaculosum

Bulbophyllum colomaculosum, commonly known as the Colomaculosum orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and intricate floral structures. The plant produces striking, often fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. Its pseudobulbs are cylindrical and serve as storage organs for water and nutrients.

This orchid thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
