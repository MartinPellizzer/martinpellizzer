Boronia inornata subsp. inornata

Boronia inornata subsp.

inornata, commonly known as the slender boronia, is a shrub native to southeastern Australia. It typically grows in heathlands and dry forests, preferring well-drained soils.

The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in spring, attracting nectar-feeding birds and insects. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often covered in fine hairs.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
