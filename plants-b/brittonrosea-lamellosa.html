Brittonrosea lamellosa

Brittonrosea lamellosa, commonly known as the fluted rose.

It is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States, characterized by its unique, fluted petals that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant belongs to the Rosaceae family and is known for its showy, pink to red blooms that appear in the spring. It typically grows in rocky soils and is adapted to arid environments.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for xeriscaping and native plant gardens.
