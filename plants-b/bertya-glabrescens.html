Bertya glabrescens

Bertya glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth bertya, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features small, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and covered in fine hairs, giving them a slightly rough texture.

Bertya glabrescens is adapted to dry, sunny environments and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.
