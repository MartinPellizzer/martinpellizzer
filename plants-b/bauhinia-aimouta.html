Bauhinia aimouta

Bauhinia aimouta, commonly known as the flame tree or red bauhinia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, flame-like inflorescences that bloom in clusters of bright red or orange flowers. The plant has palmate leaves composed of three to five leaflets, and its bark is often smooth and grayish in color. Bauhinia aimouta thrives in well-drained soils and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

It is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
