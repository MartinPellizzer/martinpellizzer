Botrychium Obliquum Var. Dissectum

Botrychium obliquum var. dissectum is a unique fern species known for its intricate leaf structures. This variant of the common moonwort displays a finely dissected frond that creates a delicate, lace-like appearance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forests and along stream banks. The plant's distinctive morphology makes it a subject of interest for botanists and nature enthusiasts alike. Its presence in the wild highlights the diversity within the Botrychium genus.
