Bunium cylindricum subsp. badghysi

Bunium cylindricum subsp.

badghysi, commonly known as the Kashmiri fennel, is a perennial herb native to the high-altitude regions of the Himalayas. It features a rosette of lance-shaped, aromatic leaves that are covered in fine hairs.

The plant produces a tall, slender flowering stem topped with a dense cluster of small, yellowish flowers. Its seeds are cylindrical and have a distinctive, spicy aroma.

This subspecies is valued for its culinary and medicinal uses in traditional practices.
