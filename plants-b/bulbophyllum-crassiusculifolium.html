Bulbophyllum crassiusculifolium

Bulbophyllum crassiusculifolium, commonly known as the thick-leafed orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its robust, fleshy pseudobulbs and broad, leathery leaves. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers with long, slender petals. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its striking appearance and ease of cultivation, it is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
