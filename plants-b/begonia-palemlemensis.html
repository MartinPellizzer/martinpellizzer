Begonia palemlemensis

Begonia palemlemensis, commonly known as the Palellemensis begonia, is a species native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Begoniaceae family and is characterized by its unique foliage and flowering patterns. The plant typically features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and produces clusters of small, tubular flowers. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
