Britoa sessiliflora

Britoa sessiliflora, commonly known as the sessile-flower britoa, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are narrow and often covered in fine hairs, while its stems are usually short and upright.
