Bulbophyllum maxillare

Bulbophyllum maxillare, commonly known as the golden spike orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, spike-like inflorescence. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are often adorned with intricate patterns. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is typically found in tropical rainforests.

Bulbophyllum maxillare is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
