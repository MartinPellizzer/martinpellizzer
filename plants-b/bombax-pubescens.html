Bombax pubescens

Bombax pubescens, commonly known as the silky cotton tree, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from pink to red, which bloom in clusters. The tree produces fluffy, cotton-like seeds enclosed in a fibrous pod, which are dispersed by wind. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often covered with fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

The plant is valued for its ornamental beauty and has traditional uses in various cultures.
