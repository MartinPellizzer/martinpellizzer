Bulbophyllum cheiri subsp. subuliferum

Bulbophyllum cheiri subsp.

subuliferum, commonly known as the slender-leafed bulbophyllum, is a member of the Orchidaceae family. It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in regions like Malaysia and Indonesia.

The plant is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its striking floral display.

This epiphytic orchid thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining substrate for optimal growth.
