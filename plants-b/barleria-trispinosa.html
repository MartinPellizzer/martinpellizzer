Barleria trispinosa

Barleria trispinosa, commonly known as the three-spined barleria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features slender, upright stems covered in small, sharp spines. Its small, tubular flowers are usually pink or red and bloom in clusters during the summer months.

Barleria trispinosa is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
