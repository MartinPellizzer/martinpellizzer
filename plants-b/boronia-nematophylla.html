Boronia nematophylla

Boronia nematophylla, commonly known as the slender boronia, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of southern Australia.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 meter, characterized by its aromatic, glandular leaves and showy, fragrant flowers. The flowers are usually white or pale pink, with a distinctive, narrow shape, and are borne in clusters along the stems. This species is adapted to sandy soils and is often found in heathlands and coastal dunes.

It plays an important ecological role in supporting local pollinators and maintaining biodiversity in its habitat.
