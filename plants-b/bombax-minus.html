Bombax minus

Bombax minus, commonly known as the lesser cotton tree, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family, which includes cotton and hibiscus plants. The tree is characterized by its broad, ovate leaves and showy, pale pink to white flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release fluffy, cotton-like seeds.
