Begonia quadrialata var. pilosa

Begonia quadrialata var. pilosa, commonly known as the velvety begonia, is a striking plant characterized by its dense, velvety foliage.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a deep green color and a soft, fuzzy texture that gives it a unique appearance. The plant produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, often with a contrasting veined pattern. It thrives in partial shade and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor and shaded garden settings.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
