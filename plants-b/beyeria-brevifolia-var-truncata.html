Beyeria brevifolia var. truncata

Beyeria brevifolia var. truncata, commonly known as the Truncated Beyeria, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its short, fleshy stems and small, rounded leaves that are often tinged with red or purple. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect sunlight to flourish.

Due to its compact size and ornamental appeal, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation.
