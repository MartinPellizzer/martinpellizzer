Brachyspatha zeylanica

Brachyspatha zeylanica, commonly known as the Ceylon orchid, is a species of orchid native to Sri Lanka.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and strikingly colored flowers, often featuring a combination of white, yellow, and green hues. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments within tropical forests. Its leaves are broad and glossy, providing a lush backdrop to its delicate blooms.

Brachyspatha zeylanica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and greenhouses.
