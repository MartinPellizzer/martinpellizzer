Bilderdykia multiflora

Bilderdykia multiflora, commonly known as the multibract bellflower, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, upright stems that bear clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of blue or purple. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are arranged in a rosette at the base. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, often growing in rocky or sandy habitats.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.
