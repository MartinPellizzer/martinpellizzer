Bulbophyllum thaiorum

Bulbophyllum thaiorum, commonly known as the Thai orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers that are typically pale yellow to orange in color. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, enabling it to thrive in humid, shaded environments.

Bulbophyllum thaiorum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens and greenhouses.
