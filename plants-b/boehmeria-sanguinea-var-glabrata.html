Boehmeria sanguinea var. glabrata

Boehmeria sanguinea var. glabrata, commonly known as red bamboo or red giant bamboo, is a variant of the Boehmeria sanguinea species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its robust, bamboo-like stems that are typically reddish in color. The plant has large, heart-shaped leaves that are smooth and glabrous, giving it its varietal name. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

This variant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated for its aesthetic appeal.
