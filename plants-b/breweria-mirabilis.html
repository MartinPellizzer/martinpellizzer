Breweria mirabilis

Breweria mirabilis, commonly known as the fairy cup, is a small, herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features delicate, cup-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow, often with a slight greenish tinge. The plant has hairy, slender stems and lance-shaped leaves that are usually covered in fine pubescence. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological role, it is a subject of interest in both botanical studies and horticulture.
