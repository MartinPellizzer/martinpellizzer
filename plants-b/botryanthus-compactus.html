Botryanthus compactus

Botryanthus compactus, commonly known as the compact bottlebrush.

It is a flowering plant native to southern Africa, characterized by its dense, cylindrical inflorescences that resemble bottle brushes. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, which attract pollinators such as birds and insects. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Botryanthus compactus is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
