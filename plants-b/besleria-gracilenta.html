Besleria gracilenta

Besleria gracilenta, commonly known as the slender besleria, is a tropical flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its trailing growth habit and showy, tubular flowers. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves and produces vibrant, often bi-colored blooms in shades of red, orange, and yellow. It thrives in humid, warm environments and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded gardens.

Due to its ornamental value, it is popular among plant enthusiasts for its beauty and ease of care.
