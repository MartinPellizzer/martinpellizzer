Begonia sandtii

Begonia sandtii, commonly known as the sandtii begonia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves that often display intricate patterns and vibrant colors. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or red in color. It thrives in humid environments with partial shade and well-draining soil.

Begonia sandtii is popular among gardeners for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
