Bihai champneiana

Bihai champneiana, commonly known as the Moonflower, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its large, white, nocturnal blooms that open at night and close by morning. The plant belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes nightshade species. Its leaves are typically broad and ovate, with a smooth texture.

The flowers emit a strong, sweet fragrance that attracts nocturnal pollinators like moths.
