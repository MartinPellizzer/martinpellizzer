Bulbostylis trichobasis var. caespitosa

Bulbostylis trichobasis var. caespitosa, commonly known as the tufted bulbostylis, is a small, herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the grass family (Poaceae) and is characterized by its compact, clump-forming growth habit. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, white or pale green flowers.
