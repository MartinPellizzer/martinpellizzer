Bidens rhombifolia

Bidens rhombifolia, commonly known as the diamondleaf tickseed, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. It produces small yellow flowers with white ray florets, which attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Bidens rhombifolia is typically found in moist habitats like swamps and wetlands, where it thrives in the humid climate.
