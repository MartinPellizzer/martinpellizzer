Bulbophyllum cochleatum var. bequaertii

Bulbophyllum cochleatum var. bequaertii, commonly known as the spiral orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique spiral arrangement of leaves, which gives it its common name. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, often with a distinctive lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining substrate.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
