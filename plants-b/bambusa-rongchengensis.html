Bambusa rongchengensis

Bambusa rongchengensis, commonly known as the Rongcheng bamboo, is a species native to China.

It is characterized by its tall, slender culms that can reach heights of up to 20 meters. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically green and arranged in a dense, compact clump. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

This bamboo is valued for its ornamental beauty and is used in landscaping and traditional crafts.
