Begonia mabberleyana

Begonia mabberleyana, commonly known as the Mabberley's begonia, is a striking ornamental plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, pink, and white, often with intricate patterns on the petals. The plant features heart-shaped leaves that are glossy and deeply veined, contributing to its aesthetic appeal. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it suitable for shaded garden areas or as a houseplant.

Due to its ornamental value and unique appearance, it is often cultivated for decorative purposes in both indoor and outdoor settings.
