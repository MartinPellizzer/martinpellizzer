Basanacantha spinosa var. guatemalensis

Basanacantha spinosa var. guatemalensis, commonly known as the Guatemalan spiny acanthus, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central America.

It features distinctive, spiny leaves with a rough, textured surface, and its flowering stems produce clusters of showy, violet to purple bracts. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or partially shaded woodland areas. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

Its botanical characteristics make it a unique addition to both horticultural collections and ecological studies.
