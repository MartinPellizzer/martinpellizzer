Biarum Fraasianum

Biarum fraasianum is a rare and enigmatic plant native to the mountainous regions of southern China. Known for its striking, bell-shaped flowers, it captivates botanists and nature enthusiasts alike. This species thrives in cool, humid environments and is often found in shaded forest undergrowth. Its unique appearance and limited distribution make it a subject of scientific interest. Due to habitat loss, Biarum fraasianum faces increasing threats to its survival.
