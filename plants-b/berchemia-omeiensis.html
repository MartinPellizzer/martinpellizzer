Berchemia omeiensis

Berchemia omeiensis, commonly known as the Chinese flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It belongs to the family Fabaceae and is characterized by its striking, bright orange to red flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant has pinnate leaves and produces pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant floral display.
