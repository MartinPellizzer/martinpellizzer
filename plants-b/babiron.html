Babiron

Babiron, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the genus Saintpaulia and is characterized by its compact rosette of velvety, waxy leaves. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in a variety of colors, including purple, pink, white, and blue. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Babiron is a popular houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
