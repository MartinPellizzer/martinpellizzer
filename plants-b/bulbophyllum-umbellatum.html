Bulbophyllum umbellatum

Bulbophyllum umbellatum, commonly known as the umbrella orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, clustered inflorescence that resembles an umbrella, giving it its common name. The plant produces numerous small, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and red, which bloom sequentially throughout the year. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture and nutrients from the air and surrounding environment.

This species is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
