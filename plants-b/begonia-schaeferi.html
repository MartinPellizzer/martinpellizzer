Begonia schaeferi

Begonia schaeferi, commonly known as the Schaefer's begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It features vibrant, heart-shaped leaves with a glossy texture and produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of red and orange. The plant is typically grown as a houseplant due to its preference for warm, humid environments. It thrives in indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for collectors and indoor gardeners alike.
