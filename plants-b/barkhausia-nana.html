Barkhausia nana

Barkhausia nana, commonly known as the dwarf barkhausia, is a small evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense, dark green foliage. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers in late winter to early spring. Its bark is smooth and reddish-brown, providing a distinctive visual feature.

Barkhausia nana is well-suited for rock gardens and containers due to its hardy nature and ornamental appeal.
