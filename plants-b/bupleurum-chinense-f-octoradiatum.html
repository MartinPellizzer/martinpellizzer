Bupleurum chinense f. octoradiatum

Bupleurum chinense f. octoradiatum, commonly known as the Chinese angelica or eight-rayed bupleurum, is a variant of the Bupleurum chinense species.

It is a perennial herb native to China and is characterized by its distinctive, branched root system. The plant features compound leaves with serrated edges and produces small, yellowish flowers in clusters. It is often cultivated for its medicinal properties, particularly in traditional Chinese medicine.

Its botanical features make it valuable for both horticultural and pharmacological purposes.
