Bauhinia baueriana

Bauhinia baueriana, commonly known as the pink bauhinia, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, flat, bipinnate leaves that resemble the shape of a fan. The plant produces showy, pink or purple flowers that bloom in clusters, typically during the rainy season. Its fruits are pods that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

Bauhinia baueriana is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
