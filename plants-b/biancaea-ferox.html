Biancaea ferox

Biancaea ferox, commonly known as the African ironwood, is a deciduous tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, hard wood that is highly valued for its strength and durability. The plant has compound leaves with long, slender leaflets and produces small, greenish-yellow flowers. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often growing in rocky soils.

The tree is also known for its ability to withstand drought conditions due to its deep root system.
