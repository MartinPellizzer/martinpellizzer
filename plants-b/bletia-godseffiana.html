Bletia godseffiana

Bletia godseffiana, commonly known as the golden orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, with a smooth texture.

Bletia godseffiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
