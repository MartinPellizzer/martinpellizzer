Blennodia blennodioides

Blennodia blennodioides, commonly known as the African violet, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, dark green leaves that are covered in a fine, white pubescence. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of purple, pink, and white, which bloom from spring to autumn. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers bright, indirect light.

Due to its ornamental value, it is widely cultivated as a houseplant.
