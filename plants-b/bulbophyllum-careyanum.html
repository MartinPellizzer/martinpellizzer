Bulbophyllum careyanum

Bulbophyllum careyanum, commonly known as the Careyan's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, often brightly colored flowers. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, aiding in its survival in tropical environments. Its leaves are typically strap-like and arranged in a rosette pattern.

Bulbophyllum careyanum is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
