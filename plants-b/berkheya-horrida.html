Berkheya horrida

Berkheya horrida, commonly known as the spiked berkheya, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking yellow or orange flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has spiny, hairy stems and leaves, which contribute to its rugged appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Berkheya horrida is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
