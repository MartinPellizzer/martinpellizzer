Byblis guehoi

It is characterized by its drooping, pitcher-shaped leaves that secrete a sticky liquid to trap insects. The plant thrives in wet, acidic soils and requires high humidity and bright, indirect light. Its flowers are small and typically yellow or green, appearing in clusters.
