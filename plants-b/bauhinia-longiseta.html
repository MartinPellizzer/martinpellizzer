Bauhinia longiseta

Bauhinia longiseta, commonly known as the coral tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, flame-like flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant has bipinnate leaves that are often mistaken for ferns due to their delicate, feather-like appearance. Bauhinia longiseta thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

It is also used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
