Buxus Obtusifolia

Buxus obtusifolia is a unique evergreen shrub known for its dense, compact growth and dark green foliage. Native to the mountainous regions of western and central Europe, it thrives in cool, moist climates with well-drained soil. This plant is prized for its ornamental value and ability to form thick hedges or topiaries. Its slow growth rate makes it ideal for shaping and maintenance. Despite its hardiness, it requires careful attention to avoid overwatering and root rot.
