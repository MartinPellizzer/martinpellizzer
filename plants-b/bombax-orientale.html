Bombax orientale

Bombax orientale, commonly known as the silk cotton tree, is a deciduous tree native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from pink to red, which bloom in the spring. The tree produces fluffy, cotton-like seeds enclosed in a large, woody capsule. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a velvety texture.

The plant is valued for its ornamental beauty and has various uses in traditional medicine and as a source of fiber.
