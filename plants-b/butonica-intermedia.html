Butonica intermedia

Butonica intermedia, commonly known as the intermediate buttercup, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It features bright yellow flowers with five petals and a hairy stem, typically growing to a height of about 30-60 cm. The plant has alternate, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows and wetlands.

Butonica intermedia is known for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
