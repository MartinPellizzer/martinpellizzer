Brachylaena discolor var. rotundata

Brachylaena discolor var. rotundata, commonly known as the roundleaf brachylaena, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, rounded foliage and clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. The leaves are typically thick and waxy, adapted to withstand arid conditions. It produces woody, spherical fruits that contain several seeds.

This plant is often used in landscaping due to its ornamental appearance and drought tolerance.
