Bombax

Bombax, commonly known as the silk cotton tree, is a genus of flowering plants in the mallow family, Malvaceae.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and yellow. The tree produces fluffy, cotton-like seeds enclosed in a hard, woody capsule. These seeds are often used in traditional medicine and as a source of fiber.

Bombax species are native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Americas.
