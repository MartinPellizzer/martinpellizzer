Bromus arcticus var. paramushirensis

Bromus arcticus var. paramushirensis, commonly known as the Paramushir grass, is a perennial grass species native to the Russian Far East and the Kuril Islands.

It thrives in cold, temperate climates and is typically found in tundra and alpine habitats. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences with pale blue to greenish flowers. It is adapted to nutrient-poor soils and can tolerate harsh environmental conditions.

Its botanical features make it an important component of the ecological systems in its native range.
