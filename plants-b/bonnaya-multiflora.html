Bonnaya multiflora

Bonnaya multiflora, commonly known as the multibract euphorbia, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It features dense, compact growth with thick, waxy leaves that are often blue-green in color. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish flowers during the summer months. It is known for its ability to thrive in arid conditions and is often used in xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
