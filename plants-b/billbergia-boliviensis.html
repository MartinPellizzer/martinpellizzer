It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Bolivia and Peru. This evergreen plant is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that form a central cup to collect rainwater. It produces striking inflorescences with bright red bracts and yellow flowers, making it a popular ornamental plant.
