Breweria rotundifolia

Breweria rotundifolia, commonly known as the round-leaf brewer's weed, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its rounded, lobed leaves and small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It is often found in coastal areas and is adapted to tolerate salty air and poor soil conditions.

Breweria rotundifolia is a hardy perennial that is sometimes used in ornamental gardening due to its unique foliage.
