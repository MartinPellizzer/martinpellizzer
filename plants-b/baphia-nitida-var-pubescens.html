Baphia nitida var. pubescens

Baphia nitida var. pubescens, commonly known as the velvet tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage, which gives it its common name. The leaves are typically elliptical and have a smooth, waxy texture. It produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually yellow or white in color.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.
