Brunfelsia uniflora f. obovatifolia

Brunfelsia uniflora f. obovatifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf nightshade, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The leaves are elliptical and glossy, with a smooth texture, and the flowers are tubular, often appearing in clusters. It produces striking blue or violet flowers that bloom at night, attracting nocturnal pollinators.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
