Baeckea ochropetala

Baeckea ochropetala, commonly known as the golden baeckea, is a shrub native to Australia.

It features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It prefers well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.

Baeckea ochropetala is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to attract native pollinators.
