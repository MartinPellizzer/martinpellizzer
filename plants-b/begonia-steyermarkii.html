Begonia steyermarkii

Begonia steyermarkii, commonly known as the Steyermark's begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to the highlands of Ecuador.

It is characterized by its vibrant, velvety leaves that often display intricate patterns and shades of green, red, and purple. The plant produces clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers with prominent yellow stamens, which bloom during the warmer months. It prefers shaded, humid environments and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Due to its ornamental value and unique foliage, it is often sought after by plant enthusiasts and collectors.
