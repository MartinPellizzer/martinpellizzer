Begonia bosuangiana

Begonia bosuangiana, commonly known as the Bosuangiana begonia, is a strikingly beautiful flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a glossy surface, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in warm, humid environments and prefers partial to full shade.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and valuable species in botanical collections.
