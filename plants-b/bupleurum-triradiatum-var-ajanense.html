Bupleurum triradiatum var. ajanense

Bupleurum triradiatum var. ajanense, commonly known as the Korean angelica or Korean peony root, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Apiaceae family, characterized by its aromatic foliage and umbellate flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its roots are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.
