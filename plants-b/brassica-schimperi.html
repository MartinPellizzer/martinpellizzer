Brassica schimperi

Brassica schimperi, commonly known as Ethiopian mustard, is a flowering plant native to Ethiopia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes other well-known species like cabbage and broccoli. The plant is characterized by its small, yellow flowers and pinnate leaves. It is often grown for its edible seeds and oil, which are used in local cuisines.

Brassica schimperi is also valued for its potential medicinal properties.
