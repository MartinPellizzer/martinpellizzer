Bulbophyllum rigens

Bulbophyllum rigens, commonly known as the rigid orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, rigid pseudobulbs and elongated, narrow leaves. The plant produces striking, often fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and green, which bloom during the rainy season. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated by enthusiasts for its aesthetic appeal and resilience.
