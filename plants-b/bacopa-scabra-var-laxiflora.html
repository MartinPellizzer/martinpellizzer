Bacopa scabra var. laxiflora

Bacopa scabra var. laxiflora, commonly known as slender leaf bacopa, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the family Plantaginaceae and is characterized by its small, creeping growth habit and opposite leaves.
