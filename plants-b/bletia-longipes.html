Bletia longipes

Bletia longipes, commonly known as the long-stemmed bletia, is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color, with a distinctive hood-like structure. Bletia longipes thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in humid forests.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for botanical gardens and orchid enthusiasts.
