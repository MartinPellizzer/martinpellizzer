Bauhinia longicuspis

Bauhinia longicuspis, commonly known as the "Bauhinia tree" or "Bauhinia longicuspis," is a species of flowering plant in the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Asia, including India, Sri Lanka, and parts of Southeast Asia. The plant is characterized by its distinctive bipinnate leaves and showy, butterfly-like flowers that are typically pink or purple in color.
