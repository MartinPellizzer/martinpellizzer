Boschia oblongifolia

Boschia oblongifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf boschia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in open grasslands and rocky areas, thriving in well-drained soils. The plant features narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. Its small, clustered flowers are usually pink or purple, blooming in the spring.

Boschia oblongifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage and flowering display.
