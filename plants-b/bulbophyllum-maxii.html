Bulbophyllum maxii

Bulbophyllum maxii, commonly known as the Giant Pterostigma Orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that often feature contrasting colors and intricate patterns. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid tropical environments. Its botanical features include pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, supporting its growth in nutrient-poor conditions.

The orchid's unique morphology and vibrant blooms make it a popular specimen among orchid enthusiasts.
