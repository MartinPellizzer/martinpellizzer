Begonia tenera var. thwaitesii

Begonia tenera var. thwaitesii, commonly known as the Thwaites' begonia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a slight reddish tinge on the underside. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers in shades of pink to red, which bloom throughout the growing season. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

This species is popular among collectors for its ornamental foliage and unique flowering habit.
