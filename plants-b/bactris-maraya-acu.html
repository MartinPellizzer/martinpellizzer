Bactris maraya-acu

Bactris maraya-acu, commonly known as the red-fruit palm, is a species native to the Amazon rainforest.

It belongs to the Arecaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate leaves. The plant produces clusters of bright red, fleshy fruits that are edible and have a sweet, tropical flavor. Its botanical features make it an important plant in both ecological and traditional contexts.
