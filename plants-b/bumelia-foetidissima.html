Bumelia foetidissima

Bumelia foetidissima, commonly known as stinkwood, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its rough, fissured bark and opposite, elliptical leaves. The species is valued for its hard, heavy wood, which has been used historically for various purposes.
