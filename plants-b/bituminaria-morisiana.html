Bituminaria morisiana

Bituminaria morisiana, commonly known as the Morisian bituminous plant, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers and hairy stems. Its leaves are compound, consisting of several small leaflets, and it produces small, pod-like fruits that contain seeds.
