Bulbophyllum belonaeglossum

Bulbophyllum belonaeglossum, commonly known as the Dragon's Blood Orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, dark red to purple inflorescences that resemble flowing blood, hence its common name. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid tropical environments. Its flowers are often fragrant and attract various pollinators, including bees and birds.

The orchid's unique appearance and rarity make it a prized specimen among orchid enthusiasts and botanists.
