Billbergia meyendorffii

Billbergia meyendorffii, commonly known as the Meyer's billbergia, is a striking bromeliad native to South America.

It features vibrant, strap-like leaves that form a rosette, creating a water-holding cup at the center. The plant produces tall, branching inflorescences with showy, long-lasting flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This species is popular in ornamental horticulture for its ornate appearance and relatively low maintenance.
