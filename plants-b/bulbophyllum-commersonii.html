Bulbophyllum commersonii

Bulbophyllum commersonii, commonly known as the Commerson's orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to tropical regions of South America.

It is a epiphytic plant, meaning it grows on the surface of other plants without deriving nutrients from them. The plant produces intricate, showy flowers that are typically yellow or orange with contrasting purple or red markings. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, enabling it to survive in humid, shaded environments.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and adaptability to various growing conditions.
