Bromheadia crassiflora

Bromheadia crassiflora, commonly known as the thick-flowered bromheadia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

Bromheadia crassiflora is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
