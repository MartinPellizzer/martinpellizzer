Barleria stellatotomentosa

Barleria stellatotomentosa, commonly known as the star-tomentose barleria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, often in arid or semi-arid regions. The plant features slender, branching stems covered in soft, white hairs, giving it a fuzzy appearance. Its small, tubular flowers are usually pale blue or purple, arranged in clusters at the ends of the stems.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate foliage and blooms.
