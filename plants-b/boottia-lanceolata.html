Boottia lanceolata

Boottia lanceolata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf boottia, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that typically reaches heights of about 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features slender, lance-shaped leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs. Its small, clustered flowers are typically pink or white, blooming in the summer months.

Boottia lanceolata thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.
