Bidens lindleyi

Bidens lindleyi, commonly known as the yellow false dandelion, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are surrounded by green bracts, giving it a dandelion-like appearance. Bidens lindleyi typically grows in moist, open habitats such as grasslands and wetlands.
