Bulbostylis glabriuscula

Bulbostylis glabriuscula, commonly known as the smooth-styled bulbostylis, is a small, herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

Its flowers are small and clustered in spikelets, which are typical of the Cyperaceae family.

Bulbostylis glabriuscula is often found in disturbed habitats and can be an indicator species for wetland ecosystems.
