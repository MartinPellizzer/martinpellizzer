Bulbophyllum latipetalum

Bulbophyllum latipetalum, commonly known as the broad-petaled orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its long, narrow leaves and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments and has a complex root system that helps it absorb nutrients from the soil. Its flowers are usually pale yellow or greenish-white, with a distinctive lip that is broader than the other petals.

Bulbophyllum latipetalum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
