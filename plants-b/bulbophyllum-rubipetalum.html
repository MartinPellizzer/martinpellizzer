Bulbophyllum rubipetalum

Bulbophyllum rubipetalum, commonly known as the red-petaled orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, velvety red petals. The plant is epiphytic, meaning it grows on the surface of trees or other structures without deriving nutrients from them. Its flowers are typically small and clustered, with a distinctive coloration that ranges from deep red to orange.

Bulbophyllum rubipetalum thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining substrates for optimal growth.
