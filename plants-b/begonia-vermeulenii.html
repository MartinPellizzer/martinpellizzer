Begonia vermeulenii

Begonia vermeulenii, commonly known as the Vermeulen's begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, velvety foliage with intricate patterns of green, red, and purple. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of white and pink, which bloom during the cooler months. It thrives in partial shade and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.
