Bauhinia hermesiana

Bauhinia hermesiana, commonly known as the Hermes' bauhinia, is a flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its distinctive bipinnate leaves. The plant produces small, showy flowers that are typically pink or white, arranged in clusters. Bauhinia hermesiana is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to thrive in arid conditions.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for cultivation in gardens and landscapes.
