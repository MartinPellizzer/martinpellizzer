Begonia obdeltata

Begonia obdeltata, commonly known as the shield leaf begonia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating, giving them a shiny appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and prefers well-draining soil.

Begonia obdeltata is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its striking foliage and ornamental flowers.
