Bulbostylis holotricha

Bulbostylis holotricha, commonly known as the hairy bulbostylis, is a small, grass-like plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Poaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and hairy foliage. The plant produces tiny, spike-like inflorescences that bear small, white to pale blue flowers. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or swampy areas.

Due to its delicate structure and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and specialized species.
