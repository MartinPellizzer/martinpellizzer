Berberis henryi

Berberis henryi, commonly known as Chinese barberry, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features spiny branches and small, oval-shaped leaves that turn yellow in the fall. The plant produces yellow flowers in early spring, followed by red berries that are edible but often bitter. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a hardy ornamental plant.

Its ornamental value and ability to thrive in various climates make it a popular choice for garden use.
