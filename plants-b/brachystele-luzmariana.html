Brachystele luzmariana

Brachystele luzmariana, commonly known as the Luzmarana staghorn fern, is a unique epiphytic plant native to the tropical rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive, tree-like appearance with a thick, fleshy base and a crown of lush, overlapping leaves. The plant's rhizomes form a dense, cushion-like structure that supports its growth on trees or rocks. Its leaves are typically leathery and can vary in color from dark green to almost black.

Brachystele luzmariana is well adapted to humid, shaded environments and requires high levels of moisture and indirect light to thrive.
