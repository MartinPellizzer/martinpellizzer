Bauhinia lilacina

Bauhinia lilacina, commonly known as the pink bauhinia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, bipinnate leaves and clusters of pink to lilac-colored flowers that bloom in spring. The plant thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in moist, well-drained soils. Its ornamental value is enhanced by its showy inflorescences and ability to tolerate partial shade.

Bauhinia lilacina is also valued for its use in traditional medicine and as a ornamental garden plant.
