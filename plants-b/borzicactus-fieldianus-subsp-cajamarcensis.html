Borzicactus fieldianus subsp. cajamarcensis

Borzicactus fieldianus subsp.

cajamarcensis, commonly known as the Cajamarca borzicactus, is a species of cactus native to Peru. It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its cylindrical stem and spiny areoles.

The plant typically grows in arid and semi-arid environments, adapting to harsh conditions with its succulent structure. It produces yellow flowers during the dry season, adding a striking visual element to its habitat.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat threats, it is considered a rare and potentially endangered species.
