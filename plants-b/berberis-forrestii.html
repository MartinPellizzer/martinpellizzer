Berberis forrestii

Berberis forrestii, commonly known as the Chinese barberry, is a deciduous shrub native to the mountainous regions of China.

It features compound leaves with three to five oval-shaped leaflets, and its small, bright yellow flowers appear in early spring. The plant produces red, berry-like fruits that are edible but often have a sharp, acidic taste.

Berberis forrestii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping for its vibrant foliage and hardy nature.
