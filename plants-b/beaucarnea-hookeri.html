Beaucarnea hookeri

Beaucarnea hookeri, commonly known as the ponytail palm, is a succulent plant native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its swollen, bottle-shaped stem that stores water, allowing it to survive in arid conditions. The plant has a rosette of narrow, gray-green leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. It produces clusters of small, white flowers that bloom in the spring.

Due to its low maintenance and unique look, it is a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardening.
