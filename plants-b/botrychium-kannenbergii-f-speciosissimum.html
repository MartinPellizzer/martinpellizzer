Botrychium kannenbergii f. speciosissimum

Botrychium kannenbergii f. speciosissimum, commonly known as the showy moonwort, is a fern species belonging to the family Onocleaceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive, spore-bearing leaves that resemble small, clustered umbels, giving it its common name. This fern is notable for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in shaded gardens due to its unique appearance.
