Bupleurum stellatum var. typicum

Bupleurum stellatum var. typicum, commonly known as the starflower, is a perennial herb native to the eastern United States.

It features delicate, star-shaped white to pale blue flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has finely divided, fern-like leaves that are often described as lace-like in appearance. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Bupleurum stellatum var. typicum is often found in woodland areas and is valued for its ornamental beauty and ecological benefits.
