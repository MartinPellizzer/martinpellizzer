Barbacenia lilacina var. lilacina

Barbacenia lilacina var. lilacina, commonly known as the Purple Passion Vine, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking violet to purple flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting a variety of pollinators such as hummingbirds and butterflies. The plant has heart-shaped, velvety leaves that are often tinged with silver or gray, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Barbacenia lilacina var. lilacina is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant color and ecological benefits.
