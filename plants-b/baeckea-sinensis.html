Baeckea sinensis

Baeckea sinensis, commonly known as the Chinese baeckea, is a flowering plant native to eastern Asia.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces small, white or pinkish flowers that are clustered in dense, spherical inflorescences. Its leaves are opposite, elliptical, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Baeckea sinensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is found in various gardens and botanical collections.
