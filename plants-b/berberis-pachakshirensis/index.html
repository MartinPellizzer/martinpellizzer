Berberis Pachakshirensis

Berberis pachakshirensis is a rare and fascinating plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Peruvian Andes. This shrub belongs to the Berberidaceae family and is known for its striking yellow flowers and spiny foliage. Its discovery in 2015 sparked significant interest among botanists and conservationists. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and is adapted to the harsh conditions of its mountainous habitat. Due to its limited distribution and unique characteristics, it is considered a valuable species for ecological and scientific study.
