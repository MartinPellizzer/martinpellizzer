Begonia buchtienii

Begonia buchtienii, commonly known as the Buchtien's begonia, is a tropical plant native to the island of Borneo.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves that often display a mix of green and red hues. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of pink and white, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and valuable species in botanical collections.
