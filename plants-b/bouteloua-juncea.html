Bouteloua juncea

Bouteloua juncea, commonly known as gray dropseed, is a perennial grass native to North America.

It typically grows in open habitats such as prairies, roadsides, and disturbed soils. The plant has fine, silver-gray foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. It produces small, fluffy seeds that are dispersed by wind, aiding in its spread.

Bouteloua juncea is often used in native plant gardens for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
