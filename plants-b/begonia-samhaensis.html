Begonia samhaensis

Begonia samhaensis, commonly known as the Samha begonia, is a flowering plant native to Ethiopia.

It belongs to the Begoniaceae family and is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves and vibrant, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often found in forest understories. Its foliage is often adorned with intricate patterns and can display a range of colors, including red, purple, and green.

Due to its ornamental value and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and sought-after species among horticulturists.
