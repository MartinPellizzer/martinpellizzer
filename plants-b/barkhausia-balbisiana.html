Barkhausia balbisiana

Barkhausia balbisiana, commonly known as the Balbisian orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant features elongated, strap-like leaves and produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange. Its botanical significance lies in its unique floral structure and its role in local ecosystems.

It is also valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.
