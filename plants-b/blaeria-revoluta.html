Blaeria revoluta

Blaeria revoluta, commonly known as the curled blazer, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, gray-green leaves that are often curled or revolute, giving it its common name. It produces small, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late summer.

Blaeria revoluta is often found in rocky soils and is adapted to dry, sunny environments.
