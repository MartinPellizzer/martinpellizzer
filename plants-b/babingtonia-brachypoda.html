Babingtonia brachypoda

Babingtonia brachypoda, commonly known as the dwarf babingtonia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 meter. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters during the winter months. Its leaves are simple, elliptical, and have a smooth, waxy texture.

Babingtonia brachypoda is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
