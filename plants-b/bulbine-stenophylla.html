Bulbine stenophylla

Bulbine stenophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leaf bulbine, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, narrow leaves that store water, enabling it to thrive in arid conditions. The plant produces yellow flowers during the summer months, adding a vibrant touch to its habitat. It is often found in rocky outcrops and sandy soils, where it forms dense clumps.

Its resilience and adaptability make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
