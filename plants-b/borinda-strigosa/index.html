Borinda Strigosa

Borinda strigosa is a unique flowering plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa. It is known for its striking appearance and ability to thrive in harsh environments. This species belongs to the Apocynaceae family, which includes many toxic plants. Its small, tubular flowers come in shades of pink and white, attracting pollinators like bees and birds. Despite its beauty, Borinda strigosa is often overlooked in favor of more common garden plants.
