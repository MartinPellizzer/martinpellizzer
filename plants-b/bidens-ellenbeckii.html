Bidens ellenbeckii

Bidens ellenbeckii, commonly known as the Ellenbeck's ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features erect, hairy stems and produces small, yellow flowers with dark centers. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and have a rough texture.

Bidens ellenbeckii is often found in moist, open habitats such as wetlands and roadsides.
