Begonia lokobeensis

Begonia lokobeensis, commonly known as the Lokobe begonia, is a species native to the humid forests of Cameroon.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its striking, velvety foliage and vibrant floral displays. The leaves are typically heart-shaped with a waxy texture, and the flowers come in shades of red, pink, and white. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in the understory of tropical rainforests.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists alike.
