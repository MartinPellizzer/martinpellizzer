Barbeya

Barbeya, commonly known as the barbeyas, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Fabaceae.

These plants are native to various regions of Europe, North Africa, and the Middle East. They are typically shrubs or small trees, characterized by their trifoliate leaves and pod-like fruits. Barbeya species often have yellow or white flowers that bloom in spring or early summer.

The genus includes several species, some of which are used in traditional medicine or as ornamental plants.
