Berberis hochreutineriana

Berberis hochreutineriana, commonly known as the Hochreutiner barberry, is a shrub native to the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and pinnately compound leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers in early spring, followed by red or orange berries in the fall. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or mountainous regions.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden planting in temperate climates.
