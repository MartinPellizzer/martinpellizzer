Begonia × ottoniana

Begonia × ottoniana, commonly known as the velvetleaf begonia, is a hybrid species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, velvety leaves that are often dark green with a slight metallic sheen. The plant produces clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in warm, humid conditions and prefers partial shade to full sun.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardens and as a houseplant.
