Berberis yalongensis

Berberis yalongensis, commonly known as the Yalong barberry, is a shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Berberidaceae family and is characterized by its spiny stems and compound leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers in early spring, followed by red berries that are edible but often considered bitter. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in rocky or mountainous areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
