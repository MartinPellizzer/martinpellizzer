Begonia melikopia

Begonia melikopia, commonly known as the Melikopia begonia, is a species native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically come in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant has heart-shaped or ovate leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and prefers well-draining soil.

Begonia melikopia is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
