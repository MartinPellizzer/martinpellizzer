Bromheadia pendek

Bromheadia pendek, commonly known as the hanging bromheadia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Bromheadiaceae and is characterized by its pendulous inflorescences. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as tropical forests. Its leaves are usually broad and elliptical, with a smooth surface.

Bromheadia pendek is valued in horticulture for its ornamental flowers and unique growth habit.
