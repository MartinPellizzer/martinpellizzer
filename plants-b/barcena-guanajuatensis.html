Barcena guanajuatensis

Barcena guanajuatensis, commonly known as the Guanajuato barcena, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its foliage consists of narrow, linear leaves that help reduce water loss.
