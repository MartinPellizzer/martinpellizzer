Biancaea decapetala

Biancaea decapetala, commonly known as the pink fairy tern, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has slender, branching stems and small, oval-shaped leaves. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in coastal or inland areas.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
