Bauhinia guianensis var. kunthiana

Bauhinia guianensis var. kunthiana, commonly known as the pink bauhinia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive, bipinnate leaves and showy, pink or purple flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and full sun.

Bauhinia guianensis var. kunthiana is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
