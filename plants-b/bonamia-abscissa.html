Bonamia abscissa

Bonamia abscissa, commonly known as the bluebonnet, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

The plant typically grows in open woods, fields, and along roadsides, reaching heights of up to 2 feet. Bonamia abscissa is known for its early spring blooming period, making it a notable wildflower in its native habitat.
