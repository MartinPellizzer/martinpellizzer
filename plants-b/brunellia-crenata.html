Brunellia crenata

Brunellia crenata, commonly known as the serrated evergreen.

It is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil and Peru. The plant is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves with serrated edges. It produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences.

Brunellia crenata is often found in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests and along riverbanks.
