Ballota sicula

Ballota sicula, commonly known as Sicilian ballota, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its small, yellowish flowers and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces small, hairy pods that contain several seeds. Ballota sicula is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to dry, sunny environments.

It has been historically used in traditional herbal medicine for its potential health benefits.
