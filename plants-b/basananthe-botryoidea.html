Basananthe botryoidea

Basananthe botryoidea, commonly known as the clustered banana orchid, is a rare and unique orchid species native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, clustered inflorescence that resembles a bunch of bananas, giving it its common name. The plant features elongated, narrow leaves that are typically green with a waxy texture. Its flowers are usually pale yellow or greenish-white, with intricate patterns that attract specific pollinators.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a conservation concern.
