Buda macrotheca

Buda macrotheca, commonly known as the large-flowered buda, is a unique member of the Gesneriaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Thailand and Vietnam. The plant is characterized by its striking, large, showy flowers that can reach up to 10 centimeters in diameter. Its foliage consists of glossy, dark green leaves with a slightly waxy texture.

Buda macrotheca is often cultivated for its ornamental value in both indoor and outdoor gardens.
