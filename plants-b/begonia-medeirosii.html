Begonia medeirosii

Begonia medeirosii, commonly known as the Medeiros begonia, is a rare and striking plant native to the mountainous regions of Brazil.

It is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves and clusters of vibrant red flowers that bloom in spring. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its unique appearance makes it a sought-after specimen among collectors and botanists.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered endangered and is often cultivated in specialized greenhouses.
