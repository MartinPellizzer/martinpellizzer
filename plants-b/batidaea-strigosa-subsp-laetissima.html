Batidaea strigosa subsp. laetissima

Batidaea strigosa subsp.

laetissima, commonly known as the cheerful star-leaf, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region. It features small, star-shaped leaves with a distinctive silver-gray underside, which helps reflect sunlight and reduce water loss.

The plant produces delicate, pinkish-white flowers during the summer months, adding ornamental value to gardens. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its low maintenance and drought tolerance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
