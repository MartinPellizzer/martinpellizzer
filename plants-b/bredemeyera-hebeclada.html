Bredemeyera hebeclada

Bredemeyera hebeclada, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its broad, velvety leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink, often blooming during the warmer months. Bredemeyera hebeclada thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.
