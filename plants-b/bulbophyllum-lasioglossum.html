Bulbophyllum lasioglossum

Bulbophyllum lasioglossum, commonly known as the velvet lip orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, velvety purple flowers with contrasting yellow lips. The plant typically grows on tree branches or rocks, thriving in humid, shaded environments. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, aiding in its survival in tropical climates.

The orchid's unique floral structure and coloration make it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
