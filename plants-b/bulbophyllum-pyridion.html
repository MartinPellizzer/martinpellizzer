Bulbophyllum pyridion

Bulbophyllum pyridion, commonly known as the Pyridion orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its intricate, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in tropical rainforests. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, allowing it to survive in humid, shaded environments.

The flowers are usually small, with a complex structure that includes showy petals and sepals.
