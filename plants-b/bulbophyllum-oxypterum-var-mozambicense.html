Bulbophyllum oxypterum var. mozambicense

Bulbophyllum oxypterum var. mozambicense, commonly known as the Mozambique Bulbophyllum, is a species of orchid native to eastern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and features lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of white or pale green pubescence. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and green, which are typically borne on terminal inflorescences. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found growing on trees or rocks in its natural habitat.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
