Bulbophyllum kewense

Bulbophyllum kewense, commonly known as the Kew's orchid, is a species of orchid native to the rainforests of Borneo.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments with well-drained soil. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers that are often a mix of white, green, and yellow hues. Its inflorescence is a raceme of elongated, slender spikes, and the flowers are notable for their unique structure and coloration.

Bulbophyllum kewense is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its aesthetic beauty.
