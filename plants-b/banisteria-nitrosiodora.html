Banisteria nitrosiodora

Banisteria nitrosiodora, commonly known as the pink banisteria, is a climbing plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the family Fabaceae, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its compound leaves and distinctive floral structures. Its seeds are enclosed in pods and can be dispersed by wind or animals.

Banisteria nitrosiodora is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses due to its attractive appearance and adaptability to warm climates.
