Blastus fengii

Blastus fengii, commonly known as the Chinese bamboo orchid, is a unique member of the orchid family, Orchidaceae, native to China.

It is characterized by its striking, bamboo-like stems that are often used in traditional Chinese medicine. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, which bloom during the spring season. Its leaves are long and narrow, arranged in a rosette pattern, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, Blastus fengii is considered a conservation concern in its native region.
