Bureavella

Bureavella, commonly known as the golden bell vine, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, bell-shaped yellow flowers that hang in clusters. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and thrives in warm, humid climates. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and can climb or trail along structures.

Bureavella prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
