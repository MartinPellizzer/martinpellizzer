Berberis ganpinensis

Berberis ganpinensis, commonly known as the Yunnan barberry, is a shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Berberidaceae family and is characterized by its thorny stems and clustered leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers in spring, followed by red or orange berries in the fall. It is often found in rocky or mountainous areas, thriving in well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in temperate regions.
