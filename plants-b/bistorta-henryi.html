Bistorta henryi

Bistorta henryi, commonly known as the Henry's bistort, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of central Asia.

Bistorta henryi thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in alpine meadows and rocky slopes.
