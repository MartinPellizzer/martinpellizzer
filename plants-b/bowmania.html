Bowmania

Bowmania, commonly known as the sacred lotus, is a striking aquatic plant native to Asia.

It features large, showy flowers that bloom in shades of pink, red, and white, often with a distinctive yellow or orange center. The plant has broad, heart-shaped leaves that float on the water's surface, while its roots anchor in the muddy bottom. Bowmania is known for its ability to thrive in still or slow-moving freshwater environments.

Its symbolic significance in various cultures adds to its botanical and cultural importance.
