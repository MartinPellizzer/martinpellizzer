Begonia sandsiana

Begonia sandsiana, commonly known as the sand begonia, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically range in color from red to pink. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with a smooth texture and a slightly waxy surface. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

Begonia sandsiana is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
