Bidens floribunda

Bidens floribunda, commonly known as the common ironweed, is an annual flowering plant native to the Americas.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy yellow flowers with dark central discs. The plant typically grows in moist to wet habitats, including marshes and riverbanks. Its leaves are alternate, heart-shaped, and have a rough texture.

Bidens floribunda is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in disturbed soils.
