Bifrenaria harrisoniae var. purpurascens

Bifrenaria harrisoniae var. purpurascens, commonly known as the purple-flowered harrisonia, is a rare and striking orchid native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant purple petals and sepals, which contrast beautifully with its green, velvety leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters, typically during the wet season. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil.

Due to its rarity and unique appearance, it is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts and conservationists alike.
