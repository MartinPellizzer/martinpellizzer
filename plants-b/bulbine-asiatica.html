Bulbine asiatica

Bulbine asiatica, commonly known as the African snowflake, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features a short, thick stem and produces clusters of white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has fleshy, rhombic leaves that store water, enabling it to survive in arid conditions. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its striking appearance and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
