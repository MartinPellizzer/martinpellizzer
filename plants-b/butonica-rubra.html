Butonica rubra

Butonica rubra, commonly known as the red-flowered bellwether, is a flowering plant native to the temperate regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its striking red, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters and prefers well-drained soils in partial shade. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.

Butonica rubra is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for.
