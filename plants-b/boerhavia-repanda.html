Boerhavia repanda

Boerhavia repanda, commonly known as redroot sandplum, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is a low-growing perennial shrub that typically reaches heights of about 30 centimeters. The plant features small, pink to red flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. Its leaves are ovate and covered in fine hairs, and it thrives in sandy or well-drained soils.

Boerhavia repanda is often found in disturbed areas and is adapted to arid environments.
