Bersama abyssinica var. holstii

Bersama abyssinica var. holstii, commonly known as the Abyssinian sandmat, is a flowering plant native to the highlands of Ethiopia.

It is a low-growing perennial that features succulent, fleshy leaves and produces small, clustered yellow flowers. The plant is adapted to arid and rocky environments, showcasing a robust root system for water retention. Its leaves are often covered in a fine, powdery coating, which helps reduce water loss.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in dry gardens for its unique appearance.
