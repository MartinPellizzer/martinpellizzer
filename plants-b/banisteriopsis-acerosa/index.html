Banisteriopsis Acerosa

Banisteriopsis acerosa is a lesser-known but fascinating plant species native to the rainforests of South America. It belongs to the Malvaceae family, which includes cotton and hibiscus, and is known for its unique physical characteristics. Despite its ecological importance, it remains under-researched and overlooked in mainstream botanical discussions.
