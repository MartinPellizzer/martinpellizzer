Blechnum plagiogyriifrons

Blechnum plagiogyriifrons, commonly known as the spiral fern, is a species of fern native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the family Blechnaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, spirally arranged fronds. The plant has a creeping rhizome and produces small, clustered sporangia on the underside of its leaves. Its fronds are typically lanceolate and have a leathery texture.

This fern thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
