Begonia tonduzii

Begonia tonduzii, commonly known as the Tonduzii begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from red to pink, often with intricate patterns. The plant features heart-shaped leaves that are typically glossy and deep green, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique appearance and limited natural range, it is often cultivated as a rare and exotic houseplant.
