Bunium bulbocastanum var. bulbocastanum

Bunium bulbocastanum var. bulbocastanum, commonly known as the bulbous fennel, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features a basal rosette of narrow, silvery-green leaves with a strong anise-like aroma. The plant produces tall, branched flowering stems that bear small, yellow flowers arranged in umbels. Its bulbous root system is thick and fleshy, often used in traditional medicine.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to dry, sunny environments.
