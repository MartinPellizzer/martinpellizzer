Bulbophyllum hoyifolium

Bulbophyllum hoyifolium, commonly known as the Hoyi leaf orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, often elongated leaves that are typically fleshy and adapted for storing water. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually white or pale yellow, arranged in a loose inflorescence. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
