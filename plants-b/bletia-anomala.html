Bletia anomala

Bletia anomala, commonly known as the dwarf orchid or small-leaf orchid, is a species of orchid native to tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is a terrestrial herb with a short, creeping rhizome and produces small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions.

Bletia anomala is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
