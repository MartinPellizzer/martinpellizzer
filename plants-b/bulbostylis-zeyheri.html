Bulbostylis zeyheri

Bulbostylis zeyheri, commonly known as the Zeyher's bulbostylis, is a small, herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the grass family (Poaceae) and is characterized by its clustered, grass-like appearance.
