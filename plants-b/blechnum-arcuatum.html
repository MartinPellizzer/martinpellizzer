Blechnum arcuatum

Blechnum arcuatum, commonly known as the curved maidenhair fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Blechnaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, arching fronds that are typically light green with a slightly glossy surface. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit, with creeping underground stems that allow it to spread gradually. Native to parts of Asia, including China and Japan, it thrives in shaded, moist environments.

Its unique morphology and ornamental appearance make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and conservation efforts.
