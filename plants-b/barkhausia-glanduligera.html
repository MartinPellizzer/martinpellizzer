Barkhausia glanduligera

Barkhausia glanduligera, commonly known as the glandular barkhausia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, glandular-pubescent stems and leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers arranged in a dense, spherical inflorescence. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological role, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists.
