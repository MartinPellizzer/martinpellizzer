Blitum virgatum subsp. virgatum

Blitum virgatum subsp.

virgatum, commonly known as slender whitetop, is a flowering plant native to North America. It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads.

The plant typically grows in moist to wet habitats, such as marshes and stream banks. It features slender, erect stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves.

Its flowers are small and yellowish, arranged in clusters that bloom in late spring to early summer.
