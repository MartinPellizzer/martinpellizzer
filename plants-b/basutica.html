Basutica

Basutica, commonly known as the Basutic plant, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers. The plant is characterized by its small, clustered yellow flowers and hairy, narrow leaves. It thrives in dry, rocky environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Basutica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
