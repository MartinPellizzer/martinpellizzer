Bulbophyllum ningboense

Bulbophyllum ningboense, commonly known as the Ningbo orchid, is a species of orchid native to China.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its unique and intricate floral structure. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically pale green to yellow in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forested areas.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered an endangered species.
