Bactris rivularis

Bactris rivularis, commonly known as the river palm, is a species of palm native to Central and South America.

It is a small to medium-sized tree that typically grows in wetlands and riverine habitats. The plant has a slender trunk and produces clusters of small, yellowish flowers. Its leaves are pinnately compound, with long, narrow leaflets.

Bactris rivularis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its ability to thrive in moist environments.
