Berchemia pubiflora

Berchemia pubiflora, commonly known as the woolly berchemia, is a deciduous shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its hairy, silver-green leaves that give it a distinctive, fuzzy appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in spring, followed by orange-red, fleshy fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

Berchemia pubiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
