Begonia rubrosetosa

Begonia rubrosetosa, commonly known as the red-bristled begonia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking red bristles on the stems and leaves, which give it its common name. The plant produces vibrant pink to red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and prefers well-drained soil.

Begonia rubrosetosa is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and unique appearance.
