Bougueria nubicola

Bougueria nubicola, commonly known as the African violets, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of purple, pink, and white, often with a contrasting central stripe. Its leaves are velvety and heart-shaped, contributing to its ornamental appeal.
