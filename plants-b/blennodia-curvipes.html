Blennodia curvipes

Blennodia curvipes, commonly known as the spiral horned orchid, is a rare and striking orchid species native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique spiral-shaped inflorescence, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, often with intricate patterns on the petals. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires specific soil conditions to flourish.

Due to its rarity and habitat specificity, Blennodia curvipes is considered an endangered species.
