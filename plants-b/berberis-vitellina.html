Berberis vitellina

Berberis vitellina, commonly known as the golden barberry, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features bright yellow foliage in the fall, which makes it a popular ornamental plant. The plant has compound leaves with three to five leaflets and produces small, orange-red berries in the fall. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for garden landscapes.

Its hardy nature allows it to adapt to various climates, enhancing its ornamental value.
