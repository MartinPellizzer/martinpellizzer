Buphthalmum crassifolium

Buphthalmum crassifolium, commonly known as the broadleaf oxeye, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy yellow flowers with dark centers, which bloom in late summer. The plant has broad, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a fine, white pubescence. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open grasslands and along roadsides.
