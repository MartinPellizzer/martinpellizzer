Berberis hsuyunensis

Berberis hsuyunensis, commonly known as the Hsuyun barberry, is a species of shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Berberidaceae family and is characterized by its spiny stems and compound leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in the spring, followed by red berries that are edible but not commonly consumed by humans.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its attractive foliage and hardy nature.
