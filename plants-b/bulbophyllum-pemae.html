Bulbophyllum pemae

Bulbophyllum pemae, commonly known as the Pemae orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in tropical rainforests. Its inflorescence consists of multiple flowers arranged in a raceme, with bright colors that attract pollinators.

The leaves are usually leathery and adapted to retain moisture in its natural habitat.
