Bulbophyllum rubrolabium

Bulbophyllum rubrolabium, commonly known as the red-labied orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, vividly colored flowers. The plant produces intricate, spurred flowers with a distinctive red labellum, which is a specialized petal structure. It typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid tropical environments.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts and horticulturists.
