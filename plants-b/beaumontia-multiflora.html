Beaumontia multiflora

Beaumontia multiflora, commonly known as the Chinese trumpet tree, is a flowering plant native to southern China and Southeast Asia.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height. The plant is characterized by its clusters of fragrant, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in spring. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and have a smooth texture.

Beaumontia multiflora is valued for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its showy blooms.
