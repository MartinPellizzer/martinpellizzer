Bouchea rusbyi

Bouchea rusbyi, commonly known as the Rusby's bouchea, is a striking evergreen shrub native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its dense, rounded form and its showy, cone-shaped flower clusters that bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant features narrow, dark green leaves and is well adapted to arid and semi-arid climates. Bouchea rusbyi is prized in landscaping for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.

It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for xeriscaping.
