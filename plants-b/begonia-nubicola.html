Begonia nubicola

Begonia nubicola, commonly known as the cloud begonia, is a strikingly beautiful plant native to the mountainous regions of East Africa.

It is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves and clusters of delicate, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in the early morning. The plant thrives in cool, humid environments and is often found growing in shaded, rocky areas. Its unique foliage and floral display make it a popular choice for indoor gardening and botanical collections.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species in the wild.
