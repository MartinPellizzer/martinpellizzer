Bletia mixtecana

Bletia mixtecana, commonly known as the Mixtec orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, lance-shaped leaves and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist habitats such as cloud forests. Its flowers, which range in color from white to pink, bloom in late spring to early summer.

Bletia mixtecana is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
