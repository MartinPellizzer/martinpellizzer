Bidens maximowicziana

Bidens maximowicziana, commonly known as the yellow oxeye.

It is a flowering plant native to Central and South America, characterized by its bright yellow flowers with dark centers. The plant has opposite, hairy leaves and produces small, spherical fruits that contain seeds with pappus for wind dispersal. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in open areas such as roadsides and fields.

Its rapid growth and adaptability make it an invasive species in some regions.
