Blechnum gregsonii

Blechnum gregsonii, commonly known as the New Zealand maidenhair fern, is a species of fern native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the family Blechnaceae and is characterized by its finely divided, feathery fronds. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit, with creeping underground stems that allow it to spread and form dense colonies. Its leaves are typically bright green and have a delicate, lacy appearance.

Blechnum gregsonii thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
