Bignonia roezliana

Bignonia roezliana, commonly known as the golden shower vine, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its long, pendulous clusters of yellow-orange flowers that bloom throughout the warmer months. The plant belongs to the family Bignoniaceae and is known for its rapid growth and climbing habit. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a velvety texture.

Bignonia roezliana is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its showy flowers and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
