Brachyscome muelleroides

Brachyscome muelleroides, commonly known as the silver frost or white daisy, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers with white petals and a yellow center. The plant produces small, fluffy seeds that are dispersed by the wind, aiding in its propagation. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to bloom throughout the year in suitable climates.
