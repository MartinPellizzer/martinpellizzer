Bromus mollis var. leptostachys

Bromus mollis var. leptostachys, commonly known as the slender spike grass, is a perennial grass species native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and narrow, blue-green leaves. The plant produces dense, spike-like inflorescences with small, pale blue to purple flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open grasslands and disturbed sites.

Its botanical features make it an important component of native plant communities in its range.
