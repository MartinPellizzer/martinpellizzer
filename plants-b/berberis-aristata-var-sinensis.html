Berberis aristata var. sinensis

Berberis aristata var. sinensis, commonly known as Chinese barberry, is a shrub native to the mountainous regions of China and Nepal.

It features thorny stems and small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically dark green in color. The plant produces yellow flowers in the spring, followed by red berries that are edible but often used more for ornamental purposes. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often cultivated for its hardiness and attractive appearance.

The plant contains berberine, a compound with potential medicinal properties.
