Bulbine altissima

Bulbine altissima, commonly known as the tall bulbine, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features succulent, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, often blooming in summer. It is adapted to arid environments and is drought-tolerant, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

The species is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
