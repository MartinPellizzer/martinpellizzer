Buddleja hatschbachii

Buddleja hatschbachii, commonly known as the Tibetan butterfly bush, is a deciduous shrub native to the Himalayas.

It features striking, fragrant, tubular flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting a variety of pollinators. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and slightly hairy. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Buddleja hatschbachii is valued for its ornamental value and its ability to thrive in a range of garden conditions.
