Blechnopsis stenophylla

Blechnopsis stenophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leaf harts-tongue fern, is a member of the Blechnaceae family.

It is a perennial evergreen fern characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often found in temperate regions. Its rhizomes are creeping and produce clustered, spore-bearing leaves.

The fronds are usually dark green and have a leathery texture, contributing to its ornamental value in gardens.
