Bulbophyllum melilotus

Bulbophyllum melilotus, commonly known as the Melilotus Orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, pseudobulbous structure and produces clusters of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest undergrowth. Its leaves are elongated and grass-like, while the roots are fibrous and adapted for nutrient absorption.

This orchid is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
