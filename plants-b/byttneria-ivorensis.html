Byttneria ivorensis

Byttneria ivorensis, commonly known as the Ivory Coast sycamore, is a tropical tree native to West Africa.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and showy yellow flowers. The plant produces woody capsules that contain numerous small seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in lowland rainforests.
