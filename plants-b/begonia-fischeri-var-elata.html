Begonia fischeri var. elata

Begonia fischeri var. elata, commonly known as the slender begonia, is a species native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and clusters of small, showy flowers that range in color from pink to red. The leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a velvety texture, adding to its ornamental appeal. This plant prefers well-draining soil and partial shade, making it suitable for hanging baskets or as a ground cover.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor gardening.
