Billbergia bakeri var. straussiana

Billbergia bakeri var. straussiana, commonly known as the Strauss's billbergia, is a striking bromeliad native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its tall, upright stem and a rosette of long, narrow, dark green leaves that are often adorned with white or silver markings. The plant produces striking inflorescences with vibrant red and orange flowers that emerge from the center of the rosette. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in tropical gardens.
