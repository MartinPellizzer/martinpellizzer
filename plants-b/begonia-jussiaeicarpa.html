Begonia jussiaeicarpa

Begonia jussiaeicarpa, commonly known as the Jussiaeicarpa begonia, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a glossy surface and a dense, fibrous root system. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for shaded garden environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in horticulture for its aesthetic appeal.
