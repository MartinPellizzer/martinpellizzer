Bulbine cremnophila

Bulbine cremnophila, commonly known as the rock bulbine, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, ribbed leaves that store water, adapting it to arid environments. The plant produces bright yellow flowers during the summer months, adding a striking visual element to its habitat. It thrives in rocky soils and full sun, making it a hardy choice for rock gardens.

Its resilience and ornamental value make it a popular plant among succulent enthusiasts.
