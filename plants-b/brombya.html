Brombya

Brombya, commonly known as the brombya plant, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Bromeliaceae.

These plants are native to South America, particularly Brazil, and are characterized by their striking, often colorful foliage. Due to their unique appearance and relatively low maintenance, brombya plants are often cultivated as ornamental plants in tropical and subtropical regions.
