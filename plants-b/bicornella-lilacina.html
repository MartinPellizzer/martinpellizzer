Bicornella lilacina

Bicornella lilacina, commonly known as the lilac-flowered orchid, is a species of orchid native to certain regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, lilac-colored flowers that bloom in clusters during the warm seasons. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and arranged in a spiral pattern around the stem.

Bicornella lilacina is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and unique floral structure.
