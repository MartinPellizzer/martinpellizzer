Boerhavia commersonii

Boerhavia commersonii, commonly known as redroot sandmat, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of Africa, Asia, and the Americas.

It is a low-growing annual herb with small, fleshy leaves and distinctive red or purple stems. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pinkish flowers that bloom throughout the growing season.
