Berberis × circumserrata var. subarmata

Berberis × circumserrata var. subarmata, commonly known as the Japanese barberry, is a deciduous shrub native to Japan.

It features compound leaves with serrated margins and produces bright orange-red berries in the fall. The plant is characterized by its thorny stems and dense, upright growth habit. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.

However, it is considered an invasive species in some regions due to its rapid spread and ecological impact.
