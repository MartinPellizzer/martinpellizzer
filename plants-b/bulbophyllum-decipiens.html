Bulbophyllum decipiens

Bulbophyllum decipiens, commonly known as the deceptive orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant features a pseudobulb that stores water and nutrients, supporting its growth during dry periods. Its flowers are small and clustered, often in a raceme, with a distinctive coloration that mimics other plants to aid in pollination.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by enthusiasts.
