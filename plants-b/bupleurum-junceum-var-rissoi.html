Bupleurum junceum var. rissoi

Bupleurum junceum var. rissoi, commonly known as the slender bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. The plant features slender, branched stems and small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale blue. Its leaves are linear and often covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
