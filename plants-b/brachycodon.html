Brachycodon

Brachycodon, commonly known as the small-leaved dandelion, is a member of the Asteraceae family.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its basal rosette of narrow, spoon-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that develop into fluffy, white seed heads. Brachycodon is native to temperate regions of Asia and is often found in disturbed soils and open areas.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural studies.
