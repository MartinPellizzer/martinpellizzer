Babiana truncata

Babiana truncata, commonly known as the purple firecracker, is a striking South African bulbous plant belonging to the Iridaceae family.

It features vibrant purple to red flowers that bloom in late summer, adding a splash of color to gardens. The plant produces a cluster of narrow, grass-like leaves that emerge from a bulbous base. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Babiana truncata is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in native plant landscaping.
