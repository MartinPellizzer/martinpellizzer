Bellevalia mauritanica

Bellevalia mauritanica, commonly known as the Mauritania bellflower, is a flowering plant native to North Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments. It is often found in rocky soils and can thrive in regions with limited rainfall.

Bellevalia mauritanica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens in Mediterranean climates.
