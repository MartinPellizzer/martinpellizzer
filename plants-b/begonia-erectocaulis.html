Begonia erectocaulis

Begonia erectocaulis, commonly known as the upright begonia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and striking, heart-shaped leaves that are often brightly colored. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, which bloom throughout the growing season. It thrives in humid, warm environments and prefers partial to full shade.

Its foliage and floral display make it a popular choice for indoor gardening and as a decorative plant.
