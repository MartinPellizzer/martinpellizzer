Boronia polygalifolia var. robusta

Boronia polygalifolia var. robusta, commonly known as the robust boronia, is a shrub native to southern Australia.

It is characterized by its aromatic, fragrant leaves and clusters of white or pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a distinctive, spicy scent when crushed.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively drought-tolerant once established.
