Brombya platynema

Brombya platynema, commonly known as the narrow-leaf brombya, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Asphodelaceae and is characterized by its grass-like appearance with narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences of white to pale yellow flowers during the summer months. Due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance, it is a popular choice for xeriscaping.
