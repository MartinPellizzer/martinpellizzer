Bulbophyllum maudeae

Bulbophyllum maudeae, commonly known as the Maude's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the rainforests of Queensland, Australia.

It is characterized by its sympodial growth habit, producing multiple pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces fragrant, greenish-yellow flowers with prominent lips. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Bulbophyllum maudeae is a popular species among orchid enthusiasts due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
