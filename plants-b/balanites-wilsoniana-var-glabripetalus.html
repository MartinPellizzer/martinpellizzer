Balanites wilsoniana var. glabripetalus

Balanites wilsoniana var. glabripetalus, commonly known as the smooth-petaled desert bean, is a shrub native to arid regions of Africa and the Middle East.

It belongs to the family Fabaceae and is characterized by its small, yellow, pod-like fruits that contain edible seeds. The plant has small, compound leaves and produces flowers that are typically pale yellow to cream in color. It is well adapted to survive in dry, hot climates with minimal water availability.
