Bidens societatis

Bidens societatis, commonly known as the societyweed, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Its leaves are heart-shaped with serrated edges, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits that disperse seeds by wind.
