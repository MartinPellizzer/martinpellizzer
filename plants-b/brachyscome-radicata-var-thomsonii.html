Brachyscome radicata var. thomsonii

Brachyscome radicata var. thomsonii, commonly known as Thomson's slender everlasting, is a low-growing perennial plant native to southern Australia.

It features compact, mat-forming growth with small, daisy-like flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant has hairy, linear leaves that are typically blue-green in hue. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
