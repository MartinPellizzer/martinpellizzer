Begonia nobmanniae

Begonia nobmanniae, commonly known as the noble begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often a vibrant green with a velvety texture. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, which bloom in clusters. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is popular among plant enthusiasts and collectors.
