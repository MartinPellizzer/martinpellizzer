Buckleya graebneriana

Buckleya graebneriana, commonly known as the Chinese buckleya, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its showy, pendulous clusters of white to pink flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant has compound leaves with three to five leaflets, and it produces small, winged seeds that are dispersed by wind. Buckleya graebneriana thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a suitable choice for garden landscapes.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
