Borzicactella

Borzicactella, commonly known as the miniature cactus, is a unique member of the Cactaceae family.

It is native to the arid regions of South America, particularly in Argentina and Chile. This plant is characterized by its small, globular shape and spiny, ribbed stems. It produces small, yellow flowers during the warmer months, adding a delicate touch to its rugged appearance.

Due to its compact size and drought tolerance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
