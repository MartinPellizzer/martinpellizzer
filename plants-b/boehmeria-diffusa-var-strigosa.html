Boehmeria diffusa var. strigosa

Boehmeria diffusa var. strigosa, commonly known as Chinese nettle or Indian nettle, is a herbaceous plant native to Asia.

It belongs to the family Urticaceae and is characterized by its hairy stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish flowers in clusters, and its leaves are often used in traditional medicine.

It thrives in moist, shady environments and is sometimes cultivated for its fibrous stems.
