Bulbophyllum leptorrhachis

Bulbophyllum leptorrhachis, commonly known as the slender-stemmed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, often branched stems and small, linear leaves. The plant produces numerous flowers arranged in a dense spike, typically in shades of yellow and green. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Bulbophyllum leptorrhachis is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
