Byttneria schumannii

Byttneria schumannii, commonly known as the African mahogany, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Central and West Africa.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and showy, red or yellow flowers. The tree produces woody pods that contain small, hard seeds. It is valued for its durable, heavy timber used in furniture and construction.
