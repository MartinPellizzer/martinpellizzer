Borzicactus longiserpens subsp. longiserpens

Borzicactus longiserpens subsp.

longiserpens, commonly known as the long-armed borzicactus, is a species of cactus native to the arid regions of South America. It is characterized by its long, serpentine stems that can grow up to several meters in length.

The plant has spiny areoles and produces yellow flowers during the warmer months. It is adapted to survive in extreme desert conditions with minimal water.

Its unique morphology makes it a distinctive and visually striking member of the Cactaceae family.
