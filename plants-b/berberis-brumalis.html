Berberis brumalis

Berberis brumalis, commonly known as the winter berberis, is a deciduous shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features spiny stems and small, bright yellow flowers that appear in early spring. The plant produces red, berry-like fruits that are edible but often have a bitter taste. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in rocky or well-drained soils.

Berberis brumalis is also noted for its hardiness and ability to withstand cold temperatures.
