Biota semperaurea

Biota semperaurea, commonly known as the golden everlasting.

This plant is characterized by its dense clusters of small, golden-yellow flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. It is a perennial herb that thrives in well-drained soils and full sun exposure. The leaves are narrow and grass-like, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

It is often used in gardens for its long-lasting blooms and low maintenance requirements.
