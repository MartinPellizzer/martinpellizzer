Balizia leucocalyx

Balizia leucocalyx, commonly known as the white-flowered balizia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive white or pale pink flowers. The plant has compound leaves and produces pods that contain several seeds. It is often found in grassland and savanna habitats, thriving in well-drained soils.
