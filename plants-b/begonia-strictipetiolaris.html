Begonia strictipetiolaris

Begonia strictipetiolaris, commonly known as the stiff-stemmed begonia, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and distinctive, elongated leaves with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, delicate flowers in shades of pink and white, typically arranged in terminal clusters. Its leaves are often marked with intricate patterns and have a slightly serrated edge.

Begonia strictipetiolaris prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
