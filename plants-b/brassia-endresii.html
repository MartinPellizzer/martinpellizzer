Brassia endresii

Brassia endresii, commonly known as the Endres's orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from white to deep purple. The plant typically grows as an epiphytic orchid, meaning it attaches itself to trees without deriving nutrients from them. Its leaves are usually broad and sword-shaped, and it produces intricate, fragrant blooms during the warmer months.

Brassia endresii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
