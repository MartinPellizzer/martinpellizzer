Barleria gibsonioides

Barleria gibsonioides, commonly known as the Cape honeysuckle, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy surface. Its showy, tubular flowers are usually bright yellow or orange, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies.

It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
