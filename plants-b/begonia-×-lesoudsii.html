Begonia × lesoudsii

Begonia × lesoudsii, commonly known as the hybrid begonia, is a popular ornamental plant prized for its vibrant, showy flowers.

It is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Begonia semperflorens and Begonia × tuberifera, combining the best traits of both parents. The plant typically features heart-shaped leaves and clusters of tubular or funnel-shaped blooms in a range of colors, including red, pink, and white. It thrives in partial shade and well-draining soil, making it suitable for container gardening and borders.

Its adaptability and prolonged blooming period make it a favorite among gardeners.
