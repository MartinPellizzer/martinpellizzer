Barleria fissimuroides

Barleria fissimuroides, commonly known as the narrow-leaf barleria, is a perennial shrub native to southern Africa.

It features slender, aromatic leaves with a distinctive linear shape and a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to purple, blooming throughout the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and long flowering season.
