Bifrenaria harrisoniae var. flavopurpurea

Bifrenaria harrisoniae var. flavopurpurea, commonly known as the Purple-flowered Bifrenaria, is a rare and striking orchid native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its vibrant purple and yellow floral hues, which are particularly prominent in its showy, spurred flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid tropical environments. Its foliage consists of broad, leathery leaves that help it retain moisture in its natural habitat.

Due to its rarity and unique appearance, it is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts and conservationists alike.
