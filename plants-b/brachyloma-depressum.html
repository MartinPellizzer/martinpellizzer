Brachyloma depressum

Brachyloma depressum, commonly known as the narrow-leafed mountain everlasting, is a perennial herb native to southern Australia.

It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils in open forests and woodlands. The plant features small, narrow leaves and produces clusters of pink to purple flowers. It is well adapted to dry conditions and can survive in low-nutrient environments.

Brachyloma depressum is often found in coastal and inland regions, thriving in full sun to partial shade.
