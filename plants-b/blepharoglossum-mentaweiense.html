Blepharoglossum mentaweiense

Blepharoglossum mentaweiense, commonly known as the Mentawei fern, is a rare and unique member of the Blepharoglossaceae family.

It is a small, epiphytic fern that thrives in the humid, shaded environments of tropical rainforests. The plant is characterized by its delicate, lace-like fronds and is native to the island of Mentawai in Indonesia. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species.

Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this botanical gem.
