Bauhinia Pottsii Var. Decipiens

Bauhinia pottsii var. decipiens is a rare and striking legume native to the Philippines. Known for its unique and colorful flowers, this plant is a member of the Fabaceae family. It thrives in tropical climates and is often found in coastal areas with well-drained soils. The variety decipiens is distinguished by its ornamental qualities and is highly sought after by botanists and gardeners. Due to its limited distribution, it remains a subject of conservation interest and botanical study.
