Bulbostylis warei

Bulbostylis warei, commonly known as the slender bulbostylis, is a small herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its compact growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces tiny, spike-like inflorescences that bear small, white to pale green flowers. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest undergrowth or along stream banks.
