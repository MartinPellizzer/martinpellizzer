Bulbophyllum manifestans

Bulbophyllum manifestans, commonly known as the showy orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, colorful flowers. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, aiding in its survival in tropical environments. Its flowers are typically large and showy, with intricate patterns that attract pollinators.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in horticulture.
