Begonia lindeniana

Begonia lindeniana, commonly known as the Lindenberg begonia, is a striking herbaceous plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range from red to pink and orange, often with contrasting dark veins. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are typically dark green and slightly velvety to the touch. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Begonia lindeniana is popular among gardeners for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
