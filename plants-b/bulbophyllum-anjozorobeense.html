Bulbophyllum anjozorobeense

Bulbophyllum anjozorobeense, commonly known as the Anjozorobeense orchid, is a rare and endemic species native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, intricate floral structure. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically greenish-yellow with intricate patterns. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires specific soil conditions to flourish.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat threats, it is considered a vulnerable species.
