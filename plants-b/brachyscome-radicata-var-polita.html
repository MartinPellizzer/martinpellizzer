Brachyscome radicata var. polita

Brachyscome radicata var. polita, commonly known as the silverhead plant, is a low-growing, mat-forming perennial native to Australia.

It features small, fluffy white or pale blue flowers that bloom in winter and spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. The plant has hairy, silver-gray foliage that helps reduce water loss in arid conditions. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
