Brachypterum canarense

Brachypterum canarense, commonly known as the Canary Islands thornbush, is a shrub native to the Canary Islands.

It is characterized by its woody stems and small, opposite leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It is adapted to the arid and coastal environments of its native habitat.

Brachypterum canarense plays a role in stabilizing soils and providing habitat for local wildlife.
