Bulbophyllum ecornutum subsp. ecornutum

Bulbophyllum ecornutum subsp.

ecornutum, commonly known as the slender bulbophyllum, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia. It is characterized by its compact, pseudobulbous structure and linear, lanceolate leaves.

The plant produces inflorescences with numerous small, white to pale yellow flowers. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

This subspecies is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
