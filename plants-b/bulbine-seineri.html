Bulbine seineri

Bulbine seineri, commonly known as the Natal bulbous plant, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features succulent, fleshy leaves that are typically gray-green in color, adapted to store water in arid environments. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained sandy or gravelly soils and prefers full sun exposure.

Its hardy nature makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
