Bulbinella latifolia subsp. doleritica

Bulbinella latifolia subsp.

doleritica, commonly known as the slender bulbine, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Australia. It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered flowers in shades of pink or white.

The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to dry, sunny environments. It is often found in coastal and inland regions, thriving in well-drained conditions.
