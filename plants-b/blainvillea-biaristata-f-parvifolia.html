Blainvillea biaristata f. parvifolia

Blainvillea biaristata f. parvifolia, commonly known as the dwarf blainvillea, is a compact shrub native to South America.

It features small, narrow leaves that are typically a deep green, often with a slight pubescence. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its ornamental value lies in its delicate foliage and prolonged flowering period.
