Bomarea campylophylla

Bomarea campylophylla, commonly known as the spiral bomarea, is a striking flowering plant native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its spirally twisted stems and vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from pink to purple. The plant belongs to the family Alstroemeriaceae and is known for its ornamental value in gardens. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem.

Bomarea campylophylla thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in cloud forests.
