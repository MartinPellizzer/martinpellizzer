Bidens esmartinezii

Bidens esmartinezii, commonly known as the smartinez false beggarweed, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features yellow flowers with ray florets and is typically found in moist, open habitats. It has hairy stems and leaves that are often lance-shaped.

Bidens esmartinezii is noted for its ecological role in supporting pollinators and its potential medicinal properties.
