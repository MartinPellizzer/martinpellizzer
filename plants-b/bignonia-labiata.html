Bignonia labiata

Bignonia labiata, commonly known as the scarlet trumpetbush, is a deciduous shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its vibrant orange to red tubular flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has opposite, simple, and ovate leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. Its stems are woody and often have a reddish hue, especially in the bark.

Bignonia labiata is often cultivated for its showy flowers and is adaptable to a variety of soil types.
