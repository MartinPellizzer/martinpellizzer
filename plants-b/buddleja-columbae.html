Buddleja columbae

Buddleja columbae, commonly known as the Columbae Buddleia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features slender, arching stems that produce clusters of fragrant, tubular flowers in shades of purple and blue. The plant is notable for its ability to attract a wide variety of pollinators, including butterflies and bees. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and lance-shaped, with a smooth texture.

Buddleja columbae is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ease of care.
