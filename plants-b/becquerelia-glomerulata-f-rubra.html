Becquerelia glomerulata f. rubra

Becquerelia glomerulata f. rubra, commonly known as the red-flowered becquerelia, is a compact, herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, clustered flowers that range in color from pink to deep red, depending on the variety. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
