Baccharoides calvoana subsp. oehleri

Baccharoides calvoana subsp.

oehleri, commonly known as the Oehleri baccharoides, is a shrub native to the Caribbean islands. It is characterized by its small, ovate leaves that are usually covered in a fine, white pubescence.

The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in coastal areas and is often found in sandy or rocky soils.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
