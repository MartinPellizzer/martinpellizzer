Boerhavia tomentosa

Boerhavia tomentosa, commonly known as silky boerhavia, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is a low-growing annual herb that typically reaches heights of 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features small, pink to purple flowers that bloom in clusters, and its leaves are covered in fine, soft hairs, giving it a silky texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed areas such as roadsides and fields.

The plant is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional remedies for various ailments.
