Byttneria hirsuta var. parvifolia

Byttneria hirsuta var. parvifolia, commonly known as the small-leaved hirsute byttneria, is a tropical shrub belonging to the Malvaceae family.

It is native to regions of Africa, particularly in countries like Nigeria and Cameroon. The plant is characterized by its hairy stems and small, ovate leaves with a hairy underside. Its flowers are typically yellow or greenish-yellow, clustered in axillary inflorescences.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, hairy seeds.
