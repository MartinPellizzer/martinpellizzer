Buddleja chenopodiifolia

Buddleja chenopodiifolia, commonly known as the Chinese butterfly bush, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its long, narrow leaves that resemble those of the Chenopodium genus, hence its specific epithet. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, pale purple to blue flowers that attract a variety of pollinators, including butterflies and bees. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Buddleja chenopodiifolia is also valued for its adaptability and ability to thrive in a range of environmental conditions.
