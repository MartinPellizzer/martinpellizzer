Betonica nivea subsp. ossetica

Betonica nivea subsp.

ossetica, commonly known as the Ossetian water-hemlock, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Caucasus region. It is characterized by its finely divided, fern-like leaves and clusters of small, white, umbellate flowers.

The plant thrives in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in mountainous areas. It is part of the Apiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and some toxic species.
