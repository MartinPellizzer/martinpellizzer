Biophytum reinwardtii var. gracilenta

Biophytum reinwardtii var. gracilenta, commonly known as the slender leaf biophytum, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features delicate, fern-like leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white or grayish powder, known as farina. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in summer, adding a subtle ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid or semi-arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
