Bishopiella elegans

Bishopiella elegans, commonly known as the elegant bishop's hat, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its unique, spiky, helmet-like inflorescence. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, daisy-like flowers in shades of yellow and white. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Bishopiella elegans is noted for its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
