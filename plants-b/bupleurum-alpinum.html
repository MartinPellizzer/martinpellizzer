Bupleurum alpinum

Bupleurum alpinum, commonly known as Alpine Bupleurum, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Europe and western Asia.

It typically grows in alpine meadows and rocky slopes, thriving in cool, moist environments. The plant features slender, upright stems and small, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Bupleurum alpinum is valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
