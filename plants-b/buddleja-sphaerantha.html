Buddleja sphaerantha

Buddleja sphaerantha, commonly known as the globe buddleja, is a species of flowering plant native to China.

It is characterized by its unique spherical flower clusters, which give it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Buddleja sphaerantha is a deciduous shrub that thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
