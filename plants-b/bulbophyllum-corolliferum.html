Bulbophyllum corolliferum

Bulbophyllum corolliferum, commonly known as the coral-tipped orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a compact, pseudobulb-like structure with fleshy, dark green leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. The plant produces striking, coral-like inflorescences with numerous small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
