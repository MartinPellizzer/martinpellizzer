Bubonium imbricatum

Bubonium imbricatum, commonly known as the overlapping leaf plant, is a rare and unique species characterized by its distinctive foliage.

Its leaves are arranged in an overlapping pattern, giving the plant a dense, compact appearance. The plant is native to specific regions with high humidity and well-drained soil. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or white in color.
