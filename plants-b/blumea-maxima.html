Blumea maxima

Blumea maxima, commonly known as the large blumea, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy on the underside. Its yellow flowers are arranged in clusters and bloom during the summer months.

Blumea maxima is often found in moist, open habitats such as forests and shrublands.
