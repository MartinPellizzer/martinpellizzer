Baeckea africana

Baeckea africana, commonly known as the African baeckea, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces small, fragrant white or cream-colored flowers arranged in clusters. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a slight waxy coating.

Baeckea africana is often found in coastal areas and is adapted to sandy soils and saline conditions.
