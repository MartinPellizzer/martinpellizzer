Bunium bourgaei var. huetii

Bunium bourgaei var. huetii, commonly known as the Huet's fennel, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Apiaceae family, characterized by its aromatic foliage and umbellate inflorescences. Its seeds are small, dry, and often used in culinary or medicinal applications.

The plant is valued for its distinctive scent and historical use in traditional medicine.
