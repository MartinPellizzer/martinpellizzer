Brassica Insularis Var. Conferta

Brassica insularis var. conferta is a rare and unique plant native to the Mediterranean region. It is known for its compact growth habit and striking, clustered flowers. This variety thrives in well-drained soils and full sun exposure. Its historical significance in traditional medicine adds to its botanical appeal. Due to habitat loss, it is now considered a vulnerable species in its native range.
