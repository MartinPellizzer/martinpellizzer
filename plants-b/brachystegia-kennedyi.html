Brachystegia kennedyi

Brachystegia kennedyi, commonly known as the Kennedy's brachystegia, is a deciduous tree native to the miombo woodlands of Central and East Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are often pubescent. The tree produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in racemose inflorescences, followed by flat, bean-like pods that contain several seeds.

It plays an important ecological role in its habitat by providing shelter and food for various wildlife species.
