Behen noveboracense

Behen noveboracense, commonly known as northern cow-wheat, is a member of the Fabaceae family and is native to North America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and forest edges. The plant has palmately lobed leaves and produces small, yellowish-white flowers arranged in clusters. Its seeds are enclosed in pods and dispersed by wind, contributing to its spread.

It plays a role in soil stabilization and provides nectar for pollinators.
