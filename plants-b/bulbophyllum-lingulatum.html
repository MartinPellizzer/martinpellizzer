Bulbophyllum lingulatum

Bulbophyllum lingulatum, commonly known as the lingulate orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit and produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically has dark green, leathery leaves and is known for its ability to thrive in humid, shaded environments. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The flowers are usually pale yellow to greenish-white, with a distinctive lip that is slightly notched.
