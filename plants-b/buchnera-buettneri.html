Buchnera buettneri

Buchnera buettneri, commonly known as the African buttercup, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features simple, alternate leaves that are usually lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are yellow and occur in clusters, resembling those of the buttercup genus.

Buchnera buettneri is often found in grassy areas and along roadsides, thriving in well-drained soils.
