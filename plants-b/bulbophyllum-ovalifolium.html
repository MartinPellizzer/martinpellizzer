Bulbophyllum ovalifolium

Bulbophyllum ovalifolium, commonly known as the oval-leafed orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its sympodial growth habit and the presence of pseudobulbs. The plant features lanceolate to ovate leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of white to grayish pubescence. Its flowers are typically arranged in racemes and are usually pale yellow to greenish-white in color.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouse environments due to its specific humidity and light requirements.
