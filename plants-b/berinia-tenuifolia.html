Berinia tenuifolia

Berinia tenuifolia, commonly known as the slender leaf berinia, is a small evergreen shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features narrow, linear leaves that are typically less than 1 inch in length, giving it a distinctive, delicate appearance. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers in early spring, which are clustered in dense inflorescences. Berinia tenuifolia thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or coastal landscapes.

Its compact size and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for ornamental horticulture.
