Brachypodium caninum

Brachypodium caninum, commonly known as curly bluestem, is a perennial grass native to North America.

It is characterized by its compact, tufted growth habit and narrow, blue-green blades. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny, wind-dispersed seeds. It thrives in a variety of soil types and is often found in disturbed areas, prairies, and along roadsides.

Brachypodium caninum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in ecological restoration projects.
