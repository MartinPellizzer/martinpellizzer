Berchemia loureiroana

Berchemia loureiroana, commonly known as the yellow bellvista, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its yellow, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has woody stems and leathery, evergreen leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens.

Berchemia loureiroana is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
