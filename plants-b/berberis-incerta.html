Berberis incerta

Berberis incerta, commonly known as the uncertain barberry, is a deciduous shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features thorny stems and lance-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green color. The plant produces small, yellow flowers in spring, followed by red, berry-like fruits that are edible but often bitter. Berberis incerta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.

It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
