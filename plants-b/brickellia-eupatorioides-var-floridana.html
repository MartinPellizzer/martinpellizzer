Brickellia eupatorioides var. floridana

Brickellia eupatorioides var. floridana, commonly known as Florida brickellia, is a perennial herb native to the southeastern United States.

It features a basal rosette of narrow, gray-green leaves that are covered in fine hairs. The plant produces showy, yellow flowers in clusters during the spring and early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable addition to native plant gardens and ecological restoration projects.
