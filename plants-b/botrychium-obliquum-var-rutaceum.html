Botrychium obliquum var. rutaceum

Botrychium obliquum var. rutaceum, commonly known as the yellow moonwort, is a fern-like plant belonging to the family Ophioglossaceae.

It is characterized by its unique morphology, featuring a fleshy, club-shaped sporangium at the base of the frond. Botrychium obliquum var. rutaceum plays a role in forest ecosystems by contributing to soil stability and providing habitat for microorganisms.
