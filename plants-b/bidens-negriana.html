Bidens negriana

Bidens negriana, commonly known as blackleaf beggarweed, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers with five petals and a central cluster of orange disk flowers. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, disturbed soils and is commonly found in wetlands and along stream banks.

Bidens negriana is known for its rapid growth and ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes.
