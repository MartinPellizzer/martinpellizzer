Begonia peii

Begonia peii, commonly known as the Pei begonia, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Begoniaceae family and is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows as an upright herb with hairy stems and can reach heights of up to 30 centimeters. Its flowers, which range in color from red to orange, bloom in late summer and are often borne on long, slender pedicels.

Begonia peii prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.
