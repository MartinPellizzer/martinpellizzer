Bulbophyllum tenuifolium

Bulbophyllum tenuifolium, commonly known as the slender-leafed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its narrow, elongated leaves and produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid tropical environments. Its roots are specialized for absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air and rain.

Bulbophyllum tenuifolium is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and resilience in cultivation.
