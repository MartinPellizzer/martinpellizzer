Bonyunia magnifica

Bonyunia magnifica, commonly known as the Royal Flame Tree, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that bloom in vibrant red and orange hues, often resembling flames. The plant has a robust, woody stem with thick, smooth bark that provides structural support. Its leaves are broad and elliptical, providing dense foliage that offers shade.

Bonyunia magnifica is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in warm, humid climates.
