Begonia obversa

Begonia obversa, commonly known as the silver leaf begonia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety silver-green leaves that are often adorned with intricate patterns. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or red, blooming in clusters. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and prefers well-draining soil.

Begonia obversa is popular as a decorative plant due to its ornamental foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
