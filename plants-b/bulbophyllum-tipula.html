Bulbophyllum tipula

Bulbophyllum tipula, commonly known as the slender pineapple orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, pseudobulb-like structure and its ability to produce multiple flowering stems. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest undergrowth. Its flowers are small and clustered, often with a yellow or white coloration.

The orchid is known for its resilience and is relatively easy to cultivate under the right conditions.
