Boltonia interior

Boltonia interior, commonly known as white coneflower, is a herbaceous perennial native to the eastern United States.

It features tall, sturdy stems that bear large, showy white to pale pink ray flowers surrounding a central cone of yellow disc flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in woodland or wetland habitats. Its foliage is lance-shaped and covered in a fine, white downy coating.

Boltonia interior is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators like butterflies and bees.
