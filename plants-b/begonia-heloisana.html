Begonia heloisana

Begonia heloisana, commonly known as the Heloise begonia, is a striking perennial plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from red to orange and pink, often with contrasting patterns on the petals. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are typically dark green and velvety to the touch. It thrives in partial shade and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for shaded garden beds and containers.

Begonia heloisana is also known for its ability to produce offsets, which can be easily propagated to create new plants.
