Begonia lealii

Begonia lealii, commonly known as the golden begonia, is a tropical plant native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with yellow and green patterns. The plant produces clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in spring. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.

Due to its ornamental value, it is popular among gardeners and plant enthusiasts.
