Brunfelsia mutabilis

Brunfelsia mutabilis, commonly known as the angel's trumpets, is a tropical shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to deep purple. The plant produces berries that change color as they mature, starting green and turning red. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Due to its toxic properties, it should be handled with care.
