Buchnera ramosissima var. parviflora

Buchnera ramosissima var. parviflora, commonly known as the small-flowered bush willow, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, branching habit and small, pale green to yellowish leaves. The plant produces small, clustered white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and hardy nature, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
