Bignonia muricata

Bignonia muricata, commonly known as the rough bignonia, is a flowering plant belonging to the Bignoniaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of the Americas and is characterized by its climbing growth habit and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers. The plant features compound leaves with serrated edges and produces bright yellow or orange blooms. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Bignonia muricata is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
