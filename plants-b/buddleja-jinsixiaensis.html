Buddleja jinsixiaensis

Buddleja jinsixiaensis, commonly known as the Jinsixia Buddleia, is a shrub native to China, specifically the Sichuan province.

It is characterized by its striking clusters of fragrant, tubular flowers that range in color from pale purple to deep violet. The plant produces small, elongated leaves that are typically gray-green in appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a suitable choice for garden cultivation.

Buddleja jinsixiaensis is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators such as butterflies and bees.
