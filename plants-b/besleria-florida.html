Besleria florida

Besleria florida, commonly known as the flame violet, is a tropical flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that resemble violets. The plant produces vibrant orange-red petals with a contrasting dark center, making it a popular ornamental species. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Besleria florida is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
