Berberis Jamesiana

Berberis jamesiana is a striking evergreen shrub known for its vibrant yellow flowers and sharp, spiny leaves. Native to the western United States, it thrives in rocky and sunny environments. This plant is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and hardy nature. Its berries, though toxic to humans, provide food for birds and other wildlife. With its unique appearance and ecological benefits, Berberis jamesiana is a fascinating subject for both botanists and gardeners.
