Bauhinia platypetala

Bauhinia platypetala, commonly known as the flat-petaled bauhinia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive, flat, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, orchid-like flowers that are typically pink or purple, blooming in clusters. Its seeds are enclosed in flat, pod-like fruits, and the plant is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its striking appearance.

Bauhinia platypetala is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
