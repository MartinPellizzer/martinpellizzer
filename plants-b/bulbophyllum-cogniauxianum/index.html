Bulbophyllum Cogniauxianum

Bulbophyllum cogniauxianum is a rare and striking orchid native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia. Known for its intricate floral structure and vibrant coloration, this species captivates botanists and enthusiasts alike. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, often growing epiphytically on trees. The plant's unique morphology and limited distribution make it a subject of scientific interest. Conservation efforts are crucial to protect this delicate species from habitat loss and over-collection.
