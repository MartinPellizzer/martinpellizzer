Bartschella glassii

Bartschella glassii, commonly known as the glassy bartschella, is a unique species of flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, glass-like appearance, with translucent leaves that reflect light in a shimmering manner. The plant produces small, delicate flowers in shades of white and pale pink, which bloom during the cooler months. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires a cool, humid environment to flourish.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, Bartschella glassii is considered a conservation concern.
