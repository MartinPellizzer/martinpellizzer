Buddleja lindleyana

Buddleja lindleyana, commonly known as the Chinese butterfly bush, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features long, narrow leaves that are typically opposite or whorled along the stems. The plant produces fragrant, clustered flowers that range in color from white to lilac, attracting numerous pollinators. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Buddleja lindleyana is valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation in garden settings.
