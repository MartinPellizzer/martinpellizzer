Barbacenia hereroensis

Barbacenia hereroensis, commonly known as the Herero's barbacenia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that are typically red or orange in color. It thrives in full sun and requires well-drained soil to flourish.
