Buddleja truncata

Buddleja truncata, commonly known as the Chinese butterfly bush, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant, panicle-like inflorescences that bloom in late summer. The plant has lance-shaped, dark green leaves that are opposite in arrangement. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Buddleja truncata is also valued for its ability to attract butterflies and other pollinators.
