Baccharis reichei

Baccharis reichei, commonly known as silverleaf baccharis, is a shrub native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are grouped in clusters, typical of the daisy family. It is often found in dry, open habitats such as shrublands and grasslands.

Its adaptability to arid conditions makes it a common component of coastal and inland ecosystems.
