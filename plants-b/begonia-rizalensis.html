Begonia rizalensis

Begonia rizalensis, commonly known as the Rizal begonia, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, typically red, pink, and white. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and a thick, succulent stem. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often grown as a decorative plant in gardens and homes.
