Ballota sagittata

Ballota sagittata, commonly known as arrowhead ballota, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with narrow, lance-shaped leaves that resemble an arrowhead. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale pink, typically blooming in the spring. Ballota sagittata thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

It is also known for its aromatic foliage, which contains essential oils with potential medicinal properties.
