Botrychium Fumarioides Var. Dissectum

Botrychium fumarioides var. dissectum is a rare fern species known for its delicate, lace-like fronds. Found primarily in moist, shaded environments, it thrives in woodland habitats across parts of North America. Its unique appearance, featuring finely dissected leaves, makes it a striking addition to any botanical collection. Despite its beauty, this plant remains understudied and is often overlooked in favor of more common ferns. Conservation efforts are crucial to protect this fragile species from habitat loss and environmental changes.
