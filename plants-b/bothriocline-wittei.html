Bothriocline wittei

Bothriocline wittei, commonly known as the Cape dandelion, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble a single bloom. The plant produces yellow flowers that are typically arranged in a cluster, with narrow, grass-like leaves. It is often found in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.

The Cape dandelion is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
