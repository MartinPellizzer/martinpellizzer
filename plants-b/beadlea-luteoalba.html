Beadlea luteoalba

Beadlea luteoalba, commonly known as the yellow whitebead, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has small, narrow leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It prefers well-drained soils and full sun exposure, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Beadlea luteoalba is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ease of care.
