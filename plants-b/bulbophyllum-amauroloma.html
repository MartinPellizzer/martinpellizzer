Bulbophyllum amauroloma

Bulbophyllum amauroloma, commonly known as the dark red orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking dark red to purple flower spikes. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers with a unique coloration that sets it apart from other orchid species. It typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments, thriving in tropical rainforests.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a conservation concern.
