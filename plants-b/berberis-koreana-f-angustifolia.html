Berberis koreana f. angustifolia

Berberis koreana f. angustifolia, commonly known as the narrowleaf Korean barberry, is a shrub native to Korea.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green and have a glossy appearance. The plant produces yellow flowers in early spring, followed by red berries that persist into winter. It is valued for its ornamental qualities and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.

This variety is often used in landscaping for its hardiness and visual appeal.
