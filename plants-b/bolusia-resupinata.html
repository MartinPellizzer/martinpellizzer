Bolusia resupinata

Bolusia resupinata, commonly known as the Cape rose, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces showy pink or red flowers that bloom in spring and summer. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

The plant is adapted to thrive in coastal and sandy soils, making it a hardy and resilient species.
