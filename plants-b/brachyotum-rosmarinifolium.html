Brachyotum rosmarinifolium

Brachyotum rosmarinifolium, commonly known as the African geranium, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub characterized by its upright growth habit and aromatic, gray-green foliage. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink, often blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Brachyotum rosmarinifolium is also valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
