Begonia princeae var. racemigera

Begonia princeae var. racemigera, commonly known as the racemifera begonia, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from red to pink and often feature intricate patterns. The plant typically has heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are dark green and slightly velvety to the touch. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is popular among horticulturists for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation under the right conditions.
