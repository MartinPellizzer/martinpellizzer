Begonia cathayana

Begonia cathayana, commonly known as the Chinese begonia, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to China.

It features striking, velvety leaves that are often brightly colored, ranging from deep red to purple and green. The plant produces showy, spurred flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, which bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for shaded garden areas.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its foliage and unique floral structure, making it a sought-after plant in horticulture.
