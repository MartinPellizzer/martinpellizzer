Bulbophyllum trichaete

Bulbophyllum trichaete, commonly known as the hairy lip orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, hairy appearance on its lip, which gives it its common name. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its flowers are usually small and come in various colors, including white, yellow, and green.

Bulbophyllum trichaete is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
