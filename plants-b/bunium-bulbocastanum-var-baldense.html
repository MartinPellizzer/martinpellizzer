Bunium bulbocastanum var. baldense

Bunium bulbocastanum var. baldense, commonly known as the Balkan fennel, is a perennial herb native to the Balkan Peninsula.

It belongs to the Apiaceae family, which includes carrots, parsley, and dill, and is characterized by its aromatic, finely divided leaves and yellow flowers. It is also used in traditional medicine for its purported digestive and anti-inflammatory properties.
