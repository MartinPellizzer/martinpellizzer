Bletia × irrorata

Bletia × irrorata, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Bletia purpurea and Bletia ciliaris.

It is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical regions of Central and South America. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest understories. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, with a distinctive spurred lip.

Bletia × irrorata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
