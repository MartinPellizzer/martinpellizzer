Bignonia cherere

Bignonia cherere, commonly known as the African trumpet vine, is a climbing plant native to tropical Africa.

It features showy, tubular flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, attracting pollinators like bees and birds. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces long, twisting vines that can climb trees or structures. Its flowers bloom in spring and summer, adding vibrant color to gardens.

Bignonia cherere is valued for its ornamental beauty and ability to thrive in warm, humid climates.
