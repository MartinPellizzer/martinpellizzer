Bupleurum tenue var. genuinum

Bupleurum tenue var. genuinum, commonly known as slender bupleurum, is a perennial herb native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It typically grows in moist, woodland habitats and has finely divided, fern-like leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in early summer. Its root system is fibrous and often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting pollinators.
