Byttneria schunkei

Byttneria schunkei, commonly known as the African satinwood, is a tropical tree native to regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its smooth, dark bark and large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically glossy green. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by woody capsules containing numerous seeds. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Due to its slow growth and ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
