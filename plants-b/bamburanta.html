Bamburanta

Bamburanta, commonly known as the red bamboo, is a striking plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems that can grow up to 10 meters in height. The plant features vibrant red and green striped leaves that add visual interest to its appearance. Bamburanta thrives in moist, well-drained soil and requires partial to full sunlight.

Its unique coloration and graceful form make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
