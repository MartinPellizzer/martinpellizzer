Bulbophyllum kermesinum

Bulbophyllum kermesinum, commonly known as the Kermesina orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its unique, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid tropical environments. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, aiding survival in its natural habitat.

The flowers are usually yellow or orange, with intricate patterns that attract specific pollinators.
