Biarum orientale

Biarum orientale, commonly known as the oriental lily, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant typically grows in woodland habitats and thrives in partial shade. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, often forming a rosette at the base.

Biarum orientale is valued for its ornamental beauty and is cultivated in gardens for its elegant blooms.
