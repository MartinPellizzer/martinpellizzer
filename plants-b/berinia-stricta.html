Berinia stricta

Berinia stricta, commonly known as the narrowleaf berinia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows in arid and semi-arid regions, characterized by its small, narrow leaves and showy yellow flowers. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain several seeds. Berinia stricta is adapted to withstand drought conditions, making it a resilient species in its native habitat.

It is sometimes cultivated for its ornamental value due to its attractive foliage and blooms.
