Biramella

Biramella, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its velvety, waxy leaves and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows as a compact shrub with upright stems and produces clusters of flowers in shades of purple, pink, and white. Its ornamental value makes it a popular houseplant worldwide.
