Bursa bursa-pastoris var. heteris

Bursa bursa-pastoris var. heteris, commonly known as the hairy shepherd's purse, is a variant of the common shepherd's purse.

It is an annual herbaceous plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family. The plant is characterized by its distinctive triangular or heart-shaped seed pods, which are often covered in fine hairs. It typically grows in disturbed soils and is found in various regions across Europe and Asia.

The leaves are alternate, simple, and have a slightly hairy texture, contributing to its overall appearance.
