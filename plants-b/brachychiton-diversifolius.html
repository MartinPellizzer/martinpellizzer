Brachychiton diversifolius

Brachychiton diversifolius, commonly known as the broad-leafed trumpet tree, is a deciduous tree native to northern Australia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. The plant has large, palmately lobed leaves that vary in shape, contributing to its common name. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in semi-arid and tropical regions.

The tree is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
