Bromus staintonii

Bromus staintonii, commonly known as Stainton's brome, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in mountainous regions. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, showing drought tolerance.

Its ecological role includes stabilizing soil and providing forage for wildlife.
