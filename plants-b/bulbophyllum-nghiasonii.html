Bulbophyllum nghiasonii

Bulbophyllum nghiasonii, commonly known as the Nghiason orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

This epiphytic plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers. It typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant has pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, supporting its survival in its natural habitat.

Its unique floral structure and ornamental value make it a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
