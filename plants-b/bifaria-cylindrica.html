Bifaria cylindrica

Bifaria cylindrica, commonly known as the cylindrical bifaria, is a species of flowering plant in the family Acanthaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its slender, cylindrical stems and small, clustered flowers. Bifaria cylindrica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
