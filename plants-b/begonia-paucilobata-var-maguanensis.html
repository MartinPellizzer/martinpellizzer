Begonia paucilobata var. maguanensis

Begonia paucilobata var. maguanensis, commonly known as the Maguanensis Begonia, is a herbaceous perennial native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves with a velvety texture, which contribute to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade, making it suitable for shaded garden settings.

This variety is valued for its aesthetic qualities and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
