Barosma Crenata

Barosma crenata is a unique and resilient plant native to southern Africa. It thrives in rocky and arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. This evergreen shrub is known for its fragrant leaves and small, ornamental flowers. Its ability to survive with minimal water makes it a popular choice for xeriscaping. Despite its hardiness, it remains a relatively obscure species in the world of horticulture.
