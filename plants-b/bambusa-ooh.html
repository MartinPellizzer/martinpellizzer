Bambusa ooh

Bambusa ooh, commonly known as the golden bamboo, is a type of grass native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender culms that can grow up to 10 meters in height. The plant has narrow, elongated leaves that are typically a vibrant green color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Bambusa ooh is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional construction and crafts.
