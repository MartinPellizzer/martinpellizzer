Bubon rigidius

Bubon rigidius, commonly known as the rigid thornplant, is a shrub native to arid and semi-arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, spiny foliage and small, pale green leaves that are adapted to reduce water loss. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers that bloom in the spring, followed by small, round fruits that contain several seeds. Bubon rigidius is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it a hardy plant in harsh environments.

Its unique morphology and ecological adaptations make it an important species in its native habitat.
