Bidens coronata var. tenuiloba

Bidens coronata var. tenuiloba, commonly known as slender crownflower, is a flowering plant native to parts of North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and often aromatic foliage. The plant features slender, hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture. Its flowers are yellow with white markings, typically appearing in clusters at the ends of the stems.

It is often found in open, disturbed habitats such as fields and roadsides.
