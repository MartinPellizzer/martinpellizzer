Bulbophyllum phaeoneuron

Bulbophyllum phaeoneuron, commonly known as the purple-veined orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments with well-drained soil. The plant produces striking, often fragrant flowers with a distinctive purple or pink coloration on the petals and sepals. Its leaves are usually broad and fleshy, adapted to store water in its natural habitat.

This species is appreciated by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and unique floral structure.
