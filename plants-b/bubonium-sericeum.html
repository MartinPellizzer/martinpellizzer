Bubonium sericeum

Bubonium sericeum, commonly known as the silky bubonium, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems covered in fine, silky hairs. The plant produces small, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a soft, downy texture.

Bubonium sericeum is typically found in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun conditions.
