Blaeria grandis

Blaeria grandis, commonly known as the grand blaeria, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that bloom in clusters, typically in shades of red and orange. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are arranged oppositely along its stems. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Blaeria grandis is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
