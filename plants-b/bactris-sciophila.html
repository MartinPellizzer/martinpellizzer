Bactris sciophila

Bactris sciophila, commonly known as the shade palm, is a species of flowering plant in the Arecaceae family.

It is native to the humid forests of South America, particularly in countries like Brazil and Peru. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers and is valued for its ornamental qualities in tropical landscapes.
