Betula chinensis f. lancifolia

Betula chinensis f. lancifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf birch, is a variant of the Chinese birch.

It is characterized by its slender, lance-shaped leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant is native to eastern Asia, particularly China, and thrives in temperate climates. Its bark is smooth and often exhibits a pale to reddish hue.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
