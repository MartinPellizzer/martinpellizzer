Bulbophyllum minus

Bulbophyllum minus, commonly known as the miniature orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid that thrives in humid, tropical environments. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture from the air and decaying organic matter.

Bulbophyllum minus is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
