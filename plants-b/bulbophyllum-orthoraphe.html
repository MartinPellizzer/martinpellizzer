Bulbophyllum orthoraphe

Bulbophyllum orthoraphe, commonly known as the narrow-veined orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, moist environments within tropical forests. The plant features a pseudobulb that supports its leaves and flowers, which are often delicate and fragrant. Its flowers are usually yellow or greenish-yellow with intricate patterns, and it produces small, fleshy fruits.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by enthusiasts for its unique appearance.
