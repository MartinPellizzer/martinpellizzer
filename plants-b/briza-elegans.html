Briza elegans

Briza elegans, commonly known as the silvergrass, is a perennial grass native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery plumes that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a silvery-blue hue that adds ornamental value to gardens.

Briza elegans is often used in landscaping for its aesthetic appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
