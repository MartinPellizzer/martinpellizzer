Bulbophyllum trilineatum

Bulbophyllum trilineatum, commonly known as the three-veined orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in humid, shaded environments. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers with three distinct veins on the petals. Its bulbs are an essential part of its life cycle, allowing it to survive in less favorable conditions.

It is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
