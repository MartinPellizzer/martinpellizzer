Bromus Condensatus Var. Pomoensis

Bromus condensatus var. pomoensis is a rare grass species native to the western United States. It thrives in moist, open habitats such as meadows and stream banks. This variant is distinguished by its compact growth and unique floral structure. Due to habitat loss and environmental changes, it faces significant conservation challenges. Understanding and protecting this plant is crucial for preserving biodiversity in its native range.
