Bulbophyllum burfordense

Bulbophyllum burfordense, commonly known as the Burford's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, pale yellow to greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows on tree trunks or branches, exhibiting an epiphytic lifestyle. Its pseudobulbs are elongated and segmented, supporting the plant's photosynthetic structures.

The flowers are often short-lived but attract various pollinators such as bees and flies.
