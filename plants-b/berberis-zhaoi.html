Berberis zhaoi

Berberis zhaoi, commonly known as the Chinese barberry, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features thorny stems and lance-shaped leaves that turn yellow in the fall. The plant produces small, orange-red berries that are edible but often have a bitter taste. It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often used in landscaping.

Berberis zhaoi is also noted for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions and climates.
