Botrychium ternatum var. alabamense

Botrychium ternatum var. alabamense, commonly known as the Alabama moonwort, is a small fern with distinctive botanical features.

It has a creeping rhizome and produces a single, spore-bearing sorus on the underside of its fronds. The fronds are typically triangular in shape and have a delicate, fern-like appearance. It is native to the southeastern United States, particularly in Alabama and Florida.

This plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.
