Bulbine subbarbata

Bulbine subbarbata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved bulbine, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, narrow leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating to reduce water loss. The plant produces yellow flowers in late spring or early summer, which are clustered in racemes. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its resilience to drought and heat makes it a popular choice for low-maintenance landscaping.
