Bauhinia tuichiensis

Bauhinia tuichiensis, commonly known as the Tuichi bauhinia, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive, bipinnate leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or white in color. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in moist, well-drained soils.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
