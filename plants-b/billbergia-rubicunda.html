Billbergia rubicunda

Billbergia rubicunda, commonly known as the red billbergia, is a striking bromeliad native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its vibrant red bracts that surround the flower, giving it a dramatic appearance. The plant features rosette-shaped leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating, which helps it retain moisture. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Billbergia rubicunda is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
