Brenania rhomboideifolia

Brenania rhomboideifolia, commonly known as the rhomboid-leafed orchid, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its rhomboidal-shaped leaves and produces small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in the understory of tropical forests. It has a complex root system that helps it absorb nutrients from the soil.

This orchid is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its unique foliage.
