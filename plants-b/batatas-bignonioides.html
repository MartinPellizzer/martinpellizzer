Batatas bignonioides

Batatas bignonioides, commonly known as the yellow bell vine, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its striking yellow, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has heart-shaped, velvety leaves that are typically a deep green color. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Batatas bignonioides is often cultivated for its ornamental value and can be grown as a climber or shrub.
