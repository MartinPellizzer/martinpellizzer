Bulbophyllum yoksunense

Bulbophyllum yoksunense, commonly known as the Yoksun orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Turkey.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and clustered pseudobulbs. The plant produces strikingly colorful flowers with intricate patterns, often in shades of yellow, orange, and red. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate to high light conditions.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species in its native habitat.
