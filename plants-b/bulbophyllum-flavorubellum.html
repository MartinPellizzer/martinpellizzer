Bulbophyllum flavorubellum

Bulbophyllum flavorubellum, commonly known as the red-bellied bulbophyllum, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, often brightly colored flowers. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, typical of many orchid species. Its flowers are usually fragrant and can be found in clusters, adding to its ornamental value.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
