Blumea aromatica var. nesogenes

Blumea aromatica var. nesogenes, commonly known as the aromatic blumea, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic leaves and yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as mountain forests. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and ecological significance in its native habitat.
