Begonia × ingramii

Begonia × ingramii, commonly known as the hybrid begonia, is a hybrid plant resulting from the cross between Begonia semperflorens and Begonia pavonina.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, pink, and white. The plant typically has heart-shaped leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Begonia × ingramii is popular in gardens and as a potted plant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
