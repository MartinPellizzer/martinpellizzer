Blackstonia grandiflora

Blackstonia grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered blackstonia, is a deciduous shrub native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, fragrant white to pale pink flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are ovate to elliptic in shape and have a smooth or slightly hairy surface. Blackstonia grandiflora thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained, loamy soils.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively low maintenance once established.
