Bermudiana minutiflora

Bermudiana minutiflora, commonly known as the small-flowered Bermuda plant, is a flowering plant native to the Bermuda Islands.

It is characterized by its tiny, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pale yellow. The plant has small, narrow leaves and thrives in well-drained, sandy soils. It is often found growing in coastal areas and is adapted to the warm, humid climate of Bermuda.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
