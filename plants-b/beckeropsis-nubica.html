Beckeropsis nubica

Beckeropsis nubica, commonly known as the cloud cushion, is a succulent plant native to the arid regions of South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and fleshy, cylindrical stems that store water, allowing it to survive in dry conditions. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange, adding a vibrant touch to its otherwise muted appearance. Its leaves are thick and waxy, reducing water loss through transpiration.

Beckeropsis nubica is well-suited for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its low maintenance and drought tolerance.
