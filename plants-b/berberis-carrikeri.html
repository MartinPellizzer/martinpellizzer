Berberis carrikeri

Berberis carrikeri, commonly known as the Carriker's barberry, is a shrub native to the western United States.

It features compound leaves with three to five elliptical leaflets, and it produces bright yellow flowers in early spring. The plant bears red, berry-like fruits that are edible but often have a bitter taste. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or arid environments.

Berberis carrikeri is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract wildlife.
