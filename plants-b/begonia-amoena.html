Begonia amoena

Begonia amoena, commonly known as the pink begonia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety pink flowers that bloom in clusters, often with a subtle fragrance. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are typically a deep green color. It thrives in partial shade and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Begonia amoena is valued for its ornamental beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
