Bulbophyllum decurvulum

Bulbophyllum decurvulum, commonly known as the curving lip orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, curving lip that gives it its common name. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers that are typically greenish-yellow with dark markings. It is epiphytic, meaning it grows on the surface of trees or other plants without being parasitic.

The botanical features of Bulbophyllum decurvulum make it a prized specimen among orchid enthusiasts.
