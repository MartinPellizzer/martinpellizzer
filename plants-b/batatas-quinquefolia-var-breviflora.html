Batatas quinquefolia var. breviflora

Batatas quinquefolia var. breviflora, commonly known as the short-flowered arrowleaf, is a member of the Convolvulaceae family.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves with five lobes and clusters of small, white to pale purple flowers. The plant produces edible tubers that are similar in appearance to sweet potatoes but have a more delicate flavor. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

This variety is valued for both its ornamental beauty and its potential agricultural use.
