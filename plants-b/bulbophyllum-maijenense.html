Bulbophyllum maijenense

Bulbophyllum maijenense, commonly known as the Malay orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

This epiphytic plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. It typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant has pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, aiding in its survival in its natural habitat.

Its unique floral structure and adaptability make it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
