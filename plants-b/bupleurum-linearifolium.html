Bupleurum linearifolium

Bupleurum linearifolium, commonly known as slender valerian, is a perennial herb native to the eastern United States.

It features slender, upright stems that can grow up to 1 meter in height, adorned with lance-shaped, gray-green leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in clusters during the summer months. Its root system is fibrous and often used in traditional herbal medicine.

Bupleurum linearifolium thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or wetland habitats.
