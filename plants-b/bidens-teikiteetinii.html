Bidens teikiteetinii

Bidens teikiteetinii, commonly known as the Hawaiian ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features slender, hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves with serrated edges. Its flowers are yellow and occur in clusters, typically blooming from late spring to early summer.

It is often found in coastal and lowland areas, thriving in well-drained soils.
