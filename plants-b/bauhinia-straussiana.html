Bauhinia straussiana

Bauhinia straussiana, commonly known as the pink bauhinia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, bipinnate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces showy, pink to purple flowers that bloom in clusters, adding ornamental value to gardens. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for arid or semi-arid climates.

Bauhinia straussiana is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, improving soil fertility.
