Bulbophyllum taeniophyllum var. taeniophyllum

Bulbophyllum taeniophyllum var. taeniophyllum, commonly known as the tiger orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, long, and often striped flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, allowing it to thrive in humid, shaded environments.

The flowers bloom in clusters and can last for several weeks, making it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
