Boehmeria hirta f. canescens

Boehmeria hirta f. canescens, commonly known as hairy Boehmeria, is a variant of the Boehmeria genus native to Asia.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its hairy, fibrous stems and broad, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It is often cultivated for its strong, durable fibers used in making textiles and paper.

This plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is valued for both its ornamental and industrial applications.
