Bondtia scoparia

Bondtia scoparia, commonly known as the desert broom.

It is a flowering plant native to arid regions of Africa, particularly in Namibia and Angola. The plant features small, yellowish flowers that bloom in dense clusters, often in late spring or early summer. Its leaves are typically narrow and succulent, adapted to conserve water in dry environments.

Bondtia scoparia is well-suited for xeriscaping due to its low water requirements and ability to thrive in poor soil conditions.
