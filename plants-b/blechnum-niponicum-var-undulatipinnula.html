Blechnum niponicum var. undulatipinnula

Blechnum niponicum var. undulatipinnula, commonly known as the wavy-pinnate shield fern, is a variant of the Japanese shield fern.

It is characterized by its distinctive, undulating fronds that give it a unique appearance. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit, forming dense clumps of foliage. Its fronds are typically leathery and dark green, with a serrated edge.

This fern thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in temperate forests.
