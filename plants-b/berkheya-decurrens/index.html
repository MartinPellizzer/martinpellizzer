Berkheya Decurrens

Berkheya decurrens is a striking member of the Asteraceae family native to southern Africa. This plant is known for its vibrant yellow flowers and unique, elongated leaves that cascade downward. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes. Its drought tolerance and ornamental value have made it a favored plant among gardeners and botanists alike. Berkheya decurrens offers both aesthetic appeal and ecological benefits, contributing to biodiversity in its natural habitat.
