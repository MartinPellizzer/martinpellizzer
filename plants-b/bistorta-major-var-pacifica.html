Bistorta major var. pacifica

Bistorta major var. pacifica, commonly known as Pacific bistort, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Pacific Northwest of North America.

It features upright, rhizomatous stems that produce clusters of bright pink to purple flowers in late summer. The plant has lance-shaped, serrated leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and streambanks.

Its botanical characteristics make it an attractive and hardy addition to native plant gardens.
