Brickellia riddellii

Brickellia riddellii, commonly known as Riddell's brickellia, is a perennial herb native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, arranged in clusters at the tips of the stems.

Brickellia riddellii thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or rocky habitats.
