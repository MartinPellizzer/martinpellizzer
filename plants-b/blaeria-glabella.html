Blaeria glabella

Blaeria glabella, commonly known as the smooth bladderwort, is a small aquatic plant belonging to the family Lentibulariaceae.

It is characterized by its simple, branching stems and small, rounded leaves that are often covered in a waxy cuticle. Its flowers are small and usually pale in color, with distinctive spurred petals that aid in pollination.
