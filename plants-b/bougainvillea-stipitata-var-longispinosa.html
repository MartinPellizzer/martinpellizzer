Bougainvillea stipitata var. longispinosa

Bougainvillea stipitata var. longispinosa, commonly known as the long-spined bougainvillea, is a compact, thorny shrub native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its dense clusters of vibrant pink bracts that surround small, white flowers. The plant has small, oval leaves that are usually covered in fine, soft hairs. It thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often used in landscaping and as a hedge.
