Bidens personans

Bidens personans, commonly known as the common beggarweed, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its alternate, heart-shaped leaves and yellow flowers with five petals. The plant produces small, hairy seeds that are dispersed by wind, contributing to its invasive potential in some regions. It thrives in moist, disturbed soils and is often found in wetlands and along roadsides.
