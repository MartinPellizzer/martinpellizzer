Brachystelma lanceolatum

Brachystelma lanceolatum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, narrow leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange, typically blooming in spring. It is often found growing on rocky surfaces or in sandy soils.

Due to its hardy nature, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
