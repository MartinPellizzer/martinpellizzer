Baumgartia scandens

Baumgartia scandens, commonly known as the climbing beautyberry, is a member of the Rubiaceae family.

It is a deciduous shrub native to the southeastern United States, characterized by its trailing growth habit and ability to climb or trail along structures. The leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a smooth texture, contributing to its ornamental appeal in gardens.
