Bouchea Rusbyi

Bouchea rusbyi is a rare and striking plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Africa. It is known for its unique, urn-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. This species is often found in rocky outcrops and dry, well-drained soils. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species. Its beauty and scarcity make it a prized subject for botanists and plant enthusiasts alike.
