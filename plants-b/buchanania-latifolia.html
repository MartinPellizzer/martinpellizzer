Buchanania latifolia

Buchanania latifolia, commonly known as broadleaf Buchanania, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Malvaceae and is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, white or pinkish flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. Due to its ornamental value and potential medicinal properties, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and traditional herbal practices.
