Bosqueia welwitschii

Bosqueia welwitschii, commonly known as the Welwitsch's bosquea, is a small, evergreen shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, typically during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, showing resilience to drought conditions.

Bosqueia welwitschii is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
