Begonia chuniana

Begonia chuniana, commonly known as the Chinese begonia, is a perennial herb native to China.

It features striking, heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and deep green color. The plant produces clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in partial shade and well-draining soil, making it suitable for shaded garden areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor cultivation.
