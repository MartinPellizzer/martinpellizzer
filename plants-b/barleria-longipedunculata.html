Barleria longipedunculata

Barleria longipedunculata, commonly known as the long peduncle barleria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features slender, erect stems covered in fine hairs and produces clusters of small, tubular flowers in shades of pink or purple. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Barleria longipedunculata is often found in grasslands and along roadsides, thriving in well-drained soils.
